9am Days of Faith

Hosted by Heather Hildebrand.
These are the days of YOUR life. Make them DAYS of FAITH! Faith is all about trusting, hoping and believing in the goodness of people and the greatness of life. But let's face it, our day to day lives can sometimes feel a bit challenging. This show is all about PRACTICAL yet PURPOSEFUL living. It is the spiritual support system we all need but rarely give ourselves the opportunity to have. Faith isn't about going it alone. Our spiritual help is unlimited but we sometimes need each other for strength. Each week I will bring you channeled messages that get to the heart of the matter. We will explore useful practical ways to enhance our every day lives. Because every day really does matter. And the best part is we will do it together which means I get to share my Days of Faith with you!
