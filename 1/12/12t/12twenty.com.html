B. Information we collect when you interact with 12Twenty


When you visit our website www.12Twenty.com, you may choose to contact us with general inquiries, request a demo of our Services, or refer us to the offices of your university’s Career Center. If you chose to contact us in these ways, we may collect standard personal information such as name, email, job title, and phone number, professional information such as school, degree type or job title, and information about your university’s Career Center, including the name and email of the publicly listed contact there.


As is true of most web sites, we gather certain information automatically and store it in log files. This information includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and clickstream data.


We use this information, which does not identify individual users, to analyze trends, to administer the site, to track users’ movements around the site and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. We do not link this automatically-collected data to personal information.


We use "cookies" to collect non-personal information. Cookies are small pieces of data we store for an extended period of time on your computer, mobile phone, or other device to make 12Twenty easier to use and to protect both you and 12Twenty. For example, we use them to store your login ID (but never your password) to make it easier for you to login whenever you come back to 12Twenty. You can remove or block cookies using the settings in your browser, but in some cases that may affect your ability to use all of the features of 12Twenty.
