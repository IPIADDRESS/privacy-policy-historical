12GO ASIA PRIVACY POLICY

12Go Asia is committed to protecting your personal data and takes the responsibility to secure any and all customer private information in connection with the use of this website or any other in control of 12Go Asia group.

12Go Asia is an online ticket agent and sells tickets of transport operators and other resellers. 12Go Asia is not a transport operator as it is and does not operate any vehicles on its own. More information about 12Go Asia you may get in our Terms and conditions.

This privacy policy sets out how 12Go Asia (referred to as “we”,” us”, “our” or “12Go Asia”) [as 12Go Asia Pte., Ltd., including subsidiary company 12Go (Thailand) Co., Ltd., and associated company Xenios IT Co., Ltd., and where appropriate, other companies in 12Go Asia group or other entities over which 12Go Asia exercises management control], uses and protects any information that you provide hereafter when you use this website.

This policy applies where 12Go Asia is acting as a “data controller” with respect to the personal data of the website visitors and service users and in accordance with the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA), the subject of governmental amendments from time to time, and Thailand governing laws including the Thailand Civil and Commercial Code B.E. 2468, Penal Code, B.E. 2499 (1956), and Child Protection Act B.E. 2543 (2003), the subject of governmental amendments from time to time.

12Go Asia believes in the protection of your privacy information. We may change this policy from time to time by updating this page. The privacy policy covers the treatment of your personally identifiable information and other information gathered by 12Go Asia through this website. The privacy policy also covers a treatment of your any personal information that 12Go Asia may share with their business partners or other third parties under very limited circumstances.

The privacy policy does not apply to general practices and treatment of information (whether personal or not) by third parties that 12Go Asia does not own or control, including but not limited to any third party websites or services that you elect to access through this website.

By entering into 12Go Asia privacy policy you ensure your clear and full consent to provide your personally identifiable data and to use this website in accordance with conditions set herein, you also ensure that you are older than 16 years; otherwise, your parents or legal guardians should give such consent.

You also acknowledge that the personal data you submit for publication through our website or services may be available, via the internet, around the world. We cannot prevent the use (or misuse) of such personal data by others.

What personal data we collect

Personal data means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person ("data subject"); an identifiable natural person is one who can be identified, directly or indirectly, in particular by reference to an identifier such as a name, an identification number, location data, an online identifier or to one or more factors specific to the physical, physiological, genetic, mental, economic, cultural or social identity of that natural person (the definition is contained in the Article 4 (1) of the the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR).

We may collect your personal data when you book a travel with us through this website (including but not limited to either directly or indirectly through our trusted third parties partners), create your personal account, use this website and / or mobile (or any other) application and other websites accessible through our website and / or mobile (or any other) application, participate in a survey or competition, or when you contact us.

We also collect the following personal data:

  • Your profile and contact data (including your name and surname, passport (ID) details or other recognized personal ID card numbers and details, date of birth, gender, address, telephone numbers, email addresses, details of your travels, other travel information on bookings made at this website, information of other passengers travelling with you (name, surname, passport (ID) details or other recognized personal ID card numbers and details, date of birth, gender, other travel information, etc.)).
  • Depending on the case, medical conditions for passengers who have special medical requirements.
  • Your payment data (including your bank details or payment cardholder details, PayPal account or other e-payment account information).
  • Other information in connection with booking tickets, which may be required by a carrier.
  • Other payment information, which may be required by governmental, tax, audit, or bank authorities to complete or check transactions.
  • Your usage data (your locations, including real-time geographic location of your computer or device through GPS, Bluetooth, and your IP address, along with crowd-sourced Wi-Fi hotspot and cell tower locations, if you use location-based features and turn on the location services settings on your device or computer; browser type and version, operating system, referral source, length of visit, page views and website navigation paths, as well as information about the timing, frequency and pattern of your service use, etc.).
  • Your correspondence data (information contained in or relating to any communication that you send to us, including file attachments you may provide).
  • Your enquiry data (information contained in any enquiry you submit to us regarding goods and/or services we provided).
  • Other information relevant to customer surveys and/or offers, a review you leave about the carrier after your travel.

What do we do with the information we collect and process

We collect your personal data to understand your needs and provide you with a better service, and in particular for the following reasons:

  • Providing you with products and services you request. Personal information you give us is used for the purpose to perform the services you have requested for in relation to your booked travel, including requests for its changes and cancellation. We send only the required data to carriers and travel operators that are directly involved in your travel including your review texts to allow the operators to improve their services.
  • Providing you with urgent information in the event of your travel time change or cancellation, weather conditions, travel delays, force majeure, etc. We may send you communication about the services you have requested for any change to such services.
  • For accounting, billing and audit purposes and to detect and / or prevent any fraudulent or money laundering activities. We may share your payment data with third parties partners (such as OMISE, PayPal, Kasikorn Bank Thailand, OCBC Bank Singapore).
  • Providing and improving of our products and services, for the purposes of operating and analyzing the use of this website, for its monitoring and improving, for ensuring the security of this website and services, maintaining back-ups of our databases and communicating with you.

Storing, controlling and security of your personal data

All your personal data is collected, processed and kept for the purpose of internal record keeping as required by the law. We will only process your personal data where we have a legal basis to do so. The legal basis will depend on the reasons we have collected and needs to use your personal data for.

Personal data that we process for any purpose or purposes shall not be retained for longer than is necessary for those purposes. Where it is impossible to specify in advance the periods for which your personal data will be retained, we will determine the period of retention based on the purposes of collecting of your personal data and exhausted of needs to retain it. For the purposes to determine the appropriate retention period we consider the amount, nature and sensitivity of the personal data, the purposes for which the personal data is collected and processed. We also have to comply to retain periods specified by Singapore and Thailand laws.

When we no longer need your personal data, we will securely delete or destroy it. We will also consider if and how we can minimize over time personal data that we use, and if we can anonymize your personal data so that it can no longer be associated with you or identify you, in which case we may use that information without further notice to you.

Notwithstanding the other provisions of this Section, we may retain your personal data where such retention is necessary for compliance with a legal obligation to which we are subject, or in order to protect your vital interests or the vital interests of another natural person.

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, we have put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

We may disclose your information to trusted third parties for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. We require all third parties to have appropriate technical and operational security measures in place to protect your personal data.

We will not sell, distribute or lease your personal information to third parties unless we have your permission or it is required by law. We may use your personal information to send you promotional information about third parties, which we think you may find interesting if you tell us that you wish this to happen.

How we use cookies

A cookie is a file containing an identifier that is sent by a web server to a web browser and is stored by the browser. The identifier is then sent back to the server each time the browser requests a page from the server. A cookie is a small file which asks permission to be placed on your computer's hard drive. Once you agree, the file is added and the cookie helps analyze web traffic or lets you know when you visit a particular site.
We use traffic log cookies to identify which pages are being used. This helps us to analyze data about web page traffic and improve our website, to monitor which pages you find useful and which you do not. A cookie in no way gives us access to your computer or any information about you, other than the data you choose to share with us.

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • authentication - to identify you when you visit our website and as you navigate our website;
  • status - to help us to determine if you are logged into our website;
  • personalization - to store information about your preferences and to personalize the website for you;
    *security - to protect user accounts, including preventing fraudulent use of login credentials, and to protect our website and services generally;
  • advertising - to help us to display advertisements that will be relevant to you;
  • analysis - to help us to analyze the use and performance of our website and services;
  • cookie consent - to store your preferences in relation to the use of cookies more generally.

We also use cookies of trusted service providers such as Google, Bing, Yahoo, Yandex, Twitter, Facebook. The information gathered relating to our website is used to create reports about the use of our website. We are not responsible for third parties’ use of your personal data where such use is permitted for their own purposes. Please consult their privacy policies for further information.

You can choose to accept or decline or block cookies. You can modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. This may prevent you from taking full advantage of the website. Blocking all cookies will have a negative impact upon the usability of many websites.
