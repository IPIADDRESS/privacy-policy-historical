Privacy
181 dot FM has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site.

• Information Automatically Logged
We use your IP address to help determine our aggregate user levels, diagnose problems with our servers or ban offensive users, and to generally administer this Web site.

• Cookies
This site uses cookies to limit display of the same ad repeatedly, and to deliver content specific to your interests. We may also use cookies to confirm our aggregate user levels.

• Advertisers
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. • Surveys, Contests, Email, and Registrations

Contact information from these forms or incoming email is used to contact site registrants, contest winners or reply to incoming email inquiries. 181.FM will not sell, submit, or furnish your name or email address to any outside party without your permission, nor will we place you on any kind of mailing list.

Basic demographic information is shared with advertisers on an aggregate basis. We use this data to tailor your experience at our site by featuring content that we believe you may be interested in, and displaying the content according to majority preferences.
