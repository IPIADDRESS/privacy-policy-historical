Privacy policy

Yuuguu Service Privacy policy

1. Personal information

  1. (a) Account activity. You need a Yuuguu Account to access Yuuguu. Yuuguu asks for some personal information when you create a Yuuguu Account, including your email address and a password, which is used to protect your account from unauthorized access. A Yuuguu Account allows you to access many of our services that require registration.

    When you use Yuuguu, we may record information about your usage, such as when you use Yuuguu, the size of your contact list and the contacts you communicate with, and the frequency and size of data transfers. Information displayed or clicked on in the Yuuguu interface (including UI elements, settings, and other information) is also recorded.
  2. (b) Content. We will store the contents of text chats in your Yuuguu account.

2. Uses

  1. (a) We use this information internally to deliver the best possible service to you, such as improving the Yuuguu user interface and maintaining a consistent and reliable user experience.
  2. (b) On a regular basis, we delete activity information associated with your account.
  3. (c) When you use Yuuguu, people included in your contact list may be able to see your online presence, username, Yuuguu address, and any information that you choose to add to your profile, including images, links, and custom status indicators.
  4. (d) We may relay content for users in some cases.

3. Your choices

  1. (a) You can change your Yuuguu account settings at any time in the "Preferences" section of Yuuguu. You may, for example, modify your online presence status or block particular users through the user interface.
  2. (b) You can uninstall the Yuuguu software at any time through the "Add or Remove Programs" Control Panel. When you uninstall the software, Yuuguu and its copies of all items are deleted from your computer. Your other files and applications remain unaffected. You may delete your contacts information or chat histories you have stored in your Yuuguu account by sending an email to support@yuuguu.com. Because of the way we maintain this service, such deletion may not be immediate, and residual copies may remain on backup media.

Yuuguu Website Privacy policy

Yuuguu is dedicated to the fundamentals of protecting your privacy. Personal information may be collected from our visitors on a voluntary basis. This may include name, e-mail address, address and telephone number as supplied by e-mail link to Yuuguu.

The personal information retained is only used by us to process an enquiry and/or for marketing and services. We don't disclose personal information to 3rd parties. Additionally, our web server collects the domain names (not the e-mail addresses) of visitors to our website. This information is aggregated to measure the number of visits, average time spent on the site, pages viewed, etc. Yuuguu uses this information to measure the use of our site and to improve our content and services. Yuuguu does everything it can to keep all information from our online visitors secure against unauthorized access and use.

All information is protected by our security measures, which are reviewed regularly.
