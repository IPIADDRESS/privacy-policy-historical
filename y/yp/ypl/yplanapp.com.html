FAQ

BOOKING

WHAT'S YPLAN ALL ABOUT?

YPlan is for the spontaneous. Get inspired by a fantastic event, pay in 2 taps and go tonight! Our curators party tirelessly around the clock to bring you the best events in London. Every day we give the YPlan stamp of approval to a special few and score you a unique deal. That's us in a nutshell.

HOW DOES IT ALL WORK?

We feature events as soon as we can, usually 48 hours before they start. Keep an eye out within event for unique YPlan perks such as: access to a sold-out show, invitation-only events, automatic upgrade, a free something with your booking, or a mouth-watering discount.

HOW DO I BOOK AN EVENT?

Select the event that tickles your fancy from the main events screen, tap 'Book Now' at the bottom, select the number of tickets, tap 'Confirm Booking' at the bottom (add your card details now if it's your first booking), and 'Yes' to confirm the purchase. That's it - just like magic, you're going tonight.

HOW DO I PAY FOR THE BOOKING?

Once you've entered your American Express, Visa, MasterCard, or Maestro card to YPlan, you can buy any event in 2 taps. To enter your card details, tap 'Settings' in the main menu, then tap 'Credit Card', and enter your details. You can also add this information when making your first booking.

YPlan is certified by STAR (Society of Ticket Agents and Retailers), an official ticket industry regulatory body, so you can be sure that with YPlan you’re booking from a trusted company. We also encrypt your payment details using the industry's leading standard, SSL (Secure Sockets Layer), and never store payment card information on the phone (that's why you only see the last four digits of the card).

ARE THERE ANY HIDDEN FEES?

Nope, we think it sucks to sneak in fees and charges just before you confirm your booking. At YPlan, you pay what you see. Always.

HOW DO I GET IN TO THE EVENT ONCE I'VE MADE THE BOOKING?

You'll find your latest booking in the side menu and all your bookings in 'My Tickets'. At the bottom of each ticket under 'How Do I Get In?' you'll find instructions on how to redeem your tickets and enter the venue. If you have any questions, who you gonna call? YPlan's team is here for you ‘round the clock, be it early breakfast or last cocktail: 02071 839 394. Or tap the 'Call Us' button in 'Help'.

DO EVENTS SELL OUT?

Of course, so snap 'em up quick. Once they're gone, they're gone.

HOW LATE CAN I BOOK AN EVENT?

If you see it in the app, it's yours for the taking. We'll keep all tickets open until the last possible moment.

CAN I BOOK AN EVENT FOR TOMORROW OR LATER?

Why, yes you can. We usually have events on 48 hours before start time, but sometimes there’s more added last minute, and even a select few that go on sale several days early. Keep checking YPlan regularly as the top events sell out quickly...why plan when you have YPlan?

CAN I CHOOSE MY SEATS?

At the moment, all the seats are on a 'best available' basis. Unless it's general admission or unreserved seating, we'll always let you know what type of seats you are buying, just not the exact seats. If you buy 2 or more tickets, we'll make sure you all sit together.

ARE MY FRIENDS SEEING THE SAME EVENTS AS ME?

Not necessarily. Everyone sees individually handpicked events, so you and your mates may see different sets of events. Use the app to share events with your buddies, or better still, snap up tickets for everyone - they'll love you forever.

USING YPLAN

HOW DO I CHANGE MY ACCOUNT INFORMATION?

Go to 'Settings' in the main menu. Easy peasy lemon squeezy.

CAN I INVITE FRIENDS TO USE THE APP?

Appsolutely. Get your friends to join the movement by going to 'My Rewards' in the main menu and 'Share Your Code' through your social networks, email, or text message.

HOW DO I GET ME SOME CREDIT?

As Frank Sinatra once sang, 'Start spreading the news' and we'll reward you. YPlan Rewards is free money for you to spend on any event. Want some? Go to 'My Rewards' and invite your friends to YPlan - they'll get £7 for joining, and you'll get £7 when they first buy something. Not too shabby, huh? Keep sharing and we'll keep dishing out the credit.

WHERE CAN I GIVE FEEDBACK?

Tell us the good, the bad, and the ugly. We'll answer any questions you may have, listen to your feedback, or just have a friendly chinwag if you're feeling lonely. Rain or shine, early breakfast or last cocktail – we'd love to hear from you!
