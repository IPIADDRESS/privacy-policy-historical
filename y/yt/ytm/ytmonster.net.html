Privacy Policy

We only collect the following information from the use of YTMonster. We will never sell your personal information to third parties. If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy policy, please contact us.

User Information

When you sign up on YTMonster you must provide us an account username and a valid e-mail address. We only use these informations for notices, warnings, newsletters, password retrival and account activation.

Access Information

Like many other websites, we make use of log files. The information inside the log files include internet protocol (IP) addresses, internet service provider (ISP), date/time, referring/exit pages.

IP adresses and other such confidental information are not linked to any other information that are personally identifiable.

Other

We, YTMonster reserves the right to change this Privacy Policy at any given time with or without notice.
