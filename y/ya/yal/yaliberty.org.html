Privacy Policy

Young Americans for Liberty is committed to providing the highest level of protection for your online privacy and security.

As you explore our website, we invite you to provide us with information about yourself, so that we can contact you with the latest news about Young Americans for Liberty. This includes information you provide us when you use our website, sign up for newsletters, participate in surveys, contests, discussion boards or other social media functions on our site, and when you report a problem with our site. The information you give us may include your name, address, e-mail address and phone number, personal description, photograph, educational and employment history, and other personally identifiable information. By using our website and providing your information, you consent to our use, storage, and reporting of such information for any lawful purpose.

You will always be able to unsubscribe from our email database quickly and easily through the link provided at the bottom of every email that we send. 

Our organization encourages you to spread the word about Young Americans for Liberty and the grassroots movement to your friends, family, neighbors, and co-workers. We reserve the right to store any information about the people you contact through our website.

YALiberty.org links to websites and blogs that we hope you'll find useful and informative, but we are not responsible for the content or the privacy policies of these 3rd party websites or social networks.

Finally, we reserve the right to change our privacy policy. We encourage you to check this page occasionally for the most current information. If you have any questions, concerns or suggestions about our privacy policy, please email us at contact@yaliberty.org. The date at the bottom of this page indicates when this privacy policy was last updated.
