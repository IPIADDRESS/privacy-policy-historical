Yappi.com respects your privacy and use of the web.

Your computer reaches us via a particular IP (Internet Protocol) address. This can reveal the identity of your Internet Service Provider and their demographic location. This data is used for statistics to determine number of visitor's daily and to prevent abusive members from accessing the site. None of this information is sold or disseminated to outside parties. The individual web pages from Yappi.com that you have requested using your browser are kept for information on traffic patterns within our site. This helps to determine the most popular pages and categories of our site. It is also used to find links to pages that no longer exist. None of this information is sold or disseminated to outside parties.

Your browser has the ability to use so-called "cookies" which, unless you disable their use, store small amounts of data on your computer about your visit to various web sites. Our message boards uses these cookies to enhance our poster's experience during their visit. None of this information is sold or disseminated to outside parties. Because of the nature of web message boards, any information that is posted on the board will become public. We encourage poster's to keep private information off the board.

Our message boards ask posters for an email address, demographic, and profile data. The customer's contact information is used to contact the visitor only when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings. No information is sold or disseminated to outside parties.

Banner ads displayed through advertising brokers on our pages may elect to use cookies to prevent the same ad being displayed too many times to the same user, or to identify Yappi.com as the site responsible for a "click-through" for an advertiser. However, cookies can not reveal any personal information about you. None of this information is available to Yappi.com, and therefore is not sold or disseminated to outside parties.

This site contains links to other sites. Yappi.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of other web sites that are not under our control.
