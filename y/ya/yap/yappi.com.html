Yappi.com respects your privacy and use of the web.

Your computer reaches us via a particular IP (Internet Protocol) address. This can reveal the identity of your Internet Service Provider and their demographic location. This data is used for statistics to determine number of visitor's daily and to identify abusive members to take actions necessary to prevent them from posting on the site. None of this information is sold to outside parties. The individual web pages from Yappi.com that you have requested using your browser are kept for information on traffic patterns within our site. This helps to determine the most popular pages and categories of our site. It is also used to find links to pages that no longer exist. None of this information is sold to outside parties.

Your browser has the ability to use so-called "cookies" which, unless you disable their use, store small amounts of data on your computer about your visit to various web sites. Our message boards uses these cookies to enhance our poster's experience during their visit. None of this information is sold to outside parties. Because of the nature of web message boards, any information that is posted on the board will become public. We encourage poster's to keep private information off the board.

Our message boards ask posters for an email address, demographic, and profile data. The customer's contact information is used to contact the visitor only when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings. No information is sold to outside parties.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp.

This site contains links to other sites. Yappi.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of other web sites that are not under our control.
