Privacy Policy

We respect your business and your privacy. Keeping this information safe is important to us. We will never sell or share information to any third parties for commercial purposes.

The information we collect is used for internal purposes only.

Registration is not required to browse content. Before buying or uploading content registration is required.

Financial information (credit card number, expiration date) is used for billing purposes only. We do not store any credit card numbers on our servers. Financial security is provided by PayPal Inc, VISA, Mastercard, 3D-secure and DnB NOR.

We always use industry-standard encryption technologies when transferring and receiving consumer data exchanged with our site. When we transfer and receive certain types of sensitive information such as financial information, we redirect visitors to a secure server.

We use 'cookie' technology to collect data regarding your visits to the site. This data is stored in aggregate form and is not used to identify any particular user or individual, by other means than to provide you with better access to our site, products or services.

We may occasionally send you e-mails about new services, products and news relevant to you.

We may link to websites, including those of our subsidiaries and third-party content providers, which have different privacy policies and practices from those disclosed here.
