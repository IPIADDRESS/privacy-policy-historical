Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting www.yslbeautyus.com, the US online store of YSL Beauté (the “Site“). YSL Beauté (“we/us/our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. Among other things, this Privacy Policy describes the personal information collected about you through the Site, why YSL Beauté collects it, how we use it and when we share it with third parties.


What does this privacy policy cover?

This Privacy Policy applies to any personal information collected through the Site, in circumstances when you knowingly provide such information to us.

 

YSL Beauté reserves the right to make changes to the Site and its disclaimers, policies, and terms and conditions at any time without notice. If YSL Beauté makes a material modification to these Terms and Conditions, including the Privacy Policy, it will notify you by sending an e-mail to the e-mail address associated with your account or by displaying a prominent announcement above the text of this Terms and Conditions or the Privacy Policy, as appropriate, for thirty (30) days. YSL Beauté is not responsible if you do not receive such notification email due to the actions of your ISP or any email filtering service.

 

Personal information collection and usage


We do not collect personally identifiable information about you, except when you provide it to us. Our goal is to record information to process your orders and if you request so, to inform you about our special offers, events and promotions.

a. Email address for newsletter

You may choose to opt-in to receive our newsletters or any other email communications from YSL Beauté and/or its affiliates during the registration process or by signing up on the Site specifically to receive such communications.
 

If you prefer not to receive such opt-in information emails, please do not opt-in to receive this information. If you do opt-in and decide later that you would no longer like to receive these emails, you may opt-out in the My Account section or on any email sent by YSL Beauté.
 

b. Names, addresses, phones

If you register to receive newsletters and/or when you purchase product(s) through the Site, we collect and store the following personally identifiable information: first and last name, billing and shipping address(as), phone, and e-mail addresses to process your order.
 

If you register with the site, we will also collect and store other personal information such as your product interest and your previous purchases on the Site.
 

YSL Beauté ships orders through trusted warehouse and shipping carriers such as UPS, FEDEX, and USPS which are not permitted to use personal information for purposes other than actual YSL Beauté shipping.
 

c. Billing information

When you purchase product(s) through the Site we will collect your billing (e.g., credit card) information to process your payment. As an ultimate protection of your privacy, we do not store, at any time, your credit card information on the Site. (We shall request your credit card information at each purchase.) YSL Beauté processes payments through certified processors and banks compliant with this Privacy Policy.
