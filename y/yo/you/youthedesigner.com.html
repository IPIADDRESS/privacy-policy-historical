Cookies

A cookie is a series of data characters that, when programmed into a web site, is placed by the web server into the browser’s application folder on your computer. Once placed onto your machine, the cookie will allow the web site to “recognize” you as a unique individual. YouTheDesigner.com uses these Coolies to personalize information for certain segments of its customer base. Secondly, in some instances, cookies are used to allow YouTheDesigner.com the opportunity to associate individual customers with their information profiles. For example, through the use of cookies, YouTheDesigner.com can “remember” your user ID when you login to YouTheDesigner.com on your computer. Should you choose to do so, cookies can be removed from your hard drive and certain web browsers can be set so that cookies are not used or saved. Please check with your browser provider for more information on removing cookies.
