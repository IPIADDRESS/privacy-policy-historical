Privacy and collection of personally identifiable information

You can visit most portions of our web site, www.youngvoters.eu, without telling us who you are and without revealing any personal information. By uploading content, the league of young voters will remember your Facebook account or your email address; that means that we will include in our database either the email used to sign up or the email you use for your Facebook log-in and all tags, titles and comments related to your contributions.

youngvoters.eu will collect email addresses and data about visitors when such data is voluntarily submitted to www.youngvoters.eu. The League of Young Voters makes every effort to ensure the secure collection and transmission of sensitive user information using industry accepted data collection methodologies.

The league of young voters will use the data in order to communicate changes in our system and future challenges in our campaign by including your data in e-mail newsletters or facebook marketing strategies.
