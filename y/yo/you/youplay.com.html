Privacy Policy – youplay.com
This privacy policy has been created in order to disclose the information gathering and dissemination practices for this web site, youplay.com ("Website"). This policy is in addition to the Terms of Use governing the Website and should be read in conjunction with those Terms of Use.

The Website is owned and operated by YouPlay Pty Limited (“YouPlay”), which is a joint venture between Lovatts Interactive Pty Ltd (a division of Lovatts Publications Pty Ltd (“Lovatts”))  and Southern Cross View Pty Ltd which is a wholly owned subsidiary of Southern Cross Broadcasting (Australia) Limited (“Southern Cross Broadcasting”) ("We" and "Us"). You can find out more about us by clicking on links at the bottom of this page. Information provided by you to the Website may be shared amongst and used by these companies, including their subsidiaries and related companies. However, except in the limited circumstances set out below, none of these companies will provide personally identifiable information about you to third parties without your express permission.

Collection of Personal Information
We respect your privacy and will endeavour to ensure your privacy is maintained when providing information electronically.  There are two kinds of information that websites can obtain about you:

Aggregate Information: Aggregate Information is anonymous data that websites use to analyze trends, administer the Website, diagnose problems on the sites' servers, track user's movement, gather broad demographic information for aggregate use and to help improve the quality of the web pages. It may derive this information from a domain name or an IP address. None of this information is connected with Personally Identifiable Information.

Personally Identifiable Information: Personally Identifiable Information is any information that personally identifies you. When you become a Website member, we ask you for personal details including name, post code, date of birth, your location, gender and email address. We may also ask you to provide us with some lifestyle information when you register with the Website or for various features of the Website.

Some of the information that we ask you for is mandatory. The primary purposes for which we collect it are:

  • to enable us to identify who is using the service
  • to enable you to get the most out of the Website
  • to provide you with casual gameplay online and via other digital media and
  • to provide you with activities and information in relation to YouPlay’s business, Lovatts’ business and Southern Cross Broadcasting’s radio stations, television stations  and other businesses.

If a question is not specified to be mandatory it is up to you whether or not you answer it.

We also record other information including responses to promotions, give-aways and competitions. Whenever a page is requested from our webserver, we record the time, date and URL of the request, along with information on the browser software that is being used. This information is collected to enable us to better understand the preferences of our members and to enable us to better target our communications including competitions and prizes.

Any information you post on bulletin boards and/or communicated in chat areas and the like on the Website becomes public information. While we strive to protect and respect your privacy, we cannot guarantee the security or treat as personal information any information you disclose in a chat room or bulletin board.

Disclosure of Personal Information

We may share Aggregate Information with companies in the Lovatts group and the Southern Cross Broadcasting group, and our partners, advertisers and sponsors. Personally Identifiable Information about you may be shared in the Lovatts group and the Southern Cross Broadcasting group, and may be shared to external service providers only for the purpose of analysis and the purpose of providing casual gameplay on websites and in digital media.  The Lovatts group will not contact you directly unless you choose to opt-in to receive information about Lovatts.  The Southern Cross Broadcasting group will not contact you directly unless you choose to opt-in to receive information about Southern Cross Broadcasting.

We plan to regularly send a data file to Full Circle Digital, the company that serves advertising on our behalf, containing the following member details of yours: member ID and the DOB, gender, state and postcode that have been provided by you as the member, but not your name and address.  Members supply these details when subscribing to the Website. Full Circle Digital will then use those details only for the purpose of displaying demographically-targeted advertisements to members when you are visiting our website.  They will not be used for any other purpose.  If, however, you would like your personal information not to be used by Full Circle Digital to deliver targeted ads, simply send an email to optout@fullcircledigital.com.

We may disclose personally identifiable information about you to any company in connection with a proposed or actual sale, merger, transfer, or exchange of all or a portion of a business or operating unit, but only if that company agrees to use the data prior to any acquisition solely for the purpose of making its acquisition decision and to observe the relevant provisions of our Privacy Policy with respect to the data. We may disclose your personally identifiable information to a particular person or company in the event that that person or company acquires assets from us, and we will require that person or company to agree to observe the relevant provisions of our Privacy Policy with respect to personally identifiable information about you.

We will also disseminate Personally Identifiable Information about you where we believe it is necessary:

  • because it is required by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary in order to conform to the edicts of the law, cooperate with law enforcement or other government agencies, or comply with a legal process served on the Website or court orders;
  • to provide you with a service which you have requested;
  • to implement our Terms of Use;
  • to protect our rights or property or those of any Website user, or any member of the public.
  • to lessen or prevent a serious threat to a person's health or safety.

Except as described above we will not share your Personally Identifiable Information with any third party without your consent unless we tell you otherwise when asking for it.

Cookies
A cookie is a piece of data stored on your hard drive which may contain information about you, your member status on the website or about websites you have visited. The use of cookies on websites is a common industry standard. Cookies act as unique identifiers, used to distinguish between different users. They can enable us to track and target your interests to enhance your experience on our site and to provide rating information, ad tracking and other functionality normally associated with the delivering of websites. For instance, by setting a cookie on the Website, you would not have to log in a password more than once, thereby saving time while on our site. Please note that even after we have terminated a cookie, remnants of that cookie may remain on your computer. These remnants are normally inactive.

We use the independent company Nielsen//NetRatings to measure and analyse usage across the Website. The Nielsen//NetRatings SiteCensus tracking code collects the core information on usage of the Website. To learn more about Nielsen//NetRatings' privacy policy, visit their website at http://www.nielsen-netratings.com/corp.jsp?section=leg_prs

One use of cookies on the Website is to allow you to access the members only sections. If you reject the cookie, you may still use the Website. However you will have limited access to the members only areas of the site. For example, you must have access to the members only area to access the competitions.

Various services available to you on the Website will require the use of cookies. We may use cookies when we conduct voting polls, surveys, and contests to limit multiple responses and registrations.

We do not use cookies to retrieve information from your computer. Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. If you'd prefer, you can set yours to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. However, it is possible that some parts of the site, such as the voting polls, surveys, contest, and game areas, will not function properly if you automatically elect not to receive cookies.
