The privacy policy has been compiled to better serve those users who are concerned about how their “Personal Identifiable Information” is being used online.  Personal Identifiable Information, as used in US privacy law and information security, is information that can be used on its own or with other information to identify, contact, or locate a person, or to identify an individual in context. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for www.youthcorner.in.

User Personal Information

We may collect personal identification information from users in a ways, including, but not limited to, when users visit www.youthcorner.in, register on the site, and in connection with other services, activities, features or resources available on our site. Non-Personal Information

We may collect non-personal identification information about users whenever they use our site. Non-personal identification information may include the browser name, the device being used and technical information about users means of connection to our site. We may use your Network IP and similar information such as internet service provider being utilised. 

User Information Protection

The information collected by us, however, only if you voluntarily submit, such as by registering at our site, sign up for newsletter or using certain site features, are never shared with third parties and is not sold, rented or traded for any commercial or individual purpose whatsoever. 

www.youthcorner.in only provides news articles and information and we never ask for private information like social security number or credit card details. The site is scanned on a regular basis for security reasons and malware vulnerabilities in order to make our site safe and protect user’s personal information, only if provided by user themselves.

In accordance with Fair Information Practices, we will notify our subscribers, users within 2 business days via email.

www.youthcorner.in may collect and use user’s personal identification information for improving customer service and support, to personalize user experience, to respond to user’s enquiries and requests, and to improve the quality of our content and services, products, based on the feedback you provide. However, We are strictly against outsourcing, selling of user’s information.

Cookies

Our Site may use “cookies” to enhance User experience. “Cookies” are used to distinguish and relate non-personal data to individuals. “Cookies” are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computer’s hard drive through your Web browser (if you allow) that enables the site’s or service provider’s systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information.

User’s web browser places cookies on their hard drive for record-keeping purposes and sometimes to track information about them. User may choose to set their web browser to refuse cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent. If they do so, note that some parts of the site may not function properly.

Compliance With Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

Protecting the personal identification information of children is very important to us. We respect Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act and for that reason, WE NEVER COLLECT OR MAINTAIN PERSONAL INFORMATION AT EXAMSWATCH FROM CHILDREN UNDER 13.

NO content or services on www.youthcorner.in are structured to attract children below 13 and we specifically do not market to anyone under the age of 13, keeping up with the COPPA compliance which puts parents in control.

These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement to compile non personal identification information about you or others who use your computer. This privacy policy does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.
