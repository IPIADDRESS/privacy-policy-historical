Information Collection and Use

Our primary goals in collecting information are to provide and improve the Services and the related features and content, to administer your subscription and to enable users to enjoy and easily navigate the Site. Below are the types and categories of information that we collect. As used herein "User Files" means the files and folders (including any information or data therein) users may store, send, receive, edit, sync, share, or otherwise organize or manage, and to which users may append electronic signatures, via use of the Services.

  • When you register with us through the Services or order certain services from us we will ask you for personally identifiable information. This refers to information about you that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Information"). Personal Information includes, but is not limited to, your name, phone number, credit card or other billing information, email address and home and business postal addresses. We use your Personal Information mainly to provide the Services and administer your inquiries. We also collect certain other information that you provide as part of registration and the administration and personalization of your profile web page (e.g., without limitation, your zip code and the state in which you live) ("Non-Identifying Information").
  • We use your Personal Information (in some cases, in conjunction with your Non-Identifying Information) mainly to provide the Services, complete your transactions, and respond to inquiries.
  • We may combine your Personal Information with Non-Identifying Information and aggregate it with information collected from other YouSendIt users (e.g., registered users and non-registered users) to attempt to provide you with a better experience, to improve the quality and value of the Services and to analyze and understand how our Services are used. Subject to the section below titled “Information Sharing and Disclosure,” we may also use the combined information without aggregating it to serve you specifically, for instance to deliver a product to you according to your preferences or restrictions.
  • We also use your Personal Information to contact you with YouSendIt newsletters, marketing or promotional materials and other information that may be of interest to you. If you decide at any time that you no longer wish to receive such communications from us, please follow the unsubscribe instructions provided in any of the communications or email us at optout@yousendit.com with an explicit request to have your information removed from our mailing list or login to your YouSendIt account and change your “user preferences” with respect to the receipt of communications from us. We will comply with your request as soon as reasonably practicable. We will send you strictly service-related announcements on rare occasions when it is necessary to do so. For instance, if a part of our Services is temporarily suspended for maintenance, we might send you an email. Generally, you may not opt-out of these communications, which are not promotional in nature. If you do not wish to receive them, you have the option to disable your account.

Log Data. When you visit the Site or use the Services, whether as a registered user or a non-registered user just browsing, our servers automatically record information about your use of the Services and your visit to the Site ("Log Data"). This Log Data includes the size and name of all User Files that you make available via the Services. The Log Data may also include information such as your computer’s Internet Protocol ("IP") address, your browser type, what YouSendIt Application you were using, the web page you were visiting immediately prior to visiting our Site, pages of our Site that you visited, the time spent on those pages, information you searched for on our Site, access times and dates, and other statistics. We use this information to monitor and analyze use of the Services and for the Site’s technical administration, to improve our Services’ functionality and user-friendliness, and to better tailor them to our users needs. We also use this information to verify that users of the Services meet the criteria required to process their requests. Because the names of the User Files you submit are not treated as Personal Information, we recommend that you do not include any personally identifiable information in the User Files that you make available via the Services.

Cookies & Other Technologies

Like many web sites, we use “cookies” to collect information. A cookie is a small data file that we transfer to your computer’s hard disk for record-keeping purposes. We use cookies for two purposes. First, we utilize persistent cookies to save your registration ID and login password for future logins to the Site. Second, we utilize session cookies to enable certain features of the Site, to better understand how you interact with the Site and to monitor aggregate usage by YouSendIt users and general web traffic on the Site. We link the information we store in cookies to the Personal Information you submit to us while you are on the Site. Unlike persistent cookies, session cookies are deleted from your computer when you close your browser. We may also use "pixel tags," "web beacons," "clear GIFs" or similar means (individually or collectively "Pixel Tags") in connection with our Site to collect usage and geographical location data. A Pixel Tag is an electronic image, often a single pixel that is ordinarily not visible to users and may be associated with cookies on a user’s hard drive. Pixel Tags help us to determine the effectiveness of promotional or advertising campaigns and allow us to deliver targeted advertising to you regarding our Services when you visit the websites of our advertising partners. Third party advertisers on the Site may also place or read cookies on your browser. YouSendIt does not have access to or control of any cookies that its advertisers may store in your browser and this Privacy Policy does not apply to the use of cookies by advertisers.

You can instruct your browser, by changing its options, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the web sites you visit. If you do not accept cookies, however, you may not be able to use all portions of the Site or all the functionality of the Services.

We partner with third party ad networks to manage our advertising on other web sites. Our ad network partners use cookies and Pixel Tags to collect non-personally identifiable information about your activities on this Site and other web sites to provide you targeted advertising on the other web sites you visit based upon your interests. If you wish to not have this information used for the purpose of serving you targeted ads, you may opt-out of the use of your information for such purposes by the certain ad networks included in TRUSTe’s "Trusted Ads Opt-out Manager" list by clicking here http://preferences.truste.com. Please note that according to TRUSTe, using its Trusted Ads Opt-Out Manager does not opt you out of being served advertising and you will continue to receive generic ads.

Other than as described in this section, YouSendIt does not rent or share information about you with third parties.

We are not responsible for any personally identifiable information you choose to submit in a comment and will not treat such information as Personal Information for the purpose of this policy. To request removal of your Personal Information from our blog or community forum, contact us at support@yousendit.com. In some cases, we may not be able to remove your Personal Information, in which case we will let you know if we are unable to do so and why.

Aggregate Information and Non-Identifying Information. Our Service Providers have access to your Personal Information only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

YouSendIt employees or representatives may access, but not view the contents of, your User Files when acting on behalf of YouSendIt to comply with a legal obligation, such as responding to a search warrant.

Business Transfers. YouSendIt may sell, transfer or otherwise share some or all of its assets, including your Personal Information, in connection with a merger, acquisition, reorganization or sale of assets or in the event of bankruptcy. If YouSendIt is involved in such a transaction, you will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on our Site of any choices you may have regarding your Personal Information.

Corporate Accounts. If you are an individual YouSendIt registered user, and the domain of the primary email address associated with your YouSendIt account is a domain owned by your employer and that email address was assigned to you as an employee of that organization, and that organization establishes a corporate account with YouSendIt and adds you to that account, then certain information concerning past use of your individual account may become accessible to the administrator of that corporate account, such as the text and subject of messages you have sent, the name and content of the User Files you have sent, the date and time messages were sent, and the email addresses of the recipients; provided, however, that such information with respect to User Files that have expired or that have been deleted by you will no longer be accessible to you or to such administrator.

Changing or Deleting Your Information & Data Retention

General. As used herein "Standard Service" means our Services except for those provided in connection with the Zosh by YouSendIt App. All YouSendIt registered users of our Standard Service may review, update or correct the Personal Information in their registration profile ("Account Information") by logging into their account and changing the "user preferences" associated with their account. Users of the Zosh by YouSendIt App are able to reset their passwords by initiating a password reset within the application.

Retention and Deletion. YouSendIt may retain your Account Information indefinitely as required by law or for legitimate business purposes, regardless of whether you have disabled your account as stated above. When you use our Standard Service by default your User Files are deleted from our systems within approximately seven (7) days after you make them available via the service. When you purchase a subscription to our Standard Service your User Files will be stored on our systems according to the “user preferences” you set for the file, if any. Users of the Zosh by YouSendIt App may delete their documents from our servers by selecting the relevant documents for deletion via the Zosh by YouSendIt App.

YouSendIt will retain Log Data consistent with applicable legal requirements. Generally, at a minimum, YouSendIt will retain the Log Data collected from you in its active, internal company databases for up to six months, at which point it will migrate such Log Data to its offline archival systems, where YouSendIt will retain the Log Data for a period of three years (together, the "Retention Period"). After the Retention Period, YouSendIt will purge the Log Data from its systems. In the event of an investigation by law enforcement or a response to claims or legal process, we may retain the Log Data until such investigation or process is completed, which may be longer than the Retention Period.

If as a YouSendIt registered user you wish to disable your account or request that we no longer use your information or provide you services contact us at optout@yousendit.com. You may also disable your account for our Standard Service by submitting a Support Request to Customer Service.
