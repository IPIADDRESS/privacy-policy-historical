Collecting personal information

Certain features on yesnetwork.com - such as sweepstakes, contests, message boards and chats - require users to provide information about themselves during the registration process. Usage of these features is voluntary, and yesnetwork.com only collects personally identifiable information in connection with your use of these features. Information required for registration for such features will vary, but will often include your first and last name; address including city, state, zip code and country; date of birth; and e-mail address.

Information collected during the registration process is used to personalize your yesnetwork.com experience and to provide you with special offers from yesnetwork.com and our partners. In addition, yesnetwork.com performs statistical analyses of user behavior in order to measure interest in and use of the various areas of the site.

yesnetwork.com may also share such information with other businesses, including our marketing, sponsorship and advertising partners. However, we will only share such information with third parties whose policies regarding personal information is consistent with our Privacy Policy.

YES Network may need to use your account profile to contact you regarding your account status or other issues relevant to the yesnetwork.com service. YES Network may also disclose user information when we believe in good faith that the law requires it.

You should be aware that any information you voluntarily disclose on bulletin boards or chat areas can be collected and used by others.

Opting out

During the registration process, you have the opportunity to indicate whether you would like to 'opt out' of receiving promotional and/or marketing information about other products, services, and offerings from YES Network or it's partners. Upon request, yesnetwork.com will allow any user to opt out of further promotional contacts at any time.

Cookies

Cookies are small data files that are stored on your computer’s hard drive. They allow Web sites to recognize returning visitors and track site usage. yesnetwork.com uses cookies to store non-personal information and make it easier for you to use certain site features.

yesnetwork.com may use advertising server technology from third parties to display ads on the site. These ads may contain cookies; yesnetwork.com does not have access to these cookies or the information in them.

If a user does not want information collected through the use of cookies, there is a simple procedure in most browsers that allows the user to deny or accept cookies; however, users should note that cookies may be necessary to provide the user with certain features on yesnetwork.com.

Changing your personal information

Upon request, yesnetwork.com will use reasonable efforts to allow you to update or correct your personal information. Also, upon request, yesnetwork.com will use commercially reasonable efforts to functionally delete your personal information from its database; however, it may be impossible to delete your entry without some residual information being retained because of backups and records of deletions.

Changes to this policy

yesnetwork.com will notify its users of any changes to this privacy policy. Your continued use of yesnetwork.com following the posting of any changes to this Policy will mean you accept and consent to those changes.
