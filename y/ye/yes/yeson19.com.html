When you visit the Coalition for Cannabis Policy website the following information is collected:

  1. The type of the domain from which you accessed the Internet.
  2. The date and time you accessed the website.
  3. The IP address from which you accessed the website.
  4. The type of browser software used to view the website.

When you visit the website, personally identifying information is not collected, unless you choose to provide such information. Personally identifying information is collected when submitted through one of the following web forms: Contribution, Email Sign-up, Contact Us, Tell A Friend, and Petition pages.

Submitted information is collected and used for campaign related purposes related to cannabis policy reform. We also work closely with parties and allied organizations and may share some basic contact information with them, and they may contact you.

Personal and financial information is required when making a contribution to the Coalition for Cannabis Policy Reform. This information is used to process your contribution or merchandise request. All contributions made through the website are regulated by Federal Election law. Federal Election law requires political committees to report the name, mailing address, occupation and name of employer for each individual whose contributions aggregate in excess of $200 per election cycle. Your contribution will be used in connection with Federal elections and is subject to the limits and prohibitions of the Federal Election Campaign Act.

If you have questions about this privacy statement please feel free to contact us.

Thanks for supporting the Coalition for Cannabis Policy Reform!
