 Security and Privacy

 • How can we protect your privacy

The security of your personal information is very important to us. We never sell or rent your personal information to any third parties under any circumstances. We value your trust very highly, and will work to protect the security and privacy of any personal information you provide to us and will only use it as we have described in our Privacy Policy.
 • Safe purchase at Yeskey.com
We want you to feel completely secure when ordering from Yeskey. We use a technology called Secure Sockets Layer (SSL), which encrypts (or encodes) sensitive information before it is sent over the Internet.
 • What information will be collected by Yeskey.com?

As set forth in more detail below, Yeskey ("Yeskey" or "We") collects personal information that you provide when using the Yeskey web site ("Site"). This information includes your first and last name, email address and a password when you create your Yeskey account. You can browse this Site without creating an account, but you will need to create an account when you purchase something and in order to take advantage of certain features, such as "Save for Later," "Address Book" and the other Yeskey services. We may ask you to confirm your personal information when you contact our Customer Service department. This will allow us to protect your confidentiality by verifying your identity.

Yeskey does not intentionally collect personal information about children under the age of 13. If you are under the age of 18 but at least 13 years of age you may use this Site only under the supervision of a parent or legal guardian who agrees to be bound by the Yeskey Terms of Use. Children under the age of 13 may not use this Site and parents or legal guardians may not agree to the Yeskey Terms of Use on their behalf. If you do not agree to (or cannot comply with) any of the Yeskey Terms of Use, do not use this Site. 

Purchase Information
When you make a purchase from Yeskey, we collect your name and payment method information. We collect this information in order to bill you for your purchase. We also collect information that allows us to ship your order. This includes the name, address, and phone number of the intended recipient(s).
When you create an account at Yeskey, you can choose to save your billing information in "My Yeskey." You can also save one or more shipping addresses in your Yeskey Address Book. You may change or delete this saved information anytime. Simply go to My Yeskey. Log in with your ID and password, then edit or delete whatever personal information you wish. Services Account Information
We collect personal information from users who wish to use any of the Yeskey services, including but not limited to, Yeskey's Transaction Platform. In order to use these services you must provide your email address and password or create an account at Yeskey.
