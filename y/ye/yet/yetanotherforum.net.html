About YAF

YAF.NET is lead by Jaben Cargman

The YAF.NET is an open source .NET project lead by Jaben Cargman. YAF.NET has additional assistance by an excellent team of international programmers who collectively push the project forward as well as generiously helping the community in the support forum. Please consider giving a donation to support the project's future growth, mission and open source ideals.
