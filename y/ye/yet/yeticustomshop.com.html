YETI Custom Shop Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2016

Thank you for visiting YETI Custom Shop ("YETI Custom Shop"). This privacy policy (the "Policy") discloses how YETI Custom Shop collects, uses, and (under certain conditions) discloses your personal information on our websites (the "Website"). In addition, you will find information about how we secure your personal information along with your options regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information.

By visiting the Website directly or indirectly you confirm your acceptance of the practices described in this Policy.

This Policy applies only to the Website. YETI Custom Shop is not responsible for the content or privacy practices on any web site not operated by YETI Custom Shop to which the Website links or that links to the Website.

Information Collection, Use, and Sharing

We are the sole owners of the information collected on the Website and we only collect and have access to information that you voluntarily give us on the Website, email, or other direct contact from you. We will not sell or rent your information to anyone.

We use your personal information to provide a premium, efficient, and meaningful customer experience. This may include making the Website easier to use by automatically filling in information, by delivering content to you that we feel is most relevant, or to respond to requests from you for information or help.

Due to our exclusive relationship with YETI Coolers, LLC (YETI) we will from time to time make available to YETI information about our customers. Information we share with YETI is subject to their privacy policy which you can read on their website: http://yeti.com/privacy-policy/.

We will not share your information with any third party outside of our organization, except for YETI, other than as necessary to fulfill your order or respond to your request except. For example, we will necessarily share your information with our shipping carriers so that they may deliver your packages to you.

Unless you ask us not to we may contact you via email or telephone in the future to tell you about special offers, new products, or other announcements.

Orders

When ordering from us on the Website we will request certain information from you. This contact information (name, shipping address, billing address, phone number, etc) and financial information (credit card number, expiration date, etc.) is used for billing and fulfillment purposes. YETI Custom Shop does not store your financial information and relies on partners for credit card processing. Our payment partners are PayPal and Braintree (owned by PayPal).

Cookies

We use "cookies" on the Website. A cookie is a piece of data stored on your computer hard drive that helps us improve your access to the Website and to identify repeat visitors so that we may provide a streamlined and customized experience while on the Website. You may disable cookies on your web browser but doing so may limit your ability to use the Website.

Security

Protecting your information is important to us so we take precautions to protect your sensitive information. The Website uses Secure Sockets Layer ("SSL") to encrypt communication between your web browser and the Website when we ask you for personal or confidential information. While we use industry-standard precautions and technology to protect your personal information we can not guarantee it, but we will make every reasonable effort to do so.

Opt Out and Corrections

You may request corrections to your personal information or choose to opt-out of emails that we might send to you. To do so, please send us an email at hi@yeticustomshop.com or call us at (512) 777-1078.

Updates

This Policy may change from time to time. When we change it we will post updates to this page.

If you feel that we are not abiding by this Policy please contact us immediately at hi@yeticustomshop.com or by telephone at (512) 777-1078.
