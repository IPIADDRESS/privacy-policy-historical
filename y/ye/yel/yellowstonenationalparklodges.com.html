Privacy policy

 

General Overview

This Site is one of several sites (“Sites”) relating to and/or operated by Xanterra Leisure Holding, LLC, its subsidiaries, affiliates and related companies (“Xanterra” or “we”). The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to disclose to you what information we may collect, how we may collect it, with whom we may share it, and certain other matters related to such information, including the choices you have regarding our collection of information and our use and disclosure to other parties of information we may have collected from you. Terms not otherwise defined herein shall have the meaning ascribed to such terms in our Terms of Use.

Except as otherwise discussed in this Privacy Policy, this document only addresses the use and disclosure of information we collect from you online. For some services, we collect and transfer personal information to a service provider, or you may visit other merchants or service providers through links on our Sites who may collect and use your personal information. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these sites before you disclose your personal information to others.

Please read the following to learn more about our data collection policies and practices:

What types of information we collect and why

We may collect personally-identifiable information and non-personally identifiable information when you use our Sites. “Personally-identifiable information” is information that can be used to identify you personally (either alone or in combination with other information), and “non-personally identifiable information” cannot be used to identify a specific individual. The personally-identifiable information we collect is typically used to facilitate the purchase and delivery of information, products or services to our customers and visitors. Specific personally-identifiable information collected may include, but is not limited to the following and may be subject to change:

Reservations & Purchases. In the case of a reservation or other purchase which is completed on one of our Sites, an online credit card transaction is required in order to complete the purchase. During this process we may generate and collect certain personally-identifiable information, such as name, name of the credit card holder, credit card billing address, credit card number, credit card security code, credit card expiration date, customer’s shipping address, phone number, and email address. We typically use this information in order to complete your reservation and/or purchase that you make. We may retain information relating to the customer, e.g. name, email and address, however we do not retain credit card information beyond the duration of your stay with us. Information we retain may be used to provide our services, and also to provide marketing and promotional content that may interest you, including our email and print newsletters.

Voluntary Health Information. For certain tours, activities, or other purposes, we may request health related information (such as medical conditions that may require the special attention of our employees during trips). You are not required to provide health information, but it may impact your ability to participate in a requested activity. You may also have the opportunity to provide dietary information or other preferences in connection with certain tours or activities, if you choose to do so. We use this information so that we can be aware of issues that may arise during your trip (e.g. health issues that may require special care,) offer you certain dietary items, or otherwise work to accommodate your needs.

Print/Online Newsletters, Sweepstakes, and other Special Content. If you wish to receive any of our newsletters, view our webcasts or other interactive features, or participate in any of our contests, drawings, sweepstakes, or promotions, we may require you to complete an online form which will require the submission of certain personally-identifiable information, such as name, mailing address, phone number, email address, and potentially other information. We typically use this information in order to deliver your newsletters, enter you in the promotion, or otherwise provide you with the service you request. We may also use this information to send you marketing and promotional materials, and we may combine this information with other information we hold about you so that we can better tailor our services, marketing, and promotional activities to you.

User Registration and Profile. We may also provide you with the option of creating your own personal profile, or a profile may automatically be created in our database if you provide certain information to us (including if you make a reservation or book a tour through Xanterra, whether through our Sites or otherwise). If a profile is created, we may combine information we receive from you or about you from various sources (including your bookings at our properties, your transactions on our various websites, or information you provide offline) in your profile. We may also use this information to send you marketing and other promotional materials.

How is information collected?

We may collect information from you in several different areas on the Sites, including but not limited to those described below:

Registration or Inquiries

If you register for a service or inquire about our properties, tours, or other travel opportunities, you may be asked to complete a registration form and provide personally-identifiable information, including the information discussed above regarding reservations, profiles, payment, newsletters, and voluntary health information, depending on the nature of the registration or inquiry. You may also be asked to choose a user ID and password in order to take advantage of certain features the Sites may offer, including, but not limited to, newsletters, contests, forums, webcasts or subscription-based services. Similarly, if you agree to provide us with optional profile information, this information will be collected through a registration form.

Newsletters, Site Emails and Mobile Offers

We may offer newsletters and/or other communications to share information with you about the Sites, about us and our affiliates and subsidiaries, about our business partners, or about third party offers or information we think would be of interest to you. In these cases, we will collect the information discussed above regarding print and email newsletters. When you provide this information, you may receive communications either by registering for a particular newsletter or type of information, or in conjunction with a special tour, property, feature or membership for which you have registered on a Site. Remember, if you consent to receive text messages from us, your wireless carrier may impose a charge on incoming texts. Please check with your wireless carrier for details.

Paid Transactions

Certain transactions within our Sites, such as booking a reservation or purchasing items in one of our online stores, may require that you provide us with the registration and payment information, described above. For example, you may provide us with your name, email, address, payment information, and other details. We will use this information to complete the transaction that you have requested, e.g. to process your reservation and to invoice you. Although some of these services are handled directly by Xanterra or its subsidiaries, others are managed on behalf of Xanterra or its subsidiaries by third party service providers, and the information you provide during these paid transactions may be submitted directly to the applicable provider, which in turn may share limited information (such as your name and email address, but not payment account details) with Xanterra or its subsidiaries.

Contests or Sweepstakes

We may operate contests, sweepstakes or other promotions through the Sites, which may require registration in order to enter. Your personally-identifiable information may be used by us to contact you for winner notification, prize delivery confirmation or other promotional purposes. Your entry may also result in your being added to our email or mailing/texting lists as well as those of our promotional partners associated with the promotion. Acceptance of a prize may require you (unless prohibited by law) to allow us to post publicly some of your information on the Sites, such as on a winner’s page, in the event you win a contest or sweepstakes. In some instances, a third-party advertiser or sponsor may operate or host a contest or sweepstakes on the Sites, and collect your information. In those cases, where we are not the sponsor, operator or host of the contest or sweepstakes, we may have no control over the information collected and accordingly urge you to review the official rules of the contest/sweepstakes as well as the advertiser’s or sponsor’s privacy policy applicable to the contest or sweepstakes before participating.

Surveys or Voting

From time-to-time we may request personally-identifiable information from you via surveys or voting polls. Participation is voluntary and you will have the opportunity to decide whether or not to disclose information. At times, you may have to register to vote or to take part in a survey. The information we collect through surveys will vary depending on the nature of the survey, but typically includes your email, name, and other profile or registration information.

The Sites use standard technology called “cookies,” (which are small data files that are transferred to your computer when you allow your browser to accept cookies) or similar technologies. Please view our Cookie Policy (which is incorporated herein) for detailed information regarding the use of cookies and similar technologies on our Sites. If you do not want information collected through the use of cookies, you generally can opt out of providing this information by turning the cookies off in your browser; however, some areas of the Sites may not provide you with a personalized experience if you have disabled the use of cookies.

Our analytics services may collect information such as how often users visit our Sites, what pages they visit when they do so, and what other sites they used prior to coming to our Sites. We use the information we get from Google Analytics and other providers to improve our Sites. Although Google Analytics places persistent cookies on your computer to identify how users arrived at a Site and the number of times they return, the cookies are not intended for use by anyone but Google to provide analytics to us. IP Addresses and click-stream data

We may identify users by their IP address (e.g. to determine if a visitor is located in a country other than the U.S.). An IP address is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer whenever you access the Internet. When you request Web pages from the Sites, our computers log your IP address. Some countries may consider IP addresses to be personal information, so you may exercise choices with regard to your IP address as with other personal information, as described further below.

We may identify click-stream data for purposes of system administration, to improve the Sites and to report aggregate information to our advertisers and sponsors and/or other internal uses related to the Sites. Click-stream data is information collected by our computers when you request Web pages from the Sites. Click-stream data may include such information as the page served, the time, the source of the request, the type of browser making the request, the preceding page view and other such non-personal information. This data helps us analyze how visitors arrive at the Sites, what type of content is most popular, what type of visitors in the aggregate are interested in particular kinds of content and advertising, and the like.
