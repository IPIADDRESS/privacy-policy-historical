Cookies may enhance your online experience by saving your preferences while you are visiting a particular website. Pixel tags are a type of technology placed on a website or within the body of an email for the purpose of tracking activity on website, or when emails are opened or accessed, and are often used in combination with cookies.

  • deliver ads that relate to your interests;

  • help you efficiently access your information after you sign in;

  • automatically update the Yellow Images application on your system and related devices; and
  • enhance our web offerings to you and to provide an enjoyable and an innovative online experience.
