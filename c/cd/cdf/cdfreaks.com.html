RankOne Media Group Privacy Policy

RankOne Media Group is committed to protecting your privacy. The following privacy policy is for all the websites we publish, including, but not limited to, CD Freaks.com.

Collection of your personal information
In order to use some services from our website, you will be asked to register and provide us with personal information such as your e-mail address, name and country. We may also collect demographic information like age and gender.

We may collect information about your visit, including the pages you view, the links you click and other actions taken in connection with our websites. We also collect certain standard information that your browser sends to every website you visit, such as your IP address, browser type and language, access times and referring Web site addresses.

Use of your personal information
We use your personal information in order to operate and improve our websites and the service we deliver. These uses may include making the sites or services easier to use by eliminating the need for you to repeatedly enter the same information; performing research and analysis aimed at improving our products and services; and displaying content and advertising that are customized to your interests and preferences.

Sharing of your personal information
Several sites have member profiles which disclose information about your account. However we will not provide or sell your personal information to a third party without your consent.

Use of cookies
Our websites use "cookies" to enable you to sign in to our services and to help personalize your online experience. A cookie is a small text file that is placed on your hard disk by a web page server. Cookies contain information that can later be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer.

Use of e-mail addresses (mailinglist)
We are just like you concerned about controlling unsolicited commercial e-mail, or "spam." Our websites may use mailinglist services for its users but e-mail addresses are always based on "double opt-in". RankOne Media Group will not sell, lease or rent our e-mail subscriber lists to third parties.
