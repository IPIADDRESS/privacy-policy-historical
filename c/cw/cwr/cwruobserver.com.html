This privacy policy covers the website and activities of CWRUobserver.com. Other sites linked to from this website have their own privacy statements which can be viewed on those sites.

We encourage you to be aware when you leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every website that collects personally identifiable information. We are not responsible for the personally identifiable information you choose to submit in these forums.

Voluntary Submission

CWRUobserver.com does not collect personally identifying information about any individual CWRUobserver.com website user except when knowingly provided by such individual.
