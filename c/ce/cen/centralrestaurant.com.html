Privacy Policy - Updated 6/18/2010

At Central Products, LLC (DBA "Central Restaurant Products"), we are commit
ted to protecting your privacy. Central Restaurant Products takes precautions to ensure the security of your Company information and strives to keep it accurate. Central Restaurant Products carefully protects your Company information from loss, destruction, falsification, manipulation and unauthorized access or unauthorized disclosure. Central Restaurant Products may use your Company information to contact you about a Central Restaurant Products offer in support of your business needs or to conduct online surveys to better understand our customers' needs and profiles. If you choose not to have your Company information used to support our customer relationship, Central Restaurant Products will respect your choice.
  • Security
    Our secure server software (SSL) is the industry standard and is among the best security software available for secure commerce transactions. Designed with the support of Verisign, the leading provider of Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) and digital certificate solutions used by web sites to ensure secure communications.

    ABOUT SSL CERTIFICATES

    We encrypt all of your personal information including credit card number, name and address so that it cannot be read as information travels over the internet.
  • Cookies
    We only use cookies to keep track of items that you put into your shopping cart and to tell us whether you have shopped with us before, making the shopping experience more convenient for you.
  • Email Notifications
     We will email you about questions or changes regarding your order (including shipping confirmations), as well as notify you of special offers and promotions. We may also occasionally email you to notify you of important functionality changes at our site. If you would rather not receive notifications of special offers and promotions, you may unsubscribe by contacting us at the address below.
  • Information Shared With Other Companies
     The information that you give us and information about your order may be combined with other personally-identifiable information (such as demographic information and past purchase history) available from our records and other sources. This information will be used to make our future marketing efforts more efficient. This information may also be shared with other marketers to bring you offers of interest. If you prefer that we do not share your name and address with other marketers, please contact us at the address below.
  • Unsubscribe and Change Information
