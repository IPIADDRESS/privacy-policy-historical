Personally Identifiable Information

CEMEX does not collect personally identifiable information about individuals except when individuals provide such information on a voluntary basis, such as by registering with the CEMEX.com Web site or sending e-mail or other communications to or through CEMEX.com. CEMEX shall maintain appropriate safeguards, consistent with the standards of the industry, to ensure the security, integrity and privacy of information collected from users of CEMEX.com.

Personally identifiable information collected by or on CEMEX.com will not be sold or provided to third parties without user consent. In addition, users of CEMEX.com will be given the opportunity to "opt out" of receiving promotional and/or marketing information about products and services offered by CEMEX or its partners and affiliates. Upon request, CEMEX will use reasonable efforts to allow users to update/correct personal information previously submitted which is no longer correct. Also, upon a user's request, CEMEX.com will use commercially reasonable efforts to functionally delete the user and his or her personal information from its database; however, it may be impossible to delete a user's entry without some residual information because of backups and records of deletions.

Notwithstanding the foregoing, CEMEX.com users are advised that aggregate statistics on visitors will be compiled by CEMEX.com, and may be used to conduct market research or other activities. In addition, CEMEX.com may share anonymous, aggregate data with advertising, business or promotional partners. Nothwithstanding any request by a user to "opt out" of receiving promotional information, CEMEX.com reserves the right to contact a subscriber regarding account status, changes to the subscriber agreement and other matters relevant to the CEMEX.com site and/or the information provided by a user.

Use of Cookies

This site may use "cookies" in connection with certain features or functions. Cookies are specific types of information that a web site transfers to a user's computer hard drive for record-keeping purposes. Cookies may make the use of a Web site easier by, for example, saving passwords and preferences while a visitor is at the site. CEMEX.com does not use cookies to obtain personally identifiable information from a user's computer that was not originally sent in the cookie. While most browsers will automatically accept cookies, you can set your browser to reject cookies. Please be aware that CEMEX.com may allow other companies presenting advertisements or other features on CEMEX.com to access cookies on your computer. CEMEX.com does not control how other companies use cookies.
