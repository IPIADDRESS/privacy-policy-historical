Binary Options, or Fixed Return Options (FROs), are different from traditional investment options. For a successful outcome, the investor is only required to accurately predict whether an asset of their choice will go up or down in value over a given period of time. Furthermore, the extent of the price movement is of absolutely no concern – what counts is its direction. In binary options, as the name suggests, only two results are possible – “up” or “down.”
