        Privacy Policy

        This Privacy Policy describes how CedCommerce uses and protects any information entrusted by you to us. It acquaints of what information we collect, use and safeguard and thereby assists you in making informed decisions while using our services.

        What Information We Collect?

        We may collect the following information:

        • Name
        • Contact Details
        • Other Relevant Demographic Information
        • Information in Regards to Market Survey and/or Offers

        What We Do With the Gathered Information?

        We use the provided information to understand your needs and hence, provide premium quality services to you. The information is utilized for:

        • Internal Record Maintenance
        • Improving our Products and Services
        • Sending Periodical Mails in Regards to New products, Special offers or Other Information Which You may Find Interesting
        • Market research

        Security

        We are committed to protecting your information from any unauthorized access. In order to safeguard and secure the online collected information, we have put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial checks.

        How We Use Cookies

        As you are aware that during the course of web browsing, most browsers automatically accept cookies that allow respective site owners to provide you a personalized experience on any of your further visits. Nevertheless, you can decline cookies with certain changes made to your browser settings.

        We use traffic log cookies for statistical analysis in order to tailor it your needs. Once the purpose is achieved, the required data is immediately removed from our system. In fact, cookies further our objective to allow you take complete advantage of our site.

        Overall, cookies help us provide you with a better website, by enabling us to monitor which pages you find useful and which you do not. A cookie in no way gives us access to your computer or any information about you, other than the data you choose to share with us. You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. This may prevent you from taking full advantage of the website.

        Links

        Our site may cite links to other websites of interest. During the course of relevant linked site visit the respective site privacy policy will come into force. In such an event we cannot protect your privacy and thus, you are advised to be acquainted of the concerned site privacy policy before divulging any information therein.

        Personal Information Control

        You may choose to restrict collection or use of personal information in the following ways:

        • When you are prompted to fill up a form on our site, select the checkbox that allows for information disclosure for non marketing reasons
        • In case you have, at any point in time, agreed for information disclosure for marketing purpose, you can alter the preference by simply writing to us at admin@cedcommerce.com

        We will not lease, sell or distribute your personal information to third parties unless we have your permission or are required by law to do so. However, if you provide your consent to us to share the information, we will endeavor to send you such promotional offers concerning the parties which we regard to be of interest to you.

        You may request details of your personal information which we hold under the Data Protection Act (DPA), 1998. In such an event, you are required to pay a small fee.

        If you require updating information provided by you, please write to us at the above mentioned e-mail address. We will promptly do the needful.
