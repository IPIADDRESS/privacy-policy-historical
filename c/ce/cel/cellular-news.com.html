Privacy Statement for cellular-news

cellular-news has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site.

Information Automatically Logged

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is also used to gather broad demographic information, such as where in the world our visitors are based or the names of companies that visit the site.

Advertisers

We use an outside ad company to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our Web site, cookies received with banner ads are collected by our ad company, and we do not have access to this information.

Surveys

Any online surveys ask visitors for demographic information, such as location, age, employer etc. They will also ask specific questions about your use ands interest in the site. Any contact information will never be disclosed out side the company or used by cellular-news for marketing reasons.

Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site.
We use this data to tailor our visitor's experience at our site showing them content that we think they might be interested in, and displaying the content according to their preferences.

E-Mail's

cellular-news may record on a file e-mail addresses supplied to us by visitors. We will never let outside companies have access to this database. We may on occasions allow companies interested in advertising on cellular-news, have access to an aggregated version of the database so that they can see the user profile, with all the usernames stripped out - namely only the domain names will be supplied. It will not be possible for anyone to extract an individual address from this database.

External Links

This site contains links to other sites. cellular-news is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control.

Contacting the Web Site

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can contact us by using the feedback button on the side menu.
