This site gives users the option to remove their information from our database and to not receive future communications from us. We also give users the option to change or modify the information previously provided.

Use Of Cookies

Cookies are pieces of information left in the settings of your web browser to identify your computer to a website when you enter. Whenever a website welcomes you and tailors data to your specifications, it is reading a cookie and identifying information you may have registered during previous visits. cellphones.ca uses cookies to record your viewing preferences and allow us to tailor information, such as your home cellular area. Cookies also help us estimate and report the size of our viewing community and traffic. While these activities depend on the use of a "cookie," visitors to cellphones.ca always have the option of disabling cookies via their internet browser preferences.

Users should be aware that when you are on cellphones.ca, you could be directed to other sites that are beyond our control. There are links to other sites that take you outside of our service. For example, if you "click" on a banner advertisement, you are directed away from cellphones.ca. You may also be directed to the websites of wireless carriers, phone manufacturers, sponsors, partners etc... These other sites may send their own cookies to users, collect data or solicit personal information.

The use of cookies is an industry standard, and many major Web sites use them to provide great features for their customers.
