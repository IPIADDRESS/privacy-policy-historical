Mobovida, LLC (dba CellularOutfitter.com) Web pages contain web analytics beacons (called a "single-pixel gif" or "Web beacon") to compile anonymous, aggregated statistics about our Web site usage. Web analytics beacons are traffic and e-commerce analysis services which enables us to better manage the merchandising on our site by informing us of what areas and product categories are in demand by our customers. Web beacons are tiny graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies, and are used to track the online movements of Web users. Similarly, the code gathers data through the use of a persistent cookie. Cookies assist us in tracking which of our features you like best. And when visitors re-visit our site, cookies can enable us to customize our content according to visitor preferences. However, both the Web beacons and cookies are not tied to users' personally identifiable information. These cookies expire once you leave the Mobovida, LLC (dba CellularOutfitter.com) site (including Mobovida, LLC (dba CellularOutfitter.com) third-party partner affiliate sites for cell phones and downloads).
