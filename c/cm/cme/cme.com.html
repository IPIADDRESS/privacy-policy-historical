CME Group’s Commitment to GDPR

The European Union (EU) General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) will become enforceable on May 25, 2018.  CME Group Inc. (CME) has taken steps to prepare for GDPR by improving its internal policies and processes.

What is the GDPR?

The GDPR is a comprehensive data protection law.  It will replace the different existing national implementations of the EU Data Protection Directive and will strengthen protection of an individual’s personal data.  The GDPR imposes enhanced requirements on companies when handling personal data, bolsters the rights of individuals with respect to their personal data, and carries significant potential penalties for breach 

Is CME preparing for the GDPR?

CME Group has taken many steps to implement appropriate changes into our practices by updating our documentation, processes, and procedures in preparation for GDPR.  We have identified areas of change for certain parts of our business and for certain suppliers.  

CME encourages you to visit CME’s privacy policy for an up-to-date understanding of why CME collects and uses your personal data and an outline of your rights under GDPR with respect to your personal data.  

Questions?

If you have questions about the CME’s preparation efforts, please contact us at privacy@cmegroup.com.  All questions and comments will be directed to the Chief Privacy Officer.
