2.   Definitions

a.   “Personal Information” is information relating to an identified or identifiable individual, such as:

·         Name,

·         User name,

·         Postal address (including billing and shipping addresses),

·         Telephone number (including home and mobile phone numbers),

·         Email address,

·         Credit and debit card number,

·         Profile picture,

·         Social media account ID (such as your Facebook username),

·         Location Information, if the Location Information are related to an identifiable individual.

·         Personal characteristics

·         Hobbies and interests

·         Consumption habits

An identifiable individual is one who can be identified, directly or indirectly, in particular by reference to an identification number or to one or more factors specific to his physical, physiological, mental, economic, cultural or social identity.

b.   “Sensitive Information” is Personal Information related to sensitive aspects such as:

·         racial or ethnic origin,

·         political opinions,

·         religion or other beliefs,

·         health or medical condition,

·         criminal background, or

·         or trade union membership

·         sexual orientation

We ask that you not send us, and you not disclose, any Sensitive Information on or through the Sites or otherwise to us.

c.   “Other Information” is any information that does not reveal your specific identity and does not allow a subsequent identification of the individual, such as:

·         Browser and device information

·         Server log file information

·         Information collected through cookies, pixel tags and other technologies, provided that a subsequent identification of the user is excluded

·         App usage data

·         Demographic information and other information provided by you

·         Aggregated information

d.   Other People’s Personal Information

You must not submit any Personal Information relating to other people to us or to our service providers in connection with the Sites.  Instead, you must ensure those other people submit their own Personal Information to us directly, including for example by using this Site alongside you.  If – despite this warning – you do submit Personal Information relating to other people to us through this Site - we will take this to mean that you represent that you have their prior authority and explicit consent to do so, including in order for us to use their Personal Information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
