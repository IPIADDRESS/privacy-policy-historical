Privacy Policy – The Coleman Company, Inc. and Affiliated entities web sites
 
1) General
The Coleman Company respects the privacy of every individual who visits our Corporate or affiliate web sites or responds to our guestbooks, contests, or interactive advertisements. This Privacy statement outlines the information Sunbeam will collect and how we will use that information. This Statement will inform you what to do if you do not want your Personal Information to be shared or divulged when you visit a Sunbeam web site or respond to our advertising.
 
2) Personally- Identifiable Information
We will not collect any Personal Information about you ( that is your name, address , telephone number, or email address) unless you provide it voluntarily.
If you do not want your Personal Information collected do not submit it to us. If you do submit Personal Information we will use it in the following ways, unless stated otherwise: we will store and process that information to better understand your needs, and to improve our products and services; and we may use that information to contact you. We will not distribute or sell your Personal Information to any outside company for marketing or solicitation and we will maintain the confidentiality of your Personal Information. It will only be used to support your relationship with The Coleman Company and its affiliated entities.
 
3) Children (under thirteen)
We have no intention of collecting any Personal Information (that is your name, address , telephone number, or email address) from individuals under thirteen (13) years of age. Where appropriate we will instruct children not to submit information to our web sites or advertisements without parental consent. If a child has provided us with Personal Information a parent or guardian can contact us at the e-mail address listed at the bottom of this statement if they want this information to be deleted from the records. We will use all reasonable efforts to delete the child’s information from our existing files.
 
4) Non- Personally – Identifiable Information Collected Automatically
In some cases, we may collect information about you that is not Personal Information. Examples of this type of information include the internet browser you are using, the type of operating system, and the domain name of the web site which linked you to our site. When you view one of our web sites we may store information from time to time on your computer. These "Cookies" allow us to tailor our web site or advertisement to better match your interests and preferences. With most Internet Browsers you can block, receive a warning or erase all "Cookies" from your hard drive. Please refer to your browser instructions or help screen to learn more about these functions.
If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy or would like to have any information deleted from our records please contact us at:
consumeraffairs@consumer.sunbeam.com.
