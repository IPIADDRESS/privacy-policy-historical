Privacy

No information is sold or released to anyone about visitors to colorgirlgames.com without prior consent or a court order. In other words, no information is released to anyone unless you give permission first.

colorgirlgames.com does not attempt to collect personal information from children. Note that colorgirlgames.com cannot always determine that a user is a child. colorgirlgames.com does not maintain databases of children or adults.

When visiting any Internet site, your unique address called an "IP address" is recorded. colorgirlgames.com does not release any information about the collection of this address to any third party except under court order. colorgirlgames.com archives the log files in order to create aggregate statistical reports, detect errors on the web site, and for security reasons. The log files are eventually deleted.

IP reports for advertisement clicks are shared with the advertiser. However, they usually have this information already since it is captured by their server when you view or click an ad. This is done to verify billing for the ads. No additional information associated with any specific user is provided to the advertisers.

Overview

Your privacy is important to us. colorgirlgames.com's service is free for all to use, and prior registration is not needed.

colorgirlgames.com will explicitly ask when it needs information that personally identifies you. All personal identifiable information colorgirlgames.com collects are only gathered with your voluntary act. colorgirlgames.com keeps all information private and does not, and will not, share it with any third parties except under these circumstances:

- When such action is necessary to conform to legal requirements or comply with legal process.

- When such action is necessary to protect and defend the rights or property of colorgirlgames.com.

colorgirlgames.com uses cookies as part of tracking process for advertisement banners so you do not see the same ad repeatedly, but cookies will not be used to personally identify you without your prior consent.

colorgirlgames.com uses subscription forms for individuals to subscribe to its ezine . colorgirlgames.com will use your email address to provide you with the ezine, which is usually mailed daily. colorgirlgames.com will never send you any unsolicited email outside of the ezine.

colorgirlgames.com does not and will not share, sell or rent your email address to outside third parties. Neither does it supplement your information with any outside third parties. The contents of colorgirlgames.com's mailing list will only be disclosed due to law or court demands.

Unsubscribing from colorgirlgames.com's ezine is simple and can be done by following the instructions at the end of every email sent.
