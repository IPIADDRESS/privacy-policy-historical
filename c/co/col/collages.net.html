Collages.net is committed to the protection of your privacy. This Privacy Statement outlines how Collages.net gathers and disseminates information that we obtain from you.

Visitors: Collages.net only uses email addresses provided by you or your photographer to notify you or your guests that your event site is active and to report the status of your website. Collages.net keeps your IP address confidential and uses it only to help diagnose problems with our server, to generate internal statistics, and to administer the Collages.net website.

Purchasers: Collages.net sells reprints and related products online. At the time of purchase, we require personal and financial information such as name, email, shipping/billing address, event code and credit card number. Collages.net keeps this information strictly confidential. We will use your name and contact information only as follows:

1. Your contact information is sent to the photographer who will fulfill the order.

2. We will occasionally send you information about Collages.net (you have the option of removing yourself from our list by following the simple directions in the opt-out section below).

3. Credit card information is only used to charge visitors for reprints and other products purchased from Collages.net. Only our order processing/customer service staff has access to your information. We do not store or access credit card information from our server after your order is processed.

Links to other sites: Our site contains links to other sites and email addresses. If you click through to these other sites, Collages.net will not forward any information it has received from you to the next site. However, Collages.net is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of other such Web sites.

Security: Collages.net transmits your order information using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology to protect your online order. We use a firewall and password protection of each event site to prevent the loss, misuse and alteration of information that we control. All of our computer equipment is in a secured environment and archived information is also kept in a secured location.

Opt-in/Opt-out: Colleges.net provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us at any time. If you do not want to receive email from us in the future, please contact webmaster@collages.net.

Contacting Collages.net: If you have any questions about this privacy statement, please contact webmaster@collages.net.
