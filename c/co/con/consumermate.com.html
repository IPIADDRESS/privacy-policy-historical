Our commitment to privacy

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect this privacy we provide notices explaining our online information practices.

On some pages you can make request to receive information on products or services or register to become a regular member of the website. Information collected on these pages include: 

  • Name

In general, the system allows us to automatically gather information that does not identify you personally, like the date and time of your visit and the pages you visit. It also allows us gather information on the type of browser you are using (like Netscape, Internet Explorer), the type of Operating System you are using (like Windows '98 or Mac OS) and the domain name of your Internet Service Provider (like VSNL, Airtel, Satyam Online). We sometimes use this non-personally identifiable information to improve our website design, content and primarily to give you a better browsing experience.

The information you provide is not given or sold to any private organisations or private persons without your advance permission or unless ordered by a court of law.
