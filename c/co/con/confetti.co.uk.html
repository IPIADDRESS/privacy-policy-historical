privacy policy

Because your privacy is important to us, confetti.co.uk and Confett Network Ltd operates by these principles:
• We explicitly ask when we need information that personally identifies you ("Personal Information"). For example, if you wish to register as a member of Confetti.co.uk, we ask you for your name and email address. We provide your Personal Information to necessary third-party merchants when you purchase services through Confetti.co.uk. We also use your Personal Information to operate Confetti.co.uk and, occasionally, to inform you of new features, services, and products from Confetti Network Ltd.
• Confetti Network Ltd may carefully select other companies to send you information. If you wish to discontinue receiving such offers from Confetti Network Ltd on behalf of suppliers or directly from other suppliers, you may opt out by indicating on the member registration page that you do not desire to obtain any promotional material.
• To make sure that your Personal Information for your member registration is correct and current, you may review and update your customer information at any time.

If at any time you believe that Confetti.co.uk has not adhered to these principles, please notify us by email to help@confetti.co.uk, and we will use all commercially reasonable efforts to determine and correct the problem promptly. Confetti Network Ltd is registered under the Data Protection Act.
