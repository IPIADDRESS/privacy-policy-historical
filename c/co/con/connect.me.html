Incorporation of the Respect Trust Framework. This Privacy Policy incorporates by reference the Respect Trust Framework listed with the Open Identity Exchange at http://openidentityexchange.org/trust-frameworks/respect-trust-framework

The purpose of the Respect Trust Framework is to define a simple set of principles and rules to which all Members of a digital trust network agree so that they may share identity and personal data with greater confidence that it will be protected and only used as authorized. Connect.Me and all Members of the Respect Network agree to operate under the Respect Trust Framework. A one-page summary of the five Respect Principles (Promise, Permission, Protection, Portability, Proof) at the heart of the Respect Trust Framework is available at http://connect.me/trust.

All capitalized terms that are not otherwise defined in this document are defined in the Respect Trust Framework. Note that the Respect Trust Framework also sets forth the terms of the Respect Reputation System.

Global and Local Terms

The purpose, principles, and rules of the Respect Trust Framework comprise the Global Terms. This Privacy Policy together with the Connect.Me Terms of Service comprises the Local Terms that apply specifically to your relationship with Connect.Me. The Respect Trust Framework requires that any Local Terms (such as these Connect.Me Local Terms) not conflict with the Global Terms.

Scope

This Privacy Policy describes Connect.Me's policies and procedures relating to the collection, processing, use, and disclosure of:

  • Information you supply at registration: information from and about you that you provide to Connect.Me when you create a Member Account.
  • Information you supply after registration. This includes Profile data, Tag and Context data, Vouching and Complaint data, and Community membership and relationship data.
  • Information others tell us about you. This includes Tag and Context data, Vouching and Complaint data, and Community membership and relationship data asserted about you by other Members.
  • Information we observe about you from your activity on Connect.Me.
  • Other information that we generate or receive about you and your activity under these terms.

Note on limited data collection. Connect.Me only has access to the information listed above and such other information as it requires to operate the Respect Network. Connect.Me does not have access to information about you that is held by third parties or by other Members, including Organizational Members, unless you make such information available. For example, when you connect one of your social networking accounts to Connect.Me, we only have access to your registration information, social graph information, and other information that you give us permission to collect on your behalf. Connect.Me is not responsible for any information held by such other businesses, whether or not they are Members.

Where do we get information about you? Connect.Me receives information from and about you through our own website, email and SMS interfaces, APIs, and from the social networking services or other third party services to which you give us explicit permission to access personal data about you on your behalf. Unless you have consented otherwise, we do not collect other information about you from any third party sources.

When using any Connect.Me Services, you consent to the collection, transfer, correlation, storage, disclosure, disposal and other uses of information from and about you as described in this Privacy Policy.

Whatever your country of residence or the location from which you create information, data and information from and about you may be used by Connect.Me in the United States or any other country where Connect.Me operates. Please note that such countries may be outside of the EEA, in which case the Directive 95/46/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council of 24 October 1995 on the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data will not be applicable.

Information Collection and Use

The purpose of Connect.Me and the Respect Network is to provide you with personal discovery, reputation, data sharing, and other personal networking services (Services). These Services provide you with greater control when you store, manage, and exchange Member Data with other Members of the Respect Network and members of other social networking services.

This Member Data falls into two categories: data from and about you that is collected, processed, and used by Connect.Me on your behalf; and data from and about you that you collect and manage using a third-party Personal Data Service Provider (PDS Provider). Connect.Me will only interchange data with PDS Providers who are Members of the Respect Network and therefore operate subject to the Respect Trust Framework.

Only Member Data that is collected and used by Connect.Me is subject to this Privacy Policy. The second category of Member Data you collect and manage solely with your PDS Provider is subject to the Privacy Policy and any other Local Terms you have with your PDS Provider.

Connect.Me uses Member Data to provide our Services and improve them over time. As provided by the Promise Principle of the Respect Trust Framework, Connect.Me promises to respect your control over any Connect.Me Member Data. Connect.Me only collects the Member Data described in this privacy policy, and only uses it for the purposes set forth in this policy pursuant to the permission you give us when you register with Connect.Me.

Information Collected at Registration: When you create or revise a Member Account with Connect.Me, you provide some information, such as your name, username, password, and email address. Some of this information you provide, for example your name and username, is made available to the public through our Services, such as on a Profile page describing you, your membership, and relationships (Profile Page), as well as in search results.

Access to some Services, such as viewing Profile Pages or search results on the Connect.Me website, does not require registration. Access to these Services is governed by the Connect.Me Terms of Service. The only information collected about non-registered users is described in Log Data, below.

Information Collected from Third-Party Sources: When you configure a Member Account with Connect.Me, you give Connect.Me permission to connect to third-party sources, such as social networking services with which you have an account, to access information about you collected by those third-party sources, such as your social graph information.Ü The current listing of the types of social graph information that we access is set forth at http://connect.me/c/verification.

By giving such permission, you confirm that such information can be shared with Connect.Me pursuant to the terms of service of such third-party sources and applicable law. Connect.Me will not access any information from third party sources for which you have not granted permission, confirmed access authorization, or otherwise consented to Connect.MeÌs collection, processing, and use of such information.

Additional Information: You have the option to provide additional Member Data to include on your Profile Page or share with other Members. You control whether this Member Data is available publicly or its access is restricted. This information may include your photo or avatar, location, mobile phone number or other personal contact information, self-description, and the Tags, Contexts, and Communities you wish to reference to describe you.

In keeping with the Permission Principle of the Respect Trust Framework, we may only use your contact information to send you information about our Services to the extent you have given us permission to do so. You may always adjust your permissions and preferences for the communications you will receive from Connect.Me via your Connect.Me Dashboard.

If you email us, we may retain your message, email address and contact information so that we can respond to your message. Providing information other than required registration information is entirely optional.

Public Information: Our Services are primarily designed to help you and other Members by providing you with a means to create, view, manage, and share your contextual Profile, your Reputation Graph, and other relevant Member Data with other Members or with the public.

Certain of the Member Data about you is required to appear on your publicly available Profile Page in order to comply with the Proof Principle of the Respect Trust Framework. This information that you are consenting to be made public includes your name and username, your photo or avatar (if you provide it), and your Reputation Graph data that is generated by your interaction with the Services and with other Members, including your Tags, Contexts, Communities, Vouches made and received, Complaints made and received, and Trust Level. You should expect that these portions of your Member Data will be broadly and instantly disseminated, so you should exercise caution and care in providing it.

In keeping with the Promise Principle of the Respect Trust Framework, Connect.Me shall provide you with a means to exercise greater control over whether any other Member Data is available to the public or its access is restricted. Such controls will be available through your Connect.Me Dashboard.

Location Information: You have the ability to share the city and country with which you would like to be associated. You may control whether this information is available publicly via your Profile Page or other Connect.Me interface, or its access is restricted.

Log Data: Connect.Me servers automatically record information ("Log Data") created by the Connect.Me systems based on the history of your use of the Services. Log Data may include information such as your IP address, browser type, referring domain, and pages visited. Other actions associated with you, such as your interactions within Connect.Me may also be included in Log Data.

Links: Connect.Me may keep track of how you interact with links across our Services including services offered by third parties by various means. We keep track of this activity to help improve our Services and to detect suspicious or malicious activity.

Cookies: We use "cookie" technology (see description below) to collect additional website usage data and to improve our Services, but may not require cookies for many parts of our Services such as looking at public Profile Pages or searches.

A cookie is a small data file that is transferred to the hard drive of your computer or your other devices. Connect.Me may use both session cookies and persistent cookies to better understand how you interact with our Services, to monitor web traffic that is routed to our Services and aggregate usage by our Members, and to improve our Services. Most Internet browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can edit your options to instruct your browser to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the websites you visit.

Third Party Services: Connect.Me uses a variety of services hosted by third parties to help provide our Services, such as hosting our various websites and providing analytics. We use some of the services to help us better understand the use of our Services. Among the information collected by these services are posts, comments, interactions, reputation data, third-party cookies, and profile information.
