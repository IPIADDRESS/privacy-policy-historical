Privacy Policy

Protecting your information:

Please do not send private information via email since it is not considered a secure method of communication. Some of the information you enter on our site is protected by Secure Sockets Layer (SSL). Pages with this technology have URLs that start with HTTPS instead of HTTP.

The types of information we collect:

Any correspondence you have with us including your registration information may be retained with your contact information including your email address.

We use a technology called cookies, which is a text document that includes anonymous unique identifiers. We also use server logs to collect information on the actions our users take on our website. We may also place pixels to track advertisement performance.

How we use the information:

Cookies is one of the ways we use the information above to make decisions on how to improve our services and your experience on our website(s). We use cookies to display relevant content for your particular visit, and to better understand visitor activity.

To help you get information about products or services that you may be interested in, we may provide your information including your name, address, phone number and email. Your information may be shared for other purposes as well. If we are required to do so by law or by subpoena, we may disclose your personal information.
