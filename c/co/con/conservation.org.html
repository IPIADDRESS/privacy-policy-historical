Privacy Statement

Thank you for visiting Conservation International's (CI) website. Visitors to www.conservation.org are guaranteed their right to privacy. Information collected on www.conservation.org is kept private and never shared with other organizations. We've created this privacy policy to inform you about the types of information gathered and disseminated on this website.

Measuring Audiences
Your IP (Internet Protocol) address is used to gather broad demographic information. CI logs IP addresses and browser types for systems administration purposes. These logs are analyzed to constantly improve the value of the materials available on the website. Your IP address also helps us diagnose problems with our server, and administer to the website. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information. This means your session will be tracked, but you will remain anonymous to us.

Outside Links
This site contains links sites outside www.conservation.org. CI is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

Affiliated Sites
Websites directly affiliated with www.conservation.org include:

  • Investigate Biodiversity, www.conservation.org/investigate
  • Center for Applied Biodiversity Science, www.cabs.conservation.org
  • Defying Nature's End (Conference site), www.defyingnaturesend.org *
  • Critical Ecosystems Partnership Fund, www.cepf.net

CI guarantees that information submitted to these sites is never shared with any other organization.

* Visitors to Defying Nature's End Conference site may subscribe to a listserv offering email notification on site updates. Users may subscribe and unsubscribe automatically at: www.defyingnaturesend.org/forms/email_signup.html

Submitting Personal Information
You can submit information to www.conservation.org in several places on the site. We use a feedback and request form for customers to request information and we collect visitor's contact information, such as their email address and/or mailing address. Contact information from the feedback form is used to send responses or information requested to our customers. This information is never shared and used only to reply.

Online membership forms and product order forms may require financial information (such as credit card numbers) as well as a mailing addresses from new members. These forms reside on secure servers maintained by outside vendors and the information is kept confidential. Contact information from the order form is used to reply to our customers as needed and is not shared with outside organizations.

Site Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.

Children's Guidelines
None of the websites covered by this policy knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under age 13. If we discover that a person under the age of 13 has provided us with any personally identifiable information, we will delete that information from our systems. We are unable to reply to information requests from children under the age of 13, if so identified. A parent or guardian must initiate any requests for information for children under the age of 13 on their behalf. We encourage parents to supervise children when they browse the Internet.

You Have a Choice
CI will not send you any communications that you do not explicitly request or initiate.
