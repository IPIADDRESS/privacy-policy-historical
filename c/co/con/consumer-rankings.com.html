Privacy Policy
We respect your privacy and our goal is to maintain your trust when handling your personal information. This Privacy Policy is part of our Terms of Use and it is intended to inform you, the end user, about how we handle this information.

Use of Web tracking and information collection technologies


In order to monitor and improve the experience of our website and services, we may use several in-house and third-party tracking technologies and tools, such as cookies, "clear GIFs", Web beacons, and the analysis of log files. Some of our Site pages utilize "cookies" and other tracking technologies. A "cookie" is a small text file is put on your computer hard drive that may be used, for example, to collect information about site activity. Some cookies and other technologies may serve to recall personal information previously indicated by a user and correlate the browsing data with other collected information. Most browsers allow you to control cookies, including whether or not to accept them and how to remove them. Furthermore, you may set most browsers to notify you if you receive a cookie, or you may choose to block cookies with your browser. Please note, however, that if you choose to erase or block your cookies, your usage experience might be affected and there might be a chance that the website will not be functional for you.

We, our partners and customers may use the information we collect to:
  • Improve or make change your future experience in the website, such as make it more personalized, alter the user interface or offer you different choices and options
  • Build and improve our database and knowledge base regarding the preferences, choices and behavior of people from your profile
  • Compile anonymous statistical information for analysis
  • Provide aggregated or unidentifiable anonymous information to third parties
  • Contact you regarding administrative issues
  • Make telephone calls to you with information and offers
  • Mail information and offers to you
  • Email information and offers to you
Information Available to Third Party Providers and Vendors We may use third parties to provide various technical and operational services to us, such as (but not limited to) hosting, email distribution and database management. All the collected information may be accessible to these third parties, in order to let them provide the necessary functionality.

Supplementing and Correlating Your Information with Information from Other Sources


We may combine the Information you provided us with third-party data. Here are some typical scenarios for such integration:
  • Correlate your IP address with your geographic location using an external database.
  • Receiving and processing information about your behavior and actions in other websites. For example, when you click a link from our website to another website, the other website may inform us that you made an action (such as purchase) as result of that link, and we may store and use that information.
  • Getting additional information about you from other sources. For example, when you provide us with your name and address we may verify that they match, based on external databases and information sources.

We are not responsible for any actions, content of privacy policies and practices of such third parties, which may be different than ours. Therefore, you should be aware that there is a risk that such information will be accessible by unauthorized third parties.
