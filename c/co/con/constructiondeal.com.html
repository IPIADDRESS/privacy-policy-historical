Privacy Policy

At ConstructionDeal.com, we recognize how important privacy is to our users. We guarantee we will not violate that privacy. We respect your right to privacy and do not share your information with anyone without your consent. Our goal is to protect and secure any information you provide to us and we will not disclose or sell that information to any third parties.

This is our privacy policy statement and we hope it will help you make an informed decision about sharing your personal information with us.

 
FOR OUR PROJECT OWNERS:

Personal information and other data collected:

  • ConstructionDeal.com collects personal information when property owners register for our service and voluntarily provide such information.
  • The personal information will be held securely in accordance with our internal security policy and the law.

The Type of Information We Need from our Project Owners:

  • Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address

How the Information is Utilized to Help You Complete Your Project:

  • We use the information you provide about yourself and your project, when registering with us, only to complete our service. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete that process.
  • We email you the information on each and every service provider who previewed your project details and requested your contact information.
  • Finally, we never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described above.

Professional Information and Other Data Collected:

  • ConstructionDeal.com collects professional information when service providers register or subscribe to our service and voluntarily provide such information.
  • Your professional information will be held securely in accordance with our internal security policy and the law.

The Type of Information We Collect from our Service Providers:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Credit Card Information

How the Information is Utilized to Help You Find Prospects:

  • We use the information you provide about yourself and your company, when registering or subscribing with us, only to complete our service. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete that process.

  • Finally, we never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described above.

Security

  • We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure we have put in place suitable physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
