ConversationExchange.com Privacy Policy

Date of Last Revision: 13 March 2012

At ConversationExchange.com we are committed to protecting the privacy of our members. We only ask for personal information that is necessary to provide the services of this site to our members. Personal information regarding any individual will never be given to outside parties. Your email address is not published on our web site and it is never disclosed to third parties or to other members of the site. Exchange of messages between members is always done via the web site. We don't send any news letters or promotional information from third parties. If we implement a newsletter feature in the future, then members' account will be set, by default, to not receive our regular news letter.

Account information

All the information you have entered in the registration form can be seen and searched by other users of the Site, except for your email address and password. Your date of birth cannot be seen, but it is used to display your age.
If you no longer wish your profile to be displayed on our Site, you can delete your account at any time.
