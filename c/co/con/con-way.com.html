Privacy Policy

We’ve created this privacy statement to show our firm commitment to your privacy. This statement talks about our information-gathering and dissemination practices for this site. By using this site you affirmatively consent to the provisions herein.

Information Gathered About You and How it is Used

Con-way doesn’t share information collected about you with other organizations. We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is used to help identify you, and to gather broad demographic information. We do offer extra services that do require registration. This registration form requires you to give us contact information. We use this customer contact data to send you information and updates about our company. This contact information may also be used to contact you when otherwise necessary.

The following information is collected during registration for services on our site:

  • Name
  • Job title
  • Company name
  • Company address
  • Work phone number
  • E-mail address
  • Business location information

Online Surveys/Information Updates

We occasionally use online surveys. Our online survey asks visitors for contact information (such as their e-mail addresses). The visitor’s contact information may be used for contact when necessary. We also periodically send informational and promotional updates to our registered users regarding Con-way services, and by using this site you consent to receive such e-mail.

Delete/Deactivate

This site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database: You can send e-mail to info@Con-way.com.

Change/Modify

This site gives users the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided:

  • E-mail us at info@Con-way.com.
  • Use our change form: select “Update Profile” from the Registration menu on the main page.
