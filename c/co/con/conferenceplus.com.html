Privacy Policy

Conference Plus, Inc. ("ConferencePlus") wants you to know that it respects your right to privacy in connection with personal information you may provide to us as a visitor to or registered with the ConferencePlus.com web site (the "Site"). Your use of the Site implies your agreement with the provisions of this Privacy Statement. If you do not agree with the ConferencePlus Privacy Statement, please do not use the Site. Please note that your use of the Site and services provided on the Site is subject to ConferencePlus' Terms and Conditions of Use.

INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE
When you become a registered conferenceplus.com user, you provide your name, title, address, phone number, email address and time zone. By providing us with this information, you consent to ConferencePlus' collection and use of it, as described in this Privacy Statement. This information is used to provide personal identification, to complete product purchases, to allow us to contact you for Customer Service purposes should such services be needed and to notify you about product improvements. If you are a user, you may choose to provide ConferencePlus with credit card information for the purpose of purchasing ConferencePlus' products online. This allows ConferencePlus to process your transaction and deliver the products to you. From time to time, ConferencePlus will sponsor contests to give the user the opportunity to win appealing prizes. The information that is collected by ConferencePlus for such contests may include contact information and survey questions. This information is used to notify the contest winners and the survey information is used to improve products or the Site. If you use the Site to apply for employment at ConferencePlus, the information you provide will be evaluated in connection with employment opportunities that may exist, and not for any other purpose. Cookies are stored to share session information. IP address are also collected by the web server.

SHARING YOUR INFORMATION
ConferencePlus shares your personal information with only a very limited number of third parties, and only to provide products or services to you or to provide you with additional information about ConferencePlus. Your personal information is not and will not be sold for any purpose to any other parties.

OTHER USES OF INFORMATION
Government Order - On occasion, ConferencePlus may find it necessary to disclose some of your private information if required to do so by law, such as by a court order or at the formal request of a governmental agency or law enforcement agency. ConferencePlus may also disclose information in the event that legal action is required against someone who may be creating interference with the Site. Business Transfers - We may buy or sell operating divisions, companies or other business assets or be acquired by another company. In such transactions, customer information generally is one of the transferred business assets.

ACCESSING AND UPDATING YOUR INFORMATION
You may access the information ConferencePlus has collected about you by sending a request via e-mail to info@conferenceplus.com; or by U.S. Mail to the Webmaster, Conference Plus, Inc., 1051 East Woodfield Road, Schaumburg, Illinois 60173. We may need to protect your privacy by verifying your identity before granting access to your information or making any corrections.

LINKS
The Site may contain links to third party web sites ("Linked Sites") that may also collect personal information about you. This Privacy Statement does not cover the practices of those Linked Sites. However, we encourage you to read the Privacy Statements of those Linked Sites.

SECURITY MEASURES
ConferencePlus has put in place appropriate procedures to safeguard and secure the information you provide. ConferencePlus uses Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology, the current standard for secure transactions over the Internet. There are some simple measures that you can take to promote protection of your information. First, to help protect the security of your account, do not share your password with others. Also, remember to "log-off" after using any of our online services when you have finished using the web site. While ConferencePlus strives to protect your information by using SSL and through measures to protect its system, such security cannot be ensured. Consequently, ConferencePlus does not insure or warrant the security of information you transmit, and you do so at your own risk.

CHANGES TO PRIVACY STATEMENT
In the event that ConferencePlus' Privacy Statement should change, new versions will appear on this web site that incorporates any changes. Your continued use of the Site implies that you have accepted those changes in the Privacy Statement. If you have any questions concerning this Policy Statement, please write to info@conferenceplus.com.
