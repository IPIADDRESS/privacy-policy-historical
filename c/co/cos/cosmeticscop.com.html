CosmeticsCop.com or its service providers may use some of the following technologies to automatically collect Information about your activities on our Site.

Cookies
Cookies are small files placed on the hard drive of your computer. You may refuse to accept browser cookies by activating the appropriate setting on your browser. However, if you refuse cookies you may be unable to access certain parts of our Site. Unless you have adjusted your browser setting so that it will refuse cookies, our system will issue cookies when you direct your browser to our Site. Depending on which browser and browser version and device you are using, your direction to refuse cookies may not be implemented.

Flash Cookies
Flash Cookies (also called local stored objects) collect and store information about your preferences and navigation to, from, and on this Site.

Web Beacons, Clear Pixels, or Pixel Tags
Web beacons are transparent image files used to monitor your journey around the Site and can help us understand how you interact with the Site.

Web server logs
Web server logs are a historical record of your activity in connection with your use of the Site. For example, a web server log may record the search term you used that directed you to CosmeticsCop.com. It may also collect information about the browser you use when visiting this Site, and other metadata related to your device.

Geo-location technologies
Geo-location technologies, including your IP address, may help determine your general location to better understand what type of content should be added to Cosmeticscop.com.
