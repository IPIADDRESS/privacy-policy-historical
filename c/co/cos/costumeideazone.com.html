USER INFORMATION DISPLAYED ON THE WEB SITE

Users of the Costume Idea Zone Web site can submit costume ideas by going to the Submissions Page. Submissions are added to the site at the discretion of AmazingFX, and will be edited as deemed necessary by the AmazingFX staff. Submissions that are listed on the site will include the first and/or last name of the submitter, if name(s) are provided by submitter. If a name is not provided, the idea will be posted without attribution. No email addresses or other personal information will be displayed. Users who do not wish to receive attribution for costume ideas should say so in their email message.
Users who have a currently listed costume suggestion, and wish to change or remove the attribution information, should send email to: customerservice@costumeideazone.com.

CONTACTING THE WEB SITE

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, please contact the Webmaster at: customerservice@costumeideazone.com.
