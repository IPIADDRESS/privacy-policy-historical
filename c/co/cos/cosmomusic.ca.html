Privacy Policy

Cosmo Music is proud to be a part of the internet community that does not promote the use of spamming and selling or renting out customer lists and information. Cosmo Music will not sell, rent, give away or disclose our customer information to other companies or third parties. All information submitted on this website will never be released to other companies. Cosmo Music supports and adheres to Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL).

Privacy Statement

Cosmo Music Co. Ltd has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website (cosmomusic.ca):

  • We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our website.
  • Our site uses cookies to keep track of your shopping cart.
  • Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information (like their name and email address) which is used to process orders and contact the visitor when necessary.
  • This site contains links to other external sites; Cosmo Music Co. Ltd is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content for these websites.
  • Our site uses order forms for customers to request information, products and services. We collect visitor's financial information (like their account or credit card numbers); information that is collected is used to bill the user for products and services rendered.

Security Guarantee

This website uses SSL technology to guarantee the safety of all information submitted on our site. Cosmo Music refuses to sell any information to any outside company. All information submitted on this site will remain confidential.

Our Core Values

Service

No other place in Canada provides such a large variety of services all under one roof! Sales, rentals and repairs of musical instruments, equipment and accessories as well as lessons, event production, on-site setup, consulting and school/institutional sales and support. We even offer camps, music hall and facility rentals and have a cafe in our atrium. Just ask, we’re sure we can help!

Expertise

We're a family business that has been growing since 1968; many of us have been a part of that family since our humble beginnings. Our sales and service teams' years of in-depth knowledge is unmatched - experts or accomplished musicians in their respective fields.

Commitment

We want to ensure that you are satisfied with every purchase – we make it part of our daily tasks by performing Quality Assurance inspections on instruments before they are made available to buy or rent. We also offer a Best Price Guarantee and 100% Satisfaction Guarantee.
