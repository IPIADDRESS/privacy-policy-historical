Depending on how you use COSME-DE.COM, we may ask you to share personally identifiable information with us to provide our services, and to administer our business.

  • When browsing at COSME-DE.COM
    COSME-DE.COM use "cookies" to collect non-personally identifiable information, such as your IP address, browser type, domain name, and the specific COSME-DE.COM pages that you visit.

    Cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to your computer's hard drive for record-keeping purposes. We use cookies to track your preferences and purchases as you navigate through our website and to save you from having to re-enter information every time you visit COSME-DE.COM. By using cookies to track your visits, we can make your next shopping experience at COSME-DE.COM better.

Most web browsers allow you to exercise control over cookies by deleting them, blocking them, or alerting you when a cookie file is stored. Please review your browser's instructions on these functions. If you choose to disable the cookies feature of your web browser, you may not be able to place an order or otherwise use some or all of the features provided at COSME-DE.COM.

  • When registering at COSME-DE.COM
    COSME-DE.COM asks you to provide personally identifiable information, such as your full name and email address. Collecting this information allows us to enhance your overall shopping experience - a more personalized experience.

    You can also subscribe for Newsletter updates alone without prior register by providing your email address through our site services.

  • When ordering at COSME-DE.COM
    COSME-DE.COM asks you to provide personally identifiable information to us when you place an order with COSME-DE.COM. We ask for your email address, shipping address and phone number of the shipping destination, your billing address, your credit card number, and your credit card expiration date. Collecting this information allows us to complete your order efficiently, to notify you of your order status, to provide personalized interaction, and to use for marketing purposes if needed.

    COSME-DE.COM asks you to provide personally identifiable information to us when you contacting us. We ask for your name, email address, your located country/region and phone number. Collecting this information allows us to provide you a more personalized assistance from our customer care department.
