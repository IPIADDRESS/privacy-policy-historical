A property will cease being advertised when we are instructed to do so by our members. However, property information will remain available on our website users to benefit from services such as identifying previous for sale or sold prices.

 

Data provided to us by phone, e-mail or otherwise will be retained for the later of 18 months from the date of communication or until your query is resolved.

COOKIES AND TRACKING

 

Like many websites, we use “cookies” to enable us to personalise your visits to our Site, simplify the signing-in procedure, keep track of your preferences and to track the usage of our website.

 

When you visit costadelhome.com (including this website, mobile site and other websites with the costadelhome.com header and/or footer), and the sites of our advertising partners (which may include third party websites), we may place one or more cookies on your computer in order to recognise your computer each time you visit these sites and/or are displayed advertising. These cookies allow us to collect certain information about your web browsing behavior in order to select what advertisements to display to you and to determine the effectiveness of the advertisements. We use the information collected from your web browsing behavior to make assumptions about your characteristics and the things you are interested in, and then display targeted advertising that we believe may be of interest to you. For example, we may select the advertisements we display based on certain interest categories that we have inferred based on:

 

  • your online activity on costadelhome.com site and our advertising partners’ websites, including pages you view and links you click;
  • the search terms you enter using search engines and services, including on costadelhome.com; and
  • demographic or interest data, for example information that you have provided when creating an online account with us or one of our advertising partner websites (e.g. age, post code, gender).

 

We also allow other ad serving networks to display advertisements the costadelhome.com site. In these instances code in our web pages direct our ad-serving platform to call the advertisement from one of our ad serving networks. The companies in this network may also participate in behavioral advertising. We do not marry any information collected via cookies with any of your personal information for the purposes of behavioral advertising. The cookies used by us for behavioral advertising have an expiry date of no more than 2 years.

 

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on costadelhome.com.
