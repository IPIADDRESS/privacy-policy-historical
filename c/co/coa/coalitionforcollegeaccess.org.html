Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to information collected by www.coalitionforcollegeaccess.org, the web site of The Coalition for Access, Affordability, and Success, and will notify you of the following:

  • What personal information is collected from you through the web site, how it is used and with whom it may be shared.
  • Your access to and control over the information you provide.
  • The security measures used by the Coalition to protect your information.
  • What other privacy policies apply when you interact with links to other websites.

Information Collection, Use, and Sharing

The Coalition only has access to the information that you voluntarily provide via email through the web site. We will not sell or rent this information to anyone. We will use your information to respond to you regarding the reason you contacted us. We will not share your information with any third party outside of our organization other than as necessary to fulfill your request or answer your question.

Your Access to and Control Over Information

You may contact the Coalition via the email address on our website to request any of the following: (i) access to personal information you have previously provided to us, if any (ii) make changes or corrections to any such information; (iii) delete any such information; and (iv) express any concern you have about our use of your information.

Security

We take appropriate precautions to protect the information you provide on our website. We do not collect sensitive information on coaltionforcollegeaccess.org.

Other Websites

The platform described on this site and offered through www.mycoalition.org is provided by the Coalition's third party technology service provider, CollegeNET. Please be aware that with respect to www.mycoalition.org, the Coalition is not responsible for any privacy or security practices, and that CollegeNET’s privacy and security policies exclusively apply. You may access these CollegeNet policies at: corp.collegenet.com/privacy.html.

The Coalition will not use, and does not have access to, any personal information you provide through www.mycoalition.org. However, CollegeNET may provide, from time to time, aggregated and de-identified metrics and other data regarding www.mycoalition.org users and/or services. These data will be used by the Coalition solely to assess and improve upon its activities and efforts aimed at increasing student access to information about colleges and improving the college application process.

This web site contains links to sites other than www.mycoalition.org. Please be aware that the Coalition is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these other sites either. We encourage you to be aware of when you leave our site and to read the privacy statements of any other site that collects personal information.

Updates

Our privacy policy may change from time to time and all updates will be posted on this page.

If you have questions or concerns regarding our privacy policy or practices, please contact us at info@coalitionapp.org.
