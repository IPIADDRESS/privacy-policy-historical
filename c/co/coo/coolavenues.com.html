CoolAvenues.com Privacy Policy

The policies stated below are applicable to www.coolavenues.com. CoolAvenues' site is maintained by Zebra Networks, India. We can be reached via e-mail at editor@coolavenues.com

 

CoolAvenues respects each individual user's right to privacy. Cool Avenues may place a "cookie" on the visitor's hard drive in order to facilitate a user friendly experience. Cookies are small pieces of information that a website places on your computer's hard drive for tracking purposes. CoolAvenues uses cookie information it receives by placing the cookie on user's hard drive to improve the efficiency of customer service on our online subscription-based sites, allowing subscribers to easily change addresses, and add or cancel subscriptions by automatically verifying that you are a subscriber.

 

CoolAvenues also uses cookies to enhance the site content by compiling aggregate information about what specific sections users visit more and which pages are more popular. Cookies may also be placed by our advertisers or independent third parties who actually operate the servers on which the ads reside. Although, CoolAvenues encourages third parties to adhere to appropriate privacy standards and policies, CoolAvenues is not responsible for the actions or policies of such parties.

 

CoolAvenues collects personal identifying information, when such information is specifically and knowingly provided, from users during -

(i) contests, surveys, sweep-stakes or site registration promotions, and
(ii) subscription registrations for sites or services requiring such registration information.

 

CoolAvenues may use this information for marketing and promotional purposes. CoolAvenues, occasionally, likes to inform users of special deals, new products, and other third party product and service offers that we believe our users will find interesting. Individual users can request, at any time, that their information not be used for such purposes by sending an e-mail request to CoolAvenues at the address given above.

 

If a CoolAvenues user knowingly provides personal information such as a username or e-mail address in a chat or bulletin board area, that user should be aware that the information provided can be collected and used by others in that area. No information should be submitted to CoolAvenues by children 16 years of age or under without their parent's or guardian's consent. CoolAvenues does not monitor the age of its user audience.

 

CoolAvenues uses third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit the website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, e-mail address, or telephone number) about your visits to CoolAvenues and its associated websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
