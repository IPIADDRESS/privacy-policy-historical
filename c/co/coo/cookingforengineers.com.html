How We Collect and Use Information

Analytics information. Cooking For Engineers uses third-party analytics tools to help measure traffic and usage trends. These tools collect information sent normally by your browser as part of a web page request, including the pages you visit, your browser information, and other information that may help us improve Cooking For Engineers. We use this analytics information in aggregate form such that it cannot reasonably be manipulated to identify any particular individual user.

Cookies. Like nearly all but the simplest websites, Cooking For Engineers uses cookies - a small piece of text data stored on your browser. A cookie is used by Cooking For Engineers to distinguish between different users (for example, cookies are what allows users to log into websites) and occassionally store state information (for example, changing a setting on the website may result in a cookie being saved to your browser to remember the new setting). Advertisers on Cooking For Engineers may also use their own cookies to help them keep track of different users and pages visited on Cooking For Engineers. Cookie settings can be altered in your own browser's settings.
