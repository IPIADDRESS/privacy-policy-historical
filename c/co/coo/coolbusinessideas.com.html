The basis of our privacy statement 

We will not send you unsolicited e-mail (known as SPAM or UCE). We will only send e-mails to those who have affirmatively requested them through our opt-in process. We will never sell or give away your personal information to third parties for purposes of building contact lists, or anything of that nature, without your permission. CoolBusinessIdeas.com stands firmly against any transfer of personal information for reasons of building the contact lists of third parties. 
We respect your right to have your email removed from our databases - you may unsubscribe at any time at your sole discretion.
We appreciate you subscribing to our newsletter mailing list and using our website. We will not abuse that privilege. CoolBusinessIdeas.com puts a high priority on list accuracy and quality content. 

Personal information that we gather

Information on general usage and any optional personal details you provide to us will be used to provide you with enhanced features such as customized content, access to special sections on our web sites and so on.

The following are details of the information we collect:
CoolBusinessIdeas.com collects data through registration from our subscribers. Additionally, usage information may be analyzed so that we can judge our system's performance or to determine whether we should offer customizable features in the future. 

Surveys may ask for contact information or unique identifying information to increase the value of the results. Contests may require contact information or other demographic/personal information in order to determine your eligibility. 

Who receives our emails

Only those who subscribe to our mailing list receive our newsletter. We will only send requested promotional email to registered visitors and members who have opted-in to our e-mail list. To ‘opt-in’ means you have entered your email address and clicked on the ‘submit’ or ‘go’ button.

What we e-mail

To those who specifically request them, we send the CoolBusinessIdeas.com newsletter at the start of every new month. Special promotional mailings from our partners are emailed to those who have specifically requested for them during the newsletter signup process.

Unsubscribing 

You may at any time choose to unsubscribe to the CoolBusinessIdeas.com newsletter. To unsubscribe, simply click on the unsubscribe link found at the bottom of every issue of the CoolBusinessIdeas.com newsletter. Your request will be processed immediately. You will be removed from our list and you will not receive future messages from us. You may also unsubscribe by entering your email address at this page: http://www.coolbusinessideas.com/lists/?p=unsubscribe

Unwanted emails

There are a few possibilities why you were sent an email you do not want:
-You have forgotten that you signed up for a service. 
-We are following up on a service you once requested. 
-Someone signed you up without your permission. 
We bear no responsibility in such cases.

Spam filtering

If you are using spam filtering software, you can enter the following email address into your filtering software's "white list" to ensure you continue to receive CoolBusinessIdeas.com email messages: newsletter@CoolBusinessIdeas.com. For best results, add our domain, www.coolbusinessideas.com, to your email client's whitelist.
