HOW WE PROTECT YOUR PRIVACY

At the Cool Mom Picks network (this includes Cool Mom Tech and Cool Mom Eats), we consider your privacy of critical importance. Hey, we visit lots of websites too! Therefore, we collect and handle all data in accordance with personal data laws.

This privacy policy describes in detail the types of personal information collected and recorded here, and how it’s used and disclosed, which is not scary at all, promise.

Please read it carefully. By entering, accessing, browsing or submitting information to us, you are acknowledging and agreeing to these terms and conditions. 

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at privacy@coolmompicks.com.

Log Files
Like many other Web sites, we use log files, which merely log visitors to the site. Pretty standard.

The information inside the log files that we may collect and store includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, your Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks or pages visited (browsing history) while you’re on our site.

We may also collect and store general, non-identifiable information like your general geographic location, like a city or metro area, but not your street address.

This information is used to analyze trends and track users’ movement around the site to help provide more relevant content for our awesome readers (that’s you!), to administer the site, and to gather basic, anonymized demographic information so we can serve you better.

It’s also used to monitor fraud or abuse of our site’s TOS — say, someone is entering a contest multiple times under different names, or spamming our comments.

To be clear, IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons
Cookies are small text files that your browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, etc.) stores on your computer. It is not a program and cannot contain a virus. In your own browser settings, you can delete cookies, block cookies, or set the browser to request your permission to accept cookies.

The more technical explanation: A cookie is a file containing an identifier (a string of letters and numbers) that is sent by a web server to a web browser and is stored by the browser. The identifier is then sent back to the server each time the browser requests a page from the server. Cookies may be either “persistent” cookies or “session” cookies: a persistent cookie will be stored by a web browser and will remain valid until its set expiry date, unless deleted by the user before the expiry date; a session cookie, on the other hand, will expire at the end of the user session, when the web browser is closed.

Cookies do not typically contain any information that personally identifies a user, but personal information that is stored about you may be linked to the information stored in and obtained from cookies.

The Cool Mom Picks network uses cookies for the most basic of purposes: To optimize website functionality, to store information about visitors’ preferences (including your device type and browser), to record which pages a visitor accesses or visits, and to personalize or customize our web page content based upon your browser type or other info your browser sends. This all allows us to offer you a better experience.

(Like, if we learn that 90% of you are using mobile devices to browse our site, we want to make sure we are mobile-optimized!)

We may also use this information to protect Cool Mom Picks and its users from spam, viruses, identity theft, illegal or harmful activities, and that’s good for all of us.

Email addresses
We can only obtain your email address if you opt-in to share it with us. That may happen in one of several ways:
