Privacy Policy

General
This Cool Quiz Network privacy policy describes the privacy practices for CoolQuiz.com and the Network web sites. As part of our commitment to your privacy, we have written this policy to help you understand what information we gather about individuals who visit our Web sites, how we use that information after we've gathered it, and when and how we disclose that information.

Information We Collect
Cool Quiz collects information in the Newsletter signup form that can be found on all pages.  Submitting this form is the only way to be entered onto the newsletter recipient list.

We will also ask you for email information on the "Hall of Fame" entry pages to notify you of a possible prize awards.

How We Use Your Information
Cool Quiz uses information only for newsletter mailings and prize notifications.

Who Else Sees Your Information.
Cool Quiz does NOT share information with anyone.

We may have to disclose information about you to law enforcement, other government officials or third parties in order to comply with applicable law or valid legal process or to protect the personal safety of our users or the public.

How You Can Update, Correct or Delete Your Information
If you want to be removed from the Cool Quiz newsletter - simply respond to the newsletter with "Remove Me" in the subject line or go to our 'Remove' Page - http://www.coolquiz.com/remove.asp  

To update your email information, you may send the request to info@coolquiz.com.
