To make this policy easy to find, we make it available on our homepage and at every point where personally identifiable information may be requested.

The Information We Collect

This policy applies to all information collected or submitted on the CoolSavings website. CoolSavings is a website that provides you with the opportunity to get all your savings in one place, including access to printable coupons, coupon codes, free samples, product deals, national and local deals and much more. We collect information you provide on our website, including but not limited to your name, street address, e-mail address, telephone number (home, work and/or mobile), date of birth, and other personal, financial, or demographic information.

Other ways in which we may collection information:

  • Surveys or Contests

From time-to-time, we may provide you the opportunity to participate in surveys or contests through CoolSavings or through one of our trusted partners who may operate the survey or contest. If you participate, we or our trusted partners may request certain personally identifiable information from you. Participation in these surveys or contests is completely voluntary and you therefore have a choice whether or not to disclose this information. The requested information typically includes contact information (such as name and shipping address), and demographic information (such as zip code). We may use this information as described in this policy and to improve our sites, monitor site traffic or personalize the sites.

  • Testimonials

From time-to-time, we may provide you the opportunity to provide testimonials about CoolSavings. We receive permission to post testimonials that include personally identifiable information prior to posting to CoolSavings.

  • Tell-A-Friend

If you choose to use our referral service to tell a friend about our website, we will ask you for your friend's name and contact information. We will automatically send your friend a one-time communication inviting him or her to visit CoolSavings. We will send you strictly service-related announcements on rare occasions when it is necessary to do so. For instance, if our service is temporarily suspended for maintenance or if new features or services are added to CoolSavings, we may send you a communication.

Use of Cookies

Our website use cookies to enhance and personalize your online experience and to enable our website to recognize you as a previous visitor. A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier that is sent to your browser from a website's computer and stored on your computer's hard drive. You can adjust the settings on your browser to erase or block such technologies from your computer if you want to, but if you do so, you may experience difficulty using or accessing CoolSavings.

Each website can send its own cookie to your browser if your browser's preferences allow it, but (to protect your privacy) your browser only permits a website to access the cookies it has already sent to you, not the cookies sent to you by other websites. Many websites do this whenever a user visits their website in order to track online traffic flows. Cookies record information about your online preferences and may be tied to your personally identifiable information such as your email address for these purposes. Users have the opportunity to set their computers to accept all cookies, to notify them when a cookie is issued, or not to receive cookies at any time. Each browser is different, so check the "Help" menu of your browser to learn how to change your cookie preferences.

Web Beacons

We and third parties employ a software technology called Web Beacons that help us better manage content on our website by informing us what content is effective. Web Beacons are tiny graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies, and are used to track the online movements of Web users. In contrast to cookies, which are stored on a user's computer hard drive, Web Beacons are embedded invisibly on Web pages and are about the size of the period at the end of this sentence. We may tie the information gathered by clear gifs to our customers' personally identifiable information such as their email address, to enhance and personalize content and your online experience.

We use Web Beacons in our HTML-based emails to let us know which emails have been opened by recipients. This allows us to gauge the effectiveness of certain communications and the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns. If you would like to opt-out of these emails, please see "Choice/Opt-out."

Our website also allows its partners and advertisers that are presenting advertisements on the CoolSavings website to set their cookies, or apply Web Beacons or similar technologies, to your computer. We have no access or control of these third party tracking technologies. This information may be used to:

  1. provide security;
  2. authenticate your Login;
  3. personalize features on the CoolSavings website;
  4. enable you to participate in certain online activities, such as making sure that each person only votes in an online poll once;
  5. help measure and research the effectiveness of the CoolSavings website, including collecting statistics on your response to advertisements, e-mail and other communications you receive;
  6. remember the preferences and registration data that you give us.

Our website and our associated third parties may also use ad network providers to help present advertisements on the CoolSavings website. These ad network providers may use cookies, or Web Beacons or similar technologies on your computer to tailor content, advertisements to your preferences, help target, measure and track your visits and the effectiveness of advertisements and promotions, using data gathered over time and across their networks of Web pages to determine or predict the characteristics and preferences of their audience and to provide security within shopping baskets or transactions. The use of cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. If you do not wish to have this information used by certain ad network providers for the purpose of serving you targeted ads, you may opt-out by clicking here: http://preferences.truste.com/. Please note this does not opt you out of being served advertising. You will continue to receive generic ads. You may find more information at the website of either the individual third-party ad network, the Network Advertising Initiative (http://www.networkadvertising.org), Privacy Choice (http://www.privacychoice.org/choose), and Digital Advertising Alliance (http://www.aboutads.info/choices/).

Log Files

As is true of most websites, we gather certain information automatically and store it in log files. This information includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and clickstream data.

We use this information to analyze trends, to administer the website, to track users' movements around the website and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole.

We may link this automatically-collected data to personally identifiable information to document your registration IP address, date and time, detect and investigate malicious activity, as necessary, comply with our corporate policies, including but not limited to legal obligations, dispute resolution and enforcement of our agreements and to assist us with enhancing and personalizing content and your online experience.

We use a third-party tracking service that uses cookies and clear gifs to track non-personally identifiable information about visitors to our website in the aggregate to track usage and volume statistics to improve our website.
