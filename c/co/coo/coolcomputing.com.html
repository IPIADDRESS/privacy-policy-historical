  • What is the CoolComputing privacy policy?

    CoolComputing is committed to maintaining your confidence and trust. We do not set up to track, collect or distribute personal information about its visitors. The server does recognize the home server of visitors, but not e-mail addresses. For example, we can tell which Internet Service Provider our visitors use, but not the names, addresses or other information about our visitors that would allow us to identify the particular visitors to our sites.

    We do track information about the visits to our web sites. For example, statistics are compiled to that show the daily number of visitors to our site, the daily requests we receive for particular pages on CoolComputing, and what countries those requests come from, etc. These aggregated statistics are used internally to better provide services to our visitors and the statistics contain no personal information and cannot be used to gather such information.
