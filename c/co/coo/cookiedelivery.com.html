Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 17, 2015
 
At Tiff’s Treats, we want you to be aware of how we collect, use, and protect your information. By visiting our website, you agree to the practices contained within this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy may change over time, so we encourage you to review it regularly.
 

What Information Is Collected?

We collect the personal information that is necessary to fulfill your order with Tiff’s Treats. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, phone number, email address, IP address, street and delivery address, and credit card information. Tiff’s Treats may implement common tracking tools, such as "cookies" to collect information about your order. By visiting our site, third parties may place cookies on users’ browsers for targeted advertising
 

Is The Information Shared?

Tiff’s Treats may share your information with third party service providers who provide support services such as credit card processing. These service providers only have access to the information needed for their function, and they are not authorized to use the information for any other purpose.  You can control our use of cookies for tracking in your browser’s setting. NOTE: If you configure your computer to block cookies, you may not be able to access certain functionality on our website. To find out more information on how to opt out of targeted advertising, please visit www.aboutads.info.
 

How Is The Information Used?

We use the information gathered during the ordering process to fulfill your order with Tiff’s Treats. We may use your phone number or email address to contact you about your order.  Tiff’s Treats, and vendors we have approved, utilize common tracking tools, such as cookies, to enhance your experience with Tiff’s Treats and inform you of special offers. This data may be used by third parties to target advertising on other sites based on your online activity.
 

How Is The Information Secured?

The information you submit via our website is secured using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology. SSL encrypts the information you input.
 
Tiff’s Treats does not store your full credit card information, and our employees do not have access to the full credit card number provided by you on our website.

If you create and utilize a user account on our website, please be sure to sign off when you are finished with the site on a shared computer. This will help protect your account from unauthorized access.
 

How Do I Update My Personal Information?

When you have created a user account on our website, you may update your personal information for future orders by signing in and selecting My Account in the upper right portion of the screen. If you need to make an adjustment to an order that has already been placed, please call your local Tiff’s Treats store for assistance.
