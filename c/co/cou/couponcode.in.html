PRIVACY POLICY

CouponCode is committed to protecting the privacy of all their customers and may collect information from you when you use the website, such as your email address (if provided) and your IP address. CouponCode is committed to protecting your privacy and will only use information that has been lawfully collected from you in full accordance with the data protection laws applicable in India. No sensitive information will be obtained unless consent is given and we will endeavour to ensure that the data collected by us is accurate and constantly updated. Should any of this information be incorrect, you have the ability to change the information accordingly. Information

  • To provide you with regular information and marketing material about CouponCode via emails
  • To have contact with you with regards to any services required

Should you sign up for the newsletter and other marketing and promotional material, these will be sent via email, using this information. Information Protection

CouponCode implements a variety of security measures to ensure the protection of the data you supply us with.

By using our website, you consent to the CouponCode privacy policy as stated above.
