What are cookies, log files and web beacons?

  1. Cookies are tiny data files which reside on your computer and allow CouponCodes.com to recognize you as a user when you return to our site using the same computer and web browser.
    1. Like most websites, CouponCodes.com uses cookies and web log files to track site usage
    2. Cookies do not store personally identifiable information
    3. Most browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser settings to decline cookies. In the event that you decline cookies it may affect the function of some interactive features on our website
  2. Log files store various non-personally identifiable data regarding your visit such as internet protocol address (IP address), information about your browser, internet service provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring and exit pages, platform type, and number of and identification of clicks.
    1. Most websites use log files as part of the Internet communication standards
    2. This information is used to analyze overall trends, diagnose problems with our server, or display content according to your preferences
    3. This anonymous information may be shared with business partners and advertisers on a aggregate and anonymous basis
  3. Web beacons are tiny graphics with a unique identifier that function similar to cookies, and are used to track online movement of web users or to access cookies.
    1. Web beacons are embedded invisibly on web pages
    2. This information is used to track user navigation and usage patterns, and to show us if our content and communication is effective
    3. Web beacons do not store personally identifiable information
    4. This information may also be used by ad networks to display targeted ads
