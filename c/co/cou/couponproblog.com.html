Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to me and I will respect the information that you submit to me in order to subscribe to my blog, enter giveaways, participate in a poll or survey, or post a comment. This blog does not collect information from minors, only from adults ages 18 and above. I will never give or sell your email address to anyone. Google’s use of the DoubleClick DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to the users of Coupon Pro based on their visit to Coupon Pro Blog and/or other sites on the internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the advertising opt-out page.
