Refund & Privacy Policy

RememberCoupons.com Refund Policy

The below listed rules apply only to our Goods and Daily Deals.

RememberCoupons.com Refund Policy is as follows: Within 30 days of your voucher purchase, you may request a refund in the form of RememberCoupons.com credits, or, at our discretion, we may issue a credit in the form of a check less any processing fees such as fees charged by credit card companies, in addition to mailing fees. These fees will never be greater than 10% of the value of the voucher.  If RememberCoupons.com credits are issued, these can be used towards any future purchase on the website. This applies also if you hold a voucher for a business that has closed as long as the voucher has not expired.

If your RememberCoupons.com voucher has expired, it is up to the merchant as to whether or not they will honor the voucher. No refunds will be issued for expired vouchers. All product warranties purchased through our website are the sole responsibility of the merchant from whom it was purchased. If the item you have purchased arrives damaged or defective, please contact the product manufacturer for further information on returning the item to either be repaired or replaced.  RememberCoupons.com is not responsible for damages occurred during shipping.

Here is a list of items that are non-refundable. Refurbished items, foods, beverages, perishable items, movie tickets, music, DVD, and/or software products.

Also please note that any item that is not in its original condition, is damaged, or is missing parts for reasons, are not due to the error of RememberCoupons.com.
