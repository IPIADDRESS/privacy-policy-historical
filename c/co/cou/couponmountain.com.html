Did You Know About Online Coupons?

Did you know that you could save money almost every time you shop online by using online coupons? Most people don.t.

Next time you buy something online, take a careful look as you take your shopping cart through the checkout process. If you see, .enter your coupon or gift certificate here., you.ve found an online merchant that uses online coupons. A quick search at CouponMountain.com can tell you if we.ve found any coupons or special promotions for that retailer at this time. We can also tell you what, if any, restrictions are on those coupons. And even if you don.t find a coupon for the online merchant you want to shop at, you might find another online store where you can buy the same item for less.


What Is An Online Coupon?

The most popular online coupons are clickable and can be redeemed online. As the name implies, you click on the coupon and are taken to the website of the online merchant. Usually you will be given a specific coupon code (e.g. SAVE20) which must be entered during the checkout process at the online merchant to receive a discount, or the coupon value will be automatically entered into the shopping basket.

There are also printable coupons available online. These are usually printed on your laser printer and then taken to a merchant.s physical store for redemption. We have found it easier to use clickable coupons for online merchants, and not found a great source of valuable local coupons. When we do, we will post them on CouponMountain.com

Where Can I Find Online Coupons?

Simply type www.couponmountain.com into the address bar of your browser, and you.ll be taken to our home page.

If you are more interested in browsing coupons for a general shopping category, click on a category link. From there you can select the coupon or promotion that best matches your interest.

Each coupon will include whenever possible:

The offer amount or savings
The restriction or terms of conditions
The dates during which the offer is valid
To redeem a coupon or offer that meets your interest, simply click on .use this coupon., and you will be taken to the merchant.s web site. A CouponMountain.com window will pop up with the coupon code, and a link to the restrictions if any. For our most popular merchants, we will also offer a link to demonstrate where to enter the coupon code during the merchant.s checkout process.
