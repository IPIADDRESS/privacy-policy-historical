Your Privacy Comes First

This Privacy Policy explains how Coding Dojo, LLC (“Coding Dojo,” “we” or “us”) collects, uses and discloses information when you access or use our websites and other online products and services (collectively, the “Coding Dojo Service Offerings”) or when you otherwise interact with us.

We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by revising the date at the top of the policy, and in some cases, we may provide you with additional notice (such as adding a statement to the homepages of our websites or sending you an email notification). We encourage you to review the Privacy Policy whenever you access the Coding Dojo Service Offerings to stay informed about our information practices and the ways you can help protect your privacy.

Collection of Information

Information you provide to us

We collect information you provide directly to us. For example, we collect information when you: create an account; join our partner program; make a payment; use any Coding Dojo Service Offering; contact customer service; respond to questionnaires; or otherwise interact with us. The types of information we may collect vary depending on the Coding Dojo Service Offerings you use and may include your name, email address, phone number, postal address and contact details for your organization if applicable.

Information we collect automatically when you use the Coding Dojo Service

When you access or use the Coding Dojo Service Offerings, we may automatically collect information about you, including:

  • Log Information: We log information about your use of the Coding Dojo Service Offerings, including the type of browser you use, access times, pages viewed, your IP address and the page you visited before navigating to the Coding Dojo Service Offerings.
  • Device Information: We collect information about the computer or mobile device you use to access the Coding Dojo Service Offerings, including, but not limited to, configuration information, network speed, hardware model, operating system and version and information about the software on your computer or device.
  • Information Collected by Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies: We use various technologies to collect information, and this may include sending cookies to your computer or mobile device. Cookies are small data files stored on your hard drive or in device memory that helps us to improve the Coding Dojo Service Offerings and your experience, see which areas and features of the Coding Dojo Service Offerings are popular and count visits. We may also collect information using web beacons (also known as “tracking pixels”). Web beacons are electronic images that may be used in the Coding Dojo Service Offerings or our emails and help deliver cookies, count visits, understand usage and campaign effectiveness and determine whether an email has been opened and acted upon. For more information about cookies, and how to disable them, please see “Your Choices” below.

Information we collect from other sources

We may also obtain information from other sources and combine that with information we collect through the Coding Dojo Service Offerings.

Coding Dojo is based in the United States and the information we collect is governed by U.S. law. By accessing or using the Services or otherwise providing information to us, you consent to the processing and transfer of information in and to the U.S. and other countries.

Security

Coding Dojo takes reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration and destruction.

Sharing of Information

We may share information about you as follows or as otherwise described in this Privacy Policy:

  • With vendors, consultants and other service providers who need access to such information to carry out work on our behalf;
  • If you live in a jurisdiction where we have an arrangement with a third party reseller or consultant, or if you are using a partner-branded version of the Coding Dojo Service Offerings, we will share information about you with the reseller, consultant or partner to provide you with the products and services you requested;

  • With your consent or at your direction, including if we notify you through the Coding Dojo Service Offerings that the information you provide will be shared in a particular manner and you provide such information.

We may also share aggregated or de-identified information, which cannot reasonably be used to identify you.
