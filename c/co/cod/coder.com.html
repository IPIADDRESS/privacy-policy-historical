How We Collect your Information
Information You Provide

We collect any information that you provide us when you use the Service.

Registration and Profile Information:

When you register for the Service, we will ask you for your name, email address, mobile phone number, and billing address. We may also collect your information from a supported third party website when you use your credentials from those sites to log onto the Service. For example, when you log in with your GitHub credentials we may collect the information you have made publicly available on that service, such as your name and username. We may also obtain other non-public information from these supported third party websites with your permission.

User Inputted Code:

We collect the code you submit to our Service in order to provide you cloud based development tools and services.

Payments:

If you make payments through the Service, we will ask you to provide your payment information, such as your credit card number and billing address, so our service providers can process your payment.

Communications and Other Interactions:

We may collect information you provide when you communicate with our customer-support team.

Information We Collect When You Use Our Service:

We may automatically collect certain technical information from your computer or mobile device when you use the Service.

Device Information:

We receive information from your device, including IP address, web browser type, mobile operating system version (to the extent applicable), and unique device identifiers. We may also collect and store your browser settings and preferences.

Usage Information:

We receive information about your interactions with our Service, such as your interactions with the Website, the time spent interacting with features, and the dates and times of your visits.

Information from Cookies and Similar Technologies:

We and our service providers may collect information using cookies or similar technologies. Cookies are pieces of information that are stored by your browser on the hard drive or memory of your computer or other Internet access device. Cookies may enable us to personalize your experience on the Service, maintain a persistent session, passively collect demographic information about your computer, and monitor other activities. Our Service may use different kinds of cookies and other types of local storage (such as browser-based or plugin-based local storage).

We use the information we collect about and from you for a number of purposes, including: providing, supporting, and improving our cloud based development tools, analyzing how you use the Service, managing our network, and better tailoring content and features.

We may use information we collect for marketing purposes, such as providing you with promotional materials that may be useful, relevant, valuable or otherwise of interest to you.
