Those who engage in transactions with Codeforwin are asked to provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to process those transactions. Codeforwin does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

Aggregated Statistics

Codeforwin may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. Codeforwin may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, Codeforwin does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Codeforwin uses cookies to help Codeforwin identify and track visitors, their usage of Codeforwin website, and their website access preferences. Codeforwin visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Coeforwin's websites, with the drawback that certain features of Coeforwin's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
