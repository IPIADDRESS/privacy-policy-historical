The information you share with CodeMyConcept including your personal information is used to complete your order. For example, your email address is used for notifications about the status of your order and communications with you.

We do not sell or trade any information you shared with us.

All the information provided to CodeMyConcept online will remain safe and secure at all times.

Retention of Information

We store your information in the United States and Europe. We will retain your information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.

We will retain and use your information to the extent necessary for accounting and reporting purposes, fraud screening, disputes resolving, enforcing our legal agreements and policies and to comply with our legal obligations (for example, if we are required to retain your information to comply with applicable laws).

To provide you with the Services your information (including personal information) may be transferred outside the United States to Europe and may be processed by our exclusive contractors and subcontractors.

Your Data Protection Rights

With respect to your personal information you have the following rights:

  • the right to access, update or to delete the information we have on you.
  • the right of rectification. You have the right to have your information rectified if it is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • the right to object. You have the right to object to our processing of your personal information.
  • the right of restriction. You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal information.
  • the right to data portability. You have the right to receive your personal information, provided to us, in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format and have the right to transmit those information to another company without hindrance from CodeMyConcept (where it’s feasible).
  • the right to withdraw consent. You also have the right to withdraw your consent to your personal information processing at any time.

To exercise any of these rights please contact us through Client Area or by emailing to data.protection@codemyconcept.com. Please note that we may ask you to verify your identity before responding to such requests.

Information Security

This website uses state-of-the-art digital encryption and security technologies to protect the privacy and security of our clients’ information, we work with specific security measures to ensure protection and prevent the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.

All data passing from your computer to us is authenticated and encrypted. Any information sent from your computer to us is carefully handled and protected at all times.

Why We Use Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of text sent by your web browser by a website you visit. A cookie file is stored in your web browser and allows us to recognize you and make your next visit easier and this website more useful to you.

Our cookies serve various purposes, but are generally either necessary or essential to the functioning of this website and its Services.

Strictly Necessary or Essential cookies:

these cookies let you move around the website and use its essential features. Without these technologies, some services you have asked for cannot be provided.
Please note that these technologies do not gather any information about you that could be used for marketing or remembering where you've been on the Internet.

Performance cookies:

these cookies collect information about how you use our website e.g. which pages you visit, and if you experience any errors.
These cookies do not collect any information that could identify you and are only used to help us improve how our website works, understand what interests our users and measure how effective our content is by providing anonymous statistics and data regarding how our website is used.

Functionality cookies:

these cookies are used to provide services or to remember settings to improve your visit.
If you do not wish to receive cookies or store them on your computer any more please change your browsing settings and/or delete all received cookies before. If you do this, however, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a website and some services and functionalities may not work.
