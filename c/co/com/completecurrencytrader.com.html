Privacy Policy

Complete Currency Trader is committed to protecting the privacy of its clients. Any information you provide to this site which could identify you personally, is only ever used in strict accordance with the privacy statement set out below. Complete Currency Trader Ltd may occasionally change or update this policy, so you should check this page from time to time to be sure you are comfortable with this policy. The current policy is effective from 20 March 2014.

COOKIES:

We use cookies to anonymously track visitor behaviour on our site. A cookie is a very small text document, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier. When you visit our web site, the cookie asks your computer for permission to store this file in a part of your hard drive specifically designated for cookies. Cookies help us analyse web traffic and we use traffic log cookies to identify which pages are being used most often and for how long. This helps us analyse data about web page traffic and improve our web site in order to tailor it to visitor needs. We only use this information for statistical analysis purposes. A cookie cannot retrieve any other data from your hard drive or pass on computer viruses. The cookie’s we utilize on this site are completely anonymous and merely identify you as a “site visitor” with no ability to retrieve any personal or sensitive information about you. The information our tracking cookie may collect is as follows:

  • Your Internet protocol address
  • The kind of browser you use
  • Number of links you click within the site
  • State or country from which you accessed the site
  • Date and time of your visit
  • Pages you viewed on the site and for how long
  • Web page you linked to our site from

Browsers are usually set to accept cookies by default because they are an integral part of virtually every website. However, if you would prefer not to receive our cookie, you can easily alter the configuration of your browser to refuse/block ours specifically, or to block all cookies from all sites. If you choose to have your browser refuse cookies, it is possible that some areas of our site will not function as effectively when viewed, and your visiting experience on our site may not be as pleasant as we hope.

INFORMATION WE MAY COLLECT ABOUT YOU:

With your cooperation we may collect the following details…..

  • Name
  • Contact information including email address, phone number, skype ID etc
  • Other information relevant to the services offered from this site

We will only collect personal information such as name and email address with your explicit permission and active consent whereby you submit your details to us in a web form. Before we proceed to contact you with additional details of our service and products in response to any request you make, we will comply with internet best practice standards and ask you to manually confirm we have your permission to send you any relevant email communications.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION:

All information we gather from you is used solely to allow us to adequately provide you with our specific services as detailed throughout this site; particularly for the following reasons…..

  • For internal record keeping and administrative needs
  • To improve our services and web site
  • To respond to any direct requests you make to us
  • To occasionally update you with information we think may be relevant to you
  • To provide the services you have requested from us

Your information will never be exchanged, shared, sold, or otherwise distributed to any third party agents, except in exceptional circumstances such as if disclosure is required by law or is necessary to protect the rights or property of Complete Currency Trader Ltd.

USE OF YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS:

When you submit any contact information to us through our web forms or other means, it will only be used for the purpose of providing information relevant to our site. You can rest assured that you will not be inundated with promotional correspondence from us recommending other sites or products. We operate a strict “No Spam” policy and guarantee never to send you unsolicited or unnecessary emails. We are committed to treating you and your privacy with the respect you expect. You can opt out of receiving correspondence from us at any time you wish using a one click unsubscribe option contained in our emails. By utilizing this function, your contact details will be permanently removed from our list database, and we will no longer have the ability to email you.

SECURITY & SAFEGUARDS:

In our commitment to ensuring your information is secure, we have implemented a number of physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure of your details.

LINKS TO EXTERNAL WEB PAGES:

Our web site may contain links to pages provided and maintained by third parties. You should note that once you have used these links to leave our site, we have no control over the other sites and cannot be responsible for their individual privacy policies or the safety of your information. Third party sites are not governed by this privacy policy so we advise you to exercise caution and look at the privacy statement applicable to the individual web site in question. This is of particular relevance to the third party merchant provider we use to process payments for our business.
