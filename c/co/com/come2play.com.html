Come2Play - Privacy Policy

Last Revised: [October 26, 2011]

Come2Play Inc. and its affiliates (“Company” or “we”) respect the privacy of its users (“User” or “you”) and are committed to protect the User's information. We believe that you have a right to know our practices regarding the information we may collect and use when you visit our online social gaming service (the “Service”) available at [www.come2play.com] (the “Site”) and/or any websites of third parties engaged with us (the “Publisher Websites” and the “Publishers”, respectively).

1. Your consent (please read carefully!)

By accessing and/or using the Service, you agree to the terms and conditions set forth in this Privacy Policy, including to the collection and processing of your Personal Information (as defined below). If you disagree to any term provided herein, you may not access or use the Service.

2. Which information we may collect on our Users?

We collect two types of data and information:

2.1 Non-personal Information. The first type is non-identifiable and anonymous information. To put it simply, we have no idea what is the identity of the User from which we have collected the Non-personal Information. Non-personal Information which is being gathered consists of technical information and behavioral information, as detailed below:

Technical information :

  • Type of operation system (e.g. Windows, Linux, etc.)

  • Type of Browser (e.g. Explorer, Firefox, Chrome, Safari, etc.)

  • Screen resolution (e.g. 800x600, 1024x768, etc.)

  • Browser and keyboard language (e.g. English)

  • IP address.

· You computer’s configurations and settings and any similar technical data and information.

Behavioral information :

  • User's clickstream on the Service (i.e. the fact that User has chosen to click on a certain link or access a certain feature on the Service) .

· The activities of the User on the Service, including information pertaining to games played by the User (e.g. which game was played, when, with whom, game result, etc.).

  • Any Similar behavioral information.

2.2 Personal Information. The other type of information we may collect is individually identifiable information. To put it simply, this information may identify an individual or is of a private and/or sensitive nature such as name, address, telephone number and email address. Personal Information which is being gathered consists of any personal details provided voluntarily by the User during registration the Site or to any Publisher (such as Facebook) which using Single Sign-On mechanisms (as shall be further explained below).

2.2.1 Registration to the Site. During the registration process to the Site you will have to provide us with certain Personal Information. Such information include your nickname and your email which are mandatory, and additional information which you may provide at your sole discretion such as name, gender, date of birth, phone number, mobile number, address, and short description of yourself.

Please note : Publishers which use Single Sign-On mechanisms may share with us certain information about you such as your name, nickname, Avatar picture file or URL, contact details, and gender. Such information is shared with us for your convenience, in order to enable you to access the Service after signing-in the Publisher Website, and without the need to register to the Service separately.

2.2.2 Public Profile. For each User we create a personal segment within the service which is accessible by other Users. The information which becomes public and accessible by other Users consists of your nickname, registration date to the Service, amount of virtual currency within the Service, games played on the Service, achievements and prizes, your friends on the Service and comments you have received and approved from other Users.

2.2.3 Virtual currency. As the Service allows transactions using virtual currency, we will have access and will collect and store information relating to the virtual currency you own, including, but not limited to, the date payments made, items you have purchased, payment method for purchasing virtual currency (e.g. PayPal) and all the information relating to transactions made between you and other Users on the Service. However, we will not have access to your Personal Information regarding the actual payment means. In other words, we will not know what is your credit card number or PayPal identification number, etc.

2.2.4 Information from Facebook: When you connect to the Service trough Facebook for the first time you will be asked to permit Facebook to share with the Service the following information about you:

§ Basic Information (your first and last name, your profile picture or its URL and gender);

§ your user ID number, which is linked to publically available information such as name and profile photo;

§ the user ID numbers for your Facebook friends, whether or not such friends are connected to the Service;

§ information in your profile's “About Me” section;

§ your gender;

§ your date of birth;

§ your email;

§ your online/offline presence in the social network as well as the online/offline presence of your Facebook friends;

§ Your living location and hometown as specified in your Facebook profile.

§ Permission to post on your Facebook Wall.

There are two main methods we use:

(a) We collect information while you access or use the Service. In other words, when you access or use the Service we are aware of your usage of the Service, and may gather, collect and record the information relating to such usage. For example, when you connect to the Service, available on the Site and/or at the Publisher, your computer tells us your IP address. We may further log the content you generate during your use of the Service.

(b) We collect information which you voluntarily permitted Publishers to provide us. For example, when you wish to connect to the Service for the first time, we will ask you to allow Facebook to provide us certain information (as described above).

· Learn about the preferences of Users and general trends on the Service (e.g. understand which features are more popular than others).

(b) Personal Information is collected in order to:

· Provide you with certain information and services available only to
registered Users.

· Identify you when conducting customer service operations (such as providing feedback, etc.).

· Allow you to register for special offers, contests or premium services.

· Monitor and regulate virtual currency transactions on the Service.

8. Cookies & Local Storage

When you access or use the Service, Company may use industry-wide technologies such as "cookies" and Flash (or similar technologies), which stores certain information on your computer ("Local Storage") and which will allow us to enable automatic activation of certain features, and make your Service experience much more convenient and effortless. The cookies used by the Service are created per session, does not include any information about you, other than your session key and are removed as your session ends (usually after 24 hours). It is easy to prohibit the Local Storage. Most browsers will allow you to erase cookies from your computer's hard drive, block acceptance of cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored. In order to erase or disable the Local Storage option in Flash you should use the settings option of Flash according to the specific instructions provided by the technology provider. However, if you block or erase cookies, or change the settings of Flash, your online experience may be limited.

We also use certain third-party cookies. These are a different kind of cookies, stored on your computer by third parties, rather than by the Company (for example see Section 6 regarding Advertising). This kind of cookie is only read (and not altered by the Service) each time you visit the Service, and also when you visit certain websites or applications that uses similar cookies. Third-party cookies usually store only a Non-personal Information, such as the web pages you have visited, the duration of your browsing, etc.
