We may use, collect, track, and store certain data when you visit this site, including:

  • Your IP address;
  • The website that referred you to this site;
  • The search terms you used to find this site;
  • Your device information, such as operating system, browser type, and device type;
  • Session information, including how you use this website; and,
  • Cookies and other technologies, such as pixel tags.

This site allows you to fill out forms for the purpose learning more about or buying products and services available for sale on this site. By filling out these forms, you agree to send to us all of the information you input into those forms. These forms may prompt you to fill in your:

  • First and Last Name;
  • Home or other street address, including street name, name of city, state, and zip code;
  • Email address;
  • Telephone number;
  • Gender;
  • Age;
  • Computer or device and network information;
  • Social Security number;
  • Driver's license number;
  • Credit card data including credit card number, expiration date, and CID.

We may associate the data you voluntarily disclose to us with the data we collect when you visit the site. We also may associate both the data we collect and the data you voluntarily disclose to us with information we collect from the call centers associated with the phone numbers listed on this site, information related to your purchases and orders, data obtained from outside sources, and customer service-related information.

SHARING OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

We do not sell or rent any of the data we collect or maintain.

We may share certain data, including personal information, with service providers, vendors, marketing agencies and services, data management and analytics services, affiliates, and contract workers. We may also share certain data, including personal information, with Comcast. Additionally, we may store certain data, including personal information, on servers located at third-party data centers. In addition, we may obtain data, including personal information, from outside sources.

HOW WE MAY CONTACT YOU AFTER YOU VISIT ComcastOffers.com

We will not send you promotional or marketing emails directly without your express consent. We may contact you via email or telephone, though, in efforts to process orders for products or services you have placed, or to remind you of upcoming service appointments.

This site also utilizes tools to engage site visitors after they leave this site. For example, this site may show advertisements to you on other sites after you have visited this site. This is sometimes known as 'remarketing' because we may re-market our products or services to you on other sites after you leave this site. We use third-party vendors, including Google, to show you these advertisements on various sites on the Internet. These third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies, pixel tags, and other technologies to serve you ads based on your prior visits to this site.

Users may opt out of Google's use of cookies by visiting the Google advertising opt-out page at http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html. This site may use the Teracent cookie for remarketing, which you may opt out of by visiting the Teracent opt-out page at http://www.teracent.com/about-us/opt-out. This site may also use Google's DoubleClick remarketing pixel, which you may opt out of by visiting the DoubleClick opt-out page at http://www.google.com/settings/ads/onweb#display_optout of the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page at http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp.
