Company Folders, Inc Privacy Policy

Company Folders, Inc is committed to privacy.


The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site: www.companyfolders.com

Registration Forms

Our site's request forms, and credit card authorization forms require visitors to give us contact information (like their name, email, and postal address), financial information (like their bank account or credit card numbers), and demographic information (like their zip code, age, or income level).

Contact information from the registration forms is used to ship purchases, information about our company. The customer's contact information is never used to send promotional material from any of our partners to our customers, but is used to get in touch with the customer when necessary.

Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.

Financial information that is collected is used to bill the user for products and services.

Unique identifiers (phone numbers) are collected from Web site visitors to verify the user's identity and for use as account numbers in our record system.

Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site. We use this data to tailor our visitor's experience at our site showing them content that we think they might be interested in, and displaying the content according to their preferences.

We collect contact information (like their email address), financial information (like their account or credit card numbers), unique identifiers (phone number)s, and demographic information (like their zip code).

Contact information from the order forms is used to ship purchases, information about our company. The customer's contact information is never used to send promotional material from any of our partners to our customers, but is used to get in touch with the visitor when necessary.

Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.

Financial information that is collected is used to check the users' qualifications for registration, and to bill the user for products and services.

Unique identifiers (such as phone numbers or email addresses) are collected from Web site visitors to verify the user's identity and for use as account numbers in our record system.

Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site. We use this data to tailor our visitor's experience at our site showing them content that we think they might be interested in, and displaying the content according to their preferences.
