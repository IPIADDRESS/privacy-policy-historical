Privacy & Cookie Policy

Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how we collect, use, or process personal information we hold about you. We also provide you useful information on how to manage your personal information on our website, or in general from your browser settings for any website you access.

Legal Entity: Collector's Network, Inc.

ComicArtFans.com is solely owned and managed by the business Collector's Network, Inc.(CNI), whose business is located in the United States. CNI is owned and managed by William Cox, with a business address of 8659 Hickory Hollow Drive, Chardon, OH, 44024.

Your Privacy

At ComicArtFans.com we recognize that privacy is important. This document outlines the types of personal information we receive and collect when you use our services, as well as some of the steps we take to safeguard information. We hope this will help you make an informed decision about sharing personal information with us.

ComicArtFans.com collects personal information (First Name, Last Name, and Email Address) from you when you register to use this website. We realize that because of the nature of this hobby your privacy is very important to you, particularly when displaying pieces from your collection. Therefore we give you the opportunity to modify your name so it can read however you like. We never display your email address on the site and only allow other site users to contact you through email forms which conceal your email address.

We will never, under any circumstance, provide your information to another party.

Periodically we may contact you with promotions or services we feel might be of interest to you.

About Cookies

When you create an account on ComicArtFans.com and log in to the website, you consent to allow technologies like persistent cookies (small files stored by your browser), to identify your computer or device so we can deliver a better experience to you and provide you the access to manage your Gallery, Communicate with other Collectors, return to ComicArtFans.com without having to log-in again, and so on. We use cookies to ensure that we give you the best experience on our website while logged in. As outlined in our Privacy Policy, the limited personal information (Name and Email Address) we maintain from your account or cookie, and your activities on ComicArtFans.com, is not shared with any other business entity.

To Withdraw Your Consent for us to store a cookie in your browser, you can request we delete your account from our website. Deletion of your account will remove all history of your activities on the website, including personal emails with other collectors, all artworks you've shared in your Gallery and artwork comments you've received or posted to another collector's artworks. Once removed, none of this information can be restored. You will still have to clear the browser cookie on your own computer.

Our systems and servers also log information like your browser, operating system and IP address. Our systems are not designed to associate personal information with your activities via these logs, and logs are deleted every 30 days. Our server logs are maintened for security purposes only and are generally never reviewed unless there is a situation that warrants investigation which is rare.

Some cookies are placed by third party services that appear on our pages, such as Amazon, eBay, Adsense, or Google Analytics. If you continue without changing your settings, we'll assume that you are happy to receive all cookies from this website. If you would like to change your preferences with these 3rd party services you may do so by following the instructions here.

Google Analytics provides a browser add-on that prevents it from being able to record your activity on sites that use their website measurement tools. You can find information about this add-on here.

Personal Information

  • ComicArtFans.com uses cookies and other technologies to enhance your online experience and to learn about how you use ComicArtFans.com services in order to improve the quality of our services.

Uses

  • We may use personal information to provide the services you've requested, including services that display customized content and advertising.
  • We may also use personal information for auditing, research and analysis to operate and improve ComicArtFans.com technologies and services.

Your Choices

  • By using our web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page, so you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it and under what circumstances we disclose it.
  • You may refuse cookies in your browser, although some of our features or services may not function properly as a result.
  • We make good faith efforts to provide you access to your personal information upon request and to let you correct such data if it is inaccurate and delete it, when reasonably possible.

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy please Contact Us.
