About Us

OUR MISSION

More than 3 billion people connect to the internet every day, yet access to products is still limited and shipping price discrimination is prevalent. comGateway believes that these barriers should be taken down. We advocate for free access to markets and a level playing field where buyers are empowered to adapt to existing price differentials.

Since its launch in 2005, comGateway hasn't stopped focusing on improvement and innovation, ultimately becoming the leader in the freight-forwarding industry. We are proud to earn the trust of every one of our customers as well as e-commerce giants Visa and PayPal.

THE CONSUMER PLATFORM

Consumers in over 150 countries use comGateway for an easier and cheaper shopping experience from U.S. online stores. With our distinguished solutions to payment and shipping restrictions, our customers gain access to the world's largest online marketplace. Here's how it works

We ensure that our customers do not pay sales tax in the U.S. (as buyers are not consuming the products there), we consolidate and repack to reduce shipping charges, we developed our Live Quote service to take the guesswork out of estimating shipping costs, and a BuyForMe team that never says no.

THE BUSINESS PLATFORM

We provide assistance to brands that aim to elevate their presence in the international market by becoming an outsourcing partner to their non-domestic shopping carts. We offer payment fraud filters, full landed costs, global shipping at competitive rates, and multilingual customer support. We are currently helping customers to sell and ship into China, while our lean and mean IT team are working with our business clients to integrate into our platform in a matter of days instead of months.

RESOURCES

We draw on our extensive logistics experience to manage and optimize shipping resources for our customers. Our combined shipping volume allows us to negotiate the best rates from the freight integrators in your behalf.

Our proprietary customer service desks in China and the Philippines augment our services with multilingual capabilities to handle pre- and post-sales support.

To adapt and innovate to the ever-changing landscape of e-commerce, comGateway’s entire IT infrastructure is built in-house and composed of highly-skilled team of elite professionals. Our strong IT team makes this possible and they work with our customers constantly to benefit from change with less effort.

How do I disable cookies?
If you want to disable cookies, you need to change your website browser settings to delete or reject them. Depending on the web browser used, further details on how to disable cookies for the most popular browsers are set out below:

Microsoft Internet Explorer:
1. Choose the menu “tools” then “Internet Options”
2. Click on the “privacy” tab
3. Select the setting the appropriate setting

For Google Chrome:
1. Choose Settings> Advanced
2. Under "Privacy and security," click “Content settings”.
3. Click “Cookies”

For Safari:
1. Choose Preferences > Privacy
2. Click on “Remove all Website Data”

For Mozilla Firefox:
1. Choose the menu “tools” then “Options”
2. Click on the icon “privacy”
3. Find the menu “cookie” and select the relevant options

For Opera 6.0 and further:
1. Choose the menu Files”> “Preferences”
