CoffeeKid.com and CoffeeGeek.com Privacy Statement

WebMotif Net Designs, owner and operator of the CoffeeKid.com and CoffeeGeek.com websites, is firmly committed to respecting the privacy of our viewers and our reviewers. The following policy explains how your personal information will be treated. Please check back periodically, as this policy may change from time to time.

Use of IP Addresses
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, administer our Web site, and improve the information and services offered.

Use of Cookies
Our site uses cookies to make sure you don't submit a review for the same product twice, to track your route through our site so that we can deliver you content in a proper order (eg, when reading the reviews for a specific product, a cookie is generated listing which reviews you have read, so that when you hit "read next review", your are delivered the next review in order) and to save your password (where applicable) so you do not need to re-enter it each time you visit our Web site during a browser session. WebMotif DOES NOT use cookies for any malicious manner, no do we currently use cookies for any sort of commerce purpose, be it advertising or marketing.

Surveys
From time to time, Coffeekid.com and Coffeegeek.com may run a survey to help better serve our readers and reviewers. Our online surveys ask viewers for contact information (like e-mail address) and other relevant information. We do not disclose your personally identifiable information to third parties. You may choose to tell us that you do not wish to have your information used as a basis for further contact with you, and we will respect your wishes.

We DO NOT create summaries of our visitor information for sharing with any third parties. This simply will not happen for the foreseeable future.

Site Visit Information
We gather information on site activity, such as how many viewers access the site, the pages they visit, how they linked to us, what route they take through the site, etc. We use this information on an aggregate, anonymous basis, to improve site content. At this time, we do not share this information with any third parties.

Database Management
The Coffeekid.com website currently runs three databases. One that is only administrator controled and contributed to, contains all the basic information about the products listed. The second is for a reviewer login procedure that is currently not implemented. The third is reviewer contributed, and contains all the reviews, email addresses, names, locations, and additional product info submitted by our reviewers. WEBMOTIF NET DESIGNS, COFFEEKID.COM, AND COFFEEGEEK.COM DOES NOT SHARE THIS INFORMATION WITH ANY THIRD PARTY. We respect your right to online privacy, and other than collecting the information for the smoother running of our review website, we have no ulterior motives or undisclosed plans to use your information.

If you have any questions about this Terms of Agreement statement, contact us at reviews@coffeekid.com.
