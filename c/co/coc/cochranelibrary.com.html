Privacy statement

Update Software Inc. is committed to protecting your privacy and security. We only use the information that we collect about you with your consent to process your order and to tell you about our services. We will never wilfully disclose personal information about you to any third party, other than to process an order or enquiry, without first receiving your permission or unless we are under a legal obligation to do so.

What information do we collect?

When you order a product from us, we collect your name, e-mail address, address, telephone and fax numbers, as well as details of the items you have ordered.

When you order and use an online version of The Cochrane Library, we also place a "cookie" on your computer's hard disk. A cookie is a small file which records details of your Cochrane Library search strategy and the Update Software web address which you have used.

Who else will we tell about you?

We will not pass on your personal details to any third party without your permission.

Your consent

By placing an order with Update Software Ltd you consent to the collection and use of the information you provide solely for the purposes of processing and fulfilling your request.

Feedback

All changes to this document will be on this page, keeping you up to date with our privacy policy whenever it may change.

You can always inquire about or check the information that you have given us by contacting us. We also welcome your questions and comments about privacy.
