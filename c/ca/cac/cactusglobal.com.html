Privacy Policy

Cactus considers any and all information related to its clients to be strictly confidential. All our employees have signed confidentiality agreements and will not make such information available to anyone outside Cactus , unless authorized in writing by the client. We respect the privacy of each of our clients; therefore, their personal information (name, address, e-mail address, contact number, etc.) will never be made available to any outside party.

Cactus reserves the right to retain information solely to resolve potential disputes. In addition, Cactus reserves the right to disclose confidential information

as mandated by law
in connection with a court action brought by a client against Cactus or any of its agents, employees, officers, directors, or service providers
in the good faith that such action is necessary to protect and defend the rights, property, or safety of Cactus , its users, or the public.
