Privacy Statement for Cats of Australia

www.catsofaustralia.com has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site: catsofaustralia.com

Information Automatically Logged

Cookies: Our site uses cookies. You may notice things like forum systems show your username when you enter. This is done with cookies. We also use cookies to show if content has changed since your last visit to that site, or area of a site.

Registration Forms

catsofaustralia.com collects data on users via registration forms on our site in which we ask visitors for general contact information (like email address), and demographic information (like zip code). 

External Links

This site contains links to other sites. catsofaustralia.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

Public Forums

This site makes  forums available to our members. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. Our forums require the collection of personal information (including but not limited to name, email address, location, and ip address). This information is used solely to insure any users violating our content rules can be contacted.

catsofaustralia.com respects the privacy of all visitors/users of the site. If for any reason you contact us via e-mail, or become a member of our forums, your contact details will remain confidential to the site owner & site staff only. The only exception to this would be if catsofaustralia.com was legally ordered to disclose information on a member.

Private Messages on Forums

Cats of Australia forums members who are over 18 have use of the private messaging system. The purpose of private messages is to allow for discreet conversations between members. Normal forums rules apply to private messages.

Members under 18 are not permitted to use the private messaging system, and members of the forums are not permitted to private message a minor. If members knowingly break these rules they will be banned immediately.

If you receive a private message which breaches Cats of Australia forums guidelines, is threatening, abusive or contains illegal content please forward it onto one of the Cats of Australia administrators immediately. Do not reply to the person sending you the private message.

While the name "private messages" gives the suggestion that they are private, they are part of the database & as such accessible to Cats of Australia administrators. So, while they are private, they are not necessarily secret. Cats if Australia has a policy of respecting member's privacy & would never access member's private message unless the following reasons applied:

We had received a complaint, information from another Cats of Australia member that Cats of Australia rules had been broken by a member using/abusing the private message system.

Legal Disclosures of Information

Cats of Australia will not share any personally identifiable information collected from visitors or members of the site unless the following reasons apply.

1) We are legally ordered to provide such information via a legal subpoena.

2) We have reason to believe that a member has endangered, threatened, harassed another member or staff member.
