INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Automatic Information:

When you visit DestinationXL.com, our server attaches a small text file, known as a "cookie", to your computer's hard drive which will automatically collect and store non-personally identifiable information such as the name of the domain and host from which you access the Internet, the Internet Protocol (IP) address of the computer you are using, the browser software you use and your operating system, the date and time you access our website, and the Internet address of the website from which you linked directly to our website, if any. This information collected by using a cookie is sometimes called "clickstream". This is the only information collected unless you voluntarily provide information to us.

We use clickstream information, which we may share with our affiliates, licensees, trusted vendors or responsible outside companies, only as anonymous aggregate data to determine the number of visitors to our website, to understand how visitors navigate our website, to determine common traffic patterns, including what site the visitor came from, to ensure that the website is working properly and to improve the design and content of our website. This aggregate information does not include individual names or personal information, so it does not identify you or anyone else. For further information regarding cookies and how to disable them, refer to "Our Use of Cookies" below.

Wireless Service:

When you use our wireless service, we automatically collect information about your use of the wireless service, including:

  • utilization data, e.g. # of users of service by store;
  • session data, e.g. date and time device entered store, length of session;
  • store data, e.g. address of store where device is used;
  • use of DXL services, e.g. mobile coupons redeemed; and
  • websites and pages visited, e.g. DestinationXL.com, google.com

Information you give us:

Whenever you shop in our stores, use our wireless service, or shop online at DestinationXL.com, we may ask you to voluntarily provide certain personally identifiable information needed to better serve you and/or complete your transaction. This "Personal Information" may include: (i) Contact Data (such as your name, mailing address, telephone number, e-mail address and zip code); (ii) Financial Data (such as your account or credit card number and billing address, if you use a credit or debit card or pay by check); and (iii) Demographic Data (such as your age, marital status and occupation).

  • If you search our website, make a purchase, communicate with any of our departments such as Customer Service, or choose to use or participate in one of our various services, promotions, programs, surveys or sweepstakes, we may collect additional information.
  • From your transactions and other interactions with us, we obtain information concerning your personal preferences and the specific products or services you purchase or use.
  • We may also gather or receive information from other companies.

SECURING YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION AND FINANCIAL DATA

We maintain administrative, technical and physical safeguards to protect your personal information and financial data. An encryption methodology called SSL (Secure Socket Layer) is used to protect personal information that is transported via the Internet. For payment related data, DXL utilizes encryption, secured networks and other protections including physical and technical access controls, and other information security methodologies and safeguards to ensure data protection. For further information, click the "Back" button on your web browser and review our Security Statement.

While we strive to protect your Personal Information, no data transmission over the Internet, including e-mail and wireless service, can be guaranteed to be 100% secure against interception. Once we receive your transmission, we use our best effort to ensure its security on our systems, however, DXL cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us or from our website, and therefore you use our website and wireless service at your own risk. If your proposed e-mail or wireless communication includes sensitive information such as your Social Security number or your account, credit or debit card number, we strongly encourage you not to send to it to us electronically, but to contact us by mail or telephone instead so that one of our customer service associates may gladly assist you.

OUR USE OF COOKIES

DestinationXL.com uses permanent cookies. A "cookie" is a small text file stored by your web browser on your computer's hard disk for added functionality and for tracking website usage and navigation. Cookies allow us to recognize who you are when you visit our website by associating the cookie with other information you have voluntarily provided us, which information is stored on our secured database. At no time is your Personal Information stored within one of our cookies.

We may also use session cookies to collect non-personally identifiable information, such as your IP address and the date and time of your visit to our website, in order to measure the effectiveness of our online presence and improve the design and content of our website. Session cookies are temporary files that are erased when the web browser is closed.

Accepting cookies through your web browser is a personal choice. By modifying your browser preferences, you have the choice to accept all cookies, receive notice when a cookie is set, or to reject all cookies. You can still shop and make purchases from DestinationXL.com even if you set your browser to reject cookies, however, we may not be able to provide you with as personalized a shopping experience.

  • to improve our merchandise selections, customer service, and overall customer shopping experience;

  • Legal compliance including: assist law enforcement and respond to legal/regulatory inquiries, enforce or ensure compliance with the DXL wireless terms of use, apply applicable terms and conditions and other agreements, or to protect the rights, property or safety of DXL, our guests or others.
