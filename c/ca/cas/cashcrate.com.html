What Information We Collect
CashCrate collects only information which you give us through the registration process and when updating your payment options. This information includes: your first and last name, your email address, your payment email (if you're using PayPal), and your IP address. Your IP address is recorded when you sign-up so that we can enforce our one account per person policy, and also to fight cheating.

Information Sharing & Disclosure
CashCrate will not share your personal information with any third-party for the purpose of advertising, i.e. sending unsolicited email. Your email address is used when completing offers for tracking purposes only--this is how we figure out who completed offers. Additionally, your information may be given to legal authorities or your ISP if you violate our terms of service and undertake actions which are illegal, fradulent, or otherwise harmful to CashCrate, its users, or its advertisers.

Managing Your Information
You may update your information at anytime by editing your account under the "My Account" section in the Members Area. Additionally, you may contact us and request that we update any part of your information.

Questions & Comments
If you have any questions or comments regarding your privacy or this policy, just contact us and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
