* Cookies Information: When you visit The Casting Frontier Sites, we may send one or more cookies – a small text file containing a string of alphanumeric characters – to your computer that uniquely identifies your browser. The Casting Frontier uses both session cookies and persistent cookies. A persistent cookie remains after you close your browser. Persistent cookies may be used by your browser on subsequent visits to the site. Persistent cookies can be removed by following your web browser help file directions. A session cookie is temporary and disappears after you close your browser. You can reset your web browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some features of The Casting Frontier Sites may not function properly if the ability to accept cookies is disabled.

* Log File Information: When you use The Casting Frontier Sites, our servers automatically record certain information that your web browser sends whenever you visit any website. * Clear GIFs Information: When you use The Casting Frontier Sites, we may employ “clear GIFs” (a.k.a. Web Beacons) which are used to track the online usage patterns of our Users anonymously (i.e., in a non-personally-identifiable manner).
