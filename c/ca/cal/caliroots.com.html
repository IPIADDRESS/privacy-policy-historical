Use of cookies


A cookie is a text file that a web server places on your hard drive. Cookies can not be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. A cookie is uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you. A key purpose of cookies is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific web page, so you can get a personalized experience when you visit the site again. You may choose whether to accept or decline cookies. Most browsers accept cookies automatically, but you can usually change your settings to not accept cookies if you prefer. If you choose to allow your computer to accept cookies, you can also choose to be automatically logged into the website, this will speed up the check out process.

Californian Roots ABs websites uses cookies to help you get a personalized Internet experience on our website’s and in our marketing. On the site we use for example  ”session cookies” to manage your visit at any of Californian Roots websites such as what pages you visits which products you look at, which products you have put in your basket. You can easily turn of the use of cookies and erase your cookie history in you web browser. The session cookies are deleted from your hard disk when all browser windows are closed down. But doing so will mean that you will not get the full experience of Californian Roots ABs websites and e-commerce. If you turn off the function of session cookies for any Californian Roost ABs websites you will not be able to make a purchase.

Beside session cookies we also use cookies that will remain on your computer after you have closed the browser. This type of cookie helps our system to recognize that you have visited this site before and to remember which settings and products you have preferred. To help us with this we do use third party cookies, these cookies are from our partners and help us give you are more personalized website and more personalized adverting from us. These cookies have a lifespan of 1 month up to ten years, after they will be automatically deleted (you can delete them before this time by erasing the cookie history in your browser).

Californian Roots use these cookies to give you a better shopping experience by giving you more personalized website/advertising/newsletters and to optimize our websites by seeing what our customers like and what areas we can improve. These cookies does not store any personalized information about you and in our statically systems like Google Analytics we can not trace any information to you specific.
