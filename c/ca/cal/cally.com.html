10. PERSONAL DATA

Except for Date Picker purposes Apps and Sites do not transmit, store or collect any of your personal data at all. We don't have any interest in your data - it's yours, after all. Date Picker events will be automatically deleted one month after the last date of the event. If you have a paid Date Picker plan, events won't be deleted (except on your specific request) but will be kept so you can reference them in the future.

Anonymous Statistics
For the purpose of building the best apps, anonymous statistics such as device type and app version are stored and send to our servers. For example, if we find out that none of our users are still using an iPhone 3GS, we can stop testing on this device, allowing us to focus on newer stuff, instead of supporting legacy. If you dont want us to gather this information either, you can simply disable it in Settings by switching off the 'Anonymously collect usage statistics' switch.

Travel Time
In order to calculate Travel Time which is based on current time and traffic, locations are send to our servers. We do not store these locations. If you disable Travel Time no locations wont be send.

Content Subscriptions
When you go to the calendar store inside the apps or when you have one or more calendar subscriptions, for example public holidays, once in a while a connection with our servers is made to check if there is new calendar content available for you. This update request contains:

  • The App version so we can send back the correct calendar version;
  • Your language preference, so you can actually read the content;
  • Your Country preference, so we can send back calendars tailored for your country. For example, we have different calendars available for British users (Soccer calendars), than we have for USA users (Football calendars);
  • Version of data you downloaded earlier, enabling us to send you calendar changes only thus saving bandwidth.
None of this information is stored on our servers.

Sharing of Events
When uploading an event or using the 'Share with Messages' functionality, the event selected will be send to our servers where it gets stored, enabling you to send a small and unique web link which can be imported as an event into their calendar. The reason for uploading the event is that it is impossible to share events via Messages, Text or SMS in any other way: only text and web links are possible. All links are deleted after a month automatically.

Information used on your device
None of the information below is send to our Servers, it is used on your device only!

Calendar App
Being calendar (related) applications and site, Apps and Site obviously write to your calendars.

Address Book
Your Address Book is accessed for:

  • Presentation of birthdays
  • Linking events to contacts (name & address)
  • Sending invitations
Other Applications
Apps are able to send data to various third party apps and your web browser, all upon your specific request.

Cookie policy

cally.com uses cookies. On this page you will find an explanation of cookies, the reason they are used and which cookies are placed. There will also be addressed how cookies can be enabled and disabled and how to remove them.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website stores on a PC, tablet or mobile phone. A website can read and/or update its own cookies on a subsequent visit.

What are cookies used for?

The stored information is used to make the website to function better, including remembering your login session and choice for sorting lists. In addition, cookies are placed for website monitoring as to improve the website on the basis of the measurements. This data is only used aggregated and can not be traced to a PC or individual. Cookies are also placed by advertisers (3rd party cookies) to track which ads are viewed and determine which ads are the best can be displayed.
