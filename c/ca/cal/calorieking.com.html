Privacy Policy

Your personal information is important to CalorieKing.com.

CalorieKing.com understands that the privacy of your personal information is very important to you. It is important to us too!

The following policies will govern how CalorieKing.com manages your personal information and protects your privacy. This Privacy Policy is incorporated into and subject to our Terms of Use, which you should refer to for additional information relating to privacy issues.

1. Collecting Information About You

CalorieKing.com will not collect or monitor personal information about you without your consent unless we consider it is necessary:

  • to comply with the law
  • to comply with our Terms of Use
  • to protect the rights and/or property of CalorieKing.com, the Network, Network Users, Network Subscribers and Members or any other individual or entity, including members of the public
  • to reduce or avoid a threat to anyone's health, safety or well-being
  • to provide services to you

CalorieKing.com will only collect personal information which has been obtained from you. The amount of information will depend on you! You may provide us or an Affiliate with information:

  • when you register with or subscribe to the Network or email lists
  • by identifying yourself when you use the Network
  • by forwarding us correspondence about yourself or feedback about the Network
  • by joining various mailing lists
  • by answering surveys or polls
  • by entering competitions or sweepstakes
  • by posting in our chat rooms, message boards and other areas

Public discussion areas on the Network are not private and are governed by the Terms of Use. Any information you post in such areas may be seen, collected and used by others and may result in unsolicited messages. CalorieKing.com cannot control this. You post at your own risk.

In the event of a complaint, we may receive personal information about you from other parties in our Network, other Users or third parties who may communicate information to us about you in relation to that complaint.

CalorieKing.com uses Cookies and other tracking methods to allow the provision of certain services and to maximize your enjoyment of the Network. We do not link this information back to other information that you have provided to us, and CalorieKing.com does not store any information inside Cookies. Any collection of personal information other than that mentioned here will require your consent.

Cookies are pieces of information transferred from a website to your computer's hard disk for record-keeping purposes. Cookies do not personally identify Users, although they do identify a User's browser. Most Web browsers are set to accept Cookies, although you can reset your browser to refuse.

Cookies and IP addresses can enhance your enjoyment of the Network by allowing us to recognize you and anticipate your requirements. Cookies allow you to store your password without the need to continually re-enter it.They allow us to tailor a website or a marketing message to provide you with information which is relevant to your particular interests. CalorieKing.com gives you the option of subscribing to the Network and receiving enhanced services. Refusing to accept Cookies may prevent you from fully utilizing some of these services.

We also collect and store statistics and other information about the online activities of Users on an aggregate basis. This does not include personal information about you.

By recognizing how all of our Users utilize the Network and what they find interesting, we can focus our resources on features that are the most popular and beneficial to our Users, and provide continuously-improving services for everyone!

You should be aware that CalorieKing.com places advertising and other material on the Network which may originate from outside individuals or entities. In some cases, if you click on or view these advertisements or other material, a Cookie may be assigned by the relevant third party. CalorieKing.com does not control these Cookies.

Despite our best efforts, "Perfect Security" does not exist on the Internet.

Our employees and data processors are bound to respect the confidentiality of any personal information held by CalorieKing.com.
