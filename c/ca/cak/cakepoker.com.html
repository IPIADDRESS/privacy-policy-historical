Cake Poker Privacy Policy

Cake Poker and its owner and operator, Cake Gaming N.V. understand the importance of privacy protection on the internet. Cake Poker remains committed to upholding the confidentiality of the personal information that we collect. The information that we collect is safeguarded with the appropriate software and hardware tools in place as well as the techniques, methods and procedures that we choose to employ.

The following information will help you to understand our policies and your rights regarding the collection, storage, use and the safeguarding of information relating to you.

Information collected by Cake Poker

When you choose to open a Cake Poker Account you will be asked to provide us with certain pertinent personal information. This information will include your full name, mailing address, e-mail address and a chosen password. You will also be asked to certify that you are at least 18 years old or older in jurisdictions where specifically required. We will use this personal information to confirm and complete any financial transactions as well as to contact you regarding news and events information and provide you with customer support services. If at any time you do not wish to receive our newsletters, you may instruct us to remove you from the distribution list by following the appropriate instructions included in each of the newsletters or by contacting the Support Team directly by E-mail. It is very important that you complete the required account details with valid information as this information will be used in the process of validating financial transactions. Players may change their account details by selecting an account option in the lobby screen.

Information used to process Deposits and Withdrawals

Players will need to provide deposit specific information when making a deposit to play for real money (example: credit card number or NETeller account number). This information may be applied along with your personal information to allow us to clear financial transactions executed by you with Cake Poker.

Confidentiality of Information

Cake Poker undertakes substantial efforts to ensure the confidentiality of the information it has collected about individual players and will not knowingly allow access to this information to anyone outside of Cake Poker, other than to the player himself or as described in this Privacy Policy. As a player, you should ensure that your Cake Poker account password remains confidential and we recommend that you take appropriate steps to ensure others are not able to access your account information.

Limits of Confidentiality

Legally Required Disclosures: Due to the existing legal, regulatory and security environment, Cake Poker may be required, under certain circumstances, to disclose personally identifiable information about our Players. Cake Poker will use its best reasonable efforts to limit such disclosure to the following: where Cake Poker believes in good faith that it is required to do so in response to a subpoena, warrant or other legal process; or where reasonably necessary to identify, contact or bring legal action against persons or entities to preserve and/or enforce Cake Poker's rights. Further, we can, and you authorize us to, disclose your Player identification, name, street address, city, state, zip code, country, phone number, e-mail, and company to third party agents of Cake Poker as we, in our sole discretion, believe necessary or appropriate in connection with an investigation of fraud, intellectual property infringement, piracy or other unlawful activity or activity that may expose us to legal liability.

Who receives your information

In addition to disclosures due to legal requirements as explained above, we may from time to time disclose your personal information to:

  1. our Cake Poker employees
  2. employees of other companies that are related to or affiliated with Cake Gaming N.V.
  3. third party service providers who use your personal information to provide services to us in respect of your being a Player with Cake Poker.

All recipients of your personal information shall be bound by applicable data protection laws to keep your personal information private. These parties will adhere to policies that, at the very minimum, reflect those of Cake Poker.

Cookies

We may send you a temporary cookie when you visit the Cake Poker website. A cookie is a text-only string of information that we place in your computer's cookie file so that we can remember who you are when you revisit our website. We may use the data that is generated from cookies to compile statistical data on your use of our website. You are not obliged to accept the cookie from us and you have the ability to accept or decline cookies by modifying the settings in your browser. Please see contact our Support Team if you require any further information about the use or deletion of cookies.

Your rights pertaining to your information

You may write Cake Poker to, where appropriate, have your personal information removed, rectified, amended or completed. In order to contact us regarding your information please e-mail our Security Team at security@cakepoker.eu. Please note that Cake Poker may levy a small charge to cover administration costs in regard to any such request.

Consent to Privacy Policy

By opening an Account with Cake Poker, you agree to the terms of this PRIVACY POLICY. We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy and encourage you to periodically review it. Your continued use of the Cake Poker services constitutes your agreement to this Privacy Policy and any updates.

In the case of any conflicting presentation between this Privacy Policy and the Cake End User Agreement captured under our Terms Of Service, the terms outlined in the Cake End User Agreement shall prevail.
