The following information will help you to understand our policies and your rights regarding the collection, storage, use and the safeguarding of information relating to you.

When you choose to open a Win Cake Account you will be asked to provide us with certain pertinent personal information. You will also be asked to certify that you are at least 18 years old or older in jurisdictions where specifically required. Players may change their account details by selecting an account option in the lobby screen.

BANKING: DEPOSITS AND WITHDRAWALS

Players will need to provide deposit specific information when making a deposit to play for real money (example: credit card number or NETeller account number). Win Cake undertakes substantial efforts to ensure the confidentiality of the information it has collected about individual players and will not knowingly allow access to this information to anyone outside of Win Cake, other than to the player himself or as described in this Privacy Policy.
