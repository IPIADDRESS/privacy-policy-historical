I. INTRODUCTION
Caesars branded casinos and resorts (collectively, "Caesars") have an unwavering commitment to providing outstanding products and services to visitors to our web sites. This Privacy Policy explains our practices for the following sites: Bally's Las Vegas, Paris Las Vegas, Flamingo Las Vegas, Caesars Palace, Reno Hilton, Flamingo Laughlin, Grand Casino Gulfport, Grand Casino Biloxi, Grand Casino Tunica, Sheraton Tunica, Caesars Indiana, Bally's Atlantic City, Caesars Atlantic City, and Caesars.com. (collectively, the "Sites").

The Caesars brand has interest in 24 gaming properties operating under the Bally's, Caesars, Paris, Flamingo, Sheraton Tunica, Grand and Reno Hilton brand names in the United States with a total of approximately 2 million square feet of gaming space, 25,000 hotel rooms and approximately 52,000 employees worldwide.

Caesars Entertainment merged with Harrah’s Entertainment, Inc. (NYSE: HET) on June 13, 2005, creating the world’s largest gaming company, with more than 40 casinos in four countries. The merged company is based in Las Vegas, Nevada, where it operates Caesars Palace and five other major casino resorts.

In general, when you visit the Sites, you remain anonymous unless you choose to provide information to us.
