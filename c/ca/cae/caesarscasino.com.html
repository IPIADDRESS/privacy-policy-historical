Cookies may be stored on your hard drive in which case they remain on your hard drive until deleted, or in temporary memory in which case they are deleted when you shut down your browser or turn off your computer. Cookies also enable us to remember your user preferences for our Site. In addition, if you choose to provide us with Personal Information, we may store some items of your Personal Information and use it with the Traffic Data to better personalize your experience on our Site. Cookies and other technologies may also be used for site maintenance and analysis, performing network communications, authenticating users, serving contextual advertisements, and protecting against fraud and theft. You can block or remove cookies using your Internet browser's settings. If you block or remove cookies, your ability to perform certain transactions, use certain functionality, and access certain content on our Site may be affected. Please consult the "Help" section of your browser for instructions on how to do so correctly. Also please note, that Flash cookies do not operate the same way as browser cookies, therefore, the cookies management tools which are available in most of the web browsers may not enable removal of such flash cookies. If you wish to learn more about management of flash cookies you can check at www.adobe.com and make changes at the privacy settings panel. If you disable cookies, you may lose some of the features and functionality of playing our games, as cookies are necessary to track and enhance some of our game activities.
