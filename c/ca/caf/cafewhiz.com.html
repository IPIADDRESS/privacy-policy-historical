Securing your privacy is Cafe Whiz’s priority. This platform values your privacy and takes utmost care in safeguarding any personal data you provide. The current Privacy Policy describes how Cafe Whiz collects, uses, processes, and discloses your information, including personal data when you visit this Website. Cafe Whiz will not use, process or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

What is this current website about ?

Cafe Whiz is a website that comprises of original content concerning parenting issues especially catering to babies, toddlers and teenagers.

What personal data does Cafe Whiz collect and why the need ?

Providing you full disclosure for all collected data below –

Comments

When visitors leave comments on the site,  Cafe Whiz collects the data shown in the comments form, and also the visitor’s IP address and browser user agent string to help spam detection.

Media

If you upload images to the website, you should avoid uploading images with embedded location data (EXIF GPS) included. Visitors to the website can download and extract any location data from images on the website.

Contact forms

When you contact Cafe Whiz through the any of the Contact Forms, the website will collect your email id and name.

Marketing

Cafe Whiz may use your personal data to conduct some marketing activities such as sending you relevant parenting related information in the form of emails from time to time. You may easily opt out of these emails by opting to unsubscribe from an email campaign.

Cookies

If you leave a comment on Cafe Whiz you may opt-in to save your name, email address and website in cookies. These are for your convenience so that you do not have to fill in your details again when you leave another comment. These cookies might last for one week or more.

If you have an account and you log in to this site,  Cafe Whiz will set a temporary cookie to determine if your browser accepts cookies. This cookie contains no personal data and is discarded when you close your browser.

When you log in,  Cafe Whiz will also set up several cookies to save your login information and your screen display choices. Login cookies last for two days, and screen options cookies last for about a week. If you select “Remember Me”, your login will persist for two weeks. If you log out of your account, the login cookies will be removed.

Embedded content from other websites

Articles on this site may include embedded content (e.g. videos, images, articles, etc.). Embedded content from other websites behaves in the exact same way as if the visitor has visited the other website.

These websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with that embedded content, including tracking your interaction with the embedded content if you have an account and are logged in to that website.

Using  Cafe Whiz on a mobile device

When you use the mobile version of  Cafe Whiz, the processing of personal information you provide happens the same way as when you use the regular desktop website. However, in this case, additional information such as device, location information etc. might also be tracked when visiting our website using a mobile device. You may opt out of displaying your location simply by turning off the location on your mobile device.

What kind of data does Cafe Whiz receive automatically?

When you view our Cafe Whiz on your devices, we collect technical information. This includes:

  • Type of device you are using (e.g. phone, computer, other)
  • Version of the operating system running on the device;
  • Anonymous usage statistics and crash reporting data; and unique identifier details for your device such as the IP address (a unique number that identifies your computer whilst accessing the internet),
  • IMEI number (a unique number that identifies your mobile phone), or advertising identifier (unique ID that enables us to show you ads that are targeted to you). However, unique identifiers are anonymous and we do not use this information to identify you as an individual.

Cafe Whiz uses this automatically gathered information about you to understand how our website is used by our parenting community and to ensure that any technical problems are reported to us. We use Google Analytics to gather and process this data.

Whom does Cafe Whiz share your data with?

Your personal data will be shared with third parties under the scenarios mentioned below:

  • Competent authorities: We disclose personal data to law enforcement agencies upon request from these bodies. This might be strictly necessary for detection, prevention, or prosecution of criminal acts or fraud or if we are legally obliged to do so.
  • Social media: We have certain social media plugins including Facebook, Twitter and Pinterest integrated into our website using which you can share and post things on these platforms. Commenting on blogs or writing reviews using third-party plugins such as Facebook and others will be available on our platform and visible for the public to view.

How long does Cafe Whiz retain your data?

If you leave a comment, the comment and its metadata are retained indefinitely. This is so we can recognize and approve any follow-up comments automatically instead of holding them in a moderation queue.

For users that register on our website (if any), we also store the personal information they provide in their user profile. All users can see, edit, or delete their personal information at any time (except they cannot change their username). Website administrators can also see and edit that information.

What rights you have over your data?

If you have an account on this site or have left comments, you can request to receive an exported file of the personal data we hold about you, including any data you have provided to us.

You can also request that we erase any personal data we hold about you. This does not include any data we are obliged to keep for administrative, legal, or security purposes.

Where ever we have used your personal data with your consent, you have the right to withdraw your consent at any time subject to applicable law.

Where we send your data?

Visitor comments may be checked through an automated spam detection service.

How we protect your data?

We are committed to protect the personal information you provide us and take reasonable measures to prevent any misuse of the same. We prevent anyone from gaining unauthorized access to your personal data with us. Only authorized personnel in our team are permitted to access your personal information.

Privacy Policy Updates

We reserve all rights to modify this Privacy Policy document at any given point in time. Any changes to the Privacy Policy will be posted here, The “Last Updated” date at the top indicates the date of revision of the policy.

Additional information

In case you have any complaints or queries about our Privacy Policy, you may contact us.
