Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy describes how The William Carter Company, a subsidiary of Carter's, Inc. ("Carter's," "we" or "us") protects, collects and uses your personally identifying information. By visiting our website, you accept the practices described in this Privacy Policy. We will post an announcement of any changes to this Privacy Policy on our website prior to those changes taking effect. Any information of yours that we collected before such changes take effect will be secured as described in the most recent version of the Privacy Policy.

How we collect and use your personally identifying information
Carter's collects information from visitors to our website to assist them with their shopping experience. When you register we actively collect your name, address, phone number and e-mail address which we save as part of your user profile. We may use this information to send you e-mail messages to inform you about our products and to provide you with special sales announcements and promotions. You have the ability to unsubscribe from promotional e-mails at any time. On occasion, we may contact you by phone to let you know of a special Carter's event or promotion. If you prefer not to be contacted by phone, please call 1-888-782-9548 and ask to be placed on Carter's do not call list. Carter's values your privacy and makes every attempt to comply with federal and state "do not call" regulations.

We also automatically gather statistical information about visitors to our website, such as IP addresses, browsers, pages viewed, number of visitors and the like. However, we will not use your personally identifying information to identify this data. Instead, we will combine this with other data and use it to determine how our website is used and how we may improve it. Your information is used only by Carter's and is not sold, rented or distributed to any other companies.

Changing your user profile
You may change the information in your user profile at any time. If you would like to change any information in your user profile, please contact us at 1-888-782-9548.

Unsubscribing
We offer our consumers the opportunity to receive e-mail messages about pertinent information and special sales announcements and promotions regarding Carter's products and services. If you are not interested in receiving communications from Carter's, please contact us at 1-888-782-9548.

Our website, www.carters.com, may contain links to websites belonging to third parties. We are not responsible for these non-Carter's websites, and you enter them at your own risk. Your personal information is protected as described in that website's privacy policy.

Website data security
We use reasonable and current security methods to protect your personally identifying information from unauthorized access, to maintain its accuracy and to ensure that it is used properly. However, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be perfectly secured. Therefore, while we try to protect your personally identifying information, we cannot guarantee the security of the information you send to us, and you do so at your own risk.
