Privacy Policy

At CareerSafe your privacy is of utmost concern. We will protect the information you provide to us and will not share, sell, rent, or otherwise distribute that information to any entity outside of CareerSafe unless you grant us permission to do so.

All online financial transactions are processed using SSL encryption technology in order to provide you with the most secure experience possible.
