Information Collection and Use

General

  • CardPlayer.com collects personal information when you register with CardPlayer.com, when you use CardPlayer.com products or services, when you visit CardPlayer.com pages or the pages of certain CardPlayer.com partners, and when you enter promotions or sweepstakes. CardPlayer.com may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies.

  • CardPlayer.com collects information about your transactions with us and with some of our business partners.
  • CardPlayer.com automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, CardPlayer.com cookie information, and the page you request.
  • CardPlayer.com uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.

Children

When creating an account, we require the new member to certify that he or she is 18 years old or over by checking the appropriate box. If a child under age 18 attempts to register with CardPlayer.com, we ask that he or she have a parent or guardian create a CardPlayer.com Family Account to obtain parental permission.

  • CardPlayer.com will not contact children under age 18 about special offers or for marketing purposes without a parent's permission.
  • CardPlayer.com does not ask a child under age 18 for more personal information, as a condition of participation, than is reasonably necessary to participate in a given activity or promotion.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

  • CardPlayer.com does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:
