Updated: May 27, 2011


Flatsix, LLC ("CarCareKiosk", "we", "our" or "us") understands that you care how information about you is gathered, used and shared. This Privacy Policy describes how the information collected when you use carcarekiosk.com and related services (the "Service") will be used and how you can control the collection, correction and deletion of information. Questions regarding this policy should be directed to info "at" carcarekiosk.com.


INFORMATION COLLECTED BY US


User Provided Information: You provide us information about yourself and the vehicle(s) you drive, such as your name and email address, the year, make, model, sub-model, mileage, vehicle identification number ("VIN") and maintenance history when you register with the Service. Any electronic correspondence with us may be retained.


Site Activity: We follow some of the actions you take on carcarekiosk.com, such as watching a video, uploading Content or referring a friend.


Cookies and other Non-Edible, but Tasty Sounding Information Collection Processes: When you visit carcarekiosk.com, we may send one or more small non-executable text files to your computer that uniquely identifies your browser and lets us help you log in faster and enhance your navigation through the site (aka: cookie). A cookie does not collect personal information about you and we use both session cookies and persistent cookies. We also use clear GIFs (aka: web beacons), JavaScript and Log File Information that may collect and store your IP address. All of this stuff is used to facilitate more accurate reporting, improve the carcarekiosk.com experience and help us diagnose and repair problems with our Service. CarCareKiosk may use Google Analytics or Mixpanel to enhance our understanding of use of the Service. Google Analytics and Mixpanel collect information sent by your browser as part of a web page request, including cookies and your IP address. Google Analytics and Mixpanel also receive this information and their use of it is governed by their privacy policies.


Third Party Log In Credentials: By logging into the Site using a set of Third Party Credentials (such as Facebook), you will be giving us access to certain general personally identifying information stored in your profiles. We do not view this information without first obtaining your consent. In some instances we will provide you tools to share our Service with other people in your networks. We encourage, and may record, the frequency of this activity.


HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION


If you submit personal information to carcarekiosk.com, we may use that information to operate, maintain and improve the features and functionality of the Service. Personal information or content that you voluntarily submit to the Service becomes available to the public.


We do not use your email address or other personal information to send commercial or marketing messages without your consent. We will use your email address to send you notices regarding the Service. If you wish to opt out of email communication from carcarekiosk.com, send an email to info "at" carcarekiosk.com with the words "Opt Out of Email" in the subject line. Please note that opting out may prevent you from receiving important messages regarding the Service.


We use cookies, web beacons and log file information to i) store information that allows you to log in automatically the next time you use the Service; ii) provide customized and personalized content; iii) monitor the effectiveness of our marketing efforts; iv) monitor metrics such as total number of visitors and pages viewed; and v) track your submissions.


HOW WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION


When you create an account on our Service, some information about your account and your account activity are provided to other users of the Service. This may include the date you created your account, your member name, the videos you submitted, comments and updates you submitted, your name, location, expertise, certifications, interests and favorite car. Should you choose to submit information about your shop to carcarekiosk.com, we may display your shop name, address and description, the hours, makes, specialties, amenities offered, affiliations, labor rates and hours of operation.


Your carcarekiosk.com user name, not your email address, is displayed to other users of the site when you submit content. Content submitted to our Service may be distributed through the internet and other media and may be viewed by carcarekiosk.com users or the general public.

CarCareKiosk may share your personally identifiable information with third parties solely for the purpose of providing services to you. If this is done, third parties' use of your information will be bound by this Privacy Policy. We may disclose personally identifiable information: i) if we have a good faith belief that doing so is required by a subpoena or other judicial or administrative order or otherwise required by law; ii) to enforce our Terms of Service, take precautions against liability, to investigate and defend ourselves against any third party claims or allegations or to protect the security or integrity of our Service; and iii) to exercise or protects the rights or property of CarCareKiosk or our users.
