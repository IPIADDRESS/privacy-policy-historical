Cars-directory.net, is firmly committed to respecting the privacy of our viewers (people who visit and/or use the site). The following policy explains how your personal information will be treated. Please check back periodically, as this policy may change from time to time.

This site uses cookies in the members area. Cookies and web beacons are also used to monitor site usage patterns.

Cookies can be deleted or blocked through the Privacy preferences of your web browser.

These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
