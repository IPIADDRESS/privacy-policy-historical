CarDekho Privacy & Safeguards Notice

At CarDekho, we value our customers and are committed to protecting your privacy, as well as safeguarding the information we receive and maintain about you. Consequently, you do not need to notify us not to share information about you, because we have chosen to limit this for you. This Notice will help you understand what information we collect, how we use it, and the ways we maintain your privacy and the security of personal information about you.
