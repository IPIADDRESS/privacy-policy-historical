We reserve the right to maintain, update, disclose or otherwise use Anonymous Information without limitation.


Monitoring List

One of the many benefits of using Carrier411 is the ability to monitor carriers (and brokers) your company uses, get custom reports about their status, and be alerted to changes in authority, insurance, safety and performance issues. In order to monitor companies with Carrier411, you need to maintain a list through our Site, which we refer to as your Monitoring List ("Monitoring List").


Carrier411 Services, Inc. treats your Monitoring List as highly confidential information. Accordingly, we neither disclose nor release information about your Monitoring List to customers or any other party, unless required by law. Simply put, your Monitoring List is not available for other Carrier411 customers to access or obtain. The only people who can access information about your Monitoring List are the Carrier411 staff of professionals, and your trusted employees who have been granted access (by your company) to use your Carrier411 account. In a related manner, we trust your company will help guard its account by carefully controlling authorized access. Please restrict access to your Carrier411 account by limiting it to trusted company employees. Should any employee who knows your account username and password leave your company for any reason, we highly encourage you to change your account username and password immediately for security reasons.
