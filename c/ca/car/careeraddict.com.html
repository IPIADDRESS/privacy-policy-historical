Privacy Statement

  • To administer any contest or other promotional offer you may enter and notify winners;
  • To answer any queries which you may send to us by CareerAddict internal mail, external e-mail or our customer support system;
  • In order to conduct customer satisfaction and employment related surveys;
  • To meet our legal compliance obligations;

  • For direct marketing purposes, as set out in detail below.

HOW DO WE USE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

Information you provide or that is obtained by us, will be used by us to enable us to review, develop and improve the Services which we offer and provide you and other Users (via internal mail, external e-mail or otherwise) with information about new products and Services and special offers we think you will find valuable. We may also inform you about new products and services and special offers of selected third parties.

We do not sell, or trade your personal information to third parties, if you have indicated a desire for us to keep the information private. When you use Services, you decide for yourself, how much contact or other personal information you wish to display.

We may give information about you as follows:

  • We may provide our third party service providers and processors with access to your personal information. These services providers may include: credit or debit card verification providers and payment processors; our customer support centre; marketing organizations, who may market and promote Services; internet service providers who administer CareerAddict, provide internet services and host our facilities; consumer research companies that assist us with understanding consumer interests by conducting surveys. We only share your personal information to the extent required for the performance of required by you services. We implemented safeguards to ensure that our service providers treat personal information in a way that is consistent with the terms of this Privacy Statement and that it is never used except to fulfill our Services to our users. We may also disclose your personal information as permitted or required by law. For example, we will disclose personal information to those governmental bodies, who have authority to obtain it, in order to comply with a warrant or subpoena issued by a court of a competent jurisdiction, and to comply with record production requirements.
  • In the event of sale of all or substantially all of the assets of DeltaQuest Media, we may transfer personal information in our control to a third party purchaser that agrees to use personal information for the same reasons identified in this Privacy Statement.
  • To anyone to whom we transfer or may transfer our rights and duties under our Agreement with you.
  • If we have a duty to do so or if the law allows us to do so.
  • To our employees and agents to do any of the above on our behalf, now or in the future.

If you choose not to provide certain personal information we request, you will still be able to visit CareerAddict, but you may be unable to access certain options or Services offered by CareerAddict.


YOUR CONSENT

By choosing to provide us with your personal information you are consenting to its use in accordance with the principles outlined in this Privacy Statement and as outlined at the time you are asked to provide any personal information. You can choose to unsubscribe to these updates or from having your personal information used for market research purposes.


WITHDRAWING YOUR CONSENT

DeltaQuest Media provides you with a way to withdraw your consent for any of the marketing communications received by you via external e-mail or otherwise. If you no longer wish to receive promotional materials you may opt-out of receiving these communications by visiting relevant page on CareerAddict or sending relevant request to our customers support centre. This will remove you from CareerAddict promotions lists. Please note that if you do not consent to use and disclosure of your personal information, this may affect our ability to provide you with particular products and Services.


SECURITY

We will take appropriate steps to protect the personal information you share with us. We have implemented technology and security features to safeguard the privacy of your personal information.


COOKIES

There is a technology called "cookies" which can be used to provide you with tailored information from a web site. A cookie is an element of data that a web site can send to your browser, which may then store it on your system. Some CareerAddict pages may use cookies so that we can better serve you when you return to our sites. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it or not. For more information, please see information readily available on the Internet of "How to work with Cookies".


AGGREGATE DATA

We may aggregate personal information and remove any identifying elements in order to analyze patterns and improve our marketing and promotional efforts, to analyze use of CareerAddict, to improve our content and product offerings, and to customize our content, layout and Services.

We gather certain usage information like the number and frequency of visitors to CareerAddict. This information may include which URL you came from, which URL you next go to, what browser you are using, and your IP address. We only use such data in the aggregate. This collective data helps us to determine how much our customers use certain parts and services of the CareerAddict, and do internal research on our users’ demographics, interests, and behavior to better understand and serve you.


ACCESS TO YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

You have the right to see personal information we keep about you. We will endeavour to provide the information you require within a reasonable time. If you are concerned that any of the information we hold on you is incorrect please login to your Account with CareerAddict and update your information.

Therefore, your information may be processed and stored in these jurisdictions and, as a result, foreign governments, courts, or law enforcement or regulatory agencies may be able to obtain disclosure of your information through the laws in these countries.
