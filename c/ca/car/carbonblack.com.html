Carbon Black does not alter, transmit or interact with any user-generated personal or proprietary information.

 

Cb Basic users will have their logs encrypted and stored separately from other users on our servers. We will not view or interact with your logs without your express permission.

 

Cb Enterprise users will maintain full control over their logs within their own enterprise and will be able to apply whatever protection mechanisms are appropriate based on policy and regulatory requirements.

 

We will keep your use of Carbon Black confidential if you ask us to.

 

We will gather the minimum amount of information we need from you to process orders. We will retain the minimum amount of information we need to provide support and upgrades. We will not sell this information to others.

 

Any payment information you provide is processed by a PCI-compliant third-party. We do not collect or retain any financial or payment (credit card, bank account) information through this site.

 

We use annual self-assessments to assure compliance within the US-EU Safe Harbor Framework as set forth by the U.S. Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use & retention or personal data from the European Union. Our privacy policy serves as our statement of adherence to Safe Harbor Principles. Complaints or disputes about the use of your personal information may be brought to our attention by writing to info@getcarbonblack.com or Carbon Black, P.O. Box 2657, Ashburn, VA 20146. We will investigate each issue raised and make a concerted effort to resolve the issue as quickly as possible. If we do not adequately resolve your issue, we agree to third-party dispute resolution via the Better Business Bureau.

 

We may use the contact information we obtain from you to let you know about new offerings or product- or company-related events. We promise not to abuse this privilege.
