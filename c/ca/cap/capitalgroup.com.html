Privacy Policy - Job Applicants

A. INTRODUCTION

The Capital Group is committed to ensuring the privacy of your "Personal Information" (the identifiable information about you which you provide to us as an applicant for a position with Capital). Your Personal Information will be used for recruitment purposes and protected in accordance with these Guidelines.

These Guidelines may be amended by the Capital Group from time to time.

These Guidelines will not form part of any contract of employment offered to successful candidates.

B. DISCLOSURE WITHIN THE CAPITAL GROUP COMPANIES

To enable the Capital Group to act as an integrated global business, your Personal Information may be transferred to Capital-affiliated companies outside the country where you reside, including to companies in countries where your privacy rights may not be protected as extensively as in your home country. We will, however, endeavor to ensure that adequate security measures are taken.

C. SECURITY

The Capital Group seeks to ensure adequate security to protect processing of Personal Information and to prevent its misuse.

The Capital Group also ensures that third parties processing Personal Information on its behalf take adequate security measures.

D. ACCESS TO PERSONAL INFORMATION

If your Personal Information is held in an electronic database, you have the right to see the Personal Information that the Capital Group holds about you. If you would like to view or correct your Personal Information, discuss the outcome of your job application, withdraw your consent or if you have any questions about these Guidelines, you should send a request in writing to:
