Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting! Capezio wants to assure you that your privacy is safe at Capezio.com. This Privacy Policy covers the treatment of personal information that this site gathers when you access or use the Capezio.com online store. For more details please read below.

 

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Our visitors - Automatic Data Collection

Like most websites, Capezio.com collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Capezio's purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how visitors use this website. From time to time, Capezio may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of this website.

Capezio also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses. Capezio does not use such information to identify visitors, however, and does not disclose such information, other than under the same circumstances that Capezio uses and discloses personally-identifying information, as described below.

Our customers - Information You Provide to Us

Certain actions on the Capezio.com website do require us to collect personally-indentifying information. Purchasers are asked to provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to process transactions. In each case, Capezio collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor's interaction with the Capezio company. We will never disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Aggregated Statistics

Capezio may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. For instance, Capezio may monitor the most popular sections or products on the site. Capezio may display this non-personally-identifying information publicly or provide it to others. However, Capezio does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information

Capezio discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on Capezio's behalf or to provide services requested by the customer, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Some of those employees, contractors and affiliated organizations may be located outside of your home country; by using Capezio.com, you consent to the transfer of such information to them. Capezio will not rent or sell potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information to anyone. Other than to its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations, as described above, Capezio discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only when required to do so by law, or when Capezio believes in good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the property or rights of Capezio, third parties or the public at large. Capezio takes all measures reasonably necessary to protect against the unauthorized access, use, alteration or destruction of potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information.

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Without cookies Capezio can't keep track of the items you've placed in your cart or perform other crucial ecommerce capabilities. Capezio does not use cookies to intrude in your privacy or do anything other than match you up with your information on the Capezio.com site.

 

COMMUNICATIONS

Capezio does not spam. Capezio will not contact you without your permission except as directly related to a specific order you have placed on the Capezio.com site. Acceptable communications include but are not limited to notification of order status and shipment & tracking information.

In the circumstance that Capezio starts a blog or newsletter mailing list to inform our customers of non-order-related information, we will solicit your permission first. In the case that you do subscribe and in the future want to be removed from the Capezio mailing list, Capezio promises to provide a one-click instant removal process.

 

CONDITIONS OF USE

If you decide to visit the Capezio website, your visit and any possible dispute over privacy is subject to this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use, including limitations on damages, arbitration of disputes, and application of New Jersey state law.

 

PRIVACY POLICY CHANGES

Although most changes are likely to be minor and infrequent, Capezio may change its Privacy Policy from time to time, and in our sole discretion. Capezio encourages visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.
