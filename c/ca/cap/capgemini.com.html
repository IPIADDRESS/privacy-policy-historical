For accessing some parts of our site, such as downloading certain documents, Capgemini may ask you to provide some personal information about yourself, by filling out an online form.

The purpose of this form is to gather the information we need in order to process your request, notably to determine who is the nearest Capgemini representative according to your country and the type of request you submit, and how s/he can contact you. The information you provide will not be communicated outside Capgemini to a third party that is not involved in processing your request, unless, in Capgemini’s good faith judgment, required by law or judicial authorities. However, you acknowledge that any information provided by you through the site shall not be considered confidential by Capgemini.

Such information may be copied, used and stored by the relevant Capgemini representatives/units in accordance with local data privacy laws. In accordance with French law, you are hereby informed that answering these questions is not mandatory unless otherwise stated, and that you have a right of access and rectification to the personal data relating to you. If you wish to enforce such rights, please sign-in and modify or delete your profile on our site. Should you have any other question or request regarding our privacy policy, please fill-in this form.

Other ways to contact us are provided on our contact page.

Capgemini may collect certain technical information about your computer and may use “cookies” to facilitate and track your use of our sites. A cookie is an element of data that a site can send to your browser, which may then store it on your system. For example, a cookie can be used to remember your login information if you choose the “Remember me” option while signing in. You are free to decline these cookies by setting your browser accordingly; however, refusing the cookies may prevent you from accessing some parts of the site.
