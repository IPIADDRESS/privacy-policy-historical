Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting our website and taking the time to review our privacy policy. This website will collect no personal information about you unless you specifically choose to provide such information. Some information about your visit to this site may be automatically recorded, but it will not identify you personally. Such information includes:

  • The internet domain and IP address from which you access this website.
  • The type of internet browser (for example, Netscape or Explorer) and the operating system used to access this site.
  • The time and date you visited this site.
  • The pages within this site that you visited.
When you sign our petition, your name and email address will be provided to the sponsoring campaign committees. You may opt out at any time. These organizations may share some basic contact information with allied organizations and campaigns, in accordance with their privacy policies. If you have any specific questions or comments regarding this policy, please contact campaigns@signforgood.com.
