Orca Bay Sports & Entertainment Privacy Policy
From time to time on our website, opportunities are presented to visitors to provide personal information (name, address and other contact or other information specifically referable to the person by whom the information is provided). By providing personal information to www.orcabay.com or www.canucks.com, you agree that the information so provided may be used for the purpose of communication with you to provide information relating to 1) the activities and promotions of Orca Bay Sports & Entertainment, General Motors Place and/or the Vancouver Canucks and 2) to the activities and promotions of those organizations' respective sponsors and suppliers. You further authorize us to provide your personal information to third parties desiring to communicate with you in light of your interest in the activities of General Motors Place or the Vancouver Canucks. You may withdraw your agreement and authorization by e-mailing to us a brief note, including your full name and address, requesting that we remove your personal information from the database.
