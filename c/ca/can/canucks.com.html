NHL Interactive CyberEnterprises, LLC (“NHL ICE”) has a Mobile Privacy Policy describing its data collection and use practices for mobile websites and mobile services operated by or on behalf of NHL ICE.  To view the Privacy Policy, please click here . Among other important provisions, the Mobile Privacy Policy provides:

  • Information Collected.  We may collect a variety of information about you, including:
  • information you provide to us directly,
  • information about your mobile device, carrier, and phone number,
  • information about your location,
  • information about your favorite teams and players, and
  • information about your use of the mobile sites, including information collected through “cookies” and other technology that allows us to remember you.
  • Use of Information.  We may use the information we collect to:
  • provide the services you request,
  • tell you about offers we think you will value,
  • customize the content and advertising you see,
  • improve your experience on our mobile sites, and
  • as otherwise described to you in more detail in the Mobile Privacy Policy [embed link] or at the point of collection. 
  • Disclosure of Information.  We may share your information:
  • with our third-party service providers,
  • within the family of NHL entities and member teams,
  • with business partners consistent with your choices and in accordance with applicable law, 
  • in connection with the sale of all or part of NHL ICE or another NHL business, and
  • to protect our users, in response to legal requests, and as otherwise described in the Mobile Privacy Policy [embed link].
  • Other Provisions.  We will protect the security of the information you share with us, give you the opportunity to access and update your contact information, and provide you with a choice about the use of information about your visit to our site by third parties to display relevant advertisements.  Please see the Mobile Privacy Policy [embed link] for information about these rights and other important provisions.
