Weidmuller Modules

The Power-Feed Unit ACT20-FEED-IN-PRO-S supplies the devices on the CH20M DIN rail bus with 24 V DC. At the same time, the FEED-IN device reads the group error contact - optionally provided by the installed devices - from the CH20M rail bus and sends a message through the status relay to the external controller. Optionally, two power supplies can be connected for the primary and secondary supplies (backup).
