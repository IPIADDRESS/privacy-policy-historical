Privacy Policy

ClickRocket is committed to earning your trust and confidence. This notice explains our online technology and information collection practices in order to better protect your privacy.

Safeguarding your personal information is very important to us. ClickRocket’s security measures are designed to protect the personal information under our control from loss, misuse, or alteration. We keep the servers that store personally identifiable information in a secure physical environment. The personal information that is collected on the ClickRocket website is only used to contact you about your inquires. We do not store or distribute personal information in any other manner. If we are going to use your personally identifiable information in a manner different from that stated at the time of collection, we will first notify you via email; you can decide whether or not we use your information in this different manner. 

ClickRocket may use "cookies" to help customize your access to the website. Cookies are pieces of data stored on your hard drive that contain information about you. They have many benefits that can enhance your experience on our website, for example by saving your log-in password for quick access in the future. While users who do not allow cookies may still use our website, this will limit their access to some areas of our website. We occasionally use cookies to anonymously track and target our users’ interests to further enhance their experience on our website or for advertising tracking purposes.

Visitors should consult the other sites' privacy notices, as we have no control over information that is submitted to, or collected by, these third parties. ClickRocket does not endorse, nor is it responsible for the accuracy of, the privacy policies and/or terms and conditions of advertisers and/or third parties accessible through the ClickRocket website.

This information is subject to the privacy policies of the individual advertisers and is processed by them.
