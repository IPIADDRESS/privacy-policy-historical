Customized popup invites will allow you to send floating chat requests to your site visitor based on several variables. Invites can be customized to trigger based on the following variables:

Online and Offline Graphics
Completely customized online and offline graphics allow you to add live chat images that fit the look and feel of your website. Since the graphics are loaded from our servers, the image will dynamically change between online and offline based on the availability of your operators.

Live Visitor tracking
Whether or not you’re chatting with visitors on your website, you can use ClickAndChat to track all of your website visitors in real time, providing you with an valuable tool to track your site visitors and monitor them in real time as they move between pages on your website.
Operators can then use this tool to send customized floaters to the site visitor’s screen, the image pop ups can be customized to show based on what content the site visitor is actually looking at.
Live visitor tracking provides you with the following real time data for each visitor on your site:

  • Site they’re browsing on (if monitoring multiple websites)
  • Visit number for the day
  • Number of times the visitor has been on your website
  • IP Address
  • Geographical Location
  • Page they’re viewing on your site
  • Number of page views
  • Referral information (how the visitor came to your site)
  • Keyword data (if referred by a search engine)
  • Browsing start time
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
