Our Privacy Policy

This Online Privacy Statement applies solely to: (1) information collected by ClickN KIDS or our "Vendors" via: (a) www.clicknkids.com and all services and webpages available under those ClickN KIDS websites that display, link to, or incorporate by reference this Online Privacy Statement ("Website"), or (b) e-mail, as described in this Online Privacy Statement; and (2) United States visitors to this Website.

Children's Privacy

This Website is intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly solicit data online from, or market online to, children under the age of 13. If we knowingly obtain PII in error on a child under the age of 13, we will delete that information from our systems. ClickN KIDS adheres to the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998 (COPPA) and the guidelines of the Children's Advertising Review Unit (CARU) of the Council of Better Business Bureaus, Inc. in our web site and in all our registration processes.

ClickN KIDS online products require registration to access content and manage your account. These accounts are registered by the parent or legal guardian and personally identifiable information, such as email address, phone number, street address, city, state and zip code are required for account registration. We do not require nor request any personally identifiable information about children for registration or usage of ClickN KIDS online products. All school student accounts for ClickN KIDS online products are created by the student's teacher, and are attached to the teacher's account. The student names entered into the program are chosen solely at the discretion of the teacher.

The Information We Collect

When you visit this Website, we or our Vendors collect information, including PII, that you provide, such as during your registration as a user or your application for a product or service. When browsing this Website, you may choose not to provide us with any PII, such as by not registering.

GLOSSARY OF KEY TERMS

Business Partner - A Business Partner is any third party with which ClickN KIDS conducts business, except that the term Business Partner does not include a Vendor, as defined below.

Clickstream Data - Clickstream Data is information that users generate as they move from page to page and click on items within a website, usually stored in log files. Website designers can use Clickstream Data to improve users' experiences with a website.

Cookie - A Cookie is a small piece of information that a website transfers to the hard drive of a visitor's computer for record-keeping purposes. Cookies provide visitors a personalized experience on this Website by enabling us to determine unique preferences and trends based on visits to this Website and to other websites.

IP Address - An IP Address is a number that is assigned to your computer automatically and required for using the Internet.

Personally Identifiable Information ("PII") - PII is information collected online by ClickN KIDS or by our Vendors that identifies the person to whom such information pertains, including without limitation name, address, telephone number, and e-mail address.

Vendor(s) - A Vendor is any third party that performs business operations on behalf of ClickN KIDS, including without limitation printing, mailing services and other communications services (e.g., e-mail, direct mail, or otherwise), marketing, data processing, customer acquisition and servicing, collections, or advertisement management.

Web Beacon - A Web Beacon, also known as a "clear GIF" (a common format for image files), is an invisible file located on webpages that you visit. They are often used in combination with a Cookie to track navigation of a site. If you visit a page on this Website that contains a Web Beacon, it communicates with your computer to determine, among other things, whether you have been to that page before or viewed a particular advertisement.
