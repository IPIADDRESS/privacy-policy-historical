PRIVACY POLICY

Click2sell (hereinafter may be also referred to as “we”) respects your privacy and is committed to safeguarding privacy of personal information that you provide. This Privacy Policy describes how we treat the information we receive, when you visit or surf our web site without purchasing any product, when you register with Click2sell, and when you use our services for selling or purchasing products. Please read it carefully, as by visiting our web site you are accepting the practices we apply.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT
This Privacy Policy covers personal information and non-personal information, which is collected on an aggregate basis as you browse our web site.

We may collect and store the following personal information:

  1. Information you voluntary give us: we receive and store any information you enter on our web site or give us in any other way, such as email address, physical contact information, (depending on the service used) sometimes financial information, such as credit card or bank account number; transactional information based on your activities  on the web site.

  2. Automatic information: we receive and store certain types of information whenever you interact with us, such as computer sign-on data, statistics on page views, traffic to and from our web site, ad data, IP address and standard web log information.

  3. We also collect and store the above-mentioned information about the visitors of the merchant’s websites whether they access them directly or via the affiliate links.

  4. This is done using standard technology called “cookies”. We use this data on an anonymous basis and we do not correlate this information with personal information of any user. You can disable cookies on your computer by indicating this in the preferences or options menus in your browser. However, if you do that, it is possible, that some parts of our web site will not operate correctly.

  5. Information from other sources: we might receive information about you from third parties and add it to our account information.

USE AND DISCLOSURE OF INFORMATION

Your personal information is collected and processed by Click2sell for the purposes which are strictly connected to the use of the website, its services and to the sale or purchase of products on the merchants’ websites. It may also be used for interrelated operations, within the limits of above mentioned purposes, in particular:

  1. contact you about our programs, products, services;

  2. provide the services and customer support you requested;

  3. customize, measure, and improve our services and our websites' content and layout;

  4. tell you about targeted marketing, service updates, and promotional offers based on your communication preferences; and compare information for accuracy, and verify it with third parties.

We may employ other companies and individuals to perform functions on our behalf. Our employees, agents and contractors who have access to personal information may use it and are required to protect the information in a manner that is consistent with this Privacy Policy.

We may disclose your personal information to respond to legal requirements: it may be disclosed for law enforcement or other governmental officials, in response to a verified request relating to a criminal investigation or alleged illegal activity (for example, it may be disclosed in connection with an investigation of fraud, intellectual property infringement, piracy, or other unlawful activity).

Your personal information shall not be transferred to the third parties, which do not ensure an adequate level of protection of individuals.

We will not share your personal information in ways unrelated to those described above without your explicit consent.

We will not process your personal information for third parties for their direct marketing purposes, also by e-mail, without receiving your consent.

Your personal information shall be stored in a way which allows Click2sell to identify you only for the period strictly necessary for the purposes for which the information was collected and subsequently processed.

CONFIDENTIALITY AND SECURITY OF INFORMATION PROVIDED

The personal information we collect is stored in limited access servers. We have adopted security measures to protect personal information against accidental or unlawful destruction, accidental loss, alteration, unauthorized disclosure or access, and against all other unlawful forms of data processing. However, there‘s no guarantee, that any data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Accordingly, we cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information that you transmit to us, so we do not promise, and you should not expect, that your personal information or private communications will always remain private. It is advisable that your computer is provided with software devices for the protection of network data transmission/receipt and that your Internet services provider has taken appropriate measures for the security of network data transmission.

YOUR RIGHT TO ACCESS PERSONAL INFORMATION AND UPDATE IT

You are entitled to obtain confirmation from Click2sell as to whether or not information relating to you is being processed, whether the information is not yet registered, and the communication in intelligible form of the data undergoing processing.

You are also entitled to receive information on the purposes, way of processing your personal information,  categories of subjects to whom your information may be disclosed.

You may do the following changes to your personal information:

  1. update your personal information if it changes or is inaccurate;

  2. delete, transform to anonymous or block your personal information (in this case we retain your personal information in order to comply with law, prevent fraud, collect any fees owed, resolve disputes, troubleshoot problems, assist with any investigations, and take other actions otherwise permitted by law).

In case you have any concern about privacy of your personal information or think it was collected or used in breach of this Privacy Policy, please do not hesitate to contact us info@click2sell.eu. 

CHANGES AND UPDATES OF PRIVACY POLICY

We may amend or update this Privacy Policy at any time by posting the amended terms on our web site. All amended terms automatically take effect as soon as they are published on the website. Therefore you are requested to regularly check the most recent and updated version of Click2sell Privacy Policy version.
