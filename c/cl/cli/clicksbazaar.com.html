We pledge that Clicks Bazaar will not release your personal data to anyone else without your consent - period. You may change the status of whether you wish to receive Clicks Bazaar offers and communications at any time. Use of traffic data
Each time a visitor comes to a Clicks Bazaar Web site, we collect some information to improve the overall quality of the online experience. At Clicks Bazaar content sites, we collect the visitor's domain name (e.g., whether the user is logged on from google.com or Facebook.com), referral data (e.g., we record the address of the last URL a user visited prior to clicking through to a Clicks Bazaar Web site), and browser and platform type (e.g., a Google Chrome browser on a Macintosh platform). The Clicks Bazaar site collects visitor information for purposes of product monitoring, product improvement and targeted advertising. We also count, track and aggregate the visitor's activity into our analysis of general traffic flows at our sites (e.g. tracking where traffic comes from, how traffic flows within the Clicks Bazaar properties, etc.). To these ends, we may merge information about you into group data, which may then be shared on an aggregated basis with our advertisers; but Clicks Bazaar will not disclose your individual identity or personal data without your permission. When Clicks Bazaar does present aggregated information to outside companies, no one will be able to identify you or contact you.
  
Use of cookies
Clicks Bazaar Web sites may use cookies for internal reporting. Cookies are a standard Internet technology that allow us to both store and retrieve login information on a user's system. Other companies which place advertising on our site also have the ability to assign their cookies to you in a process that Clicks Bazaar does not control.
