Privacy Policy

+ADV srl shall not make use of your personal details without your permission, except where necessary to supply the services expressly requested by you.

+ADV does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including membership forms or email addresses.
 
 +ADV collects information related to user's IP address, referral website, repeat visits (using cookies) and pages viewed for statistical purposes. This data does not contain users personal information such as name, email, etc. and is used to analyze our website logs (such as site hits, page views and referral websites). This helps us to constantly improve the value of the materials available on our website. Our website logs are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site. 

+ADV uses cookies to track the user's input to the website (e.g. logging what polls are voted by the user). It comprises a list of those services which the visitor has used. We also may use cookies to store a visitor's last visit to track returning visitors.
