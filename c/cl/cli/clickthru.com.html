ClickThru.com Privacy Policy

The following Privacy statement details the practices and policies of ClickThru, Inc., owner and operator of ClickThru.com:

ClickThru may collect any or all of the following information:

  • IP address / browser type - used to help diagnose problems, to log user visits, and to administer our Web site.
  • Email Address - Used as a login identity and to provide at least one form of contact. Users may opt-out of receiving any regular mailings
  • Street Address - Provided OPTIONALLY, only if users make a purchase or request something to be mailed.
  • Interest profile - Provided OPTIONALLY. The remote control tool allows users to select favorite categories which are used for default searches and ad targeting
ClickThru, Inc. will never give or sell any of the above information to a third party without prior consent. We use this data to tailor the visitor's experience to content that most closely matches individual interests. This information may only be shared with others in the form of aggregate statistics. Browser "cookies" are required to store login session identifiers.

ClickThru.com serves as a directory of other web sites and is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of Web sites to which we may link.

Security / Financial Information
Any Financial information that is collected is used solely to bill the user for products and services authorized by the user. Credit Card and Bank Account information is collected only over secure SSL Encrypted internet connections. No personal financial information is stored on ClickThru web servers.

Opting-Out
Any ClickThru users may opt-out of receiving regular e-mails by one of the following methods:

  • Adjust online settings at http://www.clickthru.com/options
  • Contact support at: http://www.clickthru.com/messages
This site gives users the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided.
  • Adjust OPTIONAL personal interest profile at http://www.clickthru.com/favorites
  • Contact support at: http://www.clickthru.com/support
