If you do not provide mandatory information for a particular Identification Activity, you will not be permitted to engage in that Identification Activity with the CleverTap Service. Depending on the Identification Activity, WizRocket may not re-ask you for Personally Identifiable Information if such are already stored with us.

We may use Personally Identifiable Information to provide products and/or services to you, administer sweepstakes and contests, enhance the operation of the CleverTap Service and/or the CleverTap Websites, improve our marketing and promotional efforts, analyze use of the CleverTap Service and/or the CleverTap Websites, improve the CleverTap Service and/or the CleverTap Websites, and tailor your experience with third parties as provided below in this Privacy Policy. Such interactions are governed by the applicable privacy policy of such social media feature.

This type of information may include things like the URL of the website you visited before coming to the CleverTapWebsites or otherwise participating in the CleverTap Service, the URL of the website you visit after leaving the CleverTapWebsites, the type of browser you are using, your Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, or general and/or aggregated location data that does constitute Personally Identifiable Information. We use Non-Personally Identifiable Information to troubleshoot, administer the CleverTap Service, analyze trends, gather demographic information, comply with applicable law, and cooperate with law enforcement activities. We may also share this information with our authorized Third Party Service Providers to measure the overall effectiveness of our products and services.

a. Cookies. To facilitate and customize your experience with the CleverTap Service and/or the CleverTap Websites, we may store cookies on your computer or other similar device through which you access the CleverTap Service and/or the CleverTap Websites. A cookie is a small text file that is stored on your computer or other device for record-keeping purposes that contains information about you. We use cookies to save you time while using the CleverTap Service and/or the CleverTap Websites, remind us who you are, and track and target your interests in order to provide a customized experience. Cookies also allow us to collect Non-Personally Identifiable Information from you, like which pages you visited, what links you clicked on, and how you used the CleverTap Service and/or the CleverTap Websites. We may use Third Party Service Providers to display advertisements on the CleverTapWebsites. As part of their service, they may place separate cookies on your computer or similar device. We have no access to or control over these cookies. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by the CleverTap Websites only and does not cover the use of cookies by any Third Party Service Provider. Most browsers automatically accept cookies, however you may be able to modify your browser settings to decline cookies. Please note that if you decline or delete these cookies, some parts of the CleverTapWebsites may not work properly.

We may use other industry standard technologies like pixel tags and web beacons to track your use of the CleverTapWebsites, or we may allow our Third Party Service Providers to use these devices on our behalf. When you access the CleverTap Service and/or the CleverTap Websites, or open or click an email, pixel tags and web beacons generate a Non-Personally Identifiable notice of that action. Pixel tags allow us to measure and improve our understanding of visitor traffic and behavior on the CleverTapWebsites, as well as give us a way to measure our promotions and performance. We may also utilize pixel tags and web beacons provided by our marketing partners for the same purposes.
