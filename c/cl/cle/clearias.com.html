We do not collect or retain any personal information unless you choose to provide it to us. When you visit our website (www.clearias.com), it collects and stores non-personally identifiable information such as: the name of the domain or IP address from which you access the Internet, the date and time you access our website, the browser version used, the device type (laptop or mobile) etc.

We are committed to take steps to protect the personal information we hold from misuse and loss and from unauthorized access, modification or disclosure. If you have any questions now or during your visit, please contact us.

Privacy policy in detail

This Privacy Policy applies to all personal information collected on this site. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable, like your name, address, e-mail address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available.

We will only provide those companies with the personal information they need to deliver the service. They are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information and are prohibited from using that information for any other purpose.

It is your responsibility to review this Privacy Policy from time to time to be aware of any such changes. Your continued use of the web site indicates your agreement to any such changes.
