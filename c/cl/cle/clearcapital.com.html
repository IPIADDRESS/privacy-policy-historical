    b. From transactions with, or from the services being performed by, us, our affiliates, or others;
    c. From our internet web sites;
    d. From the public records maintained by governmental entities; and
    e. From consumer or other reporting agencies;

2. Collects and uses the following types of non-public personal information in compliance with applicable legal requirements:
    a. Personal data such as first name, last name;
    b. Contact data such as email address, telephone number, and physical/mailing address;
    c. Financial account information (only in limited circumstances);

3. Does not disclose any non-public personal information about you except:
    a. to agents, independent contractors or representatives to provide you with requested services;
    b. to third-party contractors or service providers who provide services or perform functions on our behalf;
    c. to law enforcement or regulatory officials when applicable law or regulations require us to do so;
    d. where required by law or judicial process (such as a court order);
    e. to other parties with appropriate consent; or
    f. otherwise to the extent permitted under federal or state privacy laws;

4. Restricts access to non-public personal information to employees who need to know that information to provide services to clients;

5. Maintains physical, electronic and procedural safeguards to guard non-public personal information;

8. Relies on “cookies” (small file sent to your computer through your browser) to help with your user experience when visiting our website. You can choose to accept or decline cookies, but remember that if you decline cookies, it may impact your user experience on our website. Our website does not respond to “Do Not Track” requests enabled through your browser.
