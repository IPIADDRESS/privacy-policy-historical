  1. What is the Purpose of this Privacy Policy?
    Promethean respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.  This ClassFlow Privacy Policy (together with our ClassFlow Terms of Service, ClassFlow Acceptable Use Policy, and any other documents referred to on it) explains how Promethean collects, holds, uses, and discloses any Personal Information that we collect from you through your use of the ClassFlow Service.  “Personal Information” as used in this Privacy Policy means any personally identifiable information about Individual Users of the Service collected for the purposes of providing you the Service.

  1. What Personal Information is collected from Users of this Free Service?
    The Personal Information we collect in the free ClassFlow Service depends on whether you are a teacher or a student:

    a. What Personal Information is collected about Teachers or Other Adults?
    If you are a Teacher or you are 18 years or older, you can register for a free ClassFlow account.  When you register for a ClassFlow account, we collect and store the information that you knowingly enter on the Service, whether via computer, mobile phone, or other wireless device. This information may include Personal Information such as your first and last name, User name, password, e-mail address, school affiliation and location, what grades you teach, what subject matter you teach, and your job title or role.  You should not enter other personal information that is not requested by the Service.

    b. What Personal Information is collected about Students?
    Students are prohibited from registering for an account on the free ClassFlow Service.  If we determine that a Student has registered for a ClassFlow account, we will make that account inaccessible immediately, and we will take reasonable steps to destroy any information in that account securely and as quickly as possible. If you believe that a Student has registered for an account on ClassFlow or may have provided us personal information in violation of this paragraph, please contact us at support@ClassFlow.com.

    Since the free Service does not contain a Student registration page, the only Personal Information we may knowingly collect and store on the free ClassFlow Service about the Student is any Personal Information that is disclosed by the Teacher in any of the Teacher’s lessons created in ClassFlow or when he or she creates a class roster in the Teacher’s ClassFlow account.  (For example, the Teacher uses the Student’s first and last name to identify the Student in that roster).  If the Teacher does not disclose any Personal Information about any Students in the Teacher’s ClassFlow account (for example, the Teacher identifies the Student generically such as “Period 2 Biology Student #1” when creating the class roster), then no Personal Information is collected about a Student in the Teacher’s ClassFlow account.

For more information on FERPA, please see http://www.ed.gov/policy/gen/guid/fpco/ferpa/index.html.

    b. If you are a Teacher located in the United States, you further represent and warrant that you are solely responsible for complying with the Child Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). COPPA applies to the online collection of Personal Information from children under thirteen (13) years of age.  Before disclosing any Personal Information about a child under the age of thirteen (13) years, COPPA requires that you or the educational institution for which you work obtain advance written permission from a parent or guardian.  When obtaining such consent, you must also provide parents/guardians with a copy of or link to this Privacy Policy.  For more information on COPPA, please see ftc.gov/privacy.

    c. Since you are responsible for complying with FERPA and COPPA, if you release or disclose any Personal Information about any of your Students that are under the age of eighteen (18) years in your ClassFlow account (for example, you create a class roster within your ClassFlow account using your Students’ first and last names), then you represent and warrant that you or the educational institution for which you work has obtained advance written permission from the Students’ parents or guardians to disclose that
    Personal Information to ClassFlow. When obtaining such consent, you must also provide parents/guardians with a copy of or link to this Privacy Policy. You must keep all consents on file and provide a copy to us upon our request.  For a sample consent form see classflow.com/sampleparentalconsent.

    d. If you do not disclose any Personal Information about your Students into your ClassFlow account (for example, you create a class roster within your ClassFlow account that does not identify a particular student by first and or last name but instead identifies a student by using generic non-identifying information such as “Period 2 Biology Student No. 1.”), then you are not required to obtain parental consent.

    e. If you are located outside the United States, you are required to comply with the Terms of this Privacy Policy and all regulations and laws governing the privacy of children and children’s Personal Information in your territory. You represent and warrant that you are in compliance with all applicable data protection laws governing the protection of Personal Information and the sharing of student education records.

If you do not obtain the necessary permission, you are not allowed to use the Service. In addition, Personal Information needs to be stored safely on your device (for example, password-protected backup on separate hard disk) to avoid misuse.  Moreover, you have to ensure that only Personal Information of Students collected, used, or stored on your device is duly required to teach your courses.  In the event of uncertainty, please ask the administration of the educational institution for which you are working.  In all matters under this section, you could also contact us at support@ClassFlow.com if you need further information.

    g. If we determine that a Teacher has disclosed a Student’s Personal Information within their ClassFlow account without first obtaining the required parental/guardian consent, we will take reasonable steps to destroy that information securely and as quickly as possible, and we may suspend or terminate the Teacher’s account. If you believe that a Teacher may have provided us Personal Information about a Student or Students in violation of this paragraph please contact us at support@ClassFlow.com.

    h. We do not monitor, review, or exercise any control over the User Submissions (as defined in the ClassFlow Terms of Service) uploaded or shared when using the Service (though we reserve the right to block or take down any User Submissions that we reasonably believe may subject Promethean or our Users to liability) (See the ClassFlow Terms of Service regarding User Submissions, and see the ClassFlow Acceptable Use Policy). Accordingly, we encourage you to avoid including Personal Information about yourself, your Students, or others in your User Submissions, and we encourage you to talk with your Students about not including Personal Information in any resource or material used in connection with this Service.
