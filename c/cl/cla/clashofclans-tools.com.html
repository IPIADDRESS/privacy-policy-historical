Privacy Policy

Clash of Clans Tools is a gaming and entertainment website which exists to further general knowledge and strategies for the mobile game Clash of Clans. We do not and will never sell information provided to this site to any third parties. We do not contact users unless we have requested permission to do so via email.

We do collect visitors statistics such as referral data and content usage in order to optimize our content. This data is anonymous meaning no personal data is stored. Once again we will never sell this data to third parties.

Security
