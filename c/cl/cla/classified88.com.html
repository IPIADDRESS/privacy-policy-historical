Classifieds88 takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.

What This Privacy Policy Covers

  • This policy covers how Classifieds88 treats personal information that Classifieds88 collects and receives, including information related to your past use of Classifieds88 products and services. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable like your name, address, email address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available.
  • This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that Classifieds88 does not own or control, or to people that Classifieds88 does not employ or manage. In addition, certain Classifieds88 associated companies have their own privacy statements.

Information Collection and Use

     General

  • Classifieds88 collects personal information when you register with Classifieds88, when you visit Classifieds88  pages or the pages of certain Classifieds88  partners, Classifieds88 may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies.
  • When you register we ask for information such as your name, email address, birth date, gender, zip code, occupation, industry, and personal interests. Once you register with Classifieds88 and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.
  • Classifieds88 collects information about your transactions with us and with some of our business partners.
  • Classifieds88 automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, Classifieds88 cookie information, and the page you request.
  • Classifieds88 uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

  • Classifieds88 does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:
    • We provide the information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or with Classifieds88 under confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your personal information to help Classifieds88 communicate with you about offers from Classifieds88 and our marketing partners. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information.
    • We respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims;
    • We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of Classifieds88's terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.
    • We transfer information about you if Classifieds88 is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, Classifieds88  will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
  • Classifieds88 displays targeted advertisements based on personal information. Advertisers (including ad serving companies) may assume that people who interact with, view, or click on targeted ads meet the targeting criteria - for example, women ages 18-24 from a particular geographic area.
    • Classifieds88 does not provide any personal information to the advertiser when you interact with or view a targeted ad. However, by interacting with or viewing an ad you are consenting to the possibility that the advertiser will make the assumption that you meet the targeting criteria used to display the ad.
  • Classifieds88 works with vendors, partners, advertisers, and other service providers in different industries and categories of business.

Cookies

  • Classifieds88 may set and access Classifieds88's  cookies on your computer.
  • Classifieds88 lets other companies that show advertisements on some of our pages set and access their cookies on your computer. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to Classifieds88's cookies.
  • Classifieds88 uses web beacons to access Classifieds88 cookies inside and outside our network of web sites and in connection with Classifieds88  products and services.

Confidentiality and Security

  • We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs.

Changes to this Privacy Policy
