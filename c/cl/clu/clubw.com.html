Privacy Policy

Information Collection

For each registered member, we collect the member's e-mail address, IP address, state of residence, and zip code. The information we collect is used to customize the content and/or layout of our page for each individual member and used by us to contact members regarding their account. This information is not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes without the member's consent.

Cookies

We use cookies to store members preferences, record session information, record user-specific information on what pages members access or visit, alert members to new areas that we think might be of interest to them when they return to our site, customize web page content based on members browser type or other information that the member sends.

Changes to Privacy Policy

From time to time, Club W will update its privacy notice. If our information practices change at some time in the future we will post the policy changes to our Web site to notify you of these changes and we will use for these new purposes only data collected from the time of the policy change forward. If you are concerned about how your information is used, you should check back at our Web site periodically.

Email Opt Out

Registered members who have elected to receive Club W's email newsletter about wine deals can choose to opt-out at any time by clicking on the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email newsletter. Members can also opt out from Club W's newsletter emails from the Account section. Further questions or comments regarding privacy can be directed to support@clubw.com.
