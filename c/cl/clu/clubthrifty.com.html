Privacy Policy

Personal Information Collected on this Website
No personally-identifiable information is collected on this website from persons that browse our website unless it is provided by the user. General aggregate information (e.g., total number of subscribers, etc.) may be disclosed.

We use this anonymous information to help improve the content of this site and to compile aggregate statistics about individuals using our site for internal market research purposes. We do not combine Google Analytics info with any personally identifiable information. Google Analytics does place a permanent cookie on your web browser which identifies you as a unique user the next time you visit this site. This cookie cannot be used by anyone but Google. Google is restricted in its ability to use and share information collected by Google Analytics by the Google Analytics Terms of Use and the Google Privacy Policy. You may disable cookies on your browser if you wish to prevent Google Analytics from recognizing you on return visits to this site.

Additionally, we collect data about how people access our RSS feeds, but that data does not include any personally identifiable information about our subscribers.
