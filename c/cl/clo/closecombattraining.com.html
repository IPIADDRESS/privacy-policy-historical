Close Combat Company, LLC Privacy & Security Policy

Welcome to www.closecombattraining.com (hereafter referred to as "Site").

We understand that privacy online is important to users of our Site, especially when conducting business. This statement governs our privacy policies with respect to those users of the Site who visit without transacting business ("Visitors") and Visitors who register to transact business on the Site ("Authorized Customers") and who make use of the various services offered by the Close Combat Company, LLC (collectively, "Services") .

What information does the Site collect from our users and how do we collect it?
The Site only contacts information from people who have signed up to receive our free newsletters or information, or who have purchased one of our products. The Site collects personally identifying information from our users during online registration and online purchasing. This information includes name and e-mail address for registration to receive our free newsletters and name, postal address, and credit card information when purchasing our products. All of this information is provided to us voluntarily by you.

How do we use this information?
This information is used ONLY for internal marketing purposes only. The Site does NOT sell, exchange, or release ANY of your personal information (name, e-mail address, mailing address, etc.)  Your information is not shared with ANY other third party or company outside of for billing and shipping purposes.

How do we store your information?
Your information is stored on a secure CRM service the Site uses to deliver the Site's newsletters. Your information can only be accessed by those people who are directly involved in delivering these newsletters, or who work with or for the Close Combat Company, LLC.

What choices are available to Visitors regarding collection, use and distribution of the information?
ALL of the informational and promotional e-mails that are sent to you by the Site include an unsubscribe link in them.  You can remove yourself at any time from these newsletters by clicking on this unsubscribe link.
