Privacy policy

Privacy and Confidentiality

CloudMe is committed to protecting your privacy. We fully adhere to the principles of integrity and trust, and respect your right to the protection of any personal information you may have entrusted to us in the course of using this web site. When your personal information is collected, it is collected with your express or clearly implied consent. It is not sold or leased, and is not retained for purposes other than those for which it was intended. To ensure this commitment, CloudMe agrees to comply in all material respects with applicable privacy laws, rules and regulations. CloudMe newsletter: The information you provide when you signup to stay up to date with CloudMe's worldwide launch process is used to provide you with our email newsletter. Your information is stored in a secure environment and will not be shared outside of CloudMe, its published partners and affiliates.

Cookies

Cookies are small bites of data stored on your computer. Our web site, similar to most other web sites, uses cookies to allow us to deliver content tailored to your preferences. You may set your browser to notify you when a cookie is received, giving you the option to either accept or reject it.

Data Security and Integrity

CloudMe uses commercially reasonable safeguards and technology that complies in all material respects with applicable laws and industry standards to guard personal information against loss, destruction, misuse, improper disclosure and unauthorized access or modification. We make all reasonable efforts to ensure that the personal information we collect is not altered in any way, or reprocessed in a way that makes it incompatible with the purpose for which it was originally collected.

Access to data

Upon request, you may have private and secure access to the personal information you have provided, and if upon review it is found to be incorrect or incomplete, you may update, modify or delete your information.

Enforcement

Please email us at support{at}cloudme.com with questions or comments you may have regarding our privacy policy.

Changes to this Customer Privacy Policy

CloudMe reserves the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of modifications through this web site. Please check back for updates and changes.
