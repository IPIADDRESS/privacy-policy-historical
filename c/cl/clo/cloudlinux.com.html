Cloud Linux, Inc. (“Cloud Linux”) respects the privacy of anyone who uses the Cloud Linux website (www.cloudlinux.com).


Cloud Linux gathers important information from our visitors and users. Cloud Linux publishes this Privacy Policy to communicate how Cloud Linux gathers and uses information about you, how Cloud Linux protects it, and how you can participate in its protection.


Cloud Linux provides its services to you subject to the Cloud Linux License Agreement and this Privacy Policy. Cloud Linux reserves the right to change this Privacy Policy from time to time. If Cloud Linux makes changes, the revised Privacy Policy will be posted on this website. A notice will be posted on Cloud Linux’s website for 30 days wherever this Privacy Policy is changed in a material way.


By visiting this website, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy.


Information Collected


This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected by Cloud Linux, including personal information. “Personal information” is data that reasonably can be used to identify or describe an individual.


Cloud Linux collects personal information when you:


(1) create a user account;

(2) make online purchasers;

(3) register products online;

(4) request support for Cloud Linux products;

(5) request information or materials from Cloud Linux;

(6) participate in surveys and evaluations;

(7) submit questions or comments to Cloud Linux.


The types of personal information collected may include your name and/or your company’s name; your home, billing, or other physical address; your email address; your telephone number; information that permits Cloud Linux to make physical or online contact with you; your Cloud Linux username and password; credit card account information; and any information that Cloud Linux collects online from you and maintains in association with your account.


Use of Personal Information


Cloud Linux uses the personal information you provide to:


(1) create and maintain your accounts;

(2) process, fulfill, and follow-up on your orders;

(3) register your products;

(4) answer your questions;

(5) send you information; and

(6) send you surveys.


We also use this personal information to provide you with information related to your account and the products or services you purchased from us, to better understand your needs and interests, to improve our service, and to personalize communications.


Sharing Your Personal Information


Unless you consent, Cloud Linux will not share the personal information you provide except as follows:


  1. as may be required to fulfill orders, to conduct billing/credit card processing, to provide service, and for email housing (as a consequence of uses already described in this Privacy Statement;

  2. as required by law (such as responding to a valid subpoena, warrant, audit, or action or to prevent fraud);

  3. for research activities, including the production of statistical reports (such aggregated information is used to describe our services to current and prospective partners and other third parties, and is not used to contact the subjects of the report); and

  4. in the context of a business transaction, such as a merger, acquisition, consolidation, or divestiture (subject to a confidentiality agreement under which the recipient agrees to use the information for no purpose other than carrying out the transaction).


Your Choices about Receiving Email


Cloud Linux makes every effort to provide you with the ability to opt-in and opt-out of receiving communications from Cloud Linux and/or its partners.


Cloud Linux may send you email about your account, about the products you have purchased, or in response to your questions. Cloud Linux may also send you email with information and/or special offers about products and services that may be of interest to you, unless you indicate that you do not want to receive such information. In addition, with your consent, our business partners may send you such information.


You may let us know whether or not you would like to receive information, special offers, and promotional materials from Cloud Linux or our business partners when you create an account, when you register for a services, when you provide us with your personal information, or when we send you email.


You may opt-out of any of Cloud Linux’s direct marketing communications at any time. All promotional email sent from Cloud Linux will contain an option to decline receiving future promotional email.


Cookies and Other Browser Information


Cookies are files web browsers place on a computer’s hard drive. They are used to help Cloud Linux authenticate clients and provide timesaving shortcuts and preferences. Cloud Linux uses a persistent encrypted cookie, if the client requests, to save and retrieve individual authentication information as well as other client device preferences. When a visitor follows a link to the Cloud Linux website from a partner or promotion, Cloud Linux stores information about that partner or promotion in a session cookie. Acceptance of these cookies is optional. Cloud Linux issues a mandatory session cookie to each user of services only to record encrypted authentication information for the duration of a specific session. If you reject this cookie, access to and usage of the services will be denied.


Cloud Linux’s Commitment to Data Security


Cloud Linux has security measures in place to help protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of data under its control. Our website uses Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology, which encrypts your personal information when you send your personal information on our website. In addition, Cloud Linux enters into confidentiality agreements which require that care and precaution to be taken to prevent loss, misuse, or disclosure of your personal information.


Third Party Sites


Cloud Linux’s website contains links to other websites. Cloud Linux is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these other websites. You must check the policy statement of these other websites to understand their policies. If you access a linked site, you may be disclosing your private information. It is your responsibility to keep such information private and confidential.


How to Access, Modify or Update Your Information


Cloud Linux gives you the ability to access, modify or update your information at any time. You may log in and make changes to your login information (change your password), your contact information, your general preferences and your personalization settings. If necessary, you may also contact us and describe the changes you want made to the information you have previously provided.


Additional Information


Questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the security measures taken by Cloud Linux should be directed to Cloud Linux by emailing info@cloudlinux.com.
