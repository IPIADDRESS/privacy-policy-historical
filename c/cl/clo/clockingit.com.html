We collect personally identifiable information, like names, postal addresses, email addresses, etc., when voluntarily submitted by our visitors. Cookies
When you visit ClockingIT, we send one or more cookies - a small file containing a string of characters - to your computer that uniquely identifies your browser. We use the cookies to improve the quality of our service by storing user preferences and temporary selections, like your active View or filters. Cookies are required to use our services, but they hold no personal information.

The information is not provided to these companies for marketing purposes. All emails and newsletters from this site allow you to opt out of further mailings.

We take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of data.
