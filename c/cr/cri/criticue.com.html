Privacy Policy

In plain English

  1. We collect email addresses you may voluntarily provide but do not share them with anyone. They are used solely to improve your user experience.
  2. We will never send spam, the only information you'll ever receive relates to Criticue.com and your activity on this site.
  3. Reviews and comments your website receives are not shared with anyone without your express consent.
  4. Reviews and comments you post about other websites may be shared with anyone.
  5. Reviews are anonymous.

In case any above changes we'll be sure to ask for your consent.
