Crowdtilt.com Privacy Policy

Crowdtilt.com takes your privacy very seriously.

Crowdtilt.com has adopted this privacy guideline for the entire Crowdtilt.com Website and Crowdtilt.com's other products, services or any other features, technologies or functionalities offered by Crowdtilt.com on the Sites or through any other means.

Although we try to link only to Websites that share our respect for customer and visitor information privacy, Crowdtilt.com is not responsible for the content or the privacy policies of Websites to which it may provide links.

Crowdtilt.com encourages users of its Website to review the privacy policies of any other sites accessed by those users. Below is information on what information may be gathered through the Crowdtilt.com Website.

Anonymous Information: Crowdtilt.com may gather anonymous information which does not personally identify you, but which may be helpful for improving the services it offers. Generally this information is collected through "traffic data" and may entail the use of "cookies," "IP addresses," or other numeric codes used to identify a computer.

Cookies: Like many other Websites, Crowdtilt.com may employ cookies to recognize you and your access privileges on certain sites as well as to track site usage. Cookies allow the Crowdtilt.com server to recognize you when you visit our Website in the future. You may be able to configure your web browser to not accept cookies or to notify you when a Website is attempting to send you a cookie.

IP Addresses: The IP address uniquely identifies computers on the Internet. Crowdtilt.com logs IP addresses primarily for systems administration and troubleshooting purposes. Crowdtilt.com may also use IP addresses to better understand how visitors travel to and through the Crowdtilt.com Website to help improve Website structure, flow and content, among other purposes.

Registration: The Crowdtilt.com Website may allow registration. When you register to receive services from the Crowdtilt.com Website or fill out a form to interact with Crowdtilt.com, we may require that you supply personal information such as your full name, street address, e-mail address, unique subscriber ID, telephone number, credit card information, bank account and routing numbers, and other relevant contact information.

Crowdtilt may also require you to provide additional information that we can use to verify your identity, such as your date of birth, Social Security number or other information. Finally, we may obtain information about you from other sources, including third party websites or applications that offer the Balanced Services, and combine that with information we collect on our Sites and through our Services.

When you visit the Crowdtilt.com Website, we will track your site usage, including the pages you visit and other information that may help the Crowdtilt.com improve service to you. The method we use of tracking the pages you visit is your IP address or similar or related technologies. The Crowdtilt.com Website may allow for online resume submission. Personal information collected from resumes will be used solely for the purpose of accepting and evaluating employee applicant submissions. This information may be shared within Crowdtilt.com or appropriate affiliates regarding employment evaluation.

Using the e-mail addresses provided at registration, Crowdtilt.com may choose to periodically send e-mails to its Website users. Crowdtilt.com may contact you regarding account status, for business purposes, and to confirm registration. Upon request, Crowdtilt.com will (a) delete your user account, thereby canceling your registration; and (b) correct personal information that you state is erroneous. Please be sure to include in your request the name of the site at which you registered and the e-mail address you used to register so that we can verify your request.

Data Security: We have put in place physical, electronic and managerial procedures to protect the information we collect online. Keep in mind that no security system is foolproof; therefore, you should exercise care and good judgment when transmitting data over the internet or filling out forms for any website.

Does Crowdtilt.com share the information it gathers and/or tracks?

We do not release personal information about you as an individual to third parties, except when we have your explicit permission or under special circumstances. Crowdtilt.com will provide you all of your personal information provided at registration upon request. This information will only be sent to the e-mail address on file for the subscriber ID associated with it.
