Privacy Policy

Crown & Caliber, LLC has created this privacy statement (“Privacy Policy”) in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy.  This Privacy Policy is to inform you about the information that Crown & Caliber, LLC (“we,” “our” or “us”) may gather about you.

Personal Information We Collect

We collect information from our users at several different points on our website only for internal use for purposes to market our services.  When you complete the form on our web site, we collect your name, address, phone number and email address so we can provide you with requested products, items and/or services.  We also track the traffic on our website in order to compile overall statistics to help us determine how to best service our clients and improve our performance.  This information is not recorded at an individual level.  Cookies may be used to track the pages that you visit during each website session.  We do not link cookies to personal identifiable information you submit to our web site.  Please note that you may set your browser to reject any cookies.

Crown & Caliber, LLC is the sole owner of all collected information on this site.

Disclosure of Personal Information

Except as described in this Privacy Policy, the personal information that we collect will not be disclosed to third parties unless we obtain your prior permission to do so. However, we will from time to time disclose personal information to our employees, contractors, affiliates (including potential third-party purchasers of any items sent to us), and suppliers who process this information on our behalf or participate with us in the provision or operation of this website. We will also disclose personal information if required to do so by law, in response to a subpoena or court order, or if we determine, in our sole discretion, that disclosure of personal information is reasonably prudent to protect us against loss or damage.  We do not sell, rent, trade, share or otherwise disclose any information to third party companies in ways different from what is disclosed in this Privacy Policy.

Marketing Practices/Opt-Out

From time to time we may contact you about our services that we think might be of interest to you. If your information changes or if you no longer want to receive  promotional materials relating to services from Crown & Caliber, LLC or third parties with whom we have shared your personal information with your consent, you can change, update or delete your personally identifiable information at any time by emailing us at info@crownandcaliber.com or by calling us at (800) 514.3750.

Policy Changes

Crown & Caliber, LLC reserves the right to modify privacy policy, and any of our policies or procedures concerning the usage of information gathered through the site, without prior notice. Use of the site following such changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy then in a fact. We recommend that you periodically review this page to be informed of our current policies to protect your privacy.
