PRIVACY POLICY

Crowd Science, Inc. (“Crowd Science”) is committed to following all applicable privacy laws and regulations and keeping you informed of how it might use any information it collects or otherwise receives about you. The following Privacy Policy is part of that commitment.

PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION

No personally identifiable information is automatically collected from visitors to this website. In the event that Crowd Science requests any such information from you, it will only be collected if you provide it on a voluntary basis, and Crowd Science will alert you in advance of the use to which such information will be put and to whom it may be provided.

NON-PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION

Certain non-personal information (such as the domain name of the visitor’s Internet service provider) is collected in the normal operation of Crowd Science’s Internet servers. This information is primarily used for internal purposes, and only in non-personally identifiable form. However, Crowd Science may, from time to time, provide this information in aggregated, non-personally identifiable form to other parties for marketing, advertising or other use.

THIRD PARTY WEBSITES

The Crowd Science website may contain links to certain other websites which enable you to visit other sites through your internet browser. Crowd Science is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of any other websites. Before interacting with any website, you should be sure you are comfortable with its privacy practices.

COPPA COMPLIANCE

Activities on this site are carried out in compliance with the Federal Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act. In accordance with the requirements of that Act, no personal information (including name, mailing address, telephone number and email address) will be collected from any visitor under the age of 13 without first obtaining verifiable consent from the parent or legal guardian of the child. Accordingly, no information should be submitted to or posted on this site by visitors under the age of 13 without the consent of their parents or legal guardian. Parents or legal guardians may consent to the provision of personal information from their children under the age of 13, request to review information collected from their children under the age of 13, may ask to have that information deleted, and may refuse to allow further collection or use of any child’s information. In order to take any such actions, parents/legal guardians should contact Crowd Science as specified below under “Contact Us”.

ACCEPTANCE OF PRIVACY POLICY

By using this site, you signify your assent to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to the terms of this policy, please do not use this website. Crowd Science reserves the right, at its discretion, to change, modify, add, or remove portions of this Privacy Policy at any time. Please check this page periodically for changes. Your continued use of this website following the posting of changes to these terms will mean that you accept those changes.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may email us at privacy@crowdscience.com or write to Privacy Policy, Crowd Science, Inc., 1955 Landings Drive, Mountain View, 94043.

Note to survey participants: This Privacy Policy does not apply to participants in our marketing surveys or the information that is gathered from them. Use and handling of all such information are governed by Crowd Science’s Policies Regarding Collection, Protection and Use of Individual Survey Data, which is presented to and accepted by each individual survey participant.
