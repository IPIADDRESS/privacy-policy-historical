Privacy Policy

360 Quote LLC is committed to protecting the privacy of your personal information. We take your privacy seriously and have established this privacy statement to disclose our information practices. This privacy statement applies exclusively to the Website, www.CreditCardChaser.com.

Routine Information Collection

All web servers track basic information about their visitors. This information includes, but is not limited to, IP addresses, browser details, timestamps and referring pages. None of this information can personally identify specific visitors to this site. The information is tracked for routine administration and maintenance purposes.

Visitors to our Website can browse while remaining completely anonymous. When a user elects to contact us with an inquiry, we collect their name and email information and phone number. We use this collected personal information exclusively to respond to users with information about their inquiry. We do not store this personal information for any other purposes, promotional or otherwise, nor do we share it with third parties for their promotional purposes.

We do not send unsolicited messages to users of our website nor do we store the personal information provided to us on contact forms beyond responding to your inquiry.
