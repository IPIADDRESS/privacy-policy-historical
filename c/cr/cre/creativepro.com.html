What personally identifiable information does creativepro.com collect from me?

Some personal information is gathered when you register for membership or shop at the creativeproShop. In addition to registration we may ask you for personal information at other times, including (but not limited to) when you enter a contest or promotion sponsored by creativepro.com and/or our partners; when you make a purchase at the creativeproShop; when you submit a listing for a Web site; and if you report a problem with our site or one of our services. Wherever creativepro.com collects personal information we make an effort to include a link to this Privacy Policy on that page.


What are cookies and how does creativepro.com use them?

A cookie is a small data file that certain Web sites write to your hard drive when you visit them. A cookie file can contain information such as a user ID that the site uses to track the pages you've visited. But the only personal information a cookie can contain is information you supply yourself. A cookie can't read data off your hard disk or read cookie files created by other sites. Creativepro.com uses cookies to track user traffic patterns (as described above), when you register as a creativepro.com member, and when you click an ad:

Creativepro.com uses cookies to store and sometimes track information about you. Anywhere you are prompted to log-in or that is customizable requires that you accept cookies.

Generally, we use cookies for:
  1. Member Registration: when you register creativepro.com uses a cookie to store a unique, random user ID. We do not store passwords or any personal information about you. We use this ID to identify you anonymously in our database and to track the pages you visit on our site. Cookies also enable you to visit member-restricted areas of the sites.
  2. Ads: Creativepro.com's advertising system delivers a one-time cookie to better track ad impressions and click rates.

If you've set your browser to warn you before accepting cookies, you will receive the warning message with each cookie.

You can refuse cookies by turning them off in your browser. You do not need to have cookies turned on to use the creativepro.com site. However, you do need cookies to log in as a member or participate actively in our forums.
