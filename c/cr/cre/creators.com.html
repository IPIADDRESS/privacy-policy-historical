Privacy Policy

Home > Privacy Policy
We at Creators.com respect our visitors' right to privacy, and we understand that our visitors are concerned about maintaining the privacy of their personal information. Creators.com is committed to protecting your privacy; we do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others.

How will Creators.com use my personal information?

We will collect your personal information only when you voluntarily provide it. All voluntary submission of information to this website will become the property of Creators Syndicate. Creators.com is the sole owner of any information collected on any portion of the website. This information will only be used to provide services you have requested, such as email alerts or sending content to a friend.

We may disclose personally identifiable information to third parties when required by law or in the event such information is relevant to judicial or government investigations or proceedings, or otherwise necessary to protect our website or the safety of its users. Finally, Creators.com may also use information obtained from cookies to perform statistical analyses of aggregate user behavior, but personally identifiable information will not be used in that instance. We may use that information for internal purposes to improve content and design, and share it, in aggregate only, with partners.

If you do not want your submitted information to be become the property of Creators.com or to be used in any of the manners stated above, please do not submit your information to this website.

E-mail delivery:

If you register with our site requesting email updates when a portion of the site is updated, we may contact you at this email address from time to time with informative messages about our services that we believe may be of interest to you, such as new features or added content. If you wish to email a friend to invite them to view content on Creators.com, we will ask you for the friend's name and email address. Creators.com will then send a one-time email to this address inviting them to visit the page that you selected. Creators.com may store this information for the sole purpose of sending this one-time email. The friend may contact Creators.com at any time to request the removal of their information from the database.

Movement through the site:

Creators.com uses server logs to track the path of visitors to our site. We use this information solely to improve visitors' experience on our site and to enhance the site's navigability and ease of use.

Links to other sites:

Creators.com contains links, including advertisements, to websites not related to or affiliated with Creators.com or Creators Syndicate. Creators.com is not responsible for the privacy policies of these outside websites, nor is Creators.com responsible for the content of these sites.

Use of Materials from Creators.com:

All materials reproduced on this site remain the property of Creators Syndicate, and are intended for the user's personal use only. Any reproduction, transmission, performance, display or editing of these materials by any means mechanical or electronic without the express written permission of Creators Syndicate is strictly prohibited. If you wish to reproduce any content that appears on this site in any way, you must contact us via a permission request form here (link to permission page). Please note that Far Side® cartoons may not be used in any electronic format, and therefore may not be posted on websites for any reason. For more information about this policy, please see this note from Gary Larson (link to note).
