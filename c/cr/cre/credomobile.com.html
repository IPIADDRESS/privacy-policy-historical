Working Assets, Inc. and its subsidiaries, including CREDO Mobile, Inc. and CREDO Long Distance (“CREDO”) are committed to preserving your privacy and safeguarding your credit card and other sensitive information. The following statement describes our general information-gathering and usage practices.

  1. CREDO follows all state and federal guidelines protecting customer information.
  2. CREDO Mobile customers – Credo uses your social security number for credit verification purposes only.
  3. Credo does not sell, trade or release your e-mail address to outside third parties unless they are named partners on a given project. Credo uses your e-mail address and/or site registration information to offer you special benefits and communicate with you in the future. Credo may also share this information with its vendors and agents in order to provide you with service. Over time we will want to communicate with you via e-mail to inform you about new features and content on our sites, to request your vote on donations distribution (if eligible), and to make you aware of our special bonus offers and other services. You can opt out of receiving any communication from us by following the opt-out instructions in our e-mails. Alternatively, you can opt out of receiving communications from us by visiting the Registration page of this site and unsubscribing from the newsletter subscription information located on the page. It is important to click the "Update Account Information" button to complete your update. You can also modify any other registration information by visiting the Registration page.
  4. CREDO may track information on which site pages are being visited and used so we can make decisions about the design and usage of our sites.
  5. CREDO uses "cookies" to identify return visitors. "Cookies" are bits of information that are stored by your browser on your hard drive. This technology allows you to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our site, and remembers you when you take action on our site. You can turn off the “cookie” function through most standard browsers.
  6. CREDO may provide aggregate information such as the number of users who visit a particular linked site to other parties.
  7. Credo may place small pieces of computer code on our site pages and in our e-mails to facilitate personalized transactions.
  8. Whenever CREDO handles information that you enter on our sites, regardless of where this occurs, we take steps to ensure that your information is treated securely and in accordance with the relevant Terms of Service and this Privacy & Security Policy. Unfortunately, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. As a result, while we strive to protect your personal information, Credo cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us or from our online products or services, and you do so at your own risk. Once we receive your transmission, we make our best effort to ensure its security on our systems.
  9. Our sites link to merchants and information sites. Each of these sites has its own privacy practices and any information you share with these sites is subject to their policies and practices and is independent of CREDO.
  10. Our sites have security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.

top faqs

I just received my new phone. What should I do first?

Your new phone needs to be fully charged before you use it. Plug in your phone and charge the battery for 24 hours.

How much do text messages cost?

You’re charged 15¢ for each text message you send or receive. Or you can login to member services and add a text-messaging bundle to your service and save.

  • For $2.99 per month you get 50 text messages
  • For $4.99 per month you get 300 text messages
  • For $9.99 per month you get unlimited text messages
  • For $24.99 per month you get unlimited text messages for all lines on your family plan

Messages sent or received in excess of your bundle are 10¢ per text. Bundles are offered one per phone line and are not shared among Family Plan lines unless on you are on a family plan bundle.

When do night and weekend minutes apply?

Night and weekend minutes are minutes used Monday-Thursday between 9 p.m. and 7 a.m., and 9 p.m. Friday through 7 a.m. Monday. Call rates are based on the time the call is initiated.

Where can I recycle my old phone?

We’ve included a prepaid shipping label for CollectiveGood in your new phone box. Send them you old cellphone and they’ll refurbish it for reuse, usually by someone in the developing world.
