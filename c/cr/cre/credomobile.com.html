If you want more information, or would like to opt-out of usage of this information by our third party service providers, please click here.

top faqs

I just received my new phone. What should I do first?

If your phone needs to be activated when it arrives, please follow the instructions on your phone's screen. You may begin using your phone as soon as it is activated, but we recommended giving it a full charge first. To view additional information about your new handset, please refer to your phone's user guide.

How much do text messages cost?

You’re charged 20¢ for each text message you send or receive. Or you can login to member services and add a text-messaging bundle to your service and save.

  • For $4.99 per month you get 300 text messages
  • For $14.99 per month you get unlimited text messages
  • For $24.99 per month you get unlimited text messages for all lines on your family plan

Messages sent or received in excess of your bundle are 10¢ per text. Bundles are offered one per phone line and are not shared among Family Plan lines unless on you are on a family plan bundle.

When do night and weekend minutes apply?

Night and weekend minutes are minutes used Monday-Thursday between 9 p.m. and 7 a.m., and 9 p.m. Friday through 7 a.m. Monday. Call rates are based on the time the call is initiated.

Can I recycle my old phone?

Yes. With every new handset, we include a prepaid shipping label to make it easy to send in your old phone for reuse or recycling. Or you can go online to determine the value of your old phone (if any) and choose to get cash back or make a donation to the nonprofit groups CREDO is supporting this year.
