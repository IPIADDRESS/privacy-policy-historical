Do we use cookies?
We use cookies to enhance your experience on our web site. For example, a cookie enables us to remember the region or specific club page you have visited so the next time you return to our site you do not have to select that club or region again. You can set your Internet browser (like Internet Explorer or Netscape) to warn you every time a cookie is sent, or to turn off all cookies. See your browser's Help menu for these instructions. By disabling your cookies you will not have access to some of the features that enhance your user experience on our site.
