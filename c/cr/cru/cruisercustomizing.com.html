Privacy Policy

At CruiserCustomizing.com we recognize the importance of protecting the privacy of you as a user of this Website. This Website does not collect Personal Information about you except for information that you voluntarily provide (for example, when you submit an email to us with comments about our web site or products reviewed on the web site and you do not specifically indicate that the information contained within is to be considered confidential or provide answers to a voluntary readership survey.)
