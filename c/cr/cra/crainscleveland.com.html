We use this information in an aggregate fashion to track access to the Crain’s Products. We use this information for internal purposes to measure the aggregate number of people who view a given newsletter item.  We may also set cookies to your computer to allow subscribers and users to store login information, set personalization and navigate easily through the Crain’s Products. Through the use of additional cookies or technology, we will have the ability to track users by their behavior profile and geographic location for the purpose of displaying advertising that matches their interests and/or specific location. We retain the right to contact a subscriber or registered user regarding account status, orders for products or services, changes to the registration agreement or to enforce our subscription agreement.  We will remove any registrant (and their personal information) from our database pursuant to a request from the registrant, including full name and e-mail address, to customerservice@crainscleveland.com. You may not be able to fully access Crain’s Products or other product if you request removal of your personal information.

      Mobile Consents/Opt-Outs. We may provide you messages, including marketing, via your wireless email address or by short message service (SMS), wireless access protocol (WAP) services or mobile phone service. By providing us with you wireless email address or by signing up for any SMS, WAP or mobile phone service, you consent to receiving services and messages at the address or number you provide for such purposes, and understand that your wireless carrier’s rates apply to these services/messages. You may opt-out of SMS, WAP or mobile services by following the instructions provided in the message.
