Privacy Policy

Crated Support
September 10, 2015 21:08
Follow

Crated respects your privacy. We will never sell your personal information to any third party.

Crated requires some of your information to be collected, stored and used as set out in this Policy. By creating a Crated account or purchasing from Crated.com you consent to the terms of this Policy.

To allow you to use the services provided by Crated, the following information may be required: username, full name, email, billing and payment information, address and phone number. 

To better understand our users, Crated uses cookies to record standard session non-personal information to help us provide a better experience for all users. Non-personal information includes: IP address, browser type, operating system and device. 

If you have any questions or comments about our Privacy Policy, please Contact us.
