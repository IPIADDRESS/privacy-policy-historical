2. EU-U.S. Privacy Shield and Swiss-U.S. Privacy Shield

Code 42 Software, Inc participates in and has certified its compliance with the EU-U.S. Privacy Shield Framework and the Swiss-U.S. Privacy Shield Framework.  We are committed to subjecting all personal data received from European Union (EU) member countries and Switzerland, respectively, in reliance on each Privacy Shield Framework, to the Framework’s applicable Principles.  To learn more about the Privacy Shield Frameworks, and to view our certification, visit the U.S. Department of Commerce’s Privacy Shield List.

Code 42 Software, Inc is responsible for the processing of personal data it receives, under each Privacy Shield Framework, and subsequently transfers to a third party acting as an agent on its behalf.  Code 42 Software, Inc complies with the Privacy Shield Principles for all onward transfers of personal data from the EU and Switzerland, including the onward transfer liability provisions.

With respect to personal data received or transferred pursuant to the Privacy Shield Frameworks, Code 42 Software, Inc is subject to the regulatory enforcement powers of the U.S. Federal Trade Commission. In certain situations, we may be required to disclose personal data in response to lawful requests by public authorities, including to meet national security or law enforcement requirements.

If you have an unresolved privacy or data use concern that we have not addressed satisfactorily, please contact our U.S.-based third party dispute resolution provider (free of charge).

Under certain conditions, more fully described on the Privacy Shield website, you may be entitled to invoke binding arbitration when other dispute resolution procedures have been exhausted.

When you provide us with personal information about your contacts, we will only use this information for the specific reason for which it is provided.

If you are not a Customer or Data Subject but have submitted your personal data through the “contact us” link and wish to have your information updated or inaccuracies deleted, please contact us at privacy@code42.com. We will respond to your email request within 30 days.

If you wish to save your payment method to your account, we allow you to do so. We will retain your information for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide you Products. We will retain and use your information as reasonably necessary to conduct our business, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Moreover, if you would like to access and review your personal data, you should contact that Customer with any such requests. We will cooperate as appropriate with requests from our Customers to assist with such responses.

11. Data Security and Integrity

Code42 has global hosting centers that store and process personal data in the United States, Amsterdam, Ireland, Singapore, and Australia.  From time-to-time and to meet the demands of our business requirements, Code42 may change the location of our data centers and our service providers with whom we contract.  We will notify you of any change as set forth in the terms of your contract with us.  If you are a Customer, please refer to your contract with Code42 to understand where your personal data is being processed.  If you are a Data Subject or End User, please contact our Customer to understand the location in which your data is processed. Code42 takes reasonable precautions to protect personal data from loss, misuse and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction within our data centers and in our other corporate locations. Code42 also makes reasonable efforts to keep personal data accurate, current, complete, and reliable for its intended use.

The security of your personal data is important to us. When you provide us with your personal data, we will encrypt personal data using secure socket layer technology (SSL) or we will protect your personal data using appropriate access controls.

We follow generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal data submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it. However, no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

If you have any questions about security on our Websites or with our Products, you can email us at privacy@code42.com.

Code42 and its partners use cookies or similar technologies to analyze trends, administer the website, track users’ movements around the Websites and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. You can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level, but if you choose to disable cookies, it may limit your use of certain features or functions on our Websites or in our Products.

Google Analytics

The Websites and Products use Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google Inc. (“Google”). Google Analytics uses “cookies,” which are text files placed on your computer, to help the Website analyze how users use the site. The information collected by Google Analytics, including your internet protocol (IP) address, will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States.

Google will use this information on our behalf for the purpose of evaluating your use of the Website and Products, compiling reports on Website activity and providing further services to us relating to Website, internet, and Product usage.

You can prevent your data from being collected by Google Analytics on our Websites or Products by downloading and installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on for your current web browser.

Learn more about Google Analytics’ privacy practices.
