Privacy Policy

Written by Mr. Cable Cutter March 11, 2015

If you’re visiting CutCableToday.com, know that I value your personal privacy. My privacy policy summarizes the types of information that’s collected on our site, as well as how it may be used.

Log Files

This site uses log files, which is a common practice. This may include:

  • ISP and IP addresses
  • time stamps
  • referring pages and exiting pages
  • trends on clicks
  • type of browser
  • tracking of movement
  • demographics

Keep in mind, no data is connected to anything that will pinpoint you personally.

Web Beacons/Cookies

Cookies are used on cutcabletoday.com for storage of certain information about visitors. This can include any of the following:

  • user preferences
  • visited pages
  • browser
  • demographics info
  • misc. info

Ad Partners

Please note that our ad partners and affiliates may make use of cookies and web beacons here too.

Demographics

We recently began tracking demographic information, which again does not allow us to personally identify you. We track this through Google Analytics cookies. You can opt out if you wish through the following: Ads Settings, Ad Settings for mobile apps, or by going to Google Analytics’ currently available opt-outs.

Questions about the Privacy Policy or Getting Rid of Cable?

Have questions about the privacy policy? Perhaps something you don’t quite understand? Or maybe you just need assistance ditching cable once and for all? Email info at cutcabletoday.com and ask–We’ll get back with you ASAP!
