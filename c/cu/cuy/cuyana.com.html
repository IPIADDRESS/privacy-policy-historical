Engineer, Generalist

Do you view writing code as art? Do you believe in developing creative and innovative products? At Cuyana, we need more than just programming monkeys. Our developers are considered a critical part of the design team. Cuyana is more than just another e-commerce site. Our goal is to redefine how customers shop online. Email us!
We are looking for an engineer who loves developing a customer’s experience end to end. Our ideal candidate is an expert at the front end, but can understand and manage the backend when needed. If you are passionate about inventing new technical solutions and growing a business, come join our team. We promise you won’t regret it.

QUALIFICATIONS:

  • An independent thinker who makes technical decisions with the user and product’s best interests
  • Entrepreneurially minded who requires little oversight and meets deadlines
  • Proficiency with Magento, Wordpress, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • Proficiency in one or more common web languages: Java, Ruby, Python, PHP, etc.
  • Significant experience developing web applications
  • Project portfolio with shipped products is a huge plus
