Privacy Policy

OUR COMMITMENT TO YOUR PRIVACY

We at Culips.com respect your right to privacy. We collect only the information necessary for us to register you as a member. The information we collect includes your name, email address, password, and some basic biographical questions to improve the quality of our product. Culips.com is the sole owner of the information collected on its websites. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement

REGISTRATION

Culips.com may be viewed anonymously. However in order to access the Lipservice pages, a user must first complete the registration form which requires contact information (name and email address) and a username and password. This information is used to contact the user about the services on our site for which they have expressed interest. Culips.com also requires the user to provide demographic information such as country, first language spoken, and age group. This information is used to contact the user about the services and products we offer, including products provided by our partners, and it increases the level of service we are able to offer you by giving us an understanding of our demographic and their possible needs.

CONFIDENTIALITY

Any communication or material you transmit to the website by electronic mail or otherwise, including any data, questions, comments, suggestions, or the like is, and will be treated as, non-confidential and non-proprietary.

LINKS

Culips.com has not reviewed all of the sites that can be linked to from Culips.com. Following links to any websites is at your own risk and the fact that there is a link from Culips.com should not be construed as an endorsement of either the linked Site’s content or sponsors.

CHOICE/OPT-OUT/OPT-INKS

Culips.com will never share your information with any other party, without your express consent. This will be requested by a specific email form. By registering with Culips.com, you agree to receive promotional materials via email from Culips.com. Our registration form has an opt-out mechanism for users who register to become a member but don’t want to receive the newsletter. ?
Users who no longer wish to receive our newsletter or promotional materials sent by Culips may opt-out of receiving these communications by emailing us at remove@culips.com.

REVISIONS

Culips.com may at any time revise the terms and conditions herein by updating this Legal Page. You are bound by any such revisions and should therefore periodically visit this page to review the then-current terms and conditions.
