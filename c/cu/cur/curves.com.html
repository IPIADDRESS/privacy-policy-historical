Curves.com "Safe & Secure" On-Line Ordering

Orders placed on-line through Curves.com are taken through a secure, e-commerce server. Even if you've never placed an order on the Internet before, at Curves.com you should feel confident that your credit card number and personal information will be safe & protected. The secure server is invoked several screens after you "Check Out" or "Process Order." You can tell you're in secure mode by seeing that the URL/web address starts with: "https:" instead of "http:". Your browser may pop-up a window asking/telling you when you go in and out of secure mode. When in secure mode, you will see the web address of our e-commerce vendor: DXCart.com.

About "Cookies"

Curves.com uses Cookies only to keep track of items in your Shopping Cart, during your current browsing session. When you click ‘Add to my Shopping Cart’ or another Shopping Cart button, the product information you've selected is written to a very small file on your hard disk in the form of a Cookie. This information is private and is only used by Curves.com for the ordering process.

A Cookie is a very small text file placed on your hard drive by a web-page server. It identifies the products in your Shopping Cart, and cannot be executed as  code or deliver viruses. It is uniquely yours and can only be read by the server that gave it to you. Cookies help you & save you time. Without them, on Curves.com you would not be able to have more than one product in your Shopping Cart.

If you want to control which cookies you accept: You can order your browser to accept all cookies or to alert you every time a cookie is offered. Then you can decide whether to accept one or not. When using Curves.com, you should accept all cookies from Curves.com or DXCart.com, our Shopping Cart vendor. Doing so will allow the Shopping Cart to work properly for you.
