CURRICULUM ASSOCIATES PRIVACY POLICY

PRIVACY POLICY: Curriculum Associates is committed to respecting your privacy. We recognize your need for reasonable control of personally identifiable information that you share with us. Personally identifiable information is any information that is associated with your name. We use your information to better understand your needs and to provide you with better service.

We do not track your use of our website. We will not sell your personally identifiable information to others. We are committed to ensuring the security of your information.

Credit Card Account Information: Curriculum Associates, Inc., does not disclose credit card account information provided by its customers. When customers choose to pay using their credit cards, we submit the information needed to secure payment only to the appropriate clearinghouse.
