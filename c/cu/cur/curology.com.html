Privacy Policy

We do not, under ANY circumstances, reveal, share or sell your contact information to any third party at any time! Period!

Regarding any paid services which have the potential to include personal patient identifying characteristics, please make note of the following. First of all regarding email communication, there is an internal private messaging system on this site. It is more secure than external email communications, which are not secure on the internet at all. Please use the utmost care and limit transmitting Protected Personal Health Information (PHI) as defined by the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 and related regulations.

Regarding any individualized and personalized reports that may be developed for you by special arrangement, based on the information you provide, we prefer as little patient identifying information (PHI) from you as possible.
