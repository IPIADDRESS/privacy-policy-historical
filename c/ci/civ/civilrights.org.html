Privacy Policy

civilrights.org does not sell, rent, exchange or otherwise disclose its mailing lists or information about its site visitors.

Civilrights.org captures limited information about visits to the site, but this information is used only to analyze general traffic patterns (e.g. what pages are most/least popular) and to perform routine system maintenance.

If you choose to submit content to civilrights.org and provide personally identifiable information about yourself, civilrights.org will not use the information for any purpose other than to continue educating you about civil rights issues.

Action alerts on civilrights.org may request zip code information so that you can be matched with your members of Congress. If you are interested in receiving future alerts, you will be asked for your email address so that updates can be sent to you. This information is not used for any other purpose.

civilrights.org also collects email addresses for This Week in Civil Rights (TWICR) a weekly email newsletter. Your email address for the newsletter is not used for any other purpose.
