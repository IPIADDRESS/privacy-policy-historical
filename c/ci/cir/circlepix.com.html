Use of Cookies

On the Internet, a "cookie" is a piece of information (a file) that a Web site transfers to a user's computer for record-keeping purposes. The use of cookies is common on the Internet. Your visits to our websites may be tracked via the use of a "cookie", to enable CirclePix.com LLC to measure the frequency of visits to our websites, to accumulate statistical data pertaining to the activity conducted on our websites, and to measure the effectiveness of promotional content and advertisement. Your personally identifiable information may be stored in part or in whole to prevent the repetitive entering of static data and to enhance your experience with our websites by providing greater ease-of-use. We do not share your personally identifiable information or other information gathered via "cookie" technology with third parties.
