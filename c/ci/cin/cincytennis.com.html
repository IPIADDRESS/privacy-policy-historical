Privacy Policy

Thursday, June 25, 2009 1:31 PM ET
 

The Western & Southern Open (the tournament) is committed to providing appropriate protection for information that is collected from visitors to this website. This Privacy Statement describes the tournament's general principles for the collection and use of this information. The tournament may update this privacy policy from time to time.

Through this site, the tournament may collect information about individuals and companies who are interested in obtaining tickets or services from the tournament. This information may be seen only by the tournament and third party service companies to perform business operations for the tournament or work on the tournament's behalf to provide services. (For example, the addresses of those who buy tickets via the website will be added to our mailing lists, which are often accessed by a third-party company which the tournament employs to assist with mailings. Any third-party company enlisted to assist with mailings must sign a binding confidentiality agreement to keep all information private.)

Please review the privacy policy of any website accessed via a link found on this Website. The tournament has no control over, and is not responsible for, the privacy practices of these linked websites.
