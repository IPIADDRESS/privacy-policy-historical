Privacy Policy

Cinnamon Spice & Everything Nice is committed to maintaining the privacy and protection of your personal information. Information Collected When Leaving Comments:

When you leave comments on Cinnamon Spice & Everything Nice, you are required to provide your name, e-mail and you have the option of providing your blog or website URL. This information becomes a permanent part of the website when you provide it with a comment, and your name and blog or website URL can be seen by other visitors to the site, including anonymous visitors. Your e-mail cannot be seen and will not be shared or sold to any third parties.

We use anonymous information to analyze our site traffic, but we do not examine this information for individually identifying information. Traffic and transaction information may also be shared with advertisers on an aggregate and anonymous basis.

All websites that collect traffic data have the ability to monitor visitor information such as IP addresses, browser type, operating system, location, date/time of visit, and information about which areas of the site are visited. We monitor such information via Google Analytics but do not store it in personal files or link it to individuals.
