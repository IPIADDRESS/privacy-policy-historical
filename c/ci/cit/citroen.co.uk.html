There are sections of the www.citroen.co.uk website (“The site”) that ask you to fill in some of your personal details, such as your name, address, phone number, email address and other information such as your car’s registration number.

Any information you provide will be securely held by Citroën UK Ltd. in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998.
