Privacy Policy

Citation Machine is committed to providing the utmost in privacy security. We do not sell nor trade any personally identifiable information obtained from the site.

Citation Machine does not collect personally identifiable information from anyone without his or her request. All e-mail correspondence is kept in strict privacy and will not be disclosed to parties other than Citation Machine.

In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998 (COPPA), any person under the age of 13 must receive explicit parental permission in order to sign up for any online service where their personal information may be shared, unless that person is part of a subscribing school that provides COPPA consent on behalf of parents.

Citation Machine collects user registration upon sign up. Unless you ask us not to, we may contact you via email in the future to tell you about specials, new products or services, or changes to this privacy policy.
