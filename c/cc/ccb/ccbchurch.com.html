USAGE TRACKING

Customers: Church Community Builder utilizes remarketing via Google AdWords to promote products and services to Customers who have previously visited our sites. We use cookies to collect data for this purpose and data caching to provide a faster user experience. Through AdWords, Google may show Church Community Builder ads on sites across the internet, and may also use cookies to serve ads based on past visits to Church Community Builder websites. Customers may opt out of remarketing by visiting Google’s Ads Settings.

Contacts: Church Community Builder tracks user traffic patterns throughout our Customers’ sites. However, we do not correlate this information with data about individual users. Church Community Builder does break down overall usage statistics such as browser type, operating system, length of time on a site, and IP address. We accomplish this by reading this information from the browser string (information contained in every user’s browser and header information). The collection of IP addresses in log files is used to track users’ movements, analyze data trends, and administer the site.

COOKIES

Church Community Builder may place a text file called a ‘cookie’ in the browser files of a user’s computer. The cookie itself does not contain personal information, although it enables Church Community Builder to relate a user’s use of this site to information that user has specifically and knowingly provided. The only personal information a cookie can contain is information supplied by the user him- or herself. A cookie cannot read data off hard disks or read cookie files created by other sites. Church Community Builder uses cookies to track user traffic patterns (as described above), as well as improve user experiences while on the site.
