CCN.com Privacy Policy

CCN.com recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of information provided to us. Accordingly, we strive for compliance with all federal and state privacy laws and follow industry best practices in order to best serve our community. We consider all subscribers, customers, members, registrants, part of the CCN.com community. The following guidelines have been created to demonstrate our commitment to privacy.

Cookies

We use cookies to store visitors’ preferences, record session information , record user-specific information such as what pages users access or visit, ensure that visitors are not repeatedly sent the same banner ads, customize web page content based on a visitor’s browser type or other information that the visitor sends, and store users names and passwords

Security

CCN.com contains direct links to online applications for all featured credit cards. These applications are taken and stored on the credit card issuer's web servers. CCN.com does not collect and does not have access to any of the personal information from the application forms.

Newsletter

CCN.com offers our newsletter featuring credit card tips and occasional site news. Your email address will never be shared or sold to any third parties.

Discussion Forums

CCN.com provides discussion forums, which allow you to post questions and discuss any credit related issues. Simpy register and you are able to post and participate in the community. This service is free to all of our customers and visitors. 

Policy Updates

From time to time, we may use provided information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy policy. If our information practices change at some time in the future, we will immediately post the policy changes to this Website to notify you of these changes. If you are concerned about how your information is used, bookmark this page and check back periodically
