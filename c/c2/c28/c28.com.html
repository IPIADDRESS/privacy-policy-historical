C28store.com Online Privacy Policy

C28store.com would like to thank you for your support and wants you to know that your privacy is very important to us.

 

The information collected during registration is used to primarily to process and ship your order. Your account information is maintained privately on our secure web servers.

 

C28store.com requires this information be handled securely and responsibly. C28store.com does not sell, share, or rent email addresses.

 

C28store.com does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. Children under the age of 13 should have their parents fill out required registration or catalog information.

At C28store.com we protect the integrity of our customers' credit card information.
We use Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology to protect the security of your credit
card information as it is transmitted to us.
