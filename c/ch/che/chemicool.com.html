About Chemicool

Chemicool was created by David D. Hsu. of the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in 1996. Chemicool went online in 1996 and since then has been trying to help out anyone involved in chemistry. The information for this site was gained with the help from the following people, big thanks goes out to them all: Cabrol D.; Moore J. W and Kotz J. C. Additionally, the Perkin-Elmer Periodic Table v2.02g was used for Atomic mass, density, volume, group, heat of fusion, heat of vaporization, specific heat, and shells.

Since the site's launch we have won some awards. "Ten Cool Sites" for Exploratorium Learning Studio's best in Science, Art, & K-12 sites would be our best achievement so far. We are working harder to get more of these awards, so keep on following our progress.

Privacy

Chemicool is an information site and has no aim in collecting personal information about the users who visit this site. Non-personal information is gathered from each computer that "hits" the site. This information is used to measure traffic to the site, find broken pages and links, etc. This means that cookies are used. These cookies maintain a record of browsing habits across this site and basic information obtained from browsers. No personal information is contained within these cookies. A cookie in no way gives us access to your computer or any other personal information about you. Cookies and web beacons may also be used by companies such as Google serving ads on the site. You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. This may prevent you from taking full advantage of the website. Be rest assured that your privacy and your computers privacy are not invaded when you surf this site. You are welcome to leave any comments about the site, and help us improve.
