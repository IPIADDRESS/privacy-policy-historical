CONSUMER PRIVACY STATEMENT FOR CHECKS UNLIMITED by CURRENT
 
Protecting privacy is not new to Checks Unlimited. Checks Unlimited has a long history of recognizing and protecting the privacy of consumers. With the advent of the Internet, Checks Unlimited is committed to protecting your privacy and utilizing technology that gives you the most powerful, safe, on-line experience available. We want to assure you that when Checks Unlimited asks for consumer information, it's done with the goal of improving our relationship. Checks Unlimited participates in and complies with the Better Business Bureau's (BBB) Privacy Program. More information on the BBB and their Privacy Program is available at http://www.bbbonline.org. The following statement will help you understand how we collect, use and safeguard information you provide through our Web site.

Checks Unlimited deals only with US banking documents so the Web site is limited to US residents only. This policy is effective 8/11/1999 and applies to all consumer identifiable information Checks Unlimited obtains when a consumer visits our Web site.
 
Information Collection and Use
When you browse Checks Unlimited's Web site, you do so without revealing your identity. Personal information, including E-mail addresses, are not collected without your awareness. For each visitor to our Web site, our servers recognize the Web site you came from.

In order to manage risk in the order capture process, Checks Unlimited may track the order preferences of visitors to our Web site. Information tracked and retained by Checks Unlimited is generic and not individually identifiable.
 
Disclosure of Individual Consumer Information
Checks Unlimited limits the collection of consumer information to the minimum we require to meet consumer needs, which includes advising consumers about our products, services and other opportunities, and to administer our business. For purposes of consumer reporting, verification and risk management, Checks Unlimited will exchange information about our consumers with reputable reference sources and clearinghouse services. Checks Unlimited sometimes merges data with outside vendors to determine more accurate demographic consumer profiles. The information we gather may be used to protect consumers, employees and property against fraud, theft or abuse; to conduct industry or consumer surveys; and to maintain good consumer relations.

Checks Unlimited does not share E-mail addresses, but may selectively rent names and mailing addresses (not E-mail addresses) to reputable third parties and provide certain information to other companies or organizations whose products or services we think may interest you. These third parties are carefully screened and are aware of Checks Unlimited's privacy policy. If you do not want the information you provide Checks Unlimited to be shared with other companies for marketing purposes you have the Choice to Opt-Out as described below.
 
Identifying Information You Choose to Provide
At times, Checks Unlimited may request that you voluntarily supply personal information for purposes such as correspondence, site registration, making a purchase, participating in an on-line survey or employment opportunities. If you elect to participate, Checks Unlimited may require personally identifiable information, including your name, mailing address, phone number, bank information, credit card information or E-mail address. When you submit personal information to Checks Unlimited you understand and agree that as allowed by contract Checks Unlimited may access, store and use your information. Information you provide will be safeguarded to strict standards of security and confidentiality. Because Checks Unlimited recognizes and appreciates the importance of responsible use of the information you choose to provide, we do not share E-mail addresses.
 
Security of Information
Security measures are in place on the Checks Unlimited Web site to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. We offer the industry standard security measures available through your browser. Encrypted information is scrambled en route and decoded once it reaches Checks Unlimited. Information (including E-mail) sent to us might not be secure unless we advise you that security measures will be in place prior to your transmission. Therefore, we ask that you do not send confidential information such as social security or account numbers to us via unsecured E-mail. Such communications should be sent to us by postal mail or phone call.

Checks Unlimited requires any vendors or consultants we hire, as well as suppliers and contractors, to observe our Information Practices Policy with respect to security and any of our consumers’ individual consumer information. They must abide by the policies when conducting work for us, and they will be held accountable for their actions.
 
Choice to Opt-Out
Checks Unlimited provides consumers the opportunity to opt-out of (1) unsolicited mail and/or (2) the sharing of information with others. Options available to consumers for removing their information from our database for marketing purposes and not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service are listed below under Contacting Checks Unlimited.
