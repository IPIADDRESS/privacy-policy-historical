PRIVACY and CONFIDENTIALITY


General

  • We at Chem Supply agree to maintain the confidentiality of our client(s) and the business relationship.
  • Chem Supply has a strong commitment to privacy protection and complies with the Privacy Act, 1988, and the National Privacy principles contained in this Act. Detailed Information covering this Act is available at www.privacy.gov.au.
  • We will hold confidential the name(s) of our client(s), information about their business and / or product purchases.
  • Chem Supply will not use information or permit the use of information or transaction details to be used for any other purposes than those intended.
  • Where this website contains links to other sites, we are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of other such sites.


Electronic Information Collected

  • Chem Supply may make a record of your visit to our website for both business and statistical purposes. This record may include your server address, domain name, IP address; date and time of your visit; pages accessed; any documents downloaded from this site (eg MSDS, Chemical specification files), as well as the type of browser used.
  • If you complete any electronic forms on our website, which are subsequently forwarded to us, any personal information provided on such forms is used exclusively to allow us to respond to your request. No information of a personal nature is collected, which could be considered as unlawful, unnecessary or unrelated to the Company's business or activities.
  • If you send us an email message your email address may be recorded, but it will only be used for the purpose for which it was provided, and will not be used for any other purpose, including the addition to mailing lists, without your express approval.
  • Any personal information you have provided to Chem Supply through this site will only be used for the purpose for which you have provided it, and to assist us to conduct our business. This usage may include analysing the information we have obtained in order to assess the site's effectiveness, and to modify the site accordingly to improve the service it provides.


Further Information

Further information relating to Privacy and confidentiality can be found at http://www.privacy.gov.au
