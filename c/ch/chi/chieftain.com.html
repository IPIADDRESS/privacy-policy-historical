Information automatically gathered by our web server
For each visitor to our websites, our web server automatically recognizes only the consumer's domain name, but not the email address (where possible). We collect aggregate and user-specific information on what pages consumers access or visit. This information is used to generate reports that help The Pueblo Chieftain Online assess the value of and interest in the various web sites.

Information gathered by voluntary submissions of users to our web site
We collect the email addresses of those users who post messages to our bulletin board, communicate with us via email and make postings to our chat area. In addition, we collect information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and site registrations.

Use of information by The Pueblo Chieftain Online
The information we collect is used by us to improve the content of our webpage; to customize the content and layout of our page for each individual consumer; and to notify consumers about updates to our website. In addition, this information is used by us contact consumers for marketing purposes.

If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by sending email to us at the above-listed, calling us at the above-listed telephone number or writing to us at the above-listed address. Specify in your request that you do not want to receive postal mailings from our company. Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers online may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding orders they have placed online. They also may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such telephone calls, please let us know by sending email to us at the above-listed address or calling us at the above-listed telephone number or writing to us at the above-listed address. In your request, please provide us with your correct phone number and address and specify that you do not wish to receive telephone calls from our company.

The Pueblo Chieftain does not knowingly allow anyone younger than 13 to register with our services, nor do we seek personal information from them. If you are under the age of 13, please do not provide us with any personal information such as your name, email or phone number, and get permission from your parents before registering with this or any online service.

Information shared with other organizations
From time to time, we make our customer list available to other companies or organizations whose products or services we think you might find interesting. This list can include your email address and, if you have provided it to us online, your postal address and your telephone number. If you do not want us to share your email address, postal address and/or telephone number with other companies or organizations, please let us know by sending email to us at the above-listed address, calling us at the above-listed telephone number or writing to us at the above-listed address. In your request, please specify what information you do not want to be shared, your email address, postal address and/or telephone number, and provide us with your exact name, postal address and telephone number. We will be sure your name is removed from the list we share with other organizations.
