Privacy Policy

This information may include your name, your email address, your city,your IP address, your ISP, the browser you used to visit our site (such as Internet Explorer, Chrome, Safari or Firefox etc), the times you visited our site, the time you spent on our site and which pages you visited throughout our site.

Your personal information will never be released, sold, or rented to any entities or individuals outside Chillopedia.com under any circumstance.
