Privacy & Security Policy

China Odyssey Tours Respects Your Privacy and Protects Your Security and Confidentiality Very Seriously.


Our Privacy Policy Statement

China Odyssey Tours is committed to protecting your privacy.

China Odyssey Tours maintains the confidentiality, security and integrity of personal information obtaining from you.

All personal information you give us remains totally confidential; which will never be sold, traded, shared or passed to any third party without your prior consent unless required by law.

All your personal information is only used to help us arrange your tour in China and fulfill your special requirements for your tour.

All your data collected by China Odyssey Tours is stored in our secure databases, which can be only accessed by certain staff within China Odyssey Tours.


Credit Card Payment Security

Though online payment with your international credit card is not applicable in China yet, you do can pay for your tour with your credit card through Bank of China via its worldwide network and established relationship with leading banking systems worldwide. We don't request you give us your credit card information through internet, instead by paying us, you simply click here, follow the instructions to fill out the form and then send us a fax of it, we guarantee we'll destroy the fax paper having your credit card information after we complete the proper payment procedure. In this case you don't have to worry about the online security for the payment.

China Odyssey Tours reserves the right to modify this Privacy & Security Policy at any time with or without notice.

 

Should you have any questions about our Privacy & Security Policy, please feel free to contact us.
