    Security and Privacy Policy Effective Date: October 21, 2014

This Site is owned, operated and controlled by Charity Buzz, Inc. d/b/a Charitybuzz ("Charitybuzz"). Your privacy is extremely important to Charitybuzz. We have developed this Privacy Policy to cover the information we collect from you, and how we collect, use, disclose, transfer, and store your information. Your use of our Site and services signifies your acceptance of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, you may not use our Site or our services. Throughout this Privacy Policy Charitybuzz is sometimes referred to herein as "we," "us," and "our."

    What is Personally Identifiable Information.

    "Personally Identifiable Information" is information that can be used to uniquely identify a single person. This may include your name or associated company name, email address, postal address, and home or work telephone number. You may be asked to provide your personal information when you register on the Site and any time you are in contact with Charitybuzz. Charitybuzz may combine your personal information with other information to provide and improve our products, services, Site content, promotional materials, business practices, or customer service.

    What Personally Identifiable Information We Collect.

    You are voluntarily and knowingly supplying us with information when you create a Charitybuzz user account, register your credit card, bid on or win Property and/or an Experience through an online auction, donate property and/or an experience for use in connection an auction or other program or register to receive information from Charitybuzz. An "Experience" may include, but is not limited to, a celebrity meet and greet, dinner with a celebrity or other such experience. "Property" is tangible property, including but not limited to artwork, apparel, food, wine, sports and music memorabilia. In order to participate in an auction or other program or to donate Property and/or an Experience you must complete the registration process and some of this information will identify you personally or enable you to be identified personally, either alone or in combination with other information available to us. Such information may include your name, mailing address, phone number, email address, contact preferences, and credit card information.

    When you share your content with family and friends, invite others to join you on Charitybuzz, or bid on, purchase, or win Property and/or an Experience for another person or which you will share with another person, Charitybuzz may collect the information you provide about those people such as name, mailing address, email address, and phone number. Email addresses may be collected via forms on the Site and from time to time other information may be collected via this Site or through an offline manner.

    How We Use Your Personally Identifiable Information.

    The personal information we collect allows us to keep you posted on Charitybuzz's latest auctions and other programs. It helps us to improve our services, content, and advertising. We may use personal information for internal purposes such as auditing, data analysis, and research to improve Charitybuzz's products, services, and customer communications or share with affiliates, partnering organizations, charities, or other third parties. For more information on disclosure to third parties see the section below entitled "Disclosure to Third Parties." In addition, we may aggregate Personal Information in such a way that it is no longer identifiable and we may use this aggregated information for any purpose. At your request and where the law requires us to do so, we will make good faith efforts to confirm what personal information we hold about you, update your information, remove your information, fulfill specific opt-out requests, and/or correct any inaccuracies in such personal information if you contact us at unsubscribe@charitybuzz.com to submit such requests. We may decline to process a request that is unreasonable, jeopardizes the rights or privacy of others, is impractical or for which access is not otherwise required.

    Use of Non-Personally Identifiable Information/Cookies and Other Technologies.

    We also collect non-personally identifiable information in a form that does not permit direct association with any specific individual. We may collect, use, transfer, and disclose non-personally identifiable information for any purpose. Like many websites, our Site may use "cookies" and other similar tracking devices or technologies to collect information that is sent to us automatically by your web browser which will help us understand user behavior, tell us which parts of our Site people have visited, and facilitate and measure the effectiveness of advertisements, web searches and other content. This information typically includes your IP address, the address of the web page you were visiting when you accessed this Site, the name of your operating system (such as Windows or Macintosh), the name and version of your browser (such as Internet Explorer or Safari), and the date and time of your visit, among other things. The information we receive depends on the settings on your web browser. Although most web browsers automatically accept cookies, most computers allow you to change your browser setting to prevent acceptance of cookies. You may set most browsers to notify you if you receive a cookie or you may choose to block cookies with your browser. Please note that if you choose to erase or block your cookies certain features or parts of the Site may not work properly.

    We treat information collected by cookies and other technologies as non-personally identifiable information. However, to the extent that Internet Protocol (IP) addresses or similar identifiers are considered personal information by local law, we will treat these identifiers as personal information. Similarly, to the extent that non-personally identifiable information is combined with personal information, we will treat the combined information as personal information for the purposes of this Privacy Policy.

    Our third party partners may also employ clear gifs (a.k.a. Web Beacons/Web Bugs), images, and scripts that help them better manage content on our site. We do not tie the information gathered to our Customers' or Users' personal information.

You will receive notice when your Personally Identifiable Information will be provided to any third party for any reason other than as set forth in this Privacy Policy, and you will have an opportunity to request that we do not share such information.
