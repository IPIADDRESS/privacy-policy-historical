Privacy Policy

ChaCha Search, Inc. is dedicated to your privacy. This document outlines what information we may collect from you and what we do with that information. ChaCha Search, Inc does not sell, give or otherwise make available to third parties any personally identifying information about you with the exception as noted below and required by law.

We use your IP address for diagnostic and administrative purposes. Your IP address is used to gather broad demographic information on an aggregate basis and to help better target the content of our site to suit your needs and interests.

Employment Section of our Site

In the recruitment of Guides ChaCha Search, Inc., Inc. collects, and disseminates personal information that has been provided through this Site (such as name, address, e-mail address, and information contained in a resume or job profile). By submitting this information, the applicant agrees that it will be governed by our Site's Privacy Policy and will be subject to our Terms of Use. On the basis of the information provided, ChaCha Search, Inc. may contact the applicant regarding employment opportunities. Personal information so provided will not be sold, shared or otherwise distributed to any third parties outside ChaCha Search, Inc. except as necessary for purposes of identity verification. Where disclosure of personal data to a third party is likely or necessary for whatever reason, ChaCha Search, Inc. will, wherever possible, endeavor to ensure that the disclosure and intended use of the data are clearly indicated. Such third parties may have different privacy policies.
