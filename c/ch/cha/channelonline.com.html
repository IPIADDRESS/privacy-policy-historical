User Data

 Collection of information
 CNET Channel is committed to keeping your personal and organizational information private and abides by CNET Networks Privacy Policy. To provide its users with personalized services and custom data, CNET Channel collects registration information and distributors' access information from its users.

 Registration information
 When creating a new account on ChannelOnline, you are required to disclose certain customer registration information. This information includes customer contact information (name, address, phone, fax, e-mail, website, and password) and billing information (credit card information). This information is not disclosed to third parties without your consent. It is used to notify you of any system upgrades, technical support issues or other relevant information.

 Distributor's access information
 ChannelOnline requires you to provide relevant distributor information in order to display custom pricing and availability such as usernames and passwords. Any such distributor information that is particular to you is kept confidential. The information you enter about your customers while using CNET Channel's services is also kept confidential.

 Sharing of information
 CNET Channel takes the privacy of its users seriously. As a consequence, CNET Channel has a strict policy against sharing individual user data with third parties, except in certain select instances. CNET Channel may disclose or exchange user information when you have given your consent, when it is required by law, or, if necessary, to protect the rights or property of CNET Channel or its users.

 In certain other instances, CNET Channel will share user data with a closely integrated partner, like a distributor, in order to provide a service that a user has specifically requested, or provide limited user contact information to potential suppliers who require it in order to make their products available on ChannelOnline. CNET Channel may also obtain user consent in order to share information with individual third parties so as to generally improve its services or allow the promotion of services that may be of interest to you.
 CNET Channel reserves the right to use aggregated data to report user demographics, track sales and traffic patterns and monitor other related trends. This aggregate information may be provided to third parties such as advertisers. In the interest of protecting your privacy, this aggregated data does not contain any personally identifiable information.

 It is important for ChannelOnline users to realize that these provisions apply solely to information collected on this Site. Although the Site contains links to other sites, CNET Channel is not responsible for the privacy practices of other sites. For privacy information regarding other sites, please refer to their individual privacy policies.

 Security
 CNET Channel has security measures in place to protect against the loss, misuse and unauthorized alteration of the information under its control.

 Any and all of your confidential transactions, between you and CNET Channel, are protected within Secure Socket Layer (SSL), the industry standard protocol for maintaining security. ChannelOnline supports up to 128-bit encryption, the strongest available encryption used through a web-browser.

 CNET Channel uses encrypted "Cookies" (bits of data that can be placed on a user's hard drive as a kind of "ID") for user identification purposes. For a detailed explanation of Cookies, see CNET Networks' Privacy Policy.

 CNET Channel takes every precaution to keep any and all confidential information secure. However, due to required functionality of this application, information may be shared between users and their customers. CNET Channel is not liable for disbursal of such information resulting from user activity.

 Correcting/updating personal information
 Your personal information may be accessed and updated by you at any time. To edit your personal information, you can log in to your ChannelOnline account and click the "My Account" button and then click the "Edit" buttons. This will allow you to make any changes and modify the information you have previously shared with us.

 Contacting CNET Channel
 Any modifications to CNET Channel's practices regarding user data will be reflected first within this area of the company's web site. If you have any questions regarding this privacy statement, or regarding any other aspects of ChannelOnline, please contact us.
