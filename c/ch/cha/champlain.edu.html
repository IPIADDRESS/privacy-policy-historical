Privacy

Champlain College respects the privacy of your personal information and does not, under any circumstances, rent or sell personal information submitted by visitors to our site to any outside third party. Aggregate, non-personally identifying information may be both used internally and shared externally (for example, the number of applicants from specific countries).

Submitting a form on this website constitutes your express written consent to receive e-mails, texts, and phone messages from Champlain College at the phone number(s) and email address(es) provided in the form. You will be provided an opportunity to opt out of these communications by responding to the messages you receive from the College. You may also email us at webmaster@champlain.edu to opt out of these communications.

Should you have any questions about the privacy policy we encourage you to contact us by e-mail at webmaster@champlain.edu.
