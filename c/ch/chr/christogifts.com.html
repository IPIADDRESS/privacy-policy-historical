Privacy Declaration

Your Privacy

Christo Limited,  on behalf of www.christogifts.com  are committed to customer service and the protection of the privacy of our site visitors and customers. We will not disclose information about our customers to any third party except where it is a necessary part of providing a service to you, e.g. arranging for a product to be sent to you including fulfilment houses we hire to despatch books.

Your Consent

By placing an order with www.christogifts.com or a sub-domain set up to sell various products, you consent to the collection and use of your information by Christo Limited. Any changes to this privacy policy will appear on this page, keeping you up to date. When you place an order online we  need to know your name, address and delivery address. Our payment processor will ask you for your credit card number and expiry date, to enable you to pay for the books you have ordered and to ensure that they are delivered to you at the correct address.

We do not sell, rent or trade information about you. We are required by the Data Protection Act 1988 to follow strict security procedures to ensure that stored information is not accessible by unauthorised persons.

Checking your details. If you wish to verify the details you have submitted to www.christogifts.com you may do so by contacting us via the Contact Us page or the address given below. Our security procedures mean that we may request proof of identity before we reveal information. This proof of identity will take the form of information submitted upon registration.

What are Cookies? A cookie is a small information file that is sent to your computer and is stored on your hard drive. Cookie Policy

www.christogifts.com website uses cookies. Cookies are used by almost every other website and don’t harm your computer.

What is a Cookie?

 A cookie is a small text file containing a unique identification number that is transferred from a website to the hard drive of your computer so that the website may identify you and track your activities in the website. Our cookies only recognize your computer and do not know or store any personal information about you apart from your due date and county. You may choose not to enable your cookies in your browser if your browser permits, although you need to enable the cookies to use the site properly.

Necessary cookies 

These cookies help us to:

  • Make our website work as you'd expect (for example the shopping basket and checkout process)
  • It saves time. Save you having to login every time you visit the site
  • Remember your settings during and between visits (for example whether you’re logged in, what you searched for)
  • Improve the speed/security of the site
  • Personalize our site to you to help you get what you need faster
  • Remember if you have accepted our terms and conditions
  • Display the “Your Basket” page

Performance cookies

These cookies remember information about how you use our website. They give us vital information such as which pages are visited most often, whether customers are receiving error messages from certain pages, etc. These cookies allow us to analyze and improve the performance of our website. As a result, you receive a consistent look, feel and shopping experience. Performance cookies, or web analytic cookies don't collect any identifiable user information. In addition, all information collected is aggregated and therefore anonymous.

Social Website Cookies

So you can easily “Like” or share our content (and add comments to our site) on sites like Facebook and Twitter we have included sharing buttons on our site.
The privacy implications on this vary from social network to social network and are dependent on the specific privacy settings you have chosen on these networks.

Advertisement tracking

We use cookies and pixel tags to make our advertising and promotions on third party websites more effective. We use third party vendors like Google, who show our ads on sites across the internet.

We allow the third parties who participate with us in marketing programmes to place cookies when you visit our site. These cookies enable us to monitor which adverts you see and click on to get to our site, and help us to deliver adverts more relevant to your personal interests. They also limit the amount of times you see an advert and measure the effectiveness of adverts. This means you won't see the same adverts over and over, or continue to see irrelevant adverts. We currently work with the following companies for this; please see their privacy policies at the following addresses:

  • Doubleclick/Google Adwords: http://www.google.com/policies/privacy/
  • Cybercom/Google Adsense: http://www.google.com/policies/privacy/
  • LinkShare: http://www.linkshare.co.uk/cookies/

These cookies are as safe as the ones used whilst you browse our site – none of these third parties collects any personal data from which they would be able to identify individual customers. Quite often, targeting or advertising cookies will be linked to site functionality provided by the other organizations that generate the advert. When one of these adverts is presented to you on a site there will be a quick and easy way to click in and opt out of receiving further adverts.

www.christogifts.com is not responsible for the content or privacy policies of third parties or other websites.

Granting us permission to use cookies

If the settings on the software that you are using to view this website (your browser) are set to accept cookies we take this, as well as your continued use of our website, to mean that you are in agreement with this.

Deleting Cookies

Some of the cookies we use are vital for www.christogifts.com website to function properly and have already been set. However, you can stop cookies from being placed on your computer by changing your browser settings not to accept them or opt-out. Your 'help' facility will show you how to do this.

Alternatively, it is possible to opt out of Google's use of cookies by visiting the Ads Preferences Manager http://www.google.com/settings/ads/onweb

In addition, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/#completed

Please note, that restricting or deleting cookies will mean that our site will not work as you would expect.
