Shipping and Delivery

What are my shipping options?

Standard: 2-10 Business Days (UPS Ground)

  • Delivery in the continental U.S. within six business days, Monday-Friday, by 7:00 PM
  • Delivery times to Alaska and Hawaii may vary.

At present, ChristmasLightsEtc.com can only ship products within the 50 states. ChristmasLightsEtc.com cannot fill or ship international orders.

How are my shipping costs calculated?
We estimate all of our shipping charges by weight, delivery / ship to locations and shipping methods. Actual shipping charges may apply.

Can I have an order delivered on a weekend?
ChristmasLightsEtc.com does not currently provide for Saturday or Sunday deliveries.

When will my order be shipped?
Most orders are shipped in 1 to 3 business days.

What do I do if my order is late?
If the expected delivery time listed with your choice of shipping has expired, and tracking information does not help, please contact us immediately. Your order may be late because no one was available to accept delivery. If you are not present when a delivery is attempted, the carrier will make a second attempt, and often a third. If delivery is still not made, the shipment will be returned to our warehouse. You must report to us any undelivered order within 25 days of the date you placed the order.

What if my package is damaged when it is delivered?
If your package arrives damaged, you may refuse delivery at no cost to you. Please contact us immediately so that we can correct the situation as soon as possible.

Security

Is online ordering safe & private?

Is online ordering really safe? What about those stories I've heard where credit card information was stolen?
Those stories began with a Web site that didn't have adequate security. Ours does. Yes. In conjunction with Verisign (www.verisign.com), ChristmasLightsEtc.com uses 128-bit encryption to protect information entered -- some of the most powerful encryption available today.

Basically, encryption encodes your information. To steal your information from the Internet, someone must first find your encrypted information packet (which is flying across the Internet at light speed) - then crack the code. Nobody has ever claimed that this has been done. Indeed, if it were possible, the most sensitive information in the world would be available to them - not just your credit card number.

Look at it this way: you probably already transmit your credit card number electronically every day. When you swipe your credit card at the local store, your card information is encrypted and transmitted over a phone network. Using your card on a secure online Web site is very similar - your information is encrypted and then transmitted over a high-speed network line.

How private is my personal information?
We use any information you provide to us only to serve you better. We do not trade or sell your personal information to anyone.

Ordering

What if I can't find what I am looking for?
If you don't see a product you're looking for, let us know (if by email, CustomerService@ChristmasLightsEtc.com). We may have it under a different industry name, and if not, we will do our best to track it down for you. If there is a service you'd like to see on our Web site, we'd like to hear about that, too. The more we know, the more we can do for you.

How do I place an order?
ChristmasLightsEtc.com's purchase process is secure and simple. Just follow these easy steps:

1.Click on the product you want to buy.

  • Choose the quantity that you would like to order
  • Click on the "Add to Cart!" link or "Buy" button.

2. You'll proceed to the Cart Summary screen that shows you what's in your Cart. Here, you can:

  • Remove items by clicking on "Remove" link.
  • Go back and do more shopping by using the category links or product search.
  • Make your purchase by clicking on "CheckOut" button.
  • Update the quantity you'd like to buy.
  • Estimate shipping

3. When you're finished shopping and have clicked on "Proceed to CheckOut" button:

  • You'll be prompted to Checkout as Guest or log-in as an existing ChristmasLightsEtc.com Member.
  • This is a secure site where you will enter shipping, billing and payment information.*
  • Update the Billing, Shipping, and Payment information by clicking on "Continue"

4. You'll proceed to the Order Summary screen that shows you a summary of your order. Here, you can:

  • Enter applicable coupon codes
  • Select ship method
  • Go back to the cart to modify your order by clicking on "Edit Cart" button.
  • Modify your Billing and Shipping information by clicking on the appropriate link.
  • If you're ready to make your purchase, click on "Complete Checkout" button and you're done.

5. You'll proceed to the Order Thank you page. Here, you can:

  • View your order and the Order Confirmation Number it was given.
  • Review Shipping and Billing information.
  • Go to a Print Friendly version of the Order Summary, by selecting the appropriate link.
  • Email Customer Service if you have any concerns, by selecting the appropriate link.
  • Continue to shop.

*For added security, credit cards are encrypted within our system and screened thoroughly during the ordering process.

  • The "Bill To" address information must be on file with the issuing bank. If the Billing information does not match what the bank has on file, the order may be held for review.

Please contact us if you have any concerns regarding the safe use of your credit card online.
