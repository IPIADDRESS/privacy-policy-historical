    "Cookies" are data files that may be downloaded to your computer when you visit the Churpchurp.com site or a Publisher site and permit the Churpchurp.com server to identify your browser whenever you interact with the Churpchurp.com site or a Churpchurp.com Publisher site. These cookies contain identification information that enables us to streamline your experiences using the Services and to report such information to Publishers and Advertisers. You may set your browser software to reject cookies, but you may not be able to optimize the features of the Services. In some cases, you may not be able to access Services if cookies are disabled.
