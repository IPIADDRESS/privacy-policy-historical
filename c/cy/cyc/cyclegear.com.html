Privacy Policy

When you make a purchase from 1-800-CYCLEGEAR or cyclegear.com, we'll use your information to process your order. We will send emails to confirm that your order was received and to confirm that your order was shipped. We may need to contact you via phone, postal mail or email if we have questions regarding your order. In addition, you may sign up to receive updates about our products, discounts, contests and promotions.

We do occasionally share name and address information from your online and phone purchases with appropriate companies that offer similar products, taking great care that your information will be used responsibly. We will not share any personal financial information. If you would prefer not to have your name and address shared with other companies, just send an email to customerservice@cyclegear.com with "Opt-out" in the subject line and ask that your info not be shared. In any case, we will not share your email information.

Of course, you can unsubscribe from our catalog mailings, our emails or our sharing policy at any time. Just let us know and we'll remove you from the appropriate list(s) faster than Ricky Carmichael could get a holeshot.
