Privacy Policy

CYP ASIA Centre has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrates our organization's commitment to privacy. We are keen on ensuring that the Net experience for all the users is always safe and secured. Your privacy is as important to us as our own privacy. All of the information we obtain from you is used to provide and/or improve our level of service to you. We will not rent, sell, or otherwise disclose any information about you or your account to any other party for direct mail, solicitation, or telemarketing purposes without your consent. This Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") explains the types of information we gather and what we do with it. We may modify this privacy policy from time to time. Our current privacy policy will be published on our web site.

Cookies Our sites use 'cookies' to record information. A cookie is a small data file that certain Web sites store on your hard drive when you visit them. Cyber Youth Club use cookies to store a unique random user ID. We do not store passwords or any other personal information about users . This user ID is used to identify user's anonymity and database and track the pages that users visit and it also allow us to measure activity on our sites and to improve your user experience by remembering your passwords and viewing preferences, and allowing you to visit various members-only parts of our sites without re-registering. However, the only information a cookie can contain is information provided by users. Cookies also enable users to enter the website and use the services as a member without having to log on every time. Our sites do not use cookies to retrieve any information from your computer. Most browsers are set to accept cookies and if the user's browser is set to warn them before accepting cookies, they will receive the warning message with every cookie. Users can refuse cookies by turning them off in their browser. Cookies can neither read data off a user's hard drive nor can they read cookie files created by other sites.
