Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy will explain how KicksUSA collects, uses, maintains and discloses information collected from users (i.e. YOU or anyone else who enters this site) of http://www.kicksusa.com . This privacy policy applies to the Site and all products and services offered by KicksUSA..

You may be asked for, as appropriate, name, email address, mailing address, phone number, credit card information. but you can visit our Site anonymously. We will collect personal identification information from you only if you voluntarily submit such information to us. You can always refuse to supply personally identification information, but not signing up may prevent you from entering certain parts of the site

We may collect non-personal identification information about you whenever you interact with our Site. Non-personal identification information may include the browser name, the type of computer you are using and what servers and/or techno-sorcery you used to get onto the site.

Our Site may use "cookies" to enhance your experience. Your web browser places cookies on your hard drive to keep a smile file on you and tell us what you are up to on our site. You can choose to set your web browser to refuse cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent. If you do so, some parts of the site may not work for you.

We  might collect and use your personal information for a variety of reason:

    Information you provide helps us make KicksUSA bettter for YOU, like responding to customer service requests and helping you out with whatever KicksUSA problem you have.
  • To personalize your experience
    The info we collect from everyone who visits this site helps us find ways to make it better, whether more user friendly or getting the product you (“you” collectively) might want
  • To improve our Site
    We may use feedback you provide to improve our products and services.
  • To process payments
    We can use this info to place your orders and on the orders you told us you wanted. We won’t share this information with any third party.
  • To run a promotion, contest, survey or other Site feature
    To send you things we think you might like or enjoy
  • To send periodic emails
    We may use the email address to send you information and updates relating to your order. The info we get may also be used to respond to their inquiries, questions, and/or other requests. If you decides to opt-in to our mailing list, you will receive emails that may include company news, updates, related product or service information, etc. If at any time the you would like to unsubscribe from receiving future emails, you can by contacting us via our Site.

How we protect your information (AKA how we protect what we collected)

We make sure all your information like your username, password, transaction info, and data are protected from any attempts to alter, access or destroy it.

Sensitive and private data exchange between the Site and you happens over a SSL, A secured communication channel and is encrypted and protected with digital signatures.
