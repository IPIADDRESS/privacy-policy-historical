Privacy Policy

It is KISSinsights’ policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our websites.

Website Visitors

Like most website operators, KISSinsights collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. KISSinsights’ purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how KISSinsights’ visitors use its website. From time to time, KISSinsights may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

KISSinsights also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses. KISSinsights does not use such information to identify its visitors, however, and does not disclose such information, other than under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information, as described below.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain visitors to KISSinsights’ websites choose to interact with KISSinsights in ways that require KISSinsights to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that KISSinsights gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up to create surveys to provide their email address. KISSinsights collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor’s interaction with KISSinsights. KISSinsights does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

Aggregated Statistics

KISSinsights may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its website. KISSinsights may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, KISSinsights does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information

KISSinsights discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on KISSinsights’ behalf or to provide services available at KISSinsights’ website, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Some of those employees, contractors and affiliated organizations may be located outside of your home country; by using KISSinsights’ website, you consent to the transfer of such information to them. KISSinsights will not rent or sell potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information to anyone. Other than to its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations, as described above, KISSinsights discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only when required to do so by law, or when KISSinsights believes in good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the property or rights of KISSinsights, third parties or the public at large. If you are a registered user of the KISSinsights website and have supplied your email address, KISSinsights may occasionally send you an email to tell you about new features, solicit your feedback, or just keep you up to date with what’s going on with KISSinsights and our products. We primarily use our product blog to communicate this type of information, so we expect to keep this type of email to a minimum. If you send us a request (for example via a support email or via one of our feedback mechanisms), we reserve the right to publish it in order to help us clarify or respond to your request or to help us support other users. KISSinsights takes all measures reasonably necessary to protect against the unauthorized access, use, alteration or destruction of potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information.

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. KISSinsights uses cookies to help KISSinsights identify and track visitors, their usage of KISSinsights website, and their website access preferences. KISSinsights visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using KISSinsights’ website, with the drawback that certain features of the KISSinsights’ website may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

Privacy Policy Changes

Although most changes are likely to be minor, KISSinsights may change its Privacy Policy from time to time, and in KISSinsights’ sole discretion. KISSinsights encourages visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.
