Due to the nature of some of these activities, we may collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, address, age or date of birth, gender, employment, income level, other contact information, company name, username and password, and credit card information. Additionally, we may collect personally identifiable information about you that you provide to us when you contact us in person or via phone or that you voluntarily post in message boards, Blogs and/or forums and other users will be able to see any information that you post to such message boards, Blogs and forums. We may also use aggregate information about the use of our products and services to improve the capabilities of our products and services. This information will only be disclosed in aggregate form.
