Use of Cookies

Kia-BUZZ may collect and store information about you on your computer through the form of an internet “cookie.”  A “cookie” is a small file of characters that is stored in your computer after your first visit, which uniquely identifies your browser.  For example, in order to leave a comment, you are required to enter your name, email address and as a result, it will be filled automatically through the use of a “cookie.”  “Cookies” are used to provide you with a more personal experience on Kia-BUZZ.  Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies, but you can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.  Some Blog features and services may not function properly if your cookies are disabled or deleted.
