Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Kim Andersen uses cookies to help Kim Andersen identify and track visitors, their usage of Kim Andersen website, and their website access preferences. Kim Andersen visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Kim Andersen's websites, with the drawback that certain features of Kim Andersen's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by Kim Andersen and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.
