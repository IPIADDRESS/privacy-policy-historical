PRIVACY STATEMENT
KIDS DISCOVER is committed to safeguarding your privacy online. Please read the following statement to understand how your personal information will be treated as you make use of the KIDS DISCOVER TEACHERS WEB SITE(www.kidsdiscover.com).

INFORMATION GATHERING, VOLUNTARY SUBMISSION
KIDS DISCOVER TEACHERS (kidsdiscover.com) does not collect personally identifying information about any individual kidsdiscover.com web site user except when such information is knowingly provided by such an individual. For example, we must ask you for certain information when you choose to purchase so that we may fulfill your order. kidsdiscover.com uses secure server software and a 128-bit Secure Socket Layer (SSL) line to protect your credit card information and your associated personal information. You always have the option not to provide the information we request.

THE USE OF COOKIES
A cookie is a data file that certain web sites write to your computer's hard drive when you visit these sites. A cookie file can contain information, such as a user identification code, that the site uses to track the pages you have visited. KIDS DISCOVER uses cookies solely to track user traffic patterns throughout the KIDS DISCOVER web site. We use this data on an anonymous basis and we do not correlate this information with the personal data of any user. Most web site browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually change your browser settings to display a warning before accepting a cookie or set it to refuse all cookies. However, if you choose to disable the receipt of cookies from our web site, you may not be able to use certain features of the site.

BROWSER INFORMATION
KIDS DISCOVER web servers automatically collect information about a site user's IP address, operating system, browser type, browser features, and referrer by reading this information from the user's browser (such information is typically provided by every user's browser). This information is collected in a database and used in an aggregated, anonymous manner, in our internal analysis of traffic patterns within our web site. This information is automatically logged by most web sites and hosting systems.

USE OF INFORMATION
When you are asked for personal information on KIDS DISCOVER, you are sharing that information only with such vendors as provide web site hosting, and other services to Kids Discover Magazine and KIDS DISCOVER. You are not sharing your personal information with any third-party advertiser.

CHILDREN / MINORS
We ask that all children do not send us any information about yourself or your family, including such information as your name, address, or email address. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. Children should consult with a parent or guardian with regard to communicating with us or sending us information. In the event we learn that we have collected any personal information from a child/minor without verification or consent of a parent or legal guardian, we will delete that information from our database as quickly as possible.

MODIFICATION
KIDS DISCOVER reserves the right to modify and/or amend this privacy statement at any time, without notice.

How to Contact Us
KIDS DISCOVER Magazine, 192 Lexington Avenue, Suite 1003, New York, NY 10016. If you have any questions regarding our privacy statement, please contact us via email at teachers@kidsdiscover.com or by telephone at 1-212-677-4457 weekdays 9:00-5:00 EST. Thank you!
