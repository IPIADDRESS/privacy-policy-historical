Such information can be used to identify you personally and typically includes: your names, date of birth, contact information, address information and employment information.     This information is used to help improve the site, analyse trends, and further administer this site. We may also use this information internally to improve our products and services. We may need to change this policy from time to time in order to address new issues and reflect changes on our website. We will post those changes here so that you will always know what information we gather, how we might use that information, and whether we will disclose that information to anyone else.

    Information that you enter into a contact form on this website will be used to contact you and assist with any enquiry that is raised through that form. In addition, information that you enter into a newsletter subscription form on this website will be used to send you newsletter updates. Please see the section titled ‘Communication Preferences’ in our Website Terms of Use for further details.

This form of consent is unambiguous, specific and given freely. You can withdraw your consent at any time by following the instructions provided in these communications.
