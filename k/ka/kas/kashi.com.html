KASHI PRIVACY POLICY

Overview

Kashi provides this site for the benefit of our customers and wants you to be aware of our Internet privacy policy. We value your business and look forward to building a long relationship with you. Our products, and therefore this site, are directed to adults; if your children would like to visit our site, we recommend you assist them in their activities on the Internet. Also, please be aware that this site and the servers that make it available are located in the U.S.

Gathering and Use of Personal Information

Kashi collects personal information, like name, address, e-mail address, phone number, product preferences, and demographic information, from our online visitors on a voluntary basis to fulfill your requests, provide you with information and updates on our site and our products, improve our services, and for internal marketing purposes. For your convenience, and to serve you better, we may combine personal information about you that you provide to this site with information we obtain from business partners or other companies. We also record certain non-personal information automatically when you visit our site, such as your IP address, cookie information, and the page you request.

Visitors may access many features of our site without providing any personal information. However, to participate in some of the benefits and features offered on the Kashi site, you may have to provide personal information. These benefits are described in more detail below. Except as explained below, personal information received from any visitor will only be used for internal marketing purposes and will not be sold or provided to third parties.

If you provide information such as recipes or comments regarding our products and services, we have the right to use this information in any manner and for any purpose without obligation to you.

We do reserve the right to use any information you provide to protect our site, our property (including our intellectual property), or the safety or property of third parties; to investigate illegal activity, fraud or threats; and/or to cooperate with legal authorities or orders. We will also transfer information about you if we are acquired or merged with a third party but will notify you in advance.

Shopping

Personal information, including your name, billing and credit card information, mailing address, e-mail address and telephone number, which is necessary to fulfill orders for products, will be collected by Kashi. Kashi will also share aggregate information internally about transactions by Kashi customers and statistical data on orders placed. For your convenience and to serve you better, your customer profile will include preference information based on your orders. Kashi may also use third-party order fulfillment services to process and fulfill orders made on the site. Your personal information will only be provided to these third parties to process your order or fulfill your requests and we require that these companies agree to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

Other Kashi Programs

We offer you opportunities to sign up for newsletters and to receive information, product updates, and coupons, and to participate in our taste tester panel or share your success stories. We do not sell our mailing lists to anyone. Only those people who ask for information from us will receive promotional communications from Kashi. Should you wish to stop receiving Kashi information by email, please click here to unsubscribe from our newsletter. We also offer an "opt-out" feature in each of our promotional emails that allows you, at any time, to have Kashi discontinue email communications to you.

Cookies

Kashi may use "cookie" technology to obtain non-personal information from its online visitors. Cookies are information files that your web browser places on your computer when you visit a website. Most browsers accept cookies automatically, but can be configured not to accept them or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. We do not extract personal information in this process nor do we provide this information to third parties. Cookies allow us to customize your user interface to speed up the navigation process, make your site experience more efficient, and to offer personalized features. If your browser is configured to reject cookies, you will not be able to utilize some features of the Kashi site.

Links

The Kashi site may contain links to other sites and the privacy and data collection practices on any linked sites are entirely separate from Kashi and are not covered by this Privacy Policy. We advise you to check their privacy policies and terms and conditions as they may vary. Kashi shall not be responsible for any failure on the part of any linked sites to comply with any privacy policy posted on the linked site, or any contractual or other legal obligations to which the operators of such site may be subject. Kashi has no responsibility for the content, policies or actions of these sites.

Security

Kashi uses Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology to protect credit card data during transmission when you shop online at Kashi.com. Kashi takes reasonable steps to maintain the information you provide to Kashi securely and to guard your privacy and will not sell or provide this information to third parties other than as described herein. We may use third parties to provide technical assistance or manage the site but we will require them to agree to maintain the privacy of your data.

Changes and Updates to the Privacy Policy

Kashi reserves the right to change the information, features, products, policies, promotions, disclosures and disclaimers at any time. Changes to this Privacy Policy will be posted on our site. Please check the policy periodically for changes. The continued use of the Kashi site following the posting of changes to this Privacy Policy will mean you accept those changes.

Changes and Access to Personal Information

You may correct, update and delete personal information from our marketing database at any time by clicking on the hyperlink “MY KASHI” located on the left side of each page of the Kashi site or contacting the Kashi Consumer Relations Team.
