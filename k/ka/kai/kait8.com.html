Privacy Policy

  This document describes how we handle information we learn about you from your visit to our website. The information we receive depends on what you do while visiting our site.

  If your visit our site simply to read or download information, such as the newscasts, our recipe pages, our weather pages, or other information we provide on our pages:

We collect and store only the following information about you:

  • the name of the domain from which you are accessing the Internet

  • the date and time you access our site

  • the pages you access on our site

  • how long you stayed on the pages of our site

  • the Internet address of the website from which you linked directly to our site

We use this information to measure the number of visitors to the different sections of our site, and to help us make our site more useful to our visitors.

If you identify yourself by sending an email or in our discussion areas:

You also may decide to send us personally-identifying information, as in an electronic mail message containing a request for information or a comment about the newsletter or the website. We use personally-identifying information from our visitors in various ways to further our efforts to make the site more enjoyable for those who visit.

We want to be very clear here: We will not obtain personally-identifying information about you when you visit our site, unless you specifically choose to provide such information to us.
