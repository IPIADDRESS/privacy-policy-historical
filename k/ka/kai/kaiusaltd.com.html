KAI USA LTD. Privacy Policy

Online privacy and use policy

This Online Privacy Policy specifies the information Kai USA Ltd. (Kershaw Knives, Shun Cutlery, Zero Tolerance Knives, and Kai Housewares) collects and how we use that information. The amount and type of information that we receive depends on how you use the site.

This Policy applies to our online practices only. It was last updated on June 26, 2018. We reserve the right to change any part of the Kai USA Online Privacy Policy at any time. If you have questions or comments regarding this policy, please use the Contact Us form to get in touch with us.

ABOUT THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT & HOW WE USE IT

WHEN YOU VISIT OUR WEBSITE

As a visitor to our website, you can always browse without providing personal information. The information does not personally identify you and cannot be linked back to you unless you decide at some point to identify yourself.

Website Analytics

We use Google Analytics to gather statistics and measure our website activity so that we can better understand our customer base. If you are browsing, we may collect the following information, which is not personally identifying information:

  • Browser type and operating system
  • Date and time you accessed our site
  • Number of times you accessed our site
  • Whether your computer is enabled for Javascript or cookies
  • Language preference from your browser
  • Applications and plug-ins
  • Pages you visit
  • If you came to our site from another website, the address of that site

We have also implemented IP Anonymization in Google Analytics to further protect your privacy. All IP addresses are “masked” as they are received by the Analytics Collection Network and before they are processed or stored. This prevents data from going to Google Analytics with an identifiable IP address and protects your privacy.

Use of Cookies

  • Cookies are pieces of information that are sent by our website and stored on your computer’s hard drive or temporarily in the computer’s memory.
  • We may use session cookies, which terminate when you close your browser, and/or persistent cookies, which remain on your computer until manually deleted.
  • Cookies can help you move faster through our site, remember your preferences, and help us estimate our audience and what web pages they visit.
  • The use of cookies, or similar technologies, is an industry standard and you will find them on most major websites.
  • For more information about cookies, visit http://www.allaboutcookies.org
  • See below for information on how to control cookie use.

Advertising

Kai USA currently does not use:

  • Remarketing with Google Analytics
  • Google Display Network Impression Reporting

We do use:

  • Google Analytics Demographics & Interest Reporting
  • Google Analytics tracking codes

Should this change in the future, we will update this Online Privacy Policy.

We do not/will not facilitate the merging of personally identifiable information with non-personally identifiable information previously collected from Display Advertising features that are based on the DoubleClick cookie unless you have specifically opted-in to that merger. We will not attempt to disaggregate data that Google reports in aggregate.

How We Use this Information

Cookies and similar technologies are used in analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users’ movements around the site and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. We may receive reports based on the use of these technologies on an individual as well as an aggregated basis. By showing how and when visitors use the site, cookies help Kai USA better determine which areas of our site are popular and which areas are not.

We use Google tracking codes for advertising campaign reporting when we place advertising on other websites or email lists. These reports are aggregate and non-personally identifiable. This reporting is shared internally with Kai USA and Kai Corp personnel only.

OPT-IN/OPT-OUT

Cookies

Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. If you'd prefer, you can set your browser to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. However, it is possible that some parts of our site will not function properly without being able to use cookies.

Analytics

If you prefer that your information not be included in Google Analytics’ aggregated information on our website, you can get a browser opt-out add-on from Google here. 

Personalized Ads

There are a number of ways you can opt-out of personalized online advertising. Please note that opting out of targeted ads will not prevent you from seeing online ads. It will, however, prevent the ads from being customized to you based on your browsing behavior.

Here are links to websites where you can opt-out of personalized advertising:

  • Network Advertising Initiative
  • Google Ad Choices—Sign in to your Google Account, navigate to Personal info & privacy, click Ad Settings, then Manage Ad Settings
  • TrustArc (US & EU)
  • In the EU—Your Online Choices

Anonymous Browsing

You can also use the Incognito browsing window in Google’s Chrome browser so that it won't save your browsing history, cookies and site data, or information entered in forms. Get more information here. Other browsers have similar options; search “anonymous browsing” and the name of your browser to learn more.

OTHER INTERACTIONS VIA THIS WEBSITE

Reviews/Comments/Testimonials

What We Collect

When you provide a review or comment on our website, we ask you to provide a username, email address, and your location. Only your review or comment appears publicly on the website, along with your username and state/province.

Why We Collect It

We monitor reviews and comments as feedback on our products and services. We ask for your email in case we need to communicate with you about your review or comment privately. This information is only shared internally.

Your Options

This information is stored on our secure website servers. If you prefer not to have your comments published but still wish to communicate with us, please use the Contact Us form. If you wish to have a published review, comment, or testimonial removed, please contact us at info@kai-usa.com.

Contact Us/Customer Service/Warranty Service/Shun Knife Registration

What We Collect

We collect personally identifiable information on our Contact Us, Warranty & Sharpening Request, and our Shun Knife Registration forms.

Why We Collect It

We use this information to be able to respond to your requests and questions in a personal and timely manner, or to be able to process your warranty request and return your serviced product or products to you.

Your Options

This information is stored on our secure website servers and is only shared internally. If you wish to have this information corrected or deleted, please contact us. If you prefer, you may contact us by phone, toll free, at 800-325-2891 or locally at 503-682-1966. You may email us directly at info@kai-usa.com. If you wish to have any of these interactions deleted, please contact us at info@kai-usa.com.

Sweepstakes, Giveaways, Or Other Promotions

What We Collect

When you enter a sweepstakes, giveaway, or other promotional event via our website or social media, we may ask for personally identifiable information in order to process your entry. In those cases, your information may also be shared with other carefully selected companies, such as our sweepstakes application provider. These companies are required to maintain the confidentiality of the information and are restricted from using the information for any unauthorized purpose. Depending on the method of participation in the promotion, you may also interact with social media platforms such as Facebook and Instagram.

Why We Collect It

This information is collected solely in order to process your entry. Other than the sweepstakes application processor, we do not share this information and we do not use it to market to you, unless such use is disclosed to you at the time of entry.

Your Options

By entering a sweepstakes, giveaway, or other promotional event, you agree to provide us with your information and to allow us to share that information, as needed, with associated companies. If you do not agree, please do not enter our sweepstakes, giveaways, or promotional events.

Email Communications

What We Collect

When you sign up to our email newsletter for updates on new products, sales, promotions, and other email communications and content, we ask for your name, email address, zip/postal code, and country, as well as your preferences for what information you will receive by email. You will specifically be asked to opt-in to our email list.

Why We Collect It

We collect this information so that we can email you directly, personalize your email, and make sure that you receive the types of communications you prefer from us.

Your Options

You can subscribe to our email communications on the product pages of our website and from a link in the footer on any page of the website. You can unsubscribe anytime from the Unsubscribe link in the bottom of any email you receive from us.

We use MailChimp as our marketing automation platform. The information you provide will be transferred to MailChimp for processing in accordance with their Privacy Policy  and Terms.

We occasionally use Survey Monkey as our survey platform. The information you provide will be transferred to Survey Monkey for processing in accordance with their Privacy Policy and Terms.

Job Applications

What We Collect

As part of the job application process, we collect personally identifying information and, in many cases, applicants will also attach a resume.

Why We Collect It

We collect this information to be able to review it, then get in touch with appropriate candidates for a wide range of positions at Kai USA.

Your Options

This information is stored on our secure website servers and is only shared internally. If you prefer not to apply on our website, you can search out our positions on a wide variety of the most commonly used employment-search websites. You may also call the front desk locally at 503-682-1966.

TRANSMISSION OF INFORMATION

Kai USA and its website partners make every effort to ensure the secure collection and transmission of sensitive user information using industry-accepted data collection and encryption methodologies.

SSL (Secure Sockets Layer)

This website is secured by a RapidSSL RCA SSL certificate with a SHA256 with RSA signature, using TLS 1.2 protocol. Cipher suites have been optimized to follow best practices and return an A+ rating by ssllabs.com.

Database (Amazon Web Services Reliable Database Storage)

This website uses Amazon Web Services (AWS) for secure reliable database storage and transport within a secure Amazon Virtual Private Cloud. Backed by the security and best practices offered by AWS, we use industry standards and best practices to prevent unauthorized access to stored data. The Amazon Virtual Private Cloud allows us to control, monitor and secure traffic and access to this website and data in an isolated manner. AWS resources are kept up-to-date with the latest security protocols and patches to prevent threats from vulnerabilities.

E-commerce

Credit card information is not stored on the Kai USA website or database, and is only processed through a secure third-party payment provider over an SSL encryption.
