Talisma Corporation Privacy Statement
Talisma Corporation, with its principal offices located at 3015 112th Avenue NE, Suite 100, Bellevue, WA 98004, together with its subsidiaries (hereinafter "Talisma") are committed to protecting the privacy of their clients. Talisma will not distribute, sell, or rent names, email addresses, or any other personally identifiable information to any third party.

Talisma may share non-personal, non-individual information collectively with its marketing partners strictly for its own research purposes. For example, Talisma will not provide specifics around who purchased a specific product, but rather how many customers purchased that product.

Member profile information (e.g. name, title, address, items purchased) may be used to create personalized content, offers, services, and advertising to be used only by Talisma.

Third Party Content
Talisma Web sites may contain content from third-party content providers, however the content provider(s) will not receive Talisma user or customer information from Talisma.

Cookies
Talisma and/or third parties may use cookies to compile statistical data in order to analyze how visitors use the Talisma Web site. The process may involve setting cookies on local systems in order to gather the necessary information to perform the statistical analysis. The information collected is designed to measure the performance of various marketing initiatives and overall Web site performance. This information can include the link and referral Web site, as well as activity on the Talisma Web site, including dates and information submitted on Talisma's site.

Log Files 
Talisma uses IP addresses to analyze trends, administer the site, track user movement, and to gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

Surveys 
Talisma's site occasionally requests information from users via surveys. Participation in these surveys is completely voluntary. Information requested may include contact and demographic information. Survey data may be used to monitor or improve the use and satisfaction of Talisma's Web site, products, or services. Talisma will retain ownership to all data provided via voluntary surveys.

When you access a non-Talisma Web site, you do so at your own risk and Talisma is not responsible for the accuracy or reliability of any information, data, opinions, advice, or statements made on these sites. Talisma provides these links merely as a convenience and the inclusion of such links does not imply that Talisma endorses or accepts any responsibility for the content or uses of such Web sites. Talisma encourages all of its visitors to read the individual privacy statements of such Web sites. The Talisma Privacy Statement applies solely to information collected by the Talisma Web site.

Additionally, while the Talisma Web site may be accessed from countries around the world and contains references to Talisma products, services, and programs such references do not obligate Talisma to provide such products, services, or programs in every country.
