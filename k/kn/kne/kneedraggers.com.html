Our Commitment to Privacy

KneeDraggers.com values its customers and respects their privacy. We collect customer information in an effort to improve your shopping experience and to communicate with you about our products, services, contests and promotions. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Period.

KneeDraggers.com will never send you unsolicited email asking you for any personal information. Any e-mail or other communication requesting your password, asking you to provide sensitive account information via email, or linking to a website with a URL that does not begin with https://secure.kneedraggers.com should be treated as unauthorized/suspicious and should be reported to KneeDraggers.com immediately.

Is my information secure?

KneeDraggers.com is committed to handling your customer information with the highest standards of information security. We use 128 bit Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology to protect your online order information as it is transmitted over the Internet. Additionally, your credit card information is stored only in encrypted form. We restrict access to your personally identifiable information to employees who need to know that information in order to provide products or services to you.

The security of your KneeDraggers.com account also relies on your protection of your KneeDraggers.com password. You should not share your KneeDraggers.com password with anyone. KneeDraggers.com will never ask you to send your password or other sensitive information to us in an e-mail, though we may ask you to enter this type of information on the KneeDraggers.com website, which will always have a URL beginning with https://secure.kneedraggers.com.

If you believe someone else has obtained access to your password, please change it immediately by logging in to your account at https://secure.kneedraggers.com/user/ and changing your password settings. You may also contact us immediately if you believe this has occurred.
