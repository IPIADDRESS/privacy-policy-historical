About KHD: A short note

Kerala house designs is a home design blog showcasing beautiful handpicked house elevations, plans, interior designs, furniture’s and other home related products.

Main motto of this blog is to connect Architects to people like you, who are planning to build a home now or in future.

Also we are doing handpicked real estate postings to connect buyers and sellers, and we don’t stand as third party in any case.

Have a great stay, and enjoy your visit.
