July 2, 2011. Posted in: About

At KellyMom.com, your privacy is important to us.

In order to register for the forums, or comment on or subscribe to blog posts, a user is required to give certain information such as name and email address. This information may be used to contact you about your registration or forum activity. At your option, you may also provide demographic information (such as gender or age) about yourself. Viewing forum posts does not require registration.

To buy from us, you must provide contact information (such as name, email, and shipping address). This information is used to fill your order (possibly by a 3rd party) or contact you regarding your order. You will also need to enter payment information in a 3rd party website such as PayPal. They will have their own privacy policies.

KellyMom.com does not sell, rent or otherwise provide this information to anyone.
