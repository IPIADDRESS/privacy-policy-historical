KELOLAND: Privacy Policy

What this Privacy Policy Covers
This Privacy Policy covers KELOLAND.com's treatment of personally identifiable information that KELOLAND.com collects when you are on the KELOLAND.com site, and when you use KELOLAND.com's services. This policy also covers KELOLAND.com treatment of any personally identifiable information that KELOLAND.com's business partners share with KELOLAND.com. 

This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that KELOLAND.com does not own or control, or to people that KELOLAND.com does not employ or manage. 

Information Collection and Use 
KELOLAND.com collects personally identifiable information when you register for a KELOLAND.com account, when you use certain KELOLAND.com products or services and when you visit KELOLAND.com pages. KELOLAND.com may also receive personally identifiable information from our business partners. 

When you register with KELOLAND.com, we ask for your name, email address, address, zip code, phone number. Once you register with KELOLAND.com and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us. 

KELOLAND.com also automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser including your IP address, cookie information and the page you requested. 

Information Sharing and Disclosure 
KELOLAND.com will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone. 
KELOLAND.com will send personally identifiable information about you to other companies or people when: 
  • We have your consent to share the information;
  • We need to share your information to provide the product or service you have requested;
  • We need to send the information to companies who work on behalf of KELOLAND.com to provide a product or service to you.
  • We respond to subpoenas, court orders or legal process; or
  • We find that your actions on our web sites violate the KELOLAND.com Terms of Service, or any of our usage guidelines for specific products or services. 

Cookies 
KELOLAND.com may set and access KELOLAND.com cookies on your computer. 
KELOLAND.com allows other companies that are presenting advertisements on some of our pages to set and access their cookies on your computer. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to KELOLAND.com cookies. 

Security
Your KELOLAND.com Account Information is password-protected for your privacy and security. In certain areas KELOLAND.com uses industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect data transmissions. 

Changes to this Privacy Policy 
KELOLAND.com may amend this policy from time to time. If we make any substantial changes in the way we use your personal information we will notify you by posting a prominent announcement on our pages. 

Questions?
If you have questions please contact us.
