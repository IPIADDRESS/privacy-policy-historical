What about "cookies"?

Cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to your computer's hard disk for record-keeping purposes. Cookies can make the Web more useful by storing information about your preferences on a particular site. The use of cookies is an industry standard, and many major websites use them to provide useful features for their customers. Cookies do not personally identify users, although they do identify a user's computer. Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. If you'd prefer, you can set yours to prevent that. This should not prevent you from using most of the features on our website.

Will Kelkoo disclose the information it collects to outside parties?

No! Kelkoo.com does not sell, trade or rent your personal information to others, unless we have your specific consent to do so. We simply use the information we collect on our site to enhance your shopping experience.

Kelkoo.com may provide aggregate statistics about our customers, sales, traffic patterns and related site information to reputable third-party vendors, but these statistics will include no personally identifying information.

The only time your information may be "sold" to a third party is if the whole business of Kelkoo.com is sold to another company. If you do not wish your information to be passed on please send an e-mail to feedback.uk@Kelkoo.com

Kelkoo.com does reserve the right to access and disclose individually identifiable information to comply with applicable laws and lawful government requests, to operate its systems properly or to protect itself or its users.
