Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Is Our Concern!

The protection of your privacy is very important to us here at Kenneth Copeland Ministries. We value your trust, and want you to understand what information we collect, how we protect it, and how it is used. Personal information—nonpublic information that identifies you—is essential to KCM in meeting its goal to provide you with our services, ministry and products, and will be treated with respect and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Information We May Collect

When you contact Kenneth Copeland Ministries, we may request certain information in order to provide the services and/or products you have requested or to minister to you. This may include identification and contact information, as well as transactions and past experiences with KCM. Examples where such information may be requested include: subscriptions, prayer needs, meeting registration forms or other forms containing your name, address, telephone number and e-mail address, and past transactions you have had with us.

Our Privacy Policy

Any reference to “Kenneth Copeland Ministries” or “KCM” within the context of this Privacy Policy is intended, and shall be considered to be inclusive of each and all entities operated by Kenneth Copeland Ministries, including its International Offices.

  • KCM will not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.
  • KCM will only share personal information in the interest of public safety and as required by law.
  • KCM will notify you, and seek approval, in the event we desire to use any information covered by this policy in any manner not already described herein.
  • KCM may send personally identifiable information about you to other companies or other people when: we have your consent to share the information; or, we need to share your information to provide the product or service you have requested.
  • KCM will require other servicing companies to whom we disclose personal information to adhere to our Privacy Policy standards. All related authorizations and disclosures will conform to this Privacy Policy and applicable law.
  • KCM restricts access to your personal information to only those employees who have reasonable need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you.
  • KCM will maintain physical, electronic and procedural safeguards to protect personal information we obtain about you.
  • KCM will not share personal information if doing so requires that such information be sent to a third person or country that does not have an adequate protection system in place.
  • KCM will make every effort to ensure that the privacy and other rights of children (minors) are not compromised. It is recommended that children have a parent or guardian verify their subscription/registration with KCM.

Laws and regulations on the collection, protection and use of personal information may vary between countries. To obtain more information about your privacy, contact the KCM office nearest you.

Online Privacy

The following online privacy principles apply when you visit our Web site at www.kcm.org:

  • You can visit us on the Internet without telling us who you are or revealing any information about yourself, including your e-mail address. Our web servers collect the domain name you used to access the Internet, such as aol.com or NBCi.com, and the Web site you came from and visit next.
  • KCM uses this information to measure the number of visits, average time spent, pages viewed, and other statistics about visitors coming to our Web site in general. We may also use this data to monitor site performance and to make the site easier and more convenient to use.
  • KCM will notify you, and seek approval, in the event we desire to use any information covered by this policy in any manner not already described herein.
  • Cookies: We, along with thousands of other Web site hosts, use cookies to enhance your Web viewing experience and measure your Web site usage. (Cookies are small pieces of data stored by your Internet browser on your computer’s hard drive.)
  • SSL Encryption: We use secure socket layer (SSL) encryption to protect the transmission of information when submitting online contributions or product orders through our shopping cart.
  • E-mail: If you send us e-mail, you should know that there is no encryption to protect the transmission of that information and that it is not secured against interception
