TYPES OF INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect two basic types of information – Personal Information and Anonymous Information – and we may use these two types of information to create a third type of information, Aggregate Information. The types of information are defined/described below:

  • “Personal Information” – This refers to information that is collected online that directly or indirectly identifies a particular individual. When you engage in certain activities within the Property, we may ask you to provide certain Personal Information. Anonymous Information that is associated with Personal Information is also considered Personal Information. We may collect the following Personal Information from you:
    • Registration information you provide us when you create an account, including your name, date of birth, gender, username, password, e-mail address, and telephone number;
    • Transaction information you provide to us when you purchase a product or service from us, including your payment information, telephone number, and shipping address;
    • Information you provide in public forums on the Property;
    • Information you send on or through the Property, whether directly to another user or within a group, using our message, chat, post, or similar functionality, where we are permitted by law to collect this information;
    • Profile information you choose to share, such as your city, state, favorite bands, favorite shows, favorite music videos, favorite quote, your personal website, a picture, and a general description of yourself; and
      Other Personal Information you choose to share, such as information you provide to us when you link your account on the Property to third-party social networking sites or platforms.

  • “Anonymous Information” – This is information that does not and cannot reasonably be used to directly or indirectly identify an individual user. We use and share this Anonymous Information with third parties to improve the content and functionality of the Property and our affiliated websites or apps, to better understand our customers and markets, and to improve our products and services. Anonymous Information may include:
    • Information collected automatically through the operation of the Property, such as cookies, flash cookies, or web beacons, which are not linked to any Personal Information. These technologies provide tracking data that allows us to implement useful features on the Property and deliver relevant content to you based on your preferences, location, or usage patterns. We may associate some of this tracking data with your registration account if you choose to create one, in which case, we will treat it as Personal Information.
    • Location information when you visit or use the Property, including location information provided by a mobile device interacting with the Property (through beacon technologies or otherwise);
    • In some of our email messages we use a “click-through URL” linked to content on the Property. When you click one of these URLs, you pass through our web server before arriving at the destination web page. We tack this click-through data to help us determine interest in particular topics and measure the effectiveness of our communications. If you prefer not to be tracked, avoid clicking text or graphic links in our emails.
    • In addition we use pixel tags—tiny graphic images—to tell us what parts of the Property that you have visited or to measure the effectiveness of searches you have performed on the Property. Pixel tags also enable us to send email messages in a format you can read. Further, they tell us whether emails have been opened to ensure that we’re sending only messages that interest you. We may use this information to reduce or eliminate messages sent to you. We store all of this information in a database in the United States.

  • “Aggregate Information” – This is information created through use of Personal Information and Anonymous Information about the categories of users which does not and cannot reasonably be used to identify an individual user, such as:
    • Usage, viewing, and technical data, including your device identifier or IP address, collected automatically when you use our Property or open e-mails that we send.

The security, integrity, and confidentiality of your information are extremely important to us. From time to time, we review our security measures to consider appropriate new technology and methods. We take precautions—including administrative, technical, and physical measures—to safeguard your personal information against loss theft, and misuse, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. You can help us by also taking precaution to protect your Personal Information when you are on the Internet. Change your passwords often using a combination of letters and number, and make sure you use a secure web browser.
