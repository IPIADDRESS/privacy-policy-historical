Anonymous information can and will NOT be associated with Personally Identifiable Information.

Like many Internet sites, we also use "cookies" to collect information. A cookie is a small data file that we transfer to your computer and is stored on your hard drive. When you visit KhanaPakana.Com the cookie identifies your computer to us so that you do not have to re-register each time you visit. We also use cookies to measure the traffic to and usage of our site and its different services and features, and other miscellaneous uses.

We also collect the personal information of third parties that is provided by visitors who submit that personal data through our Sites, in connection with, for example, referrals.

If you do not wish KhanaPakana.Com to use your information as described in this section, please follow the procedure in "What Choices You Have" below.

Analyzing this data allows us to improve KhanaPakana.Com.

While we cannot predict about such an unlikely event, we would hope this could provide you continuity of service.
