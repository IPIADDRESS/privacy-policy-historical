Privacy Policy


There are many different ways you can use Konoozi LLC website – to search for information on Konoozi products and services and share information to communicate with Konoozi LLC staff.

(Last update: Mar 2012)

Information we gather

The following information is gathered from Konoozi LLC website visitors:

  • IP address

How we use the information we gather

Konoozi LLC uses the information to enhance our visitor experience and better services to all of our users. When you contact Konoozi, we may keep a record of your communication to help solve any issues you might be facing. We may use your email address to inform you about the ongoing issues updates, and our services, such as letting you know about upcoming changes or improvements.

We will ask for your consent before using information for a purpose other than those that are set out in this Privacy Policy.

Konoozi processes personal information on our servers in many countries around the world. We may process your personal information on a server located outside the country where you live.

The people that are given access to this information

We restrict access to personal information to Konoozi employees, contractors and agents who “need” to know that information in order to process it for us, and who are subject to strict contractual confidentiality obligations.

The security measures we have in place to protect your personal information

Your privacy matters to Konoozi. To safeguard your personal information, we work hard to protect Konoozi and our users from unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of information we hold. In particular:

• We make use of SSL technology to encrypting many of our services.

• We review our processes for information collection, storage and data processing practices, including security measures to guard against unauthorized access to data systems.

• We restrict access to personal information to Konoozi employees, contractors and agents who need to know that information in order to process it for us, and who are subject to strict contractual confidentiality obligations and may be disciplined or terminated if they fail to meet these obligations.

Konoozi Does Not make use of cookies

Konoozi LLC website does NOT make use of cookies.

For general information, cookies are small digital files that are stored in visitors’ web browser that enable website owner to track return visits to their website.

Your browser settings may allow you to block these cookies, however website owners recommend visitors have them enabled to help us personalize visitors’ experience to their website.

Additionally, third-party advertisers on the websites may use cookies for tracking purposes.

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads.

Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to visitors based on their visit to sites they visit on the Internet.

Website visitors may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy. Also, you can learn more about other ad-serving companies and opt out from their use of cookies<http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp> at the NAI website. Network Advertising Initiative<http://www.networkadvertising.org/> (NAI) is a committee to building responsible advertising policies across the Internet.

Enforcement

We regularly review our compliance with our Privacy Policy. Konoozi complies with the US-EU Safe Harbor Framework and the US-Swiss Safe Harbor Framework as set forth by the US Department of Commerce regarding the collection, storage and processing of personal information from European Union member countries and Switzerland.
