Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy of the Konecranes Group in indented to provide you with general information on the processing of personal data of the Konecranes Group, its employees, partners and affiliates (“Konecranes”) as related to the world wide pages of Konecranes (“Site”).This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) explains the type of information collected by Konecranes in connection with the Konecranes Site, and how Konecranes processes such information. Konecranes may provide additional or amending privacy information in connection with a particular Konecranes product or service. Such information prevails over this Policy.

Please read this Policy carefully prior to accessing the Site. By using the Site and/or by submitting personal data to Konecranes, you express your agreement to the processing of your personal data in the manner provided in this Policy. If you do not agree with this Policy, we expect that you will leave this Site and not provide Konecranes with your personal data.


The Data We Collect

Once you register at the Site or have otherwise been assigned a specific identification code to access your account, we may request that you provide certain information such as your name, email address, street address or other information that may be needed to provide the products and services you have requested or to further communicate with you.


The Purposes of Processing

The purpose of processing personal data is to ensure efficient and secure data processing.  Konecranes also strives to continue to develop the Site to ensure the highest quality and a broader scope of services. Additionally, Konecranes may use your personal data to communicate with you. Konecranes may provide additional information relating to our products and/or services or other topics related to the Konecranes Group.


Transfers of Personal Data

Konecranes will not generally transfer any personal data outside the Konecranes Group. Konecranes may, however, share your personal data with the affiliates belong to the Konecranes Group or authorized third parties who process personal data for Konecranes for the purposes described in this Policy.

Due to the technical requirements of the processing, some personal data may be located on servers maintained by subcontractors, which have agreed to the data processing standards of Konecranes.

Konecranes may provide aggregate statistics about its customers, sales, traffic patterns, and other related Site information to reputable third parties, but these statistics will not include personal data.


Use of Cookies

From time to time, Konecranes may place information on your computer which allows Konecranes to recognize your computer. This information is commonly known as a “cookie”. Typically, cookies enable collection of certain information regarding your computer, including your internet protocol (IP) address, your computer’s operating system, your browser type and the address of any referring sites. Cookies are intended to improve the Site for better service.


Your Rights and Contact Details

The processing of personal data is based on your consent. Therefore, you may at any time exercise your right to revoke your consent by contacting Konecranes. However, please note that by revoking your consent, Konecranes may not be able to provide you information regarding our services and products.

At any time, you may contact Konecranes to check the accuracy of your personal data. Konecranes will, at your request, modify or erase any incomplete, inaccurate or outdated personal data. Please contact the controller of your personal data by sending an e-mail through webmaster@konecranes.com.


Changes to this Policy

 

From time to time, Konecranes may change or amend this Policy.  In addition, Konecranes may modify or revoke access to this site at any time with or without notice. It is the recommendation of Konecranes that you revisit this Policy periodically to review any changes to this Policy. Additional questions and comments can be directed to Konecranes via e-mail at webmaster@konecranes.com.
