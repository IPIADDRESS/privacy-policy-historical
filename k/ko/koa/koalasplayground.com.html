This non-personal information may be gathered by using “cookies” and/or “clear GIF” (Graphical Interchange Format) technologies, as well as login file information. “Cookies” are small data files with a unique identifier that we transfer to your computer’s hard drive or web browser, that collect and store data about your visit to the Website. This non-personally identifiable information will be treated in the same fashion, and in aggregate to, all information provided by a Member, Guest, or Visitor.
