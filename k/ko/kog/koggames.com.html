If we learn that we have inadvertently gathered personally identifiable information from children under 13 years old, we will attempt to notify such child’s parent or guardian as the owner of the account and erase such information from our records.

 

If you are the parent or guardian of a child who is between the ages of thirteen and eighteen (18) who has created his or her own account or is authorized by you to use your account (if under the age of 13), we urge you to instruct your child to NEVER give out his or her real name, address, phone number or other personally identifiable information while using the Service.
