COMPLIANCE WITH THE CHILDREN'S ONLINE PRIVACY PROTECTION ACT OF 1998.

This website complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998. Accordingly, CHILDREN UNDER 13 YEARS OF AGE MAY NOT ACCESS THIS WEBSITE WITHOUT PARENT, TEACHER OR SCHOOL ADMINISTRATOR PERMISSION. Access to the data collection aspects of this website is made only through a student's school. Such schools obtain access only through an agreement executed by both NCASI and the particular school. Accordingly, each school is specifically responsible for monitoring student usage of this website. By utilizing the data collection aspects of this website, you acknowledge that your school is acting in the place of the students' parents in granting NCASI specific authorization to collect such information, upon the terms and conditions stated herein. In offering services to your school, NCASI collects the following information from students:

Name, address, school, age, gender, ethnicity, date of birth, email addresses, career assessment data.

Privacy and Security.

NCASI HAS TAKEN SEVERAL STEPS TO INSURE THE PRIVACY AND SECURITY OF ALL INFORMATION COLLECTED ON THIS WEBSITE. NCASI ONLY COLLECTS INFORMATION FROM INDIVIDUALS OVER THE AGE OF 12, OR FROM INDIVIDUALS UNDER THE AGE OF 13 IF THE INDIVIDUAL IS ACCESSING THE WEBSITE UNDER THE DIRECTION AND CONSENT OF A PARENT, TEACHER OR SCHOOL ADMINISTRATOR. NCASI COLLECTS AND PROCESSES THE INFORMATION TO PROVIDE THE INFORMATION TO THE PARTICULAR SCHOOLS CONTRACTING FOR ITS COLLECTION. NCASI MAY ALSO PROVIDE THE INFORMATION TO FEDERAL STATE AND LOCAL GOVERNMENTAL AGENCIES PROVIDING CAREER PLACEMENT SERVICES. THE COLLECTED INFORMATION RELATES TO STUDENTS' CAREER PREFERENCES AND INTERESTS. THIS INFORMATION IS COLLECTED DIRECTLY FROM THE CHILD, PARENT, TEACHER OR SCHOOL ADMINISTRATOR. NCASI ALSO UTILIZES COOKIES TO ASSIST CHILDREN, PARENTS, TEACHERS AND SCHOOL ADMINISTRATORS IN ACCESSING AND UTILIZING THE SERVICES PROVIDED THROUGH THIS WEBSITE. NCASI WILL NOT PROVIDE INFORMATION RELATING TO ANY CHILD UNDER THE AGE OF 13 TO ANY PARTY OTHER THAN AUTHORIZED EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS. ADDITIONALY, NCASI WILL NOT SELL THE INFORMATION TO THIRD PARTY MARKETERS OR DISCLOSE THE INFORMATION PUBLICLY. ALL OF THE THIRD PARTY EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS TO WHICH INFORMATION IS DISCLOSED HAVE AGREED TO MAINTAIN THE CONFIDENTIALITY AND SECURITY OF THE INFORMATION. IF YOU ARE THE PARENT OF A CHILD UNDER THE AGE OF 13 WHO HAS PROVIDED INFORMATION THROUGH THIS WEBSITE AND WOULD LIKE TO PREVENT THE INFORMATION FROM BEING TRANSMITTED TO AUTHORIZED THIRD PARTY EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS FOR CAREER AND EDUCATIONAL DEVELOPMENT, PLEASE CONTACT THE NCASI WEBMASTER AT THE ADDRESS LISTED BELOW UNDER CHANGING PERSONAL INFORMATION, OR DIRECTLY AT NCASI@NCASI.COM. NCASI HAS TAKEN GREAT STRIDES TO COLLECT ONLY THAT INFORMATION NECESSARY TO FURTHER THE CHILDRENS' CAREER DEVELOPMENT STRATEGIES LISTED ABOVE. IF AT ANY TIME THIS WEBSITE BEGINS TO COLLECT ANY ADDITIONAL INFORMATION OTHER THAN CAREER DEVELOPMENT AND CHILD IDENTIFICATION INFORMATION FROM CHILDREN UNDER 13 YEARS OF AGE, NCASI WILL NOTIFY THE SCHOOLS AND OBTAIN A NEW CONSENT FROM THE SCHOOL TO COLLECT SUCH ADDITIONAL INFORMATION FROM CHILDREN UNDER THE AGE OF 13.

COOKIES

A cookie is a piece of data stored on your hard drive containing information about you. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on our website. Once you close your browser, the cookie simply terminates. For instance, by setting a cookie on our website, you would not have to log in a password more than once, thereby saving time while on our website. If you reject the cookie, you may still use our website. The only drawback to this is that you will be limited in some areas of our website. For example, you will not be able to participate in any of our Sweepstakes or Contests. Cookies can also enable us to track and target your interests to enhance your experience on our website.

Some of our business partners use cookies on our website (for example, advertisers). However, we have no access to or control over these cookies.

LIMITATIONS ON DATA COLLECTION

In furtherance of NCASI's effort to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, and to insure the security of data collected from children under the age of 13, NCASI only collects data from such children pursuant to a specific agreement with an authorized school or educational institution. NCASI specifically contracts with such institutions for services which allow them to input individual information for each child onto the website. It is important that schools obtain parental permission before inputting a child's information onto the website, or before allowing a child to input the information themselves. At no time will NCASI knowingly collect online contact information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent, or the consent of a representative from an authorized educational institution. If NCASI receives a direct request from a child under 13, and the child is not affiliated with an authorized educational institution, without parental or authorized educational institution consent, NCASI will utilize the information solely for a one-time response to the child.
