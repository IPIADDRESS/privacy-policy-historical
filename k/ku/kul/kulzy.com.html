Privacy policy

www.kulzy.com (hereinafter referred to as "Kulzy") is committed to protecting the privacy of Internet users. The following guidelines discloses the privacy practices for www.kulzy.com

Collecting your personal information

When you use Kulzy we will collect various information about you or relating to you:

  • - If you register to become a user of Kulzy;
  • - If you send us a message from Kulzy or otherwise;
  • - As you visit web pages on Kulzy; and
  • - From your activities in connection with your use of Kulzy.
  • - Becoming a user of Kulzy

Before you can access and use all of Kulzy's features and services, you must register to become a user of Kulzy (by agreeing to Kulzy's Terms & Conditions).

To register, we ask that you provide us with certain personal information, which will generally include your name, phone number, email address, date of birth, academic and professional record, profile picture, Interests etc (each to allow Kulzy to customise your experience), a username and a password created by you. You may also elect to upload materials to your profile that include personal information, creatives, or articles.

Send you information about your account, the services we or our related companies provide or any changes or upgrades that we make to those services; to alert you of relevant opportunities and activities (including, without limitation, jobs, workspaces, content and notifications (for example, if another user is following your profile));

Cookies

Kulzy uses 'cookies' to identify information about your web browser session and personalisation data each time you visit Kulzy. A 'cookie' is a small data file that will be stored by your web browser when you access Kulzy. These cookies do not contain any of your personal information such as name or email, the information stored in the cookie will only identify you to Kulzy.

Cookies can be used to enhance your use of Kulzy, as we use them to help personalise Kulzy's delivery of services and material to you, such as information which is most relevant to your profile and interests.

You can set up your browser so that it does not accept our cookies. You can also manually delete our cookies from your browser. However, you will not be able to login to Kulzy without cookies being enabled on your browser.

Our web servers automatically collects limited information about your computer's connection to the Internet, including your IP address, when you visit our websites. Your IP address does not identify you personally. We use this information to deliver our web pages to you upon request, to measure traffic within our websites and let advertisers know the geographic locations from where our subscribers come.

We May Also Disclose Your Personal Information:

  • - To our related companies;
  • - To our professional advisors;
  • - To law enforcement authorities, if we suspect that an unlawful act is being committed or that such disclosure will prevent that unlawful act; or
  • -As otherwise required or authorised by law.
  • -If you apply for a position that is advertised via Kulzy, we will provide your contact details to the person or company advertising that position.

Privacy policy exclusively applies to www.kulzy.com

www.kulzy.com includes links to other websites whose privacy policies we do not control. Once you leave our servers use of any information you provide is governed by the privacy policy of the operator of the site you are visiting. That policy may differ from ours. If you can't find the privacy policy of any of these sites via a link from the site's homepage, you should contact the site directly for more information.

Statistics and Storage

When we present information to our advertisers -- to help them understand our audience and confirm the value of advertising on our websites -- it is usually in the form of aggregated statistics on traffic to various pages within our sites. If you do register with Kulzy, we may contact you about content and features that we believe may be of interest to you. All information gathered on www.kulzy.com is securely stored within Kulzy controlled database. The database is stored on servers secured behind a firewall; access to the servers is password-protected and is strictly limited. However, as effective as our security measures are, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee the security of our database, nor can we guarantee that information you supply will not be intercepted while being transmitted to us over the Internet.

Amendment to the Privacy Guidelines

If we need to change our privacy guidelines at some point in the future, we'll post the changes before they take effect. Of course, our use of any information we gather will always be consistent with the policy under which the information was collected, regardless of what the new policy may be. If at any time you believe Kulzy has not adhered to these principles, please notify us through an email at helpdesk@kulzy.com and we will use all commercially reasonable efforts to promptly determine and correct the problem. If you have questions about this policy, please e-mail us at helpdesk@kulzy.com
