Privacy Policy
 

General

Visitors browsing the Kelley Blue Book Web site do so on a completely anonymous basis. We never collect any personal information such as names, addresses, email addresses or credit card numbers from our visitors as vehicle pricing information is researched. We do, however, collect some non-specific visitor information, such as ZIP Codes and vehicle pricing requests, which we use to produce vehicle pricing reports, analyze the usage of the site, and customize online advertising and promotional offers. Kelley Blue Book does aggregate non-specific visitor information for statistical purposes, such as the production of our Hot 50 Cars list. None of this information is personally identifiable to our visitors.

Cookies

The Kelley Blue Book Web site makes limited use of "cookies" for the purpose of facilitating the gathering of non-specific visitor information to produce vehicle pricing reports. A cookie is a very small text file stored on a Web site visitor's hard disk that allows a Web server to recall certain information that the visitor has already entered previously. The cookies issued from the Kelley Blue Book Web servers store a visitor's ZIP Code and contain no personal information. If a visitor wishes to not utilize cookie technology, this feature may be disabled within the visitor's Web browser.

Partners

Our privacy policy only applies to the Kelley Blue Book Web site. We link from several points throughout our Web site to third party information and service providers who may collect personal information from our visitors in order to provide our visitors with information or services. Kelley Blue Book has no control over the privacy policy of any third party company, so please examine their privacy policies prior to submitting any personal information, as they may differ from Kelley Blue Book.

Kelley Blue Book Web site content is also featured on other Web sites, all with differing or non-existent privacy policies. These Web sites may collect personal information from their visitors that may or may not be used in conjunction with Kelley Blue Book Web site information. Prior to supplying any personal information to any third party company linking to Kelley Blue Book, please read and understand their privacy policy.

Advertisements

Advertisements served by DoubleClick, a third party Internet advertising technology company, appear on pages throughout the Kelley Blue Book Web site. Some advertisements may request information directly from our visitors or take our visitors to Web sites that may request personal information. Kelley Blue Book has no control over its advertisers' privacy policies, so please examine the privacy policy of any company advertising on Kelley Blue Book prior to submitting any personal information. In addition, DoubleClick utilizes certain technologies to deliver specific advertisements to Kelley Blue Book visitors. Privacy issues surrounding this technology, their information gathering practices and "opt-out" procedures are disclosed in their privacy policy, which can be read at http://www.doubleclick.net/privacy_policy/.

Surveys

Occasionally, Kelley Blue Book in conjunction with a third party, conducts surveys on the Kelley Blue Book Web site. Surveys are conducted at random and are completely voluntary to our Web site visitors. The survey may require a visitor to provide personally identifiable information in exchange for the information or services provided by the survey. Kelley Blue Book and the third party conducting the survey may use this information in an aggregate manner to better tailor the type of services, information, and advertising that are provided on the Kelley Blue Book Web site.

Questions?
