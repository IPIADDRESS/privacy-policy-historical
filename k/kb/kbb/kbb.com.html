What This Privacy Policy Covers

This policy covers how Kelley Blue Book Co., Inc. ("Kelley") treats information which personally identifies you ("personal information") that Kelley collects and receives on its website, kbb.com ("Kelley Site"). The personal information we collect may include your name, address, phone number, and/or e-mail address. Once you provide Kelley with personal information, you are not anonymous to us.

This privacy policy applies only to the Kelley Site. There are several text and graphic links from the Kelley Site to third party social media sites and information and service providers who may collect personal information from you in order to provide you with information or services. Kelley has no control over the privacy policy of any third party company, so please examine their privacy policy prior to submitting any personal information. In addition, the co-branded Buying Assistant pages available through the Kelley site are hosted and served by CarWoo! The Carwoo! Privacy Policy applies to the Buying Assistant pages, so please examine the CarWoo! Privacy Policy prior to submitting any personal information in connection with the Buying Assistant service.

General
Information Collection and Use

When you research Kelley vehicle values or vehicle pricing information, Kelley does not collect any personal information from you. Kelley does, however, collect some non-specific visitor information, such as ZIP Codes and vehicle data used to produce vehicle pricing reports, analyze the usage of the Kelley Site, and customize online advertising and promotional offers.

Kelley collects personal information when you: request a Free Dealer Price Quote, answer survey questions, register to use the Community Pages and/or a My KBB account. We may also receive personal information from you, for example when you: respond to a research survey with a marketing research supplier or provide personal information with comments when you Contact Us.

When you visit the Kelley Site, we may collect certain information about your visit from your browser, including the Internet Protocol (IP) address through which you access the Internet, the date and time you access the Kelley Site, the pages that you access while at the Kelley Site and the Internet address of the Web site from which you linked directly to the Kelley Web site, and Kelley cookie information. Kelley aggregates non-specific visitor information for statistical purposes, such as the production of the Kelley Blue Book Most Researched Cars list. This information is used to help improve the Kelley Web site, analyze trends, and administer the site.

Kelley uses your personal information for the following general purposes: to fulfill your requests for products and services, to contact you (if we have your permission, or to send a survey after you have requested a Free Dealer Price Quote or other products or services), to customize the content and advertising you see, and to improve our services. Kelley uses aggregate and non-personal information to conduct research and to provide reports for Kelley business partners and customers.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Kelley does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:

When you elect to receive information from Kelley, automobile manufacturers and local dealers and/or from Kelley's valued partners (finance, insurance, etc.), Kelley will use and/or share your personal information only as you have elected.

When you request a Free Dealer Price Quote or to be contacted by a dealer regarding a new or used vehicle, your personal information (including your contact information) may be forwarded by Kelley directly to the dealer or may be provided by Kelley to an automobile manufacturer or to a trusted business partner that will forward your personal information to the dealer; your personal information may also be forwarded by Kelley, the dealer, the automobile manufacturer or a trusted business partner to Experian Information Solutions, Inc., or R. You may receive a confirmation email when your contact information is forwarded by Kelley.

Advertisements served by DoubleClick, a third party Internet advertising technology company, appear on pages throughout the Kelley Site. Some advertisements may request personal information directly from you or take you to Web sites, including Web sites that host chat boxes for the advertisers, that may request your personal information. Kelley has no control over these advertisers' privacy policies, so please examine the privacy policy of any company advertising on the Kelley Web site prior to submitting any personal information in connection with the advertisement. Cookies

Kelley may set and access Kelley cookies on your computer. A cookie is a small text file that is placed on your hard drive by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a Web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you. One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. For example, if you personalize a Web page, or navigate within a site, a cookie helps the site to recall your specific information (such as your ZIP Code) on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of delivering relevant content, eases site navigation, and so on. When you return to the Web site, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the site's features that you customized.

Kelley lets other companies, including business partners and companies that show advertisements on some of our pages, set and access their cookies on your computer. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies. Advertisers or other companies may have access to Kelley's cookies.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of this or other Web sites you visit.

Web Beacons

Kelley uses Web beacons (also known as clear gif technology, pixels, or action tags) to access Kelley cookies inside and outside our Web site and to collect data and allow other companies to collect data. Web beacons may be used to assist in delivering cookies on our site. This technology is a tool we may also use to compile aggregated statistics about the Kelley Site usage, such as how many visitors clicked on key elements (such as links or graphics) on a page. We may share aggregated site statistics with other companies, and we may allow other companies to place their clear gifs or cookies on our site.

Protection of Children's Personal Information

Kelley is a general audience site and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Kelley may occasionally update this privacy policy. When we do, we will also revise the "last updated" date at the top of the privacy policy. We encourage you to periodically review this privacy policy to stay informed about how we are helping to protect the personal information we collect. Your continued use of the service constitutes your agreement to this privacy policy and any updates. Please direct any questions or comments regarding our privacy policy to Kelley@kbb.com.
