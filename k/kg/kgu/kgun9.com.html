What types of personal information do we collect about you?

We may ask you to provide various types of personal information to enhance your experience on our website. The more information you provide, the better we are able to customize your experience. The types of personal information our website may collect include your name, postal address, email address, telephone number, birth date, gender, occupation, household income, purchasing preferences, personal interest activities, bank/debit/credit card information, username and password for the website, and other personal information that you actively provide to us via our website or email.

How do we actively collect and use personal information?

We may actively collect personal information from you when you complete online forms or otherwise affirmatively provide such information to us. Our website collects information in different ways, including but not limited to when you participate in sweepstakes, contests, games, promotional offers, and surveys; subscribe to our online services, such as news, weather and traffic alerts; register for our station newsletters and listener clubs; download podcasts; purchase products or services through our shopping service; communicate with us via email; apply for employment with us; or otherwise affirmatively provide personal information to us.
We may use your personal information in many ways, including but not limited to when we deliver email and other direct marketing communications to you regarding special product offers, sales, promotions, or other information about our or our affiliates or our advertisers’ products and services, provided that you have given us your consent at the time we collect your personal information; administer sweepstakes, contests, games or other promotional programs or activities and notify winners of such programs or activities; provide you with valuable online services, such as news, weather and traffic alerts; respond to your questions or provide other requested information; notify you of important changes in the functionality of our website or other notices related to the handling of your personal information; and evaluate your qualifications for employment.

Our website may also enable you to submit personal information about other people. We may actively collect personal information about other people from you when you participate in various programs or when you submit news stories to us that involve other people.We generally use this personal information to process your request or to investigate and report new stories.

How do we passively collect and use information through tracking technology?

Our website may passively collect certain kinds of information that reveal the frequency with which you visit our website, the times and dates of your visits, the pages you reviewed, and other information, including Internet protocol (IP) addresses. Information is passively collected through various means. We use IP addresses to identify server problems, administer our website, and compile aggregated statistics for marketing and research purposes. We use navigational data, including log files and click-stream data, to identify information about your web browser and operating system, and the time period in which you visit our website or select pages or areas of our website. This information is also used to improve our website content.

Our website uses small text files called “cookies” that are sent to the hard drive of your computer to store bits of information related to your use of our website. Cookies can store a variety of information, including IP addresses, navigational data, server information, data transfer times, user preferences, and passwords needed to access our website. You may configure your browser to reject cookies. However, if you do so, you might not be able to access important functions or enjoy certain conveniences at our website.

Our website may also use “Web beacons” to help personalize your experience, similarly to how we use cookies. Third party companies that help us place advertisements on our website may use Web beacons to serve cookies and advertisements. We may also use Web beacons to gather non-personally identifiable information about the number of users who visit our website, the areas in which they spend their time visiting, and other demographic data.

Our website typically does not combine passively collected information (as described above) with actively collected personal information. We also reserve the right to introduce new passive tracking technologies and techniques related to the use of our website.

Do we share your personal information with third parties?

Except as stated in this Privacy Policy or unless your consent is obtained at the time of collection or prior to transfer, your personal information will not be shared with any third party outside of Journal Broadcast Group’s corporate parent, divisions, affiliates and service providers. We will share your personal information with our corporate parent, Journal Communications, and our divisions, affiliates and service providers primarily for business and operational purposes and occasionally for promotional and marketing purposes.

We may sometimes offer promotions or contests that are sponsored by or co-sponsored with third parties, in which we will provide you notice that your personal information will be shared with these identified third parties for purposes of fulfilling contest prizes and delivering email and other direct marketing communications to you regarding special product offers, sales, promotions, or other information about these third parties’ products and services that may be of interest to you.

We may share your personal information and non-identifying and aggregate information with our service providers, and our divisions and affiliates, to help us serve you better and to perform functions on our behalf in order to support our business and operation of our website as necessary, including without limitation to provide online services, such as news, weather and traffic alerts; to provide shopping services and fulfill and process orders for products and services; to process credit card payments; to send communications to you; to facilitate promotional marketing campaigns; to facilitate advertisements on our website; to provide technical support services, and to host our website on third party servers.

We also reserve the right to disclose your personal information to protect the security of our website; to protect ourselves and our corporate parent, divisions, and affiliates against liability; to respond to subpoenas, judicial processes, or government requests; to act in respect of an emergency that threatens life, health or security of an individual; to assist in the investigation of a contravention of a law; if we believe in good faith that the law requires it or authorizes it; or in connection with any proposed or actual purchase, sale, lease, merger, assignment, re-organization or financing of all or any portion of our station or any of the business or assets or shares of our station or a division thereof in order for you to continue to receive the same products and services from the third party.

We reserve the right to fully use, share, and process any information collected via our website that is not in personally identifiable form.

Please note that third parties are usually independent from us, and if you wish to stop all communications from third parties, you will need to address such issues with the third parties directly.

We are committed to protecting children’s privacy on the Internet and we comply fully with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If a child under the age of 13 has provided personal information to us via our website, we ask that a parent or guardian contact us using our email address below and we will delete the personal information about the child from our records. In the event that we otherwise learn that we have collected any personal information from a child under the age of 13, we will delete that information from our records.

This information would be used only for purposes of the program.

If you would like to opt-out of receiving direct marketing communications from non-affiliated third parties, our affiliates, and our station, please send your written request to us using our email address below and marking your request “Re: Your California Privacy Rights”. Your opt-out request will be processed within thirty (30) days of the date it was received. Please note that third parties are independent from our station and if you wish to stop all communications from third parties, you will need to address such issues with the third parties directly.
