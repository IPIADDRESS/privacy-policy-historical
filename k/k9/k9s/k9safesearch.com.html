Privacy Statement - March 29, 2016

 

BLUE COAT SYSTEMS, INC.

 

K9™ PRIVACY STATEMENT

FOR K9 WEB PROTECTION PERSONAL LICENSES

AND K9 SAFE SEARCH

 

 

Effective Date of this Privacy Statement: March 29, 2016

 

This statement describes how Blue Coat Systems, Inc. (“Blue Coat” or “we”) collects and uses your personal information, and how you can control certain uses of that information.

 

Scope of This Statement

 

This statement covers the personal information Blue Coat gathers when you access Blue Coat’s K9 Web Protection software and service ("K9") and K9 Safe Search (“Safe Search”, and collectively with K9, the “Services”).  For information about the personal information collected on the Blue Coat website or other specific programs, please see the Blue Coat Website Privacy Statement.  This statement does not cover K9 licensed under a commercial license; in that case, our corporate customer acts as data controller and has the obligation to provide notices to users. If you are using our Services under a corporate services arrangement, please check with your organization regarding its privacy practices and note that this statement does not apply to you. The Safe Search results are created with one or more third party search services (e.g. Yahoo!™ Search), which is controlled by and subject to corresponding third party privacy statement(s).

 

Safe Harbor Program

 

Blue Coat is a participant in the Safe Harbor program developed by the U.S. Department of Commerce, Switzerland and the European Union. We adhere to the Safe Harbor Privacy Principles agreed upon by the U.S. Department of Commerce and the E.U. For more information about the Safe Harbor and our certification, please see our General EEA/CH Safe Harbor Notice and visit the U.S. Department of Commerce's Safe Harbor website located at http://www.export.gov/safeharbor/.  If you have questions about Blue Coat’s participation in the Safe Harbor Framework, please send an email to privacy@bluecoat.com.

 

Collection of Personal Information Generally

 

As used in this statement, personal information includes information that may be used to identify you or another individual, such as a name and email address. We collect personal information in order to provide you with a better web experience, more customized emails, better products and services and to increase our responsiveness to customers and potential customers, as well as for the specific purposes and programs described below. This information may be gathered when you register for K9, use the Services or otherwise interact with us with regard to the Services.

 

Collection of Information through K9

 

Blue Coat collects personal information from you when you register to create a K9 account and when you use the K9 client.  The information Blue Coat may collect includes, for example, name, email address, installation date, uninstall date, last date seen, IP address for certain K9 requests (such as changing email address or requesting a password reset), K9 Version and Operating System, K9-generated license associated with your email address, the URL for which categorization is being requested, other meta-data about the request (such as referrer URL and user-agent (e.g., the browser type)), and the IP address of the resolved URL.  In addition, Blue Coat may collect other personal information (such as your phone number) through support requests.  Blue Coat will use such information to activate your K9 account and service, to provide you with the K9 service, and to provide you with new features and updates of the K9 service, in addition to the uses described below.

Collection of Information through Safe Search

 

No contact information is collected by Safe Search. Through Safe Search, Blue Coat collects information that your browser makes available. This information includes, for example, your Internet Protocol address, browser type, browser language, referral data and the date and time of your query.  Blue Coat will use such information to provide you with the Safe Search service.

Use of Information Collected Through the Services

 

In addition to activating your K9 account and providing you products and services, Blue Coat may use the information collected by the Services for the following purposes: to improve Blue Coat’s products and services, to inform you about products, seminars and services Blue Coat believes may be of interest to you; to contact you if Blue Coat needs to obtain or provide additional information; to comply with applicable laws and protect our and others’ legal rights; and to verify the accuracy of Blue Coat’s records.  Blue Coat may also use your information to send you, or to have our business partners send you, direct marketing information or to contact you for market research. We aim to obtain appropriate consent (where required under applicable law) to send you marketing communications.

 

We use standard technologies, such as cookies and other HTML tools as described in the Blue Coat Cookie Statement, which Blue Coat may amend from time to time. We do not allow third parties to collect personally identifiable information directly from users of the Services and currently we do not respond to ‘do not track’ signals or similar mechanisms.

Opting out of Marketing Communications

If you do not wish to receive marketing communications from Blue Coat, you can unsubscribe from future marketing communications by following the opt-out instructions contained in any such marketing communications or sending an e-mail to K9support@bluecoat.com.  Should you for any reason continue to receive unsolicited and undesired communication from Blue Coat, please forward the communication and your concerns to privacy@bluecoat.com.

We cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you provide to us, and we do not accept liability for unintentional disclosure.

You are responsible for the accuracy of the data that you provide to Blue Coat. Blue Coat will use reasonable efforts to maintain the accuracy and integrity of any personal information it receives.

 

Updating Your Personal Information

 

You can update your email address through the K9 user interface.  Should you need to update any other information used by the Services, please forward the details to K9support@bluecoat.com.

Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to request access to and receive information about the personal information we maintain about you, update and correct inaccuracies in your personal information, and have the information blocked or deleted, as appropriate.  You also may have the right to object to our processing of your personal information and withdraw your consent to our processing of your personal information at any time.  The right to access personal information may be limited in some circumstances by local law requirements.  Please note that you may not be able to benefit from all features of the Services if you request the deletion of your personal information, object or withdraw your consent to the processing your personal information.
