Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

1. protect and defend our rights and property;

2. protect the personal safety or property of others.

2. Your IP address (the unique identifier associated with your server) may be used to help diagnose issues with our server and for administration purposes.

3. Information gathered anonymously regarding the demographic distribution of our visitors, date and time of the visits, browser information and other site-traffic statistics would be used by our team to improve the website and serve you more efficiently.
