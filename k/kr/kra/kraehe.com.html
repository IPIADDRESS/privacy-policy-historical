The legal bases of these transfers are Article 6 (1) (b) and Article 6 (1) (f) of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Transfers based on Article 6 (1) (f) GDPR may only be made to the extent necessary to safeguard the legitimate interests of this website or third parties and not outweigh the interests or fundamental rights and freedoms of the data subject requiring the protection of personal data. The exchange of data with CRIF Bürgel GmbH also serves the fulfillment of legal obligations to carry out creditworthiness checks of customers (§ 505a and 506 of the German Civil Code). CRIF Bürgel GmbH processes the data received and also uses it for the purpose of profiling (scoring) in order to provide its contracting parties in the European Economic Area and in Switzerland and, if applicable, other third countries (if there is an adequacy decision by the European Commission) information on, inter alia: Assessing the creditworthiness of natural persons. Use of cookies


On various pages we use cookies to make the visit to our website attractive and user-friendly, as well as to make it more secure and to use certain features enable. These are small text files that are stored on your computer. Most of the cookies we use are deleted from your hard drive after the end of the browser session (so-called session cookies). Other cookies remain on your computer and allow us to recognize your computer on your next visit (so-called persistent cookies). These cookies make it unnecessary for you to re-enter your password or fill in forms with your data for subsequent orders. Our partner companies are not permitted to collect, process or use personal data via cookies via our website. Most browsers accept cookies by default. You can allow or prohibit temporary and saved cookies in the security settings independently of each other. If you disable cookies, certain features on our site may not be available to you, and some pages may not be displayed correctly. The data stored in our cookies are not linked to your personal data (name, address, etc.). Third parties, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on previous visits to our website. You can disable the use of cookies by Google by visiting the page that disables Google advertising. Alternatively, you can disable the use of third-party cookies by visiting the opt-out page of the Network Advertising Initiative.
