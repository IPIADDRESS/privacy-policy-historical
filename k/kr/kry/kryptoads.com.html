Cookies
Like most Internet sites, we and our ad serving party use "cookies" to enhance the Internet user's experience on the Web. Neither we nor such third party ad serving party uses these cookies to personally identify you in any way, and these cookies in no way damage your system or files.
A cookie is a unique number that is assigned to you the first time you are served an ad. This number is stored in a file on your computer. For instance, cookies help ensure that you don't repeatedly see the same ad.
Cookies can also help advertisers measure how you utilize an advertiser's site and the effectiveness of an ad. This information can then be used by advertisers to better cater to your needs. In other words, cookies help us deliver to you the types of ads in which you are more interested.
Although we believe that cookies help enhance your experience on the Web we or our third ad serving party do not need cookies to deliver ads to you.
If you do not want the benefits of cookies, there is a simple procedure that allows you to deny or accept this feature. In order to "opt out" of the benefits of cookies, you may either manually delete your cookies by following the instructions contained in the Help section of your browser, or you can opt out reading informations contained in the following link https://www.aboutcookies.org or you may click here to prevent our cookies from being stored on your browser.
