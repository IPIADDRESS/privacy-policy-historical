Updated 24-5-2018

Related: terms and conditions | cookie policy | refund policy

We know that you care how information about you is used, stored and shared and we appreciate your trust in us to do that carefully and sensibly.

We acknowledge and agree that any of your personal data will be processed in accordance with all applicable data protection laws. By using Kyero.com, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

As far as is reasonably possible, we will ensure all data is:

  • processed lawfully, fairly and in a transparent manner
  • collected for specified, explicit and legitimate purposes
  • adequate, relevant and not excessive
  • accurate and up to date
  • not kept for longer than necessary
  • processed in accordance with your rights
  • secure

For us to carry out our services, we will ask for your consent to process your data when necessary, and you have the right to withdraw this consent at any time.

This policy applies to any website we own, such as www.kyero.com, and other interactions you may have with us. If you do not agree with the terms, please do not access or use our services.

What information do we collect?

Personal data

While using Kyero.com, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that may include, but is not limited to:

Like most websites, we also gather certain information automatically. This information may include:

Internet protocol (IP) addresses, device and browser type, Internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, the pages viewed on our site, operating system, date/time stamps, and/or other data to analyse trends and administer the site.

For more details about how we use these technologies, please see our cookie policy.

Other data submitted to our websites, such as if you participate in a survey, apply for a job, request support, interact with our social media accounts or otherwise communicate with Kyero, may also be stored.

No one is under any obligation to provide any Additional data. However, you might not be able to make use of some of the features at Kyero.com.
