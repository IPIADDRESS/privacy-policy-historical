We care about the privacy of each user of the web site.

This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that we do not own or control or to people that we do not employ or manage.

Identifying the Purpose of Personal Information:

When you use this web site some basic information is collected.
The web site collects, at time of your registration and your sign-on to the web site, certain "personal information" that you voluntarily give us (information that personally identifies you) such as your name, email address, home or work address, telephone number.

The web site may automatically receive and record basic general non personally-identifiable information from your browser including IP address, browser type, access times, and page you requested for the limited purposes to compile statistics to improve the web site and make your use of the site efficient . When you visit the web site, a cookie may be placed on your computer or the cookie may be read if you have visited the site previously. The site uses cookies to allow to determine which products and services you use and the support you may need and to help detect and solve any problems you may have when using the web site. If you wish, you may set your browser to reject all cookies.

We use information for the following general Identified Purposes:
To customize the advertising and content you see on the web pages, to fulfill your requests for certain products and services and to contact you on an opt-in basis about specials and new products.

Consent for Collection, Use or Disclosure

We do not collect personal information without an individual's consent.
We make every reasonable effort to make sure that an individual understands how her or his personal information will be used.
Registration for the site's products and services by a customer will constitute the customer's consent for the web site to collect, use and disclose personal information under this Privacy Policy. We will normally seek a customer's consent for any new uses and disclosures of the customer's personal information, either at the same time it collects the personal information or after it has been collected but before it is used or disclosed for a new purpose. An individual may withdraw such consent for collection, use and disclosure at any time, on prior written notice.

Limiting the Collecting, Using, Disclosing and Keeping Personal Information

We limit its collection of personal information to only that information which is necessary for the Identified Purposes. The web site does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We will not sell, share or trade your personal information with others.
We do not disclose individual personal information to others, except if required in the course of fulfilling the identified purpose for which it was collected.

Anti-Spam

The web site is committed to permission-based (opted-in) email marketing practices.
We do not participate in spamming. Those who enter into business relationships with us (including, but not limited to, affiliates ) and choose to promote the site's products or services, or the site's affiliated products or services using spam will have their relationship with us immediately terminated.
We respect the request of any individual asking to have his/her e-mail address opted-in and opted-out of our database. As well, every e-mail sent by us provides an automated unsubscribe option at the bottom of each e-mail. All other unsubscribe requests can easily be processed by sending an e-mail, including the e-mail address you wish to remove.

Accuracy of Personal Information

We will use reasonable efforts to keep customer personal information accurate for the Identified Purposes. Customers are responsible for informing us about changes to their personal information.

Security Safeguards

We will use reasonable efforts to protect personal information against loss, unauthorized access, disclosure, misuse or modification, through security safeguards that depend on the level of sensitivity of the information.

Access to Personal Information

At the written request of individual we will tell him or her what personal information we have about the individual , what is being used for and to whom it has been disclosed.
