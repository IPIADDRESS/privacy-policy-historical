Please note that any video, image, or other content posted at the direction of Users onto the Website becomes published content and is not considered personally identifiable information subject to this Privacy Policy.

Applicability.

This Privacy Policy only applies to Websites owned and/or operated by or for 8coupons, and upon which 8coupons has placed a “link” which, when “clicked”, transfers you to this Privacy Policy. Personally Identifiable Information: You provide certain personally identifiable information (such as your name, address, birth date or e-mail address and referred to in this Privacy Policy as “PI”) to 8coupons when choosing to participate in various activities on the Website such as uploading videos, posting reviews in our forums, message board, or in the video comments section if applicable, entering contests or sweepstakes, taking advantage of promotions, responding to surveys, or subscribing to newsletters or other mailing lists.  Generally, you may browse our Website without providing us with PI. However, there may be times when we ask you to provide us with certain PI, such as when you choose to register with our Website. You can choose not to provide certain information, but then you may not be able to participate in or view some of the Website’s features. 8coupons does not sell, rent or share PI collected with other people other than that specifically described herein.

If you are under 13 years of age, do not send any information about yourself to 8coupons. If we discover that a child under the age of 13 has provided the Website with PI, we will delete that child’s PI from its system.
  3. Anonymous Information: When you visit the Website, our web servers automatically collect website usage information. Website usage information is anonymous information that describes how our visitors use and navigate our Website, and it includes the number and frequency of visitors to each web page, video and the time-span of their stays. 8coupons collects the anonymous information automatically sent to us by your web browser, referrer data that identifies the web page visited prior and subsequent to visiting our Website, and IP address. Cookies Information: When you visit the Website, 8coupons may send one or more cookies - a small text file containing a string of alphanumeric characters - to your computer that uniquely identifies your browser. 8coupons does not store any PI in cookies, but it may link the cookie to any information it collects. You can reset your web browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. Clear GIFs Information: When you use the Website, 8coupons may employ “clear GIFs” which are used to track the online usage patterns of our Users anonymously (i.e., in a non-personally-identifiable manner). In addition, we may also use clear GIFs in HTML-based emails sent to our users to track which emails are opened by recipients.
