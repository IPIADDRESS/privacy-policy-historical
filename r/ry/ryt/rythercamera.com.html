Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. We offer a Secure Socket Layer server on our ordering pages by which you will send your ordering information so the data will be sent and is able to be viewed ONLY by  authorized persons at RytherCamera.com.
Information collected is protected by the Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) protocol, which prevents any sensitive information from being compromised.

To be sure you're browsing secure pages, check your Web browser's status bar (located at the bottom of the window) for the closed padlock icon. This icon appears in the latest versions of most browsers such as Internet Explorer and Firefox.  Also, all browsers display an "s" after the "http" (https://…) in the Web site address to indicate that you're in a secure environment.

At RytherCamera.com, we are devoted to protecting your privacy online. Your Private Information will never be disclosed to anyone and will be used for business purposes only. All orders are placed through a secure online server. We Use VeriSign, a trusted third party eCommerce Security Company to assure your security-Only authorized persons have access to your information.

We also protect your purchase by preventing the shipments to a fraudulent address. All billing addresses are verified electronically and all alternate shipping addresses are verified manually. Any customer shipping to an address that differs from the billing address must put this alternate address on file with their credit card company. We then call the credit card issuer to verify that the customer has indeed put the information on file. If your uncomfortable ordering online you can always order by telephone, toll free 1-877-644-7593.
