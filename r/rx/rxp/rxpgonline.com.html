  • Privacy Statement for THE SITE

  • Information Automatically Logged
    RxPG use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer     THE SITE. Your IP address is also used to gather broad demographic information, to help cater THE SITE's content to your needs and other site management issues. 
     
  • Cookies
    RxPG sites can uses cookies to remember your login name and pass it between forms, so that you do not have to enter it on every page of database operation. Cookies are not used to automatically log you in to the website at every visit as we do not provide any remember me option on login . Whenever member sign in feature is used, the logout option is prominently displayed on every page and on being clicked will erase the cookies set in your computer.
     
  • Advertisers
    RxPG use an outside ad company to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. While RxPG use cookies in other parts of     THE SITE, cookies received with banner ads are collected by RxPG ad company, and RxPG do not have access to this information.

    THE SITE might make chat rooms, forums, message boards, and/or news groups available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.

    THE SITE has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control.
