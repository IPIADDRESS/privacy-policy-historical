Cookies

Like many websites, the Site uses a software technology called "cookies" to collect non-personally identifiable information. Cookies contain information that we may place on your computer to help personalize your experience at the Site and to keep track of your user name and password so you do not have to re-enter them each time you visit the Site. We also use cookies to collect information about the pages that you visit at our Site in a given session of use, and may also use them to keep track of where you left off in a game. Except with respect to our e-commerce activities (e.g., on-line purchases), we do not link the non-personally identifiable information in cookies with personally identifiable information about you. Third party advertisers on the Site may also place cookies on your browser to gather information; they use your information in accordance with their own privacy policies, over which we have no control. If you wish to disable cookies, the "help" portion of the toolbar on most browsers will tell you how.
