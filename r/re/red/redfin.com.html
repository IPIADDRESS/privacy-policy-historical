Privacy Policy

Redfin's Privacy Summary

  • We will never spam you.
  • We will not sell, distribute, or reveal your email address or other account information without your consent.
  • We safeguard your information under lock and key.

Privacy Policy

We know that you care about how your personal information is used and shared, and we take your privacy very seriously. By visiting the Redfin website or making use of our mobile applications, you are accepting the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use.

Information Collected by Redfin

We collect personal information to: 1) Grant you full and complete access to MLS information 2) Ensure the safety of our agents while on a tour 3) Help you buy or sell your home while using one of our brokerage agents. This information allows us to provide you and other users with a customized, efficient, and generally awesome experience.

Information You Provide to Us

We receive and store information you enter on our website or provide to us in any other way. You can choose not to provide us with specific information, but you may not be able to take advantage of all of our features. Redfin personnel may have access to user information if necessary to the normal course of Redfin business, and all will be held to high standards regarding the use of your data.

Registration

In order for you to use My Redfin or our brokerage services, such as saved searches, favorites, or tours, you must complete a registration form. As part of this registration form, we require select personal information, including but not limited to your full name and e-mail address.

My Redfin

Reviews and Ratings

We pride ourselves on transparency, and we survey all of our clients to periodically rate their experiences using our website or agents. This information may be displayed on the website in complete or aggregate form. We may also share this information directly with agents for quality control and training.

Financial Information

In order to determine which homes are in your price range, and to help you make an offer more quickly when you do find a home, we may ask you to get pre-approved with a mortgage broker or lender, and to provide this pre-approval information to one of our agents. No financial information obtained from clients will be posted to the website.

Forum Posts

Any information you post to our forums will be available publicly.

Automatic Information

We receive and store information whenever you interact with us. This includes traffic and IP address data from our server logs, Redfin cookie information, and the page you requested. Redfin uses this information to help diagnose server problems, analyze trends, and administer the website.

In addition, when you are signed in and using Redfin through Facebook, Twitter, or any other third-party authorization service, we may make use of the data that they provide us to further customize your Redfin experience.

Email Communications

We will never provide your email address to a third party without your explicit permission. Redfin may send out opt-in e-mails with Redfin-related news, surveys or promotions. You may also subscribe to Listing Alert Emails with customized summaries of homes for sale.

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us, please visit the Email Preferences section of your My Redfin: Alerts & Emails and/or follow the instructions contained in the unwanted e-mail message.
