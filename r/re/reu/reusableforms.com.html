ReusableForms.com Privacy Policy

GATHERING OF INFORMATION

ReusableForms.com may gather personal information about its visitors for internal use only. This includes, but is not limited to, customer downloads, information requests, and purchasing of software.

USE OF INFORMATION

Information gathered on our website is used for internal purposes related to accounting and site demographics. Additionally, ReusableForms.com may send announcements to our customers via email.

Customers include people who have purchased our products, downloaded them, or requested more information. Announcements that you receive from ReusableForms.com via email will contain information on how to unsubscribe from the newsletter.

SHARING OF INFORMATION

ReusableForms.com does NOT share your personal information in any way with any third parties.
