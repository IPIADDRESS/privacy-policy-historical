Our Privacy Policy

Short Version:
Retro64, Inc. keeps your personal information strictly confidential and never sells any of your information to third parties.


Long Version:
We collect anonymous information about users browsing our web site.  This is done in order to create the best possible browsing experience.  This information is not linked to an individual user.  What information do we collect and how do we do it?  Please read on.

IP Logging:
Like almost every other web site, we record your IP address along with the pages browsed on our web site.  This information is used in our statistics program to better understand how people browse Retro64.com.

Cookies:
Retro64.com uses cookies to provide user tracking.  No personal information is stored in these cookies- they are simply used to gather statistical data.

Email Newsletter:
Our newsletter is strictly run as an "opt-in" newsletter.  We never send unsolicited emails.  Also, your personal information, including email address, is never shared or sold to anyone.

Web Links:
We link to other game companies and web sites on our "Links" page.  Obviously, we cannot guarantee or be held responsible for the privacy or content of these web sites.

Security:
We have taken the appropriate steps and followed industry standards to secure any personal data stored on our web servers.
