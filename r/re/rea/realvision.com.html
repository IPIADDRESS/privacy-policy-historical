Opt-Out/Remove/Change/Modify - Our users have the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us at any time. To remove, change or modify any information you have provided to us from our database, or to no longer receive our service, please make such a request by e-mailing us or, to no longer receive communications from us, by replying to any e-mail you receive from us with the word "REMOVE" on the subject line.
