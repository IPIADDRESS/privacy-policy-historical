REALNETWORKS PRIVACY PRINCIPLES
RealNetworks does not share with third parties any personal information you provide in connection with our products, without first obtaining your informed consent.
Third parties, who may have access to personal information, include contractors or service vendors performing work on behalf of RealNetworks and are contractually subject to RealNetworks privacy policies and confidentiality agreements. In the event that court orders or laws mandate RealNetworks to disclose personal information, RealNetworks will release the personal information as required by law.

RealNetworks maintains controls to provide security over personal information, including credit card details, during and after the purchase process.
The controls include:
The use of secure connections from the customer's Web browser to RealNetworks.
The use of encryption for sensitive personal information, such as credit card numbers and user names.
The logical security of the databases that store customers' credit card numbers.
Security controls to restrict access to databases housing personally identifiable information.
Requirements that employees, contractors, and service vendors acting as agents of RealNetworks sign an agreement noting that they have read, understand, and will adhere to RealNetworks confidentiality policies.
Quality assurance procedures during product development requiring that new and upgraded products be specifically tested to ensure that they do not contradict or violate RealNetworks Privacy Policy.

RealNetworks sends you e-mail regarding products and services only if you have indicated you wish to receive e-mail from us. You may remove yourself from our e-mailing list at any time.
RealNetworks only sends e-mail to customers regarding RealNetworks products and other promotions, RealNetworks support, and RealNetworks services if the customer wishes to receive these e-mails. The customer has the ability to opt-out of these communications during the registration process, through the removal option posted throughout RealNetworks Web sites and by visiting the RealNetworks My Account area.

RealNetworks does not create individual user profiles based on the specific content that is played through our products.
RealNetworks does not create individual user profiles based on the specific content, such as the songs and videos played via RealPlayer and RealJukebox. We use cookies to verify your access rights to premium content and to enable our product features. This Privacy Policy explains our current use of cookies with respect to user profiles. RealNetworks retains aggregated log files of requested content in order to report overall product usage to premium content partners and we may use player usage data to build aggregated, anonymous data profiles. It is RealNetworks policy to undergo, on a periodic basis, independent third party audits of our privacy practices to ensure compliance with this assertion.

RealNetworks responds to your privacy questions.
You may contact us at http://forms.real.com/service/contact/privacy.html.

RealNetworks is constantly innovating and implementing new features in its products to provide the best possible user experience. Because our software, services and web properties are constantly changing and improving, this policy will continue to evolve as our software evolves. We want to make sure you have the most up-to-date information when we release new products and revise existing products.

Cookies
What is a cookie?
A “cookie” is a small element of data that a web site like Real.com can store on your system. It can be used to provide you with a tailored user experience. RealNetworks uses two types of cookies: browser cookies, which are sent to your Internet browser, and RealPlayer cookies, which are sent to the version of RealPlayer you are currently using. Both types of cookies store these small pieces of data on your machine so we may tailor your experience using our products and visiting our web sites.

How does RealNetworks use cookies?
Like many web and e-commerce sites, RealNetworks uses cookies to “recognize” your Internet browsing and RealPlayer software in order to display information that is relevant to you and to cache some elements of our web sites and services to improve their performance. When you visit our web sites and use our products and services, RealNetworks cookies are sent to our servers.

Primarily, we use cookies to better personalize the specific pages, content, banners, and promotions that you will see when you visit RealNetworks web sites and use RealNetworks products and services. For example, we are able to deliver radio stations to your RealOne Player based on zip code information you provide to us.

We also use cookies to understand traffic patterns on RealNetworks web sites so that we can enhance your experience and provide you with more relevant information about our products and the content available on our web sites.

In addition, we use cookies to authorize access to certain premium or trial content, programming, services or software features. Information is stored in a browser cookie so you can access so that you can access premium Web pages and in a RealPlayer cookie so that you can play premium audio and video.

We treat any personally identifiable information that may be contained in cookies with the same confidentiality as when you purchase something on one of our web sites.

What information does RealNetworks put into user cookies?
When you download or purchase a product from us, create a user account, sign-in to our products and services or visit RealNetworks web sites we may set both browser and RealPlayer cookies on your personal computer. These cookies may contain values for:
Cookie version
User name (your encrypted e-mail address)
User ID (your numeric representation in the RealNetworks database)
Your encrypted name
Your location information, including zip or postal code, what country you are in and your preferred language
Information about the product you are using, including the products name, type, version and build number
Each RealNetworks premium service you have purchased, your current service status, the premium features the service entitles you to and the service expiration date. This may also include your current available RealOne Music download and stream count
Your RealNetworks content preferences
Your age and gender
Your connection speed
Information on radio stations you have recently played
Information on your local radio stations
When the cookie was last updated
When the cookie is set to expire

Information that we store about you in cookies (such as user name and zip code) is a reflection of information that you have provided us during product download, purchase, registration and installation (please see What Information We Collect During Product Downloads, Purchases and Installation). You can view and edit this information by visiting the My Account area on Real.com. Editing this data will update information stored in your cookies.

This cookie data is read by RealNetworks for the purposes stated above and is not read by third parties.

You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a browser cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether or not to accept it. You may also change your RealPlayer cookie settings through preferences options in our products (please see RealNetworks Products below for more information).

RealNetworks does not require that you accept cookies, however, some functionality on our web sites, our product purchase check-out process, and products and services may be impaired if you decline to accept cookies.

IP Address
What is an IP address?
Your computer uses IP addresses every time you are connected to the Internet. Your IP address is a number that is used by computers on the network to identify your computer so that data (such as the Web pages you request) can be sent to you. Our Web servers automatically collect IP addresses. From time to time, RealNetworks may use information derived from your IP address to deliver you appropriate products, services and software and to prevent fraud. For example, RealNetworks may block the ability to purchase specific RealOne services if we detect an international IP address and we are prohibited from selling that specific service outside of the United States.

Referring URLs aka "Referrers"
What is a Referrer?
A "Referrer" is the information passed along by a web browser that references the Web URL you linked from to visit our web sites. It is automatically collected by our Web servers. This information is collected and used by RealNetworks to understand the web sites referring traffic to our download and order pages and to present appropriate content to our potential customers. Referrer information is tabulated by RealNetworks on an aggregated basis to identify general trends and traffic patterns.

From time to time, RealNetworks may enter into agreements with content partners that require RealNetworks to count the number of product downloads and/or product sales stemming from a referral from a content partner site. This information is collected, used and aggregated by RealNetworks to fulfill the reporting requirements of those agreements. This information may also be combined with the referrer information described above and tabulated on an aggregated basis to identify general trends and traffic patterns. At the time of product download and/or purchase, information you provide to RealNetworks, including personal information and referring URL's, is collected in our web server logs and customer database and is kept strictly confidential and not shared with third parties.

Unique Identifiers (GUIDs)
What is a GUID?
A Globally Unique Identifier (GUID) is an alphanumeric identifier used to indicate a unique installation of a product, and is found in many popular software applications such as web browsers and media players. RealNetworks and third party content providers use GUIDs for aggregate statistical analysis, to authenticate premium content and to personalize products and services.

RealNetworks Use of GUIDS
Some RealNetworks products, including RealPlayer 8 and RealOne Player, randomly generate a GUID during installation. RealNetworks uses publicly documented standards to generate GUIDs. From time to time, RealNetworks may use a GUID with a unique set of numbers in order to gather and aggregate information about traffic patterns, such as how many times a unique RealPlayer attempts to access content from a server. These statistics help us improve performance and reduce network congestion, providing our users with faster and more reliable access to a wide variety of content.

We collect data marked by RealPlayer GUIDs in order to understand aggregated traffic patterns and other RealPlayer usage patterns. We use this data in order to measure product performance, better understand types of content that are popular with our users and how our products are used, protect against system failure, analyze how our products inter-operate with portable devices and CD burners, and to conduct user surveys. We do not relate GUID marked traffic data to other personal information supplied by users, unless we obtain user consent to do so. Further, consistent with our privacy principles, RealNetworks does not sell or share such information with any third party unless we obtain a user’s advance informed consent, nor do we relate .
