PRIVACY IN A PUBLIC

Any information disclosed on the REALPOOR.COM Discussion Boards, or comments and replies in other sections (Articles, News, Videos) will be visible in public, in exception of "Elite Trading" section which hides all the information posted by the user, which means all the private data from Elite Trading will remain confidential.
