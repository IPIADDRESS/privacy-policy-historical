Privacy Policy

RealMe will collect and use the information you provide for the purpose for which it is provided. We will hold the information securely and will only use it in developing and running RealMe and/or providing you information about services that we think might be of interest to you. If you ask us not to provide you with this information, we will comply with your request. You may ask us to show you the personal information we hold about you and to make corrections to it.
