We will NEVER mine your customer database, we treat it like the Intellectual Property of YOURS that it is.  Your data is YOUR data, not ours.  If you ever want us to remove it, we will.

Once RepairShopr receives your transmission, we make commercially reasonable efforts to ensure its security on our system. More information about security is available on the Site and in our Terms of Use.
