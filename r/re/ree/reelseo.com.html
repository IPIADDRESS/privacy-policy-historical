At ReelSEO, we have created this privacy statement in order to show our commitment to your privacy. Information Gathering

As with most other websites, we automatically collect and utilize the data contained in our site log files. The information in the log files will include your IP (internet protocol) address, your ISP, browser used (IE, Firefox, Safari, etc…), the date and time you visited our site and which URLs you visited throughout our site.

We track your activity with Google Analytics, Quantcast, and WordPress Analytics.

We run our own advertising system which does not utilize cookies. Some of our advertisers may choose to host their own advertisement, and could thus use it set a cookie for tracking purposes. Cookies are used on ReelSEO only for login, commenting, and other related community purposes.
