This site also uses "cookies," which are pieces of information that are transferred to an individual user's hard drive for record-keeping purposes. Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. If you would prefer, you can set your browser to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. However, it may be possible that some parts of the site will not function optimally if you do so.
