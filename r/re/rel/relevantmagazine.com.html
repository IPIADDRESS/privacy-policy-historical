III. COOKIES

This site also uses "cookies," which are pieces of information that are transferred to an individual user's hard drive for record-keeping purposes. RELEVANT uses both first and third party cookies to collect information but never uses cookies to retrieve information from your computer that was not originally sent in a cookie. Cookies function by saving your passwords and site preferences—and as a result, it is possible to speed up your future activities at our site and allow us to provide you information specifically tailored to your interests. Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. If you would prefer, you can set your browser to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. However, it may be possible that some parts of the site will not function optimally if you do so.
