Privacy

digitalrelevanceTM is committed to maintaining your confidence and trust, and accordingly maintains the following privacy policy to protect your personal information and respect your contact preference. In addition, digitalrelevance is opposed to unauthorized commercial email delivery, otherwise known as spam. By using our website, you are agreeing with the policies described below so it is encouraged that you read them carefully.

digitalrelevance’s Commitment to Security

Personal information is accessible only by staff and volunteers designated to. We promise never to sell, trade, share, reveal, deliver, publicize or market your email address in any way.

Personal Information that digitalrelevance May Collect

In order to access special opportunities and resources on our website (relevance.com), we may require personal information. That information will only be collected if you choose to provide it. digitalrelevance collects the following types of personal information: names, work and personal e-mail addresses, phone and facsimile numbers, company name, company website, job title and revenue.

digitalrelevance does not knowingly collect or maintain any personal information from children under the age of 13. In addition, no part of our site is designed with the purpose of attracting any person under age 13.

How digitalrelevance May Use Personal Information Collected

Unless you consent, digitalrelevance will use your personal information for the purpose for which it was submitted such as to reply to inquiries, handle your business requests related to program participation, and we will use such information to provide operational notices, and in program record keeping and to conduct research on industry marketplace practices. Our research may lead to the publication of aggregate demographic data but will not result in the reporting or publication of any personal information provided to us. At certain points where personal information is collected on our site, there may be a box where you may indicate you would like to be on a list to receive information about other digitalrelevance programs and services. At any time you can add or remove your name from our mailing list by contacting us at info@relevance.com.

How Your Information May Be Shared

Just as in any research we may do, digitalrelevance research will not result in the reporting or publication of any personal information. digitalrelevance never sells or rents personal information.

Help Us Keep Your Personal Information Accurate

If your personal information changes or you would like to review the personal information we may have on file, please email us with the new information or your review request at info@relevance.com. Let us also know the digitalrelevance program that led to your submission of personal information so we may efficiently locate your information.

Computer Tracking and Cookies

Our web site is not set up to track, collect or distribute personal information not entered by its visitors. Our site logs do generate certain kinds of non-identifying site usage data, such as the number of hits and visits to our site. This information is used for internal purposes by technical support staff to provide better services to the public and may also be provided to others, but again, the statistics contain no personal information and cannot be used to gather such information.

A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a Web server and stored on your computer’s hard drive. digitalrelevance uses non-identifying cookies to provide easier site navigation and access to forms. It also provides us with information that can help us better serve your experience on our site. You can still use digitalrelevance’s site if your browser is set to reject cookies. Our cookies do not generate personal data, do not read personal data from your machine and are never tied to anything that could be used to identify you.

Our server logs automatically collect information such as your IP address. Your IP address is a number that is used by computers connected to the Internet to identify your computer so that data (such as the Web pages you request) can be sent to you. This information does not identify you personally. We use IP addresses for purposes such as calculating Site usage levels, helping diagnose server problems, administering the Site, and tracking your Internet usage, including identifying your geographic location which helps us understand movements and trends around our site.

Links to Other Websites

The digitalrelevance site contains links to other websites whose privacy policy may differ. If you submit any information to these sites, we encourage you to carefully review their privacy policy to understand how they will handle your personal information.

Problems

If you have a complaint about digitalrelevance‘s compliance with this privacy policy, you may contact us at info@relevance.com.
