Privacy Policy

Reviews.com is committed to protecting the privacy of your personal information. We take your privacy seriously and have established this privacy statement to disclose our information practices.

Information Collection

All web servers track basic information about their visitors. This information includes IP addresses, browser details, timestamps, and referring pages. None of this information can identify specific visitors who visit our website. The information is used for administration and maintenance purposes. Visitors to our website will remain completely anonymous while browsing. When a user elects to contact us with an inquiry, we collect the information they choose to share with us. We use this information to respond to users in regards to their inquiry. We don’t store this personal information for any other purposes and we don’t share it with third parties for their promotional purposes. We don’t send unsolicited messages to users of our website and we don’t store the personal information provided to us beyond responding to any inquiry.

Cookies and Web Beacons

When necessary, our website uses cookies to store information about a user’s preferences and history in order to better serve the user. We don’t tie cookies to any personal information. We may also employ the use of web beacons in monitoring page views and traffic. We may from time to time employ the use of a third party agent in tracking aggregated non-personal statistical data about our users. This third party agent may use cookies or web beacons.

Controlling Privacy

You can change your browser settings to disable cookies if you have any privacy concerns. Disabling cookies for all sites isn’t recommended because it may interfere with your use of some sites. The best option is to disable or enable cookies on a per-site basis. We reserve the right to modify this privacy statement at our discretion. We shall notify you of any changes made by updating the date on this statement, and we recommend you check back regularly to stay informed of any changes.

Contact Information

Concerns or questions about this privacy policy can be directed to our Contact page. We can also be reached by mail at 111 S. Jackson St, Suite #500, Seattle, WA 98104.
