    The technologies used on the Sites and Services to collect Usage Information include:

    Cookies and Flash Cookies -Cookies and Flash Cookies are small data files (text or alphanumeric strings) placed on a  computer, mobile device, browser, application, technology or other device (collectively, "System") when it is used to visit the Sites or access content that uses the Services. Cookies are also used as part of the promotion request and we may use Cookies to collect and store certain information about your interactions with our Sites or content that uses the Services such as the number of prints and shares. Flash Cookies are different from browser cookies because of the amount of, type of, and how data is stored. Cookie management tools provided by your browser may not remove Flash Cookies.     Pixels and Web Beacons - We may also include small graphic images or other web programming code called web beacons (also known as Internet tags, pixel tags, tracking beacons and clear GIFs) on the Sites (including in promotions using the Services), which we may use to collect information regarding your interaction with content on the Sites. Web beacons or similar technologies may be used for a number of purposes, including without limitation, to obtain information such as the URL of the page on which the beacon appears or the time the page containing the beacon was viewed, to measure promotion effectiveness through the Services, or to optimize promotion performance.  Pixels and Web Beacons may also be placed by third parties on content on the Sites.  This privacy policy does not govern the use of such third party Pixels and Web Beacons as they are not in our control.

    Referral Header - A Referral Header is a piece of data that identifies the address of the webpage that linked to the Sites being requested.  We may utilize a Referral Header to understand where the request for the Sites originated.

    Unique Identifiers - A Unique Identifier is a number that is automatically assigned to your System when you access a web site or its servers, and our systems may identify your System by its Unique Identifier. A Unique Identifier may include your IP address or other unique identifier.

    Executable File - An Executable File is a downloadable software application file that contains instructions that perform tasks on a computer.  We may use an Executable File to control the print limits of a promotion.

    Java Applet - A Java Applet is a downloadable software file that contains instructions that perform tasks on a computer initiated by your web browser. We may use a Java Applet to control the print limits of a promotion.
