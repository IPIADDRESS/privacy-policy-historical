Unique Identifiers - A Unique Identifier is a number that is automatically assigned to your System when you access a web site or its servers, and our systems may identify your System by its Unique Identifier. A Unique Identifier may include your IP address or other unique identifier.

Executable File - An Executable File is a downloadable software application file that contains instructions that perform tasks on a computer. We may use an Executable File to control the print limits of a promotion.
