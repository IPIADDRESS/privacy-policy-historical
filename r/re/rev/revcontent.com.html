Privacy Policy

Modified on: Tue, Nov 11, 2014 at 11:02 AM

RevContent, LLC (“RevContent”) is the operator of this website and the operator of a network platform through which web properties can display certain third party content such as articles, videos, blog posts, and advertisements on their sites. RevContent takes your privacy very seriously. We remain dedicated to providing transparency with respect to how we collect and use any of your information, and this Privacy Policy (the “Policy”) will explain in detail.


If you have any questions or need to contact us, please do so at compliance@revcontent.com or by phone at 941.225.6132.


In order to improve upon the service we provide our users, we collect certain information about your use of our website and the instances where you view sponsored content served through our widget. This information may include, but is not limited to, the following: information about the software or hardware you are utilizing, IP addresses, and/or cookies which can identify your account.

We use information we collect from advertisers and/or publishers for invoicing and billing purposes.


We also use certain information we collect from the website and/or from the content recommendation widget to maintain and constantly improve upon the services we offer. Additionally, information we collect from these sources may be used to determine specific content that is most relevant to you and ensure that such content is disseminated to you.


If you provide us with your email address, we may use this to contact you regarding our services or to keep you abreast of any changes or revisions to the services offered but as with any of our communications you can opt out of receiving further communications at any time.


Most browsers are set to default to permit cookies, including certain cookies which relate to the RevContent services. You may at any time set your browser to block all cookies, but please note that our services work most effectively with the cookies enabled so as to provide the best possible user experience to you.


We offer users the opportunity to choose (opt-out) whether their personal information is (a) to be disclosed to a non-agent third party, or (b) to be used for a purpose other than the purpose for which it was originally collected or subsequently authorized by the user as provided herein. For sensitive personal information, we will give users the opportunity to affirmatively and explicitly (opt-in) consent to the disclosure of the information to a non-agent third party or the use of the information for a purpose other than the purpose for which it was originally collected or subsequently authorized by the individual. We will at all times provide users with reasonable methods to exercise their choices.


Our company policy is not to share personal information with any third parties except in instances where you have given us your consent for us to do so.

Upon request, we will grant individuals reasonable access to personal information that it holds about them (if any), and we will take reasonable steps to permit individuals to correct, amend, or delete information that is demonstrated to be inaccurate or incomplete.


Our website and the services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. As such, we do not knowingly or intentionally collect any personal information from or relating to any child under 13.

This Privacy Policy is limited in scope only to our Website and the corresponding services as described therein. We are not responsible for any third parties who utilize the services, and this Privacy Policy does not and is not intended to apply to any such third parties.


We take all reasonable precautions to protect personal information in our possession from loss, misuse and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration and destruction. We use reasonable, industry-standard precautions, including appropriate technical, administrative, and physical procedures, to protect personal information from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no transmission over the internet or electronic storage can be completely secure, so we can not guarantee the absolute security of any data.


We uses a self-assessment approach to assure compliance with this privacy policy and to periodically verify that the policy is accurate, comprehensive for the information intended to be covered, prominently displayed, completely implemented and accessible and in conformity with applicable rules and regulations. We encourage interested persons to raise any concerns using the contact information provided and we will investigate and attempt to resolve any complaints and disputes regarding use and disclosure of Personal Information.

You agree that your continued use of the website and/or the Services constitutes your agreement with the most recently updated version of the Privacy Policy, which will be published at this location.
