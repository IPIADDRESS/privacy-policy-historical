Revlon is concerned about the safety of children when they use the Internet. However, the Revlon Sites are neither directed nor targeted to children under 13 years old. Consistent with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998, Revlon has no intention to knowingly collect any PII (personally identifiable information, such as name, address, telephone number, age or email address) from individuals under the age of thirteen (13) years. If Revlon learns that a child under the age of 13 years has provided PII (personally identifiable information) to the Revlon Sites without verifiable parental consent, Revlon will use commercially reasonable efforts to delete such information from its databases. We strongly encourage parents to monitor their children’s use of the Web and we will use our reasonable efforts to delete personally identifiable information that we have received from your under age 13 child from the Revlon Sites and our files as set forth above if you send us a request as provided under Article VI below.
