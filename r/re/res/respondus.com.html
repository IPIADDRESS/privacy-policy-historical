Statement for www.respondus.com

RESPONDUS, INC. has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: www.respondus.com.

We may use your IP address (from your provider, like Earthlink, etc., it is not your computer or related to any content on your PC) to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Our online registration and/or order form requires users to give us contact information (like their name and e-mail address). Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. Information about you is not shared with outside agencies. Your name and information is not resold or given away for any purpose at any time.

Our site uses an online form for customers to request information, products, and services. We collect visitor's contact information (like their e-mail address). Contact information from the order form is used to send orders and information about our company to our customers. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.

Notice for Children

Persons under the age of 16 may not download software from this website without permission of their parents, or fill out any information forms on this site without permission of parent or legal guardian. Children 13 and younger may not download software from this website and should not fill out any forms found on this site at any time.

Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Our site utilizes a secure certificate authenticated by VeriSign. This certificate provides secure data transmission via a Secure Socket Layer, and confirms that we are the only person receiving data you send to us such as credit card information. Data is encrypted using the latest proven security technology available with RSA data encryption of up to 128-bit.

We also participate in third-party verification programs which monitor our service and commitment to secure transactions. As an added measure of security, we process credit card information off-line, so that your private data is only transmitted once over the Internet.
