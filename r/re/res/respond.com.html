Our Commitment
Respond.com is committed to protecting the privacy of our Buyers and Sellers. Respond.com will never share any personal information without your permission. This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. All information is for our registration and accounting purposes only. Only authorized employees of Respond.com will have access to this information. If you use our services through one of our partner sites (e.g. the America Online, Inc. (AOL) "Shop By Request" service) we may share the information you submit through the partner's site with that specific partner. We are not responsible for the privacy policies of our partners. Please read below for more details on our Privacy Policy.


Buyer Protection
In order to submit a request to our community of Sellers, Buyers are required to provide us with their email address, and sometimes location. It is necessary for us to have your email address so that we can deliver responses to you after you request a product or service. No one will receive your email address except Respond.com until you elect to share it with someone directly or consent to allowing it to be shared. At Respond.com, we will use all reasonable efforts to protect your anonymity during the shopping process. If you have any questions or would like to correct or review any information you may have provided us with please contact Customer Support at support@respond.com.

We also offer you the opportunity to apply for related shopping information and/or promotions from Sellers with Respond.com. These opportunities are completely optional, and you are not obligated to take advantage of them. You will always be asked before you receive any information or promotional offering.

Respond.com reserves the right to provide aggregate statistics about our customers, sales, traffic patterns and related Web site information to reputable third party vendors, but these statistics will not include any personal or identifying information. Respond.com reserves the right to post on its Web site the text in the "Comments" box and additional product or service information inputted by you in the request form; however, we will not post any information you input in the fields calling for personally identifiable information. Buyers have the option to change or cancel requests at any time by contacting Customer Support at support@respond.com.


Seller Protection
To become a registered Seller on Respond.com, we require you to provide us with both detailed Account information including name, address and phone number and your general Contact information. Your Account information will be used for billing purposes and to keep you informed of any updates to the Respond.com service. All Account information is strictly confidential and will not be shared with Buyers without consent. You may update or revise any account information by visiting the "Edit Profile" section of our Web site.

Your Contact information will be included in the emails Respond.com sends on your behalf. This information is necessary for Buyers to contact you directly. You control the way in which Buyers contact you, either by phone, FAX, email, or through your Web site. Sellers have the option to change or cancel requests at any time.


We use cookies to enhance the shopping experience
Cookies are small amounts of data delivered to your hard drive via your Web browser. The use of cookies enables Respond.com to process requests quickly, thereby enhancing the shopping experience for both Buyer and Seller.


Information we collect
While using our site, we automatically track certain information about you. This information includes the URL that you just came from whether this URL is on our site or not), which URL you next go to (whether this URL is on our site or not), what browser you are using, and your IP address. This information is collected to analyze trends, track user movement and use, gather broad demographic information and administer the site. Many sites automatically collect this information.


We ask for your consent to our Privacy Policy
By using Respond.com you agree to the above uses of your personal information. If at any time this policy changes, those changes will be posted to this page so that you are aware of our intent to better protect your privacy. We have obtained consents from all of the Buyers and Sellers who have provided us with the testimonials we occasionally post on our Web site.


We ask for your comments and questions
If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact Customer Support at support@respond.com.
