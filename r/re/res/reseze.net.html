Our Secure Booking Guarantee

ResEze.com is all about enabling you to feel comfortable when selecting and booking your lodging so that you can travel with confidence. The ResEze.com Secure Booking Guarantee protects you while you book reservations at ResEze.com, so that you never have to worry about credit card safety.

We guarantee that every transaction you make at ResEze.com will be 100% safe. This means you pay nothing if unauthorized charges are made to your card as a result of making a reservation on ResEze.com. See guarantee details below.


Why is ResEze.com so safe?

ResEze.com is powered by the ResEze booking engine. ResEze's secure server software (SSL) is the industry standard and among the best software available today for secure e-commerce transactions. It encrypts all of your personal information, including credit card number, name, and address, so that it cannot be read as the information travels over the Internet. In addition, ResEze.com only passes your information to our own secure servers, so that your personal information is safe throughout your visit to ResEze.com.

You can take comfort in knowing that hundreds of thousands of customers have already safely booked their rooms on the ResEze.com system without credit card fraud or any other security problems.


Guarantee Details

The ResEze.com Security Guarantee: You pay nothing if fraudulent charges are made as a result of booking at ResEze.com.

Under the Fair Credit Billing Act, your bank generally cannot hold you liable for more than $50.00 of fraudulent charges. If your bank does hold you liable for any of this $50.00, ResEze.com will cover the entire liability for you, up to the full $50.00. ResEze.com will cover this liability only if the unauthorized use of your credit card resulted through no fault of your own from a booking made on ResEze.com while using our secure server.
