In general, visitors to ResMed's Web sites remain anonymous (except for IP address tracking). ResMed's Privacy Policy does not apply to sites that are beyond ResMed's control, such as linked third-party sites.

Because Internet technologies change rapidly, this Privacy Policy is subject to revision. The latest version will be posted to this site, and visitors are encouraged to review it regularly.
