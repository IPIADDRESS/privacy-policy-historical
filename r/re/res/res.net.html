Security

RES.NET is hosted on a secure Web server whose identity is certified by VeriSign, an industry leader in Web site trust services. When you login to RES.NET, your username and password are sent over the Internet from your computer to our Website via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology. SSL encrypts your Personally Identifiable Information before it times off your computer, ensuring that no one else can read your personal information.

We use our best efforts to train and oversee our employees and agents to ensure that your information will be handled responsibly and in accordance with this Privacy Policy. We currently maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with state and federal regulations to guard your Personally Identifiable Information. Whereas "perfect security" does not exist on the Internet, we strive to ensure no unauthorized parties have access to any of your information.
