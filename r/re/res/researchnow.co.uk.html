Privacy Policy

     

Research Now Group, Inc. (“Research Now” or “the Company”) and its affiliates and subsidiaries operate this website. Use of this website indicates your acceptance of our Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy covers the Company’s Corporate website and web applications served from the researchnow.com domain. This Privacy Policy does not cover the activities of Research Now’s market research panels and communities. Separate, distinct Privacy Policies are displayed on the web pages for such panels / communities.

Research Now is firmly committed to protecting your privacy. Our commitment to protecting your privacy comes from a deep respect for all of our customers. We will continue to work hard to earn and keep your trust.

You may always submit concerns regarding our Privacy Policy or our privacy practices to privacy@researchnow.com.

Information We Collect

Research Now conducts market research in numerous countries around the world using online surveys through the ‘Valued Opinions’ website and e-Rewards Opinion Panels in relation to existing products and services, advertising campaigns, and new product concepts.

We are interested in building and maintaining strong, long-lasting relationships with all those who have an interest in our services.

In order to operate this website in an optimal fashion, we collect certain information through automated means, including IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, information on actions or activities taken or engaged in on a website, and dates and times of website visits. Please refer to our “Cookie Policy” for more information.

Additionally, forms and applications on our website and within our web applications collect personally identifiable information, such as first name, last name, email address, physical address, job title, company name and phone number. By providing such personally identifiable information, you consent to its collection.

Research Now takes children’s privacy seriously and we do not knowingly collect any information regarding children, as defined by the laws and regulations within those countries in which we operate. If Research Now learns that a child has provided information online, we will take all reasonable measures to delete such information from our systems.

Uses of information

We use the information that you provide us via this website and web applications in the following ways:

  • To keep you informed of Research Now’s products, services and activities;
  • To process any inquiries raised by you and other communications initiated by you in relation to your dealings with Research Now and any services provided by Research Now;
  • To produce and deliver requested products, services and reports; and
  • To provide requested quotes and brochures, we will require that you provide personally identifiable information to ensure delivery and execution of your request.

We do not share, sell, rent or trade personal information with third parties for their promotional purposes.

We reserve the right to disclose any information that is gathered when we believe that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights and/or comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process served on our website.

Any information collected via this website will be treated in the strictest confidence.

Other Websites

This website may contain links to other third party websites. Research Now is not responsible for the privacy policies and practices of such third party websites.

Changes to our Privacy Policy

Please check this page periodically for changes as Research Now reserves the right, at its discretion, to change, modify, add, or remove portions of the Privacy Policy and the website at any time.

Your continued use of the website following the posting of any changes to this Policy will mean that you accept such changes.
