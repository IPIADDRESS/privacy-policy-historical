Aggregated Statistics

Sravan may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. Sravan may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, Sravan does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Sravan uses cookies to help Sravan identify and track visitors, their usage of Sravan website, and their website access preferences.
