As a business based in the UK, under the terms of the Data Protection Act 1998 we have a legal duty to protect any information we collect from you. It will not be disclosed to any other organisation unless you give explicit permission, or there is a legal obligation to do so.

What information do we collect?

We collect website usage information from our web server to help us assess the effectiveness of marketing campaigns and improve the usage of the website.
