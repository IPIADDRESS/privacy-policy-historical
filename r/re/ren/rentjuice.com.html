This Privacy Policy applies to information (including, personal information) collected through the Site. For the purpose of this Privacy Policy, “personal information” means information that identifies you personally, either alone or in combination with other information available to us. This Privacy Policy is part of and incorporated into the Terms of Service of the Site.

RENTJUICE.COM’S PRIVACY PLEDGE TO YOU

  • We are committed to protecting your privacy while allowing you to use the Site.
  • You may add, delete or modify your personal information at any time.
  • Subject to your permission or as specifically stated in this Privacy Policy, we will not sell, exchange or otherwise share your personal information with third parties.
  • We do not send spam, maintain spam mailing lists or support the activities of spammers.

YOUR CONSENT TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

By using the Site, you are agreeing to the collection, use and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not consent to the collection, use and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy, you may not use the Site or any of the products or services offered via the Site. If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please email us at privacy@rentjuice.com.

WHAT PERSONAL INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT ON THE SITE AND HOW WE COLLECT IT

Active Collection of Information

Agents and Agencies

Personal information may be collected in a number of ways when you visit the Site or use any of the products or services offered via the Site. We may collect certain information that you voluntarily provide to us which may contain personal information. For example, we may collect your name, email address, user name, telephone number, credit card account number when you register, set up an account, contact us by e-mail or use our products or services. When you provide us with your credit card account number, you may elect to make it only valid for your use and/or your agency’s use. If you decide that your credit card account number can be used by your agency, any agent within your agency may make charges against your credit card account number using our products or services. When a credit card account number is used, the applicable credit card owner will be notified via e-mail.

Personal information may also be collected if you provide such information in connection your participation with the interactive areas on the Site. Agents can upload photos, which can only be seen by other agents in their agency. However, if an agent desires, such uploaded photos can be made available to the public directory of real estate agencies, which is visible to any user of the Site.

From time to time we may collect personal information you provide in connection with your participation in surveys, sweepstakes, contests, promotional offers, and other activities on the Site.

We may also collect photos from you, which can only be seen by agents in your agency. You may choose, however, that the photos be shown in the public directory of real estate agencies, which may be seen by any visitor to the Site.

Renters

We may collect personal information about you from the agents and agencies that visit the Site. We may use this personal information to contact you on behalf of agents to coordinate on housing rental process tasks or communicate with you about future products.

Additionally, if an agent or agency requests a credit report concerning you, we may collect your social security number. We will store your social security number in our database for only as long as the application is pending. Once the application is completed, we will request a credit report from our vendors and then remove your social security number from our database.

Landlords

We may collect personal information about you from the agents and agencies that visit the Site. We may use this personal information to contact you on behalf of agents to coordinate on housing rental process tasks or to communicate with you about future products.

Additionally, if you submit a listing to an agency via the Site, we may send the listing to a third party in order to convert it to a specific format. We, or a third party, will then input the data from the listing in the converted format into the application for the agency to which you submitted the listing.

All Users

When you use our support services, we collect your correspondence in order to build up a public knowledge base, which will be available to all users of the Site. Your correspondence will not be used verbatim, but likely paraphrased in an FAQ format.

RentJuice RealTime

If you use the RentJuice RealTime service, we will collect your profile information and information with respect to your listing(s) including, but not limited to, photos of the unit(s) and features of the unit(s). Such information will be shared with the people you invite via the RentJuice RealTime service.

Passive Collection of Information

We may collect information that is sent to us automatically by your Web browser, such as your IP address, the address of the Web page you were visiting when you accessed the Site or an advertisement hosted by us on a third party Web site, information on your operating system and browser, and the date and time of your visit. The information we receive depends on the settings on your Web browser.

The information provided by your browser does not identify you personally. We use this non-personal information primarily to create statistics that help us improve the Site and our business. In addition, we may review our server logs for security purposes, such as detecting intrusions into our network. If we suspect criminal activity, we might share our server logs – which contain visitors’ IP addresses – with the appropriate investigative authorities who could use such information to trace and identify individuals.

More specifically, when we collect information regarding your recent activity on the Site, we may use it in different ways. Some of the information is only use to enhance your browsing experience (such as, displaying your recently viewed listings or landlords). Other information is saved to a news feed which is displayed to other agents in your agency (such as uploading photos, adding or deleting listings and closing a listing).

Like many commercial Web sites, we use “cookies,” and similar technologies to collect information and manage the Site. A cookie is a small text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web server. Cookies cannot be used to install computer programs or deliver viruses to your computer. We use cookies to track your sessions on the Site. This tracking enables us to enhance your browsing experience.

You may delete our cookies as soon as you leave the Site. Although you are not required to accept our cookies when you visit the Site, you will only be able to log into your account and certain other portions of the Site if the cookies are enabled.

We may use Web beacons on the Site and may permit third parties to place them on the Site to monitor the effectiveness of the Site and its contents. A Web beacon is a small, invisible graphic image that may be used on the Site or in emails relating to the Site to collect certain information and monitor user activity on the Site.

STORAGE OF INFORMATION

Most of the information we collect is retained by us in our database, except for social security numbers which are only temporarily retained. Also, credit card information is never stored on our servers for an extended period. Instead, credit card information is stored using a feature provided by certain of our vendors.

We use the information collected through the Site: (a) to communicate with you; (b) to process your requests and transactions; (c) to improve the Site; (d) to customize the services and/or products we provide to you; (e) to assist with our product and service development; (f) to perform marketing analysis; (g) for the creation of our public directory; and (h) for other purposes related to our business.

Your personal information and other data collected by us may be transferred to another company that has acquired our stock or assets, for example, as a result of a sale, merger, reorganization, dissolution or liquidation.
