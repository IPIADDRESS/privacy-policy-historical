For example, the cookies may make leaving a comment on our site easier by remembering your username on your return visit, or inform you know which poll option you voted for, etc. Therefore the visitor has control over the information that is stored in the cookie. The cookies normally expire after 6 months. There is a simple procedure in most web browsers that you can set to notify you when a cookie is sent to you. Visitors can accept or delete cookies at any time via your browser settings, with the knowledge that it may prevent them from interacting with the site at an optimum level. The information collected from these cookies is only used by RenewCancelTV.com and is not transferred in any way to outside vendors.

We only use personally identifiable information insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the visitors interaction with RenewCancelTV.com.
