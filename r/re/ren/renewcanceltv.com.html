RenewCancelTV Privacy Policy

RenewCancelTV.com takes your privacy very seriously and is committed to protecting your privacy on its web sites. We do not disclose personally identifiable information about our visitors, other than described below.

Type of Information Collected

RenewCancelTV.com does not collect personally identifiable information from our visitors unless they give it to us voluntarily. Personal information may include referring site, username, email. The personal information volunteered by visitors is only used to fulfill your request, provide you with information and improve our site for the benefit of our visitors.

RenewCancelTV.com may also collect personally identifiable information such as Internet Protocol (IP) addresses. This enables us to safe-guard our web sites and other users from spam activity, etc.

Use of Personally Identifiable Information

RenewCancelTV.com shall never sell or transfer your personally identifiable information to unaffiliated third parties without your approval.

You may choose to sign up for RenewCancelTV.com email newsletter and can an opt-out at any time.

Our website reserves the right to perform statistical analysis of user behavior and characteristics in order to measure the activity and interest in areas of the site to further improve our website.

Like most online web sites, RenewCancelTV.com may use “cookies” on pages of our site to better serve our repeat visitors and to provide us with feedback on how to improve certain areas of the site. “Cookies” are small text files sent by a web site to a web browser and placed on your computer. The cookies used by Fringe Bloggers contain only the information you submit. For example, the cookies may make leaving a comment on our site easier by remembering your username on your return visit, or inform you know which poll option you voted for, etc. Therefore the visitor has control over the information that is stored in the cookie. The cookies normally expire after 6 months. There is a simple procedure in most web browsers that you can set to notify you when a cookie is sent to you. Visitors can accept or delete cookies at any time via your browser settings, with the knowledge that it may prevent them from interacting with the site at an optimum level. The information collected from these cookies is only used by RenewCancelTV.com and is not transferred in any way to outside vendors.

We only use personally identifiable information insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the visitors interaction with RenewCancelTV.com.

Third Party Cookies

Our web site uses third party ad servers (e.g. DoubleClick) to serve the advertisements that you see on some pages of our web site. DoubleClick may use cookies to help serve their advertisements, but it will not collect personally identifiable information about our visitors. For more information on DoubleClick’s cookie policy, please click here.

RenewCancelTV.com will not rent or sell personally identifying information to anyone. Other than its operators RenewCancelTV.com discloses personally identifying information only when required to do so – for example, if requested by the law or if RenewCancelTV.com believes in good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the rights of RenewCancelTV.com, its network web sites, partners, or the public at large.
