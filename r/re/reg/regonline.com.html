RegOnline Privacy Policy

RegOnline is committed to respecting your privacy. When you choose to open an account, offer online registration for an event, or register for an event using the RegOnline service, all of the information provided us is kept strictly private, including names, addresses and credit card information. All information collected during a registration is owned by the event organizer who is a client of RegOnline. RegOnline does not use or distribute information collected during a registration, and only the event organizer has access to this information.

  • We do NOT rent or sell lists of personal information of any type.
  • We do NOT use personal information collected during registrations for solicitations.
  • If you are a RegOnline client, we may on occasion send you an email update about the features and services added to our site.

RegOnline takes online privacy seriously. It is our way of sustaining your trust in us and in our products and services. Any changes to our privacy policy will be posted on our web site in a timely manner.
