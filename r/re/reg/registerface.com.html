If you send SMS, MMS, or text messages to the Services, we will collect the telephone number (if any) from which such communication was sent. This type of information is collected to make our Services and solutions more useful to you and to tailor the Registerface community experience to meet your special interests and needs. If you search the Registerface community by using a toolbar that Registerface, or a third party provides, we may collect Anonymous Information about you automatically, but we will not collect Personal Information.

An "Internet protocol address" or "IP Address" is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer when you use the Internet. In some cases your IP Address stays the same from browser session to browser session; but if you use a consumer internet access provider, your IP Address probably varies from session to session. We track your IP Address when you login to the Sites.

"Cookies" are small pieces of information that a web site sends to your computer's hard drive while you are viewing a web site. Registerface may use both session Cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and persistent Cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them) to provide you with a more personal and interactive experience on the Sites. Persistent Cookies can be removed by following your Internet browser help file directions. If you choose to disable Cookies, some areas of the Sites may not work properly.


Other third parties may place advertisements and cookies on webpage’s within the Registerface network. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one.
