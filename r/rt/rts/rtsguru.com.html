What cookies do we use

Cookie is a small piece of data sent from a website and stored on the user’s computer by the user’s web browser while the user is browsing. Cookies were designed to be a reliable mechanism for websites to remember information about the user or to record the user’s browsing activity. Cookies can also be used to remember pieces of information that the user previously entered into form fields such as names, addresses, passwords etc.

Strictly necessary cookies – These cookies are required for our website operations. They help us serve you information in a secure and optimal way.

Performance cookies – These cookies collect information about how visitors use a website (visited pages, time on site, etc.). These cookies do not collect information that identifies a visitor. All information these cookies collect is aggregated and anonymous. We use this information to improve the website so you can easily navigate through it and find necessary information.

Functionality cookies – These cookies are not essential but they allow the website to remember choices you make and provide a better-tailored experience for you. For example, if you leave a comment on our Site you may opt-in to saving your name, email address and website in cookies. These are for your convenience so that you do not have to fill in your details again when you leave another comment. These cookies will last for one year.

Social media cookies – These cookies are used when you use social media features available through the Site (e.g. clicking on the “Like” or “Share” icon on a webpage). Social Media plugins may collect your IP address and the page you were visiting at out Site and set cookies to provide the necessary functionality. Your interactions with these services are subject to Privacy Policy of the feature provider.

Advertising cookies – These cookies collect web browsing activity information and may be used to provide targeted advertisements based on previous browsing activity.

You can control cookies on the personal level and edit the cookies settings in your browser.
