Privacy Policy

Rtings.com cares about the privacy of your information that you provided us.

Our policy is always changing, responding to your feedback. You should revisit this page from time to time to make sure that you are still agreeing to it.

If you have any question about our privacy policy, send us an email at privacy@rtings.com.

Cookies

Cookies are small data placed within your internet browser. Cookies are a way to track your behavior and displaying content tailored to you. Rtings.com and the third parties that we allow use cookies to improve your experience. For example, this enables us to know if you visited our website before. Cookies are also used to remember your preferences and logins.

If you do not wish to give this information, you can disable cookies by modify your browser settings to decline them.

For more information about cookies, you can read the Wikipedia page: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HTTP_cookie

How do we use the information about you?

Rtings.com uses this information to improve our web pages. By analyzing our users’ behaviors, we can build a better experience for them. We continuously make improvement and monitor how our visitors react to these changes. This helps close the feedback loop of our development process.

We do not sell, distribute or lease your personal information to third parties unless you gave us your permission or we are required by law.

If you choose to click on them, we cannot be responsible for the privacy of any information that you provide while on them. You should consult their respective privacy policy to know more on how they use your information.

Security

We are committed to keep your information secure. The internet cannot be completely secure, but we will do our best to keep your information from reaching any unwanted hands.
