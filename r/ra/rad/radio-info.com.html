We do not knowingly collect personal data from children (persons under the age of 13). 6. DISCLOSURE AND VISITOR CHOICE
We do not disclose your personal data to our subsidiaries or other organizations. Where we disclose your personal data for purposes which are different from those indicated in the list mentioned above, we offer you the opportunity to consent to disclosure.

We do not give visitors to our Web Site the option of using a secure transmission method to send us their personal data, however, we have implemented security policies, rules and technical measures to protect the personal data that we have under our control from unauthorised access, improper use or disclosure, unauthorised modification, unlawful destruction or accidental loss.
