Located just 3.5 miles south of the city centre, the Radisson SAS St. Helen's Hotel, Dublin is only 13 kilometres from the Dublin International Airport and it takes about 30-50 minutes by car to reach the hotel. The hotel is serviced by Aircoach, which operates from Dublin International Airport and stops outside the hotel. The Aircoach stop is a 3-minute walk from the hotel. The price is 7 EUR to the hotel and 12 EUR from.

The hotel is conveniently located on the N11 Stillorgan dual carriageway, the main route from Dublin City Centre to affluent Southern suburbs and the south east of Ireland.  It also has easy access to Fosters Avenue and the M50. There is ample free car parking at the hotel for 220 cars.

Dublin's D.A.R.T - Rapid Rail Systems, Booterstown Stop is 1.5 kilometres from the hotel and the journey time from the city centre is 15 minutes.

Why Dublin?

  • A cosmopolian European city with easy access to all cultural attractions and populated with friendly and welcoming people.
  • Its history of fine authors and musicians born here bears fruit in the wealth of fine music, theatre and drama performed at all levels.
  • You can reach the museums, galleries and other landmarks including the James Joyce Museum and old Jameson Distillery via the network of canals and cobbled walkways.
  • Cuisine is both traditional and contemporary.
Local Attractions

  • Six Nations International Rugby Championships, February and March. 
  • St Patricks Day Festival, March.
  • Dublin International Horse Show, August.
  • International Horse Racing, Leopardstown Race Course.
  • Temple Bar Blues Festival, June.
  • City of Dublin International Rose Trials, July.
  • Dublin Theatre Festival, October.
