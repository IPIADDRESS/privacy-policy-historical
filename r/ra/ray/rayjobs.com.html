Raytheon respects your desire for privacy. When you visit Working @ Raytheon, we invite you to perform job searches or browse the site without providing personal information.

To be considered for an employment opportunity, we need personal information that includes your name, a valid email address, address, phone number and resume. When you respond to an opportunity by submitting your resume, you've registered which allows us to save your resume for quick submission to future positions that match your qualifications. Registration also provides access to additional functions that are designed to enhance your job search experience.

Cookies are short text files recorded in your hard drive and are used to enhance your use of web sites. Cookies cannot damage your files or read information on your hard drive. We use cookies to "remember" who you are from one web page to another so your information is private. By using cookies, we can offer you a personalized experience with My Rayjobs. Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. If you prefer, you can set your browser to refuse cookies. However, you will not be able to take advantage of the features of My Rayjobs if your browser refuses cookies.

We may use your contact information to communicate with you about new site feature announcements, enhancements and general updates. Personal information is collected only by specifically requesting it from you. Here's how we use personal information obtained on Working @ Raytheon:

  • Raytheon Staffing uses personal information solely for recruiting purposes, and your data will not be sold or provided to third parties.
  • Resume information will be stored in our database, which is accessible by Raytheon recruiters, and used to locate profiles of individuals whose qualifications match those of current openings.
We collect and track browser, domain, and web page statistics from everyone who visits our site, then review the data for troubleshooting and analytical purposes.
