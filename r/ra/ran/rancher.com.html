As our services evolve and we perceive the need or desirability of using information collected in other ways, we may from time to time amend this Privacy Policy. We post the current effective date of our Privacy Policy below. If we make material changes to this Policy we will post the revised Policy and the revised effective date on this Site. Any changes to this Privacy Policy shall be effective immediately upon its posting and you will be bound by the changes if you continue to use the Site after the revised Privacy Policy has been posted.

Information Collection and Use

In general, you can visit rancher.com without telling us who you are or revealing any information about yourself. If you wish to obtain information about us you can contact us or give us your email address. We will use your information to correspond with you and for our general commercial purposes including to offer our own, our affiliates’ or third-party products and services that we think you may find of interest. You can elect not to receive emails from us by “unsubscribing” to an email following the instructions in the email you receive. We may also collect statistical information about Site visitors including their IP address. We use this aggregated non-personal information for research and commercial purposes..

Children’s Privacy

This Site is not designed nor intended to be attractive to use by children under the age of 13 (“Minors”). We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you are under the age of 13, please do not submit any information to us.

Cookies

We use cookies to manage and improve our Site and to collect aggregate information about site usage by all of our users. A cookie is a text file that our Site transfers to your computer’s hard drive for record-keeping purposes and to enhance the quality of your visit to our Site. The cookie assigns a random, unique number to your computer. We may use cookies that expire when you close your browser (session cookies) and cookies that remain on your browser for an extended period of time (persistent cookies).

We do not use technology that recognizes a “do-not-track” signal from your web browser. We may permit third parties to place cookies through the Site to provide us with better insights into the use of the Site or user demographics or to provide relevant advertising to you. These third parties may collect information about a consumer’s online activities over time and across different websites when he or she uses our website. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, but doing so will limit system performance and may even cause certain features of the Site to malfunction or not to work at all.

Disclosing Information

We may reveal your personal information to our affiliated companies for their commercial use. We may disclose your information to unaffiliated third parties: (1) if you request or authorize it; (2) to help complete a transaction for you; (3) to comply with the law, applicable regulations, governmental and quasi-governmental requests, court orders or subpoenas, to enforce our agreements, or to protect our rights, property or safety or the rights, property or safety of our users or others; (4) if the disclosure is done as part of a purchase, transfer or sale of services or assets; (5) if the disclosure is to our agents, outside vendors or service providers to perform functions on our behalf; or (6) as otherwise described in this Privacy Policy.

Security

We utilize reasonable administrative, physical and technical safeguards to protect information against unauthorized access, misuse or disclosure. Communications with our Site or by email to us are not encrypted.

Contacting Us
