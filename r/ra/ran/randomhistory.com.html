Privacy Policy

At RandomHistory.com, we are committed to protecting your privacy. Therefore, all personally identifiable information we collect from you will be used solely to process your order and improve your experience with the site. We will never sell or rent any of your personal information to a third-party company. By using this site, you signify your agreement to the terms and conditions of the following privacy policy. For your protection, we promise to strictly adhere to the following terms.

Information We Collect

All personally identifiable information we collect from you is supplied on a voluntary basis. When you choose to commission a history with us, we will need to collect your name, your phone number, and your email address. RandomHistory.com will not collect your credit card information. To accept and process payments, we use PayPal, a third-party online payment provider. PayPal utilizes cutting edge encryption technology, including firewalls and Secure Sockets Layer protocol (SSL), to protect your private information. For more details about PayPal, please view the PayPal Privacy Policy and PayPal Security Statement.

Use of Cookies

As you browse through RandomHistory.com, it is possible that cookies will be set on your computer. A cookie is a small text file that does not personally identify users, although it does identify a user's computer. A cookie will not harm your computer and does not contain viruses. We will use information gathered from these cookies only to monitor site traffic.

Links to Third-Party Websites

Throughout RandomHistory.com, you may come across links to relevant, third-party websites. When you click on these links, you will be subject to the privacy policies of these sites. While we support the protection of privacy on the web, we cannot be responsible for the content, actions, or policies of any third-party websites. We encourage you to carefully examine the privacy policy of each site you visit and be aware of where you are at all times.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to amend our privacy policy at any time. Any changes we make to the privacy policy will be posted on this page.

Questions or Comments
