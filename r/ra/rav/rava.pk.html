Privacy Policy

All information shared over the internet raises privacy concerns. When dealing with websites, such concerns are heightened. This privacy policy has been created to allay those concerns; to ensure
our visitors that the information that they provide is safe and secure.

By visiting Rava, you are implicitly accepting the privacy policy described below.

Personal Information

Rava does not require or seek any personal information from its visitors. You ae free to view the various videos and content available at our website without entering any personal information.

Until it is explicitly stated in our privacy policy, we will never seek your personal information.The only personal information that you can share at Rava is of your email address, if you choose to subscribe to our newsletter. The newsletter contains latest news and updates from Rava that are relevant to our visitors. You can choose to unsubscribe from the newsletter at any point you wish by clicking on the link at the bottom of the newsletter; or by sending an email to Contact us with ‘Unsubscribe’ in the subject line.

Cookies

Rava does not use cookies. This also means your IP address, browser history, clicking details, and other information isn’t logged on our servers.

Children

Rava is meant only for audience above the age of 13. However, children under the age of 13 may browse the site and view the content under adult supervision.

Advertiser

We use outside ad companies to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies and are collected by the ad companies, and we do not have access to this information.

We work with multiple ad companies including Adsense. Please check their websites for respective privacy policies. You can opt out of some third-party vendor’s use of cookies for interest-based advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

Please note that we strictly do not allow competitive advertisements

Other Websites

Rava uses third-party content as well, thus creating links of other websites. If you click on the link of another site, your privacy depends on the policy of that site. Before using that site, we strongly suggest that you read their privacy policy.

Rava is not responsible for third-party hyperlinks and websites; and this privacy policy is your implicit acknowledgement that we do not have any liability in that context.

You can also contact us at Contact us for details or in case of queries.
