RAIDS.COM uses cookies to help identify and track visitors, their usage of RAIDS.COM websites, and their website access preferences. RAIDS.COM visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using RAIDS.COM’ websites, with the understanding that specific features of RAIDS.COM’ websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

RAIDS.COM has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
