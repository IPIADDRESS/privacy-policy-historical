We have created this Privacy Policy to inform you of Our information collection, use and sharing practices on the websites, mobile applications, and other digital services on which this Privacy Policy is posted (collectively, the "Sites"). We believe it is important for you to know how We treat your personal information. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to be sure you understand its terms. The terms of this Privacy Policy apply to all users of the Sites. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, you should immediately cease the use of the Sites.

1. What is the scope of the Privacy Policy?

This Privacy Policy applies only to the Sites on which this Privacy Policy is posted. This Privacy Policy does not apply to Company-owned Sites to which it is not posted or to third-party websites, even though the Sites may link to such Company Sites and third-party websites. You can learn more about links from the Digital Services in the "Do the Company's Sites link to other sites?" section.

2. What information does the Company collect about me on the Sites?

Personal Information: The Company collects personally identifiable information that you voluntarily provide on the Sites' online forms, which may include: user registration, sweepstakes, contest and promotions registration and claims, contact requests, guest comments, online surveys, and other online activities. The personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") you provide on the Sites may include: your name, address, telephone number, e-mail addresses, age, date of birth, credit card information, demographic information, and any other information you may voluntarily provide. You will have the choice whether or not to disclose such Personal Information in the above activities; however, some parts of the Sites and some services or activities may be more difficult or impossible to use if you choose not to disclose Personal Information.

At the time of registration, you may have the option to receive future offers and updates from the Company by checking the appropriate opt-in box. If you choose to opt-in, We will alert you via e-mail to new features, special offers, updated information and new services.

Non-Personal Information: In addition to the Personal Information identified above, We use technologies to automatically collect certain non-Personal Information whenever you use the Sites. This information is collected using technologies such as "cookies" and other online tools such as "pixel tags" and GIF files. For more information, please see review the "How does the Company use cookies" section below.

We also collect certain non-Personal Information automatically whenever users download or play one of our mobile applications. This information is collected using Google Analytics, a common analytical tool which collects information passively from the user's mobile device. The Google Analytics Software Development Kit (SDK) is included in the applications. When installed on your mobile device, the SDK helps us understand how the application is performing and how users engage with the application. Information collected in this manner is anonymous and doesn't identify the user or your mobile device. This information may include your device type and operating system, duration of user sessions, how often a user uses the application and what actions you take within the application. This information is used improve our applications and to support their internal operations.

Location Information: The Company may use your IP address to derive your general location, such as your city or zip code. At present, we do not collect precise geolocation information.

3. How does the Company use cookies?

Cookies are small text files accepted by a web browser and then placed on your hard drive. These cookies help us understand how the Sites are performing and how visitors engage with the Sites. The Non-Personal Information we collect using these technologies, including Google Analytics, may include your Internet protocol (IP) address, domain name, browser type, operating system, device type, and traffic patterns and usage data, such as the time, frequency and duration of your use, the pages or features you access, the website that directed you to the Sites and the website you visit when you leave the Sites. To learn more about Google Analytics and your privacy choices in connection with Google Analytics, including the Google Analytics Browser Opt-Out Add On, please visit the Google website.

In addition to providing us with non-Personal Information about you, cookies allow Us to provide visitors with tailored information upon each visit. We may also use cookies to improve and track our advertising and marketing.

Most browsers accept cookies by default, but you can usually block or restrict cookies on your computer or device or purge them from your browser by adjusting your browser preferences. You should consult the operating instructions that apply to your browser or your mobile device's user guide for instructions on how to configure your settings to meet your preferences. You do not need to enable cookies to visit Our Sites; however, some parts of the Sites and some services may be more difficult or impossible to use if cookies are disabled.

4. How does the Company use targeted advertising?

We use technologies such as cookies and web beacons (also known as action tags, pixels, or transparent GIF files) to help manage online advertising. These technologies are provided by third-party advertising partners and enable Our partners to recognize a unique cookie placed by Us (when you accessed the Sites) or by Our partners or by another advertiser who works with Our partners. These technologies allow Us to learn which advertisements bring users to the Sites. They also allow Us to collect information about your online activities over time and across third-party websites so We can manage advertising effectiveness and provide you with online advertisements, including advertisements on the websites of unaffiliated third parties, more likely to be of interest to you. Advertising partners and vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads to you based on your past visits to the Sites. With this technology, the information that We collect and share is anonymous and does not contain your name, address, telephone number, or email address. For more information about targeted advertising and your choice to opt-out of receiving targeted online advertisements, visit the following:

  • To opt-out from targeted advertising from members of the Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA), please visit the DAA website.
  • To opt-out of targeted advertising from members of the Network Advertising Initiative (NAI), please visit the NAI website.

Please note that these opt-out mechanisms are browser specific. If you wish to opt-out of targeted advertising across all browsers, you need visit the above websites from each browser you use. Also, please be aware that if you opt out of receiving targeted advertising, you will still be served with advertisements, but they will no longer be targeted to your interests.

5. What does the Company do with the information collected?

We use the Personal Information we collect to fulfill the purpose for which you provided it and for other purposes disclosed at the time the information was collected. For example, the Company may use your Personal Information: (1) to administer sweepstakes, contests and other promotions and fulfill prizes; (2) to register users for online activities such as: online ordering, sweepstakes, contests, surveys, employment applications, comment forms, or any other online interactive activities; and (3) to respond to specific requests from visitors.

We may also use your information, including Personal Information (1) to understand the use of the Sites and make improvements; (2) to obtain parental consent from visitors, when necessary; (3) to provide any necessary notices to visitors or their parent or legal guardians if situations prompt such notification; (4) to protect the security or integrity of the Sites if necessary; (5) to send notices of special promotions, offers or solicitations; (6) in general, to promote and market the Company's business and various products to you and to third parties; (7) to carry out Our obligations and enforce Our rights arising from any contracts entered into between you and Us.

6. With whom does the Company share the information?

The Company will not sell, rent, loan, transfer, or otherwise disclose any Personal Information to third parties except as set forth in this Privacy Policy. The Company may share Personal Information with third parties under the following circumstances:

We may share Personal Information, with third-party service providers. For example, We may use third parties to provide hosting, development, maintenance and support services, fraud protection and risk reduction, email distribution, marketing and advertising, market research, promotions management and fulfillment, and employment advertising, recruiting, and application processing. These service providers have agreed (i) to use your personal information only to provide services to Us and not for their own purposes, and (ii) to refrain from sharing your Personal Information with anyone other than Us or Our other service providers.

We reserve the right to release your Personal Information (i) to comply with any law or regulation, when We are otherwise under legal compulsion to do so (such as a subpoena or court order), or when We believe that the law requires us to do so, (ii) to investigate and help prevent security threats, fraud or other malicious activity, (iii) when We believe it is necessary to protect or enforce the rights, property interests, or safety of the Company, our employees, the users of our Sites, or others; (iv) as We deem necessary to resolve disputes, troubleshoot problems and otherwise enforce the Privacy Policy.

In the event that the Company merges with or becomes part of another organization, or in the event that We are sold, sell all or substantially all of Our assets, sell a line of business or are otherwise reorganized, the Personal Information We collect will be one of the transferred assets to the acquiring or reorganized entity.

We may transfer your Personal Information to Our subsidiaries and corporate affiliates, including future subsidiaries and affiliates. These companies may use your Personal Information consistent with this Privacy Policy.

We may use social media platforms such as Facebook® to serve advertisements to you or to third parties that the platform determines are similar to you. In order to offer these ads, we may share personal information, such as email addresses or phone numbers, in hashed or unhashed format, with the platform provider, which can match this information to a particular platform user.

We may use and disclose your Personal Information as you otherwise consent. For example, if you enter a sweepstakes or other promotion, the official rules may include provisions related to the sharing of your Personal Information.

We may share your non-Personal Information with third parties for any lawful purpose. This information is anonymous and is typically shared in aggregate format.

7. What are my privacy choices?

Email Communications: Users may opt-out of Our promotional emails by sending an email requesting removal from Our email marketing list to heycanes@raisingcanes.com. In addition, you can opt out of receiving promotional emails from Us by clicking on the "unsubscribe" link in any promotional email we send you and following the procedures indicated. Please note that even if you opt out of receiving promotional emails from Us, We will send you transaction emails and respond to questions from you. The Company will send an acknowledgement or confirmation of the request to the e-mail address on file.

Tools on Mobile Devices: You can control the tools on your mobile device. For example, you can turn off location services or push notifications. Please consult your mobile device's user guide for instructions on clearing cookies, disabling location services and disabling push notifications.

Do Not Track ("DNT") Signals: Although your browser may deliver a DNT signal to the Sites, the Company does not currently respond to DNT signals.

Cookies and Other Technologies; Targeted Advertising: For information on how you can control cookies, please review the "How does the Company use Cookies?" section of this Privacy Policy. For information on how to exercise choices regarding targeted advertising, review the "How does the Company use targeted advertising?" section of this Privacy Policy.
