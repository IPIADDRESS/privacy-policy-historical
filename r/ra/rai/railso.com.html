Delivery policy

RAILSO.COM ships worldwide with Federal Express. Federal Express does not deliver to P.O. Boxes. It can be sent to an address selected by you (home, work, etc. - never a PO Box) or to the store of your choice.
Shipments to Italy, San Marino and Vatican City are made with SDA and are free.

Shipment Date:
Orders are shipped from RAILSO.COM in Brescia, Italy from Monday to Friday from 10:00 a.m.– 18:00 p.m. (CET) Orders placed Friday evening, Saturday, and Sunday will be processed on Monday morning.

Available merchandise: immediate shipping:
Orders with available merchandise are shipped the same day from RAILSO.COM in Brescia, Italy from Monday to Friday from 10:00 a.m.– 18:00 p.m. (CET) Orders placed Friday evening, Saturday, and Sunday will be processed on Monday morning.
In all cases, RAILSO.COM reserves the right to delay a shipment if the order is not able to be shipped for reasons beyond control. Please note that during promotions and sales there may be delays in immediate shipping.

Pre-order merchandise: shipment is upon the arrival of the merchandise
Pre-Orders are debited and invoiced the day of Order Placement during regular office hours from from 10:00 a.m. to 18:00 p.m. (CET). Shipment will be the day that the merchandise arrives.
