Privacy Policy

Our privacy policy demonstrates our firm commitment to privacy and protection of all persons who visit our website, and ensures that their visitor information is in compliance with Safe Harbor provisions. This statement sets forth our information gathering and dissemination practices for icelandingpagedesign.com and hipinspire.com. For purposes of this policy, the term "visitor" includes visitors to our site who are not yet customers as well as those who become customers.

What we collect, and how.

Our website uses forms that our visitors can use to request information about products and services or to share project information with us. From these forms, we collect the following visitor information:

  • Visitor's contact information
  • Unique identifiers
  • IP addresses
  • Project details

Hipinspire Limited is committed to industry best practices approaches concerning security measures to prevent the loss, misuse and alteration of the information in our possession. Our company databases are accessible only by persons who have entered into and are bound by a confidentiality and nondisclosure agreement with Hipinspire Limited.

What Do We Use This Information For?

Contact Information
We use visitor contact information to send information about our company to prospective clients and interested parties, and to get in touch with them when necessary.

IP addresses and cookies
We use visitors' unique network addresses (IP Address) to help diagnose problems with our servers, and to administer our website.

We use cookies to:
Help us recognize visitors as unique visitors (just a number) when they return to our websites. Allow login ability to specific web applications that track legitimate session login information with the use of cookies. Compile anonymous, aggregated statistics that allow us to understand how users use our site and to help us improve the structure of our website. We cannot identify visitors personally in this way. Count the number of anonymous users of our sites.

We gather this informational for internal use only and will not authorize the release of this information to anyone outside Hipinspire Limited.
