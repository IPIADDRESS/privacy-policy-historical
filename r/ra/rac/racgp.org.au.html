The RACGP have created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following sections disclose the information gathering and dissemination practices that are operating on our website.

Collecting Information

When you visit this website, we may make a record of your visit and log some or all of the following information for statistical or maintenance purposes:

  • Your server or IP address (the name or number which uniquely identify the computer through which you are connected to the Internet)
  • The address of the referring site
  • The date and time of your visit
  • Your top level domain name (eg .org.au,gov.au etc)
  • The address of the pages accessed and the documents downloaded
  • The type of browser you used

No attempt, however, will be made to identify individual users or their browsing activities, unless required by law, nor will broad demographic information be used for marketing or general contact purposes.

Your e-mail address will only be recorded if you send us a message via any of the contact forms. It will only be used for the purpose for which you have provided it and it will not be added to a mailing list, nor disclosed without your consent.

Our online shop utilises an order form to request products or services. On this form we collect visitors' contact information (like their email address), unique identifiers (like their member username). Contact information from the order form is used to send orders to our customers and to get in touch with the visitor when necessary. Financial information that is collected is used to check the users' qualifications and bill the user for products and services. Please see our Security Statement for more information on our security standards.

Use of Cookies

Our site uses cookies in the online shop and membership areas to identify users, this allows us to deliver a more efficient and personlised service. Information is retained via the cookies that alleviates the need for repeated entry of details. Unique identifiers (such as an authorised member username) are collected to verify the user's identity and for use as account numbers in our record system.

Member Forums

This site makes chat rooms and forums available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas will be available to the member community in which it has been posted. You should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. Please read the Terms of Use for the forums before you take part in them.

Third Party Access to Information

Except where specified to the contrary in this privacy statement, it is our policy not to sell or pass on any personal information that you may have provided to us unless we have your express consent to do so, or where we are required by law to do so.

Supplemental Information

This site supplements the information that you provide, with information that is received from third parties.

The RACGP website contains links to other sites. The RACGP is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these websites.

Choice/Opt-Out

If you are receiving information electronically via the website and no longer wish to, you have the opportunity to opt-out. The RACGP website gives users the following options for removing their information from our database so to end the delivery of future communications or to no longer receive our service.

  1. You can visit the following URL to specify your wish to not receive information: http://www.racgp.org.au/racgp/contact_college.asp
  2. You can send an email to webeditor@racgp.org.au requesting to be removed from our database and thereby no longer receive correspondance from the RACGP online.

Correct/Update Details

You can can change or modify information previously provided in either one of these two ways:

  1. Visit our contact page to make a request
  2. If you have changed any of your contact details, you can use the Change of Address form online to notify our staff.

Contacting the Website

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Website, click here to visit our contact details page.
