Privacy Policy

We are always conscious and respectful of your personal privacy.
Motor Racing Network provides this Online Privacy Statement to make you aware of our privacy policy and practices and the way information is collected and used with respect to the MotorRacingNetwork.com website (the “Site”). To make this notice easy to find, it is available on the homepage of the Site.

This Privacy Policy will answer your questions about the kind of information we collect and how we use it. If we change or update our Privacy Policy, we will post those changes here. By using the Site, you acknowledge you have read this Privacy Policy and agree to the terms thereof.

What information do you gather and how do you use it?
Motor Racing Network collects information about your visits to this Site, such as service provider, browser type, operating system, pages accessed on this Site and the date and time of access to optimize your user experience and enhance your time spent with us online. Motor Racing Network also receives information telling us what site from which you originated.

When you send Motor Racing Network an e-mail, Motor Racing Network will retain the content of the e-mail, your e-mail address, and our response in order to handle any follow-up questions you may have. Motor Racing Network also uses this information to measure how effectively Motor Racing Network addresses your concerns.

When purchasing tickets or merchandise, joining as a member or registering for promotions at the Site, Motor Racing Network will ask for your:

• Name
• E-mail address
• Credit card number and expiration date
• Billing address
• Shipping address
• Date of Birth
• Phone number

Motor Racing Network may ask you to voluntarily provide us with information regarding your personal or professional interests, demographics, experiences with us, and contact preferences. This information may be combined with information about you, which Motor Racing Network gathered from other offline sources. Motor Racing Network and its affiliates use the information to design offers customized to your interests. For example, you may receive a special offer for your most frequently visited tracks.

Who do you share my personal information with?
Motor Racing Network does not sell or share your name or other private profile information to third parties and does not intend to do so in the future. Motor Racing Network may share anonymous, aggregated information about all our users with third parties.

How does this Site use cookies and similar technologies?
This Site uses cookies, tags, pixels and similar technologies to allow Motor Racing Network to recognize your preference information, keep track of your purchases, remember your Member Login and facilitate effective site administration. Motor Racing Network uses the information we collect to enhance your visit to this site and provide you with information tailored to your needs.

You may elect to refuse cookies. Please refer to your browser Help instructions to learn more about cookies.

How does this Site support the Children's Online Privacy Protection Rule?
Motor Racing Network does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children, but if a child has provided us with personally identifiable information without parental or guardian consent, the parent or guardian should contact Motor Racing Network to remove the information and unsubscribe them from any promotional contact opportunities.

How do I tell this Site that I don't want to be contacted for promotional purposes?
Motor Racing Network only sends promotional e-mail messages to you if you have voluntarily provided Motor Racing Network with your e-mail address. You may request to be removed from this Site's e-mail subscription list by updating your personal account. By clicking on your Member Name at the top of the page you can access your personal account to make the necessary changes. You may also unsubscribe from any e-mail subscription by following the unsubscribe procedures set forth in each e-mail.

How do I update personal information such as my address?
Motor Racing Network asks that you keep your information as up-to-date as possible so you may get the maximum benefit from this Site. By clicking on your Member Name at the top of the page you can access your personal account to make the necessary changes.

How Secure Is Information About Me?
Motor Racing Network works to protect the security of your information during transmission by using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) software, which encrypts information you input. Motor Racing Network reveals only the last four digits of your credit card numbers when confirming an order. Of course, Motor Racing Network transmits the entire credit card number to the appropriate credit card company during order processing. It is important for you to protect against unauthorized access to your password and to your computer. Be sure to sign off when finished using a shared computer.

By using this Site, you signify your assent to the Motor Racing Network Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to the Motor Racing Network Privacy Policy, please do not use this Site. We reserve the right, at our complete discretion, to change, modify, add, or remove portions of this policy at any time.
