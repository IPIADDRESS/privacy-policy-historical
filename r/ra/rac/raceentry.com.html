Privacy Policy

Race Entry LLC
As of: February 8, 2013

Respect of Privacy

We respect your right to the privacy of your personal information. Race Entry LLC is committed to protecting the privacy of our visitors. In order to provide a safe, secure experience, we make every effort to ensure that your information remains private and is used only for the purposes described below.

Your information

This website provides forms and may request you to provide information including but not limited to the following:

  • Full name, residential address, phone number, email address, gender, date of birth, payment information (if applicable).
  • We use this information to register events, athletes and/or sponsors for the purposes described within our website.

Uses

We may use the information and data collected for auditing or research and analysis to operate and improve Race Entry LLC products, technologies and services. We may share aggregated, non-personal information with third parties outside of Race Entry LLC. We may also share information with third parties in limited circumstances. This may include compliance with legal processes aimed at preventing fraud or eminent harm. This may also include the goal of ensuring the security of our network and services.

Children’s Privacy Policy

Race Entry LLC facilitates registration for various events, some of which allow participants under the age of thirteen (13). We require a submission of birth date for all registration entries. For children under the age of thirteen (13), parental or guardian consent is required.

External Sites

Our website visitors have access to links to other web pages from our site; however the views of these linked sites may or may not reflect the views of this organization. Race Entry LLC is not liable for content or information gathered on any of those linked sites not owned by Race Entry LLC.

Analytical Information

Race Entry LLC uses cookies and other technologies to collect anonymous traffic data. When a user visits our website, the servers automatically record information including URL, IP address, browser type and language, including the date and time of the request.

Information Security

Race Entry LLC takes precaution to protect personal information. We utilize security measures such as data encryption, SSL secure link usage, and password protection where applicable.

Future Changes to Privacy Policy

Race Entry LLC reserves the right to update, modify, or expand upon this privacy policy. Any future changes will alter the current effective privacy policy, and it is the user’s responsibility to check for periodic changes to the terms with which users are accepting.
