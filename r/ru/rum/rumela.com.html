Rumela's Web is deeply committed to protecting the privacy of Internet users. Below is the information outlining our continued support for the responsible management of consumer information.

Consumer Information:

When you send one of our electronic greeting cards, the information you provide to us is used to customize your greeting with the names of the sender and recipient and to deliver an email notification to the recipient. The names and email addresses you provide are NOT compiled into any type of mailing list and will not be used for any type of email solicitation. Rumela's Web does not share the names and email addresses of users of its site with any other company.

We share aggregate information (not individual data) with our advertisers to help them better understand our services and to monitor the reach of their advertising on the service.
 

Data Collection and Opt Out:

Rumela's Web only stores the email addresses of the newsletter subscribers. 
Rumela's Web also links to a wide variety of other sites. We are not responsible for their privacy policies or how they treat information about their users. 

If you have any questions concerning the privacy policies of Rumela's Web which are not covered in this statement, please send email with your questions to support@rumela.com with the words "Privacy Policy" in the subject field.

Data Accuracy:

Rumela's Web tries best to keep the data and information accurate. However Rumela's Web doesn't take any responsibility of any information which is not accurate as most of the information supplied Rumela's Web users. If you think, any information provided is not accurate or wrong, please  notify us as soon as possible. We will try to keep the the data and information accurate and right as much as possible.
