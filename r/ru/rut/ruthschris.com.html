PRIVACY POLICY

 
At Ruth's Chris Steak House, we value the trust that our guests place in us. We are committed to protecting the personal privacy of everyone who visits our Web site and our restaurants.

We recognize and respect that every guest expects privacy and confidentiality in all dealings with us. We pledge that we will take the steps necessary to protect the privacy of any personal information shared with us. We will never share or sell your personal information with another company. This includes, but is not limited to your name, address, phone number, and Email address.
What are cookies and why do we use them?
Cookies are used by Web servers to recognize users and their preferences on a website. Cookies enable a Website to provide a more personalized and convenient process to the user. Cookies record information such as your computer's IP address, the time and date you are accessing the site, your browsers and operation system, and other information.
Trusted Partnerships
Ruth's Chris Steak House does partner with other companies to provide you with the best possible experience in our restaurants and online. These trusted partners adhere to exactly the same privacy policies when dealing with information about our guests. Like us, they will not share or sell your data with any other company, nor will they solicit you directly. They also pledge not to disclose the personal information but only required by law for compliance with a subpoena, fraud prevention or an inquiry from a government agency or regulator.
Collection, Use, and Retention of Personal Information.
Ruth's Chris Steak House uses information recorded on this Web site to help compile broad statistical trends that help us improve navigation and content. We do not sell or distribute non-public information, including email addresses collected via our registration and reservation process to any third party. We will never utilize data obtained from an individual guest without permission.
Security of Personal Information.
The management of Ruth's Chris Steak House limits employee access to information to those with a business reason for knowing such information. We educate our employees on the importance of maintaining guest confidentiality, and we enforce our privacy policies with appropriate disciplinary procedures.
Changes to your Personal Information.
You may update your information on our Website at any time by using the following link http://www.ruthschris.com/registration.
