Cookies

Running Magazine uses 3rd party internet cookies that allow us to track site traffic and usage patterns and to present customized content or advertising. A cookie is small piece of data sent from a website and stored in a user’s web browser while the user is browsing that website. Stored cookies do not contain any personal information, just clickpath data. If you are concerned about cookies you can turn them off in your browser preferences.
