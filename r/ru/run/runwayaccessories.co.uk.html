What are Cookies?

Cookies are text files containing small amounts of information that allow our website to recognise your device. They are then downloaded to your device when you visit the website, if you agree to the site doing this. The cookies are then sent back to our website on each subsequent visit, or to another website that recognises that cookie.

Cookies are very useful as they allow our website to recognise your device and are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, to let you navigate between pages, to remember your preferences and to generally improve your user experience on our website. They can also help to ensure that any adverts you see online are tailored to be more relevant to you and your interests.
