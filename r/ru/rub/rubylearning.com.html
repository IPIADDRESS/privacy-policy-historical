A cookie is an element of data that a Web site can send to your browser, which may then store it on your system. rubylearning.com pages use cookies so that we can better serve you when you return to our site, and maintain the quality of interaction during your visit to our site. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it.
