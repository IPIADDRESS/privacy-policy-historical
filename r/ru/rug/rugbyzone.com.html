Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy was last updated on Feb 4th, 2008.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how MediaZone.com, Inc. ('MediaZone') treats Personal Information that MediaZone collects and receives, including Personal Information related to your use of the MediaZone service. This policy applies to all Personal Information collected or submitted through the MediaZone website, e-mail communications between you and MediaZone or other means.

The term 'Personal Information' means information about an individual that is personally identifiable and not publicly available, such as one's name, contact details, and credit card information, as well as the products and services in which one expresses an interest and/or which one purchases.


Collection of Personal Information


In order to create an account with MediaZone and/or order and access certain services, products and areas provided by MediaZone (collectively, the "Services"), you are required to provide certain Personal Information requested by the online registration feature, ordering feature and/or other pages or functions within the Services. This information may include your legal name, personal address, personal phone number, personal e-mail address, subscriber name or "screen name" and password used to access the Services. The Company reserves the right to request any additional information necessary to establish and maintain your account for use of any of the materials, goods or services available in connection with the Services. Once a username is created for you, MediaZone stores such information in order to enable you to use the Services and to customize your experience.
If you choose not to provide requested Personal Information, you will not be able to register with MediaZone, subscribe to the Services, make any purchases or other transactions through the Service, or obtain other services or benefits that require such disclosure.
MediaZone is the sole owner of all Personal Information provided to it, subject to the terms of this Privacy Policy. In the event MediaZone is acquired by, is merged into or is otherwise succeeded by another company, MediaZone reserves the right to transfer its ownership of such Personal Information to such other company without notice to you. In the event of such a transfer, the privacy policy of the other company, to the extent it has one, may supplant this Privacy Policy.


Use of Personal Information


Except as set forth herein, MediaZone will not provide any of your Personal Information to other companies or individuals without your permission, unless required by law or for other legal purposes consistent with this Privacy Policy. MediaZone will not rent, sell, or share your Personal Information to any unrelated third party except as described below. MediaZone may use your Personal Information for the following purposes:

1. To verify your user status, including without limitation by confirming that you are a MediaZone subscriber, that you have paid for particular content that requires payment or that you satisfy other conditions concerning particular content (e.g., geographic or time restrictions);
2. To process your transactions, including without limitation for billing purposes and for delivery of the applicable Services to you;
3. To confirm your subscription to or cancellation from MediaZone, or any particular purchases or licenses;
4. To customize the advertising and content you see based on your characteristics;
5. To improve our services;
6. To update and inform you about MediaZone services and products, including the Services to which you have subscribed;
7. To provide you with targeted advertising, promotions and special offers from MediaZone or our affiliates, advertisers, sponsors or other business partners;
8. To conduct research and surveys and aggregate data for the purpose of reporting trends (based on aggregated data that does not personally identify you) to MediaZone and its business partners.
9. To provide specific user data to Nimbus International, a provider of content for MediaZone Cricket Service ONLY.
10. To provide specific user data to Premier Media Group, a licensor of content for the FOX SPORTS Running Rugby On Demand offer ONLY

Names, postal and e-mail addresses collected may be added to MediaZone's databases and used for future communications and mailings regarding updates, new products and services of MediaZone, upcoming events, and status of transactions you conduct online. If you desire not to receive the above information, you may contact us with such a request, in which case we will remove you from the requested lists. MediaZone reserves the right to contact you regarding your account status and changes to the Terms and Conditions of Use, Privacy Policy and any other policies or agreements relevant to the Services, without offering you the opportunity to opt out of, or opt in to, receiving them.
MediaZone may provide your Personal Information to third party subcontractors for the purpose of processing payment, delivering goods or services to you or for other purposes related to your purchase or use of the Services. MediaZone will provide those third parties only the information they need to perform the contemplated services.


Opt Out


As a subscriber or registrant to MediaZone, you may be sent email correspondence providing you with an opportunity to opt out of receiving future communications based on your preferences. Except as permitted or required by law or in accordance with this Privacy Policy, MediaZone will not send you offers or other communications if you have opted out of receiving them by clicking the appropriate box(es) during the registration or purchase processes or by opting out of receiving correspondence.


Cookies


Like other major websites, MediaZone uses cookies to track traffic patterns on our website and to provide users with a customized experience when using our products and services. A cookie is a text file that our website transfers to your computer's hard drive for record-keeping purposes and to enhance the quality of your visit to our website. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer.
Most Internet browsers are set up to automatically accept cookies. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, but doing so may limit system performance and may even cause certain features of the website to malfunction or not to work at all. You agree that MediaZone shall not be liable to you for any such malfunction or limited system performance.
MediaZone reserves the right to set and access MediaZone cookies on your computer and to authorize the advertisers on the MediaZone site to set and access their cookies on your computer. MediaZone treats any Personal Information that may be contained in cookies with the same confidentiality as other Personal Information you provide to us. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this policy.


Disclosures Required by Law and Investigations


MediaZone reserves the right to disclose your Personal Information in order to comply with any subpoenas, court orders or other legal process, or to establish or exercise MediaZone's legal rights or defend against legal claims.
MediaZone reserves the right to use and disclose your Personal Information in order to investigate, prevent or take action regarding illegal activities, infringement of the intellectual property of MediaZone or a third party, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person or violations of MediaZone's terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.


Security and Confidentiality of Personal Information


To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy and ensure the correct use of Personal Information, MediaZone has put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures which MediaZone regards as appropriate to safeguard and secure the Personal Information MediaZone collects online.
MediaZone uses encryption technology to protect the security of the information collected and transmitted through its website and Services. When MediaZone's registration and order forms ask users to enter sensitive Personal Information (such as credit card number and/or social security number), that information is encrypted and transmitted to a secure database server using Secured Socket Layer encryption technology.
In addition, your MediaZone Account Information is password-protected for your protection. After a successful login, you can change your password at your discretion. MediaZone recommends that you choose a password that would be difficult to guess, that you change your password periodically and that you keep your password secret (including by not allowing friends or family members to use your account and password). You acknowledge that you are responsible for selecting a password and maintaining its secrecy. Under no circumstances will MediaZone be liable to you or any third party for your failure to maintain the secrecy of your password.


No Liability for Acts of Third Parties


Although MediaZone will use reasonable efforts to safeguard the confidentiality of the Personal Information you submit, transmissions made by means of the Internet cannot be made absolutely secure. MediaZone will have no liability for disclosure of information and data you submit to it due to errors in transmission, unauthorized acts of third parties or through the use of third party cookies, programs, routines, viruses or other devices on your computer.
Once you leave the MediaZone site, including by clicking on links on MediaZone's website, you will be subject to the privacy policies of third party website operators and other companies which may differ significantly from this policy. MediaZone encourages you to be aware of when you leave the MediaZone site and to read the privacy policies of each website that collects information from or about you.


Children

MediaZone does not currently accept any information submitted to or through the Services from children under the age of 13. No information should be submitted to MediaZone or through the Services by children under 18 years of age without the consent of their parent or legal guardian. MediaZone does not allow children under 13 to open an account or to receive direct marketing communications arising from their use of the Services and, therefore, MediaZone does not provide any personally identifiable information from children under 13 to any third party.
