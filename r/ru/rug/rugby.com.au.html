Privacy Policy

The Australian Rugby Union Limited (ARU) respects the privacy of the individuals on whom the ARU collects, uses, discloses and holds personal information. The ARU has a Privacy Policy that observes the National Privacy Policy Principles contained in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

The ARU uses the information in this form to assess the national level of interest in the game of rugby. Along with this we may in the future offer you the opportunity to register with the Australian Rugby Union to receive electronic updates of information related to rugby.

The ARU also uses your information to send you materials and promotions that the ARU considers may interest you. The ARU may also disclose information to its sponsors and affiliates for those persons to send you promotional information. Please indicate on the registration page if you DO NOT want to receive these materials.

You are able to gain access to the information the ARU holds about you. If you wish to do so, or you would like a copy of our Privacy Policy, please contact the ARU at the address below.
