Privacy Policy

IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons
www.ruhanirabin.com does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.

DoubleClick DART Cookie

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on www.ruhanirabin.com send directly to your browsers. Other technologies ( such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons ) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see.

www.ruhanirabin.com has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
