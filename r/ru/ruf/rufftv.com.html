RuffTV may also use cookies to track and understand the traffic on the RuffTV website. Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that the RuffTV website places onto the user's computer hard drive. Cookies identify the user's computer hard drive so that the RuffTV website can recognize you the next time you visit.

Third party advertisements displayed on or in connection with the RuffTV website may also contain cookies set by such advertisers. RuffTV does not control these cookies and users of the RuffTV website should check the privacy policy of such advertisers to understand whether and how it uses cookies.
