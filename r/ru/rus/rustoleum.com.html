There are two types of information collected on the Site: Personally Identifiable Information and Non-Personally Identifiable Information.

Personally Identifiable Information
Personally Identifiable Information (“personal information”) is information that identifies you as an individual, whether directly such as by your name, mailing address, or email address, or indirectly such as by your internet protocol (IP) address, telephone number, age range, etc. Personal information is obtained via site usage tracking or when you voluntarily provide the information to us, such as, for example, by filling in forms on the Site.

Company uses personal information to answer your questions; provide products, services or information you request; to communicate with you; to provide marketing materials from Company or others with whom we have a relationship with.

Once you choose to provide us with personal information, you can be assured it will be used only to support your relationship with Company or as otherwise described herein.

For example, personal information you voluntarily provide may be collected and stored to permit you to receive Company’s e-newsletters and/or e-mail, participate in online support or “Ask the Expert” services and for marketing and administrative purposes. It may also be used to fulfill your requests for orders, sample products or information from Company from time to time, to request product support, ask a question, report a problem with the Sites, participate in promotions, sweepstakes or contests. Additionally, you cannot communicate with Company through the “Contact” or customer support links on the Site or via e-mail without providing personal information. If you contact Company or its service providers, a record of that session or correspondence will be maintained.

Company may disclose personal information that we collect or you provide as described in this privacy policy: (i) to our parent company, subsidiaries and affiliates; (ii) to contractors, service providers and other third parties we use to support our business or (iii) with your consent, to companies who offer products that may be of interest to you. Company takes appropriate measures in light of the nature of the personal information we collect and the risks associated with processing that personal information in order to keep it secure and to reduce the risk that your personal information may be accessed, altered, damaged or used without authorization. Company may combine information collected about you with information obtained directly or through its affiliated companies, business partners or other companies.

Should you wish to update your information, be removed from Company programs for which you previously registered or to prevent further use of your personal information or stop receiving communications from Company, please follow the directions set forth in Section 11 of our Terms of Use.

Non-Personally Identifiable Information
Non-Personally Identifiable Information (“non-personal information”) is information that is about you but does not identify you as an individual, either directly or indirectly, such as your browser type, the URL of the previous web site you visited, your internet connection, the equipment you use to access the Site, etc. Company may indirectly or through third parties, automatically collect certain types of non-personal information from you when you are using the Site. We may also collect non-personal information that you voluntarily provide, such as information included in response to a questionnaire or survey.

Cookie Technology
In general, you may visit the Site without telling Company who you are or revealing personal information about yourself. However, Company and companies providing services to or on behalf of Company may use various technologies, such as cookies and web beacons (1x1 pixels), to collect non-personal information discernible as a result of your visit. We will explicitly request your consent to use cookies on our site. Cookies are used to collect general usage and volume statistical data.
