By using the StarWind Website and/or Services, you ACCEPT the practices set forth in the Privacy Policy and the Terms of Use. For this reason, please frequently refer to the Privacy Policy to ensure your continued ACCEPTANCE of all StarWind practices relating to your personal data/information (“Personal Information”). Your continued use of any portion of the Website and/or Services following the posting of an updated Privacy Policy will constitute your full ACCEPTANCE of any changes.

StarWind complies with the EU-US Privacy Shield (the “Framework”) Principles regarding all Personal Information received in the United States (“U.S.”) from the European Union (“EU”) in reliance on the Privacy Shield, and is an active participant in the Framework. As such, StarWind is subject to the investigatory and enforcement powers of the Federal Trade Commission (FTC). StarWind’s commitment to the Framework’s Principles is outlined in Sections M & N, below. To learn more about Privacy Shield and the Principles You can visit the Privacy Shield Website.

When you visit StarWind’s Website at https://www.StarWindsoftware.com/ ("Website"), and use StarWind’s products or services (“Services”), you trust StarWind with your Personal Information. StarWind takes your privacy very seriously which is why this Privacy Policy explains what information StarWind may collect or receive, how it uses such information, and what rights you have regarding your Personal Information. Thus, please read through the Privacy Policy carefully before you access or use any StarWind Services.

The Privacy Policy applies to all Personal Information collected through your use of the Website and Services. StarWind is committed to protecting your Personal Information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about StarWind’s Privacy Policy or practices with regards to your Personal Information, please contact StarWind’s Data Protection Officer (“DPO”) at eugene.zvonik@StarWind.com as outlined in Section M, below.

A. UPDATES; CHANGES IN PRIVACY POLICY

StarWind reserves the right to modify or update the Privacy Policy at any time and will notify you as to any changes via the Website, by email, or as outlined in the Terms of Use. The modified or updated Privacy Policy will be indicated by a “Last Updated” or "Revised" date and the updated version will be effective as soon as it is accessible. StarWind wants you to always be aware of what Personal Information it collects, how StarWind uses that information, and under what circumstances StarWind may disclose your Personal Information. All Personal Information that StarWind may collect or receive will be handled in accordance with the Privacy Policy in effect at the time the information is collected. If the Privacy Policy changes in any significant respect in the meantime, StarWind will not (without your permission) use your Personal Information in a manner that is inconsistent with the Privacy Policy in effect at the time your information was collected.

B. PERSONAL INFORMATION YOU DISCLOSE TO STARWIND

StarWind may collect Personal Information that you voluntarily provide to it when registering on the Website to receive information about StarWind Services, obtaining demo samples or Services, or when participating in other activities on the Website or otherwise contacting StarWind. The Personal Information that StarWind may collect depends on the context of your interactions with the Website and Services. Such information may include:

Name and Contact Data: first and last name, job title, corporate e-mail address, your employer, phone number, and your country of residence.

Credentials: user account details, passwords for authentication, and account access. StarWind may also use similar credentials, like password hints and other security information, as the case may be.

StarWind requires that all Personal Information that you provide to be true, complete and accurate. You must notify StarWind or update your account if any changes to such Personal Information occurs.

C. INFORMATION AUTOMATICALLY COLLECTED

Some information is collected automatically when you visit, use, or navigate the Website. This information does not reveal your specific identity (like your name or contact information) but may include device and usage information, such as your IP address, browser and device characteristics, operating system, language preferences, referring URLs, device name, country, location, information about how and when you use the Website and other technical information. This information is primarily needed to maintain the security and operation of the Website and for StarWind’s internal analytics and reporting purposes.

Like many businesses, StarWind also may collect or use Personal Information associated with cookies and similar technologies for advertising or other similar purposes. However, any such uses of Personal Information and cookies will receive the utmost care and attention from StarWind. Any vendors or third parties used for advertising will be reputable and abide by at least similar standards of data protection to that of StarWind.

D. INFORMATION COLLECTED FROM OTHER SOURCES

StarWind may purchase or receive limited data from public databases, marketing partners, or other publicly available sources. Examples of the information StarWind may receive from other sources include: social media profile information; marketing leads; search results; and links to paid listings (such as sponsored links).

Furthermore, StarWind may collect your Personal Information when you contact StarWind’s customer support team via call, email or another communication tool. The purpose of collecting such information is to help you solve any issue you have with StarWind Services. The legal basis of collecting this information is StarWind’s fulfillment of contractual or pre-contractual obligations to you or your consent thereto. Additionally, StarWind may record support team calls. The purpose of call recording is quality control of StarWind’s customer support services and personnel training. The legal basis of call recording is StarWind’s legitimate interests in retaining you as a customer and providing you with first-class customer support services.

StarWind’s trusted partners, such as Google, help StarWind to place advertisements on and off the Website and perform analytics. Please read StarWind’s current trusted partners’ privacy and cookies policies below:

  • Bing Privacy Policy

StarWind may use your Personal Information for a variety of business purposes, as described in detail, below. StarWind may process your Personal Information for such purposes in reliance on its legitimate business interests in order to enter into or perform a contract with your consent. The following indicates the specific processing grounds on which StarWind relies, if and when it processes any Personal Information.

  5. To notify you about changes and updates to StarWind’s terms, conditions, or policies;
  6. In order to fulfill and manage your orders for contractual reasons. StarWind may use your information to fulfill and manage your orders, payments, returns, or exchanges made through the Website or otherwise;
  7. To request feedback or comments from you concerning StarWind Services;
  8. To respond to legal requests and prevent harm. For example, if StarWind receives a subpoena or other legal request, StarWind may need to inspect the Personal Information it holds in order to determine if and how to respond; and
  9. To view your online actions across the Website and Services for data analysis, specifically, identifying usage trends, determining the effectiveness of promotional campaigns, and to evaluate and improve the Website, products, Services, and your overall experience.

StarWind only shares Personal Information with your consent, to comply with laws, to protect your rights, or to fulfill business obligations. As a non-exclusive list of examples, StarWind only shares and discloses your Personal Information in the following situations:

StarWind may disclose your Personal Information where it is legally required to do so in order to comply with applicable law, governmental requests, or a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process such as in response to a court order or a subpoena (including in response to public authorities to meet national security or law enforcement requirements);
  • Vital Interests and Legal Rights. StarWind may disclose your Personal Information where it believes it is necessary to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding potential violations of StarWind policies, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the safety of any person and illegal activities, or in the event of litigation;
  • Vendors, Consultants and Other Third-Party Service Providers. StarWind may share your Personal Information with third party vendors, service providers, contractors or agents (“Third-Party Processors”) who perform services on its behalf and require access to such information. For example, StarWind may use third parties for technical and customer support, payment processing, data analysis, email delivery, hosting services, customer service, and marketing efforts. These Third-Party Processors may be located within the EU, the U.S., or another country in StarWind’s sole discretion, however, any third party with which StarWind shares your Personal Information will be held to similar data protection standards to that of the EU-US Privacy Shield Framework and the GDPR.

  • Business Partners. StarWind may share your Personal Information with its business partners to offer you certain products, Services or promotions, and/or to facilitate provision of StarWind products and Services to you, as well as with other StarWind entities; and
  • With your Consent. StarWind may disclose your Personal Information for any other purpose in accordance with your consent.

L. RIGHTS OF EU USERS UNDER THE GDPR

StarWind complies with the principles of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 April 2016 on the protection of natural persons with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data, and repealing Directive 95/46/EC (General Data Protection Regulation) (“GDPR”). If you are a natural person of the EU within the meaning of the GDPR (“Qualified Individual”), you have rights that allow you greater access to and control over your Personal Information. Such rights include:

  • Right of Confirmation. You may obtain confirmation of whether or not your Personal Information is being processed.
  • Right of Access. You may obtain information about your stored Personal Information at any time and a copy of this information.
  • Right to Rectification. You may request the rectification of any inaccurate Personal Information. Taking into account the purposes of the processing, you have the right to have inaccurate or incomplete Personal Information completed or deleted, by means of first appropriately identifying yourself and then providing a supplementary statement.
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten). You may request the erasure of your Personal Information by contacting StarWind’s DPO, as listed in Section M below. Furthermore, if and as applicable, you may delete your account information by accessing your account settings page on the Website or by contacting StarWind directly. Please note that while any changes you make will be reflected in active user databases within a reasonable time, StarWind may retain all information you submit for the prevention of fraud and abuse, analytics, satisfaction of legal obligations, or where StarWind otherwise reasonably believes that it has a legitimate reason to do so, such as for archiving purposes within the public interest.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing. As a Qualified Individual, you may have the right to restrict processing where one of the following applies: (a) the accuracy of the Personal Information is contested by the data subject, for a period enabling the controller to verify the accuracy of the information; (b) the processing is unlawful and the data subject opposes the erasure of the Personal Information and requests instead the restriction of the controller’s use of such information; (c) the controller no longer needs the Personal Information for the purposes of processing, but they are required by the data subject for the establishment, exercise, or defense of legal claims; or (d) the data subject has objected to processing pending the verification whether the legitimate grounds of the controller override those of the data subject.
  • Right to Data Portability. You may request to receive your Personal Information in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format. You have the right to transmit this information to another controller without interference. Furthermore, you may have the Personal Information transmitted directly from one controller to another, where technically feasible and where such transmission does not adversely affect the rights and freedoms of others.
  • Right to Object and Automated Decision Making. You may object to decisions based solely on automated processing, including profiling, which produces legal effects or similarly significantly affects you, as long as the decision is not (a) necessary for entering into, or the performance of, a contract between you and StarWind; (b) authorized by EU or Member State law to which You are subject; or (c) based on the data subject’s explicit consent.
  • Right to Object to Processing. You may object to the processing of your Personal Information unless there are legitimate grounds for the processing within the public interest, or for the establishment, exercise, or defense of legal claims. If StarWind processes Personal Information for direct marketing purposes, you shall have the right to object at any time to the processing of your Personal Information for such purposes. This right also applies to profiling to the extent that the processing is related to such direct marketing purposes. If you object to StarWind processing your Personal Information for direct marketing purposes, then StarWind will no longer process the Personal Information for such purposes.

To exercise any of the preceding rights as a Qualified Individual, please contact StarWind’s DPO as outlined in Section M, below.

Data Controller. If and when StarWind receives your Personal Information directly from you via its Website or Services, it performs the functions of a ‘data controller’, as defined by the GDPR, and has the ability to determine how Personal Information is collected, for what purposes, and how this data is to be processed. As the data controller, StarWind has implemented many technical and operational measures to ensure the most complete protection of Personal Information processed through its Website and Services.

Data Processor. StarWind processes your Personal Information in accordance with the Privacy Policy and uses industry standard safeguards to secure your information.

Legal Basis for Processing. Article 6(1)(a) of the GDPR serves as the legal basis for processing operations for which StarWind obtains consent for a particular purpose. If the processing of Personal Information is necessary for the performance of a contract to which You are a party, as is the case, for example, when processing operations are necessary to provide a service, the processing is based on Art. 6(1)(b). The same applies to such processing operations which are necessary for carrying out pre-contractual measures, for example in the case of inquiries concerning StarWind Services. If StarWind is subject to a legal obligation by which processing of Personal Information is required, such as for the fulfillment of tax obligations, the processing is based on Art. 6(1)(c). If the processing of Personal Information may be necessary to protect your vital interests or that of another Qualified Individual, then the processing is based on Art. 6(1)(d). Finally, processing operations could be based on Art. 6(1)(f) if processing is necessary for the purposes of the legitimate interests pursued by StarWind or by a third party, except where such interests are overridden by your fundamental rights and freedoms under the GDPR.

If StarWind relies on your consent to process your Personal Information, you have the right to withdraw your consent at any time. Please note however that this will not affect the lawfulness of the processing before its withdrawal. To withdraw your consent, please contact StarWind’s DPO as listed below.
