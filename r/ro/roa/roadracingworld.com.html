RoadracingWorld.com Privacy Policy

Roadracing World Publishing, Inc. regards your privacy to be of the highest priority. This Privacy Policy covers information/data we receive as it applies to subscribers, customers, and visitors to roadracingworld.com, and is in addition to the Roadracing World.com/Hookit Privacy Policy found here

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please email privacy@roadracingworld.com, or write to us at:

Roadracing World Publishing, Attn: Privacy, PO Box 1428, Lake Elsinore CA 92531

WHO WE ARE
Roadracing World Publishing, Inc. is a high-performance motorcycle news and entertainment service, providing content over a variety of media formats. Products include Roadracing World & Motorcycle Technology magazine, available in print and digital editions; and www.roadracingworld.com, an online service of Roadracing World magazine.

Roadracing World.com is hosted on the Hookit Network, and includes news, videos, and the Roadracing World Online Community of Racers, Riders, & Fans, a social media/networking service. Please review the Roadracingworld.com Terms of Use and Privacy Policy which cover use of the website and social media/networking service.

The official website of the Roadracing World Action Fund, a 501 (c) (3) non-profit organization promoting motorcycle road racing safety education to help prevent injuries at the racetrack, is hosted within the www.roadracingworld.com website. You may review the Roadracing World Action Fund’s Terms of Use and Privacy Policy by visiting www.roadracingworld.com/actionfund.

HOW DATA IS COLLECTED AND USED
We collect data you give us when ordering a product, and we use information that to fulfill your order.

We use an online digital magazine service, PageSuite, which processes our online subscription orders received through our website, www.roadracingworld.com and provides customer access to the online edition of Roadracing World. Through our PageSuite account, we can access the online subscriber database for the purpose of maintaining subscriptions and fulfilling print orders, and our use of the database is within PageSuite’s own Terms of Use and Privacy Policy which can be found here. Subscriptions orders that we receive directly are processed in-house. We keep subscription information on current and expired subscriptions for the purpose of servicing the customer.

We do not share our subscription mailing list or customer personal information such as addresses, phone numbers or email addresses with any third party for the purposes of marketing to you. Your data may be made available to third party service providers who help to fulfill your subscription order; for auditing or data statistics; or for sending emails on our behalf.

We may use the phone number or email that you provide to us to contact you regarding your subscription or product order. Monthly subscribers may receive Subscription Notifications through email services such as Constant Contact. Subscribers may also receive other emails or mailings regarding products, surveys, contests, or events which may be of interest to you. To opt-out of receiving these notices, click here.

DATA PROTECTION
To ensure protection of your data, safeguards have been put in place to help prevent unauthorized access, including digital security and administrative protocols. With this in mind, no system can be completely secure and we cannot assure complete security of data.

COOKIES
Roadracingworld.com uses cookies, which are small data files placed in your device’s browser. Cookies may be used for a number of purposes, such as tracking usage patterns to determine areas which are popular; measuring the effectiveness of advertising; helping us service advertisements, and facilitating your ability to navigate the website.

Opting out: If you do not wish to allow for use of Cookies, you may generally opt out of providing this information by setting your browser to reject Cookies, or by controlling the preferences in third-party applications. If you do not wish for Cookies to track your specific activity, you may set your browser to reject Cookies, adjust your email settings from HTML to text-only display, and control preferences in third-party applications. However, if you disable the use of Cookies, the website may not provide you with an acceptable user experience.

YOUR CALIFORNIA PRIVACY RIGHTS
If you are a resident of the State of California, we provide you with information on how to exercise your disclosure choice options such as your right to opt-out (which we may sometimes refer to as "unsubscribe") or opt-in for disclosure of your Personal Information to third parties (such as Advertisers) for marketing purposes. Therefore, pursuant to the California Civil Code, we are not required to maintain or disclose a list of the third parties that received your Personal Information for marketing purposes during the preceding year.

If you are a California resident and wish to request information about how to exercise your third-party disclosure choices, please contact our Privacy Administrator at privacy@roadracingworld.com.
