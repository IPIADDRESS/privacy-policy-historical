Privacy Statement for www.roxio.com

This Privacy Policy outlines our information gathering and usage practices for www.roxio.com and other web-sites hosted by Roxio. Its purpose is to notify you of what personally identifiable information of yours ("User Information") is collected; how the information is used; with whom the information may be shared; what choices are available to you regarding collection, use and distribution of the information; what kind of security procedures are in place to protect the loss, misuse or alteration of information under the company's control; and how you can correct any inaccuracies in the information.

Roxio does not rent, sell or lease User Information we have collected through our web site. However, we may share User Information, collected through our web site, with other third parties after giving you the opportunity to opt out prior to any information being exchanged. Local country laws are applied where they differ from this policy.

We are firmly committed to guarding the confidence you have placed in our company and to use, responsibly and professionally, any information you volunteer. We strive to collect only that information that we think is necessary for our legitimate business interests, such as to better understand you (our customer), to provide better service, to improve marketing of our products, to educate customers and to ensure that our proprietary information is protected. We are committed to using the information collected only for these purposes and those outlined below. Our web site visitors from Europe should note that Roxio has developed this policy with the intent of complying with the International Safe Harbor Privacy Principles between the United States and Europe. If you do not want to share personal data, please read below for information on how to opt out or change personal information Roxio collects from you.

Links to third party web sites, from this web site, are provided solely as a convenience for you. If you use these links, you will leave our site. We have not reviewed, nor do we monitor, these third party sites and we do not control, nor are we responsible for, any of these sites, their content or their privacy policy (if any). This policy discloses our information gathering and usage practices for www.roxio.com.

In order to improve our problem resolution process through better product and case tracking, Roxio's tech support department has implemented online product registration and technical support service. We require contact information and either product serial number or TSID number to identify your product. This information is used to manage your case, notify you when necessary of issues related to your product and, together with your voluntary responses to our marketing survey, it is used to analyze our customer base in aggregate. Occasionally, we may send emails to give customers information we think you will find useful, such as information about new features, products or promotions. When we ema il you, we will always provide instructions explaining how to unsubscribe so you will not receive our emails in the future. You may also opt-out of receiving future Roxio and/or partner communications by submitting your request through our on-line Feedback form.

Sweepstakes, Contests & Promotions: Roxio occasionally sponsors sweepstakes, contests and promotions. We use contact data from our promotions to send users information we think you will find useful, such as information about new products or promotions. When we email you, we will always provide instructions explaining how to unsubscribe, so you will not receive our emails in the future.

Online Store: To process your order we require name, billing address, phone number, email address, shipping address and credit card information. We use this information to process your order and, if any questions arise, to contact you about your order. In general, we will try to contact you first by email and only if that fails, or if time is critical, will we try to contact you by phone. Your order is processed, on our behalf, by Yahoo, a provider of e-commerce services. View Yahoo!'s Privacy Policy.

Online Surveys: Our online surveys ask visitors for contact information (like email address). This may be used to contact you if additional follow-up is appropriate, but will not be used for future marketing purposes, unless explicitly stated.

We require first name and last name, so that we may personalize your greeting, email address and in some cases your country, as not all offers are valid in all countries. Typically all other contact information is voluntary and may be used to send other information or offers via mail.
