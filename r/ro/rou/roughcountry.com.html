Privacy Policy

At RoughCountry.com your privacy is our number #1 priority. We use the information that we collect from you when you order with us ONLY to process your order, and to give you a more personalized and beneficial shopping experience. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to other individuals or companies. We insure and guarantee that each and every transaction that you make at this website is 100% safe and secure. We employ the latest technology to insure that your personal information is safe.

Information Collected

When you shop on our site, we collect personal information from you such as your name, e-mail address, billing address, shipping address, telephone number, product selections and payment information. We may also collect the URL you came from, IP address, domain types (e.g. .com or .org, etc.), your browser type, the country, and state where your server is located, the pages of our site that were viewed during a visit.

How Information is Used

We do not sell or rent your personal information to others. We use customer information we collect only for the purposes of filling orders, contacting purchasers to inform them of their order status, enhancing the operation of our site, for statistical purposes and to administer our systems.

Cookies

We use cookies to help identify you when you visit our store and to customize your experience. The cookies contain randomly assigned user identification numbers. The cookies make your use of the site easier, allow you to retrieve an earlier shopping cart, make the site run more smoothly and help us to maintain a secure site. You will be unable to shop on our site without your browser set to accept cookies. We do not disclose your personal account information or any information about your purchases to anyone.

If you have any questions regarding our privacy practices, please contact us or call 1-800-222-7023.
