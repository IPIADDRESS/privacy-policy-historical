How Does Cheri Media Group Collect and Use Information About You?

The information learned from Roughstock.com's users, members and customers helps Cheri Media Group personalize and continually improve your experience at Roughstock.com. Here are the types of information Cheri Media Group gathers and how this information is used:

Automatic Information: Cheri Media Group receives and stores certain types of Automatic Information whenever you interact with Roughstock.com. Here are the types of Automatic Information Cheri Media Group gathers and how it is used:

IP Address: Your Internet Protocol ("IP") address is a unique identifier used to connect your computer to the Internet. Your IP address also is stored in Roughstock.com's user registration databases when you register with Roughstock.com.

IP addresses may be used for various purposes, including to:

  • Help Cheri Media Group identify you and your shopping cart and to gather broad demographic information.
  • Diagnose service or technology problems reported by Roughstock.com users or engineers that are associated with the IP addresses controlled by a specific Web company or ISP.
  • Estimate the total number of users visiting Roughstock.com from specific countries or regions of the world.
  • Track visits to and business at Roughstock.com's stores.
  • Help determine which users have access privileges to certain content that Roughstock.com hosts.

Cookies: Roughstock.com uses a feature of your Internet Web browser called a "cookie," a small piece of information stored on your hard drive that uniquely identifies your intra- and inter-requests on Roughstock.com. Cookies may be used for various purposes, including to keep track of preferences you specify while you are using Roughstock.com. Other companies that are presenting advertisements on some of Roughstock.com's pages may set and access cookies on your computer. Cheri Media Group does not control cookies from third-party advertisers. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies and not the privacy policy of Cheri Media Group The use of advertising cookies sent by third-party servers is standard in the Internet industry.

Web Beacons: Web pages may contain electronic images (called a "single-pixel GIF" or "Web beacon") that allow a website to count users who have visited that page or to access certain cookies. Cheri Media Group uses Web beacons in the following ways:

  • Within Roughstock.com in order to count users and to recognize users by accessing Roughstock.com cookies.
  • To personalize your experience when you visit Roughstock.com.

Mobile Device Unique Identifier: Roughstock.com may from time to time provide services that allow users to access and customize Internet content through mobile phones, PDAs and other alternative devices. These devices may collect different information than what is collected by computers.

  • When connecting to Roughstock.com using a mobile device that provides Internet access, if the mobile device service provider uniquely identifies the device, Cheri Media Group will receive this information.
  • Cheri Media Group may use this unique mobile device identification information to offer extended services and/or functionality.
  • Certain Roughstock.com services may require collection of the phone number for your device. Cheri Media Group may associate that phone number to the mobile device identification information.

Physical Location

  • Some mobile phone service providers in the United States are required to operate a system that will pinpoint the physical location of devices that use their service.
  • Depending on the provider and if you are located in the United States, Cheri Media Group may automatically receive this information.
  • Cheri Media Group may use and store this information to provide enhanced location-based services as well as to serve location-targeted advertising.

Personal Information: Cheri Media Group also receives and stores information that personally identifies you. The types of personally identifiable information that may be collected at Roughstock.com include: name, address, email address, phone number, fax number, billing address, shipping address, product selections, order number, referring URL, IP address, password, username, how you got to know about Roughstock.com, gender, occupation, personal interests, your age and information about your interests in and use of various products, programs and services. You voluntarily provide this personally identifiable information when you enter it on Roughstock.com to sign up as a member of Roughstock.com, whether in connection with a newsletter subscription, when you enter a sweepstakes, promotion or contest and/or other special benefits.

At Roughstock.com, you can submit personally identifiable information about other people. For example, you might submit a person's name and email address to email for a Roughstock.com news story and, if you are located in the United States, order a gift on Roughstock.com and want it sent directly to the recipient, you might submit the recipient's name and address. The types of personally identifiable information that may be collected and stored about other people at Roughstock.com include: recipient's name, address, email address and telephone number. Cheri Media Group does not knowingly share with, rent, transfer or sell personally identifiable information that you submit about other people to any third parties or nonaffiliated entities.
