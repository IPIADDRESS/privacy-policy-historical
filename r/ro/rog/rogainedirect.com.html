PERSONALLY-IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION
We do not use this website to collect any personally identifiable information about you unless you provide it to us voluntarily. Personally identifiable information includes your name, address, phone number, e-mail address or any other information which might reasonably be used to identify you individually. Pharmacia & Upjohn collects personally identifiable information from visitors to this website only when it is voluntarily provided. If you do not want your personally identifiable information collected through this website please do not submit it to us. If you have already submitted personally identifiable information and would like us to remove it from our records, please contact us at the e-mail address listed in this website.

If you do provide us with personally identifiable information, we may use that information to improve our understanding of your interests and concerns as well as to improve our products and services. We may use the information to contact you about our products and services. We may send your name and address to advertising, consulting, and market research companies that work with us. We will not sell your name, address or phone number to a third party.

MINORS (INDIVIDUALS UNDER 18 YEARS OF AGE)
Pharmacia & Upjohn does not intend to collect any personally identifiable information from people under 18 years of age through this website. Pharmacia & Upjohn will delete information if it determines that it relates to a person under age 18. Pharmacia & Upjohn specifically instructs people under 18 not to submit information to our website. If a person under 18 has provided us with personally identifiable information, the parent or guardian should contact us at the e-mail address listed in this website so that we may remove this information from our files.

COOKIES
Pharmacia & Upjohn uses so-called "cookies" in order to personalize delivery of content based on a Rogaine user's experience with the product. When you enter the Rogaine.com website we will assign your computer browser a unique random number called a "cookie".

Cookies enhance our website by allowing you faster access to the information you are seeking and by allowing us to provide suitable offers based on your experience with Rogaine.

Your privacy and security are not compromised when you accept a cookie from our website. We do not use cookies to collect personally identifiable information. Any information you submit in the registration portion of the website will not be associated with any cookie. Cookies from our server are sent securely and cannot read data from your computer's hard disc or read cookie files from other websites.

This website requires that you accept cookies if you wish to enter. When you are in the site and you receive a pop-up window asking permission to accept a cookie, you must answer "yes" to advance beyond that area.

MESSAGE BOARD AND PASSWORD
In order to use the "message board" function in the Community Forum section of the website, you will be asked to submit a screen name and password. This information is needed to associate your comments on the board with an anonymous entity. No personal data will be collected in this area and all comments will be posted with complete anonymity.

Your comments on the message board will be moderated for appropriateness but no personal information will be associated with your posting.
