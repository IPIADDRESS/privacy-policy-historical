Non-personal data:

For purposes of this Privacy Policy, “non-personal data” means information that does not directly identify you. Additionally, non-personal data means “aggregate” and “de-personalized” information, which is data Rovio collects about the use of Rovio Services, from which any personally identifiable data has been removed.

Rovio may use tools or third party analytical software to automatically collect and use certain non-personal data that does not directly enable Rovio to identify you. Rovio may use and disclose to Rovio’s partners and contractors the collected non-personal data for purposes of analyzing usage of the Services, advertisement serving, managing and providing the Services and to further develop the Services and other Rovio services and products.
