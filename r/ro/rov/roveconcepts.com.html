Privacy Policy

At Rove Concepts, we understand that you value your privacy. We protect you with our Privacy Policy, which safeguards your personal information. If you would like to know more, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Privacy Policy

Information will remain private and will never be sold or provided to a third party unless required by law or consented to by the customer. Information may also be used to be contact the customer about other services from Rove Concepts. If the customer would prefer not to receive this material, he or she can cancel it at any time.

Rove Concepts will handle requests for access to personal information in accordance to national privacy regulations. If a customer requests access to their information and if a cost is incurred in order to retrieve that information, a fee may be charged. Personal information may be inaccessible in circumstances including, but not limited to, when giving access would: be unlawful; prejudice an investigation of unlawful activity; have an unreasonable impact on others’ privacy. After a transaction is completed, electronic and hard copy information is archived and destroyed after 7 years. All reasonable precautions are taken to protect customer information from unauthorized use or alteration. 

Any changes to this policy will be in accordance with the national privacy principles.
