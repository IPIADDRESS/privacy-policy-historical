PRIVACY POLICY
To protect your privacy, RomanceStuck has instituted policies that give you information and control.

RomanceStuck does not collect any personal information from any visitor to the RomanceStuck site.

RomanceStuck requires email addresses from those webmasters who choose to submit their romance related web site for addition into RomanceStuck's database.  This information is used only used to contact the webmaster in regards to their site submission. RomanceStuck requires email addresses from those wishing to subscribe to the weekly newsletter.  This information is only used to send the newsletter once a week. RomanceStuck will offer the option of unsubscribing from the newsletter in every issue, or you can unsubscribe by filling out this form.

RomanceStuck offers the Recommend-It! service for it's visitor's use. RomanceStuck does not collect any personal information or an email address from the user when they fill out this form. Please read Recommend-It's privacy policy for more information on their service's policies.

For privacy policies for other RomanceStuck services offered via other companies, please visit that companies website.

UNDER NO CIRCUMSTANCES WILL ROMANCESTUCK SHARE YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS WITH A THIRD PARTY.
