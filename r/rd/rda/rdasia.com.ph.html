TO SERVE YOU BETTER…
 
 
PRIVACY POLICY OF READER'S DIGEST ASIA PTE. LTD. Our contact information is: The Privacy Office, Reader's Digest, P.O. Box 600, Singapore Post Centre, Singapore 914020. You can also fax to 67491312, or email to friends@rdasia.com.sg
 
Collection of information
 
Reader's Digest keeps/maintains a record of your custom with the Company, your contact details, your prize draw history and other information which you have provided to us.
We maintain your record so that:
  1. we can provide you with timely customer service;
  2. we can send you renewal subscription offers, new book and product offers, discounts and other benefit opportunities
  3. we can send you other prize draw opportunities, and
  4. we can service you the products which you have ordered.
Without this record, Reader's Digest would not be able to do any of this and, as such, the information you provide is required.
 
In addition, Reader's Digest occasionally, through proper contractual arrangements, will rent or acquire information from reliable third party companies regarding Singapore consumers (who have so consented). The only purpose for these occasional rental activities is to afford Reader's Digest the opportunity to send these consumers promotional offers and prize draw opportunities.
 
Furthermore, Reader's Digest will occasionally, through proper contractual agreements, disclose the contact details of certain of its customer base, of those who have so consented in advance, to reliable third party companies. The sole purpose of these disclosures is to allow these third party companies to send or make you consumer, financial and insurance offers that we believe may be of interest to our customers.
 
Website cookies
 
When you visit the RD website pages, our server makes a record of the visits and logs the user's server address, the user's top-level domain name, the date and time of visit to the site, the pages accessed and documents downloaded, the previous site visited and the type of browser used for statistical purposes. No attempt is made to identify users, or their browsing activities, unless a law enforcement agency or a court exercises a valid warrant to inspect the service provider's logs.
 
Record of website visits
 
RD makes use of "cookies" to make our website perform better. A "cookie" is a feature built into your browser that assigns you a "user ID". Cookies are used to follow the pattern of movements of web users. Cookies let us know which pages on our website are visited, in what order and how often. By themselves, cookies can't be used to discover your identity ­ they merely identify your computer to our servers when you visit us. If you are unhappy about having a cookie assigned to you, you can set your browser to refuse cookies. Cookies are not used to record any personal information such as your name, address or email address.
 
Security
 
When you begin to place credit card details on the RD Online Store or RD microsites, a secure system software called "socket security layer" (SSL) encrypts all information you input before it is sent to us.
 
Third party links
 
On occasion we include links to third parties on this website. Where we provide a link it does not mean that we endorse or approve that site’s policy towards visitor privacy. You should review their privacy policy before sending them any personal data.
 
 
Our Privacy Office may be contacted at Reader's Digest, P.O. Box 600, Singapore Post Centre, Singapore 914020. You can also fax to 67491312, or email to friends@rdasia.com.sg for any purpose related to your record(s) with the Company including:
- if you do not wish to receive any material from us in the future
- accessing your record for review,
- correcting information in your record, and
- raising any concerns that you may have with regards to Reader's Digest compliance with legal privacy and data collection requirements.
 
Each communication with the Privacy Office will be dealt with as efficiently as possible and with the intention to ensure that Reader's Digest continues to be a privacy compliant company.

Pengumpulan Maklumat
 
Reader's Digest menyimpan/memelihara rekod langganan anda dengan Syarikat, butiran kontak anda, sejarah peraduan anda dan maklumat lain yang anda telah berikan kepada kami.
Kami memelihara rekod anda supaya:
  1. kami boleh memberikan anda perkhidmatan pelanggan yang tepat pada masanya;
  2. kami boleh hantar kepada anda tawaran pembaharuan langganan, tawaran buku dan produk baru, diskaun dan peluang-peluang faedah yang lain,
  3. kami boleh hantar kepada anda peluang-peluang peraduan, dan
  4. kami boleh hantar kepada anda produk-produk yang telah anda pesan.
Tanpa rekod ini, Reader's Digest tidak akan dapat lakukan semua itu dan, dari itu, informasi yang anda beri diperlukan.
 
Seterusnya, Reader's Digest dari masa ke semasa, menerusi pengaturan kontrak yang sempurna, akan menyewa atau mendapatkan informasi mengenai konsumer-konsumer di Malaysia (yang telah memberi persetujuan) dari syarikat-syarikat pihak ketiga yang boleh dipercayai. Tujuan aktiviti penyewaan sekali-sekala ini hanyalah bagi memberikan Reader's Digest peluang menghantar kepada para konsumer tawaran promosi dan peluang peraduan.
 
Tambahan lagi, Reader's Digest akan, dari masa ke semasa, menerusi persetujuan kontrak yang sempurna, akan mendedahkan butiran kontak para pelanggannya yang tertentu, yang telah memberi persetujuan terdahulu, kepada syarikat-syarikat pihak ketiga yang boleh dipercayai. Tujuan pendedahan ini hanyalah untuk membolehkan syarikat-syarikat pihak ketiga itu menghantar atau memberi tawaran-tawaran konsumer, kewangan dan insurans yang kami percaya para pelanggan kami mungkin berminat.
 
Website cookies
 
Kami menggunakan "cookies" untuk membuat Lelaman kami berjalan dengan lebih baik. "Cookies" adalah fail-fail teks kecil yang akan disimpan oleh sesebuah lelaman di komputer anda bagi membolehkan ia mengenali pengguna berulang kali. "Cookies" bukanlah program-program yang masuk ke dalam sistem pengguna untuk merosakkan fail-fail. "Cookies" digunakan untuk membantu kami mengikuti corak pergerakan pengguna Internet seperti halaman mana di Lelaman kami yang anda lawati, apakah susunannya dan sekerap mana. "Cookies" tidak boleh digunakan oleh kami untuk mengetahui identiti anda. Ia sekadar membolehkan Lelaman kami mengenal komputer anda bila anda melawatnya. "Cookies" tidak digunakan untuk mencatat sebarang maklumat diri anda seperti nama, alamat atau alamat e-mel anda. Sekiranya anda tidak mahu "cookie" diperuntukkan kepada anda, anda boleh mengubah pelayar anda untuk tidak menerima "cookies".
