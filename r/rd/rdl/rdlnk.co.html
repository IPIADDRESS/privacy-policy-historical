Publishers

General Rules - These apply to all linking methods:
- Do not advertise reducelnk.com links directly on any form of traffic exchange / PTC website.
- Do not place reducelnk.com links anywhere that may contain adult material (including advertising).

- Do not offer any incentive for a visitor to click the reducelnk.com link, including gifts/points/cash.
- Do not ask/beg people to click on an reducelnk.com link simply to generate you revenue, they must want to visit the destination website.
- Do not create 'redirect loops' with similar services (or reducelnk.com) to generate revenue.
- Do not create spam with reduce.com links anywhere, including forums / chat / comments / blogs.
- Do not participate in click 'rings' where you click on reducelnk.com links in return for others to click yours.
- Do not open an reducelnk.com link in a popup / popunder or iframe.
- Only the reducelnk.com link must be opened when a user clicks on the link. No other links / windows must be opened.
- It is permitted to automatically redirect to an reducelnk.com link only with a HTTP Redirect (unless using one of our preapproved scripts, available on the reducelnk.com website). JavaScript redirect or meta-refresh is not allowed.
- If redirecting to an reducelnk.com link, the original link for the purposes of this agreement is classed as the 'ReduceLNK.com link'.
- The only legitimate way to open an reducelnk.com link is with a mouse click, on the actual link. *
- You may click on your own reducelnk.com links 1 time to test them. You may not create links solely to 'test'. **
- We reserve the right to not pay for advert views generated by the owner of the reducelnk.com account, on their own links.
* Does not apply to the links / views generated by the 'Website Entry Script'
** Does not apply to the links / views generated by the 'Website Entry Script' and 'Full Page Script'.
