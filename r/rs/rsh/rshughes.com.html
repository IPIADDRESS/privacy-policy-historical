Privacy Policy & Legal Notice

R.S. Hughes Company, Inc. respects your privacy. The only information that we collect about you is that which you choose to submit to us, either through a product registration form, by placing an order or through other interactive forms. We will always give you an opportunity to decline to receive further information.

General visitors to the R.S. Hughes product Web site

R.S. Hughes collects personal information, such as e-mail addresses, names or mailing addresses only when individual web site visitors choose to submit such information to us. Aggregate statistics are gathered on general web site traffic patterns.

Purchases from the R.S. Hughes Online Store

When you purchase goods online from R.S. Hughes at http://www.rshughes.com, we must collect a shipping and billing name, address, and credit card information to process your order.
