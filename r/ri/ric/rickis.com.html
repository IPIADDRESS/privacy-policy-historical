Privacy

Ricki's, a division of Comark, values its customers and associates and therefore has embraced the importance of securing their privacy. We have adopted a privacy statement that complies with all legislation pertaining to the collection, use, storage and destruction of personal information.

Any personal information you provide to us, including and similar to your name, address, telephone number and email address will not be released, sold or rented to any entities or individuals for any purpose other than those identified above, except with your consent. We will protect your personal information with appropriate security safeguards, and control who has access to personal information. We will ensure that highly sensitive information is safely disposed of when it is no longer required.

We strive to keep your personal information as accurate and up to date as is necessary for the purposes identified above, and we will hour any request you may make for access to your personal information.
