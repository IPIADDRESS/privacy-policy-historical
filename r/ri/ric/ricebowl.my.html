We may disclose and transfer information to a third party who acquires any or all of RiceBowl’s business units.

You may review, update, or delete the personal information you share with us at any time by accessing your account. When you delete your personal information, we will retain logs and non-personal information about you along with an archival copy of your information, which is not accessible by you or third parties on the Internet. If your personal information was previously accessed by others using RiceBowl, we are unable to delete the information from their systems. You may turn off cookies.
