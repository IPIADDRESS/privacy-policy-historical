Privacy/ Security/ Fraud Prevention

Thank you for shopping at Rimmer Bros.
We appreciate your business and are committed to your privacy. Your personal information and customer records are held securely on our system and are never disclosed to any outside 3rd parties at any time. As a customer, and additionally when you subscribe to our email list, the information you provide is used to send you details of new products, special offers, news, etc. You can request to unsubscribe at any time.

Our website uses industry-standard Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology, to provide encryption of personal information such as your name, address and credit card details. In this way, information passed between your computer and our website cannot be read in the unlikely event of someone intercepting it. All credit and debit card transactions are secure. We will only accept orders using Secure Socket Layer technology-this means you cannot inadvertently reveal personal information by using an unsecured connection. In order for SSL security to work, you must use an SSL enabled browser.

Please note that email encryption is not always secure, so please use our website for placing your order. If you still have reservations, you can call to place your order on +44(0)1522 568000 or FAX it on +44(0)1522 567600.

We do not show your credit card details on any paperwork. To prevent fraudulent or unauthorised use of credit cards for first-time buyers, we insist that your first order is delivered directly to the card holder's registered address. Additionally, we randomly select orders for authorisation checks prior to processing.

Cookies
What are cookies?
A "cookie" is a small piece of information sent by a web server to a web browser so it can be read back later from that browser. This is useful for having the browser remember some specific information.

What are they used for?
"Cookies"may be used to store information such as user ID's or to record user preferences for site personalisation. On-line ordering systems commonly use cookies to remember the items that a person may want to buy. The Rimmer Bros on-line ordering system uses cookies to record the parts on your order. If you have any problems using the on-line ordering system and receive messages such as 'There is no order to be placed', then this may be due to your browser not accepting "cookies". To enable cookies please refer to the help facility on your web browser software.

What about security?
"Cookies" can only be accessed by the site that created them and cannot be used to get data from your hard drive, retrieve your email address or access sensitive personal information. It is possible to configure your web browser to warn you before accepting a cookie. It will show the value and expiry date of the cookie. Refer to the help facility on your browser software for further information.
