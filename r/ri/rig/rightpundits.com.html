Privacy Policy

Right Pundits desires to protect the privacy of our visitors. When leaving comments, an email address is required to post. You are free to fake that email address or leave a real one. Regardless, this information is not available to the public and will not become part of a commercial mailing list or sold. We do not even keep a mailing list.

Cookies are not utilized internally by Right Pundits for data gathering. The publishers do not attempt to gather any personal information about you during the course of visiting our website.

However we use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies,click here.
