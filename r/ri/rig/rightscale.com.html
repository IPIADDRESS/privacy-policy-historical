Privacy Policy

RightScale respects your privacy. We will not release your private information or information that can identify you as an individual to any partner or 3rd party.

Any information you provide on the RightScale site will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to. A more detailed explanation about how we safeguard your personal information is described below. If you have any questions, please don’t hesitate to let us know.

The Amazon Web Services credentials entered into the RightScale are used exclusively per your instructions through the RightScale interface. These credentials are stored in encrypted form in the database and are not released to 3rd parties, or transmitted across any network in unencrypted form.

Resale or Disclosure of Information to Third Parties

RightScale does not sell, rent, loan, trade or lease any personal information collected on its site, including membership forums and email lists unless contact has been requested during information submission. RightScale does sell, rent, loan, trade or lease anonymous information collected on its site. We think this reflects best practices in the current industry and welcome your feedback.

Browser Information Collected on the Website

RightScale analyzes its website logs to continually re-evaluate the site contents to better meet client needs. The logs are not used to identify any personal details.

Use of Cookies

RightScale uses cookies to simplifiy user login and account management. These cookies will not be sold, rented, loaned or otherwise disclosed.
