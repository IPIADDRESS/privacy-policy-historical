This Privacy Policy, effective May 25, 2018, applies to all of the products, services and websites offered by Unleashed, LLC or our subsidiaries or affiliated companies.

On May 25, 2018, the General Data Protection Regulation (“GDPR”), a data protection and privacy regulation concerning the collection, use and processing of personal information of individuals in the European Union (“EU”), will go into effect.  The GDPR replaced the 1995 Data Protection Directive (Directive 95/46/EC).  It applies to all companies which collect and process data of EU residents.  This Privacy Policy has been updated to reflect the changes and improvements Unleashed, LLC has made in response to the mandates of the GDPR. 

Information Collection and Use

According to the European Commission, "personal data is any information relating to an individual, whether it relates to his or her private, professional or public life. It can be anything from a name, a home address, a photo, an email address, bank details, posts on social networking websites, medical information, or a computer’s IP address."[1]  Personal Information includes but is not limited to identifiers such as an identification number, location data, an online identifier, or to one or more factors specific to the physical, physiological, genetic, mental, economic, cultural or social identity of a person.  We do not consider information that has been anonymized, aggregated or encrypted so that it can no longer be used to identify a specific person or non-personal or device-level information to be personal information.  To the extent Unleashed, LLC collects personal information, we require your explicit consent in order to process the data and provide Unleashed, LLC’s services, which you may withdraw at any time by sending a request to Unleashed, LLC, Attn:  Legal Department (Privacy Policy), 15 Walden Street, Concord, MA 01741.  Absent explicit consent, Unleashed, LLC is authorized to collect and process personal information about you when the processing is necessary to perform a contract with you, like the Terms of Service or in furtherance of Unleashed, LLC’s legitimate business interests, such as in improving, personalizing, and developing the services, marketing new features or products that may be of interest, and promoting safety and security as described herein.

Unleashed, LLC may collect the following types of information:

  • Log information - When you access Unleashed, LLC’s services via a browser, application or other client, our servers automatically record certain information. These server logs may include information such as your web request, your interaction with a service, Internet Protocol address, browser type, browser language, operating system, the referring web page, pages visited, location, your mobile carrier, device information, the date and time of your request, information you provide for your profile and one or more cookies that may uniquely identify your browser or your account.       
  • User communications - When you send email or other communications to Unleashed, LLC, we may retain your message, email address, and contact information in order to process your inquiries, respond to your requests and improve Unleashed, LLC’s services. When you send and receive SMS messages to or from one of Unleashed, LLC’s services that provides SMS functionality, we may collect and maintain information associated with those messages, such as the phone number, the wireless carrier associated with the phone number, the content of the message, and the date and time of the transaction. We may use your email address to communicate with you about Unleashed, LLC’s services.
  • Third Party Applications - Unleashed, LLC may make available third party applications, such as gadgets or extensions, through our services. The information collected by Unleashed, LLC when you enable a third party application is processed under this Privacy Policy. Information collected by the third party application provider is governed by their privacy policies. For example, we share your location entries with third-party ride-share providers and mapping providers to provide the requested fare results and, if you choose to sign up for our  newsletter, we share your email addresses, first and last names with MailChimp to provide you with periodic communications on our behalf. 
  • Location data - Unleashed, LLC offers location-enabled services. If you use those services, Unleashed, LLC may receive information about your actual location (such as GPS signals sent by a mobile device) or information that can be used to approximate a location (such as a cell ID).  Location services can be disabled or controlled on most mobile devices.
  • Links – Unleashed, LLC may keep track of how you interact with links across Unleashed, LLC’s services by redirecting clicks or through other means.  This enables us to improve our services, to provide more relevant advertising, and to aggregate click statistics. 
  • Advertising – Unleashed, LLC’s services are supported by advertising.  Your personal information may be used to customize our advertising to make it more relevant and effective.
  • Contests - If you contact Unleashed, LLC or participate in a survey, contest, or promotion, we collect the information you submit such as your name, contact information, and message.
  • Other sites - This Privacy Policy applies to Unleashed, LLC’s services only. We do not exercise control over the sites displayed as search results, sites that include Unleashed, LLC links, products or services, or links from within our various services. These other sites may place their own cookies or other files on your computer, collect data or solicit personal information from you.

Some of the information collected is personal information under the GDPR.  This personal information may be necessary in order to use Unleashed, LLC’s services.  You may also provide other personal information through your use of Unleashed, LLC’s services, dispute resolution, or when you otherwise communicate with Unleashed, LLC regarding our services.

You may purposely or inadvertently provide personal information about someone else, for example someone’s home address.  Any personal information provided by you about someone else is presumed to be provided with that other person’s consent. 

In addition to the above, Unleashed, LLC may use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, protect, and improve Unleashed, LLC’s  services (including advertising services) and develop new services; and
  • Protect the rights or property of Unleashed, LLC or our users.

Unleashed, LLC processes personal information on our servers in the United States of America and in other countries. In some cases, we process personal information outside your own country. We may transfer your personal information to service providers and other partners for processing, based on our instructions, and in compliance with this policy and all applicable laws. For example, Unleashed, LLC uses third-party providers to provide our services, such as web hosting, and to help us understand and improve the use of our services, such as Google Analytics.  We may share your personal information with such third-party providers consistent with this Privacy Policy and any other applicable confidentiality and security measures.

Unleashed, LLC takes appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of data. These include internal reviews of our data collection, storage and processing practices and security measures, including appropriate encryption and physical security measures to guard against unauthorized access to systems where we store personal data.

These individuals are bound by confidentiality obligations and may be subject to discipline, including termination and criminal prosecution, if they fail to meet these obligations.

When you visit our sites or use our services or applications, we may use cookies and other similar technologies to collect additional website usage data to help provide you with a better, faster, and safer experience, and for advertising and marketing purposes.  Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but some browsers allow you to decline cookies or alert you when a website it attempting to place a cookie on your computer.

You have the right to access your personal information and to request that Unleashed, LLC correct, delete or restrict usage of your personal information as required by applicable law.  You have the right to know what personal information we collect and maintain about you and to object to our processing and use of your personal information.  You can ask us to update incorrect or incomplete personal information or to delete or restrict how we use your personal information.  However, these rights are subject to applicable law and a request to stop processing your personal information or a withdrawal of consent to use or process your personal information may impact your ability to use Unleashed, LLC’s services.
