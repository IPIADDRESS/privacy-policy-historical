The Race To 61: 50th Anniversary

The Yankees played a doubleheader on this date in 1961, their 54th and 55th games of the season. Roger Maris went deep twice on the day, his 19th and 20th homeruns of the season. Mickey Mantle only hit one homer, his 19th.
