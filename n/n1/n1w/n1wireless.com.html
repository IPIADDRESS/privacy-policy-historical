   N1 Wireless.com will email all customers with specific updates regarding their order, tracking information, availability of items, and N1 Wireless.com may respond to customer questions by email or telephone.

N1 Wireless.com does log the IP address of all incoming orders, but we will not use cookies or any other means to obtain our customers personal information other than what they choose to submit on our site.  All information is collected lawfully and in accordance with the Data Protection Act of 1998.

SECURITY
