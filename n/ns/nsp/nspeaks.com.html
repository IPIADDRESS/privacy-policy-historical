Privacy Policy


NSpeaks do collects information which will be as follows:

  1. If you have subscribed to our email feeds or newsletters, we keep a record of your email ids. But since we hate spam, we never share those with anybody. I hate spam as much as you do. Period.
  2. If you are using RSS readers, information regarding the type of RSS reader may be logged.
  3. With every visit to this site, we do keep record of your IP, Browser used, Country of Origin and a cookie may be placed on your PC. This is done for pure statistical purposes and to keep a check of NSpeaks’s popularity. We don’t gain anything out of this.
  4. None of the information collected above is shared with anybody except the visitor count which is displayed as anonymously irrespective of where/who the visitor is.
  5. Google Advertising Cookie Information
    • Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user’s prior visits to your website.
    • Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
    • Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the advertising opt-out page.
  6. Infolinks Information
    • Infolinks Information – You may see some dotted links with an image to them. These links are part of a advertising program called Infolinks. If you hover with your mouse over these dotted underlined links, you will see a small dialog box containing advertisement related to the text marked. You can choose to either move the mouse away and go on with your browsing, or to press on the box and go to the ad page which is relevant to the text. Click here to opt out of them if you want.
  7. Again this policy is subject to change at any time, with or without notice.
