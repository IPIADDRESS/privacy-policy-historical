Privacy


Information Collected and Used

NeuroLeadership Group will not divulge personal information including your name and e-mail address to any third party without your express permission.

Personal Information

NeuroLeadership Group will not provide our advertisers or anyone else outside of NeuroLeadership Group with any information specific to you collected from the website unless we have your consent. The only website employees or consultants allowed access to personal information about you are those who need to have access. Any employee or consultant who violates our privacy and security policies is subject to disciplinary action, including possible termination or prosecution.

Data Protection

To the extent that that any data protection, privacy or similar laws ("Data Protection Laws") apply to this Agreement, NeuroLeadership Group agrees to be bound by, and comply with, the provisions of such laws.

Cookies

“Cookies” are small text files that allow Web sites to store and retrieve information about you from your computer system. The NeuroLeadership Group websites use cookies to track individual site usage for later aggregation.

Communications from Us

From time to time, we may send you information with announcements and updates about NeuroLeadership Group. You may elect to opt-out of ongoing email communication from us, such as newsletters, subscriptions, program information, promotional materials, survey inquiries, etc. by using a simple “opt-out” procedure. You can opt-out by clicking the link at the bottom of the email you received or by replying with the word “unsubscribe” in the subject line.

Correcting / Updating Personal Information

If your personal information changes, you may update your personal information at any time by sending an email to support@neuroleadership.com.

You may also delete your personal profile data from our database by sending an email to support@neuroleadership.com.

Policy Changes and Acceptance

This privacy policy is effective as of March 1, 2012. By using our website and its services, you signify your acceptance of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree or are not comfortable with any policy described in this Privacy Policy, you may discontinue use of our website. We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Your continued use of any portion of our websites will constitute your acceptance of these changes.

Questions

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, e-mail them to support@neuroleadership.com.
