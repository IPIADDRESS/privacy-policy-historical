4. Cookies


NESTPICK uses cookies to collect information about how this Website is used in order to keep the Website up to date and error-free. Cookies are small pieces of information in text form sent by a web server to a web browser allowing the server to uniquely identify the browser on each page. If Users want to delete cookies that are stored on Users’ computer, this can be done via respective Users’ browser settings. Please note that disabling NESTPICKs cookies may limit the access to the Website.


5. Tracking


Google Analytics: The Website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc.(“Google”). Google Analytics uses Cookies. The information generated by a Cookie about Users’ use of the Website (including User’s internet protocol address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States. Google uses this information for the purposes of evaluating User’s use of the Website, compiling reports on website activity for website operators, and providing other services relating to website activity and internet usage. Google may also transfer this information to third parties when required to do so by law or when such third parties process the information on Google’s behalf. Google will not associate User’s internet protocol address with any other information held by Google. User may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on User’s browser and/or device. By using this Website, information about Users will be processed by Google in the manner and for the purposes set out above. Users can find out more about Google’s privacy policy for Google Analytics and how to opt out of Google Analytics on https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

The tool collects potentially personally-identifying information such as internet protocol addresses and database queries. Such information is not used to identify its Users. For more information please review the New Relic privacy policy available under https://newrelic.com/privacy.
