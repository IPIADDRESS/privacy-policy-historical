SEO Tip of the Week
Write your copy with PEOPLE in mind, not search engines.
Google, Yahoo, and Bing Do take a look at your wording, BUT, they do not buy your products or services, or click your links.

Make sure that your copy is appealing to people.

Use your keywords in a logical way, not in an incorrect way. What may appeal to the search engines, will have people believing that you're illiterate, and it often turns them off from buying your products.

First and foremost, get copy that appeals to the human factor and balance that with keywords that will appeal to the search engines.
