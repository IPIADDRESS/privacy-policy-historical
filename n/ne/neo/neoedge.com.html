Questions: If you have objections to the Privacy Policy, you should immediately discontinue use of the Services enabled by the NeoEdge-powered sites and contact the Privacy Policy Administrator at privacy@neoedge.com. If you have questions about our Privacy Policy or if you do not understand any information about how we collect, maintain, use or share your personally identifiable information, you may contact us directly by sending us an e-mail to our Privacy Policy Administrator at privacy@neoedge.com.

NeoEdge Networks does not knowingly request or collect any Personal Information (as defined below) online or offline contact information from users under 13 years old and does not knowingly use or share personal information from users under 13 years old with third parties. If you are under 13 years old, please do not use the Services or send us any Personal Information such as your name, address, phone number or email address. If we find out that we have collected any such Personal Information from a child under the age 13, we will immediately delete that information. If you think that we have collected any such information, please notify the Privacy Policy Administrator at privacy@neoedge.com, we suggest that you get your parents' permission before sending any information to us over the Internet.
