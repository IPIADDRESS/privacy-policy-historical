Shift Linux is a project that was created by the Neowin community. Based on Morphix Linux, Shift is Debian based, therefore it has access to all of the software and applications as other Debian distributions.

Neowin's Shift Linux is designed to give the user an experience of being part of the Neowin community as well as having a simple, easy-to-use Live CD that can be installed to your hard drive, if you enjoy the way our distribution works. Shift is a free, GPL-based Linux distribution that can be freely distributed or modified.

    We will ask you when we need information that personally identifies you (Personal Information) or allows us to contact you.
