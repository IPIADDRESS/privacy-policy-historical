WHAT KIND OF INFORMATION WILL BE COLLECTED?

Neat Group Corporation, ("Neat") does not collect any personally identifiable information but we do track individual login activity and gather data regarding site activity such as domain names, IP addresses, browser type, access times and pages accessed.

Neat also uses cookies to identify your computer and maintain communications during your login session. A cookie is a unique identifier that is sent to your computer from a Web site's computers. While cookies do not personally identify the user, they do allow you to advance through the site without having to type your login name and password through each module. You can choose not to receive a cookie by enabling your Web browser to refuse cookies or prompt you before accepting a cookie. However, please be aware that cookie refusal will reduce the quality of your online experience.

HOW WILL THE INFORMATION BE USED?

Rest assured that any information collected by our site will be used only for internal purposes. We may use aggregate information about our users and visitors to improve our marketing efforts, statistically analyze site usage, to improve our services, and product offerings, and to improve our site's content, layout, and services. We believe these uses will improve our site and add value to your online experience.

WILL INFORMATION BE SHARED?

Neat will not sell, rent, or share the information collected through our Web site with third parties unless there is an internal purpose for doing so. Occasionally, Neat may hire a third party to act on our behalf for marketing projects such as market research surveys and will provide information to these third parties specifically for this purpose. If we do, the project will be subject to strict confidentiality restrictions and will never reveal any personally identifiable information about you.
