Privacy Policy

Network-Room.com respects and protects your privacy when you are using our free Search and Link Directory. Information that may identify you as an individual is never collected nor shared with third parties.

Cookies
Network-Room.com may send a "cookie" to your computer. Cookies are used by many sites. An Internet search on 'cookies' may provide you with more information. The intent of cookies is to identify your computer preferences and searching patterns. Network-Room.com will not disclose its cookies to third parties, except as required by a valid legal process such as a search warrant, subpoena, statute, or court order.

What info do we collect?
Network-Room.com does not secretly collect information about you (such as your name, email address, etc.) except when you specifically and knowingly provide such information during the site submission process. This information is used to contact you if there is a need for a revision to your site reviews. Network-Room.com notes and saves information such as time of day, browser type, browser language, and IP address with each query. This information is used to verify our records and to provide more relevant services to users.

Link to other content on the web
Network-Room.com has no control over other sites that link to us. These other sites may send their own cookies to users, collect data, or solicit personal information. It is important that you review their Privacy Policy if you have any concerns.

Shared information
Network-Room.com may share about you to advertisers, sponsors and third parties. Be aware that this is not specific information about you, but rather demographic and overall search preferences.

Changes to Privacy Policy
Be aware that we may change this Privacy Policy from time to time at our discresion. Any changes will be date stamped and posted here. The purpose of this policy is to make you an informed Internet user.
