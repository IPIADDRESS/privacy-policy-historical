PlazaDirect.com Privacy Policy
PlazaDirect Privacy Commitment
PlazaDirect, formerly NetDeals, respects the sensitive nature of the information you provide to us. Our Privacy Policy is designed to maximize your ability to protect your personal information while giving you the opportunity to participate in our weekly shopping spree sweepstakes, make purchases and request catalog samples from the merchants appearing on PlazaDirect.com, as well as to obtain interesting and useful information about products and services.

This Privacy Policy describes how we treat the information we receive about you when you submit your entry form. Please read the Privacy Policy carefully because it governs how we use the information you submit to us. If the Privacy Policy changes in the future, we will not use the information you have submitted to us in a manner that is inconsistent with the Privacy Policy under which the information was originally collected.

Information - What You Provide to Us and How We Use It
When you enter our weekly shopping spree sweepstakes or register with PlazaDirect.com for any other purpose, you will be providing personal information to us. Personal information includes such things as your name, address, phone number, street address, and email address.

When you enter our weekly shopping spree sweepstakes or register to make a purchase or receive a catalog, your information will be collected and used for that purpose. We also provide your name and address to trusted third parties in order that they may provide catalogs or other marketing materials to you.

If you opt-in (specifically choose) to receive e-mail offers about new products and services available from our trusted third party marketing partners, we will combine your personal information with other personal information about you that is available to us, such as past purchase history, in order provide you with email notices and offers that will be geared towards your individual preferences and tastes. Each email you receive will provide you with the opportunity to opt-out of receiving future e-mails.

Information about Children
PlazaDirect does not collect, store, or use information about children. You must be at least 18 years of age in order to participate in the PlazaDirect Shopping Spree Sweepstakes.

Cookies
PlazaDirect is sponsored by DoubleClick. You can learn more about DoubleClick by visiting its web site at http://www.DoubleClick.net. In the course of communicating with you, this site, and the advertisements you see on it, will use "cookies" to communicate with your browser.

PlazaDirect, along with thousands of other Web sites, uses cookies to enhance your Web viewing experience. DoubleClick's cookies do not damage your system or files in any way. A Cookie is a small text file that includes a unique number. DoubleClick records that number in the cookie file of your computer. The Cookie allows you to communicate with our site and allows us to remember your browser on a repeat visit. Cookies also allow DoubleClick to serve you ads that are better tailored to your needs or interests.

If you would like more information on Cookies, including information on how to opt-out of the DoubleClick Cookie, please read the DoubleClick Privacy Policy.

Protecting your Privacy
DoubleClick is committed to protecting consumer privacy online. We are active members of the Network Advertising Initiative, NetCoalition.com, Online Privacy Alliance, Internet Advertising Bureau, New York New Media Association, and the American Advertising Federation.

For more information about protecting your privacy online, we recommend that you visit www.privacychoices.org, www.networkadvertising.org, www.netcoalition.com, and www.privacyalliance.org.

Advertising
The ads appearing on this Web site are delivered to you by DoubleClick, an Internet Advertising company (and PlazaDirect, formerly NetDeals', parent company). In the course of delivering an ad to you DoubleClick collects certain non-personally identifiable information about you. This information is used to deliver tailored ads to you. If you would like to know more about the information gathering practices and "opt-out" procedures of DoubleClick, please read the DoubleClick Privacy Policy.

Security
PlazaDirect maintains internal practices that help to protect the security and confidentiality of this information by limiting employee access to and use of this information.

Access
PlazaDirect offers users who have voluntarily provided personally-identifiable information with the opportunity to review the information provided and to correct any errors. After you have created an account, you can review that information by going to My Account.

Questions?
If you have any questions about the PlazaDirect Web site, our Privacy Policy, or our collection of information, please contact us at info@doubleclick.net. So that we can route your inquiry quickly, please include "PlazaDirect" in the subject line of your e-mail.
