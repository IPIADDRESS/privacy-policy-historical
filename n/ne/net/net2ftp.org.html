Privacy policy

Summary

We keep usage statistics for 2 months to answer abuse investigation requests. These statistics include:

  • Date, time
  • Your IP address, port and your country (derived from your IP address)
  • Your browser type and version
  • Your FTP/SSH server hostname, IP address and country (derived from your server's IP address)
  • Your FTP/SSH username
  • Action executed (e.g. login, browse, edit, etc)
  • Directory and filename
We do NOT store your FTP/SSH password.

We temporarily store files in transit while the script is being executed; these files are deleted afterwards.

On the bottom of the Browse screen we display the volume of data transferred from your IP address today, and to your FTP server today. If you use a shared IP address or connect to a shared FTP server, these usage statistics are therefore shared with other users who might use this website.

None of this information is shared with anyone.

This website is hosted on a server managed by Infomaniak.com, a Swiss company which is GDPR compliant.
