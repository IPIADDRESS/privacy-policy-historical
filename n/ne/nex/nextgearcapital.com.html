This pertains to all non-public personal information you provide to NextGear Capital through the website.  Your information may be used as follows:


  • To present the website and contents to you.

  • To fulfill any other purposes for which you provide it.

  • To notify you about changes to the website or any products or services we offer or provide.

The cookies used by the website do not contain any personal information regarding the visitor, and do not retrieve any information from the visitor’s personal computer (other than your IP address and other similar information retrieved or retained in order to facilitate your use of the website).  They are used only to enhance the user’s experience on the website, and to collect anonymous traffic data to enable us to provide a better web experience for the viewing public in general.
