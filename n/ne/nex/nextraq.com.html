    Overview

    NexTraq provides you with an easy-to use, cost-effective GPS fleet tracking solution to help you automate your manual business processes, saving you time and money. Work smarter, not harder while maximizing your fleet operations.

    Tracking

    With NexTraq, you can track your vehicles to ensure drivers are following their scheduled routes, re-route vehicles on-the-fly and increase fleet productivity by as much as 50 percent by providing the most efficient route.

    Dispatching

    Use NexTraq’s dispatching feature to improve communication between your dispatchers and drivers, send voice-guided, turn-by-turn messages and improve driver safety. Quickly react to an emergency service call by sending the closest vehicle.

    Mobile App

    The NexTraq mobile app gives you 24/7 access to your important fleet data from a web-enabled smartphone. Whether you are in the office or out in the field, you can view emails, alerts and SMS to know your fleet’s activity.

    Reporting

    With more than 25 reports and 17 alerts, our GPS vehicle tracking platform gives you the tools you need to review and improve your fleet operations. Subscribe to fleet reports of interest to you and automatically receive them in your inbox.

    Alerts

    Easily set up alerts to be notified in real-time a threshold has been triggered. Know instantly when important events are happening with your vehicle and take action. You can set up Alerts to automatically be sent to your inbox.

    Dashboard

    Our fleet management dashboard gives you a single view of your fleet’s performance. You can look at fleet’s activity from a high-level view or drill down to look at an individual driver’s activity. The graphical interface makes it easy to find areas to improve.

    Web Services

    Our Web Services offering enables you to integrate your third-party application to our fleet tracking platform. Integrate accounting/billing, route optimization and mobile workforce management applications for increased productivity.

    Know - Manage - Improve

    Know. Manage. Improve. You can’t measure what you don’t know. Use our fleet tracking platform to know where your vehicles are, manage your fleet and employees and improve your overall fleet operations for increase service revenue.

    ClearPath Routing

    What was once a time-consuming and frustrating process is now simple with NexTraq’s ClearPath routing. With a click of a button, create the most efficient route for your driver, saving fuel and increasing driver productivity.

    Satellite GPS Asset Tracking

    NexTraq's new global satellite GPS asset tracking device works solely with satellite technology and is not dependent on any mobile network, enabling NexTraq customers to affordably track anything, anywhere.

    Automatic Driver Assignment

    Having trouble knowing which employee is in which vehicle? With automatic driver assignment, you can easily identify drivers with a dedicated key fob when the driver swipes the reader. Changing vehicles and drivers doesn’t have to be difficult.

    Case Studies

    NexTraq is proud of the fact that 95% of our customers would refer us to another business or associate. Our case study library shows you why. Whether is reducing fuel costs or improving driver productivity, there’s a use case for your business.

    White Papers

    Before making an investment on a GPS fleet tracking solution, most businesses will do quite a bit of research on what solutions are available. This library provides you the tool you need to make an informed decision.

    Overview

    Whether you have a fleet of five vehicles or 50, NexTraq GPS fleet management platform can help you reduce your overhead costs and increase fleet productivity. No matter your industry, NexTraq helps service businesses get real results.

    Cable & Telecom

    Give your customers accurate arrival times instead of vague service windows and they will thank you for it. With the NexTraq Fleet Tracking platform and dispatch functionality improve your customer service levels and gain new business.

    Construction

    Having trouble making sure your workers are at the job site? Use our GPS fleet tracking solution to verify your crew’s arrival, their departures and pickups, and hours worked. Protect your valuable assets to avoid expensive losses.

    Electrical

    Put a spark in your business and maximize your workforce to get more done with less. Track your vehicles to respond to an emergency service call with the closest vehicle available. Get more jobs done in a workday with fleet tracking.

    Food & Beverage

    Unlike other service businesses, your cargo is perishable. Use our temperature sensors to ensure you arrive with your customer’s order at the highest quality. What would a lost load of food mean to your bottom line?

    Government

    Making sure that your employees are at their most productive can be difficult when your vehicles are outside the four walls of the office. Our GPS fleet management solution can help you get more jobs completed in a typical workday.

    HVAC & Mechanical

    When a customer’s heating or air conditioning goes out, they want service now. Use our fleet tracking solution to provide accurate ETAs or re-route the vehicle closest to the service call. Retain and win new business with dependable service.

    Landscaping

    With crews spread out across your metro area, tracking their arrivals and departures is key to ensure they are at the job site. Protect your expensive assets from theft with NexTraq’s Asset Tracker line. Keep your crew on schedule with NexTraq.

    Pest Control

    Most customers with a plumbing issue want it taken care of immediately. And, who could blame them? Increase your fleet productivity by up to 50% with NexTraq. Re-route drivers on the fly for emergency situations and improve your customer service.

    Security Services

    How quickly can you respond to a screaming alarm in the middle of the night? NexTraq’s fleet tracking platform can help you quickly locate your client to get onto the scene as soon as possible. Take the most efficient route and get there fast.

    Utilities

    Manage your employee’s stops with our GPS fleet management solution. Tracking their idle times can significantly reduce your fuel costs and improve fleet productivity. Whether you cover a small or larger area, handle it efficiently.

    Waste Management

    Whether it’s scheduled garbage pickup or a waste water treatment facility, ensure your driver’s stick to their routes with NexTraq’s Fleet Tracking platform. Use our reporting functionality to find areas that you can optimize your operations.

NexTraq Privacy Statement

In order to improve the Company’s website and to better tailor it to meet its Customers needs, the Company gathers certain types of information about its Customers to improve marketing and promotional efforts, to statistically analyze site usage, to improve content and product offerings and to customize the Company’s website content, layout and services. This may include personal information such as email addresses and aggregate information such as the number of “hits” the Company’s website receives in a day.

The Company may use some of this information to deliver helpful information to its Customers, which may include new services and advertising and promotions that are targeted to its Customer’s personal interests. In addition, the Company may use a Customer’s Email address, and phone number to contact him or her regarding administrative notices, new product offerings, and communications relevant to the use of the website. However, a Customer may elect not to receive some of this information by notifying the Company via Email or by telephone at the Company’s main office.

The Company may use information learned about its Customers through the service and website to resolve disputes, to troubleshoot problems, and to enforce the Company’s End User License Agreement. In order to protect the Company’s Customers, the service and the website, the Company may evaluate Customer information for security and other purposes, such as checking for multiple User IDs or aliases.

NexTraq™ may disclose personal information if such information is subpoenaed, or if NexTraq believes a Customer is using the service or website to commit unlawful acts or acts that endanger the health, safety, or welfare of another user or the general public. In addition, the Company may be required to disclose information to the government or third parties under certain circumstances involving illegal acts, for example, in the unlikely event that unauthorized third parties unlawfully intercept or access transmissions or private communications. Further, by signing the NexTraq End User License Agreement, the Customer authorizes the Company to disclose any information about the Customer to law enforcement or other government officials as the Company, in its sole discretion, believe necessary or appropriate, in connection with an investigation of fraud, intellectual property infringements or other activity that is illegal or may expose the Company to legal liability. Also, by signing the End User License Agreement, the Customer authorizes the Company to disclose their User ID, name, street address, city, state, zip code, country, phone number, Email and company as NexTraq in its sole discretion, believes necessary or appropriate in connection with an investigation of fraud, intellectual property infringement, piracy or other unlawful activity.
