Your Privacy
At Next Jump, we are committed to protecting your privacy. Personal information -- including your E-mail address -- is collected only for registering to win, playing games, or purchasing something. We will NOT sell our email list to another company.

What information do we collect? How do we use it?
When you order something, we need to know your name, e-mail address, mailing address, credit card number, and expiration date. This allows us to process and fulfill your order and to notify you of your order status.

When you enter a contest or other promotional feature, we may ask for your name, address, and e-mail address so we can administer the contest and notify winners. We may also use the information we collect to occasionally notify you about important functionality changes to the Web site, new Next Jump, Inc. services, and special offers we think you'll find valuable. We occasionally have events planned such as giveaways, free prizes, contests, etc.
