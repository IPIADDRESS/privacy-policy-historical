The use of cookies is an industry standard, and you'll find cookies at most major Web sites.

Cookies help make NewsFactor Network Web sites better, by showing how and when guests use the site. Many content improvements and updates are based on such data as total number of visitors and pages viewed. This information is most easily tracked with cookies.
