Personally Identifiable Information Collected by NewsTunez

In General: By providing NewsTunez with your personally identifiable information in connection with your use of NewsTunez’s services and/or any of the features/functions of the Site, you consent to NewsTunez’s collection and use of your Personally Identifiable Information as follows:

  • To provide the products and services requested via the Site;

We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement.

Registered Users: In order to use some of the Site, a user may first register. During registration a user gives contact information (such as name and email address). We do NOT store credit card numbers in our database, nor is the credit card number stored at any point on our server, it is transmitted directly to gateway (currently authorize.net). Please click here to review their privacy and security policies.

Cookies: A cookie is a small data file that certain webSite write to your hard drive when you visit them. A cookie file can contain information such as a user ID that the Site uses to track the pages you've visited. When you register at the Site, NewsTunez uses a cookie to store a unique, random user ID. We do not store passwords or any personal information about you.We may use this ID to identify you anonymously in our database and to track information while you are on our Site. Cookies also enable you to enter the Site as a registered user without having to log on each time. You can refuse cookies by turning them off in your browser.You do not need to have cookies turned on to use the Site. However, if you turn off cookies, then you may not be able to utilize certain features of the Site.
