At NewsDen, we are committed to providing a quality online service. It is in our best interest that our users feel safe while they visit our site in order to read news and share their comments.

NewsDen complies with all the regulations set forth by the European Union and United States Department of Commerce, regarding collection, use and retention of data.

Information we collect
The information we collect from our users are vital stats that we need in order to make our services better. This type of information is typically non-personally identifiable and does not impose any risk/harm to our site visitors.
