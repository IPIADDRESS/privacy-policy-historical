Cookies

The News 14 Carolina website collects no personally identifying information about users except when specifically and knowingly provided by such individuals via email and the Interactive Viewer Center. This site may place a "cookie" in the browser files of a user's computer. The cookie itself contains no personal information. The information tracked is intended to enhance the online experience of the user by remembering the type of browser, operating system and Internet service provider used by the visitor. By having this information, web pages optimized for a particular visitor's computer are automatically made available to that visitor.
