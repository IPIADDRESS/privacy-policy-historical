INTI Business Leadership Series  
 

INTI Business Leadership Series is part of our Enrolment to Employment (E2E) initiative, with the hope of bringing the industry into the classroom while enhancing the learning process with industry assimilation.

Get unique insights into some of the industries in the corporate arena as you seize the opportunity to interact with top key leaders from established organisations, sharing their valuable knowledge and skills.
