Privacy policy for nusonline

The nusonline website is operated jointly by the National Union of Students and ITM Communications Ltd.  Both the NUS and ITM are committed to protecting your privacy when you are using its on-line services.

To protect your privacy, we apply five principles which you can read about here.

Principle 1 - Notice
We will ask for personal information that identifies you, which enables you to be contacted and about your course. We will also ask you for information relating to your interests.

We will use the information only for the following purposes:

  • To improve the content, design and layout of the nusonline website
  • To carry out research on the demographics, interests and behaviour of our registered users
  • To perform marketing and promotional activities
  • To forward information to you which we feel you may find useful such as details of products and services.

Either of us may pass your personal information to the constituent member unions of the NUS and to other selected organisations which may also use it for these purposes.

This privacy policy only covers the nusonline website at http://www.nusonline.co.uk. Other links within this site to other websites are not covered by this policy.

With your consent we may also publish some of your personal information within the nusonline website to assist other registered users of nusonline in finding your contact information.

We may continue to use your personal information for a period after you cease being a registered user.

Principle 2 - Consent and Choice
If you choose not to register or provide personal information, you may still visit the nusonline website but you will not be able to access the restricted areas available to registered users.

By clicking on "I Accept" during the registration process, you accept the terms and conditions described herein and consent to our using your information accordingly.

When you register you may choose to opt out of direct marketing activities.

You may also enable a personal page within nusonline. By enabling this personal page, you are consenting to your contact details being made available to other registered users of nusonline.

Principle 3 - Access
We have provided an administration service where you can review your personal information at any time to ensure it is correct and current. You will be able to update, correct, change or delete inaccurate details.

You may also change your choice of opting in or out of direct marketing activities, although you may continue to receive mailings for a short period while your suppression is being processed.

You have the right to ask for a copy of your personal information on payment of a small fee.

Principle 4 - Security
We have taken appropriate measures to ensure that your personal information is not unlawfully processed. However, no information transferred over the Internet or wireless network can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

We will not be transferring your personal information outside of the European Economic Area.

This website does not use cookies. Cookies are a means of storing information about you and your preferences in the hard drive of the computer you are using.  We do store some temporary session information on your computer and this does not contain personal data.

Principle 5 - Minors
The nusonline website is not intended for children and we ask that no one under the age of twelve submits personal information to us or uses the site without supervision of a parent or guardian.

Registered nusonline users under the age of eighteen will only receive limited direct marketing communications.

Questions
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, the use of your personal information or if you wish to request us to stop using your information in any way, please write to:

The Data Protection Officer

Changes to this policy
If we decide to change this privacy policy, we would post such changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it and under what circumstances we disclose it.
