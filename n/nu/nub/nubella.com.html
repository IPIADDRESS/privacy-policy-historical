Privacy Policy


Nubella, LLC. Privacy Statement


Nubella, LLC. ('Nubella', 'The N Booklet', 'us', 'we' or 'our') is committed to protecting your privacy. This Nubella Privacy Statement applies to all of Nubella's offerings, ranging from our Internet destination to our offline booklet products. This Privacy Statement is effective upon acceptance in registration for new registering users, and is otherwise effective on March 30, 2004 for all users. If you have additional questions, you may send email to info@Nubella.com or call (972) 735-0828.

Overview

Nubella provides interested consumers with the opportunity to receive Nubella's staple product, the N Booklet. Each booklet measures roughly 7" x 7" in size, and contains a variety of content, recipes, coupons and advertisements with a primary focus on health, wellness, nutrition, and beauty. Booklets are mailed directly to interested consumer no more than once per month, though all consumers may not receive a booklet each and every issue. To receive a booklet, a consumer must �opt' to receive the N Booklet through one of many of our partners (our "Partners").

During the opt-in process, consumers are asked to provide Nubella with enough information that allows Nubella to successfully mail a booklet via the United States Postal Service. Each opt-in consumer has the opportunity to provide Nubella with additional information pertaining to that consumers individual needs, taste, opinions, preferences, etc.. Such information is used by Nubella to improve the existing product offerings, to determine the most appropriate product to be mailed to such consumers, and/or for other reason relevant to Nubella's business.

Collection of Your Information

The primary purpose for collecting information through the opt-in procedure and survey taking experience is so that Nubella is better able to provide each booklet recipient with product more relevant to each consumer's overall preferences. Although Nubella is not able to fully cater to each consumer's preferences 100% of the time, Nubella take every reasonable effort to accommodate as many of our booklet recipient's needs as possible. We will not collect personally identifiable information from you, other than what you supply to us on a voluntary basis. Nubella will not willfully disclose your personal information to any third party without first receiving your permission.

We will always explain the extent of its use at the time you are asked to provide personal information. If you do not want this information to be collected or used by us for these purposes, you can simply "opt out." Under certain circumstances, opting out may prevent your participation in activities for which personal information is needed, as in accessing booklet related exclusive content.


Tracking and Use of Cookies

As you surf around Nubella websites , it is possible that several cookies* will be set on your computer. We use the information from cookies to deliver personalized content, remember browser preferences, ensure accurate survey feedback and improve our visitors' experiences on the site. No personal information is ever stored in out cookies, even if you have entered your name or e-mail address on other parts of our site. Nubella does not sell information collected by cookies nor use the information for commerce-related purposes. In addition, We will not filter content based on your preferences without permission. Nubella also employs an invisible gif image to count the number of visitors to our site. The intent here is to purely track the number of page loads we get to optimize our server capacity. We do not track any individual data and behaviour for any purpose than to provide you a better and easier to use website.

*What's a cookie? A cookie is information a Web site puts on your computer's hard drive so that the site can remember your preferences or which pages you visited on that site.

Third Party Advertising
We use MaxOnline and other third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please click here.

Third Party Cookies
In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser.


Acceptance of Nubella's Privacy Policy Terms and Conditions

By using this site, you signify your agreement to the terms and conditions of this Nubella Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to these terms and conditions, please do not use the site. We reserve the right, at our sole discretion, to change, modify, add, or remove portions of this policy at any time. Please check this page periodically for any changes. Your continued use of Nubella's websites following the posting of any changes to these terms shall mean that you have accepted those changes. If you have any questions or concerns, please e-mail us at info@nubella.net.
