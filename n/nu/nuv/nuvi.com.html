Privacy Policy

September 01, 2014

This Privacy Policy describes the privacy practices of NUVI LLC (“NUVI”)  in relation to the use its websites, applications, products, or services (together referred to as “Service”) .

All defined terms in the Privacy Policy shall have the same meaning as defined in the Definitions.

1. Definitions

A. "Non-Personal Information" is information that is in no way personally identifiable and is obtained automatically through your use of Service with a web browser.

B. "Personally Identifiable Information" is nonpublic information that is personally identifiable and obtained in connection with providing Service to you.

2. Information Collected

NUVI collects information through the use of commonly-used information-gathering tools such as email, telephone, social media interactions, cookies, web beacons, browser data, ip address, mobile devices, third parties, and the actions you take through the use of Service.

When expressing interest in obtaining additional information about products or services offered by NUVI,  the information necessary to contact you such as company name, company email, personal email, address, phone number, and other necessary information will be required.

When purchasing products or services, NUVI will require financial billing information, such as billing name and address, and credit card number.

3. Use of Personally Identifiable Information

NUVI uses and/or intends to use Personal Information to:

  • Operate and improve its Service;

  • Provide Users with the Service and other products and services that a User may request or that a User has expressed interest in;

  • Facilitate subscription processing, reviews and analysis;

  • Evaluate User interest and needs in order to improve the Service and make available other offers, products or services;

  • Evaluate the types of offers, products or services we make available to Users and potential Users;

  • Track use of the Service;

  • Provide customer support;

  • Communicate and provide additional information that may be of interest to Users through email or other means, such as special offers, announcements, and marketing materials;

  • Send you reminders, technical notices, updates, security alerts and support and administrative messages, service bulletins, or marketing;

  • Provide advertisements to you through email messages, and sell you additional services, including services provided by third parties.

  • Manage our everyday business needs such as Site administration, forum management, fulfillment, analytics, fraud prevention, enforcement of Our corporate reporting obligations, Terms of Use or to comply with the law.

4. Data Sharing

NUVI may share data about its customers and visitors with contracted service providers so that these service providers can provide services on our behalf. Without limiting the foregoing, NUVI may also share data about NUVI customers and visitors with service providers to ensure the quality of information provided, and with third-party social networking and media web sites for marketing and advertising on those web sites.

5. Exemptions from Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy does not apply to any information you may send to NUVI by email, fax, or instant messaging programs as these are not recognized as secure forms of communication. As such, we request that you do not send any information, which you consider private, to us by email, fax, or instant message. Furthermore, Our Privacy Policy does not apply to any information you post from our Services to other websites (i.e. Twitter, Facebook, etc.), due to the public nature of such postings.

6. Security Policy

NUVI utilizes various information security measures to protect your personal data. This information is kept confidential unless you have given someone else your security credentials. If you believe your security credentials have been compromised and you have trouble changing them, please contact our support department support@nuvi.com.

11. CAN-SPAM Compliance Notice

NUVI and its affiliates fully comply with the federal CAN-SPAM Act. you can always opt out of receipt of further email correspondence from us.

12. Changes to this Privacy Policy

NUVI reserves the right to change this Privacy Statement and will provide notification of the material changes to this Privacy Statement through the NUVI’s web site.

13. Questions About This Privacy Policy

Should you have a privacy question please email our customer service department support@nuvi.com)
