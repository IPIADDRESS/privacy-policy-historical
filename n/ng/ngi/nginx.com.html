    Nginx Inc. Privacy Policy

    Nginx Inc. Privacy Policy

    Nginx, Inc. understands the importance of protecting your information. We recognize that when you choose to provide us with information about yourself, you trust us to use it in a responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, store, and use the information you provide. As our services may change or expand, this policy may change; in the event of a change you will be notified promptly.

    What information do we gather?

    When you visit our website and access information you remain anonymous. However, when you engage in certain activities on this site, such as requesting product information, downloading software, or entering contests, Nginx, Inc. may ask you to provide certain information about yourself such as your name, mailing address, email address, phone number and other personally identifying information.

    How do we use the information we gather?

    Personal information collected is used to provide you with information that you request or to record and support your participation in the activities you select. Any personal information we request is only used to assist in providing the highest level of customer care, including regular newsletters. You may click the “unsubscribe” link found in the bottom of email if you wish to unsubscribe from such newsletters.

    Do we share your personal information with third parties, such as service providers or consultants?

    All information you provide Nginx, Inc. is kept confidential and never rented or sold. Information you submit may be shared with our distribution and reseller partners. However, if you would prefer that your information not be shared with third parties, you may “opt out” of future sharing by emailing nginx-inquiries@nginx.com. Please note, that it may take up to 10 days for your request to be processed and completed.

    How do we use cookies?

    Nginx, Inc. uses cookies to gather information on website usage. This information is solely gathered to improve the content, usability and functionality of the site. This helps us better understand our customers and improve their experience with us. Cookies are also used in the form of an advertisement on the Google search results page, or at a site in the Google Display Network. Such cookies are used to serve ads based on past visits to the Nginx, Inc. website. You can set preferences for how Google advertises to you using the Google Ad Preferences page, and if you want to you can opt out of interest-based advertising entirely by cookie settings or permanently using a browser plugin.

    Can you view, correct, or delete your personal information?

    If you would like to update or delete personal information you previously provided please contact nginx-inquiries@nginx.com.

    Is your user data secure?
    We employ reasonable security measures to protect your personal information in storage. Nginx uses advanced firewall and password protection for our databases to ensure your personal information is as secure as possible. Any physical access to the information is strongly limited.

    Contacting us with questions or concerns:
    If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please email nginx-inquiries@nginx.com.
