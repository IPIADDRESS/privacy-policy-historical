Privacy Policy

 

 

 

This privacy notice applies to personal information collected via the Fonterra Nutrition Centre on www.fonterra.com.

We do not collect personal information on this site unless you volunteer it.

We only use personal information that you provide to us through this site for the purposes you supplied it, for directly related purposes, or for other reasons permitted under the Privacy Act 1993.

We do not share personal information volunteered to us through this site with others unless this is necessary for the purpose for which it is provided.

You may seek access to personal information held about you through the ‘contact us’ form on www.fonterra.com in accordance with your rights under the Privacy Act 1993. You have the right to ask to have information corrected or deleted. Information provided will be held by Fonterra Co-operative Group Limited.
