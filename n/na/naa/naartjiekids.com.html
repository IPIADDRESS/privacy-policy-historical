Naartjie.com Privacy Policy

Naartjie values you as a customer and respects your privacy. We do not sell or rent the information you provide to us to third parties. However, we do contract with service providers to assist us in maintaining and managing our databases and to communicate with those who are interested in our company. We do not authorize any of those third parties to make any other use of your information. We also may use aggregate, non-personally identifiable information about you to enhance our site. Additionally, we may disclose and maintain user information in special cases, including:

  • In the event that some or all of our business assets are sold or transferred, we would transfer your information to the buyer or transferee where the above policies would apply;
  • Where we believe in good faith that the law requires disclosure of such information in response to legal process and law enforcement rights, and as necessary to protect the property, interests and rights of Naartjie, its affiliates, and others.

Except as described above, we do not share any information we collect online to any unaffiliated third party.

Cookies

Naartjie.com has limited use of cookies on this Web site. A cookie is a small data file that Web sites often store on your computer's hard drive when you visit their sites. A cookie may contain information (such as a unique user ID) that is used to track the pages of the sites you've visited. We use cookies for the limited purpose of determining whether you have visited a particular page before, so that you will not have to view introductory information that is played for first-time visitors. We do not store personally identifiable information in your cookie.
