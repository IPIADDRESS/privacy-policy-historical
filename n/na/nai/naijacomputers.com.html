We respect your privacy and we are committed to safeguarding your privacy while online at this site. The following discloses how we gather and disseminate information for this website, Business Computers.

RSS Feeds and Email Updates

If a user wishes to subscribe to my Business Computer RSS Feeds or Business Computers Email Updates (powered by Google – Feedburner), we ask for contact information such as name and email address. We hate Spam as much as you do.

Log Files and Stats

Like most blogging platforms we use log files, in this case Statcounter. Statcounter stores information such as internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring, exit and visited pages, platform used, date/time stamp, track user’s movement in the whole, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses etc. are not linked to personally identifiable information. The data gathered are meant to help the webmaster optimize the website.

Cookies

What is a cookie? A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user’s computer tied to information about the user. This blog doesn’t use cookies. However, some of our business partners use cookies on this site (for example – advertisers).We can’t access or control these cookies once the advertisers have set them.

Links

This website contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of these other sites. We suggest our users should be aware of this when they leave this blog and advise that they read the privacy statements of each and every site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this Blog.

We use outside ad companies to display ads on this blog. These ads may contain cookies and are collected by the advertising companies and we do not have access to this information. We work with the following advertising companies: Google Adsense, Kontera etc.
