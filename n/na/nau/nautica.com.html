Data about the activity of visitors to the Nautica Web Site (the "Site") (such as what pages are accessed) is automatically provided to Nautica when you sign onto the Site. Such information is used by Nautica Enterprises, Inc. ("Nautica") for internal review in an aggregate and non-personally-identifiable form. Information which visitors volunteer (such as survey or registration information) is automatically transmitted to Nautica upon the visitor's submission of such information.

E-mail addresses and other personally identifying data about visitors is known to Nautica only when it is voluntarily submitted or posted to us. Nautica.com uses personally identifying data provided by visitors to customize the merchandise offered, to notify users about updates to and activities on the Site, to contact users for information verification purposes and to tell users about carefully screened services or products which we feel may be of interest to you.

In addition, we sometimes use E-mail addresses and other personally identifying information to contact visitors who communicate with Nautica. For example, we direct E-mail to visitors who provide us with their E-mail addresses for specific purposes such as responding to an inquiry.

When you voluntarily submit your E-mail address to the Site, we offer you the option of indicating that you do not wish to receive regular communications from Nautica by E-mail which Nautica thinks may be of interest to you.
