What we provide...

Nakivo provides Small Business with a software-only disaster recovery and business continuity solution that provides backup and replication of virtual machines, applications and business services.

Simple and Fast VM Replications

Why just backup VMs when you can also replicate your VMs for much faster recovery time?  Replicate even your largest VMs with our VM transporter technology. Nakivo Backup & Replication can transport VMs, even 200 GB VMs, reliably and quickly.

Painless and Reliable VM Backups

If you prefer just backup, Nakivo Backup & Replication provides VMware backup from SAN or LAN and can transport VM backups over WAN links fast and reliably. Even high latency, low bandwidth WAN links are not a problem for Nakivo Backup & Replication. With built-in retry technology, Nakivo Backup & Replication will get the job done reliably.

 

Nakivo Differentiators

Performance

- Leading VM backup performance over WAN to storage clouds including Amazon.
- Near continuous protection, as low as one minute, VM backup with zero agents.
- Deduplication to reduce storage space requirements of VM backups by up to 30 to 1.
- Often faster backup and replication speeds versus other VM Backup & Replication solutions.

Ease of Use

- Can be operated locally by a non-IT staff or remotely by a systems integrator or service provider via the browser based interface.
- Installs in just 60 seconds, delivers a web UI for anywhere anytime access to jobs and groups, provides dashboards full of informative widgets.
- Automatically protects new VMs, and does not require reading the manuals.

Leading Functionality

- Live Windows based applications including Oracle, Exchange, Active Directory and MS SQL with Volume Shadow copy Service (VSS).
- Up to 99 recent recovery points enabling fast recovery from virus attacks and accidentally deleted files, with user defined recovery points
- Automatic retries of failed backup or replication jobs, to better utilize faulty WAN links.
- Job scheduling and VM prioritization, to mitigate possible human errors
- Encryption for more secure data transfers to remote recovery sites.
