Our site uses forms for customers to request information about our products and services. We collect visitor's contact information. We do not disclose any information we receive from our Web site users to third parties, except as it may apply to our partners (see Partners section below).

Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us at the point where we request information about the visitor.
