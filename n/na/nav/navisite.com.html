Privacy Policy

 

This Privacy Policy applies to Navisite.com, Navisite.co.uk, and navicloud.navisite.com owned and operated by Navisite, LLC ("Navisite", "We", "Us".) This Privacy Policy describes how Navisite collects and uses the personal information you provide. It also describes the choices available to you regarding the use of, your access to, and how to update and correct your personal information. The use of information collected through our services shall be limited to the purpose of providing the services for which a client has engaged Navisite to perform.
 
If you have questions or complaints regarding this Privacy Policy or Navisite’s practices, please contact us at webinfo@navisite.com. If you have an unresolved privacy or data use concern that we have not addressed satisfactorily, please contact our U.S.-based third party dispute resolution provider (free of charge) at https://feedback-form.truste.com/watchdog/request.
 

Data Collection through Navisite’s Websites

When using the referenced websites and registering for a newsletter or requesting more information, you often must first complete the registration form. During registration you are required to give contact information (such as first and last name, email address and company name). We use this information to contact you about the services offered by Navisite in which you have expressed interest.
 
You have the option to provide demographic information (such as number of employees, etc.) to us; we encourage you to submit this information so we can provide you a more personalized experience on our websites and in any responsive materials.
 
Navisite is the sole owner of the information collected on the websites. Navisite collects personal information from our users at several different points on our websites.
 
Navisite does not collect personal information about individuals except when such individuals specifically provide such information to us on a voluntary basis. Personal information in this context is identifying information about an individual such as a name, title, company name, address, email address and phone number. Information is examined and edited, as necessary, for accuracy. Additional or missing public information – such as company location, industry, etc. – may be appended with the aid of third-party sources for Sales and Marketing purposes.
 
Navisite may also collect IP addresses and/or Domain Names (where applicable) of visitors to its websites, the email addresses of those who communicate with us via email and information volunteered by the user, such as survey information and/or website registrations. Navisite may use IP addresses and or Domain Names to help diagnose problems and to administer the websites. The information Navisite collects is used to improve the content of its websites, customize the content and/or layout of the websites’ pages for each individual user, to notify users about updates to the website(s), to respond to requests for information, send newsletters, assess applications such as partner and job applications and to contact users for marketing purposes. The information gathered may be shared with trusted vendors that partner with Navisite to deliver technology and support services in which you express interest.
 

Data Sharing

The information Navisite collects is used to improve the content of its websites, customize the content and/or layout of the websites’ pages for each individual user, to notify users about updates to the websites and to contact users for marketing purposes. The information gathered may be shared with third–party service providers for commercial purposes for services in which you express interest. If you do not want us to share your personal information with these companies, please contact us at webinfo@navisite.com. Navisite provides your information to trusted vendors who work on behalf of or with Navisite and under confidentiality agreements to provide services such as help desk management and archiving services. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information.
 
Our website offers publicly accessible blogs and testimonials. You should be aware that any information you provide in these areas may be read, collected, and used by others who access them. To request removal of your personal information from our blog or community forum, contact us at webinfo@navisite.com. In some cases, we may not be able to remove your personal information, in which case we will let you know if we are unable to do so and why.
 
Navisite shares your personal information in order to investigate, prevent or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to Navisite's property or the physical safety of any person, violations of Navisite’s terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.
 
Navisite also shares your personal information in the event that Navisite, or any affiliated entity, or substantially all of its assets related to a particular geographical region, are acquired by one or more third parties as a result of an acquisition, merger, sale, reorganization, consolidation or liquidation, user information may be one of the transferred assets.
 
Aggregate data and information that otherwise is not personally identifiable to you may be disclosed to others.
 

Information Related to Personal Data Collected Through Cloud and Messaging Services

The use of information collected through our service shall be limited to the purpose of providing the service for which the Client has engaged Navisite.
 
Navisite may, in certain circumstances, have personal information of its clients’ clients transmitted into its data centers and has no direct relationship with the personal data of individuals of its clients, and in most circumstances, no access or viewing of any individuals’ data residing on its clients’ servers that is being processed in its data centers. If you are a client of one of our clients and would no longer like to be contacted by one of our clients that use our service, please contact the client that you interact with directly. We may transfer personal information to companies that help us provide our service. Transfers to subsequent third parties are covered by the service agreements with our clients.
 
Any personal information that is transmitted in the provision of services will only be processed or retained as long as needed to provide services to our clients. Further, we will retain and use this personal information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and to enforce and abide by our contractual agreements. An individual who seeks access, or who seeks to correct, amend, or delete inaccurate data should direct his query to the Navisite’s client (the data controller). If a Navisite client requests Navisite to remove the data, we will respond to their request within 30 days.
 

Microsite

Access to certain of Navisite's web pages are accessible only by email invitation containing a link to a private site created specifically for the intended user (the "Microsite"). Information that a user provides to us within the Microsite may be used for permission purposes, tracking information on how often users access their Microsite and which documents they view to give Navisite feedback on the effectiveness of its content.
 

Links to Third-Party Websites

Navisite websites contains links to other sites owned and maintained by companies other than Navisite. Those companies’ privacy practices may differ from Navisite’s. If you submit personal information to any of those sites, your information is governed by their privacy policies. Navisite is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites. Users should check the applicable privacy policy of such linked websites.
 
Our website includes Social Media Features, such as the Facebook Like button. These Features may collect your IP address, which page you are visiting on our website, and may set a cookie to enable the Feature to function properly. Social Media Features and Widgets are either hosted by a third party or hosted directly on our website. Your interactions with these Features are governed by the privacy policy of the company providing it.
 

Use of Tracking Technologies

We, and our partners, affiliates, or analytics and online service providers, use technologies such as cookies, beacons, tags and scripts. These technologies are used in analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users’ movements around the site and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. We may receive reports based on the use of these technologies by these companies on an individual as well as aggregated basis.
 
We and our third party partners use Local Storage (HTML5) to provide certain features on our website, to display advertising based on your web browsing activity, and/or to store content information and preferences. Various browsers may offer their own management tools for removing HTML5.
 
Users should also be aware that non-personal information and data may be automatically collected through the standard operation of the websites web server or through the use of 'cookies.' "Cookies" are used within the websites to track user traffic patterns. We do this in order to determine the usefulness of the information on our websites to our users and to see how effective our navigational structure is in helping users reach that information.
 
Navisite does not correlate this information with data about individual users, with the exception of users of our Microsite. Navisite may provide aggregated cookie data to third parties for analysis purposes. Navisite is not responsible for any actions or policies of such third parties.
 
If you prefer not to receive cookies on Navisite’s websites, you can set your browser to warn you before accepting cookies and refuse the cookie when your browser alerts you to its presence. You can also refuse all cookies by turning them off in your browser. You do not need to have cookies turned on to use any area of Navisite’s websites.
 
Some of our business partners (e.g., advertisers) use cookies on our websites to gather information about your activities on this website and on other sites to provide you advertising based on your browsing activities and interests. If you wish not to have this information used for the purpose of serving you interest-based ads, you may opt out by clicking here. If you are located in the European Union, click here. Please note that this does not opt you out of being served ads; you will continue to receive generic ads.
 
We gather certain information automatically and store it in log files. This information may include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and/or clickstream data. We do not link this automatically collected data to other information we collect about you.

If your personal information changes; if you want to provide additional instruction regarding the use of your information; or if you no longer desire our service; you may correct, update, delete or deactivate use of your information by emailing our Marketing Department at webinfo@navisite.com or by contacting us by telephone or postal mail at the contact information listed below. Security

Navisite uses reasonable efforts to treat all consumer information, not just the sensitive and private information mentioned above, in a confidential manner. The organization is driven by policies and controls where data and records are classified and processed appropriately. For example, websites, where technically feasible, enforce the encryption sensitive data in transit using technologies such as secure socket layer (SSL). For managed messaging services, object layer encryption and transport layer encryption (TLS) are used to secure messages. Although Navisite has a base layer of security for all clients, each environment has addition controls that are sized and positioned based on individual client requirements. Navisite observes the core security concept, CIA Triad, of protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data. The policies, procedures, guidelines, baselines, and standards employed by Navisite in conjunction with the client’s team follow industry best practices, where feasible. As a result, Navisite and subsequently clients experience a heightened level of security. If you have any questions about security on our websites, you can contact webinfo@navisite.com.
 

Notification of Changes

Please check our Privacy Policy periodically as Navisite reserves the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes to Navisite's Privacy Policy will be posted to this website. If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you here, by email, or by means of a notice on our homepage prior to the change becoming effective.
 
If, however, we are going to use users' personal information in a manner different from that stated at the time of collection we will notify users by email or by posting a prominent notice on our websites 30 days prior to implementing the change.
 

Miscellaneous

Though we make every effort to preserve user privacy, we may need to disclose personal information when required by law wherein we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a current judicial proceeding, a court order or legal process served on Navisite or its affiliates. This Privacy Policy and the statements outlined herein are not intended to and do not create any contractual or other legal rights in or on behalf of any party.
