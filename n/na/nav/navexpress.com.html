Privacy policy

The privacy policy below shows the attempts of NavExpress to preserve the privacy of all users.

1. Not to use personally identifiable information

We will not collect, rent, sell or release personally identifiable information without your permission.

2. Users control over their information

You can manage, update and control the information you provide to us.

3. Asking questions relating to the privacy policy.

You have the right to ask questions relating to the privacy policy.

4. Collection of information

We only collect your information when you register to our services. Which includes email, chat, discussion forums and the NavExpress newsletters.

5. Commercial usage

We will NOT share your personal information to other companies unless you have given us an authorization to do so.

6. Disclosure of identity

We will only disclose identities if a goverment agency or a law enforcement agency requests information. If we rceive a request by another type of party, we will contact the user of the identity that a request had been made and after negotiations decide whether it is necessary to disclose the information in regards to the circumstances.

7. Security

All information received would be password protected within the sites databases.
