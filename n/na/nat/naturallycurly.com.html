Home / Privacy Policy

NaturallyCurly.com and its advertisers and affiliates are deeply committed to protecting the privacy of Internet users. NaturallyCurly.com only collects personally identifiable information about you such as name, address, phone number, or e-mail address, when you register to become a member of the site or to shop at CurlMart. This information is used only to allow our users to receive information and reviews from NaturallyCurly.com that are relevant to you, or to ship to you products that you have ordered.

We will never share, sell, or rent individual personal information with anyone without your advance permission or unless ordered by a court of law. We will not surrender your private information even in response to a subpoena without first notifying you and allowing you time to respond.
