Web Site Privacy Statement
This privacy statement discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for www.nten.org. This website is managed by The Technology Project, as a contribution to the emerging NTEN movement. Any personal identity and contact information that you submit on this site will be treated confidentially and will not be shared with organizations not affiliated with the NTEN initiative.

The personal contact information that you submit will be used to stay in touch with you. You may receive email, phone calls, or mailings to get your feedback on this initiative.

We won't disclose any information related to you except as may be required by law.
