National Tire & Battery Privacy Policy

 

At National Tire & Battery and ntb.com, the protection of your privacy is critical to us and we understand that it is vital to the continued trust you place in our company. We do not sell, rent or relinquish customer names, addresses or customer identifiable information of any kind to anyone. Any personal data received will be used only for programs provided to you; programs which are sponsored by National Tire & Battery or ntb.com. We will not use any customer identifiable information for any other purpose. If you are just visiting our website, your domain or e-mail address is not collected

If you communicate with us by e-mail or opt-in to any promotional program that we may offer, we collect your e-mail address and any other information that you elect to provide. This information is used to make our website better, to respond to you promptly, and to inform you about products, services, offers or upcoming promotions that may interest you. Of course, you will always have the option to opt-out at any time. Any information that we collect will not be shared or sold to any other company, group or individual. That's our pledge to you!

We fully support the guidelines for consumer privacy and ethical business practices as set forth by the Direct Marketing Association and the Better Business Bureau Online, both leaders in the establishment of standards that protect the consumers right to privacy.
