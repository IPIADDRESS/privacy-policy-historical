Privacy Statement

At NBAD, we are committed to safeguarding all your personal privacy and information.

We collect, retain and use information about you, only where we reasonably believe that it will help administer our business or provide products, services and other opportunities to you.

Only those employees with a business reason to know personally identifiable information about you will have access to it.

We do not reveal specific information about your accounts or other personally identifiable data to parties unless:
1. You request or authorize it.
2. The information is provided to help complete a transaction initiated by you.
3. The disclosure otherwise is lawfully permitted or required.

These privacy principles apply to individuals and group of individuals. We reserve the rights to change them at any time.
