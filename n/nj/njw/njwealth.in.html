Privacy Policy

NJ IndiaInvest Pvt. Ltd. ("NJ") is strongly committed to protecting the privacy of its customers/users/service hirers and to keeping the personal and financial information secure. We herewith disclose our privacy practices and encourage you to read our privacy statements to understand our policy.

NJ may make use your personally identifiable information to operate the sites and services and to inform you of new features, services, and products from NJ IndiaInvest and its affiliates. At any time you may also stop the delivery of informational or promotional mail from NJ by following the instructions on the mail. Protection of each clients’/investors’ personal / financial information is a foundation of customer trust and forms a part of NJ’s sound business principles and practice. NJ restricts access to personal/financial information to those who require it to develop, support, offer and deliver products and services to you.

NJ has taken all necessary and reasonable measures to protect the confidentiality of the customer information and its transmission through the World Wide Web / internet. However, NJ or any of its associates/employees/partners cannot be held liable for disclosure of the confidential information when in accordance with this Privacy Commitment or in terms of the agreements. In the course of using this website or availing the products and services vide the online application forms, inquiries, feed-backs, subscriptions, and questionnaires, NJ and its Affiliates may become privy to the personal information of its customers, including information that is of a confidential nature.

NJ may disclose personally identifiable information if required to do so by law or in good-faith belief that such action is necessary to (i) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on the site or its owners; (ii) protect and defend the rights or property of the site-owners, the site or the users of NJ and (iii) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users NJ the site, its owners, or the public.

The njwealth.in and its associate/group sites contain links to other websites. NJ is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites.

Other Sources:

NJ may collect public and non-public information/details (personal, financial, etc.,) about you from any of the following sources for its lawful business / operational purposes:

  • You or your authorized agents through application form (information/details like name, address, birth date, income, etc.)

  • Risk profile, investment selection, beneficiary information etc.,
  • Any other documentation supporting the above information/details
