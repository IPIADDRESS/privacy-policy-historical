Information held and/or collected

Visit by GES collects your information (‘Personal Information’) if you visit the Visit by GES web site and wish to register, purchase products, obtain information regarding our products or services, request other information or otherwise communicate with us. Visit by GES may also hold your Personal Information having received it from a third party or from you by means other than the website. For example, where your Personal Information is passed to us by an event organiser in order that we may offer our products and services in connection with that event to you. ‘Personal Information’ means any information relating to a natural person who can be identified, directly or indirectly, from that information. The Personal Information we hold and / or collect about you can include your name, business address, telephone number, e-mail address, what web sites visitors to the Visit by GES web site come from, which pages are visited at the Visit by GES web site, and information about online activities that are directly linked to them. New Personal Information may be merged with other Personal Information Visit by GES already holds.

Use of personal information

Visit by GES will collect and use your Personal Information to render services to you, respond to your enquiries, and to market and sell to you products and services offered by Visit by GES or its associated companies. In addition, Visit by GES may use Personal Information to conduct other legitimate business activities, such as protecting the security of the GES web site and of Visit by GES's networks, preventing fraud, complying with law and complying with a legally permitted inquiry by a government agency or regulator.

If necessary for these purposes, Visit by GES may transfer your Personal Information to third parties and these third parties may use your Personal Information for such purposes. These third parties may include GES's associated companies, advisors, companies providing products and services to Visit by GES and, to the extent required by law, regulatory authorities.

Visit by GES may also transfer your Personal Information to companies that have entered into a contract with Visit by GES to offer or endorse Visit by GES' products or services.

If you purchase products or services online via the Visit by GES web site, Visit Connect website or ordering platform, your Personal Information will be used to complete any transactions you wish to enter into, including credit card processing and credit checks.

If all or any part of the Visit by GES business is sold to a third party, we may transfer Personal Information to the third party buyer as part of the assets of the business.

Your control and “opt out” choices

You may request, at any time, that the Personal Information you provide to GES via the Visit by GES web site is not used for direct marketing purposes by Visit by GES or transferred by Visit by GES to third parties for marketing purposes by contacting us.

GES' data security

To prevent unauthorized access to your Personal Information, we have put in place physical, electronic, and managerial procedures. Use of ‘cookies’

When you visit the GES web site and enter your details on to the site, we will place a ‘cookie’ on your computer, which helps us identify you more quickly when you return. Cookies are small pieces of information stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive and are used in website system administration to keep track of movement of an individual from one screen to another. We will use cookies alone or in conjunction with other devices, to understand how you arrived at our web site, to identify your computer when using our website in the future and to identify you for future marketing. This information may also be used by our staff to detect and resolve web site problems.

It is usually possible for you to change your browser settings should you wish to prevent acceptance of a cookie.

Links
