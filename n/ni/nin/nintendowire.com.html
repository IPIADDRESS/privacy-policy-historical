Nintendo Wire follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. All hosting companies do this, as well as record analytics. The purpose of the information is for analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users’ movement on the website, and gathering demographic information.

The information is used to optimize users’ experiences by customizing our web page content based on visitors’ browser type and/or other information.
