NinjaCat’ Use of Cookies and Web Beacons

Cookies

NinjaCat uses cookies (small text files that NinjaCat stores locally on your computer) on our Client’s websites for one or more of the following purposes: to help identify unique visitors and/or devices; assess usage patterns and perform traffic analysis; identify visitor preferences; conduct A/B testing; diagnose problems with our servers, and otherwise administer the NinjaCat Service. NinjaCat does not use methods such as browser cache, Flash cookies, or eTags, for acquiring or storing information about end users web browsing activity on Client websites or otherwise. 

Depending on how our Clients use NinjaCat’ Services, information contained in NinjaCat cookies placed on the computers of end users may be linked to Personally Identifiable Information in NinjaCat’ database. This allows our Clients to use the NinjaCat Service to better analyze and measure their users’ interactions with their website and to organize activities by the same user on their website across time. 

If you do not wish to have NinjaCat place and use cookies on your computer, you should set your browser preferences to refuse all cookies before accessing NinjaCat or other websites that may use NinjaCat. NinjaCat is not responsible for any failure by you or your browser to accurately implement or communicate your browser preferences or settings. In addition, please be aware that even if you configure your browser settings to reject all cookies, your activity on NinjaCat client websites will still be recorded by our Service, unless you opt out of our Service as set forth below. However, your rejection of all cookies will prevent NinjaCat from determining whether or not someone using your computer has previously visited the website, i.e., whether or not the visitor is a “unique” visitor. 

Please note that if you set your browser to reject cookies, some features of the websites you visit may be unavailable. For more information on how to reject cookies, see your browser's instructions on changing your cookie settings. Additionally, please note that rejecting cookies may also prevent you from opting out of our Service because that involves placing an opt-out cookie in your browser. 

If you do not elect to set your browser preferences to refuse cookies, you consent to have cookies, including cookies used by NinjaCat, placed on your computer when you visit our Client’s websites.

Web Beacons

NinjaCat may also collect certain information using Web Beacons. Web Beacons are electronic images that may be used on NinjaCat’ Website, in NinjaCat’ Service, or in our email communications. They are used, for example, to count website visits or to tell if an email has been opened and acted upon.
