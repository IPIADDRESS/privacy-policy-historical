Privacy Policy

GigaClicks LP. (“GigaClicks”) takes your privacy very seriously. Please read the following to learn more about GigaClicks LP’s Privacy Policy (“Policy”). This Policy details how GigaClicks LP receives, collects and uses information in connection with your visit to its website accessed at www.ninjasurf.net or gigaclicks.net (“Website”) and through Your use of the NinjaSurf software (“Software”).

1. Website Visitors: Collection and Use of Your Information

Information You Provide:
Except as explicitly described below, you can use the Website and access the Software (collectively, “Service”) without providing any personally identifying information such as names, email addresses, and phone numbers (“Personal Information”).

Information We Automatically Collect:
GigaClicks LP does not automatically collect any Personal Information on or through the Website, except as may be provided by you in connection with your voluntary communications with GigaClicks LP. However, when you visit the Website, GigaClicks LP may automatically collect certain information about your computer to facilitate, evaluate and verify your use of the Website. For example, GigaClicks LP may use “cookies”, “web beacons” and similar devices to track your activities as a visitor to the Website. Cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to an individual’s hard drive for record- keeping purposes. A web-beacon is a small transparent gif image that is embedded in an HTML page or email used to track when the page or email has been viewed. Cookies make web-surfing easier by saving preferences and browsing patterns while you are at the Website. Cookies may be used to analyze Website usage, improve content, customize the Website’s content and layout and for other customer service purposes. GigaClicks LP’s use of cookies or similar devices is in no way linked to any Personal Information.

When you visit the Website, GigaClicks LP may also automatically receive and record certain other non- personally identifiable information of the type that servers typically collect on webserver logs from your browser, including your IP address, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), cookie information, and the webpages that you visit. GigaClicks LP collects non-personally identifiable information for general purposes, including but not limited to analyzing trends, administering the Website, tracking user movements, conducting research, and providing anonymous reporting to internal and external clients. GigaClicks LP will not link any Personal Information, including e-mail addresses, with the aggregate data of its users. Please be aware that some non-personally identifiable information such as Uniform Resource Locators (“URL’s”) or Internet Protocol (“IP”) addresses could become Personal Information when combined by third parties with the ISP’s records. GigaClicks LP does NOT do this with your information.

Sharing and Disclosure of Information
GigaClicks LP does not rent, sell, or share any information about you with any third-parties. GigaClicks LP may disclose your information if GigaClicks LP believes such action is necessary to: (a) comply with the law or legal process served on website; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of GigaClicks LP (including the enforcement of our agreements); or (c) act in urgent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of GigaClicks LP services or members of the public.

GigaClicks LP may use certain trusted third party companies and individuals to help it provide, analyze and improve the Website (including but not limited to data storage, maintenance services, database management, web analytics, information processing and improvement of the Website’s features and services). These third parties may have access to your information only for purposes of performing these tasks on behalf of GigaClicks LP and under obligations similar to those in this Policy.

Third party advertisers and websites
If you access another website through the Website and that website is operated by any entity other than GigaClicks LP, that site is referred to as a “Third Party Site”. Unless GigaClicks LP clearly indicates otherwise at the time of collection, GigaClicks LP does not share Personal Information with Third Party Sites. Please be aware however that these Third Party Sites may collect information about you. Please see the Third Party Site’s privacy policy to better understand what information is collected, how it is used and shared, and your choices regarding control. GigaClicks LP has no control over these linked Third Party Sites, each of which has a separate privacy and data collection practice, independent of this Policy. GigaClicks LP does not monitor, control, or endorse the information collection or privacy practices of any Third Party Site. As such, GigaClicks LP is not responsible for, nor have control over, the content or the privacy policies of these Third Party Sites. You must read the privacy policies of each and every website that you visit when linking from the Website. This Policy applies solely to information collected by GigaClicks LP.

2. NinjaSurf: Collection and Use of Your Information

Information GigaClicks LP receives when NinjaSurf runs on your computer.
You do not need to provide any Personal Information in order to download or use the Software.

When NinjaSurf runs on your computer, GigaClicks LP may automatically receive non- personally identifiable information, such as your system’s IP address, when you contact our servers. NinjaSurf itself does not set any cookies.

Information NinjaSurf sends to websites and ISP’s
Like most web browsers, the websites that is automatically visited using the NinjaSurf will receive certain non- personally identifiable information, for example, this information may consist of the type of browser, type of device, and your IP address. These sites may also set their own cookies or other website data on your system. Each website determines its own privacy practices and is not controlled or monitored by GigaClicks LP. If you want to understand how these websites use your information, you must review that particular website’s privacy policy. All stored data will be automatically deleted after NinjaSurf session ends.

Information GigaClicks LP Stores when NinjaSurf runs.
NinjaSurf stores some of your information locally on your system. For example, the NinjaSurf may store basic browsing history such as URL’s of pages is visited by NinjaSurf, text and images from those pages, IP addresses linked from the pages, index of most pages you visit, cookies deposited on your system by and a record of downloads you have made. All stored data will be automatically deleted after NinjaSurf session ends. Also, NinjaSurf will put unique identifier to your computer in order to track further NinjaSurf sessions.
