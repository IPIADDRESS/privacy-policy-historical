NIC.LK Privacy Policy

 

LK Domain Registry (hereafter referred to as NIC.LK) respects your right to personal data protection and privacy. This document (‘Policy’) explains NIC.LKs privacy policy.

This Policy applies to all personal data collected by NIC.LK in rendering NIC.LK’s services such as the registration of .lk domain names. “Personal data” means any information relating to an identifiable individual which is collected and further processed by NIC.LK in order to provide NIC.LK’s services.

 

Collection of Personal Data

NIC.LK collects your personal data for the following purposes:

      i.        Administering registration of .lk domain names;

     ii.        Maintenance and/or operation of a .lk domain name registry;

    iii.        Receiving and dealing with customers’ enquiries and/or complaints by third parties;

    iv.        Obtaining your feedback on our services; and

     v.        General record keeping in the course of NIC.LK’s operations.

Unless stated otherwise, the personal data required is obligatory to be provided by you. Should you fail to provide such obligatory personal data, NIC.LK will be unable to process your request for services and/or assist you in providing the service(s) you requested.

NIC.LK may also use your personal data to inform you of updates from NIC.LK which may include price promotions, new services or products, customer satisfaction surveys, etc.

NIC.LK collects personal data directly from you and/or from NIC.LK’s appointed agents.

 

Security and Retention of Personal Data

NIC.LK implements security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, misuse, disclosure, copying, use, alteration, accidental loss or theft, destruction or damage. Such security measures include technical protection of NIC.LK’s system, and training of NIC.LK staff.

The Security Policy is aimed to protect the confidentiality, integrity and availability of NIC.LK’s critical information, processing facilities and services which includes your personal data.

 

Disclosure of Personal Data

NIC.LK may disclose your personal data to our duly appointed partners that assist NIC.LK to provide you with NIC.LK services such as:

  • .lk domain registration services
  • .lk domain customer care and related activities
  • .lk domain charging and billing purposes
  • .lk domain technical support purposes
  • .lk domain marketing and its related activities (including market research) and/or
  • any other .lk domain services necessary for the general care and management of your registration with NIC.LK.

NIC.LK will ensure that these appointed partners shall only use and process your personal data for the purpose(s) it was collected for by NIC.LK, and adopt appropriate technical and organisational security measures when processing your personal data.

Other than for the above purposes and for such other purposes as may be required by law, NIC.LK will not disclose, sell, trade or rent your personal information to any other third party unless your prior consent has been obtained.

 

Right of Review

NIC.LK reserves the right to review and amend this Policy from time to time.
