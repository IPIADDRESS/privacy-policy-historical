Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

At Nigerian Bulletin, we take your privacy seriously. Our privacy policy describes what personal information we collect and how we use it on our website (www.nigerianbulletin.com)

Routine Collection of Information

All web servers collect basic information about their online visitors. Such information usually includes – but is not limited to – IP addresses, timestamps, browser details and the referring pages. None of the information collected can be used to personally identify the specific identity of the visitor to the site. The information collected is used for routine administration purposes and for site maintenance and lets the webmaster know what pages and information on such pages is are most useful to visitors.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Where applicable, the website uses cookies to store visitor information (information about their preference and history) to offer better service tailored to the needs of the visitor. Our advertising partners and other third party applications may also use cookies, scripts and/or web beacons to track the visitors to our site in order to show advertisements and other useful information. When this happens, such tracking is done by the third parties and as such is subject to their own privacy policies.

Controlling your Private Information

You can alter your browser setting to disable cookies if you are concerned about your privacy. It is however not recommended that you disable cookies for all websites as it may negatively affect your use of some websites. The best thing to do, in these cases, is to disable and enable cookies based on the site you are visiting. Consult your browser help manual for instructions on how you can block cookies and other tracking mechanisms.
