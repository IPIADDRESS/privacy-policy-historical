About The Site

Founded in Ottawa, Canada in 2011 by Aaron Nilsen, the Nilsen Report has grown to be a globally known sports/entertainment website. We specialize in covering local events that have included the Ottawa Bluesfest, Ottawa Comiccon, Ottawa Pop Expo, Escapade Music Festival and concerts at the Canadian Tire Centre in Kanata, Ontario.
