NIB Website Privacy Policy

NIB recognises the importance of protecting your privacy. This website privacy policy ("the Policy") sets out how NIB may collect, use or disclose information about you when you use this website, and your associated rights.

For further information regarding how NIB complies with its obligations under the Privacy Act 1988 to protect your information, please refer to our Privacy Policy.


Collecting Your Information

As the operator of this website, NIB may collect and retain information about your use of this website ("Your Information"). The type and amount of Your Information NIB collects will be determined by how you use this website. If you submit information to this website by completing an application form or submitting an email, NIB may collect information that identifies you personally.

Your Information NIB collects may include your name, email address, business and company name (if applicable), and any other information NIB requires to contact you regarding matters which may be of interest to you. NIB will take reasonable steps to protect Your Information from unauthorised access or use by third parties.

When you visit this website, NIB's Internet Service Provider ("ISP") may record information about your visit for statistical purposes. The information collected may include:

  • your server address;
  • your domain name;
  • the date and time you visited NIB's website;
  • the pages you accessed;
  • the previous sites you visited; and
  • the type of Internet browser you used.

NIB's ISP provides statistical information to NIB on a daily basis.


How NIB uses Your Information

NIB will only use Your Information:

  • for the purposes set out in NIB's Privacy Policy;
  • to communicate with you regarding your use of this website; and
  • to understand how NIB might improve navigation and the content of this website.

If you do not wish to be kept informed of NIB's marketing or promotional activities electronically, please contact NIB at PrivacyOfficer@nib.com.au and we will remove your details from all future marketing correspondence.


Disclosure to others

We will not disclose Your Information to third parties unless we first obtain your consent, or we are required by law to make such a disclosure.


Access and Correction

You have the right to request access to Your Information. To request access to Your Information, you should contact NIB at PrivacyOfficer@nib.com.au.

NIB will take reasonable steps to ensure your Information is accurate, complete and up-to-date. If you believe any of Your Information NIB holds is not accurate, complete, or up-to-date, please contact us and we will review the matter.


Internet security

You acknowledge that the Internet is not a secure environment. If you send NIB any information when you use this website, such information is sent at your own risk.


External website links

This site may contain links to other websites. NIB is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other websites. NIB makes no warranty as to the accuracy or content of these linked websites, or that such links will remain current or be maintained.


Cookies

"Cookies" are a technology that can be used to provide you with tailored information from a website. A cookie is an element of data that a website can send to your browser, which may then store it on your system. This website uses cookies so that we can better serve you when you return to this website again. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it or not.


Reviewing this policy

NIB reserves the right to review and amend this Policy without notice. This Policy was last updated on 3rd June 2002.


Need more information?

For further information regarding this Policy please contact NIB at PrivacyOfficer@nib.com.au.

NIB Privacy Policy

NIB recognises the importance of privacy protection and has implemented measures to comply with its obligations under the National Privacy Principles.

The information you provide NIB as part of your personal dealings with it is defined as “personal information” under the Privacy Act 1988. This personal information may also contain sensitive information regarding your health and your choice of health services.

What personal information does NIB collect?

NIB only collects the personal information it requires to provide you with health and related services, or for the promotion or marketing of its services. NIB may collect this personal information from you directly, or from other NIB companies or health service providers including health insurers, government agencies, private and public hospitals, doctors, and medical specialists.

What happens to your personal information?

NIB may use your personal information it collects to:

• provide you health and related services;

• determine your eligibility to receive an NIB health or related service; and

• promote and market NIB’s current and future health and related services to you.

If NIB uses your personal information for direct marketing purposes, any correspondence sent to you will give you the opportunity to opt out of receiving any further marketing correspondence.

Will your personal information be given to anyone else?

In providing you its health and related services, NIB may be required to disclose your personal information to other NIB companies or health service providers including health insurers, government agencies, private and public hospitals, doctors and medical specialists.

At times NIB may be required to disclose your personal health information to people that are on your membership. It is a condition of each NIB membership that you consent to NIB sharing your personal health information in this way. You can withdraw your consent to NIB making available your personal health information to other people on your membership by advising NIB in writing.

NIB will not disclose your personal information to a third party, other than the types described above, unless:

• you authorise NIB to do so;

• your safety or the safety of others in your community is at risk; or

• NIB is required or permitted by law.

Access and correction

You have a right to access the personal information NIB holds about you. You can access your personal information by contacting NIB or NIB’s Privacy Officer.

NIB will take reasonable steps to ensure your personal information it holds is accurate, complete and up-to-date. If you believe that any personal information NIB holds about you is not accurate, complete or up-to-date, please contact NIB so we can investigate the matter.

NIB reserves the right to withhold your personal information where its disclosure to you is restricted by law, is the subject of legal action, or may compromise the privacy of another person.

Personal information security

NIB takes reasonable steps to ensure the security of your personal information held by it from risks such as loss or unauthorised access, destruction, misuse, modification or disclosure. NIB only permits your personal information to be accessed by its authorised personnel.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

NIB reserves the right to change this Privacy Policy from time to time. This policy was last updated on 14 June 2002.

Need more information?

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or NIB’s handling of your personal information, please contact NIB’s Privacy Officer on 13 14 63 or PrivacyOfficer@nib.com.au.
