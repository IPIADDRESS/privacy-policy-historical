NIB Website Privacy Policy

NIB recognises the importance of protecting your privacy. This website privacy policy (Policy) sets out how NIB may collect, use or disclose information about you when you use this website, and your associated rights.

For further information regarding how NIB complies with its obligations under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) to protect your information, please refer to NIB’s Privacy Policy.


Collecting Your Information

As the operator of this website, NIB may collect and retain information about your use of this website (Your Information). The type and amount of Your Information NIB collects will be determined by how you use this website. For example, if you submit information to this website, by completing an application form, interacting with NIB’s Online Member Services, or submitting an email, NIB may collect information that identifies you personally.

Your Information NIB collects may include your name, email address, business and company name (if applicable), and any other information NIB requires to contact you regarding matters which may be of interest to you. NIB will take reasonable steps to protect Your Information from unauthorised access or use by third parties.

When you visit this website, NIB's Internet Service Provider (ISP) may record information about your visit for statistical purposes. The information collected by the ISP may include:

  • your server address;
  • your domain name;
  • the date and time you visited NIB's website;
  • the pages you accessed;
  • the previous sites you visited;
  • the type of Internet browser you used; and
  • the type of operating platform you used.

The ISP provides statistical information to NIB on a daily basis.


How NIB uses Your Information

NIB will only use Your Information:

  • for the purposes set out in NIB's Privacy Policy;
  • to communicate with you regarding your use of this website; and
  • to understand how NIB might improve services, the navigation and content of this website

If you do not wish to be kept informed of NIB's marketing or promotional activities electronically, please contact NIB’s Privacy Officer at PrivacyOfficer@nib.com.au, and they will remove your details from all future marketing correspondence


Disclosure to others

Unless it required otherwise by law, NIB’s disclosure of Your Information will be limited to the types third parties referred to in the section titled “Will your personal information be given to anyone else?” in NIB’s Privacy Policy, and then such information will only be disclosed on a need to know basis


Access and Correction

You have the right to request access to Your Information that NIB holds about you. To request access to Your Information, you should contact NIB’s Privacy Officer at PrivacyOfficer@nib.com.au.

NIB will take reasonable steps to ensure Your Information is accurate, complete and up-to-date. If you believe any of Your Information NIB holds is not accurate, complete, or up-to-date, please contact NIB’s Privacy Officer at PrivacyOfficer@nib.com.au and they will review the matter.


Internet security

You acknowledge that the Internet is not a secure environment. If you send NIB any information when you use this website, such information is sent at your own risk.


External Website Links

This site may contain links to other websites. NIB is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other websites. NIB makes no warranty as to the accuracy or content of these linked websites, or that such links will remain current or be maintained.


Cookies

"Cookies" are a technology which can be used to provide you with tailored information from a website. A cookie is an element of data which a website can send to your Internet browser, for storage on your system. Most Internet browsers can be set to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it or not.

This website uses cookies so that NIB can better serve you when you return to this website again.


Reviewing this policy

NIB reserves the right to review and amend this Policy without notice. This Policy was last updated in August 2003.


Want more information?

For further information regarding this Policy or any other steps NIB has taken to protect Your Information or privacy, please contact NIB’s Privacy Officer on 13 14 63 or at PrivacyOfficer@nib.com.au.

NIB Privacy Policy

NIB recognises the importance of privacy protection and has implemented measures to comply with its obligations under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) (Act). The information you provide NIB as part of your personal dealings with it is defined as “personal information” under the Act. This personal information may also contain sensitive information regarding your health and your choice of health services.


What personal information does NIB collect?

NIB only collects the personal information it requires to provide you with health and related services, or for the promotion or marketing of its services. NIB may collect this personal information from you directly, from other NIB companies, or health service providers including health insurers, government agencies, private and public hospitals, doctors and medical specialists.

Your personal information will usually be collected by NIB when you request information concerning NIB’s services, apply for membership to NIB, or make a claim against your NIB health insurance policy.


What if you don’t provide NIB with the personal information it requires?

If you can’t or won’t provide NIB with the personal information it reasonably requires, NIB may be unable to provide to you the information or services you wish to receive or use.


What happens to your personal information?

NIB may use your personal information it collects to:

• provide you health and related services;

• determine your eligibility to receive an NIB health or related service; and

• promote and market NIB’s current and future health and related services to you.

If NIB uses your personal information for direct marketing purposes, any correspondence sent to you will give you the opportunity to opt out of receiving any further marketing correspondence.


Will your personal information be given to anyone else?

In providing you its health and related services, NIB may be required to disclose your personal information to other NIB companies or health service providers, including health insurers, government agencies, private and public hospitals, doctors and medical specialists.

At times NIB may also be required to disclose your personal information to other individuals shown on your NIB membership and it is a condition of your NIB membership that you consent to NIB sharing your personal information in this way.

You can withdraw your consent to NIB disclosing your personal information to other individuals on your NIB membership by advising NIB in writing.

NIB will not disclose your personal information to a third party, other than the types described above, unless:

• you authorise NIB to do so;

• your safety or the safety of others in your community is at risk; or

• NIB is required or permitted by law.


Access and Correction

You have a right to access the personal information NIB holds about you. You can access your personal information by contacting NIB's Privacy Officer, whose contact details are shown at the end of this policy.

NIB will take reasonable steps to ensure your personal information it holds is accurate, complete and up-to-date. If you believe that any personal information NIB holds about you is not accurate, complete or up-to-date, please contact NIB's Privacy Officer so they can investigate the matter.

NIB reserves the right to withhold your personal information where its disclosure to you is restricted by law, is the subject of legal action, or may compromise the privacy of another person.


Personal information security

NIB takes reasonable steps to ensure your personal information free from risks such as loss or unauthorised access, destruction, misuse, modification or disclosure. NIB only permits your personal information to be accessed by its authorised personnel.


Changes to this Privacy Policy

NIB reserves the right to change this Privacy Policy from time to time. This Privacy Policy was last updated in August 2003.


Need more information?

For further information regarding this Privacy Policy or any other steps NIB has taken to protect Your Information or privacy, please contact NIB's Privacy Officer on 13 14 63 or at PrivacyOfficer@nib.com.au.
