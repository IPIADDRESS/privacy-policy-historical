Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

When accessing our Website, NowNaija.com will learn certain information about you during your visit. The kind of information we collect and how we will handle it depends on your activity on our website.

If you visit our website to read news on our pages, we collect and store only the following information about you:

1. The name of the domain from which you access the Internet.

2. The country from where you are accessing our website.

3. The date and time you access our site.

4. The Internet address of the website you used to link directly to our site.

We Collect Your Information Only for Statistics, Performance and Security Purposes

The information collected is for statistical purposes. NowNaija.com may use software to create summary statistics, which are used for assessing the number of visitors to the different sections of our site, what information is of most and least interest, determining technical design specifications, and identifying system performance or problem areas.

For website security purposes and to ensure that this service remains available to all users, NowNaija.com uses software to monitor network traffic to identify unauthorized attempts to upload or change information which could cause damage.

NowNaija.com will not obtain personally-identifying information about you when you visit our site, unless you choose to provide such information to us, nor will such information be sold or otherwise transferred to unaffiliated third parties without the approval of the user at the time of collection.

Email

If you identify yourself by sending us an e-mail containing personal information, then the information collected will be solely used to respond to your message.
