Privacy Policy

Not Always Right is dedicated to maintaining visitor privacy, and therefore we generally do not collect email addresses or names in our submissions, except in the case of special giveaways and contests. In such cases, email addresses are collected only to contact winners. Under no circumstances will we reveal or sell individual visitor information unless required to do so by law.

General pageview and hit statistics are collected but we do not log IP addresses except for the purposes of preventing malicious use of the site.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
