Privacy policy

Privacy policy

General Information Collection

The only personal information that Northwell Health collects through its website is the information its users voluntarily provide. The only non-personal information collected from site visitors are IP addresses and cookies. IP address collection is necessary to allow communication between the different computers, which comprise the internet. This information is entered, in aggregate, into a log, for tracking usage on the Northwell Health website. Northwell Health employs strict security measures to safeguard online communications; personal information is encrypted and securely transmitted to our servers.

Limitations on Disclosures

Northwell Health will not release your name, address, telephone number, credit card number or e-mail address to any third party without your consent, except when required by law or court order. If you provide information through a co-branded section of the website (provided by Northwell Health and a third party), you are consenting to sharing your information with that third party and may be subject to that party’s privacy policy.
