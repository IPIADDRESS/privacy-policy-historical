WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are alphanumeric information files which your browser automatically saves to the hard drive of your computer when you visit a website. Having active cookies allows for quick login every time you visit the website. Using cookies does not mean that we receive new personal data about you as a visitor to our website. Data collected using cookies is statistical in nature and indicates a user’s browsing actions and patterns. It does not identify any individual. To remove the cookies at any point from your computer, you can delete them using your browser.

NoBullying.com may also use from time to time a technology that is embedded into web pages or in email that allows the website to track page views. Unlike cookies these are not stored on your hard drive. This technology is often called Clear GIF’s or Web Beacons this technology does not personally identify you.
