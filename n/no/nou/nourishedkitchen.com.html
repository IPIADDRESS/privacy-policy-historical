The Nourished Kitchen does not sell, trade, or otherwise disclose email lists   or other information of commentors, users or Cultures & Exchange participants to  third parties without your permission.

  • Nourished Kitchen Updates: Periodically, Nourished Kitchen may email you to let you know of an upcoming news story, blog post, contest or other event.
  • Promotional Offers: From time to time a sponsor of Nourished Kitchen or other company might provide a special offer to Nourished Kitchen readers, and Nourished Kitchen may keep you up to date or send you an email notifying you of an offer or impending promotion.   Please note that your email address or other contact information will not be passed on directly to sponsors.
