Privacy

TwitterIcon.com respects your privacy on the Internet. As part of our operations, we gather certain types of information about our contacts and web visitors, and we would like to explain the types of information we assemble, what we do with it and how to correct or change your information.

What Information is collected about TwitterIcon.com Contacts?

As part of the process in downloading TwitterIcon.com information and in enrolling in any TwitterIcon.com promotions, you may be asked to provide an e-mail address and other contact information, as appropriate. This information may or may not be necessary, depending upon the action being taken. In some instances TwitterIcon.com may require an e-mail address to complete an action, such as sending you information. In all other instances, an opt-out option will be offered.

Cookies

In addition to information provided by visitors to our web site, TwitterIcon.com monitors incoming and outgoing web traffic data for web administration purposes and uses this data to collect general demographic information about all visitors to our site. This information allows us to ensure that we provide the most desired information to our visitors and allows us to track the performance of our advertising campaigns. TwitterIcon.com uses cookies to store and track information.

A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a Web server and stored on your computer’s hard drive. By modifying your browser preferences, you have the choice to accept all cookies, or be notified when a cookie is set, or to reject all cookies. TwitterIcon.com will access only those cookies that we have generated and never report any personally identifiable information to third parties, unless required to do so by law.

Online Privacy and Children

The privacy of children online is of the utmost importance to us. TwitterIcon.com Solutions’ Web site is a business site and is not directed to, or intended for, children under the age of 16. In the event that you suspect TwitterIcon.com has any information about a visitor under the age of 16, please contact us immediately at privacy@twittericon.com. so all information can be deleted from our records.

Confidentiality

TwitterIcon.com will not sell or release identifiable visitor information to any third party without your permission and will limit disclosure of information to the following: when required to do so in response to a legal process; where reasonably required to do so in order to maintain data security measures; or where reasonably necessary to identify, contact or bring legal action against persons or entities to preserve and/or enforce TwitterIcon.com’s rights.

TwitterIcon.com makes all efforts to protect the confidentiality of all visitors and will never knowingly allow access to this information by any third party. We have made a significant investment in our server, database, backup and firewall technologies to protect information assets.

Information Uses

TwitterIcon.com may use visitor information to deliver promotions and to monitor, analyze and report campaign results internally. This data is in a non-personally identifiable aggregate form designed to analyse general trends and usage patterns.

Opt-Out Provision

TwitterIcon.com will offer an opt-out in all e-mail transmissions and will follow up with timely removal of requested information from our database. This information could include name, title, company, address, phone number, fax number, e-mail address or other information that has been offered.

Terminating, Reviewing and Modifying Visitor Information.

You may choose at any time to terminate, review or modify all information stored by TwitterIcon.com by contacting us at privacy@twittericon.com.

Policy Changes

TwitterIcon.com reserves the right to change this policy at any time. Please check this page periodically for changes. Your continued use of TwitterIcon.com services and website following the posting of changes to these terms will mean that you accept those changes. This statement and the policies outlined herein are not intended to and do not create any contractual or other legal rights in or on behalf of any party.

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, or feel that your privacy has been compromised, please contact us at privacy@twittericon.com.
