Privacy Policy

The information we collect is used to improve the content of our websites and the quality of our services.

Twtapps DOES NOT share with or sell any of its visitors' information to other organizations, except to provide products or services they have requested, when it has their permission or under the following circumstances:

Cookies

A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a website's computers and stored on the client's computer hard drive. Twtapps does NOT use cookies.
