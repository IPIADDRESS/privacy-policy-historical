About

Tweet Tunnel leads you to special features that you wouldn't find on Twitter.

 

  • Message Box - Private Messages for non-followers

     

  • Old Tweets - Shows someone's earlier tweets. Goes back as many as 3200 tweets

     

  • Circle of Friends - Finds out who's interacting with whom on Twitter

     

  • Photo Gallery - Showcase of Twitter members' current photos & background images

     

  • First Follower - Shows followers/following from the beginning

     

  • DM All - Sends Direct Messages to all your followers

     

  • Long Tweets - Tweets without the 140 character limit
