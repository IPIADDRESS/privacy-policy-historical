Cookies

A technology called "cookies" may be used to provide you with tailored information. A cookie is a tiny element of data that a website can send to your browser, which may then be stored on your hard drive so we can recognize you when you return. You may set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, or you may disable the use of cookies altogether. For more information about cookies, please visit http://www.microsoft.com/info/cookies.htm.
