TGI FRIDAYSsm PRIVACY POLICY

At Fridays, we want to make your online and mobile experiences satisfying and safe. The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to provide you with the means to control your privacy and give you a way to voice questions or concerns you may have. This website (“Site”) and mobile application (“mobile application” or “app”) are owned, operated and provided to you by TGI Friday’s, Inc (“Fridays,” “us,” or “we”). By using this Site or app, or signing up for Fridays Rewards™ (formerly known as Give Me More Stripes®) Loyalty Program (“Fridays Rewards”), you acknowledge that you understand and agree to the terms of this privacy policy (“Policy”).

 

WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

  1. General Browsing Information

    2. We collect general, non-personal information about visitors to our Site and users of our app, such as the domain type, browser version, service provider, and device type (for mobile) used to access our Site or app. Like many other commercial websites, our Site may employ a standard technology called a “cookie” to collect information about how our Site is used and to remember visitors. This information also provides us information about the technical stability of our Site, app and Fridays Rewards™ program. For those guests that access our in-store Wi-Fi, we will have access to such guests’ device ID and name, if available.

    When you log in to our mobile application, you will be asked whether you want the mobile application to remember you. If you choose yes, the mobile application will remain logged in to your information. At any time you can choose to log out by choosing the logout button on the application.

     

  2. Personal Information

    3. We only collect personal information from you when you voluntarily submit such information on our Site or when using our app. We use this information for the specific purpose for which it was provided, such as registering for Fridays Rewards, entering a contest, participating in a survey, finding a Fridays™ location, making a reservation, ordering online (where applicable) and other purposes described in this Policy.

     

  3. FRIDAYS REWARDS™

    4. Guests that join Fridays Rewards will be asked to provide their email address and phone number. We require your email address to communicate with you about earned rewards, program benefits, program changes, and all promotions and offers available to members. If you no longer wish to receive these email communications, you will forfeit the ability to earn and use Fridays Rewards credits that you have earned.

    You have the ability to access and edit the personal information that you have provided to Fridays Rewards. To update your information or to cancel your account, please visit www.tgifridays.com/rewards.

    If you join Fridays Rewards via the mobile application or log in to your Fridays Rewards account on the mobile application, you will be asked whether you want the mobile application to remember you. If you choose yes, the mobile application will remain logged in to your account. You may be prompted to log in again to access certain functions on the mobile application, such as requesting rewards or paying for online placed orders. At any time you can choose to log out of your account by choosing the logout button on the application. Guests that use our app to order and pay online will, for security purposes, be automatically logged out after a certain period of non-use.

    We allow our customers to opt in for text messaging from Fridays during the Fridays Rewards enrollment process. Please review the following steps and terms associated with text messaging:

    • How to Opt In: To opt in or subscribe to our service, you can register from our website, mobile application, sign-up tear pad, or text JOIN to 698443 and we’ll guide you through the process from your mobile phone.
    • How to Opt Out: To opt out from our SMS text messages, you can text STOP to 698443 from your mobile phone and we will unsubscribe you from our SMS text messaging service. You will not receive any additional messages until you re-register.
    • How to Get Help or Support: To get help, email us at http://www.tgifridays.com/contact, or call our toll-free number at 1-800-FRIDAYS. From your mobile phone, you may request our contact information at any time by texting HELP to 698443.
    • Pricing: Text message and data rates may apply. Check with your mobile device carrier or provider.

    From time to time, Fridays Rewards™ members may be eligible to participate in certain promotions and giveaways. As part of registering for those giveaways, you may be asked whether you’d like to receive notifications via text message. If you agree to those communications, you may receive text messages regarding that promotion or giveaway. This messaging may also be subject to text message and data rates.

 

HOW WE USE COLLECTED PERSONAL INFORMATION

We use the information we collect from you for the following purposes:

  • to perform the task or transaction that you request;
  • to understand your preferences and history using our Site, mobile application, Fridays Rewards and related services;
  • to design initiatives to meet those preferences;
  • to make it easier and more rewarding for you to use our Site, mobile application and related services by eliminating online hassles; and
  • to generally make your guest experience at our restaurants more satisfying (in the case of voluntary surveys, push notifications, and customized messages).

If you provide contact information to us, such as a postal or e-mail address, we may use this information to contact you with information about us and our special offers. You can opt-out of receiving these communications from us at any time by following instructions in the communication or contacting us using the information below.

We may develop and share summary reports for our advertising partners and agencies that contain non-personal aggregate raw numbers or statistics (which means such information will not tell them who it was that stated a preference or clicked an option).

The use and/or privacy provided by a linked website as to any information you provide in accessing that website is in no way subject to our privacy policy. Any linked website may have its own policy that could differ considerably from our policy, or the linked website may not display any privacy policy at all. From time to time, we may conduct promotions and giveaways on or through the Site and app. As part of registering for those giveaways, you may be asked whether you’d like to receive notifications via email and/or text message to your mobile device. If you agree to those communications, you may receive emails and/or text messages regarding that promotion or giveaway, and text messages may be subject to text message and data rates.

 

A NOTE REGARDING INTERNATIONAL USERS

This Site and app are operated in the United States. If you access the Site or use the app outside of the United States, you understand and agree that all information that you provide and that we collect will be collected, processed, stored and maintained in the United States and protected as noted in this Policy.

A NOTE REGARDING CHILDREN

Like you, we have children of our own and are concerned about their safety when they use the Internet. We want you and your family to be safe while visiting our website. Our Site and app are not designed for or directed to and we do not offer any program participation to children under the age of 13 and will never knowingly request, collect or allow children under this age to provide information about themselves or others on the Site or app. For more information about protecting the privacy and safety of minors online, we recommend you visit http://www.ftc.gov/privacy/privacyinitiatives/childrens.html.

 

A NOTE REGARDING SECURITY

We operate secure data networks protected by industry standard firewall and password protection systems. Our security and privacy policies are periodically reviewed and enhanced as necessary and only authorized individuals have access to the information provided by our customers. Any particularly sensitive information, such as a customer’s credit card number, is encrypted prior to transmission. To the extent that administration of the program requires us to grant our Service Vendors access to customer information, we insist that they implement similar controls.

If you have any questions or concerns with this Policy or Fridays’ privacy practices, or wish to opt-out of receiving promotional messages from us, please contact us at http://www.tgifridays.com/contact.
