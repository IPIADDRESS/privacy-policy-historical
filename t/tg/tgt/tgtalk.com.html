Privacy Policy of TGTalk.com

We take your privacy very seriously! This is the web site of TGTalk.com. We will never share your personal information with anyone, except as lawfully required in connection with lawful investigations associated with allegations of member misconduct or safety concerns.

We can be reached via e-mail or you can reach us by telephone at 206-337-0299. The fastest way to reach us is to contact a moderator in the chat room.

Information We Collect

For each visitor to our web page, our web server automatically recognizes no information regarding the domain or e-mail address.

We collect aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit. This may include user-specific information on what pages consumers access or visit. Information may also be volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registrations.

The information we collect is used for internal review and is then discarded. Information is used to improve the content of our web page. Information may be disclosed when legally required to do so, at the request of governmental authorities conducting an investigation. We also reserve the right to verify or enforce compliance with the policies governing our website. Finally, we may collect information to ensure adherence to applicable laws or to protect against misuse or unauthorized use of our website.

Cookies and Ads

With respect to cookies: We may set cookies to enhance the speed of your web browsing sessions. We do not use cookies for the purpose of tracking your activities on our site or any other sites.

With respect to ads: We are authorized affiliates of the dating sites that appear on this site. All site content on TGTalk.com is free of charge. You are not required to purchase anything to continue using our services. However, if you decide to join one of our sponsor sites you may incur charges related to membership. Please refer to the respective sites for information.

With respect to security: We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected.

We reserve the right to modify our privacy policy or terms of use at any time. Any modifications to our privacy policy will be published here.
