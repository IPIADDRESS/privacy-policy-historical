Cookies

Cookies are small files which some web sites transfer onto the hard drive of your computer, so that you are recognized the next time that you visit the site. tgrmn.com uses cookies to remember you when you visit our web site (e.g. to save you having to remember your forum password), to enhance your online experience and to help track affiliate sales.
We also collect cookies on our site to capture information about page visits. This information is anonymous and we use this information only internally. Information from the cookie is used to gauge page popularity, analyze web traffic patterns and guide product development. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether or not to accept it.

Online Advertising

We use Google AdWords Remarketing to advertise our software products across the Internet. AdWords remarketing will display relevant ads tailored to you based on what parts of our website you have viewed by placing a cookie on your machine (see about cookies above). This cookie does not identify you or give us access to your computer. If you do not wish to participate in our Google AdWords Remarketing, you can opt out by visiting Google's Ads Settings. You can also opt out of any third-party vendor's use of cookies by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative

Secure Shopping

No need to worry about credit card safety. All of your personal information including credit card number, name and address will be encrypted so that it cannot be read as the information travels over the Internet. At the time of payment, your credit card information is passed securely using 128bit SSL to our payment gateway. TGRMN Software never stores your credit card information for any reason.
