Privacy Policy

  2. TGV recognises the importance of your privacy and will not disclose personally identifiable information to any third party. By visiting this website you agree to be bound by the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy. TGV reserves the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time. If you do not agree please do not use TGV Services.
  3. Our primary purpose in collecting Personal Information is to provide you with safe, smooth and efficient services. This allows us to provide TGV services safer and easier. We only collect Personal Information about you that we consider necessary for achieving this purpose. At times, we may examine, compare and review your Personal Information for errors, omissions and for accuracy.
  4. TGV checks and processes all credit card transactions with its bankers and carries out this online verification with the bank over a secure line.
  5. In making any payment by credit card you must input the cardholder's name, your credit card number, card expiry date, CVC code (ie. 3-digit number shown at the reverse side of your credit card), contact telephone number and email address if applicable. The card details are encrypted before being transferred over the Internet and all incoming credit card payments are processed by TGV on your behalf using the SSL (Secure Socket Layer) protocol.
  6. Registration - All visitors to the TGV website are given the opportunity to register with TGV. The benefits of registration include the options to:
    6.1. Receive special offers and information from TGV
    6.2. Receive a bi-monthly newsletter, Cinemail.
  7. In addition to the details you supply when registering, TGV may collect information about your tastes and preferences based on your use of the website. The collection of information may include the analysis of website traffic and purchasing behaviour, including the use of cookies. TGV may use this information and the information supplied by you when registering to inform you about changes to the Website and other remote sales channels, new services, film and cinema news, film performance times and competitions or offers that you might find interesting. TGV will not send you this information without asking for your consent. Consent can be given on the Registration Page by clicking the box which asks if you want to receive special offers and information. You do not have to consent to either of these to register. Alternatively you can consent to just one of these options if more appropriate to you.
  8. TGV will not pass your details to any third party or company except those that we use to perform statistical analysis or manage communications on our behalf. All communications will come from TGV but we may choose to involve carefully selected third parties or companies from time to time in our communications. If you do not wish to receive this information then please do not give your consent during the registration process (Please note that you can just consent to receiving weekly film times without receiving any additional information). You can un-register or amend your personal details at anytime on the TGV website by clicking on PROFILES tab after logging in to the website.
  9. From time to time, TGV will compile aggregate statistics about the services and may share them with reputable third parties. These statistics will not contain information that would enable any third party to identify you personally.
  10. TGV reserves the right to access and disclose individually identifiable information to comply with all applicable laws and lawful requests by the appropriate authorities, to operate its systems properly or for its own protection or that of its other users. TGV also reserves the right to disclose individually identifiable information to third parties where a complaint arises concerning your use of the TGV Website or other remote sales channels, and we consider that use to be inconsistent with our Terms and Conditions.
  11. TGV will not store any financial details other than your 16-digit credit card number to which is linked to your ticket booking. Access to our secure web server is strictly controlled.
  12. When you use a link form our Website to another website, our Privacy Policy is no longer in effect. Your browsing and interaction on any other websites is subject to that websites?own rules and policies.
