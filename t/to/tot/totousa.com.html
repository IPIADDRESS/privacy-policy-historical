Our Site intended for use by visitors from the Americas.  We offer translations of our Site into French or Spanish for our visitors from Canada and Central and South America.  The Site is maintained on servers located in the United States, and personal information submitted is stored on our servers in the United States.

We strive to comply with laws of jurisdictions in which we maintain operations but we make no representations that the practices described in this Privacy Policy are compliant with laws outside those jurisdictions that apply to the collection, security, use and disclosure of personal information.

Keeping Your Information Secure

We have implemented and maintain reasonable physical, administrative and technical methods to protect your information. You also play a role in protecting your information. You can help to maintain the security of your online transactions by not sharing your account logon or password with anyone and by changing your password regularly.

Information We Collect and How We Use It

Information You Provide. When you register with TOTO USA, for instance to make a purchase, we collect profile information from you through our registration form. This may include your name, address, e-mail address, shipping address, and telephone number. Providing personal information is solely your choice. However, certain services may not be fully available to you if requested information is not provided. We may also ask you to select a user name and password or identification number to gain access to certain portions of our Site. We may also ask you to provide optional demographic information such as, but not limited to, your zip code, your age range, your business, or your product preferences. When you register for any TOTO USA services, your user name and password are recorded for the purpose of maintaining your own accounts within the Site. Our servers also record e-mail addresses that you submit for registration. If you access our Site through a Social Media account we may also receive information about you, your digital identity, or your contacts from your social media account depending on how you have set your preferences on such account.

Communications with Us.  If you choose to communicate with us, whether by email, telephone, postal mail or by using our online chat feature, we will collect and store the information you provide.

Our Site may allow residents of the United States to purchase certain products through our TOTO Store, an independent website which we own and operate. When you place an order we will require that you provide your contact information including address, email address and telephone number, your payment card information and associated billing address and telephone number, and your shipping address. Information about your payment is protected during transmission to us using industry standard technology.

If you would like to e-mail a page or pages from our Site to a friend, we will collect the recipient’s e-mail address for purposes of that transmission. However, we may not maintain a permanent record of that address unless the recipient independently registers with TOTO USA.

We may use the information we collect from you for our legitimate operational and development interests, for example:

  • to monitor and improve the use and satisfaction of Site users,
  • to display advertisements relevant to your interests,
  • to conduct research and analysis and
  • to expand our services and outreach within the United States.

Automatically Collected Information. When a visitor requests pages on the TOTO USA site, our servers automatically recognize the browser’s domain name and IP address. We may also collect statistical information about how visitors are generally searching or using the Site and reserve the right to compile and publish profile information about our visitors without the visitor’s name and addresses for reasonable business purposes.

We do not use technology that recognizes a “do-not-track” signal from your web browser.

Cookies. We may use “cookies” (small pieces of information stored by your browser on your computer’s hard drive) to track sessions and to personalize your experience on our Site so that we may improve our site and better tailor it to meet our users’ needs. You can adjust your browser setting to limit or refuse cookies. If you refuse cookies, this will limit system performance and may even cause certain features of the Site to malfunction or not to work at all. Further general information about cookies and how they work is available at www.allaboutcookies.org. The cookies we use on our Site include:

Session Cookies: These temporary cookies expire and are automatically erased whenever you close your browser window. We use session cookies to grant our users access to content and to enable actions they must be logged in to do.

Persistent Cookies: These usually have an expiration date in the distant future and remain in your browser until they expire or you manually delete them. We use persistent cookies to better understand usage patterns so we can improve the site for our customers. For example, we may use a persistent cookie to associate you with your user account or to remember that your choices on the Site, but this only works when cookies on our site are enabled.

Third Party Cookies: We permit limited third parties to place cookies through the Site to provide us with better insights into the use of the Site or user demographics or to provide relevant advertising to you. These third parties may collect information about a consumer’s online activities over time and across different websites when he or she uses our website. For example, we utilize an analytics program to analyze usage patterns of the Site. We do not control the use of such third party cookies or the resulting information and we are not responsible for any actions or policies of such third parties.

We may disclose your non-private, aggregated, or otherwise non-Personal Information, such as usage statistics of our Products, to our affiliates and third parties.

TOTO USA’s business and website are not designated to be attractive to children. We do not knowingly collect data from children, and we do not knowingly market to children. Our terms and conditions of use require that any registration may only be made by those over the age of 18. If we have reason to believe that information is being provided by a child under the age of 18 TOTO USA will not collect the information without parental consent or parental notification, which will include the opportunity for the parent to prevent the use of the information or to prevent the child’s participation in the activity.
