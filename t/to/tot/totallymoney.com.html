1. Introduction

TotallyMoney is a credit broker, not a lender. We offer a range of free services that aim to help you better understand your credit situation. You can use our services to get your free credit report and score, and to see what that information means in practical terms for you, and for your borrowing power. We will show you whether you are likely to be accepted for specific products, including credit cards and loans. In order to provide this level of personalised information, it is necessary for us to disclose and receive data about you. If you want to apply for a product you find using our services, we will pass you through to the relevant lender. You need to register with us to access the full range of services. The details of how we will use your data are set out below in this privacy policy. You should also read our Terms and Conditions and Cookies Policy carefully before you decide to use our services.

2. About TotallyMoney

The website www.totallymoney.com (“Site”) and the TotallyMoney App (“App”) are owned and operated by TotallyMoney Limited trading as TotallyMoney (“we”, “ us” and “our”). We are an appointed representative of MI Money Limited which is authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority in respect of consumer credit related activities including the provision of credit information services (FCA FRN 511936).

Data protection law applies to our collection and use of personal information and TotallyMoney Limited is the controller for that personal information (ICO Registration Number Z1096594) of Churchill House, 142-146 Old Street, London, EC1V 9BW — details of our registration can be found online at the ICO website.

3. The information we collect about you

In order to provide our Services to you, we collect information about you from four main sources, as more fully described below:

(i) from you when you register;

(ii) from the credit reference agencies we use (for example to provide credit report and score data);

(iii) from our third party service providers and the Lenders we work with to receive eligibility data;

(iv) information about your visit, such as our analysis of how you behave on our Site.

4. About our services

We have two sets of services available through our Site: those you need to register for, and those you do not. If you register with us, you will create an account with TotallyMoney (“TotallyMoney Account ”) that will give you access to our “Account Services” — that is, access to a free credit report and credit score service (“Credit Report Service”), plus tailored information about your borrowing power and eligibility for Products (“Borrowing Power Service”). If you do not wish to register with us, you can still access our “Intelligent Alerts Service”, which allows you to check your eligibility for Products, compare Products and to be alerted on a monthly basis to changes in your eligibility for Products.

In this Privacy Policy when we refer to “Services” we are referring to the Account Services and the Intelligent Alerts Service. When we refer to “Product(s)” we are referring to credit cards, loans, mortgages and such other financial products for consumers that we might introduce to our Site in the future.

Personalisation is an important part of our Services. We may analyse and profile your information in order to tailor the information that we present to you, to improve our Services, and to determine the offers and communications that might be of interest to you (where applicable).

5. Intelligent Alerts Service

As part of the Intelligent Alerts Service, we provide certain credit product eligibility comparison services for Products, to let you compare Products and check your eligibility to apply. This will give you an understanding of the likelihood of your application for a Product being accepted. In order to provide the Intelligent Alerts Service, we collect and use certain information about you (“ personal information”) as further described below.

  • You provide us with personal information (such as name, date of birth, email address, employment status, annual income, Product preferences, address, time at address and residential status) in order to check your eligibility for Products. We need this information to be able to provide the Intelligent Alerts Service to you including sending you the results of your eligibility check and your monthly eligibility update. If you do not provide this information you will not be able to benefit from the Intelligent Alerts Service.
  • We use the personal information you provide to do a “soft search” or a “quotation search” on your credit file and compare your personal information with the acceptance criteria for the relevant Products. This involves us sharing your personal information with:
    • Experian (our provider of pre-screening searches and eligibility checking services) in order to access your credit file, verify and validate the information you provide against publicly available databases and for fraud prevention purposes (please read Experian’s terms by clicking here); and
    • the specific third party credit issuers that provide the Products (“ Lenders”) to check your eligibility for their Products (including whether or not you are an existing customer) and for fraud prevention purposes.
    Click here for more information about soft searches.
  • Experian provides us with information about key elements of your credit file and the relevant Lenders send us the outcome of the Product eligibility checks. We use this information to provide you with your eligibility check results on the Site and we send the results to the email address provided to us by you.
  • We will repeat the “soft search” and send the results to you by email or push notification (where you are using the App) every month for as long as you are subscribed to the Intelligent Alerts Service.
  • You can unsubscribe from the Intelligent Alerts Service at any time by contacting us, by emailing us at feedback@totallymoney.com or by clicking the unsubscribe link in an email from us with your results.
  • If you unsubscribe, we will no longer perform the monthly “soft searches” or email you with your results. You would need to resubmit your details on the Site in order to recommence the Intelligent Alerts Service.

The purposes of the processing described above is to provide the Intelligent Alerts Service to you. Our legal basis for processing is contractual necessity (to perform the Intelligent Alerts Service).

6. Account Services

When you register for a TotallyMoney Account:

  • We ask you to give us certain personal information about you, your address and your finances (such as name, date of birth, employment status, annual income, Product preferences, address, time at address, residential status). We use this information to identify you and make our services more accurate.
  • We also ask for your email address and a password to create your account. We need this information to be able to provide the TotallyMoney Account and in order for us to be able to provide the Account Services to you (as further described below).
  • Callcredit Consumer Limited (“Callcredit”), who we work with to provide the Credit Report Service (as further described below), also asks you for certain personal information to verify your identity and authenticate you for the Credit Report Service (such as credit card details, information about when you last took out a credit card, any personal loans you have and/or mobile phone contracts). We do not see the information you provide (even though it is provided to Callcredit through a TotallyMoney branded interface as part of your TotallyMoney account registration), we only see the outcome of this authentication (e.g. authentication pass/failure).
  • We will send you a welcome email with information about our Account Services and how to access them.

If you do not provide the information requested as part of the TotallyMoney Account registration process, you will not be able to benefit from the Credit Report Service.

It is vital that you keep your TotallyMoney Account information accurate and up to date as inaccurate personal information will produce inaccurate results. You can update your personal information at any time by accessing your TotallyMoney Account.

12. How and where we store your personal data

All of the information that you give us is stored on secure servers. We will periodically review this information to ensure that we do not keep it for any longer than is necessary.

The internet is not a secure medium. However, we have put in place various security procedures to protect your information. Firewalls are used to block unauthorised traffic to the servers – and the actual servers are located in a secure location which can only be accessed by authorised personnel. We use industry-standard encryption technology to ensure that all your personal and transactional information is encrypted before transmission to certain Lenders or third party service providers. Our security policies are in place to safeguard your privacy from unauthorised access or improper use – and we will continue to enhance our security as and when new technology becomes available.

The data that we collect from you may be transferred to, and stored at, a destination outside the European Economic Area (EEA). It may also be processed by one of our third-party providers, who offer support services. By submitting your personal data, you agree to this transfer, storing or processing. We will take all the steps necessary to ensure that your data is treated securely – and in accordance with this privacy policy and all relevant statutory requirements.

  • Object to and not to be subject to a decision based solely on automated processing, including profiling, which produces legal effects or significantly affects you.

To exercise the rights outlined above in respect of the personal information processed by us as a controller contact us or email us at feedback@totallymoney.com.

Where we have said we process your personal information based on consent, individuals have a right to withdraw their consent at any time by contacting us or by emailing us at feedback@totallymoney.com.

If you register with us, or if you continue to use our Site, you agree to our use of cookies.

Cookies are widely used in order to make websites work, or run more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the Site.

Please note that our advertisers, Service Providers and Product Providers may also use cookies, over which we have no control.

You may opt-out or decline the use of cookies at any time – but in doing so, the Site may not perform or function as we intend, and your access may be limited. You can decline cookies through controls and settings which are unique to your chosen internet browser. For more information, consult the help file on your browser. Unless you have adjusted your settings to refuse cookies, our system will issue them as soon you visit our Site.

You can find out even more about cookies at www.youronlinechoices.co.uk.
