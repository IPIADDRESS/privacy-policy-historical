Total Telecom Private Policy Statement

TOTAL TELECOM takes the privacy of its users seriously and this Privacy Policy Statement explains the privacy and data-protection practices of the leading web site for the global telecommunications business.

It is important for TOTAL TELECOM to safeguard its users' privacy while also providing a personalized and valuable service. The collection of personal information of users is necessary if TOTAL TELECOM is to satisfy the expectations and requirements of these users, for instance by communicating with them and providing an interactive service.

  1. What Information is compiled?

    2. In addition to the details on your registration form, information is automatically retrieved, for example on browsing patterns through the site. This is used only in aggregate form; no individual user is identified. The aggregate data allows TOTAL TELECOM to analyze the site traffic and manage its relationship with advertisers and thus provide a more relevant service to its users.

  2. What do we do with the information we collect?

    3. The personal information you supplied on your TOTAL TELECOM registration form will not be disclosed outside Terrapinn ltd. The email address you provide when you first became an TOTAL TELECOM user allows us to send you information about important changes and improvements to the web site. Each time you receive a message, you have the option to decline to receive further marketing information relating to the activities of other parts of Terrapinn ltd. This can be undertaken by stating as such by reply e-mail.

  3. What is a cookie?

    4. TOTAL TELECOM uses a "cookie" to save time in allowing access to the site (it saves checking the registration database). A cookie is a small piece of information sent from TOTAL TELECOM to your computer, to help us identify you quickly. If you prefer not to accept a cookie, you can log in fully each time you use TOTAL TELECOM.
