Cookies

Like most other Web sites, Bestofmedia, LLC uses cookies. Cookies are small data files that some Web sites write to the user’s hard drive when users visit them. A cookie file can contain information such as a user ID that the site uses to track the pages the user has visited. Cookies do not tell us who you are unless you've specifically given us personally identifiable information. A cookie can't read data off the user’s hard drive or read cookie files created by other sites.
Bestofmedia, LLC uses cookies to allow automatic logins to improve the user’s experience with Bestofmedia’s websites. For example, we may use a cookie to identify our forums members so they don't have to re-enter a user id and password when they sign-in. Cookies can also be used to help Bestofmedia to better understand how visitors interact with his websites leading to the delivery of more relevant content, or they can be used to control the number of times an advertisement is served to the same visitor. Cookies may be created directly by Bestofmedia’s sites for these purposes, or by third-party companies operating on Bestofmedia’s behalf. Some of his advertisers occasionally serve the user cookies as well. Bestofmedia do not have control over cookies placed by advertisers. If the user chooses to become a member of the forums, he must have cookies enabled to access the member related pages (i.e. Discussion Boards and Member Profile pages).
Most web browsers automatically accept cookies but allow the user to modify security settings so he can approve or reject cookies on a case-by-case basis.
