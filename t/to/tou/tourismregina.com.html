Privacy Policy

Tourism Regina is committed to preserving the privacy our web visitors, both guest and Registered members alike. This website is a collaborative effort between Tourism Regina and Tourism Saskatchewan, and therefore information may be available to both partners. However, your personal information, including email address, Name, Phone number, Address, and any other contact information, wherein this information has been supplied to Tourism Regina, will not be released to any party outside of Tourism Regina and Tourism Saskatchewan. In no way will you be solicited through email nor mail by either Tourism Saskatchewan our Tourism Regina, without your agreement our consent. Should you feel that your privacy has been breached due to the actions of either of the afore-mentioned organizations, please contact the Site Administrator immediately.
