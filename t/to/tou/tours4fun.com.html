Information Collection and Use
Tours4Fun the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to any outside parties, except as outlined in this policy. Tours4Fun collects information from our users at several different points on our website in order to process orders and better serve you with pertinent information. Information collected includes name, shipping address, billing address, telephone numbers, e-mail address, and payment information such as credit card. Tours4Fun also requires you to submit a user name and password of your choice for future access to your information. Your user name and password is to remain confidential and you should not share this information with anyone.
Registration
Top
In order to process your reservation using this website, you must first complete the registration form. During registration you are required to give your contact information, which includes name, e-mail, telephone number and physical address. This information is used to contact you about your reservation on Tours4Fun.com such as sending automated order status updates.
Reservation
Top
Most of the information collected in the registration process will be used to process reservations. In the reservation process, you must provide additional financial information such as credit card number, expiration date, money order, wire transfer or account information. This information is used for billing purposes and to fulfill your reservation. If we have trouble processing your reservation, this contact information is used to get in touch with you. We do not share your personal and financial information with any third parties. The only exception is when processing your credit card information with the merchant bank for authorization and approval. This process is protected by an enhanced security system. See Security paragraph for more details on security measures and procedures.
Cookies
Top
With respect to cookies, we use cookies to customize web page content based on visitors' browser type or other information that the visitor sends. If you reject the cookie, you may still use our site; however you will be unable to use our shopping cart to purchase items online. This is because cookies are used by the shopping cart to retain information necessary to process your order. If you disable cookies, we will not be able to accept your reservation online. We do not share any information with any third party sites. Cookies are used strictly for the purpose of enhancing your personal experience with our site. When you close your browser, the cookies will be terminated.
Log Files
Top
We use IP addresses to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information. We do not distribute or share IP information with any third parties.
Sharing
Top
We use a credit card processing company to bill users for purchases and services. These companies do not retain, share, store or use personally identifiable information for any secondary purposes. Tours4Fun is a California company and shall abide by all federal and state laws. Tours4Fun shall disclose any personally identifiable information when required to by law.
Links
Top
This web site contains links to other sites. Please be aware Tours4Fun not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this Web site.
128-Bit SSL
Top
Tours4Fun.com takes every precaution to protect your information. When you submit sensitive information via the website, your information is protected both online and off-line.When our registration/reservation form asks you to enter sensitive information (such as credit card number), that information is encrypted and is protected with the best encryption software in the industry - SSL. Our SSL technology is the most advanced 128bit encryption by Geotrust. To learn more about SSL, follow this link www.geotrust.com.
Special Offers and Mailing List
Top
If you choose to receive special offers and sign up for our mailing list, Tours4Fun.com may send you such pertinent information. Our special offers and emails are sent only to customers who grant Tours4Fun.com permission to send such information. Tours4Fun.com does not share your information with third parties and does not solicit outside parties to send special offers on our behalf. Out of respect for the privacy of yours we present the option to not receive these types of communications. At the time of making reservation, you may opt to not receive special offers and news by un-checking the appropriate field. Furthermore, all special offers are sent via e-mail and have a "remove link" if you wish to no longer receive special offers and news. Otherwise, you may occasionally receive information on products, services, special deals or news. Please see our choice and opt-out below for further details of how to be removed from our mailing list.
Correction/Updating/Reviewing Personal Information
Top
You may change or review your recorded account information such as address or e-mail through our website at or by going to the home page at www.Tours4Fun.com and clicking on the "my account" button. You must have your username and password in order to access your account.
Choice/Opt-out
Top
Tours4Fun does not use your information for contact other than Tours4Fun's pertinent business. Tours4Fun does not share your personal information with any outside parties. However, if you wish to opt-out or be removed from Tours4Fun.com special offers and related news, you are provided with several methods to accomplish this request by un-checking the permission to send field when processing your reservation. You may also send us email to service@tours4fun.com with a request to be removed. Finally, you may also use the link provided with all communications to automatically unsubscribe.
