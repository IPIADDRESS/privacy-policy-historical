About

With close to 150,000 readers every month, Tokyo Cheapo is the site of choice for the dedicated cheapo looking to make their yen go further when visiting or living in Tokyo. We cover all the basics from things to do in Tokyo to how to get from Narita airport to Tokyo plus we cover a vast range of practical topics from which JR rail pass to choose to finding cheap virtual offices.
