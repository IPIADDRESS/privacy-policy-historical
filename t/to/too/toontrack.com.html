If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you should contact us by e-mail at privacy(at)toontrack.com.

Information We Automatically Collect
Like many Websites, we use technologies to collect and store certain other information automatically whenever you interact with this Website. For example, we may collect your IP address, browser information and reference site domain name when you visit this Website. We also collect information regarding customer traffic patterns and site usage. This information is used to analyze and improve this Website and to provide our customers with a fulfilling experience.

Also, like many Websites, we use “cookies”, which are files stored on your computer’s hard drive by your browser. Cookies help us to identify account holders and to optimize their log-in, account management and shopping experience. Cookies also allow us to hold selections in a shopping cart when a user leaves this Website without checking out. Most browsers accept cookies automatically but allow you to disable them. We recommend that you leave cookies “turned on” so that we can offer you a better experience on this Website.

We may from time to time also use pixels, transparent GIF files, Flash movies or other visual surfaces to help manage online advertising. These files enable our ad management partner(s) to recognize a unique cookie on your Web browser, which in turn enables us to learn which advertisements bring users to our website. The cookie was placed by us, or by another advertiser who works with our ad management partner(s). With cookies, the information that we collect and share is anonymous and not personally identifiable. It does not contain your name, address, telephone number, or email address.

We may also collect information about the device you are using when accessing the Website or the Web-shop, including what type of device it is, what operating system you are using, device settings, unique device identifiers, and crash data. Whether we collect some or all of this information often depends on what type of device you are using and its settings. To learn more about what information your device makes available to us, please also check the policies of your device manufacturer or software provider.

When you visit the Website, you will be informed about the information being collected and you may accept to provide this information to Toontrack. If you choose to decline, no such information will be stored but the Website may then not function correctly and Toontrack may not be able to provide its services to you. You have the possibility to manage cookies and other types of local data storage through your web browser. You also have the possibility to change your settings on your mobile telephone regarding data collection and sharing, e.g. location services and other data. Toontrack cannot control these choices or influence the basic settings since they are offered via mobile operators or web browsers.

Other Information
We also may collect and store information about you that we receive from other sources, such as a postal service providing notices of change of addresses and providers of consumer data, to enable us to update and correct the information contained in our database and to provide product recommendations, updates and special offers that we think will interest you.

Storage time
Toontrack only saves your personal data for as long as necessary for the purpose of the respective personal data process, or as long as necessary according to law. Information that is no longer necessary is deleted.

How and where Toontrack stores your personal data
Toontrack processes your personal data within the EU/EEA since Toontrack’s servers are located within the Area. This means that your personal data will not be transferred to third countries. Toontrack approaches security with the utmost seriousness. Toontrack uses several methods to protect the information you provide to the company. Toontrack strives to ensure that your information will not disappear, be unlawfully used, end up on the wrong hands and is not exposed. The methods used to secure your information take into account modern technology as well as how integrity-sensitive the information is which Toontrack collects, manages and stores. If you wish to know more about Toontrack’s policy regarding security and confidentiality for your personal data, we encourage you to read our Data and Security Policy.
 

IS INFORMATION COLLECTED FROM CHILDREN?

We do not sell any items ordered through this Website directly to anyone who we know to be under the age of 18. If you are under the age of 18, you should not use this Website to submit orders or purchase any items. In addition, we do not collect online any personal information from anyone who we know to be under the age of 13. If you are under the age of 13, you should use this Website only with the involvement of a parent or guardian and should not submit any personal information of any kind to us. Contact Toontrack if you discover that a child under 13 has provided personal data without the approval of their guardian.
 

WITH WHOM DOES TOONTRACK SHARE INFORMATION?

We do not share with others any personal information that you provide to us through this Website or through our Support Forum, except with your consent, when required by law or as described in this Privacy Policy.

We engage third parties from time to time to perform services in connection with the operation of our business. Examples of these services include, but are not limited to, development, operation and maintenance of our Website, management of customer accounts, payment processing and authorization, fraud protection and credit risk reduction, product customization, order fulfillment and shipping, gift card processing and management, marketing and promotional activities, Website evaluation, data analysis and, where applicable, data cleansing. We may provide personal information to these third parties, but we authorize them to use this information only in connection with the services they perform on our behalf. We may permit such third parties to disclose personal information to subcontractors, but only if the third party limits the subcontractor’s use of such information to services performed on our behalf.

We also may offer you the opportunity to purchase products and services provided by or in conjunction with third parties. If you purchase any of these products or services, we may provide your personal information to the third parties that provide the products and services that you request, but we authorize these third parties to use this information only in connection with the products and services they provide. We may also provide information, including personal information, about you to certain business partners, but only if you participate in programs offered by such business partners.

We may share non-personal information with our marketing partners, advertisers and others from time to time. Examples of such non-personal information include the number of users who visited this Website during a specific time period or purchased a specific product through this Website. This information is shared in an aggregated form.

We may use information that we collect from visitors to our Website to remarket certain products that believe that are of particular interest or benefit to individual visitors. We are implementing our remarketing efforts through the use of third-party software and services. When we are using these services, our third-party service providers may display banners and ads on other websites that our visitors may visit. These third-party service providers will more than likely collect information using cookies. You can remove these cookies by contacting those third-party service providers or disabling them on the third-party service providers’ websites. The information collected with respect to our remarketing activities will typically be maintained in our third-party service provider accounts and will be subject to the third-party service providers’ privacy policies. The visitor information that is maintained in certain of our third-party service provider accounts will be deleted thirty days after it has been collected but some third-party service providers may maintain it for shorter or longer periods of time.

We, or those with whom we have shared information in accordance with this Privacy Policy, may release personal information if we believe in good faith that: the law or legal process requires it; we have received a valid administrative request from a law enforcement agency; or such release is necessary or advisable to protect the rights, property or safety of Toontrack, or any of our respective affiliates, business partners, customers or others. We may also disclose personal information to assist us in the collection of amounts owed by you to us or to one of our business partners with whom you have authorized us to transact business on your behalf.

We reserve the right to transfer any information we have about you, including personal information, in connection with the sale or transfer of all or a portion of the business or assets of Toontrack to a third party. We also reserve the right to share the same information with any Toontrack subsidiary or affiliate.
 

YOUR RIGHTS

You have the following rights in accordance with applicable data protection legislation:

  • The right to access. You can request a copy of the personal data which Toontrack has collected and stored about you in its capacity as personal data controller. You may request a copy at no charge.
  • The right to rectification. You are entitled to have incorrect information rectified by Toontrack or to supplement incomplete information.
  • The right to erasure. You are entitled to have your personal data erased provided the information is no longer necessary for the purposes of processing, if you object to direct marketing, if the personal data has been processed in an unlawful matter, or where the personal data must be erased in order to fulfil a legal obligation covering Toontrack.
  • Right to restrict processing. You are entitled to request that Toontrack’s processing of your personal data be limited.
  • The right to object. You are entitled to object to the use of your personal data for the purposes of direct marketing or in those cases in which Toontrack process your data based on a legitimate interest.
  • Right to data portability. You are entitled to request that your personal data processed by Toontrack about you as a consequence of fulfilling the customer agreement is transferred to another personal data controller.

The Swedish Data Protection Authority (Sw: Datainspektionen) is responsible for monitoring the application of the data privacy legislation and, if you believe that Toontrack is processing your personal data in an improper way, you have the possibility to file a complaint with the Swedish Data Protection Authority.
