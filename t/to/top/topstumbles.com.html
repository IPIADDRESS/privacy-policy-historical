Privacy Policy
Premium Network is bound by the National Privacy Principles contained in the Laws of the UAE (Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Sharjah, Oman, Umm Al-Qaiwain, Ras Al-Khaimah, and Fujairah) Premium Network may, from time to time, review and update this privacy policy statement to take account of new laws and technology and changes to Premium Network's operations. All personal/business information held by Premium Network will be governed by Premium Network's most recent policy.
What kind of personal.business information does Premium Network collect and how does Premium Network collect it?
Premium Network collects personal/business information from a variety of sources, including from members of the public, advertisers, prospective employees, newsagents, mailing lists, recruitment agencies, contractors and business partners.
In general, the personal/business information Premium Network collects includes (but is not limited to) name, address, contact details, gender, occupation, in some cases membership of professional associations and, where relevant, financial information, including credit card information, banking details and income information.
Where Premium Network enters into a transaction with an individual/entity, Premium Network may collect other types of personal/business information, depending on the nature of the transaction. Premium Network will generally state the specific purposes for which this personal/business information will be used and to whom it may be disclosed, other than as set out in this policy.
Personal/business information you provide: You may provide information when you acquire a product or service from Premium Network, provide a product or service to Premium Network, complete a questionnaire, enter a competition, enter an event, seek employment, or when you communicate with Premium Network by e-mail, telephone or in writing (for example if you contact customer service or provide feedback).
Indirect collection: Although Premium Network generally collects personal/business information about individuals/entities directly from those individuals/entities, in some circumstances Premium Network may obtain personal/business information from a third party, for example from a commercially available mailing list.

How will Premium Network use your information?
Premium Network may use personal/business information for the primary purpose for which it is collected, for secondary purposes related to the primary purpose and in other ways permitted by law.
In general, Premium Network uses personal/business information to:
• provide you with products or services you have requested from Premium Network, including subscription and advertising services;
• communicate with you;
• provide you with information about Premium Network's products and services; and
• provide some third parties with an opportunity to contact company officers on our specialist business databases.
In most cases where Premium Network asks you to provide information about yourself it is necessary to enable Premium Network to provide you with the requested product or service. Disclosure
Premium Network may disclose personal/business information to other companies in the Premium Network Group who may only use it as set out above.
Premium Network may also provide your information to:
• third parties engaged by Premium Network to perform functions on its behalf, such as processing credit card information, mailouts, debt collection, marketing, research and advertising;
• third parties authorised by you to receive information held by Premium Network;
If Premium Network sells part of its business, personal/business information may be part of the assets transferred to the purchaser.
How does Premium Network treat Sensitive Information?
"Sensitive Information" means information relating to a person's racial or ethnic origin, membership of political bodies, religion, membership of a trade union or professional or trade association, sexual preferences, criminal record and health.
Premium Network may use and disclose Sensitive Information only for the purpose for which it was provided or a directly related secondary purpose or as allowed by law unless you have agreed otherwise.
Management and security of personal/business information Premium Network protects personal/business information it holds from misuse, loss, unauthorised access, modification or disclosure by various means, including firewalls, pass word access, and secure servers.
Premium Network has appointed Privacy Officer to co-ordinate Premium Network's management of personal/business information under this policy and the Privacy Act.
Updating personal/business information Premium Network endeavours to ensure that personal/business information it holds is accurate, complete and up-to-date. Please contact Premium Network at the address below if you wish to update your personal/business information. Where personal/business information is no longer required by Premium Network, it will be destroyed or de-identified.
Seeking access to the personal/business information Premium Network holds about you
You have the right to seek access the personal/business information held about you and to advise Premium Network of any inaccuracy. There are some exceptions to this right set out in the Privacy Act.
If you make an access request, Premium Network will ask you to verify your identity and specify what information you require. Premium Network may charge a fee to cover the costs of meeting your request.
How to contact Premium Network To make an access request or otherwise contact Premium Network about privacy issues, please write to Legal Affairs at legal@premiumnetwork.ae
