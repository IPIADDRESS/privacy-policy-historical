This is a privacy policy for Top Business Webs. Top Business Webs respects your privacy. Below is an outline of the information that Top Business Webs gathers, how it’s used, and how you can “opt-out.” We invite you to contact us if you have questions about this policy. Whenever you request a page through your browser, navigation and clickstream data such as:

  • your IP address,
  • browser and version,
  • operating system,
  • date and time and
  • the site from which you came

are stored in a log file and/or database. Any search terms that you enter into the Top Business Webs Site Search are also logged. This information cannot be used to identify specific individuals, and is only used for:

  • Website and system administration,
  • research and development,
  • anonymous user analysis, and
  • to provide accurate statistics to advertisers in the form of aggregated data.

This information is kept on a secure server hosted by WiredTree.

The email addresses collected from subscriptions to any Top Business Webs newsletters are not sold, or disclosed to any third parties. These addresses will only be used by our business to send out Top Business Webs communications (for example, the Top Business Webs newsletters). You will not receive any mailings you did not request. The email addresses collected at Top Business Webs are stored on a server hosted by MailChimp. You can remove yourself from our list at any time.

The email addresses collected at Top Business Webs from subscribers to the opt-in lists (AKA "Information Announcement Lists") are used to send announcements to individuals who requested them. You will not receive any mailings you did not request, and you can remove yourself at any time. Your email address will not be disclosed to any third party besides MailChimp. Instructions for removal from these mailings are included in each individual email message that is sent to you.

When a survey appears on Top Business Webs, information is collected, compiled and analyzed on an aggregated basis.

Cookies are a technology which can be used to provide you with tailored information from a Website. A cookie is an element of data that a Website can send to your browser, and which your browser may then store on your system. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie. Doing this will allow you to decide on a case-by-case basis whether to accept cookies or not.

Cookies on this site are used by ourselves. Cookies are used for:

  • customization of content (i.e. not repeating pop-up windows),
  • storing login information, and
  • statistical site-traffic analysis.

The information collected during the registration process for Top Business Webs may be used to:

  • contact members,
  • customize content based upon user preferences,
  • display member status, and
  • display the date of a member´s last visit.

Information collected in this process can also be used to prevent banned users from re-registering under a different username. Cookies may be sent to your browser to enable automatic log-ins upon further visits, as well as for content customization.

Top Business Webs complies with all law enforcement agency requests for information
