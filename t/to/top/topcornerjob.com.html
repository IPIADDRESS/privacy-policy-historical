Use of Cookies
We may store some information such as cookies on your computer when you visit the Website. You can erase or choose to block this information from your computer. The cookies do not contain or capture any personal information. Your decision to refuse cookies will not prevent you from using the Website, but may limit your ability to take advantage of some of the advanced features of the Website. We do not have access to or control over information collected by outside advertisers on our Website. You may opt out of the use of the cookies by disabling cookies option in your web browser.
