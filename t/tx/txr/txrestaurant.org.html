Privacy & Security

  • Printer-friendly
  • Email this page

When you browse the RESTAURANTVILLE website, you do so anonymously. Personal information -- including your email address -- is not collected. Some of our webpages, however, may request that you supply us with personal information, including your email for purposes such as correspondence, site registration, making a purchase, or participating in online surveys.

COOKIES

RESTAURANTVILLE may utilize a standard technology called a "cookie" to collect information about how our site is used. "Cookies" are bits of information that your computer browser stores on your hard drive. Be assured that our cookies do not contain any personally identifying information. Cookies do allow us to store information that would be useful to you; for example, items you've marked but aren't quite ready to buy, or information you've indicated that you would like us to keep you updated on.

SECURITY

RESTAURANTVILLE.COM has security measures in place to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.

When you place orders or access account information, we offer the use of a secure server. The secure server software encrypts the information you input before it is sent to us.  WE DO NOT STORE CREDIT CARD INFORMATION IN OUR DATABASE. 

We protect all customer data against unauthorized access, store all data in a secure location and grant access only to designated staff. However, no data transmission over the Internet can be 100% guaranteed. Therefore, while we strive to protect your information, RESTAURANTVILLE.COM cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information.

Measures to ensure that safety and confidentiality include:

  • physical security procedures in place at Texas Restaurant Association data centers
  • log-in IDs, passwords and firewalls restricting network access to Texas Restaurant Association's databases and customer records
  • Encryption of transmissions between Texas Restaurant Association, operator units and distributors.

BROWSER RECOMMENDATIONS

For enhanced security, we recommend using either Netscape Navigator® or Microsoft® Internet Explorer® version 6.0 or higher, both of which enable SSL. We also recommend setting your browser's preferences to accept cookies and enable JavaScriptTM.
