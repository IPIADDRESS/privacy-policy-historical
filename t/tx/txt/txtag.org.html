Privacy Policy

This policy establishes how TxTag will use information we gather about you from your visit to this Site. Regardless of the information being transmitted to TxTag.org, we will protect all such information consistent with applicable law. The privacy of our customers is of utmost importance to TxTag.

The TxTag.org web site complies with the State Web Site Link and Privacy Policy. TxTag encourages organizations that link to this Web site to comply with the provisions of the State Web Site Link and Privacy Policy, especially regarding protection of the privacy rights of individuals, and to make reasonable efforts to provide accessible sites.

Notice of Monitored Use

The TxTag.org web site is an official Texas Department of Transportation computer system operated for authorized use only. If you visit a TxDOT site . . .

To read or download information:

We may collect and store information, namely, the number of visitors to the site, for statistical purposes. For example, we may count the number of visitors to the different sections of our site to help us make them more useful to visitors.
