TVU Networks Privacy Notice

TVU Networks, Inc. This Privacy Notice is incorporated into and is subject to the TVU Networks Terms of Service.  Your use of TVU Networks and any personal information you provide on TVU Networks remain subject to the terms of this Privacy Notice and our Terms of Service.

By using the TVU Networks Platform, you expressly consent to the information handling practices described in this notice.

 

NEVER DISPLAY ANY PERSONAL INFORMATION ON THE TVU NETWORKS PLATFORM THAT COULD BE USED TO FIND YOU IN THE REAL, OFF-LINE, WORLD – for example, your full real name, mailing or home address, or phone numbers.

The Information TVU Networks Collects:

  •        "Personally Identifiable Information":  You may provide to TVU Networks, through registering for and using the TVU Networks Platform, what is generally called "personally identifiable" information (such as your name, username, email address, postal mailing address, home/mobile telephone number, credit card number and billing information, etc.).

If you post personally identifiable or other personal information on the TVU Networks Platform, then the promises made by TVU Networks in this Privacy Notice may not apply to that information because TVU Networks won’t have control over who sees and who may collect the information that you make publicly available.

  •         "Public Profile Information":  You may choose to post certain information to your public "profile" on the TVU Networks Platform such as your date of birth, general location, interests, occupation, web site URLs, videos, blogs, IM addresses, and comments.

  •         "Cookies" Information:  When you use the TVU Networks Platform, we may send one or more cookies – small text files containing a string of alphanumeric characters – to your computer.  TVU Networks may use both session cookies and persistent cookies.  A session cookie disappears after you close your browser.  A persistent cookie remains after you close your browser and may be used by your browser on subsequent visits to the TVU Networks Platform.  Persistent cookies can be removed.  Please review your web browser "Help" file to learn the proper way to modify your cookie settings.

·       "Automatically Collected" Information:  When you use the TVU Networks Platform or open one of our HTML emails, TVU Networks may automatically record certain information from your system by using different types of tracking technology, including "clear gifs" or "web beacons."  This "automatically collected" information may include Internet Protocol address ("IP Address"), a unique device or user ID, system type, the services or web pages that you visit just before or just after the TVU Networks Platform, the content and pages that you access on the TVU Networks Platform, and the dates and times that you visit the TVU Networks Platform.

The Way TVU Networks Uses Information:

  •        If you choose to create a public profile on the TVU Networks Platform, you will post, at your option, certain personal information that will be publicly displayed to other Users.

  •        TVU Networks uses all of the information that you provide or that we collect to operate, maintain, enhance, and provide to you all of the features and services found on the TVU Networks Platform.  TVU Networks will use your email address, without further consent, for administrative purposes such as sending to you account activation and "welcome" emails, notifying you of major TVU Networks Platform updates, for customer service purposes, to address copyright infringement, defamation, or User safety issues, or to contact you regarding any of your uses of the TVU Networks Platform.

  •        TVU Networks uses Automatically Collected Information and Cookies to:  (a) automatically update the TVU Networks Applications on your system; (b) remember your information so that you will not have to re-enter it during your visit or the next time you access the TVU Networks Platform; (c) provide customized third-party content, advertisements, and information; (d) monitor the effectiveness of third-party marketing campaigns; (e) monitor aggregate usage metrics such as total number of visitors and features used; and (f) track your entries, submissions, and status in any promotions or other activities.

  •        TVU Networks will not use your email address or other personally identifiable information to send promotional or marketing messages without your consent or except as part of a specific program or feature for which you will have the ability to opt-in.

When TVU Networks Discloses Information:

  •      TVU Networks does not share your personally identifiable information with other organizations for their marketing or promotional uses without your express consent.  Please be aware, however, that any personally identifiable information that you voluntarily choose to display on the TVU Networks Platform – such as when you publish video credits or profile information – becomes publicly available and may be collected and used by others without restriction.

  •       TVU Networks may disclose Automatically Collected and other aggregate non-personally-identifiable information with interested third parties to assist such parties in understanding the usage, viewing, and demographic patterns for certain programs, content, services, advertisements, promotions, or other functionality on the TVU Networks Platform. 

  •        TVU Networks may disclose User information to affiliated companies or other businesses or persons who provide web site hosting, maintenance and security services, fulfill orders; conduct data analysis and create reports, offer certain functionality and that assist TVU Networks in improving the TVU Networks Platform and creating new site features for the TVU Networks Platform.  We require that these parties process such information in compliance with this Privacy Notice, we authorize only a limited use of such information, and we require these parties to use appropriate confidentiality measures.

·        TVU Networks may disclose User information if required to do so by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with state and federal laws (such as U.S. Copyright law) or respond to a court order, judicial or other government subpoena, or warrant.  In some cases, we may make such disclosures without providing notice to Users.

  •        TVU Networks also reserves the right to disclose User information that we believe, in good faith, is appropriate or necessary to take precautions against liability; protect TVU Networks from fraudulent, abusive, or unlawful uses; to investigate and defend ourselves against third-party claims or allegations; to assist government enforcement agencies; to protect the security or integrity of the TVU Networks Platform; and to protect the rights, property, or personal safety of TVU Networks, our Users, or others.

Your Choices:

You may, of course, decline to share your personally identifiable information with TVU Networks, in which case TVU Networks will not be able to provide to you some of the features and functionality found on the TVU Networks Platform.  You may update, correct, or delete your User profile information and email subscription information and preferences at any time by going to the "edit profile" section of the TVU Networks Platform and following the applicable menu choices.  Please note that upon deletion of your account or profile information, that some of your personally identifiable information, including billing and contact information, may remain in TVU Networks’ systems for some limited period of time.

To protect your privacy and security, we take reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting you account access or making corrections to your information. HOWEVER, You are responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your unique password and account information at all times. 

Advertisers:

TVU Networks allows other companies, called third-party ad servers or ad networks, to serve advertisements within the TVU Networks Platform. These third-party ad servers or ad networks may use technology to send, directly to your browser, TVUPlayer, or TVU Broadcasting Application, the advertisements that appear on the TVU Networks Platform and they may automatically receive your IP Address and other log file information when this happens.  Such third-party ad servers or ad networks may also use other technologies (such as cookies, javascript, or web beacons) to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and to personalize their advertising content. 

TVU Networks does not provide your personally identifiable information to these third-party ad servers or ad networks without your consent.  However, please note that if an advertiser asks TVU Networks to show an advertisement to a certain audience (for example, males aged 24) or audience segment (for example, males aged 24 who have viewed certain types of content) and you respond to that advertisement, the advertiser or ad-server may conclude that you fit the description of the audience that they were trying to reach.

You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers or ad networks.  This Privacy Notice does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.  TVU Networks reserves the right to add or remove third-party ad networks or ad servers in its discretion and TVU Networks may not at all times list such updated ad network or ad server partners in this Privacy Notice.

If TVU Networks learns of a security systems breach, then we may attempt to notify you electronically so that you can take appropriate protective steps.  TVU Networks may post a notice on the TVU Networks Platform if a security breach occurs.  Depending on where you live, you may have a legal right to receive notice of a security breach in writing.  To receive a free written notice of a security breach you should notify us at privacy@tvunetworks.com.

Our Commitment to Children’s Privacy:

If you are under 13 years of age, then please do not use or access TVU Networks at any time or in any manner. 

Protecting the privacy of young children is especially important.  For that reason, TVU Networks does not knowingly collect or maintain personally identifiable information from persons under 13 years-of-age.  If TVU Networks learns that personally-identifiable information of persons less than 13-years-of-age has been collected on the TVU Networks Platform, then TVU Networks will take the appropriate steps to delete this information.

If you are the parent or legal guardian of a child under 13 who has become a TVU Networks Platform User, then please contact TVU Networks to have that child’s account terminated and information deleted.
