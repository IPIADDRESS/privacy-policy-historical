4. Privacy Policy

General

For each visitor to our Web Site our webserver will automatically:
  • Collect and save your IP address to help diagnose problems and administer our Web Site.
  • Set Cookies (Definition) to keep track of your sessions and save information required to log in.
  • Collect and save the web site you were redirected from
  • Collect and save the web pages you visit within TVRage

Personal Information

When registering to our Web Site, Users are required to give us their contact information (such as Name, E-Mail Address, Demographic Information such as country/age). This information is used to contact visitors when necessary. Demographic and Profile Data is also collected at our web site, this information can be shared with advertisers. (Information such as E-Mail, Name, Address, Telephone will never be shared with third-parties).

Third Parties

Third Parties are used to provide ads on our web site and may use information about your visits to this and other Web Sites in order to provide advertisement on this web site of products that may be of interest to you. Cookies can be set by our third-party advertisers.

Security

To our best ability this web site has set appropriate security measures in place to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you.

Public Forums/Chat

This web site includes chat rooms, forums and other ways of people to interact with each other. Take notice that information that is disclosed in these areas become public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.

Contact

If you have any questions concerning privacy on this Web Site, you can contact us at privacy@tvrage.com.
