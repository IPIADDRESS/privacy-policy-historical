Information collected

TRAVELfusion's primary goal in collecting personal information is to provide you with a highly personalised and interactive service that meets your needs and makes your experience more relevant.

To fully participate in our services, you will need to register using our online registration form, where you will be required to provide us with your name, email, User ID and other optional personal information. When our subscription services are introduced we will require additional information such as your credit card number and billing address.

We also collect information automatically about your visit to the Site and any information you publish or services you request.

We automatically track certain information based upon your behaviour on our Site. We use this information to do internal research on our users' demographics, interests, and behaviour to better understand and serve you and our community

We may sometimes ask you for additional information by way of surveys so that we can better understand the needs of our users to help us improve our features. TRAVELfusion sometimes shares the aggregated demographic information in these surveys with our advertisers and partners. We never share any information about specific individuals with any third party without the user's express consent.

In our interactive, travel sharing, lift sharing, find a partner and location aware services, we collect and processes personal information to personalise your experience and help provide the right matches between users.

Except as otherwise expressly included in this Privacy Policy, this document only addresses the use and disclosure of information TRAVELfusion collects from you. We provide your personal information to necessary third party travel and service providers when you indicate that you want to purchase travel services via our sites and services.

Use of cookies

A cookie is a small piece of information sent by a web server to a web browser, which enables the server to collect information back from the browser. Find out more about the use of cookies on http://www.cookiecentral.com/

We may use "cookies" to identify you when you visit this web site and to allow registered users to be presented with a personalised version of the site and have access to information about their account.

We may also use cookies to keep track of your browsing patterns and build up a demographic profile.
