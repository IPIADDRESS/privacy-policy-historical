What information is collected and how do we use it?

ZUJI and Travelocity India will not collect or monitor any personal information about you without your consent. The only personal information we collect is what you tell us about yourself and information about how you use our web site. ZUJI and Travelocity India may collect information from you at several different points on the site:

1. New Member Registration

(a) What information is collected?

  • When you register as a member with ZUJI, or Travelocity India, you will be asked to provide your name and provide some contact details.
  • ZUJI, or Travelocity India, site members or their duly authorized representative will be asked to create a login identification (“login ID”) and password, as well as to provide your contact information, such as your name, title, email address, language preference and country of residence.
  • You are also given the option to opt out from receiving periodical updates and/or newsletters from ZUJI or Travelocity India informing you of the latest news and deals.

(b) Why do we collect it?

We collect the above information for the following purposes:

  • To identify you when you use our site in order to enjoy a customized and personalised experience.
  • As a security measure, to send you an email confirming your membership registration.
  • To allow us to process and complete your travel reservations and purchases.
  • To send you email confirmations for your reservations and purchases on ZUJI or Travelocity India.
  • For our customer service agents to contact you when necessary.
  • To send you our optional periodical updates and/or newsletters, if you have signed up for them.
  • To send you information about products and services which may be of interest to you.

2. Existing Member Travel Profile

(a) What information is collected?

After registering as a member with ZUJI, or Travelocity India, you are given the option to create a travel profile email address and to provide ZUJI and Travelocity India with the following member travel profile information:

  • Credit card billing information, such as your credit card type, number, expiry date and billing address.
  • Ticket delivery information including telephone numbers and address details.
  • Passport information including passport number, place of passport issuance, passport issue date and passport expiration date.
  • Date of birth.
  • Travel preferences, such as preferred cities, date/time display preferences, flight seating and meal preferences, as well as others like frequent flyer numbers.

(b) Why do we collect it?

  • With your member travel profile stored in ZUJI, or Travelocity India, you will enjoy convenience whenever you make travel reservations as you will not have to type the billing and delivery information repeatedly.
  • Your travel preferences will allow you to customize your experience on ZUJI, for example, in some circumstances, ZUJI will display your local currency.
  • To further confirm your identity.
  • To prevent credit card fraud.
  • To send you information about products and services which may be of interest to you.

3. My Stuff

(a) What information is collected?

  • Any time you make a purchased reservation, on hold reservation or save an itinerary while planning your trip, your purchased reservation, on hold reservation or saved itinerary details will be saved into "My Stuff".

 (b) Why do we collect it?

  • For your convenience, ZUJI stores your purchased reservation, on hold reservation or saved itinerary information in "My Stuff", so that you may easily refer to your trip plans or complete your purchase. You are able to identify your reservations using the Trip ID.
  • To send you information about products and services which may be of interest to you.

(b) Why do we collect it? 

  • ZUJI and Travelocity India conduct market research surveys in order to learn from you how improvements can be made to our site.
  • To send you information about products and services which may be of interest to you.

In order to follow-up on the feedback you have chosen to provide, ZUJI or Travelocity India may correspond with you using the contact information that you have provided.

6. Cookies

(a) What information is collected?

  • When you visit ZUJI, or Travelocity India, as a Guest or as a member, a cookie is stored onto your computer. A cookie is a small, encrypted text file containing some information about your preferences and allows your usage to be tracked. The use of cookies is now an industry standard, and you will find them used on most major web sites. However, you can always choose to disable cookies from being stored on your computer by changing your browser settings. Disabling cookies, however, may result in a limited experience of our functionality and services and in some cases may mean that we are unable to provide you with the services, or parts of the services, that you have requested. Some of our business partners whose content is incorporated into or linked to from our site may also use cookies. However, we have no access to or control over these cookies.

(b) Why do we collect it?

  • Cookies, when stored on your computer, facilitate the following:
  • Cookies allow us to save encrypted passwords and preferences for you so that you will not have to re-enter them the next time you visit. This means you will be able to enjoy conveniences such as login without having to type your login ID every time you visit ZUJI, or Travelocity India.
  • ZUJI and Travelocity India will be able to make improvements to our site and customize your Travelocity India experience using statistics collected from cookies. These statistics are anonymous to third parties, and may also be used by third party advertising companies, to monitor the effectiveness of advertisements.

7. Other Computer Information

(a) What information is collected?

  • Session information such as IP (Internet Protocol) addresses, browser types, operating systems are also used for system administration.

(b) Why do we collect it?

  • Session information will allow us to enhance your experience on ZUJI or Travelocity India by allowing us to troubleshoot technical issues as well as to improve our site usability.

1. Opt In and Opt Out Policy

While ZUJI and Travelocity India hope that you benefit from our product and service offers, at any time, you always have the option to opt out from participating in ZUJI's, or Travelocity India’s, product and service offers. For example, you can unsubscribe to our newsletter by following the steps below::

  1. Log on to "My Stuff" at www.travelocity.co.in
  2. Click on "Update your newsletter preferences"
  3. Input your email address and click on "Submit"
  4. Select "Not Subscribed" from the dropdown list under "Newsletter"
  5. Please allow for 48 hours for your profile to be updated

We also offer you the ability to opt out of receiving information of our products and services at the point of collection, by accessing and changing your personal information or by sending an e-mail to us at customercare@travelocity.co.in Please allow some time for this request to be processed.
