REGISTRATION
If you register on TradeBoss.com and TradeHolding.com B2B Network as a member, we collect and store all the information you enter. Our Membership Statistics, Industrial and Geographical classification statistics are derived from these information. This information may be used to target our registered members for new services and promotions. We also display certain information you enter on your specific company detail page including but not limited to: your company name, your first name and lastname and your job title.

If you register in TradeBoss.com and TradeHolding.com B2B Network, you agree to:
(a) provide true, accurate, current and complete information about yourself and the company you represent
(b) provide your e-mail address, postal address, phone, fax, website address (url) ONLY on site sections and fields where we specifically ask for these details

COOKIES
TradeBoss.com and TradeHolding.com B2B Network uses cookies only to maintain your information as you move throughout this site and facilitate your member activities. At no time is any personal information stored in a cookie, nor is any tracking information made available to other sites for any reason. Cookies are used only for the intended purpose and scope of TradeBoss.com and TradeHolding.com B2B Network.
