TrafficPunk Privacy Policy

When you provide us with personal information, it is handled according to the following policy:
 

  • Customer Feedback and Support.  
    Provides a     method for requesting product information and support services from 
    TrafficPunk.     It is also used to tell us how we can improve our products/services to better meet your needs.
     
  • Correspondence: 
    Correspondence received by TrafficPunk is kept strictly confidential and is not given or     sold to third parties.
    TrafficPunk does not send unsolicited email advertisements under any circumstance and is not responsible for the actions of its affiliates.
    If you feel an affiliate has abused our Zero-Tolerance spam policy, please contact us by forwarding the offending email.
     
    We will contact you with updates, offers and relevant information as well as a newsletter as part of our service. If you do not wish to receive these, then you can delete your account in the main member's area at anytime.
     
  • Double Opt-In:
    After joining TrafficPunk, you will receive a confirmation email containing your login information. To activate your membership, you must verify and acknowledge that you joined.
                
  • Personal Information:
    Sensitive information is not gathered or stored at TrafficPunk.com.
    Authorized third parties     such as payment processors, may need access to some of your personal information.
    If we need to ship a product to you, we must share your name and address with shipping companies, etc.
      
  • Linked web sites:
    TrafficPunk facilitates links and advertisements for its members. 
    Since we do not control the content of these web sites, we encourage you to review the privacy policies posted on these (and all) third party sites.
    These third party websites may or may not contain cookies for end-user data retrieval purposes.
     
  • Cookies:
    TrafficPunk does use cookie data so that the site can function properly by confirming when you are logged into or out of this website.
