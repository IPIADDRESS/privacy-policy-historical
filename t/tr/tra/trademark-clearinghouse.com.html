Privacy Notice

WHO WE ARE

CHIP S.A. (with registered office at: 2, rue Jean Fischbach, 3372 Leudelange, Luxembourg) operates as data controller in charge of the Personal Data processing in relation to the Trademark Clearinghouse and the Trademark Clearinghouse website (www.trademark-clearinghouse.com) (‘our “website’”), with the support of Deloitte in the capacity of data processors in accordance with appointment of Deloitte by ICANN as Clearinghouse Verification Provider.. Deloitte Enterprise Risk Services (a department of Deloitte Bedrijfsrevisoren BV ovve CVBA) (with registered office at: Berkenlaan 8b, 1831 Diegem, Belgium) will serve as the authenticator and validator service provider.

The Trademark Clearinghouse consists of two primary functions: (i) the authentication of contact information and verification of Trademark Records and (ii) the storage of such Trademark Records in a database in order to provide information to the new gTLD registries to support the providing of NORNs (Notification of Registered Names).

The Clearinghouse Website is designed to provide you with more information in relation to the Clearinghouse Verification Services, a part of the Trademark Clearinghouse: the central repository for verified brands for the purpose of protecting brands in ICANN’s new gTLD program.

This Privacy Notice is only applicable to the processing of Personal Data by CHIP and Deloitte and covers the processing of Personal Data our website. This Privacy Notice is not a contract between you and CHIP or Deloitte or any other party involved in the Trademark Clearinghouse and is not intended to create any legal rights. The purpose of this Privacy Notice is only to inform you about our processing of your Personal Data.  Capitalized terms used herein have the meanings given to them in the Terms and Conditions for Trademark Agents or Trademark Holders, as applicable.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We will collect and process your Personal Data, including your name, the name of your organization, your contact information, your trademark information, whether you are a Trademark Holder or Trademark Agent, your payment information and any other information necessary to effectuate the purposes of the Trademark Clearinghouse.

We will keep your Personal Data during the time your contract is effective, and as long as required by applicable laws (e.g., due to tax requirements).

WHY WE USE YOUR PERSONAL DATA

We will use your personal data only for the following purposes:

  • Sending newsletters about our services, if you have consented to receive such newsletters;
  • Answer the questions you may ask us about the Trademark Clearinghouse through the contact form;
  • Giving you access to the  Clearinghouse User Interface and allowing you to submit Trademark Records on the  Clearinghouse User Interface in order to receive the Verification Services;
  • Perform authentication and validation services, which includes checking if the information provided is accurate and correct and meets the Trademark Clearinghouse Guidelines;
  • Processing financial transactions such as the payment of fees;
  • Comply with the Terms and Conditions of Trademark Agents or Trademark Holders, as applicable;
  • To coordinate with the Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers (see www.icann.org), registries, registrars and registrants concerning the registration of a domain name that matches a Trademark Record in the Trademark Clearinghouse; and
  • Offer you customer support.

WHO RECEIVES YOUR PERSONAL DATA

In order to perform the services we offer, we may have to disclose your Personal Data to the Database Provider, the party appointed by ICANN to operate and manage the central database for storage of Trademark Records that have achieved Activation (i.e. when we have determined that a Trademark Record meets the Eligibility Requirements as stated in the Trademark Clearinghouse Guidelines).

We may disclose your Personal Data to ICANN, registries, registrars and domain name registrants in order to effectuate the purposes of the Trademark Clearinghouse.

We may have to disclose your Personal Data in other situations, such as, but not limited to, to comply with legal or regulatory requirements or obligations in accordance with applicable law, a court order, administrative process or judicial process or subpoena.

We will also disclose your Personal Data to a new provider of the Trademark Clearinghouse, or our successors and assigns in connection with a merger or sale of our assets.

TRANSFERS OF PERSONAL DATA TO OTHER COUNTRIES

We will only transfer your Personal Data abroad to the extent necessary to achieve the purposes described in Why We Use Your Personal Data and as described in Who Receives Your Personal Data and will ensure that your transferred Personal Data will enjoy the same level of protection as provided by us.

YOUR RIGHT OF ACCESS

You have the right to access your Personal Data in order to ensure that your Personal Data is still accurate and up to date.

You have the right to ask us if we hold any Personal Data on you by making an access request. If we hold information about you, we will explain what we have, why we’re holding it and who it could be disclosed to. You may also request to have a copy of the information in an intelligible form.

To make such a request, please see the section How to Contact Us..

If we hold Personal Data about you that is incorrect, you may request for us to correct any mistake in the same manner.

HOW WE SECURE YOUR PERSONAL DATA

We recognize the importance of information security, and are constantly reviewing and enhancing our technical, physical, and logical security rules and procedures. We have established technical means, procedures and policies to preserve the integrity, confidentiality and privacy of your Personal Data, during its collection, use, transfer and storage while under our control.
