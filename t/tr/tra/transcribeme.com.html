Privacy Policy

The following Privacy Policy applies to our data collection and use practices relating to your use of the TranscribeMe services, our website and our other products and services (collectively, the “TranscribeMe Services”). As a TranscribeMe customer or a user of our smartphone app, you agree to this Privacy Policy and also to any further modifications made to this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy does not apply, however, to the co-branded pages of our partners, affiliates and service providers. The terms “TranscribeMe”, “we”, “our”, or “us” refer to TranscribeMe Inc. or Audioscribe, Ltd.

WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Depending on the way you use TranscribeMe Services, you may provide us with your name, email address, credit card information or other payment information. When you request information or content from TranscribeMe and supply information or content that personally identifies you or allows us to contact you, you agree to share that information with TranscribeMe, its agents, representatives, and affiliates.

On visiting our website or use the TranscribeMe Services or smartphone apps, we use standard Web server log files to automatically record information, including your URL, IP address, browser type, and language, specific search requests, the date and time of your request, and the results of your searches. We also collect personal information when you register for the TranscribeMe Services during registration or voluntarily provide such information. For providing better user experience and customizing content for you, we may combine personal information that we collect from you with information that we collect from other end users or third parties.

We also use cookies and obtain certain types of information when your Web browser accesses www.TranscribeMe.com. “Cookies” are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer’s hard drive. To speed up the user navigation and keep track of items, our website may use cookies to store pertinent user information during a session. To have better control on browser cookies, you can prevent your browser from accepting new cookies and receive a notification when you receive new cookies or disable cookies completely. You can find all this information on the help section of the toolbar on most browsers.

WHAT DO WE USE YOUR INFORMATION FOR?

Any information we collect from you may be used in any of the following ways:

• E-mail Newsletters: The email address you use for signing up for TranscribeMe services may be used to send you information related to your orders, our company’s news, product updates, monthly newsletters, etc.

• Order processing requirements: We use your information to deliver you the best customer-service experience while using our TranscribeMe Services. Your information will not be sold, exchanged or transferred.

• Improving customer experience: Your information helps us to better understand your needs and requirements , which allows us to better respond to your individual needs and customer-service requests.

• Improving our services, products, and website. We use your information to improve user experience on our website and our services.

DO WE DISCLOSE ANY INFORMATION TO OUTSIDE PARTIES?

Your information is safe with us. We don’t indulge in selling, trading or transferring your personally identifiable information to outside parties. However, this does not include trusted parties who assist in operating our business, services, as long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential by signing Non Disclosure Agreements (NDAs).

Some other instances where we may also release your information would be when we have to comply with the law, enforce our website/business policies, or protect our or others rights, property, or safety. However, non-personally identifiable visitor information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other uses.

CHANGES TO OUR PRIVACY POLICY

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will update the Privacy Policy modification date below. This policy was last modified on 20th of October 2014.

GET IN TOUCH
