TranscribeMe’s Privacy Policy

The following Privacy policy applies to our data collection and use practices relating to your use of the TranscribeMe services, our Web site and our other products and services (collectively, the “TranscribeMe Services”). As a TranscribeMe customer or a user of our smartphone app, you agree to this Privacy policy and also to any further modifications made to this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy does not apply, however, to the co-branded pages of our partners, affiliates and service providers. The terms “TranscribeMe”, “we”, “our”, or “us” refer to TranscribeMe or Audioscribe, Ltd.

What information do we collect?

Depending on the way you use TranscribeMe service, you may provide us with your name, email address, credit card information or other payment information. When you request information or content from TranscribeMe and supply information or content that personally identifies you or allows us to contact you, you agree to share that information with TranscribeMe, its agents, representatives and affiliates.

On visiting our Web site or use the TranscribeMe Services or smartphone apps, we use standard Web server log files to automatically record information, including your URL, IP address, browser type and language, specific search requests, the date and time of your request, and the results of your searches. We also collect personal information when you register for the TranscribeMe Services during registration or voluntarily provide such information. For providing better user experience and customizing content for you, we may combine personal information that we collect from you with information that we collect from other end users or third parties.

We also use cookies and obtain certain types of information when your Web browser accesses www.TranscribeMe.com. “Cookies” are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer’s hard drive. To speed up the user navigation and keep track of items, our web site may use cookies to store pertinent user information during a session. To have better control on browser cookies, you can prevent your browser from acceptive new cookies, receive a notification when you receive new cookies or disable cookies completely. You can find all this information on the help section of the toolbar on most browsers.

What do we use your information for?

The information collection when you sign up for TranscribeMe services could you be used for the following purposes: Any of the information we collect from you may be used in one of the following ways:

  • E-mail Newsletters: The email address you use for signing for TranscribeMe services may be used to send you information related to your orders, receive company news, product updates, monthly newsletters, etc.
  • Order processing requirements: We use your information to deliver you the best customer service experience while using our TranscribeMe service. Your information will not be given to any other company for any reason whatsoever, without your consent. It will not be sold, exchanged or transferred.
  • Improving customer experience: Your information helps us in understand your requirements and needs better. This helps us to better respond to your individual needs and respond to your customer service requests in a better way.
  • Improving our service/product/website: We utilize your information to improve user experience on our website and our services.

Do we disclose any information to outside parties?

Your information is safe with us. We don’t indulge in selling, trading or transferring your personally identifiable information to outside parties. However, this does not include trusted parties who assist in operating our business, services, as long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential by signing Non Disclosure Agreements (NDAs). Some other instance where we may also release your information would be when we have to comply with the law, enforce our website/business policies, or protect ours or others rights, property, or safety. However, non-personally identifiable visitor information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other uses.

Changes to our Privacy Policy

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will update the Privacy Policy modification date below.
