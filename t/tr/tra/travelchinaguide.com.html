Privacy & Security
TravelChinaGuide Respects Your Privacy


Last updated: June 4, 2003

TravelChinaGuide is committed to protecting your privacy and pledges to earn your trust. Any information you give us will be devoted solely to the enhancement your travel.

All personal information you give us remains confidential. We will neither sell nor disclose any information that identifies you to a third party without your prior consent.

Your Personal Information

TravelChinaGuide does not collect any personal information about you other than that you specifically provide. For example, should you use our site and provide us with material or copy by means of the China Travel Forum, travelogues or volunteer suggestions, we may obtain information such as your e-mail or home address (if provided). This is intended to enable us to take all necessary precautions to ensure the integrity of anything you may submit to us. All your data will be stored in our secure databases. Access to that information is strictly controlled and limited to certain individuals within TravelChinaGuide.

Please remember that information you share in the public areas on TravelChinaGuide will be in the public domain. For example, in Travel Gossip (a column where you can share your travel experiences with others). For that reason you are advised to exercise extreme caution before deciding to disclose any information of a personal nature.

Your Questions and Comments

TravelChinaGuide is committed to protecting your privacy. We encourage you to send us comments or questions regarding this policy. Please contact us.
