Privacy Policy

The TravelisFree.com privacy policy:
*What information is being collected and how will it be used?
- In the box titled “email” your email address is taken simply so that I can respond to your question(s). For nothing else and it will shared with no one else.
- In the box titled “name” your name is taken. Again, I only plan on responding to your email(s). The name is not important. You can use an alias if you wish.  Your name will not be shared with anyone.
* Who else do we give your information too?
- No one. It will not be given to another person, group or company. Again, it’s just for responding to questions.
*How to modify or opt out?

- In regards to the Contact page: There’s nothing to opt out of. You will not be added to a list or spammed in anyway.

- In regards to the subscription page you can unsubscribe by contacting me at drew@travelisfree.com or by the unsubscribe option you would get in the emails you originally subscribed to.

Please note that I, TravelisFree.com has financial relationships with some of the merchants mentioned here.  TravelisFree.com may be compensated if consumers choose to utilize the links located throughout the content on this site and generate sales for the said merchant.
