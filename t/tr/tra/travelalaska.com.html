Privacy Policy 

We respect your right to online privacy while using our web site. Our Privacy Policy is shared with you because we are committed to protecting your privacy while using our site.

Information Collected on TravelAlaska.com

We may from time to time collect anonymous information regarding the use of our site, which may be used by our company, or shared with other business partners. This information does not personally identify you, but may be used by us in the aggregate to improve services, or to market our products. This anonymous information is collected through traffic data reports and other resources, generally using technical identifiers and codes used to identify computers. When requesting information, the option to receive additional information is presented. If you opt-in by providing your address and/or email address this information may be made available to site advertisers through a Mailing Label or E-mail Leads program. All emails distributed through the Email Leads program will be sent by the State of Alaska, or an authorized agent of the State. Your email address will never be distributed to a third party for re-use.

How Your Personal Information is Stored

Your personal information is stored in secure operating environments that are not available to the public. Your personal information is only accessible by selected company employees and contractors who have access to selected databases, which are further protected by ID and password requirements.
