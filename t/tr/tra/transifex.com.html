Privacy Policy

Indifex operates several websites including Indifex.com and transifex.net. It is Indifex’s policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our websites.

Website Visitors

Like most website operators, Indifex collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Indifex’s purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Indifex’s visitors use its website. From time to time, Indifex may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

Indifex also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses. Indifex does not use such information to identify its visitors, however, and does not disclose such information, other than under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information, as described below.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain visitors to Indifex’s websites choose to interact with Indifex in ways that require Indifex to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that Indifex gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up for an account at transifex.net to provide a username and email address. Those who engage in transactions with Indifex – by purchasing access to the transifex.net paid Services, for example – are asked to provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to process those transactions. In each case, Indifex collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor’s interaction with Indifex. Indifex does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

Aggregated Statistics

Indifex may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. Indifex may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, Indifex does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information

Indifex discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on Indifex’s behalf or to provide services available at Indifex’s websites, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Some of those employees, contractors and affiliated organizations may be located outside of your home country; by using Indifex’s websites, you consent to the transfer of such information to them. Indifex will not rent or sell potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information to anyone. Other than to its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations, as described above, Indifex discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only when required to do so by law, or when Indifex believes in good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the property or rights of Indifex, third parties or the public at large. If you are a registered user of an Indifex website and have supplied your email address, Indifex may occasionally send you an email to tell you about new features, solicit your feedback, or just keep you up to date with what’s going on with Indifex and our products. We primarily use our development- or other blogs to communicate this type of information, so we expect to keep this type of email to a minimum. If you send us a request (for example via a support email or via one of our feedback mechanisms), we reserve the right to publish it in order to help us clarify or respond to your request or to help us support other users. Indifex takes all measures reasonably necessary to protect against the unauthorized access, use, alteration or destruction of potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information.

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Indifex uses cookies to help Indifex identify and track visitors, their usage of Indifex website, and their website access preferences. Indifex visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Indifex’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of Indifex’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

Privacy Policy Changes

Although most changes are likely to be minor, Indifex may change its Privacy Policy from time to time, and in Indifex’s sole discretion. Indifex encourages visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.
