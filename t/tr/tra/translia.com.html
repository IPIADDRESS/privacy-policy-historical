How We Do It

Pause
1 / 1

Translating, editing, and proofreading

We only hire the qualified professional translators. They are very experienced and skillful. However, even very experienced translators inevitably make mistakes during translation. Where a single round of translation is not enough, we improve it through additional editing and proofreading, once for Silver and twice for Gold. As people are inefficient in finding out their own mistakes, we arrange different translators to perform translating, editing, and proofreading tasks in sequence. This brings superior quality. We call it Alternating TEP™ - Translating (T), editing (E), and proofreading (P).

Better and faster

We automated the business process like quote, order and delivery. This gives our translators more time to do translation while making the whole turnaround time quicker. We further shorten the turnaround time with a set of collaborative translation technologies (US patent pending No. 12/783.568), which accelerate translation by enabling multiple translators to seamlessly work together in translating the same article or even paragraph at the same time, with fluency and consistency ensured. This means our clients can receive their translations earlier even we apply additional editing and proofreading for better quality.

Save without sacrificing quality

We pay our translators well for their good jobs. However this doesn't mean our clients have to pay more. Instead they get more for less because we cut our operational expenses to minimum through business process automation. And we pass the savings onto them. We do it so well that our translation rates are very competitive even we only hire the best translators and apply the most strict QA process in the trade. If you are a price-sensitive buyer, we encourage you to do your own comparison shopping. We're sure you won't find better prices for the same level of service anywhere else.
