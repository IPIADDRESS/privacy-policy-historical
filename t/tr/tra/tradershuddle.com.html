Privacy Policy

Details

Tradershuddle.com shares your concerns about your privacy and the protection of your personal information online. We are committed to treating personal information we obtain through our website with respect for you privacy concerns, and in accordance with our privacy policy.

General Purpose of this Privacy Policy

Tradershuddle.com treats with utmost confidentiality and care the Personally Identifiable Information (PII) that is collected about you when you visit our web site.

What is Personally Identifiable Information (PII)?  PII is information we can use to identify you as an individual. PII includes your name, address, telephone number and any other information that is connected with your identity.

Information we collect from our subscribers
In order to access some of the specialized site features and receive the services provided by TradersHuddle.com, you must register or subscribe with TradersHuddle.com.  Doing so requires that we collect PII from you.  This may include your name, email address, city, state and zip code, and credit card information.

Website usage information
TradersHuddle.com automatically collects IP addresses and website usage information from you when you visit our site. This information helps us evaluate how our users navigate and use our site.

Use of PII
TradersHuddle.com uses PII collected from you in the following ways:

1) To complete the subscription process;
2) To customize the content and advertisements on our site for each customer;
3) To improve our site’s design and content, and enhance our customer service;
4) To contact clients about special offers, products or services that may be of interest to them;
5) To send you information related to your service subscription;

Information sharing and disclosure
TradersHuddle.com will never share or disclose PII about you without your consent.

TradersHuddle.com utilizes third party service providers to provide products, services or functions on our behalf (such as sending emails or processing credit cards). We require these service providers to agree to maintain the confidentiality of our clients’ PII and not to use that PII for any reason except to carry out the purpose(s) for which they were contracted.
