Transparent Language is absolutely committed to protecting your privacy on-line, and to providing you with a safe and secure environment for on-line interaction.

This privacy policy explains what information we collect from our web site visitors and how we use that information to improve your experience with Transparent Language products and on-line services.

What information does Transparent Language collect?

Ordering information is required when purchases are made through our on-line store or other Transparent Language order sites. During the ordering process, we ask you to supply information such as your name, mailing address, and credit card information. This information is used solely to process and fulfill your order and to send you information about your order.

What does Transparent Language use your information for?

Transparent Language's purpose for collecting personal information is to process your order and to keep you informed of special offers. This includes interactive communications, on-line shopping, and many other types of services, many of which are completely free to you. You can update your personal information or opt out of any feature at any time.

Does Transparent Language share information about you with other companies?

Absolutely not. Transparent Language does not share any of your information with other companies.

Security

This site is secured by Verisign, which allows your data to be securely encrypted. All of the information that you supply during the ordering process is collected through a secure server which ensures that the data cannot be seen by others.
