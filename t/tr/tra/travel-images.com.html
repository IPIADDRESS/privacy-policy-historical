Travel-Images.com has created this privacy policy within our firm commitment to privacy and data protection. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for the pages under the main domain http://www.travel-images.com.  We reserve the right to change this policy at any time without previous notice. Any new version of this policy will be made available on this site.

Information Automatically Logged
We do not log IP addresses or other demographic data on our visitors.

Cookies
Our Site does not use cookies. See more under 'Advertisers'.

Forms
Some of our site's forms require users to send us contact information (like their name or email) for orders or information requests. This information is not used for any other purpose. Contact information from the forms is not shared with other companies. This information is only used on an aggregate basis.

External Links
This site contains links to other sites. Travel-Images.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

Public Forums
This site makes message boards and guest books available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.

Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control.

Advertisers

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. We use Interest-based advertising.

Advertiser profiling opt-out - behavioral advertising
To opt-out from such information collection and use please use the Network Advertising Initiative / NAI's opt-out page.To opt out of an NAI member's behavioral advertising program, simply check the box that corresponds to the company from which you wish to opt out. Alternatively, you can check the box labeled "Select All" and each member's opt-out box will be checked for you. Opting out of a network does not mean you will no longer receive online advertising. It does mean that the network from which you opted out will no longer deliver ads tailored to your Web preferences and usage patterns.

Judicial orders
Travel-Images.com only recognizes jurisdiction to Portuguese and European Union courts. We reserve the right to disclose information under a direct court order.
