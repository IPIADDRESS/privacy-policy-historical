Privacy Policy

What does this policy apply to?

This privacy policy of Trickster Cards, Incorporated (“Trickster Cards”) covers how we collect and use information acquired as part of your use of the Trickster Cards apps and websites (collectively “services”) created by us which link to this policy.

By installing or using services linking to this policy, you consent to the terms and practices outlined herein.

What information is collected?

We collect anonymous information including crash statistics, error logs, types of hardware and browsers used, locale, and the timestamp of server requests.

We also collect personal information such as names, contact information, third party account IDs, and a history of played games when you create and sign in with an account using one of our services.

How is the collected information used?

We use collected information to help improve our services. For example, crash statistics and error logs are used to help identify and fix problems with services. Similarly hardware, browser, locale, and timestamp information is used to improve testing of new features and fixes. Lastly contact information and the history of played games are used to report statistics to users and to facilitate re-connecting them with other users they've played with before.

We DO NOT sell or share collected information with third parties.

What about information collected by third parties?

Although we do not share information with third parties, third parties can collect their own information when used to access services provided by us.

Please refer to the privacy policies of relevant third parties to understand what data they collect and how it is used.

Will this policy change?

This policy may be updated by us periodically. You are encouraged to check this policy regularly to ensure you are aware of the latest terms. Continued use of services provided by us constitutes an acceptance of these changes.
