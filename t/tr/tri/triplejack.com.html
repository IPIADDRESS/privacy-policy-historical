Triplejack  will not collect personal information other than when it is willingly and knowingly provided to us by you. To utilize all Triplejack.com has to offer, you will need to make sure your browser is set up to accept all cookies.

Please check this page periodically for changes. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post any changes on this page so that you are always aware of the information that we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.
