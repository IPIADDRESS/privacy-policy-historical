Information that we may collect from you

We may collect and process the following data about you, including:

information that you provide by filling in forms on our site or apps. We may also ask you for information when you enter a competition or promotion sponsored by us, and when you report a problem with our site or apps;

a record of any correspondence with you;

details of which of our services you are interested in;

information obtained from cookies, as to which please see below.

Email addresses

We do not want you to receive unwanted email from us. We try to make it easy for you to opt out of any service that you have asked to receive. If you sign up to our email newsletters we will only share such information as explained in the section below entitled “disclosure of your information”.

Email addresses are collected via our site, apps, co-registration partners and third party marketing lists and promotions. Users can to sign up to receive our newsletter through the site. After signing up, we will send a confirmation email to the email address provided to ensure the address is correct. We are clearly named at the point of collection and in any subsequent email.

We use email addresses to send you important messages about your account, as well as offers and information which we think are of interest to you, including our weekly Travel Deals Alert Newsletter and occasional mailings to assist in market research.

We sometimes run competitions with our partners and competition entries may require you to provide an email address. These email addresses may be shared with the partner running the competition and their use of your information will be subject to their privacy policy. We try to make it very clear when this is the case, and we disclaim any responsibility for subsequent use or distribution of email addresses by our partners.

If you no longer wish to receive our newsletter and promotional communications, you may opt out of receiving them by following the instructions included in each newsletter or communication, by emailing us via our Feedback page or by managing your privacy settings in your account. Alternatively, please visit our Newsletter Unsubscribe to be removed from future communications.

If we decide to change our email practices, we will post those changes to this privacy policy, the homepage of our site, apps, and other places that we deem appropriate so that you are aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it.

Where we store your personal data

All personal data you provide to us is stored on our servers. At present we have servers located within the EEA (European Economic Area).

The data that we collect from you may be transferred to, and stored at, a destination outside the European Economic Area (“EEA”). The transfer may be to another member of the TripsShop, or to third parties as set out in this policy. It may also be processed by staff operating outside the EEA who work for us or for one of our suppliers. By submitting your personal data, you agree to this transfer, storing or processing. We will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this privacy policy.

to improve our site, apps and services including by analyzing survey results;

to manage our business and protect the quality of the site, apps, and services, including analytics and statistical research on the use of our site or apps;

to create aggregated and anonymized reports to identify trends amongst our users which we may share with third parties;

to perform functions as otherwise described to you at the point of collection;

to provide you with advertising based on your activity on our sites and applications and on third party sites and applications.

where we use third party vendors to assist us in providing our products or services we may transfer your information to such vendors who have entered into a contract with us that protects the confidentiality of your information and permits the vendor to use it only as necessary to fulfill its contract. These services may include providing customer service, technical and operational support, email services, and marketing assistance, performing business and sales analysis, billing and payment processing (if we ever accept payments from our users), and supporting contests, sweepstakes, surveys and other features offered through our site or apps; if we are under a duty to disclose or share your personal data in order to comply with any legal obligation, court order, regulatory or governmental request, or in order to enforce or apply any of our terms and conditions, including our Website Terms of Use; or to protect the rights, property, or safety of us, our customers, or others. The security of your personal information, including your email address and postcode, is important to us. If you have any questions about the security on our website, please contact by us via our Feedback page and one of our designated correspondents will respond to your query.

You have the right to ask us not to process your personal data for marketing purposes. If you follow a link to any of these websites, please note that these websites have their own privacy policies and that we do not accept any responsibility or liability for these policies. Please check these policies before you submit any personal data to these websites.

The Act gives you the right to access information held about you. Your right of access can be exercised in accordance with the Act. Log Files and Tracking

Log files and Javascript tracking are used for general web analytics such as counting unique users, visitors and page impressions. Cookies

Our website uses cookies. A cookie is a small file of letters and numbers that we put on your computer if you agree. They allow us to distinguish you from other users of our site, which helps us to provide you with a good experience when you browse our website and also allows us to improve our site and apps. For more information on cookies, visit www.cookiecentral.com.
