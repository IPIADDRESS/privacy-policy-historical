Privacy Policy

Secure Transactions-Privacy Policy

The privacy policy is very important to us.
Your name, your e-mail address, your address, your telephone number and fax as well as any number of your credit card is necessary information in order for your reservation be obtained.

Your personal data is entered when you make a reservation or purchase through our website, by electronic mail (e-mail) or by phone. When booking, your personal data is saved and stored by us. This information is exclusively used to facilitate the booking process. In no case will your data will be transferred to third persons other than associated companies selected by you in order for us to hold your reservation and perform other requests made by you.

Additional use of your information are adapted within our services by sending you offers and information of which may personally interest you, and are suited to your needs. There is an option to notify us at whatever time that you don’t wish to receive such offers and services any further.

We use the specialized SSL (Secure Socket Layer) program, which is one of the most up to date security programs in the contemporary market. In case the payment is processed by credit card we transmit your card details in encrypted form. This method provides greater security than transmitting such information via phone.

We note, however, that under present technical means and capabilities, it is not possible to guarantee the absolute security of data transferred via the Internet. The user is exclusively responsible for transfer of data via Internet.

To improve the use of the tripsta.co.uk website, we use cookies as do most companies providing internet services. Cookies are part of the browser program and store up data in order for your hard disk to process our website. This allows us to recognize your IP address the next time you use the tripsta.co.uk website. You can use our website without cookies, but the website is easier to use with cookies enabled. Most browsers automatically accept cookies. You can certainly remove all cookies any time from your hard disk. You can also modify the settings of your browser to not accept cookies without your consent. Enabling cookies is not linked to your personal information in any way.

Third party vendors, including Google, also use cookies to show ads based on a user's previous visit to our website. The user may choose to not receive cookie files from Google through their ad opt page http://www.google.com/privacy/ads.
