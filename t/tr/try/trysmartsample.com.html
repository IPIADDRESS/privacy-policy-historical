Kindly note these FAQs

Can I create multiple accounts?
To enable better delivery, tracking, and feedback processing, we request you to create a single profile with accurate information about yourself. We will work to ensure that you can try as many products. In the interest of providing our corporate partners with accurate demographic and generic member information, we would be constrained to deactivate any multiple accounts of a single user.

Do I have to complete my profile?
The information you provide during signup is key for us to understand whether the products sent to you to try are, in effect, suited to your needs, wants and desires. During registration, we urge you to provide maximum and accurate information, including your address, email, whether you are a woman or a man, so that we can send you relevant product choices.

Can I invite friends to join the program?
Referring your friends is the best way to enhance your (and their) product experience. Discussions with friends and family can be contributed to your feedback via emailing videos or photos taken during the tests to help@trysmartsample.com

Forgot your username or password?
To restore access to your smart sample account, your registered email address will receive a link to help create a new password.

I have certain changes in my profile information, how do I update it?
It is important to keep your profile information up to date as it impacts delivery timeline and user experience with our services. Keep your address and contact information updated so that products can be delivered directly and on time. You can use the "my account" section once logged into the site to update your information.

Not received the product. Now what?
We request you to log a complaint using our website to highlight the delayed receipt of the product. Products are given on First come first basis. You need to request through smart sample website in order to receive sample products. Also, before logging your complaint, we request you to check your profile information and ensure that it is complete.
