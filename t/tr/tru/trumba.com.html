Introduction to Privacy Policy

The Trumba Service is a web-based software application designed to help families, individuals, and colleagues more efficiently plan and run their lives. Trumba grew out of our founders’ desire to organize family members involved in numerous school, sports, entertainment, and work activities.

Trumba Service is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy covers our treatment of personally identifiable information you voluntarily submit through your use of Trumba Service. This policy does not apply to the practices of companies or other entities that Trumba Service does not own or control (see Your Acceptance of Our Privacy Policy Terms). If you link to another web site from the Trumba web site, please consult that site's privacy policy.

This privacy policy describes the personal information we collect, why we collect it, and explains how it is used. By using the Trumba Service, you agree to the collection and use of your personal information as described below. You also agree to abide by the terms described in the Terms of Use agreement.

General Guidelines

  • We do not use the information you provide us in any manner other than that outlined in this privacy policy.
  • You can change personal account information or delete your account at any time.
  • We will store your account information on secure servers.
  • We will provide aggregate or combined user statistics to partners without disclosing any personally identifiable information.
  • We provide tools allowing you to export your calendar data at any time for use in other calendar applications.
  • We will treat your email address with the respect it deserves and never share, sell, or rent email addresses with any partner, marketing agency, or third parties.
  • We will not disclose your personal information, without notice, unless required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on us or the site; (b) protect or defend the rights or property of Trumba or the web site; or (c) act in exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of the site or the public.

Once your transaction is complete, you will be returned to the Trumba web site. Please read the Privacy Policy and Terms of Use of all third-party web sites.

Calendar Data

Any event information you enter in a published “public” calendar can potentially be viewed by anyone, unless you choose to restrict access via Trumba privacy settings. Trumba provides a number of different privacy levels allowing you to share none, some, or all of your calendar information with select groups or individuals. Unpublished calendars can also be shared in a manner specified by you.

You can also upload an image to published calendars. Your image will be displayed with your calendar and available to others according to the privacy settings that you choose.

Trumba Email Products

Trumba provides opt-in email subscriptions to help enhance the service we provide. These products include:

  • Welcome Email — Based on the email address you supplied when you registered, we will send you a one-time welcome email to verify your user name and password. You must activate your account through this email.
  • Unsubscribe — Out of respect for your privacy, we present the option to opt-out of email communications. We provide unsubscribe links within Trumba email products and within the web site. Please visit your account preferences page to opt out of email subscriptions.
  • Service-Related Announcements — We may send you service-related email announcements on rare occasions when it is necessary to do so. For instance, if our service is suspended for maintenance. Since these emails are not promotional in nature, you may not opt-out of these communications. If you do not wish to receive them, you have the option to deactivate your account.
  • Tell a Friend Email — If you choose to use our referral service to tell a friend about our site, or to forward event information, we will ask you for your friend’s name and email address. We will automatically send your friend a one-time email inviting him or her to visit the site. Trumba stores this information for the sole purposes of sending this one-time email, tracking the success of our referral program, storing email preferences, and for providing our members with a list of most frequently “mailed to” friends. Your friend may request to not receive future email from Trumba by clicking on a link included in the email sent to them.
  • Market Research Surveys — If you choose to opt in, we may occasionally contact customers who purchase products or services from Trumba Corporation for market research and/or customer satisfaction surveys or new product announcements offered by Trumba Corporation.

Cookies

A cookie is a unique identifier sent to a user's computer during a visit to a web site. Cookies can be used to remember information such as user name and password (so you don't have to enter them each time you visit our site).

If you choose the option “Automatically sign me in on this computer,” Trumba will use one persistent cookie to store your user name and password so you don’t have to enter it on repeat visits. You can remove persistent cookies by following directions provided in your Internet browser’s “Help” file. However if you configure your browser or choose to reject cookies, you may only use a portion of our web site including “Add To My Calendar” and viewing a public calendar. To best experience Trumba you need cookies enabled.

We use cookies to:

  • Automatically log you into the service when you return
  • Access your site preferences and deliver you the appropriate calendar content
  • Deliver content or advertising specific to you based on location and/or preferences

This privacy statement covers the use of cookies by www.trumba.com only and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.
