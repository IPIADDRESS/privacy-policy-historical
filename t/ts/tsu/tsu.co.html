5. Your Choices, and How to Remove Your Content and Information

We offer a variety of convenient self-serve options for updating your information and indicating your preferences. From the Settings page of your account (accessible by clicking on your name on the upper right corner of tsu.co):

  • To modify your profile information, click on Profile.
  • To modify account information, click on General.
  • To adjust your connections with external social networks, click on Social Accounts.
  • To adjust privacy settlings, click on Privacy.
  • To adjust web and email notifications, click on Notifications. To adjust mobile notifications, visit the Notification section of the Settings area within your tsū mobile app.

You can also:

  • Remove a post you made:
    • To remove your post via our website, scroll to the bottom of the post, click “More” and then click “Remove”.
    • To remove your post via our app, tap on the vertical three dots located at the bottom right of the post. A menu appears, and you can select the option to delete the post.
  • Remove a comment you made:
    • To remove your comment via our website, click “Delete” next to the comment.
    • To remove your comment via our app, tap the comment icon in the post and then click Delete.
  • Unlike something you previously liked by clicking “Unlike”.
  • Adjust the publication settings for each post by using the options available within the post (for example, by choosing Friends Only instead of the default Public)
  • Request deletion of your tsū account as described in the next section.

The removal and deletion options may not ensure complete or comprehensive removal of the content or information you posted on the Service. For example, it generally will not remove content or information that has been stored, shared or re-posted by other users and other third parties.
