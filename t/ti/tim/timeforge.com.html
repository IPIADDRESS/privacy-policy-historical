  • We transfer information about you if Resolution Systems or TimeForge is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, Resolution Systems will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

INFORMATION GATHERING AND USAGE

  • When you register for TimeForge we ask for information such as your name, company name, email address, billing address, credit card information. Members who sign up for the free account are not required to enter a credit card.

  • Resolution Systems uses collected information for the following general purposes: products and services provision, billing, identification and authentication, services improvement, contact, and research.

COOKIES

  • A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site’s computers and stored on your computer’s hard drive.

  • Cookies are required to use the TimeForge service.

  • We use cookies to record current session information, but do not use permanent cookies. You are required to log-in to your TimeForge Project Site after a certain period of time has elapsed to protect you against others accidentally accessing your account contents.

DATA STORAGE

Although Resolution Systems owns the code, databases, and all rights to the TimeForge application, you retain all rights to your data.
DISCLOSURE

CHANGES

Resolution Systems may periodically update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your TimeForge primary account holder account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
