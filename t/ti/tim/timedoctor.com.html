Privacy Policy



Our Commitment To Data Security

Your privacy is imperative. TimeDoctor.com has developed this Privacy Statement to describe our practices of collecting and managing your information. We only collect and use your information through means allowed by law.

Types of information which TimeDoctor.com may collect

This Privacy Statement applies to all information collected or submitted on or through the Timedoctor.com website. The types of personal information collected at these pages are: Your name, email address, IP address where you entered your email address and name. If you sign up as a user of Timedoctor.com we will also allow you to upload your photo, address, credit card details and other applicable data relevant to paying for the subscription service.

Using Your Information

We do not oblige you to provide us your personal information and you agree that you are providing it to TimeDoctor.com voluntarily. We do not share, disclose or sell this information with outside parties or post any information publically without your written permission except to the extent necessary and as required by law.
In addition, we track your usage of our website to learn which information you visit or read the most, and which pages are least visited. This helps TimeDoctor.com measure the effectiveness of web site and software.
To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online. Credit card details are stored securely and all transmission of credit card and other details are handled by a secure https protocol.

Correcting Your Information

You can correct factual errors in your personally identifiable information by sending us a request that credibly shows error. To protect your privacy and security, we will also take reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting access or making corrections.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files sent through your browser and saved in your hard drive. TimeDoctor.com uses cookies to uniquely identify you and save the preferences you set from your previous visit into the site. You may choose to disable cookies by selecting this option on your browser. Disabling cookies may, however, disable some services activated using such technology.
