Privacy Policy

At Time2Track Holdings LLC (Time2Track), we value the privacy of information provided by visitors to our corporate websites. With that in mind, we have instituted the following policies concerning the collection and use of data.

Collected Information

Information about users may be collected through registration forms provided on our websites, including online surveys, discussion forums, opted-in email newsletters, and through the use of cookies. When web site users request pages, our server automatically recognizes and collects the domain name and IP address of visitors to our websites.

Use of Information

Time2Track takes every precaution to protect users’ information from unauthorized use. When users submit sensitive information via our websites, the information is considered confidential both on-line and off-line. We do not allow public access to the portion of the server that contains user information.

Email

Email addresses provided to us will only be used to send relevant and requested correspondence. No email addresses will be provided to third parties unless specifically requested through correspondence or opt-in opportunities. Upon request, Time2Track will remove users (and their information) from our database or permit them to “opt-out” of any further requested correspondence, such as email newsletters, that they had previously registered for.

IP Data

Our web server automatically recognizes and collects the domain name and IP address of visitors to our websites. In addition, we collect aggregate tracking information derived mainly from tallying page views throughout our sites, and information volunteered by the visitor, such as survey information and/or site registrations. The information we collect is used to improve the content of our web pages, customize the content and/or layout for each individual visitors, and for us to contact visitors about our relevant products and services or as requested.

Cookies

If a visitor has enabled cookies in a browser, we will send a cookie file that will only store a unique, random ID that is maintained throughout the session to track the pages visited or enhance the software presentation. This allows us to provide our site visitors with certain conveniences, such as delivering unique content and helping with lost passwords. This information is not shared or transmitted beyond the session.

About Our Policy

Time2Track makes every reasonable effort to enforce these policies and protect your information. Time2Track is not responsible for the content or the privacy policies of websites to which it may provide links or which it may host.

If you have questions about this privacy statement or your dealings with Time2Track, please do not hesitate to contact us.
