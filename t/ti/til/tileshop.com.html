  • Use of our Web Site, including the Design Studio

  • Viewing our online advertisements or emails
  • Use of our applications for mobile phones, tablets or other smart devices

Contact Information
We may collect the user names and names of customers and visitors to our Web Site. We also may collect shipping and billing addresses, email addresses, phone numbers, digital contact information and purchase histories.

Demographic Information
We may collect data about your age, gender, places you shop, reviews you give, and products and services you prefer.

Returns & Exchanges Information
If you return an item to our stores or request an exchange or refund, we may ask you to provide your name, address, email address, phone number and your driver’s license or other government issued ID. We use the information you provide, including information from your government issued ID, to process and track returns and exchanges, help detect and prevent fraud, and administer our fraud and loss prevention program. To learn more about our Returns & Exchanges Policy, click here.

Transaction Information
If you use our Web Site, we may collect location data obtained from your IP (Internet Protocol) address. We use this location data to help you find our nearest store to you, provide you with driving directions to our stores and provide available products and pricing at our stores near you. If a mobile application indicates that you are shopping in one of our stores, you may be provided with special information for in-store customers. Through the settings menu of your mobile device, you can control use of your device’s location information.

IP and Location Information
When you purchase products or services from us, we collect your transaction information, including information from your debit or credit card, PayPal account, gift card or check. If you apply for The Tile Shop credit card, we collect information related to your application.

Employment Information
If you apply for employment with us, you may provide us with certain personal information including information contained in a letter, resume, or similar employment-related materials, or provided in response to pre-screening questions.

Other Information
We may collect any of the data that you provide through our mobile applications, Web Site, or online forums. If you use our Web Site, in addition to your IP address, we may collect information such as the type of browser and operating system you are using. We also might keep track of the pages you visit, the website you came from, or the website you go to when you leave ours. COOKIES AND TRACKING
Cookies are small bits of data cached or stored on your computer based on Internet activity. We send one or more cookies to your computer or other device when you visit our Web Site. We may also use cookies in emails we sent to you. We and our online service providers use cookies to improve your experience on the Web Site and in our stores, the quality our Web Site, and the quality of other web sites of interest to you. We also use cookies to remind us of who you are and to customize our products, services, and marketing to the personal interests of you and others. We also use cookies to monitor individual activity in aggregate to improve our Web Site and marketing. In addition to cookies, we and our online service providers may use web beacons and other online tracking tools. The information we gather includes IP address, user language, the operating system, browser type, the presence/absence of “flash” plug-ins, screen resolution, connection type, and information that identifies the cookie. For example, we use cookies and other online tracking tools to:

  • Remember what items are in your Shopping Cart
  • Track new visitors to the Web Site
  • Collect geographic data determined by the IP address of your computer
  • Learn what site referred you to our Web Site
  • Recognize you when you return to our Web Site
  • Store your password if you have an account at tileshop.com
  • Enable you to use an expedited checkout, if you have created an account at tileshop.com
  • Study how our customers navigate through our Web Site and which products they request in Web Site searches
  • Provide correct pricing and advertising for the store location that you select
  • Provide you with enhanced online display advertising tailored to your interests. To opt out of this personalized display advertising from Tile Shop, click here.

CONTROLLING TRACKING TOOLS
Your browser may have some features for limited control of cookies. Certain browsers can be set to reject browser cookies, but browser settings may not control flash cookies. To control flash cookies, click here. Your mobile device may not give you the ability to control cookies. To block cookies on mobile applications, you need to delete the application from your mobile device. If you configure your computer to block cookies, you will limit the functionality we can provide when you visit our Web Sites (e.g. remembering what was in your shopping basket). However, if you configure settings to block or delete cookies, not all of the tracking that we have described in this policy will stop.

Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" feature that allows you to communicate a signal to websites that you do not want to your online activities tracked. These browser features are not uniform, and, therefore, we do have a system for a response to those signals at this time.

While we use industry standard means to protect our websites and your information, the Internet is not 100% secure. The measures we use are appropriate for the type of information we collect.
