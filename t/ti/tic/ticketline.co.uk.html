Privacy Policy

We know that you care how information about you is used and shared and we appreciate your trust in us to do that carefully and sensibly. This notice describes the privacy policy of Piccadilly Ticketline Ltd.

By visiting www.ticketline.co.uk and by registering and using this site you expressly consent and accept to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Data Protection

  • We will not send you unsolicited emails (spam).
  • We will not share your personal information for marketing without your consent.
  • Any personal information which we get from you or gather about you from your use of our site will be used to contact you by post, phone, email or SMS when necessary in connection with transactions entered into by you on the site.
  • Please note that by booking with Ticketline you are consenting to your personal information being shared with an event organiser or promoter or venue for the particular event for which you are buying tickets. You should contact them if you would prefer not to receive information from them. Information will only be passed to event organisers for certain events where this is requested.
  • Financial information that is collected from you is used to authorise payment and bill you. Your consent is given to provide your financial or personal information to those third parties necessary to process your transactions with us, such as credit card companies, banks and those companies that handle shipping and fraud protection.

Cookies

A cookie is a small file that is automatically issued to your computer when you enter this Site and which is stored by the browser on your computer's hard drive. Cookies identify your computer, not the individual user of your computer.

You can disable cookies or set your browser to alert you when cookies are being sent. Cookies can be deleted from your hard drive if you wish.

Security

Ticketline.co.uk has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. We use 128 bit industry standard Secure Server Software (SSL) for your transactions with us.

It encrypts all of your personal information, including credit card number, name, and address, so that it cannot be read as the information travels over the Internet.
