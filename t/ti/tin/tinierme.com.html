For information about browser settings, please contact your browser’s software manufacturer. In some cases, companies that advertise within the Service may also use cookies in accordance with their own policies.

    The Service may use a variety of methods for tracking purposes, including web beacons (also known as Internet tags, pixel tags and clear GIFs). Web beacons are small pieces of data that are embedded in images on the pages of websites to analyze traffic patterns, such as the frequency users visit various areas of the Service. These methods may involve transmitting information either directly to us or to another entity authorized by us to collect information on our behalf. We may also use these technical methods in HTML e-mails that we may send you to determine whether you have opened those e-mails or clicked on links in those e-mails. The information that we collect and share with web beacon technology is anonymous and not personally identifiable. It does not contain your name, address, telephone number, or email address.

The Analytics Vendors may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on the Analytics Vendors’ behalf. The Analytics Vendors will not associate your IP address with any other data held by the Analytics Vendor. By accessing or using the Service, you consent to the processing of data about you by one or more Analytics Vendors in the manner and for the purposes set out above.

Therefore, we are not required to maintain or disclose a list of the third parties that have received your personal information for marketing purposes during the preceding year. If you are a California resident and request information about how to exercise your third party disclosure choices you must send a request to privacypolicy-usa@gcrest.com. For such requests, you must put the statement “Privacy Policy Request” in the subject field of your email. For all requests, please include your name, street address, city, state, and zip code.
