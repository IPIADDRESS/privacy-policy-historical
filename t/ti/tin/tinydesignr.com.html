TD cannot be held liable for any offsite communications that result from your posting of personal information on our site. If you do not wish to be contacted offsite by other TD visitors, do not give your e-mail address or website address in the content when leaving a comment.

Use of Information Provided By User

We do not use the information provided by users, such as e-mail addresses and contents of e-mail, for any marketing purposes. Your personal information is not used by subsidiary organizations or businesses related to TD, unless clearly disclosed for specific situations.

Advertisers

The only information that they will be able to track is basic Website usage information; e-mail addresses are not passed on to advertisers for any reason even if they are provided to TD voluntarily.

Third party advertising

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads and collect information when users visit our site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements on our site, other websites and other forms of media about goods and services of interest to you.

The appearance of such links or sponsored content in this blog is determined by its informational or inspirational value and not by its commercial value to us. We will only promote products and services that we like and feel are beneficial to our audience.

Cookies

We use cookies to store visitor preferences, record user-specific information on what pages users access or visit, customize Website content based on visitor browser type or other information that the visitor sends.

Comment Policy

All comments are moderated and we reserve the right to delete comments for any reason. Common reasons for comments not being approved include:
