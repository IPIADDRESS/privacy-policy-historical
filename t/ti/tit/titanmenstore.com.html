Privacy Policy
TitanMen store is committed to protecting your privacy. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.

What this policy covers:
This policy details how we treat personal information, including your name, address, email address, or anything else that is not publicly available.

Information Collection and Use

General
  • TitanMen store collects personal information when you make a purchase.
  • When you make a purchase, we ask for your name, address, email address, and credit card information. We do this in order to complete your transaction and deliver the products you order.
  • TitanMen store keeps a record of your account, including your prior purchases in order to serve you better.
  • TitanMen store automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, cookie information and the page you request.
  • TitanMen store uses information for hte following general purposes: to fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, and to contact you with items we feel may interest you.
Minors
TitanMen store is committed to protecting minors, and ensuring that only adults who wish to receive our products or view our content have access to those. We employ strict age verification requirements. These requirements may cost us in sales, but we feel they are an acceptable cost of being good corporate citizens.

Information Sharing and Disclosure
TitanMen store does not rent, sell or share any personal information about you with anyone for any reason.

Cookies
Our site sets cookies on your browser in order to allow you to browse and shop our store more efficiently.

Your ability to Edit and Delete Your Account Information and Preferences
  • You can edit your account information at any time.
  • If we contact you via email, other than to confirm a purchase or to give you delivery information, we provide you with a link to indicate you prefer not to be contacted. Clicking such a link on one of our emails does not sign you up on any other list.
Confidentiality and Security
  • We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reaonably need access to that information to provide you with the products or services you order
  • We have physical, electronic and procedural safeguards that comply with and exceed federal regulations to protect personal information about you.
  • We employ third-party audits to review our security and test for vulnerabilities. To see our latest report, click here
If you have any questions or suggestions, please contact us!
