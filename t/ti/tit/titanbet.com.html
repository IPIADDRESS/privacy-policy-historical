Our Security & Privacy Policy

Security

At Titan Bet, we take security issues very seriously and aim to provide our players with the highest security standards possible. It is imperative to us that you feel your personal details are 100% secure before you start placing bets with us. If you have any questions regarding security issues, please contact our support team.

Information Protection
Titan Bet has adopted the most sophisticated technology to guarantee the security and protection of your privacy. Your account information and deposits are fully protected by state-of-the-art security systems, software and services.

Fraud Control
The security measures implemented by Titan Bet ensure the validity of each transaction you make, while the latest fraud control systems are utilized to protect your credit card information.

Encryption

Titan Bet uses Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology on all web pages that contain your personal information. SSL works by encrypting all the data by using complex mathematical algorithms, to ensure we maintain the highest possible level of web security and that all your information remains secure and private at all times.

Confidentiality

Any and all information received from players is held in the strictest of confidence by Titan Bet. Player information will never be forwarded to any organization for commercial or any other gain, save that required by law.

The privacy and security of all our players is of the utmost importance to Titan Bet. When playing at Titan Bet you can be assured that we will respect and protect your privacy - so that you are free to enjoy the best online gambling available. Below is a summary of our Privacy Policy.



Privacy Policy

The following statements disclose the information gathered by Titan Bet.

Account Creation Process
When you register an account with Titan Bet, you are asked to provide certain personal information in order to verify that you are legally entitled to bet and to avoid the registration of duplicate accounts. This information is kept strictly confidential for our own purpose. We do NOT share this information with any third-party company and/or organization.

This information includes a customer's email address, home address and other personal information. If you supply us with your personal details, the information will only be used for its intended purpose.

Web Site and Advertising System
As part of this website's operation and for our own statistical analysis of site traffic, our website automatically logs Internet IP addresses accessing our site. We do NOT log any e-mail address of web site visitors.

Our advertising system logs IP addresses in order to supply more accurate advertising features, such as language specific banners. Again, it does NOT log any email addresses.

Winnings
Your winnings and cash-outs are kept strictly confidential, and winnings information is stored in secure operating environments. Titan Bet keeps your winnings information private and does not provide this information to any third-party unless such information is required to be disclosed by law, regulation or any governmental or competent regulatory authority.

Email Addresses
We collect email addresses of those who communicate with us via email.

Email Addresses are NOT shared with any other organization for commercial nor non-commercial purposes. Email addresses will only be used to provide our customers with information regarding new updates, promotional material, and miscellaneous operations, such as deposits and withdrawals.

We are guided by a strict mail policy as detailed below:

Mail policy

We do not tolerate spam.
We received your consent for communication via email.
We send you promotional emails and updates until such time as you inform us that you no longer wish to receive them.

You may notify us of your wish to unsubscribe at any time by:

  • Clicking on the unsubscribe link shown in all Titan Bet emails and following the instructions.
  • Contacting us through online chat
  • Emailing us at support(put the 'at' sign here)titanbet.com

If you continue to receive promotional e-mails and updates or any other unsolicited communication from us after you have asked to unsubscribe, please report this to us at abuse(put the 'at' sign here)titanbet.com.




Use of Information

Your personal information, together with other information we hold, may be used for the following purposes:

  • Managing new and existing accounts
  • Processing player transactions including, but not limited to, deposits, withdrawals and bets
  • Improving our services to players and providing more personal service to individuals
  • Complying with all of our requirements under applicable law and regulations
  • Providing you with marketing and promotional information by post, email, telephone and other means available to us
