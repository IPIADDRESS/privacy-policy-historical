Your privacy is important to us. To protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our privacy practices and the ways your information is collected and used.

This notice applies to all information collected from users or submitted by users on our website. If you are a user of our website, including those users in the European Union Area , your continued use of the website signifies your consent to the information collection, use of cookies, our privacy policy and our terms of use.

Users in the European Union Area who do not consent to the information collection noted below, should discontinue use of our website until we are able to provide an opt-in and opt-out mechanism relating to the collection of personal information.

Personal information collected by us includes:

* Email Address (submitted by users who register for our website)
* IP Address (collected for all registered users)

We do not sell your data that we collect, or share your email address or IP address with outside parties. The information submitted by you or collected by us is used by the moderators of the website to facilitate quality control and to communicate with you as necessary.

In an effort to prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place certain physical, electronic, and managerial procedures intended to safeguard the information we collect online.

We allow third-party companies to serve ads and/or collect certain information when you visit our website. These companies may collect and use, among other data, click stream information, browser type, time and date, and the subject of advertisements clicked or scrolled over by you. During your visits to this and other websites, this information may be used to provide advertisements about goods and services likely to be of greater interest to you. These companies typically use a cookie or third party web beacon to collect this information. To learn more about this behavioral advertising practice, you can visit networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp.
