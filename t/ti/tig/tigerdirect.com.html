For each visitor to our various Websites, our Webserver(s) automatically recognizes only the consumer's domain name and internet address, but not the e-mail address (unless provided by the visitor).

We collect only the domain name and internet address, but not the e-mail address of visitors to our websites; except the e-mail addresses of those who post messages to our sites and the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail; we also aggregate information on what websites and pages consumers access or visit, and information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registrations and names & addresses from our catalog requests.

The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Websites, as well as used to notify consumers about updates to our Websites and products contained therein, used by us to contact consumers for other marketing purposes, and occasionally shared with other reputable organizations to help them contact consumers for their marketing purposes.

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by writing to us at the above address and telling us that you do not want to receive any e-mail marketing messages from our company. You will still receive your order information and notifications via e-mail.

From time to time, we make the e-mail addresses (when supplied by the visitor) of those who access our sites available to other reputable organizations whose products or services we think you might find interesting. If you do not want us to share your e-mail address with other companies or organizations, please let us know by writing to us at the above address and telling us that you do not want us to share your e-mail address with other companies.

If you supply us with your postal address on-line you may receive periodic mailings and catalogs from us with information on new products. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by writing to us at the above address. Also you may receive mailings from other reputable companies. You can, however, have your name put on our do-not-share list by writing to us at the above address and indicating that you do not want your name shared with other companies beyond TigerDirect or its parent. Please provide us with your exact name and address. We will be sure your name is removed from the list we share with other organizations.

Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers on-line may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding orders they have placed on-line. Please provide us with your correct phone number. We will be sure your name is removed from the list we share with other organizations.
