What information does TuneUp360 collect?

"Personal Information" is information that
(i) is recorded in any form;
(ii) is about you, or pertains to you; and
(iii) can be linked to you. Personal Information includes information like your name, address, e-mail address, or phone number, as well as other non-public information that is associated with the foregoing.


When does TuneUp360 store information?

We may store any information entered into the website by users of the website, or supplied to us by those with whom we do business. For example, you may be asked to provide personal information when you schedule an appointment for services; purchase products; contact TuneUp360 call centers; initiate a chat session; participate in a sweepstakes, promotion, or survey; and/or participate in other activities with TuneUp360 entities. Of course, if you are a service provider, you want to join us, you should supply your service information to us.

Use of Personal Information
In general, Personal Information is used by us
(i) to register you for our Services,
(ii) to respond to your requests and provide the Services to you,
(iii) to improve the features, performance and support of the Services, and
(iv) to offer you additional information, opportunities, and functionality. We may also use your Personal Information to provide you with promotional e-mails, should you request to receive such communications from us.

TuneUp360 personnel and any third party contractors working for or with TuneUp360 in providing the Service only get access to any of the customer information (including any Personal Information therein) on a need-to-know basis and are under contractual obligations to keep the confidentiality of such information.

How does TuneUp360 secure the personal information?

Security
Our Services help diagnose and resolve technology problems that you may have. TuneUp360 uses several types of software: the first type provides computer system information to TuneUp360 which helps us to identify, diagnose and resolve the technology problem that you have encountered and/or problems that may arise in the future, the second type generally consists of utilities and other tools to improve computer performance and help solve your personal technology problem, and the third type allows TuneUp360 Agents to remotely control your computer and modify its settings or software in our attempt to solve your technology problem. By ordering our Services, you are giving express permission to TuneUp360 to download and use the software described above (the "TuneUp360 Software"). Depending on the Services that you order, the TuneUp360 Software may allow us to temporarily take control of your computer and modify your computer settings to resolve your personal technology problem . For other Services, the TuneUp360 Software will report non-personal information about your PC's performance and configuration to you and to TuneUp360. For such Services, the TuneUp360 Software will run on a regular basis, but only after you elect to run the TuneUp360 Software and report your PC information to yourself and to TuneUp360. In every instance, TuneUp360 will solely use the TuneUp360 Software and the information that it provides to attempt to identify and resolve your existing and potential personal technology problem(s) and use reasonable efforts to limit its access of your Personal Information while performing the Services and only after you have given us permission to do so. You are responsible for and TuneUp360 has no liability for securing and backing up your computer network, all information stored on your computer and all software on your computer.
