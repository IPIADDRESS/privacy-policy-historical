our privacy pledge

Dear Valued Customer,

We understand and share your concern about privacy. We want to assure you that Tumi respects your privacy. Tumi does not sell, rent, or loan any of your personally identifiable information to any third party without your consent. When an order is placed, we collect basic information including your name, home address, phone, and email address. We use this information to confirm your order and to respond to any questions you may have. Where required, we also share this information with certain intermediaries (UPS, Cybercash) strictly to process and ship your order.

When you purchase products from tumi.com, we include you in our list to receive email updates about special offers, new products and services. We will only send you email communications that you expect. If for any reason you do not wish to receive this information, email us at info@Tumi.com, or reply to any of our emails and type "unsubscribe" in the subject line. You may opt-out of receiving future mailings at any time.

Tumi.com has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information that we collect. We utilize a Cisco firewall and use SSL encryption to keep this information secure. We use your Internet protocol address to help identify you and your shopping cart, and to gather broad demographic information. Our site uses cookies to keep track of your shopping cart and to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit.

If requested, we will provide information where required by law.

If you have questions about Tumi's privacy policy or have comments on how we can improve the tumi.com shopping experience, please do not hesitate to email us at info@Tumi.com. We appreciate hearing from you!
