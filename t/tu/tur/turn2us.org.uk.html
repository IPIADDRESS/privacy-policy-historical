Turn2us Website Cookie Policy

  • https://www.turn2us.org.uk/Turn2us-website-cookie-policy
  • 30/01/2018

The page gives an overview of how to manage your cookies

Since 26 May 2011 the law has stated that cookies can only be used by a website with the specific consent of its users. Like many other websites, we use cookies to deliver our online service . On your first visit to the site, a message will appear telling you that the Turn2us website uses cookies. You will only see this message once.

What are cookies?

"Cookies" are small files that are sent to your computer, tablet or mobile phone browser when you enter a website. These cookies are stored by the browser on your device. They can be used for a variety of purposes, such as:

  • identifying your previous visits to a website,
  • researching the most popular features of a website, or
  • letting you move between pages without asking you to log in multiple times.

How does Turn2us use cookies?

In general, Turn2us tries to use cookies only where it is strictly necessary to provide our service as requested by our users. For example, cookies can make it possible for us to identify and log in registered users of the website. We also use cookies to develop a clearer picture of which parts of this website are the most popular to inform the development of the service.Turn2us does not use cookies to serve targeted advertising.

You are not obliged to accept cookies. Most browsers accept cookies automatically, but you can set your browser to prevent this. If your browser’s preferences allow it, each website you visit is able to send a cookie to your browser.

How to reject cookies from Turn2us

Turn2us does not use cookies to collect personally identifiable information about you as a user of the site. However if you wish to restrict or block the cookies we use you can do this within your browser settings.

If you wish to reject cookies, please select the link for the browser you are using from the list below and follow the instructions to stop cookies being sent to you during your visit to the Turn2us website.

Please be aware that if you do disable cookies, certain services on this website may not be available to you. For example, your ability to access your MyTurn2us account may be impaired.

They will govern the use of any personal information which is collected by cookies while visiting these websites.
