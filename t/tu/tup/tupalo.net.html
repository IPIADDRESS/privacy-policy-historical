Privacy Policy

About Tupalo

Tupalo.com is an interactive web Service providing a tool for users to find, review and share local entertainment and shopping resources in locations around the world which are of interest to the user. Users provide all content contained within Tupalo by creating and sharing their personal experiences and opinions of establishments contained within the Tupalo database. For the purpose of this privacy policy, a “user” is defined as any member of the public who takes advantage of the Services provided by Tupalo. A “friend” is defined as any registered member of Tupalo who has been actively invited or given permission to connect and share reviews, favorites and private messages with another user via Services provided by Tupalo.

Tupalo’s Privacy Policy

Tupalo is adamant about protecting the personal information of it’s users. In this privacy policy we’ll explain how, when and why personal information is collected, used, and disclosed via Tupalo (also described as “we” and the “Site”).

By registering with and taking advantage of the Services that Tupalo offers, you consent to the privacy practices described in this policy below. These policies will be modified from time to time by Tupalo, so please check back periodically to see if changes have been made.

What Information We Collect

Tupalo collects information based on content submitted by users, such as full name and email address. Tupalo does not sell or share your email address with any third party companies or lists. Never will Tupalo display your email address to another user visiting your profile, even when a mutually agreed friendship is present.

Tupalo also collects user-submitted information such as city, state, zip and country. Your default location, entered as City and Country, is shared publicly via your personal profile by default, but can be disabled by editing your profile.

Tupalo also collects user-submitted photos, reviews and ratings of businesses and Service providers. This user-submitted information is public by default and accessible to all users of Tupalo.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads and collect information when users visit our site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements on our site, other websites and other forms of media about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please visit: http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp.

Google Adsense / Doubleclick DART

  1. Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on Tupalo.com;
  2. Google’s use of the DART cookies enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to Tupalo.com and other sites on the Internet;
  3. Users may opt out of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following url – http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

How Personal Information Is Used

Your profile page, which may contain your first name, profile picture, display name and any personally identifiable information you have chosen to including is publicly accessible to any users of the Service Tupalo provides via the reviews and ratings you have contributed to Tupalo. If a person is mutually recognized as a friend, they will also have access to your personally created list of favorite locations.

Your first name or display name (if specifically set) and email address will be used when you send any form of email notification to a friend, such as private messages, friend requests and location recommendations.

Browser and system information, IP address and time-stamps help us to diagnose problems with our server(s), measure overall website performance and administer Tupalo (fight spam, prevent fraud and abuse), allowing us to provide you the highest level of Service possible. This information is commonly collected over the internet.

Please do not post any personal information on this website which you wish to keep private.

Security

All registered Tupalo accounts are password-protected, which are hashed within our databases. This means that only you, the user, are in possession of your password. While employees of Tupalo have access to portions of our database, all passwords are stored in a form which is unreadable. While we take extreme measures in protecting your personal information, we cannot assure our users that personally identifiable information will never be disclosed.
