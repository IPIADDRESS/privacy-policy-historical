Privacy Policy

    This agreement is effective as of January 24, 2007

About Tupalo

Tupalo is an interactive web service providing a tool for users to find, review and share local entertainment and shopping resources in locations around the world which are of interest to the user. Users provide all content contained within Tupalo by creating and sharing their personal experiences and opinions of establishments contained within the Tupalo database. For the purpose of this privacy policy, a "user" is defined as any member of the public who takes advantage of the services provided by Tupalo. A "friend" is defined as any registered member of Tupalo who has been actively invited or given permission to connect and share reviews, favorites and private messages with another user via services provided by Tupalo.

Tupalo's Privacy Policy

Tupalo is adamant about protecting the personal information of it's users. In this privacy policy we'll explain how, when and why personal information is collected, used, and disclosed via Tupalo (also described as "we" and the "site").

By registering with and taking advantage of the services that Tupalo offers, you consent to the privacy practices described in this policy below. These policies will be modified from time to time by Tupalo, so please check back periodically to see if changes have been made.

What Information We Collect

Tupalo collects information based on content submitted by users, such as full name and email address. Tupalo does not sell or share your email address with any third party companies or lists. Email addresses are only displayed to the owning registered user when they are logged in and viewing their own profile. Never will Tupalo display your email address to another user visiting your profile, even when a mutually agreed friendship is present.

Tupalo also collects user-submitted information such as city, state, zip and country. Your default location, entered as City and Country, is shared publicly via your personal profile by default, but can be disabled by editing your profile.

Tupalo also collects user-submitted photos, reviews and ratings of businesses and service providers. This user-submitted information is public by default and accessible to all users of Tupalo.

Some information is collected while not informing the user. Information such as browser-related information, IP addresses, referral URL, time-stamp and entry/exit pages are such information. This information is logged and only for internal use. This information will never be shared or sold with a third party.

How Personal Information Is Used

Your profile page, which may contain your first name, profile picture, display name and any personally identifiable information you have chosen to including is publicly accessible to any users of the service Tupalo provides via the reviews and ratings you have contributed to Tupalo. If a person is mutually recognized as a friend, they will also have access to your personally created list of favorite locations.

Your first name or display name (if specifically set) and email address will be used when you send any form of email notification to a friend, such as private messages, friend requests and location recommendations.

Browser and system information, IP address and time-stamps help us to diagnose problems with our server(s), measure overall website performance and administer Tupalo, allowing us to provide you the highest level of service possible. This information is commonly collected over the internet.

User-created Content, Ratings and Reviews

Through Tupalo, you are able to publish reviews, comments and other content. Unregistered members are able to search within and access content, ratings and reviews. Registered users are allowed to rate and review locations and services as well as create mutual friendship connections to other registered users of Tupalo.

Reviews are publicly displayed unless specifically marked as 'friends-only'. Your display or first name and last initial will be displayed with every review and comment you create as well as a direct link back to your profile.

Per our terms of use, we have the right to republish your content as we see fit. We will never sell your reviews and/or content to third parties, however we may use content you have created in future promotional material.

Transfer of Personal Information

Except for the cases set forth in this policy, we will not give away, rent, share, sell or trade any of your personal information with outside parties. If required to in response to court orders, subpoenas or other legal activities we will disclose personal information about individual users to government, judicial or law enforcement authorities.

In the event of a transfer of ownership of Tupalo, through an event such as an acquisition or merger with another company, we reserve the right to transfer your personal information. If such an event were to occur Tupalo will notify all users through their registered email address informing you that your personal information has transferred and will become subject to an updated privacy policy.

By registering with Tupalo you are acknowledging that Tupalo is not responsible for any intercepted information sent via the internet and you further release Tupalo from any and all claims which may arise of or related to the unauthorized use of intercepted information.

You must be aware that when you voluntarily disclose your personally identifiable information (user name, email address, name, etc) via reviews, messages, forums, lists, personal messages, friend requests or any features involving two-way communication between users on Tupalo, this information may possibly be collected and used by third parties in ways considered abusive. Activities such as this are beyond the scope of Tupalo's responsibility. For the sake of your personal security, we ask that you not post any personally identifiable information you wish to keep private via the Tupalo site or services.

Your personal information is always able to be modified by logging into your personal profile and editing the minimal personal information we collect. Registered users who wish to have their account removed are able to by contacting feedback@tupalo.com. Upon request, we will happily remove as much information related to your account as commercially possible without affecting the quality of service and functionality of the Tupalo service. Some residual information may not be able to be removed.

Security

All registered Tupalo accounts are password-protected, which are hashed within our databases. This means that only you, the user, are in possession of your password. While employees of Tupalo have access to portions of our database, all passwords are stored in a form which is unreadable. While we take extreme measures in protecting your personal information, we cannot assure our users that personally identifiable information will never be disclosed.

External Links

Tupalo contains many links which point to third party websites which we have no direct affiliation. We will not share any personal information with third party sites and are in no way responsible for their particular privacy practices.

Cookies and Our Usage of Cookies

Tupalo, as with most websites today, require cookies in order to function properly. Cookies are small text files which copied to your computer when visiting websites. Cookies contain identifiable information unique to you as a user of a website and service and are used to enhance and personalize your browsing experience. Tupalo uses cookies to track your username and password, in the case that you want to login automatically to our service without having to retype your username and password with each visit. Please be aware that cookies are required to take advantage of the services which Tupalo offers, but if you'd prefer to disable cookies, there is an easy way via many of today's modern browsers.
