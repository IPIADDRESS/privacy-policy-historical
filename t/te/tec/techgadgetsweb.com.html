Privacy Policy

At www.techgadgetsweb.com, the privacy of all our visitors is of extreme importance and concern to us. This Privacy Policy page explains how your personal information is received, handled and taken care by Site Administrators.

Log Files
Just like any other site, we also make use of the log files.
The information inside the log files includes the following:

Internet Protocol (IP) Address
Type of Web Browser Used by the User.
Internet Service Provider (ISP)
Referring/Exit Web Pages
Date/Time Stamp
Number of clicks made by user

Most of the above information are collected to analyze the trends of the website, to better administer the site effectively, to track user activities around the site which in turn helps us to develop better content in the future and finally to gather demographic information to target content by region, if necessary.

Any of the above information collected does not contain personally identifiable resource.

Cookies and Web Beacons
www.techgadgetsweb.com does make use of cookies.

Cookies are used to store the following information:

Visitors Preferences
Record User Specific Information. It includes monitoring -on which pages the user accesses or visits
Customize effectively the web page content based on visitors browser type

These cookies may be used either directly by us or by our Advertising Partners such as Google.

Such cookies that are used by our advertising partners may tend to provide relevant ads based on the user region and past browsing content.

The Site Administrators have no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
We are in now way responsible for the information collected by such third party agents and advertisers.

If at any time, you feel your privacy is insecure, you may very well disable your cookies using your respective browser.

We respect the users who visit our site and in no way we will leak any available Personal information about the users including email ids which is available in Comments Section.
