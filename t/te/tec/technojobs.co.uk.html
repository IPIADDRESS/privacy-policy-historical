Privacy Policy

  1. Technojobs Ltd is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the security of any personal information received from you. We strictly adhere to the requirements of the data protection legislation in the UK. The purpose of this statement is to explain to you what personal information we collect and how we may use it.

  2. We value your privacy - as a registered candidate, your details are only passed on to recruiters using Technojobs. We do not sell, rent or exchange your personal information with any other party for commercial reasons. We will also use your contact information only for the purposes of sending you information on jobs or relevant information deemed to be of interest (if you are a candidate) or information on candidates or relevant information deemed to be of interest (if you are a recruiter or company hiring). We will only contact you with your consent.

  3. We follow strict security procedures in the storage and disclosure of information which you have given us, to prevent unauthorised access in accordance with the UK data protection legislation. We do not collect sensitive information about you except when you specifically knowingly provide it. In order to maintain the accuracy of our database, you can check, update or remove your personal details by clicking the unsubscribe link which is present in every email we will send to you. We use a technology called "cookies" as part of a normal business procedure to track patterns of behaviour of visitors to our site. A cookie is an element of data that our Website sends to your browser which is then stored on your system. You can set your browser to prevent this happening. Any information collected in this way can be used to identify you unless you change your browser settings.

  4. We will not transfer your information outside the EEA (European Economic Area) without first obtaining your consent.

  5. If you have any questions about privacy please contact us at support@technojobs.co.uk

Tags/Keywords

Our innovative and custom-written search engine places an importance upon tags chosen to be representative of the content, however it also indexes all content to provide you with the most relevant results

Please note this field operates as Boolean search, so you can narrow your search by including operators such as "and" if more than one term is required to feature in the results. "+" will also work in the same way. I.e. "php and mysql london" is the same as "+php +mysql london".

The operator "or" does not need to be included as this is the default, i.e. "php or mysql or london" is the same as "php mysql london". You can also use "not" or - to exclude terms.
