Privacy Policy

Techtoggle.com will never send you spam. Private contact information that you provide including but not limited to your name, address, telephone number and e-mail address will not be released, sold, or rented to any third parties. Such information is kept for the sole use of Techtoggle.com  and we take strict measures to make sure that it is well protected. We would never ask you for sensitive information such as bank account details or credit card information.

Cookies

We do use cookies to store information, such as your personal preferences when you visit our site. We also display third party advertisements on Techtoggle.com. Advertisers may use cookies and web beacons when they advertise on our site that also send advertisers information including your IP address, your ISP, the browser used by you to visit our site. Such application is generally applied for geo targeting purposes or showing certain ads, based on specific visited sites.

DoubleClick DART cookies

We may use DART cookies for ad serving, which places a cookie on your computer when you are browsing the web and visit a site using DoubleClick advertising.

The cookie is used to serve your specific ads and your interests (“interest based targeting”). The ads served are to be targeted on the basis of your previous browsing history. DART uses “non personally identifiable information”, which does NOT track your personal information, such as your name, email address, physical address, telephone number, social security numbers, bank account numbers or credit card numbers.
