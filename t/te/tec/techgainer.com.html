Privacy Policy

At TechGainer.com, we take the privacy of our users very seriously. Just like web browsers, TechGainer uses logs that includes IP (internet protocol) address, ISP (internet service provider), geographic location, time/date, browser info, referral links etc. This helps us understand how users are interacting with our website and helps us improve our service. These information are anonymous and not identifiable personally.

Cookies

In case you not sure, “cookie” is a small data that is stored in your browser locally and allows TechGainer to recognize your each visit. Cookies themselves do not contain any personal information, rather visitor specific preference.

External links

TechGainer is never responsible for external links that is not hosted on our site. It is advised to read specific privacy policies for external links/sites.

These third-party ad servers uses technologies like JavaScript, web beacons, IP address to serve relevant ads and may gather anonymous data to measure their performance. TechGainer has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

You can disable cookies if you wish using your browser settings.
