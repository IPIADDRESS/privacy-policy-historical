Privacy Policy

Posted on April 25, 2011 by shabnam sultan — No Comments ↓

At techiezlounge.com the privacy of visitor is of extreme importance.

We do not share/rent your email address to any third party. We hate spam as much as you do. Email address which you submit is solely used to reply to your comments.Use of abusive language and racial comments by the readers will not be entertained.

Cookies: Cookies do store informations such as website address,name and email address for your convenience when you visit the site next time you don’t have to type those again.

DoubleClick DART Cookie: Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on techiezlounge.com. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to your users based on their visit to our site and other sites on the Internet.Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL – http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

Log Files

Like other websites, we collect and use the data contained in log files. The information in the log files include your IP (internet protocol) address, your ISP (internet service provider), the browser you used to visit our site, the time you visited our site and the pages which you have gone through.These informations are not shared with anyone.

techiezlounge.com depends on online advertisements for revenue and for that we place ads of various companies and sites which may store certain informations about your visits to this blog which does not include your name or email address to show advertisements of your interest.
