INTERNET COMMUNICATION PROTOCOL INFORMATION: The communication standards of the Internet industry transmit the URL of the site from which you visit www.TechShout.com, the IP address of your computer, the IP address of your ISP, various attributes of your computer such as operating system, resolution and browser software, and the date and time of your various requests for pages on www.TechShout.com. NEWSLETTER SUBSCRIPTIONS: Visitors to TechShout.com may also sign up with their name and email address for a variety of newsletters that TechShout.com produces with varying frequency (e.g., News headlines) without having to register.
