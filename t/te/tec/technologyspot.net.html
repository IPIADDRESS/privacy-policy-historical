Privacy

Posted By Martin Nemko On Friday, November 9th, 2012 With 0 Comments

The terms ‘Technologyspot.net’ and ‘www.Technologyspot.net’ are used in this document (or Agreement) to refer to the Web site itself.

Privacy issues are a high priority for us at www.Technologyspot.net and we follow standard industry practices to maintain your personal information. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.

Technologyspot.net does not spam or sell/lease its user information to third parties.

Policy Coverage

This Privacy Policy covers Technologyspot.net’s treatment of personally identifiable information that Technologyspot.net collects when you are on the www.Technologyspot.net site, and when you use Technologyspot.net’s services. This policy also covers Technologyspot.net’s treatment of any personally identifiable information that Technologyspot.net’s business partners share with Technologyspot.net. This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that Technologyspot.net does not own or control, or to people that Technologyspot.net does not employ or manage.

Personal Information Collected by Technologyspot.net

ONLINE REGISTRATION: In order to become a Technologyspot.net forum member, you must first create a forum member account on Technologyspot.net by providing information about yourself including but not limited to name, email address, country, ZIP/postal code, birth date, a brief summary of yourself and a password. Technologyspot.net checks for the validity of the email addresses.

OPTIONAL PROFILE INFORMATION: Once your provide the basic information to create an forum account, you will have the opportunity to provide additional information in the Profile section describing your skills, interests, professional and academic background and favorites. Provision of this additional information beyond what is required at registration is entirely optional; it allows you to better identify yourself and create superior networking opportunities with others through shared contexts. but enables you to better identify yourself and find new opportunities on Technologyspot.net. Your Profile information can only be viewed by other Technologyspot.net visitors or registered members or members of your personal network only depending on the option you choose.

RECOGNIZING YOU AS A Technologyspot.net FORUM MEMBER: For Technologyspot.net to recognize you as a registered member, you must login with your login email and password. At the time of login, you will have the option to instruct the site to remember you so when you come back the next time to use Technologyspot.net, you would not have to login with your email and password. As is common in the industry, Technologyspot.net uses the technology of cookies and forum page log files to monitor user registration, to recognize registered members when they revisit the site and to track site usage. Our ability to identify you as a registered member allows us to customize the Technologyspot.net experience and to protect your personal information based on your account settings.

INTERNET COMMUNICATION PROTOCOL INFORMATION: The communication standards of the Internet industry transmit the URL of the site from which you visit www.Technologyspot.net, the IP address of your computer, the IP address of your ISP, various attributes of your computer such as operating system, resolution and browser software, and the date and time of your various requests for pages on www.Technologyspot.net. Technologyspot.net file logging system automatically logs all of this information in its log files for its analysis.

NEWSLETTER SUBSCRIPTIONS: Visitors to Technologyspot.net may also sign up with their name and email address for a variety of newsletters that Technologyspot.net produces with varying frequency (e.g., News headlines) without having to register. Technologyspot.net confirms the subscription by sending an email to the email address specified before sending the newsletter.

Use of Information by Technologyspot.net

Technologyspot.net is an online website that offers a variety of services designed to connect people from around the world. The information, whether personal information of any form of self-expression is used to help you describe yourself to other users. Technologyspot.net also collects additional information in order to operate and improve our service. www.Technologyspot.net is the sole owner of the information collected on the Technologyspot.net site.

VISIBILITY OF PUBLIC CREATIVE CONTRIBUTIONS: In addition to being a social and professional networking site, Technologyspot.net also provides a variety of forums for its members to express themselves in the form of articles, columns, opinions, comments, reviews, art and photographs. Since this expression is linked to the member’s identity and the expression is in the public domain, the compilation of a member’s creative contributions to Technologyspot.net will be visible to all visitors of Technologyspot.net whether or not they are registered members of Technologyspot.net.

VISIBILITY OF PRIVATE MESSAGES WITHIN NETWORK: You will be able to create and share messages within your personal forum network. The visibility of these private notes will be restricted to your network unless you or one of the members in your network choose to forward it to others within or outside www.Technologyspot.net.

SHARING OF INFORMATION WITH THIRD PARTIES: Technologyspot.net takes your privacy very seriously. Technologyspot.net will never sell, rent, or otherwise provide your personally identifiable information to any third parties unless it is to carry out your instructions and to provide specific services. As an example, we send your information to a credit card processing company to process a payment you have made for the use of a Technologyspot.net service. These third parties are bound by strict confidentiality agreements which limit their use of such information. In addition, Technologyspot.net may share your information to send direct mailers or complimentary coupons/offers to a mailing agency to process postal mailing. Again, such an agency would be bound by strict confidentiality agreements that limit their use of such information.

EMAIL COMMUNICATIONS: Technologyspot.net generally communicates with you as a registered forum member of Technologyspot.net through the primary email that you have specified at the time of registration. This is also used as the login text string in conjunction with the password to authenticate you on the www.Technologyspot.net site. Your email and password are never shared with anybody within or outside Technologyspot.net. Technologyspot.net uses this email address to send you three types of communications:

Sending you emails related to the proper functioning of your account (e.g. when you receive a response to a classified you have posted on Technologyspot.net or when you receive an invitation to join the personal network of another person). Sending you newsletters that you have subscribed to. Sending you occasional announcements about changes in Technologyspot.net’s policies or promotions introduction of new services.

Please note that these email communications may contain commercial messages of advertisers; however, Technologyspot.net does not provide your personal information or emails to any of the advertisers; we just include the advertising banners they provide us in the emails that we send. As always you can opt out of the newsletters or promotional announcements.

PARTNERING WITH OTHERS: Technologyspot.net may partner with other online services to offer a combined service. Whenever we offer such combined services, Technologyspot.net will make it clear who the partner is and it will be entirely up to you to use or not to use the service. Where any personal information is passed along to the partner you will be informed prior to that sharing.

PROVISION OF AGGREGATE DEMOGRAPHIC INFORMATION TO THIRD PARTIES: Technologyspot.net reserves the right to provide aggregate demographic information of its member base to advertisers and others so as to attract pertinent advertisers to advertise on Technologyspot.net. We do not share personal information of individual users with advertisers.

COMMUNICATIONS TO OTHERS FROM YOU THROUGH Technologyspot.net: You will be able to send emails to others – members and non-members – through the Technologyspot.net system. For example, you may send a comment on an article written by another member. Or you may send an email notification to a non-member to see an article. In these instances, these emails will list your name and email address in the header of the message. The recipients of these emails initiated by you will get to know your name and email address. Technologyspot.net is not responsible for how these recipients use this information. Your contact information will only be shared with another member only if both of you indicated you would like to establish contact with each other.

COMMUNICATIONS FROM OTHERS TO YOU THROUGH Technologyspot.net: Only registered forum members of Technologyspot.net are allowed to communicate with you through Technologyspot.net through a form on Technologyspot.net. Unless and until you respond to this email, they will not get to know your email address. You may, however, choose an account setting that prevents any other member from contacting you or inviting you to join their personal network.

CHILDREN’S PRIVACY: Protecting the privacy of young children is especially important. For that reason, Technologyspot.net does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 13 or knowingly allow such persons to register. If you are under 13, please do not send any information about yourself to us, including your name, address, telephone number, or email address. No one under age 13 is allowed to provide any personal information to or on Technologyspot.net. In the event that we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under age 13 without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information as quickly as possible. If you believe that we might have any information from or about a child under 13, please contact us at martnek@powdermail.com.

LINKS TO OTHER WEB SITES: We are not responsible for the practices employed by Web sites linked to or from our Web site nor the information or content contained therein. Please remember that when you use a link to go from our Web site to another Web site, our Privacy Policy is no longer in effect. Your browsing and interaction on any other Web site, including those that have a link on our Web site, is subject to that Web site’s own rules and policies. Please read over those rules and policies before proceeding.

These companies may use information (not including your name, address email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
