Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us, and maintaining your trust and confidence is one of our highest priorities. We respect your right to keep your personal information confidential and understand your desire to avoid unwanted solicitations.

A. Types of Information We Collect

We collect certain personal information about you – but only when that information is provided by you or is obtained by us with your authorization. We use that information only to fulfill the service that you have purchased or asked for such as a registration or men's breakfast program.

B. Parties to Whom We Disclose Information

As a general rule, we do not disclose personal information about our customers or former customers to anyone. Your personal information or any information provided regarding any children or other family members is not sold at any time.

C. Confidentiality and Security of Nonpublic Personal Information

We maintain physical, electronic and procedural safeguards to guard your nonpublic personal information and any other information, to ensure our families that their privacy is a major part of the conventions commitment to provide the finest service possible.

IV. Opt Out Provision

While our organization only used information to communicate pertinent information regarding attending one of our events. Any contact or attendee has the option to opt out of this communication at any time. However, the attendee assumes all risk for missing pertinent info regarding their registration such as start times, schedules, and other information.
