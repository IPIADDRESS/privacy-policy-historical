Log Files

Like many other Web sites, www.teapartyeconomist.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons

DoubleClick DART Cookie

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on www.teapartyeconomist.com send directly to your browsers. www.teapartyeconomist.com has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
