Privacy Policy
 
At TeamSupport LLC (“TeamSupport”), we respect and protect the privacy of visitors to our website, and our customers who use our on-demand software, tools and services offered on the site.  TeamSupport maintains a strict policy to ensure the privacy of our customers and visitors. Simply put, we will not release information about you (including your full name, mailing address, or phone number) to any third party without your consent unless:
  •          We are required to under law.
  •          The product you are using becomes owned by another party either through a merger or acquisition.

Information Gathered

TeamSupport gathers a variety of information about our visitors and customers, including, but not limited to, IP address, referring page, pages visited, and the length of time you spend on the site. In addition, TeamSupport uses Cookies to both track visitors on the website as well as to remember client’s usernames when they log into the application. People who sign up to use one of TeamSupport’s applications (collectively customers) or who request certain marketing materials will be required to provide additional information such as company name, address and, phone number.  We also require billing information (either credit card information or billing address) for customers.

Each party agrees that: (a) the Confidential Information constitutes valuable trade secrets of the party owning such Confidential Information; (b) it will use Confidential Information solely in accordance with the provisions of this policy and our Terms of Use and Service; and (c) it will not disclose, or permit to be disclosed, the Confidential Information of the other party to any third party without the disclosing party’s prior written consent.

Blogs & User Forums
TeamSupport may provide blogs or user forums on the Company’s Web sites. Any personally identifiable information you choose to submit in such a forum may be read, collected, or used by others who visit these forums, and may be used to send you unsolicited messages. TeamSupport is not responsible for the personally identifiable information you choose to submit in these forums.

Information Uses
TeamSupport may use the information gathered for billing, identification, authentication, service improvements, and contact including internal marketing efforts.

Security Measures
TeamSupport works to protect the security of your information during transmission encrypting information sent to and from our servers when using or accessing TeamSupport’s applications. Please see our security information page here.

Data Storage
TeamSupport owns the data storage, databases, and all intellectual property rights for our applications, but you retain ownership to all data you input into the system. In addition, all data is backed up multiple times each day and run on redundant servers that are in a SSAE 16 audited secure facility located in Dallas, TX, USA.

Changes
TeamSupport may, from time to time, change this privacy policy. If there are material changes to the policy we will either send an e-mail to the registered primary contact of our customers or post a prominent notice on our website.

US-EU SAFE HARBOR
TeamSupport complies with the US-EU Safe Harbor Framework as set forth by the US Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use, and retention of personal information from European Union member countries.  TeamSupport has certified that it adheres to the Safe Harbor Privacy Principles of notice, choice, onward transfer, security, data integrity, access, and enforcement.  If there is any conflict between the policies in this privacy policy and the Safe Harbor Privacy Principles, the Safe Harbor Privacy Principles shall govern.  To learn more about the Safe Harbor program, and to view our certification page, please visit http://www.export.gov/safeharbor/

In compliance with the US-EU Safe Harbor Principles, TeamSupport commits to resolve complaints about your privacy and our collection or use of your personal information.  European Union citizens with inquiries or complaints regarding this privacy policy should first contact TeamSupport at the address listed below.

TeamSupport has further committed to refer unresolved privacy complaints under the US-EU Safe Harbor Principles to an independent dispute resolution mechanism, the BBB EU SAFE HARBOR, operated by the Council of Better Business Bureaus. If you do not receive timely acknowledgment of your complaint, or if your complaint is not satisfactorily addressed, please visit www.bbb.org/us/safe-harbor-complaints for more information and to file a complaint.

Questions
If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at 800/596-2820 or via mail at: TeamSupport LLC, 100 Highland Park Village, Ste 200, Dallas, TX 75205.
