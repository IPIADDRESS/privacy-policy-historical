  Privacy statement
We respect the privacy of every individual who visits our Web sites. This Privacy Policy outlines the information

Is your online ordering secure?

Protecting your order information is our priority. We use Secure Sockets Layers (SSL) technology to protect the security of your online order information. When you input your information into our system at "Check Out," your shipping information and credit card information is sent in an encrypted code that only we can decode. This information is sent to a credit card payment processor that validates your name, address and credit card information. Your encrypted credit card information is then sent directly to the credit card processors and is never stored or seen by our computers; this is directly for your safety. Your encrypted credit card information is never stored on our computer or archived in anyway. (You can not send us an email and ask us to add to your order or sent you something based on your credit card number as we never have this information)

What do you do with my personal information?

We will not collect any personally-identifiable information about you (e.g., your name, address, telephone number or email address ("personal data") through our Web sites unless you have provided it to us voluntarily for the sole reason of ordering a product, we use this information to ship your product accurately and safely and require it only so that we may contact you in case of delivery problem.

Additional Information Collected Automatically

In some cases, we may automatically (i.e., not via registration) collect technical information when you connect to our site that is not personally - identifiable. Examples of this type of information include the type of Internet Browser you are using, the type of computer operating system you are using and the domain name of the Web site from which you linked to our site.

Information Placed Automatically on Your Computer - Cookies

When you view one of our Web sites, we may store some information on your computer. This information will be in the form of a "Cookie" or similar file and can help us in many ways. For example, Cookies allow us to correctly add products to the shopping cart when you are shopping with us. With most Internet Browsers, you can erase Cookies from your computer hard drive, block all Cookies or receive a warning before a Cookie is stored. Please refer to your Browser instructions or help screen to learn more about these functions.

While we can keep track of your name and address for future product purchases, you will need to provide your credit card number each time you order. Only authorized employees have access to stored information. We safeguard information according to established security standards and procedures, and we continually assess new technology for protecting information.

When you shop with us, you can be assured that your shopping experience is 100% secure. This means you pay nothing if unauthorized charges are made to your credit card as a result of shopping at our website. You are one of thousands of customers who visit our website and who shop with us securely.

Under the Fair Credit Billing Act, your bank can not hold you liable for more than $50.00 of fraudulent credit card charges. If your bank does hold you liable for any of the $50.00, we will cover the entire liability for you, up to the $50.00. We will cover this liability only if the unauthorized use of your credit card resulted through no fault of your own from purchases made at our website while using the secure server.

If unauthorized use of your credit card should occur, please notify the issuing bank of your credit card immediately.
