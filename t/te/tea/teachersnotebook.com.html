Effective Date: April 8, 2011

Information Collection and How it is Used
General Information:
  • Teacher's Notebook will not sell or disclose your name or email address to third parties without your explicit consent, except as specified in this privacy policy.
  • Your username, Teacher's Notebook ID or alias is displayed throughout Teacher's Notebook (and so available to the public) and is connected to all of your Teacher's Notebook activity. Other people can see your items for sale, store, feedback, ratings and associated comments. 
  • Teacher's Notebook collects personal information when you register to use Teacher's Notebook services or to post content on Teacher's Notebook. At registration you are asked for your email address and certain other optional information.
  • If you would like to obtain a Seller account, Teacher's Notebook will ask for your billing address, phone number, and credit card information.
  • Teacher's Notebook automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, cookies, and the pages you request.
  • Teacher's Notebook uses information for the following general purposes: products and services provisioning, billing, identification and authentication, services improvement, contact, research, and anonymous reporting. IP addresses are automatically reported by your browser each time you view a web page. Your IP address is stored in Teacher's Notebook's user registration databases when you register with Teacher's Notebook. IP addresses may be used for various research purposes.
Service-related Announcements 
Teacher's Notebook will send you a welcome/confirmation email when you sign up for Teacher's Notebook's services. Receipt of this email is required for using Teacher's Notebook's services.
Teacher's Notebook will send you service-related announcements on rare occasions when it is necessary to do so.

You may not opt-out of service-related communications, which are not promotional in nature. If you do not wish to receive them, you have the option to deactivate your account by emailing support@TeachersNotebook.com from the email address you signed up with.

Administrative Messages
Teacher's Notebook may periodically email you with administrative messages. Teacher's Notebook will not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes without your explicit consent. Your information may be combined with information Teacher's Notebook collects from other companies and used to improve and personalize services, content and advertising.
 
Cookies
We use cookies, web beacons, and related tools from third parties to provide features and services that improve your buying and selling experience. To help protect your privacy, we limit how these tools can be used on Teacher's Notebook.

How Teacher's Notebook uses cookies and web beacons
“Cookies" are small files placed on the hard drive of your computer. Most cookies are "session cookies," meaning that they are automatically deleted from your hard drive once you sign out from Teacher's Notebook or close your web browser.

We use cookies to provide features and services, such as:
  • Promoting and enforcing trust and safety
  • Remembering your preferences
  • Presenting information that’s targeted to your interests
  • Measuring the effectiveness of our sites, services, content, and advertising
  • Providing other services and features that are available only through the use of cookies
A “web beacon” is an electronic image placed in the code of a web page. We use web beacons to monitor the traffic patterns of users from one Teacher's Notebook page to another and to improve site performance.

Cookies or No Cookies
We offer certain features that are available only through the use of cookies. You are always free to decline cookies if your web browser permits. However, if you decline cookies, you may not be able to use certain Teacher's Notebook features, and you may be required to reenter your password more frequently during a session.

Use of cookies and web beacons by third parties
We may work with other companies who place cookies or web beacons on our websites. These companies help operate our websites and provide you with additional products and services.
