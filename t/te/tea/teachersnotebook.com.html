    How I join for FREE?
    Membership on TeachersNotebook is FREE. Just click on the orange button called 'JOIN FOR FREE' at the top of any page to create your account.
    How do I login to my account?
    You can login to your account by clicking on the orange button called 'LOGIN' at the top of any page to access the login page.
    How do I reset my password?
    You can reset you password on the LOGIN page by entering your email address. An email will be sent to you with a link that you can use to enter a new password.
    How do I download the products I ordered?
    Downloading your purchases in done from your PURCHASES page. Click on the orange button called 'MY PURCHASES' at the top of any page to access the page.
    What do I do if I can't open my download?
    Sometimes a file will not download completely. Try to download the file again and if you still have trouble, click on the 'SUBMIT A TICKET' button on this popup to submit a support ticket.
    What types of payments do you accept?
    You can purchase products directly on the site using PayPal, American Express, Discover, MasterCard or Visa. If you need to use a Purchase Order, please email us at sales@teachersnotebook.com or call us at 732.305.0023.
    Why does my PDF file have blurry text?
    Preview on a Mac sometimes has trouble with certain PDF files and the text will appear blurry or as black lines. You should then open the file using Adobe PDF Reader.
    How do I open my own FREE shop?
    Anyone can open a FREE teacher shop and begin sharing their resources. Just click on the Open a FREE Teacher Shop link at the bottom of any page or click here.
    How and when do shops get paid?
    Teacher shops are paid via PayPal or Dwolla within 3 business days of the 1st and 15th of every month.
    What if I have more questions?
    If you have other questions, just click on the 'SUBMIT A TICKET' button on this popup to submit a support ticket.

We use cookies to provide features and services, such as:

A “web beacon” is an electronic image placed in the code of a web page. We use web beacons to monitor the traffic patterns of users from one Teachers Notebook page to another and to improve site performance.
