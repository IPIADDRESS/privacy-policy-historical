PRIVACY POLICY

In general, you can visit the TeenHollywood.com network without revealing any personal information.  Like many networks, our web servers collect domain names and IP addresses of visitors, but not the e-mail addresses.  We may use this information to measure the number of visits, average time spent on the site, pages viewed, and other such statistics.  TeenHollywood.com, Inc. may then use this information to improve our sites.

At times, you may be asked for personal information, including your name, address, telephone number and e-mail address.  TeenHollywood.com, Inc. will use this information only to provide you with information regarding its products and services.  This information will not be distributed or sold to third parties.  Whenever you are asked for personal information, you will be able to opt out of receiving information from us or our select advertisers by checking the appropriate box.

At times, a "cookie" may be used to provide you with personally tailored information.  A cookie is a small text file that a site sends to your browser.  It may then be stored on your hard drive so we can recognize you when you return.  If you prefer, you may set your browser to ask for your permission before you receive a cookie.

TeenHollywood.com, Inc. works with a third party that serves ads to this site.  To find out more about how Flycast manages the privacy of information in conjunction with serving ads on this site, please go to http://www.flycast.com/about_us/about-privacy.html.

If you have any further questions, please e-mail info@teenhollywood.com.
