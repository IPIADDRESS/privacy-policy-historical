    Teaching Adult Second Language Learners (Cambridge Handbooks for Language Teachers) by Heather McKay, Abigail Tom


    Practical activities for ESOL suitable for both British English and American English students. The paperback edition addresses the special needs of adults studying English. It provides a useful summary of the principles involved in teaching adults as well as a wealth of activities specially designed for adult learners. The text is divided into three sections: Section I provides an introduction to the adult language learner and discusses the issues of assessment/placement and course/lesson organization. Section II gives teachers techniques for building community in the classroom. Section III provides activities designed for students at various levels that are organized thematically around topics such as self-identification, food, clothing, and work.

    Order from UK | US | Canada



    Test Your English Vocabulary in Use: Upper-intermediate by Michael McCarthy, Felicity O'Dell


    Test Your English Vocabulary in Use: Upper-intermdiate is a set of vocabulary tests to accompany English Vocabulary in Use: Upper-intermediate. It highlights the key vocabulary from the units of the book and can be used on its own or with the companion volume of English Vocabulary in Use: Upper-intermediate. Additional exposure to up-to-date vocabulary. Easy-to-use tests for convenient revision. Wide variety of test types make learning vocabulary memorable and fun. Clear marking system means progress can be checked easily. Comprehensive answer key for students working on their own. Suitable for self-study and classroom use.
