TextbookRush Privacy Policy

Last updated 8/12/08

When visiting any website, we encourage you to read that website's privacy policy. TextbookRush is committed to keeping your information private and confidential.

Should you have any suggestions or comments regarding our privacy policy, we encourage you to e-mail us at help@textbookrush.com. Our complete contact information, including telephone number and physical mailing address, may be found on our contact page.

Information Collection and Use

TextbookRush is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to any outside parties, except as outlined in this policy. TextbookRush collects information from users at several different places on our website in order to process orders and better serve you with pertinent information. Information collected includes name, shipping address, billing address, telephone numbers, e-mail address, and payment information such as credit card. TextbookRush also requires you to submit a user name and password of your choice for future access to your information. Your user name and password is to remain confidential and you should not share this information with anyone.

Registration

In order to process your orders using this website, a user must first complete the registration form. During registration a user is required to give their contact information, which includes name, e-mail, telephone number and physical address. This information is used to contact the user about the services on our site, such as sending automated order status updates. This information is NOT shared.

Orders

Most of the information collected in the registration process will be used to process orders. In the order process, a user must provide additional financial information such as credit card number and expiration date. This information is used for billing purposes and to fulfill customerâ€™s orders. If we have trouble processing an order, this contact information is used to get in touch with the user. We do not share your personal and financial information with any third parties. The only exception is when processing your credit card information with the merchant bank for authorization and approval. This process is protected by an enhanced security system. Note: For enhanced security and privacy, TextbookRush does NOT save your credit/debit card information. See Security section for more details on security measures and procedures.

Cookies

We use cookies to maintain cart data and to customize page content. If, as a user, you reject the cookie, you may still use our site; however, you will be unable to use our shopping cart to purchase items online. This is because the shopping cart uses cookies to retain information necessary to process your order. If you disable cookies, we will not be able to accept your order online. We do not share any information with any third party sites. Cookies are used strictly for the purpose of enhancing your personal experience with our site. When you close your browser, the cookies will be terminated, with the exception of those used to maintain your cart data. Cart-related cookies stay active for 2 days so that users may "save" their carts for a short period.

Log Files

We use IP addresses to analyze trends, administer the site, track a userâ€™s movement, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information. We do not distribute or share IP information with any third parties. Only TextbookRush technical staff has access to log files. Log files are periodically purged.

Data Sharing

We use outside shipping companies (FedEx and USPS) to ship orders and credit card processing companies (VeriSign/Penn Securities) to bill users for goods and services. These companies do not retain, share, store, or use personally identifiable information for any secondary purposes. We also use third party service providers for the shopping cart (Google Checkout & PayPal) and they do not retain, share, store, or use personally identifiable information for any secondary purposes.

TextbookRush is located in Ohio and abides by all federal and state laws. TextbookRush will disclose any personally identifiable information when required to by law, such as to comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or subpoena, but not under any other circumstances.

Outside Links

This website contains links to other sites. Please be aware TextbookRush is not responsible for the privacy practices of other such sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every website that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this website.

Security

TextbookRush's website takes every precaution to protect our usersâ€™ information. When users submit sensitive information via the website, that information is protected both online and off-line.

When our registration/order form asks users to enter sensitive information (such as credit card number), that information is encrypted and protected with the best encryption software in the industry: SSL. VeriSign uses SSL technology with advanced 128-bit encryption to process our payments. To learn more about VeriSign, SSL, or payment processing, please visit VeriSign.

All of our usersâ€™ information is classified and restricted. The TextbookRush facility is a secured and locked facility. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job are granted access to personally identifiable information. If you wish to have your information removed, you may contact us at help@textbookrush.com to do so. For future transactions, you will be required to re-enter your information.

All TextbookRush servers, including web servers and database servers, are housed at TextbookRush and maintained in-house by TextbookRush. Access to the database is strictly monitored and protected from outside access. Internet access is restricted and protected by firewall and password protection. The servers that we store personally identifiable information on are kept in a secure environment.

If you have any questions about the security at our website, you can send an e-mail to us at help@textbookrush.com.

Special Offers

If you choose to receive special offers, TextbookRush may send you such pertinent information. Our special offers are sent only to customers who grant TextbookRush permission to send such information. TextbookRush does not share your information with third parties for their promotional purposes and does not solicit outside parties to send special offers on our behalf.

Out of respect for the privacy of our users, we present the option to not receive these types of communications. Furthermore, all special offers are sent via e-mail and have a "remove link" if you wish to no longer receive special offers and news. Otherwise, customers may occasionally receive information on products, services, special deals, or news. Please see our choice and opt-out below for further details of how to be removed from our mailing list.

Correction/Updating/Reviewing Personal Information

Customers may change or review their account information such as street address or credit card information in the My Account area, or by going to the home page and clicking on the My Account button. You MUST have your username and password in order to access your account.

Choice/Opt-out

TextbookRush does not use customerâ€™s information for contact other than TextbookRush pertinent business. However, for customers who wish to opt-out or be removed from TextbookRush special offers and related news, TextbookRush provides several methods to accomplish this request. Users who no longer wish to receive our newsletter or promotional materials may opt-out of receiving these communications by un-checking the "permission to send" field when processing their order. They may also send us an e-mail to help@textbookrush.com with a request to be removed. Or, customers may also use the link provided with all communications to automatically unsubscribe.

Notification of Changes

If at any point we decide to use personally identifiable information in a manner different from that stated at the time it was collected, we will notify users via e-mail. Users will have a choice as to whether or not we use their information in this different manner. We will use information in accordance with the privacy policy under which the information was collected.

In the event that we make a material change to our privacy policy, we will notify you by e-mail. If you have opted out of receiving any communications from TextbookRush, you may visit our website at http://www.textbookrush.com where news of changes will be posted. Otherwise, you may e-mail us at help@textbookrush.com. If you send us an e-mail request, we will respond to that e-mail only. You will not receive promotional materials unless you specifically grant us permission to send those.
