Privacy Policy

TEDJOB.com takes and respects your privacy seriously. TEDJOB.com collects small amount of user information when users register with TEDJOB.com or use any TEDJOB.com services. TEDJOB.com may collect information via cookie planted in your computer. The purpose of collecting user information is to customize services and enhance customer satisfaction.

The data we collect are:

  1. The information you fill and submit by the web forms on TEDJOB.com sites.
  2. Standard web log entries, including your IP address, page URL, and timestamp.

TEDJOB.com uses 128-bit SSL encryption, the highest level of SSL encryption currently available to consumers, to process credit card and employer account login. The credit card will be processed by PayPal®. Once processed, only the last four digit of the credit card number will be stored in TEDJOB.com database.

TEDJOB.com conducts research based on collected information constantly to understand users' need. TEDJOB.com may share its research findings with other trusted parties. However, TEDJOB.com will not disclose any personally identifiable information including name, birthday and IP addresses, or any information that may be reasonably expected to lead to identification of a specific user, with any other third party unless:

  1. we are required by law enforcement agencies, subpoenas, court orders to do so, or
  2. we believe that it is necessary to share the information with investigation agencies to take action against illegal activities, or
  3. users agree to do so, or
  4. TEDJOB.com is acquired by other parties.

Please remember that your posts at TEDJOB.com become public and that you should exercise with caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. You are cautioned that any online communications may not be fully confidential. In addition, you should exercise caution when keying in information on public computers as the information could be stored in temporary files and could be retrieved.

TEDJOB.com reserves the right to update this privacy policy with or without notice.
