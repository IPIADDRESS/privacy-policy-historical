What information do we collect and how is it used?

The type of information that LCG/Telltale collects will vary depending on the activity collected and may include what is known as “personally identifiable information” or “personal information” including your first and last name, e-mail address, account information for certain third party sites or networks such as Facebook or Twitter, phone numbers (including cell phone numbers), home address, and zip code. Personally identifiable information can be accessed in order to directly contact you. This information is collected so that visitors can participate in activities such as receiving our newsletters or participating in contests, surveys or sweepstakes (your participation in contests, surveys or sweepstakes may require you to provide additional personally identifiable information such as a phone number, age or gender). Third parties may contact you to participate in surveys on our behalf, and third parties may also contact you to participate in Promotions as we discuss more fully below. We also collect additional types of personally identifiable information (e.g., credit card and billing and shipping information, including physical addresses and phone numbers) when someone purchases products and offerings at our Sites or through use of our Services. Such purchases are governed by our Terms of Service, http://www.Telltalegames.com/termsofservice , the terms and conditions of which are incorporated herein by reference. We may share your personal information with third parties solely in order to have them assist with fulfillment of product sales, including shipping and credit card processing, and companies that assist us with e-mailings, but such third parties agree to maintain the security of your personal information in a manner consistent with this Privacy Policy.

We may also share personal information with third parties to prevent abuse of our Site or Services and also to comply with any laws, notices or orders from a governmental agency or entity or to prevent harm to a user of our Site or Services.

Some of our Sites and Services use Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. (“Google”). Google Analytics uses “cookies,” which are text files placed on your computer, to help us analyze and track how visitors use the Site and our Services. The information generated by the cookie about your use of our Services will be transmitted to and stored on Google servers and may be accessed by us. Google uses this information to help us evaluate how our websites are used. Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Google’s behalf. Third party sites and services you access through our Services may also employ cookies.

Cookies also allow us to see what you may have purchased, either from our Site or through using one of our Services including game play. We collect information on our online games you have played, and we also track your play within an online game (including online games you may have purchased from a third party website) and, if such are available, purchases that you may make within an online game. We may send you notifications based on information we learn from cookies, including notifications related to purchasing additional games or products from us.
LCG/Telltale also collects IP addresses (an IP address is the string of numbers that identifies each sender or receiver of information that is sent across the Internet), for the purposes of system administration, to report aggregate information internally and to some of our business partners and advertisers and to audit the use of our Sites or Services. When visitors request pages on one of our Sites, our servers log the visitors’ IP addresses. We collect log files that record activity on our Sites and gather statistics about visitor’s time and activity on the Sites. The statistics are generated anonymously, and they help us in connection with marketing and demographic studies as we enhance our Sites. LCG/Telltale does not normally link IP addresses to anything personally identifiable, which means that a user’s session will be logged, but the user remains anonymous to us. LCG/Telltale can and will use IP addresses to identify a user when we feel it is necessary to enforce compliance with our Terms of Service, this Privacy Policy or to protect our services, Sites or a person or entity. Third parties providing services or technology on our behalf may also collect certain information such as IP addresses of our users; however, such information is provided on an aggregated basis and is not tracked to an individual user.

We may also use cookies to track postings to online discussions about our Services that are controlled by us. While you can disable cookies, some areas of our Site or our Services may not function with all available features if you do so.

You can manage Cookie settings by navigating to and opening the “Help” menu on the browser menu bar. Generally, browsers will let you how you can disable or delete Cookies or set your browser to alert you when Cookies are being sent to your computer. If you do not allow all Cookies, you may not be able to use various features of our Sites or Services.

Your use of the Sites or our Services means that we will assume that you consent to receive cookies from us unless you have changed your cookie choices as discussed above.
If we are going to use your personal information in a manner that is materially different from that stated at the time of collection, we will post a notice prominently on our Sites and may also make a modification to this Privacy Policy. You will have a choice as to whether or not we use your information in this different manner. In some cases where we post the notice we may also email users, who have opted to receive communications from us, notifying them of the changes in our privacy practices.

We may share some aggregate data that we learn about users (which is data that does not contain personal information) with third parties.
