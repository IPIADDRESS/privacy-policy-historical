Cookies:

A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's computer tied to information about the user. Session ID cookies simply terminate once you close your browser. A persistent cookie is a small text file stored on the user's hard drive for an extended period of time. Persistent cookies can be removed by following Internet browser help file directions. You can set your browser to notify you to either accept or deny the cookie according to the following information.

TerraServer uses cookies to enhance the performance of the website, to track subscription sign in status and to store items added to your shopping cart.
