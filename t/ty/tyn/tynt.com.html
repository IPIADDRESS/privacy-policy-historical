In Plain English

Our privacy policy describes how we treat your personal information when you use Tynt products and services. We also know that most people reading it aren’t lawyers, so here’s what we mean in plain English.

What kind of personal information does Tynt collect?

We DO NOT collect personally identifiable information about users (names, email addresses, phone numbers, etc). We do collect your IP address, but our policy is to not store this information.

How do we use the information we do collect?

• Tynt processes copy data in order to provide analytics to Publishers and website owners. We compile analytics information on things like: number of copies, top engaged with content, new traffic, new links.
• We use the information to improve our services for our publishers.
• We have a unique signal of what’s trending on the Internet and we are looking at ways to surface the most engaging content to our publishers.

What we DO NOT do.

• We don’t collect email addresses or email contents
• We do not sell, rent or otherwise share your personal information.
• We don’t build “profiles” of users or their activities

Do I have a choice? What if I don’t want Tynt to see me?

• You can prevent publishers from monitoring your copy actions by choosing to opt out of Tynt.
