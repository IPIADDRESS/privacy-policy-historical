By using Tyler’s website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Tyler does require you to provide personal information in order to visit restricted areas, respond to your inquiries, provide you with news and information, or allow you to register for special events. If you provide personal information while visiting the Web site, you may review or edit your profile at any time. Should you wish to stop receiving information and updates, you may unsubscribe at any time. Unsubscribing will prevent you from accessing restricted areas of the http://www.tylertech.com/ Web site. There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected by http://www.tylertech.com/. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring Web site addresses. This information is used by Tyler for the operation of the service, to maintain the quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the http://www.tylertech.com/ Web site. Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally identifiable information or personally sensitive data through http://www.tylertech.com/ public message boards, this information may be collected and used by others. Note: Tyler does not read any of your private online communications. Tyler encourages you to review the privacy statements of Web sites you choose to link to from http://www.tylertech.com/ so that you can understand how those Web sites collect, use and share your information. Tyler is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on Web sites outside of the http://www.tylertech.com/ and Tyler family of Web sites.

Use of your Personal Information
Tyler collects and uses your personal information to operate the http://www.tylertech.com/ Web site and deliver the services you have requested. Tyler also uses your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from Tyler and its affiliates. Tyler may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered. Tyler does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. Tyler may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. In those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (e-mail, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party. Tyler does not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations, without your explicit consent. Tyler keeps track of the Web sites and pages our customers visit within www.tylertech.com in order to determine what services are the most popular. Tyler Web sites will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on www.tylertech.com or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of www.tylertech.com; and (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of www.tylertech.com or the public.

Use of Cookies
The http://www.tylertech.com/ Web site uses "cookies" to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you. One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalize http://www.tylertech.com's/ pages, or register with the http://www.tylertech.com/ site or services, a cookie helps http://www.tylertech.com/ to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses, and so on. When you return to the same http://www.tylertech.com/ Web site, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the http://www.tylertech.com/ features that you customized. You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the www.tylertech.com services or Web sites you visit.

Security of your Personal Information
Tyler secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. Tyler secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to other Web sites, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol.

Changes to this Statement
Tyler will occasionally update this Statement of Privacy to reflect company and customer feedback. Tyler encourages you to periodically review this Statement to be informed of how Tyler is protecting your information.
