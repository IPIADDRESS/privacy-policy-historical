This privacy policy statement applies to TASER.com, iTASER.com, EVIDENCE.com, TASERX26.com, TASERX3.com, and protector.com web sites only (the “TASER International websites”). It does not apply to information collected and stored by a means other than these websites.

Access to information about customers collected from the TASER International websites is restricted to TASER International and third parties that need access to customer information in order to provide TASER operational or other support services, TASER product information, or product, service or upgrade offers. TASER International does not distribute, sell or rent any personal information collected via its websites.
