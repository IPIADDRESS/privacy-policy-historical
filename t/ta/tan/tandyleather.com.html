Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to Tandy Leather and we take it very seriously. Tandy Leather does not sell or rent personally, identifiable information to third parties.

If you choose to purchase products from our site or request a catalog, you will need to provide the following information so that we can fulfill your order: Name, Contact Phone Number(s), Mailing Address, Shipping Address, E-mail Address and, if you are purchasing products, Credit Card Account Information.

Tandy Leather and its affiliates may use your personally, identifiable information to deliver information to you that may be targeted to your interests, to improve Tandy Leather's marketing and promotional efforts, to analyze site usage, or to improve our content and product offerings. Tandy Leather may use your e-mail address, mailing address, or phone number to contact you regarding administrative matters, billing, special promotions or to provide associated support.

Tandy Leather may use "profile information" to create aggregate reports (with information such as user demographics and traffic patterns) that can be shared with our business partners to create personalized content and services. However, "profile information" is anonymous and does not contain personally identifiable information.

Tandy Leather reserves the right to cooperate fully with state, local and federal officials in any investigation relating to this web site or its contents (including personal or private electronic communication transmitted on this web site) or purported illegal activities of any user.

If, for any reason, you wish not to be on our electronic mail list, please send a message to tand-e-mail@tandyleather.com and we will gladly remove you from our list.
