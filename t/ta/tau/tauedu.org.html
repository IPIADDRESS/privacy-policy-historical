Safe senders list

To ensure you receive email notifications from Texila American University about your application status, program details, payment receipt confirmation etc., please add 'info@tauedu.org' to your safe sender's list. Below is the procedure to add 'info@tauedu.org' to your safe sender's list across mail clients.

Outlook : Under 'Tools' menu bar, select options. Under 'preferences', select 'junk email' and click on 'safe sender' tab. Click 'add' button. Type 'info@tauedu.org' and submit.

MSN Hotmail : In the options page, click 'mail' on the left hand menu. Then click on 'junk email protection' and click on 'add senders to safe list'. Type 'info@tauedu.org' and then click 'add’.

Yahoo! Mail : Under 'addresses' on the top menu, click 'add contact'. Type 'info@tauedu.org' in the email field and click 'save'.

Gmail : Go to 'Mail settings' and select 'filters'. 'Create a new filter' and type 'info@tauedu.org' at the 'From' field. Click on 'next step' and check 'never send it to spam'. Click on 'create filter'.
