When you create a Taafoo account, we keep track of your interactions with us, including but not limited to your site activity, order history, payment history and correspondence. Participation in these profiles, surveys or questionnaire is completely voluntary and you have a choice whether or not to disclose this information. By submitting this information, you enable us to provide you a more personalized Taafoo experience.

Please note that information you provide in an optional public profile can be viewed by anyone, and can potentially be used to contact you or send you unsolicited messages. We post customer comments on our web site which may contain personally identifiable information such as the customer's name. We do obtain the customer's consent prior to posting the comment to post their name along with their comment.

Emails and SMS Messages

By registering for a Taafoo account, you agree that we may send you the following types of email communications: welcome emails, our weekly newsletter, account status updates and promotional content. Our Services feature the distribution of content to your mobile phone, and by registering for a Taafoo account, you agree to receive SMS messages to your phone, including messages regarding Services you request, account status updates, and occasional SMS newsletters. You may also receive SMS adverts only when we send you information you request. You will also have the opportunity to opt-in and receive surveys and promotional content by our affiliates. You may opt out of our SMS newsletter by sending an email to customercare@taafoo.com or by responding to the SMS newsletter indicating that you would like to unsubscribe.

Our website provides you with the opportunity to refer your friends, family members or relatives to the Taafoo Services. For this feature of our website to work, we need to collect your friends and families' email addresses. The email addresses we collect in this context are not used for any other purpose other than to send a one-time referral.

Taafoo uses your personal information in the following general ways: To provide you with our Services, to allow for your identification and authentication onto the Taafoo website, for billing and administrative functions, to contact you, and for internal research and reporting purposes. We also use the information optionally provided by you in surveys for purposes of monitoring or improving the use and satisfaction of the Taafoo Service. Third parties who provide website support services, billing, processing, fulfillment, marketing, and other services on our behalf may also access your personal information to provide those services.

We may send you website and service announcement updates with important information about the service through the email address or cell phone number you provide. We may also notify you about special Taafoo-specific promotions available through our trusted partners. You can opt-out of receiving these promotional emails, but not service related messages, at any time either through the unsubscribe link in the email we send you.

By registering for a Taafoo account or providing information on other parts of the Taafoo site, you represent that you are 13 years of age or older. If you are between 13 and 17, you agree that you received parental permission both to complete the registration process and to receive content on your mobile phone. We will not knowingly collect personal information of children under 13 years of age, and if we learn that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will promptly delete it.

Viewing and Updating Your Account Information

If your personal information changes, or if you want to update your email preferences, or if you want to cancel the Taafoo Service, you may update your profile by visiting the Account Settings section of your profile on our Web site.
