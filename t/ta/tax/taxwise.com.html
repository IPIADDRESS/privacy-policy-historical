Information Collection and Use

Universal Tax Systems, Inc. is the sole owner of the information collected from the TaxWise.com site. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement. UTS collects information from our users at several different points on our website.

Registration

In order to access advanced support options, a user must first complete the user login form. During this login process a user is required to give his/her EFIN and registration code. This information is used to identify the user before he/she accesses the advanced support options. It is required for the user to provide this information when accessing advanced support options so we can provide a more personalized support experience.

Ordering

We request information from the user on our order form. Here a user must provide contact information (such as name and shipping address) and financial information (such as credit card number and expiration date). This information is used for billing purposes and to fulfill the customer's order(s). If we have difficulty processing an order, the contact information is used to get in touch with the user. Use of all TaxWise products is subject to the terms and conditions of the current UTS software license agreement. View License Agreement now.

Cookies

A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's hard drive containing information about the user. Once the user closes the browser, the cookie simply terminates. For instance, by setting a cookie on our site, the user would not have to login to the customer support site more than once, thereby saving time while on our site. If a user rejects the cookie, they may still use our site. Cookies can also enable use to track and target the interest of our users to enhance the experience on our site.

Log Files

We use IP addresses to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

Sharing

We process credit card transactions directly with Visa Net for all billed goods and services. Visa Net does not retain, share, store or use personally identifiable information for any secondary purposes.

We partner with other third party companies to provide specific services such as bank products. When the user signs up for these services, we will share their names, or contact information that is necessary for the third party to provide these services.

These parties are not allowed to use personally identifiable information except for the purpose of providing these services.

Personally identifiable information is not otherwise released by UTS to outside parties except as set forth in this Privacy Statement unless required by law or legal process (for example, subpoena). UTS reserves the right to make exceptions to this policy in exceptional circumstances (such as suspected illegal activity) on a case by case basis and at the sole discretion of UTS. UTS abides by applicable laws and regulations governing the disclosure of information to governmental agencies. When responding to legal process served on UTS by non-governmental agencies, unless otherwise ordered, the policy of UTS is to make reasonable efforts to notify affected customers in advance of releasing requested information and to provide customers the opportunity to pursue available legal protection.

Links

TaxWise.com contains links to other web sites. Please be aware that we, UTS, are not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by TaxWise.com.

If a user wishes to be notified when a new version of a product has been released, he/she has the ability to opt-in to the Product Update Notification. We ask for contact information such as email address so that we can notify the user when product updates are available.

Choice/Opt-out

Users who no longer with to receive Product Update Notifications may opt-out of receiving these communications by using the de-select option in the Product Update Notification system.

Request Evaluation Kit Form

Users who wish to request an evaluation kit may complete the form on our request an evaluation kit page. We ask for contact information such as name, address, telephone, and email address. This information is automatically forwarded to our Sales department to fulfill the user's request.

Return Query

Users may check the status of returns they filed through the UTS Electronic Filing Center by providing the Primary SSN of the return in our Return Query Form.

My Information

TaxWise users may review the information we have on file for their office in the My Information section of the Support site. This is the same information our Customer Support department uses to service your account. Users may automatically update certain information in this section such as contact name, company name, telephone, and email. Other information in this section can be updated by sending an email message to our Customer Support department using the links provided in the My Information section.

Information collected in the ERO Bank Application is specifically used for, with your permission, consideration of your participation in our Audit Shield extended warranty program and submission to participating banks for consideration of your participation in our porgram to offer bank products.

Participation in these surveys or contests is completely voluntary and the user therefore has a choice whether or not to disclose this information. From time-to-time we offer on-line registration for various events such as trade shows, training seminars, private TaxWise demonstrations, and user conferences. Information requested may include contact information, demographic information, financial information (if applicable) and additional information such as special requests and user preferences required to complete the registration process. This information is used for billing purposes and to fulfill the customer's request. If we have difficulty processing an event registration request, the contact information is used to get in touch with the user.

Security

Universal Tax Systems, Inc. takes strong precautions to protect your users' information. When users submit sensitive information via the website, your information is protected both online and off-line.

When our return query form asks users to enter sensitive information such as a social security number, that information is protected with encryption software that sets the Internet standart for secure transactions -- SSL. While on a secure page the lock icon on the bottom of web browsers such as Microsoft Internet Explorer becomes locked, as opposed to un-locked or open, when you are just 'surfing'. To learn more about SSL and TaxWise.com security measures, see our Security Statement.

While we use SSL encryption to protect sensitive information online, we also take strong precautions to protect a user's information off-line. All of our users' information, not just the sensitive information mentioned above, is restricted in our offices. Only employees who require the inforamtion to perform a specific job (for example, our billing clerk or a customer support representative) are granted access to personally identifiable information. Our employees must record the instance in our records each time your information is accessed. Furthermore, ALL employees are kept up-to-date on our security and privacy practices. On an ongoing basis, as well as any time new policies are added, our employees are notified and/or reminded about the importance we place on privacy, and what they can do to ensure our users' information is protected. Finally, the servers that we store personally identifiable information on are kept in a secure environment, in a limited access room inside a locked case.
