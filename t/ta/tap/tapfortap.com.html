If this privacy policy is not acceptable to you, you should not use or cease to use the service. This Privacy Policy is incorporated into and forms part of Pretio Interactive’s Terms of Service which are available at http://terms.pretio.in/.

What Does This Privacy Policy Cover?

By visiting the Website or using the Service this Privacy Policy covers information you may submit to Pretio Interactive, including your name, email address, reward gifts (“Gifts”) you send and receive, and other data you generate while using Service or the Website. It also covers information that we gather automatically when you use the Service or our Website, including your IP address, browser type, cookie information, and browsing patterns.

This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that Pretio Interactive does not own or control, or to individuals that Pretio Interactive does not employ or manage.

Personal Information You Provide to Us

We receive and store any information you enter through the Service or provide to us in any other way.

The types of information we collect include your name, email address, browser or mobile device information, and any other information necessary for us to provide the Service.

The information you provide is used to help you receive personalized Gifts, customize the content and advertising you see, and to communicate with you about specials and new features. We also draw upon this information to better adapt the Service to your needs, to research the effectiveness of our network, and to develop new products and services.

Personal Information Collected Automatically

Pretio Interactive automatically receives and stores information from your browser including your IP address, Media Access Control (“MAC”) address and unique device identifier or other persistent or non-persistent device identifier (“Device ID”), device software platform and firmware, geographical data such as zip code, postal code, area code and location, cookie information, and browsing patterns.

Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that we transfer to your computer through your browser to recognize your browser and tell us how and when you access the Service or visit pages on our Website. You have the option to disable cookies by changing the options in your browser to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the pages you visit. We recommend that you leave cookies activated, however, because cookies are needed in order to use the Service.

Pretio Interactive uses cookie and other browsing data in aggregate form to track Website usage and improve your experience. We may provide usage information to our partners in order to help them understand how often people use their services and our Website. Pretio Interactive never discloses information to a partner in a manner that would identify you personally.
