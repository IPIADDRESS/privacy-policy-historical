This privacy policy (“Privacy Policy”) describes the information collected by TapResearch, Inc. (“we,” “us,” or “our”), how we collect that information, how that information may be used, and with whom it may be shared. By accessing or using our website, currently located at https://www.tapresearch.com, (including any subpages, directories, or mobile versions of the website) (collectively the “Website”), participating in our surveys, and/or using other products or services we may provide (each a “Service,” and collectively, “Services”), you are accepting the practices set forth in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, then you must not access or use the Services.

 

Information Collected and Method of Collection

(a) Information collected automatically: When you use the Services, we automatically collect and store information about your computer or mobile device and your online activities (“Response Metadata”), which you grant us the permission to transfer and store in accordance with the provisions contained in this Privacy Policy. Response Metadata may include:

 

·      Mobile Device ID. Unique ID numbers associated with your mobile device, including advertising IDs (e.g., IDFA).

·      Geographic Location. Your mobile device's geographic location and the applicable time zone.

·      Device Information/Specifications. Technical information about your computer or device (e.g., type of device, web browser or operating system, IP address).

·      Time, Length and Extent of Usage. When you use the Services, how long you use a Service for (e.g., how long it takes you to answer a survey question) and which Services feature(s) and/or function(s) you use.

·      Cookies: We may use “cookies” to keep track of some types of information while you are using the Services. “Cookies” are very small files placed on your computer, and they allow us to count the number of users of the Services and distinguish repeat users from new or unique users. Cookies help provide additional functionality to the Services and help us analyze usage of the Services more accurately, such as user preferences or trends (e.g., the Services may set a cookie on your browser that allows you to bypass engaging with a survey due to your previous completion of a survey). We rely on cookies for the proper operation of the Services; therefore if your browser is set to reject cookies, the Services may not function properly. If you refuse cookies from the Services, you assume all responsibility for any resulting loss of functionality or benefits with respect to the Services.

·      Web Beacons: We may use web beacons in email communications in order to understand the behavior of our customers. “Web beacons” (also known as “clear gifs” and “pixel tags”) are small transparent graphic images that are often used in conjunction with cookies in order to further personalize the Services for our users and to collect a limited set of information about our users.

 

(b) Information you provide to us: We collect any information that you submit to us voluntarily through the Services or provide to us in any other way. For example, when you choose to participate in a survey offered through the Services, we will collect the information you provide within the survey (each a "Survey Response"). You grant us permission to transfer and store your Survey Responses, and these responses may be shared with and/or sold to third parties (e.g., our customers) in accordance with the section entitled “Sharing Your Information” below.

 

(c) Developers: For our developer partners, we may collect certain information from you at the time of registration and in connection with your use of the Services, including your name, email address, company name, app title(s), as well as financial information such as Tax ID, VAT or sales tax registration number or any other relevant tax number and payment information (e.g., bank account and routing number for ACH purposes).
