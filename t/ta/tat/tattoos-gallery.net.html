Tattoos Gallery Privacy Policy

Tattoos Gallery is very concerned of its visitors privacy. Therefore, we have created a short and strict privacy policy statements, with no fluff.

Tattoos Gallery servers automatically track visitors' IP addresses, visited pages and referrer pages. We also record information offered to us by our users voluntarily.

This information is used to improve our services and overall user experience.

We use third party advertisers to support Tattoos-Gallery.net. These advertisers may use technology such as cookies and web beacons when they advertise on our site. These advertisers may receive your IP address, your ISP, the browser you are using, and various other system settings. This is used to target the advertisements better. You can choose to disable our cookies or third-party cookies in your browser settings.

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on your site.

Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

We will never share or sell your personal information without your consent, except of the cases mentioned above and unless required by law.
