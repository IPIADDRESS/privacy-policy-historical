privacy policy . . .

What information does "Take Them A Meal" collect regarding meal recipients, and how is it used?
We request a name, and provide a user password. This information is used to allow meal deliverers to find the meal schedule for the recipient to whom they are delivering a meal. We also allow for a meal coordinator to provide details on when and where to deliver the meals. This information is also displayed to meal deliverers.

What information does "Take Them A Meal" collect regarding meal coordinators, and how is it used?
We request a name, e-mail address and phone number. This information is then displayed to meal deliverers in case they need to contact the meal coordinator.

What information does "Take Them A Meal" collect regarding meal deliverers, and how is it used?
We request a name, e-mail address and phone number. This information is displayed to the meal coordinator and to the meal deliverers, in case either would need to contact a meal deliverer regarding changes or cancellations of meals.

Does "Take Them A Meal" use the information collected for any other purposes not described above?
From time to time we may send an e-mail to all past meal coordinators and meal deliverers if we have a particularly exciting "Take Them A Meal" announcement. Aside from the (very) occasional update, we do not use the information for any other purposes. We do not give or sell the collected names, phone numbers or e-mail addresses to anyone, for any reason.

In what ways can the general public access the information stored on "Take Them A Meal"?
As is hopefully evident through use of "Take Them A Meal", information can only be accessed by knowing the last name and user password for a meal recipient.
