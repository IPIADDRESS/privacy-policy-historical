Cookies
Cookies are information files that are placed on the hard drive of your computer by your browser. Cookies allow websites to remember users activities and visits to a site. At T-Shirts.com, we use cookies to determine the number of unique visitors we host on our website over any given period of time. Cookies are also used to remember user screen names so multiple logins are not necessary. We do not use cookies to store or gather personal information from our visitors. You can disable cookies on your computer by indicating this in the preferences or options menus in your browser. For more about cookies see [http://www.cookiecentral.com].
