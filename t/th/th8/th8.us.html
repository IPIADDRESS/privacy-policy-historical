PRIVACY POLICY FOR TH8.US
If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at support@th8.us.

At Th8.us, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by Th8.us and how it is used.

ADVERTISEMENTS ON REDIRECTED URLS
To help cover our hosting costs, Th8.us displays Amazon, Adbrite, or Google Adsense advertisements in an iFrame above most URLs shortened through our service. If you do not want these ads displayed on your website(s), please email a list of all your domains to support@th8.us and we will promptly modify all short URLs for your domains to forward directly to the destination page using an HTTP 301 redirect, without advertising.

PUBLICATION OF SHORTENED URLS
All URLs shortened through Th8.us should be considered public, and we may in some point in the future make our databases publicly accessible, including the short URLs, long URLs, and dates of creation. If you have shortened URLs that you want removed from our database, please email the list to support@th8.us with some proof that you created them or they reveal personal information about you or someone you know.

LOG FILES
Like many other Web sites, Th8.us makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

COOKIES AND WEB BEACONS
Th8.us does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.
