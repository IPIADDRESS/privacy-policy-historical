FAQs

  • How do I log into my Citi account?
    Just click the "Sign On" button on the top-right of thankyou.com. If you have an eligible consumer Citibank checking account or Citi credit card that participates in ThankYou Rewards, sign-on to Thankyou.com with your Citi username and password. If you do not have a Citi username and password, click "Register Your Card or Checking Account" on the left side of the page and follow the online setup process.

    If you are enrolled in ThankYou Rewards through another relationship, click the "Sign On" button on the top-right of our website and then click "Register Your Card or Checking Account" on the right side of the page and follow the online setup process.
  • I am being told that I need to setup a Citi username and password, why is this?
    You will now be able to access Citibank® Online, Citicards.com and ThankYou.com with a single Citi® username and password. ThankYou.com is not going away, we're just making it easier for you to access our sites with a single Citi® username and password.
  • What happens to my points after my Thankyou.com username and password has been deactivated?
    Nothing. Your points will not be affected in any way, and neither will your ThankYou Member Account or personal settings on Thankyou.com.
  • What is a One-Time Password and how does this work?
    One-time passwords are part of an authentication process to help protect your ThankYou Member account. During a session, you may be asked to provide a one-time password. To complete this request, you may choose to have a code sent to your phone number on file either by text message or by phone call. Once you receive the code, simply enter the code online and submit. Once completed, you will be able to continue with your activity.
  • Can I combine my ThankYou Points from multiple Citi Accounts that participate in ThankYou Rewards?
    Absolutely, as long as all the Citi Accounts are owned by the same person. If you have one (or more) Citi® credit cards participating in ThankYou® Rewards, eligible Citibank® consumer or business checking account and other linked banking products and services or an enrolled Citi Corporate Travel & Entertainment Card, you can combine these accounts and pool the points into your one ThankYou Member Account. Simply go to Add a Sponsor Account in the My Citi Account section to pool all your points into one place.

    Please note that if you combine your ThankYou Member Accounts, any action taken concerning points from one Citi Account may affect all the points in your ThankYou Member Account.
  • How long will it take for my ThankYou® Points to appear on my ThankYou Points Summary?
    Depending on your billing cycle or statement closing date, ThankYou Points received on eligible new purchases made with a Citi credit card should appear on your Points Summary following your statement closing date. Bonus Points may take 1 to 2 additional billing cycles to appear (up to 30-60 days).

    ThankYou Points earned with your enrolled Citibank® Checking account are deemed awarded when transferred to your ThankYou Member Account at the end of the monthly account statement period based on the number of eligible Products and Services linked to your checking account.

    Once signed on, access your Points Summary on Thankyou.com by clicking the My Account icon on the top-right of the site, then click My Points Summary.
  • Can I return my reward?
    Most merchandise items can be returned as long as the item is in its original packaging and is post-marked within 15 days of the original shipping date. All other rewards, including gift cards/certificates rewards, can only be returned if they are damaged or defective. You may request a return through My Order History or by calling the ThankYou Service Center at 1-800-THANKYOU (1-800-842-6596).
  • What is ThankYou® Rewards?
    ThankYou® Rewards is a program that lets you earn points and pool them in a single ThankYou Member Account so you can redeem them for rewards you want faster. You can earn ThankYou Points when you make purchases with an eligible Citi® credit card (including an enrolled Citi® Corporate Travel & Entertainment Card) that participates in ThankYou Rewards. You can also earn points when you enroll an eligible Citibank consumer checking account in ThankYou Rewards and have additional qualifying Citibank accounts and services.
  • Can I book travel through chat?
    No, however travel can be booked online by clicking the Travel link under the "Shop Browse rewards" tab, found at the top-left of thankyou.com. Travel can also be booked through the ThankYou® Service Center by calling 1-800-THANKYOU (1-800-842-6596).
  • What if I have a travel redemption question?
    You can access our travel FAQs by clicking here. For additional assistance, please call the ThankYou® Service Center at 1-800-THANKYOU (1-800-842-6596).

1. What Information Do We Collect?

We collect two types of online information: non-personal and personal.

Non-Personal Information

When you visit our site, we collect the IP Address Detailed Explanation of the device you use to connect to the Internet. In addition, we gather information such as what browser and which version of it you're using, the type of operating system you have, and which site you came from or advertisement you viewed or clicked on. This information helps us provide an online experience that matches your device and to manage our online advertising.

When you browse our site or receive one of our emails, we and companies we work with use cookies Detailed Explanation and/or pixel tags Detailed Explanation to collect information and store your online preferences. Cookies are widely used and most browsers are set up to accept them automatically. If you would prefer, you can choose to not accept cookies.

It's important to note that cookies and pixel tags do not capture any information that can personally identify you. The information they gather helps us to improve your online experience and may include:

  • Your response to one of our emails
  • Time and duration of your visit to our site
  • Pages you viewed while on our site

We may also supplement the information we collect with information we receive from other companies. For example, we may use marketing segments Detailed Explanation developed by us or other companies to customize certain services to your local area and provide relevant offers tailored to you.

Personal Information

You can visit and browse this site to find out about our program, Sponsors and rewards without giving us any information about yourself. When you enroll in ThankYou Rewards and set up your user name and password, we collect personally identifiable information about you as follows:

  • Setting Up Your Account: In order to redeem your ThankYou Points for rewards, you must set up a user name and password, either online or by calling the ThankYou Service Center. This process requires certain personally identifiable information including: your name, postal address, email address, telephone number, and a unique individual password, which we can use to verify your identity. You may also choose to provide us with information about your interests and ThankYou reward preferences.
  • Transactions & Activities: If you set up a user name and password and redeem for rewards, we will collect information about your transactions with us. This information includes areas of the website that you visit, redemption history, account activity, and shipping and billing information. We may also receive information about you, such as your earning activity from ThankYou Sponsors.

    ThankYou Members may review and change contact information such as address, phone and email information by signing on and updating their personal profile.

4. Can You Control Your Information That's Collected and Used Online?

It's important to note that the information we use about you helps us provide you with products, services and experiences that benefit you. You have the ability to control how your non-personal inf`ormation is collected and used online.

E-Mail

When you provide your email address, we will use it to send you general notices or important news about your account, request your feedback or opinions and provide updates on special deals and offers that might interest you.

We believe you should be able to choose what kinds of information you receive via email. If you do not want to receive marketing materials by email, just indicate your preference on the email profile for your account or the 'opt-out' link provided in our marketing emails. Please keep in mind that we will continue to notify you by email regarding your account.

Flash objects

We may use Flash objects (sometimes referred to as "Local Shared Objects") on certain of our Web sites. We may use Flash objects to help us recognize you and your computer when you return to our site. Additionally, Flash objects may be used to help us avoid fraud or security attempts directed at our sites. Also if we display pages running Adobe® Flash® content (animated content), we use Flash objects to determine your browser type and version of Adobe Flash in order for you to view the content. As with regular cookies, we never store personal information in these Flash object files unless encrypted. Deleting cookies does not delete Flash objects. You can learn more about Flash objects - including how to control and disable them - through the Adobe interface. If you choose to delete Flash objects from our Sites, then you may not be able to access and use all or part of the Site or benefit from the information and services offered.

Cookies

You can control whether to accept cookies Detailed Explanation or not.

If you decide to not accept cookies, some features and services on our site may not work properly because we may not be able to recognize and associate you with your ThankYou Account. In addition, the offers we provide when you visit us may not be as relevant to you or tailored to your interests.

If you would prefer not to accept cookies, you can:

  • Change your browser settings to notify you when you receive a cookie, which lets you choose whether or not to accept it
  • Set your browser to automatically not accept any cookies

Targeted Advertising

If you prefer to not receive targeted advertising, you can opt out of some network advertising programs that use your information. To do so please visit the NAI Opt-Out Page. Please note that even if you choose to remove your information (opt out), you will still see advertisements while you're browsing online. However the advertisements you see may be less relevant to you and your interests.

Additionally, many network advertising programs allow you to view and manage the interest categories they have compiled from your online browsing activities. These interest categories help determine the types of targeted advertisements you may receive. The NAI Opt-Out Page provides a tool that identifies its member companies that have cookies on your browser and provides links to those companies.

The Digital Advertising Alliance's Self-Regulatory Principles for Online Behavioral Advertising govern certain online advertising and data collection practices. The principles facilitate transparency about online advertising practices and support consumer choice in managing behavioral advertising. We subscribe to these Principles. For more information about the Principles and the About Ads icon visit http://www.youradchoices.com/.

Do Not Track

Can I change my privacy choices at any time?

Yes, you can adjust these choices anytime, directly from the Privacy Choices Form or by calling us toll free at the ThankYou® Service Center, 1-800-THANKYOU (1-800-842-6596). TDD/TTY for the hearing impaired, please call 1-877-693-0218.


The privacy choices you make on the Privacy Choices Form on ThankYou.com or by calling our Service Center will apply only to ThankYou Rewards and will apply until you make a change either through ThankYou Rewards, Citi Cards or Citibank.


If you are a Citi® credit cardholder or have a Citibank® consumer checking account enrolled in ThankYou Rewards, you will need to contact those sponsors directly to change your privacy choices with them.


If you provide privacy choices to Citi Cards or Citibank after providing your choices directly to ThankYou Rewards, the choices you provide to those sponsors will apply to, and be honored by, ThankYou Rewards also and will alter the choices you previously provided to ThankYou Rewards to the extent they differ. Privacy choices made on ThankYou.com or through our Service Center will not affect the privacy choices provided to Citi Cards or Citibank.


Please allow approximately 30 days from the request of your privacy choices for them to become effective.
