Email

Our privacy policy is simple: here at theCouponScoop we love our privacy, and we hate spam, and we assume that you feel the same way. The only personal data we collect from you is your email address, and we only collect that if you choose to subscribe to our newsletter. Your email address will only be used to send you our newsletter, and you may unsubscribe at any time by using the link at the bottom of our newsletter, or by sending an email from the address to be unsubcribed to info@thecouponscoop.com, with the word "unsubscribe" in the subject.

The only third party with whom we will share your email address is our mailing list provider, Vertical Response. Vertical Response has a strict anti-spam policy, which you may view here. TheCouponScoop will never sell your email address, or share it with any third party other than our mailing list provider, and this policy is NOT subject to change. You can shop safely here at theCouponScoop, with the knowledge that your privacy is secure.
