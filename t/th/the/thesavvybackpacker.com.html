Advertising/Privacy Policy

Advertising Policy

I respect my readers, and I believe in honesty and complete transparency. That said, I do earn a small commission (between 1%-5%) when someone purchases a product/service through one of my referral links. This helps cover the costs to run this website, and all other profits are put towards further travels (and maybe the occasional beer).
I NEVER recommend any product/service that I don’t believe in. I will always suggest the best/cheapest option even if it results in no financial gain. This issue is very important to me.

If you are interested in advertising with us or have any questions please send an email to savvybackpacker [at] gmail.com

I will only accept advertisements for companies I believe to be worthwhile to my readers.

About The Savvy Backpacker

The Savvy Backpacker was created because I love traveling through Europe. I've spent many hours researching and planning my multiple European adventures and I've learned a lot of great information doing so. This site caters to the new breed of tech-savvy budget backpackers who want honest and independent travel advice.

While researching my own European travels I found that most backpacking sites were full of multiple annoying banner ads and booking portals. I want my site to be different. My goal is to create a site with minimal advertising and maximum quality information.
