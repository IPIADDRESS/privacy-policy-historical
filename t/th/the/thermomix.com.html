Collection of Information

Personal Information

We collect personal information from you when you submit it to us, for example by (i) placing an order; (ii) registering as a customer; (iii) emailing us; (iv) participating in promotions, surveys, email newsletters or other features of the Site; (v) communicating or coordinating with a customer service representative; and (vi) interacting with us through our Facebook page or other social media services. Such personal information may include your name, contact information, credit card or other payment information, shipping information, and other applicable information. If you use the Site to forward information to a friend, we will also collect the information you provide for your friend. We may combine the personal information that we collect with information available from other sources, including Thermomix Independent Consultants, promotional partners, or other third parties. We may request additional personal information if you become or apply to become a Thermomix Independent Consultant, such as your Social Security number, date of birth, and financial information in order to provide payments due to you.

Non-Personal Information

When you visit the Site, we (independently or through service providers) may collect certain non-personal information from you, including your computer’s Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type, operating system, the specific web pages visited during your connection, and the domain name from which you accessed the Site. In addition, we may collect information about your browsing behavior, such as the date and time you visit the Site, the areas or pages of the Site that you visit, the amount of time you spend viewing the Site, the number of times you return to the Site and other clickstream data. Like many commercial websites, we may collect this non-personal information using “cookie” technology. A cookie is a small text file that is placed on your computer when you access the Site and allows us to recognize you each time you visit the Site. We may also use web beacons and other similar technologies on the Site, in our emails and in our advertisements on other websites. Web beacons are tiny graphic images that are used to collect information about your Site visit, such as the pages you view and the features you use, as well as information about whether you open and/or act upon one of our emails or advertisements. We may use these technologies to collect non-personal information for a variety of purposes such as to analyze how the Site is used, personalize your experience on the Site, or improve our content or offerings. Your browser may provide tools to block or delete cookies. However, if your browser is set to reject cookies or you manually delete cookies, you may have some trouble accessing and using some of the pages and features that are currently on our Site, or that we may put on our Site in the future. We may combine the non-personal information we collect, such as through cookies and web beacons, with other information we have collected from you. Our service providers may also combine the information that they collect with information collected across other sites over time.

We may use the information we collect from and about you, including information collected through social media services, to process your requests and otherwise operate and improve our business. For example, we may use information for any of the following purposes: (i) to complete a transaction (including the fulfillment of orders); (ii) to communicate with you regarding your order/s or to notify you about other products and services that may interest you; (iii) to review Site usage and operations; (iv) to address problems with the Site, our business or our services; (v) to protect the security or integrity of the Site and our business; (vi) to monitor the Site for compliance with our Terms of Use and the law; and (vii) to contact you with Site updates, newsletters and other informational and promotional materials from us. If you become, or apply to become, a Thermomix Independent Consultant, we will also use your information to communicate with you about your business related to Thermomix, process payments to or from you, ship products to you upon request, put you in contact with potential customers, and otherwise interact with you in your role as a Thermomix Independent Consultant.
