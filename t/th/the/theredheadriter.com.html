Who Is The Redhead Riter?


 Hi! I call myself "The Redhead Riter" which is
 "Writer" purposely misspelled. I wanted the
 alliteration of the "R's" thus the "Writer" is spelled
 "Rong"...Yes, I know the proper spelling for "Wrong"
 too.

 

 I'm in the process of writing a couple books.
 Because I was diagnosed with a severe grain
 allergy, I had to change my plans a bit. One is a
 cookbook for not only grain free recipes, but also
 recipes that are filled with grain! Most people are
 not going to give up grain, so I'm all types of
 recipes.

 The other book is about PTSD. Not only do I want
 to share my journey to help others cope with
 the devastating affects of Post Traumatic Stress
 Disorder, but also to bring awareness about this
 devastating disorder that can destroy your life.

 Click here to learn more about me. Thanks for
 visiting my blog today!
