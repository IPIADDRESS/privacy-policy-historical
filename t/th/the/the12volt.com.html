the12volt.com will cooperate to the extent required by law with any law enforcement agency upon presentation of properly executed and served subpoenas or warrants.

We have designed the12volt.com so that no personal identifying information is displayed online or is accessible to the general public. Solicited Information
We do not sell, rent, or share your information to any outside parties under any circumstances.

Use of Your E-mail Address
When you join our forums or submit an installer entry, an email is automatically sent to you. If you are a forum member and have email notification enabled or you are watching any forums or forum topics for replies, an email will be sent to you. You may always un-watch topics and forums by clicking on the appropriate link in the respective area near the bottom of the page. Instructions are also included in each of these emails you receive. If you change your email address in your forum profile, an email will be sent to you to reactivate your membership. If you wish to be removed from our forums, you may do so by sending an email to: deleteMember@the12volt.com.

Cookies

Third party advertisers may be placing and reading cookies on your browser, or web beacons to collect information, in the course of displaying ads on this site. For your options on handling cookies, please see your browser's help file.
