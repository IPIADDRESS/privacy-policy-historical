Privacy Policy

Your Privacy

Please always know that your privacy is a top priority for us!  Any information that you might share with us by any method will not be shared, sold, or used in any malicious way.  Any information you provide to us will not be publicly posted without your written permission.  Our goal is to protect your information while making this site a safe haven for you to learn how to save and become more a more thrifty family.  We encourage you to view our privacy policy below and let us know if you have any questions by visiting our Contact Us page.

E-mails Collected in Comments

We understand your right to your own privacy!  We will never sell or provide your email that you give us to a third-party.  Any emails will be solely used to announce giveaway winners (without exposing the full email address) or contacting you in regards to a giveaway or amazing idea that you might have shared.  We hate spam and figure you probably do too!
