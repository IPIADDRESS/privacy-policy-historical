Privacy Policy

The Garden Helper will never share any information provided by you to any other source or entity.

If you elect to subscribe to our newsletter, your e-mail address will not be used for any other purpose other than to send you the requested information, and will never be sold, rented or otherwise provided to any other entity.

Periodically, you may be asked to provide demographic information for the purpose of attracting advertisers, but this will only be done by way of a link on a web page or in my newsletter. Only pertinent information you submit for this purpose will be released, but not your name or other personal data.

Advertisers, as well as our stat counter may use cookie files to collect anonymous web surfing information to review the effects of their ad strategies, user relevance, and reader interests, but none of this information is personally identifiable. You can prevent the use of these cookies by adjusting the preferences on your internet browser. Most browsers permit you to remove cookies that have been previously stored, as well as prevent the future use of cookies.
