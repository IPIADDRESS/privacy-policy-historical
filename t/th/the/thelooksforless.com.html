    Pronunciation: \ ri-´se-shən-ēs-tə\
    Function: noun
    Date: 3008
    : a budget conscious frugal fashionista that refuses to compromise her amazing style due to an inconvenient recession.
    ant : a chica that is happy dumping a bunch of cash on an outfit when she can get the Celebrity Looks for Less.
