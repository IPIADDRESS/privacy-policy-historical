Privacy Policy Statement

Your privacy is important to us. We strive to ensure the privacy of everyone who visits our site and we would like to tell you about what information we track and how we use it.

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes the IP address. We do not log e-mail addresses. In no case do we use IP addresses to identify individuals.

We collect aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit and information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registrations.

The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web page and to learn about traffic patterns on our site. From time to time, aggregate information may be shared with other reputable companies.

We save e-mails sent to webmaster@thestar.com in order to notify the sender of developments regarding the subject of their e-mail. If you do not wish to receive e-mail replies to your letters, please indicate so in the body of your message. Your e-mail will be read and dealt with and then deleted.

If you supply us with any personal information on-line you will only receive the information for which you provided us your address. Your personal information will not be passed on to any other companies, although aggregate information may be shared from time to time. In all cases, no identifying information will be shared.

We welcome any questions regarding our privacy policy. We can be reached via e-mail at webmaster@thestar.com or you can reach us by telephone at (416) 869-4318. Our postal address is One Yonge Street Toronto, Ontario M5E 1E6.
