Privacy Policy

At The Plan Collection we are very concerned about your privacy and your experience when using our site. We want your visit to be fun and exciting while you choose the home plan of your dreams. To make your experience better we have taken the following measures to optimize the performance of our site and enhance the security of information you have entrusted us with.

Your Personal Information

Any information that we may collect from you is used only for purchases and to inform you of upcoming promotions or changes that we may have made to our site. This information is not redistributed to any other entity and is designed to be accessed only by authorized personnel at ThePlanCollection.com and you the customer. We collect information based on customer preferences to make your experience more exciting, and to help you find the perfect home plan. We do not share this information with other companies.

Security and Encryption

We encrypt all personal and financial information while it is being transmitted via the Internet to our servers. We also encrypt sensitive information in our database. All credit card numbers are removed from our database immediately after an order is processed. This is to provide maximum protection from Internet fraud for our customers.

Site Optimization and Cookie Usage

We use cookies to maintain customer state, shopping cart information, and favorites information. We do not store any financial or sensitive information using cookies. This allows us to improve the performance of our website while maintaining your security. If you choose to disable cookies, some functions of the website will not work however, you will still be able to use our search and browse features.
