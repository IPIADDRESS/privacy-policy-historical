Cookies, the Technology

Cookies are broadly used today and TheUnion.com may incorporate them. Cookies may contain personal information; but it’s important to note that they contain only as much information as you disclose to the site that sent them to you. Cookies enable a Web site to serve you more quickly and conveniently and to provide you with information that most interests you.

Cookies are small packets of information that may be transferred from a Web site computer that you visit and your browser may optionally store the information on your computer’s hard drive. A cookie may be retrieved by a webserver on subsequent visits to a Web site

Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. You can set your browser to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. However, it is possible that some portions of TheUnion.com will not be able to provide you with all available services if you do so.

You also may occasionally get cookies from our advertisers. We cannot control those cookies and they are outside the scope of this Policy. Please refer to the privacy policies of sites that you link to for additional information.
