Information you enter. We also collect information you choose to provide us through the Site or through email. You can choose not to provide us certain personal information, but then you may not be able to obtain or use various products or services (such as CircuitNet, House of Geek, and (IC)2) or otherwise fully take advantage of the features of our Site.
