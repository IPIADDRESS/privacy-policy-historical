ENGLISH SHIRT
The Stiff Collar shirts are Inspired by the impeccable heritage of London's Jermyn Street. Every Stiff Collar shirt is meticulously crafted using the same exacting standards to keep alive & introduce to India the traditional British shirt-making principles. We have tastefully incorporated into our shirts, key English elements such as a Cutaway Collar, Split Yoke, Butterfly Gussets, Cross Stitching and a Long Tail across different fits - providing an exquisite offering for every occasion or body type.

100% COTTON
The Stiff Collar Shirts are constructed using only the finest quality fabrics from top tier mills in India. You'll find our shirts in crisp and comfortable fabric weaves such as Oxford, Poplin, Twill, Tattersall, Herringbone, Fil-a-Fil, Chambray & Satin. Available in a rainbow of diverse colors, our range is offered in different fabric patterns including solids, stripes, checks and prints.
We have also now ventured out into superior grade Linen fabrics.
