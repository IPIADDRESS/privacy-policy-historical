  Privacy statement
We respect your privacy.
Any information you provide to us will be used for internal purposes and the processing of your orders and requests. We collect basic login data; email address, password, company name, shipping address, etc. when you register with us. This information is used to identify our customers and to send order confirmations, process your orders and to contact you should there be a problem with your order.

We value your privacy.
We know that you don't want your name and personal details transferred to third parties. We will not sell, trade or rent your information to a third party. We will only provide your information to a third party, the likes of UPS or FedEx as primary examples, when it is necessary for processing your order and facilitating delivery.

We appreciate your trust.
Any information you give us will be held with the utmost care and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to. We may, from time to time, contact you with updated product information, special deals and to inquire as to the quality of our service to you.

We would be pleased to hear from you.
If you have any questions or comments, please feel free to call us (800) 790-TPMS or email us cs@thepapermillstore.com

With kind regards,
