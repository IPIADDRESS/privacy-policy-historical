Privacy Policy

Disclosures

The Performance Marketing Association (PMA) is a non-profit trade association, incorporated in the state of Delaware in December, 2008. The organization was originally incorporated under the name of Performance Marketing Alliance, and as of December 4, 2009, its name was changed to Performance Marketing Association. The PMA is a 501(c)6 non-profit trade corporation.

The PMA sustains its operations through revenue earned from a variety of methods, including but not limited to memberships, contributions, sponsorships, events, placement fees for ads and listings, and performance-based incentives from certain advertising referrals.

Privacy Policy

The Performance Marketing Association (”PMA”) web site (”Site”) is located at the URL www.thepma.org. This Privacy Policy details certain policies implemented governing the PMA’s use of personally identifiable information provided to the PMA via this Site.

Information Collection and Use

You can visit our Site without revealing any personally identifiable information about yourself. However, in certain sections of this Site, we may ask that you complete and submit an online form with personally identifiable information such as your name, username, email address, and contact information. We may use your personally identifiable information to deliver information you have requested, or to be included in letters or petitions used in the PMA’s grassroots campaigns. The PMA does not sell or provide personally identifiable information about site visitors, those who complete forms, or members, to any third parties.

Cookies

The Site may use small text files, called cookies. The intent is to improve overall Site experience. A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user’s hard drive containing unique but anonymous information about the user. Cookies generally do not permit us to personally identify you. Cookies are not required to be enabled for you to use certain areas of the Site.

Aggregate Information

The Site may track the total number of visitors to our Site, the number of visitors to each page of our Site, IP addresses, External Web Sites (defined below) linked to, and we may analyze this data for trends and statistics in the aggregate, but such information will be maintained, used and disclosed in aggregate form only and it will not contain personally identifiable information. We may use such aggregate information to analyze trends, administer the Site, track users’ movement, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use.

Disclosure

Your contact information may be made available to PMA volunteers who are assigned to contact you in response to your inquiry. The PMA volunteers will not use your information for any other purpose. If you are contacted via this information, for other purposes, please contact Rebecca Madigan, executive director, at rebecca@performancemarketingassociation.com.

Links to Third Party Sites

The Site may provide links to other Web sites or resources over which the PMA does not have control (”External Web Sites”). Such links do not constitute an endorsement by the PMA of those External Web Sites. You acknowledge that PMA is providing these links to you only as a convenience, and further agree that the PMA is not responsible for the content of such External Web Sites. Your use of External Web Sites is subject to the terms of use and privacy policies located on the linked to External Web Sites.

Security

We may employ procedural and technological measures, consistent with industry practice. Such measures are reasonably designed to help protect your personally identifiable information from loss, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration or destruction. The PMA may use encryption, secure socket layer, firewalls, internal restrictions, password protection and other security measures to help prevent unauthorized access to your personally identifiable information.

Correction/Updating Personally Identifiable Information

The PMA provides you with the ability to review, correct and delete any of the personally identifiable information that you have provided to us. If you wish to review or correct any information you have provided to us, you may contact us by email at: membership@performancemarketingassociation.com. The original Privacy Policy was effective as of: September 15, 2008. This current revision is effective as of: October 17, 2011.
