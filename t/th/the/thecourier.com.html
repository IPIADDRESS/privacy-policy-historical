PRIVACY

Findlay Publishing Company Website Privacy Statement

The privacy, confidence, and trust of individuals who visit Findlay Publishing Company websites are important to us. This privacy statement provides information about the personal information that The Findlay Publishing Company collects online, and the ways in which The Findlay Publishing Company uses that personal information. This statement is limited to only the information collected online.

Personal information collection

The Findlay Publishing Company may collect and use the following kinds of personal information:
  • Information about your use of Findlay Publishing Company websites.
  • Information that you provide for the purpose of registering with Findlay Publishing Company websites.
  • Information about transactions carried out over the Findlay Publishing Company websites including the purchase of goods and services offered by the Findlay Publishing Company.
  • Information that you provide for the purpose of subscribing to Findlay Publishing Company website services, including registering to receive text messages and email alerts.
  • Other information that you voluntarily send to Findlay Publishing Company websites.
Using personal information

The Findlay Publishing Company may use your personal information to:
  • Help diagnose problems with our servers, and to administer our web sites.
  • Personalize the websites for you.
  • Enable your access to and use of the website services.
  • Publish information you voluntarily submit on the Findlay Publishing Company websites for publication.
  • Send to you products that you purchase.
  • Supply to you services that you purchase.
  • Send you statements and invoices.
  • Collect payments from you.
  • Send you marketing communications with the choice to opt-in or opt-out.
  • Verify the authenticity of the information or comments you submit for publication.

Where The Findlay Publishing Company discloses your personal information to its agents or sub-contractors for these purposes, the agent or sub-contractor in question will be obligated to use that personal information in accordance with the terms of this privacy statement.

In addition to the disclosures reasonably necessary for the purposes identified elsewhere above, The Findlay Publishing Company may disclose your personal information to the extent that it is required to do so by law, in connection with any legal proceedings or prospective legal proceedings, and in order to establish, exercise or defend its legal rights.

Securing your data

The Findlay Publishing Company will take reasonable technical and organizational precautions to prevent the loss, misuse or alteration of your personal information.

Refund policy

Refunds over $10.00 will be refunded by paper check to the address on the account. We do not retain credit card information and thus do not have the ability to refund to the original credit card used. Any credits that are less than $10.00 can be claimed for cash at our Business Office located at 701 W. Sandusky St. Findlay OH, 45840. Some exceptions can be made to this policy with Management approval.

For a refund you will need to call 419-422-5151 and talk to a customer service representative. The paper check will be issued the following Wednesday.

Delivery policy

Once your subscription begins you should receive your newspaper daily (except on Sunday and designated Holidays) prior to 6:00 a.m. if you live inside the Findlay, Ohio city limits. If you live outside of the city limits of Findlay, Ohio you should receive your newspaper prior to 6:30 a.m. In the event that your newspaper does not arrive by the designated time you can call the Courier at 419-427-5158 or your newspaper delivery person to inquire about the status of your delivery. This designated time is merely a guide rather than a firm time. If your paper is late you will not be issued a refund or credit. A refund or credit will be issued if you did not receive your newspaper (except under mitigating circumstances).

Updating this statement

The Findlay Publishing Company may update this privacy policy by posting a new version on this website. You should check this page occasionally to ensure you are familiar with any changes.

Other websites

Findlay Publishing Company websites contain links to other websites. The Findlay Publishing Company is not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of any third party.
