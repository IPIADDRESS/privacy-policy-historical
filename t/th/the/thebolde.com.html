Privacy

This privacy policy sets out how The Bolde, Inc. uses and protects information that you provide to us in connection with your use of this website.

Please note that we may modify this policy from time to time. You should therefore check this page periodically to ensure that you are comfortable with any changes. This policy is effective as of August 14, 2014.

Name
Email address

Security
We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, we undertake various physical, electronic and managerial efforts to safeguard the information we collect.

Cookies
A cookie is a small file which asks permission to be placed on your computer’s hard drive. Cookies allow web applications to respond to you as an individual. We use cookies. Please note that cookies in no way give us access to your computer or any information about you, other than the data you choose to share with us. You may choose to accept or decline cookies by changing the settings in your web browser.

Our website may contain links to enable you to visit other websites. Please note that we are not responsible for your experience with those other sites as we have no relationship with those other sites. You should review the privacy policy of those sites if you have any questions.
