OUR PRIVACY POLICY


Adwords Profit System is committed to protecting the privacy of our online visitors. We collect information from online visitors who ask to be on our newsletter mailing list and/or receive periodic email announcements. Anyone who wishes can choose to be removed from our mailing list at any time.

 

In addition, online visitors who wish to purchase a membership through our Web site must provide their name, email address, mailing address, phone number, and credit card number and expiration date. All credit card transactions occur over a secure connection.

 

All online visitor data collected by Adwords Profit System is protected against unauthorized access. We will not sell, trade, or give your personal information to other companies or organizations.
