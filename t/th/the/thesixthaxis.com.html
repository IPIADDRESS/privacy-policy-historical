When users request pages from our server, our Web server automatically collects some information about the users, including their IP addresses. These addresses are used by computers on the network to send the requested pages to users.

TheSixthAxis does not reveal any personal information that users provide to us to any third party without their permission.

TheSixthAxis uses users’ IP addresses to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our website. We also use it to gather broad demographic information about our users in general. We may share this composite information with our advertisers to help them better understand our services, but TheSixthAxis does not give advertisers information about individual users of TheSixthAxis.

TheSixthAxis uses cookies to deliver content specific to users’ interests and to honor their preferences. This information is used to assist us in creating a site that will serve the needs of our users.

Newsletters
Using e-mail addresses provided at registration or otherwise, TheSixthAxis periodically sends out e-mail newsletters and promotional e-mail to our users about TheSixthAxis and its advertisers. Users can indicate that they do not wish to receive e-mail information from TheSixthAxis. Third parties will not contact our users through the use of our e-mail newsletter lists except through advertisements within the newsletters themselves. Users’ contact information may also be used by TheSixthAxis to contact the user when necessary.

Advertising
TheSixthAxis may use user entered information in combination with site usage information to help display advertisements relevant to the users interest and navigation patterns. TheSixthAxis may use cookies to help in tracking these patterns but does not store or collect an personal data in combination with user supplied information.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.

TheSixthAxis makes message boards available to our users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.
