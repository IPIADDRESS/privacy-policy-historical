Privacy Policy

Introduction

This privacy policy is designed to inform users of the LevelUp website (www.thelevelup.com), mobile applications, microsites, software and service (collectively, the “Service”) about how SCVNGR, Inc., doing business as LevelUp (“LevelUp”, “we” or “us”), gathers and uses personal information collected by us in connection with use of the Service. BY ACCESSING OR USING THE SERVICE, YOU ACKNOWLEDGE THAT YOU HAVE READ, UNDERSTAND, AND AGREE TO BE BOUND BY THIS PRIVACY POLICY AND OUR WEBSITE TERMS OF SERVICE. IF YOU DO NOT AGREE TO THESE TERMS, DO NOT USE THE SERVICE.

First Up, We Want You to Know This

We will never disclose your email address to any Merchant or any other third party for their marketing purposes. This allows you to use LevelUp without receiving a whole lot of direct marketing you don’t want.

What Information Do We Collect?

Personal Information: LevelUp collects some of your identifying nformation in connection with the Service. When you sign up to create a LevelUp account (whether through the application, the website, or Facebook), pay with LevelUp, engage in a Program, submit content to LevelUp, complete a survey, send us feedback, request information about the Service, apply for a job, or sign up for special offers from third parties, LevelUp may collect information from you, such as your email address, first and last name, mailing address, telephone number, date of birth, location, merchant transaction data, expiration date and authentication codes or related information (collectively, “Personal Information”). You do not have to provide Personal Information to LevelUp, but you may not be able to take advantage of all features of the Service if you do not. Additionally, LevelUp may ask for your credit card number and banking information, but we will only do so through a transparent pass through, and will never store such information.

Web Tracking Information: LevelUp and its third party service providers may use web tracking technologies, such as cookies, web beacons, pixel tags and clear GIFs, to collect data related to usage of the Service, the Programs and web and email marketing conducted by or on behalf of LevelUp. Such data may include the address of the websites you visited before and after you visited the Service, the type of browser you are using, your Internet Protocol (IP) address, what pages in the Service you visit and what links you clicked on. In order to collect Web Tracking Information and to make your use of the Service more efficient, we may store cookies on your computer. LevelUp and third party contractors engaged to provide services on behalf of LevelUp (“Contractors”) may also use web tracking technologies that are placed in web pages on the Service or in email communications to collect information about actions that users take when they interact with the Service or such email communications. Web Tracking Information is not correlated by LevelUp to individual users. Some Web Tracking Information may include data, such as IP address data, that is unique to you. You may be able to modify your browser settings to alter which web tracking technologies are permitted when you use the Service, but this may affect the performance of the Service.

How Do We Use the Information We Collect?

Personal Information: We will use and store Personal Information to deliver the Service to you, to allow Merchants to accept LevelUp as a method of payment, to deliver Program information to you (as applicable), to market the Service to you, to improve our products and services, investigate (and remedy) fraudulent or allegedly fraudulent activity, data analysis, to display some use information (in aggregate) to Merchants in an effort to improve the Service, and for the internal operational and administrative purposes of the Service. Additionally, any Personal Information that we collect via Facebook will be used for registration and open graph posts.

Aggregate Information: We will also use Personal Information to create aggregated data for analytical purposes. This statistical information does not contain any Personal Information and is used to understand our customer base and to better serve our market. LevelUp reserves the right to share such aggregate data with 3rd parties.

Web Tracking Information: LevelUp uses Web Tracking Information to administer the Service and to understand how well our Service is working, and to develop aggregate, statistical information on usage of the Services. This allows us to determine which features visitors like best to help us improve our Service, personalize your user experience, and measure overall effectiveness.

Email Communications: If you create an account and provide your email address, we will send you administrative and promotional emails. If you wish to opt out of promotional emails, you may do so by following the “unsubscribe” instructions in the email, or by editing your account settings as described below. All users receive administrative emails. If you wish to opt out of administrative emails, you must delete your account entirely and cease use of the Service.

What Information Do We Disclose to Third Parties?

Personal Information: LevelUp does not sell or rent its mailing list, and we will not disclose your email address to any third party for marketing purposes. LevelUp will disclose your Personal Information to third parties only in the following limited circumstances:

  1. if you use the Service, we may disclose your name (but not your email address) to the associated Merchant to aid in the utilization process and loyalty program tracking;

  2. to Contractors who are performing marketing services, delivering goods, administering promotions, analyzing data and usage of the Service, processing credit card payments, operating the Service or providing support and maintenance services for the Service, or providing customer service. We use reasonable efforts to enter into agreements with all Contractors that require Contractors to use the Personal Information they receive only to perform services for us;

  3. to investigate reports of fraud, and to correct any issues associated therewith;

  4. when we have your consent to share the information;

  5. if you’re the winner of a Merchant’s competition (as detailed in the Terms of Service), we will share your contact information for purposes of prize fulfillment (only);

  6. to third party providers of white-labeled LevelUp applications. In these circumstances, you will have already shared your email address with said third party, and we will provide it with additional analytics using some of your personal information for the general improvement of the white labeled LevelUp application; and

  7. credit card information passed to third party payment processors to process transactions made using the service (only).

Web Tracking Information: We disclose Web Tracking Information to third party advertisers and advertising agencies, Merchants, and Contractors, in order for LevelUp and such third parties to analyze the performance of the Services and the behavior of users, to operate and improve the Service, and to target advertising to users. We may also publish this information in order to promote the Service.

Aggregate Information: We may disclose anonymous, aggregated information to third parties such as potential business partners, Merchants, advertisers, and funding sources, in order to describe our business and operations.

Network Operators: Use of the Service may involve use of the services of third party telecommunications carriers and service providers (such as the services of the carrier that provides cellphone service to you). Such carriers and service providers are not LevelUp’s contractors, and any information that a carrier collects in connection with your use of the Service is not Personal Information and is not subject to this Privacy Policy. LevelUp is not responsible for the acts or omissions of telecommunications carriers or service providers.

Additional Disclosures: Notwithstanding anything to the contrary in this Privacy Policy, LevelUp reserves the right to use or disclose Personal Information and any other information LevelUp collects in connection with the Service (a) to any successor to LevelUp’s business, including as a result of any merger, acquisition, asset sale or similar transaction, (b) to any law enforcement, judicial authority, or governmental or regulatory authority, to the extent required by law or legal process, or (c) if in LevelUp’s reasonable discretion, such use or disclosure is necessary to enforce or protect LevelUp’s legal rights or to protect third parties.
