How We Use Your Information

Except with respect to material posted on the Campus or the Odyssey Community, CompassLearning maintains all student data, student educational information, and student files as confidential, which guards the privacy of students and families. CompassLearning and/or contractors to CompassLearning use the information collected for assessment (such as scoring), reporting, and other purposes as your school system may require. We may aggregate information without identifying students for internal research and generally to inform the public about the results achieved through the CompassLearning Odyssey Web site. We have established strict security procedures designed to prevent personally identifiable student files from unauthorized access. Authorized parties that have access to student information are limited to CompassLearning employees and contractors, school districts and/or the student's immediate family with proper request and identification.

We reserve the right to disclose your personally identifiable information as required by law and/or to comply with judicial proceedings, government investigations, court orders, or legal process served on us, as well as when we believe that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, investigate or prevent fraud or other illegal activities or to prevent breach of CompassLearning’s Terms and Conditions of Use Policy.


We Make Limited Use of Cookies

The CompassLearning Odyssey Web site makes limited use of "cookie" technology. A cookie is information about you that a Web site sends to your browser. The information may be stored on your computer and later used to recognize you and your preferences. CompassLearning Odyssey uses cookies to recognize you as a user and manage your preferences and security during your sessions in the CompassLearning Odyssey Web site. CompassLearning Odyssey uses only identifiers, without any student, parent, or school information. Please note that we do not dictate how our affiliates and partners linked to the CompassLearning Odyssey Web site use cookies. On most commercial browsers, you can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, which provides you with the opportunity to decide whether you want to accept or reject the cookie. When you are using CompassLearning Odyssey, your browser must be set to accept our cookies or you will not be able to access the application.
