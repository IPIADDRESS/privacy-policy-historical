About M.D. Creekmore

M.D. Creekmore is a full-time survival author, blog writer,  emergency preparedness consultant and is the owner and editor of  TheSurvivalistBlog.net. He is the author of four books The Prepper's Guide to Surviving The End of The World as We Know It,  31 Days to Survival, The Dirt Cheap Survival Retreat, and The Prepared Prepper's Cookbook. Over the years, he has studied all facets of survivalism, and has learned works and, more importantly, what does not work. His calling is to show ordinary people how to become better prepared for an uncertain future. His books and articles are based on real-world experience. You will find helpful tips, guides, and checklists – focusing on a very modest budget. Learn More...
