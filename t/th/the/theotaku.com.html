We respect your privacy to a fanatical degree here at theOtaku.com. Here is how we handle your information.

Newsletter Privacy Policy

Our newsletter is opt-in only. You will not receive a mailing from us unless you have asked to join our newsletter or have signed up for our "myOtaku.com" service (more information below). You will always have the option of unsubscribing from our newsletter, should you choose. Furthermore, we will never sell or rent your email to anyone for any reason.

myOtaku.com Privacy Policy

myOtaku.com requires a valid email address. We will not sell or rent this email address to anyone for any reason. The information you enter into your myOtaku.com profile will never be aggregated or collected to send you unsolicited mailings or impose on your privacy.

myOtaku.com logs a user's IP address and keeps cookies on the user's browsers. The IP address is logged for security purposes, allowing us to ban members who abuse the system. Cookies are kept on the user's browser to remember login information (so users don't always have to type in their username / password) and to prevent users from flooding our system. Cookies will never be used to invade a user's privacy or for marketing purposes.

Advertising

We use Burst Media, Google, and BUDS INC to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you. Third Party Cookies

In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser.
