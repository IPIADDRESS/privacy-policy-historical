Privacy Statement

The Infinite Actuary, hereon referred to as "TIA", is committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology that gives you an excellent and safe online experience. This Statement of Privacy applies to TIA's website and governs data collection and usage. By using TIA's website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Collection of your Personal Information

TIA collects personally identifiable information, such as your name, e-mail address, mailing address, phone number, and credit card information. TIA may also collect information about your computer. This information can include your IP address, Operating System, browser type, referring website addresses, domain names, access times, files downloaded and videos viewed. This information is for system administration and in order to create reports. Statistical data shared with trusted third parties about our users' browsing actions and patterns does not identify any individual. TIA collect information when you register on our site, place an order, subscribe to a newsletter, respond to a survey, fill out a form, or use our online seminar/education tools.

Use of your Personal Information

The information collected from you is used to personalize user's experience, to improve the website, to improve customer service, to administer contests/promotions/surveys or other site features, to process transactions and to send periodic emails.

TIA does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. TIA may share data with trusted partners to help us perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to TIA, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

TIA may also keep track of which pages our users and guests visit with TIA's website, in order to determine which of our products and services are most popular. TIA may also track your progress through our online seminars/education tools and ratings of lessons to compile your data with other users to anonymously share general statistics with other TIA instructors and users.

TIA will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on TIA or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of TIA; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of TIA, or the public.

Protection of your Personal Information

TIA secures your information using an SSL certificate when a user places an order or enters/submits/accesses their personal information. TIA use a payment gateway provider and do not store your credit card information on our servers. All the personal identifiable information you provide TIA are stored on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure.

Use of Cookies

TIA uses "cookies" to help remember and process the items in the shopping cart, personalize your online experience and compile aggregate data about site traffic and interactions in order to offer better site experiences and tools in the future. TIA may also use trusted third party services, including Google Analytics, that track this information on our behalf. Google Analytics uses first-party cookies to track visitor interactions as in our case, where they are used to collect information about how visitors use our site. TIA then use the information to compile reports and to help us improve our site.

Cookies contain information that is transferred to your computer's hard drive. These cookies are used to store information, such as the time that the current visit occurred, whether the visitor has been to the site before and what site referred the visitor to the web page.

Google Analytics collects information anonymously. It reports website trends without identifying individual visitors. You can opt out of Google Analytics without affecting how you visit our site - for more information on opting out of being tracked by Google Analytics across all websites you use, visit this Google page: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
