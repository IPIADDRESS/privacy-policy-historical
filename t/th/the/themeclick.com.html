Technologies used on such sites may vary, so please consult their privacy policy and/or site owner to determine which technologies they utilize.

themeclick.com is primarily a Web Directory for web applications. We collect information from different sites which sell web applications and publish this information which is viewable by our audience. Please note that our visitors any E-mail address is private and not published under any published item. Like many other Web sites, themeclick.com makes use of log files. IP addresses and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

themeclick.com has no access to or control over these cookies and web beacons which are used by third-party advertisers. Besides collecting data such as your IP address, such technology may record the referring URL, Web Browser used, and other non-identifiable information.
