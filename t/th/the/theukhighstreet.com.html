TheUKHighStreet.com has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: TheUKHighStreet.com.

Our homepage on the Internet is located at http://www.theukhighstreet.com

Data Collection

We collect the following information about our visitors:
  • dynamic.clickstream data
  • dynamic.http data
  • dynamic.clientevents data
  • dynamic.searchtext data
  • dynamic.interactionrecord data
  • dynamic.cookies data
This means that we track how you interact with our site. This information is used to show you the requested information, for administration of the site, for one-time tailoring of the site to give you extra information about your current visit, to analyse users visits to the site and the way that the site is used and for general research and development of the site.

COOKIES

Our site uses cookies to make sure you don't see the same adverts repeatedly and to personalise the site. We may use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests and to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our site.

We use outside ad companies to display some ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our Web site, cookies received with banner ads are collected by our ad companies, and we do not have access to this information.

INFORMATION ABOUT YOU

We have introduced the ability to register on our site so that the site can be tailored to your preferences. The registration form will require users to give us contact information (like their name and email address) and demographic information (e.g. postcode, age). It also enables you to customise how you view the site. Demographic and profile data will be shared with advertisers on an aggregate basis. Email addresses will not be shared with 3-parties unless we are given explicit permission to do so.

LINKS TO OTHER SITES

This site contains links to other sites. TheUKHighStreet.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

In some cases, TheUKHighStreet.com has special relationships with other sites. No personal information divulged on our site will be shared with these sites.

PUBLIC FORUMS

This site may make chat rooms, forums, message boards and feedback comments available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.

OPT-OUT

If you wish to either opt-in or opt-out of the use of your information you may do so by contacting us at the email address below or via the contact form.

CORRECT/UPDATE

  1. Login and make the changes to 'My Profile'.

Any disputes will be handled by applicable law. Any errors will be promptly corrected.
