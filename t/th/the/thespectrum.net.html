What does Spectrum Nexus do with my information?
If you submit an e-mail to us through our contact form we will only use that information to send you a reply to your inquiry. If you sign up for the message forum we will never use your personal information in a harmful way; nor will we share it with any third parties.

This information will not be used in any other way beside providing us with information such as if the user submitted a score through the user rating script they will not be permitted again to submit it until after a certain amount of days. The same goes with server logs and polls; IP addresses are simply used to enable our "user submitted information" to be more accurate (to discourage malicious use of our system).
