The Budget Fashionista PRIVACY POLICY and DISCLAIMER

Like most web businesses, TBF Group, LLC captures both personal and aggregated information about visitors to our site. We explain here why we need that information and what we do (and won't do) with it.

Overview: We never disclose or sell personal information about you to third parties without your permission, unless compelled to do so by law.

I. Why we retain and use information about our visitors

TBF Group, LLC collects personal and aggregate (anonymous) information about our site visitors for the following reasons:

A. To allow us to respond to your questions and comments: Since our web site revolves a great deal around being interactive with our visitors, we need to know who our visitors are so as to better respond to he questions submitted by those individual visitors.

B. To know our audience: We use aggregated (anonymous) information about our visitors to understand our audience and to describe that audience to business partners, sponsors, and advertisers, which will enable us to provided an enhanced surfing experience for our visitors.

C. For user convenience and customization: To allow us to customize the use of the site, to present the issues most important to our visitors in the fashion, style, and beauty worlds.

D. For product improvements: To help us understand how visitors use our site and how we can improve it.

E. To provide a better shopping environment: By knowing about our visitors it is possible for us to be able to offer those items and site links, that we feel our visitors most want to have, reducing the amount of time spent searching online.

II. Types of information we collect

Depending on how you use our site, TBF Group, LLC may collect the following kinds of information:

A. Your name, e-mail address and zipcode, when you register for the site or e-mail us a question.

B. Your user preferences, your interest in receiving our newsletters and product announcements, and similar information, which you can change at any time.

C. Your credit-card number and mailing address, if you purchase one of the products offered by us exclusively. Any purchases made through our affiliates are subject to their privacy policy.

D. Additional Personal information: If you subscribe to our newsletter we may collect personal information that allows us to target visitors with information, products or services which fits the visitor's specific needs or desires.

E. Demographic information that you choose to provide, such as your gender, age, martial status, occupation, income, number and ages of your children, hobbies, zip code, mailing address, and so on.

F. Aggregated usage data, such as the popularity of various pages on our site, the times of day when most site visits occur, and so on.

Personal information is stored on secure web servers, and is available on the site for you to review or modify at all times.

III. Do we use cookies ?

Yes, like other web sites, TBF Group, LLC uses the technology called "cookies" to place small, anonymous data files on your computer. Depending on the version of TBF Group, LLC you use, cookies may be used for the following purposes:

A. To help us track the number of visitors and the popularity of our pages.

B. To allow you to store personal preferences, such as your list of sites or list of purchases made.

C. To give you the convenience of having your username submitted automatically once you've registered.

You can turn cookies off if you wish (see your browser's Help instructions). But TBF Group, LLC features that rely on them will be disabled.

IV. TBF Group, LLC uses personal information about you in the following ways:

A. To interact with you about problems or questions you may want answered

B. To announce new TBF Group, LLC products and enhancements

C. To meet legal demands on us by certain administrative or legal organizations, if required to turn over such information.

D. If you send email to us to respond to you with specific information if requested by you

E. All reader letters are answered with an alias, or ghost phrase, and city rather than your real name.

V. TBF Group, LLC uses aggregate (anonymous) information about you in the following ways:

A. To help us understand our audience as we grow our business.

B. To promote ourselves to potential sponsors, advertisers or business partners

VI. Sale or transfer of personal information retained by us.

TBF Group, LLC will never disclose or sell personal information about you, including your e-mail address, to any third party without your permission, unless compelled to do so by law.
