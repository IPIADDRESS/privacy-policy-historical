Use of electronic cookies
Before you post a message in one of our exciting message boards, you can choose to enable "cookie". A cookie is a small data file that contain bits of information that Web sites transfer to your computer's hard drive for record keeping purposes. But the only personal information a cookie can contain is information you supply yourself. A cookie can't read data off your hard disk or read cookie files created by other sites. TheLion.com uses cookies for allowing our members to post messages without going through the hassle of logging in every time.

TheLion.com will only use any information voluntarily you give us to help us improve our sites. We track what our users like and don't like about our web sites, but not what they read.

Please remember that whenever you disclose personal information publicly, for example, on message boards or through email, that information can be collected and used by others. If you don't want strangers to send you email, don't post your email address on a message board. Basically, there are constantly people out there trying to obtain personal information about you without your consent. You must protect yourself.
