Cookies also allow us to hold selections in a shopping cart when a user leaves without checking out. Most browsers accept cookies automatically but allow you to disable them. To learn more about cookies and how to disable them, please consult the “Help” feature of your browser or your device’s user guide or visit www.aboutads.info/consumers.

These technologies also allow us to learn which advertisements bring users to our Online Services. With web beacon technology, the information that we collect and share is anonymous and not personally identifiable. It does not contain Personal Information.

For more information about interest-based advertising (often referred to as online behavioral advertising) and how to manage your preferences for interest-based ads, please visit www.aboutads.info/consumers.

Location Information

This information is used to provide you with location-based services, such as store location and local advertising. You may choose to receive push notifications without consenting to the collection of geolocation information, but the messages you receive will not be customized for your location. While your authorization is active, if you access or request location-based services from our Online Services, your mobile device will provide us with your location without additional authorization from you. If you would like to disable location services in connection with our mobile website, please consult your mobile device’s user guide for instructions on clearing cookies.
