Help

Getting started

Themebright is the easiest way for designers to make money. Simply upload your theme, wait a few hours or days and your theme will be listed to thousands of designers & developers willing to buy themes.

Otherwise, if you simply want to buy one or a few themes, feel free to create your account and browse our gallery of brilliantly affordable & user-friendly themes! We're updating our gallery every day, adding more and more stunning Wordpress, PSD, XHTML/CSS, Email, Joomla and Magento themes.

FAQ

  • I have a designed theme and I don't know how to sell it.
  • Simply create an account, then go on your account and click on the "Upload a theme" button, then, simply follow the steps.
  • How much will I earn when I sell a theme?
  • Between 70 and 90% of the selling price if the theme is unique.
    If you decide to sell this theme elsewhere you will earn between 50 and 70%.

    Unique themes :
    • 0 to 1000$ of sales : 70%
    • 1001 to 5000$ of sales : 80%
    • 5001$+ of sales : 90%
    Not unique themes :
    • 0 to 1000$ of sales : 50%
    • 1001 to 5000$ of sales : 60%
    • 5001$+ of sales : 70%
  • How do I get paid?
  • Click on the "Transfer money" in your panel on the "My themes" space. You'll need to have at least 10$ to request your first payment.
    We pay our designers through:
    • Paypal (up to 2 days)
    • Bank wire (up to 5 days)
  • How can I buy a theme?
  • Simply put some money on your account. You can put money using PayPal or Bank wire. You can then spend your money on our brilliantly-affordable collection of themes!
  • I have bought a theme. Can I use it for commercial use?
  • Yes, you can.
  • I have bought a theme. Can I re-sell it somewhere else?
  • No you can't, simply for the reason we want our themes to be unique on the Internet!
  • I'm not satisfied with the theme I bought.
  • Contact us through our contact form and we will give you a complete refund in the following days.
  • My theme wasn't approved on the site. What should I do?
  • We will always give you the reason why your theme wasn't approved. Try to tweak it a little more and to correct the mistakes. You can then resend it to us.

About our themes

Our content is cautiously selected by our team of reviewers. We want to keep high quality & brilliant content on our website. We love good designs that make emotions. We also love well-coded & polished websites. That's why we might ask you to correct or tweak a few things in order to get featured on our site.

To provide more exposure to our content, we feature some screenschots of our content on some friends site such as Dribbble, Deviantart or Flickr, with a link to your profile. Sometimes we'll also tweet or facebook some of the themes we love!

We will also, on a weekly basis, showcase our favourite author of the week. We love creative people!

The best-sellers will be also featured on our front-page.

What do we use your information for?

Any of the information we collect from you may be used in one of the following ways:

  • To personalize your experience
    (your information helps us to better respond to your individual needs)
  • To improve our website
    (we continually strive to improve our website offerings based on the information and feedback we receive from you)
  • To improve customer service
    (your information helps us to more effectively respond to your customer service requests and support needs)
  • To process transactions
    Your information, whether public or private, will not be sold, exchanged, transferred, or given to any other company for any reason whatsoever, without your consent, other than for the express purpose of delivering the purchased product or service requested.

How do we protect your information?

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you place an order or access your personal information.

We offer the use of a secure server. All supplied sensitive/credit information is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology and then encrypted into our Payment gateway providers database only to be accessible by those authorized with special access rights to such systems, and are required to?keep the information confidential.
