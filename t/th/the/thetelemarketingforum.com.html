Privacy Policy


Types of information we collect

This Privacy Statement covers personal information, non-personal data collection and aggregate reporting.

Personal information is information that is associated with your name or personal identity. We use personal information, to better understand your needs and interests and to provide you with better service and products. The types of personal information you might provide to us may include name, address, phone number, e-mail address, and user IDs.

Non-personal information is data that is not associated with a specific personal identity. We might collect and analyze non-personal information to evaluate how visitors and consumers use our services and products. Non-personal data we collect may include the unique URLs visited, browser type and IP addresses. Most non-personal data is collected via cookies or other analysis technologies.

A cookie is a small text file containing a unique identification number that is transferred from a website to the hard drive of your computer so that the site administrator may identify your computer and passively track its activities on the website. A cookie will not allow us to learn any personally identifiable information (such as your real name and address) that you have not otherwise disclosed.

How do we use the information we collect?

Any personally identifiable information you share with us when registering for products and services is maintained and accessible by us. We request information from users so that users can enjoy customized experiences, including personalization services, interactive and person-specific communications and much more. It will also help us better design and prepare future products and services based on your interests.

We may, from time to time, use your information to contact you about changes or enhancements to our products and services or issues regarding your account.

If you registered to receive any electronic information from us (via e-mail), we may (i) send you email and postal mail about products and services that we feel may be of interest to you, and (ii) may combine information we have about you with information we obtain about you from third parties for the purpose of providing you with more appropriate products, services, and offerings.

Personal data given to us may be transferred across state and country borders for the purposes of data consolidation, storage and simplified customer information management.
