At www.thislilpiglet.net, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by www.thislilpiglet.net and how it is used.

 

Other than third party ad uses of cookies and log files detailed below, This Lil Piglet will never sell or share your information with anyone and will not use your information for anything other than it’s intended purpose such as opt in newsletter subscriptions.  Information submitted for giveaways will only be used in the event that you are the winner of the giveaway; in this event your contact information will be forwarded to the sponsoring company for that prize in order for shipment and only your name will or may be announced on the This Lil Piglet website.  At times This Lil Piglet will collect various data from readers of the website intended for the use of determining the demographic reach of readership; any and all attempts will be announced in these instance, will strictly be a generalization and will be your choice if you choose to participate.

 

Log Files
Like many other Web sites, www.thislilpiglet.net makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track users movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on www.thislilpiglet.net send directly to your browsers. www.thislilpiglet.net has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
