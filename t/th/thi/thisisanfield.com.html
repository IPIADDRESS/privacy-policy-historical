Privacy

This page can be found linked to from the footer of every page on This Is Anfield. Last updated 12 September 2018.

This Is Anfield is committed to being open and transparent about your privacy, complying with the European Union's GDPR legislation.

In this section you will find:
  • Consent preferences
  • Your information, where it goes and how to manage your consent
  • Our privacy policy

Your information, where it goes and how to manage consent

Select the section or platform of This Is Anfield you wish to learn more about what data we collect and where it goes. You can also learn how to update your subscription preferences and consent on each one.

Cookies

What is it? Cookies are small text files that can be placed on your device via your browser to help websites, such as ours, to remember you. We use cookies to enhance user experience and show relevant advertising. Cookies necessary to the function of the site will be automatically used, but cookies deemed unnecessary to the function of the site require your consent.

Opt-in On your first visit to This Is Anfield, you will be asked to consent to all cookies deemed non-necessary for the functioning of this website. If you have previously opted-out, you can change your consent preferences here.

Opt-out If you have previously consented to all non-necessary cookies, you can change your consent preferences here.

What else? For your convenience, this privacy page is linked to from the bottom of every page on This Is Anfield.

Who has my data? This Is Anfield does not store any personal data relating to personalised ads. A list of third-party vendors is available in your consent preferences here.

Personalised Ads

What is it? This Is Anfield publishes all of its content free of charge and is supported by advertising. To deliver you the most relevant adverts we share data with third-party vendors. We conform with the IAB's consent management standards.

Opt-in Users are asked to consent to personalised ads on your first visit to This Is Anfield (per browser). If you have previously opted out and wish to opt-in, use our personalised ads consent tool here.

Opt-out If you opt-out of personalised ads you may be served less relevant and potentially more spammy adverts. To opt-out of personalised ads, use our personalised ads consent tool here.

Who has my data?  This Is Anfield does not store any personal data relating to personalised ads. A list of third-party vendors can be found in your personalised ads consent preferences here.

Morning Briefing Mailing List

What is it? A once-per-day email listing the latest content from This Is Anfield.

Opt-in Click here.

Opt-out There is a link to unsubscribe at the bottom of all Morning Briefing emails.

Who has my data? Your information will be accessible to ourselves, for the purposes of running the mailing list, and our mailing list partners MailChimp.

Browser Notifications

What is it? Alerts on Chrome, Safari and Firefox when new content is added to This Is Anfield. This requires your explicit self-serve consent to opt-in or opt-out, we cannot do this for you.

Opt-in Click the red bell icon in the bottom right-hand corner, click subscribe and allow notifications from thisisanfield.com.

Opt-out Click the red bell icon in the bottom right-hand corner, click unsubscribe. Ensure you are using the same browser you initially opted-in with. Alternatively, check your browser's notifications settings.

Who else?  This Is Anfield hold no personally identifiable information relating to this service.

Comments

What is it? Interactive comments section below articles on This Is Anfield.

Opt-in To comment, you must be verified by Disqus.

Opt-out Log into your Disqus account to change your preferences or delete your account.

Who else? This Is Anfield hold no personally identifiable data regarding this service. It is managed by a comments platform called Disqus, who store your information.

App Notifications

What is it? Push notifications from the This Is Anfield App for major breaking news stories.

Opt-in Download the This Is Anfield app for iPhone or Android here, then enable notifications when prompted. Alternatively enable notifications for the This Is Anfield app in the Settings on your phone.

Opt-out Disable notifications for the This Is Anfield app in the Settings on your phone.

Who has my data?  This Is Anfield stores no personally identifiable data in relation to our app. Your phone's operating system developer, such as Apple or Google, may also have access to your information, as per their own terms and conditions.

Forums

What is it? A moderated interactive forum of supporters discussing Liverpool FC and other topics of interest.

Opt-in Register for free here.

Opt-out Forum members can adjust what emails and notifications they receive in their user preferences. To delete your account at any time, send us an email at forums.thisisanfield.com

Who has my data? The administrators and moderators of the forums have access to your information; an up-to-date list of who they are can be found here. The forum software developers are Xenforo. Your information is secured on secure (SSL) servers with Nimbus Hosting, who also have access to this data.

Facebook

What is it? Our Facebook page.

Opt-in Log into Facebook, click here and 'Like' the page.

Opt-out Log into Facebook, click here and 'Unlike' the page.

Who has my data?  Facebook store and process all information relating to this service, as per their terms and conditions. This Is Anfield has access to names or usernames of users who have Liked our page.

Twitter

What is it? Our Twitter account.

Opt-in Follow @thisisanfield on Twitter, or log into Twitter, click here and click the 'follow' button.

Opt-out Log into Twitter, click here and click the 'unfollow' button.

Who has my data?  Twitter store and process all information relating to this service, as per their terms and conditions. This Is Anfield has access to names or usernames of users who follow our account or interact with us on Twitter.

YouTube

What is it? Our YouTube channel.

Opt-in If you have a Google account, you can subscribe to our YouTube channel here.

Opt-out Log into your Google account, click here, and click unsubscribe.

Who has my data?  YouTube (Google) store and process all information relating to this service, as per their terms and conditions. This Is Anfield has access to names or usernames of users who have subscribed to our YouTube channel, or interacted with us on the YouTube platform.

Instagram

What is it? Our Instagram feed.

Opt-in Instagram users can follow @thisisanfieldlfc on Instagram here.

Opt-out Log into Instagram, go to our profile here, and click unfollow.

Who has my data?  Instagram (Facebook) store and process all information relating to this service, as per their terms and conditions. This Is Anfield has access to names or usernames of users who have followed our Instagram account or interacted with us on the Instagram platform.

Competitions

What is it? From time to time, This Is Anfield will run competitions for Liverpool FC related prizes. These are always free to enter.

Opt-in When you enter a competition, you will be asked for your explicit permission to be contacted by This Is Anfield and the promotor of the competition, usually the brand offering the prize.

Opt-out To withdraw your entry, contact us. You can ask for your information to be deleted at any time.

What else? Your information will be stored on secure servers hosted by Google for a maximum of 30 days, before being deleted. Your information may also be shared with MailChimp for a maximum of 30 days for follow-up marketing, providing you have consented to this.

Who has my data? This Is Anfield has access to any data submitted in relation to competition and will keep it for a maximum of 30 days, before it is deleted. Your data is kept securely by Google and our mailing list partners MailChimp, as explained upon any competition entry.
