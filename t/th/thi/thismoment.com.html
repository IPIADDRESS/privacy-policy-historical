thismoment Distributed Engagement Channel (“DEC”) Privacy Policy

Overview

The application that you are viewing is called the thismoment Distributed Engagement Channel (“DEC” or the “Service”). The DEC is operated by us, thismoment, Inc., for our partner whose content and brand are featured in the DEC. The DEC is a multi-media application that enables our partner to tell you about its products, services or special events while also allowing you, the user, to participate in the experience by submitting your own content, such as comments, photos and videos. The DEC may include content from multiple social networks and sites, such as Facebook, YouTube, Twitter, and Flickr. The DEC may appear on the website of one or more of those services, on our partner’s site or on other sites.

Because we are offering the Service on behalf of our partner, all information that we collect is made available to our partner whose DEC you are viewing under the terms of this Privacy Policy (e.g., if you are interacting with a DEC for Chevy, the information we collect is made available to Chevy). This information may also be made available under the terms of this Privacy Policy to the operator of the website where you interacted with the DEC under the terms of this Privacy Policy (e.g., if you interacted with the DEC on YouTube, the information we collect may be made available to YouTube).

In some cases, the DEC may also include a link to our partner’s privacy policy, in which case the information that we collect may also be used under the terms of that policy (e.g., if you are interacting with a DEC for Chevy and a link to Chevy’s privacy policy is also displayed on the DEC, then the information collected through the DEC is also subject to Chevy’s privacy policy).

This Service is not intended for use by persons under thirteen (13) years of age. Thismoment does not knowingly collect Personal Information from or about children under the age of thirteen (13).

For the privacy policy applicable to my.thismoment.com, see my.thismoment.com/privacy.


Information We Collect

We may collect the following kinds of information.

Thismoment collects information from you when you provide it to us, such as when you register for our Service, enter a contest, submit content to the Service (such as a video or photo submission), or communicate with us, such as through email or SMS. The information you provide us may include information that personally identifies you (“Personal Information” or “PII”), such as your name, your email address, your phone number or your mailing address.

See the section below called “Public Activity” about information that you submit to public areas of the Service

We may also receive information about you from the service that you used to connect to our Service (e.g., if you use your account with Facebook, YouTube or Twitter to log into our Service). Information from cookies and similar devices

Cookies are data that we place on a user’s browser that allow us to know how often someone visits a site and the activities they conduct while using the Service (such as the pages visited, links clicked on, videos viewed, commenting activity, contest voting, etc.). Every computer is assigned a different cookie by thismoment.

Information from mobile devices

If you are interacting with our service on a mobile device, we may receive your device and hardware ID, device type, carrier, carrier user ID, the content of your request and basic usage statistics about your device and usage of our Service. If you use location-related aspects of our Service, we may also receive information about your actual location. If you provide it to us or send us a message from your mobile device, we may also collect your phone number. Your device may also store information about your usage of our Service. Please consult your device documentation on how to manage local storage.

Information based on standard web protocols

When you access our Service through a browser, application or other client, like other web providers, our servers automatically record certain information. This data is recorded in server logs and may include information such as your web request, your interaction with the Service, the website that you were visiting when you came to our Service, your Internet Protocol (IP) address, your browser type and language, the date and time of your request, and your cookie data.

Information from third-party applications

Some of our DECs include third-party applications, which have their own privacy policies that govern their collection of information. Any information received by thismoment form those third-party applications will be used in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
