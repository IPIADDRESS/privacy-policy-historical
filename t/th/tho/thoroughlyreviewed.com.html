ThoroughlyReviewed also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on https://thoroughlyreviewed.com blogs/sites. ThoroughlyReviewed only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below, except that commenter IP addresses and email addresses are visible and disclosed to the administrators of the blog/site where the comment was left.

Certain visitors to ThoroughlyReviewed's websites choose to interact with ThoroughlyReviewed in ways that require ThoroughlyReviewed to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that ThoroughlyReviewed gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up at https://thoroughlyreviewed.com to provide a username and email address. Those who engage in transactions with ThoroughlyReviewed are asked to provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to process those transactions. In each case, ThoroughlyReviewed collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor's interaction with ThoroughlyReviewed. ThoroughlyReviewed does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

ThoroughlyReviewed may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. ThoroughlyReviewed may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, ThoroughlyReviewed does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. ThoroughlyReviewed uses cookies to help ThoroughlyReviewed identify and track visitors, their usage of ThoroughlyReviewed website, and their website access preferences. ThoroughlyReviewed visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using ThoroughlyReviewed's websites, with the drawback that certain features of ThoroughlyReviewed's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
