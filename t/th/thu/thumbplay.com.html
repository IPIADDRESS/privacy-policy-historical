Information in cookies includes preferences you select while visiting a particular Web site. THUMBPLAY never saves information such as your credit card number on cookies. We do require cookies in order to manage and individual visits the THUMBPLAY site. Certain cookies are also used to determine how you were referred to the THUMBPLAY site as well as your browsing and buying activities, in order that we can determine the success of any particular online marketing campaign.
