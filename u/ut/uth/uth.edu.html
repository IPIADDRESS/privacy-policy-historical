Internet Privacy Policy

The University of Texas Houston Health Science Center maintains the UTH web site as a public service.  The following is the privacy policy for this site uth.tmc.edu:

Information Collected and Automatically Logged

Due to the nature of the Universityâ€™s diversity in teaching, research and patient care, a multitude of web authors publish information on this site.  The servers automatically log the IP address of every visitor to these web pages.  As a general rule, most sites do not collect cookies.

Our site uses IP addresses to help diagnose problems with the servers and to administer the Web site. The information logged is archived for one year.  For site management functions, information is collected for analysis purposes only.  This information is not reported or used in any manner that would reveal personally identifiable information.  This information will not be released to any outside parties unless we are required to do so in connection with law enforcement investigations or other legal proceedings.
Information Collected from E-mails and Web Forms Some pages may request and collect information that personally identifies you or allows you to be personally contacted. If you send us an electronic mail message with a question or comment that contains personally identifying information, or fill out a web form we will only use the personally identifiable information to respond to your request and to gather broad demographic information to analyze trends.  We may redirect your message to another government agency or person who is in a better position to answer your question.
Links to Other Sites This site contains links to other sites.  The University of Texas Health Science Center Houston is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
