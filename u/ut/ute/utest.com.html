The information we collect is used to improve the content of our web site, used to notify consumers about updates to our web site, not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.

No other third party receives your personally identifiable information or other transactional data except for those with whom you have transactions.

If you are concerned about how your information is used, you should check back at our web site periodically.
