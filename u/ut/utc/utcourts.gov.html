Privacy Policy for this site:


     
  1. For each visitor to the Utah State Courts web site, the Court's web server automatically gathers and stores only the following information.
     
    1. Your internet domain and IP address;
    2. The type of browser and operating system used to access this site;
    3. The date and time of access to the site;
    4. The pages viewed on the site; and
    5. The address of the web site from which you reached this site, if you linked from another site.

     
  2. This information is used to determine what information is of the most interest, design specifications, and system performance.
     
  3. No other information is collected unless provided by the user. If you email us personal information, that information is used solely for the purpose of responding to your message. We do not maintain mailing lists from email received from the web site nor do we create individual profiles with the information you provide. None of the information we collect from web visitors is given to anyone unless we are legally required to do so.
     
  4. If a user of this website is involved in litigation, public information regarding that litigation, including a user's personally identifiable information, may be publicly available on this website.
     
  5. The operator of this website is the Web Publisher, who may be contacted at: info@email.utcourts.gov
     
  6. Since the Utah state courts do not collect or maintain personally identifiable information from website users, there is no procedure to access or correct one's personally identifiable information.
