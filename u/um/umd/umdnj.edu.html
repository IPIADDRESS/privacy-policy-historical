UMDNJ Web Site Privacy Statement

Thank you for visiting the University of Medicine and Dentistry (UMDNJ) Web site. We are committed to respecting and safeguarding your privacy. The following describes privacy practices for all official pages of the UMDNJ Web site.

Information Gathering

You are able to visit and view this site without providing any personal information. We do not track or record personal information about individuals and their visits. Should you choose to provide us with your name and e-mail address by requesting additional information, this information will only be used for official purposes (for instance, to respond to your inquiry) and will not be provided to any third parties.

To increase ease-of-use, UMDNJ provides a bread crumb trail at the top of each page which allows users to quickly navigate the site pages they are visiting during their session. The information reflected in the trail is not stored in your browser or on our servers at any point.

Information Use

The University of Medicine and Dentistry of New Jersey (UMDNJ) uses standard log file information to track site usage by internal and external audiences, and help diagnose technical problems. This information is never linked to personal information or shared with any third parties. Individuals who do not choose to register can view the site through generalized public pages.

Third-Party Sites

For your convenience, links to useful Web sites outside the UMDNJ Web site are provided on various pages throughout the site. These links will appear in separate windows, indicating that you have left the UMDNJ Web site. UMDNJ is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these sites.
