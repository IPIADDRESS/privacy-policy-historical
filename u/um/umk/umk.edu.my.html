PRIVACY POLICY

Your Privacy
This page explains our privacy policy which includes the use and protection of information submitted by visitors. If you are doing a transaction or send an e-mail containing personal information, this information may be shared with other government agencies to help provide a more efficient and effective manner. An example might be in resolving a complaint that requires feedback from other agencies.

Information Collected
No personal information is gathered during the browsing of this website except for information given by you via e-mail.

What happens if I make links to other websites?
This website contains links to other websites. This privacy policy applies only to this website. It should be noted that other sites in the links may have different privacy policies and we highly recommend that you read and understand the privacy policy for every site.

Changes of Policy
If there is any change on this policy, it will be updated on this page. Frequent visits to this site will provide you with the latest information, how to use this site and how information is shared with other parties in certain circumstances.
