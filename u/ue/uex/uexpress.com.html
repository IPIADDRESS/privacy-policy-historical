uclick Network Privacy Policies
Last Updated 07/11/2001
uExpress.com (uclick, LLC) is a general audience entertainment site dedicated to providing the Internet-using public with the most complete and enjoyable online comic viewing experience. uExpress.com is also concerned about your privacy and we encourage you to read our Privacy Policy to learn all about how we use information about visitors to our Web site(s).

We also encourage you to read our Children's Privacy Policy - it details how we deal with personally identifiable information collected from children 13 years of age or younger.

What does the uExpress.com/uclick Privacy Policy cover?
  • This policy covers the treatment and use of personally identifiable information that is collected by uExpress.com/uclick, at any of its Web sites and when you sign up to use any of the services provided at the sites.
  • This policy does not cover information that may or may not be collected by companies or web sites that uclick does not own or control. This policy also does not guide the behavior of individuals not employed by uclick or uExpress.com.
  • What information does uExpress.com/uclick collect and use?
  • uExpress.com and uclick does not sell or provide personally identifiable information about you to any third party.
  • uExpress.com collects personally identifiable information when (i) you register for a uExpress.com account (the extent of the information collected depends upon what you provide during the registration process), (ii) when you use certain uExpress.com services, (iii) when you make purchases from uExpress.com-controlled or affiliated online stores and e-commerce sites and (iv) if you enter any special promotions or contests provided by uExpress.com for its users.
  • When you choose to join or register for any uExpress.com or uclick controlled Web site or service we will ask for your name, e-mail address, birth date, gender, zip code, occupation, interests and other biographic data to assist us in personalizing your uExpress.com experience.
  • uExpress.com also receives certain information automatically from our computer network and servers, including your IP address, certain "cookie" information, server log information and information that details which uExpress.com pages have been requested by your browser.
  • uExpress.com and uclick may use user data in an aggregate form for marketing and advertising purposes and may rent, sell or lease this aggregate information to responsible third parties using the information for legitimate purposes.
  • All of the information received or collected by uExpress.com is used for one or more of the following reasons: to allow for customization of content and advertising/marketing information at our site, to allow us to respond to your requests for products or services provided by uExpress.com, and to contact you about new products, changes to existing products and services provided by uExpress.com for our users.
  • uExpress.com and uclick may provide personally identifiable information about you to other companies, affiliates and people if:

    - You have consented to the sharing of the information.
    - The information must be shared to provide you with a product or service requested by you.
    - The information must be shared with companies who work with uExpress.com or uclick to provide a product or service to you.
    - We are required by law, or we are required to respond to subpoenas, court orders, legal process, to cooperate with a legitimate law enforcement investigation or to investigate a threat to our network or services.
    - We are investigating actions by users whose actions violate our Terms of Service and Conditions of Use or any other guideline or rule we have established that controls the use of our service or products

  • Other companies over whom uExpress.com and uclick have no control that serve advertisements on our Web sites may also utilize "cookie" technology to send and/or receive information from their computers to your browser and computer.
  • uExpress.com and uclick will use server logs to track your movement through our Web sites and properties - in addition, we will use this information to customize the user experience or provide registrant continuity between our various Web sites and properties.
