The United States Mint appreciates your visit to our Web site. We support your right to know about information we collect and how it will be used. Our services are growing and our practices may change, so please check back for updates to our Privacy Statement.

See our full Privacy Policy Statement.

  • You do not have to give us any personal information to visit our site.
  • We do not attempt to collect personally identifiable information from children under age 13.
  • The United States Mint does not rent or sell your personal information.
  • We may use personal information provided online to respond to electronic inquiries (job applications, product orders and inquiries, etc.) and distribute catalogs, job and other offerings, newsletters and educational materials, and we and our contractors store and use this information to process and verify requests and provide our products and services.
  • We use software to maintain security, improve service and collect data for statistical purposes.
  • We use cookie technology to keep you connected to our server and to operate our shopping cart and to conduct certain kinds of Web measurement.  See our full Privacy Policy Statement for more information about how we use cookies.
