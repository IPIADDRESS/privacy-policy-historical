Privacy Policy

Welcome to www.uspharmacist.com! This site is owned by Jobson Medical Information LLC ("JMI"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding collection, use, and disclosure of personal information that you may provide to us through this Web site.

What personal information do we collect on this site, and how do we collect it?

  • When you subscribe to U.S. Pharmacist®, we ask you for your first and last name, title, company name, address, phone number, fax number, and e-mail address.
  • When you sign up to take CE activities on this site, you create a "CE student profile," which includes your name (first, middle, last), address, phone, e-mail, and fax number, as well as the numbers, states and expiration dates of your licenses.
  • If you sign up for our Continuing Education E-Mail Newsletter, we ask you for your first and last names, address, e-mail address, cell phone number, and home fax number.

How do we use the personal information that we collect?

  • We use the personal information that we collect on your online subscription form as follows:

    • Our fulfillment house, which currently is Hallmark Data, stores all the information that you enter on the online subscription form and makes that information available to the our circulation department.
    • Our circulation department may share your name and address with our direct mail vendors (who are engaged on a job-to-job basis), who may send you renewal mailings, and with our list vendor, which currently is Statlistics, who may include your name and address in lists that it rents to third parties. We also may share your name and address with MBNA America Bank, N.A., with whom we have a credit card program. MBNA may use that information to send you offers for a U.S. Pharmacist® credit card.
    • If you provide your e-mail address, we may send you subscription renewal forms by e-mail and may forward your e-mail address to our list vendor, which currently is Statlistics, to use to send you e-mail promotional messages for other pharmacy products and services offered by JMI and other companies. We also may use your e-mail address to send you newsletters about developments and opportunities in the pharmacy and pharmaceutical industries.
    • If you provide your telephone number, we may share it with telemarketing companies (who are engaged on a job-by-job basis) who may use it tosolicit renewals of your subscription, and with our list vendor, which currently is Statlistics, who may include the phone number along with your name and address in lists that it rents to third parties.  You do not have to provide your telephone number to subscribe to U.S. Pharmacist®.
    • If you provide your fax number, you give consent for us to send you fax advertisements for JMI information products and services at the fax number you have provided. We also may share your name and fax number with our fax vendor, which currently is Express Messaging, who may send you subscription renewal forms by fax. You do not have to provide your fax number to subscribe to U.S. Pharmacist®.
  • We use the information that we collect when you register for CE activities as follows:

    • We use your name and address to grade your post-activity test and to send you a statement of credit for the CE activity. If PHE uses a third-party company to grade your post-activity test and issue statements of credit, we will give your name and address to that company for that purpose only.
    • If you sign up for our Continuing Education E-Mail Newsletter, JMI will use your e-mail address to send you the newsletter as well as JMI’s U.S. Pharmacist® eConnect® newsletter and e-pharrm/alert®, a promotional service of JMI’s subsidiary ?alert Marketing, Inc.
    • On occasion, the commercial supporter of a CE activity will ask us for a list of the people who participated in that activity, so that it may document the first level of outcomes-based evaluation in CE (i.e., who attended, which medical specialties/practices were represented, how this compares to the target audience, and whether the activity needs to be repeated because significant numbers of the target audience did not attend).  In that event, we will provide the supporter with your name, title and affiliation, but we will request in writing that the supporter not contact you directly for any purpose.
  • If we sell, assign, or otherwise transfer the business of this Web site to which your personal information relates, we will transfer your personal information to the buyer and require the buyer to agree to treat it in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
  • If we are required to disclose your personal information by any applicable law, court order, or government regulation, we will comply with such laws, orders or regulations.

Do you use cookies (and what are they)?
Cookies are pieces of information that a Web site places on your computer to facilitate and enhance your communication and interaction with that site by making it easier for you to navigate the site.  That is how we use cookies on this site.  We do not use cookies to retrieve personal information from your computer.  Cookies (and Java-Script) must be enabled for you to enter and use this site.  The Nielsen technology that BPA uses includes cookies to gather statistical information which is designed to assist in understanding what users find interesting and useful on this site.

Classifieds About USP

U.S. Pharmacist is a monthly journal dedicated to providing the nation's pharmacists with up-to-date, authoritative, peer-reviewed clinical articles relevant to contemporary pharmacy practice in a variety of settings, including community pharmacy, hospitals, managed care systems, ambulatory care clinics, home care organizations, long-term care facilities, industry and academia. The publication is also useful to pharmacy technicians, students, other health professionals and individuals interested in health management. Pharmacists licensed in the U.S. can earn Continuing Education credits through Postgraduate Healthcare Education, LLC, accredited by the Accreditation Council for Pharmacy Education (ACPE) as a provider of continuing pharmacy education.
