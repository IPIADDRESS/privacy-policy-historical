Privacy Statement

The American Immigration Center is committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect on the site mainly to process orders, make shopping at American Immigration Center possible and to enhance your overall shopping experience. The American Immigration Center is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of other Web sites. If you wish us not to use your e-mail address for UPS shipping confirmation, please let us know by placing a note on your order or by letting us know over the phone.

We also monitor customer traffic patterns usage and interaction with our web site to help us develop and improve the design and layout of the American Immigration Center.

By using our Web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by the American Immigration Center.
