This is the web site of United Service Organizations, Inc. (USO) World Headquarters. We do not set cookies or use this web site to collect any personally identifiable information about you other than what you provide voluntarily. We may use such voluntarily provided information to improve our understanding of your interests, to improve our services, or to contact you. We will not sell your name or contact information to a third party. We sometimes exchange contact lists, but only with other charities. If you do not want any personally identifiable information about yourself collected through this site, please do not provide it. At your request we will make reasonable efforts to remove any such information from our records. You may submit your request by email (Info@USO.org) by telephone (202-610-5700), or by mail (1008 Eberle Place, SE, Suite 301, Washington, DC 20374-5096).

 If you visit the web sites of any of our partners, please consult their privacy policy.
