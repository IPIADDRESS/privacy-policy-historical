The Department of Defense does not keep a database of information obtained from the use of WMCT. General instructions for how you may opt out of some of the most commonly used WMCT is available at http://www.usa.gov/optout_instructions.shtml, and for Google Analytics specifically at https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptou.. Opting out still permits users to access comparable information or services.
