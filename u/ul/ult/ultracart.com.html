Our Commitment To Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our homepage.

The Information We Collect:

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the UltraCart website. On some pages, you can submit information about your company. The type of information collected on these pages are:

Name
Address
Email address
Phone number
Credit/Debit Card Information
Product Information
Pricing Information
(etc.)

On some pages, your customers can order products, make requests, and register to receive materials. The types of personal information collected at these pages are:

Name
Address
Email address
Phone number
Credit/Debit Card Information
(etc.)

On some pages, customers can submit information about other people. For example, if a customer orders a gift online and want it sent directly to the recipient, the customer will need to submit the recipient's address. In this circumstance, the types of personal information collected are:

Name
Address
Phone Number
(etc.)

The Way We Use Information:

We use the information you provide about your company only to provide the UltraCart services that power your online store. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extend necessary to provide the expected service.

We use the information customers provide about themrselves when placing an order only to complete that order. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete that order.

We use the information customer's provide about someone else when placing an order only to facilitate shipment of the product and to confirm delivery. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete that order.

We use return email addresses to answer the email we receive. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties. We allow merchants to use opt in emails collected during the course of business to do followup marketing to their customers. We do not share their email addresses with other merchants orthird parties.

We use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design our website and to share with advertisers. For example, we may tell an advertiser that X number of individuals visited a certain area on our website, or that Y number of men and Z number of women filled out our registration form, but we would not disclose anything that could be used to identify those individuals.

Finally, we never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described above without also providing you an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise prohibit such unrelated uses. For a complete list of privacy issues please read section 8 of the Terms and Conditions of service before signing up for the service.

Our Commitment To Data Security

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

Our Commitment To Children's Privacy:

Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under 13.

How You Can Access Or Correct Your Information

You can access all your company information that we collect online and maintain by UltraCart privacy policy. We use this procedure to better safeguard your information.

How To Contact Us

Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please call us at 209-383-9870 or send us an email at support@ultracart.com.
