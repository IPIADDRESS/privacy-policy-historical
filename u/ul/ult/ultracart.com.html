Privacy Policy

Effective 05-24-2018

UltraCart has never and will never sell your data. UltraCart has never and will never give your data to third party unless explicitly instructed by you for the operation of your online store, such as a fulfillment house, payment processor, or your email marketer.

Your privacy is important to us. We provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way we collect and use your information to better protect your privacy. This privacy policy is available on our homepage.

Information from merchants:

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the UltraCart website. On some pages, you may submit information about your company. The type of information collected on these pages are:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Credit/Debit Card Information
  • Product Information
  • Pricing Information
  • Your website name
  • Additional store configuration

The way we use merchant information:

We use the information you provide about your company only to provide the UltraCart services that power your online store. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to provide the expected service.

We use return email addresses to answer the email we receive. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties. We use name and merchant account during chat help to access account information and provide assistance. Any information collected during a support chat session is retained solely to make improvements to our platform and customer service.

We use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design our website and to share with advertisers. For example, we may tell an advertiser that X number of individuals visited a certain area on our website, or that Y number of men and Z number of women filled out our registration form., but we would not disclose anything that could be used to identify those individuals. The information collected is anonymous.

We may also share your information in the following circumstances:

  • to prevent, investigate, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of our Terms of Service or any other agreement related to the Services, or as otherwise required by law.
  • to help us conduct marketing and/or advertising campaigns.
  • to conform to legal requirements, or to respond to lawful court orders, subpoenas, warrants, or other requests by public authorities (including to meet national security or law enforcement requirements).

We retain merchant personal information throughout their relationship with UltraCart. When a merchant cancels their service, they are given a choice to delete their personal data after 60 days or retain their data indefinitely. Many merchants are seasonal and desire to retain their store and settings for intermittent use. Other merchants wish to leave their store intact in case they wish to return to UltraCart at a later date.

Finally, we never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described above without also providing you an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise prohibit such unrelated uses. For a complete list of privacy issues please read section 8 of the Terms and Conditions of service before signing up for the service.

Our commitment to data security

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online as defined by the Payment Card Industry (PCI-DSS Version 3.1) and regional laws where applicable.

We perform annual audits conducted by third party auditing firms to ensure our compliance with PCI-DSS Level 1, which is the highest level of compliance available.

No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your personal information.

Our commitment to children's privacy:

Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under 13.

How you can access or correct your information

You may access all your company information that we collect online and maintain according to our UltraCart privacy policy. We use this procedure to safeguard your information.
