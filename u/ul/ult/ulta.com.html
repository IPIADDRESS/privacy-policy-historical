Privacy

At ULTA.com, we are committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of your children online. If you are a child under the age of 13, you must get your parent's or guardian's consent, and provide that consent to ULTA.com, in the manner that ULTA.com directs herein, before you provide any information on the ULTA.com website. ULTA.com will not require, and in fact is prohibited by law from requiring, children to provide personal information in order to participate in a promotion or other activity on our site unless the information is necessary for such participation.

What We Collect and How We Use It

When you place an order, we ask for your e-mail address, the mailing address(es) and phone number(s) (optional) where you'd like your items shipped, your billing address, credit card number and credit card expiration date. During the order process, you might also provide other shipping addresses (for sending gifts directly) or provide a gift tag message that's specific to a particular person. Collecting this information allows us to complete your order efficiently and to notify you of your order status.

When you create an account with us, we offer the opportunity to become a member of the Club at ULTA.com, to receive future discounts and gifts and to receive ULTA.com e-mailings. You can also sign up for e-mailings by providing your e-mail address through our site services or our affiliated retail stores.

When you participate in the Club at ULTA.com, subscribe to an ULTA.com newsletter, participate in an ULTA.com promotion, or ask to be notified when an out-of-stock product is available, we ask for your name and e-mail address (and certain other information in the case of the Club at ULTA). If you would like to stop receiving promotions, updates and/or special offers at any time, you may e-mail us at service@ULTA.com with a statement requesting that you be deleted from the Club at ULTA.com membership list or that ULTA.com stop sending such promotions, updates and special offers to you.

We May Ask You for Special Information

ULTA.com may ask customers to submit information, such as birthdays and product commentary, on a voluntary basis. We also may ask for information about our customer service. If we ask for this information, we will either tell you how the information will be used by us or we will use it in conformity with the User Agreement on the ULTA.com website.

Cookies

Like most major websites, we use a feature of your web browser called a "cookie". Cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to your computer's hard drive for record-keeping purposes. We use these cookie files to track your preferences and purchases as you navigate through our website and to save you from having to re-enter information every time you visit ULTA.com. To sum up, by using cookies to track your visits, we can make your next shopping experience at ULTA.com better. To search our website and/or place an order from the ULTA.com website, you need to have the cookies feature of your web browser turned on. However, if you choose to disable the cookies feature of your web browser, you may not be able to place an order or otherwise use some or all of the ULTA.com website.

Advertisers and partners may also use their own cookies. ULTA.com does not control use of these cookies and ULTA.com expressly disclaims responsibility for information collected through them.

Security

ULTA.com shall protect the security of your personal information and credit card detail when you shop with us online using the security protections described below. We use Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology – the industry standard – to encrypt the personal information and credit card detail you provide during the order process.

To ensure the security of your personal information, you should always provide that information over a secure connection. You can check the existence of a secure connection in one of two ways:

1. Look at the URL in your web browser. Whenever you are connected to a secure server, that connection will be represented in the URL as "https," rather than "http."

2. Look at the lower left-hand corner of your web browser. You know you have an SSL connection if you see an unbroken key in Netscape Navigator (versions 2.0 and above), or a closed lock in Microsoft Internet Explorer (version 3.0 and above) or AOL (AOL 3.0 and above).

While ULTA.com makes every effort to ensure the integrity and security of its network and systems, ULTA.com cannot guarantee that its security measures will prevent third-party "hackers" from illegally obtaining this information.
