Unbxd Privacy Policy

This is the general privacy policy that the founders and team members of Unbxd Software Pvt. Ltd., hereby referred to as “Unbxd” all agree to adhere to. If you have any questions about this or any other policy, please reach out to us at: support@unbxd.com and we will respond promptly. Unbxd privacy policy respects the following principles:

We Do Not Share Customer Information

Unbxd does not share customer information of any kind with anyone or any third party. We will not sell or rent your name or personal information to any third party. We do not sell, rent or provide outside access to our mailing list or any data we store. A user or business wholly owns any data that a user stores via our facilities. At anytime a user or business is free to take their data and leave, or to simply delete their data from our facilities.

We Only Collect Necessary Information

Unbxd only collects such personal information that is necessary for you to access and use our services. This personal information includes, but is not limited to, first and last name, pin code, email address, credit card information and/or other financial information and personal information necessary to generate proper legal documents and maintain proper billing methods for the services offered.

We Protect Your Data

Unbxd takes strong measures to protect any data that you have submitted to us, especially data that is personal in nature, including profile and financial information. We have strived to protect it from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

You Have Access to Your Data

Along with the data that you may host with Unbxd, you also have access to your personal/profile data as well as your financial data. At all times, the owner of your Unbxd account can access this data and edit this data as necessary. This allows you to ensure that this data is correct.

We Still Have to Follow Laws

Unbxd may release personal information if the partnership is required to do so by law, search warrant, subpoena, court order or fraud investigation. We may also use personal information in a manner that does not identify you specifically nor allow you to be contacted but does identify certain criteria about our Site’s users in general (such as we may inform third parties about the number of registered users, number of unique visitors, number of databases used, number of datastores, and the pages most frequently browsed, etc. without associating this data with customer name or organization).

Let’s Talk

If a customer or potential customer of Unbxd has any specific questions, concerns or complaints about our privacy policies or the methods in which we collect the necessary data to run our business, they are encouraged to send an e-mail to support@unbxd.com and one of our team members will respond promptly and will work with them to find an amicable resolution to the issue.
