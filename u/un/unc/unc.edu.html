Privacy Statement

Thank you for visiting our site.  The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill (“the University”) is committed to fulfilling its responsibilities of transparency as a state sponsored institution of higher learning, and protecting certain types of information.  The University intends to use information it collects only for appropriate purposes.

By using this website, University network, or related resources (University systems), you agree to abide by any applicable University Policies (such as the IT Acceptable Use Policy).

Information collection and use:

The University may collect information about how users access and use its web sites, networks, and related resources. The information collected on this site or from use of University systems may include the computer address used, the internet domain, the website the user came from or goes to, measurements of use, and other details. This data is used for website and network management, to improve website content, and for security purposes. Cookies and similar technology may be used to facilitate the navigation of this site. Though this general statement applies to most University sites, other sites within the unc.edu domain (signified by a different term such as “privacy.unc.edu” or “directory.unc.edu”) may have different privacy practices. If applicable, consult the privacy statement on each page or use the contact information available for that unit to get more information.

Pages on unc.edu sites may directly request information from you for specific purposes.  That information will be used in the intended manner.  If you have questions about how it will be used, please contact the page owner before submitting information.

Please be aware that the University has various legal and regulatory obligations to disclose many types of information, as well as operational reasons for doing so.  Unless specifically protected (e.g., FERPA protected student information, HIPAA protected health information) the University may disclose information obtained through your use of University systems for University business purposes in accordance with governing law.

Contacting us and reporting misconduct:

For more information or to report any misconduct or abuse of this Web site, please see our contacts or call 919-962.HELP.
