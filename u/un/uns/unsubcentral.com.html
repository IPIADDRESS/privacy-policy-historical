Notwithstanding the foregoing, use of the UnsubCentral suppression service (the “Service”) shall be governed by the applicable services agreement between UnsubCentral and you (or the appropriate third party if other than you) and our information privacy practices for customer data as described below.

We may periodically make changes to this Privacy Policy and those changes become effective immediately upon posting them to the Site. It is your responsibility to review this Policy frequently and remain informed about any changes to it, so we encourage you to visit this page often. We are not required to provide notice of changes to this Privacy Policy.

  • First and Last Name

  • Email Address
  • Occupation/Title
  • Company

  • Company URL

We are the sole owner of information collected on the Website, except for contact lists and content that you provide to us in connection with your use of the Service (including but not limited to your logo or office locations). If you provide us PII or any other information, you are representing to us that the information is true, accurate, complete and current. You are also representing to us that you have the authorization to provide it to us.

UnsubCentral may keep a cached copy of the content you provide or disclose to us as a part of your use of the Service for an indeterminate or indefinite period of time, including media files. Non-Personally Identifiable Information

Non-personally identifiable information is information collected by UnsubCentral in the normal course of operating its business that does not reveal the specific identity of the individual. We collect certain aggregate and non-personal information when you visit this Website and through the Service. This information does not relate to a single identifiable visitor. It tells us such things as how many users visited the website and the pages accessed. Collecting this information helps us tailor our Website to our visitors.

When you visit the Website to browse, read or download information, we automatically collect standard computer information that your browser sends to every website you visit, such as your IP address, browser type and language, cookie information, access times, referring website addresses and the page you request. We generally do not link standard computer information to anything that will allow us to identify users unless to prevent fraud and to protect our Service, Website, users, customers or others.

We also may collect aggregate and non-personal information through "cookies" as explained below.

The information, including PII, UnsubCentral collects and receives is used to:

  • Provide, offer or deliver the Service

  • Additionally, if you provide PII for a certain reason, we may use the PII in connection with the reason for which it was provided and for any other reason consistent with the terms of this Privacy Policy. For instance, if you contact us by e-mail, we will use the PII you provide to answer your question or resolve your problem

Cookies

Cookies are pieces of information that any website may transfer to your browser that are then stored in your computers system. Cookies are returned to the website that deposited them when a visitor returns to that site. Our Website uses cookies to keep track of your session, to save passwords and to make sure you are not subjected to repeated advertising. We may also use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests.

Most web browsers are set up to accept cookies. You can, however, reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. If you choose to decline cookies, some of the functionality of the Website may be impaired.
