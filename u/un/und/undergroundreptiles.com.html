UNDERGROUND REPTILES PRIVACY POLICY

At Underground Reptiles we value and respect our customers and their individual privacy. The following describes our policies regarding the personal information you provide us while making your purchase.

Underground Reptiles will not sell or rent your personal information. Any information you provide while making orders with us is only available to employees managing this information for the purpose of completing your order or communicating with you about your order.

If you receive a mailing from us, it is either because you have purchased from our company or your address was listed with us as someone who expressly shared it with us for the purpose of receiving future information. If you receive an unwanted email from us, please forward a copy of that email with your comments to us and we will modify your notification information in our system.

By utilizing our online ordering system or by contacting us via email, you are communicating with us electronically and in doing so agree to recognize notifications, disclosures, agreements and other communications provided to you by us electronically as satisfactorily meeting any legal requirement for such communications occurring in writing.
