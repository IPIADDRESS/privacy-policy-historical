Important Notice On Customer Privacy

At UniBank, protecting the privacy and confidentiality of your personal and financial information is important to us. We value your business and the trust you put in UniBank. It is our mission to protect the information you have entrusted with us. At the same time, we want to offer you the array of financial products and services that you need to accomplish your financial goals. We believe we can do both through the privacy practices described in this notice. In the event that you end your customer relationship with UniBank, we will continue to protect your information in accordance with these guidelines. We will provide you with our Customer Privacy Notice every year. We will notify you in advance if we change our privacy practices.

Information We Collect

We collect nonpublic personal information about you from the following sources:

  • Information we receive from you on applications or other forms;
  • Information about your transactions with us, our affiliates, or others; and
  • Information we receive from a consumer reporting agency.

Information We Disclose

We do not disclose any nonpublic personal information about our customers or former customers to anyone, except as permitted by law.

Service Providers And Joint Marketers

We may disclose all of the information we collect, as described above to companies that perform marketing services on our behalf or to other financial institutions with whom we have joint marketing agreements.

Information Sharing Within Our Corporate Family

In order to offer you the products and services to meet your needs, we may disclose all the information we collect to companies owned by UFS Bancorp, which include financial service providers UniBank, UniBank Fiscal Advisory Services, Inc., Tommark Inc. /dba Sterling Associates, Tommark Insurance and Dimen Financial Group, Inc. We will share this information only to the extent necessary to offer you appropriate products and services.

How We Protect Your Information

We restrict access to nonpublic personal information about you to those employees who have a business need to know that information. We maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal standards to guard your nonpublic personal information.

Your Rights

Under current law we may share information regarding our transactions and experiences with you with our affiliates. Unless you tell us not to, we may also share all other information we have collected. If you prefer that we not share this additional information within our corporate family, you may direct us not to by completing the OPT OUT form at the end of this notice and mailing it to:

UniBank
Attn: Operations Department
49 Church Street
Whitinsville , MA 01588-1415

An OPT OUT by one account holder will apply equally to all parties of a joint account.
