We will ask you when we need information that personally identifies you (usually your name and e-mail address). To learn more about this behavioral advertising practice or to opt-out of this type of advertising, you can visit http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp.

You need to acknowledge that, whenever you disclose information in public areas on the Website, such as comment areas on the Website, the information in the comment you post becomes public. Use of your Personal Information
Unigamesity will use the Personal Information you submit to (1) make sure we credit your work correctly in case of user submitted content; and (2) to correctly send you prizes you might win in contests held on the Website. However, when you submit User Content, we will only share the name you allow us to share (which can also be a nickname or alias). We will never send you unwanted e-mails, messages or promotional features. We will never share, rent or sell your e-mail address to any other company or third party.

Email Correspondence

How we protect information collected from you
We show great interest in confidentiality. Unigamesity is a spam free zone. Also, we will never share your Personal Information or e-mail address to third parties without your prior express consent.
