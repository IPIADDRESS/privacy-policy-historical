What is personal information?

Personal Information covers any data that enables us to identify you as an individual. This information could include, name, address, telephone number, email address, credit card details or bank details (submitted when making a donation).

How do we use your personal information?

We, United Bible Societies, use your contact details to process your requests for information, goods or services.

When you supply information such as your name or email address to us, we are legally obliged by the Data Protection Act 1998 to ensure that we only use this information for the purpose for which it was given, and to ensure that it is kept securely.

Do we share your personal information?

United Bible Societies only shares your information as shown below.

  • Your information may be passed to Bible Societies and individuals to perform functions on our behalf. Examples include fulfilling orders and supplying information. These parties may only use your information to perform these functions and may not use it for any other purposes.
  • Your information will not be passed to third parties for marketing purposes without your consent.
  • You will be invited to give your consent for your contact information to be shared with the Bible Society in your country of residence in order that the Bible Society may keep you informed of new projects, products and services. Your personal details will not be shared for these purposes without your consent.
