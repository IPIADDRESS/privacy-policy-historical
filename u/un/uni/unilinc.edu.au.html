Privacy Statement

The Privacy Statement sets out how UNILINC uses and protects any personal information that you give to us when you visit and use this web site. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. If you choose to correspond with us through e-mail, we may retain the content of your e-mail messages together with your e-mail address and our responses.

We will keep the personal information we gather for the following reasons:

  • Internal record keeping;
  • We may periodically send e-mails about news, special events or other information which we think you may find interesting, using the e-mail address which you have provided;
  • We will not sell, distribute or lease your personal information to third parties unless we have your permission or are required by law to do so.

We may record your visits to the web site for statistical purposes only. Google Analytics helps us to analyse how users use the web site and to compile reports on web site activities.

If you believe that any information we are holding on you is incorrect or incomplete, please e-mail us as soon as possible. We will promptly correct any information found to be incorrect.
