Privacy Policy
Unitemps is committed to ensuring your privacy is protected. When signing up for a Unitemps account, we collect your personal data as per the registration form. We will not contact you other than to send you the bulletins you have subscribed for or internal news about our services for jobseekers, or offers from our partner companies and we will not distribute any of the details you provide to a third party. We have put in place suitable security procedures to safeguard your information.

Cookies
The Unitemps Website uses cookies to provide enhanced functionality and to gather information about how the site is being used to help us continually improve the user experience. For more details about what cookies are, the cookies that we use and how to disable them, please see our Cookie Policy
