UNI.CC Network Privacy Statement

Because your privacy is important to us, we are committed to protect your privacy and to develop technology that gives you a powerful and safe online experience.

The UNI.CC Network (UNICC) collects information of visitors to it's network of sites, including the domain name, visited pages, referring URLs, and other publicly available information. UNICC uses this information,which does not personally identify you, to help improve its web site and services, and to customize the content of its pages for each individual consumer. Generally, this information is collected through "traffic data" and may entail the use of "Cookies", "IP addresses" or other numeric codes used to identify a computer.

UNICC maintains a database of member information which is used only for internal purposes such as demographic statistics, to display appropriate individualized advertisements, technical support and notifying members of changes or enhancements to this service, new services and products that may be of interest to them.

UNICC members may update their personal information at any time from within their UNICC accounts by going to the 'Member Login' option at http://www.uni.cc.

Any UNICC member may cancel their membership at any time by deleting all domains under his account and by clicking on the 'Click here to cancel your UNI.CC Network membership' link, which expresses the agreement to delete his record from our database. When an account is cancelled, all user information is removed from the UNICC system related to that account.

UNICC does not sell or commercially make available specific information about its users or members, except in cooperation with law enforcement bodies in regards to content violations or violations of applicable laws.

Under exigent circumstances, UNICC may be required to send you information about the service or your account via e-mail which may contain an advertisement section, but UNICC will not send you unsolicited email regarding any commercial offers or advertisements at any time.

Questions or problems regarding this privacy statement may be sent to info@nic.uni.cc. Please let us know if you have any suggestions, problems, or ideas regarding this web page.
