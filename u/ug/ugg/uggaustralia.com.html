Deckers Outdoor Corporation Privacy Policy

The Deckers Outdoor family of product lines (Teva Footwear, UGG Australia and Simple Shoes) takes the protection and proper use of your personal information seriously. We respect your privacy, and take great care to safeguard information in our possession. Your preferences for use of your information are our highest priority.

UGG Australia does not share customer information (including e-mail addresses) outside the Deckers Outdoor family of companies unless it is necessary to provide you with a Deckers Outdoor products or services, or we are legally required to do so, with the following exception: We occasionally make information about UGG Australia catalog customers, but not e-mail addresses, available to other quality companies whose offerings you might enjoy. If you are a mail order catalog customer and don't want to have your name made available to other companies, please call us at 1-888-432-8530 or contact us at www.uggaustralia.com.

Even within the Deckers Outdoor family of companies, we restrict access to your information to those individuals who need it to provide you with services. Any third-party companies we must use to provide you with services are required to keep your information secure and confidential.

The Deckers Outdoor family of companies is happy to provide further details of our privacy policies. For more information about our catalog and online privacy policy, call us at 1-888-432-8530.

Deckers Outdoor Corporation (UGG Australia) privacy policy 11/01/2004.
