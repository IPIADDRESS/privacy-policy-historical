UGG Australia Privacy Policy

UGG Australia takes the protection and proper use of your personal information seriously. We respect your privacy, and take great care to safeguard information in our possession. Your preferences for use of your information are our highest priority.

UGG Australia does not share customer information (including e-mail addresses) outside the company unless it is necessary to provide you with UGG Australia products or services.

Even within UGG Australia, we restrict access to your information to those individuals who need it to provide you with services. Any third-party companies we must use to provide you with services are required to keep your information secure and confidential. Uggaustralia.com uses "cookies" only to the extent necessary to facilitate your Internet transactions.
