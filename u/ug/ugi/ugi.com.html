Personal Information could include, but is not limited to, information that personally identifies you such as your name, address, e-mail address, telephone number, cell phone number, photographs or material submitted to the Website, UGI Utilities Social Media Sites or sweepstakes or surveys administered by or on behalf of UGI Utilities, or other information such as gender, occupation and demographic information or any other information that is identified as personal information under applicable law.

We do not sell, share, or otherwise distribute to third parties Personal Information without your consent in any way not disclosed in this Privacy Policy.

Information we automatically collect:

Cookies
Some of our web pages utilize cookies and other tracking technologies. A cookie is a small text file that a website transfers to an individual’s computer or other device for record-keeping purposes. For example, we may use cookies to collect information about website activity. You may set most browsers to notify you if you receive a cookie, or you may choose to block cookies with your browser by accessing your browser settings. You may be able to delete existing cookies within this functionality. However, please be aware that some features of the Website may not function properly or may be slower if you refuse cookies, and therefore, we recommend that your browser settings accept cookies for this Website. Cookies may be used with Third Parties to provide you with interest-based advertising or targeted content, as described below.

Tracking
Tracking technologies (including Javascript, beacons and pixels) may record information such as Internet domain and host names; Internet protocol (IP) addresses; browser software and operating system types; demographic information, click stream patterns; and dates and times that our Website is accessed. However, we reserve the right to use IP addresses and other tracking technologies to identify a visitor when we feel it is necessary to enforce compliance with the Website’s policies, to protect the Website, our customers, or others, or when we believe in good faith that the law requires it.
