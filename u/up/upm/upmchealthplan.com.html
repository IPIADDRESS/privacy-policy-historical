Privacy Statement

UPMC Health Plan is absolutely committed to safeguarding your privacy. Your health plan coverage, any records, or information you choose to share with us will be kept in the strictest confidence. We will not sell, license or transmit any of your information to any organization outside the University of Pittsburgh Medical Center (UPMC) Health System unless required to do so by legal, judicial or governmental proceedings, or unless we do so in accordance with your express authorization. In some cases, we may disclose information to our contractors or agents for the purpose of enabling them to perform certain functions for us. We will disclose information in compliance with applicable laws and regulations and will require the recipient to protect the information and use it only for the purpose provided.

By "personal information," we mean data that is unique to an individual, such as name, address, social security number, email address, or telephone number. From time to time, we may solicit personal information from you in order to deliver materials you have requested, respond to your questions, or provide a product or service.

Email

We are sincerely interested in hearing from you. If you have comments or questions about this web site or your account, please feel free to contact us . We may share your comments and questions with our customer service representatives and those UPMC employees most capable of responding to your questions and concerns. In every other respect, we will keep your comments and questions confidential. You should be aware that your email, like most non-encrypted Internet email, might be accessed and viewed by other Internet users without your knowledge or permission. While this is not a common occurrence, we suggest you do not send sensitive or confidential information (such as your social security number) via email to UPMC Health Plan. It is more secure to use the telephone for confidential or sensitive communication.

Online Forms

Any personal information you may enter on an online (data entry) form at our site will be protected by a secure transmission mechanism such as Secure Sockets Layer (SSL). This industry-standard method of encrypting information being sent to a web site protects everything from credit card transactions to enrollment information.

Monitoring Usage - "Cookies"

We may monitor and record your usage of our site during your visit; but the information is collected only to enhance your experience while you are here. For example, we use the information to make sure you receive a quick response or we provide the type of information you want to see. We monitor through the use of 'cookies.' A 'cookie' is an element of data that a web site can send to your browser while you are using the site. It is not a computer program and cannot read data from your computer or perform any action on your data.

Cookies are used to identify repeat visitors but do not contain any identifying information about you (such as your name, email address, login user ID or password). Cookies cannot obtain any personal information about you that you do not provide voluntarily.

Most browsers allow you to control whether you allow cookies to be created in your browser. You can set your browser to notify you when a web site sends a cookie to your browser. Most browsers also provide a way for you to remove cookies from your computer.

Effective Date

This statement is effective as of November 1, 2000. This statement may change at any time without prior notice.
