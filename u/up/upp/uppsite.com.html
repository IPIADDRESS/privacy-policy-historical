You may, through the Web Site, register to receive additional information (e.g. newsletters) from us, or to use one of our services now or in the future (e.g. uploading content and using our services). MySiteApp Email Marketing Communication Policy

MySiteApp sends promotional email only to:

  • Customers
  • People who have given consent to MySiteApp to receive promotional email
  • People on rented email lists who have given permission to the list owner to receive third-party promotional email
  • All of the abovementioned who have not opted out after having received promotional email from us.

In each promotional email, we include an easy and clear way to opt out of future MySiteApp email. Opt-out requests are submitted through Support@MySiteapp.com, and such Opt-out requests are processed within 10 business days. When sending email to rented email lists, we suppress addresses of those people who have opted out of MySiteApp email in the past.
We always disclose our identity in mass email communications, including the address of our headquarters location.
We never use deceiving header information or subject lines.
We never send mass, promotional email to email addresses “harvested” from websites.
We never, under any circumstances, rent or sell email addresses to any other company or individual.
When coordinating with our resellers for the purpose of follow-up on sales inquiries made to us, we provide full contact information of the inquirer to the reseller, including email address, so the reseller can follow up on the inquiry.

Our marketing email policy and practice fully conforms to the United States Federal “CAN SPAM” act of 2003 and the applicable Israeli statues such as the Protection of Privacy Law 5741-1981.  Note that some regions such as the European Union, have different email laws and guidelines, and MySiteApp conforms to local laws in those cases. We welcome and encourage your thoughts on this policy at Support@MySiteapp.com.

In addition to the information we collect as described above, we use technology to collect anonymous information about the use of our Web Site and any application created using our Web Site. This technology does not identify you personally; it simply enables us to compile statistics about our visitors and their use of our Web Site. The statistical information may be transmitted to third party sites which then use it to custom tailor media sent by them through the App.We also use this anonymous data to improve the content and functionality of this Web Site, the App and our e-mail updates, to better understand our customers and markets, and to improve our products and services.

Cookies
  • What is a cookie: In order to collect the anonymous data described in the preceding paragraph, we may use “cookies” that collect the first level domain name of the user and the date and time you accessed this Web Site.  A cookie is a small file containing a string of characters. This information is sent to your computer when you use the Web Site and it’s stored on your computer, subject to your cookies storage preferences, so that we can uniquely identify a user’s browser.
  • Diagnose Problems: We also use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web Site. An IP address is a numeric code that identifies your computer on a network, or in this case, the Internet. Your IP address is also used to gather broad demographic information, such as determining the geographical regions of our visitors and users.
  • Advertising: We may also use cookies and similar tools to customize the content and advertising you receive based on the Personal Data you have provided.  Personal Data you provide in structured profile fields or questions, information you add to open-ended profile fields and questions and other information about you may also be used to customize the online ads you encounter to those we believe are aligned with your interests.  The information used for this feature does not provide your personal information or identify you as an individual to third parties.
