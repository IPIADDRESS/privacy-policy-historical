Privacy policy

General

Ucaster is committed to protecting your privacy when you use the Ucaster website. This Privacy Policy informs you of the types of information Ucaster collects about you, how that information is used, whether and to whom Ucaster discloses it, and the choices you have regarding your personal information. Please read carefully.


Collection of Information

Ucaster may automatically collect certain aggregated data regarding your use of the website. Aggregated information is anonymous, statistical information that reflects the scope of the website's user base, sales, customer patterns and related site information. In addition, certain non-personally identifiable information is collected by Ucaster and other third-party advertising providers during your use of the Ucaster website and in serving advertisements to the Ucaster website.

Ucaster may also use cookies to track and understand the traffic on the Ucaster website. Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that the Ucaster website places onto the user's computer hard drive. Cookies identify the user's computer hard drive so that the Ucaster website can recognize you the next time you visit.

Third party advertisements displayed on or in connection with the Ucaster website may also contain cookies set by such advertisers. Ucaster does not control these cookies and users of the Ucaster website should check the privacy policy of such advertisers to understand whether
and how it uses cookies.

Ucaster does not use your email address or other personally identifiable information to send commercial or marketing messages without your consent or except as part of a specific program or feature for which you will have the ability to opt-in or opt-out. Ucaster may, however, use
your email address without further consent for non-marketing or administrative purposes
