uComics Network Privacy Policies
Last Updated 11.01.2000
uComics.com (uclick, LLC) is a general audience entertainment site dedicated to providing the Internet-using public with the most complete and enjoyable online comic viewing experience. uComics is also concerned about your privacy and we encourage you to read our Privacy Policy to learn all about how we use information about visitors to our Web site(s).

We also encourage you to read our Children's Privacy Policy - it details how we deal with personally identifiable information collected from children 13 years of age or younger.

What does the uComics/uclick Privacy Policy cover?
  • This policy covers the treatment and use of personally identifiable information that is collected by uComics/uclick, at any of its Web sites and when you sign up to use any of the services provided at the sites.
  • This policy does not cover information that may or may not be collected by companies or web sites that uclick does not own or control. This policy also does not guide the behavior of individuals not employed by uclick or uComics.
  • What information does uComics/uclick collect and use?
  • uComics and uclick does not sell or provide personally identifiable information about you to any third party.
  • uComics collects personally identifiable information when (i) you register for a uComics account (the extent of the information collected depends upon what you provide during the registration process), (ii) when you use certain uComics services, (iii) when you make purchases from uComics-controlled or affiliated online stores and e-commerce sites and (iv) if you enter any special promotions or contests provided by Ucomics for its users.
  • When you choose to join or register for any uComics or uclick controlled Web site or service we will ask for your name, e-mail address, birth date, gender, zip code, occupation, interests and other biographic data to assist us in personalizing your uComics.com experience.
  • uComics also receives certain information automatically from our computer network and servers, including your IP address, certain "cookie" information, server log information and information that details which uComics pages have been requested by your browser.
  • uComics and uclick may use user data in an aggregate form for marketing and advertising purposes and may rent, sell or lease this aggregate information to responsible third parties using the information for legitimate purposes.
  • All of the information received or collected by uComics is used for one or more of the following reasons: to allow for customization of content and advertising/marketing information at our site, to allow us to respond to your requests for products or services provided by uComics, and to contact you about new products, changes to existing products and services provided by uComics for our users.
  • uComics and uclick may provide personally identifiable information about you to other companies, affiliates and people if:

    - You have consented to the sharing of the information.
    - The information must be shared to provide you with a product or service requested by you.
    - The information must be shared with companies who work with uComics or uclick to provide a product or service to you.
    - We are required by law, or we are required to respond to subpoenas, court orders, legal process, to cooperate with a legitimate law enforcement investigation or to investigate a threat to our network or services.
    - We are investigating actions by users whose actions violate our Terms of Service and Conditions of Use or any other guideline or rule we have established that controls the use of our service or products
Collection and Use of Computer-Generated Information
  • uComics and uclick will utilize "cookie" technology by sending and/or receiving information from our computers to your browser and computer.
  • Other companies over whom uComics.com and uclick have no control that serve advertisements on our Web sites may also utilize "cookie" technology to send and/or receive information from their computers to your browser and computer.
  • uComics and uclick will use server logs to track your movement through our Web sites and properties - in addition, we will use this information to customize the user experience or provide registrant continuity between our various Web sites and properties.
  • We may also use other technologies to verify and track your use of certain services, confirm receipt of certain e-mailed products or to test the integrity of our systems, products and services.
How much control will you have over your account information?
  • uComics and uclick will provide you with the ability to edit your uComics account, registration and preference information. We will also give you the option of whether you wish to receive marketing and products information from us excluding the marketing information connected with products or services specifically requested by you. For example, if you request to receive a daily uComics comic via e-mail delivery we will, at your request attach certain marketing or advertising information to the comic you receive.
  • You will have the ability to request the deletion of your uComics registration at any time by utilizing the registration system established for this purpose. However, we may maintain certain other information as part of our internal processes to prevent accidental loss of user data or information and to prevent or discourage fraudulent activities that may involve uComics registration or account information. We may also continue to maintain information about your uComics registration or account and your computer access to our Web sites or services as part of our server logs.
Security
  • uComics and uclick utilize access passwords at our Web sites and other properties to help maintain your privacy and security.
  • In certain other areas uComics and uclick may use certain other industry-standard security measures, techniques and technologies to protect the integrity of data transmissions.
Changes to the uComics / uclick Privacy Policy

Please check this policy periodically as from time to time uComics and uclick may change or alter this policy. At the top of this policy we will indicate the date of the last update. All such changes or alterations will be effective as of the date they are posted on our Web site.

Disclaimer

Certain links within this site will take you into other Web sites for which uComics and uclick will take no responsibility. uComics is a general-audience Web site that provides content that may be of interest to children 13 years of age and younger. Because of this, uComics and uclick have certain policies regarding the use of personally identifiable information about children under the age of 13, users who fall in this age range may not have access to all of the services and products offered by uComics and uclick.

This policy, in addition to the uComics and uclick Privacy Policy, covers the use of all information and data collected at Ucomics.com or other uclick, LLC controlled and owned Web sites and properties from children 13 years of age or younger.

Information Collection and Use
  • If a user under the age of 13 attempts to register or join any Web site or property controlled or owned by uComics or uclick the registration will not be completed or accepted until the consent of the child's parent is secured.
  • If a user under the age of 13 attempts to register for an account with uComics or another uclick Web site, the user must provide information sufficient to allow uComics and uclick to secure the permission of the child's parent to utilize our products and services. This information will include the parent's e-mail address, mailing address, phone number and full name.
  • uComics and uclick may require that children under the age of 13 provide the same information required of all other users to establish a uComics user account, including first and last name, e-mail address, birth date, zip code and personal interests. uComics and uclick uses this information to personalize the user experience, maintain account integrity and accounting, and to provide customized marketing and advertising information to our users.
  • At no time will uComics or uclick, require that a child under 13 years of age, as a condition of participation in an activity or to utilize our products or services, provide more information than is reasonably necessary. This is a requirement of the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). uComics and uclick, strictly abides by this requirement.
Disclosure of Information to Others
  • uComics and uclick, will not sell or rent the personally identifiable information about children under 13 years of age to anyone.
  • uComics and uclick, will not contact children under 13 about products, services or for marketing purposes without the express consent of the child's parent
  • uComics and uclick, will share the personally identifiable information about a child under 13 years of age with other parties, companies and people when:

    - Required by law or required to do so to comply with a subpoena, court order or legal process.
    - Explicit permission has been granted by a child's parent or legal guardian.
Deleting and Updating Information
  • Users under the age of 13 will have the ability to delete or update information that is part of their uComics and uclick, Web site account. In addition, the parents or legal guardians of children under the age of 13 will have the ability review, edit and delete information relating to their child's uComics or uclick, account at any time.
  • Parents and legal guardians can send e-mail or contact uComics or uclick via the information below to stop the collection or use of information about their children by uComics and uclick.
