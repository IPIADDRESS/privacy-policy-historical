Privacy Statement

Ubi Workshop is committed to protecting your privacy. This notice describes our privacy policy. By visiting ubiworkshop, you’re accepting the practices described in this privacy notice.

The personal information we collect includes your name, physical or email address, phone number, credit card details, and other information required to provide you with the product, service, or support requested. Ubi Workshop may share information with its partners or suppliers or service provider in order to process or deliver your order. UbiWorkshop does not sell, trade or rent your personal information to others.

We will use the personal information provided for the following:

  • Process or deliver your order
  • Process or obtain payment or
  • Notify you of the status of your order.
  • Occasionally notify you about special offers we think you’ll find valuable. If you do not wish to receive any news or offers from our part, send an email to ubiworkshop@ubisoft.com and you will be removed from our mailing list.

All transactions are protected using SSL encrypted checkout to protect private data.
