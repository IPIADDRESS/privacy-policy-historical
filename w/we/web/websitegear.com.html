Privacy Policy

Internet User Privacy :

  • WebsiteGear.com shall not make use of your personal details without your permission, except where necessary to supply the services expressly requested by you.
  • WebsiteGear.com does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including membership forms or email addresses.

Information Collected on the Website :

WebsiteGear.com collects information related to user's IP address, referral website, repeat visits (using cookies) and pages viewed for statistical purposes. This data does not contain users personal information such as name, email, etc. and is used to analyze our website logs (such as site hits, page views and referral websites). This helps us to constantly improve the value of the materials available on our website. Our website logs are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.

Information collected by using cookies:

WebsiteGear.com uses cookies to track the user's input to the website (e.g. logging what polls are voted by the user). It comprises a list of those services which the visitor has used. We also use cookies to store a visitor's last visit to track returning visitors.

Option to decline the use of cookies:

Visitors may disable cookies in their web browsers.
  • How to disable cookies (Netscape 3.0 and higher)
    Choose Preferences from the Edit option in the browser menu. Click on Advanced. Deselect the radio button for Accept all cookies. Click the OK button. Your browser is now not set to accept all cookies.
  • How to disable cookies (Internet Explorer 3.0 and higher)
    Choose Internet Options from the Tools option in the browser menu. Click on Advanced. Deselect the radio button for Always accept cookies. Click the OK button. Your browser is now not set to always accept cookies.

Privacy of Other Submitted Information :
