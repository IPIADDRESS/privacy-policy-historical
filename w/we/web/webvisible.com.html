Privacy Policy

Effective September 1, 2005

We at SME Global Solutions, Inc. (“SME Global”, “we” or “us”) respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy lets you know how personally identifiably information that SME Global receives from you through the Web site available at www.webvisible.com (“Site”) is processed, disclosed and used. This Privacy Policy only governs the Site and does not govern other Web sites or the off-line data collection, disclosure and usage practices of SME Global, including information it receives from you other than through this Site.

Types and Reasons for Data Collection

Contact Information

Certain portions of our Site (such as the “Contact Us” page) may allow you to provide us with contact information (including but not limited to your name, phone number, physical address and email address) (“Contact Information”). We use your Contact Information for recordkeeping and to contact you when necessary. We may also send you email messages using your Contact Information to respond to the email questions you submit to us.

Feedback

Certain portions of the Site may permit you to voluntarily provide feedback, comments or input. These portions include bulletin boards, blogs and the like. As part of this input you may be asked to provide your name, state or other personal information along with the content of the input. All of this information may be stored by us and posted on the Site to share the benefit of this information with other users. We may also use it for our other marketing and business activities. However, we may choose not to use the information you submit. Also, we may edit your comments (including deleting sentences or words even if it may somewhat change the nature of your comments) for both format and content. You will not be notified if your information will or will not be used, or if your information is modified.

User Name/Password

In order to access certain portions of our Site, you may need use a user name and password. We will provide you with the initial password, but you are encouraged to change the password. We will use the user names and passwords to verify identities and for our internal use in maintaining your Site account. We also may transfer your name and password information as part of the log-on process to your Company’s computer systems.

Counters

We have counters on certain pages of the Site that record visits to the Site. The counter only records the fact of the visit to the particular page and in connection with such counters, the Site does not store your IP address or other information about you.

Security

We are concerned with the security of the personal information which we have collected and have agreed not to disclose under this Privacy Policy. For such information we have put in place measures to prevent unauthorized access to that information. Security measures include constant updates to our security systems, ensuring optimum protection. We constantly examine all firewalls, load-balancers, SSL accelerators and switches, as well as external developments, for any potential security events. Although we make every effort to use your personal information only in ways that are compatible with this Privacy Policy and to ensure protection of such information, we cannot warrant or guarantee the security of any information transmitted to us.

Exceptions

Except as described above and as described in the Change in Control section below, we will not otherwise use or disclose any of the information described in this Privacy Policy, except to the extent reasonably necessary: (i) to correct technical problems and malfunctions in how we provide our Site to you and to technically process your information; (ii) to protect the security and integrity of the Site, including without limitation to enforce our Terms of Use; (iii) to provide enhanced service to you; (iv) to protect our rights and property and the rights and property of others; (v) to take precautions against liability; (vi) to respond to claims that your information violates the rights or interests of third parties; (vii) to the extent required by law; (viii) to respond to judicial process; and (ix) to the extent permitted under other provisions of law, to provide information to law enforcement agencies and for an investigation on a matter related to public safety, as applicable.

You understand and agree that technical processing of your information is and may be required (a) to send and receive messages in your use of the Site; (b) to conform to the technical requirements of connecting networks; (c) to conform to the limitations of our Site; and (d) to conform to other, similar technical requirements.

Kids

If you are a child under the age of 13, please do not use the Site and do not submit information to the Site. We have not designed the Site for use by children and our personal information practices described in this Privacy Policy are not designed for children.

General Information

Third Party Site Hosts/Operators

Other companies besides SME Global may operate or host the Site or the computers that operate the Site on behalf of SME Global; if you would like more information about those companies, you can contact us using the contact information provided below.

Users outside the United States

SME Global makes the Site available for consumers located exclusively in the United States of America. Please do not use the Site if you are located outside the United States, as the Site’s personal information practices and the terms of this Privacy Policy may or may not comply with foreign laws.

Links to Other Web sites

This Site contains links to other Web sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of other Web sites.

Change in Control

If we experience a “change in control” (defined below), we may amend our information practices as described in this Privacy Policy. We will post on our Site’s home page a notice or a link to a notice which will describe how we will amend our Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to our amended Privacy Policy, you may contact us as described in this Privacy Policy and we will allow you to opt out of the proposed amendments to our Privacy Policy with respect to the information that we have previously collected from you.

“Change in control” means any of the following events:

  • we undergo a reorganization, merger, consolidation, acquisition or other restructuring involving all or substantially all of our voting securities and/or assets, by operation of law or otherwise;
  • we become insolvent;
  • we make a general assignment for the benefit of creditors;
  • we suffer or permit an appointment of a receiver for our business or assets;
  • we become subject to any proceedings under any bankruptcy or insolvency law, whether domestic or foreign; or
  • we are liquidated, voluntarily or otherwise.

Notification of Changes

If we decide to change our Privacy Policy, we will post notice of those changes on our Site’s home page so our users are always aware of what information we collect, use and disclose. Please regularly check the Site for information about revisions to this Privacy Policy. In the event we change our Privacy Policy, such changes will affect all of the information we have previously collected from you, as well as the information we collect after any such change. If you object to the change to our Privacy Policy, then you must contact us in writing regarding your objection.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, the practices of this Site, or your dealings with this Site, you can contact us at webmaster@webvisible.com
