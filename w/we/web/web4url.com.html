Web4URL Privacy Policy


Web4URL.com is committed to safeguarding your privacy online. We take this commitment very seriously. Web4URL wants you to understand how we use the information you provide to us. We believe that you should have the right to approve the use of that information. As technology changes, it may become necessary for Web4URL to make changes to its privacy policy and procedures. All such changes will be posted to this site. To protect your interests, visit this page periodically to review the current terms of our policy.

How We Gather Information

Web4URL.com appreciates and respects the privacy concerns of the visitors to its web site. Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information, name and email. The customer's contact information is used to get in touch with the customer when necessary. This information will not be displayed on our web site. As a general policy, no personal information is automatically collected from customers visiting this web site. However, certain information is recorded as a matter of course by Web4URL.com servers in order to track the number of visitors to the web site and to help provide an enhanced online experience for the visitor. The type of information tracked by our servers includes your computer browser information, operating system and your IP address. Collecting this information allows Web4URL.com to optimize the visitor’s web site experience.

In addition, we use data collection devices such as “cookies”. “Cookies” are small text files a web site can use to recognize repeat users, facilitate the user’s ongoing access to and use of the site and allow a site to track usage behavior and compile aggregate data that will allow content improvements and targeted advertising. Cookies are not programs that come onto a user’s system and damage files. Generally, cookies work by assigning a unique number to the user that has no meaning outside the assigning site. If a user does not want information collected through the use of cookies, there is a simple procedure in most browsers that allows the user to deny or accept the cookie feature; however, users should note that cookies may be necessary to provide the user with certain features (e.g., customized delivery of information) available on the Web4URL's site.

Web4URL.com uses personal information for internal purposes only .If you subscribe to the monthly newsletter, Web4URL uses your email address to send you news about or updates to Web4URL's site, and to respond to customer service issues or comments and questions received from you. Upon request, Web4URL will allow any user to “opt out” of further promotional contacts at any time. Web4URL will provide only aggregated data from these analyses to third parties.

Web4URL reserves the rights to monitor all outgoing e-mails sent from our web site for quality control purposes. All information recieved will be kept strictly confidential.
