When you wish to share a picture with your friend and click on the share button, the picture is sent to our server where it is secured. The picture is only accessible to those who you choose to send it to. You can also delete the image from our server. We do everything to insure your privacy and keep your images private. Of course you must protect your connection and insure that it is secure and that others cannot view it.

When you install the extension you receive a code which is totally random. Every picture that you send is the extension include this code. This number is for solving problems that may arise with the users, and to allow the user to view or erase previous pictures.

When the extension is installed a folder is created in your Google Chrome profile. We do not save any other files or data on your computer except on this folder. When you uninstall the extension this folder is erased.

When you take a screenshot we do not save the picture file, you must do so yourself by choosing "save" or share.
