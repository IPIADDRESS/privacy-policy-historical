The Website In A Weekend Privacy Policy

Website In A Weekend values your privacy very highly. Be assured that we will do everything necessary to maintain your privacy. You deserve our assurance.

What information do you collect and how do you collect it?

Website In A Weekend only contacts individuals, for reasons other than appointment scheduling, who specifically request that we do so or in the event that they have signed up to receive any free newsletters or have purchased one of our products. Website In A Weekend may collect personally-identifying information from our users during online registration and online purchasing. Generally, this information includes your name and e-mail address which are required for registration to receive our free newsletters, and your name, postal address, and credit card information when purchasing one of our products. All of this information is provided to us by you.

How do we use this information?

This information is used for internal marketing purposes only. Website In A Weekend promises to never sell, exchange, or release your personal information. Your information is not shared with any other third party or company outside of what is necessary for billing and shipping purposes.

With respect to cookies…

As does nearly all modern web server technology, our server use may use cookies to record session information, such as items that consumers add to their shopping cart, customize Web page content based on visitors’ browser type or other information that the visitor sends. In addition, cookies are occasionally set by the web server to provide more efficient server response. Website In A Weekend does not use information provided by customer and stored in cookies for any reason.

What information do you maintain?

In short, very little. However, upon request we provide site visitors with access to all information [including proprietary information] that we do maintain about them. Customers are welcome to request this information by e-mail, or by asking in person on their next visit. Please use the contact form when contacting by email.
