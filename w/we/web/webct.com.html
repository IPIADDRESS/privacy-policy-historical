Privacy Statement for WebCT.com

WebCT.com has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate its firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: www.webct.com.

We do not sell our customer names, addresses, or other information to third parties for any purpose.

We may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address may also be used to customize the information that you receive to make it most relevant to you.

Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information (e.g., names and email addresses). Your contact information from the registration form may be used to confirm your registration, deliver newsletters that you request, and occasionally send you information about our company and site. You may choose to opt out of future mailings. Directions on how to opt out will be provided in any email correspondence you receive from us.

This site contains links to other sites. WebCT.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

WebCT.com provides an order form for customers to request information, products, and services. Information that is supplied to WebCT.com through these forms will be used in the following ways: Customer contact information may be used to get in touch with the visitor when necessary; In addition, contact information may be used to deliver orders to customers. Financial information (like a credit card number) that you provide is used to bill for products and services through our partner Cybersource Corporation. Cybersource only uses your order information to process purchase transactions that you request, and retains a record of such transactions. Information provided to Cybersource will not be used for any other purposes.

WebCT does use cookies to provide convenient automatic login to WebCT.com.  Using cookies, your browser can "remember" your user name and password so that you’re logged in and ready to participate in the community whenever you’re at the Web site.  If you check the "Remember this password" box when prompted by the browser, and you won't have to sign in each time you come back. If you are concerned that other people might accidentally use your account, do not check the box.  Also, if you use a shared computer (in a library, Internet cafe, university, airport or other common area) DO NOT check the checkbox.  To ensure that WebCT.com asks for a userID and password the next time you visit WebCT.com, be sure to close your browser or click "LogOut" when you leave your computer.  You can always sign off at any time by clicking the "LogOut" link at the top of each page. Once you've logged out, you can logon again and choose whether your browser "remembers" or not.

Your profile information may be used to create personalized content, services and marketing on WebCT.com so that we can deliver information to you based on your interests and location. WebCT.com discloses aggregated user statistics (for example, "75% of our users are students") in order to describe our services to prospective partners and other third parties, and for other lawful purposes.

Public Forums

This site makes chat rooms, forums, and message boards available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and that you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. WebCT reserves the right to remove any posting that its editors consider inappropriate.

Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information stored therein. Order information is transmitted through Secure Socket Layer technology throughout the entire purchase process to protect you and allow you to buy with confidence. Credit card information is not stored permanently on this site.
