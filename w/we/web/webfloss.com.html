What about Cookies?



Cookies are also subject to a number of misconceptions, mostly based on the erroneous notion that they are computer programs that run on the browsing computer. In fact, cookies are simple pieces of data that affect the operation of a web server, not the client, and do so in very specific ways. In particular, they are neither spyware nor viruses. Webfloss.com ad revenue is tracked at ad load and when the ad is clicked. The function of these cookies is solely for the purpose of paying commissions when a consumer creates a lead or makes a purchase.



You can choose whether to accept cookies by changing the settings of your browser.
