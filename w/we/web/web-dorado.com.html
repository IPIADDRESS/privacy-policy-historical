Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Cookies and similar technologies (e.g. Local Storage) allow us to authenticate you, to track your browsing behavior, links clicked, items purchased, your device type, about how you use and interact with us and/or to run our Affiliate Program. This allows us to provide you with more relevant product offerings and advertisement, a better experience with our products and services, and to collect, analyze and improve our performance. We may also collect IP address to enhance security. Note that cookies may be placed by Third Party Services that we use (e.g. Google Analytics). CAN SPAM Act

The CAN-SPAM Act is a law that sets the rules for commercial email, establishes requirements for commercial messages, gives recipients the right to have emails stopped from being sent to them.
