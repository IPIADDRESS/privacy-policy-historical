Privacy Statement

This statement relates to the privacy practices of Web Reservations International (“WRI”) in connection with this website. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of other websites.

General Statement

WRI fully respects your right to privacy, and will not collect any personal information about you on this website without your clear knowledge and permission. Any personal information which you volunteer to WRI will be treated strictly in accordance with the Data Protection Acts 1988 and 2003.

Collection and use of personal information

We will not collect any personally identifying information about you when you visit our website unless you choose to provide that information to us. The information we collect depends on what you do when visiting our website.

If you visit our site to browse, read or download we automatically collect and store information about your use of the site through certain cookies that are set. Please refer to our Cookies Policy, which is available here for further information.

If you click on the “Sign Up” or “Learn More” links in the “Work with us section” you will be directed to another WRI group company website. Any personal data you provide to us through the external site will be used solely for the purposes of dealing with your application to sign up to a WRI service. Any further processing of your personal data provided during the application process will be in accordance with the terms and conditions that govern the particular service you have applied for.

If you apply for a job through our LinkedIn profile we will use any personal data you provide solely for the purposes of dealing with your application.

You may choose to provide us with personal data (e.g. name, e-mail and postal address) in an e-mail message to the address listed on our site. We use these details solely for the purpose of dealing with your query.

Right of Access

You have the right, subject to certain exemptions, to obtain a copy of any personal data we hold about you and to correct any inaccuracies in such data. If you wish to avail of any of these rights, please contact us at: customerservice@hostelworld.com Your request will be dealt with as soon as possible and will not take more than 40 days to process.
