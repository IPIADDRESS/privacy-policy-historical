Privacy

Information collected

When you look at the Webindia website we may make a record of your visit and store the following information for our own statistical purposes:

  • Your server URL and address
  • The pages you accessed and documents downloaded
  • The previous site you have visited
  • The webpage or search engine which referred you to us (if applicable)
  • The type of browser and platform you are using
Access to information collected

Webindia does not make an attempt to identify users or their browsing pattern. However, in the unlikely event of an investigation, a law enforcement agency or other government agency may exercise its legal authority to inspect our log files which we will have to comply.

Use and disclosure of information collected

Our Office will only record your e-mail address if you send us a message or submit an on-line enquiry form from our website. Your e-mail address will only be used or disclosed for the purpose for which you have provided it and it will not be added to a mailing list or used or disclosed for any other purpose without your consent.

Users are advised that there are inherent risks transmitting any form of information across the Internet.

Cookies

This website does use cookies. No cookies are employed on this website except for those associated with search engines. The website statistics for this site are generated from our own web logs.
