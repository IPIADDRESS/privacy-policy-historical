    Privacy Statement

    Webmaster Serve Privacy Statement

    Information Gathered
    As commonly done by all websites, web log files which collect information about IP address of visitors to our site, browser type, referring page, country visitor come from and the duration of visit. This information are collected mainly for security reasons and to know more about our visitors. Log files are deleted every 7 days.
    For users who register to use our forum, additional information such as name email address are also collected. User account information are kept for as long as the user keep using Webmaster Serve.


    How Your Information Is Used
    Information about registered users are used to enhance user experience, such user experience includes personalisation to ensure you get the most out of you visit to Webmaster Serve. The information you gave us including your email address i NEVER sold, rented or traded nor do we lease out your information.

    Third Parties
    This privacy policy only applies to data collected by Webmaster Serve. We sometimes use 3rd party services such as Facebook Login, Twitter, Google,Bing. You may need to read such 3rd parties privacy policy if you have concerns about their privacy policy.
