WebHostingBreak.com uses collected information for the following general purposes: products and services provision, billing, identification and authentication, services improvement, contact, and research.

COOKIES

A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site’s computers and stored on your computer’s hard drive.

Cookies are required to use the this service.

We use cookies to record current session information, but do not use permanent cookies. You are required to log-in to your this service Project Site after a certain period of time has elapsed to protect you against others accidentally accessing your account contents.
