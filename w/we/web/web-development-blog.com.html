Website visits

finalwebsites.com uses functional, analytic and affiliate cookies. A cookie is a small text file that will be stored in the browser of your computer, tablet or smartphone after the first visit to our website. finalwebsites.com cookies are used with a purely technical functionality. These ensure that the website is working properly, for example, it can remember your preferences and help us to make the website work properly. We can use these cookies and data to optimize our website. We place cookies to track your surfing habits so that we can offer customized content and a better website experience.

Affiliate cookies

We use on our site different affiliate links. If you follow one of these links, a cookie will be placed while visiting the partner's website. These cookies are used to track a possible commission for sales initiated by our visitors. The cookies value never contains your personal information.

How to deny cookies

You can opt out of this through your web browser settings and adjust it to no longer store cookies. Alternatively, you can delete information stored previously using these same browser settings.

Google Analytics web browser opt-out

To provide website visitors more choice on how their data is collected, Google has developed the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on. Learn more about the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on here.
