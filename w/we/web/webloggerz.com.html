Tracking:

When you visit this website, we can record certain information in relation to your visit such as:

  • Your IP or proxy server IP
  • Basic domain information
  • Your Internet service provider is sometimes captured depending on the configuration of your ISP connection
  • The date and time of your visit to the website
  • The length of your session
  • The pages which you have accessed
  • The number of times you access our site within any month
  • The size of the file you look at
  • The website which referred you to our website
  • The operating system which your computer uses.

This is done by making use of analytical softwares such as Google Analytics, Bing Webmasters primarily for the data analysis and web development process and we do not share this with any third-party.

Cookies:

We do make use of session cookies, which are used only during a browsing session, and expire as soon you quit your browser.
If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookies can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.
