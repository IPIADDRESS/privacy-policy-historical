Privacy Policy

Weatherzone is owned and maintained by The Weather Company Pty. Ltd. The site's primary purpose is to provide an access point for timely, accurate and indepth weather information for weather enthusiasts. Membership of the site is required to access the components of the site that are not considered public domain. By using the Weatherzone website you are agreeing to comply with The Weather Company's terms of use.

The Weather Company is committed to honouring the privacy of Weatherzone members and visitors and will only use information gained to help tailor presentations, facilitate your movement through the web site, provide personalised services and to communicate with members and visitors. Usage statistics of Weatherzone members will be used to create new weather products that will benefit both Weatherzone users and clients of The Weather Company.

Every member or visitor has the choice whether to disclose or not disclose the information requested by the The Weather Company. However certain information is required for registration as a member.

All the material on this site is copyright and cannot be copied or reproduced without our written permission.

The profile information you have provided to us is for use in developing interesting content for our internet products and names and individual email addresses will not be divulged to any third party without your express permission.

We regularly email our user group to keep them updated on new material and improvements on the site. We also recommend products or services that may be of interest to our user group by email. If you do not wish to receive emails from us, you have the option to downgrade your account to guest.

We may use location information (postcode) stored in a persistent cookie to serve more relevant advertisements to members and guests.

We endeavor to utilise the most modern technology to prevent unauthorised access to the site and have a policy of continual review of such safeguards.

Should you have any questions relating to this site, or the privacy statement, please contact us.

Thank you for visiting Weatherzone.
