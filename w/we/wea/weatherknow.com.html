Privacy

This page describes our privacy practices with respect to DX Weather Weather.

Information Collection and Use Practices

  • DX Weather. Weather uses your location, in accordance with our Location Privacy practices, to serve local weather information, photos and other information to you.
  • Mobile phone identifiers, model information, and operating systems may be collected for purposes of internal analytics, or to provide the service with the best possible experience.
  • If you are a registered user and logged in, locations that you have “Saved” within DX Weather Weather will be associated with your DX Weather account.
  • If you are a registered user and logged in, DX Weather may use some of your “Favorite Locations” from the Location Settings page to personalize your DX Weather Weather experience.
  • DX Weather Weather may offer you the opportunity to log in using Facebook. DX Weather uses this sign-in information to identify the user and may associate that information to an existing DX Weather profile to allow a single-sign on experience. Once signed in, DX Weather may configure your weather cities based on the location of your social graph. DX Weather may store your Facebook information on our servers.

Practices Regarding Your Ability to Update and Delete Information

  • You can access and manage your locations through the “Edit” link in the web-based version or the “Edit Locations” link in the mobile app version of DX Weather Weather.
  • You can also visit the Location Settings page to view, add, and delete your location information.
  • For some versions of DX Weather Weather, you may share a screenshot of the weather using Mail, Messages, Facebook or Twitter. The screenshot is retained in your device photo library.

Other

For additional information on how how Forecasts Weather treats your personal information, please visit our Privacy Policy and DX Weather Terms of Service.
