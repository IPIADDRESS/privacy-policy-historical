WeTransfer.com is a premium service-providing company in the area of digital file transferring. The Service allows users to send files (up to 2 GB) to one or more recipients identified by a valid email address.

WeTransfer is based in Amsterdam, The Netherlands at the Nieuwe Prinsengracht 33 (1018 EG) and is listed at the Chamber of Commerce under number 34380998. WeTransfer.com also has offices in San Francisco. United States of America, in the Flood Building at 870 Market Street, CA 94102.

When you visit the Site and/or use the Service, our servers automatically record information about your use of the Service and your visit to the Site (“Log Data”). This Log Data includes the filename and file size of all files that you transfer by means of the Service. The Log Data may also include information such as your computer’s Internet Protocol (IP) address, the sender email address and receiver(s) email address, the browser type, what software you are using, the web page you were visiting immediately prior to visiting our Site, pages of our Site that you visited, the time spent on those pages, information you searched for on our Site, access times and dates, other statistics and “cookies”.

Account data

If you have downloaded the Desktop Application from the Site, you must create an Account in order to use the Service. When you create such an Account, you are asked to fill out certain information about yourself. You can always adjust the information you have provided.

Corporate Information

If you have entered into a Corporate Subscription with WeTransfer.com you must create an Account, in order to use the Service. When you create such an Account, you are asked provide certain Personal Information. You can always adjust the information you have provided.

  1. 3. What are Cookies and how is WeTransfer.com using them?

Like many websites, WeTransfer uses “cookies” to collect information. A cookie is a small data file that we transfer to your computer’s hard disk for record-keeping purposes. WeTransfer uses cookies for two purposes. First, we utilize persistent cookies to save your registration ID and login password for future logins to the Site. Second, we utilize session cookies to enable certain features of the Site, to better understand how you interact with the Site and to monitor aggregate usage by users of the Service and general web traffic on the Site. We link the information we store in cookies to the Personal Information you submit to us while you are on the Site. Unlike persistent cookies, session cookies are deleted from your computer when you close your browser.

Third party advertisers on the Site may also place or read cookies on your browser. WeTransfer does not have access to or control of any cookies that its advertisers may store in your browser and this Privacy Policy does not apply to the use of cookies by advertisers.

You can instruct your browser, by changing its options, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the web sites you visit. If you do not accept cookies, however, you may not be able to use all portions of the Site or all the functionality of the Service. In order to know more about the possibility to stop accepting cookies we advise you to consult your browser instruction or the “Help” function in your browser.
