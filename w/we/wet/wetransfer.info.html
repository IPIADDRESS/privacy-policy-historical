Log data

When an user visits the Site or use the Service, our servers automatically record information about the use of the Service and the visit to the Site (“Log Data”). This Log Data includes the filename and file size of all files that you make available via the Service. The Log Data may also include information such as your computer’s Internet Protocol (IP) address, the sender email address and receiver(s) email address, the browser type, what Software you were using, the web page you were visiting immediately prior to visiting our Site, pages of our Site that you visited, the time spent on those pages, information you searched for on our Site, access times and dates, and other statistics. WeTransfer uses this information to monitor and analyze use of the Site and the Service and for the Site’s technical administration, to improve our Site’s functionality and user-friendliness, and to better tailor it to our visitors’ needs. WeTransfer also uses this information to verify that users of the Service meet the criteria required to process their requests.

Cookies

Like many websites, WeTransfer uses “cookies” to collect information. A cookie is a small data file that we transfer to your computer’s hard disk for record-keeping purposes. WeTransfer uses cookies for two purposes. First, we utilize persistent cookies to save your registration ID and login password for future logins to the Site. Second, we utilize session cookies to enable certain features of the Site, to better understand how you interact with the Site and to monitor aggregate usage by WeTransfer Users and general web traffic on the Site. We link the information we store in cookies to the Personal Information you submit to us while you are on the Site. Unlike persistent cookies, session cookies are deleted from your computer when you close your browser. Third party advertisers on the Site may also place or read cookies on your browser. WeTransfer does not have access to or control of any cookies that its advertisers may store in your browser and this Privacy Policy does not apply to the use of cookies by advertisers. You can instruct your browser, by changing its options, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the web sites you visit. If you do not accept cookies, however, you may not be able to use all portions of the Site or all the functionality of the Service.
As part of the Service, WeTransfer subscribers may send a file to one or more recipients identified by a valid email address. The message created by a WeTransfer subscriber will be sent from WeTransfer on the WeTransfer subscriber’s behalf to the email addresses the WeTransfer subscriber enters into the online form. These email addresses will be stored in the address book the WeTransfer subscriber creates and will be used only for the purpose of sending the email communication to the addressee. The recipient of a file may contact us at info@WeTransfer.com to request that we remove this information from our database.
