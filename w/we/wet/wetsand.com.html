Wetsand.com collects information about your transactions with us and with some of our business partners.

Wetsand.com automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, Wetsand.com cookie information, and the pages of videos you request.

Wetsand.com uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.

We provide the information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or with Wetsand.com under confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your personal information to help Wetsand.com communicate with you about offers from Wetsand.com and our marketing partners. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information.
