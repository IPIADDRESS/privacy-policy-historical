No Information from Children (under age 18). The Site is not directed to individuals under 18 years of age, who are not eligible to use our Service. If we learn that we have collected personal information from an individual under age 18, we will take steps to remove that information as quickly as possible. If you believe that we might have received any information from an individual under age 18, please contact us at support@wellnessfx.com.

Questions. If you have questions or concerns about our Privacy Statement, please contact us by sending an email to support@wellnessfx.com.

The Information we collect when you interact with the Site may be Personal Information (e.g., if you are logged in).
