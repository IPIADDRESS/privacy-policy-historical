Web Site Privacy Policy

 

Wescom Credit Union values the privacy of our members. All information collected through our Web site, including e-mail addresses, is confidential and is for Credit Union use only. Information collected on our Web site will not be sold or transferred to other parties.

We do not collect personal identifying information about visitors to our site. Individual visitors to Wescom Credit Union's web site remain anonymous. Our servers collect standard non-identifying information about visits to the site, such as date and time visited, IP address, city, state, and country. This information is used to compile standard statistics on site use.

If we obtain your e-mail address, Wescom may, with your consent, send updates and special offers via e-mail from time to time. We will remove your name from our e-mail list upon request.
