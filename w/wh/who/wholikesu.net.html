This Privacy Policy is aimed at protecting the Privacy of our users and demonstrates that we respect the information you put on our website. Your profile is available for view on our Site, by both registered users and anonymous visitors who view our Site. Description, photos, age, city (country, Not your postal address) will be shown on your profile page for other users.Your email address is confidential and will not be posted by us in your profile and newsletters or shared with third parties.We don`t include email address to your visible profile information. Refrain from putting your personal information on your visible profile. We cannot control who reads your posting or how other users can use that information. For example, if you post your email address, you may receive unsolicited messages.

"Cookies" are small files placed in memory and/or disk on your computer that assist us in providing customized services and better functionality as you move from page to page within our Site. Cookies make the personalization of your Internet experiences possible. For example, when you surf from page to page on our website, cookies help us to remember your login and password so that you don`t need to log-in each time you click on someone`s page. For the proper functioning of our Services we need to know who you are and what actions have you taken in course of session.
