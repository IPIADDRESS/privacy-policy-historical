Who Deleted Me Privacy Policy

Who Deleted Me is run by The Media Dudes. You can contact us by clicking here .

What information do we collect?

We store the IP address of every person who visits our website/application for statistical purposes.

When a snapshot is made, the following information is stored: Your Facebook user ID, the Facebook user ID of all of your friends and the first and last name of all of your friends.

We use the Demographics & Interests feature of Google Analytics to anonymously collect users Age, Gender and/or Interests.

How is your information used?

We only use the stored information for the purposes of comparing your current friends list with the stored friends list and to improve our service. We do not share any stored information about you. However we may release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others rights, property, or safety.

Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

We are in compliance with the requirements of COPPA (Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act), we do not collect any information from anyone under 13 years of age. Our website, products and services are all directed to people who are at least 13 years old or older.

Your Consent

By using our application / website you consent to our privacy policy.
