Privacy Policy

WholeMom.com is committed to protecting your privacy on the Internet. We never share your personal information with other companies without your prior permission.

Information We Collect

We collect information from you when you register on our website. When you register we collect name and email although you can also visit our site anonomously. To participate in the forums you must provide a valid working email address. Others users cannot see this address. We will never give or sell your email address to any third party; nor will we reveal your address to other users.We may, from time to time, email our members about exciting new website features or special promotional offers. Upon receiving such an email, you will be given the opportunity to permanently and immediately opt-out of such mailings.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect from you may be used in the following ways:

  • To improve you experience with us.
  • To personalise your experience with us.
  • To send you emails about exciting new website features or special promotional offers. You will always be given the opportunity to immediately and permanently unsubscribe from these mailings.

Other Websites We Link To

We may include links to third party websites on our website. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites and do not necessarily endorse such websites.

How We Use Cookies

We may set and access WholeMom.com cookies on your computer. These are used to automatically differentiate you from other users of our website. We sometimes let other companies that show advertisements on some of our pages set and access their cookies on your computer. Other companies’ use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not our privacy policy. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to our cookies.

Security & Confidentiality

We will not release any information about you, including your email address, to any third party other than the exceptions noted above. We have physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal regulations to protect personal information about you.

Protection of Children

We comply with the requirements of COPPA (Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act), we do not collect any information from anyone under 13 years of age. Our website, products and services are all directed to people who are at least 13 years old or older.

California Online Privacy Protection Act

Because we value your privacy we have taken the necessary precautions to comply with the California Online Privacy Protection Act. We therefore will not distribute your personal information to outside parties without your prior consent.

Consent

When you use our website you consent to our privacy policy.

How to Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy please contact us at: privacy@WholeMom.com.
