The information we collect helps us ensure that our services are best designed to meet your needs. We receive and store any information that you enter on any registration page. They may also receive mailings from other reputable companies.

B. Other Information

Profile data also is collected at our site. Depending on the specific Whole Mom products for which a user (“User”) registers, various information will be requested. We use this data to tailor the visitor’s experience through our interest-focused e-mails. By obtaining this information, we can provide our Users with specific content that we think they might be interested in, according to their preferences and interests. The User may include only the information he or she feels comfortable including. However, the omission of certain information may limit the value of the experience for the User.
