Privacy Policy

WhenToWork.com takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.

What this Privacy Policy Covers

  • This Privacy Policy covers WhenToWork.com's treatment of information entered into WhenToWork.com when you using the WhenToWork.com site, and when you use WhenToWork.com's services. 
Information Collection and Use
  • WhenToWork.com requests information when you register for a WhenToWork.com account
  • When you register with WhenToWork.com, we ask for certain information. Once you register with WhenToWork.com and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

  • WhenToWork.com will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone.
Cookies
  • WhenToWork.com may create and access cookies on your computer, the information stored on your computer will typically contain only an "id number" which helps us remember who you are.  No personally identifiable information is ever stored in the cookie.
Your Ability to Edit and Delete Your Account Information and Preferences
  • WhenToWork.com gives you the ability to edit your WhenToWork.com Account Information and preferences at any time.
  • You may request deletion of your WhenToWork.com account by sending an email to us. 
Security
  • Your WhenToWork.com Account Information is password-protected for your privacy and security.
  • In certain areas, WhenToWork.com uses industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect data transmissions.
Changes to this Privacy Policy
  • WhenToWork.com may amend this policy from time to time. If we make any substantial changes in the way we use your personal information, we will notify you by posting a prominent announcement on our pages.
