The Information We Collect and How We Collect It

How we collect and store information depends on the Whiskey Riff Services you use and the devices you use to access the Whiskey Riff Services. You can use some of the Whiskey Riff Services without providing any information other than that automatically collected as described below.

We may collect information about you during your use of the Whiskey Riff Services, including your name, username, password, email address, and information you provide or post on the Whiskey Riff Services or allow us to access when you do certain things, such as:

  • Enter registration information for the Whiskey Riff Services;
  • Enter a sweepstakes or contest or register for a promotion;
  • Participate in voting or polling activities;
  • Request certain features (e.g., newsletters);
  • Sign up to receive SMS (text message) notifications;
  • Download or use one of our mobile applications;
  • Connect with the Whiskey Riff Services or otherwise allow us to access certain information about you via Facebook and other social networking services (collectively, “social networking services”); or
  • Post content to or on the Whiskey Riff Services.

We may collect and store your user ID associated with the social networking site account that you use to sign in to your Whiskey Riff account or connect or use with the Whiskey Riff Services. When you sign in to your Whiskey Riff account or mobile application with your social networking site account information or otherwise connect to your social networking site account with the Whiskey Riff Services, you authorize Whiskey Riff to collect, store, and use in accordance with this Privacy Policy any and all information available to Whiskey Riff through the social networking site interface, including any information that you have made public in connection with your social networking site account, information that the social networking site shares with us, or information that is disclosed as being shared during the sign-in process.

We may also combine information that we collect from you with information we obtain about you from third parties and information derived from any other service we provide.

Please be careful whenever you are online. Should you choose to voluntarily disclose information through the Whiskey Riff Services, such as via social networking sites or in comments you post, that information can be viewed publicly and can be collected and used by third parties without our knowledge.

Cookies and Other Electronic Technologies

When you use the Whiskey Riff Services, we use persistent and session cookies and other tracking technologies to: (a) store your username and password; (b) analyze the usage of the Whiskey Riff Services; (c) customize the Whiskey Riff Services to your preferences; and (d) control the advertising displayed by the Whiskey Riff Services. We also may include Web beacons in e-mail messages or newsletters to determine whether the message has been opened. As we adopt additional technology, we may also gather additional information through other methods.

We use these automated technologies to collect and analyze certain types of information we consider to be non-personal information, including: (a) information related to the devices you use to access or interact with our services, such as: IP addresses, geolocation information (as described further below), unique device identifiers and other information about your mobile phone or other mobile device(s), browser types, browser language; and (b) information related to the ways in which you interact with the Whiskey Riff Services, such as: referring and exit pages and URLs, platform type, the number of clicks, domain names, landing pages, pages viewed and the order of those pages, the amount of time spent on particular pages, the date and time you used the Whiskey Riff Services, and other similar information. We may also capture other data, such as search criteria and results. In some cases, we will associate this information with a unique Whiskey Riff identifier (that cannot be used by it to identify you) for our internal use, in which case your Whiskey Riff identifier will be considered Whiskey Riff information and nothing in this Privacy Policy restricts how Whiskey Riff may use this information.

Most browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser setting to decline cookies by visiting the Help portion of your browser’s toolbar. If you choose to decline cookies, please note that you may not be able to sign in, customize, or use some of the interactive features of the Whiskey Riff Services. Flash cookies operate differently than browser cookies, and cookie management tools available in a web browser will not remove flash cookies. To learn more about how to manage flash cookies, you can visit the Adobe website and make changes at the Global Privacy Settings Panel.
