Privacy Policy

Tweet
Details
Read: 1417 times


WhaTech is committed to preserving your privacy and fully recognises the need for appropriate protection and management of any personally identifiable information you share with us (any information by which you can be identified, such as name, address, e-mail address or telephone number).


There are a few pages where you may choose to register for an event or to fill out an registration form / questionnaire. The information you fill out on those forms can help you register for an event, post an event, receive contact or request information. Your information is not shared outside of WhaTech unless you are making an enquiry directly into a product such as a whitepaper download which may be used to contact you at a later date or subscribe you to a product specific mailing list. If you have subscribed to one of our services we will always give you an opt-out on any communication we have with you should you not wish to be contacted in the future.


We may also ask you to provide information such as, your interests, demographics and experience with certain products and services, to help determine what is most beneficial for our readers. Under no circumstances will WhaTech provide this information to other organisations or third parties. It is our intention to retain any personal information gathered from customers to be internally used by WhaTech only.


The WhaTech web site may contain links to other websites. Whilst WhaTech makes a conscious effort to link only to sites that share respect for privacy, WhaTech cannot take responsibility for the content or privacy practices adopted by such sites.


Information collected
When you look at this web site, our Internet Service Provider may make a record of your visit and log the following information for statistical purposes:
your server address
your domain name (for example .com, .gov, .com.au, .co.uk etc)
the date and time of your visit to the site
the pages you accessed and documents downloaded
the previous site you have visited
the type of browser you are using


Access to information collected
WhaTech will not make an attempt to identify users or their browsing activities. However, in the unlikely event of an investigation, a law enforcement agency or other government agency may exercise its legal authority to inspect our Internet Service Provider's logs.
Use of information collected


We will only record your e-mail address if you send us a message. Your e-mail address will only be used for the purpose for which you have provided it and it will not be added to a mailing list or used for any other purpose without your consent.
