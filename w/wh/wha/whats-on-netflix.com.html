Privacy Policy

What’s on Netflix respects your right to privacy and will make sure any information provided by you and about you remains private and confidential in compliance with the Data Protection Act.

Information tracked may include Internet addresses and pages visited. Personal information, such as names and e-mail addresses, are not stored in this process and hence your privacy is safeguarded.
