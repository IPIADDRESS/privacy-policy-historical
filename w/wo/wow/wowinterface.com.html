Privacy Statement

WoWInterface/WoWGui values your privacy. To keep you informed, we have created this privacy statement that discloses our purposes and methods for online collection and use of information. This statement applies to all information collected through the WoWInterface/WoWGui.com website and information gathered from any web pages that we host.

Disclosure of Personal Information
We require all members to register before you may post to the forums. A valid E-Mail address is required to register. All Registration Information is kept private. At no time will any information gathered be sold, distributed, or relayed to another company without your permission. At any time, you have the ability to keep your e-mail address hidden from other users by selecting that option within your user profile.

It’s also important to be aware that when you voluntarily provide personal information (e.g., address, phone number) in a posting on the bulletin board, that information is publicly available and may be used by someone in a manner unintended by you. For that reason, we encourage using caution before offering of any personally identifying information within the message board itself.

If you abuse or are suspected of abusing our sites please see "Abuse Actions".

Abuse Actions
Anyone found to be abusing our system, Blizzard, or any other affiliate systems will be banned, and their info reported to the appropriate agencies for investigation. In severe cases, such as any form of virus being uploaded, your information will be given to the FBI Cyber Crime Division. We value the protection of our members, their privacy and fair game play.

Cookies
Certain activities on the WoWInterface/WoWGui website involve the use of "cookies" to facilitate your experience. This includes retention of your username and password for the WoWInterface/WoWGui forums. If you do not normally have cookies enabled please see the FAQs for information on how to setup a cookie for only our sites.

Change of Personal Information
You can update or change your user profile by clicking on the "USER CP" option on the top of our pages.

Contact Us
If you have any privacy questions please email privacy@WoWInterface/WoWGui.com.



We reserve the right to change our statement at any time.
