Privacy Policy

WorldGilt takes the privacy of its clients very seriously. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used.We do use Google Analytics as a means to improve our service to our clients.

Google Analytics (General)

We may collect information about your computer, including your IP address, operating system and browser type, for system administration and in order to create reports. This is statistical data about our users’ browsing actions and patterns.

The cookies in use on our site are for Google Analytics. Google Analytics is a web analytics tool that helps website owners understand how visitors engage with their website. Google Analytics customers can view a variety of reports about how visitors interact with their website so that they can improve it.

Like many services, Google Analytics uses first-party cookies to track visitor interactions as in our case, where they are used to collect information about how visitors use our site. We then use the information to compile reports and to help us improve our site.

Cookies contain information that is transferred to your computer’s hard drive. These cookies are used to store information, such as the time that the current visit occurred, whether the visitor has been to the site before, and what site referred the visitor to the web page.

Google Analytics (Demographics and Interest Reporting)

This website has also enabled the Google Analytics Demographics and Interest Reporting, which enables Google Analytics to collect data about our traffic via Google advertising cookies and anonymous identifiers, in addition to data collected through a standard Google Analytics implementation.

Google Analytics collects information anonymously. It reports website trends without identifying individual visitors. You can opt out of Google Analytics without affecting how you visit our site – for more information on opting out of being tracked by Google Analytics across all websites you use, visit the relevant Google Analytics web pages.

Information Usage

We may use personally identifiable information collected through our website to contact clients regarding products and services offered by our Group Companies and otherwise to enhance a clients’ experience. We may also use information collected through our website for research regarding the effectiveness of the website and the marketing, advertising and sales efforts of our business.

Information Disclosure

We do not collate information about our clients for on-sale to retailers or third parties. However, we may disclose aggregate, anonymous data based on information collected from clients to our shareholders and partners.

Emails

If you wish to unsubscribe from receiving future emails, you can follow the instructions at the bottom of each email. We undertake to promptly remove you from the correspondence.
