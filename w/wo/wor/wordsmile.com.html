Log Files

Like many other Web sites, www.wordsmile.com makes use of log files. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons

www.wordsmile.com has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
