Privacy Policy

(Updated on July 04 2008)

Worx International Inc. respects and protects your privacy. While browsing our website pages you do not need to provide any information about yourself. By using this website, you consent to collection, use and storage of your personal information by us in the manner described in this privacy statement and elsewhere on the website.
