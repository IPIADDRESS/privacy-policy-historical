Privacy Policy and GDPR

Log files are maintained (and retained for a few days, before being deleted by rotation) and analysed of all requests for files on this website's web servers. Log files do not capture personal information but do capture the user's IP address and the data sent to this website.

This website does not store any information that would, on its own, allow us to identify individual users of this service or their personal details. We tell Google Analytics to anonymise IP addresses from the EEA.

Cookies used by this website

Cookies are small computer files that are placed in your browser's storage space in order to remember something between visits - almost all websites use cookies. Please note that cookies can't harm your computer and we don't store any personal information in the cookies we create.

To use this website as it was designed you will need to have cookies enabled on your web browser. Most web browsers have cookies enabled. If you don't want to enable cookies, the basic features of the site should still work. To disable cookies on your browser, please go to aboutcookies.org.

This website uses cookies:

  1. to store your dictionary preference and search-type so that it is remembered the next time you return to this website.
  2. in order to collect anonymous analytics statistics (so we can see which pages are popular, for instance).
  3. for social media sharing and like buttons such as the facebook like (facebook privacy policy), and add this (addthis privacy policy and opt-out).
  4. for advertising. We use Google Adsense for our advertising, and they have their own Google Advertising Privacy Policy and opt-out controls
WordSolver was originally written as a tool to help solve back-of-the-newspaper anagram-type word puzzles, but its application is quite wide across many games including online word games like scrabble. Not only is wordsolver a scrabble solver, but it can unscramble letters for many anagram games such as words with friends, draw something, 4 pics 1 word too. It also opens up the possibility of playing pro-level games of scrabble where all the players use Wordsolver to assist in finding words, but use skill in working out where to play the words on the scrabble board and deciding which letters to retain. Great fun!
