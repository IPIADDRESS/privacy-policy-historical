Privacy

World Nomads upholds the standard for privacy protection and is dedicated to protecting your personal information from the time you share it with us.

World Nomads is committed to observing its obligations under the amended Privacy Act 1988 which regulates how the company should manage and use personal information collected and held about people.

What personal information does World Nomads collect?

We only collect personal information for the purpose of providing you with insurance and to facilitate contact with you in case of emergency. We will not sell, rent, share or disclose any information about you to any third parties other than those required to provide your insurance.

We take reasonable steps to ensure that the information we hold about you is accurate when we use it. You should contact us if you think your personal information may be incorrect.

How to access your personal information

After signing into World Nomads you can click on the link to edit your personal details.
