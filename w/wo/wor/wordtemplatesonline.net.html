Privacy Policies

Private Policy stating the rights, services, and uses for our website

How We Collect Information

We collect information from new subscribers to our site as well as someone that leaves us a comment. We do not store personal information and the only thing we will use is the email address you provided.

Collected Information

We use your email address to inform you when updates and changes have been made to the website. We update our subscribers on the latest happenings. When a person requests information we will email them back. The email address will go into our files. A user may request to stop receiving emails by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of the email.

Safeguarding Your Information

We have a secure database where every email address is stored. The database is linked with a software program and a web update program. There is password protection on this database. The only people that can access the database are the administrators of the website.

Use of Cookies

When our templates are opened or a person makes our link a favorite we use cookies to track this information.

A third party will not have access to our user’s information. We do not trade or sell the information of our users to advertisers or promoters.

Google Adsense

Google Adsense may place advertisements on our website. Google will track activity with their Dart cookies. They will use this information to decide on the types of ads they want to place on the web page. You can read the Ads and Content Network Privacy Policy from Google at http://www.google.com/policies/technolgoy/ads/.

Access to Our Conditions and Private Policy

Word Templates Online reserves the right to make changes to our private policy as we see fit. We will inform users for changes right after they are made. They will be highlighted so users can see them. Users should check back regularly to see if we have made any changes. The user is responsible for looking for updates that have been made to our website.

Anyone that is at Word Template Online website user agrees to this private policy and as a subscriber you state that you agree to this policy.

Contacting Us

If a user has any questions or needs assistance understanding our private policy they should contact us right away.
