Woolworths.co.uk Privacy Pledge

Who we are

Woolworths.co.uk Ltd is a member of the Woolworths Group, which includes MVC Entertainment Ltd, EUK, Woolworths plc and VCI. We, the Group, are committed to providing users with an Internet experience which respects their privacy. We adhere strictly to the requirements of the UK Data Protection Act 1998.

Woolworths.co.uk have an up-to-date entry under the Data Protection Register. This can be seen at: http://www.dpr.gov.uk/, when you reach this page simply use the search function provided and type 'Infront Ltd' (our registered corporate name) to locate our specific page.

About this statement - Your consent

The purpose of this statement is to explain to you how we may use your personal information. By registering as a user of the services provided by Woolworths.co.uk and by using the Woolworths.co.uk web sites generally you agree to this use.

Information you provide

  • Web usage information to develop and improve our website.
  • Registration information to keep you informed.
  • Name, address, e-mail, credit card and order details.
  • Other data, from time to time, to help us provide you with improved products and services.
How we use personal information
  • To process your order and administer your account (including fraud prevention).
  • To enable us to review, develop and improve the products, services, special offers and events that we offer.
  • To provide access to privileged areas of the website and provide additional information.
  • To administer contests, competitions and news email service.
  • To carry out market research and personalise your visit to our website.
Who do we pass personal information to?
We may provide information about you as follows:
  • To employee and agents of the Group to administer any accounts, products and services provided to you by the Group now or in the future.
  • Other than as necessary to fulfil your order, we will not provide your personal information (i.e. information you supply that identifies you) to any third party without your consent or unless we are required to do so by law.
  • Some general statistical information about the user base, sales patterns, traffic volumes and related matters may be passed on to reputable third parties but these details will not include information personally identifying the individual user.
Data Security

We endeavour to take all reasonable steps to protect your personal information. All the data collected by us is stored on a secure server. The secure server software encrypts all information you input before it is sent to us.

Your use of this site

In order that we can monitor and improve the site, we may gather certain information about you when you use it, including details of your domain name and IP address, operating system, browser, version, cookie details and the web site that you visited prior to our site.

Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of information stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. We use cookies to allow us to understand who has seen which pages and advertisements, to determine how frequently particular pages are visited and to determine the most popular areas of our website. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, though you do not have to. If you wish to disable cookies please refer to your user manual although please remember that by disabling cookies you may not be able to place an order on this website.

3rd party information

We may supplement the information that you provide to us with information that we receive from third parties, for example, collected from other members of your household.

Access to your information

You may access all your data via: https://www.woolworths.co.uk/common/account/secure/index.jhtml This is password protected and you are the only person with access to this data.

Other links

Please be aware that our site may link to other web sites which may be accessed through our site. We are not responsible for the data policies, content or security of these linked web sites.

Our terms

Please note that all products and services are supplied subject to our terms and conditions.

Contact Us

If you have any comments or queries in connection with our privacy policy, please contact our Customer Services team.
