WOBI Privacy Policy

HSM Americas Inc. and its subsidiaries and affiliates ("HSM", "us", "we") are strongly committed to protecting the privacy of consumers of its products and services, and other users of its site. Your privacy on the Internet is of importance to us. Because we gather certain types of information about consumers and users of our site and services, we want to inform you about our privacy policy. Please note that this policy applies only to WOBI, and not to other companies' or organizations' Web sites to which we may link.

We will ask you for your personal information when you register at our site; registering with us on our site is optional; however, if you choose not to register, you can still use the web site, but you will not be able to purchase products and services on line. We do not collect any personal information regarding your use of the site, when you are just browsing it. We need certain information about you in order to provide you with the product or service requested by you (i.e. name, billing address, credit card number, phone number). We also collect information for market research purposes to gain a better understanding of our customers and thus provide more valuable service. This helps us to determine how best to provide useful information to customers and to understand which of services are of most interest to them. When registering, you are authorizing us to also use this information to let you know about new products or services that may be of your interest. (Please see Disclosure below).

When registering, our site sets a cookie that will allow us to provide you with a more customized experience on our site; thus, enabling you to manage your subscriptions and online profile, remembering you the next time you visit, helping you find information more easily, and confirming your purchases and registrations on our site. Also, our site registers your IP address, and other information regarding your operating system and browser. This information is never connected with the personal information you supply to us if you register on our website.
