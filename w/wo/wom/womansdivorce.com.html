Privacy Policy

At WomansDivorce.com, we take your privacy seriously.  We use visitor information to enhance the overall experience of our website.  We will not sell, trade or rent any personal information to others, unless required by law.   

How information is collected and how it's used:

IP Addresses 

  • When a visitor comes to our website, our web server recognizes only your IP address, not your e-mail address.  This allows us to track visits to our site, while visitors remain anonymous.

Cookies 

  • Cookies are pieces of data that your web browser stores on your hard drive, identifying you as a unique visitor.  This helps us to improve the content of our site, based on the individual pages visited during a viewing session.  We also track which websites refer visitors to our site.  

  • Visitors to this site always have the option of disabling cookies via their browser preferences.   Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies, but you can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

Other Information 

  • This website may also collect contact information if you sign up for our newsletter, allowing us to send updates or user information about our company.  This contact information is not shared with any third parties.  Users may opt-out of receiving future mailing by following the unsubscribe link in any of our mailings.  

  • This website also makes use of surveys to gather general information about its viewer base.  These surveys are voluntary, helping us to serve you better by providing pertinent information based on survey results.  Unless an email address is requested, only the IP address is recorded.

Links to Other Sites 

  • This site contains links to other sites in an effort to provide you with related information.  When you enter these other sites, you are entering a site for which we take no responsibility for their privacy practices or content.
