About WDWNT

WDW News Today (WDWNT) is a daily-updated resource for news and information on the Disney Parks worldwide, focusing on the Walt Disney World Resort in Orlando, Florida. We also produce various podcasts, as well as a weekly FM radio show called WDW News Tonight. WDWNT was founded on July 8, 2007 by Tom Corless.
