To learn more about this behavioral advertising practice or to opt-out of this type of advertising, you can visit networkadvertising.org.

Will WakingTimes.com disclose collected information to outside parties?

WakingTimes.com may eventually disclose the aforementioned GENERAL information about visitors – information that does NOT identify who you are. The last thing we want is “spam” reaching our visitors.

By using our Web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by WakingTimes.com.
