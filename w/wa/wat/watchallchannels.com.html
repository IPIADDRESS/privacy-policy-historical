Privacy & Policy of WatchAllChannels.com

In short, the following text will explain simply what information our website collects and what we do with it.

Your personal information and your trust in what we do with that information is very important to us. After all we want you to keep coming back to our site time and time again.

What information do we collect?

How do we collect it?

We use cookies! Not the eatable kind, but the small text file sized ones which are stored by a website on your computer which keeps track of information of how you are browsing on our site.

They can help us improve our content and target advertising especially if we know our viewers interests. We may also use the IP address, for example, to select ads based on your general location. Cookies help to make advertising more effective – and that is what keeps thousands of websites free of charge.

Without cookies, it’s harder for an advertiser to reach its audience, or to know how many ads were shown and how many clicks they received.If you don’t want to share this information with us, what can you do?This information is collected for traffic analysis only. The cookie does not contain personal details.

But if at any moment, you decide not to share this information you are free to accept/reject or selectively turn off our cookies or third-party cookies in your browser settings. But you should note that cookies may be necessary to provide you with features such as bookmaking or adding a website to your personal favourites list.

Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on www.watchallchannels.com. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

Are there any risks with sharing this kind of information with us?

No. Cookies are not programs which cause damage to a user’s system or files. There are no harmful technical risks or consequences by sharing this information with us. Cookies cannot introduce viruses to your system nor extract personal contact information. Should you wish to leave a comment or suggestion on any of the channels contained within our website, we require you to input your email address. Under no circumstances will your personal information or statistics be used, sold, shared, published online for other viewers to see or traded with any third party.
