  1. Purpose and Function
    • Wajam
      • Wajam uses a proxy or DLL to re-route internet searches through its servers in order to append social search results and to display advertising.
    • Wajam VPN
      • Wajam VPN creates a VPN tunnel from the user device to an exit IP address in another region in order to give the user added privacy and security while browsing the internet.
    • Wajam Social Search Mobile
      • The Wajam Social Search Application provides users with social results from their friends, within their existing search results, on their favourite search engines.

    • Wajam may install itself as an add-on, local proxy and/or dll and may also collect the following usage information, whether you are accessing the Website or use the Wajam Application as a registered or non-registered user: your IP address, Wajam cookie information (see below), the date and time of your visit or use of the Wajam Application, the type of Internet browser or hardware device (computer or mobile/smartphone) you use, URLs of the pages you visit and other information including the content of encrypted web pages in order to give you personal search results and the URL of any other website that referred you to our Website. The installed programs list is linked to the users unique id in order to allow applying optimization or bug fixes.

    • A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier that is sent to your browser from a web site or Mobile Application and stored locally. We do link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information you submit while on our site or when using our Mobile Application. Wajam may set and access certain cookies on your computer or your mobile device for the following reasons:
      • to facilitate your "signing in" on the Website or the Wajam Application or Mobile Application;
      • so that we can provide you with customized content;
      • to keep track of preferences you specify while you are using our Services;
      • to provide general internal and user analytics;
      • to conduct research to improve the content of the Website or the Mobile Application, the Wajam Application and the Services; and
      • to assist in identifying possible fraudulent activities.
      • If you configure your browser to reject cookies, you may not be able to use certain or all of our Services.
      • You may also request to delete your account in full from your profile’s settings page and remove the add-on from your browser’s “extension or add-on menu” or from your Windows Add/Remove Programs.

We do link this automatically collected data to other information we collect about you. Some of our business partners (e.g., Google Analytics) use cookies on our site. We have no access to or control over these cookies. This privacy statement covers the use of cookies by Wajam only and does not cover the use of cookies by any third-party.

These technologies are used in analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users’ movements around the site and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. We may receive reports based on the use of these technologies by these companies on an individual as well as aggregated basis.
    • As is true of most Web sites, Desktop Applications and Mobile Applications, we and our third party tracking-utility partner use log files to gather and store certain information automatically. This information includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and clickstream data. We use this information, which does not identify individual users, to analyze trends, to administer the site, to track users’ movements around the site and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole.
