Privacy Policy

In summary We are committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect on the site to make shopping at Waiter.com possible and to enhance your overall shopping experience. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. What information do we collect? How do we use it?

  • When you order, we need to know your name, e-mail address, street address, and phone number. Credit card information is optional. This allows us to process and fulfill your order and to notify you of your order status.
  • We may also use the information we collect to occasionally notify you about important functionality changes to the web site, new Waiter.com services, and special offers we think you'll find valuable.

Your consent By using our web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by Waiter.com. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it. Tell us what you think Waiter.com welcomes your questions and comments about privacy. Please send e-mail to web.master@waiter.com.
