Resilient Community, LLC Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our homepage and at every point where personally identifiable information may be requested.

When visiting waldenlabs.com and secure-publications.com, the IP address used to access the site will be logged along with the dates and times of access. The contents of this statement may be altered at any time, at our discretion.

About Walden Labs

Our goal at Walden Labs is to be the ultimate resource for building self-reliance and resilience, and learning about the survival skills of our ancestors.

Walden Labs is a place for information and ideas to be shared about self-reliance, homesteading, permaculture, preparedness, and decentralization. We strive to help people of all experience levels take steps towards happier, healthier, and more self-reliant lives. Getting back to our roots, one step at a time.
