This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect about you when you use the Service, how the information will be used, with whom we share your information, and how you can control the collection, use and sharing of your information or update your information. We will not knowingly use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

By accessing or using the Service, you consent to the collection, use, processing and sharing of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

You may access the Service from a mobile device as well as through a third party social networking site or service (a “Social Networking Site”). If you access the Service through a Social Networking Site, you may also be required to accept that Social Networking Site’s terms of use agreement and privacy policy.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the way we collect, use, process or share your information, please contact us at privacy@freshplanet.com.

PLEASE NOTE THAT THIS PRIVACY POLICY MAY CHANGE FROM TIME TO TIME. We will send you notice by email of any material changes to this Privacy Policy, and, therefore, it is important that you keep your email address with us current. We indicate at the top of the page when this Privacy Policy was last updated.

1.Information We Collect:


A. Information Received from Social Networking Sites:

When you access the Service by adding one of our applications on a Social Networking Site, you provide us with information associated with your account or profile on that Social Networking Site, as authorized by you and the Social Networking Site based on your privacy settings for the applicable Social Networking Site. This information may include your name; your gender and birthday; your profile picture; your user ID number and the user ID numbers of your friends; data made available by you and your friends; and your location and the location of the devices you use to access the Service. You should check the privacy policy, and your privacy settings and controls, for the applicable Social Networking Site to learn more about how you and your friends can control the information collected and shared with us through the applicable Social Networking Site.

B. Information Received from Mobile Devices or Personal Computers:

When you access the Service from a personal computer, we will receive and collect the IP address used to access the Service. When you access the Service through a mobile device we will receive and collect the device type and device i.d. If you access the Service through a mobile device, your location may be viewed by other users of the Service unless you deactivate the geolocation feature through the Settings Page of the Service. We may also use these identifiers and information to track your use of the Service and allow you to invite, or be invited by, other users to play or use one of our applications.

C. Information You Provide to Us Directly:

By accessing or using the Service, we will directly collect information about you as follows:

i. User Account and Profile: If you access the Service through a means other than a Social Networking Service, you will be required to provide your email address in order to create an account within the Service. In addition, we may allow you to create a user profile which will be visible to other users of the Service. This profile information may include: your name and/or user name; profile picture; biographical information, information about your use of the Service (such as playlists, songs liked, your opponents and scores), and links to profiles of you on Social Networking Sites. This information is considered public and may be indexed by search engines or other means, however, you may opt out of sharing your profile information as well as whether other users can see that you are on-line or off-line by adjusting the settings at the Settings Page of the Service.

ii. Correspondence and Public Postings: If you correspond with us by email (including for support purposes), we may retain the content of your email messages, your email address and our responses for internal use. We may also retain any messages you send through the Service either to us or other users. In addition, any information you provide in any chat room, User Content or other public areas of the Service may be accessed by us and other users, and we may store this information.

iii. Gameplay and User Activity: When you use the Service, we collect information and statistics about your use of the Service and interactions with other users to improve the Service and offer a richer and more personalized user experience. Certain information about your gameplay and user activity, including scores and challenge results, will be automatically loaded into our other applications included in the Service. You can control the sharing of such information among our applications by adjusting the settings at the Settings Page of the Service.

iv. SMS and Our Notifications: If you sign up to have SMS notifications sent to your mobile device, we receive and store the telephone number(s) that you provide to us. You can control or manage SMS notifications by adjusting your SMS notification settings.

D. Payment Information:

If you conduct paid transactions on the Service, you will be required to provide payment information, such as your credit card number, to our payment partner(s). However, we do not receive the information you provide to our payment partner(s), and your payment information is subject to the payment partner’s terms of use agreement and privacy policy, and not this Privacy Policy or our Terms of Use.

2. Methods Used to Collect Information:


A. Cookies:

When you use the Service from a computer, we may send one or more cookies or small text files containing strings of alphanumeric characters to your computer that uniquely identify your browser and let us help you log in faster and track and enhance your navigation through the Service. We also use cookies to collect information about how you use the Service. Third parties that deliver advertisements to users of the Service may also use cookies, and their use of cookies is governed by their own policies and practices. You can set your web browser to refuse certain types of cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, your web browser may not be able to refuse or remove flash cookies. To learn more about flash cookies, you may visit http://helpx.adobe.com/flash-player/kb/disable-local-shared-objects-flash.html. If you set your browser to limit or remove cookies, some features of the Service may be unavailable or unable to function properly.

B. Log Files:

Log file information is automatically reported by your browser or Social Networking Site each time you access the Service from your computer. We also use log files and server logs to collect information about your use of the Service to provide a more personal and richer user experience. These log files and server logs may include information such as your web request, Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, browser type, referring/exit pages and URLs, number of clicks, domain names, landing pages and pages viewed.

C. Clear GIFs or Web Beacons:

When you use the Service from your computer, we may employ clear GIFs (also known as web beacons) which are used to track the online usage patterns of our users so that we may better understand how people use the Service. In addition, we may also use clear gifs in HTML-based emails or Facebook messages sent to our users to track which emails or messages are opened by recipients. We use this information to enable more accurate reporting, improve the effectiveness of our marketing, and improve the SongPop user experience. We may also use this information to track user content and users to the extent necessary to prevent illegal activities and enforce our Terms of Use Agreement and comply with applicable law. We do not share usage information with third parties, except in the form of aggregate data that does not specifically identify individual users and except as necessary to prevent illegal activities and enforce our Terms of Use Agreement.

D. Invitations from Other Users:

If another user chooses to use our invitation service to invite you to use the Service, that person will provide us with your email address and we will automatically send an email invitation to you on that person’s behalf. SongPop will only use your email to send this invitation to you on the other user’s behalf and, if you accept the invitation from the other user, to register you as a user of the Service.

A. General:

We use the personal information we collect to operate, maintain, provide and improve the features and functionality of the Service and to create a personalized and rich user experience. In addition, we use your personal information to: (a) create your Service account and allow use of the Service; (b) remember information so that you will not have to re-enter it during your next visit or use of the Service; (c) update your user profile; (d) identify and suggest connections with other SongPop users and enable user-to-user communications; (e) customize and personalize the user experience; (f) monitor the effectiveness of our Service; (g) monitor aggregate metrics such as total number of users, traffic and demographic patterns; (h) notify you of Service updates or new products or promotional offers; (g) solicit feedback from you about how we can improve the Service; (i) respond to your inquiries; (j) provide technical support and diagnose or fix technology problems reported by our users or engineers that are associated with certain IP addresses; (k) help you efficiently access your information after you sign in; (l) track user content and users to the extent necessary to prevent illegal activities and enforce our Terms of Use Agreement and comply with applicable law; and (m) deliver targeted advertisements.

By providing SongPop your email address, including by adding one of our applications on a Social Networking Site, you consent to our using the email address to send you Service-related notices, in lieu of communication by postal mail, except as required by applicable law. If you do not want to receive email messages from us, you may opt out by sending an email with “opt out” in the subject line to privacy@freshplanet.com or by following the unsubscribe link in the applicable email. Opting out may prevent you from receiving email messages regarding updates, improvements, or other offers. You may not opt out of Service-related e-mails.

C. SMS Notifications:

If you sign up to have SMS notifications sent to your mobile device, we will send a verification request to the phone number you provide to us so that you can confirm that you want to receive SMS notifications from us. If you verify that you want to receive SMS notifications from us, we may use the phone number you provide to notify you of activity within SongPop or to allow you to play SongPop from your mobile device. In addition, from time to time we will send you messages directly to your mobile device related to the Service, including to notify you of new SongPop features. You can control or manage these messages, or opt-out of receiving sms notifications from us, by adjusting the settings in the Settings Page of the Service.
