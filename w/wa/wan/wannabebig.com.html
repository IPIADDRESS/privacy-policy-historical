Privacy Notice

At Large Nutrition knows that you care how information about you is used and shared, and we appreciate your trust that we will do so carefully and sensibly. The information we learn from customers helps us personalize and continually improve your shopping experience at AtLargeNutrition.com.

What personal information about customers does AtLargeNutrition.com gather? How is this information used?

When you sign up for an account or place an order we will collect the following pieces of information:

* Full name
* E-mail address
* Mailing address
* Credit card number and expiration date.

This allows us to process and fulfill your order and to notify you of your order status.

We may also use the above information to notify you about important changes to AtLargeNutrition.com and to make you aware of any special offers we think you would find valuable.

From time to time we may run promotional features/contests. We may collect information such as your name, postal address and email address during these times. This information will be used to run the promotional feature and also notify contest winners.

 

Will At Large Nutrition share the information it receives?

Information about our customers is an important part of our business, and we are not in the business of selling it to others. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others.

The only time we may release account and other personal information is when we believe release is appropriate to comply with law; enforce or to protect the rights, property, or safety of At Large Nutrition, our users, or others. This includes exchanging information with other companies and organizations for fraud protection and credit risk reduction.

 

How Secure Is Information About Me?

The information you provide is kept on a secure dedicated server and is protected against unauthorized access. The secure server software (SSL) encrypts all information you input before it is sent to us.
