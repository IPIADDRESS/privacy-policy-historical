The personal information that Wanderlust may collect may include your name, username, password, email address, age, date of birth, gender, address, telephone number, credit card and debit card numbers (with expiration dates), personal preferences, and any other personal information that you choose to include in your profile, your submissions of your goals to Wanderlust, or in other communications to Wanderlust. Cookies

Like many websites, Wanderlust utilizes “cookies” to maintain a record of your visit to the Sites. A cookie is a small text file that is transferred by a web server and stored on the hard drive of your computer. It can only be read by the server that sent it to you. This information does not identify you personally and you remain anonymous unless you have otherwise provided Wanderlust with personal information. Cookies help Wanderlust to improve the Sites and to deliver more personalized service by enabling Wanderlust to estimate audience size and usage patterns; to store information about your preferences; to speed up your searches and to recognize you when you return to the Sites. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies. If you would prefer to prevent your computer from accepting Wanderlust’s cookies, you may follow your Internet browser’s steps for doing so. Please note, however, that if you do disable cookies from your browser, you may not be able to access certain sections of the Sites.

IP Addresses

An IP address is a computer’s numeric address within a network. Wanderlust may record your IP address when you visit or use services or features on the Sites. Wanderlust may use your IP address for the purposes noted above, and also to help diagnose problems with Wanderlust’s server, or to administer the Sites. Wanderlust may also use your IP address to identify you when Wanderlust feels it is necessary to enforce compliance with this Privacy Policy or the Terms of Use, or to protect the Sites, systems, information, employees, service providers, business partners, users, customers and others.

Nevertheless, Internet transmissions are never completely private or secure. You understand that any messages or information you send to the Sites may be read or intercepted by others. If you have any questions about the security of personal information collected by Wanderlust, please contact info@wanderlust.com.

Any submissions that you may post to any publicly available blogs or other publicly available features of the Sites are not confidential and may be viewed by other users of the Sites. By making personal information publicly available where the Sites allow you to do so, you consent to such publication by Wanderlust or its service providers and agents. Please refer to the Terms of Use for additional provisions relating to user submissions that may apply to you.
