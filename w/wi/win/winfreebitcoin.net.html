Privacy

The privacy of all customers to Win Free Bitcoin is very important to us.

At Win Free Bitcoin, we understand the importance of keeping your personal information private. Below you will find the types of personal information we collect when you access and visit Win Free Bitcoin, and how we always try to safeguard your information. We do not sell personal information to third parties.

Log Files As with most other websites, we collect and use the data contained in log files. The information in the log files include your IP (internet protocol) address, your ISP (internet service provider, such as AOL or Cable), the browser you used to visit our site (such as Internet Explorer or Firefox), the time you visited our site and which pages you visited throughout our site.

Cookies and Web Beacons We do use cookies to store information, such as your personal preferences when you visit our site. This could include only showing you an advertisment once on your visit, or the ability to login to some of our features, such as posting comments.

We also use third party advertisements on Win Free Bitcoin to support our site. Some of these advertisers may use technology such as cookies and web beacons when they advertise on our site, which will also send these advertisers (such as Google through the Google AdSense program) information including your IP address, your ISP , the browser you used to visit our site, and in some cases, whether you have Flash installed. This is generally used for geotargeting purposes (showing UK clothing store ads to someone in the UK, for example) or showing certain ads based on specific sites visited (such as showing investing ads to someone who frequents investment sites).
