Cookie

A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. It is uniquely assigned to you and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issues the cookie to you.

The Company’s site including those optimised for mobile devices use cookies and similar technologies to manage login sessions, provide personalised web pages and tailor advertisements according to your needs and interests.
