PRIVACY POLICY






Intrawest/Winter Park Operations Corporation ("Winter Park" or "we") and its partent corporation, Intrawest Corporation are very mindful about your sense of privacy and security while on our Web site, as well as your concern with how we care for your personal information. The following is Winter Park's privacy pledge to you and deals with the following issues:



  1. Security

  2. Information Collection

  3. Cookies

  4. Information Use

  5. The Benefits of Providing Information

  6. Non-Use and Disclosure

  7. Opting Out, Editing and Deleting Profile, and Unsubscribing

  8. Children's Privacy

  9. External Links

  10. Legal Liability


Security

Winter Park has gone to great lengths to ensure the security of your credit card and other personal booking information. The security measures we use include firewalls and encryption programs. Our industry-standard firewalls are a combination of hardware and software that securely separate the Internet from our organization's Web servers and our Web servers from our data. The firewalls are designed to prevent unauthorized access to our Web servers as well as our data.


We use Secure Socket Layer (commonly known as "SSL") Encryption whenever financial information, customer identification, personal information, and passwords are transmitted to our Web servers over the Internet.


In addition to the technically-driven security measures, we also follow company-wide internal privacy procedures. In most cases, our employees will see personal information only in the aggregate. For example, if you book a room, specific employees will see only the amount of personal information they need to complete your booking. Other employees will see the total number of rooms booked for a specific date, but not specific information about your booking.


Information Collection

In a few areas on Winter Park's Web site, we ask you to provide information that will enable us to enhance your Web site visit, process your order correctly, and if necessary, follow up with you before or after your visit. For instance, we want your e-mail address or phone number so that we can contact you if, due to unforeseen circumstances (such as flood, fire, storm, avalanche), we need to rebook you into another room or get additional information.


From time to time, we may ask you questions, or use the data we collect from order processing, to better understand our customers' preferences. This information allows us to improve our products and services, and to offer you packages tailored to your preferences when appropriate. While some of this information is provided at your option, other information (e.g., the personal information required to process an order) is not optional.


We will request information from you when you:



  1. Register at the Winter Park website;

  2. Plan a trip to Winter Park;

  3. Place an order with Winter Park ( i.e. rent skis, ski and snowboard school, competition programs etc.;

  4. Provide feedback in an online survey;

  5. Participate in a sweepstakes or other promotional offer; and

  6. Subscribe to a newsletter or a mailing list.


In each of these instances, we may ask for any of the following types of information: name, e-mail address, phone number, mailing address, customer and trip preference information, customer ID, and password, as well as other similar personal information that is needed to register or subscribe you to services or offers.


If you do not want to receive promotional materials, special offers, or newsletters from us, we ask that you tell us this by opting-out, changing the type of information you receive from us, or unsubscribing, as explained below.


Cookies

Depending on how you configure your Web browser, when you visit the Winter Park Web site, a small text file (a "cookie") is stored on your hard drive. Whenever you log onto the Winter Park Web site, our Web server looks for your cookie, and that's how we recognize that you've visited our Web site before so that you will not need to re-enter your personal information. Your Web browser can be configured to reject cookies, but if you do this, you'll have to re-enter your personal information each time you use our online booking and other pages. Also, if you reject cookies, you will not be able to take advantage of personalized content delivery from our and various other Web sites.


Winter Park does not allow companies that maintain advertising banners on our Web site to automatically store cookies on your hard drive when you visit our Web site. The companies can only store cookies if you access their Web sites.


Information Use

Different information collected by us will be used for different purposes. Some personal information is required to process your order. For instance, we need to know your correct age, height, weight, foot size, skill level, and gender so that we can set up ski or snowboarding rental equipment before you arrive.


We may also use personal account information to generate statistics and aggregate reports for internal use and for sharing with affiliates. These statistics and aggregate reports will not contain any personally identifiable information. Your identity is kept anonymous. This information may be used to design programs and promotions that we and our affiliates think would be of interest to an identified group of customers. For example, we may want to develop special programs if our analysis of purchase data shows that a sufficient number of our guests share specific interests that would be better served with a special program.


In addition, we may use these detailed profiles in order to target special offers and promotions more effectively. Knowing more about you will allow us to personalize the delivery of our Web site's content to you should you wish to receive offers from us. We presently deliver personalized content via e-mail.


The Benefits of Providing Information

Knowing more about you allows us to personalize the types of information we send to you. This means that you will probably receive less mail from us and what you do receive will be more likely to apply to you and your interests.


Non-Use and Disclosure

We do not and will not sell or rent the personal information of any user of the Winter Park Web site to any other entity or third party outside of the Intrawest Corporation family of companies


Please remember that anything posted on our message boards becomes public information. Therefore, you should exercise caution about disclosing personal information on these pages.


In all cases, Winter Park reserves the right to cooperate with local, state, federal, and international officials in any investigation requiring either personal information (including any information provided online through the Winter Park Web site ) or reports about unlawful user activity on this Web site.


Winter Park also reserves the right to disclose any information in an individual's account:



  1. To satisfy any law, regulation, or government request;

  2. If such disclosure is necessary or appropriate to the Winter Park Web site operation; and

  3. To protect the rights or property of Winter Park and its guests, Web site visitors, sponsors, providers, licensers, or merchants.


Unless otherwise required by law, Winter Park reserves the right to decide if it will contact customers should Winter Park be required to disclose any personal information (as described in this Non-Use and Disclosure section).


By using the Winter Park Web site, you signify your acceptance of the terms of this Privacy Policy. Winter Park reserves the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be effective upon posting on this Web site.


Opting Out, Editiing and Deleting Profile, and Unsubscribing

Should you want to change or unsubscribe from our email or directed mail services we ask that you either use edit your profile and uncheck the boxes of the services you are subscribed to at https://secure.intrawestcolorado.com/profile/login.aspx. You may also update your email and physical address there too. You may also unsubscribe to any email service by clicking on the unsubscribe link included in every email message sent.


We will also delete your name and other personal information from our database upon your written request sent via e-mail to the Winter Park Web Master by way of the link at http://www.skiwinterpark.com/contact/index.html. When you register and your account is created, we will confirm your registration by sending an e-mail to the address that you supplied to us. All future e-mails to your account will contain information on how to delete your name and other personal information from our database and how to unsubscribe from our mailing list. Please follow the e-mail's instructions exactly when requesting that this information be deleted or to unsubscribe.


Contacting Customers

Winter Park and Intrawest Corporation reserve the right to decide if they will contact customers should they be required to disclose any information as set forth in the non-use and disclosure section above.


Children's Privacy

Winter Park cares about the safety and privacy of children while they are online. Therefore, we comply with the Children's Online Privacy Act of 1998. Winter Park does not knowingly solicit data from, or about, people under the age of 13 years for any reason and will not accept such data without first obtaining parental consent. If we are notified that we have unknowingly received personal data from a child under the age of 13, we will promptly obtain parental consent or delete the information from our database.


Note: Winter Park assumes that visitors to its Web site are adults. We do not require any visitor to provide a date of birth at registration, although we do ask for an age or birth date when applicable for pricing purposes (for instance, children and seniors qualify for discounts on lift tickets). Federal law prohibits discrimination in public accommodations based on age, so when we send out information on lodging bookings we are required to treat all Web site members equally, regardless of age.


We will make every reasonable attempt to send real estate offers only to customers 21 years old and older. If we are notified that we have unknowingly sent a real estate offer to a customer under the age of 21, we will promptly delete the customer's information from our mailing database.


External Links

The Winter Park Web site provides links to various other Web sites that Winter Park does not control. By clicking on one of these links, you will leave the Winter Park Web site and be transferred to the Web site of the company you selected. Each of these Web sites may have its own privacy policy, data collection, and data use policies. Please consult the respective Web site's privacy policy before providing any personal data.


Legal Liability

Under no circumstances, including, but not limited to, negligence, shall Winter Park be liable for any direct, indirect, special, incidental or consequential damages, including, but not limited to, loss of data or profit, arising out of the use, or the inability to use, the materials on this Web site, even if Winter Park or a Winter Park authorized representative has been advised of the possibility of such damages.
