Privacy Policy

Winbond Electronics Corporation ("WEC") reserves the right to revise this Privacy Policy at any time. Your use of our website constitutes your acceptance of the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you have any questions about this Policy, please contact us at privacy@winbond.com .

This Privacy Policy applies solely to all personal information which is collected or used through this website.

You do not have to provide any personal information to visit this website. However, you may wish to use certain services on this website, search for WEC's products information or need assistance in products that may require you to provide personally identifiable information, such as your name, mailing address, email address and other contact information.

The personal information collected is used to respond to your inquiries and to send you materials that you request. The information collected is also used to help us to maintain and/or upgrade the quality of our website and the services that we provide on the website. We will not provide such information to unaffiliated third parties; however, we may provide such information to our subsidiaries, officers, employees, contractors and agents to the extent necessary to enable them to execute certain web services. We also reserve the right to disclose such information to any third party if we consider there is any of the following: (i) by law; (ii) to comply with legal processes or governmental requests; (iii) to prevent, investigate, detect or prosecute criminal offences or attacks on the web site; and/or (iv) to protect the rights, property, or safety of WEC, the users of the website.

Our website may send a "cookie," a small text file, to your computer in order to facilitate your use of our website. If you do not wish to receive cookies, you may set your Internet browser to either block all cookies or receive a warning before a cookie is stored. To learn more about cookies, you may visit www.cookiecentral.com

Our web server automatically records information incidental to your use of our website, such as the name of the domain and host from which you access the Internet, the Internet Protocol (IP) address of the computer you are using, the date and time you access our website, and the Internet address of the website from which you linked directly to our website. We use the foregoing information to track the aggregate traffic patterns throughout this website.

If you wish to modify your personal information, or if you wish to discontinue receiving materials from us or to remove your personal information from WEC's database, you can contact us at privacy@winbond.com .
