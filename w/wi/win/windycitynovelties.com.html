Access to All Sections of the Web Site Without Providing Personal Information
Visitors can access all sections of the web site without providing any personal, identifying information.

Voluntary Use of Contact Forms
Windy City Novelties Forms are where visitors may voluntarily provide personal information, including name, company/organization, city and state of residence, and telephone numbers. The contact information is only used to contact the visitor as requested.
