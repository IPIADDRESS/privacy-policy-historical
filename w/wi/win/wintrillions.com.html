Privacy Policy
 

Thank you for visiting trillonario.com. Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used.

Collection of information

When you subscribe to our service we ask for your name, address and other contact information, billing information, some preferences and certain demographic information. While some of the information you provide is essential to conduct our business, other information is optional, clearly marked as such, and may be given at your discretion.

The information you provide allows us to provide an efficient service and helps us improve our level of service to better meet your needs.

How we use the information

We use the information you provide about yourself to fulfill your requests for our products and services and to respond to any inquiries about our offerings.

We sometimes use this information to communicate with you, such as to notify you when you have won lotteries or contests, when we make changes to our subscriber agreements, to fulfill some of your requests, or to contact you about your account with us.

We also may use non-personally identifiable information that we collect in order to improve the design and content of our websites and to collect usage statistics.

We may disclose personally identifiable information when required by law and in response to a legal process. We also may disclose such information in response to a law enforcement agency’s request, or where we believe it is necessary to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of our terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.

Cookies

To enhance your experience with our site, many of our web pages use “cookies”. Cookies are small bits of information that are stored on your computer. Cookies do not provide any personally identifiable information to us but allow your navigation through our site to be more enjoyable.

Security

We implement appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and prevent unauthorized access to the information we maintain in our systems.
