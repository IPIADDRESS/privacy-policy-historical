Additionally, cookies may be used to provide easier and faster access to website features such as logins and/or repeat visits.  Cookies are very small text files that are stored on personal computers for reference purposes. We use session ID cookies. A session cookie expires when your browser is closed. Session cookies make it easier for you to navigate our site.
