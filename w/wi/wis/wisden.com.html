Privacy Policy

Cricket Properties delivers content, events and other services to rights holders and brands within the world of cricket, and operates as the Wisden brand in Europe, Africa and Australasia manifesting as wisden.com, Wisden Cricket Monthly and The Nightwatchman.

We want to give you clear information about what information we collect and how we use it.

This privacy policy notice sets out how Cricket Properties Ltd uses and protects any information that you give Cricket Properties Ltd when you use this website.

Cricket Properties Ltd is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. Should we ask you to provide certain information by which you can be identified when using this website, www.wisden.com, then you can be assured that it will only be used in accordance with this privacy statement.

If you consent to Cricket Properties contacting you, you can withdraw this consent at any time by contacting us via contact@wisden.com or by unsubscribing via the ‘unsubscribe’ link found on all emails we send. You can update your personal details and email preferences via the ‘update preferences’ link included on all emails we send.

What we collect

We may collect the following information:

  • name
  • contact information including email address
  • demographic information such as postcode, preferences and interests
  • other information relevant to customer surveys and/or offers

How we collect information

  • Information we collect, via cookies and related technology, from the devices you use to receive Wisden content, products and services, about your use of Wisden, and/or third party content, products and services. This may include fingerprint information about your device, machine or browser so that we can make sure you get the products and services you are entitled to receive. This information may be collected when cookies are turned off. For more information on cookies and how to manage them, please see our cookies notice.
  • When you create an account on Wisden.com
  • Shop purchases
  • When you subscribe to a Wisden mailing list
  • IP address, identification number, online identifier, browser information, location data and other similar identifying information required for your devices to communicate with websites and applications on the internet.
  • Information provided by other organisations who’ve obtained your permission to share information about you with us.
  • Competitions

What we do with the information we gather

  • We require this information to understand your needs and provide you with a better service.
  • We may use the information to improve our products and services.
  • We may periodically send promotional emails about our or our partners new products, special offers or other information which we think you may find interesting using the email address which you have provided. We will only send you promotional emails if you have given us consent to do so.
  • From time to time, we may also use your information to contact you for market research purposes. We may use the information to customise the website according to your interests.
  • We will never share your data with third parties unless you give us explicit consent.

Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorised access or disclosure, we have put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

Links to other websites

Our website may contain links to other websites of interest. However, once you have used these links to leave our site, you should note that we do not have any control over that other website. Therefore, we cannot be responsible for the protection and privacy of any information which you provide whilst visiting such sites and such sites are not governed by this privacy statement. You should exercise caution and look at the privacy statement applicable to the website in question.

Controlling your personal information

You may choose to restrict the collection or use of your personal information in the following ways:

  • not submitting your email address via any submission box on the website
  • you may choose not to allow us to use your personal information for marketing purposes
  • if you have previously agreed to us using your personal information for marketing purposes, you may change your mind at any time by writing to Cricket Properties Limited, Fourth Floor, Bedser Stand, The Kia Oval, Kennington, SE11 5SS or emailing us at contact@wisden.com.

We will not sell, distribute or lease your personal information to third parties unless required by law to do so.

You may request details of personal information which we hold about you under the Data Protection Act 1998. If you would like a copy of the information held on you please write to Cricket Properties Limited, Fourth Floor, Bedser Stand, The Kia Oval, Kennington, SE11 5SS or email contact@wisden.com.

If you believe that any information we are holding on you is incorrect or incomplete, please write to Cricket Properties Limited, Fourth Floor, Bedser Stand, The Kia Oval, Kennington, SE11 5SS or email info@wisden.com. We will promptly correct any information found to be incorrect.

Keeping your data up to date

You may notify us of any updates, amendments and corrections to your information, or restrict or delete your data (where applicable), by contacting us via contact@wisden.com.

Please note that we may need certain information to enable us to provide you with the products, goods and services you ask for, so changes you make to your data or restrictions you ask us to make on how we use your data may affect what services we can provide.

How long we keep your information

We will contact you at least once every 12 months to re-confirm the consent you have given us to store and use your data. If you don’t not re-confirm your consent, we will remove your data from our records. Please note that we will consider consent to have been given if you open and/or click on a link within an email or log in to your user account.

Complaints

If you wish to make a complaint about how we use your information, please contact us at contact@wisden.com and we will do our best to help. If you are still unhappy, you can contact the Information Commissioner’s Office via their website.

Changes to this notice

We will occasionally update our Terms and Conditions. We will post a notice of any material changes on our website prior to implementing the changes, and, where appropriate, notify you using any of the contact details we hold for you for this purpose. This may include e-mail, post or interactive social media. We encourage you to periodically review our notice to be informed of how we use your information.
