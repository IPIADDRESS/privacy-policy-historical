HOW INFORMATION IS COLLECTED
The information we learn from Users helps us personalize and continually improve this Site. Below are the general types of information we gather. Information You Give Us In general, you may visit this Site without providing any Personally Identifiable Information about yourself, except as otherwise stated, unless you choose to provide such information. For example, you are not required to but you may choose to provide us with Personally Identifiable Information if you submit questions or comments, answer or respond to surveys or reviews, save your products or research, utilize certain on-line services or participate in certain online activities. Information Gathered Automatically We receive and store certain types of information whenever you interact with us. For example, like many Web sites, we use “cookies” to obtain certain types of information when your Web browser accesses this Site. Briefly, “cookies” are small files that we transfer to your computer’s hard drive or your Web browser memory to enable our systems to recognize your browser and to provide convenience and other features to you. Examples of the information we collect and analyze in this manner include the Internet protocol (IP) address used to connect your computer to the Internet; computer and connection information such as browser type and version, operating system, and platform; cookie number; your activities on our Site and related services, including the products and/or services you view or search for, as well as the URL you come from and go to next (whether this URL is on our site or not); and products and/or research you have chosen to save. You are always free to decline our cookies if your browser permits, although, in that case, you may not be able to use certain features or services on the Site.
