Some information shared and available on our sites might be very specific to your location. It will be possible for you to define the same and change the location from your account settings.

We allow you to create and link your Wishberg account with social networking sites such as Facebook and Twitter. When you allow us to add our application with your permission, we may receive information about you which includes your name, picture, email address, gender, date of birth, your profile id, your friends profile ids and other information that is made available through their APIs. This information may be used to also display promotional information or advertisements which may or may not be based on your location. We may also use this information to understand your mobile application usage for our application while your try to access our services through such devices. Cookies
A cookie is a small data file that is transferred to your computer's hard disk. Like many websites, we use "cookie" technology to collect additional website usage data and to improve our Services. Our sites may use both session cookies and persistent cookies to better understand how you interact with our services, to monitor aggregate usage by our users and web traffic routing on our Services, and to improve our Services. A session cookie enables certain features of the service and our service and is deleted from your computer when you disconnect from or leave the service. A persistent cookie remains after you close your browser and may be used by your browser on subsequent visits to the service. Persistent cookies can be removed by following your web browser help file directions. Most Internet browsers automatically accept cookies. You can instruct your browser, by editing its options, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the websites you visit.
Log Data
Our servers automatically record information ("Log Data") created by your use of the Services. This Log Data may include information such as your IP address, browser type or the domain from which you are visiting, the web-pages you visit, the search terms you use, and any links on which you click. Other actions, such as interactions with advertisements, may also be included in Log Data. For most users accessing the Internet from an Internet service provider the IP address will be different every time you log on. We use Log Data to monitor the use of the Service and of our Service.
