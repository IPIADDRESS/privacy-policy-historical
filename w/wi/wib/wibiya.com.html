Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the website located at www.wibiya.com (the "Site"), the tools and interactive forums made available through the Site and the Wibiya application and web toolbar platform, as well as a number of related services (collectively the "Services").

By visiting the Site or using the Services, you are accepting the policies and practices described in this Privacy Policy.

To be clear about the terminology we are using, when we use the phrase "Personal Information" in this Privacy Policy, we mean information that can be used to identify you like your name, address, e-mail address, or phone number, as well as other non-public information that is associated with this personal information. When we use the phrase "Anonymous Information" in this Privacy Policy, we mean information that cannot be used to identify you and is not associated with or linked to your Personal Information.

As a general rule there are two categories of users of the Site and Services: "Publishers" who are operators or publishers of websites that make the the Wibiya application and web toolbar platform available on their website, and "End Users" who are individual visitors of a website and use the the Wibiya application and web toolbar platform for different use cases.

The Site and Services are hosted in the United States. If you access the Site or Services from the European Union, Asia, or any other region with laws or regulations governing personal data collection, use, and disclosure that differ from United States laws, please be advised that through your continued use of the Site or Services, you are transferring your Personal Information to the United States and you consent to that transfer. Additionally, you understand that your Personal Information may be processed in countries (including the United States) where laws regarding processing Personal Information may be less stringent than in your country.

Our Site and Services

(a) The Information We Gather and How We Use it:
When you access the Site or Services, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information - We collect Personal Information when you knowingly provide it, including when you register for the Site, provide feedback, or request information. We use your Personal Information to administer your account and provide you services, to send communications and administrative emails, to respond to your inquiries, to market to you, and to improve the quality of the Site and Services. Additionally, we may strip your Personal Information of any information that specifically identifies you to effectively create Anonymous Information and we will use this Anonymous Information as provided in this Privacy Policy. As the Services we offer that require registration are designed for Publishers.
  • Anonymous Information - When you access our Site or use our Services, we use Cookies and log files to automatically record information that your browser sends. This includes your Internet Protocol address, browser type, browser language, the date and time of your request, and the web pages you have visited. "Cookies" are small pieces of information that a website sends to your computer's web browser while you are viewing a website. We may use both session Cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and persistent Cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them) to provide you with a more personal and interactive experience. Persistent Cookies can be removed or blocked by following the help file directions in your Internet browser. If you elect to block Cookies, you may not be able to access certain personalized sections of our Services such as Publisher reports. We use log files and Cookies to improve the quality of our Site and Services; to keep track of your login name, passwords, and pages you have visited; and to tailor content to your interests and to personalize the Services on your behalf.

Additional Information about the Wibiya application and web toolbar platform

We operate the Wibiya application and web toolbar platform (the "Wibiya Platform"), which ads functionality to publishers. If you are reading this policy, you are probably familiar with our web toolbar (collectively, the "Wibiya bar") that Publishers place on their websites. This section describes how we collect, use and share your information as part of the Wibiya platform.

(a) The Information We Gather and How We Use It:

  • When they help us operate our business, such as with vendors that assist us in sending email, marketing and customer service.
  • Personal Information - If you are accessing the Site or Services on behalf of a Publisher, you may provide your email address when you register the Publisher on the Site and create a user name and password. Any choices you make, information you knowingly provide us or data we gather and provide to you in analytics reports about the use of the Wibiya Platform on the Publisher's website is the Publisher's Personal Information. In addition, we include the user name you have chosen in the Wibiya code the Publisher places on its website, which enables us to associate analytics data with the Publisher's account. For this reason, you may wish to choose a user name that does not include your personal name and information, as the Publisher website's code is publicly viewable.
  • Anonymous Information - When you, an End User, download a page that uses the Wibiya platfom, our server logs collect your Internet Protocol address, browser type, browser language, and the date and time of your request. We use this information to improve our internal operations and software. In addition to log files, when a page containing the Wibiya platfom is downloaded, we may also set Cookies. Without knowing who you are, our Cookies may record information about how you use the web, for example, the websites and webpages you have visited, web searches that landed you on a page of interest, categories of interest, and if and how often you share content with others using the Wibiya platform. We may use this information to customize different types of functionality of the Wibiya bar or application that is triggered through the Wibiya.

(b) Information Sharing

We share the information we collect through the Wibiya platform in the same way described in Our Site and Services section above. In addition, we may assist our advertising partners in setting cookies so that we can share all or some of the Anonymous Information with them. Our advertising partners use the Anonymous Information to deliver targeted advertising. Please see the section below on Choice if you do not want us to share information with our advertising partners. To be clear the Anonymous Information provided to our advertising partners does not contain any Personal Information of End Users or any information that can be associated with any Publisher or Publisher domain.

(c) Choice

As noted above, we may have helped our advertising partners set cookies. In order to ensure that no additional information is collected by these Cookies for the purpose of delivering targeted advertising, you can clear all of your Cookies as provided in your browser's instructions. You can also opt-out from other network advertising from the opt-out page of the Network Advertising Initiative.

Finally, we allow Publishers to prohibit us from collecting information about visitors to the Site.
