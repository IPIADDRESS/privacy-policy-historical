Bontrager Connection LLC Privacy Policy

Bontrager Connection, LLC has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our commitment to privacy.

The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for the web sites maintained by Bontrager Connection, LLC (the "Web Site" or "Web Sites").

Links

The Web Sites contain links to other web sites, which may be information, advertising, or other links. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites.

Third Party Cookies

Third parties may be placing and reading cookies on your browser, or using web beacons to collect information, in the course of ads or other content being served on the web sites. Bontrager Connection, LLC has no authority over, and can not control, how third parties use cookies or information gathered therewith. Most browsers have options allowing users to block or otherwise manage cookies.

Ads

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit the Web Sites. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to the Web Sites and other sites on the Internet in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click heresee the section below.

Interest based advertising choices and information:

Please follow links of interest to you. If any links are disabled or broken, use a contact link at Bontrager Connection, LLC's WillMaster.com Web Site and request up-to-date links.

  • Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on the Web Sites.

  • Google's use of the DoubleClick DART cookie enables it to serve ads to you based on your visits to the Web Sites and other sites on the Internet.

  • You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

Children

The Web Sites are designed for the use of, and interaction with, adults. Any children at or viewing the Web Sites must be accompanied by responsible adults and any interaction with the Web Sites must be performed by adults.

Data Collection On The Web Sites

We may compile or mine information collected by the Web Sites for statistical, demographic, visitor usage, diagnostic, planning, administrative, content creation, anonymous statistics, site design, and/or other purposes.

No personal information collected by the Web Sites will be voluntarily released to any person or entity not involved with the maintenance or support of any of the Web Sites, unless a Web Site is sold, in which case all information and records pertaining to that Web Site may be transferred to the new owner(s). Bontrager Connection, LLC would not be responsible for the privacy practices of the new owner(s).

We may keep a record of any and all use of forms on the Web Sites. Email addresses of form users may be used for communication initiated with the form, or to send messages or content related to the purpose of the form. Unless such is within the purpose or intent of the form, no email addresses provided on our forms are added to any of our subscription or notification lists.

The Web Sites may collect and aggregate information, including anonymous information. Domain names, users' system data, IP addresses, and other information may be logged. When personal information is provided, that information may be logged.

Contact

If you wish to unsubscribe, choose to opt-out, or wish to be removed from any Web Site's subscription or other database records, use the method provided with emails you receive or, failing that, use a contact link at Bontrager Connection, LLC's WillMaster.com Web Site and provide us with all information we may require to remove you from our records.

Questions

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of any of the Web Sites, or your dealings with any of the Web Sites, use a contact link at Bontrager Connection, LLC's WillMaster.com Web Site and provide us with all information we may require to understand your questions or concerns and to respond to you.
