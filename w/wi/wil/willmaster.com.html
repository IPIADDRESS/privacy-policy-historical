Bontrager Connection Privacy Statement

Bontrager Connection, LLC has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our commitment to privacy.

The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for the Web Sites maintained by Bontrager Connection, LLC ("Web Site" or "Web Sites").

The Web Sites contain links to other web sites, which may be information, advertising, or other links. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites.

Children

The Web Sites are designed for the use of, and interaction with, adults. Any children at or viewing the Web Sites must be accompanied by responsible adults and any interaction with the Web Sites must be performed by adults.

Data Collection On Our Web Sites

No personal information collected by the Web Sites will be voluntarily released to any person or entity not involved with the maintenance or support of any of the Web Sites, unless a Web Site is sold, in which case all information and records pertaining to that Web Site may be transferred to the new owner(s). We may keep a record of any and all use of forms on our Web Sites. Email addresses of form users may be used for communication initiated with the form, or to send messages or content when such is the purpose of the form. Unless such is within the purpose or intent of the form, no email addresses provided on our forms are added to any of our subscription or notification lists, and no such addresses are voluntarily transferred to any other person or entity, without the consent of the person using the form.

All Web Sites collect and aggregate information, including anonymous information. Domain names, users' system data, IP addresses, and other information may be logged. When personal information is provided, that information may be logged.

If you wish to unsubscribe, choose to opt-out, or wish to be removed from any Web Site's subscription or other database records, use the method provided with emails you receive or, failing that, use the "Contact"/"Contact Us" link at the Bontrager Connection, LLC BontragerConnection.com Web Site or other Web Site.
