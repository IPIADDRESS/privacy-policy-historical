WillowTree, Inc. Privacy Policy


Welcome to WillowTree, Inc. We value your privacy, and we want to make sure you understand our policies regarding privacy and our website. We use our website to communicate with the public and potential customers about our products and services and to take in personal information by people and entities who wish to be in touch with us (the “Service”).

By accessing our website, you agree to our collection, storage, use and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy. We want you to know that your information, described below as both “personally identifying information” and “user information,” may be used for the following purposes: to provide and market our services to you, which includes operating the website; to improve the services we provide; to remember your preferences; to conduct in-house research; to prevent fraud or abuse; and to communicate with you and address your concerns.

If you use the website on behalf of a company, organization, government, or any other entity, then (a) “you” includes both you and that entity; and (b) you represent and warrant that you are legally authorized to consent to our collection, storage, use, and disclosure of the information we collect from you as described in this Privacy Policy.

Registration and contact information

We provide certain services on our website, and other third-party websites we use, that require visitors to enter their personal information, such as contacting us, registering for our newsletters, or completing a job application. In these cases, we may collect certain personal information such as your name, your company name, email address, and your messages (“personally identifying information” or “PII”).

We use personally identifying information to operate and increase the effectiveness of our services and to communicate directly with you. We may make phone calls to you and send you emails messages, including information such as company newsletters, changes or updates to features of the Service, and technical and security notices.

Information we collect from all website visitors

User Information

We collect user information (“user information”) from your browser or mobile device each time you access the website. User information may include anonymous information such as your web request, Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, browser type, information about your mobile device, referring/exit pages and URLs, domain names, landing pages, pages viewed, and other such information. When you access our Service by or through a mobile device, we may receive or collect and store a unique identification number associated with your device, mobile carrier, device type and manufacturer, phone number, and, depending on your mobile device settings, your geographical location data, including GPS coordinates (e.g. latitude and/or longitude) or similar information regarding the location of your mobile device.

Internal Cookies

We use different types of cookies (both temporary and persistent) to make it easier for you to navigate the site, track usage of our site to improve your experience, and remember your preferences. Disabling cookies may limit your ability to use some areas of our site. If you would like to disable cookies, you can often do so with your browser settings. Check your browser or learn more here. Be aware that you may be unable to use certain features of our products if you disable cookies.

Third Party Providers

We may work with third party providers to store and receive data, reports, and other user information sent by your browser or mobile device, including the pages you visit, your use of our website, your use of third party applications, and other information that we may use to analyze and improve the Service. These third parties may use both temporary and persistent cookies.

Web Beacons

Web beacons are electronic images that may be used on our website or in our emails. We and third party partners may use web beacons (also known as clear gifs or web bugs), images, tags and scripts that help us and them better manage content on our site to deliver cookies, count visits, understand usage and campaign effectiveness, and to determine whether an email has been opened and acted upon.

Local Shared Objects and Flash Cookies

To personalize your visit, we may use local shared objects such as HTML5 to store your preferences such as volume control or to display content based on what you view on our website. Third party partners provide certain features on our sites and may display advertising based on your browsing activity using Flash cookies or HTML5 to collect and store information. Various browsers may offer their own management tools for removing content stored locally using HTML5.

We may also use Flash cookies to collect information. Flash cookies are different from browser cookies because of the amount of data stored, type of data stored, and way in which data is stored. Cookie management tools provided by your browser usually will not remove Flash cookies. To learn how to manage privacy and storage settings for certain Flash cookies, click here.

We may share information in an aggregated and anonymous form that does not reasonably identify you directly as an individual or entity, in the ways described below. For further information on your choices regarding your information, see “Your Choices Regarding Your Information.”

In addition to WillowTree and third parties, the following entities may use your personal information under this Policy: service providers that perform services on our or third parties’ behalf, which may include payment processing, content or service fulfillment, or providing analytics services; third parties, at your request; other parties in connection with any company transaction, such as a merger, sale of company assets or shares, reorganization, financing, change of control or acquisition of all or a portion of our business by another company or third party or in the event of bankruptcy or related or similar proceedings; and third parties as required to (a) satisfy any applicable law, regulation, subpoena/court order, legal process or other governmental request, (b) investigate and defend ourselves against any third party claims or allegations, © protect against harm to the rights, property or safety of WillowTree, our users, or the public as required or permitted by law, and (d) detect, prevent or otherwise address criminal (including fraud or stalking), security, or technical issues.

Your choices regarding your information

Marketing Communications

If you do not wish to receive promotional emails, you can click the “unsubscribe” button on promotional email communications. Please note that you may not unsubscribe or opt out of non-promotional messages regarding your account, such as account verification, password requests, order confirmations, changes or updates to features of the Service, or technical and security notices.

Data Retention

At our discretion, we will retain all PII and user information that we collect. Please contact us if you wish us to remove your PII, at info@willowtreeapps.com.

Do-Not-Track

Because we want to provide you with a personalized experience on the Service, we do not change our behavior based on a web browser’s Do Not Track signal.

Security and storage of information

WillowTree cares about the security of your information and uses commercially reasonable physical, administrative, and technological safeguards to preserve the integrity and security of all information we collect and that we share with our service providers. However, we cannot make a 100% guarantee of the security of our systems. If information under our control is compromised, we will take commercially reasonable steps to investigate the situation. Where appropriate, we will notify those individuals whose information may have been compromised. We may take other steps in accordance with any applicable laws and regulations.

Your information collected through the Service may be stored and processed in the United States or any other country in which WillowTree or its subsidiaries, affiliates or service providers, heirs, assigns, or acquirers maintain facilities.

If you are located in the European Union or other regions with laws governing data collection and use that may differ from U.S. law, please note that we may transfer your information to a country and jurisdiction that does not have the same data protection laws as your jurisdiction.

While we will attempt to maintain the confidentiality of all PII submitted, we disclaim any liability for any claims or damages related to any unauthorized disclosure or interception of any information that you submit to us. For your own privacy and protection, we encourage you not to include sensitive personal information in any emails you send to us.

Persons under the age of 13

Our Service is not designed for those younger than 13. If you are under the age of 13, please do not use our Service and please do not send us any information about yourself. If we discover that we have collected any PII from a child under the age of 13, we will remove that information from our records.
