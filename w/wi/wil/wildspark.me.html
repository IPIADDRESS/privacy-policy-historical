What Information We Collect

We collect several types of information and may use each kind differently as we will detail below:

Information You Submit to Us

When you interact with our services, we collect the information that you choose to share with us. For example, when you set up an account, we need to collect a few important details about you, such as: a unique username, a password, an email address, a phone number, date of birth, etc. We may decide to collect additional (or less) information in the future. Other services may require you to provide us with a debit or credit card number and its associated account information.

Additional information might be collected when you contact our support or communicate with us in any other way.

Information We Get When You Use Our Services

When you use our services, we collect information about which of those services you’ve used and how you’ve used them, which pages you visited, etc.

Here’s a more detailed explanation of the types of information we may collect, now or in the future:

  1. information about your activity through our services (e.g. access times, pages viewed, IP address, , etc.)
  2. information about how you interact with other users.
  3. information about any content you choose to provide us, or share through our services and the use that other users are doing with such content.
  4. device-specific information, such as the hardware model, operating system version, advertising identifier, unique application identifiers, unique device identifiers, browser type, language, wireless network, and mobile network information (including the mobile phone number).
  5. information about your location.
  6. information collected by cookies and other technologies (please see our Cookies Usage Policy).

Information We Collect from Third Parties

We may collect information that other users provide about you when they use our services. For example, if another user allows us to collect information from their device phonebook—and you’re one of that user’s contacts—we may combine the information we collect from that user’s phonebook with other information we have collected about you. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for several purposes, including for operating our services, improving and personalizing them, securing them and providing you with better services. We also use the information we collect to communicate with you, respond to inquiries or share information about our services. Another use is protecting you and your account in order to prevent fraud or other unauthorized or illegal activity.

How We Share the Information We collect

We may share information about you with other users (by allowing users to view you profile, follow your activity, etc.), with our partners, affiliates, service providers and with the public. Please note that we will never share personal identifiable information with others without your consent other than for legal purposes (such as in case of a court order, under any applicable law, in a dispute between us, for protecting our other users, etc.).
