Privacy Policy




WCCF/WCCFTECH has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to personal privacy. The following discloses WCCF/WCCFTECH’s information gathering and dissemination practices for following websites: www.WCCFTECH.com, www.WCCFWORLD.com.

Log files

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site, in other words for normal web statistics. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

Cookies
We use cookies to:

  1. Count return visits to our site.
  2. Reporting anonymous statistical information on Web-site and advertisement usage.
  3. Enabling automatic logins.
  4. Personalize the website and to deliver content specific to your interests.
  5. Enable Google analyzer to monitor traffic to our website.
  6. By using this website, you agree that both the WCCFTech.com webserver, and any trusted 3rd party we chose, such as advertising networks, may place a tracking cookie on your machine and access it at any time during your session.

Cookies are linked to a user account. If no user account exists, cookies are not used.

Personally Identifiable Information

Any personally identifiable information collected without limitation throughout the website, public forum areas of the web site, may be used for WCCF/WCCFTECH marketing and online services. No personally identifiable information is sold, or handed over to third parties in a form that enables identification of any single user of the web site.

Public Forums Disclaimer

This site makes forums available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.

Contests

We may run contests on our site in which we ask our users for contact information (like their email address). We use the customer contact information from the entry form to send the user information about our company and its products, or to contact them when it becomes necessary (notices like, updates, patches, etc.). Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings (see the choice/opt-out section below).

Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.

Choice/Opt-Out

This site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database so that they no longer receive future communications or access to our other services:

  • You can send email to admin[at]wccftech[dot]com stating your request.
  • You can reply to any unwanted communications that you receive, stating your request.
  • You can use the Profile Manager to change/remove your information and to opt out of any promotional / newsletter material.

Correct/Update

If your e-mail address changes or if you want to remove your data, please email our web administration at admin[at]wccftech[dot]com for any corrections or updates.

Product Give-Away Contest Rules

WCCF/WCCFTECH may choose from time to time to give away prizes by random drawing. The method or methods for entering such contests will be communicated on the Web Site. If you’re selected as a winner, you will be notified by email at the address you provided. You will be asked to acknowledge a “Release”, and depending on the Prize, you may be asked to provide a mailing address, so that your prize can be delivered.

Notifications of Changes

If there are any changes to this policy, we will give notice about it on our Homepage. If we decide to change the way we are using personally identifiable information, we will notify the users by email. The users will have a choice as to whether or not we use their information in this different manner. We will use information in accordance with the privacy policy under which the information was collected.

Legal Disclaimer

Though we make every effort to preserve user privacy, we may need to disclose personal information when required by law wherein we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a current judicial proceeding, a court order or legal process served on our Web site.

Contacting the Web Site If you have any questions or comments about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web Site, you can contact admin[at]wccftech[dot]com.
