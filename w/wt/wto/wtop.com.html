We gather this information in a variety of ways, such as when you email us, fill out a user profile, register on our web or mobile app stream, or enter a sweepstakes or contest. We may also ask you to create a personal password when you register for one of our products or services. We also collect certain non-personally identifiable information whenever you visit our website, such as the type of browser you are using (for example: Chrome, Safari, etc.), the type of operating system you are using (for example: Windows or Mac OS), the domain name from which you receive Internet access, and aggregate information regarding what pages you access or visit.

Cookies and Other Technical Information

We send “cookies” to your computer, mobile device, or tablet primarily to enhance your online experience and to store visitor preferences, record session information, record user-specific information on what pages users access or visit, alert visitors to new areas that we think might be of interest to them when they return to our Platforms, record past activity on our Platforms in order to provide better service when visitors return to our Platforms, ensure that visitors are not repeatedly sent the same display advertising, customize content based on visitors’ browser type or other information that the visitor sends. Cookies are files that can identify you as a unique customer and store your personal preferences as well as technical information (including click through and click stream data). Cookies can either be permanent (that is, they remain on your computer until you delete them) or temporary (that is, they last only until you close your browser).

We may also use “Web beacons” that monitor your use of our Platforms. Web beacons are small strings of code that provide a method for delivering a display advertisement on a webpage for the purpose of transferring data, such as the IP (Internet Protocol) address of the device that downloaded the page on which the Web beacon appears, the URL (Uniform Resource Locator) of the page on which the Web beacon appears, the time the page containing the Web beacon was viewed, the types of browser that fetched the Web beacon and the identification number of any cookie on the device previously placed by that server. When corresponding with you via HTML-capable e-mail, Web beacons let us know whether you received and opened our e-mail.

We also use Remarketing with Google to advertise online. Third-party vendors, including Google, may show you our ads on websites across the Internet. We and third-party vendors, including Google, use first-party cookies (such as the Google cookie) and third-party cookies (such as the DoubleClick cookie) together to inform, optimize, and serve ads based on someone’s past visits to our Platforms.

If you have installed an app on your mobile device or tablet and you wish to stop receiving push notifications, you can change the settings either on your device or through the app.

If you choose to furnish one of our Platforms with personally identifiable information, this information can be stored in or linked to other data stored in the cookies/Web beacons. By accepting this Privacy Policy, you specifically agree to our use of cookies and Web beacons as described herein.

You may adjust your browser to reject cookies from us or from any other website. Additionally, by setting your Web browser to display HTML e-mails as text only, you may be able to prevent the use of some Web beacons. Please consult the “Help” section of your browser for more information. However, there is certain information on our Platforms that can only be accessed in conjunction with cookies or similar devices and you should be aware that disabling cookies or similar devices might prevent you from accessing some of our content or otherwise impair, prevent, or diminish certain functionality of our Platforms.

Although we take appropriate measures to safeguard against unauthorized disclosures of information, we cannot assure you that personally identifiable information that we collect will never be disclosed in a manner that is inconsistent with this Privacy Policy.
