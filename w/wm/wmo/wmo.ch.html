Privacy policy

By accessing this site, certain information about the user, such as Internet protocol (IP) addresses, navigation through the ste, the software used and the time spent, along with other similar information, might be stored on WMO servers.

These will not specifically identify the user. The information will be used internally for Website traffic analysis only.
