Privacy Policy

We feel very responsible in ensuring highest customer satisfaction and maintaining the security of your personal information.

It is therefore a matter of course for us to respect and take great care of your private information, in observance of the valid national and european data protection policies. Personal information is only recognized, collected and stored if this is legally permitted.

Personal Information

Personal information is information about your identity e.g. name, address and telephone number. Information such as your favorite cooking recipe is not relevant, as there is no direct relation to your identity.

We assure you that your data will be handled on a confidential basis, will not be passed onto third parties and will not be used for spam (unwanted electronic junk mail or junk newsgroup postings).

 Website

The information contained on our website has been compiled with the greatest care and you can use this without having to reveal any personal information. For example, if you register online, purchase online, or confirm a subscription, then we will ask you for your name and other personal information. It is, however your decision to input this information.

WMF Americas, Inc. takes great care in maintaining the security of your personal information and your data is saved on a extra secure server. Access to this server is only permitted to authorised people, who take care of technical matters.

 Children

WMF Americas, Inc. does not knowingly collect, use or distribute personally identifiable information from children or persons under the age of 13. Our information collection, use and dissemination practices are structured such that no person under the age of 13 can provide WMF Americas, Inc. with personally identifiable information. If you are under the age of 13, please do not use this web site or attempt to provide any personally identifiable information to WMF Americas, Inc.

 Links

Our website may contain links to third party websites. WMF Americas, Inc. does not have any influence on the contents of such links. As far as technically possible links containing illegal content will be deleted from our website.

Website contents

The contents of our website are copyrighted. However the contents may be used on other websites if prior permission is obtained from WMF Americas, Inc.

 Cookies

We may track your visit to our site by giving your web browser an "HTML cookie" when you enter the web site. Cookies are pieces of information that websites transfer to your computer's web browser for record-keeping purposes. WMF Americas, Inc. uses cookies to make visiting our sites easier. Cookies allow us to save preferences for you so that you won't have to re-enter them the next time you visit. Cookies help us to collect anonymous click-stream data for tracking user trends and patterns. The use of cookies is now an industry standard, and you'll find them used on most major websites. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies. If you prefer, you can reset your browser either to notify you when you've received a cookie or to refuse to accept cookies. You should understand that areas of certain sites will not function properly if you set your browser not to accept cookies.
