What types of personal information do we collect?
We collect and store personal information that you provide to us when you shop online, request information or contact customer service by telephone or e-mail. This information is used to process orders, suggest products or services that may be of interest to you and to provide services that will give you the best shopping experience while using our retail website. Personal information may include, but is not limited to the following:
  • Online Purchases - When you make an online purchase, it is necessary to give us your name, e-mail address, shipping address, billing address and credit card information to complete your purchase. If you create an online account, the same type of information is collected and stored as a convenience to expediting your shopping process.
  • Catalog Requests - If you contact us for a free catalog or request information about other products or services, we need your name and mailing address in order to mail that information to you. We may also ask for a phone number or an e-mail address as a method of contacting you if there is a problem with sending the catalog or requested information out to you. To stop receiving catalogs, please call 1-800-603-4383.
  • E-mail Offers - When you sign up for our e-mail offers we store your e-mail address and will notify you of exclusive customer offers and opportunities for additional savings on select products. You have the option to unsubscribe at any time..
  • Customer Service - Customers who contact our customer service center, may be asked to provide personally identifying information so that we can create an account or reference your account in order to best assist you with your call.

What types of non-personal information do we collect?
Unlike the personally identifying information that you provide to us so that you are able to shop online, receive catalogs, or contact us with questions about your order, we also collect and store information that is non-personal in nature by using cookies and other technologies.

What are cookies?
Cookies are small pieces of data that are sent by our web server and stored on your web browser once you access our website. Cookies help track website activity and process you through our website by storing information such as visitor preferences, pages accessed, etc. If you come to our website via one of our email campaigns, we do store your email address in our "cookie"; otherwise, we do not store any other personally identifying information. Cookies help us to remember your information so that you don't have to reenter it every time you visit our websites.

If you do not want to receive cookies, you can disable them in your browser preferences; however, you will not be able to enjoy the full capabilities of our website. We suggest placing your order and then deleting the cookies once you have finished your shopping or browsing session.

 How do we protect your information?
Protecting your information is our top priority. Your information is secured by our use of a secure server (SSL) when you place your order. Your information is encrypted by the software before it is sent to us. All access to this information is limited and protected against unauthorized use. Our secure site is recognized and certified by CyberTrust, a leading computer network security company.

 How do we use the information that we collect?
Our goal is to provide you with the most efficient, hassle-free shopping experience possible. We may use the information that you provide us:
  • To create account information, user friendly shopping tools and other site features that will give you a more personalized shopping experience;
  • To enable you to make online purchases;
  • To send you catalogs, direct mailings and requested product and service information in the mail;
  • To notify you by mail or e-mail of special promotions, upcoming events or new products or services;
  • To provide our authorized third parties and affiliates with your information when deemed necessary.
