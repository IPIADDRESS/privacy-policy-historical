Privacy Policy

We record any data entered into our website forms and may use this information to contact you through email or telephone. This information is never sold or passed on to any third-party. JunoWebDesign.com is also part of ‘Real Web SEO’ (RealWebSEO.com) and ‘JPR Marketing’ (JPRMarketing.com) which are based in the same premises and are not third-parties; these companies may also contact you through any information submitted to forms.

We use standard ‘Google Analytics’ tracking script to record visitors behaviour on our website such as page-views, search queries and the time spent on each page. This collected information is anonymous and is just used to improve our website content, design and optimisation.

Information Gathering and Use

Any data submitted through contact forms can be used to make contact through either the email address or telephone number.
There is no guarantee that Juno will contact you after you request a quote or any other query.

Cookies

Cookies contain small amounts of data, typically an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site server and stored on your computer’s hard drive.
Juno Web Design uses cookies to store useful information, most websites use cookies, to read more visit: http://www.allaboutcookies.org.
