Privacy

Juniper Networks respects and protects the privacy of visitors to our website. Juniper Networks does not collect personal information from a visitor to our site unless the visitor intentionally provides it or otherwise provides express consent. When accessing certain documents or services on our website, visitors may be asked to register, provide contact information, and depending on what they are accessing, provide additional information, such as where they work and what they do. Juniper Networks collects this information in an effort to better meet the needs of its customers and answer questions of, and provide information and services to, visitors to its website. The information is used solely by Juniper Networks, its affiliates, and those companies contracted by Juniper Networks to deliver requested information and services. We do not sell information to third parties.

Information Collected

Juniper Networks collects visitor information in various areas of its website, including the following:

  • Viewing of product demonstrations
  • Accessing of white papers (Note: once you have registered for a white paper or tool, you may visit at any time using your password and user ID.)
  • Participation in marketing promotions
  • Participation in seminars or events
  • Career opportunities
  • User surveys
  • Use of "Locate Channel Partner" page
  • Secured Partner sites
  • Registration of products

Ability to Opt-Out

Juniper Networks provides visitors who elect to receive electronic or other communications the ability to opt-out of those communications by sending us a notice to unsubscribe. Juniper Networks respects your wishes and will delete you from mailing or other types of lists.

Changes

Juniper Networks may amend this policy from time to time. If we make significant changes that affect the way we use your personal information, we will notify you by posting a revised statement on this site.
