JustPlay Privacy Policy

 

Last updated: 1 May 2009

 

Digital Activators cc owns and operates JustPlay - a South African prize draw website that is governed by the Privacy Policy Statement described below as well as the JustPlay Terms and Conditions Statement.

 

Your Privacy
JustPlay is 100% committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect about you to enable you to enjoy the prize draws on the website, to provide support for our product and - with your express consent - to support sponsor promotions and market research.

 

What information do we collect? How do we use it?
When you register we ask for your name, email address, mobile number, prize preference categories and South African identity number. We use this information to tailor your online experience and to manage the prize draws. We email winners to let them know they have won and we ask them to show us their SA ID to ensure we are delivering the prize to the right person.

When you complete sponsor questionnaires as part of your prize draw entry, you may choose - but are not obliged - to provide information about your lifestyle and your preferences to participate in sponsor promotions or market research. If you consent to sharing information with sponsors they may use this information to provide you with product or service related offers or they may aggregate the information for the purposes of market research.

 

Will JustPlay disclose the information it collect to third parties?
In order to enter a prize draw on JustPlay, you complete a questionnaire. Most questionnaires are sponsored by a third party in order to conduct a promotion or market research. By giving a positive response on the sponsor's questionnaire you give permission for JustPlay to share your information with that sponsor/client in order for the sponsor/client to contact you or send you an offer: information about products and/or services that may be of interest to you. You can accept or decline the sponsor's offer on each questionnaire.

 

How does JustPlay protect member information?
We use a Secure Server for collecting personal information. The secure server layer [SSL] encrypts all the information you enter before it's transmitted over the internet and sent to us. Furthermore, all of the member data we collect is protected against unauthorised access.

 

What about cookies?
"Cookies" are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. Whenever you log in to JustPlay we store your unique ID [not your SA ID] and the time you signed in, in an encrypted session cookie on your hard drive. When you log out of JustPlay, these session cookes are expired. The functionality of JustPlay depends upon your browser accepting session cookies.

JustPlay tracks visitors to and on the Just Play website by using referrer tracking and we use click through and open mail tracking when sending emails.

 

Your Consent
By using our website, you consent to the collection and use of the information as described above by JustPlay. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it. We'll also revise the "last updated" date at the top and bottom of the Privacy Statement.

 

External Sites and Emails
JustPlay provides links to other sites from our website and our emails. The privacy policies of these linked sites are the responsibility of the linked site and Just Play has no control or influence over their policies. Please check the policies of each site you visit for specific information. JustPlay cannot be held liable for damage or misdoings of other sites linked or otherwise.

JustPlay Terms and Conditions

Last updated: 1 May 2009

 

Using JustPlay
Digital Activators cc owns and operates JustPlay - a South African prize draw website that is governed by the Terms and Conditions described below as well as the Just Play Privacy Policy

 

In addition to these terms and conditions there may be special conditions associated with individual prize draws - you'll find a link describing these on each prize.

 

Eligibility
JustPlay is open to individuals who have a South African identity number, who are 18 years or older and are currently living in South Africa.

 

Registration and Activation

  • You can enter prize draws on JustPlay if you become a member with an activated account.
  • To become a member, you need to successfully register on JustPlay and then activate your account by clicking on the link in an email that will automatically be sent to the email address you provided in the registration form.

 

Member Privacy Protection
You are protected by the JustPlay Privacy Policy. This terms and conditions statement includes the terms of the Privacy Policy published on the JustPlay website and updated from time to time.

 

Entry into Prize Draws

  • You enter prize draws by logging in to your account and clicking on the prize draw of your choice and then completing a questionnaire.
  • You receive an entry for every completed questionnaire.
  • You may enter prize draws as long as there are questionnaires available for your profile.
  • You may enter any prize draw mulitple times unless otherwise stipulated.
  • Participating in the prize draws is free unless you choose to enter via SMS.
  • You may receive bonus entries when you participate in the bonus scheme outlined on the website. You are responsible for allocating bonus entries to prize draws.
  • Prize draws may be promoted on several different websites simultaneously.
  • You are not permitted to participate in the prize draws if your are employed by Digital Activators or our promotions agencies [or are in the immediate family of employees or agencies].

 

Referral Service

  • If you choose to enter our Refer-a-Friend prize draws or participate in any other friend referral service, we will ask for your friend's email address only and send them a one-time-only email inviting them to the JustPlay website
  • We do not retain your friend's email on the JustPlay database and they will receive no further communications from JustPlay unless they agree to sign up as a Just Play member

 

Questionnaire Sponsor Offers

  • You complete a questionnaire in order to enter a prize draw.
  • Each questionnaire on JustPlay is sponsored by a client either conducting a promotion campaign or market research. By giving a positive response on the sponsor's questionnaire you give permission for that sponsor/client to contact you or send you an offer: information about products and/or services that may be of interest to you. You can accept or decline the sponsor's offer on each questionnaire.

 

Prize Guarantee, Availability and Quality

  • All prizes are guaranteed to be drawn on the stated prize draw date and delivered to the verified prize winner within the delivery period if the winner complies with the verification and claim process outlined below.
  • All prizes are subject to availability and should any prize be unavailable, a prize of similar price or specification/quality will be substituted
  • Prizes are as stated but in some circumstances a cash equivalent will be offered where stated in the individual prize draw special terms and conditions.

 

Prize Draw Winner Selection and Notification

  • The winner for each prize draw is selected by computer program at random from all entries received for that prize draw at 00h00 on the stated prize draw date.
  • Winners are notified by email only. You are responsible for keeping your email address up to date on the JustPlay system to enable prompt notification should you be chosen as the winner of any prize draw.
  • Winners' names are immediately posted on the JustPlay website on the Winners' List. You'll be able to track prize claim progress on this page on the site.
  • Prizes must be claimed within 40 days otherwise the winner will forfeit the prize and a new winner will be chosen from the original entries for that prize draw. The new winner will be posted on the website.
  • The winner is responsible for contacting JustPlay to claim their prize.

 

Prize Draw Winner Verification

  • All winners must verify their identity by sending us a copy of their South African identity document.
  • Winners are also obliged to provide an Affidavit to confirm that they are not related to any employee of JustPlay or our advertising and promotions agencies and are thus eligible as a winner.

 

Prize Delivery

  • JustPlay will arrange the delivery of the prize as soon as the winner has been verified and advice the winner of the delivery date at that time.
  • Prizes will be delivered to the physical address provided by the winner.
  • Prizes can only be delivered to the winner and not a representative.

 

Closing your Account

  • Should you wish to close your account, you may do so at any time by logging in and clicking on "My Profile" link and then click on the "Close my Account" button.
  • You'll no longer receive any further communications from JustPlay when you close your account.
  • You'll only receive communications from sponsors to whom you have given your permission to contact you. You'll need to unsubscribe from sponsor communications programs directly with each sponsor.
  • If you have any prize entries for active prize draws after the closure of your account these will be excluded from those prize draws.
  • You may re-open your account at any time using the same login. If you do so, your entries will be re-instated from the date your account is re-opened.
