Privacy Policy

Just Tweet It completely respects your privacy. You can always visit justtweetit.com without having to tell us who you are or reveal any personal information about yourself. It is the intent of Just Tweet It to always let you know before we collect any such personal information on our website. If you choose to give us your personal information we will not use your information for anything other than intended purposes. If you tell us that you do not wish to have this information used as a basis for further contact with you, we will respect those wishes as well. Information collected by Just Tweet It is used within our company by authorized Just Tweet It employees only. We absolutely DO NOT sell, rent, loan, or transfer any personal information regarding our visitors to any unrelated third parties. We appreciate your visiting our site and thank you for your trust.
