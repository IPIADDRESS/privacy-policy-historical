Privacy Policy

This policy covers how LuxeMont LLC, and their Web property JustLuxe.com, treats personal information that we collect and receive, including information related to your past use of LuxeMont products and services. Personal information includes your name, mailing address, phone number, e-mail address, and any other information that is not otherwise publicly available. This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that JustLuxe does not own or control or to people that JustLuxe does not employ or manage.

Information Collection and Use

JustLuxe collects personal information when you subscribe to our free regular e-mail service, when you use JustLuxe products or services, when you visit JustLuxe web pages or the web pages of certain JustLuxe partners, when you sign up for the monthly newsletter, and when you enter promotions and giveaways. JustLuxe may combine information that we have about you with information that we obtain from business partners and other companies. JustLuxe may collect information about your transactions with us and with some of our business partners, including information about your use of financial products and services that we offer. JustLuxe automatically receives and records information that our server logs from your browser, including your IP address. JustLuxe uses information for the following general purposes: to customize advertising and content you see, to fulfill your requests for products and services, to improve our services, to contact you, to conduct research and to provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.

We allow third-party companies to serve ads and/or collect certain anonymous information when you visit our web site. These companies may use non-personally identifiable information (e.g., click stream information, browser type, time and date, subject of advertisements clicked or scrolled over) during your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services likely to be of interest to you. These companies typically use a cookie or third party web beacon to collect this information. To learn more about this behavioral advertising practice or to opt-out of this type of advertising, you can visit networkadvertising.org. To opt-out of any behavioral advertising practice, you can visit Network Advertising Initiative.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We provide information to trusted partners who work on the behalf of or with JustLuxe under strict confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your personal information to help JustLuxe communicate with you about offers from JustLuxe and our marketing partners. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information. We respond to subpoenas, court orders, and legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights to defend against legal claims. We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of JustLuxe’s terms and conditions, or as otherwise required by law. If JustLuxe is acquired by or merged with another company, we transfer information about you. In this event, JustLuxe will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

JustLuxe may set and access JustLuxe cookies on your computer in order to track you when you’re on our websites and to recognize your visits. JustLuxe may use web beacons to access JustLuxe cookies inside and outside of our network of websites and in connection with JustLuxe products and services.

Confidentiality and Security

We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe have a reasonable need to come in contact with that information to or in order to do their jobs. In certain areas, JustLuxe uses industry standard SSL encryption to protect date transmissions.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

JustLuxe may update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your email account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
