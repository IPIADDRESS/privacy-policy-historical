Privacy Commitment to our Members

  1. Commitment

    JustEngineers is committed to protecting the privacy of our members. We aim to provide a safe and secure environment in accordance with the principles of the data protection acts 1984 and 1998 and information provided is only used for the purposes described on our site. Personal information will only be placed on-line with your consent.

    Please be aware that the information on the net is available to people in countries who do not have adequate laws to protect the use of such data.

  2. Privacy of your CV

    JustEngineers Limited offers its services to you the Member and to Recruitment Agencies and to potential employers. We do not allow direct employers access to any personal or contact details apart from your email address.

    If you wish to retain a high level of privacy, we suggest you remove any confidential items from the body of your CV.

  3. Corrections and updates

    Corrections and updates or changing from hide to full details can be made on-line.

  4. Unsubscribing to JustEngineers Limited

    You may remove your CV details at any time by logging in to your personalised Members Home page and under the My Details section, click the 'Edit My Details' link. Then remove all the text inside the CV text box and click 'Update' button.

  5. Data Analysis

    In general we gather information about our members collectively such as areas of our site which have been visited most. This enables us to target areas for improvement.

    We do not disclose information to third parties about your individual visits to JustEngineers Limited, nor personal information which you provide such as, your name, address, telephone number or e-mail address.

    For further details please contact us on info@jobsgroup.net or call 0845 050 2000
