Privacy Policy

At JustOneCookbook.com, the privacy of my visitors is very important to me. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information that is received, collected and/or recorded by JustOneCookbook.com and how it is used.

If you require any further information or have any questions about the privacy policy below, please feel free to contact me by email at nami {at} justonecookbook {dot} com.

—————————————

Log Files

Like many other websites, JustOneCookbook.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, the type of web browser used, the Internet Service Provider (ISP) used, a date/time stamp of the visit(s), the referring/exit pages, and the length of the visit in order to analyze trends, administer the site, track users’ movement around the site, and gather demographic information, to be used in adapting the content of the site to better suit the visitors. IP addresses and other such gathered information is not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons

JustOneCookbook.com sometimes uses cookies to store information about visitor preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user accesses or visits, and to customize or dynamically adapt web-page content based on the visitors’ browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser, in order to provide the visitor with the best viewing experience.

Google Analytics

Like many other web sites, Just One Cookbook uses Google Analytics for web data analysis.  This include Google’s Interest-based advertising or 3rd-party audience data (such as age, gender and interests) with Google Analytics.

DoubleClick DART Cookie

  • Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on JustOneCookbook.com.
  • Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve targeted ads to users based on their visit to JustOneCookbook.com and other related websites.
  • Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy.

Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. Our advertising partners include but are not limited to:

  • BlogHer
  • iFood.tv
  • Google AdSense
  • Amazon

These third-party ad servers or ad networks send the advertisements and links that appear on JustOneCookbook.com directly to your browser. The advertising partners automatically receive your IP address when serving an ad to you. Other technologies (such as cookies, JavaScript or Web Beacons) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and/or to personalize the advertising content that is served to you.

JustOneCookbook.com has no access to, or control over, these cookies, Web Beacons or scripts that are used by third-party advertisers, nor control over which advertisements are shown.

You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices, as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices if so desired. JustOneCookbook.com’s privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, advertising partners or websites.

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.
