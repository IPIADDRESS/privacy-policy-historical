What are Cookies?

A cookie is a small information file that is sent to your computer and stored on your hard drive. If you have registered with us, then your computer will store an identifying cookie to save you time each time you come back to jeulia.com by remembering you. You can change the settings on your browser to prevent cookies being stored on your computer without your explicit consent.
