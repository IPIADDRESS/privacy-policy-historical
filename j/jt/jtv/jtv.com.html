Frequently Asked Questions


  1. What information do we collect online?
    How do we use it?

    a)Information you provide to us. Email. When you subscribe to Jewelry Television™ emails, you only have to provide your email address. If you should choose to purchase products, you will be required to register on the site (see Merchandise Orders.)

    Merchandise Orders. When you first place an order on our site, we ask you to provide personally identifiable information by filling out and submitting an online customer profile form. This information includes your name, mailing address, credit card billing address(es), shipping address(es), email address, telephone number, a password, credit card security code (will be requested at point of sale but will not be retained for our records), your credit card number and expiration date. We use this information to process and fulfill your order and to notify you of your order by email. When you return to the site and place another order, if you use your password or provide other personally identifiable information, we use this information to provide you with access to your customer and order information. We save the addresss(es) you provide for shipment, and when you place an order, we provide the address(es) to you as a selectable list for your convenience. We also ask you for personally identifiable information about other people to whom you are sending gifts. Jewelry Television™ uses this information only to ship your gifts, and, if you request it, to send each gift recipient an email notification of the gift.

    Our site users can choose to electronically forward an item link to someone else by clicking “tell a friend.” The user must provide their email address, as well as that of the recipient. This information is used only in the case of transmission errors and, of course, to let the recipient know who sent the email. The information is not used for any other purpose.

    Contests. When you enter a Jewelry Television™ contest, sweepstakes, or other promotional event, we collect information to administer the program and notify the winners. Typical information requested includes your name, address, telephone number, email address, and Jewelry Television™ account number. You may receive promotional emails from Jewelry Television™ as a result of entering the contest, but you always have the option of choosing not to receive additional communications.

    Surveys. Occasionally Jewelry Television™ conducts surveys to improve your customer experience. We may use the site to conduct surveys, or may contact you via email or phone. Surveys are anonymous and you always retain the option not to participate.

    Site Optimization. In order to assure your visit to JewelryTelevision.com is completely satisfactory, our system automatically identifies which browser you are using, the IP (Internet Provider) you used for access, and your Operating System. We use tracking information to determine which areas of our site users like and don’t like based on traffic to those areas. We do not track what individual users read, but rather how well each page performs overall. This helps us continue building a better site for you.

The cookie itself does not contain personal information although it will enable us to relate your use of this site to information that you have specifically and knowingly provided. The only personal information a cookie can contain is information you supply yourself. A cookie can’t read data off your hard disk or read cookie files created by other sites. Jewelry Television™ uses cookies to track user traffic patterns (as described above).

    You can refuse cookies by turning them off in your browser. If you’ve set your browser to warn you before accepting cookies, you will receive the warning message with each cookie. You do not need to have cookies turned on to use this site. However, you do need cookies to place an order or participate in an auction.

    Jewelry Television™ does use third party companies to store data and help us provide our products and services to you. These companies do not have direct access to your data and are contractually restricted from using your information in any manner. For more information, including how to opt out, please visit My Account to edit your preferences.
