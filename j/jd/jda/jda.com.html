Our Revenue Management Vision

Since acquiring its Revenue Management business in 2006, JDA has been working to define a next-generation revenue optimization vision. That vision is based on two key attributes: Our long and successful history of innovation in pricing and revenue management; and JDA’s portfolio of leading edge software modules that are widely installed and proven across multiple industries.

Our RM vision brings together innovative approaches to today’s challenges, and a leading technological advantage:

  • Price Sensitive Revenue Management (PSRM) is JDA’s response to a clear and growing trend in the travel industry. The way that people buy Cruise, Hospitality, Tour, Passenger Rail and other Travel products has changed. Prices are more transparent than ever before, and that calls for a different approach to Revenue Management. Learn more at: http://www.jda.com/solutions/price-sensitive-revenue-management.html
  • JDA® Enterprise Architecture enables users to synchronize multiple decisions using “One View of Demand”, maximizing flexibility and IT efficiency, and supporting competition in today’s expanding global markets. Learn more at: http://www.jda.com/solutions/enterprise-architecture.html
