Privacy Policy

1. Preamble

Reliance Payment Solutions Limited ("RPSL") offers JioMoney account ("JioMoney") under the authorization received from Reserve Bank of India under the Payment and Settlement Systems Act, 2007 and Rules/Regulations thereunder. This document details about the manner we collect, store and use your Personal Information and its security.

2. Personal Information

During course of your association with us, we collect and store your Personal Information. Personal Information shall mean information that could reasonably be used to identify you personally. This includes, but not limited to the following:

  1. Information which you may provide during the registration process such as Name, Phone Number, Email Address, Communication or Permanent Address;
  2. Photograph and/or any other personal document provided during registration or during the profile update such as proof of identity, proof of address;
  3. Usage logs or transactions performed which can identify you as an individual through your customer ID or other relevant unique identifier;
  4. IP Addresses;
  5. Media Access Control address;
  6. Device specific information or identifiers such as IMEI, OS specific identifiers;
  7. Usage logs/Server logs/Cookies having Personal Information;
  8. Any other sensitive personal data or information, such as - Passwords, Financial information, such as details of bank account, credit card, debit card, or other payment instrument details, financial transactions etc.

This information can be in electronic or physical format.

Any comments, messages, feedback, complaints blogs, scribbles etc. posted /uploaded /conveyed /communicated by you on the public sections of the application becomes published content and is not considered Personal Information subject to this Policy.

3. Use of Personal Information

We value the trust you place in us, and hence insist upon the highest level of protection for securing your Personal Information with us. RPSL may use your personal information for:

  1. Verification of your identity, access, privileges assigned in relation to your JioMoney account;
  2. Provisioning of products/services, testing or improvement of services, recommending various products or services, including that of affiliates of RPSL;
  3. Processing your requests, transactions, enquiries and complaints, customer services and related activities;

  7. Ensuring adherence to legal and regulatory requirements for prevention and detection of frauds and crimes etc.
