privacy policy

SKYSPORT&SPA respects your privacy.

At SKYSPORT&SPA we are committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect about our site visitors to provide a more personalized and relevant experience. Please read the following details about our privacy policy:

What do we collect?
SKYSPORT&SPA collects the domain name of each visitor to our site. Aggregated information, such as which pages users visit, or access and information that is volunteered by users, such as survey information and/or site registrations is also collected. E-mail addresses are collected only from those who first communicate with SKYSPORT&SPA via e-mail or proactively provide their e-mail address to SKYSPORT&SPA. We do not sell E-mail address that we collect to a 3rd party.

What about cookies?
"Cookies" are bits of information that are captured and stored by your web browser on your computer's hard drive. SKYSPORT&SPA does not use cookies at the present time.

How is this information used?
The information we collect is used to improve the content and layout of our web site. It may be used to customize the content and/or layout of our page for each individual user, to notify consumers about updates to our web site, and used by us to contact consumers for marketing purposes. please feel free to contact us at any time with any questions. We reserve the right to change our privacy policy at any time.
