Privacy Policy

This privacy page explains what information this website collects in order to provide services to:

Visitors to our website (s)

People who use our services, such as newsletter subscriptions or purchasing themes and plugins.

Users of our support forums and Premium Membership Options

Website visitors

When someone visits Jigoshop.com we collect standard internet log information and details of visitor behaviour patterns, which is collected in a way which does not identify anyone, and we do not associate any data gathered with any personally identifying information from any source. Any personal information collection will be clearly marked and will explain our purpose in collecting it.  We do not store credit card details nor do we share customer details with any 3rd parties”

Cookies used by Jigoshop.com

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer by websites that you visit. They are widely used in order to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

Google Analytics  (_utma, _utmb, _utmc, _utmv, _utmz). Cookies which collect anonymous data about how visitors use our site. Visit:  http://www.google.co.uk/intl/en/analytics/privacyoverview.html for more information.

WordPress session cookies: (PHPSESSID) These are required to enable WordPress to function.

Youtube cookies: We embed Youtube videos from our own and other channels which may set cookies once you click on the Youtube video player. Visit:  http://www.google.com/support/youtube/bin/answer.py?hl=en-GB&answer=171780 for more information.

Email newsletter subscriptions

Personal information supplied as part of the sign-up process to subscribe to the Jigoshop weekly email newsletter will only be used to provide the requested email communications, and will be stored by Campaign Monitor, our email service provider.

Purchase information

Personal data supplied during the purchase process will be used by our payment providers (Paypal and Sagepay) to facilitate payment. This includes Support packages, which will also store user data to enable access to our support forums and services.
