Privacy Policy

Who we are

This is the blog of marketing mentor, Jim Connolly.

The personal data I collect and why

Contact forms: The only personal information collected here, is the information you send me via our contact forms.

If for any reason you have used a contact form on this site and would like me to delete your form data, just request it here and it will be deleted immediately.

Jim’s Marketing Blog is protected by internationally respected security experts, Sucuri. They have a strong firewall, between the server where the blog (and all it’s data) is, and anyone accessing the site. No personally identifiable form data or metadata is retained locally on our devices; it’s exclusively online, behind Sucuri’s world-class security.

Cookies

Because this site doesn’t have a comments section or serve advertising, we have very limited use of cookies. This is our cookie policy, in full.

Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics to measure activity on the site. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Who we share your data with

No one.

How long we retain your data

If you use a contact form, the form content and metadata is retained for 1 year. You can ask for your data to be deleted at any time and requests will be dealt with immediately.

What rights you have over your data

Unless you have sent me a message via a contact form, this site collects no personally identifiable information on you. If for any reason you have used a contact form on this site and would like me to delete it (no reason required), just request it here and it will be deleted immediately.

What data breach procedures we have in place

Jim’s Marketing Blog is protected from data breaches by internationally respected security experts, Sucuri. They have a strong firewall, between the server where the blog (and all it’s data) is, and anyone accessing this site. No personally identifiable information is retained locally on our devices; it’s exclusively online, behind Sucuri’s security.
