Jimmy Jazz takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.

INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE


General

Jimmy Jazz collects personal information when you register with JimmyJazz.com and Jimmy Jazz stores, when you buy or return to JimmyJazz.com and/or Jimmy Jazz stores, when you visit Jimmy Jazz pages or the pages of certain Jimmy Jazz partners, and when you enter promotions or sweepstakes. Jimmy Jazz may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies.


When you register we ask for information such as your name, email address, birth date, gender, ZIP code, occupation, industry, and personal interests. When you register with Jimmy Jazz and sign in to our website, you are not anonymous to us.


Jimmy Jazz collects information about your transactions with us and with some of our business partners, including information about your navigation through our site.
Jimmy Jazz automatically receives and records information from your computer and browser, including your IP address, Jimmy Jazz cookie information, software and hardware attributes, and the page you request.


Jimmy Jazz uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.
