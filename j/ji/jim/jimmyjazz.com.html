Privacy Policy

 

This privacy notice discloses the privacy practices for www.JimmyJazz.com.  Jimmy Jazz takes your privacy seriously.  This notice applies solely to information collected by this web site.  It will notify you of the following:

1.       What personally identifiable information is collected from you through the web site, how it is used and with whom it may be shared.

2.     What choices are available to you regarding the use of your data.

3.     The security procedures in place to protect the misuse of your information. 

4.     How you can correct any inaccuracies in the information.

Information Collection, Use, and Sharing

We are the sole owners of the information collected on this site. We only have access to/collect information that you voluntarily give us via email or other direct contact from you.  We will not sell or rent this information to anyone.

 

Jimmy Jazz collects personal information when you register with JimmyJazz.com and Jimmy Jazz stores, when you buy or return to JimmyJazz.com and/or Jimmy Jazz stores, when you visit Jimmy Jazz pages or the pages of certain Jimmy Jazz partners, and when you enter promotions or sweepstakes. Jimmy Jazz may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies.

 

When you register we ask for information such as your name, email address, birth date, gender, ZIP code, occupation, industry, and personal interests. When you register with Jimmy Jazz and sign in to our website, you are not anonymous to us.

 

Jimmy Jazz collects information about your transactions with us and with some of our business partners, including information about your navigation through our site. Jimmy Jazz automatically receives and records information from your computer and browser, including your IP address, Jimmy Jazz cookie information, software and hardware attributes, and the page you request.

 

Jimmy Jazz uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.

 

We will use your information to respond to you, regarding the reason you contacted us.  We will not share your information with any third party outside of our organization, other than as necessary to fulfill your request, e.g. to ship an order.

 

Unless you ask us not to, we may contact you via email in the future to tell you about specials, new products or services, or changes to this privacy policy.

 

Your Access to and Control Over Information

You may opt out of any future contacts from us at any time.  You can do the following at any time by contacting us via the email address or phone number given on our website:

 

-          See what data we have about you, if any.

-          Change/correct any data we have about you.

-          Have us delete any data we have about you.

-          Express any concern you have about our use of your data.

 

Security

We take precautions to protect your information.  When you submit sensitive information via the website, your information is protected both online and offline.

 

The federal government and technology industry have developed practical tips (http://www.onguardonline.gov/) to help you guard against Internet fraud, secure your computer and protect your personal information.

 

Wherever we collect sensitive information (such as credit card data), that information is encrypted and transmitted to us in a secure way.  You can verify this by looking for the “Verified and Secured” badge located in the website footer, or looking for "https" at the beginning of the address of the web page.

 

While we use encryption to protect sensitive information transmitted online, we also protect your information offline. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job (for example, billing or customer service) are granted access to personally identifiable information.  The computers/servers in which we store personally identifiable information are kept in a secure environment.

 

Privacy Questions

If you have any questions or concerns about the Jimmy Jazz’s Privacy Policy or data processing, please contact us.  Jimmy Jazz may update its Privacy Policy from time to time. When we change the policy in a material way, a notice will be posted on our website along with the updated Privacy Policy.
