The J Raines headquarters issued a notice that, in view of the strengthened regulations of the People’s Bank of China on virtual currencies and control over the bank payment and receipt business at the end of the year and traditional Chinese New Year would cause inconvenience to a third party’s payment and receipt, in order not to affect the market development, the business division hereby makes the following adjustments: 1. Raines China will shuts down China UnionPay payment and receipt business temporarily and use free dealer trading, i.e., to open EP trades. The business division will reopen policy in the right environment according to the changes of the central bank policy. 2. Dealers can also choose Perfect Money trading. Given that Perfect Money belong to foreign countries, in order to allow dealers to withdraw cash safely in time, PM withdrawal per deal should not exceed $800. The same Perfect Money account and IP should not be submitted repeatedly. The withdrawal cycle is 7 to 15 days. 3. As to the entrusting problem of Lei Gold Coins suggested by dealers, the Chinese business division will adjust accordingly! In order to make the market sustain for a long term, the business division will perfect and adjust the problem according to the market changes in a timely manner, to ensure J Raines undertakings develop stably and sustainably. 4. Conference that directors should open incentive accounts, the business division will implement this incentive within 2 working days. 5. After reopening top-up and withdrawal business, in order to strengthen the supervision of market, the company will close direct top-up of dealers and top-up orders should be reported by regional directors. 6. After the Lantern Festival, mainland directors will set up a director committee. The business division will fund and establish a committee office. 7. The first batch of the Second-generation Wormhole Yuan is expected to market in the best season in Chinese mainland— spring and realize docking with the Second-generation Lei Gold Coin mall. All regional directors and deals are supposed to cooperate with the business division and copy the above notice terms carefully! Please accept our apology if there is any inconvenience!
