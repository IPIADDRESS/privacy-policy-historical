(b)    This is data that identifies you or can be used to identify, contact you or process applications on your behalf and may include your name, address, email address, detailed financial information, user IP addresses in circumstances where they have not been deleted, clipped or anonymised, telephone number, and billing and credit history information. 8. Jobbuddy Profile
 
Jobbuddy provides registered members with the option of creating a personal profile. A profile includes your CV, employment and educational history, contact details, minimum salary expectation and the classifications and locations ('Profile') registered members would like to work in. Registered members' profiles can be seen by Jobbuddy clients who place job ads in matching classifications and locations. Members can of course choose to hide their profile at any time. Registered members' Profiles will remain  anonymous until the Jobbuddy client purchases access to a registered members CV and contact details. Jobbuddy provides registered members with the following privacy options in relation to the access allowed to their Profile:
 
Provide resume and contact details immediately
 
When a Jobbuddy client purchases a registered member’s Profile that resume and the contact details in that resume will be emailed immediately to the relevant JOBBUDDY client.
 
Hide or delete Profile
 
Registered members can elect to hide their Profile, so JOBBUDDY clients cannot see Private Profile at all, whilst allowing the Profile to be stored in the My Account area of the site. Alternatively Registered Members can elect to delete their Profile. Deleting a Profile from the My Account area of the site will mean it is no longer accessible to the registered member. Please note that deleted Profiles may still be stored by JOBBUDDY in testing or backup systems for a period of time.
 
All information provided when creating a Profile including name, resume, email address, telephone numbers, employment and educational history are used for the purposes of facilitating registered members job seeking efforts.
 
Registered Members can modify, hide or delete their resume at any time.
 
 
JOBBUDDY reserves the right to disable or delete a Profile at their sole discretion.

 15. Cookie Policy
 
To give you the best possible job search experience, this site uses cookies. Continuing to use Jobuddy.ie means you agree to our use of cookies. This document (anchorlink to cookie policy )   explains “what cookies are” and the types of cookies Jobbuddy  uses on our  websites.
 
What are cookies?
 
A "cookie" is a text file that websites send to a visitor's computer or other Internet-connected device to uniquely identify the visitor's browser or to store information or settings in the browser
 
Does Jobbuddy.ie use cookies on its websites?
 
Yes. Jobbuddy  uses cookies on its websites to enhance the website and provide services and functionality to its  users. You may restrict or disable the use of cookies through your web browser; however, without cookies, you may not be able to use all of the features of the websites.
 
What types of cookies are used on our website?
 
Strictly Necessary Cookies
 
These cookies are essential in order to carry out a transaction or request initiated by you. Strictly necessary cookies are essential for the use of the features on Jobbuddy.ie. Examples include, remembering information provided by you as you move through the site to apply for jobs, purchase job credits, track your application, using our Applicant Tracking System and Interview Scheduler and managing your logged-in status during the session.
 
Functional Cookies
 
These cookies allow the website to remember choices you have made in order to provide functionality for your benefit. For example, functional cookies allow the website to remember settings specific to you, such as your country selection and persistent logged-in state if selected by you, and display application status, jobs applied, reviewed and other customized features.
 
Analytic Cookies
 
These cookies allow us to collect data relating to your use of the website, including the content on which you click while browsing the website, in order to improve the performance and design of the website.
 
These cookies may be provided our third party analytic tool provider but are only used for purposes related to our websites. We use  Google Analytics to analyse how jobbuddy.ie is used
 
Targeting Cookies
 
These cookies remember information regarding your use of the website so that we can serve promotional and other targeted information to you on our website.
