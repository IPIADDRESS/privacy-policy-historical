JobsByRef.com Privacy Statement

Effective Date: September 1st, 2008

Introduction

At JobsByRef.com, we ("We") are commited to protecting your personal information. Your privacy is our top concern, in fact it is core to our service ("Service"). This privacy policy ("Privacy Policy") explains how we collect, use and share your personally identifiable information. It also describes the choices available to you regarding JobsByRef's use of your personal information and the steps you can take to access this information and to request that we correct or delete it. Since this website and/or Service is a career site, it is intended to provide Jobseekers ("User"), Employers ("User") and Recruiters ("User") to find each other to fulfill their career and hiring objectives. This website is not intended for people under 17 years of age.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully, and if you have any questions feel free to contact us at privacy@jobsbyref.com.

By using the JobsByRef.com website and/or Service you consent to the collection and use of your personally identifiable information as specified in this Privacy Policy. JobsByRef.com reserves the right to change this privacy policy from time to time, and will post any revisions on this website at http://www.jobsbyref.com. We may also, but not always, send any signigficant changes to the Privacy Policy to the email address you specified at the time of registration on this website. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes. If you continue to use the JobsByRef.com website and/or Service after notice of changes have been published on our website, or sent to you via email, you hereby provide your consent to the changed practices.

What and How we collect Personal information

JobsByRef.com collects your personal information in the following ways:

User Registration

To use the JobsByRef.com service, you must first create an account on this website. To create an account you will be asked to enter your name, email address, country, ZIP/postal code, password chosen by you, and possibly a brief summary of your professional background, your intended purpose of using this service, or any such information as we feel necessary to provide you the Service. You will use the email address and password provided here to sign-in and use the Service.

Profile Information

Once you register as a JobsByRef.com User, you may provide additional information in the Profile Information section describing your skills, professional experiences, and networking objectives. Providing additional information beyond what is required at registration is entirely optional, it enables you to better identify yourself and find new opportunities in the JobsByRef.com system.

Contacts Information

In order to connect to others you will invite users to JobsByRef.com. You will enter their names and email addresses. This information will be used by JobsByRef.com to send your invitation including a message that you write. The names and email addresses of people that you invite to join your JobsByRef.com network will only be used to send your invitation and reminders.

You may also choose to upload data or manually enter information about your contacts into the "Invite People" section of our website. All information entered there will only be viewable by you and will not be searchable by others in the JobsByRef.com network without your permission. We treat all your contacts information as your own and the terms as applicable to your personally identifiable information, is applied to their information also.

Cookies

JobsByRef.com uses software tags called "cookies" to identify Users when they visit our website. Cookies are used to remember user preferences and maximize performance of our services. The information we collect with cookies is not sold, rented, or shared with any outside parties. Users who disable their Web browser's ability to accept cookies will be able to browse our Website but will not be able to successfully use our Service.

We use both session cookies and persistent cookies. A session cookie expires when you close you browser. A persistent cookie remains on your hard drive for an extended period of time. You can remove persistent cookies by following directions provided in your Internet browser's "help" file. Persistent cookies enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance the experience on our site.

We also use cookies to analyze how visitors, in the aggregate, use our website, in order to make our website as useful as possible for them. We do not use cookies to collect or store personally identifiable information.

This privacy statement covers the use of cookies by JobsByRef.com only and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.

Third Party Cookies: We may from time to time engage third parties to track and analyze non-personally identifiable usage and volume statistical information from visitors to our website to help us administer our website and improve its quality. Such third parties may use cookies to help track visitor behavior. Such cookies will not be used to associate individual website visitors to any personally identifiable information. All data collected by such third parties on behalf of JobsByRef.com is used only to provide us with information on site usage and is not shared with any other third parties.

Web Beacons/Web Bugs

JobsByRef.com may include a file, called a web beacon, that allows the website to count users who have visited that page or to access certain cookies.

From time to time we will/may allow other companies, and ad networks, to serve advertisements to JobsByRef.com users. Ad networks include third party ad servers, ad agencies, ad technology vendors and research firms. JobsByRef.com may "target" some ads to users that fit a certain general profile. JobsByRef.com does NOT use personally identifiable information to target ads.

To deliver these ads properly, a web beacon is usually used, from these ad networks within pages served by JobsByRef.com so the networks may provide anonymized, aggregated auditing, research and reporting for advertisers as well as to target ads to you while you visit other websites.

Because your web browser must request these advertisements and web beacons from the ad network's servers, these companies can view, edit or set their own cookies, just as if you had requested a web page from their site.

These general targets may also be used by ad networks to display targeted ads on other websites as a substitute for random, irrelevant ads. JobsByRef.com never provides personally identifiable information to any advertiser nor any third party.

Web Site Analytics

We use a third party Web analytics service provider that uses cookies and Web Beacons to help us analyze how users use this website and the effectiveness of our marketing search terms. The information generated by the cookie about your use of this website (including, without limitation, your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by this service provider. They will use this information for the purpose of evaluating your use of our website and compiling aggregated reports for us. The information collected by our service provider and provided to us is not personally identifiable.

Links to Other Sites

This Web site contains links to other sites that are not owned or controlled by JobsByRef.com. Please be aware that we, JobsByRef.com, are not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites.

We encourage you to be aware when you leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every Web site that collects personally identifiable information.

This privacy statement applies only to information collected by this Web site.

Log files, IP addresses and information about your computer

Our servers automatically collect certain non-personally identifying information, such as your computer's IP address or the proxy server you use to access the internet), the type of browser in use, and pages viewed, when you visit our website. This information is used to analyze overall trends to help us improve the JobsByRef.com service. The linkage between your IP address and your personally identifiable information is never shared with third parties without your permission or except when required by law.

E-mail

HTML emails sent from the JobsByRef.com website and/or Service or its agents, can contain Web Becons to determine if the email was opened and acted upon by the User.

How we use the personal information collected

JobsByRef.com is a career site dedicated to helping all types of Users to find jobs, hire people, effectively connect with others. The personally identifiable information you choose to provide on this site is used to help you describe yourself to other users. Other information, that does not personally identify you as an individual, is collected by the website from JobsByRef.com users(such as, by way of example, patterns of utilization) and is exclusively owned by JobsByRef.com. This information can be utilized by JobsByRef.com in such manner as JobsByRef.com, in its sole discretion, deems appropriate.

Customized and Personalized content

Information you provide at the time of registration or in your Profile Information section is also used to customize your experience on the website. For example, when you login to your account we will display the jobs specific to you, names of people who connect to you, etc.

JobsByRef.com Communications

JobsByRef.com will communicate with you through email or through any other means available through the Service, including text and other forms of messaging. If we send any communications to you via the carrier service with which you have a mobile communications subscription or otherwise have access, you understand you will pay any service fees associated with any such access (including text messaging charges). We may from time to time send you emails which inform you about various features of the Service. You will also receive emails from JobsByRef.com related to proper functioning of your JobsByRef.com account. In case your profile on JobsByRef.com is not complete, you will receive emails from us frequently asking you to complete the missing information. Once you finish filling all the profile information, you will not receive specific emails asking you to complete your profile. You will also recevie emails when a User sends you an invite, requests to access your personal information, any job opportunities that come up for you, etc. JobsByRef.com may send you promotional information unless you have opted out of receiving such information. You can change your personal information, and communication preferences by signing-in to your JobsByRef.com account.

Sharing Information With Third Parties

JobsByRef.com takes the privacy of our Users very seriously. Jobseekers check out Search in Stealth™ to know more. We will never sell, rent, or otherwise provide your personally identifiable information to any third parties for marketing purposes. We will only share your personally identifiable information with third parties to carry out your instructions and to provide specific services or information. For instance, we will/may use a credit card processing company to bill Users for subscription fees. These third parties do not retain, share, or store any personally identifiable information except to provide these services.

We may provide aggregated anonymous data about the usage of our services to third-parties for such purposes as we deem, in our sole discretion, to be appropriate, including, without limitation, to prospective advertisers on JobsByRef.com.

We may also use the aggregated, anonymous profile and usage data to provide reports to our individual members, registered organizations and other companies.

Communication to other Users

An Invitation email initiated by you, sent to a non-user from the JobsByRef.com website for instance, will list your name and email address in the header of the message. This may also provide aggregate information, but will never list the people to whom you are connected. Your contact information will only be shared with another User if both of you have indicated that you would like to establish contact with each other.

Legal Disclaimer

We disclose information if legally required to do so, if requested to do so by a governmental entity or if we believe in good faith that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to legal requirements or comply with legal process; (b) protect our rights or property or our affiliated companies; (c) prevent a crime or protect national security; or (d) protect the personal safety of users or the public.

Disclosures to others

We may also disclose your personal and other information you provide, to another third party as part of a reorganization or a sale of the assets of JobsByRef.com division or company and only after taking steps to ensure that your privacy rights continue to be protected. Any third party to which we transfer or sell JobsByRef's assets will have the right to continue to use the personal and other information that you provide to us.

Forums, Blogs, Testimonials/ Success Stories

On the JobsByRef.com website you may participate in a forum to discuss with other Users of JobsByRef.com, or comment on the official JobsByRef.com blog. This may require that you give your personal information like name, email address. Please remember that doing so will allow other Users to send you unsolicited messages. The information here can be publically available, indexed by various search engines.

JobsByRef.com will ask your permission to post your testimonial or success story. If you agree to post your material on this site, you should be aware that any personally identifiable information you submit can be read, collected, or used by other users of this site and could be used to send you unsolicited messages.

JobsByRef.com will not be responsible for the personally identifiable information you may choose to submit in these forums, blogs, testimonials/ success stories, or any public areas of the website.

Information Security

At JobsByRef.com, the security of your personal information is important to us. We follow generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, however. You should, therefore, always use caution when submitting personal information online. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

All information gathered on the site is stored and maintained in secure facilities that limit access to authorized personnel only.

Accessing and Changing Your Account Information

You can review the personal information you provided us and make any desired changes to the information you publish, or to the settings for your JobsByRef.com account including your email, contact preferences, communication preferences, at any time by logging in to your account on the JobsByRef.com website. Please be aware that even after your request for a change is processed, JobsByRef.com may for a time retain residual information about you in its backup and/or archival copies of its database.

Children

Children are not eligible to use our service and we ask that minors do not submit any personal information to us or use the service. Since this is a career site, you will have to attain a workable age to use the JobsByRef.com website and the service. This website is not intended for people under 17 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from people under 17 years of age.

Contact Us

If you submit personal information, such as your email address, to us by following the "Contact Us" link located throughout our website, we will use that information to respond to your question or request for information. We will not share your information with third parties without your explicit permission.

We welcome your comments or questions concerning our Privacy Policy. If you would like to contact JobsByRef.com regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us by emailing us at privacy@jobsbyref.com. We will use commercially reasonable efforts to promptly determine and remedy any problem.
