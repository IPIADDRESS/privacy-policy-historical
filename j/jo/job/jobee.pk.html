Please keep in mind that any information contained within your profile is shared with employers / customers who have access to employer services on our website. We also use this information to improve Our services.

  • To connect Employers or Users with access to our employer services with user profiles within our system.
  • To personalize user's experience and to allow us to match you to jobs or services that fit your needs based on our recorded data on your preferences and searches.

When you join Jobee.pk or apply for a job through us, we share your Personal Information with employer customers. These customers may use this information to evaluate you for jobs and contact you. These customers may also store your information and in such cases, the use of this information will be subjected to the privacy policy of that company. We also share employer customer’s information with job seeker customers. Our employer customers are able to post jobs on our website that we share with our job seeker customers. Jobee.PK will not be responsible for misuse for this information stored by the Job seeker customer or the employer customer.

When you apply for a job through Jobee.PK, this information becomes a part of our database and it might also become a part of the employer’s database. In the case that it becomes a part of the employers database, the use of the information will be subjected to the privacy policy of that employer / user.

When you post a job on Jobee.PK, this information becomes a part of our database and it might also become a part of some other user’s database. In the case that it become a part of a user’s database, Jobee.PK is not responsible on how this information is used by the users.

Users should keep in mind that when they display personal information through Jobee.PK, this information can be accessed, collected, and used by others. It could result in third parties attempting to contact this user. Jobee.PK is not responsible for unsolicited contact or message requests from third parties.

At times, we may aggregate data to provide our employer customers or our Job Seeker customers with reporting to help serve them better and better our services.

Members are our top priority. We will guard your information as much as we can while being a professional platform for Job Seekers and Employers / Companies. Please note that once you create your Jobee Profile, your information will be available to anyone who has a link to your profile / resume and also any user / companies with access to our platform through our employer services. If you are an employer or company advertising on our website, the job information and company information you provide is public and will be shared with users and on any other platforms that we may see fit.
