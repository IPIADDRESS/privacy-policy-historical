Privacy Policy

JOBSEEKERS

This part of the Privacy Policy is specific to Jobseekers.

jobs.ac.uk is committed to ensuring your privacy is protected. When signing up for a jobs.ac.uk account, our Jobs by Email service or our CV Search Service, we collect your name, email address, location, current role, job seeking status and job searching preferences. We will not contact you other than to send you the bulletins you have subscribed for or internal news about our services for jobseekers, or offers from our partner companies and we will not distribute any of the details you provide to a third party. We have put in place suitable security procedures to safeguard your information.

Cookies

A Cookie is a small file which asks permission to be placed on your computer's hard drive. The cookie helps analyse web traffic and allows applications to respond to you personally. We use cookies to identify which pages are being used in order to improve our website, to provide our service to you and tailor it to your needs (the "Purpose"). Cookies are used for no other purpose; it does not give us access to your computer or any information about you.

We are currently working on a technical solution to ensure that we do not deploy cookies to your machine without your prior consent in accordance with recent legislation changes. However, in the interim, you may delete and block all cookies from the jobs.ac.uk website, but this may affect how the jobs.ac.uk website works for you. Most web browsers allow some control of most cookies through the browser settings. To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set and how to manage and delete them, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.

Right to Access Your Personal Information

As an individual, the Data Protection Act 1998 gives you a right to access information we hold about you. You can obtain access to your personal information held by us by completing our Data Subject Access Request Form and submitting the Form with a cheque for £10.00 (to cover administration costs for collating this information), made payable to University of Warwick Services Limited, to:

RECRUITERS AND ADVERTISERS

This part of the Privacy Policy is specific to Recruiters and Advertisers.

This policy sets out how jobs.ac.uk uses and protects any information that you give when you use this company.

jobs.ac.uk is committed to ensuring your privacy is protected. We may keep the following information about you: name and job title and contact details. We keep this information to provide you with a better service and for internal record keeping.

We will not distribute your name, address or interests to a third party. We have put in place suitable electronic and managerial security procedures to safeguard your information.

We may contact you from time to time either by email or telephone about new products, services and company news or for market research purposes but you may opt out of these by contacting our marketing team.
