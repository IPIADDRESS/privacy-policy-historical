Cookies

Like most web sites, Johnsonville.com uses cookies to to improve your experience and provide a better service. 

A "cookie" is a piece of data stored on the user's hard drive, containing information about the user, such as which pages of the site you visit or which products or recipes you click on for more information. We use cookies on our site, but none of them contain any information that could actually identify you as a person. Rather, cookies can help us provide you with information targeted to your interests. Cookies are stored on your hard drive, not on our site. Most cookies are "session cookies," meaning that they are automatically deleted at the end of a session.

If you don't want to receive cookies, you can adjust the settings of your web browser so it tells you each time you receive a cookie. Then you can reject them as they appear.
