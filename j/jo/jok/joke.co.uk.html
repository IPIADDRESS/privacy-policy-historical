Privacy Statement

Introduction
This privacy policy sets out details of the ways in which personal information we collect about may be used. This privacy policy only related to personal data collected by the The Jesters via the Joke.CO.uk website. It does not apply to personal data provided to the The Jesters by any other means or via any other website. You should be aware that if you subsequently give any personal information to another company, the uses to which that information may be put will be determined by that company's privacy policy and the The Jesters privacy policy will no longer apply.

Use made of personal information
Personal information provided to the The Jesters via the Joke.CO.uk website will be used for the purpose outlined at the time of collection. In addition, it may be used for statistical analysis.

Use of data for direct marketing purposes
From time to time, The Jesters may, if they perceive in the benefit to the Joke.CO.uk membership as a whole, undertake to send appropriate and interesting emails which may be product related with a suggestion to buy a particular product or service. You can be assured that these emails will be sent to you from The Jesters and will have been totally vetted beforehand.

Disclosure of data to third parties
Under no circumstances will The Jesters sell, let, trade, pass or otherwise disseminate your data or any part thereof to any third parties whatsoever. However, we may release account information when we believe, in good faith, that such release is reasonably necessary to (i) comply with law, (ii) enforce or apply the terms of any of our user agreements or (iii) protect the rights, property or safety of The Jesters, Joke.CO.uk, our users or others.

Use of Cookies
A "cookie" is a piece of information, like a tag, which some websites create on your PC. Our cookies allocate each user of this website with a random number. The cookies used on this site are not used for the collection of any personal information. Our cookies will not be used to analyse your visits to other websites or track any internet searches you may undertake whilst on the website. The information collected via cookies will be used to track movement on a company basis and for statistical analysis.

Updating and amending your personal information
If, at any time, you wish to verify, update or amend the personal information you have provided to the The Jesters, you can do so online by either clicking on the appropriate URL at the bottom of each Daily Joke or by selecting the .Edit My Membership Data. from the Joke.CO.uk web site.

Third party sites
Our privacy policy does not apply to third party sites that you may access from this website. We recommend you familiarise yourself with the applicable privacy policy before entering any personal information on a third party site.

Our security measures
We have implemented several security measures to ensure that your personal information is protected. Your password is stored in an encrypted format. In addition, we use a firewall to prevent third parties from accessing your personal information and other physical measures to prevent registered users from accessing the personal information of other users.

Your consent
By providing your data to the The Jesters you give your consent to the collection, storage, processing and use of your personal information by us as described in this privacy policy. We may change our privacy policy at any time and without notice. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.
