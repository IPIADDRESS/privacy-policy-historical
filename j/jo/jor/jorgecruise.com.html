Privacy Policy

JorgeCruise.com has created this privacy policy to let you know why we collect information from you and what we do with it.

The first thing to remember about privacy is that there is only so much information that any web site can collect without your answering specific questions. Jorge Cruise, Inc. logs IP addresses (in other words, the location of your computer on the Internet) for system administration, troubleshooting, and to examine overall traffic trends. This information (in aggregate form) is recorded for administrative purposes only; IP addresses are not linked to any personal information.

You can browse the website  and read our editorial content without registering or providing any personal information. What do we do with the information that we collect?
The information we collect from your membership helps us to improve our content, customize content of certain pages for you, and to send you updates about the site.
