Privacy Policy

As of December 10th, 2009

Privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy applies to all of the sites, downloads and services offered by Jott Networks Inc. Jott Networks Inc., for purposes of this Privacy Policy includes Jott and its parent company Nuance Communications, Inc.

Information collection, handling and use

In order to provide our full range of services, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Registration information that you provide - When you sign up for a Jott account, we ask you for information such as your name, a phone number, an email address, and an account password. We use this information to provide access to our services from phone calls, web sites, and client software.
  • Jott.com cookies - When you visit web pages associated with Jott services, we may use a common feature of a browser called a "cookie". We use cookies to improve the quality of our service, and to store your preferences for your next visit. If you do not want cookies to be set, you can usually set your browser to prevent this.
  • Log information - As is common, our servers automatically record information given by browsers and the phone systems, such as browser types, Caller ID, IP addresses, linking paths, etc.
  • User created jotts and other communications - When you create a Jott either by phone, email, SMS, instant messaging or otherwise, we save your data on our servers.
  • Affiliated sites - We may offer some services in connection with other web sites or third parties. A common example is the Jott Link to Google Calendar. To the extent that we receive data from these third parties, we treat that data in accordance with this privacy policy. These third party sites may have different privacy practices than Jott and we encourage you to read their privacy policies.
  • Use of data - Jott will process information for the purposes of providing the services. Agents with appropriate confidentiality agreements may process information for the purposes of providing the services. As part of the service, Jott and its affiliates collects and uses Speech Data, as defined below to tune, enhance, and improve the speech recognition and other components used to provide the service, and other services and products. Speech Data means the audio files, associated transcriptions and log files provided by you hereunder or generated in connection with the service.
  • Advertising - We do not serve advertising on Jott.com, Jott for iPhone, or other products produced by Jott Networks. If we do choose to support our business with advertising based on information collected, you will have the option to choose a version of Jott with no advertising, or discontinue your service. In no event shall Jott give away or sell your personal data to third parties.

Sharing of Information

Jott shares information or personal information with other companies or individuals outside of Jott in the following circumstances:

  • When access, use, preservation or disclosure of such information is reasonably necessary to (a) satisfy any applicable law, regulation, legal process or enforceable governmental request, (b) enforce applicable Terms of Service and investigation of potential violations thereof, (c) detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security or technical issues, or (d) protect against imminent harm to the rights, property or safety of Jott, its users or the public as required or permitted by law as otherwise expressly set forth in this privacy policy.

We may also share with third parties acting under the direction of Jott and with appropriate confidentiality agreements, certain pieces of aggregated, non-personal information; and Speech Data to tune, enhance and improve the speech recognition and other components of the service and other services and products.

Information security

We take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of data. Our security measures include Physical, Technical, Organizational and Administrative safeguards. These include internal reviews of our data collection, storage and processing practices and security measures, as well as physical security measures to guard against unauthorized access to systems where we store personal data.

To the extent you choose to use downloaded Jott software or services, Jott cannot be responsible for your protection of that data.

Enforcement and Changes to this Policy

Jott regularly reviews its compliance with this Policy. Please feel free to direct any questions or concerns regarding this Policy or Jott's treatment of personal information by emailing us at feedback(at sign)jott.com.

Please note that this Privacy Policy may change from time to time, and those changes will usually be minor. Regardless, we will post any Policy changes on Jott.com.
