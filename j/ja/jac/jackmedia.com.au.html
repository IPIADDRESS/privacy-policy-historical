Your continued use of the Site, use of your Account and/or acceptance of our email communications following the posting of changes to this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of any and all changes.

Personally identifiable information


We may collect personally identifiable information from Users in a variety of ways, including, but not limited to, when Users visit our Site, register for an Account, place an order, subscribe to a newsletter, or otherwise choose to provide personally identifiable information. Personally identifiable information is any information that can be used to identify or locate a particular person or entity. This may include, but is not limited to: your name, business entity name. your title within the business, as well as your personal and/or business entity related e-mail address, mailing address, daytime and/or mobile telephone numbers, fax number, account information (or other information that we require in order to pay any amounts due to you under the Affiliate Network) and/or any other information that we request or you choose to provide.

Users may, however, visit our Site anonymously. We will collect personally identifiable information from Users only if they voluntarily submit such information to Us. Users can always refuse to supply personally identification information, except that this may prevent them from engaging in certain Site related activities, creating or accessing an Account, or doing business with us if they do not provide the information that We need.


Non-personally identifiable information


We may collect certain non-personally identifiable information about Users whenever they interact with our Site and/or register for an Account on the Affiliate Network. Web browser cookies


Our Site may use "cookies" to enhance Users’ experiences. Cookies are small packets of data stored on your computer. Cookies are used by your computer's browser to store your preferences, to help Us recognise You when You visit the Site again, and to keep You logged in to your Account when You use the Site.

Most browsers are set to accept cookies. You may choose to set your web browser to block cookies or to alert You when cookies are being sent. Each browser is different, so check the "Help" menu of your browser to learn how to change your cookie preferences. If you change computers, operating systems or browsers, or use multiple computers or browsers, you will need to repeat this process for each computer and each browser. If You block cookies from the Site, note that some parts of the Site may not function properly. For example, you cannot register for or log in to an Account on the Affiliate Network without accepting the cookies that we use with the Site. For more specific information on the cookies we use, please see Exhibit A, below.

Cookies, by themselves, do not tell us your e-mail address or other personally identifiable information. We use cookies to understand Site usage and to improve the content and offerings on the Site.


Web beacons
We may collect information using web beacons, which are commonly referred to in the industry as web bugs, pixel tags or clear GIFs. Web beacons are electronic images that may be used on the Site, in your Account, or in the emails We send to You to deliver cookies, count visits and determine if an email has been opened and acted upon.
