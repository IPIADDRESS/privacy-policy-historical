You may choose not to provide your Personal Information; however providing Personal Information is a pre-requisite for certain activities and services available through the Sites, such as creating an account, renting equipment, registering for certain classes or staying at the Jackson Hole Mountain Resort (the “Resort”).

How we Collect your Personal Information
We may collect your Personal Information in any of the ways set forth below (each a “Channel”):
• Online. In order to use certain functions or features of the Sites, you will need to create an account. In addition to such tailoring, we use Usage Information to ensure that our online presence operates properly and efficiently for you and for your individual customer experience, to evaluate the use and benefit of such presence, and to support our Sites and our marketing efforts.  Currently our Sites do not respond to Do Not Track features enabled through your browser.   We do not use spy ware, adware or any other similarly intrusive software. Collectively, Usage Information and Personal Information are referred to in this Privacy Policy as “Information.”

It is our policy to require only the Personal Information necessary to complete the specific transaction that you request.
