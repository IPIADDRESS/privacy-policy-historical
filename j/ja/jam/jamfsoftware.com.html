Privacy Policy for JAMFSOFTWARE.COM

JAMF Software, LLC (“JAMF”) makes reasonable efforts to protect the privacy of your personal information.  This Privacy Policy was created to demonstrate our commitment to fair information practices.  This Privacy Policy covers JAMF’s use of personal information that JAMF collects when you use its http://www.jamfsoftware.com website (the “Site”).  JAMF complies with the U.S.-EU Safe Harbor Framework as set forth by the U.S. Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use, and retention of personal information from European Union member countries.  JAMF has certified that it adheres to the Safe Harbor Privacy Principles of notice, choice, onward transfer, security, data integrity, access, and enforcement.  To learn more about the Safe Harbor program, and to view our certification page, please visit:  http://www.export.gov/safeharbor.  JAMF periodically conducts objective reviews of its Privacy Policy and procedures to ensure continued compliance with the Safe Harbor Privacy Principles. 

1.    Types of Information Collected

a.    Personal Information Collected. There are areas on the Site where you can share information that is unique to an individual; such as your name, company name, postal address, e-mail address, phone number, zip code, and credit card information (“Personal Information”).  You can also purchase products and certification course packages with your credit card.  Accordingly, we may request your credit card information for shipping, billing, and credit card validation purposes.  We ask for this information in order to deliver requested materials to you, respond to your questions, or deliver a product.  If you communicate with us by e-mail or complete online forms, we may require Personal Information that personally identifies you.  JAMF may also request your e-mail address or mailing address for the purposes of having someone contact you for additional information, or in response to your request for information from us.  All of the foregoing is treated as Personal Information, as described in this policy.

b.    Information from Children.  JAMF does not knowingly solicit or collect information (including Data or Personal Information) from anyone under 13 years of age.  The use of the Site by anyone under 13 years of age is prohibited.  We ask that anyone under 13 years of age not submit information to us.  However, when we e-mail information to you, we will provide prominently displayed instructions that show you how to unsubscribe from receiving such information.  If your child subscribes to receive information from JAMF without your knowledge, simply follow the instructions on our e-mail and unsubscribe immediately.  Parents can also contact our customer service department to access, change, or delete the Personal Information that has been submitted by a child.

2.    Use of Data and Personal Information Collected 

JAMF will use the Data and Personal Information directly submitted by you for the purpose for which it was provided.  JAMF may also use Personal Information you provide to us to contact you for reasons related to your original communication to us.  JAMF may use your Personal Information to customize your experience on the Site and display content that we think you might be interested in according to your preferences.  JAMF may also provide the Personal Information submitted by you to third party business who JAMF believes may provide products or services of value to you or to intermediaries who may identify such businesses.  JAMF may use the Personal Information on its own behalf for these purposes.  JAMF may use the Data in connection with the development, design, marketing or quality improvement of the Site and for JAMF's general business purposes.  JAMF may collect, store, aggregate, analyze and disclose data gleaned from Personal Information or other information which identifies you or any individual user. This data, which includes data describing your usage of the Site, will be owned by JAMF and you consent to JAMF’s use of data identifying you for this purpose.

3.   Sharing of Personal Information

From time to time, JAMF may engage other companies and individuals to perform functions such as conducting contests, sending postal mail and e-mail, removing repetitive information from customer lists, analyzing data, providing marketing assistance, providing search results and links (including paid listings and links), and providing customer service.  Such companies shall only use your Personal Information for those purposes required to perform their functions. 

JAMF may share your Personal Information, without your prior consent, for the limited purposes noted below:

a.    Business Transfers.  As JAMF continues to develop its business, we might sell or buy subsidiaries or business units.  In such transactions, customer information generally is one of the transferred business assets but remains subject to the obligations made in any pre-existing privacy policy.

b.    Legal Obligations.  JAMF may share your Personal Information if we have a good faith belief that such action is necessary to:  (i) comply with legal process or other legal requirements of any governmental authority; (ii) protect and defend the rights or property of JAMF; (iii) enforce this Privacy Policy or the Site’s Terms and Conditions of Access and Use Agreement; (iv) protect the interests of other users of the Site or any other person; or (v) operate or conduct maintenance and repair of the Site or equipment.

4.    Protection of Personal Information

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, JAMF has put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the Personal Information JAMF collects.

6.    Other Limits to Your Privacy

The Site may contain links to other non-JAMF websites.  JAMF is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites.  JAMF has no control over the use of such websites and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose any Personal Information on these websites.

7.    User Choice Regarding Collection, Use, and Distribution of Personal Information

You may choose not to provide JAMF with any Personal Information.  In such an event, you will be only able to access public areas of the Site.  If you do not wish to receive information and promotional material from us, you may choose not to submit your information or select the appropriate "opt-out" option each time we ask you for Personal Information.

For JAMF Cloud services, users cannot opt-out of receiving information from us.

8.    How You Can Access Or Correct Your Information

If you have provided your Personal Information and your Personal Information changes (such as your address or telephone) or you want to delete your information from our systems, you can contact us using the method described below.  JAMF uses this procedure to better safeguard your information.  To protect your privacy and security, JAMF may also take reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting access or making corrections.

9.    Changes to Privacy Policy

JAMF reserves the right to changes this Privacy Policy at any time by posting the revised Privacy Policy on the Site.  Any revised Privacy Policy will be effective immediately upon posting on the Site.  Changes to the Website Terms and Conditions of Use Agreement and the Privacy Policy will govern your use of the Site even if you do not receive notice of those changes. We recommend you check this Privacy Policy frequently to stay abreast of any changes.

10.    How to Contact Us

Should you have other questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please send JAMF an e-mail to support@jamfsoftware.com.
