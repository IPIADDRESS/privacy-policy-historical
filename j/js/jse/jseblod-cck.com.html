Privacy Policy

jSeblod is committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology that gives you the most powerful and safe online experience. This Statement of Privacy applies to the jSeblod.com and jSeblod-CCK.com Web site and governs data collection and usage. By using the jSeblod website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Collection of your Personal Information

jSeblod collects personally identifiable information, such as your e-mail address, name, home or work address or telephone number. jSeblod also collects anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your ZIP code, age, gender, preferences, interests and favorites.

There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected by jSeblod. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring Web site addresses. This information is used by jSeblod for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the jSeblod Web site.

Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally identifiable information or personally sensitive data through jSeblod public message boards, this information may be collected and used by others. Note: jSeblod does not read any of your private online communications.

jSeblod encourages you to review the privacy statements of Web sites you choose to link to from jSeblod so that you can understand how those Web sites collect, use and share your information. jSeblod is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on Web sites outside of the jSeblod and jSeblod family of Web sites. Use of your Personal Information.

jSeblod collects and uses your personal information to operate the jSeblod Web site and deliver the services you have requested. jSeblod also uses your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from jSeblod and its affiliates. jSeblod may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

jSeblod does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. jSeblod may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. In those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (e-mail, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party. In addition, jSeblod may share data with trusted partners to help us perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to jSeblod, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

jSeblod does not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations, without your explicit consent.

jSeblod keeps track of the Web sites and pages our customers visit within jSeblod, in order to determine what jSeblod services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within jSeblod to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

jSeblod Web sites will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on jSeblod or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of jSeblod; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of jSeblod, or the public.

Changes to this Statement

jSeblod will occasionally update this Statement of Privacy to reflect company and customer feedback. jSeblod encourages you to periodically review this Statement to be informed of how jSeblod is protecting your information.
