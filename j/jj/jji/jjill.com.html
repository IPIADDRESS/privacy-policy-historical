Privacy Policy

At J. Jill, we understand your concerns about online security and privacy. We seek to ensure that your personal and payment information is secure and your privacy is respected. We hope this policy statement provides you with answers to the questions you may have about our privacy and security policies. If you have further questions, please contact us by phone at 1.800.343.5700 or
e-mail us anytime.

What information do we collect?

In order to provide the services you request, we ask you for certain kinds of personal information. When you sign up with us, visit our web site, request to "chat" with a customer service associate, send us an e-mail, place an order or make a purchase at our stores, you provide us with certain personally identifiable information, such as your name, e-mail address, postal address, phone number and credit or debit card number. If you participate in one of our promotions, contests or surveys, you may provide us with additional personal information such as your age or date of birth. In addition to this information, we also maintain a record of your purchases from jjill.com, our catalog and our stores. On occasion, we may combine information you provide with other demographic information available to us.

In addition, for each visitor to the web site, our web server automatically collects the following non-personally identifiable information: your Internet service provider's address, the referring URL, and information on the pages you access on our site.

What do we do with the information we collect?

When we take an order on the phone or when you make a purchase online or at a store, we use your information to process your order or purchase, answer questions you may have, mail catalogs to you, and provide you with periodic e-mails and mailings about our merchandise and promotions. From time to time we may also send you e-mail offers on behalf of marketing partners whose products and/or services we think may be of interest to you.

Currently, we also gather online information about your purchases and shopping activities to improve the content of our web site, to enhance your online shopping experience, and to use in future marketing and promotional activities for our web site, our catalogs and our J. Jill stores.

Will J. Jill share my information with third parties?

In some instances, we use third party companies to provide services for us, including assistance in maintaining and managing customer information, providing customer and Internet services, taking and fulfilling orders, conducting J. Jill promotions and surveys, and communicating effectively with our clients. Some of these vendors are given access to some or all of the information you provide to us, including your e-mail address and credit card information, and may use cookies on our behalf. Except as disclosed in this Policy, J. Jill does not authorize such third parties to make use of personally identifiable information other than to provide J. Jill with these services. If you apply for the J. Jill credit card online, you provide the personal information needed to process your application (including your social security number) directly to Citibank U.S.A., N.A. ("Citibank"), the issuer and the administrator of the credit card program. If you apply for the J. Jill credit card by phone or in the stores, we collect that information from you and provide it to Citibank. Citibank is independent of J. Jill and has its own privacy and security policies. You can review their policies at Citigroup Privacy Promise.

Does J. Jill use cookies?

J. Jill regularly uses cookies (and other similar tracking technologies) to help us keep track of your order as you visit on our site. Cookies are bits of information that are automatically stored on your computer to recognize your preferences. Cookies stay on your computer unless erased by the user.

We also currently use the services of a marketing company to help us measure the effectiveness of our advertising and how visitors use our site. To do this, we use web beacons and cookies provided by our marketing company on this web site. The type of information we collect includes the areas of the web site visited and information about transactions made. By supplementing our records, this information helps us learn what kinds of customers our site attracts, which of our products most interest our customers, and what kinds of offers our customers like to see. We may also use the data collected by the marketing company to conduct specialized marketing programs. As a visitor to jjill.com, you may "opt out" of the collection by such company of such data. To opt out, go to wvw.jjill.com/optout.html.

You also have the option to refuse cookies altogether by turning them off in your browser.

What steps does J. Jill take to secure information on the web site?

The jjill.com web site makes use of Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology to encrypt credit card and billing information while it is transmitted to jjill.com. Your computer must be SSL-enabled to ensure secure transfer of your credit card information. When we transfer and receive financial and other types of sensitive information, we redirect visitors to a secure server. Our browser technology is designed to ensure that no one outside of our professional billing staff will have access to your credit card information. If you are having trouble with your browser, you can e-mail your technical questions to techhelp@jjill.com for assistance.

If you are more comfortable ordering over the phone, call a customer service representative at 1.800.343.5700, 7 a.m. to 11 p.m. ET, seven days a week, or fax credit card orders to 1.603.266.2802.

Privacy of Young Children

We are concerned about the privacy of young children and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from a child under 13 years of age.

Technical Specifications

The J. Jill web site is designed for use with the latest versions of Internet Explorer and Netscape Navigator. If you currently use older versions of these browsers (including Internet Explorer 3.0 or earlier, Netscape Navigator 3.0 or earlier, and AOL 3.0 or earlier), you may experience technical difficulties with online ordering. As mentioned above, your computer must be SSL-enabled to ensure secure transfer of your credit card information.

Policy Changes

We may, from time to time, use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change at some time in the future, we will post those policy changes on our web site. If you are at all concerned about how your information is used, you may periodically check our web site for updates.
