Steps to Clear Cache
We regret the issue of "Username doesn't exist" you are facing while trying to login at www.greencart.in. We would suggest you to clear your cache which could be affecting the login owing to old website data. The steps to clear cache is different in different browsers:
 
In Google Chrome:
1. Keep "Ctrl" key pressed on your keyboard and press "H" key.
2. Click on "Clear browsing data"
3. In the pop up, Keep "Empty the cache" checked and click on "Clear browsing data" button.
4. Please try to login again by going to www.greencart.in. Please enter your registered email id and password on top right and click on Go button.

In Firefox Mozilla:
1. Keep "Alt" key pressed on your keyboard and press "s" key.
2. Select "Clear recent History" option in the menu drop down
3. In the pop up select "Cache" and click on "Clear now".
4. Please try to login again by going to www.greencart.in. Please enter your registered email id and password on top right and click on Go button.

In Internet Explorer:
1. Keep "Alt" key pressed on your keyboard and press "t" key.
2. Select "Delete browsing History" option in the menu drop down
3. With "Temporary Internet Files", "Cookies" and "History" selected, please click on Delete button.
4. Please try to login again by going to www.greencart.in. Please enter your registered email id and password on top right and click on Go button.
