Great Scores Ltd is committed to protecting the privacy of all visitors to our websites.

In practice, every time you view a web page, we may store logs which show your IP address (the Internet number which your machine uses when it is connected to the Internet) or the IP address of any proxy you use. This data is used strictly for statistical purposes only. This tells us
