General groovyPost.com Privacy Policy

As a groovyReader, you can expect the following in regards to your privacy:

  1. groovyPost.com will NEVER ask for or collect any Personal Identifiable Information for any reason.  In the odd chance its been collected on a groovyPost site, please contact me and I will attempt to purge it from the site in a groovy flash!
  2. groovyPost.com will NEVER sell your email address EVER.  Period, end of story.

 

Cookies (no, not the kind you eat)

Here at groovyPost.com I use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit us.  Although this will never make me rich, it does keep the groovyPost family of sites from draining my pocket book (which keeps MrsGroove happy).  These third-party companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

It should be noted that pretty much all websites in the world use Cookies in order to identify your session on their website and enable you to do things like login etc…  If you would like more information about cookies and learn about the choices you have to disable some of the tracking by these companies, click here.

Another option is to disable cookies in your browser however by doing this you will no longer be able to use most websites.  Any questions or comments regarding the privacy policy should be sent to privacy@groovyPost.com.
