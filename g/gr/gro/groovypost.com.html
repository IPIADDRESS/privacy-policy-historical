Privacy Policy

General groovyPost.com Privacy Policy

As a groovyReader or subscriber to my newsletter, you can expect the following in regards to your privacy:

  1. If you sign up for my newsletter, your email address will only be used so I can deliver it to you. If you cancel your subscription (not that you ever will…) I will delete your email address immediately from my newsletter system.
  2. I will never sell, trade, transfer or give away any information you provide me. Period. No exceptions!
  3. I will never ask for or collect purposely any personal identifiable information for any reason. In the odd event its been collected or left on my site, please contact me and I will purge it from the site in a groovy flash.

 

Cookies (no, not the kind you eat)

Like almost all sites on the internet, I use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit my site. Although this will never make me rich, it does allow me to operate the site and keep it free for anyone who is groovy. These third-party companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

Pretty much all websites in the world use Cookies in order to identify your session on their website and enable you to do things like login, personalize your session etc…  If you would like more information about cookies and learn about the choices you have to disable some of the tracking by these companies, click here.

Another option is to disable cookies in your browser however by doing this you will no longer be able to use most websites.  Any questions or comments regarding the privacy policy should be sent to privacy@groovyPost.com.
