Your Privacy Is Important to Us!
We, at Grass Valley, a Belden brand, value your trust and confidence.

We believe that we earn your trust and confidence when we continue to bring you exceptional products or services and do so in a very private and secure manner. We believe that trust and confidence is sustained as we honor that commitment to you, day in and day out.

Grass Valley is a global company, offering digital products and services worldwide, and forms part of the Belden Inc. group of companies, with business units, divisions and subsidiaries spread across the globe, including within the European Union. This privacy statement is applicable to the information we collect on our websites.

We created this privacy statement to inform you of our global commitment to you.

Keeping the personal information we collect about you secure is one of our most important responsibilities. We value your trust and handle personal information with care. WORKING TO MEET YOUR NEEDS THROUGH INFORMATION IN THE COURSE OF DOING BUSINESS, WE COLLECT AND USE VARIOUS TYPES OF INFORMATION, SUCH AS INFORMATION AVAILABLE FROM PUBLIC RECORDS AND MARKET RESEARCH, AS WELL AS INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE TO US. We collect personally identifiable information from you, for example, when you purchase a product or service contract, when you register on one of our websites, complete a survey, or when you request information from one of our family of companies.

COOKIES
Grass Valley takes users' privacy very seriously and is committed to comply with the EU Directive on privacy in the electronic communications where it is serving customers in the EU. For this purpose we have developed the following notice which intends to give you all the keys to manage the cookies we collect while you visit our website.

What is a cookie?
Cookies are small files which are downloaded on to a device when the user accesses to certain websites. Cookies allow a website to recognize users, to understand the users' navigation on different websites.

In practice, the website you visit sends information to the browser which then creates a text file. Every time the user goes back to the same website, the browser retrieves and sends this file to the website's server.

Grass Valley Cookies:
Grass Valley intends to ensure that you enjoy your experience on our website. For this reason, when visiting our website, cookies are installed on the computer.

When surfing on our website(s), data logs of your surfing activity may be collected with the purpose of understanding aggregate usage of features and pages.

How long does Grass Valley keep the cookies?
Cookies remain on your computer until you delete them, either manually or through software. You can decide to delete all cookies anytime when going into your browser settings.

Cookies Settings:
If you don't agree with the use of such cookies, you can set up your browser to limit or exclude their use. Your browser may provide you with information and control over cookies. You can set your browser to alert you when a cookie is being used, and accept or reject the cookie. You can also set your browser to refuse all cookies or accept only cookies returned to the originating servers. You can generally disable the cookie feature on your browser without affecting their ability to use our website(s).
