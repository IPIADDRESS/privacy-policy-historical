About Grabhouse

Founded in July 2013, Grabhouse is India's first 100% broker free online rental platform, which aims to solve real estate problems through technology. We offer personalized services to all the users to help them find suitable flats on rent along with compatible flatmates. In the past two years, Grabhouse has managed to build a team of 100+ efficient employees, who give their best to improve the user experience and help them find their desired apartments and paying guests.



Our USP

We are India’s first house renting portal that offers a completely broker free platform to find houses and paying guests on rent.The transparency in our system ensures a unique user experience for all the our customers. We also make sure that the online house renting experience is not a painful one for our users. The sole purpose of Grabhouse is to make house renting as easy and as broker free as possible. We not only help people in renting a flat but also give our 100% to help them find a great deal.

What drives us?

We are driven by the motivation and appreciation we get from our customers. Thousands of youngsters look for apartments on rents and most of them are new to the city. That’s where we come in play. We, at Grabhouse, strive hard to remove the middleman from the rental real estate industry and bring transparency in the process and the same can be achieved by the best technology and design. We do not mind going an extra mile if we see the light. The biggest challenge we face is that the whole house renting market is dominated by brokers. We have an efficient system that enables us to block brokers or any middleman. It enhances the user experience and helps them find PGs and flats on rent without paying any brokerage.
