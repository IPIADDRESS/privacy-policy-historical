The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to inform our Users of:
  1. The types of Personal and Confidential  Information which we may collect and how it may be used;

  3. Extraordinary cases, when Personal and/or Confidential Information may be disclosed to third parties;
  4. User’s options to correct, update and delete his/her Personal Information;
  5. The security measures we undertake to prevent the leak, loss, misuse, or unauthorized alteration of Personal and/or Confidential Information

During the User’s using GRAVIEX site (www.graviex.net) the Service collects  the User’s Personal and Confidential Information and stores it on the same site.
We may use the User’s Personal and/or Confidential Information for the following purposes:
  1. To allow the User to open and operate an Account on the Service;

  3. To make analysis and provide advice for the User in case of User’s requests and claims;
  4. To analyze User’s activity at the Site;
  5. For AML purposes which may apply;
  6. For own marketing research without transferring any part of Personal Information to any third parties;
We hereby  declare that GRAVIEX do not report and will never report any part or of User’s Personal and Confidential Information in any form (incl. pre-processed or aggregated forms) to any advertisers.

3 IP Addresses and other technical and session details

We may collect information about users’ IP address, operating system and browser type for testing compatibility of the Service with frequently  updating users’ software and for security reasons.

We use a browser feature known as a "cookie", which assigns a unique identification to your computer. Cookies are typically stored on the User’s computer's hard drive. Information collected from cookies is used by us to evaluate the effectiveness of our Site, analyse trends, and maintain the Service.
