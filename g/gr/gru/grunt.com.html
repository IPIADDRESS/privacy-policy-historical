Our online shopping pages are secured through SSL (Secure Socket Layer) technology to encrypt the data on our secure server. Using encryption, your credit card information will be kept secure from the public Internet.

Our website is also scanned by Trust Guard Security Scanner to ensure that your information remains secure and that we are always at the highest compliance level for data security. Click on the Trust Guard graphic at the bottom of the page for more information.

When you submit content to Sgt Grit (including, but not limited to, stories, photos, comments, ratings and reviews), you give permission to Sgt Grit to use that content on grunt.com or to promote grunt.com. Your name will be posted as the author of the content, if available, but other personal information will not be published without your express permission.

Occasionally, we make the Sgt Grit mailing list available to other companies who meet our standard of excellence. If you do not wish to receive such mailings or our catalog, please notify our Customer Service Department at 888-NOV-1775 (888-668-1775) or by e-mailing us at optout@grunt.com. Cookies

When visiting Grunt.com, your shopping and browsing activity is tracked via a cookie stored by your browser on your device. We currently use this cookie to store information on your browsing/shopping session. The cookie will contain information about the products in your seabag, the pages you have viewed on Grunt.com, and may be used on Grunt.com or other websites in advertisements featuring Sgt Grit products and content. Information pertaining to payment is not stored within the cookie.
