Privacy Policy

Gridiron Experts knows that you care about how your personal information is used and shared, and we take your privacy very seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy. By visiting our website, you are accepting the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy covers Gridiron Experts’s treatment of personal information that Gridiron Experts gathers when you are on the Gridiron Experts website and when you use Gridiron Experts services. This policy does not apply to the practices of third parties that Gridiron Experts does not own or control, or to individuals that Gridiron Experts does not employ or manage.

Information Collected by Gridiron Experts

We only collect personal information that is relevant to the purpose of our website. This information allows us to provide you with a customized and efficient experience. We do not process this information in a way that is incompatible with this objective. We collect the following types of information from our Gridiron Experts users:

1. Information You Provide to Us: We receive and store any information you enter on our website or provide to us in any other way. You can choose not to provide us with certain information, but then you may not be able to take advantage of many of our special features.

2. Automatic Information:

We receive and store certain types of information whenever you interact with us. Gridiron Experts and its authorized agents automatically receive and record certain “traffic data” on their server logs from your browser including your IP address, Gridiron Experts cookie information, and the page you requested. Gridiron Experts uses this traffic data to help diagnose problems with its servers, analyze trends and administer the website. Gridiron Experts may collect and, on any page, display the total counts that page has been viewed.

Many companies offer programs that help you to visit websites anonymously. While Gridiron Experts will not be able to provide you with a personalized experience if we cannot recognize you, we want you to be aware that these programs are available.

E-mail Communications
Gridiron Experts is very concerned about your privacy and we will never provide your email address to a third party without your explicit permission, as detailed in the “Sharing Your Information” section below. Gridiron Experts may send out e-mails with Gridiron Experts-related news, products, offers, surveys or promotions.

Cookies

Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that we transfer to your computer’s hard drive through your Web browser to enable our systems to recognize your browser and tell us how and when pages in our website are visited and by how many people. Gridiron Experts cookies do not collect personal information, and we do not combine information collected through cookies with other personal information to tell us who you are or what your screen name or e-mail address is.

The “help” portion of the toolbar on the majority of browsers will direct you on how to prevent your browser from accepting new cookies, how to command the browser to tell you when you receive a new cookie, or how to fully disable cookies. We recommend that you leave the cookies activated because cookies allow you to use some of Gridiron Experts’s coolest features.

Gridiron Experts’s advertising partners may place a cookie on your browser that makes it possible to collect anonymous non-personally identifiable information that ad delivery systems use to present more relevant ads.  If you would prefer to opt-out of this standard practice, please visit our advertising partner Platform-A’s privacy policy and opt-out page.

Sharing Your Information

Rest assured that we neither rent nor sell your personal information to anyone and that we will share your personal information only as described below.

Gridiron Experts Personnel: Gridiron Experts personnel and authorized consultants and/or contractors may have access to user information if necessary in the normal course of Gridiron Experts business.

Business Transfers: In some cases, we may choose to buy or sell assets. In these types of transactions, user information is typically one of the business assets that is transferred. Moreover, if Gridiron Experts, or substantially all of its assets, were acquired, user information would be one of the assets that is transferred.

This includes exchanging information with other companies and organizations for fraud protection and credit risk reduction.

Syndication: Gridiron Experts allows for the RSS syndication of all of its public content within the Gridiron Experts website.

With Your Consent: Except as noted above, we will contact you when your personal information is shared with third parties or used for a purpose incompatible with the purpose(s) for which it was originally collected, and you will be able to opt out to prevent the sharing of this information.

If we learn that Gridiron Experts has collected information from a child under the age of 13, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.
