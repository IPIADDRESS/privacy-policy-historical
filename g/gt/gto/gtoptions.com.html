2. Our clients undertake to supply us with true, updated and accurate information about their identity. Furthermore they are required to state categorically that they register and trade on their own behalf and that they are not seeking at any time to act any manner which could be considered fraudulent nor are they seeking to impersonate any other individuals for any purposes whatsoever. It is important to keep us informed about your personal data as soon as something important changes, so your profile will always be up to date, and as required under any law.

3. We share information with the associated firms and other persons processing your personal information, or with banks and clearing houses in order to deliver the products you need for the best binary options trading.

4. GToptions’s data collection procedures include the collection of client’s freely disclosed information as shared with the company, in addition to the placement of cookies for the purposes of gathering data about the manner in which client’s interact with the GToptions website. These tools for gathering client’s information are employed for the purpose of ensuring the customer’s own security and all data collected by the company is shared only with individuals within the company who are involved with the verification of customer account information for the express purpose of ensuring the customer’s confidentiality and security. The foregoing notwithstanding, GToptions is entitled to gather and use any statistical, usability and analytics information at its sole discretion.

5. GToptions will never disclose any private or otherwise confidential information with regards to our clients and former clients to third parties, without the express consent of our clients, except in such specific cases in which disclosure is a requirement (a) under law or court order, (b) to credit reference and fraud prevention agencies; (c) national and international enforcement bodies and courts when they require this kind of information, as laws and regulations demand; (d) to defend GToptions against any claim by you or by third parties; or (e) is otherwise necessary in order to perform verification analysis on the client’s identity for the purposes of safeguarding their account and securing their personal information.

By registering with GToptions and through the voluntary interaction they undertake with GToptions’s products and services the client confirms and agrees that they consent to the use of all or part of the information they supply concerning their GToptions trading account, the transactions they undertake through it and the interactions which they perform with the company on behalf of the company. 7. GToptions ensures the confidentiality of its clients' personal information including the implementation of data protection procedures. GToptions ensures that its data protection policy is regularly updated in order to ensure that client’s confidential information is continually safeguarded.

8. From time to time GToptions may contact clients, including by phone or email for the purpose of offering them further information about GToptions services and products, binary options trading or financial market trading.
