GSO Website (“gso.com”) Privacy Policy

We require customers who register to use the services offered on our web site to give us contact information, such as their name, company name, address, phone number, and e-mail address. In addition, for Online New Account Requests and GSO QuickShip, billing and credit card information of the individual or organization that will be using the Service is requested. Customers can review and update personal information at any time by logging into the website.

We use secure socket layer (SSL) technology to encrypt your credit card number and other personal billing information when you place an order. We host our Site in a secure server environment that uses a firewall and other advanced technology to prevent interference or access from outside intruders. We provide unique user names and passwords that must be entered each time a customer logs on. These safeguards and helps prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the appropriate use of Data. Customers are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality and security of their user registration and password.

This information allows us to process your online transactions and requests, do not identify you personally and exist only during an active online session.
