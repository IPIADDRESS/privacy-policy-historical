We use cookies to give you a more unique experience when using the website and to improve the quality of our service. If you have Cookies enabled on your browser, gomy.co.uk can remember your personal preferences each time you return. This allows us to:

  • Recognise You when you return to gomy.co.uk
  • Remember Your individual interests: (Using Manchester as an example) if you searched for Restaurants in Manchester and closed down the website, the next time You returned to the site, a cookie will remember to open the Manchester Homepage instead of You searching for it again.
  • Speed up some of your searches

A cookie is a small piece of information that a website sends to Your computer while You are viewing the site. We may use both session cookies (which expire once you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them). Persistent cookies can be removed by following your browser help file directions. If you choose to disable cookies, some areas of gomy.co.uk may not work correctly.
