Privacy Policy

IMPORTANT: BY USING GONLA.COM SITES AND SERVICES YOU GIVE YOUR CONSENT THAT ALL PERSONAL DATA THAT YOU SUBMIT MAY BE PROCESSED BY US IN THE MANNER AND FOR THE PURPOSES DESCRIBED IN THE FOLLOWING PRIVACY POLICY.

Privacy Policy

GONLA.com acknowledges your trust in having your booking and payment transactions with us. It is of utmost importance for us to keep your trust in our product and services, therefore we adhere to the following principles to safeguard your privacy.

As a general rule, no personally identifiable information about users of GONLA .com Sites or Services is collected automatically. Personally identifiable data about users of our Sites and Services are known to us only when voluntarily submitted by users of the Site or Services.

Our Privacy Policy details important information regarding the use and disclosure of user information collected on GONLA .com Sites and Services. This Privacy Policy is incorporated into and is subject to our Terms and Conditions. Your use of the Sites and Services and any personal information you provide on our Sites and Services remains subject to the terms of this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Service.

However, any video, image, picture, photo, location or other content posted at the discretion of users onto the GONLA .com Sites and Services becomes published content and is not considered personally identifiable information subject to this Privacy Policy.

Personal Information Collected

GONLA .com will not collect your information when you browse our site.

GONLA .com only collects your personal information as given by you through booking and/or payment transactions.

In order to complete your booking and payment, you will be asked to provide your name, email address and other personal details. All information you submit will be used for that purpose only. For receiving information on latest deals and promotions, we will ask you to opt in or out. We will not be using your email address to send such materials unless you explicitly agree.

Mobile Device Information Collected

For mobile users, we derive location information from your mobile carrier or from the mobile device that you use for our Service. Your location will be displayed and shared with others only in accordance with your privacy settings.

As a result of current standards on mobile devices, we will record certain information about your mobile device any time you use your mobile browser to conduct activities on GONLA.com Sites or Services. This information contains information such as the device manufacturer’s name, device model number, operating system, network carrier, etc. This information is solely used to improve our customer support and services and will never be sold to other entities for any commercial gain.

Personal Information Usage

GONLA.com uses your personally identifiable information and location information to operate, maintain and provide you with the features and functionality of our Sites and Services.

We do not use your contact number, email address or other personally identifiable information or location information to send commercial or marketing messages without your consent or except as part of a specific program or feature that allows you to choose to opt in or out. Nevertheless, we will be using your contact number, email address and/or location information to notify you with non-sale, non-marketing purpose such as changes in our Site and/or Service that may affect your booking process and other customer services related aspects.

We will also use both your personally identifiable information and some non-personally identifiable information (such as cookies, IP address, browser type, aggregated date, etc.) to enhance our provision in terms of Sites and Services, as well as development of new functions, features and other services in accordance to ongoing trends and preference. On some circumstances, GONLA .com will share your personal information with third parties we employ to perform services and analysis on our behalf such as web hosting, market research analysis, credit card transactions, etc. All of our third parties will be signing a non-disclosure agreement to maintain the security and confidentiality of your personal information and to only use it for the sole purpose as per our instruction.

GONLA.com may share your personal information as part of our legal responsibility to respond to legitimate request of law enforcement officials, judicial processes, and other compliance to applicable law, and/or to the purchase or transfer of all or part of our business or company.

Any personal information, location information or content that you voluntarily disclose online (such as forums, discussion boards, blogs, posting comments, your pictures, etc.) will be publicly available for others to view and access.

Cookies and Log Files

GONLA.com exercises the same component as any other web sites to operate its Sites and Services. We use both session-based and persistent cookies which recognize you as the user of GONLA.com Sites and Services. Several features and functions of GONLA.com Sites and Services will require your browser to accept cookies. The session-based cookies are temporary in nature and will disappear once you close your browser; the persistent cookies will continue to recognize you following your subsequent visits to the site. You can set your web browser to reject cookies or to identify a cookie.

We also utilize our servers to record certain information that may uniquely identify your browser. The server logs will collect among others information related to your web request, Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, pages viewed and times spent on each page, browser type and language, number of clicks, as well as cookies that identify your browser. Such information will only be solely used to help us analyze ongoing trends and preferences and will not be linked to personally identifiable information in any kind or way.

Third Parties and Links to Other Sites

GONLA .com collaborates with other companies, such as advertisers, media groups, and payment entities, referred to here as third parties, to support the functionality and features of our Sites and Services. We do not provide any personally identifiable information to such third parties directly. However, please note that once you click to access websites of third parties, you are automatically exposed to the technology used by third parties, which may be able to retrieve information or send you other information according to their respective Privacy Policies. It is advisable that you familiarize yourself with their Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policies. GONLA .com will not be responsible to control the impact of your activities on websites of third parties.

Security Commitment

We are committed to safeguard the security of your account and personally identifiable information by implementing several measurements. First, we set strict standards, which are legally enforceable to all of our employees, third-parties and partners, to safeguard your personal details and usage as you conduct booking and payment through our system. Second, we are integrating the most advanced technology and procedures, including industry standard, into our operational system. Third, we give our customers the ability to manage their own password as the only mechanism to retrieve private information. Nevertheless, GONLA.com is not responsible to warrant the security of any type of information sent by you using public networks. For any breach in our security system, we will post notification on GONLA.COM Sites and Services and/or send email to all registered members to take appropriate actions.
