What Information Is Collected?
“Personally Identifiable Information” means any information that may be used to identify an individual, including, but not limited to, a first and last name, home or other physical address, an email address, phone number or other contact information, whether at work or at home. GoingOn collects the following Personally Identifiable Information from you when you use our Site: first and last name, email address. Any additional information is not required but provided by the user at his/her discretion. GoingOn also automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, cookie information and the page you request. You can change your GoingOn account settings, including your profile information, at any time in the Edit Profile section. You can edit, save drafts and remove your posts at any time, as described in the Help section. If you delete your weblog, we will remove all posts from public view. Because of the way we maintain this service, residual copies of your profile information and other information associated with your account may remain on back-up media. You can choose not to provide us with certain information, but then you might not be able to take advantage of many of our features.
