ABOUT HACHI

HACHI FINDS THE SMARTEST WAY FOR YOU TO CONNECT WITH ANYONE YOU WANT.

Today, your connections are spread across multiple social and professional networks, your smartphone address books, your emails, IMs, etc. So, if you have to reach out to someone new, how would you go about it?

  • Drop a cold mail: but we all know that unless you are a celebrity or a top management person, chances of getting a response are fairly low
  • Look up for that person on your different social and professional networks (one by one)
  • Email few friends to see if they happen to know this person
  • Ask around... check with your co-workers, buddies..

And then, if you happen to find few mutual acquaintances who can connect you with that person -

  • Which one of those acquaintances to ask for the introduction? Favors are currency - you need to use them discreetly.
  • Who amongst them knows the target person well enough?

Hachi helps you get successful introduction with anyone you want - for business, social and personal reasons. It searches through your connections spread across various social and professional networks, and finds the best person in your entire network (based on ranking algorithm) to ask for an introduction. And, it facilitates the introduction (using behavioral cues).

Hachi is the roadmap to your connections graph.Check out the demo video on our home page.

HOW IT WORKS ?

HERE'S HOW HACHI CAN HELP YOU

Hachi (pronounced "Ha-chee") searches through all your professional and social networks to give you the smartest way to reach any one you want. 'Smartest' is the key word here, because not only does Hachi give you all the possible paths to reach your target, but it also recommends the smartest of those paths, based on intelligence about you and your networks. It goes further to actually get you communicating with your target

HOW TO USE HACHI?

  1. Connect your social and professional networks with Hachi.
  2. Start searching for the person you want to connect with. You can search by their: Name, Title, Company, Education, Industry, or Location.
  3. Hachi will display people from your network that meet your search criteria.
  4. Click on “SEE MORE” to learn more about the searched person.
  5. Click on “Find Path” button to find out how you can reach that person.
  6. Hachi will display the different ways you can connect, and suggest the smartest way.
  7. Click on “Get Introduced” to request your contact for an introduction.
