Our Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy! Any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to GoGreece.com will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to. A more detailed explanation about how we safeguard your personal information is described below. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact us at comments@gogreece.com

Resale or disclosure of information to third parties:
GoGreece.com does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including membership forms or email lists.

Browser information collected on the website:
GoGreece.com analyzes our website logs to constantly improve
the value of the materials available on website. Our website logs are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.

Use of cookies:
Cookies may be used throughout the GoGreece.com web site for technical reasons or to enhance user experience. Cookies are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually
browse the site

Privacy of our email lists:
GoGreece.com maintains several email lists to keep GoGreece members informed about breaking events or news relevant to our web site. We also use our mailing lists to send newsletters and special offers available exclusively to our members. Individuals must affirmatively ask to join our mailing lists by contacting GoGreece.com staff directly or by signing up using our online forms available on our web site. We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on our list to anyone. In addition, we configure our list server software to refuse to divulge the email addresses of our list subscribers to anyone but authorized GoGreece.com staff.
