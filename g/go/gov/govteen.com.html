  • Privacy Policy

    GovTeen is committed to safeguarding your privacy online.

    Please read the following policy to understand how your personal information will be treated as you make full use of our many offerings. This policy may change from time to time, so please check back periodically. If you have any comments or questions about any of the below, please contact a member of the GovTeen staff.

    Our promise to you...

    • All the information we collect from you will be for statistical and demographic purposes only
    • We will not give out your information or sell it to others, or to third parties. It will be used for non-commercial purposes only
    • We will not send you junk or "spam" e-mail
    • We may occasionally email you information about the site, or service announcements

    Server Log Files and Web Site Management

    • Like many Web sites, the Service's Web site servers use log files. These logs record aggregate (non-personally identifiable) information about site usage such as Internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser types, Internet service providers, referring/exit pages, pages accessed, platform types, date/time stamps, times of use, and numbers of clicks, for example
    • This information is necessary to analyze trends, administer the site and forums, balance web traffic, evaluate users' electronic browsing in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use
    • We do not link IP addresses to personally identifiable information except as may be required to comply with law
    • This information may be used by us, at our discretion, to prevent abuse of the site and/or ensure/increase information and visitor/member security, or to enforce any of our site policies

    Cookies

    • Cookies are small files stored on a computer's hard drive to simplify and improve a user's Web experience. A Web site may store information in a cookie about your computer configuration so that it can more efficiently provide information to you the next time you visit the site. Or, your browser program might save a cookie with your username and password so that a Web site's server will automatically recognise you the next time you access the site
    • GovTeen uses cookies to store the login information to the forums. The option to save your login information is entirely up to yourself, however you yourself are responsible for ensuring that your account password and profile remains secure from others using your computer. We recommend you do not check the Log me in automatically box when you login the board will only keep you logged in for a preset time. This prevents misuse of your account by anyone else. To stay logged in check the box during login, this is not recommended if you access the board from a shared computer, e.g. library, internet cafe, school, etc
    • We do not belong to any groups that track users across Web sites

    Communications from GovTeen

    • Site Updates - We send all new subscribers to the forum a welcome e-mail and sometimes other information. Subscribers who would like to receive information from us about site updates and information may do so. See the Choice section of this Policy to learn how to opt in or opt out of receiving these types of communications
    • Service Announcements - We send forums subscribers Service-related announcements from time to time. For example, we may send you an e-mail announcement about a change in operating policies. You may not opt-out of these Service-related communications, though you can deactivate your account if you wish. If you fail to check your Service account and primary e-mail address for Service-related announcements, you may miss important information about the Service for example
    • Member Service - We communicate with you to provide requested services and support for questions and issues relating to your forums account and this site. We will reply to your requests for member services by e-mail

    Other Sites and Links

    • The GovTeen.com web site contains links to other sites. GovTeen.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these other sites
    • We encourage you to be aware when you leave our site and to read the privacy policies of every site that collects personally identifiable information about you

    Security

    • Personally identifying information is protected in a secure database and by passwords. While no security system is fail-safe, the GovTeen staff will take reasonable measures, within our power, to ensure that our server and database are secure
    • embers/visitors are likewise responsible for taking reasonable precautions to protect the secrecy of their passwords
    • Due to the nature of the forum software we are using, we do not store any members passwords themselves on the server, only MD5 hashes of them

    Preferences

    • We store information that you provide to personalise your settings on the site, in particular on the GovTeen Forums, so that you don't have to change these settings each time you use the site or the forums
    • We do not share your preferences with third parties

    How do I prevent my username appearing in the online user listings?

    • In your profile (user control panel) you will find an option Hide your online status, if you switch this on you'll only appear to board administrators or to yourself. You will be counted as a hidden user

    I keep getting unwanted private messages

    • If you keep receiving unwanted private messages from someone inform any Administrator, they have the power to prevent a user from sending private messages at all

    Preventing others from seeing your information

    • The forum software we provide allows you to prevent all regular users from seeing your e-mail address
    • It is your decision as to how much information you provide in your account profile
    • GovTeen strongly recommends you select the option in your user control panel to prevent users seeing your e-mail address, or other similar personal addresses
    • We recommend you do not give out "Instant Messenger" (IM) addresses, examples include (but are not limited to) MSN, AIM, YIM etc
    • We recommend you never give out your name, address or location to anyone
    • We accept no responsibily if you receive unsolicited/spam e-mails. GovTeen has no control over activities and services outside of this site, with examples including (but not limited to) e-mails and all "Instant Messenger" (IM) services. We provide you with a fully functional forum system, with a wide variety of services, and thus we strongly recommend you make use of the privacy (and other) functions it provides

    Changes to this Privacy Policy

    • If we decide to change this Policy, we will post those changes on the homepage of GovTeen.com, or in other places we deem appropriate, so our members are aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it
    • We will use information in accordance with the Privacy Policy under which the information was collected
    • Your decision to continue using this website/service after we make any changes to this Policy, shall be deemed to be your express consent to the changes in the revised Policy
    • As always, if you do not agree with this privacy policy, or any future revised version of it, you have the option of discontinuing use of this site and its services and/or requesting that we delete your personally identifying information from the site

    Legal Disclaimer

    • Although we make every reasonable effort to preserve your privacy as described in this Policy, we will disclose personally identifiable information about you without your consent when required by law in order to comply with a valid legal process
    • We may also use or disclose personally identifiable information about you without your consent to protect our members in emergency situations, to enforce our rights directly or indirectly with you, and for violations of the sites Terms of Service and all other site policies

    Anything Else...?

    • Are we forgetting anything? Do you have any other questions we can answer for you and place on this page so others don't have the same questions? Is any part of this Policy, or any other of our site policies, unclear in any way? Please contact us
