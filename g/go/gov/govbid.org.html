I. Profile Registration

I. Add an operational balance to your account via the “Secure Area”.

To bid on any of the contract available you would need an operational balance, deposited and confirmed at your G.B.P. account. As soon as your Account Registration process is complete and you have successfully set your payment wallet data you may go to “My Wallet” section of the profile, and by clicking at the DEPOSIT button which is located at the “Transactions” area of the page, choose a desirable payment method and specify a sum you would like to enroll your profile with.
You then will be redirected to the merchant gate, so in order to finish an operation it will be asked from you to send an exact amount required to complete your deposit.


After performing all the necessary steps, where based on the merchant's technical specification, you may be redirected back to the G.B.P. account (E-Payment) or will complete an operation by waiting for at least 3 confirmations from the network (Blockchain-based transactions).


Please be aware, that in order to successfully enroll your account with an available balance, you would always have to send an exact amount required by the merchant (including all the decimals) and to make sure our system will display your deposit in no time, we recommend you to allow a maximum Transaction Fee (for Bitcoin & similar payments).

II. Bids & Contracts

II. Perform your bids on the selected List of Contracts

By clicking at the “Available Contracts” section of your profile, you will be able to explore all contracts with different metrics bound to them. Higher daily M.D.F. will allow you to gain more revenue, yet might take a longer cycle under the greater entrance bid, as by performing your bids on contracts with a short life-span, may create a rapid return of your bids.


You soon will see, that despite the fact of many contracts to be available for bidding, each one of them is quite unique. Having such a vast number of contracts in stock will expand your possibilities at creating an exclusive strategy which fits your needs as a venture backer in the first place.

The expiration timer will define when exactly the contract is to be removed from the listing, thus all users may obtain their principal, in addition to the Margin Difference in a form of revenue.


It should be noted, that to obtain a particular contract’s terms there might be a different sum of an entrance bid required to work with a given contract. Contracts with greater entrance bid may have some pretty bankable margin difference, yet typically with a cost to the expiration timer, which eventually may lead for longer waiting period prior to the final bids sell-off. Also, it is very important to understand, that when you bid at any given moment, contract's expiration timer will continuously define its key metrics, resulting with a decreased M.D.F. if your bid hasn’t been placed within the first hours of the lot's timer. Jumping in early on is the key of climbing to the peak of your bidding performance!

III. Referrals & Teamwork

III. After the bidding, unite with teams to gain extra results.

Each user at G.B.P. has a revenue-based referral program available by default. Your account will be credited with 20% affiliate commission from all takings made by your downline bidders. Sharing your affiliate link will allow new users to create an account within your referral structure, thus to provide you with a passive income.


It is important to work as a team, due to the fact, that your 20% commission will be given only after your invited partners make revenue from their bids.

Supporting your colleagues and exploring new ways of creating an effective strategy of bidding will result in a higher potential profit for all players.


When you will gain enough experience as a team leader, you may access an extended affiliate program offer thus becoming a Representative of the platform.

To learn more about G.B.P.’s unique opportunities, please visit our “Affiliate Program” web-page.

IV. Extra Opportunities

IV. Grow your available balance to acquire GB Packages & perform continuous bids.

There is a whole new world of advantageous possibilities which may become reachable under the list of achievement-based account’s upgrades, available as “GB Packages”. Based on your bidding and affiliate activity, total amount withdrawn and deposited, you might become eligible to access a particular package, thus to extend the functionality of your profile by partially removing restrictions.

V. Withdrawing revenue

V. Withdraw your revenue & enjoy all the fruits of your labor.

At some point, it is the right time to make your revenue work for you. Going back to “My Wallet” section will allow you to request a particular amount of digital funds, when by clicking at the “Withdraw” button and confirming the request, funds are about to be transferred to the specified wallet…


After submitting your withdrawal request, the transaction's status will be displayed at the History of your Wallet, when upon the expiration of the security timer, the amount requested will be instantly transferred at your e-wallet.


Requesting your funds at the weekend day (after Friday) will add up 2 additional days to the withdrawal timer. There is a maximum of 3 (Three) Pending Withdrawal requests allowed at all times.


G.B.P.’s Administrative & Accounting teams are constantly monitoring the financial activity within the system, hence there is a set of security measures applied which might be useful for any user to become familiar with. To find out more, please explore the “G.B.P. Financial Monitoring” web-page.
