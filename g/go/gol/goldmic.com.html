Cookies

You should be aware that non-personally identifiable information and data may be automatically collected through the standard operation of Goldmic's web servers or through the use of "cookies." Cookies are small text files a website can use to recognize repeat users. Generally, cookies work by assigning a unique number to the user that has no meaning outside of the website that he or she is visiting. Goldmic uses cookies to recognize visitors and more quickly provide personalized content or grant You unimpeded access to registered areas of our website.

Information gathered through cookies also helps Us measure use of our Site. Cookie data allows Us to track usage behavior and compile data that We can use to improve the Site and target advertising. This data will generally be used in aggregate form; however, We may track specific users from time to time to help Us improve our products and services. Sometimes, advertisers or third parties hosting data for Goldmic will issue cookies. We cannot control the use of these cookies or the information that they gather.

You can easily turn off cookies in your browser. However, cookies are necessary to provide You with certain features available on our Site. Some areas of our Site, such as the login system, will not function properly without cookies. Enabling cookies ensures a smooth, efficient visit to our Site.


IP Addresses

An IP address is a number automatically assigned to your computer whenever You access the Internet. All computer identification on the Internet is conducted with IP addresses, which allow computers and servers to recognize and communicate with each other. Upon visits to our Site, We will log your IP addresses, or the location of your computer on the Internet, for systems administration, troubleshooting, record keeping, advertising reporting, improvement of our products and service and prevention of abusive activity on the Site.
