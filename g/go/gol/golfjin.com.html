Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us and we do not share your information or your e-mail address with third parties without your permission, except to the extent necessary to complete payment processing on our website. Any information submitted through our online order forms is transferred via secure encryption and kept in secure databases.

When you supply information about yourself for a specific purpose, we use the information for that purpose only (such as to provide the service or information you have requested). For example, you may be asked to give us information to receive information from us or to register for an event.

We maintain a strict "No-Spam" policy. We do not intend to sell, rent, or otherwise give your e-mail address to a third-party, without your consent.

We collect certain anonymous information for every visitor to this site. This may include pages viewed, date and time, browser type, etc. We or our advertisers may send a "cookie" to your computer. You can choose to not accept cookies by changing the settings of your browser. Doing so, however, may interfere with your ability to use certain sections and features of the Golfjin website.

Contact us at anytime if you questions about our privacy policies.
