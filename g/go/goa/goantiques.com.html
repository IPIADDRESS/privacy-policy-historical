Privacy Policy

Information Collection and Use

1) GoAntiques collects personal information when you register to buy or sell with us, when you use GoAntiques services, when you visit GoAntiques pages or the pages of certain GoAntiques partners, and when you enter promotions or sweepstakes. GoAntiques may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies.

2) When you register to buy or sell items on the site we ask for information such as your name, address, email address, phone number, cell number, fax number, user name, credit card information and notification preferences. Once you register with GoAntiques and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.

3) GoAntiques collects information about your transactions.

4) GoAntiques automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including GoAntiques cookie information. GoAntiques uses two types of cookies, transient and persistent, to recognize our members and remember shopping list items. GoAntiques uses transient cookies to provide continuity from page to page. Within a single visit, these cookies store information such as your wish list and shopping list to simplify checkout. They do not contain any personally identifiable information. GoAntiques uses persistent cookies to identify members after the first visit. These cookies remain on the hard drive of a PC and allow a user to be recognized when entering our site. To access any secure areas such as your account information, you must still enter your password. You can disable cookies at any time through your browser options and still view the site. The cookie itself does not tell us who you are, your email address or anything else personal. We use cookies to provide our visitors with a better experience on our site.

5) GoAntiques uses your information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfill your requests or improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

1) GoAntiques does not sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except under the following circumstances:

a) We provide limited information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or with GoAntiques under confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your personal information to help GoAntiques communicate with you about offers from GoAntiques and our partners. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information.

b) We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to any person, violations of GoAntiques’ terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.

c) We may transfer information about you if GoAntiques is acquired by or merged with another company.

Data Security and Storage

Notwithstanding these procedures, we do not accept any liability for misuse of the site by others or from viruses, or links to other sites or to objectionable material.

We do not guarantee the preservation of data, records or other information that may expire or be removed or deleted, all without notice or liability. Registered Sellers grant GoAntiques non-exclusive publishing rights in print or electronically of uploaded images and data.

Records Retention, Usage
