The Way We Use Information

We use the information you provide about yourself when placing an order only to complete that order. We are required to collect this information in order to complete your travel documents. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete your reservation.

Personal information collected by goaheadvacations.com is stored in secure operating environments that are not available to the public. However, should you provide personal information through e-mail exchanges, the Privacy Policy does not cover this type of transfer of your personal information. You should always be careful when distributing your personal information in this manner.

We use the information you provide about someone else only to confirm message delivery. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to confirm that message. Information you provide to us to create a message is only used for that purpose, and it is only disclosed to the recipient.

We use return e-mail addresses to answer the e-mail we receive. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties.

You can indicate that you would like to receive our catalog as well as updates on our new products and services. Information you submit on our website will not be used for these purposes unless you request these services.

Goaheadvacations.com may provide aggregate statistics about our customers, sales, traffic patterns, and related site information to reputable third-party vendors, but these statistics will include no personally identifying information.

Finally, we never use or share the personally identifying information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described above without also providing you an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise prohibit such unrelated uses.

Use of "Cookies"

"Cookies" are pieces of information that a website transfers to your computer's hard disk for record-keeping purposes. The use of cookies is an industry standard, used to provide useful features for customers. Cookies do not personally identify users, although they do identify a user's computer. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually change your browser to prevent that. However, you may not be able to take full advantage of our website if you do so.
