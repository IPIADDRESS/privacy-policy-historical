Privacy Policy
 

1. How is personal information collected?

In order to provide customers with a quality service, GoDateNow offers customers the ability to subscribe to our newsletter, or sign up as a community member, In order to provide these services, GoDateNow must collect personal information about customers. Collection of this information will occur at the time you create an account through the GoDateNow website.

2. What personal information does GoDateNow collect and why?

If you register as a member we need to collect your email address. The reason we need this information is so we can contact you verify your email and activate your account.

3. Will personal information be given to anyone else?

GoDateNow will not sell or trade personal information about you to or with third parties. GoDateNow will only disclose information about you, if you have given your consent, so that you can be provided with promotional material and special offers. Otherwise, GoDateNow will only disclose personal information if this is required by law or permitted under the Privacy Act.

4. Security of personal information

GoDateNow protects the personal information it collects in a secure database, which can only be accessed by employees with the highest security clearance. GoDateNow personel are required, to treat personal information held by GoDateNow as confidential, and to maintain the confidentiality of that personal information.

5. Access and correction

Under the Privacy Act, you have a right to seek access to information that GoDateNow holds about you. You also have the right to ask us to correct information about you, which is inaccurate, incomplete or out of date. As an GoDateNow member, you are able to access or correct the personal information that GoDateNow holds about you - simply by logging onto our website at http://www.GoDateNow.com/ Both members and non-members may ask us to correct information, which is inaccurate, incomplete or out of date. To correct any innacurate information on GoDateNow please send an email to admin at mailto:admin@GoDateNow.com.

6. Links to other websites

Sometimes our website contains links to third party websites, for your convenience and information. When you access a third party website, please understand that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of that site. We suggest that you review the privacy policies of each site you visit.
