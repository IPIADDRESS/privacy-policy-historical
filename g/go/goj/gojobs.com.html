Examples of areas on GO Jobs Web sites where we collect personal or combined personal and demographic data are pages where you can open a MyGOJobs account, sign up to use a service, post a resume, enter a contest or purchase a product.

Of course, you can correct or update your MyGOJobs account profile and resume at any time. Moreover, you can delete your resume from our online database or close your MyGOJobs account at any time, in which event we will remove all our copies of your resume and MyGOJobs account profile information except for an archival copy which is not accessible on the Internet.
