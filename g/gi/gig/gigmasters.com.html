Log Files
Like most standard Web site servers we use log files. This includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses, etc. are linked to personally identifiable information to help identify you and your shopping cart.
Cookies
Our site uses cookies to keep track of your shopping cart. We use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests and to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our site. These cookies are not linked to any personally identifiable information.
Contact Information
Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information such as their name and e-mail address. We use this information to initially enable our Gigmasters Booking Service. This will mean your contact information could eventually be shared with potential bands/event organizers who are interested in hiring you. We also use customer contact information from the registration form to send the user promotional material on behalf of some of our partners. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor when necessary for following up with their account. Users may opt-out of receiving future promotional mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.
