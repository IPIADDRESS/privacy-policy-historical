In order to provide complete functionality and ensure, to the extent possible, safe, secure transactions, the Site uses the Personal Information collected from registered users, amongst other things, to:

  • Contact registered users (who have provided their consent) with targeted marketing and promotional services and provide them with information on important changes and developments to the Site and its services.

Sharing Your Information

The Owner may access a registered user's Personal Information and disclose such Personal Information:

  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements;

Use of Cookies

The Site uses "cookies" which refers to a small text file sent from a server to your hard disk by a website browser. Cookies contain a unique identification number that identifies your browser, but not you, to the Owner's computers each time you visit the Site. Cookies allow the Site to authenticate users, customize the Site experience, track user preferences, and receive information on the volume and characteristics of website traffic. Certain Site services are only available through the use of cookies. Some browsers may allow you to decline cookies; doing so, however, will interfere with the functionality of the Site.

The Owner uses IP addresses to analyze trends, administer the Site, track user movement, and collect general demographic information for non-individualised use. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

The Site may contain links to other websites. The Owner does not control or affirm the privacy practices of such other sites. Users are encouraged to be aware when they leave the Site and read the privacy statements of all other websites that may be accessed through the Site. This Privacy Policy applies solely to information collected by the Owner or any authorised person on the Site.
