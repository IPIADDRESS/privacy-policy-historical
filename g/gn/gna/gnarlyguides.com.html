Cookies

This site uses the “Doubleclick” cookies, which tracks use of gnarlyguides.com and user behavior concerning ads, products and the services we offer. “Doubleclick” cookies contain no personally identifiable information.

A cookie is a small text file that is downloaded onto device (eg a computer or smartphone) when the user accesses a website. It allows the website to recognize that user’s device and store some information about the user’s preferences or past actions.

You may decide to opt out of personalized advertising by disabling “Doubleclick” cookies. It is possible to do so - here .
