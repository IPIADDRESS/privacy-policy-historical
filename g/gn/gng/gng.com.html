Your privacy is important to Georgia Natural Gas®. Personal information is required only to sign up for service with GNG. Any information provided will be used exclusively by GNG, our agents, suppliers, and third-party service providers for internal use or to respond to your request. GNG may, however, release your name and address in connection with joint marketing efforts and strategic alliances. GNG will not sell or distribute any other personal information collected at this site to any unaffiliated party without your consent, unless doing so is required by law or necessary to protect or deliver our services, enforce terms of service, or prevent a violation of someone's rights. GNG also may exchange information with other companies and organizations for fraud protection, credit risk reduction, and collection activities.

GNG may send you e-mails with promotional offers only if you opt-in to receiving such emails. If you would no longer like to receive emailed special event information, sales notifications or other promotional messages from GNG, please click the link at the bottom of any email you receive from us and follow the instructions, or alternatively you may send an email to GNGemail@response.gng.com with the word “UNSUBSCRIBE” or “REMOVE” in the subject line. Your email address will be removed from our marketing list. Please allow us a reasonable period of time in order to satisfy your request, as some promotions may already be in process.

From visitors to our Web site, GNG's server gathers nonpersonal information (“cookies”) for statistical and market research purposes. A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's hard drive containing information about the user. The information obtained from “cookies” is used to improve and update the site. This information includes, but is not limited to, the type of browser, domain name, and total visitors. Once you close your browser, the cookie will terminate.

Linked sites are not under the control of GNG, and GNG is therefore not responsible for the content or privacy policy, which may differ from the GNG policy, of any linked site. GNG is providing these links as a convenience to its visitors. GNG reserves the right to terminate a link at any time. Third parties are not authorized to include active links to the GNG Web site without the express written consent of GNG.

 

By using the GNG Web site you agree with our Privacy Policy. If you do not accept the policy, please do not use our Web site. Revisions to the privacy policy may be made without notice, at any time, and for any reason. Changes will be posted on the site. If you continue to use the GNG Web site after an amendment has been made, you agree to the new provisions. The GNG site is secured by Equifax or Verisign.
