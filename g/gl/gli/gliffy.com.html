Gliffy Privacy Policy

Gliffy knows that you care how information about you is used and shared, 
and we appreciate your trust that we will do so carefully and sensibly. 
By visiting Gliffy, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

What This Privacy Policy Covers

* This privacy policy pertains to the use of the Gliffy web site.
* This privacy policy covers how Gliffy treats personal information 
  that Gliffy collects and receives. Personal information is information 
  about you that is personally identifiable like your name, address, 
  email address, or phone number.

Information Collection and How it is used

General Information

* Gliffy collects personal information when you register with Gliffy
  to use Gliffy services or to post content on Gliffy.
* When you register we ask for your email address and possibly certain optional 
  information such as your name, gender, biographical information, city,
  country, and other personal interests.
* Gliffy automatically receives and records information on our server 
  logs from your browser, including your IP address, cookie, 
  and the page you request.
* Gliffy uses information for the following general purposes: 
  products and services provision, billing, identification and authentication, 
  services improvement, contact, research, and anonymous reporting.

IP Addresses are automatically reported by your browser each time you view a web page. 
Your IP address is stored in our logs when you register with Gliffy.

IP addresses may be used for various purposes, including:

* To diagnose or service technology problems reported by our users or engineers 
  that are associated with the IP addresses controlled by a specific web company or ISP.
* To tailor advertising based on geographic area or other information derived 
  from your IP address. Aggregate information derived from IP addresses may 
  also be reported to advertisers.
* To estimate the total number of users visiting Gliffy from specific geographical regions.
* To make sure that people are not voting for themselves or friends during contests or promotions.

Cookies

A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique 
identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site's computers and 
stored on your computer.

* You can configure your browser to accept all cookies, reject all cookies, 
  or notify you when a cookie is set.
* If you reject all cookies, you may not be able to use Gliffy products 
  or services.
* Gliffy uses its own cookies for a number of purposes, including to:
      o Access your information when you "log in".
      o Keep track of preferences you specify while you are using Gliffy's services.
      o Display the most appropriate advertising banners, based on your interests and activity on Gliffy.
      o Estimate and report our total audience size and traffic.
      o Conduct research to improve Gliffy's content and services.
* Gliffy lets other companies (such as Google or AdBright) that show advertisements or provide statistical information 
  on some of our pages set and access their cookies on your computer. 
  Other entities' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy 
  policies, not this one. Advertisers or other entities do not have access to Gliffy's cookies.

Information Sharing and Disclosure 

* When you publish diagrams, and Content contained in those diagrams will be visible to the public.
* If you post personal information online that is accessible to the public, you may receive 
  unsolicited messages from other parties in return.
* Gliffy may be required to disclose personally identifiable information under special circumstances, 
  such as to comply with subpoenas or when your actions violate the Gliffy Terms of Use.
* Gliffy may share demographic information with business partners, 
  such as "53% of users who regularly user Gliffy are between 18 and 24" or the like. 
  No personally identifiable information will ever be used without your permission 
  (i.e. we will ask you for a quote or testimonial)
* Gliffy does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with 
  other people or nonaffiliated companies except to provide products or services 
  you've requested or when we have your permission.
* It may be necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, 
  or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving 
  potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of Gliffy's 
  Terms of Use, or as otherwise required by law.
* We will transfer information about you if Gliffy is acquired by or merged 
  with another company. In this event, Gliffy will notify you by email or by putting a 
  prominent notice on the Gliffy web site before information about you is transferred 
  and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.  

Editing and Deleting Account Information

* You can change your Gliffy Account Information, at any time.
* We may send you certain communications relating to the Gliffy service, 
  such as service announcements and administrative messages, that are considered 
  part of your Gliffy account.  
* We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe 
  reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products 
  or services to you or in order to do their jobs.

What Choices and Access Do I Have?

* As discussed above, you can always choose not to provide information, even though 
  it might be needed to take advantage of certain Gliffy features.
* You can review the personal information you provide and make changes to that information, 
  or to the settings for your account, at any time, by logging in to your account and
  editing the information on your profile page.  

Children

Gliffy is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. 
If you are under 13, you may not submit information about yourself to Gliffy.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

Gliffy may update this policy at any time for any reason. 
If there are any significant changes to how we handle personal information we will 
send a notice to the primary email address specified in your Gliffy account or 
by placing a prominent notice on our site.

Questions
