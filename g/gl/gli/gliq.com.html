We at Global IntelliSystems value your business and your privacy. As a result, we are making this statement which describes what informaiton we may collect about you, how we use this information, and with whom we may share it.

As long as we continue to do business with you, we will send you a privacy notice, no less than once a year. We will also send you a notice if there are any changes to this policy.

WHY WE COLLECT INFORMATION

We collect information about you and your firm that is necessary to process, service, and maintain our products to give you the best product available on the market. For example, we may collect public information on your company to determine what services we offer are best suited for your company.

WHAT TYPES OF INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Most of the information we collect is obtained from you, and if applicable from your employees. Examples of information we collect from you are name, address, phone number, corporate identification, length of time in business, business status, and business purpose. We may also collect information from third parties for verification purposes such as length of time in business, D&B number and status, and branch office information.

WHAT INFORMATION DO WE DISCLOSE AND TO WHOM

The information we collect is used to process and open accounts that we service. We may share this information, as permitted by law, with nonaffiliated third parties in order to monitor bandwidth consumption, maintain off-site equipment, and perform routine security audits of our services and systems. The information we may disclose will never contain e-mail addresses belonging to you or your clients, nor will the information ever contain information about your clients, the demographic makeup of your clients, or any of the mail-merge information you may have stored in your account.

HOW WE PROTECT INFORMATION

We restrict access to your information to our employees and service providers who need to have it in order to perform the daily operations and maintenance of your account. We have in place electronic and procedural safeguards to ensure the privacy of your information, especially to ensure your mailing lists or any other data you may have stored on this service are tightly controlled and never released to the public.
