Cookies: "Cookies" give users a special, random ID that are small text files, which are stored on the hard drive of a user's computer. IP Addresses: GlobeSt.com also logs IP addresses for systems administration and troubleshooting purposes. (Your IP address indicates the location of your computer on the Internet.) We do not link IP addresses to anything personally identifiable.
