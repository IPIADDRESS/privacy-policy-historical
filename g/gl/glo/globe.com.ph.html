Privacy Policy

 

 USER PRIVACY STATEMENT
 (Our Pledge to You, Our Customer)

Globe Telecom respects your privacy. Any information provided by you is held with the utmost care and confidence and shall not be used in ways to which you have not consented. Globe Telecom has therefore taken several steps to ensure that the information you have provided is processed accurately and confidentially.
 USE OF INFORMATION
 Globe Telecom will not collect information pertaining to you other than the information you specifically and knowingly provided to Globe Telecom. Examples of such information are: name, postal address, e-mail address, phone number, birthday, and product commentary.

Globe Telecom will not reveal, sell or provide, to any third party, any identifiable information pertaining to you personally, without your consent. Globe Telecom, however, reserves the right to perform statistical analysis of its customers' behavior and characteristics in order to improve the Service and products offered to you. Globe Telecom will provide only aggregated data from statistical analysis with its affiliates, partners or other third parties.
 SECURITY OF THE SERVICE
 Globe Telecom utilizes several security measures to help safeguard the confidentiality of your personal identifiable information. These practices include, but are not limited to firewalls, username and password authentication, 128-bit Secure Socket Layers (SSL) encryption, data encryption, and other industry standard practices.
