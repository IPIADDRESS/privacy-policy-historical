Correct/Update
Corrections or updates to your account information can be made on-line, once you have logged-on to the globalindustrial.com site. The "Your Account" page contains links to Add or Edit personal information, addresses, payment methods and shipping preferences. If you have any questions about your company profile, send an e-mail to service@globalindustrial.com.

Use of Information/Cookies
A cookie is a small text file, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier. When you visit a web site, that site's computer asks your computer for permission to store this file in a part of your hard drive specifically designated for cookies. Each website can only send and access its own cookie to your browser and only if your browser's preferences allow it.

How Do We Use Information We Collect From Cookies?

Our website uses cookies to differentiate you from other customers. In some cases, we also use cookies to prevent you from seeing unnecessary advertisements or to make it more convenient to login to our website. Cookies, in conjunction with IP addresses and our web server's log files, allow us to calculate the aggregate number of people visiting our web site, the click stream to and through our web site and which parts of the site are most popular. This helps us gather feedback in order to constantly improve our web site and better serve our customers.

Security
