The Global Direct Media Privacy policy explains what information is collected via its websites (www.globaldirectmedia.com, globaldirectmedia.directtracks.com & www.globaldirectmedia.com/blog) and how or when this information may be used.

GlobalDirectMedia.Com
The only information stored through globaldirectmedia.com currently is when somebody enquires via our contact page. This information can be the applicants name, phone number and email. The information is stored offline and solely used to respond to the query being made.

GlobalDirectMedia.Directtracks.Com
If you are signed up to our affiliate service then you can read through your terms of agreement by signing into your account. Click account info on the top navigation and from there select terms of agreement.

GlobalDirectMedia.Com/Blog
Currently no information is stored via our blog.

Any changes to the way Global Direct Media uses the information gathered via its sites will be added to this policy at least 30 days prior to them coming into effect. We will also contact you for your consent before going ahead.

If you have registered but no longer wish to receive email updates from GlobalDirectMedia please contact by one of the methods listed below. You will then be removed from our distribution list as soon as possible. Further more, if you would like to check or update any information you have sent us please contact us on the email provided below.

Our sites are likely to contain external links. Once you have left GlobalDirectMedia via one of these links our privacy policy no longer applies. You are encouraged to read the privacy statements of these linked sites as their policy may differ from ours.

Sometimes we may have to disclose personal information when it is required by law. This action is only taken when necessary to conform to edicts of the law or to comply with legal process served on Global Direct Media.
