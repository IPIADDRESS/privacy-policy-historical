Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which Gemioli collects, uses, maintains and discloses information collected from Users (each, a “User”). Gemioli applications and games (an "App", and collectively "Apps") are intended for use by the entire family, including children with adult engagement and supervision. Gemioli website http://www.gemioli.com ("Website") and related services, including Apps, (taken together, the "Service") are for the sole use of parents.

2. Collection and Use of Personal Information (Service and Apps)

The term "personal information" refers to personally identifiable information through which a User can be identified as an individual.

If Gemioli Apps include social media, web links, contact forms and App Store links ("App Links"), these resources are found only in a locked parent menu. App Links are used only to connect parents to other Gemioli Apps. The only information Gemioli stores is the data that the Users submit through the contact forms of Gemioli Apps. Gemioli does not share this personally identifiable information (such as name, address, email or phone) with third party companies or individuals. It used to contact with Users and to improve Gemioli Apps and Services.

Gemioli does not automatically collect personal information through the Website. The only information Gemioli stores is the data that the Users submit through the contact form of the Website. Gemioli does not share personally identifiable information (such as name, address, email or phone) with third party companies or individuals. The Website does not use "cookies" or any other tracking technologies.

3. Collection and Use of Non-Personal Information (Service and Apps)

The term "Non-personal information" refers to information that does not directly identify a User.

Gemioli collects non-personal information only through Google Analytics. This data is in a form that does not permit direct association with any specific individual. All such information is collected via Google Analytics online service and is used for statistical purposes to continue creating better Apps for children and parents.

4. Confidentiality

Gemioli has taken security measures to protect the information collected from Users, which will be kept strictly confidential. Access to personal data is restricted only to the employees who require access to such information in order to perform their duties and/or to develop or improve Gemioli Apps and Service.

6. Disclosure to Third Parties

Gemioli does not share personal User's information with third parties unless for legal reasons.

7. Children

Gemioli do not offer products for purchase by children, and do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are under 13, you may use and access the Gemioli Apps only with the involvement of a parent or guardian. Gemioli respects the privacy of parents and children and is committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act. If a parent believes that his or her child has submitted personal information to Gemioli, he or she can contact us. We will promptly delete the information upon learning that it relates to a child under the age of 13.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@gemioli.com
