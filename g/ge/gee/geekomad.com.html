We are committed to ensuring that every user’s needs are addressed.

Collection of Personal Information:

In the course of delivering this site to you, geekOmad.com does NOT collect any personally-identifiable information about you, such as your name, address, phone number or email address from users of this web site without your knowledge, and are taking measures to protect your privacy.

DoubleClick Dart Cookies: Third-party vendors, including Google, also use cookies to serve ads on our website based on a user’s prior visits. These companies typically use a cookie or third party web beacon to collect this information. To learn more about this behavioral advertising practice or to opt-out of this type of advertising, you can visit http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp.

Subscribers may unsubscribe anytime, and every email delivered will contain a link which can be used for that purpose.

You can send email requesting help and your email will NOT be shared in any way with any third party(ies), and will only be used to send replies to you.
