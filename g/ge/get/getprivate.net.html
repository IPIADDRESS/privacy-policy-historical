Last Modified: October 19th, 2012

This document describes what information is collected by us from any user of the GetPrivate VPN (collectively or individually "User(s)"), as well as our use and disclosure of that information.

NOTE TO KIDS: GETPRIVATE VPN IS NOT AVAILABLE TO PERSONS UNDER THE AGE OF 18.
If you are under 18 years of age, please do not use GetPrivate VPN.


OUR COMMITMENT TO USER PRIVACY:
GetPrivate does not collect any personally identifiable information on GetPrivate VPN. GetPrivate VPN automatically collects certain non-personally identifiable information from Users that is used in the aggregate only. GetPrivate does not collect, store, or share any permanent identifiers of Users, including IP addresses. Please note that GetPrivate VPN only enables privacy and encrypts user Internet sessions when it is turned ON. Having GetPrivate VPN on the desktop, without turning it ON does not do anything. Unless Users turn GetPrivate VPN ON and opt into the service, GetPrivate VPN will be completely idle.

However, please note that this is not a guarantee that such information may not be accessed, disclosed, altered, or destroyed by breach of any of our physical, technical, or managerial safeguards. We recommend getting anti-virus/anti-malware solutions from one of our reputable partners in order to also help secure your computer.


THE INFORMATION GETPRIVATE VPN COLLECTS:
"Automatically Collected" Information. When you use the GetPrivate VPN, some information may be automatically recorded using different types of proprietary technology.
