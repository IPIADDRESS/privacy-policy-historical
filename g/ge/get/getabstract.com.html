Privacy Statement

At getAbstract we understand that your personal information is yours. We support the principle that online consumers are entitled to full disclosure about how we collect and use the information you give us. Therefore, we want you to know that we adhere to the following principles and policies:

  • To continually improve our products, we collect the minimum profile information necessary to understand our customers.
  • We do not intentionally collect information from children under the age of 13.
  • Your electronic purchase is secure: personal information and payment information is encrypted using the highest standards available and sent in a secure connection.
  • When you make a purchase from us, we create a customer profile.
  • getAbstract does not sell, share or otherwise distribute this customer profile or the email addresses to any outside companies or organization. Also, all personal information will be removed should this service cease to exist.
  • You may view or make changes to your customer profile and personal information at any time by clicking the MyAccount button on the top right corner of this page using your person login information.
  • You may ask at anytime to have all of your personal information removed. We keep the information on our system, unless it needs to be removed for other reasons.
  • We use cookies only for automatic login if you choose this option. A cookie will store your username and password on the computer from where you access getAbstract.
  • We do not store any credit card numbers on our system.
  • We will respond to questions you have about your privacy rights. To ask questions send us an email to the following address: privacy@getAbstract.com.
