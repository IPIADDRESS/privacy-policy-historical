Cookies

Cookies are small text files containing a user's settings and other data used by websites, and are stored on the user's computer. They are transmitted as parcels of text sent by a server to a Web client (usually a browser) and then sent back unchanged by client each time it accesses that server. Cookies are required to use the Flow service.

We use semi-permanent cookies to record current session information. Your browser will remain logged-in to your Flow account until you explicity log out or a certain period of time elapses. Log out to prevent others from accessing your account contents.
