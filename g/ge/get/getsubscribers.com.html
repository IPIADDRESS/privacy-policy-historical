Need more answer?
  • How does GetSubscribers work?

    We display your ad on many websites on the Internet, along with the fields where visitors may leave their name and e-mail address if they are interested in receiving more information about the product/service that is described in the ad. The information they submit is instantly delivered to your GetSubscribers account.

  • How fast can an Opt-in list
    be built?

    The time of completing your lead campaign depends on what is the demand for what you are offering in your ad. While some campaigns can get a few signups every day, other get around 25-40.

  • How will my leads be delivered?

    You will choose the target for your leads upon configuring your account. By default, your GetSubscribers leads are stored in GetSubscribers account, from which you can import them to a .csv file. Additionally, they can be instantly delivered GetResponse account or via E-mail.

  • What kind of ad should I submit
    for the campaign?

    The ad that you submit to us at the time of purchase should consist of the title (up to 50 characters long) and the body (up to 150 characters long). Please note that a space is also counted as a character.

  • Will I be able to see my ad when
    it is running?

    Your ad will be rotating on different pages, along with other active campaigns. Therefore, we cannot tell exactly what time and where will your ad be shown.

  • Can I edit my ad after
    submitting it?

    Yes! You will always be able to edit your campaign.

  • Can I submit an ad in a language other than English?

    At the moment, we only accept ads in English.

  • Do these leads need to confirm their subscription?

    Your leads are entered into your account automatically, without confirmation.

  • Can I choose to receive leads from a specific country only?

    Your ad will be displayed on various pages on the Internet. Although we can't guarantee that your ad will be displayed for subscribers of a certain nationality only, you can state your desired target range within the text of your ad (i.e. US customers only, car dealers, gardening fans, young mothers etc.)

  • Do I need to worry about
    spam complaints?

    With us spam problem is non-existent! As we provide your leads, all the risk and work associated with potential spam complaints is on us. No more worries that your account might get terminated because you failed to provide the evidence that your subscribers did really sign up.

  • Can I add links to my ad?

    Unfortunately, due to Compliance reasons, we do not accept ads with links in them.
