Who We Are

The personal information that we collect from you is:

  • Name

We collect information in the following ways:

While we do not automatically collect personal information, we will collect it when you contact us directly via email or contact forms on the website. Get Holistic Health respects user privacy and, unless required to do so by law, we will under no circumstances disclose your information without your clear and verifiable consent.  We will only keep your information for as long as is necessary and for the purpose(s) stated in this policy. We use cookies to process information that helps us to personalize user experience and improve the overall design and functionality of our website. Cookies are only placed on your device’s internet browser if your device is set to accept cookies.  As a provider of overall health-related information, cookies help us to remember user actions on the site and offer content that is more relevant to you.

What Are Cookies?

A cookie is a very small text document, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier. When you visit a Web site, that site’s computer asks your computer for permission to store this file in a part of your hard drive specifically designated for cookies. Each Web site can send its own cookie to your browser if your browser’s preferences allow it, but (to protect your privacy) your browser only permits a Web site to access the cookies it has already sent to you, not the cookies sent to you by other sites.

Third party cookies
We use third party services that may set cookies on your device. We use Double-click cookie which is developed by Google to display ads on our website, control how often ads are shown to you, as well as personalize ads basing  on user’s previous interaction with our website or other websites that use the double-click cookie. Double-click cookie may collect your IP address and browser information. We do not collect personal information using third party cookies and we do not combine or associate information collected by double-click cookie with any personal information about you.
