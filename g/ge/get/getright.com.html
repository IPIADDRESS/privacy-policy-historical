Privacy.

Headlight Software, Inc. does not collect any personally identifiable information about you such as name, address, phone number, or e-mail address from users of this web site without your knowledge. When purchasing GetRight, additional information is needed to fill your order. Any information provided will not be sold or given to anyone; and it will only be used by Headlight Software for filling your order and notifying you by email of updates.
