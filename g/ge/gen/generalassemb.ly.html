THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY SERVICE COLLECTS YOUR INFORMATION

We collect Personal Information when you:

  • create an account;
  • register or apply for a Course, either directly or by using your social networking credentials on the General Assembly Service or a third-party website;
  • apply for a membership to use our co-working space;
  • register for a Premium Content subscription;
  • post information to the General Assembly Service that includes Personal Information; and
  • communicate with us.

We also collect information, such as anonymous usage statistics, by using cookies, server logs, and other similar technology as you use the General Assembly Service.

Registration. To create an account, you may need to provide Personal Information, such as your name, email address, and address. If you register for a Premium Content subscription, you will also need to provide your credit card number and billing address. You may also provide other optional information.

Using the General Assembly Service. We may collect the Personal Information you post as you use the General Assembly Service, such as when you sign up for a Course or newsletter, apply for membership to use our co-working space, or post your comments in a chat room or on a blog post. We discourage posting sensitive or Personal Information in comments as they are viewable by the public.

Social Log-In. When you use your social media credentials to register for a Course or to post a comment to the General Assembly Service, we collect Personal Information from the social media website. For example, when you log in with your Facebook credentials, we may collect the Personal Information you have made publicly available in Facebook, such as your name and profile picture or logo. We may also collect your email address.

From Third Parties. We may use third parties to perform certain tasks for us, such as facilitating your purchase of a Course. Such third parties will provide us the Personal Information, such as your name, address, and email address, you provide to them so that we have your information for Course rosters.

Customer Support. We may collect Personal Information through your communications with our customer-support, marketing, and sales teams, such as when you sign up for a newsletter.

Making Payments. When you make payments to pay for a Course or Premium Content subscription, you may need to provide Personal Information to our third-party service providers, such as your credit card number.

Cookies, Automatic Data Collection, and Related Technologies. The General Assembly Service collects and stores information that is generated automatically as you use it, including your preferences and anonymous usage statistics. For example, we will also use your IP address to determine your physical location when you log in so we can personalize the Service for you. You may change your location information in your account settings.

When you visit the General Assembly Service, we and our third-party service providers receive and record information on our server logs, including your IP address, and from cookies and similar technology. Cookies are small text files placed in visitors’ computer to store their preferences. You can use your browser to block and delete cookies from your browser. However, if you do that, the General Assembly Service may not work properly.

By using the General Assembly Service, you are authorizing us to gather, parse, and retain data related to the provision of the General Assembly Service.
