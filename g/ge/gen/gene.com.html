Privacy Policy

We have established this Online Privacy Policy so that you are informed about how we treat any personal information that you provide to us in the course of using this website. This section provides a summary of the most important elements; however, you should also review the full Policy. We may update this Policy from time to time.

  • Collection of Data

    Genentech collects personal information when you voluntarily provide it to us. For example, you may complete a web survey, subscribe to a newsletter, or register for certain offers or promotions. We may combine the data you provide online with other data you have shared with us offline, as well as with records provided by third parties. For more information on the personal information collected by Genentech, see Information Collection and Use.

    We also may collect data concerning your use of this website, such as the pages visited or links clicked. This data is collected for analytics purposes. The types of data collected are described more fully in the sections Cookies and GIF Files and Log Files and Aggregate Information.

  • Uses & Disclosures of Your Data

    Genentech uses your personal information for the purposes for which you gave it to us. For example, if you have expressed an interest in a particular product or program, we will use your data to provide you with the requested information or service. We may also ask for your permission to send you information on other Genentech products or services that we believe may be of interest to you.

    We also may use your personal information and data concerning your use of this website for analytics purposes. For example, we may use this data to determine which parts of our site are of most interest to users, which parts are of least interest, and to modify the site accordingly.

    We may share your personal information with other Genentech affiliates, partners with whom we are jointly marketing a product or service, and service providers whom we have engaged to assist us in our business functions. These entities will be required to abide by this Privacy Policy. For more information, see Sharing and Disclosure.

  • Your Rights & Choices

    You can utilize many parts of our website without providing personal information. However, certain options and services necessarily require the collection of personal information.

    If you subscribe to receive one or more promotional communications from us, you can later unsubscribe by following the instructions in such communications. Should you wish to access or correct your personal information, you may contact us at 877-GENENTECH. For more information, see Correction/Updating Personally Identifiable Information.

  • How to Reach Us

    Please contact us with any questions relating to this Privacy Policy:

    • Call us at 877-GENENTECH
    • Write us at:
