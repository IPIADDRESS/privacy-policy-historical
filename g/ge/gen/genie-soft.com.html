Genie-Soft Privacy Statement  
   
   
Genie-soft has created this privacy statement to demonstrate our firm commitment to the privacy of the users of our website and online service. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy, and how we treat personally identifiable information collected from our users.  
   
What this Privacy Statement Covers  
   
  • This privacy statement covers treatment of personally identifiable information collected by Genie-soft through a website owned and operated by Genie-soft.
  • This privacy statement does not apply to the practices of companies that Genie-soft does not own or control, or of persons that Genie-soft does not employ or manage, including any third-party content contributors bound by contract and any third-party websites to which Genie-soft's websites link.
 
   
Collection and Use of Personal Information  
   
  • You can visit the websites of Genie-soft without revealing any personal information. However, Genie-soft needs certain personal information if you wish to register for a members account, receive a Genie-soft newsletter, purchase a product, or use certain Genie-soft services.
  • Where required, this information may include your company contact information and your personal contact information. Genie-soft will use this information to reply to your inquiries, to set up your account, to provide you with requested services, and to contact you regarding new services.
 
   
Information Sharing and Disclosure  
   
  • Genie-soft will not share any information with anyone else and will remain confidential unless requested explicit permission from users to share information.
 
   
Changes to this Privacy Statement  
   
  • Genie-soft may at any time, without notice to you and in its sole discretion, amend this statement from time to time. Please review this statement periodically. Your continued use of Genie-soft services after any such amendments signifies your acceptance thereof.
