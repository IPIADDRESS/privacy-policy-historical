A cookie is information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Guff.com uses cookies to help track and identify visitors, monitor the usage of Guff.com websites and website access preferences every time the visitor returns to the site. Should a visitor refuse to have cookies placed on their computers, will have to set their browsers to refuse cookies prior to using the Guff.com websites. Users acknowledge and accept that disabling cookies may cause certain features of Guff.com websites to not function properly.
