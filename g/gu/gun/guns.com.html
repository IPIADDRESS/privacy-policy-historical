Cookies

Guns.com uses cookie technology to increase the ease of use of the web site. When you make selections or set preferences on our site, the site will send a request to your browser to record some of your selections in a "cookie" file. The cookie is a small text file that resides on your hard drive. From time to time, our site will request the cookie from your browser so we can retrieve the stored information and you do not have to repeatedly enter information. Should you choose not to accept these cookies you may not be able to use portions of our site.

Guns.com never uses cookies to store credit card information, nor is the information we store in the cookie intended to be read by any other web site.

Many browsers allow users to set the preferences to show a warning each time a cookie request is received. You can also set most browsers not to accept cookies at all.

At times, some of the advertisers on our site set cookies when you select their ad banner. We do not receive the data collected by those cookies.

Security

Guns.com has security measures in place to safeguard against the loss, misuse and alteration of information under our control. By making your bids and or purchases through our secure server, you are protected by Secure Socket Layer ("SSL") technology, used by most browsers, including Netscape and Microsoft Explorer. This technology allows us to utilize advanced encryption in order to protect the information transmitted between your computer and our server. Through encryption, the personal information you enter, including your name and credit card information, is securely dispatched over the Internet.
