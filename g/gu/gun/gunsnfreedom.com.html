IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Gunsnfreedom.com does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.
