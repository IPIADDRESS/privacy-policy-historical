1. Introduction & General Terms

Gurobi Optimization is committed to protecting your personal data. Our Privacy Policy is designed to protect the information that we collect from you over the web or other means while providing you with our products and services. This Privacy Policy relates to our use of any personal data you provide to us via phone or text, by email, at events, in letters or correspondence and via our website at www.gurobi.com.

In order to provide you with the best experience, we sometimes need to collect information. This can range from when you set up an account on our website so you can download a free trial of our product, to our complying with US and International laws, as well as the General Data Protection Regulation (“GDPR”) of the European Union, to our keeping track of your email preferences so we only send you relevant emails.

This Privacy Policy explains the following:

  • what information Gurobi Optimization may collect about you;
  • how Gurobi Optimization will use information we collect about you;
  • when Gurobi Optimization may use your details to contact you;
  • whether Gurobi Optimization will disclose your details to anyone else;
  • your choices regarding the personal data you provide to us;
  • the use of cookies on Gurobi Optimization websites and how you can reject these cookies;
  • and your rights with regard to your personal data.

Gurobi Optimization is committed to safeguarding your personal data. Whenever you provide such data, we are legally obliged to use your data in line with all laws concerning the protection of personal data.

Gurobi Optimization websites contain hyperlinks to websites owned and operated by third parties. These third party websites have their own privacy policies, and are also likely to use cookies, and we therefore urge you to review them. They will govern the use of personal data you submit, which may also be collected by cookies whilst visiting these websites. We do not accept any responsibility or liability for the privacy practices of such third party websites and your use of such websites is at your own risk.

3. What personal data will Gurobi Optimization collect about me?

When you participate in, access or sign up to any of Gurobi Optimization’s services, activities or online content, sign up for a webinar or training event, or create an account using Gurobi Optimization’s online registration system, we may receive personal data about you. If you do not provide those personal data to us, you cannot set up an account with Gurobi Optimization, cannot attend a webinar or training event and cannot use our products and services.

By submitting your details, you enable us to provide you with the services, activities or online content you select. The legal basis for such processing is Article 6(1)(b) GDPR since it is necessary for the performance of a contract to which you are a party or in order to take steps at your request prior to entering into a contract.

When you visit the Website, we collect the following personal data from you:

  • Your IP address
  • Date and time of visiting the Website
  • The website which referred you to our Website
  • Your browser (type and version) and browser settings
  • Your operating system

The processing of this information and personal data is necessary for the purposes of providing you the content of our Website adequately, to personalize the information provided so it is most relevant to you and to ensure network and information security and to safeguard the Website from unauthorized access, attacks and damage to computer and electronic communication systems; these are legitimate interests pursued by Gurobi Optimization and thus the processing is lawful pursuant to Article 6(1)(f) GDPR.

Gurobi Optimization also uses cookies (see section 11 below for details) when visiting the Website. Data processing by using cookies is necessary pursuant to Article 6(1)(f) GDPR for the purposes of the legitimate interests pursued by Gurobi Optimization to improve your experience on our Website by analyzing the use of our Website and by providing adequate information and offers to each user.

If you do not provide your personal data to us when visiting our Website the use of our Website could be restricted or impossible.

11. Cookies Policy - Information Gurobi Optimization collects from you

a. What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes a unique identifier that is sent to your computer, tablet or mobile phone (referred to here as a "device") browser from a website's computer and is stored on your device's hard drive. Each website can send its own cookie to your browser if your browser's preferences allow it, but (to protect your privacy) your browser only permits a website to access the cookies it has already sent to you, not the cookies sent to you by other websites. Many websites do this whenever a user visits their website in order to track online traffic flows.

On Gurobi Optimization’s Website, cookies record information about your preferences and interests on www.gurobi.com, allow us to set up an account for you, and allow us to keep track of if you are logged in or not (to access your account).

b. How does Gurobi Optimization use cookies?

Information supplied by cookies can help us to analyze the profile of our visitors and help us to provide you with a better user experience. For example, if we find that many people go to a particular webpage on our Website but then quickly leave the page and go to another page, we can investigate if we need to change the content on that first page to better meet the expectations/needs of Website visitors. Any third parties are strictly required not to use any information for their own business or other purposes (see section 7 above). Certain areas of Gurobi Optimization Websites may use cookies for a specific reason - for example, to identify you and allow you to access your license information.

c. Gurobi Optimization cookies and how to reject cookies?

If you don’t want to receive cookies, you can modify your browser so that it notifies you when cookies are sent to it or you can refuse cookies altogether. You can also delete cookies that have already been set.

If you wish to restrict or block web browser cookies that are set on your device, you can do this through your browser settings; the Help function within your browser should tell you how. Alternatively, you may wish to visit www.aboutcookies.org, which contains comprehensive information on how to do this on a wide variety of desktop browsers.

It is important to note that if you change your settings and block certain cookies, this means that certain personalized features cannot then be provided and accordingly you may not be able to take full advantage of all of the websites' features.

d. Google Analytics

This Website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google Inc., 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA ("Google"). Google Analytics also uses cookies. Usually the information stored in cookies about your Website usage is transmitted to a Google server in the USA and saved there. Since IP anonymization is enabled on this Website, Google will shorten your IP address before transmission. Only in exceptional cases will your full IP address be transmitted to a Google server in the USA and shortened there. On our behalf, Google will use this information to analyze your Website usage, to compile reports about Website activities, and to provide us with further services associated with the use of the Website and of the internet. The IP address that your browser transmits within the scope of Google Analytics will not be merged with other Google data. You can configure your browser software to disable the use of cookies but we would like to point out that this may prevent you from making full use of this Website. You can also stop data (incl. your IP address) created by cookies and associated with your Website usage from being sent to Google or processed by Google by downloading and installing the browser plug-in available at the following link http://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=en.
