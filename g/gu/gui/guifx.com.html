Privacy Policy

The user's contact information is used to contact the visitor when necessary. Contact information from these forms may also be used to send information about our company and scheduled events, merchandise specials, contests, or other information relating to Guifx and its strategic partners. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.

Public Forums

Security
This site employs security measures to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.

Subscription Mailing List
If you have subscribed to our mailing list and now wish to remove yourself from it, you may do so via the included unsubscribe link located inside all of our sent email correspondence.

Choice/Opt-Out
