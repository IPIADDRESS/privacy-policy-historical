About Engineered Systems

Galco Industrial Electronics is dedicated to providing world-class control engineering solutions through the integration of high performance products such as FANUC FA America CNCs, ABB Drives, Control Techniques, Yaskawa and Eaton Automation. Galco's talented engineering staff serves Automotive, Aerospace, Primary Steel, Tier 1 and 2 Suppliers as well as Research Dynamometer Laboratories.

FANUC CNC Retrofit

Galco Industrial Electronics can retrofit obsolete CNCs on machine tool mills, grinders, lathes and routers with FANUC FA America products. Machine tools represent significant capital investments and must maintain their viability for many years. Keeping them updated with the latest control technologies via CNC retrofit is one way to do that successfully today.
Contact A Galco Expert
Galco Industrial Electronics retrofits obsolete CNCs on machine tool mills, grinders, lathes and routers with FANUC FA America products. A complete retrofit upgrades CNC components, motion control equipment, and motors and drives.
FANUC CNCs offer the best performance for machine tools in virtually every industry, from mills and lathes to highly complex 5-axis applications. Fine-tune your control with the Series 30i, 0i, 35i, iHMI, and more.
FANUC's Power Motion i-MODEL A Motion controller features pressure and position control, multi-axis synchronization, electronic CAM and high-speed response, scalable up to 32 axes.
FANUC servo, spindle, linear and torque motors and drives are engineered to provide the best possible return in precision, speed, and efficiency. Options apply to entry-level machines, precision machines, and 5-axis machines.
