Privacy Policy

Information collected from web site users is kept to an absolute minimum. GasBuddy Organization Inc. is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We will not sell, share, rent or release this information to any marketers, advertisers or other third parties under any circumstances.

Although GasBuddy Organization Inc. makes every effort to protect member's user identifications and passwords, ultimately you are responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your passwords and/or any account information. Do not disclose your passwords to anyone.
Cookies:
Cookies are employed in the normal operation of the web site where they are used for the sole purpose of controlling page views and they are not linked to any personally identifiable information. The majority of cookies employed in the web site are session only cookies. Session cookies are only active during the time your browser is open and they are automatically are deleted after 10 minutes. For optimal site performance we recommend the use of cookies by your browser.

We record visitors IP addresses to analyze trends and administer the site. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information and they cannot be used to track users identities without the permission of their internet service provider.

Privacy Policy - Tell a Friend
The Tell-a-Friend feature allows web site visitors to send an email notification to a friend, inviting them to visit GasBuddy.com. During this process only the friend's email address and the visitors name are asked for. GasBuddy Organization Inc. will automatically send the friend a one-time email directing them to visit the site. The email address and visitor's name are stored for the sole purpose of sending this one-time email. The information is then periodically deleted from the database and it will not be used for any other purposes.

The friend may contact GasBuddy.com at privacy@gasbuddy.com to request that they be blocked from receiving any Tell-a-Friend e-mails.

Sample Email: Hello,This message was sent by your friend, Roger, who was recently at www.GasBuddy.com and thought you might find our site useful. GasBuddy.com is a web site that has developed a live forum to find or post recent low and high gasoline prices in your local area area. It is an entirely free service. To see what Roger is all excited about, visit www.GasBuddy.com today.

www.GasBuddy.com
-------------------------------

Please Note: This is a one-time message sent by a friend of yours. You will not receive any further messages from GasBuddy.com in the future unless another "Tell-a-Friend" message is sent by a web site visitor. In order to prevent receiving any further messages of this type you must reply to this email with "Block Me" in the subject line.
Privacy Policy - Members Area
The Members Area offers many personalized features accessible using a member id and password. A unique email address is used to verify the member id. Signed up members will never receive unsolicited email from GasBuddy Inc. In order to enhance a visitors web site experience, we will store information about the preferences of users (ex. favorite stations) to create a personalized user profile. When a user visits the member's area specific information to their needs will be displayed. None of this information is of personal nature and it will not be shared with any third parties.

The message forums provide a location for gasoline related discussion. Please remember that any information disclosed in these areas becomes public. You should exercise caution when deciding what personal information to disclose.
