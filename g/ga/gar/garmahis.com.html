If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us through our website.

Log Files
Like many other Web sites, Garmahis.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons
Garmahis.com does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.

– Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to Garmahis.com and other sites on the Internet.
– Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL – http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on Garmahis.com send directly to your browsers. Garmahis.com has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
