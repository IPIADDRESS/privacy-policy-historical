GameChanger Media, Inc. Privacy Policy

The GameChanger Media, Inc. Electronic Privacy Policy has been written to answer questions you might have about online privacy. As a company that develops technology for amateur sports teams we are very concerned about privacy and make an effort to safeguard any personal information we may collect about you or your children. This policy refers only to information collected by the GameChanger mobile application and displayed on the www.GameChanger.io website.

Our Commitment to Security

GameChanger Media, Inc., has put in place appropriate physical, electronic and managerial processes to safeguard the information we collect and prevent unauthorized access of information.

Advice to Children and Parents

We strongly advise children to get a parent or guardian's permission before submitting any kind of personal data over the Internet. We urge parents to be very clear with their children regarding the danger of giving personal information to anyone (online or in person) they don't know. We also encourage parents and guardians to spend time online with their children and to participate in the activities offered on the sites they visit.

If children under 18 elect to register for any Gamechanger services, they should ask a parent for permission before sending any information to our website. We will not request or knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from a child under 13.

What personal information do we collect?

All registered members of the www.GameChanger.io website are requested to provide an email address and password in return for access to the site.

In addition, team administrators (coaches, scorekeepers, volunteers) may input a limited amount of optional information about players on their amateur athletic teams. As of the writing of this document the following information is requested (but not required): first name, last name, position and preferred throwing and batting hand (left or right). For all youth teams (13 years and under) a full first and last name is never displayed online to anyone other than the team administrator(s) for that team.

How do we use this information?

GameChanger members who have agreed to receive emails from GameChanger Media, Inc. may be contacted on a semi-regular basis with product updates or other marketing messages. GameChanger Media, Inc does not share this contact information with any third party companies.

GameChanger Media, Inc. uses the information team administrators provide to create live play-by-play and stats content for fans to view online. However, these team administrators can make personal player statistics private (ie, not visible to fans) at any time.

Cookies -- What are they?

Cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to an individual's hard drive for record-keeping purposes. Cookies make Internet surfing easier for you by saving your passwords and preferences while you're at a site. The use of cookies is an industry standard -- you'll find that they are used by most major websites. By showing how and when visitors use the site, cookies can also help website developers see which areas are popular and which are not.

Does the GameChanger website use cookies?

Yes. The GameChanger website stores some preferences for team administrators and for other viewers of GameChanger’s content.

Questions or Concerns

GameChanger Media, Inc. reserves the right to modify these regulations from time to time to better address your concerns or to make other additions, deletions or changes that GameChanger Media, Inc. determines to be appropriate.

If you have any questions, comments or concerns regarding the GameChanger Media, Inc. privacy policy, please contact us.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of March 24, 2010.

Official Scoring Team

We encourage any user to create a GameChanger team and utilize our scorekeeping and stat management tools. As a result of this open platform, there may be several versions of the same team.

GameChanger is beta testing solutions to establish authenticity of teams so fans can easily trust that the teams they follow will provide consistent game data.

Official GameChanger teams will be marked with an official tag in search results and on team home pages.

GameChanger reserves the right to contact a team's head coach to confirm that it is an official team.

GaMECHaNGER Team Upgrades

GameChanger is pleased to offer our users several levels of team-specific features:

  • Basic Access (Free): live scores, basic post-game box scores and season stats, and team schedules are free for all GameChanger users.

GaMECHaNGER Inside Access

GameChanger is pleased to offer our admins the ability to purchase Inside Access Coupons for fans. Coupons can be purchased in packages of 3 or 10 and then distributed to team fans.

Once a coupon is redeemed, a fan will enjoy all the benefits of Inside Access including:

  • live streaming box scores and expanded box scores
  • live streaming and post-game play-by-play updates
  • expanded season-to-date statistics
  • player game logs (expanded stats by game)
  • player spray charts
  • ...plus more Inside Access features coming soon

How do coupons work?

  1. An admin purchases a package of 3 or 10 coupons on your team's upgrades page
  2. The admin then distributes coupon codes to team fans
  3. Fans can redeem their coupons by selecting "Inside Access" and entering the coupon code on the team's upgrade page

    Innings per Game

    Team innings-per-game is used to calculate Earned Run Averages (ERA).

    GameChanger team innings-per-game are defaulted to:

    • Youth: 6
    • Middle/High School: 7
    • College, Adult: 9

    Team admins, via the Team Settings tab, may change the innings-per-game value to any integer between 5 and 9.

    There are currently three GameChanger user roles.

    Administrators

    Administrators have full website administration and editing capabilities for their team and set the privacy settings for player stats.

    • They can also grant "Admin" permission to up to 2 other team followers.
    • In order to score a team game, a scorekeeper must be logged into the mobile scorekeeping application as a team administrator.
    • The original creator of a GameChanger team is made an Admin by default.
    Fans

    Fans have "read-only" capabilities on a team's GameChanger website.

    • Fans can view season stats, player game logs and spray charts.
    • Fans cannot view next game's lineup, and cannot edit rosters or schedules.
    Users

    Registered Users have limited "read-only" capabilities on GameChanger teams that they are not Fans of.

    • Users can view team rosters, schedules and box scores, and the team's FanBase.
    • "Non-Fan" Users cannot view player stats, game logs or spray chart data. Users must become Fans for access to statistics outside of individual box scores.

    GaMECHaNGER

    Completed Games

    Differences between "Unfinished" and "Final"

    A scored game's statistics do not become official in the GameChanger scorebooks until the Scorekeeper or Admin finalizes the game.

    • When a scored game is over, the Scorekeeper clicks [End Game], which makes the game "Final".
    • If [End Game] is not selected within 30 minutes of the last scored play, the game is marked "Unfinished" and denoted by this orange 'carrot symbol': ^
    • Unfinished games can also be made "Final" on the GameChanger website by authorized Admins.

    Effect on Season Statistics and Spray Charts

    Individual game statistics do not count towards season statistics until its box score is finalized and made "official". Additionally, 'Exhibition' game statistics are viewable only if selected from the Game Type drop-down menu. 'Exhibition' games never count towards season statistics or standings.

    Explaining "Other" actions for "My Teams"
    Administrators
    • A co-Admin may safely Unfollow a team if there will be Admin(s) remaining.
    • Admins may not Delete a team if that team has Fans.
    • Admins may Delete their team only if it has zero Fans.

    • A team Fan may Unfollow that team at any point.
    • Upon un-following, all alerts (email and premium SMS) for that team are de-activated until re-follow.
