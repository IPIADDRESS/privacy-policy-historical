Privacy Policy

At GameStop.com , protecting the information you provide us is our top priority. We want your shopping experience to be enjoyable, fun and worry free. GameStop.com is committed to respecting the privacy rights of everyone who visits our website. We also recognize that our visitors, and the parents of children visiting our site, deserve a high level of assurance that any information they choose to share with us will remain private.


GameStop.com & Children

GameStop.com is concerned about the privacy of young children and does not knowingly collect any personal information from a child under 13. We suggest that you become involved with your child's access to the Internet to ensure that his or her privacy is protected.

Secure Shopping

GameStop.com uses encryption technology to protect the information you provide during the ordering process. Our servers are secured to protect all personal information. GameStop.com is an Authentic VeriSign Site and displays the VeriSign logo to prove it.

All credit card information is stored on a secured server, and the data is available only to GameStop.com.

    Cookies

    Cookies are bits of electronic information which a website can transfer to a visitor's hard drive to help tailor and keep records of his or her visit at the site. Cookies allow GameStop.com to better tailor visits to the site to the visitor's individual preferences. The use of cookies is standard on the Internet and many major web sites use them. You can choose to set your browser to notify you whenever you are sent a cookie. This gives you the chance to decide whether or not to accept it.

GameStop.com cannot control these links and is not responsible for any content appearing on these sites.
