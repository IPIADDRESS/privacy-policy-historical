Privacy Policy:

Gagnam.com (“Gagnam“) operates Gagnam.com and may operate other websites. It is Gagnam’s policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our websites.

Gagnam’s purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Gagnam’s visitors use its website. From time to time, Gagnam may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

Gagnam also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on Gagnam.com blogs/sites. Gagnam only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below, except that commenter IP addresses and email addresses are visible and disclosed to the administrators of the blog/site where the comment was left.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain visitors to Gagnam’s websites choose to interact with Gagnam in ways that require Gagnam to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that Gagnam gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up at Gagnam.com to provide a username and email address. Those who engage in transactions with Gagnam are asked to provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to process those transactions. In each case, Gagnam collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor’s interaction with Gagnam. Gagnam does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

Aggregated Statistics

Gagnam may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. Gagnam may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, Gagnam does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information

Gagnam discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on Gagnam’s behalf or to provide services available at Gagnam’s websites, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Other than to its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations, as described above, Gagnam discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only in response to a subpoena, court order or other governmental request, or when Gagnam believes in good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the property or rights of Gagnam, third parties or the public at large. Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Gagnam uses cookies to help Gagnam identify and track visitors, their usage of Gagnam website, and their website access preferences. Gagnam visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Gagnam’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of Gagnam’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by Gagnam and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.
