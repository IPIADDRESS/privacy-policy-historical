Web browser cookies

Our Site uses “cookies” to enhance User experience. User’s web browser places cookies on their hard drive for record-keeping purposes and sometimes to track information about them. Cookies are harmless and only used for local purposes. No user data is stored on the server or used in anyway without user’s consent.

User may choose to set their web browser to refuse cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent. If user does so, note that some parts of the Site may not function properly.
