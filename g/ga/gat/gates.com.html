Privacy Statement for www.gates.com
 
Gates Corporation has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site: www.gates.com.

Use of Your Information
Gates Corporation collects user information in various ways from many parts of our Web site. Some personal and business information is gathered when you register, or fill out a form. Information such as name, address, email, phone number, fax number, business name, zip code, occupation, industry and other personal information may be requested. We do not sell or rent any personally identifiable information about you to any third party. The main purpose of collecting this information is to personalize and enhance the use of our Web site and tailor it to users' needs, showing them content that we think they might be interested in, and providing content according to their preferences. We may also use customer contact information from these forms to send the user information about our company and our partners. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor when necessary. User may opt-out of being contacted via email by Gates at any time. Unique identifiers (such as customer account numbers) are occasionally collected to verify the user's identity, for use as account numbers in our record system, and for other purposes. Demographic and profile data may also be collected at our site.

This site contains links to other sites. Gates Corporation is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

Cookies

As part of our personalization services on occasion Gates Corporation may use cookies to keep track of user information. A cookie is simply a small data file that is sent from your web browser and stored on your computer's hard drive. We may use cookies to deliver personalized content specific to your interests and for other purposes such as measuring Web site traffic patterns. Gates Corporation may also use IP address for system administration and tracking purposes.

Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.

Choice/Opt-Out

Gates Corporation may occasionally send you an email regarding products, services and site updates, as well as special offers if you stated that you were interested in receiving such offers when registering at the Gates Corporation Web site. As well our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us at the point where we request information about the visitor, although occasionally we may be required to send you information via email we deem to be important.

This site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service.

Correct/Update

This site gives users the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided.
