Privacy Policy

Your right to privacy is important to us. We are keen to strike a fair balance between your privacy and ensuring that you obtain full information from this site.

Personal Data

In some areas of our website we ask you to provide information that will enable us to enhance your visit to the website or reply to you after your visit. This includes where you complete a form to make an enquiry or request.

We may use your personal data for future email contact, support, general correspondence and other correspondence which we feel may be of interest to you. If you would rather not receive future emails from us please contact us.

The information you provide will be kept confidential and will be used only to support your relationship with us as outlined in this policy or elsewhere on this website. We will not transfer or disclose your information to third parties without your prior consent.

Updating your details

Members can update their details through this website by logging into their account. Requests for removal from any mailing list which we operate will always be dealt with promptly.

Your consent

By submitting your information you consent to the use of that information as set out in this policy. If we change our privacy policy we will post the changes on this page, and may place notices on other pages of the website so that you may be aware of the information we collect and how we use it at all times. Continued use of our services will signify that you agree to any such changes.

We welcome your feedback

We welcome your views about all aspects of this website including this privacy policy. If you would like to contact us with any queries or comments please contact us.
