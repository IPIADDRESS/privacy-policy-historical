Privacy Policy and Copyright

The foundation is committed to protecting the privacy of our Web site visitors. We use the information that you provide or that is derived from Web tracking only in connection with our internal purposes. When necessary, we may share information with our service providers and others, but only for purposes relating to the informationâ€™s submission or our mission or operations.

    

Personal Information
The Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation collects personal information upon a visitor's request to subscribe to newsletters, announcements (press releases), and multimedia stories, or as a part of applying for a job. The information provided is not sold or rented to any individual or organization.

Cookies
The Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation currently uses cookie technology to analyze site traffic and use. Information gathered is solely used to assist in improving Web site design and function. This Web site is functional without the retention of cookies. You may elect to block cookies from this site through use of your browser settings.
 
The Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation reserves the right to change and update the way in which visitor information is collected.
