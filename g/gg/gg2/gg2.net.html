Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy at Asian Media & Marketing Group

At Asian Media & Marketing Group we take privacy very seriously. Throughout the data collection process, all necessary steps are taken to ensure that data is used only according to the purposes that we have clearly stated.

Purposes for which information is collected All information collected is regulated by the UK Data Protection Act. Asian Media & Marketing Group, is duly registered as a data collector for various purposes under the Act. In the case of gg2.net, data collected may be used for the following purposes:

For the purpose of providing the service requested by you, e.g. sending publications and/or related material. Asian Media & Marketing Group will screen your application against our terms of control and inform you if you do not qualify for any of our publications. We will also use your details to contact you when it is time to renew for another year.

To learn more about our readers and thus to improve the content and relevance of our editorial. Some of the demographic data is used to provide reports and statistics to prove the quality of our readership to our advertisers and this is verified by our circulation auditors. The data shown to advertisers is always provided in report format as anonymous data. Without advertising we simply would not be able to keep sending our free magazines.

For the purpose of direct marketing by Asian Media & Marketing Group and other companies. This implies that we may from time to time send you information about Asian Media & Marketing Group or other products, magazines, or conferences, and that we may pass your name and address or other contact details, on to third parties whose products or services we believe you might be interested in.

The use of data for direct marketing purposes

We would like to keep you informed by mail and email of Asian Media & Marketing Group's products and services, including information about magazines, online services, conferences and exhibitions. If you do not wish to receive this information, please contact :

The Circulation Director
1 Silex Street London SE1 0DW
webmaster@gg2.net

Disclosure of data to third parties

At the point of data collection, you will be given the opportunity to opt-out of having your data passed on to third parties by checking the box on the registration form. If you do not opt-out, Asian Media & Marketing Group, may pass your details on to carefully selected third parties whose products or services may be of interest to you.

For more information

This statement is a summary and does not represent a complete statement of intent on behalf of Asian Media & Marketing Group in respect of information privacy. If you require more information, please mail webmaster@gg2.net with your comments and we will do our best to answer you as quickly as possible.
