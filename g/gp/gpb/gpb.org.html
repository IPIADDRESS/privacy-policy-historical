Privacy Policy
GPB Site Map


Georgia Public Broadcasting is committed to protecting your personal privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for the website, GPB.org.

Your IP address is used to help identify you while visiting our site. Cookies are small data files stored on your computer and are only used to store your status while visiting the site.

Information Collection and Use
You do not need to register or submit any information to browse our site. We ask for an email address only when you register to receive our email newsletter. When you make a donation online to Georgia Public Broadcasting, we also ask for contact information, and financial (credit card) information.

Information Sharing and Disclosure
Georgia Public Broadcasting only collects personally identifiable information when you make an online donation or request an email newsletter. Georgia Public Broadcasting will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone.

This site may contains links to other sites. GPB.org is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

Contacting the Web Site
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, please email Member and Audience Services or visit our contact page for a complete list of contact information.
