What Our Privacy Policy Covers

Please read this privacy policy to learn more about:

  1. What personally identifiable information of yours is collected by Get-Paid.com.com (“Get-Paid.com”) or by third-party advertisers (together, the “Third-Parties” or individually a “Third-Party” );

  5. How to contact Get-Paid.com.

     

Information Collected by Get-Paid.com

To become a member of Get-Paid.com, you must register and create an account on the Get-Paid.com website. The only information that Get-Paid.com collects is the information you provide through the Get-Paid.com registration process, and the information you provide when you update your payment options. This information includes your first and last name, your email address, your Internet Protocol Address (“IP address”), and other contact information. Your IP address is recorded when you register, so that Get-Paid.com can enforce its one account per person policy and other provisions of our Terms of Service and Rules.

Other information, that does not personally identify you as an individual, is collected by Get-Paid.com from the website (such as, by way of example, patterns of utilization), and it is exclusively owned by Get-Paid.com. This information can be utilized by Get-Paid.com in such manner as Get-Paid.com, in its sole discretion, deems appropriate.

Information Sharing & Disclosure

In the event that you subscribe to or opt-in a Third-Party offer, Get-Paid.com may provide your information to the Third-Party in order to directly faciliate the relationship between you and the advertiser.

Your email address may also be used for tracking purposes, when you complete offers, so that Get-Paid.com can determine that you completed the offers. Get-Paid.com will also use your email address to send you the Get-Paid.com newsletters, unless you have opted out of receiving such newsletters. You can OPTOUT anytime by going to 'My account', under PERSONAL DETAILS .

Get-Paid.com is committed to protect its users' privacy, and will not share this information with anyone else, beside in the cases mentioned above.

In addition, there are some cases when a user gives out more information than what mentioned above.
For instance, if there's a problem with an offer that a user tried to complete and the user needs to provide Get-Paid.com with personal information (such as print screen of an order that he placed).
Get-Paid.com is committed to protect users' privacy in any case whatsoever and all information Get-Paid.com gets from its users will be kept confidential.
