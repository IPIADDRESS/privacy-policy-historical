LyricFind.com Privacy Policy
Your Privacy On This Site

What personally identifiable information is collected on this site?
No personally identifiable information is collected by LyricFind.com or it's affiliates, unless entered by you (such as information collected by CDnow when ordering a CD through us, or subscribing to our mailing list).  Engage Media, LyricFind's ad serving company, collects anonymous data profiling the patterns of visitors to the site; no identifying information is collected.  Anonymous clickstream information is collected for every visitor to this site. This includes pages viewed, date & time, and browser type. IP numbers are not stored, but are temporarily used to determine domain type and in some cases, geographic region. There is no association between this information and a visitor's identity.  To view Engage's Privacy Policy, please visit http://www.flycast.com/about_us/about-privacy.html.

How is it used?
The information is used by Engage to build anonymous behavioral profiles which are written to the Engage Knowledge database -- securely housed at the Engage data center. These profiles are accessed by Engage-enabled web sites to offer you personalized content and promotions, and to target relevant, more interesting ads -- all without knowing your identity. Again, there is no association between an Engage Knowledge profile and a visitor's identity. For more information, click here.

For users who prefer not to have their web usage information reflected in Engage Knowledge, Engage provides an opt-out procedure

Mailing List
LyricFind.com will not divulge your e-mail address to any third party organization under any circumstances.  This information will be kept confidential at all times.  In the case of advertisements within the newsletter, advertisers will not be informed of any personal information of anyone subscribed to the mailing list.

Posting
In the case of posted requests or messages, LyricFind.com is not responsible for your personal information.  Any information posted tot he public by you is done at your own risk of exposure; LyricFind.com cannot garauntee your privacy under these circumstances.  Please note that, in all cases, all information provided is optional; you are never required to enter any information you do not wish to.
