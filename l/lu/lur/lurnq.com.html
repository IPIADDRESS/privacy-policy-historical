PRIVACY POLICY

Technium Labs Private Limited (“Technium Labs”) provides this Privacy Policy to inform you of our policies and procedures regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of the information that we collect and receive from users of our services at www.LurnQ.com (the "Services"). This Privacy Policy applies only to information that you provide to us for the purposes of using the Services. Unless otherwise defined in this Privacy Policy, terms used in this Privacy Policy have the same meanings as in our Terms of Service (the "Terms"). As used in this policy, the terms 'using' and 'processing' information include using cookies on a computer, subjecting the information to statistical or other analysis and using or handling information in any way, including, but not limited to collecting, storing, evaluating, modifying, deleting, using, combining, disclosing and transferring information within our organization or among our affiliates within India or internationally.

To utilize the Services available, our registered users identify themselves to the site and we may display personal information to service providers as needed for the provision of the Services. Technium Labs cares deeply about online privacy. If you have any questions concerning this privacy policy, please email us at team@lurnq.com.

WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT

The types of information that www.LurnQ.com  collects fall under two general categories: personally-identifiable-information (PII) and non-personally-identifiable information (non-PII). PII consists of any information which can be used to specifically identify you as an individual. Non-PII consists of aggregate information or any information that does not reveal your identity.

www.LurnQ.com  collects user submitted information such as name, email address, age to authenticate users and to send notifications to those users relating to the www.LurnQ.com  Service. www.LurnQ.com  also collects other profile data including but not limited to: personal interests, gender, age, education etc., in order to provide the optimum Services.

When you visit our web sites, you might be providing statistics collected as you browse and personal information you knowingly give us. Be assured that safeguarding the contents of information related to you is our priority.

We use your personal information to:

  1. help us provide personalized features
  2. tailor our sites to your interest
  3. to get in touch with you when necessary
  4. to provide the services requested by you
  5. to preserve social history as governed by existing law or policy

www.LurnQ.com  also logs non-PII including IP address, profile information, aggregate user data, and information your browser sends, from users and visitors to the site. This data is used to manage the website, track usage and improve the website Services. User IP addresses and/or mobile location may be recorded for security and monitoring purposes.

The following sections describe how your PII and non-PII are collected by us, and how we use such information.

HOW WE COLLECT AND USE INFORMATION

Log Files

 

Any time you visit the site, our servers automatically gather information from your browser (such as your IP addresses, browser type, Internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and number of clicks). This information may be used to analyze trends, administer the site, prevent fraud, track visitor movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information. We do not share this information externally.

Cookies

 

We use "cookies" to keep track of some types of information while you are visiting the Site or using the Service. Cookies are very small files placed on your computer, and they allow us to count the number of visitors to our website and distinguish repeated users from new users. They also allow us to save user preferences and track user trends.  We do not link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information (PII) you submit while on our site, and any cookies used by us would not enable us to view, store or disseminate any PII relating to the user.  You may be able to configure your browser to accept or reject all or some cookies, or notify you when a cookie is set. Each browser is different, so check the "Help" menu of your browser to learn how to change your cookie preferences -- however, you must enable cookies from www.LurnQ.com  in order to use most functions on the site. We rely on cookies for the proper operation of our website; therefore if your browser is set to reject all cookies, the site will not function properly. Users who refuse cookies assume all responsibility for any resulting loss of functionality, and shall not at any later point in time, hold us responsible for any such resultant loss in functionality.

Web Beacons

 

"Web beacons" (also known as "clear gifs" and "pixel tags") are small transparent graphic images that are often used in conjunction with cookies in order to further personalize our website for our users and to collect a limited set of information about our users. We may also use web beacons in email communications in order to understand the behavior of our users.

Information About You

 

When you register for the Service, we collect a variety of information about you to complete your online profile. Except as otherwise stated in this privacy policy, we do not disclose your PII to other users of the Service.

E-mail

 

We require an email address from you when you register for the Services. The email messages we send you may contain code that enables our database to track your usage of the emails, including whether the email was opened and what links (if any) were clicked. If you would rather not receive promotional emails from us, please see the section below labeled "Choice/Opt-Out". We reserve the right to send you certain communications, such as service announcements and administrative messages, without offering you the opportunity to opt out of receiving them. We may also contact you by text message (including to any wireless number you may provide to us) solely in connection with the Services.  

Logging onto the website

Log in to the website would be undertaken by the User using his user name and password.

Demographic Data

 

Demographic data is also collected at the Site. We use this data to tailor our users’ experience, showing them content that we think they might be interested in, and displaying the content according to their preferences. Some of this information may be shared with advertisers on a non-personally identifiable basis.

Blogs, Bulletin Boards, Buzz and Public Forums

 

We make discussion areas of the website available to our users. Any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and that can be used to send you unsolicited communications. You should exercise caution when deciding to disclose any PII in these areas. Please consult the Terms of Use and other guidelines available on our website when disclosing any such PII.
