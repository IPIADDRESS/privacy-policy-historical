Privacy
We keep your personal information private and secure. When you make a purchase from our site, you provide your name, email address, address, phone number, and a optionally a password. We use this information to process your orders, to keep you updated on your orders and to personalize your shopping experience.

Here at Lukie Games your privacy is very important to us! We will never sell, rent, or share your information with any third party. Unless you request otherwise, we will only contact you regarding orders you have placed. You will never receive any unsolicited e-mails from us. For the highest level of security we do not store your payment information.

Our secure servers protect your information using advanced encryption techniques and firewall technology.

We use "cookies" to keep track of your current shopping session to personalize your experience and so that you may retrieve your shopping cart at any time.
