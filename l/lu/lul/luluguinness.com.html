Privacy statement

Please be assured that any information you pass to us is keep private and confidential. We will not share, rent or sell your personal information without your consent.

We collect information when an order is placed on our site. We hold personal information such as name, address and phone number to allow us to fulfil your order and manage your account. We do not hold credit card details. These are passed directly onto our payment partners system and are not recorded on our system.

Occasionally we may need to ask for additional information to verify your identity or for authentication. Your personal information may be checked with a credit agency to prevent fraud. We also reserve the right to disclose your details to a third party if a complaint arises concerning your use of our site that is inconsistent with our Terms and Conditions. We reserve the right to disclose your details to comply with any applicable laws or lawful government requests we may receive.

When you enter our members' section you may subscribe to our update service. If you chose to subscribe to this service the information you provide will allow us to alert you of news, special offers or events of interest. We may use your personnel information for our internal marketing and demographic studies together with non-personnel data to monitor customer patterns and help us to improve our site.

We may collect non-personnel data about the use of our website. For example the length on time spent on the site, pages visited or volume of usage.

If you have any further enquires or would like us to remove your data from our database please email us at shop@luluguinness.com and we will do all we can to help.
