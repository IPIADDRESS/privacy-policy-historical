Privacy Policy

Effective December 20, 2010 Lullabot, Inc. (“Lullabot” or “we”) has created this Privacy Policy to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for Lullabot.com and Drupalize.Me.

Collection of Data/Contact Information/Financial Information
IP Address and Cookies

We use your internet protocol address to help diagnose problems with our servers and to administer our websites. We also use your internet protocol (IP) address to help identify you and, if you subscribe to Drupalize.Me, your subscription. We may place “cookies” on your computer’s hard drive through your web browsers to keep track of your identity for the purposes of saving your logged-in state so that you do not have to log in each time you visit our website. “Cookies” allow our website to recognize your computer when you access our websites. If you would like to prevent the placement of cookies on your computer, or remove cookies from your computer, please access the “Help” portion of your browser or contact your browser provider. Please be advised that if you block or remove cookies from your computer, our websites will not recognize your computer and you may not have access to all portions of the websites.

Contact Information/Financial Information

Lullabot's registration and order forms require users to provide us with contact information to establish subscription accounts. Lullabot may collect each user’s name, address, city, state, postal code, telephone number and e-mail address. Lullabot uses this information to contact the user regarding subscriptions and administrative matters. New users must opt in to receive commercial communications and may opt out of receiving future commercial communications at any time (see “Anti-Spam Policy/Opt-out Choice” below). Lullabot also stores some financial information entered by users, such as purchase order numbers and related identification information. Lullabot does not store credit card numbers on our sites or servers. The financial information is only used to bill the user for products and services. Lullabot does not rent, sell or exchange user information, and generally restricts access to user information to qualified Lullabot employees and agents. Lullabot may transmit a customer’s credit card data only to the appropriate credit card company and/or credit card payment processing company. Third parties who need access to Lullabot user information to provide operational or other support services are required by Lullabot to maintain high standards of security. Lullabot gathers only user information that is needed to administer its business and enhance and improve its services and products. Lullabot may disclose a user’s contact or financial information if required to do so by law or in the good-faith belief that such disclosure is necessary to comply with an order of a court or government authority, or a subpoena. Lullabot may also disclose a user’s contact or financial information in the good-faith belief that such disclosure is necessary to protect Lullabot or its websites, or enforce the applicable Terms and Conditions, in conjunction with an investigation or legal, equitable or administrative action or proceeding. Lullabot may collect information on the services and products viewed by you on its websites. Lullabot may use this information to customize your experience and suggest and offer you products according to your preferences. Lullabot may use this information to identify patterns and trends of use, but will only release or publish any such information in a manner that preserves your anonymity. Lullabot may transfer part or all of your information in the case of a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, or other sale of all or a portion of Lullabot’s assets or stock.
