How the LTA handle information collected about visitors to LTA Online.

The Lawn Tennis Association does not capture nor store any of your personal information when you access LTA Online, except where you voluntarily choose to give us your personal details via email, or by enquiring about or ordering any of our services.

In these latter cases, the personal information you give us is used exclusively by The Lawn Tennis Association for providing information, product or service as is described in LTA Online.

We do not pass any of your personal data to outside organisations and/or individuals, except with your express consent.
 

 

 


Cookies

A cookie is a piece of information that is stored on your computer's hard drive by your web browser. We may use cookies to identify returning visitors to our websites and obtain information about your last visit.

Most browsers accept cookies automatically, but usually you can alter the settings of your browser to prevent automatic acceptance. If you choose not to receive cookies, you may still use most of the features of our web site.


Traffic Data

We may provide aggregate statistics about our sales, customers, traffic patterns and other site information to third parties, but these statistics will not include any information that could personally identify you.
