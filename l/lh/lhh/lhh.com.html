Privacy policy

 
Lee Hecht Harrison, the group of companies it belongs to and our global partner entities want you to feel confident using our Web site and our other online IT tools to which you may have access in the context of the provision of our career transition services (the “Online Tools”). In order to gain your confidence this Privacy Policy has been included online so as to provide you with a clear indication of how we address this important issue.

By visiting this site and using our Online Tools you are agreeing with and consenting to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. Should you not agree with this Privacy Policy you should cease using the Web site immediately.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as the Web site and its content develops and matures. We encourage you to check this Privacy Policy regularly.

RESPECTING YOUR PRIVACY

If you visit an area of our Online Tools to which you are not required to log on in order to read, browse or download information, we may record the date and time of your visit, the pages visited and any information downloaded. Our Online Tools will not record any information that would identify you personally. If you complete an online registration form via Resume Reserve™ on one of our Online Tools, the information that you enter into the form will only be collected if you actually submit the form.

We may also use the information collected to notify you about changes to our Online Tools, and to inform you of services, opportunities and special events that we believe you will find valuable and, of course, for our coaches/consultants to communicate with you during the course of delivering our services.

From time to time we may also use the information to send you information about us, our related companies and possibly about third parties which may be of interest to you.

We may also use your information to prepare statistical and quality reports on our services and the population we serve without identifying you in the report.

For that purpose and in order to improve our services, we may send you a survey, the answers to which will be shared with our management as well as with your Career Coach. If you would rather not share your name directly with your coach/consultant, elect to remove your name in the appropriate box on the survey. Your feedback will then be given to your Coach anonymously. We also report quality results to employers who sponsor individuals in our programs. However, names are never associated with those quality results provided to your employer. If you have secured a new opportunity, please update your profile in our Career Resource Network. We only use this information in the aggregate, so it is not associated with your name. This information about new jobs is vital to our ability to provide the best service and follow workplace trends. For the sake of clarity, by answering any of our surveys, you consent to the processing of your data as described herein.

We may also use your information to report on the services we perform with you to our relevant client. What we tell them includes: the name of your consultant, program activities in which you participate, and when you complete your program with us. Many clients request regular written feedback from us. If you wish to review the feedback we are providing at any time, please speak to your coach/consultant.

To facilitate our efficient use of your information, and to provide you with the best services and/or opportunities, it may sometimes be necessary for us to disclose your information to third parties. However, this disclosure will only occur in the following circumstances:

- To our Employees, Contractors and Agents From time to time we may engage or employ other companies and individuals to perform functions on our behalf. Examples may include, administrative functions associated with our business activities. Such parties will have access to only that personal information required by them to perform their functions, and they may not use such information for any other purposes.

- Where required by Law we will share information with government or law enforcement authorities in our sole discretion or as required by law.

- In the case of employers or recruiters who apply for a password to view resumes that are already posted on the site via Resume Reserve™, their use of the information is subject to the Resume Reserve Terms of Use.

Like most international businesses, we have centralized certain aspects of our data processing activities in accordance with applicable laws, which in many instances will result in the transfer of your personal information from one country to another. The jurisdictions where that information will be processed may or may not have laws that seek to protect personal information. Nevertheless, whenever your personal information is transferred within the group of companies LHH belongs to, your personal information will be processed in accordance with the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy.

COOKIES

We use "cookies" to enable our Online Tools to interact with your browser so as to provide you with better service and a more effective Online Tool.

"Cookies" are small pieces of information stored by your browser on your computers hard drive. Cookies are used to maintain session information between your browser and our Online Tools and are required for our Online Tools to operate. our Online Tools can later retrieve these cookies if you visit again.

We allocate a cookie to each Internet browser that visits our Online Tools. This cookie does not allow us to collect personally identifiable information about you. Our Online Tools use this cookie:

• to allocate a unique identification number to your Internet
• to identify pages you have accessed on our Online Tools
• to determine if you have previously visited our Online Tools.

If you wish to make full use of our Online Tools, you should configure your browser to accept cookies.

Our Online Tools also use cookies for our secure services. It is necessary for you to configure your browser to accept cookies if you wish to use these services which include third-party premium online database vendors.

IP ADDRESS

Our Online Tools gather your IP address to help diagnose problems with our service, to administer our site, and to gather broad demographic information. Demographic information is gathered in aggregate only and cannot be traced to an individual user.

REGISTRATION FORM

Our candidate use of our Career Resource Network (CRN) requires a brief registration in which full name, email address and postal code are collected. CRN's personalization features are dependent on this quick registration. Information collected in the CRN registration process is subject to the privacy controls described throughout this document.

USER FEEDBACK

We welcome ideas and feedback from you about all aspects of the site. We store feedback that users send to us. This feedback is used to administer and refine our service, and, at times, may be shared with our partners either in aggregate form or with specific identifying characteristics (e.g., e-mail address, name) removed. We may also use the information and ideas you send us to improve or promote the site, but we will not publish your name in association with any information you provide without contacting you and gaining your permission.

CONFIDENTIALITY OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

We recognize that information related to your job search is highly personal and sensitive. Access to your personal information is provided by use of your username and password. You are responsible for ensuring the privacy and security of your username. You may choose to send or store personal information with one of our representative. This could include job applications sent directly via e-mail to us, or making your resume available for searching by our Consultants, authorized third parties, recruiters authorized to access Resume Reserve™ (RR), and candidates via RR networking. These services are optional. Your resume privacy preferences may be edited at any time. We will not sell, trade, rent, lend or give your personal information to others without your authorization, except as expressly indicated in this Privacy Policy. If you contact us with a request for assistance in using site features or report an error, our authorized staff may access your user registration details solely in order to diagnose your problem and identify solutions.

You have rights to access and update personal information held about you. If you want to inquire about any personal information we may have about you, you can do so by contacting us (see Contacting Us section). You may also request that we correct, or stop processing, personal information that we hold about you.

DELETE/UNSUBSCRIBE INFORMATION

Users can unsubscribe from services at any time, by following the instructions on the site or in e-mail sent to support@lhh.com.
We are not responsible for removing information from third-party lists.

HOW SECURE IS YOUR INFORMATION?

We attempt to keep our server secure and free from unauthorized access and use, by enforcing physical security at the premises in which the server is located, and by using industry standard technological measures to prevent unauthorized remote access to the server.

Unfortunately, no server on the Internet or any data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. Despite our best efforts to keep our server and transmissions to and from our server secure, we are unable to absolutely guarantee security and there will always be a risk of unauthorized access to your information. Although we will take all reasonable steps to keep secure any information that we hold about you, we will not be held responsible for events arising from unauthorized access to such information.

Collection of Information by Third-Party Sites and Sponsors:

Our site contains links to other sites whose information practices may be different than ours. Registered users should consult the other sites' privacy notices, as we have no control over information that is submitted to, or collected by, these third parties.

The posting or distribution of registered user resumes may require the display to others of personally identifiable information contained in the resumes, which may result in registered users receiving unsolicited messages from third parties over whom we have no control.

Our Online Tools use reputable third party information providers. However, our privacy policy does not cover any information that may be collected at third-party partners Web sites.
