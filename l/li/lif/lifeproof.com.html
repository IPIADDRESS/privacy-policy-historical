Cookies may be prevented by turning them off in the user's browser. For instance, the user's browser may be set to give a warning before cookies are accepted, at which point a user may reject them. Credit Card Authorization and Capture

When you place your order LifeProof authorizes your purchase by verifying that your card has the available funds. Authorizing your card confirms you have the funds available for purchase. Your credit card provider sets aside those funds so you may not spend it on anything else. LifeProof then captures the funds for purchased items when they are pending shipment (when the items are boxed and ready to be picked up by shipping company). If an item is back ordered for 29 days or more, LifeProof will capture $0.05 (5 cents), before the 29 days are up so as to keep the authorization from expiring. If an order is expected to ship later than 90 days and earlier than 120 days, LifeProof will again capture funds before the 90 days are up.

Do not use email to send us your financial information; email is not a secure channel.
