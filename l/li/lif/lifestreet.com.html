Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 1, 2015

This privacy policy (this “Policy”) describes the types of information that LifeStreet Corporation and LifeStreet International (including their subsidiaries and affiliates, “LifeStreet” or “we”) collects from users of its corporate Web site, www.lifestreet.com, as well as any Web site that directly links to this Policy when you click on “Privacy” or “Privacy Policy” in the Web site footer (each, a “Site”). This Policy also describes the services we provide to our clients (our “Services”) as well as the information we collect pursuant to our provision of our Services. Unless otherwise provided in this Policy, this Policy does not cover our offline data practices.

This Policy is effective as of the date set forth above. We encourage you to check this Policy from time to time. If we make material changes that will affect any personal information that we have already collected from you, we will make reasonable efforts to notify you of the changes and to give you the opportunity to opt out. Your continued use of this Site constitutes your acceptance of the current posted Policy. If you have questions about our privacy policy, please contact us as provided below.

DEFINITIONS

The capitalized terms listed below have the following meanings when used in this Policy:

  • Aggregated Information is information about groups or segments of individuals that use our Services, e.g., how many people accessed a Property on a given day or from a given geographical area. Aggregated Information does not include Personally Identifiable Information.

  • Anonymous Information is information that does not (and cannot reasonably be used to) identify an individual. However, Anonymous Information when combined with Personally Identifiable Information becomes Personally Identifiable Information.

  • Client refers to a mobile application developer, advertiser, or publisher that directly or indirectly uses our Services.

  • Cookies are files that Web servers place on an Internet user’s computer that are designed to store basic information and to recognize the user’s browser. As discussed below, we use Cookies in connection with your use of the Site. We also use Cookies in connection with our provision of Services, which permits us to associate your device with other information that resides in our database so that we can personalize your experience with the Services. These Cookies do not store any Personally Identifiable Information. As with all Cookies, you can set your browser to notify you before you receive these Cookies so that you have the chance to accept them. You can also set your browser so that it will not accept Cookies. However, if you disable Cookies, then you will not have access to many features that make your interaction with our Site and the Services more efficient and some of the Services may not function properly.

  • Device Information refers to the information automatically collected from a consumer’s device pursuant to our Client’s use of our Services. Device Information is Anonymous Information (i.e., not Personally Identifiable Information).

  • Personally Identifiable Information is information that can be used on its own or with other information to identify, contact, or locate a single person, or to identify an individual in context.

  • Property is an application or Web site that is owned, operated, or controlled by a Client.

  • Web Beacon (or a “clear GIFs” or “pixel tag”) is software that can be used to display an image (usually a single pixel) on a website, email, or other communication. A Web Beacon is usually used to transfer an individual’s unique user identification (often in the form of a Cookie) to a database in order to associate that individual with previously acquired information about that individual. We use Web Beacons keep track of your online behavior (e.g., the websites you visit, the mobile applications you use, etc.) in order to help us to determine your interests.

YOUR USE OF THE SITE

You can visit and navigate through our Site to information about our company, our Services, and the products offered without telling us who you are and without revealing any information that can personally identify you. As discussed above, we use Cookies and other technology on our Site in connection with a hosted Web site analytics service that helps us better understand how our visitors use the Site. We review the anonymous information provided by these uses of Cookies (e.g., browser type, operating system, IP address, Internet domain) to better understand how our visitors navigate through the Site; learn what content is being reviewed; administer the Site; manage online advertising on the Site; and communicate with you about your LifeStreet account, your interactions with the Services or your contacts’ interactions with the Services. No Personally Identifiable Information is collected through our use of these Cookies.

You may wish to request information about our Services, the Site, or our company. In order to facilitate the distribution of this content, we may ask you to provide Personally Identifiable Information. Based on the nature of your request, we may also ask for additional information, such as how you anticipate you (or your company) will make use of our Services and how you heard about us. We store the information you provide for archival purposes and for our own marketing purposes if we feel that it is consistent with the nature of the inquiry. We will not use this information for any other purpose unless you give us permission to do so. Again, all of this information is provided by you on a voluntary basis only and is not required in order for you to use and enjoy our Site.

Our Sites may offer opportunities for you to register for free information, seminars, or demonstration copies of our Services. These registration forms are often linked to LifeStreet mailing lists, and require users to provide Personally Identifiable Information (such as name and e-mail address), which we use to send users information about our company and our Services. We also use this information to contact site visitors by e-mail for marketing purposes if they have granted us permission (on the mailing list form or other forms on our Site) to send it to them, and for administrative purposes such as providing time and location of seminars. Users may also be contacted by phone or postal mail for marketing purposes.

OUR PROVISION OF SERVICES

LifeStreet provides application Services that enable its Clients to deliver advertising to consumers based on their behavior and interest. If you interact with a Property or otherwise with our Services, we may collect the following information so that we can provide our Service to our Client:

  • Geographic location (e.g., current city).

  • Carrier.

  • Device make/manufacturer.

  • Operating system.

  • Property name.

  • Activities regarding ad interaction (e.g., views, clicks, and other actions).

  • Language settings.

  • Connection speed.

  • IP Address.

  • Android Advertising ID.

  • Browser type.

  • Apple’s Identifier for Advertiser.

All of this information is Anonymous Information (i.e., not Personally Identifiable Information). Once we collect this Device Information, it may be encrypted or hashed depending on the sensitivity of the information in question. We may also combine your Device Information with other, similar data from other consumers to create Aggregated Information.

We use Cookies, Web Beacons, and other technologies to collect and use Device Information. Our Cookies are not specific to any of our Clients. Accordingly, if you choose to opt-out of our Cookie for any LifeStreet Client, you will be opted out for all LifeStreet Clients. If you would like to opt out of our use of Cookies for these purposes there is a simple procedure to follow. Click here to replace your LifeStreet Cookie with an opt-out LifeStreet cookie.

When our Client uses our Services to request that we serve an ad to you, we collect Device Information that we use as provided in this Policy (you grant the necessary consents for such collection to occur when you download or use our Client’s application). We use Device Information as follows:

  • To provide our Services to our Clients.

  • To improve our Services.

  • To deliver targeted advertisements.

We may track and use Device Information over time and across multiple Client applications (or Online Behavioral Advertising) and/or combine Device Information with other Anonymous Information in order to deliver targeted advertisements through the Services.

As discussed above, we do not automatically collect any Personally Identifiable Information from you (unless you volunteer such information via our Site) but may obtain Personally Identifiable Information from our Clients in connection with their use of the Services. If you are a Client, we collect the following types of from you in order to provide our Services to you. Some of this information we must collect is Personally Identifiable Information:

  • Registration information when you create your account with us. In order to register for the Services, we require you to provide us with a username and password and your contact information, such as first and last name, email address, company name, company address.

  • Transaction information when you request information from us, pay us for your use of the Services, or receive a payment from us in connection with your use of the Services, such as your contact information, bank or financial account information, credit card or debit card numbers, and other information we need in order to respond to your request or process your payments.

  • Technical, viewing, and usage information that relates to your interaction with the Site, such as your location, the unique identifier or Internet Protocol address of the device or computer you use to access the Site, and the amount of time you spend on the Site.

OPTING OUT

If you decide to opt out of our targeted advertising, then ads we deliver to you on behalf of our Clients will be delivered to you based only on the Anonymous Information that is automatically transmitted when an ad request is received by our ad servers. In such case, any unique identifier such as the Android Advertising ID, Apple’s Identifier for Advertising, or the LifeStreet Cookie will not be used to deliver ads to you. The Anonymous Information that is automatically transmitted includes your device type, browser type, Internet service provider, information about the general content of the site or page displayed on your browser, and other Anonymous Information provided by the Web site or content provider. When you opt out, our systems will know that you have opted out and will not attempt to assign other unique identifiers in the future. PLEASE NOTE, however, that if you erase or otherwise alter your browser's or device’s cookie file (including upgrading certain browsers) or use a new or different device, you may need to opt out again.

You may opt of receiving targeted ads from us by clicking here.

As discussed above, we do not automatically collect Personally Identifiable Information from you (unless you volunteer such information) but may obtain Personally Identifiable Information from our Clients in connection with use of the Services
