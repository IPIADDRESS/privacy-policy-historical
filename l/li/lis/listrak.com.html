Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies

Some of our web pages, and some of our partners and clients, utilize cookies and other tracking technologies, such as a single pixel gif, to record session information.

We use both session ID cookies and persistent cookies. The following cookies are in use:

  • ASP.Net Session Cookie: This is a cookie used by ASP.Net to track session data. This cookie holds a SessionID which is used to track a user after they have logged into the Listrak application. That SessionID is used for security, access checks and ownership, and site customization. This is a session cookie and will be disposed when a user closes their browser.

  • Classic ASP Session Cookie: This is a cookie which performs the same function as the ASP.Net session cookie, but used by our legacy code.

  • Client Authorization Cookie: This cookie is used to track Client Authorization. This is a security mechanism to validate a user based on their machine. Once validated, a cookie will be created to avoid having to validate again. This is a persistent cookie and is set to expire 60 days after creation.

You can remove persistent cookies by following directions provided in your Internet browser’s “help” directory. If you reject cookies, you may still use our site, but your ability to use some areas of our site will be limited.

We employ a software technology called single-pixel gifs (a.k.a. web beacons). Single-pixel gifs are tiny graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies, and are used to track the online activity of web users. In contrast to cookies, which are stored on a user’s computer hard drive, single-pixel gifs are embedded invisibly on web pages and are about the size of the period at the end of this sentence. Listrak uses single-pixel gif images to count web page accesses and gather some general statistical information such as if an email has been opened or read. Collected information is tied to the users’ personally identifiable information to provide offers and improve the content of the site for the user. This profile is used to tailor a user’s visit to our web site, and to direct pertinent marketing promotions to them. We do not share your profile with other third parties.

The use of tracking technologies by our service providers, technology partners or other 3rd party assets (such as social media) on the site is not covered by our privacy policy. These 3rd parties may use cookies, clear gifs, images, and scripts to help them better manage their content on our site. We do not have access or control over these technologies. We do not tie the information gathered to our Customers’ or Users’ personally identifiable information.
