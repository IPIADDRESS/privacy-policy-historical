IP ADDRESS
Your Internet Protocol (’IP’) address is collected from all users because this information is automatically reported by your browser each time you view a web page. Your IP address is usually associated with the place from which you enter the Internet, which could be your Internet Service Provider. We may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, gather broad demographic information, estimate the number of people using the Site, track your movement within our Site, or for administrative purposes. Your IP address is not linked to personally identifiable information.

COOKIES/CLEAR GIFS
The Company uses ‘cookies’, which are pieces of data sent to your browser from the Site and then stored on your computer. Cookies benefit the user by requiring login only once, thereby saving time while on the Site. Cookies are also used to estimate and report total audience size and traffic, keep track of what pages are viewed and allow you access to certain areas on the Site.

It is up to you to configure your browser to either accept or reject cookies or to notify you when a cookie is sent. The ‘help’ section on your browser can assist you to change your cookie settings. However, you might not have access to some areas of the Site if you choose to reject all cookies. The Company does not use such cookies to collect personally identifiable information.

In addition to the Company using cookies, some of our web pages may contain electronic images (also called ‘clear gifs’) which allow the Site to count users who have viewed or visited that page or to access certain cookies. Any electronic image viewed as part of a web page, including banner ads, can act as ‘clear gifs’.

Some of our business affiliates may use cookies and/or clear gifs on our Site in ads or promotions. However, we have no access to or control over these business affiliates’ cookies and/or clear gifs and we urge you to read their privacy policy for information regarding their use of cookies and/or clear gifs.
