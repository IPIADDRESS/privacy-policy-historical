Privacy Policy

LIKEFOREX.COM takes and respects your privacy seriously. LIKEFOREX.COM collects small amount of user information when users register with LIKEFOREX.COM or use any LIKEFOREX.COM services.   2. Standard web log entries, including your IP address, page URL, and timestamp.
