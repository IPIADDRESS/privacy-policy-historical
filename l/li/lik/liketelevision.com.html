This statement discloses the privacy practices and policy for the web site LikeTelevision.com. Information Collected
We use cookies to authenticate Members, to deliver content specific to your interests and to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it every time you visit LikeTelevision™'s web site. LikeTelevision™ does not receive any information about you from these cookies. Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information (name and email) and demographic information (city, state, zip code, country, age and gender). We use customer contact information from the registration form to send our users information about new content and information about the company. LikeTelevision.com does not share any user information with third parties. Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site. LikeTelevision™ uses this data to understand our customer base and to improve the visitor's experience and navigation at our site, by recommending content we think they might enjoy. We also use this data to compile a monthly summary profile of our Members. The profile is an overall snapshot of all our Members. For instance, in early 2001, 81% were male, 19% female, and 80% have a broadband connection. The profile summary does not contain any contact information on any user and is merely a composite view of our Members at a given time. The profile summary information is provided to qualified ad agencies or corporate clients seeking to purchase advertising on LikeTelevision™. This site contains links to other sites; LikeTelevision.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites.
