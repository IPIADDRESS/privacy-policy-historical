This privacy policy (the “Policy”) outlines the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information that may be collected by Limkokwing University of Creative Technology (“Limkokwing”) when you use this website located at www.limkokwing.net, and all associated, subsidiary and affiliated websites linked to www.limkokwing.net by Limkokwing (collectively, the “Website”).

Limkokwing reserves the right to modify the Policy at any time without prior notice. By continuing to access the Website after such modifications have been published you signify your agreement to such modified Policy.

Information Collection & Use

There are a number of situations in which personal information may be collected from you. We do not share this information collected from the Website with third parties.

Feedback and Enquiry

We collect information including, but not limited, to your name, mailing address, phone number and email address when you register an account at the Website, making enquiries, signup for newsletters/updates, participating in contests and sending us feedbacks.

Course Registration

If you register for courses, we require that you provide certain personal, identification information, including, but not limited, to your personal information, guardian information, academic information and your credit card information. This information is necessary for us to process your registration and for us to get in touch with you.

Statistics Analysis

We also collect information about your computer and about your visits to and use of the Website, such as your IP address, geographical location, browser type, referral source, Internet service provider, operating system, length of visit and page views. This information generated, which does not identify individual users, is used to generate statistics and reports about the Website usage by using cookies, which are stored on users' computers.

Feedback and Enquiry

We welcome any questions, comments or concerns you might have with regard to your privacy when using the Website. If you have any questions about the Policy or our treatment of your personal data, please notify us.
