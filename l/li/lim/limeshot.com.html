What is a Logo?

  1. The primary symbol for your business.
  2. A sum of the values you stand for.
  3. The single item you will spend most time and money promoting (it will be on your cards, flyers, brochures, website, invoice and every customer communication).
  4. The one aspect of your brand that is therefore very costly to get wrong.
  5. Something that has to be meaningful for decades, since that's how long it will last.
  6. Something that should make you proud.
