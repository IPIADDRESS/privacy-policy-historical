Wedding Tip on the Week

Figure It Out

If the bride-to-be has a small waist, but full hips and top, she should seek a gown containing simple lines. Sheath and mermaid style dresses work well for this body type. She also may want to try sleeves that sit off the shoulder or even a strapless gown.
