Lizard Point Consulting takes online privacy seriously. Please read the following to understand what information is collected and how it is used.

Pages on this website use Google Analytics to track demographic information (such as the visitor's country), technology information (such as browser and operating system), and pages visited. This information is only shown to Lizard Point Consulting in aggregate form. Lizard Point does not see an individual's information or activity while visiting this site.

Lizard Point does not ask you for any personal information such as your name or email address. If you share that information with us by sending an email, rest assured that the information will not be used for anything except to reply to you.

Advertising

Some pages on this website contain third party advertisements. Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website.

Google uses the DART cookie to enable it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You can get more information on Google's privacy and advertising policies, and you may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting Google's Privacy Center.
