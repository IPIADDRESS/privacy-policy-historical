Please be advised that we may use your financial information, including credit card information, to verify the accuracy of your name, address, and other personal information, as well as to bill you for use of our services.

We may collect browser and Internet-related information through access to our Site, via cookies, and/or web beacons. We use your personal information to facilitate the services you personally request.
