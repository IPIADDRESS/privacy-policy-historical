Privacy Policy

We want you to understand what types of information we are collecting and what may happen to that information, especially because an essential function of this site is helping you with career assessment, development and planning largely based upon the information you provide. LiveCareer has created this Privacy Policy as part of our commitment to protecting information you submit. Please be aware that this Privacy Policy only applies to data gathered on web sites owned by LiveCareer (collectively "Site"). This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that LiveCareer does not own or control or to people that LiveCareer does not employ or manage.

By using the Site, you are agreeing to our Privacy Policy. Please review our Privacy Policy periodically as we may update it from time to time. Our privacy policy covers these areas:

1.     COLLECTION OF INFORMATION

Information is collected from you, our customer, at several different points during your visit(s) to the LiveCareer Site.

Registration--Any information you provide before registering on our Site is not associated with you individually and is not retained by LiveCareer unless you register with us. If you decide to register with us, you are required to provide certain categories of information, such as name, e-mail address, a password, whether you are a student, employed, or in another category, date of birth, gender and country of citizenship. In addition, we require that you provide other categories of information, such as an area of study that interests you and career interests you have. For some products, you are required to provide other information in the form of uploaded documents as well as resume information, such as contact information, career objectives and employment history and job descriptions. LiveCareer asks for this information so that you are able to use and maximize the effectiveness of our career tools and have the best possible experience with LiveCareer.

Optional Information--At various other points during your visit to LiveCareer, you may be asked to provide information about yourself. For example, you may wish to sign up for information about online courses relevant to your career interests. The use of other services which require the input of additional information is completely voluntary on your part. You have the choice about whether to disclose the information necessary to use these features of our Site.

In addition, during your visit to our Site, you may be asked to provide feedback, ideas, suggestions, and/or proposals (“Contributions”) with regard to our products and services. Submission of Contributions is optional, although encouraged, as it allows LiveCareer to better address our users’ needs and continuously work towards improving our products and services. By submitting your Contributions, you acknowledge and agree that i) such Contributions do not contain confidential or proprietary information; ii) LiveCareer shall be entitled to use or disclose (or choose not to disclose) any such Contributions for any purpose, in any way, in any media worldwide; iii) you irrevocably assign to LiveCareer all rights to your Contributions; iv) LiveCareer is not under any obligation of confidentiality, express or implied, with respect to the Contributions; and v) you are not entitled to any compensation or reimbursement of any kind from LiveCareer under any circumstances for submission of your Contributions.

Credit Card information—While many products and services on our Site are provided free, some require payment from you by credit card. To the extent we collect credit card information, we do not share that information with any third parties except to the extent necessary to complete a transaction. We also do not store complete credit card information (meaning we do not store your entire credit card number) unless you ask us to in connection with a subscription-based product. All credit card information is removed from our systems at your request. We also use a third party payment service to process your credit card transactions and any information you provide through that third party payment service will be subject to its privacy policy. PLEASE NOTE THAT OUR PRIVACY POLICY DOES NOT GOVERN INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE TO THE THIRD PARTY PAYMENT SERVICE.

Passive Collection of Information--In addition to the information which you actively provide to LiveCareer by, for example, completing the registration form, LiveCareer collects information about how you interact with our Site. LiveCareer collects passive information for purposes such as improving a user's experience at the Site and the compilation of broad aggregate demographic data and related usage information for internal purposes and for disclosure to third parties such as advertisers and content partners. The types of passive information collected by this Site include, without limitation, your IP address, which type of browser you are using, how you came to our Site, where you go on our Site and how long you stay on any part of our Site. Passive information does not identify you to LiveCareer by name and LiveCareer does not link your IP address to your personally identifiable information. LiveCareer may use its own or third party cookies to collect this information and to enhance your experience on our Site. Cookies are an industry-standard and are small data files containing certain information written to your hard drive when you visit a website. You may opt to decline LiveCareer's or third party cookies, but this might impact the functionality of the Site and the quality of your experience with us.

Collection of Information from Children--LiveCareer abides by federal law and the guidelines set up by the Federal Trade Commission regarding youth privacy. As such, LiveCareer will not knowingly collect any personal information from or allow the registration of young people under the age of 14.

Tell-A-Friend Program--You can choose to let a friend, your parents, or your son/daughter know about LiveCareer through our Tell-A-Friend referral service. If you choose to use the Tell-A-Friend service, we will ask you for your friend/relative's name and e-mail address and LiveCareer will send your friend/relative an e-mail inviting him or her to visit LiveCareer to learn more about our services. You will also have the opportunity to send your career test results or your resume to your friend/relative to facilitate a discussion with them about your career and/or your resume. LiveCareer stores your friend's contact information for the sole purpose of sending the e-mail.

     

2.        USE OF INFORMATION

The information which you supply to LiveCareer enables us to provide the assessment, salary data, resume tools and other tools which you select by matching the information you provide to our database of occupations, educational opportunities and other resources, including data provided by others who have visited our Site. Your information may be used in various ways, including:

(i) For some tests/tools, the information you provide is aggregated with information from other Customers to create a comparative database that allows LiveCareer to produce results and reports for you. When your information or the information of other Customers is used in this way, it is aggregated and is not used or disclosed in a way that allows third parties (including other Customers) to identify any individual user.

(ii) LiveCareer uses your information in order to customize our Site to your interests, enhance your overall experience on our Site and notify you of current and upcoming LiveCareer products and services. To save you time and effort, LiveCareer may pre-populate data fields within our Site or on third party affiliate offer forms with the information and materials, including resumes, you previously provided or submitted to the Site. In addition, LiveCareer tries to recognize you using various methods, including having you log in with your e-mail address and your password. Once LiveCareer recognizes you, pages, advertisements and other content is customized for you so that you can see what interests you the most.

(iii) In addition, as described above, LiveCareer utilizes passive information to learn more about how you interact with our Site. This passive information may be combined with your other information for purposes such as testing and improving your experience on our Site and the compilation of broad aggregate demographic data and related usage information for internal purposes and for disclosure to third parties such as advertisers and content partners and others who may be interested in learning about the type of information LiveCareer collects. This aggregate passive information does not contain any personal information which can identify any individual user.

Contacting You by E-Mail--LiveCareer may use your contact information periodically in order to send you e-mail regarding updates at the LiveCareer Site, such as new LiveCareer opportunities and additional listings which may be of interest to you. The frequency of these messages will vary depending upon your profile, including the types of career resources and other activities that you have indicated an interest in. In addition, we may send you free informational newsletters or notices relating to topics which may be of special interest to you, including career planning tools, financial aid, admissions tips, job opportunities and job advice. Finally, if you provide LiveCareer with permission at the time of registration, you may also receive commercial e-mails or communications from third parties.

To opt-out of electronic communications before you have registered with us, please uncheck the box above the "Continue" button on our home page.
