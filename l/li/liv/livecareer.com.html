LiveCareer Privacy Policy Highlights

This page summarizes the key highlights from our Privacy Policy. For more information, read our full Privacy Policy.

Personal information collected

We collect information in the following ways:

  • When you register an account, use our services, or make a purchase
  • When you view and interact with our features, and functionality, including sharing your resume, career profile, cover letters or other information with career, employment, education, workforce and other service providers who can help you advance your working life
  • Through cookies and other online tracking technologies, which collect usage information such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, mobile device, and receive the urls of sites from which you arrive or leave LiveCareer Applications, or sites that have embedded LiveCareer platform technology that allow us to recognize you, customize your experience, and serve advertisements both on and off LiveCareer Applications. Learn more about such technologies in our Privacy Policy
  • When you voluntarily interact with services available through LiveCareer, such as editing or resume writing services
  • When you participate in our surveys and promotions
  • When you contact us with a question, comment or complaint

Uses of personal information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Administer your account with us and customize the services we provide to you and other users
  • Fulfill products or services or process transactions that you have requested or agreed to receive
  • Enable you to communicate with other users
  • Share your information, including your resume, career profile, cover letters and other information with third parties offering their own services or combined services with LiveCareer
  • Connect you to opportunities by enabling third parties to contact you if you express an interest
  • Create and distribute advertising relevant to your experience on LiveCareer Applications
  • Send you transactional or promotional communications through email
  • Administer your participation in our promotions
  • Understand and improve our product and service offerings
  • Respond to your questions, comment or complaint

Your Information Choices

You can:

  • Access, review and edit the information you provide directly to us, such as your billing information and email address by logging onto My Account” and clicking the “My Settings” and “Overview” links or by contacting us as set out below
  • Control the messages you receive from LiveCareer and other third parties by logging onto My Account” and clicking the “My Settings” and “Preferences” links or by contacting us as set out below
  • Tell us to close your account by logging onto My Account” and clicking the “My Settings” and “Subscription” links or by contacting us as set out below

Important information

  • We encourage you to read our full Privacy Policy.
  • Our products and services are for a general audience and intended for adults only. We do not knowingly request personal information from children
  • Personal information you provide may be transferred, processed, and stored by our service providers in the United States, Canada and in other countries.

How to contact us

Questions about our Privacy Policy or about our handling of your information may be sent to privacy@LiveCareer.com or as set forth in the Privacy Policy.
