You can generally visit our Site without revealing any personally identifiable information about yourself. We may use your personally identifiable information to deliver certain services, products or information you have requested, provide you with notices regarding the products or services you have purchased, provide you with notices regarding products or services you may wish to purchase in the future, verify your authority to enter this Site and improve the content and general administration of the Site. We also may collect your personally identifiable information that voluntarily provide to us when you submit your comments and/or feedback to the Site.

Cookies

We may use small text files called cookies to improve overall Site experience. A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user’s hard drive containing information about the user. Cookies generally do not permit us to personally identify you other than as referred to above.

Aggregate Information

The Site may track the total number of visitors to our Site, the number of visitors to each page of our Site and IP addresses, and we may analyze these data for trends and statistics in the aggregate, but such information will be in aggregate form only and it will not contain personally identifiable information. Such aggregate information is not linked to any personally identifiable information that can identify any individual person. We may use such aggregate information to analyze trends, administer the Site, track users’ movement, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use.
