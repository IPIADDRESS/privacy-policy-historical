Avvio Reply Privacy Policy

We believe your right to privacy should be protected at all times. We are committed to protecting your privacy and the information we collect is either essential to use our service or for the purpose of enhancing your experience. We will never supply this information to anyone without your approval. Read on if you require more details.

What information do we collect?

When you contact us requesting further details of our services we collect contact information which will help us answer your queries. We also track anonymous website usage using Google Analytics so that we can better understand how the site is used and improve it in future.

How do we collect information?

We collect information on our website by asking you to input your details. 'Cookies' are also used to track your activity on our website, however it is entirely anonymous.

Why do we collect information?

The main reason we collect information is to enable us to effectively answer any queries you have regarding Avvio Reply and our range of services.

Do we share your information?

The answer is NO, unless you have given us explicit permission to do so.

Cookies

When using our website the following categories of cookies may be stored on your device:

  • Functional cookies. These are used to help us remember any settings that you may have chosen or details that are required to ensure the site works correctly.

  • Analytics cookies. These are used to anonymously record your website usage to help us ensure we are providing a useful service.

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies. These cookies are essential in order to enable you to move around the website and use its features, such as accessing secure areas of the website. Without these cookies services, such as secure login, cannot be provided.

Your consent

By using the website you consent to the collection and use of your personal information by Avvio Reply as outlined in this Privacy Policy.
