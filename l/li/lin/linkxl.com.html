We understand and appreciate the fact that our customers are concerned about the privacy afforded to their information collected when dealing with LinkXL™.. To display this commitment, we provide the following privacy statement for our customer's viewing.

The type of information generally collected by LinkXL™ includes your name, address, telephone number, user messages, email address, city, region and billing information in addition to tax information required by the IRS. This information is collected in order for LinkXL™ to perform the services that you have requested and to enforce the LinkXL™ User Agreement.

Information Collected on this Site

When you look at this website, our Internet Service Provider makes a record of your visit. Also, the following information is logged, used, and monitored for statistical purposes, enforcement of the User Agreement and to facilitate member disputes.

  • your server address
  • your top level domain name (for example .com, .gov, .au, etc)

  • the pages you accessed and documents downloaded
  • messages you have sent via member messaging
  • the previous site you have visited the type of browser you are using
  • any other information we choose to collect in reference to any member accessing our website

We do not use or disclose your personal information collected for any other purpose other than the primary purpose without firstly gaining your consent, or where it is required by law.

Credit Card Information

LinkXL™ which is fully owned and operated by LinkXL.com LLC doing business as LinkXL™. LinkXL™ is aware of the potential risks inherent to online transactions. As such, we have the most advanced security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.

Authorize.Net encrypts your personal information with secure server technology using Secure Socket Layers (SSL). SSL software will encrypt (scramble your personal information so that it becomes illegible to everyone but LinkXL™) before it is sent over the Internet to our secure site. You will know if you have entered a secure page when an icon appears on your browser, either a key for Netscape, Firefox, or a lock for Microsoft Internet Explorer.

Furthermore, each employee of LinkXL™ has been and will be made aware of security issues and practices as part of our extensive job training, and these practices are reviewed monthly at team meetings. As well, web security is reviewed ongoing.

Email

Under no circumstances will your email address be added, sold or given to a mailing list or used for any other non-LinkXL™ related purpose or by approved partners without your consent being obtained or where it is required by law.
