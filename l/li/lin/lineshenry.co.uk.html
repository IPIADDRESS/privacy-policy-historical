The Company may process information collected via this website for the purposes of: providing the Company's services; answering your queries; dealing with your order(s) and account(s); and providing you with information about products and services.

Or alternatively, you may opt-out by sending the Company an e-mail at help@lineshenry.co.uk , calling us on 08081 446611, or writing to the Company at the following address: The Graftons, Stamford New Road, Altrincham, Cheshire, WA14 1DQ

Use of Cookies.
At present the Company does not use cookies although the Company reserves the right to use cookies to identify you when you visit this Website, track your browsing patterns and build up a demographic profile of your business.

A cookie is a small piece of information stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive which enables the Company to know when you visit this website.

Use of cookies also allows you as a registered user of this website to be presented with a personalised version of this website, carry out transactions and have access to information about your account.

Most browsers allow you to turn off cookies. If you do this please look at the help menu on your browser. However, switching off cookies may restrict your use of this Website.

Disclosure.
The Company does not disclose information about you or your business to any third parties unless required to do so by government bodies and law enforcement agencies.

If you access other sites using the links provided, the operators of these sites may collect information from you which will be used by them in accordance with their own privacy policy.
