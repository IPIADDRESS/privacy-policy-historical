Privacy Policy Statement

Last Updated:   June 22, 2011

LinkShare Corporation (“LinkShare” or “we” or “us”) uses this website, www.linkshare.com, to promote or operate our families of advertising services.  The word “our” is a possessive pronoun referring to LinkShare. 

While certain areas of this website may be accessed and viewed by members of the public, not all visitors to this website may use our networks.  Each LinkShare network is available for use only by persons or entities that join that network as either an “Advertiser” or a “Publisher.”

Only persons or entities that operate websites through which they conduct a business are eligible to become Advertisers. Publishers must be corporations or other legal entities or adults (at least of the legal age to be able to enter into contracts in the jurisdiction in which they reside) that also manage or operate websites which they use to conduct a business or generate revenues. In order to become an Advertiser or Publisher, a person or entity must apply for membership and have the application accepted by LinkShare, which includes accepting certain contractual terms.

Information Collected from Visitors to this Website

We gather standard information that accompanies Internet transmissions, which in some jurisdictions is personally-identifiable information.  Other than this information, we do not gather personally-identifiable  information about or from visitors to or users of this website solely as a result of such visits or usage, other than from (i) people requesting to be contacted with more information about LinkShare offerings, (ii) persons or entities whom we allow to join a network as an Advertiser pursuant to a written contract that we and the Advertiser sign and physically deliver (an “Advertiser Agreement”) and (iii) persons or entities whom we allow to join a network as a Publisher pursuant to either a written contract that we and the Publisher sign and physically deliver or an “online” contract that the Publisher can review on the Publisher registration page on this website and agree to by clicking on a button on that page (a “Publisher Agreement”). We use the personally-identifiable information submitted with inquiries about LinkShare’s offerings to respond to those inquiries.

Our servers log the standard information that accompanies Internet transmissions, like the date and time of a visit, browser type, IP address, domain names, access dates and times, referring website addresses and operating system.  We use the services of a third party to analyze information about how visitors use our website. This third party uses web tracking technology such as, but not limited to, cookies, Javascript and/or other kinds of Web beacons. We have no control over this third party’s tracking devices.  We receive only aggregated, non-personally-identifiable information generated by this tracking technology. LinkShare may store cookies on a visitor’s computer in order to manage a visitor’s session on our site. By accessing our site, you agree that LinkShare may place cookies on your computer and our third party may use web tracking technology, in each case, as described above. You may opt-out of receiving cookies by adjusting your web browser settings to reject cookies. If you reject cookies, you may not be able to take full advantage of all of the features of our site, including “signing in” to password protected areas. A detailed summary of cookies can be found below under “Data about Customers of Advertisers and Publishers.”

Our networks are membership-based, and members must enter into a legal contract with LinkShare in order to join and use our networks.  Each of our contracts with Advertisers and Publishers contains terms that set forth the obligations of LinkShare with respect to the use and disclosure of the information that we gather from our Advertisers and Publishers.  Advertisers and Publishers should refer to those terms and not to this Privacy Policy.

 

Children’s Information

We believe it is important to provide added protection for children online.  We encourage parents and guardians to spend time online with their children to participate in and monitor their online activity.  LinkShare does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13.  If a parent or guardian believes that LinkShare has in its database the personally-identifiable information of a child under the age of 13, please contact privacy@linkshare.com and we will use our best efforts to remove the information from our records.

 

Information about Advertisers

We use a “template” form of contract to enroll Advertisers.  That template changes from time to time. that we publish on this website that are accessible only by Advertisers or prospective Advertisers to whom we grant access. We do not publish our template Advertiser Agreement on this website or make the incorporated network policies that are posted on this website available to the public.  We do provide the template and access to the incorporated network policies to prospective Advertisers with whom we are interested in pursuing a network relationship. Each such prospective Advertiser is encouraged, and has the opportunity, to review the template and any incorporated policies, including the terms that define the respective obligations of LinkShare and the Advertiser regarding use and disclosure of confidential information and privacy. 

LinkShare’s rights and obligations with respect to the disclosure and use of the information we gather about any Advertiser are governed by the Advertiser Agreement that we and the Advertiser sign.

Information about Publishers

In order to join a network as a Publisher, a person or entity has to apply for membership by completing an “online” application on this website.  The online application requires personally identifiable information about the applicant. The application form may be viewed by clicking on the Publisher registration page of this website. Besides the information that we gather by means of the online application, we may obtain a great deal of personally- identifiable information about a Publisher through a variety of online or offline means. That information includes the Publisher’s contact information, Taxpayer Identification Number (which may be an individual’s Social Security Number) and VAT registration numbers (for non-U.S. networks), as well as information about the business conducted by the Publisher on its website related to the use of our networks, including information about the online marketing strategies and plans and the kinds and volumes of transactions conducted through our networks generally and with specific Advertisers. We may use third party vendors to authenticate and/or supplement the information a Publisher applicant submits.

LinkShare’s rights and obligations with respect to the disclosure and use of the information we gather about any Publisher are governed by the Publisher Agreement that we enter into with that Publisher. Usually, that Publisher Agreement is an “online” form that, as a condition to joining any of our networks, we require each Publisher to accept by clicking on a button on the Publisher registration page of our website used for the particular network. In a few special cases, the Publisher Agreement may be a paper document that is manually signed and physically delivered.

We post on the Publisher registration pages of this website our “template” form of contract by which we enroll Publishers. That template changes from time to time and may incorporate policies that we also publish on this website. Each prospective Publisher is encouraged, and has the opportunity, to review the template and any incorporated network policies, including their terms that define the respective obligations of LinkShare and the Publisher regarding use and disclosure of confidential information and privacy. 

All applications for membership as a Publisher are subject to LinkShare’s acceptance. Data about Customers of Advertisers and Publishers

In providing our affiliate marketing, search management and related services, LinkShare does not deal directly with the customers of Advertisers or Publishers. We enable Advertisers and Publishers to create links between their own websites. A visitor who clicks on an Advertiser’s banner or other promotion on a Publisher’s website is redirected, through our website, to the Advertiser’s website, where he or she may purchase a product or service or engage in another transaction or activity that entitles the Publisher to compensation from the Advertiser. As a result of such redirection through our website, our technology automatically records the IP (Internet Protocol) address of the browser used. In addition, during such redirection, we may place one or more “cookies” on the hard drive of the visitor’s computer through his or her Web browser. Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that enable the systems used by the website operator who places the cookie to recognize the visitor’s browser. In connection with certain features of our services to Advertisers, we use cookies in order to determine whether and when a “cookied” Web browser is used to return to the Advertiser’s site after a prior visit. The cookies we currently use permit us to determine when a browser is used to make repeat visits or clickthroughs to a website and to determine whether the same browser has accessed more than one site on our network, but do not permit us to determine the identity of any user of that browser or gather any other personally-identifiable information about that user, such as postal address, email address, phone number, social security number or any credit card number. To learn more about cookies, click these links:

Our rights and obligations with regard to that information, therefore, are solely defined by our contract with that Advertiser or Publisher. We do not, through this Privacy Policy or otherwise, make any promise or undertake any obligation directly to any user of any Advertiser’s or Publisher’s website with regard to any use or disclosure of information that a user furnishes directly to any Advertiser or Publisher.  Each Advertiser and Publisher is solely responsible for establishing its own privacy policy, communicating that policy to the users of its website and ensuring that any access to, processing, storage and use by LinkShare, in accordance with the applicable Advertiser Agreement or Publisher Agreement, of any data about those users are consistent with that Advertiser’s or Publisher’s own privacy policy, applicable legal requirements of other obligations or commitments to such users.  To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, LinkShare expressly disclaims any obligation or liability to any user of any Advertiser’s or Publisher’s website of any nature, including with regard to any data about that user that LinkShare obtains, processes, stores, uses or discloses.
