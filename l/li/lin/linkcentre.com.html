Linkcentre Privacy Policy

This policy applies to the web pages held within the www.linkcentre.com domain.

Linkcentre is committed to maintaining the privacy of it's users. When a user performs a search across either the Search Engine or Directory, Linkcentre does not store any information which could identify the user. Linkcentre does collect statistical information about the number of keyword and directory searches but Linkcentre does not store any information which could identify who performed the search.

The Linkcentre database holds information about web pages. The following information is voluntarily entered into the database by Web Publishers:

Web Page Title
Web Page Address (URL)
Description of Web Page
Name of person entering the information
Email address of person entering the information
Comments through a feedback form

The name and email address of the person entering the information is stored in order to send a confirmation email when the information has been added to the database. Linkcentre does not disclose the name and email address to any third party or make it accessible through the web site.

Linkcentre works with a third party that serves ads to this site. To find out more about how Engage Media manages the privacy of information in conjunction with serving ads on this site, please go to http://www.engage.com/privacy.

Linkcentres email and discussion services are provided by Everyone.net. To find out how Everyone.net manages the privacy of information please visit http://www.everyone.net/main/html/p_policy.html

If you have any questions of concerns about this policy, please contact Nick Cookson by email or by writing to Interlink Advertising, 26 Stronsay Place, Bispham, Blackpool, Lancashire, UK, FY2 0HE.

Linkcentre reserves the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time. All changes will be posted on this page. Last updated 2nd July 2000.
