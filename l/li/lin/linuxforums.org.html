Our Privacy Policy


The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for the following domains and all subdomains thereof:
- linuxforums.org.uk
- linuxforums.org

This site makes chat rooms, forums, message boards, and/or news groups available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas b ecomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.

We may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site and maintain aggregate statistics on the use of our site.

Cookies are used to help identify you and your browsing session for SITE FUNCTIONALITY PURPOSES ONLY.

A record of your session MAY be kept in a database as part of the PHPBB software that we use on this site.

It is possible that some sites which you may find referenced from our site has a different privacy policy, you are advised to consult these privacy policies accordingly.

We do not pass on your email address to any 3rd party at any time except where required to do so by law.

We do retain a copy of your email address for as long as you have a user account with this site. You may cancel your account at any time by emailing info@linuxforums.org, your account details will be removed from our database within a reasonable timeframe; We aim to process & action all requests within 24 hours.

We occaisionally send NON-COMMERCIAL emails(site newsletters) to all of our users(a MAXIMUM of 2 per month), you have the right not to receive such emails, you may opt-out of these mails by emailing info@linuxforums.org with the word "REMOVE" in the subject line, or editing your user profile on the forums.

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, please email info@linuxforums.org with your enquiry and someone will contact you shortly.
