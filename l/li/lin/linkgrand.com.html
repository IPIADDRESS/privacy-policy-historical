Information Collected and How It Is Used
  LinkGrand will collect personal information from a user at the time of his/her sign-up or purchase. This information is used for contact purposes by LinkGrand only. Security

Access To Information
  Members of LinkGrand will have access to their personal information via the Member's Area. Such access will require the member to enter their username and password. All information, except the username, can be edited by the member.
Option To Provide Information
  Users that wish to become a member of LinkGrand are required to provide all information requested for the purpose of contacting and delivering traffic to the member. Information will never be used for any other purpose.
Enforcement
  This privacy policy will be strictly followed by all LinkGrand administrators.
