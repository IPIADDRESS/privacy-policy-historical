Introduction

Thank you for visiting usa.lkk.com. This website is owned and operated by Lee Kum Kee (U.S.A.) Inc. (“LKK”). The privacy practices set forth in this privacy statement are for this website only. Other Lee Kum Kee websites may have different practices. If you link to other Lee Kum Kee websites, please review the privacy statements posted at those websites.



 
What We Collect and How We Use It

 LKK collects personally identifiable information such as name, age, gender, postal address, email address and other information when voluntarily submitted by the visitor of this website. For example, visitors may provide such personally identifiable information when registering to become a member of the “Members’ Kumunity” program or may submit recipes or questions in the “Recipe Exchange Network.”

The information you provide by registering to become a member of the “Members’ Kumunity” is used by LKK to provide a customized and enhanced experience while using this website. For example, LKK may use your addresses to send newsletters, recipes, samples, and other information on LKK product and service offerings, and to allow you to participate in certain promotions, marketing events or services. LKK may also use your personal information to improve the features and content LKK provides on this website. LKK does not share such personally identifiable information with any third parties, except (i) to contractors or other parties who are working for LKK, (ii) if LKK is required to do so by applicable law or (iii) if necessary to protect LKK, its customers or the public.

However, LKK may share and use any other information you voluntarily submit, such as any recipes or questions, for any purpose including posting such information you submit on this website. For example, any recipes, questions or comments that you voluntarily submit may be posted as a submission originating from your username; other users may view such posting. In addition, LKK may share aggregated demographic and survey information (as opposed to personally identifiable information) collected from this website with third parties and use such information for any purpose.

 


 
Cookies and Tracking Technology

 This website may use cookies and other tracking technology to enhance your experience during your visit of this website. Cookies and tracking technology are useful for gathering information such as browser type and operating system, tracking the number of visitors to the website, and understanding how visitors use the website. Your personal information is not collected by this website through cookies and other tracking technology. LKK may, however, share aggregated cookie and other tracking information collected from this website with third parties and use such information for any purpose.

 


 
Information Security

Although LKK takes appropriate measures to safeguard the security of your personal information, LKK cannot assure you that personally identifiable information that LKK collects will never be disclosed in a manner that is inconsistent with this privacy statement. LKK has, however, put in place appropriate physical, electronic and administrative safeguards to help prevent the unauthorized access and use of such information.

If you want to access and update your information which LKK has collected through this website, you may do so by accessing and updating directly through this website or by contacting LKK at the contact information provided below.
