PRIVACY POLICY

LenSaunders.com is the official web site of Len Saunders. It is designed to provide updated information about Len Saunders and things related to Len Saunders. No information is collected or shared on this web site without your knowledge. You have the option to sign up for the Len Saunders newsletter, or sign up for programs, but this information is not shared with any third parties. We are committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998, and we urge children under age 13 to check with their parents before entering personal information on any web site. Also, we strongly recommend that parents discuss with their kids the risks of sharing personal information online.
