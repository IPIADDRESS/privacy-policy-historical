Privacy Policy

Our Pledge to Our Customers
Irving Levin Associates is proud of its reputation as the premier content provider to the health care and seniors housing M&A industry. We also have the utmost respect for your privacy. Any information you give us is held with care and security, and will not be used in ways to which you have not consented. Your credit card information is not stored on our server.
When you purchase any product or register for one of our news services, we automatically place you on our list to receive e-mail and direct marketing pieces regarding updates about special offers, new products, and new services geared towards helping you in your business decision-making. From time to time, we make lists of email and postal addresses available to other carefully screened organizations or companies whose products or services you may find of interest. If you do not wish to receive e-mail updates, please follow the unsubscribe instructions that are outlined in the e-mail updates or send an e-mail to info@levinassociates.com and type “unsubscribe” in the subject line and include the email address that you would like to remove.
Additionally, we invest significant resources to keep each customer’s business address, e-mail, fax, and phone information secure and current. This allows us to accurately distribute job-specific data and analysis through print, phone, e-mail, fax and other media.

Cookies
Irving Levin Associates, Inc. uses a feature of your Internet Web browser called a “cookie” to assign a unique identification to your computer. A cookie is small text file stored on your computer’s hard drive. A cookie cannot harm your computer, i.e. delete files, reformat your hard drive, etc
