Learnvest Online Privacy Statement

Last Revised: May 17, 2019

About

This online privacy statement (“Statement”) describes how The Northwestern Mutual Life Insurance Company (“We”, “Us”, “Our”, or “Northwestern Mutual”) accesses, collects, shares and uses information through use of the learnvest.com website (collectively, the “Website”). The LearnVest brand is owned by Northwestern Mutual.

This Website provides information about financial planning for consumers and does not sell products or services. Through this Website, We gather information that helps Us protect Website users, maintain the security of Our Website, and for the purposes described below.

Collection of Information

The Website automatically gathers aggregated information about people who visit Our Website (“Users”) to enable Us to continually improve on the content We offer Our Users, as well as for systems administration purposes.

The Website collects information automatically through a variety of technologies, including cookies and web beacons. The Website does not contain any functionality to respond to or honor Web browser "do not track" signals or other similar mechanisms, and thus does not respond to or honor such signals or mechanisms. Most web browser preferences can be adjusted to alert a User that a cookie or tracking code will be sent to a User’s hard drive, or a User can refuse to receive a cookie.

We do not sell User information to third parties. This Website does not enable unaffiliated third parties to collect personally identifiable information about a User’s online activities over time or across different websites.

Cookies

A cookie is a small text file that is sent to a browser when a User accesses the Website. The Website does not require a User to use cookies, but it is possible that certain features may not function properly if a User chooses to refuse to receive a cookie. We use machine cookies, which remain on a computer hard drive, to gather aggregated, non-identifiable website statistics through the website analytic services. No personal information is collected using this technology.

Changes and Revisions

We reserve the right to amend this Statement from time to time. Any material change to Our online privacy practices will be reflected in this Statement. We will indicate the date this Statement was last updated above.
