Privacy Policy

1) The information we gather from our members include our member's name, email address, Paypal email, Clickbank ID and Paydotcom ID.

3) However, members may provide information in our advertisers website voluntarily. This information is not store in our database and we have no control over the use of this information.

4) Members can change any of the information they have submitted at anytime. Members can also cancel their membership at anytime.
