Users will never be implicitly added to any marketing lists. Users subscribe to mailing lists by clicking on a checkbox in a form that clearly states what the checkbox means. The User has the right to opt-in or opt-out at any time.

We may use “cookies” to track how Users use our Web site. A cookie is a piece of software that a Web server can store on the Users’ PC and use to identify the User should they visit the Web site again. While not all of the information that we collect from Users is personally identifiable, it may be associated with personally identifiable information that Users provide us through our Web site.

LeadFans may also use information collected through our Web site for research regarding the effectiveness of the Web site and the marketing, advertising and sales efforts of LeadFans.

How do we protect your Information

After a transaction, your private information (credit cards, social security numbers, financial’s, etc.) will not be stored on our servers.

LeadFans will not disclose information collected from Users to third parties.
