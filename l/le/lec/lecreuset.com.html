Personal Information means any information that may be used to identify an individual, including, but not limited to, a user ID and password, a first and last name, shipping and billing addresses, e-mail addresses, phone numbers or other contact information that is gathered by Le Creuset as described below. To help improve the privacy policy, please send your comments to Consumer-Comments@LeCreuset.com.
 
 

HOW LE CREUSET GATHERS YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

 
When you register an account, you will be asked to provide your name, telephone number and e-mail address. Additionally, some of the websites' registry services, such as My Collection or contests, may ask for your birth date, gender, occupation, personal interests, etc. If you are a registered user your contact information is automatically saved.
 
LeCreuset.com may send you e-mails or postal mail promoting products or events that, based on your preferences, may be of interest to you, unless you have chosen not to receive them. During registration, you may choose not to receive e-mail solicitations from LeCreuset.com by selecting the "opt out" option.

Your credit card information is not saved, nor is it accessible in the LeCreuset.com data center or any of the LeCrueset.com files or records. LeCreuset.com will maintain a record of the products you purchased for yourself or as gifts for others. If you purchase a gift for someone, the recipient's contact information is automatically saved in your Address Book for your reference. It is your option to provide the information requested. However, if you choose not to provide the information we request, you may be unable to purchase products or subscribe to various services on the website.
 
When you request to be on the LeCreuset.com mailing list (for e-mail or regular mail) you will be asked to provide your name, address, e-mail address and telephone number. You have the option to remove yourself at any time from the LeCreuset.com and Le Creuset corporate mailing lists.Any requests to remove or update your contact information may take up to ten days for an e-mail request and six to eight weeks for a postal mail request.
 
Le Creuset’s Use of Cookies and Internet Monitoring Technologies
 
Cookies are small text files stored by your browser in your computer when you visit our Sites. We use cookies to improve the Sites and make them easier to use. Cookies permit us to recognize users and avoid repetitive requests for the same information.  Cookies also assist us by identifying the types of browsers and operating systems used most frequently by our customers, and how visitors move through the Sites. All of this information enables us to improve the Sites and tailor them to our customers’ needs and preferences. We use cookies for a variety of purposes intended to make our Sites a better service for you. We reserve the right to use cookies to deliver content specific to your needs and interests. We may also use cookies to track user trends and patterns in order to better understand and improve areas of our sites that our users find valuable.  Most browsers will accept cookies until you change your browser settings to refuse them. At any time, you can disable the use of cookies in your browser and still have access to much of the information on the Sites. However, you may not be able to access your account information or complete transactions on the Site unless you permit the use of cookies. In addition, our servers track various technical details about your visit, such as your computer’s Internet protocol address and the pages that you visited.
 
We also reserve the right to use outside ad companies to display ads on our sites.  These ads may contain cookies. Cookies received with banner ads are collected by our ad companies, and we do not have access to this information.
 
Interactive or Social Features of the Le Creuset Sites
 
We offer interactive and/or social aspects on the Sites. For example, if you choose to express an opinion in an online forum, post a recipe, respond to user polls, or enter any contests, we may ask you for Personal Information. If you request to sign up for newsletters or other promotional materials, or if you send an inquiry to one of our customer service representatives, we may store a record of your request along with any Personal Information allowing us to respond to your inquiry.

LeCreuset.com's current policy is to not collect Personal Information from any person under age 13.

When LeCreuset.com becomes aware that someone or some company is using LeCreuset.com without our authorization, LeCreuset.com will take reasonable steps designed to stop that activity. This may take time, and it may be difficult or even impossible to stop that activity. Thus, you should take precautions to protect yourself.
 
You should keep your password and other Personal Information in a safe location and not share them with anyone. After you complete your visit, please log off your account and close your browser window; this should prevent someone else from accessing your account.
