Information You Voluntarily Provide. We collect any information that you provide us when you use the Services.

  • Registration and Profile Information: If you register for one of our Services, we will ask you to provide your name, email address, and title. You may optionally provide additional profile information such as your phone number, date of birth, IM screen name, Twitter handle, and professional experience.
