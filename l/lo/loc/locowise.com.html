PROTECTING CHILDREN

As of April 21, 2000 the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act became effective. The regulations apply to the online collection of personal information from children under the age of 13. LocoWise Ltd is very sensitive to the issue of children's privacy and makes every effort to protect the privacy of children using the Internet. If you are under 13 years of age you should not use this website, and under no circumstances should you send information about yourself to LocoWise Ltd, this Web site, products, and services are not developed for or directed at children. Our policy is not to permit anyone under the age of 18 to do business with us. If LocoWise Ltd discovers that a child under the age of 13 has provided LocoWise Ltd with personally identifiable information, LocoWise Ltd will immediately delete that child's personally identifiable information from the website.
