Privacy Policy

The Home Swapping Company Limited (“the Company”) recognizes that its Members requirements for discretion and confidentiality in use of information and has created this Privacy Policy as part of the Company’s Terms and Conditions in order to make Members aware of the information the Company holds about them and how it will be treated.
The Company only collects personal information by lawful means and will treat personal information with discretion and confidentiality and in adherence with the European Union Data Protection Directive.
Your access to and use of the website located at www.lovehomeswap.com (the "Site") is conditional upon your acceptance of the Company’s privacy policy ("Privacy Policy"). In using the Site You consent to the use and disclosure of Your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.


Section 1
Collection and Use of Personal Information

The Company collects personal information from Members who register on the Site. The Company collects personal information in order to administer the home exchange business. The Company’s objective is to provide Members with a high quality, reliable, user friendly, secure, efficient and personalized service. The Company will not sell or rent any personal information about you to any third party.
Under normal circumstances the Company does not share the identity, address or email address or other contact details of one Member with other Members but facilitates communications between Members through a messaging system on the Site. However, Members may communicate their contact details to other Members for the purposes of facilitating home exchanges through the Company.
Circumstances may arise through the course of a home exchange in which one party to the exchange may have an urgent need to contact their exchange partner. In those circumstances, the Company may, at our sole and absolute discretion, provide a Member's contact information to the other Member.
If you choose not to provide the Company with personal information then the Company may be unable to provide you with its services.
Personal information may also be collected as users interact with the Site through cookies (text stored on a user's computer by a web browser).
You consent to the Company using Your personal information for the purposes of:
1. providing You with its home exchange services
2. responding to requests for information from You
3. marketing and advertising its services
4. providing You with information the Company considers may be of interest to You
If You do not wish to receive marketing communications from the Company You may opt out by sending an email making the request to info@lovehomeswap.com.
The only information which will be posted on the Site is the information which You chose to display on your listing. Other Members and the general public can view the listing information on the Site. The content of the Site is protected by copyright however the Company cannot prevent third parties copying or distributing information publicly available on other websites. Such action by third parties would breach the Terms and Conditions of use of the website and the Company would take any such a breach seriously and would prosecute any party found to be copying information from the Site in breach of our Terms and Conditions.
By accepting this Privacy Policy You agree that You will only use other Member’s personal information obtained through the Company only for:
1. the arrangement of home exchanges through the Company
2. any other purpose to which the other Member expressly agreed after complete disclosure of the purpose
In all cases You consent to give Members the opportunity to remove their personal information from your databases and records.
Under no circumstances, except as defined in these Terms and Conditions will You disclose another Member’s personal information to any third party without the express consent of that Member.


Section 2
Management of Personal Information
After registration Members can review and edit their information such as billing address, email address and password through the Site. Members can hide their listings from the Site as they require.
Should Members terminate their Membership the Company will remove their listings from the Site as soon as is reasonably possible.
The Company reserves the right to retain information about Members listings, communications and account in order to enforce our Terms and Conditions, analyse customer experiences to improve the business and resolve disputes. Members should not expect that all their personal information will be removed from our databases after termination.
On request by You the Company will provide You with access to Your personal information held by the Company except where access may be denied under applicable privacy legislation. In order to request access to Your personal information, please contact the Company on info@lovehomeswap.com. The Company will endeavour to respond to Your request for access to Your personal information within 14 days of the receipt of the request.

For answers to frequently asked question about how our Social Circle feature works with Facebook, please see here. 

Section 3
Financial Information

The Company may require certain information, including but not limited to Your name, address and credit card billing information to bill You for use of the Company’s services.
By making a purchase, or engaging in any other kind of activity or transaction that uses financial information on the Site, You consent to our providing Your financial information to the Company’s service providers and to such third parties as the Company determines necessary to process your transactions, as well as to Your credit card issuer for their purposes. These third parties may include the credit card companies and banking institutions used to process and support your transaction.
By purchasing, or registering for, or making reservations for products or services of third parties or by participating in transactions offered on the Site offered and or administered by third parties which require You to submit financial information You also consent to the Company providing your financial information to those third parties. Any of these various third parties may be authorized to use Your financial information in accordance with the Company’s contractual arrangements with such third parties and in accordance with their own privacy policies, over which the Company has no control. You agree that the Company is not responsible or liable for any acts or omissions. You agree that the Company may use and disclose all information submitted to such third parties in the same manner in which the Company is entitled to use and disclose any other information that You submit to the Company.


Section 4
Data Security

The Company uses industry standard practices to enforce data security. Members personal information will be stored securely and processed on computers in the UK. You acknowledge that no data transmission over the Internet is completely secure. Consequently the Company does not warrant the security of information that You transmit to the Company through the Site. Once the Company receives Your transmission, the Company will take all reasonable measures to protect Your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse or loss which may include but is not limited to the following: firewalls, secure network links, password protection and anti-virus protection.

Section 5
Changes to Privacy Policy

The Company reserves the right to change the Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective immediately on publication on the Site.


Section 6
Contact Details

If You have any questions or complaints relating to the handling of personal information, then please contact info@lovehomeswap.com.
