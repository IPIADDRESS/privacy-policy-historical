.About This Blog

Love My Surface was started by a husband and wife team, who worked as IT Directors for a large public university in U.S.A. They both strongly believed in the Windows tablet technologies and felt that MS Surface tablets received unjust reviews from the media. So, three years ago, they started the Love My Surface blog to help other MS Surface owners become more productive with their tablets.

Since then, Love My Surface had grown exponentially and acquired a loyal reader base. In 2015, over 2,500,000 readers visited the site.

Today, Love My Surface is the top Surface-related blog on the internet. Thousands of visitors come to the website each day to read and implement the how-to’s, work-arounds, and suggestions that Love My Surface provides.
