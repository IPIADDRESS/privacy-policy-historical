Privacy Policy

What We Collect

LoveMyFlash collects no personal details from its users. The only information we store is a users MySpace userid and information relating to the layout they have chosen. LoveMyFlash uses your MySpace userid to access your profile data to display in your Flash layout. No profile data is permanently stored however we may choose to cache data for as long as 24 hours to speed up loading times.

Third-Party Advertising Companies

We allow third-party companies to serve ads and/or collect certain anonymous information when you visit our web site. These companies may use non-personally identifiable information (e.g., click stream information, browser type, time and date, subject of advertisements clicked or scrolled over) during your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services likely to be of greater interest to you. These companies typically use a cookie or third party web beacon to collect this information. To learn more about this behavioral advertising practice or to opt-out of this type of advertising, you can visit networkadvertising.org.

Newsletter

Users may opt-in to our newsletter by providing their e-mail address when customizing their layout. This e-mail address will be used solely to send updates relating to the LoveMyFlash website and service and/or one of it's partner websites or services. Your e-mail address will never be shared with a third party. A link will always be provided in any e-mails sent out to opt-out from our newsletter.
