Information Collection and Use
Lovingyou.com, Inc. is the sole owner of the information voluntarily given to us by the users of this site. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement. Lovingyou.com collects information from our users at several different points on our website, as detailed below.

Our mailing lists & opt out policy
If a user wishes to subscribe to our mailing lists, we ask for contact information such as name and e-mail address. We have never purchased or used e-mail lists in order to spam. Any user who is subscribed to one or more of our mailing lists, is automatically also subscribed to the weekly LoveWire newsletter. Users who no longer wish to receive our newsletters, mailing lists (including the LoveWire) or promotional materials may opt-out of receiving these communications by sending a completely blank e-mail to unsubscribe@lovingyou.com from the e-mail address that is registered to receive these communications. Unsubscribe instructions are also contained in every subscriber-based newsletter.

Poetry, Stories, Quotes, Romantic Ideas, Dedications and similar sections of www.lovingyou.com. On these and similar interactive sections of www.lovingyou.com the user is asked to submit his/her name and/or e-mail address. Such sections include but are not limited to, Forums, Poetry, Stories, Romantic Ideas and Love Quotes. The information collected in these sections is not used in any way other than to identify the origin of the submission. In some cases, such as the Poetry and Stories sections, the user's e-mail address will be displayed along with their submission.

Automatically Generated E-mails.
In certain section of the site, such as Poetry, Stories, Quotes, Postcards, Forums and similar sections of www.lovingyou.com, users may receive an e-mail notification regarding their submission to the e-mail address the user specified when originating their submission. These computer-generated e-mails are used solely for the purpose of submission status notification.

Postcards
Our postcard services require visitors to enter names or nicknames and e-mail addresses of the sender and the recipient of a postcard and may contain private information inside. Lovingyou.com, Inc. does not share this information with third parties. Cards expire in 7 to 14 days and after that cannot be retrieved after this period of time, either by Lovingyou.com, Inc. or by the sender or the recipient, unless the sender or recipient prints or saves a card to their personal hard drive or other storage media. No copies of the postcard data is created and stored online or offline by Lovingyou.com, Inc. after the cards expire. Lovingyou.com, Inc. is not responsible for the grammar, spelling, views, 3rd party links, opinions and tastes expressed on any postcard sent from our sites nor for the frequency at which the postcard may be sent. We do not collect or use e-mail addresses of the senders or the recipients for any purposes. Lovingyou.com, Inc. takes no responsibility for incorrect information supplied by the sender.

Web Server Logs
Our servers log your IP address and browser types for systems administration purposes and statistics only. We do not link IP addresses to anything personally identifiable. IP addresses are used to gather broad demographic information. This means that a users session will be tracked, but the user will be anonymous.

The demographic and profile data is used for marketing purposes and for improving the services we offer. For example, the data allows us to determine the most popular pages in our site, how long users have visited, what browsers are being used so we can design our site better. Generally this information is collected through "traffic data" and may entail the use of "Cookies" "IP addresses" or other numeric codes used to identify a computer, but not personal data about our users. This information is shared with advertisers on an aggregate basis.

Other Sites
Please note that if you provide any information on your own or directly to parties who provide services to Lovingyou.com (such as Advertisers), any merchants in our Store, sites that we link to, or any other sites you encounter on the Internet (even if these sites are branded with our branding), different rules may apply to their use or disclosure of the personal information you disclose to them. From time-to-time our site requests information from users via surveys or contests. Participation in these surveys or contests is completely voluntary and the user therefore has a choice whether or not to disclose this information. Information requested may include contact information (such as name and shipping address), and demographic information (such as zip code, age level, occupation, etc.). Contact information will be used to notify the winners and award prizes. Survey information will be used for purposes of monitoring or improving the use and satisfaction of this site.

Public Forums

Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and is subject to our terms and agreements located at http://www.lovingyou.com/info/notices.shtml and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.
