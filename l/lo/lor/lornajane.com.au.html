BUY ONLINE - RETURN ONLINE OR EXCHANGE INSTORE

We want your Lorna Jane garments to fit just right and understand that when you order online you may occasionally receive a style or size that is not quite what you expected. To make sure you get the perfect fit we are happy to offer you a full refund on any unworn item in original condition, with tags attached, within 14 days of purchase.

Refunds can be issued on purchases made online on lornajane.com.au only. Refunds are not accepted in Lorna Jane retail stores. You may exchange any items purchased on lornajane.com in any Lorna Jane retail store.
 
All garments are subject to inspection. Shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer and cannot be refunded unless the garment is faulty. In the interests of hygiene, and due to health regulations we can not accept returns or exchanges on swimwear or underwear at anytime unless the garment is faulty.

3. Use of Information

We use your personal information for the primary purpose for which it has been provided, or for purposes related to or ancillary to that primary purpose, that is, for the purposes of you becoming a registered user of the website and any other purposes outlined above as nominated or requested by you.

Your personal information is only provided to third parties where required by law or to our agencies, contractors, related companies and promotional partners where they require your information for the purposes of the promotion or to contact you with special offers. If any personal information held by Lorna Jane about you is incorrect, you should contact us immediately, so that we can take reasonable steps to correct incorrect information about you.
