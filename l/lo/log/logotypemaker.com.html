Privacy Policy

This policy covers how we use your personal information. We take your privacy seriously and will take all measures to protect your personal information. We truly care about protecting your privacy. When you use LogotypeMaker, you agree to the collection and use of some personal information as described below. Any future changes to our policy will be noted on this page.

If you make a purchase, we collect your email and billing details. For your security, we do not store credit card data. You do not need to provide a credit card number to use the site. The payments are processed by stripe.com payment processor. Your billing information will be stored on stripe.com in order to have possibility to issue an invoice.

We will never sell, rent, share, or give your personal information to anyone without your express permission, unless required by law. You have our word on that. We do send email notifications or receipts when you create, save or purchase a new logo. We may also send a regular newsletter, but you can easily opt out by clicking the opt-out link at the bottom of the email.

If you are signing in/signing up using a facebook or google+ account we will store only your email and name. No other info will be stored from your social accounts.

LogotypeMaker.com links to several other websites and products. Any data you provide to other sites is not covered by this privacy policy.

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us

Help Cookies

LogotypeMaker.com puts small files (known as ‘cookies’) onto your computer to collect information about how you browse the site.

Cookies are used to:

  • measure how you use the website so it can be updated and improved based on your needs
  • remember the notifications you’ve seen so that we don’t show them to you again
  • keep you logged into your account

LogotypeMaker.com cookies aren’t used to identify you personally.

Find out more about how to manage cookies.

How cookies are used on LogotypeMaker.com

Measuring website usage (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics software to collect information about how you use LogotypeMaker.com. We do this to help make sure the site is meeting the needs of its users and to help us make improvements

Google Analytics stores information about:

  • the pages you visit on LogotypeMaker.com
  • how long you spend on each LogotypeMaker.com page 
  • how you got to the site  
  • what you click on while you’re visiting the site

We don’t collect or store your personal information (eg your name or address) so this information can’t be used to identify who you are.

We don’t allow Google to use or share our analytics data.
