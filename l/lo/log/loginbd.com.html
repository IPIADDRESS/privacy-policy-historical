Privacy & Policy

Overview
Individuals who visit this web site are important to us. We endeavor to collect only the minimum amount of information needed to meet the purposes for which the web site was created. User and personal data are two distinct terms referenced in this policy; user is nonpersonal data. Personal data is submitted information by the user's choice. At no time will this web site prevent or trick the user into visiting other web sites. 

User Data Collected
When you visit this web site, we collect and store the domain name which you access the Internet (e.g., yourdomain.com), your browser software and operating system, the date and time you access the site and the Internet address of any web site from which you linked directly to this site or to which you link directly after visiting. The information we collect is used to improve the content of our web services and help us understand how people are using our services. We may collect some or all of the following information about visitors who view our website.

Cookies
Cookies are small text files that include anonymous unique identifiers that your browser will reside on your computer hard drive. We use cookies to improve the quality of the loginbd.com services and to better understand how users interact with us. The web site experience is intended to make use of Cookies to benefit the user experience. The cookie technology we use cannot obtain information from your hard drive or pass on a computer virus. Users have the ability to modify browser settings to reject cookies; such actions may affect usability of the web site.

E-mail
E-mail or other information requests sent to this web site will be stored in order to respond to the request or to provide the webmaster/Admin with valuable customer feedback to assist in improving the site. E-mail addresses obtained as a result of a request to the state site will not be sold or given to private companies for marketing purposes.

Information security
We take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of data. We restrict access to your personally identifying information to employees who need to know that information in order to operate, develop or improve our services.

Children's Privacy
Loginbd.com does not knowingly request or collect personally identifiable information online or offline contact information from users under 13 years of age nor do us knowingly use or share personal information from users under 13 years of age with third parties.

Limited Guarantee
The site is in no position to guarantee the reliability of data presented. It is our best intention to ensure you have the most current and factual information available.

Privacy Policy Changes
Our website privacy policy will be reviewed periodically and we reserve the right to change and update our privacy policy without notice. We encourage you to visit this page from time to time to ensure you are aware of any changes we may have made.
