We may combine personal information collected from you with information from third parties or other companies for providing better user experience and customized services for you.

  • Customizing the content and advertising that you see

We do not rent, sell or share your personal information with other people or companies except when we receive your permission.

You have the right to view or edit your personal information yourself when you are signed into your account, or have the right to request that LottoMasterFormula.com edit the information on your behalf.

For personal information that may not be accessible via the websites, you may still access that information by writing to us by email to admin@LottoMasterFormula.com and we will contact you within 30 days regarding your request.

You also have the right to limit certain personal information from being displayed on your user profile for the public to view. You may request that LottoMasterFormula.com limit the information for you.

We limit access to your personal information to persons under the employment of, contracted by, or managed under The Palette who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with your information in order to do their jobs.

5. How To Opt Out
Our Newsletter is maintained by GetResponse. There is an opt out link below every email you receive from us. Click once on that link to instantly opt out from all future communications.

LottoMasterFormula.com may use "cookies" to enable you to sign in to our services. Your preference and information may be stored on the cookies in order to simplify your experience by saving you time from repeatedly entering the same information.

If you are using a public computer or do not otherwise want this information to be stored, you can select the appropriate radio button on the sign-in page, and this cookie will not be used. You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer.
