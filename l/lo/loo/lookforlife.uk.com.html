Privacy Policy

Protecting your privacy is of the utmost importance to us and we are fully committed to providing a service that safeguards your privacy. This page outlines how we will use the information that you provide. It also outlines what we will not do with your information.

We may collect information about you when you contact us via phone, internet of by any other means of communications. This may be through our website or in response to a communication from us.

Your confidentiality is important to us. We will use your personal information for purposes including:

i) The provision of quotations

ii) To pass on to our chosen FSA authorised advisers so that they can determine suitable policies

iii) So that we can perform market research in order to ensure that we are targeting the market in the most efficient way


Communication from Look For Life and its FSA authorised advisers will generally be over email, by telephone and post. If you have preferences with regards to contact please contact us and let us know.

Information you supply may be used by FSA authorised advisers to whom we provide services for the purpose of administering and processing your application
