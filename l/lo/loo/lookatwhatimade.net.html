Log Files

This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information received and collected by www.lookatwhatimade.net and how it is used.

Like many other Web sites, www.lookatwhatimade.net makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.
