Privacy Notice

("Loopt") values the privacy of our customers (collectively or individually, "Users") who use the mobile applications, www.loopt.com Web site, and other web sites, online services, or content (e.g., widgets, feeds, and applications) owned or operated by Loopt (collectively, the "Loopt Services").

This Privacy Notice governs the Loopt Services, and details important information about the use and disclosure of User information collected through the Loopt Services. Loopt provides this Privacy Notice to help you make an informed decision about whether to use the Loopt Services.

This Privacy Notice is incorporated into and is subject to the Loopt Terms of Service. Your use of the Loopt Services and any personal information you provide on the Loopt Services remains subject to the terms of this Privacy Notice and the Terms of Service.

IMPORTANT DISCLOSURES AND CONSENTS:
  • Unlimited Data and messaging plans are recommended for the Loopt Services. For some services, Loopt requires the use of SMS text messages. Standard data and messaging charges and taxes from your carrier apply.
  • Loopt collects, maintains, uses, and displays your personal data and the geographic location of your mobile device (we call this location fix of your device "location information" or "geo-location") to provide the Loopt Services.
  • By using the Loopt Services, you have consented to: (1) the use of your device's location to provide Loopt's services including relevant advertisements, and the display of that location information to your Loopt friends and within your geo-tagged messages and content; (2) receive text messages and emails; and (3) pay carrier data, messaging, and other fees resulting from Loopt usage.
  • The Loopt Services default privacy settings are set for the way that most Users use the services: we save your password for the mobile applications, your mobile device's geo-location is updated automatically, and that location information may be displayed to your Loopt friends even when Loopt is not open and running on your device. Changing these settings is easy - just select 'Settings' on the Loopt mobile application or manage your preferences via the Loopt.com Web site.
  • Please note that any personal information you voluntarily disclose online becomes publicly available and can be read, collected, and used by others and could be used to send you unsolicited messages. Loopt is not responsible for the information that you choose to disclose publicly on or through the Loopt Services.
THE INFORMATION LOOPT COLLECTS:
  • "REGISTRATION INFORMATION": You provide certain personally-identifiable information (such as mobile phone number, wireless carrier / operator, your name, email address, phone number, date of birth, password, etc.) when subscribing to the Loopt Services.
  • "PROFILE INFORMATION": When using the Loopt Services you provide certain other personal information and content - such as event times and locations, interests and hobbies, age, gender, sexual orientation, relationship status, and favorite places for breakfast, drinks, dancing, outdoor activities, etc. - that will be publicly displayed to other Users and on third-party sites and services (for example, when using the Loopt Services to publish updates to Twitter, Facebook, etc.) in accordance with your privacy settings.
  • "LOCATION INFORMATION": To provide the Loopt Services, we derive location information from your wireless carrier, certain third-party service providers, or directly from the mobile device that you used to register. This location tracking of your mobile device may occur even when the Loopt mobile applications are not actively open and running in the foreground, but your location is displayed and shared with others only in accordance with your privacy settings.
  • "COOKIES, PERSISTENT FILE INFORMATION": When you use the Loopt Services, we may send one or more cookies (small text files containing a string of alphanumeric characters) to your computer. Loopt may use both session cookies and persistent cookies. A session cookie disappears after you close your browser. A persistent cookie remains after you close your web browser and may be used by us during your subsequent visits to the Loopt Web site. Persistent cookies set by the Loopt Web site can be removed. Please review your web browser "Help" file to learn the proper way to modify your cookie settings.
  • "LOG FILE INFORMATION": When you use the Loopt Services, our servers automatically record certain information about your usage. These server logs may include information such as a mobile device identification number and device identifier, web requests, Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser type, browser language, referring / exit pages and URLs, platform type, number of clicks, domain names, search terms, ratings, landing pages, pages viewed and the order of those pages, features used in the Loopt mobile applications, the number of messages and friend requests related to your profile, the amount of time spent on particular screens, the dates and times of your requests, and one or more cookies that may uniquely identify your browser.
  • "INVITE-A-FRIEND INFORMATION": If you choose to use our invite-a-friend feature, then Loopt will ask you for your friend's mobile phone number or email address. If you provide a friend's mobile phone number, then Loopt will automatically send to that friend a one-time text message inviting your friend to join the Loopt Service and to add you as their friend. If your friend's phone number is on a wireless provider or mobile device that is not supported, then your friend will not receive this text message until their wireless provider or mobile device supports the Loopt Services. If you provide a friend's email address, then Loopt will automatically send that friend a one-time email inviting your friend to register on the Loopt website. Your friend may contact Loopt at privacy@loopt.com to request that Loopt remove this information from our systems, or simply reply with STOP to any Loopt friend-invitation text message to opt-out of receiving future invitations via text message.
THE WAY LOOPT USES INFORMATION:
  • Loopt uses your personally identifiable, registration, profile, and location information to operate, maintain, and provide to you and other Users all of the features and functionality of the Loopt Services.
  • To provide the basic functionality of the Loopt Services - such as displaying your live location to Loopt friends on the Friends Map or alerting your friends when you are nearby - Loopt maintains for its use only each User's most recent location fix. If Users choose to "geo-tag" certain content or data with location information, then Loopt maintains these geo-tags for so long as the applicable content remains on Loopt's systems.
  • Loopt uses your personally identifiable, location, and certain non-personally-identifiable information (such as anonymous log file information) to track and analyze User preferences and trends in order to improve the quality and design of the Loopt Services and to create new features, promotions, functionality, and services.
  • Loopt uses cookies and log file information to: (a) remember information so that you will not have to re-enter it during your visit or the next time you visit the Loopt Services; (b) provide custom and personalized content, advertisements, and information; (c) monitor the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns; and (d) monitor aggregate usage metrics.
  • Loopt uses a third-party data source to verify that your mobile phone number is on a wireless provider supported by Loopt. If you have a supported mobile phone number and device, then Loopt sends a text message to your phone in order to verify your custody of that handset. If your carrier or handset is not supported, then Loopt stores your mobile phone number and will send a one-time text message to you when the Loopt Service does become available on your wireless provider or handset.
  • Loopt uses your email address or mobile number from time-to-time to send you messages from Loopt such as privacy or security related notices, notifying you of major Loopt Services updates, or for other customer service purposes.
  • Loopt uses your personally identifiable, registration, profile, and location information to serve and personalize in-service advertisements specifically tailored to you and your interests. For example, if you put a favorite band in your profile, then we might serve you an advertisement highlighting the concert of a similar band playing nearby in your city. However, we do not disclose who you are to the music company or advertiser.
  • Loopt does not use your personally identifiable information or location information to send commercial SMS text messages to you without your express consent or unless if you have opted-in to be part of a specific program or feature in accordance with the applicable Loopt Services consent procedures.
