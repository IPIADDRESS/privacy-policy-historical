Geary LSF Group Privacy Policy

Geary LSF Group demonstrates a solid commitment to the privacy of visitor to our website. The following discloses the information acquirement and disbursement practices for the website, www.gearylsf.com

The Information We Collect

We automatically collect and use your IP address to assess problems with our server and to administer our website. You may choose to provide us with you name and email address or any other information about your digital marketing needs or questions at various pages on the website, and we will store this information. In addition, we may keep track of your activity on our website and at different websites you ink to through ours.

How We Use The Information

We use the information that you choose to provide about yourself in order to better serve your needs. We may use the information you provide us to keep you updated on any new services available by Geary LSF Group, as well as its affiliates and strategic partners.

All About Cookies

“Cookies” are pieces of information that a website transfers to your computer’s hard drive for record-keeping purposes. Cookies eliminate the need to re-enter information on a particular site, every time you visit that site. Cookies do not identify you personally, but can be used to identify your computer. Many website operators use this technology to better serve the visitors the their sites. Most browsers are set to accept cookies, but you can set your browser to refuse them. By doing this, though, you may not be able to use all of the features of the Geary LSF Group site and other websites. We do not use Cookies to track your use of any website other than ours and websites you may link to through the Geary LSF Group website.

External Links and Strategic Partners

The Geary LSF Group website may provide links to sites maintained and operated by strategic partners that we feel could be useful to you. We have also established marketing relationships with other companies who operate independent websites. You may have been directed to our site through a website operated by these or other companies. Information we collect is not shared with these entities, however, information may be separately collected by these companies.

Geary LSF does not own, have access to, or control, any information collected by these or other companies and is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such companies. You should separately review the privacy policies posted on these websites.

Email and SPAM Policy

Geary LSF Group does not sell any contact information you provide to us. We may contact you via email if we have an existing business relationship, if you have expressly opted in to receive emails from Geary LSF and our partners, or with occasional special announcements. Any official emails we send will come from the following domain names: @gearylsf.com or @lsf-corp.com.

We currently publish a monthly newsletter, Engage, which is emailed to readers. If subscribed, you may also instantly unsubscribe from that newsletter by clicking the ‘Unsubscribe’ link at the bottom of the email.

Geary LSF Group is committed to respecting the privacy of those who visit our site. Any online data collection from emails and forms submitted by you will only be used in the normal course of business to help us contact you and provide solutions to your challenge.

Your Security

The Geary LSF Group site has security measures in place to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.
