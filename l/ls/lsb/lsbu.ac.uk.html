Privacy Statement

The website only gathers personal information from you via any emails you send to us or an online form that you may submit.

London South Bank University collects personal information in compliance with the Data Protection Act 1998 and this is fully explained at each collection point. Unless requested by the authorities personal information will be held securely and not passed on to others.

All our web pages are designed to comply with the JANET Acceptable Use Policy.

Site Logs

We do log site statistics, such as what pages you left us on or what pages you viewed while on our site. This information is used to help us improve our website in the future by analysing which pages are working and which not.

We do not log your website movements after you have left our website.

Cookie Files

A cookie file is a small file that is placed on your computer by a website you have visited. They are only set for websites you have visited and hold preferences you have set on your previous visit. They ensure that next time you visit the site your preferences are still used opposed to the default.

Currently we do not place cookie files onto your computer or read cookies that are already there.

We do plan however to use a cookie files to hold your website preferences on font sizes or colour profiles in a future development. This will be available via our accessibility section and will be the only place where cookies are used on our site.
