This information is used to complete the purchase transaction. We may gather additional personal or non-personal information in the future.

Information about your computer hardware and software may be automatically collected by LPSG.COM. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring website addresses. This information is used for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the LPSG.COM website.

LPSG.COM encourages you to review the privacy statements of websites you choose to link to from LPSG.COM so that you can understand how those websites collect, use and share your information. LPSG.COM is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on websites outside of the LPSG.COM website.

Use of your Personal Information

LPSG.COM collects and uses your personal information to operate its website(s) and deliver the services you have requested.

LPSG.COM may also use your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from LPSG.COM and its affiliates. LPSG.COM may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

LPSG.COM does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties.

LPSG.COM may share data with trusted partners to help perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to LPSG.COM LLC, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

LPSG.COM may keep track of the websites and pages our users visit within LPSG.COM, in order to determine what LPSG.COM services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within LPSG.COM to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

LPSG.COM will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on LPSG.COM or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of LPSG.COM; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of LPSG.COM, or the public.

Use of Cookies

The LPSG.COM website may use "cookies" to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.

One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalize LPSG.COM pages, or register with LPSG.COM site or services, a cookie helps LPSG.COM to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses, and so on. When you return to the same LPSG.COM website, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the LPSG.COM features that you customized.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the LPSG.COM LLC services or websites you visit.

Security of your Personal Information

When personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to other websites, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) protocol.

We respect your privacy and give you an opportunity to opt-out of receiving announcements of certain information. Users may opt-out of receiving any or all communications from LPSG.COM by adjusting your account privacy settings.
