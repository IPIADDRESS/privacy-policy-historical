ABOUT FAIRPLEX

PRIVACY POLICY

Los Angeles County Fair Association uses personal information to better understand your needs and interest and provide you with better service. Once you choose to provide us with personal information, you can be assured that it will only be used to support your customer relationship with Los Angeles County Fair Association. We take seriously the trust you place in us. Los Angeles County Fair Association will not sell, rent or lease your personally identifiable information to others.

This privacy statement covers personally identifiable information, anonymous data collection and aggregate reporting. Personally identifiable information is any information that is associated with your name or personal identity. Anonymous (non-personal) information is data about usage and service operation, reported in aggregate, which is not associated to a specific personal identity. Aggregate information is data collected and summarized for a broad category of customers or Los Angeles County Fair Association services.

Los Angeles County Fair Association will not sell, rent, or lease your personally identifiable information to others and will not share your personal information with a business partner or any unrelated third party company, except as explained in this privacy policy or unless you give your permission.

Credit card transactions are handled by the ecommerce application, credit processing, however, is handled by a third-party financial institution and their vendors, which receive the credit card number and other personal identifying information only to verify the credit card numbers and process transactions.

SECURITY

Los Angeles County Fair Association is committed to ensuring the security of your information. To prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, maintain data accuracy and ensure the appropriate use of the information, Los Angeles County Fair Association has in place appropriate physical, technological and managerial procedures to safeguard the information we collect.

We use Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption when collecting or transferring sensitive data such as credit card information. We use this SSL technology to prevent such information from being intercepted and read as it is transmitted over the Internet. The encrypted data goes to a secure site where your information is stored on restricted-assess computers located at restricted-access sites. Credit card numbers are only used for processing payment and are not used for other purposes. Our efforts to protect credit card fraud help protect your financial data security.
