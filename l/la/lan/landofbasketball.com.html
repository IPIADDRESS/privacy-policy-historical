LandOfBasketball.com Privacy Policy

This page is the privacy policy of LandOfBasketball.com, a website that provides baketball information, including data about the NBA, its teams and players.

Privacy Policy of LandOfBasketball.com

LandOfBasketball.com, and its advertisers and affiliates are deeply committed to protecting the privacy of Internet users. Below there is information on what types of personal information we receive and collect when you use and visit LandOfBasketball.com

Cookies

LandOfBasketball.com does not use cookies to store any kind of personal information when you visit our site.

Statistical Service

The statistical service we use, Google Analytics, uses cookies to collect anonymous traffic data to provide us information such as visited pages, referring URLs, browser used and other publicly available data. This data does not contain information that allows us to personally identify you in any way. You can read more about how this statistical service works at Google Analytics' own privacy policy.

Third Party Advertising, Cookies and Web Beacons

To support LandOfBasketball.com we use third-party advertising companies such as Google AdSense to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use general information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.

This website, LandOfBasketball.com, also uses the services of other advertising companies and affiliate programs like Commission Junction, Amazon, eBay Partner Network, and All Posters for the serving of ads. To do this, we manually select the ads. Some of these advertisers may use technology such as cookies and web beacons when they advertise on our site, which will also send these advertisers non-personally identifying information.  This is generally used for geotargeting purposes or showing certain ads based on specific sites visited. All this companies have their own privacy policies, if you want to review them you can follow these links: CJ Privacy Policy, EPN Privacy Policy, Amazon Privacy Policy and All Posters Privacy Policy.

You can chose to disable or selectively turn off cookies in your browser settings, or by managing preferences in programs such as Norton Internet Security. However, be careful with this because it could affect how you are able to interact with some Internet websites. This could include the inability to login to services or programs, such as logging into forums or accounts.

Spam

LandOfBasketball.com does not intentionally send unsolicited e-mail messages that could legitimately be construed as spam. Our advertisers and affiliates are held to the same standards. We only send e-mails to answer questions from our visitors.

Logos and Trademarks

NBA logo, team names and logos are property of the National Basketball Association. This site has no affiliation with the NBA.

All other logos and trademarks belong to their respective owner.

If you have any comments or questions you can contact us.
