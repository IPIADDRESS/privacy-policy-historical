PRIVACY POLICY

Security/Privacy Policy
Thanks for shopping at landofnod.com. We've done our best to ensure the security and privacy of your transactions with us. Rest assured that your credit card information and other personal data will be treated with the highest standards of safety, security features and confidentiality.


Mail Preference
Occasionally we make our mailing list available to other mailers that may be of interest to you. If you prefer that your name not be included, please call us at 800.933.9904 or send us your mailing label indicating your request.


Secure Shopping
For your security, orders on our site are encrypted with SSL technology on both the client and server end. Your information sent via the Internet can be trusted to arrive privately and unaltered, to our server only. In order for SSL security to work, you must use an SSL-enabled browser. For more information about SSL, see Netscape's Website. (Please note that email is not encrypted, and is not considered a secure means of transmitting credit card numbers.)

If you're still concerned about ordering electronically, you can place your order by phone at 800.933.9904 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.


Your Privacy Online
At The Land of Nod, we are committed to ensuring the privacy of our customers. We keep confidential any information entered during ordering and registration. While our website automatically recognizes your Internet Service Provider, you cannot be identified as an individual. When someone makes a purchase at landofnod.com, we keep track of which pages of our site are visited in order to improve your overall shopping experience. We also use anonymous information about your visits to our website, and your interaction with our products and services, to target advertisements for goods and services on other websites. This anonymous information is collected by a third-party service provider through the use of a pixel tag, which is industry standard technology used by most major web sites. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this anonymous information used by our third party service provider, please click here.

In an effort to offer you the best and most current shopping opportunities, if you sign up for our email list, we may contact you with news about our products, services and events. If, for any reason, you would like to be removed from our email list, please contact us.


Cookies
Cookies are pieces of information that are stored by your hard drive, used for record keeping purposes. We use cookies to make visiting and using our site easier. Cookies can help us see which parts of our site are the most or least popular and provide us with direction to improve our customer's shopping experience. We never save confidential information like passwords or credit card information. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies. You can change your browser option to accept cookies by:

Netscape Navigator/Communicator: On your task bar menu, select "Edit" and "Preferences." Select "Advanced" and click "Accept All Cookies." Click "OK."

Internet Explorer: On your task bar menu, select "Tools" and "Internet Options." Select "Security" and "Custom Level." Scroll down to "Cookies" and click "Enable." Click "OK."
