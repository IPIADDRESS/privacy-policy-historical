What This Privacy Policy Covers

This policy covers how PicRate treats information that PicRate collects and receives, including information related to your past use of PicRate products and services. This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that PicRate does not own or control (including, but not limited to, advertisers), or to people that PicRate does not employ or pay.

Information Collection and Use

PicRate collects information when you register with PicRate, when you use PicRate products or services, and when you visit PicRate pages or the pages of certain PicRate affiliates, vendors, or advertisers. PicRate may collect information about you and your use of PicRate that is both personal and non-personal. For purposes of this policy, “personal information” is information about you that can be used to personally identify you, such as your email address, and that is not otherwise publicly available. “Non-personal” information is everything else.

When you register to use PicRate we ask for information such as your email address, birth date, gender, etc. Once you register with PicRate and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.

PicRate may also collect information about your transactions with us and your transactions with our business affiliates, vendors, and advertisers, including information about your use of products offered through PicRate. PicRate may also collect information about you from other sources, such as blogs and instant messaging services, and other users of PicRate services. PicRate automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, PicRate cookie information (including without limitation cookies, flash cookies, and beacons), and the pages you request.

When you use or connect to the PicRate website or download a PicRate mobile app by or through a Third Party Platform, such as Facebook or another social networking site, we may also access and/or collect certain information from your Third Party Platform profile/account (such as your Facebook account) or via any cookies placed on your device by such Third Party Platform as permitted by the terms of the agreement and your privacy settings with the Third Party Platform. We will share such information with the Third Party Platform for their use as permitted by the terms of the agreement and your privacy setting with the Third Party Platform.

PicRate may use some or all of your information for the following general purposes:

  • To customize your experience

These activities, in part, help to underwrite the cost of the content and services made available to you online.
