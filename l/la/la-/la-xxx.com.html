Users also should be aware that non-personal information and data may be automatically collected through the standard operation of EDPS, Inc.'s internet servers or through the use of "cookies." "Cookies" are small text files a web site can use to recognize repeat users, facilitate the user's ongoing access to and use of the site and allow a site to track usage behavior and compile aggregate data that will allow content improvements and targeted advertising. Cookies are not programs that come onto a user's system and damage files. Generally, our cookies work by assigning a generic file to the user that has no meaning outside the assigning site. They are simply used to verify the login to the members' areas and NO individual-level data is collected. Users should be aware that EDPS, Inc. cannot control the use of cookies or the resulting information by advertisers or third parties hosting data for EDPS, Inc.
