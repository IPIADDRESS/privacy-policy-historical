Cookies are files that your Web browser places on your computer's hard drive. We use these cookie files to track your clicks as you go through the La‑Z‑Boy Web site, to tell us whether you have visited La‑Z‑Boy previously, and to help us determine whether you came to La‑Z‑Boy from a particular Internet link or banner advertisement. La‑Z‑Boy does not and cannot use cookies to retrieve personal information about you from your computer.
