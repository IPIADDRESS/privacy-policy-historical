    privacy policy

    Personal information is private so we treat it with the highest standards of security and confidentiality.

    We never sell or give any personal information we have about you to any other company. You can rest assured that your credit/debit card and personal information are safe with us.

    With your permission, we'll send you our catalogues and email newsletters, tell you about our latest products and offers, and keep you up to date with what's going on at Lakeland.

    That's only if you want us to of course! It's up to you - you're in control of your catalogues & email newsletter subscription settings.

    use of cookies

    Like many retailers, as well as using cookies to measure the effectiveness of our website, we also use cookies to help us select the products that are shown to you, both on our website and within Lakeland adverts shown on other websites.

    These cookies store information regarding the pages and products you have viewed on the Lakeland site but do not store personal information that could be used to identify you as an individual. You may disable the use of such cookies within your browser preferences. More information on cookies can be found in our guide to cookies.
