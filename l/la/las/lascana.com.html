We have taken extra steps to make sure your online shopping experience is the best and is safe and secure.

As a visitor to LASCANA’s web site, www.LASCANA.com, you have the right to understand our information privacy practices prior to providing LASCANA with any of your personal information (Personal information includes information that can be linked to a specific individual, such as name, address, phone number, or e-mail address).

By providing you with this Privacy Statement, LASCANA is pledging our continued commitment to protecting the information you provide to us. LASCANA regularly examines our information privacy practices to determine whether we are adhering to the disclosures about the collection, use, sharing, and protection of your personal information made in this privacy statement.

Privacy Statement

This LASCANA Privacy Statement discloses the information privacy practices for LASCANA's web sites, http://www.LASCANA.com and http://m.LASCANA.com ("LASCANA").

Cookies and other web site technologies

Some information is collected automatically every time you visit LASCANA. LASCANA and our third party marketing partner collect information about your computer and assign you "cookies" (small, unique identifier text files) when you visit LASCANA. You do not provide this information—it is collected automatically as a means of supporting your visit to our site. Cookies do not contain any personal information. This anonymous information may be used by our marketing partner to deliver advertisements for products and services tailored to your interests on this site or other sites that you visit.

Most browsers are automatically set to accept cookies but provide features that enable you to be notified when a cookie is to be placed on your browser, decline the cookie, or delete cookies that have been placed on your browser. Most mobile operating systems also provide you an option in the device settings to limit ad tracking associated with your device id. You may also check your web browser’s instructions, help files or privacy documentation for further information. You should be aware, however, that some functions of our website or application may not work or may work slowly if a cookie is refused. This LASCANA cookie may be used to provide enhanced online display advertising tailored to your interests. To opt out of this advertising provided directly by LASCANA, click here. When you access the My Account area of the LASCANA web site, LASCANA requires you to sign in using your e-mail address and password. Once you sign in we record the e-mail address in the cookie file on your computer. We may also record your password in this cookie file. Permanent cookie files remain on your computer's hard drive until you manually delete the file.

How LASCANA Uses Online Remarketing Campaigns

LASCANA uses remarketing programs allowing us to reach people who previously visited the LASCANA.com website, and match those people with the appropriate message. If you want to opt out of the display of our online advertising, which appears on other Web sites, and which is based on online interactions with LASCANA.com, then please click the preference icon that may appear on such advertising.

For more information about your options regarding the use of data by Google Analytics, please go to: https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/181881?hl=en .

We do not respond to web browser “do not track” signals at this time. We await the result of work by the policy community and industry to determine when such a response is appropriate and what form it should take. We do allow you to exercise choice regarding the collection of information by third parties about your online activities over time and across third-party websites or online services for online interest based advertising purposes by going to http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/ or http://www.aboutads.info/choices/ or http://www.youronlinechoices.com .

For more information on your opt-out choices on your mobile device, please visit the iAd Network Advertising Settings page at https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT202074 for iOS-based devices and Google’s Ads Settings page at https://www.google.com/settings/u/0/ads/authenticated for Android-based devices.

Protecting your information

We want our site visitors to feel confident about using LASCANA to make a purchase, so we are committed to protecting the information we collect. LASCANA has implemented a security program to keep information that is stored in our systems protected from unauthorized access. Currently, LASCANA only stores the information you provide when registering at LASCANA.

Our systems are configured with data encryption, or scrambling, technologies, and industry-standard firewalls. When you send personal information to LASCANA over the Internet, your data is protected by Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology to ensure safe transmission.

To verify the security of our site, look at our URL in your browser window. The letter "s" added to the "http" indicates that SSL is in effect. In your internet browsers, a closed padlock appears in the corner when you are in secure mode. Also, in many cases, your browser will warn you if you are about to send information via a site that is not secure.
