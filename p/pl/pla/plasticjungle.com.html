Information Collection

Information You Provide To Us

We collect personal information from you when you affirmatively provide it to us, such as when you register, post, or contact us. This information may include your name, address, email address, telephone number, cell phone number, date of birth, Plastic Jungle Service login name and password. In addition, if you choose to buy gift cards through the Plastic Jungle Service, we will ask you for your credit card information or other payment information. We may combine the information you provide us with information we receive about you from other online and offline sources.

Information We Collect Automatically

When you use the Plastic Jungle Service, we collect certain information from you, including but not limited to your browser type, operating system, IP address, device ID, Internet service provider, search query you used to find Plastic Jungle through a search engine, and the web site access immediately before and after the Plastic Jungle Service. In addition, we may collect information about your browsing behavior, such as the date and time you access the Plastic Jungle Service, the areas or features of the Plastic Jungle Service you access, the amount of time you spend using the Plastic Jungle Service, the number of times you return to the Plastic Jungle Service and other clickstream information. We may combine this information with the personal information you provide to us.

We (or service providers working on our behalf) may use cookies and web beacons (also called "pixel tags" or "clear gifs") to collect the non-personal information described above. A cookie is a small text file that may be stored on the hard drive of your computer when you access the Plastic Jungle Service. A web beacon is a tiny graphic image that we place on the Plastic Jungle Service, in our emails and in the advertisements we place on other websites. We or our service providers may also use cookies and web beacons: (1) to recognize you when you return to the Plastic Jungle Service; (2) to provide you with customized content, advertising and marketing offers; (3) to measure the effectiveness of the Plastic Jungle Service, our emails and our advertising (for example, by telling us when you open or act upon an email or when you click on one of our advertisements); (4) to monitor Plastic Jungle Service usage; (5) to recognize the affiliate of ours that referred you to the Plastic Jungle Service (if any); (6) to conduct research to improve our content and services; and (7) for other internal business purposes. You are free to decline or delete cookies, but by doing so, you may not be able to use certain features on the Plastic Jungle Service or take full advantage of all of our offerings. Check the "Help" menu of your browser to learn how to change your cookie preferences. Blocking certain cookies will also block the use of certain web beacons.

We may also use third-party advertising service providers, who may use cookies and web beacons to track your behavior on the Plastic Jungle Service. These service providers may use this information, together with anonymous information about you that they receive from us, to deliver targeted advertisements to you when you are on this Plastic Jungle Service or one or more of the other websites where they deliver targeted advertisements.

Information Collected Through Our Referral Service

If you choose to use our refer-a-friend mechanism, we will collect your friend's name and email address, which we will use solely for the purpose of sending that person an email. Your friend may contact us at customerservice@plasticjungle.com to request that we remove his or her information from our database.

Information Collected by Third Parties

We may collect information from you when you use our services on another company's website or on a co-branded website. This may include, for example, when we make our Online Gift Card Registry service available on other companies' websites. All information that we collect through such websites is subject to this Privacy Policy. All of the information collected is owned and controlled by Plastic Jungle. We may share some of your transactional information and your name and email address in order for our partner to fulfill all or part of your order. Please note that the information you submit on such a website may alternatively be subject to the privacy policy applicable to that site.

You may change or update the personal information we have collected from you by going to the My Account page and clicking the "Edit Account Info" button [or by emailing us at customerservicet@plasticjungle.com].
