Plastics News.com has developed this Privacy Policy to advise site visitors of our possible uses of information collected during an Plastics News.com visit. We can be reached via e-mail at webmaster@plasticsnews.com. Inquiries may also be addressed to Ron Shinn at rshinn@crain.com or by telephone at (330) 865-6212.

Plastics News.com utilizes cookie technology for the purpose of identifying paid subscribers to our weekly newspaper. This identification allows paid newspaper subscribers to access all content on our Web site. Cookies are only used in conjunction with newspaper subscription account numbers to identify valid paid subscribers; no other information or tracking is collected or conducted through the use of cookie technology.

Our web server automatically attempts to identify the user's domain name, but not the e-mail address. We collect and store the domain name of users in our log files. We also track information, in aggregate, on pages and features that users access or view. Information collected about users in aggregate may be used to perform audits of our traffic or statistical analysis of user behavior in order to measure interest in the various areas of our site (for Web site development purposes) and to inform advertisers as to how many users have seen or "clicked" their advertising banners. We will disclose site usage information to third parties only in aggregate.

E-mail addresses submitted to Plastics News via our Web site or other methods are used for the maintenance of our content products and services such as the Web Watch Directory, PN DAILY NEWSMAIL, PN MATERIALS ALERT, EARLY CLASSIFIEDS BY E-MAIL or our annual processor rankings. Occasionally Plastics News will send information via e-mail from its select marketing partners that we feel will be of value or interest to our customers. If you prefer not to receive this information, please contact Lauren Cialella at lcialella@crain.com.

From time to time, Plastics News may make mailing information available to carefully selected third parties to send information through e-mail or postal mail about their products or services. If we share information with third parties, we require them to treat the information in strict confidence and to use it only for the purpose for which it was shared. At any time you have the right to request not to receive these types of offers. Plastics News retains the right to contact a registered user regarding account status or changes to the registration agreement.

To opt-out of receiving e-mail communications from Plastics News please click here. This will allow you to set your e-mail preferences. Alternatively, you can send a written request to: Plastics News Subscriber Services, 1155 Gratiot, Detroit, Michigan, 48207. Please include your name, full mailing address, and e-mail address. The letter should state: "Please remove my name from your e-mail database."

If you do not wish to receive other types of mailings from Plastics News, you may remove your name by sending an email to subs@crain.com with "Do Not Mail" in the subject line. You must provide your full name, your complete mailing address, and the publication name from which you wish to no longer receive mailings. You may also send the same information in a written request to: Plastics News Subscriber Services, 1155 Gratiot, Detroit, Michigan, 48207.
