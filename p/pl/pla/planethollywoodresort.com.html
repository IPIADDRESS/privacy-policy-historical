Cookies

PLANET HOLLYWOOD RESORT AND CASINO Web Sites use cookie technology. A cookie is a small file that contains information sent by a web site that is saved on your hard disk by your computer's browser. Cookies store information that a web site may need to personalize your experience and to gather web site statistical data, such as which pages you visit, what material you download, your Internet provider's domain name and country, and the addresses of web sites you visited immediately before, and after, visiting a particular web site. PLANET HOLLYWOOD RESORT AND CASINO Web Sites use cookies to collect and store your preferences for viewing the PLANET HOLLYWOOD RESORT AND CASINO Web Sites, such as whether you disable Flash so that you will not have to do so on follow up visits.

In addition, PLANET HOLLYWOOD RESORT AND CASINO Web Sites use cookies to provide a unique identifier to your computer so that we can generate statistics regarding usage of PLANET HOLLYWOOD RESORT AND CASINO Web Sites, such as the percentage of repeat visitors and other general statistics. In situations where PLANET HOLLYWOOD RESORT AND CASINO has Personal Information, the unique identifiers may be matched with Personal Information. In addition, when you book a reservation online to stay at a PLANET HOLLYWOOD RESORT AND CASINO resort, the reservation or confirmation number may be stored in a cookie on your computer and in an order file generated by Strategic Partners.

To browse the Internet and PLANET HOLLYWOOD RESORT AND CASINO Web Sites without cookies, you can configure your browser to reject all cookies or to notify you when each cookie is offered so that you can accept or reject each cookie. You may not be able to conduct some transactions or use some services on PLANET HOLLYWOOD RESORT AND CASINO Web Sites if you disable your browser from accepting cookies.
