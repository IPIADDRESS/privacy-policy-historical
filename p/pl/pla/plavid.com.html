    About Plavid

    Plavid is the exclusive videoadvertising platform of Mosaicoon specialized in distributing online video content for international brands.

    Thanks to a network of more than 10,000 premium websites, blogs, apps and social profiles, allowing to achieve 800 million users worldwide, Plavid guarantees the maximization of views and engagement around the branded video.

    Advertisers can plan their video campaigns in over 50 countries worldwide through engaging video formats user-initiated and fully responsive.

    Mosaicoon is the first Digital Media Company focused on the production, distribution and monitoring of video campaigns, managing the entire supply strategic and creative chain to turn a brand into a media company.

    Besides Plavid, Mosaicoon has developed two other proprietary platforms: Crevity – a creative agency for the design and production of creative projects and viral videos in crowdsourcing -, and Tracking, an advanced tool for real-time monitoring of the campaigns online’s performance.

    Founded in 2010, with offices in London, Milan, Rome and Mondello (Palermo), Mosaicoon won numerous international awards for the innovativeness of its business model.
