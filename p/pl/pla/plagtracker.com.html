Privacy Policy

This Policy Statement explains our policy regarding the use of all information collected about visitors to the Plagtracker.com website. The raw information that is collected is used to help us improve our website. This includes customization of content, formatting and layout. The information collected is never rented, shared or sold to any third party. Plagtracker.com web servers automatically collect information when you visit our website. This information includes your browser type, operating system, IP address, the duration your visit, the number of pages visited, and time spent on each page. We may employ cookies in order to customize web content based upon the aforementioned information.
