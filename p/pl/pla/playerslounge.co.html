How To Play

To start playing, you can create a match or join an open match or tournament.
Matches for money will remove the entry fee from your account and hold it until the match is completed.
Once your match has started, send a friend invite to your opponent on PSN or Xbox Live.
Within the game, invite your opponent to a match. For example here is the Online Friendlies mode in FIFA.
Once the match is finished, report the score back to Players' Lounge on your match page.
Once the score is confirmed the money is automatically released to the winner!
After playing in three (3) matches for $2.50 or higher you will receive a Players' Lounge Rating (PLR).
With your PLR you can find matches and tournaments are within your skill level.
Playing a 93 rated player is like playing against Messi in FIFA or Lebron in NBA 2K!
