The PlacidWay.com website employs "cookies" to provide users with tailored information. A "cookie" is an element of data that a website, when visited by a user, sends to that user's browser which, in turn, may store that element on the user's hard drive or memory. PlacidWay, LLC uses cookies to better serve users of our PlacidWay.com website; any cookies sent by PlacidWay will be marked so that they will be accessible only by the PlacidWay.com website subject to this Privacy Statement.
