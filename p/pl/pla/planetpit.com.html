PLANET PIT PRIVACY POLICY

Information We Receive and How It Is Used User information received will be used for the sole purpose of PlanetPit.com and its affiliates. User information will not be shared or sold to anyone else or any other company not directly related or affiliated with PlanetPit.com. The user information will be used for the sole purpose but not limited to promotions, contests, and to allow a better understanding of the user base to help provide better content for the users.


Registration Information When you sign up to PlanetPit.com, you are required to provide your name and email address. Additionally, you can choose to provide more information about yourself in your account profile.


Other Information We Receive About You We also receive other types of information about you:

  • We receive data about you whenever you interact with PlanetPit.com, such as when you look at another person's profile, search for a blog, click on a blog, or comment on a blog.
  • We receive data from the computer, mobile phone or other device you use to access PlanetPit.com. This may include your IP address, location, the type of browser you use, or the pages you visit on PlanetPit.com.
  • If you sign up to PlanetPit.com, we may ask you for more information about yourself such as your birthday, location, and gender. You always have the option to deny the request and still sign up for PlanetPit.com using the traditional Sign Up Page.


By signing up to PlanetPit.com, you are allowing us to use the information we receive about you. Nevertheless, you always own all of your information. Your trust is important to us, which is why we don't share information we receive about you with others unless we have:

  • Received your permission.
  • Given you notice, such as by telling you about it in this privacy policy.



How We Receive Your Information All information being transfered on PlanetPit.com is encrypted with 256-bit encryption. All connections use TLS 1.0.

The connection is encrypted using AES_256_CBC, with SHA1 for message authentication and DHE_RSA as the key exchange mechanism.


Deleting and Deactivating Your Account If you want to stop using your account, you can either deactivate or delete it.

Deactivate Deactivating your account puts your account on hold and you will be unable to login to PlanetPit.com. None of your information will be removed or deleted including comments left on PlanetPit.com. You can always inform us to reactivate your account at some point in the future. To deactivate your account, please email us and inform us you would like your account "Deactivated."

Deletion When you delete your account, it is permanently deleted from PlanetPit.com. All of your information will be removed including comments and all of your personal data. It usually takes about 48 hours to delete an account, but some information may remain in backup copies and logs for up to 30 days. Please note, you should only delete your account if you are sure you never want to reactivate it. To delete your account, please email us and inform us you would like your account "Deleted."

To deactivate or delete contact us at: info@planetpit.com


For questions, please contact us at: info@planetpit.com. Please allow up to 48 hours for a response. Thank you.
