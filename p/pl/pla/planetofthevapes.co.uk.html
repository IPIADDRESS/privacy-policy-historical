Planet of the Vapes Privacy Policy

Planet of the Vapes Limited revised website privacy notice. Published March 2019.

Introduction

Planet of the Vapes Limited (planetofthevapes.co.uk) is a source of information and news for vapers and people in the vape industry. Planet of the Vapes keeps its readers informed on world-wide vape news regarding products, legislation, health information and more. Planet of the Vapes Limited is a business, and as such the collection and processing of your personal data is in regard to the fulfilment of our business aims.

Your own personal privacy should be of importance to you and we take our position seriously. This Privacy Notice describes our policies and practices regarding the collection and use of your personal data and explains your privacy rights. We recognise that information privacy is an ongoing responsibility, and so we will from time to time update this Privacy Notice as we undertake new personal data practices or adopt new policies.

Data Protection Officer

Planet of the Vapes Limited is headquartered in London in the United Kingdom. Planet of the Vapes Limited have not appointed an internal data protection officer as our processing is not of a type or scale that requires one. However, should you have any questions or concerns about Planet of the Vapes Limited personal data policies or practices, you can contact us at anytime:

How we collect and use (process) your personal information

1. Personal information you give to us:

A. Corporate Customers

When you become a customer of Planet of the Vapes Limited or contact us regarding the services we offer, we collect information about you including (but not limited to) your name, your employer’s name, your work address (including your country location), your telepphone number and your email address.

We process your information under the General Data Protection Regulation Article (6)(b) to provide you will the product / service you have purchased, undertake standard business functions such as billing, and to inform you of relevant events, content, and other benefits or opportunities. Planet of the Vapes Limited may also use this information to help us understand our customers’ needs and interests and to better tailor our products and services to meet those needs.

B. Registered Users - Newsletters & Forum Accounts

When you become a registered user of Planet of the Vapes, we collect information about you including (but not limited to) your name, and email address. We collect these details for the purposes of sending a newsletter or allowing you to log in to the forum to post messages.

We process your personal information under the General Data Protection Regulation Article (6)(b) in order to provide you with information and internet forum services. We may also process this information to undertake standard business functions and under the General Data Protection Regulation Article (6)(f) to inform you of relevant events, content, and other benefits or opportunities. Planet of the Vapes Limited may also use this information to help us understand our customers’ needs and interests and to better tailor our products and services to meet those needs.

C. Publications

Planet of the Vapes Limited offers a large range of content on our website. In addition to producing original content, we also subscribes to content produced by others which we often link to from our website. This means you may find yourself on the Planet of the Vapes Limited website or reading an email from us and we will offer you a link to another organisation’s website where you will find content that we feel will be relevant and useful to you. At these times, you will be leaving the Planet of the Vapes Limited website. Planet of the Vapes Limited is not responsible or liable for content provided by these third-party websites or personal information they may happen to gather from you.

You may manage your subscriptions to Planet of the Vapes Limited newsletters by subscribing or unsubscribing at any time. If you unsubscribe from our newsletter we will still retain your email address but we will honour your preferences and not send you any further messages. If you would like your email address completely removed from our systems or if you have any difficulties managing your email or other communication preferences with Planet of the Vapes Limited, please contact us at support@planetofthevapes.co.uk.

Planet of the Vapes Limited uses Google Analytics to track how often people gain access to our website. We use this information in aggregate to understand what content our customers find useful or interesting to help us produce content and services to meet your needs.

D. Your correspondence with Planet of the Vapes Limited

If you correspond with us by email, the postal service, or other form of communication, we may retain such correspondence and the information contained in it and use it to respond to your inquiry; to notify you of Planet of the Vapes Limited services; or to keep a record of your complaint and the like. As always, if you wish to have Planet of the Vapes Limited “erase” your personal information or otherwise refrain from communicating with you, please contact us at support@planetofthevapes.co.uk.

E. Purposes for processing your data

As explained above, Planet of the Vapes Limited processes your data to provide you, or the company you work for, with the goods or services you have requested or purchased from us. We use this information to refine our goods and services to better tailor them to your needs and to communicate with you about other services we offer that may assist your business.

2. Personal information we get from third parties

Planet of the Vapes Limited does not receive personal information about individuals from third parties.

3. What happens if you don’t give us your data

You can enjoy much of the information on our website without giving us your personal data. Some personal information is necessary so that Planet of the Vapes Limited can supply you with the forum service you have requested or newsletters, and to authenticate you so that we know it is you and not someone else. You may cancel your forum account or newsletter subscription at any time by emailing us at support@planetofthevapes.co.uk to make sure that all personal data is removed.

Use of the Planet of the Vapes Limited Website

As is true of most other websites, the Planet of the Vapes Limited website collects certain information automatically and stores it in log files. The information may include internet protocol (IP) addresses, the region or general location where your computer or device is accessing the internet, browser type, operating system and other usage information about the use of our website, including a history of the pages you view. We use this information to help us design our site to better suit our users’ needs. We may also use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our website, analyse trends, track visitor movements, and gather broad demographic information that assists us in identifying visitor preferences. Planet of the Vapes Limited website also uses cookies, web beacons and click tracking. It does not track users when they cross to third party websites, does not provide targeted advertising to them, and therefore does not respond to Do Not Track (DNT) signals.

Cookies, web beacons and click tracking

Cookies are pieces of data that a Web site transfers to a user's hard drive for record-keeping purposes. Web beacons are transparent pixel images that are used in collecting information about website usage, e-mail response and tracking. Click tracking is a means of measuring the number of clicks a particular advert/image/banner receives.

The Site uses cookies to provide enhanced functionality on the site (e.g., user ID and password prompts) and aggregate traffic data (e.g., what pages are the most popular). These cookies may be delivered in a first-party or third-party context. Planet of the Vapes Limited may also use cookies, click tracking and web beacons in association with e-mails delivered by Planet of the Vapes Limited. Our Site also captures limited information (user-agent, HTTP referrer, last URL requested by the user, client-side and server-side clickstream) about visits to our Site; we may use this information to analyse general traffic patterns, to perform routine system maintenance or to provide our clients with metrics related to the success of their advertising campaign. You have many choices with regards to the management of cookies on your computer. All major browsers allow you to block or delete cookies from your system. To learn more about your ability to manage cookies and web beacons, please consult the privacy features in your browser.

This website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. ("Google"). Google Analytics uses "cookies", which are text files placed on your computer, to help the website analyse how users use the site. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the website (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States. Google will use this information for the purposes of evaluating your use of the website, compiling reports on website activity for website operators and providing other services relating to website activity and internet usage. Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Google's behalf. Google will not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google. You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser, however please note that if you do this you may not be able to use the full functionality of this website. By using Planet of the Vapes Limited website, you consent to the processing of data about you by Google in the manner and for the purposes set out above.

Registered Users:
When you register with Planet of the Vapes we create an account for you and store your data in one of our UK based servers.

Corporate Clients:

In both cases we do not otherwise reveal your personal data to third-parties for their independent use unless: (1) you request or authorize it; (2) it’s in connection with a request, from you, for information about a service offering from one of our partner organisations; (3) the information is provided to comply with the law (for example, to comply with a search warrant, subpoena or court order), enforce an agreement we have with you, or to protect our rights, property or safety, or the rights, property or safety of our employees or others; (4) the information is provided to our agents, vendors or service providers who perform functions on our behalf; (5) to address emergencies or acts of God; or (6) to address disputes, claims, or to persons demonstrating legal authority to act on your behalf. We may also gather aggregated data about our customers and Site visitors and disclose the results of such aggregated (but not personally identifiable) information to our partners, service providers, advertisers and/or other third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

The Planet of the Vapes Limited website uses interfaces with social media sites such as Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter and others. If you choose to "like" or share information from the Planet of the Vapes Limited website through these services, you should review the privacy policy of that service. If you are a member of a social media site, the interfaces may allow the social media site to connect your site visit to your personal data.

If you wish to confirm that Planet of the Vapes Limited is processing your personal data, or to have access to the personal data we may have about you, please contact us at support@planetofthevapes.co.uk.

You may also request information about: the purpose of the processing; the categories of personal data concerned; who else outside of Planet of the Vapes Limited might have received the data from us; what the source of the information was (if you didn’t provide it directly to us); and how long it will be stored. You have a right to correct (rectify) the record of your personal data maintained by us if it is inaccurate. You may request that we erase that data or cease processing it, subject to certain exceptions. You may also request that Planet of the Vapes Limited cease using your data for direct marketing purposes. In many countries, you have a right to lodge a complaint with the appropriate data protection authority if you have concerns about how Planet of the Vapes Limited processes your personal data. When technically feasible, Planet of the Vapes Limited will — at your request — provide your personal data to you or transmit it directly to another controller.

Reasonable access to your personal data will be provided at no cost upon request made to Planet of the Vapes Limited at support@planetofthevapes.co.uk. If access cannot be provided within a reasonable time frame, we will provide you with a date when the information will be provided. If for some reason access is denied, we will provide an explanation as to why access has been denied.
