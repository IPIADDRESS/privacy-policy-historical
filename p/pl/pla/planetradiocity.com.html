  • Music Broadcast Limited owns Radio City 91.1FM, India 's first and leading private FM radio brand and Radiocity.in, India 's mega music portal. Music Broadcast Limited set up India 's first private FM station when it started broadcasting Radio City Bangalore in June 2001. As part of Phase II expansion of FM Radio, Music Broadcast Limited has taken the Radio City experience to 20 of India 's largest metros and mini metros. Taking the lead to offer a distinctive listening experience, Radio City 91.1FM's differentiated music offers listeners along with a fresh outlook, 'FM Bole Toh Radio City', giving listeners a unique and uniform brand experience, in line with the vision of creating a national brand.
