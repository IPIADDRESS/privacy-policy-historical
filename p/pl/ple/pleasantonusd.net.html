Privacy Policy

Pleasanton Unified School District Web Site

Information We Record

Our website’s operating system will automatically record some general information about your visit:

  • The type of browser (such as “Firefox version X” or “Internet Explorer version X”) that you are using.
  • The type of operating system that you use (such as Macintosh, Unix, or Windows).
  • The date and time you visit our site, and the web pages that you visit on our site.
  • Data about your browsing session variables, including your browser window pixel dimensions, bandwidth availability, screen bit depth, JavaScript status, availability of plugins such as Flash, and time spent on different pages within the site.
  • The address of the previous web site you were visiting, if you linked to us from another web site (such as a search engine, municipal web site, etc.).

We use this information for statistical analysis, to help us make our site more useful to visitors. The information is not reviewed on an individual basis, but is aggregated with other users for comparison purposes. This tracking system does not provide us with information about individual visitors, though Google may track your individual web usage via the Google Analytics code on our site, particularly if you are actively logged into your Google account (please visit Google for more information about their privacy practices).

Information Received in Online Forms

If you decide to join our e-mail group or complete one of the online forms on our website, you may be asked for personally identifying information, such as your name, phone number or e-mail address. We may need said information because of a service we provide (such as the E-Connect Newsletter) or because we need your contact information (if you have elected to sign up for e-mail updates, for example). This information will not be shared with any organization or individuals except employees of Pleasanton Unified School District as needed.

Links to Other Websites

Our policy discloses the privacy practices for the Pleasanton Unified School District website, but we also provide links to other websites. When you leave the Pleasanton Unified School District website (www.pleasantonusd.net), you will go to sites that are not under the restrictions of our privacy policies and procedures. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any site you link to from ours, especially if you share any personal information. It is the intent of Pleasanton Unified School District that any links provided on our website are educational in nature, and support the policies and goals of the district. Pleasanton Unified School District does not control or guarantee the accuracy, relevance, timeliness, or completeness of this outside information. Further, the inclusion of links or pointers to particular items in hypertext is not intended to endorse any views expressed, or products or services offered, on these outside sites, or the organizations sponsoring the sites.

Student Safety

A student’s picture and name will not appear on the same page. Documents will not include a child’s full name, phone number, street address, or names of other family members or include any information that indicates the physical location of a student at a given time other than attendance at a particular school and in a particular grade.

Copyright

The Pleasanton Unified School District website uses only copyright-free images, unless otherwise noted.
