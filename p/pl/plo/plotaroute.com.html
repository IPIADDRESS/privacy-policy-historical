Our Privacy Policy


Privacy Statement
Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy, we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used by us.


How We Use the Information
We collect personal information about registered users to help administrate and enhance the service.

We do not collect personal information about people without their permission. The personal information we gather during registration is not shared with other organisations or third parties without your permission.

When you register with us, you consent to receive emails from us which may contain promotional information from us or third parties.  You can opt to not receive these emails at any time by changing your account settings. We may also send you emails from time to time regarding the administration of your account.

In common with most organisations, we may share aggregate information about our users with other third parties. This information shows user activity as a whole rather than on an individual basis and cannot be used to individually identify you.


Updating Your Personal Information
We provide a way for you to correct or change information collected during registration. This can be done through the My Account page.


Provision of Information to Third-Party Sites and Sponsors
We will not pass your personal details to any third parties advertising on our website without your permission. Some of our website pages contain links to other sites whose information practices may be different than ours. Visitors should consult the other sites' privacy notices as we have no control over information that is submitted to, or collected by, these third parties.


The Use of Cookies
Cookies are text files we place in your computer's browser to store your information that our website can use access later. We may use cookies to store information on your computer if we feel this will improve the service we offer by personalising your experience. We may also store information in your browser's local storage for the same reason.


Our Commitment to Security
We have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and help prevent unauthorised access, maintain data security, and correctly use the information we collect online. To protect your privacy and security, we will take reasonable steps to help verify your identity before granting access or making corrections.

The password you have chosen for signing is stored in encrypted form on our database and is not accessable in it's unencrypted form.


Privacy Notice Updates
From time to time, we may update this privacy notice. We will notify you about material changes in the way we treat personally identifiable information by placing a notice on our site. We encourage you to periodically check back and review this policy so that you always will know what information we collect, how we use it, and to whom we disclose it.


How to Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy please contact us using the Contact Us form on the website.
