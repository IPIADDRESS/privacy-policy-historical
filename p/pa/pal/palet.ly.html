Privacy Policy
Thank you for using Paletly! This Privacy Policy has been written to help you understand what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have regarding your information.

When you sign up on Paletly, you voluntarily give us certain information. This can include your name, profile photo, comments, uploads, email address etc you used to sign up, and any other information you provide us. If you’re using Paletly on your mobile device, you can also choose to provide us with location data.

You also may give us permission to access your information in other services. For example, you may link your Facebook account to Paletly, which allows us to obtain information (e.g., your friends or contacts). The information we obtain often depends on your settings or privacy policies.

When you use Paletly, our servers automatically record information about your interaction with the products, including information that your browser sends whenever you use it to access Paletly. This data may include your Internet Protocol address, the address of the web page you were visiting before you came to Paletly, browser type and settings, the date and time of your request, and how you used Paletly.

Depending on how you’re accessing our products, we may use cookies or similar technologies to record log data. When we use cookies, we may use “session” cookies (that last until you close your browser) or “persistent” cookies (that last until you or your browser delete them).

In addition to log data, we may also collect information about the device you’re using Paletly on, including what type of device it is, what operating system you’re using, device settings, unique device identifiers, and crash data. Whether we collect some or all of this information often depends on what type of device you’re using and its settings. We use the information we collect to make our website better.

Our goal is to give you simple and meaningful choices over your information. If you have a Paletly account, many of the choices you have on Paletly are built directly in to the product or your account settings.

For example, you can:
  • Access and change information in your profile page at any time, your profile picture, etc.
  • Link or unlink your Paletly account from an account on Facebook. For Facebook, you can also choose whether or not to publish your activity on Paletly to your wall.
  • Close your account at any time. When you close your account, we’ll deactivate it and remove all your uploaded images, wish lists and any other information from Paletly.
You may have choices available to you through the device or software you use to access Paletly.
  • The browser you use may provide you with the ability to control cookies or other types of local data storage;
  • Your mobile device may provide you with choices around how and whether location or other data is shared with us.
Limited instances where we may share your personal information include:
  • When we have your consent. This includes sharing information with Facebook when you’ve chosen to link it to your Paletly account or publish your activity on Paletly to your wall.
  • We may employ third party companies or individuals to process personal information on our behalf based on our instructions and in compliance with this Privacy Policy. In addition, some of the information we request may be collected by third party providers on our behalf.
  • If we believe that disclosure is reasonably necessary to comply with a law, regulation or legal request; to protect the safety, rights, or property of the public, any person, or Paletly; or to detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security or technical issues.
  • We may engage in a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, dissolution, reorganization, or similar transaction or proceeding that involves the transfer of the information described in this Policy.
We may also share aggregated or non-personally identifiable information with our partners or others. For example, we may tell a business using Paletly how many people viewed any product or category of products or even most popular sold brand from our website, most searched color etc.

Change in policy
We may change our policy from time to time, and if we do we’ll post any changes on this page. If you continue to use Paletly after those changes are in effect, you agree to the revised policy.
