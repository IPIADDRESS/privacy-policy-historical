Privacy Policy

PartyCheap.com is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers. Data Security is extremely important to us. When you purchase from us your personal information is encrypted using secure socket layer technology (SST) which prevents anyone from seeing it during transmission. In addition, personal information is stored securely with firewall protection to prevent unauthorized access. We also regularly update and maintain the security features of our computing software and anti-virus software. We do use a technology called "cookies" to improve your shopping experience. A "cookie" is a small piece of data that is sent to your browser from a website. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, letting you decide whether or not to accept it.

In general, anyone can visit PartyCheap.com and remain anonymous. We do keep track of the domains from which people visit for informational and analytical purposes. We do not sell or share your private information, but will disclose any information required by law to comply with legal proceedings or court orders. While we do not sell our information, we do reserve the right to contact you regarding orders you have placed, changes to our website, or with information regarding sales, discounts, or special promotions.

PartyCheap.com does not collect information from children or those under the age of eighteen. If it were discovered that a minor submitted personal information, the information would be deleted immediately.
