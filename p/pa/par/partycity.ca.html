Privacy and Security

It is important to us that you feel confident about the security of PartyCity.com and the privacy of the information you provide to us.

Questions regarding this statement should be directed to webmaster@partycity.com.

Surveys 
Now and then, PartyCity.com or a company selected by Party City may offer you the opportunity to participate in customer satisfaction, store improvement, and other business-related surveys. You may be asked to provide certain membership information to participate. All surveys are optional.

Cookies 
Put simply, a cookie is a small piece of information that Web sites use to remember information about user connections. PartyCity.com uses a randomly generated cookie which contains no personal information and allows you to navigate and use the search capabilities of PartyCity.com.

You can choose to disable cookies by deselecting that option under browser preferences. When you disable cookies, you can browse the PartyCity.com site. Disabling cookies can also interrupt audio and cause breaks in navigation.

Usage Tracking 
PartyCity.com's database tracks the traffic throughout our site. This information is used to compile overall statistics and is not recorded at an individual level. The statistics help us decide which products and services best serve our members and guests.

Aggregate Information 
PartyCity.com uses aggregate information from demographic surveys and traffic patterns to customize our site. State-of-the-art comparison technology allows us to use this information to make recommendations to individual members based on previous traffic patterns and purchases.

PartyCity.com has strict guidelines requiring all third parties to keep any shared personal information confidential.

Electronic Mailings 
At any time, a member of PartyCity.com may choose not to receive further electronic direct mail.

If we make changes to our privacy policy, we will post updates on our website. Further questions about our privacy policy can be sent to webmaster@partycity.com.
