PRIVACY

PartyLite is committed to protecting your privacy and using your personal information responsibly. Please read below for detailed information about our privacy policy.

Information Collection and Use

PartyLite together with our affiliated companies, is the sole owner of the information collected on our sites. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement. PartyLite may collect information from several different points on our web sites.

At certain points in interactive communication with us, you may be required to give us your contact information (such as name and email address). This information is used to contact you about the products, services, or opportunities offered on our site in which you have expressed interest.

Security

We take every precaution to protect your personal information.

Cookies and IP Addresses

A cookie is a piece of data stored on your hard drive containing information about you. PartyLite sites do not store cookie information from consumers at this time.

We may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our servers and to administer our web sites. Your IP address also may be used to gather broad demographic information and to recognize customer traffic patterns and site usage. This information aids us in merchandising and in developing the design and layout of the site. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

Children

We do not intend to collect any personal information (such as name, address, telephone number, age or email address) from persons under thirteen years of age. If a parent or guardian of a child who has provided us with such information would like the information deleted from our records, he or she should contact us at the email address or phone number listed at the bottom of this policy statement. We will make reasonable efforts to delete the child's information from our existing files.

Sharing Personal Information

We may share aggregated demographic information among our affiliated companies. This is general information, which is not linked to any personal information that can identify any individual person.

Surveys & Contests

Our sites may request information from you via surveys or contests. Participation in these surveys or contests is completely voluntary and you therefore have a choice whether or not to disclose this information. Information requested may include contact information (such as name and shipping address), and demographic information (such as zip code, age level). Contact information will be used to notify the winners and award prizes. Survey information will be used for purposes of monitoring or improving the use and satisfaction of this site and for our internal marketing research purposes.

Special Information

Occasionally we may send you, based on your preferences, information on our company, products, services, special deals, and other information. Out of respect for your privacy, we will present an option to not receive these types of communications.

Notification of Changes to the Policy

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post the changes so you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under circumstances, if any, we disclose it.

Contacting Us

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of our web sites, or your dealings with us, please email contactus@partylite.com.
