Privacy Policy

PartRequest.com is committed to protecting your privacy.

Here is what you should know about our Privacy Policy

In order to submit a part request to our network of subscribers, you are required to provide us with your email address, as well as some other personal information, including your name and the city, state and zip code where the part will be shipped. You are not required to provide us with a phone number or with a street address.

The information that is required from you is necessary in order to provide our subscribers with enough information to reply to you after you place a part request. Your personal information, including your email address, will not be shared with anyone outside the network of subscribers and each subscriber has agreed to the terms and conditions of our Subscriber Agreement which states, in part, that each subscriber is required to keep all information that they receive through our service confidential.

If you believe that your privacy has been violated as a result of using this web site, please contact our support team immediately.

This is what we do with the information we collect

PartRequest.com reserves the right to collect and store information and statistics about visitors to our site. For the most part, we use this information periodically to send surveys to past users to inquire as to their experience with our site. PartRequest.com does reserve the right to transmit some of the collected data to third party vendors. However, in no event will the data transmitted to third party vendors contain your email address or any personal or identifying information.

At PartRequest.com we strive to help you find the part you need at the price you want. We also value your use of our service and recognize that without you there wouldn't be a business to run.
