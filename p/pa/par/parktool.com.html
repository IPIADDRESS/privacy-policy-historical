Legal and Privacy Policies

Park Tool Shipping Policy

Items from the Shop section can only be shipped to USA addresses.

Orders are processed in 1-2 business days. Park Tool's normal business days are Tuesday through Friday. Expedited shipping options are available during the checkout process, but normal order processing time will still apply.

Orders containing taxable merchandise that are shipped within Minnesota are subject to 7.125% state sales tax. Shipping charges are also taxable.


Park Tool Refund Policy

All returns must be authorized in advance. Please call customer service at 651-777-6868 if you feel a return is necessary. Any refunds will be provided as a credit to the credit card used at the time of purchase.
