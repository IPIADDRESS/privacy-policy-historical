Cookies

PaulStamatiou.com uses cookies to help PaulStamatiou.com identify and track visitors, their usage of the PaulStamatiou.com website, and their website access preferences. PaulStamatiou.com visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using PaulStamatiou.com’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of PaulStamatiou.com’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
