Paul Fredrick is committed to respecting the privacy of our customers.

The information collected on our site is used to improve your shopping experience and communicate with you regarding Paul Fredrick products. The information collected includes your first and last name, address, email address, telephone number and credit card information. This information is used to process your order and contact you regarding your order, if necessary. Optional clothing and size preferences are also collected if provided by you. This information is used to contact you about Paul Fredrick products specific to your preferences.

We currently work with partners to help manage and optimize our Internet business and communications. We use the services of a marketing company to help us measure the effectiveness of our advertising and how visitors use our site. To do this, we use Web beacons and cookies provided by our marketing company on this site. The type of information we collect includes product codes and prices for merchandise that visitors purchase, the pages visited, etc. By supplementing our records, this information helps us learn things like what pages are most attractive to our visitors, which of our products most interest our customers, and what kinds of offers our customers like to see. Although our marketing company logs the information coming from our site on our behalf, we control how that data may and may not be used. If you do not wish to have your visit information used for this purpose, you can “opt-out” by clicking here.

We currently work with a third party advertising partner and its service providers. The information we disclose may include your transactional information with us. Our advertising partner makes the data we provide anonymous and does not use your personal information such as your name, address, email address, etc. This non-personal information is used in order to deliver advertising to you that is tailored to your interests. To choose not to have your transactional information given to, or used by, our third-party advertising partner in this way, you may “opt-out” by clicking here

We will not rent, sell or exchange your email address. A third party is used for email transmission. A blank image is used in individual html emails to track the open rates for email campaigns. If you would like to unsubscribe from our email newsletter at any time, click here.

We utilize cookie technology in order to enhance your shopping experience. Cookies are used to assign you a unique and encrypted id when you visit PaulFredrick.com.
