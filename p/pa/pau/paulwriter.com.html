Privacy Policy

Privacy

 

To become a member of Paul Writer we require your contact information.  But we promise not to sell or rent your email address or personal information to any third party without your express permission.  We may use your contact information to send you information deemed relevant to members such as a newsletter or special offers.  It’s very unlikely you’ll hear from us more often than once a week, and it is more likely to be once a month.

 

AntiSpam policy

 

Paul Writer recognizes the receipt, transmission or distribution of spam emails (unsolicited bulk emails) as a major concern and has taken reasonable measures, to minimize the transmission and effect of spam emails in its computing environment. Paul Writer reserves the right to reject and/or report any suspicious spam emails, to the authorities concerned, for necessary action.

 

Links to Third Party Sites

 

Please note that this website contains links to other web sites that are not controlled by Paul Writer. We would not be responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. Paul Writer’s privacy policy applies solely to information collected by this website.
