Privacy Policy

Information Collection

PANMORE does not collect names and other identifying information, unless the visitor elects to disclose such information by commenting or sending an email address to the site administrator.

Information Disclosure

  • PANMORE does not disclose identification, email addresses, and other identifying information, unless there is an agreement between PANMORE and the visitor to allow disclosure.
  • The visitor may disclose personal information on his/her own volition by posting comments or sending emails to the site administrator.

Cookies

  • In the European Union (EU) and the European Economic Area (EEA): To comply with the requirements of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), Panmore.com does not use personally identifiable information to serve ads. Instead, Google serves contextual non-personalized ads on this website. Also on this website, Google uses cookies for frequency capping and aggregated ad reporting, and to combat advertising fraud and abuse.
  • Outside the EU and the EEA: Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user’s prior visits to this website or other websites. Google uses cookies for frequency capping and aggregated ad reporting, and to combat advertising fraud and abuse.
  • Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to Panmore.com users based on their visit to Panmore.com and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings. (Alternatively, users can opt out of a third-party vendor’s use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.)
