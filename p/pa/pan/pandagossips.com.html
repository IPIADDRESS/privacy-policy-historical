Additionally, your Personal Information may be obtained and processed in the country and/or other countries, including the United States, where laws regarding processing of Personal Information may be less strict in comparison to the laws in your country. Sharing your information with Panda Gossips means that you are agreeing to such transfer.

For clarification, the phrase “Personal Information” in this Privacy Policy refers to the information that allows one to identify or contact you. For example, your name, address, telephone number, e-mail address, to name a few. In other words, any non-public information about you that is related to any of the foregoing information. In terms of “Anonymous Information” in this Privacy Policy, we mean any information that is not associated or linked to your Personal Information. Anonymous Information does not enable identification of or contact with individual persons.

A note in relation to children. Panda Gossips’ Website and Services are not directed to children in any means. Children are not suitable to use our Website as well as Services. We do not create content for our Website and Services that are designed to attract people under the age of 13. Protecting the privacy of children is important to Panda Gossips. Thus, we reject any Personal Information from people we are able to identify who are under 13 years of age. Any users who are under 13 years of age will be removed immediately. Meaning, that user’s Personal Information will be permanently removed from our databases to prevent them from using our Site and Services.

Anonymous Information

Panda Gossips collect the following types of Anonymous Information:

  1. 1. Information collected by our servers – Panda Gossips’ servers (note, it may be hosted via a third party service provider) are responsible for collecting information from our users. This may include users’ browser type, operating system, Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, to name a few.
  2. 2. Log files – Panda Gossips does gather particular information automatically and store it in our log files. Please note as specified in this Privacy Policy, we do not link this automatically-collected date to Personal Information.
  3. 3. Cookies – They are known as small pieces of information that a website sends to your computer’s hard drive while you are viewing the website. Panda Gossips may use both session Cookies (which expire once the browser is closed) and persistent Cookies (stays on your computer until you delete them) to provide our users with a more personal and interactive experience on our Website. It helps make Panda Gossips more useful to our users and tailors the experience to meet our users’ interests, needs and wants. If you do not wish to accept Cookies from our Services, then please instruct your browser, by changing its settings, to stop accepting Cookies or to prompt you before accepting a Cookie from the websites you wish to visit. For more information, please contact your browser’s technical information. Given that you do not wish to accept Cookies, unfortunately, you may not be able to use all portions of the Service or all functionality of the Service.
  4. 4. Collection of information by Advertisers – Panda Gossips may use third parties to serve advertisements on the Website. Certain third parties may automatically collect information about your visits to his and other websites, your IP address, your ISP, the browser you use to visit our Website (but not your name, address, e-mail address or telephone number). Information collected may be used, among other things, to deliver advertisements targeted to your specific interest. Additionally, to better understand the usage and visitation of our Website and the other sites tracked by these third parties. This policy does not apply to, and Panda Gossips is not responsible for, Cookies or pixel tags in third party advertisements. We encourage all our users to check the privacy policies of advertisers and/or ad services to learn more about their use of Cookies and possibly other technologies.

Such third party services use Cookies to collect information. This may include how often users visit the Website, what pages they visit, and what other sites they used prior to coming to our Website. We obtain this particular kind of information to help improve Panda Gossips’ Website and Services. Please note, we do not combine the information generated through the use of Google Analytics with your Personal Information. However, please be aware that Google Analytics plants a persistent Cookie on your browser to identify you as a unique user the next time you visit our Website. This Cookie cannot be used by anyone else, but Google. Google’s ability to use and share information collected by Google Analytics about your visits to our Website is restricted by their Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.
