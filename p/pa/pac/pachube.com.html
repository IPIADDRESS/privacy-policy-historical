Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to Connected Environments Ltd. This privacy statement provides information about the personal information that Connected Environments Ltd. collects, and the ways in which Connected Environments Ltd. uses that personal information.

Personal information collection

Connected Environments Ltd. may collect and use the following kinds of personal information:

  • Information about your use of this website (including your IP address);
  • Information that you provide for the purpose of registering with the website and subscribing to the website services (including your name and email address);
  • Information about transactions carried out over this website (including use of the API and use of the internal messaging system);
  • Your mailing address and PayPal account info (if subscribing to Pachube Professional)
  • Any other information that you send to Connected Environments Ltd.

Information we don't collect

When purchasing services from Connected Environments Ltd. we may ask for your credit/debit card details in order to process recurring payments for services, however we will never store these details on our systems, they will only ever be stored by our fully PCI compliant payment processing partners.

Using personal information

Connected Environments Ltd. may use your personal information to:

  • Administer this website;
  • Personalize the website for you;
  • Enable your access to and use of the website services;
  • Supply to you services that you purchase;
  • Collect payments from you;
  • Send you communications about the service;

Where Connected Environments Ltd. discloses your personal information to its agents or sub-contractors for these purposes, the agent or sub-contractor in question will be obligated to use that personal information in accordance with the terms of this privacy statement.

In addition to the disclosures reasonably necessary for the purposes identified elsewhere above, Connected Environments Ltd. may disclose your personal information to the extent that it is required to do so by law, in connection with any legal proceedings or prospective legal proceedings, and in order to establish, exercise or defend its legal rights.

Connected Environments Ltd. agree to abide by the communications preferences you set up in your profile and will not use your email address for unsolicited emails. We will never sell your email address to third parties.

Securing your data

Connected Environments Ltd. will take reasonable technical and organisational precautions to prevent the loss, misuse or alteration of your personal information.

Connected Environments Ltd. will store the personal information you provide on its secure servers.

Information relating to electronic transactions entered into via this website will be protected by encryption technology.

Data ownership

Your data belongs to you and you are free at any time to download an archive of it should you choose to discontinue using the services provided by Connected Environments Ltd. Additionally, if requested, all personal data linked to you will be removed from the service.

By using the services provided by Connected Environemnts Ltd. you agree to allow Connected Environments access to your data. We will at all times respect the privacy settings as set in your account

Cross-border data transfers

Information that Connected Environments Ltd. collects may be stored and processed in and transferred between any of the countries in which Connected Environments Ltd. operates to enable the use of the information in accordance with this privacy policy.

In addition, personal information that you deem non-private and that you submit for publication on the website will be published on the internet and may be available around the world.

You agree to such cross-border transfers of personal information.

Updating this statement

Connected Environments Ltd. may update this privacy policy by posting a new version on this website.

You should check http://www.pachube.com/privacy occasionally to ensure you are familiar with any changes.

Other websites

This website contains links to other websites.

Connected Environments Ltd. is not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of any third party.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or Connected Environment Ltd.'s treatment of your personal information, please contact us:
