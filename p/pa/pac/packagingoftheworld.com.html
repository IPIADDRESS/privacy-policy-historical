Packaging of the World uses cookies to help Packaging of the World identify and track visitors, their usage of the Packaging of the World website, and their website access preferences. Packaging of the World visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Packaging of the World’ websites, with the drawback that certain features of Packaging of the World’ websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
