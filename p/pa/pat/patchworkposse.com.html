Patchwork Posse is a blog founded in 2007. Our Online Quilt Group was formed in 2015.  We provide exclusive patterns, inspiration through sewing and quilting projects with a community to connect and share.

Our Mission

  • Promote sewing and quilting by providing inspiration, projects and instruction.
  • Explore and explain new techniques, tools, products, books and patterns.
  • Participate in an Online quilt group, community and blog featuring talented designers, product resources and exclusive patterns for all levels of sewers.
  • Learn. Create. Share.

Join our Community

Are you ready to start learning how to sew? Meet and share with others?  Be inspired?  We offer a variety of ways you can participate.

  • Online Quilt Group:  You'll find exclusive patterns, sew alongs, swaps, members from around the world and a private community where you can get to know others, share your own projects, and ask questions.  Join here!
  • Blog:  You'll find quilting hints, tips, and projects that will get you sewing along with inspiration and tutorials. Receive email news.
  • Facebook:  Are you connected there? Join us there for more inspiration.  Facebook Page.
