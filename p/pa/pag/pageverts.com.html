Cookies:

Our site uses cookies to save your login information so you don't need to enter it everytime you come to the site. You may be able to configure your browser to accept or reject all or some cookies, or notify you when a cookie is set. Each browser is different, so check the "Help" menu of your browser to learn how to change your cookie preferences. However, you must have cookies from PageVerts enabled in order to use most functions on the site. Please note that if we do decide to use third party advertisers they may use cookies. See the appropriate section in this privacy policy for more information.

This information allows an ad network to deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This privacy policy covers the use of cookies by PageVerts.com and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.
