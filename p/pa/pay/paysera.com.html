Privacy policy

General provisions

We respect the right to personal privacy and we will put in all reasonable efforts to ensure the security and confidentiality of personal data and other information on this website.

By visiting this website and/or using the information and/or the services contained therein, you accept and agree that you are familiar with and understand the privacy provisions. These privacy provisions do not apply to our other websites, products or services. We retain the right to change this privacy policy and therefore, please make sure that you are familiar with the newest version of the privacy provisions applicable every time you visit the website. This version of privacy provisions was updated on 01/08/2011.

Personal data management

Some of the data contained in this website received directly from you and/or public information files may be considered to be personal data and this data will be processed in accordance with the laws and other legislation of the Republic of Lithuania and these privacy provisions.

You can visit most our websites without providing any information about yourself, but if you want to use some of our services, we will ask you to provide us with your e-mail address, phone number as mandatory information and your name or company name and other information as optional information.

On this website we may use cookies. Cookies are small files sent to your web browser and stored on your computer's hard drive. Cookies are sent to your computer the first time you visit the website. Later on, the cookies are used to identify your computer and facilitate your access to this website or the information contained therein. Most web browsers accept cookies, but you can change your browser settings so that cookies are not be accepted. In this case, some functions may not work.

Like many websites, we monitor the hits of the site and collect the information on how many people have visited the website, what is the server domain name of the visitors' internet service provider, etc. This information is collected automatically upon accessing the website. It helps us understand how the visitors use the site and allows improving our services.

All of the above-mentioned information comprising your personal data will not be transferred to any third parties without your consent, unless applicable laws require otherwise or it is necessary to provide the services. We retain the right to hire other people, if necessary, to carry out certain functions on our behalf at our discretion. In order to carry out the functions assigned for such people, some of your information may be made available to them. However, such people cannot use this information for any purposes other than to perform their functions.

In this website we provide the opportunity for natural and legal persons to provide the information on their goods or services. The information is displayed "as is" and at the submitted amount without any changes to its content to all visitors of the website who have signed the agreement of services granting the right to access this information.

The manager of personal data in this website is EVP International, UAB, reg. No. 300060819, address Mėnulio g. 7, Vilnius, website www.evp.lt.

Access to personal data, correction and refusal

Personal data entity has the right to demand access to personal data stored by us, to learn how it is processed, and to request to submit such data to him/her. The data may be provided free of charge once per calendar year, but in other cases, the provision of data may be charged at the amount not exceeding the cost of data provision.

Personal data entity has the right to demand to correct all his/her personal data free of charge. Personal data entity has the right to refuse to have his/her personal data managed and to have it disclosed to third parties, except when it is necessary to provide the services.

Request for access, correction and refusal may be sent by e-mail to support [eta] webtopay.com. Please, state your name and username clearly in the request.

Third-party websites

We are not responsible for ensuring your privacy on the websites of third parties, even when third-party websites are available through links presented in this website. We recommend reading the privacy terms of each site that is not owned by us.

Ensuring Information Security

Our goal is to ensure the maximum level of security of all information received from you and other public files. In order to protect such information from unauthorized access, use, copying or disclosure, we use a variety of administrative, technical and physical security tools.

Final Provisions

These privacy provisions are subject to the laws of the Republic of Lithuania. All disputes regarding these privacy provisions shall be resolved through negotiation, and in the event of failure to reach an agreement – in the courts of the Republic of Lithuania.
