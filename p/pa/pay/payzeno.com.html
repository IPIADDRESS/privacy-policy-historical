Our Privacy Policy

The privacy and confidentiality of our members and users is upheld with the highest consideration. We will never disclose of any personal or identifying information to any third party in any form. A member's financial information is kept fully secured as to prevent any possibility of identity theft and the contact information is protected from any third parties seeking marketing information.

The only exceptions to the above statement are when action is necessary to: (1) conform to legal requirements or comply with the legal process, (2) protect and defend the rights or property of Payzeno Inc. and its owners, (3) enforce our terms of use, (4) act to protect the interests of our users or public welfare, or (5) tracking our affiliates with the usage of an internet browser cookie.

To better serve our users, we may reserve the right to collect aggregate data to improve the site's layout and performance. This will never be any individual data and will be used as a statistical study only. By accessing this page, a user has consented to our right for such aggregate information collection.
