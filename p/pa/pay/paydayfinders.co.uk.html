Privacy Policy

Registration

PayDayFinders.co.uk only collects personally identifiable information ("PII") that is volunteered during the registration process or in response to specific information requests. Examples of PII that may be requested include: first and last name, address, email address, and telephone number. Anybody completing and submitting any of the forms (including our newsletter) on the PayDayFinders.co.uk domain is considered a "registrant." In addition, PayDayFinders.co.uk may collect your IP address and information through use of electronic "cookies."

By using this Site, you agree that you will be registered with PayDayFinders.co.uk, and that you accept this Privacy Policy. You will not be registered merely by visiting the Site; you will only be registered if you affirmatively click on one of the Site's options to directly match you with a loan or provide other services. Also, by providing us with your personal information on our Site or in the course of using our services, you consent to the transfer of your Personal Data and Sensitive Personal Data, and any other data we acquire about you from third-party service providers in the process of evaluating you for a loan, to companies outside the EU, including but not limited to, the Network Sites and other consumer credit lenders and their affiliates. 

When you register with PayDayFinders.co.uk you automatically consent to registration in all the other online properties in the PayDayFinders.co.uk network (collectively, the "Network Sites"). Accordingly, PayDayFinders.co.uk may share registrant PII with the Network Sites.

Use and Sharing of PII

PayDayFinders.co.uk reserves the right to use registrant PII: (1) for the specific purpose for which such information was provided (including matching registrants with a cash advance loan company or companies); (2) as disclosed at the time such information is provided; and (3) as disclosed in this Policy. However, please note that PayDayFinders.co.uk may change this Policy at any time and therefore PayDayFinders.co.uk may in the future use registrant PII for additional purposes not currently included in this Policy.

The data we collect during registration with us or during the application process may be shared/sold to trusted third parties who may also contact you about other products and services. These third parties may contact you via telephone, email and/or SMS. Any third party receiving your data will always give you the option to unsubscribe from receiving information in respect of such offers and/or services/products. Third parties with whom we work take all appropriate and reasonable steps to ensure that your data is protected. Email and Unsubscribe Policy

PayDayFinders.co.uk uses Individual Information to provide promotional offers by email to individuals. Promotional offers include third party marketing messages as well as email advertising from partners and affiliates of PayDayFinders.co.uk. PayDayFinders.co.uk may maintain separate email lists for different purposes.  If email recipients wish to end their email subscription from a particular list and/or to unsubscribe from all PayDayFinders.co.uk's email list, they need to follow the instructions at the end of each email message to unsubscribe.

PayDayFinders.co.uk's unsubscribe process impacts only the future delivery of electronic mailings disseminated by PayDayFinders.co.uk's on its own behalf.  You may still receive electronic mailings sent on behalf of Third Parties and your personal information may still be shared with Third Parties for use in offline marketing and data appends, including email appends.

You should also note that unsubscribing from PayDayFinders.co.uk's electronic mailings will not automatically unsubscribe the subscriber's information from any Third Party business associates and licensees of the data.  Since Third Party associates and licensee partners maintain separate databases from PayDayFinders.co.uk, and you will need to unsubscribe from each source individually, if desired.  This allows you the freedom to pick and choose which subscriptions to maintain and which to discontinue.

In certain commercial email messages sent by PayDayFinders.co.uk, an Advertiser's name will appear in the "From:" line but hitting the "Reply" button will cause a reply email to be sent to PayDayFinders.co.uk.  The "Subject:" line of Company email messages will usually contain a line provided from the Advertiser to PayDayFinders.co.uk or from PayDayFinders.co.uk in its own promotions.

Solicited Email

PayDayFinders.co.uk only sends email to individuals who have agreed on the Websites to receive email from PayDayFinders.co.uk or to individuals who have agreed on a Third Party website to receive email from Third Parties.  As such, PayDayFinders.co.uk does not send unsolicited email messages.  As a result, The United Kingdom's statutes requiring certain formatting for unsolicited email may not be applicable to Company's email messages.
