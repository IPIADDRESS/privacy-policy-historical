PDF-Archive.com Privacy policy

PDF-Archive.com respects your privacy. Please read the following to learn more about our policies.


Information we collect
PDF-Archive.com collects and stores the personal information (such as your email address) that you provide when submitting a file.
Our log files automatically record certain non-personal information from your browser including your IP address, the pages you access, and the referring URL. When you submit content, we may also store this information in a database used only by us.
PDF-Archive.com uses cookies to identify your browser. PDF-Archive.com cookies do not collect personal information.


How we use this information
PDF-Archive.com will not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We will only share your information if required by law, to apply our Terms of Service, to protect our rights and property, or if PDF-Archive.com is acquired by a third party.
We will not use your personal information to send commercial or marketing messages without your prior consent. We may use your information in order to contact you regarding PDF-Archive.com functionality and other customer service purposes.
Be aware that any personal information that you voluntarily disclose via a content submission become available to the public.
