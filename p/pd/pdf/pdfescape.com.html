Information Collection

When visiting Red Software Websites, certain information about you and your devices can sometimes be collected. This section explains what information Red Software collects, and how it is used.

Information Collected

What information do we collect?

We collect and use the following information to provide, improve, protect, and promote Red Software Websites and our related services (e.g. PDFescape Desktop):

  • Account Information – We may collect, and associate with an account, information including your name, email address, phone number, payment info, and billing address.
  • Cookies – We use cookies and similar technologies to do things like log you into your account on a Red Software Website, remember your settings, or help us test out promotions and improvements.
  • Customer Data – We may store, process, and transmit your data (including metadata, files, settings, etc.). When using the PDFescape Website, you can manage your files in your account; if your file is not associated with a PDFescape account, it is typically removed from our servers after forty-eight (48) hours of inactivity.
  • Usage – We may collect information from and about the devices you use to access Red Software Websites. This information includes IP addresses, the type of browser you are using, the web pages visited on Red Software Websites, the web page which referred you to a Red Software Website, and identifiers associated with your devices (which may include their location depending upon your device settings).

Information Recipients

Who has access to the information collected?

We may share information as discussed below, but we won't sell it to advertisers or other third-parties.

  • Others Working for Red Software – We use certain trusted third parties to help us provide, improve, protect, and promote Red Software Websites and our related services. These third parties will access your information only to perform tasks on our behalf.
  • Other Applications – We offer tools on Red Software Websites allowing Account Information and Customer Data to be shared with third party applications (e.g. Google Drive). If using such an application, please remember that their use of your information will be governed by their policies and terms.
  • Other Visitors – We offer tools on Red Software Websites allowing Customer Data to be published or shared. Data shared using these tools, obviously, will be available to other visitors with valid credentials (e.g. the URL).
  • Legal Authorities – We may disclose your information to third parties if we determine that such disclosure is reasonably necessary to (a) comply with the law; (b) protect any person from death or serious bodily injury; (c) prevent fraud or abuse of a Red Software Website or our users; or (d) protect our property rights.

Information Retention

How long is this information retained?

  • Account Information and Customer Data may be retained by us for as long as we need it to provide you services, or as required by law.
  • Cookies are stored by your browser and can be cleared using your browser's preferences at any time.
  • Usage is typically archived for a minimum of 1 year to help improve and secure our Red Software Websites.
