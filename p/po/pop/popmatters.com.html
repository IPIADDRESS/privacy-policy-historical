RATINGS KEY

10 - The Best of the Best. Transcendent, essentially perfect, important.
9 - Very Nearly Perfect. A superlative example of music of any form, a pinnacle of an artist's achievement.
8 - Excellent. Among the best work of the given artist, or among the best in the given genre.
7 - Damn Good. Stands out and is definitely worth purchasing, though it has some flaws.
6 - Good. Enjoyable and should be of interest to fans of the artist or genre, but has specific weaknesses.
5 - Average. Decent, but will probably appeal to fans of the artist the most.
4 - Unexceptional. Truly a mixed bag, with some highlights, but not enough to distinguish itself from similar material.
3 - Weak. Bland, with little to distinguish it or recommend it, even to fans of their particular artist or genre.
2 - Seriously Flawed. Contains major flaws which significantly limit appreciation.
1- Terrible. So poorly written / performed / produced that it is an embarrassment to the genre and the artist.
