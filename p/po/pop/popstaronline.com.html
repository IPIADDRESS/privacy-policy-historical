Keeping Your Information Secure

Nothing is more important to Popstar! than protecting the private information of visitors to our Web site, www.PopstarOnline.com (the “Web Site”). Below you will find information about our commitment to ensuring the privacy of you and/or your children’s personally identifiable information, such as your name, address, telephone number, e-mail address and credit card information. We have adopted this Web Site privacy policy (“Privacy Policy”) to explain what information may be collected on the Web Site and how such information may be used and/or shared with others. This Privacy Policy applies solely to information collected through the Web Site and not through any other sources. Please review this Privacy Policy each time you visit the Web Site to ensure that you have read and agree with the current policy, as it may be amended without notice.

Gathering and Use of Information

Active Collection of Information

PopstarOnline.com will only collect personally identifiable information which is provided to us, by you, on a voluntary basis such as your name, date of birth, email address, city/state/zip and gender when you register with the Web Site. Visitors to the Web Site may also voluntarily supply certain personally identifiable information in connection with e-commerce transactions, including address, telephone number, and credit card information. This information will not be re-used, sold or shared in any manner by PopstarOnline.com. PopstarOnline.com will never willfully disclose any personally identifiable information about our online audience to any third party without first receiving the user’s permission, except to the extent that PopstarOnline.com is compelled to provide such information by law. In addition, personally identifiable information is voluntarily provided in connection with community postings (i.e., blog comments). Please remember that any community postings become public information. Use caution when posting. Users under the age of 18 should be especially careful not to provide any personally identifying information while using the blog.
