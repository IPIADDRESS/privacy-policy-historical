Although Poptent takes what we believe are commercially reasonable security precautions, Poptent does not guaranty security. You agree to do what You reasonably can to help promote security (e.g., never reveal your password or make it accessible to others).

Cookies are uniquely assigned to You and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to You.

About

Poptent.net is our social networking site dedicated exclusively to the videographer community. This proprietary portal gives Brands access to 41,000+ creators and videographers, all anxious to work on your next video project.

These filmmakers, directors, animators, and actors are all looking to gain recognition, build their demo reel, and hopefully earn some money while they’re at it.
