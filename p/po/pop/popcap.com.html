The PopCap Promise


No Spam. No Spy-ware. Ever. That's the PopCap Promise.

PopCap Games is committed to making our website a fun place to visit. And if you're worried about your privacy — or the security of our site — it's hard to have a good time! That's why we work hard to protect your rights.

Please read the following privacy policy to understand how we use any information we may collect from you. We reserve the right to change this policy from time to time, so be sure to check back periodically. (Our newsletter is a great way to stay posted, too.)

What information about me will you collect and how will you use it?


Wherever possible we collect the minimal amount of information from you that is needed. If you subscribe to our newsletter or our email list for special offers from us, we simply collect your email address so we know where to send it.

If you buy a game from us, we collect billing information so we can charge you. We also ask that for your email address to let you know about product updates and more. (For example, if one of our new games has a problem that we missed, we fix it right away and will let you know about it!)

On a more general level, we do collect information that we use in aggregate to get a sense of how many people have visited our site, which pages are most popular, and things of that nature. Take a look at our complete Privacy Policy and Terms of Use (below) to get really specific information about how this works...

If I give you my email address, what exactly will you do with it?


Friends don't send friends Spam. If you're anything like us, your inbox is probably plenty full as it is. Respecting that, we'll only send you email when we have something important to tell you.

Plus, you'll have to explicitly let us know if you'd like to receive our newsletter, The Fizz. You might also want to sign up for special offers and announcements so you can get exclusive discounts and hear about new games. In every email we send, there will be clear instructions about how to unsubscribe, and you're free to do so at any time!

Other than that, you'll probably get confirmation emails from time to time, like when you buy a game or first sign up for the newsletter.

Do you ever sell or give my email address to third parties?


No way! We make our money selling games, not email addresses. Any information we collect is for our own use as described above and any exceptions will be clearly identified as such in advance. If we ever use a third party to say, help us send out our newsletter, they're held to strict privacy standards and are working on our behalf.

However, our games are widely available through several partners we have. Be sure you understand their privacy policies before sharing any personal information with them.

Is it safe to download your games?


Absolutely! And it's free and easy!

When you download our free trial games or buy a deluxe download, you'll be asked to "Save" or "Run" the game. It only takes a minute, and we'll walk you through each step. Then you can start playing right away...without being connected to the Internet!

What about spyware?


Spyware is sneaky and deceptive, and we will never install any spyware software on your computer. We sell games, pure and simple...and we're not going to snoop into your privacy. The only information we know about you is information you offer us yourself and generalized, aggregate information about how all our visitors use our website and games.

Do you ever store "cookies"?


Yes we do, to give you the best web experience possible. Cookies are convenient ways for the website to remember you, which means we can personalize your experience on our website. For example, cookies help us keep track of what you've added to your shopping cart when you're ready to buy our games. They're really quite handy, and are a time-saver for you!

What should I do if I think you're violating this policy?


Tell us about it! If you ever feel that we are not following this policy, please send us an email and explain how or why you feel we are violating it. Here's our mailing address, too, if you'd rather send snail mail:
    PopCap Games
    2401 4th Ave, Suite 810
    Seattle, WA 98121
That about does it. Give a shout if you have any questions or concerns. We'd love to hear from you!
