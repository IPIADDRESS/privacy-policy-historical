Quote of the Day

December 15, 2008

"Speaking of filling Senate vacancies, it's no secret Reps. Steve Rothman of Fair Lawn and Frank Pallone of Long Branch see themselves as potential rivals the next time a seat opens up in the U.S. Senate.

One of the factors in Corzine's selection of Menendez was that he already had a big bankroll to run a statewide campaign. And as of Nov. 24, Rothman was sitting on a campaign bankroll of nearly $1.9 million, while Pallone had nearly $3.5 million.

Both Rothman and Pallone have had a chance to get Corzine before committees on which they serve in Congress in the past month.

When Rothman got his five minutes to question Corzine during an appearance before the House Appropriations Committee on Thursday, he showed what a hard-nosed questioner he could be.

He opened by telling the committee Corzine 'is one of the hardest-working, smartest people I know and he's doing a great job for New Jersey.'" -- Herb Jackson, in his Capital Games column.

-The Record, 12/15/08
