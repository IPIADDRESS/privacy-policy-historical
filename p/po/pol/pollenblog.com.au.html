Collection Of Personal Information

We do not collect personal information on the sly. We only collect personal information that is necessary for the functioning of our site, and only with your consent. We only use lawful and fair means to do so, and not in an unreasonably intrusive way.

Data Security

Securing your personal information is very important to us. iconpr.com.au will take reasonable steps to protect the personal information we hold from misuse and loss and from unauthorised access, modification or disclosure. iconpr.com.au will take reasonable steps to destroy or permanently de-identify personal information if it is no longer needed for any purpose for which the information may be used or disclosed.
