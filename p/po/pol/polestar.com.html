Privacy Policy

Polestar recognizes and respects your right to privacy while viewing information on the Internet. We are committed to protecting your personal privacy online.

We do not collect personal information about individuals visiting our website unless you specifically and knowingly provide this information to us via email or other communications with us. Polestar does not trade, sell, or otherwise provide personal information to any third party. From time to time, we may collect aggregate statistics about numbers of visitors to our site and/or browser information and other generic data in order to improve the performance of our web site. This generic information about visits to our web site is logged using an industry-standard access log protocol. This access log information is not collect in a manner that can be linked to any personal contact information that you provide.
