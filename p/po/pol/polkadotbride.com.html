Products sent for consideration are not returned unless otherwise discussed.

Polka Dot Bride reserves the right to determine the blog content and does not participate in pay for post.

For more information on how to get involved with Polka Dot Bride please contact us.

Comment Policy

Polka Dot Bride welcomes your comments and feedback! However, to keep Polka Dot Bride a friendly place to be, there are some guidelines.

Polka Dot Bride comments are not moderated. Polka Dot Bride does use spam filters so your message may be caught. The filters are checked frequently but if your comment has not shown up please contact Ms Polka

Polka Dot Bride encourages healthy debate and discussion and do not censor any comments. However, comments that contain:

  • Blatant Spam
  • Attacks on others
  • Inflammatory statements
  • Vulgar or obscene language
  • Abuse
  • Off topic commentary

will not be tolerated on the blog and may be removed as Polka Dot Bride deems necessary.
