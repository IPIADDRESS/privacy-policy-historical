Privacy Policy

Politics4All takes your right to privacy seriously, and wants you to feel comfortable with using our site, contributing content, and interacting with other members. This policy does not apply to other entities that are not owned or controlled by Politics4All, nor does it apply to persons that are not employees or agents of Politics4All. Please take time to read our Terms of Use.

By using Politics4All, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Information Collected

We only collect personal information that is relevant to the purpose of our Website, which is to enable users to discover and share information with one another. We do not process this information in a way that is incompatible with this objective. We collect the following types of information from our users: registration, personal information, location, photos, traffic data, IP address, cookie information, and any other information that allows us to provide you with a customized and efficient experience.

Information may be used to customize and improve your user experience on this site. In the event you choose to use links displayed on this Website to visit other Websites, you are advised to read the privacy policies published on those sites.

Email. Politics4All is very concerned about your privacy and we will never provide your email address to a third party without your explicit permission.
