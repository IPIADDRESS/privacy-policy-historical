Privacy Policy

Your personal information
We will get personal information from you when you book tickets, register with us or subscribe to one of our services online. Personal information includes your name, address, email address, telephone number, country of residence and credit or debit card details. We need this information to process your booking and to contact you if there any problems with your ticket purchase.

How we use your personal information
Personal information will be held on our system and used to process your bookings, for mailing list subscriptions and to provide you with any other services or information you have requested. In order to do this, we may need to share your personal information, including card details, with our secure service providers and agents. Credit or debit card details will only be used for the purpose of handling an individual transaction.

We will not sell your personal information to others and we will only use your information in accordance with your decisions.
