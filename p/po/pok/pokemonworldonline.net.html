Our Privacy Policies

Pokemon World Online and its employees will never ask you for your password or account information.

Your E-mail address used upon registration will not be shared with the public, nor will it be given to third party companies. E-mail security is very important, and as such it will remain strictly within our records for your own use.

You are not required to provide specific details about yourself including your name, address, and other personal aspects of your life.

Pokemon World Online refuses to sell or otherwise give personal information about its players to third parties.

Pokemon World Online will not gather or store personal information about its players (unless the information is provided willingly, such as paypal information for Membership donations).am
