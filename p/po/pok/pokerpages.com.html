Once you register with PokerPages and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

    • We have your consent to share the information;

(Unless we tell you differently, these companies do not have any right to use the personally identifiable information we provide to them beyond what is necessary to assist us.);

Cookies
  • PokerPages may set and access PokerPages cookies on your computer.

Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to PokerPages' cookies.

You can change nearly all your account information except your Username (see delete account below to change your username).
