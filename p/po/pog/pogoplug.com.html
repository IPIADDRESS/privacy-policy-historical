INFORMATION ABOUT YOU

We collect two basic types of information with our Product and Service, namely personal information and aggregate information. Personal information is information that is directly associated with a specific person or entity such as a user's e-mail address, usage information and user-chosen ID and password. Our definition of "personal information" may include any content that you opt to access via the Product and Service.

Our definition of personal information does not include "aggregate" information. Aggregate information is data we collect about a group or category of services or users from which individual user identities have been removed. In other words, information on how you use our Product and Service may be collected and combined with information about how others use the Product and Service, but no personal information will be included in the resulting data. Aggregate data helps us understand trends in our users' needs so that we can better consider new features or otherwise tailor our Product and Service. We may share aggregate information about our users with third parties for various purposes, including to help us better understand and improve our Product and Service, and for advertising and marketing purposes.

Those are the types of personal and other information we collect. You should refer to the rest of this Policy to see how we use, disclose and protect this information: Information You Give Us.
