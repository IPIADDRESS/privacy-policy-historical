Internet Privacy Policy

Porsche recognizes the importance of protecting the personal privacy of its customers, potential customers and website visitors. This internet privacy policy (“Privacy Policy”) describes the information that may be collected through our websites, the choices you can make about our use of your personal information and how we protect your personal information.

Scope and Terms Used

This Privacy Policy applies only to websites operated by or on behalf of Porsche Cars North America, Inc. and only to those websites which provide a direct link to this Privacy Policy (the “Sites”). Unless otherwise indicated, all references to “Porsche,” “we” or “our” in this Privacy Policy shall mean Porsche Cars North America, Inc. This Privacy Policy does not apply to any offline data collection or practices of Porsche Cars North America, Inc. or any other Porsche companies.

Some of the Sites may contain links to the websites of other Porsche companies or to the websites of third parties. Porsche is not responsible and has no liability for the privacy policies or practices of those websites. We recommend that you review the privacy policies of each website you visit.

The Sites are designed for consumers located exclusively in the United States of America. If you are located outside the United States, please return to www.porsche.com and use the drop down box to find the website for users in your location, but please do not use the Sites, as the Sites’ personal information practices and the terms of this Privacy Policy may or may not comply with foreign law.

As used in this Privacy Policy, “personal information” means information that may be used to identify a specific individual, or that is about an identifiable individual, such as name, e-mail address, phone number or credit card number. “Aggregate information” refers to combined demographic information which has been derived from personal information but which cannot be used to identify any individual or that is not about an identifiable individual.

Information We Collect and How We Use It

You can visit the Sites and learn about our products and programs without providing personal information. However, to participate in our programs or receive newsletters, you will need to provide personal information. Porsche does not collect any personal information on the Sites unless you provide it voluntarily.

In addition to our General Uses listed below, we use the personal information you provide only for purposes consistent with the reason you provide it, such as:

Registration / Porsche Travel Club and Other Programs / Webspecials: At your option, you may register as a Porsche Site user to allow for easy registration for programs through the Porsche Travel Club or our driving schools, access to Webspecials offered only to online users, or other programs offered on the Sites. As part of registration, we collect your name, e-mail address, mailing address and other contact information. We may ask for other information in connection with specific programs (e.g. travel dates for the Travel Club), or more generally about your vehicles or interests in order to better customize your website experience or our offers to you. We indicate at the time of collection which information is mandatory and which information is optional to provide.

Information Requests: We may ask you to provide personal information in order to allow us to respond to your requests for information or other requests made over the Sites.

Purchases of Products and Services: Where the Sites allow you to purchase products or services online, we collect your credit card or other payment information in addition to your name, address and other identifying information. We may also verify your eligibility for certain programs, such as requesting your drivers’ license number for driving school programs.

Newsletter: On the newsletter section of the Sites, you may provide us with your e-mail address in order to subscribe for the Porsche newsletter, which contains the latest information on our products, events and our other offerings.

Contests and Surveys: In addition to your name, address and other identifying information, we may ask you to provide other information about your household and interests if you choose to participate in Porsche’s contests or surveys. You can always choose not to participate.

Employment Opportunities: Where the Sites allow the option of submitting your resumé or other indications of interest for employment at Porsche, we collect personal information such as name, contact information, job history and other background.

Non-personal Information: We collect non-personal information such as your Internet browser type, operating system, and the IP address of the website from which you are linked to the Sites. We use this information to help us tailor our Sites in a manner to better match your computer settings, interests and preferences.

Porsche Dealers and Other Sources: To the extent permitted by law, we may augment the information we have collected from you on the Sites with data obtained from other sources, including information you have provided to Porsche dealers, our affiliates or Porsche clubs, or information you have provided to us by phone or by mail. We may also obtain other information about you such as change of address, contact information or demographic information from commercially or publicly available sources.

General Uses: We will use your personal information as specifically described in the Site to which you submit it, in accordance with any published legal terms for such Site. We may also use your personal information internally to customize your experience with our company and our Sites, to communicate with you about our products or services, to help us develop new products and services, to verify your identity, to collect debts owed to us, or to investigate or settle inquiries or disputes, and for other legally required or permissible purposes.

What Are Cookies and How Does Porsche Use Them?

Cookies are small text files stored by your browser that allow us to learn more information about your visit or use of the Sites. We may use cookies to remember your personal information, including your registration information, and to deliver personalized and relevant information specific to your needs. We may also use cookies to track patterns on the Sites to help us improve visitor or customer experience.

Most browsers automatically accept cookies. To learn more about cookies, including how to refuse cookies on your computer, click here: http://www.microsoft.com/info/cookies.htm.

Google Analytics

The Sites use Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. (“Google”). Google Analytics uses cookies to help the Sites analyze how users use the Sites. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the Sites (including your anonymized IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States . Google will use this information for the purpose of evaluating your use of the Sites, compiling reports on website activity for website operators and providing other services relating to website activity and internet usage. Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Google's behalf. Google will not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google.  You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser, however please note that if you do this you may not be able to use the full functionality of the Sites.  By using the Sites, you consent to the processing of data about you by Google in the manner and for the purposes set out above. To provide visitors more choice on how their data is collected by Google Analytics, Google developed the Opt-out Browser Add-on: http://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=en. By installing this Add-on, no information is being sent to Google Analytics.

Your Choices In How We Use Your Personal Information

While Porsche may contact you with offers and information concerning Porsche or its products, services or promotions, you always have the choice not to receive such items. You may contact us at 1-800-PORSCHE to communicate your marketing preferences and request to be removed from marketing lists, or you may write to us at the address provided below under “Contact Us.” You may also request that your personal information not be shared with independent Porsche dealerships as part of related marketing and customer care programs (although you should be aware that these dealerships also collect personal data directly from you).

In addition, the Sites allow you to communicate your marketing preferences as follows (or as otherwise specifically indicated on the Sites):

  • At the time of registering as a Porsche Site user, you will be given a choice whether to receive promotional e-mails from Porsche. Each promotional e-mail you receive from us will provide you a specific opt-out mechanism to allow you to unsubscribe from receipt of these promotional e-mails.
  • By accessing your profile under “Porsche Login” on the Site, you may also update your personal information in our database or change your marketing preferences.
  • Whether or not registered as a Porsche Site user, users who subscribe to the Porsche Newsletter may unsubscribe from the newsletter by following the link provided in the newsletter, by calling 1-800-PORSCHE or by updating their preferences on the Newsletter section of the Sites.

You may contact us at 1-800-PORSCHE with any questions or concerns you may have about our privacy practices, including requests to update or correct your personal information. Even if you choose not to receive promotional e-mails or mailings from Porsche, we may continue to use your personal information in order to contact you in connection with product recalls or other transactional purposes.

Our Sharing of Your Information

Porsche does not sell or rent your personal information collected on the Sites to non-affiliated third parties for those parties’ promotional purposes, except that as part of our customer care and marketing efforts, we may provide your information to independent, authorized Porsche dealers. In addition, Porsche Cars North America, Inc. may share your personal information submitted at the Sites with other members of our affiliated group of companies, who may use that information for promotional or other purposes. If you wish to opt out of any of the data sharing practices described in this paragraph, please contact
1-800-PORSCHE or write to us as shown below under “Contact Us.”

Other than as described in the paragraph above, Porsche will not share your personal information collected on the Sites with third parties without your permission, except as necessary or appropriate to (I) process or fulfill your order, (II) respond to your requests or answer your inquiries, (III) enter you in contests, sweepstakes or other promotional activities upon your request, (IV) comply with any applicable law, court order or other judicial process, or (V) enforce or apply agreements with you, or protect the rights, property or safety of Porsche or third parties, or as otherwise required or permitted by law. In addition, in the event of a corporate transaction such as a sale, merger or reorganization involving Porsche or its parent companies, personal information may be disclosed or transferred to the acquiring company(ies). Porsche may collect and share aggregate website statistical information (such as sales, traffic patterns or related information) with third parties. No such shared statistics will contain any personally identifying user information.

Porsche may occasionally hire contractors to provide certain services on its behalf, including information processing and storage or the management of contests, sweepstakes and surveys. These contractors are required to maintain the confidentiality of information they receive from Porsche and are restricted from using the information for any purposes other than those authorized by Porsche.

How We Protect Your Information

When you submit sensitive information via our Sites, such as credit card information, that information is subject secure sockets layer (SSL) encryption technology. However, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed as 100% secure. As a result, while we strive to protect your personal information during transmission, we cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us or receive from us.

Submissions

Suggestions, materials or other intellectual property sent or transmitted to Porsche via our Sites are governed by the separate terms applicable to such materials identified at the time of submission (if any). If no such terms are identified, all such items shall be deemed to be non-
confidential, and Porsche shall have no obligation of any kind with respect to such items and shall be free to use and distribute them to others without limitation, including, but not limited to developing and marketing products incorporating them.

Children’s Privacy

Our Sites are not intended for use by children under 13. We ask that children under the age of 13 do not use our Sites and do not submit any personal information to us.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or comments about this Privacy Policy or your personal information, you may contact us at 1-800-PORSCHE, by sending an e-mail to privacy@porsche.com, or by mail to Porsche Cars North America, Inc., 980 Hammond Drive, Suite 1000, Atlanta, Georgia 30328, Attn: Privacy/Legal Department.

Your Acceptance of Our Privacy Policy

By using our Sites, you signify your acceptance of this Privacy Policy, and you agree to adhere to the terms and conditions posted on the Sites under “Legal notice.” If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not submit any personal information to us through our Sites.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

Porsche reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time, so you should review it frequently. If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will post such changes here and/or on our Sites.
