Return Policy

Sealed packages:
Because we cannot accept returns of products once they have been opened, before opening each product please check its packaging and label carefully to be sure you received the correct product and version.
If the original packaging and seal is still intact, you may return the product to us within 30 days from purchase for merchandise credit (excluding shipping and handling charges), subject to a 15% restocking fee. Return shipping to us is at your own expense.

Defective Returns:
If your product is not functioning properly, please email our Customer Support Department at support@pornstar.com as soon as possible (but not later than 30 days from purchase).
In such case, we will replace the product with an even exchange of the same item, subject to in-stock availability. No toy can be accepted for a return if it has been used. If any item is returned and our inspectors deem that it has been used it will be returned to you at your expense. We stress that you should inspect any item thoroughly before you use the item.
Replacement items ship the same day the defective item is received and inspected by our Returns Department. All products returned to us must be accompanied by a valid Return Authorization (RA) number issued by us within 30 days of your original invoice date. After 30 days from purchase we can no longer issue an RA. RA's expire in 15 days after they are issued and cannot be extended or re-issued. All returned products must be 100% complete in original packaging. A valid RA number must be written clearly and conspicuously on the outside of the packaging. Incomplete or unauthorized returns will be refused.
