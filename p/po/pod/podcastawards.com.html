Podcast Connect Privacy Policy

In regards to any information you may share with us during the collection of data for the People's Choice Podcast Awards we will not share any personal data with any third party. While we collect your name and e-mail during the nomination process this is for internal validation and we will never sell or share your information with any other company.


Podcast Connect may contact you during the nominations and voting phase only to validate a nomination or vote. Those involved in the nomination reviews will not have access to any of your personal information with the exception of your comments during the nomination phase.


We respect each and every participants privacy and will safeguard your e-mail and any other contact information as it were our own aka we don't d anything evil with the information.
