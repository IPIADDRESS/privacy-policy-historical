Your Privacy is not for sale & will NEVER be for sale(Back to Top)

Simply put, we do not and will not sell or rent your personal information to anyone, for any reason, at any time. Pocodot uses and discloses your personal information only as follows:

to analyze site usage and improve the Service;

to deliver to you any administrative notices, money alerts and communications relevant to your use of the Service;

to fulfill your requests for certain products and services;

for market research, project planning, troubleshooting problems, detecting and protecting against error, fraud or other criminal activity;

to third-party contractors that provide services to Pocodot and are bound by these same privacy restrictions;

to enforce Pocodot's Terms of Use; and

as otherwise set forth in this Privacy and Security Policy.
