What PII We Collect

We collect PII that you specifically and voluntarily provide on the Site or by email to us. The Site includes both public areas, open to all visitors, and private areas that are open only to those visitors who choose to register. In addition, visitors can sign up on the Site to request email newsletters and other information from us. We collect PII from you when you:

  • Make a purchase,
  • Create an account or register at our Site,
  • Contact a phone sales representative,
  • Are contacted by a phone sales representative,
  • Subscribe to our promotional email newsletter(s),
  • Request product information or samples,
  • Participate in a survey,
  • Or otherwise provide PII on our Site or to our agent or representative.

How We Use the PII We Collect

The following are examples of ways we may use the PII we collect about you:

  • Fulfill requests for products, services and/or information
  • Process payment for purchases or other services
  • Provide customer service
  • Create and manage your account
  • Send samples, premiums, products and information
  • Send promotional email newsletter(s) for which you have subscribed

  • Analyze the use of our products, services, and Site
  • Understand how you arrived at our Site
  • Measure and improve the effectiveness of our Site and our marketing endeavors

When you make a purchase on our Site we collect credit card information including card number, card type, cardholder name and billing address, and expiration date, in order to bill you for purchases made through the Site.

We may disclose PII to third party providers, suppliers, consultants, professional advisors and contractors who perform services on our behalf, but only to the extent necessary for them to perform such services. Marketing Communications:

Email Opt-Out / Unsubscribe
We only send you promotional mobile or email messages if you explicitly ask to receive these. (You opt in). You can stop receiving promotional mobile and email messages by following the instructions provided in any such message.

Postal Mail

Where permitted by law, we may mail you unsolicited offers or product information that we believe may be of interest to you.

Cookies

A cookie is a small file placed on your computer when you visit a site that can be understood by the site that issued the cookie. We use cookies to monitor how our Site is used and to help personalize your online experience. For example, when you register on our Site, we may store a unique code in a cookie on your computer. The next time you come back to the Site from that computer, our servers use the cookie to recognize you. We can then use the information you provided when you registered combined with your activities on our Site to provide you advertising and offers of interest.

You can accept or decline cookies. Most browsers automatically accept cookies. To learn more about cookies, including how to refuse cookies on your computer, please consult the help feature of your web browser.

Web Beacons

Web beacons are small bits of code embedded in web pages or in emails. We may use web beacons to deliver or communicate with cookies, to count users who have visited a web page, and to understand usage patterns. We also may include web beacons in e-mails to learn if messages have been opened, acted on, or forwarded.

You can disable the ability of web beacons to capture information in this manner by declining cookies as described above.

Third-Party Advertising

We may allow third parties, including business partners and ad networks, to display advertising on our Site. Some of these companies may place a cookie on your computer to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement. This enables them to understand where you, or others who are using your computer, saw their advertisement and deliver ads that they believe are of interest to you. We do not control these companies’ use of cookies. Some of these companies are members of the Network Advertising Initiative (NAI), which offers a single location to opt out of their cookies. Please visit the Network Advertising Initiative’s website at: http://www.networkadvertising.org/optout_nonppii.asp

How We Protect Your PII

To help ensure the confidentiality of your PII, we seek to use security technology and procedures, which are reviewed periodically. We use encryption technology and Secure Socket Layers (“SSL”) in all areas where your PII and account information is required. This means that the PII you submit to us via our Site is encrypted by your computer, and then decrypted again on our side, discouraging others from accessing your PII during transmission. If your browser does not support SSL, or if you are unable to establish a secure connection due to a company firewall, please call us at
(877) 888-2678 to place your order by phone.

In addition, your PII, account information, and transaction history are kept behind our firewall.
