  • Why We’re Here

    Bottom Line: We enjoy penny auctions and we want to help make the penny auction industry a better place.

    Penny Auction Watch was developed to be a place for penny auction enthusiasts to go for penny auction information. To provide an outlet for open discussion & a place to let users know which penny auctions seem to be legitimate; auctions where one has a chance at winning, as opposed to sites that don’t deliver what they advertise and are quite possibly set up to rip penny auction bidders off. When we find these less-than-honest sites we'll tell you about them and what we think they're doing wrong. But we can't do any of this without your help, post it to our forum or e-mail us if you suspect a penny auction site or have any questions!
