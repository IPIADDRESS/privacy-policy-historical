PeopleKeep Privacy Policy

 

During your visit to our website(s) we automatically collect and store the following information:

  • the IP address you are using to access the Internet,
  • the date and time of your visit,
  • the pages you visited, and
  • the address of the web site you came from when you came to visit.

Unless it is specifically stated otherwise, no additional information will be collected about you.

Personally Provided Information

You do not have to give us personal information to visit our web sites.

If you choose to provide us with additional information about yourself we maintain this information until specifically requested by you to delete it or until a reasonable length of time has passed such that the information is no longer needed to serve you.

Disclosure

PeopleKeep and its related websites does not disclose, give, sell or transfer any personal information about our visitors to unrelated third parties, unless required by judicial order or statute. If you supply personal information through an associate or affiliated website, the associate or affiliate may also have access to such information as allowed by law—for example, we do not share Protected Health Information (PHI) with unlicensed affiliates or associates.
