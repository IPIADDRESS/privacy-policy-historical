The people that are given access to this information

The security measures we have in place to protect your personal information
To safeguard your personal information, we are registered with the Data Protection Commissioner and act in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998. This only applies to data that PeopleBank is handling, not to any third party.

Our use of cookies
Our website makes use of cookies which are small digital files that are stored in your web browser that enable us to track your return visits to our website. Some of these cookies are provided by Google as a third party.

Your browser settings may allow you to block these cookies, but we recommend you have them enabled to help us personalise your experience of our website.
