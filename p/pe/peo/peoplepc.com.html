    1. Types of Personal Data We May Collect.

    Personal registration information.
    When you register as a PeoplePC Member, you will provide to us the following Personal Data: your name, telephone number, date of birth, billing and shipping addresses, email address and billing information (i.e., a credit card or debit account number). In addition, when you register, you will choose a Member Name and Password. You may also be asked to provide Personal Data when registering for other online services provided by PeoplePC. PeoplePC and its partners (see below) may use your contact information to ship your purchases to you and to provide membership services to you.

    We may use your Personal Data to communicate with you via email or conventional mail. These communications may relate to PeoplePC or to products and/or services offered by our merchant partners. We may use your Personal Data to communicate with you via telephone about your PeoplePC account.

    Information you decide to publish on the Internet.
    You may supply additional information about yourself in your online activities. For instance, you may publish your own web page or participate in online chat rooms. You have decided to make this information publicly available on the Internet. Information you voluntarily publish is not subject to this Privacy Policy.

    Responses to promotional events and contests. From time to time PeoplePC or its partners may conduct contests or other promotional events. As a condition of participating in these events, Members may be asked to provide Personal Data. You will be notified of the use to be made of such information at the time the information is requested, and you will have the opportunity to decline to participate. Information you provide through promotional events will be used as disclosed in the event solicitation materials.

    Responses to surveys. From time to time, we may conduct online, phone and/or mail surveys. Our surveys may ask for information such as profile data or Member experience opinions. Depending on the nature of the survey, we may disclose survey results to third parties on an aggregate basis without restriction. We may also use this data to personalize our site so that you see content in which we think you might be interested.

    Communications with us. You may contact us from time to time regarding matters related to your membership and the Services we provide. If you contact us by phone, email, or mail, we may keep a record of our communications with you, and use the information you provide so that we can respond to your inquiry.

    Choice/Opt-Out. We provide you the opportunity to opt-out of having your Personal Data used for certain purposes in the "My Account" section of the PeoplePC Member site (http://home.peoplepc.com/account). For example, you may opt-out of receiving email marketing communications from PeoplePC, its merchant vendors or partners by updating the email preferences found in this section.

    2. Usage Information We May Collect.

    IP address. We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our servers and to administer our web sites, and to help identify you. We may use this information for such things as helping us to track the success of new features.

    Use of cookies. Our site uses cookies to deliver content specific to your interests, and to allow us to better understand how you like to use our site. For example, we might track which of our site pages you visit and save this information in the form of a "cookie" or similar file. We also use cookies to save your Password so you do not have to re-enter it each time you visit our site. Cookies are stored on your computer hard drive, not on our servers. Cookies do not spy on you, and they cannot be used to breach the security of your hard drive and steal information. You are always free to decline cookies if your browser permits.

    Advertisers and partners may also use their own cookies on their own sites. We do not control use of these cookies and expressly disclaim responsibility for information collected through them.

    Web Beacons. Third party vendors who provide PeoplePC with reporting and email services employ a software technology called clear gifs (also known as web beacons). Clear gifs are tiny, invisible graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies. Clear gifs are only tied to your personal data for the purpose of tracking your initial registration, changes in account status and addition of account services. Clear gifs may be included in html emails for reporting purposes.

    Your use of the PeoplePC Sites. In order to provide better service to you, PeoplePC monitors usage of the PeoplePC Sites. For instance, we may track hits to each of the pages in our site, the access numbers you are using, the length and time of your Internet sessions. PeoplePC and its e-commerce partners may also collect data on transactions you engage in on the PeoplePC sites and partner sites. We may share aggregated statistical information with advertisers, business partners, sponsors or other third parties, but none of them receives individual Personal Data about you collected at our sites. For example, we may tell an advertiser that X number of visitors visited a certain area on a Web site but we would not disclose any information that would identify those individuals. We share this information only on an aggregate, anonymous basis and never on an individual basis, except as otherwise provided for in this Privacy Policy.
