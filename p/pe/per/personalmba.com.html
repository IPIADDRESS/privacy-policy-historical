Book Recommendations

Jump directly to one of the categories on the PMBA Recommended Reading List:

  • Quick Start - Understand yourself and learn how to absorb written material in record time.
  • Productivity & Effectiveness - Optimize your productivity, efficiency, and priority-setting skills.
  • Psychology & Communication - Learn how the human mind works, then put that knowledge to practical use.
  • Design & Production - Designing, creating, producing, and selling new products and services.
  • Marketing, Sales, & Negotiation - generating leads, creating interest, and closing the sale.
  • Entrepreneurship - Practical advice about starting a new venture, large or small.
  • Management & Leadership - Develop your direct reports and understand how to work effectively within an organization.
  • Strategy & Innovation - Examine your industry and your competition, plan, and innovate.
  • Finance & Analysis - Dig into the numbers to improve the health of your business and operations.
  • Personal Finance - Save, manage, and invest your well-deserved income.
  • Business History - Learn directly from people who have mastered their craft.
  • Business Reference - You'll these books close at hand while you work.
