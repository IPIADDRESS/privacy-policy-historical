Privacy Policy

Perdue follows the highest ethical standards in all of its operations and considers protecting the privacy of all visitors to Perdue At Home a priority. We have developed our Privacy Practices from guidelines established by the American Association of Advertising Agencies and the Association of National Advertisers.

While personal information is not required to have access to our web site, we do occasionally collect information provided from our visitors. Perdue will not knowingly contact or collect information from minors. This information will never be rented or sold to third parties. The personal information visitors voluntarily provide will only be used by Perdue, our affiliates and business partners to learn more about our customers' preferences and to help us provide better service and product quality. Please note that any information you submit implies consent for use of that information. Perdue has taken steps to make sure all information received is protected from unauthorized access and use.

Perdue's web site may contain links to other web sites. We make every effort to only link to web sites that share our privacy standards.
