Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting www.pediped.com. Your privacy is very important to us. When you provide us with information about yourself or others, it is our duty to act responsibly. Please read the following to better understand how we collect information from you and how it is used.

What if you no longer want to receive email?

If you wish at any time to opt out of future pediped® mailings, please send an email to customerservice@pediped.com with UNSUBSCRIBE in the subject of your email. Please include your name and email address in the body of the email. We will immediately remove you from our mailing list.

How does pediped® use my information?

We may use the information you provide to us to respond to your inquiries about our products, store locations and offerings. We may also offer you other products, programs or services we think you may find valuable. We may also share your information with pediped footwear's sister companies including pediped® Outlet, Brian James Footwear and others.

Does pediped® use cookies?

Yes. Cookies are bits of electronic information that a website can transfer to a visitor's hard drive. Cookies, by themselves, do not tell us your email address or other personally identifiable information. We use cookies to help make our site better. pediped® does not use information transferred through cookies for any promotional or marketing purposes, nor is the information shared with any third parties. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can change your browser to prevent them if you would rather not store the information for future use.

Security Statement

Maintaining the confidentiality of your personal information is important to pediped®. All payment information, including names, addresses, and credit card related data, is treated as confidential information. We utilize Secure Server Software (SSL) to encrypt all payment information.

Questions

Should you have any questions regarding our privacy policy, please email customerservice@pediped.com. You can also view our Terms and Conditions.
