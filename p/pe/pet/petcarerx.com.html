Privacy Policy

Your privacy and security are our top concerns. We understand how valuable your personal information is to you, and we assure you that it is just as valuable to us. We will never share your information with anyone else. We will never rent, sell, or share anything about you or your purchase history to anyone, no matter what.

Security

Protecting the information you give us is our greatest priority. Your personal data is protected against unauthorized use. You do not have to worry about the security of your credit card information. We protect your online order information using the most up to date Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology.

Communications Preferences

From time to time, PetCareRx may contact you with special offers and discounts, and information we think may be of interest to you and your pets. You can manage your email and mailing preferences in the ‘My Account’ section of our website. We let you decide if you want to hear from us. Click here to access your communications preferences.
