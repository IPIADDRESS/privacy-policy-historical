PetMD, LLC ("PetMD") provides a network of content and e-commerce websites, mobile applications and other online platforms dedicated to making Pet Parenting easier through a highly personalized experience that offers convenience and resources to enhance Pet Parenthood. This Privacy Policy explains how information about you is collected, used and disclosed by PetMD, and its parent, sister, subsidiary or affiliated companies, when you explore or interact with our content or make purchases on our websites, mobile applications or online platforms. We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by revising the date at the top of the policy and, in some cases, we may provide you with additional notice (such as adding a statement to our homepage or sending you a notification). We encourage you to review the Privacy Policy whenever you access the Services or otherwise interact with us to stay informed about our information practices and the ways you can help protect your privacy.

 

Collection of Information

 

Information You Provide to Us

 

We collect information you provide directly to us. For example, we collect information when you: register for an account; make a purchase; subscribe to our newsletters or other emails; fill out a form; participate in a contest, survey, or chat room; return or exchange a product; participate in any interactive features of the Services; ask questions of us or our veterinarians or vet techs; apply for a job; request customer support; contact our call center; or otherwise communicate with us. The types of information we may collect include your name, email address, postal address, phone number, fax number, credit card information, information about your pet (including the pet’s name, breed, gender, age, and photo), and any other information you choose to provide. If you place an order that requires a prescription, we collect information about your Veterinarian so that we may confirm the prescription and fill your order. We may also collect information about gift recipients such as name, email address, and shipping address so that we can fulfill the gift purchase. The information we collect about gift recipients is used for purposes of sending the gift and will not be used for other purposes.

 

Information We Collect Automatically When You Use the Services

 

When you access or use our Services, we automatically collect information about you, including:

 

  • Log Information: We collect log information about your use of the Services including, but not limited to, the type of browser you use, access times, pages viewed, your IP address, your device type, the operating system you are using, the page you visited before navigating to our Services and any other related information.
  • Information Collected by Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies: We and our service providers collect information using various technologies, including cookies and web beacons. Cookies are small data files stored on your hard drive or in device memory that help us improve our Services and your experience, store your preferences, manage your shopping cart, provide more relevant advertising, see which areas and features of our Services are popular and count visits. Web beacons are electronic images that may be used in our Services or emails and help deliver cookies, count visits and understand usage and campaign effectiveness. For more information about cookies, and how to disable them, please see "Your Choices" below.

 

Information We Collect From Other Sources

 

We may also obtain information from other sources and combine that with information we collect through our Services. For example, if you create or log into the Services through a social media site, we will have access to certain information from that site, such as your name, email address, and other information provided in accordance with the authorization procedures determined by such social media site. We may also obtain information about you from list brokers and publicly-available sources.

 

Use of Information

 

We may use information about you for various purposes, including to:

 

  • Provide, maintain and improve our Services;
  • Provide and deliver the products you order, process transactions, and send you related information, including confirmations and invoices,
  • If you place an order that requires a prescription, to contact your Veterinarian, confirm the prescription, and fill your order.
  • Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts and support and administrative messages;
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests and provide customer service;
  • Communicate with you about products, services, offers, promotions, rewards, and events offered by PetMD and others, including our affiliated companies, and provide news and information we think will be of interest to you;
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our Services;
  • Personalize and improve the Services and provide advertisements, content, or features that match user profiles or interests;
  • Process and deliver surveys, contest entries, and rewards; and
  • Link or combine with information we get from others to help understand your needs and provide you with better service.

 

PetMD is based in the United States and the information we collect is governed by U.S. law. By accessing or using the Services or otherwise providing information to us, you consent to the processing and transfer of information in and to the U.S. and other countries.

 

Sharing of Information

 

We may share information about you as follows or as otherwise described in this Privacy Policy:

 

  • With vendors, consultants and other service providers who need access to such information to carry out work on our behalf;
  • With third parties for their own marketing purposes, unless you opt out of this type of sharing by following the opt-out instructions under “Your California Privacy Rights,” below];

  • With your consent or at your direction.

 

We may also share aggregated or de-identified information, which cannot reasonably be used to identify you.

For example, search engines may index your questions, answers, and other posts to allow them to appear in search engine results. We recommend that you not include your real name or any personally identifying information in your questions, answers, and other posts.

The Services may offer social sharing features and other integrated tools, which let you share actions you take or articles you enjoy on our Services with other media, and vice versa.
