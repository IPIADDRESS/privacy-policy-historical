PKWARE Privacy Policy

PKWARE is committed to preserving your right to privacy. PKWARE does not use personal identifying information received from you through our web site, online shopping cart, or other means, in any way, other than to provide information about products available from or through PKWARE or specifically support your customer relationship with PKWARE. Personal identifying information given to us for the purpose of product registration is only used to notify you of PKWARE related news or information.

PKWARE neither sells nor barters information from either our mailing or customer lists.

Anonymous data like pages viewed, IP numbers, date and time, and browser type is collected for every visitor to this site. This information is used in aggregate to analyze and improve our web site.

If you have registered at our site's News & Events section to receive an e-mail newsletter from us and later change your mind, you may at anytime have your name removed from our newsletter distribution list by using the "Join/Remove" feature at that same location.

You may at anytime choose to have your name removed from any mailing list by contacting us, calling us at 414-354-8699 or using the opt-out link within our e-mail message to you.
