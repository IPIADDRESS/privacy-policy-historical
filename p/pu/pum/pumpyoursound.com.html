Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to W. A. Production. Accordingly, W. A. Production has developed this Privacy Policy in order for you to understand how W. A. Production collects, uses, communicates, discloses and makes use of personal information. The following outlines the Privacy Policy but it is to your benefit to read the specific elements that follow below.

i.        Before or at the time of collecting personal information, W. A. Production will identify the purposes for which information is being collected.

ii.        W. A. Production will collect and use of personal information solely with the objective of fulfilling those purposes specified by W. A. Production and for other compatible purposes, unless W. A. Production obtains the consent of the individual concerned or as required by law.

iii.        W. A. Production will only retain personal information as long as necessary for the fulfillment of those purposes.

iv.        W. A. Production will collect personal information by lawful and fair means and, where appropriate, with the knowledge or consent of the individual concerned.

v.        Personal data should be relevant to the purposes for which it is to be used, and, to the extent necessary for those purposes, should be accurate, complete, and up-to-date.

vi.        W. A. Production will protect personal information by reasonable security safeguards against loss or theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use or modification.

vii.        W. A. Production will make readily available to customers information about W. A. Production’s policies and practices relating to the management of personal information.

viii.        W. A. Production is committed to conducting our business in accordance with these principles in order to ensure that the confidentiality of personal information is protected and maintained.

Below are the specific elements of the Privacy Policy.

1.    Information W. A. Production Collects and How W. A. Production Uses It When you use the Website, W. A. Production may collect the following information:

a.    Should you sign up for a “pro” subscription, W. A. Production will collect the information necessary to complete that transaction, including credit card, PayPal and/or other payment information. W. A. b.    You may be required to complete subscription registration forms that provide W. A. Production with correct and up to date contact information (such as your name and street address), your email address, and your phone number(s).

c.    Computer-specific information including, without limitation, the unique computer and/or device ID assigned to your computer, platform type and version, hardware and processor type and human readable hardware name. This information is necessary to identify your account to simplify sign-on, and to determine the hardware requirements of W. A. Production’s users.

d.    Aggregate (general tracking) and user specific information about pages that users visit on the Website.

e.    Cookies: W. A. Production may employ cookies when users create user accounts by completing the subscription registration process and W. A. Production may further employ cookies to assist the functionality of the Website in the future. Cookies are small pieces of information, stored in simple text files, placed on your computer by a website which can be read by the website on your subsequent visits; the information stored in a cookie may relate to your browsing habits on the website, or a unique identification number so that the website can “remember” you on your return visit and W. A. Production may use cookies to enhance your usage of the Website. Generally speaking, cookies do not contain personal information from which you can be identified, unless you have furnished such information to the Website. You may disable cookies at any time via your browser preferences but if you disable cookies on your browser, please note that some parts of the Website may not function as effectively or may operate slower,

f.     User session information (which is not shared with third parties), including identification and length (a randomly generated identification created for each time you use the Website, and the amount of time you use the Website, in a single session), which shows W. A. Production user preferences regarding return visits to the Website, helps W. A. Production to mange subscriptions and private areas on the Website, records user specific tracking and/or aggregate tracking information of W. A. Production’s users and delivers customized content based on user specific information, such as browser type, screen type and resolution; W. A. Production may use this information in aggregated form in order to improve future development of the Website.

g.    As an automatic process, W. A. Production may record email and internet protocol (IP) addresses that access the Website for evaluation and diagnostic purposes in order to seek out and repair functionality issues with W. A. Production’s servers and related programming, and to monitor traffic patterns, analyze trends, administer the Website, track use patterns, and identify the most popular areas of the Website to deliver content most relevant to W. A. Production’s users. IP addresses are not linked to your personally identifiable information and are only used to gather broad demographic information for aggregate use and in connection therewith, W. A. Production hereby reserves the right to share aggregated demographic information with W. A. Production’s partners provided that such information will not be linked to your personally identifiable information that identifies you, except in the case of fraud or if W. A. Production determines, in its sole discretion, that it is necessary to enforce compliance to protect the Website, W. A. Production’s users and others.

h.    W. A. Production may be required to disclose your personally identifiable information if a governmental agency, or an attorney, subpoenas your private information, or makes a formal request for discovery pursuant to any legal action, or pending legal action.

i.      W. A. Production may disclose your personally identifiable information if W. A. Production believes you have committed fraud or copyright infringement or have violated our Terms any of the other policies and/or guidelines incorporated therein by reference which govern your use of the Website.

k.    W. A. Production may also use the information that it collects for internal tracking, review, or for contact purposes and such information is then archived by W. A. Production for future usage.

These policies, rules and technical measures help to protect your personally identifiable information from unauthorized access, improper use or disclosure, unauthorized modification, and unlawful destruction or accidental loss.
