  • any other information that you send to PunchOut2Go (www.punchout2go.com).

How do we use your information?

PunchOut2Go (www.punchout2go.com) may use your personal information to:

  • administer this website;

  • enable your access to and use of the website services (including PunchOut2Go PunchOut Catalog Gateway);

  • send you products that you purchase;
  • supply services that you purchase;
  • send statements and invoices;
  • collect payments from you; and
  • send you marketing communications.
