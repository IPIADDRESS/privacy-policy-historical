Security and Privacy Policy

We have created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy. We recognize our obligation to you to maintain confidentially certain information you provide on this website and to use responsibly any information provided.

The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for the purecostumes.com website.

When you visit our website at purecostumes.com, we will not collect any personally identifiable information about you (such as your name, address, telephone number, or e-mail address), unless you specifically provide such information. We may then store such information and use such information to contact you directly regarding products we believe may be of interest to you. We do not give or sell any user information to any third party.

The administrators of the store you are visiting have implemented all necessary security measures to provide a secure shopping experience.

These measures include:
1. The database with your personal information has been placed out of the reach of intruders
2. Passwords are stored in encrypted form
3. The store uses Verisign SSL, McAfee Monitoring and Authorized.Net Security when requesting your credit card information
4. The store is protected against common SQL Injection attacks
5. The store is protected against Cross Site Scripting
6. The system's control panel folder has been placed in a private folder
7. Staff passwords can't be easily deducted
8. The store's control panel has been prepared to avoid Brute Force attacks
9. The store records the IP addresses of customers who might be performing fraudulent actions and putting legitimate customers at risk
10. Store administrators are up to date with the latest bug fixes and security patches related to the Web Server, store software, and database

This site, from time to time, may contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites.
