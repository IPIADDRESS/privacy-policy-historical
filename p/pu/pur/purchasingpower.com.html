Why do I receive communications to participate in research?
Purchasing Power is committed to providing excellent customer experience. In order to measure how well we are doing and better understand how we can continually improve our product offering, we may contact you by phone or e-mail and solicit your participation in research. An account manager may occasionally send promotional or product information. In addition, Purchasing Power may send you updates on important information about our company and services. If you do not wish to receive such e-mails, simply let your account manager know.

How do we protect your information?
We employ powerful 128-bit encryption technology and Secure Socket Layers (SSL) in all areas where your personal identity is required. For your protection, this site is certified by a trusted SSL Certificate. This is your assurance that our site is authentic and that we're employing SSL security. You can check this security protection setting within your browser. To view our SSL certificate, click on the image of the closed lock or the solid key at the bottom of your browser. Our site security information will appear. For more information, contact your browser's publisher. For additional protection, your account information and order history are kept behind a firewall on separate servers. All transaction information is processed under encrypted channels. To be sure you have the most protection available, be sure to download the latest version of today's most popular browsers, Microsoft Internet Explorer and Firefox. In addition, access to your personal information is restricted within our company to provide maximum security.

What are cookies?
Your browser offers cookies that store small amounts of data on your computer about your visits to our sites. Cookies assist us in tracking which of our features you like best. And when you re-visit our sites, cookies can enable us to recognize that you are a return visitor and to customize our content according to your preferences. However, cookies tell us nothing about you personally unless you choose to give us additional information.

We may use cookies to:

  • Keep count of your return visits to our website
  • Accumulate and report anonymous, aggregate, statistical information on website usage
  • Deliver content specific to your interests
  • Save your username so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our website
