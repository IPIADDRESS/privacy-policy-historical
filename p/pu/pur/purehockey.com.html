Pure Hockey Online Privacy Policy

Pure Hockey's Commitment to Privacy

Your privacy is important to purehockey.com and to better protect your privacy, we provide this notice explaining our online information practices. We do not sell, rent, or loan any personal information (any information from which you can be identified) regarding our users to any external companies. Any information you give us is treated with sensitivity, and will not be used in ways to which you have not consented, or contradictory to Pure Hockey's online privacy policy.

The Information We Collect

We collect your IP address to help diagnose problems with our servers, to administer our Web Site ("Site"), and to gather broad demographic information using standard Site server logs. We also use cookies for certain areas of the site. A "cookie" is a small line of text that is stored with your web browser for record-keeping purposes and helps Pure Hockey (purehockey.com) provide better service to you. Pure Hockey never uses cookies to store sensitive information such as usernames and passwords.

Users must explicitly opt-in for "Remember Me" functionality. "Remember Me" uses cookies to allow access to areas of the purehockey.com site restricted to members only. In all cases, your username and password must be entered to modify any personal information stored on the purehockey.com site, even for users that have opted in for "Remember Me" functionality.

Pure Hockey will never store a user's credit card information in any form in our databases, and will not use your credit card for any purposes subsequent to the transaction at hand.

Information Submitted to Us

When you submit information to us using web-based form submissions or one of the e-mail links provided on our Site, we will attempt to respond to your request. We use reasonable industry standard technology to keep information that you submit to us secure. To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the appropriate use of information, we have put in place physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online. Unfortunately, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. As a result, we cannot warrant the security of any information transmitted to us and you do so at your own risk.

Your Consent

We value your privacy and reserve the right to disclose information only where required by law; to comply with valid legal processes such as a search warrant, subpoena or court order; to protect purehockey.com rights and property; or during emergencies when safety is at risk. By using this Site, you agree that we may collect and use information for the purposes for which the information was collected and in accordance with Pure Hockey's online privacy policy. Your continued accessing of purehockey.com will mean you accept and agree to be bound by information presented in Pure Hockey's policy.

Use of Third Party Opt-in Lists

Pure Hockey (purehockey.com) will present an opt-in question for your authorization before you are contacted from any external organizations affiliated with purehockey.com. The opt-in features are specific to each organization, but you should never receive e-mail from these organizations by way of purehockey.com providing your information unless you have indicated that it is acceptable. Targeted e-mail from purehockey.com, or from partners of purehockey.com who gain your permission to send you commercial e-mail, will include an opportunity to opt-out of future communications.

Links

Pure Hockey (purehockey.com) websites may contain links to other sites. Pure Hockey assumes no responsibility for the content or the privacy policies and practices of such websites. Pure Hockey encourages you to read the privacy statements of these linked sites as their privacy practices may differ from what has been provided in this Privacy Policy statement.
