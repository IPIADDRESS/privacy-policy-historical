Pricing and Misprints
On highly competetive markets like the GSM handsets and OEM accessories market, prices change constantly, therefore prices are subject to change without notice. We don't offer price protection after orders are shipped. However, all orders will be re-adjusted to new prices if they have not been shipped.

PureMobile reserves the right to refuse or cancel any orders placed for products listed with incorrect prices.
