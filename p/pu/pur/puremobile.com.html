Our commitment to your privacy

At Pure Mobile, we acknowledge that online business brings new opportunities and new responsibilities. We embrace the opportunity to provide personalized eCommerce services for unlocked cell phone, wireless devices and their related accessories to you in a highly secure environment. Despite, our infrastructure being among the most technically advanced and secure networks, we share with you the responsibility of controlling the exchange, storage, and use of selected personal information.

Confidentiality is a priority for us and we take all the necessary measures to safeguard the strict privacy of the personal information contained in your file, whether you purchase your unlocked cell phone, wireless devices and related accessories from us or not.

 

The information we collect

Pure Mobile collects information you agree to provide us with when registering to purchase unlocked cell phones, tablets, notebooks and related accessories. Information—such as your name, civic and email address, telephone number—is collected and retained. We may also use this information to keep you informed of upcoming promotions or new products that may be of interest to you. Anonymous information is collected mostly through standard analysis of our web-server and analytic log files. We may use this data to monitor which areas of the site are used most and to determine which areas we need to focus on improving. This data is used exclusively by Pure Mobile so that we may continue to improve the value of the services and information on the website.

Entering personal data on Pure Mobile is not only easy but also safe. We use SSL (Secure Socket Layer) protocol, which is among the best Internet security software in the world along with GeoTrust for securing the exchange of confidential data. This security technology encrypts the personal information that you send us (including things like names, addresses, emails, telephone numbers and credit card numbers), so it cannot be read by anyone as it travels over the Internet. Moreover, for added security we scramble your credit numbers in the event that our data base is compromised by external forces. We will evaluate and adopt new security technology as it becomes available.

 

Personal information use and disclosure

Internal Use

We use your personal information to process your order and provide you with customer service. We may internally use your personal information to improve the Sites’ content and layout, to improve outreach and for our own marketing efforts (including marketing our services and products to you), and to determine general marketplace information about visitors to the Site.

Communications with you

We will use your personal information to communicate with you about the Site and your orders and deliveries. Also, we may send you a confirmation email when you register with us. Also, you may submit your email address for reasons such as to register for a contest or sweepstakes; to request us to notify you of new brands, new product styles or promotions; to sign up for email newsletters and special offers; or to sign up for the our email Alert service. If you submit your email address, we use it to deliver the information to you. We always permit you to unsubscribe or opt out of future emails (see the opt out section, below, for more details). Because we have to communicate with you about orders that you choose to place, you cannot opt out of receiving emails related to your orders.

External Use

For example, in order to deliver products to you, we must share some information. We partner with third parties (such as the U.S. Postal Service, United Parcel Service, and Federal Express) to ship products, to ensure delivery, and so that we can obtain feedback, improve the quality of our service, and measure and improve the quality of the service of the third party. In the example of shippers, we provide them some personally identifiable information such as your name, shipping address, email, and phone number.

 

Why we collect it

You can choose to visit our site without telling us who you are or revealing any personal information about yourself. If you do provide specific consent to obtain information, it is always to gather only the information needed to serve you better, and provide you with the right unlocked cell phones, tablets, notebooks and related accessories at the right time and at the right price.

 

Our use of cookies

Pure Mobile's eCommerce engine uses cookies to allow and facilitate your user session with us. During the quotation process, cookies store session information to ensure that the data is passed on in proper sequence from step to step. The cookie itself is stored on your own PC and does not contain any personal information. Pure Mobile also uses third party Analytics Code to help us better evaluate the effectiveness of our advertising and to determine the behaviour of visitors on our website in an effort to provide you with the most ongoing enhanced customer experience.

Cookies are small pieces of information that are stored as text files by your Internet browser on your computer’s hard drive. Most Internet browsers are initially set to accept cookies. You can set your browser to refuse cookies from web sites or to remove cookies from your hard drive, but if you do, you will not be able to access or use portions of the Site. We have to use cookies to enable you to select products, place them in an online shopping cart, and to purchase those products. If you do this, we keep a record of your browsing activity and purchase. THE SITE’S COOKIES DO NOT AND CANNOT INFILTRATE A USER’S HARD DRIVE TO GATHER A USER’S CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION. Our cookies are not “spyware.”
