Pricing and Misprints
On highly competetive markets like the GSM handsets and OEM accessories market, prices change on constantly, therefore prices are subject to change without notice. Due to the constant manual update of prices, there is a high probability of having over-priced or under-priced products. In this event, PureMobile reserves the right to refuse or cancel any orders placed for the product listed with the incorrect price.
An order will be re-adjusted to new prices if it has not been shipped.

No refunds will be provided for difference caused by a price change after an order is shipped.
