Privacy Policy for Purple Carrot

PII may include other types of information depending on the legal definition of PII that applies in your physical location. Only the definition of PII that applies in your location will apply to you under this Privacy Policy.

Unless otherwise defined in this Privacy Policy, terms used in this Privacy Policy have the same meanings as in our Terms of Use, accessible at www.purplecarrot.com/terms. This website is owned and operated by Three Limes, Inc. d/b/a The Purple Carrot.

Agreement

Information Collection and Use; Choice and Consent

Sign Up

If you sign up for the Site through Facebook, we collect your email address and Facebook password. We may also collect information about you from Facebook, including PII, in accordance with the Facebook privacy policy and terms of use. If you sign up directly on the Site, we collect your first and last name, address, zip code, state, city and phone number.

We use this information to process your registration, identify you, activate and manage your account, create your user profile, respond to your requests and inquiries and provide you with requested Services. We also use this information to communicate with you about your deliveries and account and to send you promotional information about Purple Carrot.


Recipes

When you request a recipe, we collect your email address in order to provide the recipe and to send you promotional emails.


When You Contact Us

When you contact us through our Contact Us form, we collect your name and email address in order to respond to you and to send you promotional emails.


Purchases

When you make a purchase on the Site, such as a subscription or Gift Card, your payment card information, order number and email address is collected by our third party payment processor in order to process your transaction. The information collected includes your payment card number, CVV and expiration month, in addition to billing information such as your name and billing address. If you purchase a subscription, our third party payment processor retains your information in order to process your regular subscription payments.


Gift Cards

If you purchase a Gift Card, we collect your name and email address and the name and email of the person receiving the Gift Card in order to provide the Gift Card. In order to redeem a Gift Card, the gift recipient must Sign Up for an account on the Site and provide the requested Personal Information.


Refer a Friend

We use a third party service provider, such as Friend Buy, to operate our Refer a Friend feature. If you refer a friend to the Site, the information you provide will be collected by Friend Buy in accordance with the terms of their privacy policy. Friend Buy provides us with a copy of the information collected by them about you. We use this information to ensure the referral credits are given in accordance with the current offering. We also collect your friend’s email address in order to contact your friend and provide the referral credit We will also use your friend’s email address to send promotional emails about Purple Carrot.


Postings

The Site allows users to post comments in public areas of the Site and on social media sites, such as Facebook. If you wish to post a comment, your name and any other information you post will appear with your comment on areas of the Site and/or on social media sites that can be viewed by others. We may collect the information you post. Please be aware that any information you post on public areas of the Site may be copied, used and disclosed by others. Public postings are not covered by the terms of this Privacy Policy.


Jobs

If you apply for a job with Purple Carrot, we will collect your first and last name, email, phone number, address, photo, resume, and cover letter, which may include other information about you, including PII. You may also apply for a job with us using your LinkedIn profile. We will collect the information you provide to us. We will also collect information about you from LinkedIn if you provide your LinkedIn profile, in accordance with LinkedIn’s policies. We use this information to assess your qualifications for open positions and to contact you if we deem necessary.


Email Addresses and Promotional Messages

We collect email addresses of users and their friends that are provided to us through the Site or Services. We use these emails to send promotional messages about Purple Carrot. You may opt out of receiving these emails by following the unsubscribe link or instructions provided in any email we send or by contacting us.


Information Choices

It is always your choice whether or not to provide us with such information. If you do provide PII to the Site, you are giving your express consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your PII as described in this Privacy Policy. If you choose not to provide the requested information you may not be able to access certain features or functions of this Site and you will not have access to the Services. You may access, change or delete your information at any time as described below. You may opt out of receiving any, or all, communications from us by following the unsubscribe link or instructions provided in any email we send or by contacting us.

Retention of Your Information

We will retain your information, including Personal Information, as long as you maintain an account with us or as needed to provide the Services. We will retain your information, including Personal Information, after your account is closed if we believe it is necessary to do so, such as for legal compliance purposes, to resolve disputes, enforce our agreements or otherwise operate our business.

Cookies

When you visit the Site, we use session “cookies,” a piece of information stored on your computer temporarily and deleted from the user’s device when the browser is closed, to allow us to uniquely identify your browser while you are browsing or interacting with the Site.  Session cookies also help us confirm your identity and are required in order to log into your account. We also use cookies to help us improve the Site.  If you wish to prevent cookies from being used as you navigate our Site, you can reset your browser to refuse all cookies and/or indicate when a cookie is being sent.

Users who disable their web browsers’ ability to accept cookies will be able to browse the Site, but will not be able to access or take advantage of all of the features and Services on the Site. We therefore recommend you do not block or disable these cookies. To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been sent and how to manage, as well as delete them, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.
