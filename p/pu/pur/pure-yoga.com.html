Cookies
Cookies are small pieces of information which are stored by your browser on your computer. Many websites generate cookies which will be stored on your hard drive so that on subsequent visits to that site it will recognize that you have previously used that site and make it easier for you to use that site.

The Pure websites use two types of cookies: Temporary cookies which remain in the cookie file of your browser until you have finished that particular visit to the Pure websites, and which allow you ease of use of the Pure websites and avoid you having to re-enter information and the like.

Persistent Cookies help us to recognize you and allows us to personalize the presentation and content of the Pure websites to match your preferences.
