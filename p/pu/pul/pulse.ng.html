Privacy Policy

At pulse.ng, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by pulse.ng and how it is used.

Website Analytics

Like many other Web sites, pulse.ng makes use of website analytics. The information inside the analytics files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Pulse.com.ng does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.

Admanager Cookies 

Google and other third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on pulse.ng. Admanager vendors cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to pulse.ng and other sites on the Internet. We currently use Google as our admanager but this could change.  Pulse.com.ng has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. Pulse.com.ng’s privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.
