This statement created by PublicityWires.com is to demonstrate its commitment to protecting your privacy. When you post or submit press releases for distribution through PublicityWires.com, such press releases and personal information therein contained to its media contact profile or in the forms will be on distribution. By submitting press releases and personal information in the requisite forms, You certify that all information provided to the PublicityWires.com is true and correct and indicates your consent with this policy.

This information is used by PublicityWires.com to further our statutory objectives. Failure to provide such information may result in our being unable to provide you with our services including access to the PublicityWires.com website. Your contact information from the forms is used to send promotional materials and information required to you. You may "opt-out" of receiving future promotional mailings. We do these so that we can measure traffic, gauge the popularity of various parts of the PublicityWires.com, gain general knowledge about our users and compile aggregated statistics.

If you choose to purchase products or services from us, you may need to give personal data and authorize us to obtain information from various credit services. For instance, you may need to provide the following information:- 
- Name on credit card

PublicityWires.com provides the same protections for these electronic communications that we employ in the maintenance of information received by mail and telephone.

All data collected by the PublicityWires.com is used only for the purposes indicated above. PublicityWires.com will not share User personal information with any third parties except under the following circumstances:

(b) As we may be required to do so by laws.

(d) Banks and other financial institutions in connection with the collection of payments.
(e) Any actual or proposed transferee of our business or the PublictiyWires.com website or any part of our business or PublicityWires.com website or participant in our operations.
