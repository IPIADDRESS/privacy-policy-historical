Privacy Policy

PTO Today, Inc. has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website, ptotoday.com, as well as for our affiliated website: schoolfamilynights.com.

IP Addresses and Cookies

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our website. We use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests and to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our site.

Data We Collect

Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information (like their name and email address) and demographic information (like their zip code or parent group status). We use customer contact information from the registration form to send the user information about our company when requested to do so. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site. We use this data to improve the operations of our site and to improve our ability to serve our customers over the long term.

Our online surveys ask visitors for contact information (like their email address). The customer's contact information is used to contact the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.

We run contests on our site in which we ask visitors for contact information (like their email address). The customer's contact information is used to contact the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.

Links to Outside Websites

This site contains links to other sites, including many not owned or controlled by PTO Today, Inc. PTO Today, Inc. is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites.

Public Message Boards

This site makes forums/message boards available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information or any information (like your school name or town) that could be used to identify you.

Security

This site takes reasonable steps to protect the loss, misuse and unauthorized access of the information under our control. Bear in mind, though, that no Web-based transmission can be guaranteed completely secure or error free. Email, especially, may not be secure.

Children

This site is intended for the adult leaders of school-parent groups and the parents of school-aged children. It is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from site visitors age 12 or under.

Our Relationship with Third Parties

Several of our programs (for example: our School Family Nights program and our Parent Group of the Year initiative) are run in conjunction with third party companies. We may disclose personally identifiable information you provide to this site to these and other third parties. These companies and vendors are not affiliates of PTO Today. We only share this information if those parties agree to treat the information in accordance with this privacy policy.

If you inquire about a program of ours that is run in conjunction with a Third party company, your inquiry and accompanying personal information may be shared with said Third Party company. If appropriate, those companies may contact you regarding the specific program you inquired about.
