Last modified: October 22, 2008.

 

Privacy Policy applies to all of the services provided at praguepost.com web sites. This Privacy Policy is subject to change.

When using praguepost.com web sites, we may collect the following types of information:

Information we collect is used only to enhance our services, to perform research and to further develop praguepost.com
