Those who engage in transactions with PrajwalDesai.com are asked to provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to process those transactions. PrajwalDesai.com does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

PrajwalDesai.com discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on PrajwalDesai.com behalf or to provide services available at PrajwalDesai.com websites, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. PrajwalDesai.com uses cookies to help PrajwalDesai.com identify and track visitors, their usage of PrajwalDesai.com website, and their website access preferences.
