Privacy Statement

practicalfishkeeping.co.uk and its publisher, Emap Active Ltd., is committed to protecting your privacy and security. We only use the information we collect about you with your consent to process your enquiry or order and to tell you about our services. We will never wilfully disclose personal information about you to any third party, other than to process an order or enquiry, without first receiving your permission or unless we are under a legal obligation to do so.

Who Else Will We Tell About You?
