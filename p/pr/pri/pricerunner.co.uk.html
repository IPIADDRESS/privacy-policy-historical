Pricerunner Privacy Commitment

At Pricerunner we appreciate your privacy and hope that you feel comfortable trusting us. We make every effort to keep your information confidential and protect your privacy when using our site and services. We take your privacy and our responsibility under the data protection legislation very seriously.

Collecting Your Information
Pricerunner does not automatically collect any personal information about you. However we are able to deliver a more personalised service to you when you personally provide us with details such as your name, address, etc. We collect this information either through membership registration, newsletter competitions or site competitions.

Cookies
Pricerunner uses cookies - a small text file containing a unique string of numbers or letters that is placed on a computer – to ‘remember’ your computer when you visit the site. Using cookies helps us serve ads, take polls and manage the services we offer. This will not collect any personally identifiable information about you. Cookies do not recognise you as a person, just your computer. They cannot be used to obtain other information from your hard disk. If you need to block cookies this can be done by changing your browser settings. It may help to contact your ISP for assistance.

Third Party Advertising
The Pricerunner website includes ads delivered on our behalf by DoubleClick. When you visit the site, information such as the number of times you have viewed an advertisement, but NOT your name, address or other personal information, is used to deliver ads to you. For more information please visit: http://www.doubleclick.net/us/corporate/privacy.

Third Party Cookies
In order to deliver advertisements to this site, DoubleClick our third-party advertiser may place or recognise a unique cookie on your browser. For more information about, and how to "opt-out" of, DoubleClick cookies please visit: http://www.doubleclick.net/us/corporate/privacy/privacy/ad-cookie/default.asp?asp_object_1=&.

Members
When you sign up as a member of Pricerunner we need your e-mail address and a password. You have the option to provide us with your name, phone number and postcode. These details help us to personalise your membership and will enable us to provide a better service to you.

Disclosing Information
Personal information and information relating to use of our services, is administered by Pricerunner.com. This information will be used for administration of our offered services and forwarded by post, telephone or email. By registering, you have accepted that Pricerunner.com automatically co-operates with regulated information banks. Pricerunner.com use these to gain information for adding and updating, which is a pre-requisite for being able to offer an efficient service. You can at any time request a correction to your record. We may, from time to time, pass on your details to other relevant companies. If you object to your personal details being used by other companies, please state your objection to us in writing and we will ensure your information is used by Pricerunner.com alone. To request that we do not share your information with other companies please e-mail jodie@pricerunner.com and include the e-mail address you signed up with.

Newsletter
Pricerunner operates a free e-newsletter service – the Pricerunner Update - to help you keep up-to-date with the best deals, competitions and Pricerunner news. This is an opt-in service so, as a member, you will receive the newsletter, to unsubscribe simply send an e-mail to jodie@pricerunner.com and include your e-mail address.

It is recommended that you read the Terms of Use if using the Pricerunner site.

If you have or intend to sign up it is recommended you read the Membership Conditions and Membership FAQ.
