Privacy Policy:  Your privacy is very important to YTB Travel & Cruises. We have established and implemented practices that we believe are consistent with the highest standards and best practices of organizations doing business on the Internet. This privacy policy ("policy") describes the practices that apply to our travel product, including, specifically, the information we collect about you, when and how we collect that information and what may happen to that information.

  • YTB Travel & Cruises will not collect personally identifiable information like your name, e-mail and mailing address and telephone number ("Personal Information") without your knowledge and permission.

  • YTB Travel & Cruises will not disclose your Personal Information to third parties other than those required to complete your travel purchases.

  • YTB Travel & Cruises will not sell your Personal Information to anyone.

  • YTB Travel & Cruises will allow you to view, correct or remove your Personal Information and other information associated with your Traveler Profile as a user of our travel product.

  • YTB Travel & Cruises will take reasonable steps to protect the security of the Personal Information we collect from you.

How We Use Your Personal Information:  Personal Information such as your name, telephone numbers, personal travel preferences and credit card information is provided to airlines, car rental companies, hotels and other travel suppliers when necessary to obtain travel service availability and rates, and to complete a reservation or finalize the purchase of a travel itinerary. YTB Travel & Cruises does not warranty the way your Personal Information is used by travel suppliers to whom this information is provided. We use your email address to notify you of purchase confirmation. Personal Information may also be provided to support personnel as necessary to ensure the integrity and operation of our travel product.

Sharing of Personal Information and Disclosure:  Other than as explained in this policy, we will not disclose any Personal Information we collect from you to others without your permission, except in certain limited circumstances:
 (a) to protect the security of our travel product and our rights under Terms and    Conditions of Use;
(b) to protect ourselves against liability;
(c) in response to legal process,
(d) if we believe in good faith that the law requires it; or
(e) in connection with a sale of our business or company.

Other Information:  You may choose to provide other information about yourself and your travel preferences, such as, preferred seat selection, airline/hotel/car-rental loyalty or awards program membership numbers, special meal requests and ticketing options. We will use this information to automatically record your travel preferences in reservations you confirm and to provide more appropriate travel recommendations to you. Travel Preferences allow us to customize the information we return to you so that your favorite services and companies are considered for inclusion. You may use our travel product to search for and purchase travel services even if you do not share this type of information with us.

Security:  Whenever you use our travel product, you are automatically signed in securely unless you choose otherwise. All your transactions are protected by powerful Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption technology supported by Microsoft Internet Explorer 4.0, 5.0, Netscape and other popular browsers. Whenever personal or financial information is transmitted via the Internet, YTB Travel & Cruises utilizes the leading security technology on the Internet known as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol. SSL protects the confidentiality of personal and financial information shared between the user and YTB Travel & Cruises by encrypting the data during transmission. SSL encrypts your credit card number, name, address, and phone number before they travel over the Internet.
