Brierley+Partners Corporate Privacy Policy

Brierley+Partners provides loyalty and customer relationship management (“CRM”) services to our clients. These services include data collection and bulk e-mail services. The privacy policy applies to our corporate web site, www.brierley.com. Brierley+Partners also adheres to the Privacy Policy of its clients when collecting, storing, and transmitting data for the purposes of loyalty and CRM services.

Brierley+Partners is firmly committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of our clients’ customers. Because protecting your privacy is important to us, you may always submit concerns regarding our Privacy Policy to our privacy practices to privacy@brierley.com. If you choose to write us an e-mail, please reference the Privacy Policy in your subject line. Brierley+Partners will attempt to respond to all reasonable concerns and inquiries expeditiously.

Corporate Anti-Spam Policy
Clients of Brierley+Partners have agreed to distribute commercial e-mail through Brierley+Partners systems for the purpose of their marketing efforts. Brierley+Partners and its Clients have agreed to adhere to the CAN-SPAM Act.

If you feel that you have received unsolicited commercial email from Brierley+Partners please send an email to: abuse@brierley.com. Please include or attach the unsolicited e-mail referenced in the abuse e-mail.

The Information We Collect and How We Use It
Brierley+Partners does not sell, rent, or share information collected on behalf of its clients.

Website Activity
Brierley+Partners collects anonymous information about visitors to it’s corporate web sites and web sites it hosts on behalf of its customers. The type of information collected includes Internet Protocol (IP) addresses which is used to identify information like web pages visited, unique visits, types of browsers used, and web browser session-based activity. This information is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

Website Security
Information collected through www.brierley.com and client hosted web sites is done via a secure (SSL) web interface and maintained within a secure operating environment. Our team of infrastructure and security professionals continually monitors our web interfaces and ensures technology updates are made regularly to deter any attempts for unwanted intrusion.

Use of Cookies
A cookie is a small text file that is stored by the web browser on a user’s computer for record-keeping purposes. Brierley+Partners does not use cookies on its’ corporate web site. However, Brierley+Partners may use cookies on our customer web sites in an anonymous fashion to track overall website performance, yet reserves the right to use cookies to identify unique customers in accordance with loyalty and CRM services contracted by its clients. Additionally, Brierley+Partners may use third party web analytics services to help us track and optimize our website performance. These third parties are prohibited from using collected data for any purpose other than as a service provider to Brierley+Partners.

Links
The Brierley+Partners corporate web site may include links to other Internet sites that are not operated or controlled by Brierley+Partners. We cannot control and are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. This may additionally be true for web sites hosted by Brierley+Partners on behalf of our customers.

Notification of Changes to our Privacy Policy
If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes to this privacy statement hosted at www.brierley.com. We may additionally post these changes in other places we deem appropriate so that you are aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it.

We reserve the right to modify this privacy statement at any time. If we make material changes to this statement, we will notify you on this web page.
