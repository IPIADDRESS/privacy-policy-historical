Privacy / Cookie Policy

Our Privacy policy is simple.

  • We won't send you anything to you unless you ask for it.
  • We won't give your personal information to anyone. We simply use it to create your detective account.
  • We use cookies. This enables us to build your detective portfolio. The cookies are in no way used for any other reason. (see below for more details on our cookie policy)
  • We show adverts discreetly. Only view targetted adverts if you choose to.
  • Members will only be asked for a few personal details, in order to keep your product searching in one place for you to visit again.
Cookies

Due to new European legislation regarding how websites store information about you, PriceInspector has updated its cookie policy.

What are Cookies?

A “cookie” is a small file of letters and numbers downloaded onto a device when the user accesses PriceInspector UK. These cookies are used to differentiate one user from another user and pass information from webpage to webpage during your use of the website. Cookies allow us to recognize and remember your preferences, customize your search results based on your prior searches, serve you with relevant advertisements from third party vendors and track general analytical usage of the PriceInspector site.

Cookies that we use on PriceInspector UK:

Anonymous analytics cookies:

Every time a user visits our website, web analytics software provided by a third party generates an anonymous analytics cookie. This cookie can tell us whether or not the site you are viewing has been visited before. It allows us to track such things as how many individual users we have and which browsers users are utilising, or how the user interacted with our main features.

Price Detective Member area/Registration cookies:

When you sign up to become a Price Detective, we generate a cookie so that we can track your recent cases, price surveillance, and recently view products. We collect a bit of personal information such as Name and Email Address - this information is NEVER used for any other purpose other than creating your Detective Account.

Search interface cookies:

We offer specific optional search features that help users to refine, sort, store searches, which are available the next time you visit. These are only available if you use our search options (available from the top right of our website)

Third Party Advertising Cookies

DoubleClick DART Cookie

  • Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on www.priceinspector.co.uk.
  • Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to www.priceinspector.co.uk and other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL - http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. Our advertising partners are Google Adsense.

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on www.priceinspector.co.uk send directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies (such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see.

If you object to us using cookies to track and personalise your experience on PriceInspector UK, please email us and we will advise on how to block them.
