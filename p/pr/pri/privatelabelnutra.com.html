1. Collection of Information.

1.1 Survey Information.

Private Label Nutraceuticals collects information from individuals by various methods, including, but not limited to, when an individual voluntarily completes a Private Label Nutraceuticals survey, order form, or a registration page either online or offline, or by means of online or offline surveys, order forms, or registration pages operated by third parties (collectively, a "Survey"). In the Surveys, Private Label Nutraceuticals or a third party (a "Third Party") may ask an individual to provide various information to Private Label Nutraceuticals, which may include his or her name, email address, street address, zip code, telephone numbers (including cell phone numbers and carriers), birth date, gender, salary range, education and marital status, occupation, social security number, employment information, personal and online interests, and such other information as may be requested from time to time (together, "Survey Information"). Private Label Nutraceuticals may also collect information concerning an individual from another source and uses that information in combination with information provided from this web site. Completing the Surveys is completely voluntary, and individuals are under no obligation to provide Survey Information to Private Label Nutraceuticals or Third Party, but an individual may receive incentives from Private Label Nutraceuticals or a Third Party in exchange for providing Survey Information to Private Label Nutraceuticals.

1.2 Third Party List Information.

Private Label Nutraceuticals collects information from individuals when an individual provides information to a third party and Private Label Nutraceuticals subsequently purchases, licenses, or otherwise acquires the information from the third party (the "Seller"). Such purchased information may include, but is not limited to, an individual's name, email address, street address, zip code, telephone numbers (including cell phone numbers and carriers), birth date, gender, salary range, credit card information, education and marital status, occupation, industry of employment, personal and online interests, and such other information as the individual may have provided to the Seller (together, "Third Party List Information"). When acquiring Third Party List Information, Private Label Nutraceuticals seeks assurances from the Seller that the Seller has a right to transfer the Third Party List Information to Private Label Nutraceuticals and that the Seller has a right to provide offers from advertisers to the individuals whose personal information is included on the Seller's list.

1.3 Other Informations.

Other occasions when Private Label Nutraceuticals obtains information from individuals include (1) when an individual is making a claim for a prize or seeking to redeem an incentive offered by Private Label Nutraceuticals or by a third party, (2) when an individual requests assistance through Private Label Nutraceuticals's customer service department, and (3) when an individual voluntarily subscribes to a Private Label Nutraceuticals service or newsletter (together, "Other Information").

1.4 Cookies, Web Beacons, and Other Info Collected Using Technology.

Private Label Nutraceuticals currently uses cookie and web beacon technology to associate certain Internet-related information about an individual with information about the individual in our database. Additionally, Private Label Nutraceuticals may use other new and evolving sources of information in the future (together, "Technology Information").

(a) Cookies.

A cookie is a small amount of data stored on the hard drive of the individual's computer that allows Private Label Nutraceuticals to identify the individual with his or her corresponding data that resides in Private Label Nutraceuticals's database. You may read more about cookies at http://cookiecentral.com. Individuals who use the Websites need to accept cookies in order to use all of the features and functionality of the Websites.

(b) Web Beacons.

A web beacon is programming code that can be used to display an image on a web page (by using an <img src="x"> programming function -- see http://truste.org for more information), but can also be used to transfer an individual's unique user identification (often in the form of a cookie) to a database and associate the individual with previously acquired information about an individual in a database. This allows Private Label Nutraceuticals to track certain web sites an individual visits online. Web beacons are used to determine products or services an individual may be interested in, and to track online behavioral habits for marketing purposes. For example, Private Label Nutraceuticals might place, with the consent of a third party website, a web beacon on the third party's website where fishing products are sold. When Bill, an individual listed in Private Label Nutraceutical's database, visits the fishing website, Private Label Nutraceuticals receives notice by means of the web beacon that Bill visited the fishing site, and Private Label Nutraceuticals would then update Bill's profile with the information that Bill is interested in fishing. Private Label Nutraceuticals may thereafter present offers of fishing related products and services to Bill. In addition to using web beacons on web pages, Private Label Nutraceuticals also uses web beacons in email messages sent to individuals listed in Private Label Nutraceuticals's database.

(c) New Technology.

The use of technology on the Internet, including cookies and web beacons, is rapidly evolving, as is Private Label Nutraceuticals's use of new and evolving technology. As a result, Private Label Nutraceuticals strongly encourages individuals to revisit this policy for any updates regarding its use of technology.

Private Label Nutraceuticals may receive information about individuals from third parties or from other sources of information outside of Private Label Nutraceuticals including information located in public databases ("Outside Information").

1.6 Individual Information

As used herein, Individual Information means Survey Information, Third Party List Information, Other Information, Technology Information, and Outside Information, and any other information Private Label Nutraceuticals gathers or receives about individuals

Private Label Nutraceuticals will never knowingly collect any personal information about children under the age of 13. If Private Label Nutraceuticals obtains actual knowledge that it has collected personal information about a child under the age of 13, that information will be immediately deleted from our database. Because it does not collect such information, Private Label Nutraceuticals has no such information to use or to disclose to third parties. Private Label Nutraceuticals has designed this policy in order to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act ("COPPA").
