Primagames.com is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. The following privacy policy describes what kind of information we collect and how it is used. We believe that this policy will enable our visitors to protect their privacy while having fun at our site.

INFORMATION THAT IS COLLECTED AND HOW IT IS USED

A. Overview of Policy

Primagames.com regularly tracks general data about our site, such as the number of visitors, to improve and enhance our site and its features. Prima uses this general demographic information to tailor our services and features to our users’ needs and preferences. We may share this general data with third parties from time to time.

In addition, Primagames.com also collects certain personally identifiable information from its users. This information is specific to the user and is used for a variety of purposes as set forth below. The following describes the kinds of personally identifiable information that we collect and how it is used.

B. Membership Registration

Primagames.com requests personally identifying information about you and your interests when you register within the Primagames.com site. Such information may include, but is not limited to, name, address, e-mail address, gender and age and information on the game systems that you use. Primagames.com uses this information to track the overall demographics of our user base. We also use the personal information internally to determine what kinds of features and information to offer our users, to enhance the design and layout of our site, and to inform users of new features, products or offerings on our site.

Primagames.com may share this registration information with third parties. If we do so, we will require such third parties to use the information only to the extent necessary to provide you their services or to notify you of new products and services. We will also require such third parties to take reasonable measures to protect the security and privacy of this information and to allow our users to request that they not be contacted with unsolicited emails or other communications.

C. E-mail Newsletters and E-mail Updates

When you register as a user of Primagames.com, you will be asked whether you wish to receive our free weekly e-mail newsletter. The newsletter will provide links to hot new information of the Primagames.com web site as well as other information or services that may be of interest to our users. You will also be asked whether you wish to receive regular service announcements about special offers and features on the Primagames.com site. These announcements will be less frequent than our regular e-mail newsletters. We expect to send 6 to 10 such announcements each year.

Primagames.com does not currently rent or sell its newsletter subscriber lists to third parties. However, the newsletters may contain advertisements and promotions on behalf of third party sponsors or advertisers and we may send you notices on behalf of those sponsors or advertisers. We may also notify our third party sponsors or advertisers of your email address if you have indicated an interest in finding out more about their products and services. We will require our third party sponsors or advertisers to take reasonable measures to protect the security and privacy of your e-mail address.

If you want to update your e-mail address or want to request that you stop receiving e-mail newsletters or e-mail announcements, please contact us at webmaster@primagames.com.

D. Contests and Promotion

Primagames.com also requests personally identifiable information about you when you enter one of our contests, sweepstakes or promotions. We may share the personally identifiable information with third parties in the same manner as we share registration information. If you enter a contest or sweepstakes, there will often be a separate set of rules that will govern your participation in such events. You should also review the contest rules for any special arrangements that might apply to that contest. For example, we may notify third parties of your name and address so that they can send you the prize.

E. E-Mail Services

Primagames.com may offer free e-mail services through its site. The use of these services will be governed by a separate agreement that you will be asked to accept when you sign up. You will be requested to provide certain personally identifiable information when you sign up for this service. Prima’s use of that information is described in more detail in the e-mail service agreement.

F. Surveys and Polls

Primagames.com may conduct user surveys or polls from time to time. You are not required to respond to such surveys or polls. We may share the general aggregate data from such polls with third parties to better understand the needs of our users and the effectiveness of the services and advertising on the Primagames.com site. We will share personally identifiable information from such polls with third parties, although we may store such information on our systems for use in developing services and features that suit our users’ needs and interests.

G. Use of Cookies

Like most other major Web sites, Primagames.com uses "cookies." Cookies are pieces of information that a Web site transfers to your computer's hard drive for record-keeping purposes. Cookies store information such as your viewing preferences, subscriptions, logons and passwords which make your Web-surfing easier and more enjoyable. Any information gathered using cookies is used to maintain the quality of our sites to make them better reflect the information needs of users who visit.

Most browsers are automatically set up to receive cookies. You can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or indicate when a cookie is being sent. If you do reset your browser, however, certain aspects of the Primagames.com site may not function properly or may operate more slowly.

H. Chat Rooms and Message Boards

Prima may establish chat rooms, message boards or usenets to facilitate the exchange of information between users of the site. Please be aware that any information you post is publicly available and not subject to the security or privacy protections of this site. Prima cannot be responsible for such information and you post any such information at your sole risk. For this reason, you should exercise common sense in disclosing any personally identifiable information in these areas of Primagames.com site.

I. Required Disclosures

Prima reserves the right to disclose any information provided by users of the site when it is necessary by law or when we feel it is necessary to identify, contact or bring legal action against someone who may be causing harm to the Primagames.com site or to anyone else that could be harmed.

OPTING-OUT

Information submitted by anyone over the age of 13 may be used for marketing and promotional purposes by Primagames.com. In order for you to be in control of your personal information, we provide you with the option to opt-out of the following services: receiving e-mail newsletters from Prima, receiving information from Primagames.com about new features or services and receiving information from third-party companies that have relationships with Primagames.com. Please send your e-mail requests to opt out of receiving such information to us at webmaster@primagames.com.

IF YOU'RE UNDER 13 YEARS OLD

Primagames.com is particularly mindful of the privacy rights of children and is committed to compliance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act that takes effect on April 21, 2000. We encourage parents and guardians to spend time online with their children and to participate in the activities offered on this site. No information should be submitted to or posted at this site by any person under 13 years of age without the consent of their parent or guardian.

Primagames.com may request personally identifying information from persons under the age of 13 years old such as name, address, e-mail address, parent’s e-mail address, and game-related interests and preferences. We use this information internally for several purposes: (a) to notify winners of contests and prizes; (b) to develop and enhance the design and layout of our site; and (c) to notify our users of features and services that may appeal to them. Primagames.com does not provide any personally identifying information about persons under 13 years of age to third parties, without notifying the parent or guardian and obtaining their consent to such disclosure.

Any person under the age of 13 that registers on the Primagames.com site or is requested to provide personally identifying information must provide an e-mail address for their parent or guardian. A notification message will be sent to the parent or guardian which notifies them of their child’s participation in the site and the kind of information being collected. Parents will be requested to signify their consent to the collection of the information. Parents will also have an opportunity to review their child’s personal information, to ask to have it deleted, or refuse to allow any further collection or use of their child’s personal information.

Visitors under the age of 13 years may be allowed to win contests and participate in promotions; however, if such a visitor wins, notification and prizes are sent to the parents or guardian identified in the initial registration process.

Note to Parents: Please contact us at webmaster@primagames.com if you want to know if you child has registered to use the Primagames.com services or if you wish to cancel your child’s membership.

IF YOU’RE ARE 13 TO 17 YEARS OLD

Primagames.com encourages parents of visitors who are 13 to 17 years old to be aware and monitor their child’s participation on the site. Such visitors are encouraged to give the e-mail address of their parents or guardians so that we can send them a message notifying them of their child’s involvement on Primagames.com. Visitors who are 13 to 17 years old may also be allowed to win contests and participate in promotions; however, if such a visitor wins, notification and prizes are sent to the parents or guardian identified in the initial registration process.

SECURITY AND SAFETY

Primagames.com will use reasonable care to protect the privacy and security of information that you provide to us once it is received on our systems. You should be aware, however, that no transmission over the Internet is entirely secure. Therefore, Prima cannot guarantee or warrant the security of information sent over the Internet. You send such information at your own risk.

In addition, your member and e-mail accounts with Primagames.com site are password protected. You should keep these passwords private and not give them to any other person, even your best friend. You should completely log off Primagames.com and close your browser window when you are finished using the site.

While Primagames.com cannot be responsible for your general safety while using its site or the Internet in general, we offer the following safety tips:

  • Don’t post personally identifiable information, such as your name and address or photos or yourself or your family, in any chat room, bulletin board or other publicly available area of Primagames.com site.
  • Keep your password private and secure.
  • Delete unknown e-mail attachments since they may contain viruses that might be destructive to your computer.
  • If you receive any unwelcome, threatening or offensive communications over the Internet, you should report it to your online service provider. Do not respond to the communication.

APPLICATION OF THIS POLICY

This policy only applies to the Primagames.com site. It is possible that when you are online at the Primagames.com site, you may receive messages or links to other sites that are beyond our control. These sites may send you "cookies" or request your personal information. Please be sure to review the privacy policies for these other sites before your give out such information. Primagames.com cannot be responsible for the safety or security of personal information that you disclose online to others and you do so at your own risk.

By using this site, you agree to the terms mentioned above. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use our site. This policy may be revised or changed from time to time. Your continued use of the Primagames.com site after the posting of changes means you accept those changes.
