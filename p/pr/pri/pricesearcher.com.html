4.2           We may receive and store certain information automatically when you interact with us. 5.              What about cookies?

5.1           We use cookies (or may do so in future). A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer’s hard drive. Cookies are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the site owner or others.

5.2           Session cookies are temporary cookies that remain in the cookie file of your browser until you leave the site. We use session cookies to recognize you while you are logged into our site. Session cookies are essential for our service.

5.3           Usually you can modify the settings of your browser to accept or reject all cookies or be notified when a cookie is set. For further information on cookies and cookie settings, including how to delete cookies, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.  Our site’s functionality will be limited if you reject cookies.
