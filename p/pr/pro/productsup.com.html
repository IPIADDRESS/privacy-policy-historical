Insight Pages:

Insight Pages can be based on personal data, which are captured in relation with a visit or interaction of a person on or with a page and its content.

Legal basis for the processing of personal data

Insofar as we seek the consent of the person concerned for the processings of personal data, art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR serves as the legal basis for the processing of personal data.

While processing personal data, necessary for the performance of a contract to which the data subject is party serves art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR as legal basis. This also applies to the processing necessary for the implementation of precontractual measures.

Insofar as a processing of personal data for the compliance with a legal obligations required, which is subject to our company, art. 6 para. 1 lit. c GDPR serves as legal basis.

If vital interests of the person concerned or another natural person require a processing of personal data, art. 6 para. 1 lit. d GDPR serves as legal basis.

If the processing for protection of a legitimate interest of our company or of a third party is necessary and the interests, fundamental rights and freedoms of the person concerned do not outweigh the first-mentioned interest, art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR serves as legal basis

Information obligation & rights of the data subject:

Facebook Ireland is obligated to novate the primary responsibility according to GDPR for the processing of Insights Data and to fulfil all duties of the GDPR in regards to the processing of Insights Data. This includes the information obligations in Articles 12 and 13 GDPR, rights of the data subject in Articles 15 to 22 GDPR and the security of the processing in Articles 32 to 34 GDPR.

Contacting

We forward requests regarding the rights of the data subject (art. 15 – 22 GDPR) to the primary responsible Facebook Ireland via an appropriate Facebook Page Insights Controller Form. This also includes requests for access to, the correction, the deletion and/or objections or the restriction of Insights Data as well as requests from data protection supervisory authorities relating to the processing of Insights Data within the framework of the General Data Protection Regulation.
