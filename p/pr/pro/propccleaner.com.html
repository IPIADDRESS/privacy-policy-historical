Privacy Policy

This privacy policy (the “Privacy Policy”) discloses how Rainmakers Software, LLC uses the personal information it gathers on this site. We will notify you of changes by posting the new policy on this website.

By using our services or submitting information, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy. Your assent to these practices is essential for us to provide our services. You can opt-out and decide not to provide the requested personal information. However, by doing so, we will be unable to contact you. You can also request that we not use any information you provided by emailing customerservice@propccleaner.com.

The personal information we collect includes your name and email address. Our primary use of your personal information is to respond to an inquiry you sent us through our website.

We automatically collect certain information from visitors to our site, such as Internet addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), referring and exit page, operating system, timestamps, and clickstream data. We also track and analyze non-identifying and aggregate usage and volume statistical information from our visitors and customers. This information is logged to help diagnose technical problems, and to administer our site so that we can constantly improve the quality of the services we provide. We do not respond to your browser’s “do not track” signals or other mechanisms for indicating your preference regarding the collection personally identifiable information.

A complete description of the information that is collected and used, as well as instructions on how to opt-out, are available at www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners/. We take measures to prevent the loss, misuse, and alteration of your information. We carefully limit access to the database in which your personal information is stored. Additionally, we share and transfer your personal information only in the ways set forth in this privacy policy.
