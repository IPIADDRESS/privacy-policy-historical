Promo Direct Privacy Statement

Promo Direct has created this privacy statement to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy. This policy describes our practices regarding personal information collected through our website or through any other means. We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy from time to time; the effective date noted below will indicate the date the policy was last updated. Please visit our website periodically to review any changes.

What happens to the information we collect from you?

All information you provide will be kept confidential. It is used to support our business relationship and the service we provide. This website uses an order inquiry form to allow you to request information, products, and services. We collect your contact information (such as your e-mail address or your company information) as it allows us to send you samples, orders, information about our products, or/and promotional materials. Your contact information is also used to get in touch with you when necessary. You can opt out of receiving future communications by e-mailing your request to info@promodirect.com

Personal Information Disclosure

Promo Direct will not give or sell any information you provide, to any third parties under any circumstances for its use in marketing or solicitation, without your consent. This protected information includes any information about you, your order, and your purchase history. Your information may be shared with contractors of Promo Direct, for the purpose of performing services for Promo Direct, related only to your order or sample request. Promo Direct may release personal identifiable information if and when required to by law, wherein we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal process.

Choice/Opt-Out

When you opt to receive communications from Promo Direct, you will receive information which we believe, will be of use and interest to you. For example:

  • We keep you informed about the latest deals, new service, sell-out specials, etc. that we think you will find valuable.

  • We may update you on any important changes to our website.

If you choose to opt out of our communication, you may do so in the following ways:
