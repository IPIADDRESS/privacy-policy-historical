1. What information of yours do OLGC Web sites collect?
The goal of OLGC's Web sites is to be your premier destination for OLGC lottery and gaming related information on the Internet . To achieve this goal, OLGC needs to collect information to understand what services and products best meet the needs of our consumers. OLGC collects two types of information, "anonymous information" and "personal information".

Anonymous information refers to information that is not about an identifiable individual. Each time a visitor comes to an OLGC Web site, OLGC may collect information that is necessary to improve the overall quality of the visitor's online experience. OLGC may collect computer and connection information such as the visitor's IP address (e.g., whether the user is logged on from a .ca, .com, .gov or .edu domain), referral data (e.g., the address of the last URL a user visited prior to clicking through to an OLGC Web site), and browser and platform type (e.g., a Netscape browser on a Macintosh platform). OLGC also collects aggregate statistics relating to page views to gauge the performance of certain areas of our Web sites. OLGC collects this user information for purposes of product monitoring and continuous Web site improvement. To collect and analyze this information, www.OLGC.ca utilizes a log analysis tool. Note that, www.OLGC.ca utilizes a log analysis tool together with a data-tagging technique to compile their aggregate, non-personally identifiable information. Data tags allow OLGC to measure which software plug-ins our consumers are utilizing, thus resulting in more effectively designed Web sites.

In addition to this anonymous information, OLGC may also collect information that is about an identifiable individual, referred to within as personal information. Personal information includes information that tells us specifically who you are, such as your name and email address.

OLGC collects personal information in the following ways from different parts of OLGC's Web sites:

  • Personal information is gathered when you voluntarily register with one of OLGC's Web sites. Specific functionality, such as winning number email alerts, requires registration information in order to provide the requested service. Upon registering, you will be asked to provide OLGC with your name, email address or other information as requested. Consumers can continue to utilize OLGC's Web sites without providing personal information however customized functionality will not be available.
  • In addition to registration for services at OLGC Web sites, OLGC will ask you for other personal information when you enter a sweepstakes, contest or promotion offered through an OLGC Web site.
  • If you contact the OLGC by email, we will keep a record of that correspondence, but only so that we may respond to the email.


2. What are "cookies" and does OLGC use them?
A cookie is a small text file containing a unique identification number that is transferred from a Web site to the hard drive of your computer. This unique number identifies your browser, but not the individual, whenever you visit one of OLGC's Web sites. These cookies will NOT let a Web site know any personal information about you, such as your name and address. Since these cookies are only text files, they cannot run on your computer, search your computer for other information or transmit any information to anyone. Cookies are used on many major Web sites. Many browsers are initially set up to accept cookies. If you prefer, you can reset your browser either to notify you when you have received a cookie, or to refuse to accept cookies.

To help serve you better, OLGC uses cookies to help us remember your choice of language preference, English or French, when visiting, www.OLGC.ca. In addition, cookies are utilized to set the pay-out calculator wager amount.

3. Who is collecting the information?
When you provide personal information at any OLGC Web site, that information is collected by OLGC. OLGC may offer sweepstakes, contests or promotions on OLGC Web sites. If you do not want to have OLGC share your personal information for such purposes, you can choose not to opt-in to the sweepstakes, contest or promotion.

4. How does the OLGC use the information it collects?
Any personal information you provide to OLGC when registering for products and services at any of OLGC's Web sites is maintained by OLGC and OLGC's authorized web hosting provider, unless specifically stated otherwise. Furthermore, only certain employees of OLGC have access to personal information submitted through OLGC Web sites. OLGC requests information from its users so that they can enjoy customized experiences across OLGC Web sites. OLGC may use the information it collects as follows:

  • To offer its users the ability to customize or personalize content on OLGC Web sites.
  • To deliver products and services requested by consumers.
  • OLGC also uses the anonymous, aggregate data to perform statistical analyses of the collective characteristics and behaviour of our Web site visitors. Without such data, it would be difficult for us to know what the most popular areas of the OLGC Web sites are, and it would be difficult for us to change and update the content and services appropriately. OLGC also uses such data to monitor and improve the operation of our Web sites and to maintain the quality of the products and services available through our Web sites.
  • If you enter a sweepstakes, contest or promotion through OLGC's Web sites, OLGC will use the personal information you provided to contact you in the event you win. All such OLGC contest entry forms will provide a way for a consumer to opt-in to any communications that is not related to awarding prizes for the promotion. Any information you provide will not be shared with such third parties unless you agree through the appropriate consent box on the entry form. If you enter a contest, you may be asked, under the rules of the contest, to agree that the contest sponsor may use your name in relation to announcing and promoting the winners of the contest.
  • OLGC may disclose personal information where required by the operation of law or by a judicial or governmental body.

Such entities will be required to use personal information provided solely for such purposes. OLGC does not sell, rent or lease customer lists generated through OLGC Web sites to third parties.

OLGC is not liable for any use or disclosure of your personal information that is beyond our reasonable control.
