Privacy Policy

This information is only used to fulfill our customers orders.

Financial information is collected and used solely for the billing of products and for services offered by our website.
During a purchase we collect your address, phone numbers, email address and credit card details, which is used solely for shipping and contact purposes.

An order history of your purchases is also maintained, enabling tracking of deliveries and reviewing your past purchases. Based on your order history we may create special offers or purchase rewards.

We collect general information regarding the number of 'hits' to our website and monitor traffic patterns that help us build a more user-friendly site. We do not track individual user pathways through the site!

Our site uses 'cookies' - small pieces of code sent to your web browser by a web server. They do not harm your computer in any way. These small text files can be found inside your 'Temporary Internet Files' folder under Windows. Cookies keep track of the items in your Shopping Cart and act as temporary place markers when you move around the site. If set your Security Settings in Internet Explorer to 'high' cookies are disabled. In order to use this site you need to set the security level for the Internet to Medium (under Tools/Internet Options). This provides "Safe browsing but still functional." Alternatively you can add this site to your list of trusted sites.

If you have any concerns regarding our site, please contact us and we`ll be glad to discuss any issues with you.

We NEVER exchange, sell or otherwise give away your email address to any other companies. Your email address is used solely by you, for purposes of logging into your account. Your email address is used solely as a means for contacting your about your orders and the newsletter.

In order to complete your order we have to disclose some of your information to service suppliers, including EMS and USPS.

When you place an order online, your personal information and credit card or debit card information is encrypted using SSL encryption technology, before being sent over the Internet.
