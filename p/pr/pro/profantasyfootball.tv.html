Privacy Statement

We have created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices of SilentWorld Ltd.

We use your IP address to gather general page view statistics. Your IP address is also captured by our secure payment provider to protect against fraud. The IP address will not be used for any other means.

Personal Information

Our site's registration form asks you to provide your email address. We use the contact information gathered from the form to send you information relating to the game and about upcoming games and services.

From time to time we purge old data from our databases. If you leave the game for a prolonged period of time the information we hold about you is destroyed.

This site contains links to other sites. SilentWorld is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

Choice/Opt-Out

You may opt out of playing the game at any time by sending an email to info@silentmanager.co.uk We delete all information relating to you when your game details are removed.
