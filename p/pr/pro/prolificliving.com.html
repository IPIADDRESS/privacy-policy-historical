Cookies
A “cookie” is a small data text file that is placed in your browser and allows you to be recognized you each time you visit this site. Cookies themselves do not contain any personal information, and Prolific Living Inc. does not use cookies to collect personal information. Cookies may also be collected by some of the services used on this website like Facebook Like Buttons, Twitter Tweet buttons, etc.

Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user’s prior visits to your website. Google’s use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
Users may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Information on how to opt-out of other third-party vendor’s use of cookies can be found by visiting aboutads.info.
