Privacy
What Information Do We Collect?
When you register with our site, we will collect your name, country, company name, company type and job description, e-mail address and phone numbers. We also monitor customer traffic patterns and site use which enables us to improve the service we provide.
What Do We Do With the Information?

We will use the information provided by you to promote a more personal service. We are required by best practice to follow strict procedures to protect customer information.

As some of our computer systems are based outside the country, it may be necessary to pass on some of your personal data outside Nigeria for compliance purposes. By agreeing to the terms and conditions you accept that such transfers will take place.

We may use this information to tell you about changes to our site and let you know about our service and those products and services of other companies we work with. If you do not wish to receive this information you may email us at unsubscribe@proshareng.com and we will remove your account.

We would not pass the information we collect about you to any partner or strategic company even when their products and services are thought could be of interest to you. We may however send their information to you if you ticked the send me product offers box. if you change your mind, you can simply send us an e-mail at club@proshareng.com specifying your request to be removed from the mailing list to receive product offers.

What About Cookies?

"Cookies" are small pieces of information that are stored on your computer's hard drive by your browser. These allow service providers store your details and provide features to make your use of the Service easier. Most browsers allow the cookie function to turn off.

We currently do not use cookies on our service but request that you kindly bookmark the page and save your password on your system for ease and convenience of retrieval and logging in to MyProshare.

Further, we advice that you set your browser to allow mails from Proshare; otherwise important mails and updates may drop into your bulk folder or be deleted completely.

For those using company addresses, we advice that you contact you IT department to obtain necessary permissions to receive this updates in compliance with best practice e-mail download/access requirements.
