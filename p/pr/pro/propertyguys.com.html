PropertyGuys.com Privacy Policy
Updated January 2004
1) Our Objective

Our customer's privacy is important to PropertyGuys.com. This policy is our guaranteed commitment to the protection of any personal information collected and how it is used and disclosed.

2) Collection and Use of Personal Information

PropertyGuys.com collects personal information such as names, addresses, contact information, and specific homeowner and property details. Such information is provided to us by the customer and is used for the marketing of a property, the identification of a property for a potential buyer, or the promotion of our home marketing services.


3) Disclosure of Personal Information

PropertyGuys.com does not sell any information collected about our customers. PropertyGuys.com only discloses personal information on a strictly confidential basis:
  1. To our corresponding Head Office and or industry associates affiliated with our system to complete transactions initiated by you the customer.
  2. If you request or consent to the disclosure.

4) Storage and Security of Information

Each PropertyGuys.com franchisee maintains his or her own client information and is not an employee of PropertyGuys.com Inc. All PropertyGuys.com client files are kept physically at the office of each franchise location, at PropertyGuys.com Inc. Head Office, and electronically on PropertyGuys.com computer systems. We insure that access or alteration to personal information is limited to those individuals who have a valid business reason to access the information.

5) Have Questions?

PropertyGuys.com is committed to its customers. We welcome and encourage your feedback. If you have any questions or comments regarding this privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by sending an e-mail request to the Privacy Department or in writing to: Privacy Officer, PropertyGuys.com, 128 Highfield Street, Suite 210, Moncton, NB, E1C 5N7.
