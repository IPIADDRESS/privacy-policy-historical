By using our Site, you agree to follow and be bound by the following privacy policy.

Users may be asked for an email address. Users may, however, visit our Site anonymously. We will collect personal identification information from Users only if they voluntarily submit such information to us. Users can always refuse to supply personally identification information, except that it may prevent them from engaging in certain Site related activities.

In application of the Data Protection Act of January 6th, 1978 (loi Informatique et Libertés), ProRealCode carries out all formal declarations of its data processing and will only carry out processing for purposes which are compatible with the Site and the Services. We may collect non-personal identification information about Users whenever they interact with our Site.
