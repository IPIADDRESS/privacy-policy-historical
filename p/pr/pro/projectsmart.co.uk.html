Privacy Policy

Project Smart created this privacy policy to show our firm commitment to your privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for our website.

Our website uses cookies. A cookie is a small text file that we put on your computer. These cookies allow us to anonymously distinguish you from other users of the website, which helps us to provide you with a good experience and also allows us to improve our site. Cookies, by themselves, cannot be used to find out the identity of any visitor. Project Smart will only store information provided voluntarily, and this information will never be kept online or made available to anyone else.

We occasionally display advertisements on our site. These may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our website, cookies received with advertisements are collected by third parties, and we do not have access to this information. For detailed information about how we use cookies see our Cookie Policy.

Our feedback and newsletter forms require visitors to give us contact information in the form of name and email address. We use visitor contact information from these forms to send the information requested and nothing else. Your personal information will never be sold or given to anyone.

This site contains links to other sites. Project Smart is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these websites.

Our site uses a third-party to process payments securely. Financial information collected is used to bill the customer for the products and services requested. Project Smart does not have access to any customer's personal financial details. Information about purchases will not be disclosed.

If you have any questions about this policy, please feel free to contact us.
