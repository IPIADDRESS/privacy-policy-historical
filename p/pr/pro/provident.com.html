Privacy and Security

Provident Funding Associates, L.P., also doing business as Provident Funding Group, Inc., Provident Home Loans and PFG Loans, Inc. (collectively "PF") is committed to respecting the privacy rights of all users of its website. The following policy describes how PF uses and protects the information that is provided or collected through this website.
Does PF disclose any of the personally identifiable information that it collects from its visitors to outside parties?
PF may share the non-public information of our customers and former customers (but not of consumers who are only visitors to our site who have never registered and obtained a user name and password). To print an opt-out notice, please select click "Form", from the "My Account" section of our website.
What information does PF collect and how is it used?
You may visit and use certain features of our site without any registration in which case none of your personal information will be collected or retained. However, to access your account or other non-public information on our site, as an existing or former PF customer, you will need to register by creating a user name and password. To register you are required to provide your email address, last name, property zip code and your social security number for identification purposes. You will also have to select a username and password.

If you are applying for a loan on our site for the first time, you need to complete a loan application by providing necessary personally identifiable information, including contact, property, employment, income, assets and liabilities information, as well as various additional demographic information and declarations.

Whether you are a new or an existing customer, the information you provide allows PF to establish a user profile so that we may customize your preferences, send you the information that you desire and establish security measures to ensure that only you, and those with your authorization, have access to your account. It will allow you, as a customer, to access your application information and status. It also allows us to provide a faster method for you to submit and receive information to and from PF. We may also use your computer's IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to otherwise administer our site, as well as to gather broad aggregated demographic information such as the number of visitors from a particular geographic area.

How does PF protect visitor information?
We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the privacy of your information. Personally identifiable user information is contained behind secured networks and is only accessible by a limited number of employees who have special access rights to such systems. We offer the use of a secure server when you post or access your information. All sensitive information supplied by you will be transmitted and protected via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) software and the HTTPS protocol for encrypted transfer of data.

To ensure compliance with updated security technology, PF recommends that you use one of the following browsers which can be downloaded free of charge: Netscape Navigator (http://www.netscape.com), or Microsoft Internet Explorer (http://www.microsoft.com/ie). To ensure the highest level of security available today, PF suggests that you choose the 128-bit encryption and most updated version from the download options. If you do not have access to a browser that supports at least 48-bit encryption, your information may not be processed by our servers, or may not be securely transferred through your web browser to our servers.

When you submit information to PF, or to any other website, over the Internet the information may travel over many networks and systems that are not under our control. The Internet is not completely secure and others may intercept the information that you are sending. Further, anything that you voluntarily provide in any public non-secure area of the Internet, including on our website, will be publicly available to other visitors on that website and potentially to other third parties. You should always exercise caution when deciding to publicly disclose any of your personal information.

Does PF use "cookies"?
Cookies are bits of electronic information that a website can transfer to a visitor's hard drive to help to tailor and keep records of his or her visit to the site. Cookies allow website operators to better customize visits to the site based on the visitor's preferences. The use of cookies is standard on the Internet and many websites use them. Although most web browsers automatically accept cookies you can usually change your browser to prevent or notify you whenever you are sent a cookie. This gives you an opportunity to decide whether or not to accept it. Even without accepting a cookie you can still access most of the features on our site. We use cookies so that we can provide our customers and registered users with a better and more efficient experience on our site as well as to provide for a faster method of logging-in to your account. We suggest that you visit http://www.cookiecentral.com if you would like more information about cookies.
PF’s special note to parents
PF is specifically a financial services website that does not contain any content directed to children under the age of 13. However, to the extent that any child under the age of 13 enters our site, pursuant to the provisions of the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), we are not permitted, and therefore do not, collect any personally identifiable information from any child that we actually know to be under thirteen years of age without the consent of that child's parent or legal guardian.
How does a registered user remove or modify the information that they have provided to PF?
After registering on our site, you may choose to remove your registration information from our database so as to no longer interact with those features of the website which are available only to registered users. You will have to re-register if you later decide that you want to access such areas of our site. Simply submit any request to unregister through the web page located at http://www.providentfunding.com.
Third party links and co-brand relationships
PF may choose various third party websites to link to and from, and frame within, its own site to provide increased value to our visitors. PF may also participate in co-branding and other relationships to offer information, programs and other services and features to its users. However, PF has no control over these linked sites, each of which have separate privacy and data collection practices independent of PF, even if such third party is affiliated with PF.

Data collected by our co-brand partners or third party websites, even if it is offered on or through our site, is not received, stored, or used in any way by us. As such, we have no responsibility or liability for these independent policies or actions and are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of any such websites. These linked sites are only for your convenience and you access them at your own risk. Nonetheless, PF seeks to protect the integrity of its website, and the links placed upon it, and we therefore request your feedback on our website and any that we link to as well (including if a specific link does not work).
