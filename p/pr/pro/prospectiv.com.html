Privacy Policy
 

Prospectiv's Privacy Policy

Prospectiv takes privacy seriously. As a leading provider of online marketing solutions, we are committed to establishing and maintaining policies which protect the privacy of all information gathered from our customers, prospects and other visitors to our website.

Prospectiv collects information from site visitors through the following methods:

Registration

Prospectiv collects personally identifiable information only when you:

  1. register for premium content such as a whitepaper
  2. complete a registration form to learn more about our products and services

This information is only used by Prospectiv to contact individuals about the services on our site for which they have expressed interest. Prospectiv will not sell, rent, loan or share any personal information collected on our site with any other organizations.

Log Files

Log files are generated on all web sites on the Internet, including Prospectiv.com. These files are set according to the standards for HTTPD web server technology as described by the standards organization, W3C (WorldWideWeb Consortium). These Log files contain 'clickstream information' (i.e. transactions about the web pages viewed during a session, including the originating web site/web page, navigation to another web site/web page, the type of browser used, date/time, and success status of the server transaction, and the originating IP address of the page request).

Links between pages are described using a URL (Universal Resource Locator) address, e.g. http://www.prospectiv.com. Each page viewed has a unique address that is recorded as a transaction in the server log. Transactions about all pages viewed on Prospectiv.com are used for marketing analysis and website administration. The log entries from Prospectiv do not record personally identifiable information.

Cookies

Cookies are a standard internet technology that allow us to store and retrieve the login information on your system. Cookies, by themselves, cannot be used to determine the identity of any of our users.

Cookies are used on Prospectiv.com for session management, so that the web pages presented to you are consistent. These cookies are not tied to any personally identifiable information.
