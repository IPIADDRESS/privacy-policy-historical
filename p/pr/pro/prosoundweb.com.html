Privacy Policy

ProSoundWeb has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for our websites: ProSoundWeb.com, LiveSoundInt.com, ChurchSoundMagazine.com, srforums.prosoundweb.com, recforums.prosoundweb.com, marsh.prosoundweb.com, networkforums.prosoundweb.com, and Live-Audio.com and any and all subsidiary sites, hereafter referred to as “our site” or “this site.”

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our site. Your IP address is used to help identify you, track the state of your session and to gather broad demographic information.

Our forums are membership based and require a log-in for participation. The required demographic information gathered during the log-in process is utilized for marketing purposes to generate advertising revenue. By this we mean that we use general demographic information to describe the average PSW visitors in an attempt to encourage manufacturers to utilize the site for advertising purposes.

We may also use this information to query individuals about interest in participating in additional on-line activities (on-line surveys, e-newsletters etc…). Members will be asked if they are interested in any of these additional opportunities. Once asked and answered, you will not be asked again about the same opportunities (we will not bug you to death with unwanted e-mails). If members choose to participate they may opt out of participation at any time. Many sections of the site (including the forums) use cookies to keep track of your session and to authenticate to certain areas of the site, such as our forums. We use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests and to save your password so you don’t have to re-enter it each time you visit our site.

We will never sell our user list—any information provided to us will be kept confidential at all times.

Our site’s registration form (for classifieds) also requires users to give us contact information. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.

This site contains links to other sites. ProSoundWeb is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

Our site may use an order form for customers to request information, products, and services. We collect the visitor’s contact information (like their email address) and financial information (like their account or credit card numbers) as needed given the order request. Any financial information that is collected is used to bill the user for products and services requested.
Our online surveys ask visitors for contact information (like their email address). Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.
