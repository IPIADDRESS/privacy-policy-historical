Privacy Statement

PromoPro UK is committed to safeguarding your privacy online. Please read the following policy to understand how your personal information will be treated as you make full use of our offerings. This policy may change from time to time so please check back periodically. This Privacy Statement only covers the site PromoPro UK.

Personal Data

Only a user's email address is collected when users provide registration to PromoPro UK. By registering, you agree that PromoPro UK may send you periodical emails promoting vouchers and specials from the PromoPro UK merchant base that may be of interest to you. Email addresses are never shared or sold to third-party marketers or third-party agencies.

Aggregated Information

Aggregated information, such as which page users access or visit, and information volunteered by users is collected through various methods. For example, PromoPro UK collects IP addresses; an IP address is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer whenever you are surfing the Web. Web servers (the computers that "serve up" Web pages) automatically identify your computer by its IP address; when you request a page from PromoPro UK, our servers log your IP address.

PromoPro UK only uses IP addresses for the purposes of Web traffic analysis and trends.

Use of Cookies

PromoPro UK uses cookies for this site. A cookie is a small data file that most major websites write to your hard drive for record-keeping purposes. Cookies allow PromoPro UK to measure activity on the site and to improve your user experience, for example by remembering your passwords and viewing preferences.

Children and Privacy

PromoPro UK encourages parents and guardians to spend time with their children online, and to be fully familiar with the sites visited by their children.

Individuals under 13
No information should be submitted to or posted on PromoPro UK by users under the age of 13 years without consent of their parent or guardian. PromoPro UK does not provide any personally-identifying information for users under the age of 13, regardless of its source, to any third party for any purpose whatsoever unless disclosed during collection.

Once you have successfully registered, it means you accept our privacy policy. PromoPro UK may send you voucher alerts and newsletters. If you want to opt out, just click "Unsubscribe Here" at the bottom of our newsletter or voucher alert.
