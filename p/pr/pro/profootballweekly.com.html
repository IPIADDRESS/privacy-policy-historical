Privacy Statement

Your personal privacy is important to us at ProFootballWeekly.com. The information that we gather and how it is used is described below. This policy applies only to Pro Football Weekly. Be sure to check the privacy policies of any sites that advertise with us as they may differ from our privacy policy.

About IP Addresses

An IP address is a number that's automatically assigned to your computer when you connect to the Internet. This address is used to serve the pages that you request to your computer. IP addresses are used by us to count unique visitors, to diagnose problems with our server and to administer our web site. They are also used to report anonymous aggregate information to third parties. Your IP address is not connected to your personal information and so, when we use IP-related data, we are looking at a logged user session but we do not know who you are.

About Cookies

Cookies are used by many sites including ProFootballWeekly.com to customize your experience on the site. To do this, bits of information called cookies are placed on your computer's hard drive for tracking purposes. You may disable cookies on your computer; however if you do so, you may not be able to use certain features of our site.

Anonymous Aggregate Information

As part of the tracking process, ProFootballWeekly.com will collect anonymous aggregate information and may share it with advertisers and affiliates for the purpose of providing the most beneficial content possible to our visitors. This information includes such items as the number of visitors to the site, access times, referring web site addresses and demographic information (such as age, gender, etc.), all of an anonymous nature.

Personal Information

There are many things that can be done on our site without providing any personal information to us at all. There are also instances where we may offer you the opportunity to register for premium services, complete a survey online or to enter a contest or a sweepstakes. In these situations, we may ask you to provide personal information such as email address, full name, street address, telephone number, hobbies, and preferences. This information will be used to contact you or for internal purposes and will not be shared with any party outside of Pro Football Weekly.

We also may use personal information to display custom, personalized content to our visitors or to send newsletters, emails, promotional materials and other updates on a periodic basis. A user may, at any time, opt out of receiving such information.

Links to Other Sites

ProFootballWeekly.com may offer links to other sites so that you can conveniently visit these sites. However, we are not responsible for the privacy policies of these sites and recommend that you review and understand the privacy statement of each site that you are on.

Security

Security of your personal information is very important to us. We use Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology to encrypt important information such as your credit card information in order to prevent unauthorized access. We incorporate standard industry practices internally and with our service providers to maintain the security of your personal information.

Children's privacy

The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) in the United States does not allow the collection of any personal information from anyone under the age of 13 who resides in the United States. Therefore, children under 13 should not submit any information about themselves via forms. We will not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If you submit such personal information, you warrant that you are over the age of 13 and are not subject to COPPA.

Your agreement

Your use of this web site constitutes your express consent to the collection and use of information as described in this privacy statement. This privacy statement is subject to change. Any material change in the manner in which we handle personally identifiable information will be prominently posted prior to implementing the change. Your continued use of the site more than 30 days following the posting of changes to these terms will mean that you accept those changes.
