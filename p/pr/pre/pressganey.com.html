Privacy Policy

Press Ganey Associates, Inc. and all affiliates ("Press Ganey") respect the privacy of all patrons who use our web sites. Our Privacy Policy reflects our intent to protect the private information of all users of our web sites. We urge you to please read this privacy policy as, together with the terms and conditions, it will form the basis for your use of Press Ganey web sites.

Except to the extent required by law, we will not sell, lease, or release your name, address, telephone number, e-mail address or other personal data without your consent. Press Ganey does not contribute to or participate in cooperative databases, which give other companies access to such personal information.

By accessing and using this web site you will be deemed to have read and accepted the terms and conditions, together with the practices set out in this privacy statement.

The information we collect from you may include personal data. We will only collect and process such information as is appropriate and necessary according to the type of services you wish to establish with us. To the extent that the information you provide to us includes personal data, you agree to Press Ganey processing all such personal data for the purposes of providing requested services.

Press Ganey's corporate web site (http://www.pressganey.com) collects and stores only personal information that clients and visitors knowingly provide.

Online Behavioral Advertising (“OBA”) Notice

In order to provide you with a better, more personal experience on our Site, Press Ganey (“PG”) uses an Online Behavioral Advertising BA platform called Eloqua to identify how we can better serve your interests. Through Eloqua, PG asks for and maintains the personally identifiable information provided by you to deliver certain services or information to you. “Personally Identifiable Information” means any information that identifies you personally, including, without limitation, your name, e-mail address, password, login ID and other contact information that you voluntarily submit to us. You can also choose not to enter any of their personally identifiable information.

Information Gathered
PG will gather information such as what kind of browser you are on, what operating system you are using, your IP address, cookie information, time stamp (time page accessed as well as time spent per web page) and your click stream information (for example, which pages you have viewed and how long you have been there). Note that information is only gathered while you are on our Site. PG monitors how you arrive at our Site, but cannot and will not gather information about other third party sites you have visited.  PG automatically receives and records such information on our server logs from your browser.

Cookies
PG employs cookies in order for its server to recognize you as a return visitor and unique user. Cookies are small text files placed in the “Cookies” folder on your hard disk and allow PG to remember you. The cookies placed are readable only by PG, and cookies cannot access, read or modify any other data on a computer. All web browsers offer the option to refuse any cookie. If you refuse the cookie, PG does not gather any information on you, but doing so will require you to re-enter certain information at each visit, or prevent us from customizing the site's features according to your preferences. You may also delete a cookie manually from your hard drive. Cookies themselves do not contain any personal information. We do link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information you submit while on our site.

OBA Opt-Out
We provide you the opportunity to “opt-out” of having your personally identifiable information used for certain purposes, when we ask for this information. If you would like to opt-out please send an e-mail to optout@pressganey.com.
