Cookies. A "cookie" is a string of information that is sent by a website and stored on your hard drive or temporarily in your computer's memory. Although you can disable cookies from your web browser, doing so will prevent you from taking advantage of some security features and customization on the Prefixmag.com Website.

 

Use of Anonymous Data

 

We use a service provider to track and analyze anonymous data for purposes of generating statistics regarding the use of the Prefixmag.com Website. We do not link this anonymous data with your personal data.
