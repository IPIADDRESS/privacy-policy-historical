PRIVACY AND SECURITY POLICY



This Privacy and Security Policy is provided for the benefit of existing and prospective customers of www.prep4usmle.com (“Website”) provided by Prep for USMLE, as well as other consumers and parties who seek information from or about the Website and/or Prep for USMLE. Please read this Privacy and Security Policy carefully.



1. OPENING STATEMENTS


1.1 Prep for USMLE
This Privacy and Security Policy governs the privacy and security practices of Prep for USMLE and its website www.prep4usmle.com.

1.2 Applicability of Privacy and Security Policy
Our Privacy and Security Policy applies to all individuals and entities from whom or for the benefit of whom we receive personally identifiable information (also commonly referred to as personally identifying information) (“PII”), whether the PII be collected through our websites; traditional mailing and/or faxing channels; telephone or electronic communications; from some other medium; or from our customers.

1.3 Our Commitment to Privacy
At Prep for USMLE, we respect the privacy and the confidentiality of our customers’ PII and financial information. We are committed to keeping confidential the PII and financial information you share with us. We promise to use PII and financial information only for the purpose for which it has been collected. This Privacy and Security Policy explains how Prep for USMLE collects, uses, discloses and protects the information we obtain.



2. PERSONAL INFORMATION COLLECTED AND HOW IT IS USED


2.1 Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is any information that would, by itself, identify you or tend to identify you as a particular individual or entity. This personally identifiable information may include, but not be limited to, your name, your address, credit card number, bank account information, and/or personal identification number (such as a social security number or driver’s license number).

2.1.1 Personally identifiable information does not include any public information that is readily attainable through federal, state or local public record distribution channels.

2.1.2 Personally identifiable information does not include any information that, by itself, could not identify you as a particular individual or entity.

2.1.3 Personally identifiable information also does not include raw data that becomes aggregated for statistical purposes and cannot, thereafter, be identified with you as a unique individual or entity.

2.2 Information Collected

2.2.1 Prep for USMLE collects information that is considered PII and other information that, by itself, would not be considered personally identifiable information.

2.2.2 Typically, Prep for USMLE limits the PII collected about an individual or entity to the individual’s or entity’s name and electronic mail (“e-mail”) address.

2.3 Collecting and Using Personal Information

2.3.1 Prep for USMLE will explain how it intends to use your personally identifiable information either before it collects the information (such as in this Privacy and Security Policy) or contemporaneously with the solicitation of such information about you. Exceptions may exist where the intended use is obvious to a reasonable person.

2.3.2 We will use the information collected only for the purposes explained to you. Further, we limit the collection of personally identifiable information to only that information which is necessary for the purposes explained to you.

2.3.3 Unless we are required to do so by law, we shall not obtain your consent before collecting personal identifying information about you from parties other than Prep for USMLE. Rather, Prep for USMLE shall presume that you have previously provided such consent to any such third party from whom Prep for USMLE obtains such information.

2.3.4 Prep for USMLE restricts the use of your PII to Prep for USMLE’s personnel.

2.3.5 We shall not sell your PII in the normal course of business operations. Should the Website be sold, this paragraph shall not restrict the transfer of your PII as part of the Website sold to a third party.

2.3.5. We may use your e-mail or regular mail address to send you communications about your transactions and our company as well as to notify you of relevant product news, new products and services, or other information that we believe may be of interest to you. If you do not wish to receive electronic communications unrelated to your transactions with Prep for USMLE, you may “opt out” of the electronic distributions upon receipt of any such communication. To discontinue receipt of email communications, you can change preferences in your Profile Controls.

2.3.6. At the time of registration, Prep for USMLE allows, but does not require, you to provide additional information such as any “instant messaging” handles, a brief biography of yourself, or links to your website(s). Please be advised that this information is visible to other Prep for USMLE consumers.

2.4 Disclosing Personal Information

2.4.1 General. Prep for USMLE may provide your personally identifiable information to other persons, but only where we have your consent or where we have obtained information about you from a third party (presuming that you have previously provided such consent to any such third party from whom Prep for USMLE obtains such information) or where we are required or permitted to do so by law.

2.4.2 Strategic Partners. Prep for USMLE may employ, engage or retain certain trusted third parties (“Strategic Partners”) to perform functions and/or provide services on its behalf. Prep for USMLE will share PII with these strategic partners only to the extent necessary to perform the functions and/or provide the services for which they have been employed, engaged or retained. Prep for USMLE shall also secure contractual obligations from our strategic partners to maintain the privacy and security of the PII shared with them.

2.4.3 Business Transfers. Prep for USMLE reserves the right to sell, transfer or otherwise share some or all of its assets, including your PII, in connection with a sale of assets; a merger, acquisition, or reorganization; or, filing for bankruptcy. You will have the opportunity to opt out of any such transfer if the planned processing of your information by the purchaser differs materially from that set forth in this Privacy and Security Policy.

2.4.4 Legal Compliance. Prep for USMLE may disclose any information about you to government or law enforcement officials or private parties as we, in our sole discretion, believe necessary or appropriate to respond to claims or legal process (including subpoenas); to protect the property and rights of Prep for USMLE, its strategic partners or other third parties; to protect the safety of the public or any person; to prevent or stop any illegal, unethical, or legally actionable activity; or, generally, to comply with the law.

2.5 Non-Personally Identifiable Information

2.5.1 In addition to collecting personally identifiable information as previously described, Prep for USMLE also collects other non-personally identifiable information (“non-PII”).

2.5.2 Non-PII collected by Prep for USMLE may be utilized without restriction.

2.5.3 Although non-PII can be used in a number of ways, you can be assured that the use of the non-PII cannot identify you as an individual or entity.

2.5.4 Because non-PII cannot be used to identify you, Prep for USMLE may in its discretion provide non-PII to other persons.

2.6. Information Obtained From Use of Prep for USMLE’ Websites

2.6.1. Cookies

2.6.1.1. Cookies can be defined as bits of data stored on your computer that allow our website(s) to identify your browser and session.

2.6.1.2. Prep for USMLE presently utilizes “Cookies”.

2.6.1.3. Cookies allow us to recognize certain information about you in an effort to provide an extra layer of security protection against unauthorized users.

Based upon your web browser’s security settings, you have the ability to reject all cookies.

2.6.1.4. Unfortunately, disabling or rejecting Cookies may alter your Prep for USMLE experience.
2.6.2. IP Addresses

2.6.2.1. Prep for USMLE maintains a log of your IP address when you access www.prep4usmle.com. This IP address by itself does not allow us to collect additional information about you. However, it allows us to see which parts of the site you visit and how much time you spend browsing the site.

2.6.2.2. OTHER THAN YOUR IP ADDRESS, PREP FOR USMLE DOES NOT DIRECTLY COLLECT ANY PERSONAL INFORMATION ABOUT YOU UNLESS YOU VOLUNTARILY PROVIDE US WITH THIS INFORMATION OR PLACE A FORMAL REQUEST FOR SERVICES ONLINE.
2.7 Data Retention

2.7.1. The length of time for which Prep for USMLE retains your PII and other information will vary depending on the nature of the services for which the information had been collected and the nature of the information.

2.7.2. Although this period may extend beyond the end of your relationship with Prep for USMLE, it will be retained for only so long as necessary for Prep for USMLE to respond to any issues arising from your relationship with Prep for USMLE.

2.7.3. When Prep for USMLE no longer needs or requires your personal information, Prep for USMLE will destroy, delete, erase or convert information to an anonymous form.

2.7.4. As all purchases from Prep for USMLE are made through third-party service providers, Prep for USMLE does not possess or retain your financial information.
