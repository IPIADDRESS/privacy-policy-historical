Cookies
Occasionally we use cookies on our Web site. Cookies are stored on your computer and send information back to us, letting us know whether you have visited us before or are a new visitor. The cookie is sent back to our site only and doesn’t contain any personal information about you or provide us with any means to contact you. In addition to visit information, a cookie may allow you to log in automatically to features on our network such as the Pregnancy Daily or the Baby Daily. It also allows you to store your birth plan at Birthplan.com. In general, cookies allow for a more personalized user experience.

If you wish to delete cookies from your computer, you can consult the operating instructions of your browser to do so. In some browsers, you can turn off cookies, preventing Web sites from setting them on your computer. No user will be refused access to our network based on allowance of cookies.
