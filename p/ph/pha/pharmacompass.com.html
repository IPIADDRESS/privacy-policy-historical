    10. Cookies have been used in our website. A cookie is a piece of software code that an internet web site sends to your browser when you access information at that site. The cookie makes it easier for you to use the dynamic features of webpages. Cookies from our webpages only collect information about your browser's visit to the website; they do not collect personal information about you. Our website may use either single session (temporary) or multi-session (persistent) cookies. Session cookies last only as long as your web browser is open. Once you close your browser, the cookie disappears. Persistent cookies are stored on your computer for longer periods. If you do not wish to have session or persistent cookies placed on your computer, you can disable them at any time from your web browser. If you opt out of cookies, you will still have access to all information and resources at PharmaCompass, but may be unable to use cookie-dependent features.
