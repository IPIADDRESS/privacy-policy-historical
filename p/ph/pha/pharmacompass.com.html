      • your server's address;
      • the name of the top-level domain from which you access the Internet (for example, .gov, .com, .in, etc.);
      • the type of browser you use;
      • the date and time you access the website;
      • the pages you have accessed and the documents downloaded and;
      • the previous internet address from which you linked directly to our website.
    4. We will not share your personal information, except-
      • when a law enforcement agency may exercise a warrant to inspect the service provider's logs
      • meet any applicable law, regulation, legal process or enforceable governmental request.

      • For detecting, preventing or otherwise addressing security or technical issues.
    5. Your email address will only be recorded if you choose to send a message. It will only be used for the purpose for which you have provided it and will not be added to a mailing list. Your email address will not be used for any other purpose, and will not be disclosed, without your consent.
    6. If you are asked for any other personal information, you will be informed how it will be used. If at any time you believe the principles referred to in this privacy statement have not been followed, or have any other comments on these principles, please notify the concerned through the “Feedback” page.

If you do not want your Personal Information to be used by our website as provided in this privacy policy, you should not register as a member or for any service that collects Personal Information. You may correct, update or review Personal Information you have submitted by signing in and updating your registration information. If you have registered and no longer want us to use your Personal Information, you should delete your information.

    10. Cookies have been used in our website. A cookie is a piece of software code that an internet web site sends to your browser when you access information at that site. The cookie makes it easier for you to use the dynamic features of webpages. Cookies from our webpages only collect information about your browser's visit to the website; they do not collect personal information about you. Our website may use either single session (temporary) or multi-session (persistent) cookies. Session cookies last only as long as your web browser is open. Once you close your browser, the cookie disappears. Persistent cookies are stored on your computer for longer periods. If you do not wish to have session or persistent cookies placed on your computer, you can disable them at any time from your web browser. If you opt out of cookies, you will still have access to all information and resources at PharmaCompass, but may be unable to use cookie-dependent features.
