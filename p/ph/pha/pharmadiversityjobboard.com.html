Privacy Policy

PharmaDiversity Job Board is committed to protecting the privacy of employers and job seekers using our service. Your privacy is very important to us. PharmaDiversity Job Board offers you the option of providing specific personal and professional information that will help in the selection process to match jobs with applicants. The information you provide is voluntary. PharmaDiversity Job Board affords you the opportunity to provide descriptive, cultural, behavioral, life style information about yourself, you decide the information you put forth .PharmaDiversity Job Board allows employers to view information you have provided. PharmaDiversity job Board does not sell e-mail addresses or information of our registered members. PharmaDiversity Job Board is an internet based online resume database for employers searching for diversity, military and sales experienced job candidates interested in securing employment in sales and sales management. The information posted by users is for the sole use of employers, recruiters, and human resource personnel who are searching for our targeted selection of qualified candidates for their job opportunities.

PharmaDiversity Job Board provides a job candidate referral service. If you choose to use this service and and tell a friend about the PharmaDiversity web site or refer a job posting to a friend, we will ask you for your friend's name and email address. We will automatically send your friend an email inviting him or her to visit the PharmaDiversityJobBoard website or view the job posting.

PharmaDiversity Job Board employs practices to grant access to this database only to paying employers, recruiters, hiring managers, and human resource professionals, but cannot guarantee that other parties will not, without our consent, gain access to this database. You may remove your resume from our searchable database at any time. However, employers and recruiters who have access to the database, and other parties who have otherwise gained access to the database, may have retained a copy of your resume in their own files or databases. We are not responsible for the retention, use, or privacy of resumes in these instances, or for the use or privacy of resumes by any of such parties while resumes are in the database.

PharmaDiversity Job Board resume posting and information software provides all our registered members a simple edit program so you can update, change add and delete your information at  anytime.

Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. We will ensure the proper safeguarding of your personal information.

Contact Information

This Privacy Policy may be modified from time to time. Any notices that reflect a change will be posted on this page. We hope this privacy policy clarifies our handling of your personal information.
