The Thaiger does not use e-mail to solicit business, nor does it sell (or give away) names, e-mail addresses or other information out of its databases without the express permission of the companies or individuals involved.

Our site’s registration form requires users (“Thaigers”) to give us contact information. This is used internally to determine the identity of parties placing classified ads in the Thaiger’s online classifieds, entering (or modifying) listings in the Thaiger Guide, or placing events on our Phuket Events Calendar.

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, to manage our website, and to gather broad demographic information. We use cookies to save your password so you don’t have to re-enter it each time you visit us.

This site uses Google Tag Manager, Facebook Analytics, OneSignal Push Notifications and CloudFlare Caching over secure SSL connections. These companies may share your data with their partners.

The Thaiger App

The Thaiger apps on iOS and Android do not require special permissions or store user data or tracking information.
