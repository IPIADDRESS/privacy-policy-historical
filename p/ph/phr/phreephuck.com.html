Part II

Ad-Serving Information
This Part II describes Phreephuck.com’s use of Personal Information and Anonymous Information that we collect in connection with the Phreephuck.com’s video advertisement serving technology. As used in this Part II, “you” and “your” refer to individuals who visit websites that use Phreephuck.com’s video advertisement serving technology (Network Sites). The collection, use and disclosure of information by Network Sites is governed by the privacy policies and practices of the Network Sites and their operators; Phreephuck.com does not control such policies and practices.

  1. WHAT IS AD SERVING?

In order to support valuable content without charging visitors, websites sell advertising space on their Web pages or within video content streamed on their Web pages. Companies like Phreephuck.com provide ads to websites and provide technology for website publishers and advertisers to display or to stream ads.

When you visit a website, your Internet browser transmits a “request” to that website’s server “asking” that server to send you the Web page that you are seeking. Most Web pages contain components that are pulled from different sources, for example, a Web page at a news site may get its weather section from one provider, its sports results from a different source, and advertisements from other servers.

If the website is a Network Site and is using Phreephuck.com’s technology to display ads on its site, the Web page will contain coding that directs your browser to fill the ad space on the Web page with content from one of Phreephuck.com’s ad servers. Phreephuck.com’s platform for streaming video ads enables advertisers to target their campaigns against particular contextual, geographic, interest or demographic criteria associated with the website or browser, and bid an amount they are willing to pay for this targeted impression. Each time a Network Site queries our platform for an advertisement, Phreephuck.com serves the most valuable advertisement to the publisher’s ad slot. Please note that Phreephuck.com does not control the privacy policies of the Network Sites, publishers and advertisers who use our services.

  1. COLLECTION AND USE OF AD-SERVING INFORMATION

Each Network Site includes video ad serving code on its website that enables Phreephuck.com to serve ads on the site and to place Phreephuck.com Cookies. Phreephuck.com Cookies are used to help manage ad delivery and frequency, and to identify audience segment(s) for customized advertising. Phreephuck.com does not collect or store any personal identification or sensitive information, such as your name, address, phone number, address, financial account information, social security numbers, or health information.

The video ads served may include instrumentation tags that enable Phreephuck.com to collect information about a person’s interaction with the ad for use in billing and analytical purposes. Each time one of Phreephuck.com’s servers receives a request for a video advertisement, Anonymous Information about the request is recorded for example, the date and time, the geographic location of the browser (based on IP address), the website and page to which the ad or image was delivered, any associated search terms, the Phreephuck.com Cookie ID, and the browser operating system. This information is used for reporting, analytical, modeling and targeting purposes, and also to better manage ad campaigns and the user experience, for example, to manage the number of times a particular user is exposed to the same advertisement.

Phreephuck.com may also make requests to multiple third-party data vendors for additional anonymous user audience information that is used for targeting, analytic and reporting purposes. No Personal Information is ever shared by Phreephuck.com with its third-party data vendors, although the third-party data vendors may provide Phreephuck.com with Anonymous Information that can be merged with existing Anonymous Information. Phreephuck.com only works with third-party data vendors who are members of the Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) which sets the industry’s standards for online privacy accountability.

Phreephuck.com also works with approved affiliates for the collection and aggregation of search terms used for audience modeling and targeting purposes. No Personal Information is ever shared by Phreephuck.com with these affiliates, but Phreephuck.com may share aggregate Anonymous Information with these affiliates. Phreephuck.com may retain and target keyword category associations for up to 30 days.
