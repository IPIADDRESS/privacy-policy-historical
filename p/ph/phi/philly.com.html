Cookies. Philadelphia Media Network, Inc. uses cookies to store and track your preferences to provide you with a more personalized user experience. You can set your browser preferences to reject all cookies but then you will not be able to participate in most customized services, and you may be required to repeatedly log in to participate in various services, promotions, or product offerings. Cookies make your experience easier by saving your preferences and passwords.

Most browsers can be configured to reject cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. Keep in mind that if you refuse cookies, it is possible that some portions of Philadelphia Media Network, Inc. sites may not function exactly as intended.

Philadelphia Media Network, Inc. may also collect IP addresses for the purposes of systems administration or to report information in aggregate form to our advertisers (e.g. how many visitors logged in to a Philadelphia Media Network, Inc. site). An IP address is a number that is assigned to your computer automatically when you use the Internet.
