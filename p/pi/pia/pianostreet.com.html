Your Personal Information

Any information the Consumer/User shares with us is strictly confidential and will only be used in the way the Consumer/User requests.
NO information will be shared or sold to a third party for any reason whatsoever.

Any Consumer/User may delete all his details from our customer database at any time by requesting so using the contact details below.

E-mail

When registering for an account your email will be stored and used for sending you essential information about your account.
Your e-mail address will NOT be shared or sold to a third party for any reason whatsoever.

If you have your account preferences set to receive our newsletters we will send you news, information about added content and special offers (approximately one e-mail per month).

If you do not want to receive newsletters and website information from us, please let us know by using the contact details below, by following the "unsubscribe" link in any of our emails or by checking the "Unsubscribe" setting in your membership profile at http://www.pianostreet.com/amember/profile.php.

Your e-mail address will not be visable to other forum members unless you deliberately chose to share it by changing the setting at http://www.pianostreet.com/amember/profile.php.

Credit Card and PayPal Transactions

All credit/debit card details are handled by WorldPay's (www.worldpay.com) secure servers and is not stored or processed by Op 111 Productions.
All PayPal (www.paypal.com) transactions are handled by PayPal's secure servers.

Cookies

We use client side cookies to store session information such as login/logout status and, if applicable, for tracking visitors referred by our affiliated advertisers.
