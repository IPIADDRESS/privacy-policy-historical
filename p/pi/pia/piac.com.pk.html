We are committed to protecting your privacy and implementing technology that gives you the most powerful, safe, online experience that you can get anywhere.

Your Privacy

Pakistan International Airlines Corporation (PIAC) or its affiliates may ask you to register and provide information that personally identifies you ("Personal Information") in certain circumstances, including for example when you register. We use your personal information in the following ways:

  • To help you quickly find services or information about PIAC products and services
    • \
  • To provide a mechanism for requesting information about PIAC's products and services
  • E-mail Correspondence - used to respond to and raise issues and inquiries relating to PIAC's products and services
  • Identify you when you wish to interact with PIAC representatives

We will only use your personal information in the ways specified when it was collected. We will not subsequently change the way in which we use your personal information, unless you consent to the new usage. In addition, only authorized PIAC employees will have access to some of your personal information. We do not otherwise share your personal information with third parties, unless you have granted us permission to do so.

Links to Other Websites

Our website has links to other websites on the World Wide Web. The privacy policies of these websites are not under our control. Once you leave our servers, use of any information you provide is governed by the privacy policy of the operator of the website you are visiting. It is advisable to read their privacy policies for further information.

What Security Procedures are in Place to Protect Information from Loss, Misuse or Alteration?

To protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control, we have in place appropriate physical, electronic and managerial procedures. For example, our servers are accessible only to authorized personnel.

Although we will endeavor to safeguard the confidentiality of your personally identifiable information, transmissions made by means of the Internet cannot be made absolutely secure. By using this website, you agree that we will have no liability for disclosure of your information due to errors in transmission or unauthorized acts of third parties.

Data Retention

PIAC and its authorized affiliates will not retain your personal information longer than is necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.

If for some reason you believe PIAC or its affiliates have not adhered to these principles, please notify us by e-mail at info@piac.aero, and we will use commercially reasonable efforts to promptly determine and correct the problem.

By using this website, you signify your assent to the Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use our website. Your continued use of the website following the posting of changes to these terms means you accept these changes.
