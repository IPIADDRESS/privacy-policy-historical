Passive Information Collection

Within the gaming environment, we may also collect information on your in-game activities, character development and character interaction.

We may also use technology to passively gather operational information about the technology you use, such as your browser, type of computer, operating systems, Internet service providers and the domain name of the web site that linked you to the Site.

In addition to the above information, when you access our Site on a mobile platform (e.g., iOS or Android), we may collect certain additional information such as your unique device ID (persistent / non-persistent), hardware type, media access control ("MAC") address, international mobile equipment identity ("IMEI"), the version of your mobile operating system, the platform used to access or download the Site (e.g., Apple, Google, Amazon, Windows), and Internet Protocol address.

We may tie personally identifiable information actively collected from you to information collected passively as described above or from third parties.

Do Not Track: Some web browsers may transmit "do-not-track" signals to websites with which the browser communicates. The webserver for our Site does not currently respond to these signals.

INTERACTION WITH SIGN-IN SERVICES AND SOCIAL MEDIA ACCOUNTS

From time-to-time, the Site may allow you to log in using sign-in services such as Facebook Connect, Apple’s Game Center, or an Open ID provider. These services will authenticate your identity and may provide you the option to share certain personal information with us such as your name, email address, and resume to pre-populate our sign up form. Once your identity has been authenticated, we may collect certain information such as the sign-in service used and a unique identification code associated with the sign-on service.

From time-to-time, the Site may also contain functionality which enables you to import profile information or contacts from third party services (e.g., LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter, Gmail, FourSquare, Instagram, Vine, etc.) (collectively, “Third Party Social Media Accounts”). The copy of the information received by us shall be subject to this Privacy Policy. However, any data maintained by such third party service and your use of that third party service will be subject to that third party’s terms of use and privacy policy. You may have the ability to post information from the Site to your Third Party Social Media Accounts (including, without limitation, by taking advantages of features such as the Facebook “like” button appearing on the Site). All such postings are also subject to the terms of use and privacy policy of the applicable third parties as well as the terms of this Privacy Policy.
