Privacy Statement

Piedmont Healthcare is dedicated to protecting the privacy of those who may visit the Piedmont web site. Any usage or personally identifiable information collected at this site is not shared with any organization or business that is not affiliated with Piedmont.

Piedmont does not sell personal information to third parties (i.e., companies or individuals that are not affiliated with Piedmont).

Piedmont does not disclose any personal information except as permitted and/or required by state law and Federal laws.

The only personal information collected at this site is as follows:

Pre-registration

If you choose to pre-register via our website for classes, events or clinical services, the personally identifiable information that is collected will be used by Piedmont Healthcare, Inc. and its affiliated organizations for the purpose of providing treatment, obtaining payment for treatment and various health care operations purposes, such as conducting ongoing quality improvement activities. Please see our separate Joint Notice of Privacy Practices for information about how your health information may be used and disclosed.

Aggregate Usage Information

This site automatically collects specific information on the number of visitors to our site and the pages viewed during the visits. The information thread for this purpose is not personally identifiable.

Other Sites

Our privacy policies and practices apply only to your use of our site. Our site may contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy policies and practices of these sites. You should read the privacy policies and practices of each site you visit to determine what information that site may be collecting about you.

Changes to Our Privacy Policies and Practices

We may change the terms and conditions of our Privacy Policies and Practices at any time by posting revisions on this site. You agree to review the Privacy Policies and Practices portion of this site each time you visit so that you are aware of any changes made to these policies and practices.
