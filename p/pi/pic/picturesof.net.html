We do not collect any personal information, such as your name, address, phone number, or email address unless you choose to subscribe to a mailing list or subscribe to a pay service, in which case all personal information is freely contributed by you, the visitor. No credit card information is kept on the publicly accessible web server.

We do note details such as the server you are logged onto, the geographic location of the ISP you use, the type of browser you use, and your IP address. This information is obtained solely for the purpose of measuring site traffic so that we can tell which pages are most poular and how visitors are moving about the site.

PicturesOf.NET may send your computer a "cookie," a small file that resides on your computer's hard drive. Cookies allow us to keep track of your preferences and choices. For example, if you add pictures to a shopping cart, we will send your computer a cookie that identifies the images you have placed in your shopping cart. Cookies cannot identify a user, though they can identify a specific computer. You can set your browser's option to not accept cookies, but this may limit your access to certain features of our site.
