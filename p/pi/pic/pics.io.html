Cookies and traffic data

Cookies are small text files which are transferred from our website and stored on your computer's hard drive. It helps a website to 'remember' who you are. Cookies are used and relied on heavily for everyday use of the internet as well as allowing us to provide you with a good experience when you browse our site and also allows us to improve our site. You can disable cookies using your browser (see below), but please note that if you decide not to accept all of the cookies you won't be able to use our services. There are many different types of cookies so we have produced the below guide to help you understand what cookies we use on our site:

Session cookies

These are temporary cookies which are deleted when you close your browser or leave your session on our site. We use session cookies on our site and in some of our products and services to identify and track users and to store information about your preferences.

Persistent cookies

Persistent cookies enable our site to 'remember' who you are and to remember your preferences on our site. Persistent cookies will stay on your computer or device after you close your browser or leave your session on our site.

Web analytics cookies and similar technologies

Our site uses web analytic cookies. These cookies allow us to recognise and count the number of visitors and see how they move around our site. This helps us make our service to you better.
