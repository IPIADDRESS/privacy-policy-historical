What information does THE PICTURE PEOPLE collect?

Information that you provide to us

We collect information you provide when you visit our physical studios, register with us, open an account, place an order, upload photos, invite others to view your photos, send us an e-mail or call us, enter a sweepstakes or enroll in a club or service. This information may include the following:

• Your name
• Your mailing address
• Your e-mail address
• Your phone number
• The names and birthdates of your children
• Applicable Picture People membership program numbers

It may also include information you submit about other people; for example, the name and address (e-mail and postal) of other people you invite to the site, or ask us to send products or gift cards to. We also collect information you submit for the personalization of products, such as calendars or mugs.

Information collected through web technologies

We may track and collect your IP address, type of web browser you are using, your domain server, the type of computer you use and and use "cookies."

IP address

We do log IP addresses and we use them to monitor things such as the number of visitors to our web site. An IP address is a number that is assigned to your computer when you are using your browser on the Internet. Like most web sites, the servers that serve our web site automatically identify your computer by its IP address.

Cookies

We may utilize "cookies." A cookie is a file that can be placed on your computer’s hard drive by our web site. It may contain coded information that allows us to remember who you are and how you use our site. For example, a cookie may remind us that you are a registered user and tell us that you do not need to log in every time. We try to ensure that cookies do not contain personal information that may be identified by a third party reading the contents of a cookie. We can only use the i nformation in a cookie to identify you after the information in the cookie is combined with information kept by us. We use this information to develop a better site for our customers, and reserve the right to disclose it to third parties on an aggregate basis. Site usage patterns for individual customers are protected from disclosure. If you choose to set your browser to reject cookies, we will not be able to make auto-login functionality available to you, including giving you access to your address book without first entering your THE PICTURE PEOPLE user name and password.

Other information

We use technology that allows us to identify what type of browser you are using, the web site from which you enter THE PICTURE PEOPLE and the pages you view as you tour our site. This technology helps us compile aggregate statistics about our online guests and their use of THE PICTURE PEOPLE, which allows us to make improvements in design and content to better serve you. This technology does not identify you personally - it simply helps us compile statistics about our visitors and how they use our site.
