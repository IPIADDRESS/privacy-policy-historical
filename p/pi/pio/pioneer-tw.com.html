About Us

Introduction

Pioneer Group has been established in the southern part of Taiwan since 1999 with it’s headquarter located in Kaohsiung, the most beautiful harbor city, and factory in Sinying, Tainan County. Our team consists of Reputed Aquaculture Specialist, Professional Mechanical Engineer and Experienced Installing Technicians to fulfill the different Aquaculture requirements and develop the most ideal, innovative and cost effective equipments with its 100% attention on the quality, durability, performance and customers’ profitability.

Due to our insisting in strict quality control and upgrading our equipment continuously, which allow Pioneer Group being the well-known number one brand in the America Continent as well as expending its high market rates to the rest 4 continents rapidly, thus, more and more Pioneer Group products can be seen in those important Aquaculture territories worldwide.

Business Scope

  1. Aquaculture Equipment : Paddlewheel Aerators ("Teco" Motor made in "Taiwan" under ISO9001/ISO14001), Air-Injectors, Automatic Feeders, Roots/Rings Blowers (ISO9001), and Water Pumps (ISO9002).
  2. Cage Farming System & Accessories. (Turnkey Package Offered !)
  3. Automatic Grits Manufacturing Machines, Mixer Machines, Fish Chopping Machines, Fish Mincing Machines.
  4. Power Control Panels (ISO9001).
  5. Electrical Cables (ISO9001).
  6. HDPE Pond Liner (ISO9002).
  7. Aquaculture Consultant Service… etc.
THE COMPANY'S PHILOSOPHY IS TO PROVIDE TOP QUALITY PRODUCTS AND GENUINE "PROFESSIONAL" SERVICES TO THE CUSTOMER & FULLY SATISFY THEIR NEEDS IN THE MOST ECONOMICAL WAY!

As the American Continent’s most distinguished Taiwan-Made Paddlewheel Aerator brand, Pioneer Group equipments have been selling globally for many years and obtaining the sound reputations and satisfactions from the clients, furthermore, several hundreds of its Cage Farming Systems have been widely used not only by the largest tilapia farm in the world but also some governmental projects in the Asian Continent due to its insisting on the "Professionalism, Top Quality, Unique Design & Attractive Price".
