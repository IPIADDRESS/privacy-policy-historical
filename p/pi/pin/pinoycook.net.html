Privacy policy

Posting a comment requires that you provide your e-mail address in a special required field. The system also records your IP address. Your privacy is valued and assured; no private information will be shared with third persons nor used in any way that compromises your privacy.

However, if you do post your e-mail address within the body of your comment text, it will be publicly visible and any invasion of your privacy resulting therefrom is not the responsibility of this blog’s owner. If you have any comment that you wish to relay privately to the blog owner, please send an e-mail to foodie(at)homecookingrocks(dot)com.

From time to time, your feedback may be asked (never required). Rest assured that your participation and responses will not in any way be used to invade your privacy but merely to gather relevant collective information to make this site better and more responsive to its readers.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please see: http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp.
