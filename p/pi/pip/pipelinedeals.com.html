Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy

PipelineDeals, Inc. ("PipelineDeals") is committed to protecting your privacy. We take privacy very seriously here at PipelineDeals and post this PipelineDeals Privacy & Security Policy ("Policy") to explain our policy and what PipelineDeals does with the information we collect from you which may identify you (referred to in this Policy as "personal information") when you visit and use our website and the applications and services offered by PipelineDeals (the "Services").

What Information We Collect:

Our vision at PipelineDeals is to introduce visitors to the benefits of our services and to facilitate contact management for all of our customers ("Account Holders") and our collection and use of your personal information is intended to help us achieve these objectives.

During your visit to the PipelineDeals website, we (or third parties acting on our behalf) may capture certain personal information about you:

  • upon sign up with PipelineDeals (for a trial or for purchasing the service), you may be required to provide your name, billing address, email address, password, and login id (which you create in the registration process);
  • we may capture your company name, billing phone number, time zone, date format, time format and credit card information so you may subscribe to and pay for the Service, but we do not store any credit card information. All credit card information is stored by a third party credit card processor. All recurring credit card charges (either monthly or yearly) are authorized in advance by the customer;
  • periodically, PipelineDeals may conduct webinars or other similar web events in which personal information may be collected (through our website or a third party service such as Citrix's Gotomeeting). In order to participate in such events, you are required to provide your name, email address, number of employees, and how you heard about the web event; and
  • PipelineDeals may capture the following personal information: address, phone number, industry, organization name and role in the purchase process.

Any personal information you or anyone with your login id and password upload into the Services in the form of files, contact information, industry knowledge, maps, financial information, and the like remains your sole and exclusive property, and we will not authorize any third party to access that personal information except pursuant to this Policy or where you have provided your explicit consent for us to do so. Please note that this information is also subject to the PipelineDeals Terms and Conditions of Use which may be found at: http://www.pipelinedeals.com/terms.

Why We Collect Your Information:

We use personal information we collect about you or that you provide to us to:

  • present our website and its contents to you;
  • provide you with information, products or services that you request from us;
  • deliver or perform our products or services;
  • enhance or improve our products or services;
  • provide you with notices about your account;
  • provide the requested services and improve the website and deliver a better more personalized service to you;
  • carry out our obligations and establish, exercise or enforce our rights arising from any contracts entered into between you and us which may include where we reasonably consider it is in our legitimate interests or the legitimate interests of others, or where we are legally required to do so;
  • in any other way we may describe when you provide the information; and
  • for any other purpose with your consent.

Legal Basis for Use of Your Personal Information:

We consider that the legal bases for using your personal information as set out in this privacy policy are as follows:

  • our use of your personal information is necessary for our legitimate interests or the legitimate interests of others (for example, to ensure the security of our website). Our legitimate interests are to:
    • Provide a business to business tool for Contact Relationship Management for our customers.
    • Provide a mechanism for businesses to contact and communicate with other businesses.
    • Provide product communications to our customers regarding product features and enhancements
    • Provide service status and outage information
    • Provide product training and tips.
    • Provide records of communication between PipelineDeals and our Customers to ensure customer satisfaction with our product.
  • our use of your personal information is necessary to perform our obligations under any contract with you (for example, to fulfill any order which you place with us, to comply with the terms of use of our website which you accept by browsing our website and/or to comply with our contract to provide services to you); or
  • our use of your personal information is necessary for complying with our legal obligations (for example, providing information to government tax authorities).

Other use may be subject to your consent (which can be withdrawn at any time after giving it, as described below). Where this is the case, that need for consent will be identified in this Privacy Policy.

Website Navigational Tools and How they are Used:

As you navigate the PipelineDeals website (whether or not you register as a user), personal information may be collected automatically through the use of commonly used information gathering tools such as cookies (imbedded code that recognizes your browser), web beacons (clear electronic images that can recognize certain types of information) and other existing or future tracking technology. Additionally, we collect other information from your Web browser, such as browser type, your internet protocol address and actions you have taken on our website (i.e., pages viewed and clicked links).

Cookies are small text files that a website can write to your computer. We may use cookies to identify you and facilitate subsequent access to the website. Flash cookies are local stored objects on your computer that collect and store information about your preferences and navigation to, from and on the PipelineDeals website.

Web beacons are small electronic files that permit PipelineDeals to collect website statistics such as how many users visited the website. Such information is used to determine the popularity of certain website content and verify system and server integrity.

Internet Protocol Addresses are collected by PipelineDeals to track and aggregate non-personal information. These addresses are collected when you log in.

Where we use cookies on our website, you can set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent. If you disable or refuse cookies, please note that some parts of this site may then be inaccessible or not function properly.

By continuing to use our website, you are agreeing to our use of cookies.

How We Keep Your Information Secure:

PipelineDeals has implemented appropriate organizational and technical measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access including a secure, encrypted connection (called an SSL connection) on all pages where you transmit personal data to us. Whenever you access any of your account pages or change any of your user information, it is done over an SSL connection to PipelineDeals. All data transfer to (upload) or from (download) our website will be via over a SSL connection using a current certificate.

In addition, your data is stored at our secure data centers. We maintain our servers in secure data centers in two (2) separate geographical locations. The data centers are staffed twenty four (24) hours per day, seven (7) days per week. Your data is replicated in both data centers in real-time thus always providing a redundant backup of your data in the event that the Service should fail.

In addition, if the redundant servers should fail, PipelineDeals backs up data every four (4) hours to a separate backup production facility.

If you sign up on our website, you will create your own log in id and password that is known only to you and PipelineDeals. PipelineDeals strongly discourages the sharing of your login id and password with anyone. You, the Account Holder, are responsible for the safety and security of your login id and password.

Retention of Personal Information

There are two types of personal information collected in the PipelineDeals CRM. First, we collect our customer contact information during registration in order to maintain our relationship with them and provide our software to them. We delete our customer records after 2 years from the time of subscription cancelation. Our software allows our customers to collect similar types of information about their own customers and enter it into the PipelineDeals CRM. This customer data is deleted 6 months from the time of subscription cancellation.
