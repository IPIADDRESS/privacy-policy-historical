iSpring Privacy Policy

iSpring Solutions, Inc. respects your right to privacy and we understand that, as a visitor to our corporate website and sites of our products, you prefer to control the way you share your personal information and preferences. iSpring Solutions, Inc. provides this Privacy Policy ("Policy") to inform you of our practices about the way your information is collected online and how that information is used.

Collection of Personal information

Throughout this Policy the term "personal information" means information relating to an identified or identifiable individual (i.e. a natural person), such as your name, phone number, company name, email address. "iSpring Solutions" refers to iSpring Solutions Inc., including its affiliates (also referred to as "we", "us", or "our"). In order to give you superior customer service, iSpring Solutions collects your personal information in several ways.

To improve our products and services, we may also ask you to provide us with information regarding your personal or professional interests, demographics, and experiences with our products or services. Providing this additional information is optional.

By using this website and/or by submitting personal information to iSpring Solutions, you agree to the processing of your personal information in the manner provided in this Policy. If you do not agree with this Policy, please do not use this website or provide iSpring Solutions with your personal information.

How Your Personal Information is Used

iSpring Solutions uses your personal information for the purposes described in this Policy. Please note that one or more purposes may apply simultaneously.

  1. Provision of products and services. We may use your personal information to deliver services and information (such as technical support, events, and training) you have requested; to develop our products and/or services; and for internal research to ensure the site is relevant to your needs. We may also use your personal information to provide you with service more relevant to you; for example, to display customized content in our website. We may combine personal information collected in connection with your use of a particular iSpring Solutions product and/or service with other personal information we may hold about you, except where such personal information was collected for a different purpose.
  2. Communicating with you and marketing. We may use your personal information to communicate with you; for example, to provide information relating to our products and/or services you are using, to notify about product updates or to contact you for customer satisfaction queries. We may use your personal information for marketing or research purposes, for example, to conduct market research and we may, in accordance with applicable law, communicate information to you regarding new products, services, and special offers available from iSpring Solutions.

If you do not wish to receive information from iSpring Solutions, please let us know by emailing us at: info@ispringsolutions.com. In some cases, especially if you wish us to delete or cease the processing of your personal information, this may also mean that we may not be able to continue to provide the services to you.

When We Disclose Personal Information

iSpring Solutions will not sell, rent or lease or otherwise disclose your personal information to third parties without your permission, unless otherwise stated below.

  1. iSpring Solutions may share your personal information with authorized third parties who process personal information for iSpring Solutions for the purposes described in this Policy. Such parties are not permitted to use your personal information for other purposes, and we require them to act consistently with this Policy and to use appropriate security measures to protect your personal information.
  2. iSpring Solutions may be obligated to disclose your personal information by law enforcement action, subpoena, or local law.

How We Protect your Personal Information

iSpring Solutions is committed to protecting the security of your personal information. While there are always risks associated with providing personal information, whether by phone, via the internet or otherwise, and no technology is completely safe, iSpring Solutions takes appropriate technical and organizational information security measures to to help minimize such risks.

Such measures include, where appropriate, the use of firewalls, secure server facilities, encryption, implementing proper access rights management systems, and other technically and commercially reasonable measures to provide appropriate protection for your personal information against unauthorized access, use or disclosure. Where appropriate, we may also take backup copies and use other such means to prevent accidential damage or destruction to your personal information.

We urge you to take every precaution to protect your personal information when you are on the Internet: change your passwords often, use a combination of letters and numbers, and make sure you use a secure browser.

Collecting Other Personal Information

When you browse iSpring Solutions's websites, you are able to do it anonymously. Generally, we don't collect personal information when you browse - not even your email address. Your browser, however, does automatically tell us the type of the computer and operating system you are using.

Like many websites, the iSpring Solutions websites use "cookie" technology. When you first connect to our sites, the cookie identifies your browser with a unique, random number. The cookies we use do not reveal any personal information about you. Cookies help us to register the most popular topics and pages of the site, as well as the time our visitors spend there. We use cookies to study traffic patterns on our site to improve and perfect it.

Non-U.S. Residents

Your contact request may be routed to an authorized iSpring Solutions partner in your local area.

Changes to this Statement

If there are updates to the terms of iSpring Solutions's Privacy Policy, we will post those changes and update the revision date in this document, so you will always know what information we collect online, how we use it, and what choices you have. We recommend that you review this Policy from time to time to learn of any changes to this Policy. Your continued use of our site signifies your acceptance of our Privacy Policy.
