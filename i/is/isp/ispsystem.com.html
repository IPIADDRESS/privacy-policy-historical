Privacy Policy

ISPsystem respects your privacy. We have established this Privacy Policy to disclose what information we collect about you and how we use and protect such information.

ISPsystem reserves the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time. If we make changes, the revised Privacy Policy will be posted on this website.

What information do we collect?

We collect personal information from you when you create a user account, make online purchasers, register products online, request support for ISPsystem products.

The types of personal information collected may include your name and/or your company's name, your home, billing, or other physical address, your email address, your telephone number, information that permits ISPsystem to make physical or online contact with you and any information that ISPsystem collects online from you and maintains in association with your account.

What do we use your information for?

Any of the information we collect from you may be used in one of the following ways: create and maintain your accounts; process, fulfil, and follow-up on your orders; register your products; answer your questions; send you information.

How do we protect your information?

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. ISPsystem does not share your Personal Information with unaffiliated third parties. ISPsystem will never disclose your Personal Information to an unaffiliated third party for the purposes of email or other marketing unless we first obtain your consent to do so.

Does ISPsystem use cookies?

Yes, ISPsystem uses cookies to keep you logged in after you log in to the Site. Otherwise, you would have to log in again for each page of the Site that you visit. Cookies are files web browsers place on a computer's hard drive.

Additional information

Questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the security measures taken by ISPsystem should be directed to ISPsystem by emailing marketing@ispsystem.com
