privacy policy

This is our privacy policy and aims to explain what information we collect and what it is used for. If you have any questions or concerns regarding this statement please email us at information@isleofman.com

We use the personal data you may provide solely for the reason that caused you to do so. For example, if you requested a brochure and submitted your name and address we will use that data for that purpose only and no other. We do not retain that information, or sell it or pass it to others.

We also use cookies to provide us with visitor and user statistics but that does not identify you as a person.
