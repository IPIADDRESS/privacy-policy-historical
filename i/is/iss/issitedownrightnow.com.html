What we do?

If a website is not working for you, there may be several reasons. There may be a fault at your end or the site could actually be down. In addition, there could be a condition of disruption causing site to not load properly. We made this tool to identify whether only you are facing problem or the website is down/facing disruption everywhere else.

How we do our job?

To know if a site's server is down or not working, two approaches are combined to get a result. Firstly, localized ping access is made. Secondly, direct user inputs are taken. Both inputs are then fed into an algorithm that decides the true status of any website.

Why our service is unique?

As told earlier, we tend to mix-up two approaches with respected weights – ping responses with localized servers and direct input from users. This approach helps us to give more genuine status of the given website.
