We highly respect your privacy and take special measures to ensure that any personal information collected by us is used solely in accordance with our Privacy Policy. It is important for us that you understand both what kind of information is collected and how it is used.
As part of the services granted by the Site, people using the Site may leave their private details on our registration form. We will not share this information with any non-affiliated third parties unless you specify otherwise.

Information may be collected in any of the following ways:

  1. Information you provide us with

At the outset of joining the services provided on the Site, you may be prompted to provide certain contact information, in order for us to be able to identify and/or contact you regarding our services and/or products. Such prompted information may include your first and last name, mailing address, e-mail address, phone number, Instant Messenger ID, and certain location information such as city, state, and/or zip code, as well as other necessary contact related information (“Personal Information”). For the purpose of this Privacy Policy, the term “Personal Information” means information that may be used either alone or in combination with other information, to personally identify an individual (as detailed in this section). Please note that you are not legally obligated to provide us with your Personal Information, and you acknowledge that providing us with your Personal Information is done by your own free will.

Here are some examples of information you may be required to provide us with when using the Site:

  1. Information We Collect from You

Kindly note that Personal Information may also include certain information which is not explicitly submitted by you, for example, as we log your browser-type related information, IP address, requested URL, referring URL, and timestamp.

Information, including Personal Information, is collected from you in the following ways:

  • Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies. The Site may use “Cookies” and other tracking services such as “web beacons” and pixel tags”. Upon your first visit to the Site, a “cookie” is sent to your computer that uniquely identifies your browser. A “cookie” is a small file containing a string of characters that is sent to your computer when you visit a website and is stored by your browser. Some of the cookies are set by eTeacher, however some may be set by third parties. Cookies cause no harm to your computer or to your web browsing experience; on the contrary, we use cookies and other tracking technologies to improve the quality of our service, to improve the user experience of the Site, to monitor our servers, to better understand how people interact with us (for example, by collecting statistical data about how you and other users interact and use the Site) and to provide you with advertisements. Please also note that our advertisers may also place or recognize cookies on your browsers.

You may delete cookies that have been set on your computer and/or change your browser settings in order to block cookies or to receive notifications when cookies are set. For more information, please consult your browser settings. Please note that if you choose to restrict or block cookies on your browser, you will still be able to use our Site, however, several features offered on the Site may not be available to you.

 

  • Device and Usage Data; Log Files. We collect information through server log files and tracking technologies within the Site. Such information is collected for the purpose of tracking user movement around the Site and user clicks-through other websites, gathering demographic information, analyzing trends, and to generally collect information that we may find insightful or helpful. Such information may, without limitation, include the following: IP and Mac addresses, the type of your browser, operating system and device, your browser version, screen resolution, user agent, language and identifier, URLs and traffic channels (such as the previous URLs or platforms you visited), keyword searches and bids, city, region code, country, latitude, gender, age, date/time stamp of Site use, Internet Service Provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, clicks, timing and statistical data for clicks and page views, and clicked pages. For example, our Site uses Google Analytics service (more information regarding such service can be found on: https://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/partners/).

By consenting to this Privacy Policy, you acknowledge that the information from you, as described in this sub-section titled “Information We Collect from You”, may also be used by third parties (as detailed in this Sub-Section) which may have their own privacy policies which are not necessarily adhere to this Privacy Policy.

If you allow your contact information to be public, you may receive unsolicited messages or activity from other users of the Site outside our control.
