PRIVACY POLICY STATEMENT

            

                This Privacy Policy explains how IBISWorld Pty Ltd. and its Affiliates (collectively "IBISWorld" “we” or “us”) may collect, use, manage and safeguard personal information through 
                www.ibisworld.com.au, www.ibisworld.co.uk, www.ibisworld.com, 
                www.procuremtentiq.com, www.ibisworld.ca, www.ibisworld.co.nz, 
                https://clients1.ibisworld.com, https://client.procurementiq.com/procurement/, 
                applications, and any other services provided by us (the “Services”).  
            

            

                
                We follow principles in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (the "GDPR") and worldwide practices for customer privacy and data protection.
                
            

        


        

            
INFORMATION WE COLLECT AND THE REASON FOR THE COLLECTION

            

                We do not collect more information than we need to fulfil the stated purposes listed below. And we will only use the information for that purpose. 
            

            
Types of Information We Collect:

            
Personal Data:
                We may collect information that identifies you (“Personal Data”).  It does not include data where the identity has been removed (this is anonymous data). The types of Personal Data we may collect includes:
            


            

                Identity Data: name 

                Contact Data: email address, billing address, telephone number, company title 

                Financial Data: credit card data or other financial account information 

                Technical Data: internet protocol (IP) address, browser type and version, time zone setting and location, browser plug-in types and versions, operating system and platform and other technology on the devices you use to access our websites 

                Usage Data: information about how you use our products, services and website 

                Marketing and Communications Data: your preferences in receiving marketing from us and our third parties and your communication preferences 

            

            
            
Sensitive Data:
                We will not collect, and do not want you to provide, any Sensitive Data revealing your racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious or philosophical beliefs, or trade union membership. We will not process any genetic data, biometric data, data concerning your medical or health condition and data concerning your sexual orientation.

SHARING OF YOUR INFORMATION

            

                We will only share Personal Data with others when we are legally permitted to do so.  When we share data with others, we put contractual arrangements and security mechanisms in place to 
                protect the data and to comply with our data protection, confidentiality and security standards.  
            

            
How We May Share Your Personal Data with Third Parties:

            
            
    
                
  • 
                    Affiliates. We may disclose the Personal Data we collect to our Affiliates where necessary for administrative purposes and to provide out Service to you. 
                    Their use and disclosure of your Personal Data will be subject compliance with the GDPR.
                
    • 
                
  • 
                    Vendors. We may disclose the Personal Data we collect from you to third party vendors and service providers who perform functions on our behalf in the normal 
                    course of business. Their use and disclosure of your Personal Data will be subject compliance with the GDPR. We will not share your Personal Data with any marketing companies. 
                    Each vendor has their own privacy policy. Please refer to our vendor list to see which vendors we will disclose your Personal Data in the normal course of business. 
                
    • 
                
  • 
                    In Response to Legal Process and Regulatory Purposes. We also may disclose Personal Data we collect from you in order to comply with the applicable laws, regulations, 
                    judicial proceedings, or other legal processes, such as in response to a court orders or subpoenas or to meet national security or law enforcement requirements and where we 
                    believe it is necessary for regulatory reasons. 
                
    • 
                
  • 
                    To Protect Us and Others. We may disclose the Personal Data we collect where we believe it is necessary to investigate, prevent or take action regarding illegal activities, 
                    suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the safety of any person, violations of any agreement between or this Statement, or as evidence in litigation in which 
                    IBISWorld is involved.
                
    •

How We May Share Your Personal Data with Third Counties and International Organizations Outside the European Union:

            
            
We are an international organization with our Affiliates located around the world including the United States and Australia. Additionally, we use third parties located outside the European Union (the “EU”) to help us run our business.  As a result, Personal Data may be transferred outside the countries where we and our clients are located.  This includes countries outside the European Union and to countries that do not have laws that provide specific protection for Personal Data.   We have taken steps to ensure all Personal Data is provided with adequate protection and that all transfers of Personal Data outside the European Union are done lawfully and, if you are located in the EU, by your consent.  

            

        


         

            
HOW WE STORE AND PROTECT YOUR INFORMATION

            

               We care about the security of your Personal Data and uses commercially reasonable physical, administrative, and technological safeguards to preserve the integrity and security of all 
               information collected through the Service.  
            

            
Storage and Processing: We strictly protect the security of your Personal Data and honor your choices for its intended use. We carefully protect your data from loss, misuse, unauthorized access or disclosure, 
                alteration, or destruction. The Personal Data collected through the Service may be stored in the European Union or any other county in which IBISWorld Affiliates maintain facilities, 
                including United States of America and Australia. 
            

            
Protection: We will maintain appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect your Personal Data from accidental loss, unauthorized use, 
                alteration, or disclosure The Service uses industry standard high encryption security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control 
                and all information is transmitted using Secure Socket Layer (“SSL”) certificates on our web server, giving you the ability to communicate securely with the website server. SSL security
                makes eavesdropping almost impossible. No credit card details are stored on the IBISWorld server. Although we implement and maintain security measures that are appropriate for our business 
                activities, please be aware that no security measures are perfect or impenetrable and any transmission of Personal Data is at your own risk.
            

            
Retention: If you are not a current client and you have submitted Personal Data, we will store your Personal Data for a period of two (2) years. If you are a client, and 
                you elect to cancel your subscription to our Service, we will store your Personal Data for a period of five (5) years for regulatory reasons.  If we process your Personal Data for various 
                purposes, it will be erased automatically or saved in an anonymized manner in which cannot identify you.
            

        


        

            
YOUR CHOICES ABOUT YOUR INFORMATION

            
Individuals have certain rights over their Personal Data and we are responsible for fulfilling these rights.

            
Access your Personal Data

            
You have a right to access Personal Data held by us.  This right may be exercised by emailing us at data.protection.office@ibisworld.com We will aim to respond to any requests for information promptly, and in any event within the legally required time limits.

            
Amend your Personal Data

            
To update Personal Data submitted to us, you may email us at data.protection.office@ibisworld.com. When practically possible, once we are informed that any Personal Data processed by us is no longer accurate, we will make corrections based on your updated information provided.

            
Withdrawal of your Consent

            
Where we process Personal Data based on consent, you have a right to withdraw consent at any time.  To withdraw consent to our processing of your Personal Data please email us at data.protection.office@ibisworld.com or, to stop receiving a marketing emails from us, please click on the unsubscribe link in the relevant email received from us.  

            
Other Rights including Deletion, Objection & Data Portability

            
This Privacy Policy is intended to provide information about what Personal Data we collect about you and how it is used.  As well as rights of access and amendment referred to above, you may have other rights in relation to the Personal Data we hold, such as a right to erasure/deletion, to restrict or object to our processing of Personal Data and the right to data portability.  If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please send an email to data.protection.office@ibisworld.com.  

            
Complaints

            
We will attempt to investigate and promptly resolve any disputes or complaints regarding the interpretation or compliance with this Policy. If you wish to file a complaint about your Personal Data, please send an email with the details of your complaint to data.protection.office@ibisworld.com.  We will look into and respond to any complaints we receive.

            
If you are located in the EU, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority, in particular in the Member State of your habitual residence, place of work or of an alleged infringement of the General Data Protection Regulation.

        


        

            
HOW WE USE COOKIES AND OTHER TRACKING TECHNOLOGY TO COLLECT INFORMATION

            
Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer by websites that you visit. They are widely used by us in order to make the IBISWorld websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to us about visitors use of the IBISWorld websites. Most web browsers allow some control cookies through the browser settings. 

            
To find out more about cookies, including how we use third party analytics tools, to see what cookies have been set and how to manage and delete them, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

        
  
            
        

            
LINKS TO OTHER WEB SITES AND SERVICES

            
Our Services may contain links to third-party websites and these websites are not subject to this Privacy Policy. We have no control of or responsibility for linked websites and provide these links solely for the convenience to you. We encourage you to read the privacy policies on the other websites you visit.

        
         
   
        

            
CHILDREN’S PRIVACY

            
We are committed to safeguarding our users' online privacy and recognize the importance of protecting the privacy of Personal Data, particularly that of children.  IBISWorld takes all required steps to protect the privacy and safety of children in accordance with the GDPR.  IBISWorld does not knowingly collect or solicit any information from anyone under the age of 13 or knowingly allow such persons to register as users.  In the event that we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under age 13, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.  If you believe that we might have any information from a child under 13, please contact us at data.protection.office@ibisworld.com.
