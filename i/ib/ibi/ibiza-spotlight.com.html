What Data do we Collect?

Dynamic Data

  1. HTTP cookies / Query-string portion of URI
  2. Full IP address
  3. HTTP request method
  4. Data bytes in response
  5. Response status code

By using our website you agree that we can set cookies and collect the above data for the following purposes:

  1. Anonymous user analysis.
  2. Web site and system administration.

This type of information is deemed non-identifiable. This means that there is no reasonable way for the site to identify the individual person this data was collected from.

The query string portion of the URI and HTTP Cookies are used to track referrals from Search Engines. Our Web Server collects access logs containing some of this information. We use it for maintaining and improving our website.

[Cookies are a technology which can be used to provide you with tailored information from a Web site. A cookie is an element of data that a Web site can send to your browser, which may then store it on your system. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it. We use cookies in a completely anonymous way to help track search engine referrals and incoming visitors from other websites.]

We use third-party advertising companies (Google) to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.

User Information

  1. We do collect some online contact information (email address) and sometimes the name and address and contact details (telephone, email address) from visitors who use our online hotel booking system, purchase club tickets, rent a car or book an airport transfer.

This data will be used for the following purposes:

  1. To make visitors aware, in a regular newsletter, of forthcoming events and products/services which we think may be of interest to them. The user is allowed to opt-out at any time. Clear instructions to unsubscribe are sent with every mailing.

Facebook Apps Privacy Policy

  1. On our facebook pages we have created two apps which are loaded within an iframe which contain links back to our site. We do not collect any user information within these apps. Your use of Facebook in general and your relationship with Facebook are subject to Facebook's own Privacy Policy and Facebook’s other terms and policies, which Ibiza Spotlight S.L. does not control.

Our Policy

With one exception, this data will be used only by ourselves. Ibiza Spotlight will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone.

Exceptions:

We send the emails of customers who purchase a ticket for Pacha to Pacha every month, so they can be included in the Pacha newsletter database.

We also send the emails of customers who purchase a ticket for Together (Tuesdays at Amnesia - 2012 season) to the promoters of the event: Lock'N'Load Events.

Dispute Resolution

We have the following dispute resolution mechanisms.

Ibiza Spotlight complies with the Organic Law 15/1999 from 13th of December regarding Personal Data Protection, as confirmed by the Spanish Data Protection Agency. All disputes regarding the access to and storing of personal user information will be handled by our office. You may contact us using the contact form on this page.
