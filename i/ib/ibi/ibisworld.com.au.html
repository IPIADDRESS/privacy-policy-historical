Privacy Policy

To protect your privacy, IBISWorld.com follows principles in accordance with worldwide practices for customer privacy and data protection.

1. Notice
We will ask you when we need information that personally identifies you (Personal Information) or allows us to contact you. Generally this information is requested when you are asked to register before downloading free software, entering a contest, ordering e-mail newsletters or joining a limited-access premium site. We use your Personal Information for four primary purposes:

  • To make the site easier for you to use by not having to enter information more than once.
  • To help you quickly find services or information on ibisworld.com.
  • To help us create content most relevant to you.
  • To alert you to product upgrades, special offers, updated information and other new services from IBISWorld.
2. Consent
If you choose not to register or provide personal information, you can still use most of IBISWorld.com. But you will not be able to access areas that require registration.

If you decide to register, you will be able to select the information you want to receive from IBISWorld.com by subscribing to various services, like our electronic newsletters. If you do not want IBISWorld.com to communicate with you about other offers regarding IBISWorld products, programs, events, or services by e-mail, postal mail, or telephone, you may visit the IBISWorld.com and establish your preferences in the Newsletters and Contact Options sections.

3. Access
We will provide you with the means to ensure that your personal information is correct and current. Registered users may review and update this information at any time using the IBISWorld Administration Module.
  • View and edit personal information you have already given us.
  • Subscribe or cancel subscriptions to electronic newsletters about our services and products.

4. Our use of cookies
Cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to your computer's hard disk for record-keeping purposes. Most Web browsers are set to accept cookies. However, if you do not wish to receive any cookies you may set your browser to refuse cookies. In some instances this may mean you will not be able to take full advantage of parts of the Network.

Cookies in and of themselves do not personally identify users, although they do identify a user's browser. The cookies simply operate as a unique identifier which help us to know what our customers find interesting and useful on our website, almost like a continuing questionnaire. We do not link this information back to other information that you have provided to us.

5. Secure Online Purchases
IBISWorld.com uses Thawte SSL certificates on the IBISWorld web server, giving you the ability to communicate securely with the IBISWorld web server. SSL security makes eavesdropping almost impossible. No credit card details are stored on the IBISWorld database.

6. Security
IBISWorld.com strictly protects the security of your personal information and honors your choices for its intended use. We carefully protect your data from loss, misuse, unauthorized access or disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

7. Enforcement
If for some reason you believe IBISWorld.com has not adhered to these principles, please notify us by e-mail to webmaster@ibisworld.com, and we will do our best to determine and correct the problem promptly. Be certain the words Privacy Policy are in the Subject line.
