Privacy

By: Sahil Umatia | Posted on: Sunday, January 09, 2011


We Respect Your Privacy 
We are never going to reveal, share, sell or rent your personal details including your name, email, address and telephone number.to any other individual outside iBlognet. We have a strict spam policy. We won't send you any emails unless and until you subscribe to receive our email updates.



Cookies
A "cookie" is a small data text file that is placed in your browser and allows iBlognet to recognize you each time you visit this site. Cookies themselves do not contain any personal information, and iBlognet does not use cookies to collect personal information. Cookies may also be used by 3rd party content providers such as news feeds. 


Third Party Advertising Cookies
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. Some of them like Adsense uses the DART cookie to serve advertisement based on user interest and previous interaction. Though user can always opt out of DARt cookie settings by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.



Visitor Tracking
We use third party software such as Google Analytics and Sitemeter to track your IP (Internet Protocol) Address, Browser Type, Screen Resolution, OS Type for statistical purposes. We only use this information for statistical purpose and we do not share this information with any individual outside iBlognet.

Commenting Policy
We love comments and at iBlognet, every reader is allowed to comment. We have a strict spam policy. Spam comments will deleted immediately. Any comments which is abusive, speech hatred, racial or any way hurting anyone opinion will not be entertained. We keep our comments moderated to maintain the integrity of every individuals. We hold all rights to accept or reject any comments based on our discretion. Off-topic comments will not be approved.

We will never ask for your Credit Card details by email or through any means. We respect your privacy and do the best to protect your privacy.


Please feel free to contact us if you have any query or suggestion through the "Contact Us" form.
