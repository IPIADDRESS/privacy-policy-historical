At IBPS your privacy is of the utmost concern to us. That is why we are dedicated to caring for your personal information and are firmly committed to assigning it the highest level of security and confidentiality. The following describes details on information gathering on this site and our use of your information.

Personally Identifiable Information
IBPS collects shopping specific customer information in an attempt to provide the best on-line shopping experience. The company collects information such as the name, address and phone number of the customer. Also included are information used for billing and shipping of customer purchases. These and other necessary personal information are collected on forms when voluntarily submitted to us on the site and are used for customer processing. While some of the information collected is necessary, others are left to the customer’s discretion. However, failure to provide required information may lead to an inability to process orders or use particular site features. We do not share your customer contact information with third parties for promotional purposes.


Processing
In order to efficiently process and ship customer orders, it may be necessary to share client information with intermediaries such as product manufacturers and shipping/postal services. The information is used strictly for these purposes. E-mails are also used to send information about the ability or inability to process orders, shipping details and general correspondence to the customer.


Security
Security measures are in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. User information collected while registering is collected on secure servers. When purchases are made on our website, shoppers are transferred to a secure server using SSL technology (“Secure Sockets Layer”) supported by most major browsers to complete the transaction with their credit card. We do not have access to your credit card information, nor do we store it on our computers.

Purposes of law
When inquiries are made by law enforcement agencies IBPS reserves the right to use discretionary measures in providing such agencies with specific customer information. This is information pertaining to fraud or other criminal activity that may result in liability to the customer or IBPS.
Accessing and Updating Your Information
IBPS customers have the right to personally edit and update their on site information. Simply by logging on to their account profile a customer can edit name, contact or other information. Information dealing with past purchases cannot be deleted but will be deactivated by the company depending on specific client account activity. For security, data stored in databases is backed up and so complete removal according to customer requests should not be expected. However a client can have personal information removed from active databases.

Privacy Policy Changes
At IBPS we reserve the right to make changes at any time as needed to our privacy policy and these changes will go into effect immediately upon being posted on the site. Any specific deviation from this will be posted on the site. For further information regarding our privacy policy, you may contact us at privacy@ibps.com.
