Privacy Statement for Iridium Communications Inc.

Iridium Communications Inc. has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for the Iridium Communications Inc. web site.

Information Gathered

Your IP address and referral page are logged in order to help diagnose system problems and to administer our web site more effectively. This information is used to gather broad demographic information and to generate web site usage statistics. Statistic reports are never used to associate an IP address to any specific user or subscriber.

When a user requests further information about our services or products, contact information is requested in order to provide answers and solutions in a timely manner. Inquiries pertaining to pricing or product availability will be forwarded to an authorized Iridium Communications Service Partner, who will provide relevant feedback based on the user's request. Contact Information is not used for marketing or bulk mailings and is not released to any third-parties.

Security
This site has implemented the proper security measures in order to prevent the loss, misuse, and/or alteration of the information under our control.

Contact Information
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this web site, you can contact our Webmaster at info@iridium.com.
