Our Privacy Policy

At irishhealth.com we believe your privacy is paramount. Our policy is that we ask for basic information from visitors who wish to register with the site. This enables us to collect data about usage of the site for several reasons:

  • To improve the quality of the site and the experience it offers to visitors.
  • To collect information about the type of person who visits the site and their areas of interest.
  • To collect statistical data on the numbers of visitors to the site and the pages they view.
  • To offer visitors additional information about their areas of interest or about developments on the site.

We do not disclose any of your personal details to third parties. We do not sell or rent our e-mail lists.

If you wish to register with irishhealth.com you will be asked for your e-mail address and some basic information which helps us to categorise the groups of people who visit the site. We cannot identify individuals, but your e-mail address provides us with the means of transmitting information to you should you wish us to do so.

If you do not wish to register with the site, your visits are simply recorded with no data attached to them. Please note that some advanced site functionality is only available to registered users.
