Commitment

Registering IP addresses
 
For each visitor, our web server does not recognize any information regarding the domain or e-mail address. IP addresses are logged and used identify our visitors.
 
 
Collecting e-mail addresses
 
Our service collects email addresses belonging to senders and receivers for the purpose of logging and measuring usage. i-FileZ.com does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with nonaffiliated companies.

We may disclose your information if necessary to protect our legal rights or if the information relates to actual or threatened harmful conduct or potential threats to the physical safety of any person. The disclosure of personal information may be required by court or law enforcement officials.
 
 
Using cookies
 
i-FileZ.com uses cookies to store the preferences and tracking trends of users. You can always choose not to receive a cookie file by enabling your web browser to refuse cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie.
 
 
File transfer security
 
We accept all the possible security measures on our servers in order to prevent the loss of data, inaccurate use of information, as well as file damage. Files saved for transferring are available for viewing only to i-FileZ.com representatives or via the link that is given to the user who uploaded the file. Files saved on our server are deleted automatically after a certain period of time.
 
 
Credit card security
 
All purchases on i-FileZ.com are handled by a third party payment systems: PayPal.com. i-FileZ.com does not arrange and/or save numbers or any information from your credit cards.
