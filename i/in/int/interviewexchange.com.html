The Interview Exchange is committed to providing you with outstanding service for our customers. This privacy Statement discloses our privacy practices for the entire site www.interviewexchange.com
.
This Privacy Statement will inform you of:
· What information our site collects about you
· How we use and with whom we share the information we collect
· Security procedures we have in place to protect your information
· How you can correct or modify your information
· How you can contact a customer service representative at the Interview Exchange

Overview
Privacy is a very important concern to all users of the Internet. We at The Interview Exchange are well aware of and sensitive to the privacy concerns of our customers to our Web site.
If you are registering for a InterviewExchange account, you may be asked to provide certain personal information. This information is needed for the limited purposes of providing job search and recruiting services, and authenticating your identity in order to complete any online transaction. You should also be assured that the InterviewExchange does not provide or sell information about applicants customers or site visitors to vendors that are not involved in the provision of The Interview Exchange services.

Information Collection and Use
The Interview Exchange is the sole owner of the information collected on www.interviewexchange.com The Interview Exchange collects information from our users at several different points on our Web site.

Registration, Username and Password
In order to use this web site, as an applicant or an employer, you must first complete the appropriate registration form. A applicant user must provide contact information including name, email, and contact address as well as credit card information. Employer users must also provide relevant company information as called for on the registration form. Employer user has an option of providing company credit card information.

When you register, you will be prompted to select a User name and a User password. Your email address is your user name and you select a personal password. You are solely responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of Your User name and User password. If you forget your password, you can send us an email and we will send your password to the registered email address. You are obligated to complete those transactions that occur using Your User name and User password, whether such transactions are authorized or unauthorized. You agree that you will immediately notify The Interview Exchange in writing or by electronic mail of any unauthorized use or disclosure of Your User name or User password.

The InterviewExchange Services and website are only available to persons who are at least 18 years of age. The Interview Exchange may, at its sole and absolute discretion, refuse to accept a User's registration, and may, at any time after accepting registration, refuse to permit the User's continuing use of the Services and the Website for any reason at any time and in its sole discretion.

Statistics
When you visit www.interviewexchange.com our computers may automatically collect statistics about your visit. This information does not identify you personally, but rather about a visit to our site. We may monitor statistics such as how many people visit our site, the user's IP address, which pages people visit, from which domains our visitors come and which browsers people use. These statistics are used for internal purposes only. These statistics are used to help us improve the performance of our services.

Cookies/ Log Files/ Clear Gifs
A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's computer tied to information about the user. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on our site. We are using session ID cookies only. For the session ID cookie, once users close the browser, the cookie simply terminates. By setting a cookie on our site, users would not have to log in a password more than once, thereby saving time while on our site. If users reject the cookie, they may still use our site. The only drawback to this is that the user will be limited in some areas of our site. For example, the user will not be able to participate in viewing the public profile of other applicants etc

Like most standard web site servers we use log files. This includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement in the aggregate, and gather aggregate demographic information for analysis. IP addresses are linked to personally identified information upon login. For log file analysis we are currently not using any particular software. We are not employing any clear gifs.

Customer Service
The InterviewExchange maintains a customer service capability to respond to employer and applicant questions including questions on privacy. Persons may contact an Interview Exchange customer service via email at privacy@adamshand.net Please allow us for 4-8 business hours for prompt reply.

Legal Disclaimer
Though we make every effort to preserve user privacy, we may be required to disclose personal information when required by law wherein we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a current judicial proceeding, a court order or legal process served on our Web site. We DO NOT Share Personal Information with Third Parties

Business Transitions
In the event The Interview Exchange goes through a business transition, such as a merger, being acquired by another company, or selling a portion of its assets, users' personal information will, in most instances, be part of the assets transferred. Users will be notified via email prior to a change of ownership or control and potential transfer of personal information to another business entity.

Links
This Web site may contain links to other sites. Please be aware that The Interview Exchange is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every Web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this Web site.

Processing Payments: (when you are using our payment services)
If you use The Interview Exchange Payment Services payment gateway for online transactions, we may provide your personal information to appropriate financial institutions, processors, and third parties under contract with The Interview Exchange for providing a subset of the payment services (for example, credit authorization, address verification). For financial institutions and processors, the use of personally identifiable consumer information is governed by federal and state privacy laws. For other third parties under contract with The Interview Exchange, the use and distribution of your personal information will be used by such entity for its internal use related to fulfilling the transaction services for The Interview Exchange. All information will be treated as confidential by this entity, and will be transferred between The Interview Exchange and this entity only via encrypted means.

Surveys
From time-to-time we may request information from customers via surveys. Participation in these surveys is completely voluntary and the user therefore has a choice whether or not to disclose this information. Survey information will be used for purposes of monitoring or improving the use of and satisfaction with this Web site, and improving our customer service.

Security
This website takes every precaution to protect our users' information. When users submit sensitive information via the website, your information is protected both online and off-line. When our registration/order form asks users to enter sensitive information (such as credit card number), that information is encrypted and is protected with the best encryption software in the industry - SSL. While on a secure page, such as our order form, the lock icon on the bottom of Web browsers such as Netscape Navigator and Microsoft Internet Explorer becomes locked, as opposed to un-locked, or open, when you are just 'surfing'. To learn more about SSL, follow this link http://www.verisign.com/products/site/faq/128-bit.html. While we use SSL encryption to protect sensitive information online, we also do everything in our power to protect user-information off-line. All of our users' information, not just the sensitive information mentioned above, is restricted in our offices. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job (for example, our billing clerk or a customer service representative) are granted access to personally identifiable information. The servers that we store personally identifiable information on are kept in a secure environment, behind a locked cage. If you have any questions about the security at our website, you can send an email privacy@adamshand.net

Correcting/Updating/Deleting/Deactivating Personal Information
If a user's personally identifiable information changes (such as zip code, phone, email or postal address), or if a user no longer desires our service, users may make changes or deactivations at the user registration/account information page.

Notification of Changes
If changes are made to this privacy policy, changes will be posted within this privacy statement. We will use information in accordance with the privacy policy under which the information was collected.
If, however, users' personally identifiable information will be used in a manner different from that stated at the time of collection we will notify users via email of such changes. Users will have a choice as to whether or not we use their information in this different manner. However, if users have opted out of all communication with the site, or deleted/deactivated their account, then they will not be contacted, nor will their personal information be used in this new manner. In addition, if any material changes are made in our privacy practices that do not affect user information already stored in our database, we will post a prominent notice on our Web site notifying users of the change.
