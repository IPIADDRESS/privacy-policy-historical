InterCall Privacy Policy
InterCall fully respects your rights to privacy. When you visit the InterCall site, we place a text file called a "cookie" in the browser directory of your computer's hard drive. The cookie enables the website to recognize any personal information you have consented to give the site. As a result, we are able to identify you when you return to the site. You need not disclose any personal information in order to gain access to any part of our site, except that you must be a current customer of InterCall with a valid PIN to access the "My Account" section.

Your name, address and contact information is used only for mailing materials you have requested, for informing you about product and service offerings that may be of interest to you, and to help us monitor the effectiveness of our site. If you respond to offerings made on our web site or participate in one of our surveys, you may rest assured that we do not sell, rent or distribute this information in any way to any other organizations.

Our server cannot automatically collect the e-mail addresses of visitors who do not provide them to us. If you choose to provide us with your e-mail address and then wish to be taken off our mailing list, simply write to us from your email account. Send e-mail to <remove@intercallinc.com> and ask that you be removed from our list.

If at any time you believe that InterCall has not followed the above policy, or if you would like your information deleted from our system, please contact us via email at admin@intercallinc.com
