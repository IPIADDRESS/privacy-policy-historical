By using the Service, you consent to the collection, use and disclosure of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.IF YOU DO NOT ACCEPT THIS PRIVACY POLICY, DO NOT USE THE SERVICE.If you have any questions, please contact us by sending an email to [support@intent.com] or writing to [Mail Address].

  1. Collection of Information
    2. Information collected automatically:

    Usage Information.When you access or use the Service through our mobile application, we automatically collect and store information about your mobile device and your use of the Service ("Usage Information"), including:

    1. Your mobile device's geographic location while the app is actively running
    2. Your mobile device's IP address while the app is actively running
    3. Technical information about your mobile device (such as type of device, operating system, etc.)
    4. Your preferences and settings (time zone, language, privacy preferences, application preferences, etc.)
    5. The URL of the site that referred you to the Service
    6. The buttons and controls you clicked on (if any) within the Service
    7. How long you used the Service and which parts and features you used
    8. Session times and lengths when the Service is running

    Mobile Device Tokens.When you access or use our Services through our mobile application, you will also be asked to allow push notifications to be delivered to your device.If you do so, a device token will be generated, which is associated with you and the unique application instance and is used to identify you on your device.We will collect and store these device tokens on our servers.To read more about how you can turn off push notifications, see Section 3 (Your Choices).

    Information you choose to provide:

    Personal Information .We may collect and store information that can identify you personally ("Personal Information"), such as your name, email address and telephone number. In order to register, you will be asked to sign-in using your mobile telephone number. We will also require you to submit your name and age. You may also (but are not required to) provide a profile photo of yourself.

    Address Book .You will be asked to grant us access to your mobile device's Address Book. If you do so, you authorize us to access your Address Book information, including information about your contacts including their names, contact pictures, phone numbers, and email addresses. You must grant us such access in order to use the Service.

    Facebook .You will have the option to grant us access to your Facebook account. If you do so, you authorize us to access your Facebook account information, including information about you and your Facebook friends such as name, birthday, location, hometown, education, work, and gender.

    Information others share about you:

    We may collect and store information about you that we receive from other Service users to enable us to update and correct the information contained in our database and to better customize your experience with the Service.For example, we may receive information about you from another user who knows you and who gives us access to his or her Address Book or invites you to use the Service.

  2. How We Use and Share Your Information
    3. Information you choose to provide

    We use the information we collect to: provide and improve the Service and our other products and services; customize the Service for you; better understand our users; communicate with you about the Service and our other products and services that we think may interest you; help you find and connect with your acquaintances, and to help your acquaintances find you; and diagnose and fix problems with the Service.

    Without limiting the previous paragraph, we use your Address Book and Facebook account information to generate a list of your acquaintances to give you the ability to reconnect with those acquaintances, via the Service. We may also use your Facebook account information to present the names and public profile pictures of your Facebook friends, and the fact that you are Facebook friends with these individuals, to other users of the Service, if we determine that you and such other user have such friends in common.

    Disclosure of Your Information

    Information Shared With Other Users .When you register as a user of the Service, your user profile may be viewed by other users of the Service who have you in their Address Book or Facebook friend list, or who share common acquaintances with you.Your user profile will include your name, profile picture (should you use to set one) and location (if you have given us access to this information).This profile includes information you have provided to us directly or through Facebook, including your Facebook photos, your profile picture, your name, your location (if available), and the acquaintances you have in common.We never disclose your phone number or email address to any other user of the Service.

    Reconnecting Anonymously.We only disclose your identity to those acquaintences that you want to connect with and who also want to connect with you .When you use the Service to submit a request to reconnect with one of your Address Book contacts or Facebook friends who is not a user of the Service, we use the contact information you have provided or we have otherwise collected to send an anonymous message that individual, by text message or email.If that person is also interested in reconnecting with you, your identities will be revealed, and you will be able to chat using the Service.

    Here are some of the other ways in which we share your information:

    1. We occasionally provide information about you to companies that provide support services to us or perform functions on our behalf, including analytics services.We only provide such information as necessary for these companies to perform their services for us.For example, we will share your provided mobile telephone number with [         ][1], a vendor that helps us to send an SMS text message to you, to notify you of new requests made to you by other users of the Service.We do not authorize any of these companies to use the information we share with them for any purpose other than to provide their services for us.
    2. We may share the name, location (if available) and Facebook picture of your Facebook friends or Address Book contacts with other users of the Service who also know those individuals or have acquaintances in common with those individuals.
    3. We may disclose aggregated or anonymized information about our users for any purpose.
    4. We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary (i) to conform to applicable law or comply with legal process, (ii) to protect and defend Intent, our rights or property, or our users, and (iii) under emergency circumstances to protect the personal safety of us, our affiliates, agents, our users, or the public.

In the event of a proposed or actual business transaction, corporate restructuring, sale or merger of Intent, or involving some or all of our business, assets or equity, we may disclose and/or transfer information we have collected to those involved in assessing, planning and completing the sale (including the buyer and its employees, agents and representatives).
