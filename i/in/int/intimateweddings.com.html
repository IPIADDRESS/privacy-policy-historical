Privacy

You value your privacy, and so do we. That’s why we will not sell, distribute, or share any of your personally identifiable information to companies or organizations outside of IntimateWeddings.com.

As of the update of this privacy policy the membership section of this site where user data is collected is used solely for the purpose of allowing users to save favorite venues, choose to receive the IntimateWeddings.com newsletter, as well as for venue owners to update content about their venue and provide us a means to communicate with them. Thats it. Nothing else.

This site uses cookies for the benefit of users as well as for tracking website usage via Google Analytics. This site also serves Google Adsense ads where cookies and/or web beacons served by Google are placed on our site in order to target ads relevant to visitors and the content on the site. Users who want to familarize themselves with Google’s privacy policy are encouraged to click through to the google privacy policy.

The blog serves ads from the Chitika ad network. You can read their privacy policy here.

The site serves ads from the Brideclick.net ad network. You can read their privacy policy here.
