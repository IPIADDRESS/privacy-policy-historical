Supplemental Information

Users may request that third parties contribute information to their file (ex, professor writing a letter of recommendation). Such information will supplement that information already provided by the user, and will be used in accordance with that user's consent.

Security

We follow generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, however. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. If you have any questions about security on our Web site, you can send email us at help@interfolio.com. This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control:

  1. In general, access to confidential data is limited by user name and password to the appropriate user, and only the appropriate user can authorize consent for that information to be released to others.
  2. Please note, however, that unless you manually change your account settings, authorized administrators at your affiliated institution will be able to view your documents and delivery history.
  3. Password, document identification numbers and other sensitive are encrypted in the database.
  4. Hardware firewalls to prevent network intrusion.
  5. Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption for all communication with the client.
  6. User cannot see information to which he/she has waived his/her right of access.
  7. We annually review our Privacy Policy, security measures and user access rights to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of your data.
  8.  We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs.
  9. We have implemented a company-wide education and training program about security that is required of every Interfolio employee.
  10. We retain and maintain all documents you send to us in paper form for the purpose of scanning them into an electronic form.  Thereafter, we maintain them in an electronic form.
  11. We back-up this data by following established procedures for off-site storage and business continuity readiness. Regardless, you should make backup copies of important data.
