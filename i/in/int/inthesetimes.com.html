Privacy Policy

In These Times is committed to preserving your privacy and safeguarding your information online. The following statement describes the general information-gathering and usage practices of our Web site.

Membership and Comments

We offer free memberships at In These Times to participate in discussion forums and to use other portions of the site. We collect some personal information during the sign-up process, including your e-mail address and name. This information is kept strictly confidential and is used for communication about your account. We occasionally publish comments from web discussions in our print edition.

eNewsletters

In These Times publishes an email newsletter and invites all readers to opt-in for a free subscription to this publication. We allow all e-mail newsletter recipients to opt-out of their subscription at any time from links included in each message. Our email newsletter is sent once per week.

Subscriptions

In accordance with standard industry practice, In These Times may rent, exchange, or sell information provided to us from subscribers to our print magazine. We also use this information to make occasional contact with our readers.

Traffic Analysis

In These Times analyzes traffic patterns of all our visitors based on the IP address, web browser information, and what you read when you're at our site. This information gives us insight into what types of articles our readers prefer.
