What information do we collect?

We collect certain types of personal information, such as your name, address, e-mail address, age, type of car you're interested in, and other similar information, to respond to your questions, fulfill your requests, and provide content tailored to your interests. We may ask if you would like to submit certain financial information so we may submit that information to organizations offering automobile financing services; we do not provide such services directly and do not otherwise use that information, except in an aggregate, anonymous form.

Advertisers at this site may also offer contests, sweepstakes, or other features where visitors may volunteer certain personal information. We do not control either the information third party advertisers collect or how it is used. IntelliChoice.com is not responsible for the content or privacy policies of Web sites to which it may link, including, and without limitation, online automobile purchasing and/or financing services.

We also collect certain information automatically to help our visitors navigate the site, and to assist in developing content and information we believe would be of most interest. IntelliChoice.com collects a user's Internet Protocol (IP) address automatically. This is an identifier assigned to your computer that tells us information such as the type of browser and operating system you use to help facilitate your online experience at our site. In addition, we use cookies to track usage patterns at our site, and to control the display of ads. Cookies are alphanumeric information files that your web browser places on your computer when you visit a web site. Most browsers accept cookies automatically, but can be configured not to accept them or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. Cookies allow us to customize your user interface to speed up the navigation process and to make your visit more efficient and enjoyable. We may also use cookies to record your registration at our site or in one of our sweepstakes or contests. You may set your web browser to disable cookies. If you do so, we may be unable to personalize content and information for you, and some features of the site may not be available to you.

We may use third party advertising companies to serve these ads.

How is information used?

IntelliChoice.com may perform statistical analyses of user behavior in order to measure relative consumer interest in various services (for product development purposes) and to inform advertisers as to how many consumers have seen or "clicked" their adverting banner. In performance of these statistical analyses, we will disclose information to third parties only in aggregate form. Personal information on individual users or subscribers is not included in these analyses. Survey and feedback information enables IntelliChoice.com to develop content and services of interest to our users and advertisers. We also use personal information (like your e-mail address) to respond to your requests. IntelliChoice.com may send out emails to users who have opted in for an online service to announce special offers, services and announcements from our site and/or advertisers. We perform those services for our advertisers, and do not share your personal information with them in providing these announcements.

Remember, if you register for a contest or sweepstakes with one of our advertisers, you are providing personal information to the advertiser, not to us. You should check their privacy policy to be sure you understand how they may use your personal information. Cookies, in and of themselves, do not contain any personally identifiable information, but we will associate personal information (like your name) with cookie data in order to provide registered users with a personalized greeting and personalized updates reflecting the type of information you have indicated you are interested in.

How is information shared?

IntelliChoice.com shares the information it tracks, in aggregate form, with advertisers and other partners. Our ad server firm may have the ability to identify users by name or e-mail address; our agreement with them prohibits them from disclosing this information to any third party. We may occasionally make the list of postal addresses volunteered on its Web site via e-mail registration, contest, sweepstakes and surveys available to other carefully screened associations or companies whose products or services we think you may find of interest. We pre-screen all use of our information for our customers.

Personal information is otherwise available only to members of the IntelliChoice.com and Primedia staffs, and their interactive, web and other consultants, who help us make this site and the content and services on it available to our visitors. Consultants and agents that provide technical and fulfillment services on our site (for example, to keep our site functioning, to respond to your questions or requests, to handle subscription questions, or to perform statistical analyses) may have access to personal information, but do so only to implement their responsibilities. They are not authorized to share or sell data to other third parties.

Customer lists will be transferred in the routine case of an acquisition or transfer of ownership of the Company. We will not otherwise share personal information on individual subscribers or visitors with any third party, except to satisfy any law, regulation or legal request, to protect the integrity of the site, or to cooperate in any legal investigation.

Opting out and accessing personal data
