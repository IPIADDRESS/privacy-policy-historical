  • Data Controller: Data Controller means the natural or legal person who (either alone or jointly or in common with other persons) determines the purposes for which and the manner in which any personal information are, or are to be, processed. For the purpose of this Privacy Policy, we are a Data Controller of your Personal Data.

  • Data Subject (or User): Data Subject is any living individual who is using our Service and is the subject of Personal Data.

Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

Our simulations are a limited-time subscription that allows Students/Participants to use our simulations for a class, seminar, or training. Below we have outlined some specifics about the privacy issues with those accounts as it pertains to privacy and security. Note that this the above privacy policy applies to these categories, but we wanted to provide some specific information regarding those accounts.

Student / Participant Accounts

Privacy: Interpretive Simulations collects names and email addresses from our simulation participants for the sole purpose of communicating during the simulation event. Participant billing address and/or shipping address will be collected during the payment process to fulfill the requirements of the credit card processor used. Shipping addresses will also be used if the order includes an item that will be shipped to the student. Participant contact information is never sold or shared for any purposes other than those stated in this policy.
