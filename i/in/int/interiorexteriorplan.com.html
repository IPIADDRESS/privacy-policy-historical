Interior Exterior Plan was founded in 2009 By Harjeet Virdi. In the beginning it was featuring only great architectural images from unique designed houses. Later on when the website started to get viral through the social networks it continued with posting articles related to interior and exterior design but also modern and minimal furnitures from various vendors.
