Interior Exterior Plan was founded in 2009 By Harjeet Virdi. In the beginning it was featuring only great architectural images from unique designed houses. Later on when the website started to get viral through the social networks it continued with posting articles related to interior and exterior design but also modern and minimal furnitures from various vendors. In the beginning of 2013 Interiorexteriorplan.com was acquired by Cloudie Co which is a network of creative websites based in Athens Greece. Along with the help of Harjeet Virdi we run the website by publishing high quality content and engaging architecture enthusiasts from all over the world.
