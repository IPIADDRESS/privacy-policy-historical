inttra privacy and security policy

Last Revised on March 23, 2017

This Privacy and Security Policy (“Privacy Policy”) applies to the websites owned and operated by inttra Inc. (including its subsidiaries, inttra Germany GmbH and inttra A/S) (“inttra”, “Us”, “We”, or “Our”). This Privacy Policy addresses our use and disclosure of information we collect from you on our product platforms and corporate website. The product platforms are:

  • www.inttra.com
  • www.ship.inttra.com
  • inttraworks.inttra.com

The corporate website resides at www.inttra.com. The product platforms and the corporate website listed here are collectively referred to as the "Websites".

This Privacy Policy applies to all data and information that inttra may obtain about you, in connection with its provision of the inttra systems defined below, including, without limitation, the Websites and transmissions via electronic data interchange (“EDI”) (collectively referred to as the “System”), and all related services (provision of the System and all related services are collectively referred to as the “Services”). This Privacy Policy should be read in conjunction with one or more agreements between your company (the “Company”) and inttra (the “Agreement(s)”) that apply to the System and the Services and with any other documents or guides that are provided from time to time in relation to the Services.

inttra recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of users of the System (“Users”) and this Privacy Policy sets forth inttra’s practices with respect to the collection, use and disclosure of personal data of Users. It also describes the choices available to you regarding our use of your personal data and how you can access and update this information.

EU-U.S. Privacy Shield

inttra Inc., on behalf of itself and its subsidiaries inttra Germany GmbH and inttra A/S, participates in and has certified its compliance with the EU-U.S. Privacy Shield Framework. inttra is committed to subjecting all personal data received from European Union (EU) member countries, in reliance on the Privacy Shield Framework, to the Framework’s applicable Principles.  To learn more about the Privacy Shield Framework, visit the U.S. Department of Commerce’s Privacy Shield List. https://www.privacyshield.gov/list

inttra is responsible for the processing of personal data it receives, under the Privacy Shield Framework, and subsequently transfers to a third party acting as an agent on its behalf. inttra complies with the Privacy Shield Principles for all onward transfers of personal data from the EU, including the onward transfer liability provisions.

With respect to personal data received or transferred pursuant to the Privacy Shield Framework, inttra is subject to the regulatory enforcement powers of the U.S. Federal Trade Commission. In certain situations, inttra may be required to disclose personal data in response to lawful requests by public authorities, including to meet national security or law enforcement requirements.

If you have an unresolved privacy or data use concern that we have not addressed satisfactorily, please contact our U.S.-based third party dispute resolution provider (free of charge) at https://feedback-form.truste.com/watchdog/request.  

Under certain conditions, more fully described on the Privacy Shield website https://www.privacyshield.gov/article?id=How-to-Submit-a-Complaint, you may invoke binding arbitration when other dispute resolution procedures have been exhausted.

Collection and Use of Personal Data

Your personal data (which includes your name, job title, contact details including business telephone and fax number and e-mail address) may be collected through the System and through e-mail and other correspondence with inttra as well as from third parties, or by any other means, in connection with providing a Service to you on our behalf. We also may collect sensitive information, such as invoice/payment information or tax ID in connection with specific inttra products. We will not share, sell or trade your personal data with third parties for promotional purposes without your consent. We will share your personal data with third parties only in the ways that are described in this Privacy Policy.

inttra and/or its affiliates and third party service providers may use the personal data to:

  1. perform the Services or in connection with the operation of the Services;
  2. monitor and analyze System activity including how the System is used, and the volume and frequency of access, so as to improve the System and the Services;
  3. contact you regarding your inquiries, your use of the System, changes to the System or the Services and to otherwise operate the System;
  4. comply with any applicable law or other legal or regulatory requirements, to respond to a request or subpoena by a government, regulatory or judicial authority, where necessary in connection with a dispute, actual or potential litigation or other legal process or an internal or external audit, or where otherwise necessary to protect the interests of inttra, its affiliates, third party service providers or other participants in the System; and
  5. supplement your personal data with third party sources for background verification.

With your consent, inttra and/or its affiliates may use the personal data to market products and services of inttra and/or its affiliates and third party service providers to your Company through use of post, fax or by e-mail; please note that if inttra sends marketing materials to you we will include a working unsubscribe link so you can unsubscribe.

inttra will also send you Service related e-mail announcements on rare occasions when it is necessary to do so. For instance, if Service is temporarily suspended for maintenance, we might send you an e-mail. You do not have an option to opt out of these e-mails, which are not promotional in nature.

In addition, any third party offering a service directly to your Company in connection with our Services or on our Websites (including, without limitation, live chat service providers, e-mail service providers and recruiting platforms) (collectively “Partners”) may use your personal data to: (a) provide the Services; (b) contact you regarding your inquiries; and (c) market its products and services to your Company through use of post, fax or by e-mail. If you do not want us to share your personal data with these companies, contact us at  service@inttra.com.

Third party personal data may also be entered on our System, such as if your Company has elected to use an administrator to enter the personal data about its users. If this data is collected on our System, it will only be used for the stated purpose for which it was collected.

User Data Supplementation

We may receive information about you from other sources, including publicly available databases or third parties from whom we have purchased data, and combine this data with information we already have about you.  This helps us to update, expand and analyze our records, identify new customers, and provide products and services that may be of interest to you.  If you provide us personal information about others, or if others give us your information, we will only use that information for the specific reason for which it was provided to us.

Examples of the types of personal information that may be obtained from public sources or purchased from third parties and combined with information we already have about you, may include: Purchased marketing data about our customers from third parties that is combined with information we already have about you, to create more tailored advertising and products.

Disclosure of Personal Data 

In certain situations, inttra may be required to disclose personal data in response to lawful requests by public authorities, including to meet national security or law enforcement requirements. inttra and/or its affiliates, third party service providers and Partners may disclose your personal data to:

  1. any affiliate, agent or sub-contractor of inttra, and/or its third party service providers or Partners, as applicable, including companies that provide technical services to inttra, such as data processing companies. These companies are authorized to use your personal data only as necessary to provide these Services to us. For the avoidance of doubt, a carrier that is a participant in the System is not considered to be a company that provides technical services to inttra;
  2. any affiliate, agent or sub-contractor of inttra and/or its third party service providers or Partners, as applicable, for the purposes of operating the System and, with your consent, for the marketing and/or provision of third party services. As stated above, all of the third parties offering services through the System may receive your personal data;
  3. any affiliate, agent, sub-contractor or adviser of inttra and/or its third party service providers or Partners, or a government, regulatory or judicial authority, as applicable, in connection with an internal or external audit or examination, a dispute or actual or possible litigation, a request, subpoena or similar legal process, to satisfy a port, state or customs security initiative, or where disclosure is otherwise permitted or required by law;
  4. any affiliate, agent, sub-contractor or adviser of inttra, and/or its third party service providers or Partners, or a participant in the System, or a government, regulatory or judicial authority, as applicable, where necessary to protect the interests of inttra, the public, or other participants in the System, for example, in the event of suspected fraud or illegal activity; and
  5. anyone to whom you authorize inttra to give your personal data, including parties you have authorized to access your personal data in connection with your enrollment as a User and as otherwise provided in this Privacy Policy.

In the event that inttra, or any of its assets, are acquired or become the subject of a merger, then your personal data may be transferred with the business or disclosed to potential acquirers or others involved in any such transaction, but shall not otherwise be sold or rented to any third party. You will be notified of this change as described below in “Changes to this Policy”.

You acknowledge that inttra is not responsible for information or data in the possession of third parties, except for data processors acting on behalf of inttra, regardless of how such data was obtained. Please consult the privacy policies of these third parties to learn how they may use or disclose your information. You may be contacted directly by an individual carrier.

Aggregate or other information not identifiable with any individual may be used or disclosed for other purposes, and to other parties, as deemed appropriate by inttra subject to any obligations of confidentiality under the Agreement(s) and applicable laws.

Data Renention

inttra will retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you Services, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes and enforce our Agreements.

Data Transfers

You consent to inttra, its affiliates, third party service providers and Partners transferring your personal data to inttra, its affiliates and to the recipients referred to in this Privacy Policy in countries outside the European Economic Area (the “EEA”) (including the United States of America) which do not provide a similar or adequate level of protection to that provided by countries in the EEA from which you may have submitted your personal data (if applicable).

Security

While no security procedures are fool-proof, inttra maintains industry standard appropriate technical and organizational measures to minimize the risk of unauthorized access to the data transmitted to and from, presented, and stored by inttra. The security of your personal data is important to us. When you log into our System using your User ID and password, we encrypt the collection and transmission of all information using secure socket layer technology (SSL). However, inttra cannot guarantee the security of your personal data during its transmission to inttra. You shall take commercially-reasonable efforts to ensure the secure transfer of all data sent by you across data networks.

inttra’s information security measures are designed to:

  1. ensure the security and confidentiality of personal data;
  2. identify potential threats or hazards to the security or integrity of personal data and protect against any anticipated threats or hazards; and
  3. protect against accidental or unauthorized destruction or loss or unauthorized disclosure or access to or use of the personal data.

To access certain parts of the System you will be required to create and maintain a user name and password, which will allow you as a User to access the secure customer section of the System. It is your responsibility to keep your user name and passwords secure and to not share this information with others. If you have any questions about security on our website, you can contact us at service@inttra.com.

Passive Collection

As is true of most websites, we gather certain information automatically. This information may include Internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, the files viewed on our site (e.g., HTML pages, graphics, etc.), operating system, date/time stamp, and/or clickstream data to analyze trends in the aggregate and administer the site.

Tracking Technologies

inttra and its partners use cookies or similar technologies to analyze trends, administer the website, track users’ movements around the website, and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. You can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level, but if you choose to disable cookies, it may limit your use of certain features or functions on our website or service.

inttra Partners with a third party to either display advertising on our Websites or to manage our advertising on other sites. Our third party partner may use technologies such as cookies to gather information about your activities on the Websites and other sites in order to provide you advertising based upon your browsing activities and interests. If you wish to not have this information used for the purpose of serving you interest-based ads, you may opt-out by clicking here (or if located in the European Union click here). Please note this does not opt you out of being served ads. You will continue to receive generic ads.

inttra may present links in a format that enables us to keep track of whether these links have been followed. We use this information to improve the quality of the Service, search technology, customized content, advertising and other business purposes. For example, when you click on a link displayed on the Websites, the fact that you clicked on the link may be sent to inttra. In this way, inttra is able to record information about how you use the Websites and Services, and in order to calculate how much the advertiser should be charged. In addition, clicking on certain buttons (e.g. “Request for Carrier Contact”) may result in your personal data being sent to a third party, in order for the third party to contact you. You can decline to submit personal data, in which case inttra may not be able to provide certain Services to you. If you follow a link to any of third party websites, please be aware that these websites may have their own privacy policies and that inttra does not accept any responsibility or liability for these websites. If you submit personal data to any of those sites, your information is governed by their privacy policies. We encourage you to carefully read the privacy policy of any website you visit.

Upon request inttra will provide you with information about whether we hold any of your personal information. If you are not directly entering your personal data on our Websites, and it is entered by a Company on your behalf, the following applies: We operate under the assumption that it is generally the Company’s obligation as data controller to notify individuals about the purposes for which they collect and use information about them, how they can contact them with any inquiries or complaints, the types of third parties to which they disclose your information, and the choices and means they offer for limiting your use and disclosure. We make available to you this Privacy Policy so that you can better understand our data practices and whether they are consistent with privacy notices you may have received from your Company.

If you would like to access the personal data we hold about you to update it, please log into the appropriate part of the System and update this data within your account. You can also contact us at the e-mail address below to access and update your personal data we hold about you.

You may have certain rights under applicable data protection laws which may include the right for you to receive a copy of the personal data that inttra holds about you.

In addition, you may have a right to request that personal data is corrected, blocked or deleted if the data is factually incorrect, incomplete or irrelevant for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. For any of these requests, please contact inttra at the address set out below or through the feedback button on the Websites and the System. We will respond to your request within 30 business days.

You may withdraw your consent for the processing of your personal data at any time, but if you do so, inttra may not be able to provide certain Services to you, including access to parts of the Websites. To cancel your account or request we no longer use your information to provide you Service or as otherwise outlined herein let us know by one of the these methods:

  • Send an e-mail with your request and current contact information to service@inttra.com; or
  • Send a letter with your request and current contact information to inttra Inc., Attention: Legal Department, One Upper Pond Road, Morris Corporate Center II, Building D, Parsippany, NJ 07054.

You represent and warrant that any data you provide to inttra is submitted in connection with transactions conducted for business purposes only. Access to and use of the System is subject to this Privacy Policy and to the applicable Agreement(s) between your Company and inttra.

Also, if you choose to use our referral service to tell a friend (email a friend, refer a friend, send to a friend, email a page, invite a friend) about any of our Websites, we will ask you for your friend’s name and email address. We will automatically send your friend a one-time email inviting him or her to visit the site. inttra does not store this information.

inttra collects information under the direction of its Customers, and may have no direct relationship with the individuals whose personal data it processes.
