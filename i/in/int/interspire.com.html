Our Privacy Policy
This is the web site of Interspire.com. We can be reached via email at help@interspire.test.com.

For each visitor to our web site, our web server automatically recognizes no information regarding the domain or e-mail address.

We collect the e-mail addresses of those who subscribe to our newsletter and order our products. Information collected from customers is used for order notification and to send product information only. It is not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us via email at help@interspire.test.com.
