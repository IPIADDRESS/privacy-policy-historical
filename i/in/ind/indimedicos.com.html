IndiMedicos takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.

Information Collection and Use

IndiMedicos may also receive personally identifiable information from our business partners.

If you post personal information online that is accessible to the public, you may receive unsolicited messages from other parties in return and IndiMedicos will not be responsible for the use or misuse of such information.

IndiMedicos is not responsible for the accuracy, completeness, veracity and reliability of any targeted content, products and services which may be accessed or advertised to you and IndiMedicos will not be liable for any loss or damage arising out of the same.

Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to IndiMedicos's cookies.
