We respect your privacy! Any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Resale or disclosure of information to third parties:

India Results does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including membership forms or email lists.

Browser information collected on the website:

India Results analyzes our website logs to constantly improve the value of the materials available on website. Our website logs are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.

In order to improve browsing experience, we use cookies for redirection to concerned region.

India Results maintains several email lists to keep our members informed about breaking events. We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on our list to anyone.
