Collection of your Personal Information
IndianDating.com explicitly asks when it needs information that personally identifies you ("Personal Information") pertinent to the specific product or service you are choosing to use or subscribe to on the IndianDating.com web sites. This information can include your e-mail address, name, gender, address, and telephone number, as applicable. IndianDating.com also may ask for non-personal information or demographic information. None of the information we gather can be used by the public (visitors, other members, and anyone not employed by IndianDating.com, its corporate affiliates, or the financial institutions we use to process membership charges) to identify a specific individual. Members choose when to disclose their personal information at their own risk such as when contacting others through the contact system, listing any personal contact information on their profile, participating in the online chat rooms or the instant messenger service.

We are a membership-based service. We collect the name, country, gender and email address of standard account members. For Deluxe account members we collect credit card, address, or bank information only from those who choose to pay for a Deluxe account. No credit card information is stored on our servers. Anyone on the Internet can search our profiles or sign up for a standard free account and use all standard member features at no charge. Anyone is also welcome to create a online profile free of charge. All sites of IndianDating.com are owned and operated by Webcapacity Incorporated and none of the information is shared with any third party unless you join on our partner "co-brand" sites in which you will be notified up-front that your IndianDating.com information will be shared with the IndianDating.com co-brand partner. All IndianDating.com web sites and profiles reside on our one central database and on no third party systems.

IndianDating.com gathers two types of information: information that users submit to us and information not directly submitted by users.

Information submitted to IndianDating.com
There are two types of information we collect that users submit: public and private. We define private information to be any information that allows others to contact a user other than through IndianDating.com or allows the collection of information about the user other than what is displayed on the site. Public information is personal information that may be displayed on the site, such as gender, age and height, but cannot be linked to a particular user. (Except in the case of a Deluxe member who chooses to list their contact information in their profile which is viewable to all.)

Information that users do not submit to us is aggregate information: This is information we collect that is not personally identifiable, such as what kind of browser you are using, IP address and occasionally trace route to your computer. This information is gathered for all visitors to the site.

Use of Cookies
 To make it easy and convenient for you to login to our services, the IndianDating.com site does require you to enable cookies to utilize our services. Because we use cookies, you can bookmark your IndianDating.com start page and access your account without logging in.

A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you. One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. Our server generates a random pair of numbers that is placed in three cookies, and is set in your browser. This random pair of numbers is then sent back to the server each time you request a page on the IndianDating.com site. Our server uses those numbers to identify your computer, and for example, remember where you were in your search results or any of the various things that can be customized on IndianDating.com. Rest assured that there is absolutely NO PERSONAL information being stored on your machine or sent back and forth between your computer and IndianDating.com. Our cookies only store those two random numbers and cannot personally identify you. Note however that if you are a logged in member on a shared computer or public kiosk remember to click on the logout button. This will log you out of your session and prevent others from accessing your account.

Also note that if you are a member and want to login, we provide two ways to do this. The first being a standard login which your username and password are transmitted in clear text, and the second being a SSL encrypted login form which transmits your username and password in a more secure encrypted fashion. Also note that, unlike some other sites which pass your username and password in each HTTP request's Authorization header, your username and password are only transmitted once and not each request. Once again, your username or password are not stored in a cookie. IndianDating.com also makes certain pages accessible via SSL to its members, such as billing history or editing/viewing your personal information. It is optional for members to use however we added it for our members convenience and privacy. For paying members also note that absolutely no credit card numbers are stored on our servers.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you will not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the IndianDating.com site and on other IndianDating.com Web sites or other web sites you visit.

Use of Third Party Ad Networks
Many of the online banner advertisements you see on the IndianDating.com Web sites are displayed by IndianDating.com. In addition, IndianDating.com also allows other companies, called third-party ad servers or ad networks, to display advertisements on IndianDating.com Web sites. To do this, ad networks place a persistent cookie on your computer. Doing this allows the ad network to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement. In this way, ad networks may compile information about where you, or others who are using your computer, saw their advertisements and determine which ads are clicked on. This information allows an ad network to deliver advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. IndianDating.com does not have access to or control of the cookies that may be placed by the third-party ad servers or ad networks.

IndianDating.com may maintain relationships with a number of the third-party ad networks currently operating such as Burst Media, India Ad Network, India Link Exchange, and Microsoft BCentral. If you want to prevent one of these third-party companies from sending and reading cookies on your computer, you will need to either set your browser to decline third party cookies or visit each ad network's web site individually or request that you be removed from its system.
