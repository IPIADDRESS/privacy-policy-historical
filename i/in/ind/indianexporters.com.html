Privacy Policy

Our Commitment To Privacy:

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used.


The Information We Collect:

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on our site. On some pages, you can order products, submit your information for listing, show interest to get more information, and get register for more benefits. The types of personal information collected at these pages are: Name, Address, Phone, business description etc...


The Way We Use Information:

We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete that particular order or requirement.
We use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design our website and to share with advertisers. For example, we may tell an advertiser that X number of individuals visited a certain area on our website etc, but we would not disclose anything that could be used to identify those individuals.


Our Commitment To Data Security:

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.


How You Can Access Or Correct Your Information:

You can correct fatal errors in your personally identifiable information by sending us a request that credibly shows error.
To protect your privacy and security, we will also take reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting access or making corrections.


How To Contact Us:

Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please send us an email.
