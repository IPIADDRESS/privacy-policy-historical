    Cookies

    A cookie is a small computer file that is stored on a user's computer and contains information that this Website uses. We use cookies to store information that helps us analyze what is happening on our site so we can improve the functionality. Our cookies do not contain financial, tax-related, or personally identifiable information (such as social security number, log-in id, or user name). IRL does not correlate the cookie information with data about individual users, nor does it share this information or sell it to any third party. If you prefer not to receive cookies from this Website, you can set your browser to warn you before accepting cookies and refuse the cookie when your browser alerts you to its presence. You can also refuse all cookies by turning them off in your browser. Please be aware, however, that certain features of this Website may be unavailable if your browser is not set to support or enable cookies.
