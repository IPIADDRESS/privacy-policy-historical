FAQ

What information do I need to provide to make a purchase?  
We ask you for basic information only, like your name, email address, address, contact number, product selections, credit card or other payment information and required passwords. The process is entirely authenticated and SSL secured, ensuring paramount safety of your details.

What other information do you record, as I visit your website?  
We also track and record information about your location, IP address, the browser you're using, the country where your PC is located, the pages you visited on our website, etc. The technology we use is only to track the behavior of visitors to our site and for no other purpose. It helps us to improve our services and strategies, so that we can serve you even better.

How do you use this information?  
In order to track your system and to keep a regular contact with you, we use a tiny text file called "cookie" that is stored on your browser. It helps us personalize your experience by keeping a record of your current shopping session.

Is the process harmful in any way?  
No, it's not at all harmful, as it doesn't record any personal or private information. However, if you wish to, you can modify your browser to prevent this from happening.
