If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to Contact Us.

How do we use the information we gather?

IndiaTravelBlog is a free community site and we publish almost all information we gather in this site. We do not share or sell your personal information to any third parties . But all personal information you submit as part of your public profile will be publicly accessible in our site, except the email address. We try not to publish your email address to avoid spam, but it is possible that in certain cases your email address may be exposed to public.

We store any information you submit to our site.
