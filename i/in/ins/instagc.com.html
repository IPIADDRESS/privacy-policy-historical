By using instaGC.com, you signify that you have read, understand and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy as well as our Terms of Service.

instaGC Privacy Policy is as follows (Last Updated: 11/30/2014):

Information We Collect and How We Use It
The Company may collect certain information from and about its users by creating a browser Cookie or dropping a Web beacon through third party websites. A cookie is a small text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a Web Server in the domain that issued the cookie to you. Some personally identifying information is collected and includes, but not limited to: name, username, home address, email address, phone number, birth date, gender, and bank account information (only obtained if choosing to receive a direct deposit reward).
Account/ID Verification
At times, circumstances may require users to provide valid identification information for verification purposes. This verification can include any to all items listed in our "Information We Collect and How We Use It" section and will always follow our "Information Never Asked For" and "Information Received" guidelines.
Information Sharing
As offers are completed and rewards are requested, publicly viewable posts will be made in the chat area to other members. The username of the member will be posted along with the offer completed or reward requested. We also run contests where a members username may be posted. You can opt out by not having this information shown by updating your Privacy settings within your account. In addition - the Company does not disclose, lease, rent, share, or sell member information or activity unless required by law or in the instance of a member is suspected of committing fraud.
Information Never Asked For
The Company will never ask for any of the following pieces of information, during registration or participation: DLN (drivers license number), SSN (social security number), credit card information, passport number, or photo. However - at times you may be asked to submit ID verification that may include items stated above - so feel free to blur/hide the information as desired.
Information Received
Any email sent to the Company, whether for verification or offer completion purposes will be immediately deleted and not kept/saved after it has been used to either verify your account or used to issue credit to your account.
Opt Out
Opting out of our services will prevent us from collecting data when a user visits any of our partner sites. By doing so, advertisements will no longer be tailored to your interests. To opt out of having data collected and written to cookies placed by our website or third party partners, please click here. Note that after choosing to opt out, if you use a different computer or a different browser, or if you delete or block browser cookies, you may need to repeat the opt-out steps. Most web browsers also enable you to block browser cookies. You can refer to your web browser's documentation to get more information on how to block or delete cookies directly through your browser settings.
Changes to This Policy
Please note this Privacy Policy will change from time to time. We expect most such changes to be minor, but there may be changes that are more significant. Regardless, we will post those changes on this page and, if the changes are significant, we will also provide a more prominent notice.
