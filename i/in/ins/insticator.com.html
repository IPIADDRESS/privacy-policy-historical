    • Redemption Points Information: If you access or use certain features, portals, content or functions of the Services, including the Widget and accumulate Redemption Points, you may need to provide us with certain information in order for you to use or redeem the Redemption Points. If you are using Redemption Points to make certain purchases, including in-game purchases, we may require you to provide us with your name, email address, mailing address and profession.

If you no longer wish to have this location information processed, you can withdraw your consent at any time by disabling the location and/or Bluetooth features in the operating system of your Device and/or in the App. If you have any questions about disabling you location and/or Bluetooth features, you can contact us at privacy@insticator.com.

For example, if you sign up to receive newsletters, marketing emails, promotions or other communications regarding the Services and/or promotions, from us or our preferred third-party products or services providers, or participate in contests, surveys, questionnaires, games and other promotions related to the Services (including the Widget), we may process your name, email address, mailing address, interests, IP address, device identifier, search history, profession and date of birth in order to provide the additional features or Services.

We will process any information you submit, post or provide in the course of such activities to respond to your comments or feedback. In the event you contact us, we may process information related to conversations, including recording or monitoring customer service calls for quality assurance and training purposes, and to verify your identity.

The information you post or provide to Third Party Platforms, as well as the controls surrounding these disclosures are governed by the respective policies of these third parties.

Third Party Advertising Providers enable us to provide you with more useful advertisements about our Services or the services of third parties which are tailored specifically to your interests or to individuals with similar characteristics to our customers and users, including by using tools like Google’s “Customer Match” or Facebook “Custom Audiences”. Insticator does not control these Third Party Advertising Providers and/or their use of cookies, which is instead governed by the privacy policies and/or cookie policies of any such Third Party Advertising Provider.
