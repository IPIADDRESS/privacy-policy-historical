Privacy Policy


This privacy policy discloses the privacy practices for insubuy.com.


Frames and third party links

Insubuy ®, Inc. provides various insurance products through numerous insurance companies and intermediaries to its visitors and clients. In order to facilitate better user experience, we may use HTML frame and links to third party web site. Many insurance products can be purchased instantly online at our web site and in doing so, each insurance company collects specific information about the insured persons, beneficiaries etc., that the insurance company considers necessary to issue the insurance policy or certificate of insurance. All the collected data is captured directly by the individual insurance company and stored by the individual insurance company. Privacy policy of third parties may be different than our privacy policy and information collected by third parties is not covered by this privacy policy.


Use of collected information

When you contact us by email, phone, fax, in person etc., any personal information you give us is kept strictly confidential and is not used for any purpose other than to help you buy the insurance plan through us and once you buy it, for customer service through us and with the insurance company.


Cookies

"Cookies" are used on this web site. A cookie is a piece of data stored on a site your computer when you visit our web site. Cookies do not contain any personally identifiable information about you. Cookies can also enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance the experience on our site. Cookies will enable us to customize our content according to your preferences. You have the right to accept or decline cookies. However, if you decline cookies, many features of the web site may not be available to you.


Tracking, Advertisement and Marketing

This web site uses Google Analytics. That includes obtaining specific visitor cookie data, such as the source, medium and keyword use to visit our web site. However, Google Analytics does not store any visitor specific data but only aggregate data. Insubuy has implemented and uses Google Analytics for Display Advertisers and Google Display Network. Google Analytics collects anonymous data about your interaction with our website and through the standard Google Analytics implementation. Anonymous data may be used for purposes including delivering to you banner advertisements and other advertising tailored to your interests when you visit certain websites and selecting offers and products to display to you when you visit our website. You can opt-out of Google Analytics for Display Advertising and customize Google Display Network ads using the Ads Preferences Manager. You can find out more about how Google uses cookies in advertising and opt-out via the following links: Google Display Network & Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.


Children policy

Our web site is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 years. We do not use our web site to knowingly solicit information from or market to children under the age of 18 years. Parents and/or guardians are solely responsible for supervising their minor children's use of our web site.


Changes

We may need to change our privacy policy at any time without notice to users. However, in that case, updated privacy policy will be posted here.


If you have any more questions regarding our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us.
