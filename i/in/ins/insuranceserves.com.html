Data Collection
insuranceever-as well as most sites on the Internet-use "cookies" to enhance your experience on the web. What are cookies and why do we use them? First, insuranceever cannot use these cookies to identify you personally in any way. A cookie is a unique number that is provided to you the first time insuranceever-or any site that uses cookies-serves an ad to you. This unique number, which is stored in a cookie file on your computer, helps insuranceever target ads to you. This cookie can help insuranceever ensure that we do not serve you the same ad multiple times and help our advertisers understand what you like and do not like about their offerings so they can cater better to your needs the next time you are interested in their products or services. While we believe cookies enhance your Internet experience by limiting the number of times you see the same ad and delivering more relevant content to you, they are not required for insuranceever to deliver you an ad.

If you do not want the benefit of cookies, there is a simple procedure to delete your cookies manually. Please consult your web browser's help documentation for more information.
