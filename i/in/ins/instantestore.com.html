Privacy Policy
InstanteStore.com does collect demographic data and personal data to provide you with a better internet experience.
Your personal details will remain private and confidential. We will never sell, rent, barter or share your personal details in any way with any other company. All data collected is for our internal use only.
We hate spam as much as you do. Actually, I think we hate it more since we get something like 2000-odd junk e-mails each day. So, we will NEVER send you any unsolicited e-mail. If you choose to subscribe to our free weekly newsletter, you will receive your newsletter once every week. Clear unsubscribe details are always shown on the newsletter.
If you choose not to receive our free weekly newsletter, we will only contact you to answer your queries and/or send you your order confirmation and monthly invoices. We may also contact you should there be a major change in the InstanteStore service. We will not contact you anytime else.
We may from time to time, send you pertinent information on special offers, giveaways or special discounts should you choose to subscribe to it. In any case, you will receive no more than one (1) of such e-mail every week.
This site does use cookies. However, we do not keep any of your personal details on cookies. We simply store a cookie token (just a number) in cookie format. This cookie token does not correlate to any of your personal information.
We may from time to time post up surveys to measure your response to certain new additions to the website. As a rule, you are never forced to fill in these surveys. Furthermore, should you choose to fill in the surveys, you can choose to remain anonymous.
InstanteStore.com will from time to time run competitions/sweepstakes on our website. Any information entered through these competitions/sweepstakes also complies with this privacy policy. We will never ever share your personal details with another company.
