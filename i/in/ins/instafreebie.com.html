Personal information

We collect several types of information from and about Users including, but not limited to, information: (i) by which you may be personally identified, directly or indirectly, such as name, postal address, e-mail address, telephone number or any other identifier by which you may be contacted online or offline ("Personal Information"), (ii) that is about you but individually does not identify you, and/or (iii) about your internet connection, the equipment you use to access our Site, the version of the Application you use to access our Site, your location and usage details.

We collect your information in a variety of ways, including:

  1. directly from you when you provide it to us
  2. automatically as you navigate through the Site. Information collected automatically may include usage details, IP addresses and information collected through cookies, web beacons and other tracking technologies and
  3. from third parties.

We may collect Personal Information from Users in a variety of ways, including, but not limited to, when Users visit our Site, respond to a survey, fill out a form, and in connection with other activities, services, features or resources we make available on our Site, Instafreebie content sharing platform, and Services. If they do so, note that some parts of the Site and Instafreebie content sharing platform may not function properly.

Some content or applications, including advertisements, on the Site are served by third-parties, including advertisers, ad networks and servers, content providers and application providers. These third parties may use cookies alone or in conjunction with web beacons or other tracking technologies to collect information about you when you use our Site. We do not control these third parties' tracking technologies or how they may be used. Our Site, Instafreebie content sharing platform, and Services process information in a way that is compatible with and relevant for the purpose for which it was collected.

Minors

We do not knowingly collect or maintain Personal Information from individuals who are under 13 years of age, and no part of our Site, Instafreebie content sharing platform or Services are designed or intended to attract people under the age of 13. Protecting the privacy of children is very important to us. If you are under 13, do not use or provide any information on the Site on or through any of its features/registration, make any purchases through the Site or provide any information about yourself to us, including your name, address, telephone number, e-mail address or any screen name or user name you may use. If you believe we might have information from or about a User under 13, please contact us at support@instafreebie.com.
