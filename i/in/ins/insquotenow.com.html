  • To respond to any question or request for assistance communicated though this Site;
  • For promotional purposes and to provide you with information about other products and services offered by us, or those organizations with which we have relationships, which may be of interest to you; and
  • In instances when we are legally obligated to collect, use or disclose the personal information.
