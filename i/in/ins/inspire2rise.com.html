Log Files :
Like many other Web sites, www.inspire2rise.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable as is mentioned in our privacy policy further.

Cookies and Web Beacons :
www.inspire2rise.com does not use cookies.

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on www.inspire2rise.com send directly to your browsers. Other technologies ( such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons ) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see.

www.inspire2rise.com has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers. Our privacy policy is strictly limited to things within our control domain.

You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. www.inspire2rise.com’s privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of (or privacy policy of), such other advertisers or web sites.
