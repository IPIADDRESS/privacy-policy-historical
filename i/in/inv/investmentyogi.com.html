Effective Date: January 1, 2009

We use the information that you provide to us when you register on a page on this site, fill out a survey, or otherwise voluntarily tell us about yourself or your activities, for our own use to improve our products and services and the content of this site. In addition we may use the information you provide us to send you marketing information about InvestmentYogi products and services.
