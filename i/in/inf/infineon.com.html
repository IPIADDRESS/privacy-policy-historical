    1.) Scope of this Privacy Policy
    The Privacy Policy applies to all personal information collected or used by the Infineon Group (hereinafter “Infineon”) from Infineon websites.

Upon registration within these pages, you voluntarily provide us with some personal information of you. Once you are registered you are no longer anonymous to Infineon - you are given a user name and are able to take advantage of Infineon's offers.
