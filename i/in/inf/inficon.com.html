Privacy Policy

INFICON respects the individual privacy of its employees, vendors, consumers, business partners and others. As a user of this website, your privacy is very important to us and we want you to know how we protect the privacy of our visitors. INFICON aims to collect, use, and disclose the personal information of our sites visitors in a manner consistent with the laws of the countries in which we do business. Please take a moment to read the following policy describing our tactics for collecting and using personally identifiable data on our website, inficon.com. If you do not agree with the following policy, please do not use this website.

Any personal information we collect is used to help us deliver world-class customer service. The information gathered will enable us to provide you, the visitor, with convenient access to our products and services, as well as focus the site on categories that target your individual interests. Additionally, your personal information will allow us to keep you up-to-date on product announcements, software updates and upcoming events. INFICON will not collect personally identifiable information about you on this site without your consent. For example, we may ask you to enter an email address in order to inform you of a product upgrade or service alert. If you prefer not to receive such communication from INFICON, please contact us at the address listed at the bottom of this page.

Some information may be rendered automatically as your browse our site. Our server automatically collects information in the form of clickstream data. This data includes, but is not limited to the IP (internet protocol) address used to connect your computer to the internet, the domain name of the service providing your internet access, browser type and version, operating system, web beacons, and cookies. Cookies are used for browser data and to record website transfers. These help our administrators keep records of previous visits and help us to determine which areas of our website are of greatest interest to you.

Our site contains several forms where visitors can enter their contact information in order to request documents, downloads or additional information. We will not disclose, sell, trade, or rent personal information without your prior written consent. INFICON maintains the confidentiality of the information collected and does not sell or rent your contact information to other marketers. In addition, INFICON limits employee access to and use of this information. In certain rare circumstances, however, we may be required by law to disclose your personal information to support security-related or law enforcement investigations, such as matters regarding a lawsuit or other related legal proceedings.

INFICON has the right to maintain personal information if we have suspended or terminated a visitors access to the site in the case that he/she has violated our Terms of Use.
