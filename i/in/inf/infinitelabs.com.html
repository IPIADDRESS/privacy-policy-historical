Privacy Policy

We value your privacy, and it is a priority of ours to ensure you are protected. This Infinite Labs Privacy Policy (“Privacy Policy”) describes how we collect and use your information through a variety of digital methods. By accessing or using this website, mobile application or other Infinite Labs product or service (collectively the “Platform”) on any computer, mobile phone, tablet, console or other device (collectively, “Device”), you consent to our Privacy Policy. Your continued use of this Platform constitutes your acceptance to this Privacy Policy and any updates. Infinite Labs may modify this Privacy Policy at any time, so we strongly recommend checking this page periodically to ensure you are informed about any changes that are applied. This Privacy Policy is incorporated into, and is subject to, the Terms of Use.

TYPES OF INFORMATION COLLECTED

PERSONAL INFORMATION

We collect information you provide to us or permit us to access. This personal information may include, but is not limited to, your name, image, birth date, email and physical address, telephone number, gender, contact lists, social media information and profile, location (GPS) information, activity and performance information, and when necessary, credit card information (e.g., making a purchase on the Platform).

NON-PERSONAL INFORMATION

We may automatically collect information regarding your interaction with, and use of, our products and services. Information we may collect includes, but is not limited to your Device identifier and hardware information, IP address, browser type and language, cookie information, system type, whether you have enabling software to access certain features, access times, referring website URLs, information about your purchases and other information about your interactions on the Platform.

USE OF INFORMATION COLLECTED

Information that is collected may be used for, but not limited to, the following:

  • Improving your Platform experiences and communications
  • Analyzing user behavior and trends
  • Sending administrative messages and other information about Infinite Labs
  • Communicating with you about your purchase, account information or customer service
  • Providing and conducting marketing and promotions
  • Administering contests, promotions, events, surveys and features alike

INFORMATION SECURITY

We work hard to protect Infinite Labs and our users from unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of information we hold. In particular:

  • We encrypt our Platform using SSL.
  • We restrict access to personal information to Infinite Labs employees, contractors and agents who need to know this information in order to process it for us, and who are subject to strict contractual confidentiality obligations and may be disciplined or terminated if they fail to meet these obligations.
  • We review our information collection, storage and processing practices, including physical security measures, to guard against unauthorized access to systems.

Even with the above measures in place, the transmission of information via the Internet is not completely secure, and Infinite Labs cannot fully guarantee the security of your information transmitted to our Site; any such transmission is at your own risk.

Email Security: If you receive an e-mail that looks like it is from Infinite Labs asking you for your personal information, do not respond. Infinite Labs will never request your password, user name or credit card information via email.

COOKIES AND OPT OUT INFORMATION

Cookies are small files which enable the Platform to recognize your browser and carry certain information. Infinite Labs may utilize cookies and other identifiers to improve your experience and track information about visitors and visits to the Platform. Users have the option of turning cookies off through their browser, however this may interfere with the normal functionality of the Platform.

Infinite Labs may utilize e-mail subscriptions to communicate with you about promotions, products, upcoming events and other related information. Users have the option to opt out and/or unsubscribe to these emails by scrolling to the bottom of the email message and clicking ‘Unsubscribe’.

THIRD PARTY SITES

The Platform may contain links to third-party websites. Information collected by these parties is solely governed by their privacy practices, and Infinite Labs cannot be held responsible for the protection and privacy of any information provided by you while visiting these third-party websites.

DISCLOSURE TO THIRD PARTIES

Infinite Labs may make certain personal information available to strategic partners that work with us to provide products and services, or that help us market to customers. Personal information will only be shared by us to provide or improve our products, services and advertising; it will NOT be shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Infinite Labs may also disclose any information that we possess about you (including, but not limited to your identity) if we determine that such disclosure is necessary in connection with any investigation or complaint regarding your use of the Platform, or to identify, contact or bring legal action against someone who may be causing injury to or interference with (either intentionally or unintentionally) our rights or property, or the rights or property of visitors to or users of the Platform, including our customers.

We reserve the right at all times to disclose any information that we deem necessary to comply with any applicable law, regulation, legal process or governmental request. We may also disclose your information when we determine that applicable law requires or permits such disclosure, including exchanging information with other companies and organizations for fraud protection purposes, or if we determine that disclosure is reasonably necessary to enforce our Terms of Use. Moreover, in the event that we undergo a corporate reorganization, merger, or sale, we may transfer any and all personal information that we collect to any third party involved in such transaction.

Our digital operations are conducted, in whole or in part, in the United States. Regardless of where you live, you consent to have your personal data transferred, processed and stored in the United States and allow Infinite Labs to collect and use your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Your use of our Platform, products and services, and any disputes arising from them, is subject to this Privacy Policy as well as our Terms of Use. Please visit our Terms of Use, which explains other terms governing the use of our Platform and services.
