Privacy Policy

We respect the privacy of every individual who visits this site. Our policy on the collection and use of personal data is outlined below

In order to:

  1. administer or otherwise carry out our obligations in relation to any agreement that you may have with us;
  2. anticipate and resolve problems with your service;
  3. create and inform you of products and services that you may be interested in;

We need to collect and process personal information about you (including your name, address, your employer, telephone number and email address). We may ask you for this information on our web site.

We will not disclose any personally identifiable information about you without your permission, unless we are legally entitled to do so (for example, if required to do so by a court order or for the purposes of the prevention of fraud or other crime). You will be treated as having given your permission to the disclosures in this policy.

In order to help us provide you with the highest level of service, we may use the information you supply us with for:

  1. market research;
  2. sending you information about our products and services.

We may also give the information that you supply us with to a third party (including third parties that are based outside the E.U., for example in the U.S.) or to our associated companies to enable them to send you information that may be of interest to you.

They may use this information for:

  1. market research;
  2. sending you information about their products and services;
  3. giving the information to their associate companies to enable them to send you information that may be of interest to you.

If you do not want to receive mailings from us or from third parties, please refer to the opt out details on the subscription form.

Non-personal details
We may automatically collect non-personal information about you, such as the type of browsers you use or the web site from which you linked to us. This information is only used to assist us in providing an effective service on this web site. We may, from time to time, supply the owners and operators of third party web sites which have a link to our site with information about the number of users linking to our site from their web site. You cannot be identified from any of this information.

Cookies
We may store some information (commonly known as a 'cookie') on your computer when you look at our site. This information facilitates your use of our web site, and ensures that you do not need to re-enter your details on each visit. You can set up your browser to notify you when you are sent a cookie, which allows you to choose whether or not to accept it (your help screen or manual should tell you how to do this).

Accuracy
We aim to keep our records as accurate as possible. If you would like to review or amend the details that you have supplied us with at any tie, please contact us as set out below.

Security
The technology that we use and the policies that we have implemented are intended to safeguard your privacy from unauthorised access and improper use. We will continue to update these measures as new technology becomes available.

Links
This site contains links to other sites. We cannot be responsible for the privacy policies and practices of other web sites. We recommend that you check the privacy policy of every site that you visit.

You may have linked to this site from a third party web site. We cannot be responsible for the privacy policies and practices of the third party web site from which you linked to our web site. We recommend that you check the policy of that third party web site and contact its owner or operator if you have any concerns or questions.

If you have any further questions please contact us.
