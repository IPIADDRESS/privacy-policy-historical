By using the Websites, you signify your assent to our Privacy Policy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and honoring the integrity of your personal information. We do not sell any of your Personally Identifiable Information (“PII”) collected through the Websites to others except as disclosed in this Privacy Policy. See definition of PII in Section 1. If you have any requests concerning your personal information or any queries with regard to these practices, please contact our support team by email at legal@inform.com or via mail to Inform, Inc., Attn: Legal Department, 3445 Peachtree Rd. NE, Suite 1000, Atlanta, GA 30326.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our policies and practices regarding your information and how we will treat it. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, your choice is not to use our Websites.

1. What this Privacy Policy Covers

  • This Privacy Policy applies to the services offered by INFORM via the Websites and other features, content or applications offered from time to time by INFORM in connection with the Websites. The Websites are hosted in the United States.
  • This Privacy Policy covers how INFORM collects, uses, maintains, retains, protects, and shares information, including PII that you provide to us and information we collect automatically related to your use of the Websites and services.
  • This Privacy Policy does not apply to information collected by us offline or the practices of companies that INFORM does not own or control, to content or information not generated by INFORM, any third party, including through any application or content (including advertising) that may link to or be accessible from or on the Websites, or to acts of people that INFORM does not employ or manage.
  • PII means individually identifiable information that INFORM owns, licenses, or maintains and that is from or about an individual, including, but not limited to (a) first and last name; (b) home or other physical address; (c) email address or other online information, such as a user name and password; (d) telephone number; (e) government-issued identification or other number; (f) date of birth; and (g) any information that is combined with any of (a) through (f).

2. Information Collection

When you visit the Websites, you provide us with three types of information: (1) PII and other information about you that you knowingly choose to disclose, (2) information we collect automatically as you interact with the Websites, and (3) information we collect from other sources.

We collect personal information from you when you submit questions or material to us, register on the Websites, subscribe to our service, purchase a product from us, request further services from us, email us, enter one of our contests or sweepstakes, or use the Websites, such as setting up your personal profile, making connections, sending messages, performing searches and queries, forming or joining groups, submitting content, participating in discussions and forums, or sending and receiving information through various channels, and when you report a problem with our Websites.

We use personal information collected through our Websites to:

  • present our Websites and their contents to you,
  • provide a personalized service,
  • conduct market research surveys,
  • fulfill product orders,
  • run features that incorporate submissions to the Websites,
  • provide you with notices about your account or subscription,
  • carry out our obligations and enforce our rights arising from any contracts entered into between you and INFORM,
  • notify you about changes to our Websites,

  • provide you with information about, and access to, products and services we offer, either ourselves or jointly with or on behalf of other organizations,
  • in any other way we may describe when you provide such information, and

If you do not wish to receive information about our products or services, or the products or services offered jointly with or on behalf of other organizations, please check the appropriate box on the registration page or other relevant form or page through which the information is collected.

4. Automatically Collected Information

We collect information in the aggregate form through the use of cookies and internet protocol (“IP”) addresses, such as demographic information and browsing patterns, including the domain and host from which you access the internet, your domain name, the IP address of the computer you are using, the name of the page that referred you to us, the date and times you enter and exit the Websites, the pages you visit before and after visiting the Websites, the web pages, video content and advertisements you view and links you click on within the Websites, unique identifiers, including mobile device identification numbers that can identify the physical location of such devices in accordance with applicable law, and information collected through web beacons and other technologies.   • store information about your preferences,
  • build up marketing profiles,
  • aid strategic development,
  • manage our relationship with advertisers and content providers, and
  • audit usage of the Websites.

We may share such information, in the aggregate form only, with our affiliates, partners or advertisers, or other third parties.

5. Information Collected from Other Sources

Where permitted by law, we may collect information about you from other sources, including through interactive applications (such as mobile devices, third-party services and embedded video players), from co-branded partners and sites and from commercially available sources (such as data aggregators and public databases). In addition, our advertisers may use other third-party advertising technology to target advertising on the Websites and other sites.
