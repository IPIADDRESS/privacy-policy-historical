About the Author

When Beth went from the busy world of Marketing to the even busier world of SAHM, she refocused her energy into her family and their very tight budget. Challenging herself to live within their means, while still enjoying the finer things in life, she cut her family's grocery budget to $50/week. Now she shares her recipes, deals, tips, and more at In Good Cents to help other families enjoy life too, regardless of their budget!
