Privacy Policy

This site does request information during giveaways or surveys, such as your e-mail address. If you win a giveaway, additional information is necessary to provide you with your prize(s). Aside from prize winners, this site does not collect information such as your phone number, address, or other private information. I understand that privacy of your information is very important.

Information, such as your e-mail address, is also collected when you comment or ask a question.  This is used to answer any questions or reply to your comment, as well as alert you if anyone else replies to your comment.  Your e-mail address is only available to me and can not be seen by other readers.  Also note, that I do “follow” (or at least I let Google “follow”) all website links in comments and pingbacks or links back to my site, unless they are spam comments.

In addition, this site is supported by 3rd party advertisers which may collect information including your IP address, your ISP, and other information about your browsing capabilities using cookies and/or web beacons. This information is used only to target advertising.

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this site.   Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to you based on visits to this site and other sites on the Internet.   You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

This site  collects information to:

  • Improve the site
  • Administer giveaways, contests, promotions, surveys, and other site features
  • To distribute daily e-mails to subscribers

If you subscribe to In Good Cents by e-mail, you do opt in to receiving one e-mail per day with a round-up of the latest deals.  These e-mails are sent each day between 5:00 PM and 7:0o PM.  Each e-mail has directions for you to easily unsubscribe, if you choose to do so.

As a frugal blog, I often link to offers from third parties or services that are not part of this site.  These parties have separate and independent privacy policies.  Therefore, I have no responsibility or liability for the content or activities of those linked sites.  Nevertheless, I do try hard to only link to reliable and reputable sites and seek to protect the integrity of my own site.

As a website which may receive visitors from the state of California, I must comply with the California Online Privacy Protection Act.

Because I value your privacy we have taken the necessary precautions to be in compliance with the California Online Privacy Protection Act. I therefore will not distribute your personal information to outside parties without your consent.

About the Author

When Beth went from the never-sleeping world of marketing to the other never-sleeping world of stay-at-home mom, she refocused her energy into her family and the family budget. Challenging herself to live within their new (very tight) budget, while still enjoying the finer things in life, she cut her family's grocery budget down to $50 a week.

It wasn't long before friends and family started to beg for insider tips to save money too. Now she speaks and teaches classes locally, while sharing her latest money-saving deals, frugal tips, and more on In Good Cents.
