PRIVACY POLICY           

 

Ingram Book Group, Inc. ("IBG") will collect certain information from you while using websites controlled or owned by IBG including Personally Identifiable Information to help customize content supplied to you and provide information as may be needed for your use of the selected Products.  Information about you is important to us and we agree not to sell or share this information except to Third Party Service Providers or IBG affiliated companies who are either subject to this Privacy Policy or follow practices at least as protective as those described in this Privacy Policy.  Third-Party Service Providers perform functions on our behalf and may include analyzing data, providing marketing assistance, processing credit card payments, and providing customer service. They have access to personal information needed to perform their functions and may not use it for other purposes.  The following Privacy Policy will help you understand how IBG collects, uses, and safeguards the personal information you provide on any Products offered and is part of and subject to the IBG Terms and Conditions. 

 

INFORMATION WE COLLECT FROM YOU AND HOW IT IS USED

 

Our registration and user forms require users to provide us with information, including your name and e-mail address.  The forms allow you to request additional information or order our Products or additional material as may be available from IBG.  This information falls into two categories: Personally Identifiable Information (“PII”) and Non-Personally Identifiable Information (“Non-PII”) (collectively “Information”).  PII includes information that identifies you specifically, such as your name, address, email address, phone number, other contact information, employer, business name, social security number and credit card numbers.  Non-PII is anonymous data that does not identify you specifically, such as aggregate data about the number of people who have accessed a particular Product.  We may collect, use, transfer and disclose Non-PII for any purpose. You can choose not to provide PII we may request of you, but, in general, most of the PII we request is required in order to provide our service and the lack of such PII will prevent us from doing so.

 

When you browse any IBG website, you do not do so anonymously.  Other information to be collected and logged includes: browser type, browser language, operating system, Internet Protocol (IP) address and domain, date and time of your request, and the device you are using.  This aggregate information is used to properly administer our Products, make updates to our website, and analyze how our site is being used. 

 

Cookies.  This site will send a "cookie," a small text file, to your computer. This allows us to recognize your computer the next time you visit, without requesting additional security information from you. If you do not wish to receive cookies, please refer to the "Help" file of your Internet Browser to learn how to either block all cookies or receive a warning before a cookie is stored.  We collect and store some "visitor information" from you, such as the name of the domain and host from which you access the Internet, the Internet Protocol (IP) address of the computer you are using, the date and time you access our Web Site, and the Internet address of the website from which you linked directly to our Web Site. We use this information to help our website function better and to understand how our publishers are using our website.  Blocking cookies may hinder us from delivering the personalization of information from our site.

 

IBG’s Use Of Your Information.  IBG uses your Information by itself or in conjunction with data collected through other sources to meet our contractual commitments to you, to provide the Products to you, to respond to your requests, to properly administer and maintain the Products, to customize content supplied to you, to provide you with information related to the Products, to update the website and analyze how it is used, to offer you the Product, to improve the Products, to prevent fraud, violation of intellectual property rights and unauthorized use of Products or your account, to track your purchase history, to conduct research, to contact you, to inform you about certain changes to our Products, to develop new Products, or as otherwise appropriate to the business needs of IBG related to the Products.  If you no longer use the Products, IBG may retain any Information that you have provided and may continue to use it for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

 

Content Owners.  IBG may share Information about you and your use of the Products with content owners or licensees that have rights related to the Products that you access (“Content Owners”).  Content Owners will use this Information for their own research and tracking purposes, to evaluate Product use and access, to improve Products and related analysis.  IBG will not reveal your PII to Content Owners, just anonymous Product usage data that does not identify you specifically. 

 

Disclosures As Required By Law Or To Prevent Harm.  IBG reserves the right to disclose Information if we reasonably believe that access, use, preservation or disclosure of such Information is reasonably necessary to (a) satisfy any applicable law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request, (b) enforce applicable terms of use, including investigation of potential violations thereof, (c) detect, prevent, or otherwise address illegal or suspected illegal activities, security or technical issues, or (d) protect against harm to the rights, property or safety of IBG, its users, the Content Owners or the public as required or permitted by law.

 

CHILDRENS PRIVACY

 

You must be 18 years of age or older to create an account on this website.  IBG does not solicit orders or collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of thirteen.  If we have reason to believe that any personally identifying information has been submitted to us by a child under thirteen, we will delete that information as soon as possible and not disclose it to any third party or use it ourselves in any way. 

 

SECURITY

 

We work to protect the security of your information during transmission by using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) software, which encrypts information you input.  We use encryption to protect all information and a firewall to help protect against unlawful intrusions.  It is important for you to protect against unauthorized access to your password and to your computer. Be sure to sign off when finished using a shared computer, however IBG cannot guarantee complete security, as it does not exist on the Internet.

 

CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

 

The policies described in this Privacy Policy are current as of the last updated date at the beginning of this policy. IBG reserves the right to periodically update this Privacy Policy at its discretion.  Notice of any revisions will be posted to this page and are effective once posted.  If you are concerned about how your Information is used, bookmark this page and check back periodically.

 

TRANSFER OF INFORMATION AND INTERNATIONAL USERS

 

Given that the Internet is a global environment, using the Internet to collect and process information necessarily involves the transmission of data on an international basis. Therefore, by using IBG Products, you acknowledge and consent to the transfer of your information outside your country of residence to any country (including the United States) where we have facilities or engage third parties (including but not limited to payment processors, cloud service or other IT providers, and other companies that provide services to us). You understand that the countries to which we may transfer information may not have as comprehensive a level of data protection as in your country.
