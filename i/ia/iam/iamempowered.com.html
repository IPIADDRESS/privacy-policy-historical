Privacy Policy

This privacy policy covers products, services and websites offered by NUL or its subsidiaries or affiliated companies collectively, NUL’s “services”, including the websites www.nul.org; www.iamempowered.com; and www.nationalurbanleaguestore.org (the “sites”).  This Privacy Policy covers information that is collected when you use the services, including when you browse or log on to the sites.

By using the services, you the user (“you”) indicate that you have read and agree to be bound by this privacy policy.  If you do not agree to this policy, do not use the services in any manner.

  1. Introduction:
    We are committed to the privacy of our visitors.  We do not make any visitor-provided information available to third parties, except as disclosed below. Here is how we handle information about your visit to our sites and the use of our services:
  2. Information collected and stored automatically:
    We offer a number of services that do not require you to register for an account or provide any personal information to us.

    If you do nothing during your visit but browse through the sites, read pages, or download information, we will gather and store certain information about your visit automatically.  In order to provide our full range of services, we may collect the following types of information:

    • The Internet domain (for example “abccompany.com”)
    • Your IP address (an IP address is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer whenever you are surfing on the Web) from which you access the sites
    • The type of browser and operating system you use to access the sites
    • The date and time you access the sites
    • The pages you visit within the sites
    • If you linked to our web site from another site, the address of that website

    This information does not identify you personally.  We may share with third parties certain pieces of aggregated, non-personal information, such as the number of users who searched for a particular term, for example, or how many users clicked on a particular advertisement.  Such information does not identify you individually.  NUL only processes this information for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy for specific services.  In addition to the above, such purposes include:

    • Providing our services, including the display of customized content and advertising;
    • Auditing, research and analysis in order to maintain, protect and improve our services;

    • Protecting the rights or property of NUL or our users; and
    • Developing new services.
  3. Information that you voluntarily provide:

    • We collect personally identifiable information when you send an email or other communications or fill out a form.  We may retain those communications in order to process your inquiries, respond to your requests and improve our services.
    • When you use certain services, such as our E-Store and our Donations page, we also request credit card or other payment account information which we maintain in encrypted form on secure servers.
    • We offer some of our services on or through other web sites. Personal information that you provide to those sites may be sent to us in order to deliver the service.  We process such information under this Privacy Policy. The affiliated sites through which our services are offered may have different privacy practices and we encourage you to read their privacy policies.

    • We collect information when you sign up for an IAE account or other NUL service or promotion that requires registration, or when you download or enable any gadget, mobile applications or software from the sites. The information collected by us when you enable a gadget or other application is processed under this privacy policy.
    • We may make available third party applications, gadgets or other tools through the services.  Information collected by the application, gadget or tool provider is governed by their privacy policies.
    • We may present links in a format that enables us to keep track of whether these links have been followed. We use this information to improve the quality of our search technology, customized content and advertising.
  4. Use of Information:
    We use your personal information to be able to give you personalized service (for example, information about your local area or your favorite subjects); to provide you with the information and services that you request; to send you email alerts; to let you donate online, etc.  You can easily opt-out at any time to stop any further contact with us. It also provides us aggregate data about our traffic (not identifying you personally, but showing how many visitors used which features, for example) to outside parties.

    We may combine the information you submit under your account with information from other NUL services or third parties in order to provide you with a better experience and to improve the quality of our services. For certain services, we may give you the opportunity to opt out of combining such information.

    If we become involved in a merger or acquisition, we will ensure the confidentiality of any personal information involved in such transactions and provide notice before personal information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

You decide how much information you feel comfortable sharing on IAE and you control how it is distributed through your privacy settings.  You should review the default privacy settings and change them if necessary to reflect your preferences. You should also consider your settings whenever you share information.  In addition, the affiliated sites through which our IAE services are offered may have different privacy practices and we encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Once you link to another website, you are then subject to the privacy policies of the new website.  We cannot control nor are we responsible for any third party collection or use or your personal information. Wildfire Call to Action

Pledge

I pledge to responsibly commit my time and talent to ensure that the nation is empowered to achieve the following goals by 2025:

  • Every American child is ready for college, work and life.
  • Every American has access to jobs with a living wage and good benefits.
  • Every American lives in safe, decent, affordable and energy efficient housing on fair terms.
  • Every American has access to quality and affordable health care solutions.
