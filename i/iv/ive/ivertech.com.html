Privacy Policy

Ivertech protects your privacy. Ivertech will not sell, rent or give away your personal information to any third party.  Any information that you trust us with and give to us is protected and held with the utmost security.

You can be assured that you will never receive any unsolicited e-mails or postal mail from any third party as a result of information you have given to Ivertech.

When you download evaluation products from Ivertech, we collect your email address for internal tracking purposes. When you purchase products from us, you will be required to provide your name, mailing address and email address so that we can send you a confirmation email and charge your credit card. Your personal information is safe with us and it will never be sold to any third party.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.

  • Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site.

  • Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

Ivertech takes your privacy seriously. Ivertech understands that the confidentiality of your data is important to you. Ivertech agrees to not sell, lease, or divulge personal or corporate data.

When you purchase or download products from Ivertech, you agree to accept this privacy policy and are aware that our privacy policy may change at any time. This policy will always be available for review at a visible place on our Web site.
