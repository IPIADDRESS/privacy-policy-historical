PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which Ivipid.com collects, uses, maintains and discloses information collected from users (hereinafter referred to as "a User") of the http://ivipid.com website (hereinafter referred to as "Site"). This privacy policy applies to the Site and all products and services offered by Ivipid.com

Personal identification information

We may collect personal identification information from a User in a variety of ways including but not limited to occasions when User visits our Site, registers on the Site, places an order, fills out a form, responds to surveys, as well as it concerns other activities, services, features or resources we make available on our Site. As appropriate, a User may be asked for his name or email address. A User may, however, visit our Site anonymously. We will collect personal identification information from a User only if he voluntarily submits such information to us. A User can always refuse to supply personal identification information, except that it may prevent him from engaging in certain Site related activities. We do not collect information about the specifics of who a User is unless he specifically provides it to us.

Non-personal identification information

We may collect non-personal identification information about Users whenever they interact with our Site. Non-personal identification information may include browser name, type of computer device and technical information about User’s means of connection to our Site, such as the operating system and the Internet service provider as well as other similar information.

Web browser cookies

Our Site may use "cookies" to enhance User experience. User's web browser places “cookies” on a his device’s hard drive in order to keep records and sometimes to track information about a User. A User may choose to set his web browser to refuse “cookies” or to alert him when “cookies” are being sent. If it happens so, note that some parts of the Site may not function properly.

How we use collected information

Ivipid.com may collect and use User’s personal information for the following purposes:

  1. To run and operate our Site
  2. To improve customer service

    Information you provide us with helps to respond to your requests to support service more efficiently.

  3. To personalize user experience

    We may use information to generally understand how our users as a group use the services and resources provided by our Site.

  4. To improve our Site

    We may use feedback you provide us with to improve our products and services.

  5. To process payments

    We may use the information a User provides about himself when placing an order only in order to execute this order. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to provide the service.

  6. To run a promotion, contest, survey or other Site activity

    To send User information he has agreed to receive about topics we think will be of interest to him.

  7. To manage product reviews and ratings

    We may use third party service providers to help us manage product reviews and ratings submitted by users.

  8. To send periodic emails

    We may use the email address to send User information and updates regarding his order. It may also be used to respond to User’s inquiries, questions, and/or other requests.

How we protect your information

We adopt appropriate data collection, storage and processing practices and security measures to protect User’s personal information, username, password, transactions information and data stored on our Site against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction. Security of your personal identification information is of utmost concern to us. User’s ivipid videos and other content are limited-access where access is gained through an obscured URL system. This obscured URL makes it difficult if not impossible for a third party to guess the URL address of User’s video or content without an invitation from this User including the relevant URL. However, because videos and content are not password-protected, a possibility exists that an unauthorized third party could discover User’s videos and/or content.

Sharing your personal information

We do not sell, trade, or rent User’s personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information irrelevant to any personal identification information of Site’s visitors and users with our business partners, trusted affiliates and advertisers for the purposes mentioned above. We may use third party service providers to help us to operate our business and the Site or administer activities on our behalf such as sending out newsletters or surveys. We may share User’s information with these third parties for those limited purposes provided that a User has given us his permission.

Accessing Web Account Information

A User must update his personal identification information promptly after it changes. A User may change this information by sending us an e-mail. A User may ask to have the information in his account to be deleted or removed at any time.
