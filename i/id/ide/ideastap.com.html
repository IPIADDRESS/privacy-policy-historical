Privacy Policy

1.1 General Information

The Peter De Haan Charitable Trust own and are responsible for the IdeasTap website, www.ideastap.com.

1.2 IdeasTap Privacy Statement

IdeasTap will use any personal information collected from the website in accordance with all laws and regulations relating to the protection of personal data including the Data Protection Act 1998 (the "Data Protection Act"). Further details on the Data Protection Act can be found on the Information Commissioner's website (www.informationcommissioner.gov.uk)

1.3 What information do we collect and why?

The information collected on the website includes:

1.3.1 Users' Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, which is automatically logged by our web server. IP addresses are used to observe users' interest in this website (number of visits, etc).

1.3.2 Cookies are very small text files saved to your computer used to track user navigation around a website. Session cookies are also used to store information on which content, including advertisements, have been seen by a user. Some areas of the IdeasTap website also use persistent cookies which are permanently stored on the users' computer. The IdeasTap website will use such cookies to identify unique users to our website.

We use website analytics to help us measure the effectiveness of our site and how visitors use our site. The type of information we collect includes content viewed, pages, site sections and videos. By supplementing our records, this information helps us learn things like what pages are most attractive to our visitors and what kinds of offers our customers like to see.

Disabling/Enabling Cookies - users have the ability to accept or disable cookies by modifying the settings in their browser. Disabling cookies however may mean that some functionality may be affected. For further directions on how to disable cookies please visit: www.allaboutcookies.org/manage-cookies/stop-cookies-installed.html

1.3.3 E-mail addresses, contact details and/or other details which users voluntarily provide in order to:

  • (a) provide goods and services to you;
  • (b) basic profiling purposes;
  • (c) let you know about other members, groups and services in which you may be interested;
  • (d) inform you about changes and improvements to the website;
  • (e) register for competitions and other premium content;
  • (f) participate in discussion forums (see below); and
  • (g) provide content to IdeasTap (see below).

If you are a member of a group, the group creator/administrator has the option to send you, via the IdeasTap, e-mail communications alerting you to information posted on the IdeasTap regarding the group.

IdeasTap will not disclose voluntarily provided details to third parties or use the information in any other way without your prior consent. On all pages where personal data is collected, specific details on the use of this information will be provided.

1.4 Your rights

You have the right to access your information held by IdeasTap and, where necessary, to have it amended. For more details, please contact: The Peter De Haan Charitable Trust, 1 China Wharf, 29 Mill Street, London SE1 2BQ or us the info@ideastap.com

1.5 Forums, Profiles and User Generated Content

Users are reminded that forums and areas of the website that allow you to submit content or set up a profile on the website are for public viewing and discussion. Any personal information supplied by users here is widely accessible. IdeasTap is not responsible for the content posted by users of these services.

1.6 Links to other websites

This website contains links to other sites that are completely unrelated to the IdeasTap. This privacy statement does not apply to these sites nor are we responsible for the content and practices of these websites.

1.7 Changes to our privacy statement

This privacy statement may change at any time, so you may wish to check it each time you visit our website.
