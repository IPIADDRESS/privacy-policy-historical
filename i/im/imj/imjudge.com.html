Privacy

At www.imjudge.com, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by www.imjudge.com and how it is used.

Log Files
Like many other Web sites, www.imjudge.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons
www.imjudge.com does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.

DoubleClick DART Cookie

  • Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to www.imjudge.com and other sites on the Internet.

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on www.imjudge.com send directly to your browsers.
