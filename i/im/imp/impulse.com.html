Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting the Impulse Point web site. Your privacy is very important to us and we are committed to protecting both the privacy and security of your personal information. Simply stated – our policy is to collect no personal information about you unless you choose to make such information available to us.

If you do choose to share personal information with us via the Web, we will not sell, rent, trade, or share that information; however, the information will be saved for a designated period of time to comply with the state of Florida’s archiving policies. Information will not be disclosed to third parties or other government agencies unless required by state or federal law.

Impulse Point will never collect or store personal information from its customer's constituents, and will never communicate directly with end user Safe•Connect Policy Key-enabled devices that exist outside of a Safe•Connect Managed Network environment.

children’s online privacy protection

Impulse Point is committed to protecting children’s privacy online. Our web site is not intentionally designed for or directed at children.
