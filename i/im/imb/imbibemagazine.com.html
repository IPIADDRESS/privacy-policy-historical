PRIVACY POLICY

When ordering a magazine subscription, single copy or book, we may ask you to supply certain personally identifying information, including: your name, postal address, e-mail address, phone number, organization or any other identifying information. This information will be shared with logistics partners—with whom we have non-disclosure agreements—for the express purpose of providing you with the products and services you have requested/purchased from Imbibe or its affiliates.

 

Occasionally we may make our customers' names and addresses available to carefully screened companies whose products or services we feel may interest you.
