Privacy Policy

When you use or visit our Platforms, you are accepting and consenting to the terms contained in this Privacy Policy .

Please note that if you click through to third party websites or other platforms, the terms of this Privacy Policy do not apply.

If you would like any more information or you have any comments about our Privacy Policy, please either write to us at Data Protection Manager, Icelolly Marketing Limited, 19-20 Park Row House, Park Row, Leeds LS1 5JF, or email us at data.protection@icelolly.com

1. The categories of Personal Information

1.1 When you use or visit our Platforms, we may collect information from you. Some of it may be Personal Information. This information can include search history, IP address, screen resolution, browser used, operating system and settings, access times and referring URL. If you are using a mobile device, we may also collect data that identifies your device, your settings and your location.

1.2 If you choose to register or login to our Platforms, we may collect your email address and password. If you choose a third-party service, like Facebook Login, to register or login to our Platforms, you are permitting us to use and store information related to your social media account.

1.3 When you give us Personal Information, you are telling us that the information is true, accurate, complete and current. You are also telling us that you have the authorisation to provide it to us.

2. How we collect your Personal Information

2.1 Directly from you. For example, when you provide us with your Personal Information when you register or use our online chat products

2.2 Passively. For example, We collect information about you over time when you visit our Platforms. We also use tracking tools like cookies, analytical tools and beacons.

2.3 From third parties. This can include when you log in using a third party platform, such as Facebook.

2.4 Platform features may make use of your device attributes and settings that will allow us to determine your physical location. Such technologies may include IP address mapping, WiFi, GPS signals, cell tower positioning or other technologies. 

3. Why we collect your Personal Information

3.1 We may use your Personal Information to enhance and personalise your experience and provide you with offers and services that may be of interest to you. For example, We may help you find deals flying from your closest airport or send you messages that may be of interest to you based on your current location. 

3.2 icelolly.com does not have control over your device settings, but We do recommend enabling location services on your device so you can take advantage of the location based features and functionality offered.

4. Using your Personal Information

4.1 Personal Information about our customers is an important part of our business and we shall only use your Personal Information for the following purposes and shall not keep such Personal Information longer than is necessary to fulfil these purposes:

4.1.1 To provide you with the products you are looking for, and improve our Platforms and services.

4.1.2 To better understand the users of our Platforms and services, to protect our property and to prevent damage to them.

4.1.3 To display more relevant advertising and recommendations, or suppress advertising and content that you might find irrelevant.

4.1.4 To contact you as follows:

4.1.4.1 To send alerts and notifications you have subscribed to, including to your mobile device;

4.1.4.2 To solicit reviews;

4.1.4.3 To get in touch if you contact icelolly.com customer service;

4.1.4.4 To send you marketing emails from an icelolly.com address regarding our services or those of our partners, if you have opted-in to receive emails from us;

4.1.5 To estimate our audience size and usage pattern;

4.1.6 To store information about your preferences, and so allow us to customise our platforms according to your individual interests.

4.1.7 To speed up your searches

4.1.8 To recognise you when you return to our platforms.

5.1 We may share your Personal Information with third parties for our legitimate business purposes as permitted by law. 5.5 We may disclose Personal Information if required by law, for example to law enforcement or other authorities. This includes court orders, subpoenas and orders arising from legal processes, and administrative or criminal investigations. We may also disclose your Personal Information if the disclosure is necessary for the prevention, detection or prosecution of criminal acts or to prevent other damage, or in response to a legal action or to enforce our rights and claims.

5.6 We may disclose your Personal Information to any member of our group of companies, which means any subsidiary or holding company of icelolly.com and a "subsidiary" includes a "subsidiary" and a "subsidiary undertaking" (each as defined in the Companies Act 2006) and a "holding company" includes a "holding company" and a "parent undertaking" (each as defined in the Companies Act 2006).

If we become aware that a minor is attempting to submit Personal Information, we will remove this Personal Information from our records. If you are the parent/legal guardian of a minor who has given us Personal Information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

5.8 We may also share anonymous aggregated usage information with others.

6.1 Our servers and data centres are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), although the data that we collect from you may be transferred to, and stored at, a destination outside the EEA.

6.2 It may also be processed by staff operating outside the EEA who work for us or for one of our business partners. Such staff may be engaged in, among other things, the fulfilment of your order, the processing of your payment details and the provision of support services.

7.1 Cookies

7.1.1 When we provide services, we want to make them easy, useful and reliable. This sometimes involves placing small amounts of information on your computer’s hard drive that records how you’ve used a website. These are called ‘cookies’. These cookies cannot be used to identify you personally and are used to improve services for you. So, for example, the next time you visit that website, it can tailor your options based on the information it has stored about your last visit. 

7.1.2 To learn more about cookies, see www.allaboutcookies.org or www.google.com/policies/technologies/cookies/

7.1.3 Your browser may give you the ability to control cookies. How to do this varies from browser to browser. You should view the Help menu on the browser you use for further information. By turning off cookies, you might not be able to use some of the products or services on our platforms.

7.1.4 We or our third party service providers may set and use analytics cookies. These allow us to gather aggregated or segmented information about the types of visitors that access our Platforms and the pages and advertisements that they view.

7.1.5 Icelolly.com also use data collection cookies to enhance your use of our website. These cookies are used to help us understand how you engage with the icelolly.com website so we can tailor content based on your interests.

7.1.6 By using the icelolly.com website, you agree that we can place these types of cookies on your device.

7.1.6.1 You may not be able to use any of the above features if you set your device to block cookies, delete cookies or opt out of online behavioural advertising. 

Please note that our advertisers or suppliers accessed through links displayed on our website may also use cookies, over which we have no control.

7.2.1 icelolly.com, our advertisers and our business partners use several common tracking tools. We use these tools in order to:

7.2.1.1 Track new visitors to our Platforms;

7.2.1.2 Help us recognise your browser as a previous visitor. This includes saving and remembering any preferences that may have been set while your browser was visiting our Platforms, such as language;

7.2.1.3 Store your username and password if you are registered with us;

7.2.1.4 Work with online advertising companies to display targeted advertising on our Platforms and third party platforms that you visit. This targeting may be based on information you submit on our Platforms or third party platforms. This targeting may also be based on your activities or behaviours on our Platforms or those of third parties;

7.2.1.5 Help improve our website offering and for capacity planning purposes. In particular, We, our advertisers and our business partners may use tracking tools such as web beacons.
