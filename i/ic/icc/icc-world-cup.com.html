ICC Cricket World Cup 2015

The ICC Cricket World Cup 2015 is all set after a long time wait of four years. It is good new for India, the country will get to host the tenth world Cup of Its most loved sport.


The Indian fans are crazy about the game of Cricket. India will host the world cup along with Sri Lanka and Bangladesh. It will be great proud for Bangladesh as they hosting their first Cricket world Cup. The Top fourteen teams of World Cricket are scheduled to compete with each other for this coveted cup.


The battle will be began on 19 feb when India takes on Bangladesh in the 1st match. The formerly announced time-table of 2011 world cup also included Pakistan as a co-hosting nation but the 2009 attack on Sri Lankan cricket team at Lahore which left seven players injured, forced the world cup governing body (ICC) to strip off Pakistan of its co-hosting rights.


It is a big cricketing event as all the cricketing nation bidding for hosting rights. In the bidding of 2011 world cup event Australia and New Zealand had an upper hand over the trio of India, Sri Lanka and Bangladesh. The Asian countries not got the chance of hosting this major event since 1992 but the India's argument and their good relation ship with their neighbor countries helped India to clinch the final deal. The 2015 cricket wold cup will be held in Australia or New Zealand.
