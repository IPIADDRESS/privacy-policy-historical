ICMA Web Site Privacy Policy

icma.org is the Web site for the International City/County Management Association (ICMA).

ICMA can be reached via e-mail through contact information at the Contacting ICMA Web Page, and by telephone at (202) 289-4262.

ICMA is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to our Web site. When you visit icma.org, our server automatically collects only your domain name (for example, "aol.com, ibm.com," etc.) and the type of Web browser software that you use. ICMA does not acquire your e-mail address or other information without your knowledge. The information that ICMA collects about your domain name and browser is used to help ICMA improve the content and functionality of its Web site.

ICMA’s Web server may ask your Web browser to store a "cookie," which is a small piece of information that the Web server uses to keep track of various user preferences. Cookies are not used to save or retrieve any sensitive data about visitors to icma.org.

If you supply ICMA with a postal address or e-mail address by completing an online form, you may receive periodic mailings from ICMA regarding either orders you have placed or information about new products and services or upcoming events. In addition, because ICMA occasionally shares its mailing list, you may receive information from selected reputable organizations whose resources or services may interest you. ICMA will not share your telephone number or e-mail addresses with other companies.

If you would prefer that ICMA not share your contact information, please send e-mail to do-not-share@icma.org. The e-mail message must include your complete name and address, and you must request that you be added to the "do-not-share" list. If you would prefer not to be contacted by ICMA, please send e-mail to "do-not-contact@icma.org." Include your complete name and address and we will be sure that your name is added to our "do-not-contact" list.

If you supply ICMA with your telephone number, you may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding an order you have placed online. Very rarely you also may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such telephone calls, please let us know by sending e-mail to "do-not-call@icma.org." Please provide us with your full name and correct telephone number, and we will be sure your name is added to the "do-not-call" list.

Thank you for visiting icma.org.

Its membership includes city managers, county managers, and other chief appointed officials and assistants in local governments throughout the world.
