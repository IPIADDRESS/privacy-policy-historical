Privacy policy

This privacy policy applies only to this website and does not include any third party websites linked to icould. Users should carefully examine the privacy statements of sites appearing on or linking to or from icould. Personal information that you choose to provide to other sites will be subject to the privacy policies of that website.

The icould website automatically collects a small amount of non-personally identifiable data from you when you visit; this is limited to the pages you visit, and at what day/time. It also collects information about the browser and operating system you are using and from which domain (internet address) you originate. This information is collected from our server logs.

The details which you provide about yourself and any other information which identifies you are held on our computer system. These details are used for operational purposes, for example to verify your password to allow you access to extra functionality on the website.

We will not pass your personal information to anyone outside icould.

We may use your personal information to contact you (including by phone, fax, email and SMS) about new features, services, products and special offers. If you do not wish to receive this information you may choose not to do so by clicking where indicated on the registration page or by telephoning us. If you initially wish to receive such material but you change your mind later, you may tell us by editing your account with us, sending us an email at webmaster@icould.org.uk or by telephoning us on 0800 112 3504.
