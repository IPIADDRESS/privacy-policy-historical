  4. We may also use any information you provide to us where required to do so by law or to enforce our terms and conditions of use of the Web Site.
  5. To enable us to monitor and improve the Web Site, we may gather certain information about you when you use it, including details of your operating system, browser version, domain name and IP address, and the details of the web site you came from. This information will not identify you personally.

  8. We endeavour to take all reasonable steps to protect your personal information including the use of encryption technology, but cannot guarantee the security of any information you disclose online.

You have the right, in certain circumstances, to receive information about the personal information we maintain on you.
