ICONOCAST Privacy Statement      

ICONOCAST Inc. is a licensee of the TRUSTe Privacy Program. This statement discloses the privacy practices for the ICONOCAST Web site. When you visit a Web site displaying the TRUSTe trustmark, you can expect to be notified of:

  1. What personally identifiable information of yours is collected;
  2. What organization is collecting the information; How the information is used;
  3. With whom the information may be shared;
  4. What choices are available to you regarding collection, use and distribution of the information;
  5. What kind of security procedures are in place to protect the loss, misuse or alteration of information under the company's control; and
  6. How you can correct any inaccuracies in the information.

Security
Protecting your privacy and your information is a top priority at ICONOCAST. We have taken careful measures to secure your information and our physical premises.

Information Practices
Questions regarding this statement should be directed to ICONOCAST's site coordinator info@iconocast.com. If the site has not responded to your inquiry within seven days, or your inquiry has not been satisfactorily addressed, contact TRUSTe. To return to the site, please use your browser's "Back" button.

ICONOCAST uses your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our site. Your IP address is also used to gather broad demographic information.

Site Feedback
Under "About Us," we maintain email links to various members of the ICONOCAST organization for feedback on products and services. We collect visitors' contact information (like e-mail address). The customer's contact information is used only to respond to their inquiries.

E-mail List
If you choose to receive our free weekly e-cast, we ask for an e-mail address you would like ICONOCAST sent to. We only use e-mail addresses to send ICONOCAST. We do not sell or rent out your e-mail address to marketers. In order to provide you with this service, e-mail addresses are shared with Media Synergy Inc. The customer's contact information is used to contact the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see "Delete/Deactivate" below.

Online Surveys
Occasionally we send out an online survey via e-mail to random subscribers of the newsletter. Our online surveys ask visitors for contact information (like their e-mail address) and general demographic data (like their gender, geographic location or occupation). We use the results of surveys on an aggregate basis, so that we can improve ICONOCAST. No personal information is shared with third parties. We do not sell or rent out your e-mail address to marketers. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see "Delete/Deactivate" below.

Tell Jaco
Optionally, visitors to the "Tell Jaco" page on our site can submit timely tips and rumors for potential publication in the "Jacobyte" section of our weekly ICONOCAST e-mail newsletters. In exchange, users are rewarded with gifts supplied by an ICONOCAST marketing partner. For gift mailing purposes only, we collect name, mailing address and phone number. We do not sell or rent out this information to marketers.

Delete/Deactivate
To remove yourself entirely from our database, visit our ICONOCAST Opt-Out Center.

Change/Modify
To change or update your profile, follow the instructions at the bottom of your ICONOCAST newsletter.
