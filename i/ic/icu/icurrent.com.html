iCurrent Privacy Policy
iCurrent cares about your privacy and takes it very seriously. By using iCurrent you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy.

What information do we collect
We collect both personal and usage information.

Personal
Your name, email address, password, physical address, and phone numbers. We may also collect additional information that will help us provide you with better service. This information might include your age, gender, zip-code, occupation, and user profile information you submit.

Usage of Service
Information about your usage of iCurrent, including your login history, activity, and error logs. We will also collect information about your computer, and may use what is called a “cookie” to help provide you with a continuous experience and help us record how our service is being used.

What we do with your information
Our goal is to provide you with the best user experience possible. The information you provide will help us enhance and improve our service for the benefit of you and our other customers.

We may share your information with our authorized agents and suppliers or resellers in order to provide you with better service. We may also disclose your information in order to comply with legal requirements and obligations.

We will never sell or rent your personal information to a third party without your express permission. Never!

How we keep your information safe
We store your information of secure servers and restrict access. We use encryption, firewalls, other methods to keep you information very safe. If you lose your password, we will generate a new one and send it to you after verifying your identity.

Control of your information
You can ask that we stop using your information for certain purposes by sending us an email at privacy@icurrent.com or by writing us at Privacy, iCurrent, Inc., 400 Oyster Point Blvd., Suite 124, Oyster Point Marina Plaza, South San Francisco, CA 94080 USA.

You have full control over your information and preferences. You can update your information, preferences, and interests at any time. If you need to make a change and are having trouble, just send us an email. If at anytime we change this policy, we’ll let you know.
