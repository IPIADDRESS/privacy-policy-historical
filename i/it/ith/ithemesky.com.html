Your privacy is important to iThemeSky, as is your trust in iThemeSky services. iThemeSky does not divulge or sell your information to any 3rd party unless under legal order to do so. This privacy policy outlines how your information is processed and used here.

Information Collection

During the contact process, iThemeSky requires customers to supply necessary information such as their names, emails, project information, etc. We collect only necessary information so customers can be contacted for business or informational purposes and payments verified. We may send you email notifications or otherwise contact you about our new or existing products, services and special offers.

Cookies

When you use some online services of iThemeSky, a cookie text file may be stored on your local computer for sessions only. This enables you to remain logged in while using the services. iThemeSky does not track your browsing habits with its cookies.

Cookies are small files which many websites transfer to your hard disk. They can inform the website what pages you visit, and your preferences, which enable websites to provide you with a more personalized service and experience. You may set your browser to refuse cookies, or to warn you before accepting them.
