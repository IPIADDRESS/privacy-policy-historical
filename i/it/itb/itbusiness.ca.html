Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to us. At IT Business Group we believe in full disclosure regarding the information we gather and what we do with it. We also believe in giving you a choice regarding the use of your personal information.

Information Collection & Use:

When you become a subscriber to daily eNews or to an IT Business magazine, we will contact you using several different methods, each of which you can control:

EMAIL

Daily eNews – The daily eNews provides late-breaking news of interest to IT professionals. If you prefer to stop receiving the daily eNews, please choose the Unsubscribe option available on every communication. Or follow the Contact Us procedure (below.)

Editorial Supplements/Advertorials – On occasion, you may receive editorial and/or product and service information by email that we think is of interest to you, based on information you provided. If you prefer to stop receiving such emails, please choose the Unsubscribe option available on every communication. Or follow the Contact Us procedure (below.)

3rd Party Advertising - On occasion we may send you advertising material from carefully screened 3rd parties. These email communications will always identify IT Business Group as the source. If you prefer to stop receiving such emails, please choose the Unsubscribe option available on every communication. Or follow the Contact Us procedure (below.)

Magazine Subscription – When appropriate you will receive renewal notices by email. If you prefer to stop receiving such emails, please choose the Unsubscribe option available on every communication. Or follow the Contact Us procedure (below.)

ITBusiness.ca WEBSITE

Like many commercial web sites, our site utilizes a standard technology called a "cookie" to collect information about how our site is used. Our web site uses cookies to enhance your visit. A cookie can securely store a password, personalize a home page, identify a part of a site that has been visited before or keep track of a selection (such as an item in a "shopping cart.") Cookies on the ITBusiness.ca web site may collect your domain name and track your movement through our web pages. A cookie cannot retrieve data from your hard drive, pass on a computer virus, or capture your e-mail address.

The IT Business Group uses "cookies" only to gather the information indicated in this policy. The IT Business Group does not use cookies to gather personal identifying information about you.

Some of the banner ads displayed on our ITBusiness.ca are provided by outside companies. These ads may contain cookies. Cookies associated with such banner ads collect information for the advertiser. IT Business Group does not have access to this information. Cookies, in and of themselves, do not contain any personally identifiable information about you.

WEBSITE TERMS OF USE:

By using ITBusiness.ca, you consent to the collection and use of your information by IT Business Group within the policies stated above. If we update our privacy policy, we will post the updated policy on this page. Our goal is to make you fully aware of the information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.
