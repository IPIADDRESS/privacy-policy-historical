About DMO?

Digital Media Online, Inc. (DMO) publishes on-line magazines covering several unique yet related vertical industries including the consumer electronics industry and the digital media industry. The company's goal is to expand its' reach into numerous vertical markets, to provide its' readership with a wide array of editorial options and professional tools, and to offer its' vendors with various opportunities to reach those readers.

DMO is the world's largest publisher in the digital media market (with over 1 million unique visitors on a monthly basis) and its' home page into this market is www.digitalmedianet.com (DMN). This site serves as a hub to 40-plus sites in that market. The sites comprise a community or a one-stop destination where digital media professionals can find the tools and information they need to enhance their professional skills. These tools include industry news, product tutorials, feature articles, research, product reviews, profiles of industry leaders, and an extensive network of user forums at DMN Forums (www.dmnforums.com).

DMO's home page into the newly entered consumer electronics market is www.consumerelectronicsnet.com (CEN). This site is directed at the newest and varied elements of personal electronics. The editorial focus on this community of sites is aimed at providing the readers with product reviews and information that will assist them in their purchasing decisions. The sites also provide the opportunity to shop the various on-line resellers for their best price.

DMO will be launching other vertical online communities in the near future.

DMO's Online Privacy Policy

Digital Media Online, Inc. (DMO) believes strongly in the right of individual privacy. In accordance with the guidelines prepared by the Online Privacy Alliance (http://www.privacyalliance.org/), the following paragraphs detail our information-gathering policies.

Browser information collected on the website: DMO analyzes its website logs to constantly improve the value of the materials available on the website. These website logs are not personally identifiable, and no attempt is made to link them with the individuals that browse our sites.

Use of cookies: As part of offering and providing customizable and personalized services, DMO may use cookies to store and sometimes track information about our readers. A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. DMO will not sell or provide this information to outside parties.

Privacy of our email lists: DMO maintains several email lists to keep its' members, the press, and interested members of the general public informed about breaking events. Individuals must affirmatively request to join our mailing lists by providing information on a form on our web site, by requesting this information via email, by signing up for this information at industry tradeshows, or by entering one of our contests. In addition, we configure our list server software to refuse to divulge the email addresses of our list subscribers to anyone including other list subscribers. Only a limited number of authorized Digital Media Online staff has access to this data.

Vendor Sponsored Promotions: In some promotions where prizes are given, we do gather information on individuals entering the contests via entry forms or email. In some cases that information will be shared with the sponsoring vendor. In these instances the contest terms will clearly state this fact.
