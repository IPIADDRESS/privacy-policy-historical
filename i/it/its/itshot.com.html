Security

ItsHot utilizes a number of security technologies and measures to assure the protection of your personal information when ordering over the net. All personal information will be kept confidential by using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology. SSL encrypts your order information to prevent any improper use or inaccuracy of your personal information.

ItsHot will replenish, rectify or erase any incomplete, inaccurate or outdated personal data retained by ItsHot in connection with the operation of this Site, at your request. Cookies

ItsHot uses cookies on certain pages to determine the traffic on our site, measure promotional effectiveness, track visits from our affiliates and partners and allow the use of our shopping cart. We do not use cookies to save or gather any personal information.
