    2. Personal Information

    ItGetsBetter collects and stores information that you enter in connection with the Services. For example, ItGetsBetter may collect your IP address, browser information and website domain name every time you use the Services.     4. Use of Cookies and Spyware

    ItGetsBetter does not use or install spyware on your computer. ItGetsBetter does not use spyware to retrieve information from your computer. Like many websites, ItGetsBetter uses “cookies,” which are files stored on your computer’s hard drive by your browser. Cookies help ItGetsBetter identify users and optimize their website experience. Most browsers accept cookies automatically but allow you to disable them. ItGetsBetter recommends that you leave cookies enabled so it can offer you a better website experience.

ItGetsBetter may also use or share with others Anonymous Information in any other manner that ItGetsBetter deems appropriate or necessary.

    ItGetsBetter’s Use of Your Personal Information.

    ItGetsBetter may use your Personal Information to contact you about programs and opportunities related to the mission of ItGetsBetter. For example, ItGetsBetter may use your Personal Information to contact you about new offers, newsletters, action alerts, press releases, information about ItGetsBetter programs, volunteer opportunities and donation appeals.

    ItGetsBetter may share your Personal Information with non-affiliated third parties that are trusted, like-minded organizations. These practices are intended to allow ItGetsBetter and trusted, like-minded organizations to communicate with a broader audience about community events, policy concerns, fundraising endeavors, and other information that is likely to be of shared interest.

These entities have access to the Personal Information needed to perform their functions and are contractually obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of Personal Information. Even though we are distant, we are not so different, you and I. This is a picture of me when I was a teen. You can tell by looking at me that I felt different, unhappy.
