We will collect and use personal information solely with the objective of fulfilling those purposes specified by us and for other compatible purposes, unless we obtain the consent of the individual concerned or as required by law.

Our website does not provide any personal information to these third parties. You can choose to disable or selectively turn off cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

Distribution of Information

This site does not share visitors’ personally identifiable information to any third party for marketing purposes. We may share information with governmental agencies or other companies assisting us in fraud prevention or investigation.
