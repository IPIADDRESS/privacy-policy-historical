iLOVEclicks Privacy Policy

The iLOVEclicks Network has the utmost respect for your privacy.

Any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to the iLOVEclicks Network will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to. A more detailed explanation about how we safeguard your personal information is described below. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to let us know.

Resale or disclosure of information to third parties:

The iLOVEclicks Network does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including membership forms or email lists.

Browser information collected on the web site:

The iLOVEclicks Network analyzes our website logs to constantly improve the value of the materials available on website.

Moreoever, our website logs are an invaluable tool in catching people who might be attempting to cheat the network and, by association, you. Thus, we will occassionally attempt to link data found in our logs to individual members. That said, we will never distribute this information to any third party.

Use of cookies:

The iLOVEclicks Network uses session-level cookies (cookies that are destroyed as soon as you close your browser) to keep track of your user information as you move throughout the site. We are not using this information to "profile" you in any way. This is merely a tool that the software uses to remember who you are as you move from page-to-page.

The iLOVEclicks Network uses permanent cookies so that you do not have to enter your username each time you visit our login page. If you turn permanent cookies off in your browser, this will not in any way impact your ability to use the site.

Privacy of our email lists:

The iLOVEclicks Network maintains several email lists to keep iLOVEclicks members informed about new opportunities, site changes, etc.. Individuals must affirmatively ask to join our mailing lists when they sign up for an account. Users may remove themselves from our mailing list at any time by visiting http://www.iloveclicks.com/updateprofile.asp.

We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on our list to anyone. Only iLOVEclicks.com employees have access to the information you provide to us.

Collection of demographic data:

When you sign up for an account at iLOVEclicks, we ask you to provide us with your country and zip code. This information is optional and you do not have to provide it. We will only ever use this information for demographic targeting and will not provide it to any third party for any reason.
