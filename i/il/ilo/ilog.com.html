ILOG Privacy Policy

ILOG respects your privacy and recognizes your need for management and protection of personal information you share with ILOG, such as your name, address, and telephone number, etc.

You are welcome to visit our web site without informing ILOG who you are or revealing personal information about yourself. ILOG tracks the internet addresses of the domains from which visitors access this site, and analyzes this data for trends and statistics; individual users, however, remain anonymous.

ILOG has adopted the following principles to protect your privacy:

Cookies
Some ILOG web pages use cookies so we can better serve you with tailored information when you return to www.ilog.com. Cookies are identifiers web sites can send your browser to keep on your computer to facilitate subsequent visits. You can set your browserâ€™s preferences to notify you when you are sent cookies and let you decide whether to accept or reject them. The information ILOG collects with cookies and analyzes is used to improve our service to you and other visitors.

Choice
There are times when ILOG may collect personal information from you. ILOG will inform you before it does so and clearly explain what it intends to do with the information. You have the right not to provide the information, and in the future, you will be able to exclude certain uses of the information. If you choose not to provide the information requested, you are still welcome to visit most of our web site, but may be unable to access certain options, offers, and services.

Security
ILOG takes all appropriate and reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Commitment
ILOG is committed to the principle of individual personal privacy, and is actively involved in and supports industry initiatives to preserve individual privacy rights on the internet. Personal data privacy is a new and evolving area, and our web site is evolving to meet these demands. Please contact ILOG at webmaster@ilog.com with any privacy issues and/or comments or questions regarding this policy.
