Our Privacy Policy


WE RESPECT YOUR PRIVACY

Last Updated: April 30, 2019

NOTICE: Click here for important information about safe surfing from the Federal Trade Commission.

Maximilian Ventures LLC, a Delaware limited liability company doing business as "PublicLegal" and "PublicLegalForms.com", owns and operates the Internet Legal Research Group website, www.ilrg.com (hereafter, the "Site" or "ILRG.com"). Maximilian Ventures LLC promotes trust, privacy and security as core attributes of our service to our user. Maximilian Ventures LLC has adopted this Privacy Policy ("Policy") to communicate clearly our firm commitment to your privacy. It is this respect for user privacy that is a fundamental component to our mission. Using our website demonstrates your consent to Maximilian Ventures LLC's collection and use of information pursuant to this Policy, and continued use of this site following the posting of changes to this Policy will signify your acceptance of such changes. This Policy governs the use of your name, e-mail address, payment information, and similar personal information that you may provide us in the course of using our website, products or services.

If you have an unresolved privacy or data use concern that we have not addressed satisfactorily, please contact our U.S.-based third party dispute resolution provider (free of charge) at https://feedback-form.truste.com/watchdog/request.

Information Collection and Use. Maximilian Ventures LLC collects personal information to provide certain products and services to our customers. For example, if you:

  • Place a product or service order, Maximilian Ventures LLC or one of our contracted third-party payment processing partners (Apple, Amazon, PayPal, or Stripe) will receive your name, e-mail address, and credit card information only to fulfill your order and charge you for the product or service you have ordered. We or our partner encrypts your information using industry-leading encryption technology. We do not retain or store credit your card information, except for the last four digits of your credit card number, which we retain only for the purpose of authenticating you in case you contact us later for help or support with your order.
  • Post information on any of our interactive areas, Maximilian Ventures LLC will collect information from you in order to e-mail you major updates about Internet Legal Research Group, the products and services;
  • Sign-up for the Internet Legal Research Group mailing list, Maximilian Ventures LLC will collect information from you to facilitate the publishing of that mailing list; and
  • Send our link to others through our website, Maximilian Ventures LLC will collect the information from you only for the purpose of facilitating the transmission of the e-mail. We will send this email only and no following promotional emails unless the recipient specifically chooses to receive future promotional emails from us.

Information that you submit about yourself may be used by Maximilian Ventures LLC to provide you requested services, communicate with you about our products, improve your online experience, or support our continuing efforts to offer you the information and services you want most. We do not share personal information about you with non-affiliated companies or organizations outside of Maximilian Ventures LLC without your explicit permission. Maximilian Ventures LLC does, however, provide aggregate statistics and information about our sales, traffic patterns and related site information to third parties.

We will share your personal information with third parties only in the ways that are described in this privacy policy. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

In certain situations, Maximilian Ventures LLC may be required to disclose personal data in response to lawful requests by public authorities, including to meet national security or law enforcement requirements. We reserve the right to disclose your personal information as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, bankruptcy proceedings, or similar legal process, and when we believe that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights and/or comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process served on our Web site. If Maximilian Ventures LLC is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, you will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on our Web site of any change in ownership or uses of your personal information, as well as any choices you may have regarding your personal information.

Confidentiality and Security. Maximilian Ventures LLC limits access to personal information about you to employees and contractors who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs. Maximilian Ventures LLC has physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal regulations to protect personal information about you. In addition, as a condition of employment or business partnership, Maximilian Ventures LLC requires all its employees and contractors to sign confidentiality contracts. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, however. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. If you have any questions about security on our Web site, you can contact us by emailing webmaster03@ilrg.com.

Annual Certification of Responsible Data Collection and Processing Practices. To help ensure purchasing from us is safe, Maximilian Ventures LLC undertakes extraordinary efforts to adopt and to maintain policies, practices, and procedures consistent with regulatory expectations and external standards for privacy accountability. We place the highest priority on safeguarding the personally identifiable information of our customers and all other highly sensitive information, such as your credit card details. Our privacy practices are certified and audited on an annual basis by TrustArc (TRUSTe), the leading organization that independently certifies the privacy policies and practices of e-commerce businesses. TrustArc has certified Maximilian Ventures LLC for meeting the extensive criteria of the TRUSTe Enterprise Privacy & Data Governance Practices program.
