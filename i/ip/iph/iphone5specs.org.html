Privacy Policy

We at www.iphone5specs.org do not gather any personal information or engage in any activities that are considered spam.

Log Files

Like the majority of website servers, we use log files: IP address, type of browser used, referring website, time&date. These are used to analyze trends, optimize our website, gather demographic info, and are not linked to the user’s identity info.

Cookies

Our 3rd party vendor, Google, uses DART cookies in order to serve ads on our website. This type of cookies help Google served ads based on the user’s visits to our website and other websites on the Web. Visitors can opt out of the use of DART cookies by accessing Google’s privacy policy here: http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html.

Links

Our website contains links to other websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices, and we advise our visitors to always check the privacy policy of all websites.

Advertisers

We work with 3rd party companies, like Google, to display ads on our website. These advertisements may contain cookies that are collected by the advertisement companies. We do not have access to this info.
