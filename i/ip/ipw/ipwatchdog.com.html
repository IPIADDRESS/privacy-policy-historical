Privacy Policy

This is how information learned about you from your visit to our website is handled. The information received depends on what you do when you visit IPWatchdog.com.

If you visit IPWatchdog.com to browse, or to read or download information:

My hosting company collects and stores various information, but to the best of my knowledge and understanding none of the information collected is personally identifying information. The information that is collected, and to which I have access, includes: (1) the name of the domain and host from which you access the Internet (for example, aol.com or princeton.edu); (2) the browser software you use and your operating system; (3) the date and time you access the site; and (4) the Internet address of the website from which you linked directly to IPWatchdog.com.

Information collected by my hosting company is used to measure the number of visitors, where and how visitors find out about IPWatchdog.com, and daily traffic. I do not consider these and other general Web site statistics to be confidential.

If you choose to identify yourself by sending me an email:

You have the freedom to select what if any personally identifiable information is sent to me when you send an e-mail. Please know that when responding to e-mail questions I typically respond to the comment or inquiry and delete the communication. In some circumstances I do save e-mail communications in order to facilitate further correspondence. I also typically save e-mail addresses and other contact information in the contact folder of my mail program. Typically, I will periodically go through my contact folder and delete addresses and contact information that I no longer need.

If you subscribe to News Alerts your e-mail address and name will be saved and used to provide periodic e-mail alerts. If at any time you wish to unsubscribe just sent an e-mail to unsubscribe@ipwatchdog.com.

If you post a question or comment to the Message Board:

Any information contained in a post to my Message Board is available for everyone to see. You have the freedom to select what if any personally identifiable information is posted. You should not post sensitive information or personally identifiable information on the Message Board. You may post anonymously and need not provide your name or e-mail address. If you do wish to provide an e-mail address you may wish to consider setting up a web based e-mail account first and then providing that address. This will allow others who read your post to contact you directly and privately, but you will not be providing your personal e-mail address.

Please be aware that there are many links on IPWatchdog.com to sites that are owned, operated and maintained by others. I do not have any control over these sites. When visiting a new site you should refer to the privacy policy that governs that particular site. I make no representations regarding the privacy practices of sites that you link to from IPWatchdog.com. I merely provide these links to facilitate the acquisition of hopefully useful information.
