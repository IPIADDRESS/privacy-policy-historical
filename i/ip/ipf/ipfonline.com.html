Privacy Policy

IPFONLINE is committed to your privacy. IPFONLINE is committed to protecting your privacy when you visit our web site. We believe in, and practice, full disclosure and informed consent with respect to your personal information.

It is our hope that by doing so we will earn your trust and confidence.

The following is a disclosure of our privacy policy with respect to your information.

Generally, no information is requested from you.

At IPFONLINE we let you retain as much control as possible over your personal information. Generally, you may visit and use our site at anytime without telling us who you are or revealing any information about yourself. This applies to those portions of our site that are not restricted to use by our registered members. The download portion of our site is an example of a site section restricted to use by our registered members.

Occasionally, limited information is needed from you.

Sometimes we need information from you. On occasion, we might request your e-mail address (to correspond with you), your name and address (to register you at your request as a member) or your user name and password (to allow you to download sample contracts, employee handbooks and other new products in the industrial market from India and abroad from our site).

We do not sell or lease your personal information.

When you choose to provide us with your personal information via the Internet, we presume you want that information kept private. Therefore, we do not sell or lease your personal information with other companies or individuals.

Like most Internet sites, when possible, we track how people like you came to visit our site. Such data is then analyzed to determine trends, statistics and the effectiveness of our links and ad banners located on other Internet sites.

We have implemented these practices for your protection.

Please direct your questions regarding our privacy policy to our IPFONLINE site coordinator for clarification.
