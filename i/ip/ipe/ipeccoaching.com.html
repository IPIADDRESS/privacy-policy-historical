Our Privacy Policy
We never share your information, except as necessary to complete transactions initiated by you, the web visitor. This site collects personal information for the purposes of its functions, such as collecting necessary information to facilitate of purchasing merchandise or registering for an event online.

This information is kept in the strictest confidence by iPEC.
Security
Our online purchasing system is a robust and secure online payment gateway, protected by 128-bit strong encryption on our secure servers. This is the highest level of server security available for online purchases, and it is the same encryption system used by major online stores.
Your credit card information is not kept on file. Once your order is processed, or the event for which you registered has occurred, your order will be deleted.
If you have any further questions about privacy, please feel free to contact us.
