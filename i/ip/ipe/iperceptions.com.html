Overview

iPerceptions Inc. ("iPerceptions") recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of all respondents to our on-line surveys. Your privacy is fundamental and essential to ensure the validity of our survey process. We are committed to uphold the principles of honesty and integrity with all respondents to our surveys. It is your opinion that is requested, not your identity.

iPerceptions does not collect any information to be used to identify an individual respondent during the course of a survey. If an incentive award is offered to complete a survey questionnaire, iPerceptions acts solely as an agent of the website owner whose website is being surveyed (the "Owner"). As the Owner's agent, iPerceptions collects only the information necessary to deliver the incentive award. When offered, it is completely optional as to whether you elect to receive the incentive award. See below for details on Contact Information.

Information Collected by iPerceptions

Surveys
The information provided by you on our survey is compiled and aggregated to provide generic profiles of the users of the website being surveyed. Open-ended responses are compiled and reported 'as is. Our survey process does not require us to know, nor report, the identity of any individual survey respondent.

In addition to the information provided by you, our survey tracks user IP addresses, browser type and access time for demographic and web site administration purposes. This information does not contain anything that can identify respondents personally.
Cookies
iPerceptions uses non-persistent cookies only for the purpose of ensuring that you are not solicited to reply to our survey more than once during the survey period. See below for a definition and use of cookies.
Contact Information
In order to protect your anonymity and respect your privacy our surveys and their reports do not collect your name or personal contact information.
If it is your wish to receive the award incentive if same is offered, iPerceptions will provide the means to you to voluntarily supply sufficient information to allow the Owner or incentive award distributor to deliver it to you.

This contact information includes only the information needed to deliver the incentive award such as your e-mail address, or your name, telephone number and postal address. IT IS COMPLETELY OPTIONAL AS TO WHETHER YOU PROVIDE THIS INFORMATION.

On the close of a survey, only the contact information supplied by you is forwarded to the Owner and/or incentive award distributor. No other information is supplied. Incentive awards are administered by, and are the sole responsibility of, the Owner. iPerceptions's responsibility is limited to providing the Owner and/or the incentive award distributor with only the contact information as provided by you and collected during the course of a survey.

The contact information is stored on iPerception's servers at the address below. It will not be provided to any other third party, nor will it be used by iPerceptions to contact you or for any other purpose.

General

iPerceptions is not responsible for the privacy policies and content of websites which are being surveyed nor to websites to which it may provide links. We encourage you to read the privacy statements of these sites, as they will differ from ours.

iPerceptions uses all reasonable precautions to keep secure any personal information disclosed during the survey process. This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse or alteration of the information under our control.

Cookies

Cookies are commonly used by many web sites to facilitate the site visit. A cookie is a data file that is transferred to your browser by a Web Server. Only the Web server sending the cookie has access to it.

Our surveys require that your browser be set to accept cookies only to ensure that you are not solicited to reply to our survey more than once while the current survey is running. After that it automatically expires and our Web server can no longer access it.

On initial setup, most web browsers are set to accept cookies. On most browsers, you can set an option for the browser to notify you when a cookie is being sent with the option of accepting or rejecting the cookie.
