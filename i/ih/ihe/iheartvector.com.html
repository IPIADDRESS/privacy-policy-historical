Privacy Policy:

IHeartVector ("IHeartVector") operates IHeartVector.com and may operate other websites. It is IHeartVector's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our websites.

Website Visitors

Like most website operators, IHeartVector collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. IHeartVector's purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how IHeartVector's visitors use its website. From time to time, IHeartVector may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

IHeartVector also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on IHeartVector.com blogs/sites. IHeartVector only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below, except that commenter IP addresses and email addresses are visible and disclosed to the administrators of the blog/site where the comment was left.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain visitors to IHeartVector's websites choose to interact with IHeartVector in ways that require IHeartVector to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that IHeartVector gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up at IHeartVector.com to provide a username and email address. Those who engage in transactions with IHeartVector are asked to provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to process those transactions. In each case, IHeartVector collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor's interaction with IHeartVector. IHeartVector does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

Aggregated Statistics

IHeartVector may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. IHeartVector may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, IHeartVector does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information

IHeartVector discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on IHeartVector's behalf or to provide services available at IHeartVector's websites, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Some of those employees, contractors and affiliated organizations may be located outside of your home country; by using IHeartVector's websites, you consent to the transfer of such information to them. IHeartVector will not rent or sell potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information to anyone. Other than to its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations, as described above, IHeartVector discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only in response to a subpoena, court order or other governmental request, or when IHeartVector believes in good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the property or rights of IHeartVector, third parties or the public at large. If you are a registered user of an IHeartVector website and have supplied your email address, IHeartVector may occasionally send you an email to tell you about new features, solicit your feedback, or just keep you up to date with what's going on with IHeartVector and our products. If you send us a request (for example via email or via one of our feedback mechanisms), we reserve the right to publish it in order to help us clarify or respond to your request or to help us support other users. IHeartVector takes all measures reasonably necessary to protect against the unauthorized access, use, alteration or destruction of potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information.

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. IHeartVector uses cookies to help IHeartVector identify and track visitors, their usage of IHeartVector website, and their website access preferences. IHeartVector visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using IHeartVector's websites, with the drawback that certain features of IHeartVector's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

Business Transfers

If IHeartVector, or substantially all of its assets, were acquired, or in the unlikely event that IHeartVector goes out of business or enters bankruptcy, user information would be one of the assets that is transferred or acquired by a third party. You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer of IHeartVector may continue to use your personal information as set forth in this policy.

Ads

Ads appearing on any of our websites may be delivered to users by advertising partners, who may set cookies. These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement to compile information about you or others who use your computer. This information allows ad networks to, among other things, deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by IHeartVector and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.

Privacy Policy Changes

Although most changes are likely to be minor, IHeartVector may change its Privacy Policy from time to time, and in IHeartVector's sole discretion. IHeartVector encourages visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy. If you have a IHeartVector.com account, you might also receive an alert informing you of these changes. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.

About IHeartVector

IHeartVector creates royalty free stock vector and clipart for illustrators and designers. We love poking around old stock books, vintage catalogs, retro ads, printers’ catalogs, you name it. Some of our vector is traced with a Wacom tablet, others are vectorized in Illustrator with it's trace feature. The rest is created from scratch in Affinity Designer or Poser Pro. It depends on the source and the result. The idea of bringing something to life from hundreds of years ago, that will be appreciated and used by artists everywhere, is what drives us to add new vector.
