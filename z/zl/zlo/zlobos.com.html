What Information We Collect and How We Use That Information:

Contact Information: Some areas of our Site request or require contact and other information. During the registration process, we collect personal and other information such as your name, address, and e-mail address. Your contact information may be used to get in touch with you when necessary with respect to transactions conducted through the Site, material changes in our Site's policies, or otherwise. We do not share your contact information with any third party without your consent, except to a court or governmental agency or if otherwise required by law.

Financial Information. Users making purchases on the Site are required to provide a valid credit card number and expiration date along with the name on the credit card, the corresponding billing address and the mailing address (if different), telephone number and e-mail address. We use this information to provide you with the goods and services you order through our Site and such information may be shared with third party goods and services providers so that your purchase orders can be fulfilled. To purchase goods and services through our Site, you must be 18 years of age or older.

Demographic Information: Demographic and profile data (e.g., age, gender and income level) may also be collected at our Site. Optional and other demographic may be shared with advertisers or other third parties on an aggregated basis. We may also maintain a database of transactions on the Site and use this information in aggregate form to track the success of our Site.

Internet Protocol Address: We collect an IP address from all visitors to our Site. We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web Site.

Use of cookies:

Our Site, like most Web sites, uses cookies to enhance your experience while using our Site. Cookies are pieces of information that some Web sites transfer to the computer that is browsing that Web site and are used for record-keeping purposes at many Web sites. Use of cookies makes Web-browsing easier by performing certain functions such as saving your passwords, your personal preferences regarding your use of the particular Web site and to make sure you don't see the same ad repeatedly. Many consider the use of cookies to be an industry standard.

Your browser is probably set to accept cookies. However, if you would prefer not to receive cookies, you can alter the configuration of your browser to refuse cookies. If you choose to have your browser refuse cookies, it is possible that some areas of our Site will not function properly when you view them.

Public Forums:

This Site makes chat rooms, forums, message boards, and/or news groups available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.

Surveys, Contests and Sweepstakes:

To the extent that you choose to participate in any of our online surveys, contests or sweepstakes, you may be asked to volunteer certain information about yourself such as your name, zip code, age, income level, etc. With respect to surveys, you may provide some, all, or none of the information as you so choose and such information shall be used to improve the Site as we add more features in accordance with your input. With respect to contests and sweepstakes, we do ask that you submit your true identity and other information so that we may contact you, if necessary. We may share such information with third parties to facilitate the delivery of prizes when such prizes are supplied by third parties. The exact rules of our contests and sweepstakes will be posted and may vary from contest to contest.

zUniversity.com and the Affiliate take the protection of children seriously. zUniversity.com and the Affiliate do not knowingly collect the personal information of children, send e-mails or other communications to children, or allow them to post messages in our chat rooms or on our bulletin boards. zUniversity.com and the Affiliate do not knowingly entice children with special games, prizes or other rewards to divulge additional or superfluous information. zUniversity.com and the Affiliate encourage parents and guardians to advise children to not offer personally identifiable information about themselves on the Internet.
