Cookie
During the course of any visit, the pages you see, along with something called a cookie, are downloaded to your computer. Most, if not all, websites do this, because cookies allow the website publisher to do useful things like find out whether the computer (and probably its user) has visited the site before. This is done on a repeat visit by checking to see, and finding, the cookie left there on the last visit.

We gather non-personal data regarding the visitors to our site using cookies and code which is embedded in the site.
