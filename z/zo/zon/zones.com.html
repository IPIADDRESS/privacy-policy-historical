Privacy Policy
Zones, Inc. is committed to our customer's privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices updated September 9, 2003. Any subsequent updates or changes to our Privacy Policy will be disclosed here.

Personal Information that we may collect
Zones collects personal information such as name, address, billing information, shipping addresses, phone number, email address, and credit card and other financial information when provided to us through secure registration and order forms.

Zones may also collect personal information such as name and email address as a result of email subscriptions, surveys, requested quotes, or other registration activities when provided to us.

How we use that information
Zones may use the personal information to complete and satisfy orders placed and/or contact the customer about their order or registration when necessary.

We may also use your personal information to deliver our promotional offers through email and postal mail. We may also occasionally share your name and postal address (but not your email address) with reputable third parties with products and services we think may be of interest to you. You may opt-out of these programs at any time via our opt-out instructions below.

Zones will use opt-in email registration to deliver our promotional offers through email. We use the profile information you provide us to help tailor of offers to you. This profile data is held on a secure server and not distributed to third parties for marketing purposes.

If you volunteer to participate in a Zones survey, the information you provide will be held on a secure server. Use of this information is analyzed at an aggregate level and used to help us improve our business practices and serve you better.

In extreme case and where required by law, Zones may be forced to release customer information to legal authorities in the event of credit card fraud, illegal internet use, or unauthorized use or acts committed in connection with this site.

Cookies and Web Logs
Zones uses cookies, IP address and web log data to collect aggregate data about web site visitors to help us understand how to best serve our customers best. We may also use these methods in conjunction with your shopping cart, registration, and to make specific recommendations of products and services to you.

Third Party Tracking Software
Zones may use third party tracking software to gather information about where users are coming from and how they are using our site. This data is collected and used in an aggregated form to evaluate our web site performance and marketing programs.

In some cases, we may provide contract service providers with your personal and transactional information in order to administer services to you. Our contracts with these companies require them to maintain the confidentiality and security of all personal and transactional information and to use it only for the purpose of delivering the service for which they were contracted.

Zones web sites may contain links to other sites. While Zones proactively seeks to promote sound privacy polices with all its partners, it is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites and suggest you review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Affiliates and Rebate Programs
Zones affiliate program uses tracking links from affiliate sites to Zones site. We use a third party company to track and identify any purchases made through these links back to the originating site. This tracking is limited to non-identifying information and Zones does not share personal information with the affiliate partner unless you give us permission to do so.

Security and Protection
Zones use commercially reasonable efforts to store and protect personal information against loss, illegal access, misuse, and destruction. We employ SSL-protected Web pages and 128-bit encryption technology on all pages that require personal information. This means that your information will be transmitted across the Web to us utilizing the highest degree of security currently available. Additionally, we store your account information on a secure server and limit access of your information to authorized personnel.

Zones is a Verisign certified web site. You can check the security of Zones web sites by accessing our Verisign certificate through the Verisign image on our web site home pages. We also suggest that you use the latest version of today's most popular browsers, Microsoft Internet Explorer and Netscape Communicator to insure you have the highest Internet protection available.

Opt-Out
Zones may use your customer information to contact, mail, or email you about new products and services, special promotions, exclusive offers, or upcoming events. We offer you the option to opt-out of these communications at any time. Additionally, we may share your name and postal address (but not your email address) to reputable companies offering products or services we feel may be of interest you. If you do not want us to share this information with these companies, please let us know.

To be removed from Zones marketing mailing and emailing list, please contact our customer service department with your first name, last name, address or email address, and zip code (for confirmation purposes only) and we will remove your name promptly.
