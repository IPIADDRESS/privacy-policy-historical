Privacy Policy Information Collection and Use
Magnet Media is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. Magnet Media does not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement. Magnet Media collects information from users at several different points on this website.

Registration
Some portions of this website require a user to first complete a registration form. During registration a user is required to give their contact information (such as name and email address). This information is used to contact the user about the services for which they have expressed interest. It is optional for the user to provide demographic information and unique identifiers, but encouraged so Magnet Media can provide a more personalized experience.

Cookies
A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's hard drive containing information about the user. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information. Once the user closes their browser, the cookie simply terminates. If a user rejects the cookie, they may still use Magnet Media, but will be limited in some areas of the site. Some of Magnet Media's business partners use cookies (for example, advertisers). However, Magnet Media has no access to or control over these cookies.

Log Files
Magnet Media uses IP addresses to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

Sharing
Magnet Media may share aggregated demographic information with partners and advertisers. This is not linked to any personal information that can identify any individual person.

Magnet Media partners with other parties to provide specific services. When the user signs up for these services, Magnet Media will share names, or other contact information that is necessary for the third party to provide these services.
These parties are not allowed to use personally identifiable information except for the purpose of providing these services.

Links
Magnet Media contains links to other sites. Please be aware that Magnet Media is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. Magnet Media encourages users to be aware when they leave this website and to read the privacy statements of each and every web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this website.

Newsletter
If a user wishes to subscribe to the newsletter, Magnet Media asks for contact information such as name and email address.

Surveys + Contests
From time-to-time Magnet Media requests information from users via surveys or contests. Participation in these surveys or contests is completely voluntary and the user therefore has a choice whether or not to disclose this information. Information requested may include contact information (such as name and shipping address), and demographic information (such as zip code, age level). Contact information will be used to notify the winners and award prizes. Survey information will be used for purposes of monitoring or improving the use and satisfaction of this site.

Tell-A-Friend
If a user elects to use the referral service for informing a friend about our site, Magnet Media will ask them for the friend's name and email address. Magnet Media will automatically send the friend a one-time email inviting them to visit the site. Magnet Media stores this information for the sole purpose of sending this one-time email.

Security
This website takes every precaution to protect users' information. When users submit sensitive information via the website, your information is protected both online and off-line.

Special Offers
Magnet Media sends all new members a welcoming email to verify password and username. Established members will occasionally receive information on products, services, special deals, and a newsletter. Please see choice and opt-out below.

Site and Service Updates
Magnet Media also sends the user site and service announcement updates. Members are not able to un-subscribe from service announcements, which contain important information about the service. Magnet Media communicates with the user to provide requested services and in regards to issues relating to their account via email or phone.

Choice/Opt-out
Users are given the opportunity to 'opt-out' of having their information used for purposes not directly related to this site at the point where Magnet Media asks for the information.

Users who no longer wish to receive our newsletter or promotional materials from our partners may opt-out of receiving these communications by following the directions in each newsletter.

Internet Marketing
Magnet Media uses pixels, or transparent GIF files, to help manage online advertising. These GIF files are provided by our ad management partner, DoubleClick. These files enable DoubleClick to recognize a unique cookie on your Web browser, which in turn enables us to learn which advertisements bring users to our website. The cookie was placed by us, or by another advertiser who works with Double Click.
