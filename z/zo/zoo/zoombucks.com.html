ZoomBucks Privacy Statement
Anonymous & Personal Information:

Zoombucks.com collects anonymous information for statistical purposes. The information we collect is as follows:

  • IP Address
  • Browser
  • User Agent
  • Referrer
  • Platform Type

We may also collect other computer or geographical information (geographical information is determined by the IP Address). Zoombucks does not require your email address and personal information unless you sign up for a free account. Searches and clicks are also tracked along with the IP for statistical and user account purposes. The information is used to track trends, user behavior and other statistical purposes. Personal information is only collected if you sign up an account. You can change or correct the personal information at anytime. If you wish to cancel your account, please use our contact us form.

Contact Us

When users contact us, the personal and query information is not stored in our database. The email may be kept for future correspondance.

Links to other sites

Zoombucks.com contains link to external sites. Zoombucks is not responsible for the privacy or terms of other sites. Once you leave our site, please read the privacy statements of every website.

Cookies

Zoombucks stores cookies for the following purposes:

  • Statistical - Cookies are stored when an anonymous user visits our site so that we can track browsing patterns. Please refer to "Anonymous User Information" to understand what's tracked.
  • User sessions - User sessions are created when a user logs into their account. The user information is kept in a cookie to track the user is logged in.

Tell a Friend

You will need to supply your friends emails and names if you suggest friends to our service. This information is NOT stored in our database and a one time email will be sent.

Transfer, Merger, or Sale

If ZoomBucks is involved in a merger, or acquired by another company, the users personal information may also be part transferred.

Sharing Information with 3rd Parties

You will receive opportunities to sign up with additional offers during the join process. The information entered on the ZoomBucks sign up form will be automatically passed to third parties and if you confirm your information with the third parties (sign up with the third parties), the third parties will be entitled to your information and can use your information to market offers. To get more information on the privacy policy and terms of the third parties, please visit the third parties websites.

Changes to Privacy Statement

In the event that our privacy statement is to change, we will send an email to all members and will post a notice on our web site.
