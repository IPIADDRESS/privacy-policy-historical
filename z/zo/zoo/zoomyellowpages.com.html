ZOOM YELLOW PAGES PRIVACY POLICY

We take your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy

Zoom Yellow Pages is an online local search service that helps people find relevant providers of products and services in their locality, area, or city. To accomplish this, our users add listings that are posted to the Zoom Yellow Pages Site. These listings can then be accessed and read by other Zoom Yellow Pages users. For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, a "user" is defined as someone using and accessing the Zoom Yellow Pages Site. You should visit this page periodically to review any changes to the Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you visit this Website, Zoom Yellow Pages may collect the following types of information:

  • Personally Identifiable Information You Knowingly Provide
  • Information Zoom Yellow Pages Collects Automatically
  • Non-Personally Identifiable Information You Knowingly Provide

Zoom Yellow Pages collects personally identifiable information that you knowingly provide to us. For example, Zoom Yellow Pages may ask you to provide personally identifiable contact information (such as name, address, e-mail address and telephone number). If you purchase a product or service, Zoom Yellow Pages may ask you to provide additional information including photos, videos, menus and catalogs and/or description of your products and services, your credit card information, . Zoom Yellow Pages may also ask you to provide other information such as a personal ID, password, security question and answer, and birthday etc. You are responsible for the accuracy and completeness of all personally identifiable information that you knowingly provide.

In addition, as you (and other users) browse This Website, our web servers automatically collect certain information about such visits (usually through the use of cookies, JavaScript tags, clear .gif files --also known as "web beacons"-- and the analysis of http headers and other technology), such as the user's IP address, the user's domain name, the user's browser type, the user's user agent, how many users visit This Website, which pages the user visits and for how long, the date and time the user accesses This Website, the Internet address of the site from which the user directly linked to This Website and the links on This Website on which the user clicks. In addition, Zoom Yellow Pages may collect certain search parameters relating to searches conducted by the user which are not entered by the user.

In some cases, we may also collect non-personally identifiable information that you provide, such as search parameters in connection with a search you conduct through This Website, ratings and reviews, and zip code.

To improve the services we can offer you, we may decide to expand This Website's capabilities for obtaining information about users in the future.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your full name and email address may be used when sharing business listing information with your friends. We may also use your email address to send you updates or news regarding our site, but you may choose not to receive email of this type by sending us a postal mail mentioning your email address at Zoom Yellow Pages Unsubscribe C/o Zoom Yellow Pages, 503 T C Jaina Tower-III,Local Shopping Centre Plot,Janakpuri, New Delhi-110058.. In addition, we use this information to perform statistical analyses of user behavior and characteristics in order to measure interest in and use of the various areas of the site and to inform advertisers of such information as well as the number of users that have been exposed to or clicked on, their advertising banners.

3. Transfer of Information

Except as set forth in this Privacy Policy, we do not sell, rent, share, trade or give away any of your personal information. Zoom Yellow Pages will disclose information about individual users to governmental or judicial authorities or law enforcement agencies, or to other individuals or entities in response to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal processes. We reserve the right to transfer your personal information in the event of a transfer of ownership of Zoom Yellow Pages, such as an acquisition by or merger with another company. In such an event, Zoom Yellow Pages will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy. Zoom Yellow Pages will notify you when information about you may be provided to third parties in ways other than explained above, and you will have the option to prevent such information sharing.

4. Links

Our website may contain links to third party websites to which we have no affiliation. Zoom Yellow Pages does not share your personal information with those websites and is not responsible for their privacy practices. We suggest you read the privacy policies on these third party websites.

5. Use of Cookies

Like most websites, Zoom Yellow Pages requires cookies to function properly. Cookies are small text files that reside on a user's computer and identify you as a unique user. We use cookies to refine our site and simplify the user experience. For example, the use of cookies allows registered users to enter our site without explicitly typing in their email address and password every time. You should be aware that Zoom Yellow Pages cannot control the use of cookies by advertisers or any information advertisers may gather from their use of cookies. If you do not want information collected through the use of cookies, there is a simple procedure in most browsers that allows you to deny or accept the cookie feature; however, you should note that cookies may be necessary to provide you certain features (e.g., customized delivery of information) available on the Zoom Yellow Pages site.

6. Controlling Your Personal Information

As a registered Zoom Yellow Pages user, you can modify your personal information at any time by accessing the "Account" section of the site. Also, upon your request, Zoom Yellow Pages will use commercially reasonable efforts to functionally remove you and your personal information from the site; however, it may be impossible to remove your entry without some residual information being retained by Zoom Yellow Pages. Registered users who wish to close their account should contact info@zoomyellowpages.com.

Your Zoom Yellow Pages account is password-protected. Zoom Yellow Pages tries very hard to protect our users' information. We use industry standard measures to protect your information that is stored within our database. We limit the access to your information to those employees who need access to perform their job function such as our customer service personnel. If you have any questions about the security at our website, please contact us. Although we take appropriate measures to safeguard against unauthorized disclosures of information, we cannot assure you that personally identifiable information that we collect will never be disclosed in a manner that is inconsistent with this Privacy Policy. You hereby acknowledge that Zoom Yellow Pages is not responsible for any intercepted information sent via the Internet, and you hereby release us from any and all claims arising out of or related to the use of intercepted information in any unauthorized manner.
