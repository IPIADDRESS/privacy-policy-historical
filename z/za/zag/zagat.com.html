  6. The kind of security procedures that are in place to protect the loss, misuse or alteration of information under Zagat Survey's control

How you can correct any inaccuracies in the information

We always have and always will respect your privacy. Any information that you give us is held with the utmost care and will not be used other than as described in this privacy policy.

Zagat Survey has created this privacy policy to demonstrate our commitment to your privacy on zagat.com. The "Zagat" name and reputation have been built on the trust of our customers, and we hope that our commitment to online privacy will inspire the same trust in you while you are visiting zagat.com.

We have adopted a set of information management guidelines that serve as the basis for our customer relationships. These guidelines have been developed with the recognition that Internet technologies are rapidly evolving. Accordingly, guidelines are subject to change. Any such changes will be posted on this page. Please read this privacy policy to understand how your personal information will be treated as you use zagat.com. Please see our Terms of Use for more information about our online policies in general.

If you have questions or concerns regarding this statement, you should first contact: feedback@zagat.com. If you do not receive acknowledgment of your inquiry or your inquiry has not been satisfactorily addressed, you should then contact TRUSTe: http://www.truste.org/consumers/watchdog_complaint.php. TRUSTe will then serve as a liaison with the Web site to resolve your concerns.

Note: We permit zagat.com users to access and download the ZAGAT TO GO SM software application. This Privacy Policy does not cover any Personally Identifiable Information that may be collected through that software application.

The Information We Collect

We collect personal information from you on zagat.com when you provide it and in some cases, from third party sources such as credit card companies in order to ensure that your credit card information is up-to-date.

User Registration. Registration on zagat.com is strictly voluntary. To register on zagat.com, you must provide your name, address, e-mail address and year of birth. This information is required in the registration process at zagat.com, but we recognize that this is your personal information and, except as we explain below, we do not give it out to third parties without your approval . Becoming a registered user on zagat.com allows you to vote and purchase items in our Shop. Additional demographic information, such as your occupation, level of education, household income and special interests, are also collected in an "optional information" area. In this section, you are not required to provide additional information about yourself that you may find personal. We do this to learn more about you in tailoring our Web site for your needs and preferences. We also will collect your phone number for shipping and billing information if you purchase an item at the zagat.com store. Also, at some point we may ask you for optional information with regard to your personal preferences on dining, travel and leisure activities to personalize your experiences on zagat.com, but this information will be optionally provided by you as well. At any time you may later review or update the personal information we have collected online from you by logging on with your user name and password and then going to the "My Account" link.

Subscriptions to Zagat.com. To subscribe to zagat.com, you must provide the personal information requested in the zagat.com subscription process, including, without limitation, your name, address, e-mail address, other information required in order to become a registered user of zagat.com, and your credit card information for subscription fee billing. This information is required in the subscription process, but we recognize that this is your personal information and, except as we explain below, we do not give it out to third parties without your approval. Becoming a subscriber of zagat.com provides you with the benefits described in the subscriber section of zagat.com, including access to certain content that is not available to persons who are not subscribers and a discount for purchases in our Shop. The Subscriber Agreement applies to your subscription to zagat.com. In addition, as a subscriber to zagat.com, you will also automatically become a registered user of zagat.com, thereby subjecting you to the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy applicable to registered users, and you will be entitled to all of the benefits and privileges available to registered users of zagat.com.

Member Reviews. In order to submit your ratings and reviews on the site, you will need to create a username. You will have the option of sharing your reviews with other members of the site. If you so choose on your personal profile, the following pieces of information will be shared on the site along with your reviews: (i) your username, (ii) your home city and state, (iii) the month and year that you became a subscriber to zagat.com and (iv) the total number of zagat.com reviews submitted. In addition, you will have the option to add the following additional information to your profile, in which case this information will also be shared on the site: (i) gender, (ii) age range and (iii) personal description.

Subscriptions to the ZAGAT TO GOSM Service. To subscribe to the ZAGAT TO GO SM service, you must provide the personal information requested in our subscription process, including, without limitation, your name, address, e-mail address, other information required in order to become a registered user of the service, and your credit card information for subscription fee billing. This information is required in the subscription process, but we recognize that this is your personal information and, except as we explain below, we do not give it out to third parties without your approval. You must become a subscriber of the ZAGAT TO GO SM service in order to take advantage of the service, unless you are subject to a free trial. In order to be eligible for a free trial you may be required to provide your name and email address.

Credit Card Policy. To simplify ordering and product fulfillment in our Shop and/or for your subscription to zagat.com or the ZAGAT TO GO SM service, if applicable, we request your credit card number, its expiration date and your billing and shipping address. We transmit this information safely over the Internet in encrypted form and store it on a secure server. This information is used only to bill you for and ship to you the zagat.com products that you order or to bill you for your subscription to zagat.com or other services available on zagat.com.

Cookies. zagat.com employs cookies (small pieces of text stored on your computer) to recognize you and your access privileges. For example, we use cookies to allow you to log on to zagat.com automatically without entering your ID and password each visit. Cookies also identify you and your preferences on our site. You may refuse to accept our cookies, but we will not be able to recognize you as a registered user or subscriber, which will limit your access to the site.

Location Selector. If you choose to enter a street address when selecting a location, this information will be saved for your convenience in your Recent Locations list or in your Saved Locations list should you so indicate. This information may make it easier for you to browse and search our content and will not be used for any other purpose.

E-mail a Review & E-mail a List. If you choose to e-mail a list or a review to a friend, we collect the friend's name and e-mail address. This information is used only to deliver the review to your friend and is not stored on our servers. If "a friend" decides that he/she will register with zagat.com, he or she will be given the same rights of access as registered users of zagat.com. The prospect information that you submit for "a friend" will be used only for the use for which it is submitted. Additionally, for "a friend" to register, he or she will have to follow zagat.com's registration process (see above) and will be given the same option in regard to receiving marketing materials that zagat.com offers to all registered users.

IP Addresses. zagat.com logs IP addresses, or the location of your computer on the Internet, for systems administration and troubleshooting purposes. Your IP address is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

Third Party Advertising

The ads appearing on this Web site are delivered to you by DoubleClick, our Web advertising partner. Information about your visits to this site, such as number of times you have viewed an ad, is used to serve ads to you. For more information about DoubleClick, cookies, and how to "opt-out", please click here.

Third Party Cookies

In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser.

How We Use Your Information

We use your e-mail address and home address to send you updates and newsletters periodically about new features, special offers and targeted promotional items available on zagat.com. You may choose to not receive certain of these communications by so indicating during the registration process or in response to one of our e-mails or postal mailings. Each communication will clearly explain how to opt out of future communications. Any demographic information that you voluntarily share with us is used to tailor zagat.com to your interests. We also may share this demographic information with other companies with which we do business.

With Whom We Share Information

We share your personally identifiable information with intermediaries. These intermediaries use your information in connection with processing payments for online purchases and with delivery of e-mail and postal mailings to our registered users. We do not permit these intermediaries to use, store or retain this information for secondary purposes, unless they indicate otherwise in communications to you. From time to time we may allow some of our select partners to send you special promotional offers that we believe may be of special interest to you. You may choose not to receive these communications from our partners by so indicating during the registration process or in response to any of these communications, each of which will clearly explain how to opt out of future communications. You may also opt out of having your personal information shared with these third parties by sending a request to have your zagat.com user account deactivated to feedback@zagat.com. If you do so, however, you will not be able to take advantage of many of our features, including the right to vote online, purchase items from our store and even continue as a subscriber to zagat.com. This site makes use of certain technology designed to enhance your browsing experience and provide analytics including ClickTracks, SearchIgnite and Fireclick. These technologies, provided as a hosted service application, provide aggregate reporting information to the site owner only, through cookies that they set at zagat.com. No personal or personally identifiable information is gathered for this process. Additional information on Fireclick's technology may be found at www.fireclick.com . Additional information about SearchIgnite and ClickTracks may be found at www.clicktracks.com and www.searchignite.com . We do retain the right to share and transfer all user information with a purchaser of our company or all or substantially all of our assets if that purchaser agrees to abide by the terms of this Policy.

Legal Disclaimer

We may disclose personal information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to conform with the law or legal process or to protect or defend our rights or property.

Security

zagat.com has security measures in place to help protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. All information is stored in a secure operating environment. When sending sensitive information across the Internet - including your login ID, password and purchasing information such as credit cards - we use Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption provided by VeriSign. Internally, all customer information is protected with multiple security checks. Employee access to the system is limited to the functionality necessary for each person's job. Relevant employees are informed of our security policies and their access to applications and data on our network.

If you still do not feel comfortable about leaving your credit card information on our secured Web site, please contact our customer service department at (800) 333-3421 and we'll be happy to help you with your order or subscription.

Special Policy With Regard to Information From Children Under Thirteen

During the zagat.com registration process, users are asked to enter their "year of birth." This information is mandatory for registration on zagat.com because we do not allow children under the age of thirteen to go through the registration process at zagat.com.

Links

We may provide links to third party Web sites as a service to our users. Please be aware that we cannot control and are not responsible for the information collection practices of such Web sites, which may differ from those of zagat.com. We encourage you to review and understand the privacy policies on these Web sites before providing any information to them.

How You Can Access or Correct Your Information

If you are a registered zagat.com user you can make changes to your personal information at any time by clicking on "My Account" on zagat.com or by e-mailing us at feedback@zagat.com. At any time you can also review your personal information by clicking on "My Account" as well.

Questions

Protecting your privacy is one of our highest priorities. If you have any questions about our privacy policy, you may e-mail us at feedback@zagat.com, or write to us at Zagat Survey, LLC, 4 Columbus Circle, New York, NY, 10019, Attn: Privacy.

Consent and Opting-Out

Upon request, zagat.com will:

  1. Remove you and your personal information from our database, thereby canceling your registration
  2. Correct personal information that you state is erroneous
  3. Permit you to "opt out" of further e-mail contact (excepting notification from zagat.com of major changes to the service) while still allowing access to the site

To request a correction or cancellation, send e-mail to feedback@zagat.com.

Notification of Changes

If we decide to materially change our privacy policy, we will post changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it and under what circumstances we disclose it. Regardless of later updates, we will never use the information you submit under this current policy in a new way without first providing you an opportunity to opt out or otherwise prevent that use (we will post a notification of such changes 30 days prior to their implementation). You may determine when this privacy policy was updated last by noting the "Last Updated" date directly beneath the title of this page.

Thank you for using zagat.com.
