Zinch.com Privacy Policy

Zinch, LLC (together with Zinch.com, “Zinch”) is committed to respecting the privacy of our users. We strive to provide a safe, secure online user experience. This Privacy Policy sets forth the online data collection and usage policies and practices that apply to Zinch.com and does not apply to information we collect in any other fashion. We are not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of other websites to which you choose to link from Zinch.com or any of our affiliate sites (the “Zinch Sites”). We encourage you to review the privacy policies of the other websites to which you choose to link from Zinch.com, including the Zinch Sites, so you can understand how they collect, use and share your information.

High school students participate in Zinch voluntarily. Participation in this website enables students to receive information which is related to their educational needs and helps them plan for the future. The most important benefit of participating in Zinch is the information students and their families will receive from colleges and universities. Zinch also uses student data for research purposes to enable colleges, universities and other educational institutions to have a greater understanding of the current pool of potential applicants.

Zinch allows full access to our online information free of charge when users of Zinch.com (“Users”) register with Zinch through our Zinch.com service. This service allows prospective students to create profiles on our servers. Data collected can then be accessed and purchased by educational institutions such as, but not limited to, universities and colleges.

What Information is Collected

Information collected may include, among other things, your name, physical address, e-mail address, phone number and other information about yourself. We may also ask for information such as your age, citizenship, details of your academic and work history, and your preferences about your educational future.

Examples of areas on Zinch.com where we collect personal or combined personal and demographic data are pages where you can open a Zinch account, create a Zinch profile or account (a “Zinch Profile”), sign up to use Zinch.com services or the services of other Zinch Sites, enter a contest or purchase a product. Zinch.com has an array of possible information to fill out in order to better help you be recruited, but it is solely up to you whether you furnish such information. If you do provide such information, you are thereby consenting to the use of that information in accordance with the policies and practices described in this Privacy Policy. All posting done in your private profile that is your original work including, but not limited to, music, essays, and art work is not available for public use or for sale by Zinch. You maintain full rights and ownership of your personal work.

Schools

We work with schools and institutions to provide targeted recruiting helping both the student and the university find the right fit. Your school, district, or state may arrange for you to participate in our programs and may purchase products on your behalf. We may share personally-identifiable information that you provide to us through your participation in these school programs with your school, district and your parents. All personal information is gathered directly from Users through online registration forms. Only you can modify your Zinch Profile once it has been created.

Personal Information Collected About You May Be Utilized For the Following Purposes:

• To allow institutions of higher education and related businesses to purchase information in order to contact and recruit students;

Use of Information Obtained by Zinch

When you register with Zinch, you create your own Zinch Profile and privacy settings. Your Zinch Profile information, as well as your name, email and photo, are displayed to universities and colleges that search through our database to recruit you. This is not a social networking site. Other students on Zinch.com will not be able to view your Zinch Profile or any information therein. Your Zinch Profile may be utilized by Zinch to service your account and inform you of Zinch services. You are also able to edit and update the information you provide in your Zinch Profile. Your data may also be reviewed and accessed by other academic institutions, which may contact you in an effort to recruit you to attend their institution. Also, we can allow related businesses (i.e. test prep services, loan providers, and other businesses deemed appropriate by Zinch) to access your Zinch Profile and to contact you and let you know about their products and services.

What We Will Not Use Your Information For

We respect your privacy! Except as described above, or as otherwise provided or permitted by law, your information collected by Zinch.com will not be used in any other way.

Cookies

We use cookie technology within Zinch.com. We use cookies for remembering User names and passwords and for tracking click streams. This allows you to send inquiries to multiple institutions without the need to re-enter your username and password each time. We do not utilize cookies to track your activities in any other way. Cookies cannot obtain personally identifiable information such as your e-mail address, or any other information stored on your hard drive. Cookies are pieces of data normally stored by your browser, and you can quite easily prevent cookie data from being sent by adjusting your browser settings; however Zinch.com will not function properly if Cookies are not enabled.

One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalize Zinch pages, or register for services, a cookie helps us to recall your specific information (such as user name, password and preferences). Because of our use of cookies, we can deliver faster and more accurate results and a more personalized site experience.

Information Protection and Security

When you submit your information to Zinch, whether it be your name, address, email or phone number, we do our utmost to ensure that information is protected against loss, misuse, alteration, destruction or unauthorized access. Zinch uses standard encryption tools such as SSL (Secure Socket Layer) to help protect your personal information and to keep information collected secure.

Encryption can be used after your data has left your internal system. Encryption renders your data unreadable using a mathematical formula known as the key. Your data is turned back into readable form, i.e. deciphered, by applying the correct key.

Due to the nature of this technology, there is always a remote danger of information interception no matter what barriers are in place.

Our system does provide for profiles to be sent via un-encrypted email, and may be susceptible to intercept at this point.

Employees within Zinch with access to your information provided are instructed to utilize such information only in adherence to this Privacy Policy. Employees who utilize information for any reason other than the reasons stated within this Privacy Policy may be subject to reprimand or even legal proceedings.

Third parties which access your data are instructed and agree to utilize such information only in adherence to this Privacy Policy; however Zinch is not responsible for the actions of third parties.
