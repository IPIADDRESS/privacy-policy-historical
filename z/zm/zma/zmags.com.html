    Zmags - Privacy policy

    Zmags is dedicated to protecting the privacy and security of our website visitors. The following document represents Zmags' privacy policy governing how we use information collected through our website. We may use personally identifiable information (such as your name, e-mail address, street address, telephone number) subject to the terms of this privacy policy. Please note this policy applies only to information collected by Zmags' website (www.zmags.com) online and does not impact information collected or used by Zmags through other means. If you have questions about these policies, please contact us at the address listed below.

    How information is used

    Some pages on our site may ask you to provide personal information, such as when you register for access to a document or an event. In most cases, you will receive a personalized message from one of our sales representatives with more information about Zmags' products and services, via phone or email. If you have opted-in to receive email communications from Zmags during any such transactions, you may receive periodic email about Zmags' products and services until you opt-out using one of the clearly specified methods in every email communication.

    Cookies

    Zmags may make use of "cookie" technology, which enables us to customize our site to better suit your preferences. For example, a "cookie" could store your log-in information so that you do not need to register for every subsequent download that requires a form. Cookies may also be used for visitor counting on our web site. In all cases, you can set your browser to choose not to accept cookies, but doing so may require you to re-enter certain information at each visit, or prevent us from customizing the site's features according to your preferences.

    Third parties

    Zmags will never sell or share personally identifiable information with an unaffiliated third party for any reason.

    Security

    Zmags maintains industry-standard security measures in our physical facilities to prevent against the loss or misuse of all personal information collected on our website.

    Access to personal information collected by Zmags

    Zmags uses the personal information you provide on our site only as described above. If at any time you would like to review this information for accuracy, or would like Zmags to update or remove your personal information from our servers, please notify us at the contact address below with your request.

    Changes to this policy

    Zmags reserves the right to change this policy at any time. Please review our website periodically for changes. Your continued use of our site following the posting of changes to this privacy policy will mean you accept those changes.
