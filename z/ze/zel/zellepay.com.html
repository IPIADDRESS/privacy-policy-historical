We collect or access information from you and about you when you use the Site, including:

In doing so, we will access and display information about each of the financial accounts you hold at that Network Financial Institution that is eligible for use with the Zelle service (as determined by the Network Financial Institution), including the account balance for each of those accounts, and your email address(es) on file with the Network Financial Institution.

    If you are an enrolled User and have a financial account at one of our Network Financial Institutions, we may also collect personal information from that Network Financial Institution if it receives a notification that your financial account information in our database is inaccurate and needs to be changed. Additionally, if you are an enrolled User and have a financial account at one of our Network Financial Institutions, your bank's privacy policy may also apply to the information that we collect through our Site and to the information that your bank provides to us.

    In addition, we may collect personal information from third party services in order to process your registration, verify your identity, verify your financial account information, and protect against or prevent actual or potential fraud or unauthorized use.
  2. Information About Your Transactions: When you use the Zelle mobile app to send or request money, we collect information such as when and where the transaction occurs, the names of the transacting parties, the mobile phone numbers or email addresses of the transacting parties, a description of the transactions (including text you put in the memo field), the transaction amount, and the devices and payment methods used to complete the transactions.
  3. Information to Respond to Your Requests: You may give us your personal information when you contact us for assistance and to sign up for our newsletters and for other communications.
