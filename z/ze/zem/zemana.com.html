Privacy Policy

Here at Zemana, we are making security products that are very powerful but also very easy to use. The key is in simplicity. We believe that your rights about privacy and control over your information should be also simple and easy to understand.

We will be transparent about the information (data) we collect and why we collect them. We do not and will not sell your data to third parties. Our primary purpose in collecting your data is to be able to deliver you better security products and services.

This Privacy Policy applies to Zemana‘s website and all products and services offered by Zemana Ltd. It describes how we collect, use, share and secure your personal information. It also describes your choices regarding use, access and correction of your personal information.

Where do we collect your information?

We may collect your information from:

Our website

Including, but not limited to, when you visit our site, register on the site, place an order, subscribe to the newsletter, respond to a survey, fill out a form, and in connection with other activities, services, features or resources we make available on our Site.

Direct communication with us

When you contact us by email, contact form, or otherwise.

Our software

When you use or products (download, install, uninstall, or buy)

How do we collect your information?

Some information is provided directly by you, when you choose to share it with us.

Our software collects information about your use of the software. This is necessary to ensure our software is operating correctly and to confirm the status of your license of our software.

What kind of information do we collect?

There are two types of information we collect: personal and non-personal.

Personal information is information that is either expressly provided by you or an information that can be used either alone or in combination with other information to personally identify you.

We will collect personal identification information from you only if you voluntarily submit such information to us. You can always refuse to supply personally identification information, except that it may prevent them from engaging in certain Site related activities.

When using our website or you have direct communication with us

We may collect the following personal information from you: Contact Information (such as name, email address, mailing address, or phone number); Information about your business (such as company name, company size, business type) and; Information related to your usage of our products.

Our third-party service provider may collect billing information (credit card number and billing address) on our behalf to process orders.

Non-personal information is all information that is not personal or that was personal but which we modify and/or aggregate with other data to make it non-personal information. We gather certain information automatically. This information may be the browser name, the type of computer and technical information about connection to our Site, such as the operating system and the Internet service providers you use and other similar information.

When using our software

By enabling the Zemana Ltd. software, you agree to allow the program to collect and report data to the Zemana IntelliGuard Cloud Infrastructure (hereinafter referred to as IntelliGuard Cloud Data), anonymously and upon certain events. These events are generated by program activity on your computer, which activates a trigger (or policy) within the Zemana Ltd. software. A policy is a rule within the software that monitors areas of your computer system for malicious program activity.

When an event is triggered, the following data about the suspicious program is transmitted:

The action that you apply or that Zemana applies automatically

The MD5 sum of the file

The digital signature of the file

The size of the file

Version information of the file

Zemana IntelliGuard also collects standard computer information, which includes information regarding your computer software and hardware, such as your IP address, operating system, and version.

The IntelliGuard Cloud Data is anonymous and does not collect and process any personal user information.

The IntelliGuard Cloud Data is combined into our Zemana Threat Database and used to create rules which will determine if a file/program is “safe” or “malicious”. In addition, the IntelliGuard Cloud Data is used to produce general statistics on attacks and threats; these statistics may be used by Zemana Ltd. in its malware research, publicity, marketing and promotional materials.

The IntelliGuard Cloud Database Service may be subject to occasional downtime for maintenance, repair, upgrade and update.
