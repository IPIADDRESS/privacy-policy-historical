    2. Information Collection Practices

    2.1. Types of Information Collected

    (a) Traffic Data Collected. We automatically track and collect the following categories of information when you visit our Site: (1) IP addresses; (2) domain servers; (3) types of computers accessing the Site; (4) types of web browsers used to access the Site; (5) 'Referrer' data reflecting the route by which you came to the Website (collectively "Traffic Data") (6) Demographic Data regarding your anonymous location, status, and other non-identifying data. Traffic Data is anonymous information that does not personally identify you but is helpful for marketing purposes or for improving your experience on the Site.

    We use a third-party services (currently, Google Analytics, and others such as various Social Networks including but not limited to, Twitter and Facebook) to collect aggregate data about site use. This service uses cookies to collect anonymous traffic data in order to improve the quality of our services. Social Networks may send email follow-up messages.

    (b) Personal Information Collected. In order for you to access certain premium, services and to purchase products, including eyeglasses and related products that we offer via the Site, we require you to provide us with certain information that personally identifies you ("Personal Information"). Personal Information includes the following categories of information: (1) Contact Data (such as your name, mailing address, and e-mail address); (2) Financial Data (such as your account or credit card number); (3) Address Data (such as your address for shipping zip code); (4) Rx and Prescription Data and measurements regarding your eyes, vision, vision health, and needs for eyeglasses and potentially, your uploaded photograph. If you communicate with us by e-mail, add Favorites to the Favorites Section, or otherwise complete online forms, surveys, or provide other information, any information provided in such communication may be collected as Personal Information.

    2.2. Uses of Information Collected.

    (a) COMPANY USE OF INFORMATION. We use Contact Data to send you information about our company or our products or services, or to contact you when necessary. We use your Financial Data to verify your qualifications for certain products or services and to bill you for products and services. We use your Demographic Data to customize and tailor your experience on the Site, displaying content that we think you might be interested in and according to your preferences, to be sure you receive the correct order and to ship your order to you, with follow-up emails to secure your satisfaction and feedback and Social Network opportunities to keep in touch and follow the Company’s progress, sales items and other information. We use your Rx and Prescription Data to make your eyeglasses.

    (b) Sharing of Personal Information. We share certain categories of information we collect from you in the ways described in this Privacy Policy.

    (c) We NEVER share your information with any third party that is not necessary to process and fulfill your order other than for surveys and social network data. There is no onward transfer, no personally identifying information shared at all, no sharing of any data to any strange companies who may want to send you information.

    (d) We also share Contact Data and Financial Data with our business partners who assist us by performing core services that are necessary to processing, making, fulfilling and shipping your order to you (such as hosting, billing, fulfillment, or data storage and security) related to our operation of the Site and to follow-up to your orders. Those business partners have all agreed to uphold the same standards of security and confidentiality that we have promised to you in this Privacy Policy, and they will only use your Contact Data and other Personal Information to carry out their specific business obligations to our Company. If you do not want us to share your Contact Data with any third parties, please email us at privacy@zennioptical.com but please understand that such a request will likely limit your ability to take advantage of all of the features and services we offer on the Site and you cannot place orders if we do not have your Personal Information, just like a regular store. In addition, we maintain a procedure for you to review and request changes to your Personal Information; this procedure is described in Section 3.1, below. Of course if we are ever ordered for any reason by a Court or Governmental Agency to disclose any Data or Information, it is possible that some of your Personal Data might be visible to government inspectors and laboratories.

    (e) Our Sensitive Information Principle: For sensitive information including your personal information specifying medical or health conditions. ZENNI OPTICAL will always treat as sensitive any information received from you or any third party where the third party treats and identifies it as sensitive. We only share your Rx and Prescription Data with our own direct factories that make your lenses and assemble your order, with customer service telephone assistants customer service reps, and with no others.

    (f) User Choice Regarding Collection, Use, and Distribution of Personal Information. You may choose not to provide us with any Personal Information. In such an event, you can still access and use much of the Site; however you will not be able to access and use those portions of the Site that require your Personal Information, including, but not limited to, being able to order eyeglasses, make a purchase of any accessory or item sold from this Site or establish an account.

    (g) To keep up with our customers and their needs we use follow-up contact services like PowerReviews to contact customers after a purchase and obtain data about your experience with our products and services. You may see the PowerReviews privacy policy at http://www.powerreviews.com/legal/ . Content provided by our customers include, but are not limited to, customer information, name, email, display name, images, videos, free-form content, product ratings, and click feedback. Such feedback is used to improve the quality of our services and some feedback may be posted on our website as the Company chooses in its sole discretion.

    3. Confidentiality and Security of Personal Information. Except as otherwise provided in this Privacy Policy, we will keep your Personal Information private and will not share it with third parties, unless such disclosure is necessary to: (a) comply with a court order or other legal process; (b) protect our rights or property; or (c) enforce our Terms of Service. Your Personal Information is stored on secure servers that are not accessible by third parties. We use SSL security for all our ordering information. We are an Authorized Merchant by Authoirize.net and a VeriSign Trusted site.

    3.1. USER ABILITY TO ACCESS, UPDATE, AND CORRECT PERSONAL INFORMATION. We maintain a procedure in order to help you confirm that your Personal Information remains correct and up-to-date. At any time, you may visit your personal profile at www.zennioptical.com. Through your personal profile you may review and update your Personal Information that we have already collected. You may request that your account be deleted via an email to: privacy@zennioptical.com
