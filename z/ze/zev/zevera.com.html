General Information

Zevera collects four types of information:

(i) Information related to your account ("personal information")

This information is collected for the purpose of administering your Zevera subscription, and includes your email and payment information, which you submit on our order page when you purchase a membership.

(ii) Aggregate connection summary statistics

Zevera collects minimal information about usage. These statistics never include anything about what the user did with the site: no data about the contents or destinations of traffic and no IP addresses are stored permanently.

(iii) Anonymous connection diagnostics

Connection diagnostics are anonymized and cannot be tied back to individual users. This is needed for billing and to check usage on our services.

Personal Information

We require you provide personal information, such as an email address and payment information, so that we can email you, process payments and respond to support queries. Important: The specific information collected varies depending on the payment method you choose. To minimize the amount of information, we encourage that you use bitcoin.

With some forms of payment we use a third-party payment processor to complete the transaction. To understand what personal information these processors collect and store, please refer to the respective processor's terms and privacy policy.

Zevera uses your email address for the following reasons:

  • To send emails related to payment transactions;
  • To provide links to our Site, including password reset emails;
  • To send you updates and announcements;
  • To send marketing information, such as Zevera offers, surveys, invitations, and content about other matters in connection with Zevera (“Marketing emails”). You may choose to not receive Marketing emails by following the opt-out procedure described in these emails.

Zevera uses your personal information only for the purposes listed in this Privacy Policy. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Aggregate Connection Summary Statistics

We ensure that we never log traffic destination, data content, IP addresses. Therefore:

  • We do not know which user ever accessed a particular website or service.
  • We do not know which user was connected at a specific time or which IP addresses they used.
  • We do not know the set of original IP addresses of a user’s computer.

We cannot supply this information because the data don’t exist.

In order to maintain excellent customer support and quality of service, Zevera collects the following information related to your usage:

Aggregate sum of data transferred

We collect information regarding the total sum of data transfer so we know which services are popular and need more ressources.

Summary

We collect minimal usage statistics to maintain our quality of service.

We’ve engineered our systems to categorically eliminate storage of sensitive data. We may know THAT a customer has used Zevera, but we never know HOW they have utilized our service.

Anonymous Connection Diagnostics

We collect anonymized data used for network diagnostics. We use these data in our network tools to help optimize network speeds and to let us identify problems related servers, or ISPs.

Jurisdiction and Applicable Law

Zevera wants to keep your information private. Zevera’s registered place of business is in Malaysia, which has stricter laws concerning information disclosure than most countries.

In Malaysia are no data retention laws, and any legal order must come from court. Information requests from foreign courts are not respected. Should we receive a valid legal order from the Malaysian Court, it is important to note that Zevera does not keep worthy logs of any IP addresses or traffic data.

Storing of Information Related to Support Tickets

Zevera uses a helpdesk system which keeps the conversation you submit to us along with our response. The helpdesk collects your email address and any additional information you provide to us. These records are deleted manually but regularly.

Security Measures to Protect Your Information

Zevera uses best practice technical security at our servers so to prevent misuse and unauthorized access. Access to user information is restricted to only the admins on need to know basis to perform their job functions.

We believe these systems are robust, but no data security measures in the world can offer 100% protection.

Servers are housed in data centers with strong security practices.

Cookies

What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file used to store information about your visit to the Site. Cookies let Zevera optimize and improve the user experience of the Site by helping us deliver certain functionalities, such as website login and language settings. The cookies we use may vary over time as we continuously update and improve our Site.

Disabling cookies

You are free to change your cookie preferences at any time. You can do this in the settings panel for your browser. Depending on which browser and device you use, you may be able to control which cookies you allow, which cookies you want to block in the future, and delete cookies. For more information about these settings, please refer to the “help” section of your browser. Note that Zevera’s Site may not work as intended if you choose to disable cookies.

Zevera’s cookies

The cookies set by Zevera enable us to securely show you information that is specific to your account. The cookies contain a user identifier, but no directly personally identifying information.

Third-party cookies

Zevera might use third-party services such as Google Analytics and Adwords. Cookies from such services are used to collect data for statistical reports and marketing.

Zevera uses Google AdWords remarketing to show advertisements on third-party websites (including Google) to users who have visited our Site. We may show such users advertisements on a Google search results page, or on a site in the Google Display Network.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on someone’s past visits to the Site. Any data collected will be used in accordance with our Privacy Policy and Google’s privacy policy. Users may opt out of Google’s use of cookies by visiting the Google Advertising Opt-out Page. Users may opt out of Google Analytics by visiting the Google Analytics Opt-out Page.
