Privacy Information

This policy will help you understand how we collect, store, use and disclose client information as well as how you can change or delete said information.


The Information We Collect

When visiting our website you may provide us with two different types of information, Active Information and Passive Information.

Active Information is information you chose to reveal to us while Passive Information is information we collect from all our visitors in less than visible ways.


No Use of Our Site by Persons Under 13

No person under 13 should disclose information on this site. You may learn more about protecting childrenÕs privacy online by visiting www.ftc.gov/kidzprivacy.


Active Information

Active Information is collected in the following ways:

- when you make contact with us through our website.

- when you sign up for one of our services.

We may retain this information indefinitely.


Passive Information

We use cookies and javascript on our website to collect information that helps us to better serve our current and potential clients. We may gather and retain indefinitely such information as dates and times of visits, pages viewed, time spent on the website, IP Address, User Agent information and username (only for current, logged-in clients).

If you do not wish to transmit this information to us, you may turn off cookies and javascript in your web browser, however this may degrade your ability to use our website(s).


What is a Cookie?

Cookies are small data files saved to your computer by a web browser that are used to simplify subsequent interactions as well as identify the user to the website.


How we Use the Collected Information

Persons we employ directly use the information we collect for to administer our business activites, provider customer support and analyze usage patterns to improve service and sales.

We will only disclose information we have collected when legeally obliged to do so.


Controlling Usage of Your Information

If you wish to access, change, delete or otherwise alter our usage of your information please contact us.
