We do not collect Personal Information in connection with "browsing" our web site. How do I subscribe to mobile weight updates?

A: Submit your phone number to us from your Mobile Settings page to begin your subscription. Once you receive our confirmation message, you're ready to start updating your weight.
Does it cost anything to update my weight from my mobile phone?

A: It's free to subscribe to the Mobile Weight Updates feature, but your mobile service's standard text messaging rates may still apply.
How do I update my weight regularly via my mobile phone?

A: Once you've subscribed to mobile weight updates, simply text your current weight to 50 MPC (50672).
Is there a mobile shortcut for updating my weight?

A: Save 50 MPC (50672) to your Contacts for quick and easy weight updates.
How do I cancel my mobile weight updates?

A: Just text STOP to 50 MPC (50672) to cancel your subscription.
