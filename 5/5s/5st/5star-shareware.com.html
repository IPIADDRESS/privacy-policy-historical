Privacy Policy

5 Star Shareware respects the privacy of all visitors to its Website.

When a visitor requests pages from our website, our Web servers automatically recognize the browser’s domain name and IP address. Our servers do not record e-mail addresses. We collect the domain names and IP addresses in order to aggregate information on the pages users access or visit. This information is used solely for statistical purposes and cannot identify individuals.

From time to time, we may ask users to provide an e-mail address for the purpose of conducting a survey or to provide additional services (e.g. subscriptions to e-mail newsletters or to place user software reviews). Before the identity of a visitor is recorded, we will clearly indicate the purpose of the request for information.

5 Star Shareware maintains a strict no-spam policy. If you sign up for our free newsletter, or place a software review, your e-mail address will not be sold or given to any third party. We ask for your email address so that we know that the review in not being posted by a bot.

Third Party Ads

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
