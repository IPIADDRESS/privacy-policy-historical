admanGo.com Limited Privacy Statement
Your privacy on the Internet is of the utmost importance to us. We have created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: admanGo.com.

Member ID and Password
When you register as a member, we require that you select a Login ID and a Password. Your account is only accessible through the use of your Password. If you lose control of your Password, you may lose control over your personal or corporate information, and may be subject to legally binding actions entered into on your or your company's behalf. Therefore, if your Password has been compromised for any reason, you should immediately contact us at info@admango.com.

Collection of Information
We do not collect any personal information about you unless you voluntarily provide it to us. You provide your personal information to us if you do the following:-
1. register as a general member;
2. register as a corporate member;
3. create a personal user profile, post your personal information and portfolio on admanGo.com;
4. create a corporate profile, post your company information and portfolio on admanGo.com;
5. submit your work to our showcase; and
6. submit your job posting.

Disclosure of Information
We do not give, sell or rent your personal information to any third parties. However, we may give, sell or rent aggregated (anonymous) information about our users generally. If you create a personal profile, corporate profile, submit your work to our showcase or submit your job posting, you agree that we may disclose your personal information that you submitted in the registration process to other visitors of admanGo.com. Your personal information will be viewable by other visitors of admanGo.com and such visitors may contact you directly based on this information.

Cookies
A cookie is a small data file that certain web sites write to your hard drive when you visit them. A cookie file can contain information such as a user ID that the site users to track the pages you've visited. But the only personal information a cookie can contain is information you supply yourself. A cookie can't read data off your hard disk or read cookie files created by other sites. When you view our web site, we may use cookies to store some information on your computer. We may save your password and other personal information so it does not need to be reentered.
