The Advertising Council’s Privacy Statement
Updated: November 7, 2000

Your privacy while visiting our site is important to us. Because we gather certain types of information about the users of our site, we feel you should fully understand the terms and conditions surrounding the capture and use of that information. This privacy statement discloses what information we gather, how we use it, and how to correct or change it.

Overall
The Ad Council may use cookies to track aggregated traffic patterns. Cookies are automatically assigned by a Web server. A cookie is a small data file that certain Web sites write to your hard drive when you visit them. A cookie file can contain information that the site uses to track the pages you have visited. But the only personal information a cookie can contain is information you supply yourself. A cookie can't read data off your hard disk or read cookie files created by other sites. You can refuse cookies by turning cookies off in your browser.

This site contains links to other sites in which the Ad Council is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such sites. The Ad Council encourages you to review the privacy policies of the sites that you visit, especially before submitting your personal information.

Unless otherwise noted in this privacy statement, the Ad Council does not give, sell or rent individual user information collected on our site to any third party.

Material Fulfillment
Members of the media industry can order Ad Council materials from the site by submitting their information and request via an online form. The form is then emailed to the designated Ad Council representative and may be forwarded to our fulfillment vendor. Unless you have requested to be added to our database, the contact information submitted by you is not used by the Ad Council for any additional purposes other than to fulfill the specified request.

Online Donations
Visitors interested in making a donation to the Ad Council may do so by visiting a third party’s site (http://www.givedirect.org) that uses a secure server. The Ad Council is not responsible for the privacy practices of this site.

Donated Online Advertising
Representatives from sites donating online advertising space have the option to subscribe to an email discussion list that informs sites of new creative and other updates. This list is moderated by an Ad Council staff member, and only list subscribers may post to the list. The Ad Council does not give, sell or rent this list’s subscriber information to any third parties. However, please note that content sent to the list is viewable by all subscribers. For more information about this list, email banners@adcouncil.org.
