The New Wave Media inc. (AdoTube) Services

New Wave Media inc. (AdoTube), Inc. provides easy to use Services to businesses with websites and to advertisers that are trying to reach the users of those websites. The Services link companies that are collecting consumer data on their websites ("Web Publisher Clients") and companies that want to collect consumer data ("Advertisers") by allowing consumers visiting and/or registering on websites of Web Publisher Clients to explicitly request that their registration data be sent to one or more specific Advertisers presenting offers using New Wave Media inc. (AdoTube)'s Services.

You may Opt-In to Advertiser Offers

The New Wave Media inc. (AdoTube) Services allow You, as a visitor or a registrant on the Web Publisher Client website, the ability to view, request and receive special offers provided by Advertisers working with New Wave Media inc. (AdoTube). When You visit a website of a Web Publisher Client that is working with New Wave Media inc. (AdoTube), You may be presented with an New Wave Media inc. (AdoTube) offer placement or web page each listing one or more special offers from certain Advertisers working with New Wave Media inc. (AdoTube). You may choose which offer(s) You would like to receive or hear more about by simply clicking on the box or [yes] radio button adjacent to the offer(s) or selecting a positive response in the manner indicated. You can choose some offers, all of them, or none of them - it's up to You.

From time to time, as You opt-in to certain offers via an offer placement or offer page, New Wave Media inc.
