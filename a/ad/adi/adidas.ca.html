Privacy and Security

In addition to the general adidas Group Privacy policy, which can be found here, the following policy applies to shopadidas.ca: We will use the information we have gathered about you only to provide a convenient shopping experience for you in the adidas Online Store and to fill your orders, except in those cases where you have also registered to have one or more newsletters sent to you.

What information do we collect and how do we use it?

Personal Information

adidas Canada collects personal information when you open an account, submit orders or purchase merchandise from the Website, or otherwise provide information to participate in the services and offerings on the Website. On such occasions, the personal information collected by or on behalf of adidas may include, without limitation: your name, e-mail address, date of birth, billing address, shipping address, postal code, telephone number, mobile phone number, credit card information, debit card and related information, information concerning your interests, and other demographic information, as applicable. We may also ask some additional optional questions to help us better serve you. Please note that when you make a purchase from any of the Websites, we may need to contact you via phone, postal mail or e-mail if we have questions regarding your order.

To customize our response to your preferences with your consent, adidas may ask you for personal information, such as your name, address, date of birth, gender, e-mail address, telephone number, mobile phone number and similar information. We do this, for instance, when you establish a user profile or account, register for contests and promotions, submit requests to adidas Canada customer service, respond to communications from adidas Canada and for other services that require registration or subscription. When you establish a user profile, we may also request optional information from you regarding certain user preferences and similar information. In addition, personal information is collected in certain portions of the Websites in which visitors specifically and knowingly provide such information along with content submissions, community postings (e.g., chat or bulletin boards), suggestions, surveys, polls or voting.

adidas Canada may also collect personal information from you about a third party to carry out services relating to you or that third party at your request. For instance, you may provide us with personal information about a third party when you request that we ship an order to a third party or to send an e-mail to a third party. That information will only be used for the specific purpose of responding to your request, such as delivering the e-mail or to process and ship the order and confirm shipment.

Non-Personal and Aggregate Data

When you visit our Websites, we may collect "click stream" data (IP address, server address, domain name, browser information, and other non-personally identifiable information regarding your visit. This information may be combined with information you have provided to us (for instance by registering), which will enable us to analyze and better customize your visits. Like many websites, we may collect information regarding website usage by using cookies and tracking technologies. We may use information gathered from the cookies and tracking technologies to analyze and understand how the Websites are used and to enhance your visits to the Websites. For example, we may use cookies and tracking technologies to: (i) customize account pages for you; (ii) track user visits and account set-ups; (iii) determine what set-ups are most frequently requested; and (iv) provide for a "remember me" function to save your user name and password so you do not have to provide it each time you visit the Websites. You can find more information on how we use cookies in the section titled "Use of Cookies."

We may also use a third-party service provider to help us optimize the products and services we offer through the Websites. That third party may place web beacons or data tags on the Websites and/or a cookie on your computer to facilitate collection of information on use of the Websites each time you visit the Website ("Session Data").

Use of Cookies

A "cookie" is a small string of information that the website that you visit transfers to your computer for identification purposes. Cookies can be used to follow your activity on the Websites and that information helps us to understand your preferences and improve your website experience. Cookies are also used to remember user information such as your user name and password.

You can turn off all cookies if you prefer not to receive them. You can also have your computer warn you whenever cookies are being used. For both options you must adjust your browser settings. Please note than when you set your computer to reject cookies, the settings may limit the functionality of the website you visit and it is possible that you will not have access to some of the features on the website.

Use of Visitors' Information

Except for the purposes set forth below and otherwise provided in this Privacy Policy, adidas Canada will not share, sell, trade or otherwise transfer personal information provided by you on or through the Websites to third parties without informing you in advance. We will primarily use your personal information to provide products and services to you and to perform the following:

Business Uses - adidas Canada uses the personal information that you provide to: (i) establish user profiles and accounts and store user preferences; (ii) administer and fulfill contests, promotions, surveys or other site features (for instance to deliver a prize you may have won in a contest); (iii) provide you with the products or services that you have ordered or requested; (iv) process and ship orders; (v) send order and shipping confirmations; (vi) provide customer service; (vii) process returns or exchanges; and (viii) protect you, and us, against error and fraud; (ix) keep records of returns and exchanges; and (x) as needed to investigate or enforce legal rights and obligations.

In addition, if you have opted-in to receive communications, including e-mails and/or SMS messages from adidas Canada; we will use personal information provided by you to customize advertising and content we present to you, and to send you information and materials, including, without limitation, via e-mail or SMS messages about products, offers and news we think could interest you. This information may relate to products, offers and news of adidas Canada or its licensees or carefully selected commercial partners.

Third-Parties - Information collected on or through the Websites may be shared or transferred to third parties who may be located outside the United Kingdom who perform services for us or with whom we contract for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. For example, we may use third parties to host our Websites, assist in designing and operating the Websites or certain features, perform services (e.g., prize fulfillment), print or send communications on behalf of adidas Canada, authorize and process payments, process and administer gift cards, provide customer service and help us analyze data.

adidas Canada requires that these third-party providers employ a comparable level of protection for personal consumer information. Further, adidas Canada only provides these third-party providers with the personal information necessary to perform their designated functions and services for us. You may be asked on certain areas our Websites whether you wish to receive information from third parties and, upon your consent, adidas Canada and/or those third parties may use the personal information you provide to contact you consistent with the consent that you provided as permitted by applicable law.

Affiliated Businesses; Business Transfers - We may share the personal information you have provided us among our affiliated businesses that we control or that are under common control with us. They may use this information to offer products and services to you consistent with the purposes identified in this Privacy Policy, to the extent permitted by applicable law. The personal information that we collect may also be transferred as an asset in connection with a merger or sale (including any transfers made as a part of an insolvency or bankruptcy proceeding) involving all or part of adidas Canada or as part of a corporate reorganization, stock sale or change of control.

Required Disclosures - We may share personal information you provide to us with a third party if we are required by law to disclose that personal information. For example, we may be required to disclose personal information to respond to a subpoena, court order or other legal process, including any administrative proceeding, or in connection with an investigation. We may also disclose this information in response to a law enforcement agency's request or as otherwise required or permitted by law when the information is relevant to investigating a violation of law or to address an emergency that threatens an individual's life, health or security.

Non-Personal Information - We may use non-personal information to analyze visits to the Websites and learn about the interests of our visitors in aggregate and to better understand your interests and needs, so we can improve our products and services and deliver to you the type of content, features and promotions in which you are most interested. We may share non-personal, aggregate data with our marketing partners or other third parties for research and marketing purposes.

We may on occasion combine information we received on or through the Websites with information obtained from other sources and outside records to enable us to update and correct the information contained in our database.

Who Manages Visitors' Information

The websites of adidas Canada and the information collected, are managed and controlled by adidas Canada or by third- party service providers that are required to maintain the security and confidentiality standards described in this Privacy Policy on behalf of adidas Canada. The servers that make the Websites available and that store the information collected by adidas Canada are located in the United Kingdom.

Protection of Visitors' Information

adidas Canada takes administrative, technical and physical precautions to safeguard your personal information against loss, theft, and misuse, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction and requires its third-party service providers to do the same.

You can help us by also taking precautions to protect your personal data when you are on the Internet by changing your passwords often, using a combination of letters and numbers, and making sure you use a secure web browser.

Children's Privacy

adidas Canada does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 14 for marketing purposes, because some information is collected electronically, it can appear to be the personal information of the adidas Canada purchaser of the product, and will be treated as such by this privacy policy. If a child under 14 submits personal information to adidas Canada and we learn that that personal information is the information of a child under 14, we will attempt to delete the information as soon as possible.

If you are under the age of 14 you should not provide any personal information to us and you should use the Websites only with the involvement or a parent or guardian.

 

Right of Access

You have a right to access your personal information. You may exercise this right by visiting the "My Account" section of the website. From this link, you can view, challenge, modify or delete your personal information from our database. Please note that adidas Canada reserves the right to retain personal information relating to you reasonably necessary to document its business activities or as needed for required disclosures.

Choices You Have

No one is required to provide us with personal information. Your provision of any personal information to us on or through our Websites or by other means must be voluntary and with your consent to the uses described in this Privacy Policy. In some cases, only persons who provide the requested personal information will be able to order products and services or otherwise participate in the Websites' activities and offerings.

If you would like to be on our e-mail list, you will be provided with the opportunity to designate your preference on the Website. You will also have the opportunity to "opt-in" to join our mailing list to receive catalogs and related materials through the mail. In addition, if you provide your mobile phone number and would like to receive text messages and/or SMS messages from us, you will have the opportunity to "opt-in" to receive those communications.

Please note that unless you cancel your registration, you will not be able to opt-out of receiving all types of communications, such as service announcements and administrative messages, as these types of communications are a necessary part of your account and use of the Websites.

Cancellation of Registration

You may cancel your user registration at any time, and you can choose to opt-out of receiving any future e-mails and/or SMS messages from adidas Canada. Please note that modifications of the status of personal information will not stop e-mails and/or SMS messages (i) generated before adidas Canada can accomplish the modification of the status of personal information or (ii) from third parties with whom adidas Canada my have already shared that personal information consistent with this Privacy Policy.

 

Changes to the Policy

adidas Canada may update its privacy policy from time to time due to changes in regulations and to keep up with new developments and possibilities on the internet. When we change the policy in a material way a notice will be posted on our website along with the updated privacy policy.
