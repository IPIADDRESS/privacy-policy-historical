Privacy Policy

Our Commitment To Privacy
Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our homepage and at every point where personally identifiable information may be requested.

The Information We Collect
This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the Adfusion.com website. We do this when you register your company or yourself as a media seller or buyer. Upon registration, you will be asked to provide certain information about your company, such as the company's name and address, the name, e-mail address, and telephone/fax numbers of the primary contact person and any other information about your company that we may reasonably require. All the information you have provided in the registration process will be kept stored and confidential in our database of potential customers.

The Way We Use Information
The information you provide about your company or yourself during registration allows us to notify you of the password you need to gain access to the site and/or request additional contact information. We do not share this information with outside parties.

Adfusion.com may also collect information such as the type of web browser you use or your IP address. This information is used to ensure that our website is compatible with the most commonly used browsers. This information is also used to help identify you on the service and to gather broad demographic information.

Cookies
Adfusion.com may place a "cookie" in the browser files of your computer. A cookie is a file that identifies you as a unique user. The cookie itself does not contain any individual information about you; however, it allows us to relate your site usage to information you have given us. Additionally, cookies help us offer you information and special opportunities tailored to your interests. Cookies may also save you from re-entering your username and password by remembering this information each time that you visit the site.

The first time you log on to our site, you will be asked to accept an Adfusion.com cookie. You do not need to accept the cookie to use our services, however refusing it will force you to enter your username and password every time you return to the site.

Security Precautions
Your individual Adfusion.com user information is password protected. We recommend that you do not share your password with anyone. While Adfusion.com strives to protect your individual information, we cannot guarantee the security of any information that you transmit to Adfusion.com. You are solely responsible for maintaining the secrecy of any passwords or other information.

Adfusion.com will NOT disclose any personal information except as described above, as required by law or to protect our interests. Adfusion.com does disclose user information, including demographic data, in an aggregated form that does not identify individual users in order to describe its services to prospective partners, advertisers and other third parties and for other lawful purposes.

If at any time you believe that Adfusion.com has not adhered to these principles, we will use all commercially reasonable efforts to promptly determine and correct the problem. Please notify us by email at client.service@adfusion.coms or via mail at:

Adfusion.com
11111 West Olympic Blvd
Suite 401
Los Angeles, CA 90064

Changes in Privacy Policy
Adfusion.com reserves the right to change this privacy statement at any time without prior notice to you. Any such change will be effective immediately upon the posting of the revised privacy statement on our web site. We recommend that you visit this web page periodically to view our latest Privacy Statement.
