HOSTARS also utilizes cookies in conjunction with its web sites or advertisements (collectively for purposes of this paragraph "web sites"). Thereafter when you visit a web site, a message is sent back to the web server by the browser accessing the web site. You may elect not to accept cookies by changing the designated settings on your web browser. However, not utilizing cookies may prevent you from using certain functions and features of web sites. The information collected from the use of cookies is used and analyzed to improve HOSTARS's web sites.
