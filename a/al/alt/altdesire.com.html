Certain portions of altdesire.com, which may contain content considered offensive to some, are inaccessible to individuals under the age of 18 via a standard "credit card" wall, as advocated by the Communications Decency Act. For those portions, that may be accessible to individuals under the age of 18, such as the altdesire.com home page and other areas presenting non-offensive content, altdesire.com has no intention of collecting any personally identifiable information (that is, name, address, telephone number, or email address) from individuals under eighteen years of age.
