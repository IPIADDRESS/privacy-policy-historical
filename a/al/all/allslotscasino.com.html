Adware is a piece of software that automatically displays advertisements to a computer without your consent. What is malware?
Malware (or malicious software) refers to harmful software, often called viruses, worms or Trojan Horses, designed to infiltrate or damage your computer's operating system or other programs.  Malware is sometimes bundled covertly with other download software and loads in the background.  Our software contains no such malicious content.
What is spyware?
Spyware is a type of software that automatically installs itself on your computer, usually without your knowledge, and covertly collects and transmits data about your computer use or online activity, often use in identity theft and fraud.  We do not allow any spyware to be installed in tandem with our software.
