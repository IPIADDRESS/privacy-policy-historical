  • Right to Access your Data - Art. 15 GDPR - Allows you to obtain from us, the confirmation that we process or not personal data that refers to you, and if yes, allows your access to the data and other useful information in regards to your personal data.

  • Right to Restriction of processing - Art.18 GDPR - It is a temporary right. The data subject shall have the right to obtain from the controller restriction of processing.   • Right to Object - Art. 21 GDPR. - The data subject shall have the right to object, on grounds relating to his or her particular situation, at any time to processing of personal data concerning him or her which is based on point (e) or (f) of Article 6(1), including profiling based on those provisions.
