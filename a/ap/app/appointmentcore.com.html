Privacy Policy

AppointmentCore (“Us,” “Our,” “We,” or “AppointmentCore”) created this Privacy Policy (“Policy”) to explain how we collect, process, and protect your personal data (“PII”). This includes any PII we might collect when you use our website www.appointmentcore.com (the “Site”), booking links, or when you contact us. By using the Site or otherwise providing your PII to us, you consent to the privacy practices described in this Policy.

Please note that your use of our Site is also subject to our Terms of Use.

Information We Collect

The personal information that we collect and process may include:

  • Information You Provide Us. You can provide information to us on our Site through various means, including creation of an online account, newsletter sign-up, feedback forms, contact us forms, e-mail forms, information pages, smart phone or mobile apps, surveys and contests, user generated content (including blog posts, comments, photos, videos, messages, social media, etc.), connecting to us via social media sites such as Facebook, request for information forms and via the Infusionsoft API. The information we collect includes PII, such as your name, home address, work address, billing address, shipping address, telephone numbers (both work and personal), and e-mail address. If you purchase any products or services through our Site, we also collect your credit card or debit card number, payment history and related information, as applicable. Should you provide us with your Infusionsoft access information, we store this access information for better customer support.
  • Information We Collect Automatically. We automatically receive certain types of data whenever you interact with us. For example, when you use our Site, our system receives device information, such as your unique device identifier and operating system version, and log information such as your IP address, browser type, geolocation, the date and time of your request and referral URL. We also receive data about you from third parties, such as Infusionsoft. If you are downloading one of our e-mail plug-ins (“plug-in software”), your e-mails and contacts are synced with our server when you launch the plug-in software. We collect your e-mail addresses and contacts to provide the functionality for our plug-in software.
  • No Information From Children Under Age 13. If you are under the age of 13, please do not attempt to register with us at our Site or provide any personal information about yourself to us. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under the age of 13, we will delete that information. If you believe we might have any information from a child under the age of 13, please contact us at privacy@appointmentcore.com.

How We Use Your Personal Information

Whether we receive your personal information directly from you or from a third party source, we will only use your personal information in connection with our ordinary professional activities. These activities may include:

  • Internal Use. All submitted PII is stored in our database and may be archived elsewhere. Our system administrators and support personnel have access to PII that is processed by AppointmentCore. We use PII internally to serve our users and enable them to receive the full benefits of AppointmentCore. For example, PII is used to resolve technical support cases, to conduct marketing analysis, to create user profiles, to provide services or complete transactions users request, to anticipate and resolve issues with the AppointmentCore’s products and services, and to create and inform you of new products and services that better meet your needs. We may communicate with users at any of the contact points provided to us for any of the aforementioned purposes. Please note that AppointmentCore will never initiate contact with our users’ customers.
  • Cookies. When you visit our Site or click on one of our advertisements on other properties (e.g. Facebook) we send one or more “cookies” to your computer or other device. A cookie is a small file containing a string of characters that is sent to your computer when you visit a website. When you visit the website again, the cookie allows that site to recognize your browser. Cookies may store unique identifiers, user preferences and other information. You can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some website features or services may not function properly without cookies. We use cookies to improve the quality of our service, including for storing user preferences. We use persistent, session, and flash cookies, and may use other types of cookies in the future.
  • Google Analytics. Google Analytics uses “cookies,” which are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information in an anonymous form. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the Site (including IP address) is transmitted to Google. This information is then used to understand the services that you use and how you use them. Any information collected from you, including PII, is anonymous and unlinked. For more information about Google Analytics, including Google’s privacy policy, please visit: http://www.google.com/intl/en/analytics/privacyoverview.html.

If you have given us your express consent, we may use your personal information for additional purposes. You may withdraw your consent at any time. Additional purposes for which we may process your personal data may include:

  • Using PII submitted to us or otherwise collected to perform website and marketing analytics to improve the quality of our communications and interactions with you;
  • Communicating with you with respect to software developments, announcements, events, and AppointmentCore products and services which may be of interest to you;
  • Distributing surveys or marketing materials; and
  • Any other purpose for which you have given consent.

How We Share Your Personal Information

Any sharing or transferring of your personal information is done in accordance with applicable data protection laws, and we will take appropriate safeguards to ensure its integrity and protection.

  • To Provide Products, Services, and Information. As described above, we collect information from you so that we can provide products and services that you purchase using our Site. We may provide information to third party vendors who assist us in processing and completing transactions requested by you in accordance with the products and services we offer. For example, we may use third party vendors to help host our website, send out e-mail updates about the Site, process payments, and assist with marketing services. These service providers may have access to your PII for a limited time. When third parties have access to your PII, we implement reasonable protections to limit their use of such information to the service they provide, and prohibit such vendors from using or disclosing your PII for any other purpose.
  • Online Account. AppointmentCore stores your PII on our servers in the United States. However, AppointmentCore is a global technology company, and you agree and acknowledge by using our Site that AppointmentCore may transmit your PII outside of the United States. Please note if you are not a U.S. citizen that the privacy laws of the United States may be less stringent than the laws of your country. If you object to your PII being transferred or otherwise processed as described in this Policy, please do not use our Site.
  • Newsletters. If you sign-up to receive newsletters or other communications from us, we use your e-mail address and other relevant data to provide the newsletters and other communications to you. You can always opt-out of receiving e-mail newsletters or advertisements by using the opt-out feature provided within the e-mail communications.
  • Advertising. We use cookies and other information to provide relevant interest-based advertising to you. Interest-based ads are ads presented to you based on your browsing behavior in order to provide you with ads more tailored to your interests. These interest-based ads may be presented to you while you are browsing our site or third-party sites not owned by AppointmentCore. We belong to ad networks that may use your browsing history across participating websites to show you interest-based advertisements on those websites.
  • Legal Requests or Requirements and to Prevent Harm. We reserve the right to disclose your PII as required by law and when we believe that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights and/or to comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, subpoena or legal process. We may also share information when we have a good faith belief it is necessary to prevent fraud or other illegal activity, to prevent imminent bodily harm, or to prevent harm to AppointmentCore, the Site, or our customers. This may include sharing information with other companies, lawyers, courts, or other government entities.
  • Transfer in the Event of Sale or Change of Control. If the ownership of all or substantially all of our business changes or we otherwise transfer assets relating to our business or the Sites to a third party, we may transfer your personal information to the new owner to be used consistent with this Policy. In such a case, your information would remain subject to the promises made in the applicable privacy policy.

Access and Control of Your Personal Information

In addition to your rights under applicable data protection legislation and where we are permitted or required by applicable law and regulation and by our professional obligations, we will provide you, upon request, with a copy of your personal information. We will also correct any errors that you identify to us.

  • You can access, change, or update your PII by emailing us at privacy@appointmentcore.com. We will implement any requested changes as soon as we reasonably can, subject to reasonable limitations, such as the need to verify your identity.
  • You can opt-out of receiving marketing and promotional e-mails from AppointmentCore by using the unsubscribe feature located in our e-mails, or by emailing us at privacy@appointmentcore.com.
  • Your personal information will be deleted when it is no longer reasonably required for our ordinary professional activities or you withdraw your consent (where applicable), provided that we are not legally required or otherwise permitted to continue to hold such data.
  • If you close your account, we will no longer use your online account information or share it with third parties. We may, however, retain a copy of the information for archival purposes and to avoid identity theft or fraud.

Security of Your Personal Information

We implement and maintain reasonable security controls to safeguard the confidentiality, integrity, and access to your PII. We protect your data with several layers of protocols including password-protection, firewalls, and employing SSL encryption. We regularly audit our system to protect any data you store on our servers from possible vulnerabilities. We may also utilize secure technology to transfer data provided by users and additional measures in the processing of sensitive PII. Additionally, to protect your data, we have in place confidentiality agreements with our employees, various service providers, and third parties. While we have taken multiple measures to protect and secure your data, we cannot guarantee, ensure, or warrant the security of your information may not be accessed, destroyed, or disclosed by way of the unauthorized acts of others. Please take care to protect the security of your login information. Please note that e-mails you send to us through our Site are not encrypted.
