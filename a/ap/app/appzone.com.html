Privacy Policy


Privacy Statement

AppZone is committed to protecting the privacy of all of our customers and visitors to the highest industry standards and to using technology that provides an exceptional, intuitive, and safe online experience. This Statement of Privacy applies to the AppZone website and related services and governs data collection and usage. By using the AppZone website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

AppZone complies with the EU Safe Harbor framework as set forth by the Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use, and retention of data from the European Union.  Information regarding the EU Safe Harbor Framework can be found at: http://export.gov/safeharbor

Collection of your Personal Information

AppZone collects personal information (including personally identifiable information) from you on the registration form when you create an account. This information might include, but may not be limited to, your e-mail address, name, home and/or work address, home and/or work telephone number, and credit card information. We also collect your personal information when you contact us (e.g., sales/product inquiries, technical support). We use this information to communicate with you. AppZone may also collect anonymous information such as your ZIP code, age, gender, preferences, interests, and favorites for market research. There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected by AppZone. This information may include, but is not necessarily limited to, your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times, and referring website addresses. Collectively, this information is used by AppZone for the delivery and/or operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide both specific and anonymous statistics regarding use of the AppZone website.

Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personal information or personally sensitive data through AppZone websites or services hosted on AppZone.com or its subdomains, this information may be collected and used by others. To request removal of your personal information from our website, contact us at: support@AppZone.com.  In some cases, we may not be able to remove your personal information, in which case we will let you know if we are unable to do so and why.

AppZone encourages you to review the privacy statements of websites you choose to link to from AppZone so that you can understand how those websites collect, use, and share information. AppZone is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on websites outside of the AppZone family of websites.

General
AppZone collects and uses your personal information to operate the AppZone website and deliver the services you have requested. AppZone also uses your personal information to inform you of other products or services that may be available from AppZone and/or its affiliates.

AppZone may also post surveys to our website or contact you regarding surveys. We use surveys to better understand our customers, to provide higher quality services, to conduct market research, and to provide guidance to the products and services we offer, among other things. Participation in any survey or contest is completely voluntary and you therefore have a choice whether to participate. By participating in any contest, survey, and/or promotion you consent to the use of any information you provide, at the sole judgment of AppZone.  AppZone may use a third party service provider to conduct these surveys, contests, and/or promotions. Any third party service provider will be prohibited from using the information for any other purpose.  We will not share the personal information you provide through a contest, survey, or promotion with any outside individual or organization for any unrelated purpose except as provided herein.

We will retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services to you.  If you wish to cancel your account or request that we no longer use your information to provide you services, contact us via email at: support@AppZone.com. In the sole judgment of AppZone, we may retain and use your information indefinitely to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce our agreements, maintain business records, support client sales and/or service histories, or as otherwise in the normal course of business.

Use of Cookies
The AppZone website uses "cookies" to help personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a website. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you. One of the primary purposes of cookies is to tell the web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalize AppZone pages or register with AppZone site or services, a cookie helps AppZone to recall specific information and details on subsequent visits to make the site faster and more convenient, among other things.  

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the AppZone services or websites you visit.

The use of cookies by any third party is not covered by our privacy policy. We do not have access to or control over these cookies.

When personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted over the internet, it is protected through the use of encryption (e.g., Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol).
