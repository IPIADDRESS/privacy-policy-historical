App4Smart Privacy Policy

  • “Personal Data” means information or its combination that can be used to identify you as an individual (natural person), such as, for example first and last name.

To access the Website or App, to provide you with the services and for other purposes that will be mentioned further in this Policy we may collect the following Personal Data (by the way of example):

Your submission of any Personal Data is voluntary and you have the choice not to disclose any not mentioned above Personal Data to us.

In the course of using the Website and App you will be able to voluntarily disclose, transfer or publish openly and publicly, or share with certain other Users any Information about yourself, including Personal Data. 4. Your Location Information
We collect geographical identification metadata (GPS, WiFi, user-entered) when you use the App. We may use GPS technology (or other similar technology) to define your current location in order to determine the place you are located at the moment so you can use the services to the full extent. We will not share your current location with other Users or anyone else.
If you do not want us to use your location for the purposes set forth above, you should turn off the location services in your device settings when using the App. You understand that in such a case you will not be able to utilize the services in full or at all.
To opt out of the Services that use your location Information you may uninstall the App at all times.

If you connect your App4Smart account with your Third-Party Service account, we ask for your permission to collect certain information from your Third-Party Service account (such as information from your Third-Party Service profile).
We use your Information from such Third-Party Service account only in accordance with your privacy settings you have set up with that Third-Party Service.
6. Information We Do Not Collect
We do not process and collect any Personal Data or personally identifiable information, such as (by way of example):

  • Financial and payment information about you.

The Website automatically collects usage information, such as:
  • Your IP address,
  • The type of device you use,
  • Browser type, version and language,
  • Type and version of your operating system,
  • The number and frequency of visitors to the Website.
