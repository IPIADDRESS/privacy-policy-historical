Pursuant to Abe's Market's Terms of Use documents, you must be at least 18 years of age to use the Site or have permission from a legal guardian. This policy makes no provision for individuals under 18 years old. This policy does not apply to the practices of third parties that Abe's Market does not own or control, or individuals that Abe's Market does not employ or manage.


Data We Collect

You may browse the Site without revealing personally identifiable information about yourself. Once you choose to register for an account or to make a purchase, you are not anonymous to us. We may collect and store the following information:

  • Email address, physical contact information, and financial information, such as credit card or bank account numbers
  • Shipping, billing and other information you provide when purchasing a product
  • Transactional information based on your activities on the Site
  • Correspondence through our Site, and correspondence sent to us
  • Information from your interaction with our Site, including computer and connection information, page views statistics, IP address and standard web log information;
  • Supplemental information from third parties (for example, results of credit checks from a credit bureau, as permitted by law).

How We Use the Data We Collect

Our primary purpose in collecting personal information is to provide you with a safe, efficient, and customized experience. You agree that we may use your personal information to:

  • Provide the services and customer support you request
  • Resolve disputes, collect fees, and troubleshoot problems
  • Prevent potentially prohibited or illegal activities, and enforce our agreements including the Terms of Use
  • Customize, measure and improve our services and content
  • Offer targeted marketing, service updates, and promotional offers based on your preferences
  • Compare your information for accuracy, including verification with third parties

Marketing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes without your explicit consent. We may combine your information with information we collect from other companies and use it to improve and personalize our Site and services.

Abe's Market will send you an email when you register to welcome and confirm your account. Receipt of this email may be required for using our services.

Abe's Market may send you service-related announcements when it is necessary to do so. You may not opt-out of service-related communications which are not promotional in nature. If you do not wish to receive them, you can deactivate your account by emailing support@abesmarket.com from the email address you used to register with our Site.


Cookies

Cookies are small data files (which often include an anonymous unique identifier) that are sent to your browser from a web site's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive.

We use cookies on certain of our pages to: access your information when you "sign in"; keep track of your preferences; gather information about traffic; analyze our web page flow; customize our services, content and advertising; measure promotional effectiveness, and promote security.

You can configure your browser to accept all cookies, reject all cookies, or notify you when a cookie is set. If you reject cookies, you may not be able to use certain features or functionalities that require you to “sign in” and you may not be able to take full advantage of our services.

Information about our customers is an important part of our business, and we are not in the business of selling it to others.

Obviously, however, this does not include selling, renting, sharing, or otherwise disclosing personally identifiable information from customers for commercial purposes in violation of the commitments set forth in this Privacy Policy.

Abe's Market may transfer information about you if Abe's Market is acquired by or merged with another company.
