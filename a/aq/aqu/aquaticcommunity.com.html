Privacy Policy

We respect and are committed to protecting your privacy. That is why we have adopted this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy lets you know how your personal information is processed and used. We garante that we will take steps to use your personal information only in ways that are compatible with this Privacy Policy. The following discloses our Privacy Policy.

Information gathering:

Personal Information
We request personal information when you register as a member of this site. You make the decision whether to proceed with any activity that requests personal information. If you do not wish to provide the requested information, however, you may not be able to use the activity/resource in question.

Cookies
When you register as a member of Aquaticcommunity.com, our Web server will send a "cookie" to your computer. A cookie is a small piece of data that is sent to your browser from a Web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. A cookie can't read data off your hard disk nor read cookie files created by other Web sites. Cookies do not damage your system. We use cookies to recognize you when you are logged into the Aquaticcommunity.com network.

IP Adresses
Every computer connected to the Internet is given a domain name and a set of numbers, that serve as that computer's "Internet Protocol" IP address. When a visitor requests a page from any Web site within the Aquaticcommunity.com network, our Web servers automatically recognize that visitor's domain name and IP address. The domain name and IP address reveal nothing personal about you other than the IP address from which you have accessed our site. We use this information to examine our traffic in aggregate, and to investigate miss use of the Aquaticcommunity.com, its users, or to cooperate with law enforcement.

Information usage:

Any and all information you willingly disclose to Aquaticcommunity.com network is held strictly confidential. We will not sell any personally identifying information obtained through this Web site to any other organization, and we will never willfully disclose any personal identification information about our audience to any third party.
