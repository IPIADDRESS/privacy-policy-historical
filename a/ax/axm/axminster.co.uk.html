How to Order

  • In the left-hand navigation bar - click on the category that you are interested in.
  • A list of sub-categories will be listed on the right hand side, click on desired sub-category.
  • The products within the selected sub-category will then be listed in the centre of the screen.
  • When you have located the desired product, select the quantity required from the drop-down field.
  • Then click the 'Buy' button, this will add the item to the 'Shopping Trolley'.
  • Repeat the above process for each item required.
  • If however you know the 'Stock Code' you can use the 'Fast Track Ordering' screen, simply enter all the items you require and click 'Add to Trolley' once you are happy with the items then click 'Confirm this Order'.
  • When you have items in your 'Shopping Trolley', the number of items will be displayed near the right-hand side of your screen.
  • Within the 'Shopping Trolley' screen you can review the items you have selected.
  • Once you are happy to proceed click the 'Process Order' button.
  • You will be prompted to enter your personal details, if you have ordered previously your details will be displayed automatically otherwise click 'I am a New Customer' (providing you have 'Cookies' enabled).
  • Once you have entered all your details, click the 'Send Order' button.
  • You will then receive a confirmation via email, if you do not receive a confirmation please contact Customer Service immediately click here for details.

Payment Information

Payment may only be made by credit card (MasterCard or Visa) or Debit Card (Switch, Solo or Delta). Please note that stringent security checks are observed to ensure against the fraudulent use of cards. Unfortunately we are unable to accept orders over the Internet from account holders unless you are using a credit/debit card. Please note that we are unable to accept American Express or Visa Electron cards.

We will not charge your credit/debit card until your order is ready for despatch.

 

Delivery Information and Carriage Charges

All orders to UK mainland are delivered carriage paid (web orders only). However we do recommend certain products be delivered by our own fleet and this service is subject to a surcharge of either £30 or £45. You will be notified of any postal or carriage costs before we process your order. Goods in stock are usually delivered within 3 to 4 working days (orders despatched via our own fleet are usually delivered within 14 working days, you will be contacted by our Transport Manager to discuss delivery arrangements). We will inform you either by letter or email if the goods are out of stock and state an estimated delivery date. We will also give you the option to cancel your order. We will not charge your credit/debit card until your order is ready for despatch.

 

Site Security

Your personal details and credit/debit card information are sent securely over the Internet via our Secure Server Software, providing your browser supports Secure Socket Layer (SSL) Technology. Microsoft Internet Explorer 4.0 or higher and Netscape Navigator 4.5 or higher supports SSL technology. If your browser does not support SSL Technology you will need to contact the manufacturer or your PC vendor. To view our VeriSign certificate double-click on the yellow padlock in the status bar of your browser when you process your order.
