2. Which information we may collect on our Users?
We collect two types of data and information:

2.1 Non-personal Information. The first type is non-identifiable and anonymous information (“Non-personal Information”). To put it simply, we have no idea what is the identity of the User from which we have collected the Non-personal Information. Non-personal Information which is being gathered consists of technical information and behavioral information, as detailed below:
Technical information:

· Type of Browser (e.g. Explorer, Firefox, Chrome, Safari, etc.)
· Screen resolution (e.g. 800×600, 1024×768, etc.)
· Browser and keyboard language (e.g. English)
· Any Similar technical information.

Behavioral information:
· User’s clickstream on Shaker (i.e. the fact that User has chosen to click on a certain link or access a certain feature on Shaker).
· Any Similar behavioral information.

2.2 Personal Information. The other type of information is individually identifiable information (“Personal Information”). To put it simply, this information may identify an individual or is of a private and/or sensitive nature. Personal Information which is being gathered consists of any personal details provided voluntarily by the User to Facebook and which the User specifically permits Facebook to Provide Shaker with, upon any access to or use of the Shaker application.
When you connect to Shaker for the first time you will be asked to permit Facebook to share with Shaker the following information about you:
· Your e-mail account (for user verification purposes)
· Basic Information (your first and last name, your profile picture or its URL, you email & gender);
· your user ID number, which is linked to publically available information such as name and profile photo;
· the user ID numbers for your Facebook friends, whether or not such friends are connected to Shaker, their Birthdates, and information from their “About me” section;

· information regarding and/or relating to your birthday date;

· your uploaded profile pictures album;
· information regarding and/or relating to family & relationship status;
· photos and/or videos tagging you, uploaded by you or by others;
· Information regarding and/or relating to your Likes and Interests (including all pages you performed a “Like” on;

· Information regarding and/or relating to your Check-in activity and Events attendance;
In addition, logs of conversations you initiate on Shaker will be kept in confidence for up to six (6) months, and deleted after the lapse of such period. Please note that such logs shall not be put to any commercial use by us or disclosed to any third-party, except for limited disclosure for security purposes (see Section 4(a)-(f) below).

5. Sharing personal information with Users
Shaker allows you to share your Facebook profile information with other Users on Shaker. Please note that certain information which you have defined as private on Facebook (e.g. information which is visible to Facebook friends only) shall become public through Shaker (i.e. enabling Shaker Users to view such information).

Shaker provides three privacy modes for you to choose from:
1st mode – defined as “Shy mode”
When in Shy mode other Users which are logged on to Shaker with you, will be able to view the following info:
· Basic Facebook account information
· Liked Music, Books, Movies& TV shows
· Education and Work information
· Relationship status
· Hometown and location
· Any information you and the person browsing your profile have in common
2nd mode – defined as “Normal mode”
Normal mode is identical to Shy mode only that you also share your tagged Facebook pictures

3rd mode – defined as “Friendly mode”
Friendly mode is identical to Normal mode only that you also share pictures from albums you uploaded to Facebook (this feature is not yet implemented).

When first entering Shaker, your privacy mode is set on Shy mode by default. However, you can alter your privacy mode as you please at any time, during your use of Shaker. In all the privacy modes, some of your Facebook information may be visible to other users logged onto Shaker with you, even if they cannot view such information on Facebook.

For example, say “A” is a Facebook user that cannot normally see your tagged photos on your Facebook account, and your Shaker Privacy mode is set to Normal. When both “A” and yourself are present on Shaker, “A” will be able to see your tagged photos (“A” will be able to view these photos only from Shaker and won’t be able to see them on Facebook).

To change your privacy mode while using Shaker, look for the “Privacy mode button” on the top bar of the application.
The button reflects your current privacy mode (Shy, Normal or Friendly) .

Clicking the privacy mode button will present you with a pop-up allowing you to change the privacy mode. Approving the request, your privacy mode will change.

The cookies used by Shaker are created per session, does not include any information about you, other than your session key and are removed as your session ends (usually after 24 hours). It is easy to prohibit the Local Storage. Most browsers will allow you to erase cookies from your computer’s hard drive, block acceptance of cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored. In order to erase or disable the Local Storage option in Flash you should use the settings option of Flash according to the specific instructions provided by the technology provider. However, if you block or erase cookies, or change the settings of Flash, your online experience may be limited.

We also use certain third-party cookies. These are a different kind of cookies, stored on your computer by third parties, rather than by the Company. This kind of cookie is only read (and not altered by Shaker) each time you visit Shaker, and also when you visit certain websites or applications that uses similar cookies. Third-party cookies usually store only a Non-personal Information, such as the web pages you have visited, the duration of your browsing, etc.
