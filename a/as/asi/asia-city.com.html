About Asia City and the Information We Collect

Asia City is a lifestyle portal cum online social networking community that connects people through common networks of friends and interests. To accomplish this, our users create their own profiles which are shown to the people in their personal network and indicate their favorite things or events. A personal network consists of a user's friends as well as the extended group of people that a user is connected to via chains of mutual friends. Favorite things can include restaurants, CDs, books, film and etc. They may also highlight their personal event calendar on their profile. To fulfill the purpose of this site, some personal information we ask for is displayed to people within these personal networks.

Information Collected/Tracked by Asia City Information submitted to Asia City

Use of Information Obtained by Asia City Information users submit to Asia City

Profile information as well as first name and photos are displayed to people in a user's personal network, to support the function of the site as social networking community where users can meet new people through their friends. Use of Cookies

A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's computer tied to information about the user. We use both session ID cookies and persistent cookies. For the session ID cookie, once users close the browser, the cookie simply terminates. A persistent cookie is a text file stored on the user's hard drive for an extended period of time. Persistent cookies can be removed by following Internet browser help file directions. Users must enable cookies to use our site.

We use cookies and tracking technologies in a variety of ways, including:

  1. Keeping count of return visits to our site or our advertisers' or partners' sites;
  2. Accumulating and reporting anonymous, aggregate (data collected in mass), statistical information on Web-site and advertisement usage;
  3. Determining which features our overall user population likes best;
  4. Saving your password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our sites;
  5. Displaying the most appropriate advertising or content based on your interests.
  6. Maintaining your previous selected city.

We do not and will not use cookies to collect private information from any user which they did not intentionally submit to us.
