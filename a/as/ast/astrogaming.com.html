The Information We Collect

For instance, if you order a product from us, we may ask for your mailing address so we can send the requested goods to you, and we may also occasionally send you other materials in the mail. If such additional information is optional, you may opt out of providing additional information by not entering it on the ASTRO Gaming website. You may change the information we have on file about you at any time by emailing us at customerservice [at] astrogaming.com with your request. Regardless of the above, we will never store credit card numbers at ASTRO Gaming. All payments are processed through a third-party processor and the information you submit to or through such third-party is governed by and subject to such third-party’s terms of use; we take no responsibility or liability for any transaction you may have with any such third party.

“Personally Identifiable Information” is any piece of information which can be traced back to a specific individual user and thus potentially be used to uniquely identify, contact, or locate that specific individual. Examples of Personally Identifiable Information include names, telephone numbers, social security numbers, and other unique identifiers. In no event will ASTRO Gaming sell or transfer Personally Identifiable Information to third parties, or otherwise provide third parties with access thereto, except as specifically provided in this Privacy Policy and/or in our Terms of Use.

Non-personally identifiable information is information that, by itself, cannot be tracked to a specific individual. Examples of non-personally identifiable information include, but are not limited to, the type of internet browser you are using, the type of computer operating system you are using, your IP address, the time and date of your use of the ASTRO Gaming Offerings, and whether or not you clicked on hypertext links within the ASTRO Gaming website. We may collect non-personally identifiable information while you use the ASTRO Gaming Offerings.

ASTRO Gaming does not process orders or credit cards directly. ASTRO Gaming takes no responsibility for any such third party or their website, privacy practices, billing practices, or any other aspect of the business of such third party. ASTRO Gaming may also collect non-personally identifiable information from users of the ASTRO Gaming Offerings.
