By visiting the Site or using any of the Services, you authorize us to collect, use, disclose, transfer and store information about you as set out in this Privacy Policy.

What kind of information do we collect?
In some cases, We may ask you for personal data, such as your name, address, date of birth, gender, e-mail address, and other personally identifiable information ("Personal Data"). This is especially the case when you take part in contests / promotions, you reply to e-mails, and for some Services that require your subscription. If you wish to be included in our address book, We will ask you to indicate it formally. We may also collect your Personal Data from a third party, for example if the Site allows you to send an e-mail to refer someone to our Site or to an active promotion, and where We would need to know the name and e-mail address of the beneficiary. This information will only be used for the specific purpose (to send the e-mail) and We will not contact the third party unless the third party contacts us. We may use "cookies" to collect information about your visits to our Site and to enhance your experience. You can also find more information about how We use cookies in the section titled "Use of Cookies". When you visit our Site, We may collect data for the analysis of click flows (server address, domain name, etc.). This information may be added to the information you provided to us.

The information you provide
While We do not generally require you to provide Personal Data to view and use the Site, please note that to participate in certain activities, such as lotteries, contests and other promotions, and take part in forums or e-newsletters, We may ask you to provide Personal Data or information about your preferences and hobbies. If you choose not to provide us with this Personal Data or other information, you may not be able to participate in certain activities We offer. If We associate any demographic or other information We have collected about you with your Personal Data, then once associated, that information will be treated as Personal Data. There may be promotions We offer, either personally or jointly with a third party. These kinds of promotions may require registration and disclosure of both Personal Data and other information. In the event that a promotion is jointly offered with a third party, and if you take part in such a promotion, your Personal Data and other information may be shared with the relevant third party. You will then be informed when you provide your Personal Data that it may be disclosed to the third party. By participating in a promotion, you agree to the official rules governing this promotion, you must accept certain conditions, including, unless prohibited by law, to authorize the sponsor and / or other third parties related to the promotion, to use your name, voice and / or portrait in advertising campaigns and marketing materials. Thus, you should always carefully read the promotion rules before you participate.

Automatically Collected Information
In addition to Personal Data or other information that you choose to provide to us, when you access or interact with the Site, We automatically collect certain information such as your IP address and or other unique identifier ("Device ID") for the computer, portable device, mobile technology device or other device ("Device") you use to access the Site and the website where it is located (A Device ID is a number that is automatically assigned to your Device when you access a website or its servers. Our computers identify your device using the Device Identifier). Some mobile providers may also provide to us or to our service providers information about the geographical position of the Device when accessing the Site. Moreover, in addition to certain external companies specialized in analyzes and other telephony providers, We may use a variety of technologies that automatically or subsequently collect information on how you use the Site (" use "). The Usage Information may include, in part, the browser type, operating system, time of day the page was viewed, as well as previously viewed pages. Usage Information generally does not identify the user, but if We can associate them to you as a specific and identifiable individual, We will treat this information as Personal Data. More information on this subject is available below in the section entitled "Use of cookies".

Information about friends and family
When you share information and site links, send or share promotions and marketing messages from us, or if you send products to family and friends and other people, We may collect the information you provide about such people as their name, mailing address, e-mail address and phone number.

Shared information on a website or network belonging to and controlled by a third party
The features of the Site may allow interactions between the Site and other sites belonging to a third party. This is especially the case when you can "like" a product or "share" the content on external websites. If you select "like" or "share" the content of a page or if you post information from or from the Site on a website, function or external application, this information may become public and the external website may have access to your information and use of the Site. You can also choose to use an external application or function (such as one of our applications on Facebook or an application or function of this type on a third-party web site) and as such, you authorize us to collect and share (or authorize the third-party web site to collect and share) your data, including Personal Data. The third party may allow you to uninstall the application or function, in which case We would no longer be able to collect your data through the application or function, but We could retain your previously collected data.

The use of the information of the persons who access the Site
All your personal data to which We have access will be registered, used and protected in accordance with the applicable data protection legislation and this Privacy Policy. We will use your Personal Data primarily to provide you with products and services and, for example:

  • In a contest, promotion, survey or other function of the Site and to provide you with relevant products or services (for example, to give you a prize you would have won in a contest). You may be contacted by phone or e-mail for this purpose.
  • To send you information/materials through e-mail or SMS-on products, offers and information that We believe may be of interest to you. This information may relate to products, offers and information relating to us and/or the licensees or its carefully selected business partners. You will only receive this information if you have agreed to receive e-mails and/or SMS from us. If you no longer wish to receive e-mails and/or SMS messages, you may refuse to receive this type of information by following the instructions to remove your contact information from the e-mail and/or the SMS, please refer to the paragraph below entitled "Withdrawal of your details, deleting or modifying Personal Data". The process described in this paragraph can also be used if you want to change your personal data or if you want your personal data to be deleted from our database.
  • To analyze the pages of the Site consulted and to know what interests the people who visit our Site in general and to understand what interests you may have. This allows us to improve our products and services and to provide you with the type of content, features and promotions that interest you most.

Who manages the information of persons viewing our Site
The Site and the information collected are managed and controlled by us. If you are domiciled outside of the United States, please note that your Personal Data may be transmitted to the United States. By submitting information, you authorize such a transfer.
When We transfer personal data from the European Economic Area to other countries, We use a variety of legal mechanisms, including contracts, to help ensure your rights and protections travel with your data.

The protection of the information of the persons who visit the Site
In order to protect the information that you provide us by consulting our Site, We have implemented various security measures. Your Personal Data is stored on secure networks and is only accessible to a limited number of people who have special access rights and are required to preserve the confidentiality of the data. Please be aware that as soon as you provide Personal Data online, the risk that third parties may intercept and use this data cannot be ruled out. Although We strive to protect the Personal Data of its users, We cannot guarantee the security of any information you disclose online and you do so at your own risk

Child personal information
We do not knowingly collect personal information from users under the age of 13. If We are made aware that We have such personal information, We will promptly take all reasonable steps to delete that information.

Except as set out in this Privacy Policy, We do not sell, trade or transfer your Personal Data to third parties without notifying you in advance and in accordance with applicable Protection of personal data. Your data may be transferred to partner hosting sites and other parties that assist us in designing and operating the Site, providing services (such as awarding of prizes), or to analyze collected data. These parties will only use your data for performing their work as indicated and shall retain the confidentiality of the information.

This includes links to partner sites that may use our logos as part of a co-branding agreement or the sale of our products. The sites to which you have access by the links have their own privacy policy and although We seek to protect the integrity of our Site, We are not responsible and cannot be held responsible for the content and activities of these sites. The pages you view / access to these sites are therefore entirely at your own risk. Please note that these other sites may send their own cookies to users, collect data or solicit Personal Data.

Withdrawal of your details, deleting or modifying Personal Data
You can unsubscribe at any time, and you may decide to remove your details that allow you to receive e-mail and/or SMS. In addition, you may request that all your Personal Data be deleted from our database. If you wish to remove your contact information, you can follow the instructions that explain how to proceed and which appear in the e-mail and/or SMS. You can also remove your contact information, or delete, block, correct, complete or modify your Personal Data. To do this, please consult the registration page or contact us at the address below ("How to contact us").

The terms of this Privacy Policy will govern the use of any information gathered as long as they are applicable. Your use of the Site will constitute your acceptance of all changes made to the Privacy Policy and your consent to be bound to such changes.
