Our Commitment To Children’s Privacy

Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. For that reason, Ask Anna Moseley will never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 18, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under 18. Under our Terms of Service, children under 18 are not allowed to access our service.

This information is purely used to analyze trends, administer the site, track users movement and gather broad demographic information for internal use.
