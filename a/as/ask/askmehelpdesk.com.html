Mission

Ask Me Help Desk's mission is to universally facilitate the sharing of timely, useful advice.

With the expansion of the internet it has become much easier for people with access to it to communicate. Social sites provide real-time sharing of thoughts and experiences. And people can record their knowledge on any number of formats: blogs, wikis, Q&A sites, etc. But despite the proliferation of forums and methods of sharing, timely access to the most relevant expertise remains fragmented. Ask Me Help Desk aims to allow anyone to easily share their most relevant expertise with those looking for it.

Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy at AskMeHelpDesk. We will not collect any personal information from you that you do not volunteer, and we are the sole owner of all information collected on this site. We do not sell, share, or rent this information to others.

Cookies:
Like many other websites, AskMeHelpDesk uses cookies to identify members so they don't have to re-enter a user id and password when they login. This may be disabled by unchecking the "remember me" option when logging in.

Third parties:
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.

Your consent:
By using our Website, you give AskMeHelpDesk permission to collect information about the pages served to you as an anonymous user for the purpose of calculating aggregate site statistics. If you register at AskMeHelpDesk, you authorize AskMeHelpDesk to use information you provide to personalize the information we deliver to you and to use your demographic information when calculating aggregate customer data. AskMeHelpDesk may occasionally amend this policy. We suggest that you bookmark this page and check to see if it has changed from time to time.
