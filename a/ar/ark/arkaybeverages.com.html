Such staff maybe engaged in, the provision of support services. By submitting your personal data, you agree to this transfer, storing or processing. We will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this Policy.

The transmission of information via the Internet is not completely secure. We will do our best to protect your personal data, but cannot guarantee the security of your data transmitted and any transmission is at your own risk. Once your information is received, strict procedures and security features will be employed to endeavor to ensure unauthorized access.
