What is Artsonia?

Artsonia LLC ("Artsonia," "we," "us," and "our") is an online student art museum with Artworks published from thousands of schools worldwide. Thousands of teachers, parents and students use Artsonia every day. Teachers use it to manage school art galleries in a safe and educational manner, providing a positive and safe educational environment for students to showcase their creative works, developing students' pride and self-esteem and involving parents and relatives in student arts education. As a parent, if you create an account on Artsonia, teachers might send you newsletters about school art projects, or you might receive notifications from Artsonia of new Artwork your child has created. Additionally, you will be able to do certain other tasks, such as:

  • View feedback from the art teacher
  • Approve comments left on your child's Artwork
  • Add and edit "fan club" members for your child

Parents can only access the above features and create an account linked to their child's school after receiving either 1) an email invite directly from their child's teacher and using the link provided in that email (which embeds a unique parent code); or 2) through providing a unique parent code during registration.

By using the Classroom Mode feature, students can upload their artwork, photography, voice or videos (collectively referred to as "Artwork"), art titles and statements, view stats about their portfolio, and leave comments for other students. If the student is under 13, they will not be able to utilize Classroom Mode unless either a) Artsonia first obtained parental consent directly from the parent, or b) the teacher has enabled the Classroom Mode feature and certified that he/she has collected the appropriate parental consent (or acted as the agent of the parent and consent on their behalf) prior to allowing the students access to the Classroom Mode feature. Additionally, the first student in the class to use the Classroom Mode feature must enter or scan the unique school access code they receive from their teacher. In addition, prior to utilizing the Classroom Mode feature, the student's teacher must first create a child's screenname by submitting a list of students for his/her classroom to Artsonia (the screenname is then generated by Artsonia). After the unique school access code has been entered, students then select their name from a list of names shown to them once they begin typing in their name. This list of names shown is generated by Artsonia from the list of names provided by the student's teacher to Artsonia and consists of their first name, last initial and assigned screenname. Additionally, at the teacher's option, they may enable a PIN feature which means that the student must also enter the PIN as well as selecting their name from the list shown to them.

None of the Artwork of a student under 13, either submitted by students or teachers, will be shown publicly until Artsonia has also obtained parental consent. In addition, all Artwork will be reviewed by the teacher to determine if there is any personal information on the Artwork, title or artist statement. If the Artwork does contain personal information, the teacher will either remove or mask any student personal information or will indicate that the Artwork submission contains personal information. If the teacher indicates the Artwork contains personal information, then the Artwork will NOT be made public.

More information on how the Service operates is located here and your participation and use of the Service is governed by our Terms of Service ("Terms").
