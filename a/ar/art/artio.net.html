Prozeny.cz website being a Top Performer in 2009

Ostrava, 5.2.2010 - The most popular women's magazine Prozeny.cz, developed and technically maintained by ARTIO, has been a website with the biggest popularity growth in 2009 in the Czech Republic. The original analysis is available at Lupa.cz.
