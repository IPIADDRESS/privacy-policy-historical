How Archeage Database uses Your Personal Information

This policy covers how Archeage Database treats personal information that Archeage Database collects and receives, including information related to your past use of Archeage Database products and services. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable like your name, address, email address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available.



This privacy policy only applies to Archeage Database

This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that Archeage Database does not own or control, or to people that Archeage Database does not employ or manage.





INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE

Archeage Database collects personal information when you register with Archeage Database, when you use Archeage Database products or services.



When you register we ask for information such as your name, email address, birth date, gender, ZIP code, occupation, industry, and personal interests. When you register with Archeage Database and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.



Archeage Database collects information about your transactions with us and with some of our business partners.



Archeage Database automatically receives and records information from your computer and browser, including your IP address, Archeage Database cookie information, software and hardware attributes, and the page you request.



Archeage Database uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.



INFORMATION SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

Archeage Database does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or non-affiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:



We respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process(such as law enforcement requests), or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims.

We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of Archeage Database's terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.

We transfer information about you if Archeage Database is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, Archeage Database will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

Archeage Database displays targeted advertisements based on personal information. Advertisers (including ad serving companies) may assume that people who interact with, view, or click targeted ads meet the targeting criteria—for example, women ages 18-24 from a particular geographic area.



Archeage Database does not provide any personal information to the advertiser when you interact with or view a targeted ad. However, by interacting with or viewing an ad you are consenting to the possibility that the advertiser will make the assumption that you meet the targeting criteria used to display the ad.



COOKIES AND SIMILAR TECHNOLOGIES

Archeage Database may set and access Archeage Database cookies on your computer. We may also set and access device identifiers which could include IP address, user agent information (browser version, OS type and version), and device provided identifiers.



Archeage Database lets other companies that show advertisements on some of our pages set and access their cookies on your computer. Other companies' use of their cookies and device identifiers is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one.Advertisers or other companies do not have access to Archeage Database's cookies.



Your Ability to Edit and Delete Your Account Information and Preferences

You can edit your Archeage Database Account Information at any time. You can also modify information you have provided to Archeage Database through the Archeage Database products or services you may use.



We reserve the right to send you certain communications relating to the Archeage Database service, such as service announcements, administrative messages and the Archeage Database Newsletter, that are considered part of your Archeage Database account, without offering you the opportunity to opt out of receiving them.



If you wish to delete your Archeage Database account, ask us via e-mail or PM on our forum.



CONFIDENTIALITY AND SECURITY

We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs.





CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

Archeage Database may update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your Archeage Database account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.



QUESTION AND SUGGESTIONS

If you have questions, suggestions, or wish to make a complaint you can contact us via PM on our forum.
