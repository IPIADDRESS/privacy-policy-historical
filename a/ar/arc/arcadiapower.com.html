Privacy Statement

At Arcadia Power, we take your privacy seriously. This document explains what kind of information Arcadia Power, Inc. and its subsidiaries and affiliates (collectively, “Arcadia”) collects and stores, including Personally Identifiable Information (i.e., data that can be reasonably linked to a specific individual or household) in connection with Arcadia Power’s software platform services.

We pledge to:

1. Be transparent about the different types of information we collect and how we use them.

2. Ask your permission before sharing your Personally Identifiable Information with third parties for purposes other than to provide Arcadia services, and to do so only when we think they will provide you with a welcome additional service.

3. Use best-in-class data security tools to keep your data safe and protect the Arcadia Power Platform from unauthorized access.

By using this service, you agree to allow us to collect and process information as described below.

What information does Arcadia Power collect?

Arcadia Power collects:

-       Information input during account registration

-       Monthly energy usage

Information input during setup: When you signup for Arcadia Power, you’ll be asked several questions about your utility account to help setup your clean energy plan. These questions include name, address, email, phone, utility account username, password, payment information, and plan type.

Monthly Energy Usage: We collect data from your Poles and Wires utility account on your monthly energy usage at the address given upon signup. With this information Arcadia Power accesses the clean energy market and buy an equal amount of kwh’s in the for of Renewable Energy Credits (REC). Arcadia Power can use energy usage and environmental information to help end user’s with energy efficiency upgrades, tips, and provide services and products to help lower energy use. Arcadia Power will never share private energy usage data with third party providers unless given authorization by end user

If Arcadia Power interfaces to third party products and services, will information be shared?

Over time, Arcadia Power may provide the opportunity for you to interface with one or more third party products and services, which will involve the exchange of information from your account. Your explicit consent and authorization will be required for this interface, and will be revocable by you at any time.

Additionally, when you create a Arcadia Account, we collect and store your email address. From that point forward, your email address is used for communications from Arcadia Power.

Like most Internet sites, we routinely record log entries (including information such as your IP address) and technical information (such as your browser type and version) when your browser or mobile device connects to Arcadiapower.com. These log files are used for troubleshooting and are stored on our cloud servers.

How does Arcadia Power use the information it collects?

We use this information to provide and improve Arcadia Power’s products and services, including information and recommendations about your energy use. We may use your email address to send you this information, or to ask you to participate in surveys about your Arcadia Power use. We use service providers to perform some of these functions. Those service providers are restricted from sharing your information for any other purpose.

We use industry-standard methods to keep this information safe and secure while transmitted over your home network and through the Internet to our cloud servers.

With whom does Arcadia Power share my information?

Under no circumstance do we share Personally Identifiable Information for any commercial or marketing purpose unrelated to the delivery of Arcadia Power’s Clean Energy Products and services without asking you first. Period. We do not rent or sell our customer lists.

Our service technicians and other Arcadia Power employees have supervised access to your information so they can answer your questions if you contact us for help and so they can monitor our servers for technical problems.

Except as described herein, we will only provide Personally Identifiable Information about our users to a third party if required to do so by law, or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with state and federal laws or respond to a court order, subpoena, or search warrant.

We may share your aggregated and anonymous information in a variety of ways, including to publish trends about energy use and conservation, to help utilities provide demand-response services and to generally improve our system. We’ve taken steps to ensure that the information cannot be linked back to you and we require our partners to keep all information in its anonymous form.

Your personal information may be collected, processed and stored by Arcadia Power or its service providers in the United States and other countries where our servers reside. As a result, your personal information may be subject to legal requirements, including lawful requirements to disclose personal information to government authorities, in those jurisdictions.

How long does Arcadia Power save my personal information and how can I delete it?

Arcadia Power stores your Personally Identifiable Information on our cloud servers for as long as you remain an Arcadia Power customer in order to provide you with the service. All information is encrypted and cannot easily be accessed.

If you want to delete your Personally Identifiable Information from Arcadia’s servers, please contact us at privacy@arcadiapower.com. Because of the way we maintain certain services, after your information is deleted, backup copies may linger for some time before they are deleted.

If you have any questions, please contact us at privacy@arcadiapower.com
