Our Privacy Statement

AccessGenealogy is committed to protecting your online privacy. We have developed this page to address the concerns that you may have concerning any data that we receive through the use of cookies, or that you submit through the various online submission forms.

General Statement

There are various methods and ways for you to submit your data to the AccessGenealogy in order to be listed with us. Whether you submit for listing in our registry, origin, or SRC listing's, we request certain data from you, so that we can properly list your information, and that we can contact you later if we have a question about the data you've submitted. None of this data is shared or otherwise distributed beyond the server and the processing computer. The processing of submissions and the creation of the pages are done offline, and are then uploaded. We use every means available to us in order to secure your data.

Choice/Opt-Out

Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us and our partners at the point where we request information about the visitor. This site gives users the following option for removing their information from our database so as not to receive future communications, and no longer receive our service.

You can fill out the form at Comments and state at which services you have placed your information, and would like removed.

Correct/Update

This site gives users the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided.

You can fill out the form at Comments and correct or modify any information in our database.
