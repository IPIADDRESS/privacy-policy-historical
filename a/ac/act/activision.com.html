COOKIES:
Activision uses cookies on its website, www.activision.com. Cookies are a feature of your web browser that allow websites to transfer bits of information to your computer for record-keeping purposes. A cookie stored on your computer can be used to "remember" things like your password, or that you have already registered. This allows us to speed up your future activities here at www.activision.com, saving the time you would normally spend registering or entering your password.

In addition to using cookies to save time, www.activision.com may use cookies, to retrieve user information for promotional and marketing purposes.
