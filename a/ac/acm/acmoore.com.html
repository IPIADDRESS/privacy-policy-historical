A.C. Moore Privacy Statement

This Privacy Statement (“Privacy Statement” or “Statement”) explains information we collect from you and how we use it. We may collect this information online, such as on our website, www.acmoore.com, or via social media, or offline, such as when you visit A.C. Moore stores (collectively, “A.C. Moore Sites”). By using the A.C. Moore Sites or otherwise providing us with your information, you consent to and agree to be bound by this Privacy Statement.

I. Types of Information We Collect

The types of information we collect from you depends on the services and features you use on A.C. Moore Sites, but may include:

  • Name and Contact Information: We may collect your first and last name, email address, postal address, phone number, company, and other similar contact data.
     
  • Demographic Information: We may collect data about you such as your age, gender, city, state, and country.
     
  • Personal Interests and Favorites: We may collect data about goods or services that you are interested in.
     
  • Purchase and Payment Information: We may collect data necessary to process your payment if you make purchases. This data may include your name, email address, phone number, billing information, shipping information, and payment information, including your credit card number and its expiration date, in order to process and track your order. We may use a third party to process your purchase and to handle your payment information. We may also maintain a record of your product purchases.
     
  • Usage Information: We may collect data about how you and your device interact with A.C. Moore Sites and our related services. This could include features you use, the items you purchase, the web pages you visit, and the search terms you enter. This could also include data about your device and the network you use to connect to our services, including IP address, device identifiers, and data about the performance of the services you use on A.C. Moore Sites and any problems you experience with them.
     
  • Location Information: We may collect data about your location. This information may help us provide recommendations to you based on your location, serve you with advertisements or direct you to a product or service that we believe may be of interest to you and that is marketed to people near your location.
     
  • Content You Provide to Us: We collect additional content that you voluntarily provide us, such as content you submit when entering a sweepstakes or promotion, information you send us when you contact us via email, or questions and information you provide us for customer support.

You have choices about the data we collect. When you are asked to provide personal data, you may decline. But if you choose not to provide data that is necessary to provide a service, you may not be able to use some features or services.

II. Information Collection Practices

We collect personal information from a variety of sources, both online and offline. When you visit A.C. Moore Sites, for example, with the help of our third party authorized agents, we may collect your personal information as described below:

A. Passive Collection

We may collect information passively, while you are visiting or interacting with A.C. Moore Sites. We call this “passive” collection since you may not know that this data is being collected when you visit or interact with A.C. Moore Sites. This information may consist of the following items: website visitors’ IP address, IP address-related information, system Media Access Control address, network configuration information, network device information, browser plug-in type. and versions, and operating system.

  • Cookies and Web Beacons: One way that we collect information passively is through cookies or web beacons. Cookies or web beacons serve to improve your experience when visiting A.C. Moore Sites. Cookies are small text files placed on your device that uniquely identifies your device and which a website can transfer to a consumer’s hard drive to keep records of his or her visit at a website. Web beacons are small pieces of data that are embedded in images on the pages of websites. We may use cookies or web beacons to improve your experience on A.C. Moore Sites, such as remembering you when you return to A.C. Moore Sites and what you have saved in your Shopping Bag, anonymously track interest in and collect aggregate information on A.C. Moore Sites, and analyze traffic patterns on A.C. Moore Sites. You may set most browsers to notify you if you receive a cookie, or you may choose to block cookies with your browser, but if you do, you may not be able to take advantage of the personalized features enjoyed by other users to our Sites. Some of the cookies we use may be more persistent in nature. These persistent cookies may not be deleted when your cookies are deleted. Please check your browser and browser settings to determine where these types of cookies are stored and whether and how they may be deleted. In any event, if you reject our cookies, you may still use A.C. Moore Sites, but you may be limited in some of the features. We may also work with service providers that use cookies and web beacons to collect information about your visits to A.C. Moore Sites and to other websites you may visit, and to serve advertisements to you across the Internet based on that information.
     
  • Location Data: We may also collect your location if you choose to allow us to track your location through A.C. Moore Sites or if your personal or non-personal information indicates a certain geographic location. This information may help us provide recommendations to you based on your location, serve you with advertisements or direct you to a product or service that we believe may be of interest to you and that is marketed to people near your location. Finally, in order to help facilitate the delivery of relevant content, we may utilize third party data analytics tools to identify how users engage with A.C. Moore Sites and to provide us with information regarding our users.
     
  • Other Sources: We may collect your personal information from other sources, such as public databases, joint marketing partners, and social media platforms. If you elect to connect your social media account, we may also receive your personal information from social media platforms. The purpose of this data collection is to optimize your experience on A.C. Moore Sites, to ensure we are partnering with third parties that offer products and services that are of interest to you, to facilitate a transaction or fulfill the terms of an agreement, or to ensure that the advertisements offered to you are relevant.

If you sign up to receive offers from us, we will ask you for your email address. When you register to join the A.C. Moore Rewards program, we ask you for your name, A.C. Moore site account information (e-mail address and password), birthdate, address, and related interests.
     
  • Social Media Sites/Applications: If you choose to link a social media account to the A.C. Moore Sites, we may request your permission to access certain personal information on these social media sites, such as your name, profile picture, gender, networks, user ID, list of friends, and other profile information. Depending on the social media site/application, you may have the opportunity to post reviews, questions, answers, and other content.
     
  • Sweepstakes, Contests, and Promotions: If you enter one of our sweepstakes, contests, or other promotions, we may ask you for information such as your name, date of birth, and email address.
     
  • Feedback: If you contact us to give us feedback or ask questions about A.C. Moore Sites or any of our services or products, we may store your communications, including any personal information you include in them, so we can effectively respond to you.

This information includes name, contact information, job preferences, educational history, employment history, personal references, and other hiring-related information.

The above list provides examples of how and when we may collect personally information about you from A.C. Moore Sites. If you do not want us to collect your personally information, please do not provide it to us.
