Aceable Privacy Policy

I. What information does Aceable collect?

Aceable is unique because the information we collect from users goes into state certificates, such information includes names, birthdays, and gender. If you choose to login via Facebook, we will collect your credentials from them. For questions concerning Facebook’s privacy policy, please visit this page.

In order to better tailor our user experience, we also collect information including, but not limited to, the users’ location, type of mobile device, and sometimes their school (since we largely work with teenagers).

We may also collect and store information such as your browser type, IP address, language, operating system, unique device identifier, the date and time of your visit, the pages you view, and the website that referred you to Aceable. The purpose of this practice is to identify which content our users find most useful.

II. What do we use your information for?

As stated above, our collection of information is first and foremost for the purposes of filling out state certificates and verifying compliance with state requirements. All other subsequent collection of information with only be used within the Aceable organization to better user experience. We will not release your information to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

III. Disclosures

We may share your information with certain third parties selected by us to help support our operations. These include, for example, services that help us process payments, analyze web traffic, send emails, and track customer service requests. In addition, we may share limited personal information to third parties that help us market or advertise.

As you browse Aceable, advertising cookies will be placed on your computer so that we can understand what you are interested in.  Our display advertising partner, AdRoll, then enables us to present you with retargeting advertising on other sites based on your previous interaction with [company website]. The techniques our partners employ do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, postal address or telephone number.  You can visit this page to opt out of AdRoll and their partners’ targeted advertising.

We may disclose your information when we believe we have your consent to do so, such as when you contact customer service and ask us about your account, or when we have the consent of someone we believe is authorized to consent on behalf of you, such as the individual associated with the payment method for your account.

We may also disclose your information when we have a good faith belief we are required to do so by law, or in response to a subpoena, court order, or other legal mechanism.

IV. Policy changes

We reserve the right to change our policy for any reason necessary, but we will never do so without notifying you in advance. We strive to protect your information and keep you in the loop at all times. We will make necessary adjustments in order do so. It’s the Aceable thing to do.

V. Modification

You may modify your personal information within the Aceable app. Should you wish to delete your Aceable account, you may contact ace@aceable.com. A member of the Aceable team will be happy to help you.

VI. Opt-Out

Should you wish to opt out of all communications from Aceable, you may unsubscribe from our emails at any time. In the alternative, you may also delete the Aceable app, which will remove you from all future correspondence. We’ll shed a tear, but respect your wishes.

VII. Security

While no method is absolutely secure, we can assure you we take every measure necessary to keep your information safe.

VIII. Contact

We want to hear from you! with any questions concerning any of our policies.
