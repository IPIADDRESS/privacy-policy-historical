Privacy Information...

Our Third-Party Ads Use Visit Information From Your Browser

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.


Password Protection

When you provide Amen Me! your password, you can rest assured that it is safe. As soon as you send it to us it is immediately scrambled. It is stored in the scrambled state so that even our administrators cannot read it. For this reason, we cannot send your password to you when you forget it. Instead, we can send you a password we generate which we scramble and forget. You can also log on using that new password and immediately change it to something else that we didn't generate.

Each password gets scrambled using an irreversable scramble. When you log in, the password you log in with is also scrambled before it is compared the the one we have on our database.

We have also taken precautions to make sure that if the database is ever stolen, that common passwords couldn't be matched as well.

When you come to Amen Me! we want you to rest assured that you can enjoy it without worrying about your security.


Email Addresses

Your email address is never shared with anyone for any reason. We may occasionally send you notifications that we believe are important regarding the service itself. We do not send you anything else unless you specifically request to have it sent to you by signing up for it.


Usage Information

We do keep and analyze usage information in order to provide you with the most optimal service we can. Without this kind of information, Amen Me! wouldn't work because we wouldn't be able to count your Amens. This is also how we determine what you have seen and haven't yet seen so that the Crossroads works for you, among other features.


Personal Information

Amen Me! doesn't keep any personal information other than your email address and your scrambled password. One major reason we can do this is because we use PayPal when we deal with money. This allows you to keep your personal information with PayPal and makes our job easier as well. This way only you and PayPal need to see that information. We believe that this is a great way to maintain your privacy, but it does require that you sign up with PayPal in order to get rewards for your submissions.
