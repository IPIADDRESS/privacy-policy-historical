Password Protection


When you provide Amen Me! your password, you can rest assured that it is safe. As soon as you send it to us it is immediately scrambled. It is stored in the scrambled state so that even our administrators cannot read it. For this reason, we cannot send your password to you when you forget it. Instead, we can send you a password we generate which we scramble and forget. You can also log on using that new password and immediately change it to something else that we didn't generate.

Each password gets scrambled using an irreversible scramble. When you log in, the password you log in with is also scrambled before it is compared the one we have on our database.

We have also taken precautions to make sure that if the database is ever stolen, that common passwords couldn't be matched as well.

When you come to Amen Me! we want you to rest assured that you can enjoy it without worrying about your security.
