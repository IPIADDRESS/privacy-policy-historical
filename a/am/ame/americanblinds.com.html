Cookies
Cookies are a technology that can be used to provide you with tailored information from a Web site. A cookie is an element of data that a Web site can send to your browser, which may then store it on your system. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it.
Our site makes use of cookies. Cookies are used for the following purposes:

Security Practices
We have appropriate physical, electronic and procedural security safeguards in place to protect and secure the information we collect.

In addition, our web site uses Secure Sockets Layering (SSL) to encrypt your personal credit information, including your credit card number, before it travels over the Internet. SSL is the industry standard for secure online transactions. By using SSL, we make placing an order online at our web site just as safe as giving your credit card number over the phone

Safe Shopping Guarantee
We guarantee that every transaction you make at americanblinds.com will be safe and secure. In the unlikely event that unauthorized charges are made to your card as a result of shopping at americanblinds.com, you'll pay nothing.

Under the Fair Credit Billing Act, your bank account cannot hold you liable for more than $50.00 of fraudulent charges. If your bank does hold you liable for any of this $50.00, americanblinds.com will cover the entire liability, up to the full $50.00. americanblinds.com will cover this liability only if the unauthorized use of your credit card resulted through no fault of your own from purchases made at americanblinds.com while using our secure server.

In the event of unauthorized use of your credit card, you must notify your credit card provider in accordance with its reporting rules and procedures. Any material changes to the privacy policy will be posted on the web site and communicated via e-mail.
