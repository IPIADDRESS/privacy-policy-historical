When you visit this Website, the American Express Network or our Vendors collect information, including PII, that you provide, such as during your registration as a user or your purchase of a product or service. We automatically collect your "IP address" used to connect your computer to the Internet, browser type and version, operating system, and computer platform. We or our Vendors also use "Cookies" or similar technologies, sometimes in combination with "Web Beacons," to collect some statistical data when you receive our e-mails or browse this Website, such as e-mail response rates, average time spent on this Website, pages viewed, information searched for, access times, "Clickstream Data," and other relevant information about your online experience. If you prefer not to accept Cookies, you may adjust your browser settings to notify you when a Cookie is about to be sent, or you may configure your browser to refuse Cookies automatically. However, if you turn off Cookies, or refuse to accept a request to place a Cookie, some features and services on this Website may not function properly and the advertising you receive when you visit this Website may not be as relevant to you or tailored to your interests.
