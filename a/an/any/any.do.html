In brief:

Any.do is a collection of productivity applications that helps you organize your everyday life and get things done. We have implemented this Privacy Policy because your privacy, and the privacy of other Service users, is important to us.

There are two types of information collected by us: Personal Information and Non-Personal Information (each, as defined below). This Privacy Policy explains our online information practices, the way we use the Personal and Non Personal Information we collect from our Service users and the choices you can make about the way your Personal Information is collected and used in connection with the Service.

"Personal Information" means any information that may be used, either alone or in combination with other information, to personally identify an individual, including, but not limited to: name, user name, password (and other login information), email address and contacts, Facebook® friends, home or other physical address, or other contact information. “Non-Personal Information” is information that does not indentify you as an individual, such as your browser type, IP address, the URL of the previous website you visited, the activity of a user of the Service, a user's 'click-stream' and scrolls on the Service, mobile device ID, date and time stamps of use of the Service and third party applications, email addresses in hashed form (i.e. a user’s email corresponds to a series of characters that does not permit the user’s identification) etc; although some opinions (such as the European Union) would consider IP address as Personal Information.
