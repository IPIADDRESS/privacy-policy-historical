Non-Personal Information is not typically used to personally identify you.

The information collected by the technology (including your IP address) will be disclosed to these service providers, who use the information to evaluate your use of the Site.

We also may use this information to target custom content and ads to you on this and other websites.

These networks track your online activities over time by collecting information through automated means, including through the use of the technologies described in this section, and they use this information to show you advertisements that are tailored to your individual interests.
