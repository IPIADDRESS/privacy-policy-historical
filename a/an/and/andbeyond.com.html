4. How do we process Personal Information?

Information may be collected either through or by electronic means, which includes, without limitation, the use of our Websites, electronic mail (e-mail), text, voice, sound or image messages, or through non-electronic means which includes, without limitation, the capturing of Personal Information on hard copy documents.

When using our Websites your Personal Information may be collected either actively where you or your appointed agent provide us with certain Personal Information or inactively where we collect information from you that you have not actively provided. See below for a more detailed discussion.

a) Active Information Collection:

Active information collection refers to instances where we obtain Information from you where you have directly provided us with Information, such as when you submit an enquiry in respect of a specific product or service, or when you subscribe to our newsletter. When submitting an enquiry through our Websites you will be required to provide us with your first name, last name, e-mail address, country of residence, as well as your preferred dates of travel, which Information will be processed by us to create an itinerary that suits your particular needs. Upon your departure from our Lodges we may request that you provide us with your views and / or opinion in the form of a questionnaire, such information being required to assist us to not only improve our products and services to our guests, but also ensure that our guests continue to experience products and services that are of exceptional value. We may also use your information to provide you with electronic updates, alerts, and other information regarding our products and services.

b) Inactive Information Collection:

Inactive or passive Information collection refers to instances where we collect Information from you which you have not actively provided as contemplated above. To this end we may use technologies to passively collect information while you are visiting, accessing or using our Websites. These technologies allow us to collect certain kinds of Information which cannot be linked to you as a person (i.e. non-personally identifiable information), such as your internet domain name, the type of web browser you use, the operating system you use, the date and time of your visit to our Websites, the specific pages you may have visited, and the address of the website which you have visited prior to entering our Websites. Kindly note that we do not link this passively collected non-personally identifiable information with personally identifiable information you have actively provided elsewhere on any of our Websites or at our Lodges. We reserve the right to share with third parties, including our business partners and advertisers, any anonymous aggregated non-personal information which we may have passively collected via our Websites.

We may from time to time use the following technologies (without limitation) to passively or inactively collect information through our Websites, namely:

(i) Cookies:

Our Websites use cookies, which are small text files that store non-personally identifiable information about you, either temporarily in connection with your Internet Protocol (IP) address (known as a temporary or session cookie, and deleted once you close your browser window) or more permanently on the hard drive of your computer (known as a permanent or persistent cookie). Our Websites use session cookies so that you do not have to fill in the same information from page to page within our Websites. If you elect not to receive cookies, you may be able to view some, but not all, of the content on our Websites.

(ii) Web Beacons:

We may include small graphic images called web beacons, also known as “Internet tags” or “clear gifs,” in our web pages and e-mail messages. Web beacons may be invisible to you, but any electronic image inserted into a web page or e-mail can act as a web beacon. We may use web beacons or similar technologies for a number of purposes, including, without limitation, to count the number of visitors to our Websites, to monitor how users navigate the Websites, to count how many e-mails that we sent were actually opened or to count how many particular articles or links were actually viewed.

(iii) Embedded Scripts:

An embedded script is programming code that is designed to collect information about your interactions with the relevant Websites. It is temporarily downloaded onto your computer from our web server or a third party we may work with, is active only while you are connected to the relevant Websites, and is deleted or deactivated thereafter.

(iv) Mobile Device Identifiers:

Certain mobile service providers uniquely identify mobile devices and we or our third-party service providers may receive such device information if you access our Websites through mobile devices. Certain features of the relevant Websites may require collection of mobile phone numbers and we may associate that phone number with the mobile device identifiers. Additionally, some mobile phone service providers operate systems that pinpoint the physical location of devices that use their service. Depending on the provider, we and our third-party service providers may receive this information. If we associate any such passively-collected information with your Personal Information, we will treat the combined information as Personal Information as contemplated in this Policy.
