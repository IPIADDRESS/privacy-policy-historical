Cookies

A cookie is a very small text file placed on your system upon logging on to AnimeFushigi (and most other websites). This file serves as your identification card and is uniquely yours, and can only be read by the server that gave it to you. Cookies tell us that you have returned to a specific web page on AnimeFushigi and help us track your preferences and transactional habits. The basic function of cookies is to help our server remember who you are.
