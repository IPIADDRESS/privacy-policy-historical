ANI-VIEW PRIVACY POLICY

Last Revised: October 22, 2014

Ani-View Ltd. (“Ani-View“, “us” or “we“) respects the privacy of the users of its hosted advertising management platform that provides targeted advertisement insertion and scheduling, campaign management and reporting on a real-time basis primarily used in the monetization of mobile web pages and mobile applications (the “Services“). We believe that you have a right to know our privacy practices regarding the information we may collect and use when you use our Services. This privacy policy (“Privacy Policy“) explains such practices.

Chapter A of this Privacy Policy describes our privacy practices with respect to information we collect about our customers (including their representatives and business contacts) (“Customers“). Chapter B of this Privacy Policy describes our privacy practices with respect to information collected about end-users of our Customers’ websites, mobile web-pages, mobile applications and other Internet-accessible destination which use our Service to display ads on such network sites (“Network Sites“). The general provisions in Chapter C of this Privacy Policy apply to both Chapter A and Chapter B.

As used in this Privacy Policy, “Non-personal Information” is non-identifiable and anonymous information. Non-Personal Information is any unconcealed information which does not enable identification of an individual and may consists of technical information, behavioral information and aggregated information. “Personal Information” is individually identifiable information which may identify an individual or may be of a private and/or sensitive nature. For avoidance of doubt, any Non-personal Information connected or linked to or associated with any Personal Information shall be deemed as Personal Information as long as such connection, linkage or association exists

As used in this Chapter A, “you” refer to our Customers.

6. Cookies

 

When you use our Service, we and/or our third party service providers may use industry-wide technologies such as “cookies” and local storage (or other similar technologies), which store certain local information on your device (“Cookies“) which may enable us, inter alia, to recognize your login information and personalized settings. Such information is locally stored in the Customer’s device. Ani-View and/or our authorized third party service providers may access such information. Ani-View and/or our authorized third party service providers may use both session cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and persistent cookies (which stay on your device until you delete them). We also use Cookies and other navigational data that is passively collected, to gather information about your visit to the Services and the information which you searched and viewed on the Services. Such Cookies used by the Service may store non-personal information as well as user persistent identifier and geo-location data, which will be collected in accordance with the terms specified herein. It is easy to prohibit and/or delete the Cookies, inter alia, through the Settings option of your device. Most platforms will allow you to erase cookies from a device, block acceptance of cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored. In order to erase or disable the Cookies option you may use the settings option of your device or according to the specific instructions provided by the third party service provider’s privacy policy and terms of use. However, if you block or erase cookies, or change the settings of your device, your experience may be affected and may be limited. Please note we have access to your mobile devices’ local storage and may store information locally.

9. What is our Service?

 

Ani-View, directly and/or through its third party service providers, provides ads to its Customer’s Network Sites and provides technology for mobile website and mobile application publishers and advertisers to display or to stream ads. If our Customer’s Network Site is using our Service and technology to display ads on its site, the web page will contain coding that directs your browser to fill the ad space on the web page with content from one of our ad servers. Our platform for streaming video ads enables advertisers to target their campaigns against particular contextual, geographic, interest or demographic criteria associated with the website or browser, and bid an amount they are willing to pay for this targeted impression. Each time a Network Site queries our platform for an advertisement, we serve the most valuable advertisement to the publisher’s ad slot. Please note that Ani-View does not control the privacy policies of the Network Sites, publishers and advertisers who use our Service.
