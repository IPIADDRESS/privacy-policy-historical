Surveys

Recent Reviews
RIN-NE GN 8
If you're a Takahashi fan, chances are you're still enjoying Rin-ne. It isn't anything new or groundbreaking, but it is still fun in the way that most of her longer series are. Those who got fed up with Ranma ½ or Inu Yasha for dragging on, however, will be nearing the end of their patience.
Starry Sky GN 1
Starry Sky starts off slow and while it does become more enjoyable, it never really achieves as much as other reverse harem tales. Perhaps the best way to enjoy this book is to simply turn off your brain for a bit and let Tsukiko's slightly insipid memories carry you away.
Cage of Eden GN 4 & 5
Cage of Eden is still an exciting, engaging story of the struggle to maintain some sort of society in a harsh, unfamiliar environment. Volume five slips up a bit in our ability to buy into the story and Yamada's dim view of adults can be distracting, but with reduced fanservice and Rion's increasing competence, this remains a great escape into adventure for a few hours.
Dirty Pair: Features DVD Collection DVD
All in all, this is a very nice collection. It is equally as enjoyable for those who are coming from the Dirty Pair TV and OAV releases and for those who are curious but don't want to invest in the longer shows. While all three employ different methods to tell their stories, Kei and Yuri remain engaging heroines and even when the story isn't as good as it could be, it is always entertaining.
Lollipop Chainsaw
Is Lollipop Chainsaw worth the effort? Sometimes, it is. It is fun when, eventually, it gets its ass into gear. And it is endearing, when it isn't actively working towards ruining its main character.
Puella Magi Madoka Magica Vol. 3 Blu-ray
It's difficult to have any reaction to this other than a standing ovation. Warning: spoilers abound!
Jormungand Episodes 1-7 Streaming
Like its crew of gunrunning mercenaries, Jormungand is fast, vicious and amoral—perfect for a night out with your mommy.
Eureka Seven AO Episodes 1-6 Streaming
As a piece of sci-fi eye candy, this show is clearly a worthy successor to Eureka Seven. It's just the actual story that still needs time to prove itself.
Zetman Episodes 1-6 Streaming
It is rarely a good sign when someone who has not read the manga can tell that the anime adaptation is lacking.
Is This a Zombie? of the Dead Episodes 1-6 Streaming
If the end of season one suffered from too much plot and not enough humor, the beginning of season two has the opposite problem: too much demented guffawing, not enough demented plotting.
