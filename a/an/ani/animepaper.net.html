Twist by °Nysha 3 weeks 3 days ago

^ShiNN
There was a time, years ago, when grunge wallpapers used to be your everyday fix at many communities, AP included. Then vectors, the clean ones, started getting the spotlight and the grunge "movement" slowly started to fade. Now that vectoring has become an everyday fix as well, people have started to experiment with them.

But it's been a while since I've seen someone actually trying to portray a grunge wallpaper by also including vectoring techniques in the process as well. Nysha's wallpaper is a fine one which manages to combine both styles and gives a "retro" feelings reminiscent of the days when this style used to be very common and appreciated among wallpaper makers. Moreover, the style perfectly fits the theme of the show and of its character as well, with its perfect choice of colors and the right balance between them.

So there you have it, a fine piece of grunge art. If you feel like discovering this genre (or re-discovering it), give it a look, and you might as well check out the awesome show that Kara no Kyoukai is!
