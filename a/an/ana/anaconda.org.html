PRIVACY POLICY

Anaconda Cloud (“Anaconda”) is committed to protecting the privacy of your information. We provide this Privacy Policy to inform users of our policies and procedures regarding the collection, use and disclosure of personally identifiable information (“PII”) received from users of our website (the “Website”) collected via the Website, email, SMS, telephone - or other means. This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason, at our sole discretion. We will notify you of any material changes to our Privacy Policy by posting the new Privacy Policy on our Website. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes. By using or accessing the Website, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy, and you are consenting to our processing of your information as set forth in this Privacy Policy now and as amended by us.

If you have any questions or comments about this Privacy Policy or our use of your PII, please contact us.

  • Website Covered.

This Privacy Policy covers the information practices of https://anaconda.org and all of its respective sub-domains.

  • Information Collected.

Anaconda offers a variety of services that are collectively referred to as the “Services.” Anaconda collects information from individuals who visit Anaconda’s Website (“Visitors”) and individuals who register to use the Services (“Customers”). When expressing an interest in obtaining additional information about the Services or registering to use the Services, Anaconda may require you to provide Anaconda with personal contact information, such as name, company name, address, phone number, and email address (“Required Contact Information”). When purchasing the Services, Anaconda may require you to provide Anaconda with financial qualification and billing information, such as billing name and address, credit card number, and the number of employees within the organization that will be using the Services (“Billing Information”). Anaconda may also ask you to provide additional information, such as company annual revenues, number of employees, or industry (“Optional Information”). Required Contact Information, Billing Information, and Optional Information are referred to collectively as “Data About Anaconda Customers.” As you navigate Anaconda’s Website, Anaconda may also collect information through the use of commonly-used information-gathering tools, such as cookies and Web beacons (“Website Navigational Information”). Website Navigational Information includes standard information from your Web browser (such as browser type and browser language), your Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, and the actions you take on Anaconda’s Website (such as the Web pages viewed and the links clicked). By choosing to provide any information to Anaconda, you are giving Anaconda permission to use and store such information consistent with this Privacy Policy. Anaconda also collects and uses personal data regarding its employees (“Employee Data”), which may include: name, address, job title and other job information, location, compensation information, identification number, employment history, and copy of employment agreement.

  • Use of Information Collected.

Anaconda uses Data About Anaconda Customers to perform the services requested. Anaconda may also use Data About Anaconda Customers for marketing purposes. For example, Anaconda may use information you provide to contact you to further discuss your interest in the Services and to send you information regarding Anaconda and its partners, such as information about promotions or events. Anaconda uses credit card information solely to check the financial qualifications of prospective Customers and to collect payment for the Services. Anaconda uses Website Navigational Information to operate and improve Anaconda’s Website. Anaconda may also use Website Navigational Information alone or in combination with Data About Anaconda Customers to provide personalized information about Anaconda. Employee Data is only used for the for the purposes of facilitating the performance of certain administrative tasks and functions relating to general employment and for processing and investigating reports under Anaconda’s codes and policies (“Employee Data Purposes”).

  • Website Navigational Information.

Anaconda may use commonly-used information-gathering tools, such as cookies and Web beacons, to collect information as you navigate Anaconda’s Website (“Website Navigational Information”). This section describes the types of Website Navigational Information that may be collected on Anaconda’s Website and how this information may be used.

  • Cookies. Anaconda may use cookies to make interactions with Anaconda’s Website easy and meaningful. When you visit Anaconda’s Website, Anaconda’s servers send a cookie to your computer. Standing alone, cookies do not personally identify you. They merely recognize your Web browser. Unless you choose to identify yourself to Anaconda, either by responding to a promotional offer, opening an account, or filling out a Web form, you remain anonymous to Anaconda. Anaconda uses cookies that are session-based and persistent-based. Session cookies exist only during one session. They disappear from your computer when you close your browser software or turn off your computer. Persistent cookies remain on your computer after you close your browser or turn off your computer. If you have chosen to identify yourself to Anaconda, Anaconda uses session cookies containing encrypted information to allow Anaconda to uniquely identify you. Each time you log into the Services, a session cookie containing an encrypted, unique identifier that is tied to your account is placed your browser. These session cookies allow Anaconda to uniquely identify you when you are logged into the Services and to process your online transactions and requests. Session cookies are required to use the Services. Anaconda may use persistent cookies that only Anaconda can read and use to identify browsers that have previously visited Anaconda’s Website. When you purchase the Services or provide Anaconda with personal information, a unique identifier is assigned to you. This unique identifier is associated with a persistent cookie that Anaconda places on your Web browser. Anaconda is especially careful about the security and confidentiality of the information stored in persistent cookies. For example, Anaconda does not store account numbers or passwords in persistent cookies. You may refuse to accept cookies by activating the setting on your browser which allows you to refuse the setting of cookies. If you disable your Web browser’s ability to accept cookies, you will be able to navigate Anaconda’s Website, but you will not be able to successfully use all the Services. Unless you have adjusted your browser setting so that it will refuse cookies, our system will issue cookies when you log on to our site. Anaconda may use information from session and persistent cookies in combination with Data About Anaconda Customers to provide you with information about Anaconda and the Services.
  • Web Beacons. Anaconda may use Web beacons alone or in conjunction with cookies to compile information about Customers and Visitors’ usage of Anaconda’s Website and interaction with emails from Anaconda. Web beacons are clear electronic images that can recognize certain types of information on your computer, such as cookies, when you viewed a particular Website tied to the Web beacon, and a description of a Website tied to the Web beacon. For example, Anaconda may place Web beacons in marketing emails that notify Anaconda when you click on a link in the email that directs you to one of Anaconda’s Website. Anaconda uses Web beacons to operate and improve Anaconda’s Website and email communications. Anaconda may use information from Web beacons in combination with Data About Anaconda Customers to provide you with information about Anaconda and the Services.
  • Flash Cookies. Anaconda may use local shared objects, also known as Flash cookies, to store your preferences or display content based upon what you view on our site to personalize your visit. Third parties, with whom Anaconda partners to provide certain features on our site or to display advertising based upon your Web browsing activity, use Flash cookies to collect and store information. Flash cookies are different from browser cookies because of the amount of, type of, and how data is stored. Cookie management tools provided by your browser will not remove Flash cookies.
  • IP Addresses. When you visit Anaconda’s Website, Anaconda may collect your Internet Protocol (“IP”) addresses to track and aggregate non-personal information. For example, Anaconda may use IP addresses to monitor the regions from which Customers and Visitors navigate Anaconda’s Website.
  • Third Party Cookies. From time-to-time, Anaconda may engage third parties to track and analyze usage and volume statistical information from individuals who visit Anaconda’s Website. Anaconda may also use other third-party cookies to track the performance of Company advertisements. The information provided to third parties does not include personal information, but this information may be re-associated with personal information after Anaconda receives it. Anaconda may also contract with third-party advertising networks that collect IP addresses and other Website Navigational Information on Anaconda’s Website and emails and on third-party Websites. Ad networks follow your online activities over time by collecting Website Navigational Information through automated means, including through the use of cookies. They use this information to provide advertisements about products and services tailored to your interests. You may see these advertisements on other Websites. This process also helps us manage and track the effectiveness of our marketing efforts.
  • Public Forums, Refer a Friend, and Customer Testimonials.

Anaconda may provide bulletin boards, blogs, or chat rooms on Anaconda’s Website. Any personal information you choose to submit in such a forum may be read, collected, or used by others who visit these forums, and may be used to send you unsolicited messages. Anaconda is not responsible for the personal information you choose to submit in these forums. Customers and Visitors may elect to use Anaconda’s referral program to inform friends about Anaconda’s Website. When using the referral program, Anaconda requests the friend’s name and email address. Anaconda will automatically send the friend a one-time email inviting him or her to visit Anaconda’s Website. Anaconda may store this information. Anaconda may post a list of Customers and testimonials on Anaconda’s Website that contain information such as Customer names and titles. Anaconda obtains the consent of each Customer prior to posting any information on such a list or posting testimonials.

  • Sharing of Information Collected.

Anaconda may share Data About Anaconda Customers with Anaconda’s service providers so that these service providers can contact Customers and Visitors who have provided contact information on our behalf. Anaconda may also share Data About Anaconda Customers with Anaconda’s service providers to ensure the quality of information provided. Unless described in this Privacy Policy, Anaconda does not share, sell, rent, or trade any information provided with third parties for their promotional purposes. From time to time, Anaconda may partner with other companies to jointly offer products or services. If you purchase or specifically express interest in a jointly-offered product or service from Anaconda, Anaconda may share Data About Anaconda Customers collected in connection with your purchase or expression of interest with our joint promotion partner(s), once Anaconda has notified you of the information to be shared and obtained your consent to the sharing of the information. Anaconda does not control our business partners’ use of the Data About Anaconda Customers we collect, and their use of the information will be in accordance with their own privacy policies. Anaconda may use third-party service providers to manage credit card processing. Such service providers will not be permitted to store, retain, or use Billing Information except for the sole purpose of credit card processing on Anaconda’s behalf. Anaconda reserves the right to use or disclose information provided if required by law or if Anaconda reasonably believes that use or disclosure is necessary to protect Anaconda’s rights and/or to comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process. Employee Data is only disclosed to third parties for the Employee Data Purposes. In no case does Anaconda disclose to third parties Employee Data for any incompatible purpose without first notifying the data subject and providing the data subject with an opportunity to affirmatively opt-out or object to such use or disclosure.
