privacy policy

Effective November 9, 2015

THIS PRIVACY POLICY GOVERNS YOUR USE OF THE WEBSITES AND MOBILE SOFTWARE APPLICATIONS (COLLECTIVELY, THE "SITE") OFFERED BY ANKI, INC. AND/OR ITS SUBSIDIARIES AND AFFILIATES (REFERRED TO AS "ANKI", "WE", "OUR", OR "US").

For our policies regarding children users, please click here

Anki, Inc. ("Anki," "we," or "us") continually strives to improve the experience of using our products and this is the primary goal in collecting information from our customers and web and mobile application users ("you"). This policy describes how we collect, maintain, protect, use, and disclose information we receive from you when you use our website(s) (the "Website(s)" or "Site(s)") www.anki.com, the Anki DRIVE or Anki OVERDRIVE mobile applications (“the Apps") and via email to us. This Privacy Policy does not apply to information collected by us offline or through any other means; or by any third party, including through any application or content (including advertising) that may link to or be accessible from or on the Website.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. We will notify you of any changes to our Privacy Policy by posting the new Privacy Policy here or, if the changes are material with regard to information collected from you or your child, by posting a prominent notice on the Website or mobile application or by sending an email to the email address you provide to us prior to the change becoming effective. If required, we may also seek your prior consent or the consent of a parent or legal guardian. You should consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes.

Types of Information We Gather from You And How We Gather Information

Information Collection

We collect several types of information from and about users of our Website and Apps, including:

Personal Information

Personal information is information about you that can be used by itself, or combined with other information, to personally identify you. We collect this information directly from you when you provide it to us.

When you purchase an Anki product from Anki.com, you provide us with your name, email, phone number, shipping and billing addresses, credit card information, and other information you affirmatively provide. When you register to receive Anki news we collect age and email address. When you sign up for an Anki account (an "Account" or a "Connected Profile"), we will collect your Driver Name, email address, birthdate, and password.

If you choose to purchase Anki DRIVE or Anki OVERDRIVE as a gift and send to a third party, we will ask you for friend or family member's name and shipping information. We do not use the shipping information for any secondary purpose.

We may also collect your Internet Protocol ('IP') address. We use your IP address to diagnose problems with our servers, software, to administer our Services and to gather demographic information.

We do not collect more personal information than is reasonably necessary to participate in an activity on the Website or Apps or enhance your gameplay experience.

Specific to the Anki DRIVE and Anki OVERDRIVE Apps

When you use the Apps we collect your device make and model, randomized device ID, vehicle ID, operating system version, and language your device is set to. In addition, we collect app and gameplay data to help improve our products and services. This information may include, but is not limited to, game scores, game achievements, game performance, and your usage of various game features. All information collected by Anki automatically or passively (such as information collected prior to when you create an Account) is intended to be anonymous information that does not disclose your identity or constitute personal information. It will automatically be transmitted to Anki and used as described herein. The information collected by Anki may be posted in an anonymous and aggregated format by Anki on publicly-accessible web sites such as Facebook, shared with hardware manufacturers, shared with platform hosts, shared with Anki's marketing partners or used by Anki for any other lawful purpose.

We use mobile analytics software to allow us to better understand the functionality of our Application on your mobile device. This software may record information such as how often you use the Apps, the events that occur within the Apps, aggregated usage, and performance data. We do not link the information we store within the analytics software to any personally identifiable information you submit within the Apps without your explicit permission or the permission of a parent or legal guardian.

We do obtain non-personally identifiable zip-code level geographic information using devices' IP addresses.

If you choose to use our results sharing feature, the Apps will provide you with options to share your race results via text messaging, email, or social applications you have on your mobile device.

You will have the ability to save images to your device in our sharing of results feature. If you wish to do so, you will receive a notification indicating that the application would like to access your photos which you may allow or deny. We do not access other photos, that you may have saved to your device, we only allow you to save the image you create within our mobile application to your device.

We send push notifications to provide you with alerts and information. You may opt-out of push notifications at the device level.

Our Apps request permission to use Bluetooth on your device. The Apps use Bluetooth to communicate with Anki vehicles and other in-game devices, during gameplay. You can turn on / off Bluetooth at the device level.

Automatically as you navigate through the site: usage Details Cookies, Cookies and Other Technologies

When you visit our Website, certain information is or can be automatically recorded. Generally, we use this information in an aggregated and anonymized way. If we need to combine this type of information with personal information, we treat it as such. Automatically collected information includes:

  • Statistical or technical information such as, for example, your computer's Internet Protocol address, operating system and browser type, time and date, and how you navigate through and interact with the Website.
  • Information collected via "cookies" “tags”, “scripts” or "web beacons," which are small data files that we transfer to your computer for record keeping purposes. We primarily use cookies for login session management. Anki does not collect personal information from you on our Site via cookies, tags, scripts or web beacons, however, we may combine the information collected through cookies with other personal information you provide us (for example, we may link cookie information to your account information so we can remember what you’ve stored in your shopping cart). You can change the options on your browser to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from a website. If you do not accept cookies, however, you may not be able to use all portions or functionality of our Website.
Accounts

You can sign up for a Connected Profile to backup stats, track progress on multiple devices, and reserve a unique Driver Name. If you sign up for an Anki Account via the Apps or Website, we will collect your Driver Name, email address, birthdate, and password.

Once you sign up for an Account (and, in the case of children users, a parent authorizes the account), the information we store within the analytics software is connected to your Driver name and email address in your Account. We may contact you from time to time via email based on your gameplay data.

You may modify your Account information or request deletion of your Account by emailing us at support@anki.com.

From Third Parties, such as our business partners: Advertiser Use of Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies.

When you visit our website, we use third parties to collect anonymized and aggregated demographic and Website use information such as, for example, type of browser and operating system, location, referring website, Internet service provider (or, in the case of mobile devices, mobile carrier, UDID), and type of device.

We may partner with a third party ad networks to manage our advertising on other sites and retarget visitors to our site. Our ad network partners use cookies and Web beacons to collect non-personally identifiable information about your activities on this and other Web sites to provide you targeted advertising based upon your interests. If you wish to not have this information used for the purpose of serving you targeted ads, you may opt-out by clicking here, or by visiting our list of 3rd Party Operators here. Please note this does not opt you out of being served advertising. You will continue to receive generic ads. We do not engage in retargeted advertising for children users of our Site or Apps.

We use Google Analytics with anonymized IP. Google Analytics is a web analysis service provided by Google Inc. (“Google”). We use Google Analytics to track and examine the use of our Apps. This integration of Google Analytics anonymizes your IP address. It works by shortening all Users' IP addresses, including those within Member States of the European Union or in other Contracting States to the Agreement on the European Economic Area.

We use Local Storage, also referred to as HTML5, to store content information and preferences. Third parties with whom we partner to provide certain features on our website or to display advertising based upon your web browsing activity also use HTML5 to collect and store information. Various browsers may offer their own management tools for removing HTML5.

Emails and Other Communications and Your Choices

From time to time, we send education, marketing, product support, and research emails. Our marketing emails relate to our product and services offerings: software, hardware products, promotions, news, and events. Our emails may contain "beacons," which are small images that inform us about recipients' receptiveness to our emails. Our third-party mail service provider also provides us with anonymized and aggregated demographic information about the respondents to our emails. In our emails, we may also invite you to participate in online surveys or give us product feedback online. Generally, we use information from our marketing and research communications in an aggregated and anonymized way. When our business needs require, we combine it with your personal information, and in such cases we treat it as personal information.

You can unsubscribe from our marketing and research emails by following the instructions included in them.

We may also communicate with you about your order or Account, or to address your questions. We may also ask you to participate in surveys. These surveys are purely optional and help us improve our products and provide aggregate information, which we think may interest our registered users and visitors. We may use the information that you voluntarily provide as part of your survey response in the manner outlined in this Privacy Policy. In addition, if you choose to participate in an Anki survey, any information you provide to us, including personal information, will be linked with your Account in our system and to any personal information contained therein. We may also use the information you provide in the aggregate to help us generate statistics regarding use of our Website and the Apps. If you would not like your information to be used in this way, please do not participate in Anki surveys or, if participating, please provide us only with information you consent to our use of in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Social Media

We have a presence on various social media platforms, such as Facebook, Twitter, and YouTube. We receive from them anonymized and aggregated demographic and use information related to our presence on the respective social media platform when you choose to visit us on those sites or to communicate with us.

From time to time, we may conduct contests and sweepstakes on our social media channels or in cooperation with other partners. If you choose to participate in such contests and sweepstakes, we may collect information from you, as described in the terms of the respective contest or sweepstake.

In addition, we may from time to time, offer social sharing features and other integrated tools (such as the Facebook "Like" button), which let you share actions you take on our Website and the Apps with other media, and vice versa. The use of such features may enable the sharing of information with your friends or the public, depending on the settings you establish with the third party that provides these features. These features may also collect your IP address, which page you are visiting and may set a cookie to enable the features to function properly. For more information about the purpose and scope of data collection and processing in connection with social sharing features, please visit the privacy policies of the third parties that provide these features.

How We Use this Information

We use information that we collect about you or that you provide to us, including any personal information to:

  • Provide and deliver products and services you request
  • Allow you to participate in interactive features on our Website
  • Personalize your experience and customize content, marketing, and recommendations
  • Respond to requests that you make, provide you with customer service, send you press releases, general informative announcements, and promotional information about us or our products, and to fulfill any other purpose for which you provide it
  • Assist those who help us in designing and operating our Website, Apps or help us analyze collected data
  • Analyze, use and learn about the interests of our users in the aggregate and also on a personal level to better understand your interests and needs, or to aid us in serving you better
  • Carry out our obligations and enforce our rights arising from any contracts entered into between you and us, including for billing and collection
  • Protect, investigate, and deter against fraudulent, unauthorized, illegal activity, or violations of our policies
  • Notify you about changes to our Website or any products or services we offer or provide though it
  • In any other way we may describe when you provide the information for any other purpose with your consent or the consent of a parent or legal guardian
Sharing Your Information

We may share your personal information as follows:

  • With third parties that support our business, such as to process payments, or to store information collected through our site, the Apps, and services, and who are bound by contractual obligations to keep personal information confidential and use it only for the purposes for which we disclose it to them.
  • With a parent company, subsidiaries, joint ventures, or other companies under common control with us.
  • For the purposes of a business deal (or negotiation of a business deal) involving sale or transfer of all or a part of our business or assets. These deals can include any merger, financing, acquisition, or bankruptcy transaction or proceeding.
  • To fulfill the purpose for which you provide it, for any other purpose disclosed by us when you provide the information, or otherwise with your consent or the consent of a parent or legal guardian.
  • To (a) comply with relevant laws, regulatory requirements and to respond to lawful requests, court orders, and legal process; (b) to protect and defend the rights or property of us or third parties, including enforcing agreements, policies, and terms of use; (c) in an emergency, including to protect the safety of our employees or any person, or (d) in connection with investigating and preventing fraud.

We may also disclose to third parties non-personal aggregated information about our users and/or information that does not identify any individual. This may be done for a variety of reasons, such as for research and development, product improvements, and other related business purposes.

Your Consent

You (or a parent or guardian on your behalf) expressly consent to our processing of information as described in this Privacy Policy when you provide information to us through our Website or the Apps including when you create an Account. In addition, through these uses and interactions with us, our Website and our Apps, you specifically consent to our collection of personal information by automated means, such as cookies or the intended functionality of a product or service, and to our subsequent processing of the information in accordance with this Privacy Policy and to the storage and transfer of the information to locations wherever Anki and its service providers have facilities.

Accessing Your Information; Your Opt Out Rights

We offer certain choices about how we communicate with you and what information we obtain about you and your child. You may opt out of receiving any emails from Anki at any time. You may modify your personal information or request deletion of personal information or your Account by emailing us at support@anki.com. We will respond to all access requests within 30 days. We may not accommodate a request to change information if we believe the change would violate any law or legal requirement or cause the information to be incorrect.

Please note that although you may opt out of receiving promotional emails, Anki reserves the right to communicate with you via email regarding specific products and services you have ordered, requested, or inquired about.

We will retain your information for as long as your Account is active or as needed to provide you services. We will retain and use your information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

The Apps and Anki Website are intended for audiences of all ages including children under 13. In compliance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (“COPPA”), when we collect personal information, we will ask users of the Website and the Apps to input their age.

If a child signs up for an Account, his or her parent will be provided with notice via email and the parent must approve the account before it is activated, or, we may choose to obtain verifiable parental consent via other acceptable methods. The personal information collected and stored from the child (with parental permission) may include a username (e.g., driver name), account password, parent’s email address, and age/date of birth (as noted above). We may also collect and link to the child’s account the information we store within the analytics software.

The child’s personal information will be used for the purposes stated in this Privacy Policy, as permitted under COPPA. The child’s personal information may also be shared with certain third party vendors that help us operate the Anki Site or Apps, such as send out email communications to users or process account information for internal purposes.
