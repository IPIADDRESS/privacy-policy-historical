Privacy Policy:

Like most website operators, annasand.com collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. annasand.com purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how annasand.com visitors use its website. From time to time, annasand.com may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

annasand.com also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on annasand.com blogs/sites. annasand.com only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below, except that commenter IP addresses and email addresses are visible and disclosed to the administrators of the blog/site where the comment was left.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain visitors to annasand.com websites choose to interact with annasand.com in ways that require annasand.com to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that annasand.com gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up at annasand.com to provide a username and email address. Those who engage in transactions with annasand.com are asked to provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to process those transactions. In each case, annasand.com collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor's interaction with annasand.com. annasand.com does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

Aggregated Statistics

annasand.com may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. annasand.com may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, annasand.com does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information

annasand.com discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on annasand.com behalf or to provide services available at annasand.com websites, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Some of those employees, contractors and affiliated organizations may be located outside of your home country; by using annasand.com websites, you consent to the transfer of such information to them. annasand.com will not rent or sell potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information to anyone. Other than to its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations, as described above, annasand.com discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only in response to a subpoena, court order or other governmental request, or when annasand.com believes in good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the property or rights of annasand.com, third parties or the public at large. Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. annasand.com uses cookies to help annasand.com identify and track visitors, their usage of annasand.com website, and their website access preferences. annasand.com visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using annasand.com websites, with the drawback that certain features of annasand.com websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by annasand.com and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.
