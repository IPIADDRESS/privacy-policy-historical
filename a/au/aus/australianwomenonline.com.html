Privacy & Disclosure


Privacy Policy

Here at Australian Women Online your privacy is important to us. We do not distribute spam and we do not buy, sell or distribute any personal information or contact details collected by us or any third party.

Newsletter Sign-up
Names, addresses and other contact information collected by us during the newsletter sign-up process will not be used for any purpose other than to distribute newsletters, site news and other important announcements directly related to the Australian Women Online website. To ensure your privacy we use the trusted third party SafeSubscribe system from Constant Contact to collect, store and safely destroy all personal information submitted during the newsletter sign-up process.

Newsletters subscribers can safely unsubscribe from our email list at any time. All emails sent by Australian Women Online contain an unsubscribe link which you can click on at any time to unsubscribe from our email list and receive no more emails from us.

Competitions
From time to time, Australian Women Online will run competitions on the website. All personal information and contact details collected for competitions is used for the purpose of notifying the winner(s) of the prize(s) only. All personal information submitted via entry forms are stored securely for the duration of the competition and are then safely destroyed after the winner(s) are notified. No personal information or contact details submitted for competitions run on this website are kept after the competition has ended.

Contact Form
To minimise spam, we do not publish email addresses on the website. Individuals can connect us via an online form located in the ‘About Us’ page of the site. Please be assured all details submitted via this Contact Form are safely stored by the administrator of Australian Women Online (currently, Deborah Robinson) and will not be sold or otherwise distributed to any third party.

Discussion Forums
Australian Women Online maintains an online forum at http://forum.australianwomenonline.com for the purpose of facilitating interaction between readers of the online magazine. The online forum has been set up to provide a safe and secure meeting point for women who are supporters of the website. Any personal information and contact details collected during the registration process of the online forum is used for the purposes of maintaining the smooth operation of the forum only. Please be assured we do everything necessary to store your information safely and securely. Australian Women Online does not buy, sell, or otherwise distribute personal information and contact details collected to any third parties.

Disclosure Policy

This policy is valid from 19 July 2008.

This website does not accept any form of cash advertising, sponsorship, or paid topic insertions. However, we will and do accept and keep free products, services, travel, event tickets, and other forms of compensation from companies and organizations.

The compensation received will never influence the content, topics or posts made on this website. All advertising is in the form of advertisements generated by a third party ad network. Those advertisements will be identified as paid advertisements.

The owner of this website is not compensated to provide opinion on products, services, websites and various other topics. The views and opinions expressed on this website are purely the individual authors. If we claim or appear to be experts on a certain topic or product or service area, we will only endorse products or services that we believe, based on our expertise, are worthy of such endorsement. Any product claim, statistic, quote or other representation about a product or service should be verified with the manufacturer or provider.

This website does not contain any content which might present a conflict of interest.

If you have a privacy or disclosure concern related to the Australian Women Online website, please contact us via the Contact Form located on the ‘About Us’ page of this website.
