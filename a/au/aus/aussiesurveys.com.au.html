Privacy Statement

The Great Australian Survey takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.

This Privacy Policy covers The Great Australian Survey's treatment of personally identifiable information that we collect when you register and complete any surveys with The Great Australian Survey. This policy also covers The Great Australian Survey's treatment of any personally identifiable information that The Great Australian Survey's business partners share with The Great Australian Survey or vice versa.

This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that The Great Australian Survey does not own or control, or to people that The Great Australian Survey does not employ or manage.

The Great Australian Survey collects personally identifiable information when you register and/or complete surveys with The Great Australian Survey.

When you register with The Great Australian Survey, we ask for your name, email address, birth date, gender address, and marital status among other personal details. Once you register with The Great Australian Survey and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.

The Great Australian Survey uses information for two general purposes: to allow advertisers to market their products and services to you based upon your profile, and to analyse and provide analysis in aggregate form based on member profiles.

From time to time The Great Australian Survey will send personally identifiable information about you to other companies or people for marketing or research purposes.

  • By registering and/or completing surveys with The Great Australian Survey you are providing your consent for this to happen.  You may unsubscribe from the service at any time, which will have the effect of withdrawing your consent.

In addition we may provide your details to other companies when:

  • We need to share your information in order to provide the product or service you have requested;
  • We need to send the information to companies that work on behalf of The Great Australian Survey to provide a product or service to you. (Unless we tell you differently, these companies do not have any right to use the personally identifiable information we provide to them beyond what is necessary to assist us.);
  • We respond to subpoenas, court orders or legal process; or

You may request deletion of your account with The Great Australian Survey by logging into your account and choosing ‘unsubscribe’.  If you unsubscribe from The Great Australian Survey your account will be deleted after the next prize draw and you will:

  • Not be able to earn any additional entries into the next prize draw (but your existing entries will still be valid for the next prize draw)
  • Not be contacted by The Great Australian Survey other than for administrative purposes
  • Not have your data provided to any third party companies for marketing or other purposes

Your Great Australian Survey account information is password-protected for your privacy and security.

In certain areas The Great Australian Survey uses industry-standard SSL encryption to protect data transmissions.

The Great Australian Survey may edit this policy from time to time. If we make any substantial changes we will notify you by posting a prominent announcement on our pages.
