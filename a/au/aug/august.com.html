Deadbolt Compatibility

The August Smart Lock is designed to work with Single Cylinder deadbolts, which are the most popular door locks sold today. Mortise, Integrated Lock & Handle, and Rim Cylinder deadbolts are not compatible.

Yes! Single Cylinder

Deadbolt is mounted and operates independently of the door handle

 No! Mortise

An integrated door handle and deadbolt in a single metal unit

 No! Integrated Lock & Handle

Handle and lock turn-latch are both mounted together on a metal plate

 No! Rim Cylinder

Mounted on the inside, on top of the door and door jamb


If you are not sure whether your existing lock is compatible, please send photos of the inside, outside, and side view of your lock to photos@august.com.
