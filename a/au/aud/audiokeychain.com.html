AudioKeychain Privacy Policy

1. What information do we collect?

We collect non personally identifiable information such as your IP address.

2. What do we use your information for?

Any of the non personally identifiable information we collect from you may be used in one of the following ways:
  1. Analytics
  2. Security
  3. Marketing/Advertising

3. What do you do with my uploads?

Any uploaded tracks are removed from our servers immediately after AudioKeychain collects the necessary data to display to the user.

4. Do we use cookies?

We do not use cookies.

5. Do we disclose any information to outside parties?

Non-personally identifiable visitor information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other uses. We do not collect any personally identifiable information.

6. Third party links

Occasionally, at our discretion, we may include or offer third party products or services on our website. These third party sites have separate and independent privacy policies. We therefore have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites. Nonetheless, we seek to protect the integrity of our site and welcome any feedback about these sites.

7. California Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

Because we value your privacy we have taken the necessary precautions to be in compliance with the California Online Privacy Protection Act.

8. Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

We are in compliance with the requirements of COPPA (Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act), we do not collect any information from anyone under 13 years of age. Our website, products and services are all directed to people who are at least 13 years old or older.

9. Online Privacy Policy Only

This online privacy policy applies only to information collected through our website and not to information collected offline.

10. Your Consent

By using our site, you consent to our privacy policy.

11. Changes to our Privacy Policy

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will update the Privacy Policy modification date below.
