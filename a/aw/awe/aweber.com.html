Customer Information:
All AWeber Systems, Inc. customer account information is held in strict confidence. We do not sell or share our customer list with any other entities. Customer Lead Information:
All leads (e-mail addresses and other associated information) generated by the subscriber are held in strict confidence. AWeber Systems, Inc. does not contact subscriber leads, nor does it sell or share your lead information with any other party.

All AWeber Affiliate information, including address, phone number, e-mail address, and tax id number is held in strict confidence. This information is collected for the sole purpose of generating commission checks and meeting United States federal tax requirements.
