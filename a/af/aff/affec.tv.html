Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Affec.tv respects your privacy. We will not wilfully disclose any personally identifiable information (PII) about you to any third party without first receiving your permission. Furthermore, we do not specifically target children under the age of 13, nor do we knowingly collect, without parental consent, individually identifiable information from such children.

What Information Do We Collect?

From time to time we may collect certain personal information (such as their name, email address, etc.) in connection with various services offered from our advertisers (such as ‘share this content with friends’). We do not collect any personal information except when an individual specifically and knowingly provides such information. When we request information, we explain how this information may be used and give you an opportunity to request that the information not be used for those purposes. We also give you an opportunity to opt out of future communications from us.

Social Data

Affec.tv uses data provided from social platforms to show users relevant adverts. A typical example of this could be users commenting, or sharing, the same media (such as a video or photo). Affec.tv does not use any PII from social platforms and uses anonymous cookie data to understand connections across social media platforms.

Cookies

Affec.tv uses cookies. Cookies are data files stored on your computer that identify your web browser. Cookies enable websites to identify users that have visited them before and therefore customise that users browsing experience. Most browsers permit users to refuse cookies or to be notified before a cookie is accepted. You can visit our site without using cookies.

Links to Other Sites

Affec.tv contains links to other sites. These sites may send their own cookies to users, collect data, or solicit personal information. Since we do not control these sites; we cannot be hold responsible or liable for the privacy practices or content of such sites.

With Whom Do We Share Information?

Affec.tv may share non-personal and non-personally identifiable information about our users with advertisers and other third parties. We will not share personally identifiable information with any third party without your express consent.

Consent and Changes to this Privacy Statement

By using our site, you consent to the collection and use of your information as we have outlined in this privacy statement and to our Terms of Service. Affec.tv may decide to change this privacy statement from time to time. When we do, we will post the changes on this page so that you are always aware of the information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.
