AFFINNOVA PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: April, 2011

This privacy policy ("Privacy Policy") discloses the information that Affinnova, Inc. ("Affinnova") gathers on www.affinnova.com (the "Site"), as well as ways in which Affinnova processes and sometimes shares such information.

Except where otherwise expressly indicated in this Privacy Policy or on the Site, this Privacy Policy applies only to information collected on the Site and does not apply to information collected outside of the Site. Before using or submitting information to the Site, please review this Privacy Policy carefully.

Certain areas of the Site contain information, content or services intended only for users who register to use those areas of the Site ("Registered Users"), and Affinnova reserves the right to limit those areas to Registered Users. This Privacy Policy addresses Affinnova's privacy practices relating to general use of the Site, as well as use of the Site by Registered Users.

A. Information Affinnova Collects

Affinnova collects the following types of information from users of the Site:

1. Personally Identifiable Information

Affinnova may receive and store any information that you enter on the Site or provide to Affinnova in any other way on or through the Site that would allow Affinnova to identify you or contact you (collectively "Personally Identifiable Information"). The Personally Identifiable Information that you provide will be used by Affinnova only in accordance with this Privacy Policy. Examples of Personally Identifiable Information Affinnova may collect from you are:

  • your name, e-mail address, telephone number, employer's industry, name and address and your job title if you are a Registered User or are otherwise asked to provide such information, such as information you provide in connection with your request to view a demonstration of our products and services;
  • the names and e-mail addresses of those who communicate with Affinnova by e-mail;
  • email addresses of any person that you provide in connection with your use of certain features of the Site; and
  • information you provide about yourself when you apply for employment via the Site.

You may choose not to provide Affinnova with certain Personally Identifiable Information, but then you may not be able to take advantage of certain features of the Site.

2. Automatic Information

Affinnova may receive and store certain types of technical information whenever you interact with Affinnova through the Site. We may automatically receive and record your browser information, operating system information, IP address, the page you requested and the page the directed you to the Site. This allows Affinnova to enhance your experience and better support your needs. This information is not stored in a manner that is personally identifiable with you and may include the following:

(a) Aggregate Information

In connection with your visit to the Site, Affinnova may automatically collect usage information, such as the numbers of users and frequency of use. With respect to the Concept Studio Service, Affinnova may automatically collect certain statistical information including your last sign-on, number of concepts, number of dynamic elements, number of image objects, number of text objects per concept and word count. This data is only used in the aggregate as a statistical measure and not in a manner that would identify you personally. Aggregate information generally is collected through the use of cookies as discussed below.

(b) Cookies

Affinnova uses "cookies" to help you access and navigate the Site more efficiently and to statistically monitor how many people are using the Site. A "cookie" is a small file or tool stored on your hard drive containing information that allows Affinnova to deliver customized information to you. If you become a Registered User, Affinnova may use cookies to store your e-mail address, password and other technical information related to the Site so that Affinnova can identify you when you return to the Site, track your use of the Site and customize the Site for you. You may set your browser to notify you of or decline the receipt of cookies. Certain features of the Site may not, however, function properly or be available if your browser is configured to disable cookies.

Affinnova uses both "session" and "persistent" cookies on the Site. Session cookies are temporary bits of information that are erased once you exit your web browser or turn off your computer. Persistent cookies are more permanent bits of information that are placed on the hard drive of your computer and stay there unless you delete the cookie. Persistent cookies store information on your computer for a number of reasons, such as retrieving certain information you have previously provided (i.e., email address and password) and helping to determine what areas of the Site you find most valuable. Many companies offer programs that help you to visit web sites anonymously and Affinnova wants you to be aware that these programs are available, but use of such programs may block your access to certain features of the Site.

(c) E-mail Communications

When you send an e-mail to Affinnova, either through the "Contact" tab on the Site or otherwise, Affinnova may retain your e-mail address and the contents of the e-mail that you send. This Privacy Policy does not apply to content, business information, ideas, concepts or inventions that you send to Affinnova by e-mail. Please refer to Affinnova's Terms of Use for Affinnova's policy with respect to such information. If you want to keep the information in your email(s) private or proprietary, please do not send such information in an e-mail to Affinnova.

B. How Affinnova Uses Information

When you provide Affinnova with your Personally Identifiable Information, Affinnova may use it to manage Affinnova's registry of Registered Users, facilitate communication between Affinnova and you and/or provide you with other information Affinnova feels may be of interest to you. Affinnova also uses information collected to customize, analyze, adjust and improve the Site to meet Affinnova's users' needs. Affinnova also may use the information to enforce Affinnova's agreements with you, to prevent fraud and other prohibited or illegal activities and for other legally permissible purposes. These uses of your Personally Identifiable Information may include, without limitation, the following:

1. Public Areas

Personally Identifiable Information collected in public areas of the Site is used solely for the purpose for which it is collected. For example, Personally Identifiable Information provided in connection with your request for a demonstration of our products and services is used solely to contact you about a demonstration; likewise, Personally Identifiable Information that you submit to the Site in connection with your interest in the Affinnova employment opportunities described on the Site is used solely to consider you for such employment opportunities.

2. Registered User Areas

Certain areas of the Site are accessible only to Registered Users ("Registered User Areas"). Information that you make available in or through a Registered User Area may be viewed, accessed, or collected by, or disclosed to, other Registered Users that you invite to view such User area and may be used by such other Registered Users in ways neither you nor Affinnova are able to control or predict, including, without limitation, disclosure of your information to the public or to contact you for unauthorized purposes.

Affinnova may use the Personally Identifiable Information that you provide when registering to become a Registered User to send you information about Registered User benefits, features included in the Registered User Areas and other information that Affinnova believes is relevant and useful to its Registered Users. Affinnova does not sell mailing lists (names and mailing addresses) and e-mail addresses of Registered Users to third parties.

3. Aggregate Information

The aggregate information collected by Affinnova is used to create profiles of the audience for the Site that include anonymous statistical demographic information but no Personally Identifiable Information. This aggregate information is used to, among other things, determine trends and needs and enhance the Site experience for its users.

4. Emails and Newsletters

From time to time, Affinnova may use your Personally Identifiable Information to send you important administrative information regarding the Site and related services or changes to Affinnova's Terms of Use and policies. Affinnova also may use your Personally Identifiable Information to send you periodic emails and newsletters with information on new products and services, updates to the Site or upcoming events. You may at any time "opt out" of having your Personally Identifiable Information as part of Affinnova's marketing mailing list or otherwise (see "Opting Out" in Section D. below).

C. With Whom Affinnova Shares Information

Affinnova will not give, share, sell, or trade your Personally Identifiable Information to or with any third parties in any way, other than under the following circumstances:

1. Third-Party Service Providers

Affinnova may retain other companies and individuals to perform functions on Affinnova's behalf. Affinnova may share your Personally Identifiable Information with such third-party service providers to perform these functions consistent with this Privacy Policy. Examples include web hosting companies, data analysis firms and e-mail service providers.

2. Compliance with Law and Fraud Protection

Affinnova may disclose any information, including without limitation Personally Identifiable Information, that Affinnova deems necessary, in its sole discretion and without your prior permission, to comply with any applicable law, regulation, legal process or governmental request. Affinnova also may exchange information, including without limitation Personally Identifiable Information, with other companies and organizations for credit fraud protection, risk reduction and related activities or to protect the rights, property of safety of Affinnova and its agents, employees or affiliates or others. Affinnova reserves the right to disclose a user's Personally Identifiable Information if Affinnova believes the user is in violation of Affinnova's Terms of Use, even without a subpoena, warrant or other court order.

3. Sale or Transfer of Information

In the event of a sale, merger, liquidation, dissolution or sale or transfer of substantially all of the assets of Affinnova or the Site, Personally Identifiable Information and/or other information collected about you via the Site may be sold, assigned or transferred to the party acquiring all or substantially all of the equity and/or assets of Affinnova or the Site in order to permit the party to continue the operation of the Site, including the Registered User Areas. By using the Site, you consent to the sale and transfer of your information as described in this paragraph.

7. California Privacy Rights Under California Civil Code Section 1798.83

If you are a resident of California ("California Users"), in addition to the rights set forth in this Privacy Policy, you generally have the right to request information from Affinnova regarding the manner in which Affinnova shares certain categories of Personally Identifiable Information with third parties for their direct marketing purposes.

Under the law, however, a business is not required to provide this information if it adopts and discloses to the public (in its privacy policy) a policy of not disclosing customer's personal information to third parties for their direct marketing purposes unless the customer first affirmatively agrees to the disclosure, as long as the business maintains and discloses this policy. Rather, the business may comply with the law by notifying California Users of their rights to prevent disclosure of personal information and providing a cost free means to exercise that right. As stated above, Affinnova does not share such Personally Identifiable Information with third parties for their direct marketing purposes unless you have requested Affinnova to do so or have otherwise consented and then subject to your right to opt-out of sharing your personal information for third party marketing purposes.

Please note that whenever you opt in to receive future communications from a third party, your information will be subject to the third party's privacy policy. If you later decide that you do not want that third party to use your information, you will need to contact the third party directly, as Affinnova has no control over how third parties use information. You should always review the privacy policy of any party that collects your information to determine how it will handle your information.

Please note that Affinnova is only required to respond to one request per user per year. Affinnova is not required to respond to requests made by means other than through info@affinnova.com.
