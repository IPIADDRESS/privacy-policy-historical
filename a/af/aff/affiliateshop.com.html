AffiliateShop Privacy Policy

AffiliateShop has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site: AffiliateShop

Information Automatically Logged

AffiliateShop will automatically log all IP addresses of surfers coming to our clients' web sites as a result of a click on the redirect links provided by us to our clients. We use the IP address to help identify the client machine that is sending the click through. The purpose is to implement our AffiliateShop antifraud mechanism. The IP addresses are not used for any other purposes nor revealed to third parties.

Cookies

Our Site uses cookies to keep track of our clients' pay per sale affiliate program. The cookie allows us to track which affiliates are responsible for bringing the customers who purchased products or services on our clients' web sites. The cookie is not used for any other purposes throughout the entire AffiliateShop tracking system.

Advertisers

We use an outside ad company to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our Web site, cookies received with banner ads are collected by our ad company, and we do not have access to this information.

Registration Forms

Our site's registration form requires our clients and their affiliates to give contact information (like their name, email, and postal address), and unique identifiers (like their social security number).

Contact information from the registration forms is used to ship purchases, information about our company and for payment to affiliates from our clients or partners. The client's contact information is also used to send promotional material from some of our partners to our clients, and to get in touch with the client when necessary.

Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.

Unique identifiers (such as social security numbers) are collected from Web site visitors to verify the affiliate's identity, and for use as account numbers in our clients' record systems. AffiliateShop does not make use of the private Unique Identifiers. The Unique Identifiers are used only by our clients to facilitate their payment processing to the affiliates.

Order Forms

Our site uses an order form for customers to request information, products, and services. We collect contact information (like their email address), and financial information (like their account or credit card numbers).

Contact information from the order forms is used to get in touch with the visitor when necessary.

Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.

Financial information that is collected is used to check the users' qualifications for registration, and to bill the user for products and services. AffiliateShop uses our authorizes payment processing company to acquire the information from our clients. AffiliateShop will not store or make use of any confidential financial information (such as credit card information) collected from the order form except the email addresses and contact information, which are neccessary in order for AffiliateShop to provide service details to our clients.

External Links

This site contains links to other sites. AffiliateShop is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

Public Forums

This site makes chat rooms, forums, message boards, and/or news groups available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.

Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. AffiliateShop uses the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) to implement a secure access mechanism for our clients and their affiliates. All information sent from the login screen and the web based affiliate management interface are encrypted before being sent to the server. AffiliateShop acquires its digital certificate from Thawte of Versign Inc. The issue of this digital certificate proves the authenticity of the Pendulab's business existence and the operation of AffiliateShop.

Supplementation of Information

This site supplements the information that you provide with information that is received from third parties. We make use of the Transaction ID and Subscription ID generated to our clients from our authorized billing agent in order to verify the authenticity of each client's purchases for our AffiliateShop service.

Choice/Opt-Out
