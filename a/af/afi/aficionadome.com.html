Aficionado occasionally needs to collect information for convenience, practicality, or technical requirements. This includes:

  • Information supplied to Aficionado by Apple, which may include your name, email address, and ZIP code if you opted into the collection of this information upon subscribing.

Like most websites, Aficionado also collects and stores information automatically and through the use of electronic tools that may be transparent to visitors. We may also use “cookie” technology for your convenience as we see fit, such as to keep you logged in between visits if we add a login feature.

How is my personal information used?

We use the information you provide to operate Aficionado and its various features.

We use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design Aficionado. For instance, we may make feature decisions based on the percentage of our readership using a certain device or operating system.

We may also share non-identifying and aggregate statistics with potential future advertisers and partners. For instance, we may tell an advertiser or partner how many people visited a certain area on our website, used a certain feature of the application, or viewed a certain issue or article. When information is used in this or a similar manner, we do not disclose anything that could be used to identify the individuals on whom the information is based.

We may disclose your information in response to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or to defend against legal claims.

We may disclose your information when we believe it necessary or desirable in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of our policies, and/or to protect our rights and property.

In the future, we may sell, buy, merge, or partner with other companies or businesses. In such transactions, user information may be among the transferred assets.

External Services

External services beyond Aficionado’s control are integrated with Aficionado where necessary, such as Google Analytics for traffic measurement and tracking. Aficionado makes reasonable efforts to ensure that all integrated external services are trustworthy and reputable, but we cannot be held responsible for data collection and usage by such services.

Will Mediaquest sell or give my personal information to third parties?

We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to accomplish Aficionado’s functionality.

We use return email addresses to answer the email we receive. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties.

How does Mediaquest protect my personal information?

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
