AddFreeStats Privacy Statement

AddFreeStats has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate its commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this site.

This Policy applies to each users or members who obtain Services from AddFreeStats.

User Information

AddFreeStats does not acquire or collect any personal information about you. Your name, address, phone number, and email address all remain your private properties. AddFreeStats does, however, collect information which is specific to how you use the Internet. AddFreeStats servers note non-personally identifying details such as, but not limited to, the server you are logged onto, the geographic location of the ISP you use, the type of browser you use, and your IP address. This information is obtained solely for statistical purposes.

For AddFreeStats Registered Members :

AddFreeStats regards all personal information collected from its registered members as completely confidential. This includes personal data provided during registration processes.

AddFreeStats javascript code

When used on registered members website pages AddFreeStats javacript code will only gather non-personally identifying details such as, but not limited to, the server they visitors are logged onto, the geographic location of the ISP they use, the type of browser they use, and their IP address. This information is obtained solely for statistical purposes.

Use of Information

AddFreeStats does not sell any collected information to third parties.

Cookies

"Cookies" are identifiers which a web site can send to your browser and keep on your computer to facilitate your next visit to a site. Most cookies expire by themselves after a short period of time (often after only 12 hours). Our web pages may utilize cookies only for the purpose of authenticating your current session while you are logged in. We do not set or track cookies over separate sessions, nor do we transfer any cookie information to other sites.

If you do not want the benefit of cookies, there is a simple procedure to manually delete your cookies. Please consult your web browser's Help documentation for more information.

Third Party Advertising


We use DoubleClick and other third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please click here

Third Party Cookies

In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser.

Contacting Us

If you have any additional questions about AddFreeStats's dedication to consumer privacy, please contact us at afsteam(at)addfreestats.com.
