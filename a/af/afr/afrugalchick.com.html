  • About A Frugal Chick

    Laura does her shopping and blogging out of Chesapeake, VA.

    Laura started couponing and blogging when her employer backed out of a promise to pay for graduate school and she was determined to finish. By learning how to cut back on expenses and pick up many items for free she finished school, paid off her debt and quit her job to blog full time. She is learning how to "Live Well Without Going Broke" and wants to help you do that too!
