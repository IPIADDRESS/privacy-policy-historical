Privacy Policy:

 

This statement relates to the privacy practices of www.oooms.org in connection with our website.

General statement

www.oooms.org respects your right to privacy and actively seeks to maintain the privacy rights of those who share

personal information with www.oooms.org

Gathering and use of personal information

www.oooms.org does not collect any personal information about you on their website other than information which you

voluntarily provide. www.oooms.org only makes use of such information in line with the purpose for which you provided it

as is necessary to provide you with the information and/or service you require. www.oooms.org will not disclose your

information to any third party without informing you first or unless it is required to use a third party to process the

information for us. Should we need a third party to process information www.oooms.org requires these third parties to

comply with their instructions and also require that the third party agree not to use your personal information for

their own purposes unless you have explicitly consented for them to do so.

Use of cookies

Our website uses cookies which are small text files placed on your computer by our website so that www.oooms.org’s

website scripts are able to analyze how visitors use our website and record visitor preferences. www.oooms.org’s website

uses two different types of cookies:

www.oooms.org uses cookies to collect traffic data to the website to help us analyze how visitors use the

site. Such cookies are used to collect anonymous data on user behavior and are not used to

identify a user personally. The information stored in the cookie is used to evaluate use of this

website and to compile statistical reports on website activity for www.oooms.org.

On your first visit to this website you will see a message informing you about cookies. Clicking

the “hide this message” button causes the website script to set a cookie which records your

preference improving your experience on our website

Disabling cookies

Cookies may be blocked by adjusting the browser settings on your device. Blocking cookies will cause no loss of

functionality.

Links

www.oooms.org is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of other websites. The privacy practices on these

websites may differ from that on the www.oooms.org’s website, and it is advised that you review the other websites’

policies before providing personal information.

Right of Access

You have the right to ask for a copy of your personal data. Should you want to take advantage of this right email

us at oooms.org@gmail.com To protect your privacy, you may be asked to provide suitable proof of identification. If any

of your details are incorrect please let us know and we will correct them.

Revisions to Privacy Policy

www.oooms.org may revise this privacy statement from time to time. Changes to this privacy policy will be posted here so

you should check this page periodically to review the most recent statement.
