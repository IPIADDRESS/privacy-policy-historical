What are cookies? Cookies are small text files that are fixed on your browser by websites that you visit. We use cookies on our website to enhance user experience and to make our site work efficiently, for example, to save your preferences for the duration of your visit, and also to help us identify product features in which you may have the greatest interest.

How to Manage Cookies: Most browsers will tell you how to stop accepting new cookies, how to be notified when you receive a new cookie, and how to disable existing cookies. To find out more about cookies and how to control and manage them, visit www.allaboutcookies.org. Please note, however, that without cookies you may not be able to take full advantage of all functions of the website.
