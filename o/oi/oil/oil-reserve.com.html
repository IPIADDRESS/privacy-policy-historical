Privacy Policy


Confidentiality extends to all the information that our company has received about users, including any information received during their visit to our website.

Personal Information

Oil Reserve obtains personal information about you when you register on our website, when you contact our support team and when you are on our website.
When you register at Oil Reserve, we ask for your name, country, and other optional data.
