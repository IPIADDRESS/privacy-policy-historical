OnTheClock.com Privacy Policy
Overview

At OnTheClock.com we recognize that privacy is important to you. This document outlines the types of personal and company information we receive and collect when you use our web based time clock,  as well as some of the steps we take to protect your information. We hope this will help you make an informed decision about sharing your information with us.

Collection & Use Of Personal Information

We do not sell, rent, or lease your private information to third parties. We may from time to time use email to keep you informed of new features and site upgrades.  You will have the option to opt-out of future emails if you prefer.

Use Of Cookies
When you visit OnTheClock.com, we send one or more cookies(small files containing a string of numbers & characters) to your computer that uniquely identifies your web browser. We use cookies to improve the security and quality of our service by storing a unique number which identifies your browser to our servers.

Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies, however you can reset your browser to refuse cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, Your experance using our online punch clock may be comprimised if your cookies are disabled.

Information Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of data. These include internal reviews of our data collection, storage and processing practices and security measures, as well as physical security measures to guard against unauthorized access to systems where we store personal data. We restrict access to personal information to OnTheClock.com employees, contractors and agents who need to know that information in order to operate, develop or improve our services. These individuals are bound by confidentiality obligations and may be subject to discipline, including termination and criminal prosecution, if they fail to meet these obligations.


Consumer Recourse

If you believe we have not complied with our stated privacy policy concerning our usage of personally identifiable information that we may collect over the Internet, or that information we have provided to you based upon your Internet activity is inaccurate, you may Contact Us.

To Sum It Up

Our primary go is to give our customers the best web clock system.  We are not interested in collecting or selling your information, only in providing the best online time clock system in the world.
