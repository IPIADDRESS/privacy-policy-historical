1. Policy Regarding Minors

Mobeze does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 18, or knowingly allow such persons to register. If you are under 18, please do not attempt to register for OBC or send any information about yourself to us, including your name, address, telephone number, or email address. No one under age 18 may provide any personal information to or on OBC. In the event that we learn that we have collected personal information from a minor under age 18, we will delete that information as quickly as possible. If you believe that we might have any information from or about a minor under 18, please contact us at support@onlinebootycall.com

2. Information We Collect

When you visit OBC you provide us with two types of information: personal information you knowingly choose to disclose that is collected by us and website use information collected by us as you interact with our website.

When you register with OBC, you provide us with certain personal information, such as your name, your email address, your telephone number, your address, your gender, and any other personal or preference information that you provide to us.

When you enter OBC, we collect your IP address, browser type, hardware profile and the address of the pages you request. In addition, we store certain information from your browser using "cookies." These small files are needed for the OBC site to operate properly. If your browser is set to reject all cookies, the OBC website will not function properly.

When you use OBC, you may set up your personal profile, send messages, and perform searches. We collect this information so that we can provide you the service and offer personalized features. We retain personal profile information indefinitely as long as you remain registered at OBC. We retain information related to messages for 30 days. We do not retain any personally identifying information related to user searches. When you update any information, we usually keep a backup copy of the prior version for a reasonable period of time to enable reversion to the prior version of that information.

You post user content on OBC at your own risk. Although we attempt to limit access to OBC members, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable. We cannot control the actions of other users, therefore, we cannot and do not guarantee that user content you post on the OBC will not be viewed by unauthorized persons. We are not responsible for circumvention of any privacy settings or security measures contained on the OBC. You understand and acknowledge that, even after removal, copies of user content may remain viewable in cached and archived pages or if other users have copied or stored it.

If you choose to use our referral service to invite a friend to become a member of OBC, we will ask you for information needed to send the invitation, such as your friend's email address. We will automatically send your friend a one-time email or instant message inviting him or her to become a member. OBC stores this information to send this one-time invitation, to register a friend connection if your invitation is accepted, and to track the success of our referral program. Your friend may contact us at support@onlinebootycall.com to request that we remove this information from our database.
