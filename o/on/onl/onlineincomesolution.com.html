How We Use Affiliate Information

 The affiliate information that we collect during SFI’s notification process and SFI group related business is or may be used:

  • For affiliate communication purposes;

  • To examine affiliate status and progress;

  • To make sure that only SFI affiliates within or related to our group are able to access certain affiliate-only areas on our www.e-mastersteam.com, www.uniqueisp.com and/or www.mlmopportunity.net and affiliated websites;

  • With prior affiliate’s authorization, we provide some information to companies that we use  to perform certain functions for us -- for example, name, address, email address, website address and other needed information are provided to advertising companies, for affiliate’s business advertising purposes.

  • Frequently, we use our Affiliate database to distribute SFI team newsletters, updates or important notices to our affiliates.

  • CWW may also use affiliate information to enforce our Appropriate Use Policy, but only to the extent necessary.

  • We will share affiliate information with governmental authorities when legally required to do so, and may cooperate with these authorities investigating claims of illegal activity such as (but not limited to) illegal transfer or use of copyrighted material, or postings or e-mail containing threats of violence. Except under the limited circumstances described above, we do not disclose Affiliate information to third parties.

Web Site Use and Navigation Information

Each time a user visits our Web Site, certain anonymous use and navigation information is collected by our servers. This information is aggregated and anonymous. It tell us how our Web Site is used and navigated, including the number, frequency and duration of visits to each Web page.

Web Site use and navigation information that we collect may include the domain names of visitors who are not already SFI team affiliates, but it does not otherwise contain individually identifiable information about any visitor to our Web Site.

 

How We Use Web Site Use and Navigation Information

Like affiliate survey information, we use the anonymous, aggregated information to improve the content and navigation features of our Web Site. We also may use the anonymous, aggregated information to enable CWW Partners or advertisers to evaluate whether to post advertising banners or other material on the CWW Web Site.

 

Cookies

Like most Web sites, CWW makes use of browser "cookies". Cookies are files written to a user's hard disk and read by server programs to inform a Web site if the user has previously visited that site. We use cookies to continually enhance your CWW experience, and may use them in the future to store affiliate preferences and choices.

Our Account Management Web pages use cookies to store and retrieve session identifiers that correspond to a secure database containing each Affiliate's user name and password information. This is designed to facilitate access by affiliate to our team affiliate services (when applicable.) These cookies do not enable third parties to access any of your affiliate information.

Companies who advertise on our Web Site may also use cookie technology. We do not control the use of cookies by our advertisers.
