Cookies: Cookies are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on the hard drive or memory of your computer or Internet access device. These cookies enable us to personalize your experience on the Websites, maintain a persistent session, passively collect demographic information about your computer and monitor advertisements and other activities. Cookies do not include or collect Personal Information but rather, they identify your computer, usually by its Internet Protocol ("IP") address. An "IP address" is a temporally unique number that can be used to identify your computer whenever you log on to the Internet. Please remember that with most modern browsers you always can disable cookies or set your browser to alert you when cookies are being sent to your computer. The decision to disable cookies, however, may prevent you from being able to use certain portions of the OnLive Websites.
