Log Files

Like other blogs/websites ,OnlineAppsForPC.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit page, gather demographic information, IP addresses and the other such information not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.
