Only.com™ will not sell, exchange, or release your individual personal information to a third party without your express permission.

Do we collect information?

Yes. When you register for one of our auction sites, or place a classified ad, or use any of the resources we provide that requires a response from us, we must of course ask who you are and request certain basic information from you. This information is used only within our company and is never sold, traded or exchanged with any other company or individual.

Do we place cookies?

Yes. Cookies are small data files that are stored on your computer. They contain information that allows you to maintain your information while searching and using forums, auctions classified ads etc.... Cookies do not contain any personally identifiable information.

We use cookies to let us know if our visitors come back again, which indicates to us if we are delivering a good product to our visitors. If you prefer not to accept cookies from Only.com™ - or from other sites too - you can set your browser to reject cookies. You can find this feature in "Preferences" if you are using Netscape or in "Internet Options" if you are using Microsoft Internet Explorer but you will not be able to enjoy many of the benefits of an Only.com™ site.

What about links to other sites?

Only- sites contain links to other sites and we cannot be responsible for the privacy practices of these other sites. Only.com™ does not share any personal information you may provide to us with other sites.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the practices of our sites, please send an email to privacy@Only.com
