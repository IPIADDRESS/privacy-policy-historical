Privacy Policy

Our Practice Regarding Your Privacy
We at onlinebestcolleges.com are committed to respecting your privacy and understand your need for appropriate protection and use of any information you share with us by using our web sites.

User will many times give his personal information to the college or university for getting more information from them by filling a simple request form. The information given is directly transmitted to such colleges and and their associates. Our site does not retain any personally identifiable information of any user who visits our site and does not take responsibility of any school or colleges reached through us.

Sharing of Information
When an user submit a request for more information from schools and colleges, your information is directly transmitted to such entity or their marketing agencies. They may then contact you by telephone or e-mail and provied with more information if you qualify. In all cases, you may opt-out of sharing your Information with such providers by choosing not to fill the request form.

Security
We intend to take reasonable and appropriate steps to protect the information you shared with us from unauthorized access or disclosure. Please note that while onlinebestcolleges.com will make every reasonable effort to protect your information, no transmission of data over the Internet is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy
onlinebestcolleges.com is not structured to attract children. Accordingly, we do not intend to collect personally identifiable information from anyone we know to be under 13 years of age.

Changes in Privacy Policy
Protecting your privacy online is an evolving area, and our web sites are constantly changing to meet these demands. We therefore reserve the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time. By using this web site, you consent to the terms of our Online Privacy Policy.
