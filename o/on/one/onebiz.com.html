Web Beacons

We or third parties who are acting in our name, may utilize web beacons or similar technologies in our messages, websites or within OneBiz.com AG’s messages which you receive from third parties. A web beacon is an electronic image with which a cookie may be recognized on your system if you are visiting a website or requesting an online message. Due to web beacons, we are able to measure the activity of our website and our interaction with you in various ways. This occurs through counting of people who are visiting our website through certain messages or after making a purchase on our website after clicking through a message. In addition we analyze whenever a website is being visited and a description of the website that contains the web becaon image will be made ready. Web beacons also enable us to measure the effectiveness of our messaging campaigns, such as through counting the peoplpe who are viewing a message or becoming active on it, or through research, when a message is being opened and how often it is being shared. The collected data may contain person specific data and using web beacons we may recognize user’s access to cookies that are provided by OneBiz.com AG. In addition we are authorized to combine the collected information with other personal data which we have collected from you. All this information is used to make our contact with you more individual. If necessary we will also utilize this information for other purposes, such as to adjust content on our websites, to offer a shopping cart, or to run internal analysis. Personal data, which is recorded through OneBiz.com AG web beacons will not be shared with third parties, with the exception of service providers who are acting in our name and who are bound by contract not to utilize the information for any other purposes or to make it public. Third parties may place their own web beacons or ads on our websites, as long as they are determined to ensure user’s privacy and data protection. Through the refusal of cookies you may neutralize certain web beacons.
