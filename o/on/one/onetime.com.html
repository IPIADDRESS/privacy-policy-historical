OneTime Privacy Policy

Thank you for using OneTime. We know that your privacy and security are important to you, so we'd like to set your mind at ease.

Personal Information Collection
OneTime does not collect personal information from our users, including name, address, phone number and credit card number. The deal providers with whom we are partnered may ask for such information when users book hotels, flights, car rentals or cruises.

Cookies
OneTime may collect cookies during a user's visit to evaluate overall site performance and user experience. OneTime does not share its cookie information with its deal providers or any other parties. All data is kept confidential and is used only for internal analysis. Our deal providers may also collect cookies.

Other Information
OneTime logs the total number of visitors to our website by page visited. OneTime also tracks IP addresses and the domain names of our users' Internet service providers. This data is used for internal analysis only, and you can rest assured that this data is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

Visiting our Deal Providers
OneTime helps you to quickly and easily compare rates and availability for hotels, flights, car rentals and cruises from leading travel deal providers by deep-linking you into their websites based on your travel criteria. OneTime assumes no responsibility or liability for any transactions or experiences that occur on the websites of our partnered deal providers. (For a complete list, please check out the OneTime Deal Providers.) Our deal providers have their own Privacy Policies and Terms of Use designed to inform and protect you while using their respective websites. OneTime does not have any control over or responsibility for the Privacy Policies or Terms of Use of these websites.
