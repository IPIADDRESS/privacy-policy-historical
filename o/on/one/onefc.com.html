Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting ONE Fighting Championship Website. Your privacy is important to us. We will not collect any information from you for use on this website or any other related website unless you choose to provide your information to us.

If you provide your personal/ company information to us (via online inquiry and eNewsletter subscription form), please note that this information provided is through optional, voluntary submissions. Your contact information will be saved for record and follow-up communication but will not be distributed or sold to other third parties.

ONE Fighting Championship reserves the right to change the privacy policy but will notify users of such changes via email or by posting a website notice, where appropriate.
