Privacy Policy

ONE PLUS Corporation is concerned about the privacy of users and is committed to protecting their personal information. We do not share email information, phone, address or other personal information with any outside organization or group. Unless specifically directed otherwise, you may receive email messages from us. If you do not want to receive email from us in the future, please let us know by sending email to us at the above address and telling us that you do not want to receive email from our company.

We may retain the email addresses of persons who contact us via email, summarize information on what pages users access or visit and information volunteered by the user, such as survey information and/or site registrations. Information collected on the site is used to improve Website content, and to notify users about updates to our Website or product lines. Cookies may be used in order to collect statistical data on users or to personalize information provided to you.

When you request information to be sent to you, you may receive future mailings from us with information on new products and services. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by sending email to us at the address below and telling us that you do not want receive additional mail from our company. Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers online may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding their on-line inquiries. Phone numbers provided will not be used for unsolicited telemarketing of any kind.

Send email communications pertaining to the preceding to info@onepluscorp.com.
