Safety Tips for E-Trading

  • NEVER reveal your password to anyone. Be suspicious of any person/ email asking you to provide sensitive trading account information.
  • If you receive such request from anyone/ emails, do take extra precaution to verify that the source is known to be genuine. Do not reply to the person or the email or click on any hyperlink to verify your information and details.
  • Act quickly and contact our credit department at +65 66711833 if you think that your account has been compromised.
  • Do NOT open email attachments from strangers.
  • Delete spam and junk emails.
  • Passwords should be at least 8 alphanumeric characters.
  • Do NOT use obvious passwords, such as your User ID, initials, family names, company name, your date of birth, IC number, telephone number or other numbers / letters that could be guessed easily.
  • Do NOT use passwords from your other registered Internet sites.
  • Do NOT disclose your password to anyone, including Ong First Tradition Pte Ltd staff.
  • Do NOT write down or record the password without first disguising it.
  • Do change your password regularly, e.g. within 90 days. In addition, the new password should NOT be the same from the previous passwords.
  • Always remember to log off from the E-Trading platform when you have completed your trading activities for the day or whenever you are leaving your workstation unattended.
  • Do NOT select the browser option for storing or retaining username and password.
  • Check the authenticity of the eTrading website by comparing the URL with its digital certificate.
  • Ensure the website changes from http:// to https:// for an encrypted session.
  • Check your bank account balance and transactions frequently and report any discrepancy.
  • Ensure your computer is protected with anti-virus, anti-spyware, firewall and have the latest updates.
  • Do NOT use public, internet café or computers, which cannot be trusted to access/perform online transactions.
