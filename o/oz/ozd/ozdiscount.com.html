Aggregated Information

Aggregated information, such as which page users access or visit, and information volunteered by users is collected through various methods. For example, OzDiscount.com collects IP addresses; an IP address is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer whenever you are surfing the web. Web servers (the computers that "serve up" web pages) automatically identify your computer by its IP address; when you request a page from OzDiscount.com, our servers log your IP address.

OzDiscount.com only uses IP addresses for the purposes of Web traffic analysis and trends.

Use of Cookies

OzDiscount.com uses cookies for this site. A cookie is a small data file that most major websites write to your hard drive for record-keeping purposes. Cookies allow OzDiscount.com to measure activity on the site and to improve your user experience, for example by remembering your passwords and viewing preferences.

Children and Privacy

OzDiscount.com encourages parents and guardians to spend time with their children online, and to be fully familiar with the sites visited by their children.

OzDiscount.com does not provide any personally identifying information for users under the age of 13, regardless of its source, to any third party for any purpose whatsoever unless disclosed during collection.
