What we collect:

In various places on the our site, we collect “Personally Identifiable Information,” such as your name, age, e-mail address, or other information that can be used to contact you or identify you as an individual. For example, we collect this information when you register for membership. From time to time in the future we may offer additional features, services, and promotional opportunities that would require you to submit Personally Identifiable Information to participate.

We also collect “Anonymous Information” such as your IP address, the type of browser you use, the pages on our site that you have visited, and the advertisements that you have clicked on. Your Personally Identifiable Information is linked to your Anonymous Information.

Like many Internet sites, we also use “cookies” to collect information. A cookie is a small data file that we transfer to your computer and is stored on your hard drive. When you visit our site the cookie identifies your computer to us so that you do not have to re-register/login each time you visit. We may also use cookies to measure the traffic to our site and the use of different services and features of the site. Advertisers that appear on the Owen’s World may place cookies on your computer and their use of the information stored on those cookies is subject to their own privacy policies. You should visit their sites to learn about their information practices. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to Owen’s World cookies.

We do not intend that children will use our sites. We do not solicit or knowingly collect information from children under 13 years old, nor will we accept registration from them.


How we use the information we collect:

We use Personally Identifiable Information that you provide to communicate with you, to customize advertising and content available on the Owen’s World, to conduct research for internal purposes and to use such information to improve our services and the site.

We use information that is not personally identifiable to determine how people use our web site and its services. For example this data tells us information including how often users click on our advertisements, which files are downloaded most frequently, and which areas of our site are the most popular. Analyzing this data allows us to improve the offerings on and performance of our site. Using your IP address, we can also determine the general physical location of a user and may at times use this information to better target advertising to users.

Except as noted below, Owen’s World does not share your Personally Identifiable Information with third parties unless you give us your permission.
