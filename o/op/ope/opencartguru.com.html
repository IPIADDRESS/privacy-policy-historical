Privacy Policy

We guarantee your privacy and on-line shopping safety.

We do not store any credit card information. All billing information is processed by the payment processer (Bank or PayPal.com). Paypal is an online bank owned by eBay and considered trustworthy today. PayPal is secured by multiple industrial security technologies including solutions from VeriSign.
