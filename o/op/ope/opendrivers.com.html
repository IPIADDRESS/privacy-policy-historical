When you are first served an ad by OPENDRIVERS.COM, the advertiser assigns you a unique number and records that number in the cookie file of your computer. Then, when you visit OPENDRIVERS.COM, the advertiser reads this number to help target ads to you. The cookie can help ensure that you do not see the same ad over and over again. Cookies can also help advertisers measure how you utilize an advertiser's site. This information helps our advertisers cater their ads to your needs.
