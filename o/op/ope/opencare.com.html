Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes our privacy practices and how we treat the Personal Information that we collect from You when You use products and services of Company, including any Personal Information we collected from You when You created Your account. This privacy policy applies to all of the products, services, and websites offered by Company or our related, affiliated and/or subsidiary companies.

1. Information Collection and Use We collect personally identifiable information when you register for account, when you visit pages. If you decline to submit Personal Information, we may not be able to or may choose not to provide to You some or all of our Products and Services. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to Ours cookies. If you prefer, you can turn off cookies in your web browser by changing its security settings. If we make any substantial changes in the way of use your personal information we will notify you by posting a prominent announcement on our pages.
