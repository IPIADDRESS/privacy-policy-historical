When we refer to "Personal Information" we mean information that allows someone to identify or

not associated with or linked to your Personal Information and which cannot be identified as

belonging to you.

We may collect Personal Information from you, such as your first and last name, mailing address,

phone number, e-mail address, billing address, credit card or debit card information, PayPal

from you, including your browser type, operating system, IP address, web pages visited, web

pages viewed, buttons or links clicked, time spent on website, time spent on web pages, etc.

Policy Statement;

implied, depending on the sensitivity of the Personal Information, legal requirements, and

collect will be relevant, adequate and not excessive for the purposes for which it is

collected;

was originally collected or to which you have subsequently consented; and

• not use your Personal Information for direct marketing purposes without giving you an

opportunity to “opt-out”.

performance;

be of interest, both on and off the Site.

Services.
