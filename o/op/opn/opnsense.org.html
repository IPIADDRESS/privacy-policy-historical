Personal Information

The personal information you enter on this site will be used for the purpose you supplied the asked information (info request and/or order of products/services).

Besides the above purpose Deciso B.V. uses the information in order to enhance our service to you. This information will not be supplied to third parties.

We are committed to ensuring the privacy of our visitors and customers.

Unsolicited Email [SPAM]

Your e-mail address will not be used for sending unsolicited email and if you applied for our newsletter we assure you that we keep our newsletters for news and not to just fill-up your mailbox and you can, at anytime, unsubscribe.

Type of information collected

Deciso will collect information necessary to use our products, forums, support and services. We may collect your name, address, phone number, fax number, email address, login id, password, contact preferences, IP address and referring URL.

Use of Cookies

Our website only uses cookies for the order process in order to know what is in your shopping card and for our site statistics.

We do not use them for any other purpose.

Changes to the Privacy Notice

January 1, 2014.
