Individuals always have the ability to stop their information being used for such purposes.

To ensure the security of your data, including address information and credit card numbers, Oxygen always uses industry-standard encryption technologies when transferring and receiving consumer data exchanged with our site. If you visit these sites, please look for their privacy policy.

These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
