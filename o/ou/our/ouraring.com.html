Privacy Policy

Last update: October 1, 2015

We are on a mission to improve your quality of life, enable you to be at your best. Our products, such as the ŌURA ring, enable you to learn how your lifestyle choices affect your sleep, and how the quality of your sleep affects your ability to perform.

When worn, the ŌURA ring automatically collects data of your body responses during your sleep and daily activity. That data is uploaded wirelessly to your mobile phone via our ŌURA mobile app. The ring or the app may also connect to your computer or a cloud service and your data is made available to you there. In addition, when you interact with the ŌURA app, purchase the ŌURA ring or register as a customer – or when you browse our web site – certain information about you is collected. We feel that data does not get much more personal than this, and thus respect of your privacy. Safety of your data is of paramount importance to us. The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to help you understand what information we collect and how we use it, so please take a moment to review it.

First you need to understand what this Privacy Policy relates to. When we use the terms “we”, “our”, “us” or Ōuraring in this Privacy Policy, we mean our company called Jouzen Oy and its subsidiaries and affiliates worldwide. This Privacy Policy applies to our wellness computer products, such as the ŌURA ring (“Devices”), our website located at ouraring.com and any other website we may establish in the future (individually a “Site” and collectively “Sites”), the ŌURA mobile application and any other app we may establish in the future (each an “App” and collectively the “Apps”), and also any software or cloud service for any of our Devices or Apps. The Devices, Sites, Software and Apps are collectively referred to in this Privacy Policy as the “Service.”

By using the Service, you consent that we may process the data that we collect from you in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

What data do we collect?

Personal Data. Personal Data is information that identifies a particular individual (“Personal Data”). In order for us to provide the Service to you, we need to collect and use certain Personal Data about you. We may collect the following Personal Data:

  • Your first and last names
  • Your e-mail address, telephone number, address and other contact information

When you purchase our Service on our Website, you will need to provide your credit card or other applicable payment method. We do not collect this information because we use a third party to process payments for our Service.  When making a payment, you should refer to the Privacy Policy of our payment processor stripe.com.

Service Data. Service Data is information that you need to input to the Service in order for us to provide the Service to you and measurement data we collect when you use our Devices. We may collect the following Service Data:

  • Geographics
  • Gender
  • Birth date and year
  • Height and weight
  • Measurement data

 

Measurement data includes your heart rate, movement and temperature data collected via the ŌURA ring, and based on it, the ŌURA ring and the App calculate a variety of parameters for your use via the Service, such as sleep duration, sleep phases (deep, light, REM, awake), and time spent at different activity levels during the day. This information is used to gauge the quality of your sleep, level of your recovery, and balance of your activity.

Other Data. When we provide the Service to you, we want to make the Service easy, useful and reliable. This involves collecting other information which does not identify you and we do this by utilizing:

  • Google Analytics
  • Server Logs

Google Analytics uses “cookies”, which are small text files placed on your computer, to collect standard internet log information and visitor behavior information in an anonymous form – no personally identifiable information is collected about you unless you explicitly submit that information to us. Google Analytics is a free tool that lets us see what’s going on with our website traffic, where our visitors are coming from and what are they doing on our website. Most web browsers allow you to turn off cookies. To do this look at the “help” menu on your browser. Switching off cookies may restrict the use of the Service and/or delay or affect the way in which it operates.

We and our service providers use server log information for purposes such as calculating site usage levels, helping diagnose problems with ŌURA’s servers and administering ŌURA’s websites. These server logs may include information such as your Internet Protocol (IP) address, host name, browser type, browser language, the date and time of your request, the time(s) of your visit(s) and the web page(s) that you visited. Collecting IP addresses is standard practice on the Internet and is done automatically by many web sites.

Special Note About Children. Our Service is not directed or suitable for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect any Personal Data from children under 18. If you are aware of a user under the age of 18 using the Service, please contact us immediately.  We reserve the right to delete any account if we suspect the account holder is a child under the age of 18.

What do we do with the data we collect?

We may use your Personal Data for the following purposes (excluding your Service Data):

  • to register you to use our Service;
  • to deliver and maintain our Service;
  • to communicate with you about the Service;
  • to enable us to monitor, review and improve the Service;
  • for our internal records regarding the use of the Service;
  • for marketing purposes (see also Direct Marketing below); and
  • so that we can fulfill our obligations to you relating to the Service and to help you resolve support issues.

Ouraring uses your Service Data to the extent necessary to provide the Service to you and to help you resolve support related issues with your Service. We may also make aggregate compilations, statistics and analyses of the Service Data – on an anonymous basis. We may use such compilations, statistics and analyses for research, scientific and commercial purposes, again, without disclosing any Personal Data that would permit identification of our customers.

In addition, all data we collect may be used as follows:

  • International transfers. Our Services may be provided using resources and servers located in various countries around the world. Therefore the data we collect (including Service Data) may be transferred across international borders outside the country where you use our Services, including to countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA) that do not have laws providing specific protection for personal data or that have different legal rules on data protection, for example, the United States of America. In such cases we take steps to ensure that there is a legal basis for such a transfer and that adequate protection for your data is provided as required by applicable law, for example, by using standard agreements approved by relevant authorities (where necessary) and by requiring the use of other appropriate technical and organizational information security measures.
  • Mandatory disclosures. We may be obligated by mandatory law to disclose the data we collect to certain authorities or other third parties, for example, to law enforcement agencies in the countries where we or third parties acting on our behalf operate. We may also disclose and otherwise process the data we collect in accordance with applicable law to defend our legitimate interests, for example, in civil or criminal legal proceedings.
  • Corporate Transactions. We may disclose or transfer the data we collect in connection with the sale, merger, bankruptcy, sale of assets or reorganization of our company, or to transfer the data within our corporate family.

Direct marketing. At your request we may use your contact information in any commonly used method in order to provide you with information about products, special offers and other information relating to our products or Services or which we believe may be of interest to you.

You can change your mind later on in respect of direct marketing by contacting us at any time. In addition, if you receive direct marketing from us in writing you will be reminded in that material that you are able to request a change to your preferences.

How do we keep your Personal Data safe?

In order to keep your Personal Data and all the other information we have stored safe, we have implemented various security measures and taken reasonable steps to prevent unauthorized access, use, alteration, destruction or disclosure of your Personal Data.

Your Personal Data will be stored in a secure environment and treated confidentially. We will, however, use a hosting provider to store data, including Personal Data. The hosting provider may not use your Personal Data for any other purposes besides storing it, and we require the hosting provider to act consistently with this Privacy Policy and to use appropriate security measures to protect your Personal Data.

We will take all reasonable precautions to ensure that our staff and employees who have been specifically granted access to information about you have received adequate training to ensure that they process that information only in accordance with this policy and with our obligations under applicable legislations.

Should you wish to review the Personal Data being held by us about you, you have the right to request details. Please make any requests in writing to us and we will comply with your request promptly.

Should you wish us to permanently delete your Personal Data being held by us, we will do so upon your request. In such case you understand, that we will not be able to provide the Service to you anymore.
