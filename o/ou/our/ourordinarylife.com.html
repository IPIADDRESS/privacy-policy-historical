About The Author

Parenting blogger Kristin writes Our Ordinary Life, a website in which she shares her life with her ordinary family. On this website you will find everything from family friendly organic recipes, reviews of products she likes, fun family giveaways, and places to visit. She even throws in her parenting advice along the way which includes everything from tips for traveling with kids to dealing with the increasing tantrums of toddlerhood. She has been consistently blogging and enjoys it.
