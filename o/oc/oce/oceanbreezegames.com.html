Anonymous information refers to data that cannot be tied back to a specific individual. OCEANBREEZEGAMES.COM collects this user information for purposes of product monitoring, product improvement, and to aid future development decisions. You do not have to register a product with OCEANBREEZEGAMES.COM before we can collect this anonymous information.
