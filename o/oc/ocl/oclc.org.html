Privacy Policy

We at OCLC do not share information about individual users with any third party. We use registration information to improve content and tailor it to suit your needs, to provide easier access to advanced features, and to help us understand our users. We ask visitors to the OCLC Web site to provide their E-mail addresses, postal addresses, or telephone numbers when needed to answer inquiries, provide information, or ship requested publications or products. You will receive only the information for which you provided us your address or telephone number.

If a user submitting their E-mail address, postal address or telephone number is suspected of being younger than 13 years of age, OCLC will delete this information from its records in accordance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (15 U.S. Code, 6501 et seq.).

Please use the Web Site Feedback form to send us questions or comments about this policy.
