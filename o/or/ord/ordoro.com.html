Information Ordoro Collects

In general, we receive and collect all information you provide via the Service, including by phone, email, and web chat. How We Use Personal Data

The personal data that you provide will be kept confidential and used to support your relationship with Ordoro and the use of our products and services, including the Service. Among other things, we want to help you quickly find information through our Service, leverage our tools in connection with your authorized use of our products and services, process payment, facilitate interaction with third party services that you opt into via the Service, and alert you to product upgrades, special offers, updated information and other new products and services from Ordoro.

More specifically, we may use your personal data:

  • To personalize the user experience - We may use your data to understand how you and our other users, as a group, use the Service, to analyze you shipping and spend preferences, and provide recommendations based on your past actions.
  • To improve the Services - We continually strive to improve the Service based on the information and feedback we receive from you.
  • To improve customer service - Your information helps us to more effectively develop the Service and respond to your and other users’ support needs. For example, we may use your email address and phone number in response to your request for customer support on an existing transaction.
  • To send periodic emails - The email address you provide, will be used to send information and updates pertaining to the Service. It may also be used to respond to your inquiries, and/or other requests or questions. If you opt in to our mailing list, you may receive emails that include Ordoro news, updates, related product or service information, and marketing material. If at any time you would like to unsubscribe from receiving future emails, we include detailed unsubscribe instructions at the bottom of each email or you may contact us via contact information below.

Ordoro will retain your information as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy and for a commercially reasonable time thereafter for backup, archival, fraud prevention or detection or audit purposes, or as otherwise required by law.

Ordoro takes technological and organizational measures to protect your personal data against theft and unauthorized access, use, disclosure or modification on Ordoro systems. For example:

  • Our systems are designed to protect personal data from unauthorized usage on shared computers in that users are required to log in using passwords through our Service.
  • Our systems are located in controlled facilities managed proven services providers like Amazon Web Services, Google Cloud Platform and Rackspace.

Accordingly, you assume full responsibility for posting such data and agree that Ordoro is not responsible in any way for such publicly posted personal data.

Ordoro complies with applicable data protection laws, including applicable security breach notification requirements.
