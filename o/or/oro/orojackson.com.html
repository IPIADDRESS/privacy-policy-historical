Privacy Policy
This document contains the privacy policy of http://orojackson.com, for the disclaimer please click here.

1) Personal Information
OroJackson will never ask*, collect or store any information such as names, phone numbers or addresses from users who visit our site. However, our systems do take note of your geographic location to use it as statistic to see where our users come from. Besides that, we will never store any of your private information.

*In the case of you winning a competition you participated in, and if the prize has to be shipped/couriered to you we would need some details, these details will not be disclosed to anyone else other than the shipping service provider. These details will ONLY BE ASKED by CapnLuffy or Freeze. Do not provide personal details to anyone claiming to be a OroJackson Staff Member.

2) Forums
If you are using our forums, you must acknowledge that anything you posts or submit here can be read by other people. We are not responsible for the personally identifiable information you choose to submit in the forums. However, we do take serious action against those who attempt to do something wrong to our members!

3) Advertising
Advertising on our site is provided by a third party vendor, they use cookies to serve ads on our site. They may also make use of the DART cookie, which enables them to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and other sites on the Internet. Advertising companies collect information including your IP address, your ISP , the browser you used to visit our site, and in some cases, whether you have Flash installed. This is generally used for geotargeting purposes (showing New York real estate ads to someone in New York, for example) or showing certain ads based on specific sites visited (such as showing cooking ads to someone who frequents cooking sites).

If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.
