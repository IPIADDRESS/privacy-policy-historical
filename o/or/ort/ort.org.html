Privacy Policy

Registration

Certain of the Products and Services offered by World ORT will require the user to register and provide certain information such as your name, e-mail address and postal address when registering for programs such as EnglishSpace and DO I.T. or for emailed news updates. In consideration of use of such Products and Services, in registering and providing such data, you represent and warrant that: the information about yourself is true, accurate, current, and complete. This information is used for purposes such as personalisation and verification.

Cookies

We may track your visit to our site by giving you a “cookie” when you enter. Cookies are pieces of information that Web sites transfer to your computer’s hard drive for record-keeping purposes. World ORT uses cookies to make visiting our site and program sites, easier. Cookies allow us to save passwords and preferences for you so that you do not have to re-enter them the next time you visit. Cookies also help us collect and track anonymous user trends and patterns.

Use of Information

Any information you provide to World ORT when registering for World ORT programmes is maintained and accessible only by World ORT; the information provided by you will be kept confidential. World ORT will never wilfully disclose individually identifiable information about its users to anyone outside of itself without first receiving that users permission, thus preventing this information from appearing in unauthorised mailings and other solicitations.

Its e-commerce vendors, WorldPay and PayPal, will also collect personal information which will only be transfered over a secure socket layer (SSL). They also will also keep all personal data confidential and will not disclose it to anyone or use it for marketing purposes.

Your Consent To This Agreement

By using World ORT’s Web sites, you consent to the collection and use of information by World ORT as specified above. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.
