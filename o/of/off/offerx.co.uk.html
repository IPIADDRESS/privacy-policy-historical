Privacy

 

Offerx Ltd is registered under the Data Protection Act 1998. The information you provide is safeguarded under the terms of that Act.
 

Data to be Collected and Uses of the Data

Offerx Ltd collects data about you and answers to specific questions asked by sponsors. Offerx Ltd, its clients or sponsors will contact you with offers and information through mail, telephone or e-mail.
 

Information collected in the Registration

Once you have accepted to complete the Registration your information will be transferred at the end of each page (i.e. when you click on the "continue" button) to Offerx Ltd. On clicking the 'play the game' button at the end of the registration you will have "completed" the registration.
 

Offerx Limited ("Offerx ltd") may use other reputable partners to provide its services in connection with the survey. However this will not affect your statutory rights under the Data Protection Act. No other information will be gathered other than that which you have completed on the Registration.
 

Use of your responses

Information will be made available to responsible companies and organisations including charities, political parties and suppliers we engage with. These third parties may wish to contact you by mail, telephone or e-mail for marketing purposes. By completing the registration you accept that these companies may make contact with you.
 

Cookies

When you visit Offerx.co.uk a small text file (known as a 'cookie') will be placed on your computer; the only thing this cookie is used for is to enable us to recognise you when you return to Offerx.co.uk and show you offers which match your interests based on the information you gave us when you registered. The cookie will not allow a website to learn any personally identifiable information (such as your name and address) that you have not otherwise given to us. The cookie will also not track where you go when you leave Offerx.co.uk or where you came to our website from. It's only purpose is to make your visit to Offerx.co.uk easier and more rewarding for you.
 

Deregistration

You may at any time deregister from Offerx Ltd by using the following link: unsubscribe. The consequences of deregistration are that your account details will be placed in a suppression file and you will not receive any further communications from Offerx Ltd
 

OfferX is NOT spam : should you ever decide that you don't want to receive any additional OfferX email, all you need to do in order to cancel your OfferX membership is to click on the unsubscribe link that will appear on every email that we will send you.
 

Further Information

If you have any questions regarding the privacy policy of Offerx Ltd or require any further information please use the Contact Us page.
