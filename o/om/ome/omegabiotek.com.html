    Personal data collected online may also be combined with information you provide Omega Bio-tek, Inc. through other sources such as product registration, call centers or public events such as trade shows or seminars.

    Personal data given to Omega Bio-tek, Inc. may be transferred across state and country borders for the purposes of data consolidation, storage and simplified customer information management.

    Omega Bio-tek, Inc. collects and uses your personal information to operate the Omega Bio-tek, Inc. website and deliver the services you have requested. Omega Bio-tek, Inc. also uses your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from Omega Bio-tek, Inc. Omega Bio-tek, Inc. may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

    Omega Bio-tek, Inc. does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to Omega Bio-tek, Inc., and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

    Omega Bio-tek, Inc. does not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations, without your explicit consent.

    Omega Bio-tek, Inc. keeps track of the websites and pages our customers visit within Omega Bio-tek, Inc., in order to determine what Omega Bio-tek, Inc. services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within Omega Bio-tek, Inc. to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

    Omega Bio-tek, Inc. websites will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Omega Bio-tek, Inc. or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Omega Bio-tek, Inc.; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Omega Bio-tek, Inc., or the public.

    Use of Cookies

    The Omega Bio-tek, Inc. website use “cookies” to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.

    One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalize Omega Bio-tek, Inc. pages, or register with Omega Bio-tek, Inc. site or services, a cookie helps Omega Bio-tek, Inc. to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses, and so on. When you return to the same Omega Bio-tek, Inc. website, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the Omega Bio-tek, Inc. features that you customized.

    You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the Omega Bio-tek, Inc. services or websites you visit.

    Security of your Personal Information

    Omega Bio-tek, Inc. secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. Omega Bio-tek, Inc. secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to our online financial institution, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol.
