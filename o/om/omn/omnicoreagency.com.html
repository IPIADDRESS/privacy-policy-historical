How Cookies Work

A “cookie” is a small text file that a web site can place on your computer’s hard drive in order, for example, to collect technical information about your activities on the site e.g. make it possible for you to use an online “shopping cart” to keep track of items you wish to purchase. The “cookie” transmits this information back to the web site’s computer that, generally speaking, is the only computer that can read it. You may set your web browser to warn you whenever a site attempts to place a cookie on your computer or refuse to accept cookies. Omnicore Agency will use cookies collected during your visit to this site for the purpose of facilitating your use of our site and for maintaining statistics concerning use of our site that will help us make improvements. The other purpose of a cookie is to tell the web server that you have returned to a specific page, so that when you visit the site again, the experience will be personalized. Technical information collected by cookies will not be linked to your personal information. We do not have access to your computer or any other information about you through the use of cookies. Third party vendors, including Google, use first-party and third-party cookies to serve ads based on a user’s prior visits to Omnicore Agency’s website. Users may opt out of Google’s use of cookies by visiting the Google advertising opt-out page.
