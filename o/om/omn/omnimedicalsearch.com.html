Overview

OmniMedicalSearch.com has no interest, financial motivation or desire to compromise the privacy of our search users.

OmniMedicalSearch.com does not collect personally identifiable information on any of it's users. Nor does it place cookies or information on your computer's hard drive with the exception of our BookMark option which adds our link to your Favorites Folder, or our search toolbar.

Cookies & Web Beacons

A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user’s computer tied to information about the user. Our website does not use cookies. However, Google Adsense, our Third Party advertising partner, may use cookies via our website. We have no access to or control over these cookies, once we have given permission for them to set cookies for advertising.

The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site.

Log Files

Like most standard Web site servers we collect and use log files. This includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses, etc. are not linked to personally identifiable information.

Though our traffic monitoring devices, OmniMedicalSearch.com does temporarily collect information about users to include:search terms in which they entered our website, IP address, referring website or search engine, browser type and settings.

Advertisers

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.

The DoubleClick DART cookie is used by Google in the ads served on publisher websites displaying AdSense for content ads. When users visit an AdSense publisher's website and either view or click on an ad, a cookie may be dropped on that end user's browser. The data gathered from these cookies will be used to help AdSense publishers better serve and manage the ads on their site(s) and across the web.

Aspects of the DoubleClick DART cookie: Include:

  • Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on publisher's websites.
  • Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to OmniMedicalSearch.com and other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.
