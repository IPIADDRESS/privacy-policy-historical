Upon logging on to OtakuZone such things as your IP address and hostname are logged for statistical and security reasons.

  • 1.  Cookies

    A cookie is a very small text file placed on your system upon logging on to OtakuZone (and most other websites). This file serves as your identification card and is uniquely yours, and can only be read by the server that gave it to you. Cookies tell us that you have returned to a specific web page on OtakuZone and help us track your preferences and transactional habits. The basic function of cookies is to help our server remember who you are.

If you are under 13, please do not send any information about yourself to us, including your name, address, telephone number, or email address. No one under age 13 is allowed to provide any personal information to or on OtakuZone. In the event that we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under age 13 without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.
