Oki Electric Industry Co., Ltd. (hereinafter called "Oki") properly handle personal information (the information which can identify an individual - hereinafter referred to as "Personal Information") which Oki may collect through questionnaire format or e-mail in Oki's web site (http://www.oki.com - hereinafter referred to as "Oki's Website") in accordance with the Privacy Policy stated below. Provided that Oki is not responsible for any privacy practices of third party's web site which link with Oki's Website.

  1. Collection of Personal Information

    Whenever Oki asks for Personal Information, Oki in advance notify you of the purposes of collection, usage of Personal Information and contact information. You may choose not to provide information. If you so choose, you may not be able to use certain services in Oki's Website.

  2. Protection of Personal Information

    Oki protects the Personal Information provided to Oki using security measures for preventing unauthorized access, loss, dissemination and improper alteration.

  3. Use of Personal Information

    Oki does not use the Personal Information provided to Oki for any other purpose than those in advance notified to individuals. In the event Oki desires to use the Personal Information for the additional purposes not notified to individuals, Oki will notify each individual of such additional purposes in advance. Any individual may refuse Oki's use of his/her own Personal Information for such additional purposes.
    Oki may statistically analyze Oki's Web Side but Personal Information will not be contained nor used in such analysis.

  4. Disclosure of Personal Information

    Oki does not disclose the Personal Information provided to Oki to any third party except for the following event:

    • When the person providing his/her Personal Information consent to the disclosure
    • When the realization of the purposes notified to individual requires Oki to disclose the Personal Information to the third party who entered into non-disclosure agreement with Oki
    • When an inquiry to Oki is better responded by Oki's affiliates or distributors
    • When the disclosure of the Personal Information is required under laws and/or regulations

  5. Use of Cookies

    Cookie means small packets of data transmitted from web server to browsers of individuals which may be stored on the disks of individuals as a file. Cookie may identify computers of individuals but cannot identify Personal Information.

    Oki may use cookie in order to provide better services by customizing Web site suitable for each individual's purposes. You may set your browser to refuse cookies. If you do so, you may not be able to use certain services in Oki's Website.

  6. Compliance with Laws and Regulations

    Oki complies with laws, regulations and other rules applicable to the protection of Personal Information provided to Oki.
