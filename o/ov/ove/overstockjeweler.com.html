Privacy Policy

ORDER PROCESSING

When you place an order, you are required to provide standard personal information on our order form such as contact information such as name, phone number, email address, shipping address and financial information (credit card information). This information is used for billing purposes and to process orders and is kept secured in protected system. If required, Overstockjeweler.com may use this contact info to get in touch with you regarding your order. When placing an order, your information is used for these purposes: credit card verification, order processing, contact regarding order status, anonymous traffic monitoring.

Overstockjeweler.com does not sell, lease, or rent your email address without your permission.
