How Do We Use your Information?

We may use the information we collect from you in the following ways:

  • To personalize your Website experience and to allow us to deliver the type of content and product offerings which are of interest to you.
  • To quickly process your purchases.
  • To allow us to respond efficiently to your service requests.
  • To administer a contest, promotion, survey, product review or other Website feature.
  • If you have "opted-in" to receive email communications from us, to send you our newsletter or promotional material. If you would no longer like to receive promotional email from us, please refer to the "How Can You Modify or Delete Your Account Information or Opt-out of Receiving Promotional Emails?" section below.

How Do We Protect Personal Information?

Although we cannot guarantee the impenetrability of our servers, we have established and implemented reasonable physical, electronic, and administrative procedures to safeguard and secure the personal information we collect online.

This Web site may contain customer reviews, forums and/or message boards.  Please remember that any information disclosed in these areas becomes public information, and you should exercise caution when considering the disclosure of your personal information.  You agree that Company is not responsible for material posted by you or others in these public forums.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to third parties your personally identifiable information unless we provide you with advance notice or also as described below:

We will disclose personal information to third party consultants and service providers (such as providers of hosting, support, maintenance and remedial and repair services) to the extent that they require access to our databases, or the information contained in our databases, to service Company and the Website.

We reserve the right to release personal information (i) when we are under legal compulsion to do so (e.g. we have received a subpoena) or we otherwise believe that the law requires us to do so, (ii) when we believe it is necessary to protect and/or enforce the rights, property interests, or safety of the Company, our users or others, or (iii) as we deem necessary to resolve disputes, troubleshoot problems, prevent fraud and otherwise enforce the Privacy Statement and Website Terms of Use.

  • As Otherwise Allowed by Law. We may transfer personal information to third parties where we are expressly authorized by applicable law to do so.

If you registered for an account, you may log into your account at any time and modify, delete or update your information and change your preferences such as your email subscriptions.  Please note that due to email production schedules you may receive any emails already in production.  Additionally, you can unsubscribe from promotional emails by following the instructions contained in the email.
