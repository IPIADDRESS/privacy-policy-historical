Concerning Your Privacy

Open Source Web Design does not distribute your personal information to other parties.

The information you provide us with (ie. when registering for a user account) stays with us. We will never know your user password since it will be encrypted before being stored on our servers. We will take reasonable measures to ensure your email address does not get spammed. We may place cookies on your computer to improve the functionality of the site. These cookies will not store personal information about you and will not be used in a malacious way.

Questions or Concerns

Email any questions you have about Open Source Web Design's Privacy Policy to staff@oswd.org.
