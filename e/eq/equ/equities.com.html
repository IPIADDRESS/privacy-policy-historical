Article Writing Tips

Choosing a Topic:

When selecting a topic to write about, consider what aspects will make your article unique to the reader. Provide your own perspective or analysis (fundamental, technical, proprietary, etc.) to major stories that are being covered by the news media, or write about trends or opportunities that the rest of the investment community is missing.

Crafting a Title:

A compelling title will attract more readers. Be direct and concise with your word choice. State the point your article wants to make, but will also stimulate discussion with your readers. Do not mislead readers with deceiving headlines.

Writing Your Article:

Use headers, subheaders, bullet points, etc. to help organize your points. Cite your sources by hyperlinking text in your articles, preferably to other articles written by Equities.com writers.

Using Images:

We encourage the use of charts, graphs, tables, etc. whenever suitable. Writers can also include other relevant images for topical articles, so long as they have the sufficient rights to do so.

Tags/Stock Tickers:

Be sure to include ticker symbols mentioned in the story in all caps. Example: GOOG, AAPL
