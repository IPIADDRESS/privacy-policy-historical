Privacy Policy

What information do we collect?

When you register on our site, purchase a product, subscribe to our newsletter, respond to a survey or fill out a form, you may be asked to provide your name, e-mail address, or credit card information. However, you can visit our site anonymously.

What do we use your information for?

Any of the information we collect from you may be used in one of the following ways:

  • To personalize your experience and improve our website

Your information helps us give you the content that’s best for you, and create new courses and lessons based on your needs and interests.

  • To process transactions

Your information is used only for the purpose of processing the payment and delivering the purchased product.

  • To send periodic emails

When you sign up for English tips by e-mail or register for a course, you will receive e-mail newsletters with new lessons, new courses, special offers, and other updates.

If you would like to unsubscribe from receiving future emails, you can click the “Unsubscribe” link at the bottom of any email.

How do we protect your information?

All credit card information is transmitted through a secure (SSL) connection and processed with a safe payment processor such as PayPal, PagSeguro, Clickbank, or Gumroad. We do not keep any payment information on our servers.

Do we use cookies?

Yes. Cookies are small text files that a site transfers to your computer through your Web browser. Cookies enable websites to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information.

We use cookies together with Google Analytics and the Google Remarketing Program to collect anonymous information that helps us measure page popularity and show you advertisements that are in line with your interests. Other websites may display Espresso English ads based on the pages you have visited on the Espresso English website, but we do not share any of your personally identifiable information with these other sites.

If you prefer, you can choose to have your computer warn you each time a cookie is being sent, or you can choose to turn off all cookies via your browser settings. You can also opt out of Google’s use of cookies by visiting the Ads Preferences Manager.

Do we share any personal information with outside parties?

No – Espresso English does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to other companies, except for trusted companies that help us operate our website and process payments, as long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

Non-personally identifiable visitor information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other uses.

Third party links

Our website may include links to other websites or products. These third party sites have separate and independent privacy policies, so we have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites.

Your Consent

By using our site, you consent to our website’s privacy policy.

Changes to our Privacy Policy

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page, and/or update the Privacy Policy modification date below.
