Privacy Policy

At Essential Baby we want you to be able to spend time on our site without having to worry about the privacy and security of your personal information.

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy after reading the following privacy information, please feel free to contact us at customerservice@essentialbaby.com.au.

Are my credit Card details secure?

The Essential Baby shop uses 128-bit SSL encryption ensuring the highest security of your credit card details and personal information. Once your order has been processed, your order records are removed from our server once processed and only receipt details are retained for our banking records. This receipt information is stored in a secure location.

What happens to my personal information when I visit the site?

When you visit the essential baby site, we track your visit using a randomly generated number, which tracks your visit you through the site. At no time during your visit is your personal information stored on the browser. Some basic information is collected from your visit to assist us in understanding how many visitors have been to the site and basic information such as where those visitors are from, what pages they are visiting and what browser they are using. We do not store any personal information or email addresses unless you register to become part of our Bulletin Board discussion groups.

What happens to my personal information if I register for the Bulletin Boards or Diaries?

When you register for the Essential Baby Bulleting Board, we retain your personal information in a secure ocation to enable us to notify you of updates to the bulleting boards and to forward resposnes to you. At no time will your personal information be supplied to any third party. Shouldany other bulleting board member wish to contact you, we will request that they post the request to you via the bulletin board. We will not supply your personal details to any third party under any circumstances.
