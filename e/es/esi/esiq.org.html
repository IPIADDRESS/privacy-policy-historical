Privacy Policy

eSiq.org enforces a policy to protect your privacy while on our website.This policy covers three key areas:

Cookies

We do not use any cookies on eSiq.org, however the website is not responsible for the cookies used by third party advertisers on our site.

Personal Information

eSiq.org collects personal information only for the purpose of responding to your enquires via the contact form on our Contact Us page. This information will be discarded once our correspondence with you is completed. Under no circumstance will we share your information with third party organizations. Your personal information is confidential. As a user to eSiq.org, you have the right to access any personal information that Boshuda.com stores. You can contact us for corrections or deletions to this information. In respect to the privacy of yourself and other parties, personal identification might need to verified before access to information can be altered.

Non-Personal Information

We collect anonymous usage data in the form of IP addresses and browser data. This data is used for site performance statistics, and is not used to identify you personally.
