    1. eStoreBuilder.com does not sell, rent or disclose your personal information to anyone else, except:
    2. 5(a). to someone you have designated to act as your agent, for one or more of the Identified Purposes (listed in Section 2, above);

    4. 5(c). as necessary if eStoreBuilder.com has reason to believe that disclosure is necessary to identify, contact or bring legal action against someone who may be causing injury to or interference (either intentionally or unintentionally) with eStoreBuilder.com' rights or property, other users of eStoreBuilder.com' web site, products or services, or anyone else that could be harmed by such activities; and
    5. 5(d). to respond to judicial process and provide information to law enforcement agencies or in connection with an investigation on matters related to public safety, as permitted by law, or otherwise as required by law.
