Privacy policy

Eslbase.com is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the security of any personal information received from you. We strictly adhere to the requirements of data protection legislation in the UK. The purpose of this statement is to explain to you what personal information we collect and how we may use it.

What information do we collect about you?
When you register with us we ask you for your name and email address, and to tell us whether you are a teacher or an employer / training centre. We do not collect sensitive information about you except when you specifically knowingly provide it. You can check, update or remove your personal details by signing into your account.

We also use cookies (see below for details) and collect IP addresses (an IP address is a number that can uniquely identify a specific computer or other network device on the internet) from visitors to Eslbase.

How do we use this information?
We use this information to give you the best possible service. We may contact you from time to time to give you information about eslbase. However, we will only contact you if you consent to us doing so during the registration process.

We do not sell, rent or exchange your personal information with any third party for commercial reasons.

Cookies
A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a website's computer and is stored on your hard drive. We use cookies to analyse traffic flows on Eslbase and provide our visitors with the best possible service. You can choose to disallow cookies by changing the relevant setting in your browser.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit eslbase. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and would like to know your options in relation to·not having this information used by these companies, click here. Google, as one of these third-party vendors, uses the DART cookie to serve advertisements. You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

Data protection
We follow strict security procedures in the storage and disclosure of information which you have given us, to prevent unauthorised access in accordance with UK data protection legislation.

Other information you provide
If you add a comment to any of the pages on Eslbase, the information you provide will be available for other visitors to see. If you are an employer / training centre and post a job advert or add information about your training course or school, this information, including any contact details you provide, will be available for visitors to see.

Specific privacy information for the forum
You can also register on the forum. In this case we will ask you for a username and an email address. Once you have registered you can choose to provide more information about yourself in your account area, for example your interests, birth date and location. In all cases, you have the option of what information in your account is publicly displayed. You also have the option to opt-in or opt-out of automatically generated emails from the phpBB software which powers the forum.

When you browse the forum, the phpBB software will create a number of cookies. The first two cookies contain a userid and an anonymous session id, automatically assigned to you by the phpBB software. A third cookie is created when you browse topics within the forum and is used to store which topics have been read, to improve your user experience.

Links to third party websites
Eslbase.com may include links to third party websites that are controlled and maintained by others. It is your responsibility to review the privacy policies of these sites. Eslbase.com is not responsible for the policies and practices of third party sites.

More information
You can find more information about UK data protection legislation at the Information Commissioner's website. If you have any questions about this privacy policy please contact us at eslbase@eslbase.com or click here.
