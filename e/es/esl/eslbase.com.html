your privacy

eslbase.com is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the security of any personal information received from you. We strictly adhere to the requirements of data protection legislation in the UK.

The purpose of this statement is to explain to you what personal information we collect and how we may use it.

1  When you register with us we ask you for your name and email address, and to tell us whether you are a teacher or an employer / training centre. We use this information to give you the best possible service, and we may contact you from time to time to give you information about eslbase. However, we will only contact you if you consent to us doing so during the registration process.

2  We do not sell, rent or exchange your personal information with any third party for commercial reasons.

3  We follow strict security procedures in the storage and disclosure of information which you have given us, to prevent unauthorised access in accordance with UK data protection legislation.

4  We do not collect sensitive information about you except when you specifically knowingly provide it. In order to maintain the accuracy of our database, you can check, update or remove your personal details by clicking here to sign in to your account.

5  If you add a comment to any of the pages on eslbase, the information you provide will be available for other visitors to see.

6  If you are a teacher and post your CV, the information you provide, including any personal information or contact details, will be available for member employers to see.

7  If you are an employer / training centre and post a job advert or add information about your training course or school, this information, including any contact details you provide, will be available for visitors to see.

8  You can find more information about UK data protection legislation at the Information Commissioner's website.

9  If you have any questions about privacy please contact us at eslbase@eslbase.com or click here.
