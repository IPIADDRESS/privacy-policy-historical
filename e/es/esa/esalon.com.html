Cookies

Cookies are bits of text or identifiers that enable a website to "remember" you, either for the duration of your visit, a "session cookie," or for repeat visits (a "persistent cookie"). Cookies enable you to navigate between pages efficiently, storing your preferences, remembering what is in your shopping cart, and generally enhancing your shopping experience by making your visit faster and easier. If we did not put cookies on your hard drive through your browser, you would appear to be as new visitor each time you came to the Site, and you would not remain logged in if you have an account. Moreover, because we use cookies, if you return to the Site to reorder products, you may not have to answer questions that you answered on prior visits (e.g., your preferred shipping address). The Help function on the toolbar of most browsers will tell you how to prevent your browser from accepting new cookies, how to have the browser notify you when you receive a new cookie, and how to disable cookies altogether. However, because cookies allow you to take advantage of some of eSalon's most important features, we recommend that you leave them turned on. For instance, if you block or otherwise reject our cookies, each shopping experience on the Site may feel like the first time you made a purchase on the Site and entered all of the information we required.

There may be cookies on our Site placed by other websites who run content on the page you are visiting ("third party cookies") and we have no access or control over these cookies. This privacy statement covers the use of cookies by eSalon only ("first party cookies") and does not cover the use of cookies by third parties.
