Our Collection and Use of Your Personally Identifiable Information.

Because of the nature of our business our products and services are not designed to appeal to minors, we do not knowingly attempt to solicit or receive any information from children.

It will be necessary for you to provide your birthday, social security number, employment, income and related tax payment information for our software to compute and complete your tax return for e-file. We use tax return information only in accordance with applicable laws and in order to provide the tax return and electronic filing or other services you have requested. Except as described herein we do not disclose tax return information to third parties nor use it to market third parties' products and services. For our records, we may, in our sole discretion, retain original and updated information for reasons such as technical constraints, dispute resolution, troubleshooting and agreement enforcement.

Information You Provide To Us
Before you can use the Services (as defined in the User Agreement) on the Site, we ask that you register with C&S and provide to us your Username, e-mail address, Password, Name, Address, Phone Number, Password Prompt Question and Answer. We will send you service-related announcements on rare occasions when it is necessary to do so. For instance, we might send you an e-mail announce the start of the IRS filing season and our service each year, the filing status and result for your tax return, actions required to complete your e-file, or if our service is temporarily suspended for maintenance.

Cookies
Cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to a user's hard drive for record-keeping purposes. Like many websites, we use cookies at our Site. Cookies can make using the Internet easier by, among other things, saving your passwords and preferences for you. For example, a cookie lets the Site remember that you've registered, which allows us to speed up your future activities at our Site, and which allows you to enter your password/log in ID less frequently when visiting our Site. Cookies allow us to track the user's activities and to determine the customer's preference. You may choose to decline the cookie, however by doing so, you may be limited to access some parts of the website. We may also use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests. There are products commercially available that are designed to help you disable cookies. Although we want you to be aware that such products exist, please note that if you choose to use them we may not be able to provide you with personalized service, your use of the Site may be slowed, and it may result in certain features and services not being available to you. We do not link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information you submit while on our site.

Surveys & Contests
From time-to-time, our Site may request information from you via surveys or contests. Participation in these surveys or contests is completely voluntary and you may choose whether or not to disclose this information. Information requested may include but is not limited to contact information (such as name and shipping address), and demographic information (such as zip code, age level).

We may receive information about you from other sources, such as our agents, vendors and service providers and from publicly available sources. For example, sometimes we receive updated address information from third parties that we may use to update our records.
