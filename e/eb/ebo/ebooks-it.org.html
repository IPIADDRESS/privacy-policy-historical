Security

The connection to the website eBooks-IT.org is encrypted. We care about protecting your privacy.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your computer.A cookie can contain text, numbers, dates etc., but it does not hold any personal information. A cookie is not a program and it cannot contain a virus.
First Party Cookies
Cookies set by eBooks-IT.org
eBooks-IT.org uses captcha on contact and upload pages to prevent bot generated spam. The cookie created by the captcha code is a part of the captcha for the contact and upload forms for each property. eBooks-IT.org uses cookies to monitor the general performance of visitors' expirience on this website, which help us to improve the usability of this site.
Third Party Cookies
Cookies set by third party advertisers
eBooks-IT.org uses third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit this site. These companies may use non-personally identifiable information (e.g., click stream information, browser type, time and date, subject of advertisements clicked or scrolled over) during your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services likely to be of greater interest to you. These companies typically use a cookie or third party web beacon to collect this information.
