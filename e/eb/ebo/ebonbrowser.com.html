We use your Personal Information mainly to provide the Services and to respond to your requests for support.

How We Use Your Information

We don't sell your Personal Information. We use your Personal Information (in some cases, in conjunction with your Non-Identifying Information) to provide you with better services and improve Ebon, and to respond to your requests for support. Your Non-Identifying Information, such as device ID's and profile information can be made available by Ebon and its partners to help target ads to you. NON-PII collection/storage

Ebon browser collects anonymous non personal information, including, but not limited to: cookies, browser language and versions data, IP and MAC addresses, geolocation data, browser history. This information may be used by us and shared with Ebon browser Partners in order to better serve your needs and provide you with relevant content and advertisement. Among the kinds of information we track, without limitation, are the following.

  • Which Ebon features you use and how often (for instance, how often you use the privacy button, share button, search feature, etc.);
  • The amount of time that Ebon runs and the amount of time it is active;
  • Ebon crashes and associated conditions around the time of the crash;
  • What you've selected for certain Ebon preferences and options;
  • Which other Internet browsers run on your system, and which browser is set as the default browser; If you choose to use it as default browser, Ebon will automatically import existing preferences, bookmarks, history, passwords, cookies and favorites from your current browser for your convenience
  • Your screen resolution, operating system version, CPU and memory configuration of your machine, and other configuration information;
  • The number of tabs you have open;
  • Which partners and pages you visit so we can better tailor and offer you customized rewards;

Log Data

When you visit the Ebon Website (like www.ebonbrowser.com), whether you're using Ebon, or as a non-registered user just visiting, our servers automatically record information your browser sends whenever you visit a website ("Log Data"). This data is collected whether or not you use Ebon, but only when you visit our website. This Log Data may include information such as your computer's Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser type, the web page you were visiting before you came to our Sites, pages of our websites that you visit, the time spent on those pages, information you search for on our websites, access times and dates, and other statistics. We use this information to monitor and analyze use of the websites and the Service and for the websites technical administration, to increase our websites functionality and user-friendliness, and to better tailor it to our visitors' needs. We also use this information to verify that visitors to the websites meet the criteria required to process their requests.
