Privacy Policy

Ebookmarket.org (“Ebookmarket.org”) operates the Ebookmarket.org. It is Ebookmarket.org’s policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our websites.

Website Visitors

Like most website operators, Ebookmarket.org collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Ebookmarket.org’s purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Ebookmarket.org’s visitors use its website. From time to time, Ebookmarket.org may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

Ebookmarket.org also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses. Ebookmarket.org does not use such information to identify its visitors, however, and does not disclose such information, other than under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information, as described below.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain visitors to Ebookmarket.org’s websites choose to interact with Ebookmarket.org in ways that require Ebookmarket.org to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that Ebookmarket.org gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we may in the future require users who wish to sign up for an Account with Ebookmarket.org to provide a username and email address. Those who engage in transactions with Ebookmarket.org are asked to provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to process those transactions. In each case, Ebookmarket.org collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor’s interaction with Ebookmarket.org. Ebookmarket.org does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

Aggregated Statistics

Ebookmarket.org may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. For instance, Ebookmarket.org may monitor the most popular searches on Ebookmarket.org. Ebookmarket.org may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, Ebookmarket.org does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information

Ebookmarket.org discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on Ebookmarket.org’s behalf or to provide services available at Ebookmarket.org’s websites, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Some of those employees, contractors and affiliated organizations may be located outside of your home country; by using Ebookmarket.org’s websites, you consent to the transfer of such information to them. Ebookmarket.org will not rent or sell potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information to anyone. Other than to its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations, as described above, Ebookmarket.org discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only when required to do so by law, or when Ebookmarket.org believes in good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the property or rights of Ebookmarket.org, third parties or the public at large. If you are a registered user of an Ebookmarket.org website and have supplied your email address, Ebookmarket.org may occasionally send you an email to tell you about new features, solicit your feedback, or just keep you up to date with what’s going on with Ebookmarket.org and our products. We primarily use our various product blogs to communicate this type of information, so we expect to keep this type of email to a minimum. If you send us a request (for example via a support email or via one of our feedback mechanisms), we reserve the right to publish it in order to help us clarify or respond to your request or to help us support other users. Ebookmarket.org takes all measures reasonably necessary to protect against the unauthorized access, use, alteration or destruction of potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information.

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Ebookmarket.org uses cookies to help Ebookmarket.org identify and track visitors, their usage of Ebookmarket.org website, and their website access preferences. Ebookmarket.org visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Ebookmarket.org’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of Ebookmarket.org’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

Privacy Policy Changes

Although most changes are likely to be minor, Ebookmarket.org may change its Privacy Policy from time to time, and in Ebookmarket.org’s sole discretion. Ebookmarket.org encourages visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy. If you have a wordpress.com account, you should also check your blog’s dashboard for alerts to these changes. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.

More

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads and collect information when users visit our site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements on our site, other websites and other forms of media about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, check out http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp
