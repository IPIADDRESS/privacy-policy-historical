Gatherings

eBay, it's representatives, affiliates, agents, employees, and third party partners (hereinafter "eBay") and/or designated team leaders (each a "Team Leader"), may arrange for or publicize physical meetings at venues that include, without limitation, public halls, public parks, private homes or private enterprises (i.e. coffee shops, restaurants, playhouses) for the primary purpose of discussing policy issues and other subjects designed to increase awareness of issues impacting the eBay marketplace (a "Gathering(s)"). Neither eBay nor Team Leaders supervise these Gatherings and are not involved in any way with the actions of any individuals at these Gatherings. As a result, neither eBay nor Team Leaders exercise any control over the identity or actions of the individuals who are present at these Gatherings. All participants should exercise caution and good judgment when attending a Gathering.

Because we do not supervise the Gatherings between users and other attendees, and because we are not involved in any way with the physical transportation to and from these Gatherings or with the actions of any individuals at these Gatherings, you agree to release eBay (and its officers, directors, shareholders, agents, employees, affiliates, subsidiaries, and third party partners) from claims, demands, and damages (actual and consequential) of every kind and nature arising out of or in any way connected with your participation in Gatherings or your transportation to or from, attendance at, or the actions of you or other persons at, a Gathering.

Communication and Gathering Team Leader

You acknowledge and agree that eBay and/or a Team Leader may use your Information to contact you and deliver information to you (i.e. topic group emails, newsletters or announcements, administrative notices, phone calls) and other communications relevant to Gatherings or the advancement of issues impacting the eBay marketplace. You also acknowledge and agree that your email address may be included in mass-distribution lists and that neither eBay nor a Team Leader shall be held responsible for any third party's use of your email address obtained therefrom.

You understand and agree that you may receive communications from a Team Leader in the normal course of your participation in Gatherings. By voluntarily attending a gathering and providing your Information, or joining any efforts organized in connection with a Gathering, you agree to receive communications from eBay and/or the Team Leader. If you are a Team Leader, you agree to receive messages from individual members of your team and that you shall not use Information for your own purposes or purposes not related to Gatherings or the advancement of issues impacting the eBay marketplace.

Neither eBay nor Team Leaders necessarily control or censor the information provided by participants at Gatherings or in communications.
