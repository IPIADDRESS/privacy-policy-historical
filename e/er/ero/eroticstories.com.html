Privacy Statement for EroticStories.com

Artware Internet Consultancy, the owner of EroticStories.com, has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: EroticStories.com.

This privacy statement has two sections:

1. A section describing practises that apply to users of the free section of the site.

2. A section that describes practises that apply to members of the member section ("Fanclub") of the site.

1. The FREE section of EroticStories.com

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. We use IP addresses and cookies as an aid to detect visitors who vote more than once for a certain story.

Our site's notification service requires users to give us their email address. We use this email address to send users notifications of newly added stories on the site. This is only done at the user's explicit request, and after the email address has been verified. Users may opt-out of this service at any time from this URL: http://www.eroticstories.com/notify/remove.phtml or by emailing the webmaster (art@artware.net).

This site contains links to other sites. EroticStories.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

Authors who submit stories to the site are required to give their real name and email address. This information will be only used when EroticStories.com needs to contact the author, for example in case of copyright issues. This information will never be given out to a third party, except when required by law and when breach of copyright was detected.

Authors can also supply an email address to be displayed alongside the stories they wish to publish (the "public" email address). However, this is not required. When supplied, this is considered public information. Although we will never supply a third party with a list of these email addresses, we obviously cannot keep anyone from collecting this information from the website.

Public Forums

This site makes chat rooms, forums, message boards, and/or news groups available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.

Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. No access to the databases is granted to any third party. In fact, no access to any part of the site or server is granted to any third party.

Correct/Update

Authors may email the webmaster at any time to change the contact information they provided when they submitted the story. The email address to use is art@artware.net. In addition, authors have the option to remove a story from the site (and the database) completely from the following URL: http://www.eroticstories.com/delsearch.phtml

2. The FANCLUB section of EroticStories.com

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. We use the provided username/password to keep track of the voting process and prevent users from voting twice for a story.

Our site's notification service requires users to give us their email address. We use this email address to send users notifications of newly added stories on the site. This is only done at the user's explicit request. Users may opt-out of this service at any time.

This site contains links to other sites. EroticStories.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

Our site uses an order form for customers to purchase a membership to the Fanclub. We collect visitor's contact information (like their email address) and financial information (like their credit card numbers). Contact information from the order form is used to verify the credit card. Financial information that is collected is used to bill the user for products and services.

For authors who wish to publish stories in the Fanclub section, identification is required. This can be done by providing EroticStories.com with the details of a valid credit card, or by sending a copy of a driver's license or passport to the webmaster. All information gathered by EroticStories.com this way is strictly confidential. It will only be used for this one purpose (verifying the identity of the author) and will not be used for anything else, nor will this information be given out to a third party under any circumstances, with the exception of a proven breach of copyright (or other) laws.

Authors can also supply an email address to be displayed alongside the stories they wish to publish (the "public" email address). However, this is not required. When supplied, this is considered public information. Although we will never supply a third party with a list of these email addresses, we obviously cannot keep anyone from collecting this information from the website.

Public Forums

This site makes chat rooms, forums, message boards, and/or news groups available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.

Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. No access to the databases is granted to any third party. In fact, no access to any part of the site or server is granted to any third party. Financial information is only transmitted to financial istitutions using strong encryption to ensure the highest level of security.
