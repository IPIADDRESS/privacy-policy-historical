Privacy Policy

Erik's Bike and Board Guarantee


Erik's Bike and Board will not sell, share, trade, or give away personal information that is received regarding our customers. We are committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect about you to process orders and to provide a more personalized shopping experience.
Please read on for more details about Erik's Bike and Board's privacy policy.

What information do we collect? How do we use it?

We collect the domain name of e-mail addresses of visitors to our site and aggregate (not personally identifiable) information on what pages visitors access. We use this information to monitor the use of the site and to improve its performance and usefulness. We also collect information you volunteer, such as survey responses, comments, purchasing and billing information, and your requests.

When you order, we need to know your name, e-mail address, mailing address, credit card number, and expiration date. This allows us to process and fulfill your order and to notify you of your order status.

We may also use the information we collect to occasionally notify you about important functionality changes to the Web site, new services, and special offers we think you'll find valuable.

How do we protect customer information?

When you place orders or access your account information, we offer the use of a secure server. The secure server software (SSL) encrypts all information you input before it is sent to us. Furthermore, all of the customer data we collect is protected against unauthorized access.

How do we allow customers to update or change the information we collect?

You may update or change information related to your account by updating your profile, accessible via the home page once you are logged in, or by sending us email. Yes, we do use cookies, but we do not overuse them! We use cookies to enhance your shopping and browsing experience. The text in a cookie, by itself, only tells us that you have visited our site before. It does not store any personal information such as your name, e-mail address, street address, or phone number. Cookies are also used to maintain your shopping basket. All data contained in the shopping basket is kept on our server and not in the cookie your machine is assigned.

Your consent

By using our website, you consent to the collection and use of this information. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.

Welcome to the Web site for Erik's Bike Shop. Erik's Bike Shop is the Midwest's Bike & Snowboard experts. The #1 dealer for Specialized and Raleigh. Erik's Bike Shop offers the best selection of Bicycles, cycling apparel, car racks, cycling or spin shoes, sometimes called bike shoes, cycling or bike pedals, bike helmets, protective gear, like head covers, arm, knee or leg warmers and even calientoes (these are the best), bike parts, hydration and nutrition products, cycling, skateboarding or snowboarding DVDs, books and bike maps too, plus, bike lights, and reflective apparel or gear for safety on the road, variety of tires and tubes (which we recycle free of charge), wheels and rims, all types of bike and snowboard tools, bike racks, car racks, packs for your bike and packs for your back, locks, saddles, computers and heart rate monitors to keep track of your training and health, indoor cycles and bike trainers for the cold days, pumps of all types, frame pumps, or floor pumps, even shock pumps and we also can help out with trying to store all this cool stuff - storage aids for the basement or the garage, child carriers or trailers for your children or your pets.

Erik's Bike Shop has been helping peole find the right bike for over 30 years in the Twin Cities, and recently expanded to Rochester, St. Cloud and Madison, Wisconsin (WI). We pride ourselves on knowing that our bike shop is not only a business in the community, but also a partner in that community. In Burnsville, Eden Prarire, St. Louis Park, Maple Grove, Coon Rapids, Dinkytown, Roseville, Vadnais Heights, Woodbury, Bloomington, Rochester, St. Cloud, we provide gift cards and waterbottles to various children's charities, local school fundraisers and hospital or clinic fundraisers. We host Boy Scout or Girl Scout fundraisers (from August to March), offer bicycle education classes and clinics to help ensure people are riding their bikes safely. Plus, we support over 40 bike, skateboard and snowboard charity rides, races and fundraisers with our mechanical bike or event sponsorship. Erik's Bike Shop supports the sports we sell everyday. This year, we are excited to be the Title sponsor for the Ironman Bike ride and we are a sponsor for the Twin Cities Komen Race for the Cure.

People often times have a hard time spelling Erik's Bike Shop, people ask is it Erick's Bike Shop, Erik Bicycle Shop, Eric's Bike Shop or Ericke's bike Shop. Erik the Bikeman, owner of Erik's Bike Shop, has been asked often how to spell out his name - it is amazing for such a short name, how many different ways you can spell Erik.

A few years ago, Erik the Bikeman decided to expand his business to offer not only bikes and skateboards, but also snowboards. Erik's Bike and Board as a name began to be used, but people still know us as Erik's Bike Shop. When there is snow on the ground, you can find snowboards at Erik's Bike Shop, great brands like Forum, Ride, Foursquare, Morrow, DC Boots, 32, World Industries, Flow, Sessions, Special Blend, Grenade and Dragon. But the best part, is that now we are selling our snowboards year round, but on our website. Year round, Erik's Bike Shop sells snowboard, boots, bindings, helmets, goggles, pants, jackets, gloves, everything you need to get out and ride, whereever it is snowing, online. Buy online all year through our website. PLus, we have expanded this now to a variety of our bicycle products as well. So, you can stop by anyone of our shops or buy online 24 hours a day. Now that is cool.
