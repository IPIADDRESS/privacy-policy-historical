Eolss Publishers Co Ltd will ensure that any information it collects will be adequate, relevant and not excessive for the purposes of operating EOLSS-Online; it will be kept accurate and up-to-date based on information provided and will be deleted when no longer needed. We may use "cookies" to identify you and your access on the EOLSS-Online Site as well as recording usage of the EOLSS-Online Site. A cookie is a piece of information that is sent to your Web browser from a Website and stored on your computer's hard drive. Each time you return to the EOLSS-Online Site, we are able to identify you as a previous or registered visitor. Cookies also enable the traffic to the EOLSS-Online Site to be measured, including the number of user revisits versus new visitors, the time users spend on the Site, and their navigation behavior.
