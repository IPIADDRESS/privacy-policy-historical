  • 3.1 Your Personal Data shall be kept confidential on your secure database.

    • 3.3.1 click on the "Unsubscribe" link in the emails we have sent you (if any); or

If you have any queries about how we use your personal data, please contact us by writing to Egress Software Technologies Limited at Unit 16, Quadrant Business Centre, 135 Salusbury Road, London NW6 6RJ or email us at info@egress.com
