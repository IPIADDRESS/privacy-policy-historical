In cyberspace, cookies are pieces of information that a Web site transfers to an individual’s hard drive for record-keeping purposes. The use of cookies is an industry standard-you’ll find them at most major Web sites. EpicCarnival.com, it’s subsidiaries and affiliates, only use cookies to make your overall user experience better. Any information that is collected by us is strictly for use within EpicCarnival.com for the purpose of improving our service for our customers.
