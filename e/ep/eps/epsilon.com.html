Privacy Policy

Epsilon is a leading provider of multichannel marketing services, technologies, database and data solutions. Being proactive in protecting consumer privacy has always been an important focus for Epsilon. We continuously seek ways to protect and assure appropriate use of information related to consumers and to promote policies within the industry that do the same.
 

Privacy Policy for Information Collected at www.Epsilon.com

Epsilon's online privacy practices apply to the collection and use of information gathered online from individuals, prospects and customers. 

 

Epsilon International Privacy Policies

Epsilon does business in many countries, and our privacy policies are shaped by the laws and regulations of the areas of the world in which we have offices.

 

How can I request access to the information Epsilon has about me?

We are happy to provide you with information Epsilon has about your household in our databases. If you would like to request a copy of the information we have about your household, you can complete and return the Epsilon Marketing Data Summary Request Form. More information on the Marketing Data Summary is available in our Consumer Knowledge Center. If you have any issues with the request form, please contact our Privacy Department at 1-888-780-3869.
