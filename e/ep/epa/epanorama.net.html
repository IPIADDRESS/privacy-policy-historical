Personal Data

Non-personally Identifiable Information Collected Automatically

For each visitor to www.epanorama.net, our Web server automatically recognises your domain, the type of Internet browser you are using, the type of computer operating system you are using, and the domain name of the Web site from which you linked to our site. This non-personally identifiable information is collected automatically, used for internal review, then discarded.
