Privacy

eyeReturn provides technology and marketing services to it's clients. In this capacity eyeReturn has been contracted by a client to act as an agent for media delivery or promotional campaign management. The information eyeReturn collects and stores on behalf of its clients is owned by the client, and is not used by eyeReturn or other third parties.

eyeReturn does not release, sell or give your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information without consent. eyeReturn encourages all companies with which we do business (also called "clients"), to engage in fair information practices. eyeReturn is committed to protecting individual's privacy, and being in strict compliance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

Specifically, eyeReturn strongly encourages its clients to:

  • be accountable for the data they collects
  • identify the purposes of any personal data collection
  • obtain consent from individuals prior to collecting personal information
  • reasonably limit the collection of personal data
  • limit the use, disclosure and retention of personal data
  • be accurate in the collection and storing of personal data
  • use appropriate safeguards to protect the integrity and security of the personal data
  • be open about data collection and retention, use and handling practices
  • provide individuals access to their data for review or correction
  • provide a mechanism for data collection complaints or recourse
