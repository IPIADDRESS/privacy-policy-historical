Privacy Policy

Eyecandys.com will never ask for or come into contact with personal credit card or bank information. We use a certified and secure 3rd party agent for money transfer.

This service allows anyone to pay in any way they prefer, including through credit cards, bank accounts, buyer credit or account balances, without sharing financial information with us.

Checkout at EyeCandy's is secured by GeoTrust SSL.  Sites secured by GeoTrust SSL certificates are providing up to 256 bit encryption thereby offering the highest level of encryption and security possible. This means you can rest assured that communications between your browser and this site's web servers are private and secure. 

Should it be necessary, and with your consent, we may require some pieces of information, such as address, name, or phone number, etc. to ensure the delivery of the order. Any information that is received will be kept private and confidential. Eyecandy Lens is committed to keeping your personal information safe.  If you have any further questions, please contact us.
