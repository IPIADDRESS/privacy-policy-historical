Privacy Policy

Our Commitment To Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. This notice describes how we use information you supply to us and applies to all information collected on the EatDrinkDeals website. You are invited and encouraged to register on our site by clicking on the Login link in our top menu bar. The only required information is:

Username
Email address
Password to login to eatdrinkdeals
The Way We Use Information:

We use email addresses to answer the email we receive. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties. If you would like to contribute to our forums, you must register a name and email address. You are not required to register to update our specials information, only to append to our forums.

We never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online without providing you an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise prohibit such unrelated uses.

How You Can Access Or Correct Your Information

You can access all your personal information that we collect by going to the Login page and clicking on User Control Panel.

Should you have questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please send us an email at owner@eatdrinkdeals.com.
