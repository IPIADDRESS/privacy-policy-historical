About EarthLink:
 
Privacy Statement for Earthlink Network
At Earthlink, protecting your privacy is very important. We want to make your experience on the Internet as comfortable and rewarding as possible, and we want you to make use of the Internet’s vast array of information, tools and opportunities with complete confidence.

We’ve created this statement to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy and security, and to let you know about the processes we use. The statement discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices we use on our Web sites: www.earthlink.net, help.earthlink.net, start.earthlink.net, and themall.earthlink.net.

User information
When users visit the pages of the Earthlink sites, they may be asked to give us contact information, such as their names and email addresses. Financial information that is collected is used to bill the user for products and services.

Our sites use order forms for customers to request information, products and services. Contact information from the order forms, such as a user’s email address, is used to send orders and information about our company to our customers

Our online surveys ask users for contact information (such as their email addresses) and demographic information (such as zip codes, age, or income levels). We use contact data from our surveys to send information about our company to our users. We use demographic and profile data to tailor our sites and display content according to our users’ interests and preferences. This information is shared with advertisers only on an aggregate basis. We do not share any personal information about our users. We run contests on our sites in which we ask users for contact information, such as their email addresses. The customer’s contact information is used to contact the user when necessary.

In all cases above, users may opt out of receiving future mailings that may result from providing email addresses. See the Choice to Opt Out section, below.

Our sites may also record the user’s IP address, which helps us diagnose problems with our server and administer our Web site. Your IP address also is used to gather broad demographic information. If you are an Earthlink member and have created your own Personal Start Page, we will use a "cookie" to save your settings and provide your own personalized information. Cookies are tiny data files that are placed on your hard drive when you visit some Web sites. Only information that you specifically supply, such as a user ID, will be included in the cookie.

Public Forums
Please remember that any information that you disclose in Earthlink’s Member Directory, or other public areas of the Internet, becomes public information. You should exercise caution when deciding to disclose personal information.

Security
Our sites have security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.

Children’s Guidelines
1. We do not collect online contact information without prior parental consent or parental notification, which will include an opportunity for the parent to prevent use of the information and participation in the activity. Without prior parental consent, online information will only be used to respond directly to the child’s request and will not be used for other purposes without prior parental consent.

2. We do not collect personally identifiable offline contact information without prior parental consent.

3. We do not distribute to third parties any personally identifiable information with out prior parental consent.

4. We do not give the ability to publicly post or otherwise distribute personally identifiable contact information without prior parental consent.

5. We do not entice by the prospect of a special game, prize or other activity, to divulge more information than is needed to participate in the activity.

Choice to Opt Out
Our sites provide users the opportunity to opt out of receiving communications from us and our partners at the point where we request information about the user.

Change/Modify
Our sites give users options for changing and modifying information previously provided. To make or inquire about such changes, send email to help@earthlink.net, or contact Customer Service at (800) 890-6356.
