PROTECTING YOUR PRIVACY
Earwaks.com does not collect information that personally identifies individuals unless such individuals submit information on a voluntary basis. All information that is collected will be kept confidential and will not be sold or made available to any third party.


COLLECTION OF INFORMATION

Relevant information with accordance to online purchases, surveys, or other services will also be requested. Such information may include name, residence, industry, job position, etc. This information is used to provide you with the immediate service you have requested, and may be used to provide you with relevant content. For example, we may use your e-mail address to send you a confirmation notice. Similarly, we may also use this address when communicating to you new features, products, or services available to you as a member, or by improving the quality of services that we offer by tailoring them to your needs.

Earwaks.com also collects certain information about your computer hardware and software. This information may include your IP address, browser type, operating system and domain name. This information is used to maintain the quality of the services we provide, and in collecting general statistics regarding use of Earwaks.com. As do most websites, Earwaks makes use of cookie technology. Users should be aware that cookies transfer non-personal information and data from the client to Earwaks.com. Through the transfer of cookies, web sites can identify returning users, enable usage access, and monitor site traffic. Earwaks.com uses the data contained in the cookies for the purposes mentioned above and will not use them to identify users or to track non-Earwaks related Internet usage.

The Internet is, for the most part, an insecure network.
