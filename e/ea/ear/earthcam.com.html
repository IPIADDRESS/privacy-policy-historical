EarthCam Privacy Policy

Your secret is safe with us...

EarthCam's policy is to respect and protect the privacy of our users. EarthCam takes your privacy concerns seriously and firmly supports protecting your privacy online. EarthCam will never deliberately reveal private information about our users to any third party without first receiving that user's consent. EarthCam will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone. Please read through this policy statement to understand our practices regarding privacy and learn how we use the information collected from you. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: EarthCam.

EarthCam shares information about our users with advertisers, business partners, sponsors and other third parties. We refer to our users as an entirety, not as individuals. For example, we will compare EarthCam's audience of females vs. males with the use of variables such as x and y. The data collected is used to tailor EarthCam content and advertising for our users.

There are instances where EarthCam asks for your personal information such as your name and e-mail address. We do this when you submit a camera site to EarthCam, sign up for EarthCam's free newsletter, sign up for EarthCam's services, send postcards or whenever you enter an EarthCam sweepstakes or other EarthCam promotions. The user's contact information is used to contact the user when necessary and to advise them about new services, products and/or software upgrades. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. If EarthCam intends to disclose personal information in some restricted situation, this is specifically explained to you when we collect the information in the rules of sweepstakes or other promotions.

Information Gathering

EarthCam automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser including your IP address and the page you requested. We use your IP address to help identify you, generate our statistic logs and to count the traffic statistics for our site. EarthCam will not reveal individually identifiable information about its users except when we believe, in good faith, that the law requires it or to protect the rights or property of EarthCam. EarthCam will send personally identifiable information about a user to other companies or people only when we find that the user's actions on our website violate the EarthCam Terms of Service or any of our usage guidelines for specific products or services.

We use an outside ad company (www.247media.com/privacy.html) to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. While we do NOT use cookies on our website, cookies received with banner ads are collected by a third party advertising company. Click here for more information about cookies.

Links Outside EarthCam

Additionally, be aware that when you use EarthCam, the site contains links to other sites. You could be directed to other websites that are outside of our services and control. EarthCam is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites. You are responsible for voluntarily disclosing personal information online through email, chat areas, message boards, etc. This information is accessible to the public and can be collected and used by others. Keep in mind that when you visit a site, it may be collecting information about you without your knowledge. Remember to be responsible and safe when online.

Public Forums

This site makes chat rooms, forums, message boards, and/or news groups available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.

Choice/Opt-Out

Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us and our partners at the point where we request information about the user.

This site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service:

1. At the bottom of all EarthCam newsletters and advisory notices regarding new services, products and/or software upgrades, a user can elect to unsubscribe.

2. Send an e-mail to cammaster@earthcam.com, re: Unsubscribe.

Correct/Update

Users can change and/or modify the information they have previously provided on EarthCam by selecting the Add/Change Cam URL option on the bottom of the EarthCam home page. User can simply make all their changes they want and then click the "Done" button located on the bottom of the page. The new information is updated within 24 hours. User can also send an e-mail to cammaster@earthcam.com in which they document the necessary changes they wish to make. The new information, again, will be updated within 24 hours.
