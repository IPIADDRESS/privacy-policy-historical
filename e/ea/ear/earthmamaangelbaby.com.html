Users may revoke consent for future contact with Earth Mama Angel Baby™ at any time by sending an email message to mama@earthmamaangelbaby.com. If you revoke your consent, all information we have regarding you will be erased within one week.

Access

Users may also change or modify the information previously provided by sending an email to mama@earthmamaangelbaby.com

The security of the information that you provide to us is very important to us. Hence, we have taken several measures to protect your information. All transactions involving sensitive information, such as your credit/debit card number, are conducted using an SSL (Secure Socket Layer protocol) connection.
