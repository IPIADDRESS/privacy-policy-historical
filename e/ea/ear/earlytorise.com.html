Early To Rise Privacy Statement

Early To Rise takes individual privacy very seriously. We are also aware of the threats to your security and privacy on the Internet. Please take a few moments to review the steps that Early To Rise takes to ensure your privacy and security while giving you the best possible customer service as you use our web sites.

If you subscribe to Early To Rise, your name and e-mail address will be placed in a database. As with all other personal information, only employees of Early To Rise have access to this data.
