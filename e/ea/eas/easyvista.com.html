Data & Privacy Policy

EasyVista respects the privacy of visitors to this website who choose to provide personal information. We recognize the need for appropriate protection and management of personal information that is provided. This statement will assist visitors to understand the information we may collect, how that information may be used, and with whom the information may be shared.
This Data & Privacy Statement applies to information collected from this website only.

What Personal Information Is Collected?
On this website a visitor can request contact or download information or assets from EasyVista. The personal information collected will not be used for any other purposes unless opted for by the individual. Personal information for purposes of this Privacy Statement is information that identifies or can identify a specific individual. EasyVista may collect information such as name, address, telephone number or email address and contact preferences.

How does EasyVista use Personal Information?
EasyVista may use personal information to provide further information and to help us better understand needs and interests. We may use visitor information to contact the individual.
EasyVista is not responsible for the personal information submitted in this form. In accordance with applicable law, personal information collected may be transferred, stored and processed in the United States or any other country in which the Company maintain facilities, even if the level of data privacy required is less than that required under Safe Harbor or by the European Union. By entering your personal information, you consent to worldwide processing of your personal information. In addition, we may aggregate demographic information to improve our website, but that information is not personally identifiable.

With whom does EasyVista Share Personal Information?
Unless permitted by relevant laws, or consent has been provided, EasyVista will not share, sell, trade or lease your personal information to others, unless provided in this Privacy Statement.

Choice and Access
The decision to provide personal information through this website is voluntary. If no personal information is provided, however, a visitor may not be able to proceed with the activity or receive the benefit for which the personal information is being requested. A visitor can unsubscribe or choose not to receive information from us by following the specific instructions in the confirmation email received.

Security of Personal Information
EasyVista is committed to taking reasonable efforts to secure the personal information provided. To protect the privacy of any personal information provided. EasyVista employs Internet firewalls, intrusion detection, anti-virus protection, and network monitoring, and, where appropriate and required by applicable law, Transport Layer Security (TLS) or similarly encrypted browsers. If a password is used to help protect accounts and personal information, it is that individual’s responsibility to keep password details confidential.

Links to Non-EasyVista Websites and Third Parties
This website may provide links to third-party websites. Access to those links will exit this website. EasyVista does not control those sites or privacy practices, which may differ from this website. EasyVista does not endorse or make any representations about third party websites. The personal information a visitor chooses to give to unrelated third parties is not covered by this Privacy Statement. EasyVista encourages visitors to review the Data & Privacy policy of any company before submitting your personal information.

Changes to this Statement
EasyVista will review and update this Statement periodically. If material changes are made to this Statement, EasyVista will prominently post a notice of such changes prior to implementing the changes. EasyVista encourage visitors to review this Data & Privacy Statement to be informed of how EasyVista is protecting your information.
