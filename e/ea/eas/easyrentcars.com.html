Cookies and other Browsers

 

We use cookies and similar technologies to collect information. Cookies also help us keep your account safe. By continuing to visit or use our services, you are agreeing to the use of cookies and similar technologies for the purposes we describe in this policy.

A cookie is a small file placed onto your device that enables www.easyrentcars.com  features and functionality. For example, cookies enable us to identify your device, secure your access to www.easyrentcars.com and our sites generally, and even help us know if someone attempts to access your account from a different device. Cookies also enable you to easily share content on www.easyrentcars.com and help us serve relevant ads to you.

Cookies can be used to recognize you when you visit www.easyrentcars.com, remember your preferences, and give you a personalized experience that's in line with your settings. Cookies also make your interactions with www.easyrentcars.com faster and more secure. Additionally, cookies allow us to bring you advertising both on and off the www.easyrentcars.com sites, and bring customized features to you through www.easyrentcars.com plugins such as our "Share" button.

You can control cookies through your browser settings and other tools. By visiting our Services, you consent to the placement of cookies and beacons in your browser and HTML-based emails in accordance with this Privacy Policy, which incorporates by reference our Cookie Policy.
