IMPORTANT: Before deleting your account, make sure you have deleted also Classifieds, CV or Job Offers you may have posted (find them in the tab of your profile, or in your myExpat area and you can delete them one by one with the link at the bottom of each post).

  • In order to remove Directory listing or Comments, you will need to contact us.
