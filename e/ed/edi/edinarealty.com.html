Our Privacy Policy

At Edina Realty Home Services, we know how important your personal privacy is to you. We recognize that you expect privacy and security when it comes to information that personally identifies you and allows you to be individually contacted ("Personal Information"). Edina Realty Home Services has adopted the following privacy policy because we understand the need to safeguard the information you may be providing to us at our web site.

Why we request information about you.
 Edina Realty Home Services offers a variety of programs and services to you from personalized home searches to online mortgage applications. We collect and retain information about you only when we believe it will help us provide you with the services and products that you desire. When you sign up for our personalized home search, we may use that information to occasionally notify you about important functionality changes to our web site, new Edina Realty Home Services products or services, and special offers we think you'll find valuable. If you would rather not receive this information, return to the My Profile to change your preferences.

When you request other information from us through our various order forms, we need to know your name, e-mail address, mailing address and telephone numbers so we can be sure to get in touch with you when and how you ask us to. For instance, when you apply for an on-line mortgage loan, we need to know your name, social security number, e-mail address, mailing address, income, debt and asset information. This information is necessary to process your mortgage application and to obtain your credit report. We will not obtain your credit report without your authorization, but we cannot approve you for a mortgage loan without it. We can, however, pre-qualify you for a mortgage loan without your credit report.

How we protect your financial information.
 When you apply for an on-line mortgage loan, we offer the use of a secure server. The secure socket layer software (SSL) encrypts all information you input before it is sent to us. You can tell you're protected by the small lock symbol in the corner of your Internet browser. The information we receive from you also is protected against unauthorized access once we receive it. We limit access to your information to employees who have a business reason for accessing it. We also educate our employees about the importance of your privacy through our educational programs and company policies.

We use "cookies."
 Cookies are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. Our cookies allow us to welcome you back to our site and provide you with your personalized home searches. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can change your browser to prevent cookies from being placed on your computer without your consent. You can still use our regular home search feature and every other function on our site even though your computer will not accept cookies. Display ads on our site may also contain cookies. Cookies received with ads are collected by our advertising company, and we do not have access to this information. Some customer data may be shared with the advertising companies. Cookies, by themselves, cannot be used to discover your identity and we do not know your identity unless you tell us.

We do not disclose your information to parties outside of our affiliated companies.
 Edina Realty Home Services is a member of a family of companies, which includes HomeServices.com, Iowa Realty, First Realty Better Homes & Gardens, CBS HOME Real Estate, J.C. Nichols Residential, Paul Semonin Realty, Long Realty and their affiliates. We do not sell, trade, rent or otherwise reveal Personal Information or other personally identifiable data about you to parties outside our affiliated companies for their independent use unless: (a) you specifically request or authorize it; (b) the information helps to complete a transaction initiated by you; (c) it is necessary to comply with the law or a good-faith belief that disclosure is necessary to comply with the law; (d) it is to protect and defend our rights; (e) the information is provided to a reputable credit bureau or similar information reporting agency in connection with the completion of your on-line mortgage application; (f) or it is necessary for personal safety (i.e., home security).

Links to other web sites.
 Our web site contains links to and from other web sites and may be in the form of display advertising. We cannot guarantee that web sites other than edinarealty.com will respect and protect your Personal Information in the same manner that we do. We urge you to go to and read the Privacy Statements of other web sites when you visit them for your own protection. We also are not responsible for the content of web sites other than edinarealty.com.

Opt-in/Opt-out
 Our web site generally requires that if you want us to contact you or provide your Personal Information to outside businesses, you must instruct us to do so ("opt-in"). Otherwise, we will not contact you and your Personal Information will be kept private by us. There are some features on our web site, such as the personalized home search, which will allow us to collect information which allows us to contact you as described in the section "Why we request information about you" above. However, you will have the opportunity to "opt-out" of receiving communications from us and our affiliates at the point where we request information about you. We also provide you with the option of modifying or correcting your information or removing it from our database unless it is required for a transaction initiated by you.

Frequently Asked Questions

1.  How does Edina Realty Home Services use my Personal Information?
 At Edina Realty Home Services, we use your Personal Information to operate our site and provide you with the services and products that are available to you at our site. Your Personal Information may also be used by Edina Realty Home Services and its affiliates to notify you of products, services or bargains that may be of interest to you.

2.  Does Edina Realty Home Services sell my Personal Information to other businesses?
 No. Edina Realty Home Services does not sell web site collected customer lists. We do share information with affiliates when it is necessary to do so in order to provide you with a service or product that you have requested. For example, when you sign up for the The Hook Up service your information will be shared with the companies you have selected in order to provide you with the products and services you are requesting, or, when you apply for a mortgage loan online your information will be shared with a credit reporting agency and lenders as part of the mortgage underwriting process.

3.  How can I keep my Personal Information from being shared with other businesses?
 Whenever we request information from you, you are given the opportunity to tell us not to share your Personal Information with other businesses. You also have the option of notifying us as described under the Opt-out/opt-in section above at anytime.

4.  What other kinds of information does Edina Realty Home Services gather about me?
 At Edina Realty Home Services, we may also gather anonymous information about the visitors to our site to help us improve our site by telling us which products and services visitors to our site are most interested in. This type of "traffic data" may include the use of cookies, IP addresses or other identifying codes. Traffic data is not used to personally identify you, but to develop statistical databases.

In summary
 We are committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect on this site to make searching for a home, applying for a mortgage and gathering home buying and selling information possible. We do not sell, trade, or rent your Personal Information to others. We do provide your information to the select services you chose through our Home Service Connections program. If you never want to receive any announcements or special offers from us, return to the My Profile to change your preferences.
