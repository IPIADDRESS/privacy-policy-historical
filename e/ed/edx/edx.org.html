Privacy Statement for LearnWell Resources

LearnWell Resources has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for these websites and all LearnWell-owned links: LearnWell Online at http://www.learnwell.org  and its 4 divisions, namely DrugFree USA at http://www.drugfreeusa.net, LearnWell Forgiveness Center, at http://www.forgiver.net, Online Continuing Education Center at http://www.ce5.com, and the Klimes Outreach Center at http://www.klimes.org.

As shown below, we follow privacy principles as they deal with notice, choice, access and integrity. 

1. Notice: Information Collection

LearnWell Resources and all its sites do not collect any personal information on any of its visitors. LearnWell collects only general information in aggregate, such as number of hits, types of browsers used and so on. Since we do not collect personal information, we cannot and do not disseminate it. 

LearnWell receives general information on persons taking its courses and requesting information. That information is held in strictest confidence and not sold or given to anyone else.

Public Forums

This site makes message boards, and/or news groups available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.

2. Choice: To Opt-Out

Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us and our partners at the point where we request information about the visitor. This site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service. You can send email to edu@learnwell.org

3. Access: Correct/Update

This site gives users access to any information we may have about them and the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided. Email edu@learnwell.org

4. Integrity: Information Protection

All consumer information is well protected from unauthorized use. It is accessible only to the authorized staff.
