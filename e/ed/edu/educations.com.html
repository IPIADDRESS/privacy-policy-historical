EMG Privacy Policy

Cookies Policy

Cookies are a technology used to provide tailored information from a website. A cookie is an element of data that a website can send to a browser, which may then store it within the system. It is possible to set browsers to notify users when they receive a cookie, giving them the chance to decide whether to accept it.

EMG collects HTTP cookies and the data is used for the following purposes:

  • Completion and support of the current activity.
  • Web site and system administration.

The following explanation is provided for why this data is collected: EMG sets a cookie when users arrive on the site so that, IF you should register or request a contact, EMG can capture the URL of the Web site that referred you to us. If you do not register or make a request, that information is never linked to any personal information. No other data is captured in the cookie.

Information collected through the use of cookies is used to evaluate and improve its website and to help diagnose problems with its server. EMG may collect the following information:

  • URL of requested resource
  • Full IP address
  • HTTP request method
  • Last URL requested by the user

EMG Opt-out Policy

If, at any time, you change your mind about receiving information from EMG or about sharing your information with third parties, please send EMG a request specifying your new choice. Simply send your request to opt-out@educations.com

Every person making an information request or an educations purchase via an EMG website automatically becomes a member in EMG. Becoming a member enables you to log on to our user portal and managing information request and areas of interest within education. As a member, you will also receive our monthly newsletter and inspiring training suggestions based on your interests. If you would like to opt-out of receiving emails from EMG, please go here and follow the instructions.

Data Collection

In general, you can visit the EMG website without providing personal information, but there are times when EMG will request personal information; for example, when you make an information request on an education program. Personal information is any information that individually identifies you, such as your name, telephone number, physical address or email address. The information is forwarded to the school which may contact you via mail, email or telephone to provide you with more information on their courses.


We at Educations.com are committed to the security of personally identifiable information and we take precautions to maintain that protection. Your personal data will never be sold to third party.
