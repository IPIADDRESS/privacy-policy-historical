edhat takes very seriously the private nature of your personal information.  This Privacy Policy describes how we treat the information we collect when you visit our web site.

Personal Information

We need your email address so we can send you newsletters. edhat does not collect any other personal information that you do not expressly provide.  edhat will not disclose your personal information to any third party without your consent.

Usage Information

In addition to collecting personal information, edhat may also collect non-personal, aggregated information about subscribers' use of our site and responses to our newsletters.  This information is not personally identifiable and will only be used to find out how subscribers use our service and site.  For example, this information will tell us how much time users spend on our site, from which other sites subscribers came, and to what other sites subscribers go.  The collection of this information allows us to, among other things, prepare for traffic load demands and to assess the types of content most enjoyed by our users.

Cookies

Like most sites, edhat makes use of browser "cookies." Cookies are small text files placed on your computer's hard disk by our server. They allow us to quickly check your status as a subscriber and facilitate access to your preferences.  edhat will never use cookies to retrieve information from a computer that is unrelated to our site or services.

Links to Other Sites

As part of the service, edhat may create links allowing you to access third party sites. Sometime purchasing items on these sites will result in edhat receiving a referring commission.  edhat is not responsible for the content that appears on those sites and does not endorse these sites. Please consult those sites' individual privacy policies in order to determine how they treat user information.
