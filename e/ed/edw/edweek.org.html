In order to process a product order or allow you to access registered content without a login and password, we may set "cookies." Cookies are small sets of data that are placed on your computer in order to keep track of what you view while on our site. Cookies do not collect personally identifiable information about you, and we do not use them for any purpose other than your convenience in ordering a product or accessing registered content.

If our information practices ever change, we will post the changes to our Web site and provide you with the ability to opt out of these new uses. If you are concerned about how your information is used, you should periodically check our Web site, or write to webeditor@epe.org.

Product order forms you fill out on our site will normally be protected under an industry-standard secure process. (Your browser is part of this process, which is usually indicated by a picture of a closed lock, key, or similar symbol.) Your personal information and credit card information is encrypted when you send them to us through a secure page.

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, contact us at the above address or phone number, or e-mail us at webeditor@epe.org.
