PRIVACY POLICY

 

Your privacy is important to Edelweiss Web Services Limited. This privacy statement provides information about the personal information that Edelweiss Web Services Limited collects, and the ways in which Edelweiss Web Services Limited uses that personal information.

 

 

Personal information collection

 

Edelweiss Web Services Limited may collect and use the following kinds of personal information:

  • information about your use of this website
  • information that you provide using for the purpose of registering with www.edelweiss.in (“the Website”).
  • information about transactions carried out over this Website .
  • information that you provide for the purpose of subscribing to the Website services
  • any other information that you send to Edelweiss Web Services Limited.

 

 

Using personal information

 

Edelweiss Web Services Limited may use your personal information to:

  • administer this Website;
  • personalize the Website for you;
  • enable your access to and use of the Website services;
  • publish information about you on the Website;
  • send to you products that you purchase;
  • supply to you services that you purchase;
  • send you statements and invoices;
  • collect payments from you; and
  • send you marketing communications

 

Where Edelweiss Web Services Limited discloses your personal information to its agents or sub-contractors for these purposes, the agent or sub-contractor in question will be obligated to use that personal information in accordance with the terms of this privacy statement. Edelweiss Web Services Limited shall or its agents and/or sub-contractors may use your personal information to make contact you for promotion of products/schemes etc of Edelweiss Web Services Limited or of its partners from time to time. You hereby agree and understand that such promotional calls shall be made to you unless you specifically convey to Edelweiss Web Services Limited to not call you further for such promotions, schemes etc.

 

In addition to the disclosures reasonably necessary for the purposes identified elsewhere above, Edelweiss Web Services Limited may disclose your personal information to the extent that it is required to do so by law, in connection with any legal proceedings or prospective legal proceedings, and in order to establish, exercise or defend its legal rights.

 

 

Securing Your Data

 

Edelweiss Web Services Limited will take reasonable technical and organizational precautions to prevent the loss, misuse or alteration of your personal information.

 

Edelweiss Web Services Limited will store all the personal information you provide on its secure servers.

 

Information relating to electronic transactions entered into via this Website will be protected by encryption technology.

 

 

Cross-border data transfers

 

Information that Edelweiss Web Services Limited collects may be stored and processed in and transferred between any of the countries in which Edelweiss Web Services Limited operates (if at all) to enable the use of the information in accordance with this privacy policy.

 

In addition, personal information that you submit for publication on the website will be published on the internet and may be available around the world. You agree to such cross-border transfers of personal information.

 

 

Updating this statement

 

Edelweiss Web Services Limited may update this privacy policy by posting a new version on this Website. You should check this page occasionally to ensure you are familiar with any changes.

 

Other websites

 

This website contains links to other websites. Edelweiss Web Services Limited is not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of any third party.
