                              Privacy Policy

                              At Edens Garden we are committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect to process orders and to provide you the service you expect.

                              What do we do with the information we collect from you?

                              In order to process an order, we need to know such details as your name, e-mail address, shipping address, credit card number and expiration date. This information allows us to process and ship your order quickly, with no hiccups.

                              At Edens Garden your privacy is very important to us. We will never give your name, address or any other information to any other company or individual for their promotional or financial gain. The information you submit is used to help you build and maintain your personal account.

                              Emails/Newsletters

                              We may also use the information we collect to notify you about important changes to Edens Garden website, new services and special offers we think you will be interested in. If you would rather not receive this information, just reply to any email you receive from us with the word REMOVE in the subject.

                              What do we do to insure your information is kept safe?

                              The Edens Garden website utilizes various security measures, including encryption technologies and password protection, to protect the security of your information. This ensures that only you and Edens Garden see any of your personal information.

                              At Edens Garden we respect your privacy, If you have any questions about this privacy policy, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this website, you can contact us by sending an email to info@edensgarden.com or fill out our contact form.

                              By using our website, you (the visitor) agree to allow third parties to process your IP address, in order to determine your location for the purpose of currency conversion. You also agree to have that currency stored in a session cookie in your browser (a temporary cookie which gets automatically removed when you close your browser). We do this in order for the selected currency to remain selected and consistent when browsing our website so that the prices can convert to your (the visitor) local currency.

                              This privacy policy is subject to change at any time.
