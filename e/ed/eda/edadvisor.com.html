privacy policy

EdAdvisor treats your personal information as confidential and only provides it to third parties upon your request. Information is only shared with your explicit permission and at your request.


Contact information

Contact information is only disclosed to appropriate employers, colleges or advertising partners upon explicit request of the individual member. EdAdvisor will not provide your information to third parties unless you ask us to pass it along to such a party.


Communication from EdAdvisor

Your contact information will be used to keep you updated on the status of your membership, and to inform you of matching employers and colleges, to resolve any problems with transmission of your information, to provide a confirmation of requests, or to remind you of important dates on the calendar.


Customer Support

Customer support is provided through e-mail. When a customer support issue is received, we will reply via e-mail to help you solve the problem that has been encountered.


Security

EdAdvisor takes every precaution to protect our users' information. When users submit sensitive information via the web site, their information is protected both on-line and off-line. EdAdvisor uses Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption technology to protect payment information. SSL technology is the most commonly-used method on the internet to safely transmit secure data. Access to your personal information is protected by a password that you select to protect your account. Only you, or someone who knows your password, can make changes to your account information.


Contact Information

If you have additional questions regarding our privacy or security policies, please Contact Us.
