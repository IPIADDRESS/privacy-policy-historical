Our Privacy Policy

Print page

The privacy of your personal data is important to EDF Energy.

The privacy of your personal data is important to EDF Energy. We are committed to protecting your privacy. We do not share with or sell any personal information to third parties. Please note that you are not required to provide any personal information to use this site. Any contact you make with us by email will be treated in the strictest confidence.

If you send us your personal details in relation to our work, you have the right to request a copy of the information we hold on you at any time, and to have that information corrected if it is inaccurate. If you wish to exercise this right, please contact us.

The privacy of your personal data is important to EDF Energy. The following sets out what we do with the information we collect using this Website:

  • The Website creates a cookie (a small piece of computer code) which is sent to and stored on the user's computer containing a unique session identifier only. This identifier is used to record site usage statistics.
  • In a separate automatic operation the web server will collect the user's IP address and details of the website where the user came from before entering the Website. This information is used to:
    • work out how many times the Website has been visited
    • record which parts of the Website have been visited so we can improve the content and layout of the Website. The information collected is not linked to you as an individual. Cookies stored on your computer will automatically be deleted.
  • The Website is hosted on a server in the UK.


If you have any questions in relation to the Conditions of Use or the Privacy Policy please do not hesitate to contact us.
