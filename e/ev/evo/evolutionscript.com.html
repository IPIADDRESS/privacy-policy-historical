Privacy Policy

EvolutionScript collects personal information when you purchase one of our products or services.

When you make a purchase we ask for information such as your name, email address, address, zip code, city, country and personal interests.

EvolutionScript automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, EvolutionScript cookie information, and the page you request.

We transfer information about you if EvolutionScript is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, EvolutionScript will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of EvolutionScript's terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.
