Data Privacy Policy / Datenschutz

EventStudyTools.com is strongly commited to the privacy of your personal information. This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which EventStudyTools.com uses, maintains and discloses information collected from its customers and users of its website. You find our Swiss data privacy policy in German language further down this page.

General Data Policy
EventStudyTools.com does not collect personal information. There is no login required (or even possible) to use this website or its services. If you should use a server-side program provided by EventStudyTools.com and upload CSV files as inputs for this program, no data included in these CSV will be stored beyond the time required to produce the requested results and allow the download of the analysis' results. All data will be cleared from the server's memory after a short period of time following the completion of the calculations implied by the requested analysis.

What are Cookies?
A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's computer tied to information about the user. We may use both session ID cookies. For session ID cookies, once you close your browser, the cookie terminates and is erased. Session ID cookies may be used to track user preferences while the user is visiting the website. They also help to minimize load times and save on server processing. Cookies used on the EventStudyTools.com website do not contain personally identifiable information.

Log Files
Like most standard websites, we use log files. This information may include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. However, none of the information stored in our log files, including but not limited to IP addresses, is linked to personally identifiable information.

How we use this information
We use the presented information for research purposes regarding the effectiveness of our website services, marketing, advertising and sales efforts. EventStudyTools.com will not in any way share this information with a third party.
