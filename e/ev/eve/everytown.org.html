Cookies

This Site is cookie-enabled to give you a more customized user experience. A cookie is a small piece of information that a website can store on a computer and later use to recognize a user. A purpose of cookies is to enhance your ability to receive the information you want. We use two types of cookies to enhance the experience of users: session cookies and persistent cookies. A session cookie expires when a user closes the browser in which this website was viewed. A persistent cookie expires on a pre-determined date. Users can personalize their browser settings to reject cookies, although certain features of the Site may not function as intended.

Sharing Information

From time to time, we may send you information from other like-minded organizations we think you might find interesting. We may share personal information with third parties who provide services, such as service providers, affiliates, and subsidiaries in connection with the services we provide.

If we or some or all of our assets are acquired by another organization, that organization will possess the information collected by us, and it will assume the rights and obligations regarding your information as described in this Privacy Statement.

We may disclose personally identifiable information or other data to comply with law or legal requirements, enforce or apply our Terms of Use and other agreements, or protect our rights, property, or safety of our users or others.

For your convenience, this Site may contain certain hyperlinks to other websites. We make no promises or guarantees regarding data collection on the hyperlinked pages that are not operated by Everytown for Gun Safety Action Fund. We recommend that you read the privacy policy/statement for each site you visit.

You can unsubscribe from the Everytown.org / Everytown for Gun Safety Action Fund mailing list at any time by following the instructions included within any email you receive from us.
