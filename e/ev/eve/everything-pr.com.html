Cookies

A “cookie” is a bit of data sent by a Web site through the browser to the computer of the user visiting the site, and enables the site to return the results the browser expects. Pamil Visions PR uses temporary, session-specific cookies to ensure visits to its Web site are smooth and customized for the visitor. Such cookies allow us to provide a visitor’s browser with information tailored to the visitor’s preferences and needs. If you do not want your browser to accept cookies, you can turn off the cookie acceptance option in the browser’s settings.

We use advertising companies to develop banner ads for our Web site. These ads may contain cookies. These companies collect cookies sent to your browser through the banner ads and we do not have control or access to this data.
