Collection and Use of Information

It is Orlando Bathing Suit, LLC's ("OBS'") policy to respect the privacy of (a) visitors to Everything But Water's website(s) at www.everythingbutwater.com, including the mobile versions of such website(s), (b) visitors to our Amazon store, (c) visitors to our social media channels (e.g., our Facebook page), (a, b and c collectively ("Websites"), and (d) visitors of our retail stores ("Stores"). OBS will collect personal information such as names, home addresses, phone number, e-mail addresses, birth month and date, credit card and other billing information, and shipping information, etc., but only when a visitor of our Websites or Stores voluntarily gives it to us in the process of subscribing to OBS promotional emails, postal mail, and phone calls (e.g., to call you when new items from your favorite designer have hit the store) (see the Communications section below for more details), placing orders on our Websites or in Stores, or registering for an account with OBS. When you participate in one of our surveys, we may collect additional profile information. When you participate in contests, sweepstakes, or other similar activities ("Sweepstakes") that we make available on our Websites or in our Stores, which may be facilitated via a third party partner and/or conducted in partnership with a third party partner ("Sweepstakes Partner"), we will collect the information designated along with such Sweepstakes. We may also receive information about you through third party companies that we partner with. You might also provide OBS with the personal information of other persons, such as family or friends to whom you ship purchases. We may also collect information from you via technology as described in the Cookies section below.

This information is used by OBS for the following purposes: to identify you as a user, create and manage your account, place and manage your order, fulfill or respond to your other requests, administer Sweepstakes, improve our products and services, send you service related communications via emails, postal mail, or phone calls, send you promotional communications via emails, postal mail, or phone calls (see the Communications section below for more details), and for our marketing and research purposes.

Cookies

OBS uses "cookie" technology to enhance a visitor's online experience by making it easier to navigate through our Websites or to make an activity work better.

Cookies are small pieces of information or files that a website puts on a computer's hard disk for record-keeping purposes. Cookies allow a computer to "remember" information from our Websites for future use. We may use both session cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and persistent cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them). Persistent cookies can be removed by following Internet browser help file directions. If you choose to disable cookies, some areas or features of our Websites may not work properly. OBS uses information collected by cookies to learn about our visitors' preferences, so that we can make our Websites better.

We may use clear gifs (otherwise known as web bugs or web beacons) to gather information about your visit to our Websites, such as what you click on and pages and information you viewed. Clear gifs are tiny graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies. In contrast to cookies, which are placed on a user's hard drive, clear gifs are embedded invisibly on web pages.

In addition to cookies, we may collect information from you, including your browser type, operating system, Internet Protocol (IP) address (a number that is automatically assigned to your computer when you use the Internet, which may vary from session to session), domain name, and/or a date/time stamp for your visit.

Our third party analytics providers and advertisers may also use their own cookies, flash cookies or local shared objects (LSOs) or other technologies on our Websites. We do not control use of these cookies, LSOs or other technologies used by these third parties and expressly disclaim responsibility for information collected through them. Please see below for more information about these third parties.

Our Websites use cookies served by a third party service provider (currently Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. ("Google")), to help us analyze how users use our Websites through the collection of anonymous user information. Please see the following link for more information on Google's web analytics services http://www.google.com/analytics/tos.html.
