Cookies

A cookie is a small amount of data, which may include an anonymous unique identifier. Cookies are sent to your browser from a web site's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive. Gibraltar requires you to log in to your account after a certain period of time has elapsed to protect you against others accidentally accessing the content of your account. Session cookies are removed upon termination of session while Remember Me cookies remain until cleared by the user or browser.
