    When you place an online order we need to know your name, e-mail address, mailing address, credit card number, and the card's expiration date. This information allows us to process your order, and to notify you of your order status.
    - When you make a credit card purchase in one of our stores, we need to know your credit card number, and the card's expiration date.
    - When you sign up for our e-mail newsletter, we need only your e-mail address.
    - When you sign up to receive notice about our sales or store events through the mail, we need your name and mailing address.

    What's A "Cookie"?

    A cookie is a small file stored on your computer by a web site to give you a unique ID. A cookie generated by our site contains no information about you. It contains only a numeric ID.

    EMS.com uses cookies to
    - Allow you to retrieve your shopping cart on your next visit, as long as it is within a 12 hour period.
    - Estimate the number of shoppers on our site.
    - Determine the browser our visitors use so the site can be designed to work properly with the most common browsers. provide aggregated, statistical user information to third parties without releasing individual information. For example, we may disclose that "30% of our customers have bought hiking boots" in order to describe our site or services to trading partners, advertisers, or other third parties.Return to top
