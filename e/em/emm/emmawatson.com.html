1.1

1.1.3 your email address; and
1.1.4 your gender.

2.1 If you are under 13 years of age you must get your parent of legal guardian’s consent before registering as a Member. Access to certain areas of the Website may be restricted to users under 13 years of age.
2.2 If you are under 13 years of age we will send an email to your parent or legal guardian seeking permission for you to register as a Member. If we do not receive permission, we will suppress any personal data that we have collected from you.
