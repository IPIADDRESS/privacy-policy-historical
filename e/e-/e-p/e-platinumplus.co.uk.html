The United States Department of Commerce and the EU developed a set of Safe Harbor Principles regarding personal data privacy and security that, when followed, permit and organisation to certify that it provides adequate protection for the transfer of EU personal data to the US for processing. E – Credit Plus fully commits to follow the Safe Harbor Principles with respect to all personal data received from any individual or entity in the EU or the EEA.
