Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is intended to help you understand how we collect and use information you provide to us, either by choice as an individual, or in aggregate form as you and others browse our site.

Your Email Address

If you provide us with your email address for the purposes of receiving information from us, or if you communicate with us through email, we may retain your email address for future communications. We may use this address to send you information about our products and services. If such information is sent to you, we will provide you with instructions on how to unsubscribe. We will not sell, trade, or otherwise distribute your email address to anyone.

Other Personal Information

If you provide us with your name, address, phone number, company name, position title, or other personal information we will not sell, trade or otherwise distribute your information. We will use it for the purpose of contacting you per your requests. We may use it internally to understand the needs of our customers or to improve our website.

Website Use Information

Like other online sites, e-academy uses standard "cookie" technology and Web server logs to collect information about how our website is used. Information gathered through cookies and Web server logs may include the date and time of visits, pages viewed, and time spent on our site. We may also use "Web beacons" or "clear gifs" to determine if any promotional offers have been seen and responded to.

Why Choose Us?

Through a growing network of over 60,000 academic institutions and departments, we currently support millions of transactions annually in 195 countries and several languages. The majority of transactions are via ESD, although delivery of physical media is also supported.
