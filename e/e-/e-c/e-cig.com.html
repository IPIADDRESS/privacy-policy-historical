Cookies

We use "cookies" only if you come from an external source or E-CIG Affiliate. A "cookie" is a small text file placed on your computer by our web page server. A cookie can later be retrieved by our web page servers.  No personal information is collected with this cookie.

Cookies are frequently used on Web Sites and you can choose if and how a cookie will be accepted by configuring your preferences and options in your browser. You may still be able to use some information-only pages even if you do not accept cookies.
