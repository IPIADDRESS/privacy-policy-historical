    emSafe

    emSafe is a product that ensures confidentiality of data by encryption and decryption of files or data in transit. The solution uses combination of symmetric and asymmetric keys to ensure the end-to-end data security while ensuring speed of data encryption.

    emSafe is a highly scalable application which can be hosted across multiple platforms and environments. The solution has proven capabilities to encrypt file sizes upto 10GB. The solution is successfully deployed across Banking, Insurance and Government customers.

    Typical use cases include multi-platform enabled secure data transfer, enterprise host-to-host secured communication, secure remote data sharing, and personal level file protection. emSafe is available in desktop, SDK and Web service versions.

    emCA

    As your Enterprise crosses the digital chasm and adopts Cloud, Mobility, how do you ensure that your digital assets are protected? Adopt the power of eMudhra PKI suite of solutions that use digital certificates to create your very own trusted network.
