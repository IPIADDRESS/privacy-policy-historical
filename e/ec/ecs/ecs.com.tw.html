You do not need to provide any personal information for the use of this Website. However, there are circumstances that ECS may ask you to provide us with certain personal information for some services provided by ECS on the Website (including but not limited to product support, registration, event or activities). When you submit personal information (such as your name, mailing address, email address and other contact information), it constitutes your consent to the use of such information for the purpose(s) stated below.

Use and Disclosure of Personal Information Collected

  • The personal information ECS collected is used to respond to your inquiries that you request and to help ECS to upgrade the quality of the Website and the services that ECS provides over the Website. ECS will not disclose, sell or provide your personal information to any third party except the situations stated in this Policy.

    • to obtain customer feedback and to analyze user experience.
