Privacy Policy

eCommHub, Inc. (hereinafter "eCommHub", "we", "our" or "us") Privacy Policy ("Policy") sets out what information we collect, how we collect it, and what we do with it. We may update this Policy from time to time, so please check this Policy periodically for changes. eCommHub is the provider of a web-based drop shipping software and services that automate inventory management and order fulfillment for online stores that rely on third-party vendors, distributors, suppliers, or fulfillment centers for their inventory. Using the eCommHub platform, both retailers and suppliers can automate inventory management and order fulfillment, thus expanding product offerings, routing orders intelligently and minimizing backorders.

Personal and anonymous information may be collected directly from our customers and prospective customers, including when our customers or prospective customers communicate with us through our website located at http://ecommhub.com ("Website"). We may collect certain information actively from users within our Website and other information passively (e.g., IP address). We will update your information whenever we can to keep it current, accurate and complete.

An Internet Protocol ("IP") address is a unique identifier that certain electronic devices use to identify and communicate with each other on the Internet. When you visit our Website, we may view the IP address (which may indicate the city, domain address and service provider) of the device you use to connect to the Internet. We use this information to determine the general physical location of the device and understand from what regions of the world our Website visitors are originating. We also may use this information to enhance our Website and services.

Certain pages on our Website contain "web beacons" (also known as Internet tags, pixel tags and clear GIFs). These web beacons allow third parties to obtain information such as the IP address of the computer that downloaded the page on which the beacon appears, the URL of the page on which the beacon appears, the time the page containing the beacon was viewed, the type of browser used to view the page, and the information in cookies set by the third party.

Google will not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google. You may disable cookies within your browser as described above; however, please note that if you do this you may not be able to use the full functionality of the Website or applications. By using this Website or any of its applications, you consent to the processing of data about you by Google in the manner and for the purposes set out above. For certain browsers, you can also prevent Google from collecting information (including your IP address) via cookies and processing this information by downloading this browser plugin and installing it: http://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.
