Who or What Is EclaimsLine.com?

EclaimsLine.com ("Eclaims") is an online claims center that enables Visa cardholders to take full advantage of the Auto Rental Collision Damage Waiver Benefit provided on eligible cards . The Auto Rental Collision Damage Waiver Benefit,, is made possible through the collaborative efforts of several business partners, all of whom are committed to serving you, the cardholder. The EClaims site is brought to you by the Benefit Administrator and not by Visa U.S.A. Inc. (“Visa”). Visa is not affiliated with Eclaims.

You will notice links on this site that lead you back to the "Visa Home Page". This is because when you access Eclaims, you leave the Visa.com site. We provide these links because we understand that you may want to return to Visa.com once you have finished filing or checking the status of a claim. Please note that this Privacy Policy and the Terms and Conditions of Use, which follow, apply only to the Eclaims site. If accessing other web sites, including Visa.com, please be sure to consult the policies and terms of use specifically applicable to those sites.

Throughout this Privacy Policy and the Terms and Conditions of Use you will see the words "you", "your" and "yours". These refer to you as a visitor to Eclaims and/or as the holder of a Visa card that entitles you to the Auto Rental Collision Damage Waiver Benefit. You will also see "Eclaims", "we", "us" and "our". These refer to the Benefit Administrator.

What Information Will Be Collected?

In the process of visiting Eclaims, you will be providing information. You provide information directly by entering it in yourself (e.g., your name, email address or other personal data). This includes the information you provide via the log in page, the online claims form, status inquiry pages and any emails you may send to us. The information you provide via the log in page allows us to personalize the site for you when you visit. All other information you provide, on this site enables us to better process and service your claim(s).

You also provide information indirectly, in the form of data that is automatically collected by the web site. This may include our retaining a record of a user's entry and exit page points (in other words, the page on which you start your use of the site and the page from which you leave). This data, if collected, will not be personally connected to you and will only be collected in the aggregate, in order to assist us in creating a site that is easier and more convenient for you to use.

We collect the information that you provide, both directly and indirectly, to assist with site development, to create usage reports, and to enhance claims servicing. We will not sell your personal information to third parties for any purposes, including, but not limited to: marketing or other consumer related mailings. However, because Eclaims is serviced collaboratively, we may share the information you provide among the companies that provide and support your Auto Rental Collision Damage Waiver. For more information regarding this, please see the section below entitled "Use of Information".

How Is Your Information Protected?

We are committed to ensuring that all of your EclaimsLine.com transactions are safe and secure and protected by VeriSign..

To ensure that every transaction is protected, we use advanced encryption technology; we treat your credit card and personal information with the highest standards of confidentiality and safety. To that end all such information about you, which is maintained on our server, is protected by the use of technologically advanced firewalls. We restrict access to your personal information to only the employees or agents of VISA, the underwriter, benefit administrator and the program quality assurance monitor, who need to know that information in order to service your account. We maintain physical, electronic and procedural safeguards to protect your personal information.

By filing your claim on our secure server, you are further protected by Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology, utilized by most popular browsers, including Netscape and Microsoft Explorer. This technology allows us to use advanced encryption tools to protect information transmitted between your computer and our server. Once processed by Visa Travel Assist, your credit-card information is not transmitted over the Internet again.

Does Eclaims use Cookies?

A cookie is a small piece of information sent by a web site that is saved on your hard disk by your computer's browser. Cookies do not capture your individual email address or any personal information about you; instead they hold information a site may need to personalize your experience and to gather web site statistical data. This notwithstanding, Eclaims does not utilize cookie technology.

Use of Information

Non-personal information is collected to track the total number of guests visiting this site and to guide our efforts to improve our site. This information is used exclusively for our internal purposes and is not disclosed to any non-affiliated third parties.

Personal information is information that can identify a visitor, such as name, address or policy information. You can be assured that when you file an Auto Rental Collision Damage Waiver Benefit claim with us, we will only use your personal information to process your Auto Rental Collision Damage Waiver Benefit claim. Except as otherwise set forth in this privacy policy notice, we will not disclose or sell any of your personal information, including your name, address, credit-card number, or claim history to any third party.

Personal information is collected only when voluntarily offered and solely for purposes that are clearly identified in this privacy policy notice or at the time you submit your information. At a minimum you will need to provide a name and a credit card number in order to log in. After providing such information, you will be able to navigate freely within the site and read about the benefit and its features. However (for obvious reasons), if you want to file a claim and/or inquire about pending claim, you will have to enter in enough information to enable us to confirm your eligibility for the benefit and establish a claim file or identify the claim about which you are inquiring.

We will not share your personal information without your consent, except as otherwise set forth in this privacy policy notice and as is necessary in order to provide our products and services to you. To this end, information relating to you and your claim may be shared among Visa and the program partners. The program partners, all of whom are listed in the first portion of this policy, are those companies that provide and administer the Auto Rental Collision Damage Waiver. Such information may individually identify you and your claim, or be depersonalized in the aggregate for program related statistical reportings.
