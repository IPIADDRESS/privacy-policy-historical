Why We Collect Information

As a provider of complete online educational services, Learning Sciences needs to collect and store certain personal information from its users for several reasons. Learning Sciences must know who each member is, have proof of their identity, know how to contact them, and, occasionally, verify how the member is using the site. Details of the users' identities are necessary to verify that the participants using the site are legitimate. Financial information is used only to bill the user.

Personal information is required for registration, to log your use of the site, and when applicable, to log your progression through the courses and grant credit. If at any time, information you submit can be viewed publicly, we will inform you how it will be used. There will also be a direct link to this policy to make our intentions clear.

How We Collect Information

Learning Sciences primarily collects information through the registration and purchase screens. We use "cookie" technology to enable certain functions. Cookies are used to gather non-personal information, such as progress tracking.

How We Protect Information

We offer the use of a secure server. The Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encrypts all information you input before it is sent to us. Furthermore, all the data we collect is protected against unauthorized access.

Learning Sciences has security measures that protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of information under our control. The site requires a username and password combination to utilize all features.
