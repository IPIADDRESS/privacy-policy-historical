Privacy Policy

Any information provided throughout the ordering and/or registration process is kept strictly confidential. It will not be shared or sold to third parties. We respect your privacy and have taken every precautionary step to ensure that it is protected as you order from our web site.

Shopping online at eFavormart.com is completely safe. We have a firm commitment to do our utmost best in ensuring that others may not intercept or have access to the private information that you provide to us This site has security measures in place to protect against the loss, misuse, and alteration of your information. All sensitive information will be encrypted before being sent over the Internet. This means that your private information will remain private.
