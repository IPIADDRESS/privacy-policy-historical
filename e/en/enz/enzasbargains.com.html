Use of Enza’s Bargains may use cookies to store information about your preferences and other information. Cookies themselves do not contain any personal information, and Enza’s Bargains does not use cookies to collect personal information. Cookies may also be used by 3rd party content providers. Some advertising partners may use cookies. Some of the partners include, but aren’t limited to Groupon, Google AdSense, and Amazon.

Enza’s Bargains will never ask for Credit Card details and request that you do not enter it on any of the forms on Enza’s Bargains.

Enza’s Bargains is not responsible for the content of external internet sites. You are advised to read the privacy policy of external sites before disclosing any personal information.

While Enza’s Bargains may occasionally ask for personal information to be provided, such as in the case of giveaways, this information will not be sold or used for use other than to contact the winner of the giveaway.
