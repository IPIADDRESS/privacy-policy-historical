This privacy policy covers any personal information that EnergyMuse.com obtains from you when you use services on our site. This policy does not cover the practices of companies that EnergyMuse.com does not own or control, or the actions of people that EnergyMuse.com does not employ or manage.

Our privacy policy may change in the future. We invite you to check back periodically to see if we have made any changes. By using this site, you agree to the terms of our policy.

When you buy something on EnergyMuse.com, you have to supply a billing address, phone number, e-mail address, shipping address, and credit card information.

EnergyMuse.com may share your personal information with companies that perform services for us, such as fulfilling orders, delivering packages, providing customer service, processing credit card payments and investigating fraudulent activity. These companies only have access to information needed to perform their services. They are prohibited from using your information for other purposes.

Some personal information is stored on servers owned by other companies. Only Energy Muse has the right to access this information.

Personal information may be used to fulfill orders, answer technical questions, or contact you directly if requested. We do not sell, share, trade, loan or rent this information to anyone. Any email addresses sent to us are used only to send requested emails and information back to you. Energy Muse follows a strict opt-in permission based email policy. We will never send unsolicited, bulk or commercial offers or advertisements.

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our privacy policy, please email us at info@energymuse.com
