Nexus Web Site Privacy Policy

Nexus Energy Software, which operates a portion of the Web site on behalf of our utility partners, is a licensee of the BBB On-Line Privacy Program. This statement discloses the privacy practices for Nexus Energy Software. When you visit a Web site displaying the BBB On-Line trust mark, you can expect to be notified of:

  • What personally identifiable information is collected
  • Which organization is collecting the information
  • How the information is used
  • With whom the information may be shared
  • What choices are available regarding collection, use and distribution of the information
  • Which security procedures are in place to protect the loss, misuse or alteration of information under the company's control
  • How you can correct any inaccuracies in the information

If you have questions or concerns regarding this statement, you should first contact privacy@nexusenergy.com or by calling 781/694-3300 or via US Mail at 16 Laurel Avenue, Suite 100, Wellesley, MA 02481. If you do not receive acknowledgment of your inquiry or your inquiry has not been satisfactorily addressed, you should then contact Better Business Bureau On-Line who will then serve as a liaison with the Web site to resolve your concerns.

Our Commitment to Privacy

Nexus has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to the privacy of users of our sites and the information that they provide us. Nexus collects and processes information for specified legitimate purposes either directly on the Nexus site or through a Partner site (see Co-Branded and Private Label Partners below). Neither we, nor our Co-Branded and Private Label partners, will give away, trade, sell or release any information obtained from our users. The following headings address how Nexus and our co-branded partners gather and use information.

Co-Branded and Private Label Partners

Nexus partners with other sites (e.g. utility sites) to provide energy-related content to their visitors in a Co-branded or Private Label relationship. You can identify that you are using a co-branded partner site by seeing a "Powered by Nexus" logo present within another party's site. In some cases the Nexus site is operated on a Private Label basis and no "Powered by" logo will appear. In either case, your information is only used for the primary purpose for which it was collected as described throughout this Privacy Statement.

IP Addresses and Cookies

Nexus applications collect the user's IP address and any referring URL. The user's IP address is often not an address that can be traced to their machine, as most users do not employ static IP addresses. Rather, they connect through an Internet Service Provider, a service that uses DHCP or a proxy server and dynamically generates IP addresses. We use the IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to allocate load. IP addresses are also used in aggregate form for traffic analysis.

When a person visits the site, a cookie is created on the user's machine that stores that visitor's user ID. The cookie persists on the user's machine for one year at which point it expires. A second cookie is also created to hold session information. It expires as soon as the session terminates (after twenty minutes). Nexus may utilize cookies to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit the site.

Registration Information

Nexus's registration forms require users to provide contact information including at a minimum, name, ZIP code and email address. We or our Co-Branded or Private Label Partner may use this contact information to send users information about the applications or other related information and to deliver surveys to track their site experiences and notifications of any site performance issues. Users may opt-out of receiving such information. (See the choice/opt-out section below).

Financial Information

Nexus does not collect any financial information.

Analysis Information

For sites that include energy and billing analysis, Nexus stores information that may or may not be personally identifiable, including data provided by the user through the various tools, calculators and other analytical content on the site. This information is collected for internal use only so that we may personalize the site and results for the user. This information is used anonymously at an aggregate level to analyze user data.

Contest Information

From time to time, Nexus or a Co-Branded or Private Label Partner may sponsor contests in which we ask visitors for contact information (such as their e-mail address). We utilize contact data to notify the contest winners. Users may opt-out of receiving future communications (see the choice/opt-out section below).

Links

Nexus sites may contain links to other sites, and is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. We encourage users to read the privacy statements for any pages located on other sites.

Content Partners

On some sites, Nexus partners with other sites to provide content and to make product and service offers to visitors through "Powered By" relationships. You can identify that you are using a partner's content, etc…when you see a "Powered By" logo for such partners within the Nexus site.

Choice/Opt-Out

Nexus offers users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from both our partners and us when we request information from them. Nexus offers users the following options for removing their information from our database in order to stop receiving subsequent communications:

  • Upon registering or entering a contest, you are given the choice of participating in periodic mailings, and may choose to opt-out;
  • You can send email to webmaster@nexusenergy.com;
  • All email communications include the option of opting out of future communications.

Nexus reserves the right to contact you for strictly administrative purposes if it becomes necessary for some unforeseen reason.

Security

Nexus sites have implemented a comprehensive set of security measures to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information under our control. Our servers reside at a secure location provided by Verizon. These servers employ an all-inclusive implementation of Windows security measures that prevent unauthorized system access. All customer information is conducted via encrypted data transfer methods. These measures are reviewed on a regular basis. All Nexus employees are aware of these security procedures via internal education and are periodically briefed on any new security considerations.

Notification of Changes

Nexus reserves the right to change, modify or augment any or all parts of this privacy policy. Any changes will be posted immediately on this privacy page so that users are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it and, under specified situations, how we disclose it. Any changes that may influence the use or storage of user information will first be proposed to all users via e-mail at which point each user will decide whether or not we continue to store and/or use their information. Nexus will only use information in accordance with the privacy policy under which the information was collected.

Correcting/Updating Information

If you need to correct, access or update pertinent personally Identifiable Information obtained through a Nexus-operated site, you can use the contact options listed above.

Disclaimer

Though we make every effort to preserve user privacy, we may need to disclose personal information when required by law wherein we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a current judicial proceeding, a court order or legal process served on one of our sites.
