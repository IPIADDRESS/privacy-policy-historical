For example, we, or our service providers, may track your IP Address when you access Ensogo to assist with ad targeting.

We may use both session Cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and persistent Cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them) to provide you with a more personal and interactive experience with Ensogo Malaysia. Persistent Cookies can be removed by following your Internet browser help file directions. In order to use our services offered through Ensogo Malaysia, your web browser must accept Cookies. If you choose to disable Cookies, some aspects of Ensogo Malaysia may not work properly, and you may not be able to receive our services.

These advertisers and ad servers may use Cookies and/or "Web Beacons" (which are usually small, transparent graphic images) in order to monitor information related to served advertisements.
