Privacy Statement

Data protection

We appreciate your visit to our website and your interest in our company and our products. In doing so, we respect your privacy and ensure the protection of your personal information by processing your personal information in accordance with the terms of this Privacy Policy and the applicable privacy laws.

 

You can visit our websites without telling us who you are. In order to display our web pages, you are only obliged to provide the data transmitted to our server (see “Logfiles”) by your browser. Other personal information about you will only be stored if you voluntarily enter it on the website, or use the appropriate features, e.g. for entries via our contact or application forms.

Contact and other online forms

You may enter your personal information on our website to contact us or to order a trial version of our software. Only data, indicated with an asterisk, is data that is considered to be obligatory and that you are required to provide. Any additional data submitted may be helpful in processing your request, but it is not mandatory (optional). With your consent, these data will only be used and stored for the purpose of processing your message (Art. 6 Abs. 1 S. 1 lit. a DSGVO) or for carrying out your order/request (Art. 6 Abs. 1 S. 1 lit. b DSGVO).

When ordering a trial version, we also process personal data for marketing purposes within the framework of Art. 6 Abs. 1 S. 1 lit. f DSGVO. You may object to advertising use at any time with future effect.

Orders, customer account

To place an order, you must register as a customer. For registration, the data provided with an asterisk must be entered as mandatory information. We process this data for the purpose of carrying out your order and for the necessary communication with you, as well as to guarantee support (Art. 6 Abs. 1 S. 1 lit. b DSGVO). The specification of further data may be helpful for the processing of your order, but it is not compulsory (voluntary information, Art. 6 Abs. 1 S. 1 lit. a DSGVO). You can have your user account deleted by sending an e-mail to us under the contact details listed under “Responsible”. We carry out the deletion after termination of any existing contractual relationship, taking into account any existing statutory retention requirements. We also process your personal data for marketing purposes within the framework of Art. 6 Abs. 1 S. 1 lit. f DSGVO. You may object to advertising use at any time with future effect.

Webinars

You have the opportunity to participate in webinars or to book a webinar via our platform. For this, registration is required and the mandatory information, indicated with an asterisk, that is provided and is for the purpose of carrying out the event, is collected and processed; any further data provided outside the mandatory information required will be considered advantageous, but not mandatory (voluntary information). The legal basis for data processing is Art. 6 Abs. 1 S. 1 lit. b und a DSGVO. The event will be carried out via a technical platform of a technical service provider, which processes the personal data on our behalf. We use this data solely for the purpose of conducting the webinar (and any related billing purposes).

Our forum can be read without requiring registration. If you would like to actively participate in the forum, you must register yourself by entering your e-mail address, a password of your own choice, and your own username. Cookies

Cookies are very small text files used by internet sites, which your browser stores on your computer and allows certain information to flow through the site that sets the cookie (here, through us). Cookies can not run programs or transmit viruses to your computer.
Transient cookies are automatically deleted when you close the browser. These include in particular the session cookies. These store a so-called session ID, with which various requests from your browser can be assigned to the common session. This will allow your computer to be recognized when you return to our website. The session cookies are deleted when you log out or close the browser.
Persistent cookies are automatically deleted after a specified period, which may differ depending on the cookie. You can delete the cookies in the security settings of your browser at any time.
This information is used to optimize our offerings and to offer you more comfort while browsing our pages. The legal basis for this data processing is Art. 6 Abs. 1 S. 1 lit. f DSGVO.
You can set your internet browser to notify you as soon as a web server wants to send a cookie to you. You can then agree to the acceptance of the cookie, or reject it. You can configure your browser settings according to your wishes. For example, you can choose to decline the acceptance of third-party cookies, or all cookies. Please be aware that you may not be able to use all the features of this site if cookies are disabled. You can find out more about this in the help system of your Internet browser.
