Privacy Policy

Last revision: March 1, 2009

Enjin has been developed for all gamers, clans, guilds, and gaming communities, to organize and stay connected through the creation of gaming websites and profiles. This Privacy Policy is designed to articulate our privacy policies and how we collect and use the information you choose to share. We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time and without any notice. Please note, that by using Enjin Services you accept this policy.

Information Collection

Enjin collects information that you choose to disclose, and information while you interact with our services. For all visitors, we may collect and store information through "cookies" such as your IP and browser type. A "cookie" is a data segment or message which contains information about a user. We use session ID cookies to confirm if users are logged in. For your privacy we terminate these session ID cookies every 30 days or you may clear your cookies in your browser at anytime.

While using Enjin the information you provide and we collect is retained to enhance the services that we provide. We may store information prior to any updates you may make to your account as well. The information you provide and modify is at your own risk. Please understand that we do make reasonable efforts to ensure the safety of this information, however, no security measures are perfect. Additionally, we cannot control how others may use the information you choose to share and cannot assure that unwanted persons are restricted to viewing your information. Although your information may be removed or modified please be aware that your content may still be viewable through the means of cached or archived pages. At no point will we ever provide or exploit the personally-identifying information you provided to companies or organizations for commercial purposes without your prior consent.

Registration - Free

In order to use Enjin services, you must complete the registration process which requires the creation of a username 'Enjin ID' and password. During the registration process the only personally-identifiable information we request is your email address. We will use this information to contact you about the services on our site in which you have expressed interest.

Registration - Paid

We do offer paid services in addition to our free offerings. In order to use our paid services, you must complete the paid registration process which requires the creation of a username 'Enjin ID' and password. During this registration process you may be required to provide personally-identifiable information such as your name, email address, address, and phone number. We use this information for payment processing and verification, and for contacting and billing you about the services on our site in which you have expressed interest. We will not request any credit card information as all personally-identifiable financial information is handled by our third-party merchant solution.

Upgrading to Paid Services

During the use of our free services you have the ability to upgrade to a paid account at any time. In order to use our paid services, you must complete the upgrade process which may require you to provide personally-identifiable information such as your name, email address, address, and phone number. We use this information for payment processing and verification, and for contacting and billing you about the services on our site in which you have expressed interest. We will not request any credit card information as all personally-identifiable financial information is handled by our third-party merchant solution.

Contacting

If you are contacting our firm through our website, we require that you provide personally-identifiable information, including your name, email, and phone number. This information is necessary to process your request and to contact you. We will use this information to communicate special offers, updates, newsletters, service-related announcements, and customer service requests.

Invite-A-Gamer

If you choose to use our referral service to tell another gamer about our site, we will ask you for the gamer's email address. We will automatically send the gamer a one-time email inviting him or her to join the site. We store this information for the sole purpose of sending this one-time email.

Enjin is the sole owner of the information collected on Enjin.com.

Profile

We store the information that we collect to create a profile of your preferences. We tie your personally-identifiable information, and your purchasing history to information in your profile in order to provide tailored promotions and marketing offers to improve the content of the site for you. You are not required to provide any additional personally-identifiable information on your profile, however feel free to submit as much information as possible so we can provide you with a more personalized experience on our site.

Advertising

Enjin does offer free services which are ad-supported. Therefore, we allow advertisers to campaign through our services. The information you provide will allow for better targeting and placement of their advertisements however at no time will we provide or sell any of your personally-identifying information to the advertisers without your prior consent.

  • Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on your site.
  • Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

At no point will we provide or exploit your personally-identifying information to companies or organizations for commercial purposes without your prior consent.
