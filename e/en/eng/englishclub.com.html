Privacy Policy

EnglishClub is firmly committed to privacy on the Internet. This means that we consider that any information you give us is confidential. We are committed to keeping it confidential unless you give us your specific permission otherwise.

Personal information
We collect only information that is voluntarily provided by our visitors, and we use it only for the purpose for which it was provided. We securely log this information and discard it after a reasonable time.

This information is not shared with or sold to any third party, and would only be made available to certain law enforcement agencies in the event of a criminal offence and a resulting court order requiring us to comply. This information is not collected for advertising purposes, and is never shared with advertisers.

Advertising

  • Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on EnglishClub.
  • Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to EnglishClub users based on their visit to our sites and other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

Questions or concerns
If you have any questions about EnglishClub's privacy policy or practices, or about your dealings with this website, please do not hesitate to contact us.
