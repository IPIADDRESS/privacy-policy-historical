Policy overview: 
Safeguarding your privacy is serious business. That's why English, baby!'s policy is simple: Except as provided in this statement, English, baby! will not disclose, sell or rent any personally identifiable information without proper consent. You, the English, baby! user, always have the final word on whether English, baby! knows who you are. It is your choice whether you wish to share personally identifiable information with English, baby!. We encourage you to consider sharing information with English, baby! only if you receive true value in exchange for that information. Please use discretion when revealing personally identifiable information while using English, baby! tools, as it may be collected and used by unauthorized third parties. Such activities are beyond the control of English, baby!. In other words, if for some reason you post your home phone number on the English, baby! message boards (which you should NOT do), you're on your own. Please take a minute to read our policy so that you understand your options and how English, baby! collects, processes and shares information. If you have any questions about English, baby!'s information practices, send an email to English, baby!'s Policy Manager.

The following is a summary overview of English, baby!'s privacy policy. Click on the "more" button after each section to read the complete description of English, baby!'s information collection practices.
What information does English, baby! collect?
If you decide to join English, baby!, registration is the only place where you are required to submit information about yourself. To become an English, baby! member you need to submit your email address (which can be your new English, baby! email address), gender, birthdate, zip or postal code and the name of the country where you live.
What are cookies and how does English, baby! use them?
The main function of cookies is to connect members with their stored account information so that English, baby! can deliver better and more personalized service. Cookies do not enable the English, baby! staff to access personally identifiable information that you haven't already provided.
How does English, baby! use member information?
English, baby! uses information collected from you and other members primarily to provide more personalized service, and to learn more about its audience demographic. English, baby! also collects market research data in order to generate sales to corporate clients. Market research data are always anonymous and are never used to sell anything to individual respondents based on their personal responses.
What information does English, baby! log?
English, baby! logs information automatically transmitted from your browser (URL of the web page you were on before coming to English, baby!, IP address and browser version); information you submit through registration, forms for optional programs, surveys and other entries; and communications transmitted through English, baby! tools.
What is English, baby!'s information retention policy?
To preserve our database, English, baby! retains information submitted by members for an indefinite length of time. English, baby! understands your submissions as consent to store all your information in one place for this indefinite length of time. English, baby! will nullify (scramble so that it is unrecognizable) account information if required by law or in response to a written member request.
How does English, baby! secure collected information?
English, baby! makes every effort to make sure your information is secure. Your English, baby! account is password-protected so that only you and authorized English, baby! employees can access your information.
With whom does English, baby! share information?
When you submit information to English, baby!, it is shared only with the owners of the referring educational program web site. English, baby! reserves the right to disclose user information if required by law or if English, baby! has reason to believe that the law is being violated.
What is English, baby!'s opt-in/out policy?
During registration you choose whether to receive correspondence from either English, baby! or its partners, and whether you want to participate in a variety of English, baby! programs. This information lives on your Profile where you can, at any point, easily edit it to indicate that you have changed your mind. As for promotions, contests, submission opportunities and programs, you will not be included unless you opt-in.
How can English, baby! members edit their personal information?
English, baby! believes strongly in giving you the ability to access and edit your personal information. You can always update the personal information in your Profile by clicking on the "Update Profile" link on the site. You can edit your Profile or account settings at any time -- all you need is your member name and password.
What action has English, baby! taken to protect children's privacy?
Because English, baby! is intended for people ages 15 and over, its registration policy is intentionally designed to identify people who are 14 or younger. English, baby! will not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. English, baby! has developed a process to verify and delete accounts belonging to members who are discovered to be under 13.
Note to Parents:
If your child is under 13 and has become an English, baby! member using a false age, please notify English, baby!'s Policy Manager, who will immediately close the account and nullify all personally identifiable information collected from them. If your child is under the age of 18 and you would like to know more about English, baby!'s site, information practices, stored information or how to close your child's account, contact the Policy Manager.
Notification of Changes:
English, baby!'s security and privacy policy are periodically reviewed and enhanced as necessary. This policy might change as English, baby! updates and expands its services. English, baby! will contact you via email and post notification if there are any material changes in its privacy practices. English, baby! also encourages you to also check the privacy policy out periodically.
How can I reach English, baby! staff?
If you have technical problems, questions or other issues related to your use of English, baby!, please email us. English, baby!'s Policy Manager will respond to all requests sent through mail or email from members and parents interested in knowing more about personal information stored on the English, baby! database, having their personal information nullified, or have additional questions regarding privacy.

What information does English, baby! collect?
English, baby! wants to be your favorite online destination. To encourage your participation, your English, baby! experience is personalized so that you are offered the services, products and information most relevant to you. Below is a summary of the different ways English, baby! collects various kinds of information about members:

If you decide to join English, baby!, registration is the only place where you will be required to submit information about yourself. Public forums (privacy warning)

One last thing to keep in mind: Whenever you voluntarily disclose personal information on message boards, through email, English, baby! Member Galleries, Alumni Spotlights, Message Boards, or in chat, it can be collected and used by anyone, including law-enforcement agencies acting under subpoena power. If you post personal information in public places, others may send you unsolicited email messages. Always be smart about the information you provide online. And, be aware that if English, baby! employees find personally identifiable information posted in public areas, they will delete it.

What are cookies, and how does English, baby! use them?
English, baby! collects anonymous information through cookies. A cookie is a small piece of data containing a unique identification number that is sent to your browser from a web server, and is stored on your computer's hard drive. The unique ID number identifies your browser to English, baby!'s computers whenever you visit. Cookies do not enable anyone to access personally identifiable information that you have not already given us. English, baby! primarily uses cookies to connect members with account information stored on English, baby!'s computers. This helps English, baby! deliver better and more personalized service. English, baby! also uses cookies to estimate English, baby!'s audience size for advertisers; to track the number of entries in English, baby! promotions, sweepstakes and contests; to identify the areas of the site you visit; to hold session information; and to track user preferences.

Cookies are part of the technology implemented by your browser. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies. You can reset your browser to either notify you when you have received a cookie, or to refuse them all together. To find out more about cookies on Netscape, click here. For information about cookies on Microsoft Internet Explorer, click here.

English, baby! uses both permanent and temporary cookies. Permanent cookies stay with your browser, whereas temporary cookies go away after you close your browser. English, baby! uses permanent cookies in order to recognize you between visits. English, baby! uses temporary cookies to store information about your current visit to the site. That information goes away when you close your browser or shut down your computer. If you choose to not accept cookies, you cannot participate as a registered, signed-in member.

How does English, baby! use member information?
English, baby!'s decisions regarding the use of information collected from English, baby! members are based on two areas of interest: market research, and improving user experience by offering personalized service and content.

English, baby! may analyze information provided directly by you, or obtained through cookies or third parties to direct services and content based on your interests. This is done in the aggregate (for example, let's say half of English, baby!'s members are females who visit the boards more than the chat rooms) and at the individual level (for example, English, baby! User1234 mostly visits the Eavesdropping Lesson). Again, this helps the staff produce and appropriately feature tools and content tailored to your interests.

English, baby! collects market research data in order to generate sales to corporate clients. Market research data are always anonymous and are never used to sell anything to individual respondents based on their personal responses. Market research data, however, may be used at the aggregate level to:
1. Increase the likelihood that a group's experience on English, baby! is relevant to its interests as a whole
2. Formulate group-level statistical models that are used to accomplish goal #1 (above).

English, baby!'s market research data is collected in two ways:
1. Among individuals who opt-in to take surveys during the registration process or while on English, baby! (those who sign up are contacted and take surveys through email).
2. Through surveys that we publicly post on English, baby! (it is never mandatory to answer these surveys).

English, baby! uses data at the individual level to sell products and services to English, baby! members who express an interest in these products and services, through a poll for example, or to members who can be presumed to have an interest based on results from a statistical analysis.

How does English, baby! secure collected information?
Your English, baby! Profile is password-protected so that only you and authorized English, baby! employees have access to your account information. In order to maintain this protection, do not give your password to anyone. If anyone ever asks you for information claiming they are English, baby! staff, check their Member Profile -- if it doesn't say they are staff, they're not. Also, if you share a computer, you should sign out of your English, baby! account and close the browser window before someone else logs on. This will protect your information on public terminals.

English, baby! makes every effort to ensure that your information is secure on its system. Only certain authorized English, baby! employees can access this information if it's relevant to their job duties. English, baby! has staff dedicated to maintaining this Privacy Policy and other privacy initiatives, periodically reviewing web security and making sure that every English, baby! employee is aware of our security practices. Unfortunately, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed 100% secure. As a result, English, baby! cannot guarantee the security of any information you transmit to us, and you do so at your own risk.
