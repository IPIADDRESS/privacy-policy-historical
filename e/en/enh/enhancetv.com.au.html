Introduction
 
EnhanceTV understands the importance of privacy to its members and other users of this web site. This privacy statement outlines how EnhanceTV will use, store and protect any information you provide to us when you visit this web site. By using the site you consent to the uses described.
 
Information Logged
 
Apart from any personal information that you may choose to provide to us, when you visit this web site our server will gather some information provided by your browser. This may include:
  • the date and time of your visit;
  • the pages you access and documents you download;
  • your server's IP address; and
  • the address of the referring site (if you have been referred to this web site from another site).
 
We gather this information to monitor site use. This enables us to improve and enhance the web site and make it more user-friendly and relevant to visitors.
 
Personal Information
 
Sometimes we may specifically ask to provide personal information. This may include your name, postal address, internet or email address, telephone and facsimile numbers. We may also ask you about your particular interests.
 

This information may be used to inform you of upcoming events, television programs or other sites or publications which may be of interest to you. You can choose not to receive such information.
 
Cookies
 
Cookies were originally designed to assist a web site to distinguish a user as a prior visitor and to recall any prior preferences of that user.
 
A cookie is a small string of text that a site sends to your browser. A cookie cannot retrieve any other data from your hard drive, pass on computer viruses or capture your email address. Cookies are used to enhance a user's visit. For example, cookies can securely store a user's ID and password, personalise home pages, identify previously visited parts of a site and keep track of selections in a "shopping cart".
 
If you become a registered customer (with a designated ID and password) EnhanceTV may use cookies to provide you with personalised information which we feel, based on the preferences you have made when visiting this web site, might be of interest and value to you.
 
Use of Information
 
EnhanceTV will not sell, share, rent or receive payment for licensing or disclosing your personal information.
 
There will, of course, be times when it will be necessary for EnhanceTV to disclose your personal information to third parties. EnhanceTV may be required to do so by law or in order to provide you with the service you have requested. However, any disclosure of personal information will only be made where it is necessary to fulfil the purpose for which you disclosed the information or where you have specifically authorised us to do so.
 
Access to Information
 
Whenever it is possible, EnhanceTV will provide you with access to your personal information to ensure that it is accurate, complete and up to date. If your personal information is not accurate, complete or up to date please inform EnhanceTV and we will take all reasonable steps to correct the information.
 
Security of Information
 
Whilst no transmission to and from web sites can ever be totally secure, EnhanceTV will take all reasonable steps to ensure that the information you provide to us when visiting this site is kept secure. All transactions which involve the provision of personal information for the ordering of video tapes is fully encrypted by our secure server. Opting Out.
