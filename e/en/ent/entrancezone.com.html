How We Collect and Use Information

User Registration

We ask for certain information such as your username, address, phone number and e-mail address when you register for a entrancezone account. We use this information to operate, maintain, and provide to you the features and functionality of the Services. entrancezone.com provides for visitors who want to ask questionnaires from counsellor or post conmment to the News or access registration-only pages. To obtain a user registration, you must submit your first name, last name, email address,state,city and education interest to entrancezone.com, along with a username and password that you create for user authentication purposes. At your option you may submit a photo as well, but a photo is not required for registration purposes. The Site will include a profile page that will include your first name, last name, username, and, if applicable, your photo. Site will publish your username alongside any comment that you post.

Google Analytics

entrancezone.com uses Google Analytics software to perform Site usage analytics. Google Analytics collects anonymous information from users to help entrancezonce to track Site usage and referrals from other websites. These data are used primarily to optimize the website experience for our visitors, but we may use the data as well to assist us in developing marketing plans for the Site.
