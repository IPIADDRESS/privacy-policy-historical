All the information you submit is used solely in conjunction with executing the affiliate program as described, and none of the information is shared in any manner with any third party.

How We Use Information
 An explanation of the information we collect, and how we use it is detailed here.  To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
