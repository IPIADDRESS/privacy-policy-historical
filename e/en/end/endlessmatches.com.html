Endless Matches's Privacy Statement
Endless Matches is the sole owner of the information it gathers on its site. Endless Matches will only share your information with outside parties in ways that are described in this privacy statement or if we are required to do so by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary in order to conform to the edicts of the law, cooperate with law enforcement agencies, or comply with a legal process served on our Web site.



What Information is Collected and How it is Used?
  • Adding Your Site
    We collect contact information such as name, email address, IP address, and company information such as company name, Web site description and URL. This information is used to add your website to the directory.

  • Contacting Us
    We use an email form located on the "contact us" page and other areas of the site to allow you to contact us directly with any questions or comments you may have. We read every message sent in and try to reply promptly to every one. This information is used to respond directly to your questions or comments. We may also file your comments to improve the site and functionality, or review and discard the information. Your personal information is only shared with third parties with your explicit permission.

  • Logged Files and Cookies
    The Endless Matches Web site logs IP addresses and browser types for search engine and reader trend purposes. These logs will be analyzed to improve the value of the directory. A user's session will be tracked, but the user will be anonymous. We do, however link IP addresses to any "site submissions" to track our sales and marketing campaigns. This information does not contain any personally identifiable information.

    This Web site does use cookies in the forums.

    Third Party Links
    Endless Matches lists websites in virtually every subject available. Given the nature of the field that we are in, we link to thousands of different websites. Please note that these Web sites' privacy policies may differ from those of Endless Matches. We encourage you to read the privacy statement of any Web site you may visit.

Choices Regarding the Use of Your Information

Security
Protecting your privacy and your information is a top priority at Endless Matches. We have taken careful measures to prevent the loss, misuse, and alteration of your information and our database. Once we receive your information that was entered into our web site, it is stored into a secure mysql database. Your information is only accessible to our employees who need it in order to perform their jobs.



Notification of Changes
If we are going to use your personally identifiable information in a manner different from that stated at the time of collection through this Web site, we will notify you via email. You will have a choice as to whether or not we use your information in this different manner. In addition, if we make any material changes in our privacy practices that do not affect user information already collected through our site, we will post a prominent notice on our web site notifying users of the change.

Aquarius

The same passion, drive and urgency that Mars, planet of passion and the warrior planet of the cosmos gave to your work situation over the last 2 months he is now investing into your relationships, ready to stir things up if need be. With the Sun, Mercury, Venus and Ceres having all moved through your relationship sector over the last few months this has left you with a clear sense of what you want from your relationships and what they need from you. It's time now to start walking the talk.

Taurus

As Mars, planet of passion and the warrior planet of the cosmos spends his first full week in your home and family sector he's able to draw on the work that has already been done. You've reached a point where you should know what you want and need and with most, if not all your options on the table, it's now all about picking which battles you're going to wage now and which you'll keep as long term objectives.

Gemini

Just 6 days after Mars returned to your communication sector to take over from where the other planets left off, the Moon returns to help stir things up. Where communication has been an important theme over the last few months Mars is here to take things to a whole new level, focused more on breaking through old barriers and achieving some real communication breakthroughs. It's by giving your emotional responses a voice that some real healing is possible.

Cancer

This is an extremely auspicious time for both friendship and relationship building, especially now that you're not only feeling a lot more confident but with the communication lines now open. With a New Moon in your communication sector in just 2 days time and with the support of forces on the friendship and relationship fronts, this is a day for reaching out, taking down your defences and becoming more of a team player.

Leo

In his early days in your sign Mars, planet of passion and the warrior planet of the cosmos is already evoking your fighting spirit, giving you the energy and drive to start fighting for what you want and deserve, across the board. This would have been the case anyway, but with an alliance between forces on the income, work and career fronts coming together this is likely to see your competitive spirit come out of hibernation.

Virgo

As the Moon makes the first of two visits he'll make to an intuitive and imaginative part of your chart while Mars, warrior planet of the cosmos is here, this is likely to inspire some vivid daydreams and fantasies. This is a cocktail of forces that is best served with your rose coloured glasses on, for this is part of the process of exploring what's possible, as well as a chance to change the way you look at and perceive things.

Libra

The Moon's return to a social and serendipitous part of your chart will always give you an intuitive sense of timing, read on people and situations and the kind of hunches that if followed can put you in the right place at the right time. Yet returning for his first visit since Mars returned last week this will meet a new and bolder attitude, one where you're a lot more willing to take a bold leap of faith and to trust what your gut is telling you.

Scorpio

As the Moon makes his first visit to your career sector since Mars, warrior planet of the cosmos returned last week, this is a chance to put your professional instincts, passions and fighting spirit on the same page, getting a better read on what excites you. Mars returned last week to find most of your options already on the table, your professional desires and expectations and plenty of ideas on the table and now it's more about deciding which battles to commit to.

Sagittarius

Despite the fact that you're just 3 days away from a New Moon in your career sector and with Mercury in his final week there is a real sense of urgency, a sense of wanderlust and some very active daydreams are likely to make it hard to focus. With a passion for adventure inspired play hooky if you can, with blowing off some cobwebs while you can more likely to improve your professional creativity.

Capricorn

Just 6 days after Mars, planet of passion and the warrior planet of the cosmos returned to your financial sector to take over from where the other planets had left off, the Moon makes a timely return. Coming full circle from last month's New Moon and with so much already invested, as your financial instincts, passions and fighting spirit come together this will give you a much better sense of what you're fighting for, with a chance to pick your battles wisely.

Pisces

Having the Moon return to your work sector while Mars is still in his first week, gives you a chance to get your bearings and a better read on the fighting spirit, drives and passions he's evoking. With a lot of the hard work already done before Mars got here, especially when it came to establishing where things stand and getting all your options on the table, the Moon's intuitive edge allows you to channel Mars' fighting spirit more effectively.

Aries

As the Moon makes his first visit to a playful part of your chart since Mars, warrior planet of the cosmos returned, this gives intuitive access to the romantic and creative passions that are just starting to take hold.
