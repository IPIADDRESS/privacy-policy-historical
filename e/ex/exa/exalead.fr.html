Exalead.com/software Privacy Policy

Please Review Before Using This Site

Exalead will not disclose personal information to third parties (other than to our partners for the purposes of fulfilling specific requests of a user) that would uniquely identify a without a user's express permission.
A user's information will only be disclosed when compelled by legal order from law enforcement officials or courts of competent jurisdiction, or for Exalead's legal protection.
Exalead collects information from its users in several areas of the site:

  • Exalead uses "cookie" technology to obtain non-personal information from on-line visitors, such as tracking user movement through the site. Exalead does not, however, use cookies to collect personally identifiable information.
  • Certain portions of the Site (such as registering for events or downloading resources) may require the submission of personal information, such as name, address, and e-mail. This information is only used to contact users as necessary, to fulfill requests. Should disclosure inadvertently occur, Exalead will use reasonable efforts to limit and remedy the disclosure, but will accept no liability for such disclosure.
  • Some portions of the Site may permit personal information to be posted in a publicly accessible area. If you decide to use those portions of the Site and post personal information, such information will become and remain public.

Users may change the information submitted to Exalead, either at the area of the Site where it was first provided, or by contacting Exalead.
Should you not agree with these policies, you should not use the Exalead Site.
