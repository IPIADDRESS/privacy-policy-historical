Privacy Policy

Types of Information we collect

Personally Identifiable Information: We do not collect any information about you, we also do not use cookies. Only personal information which is stored is the information you fill in the signup form of new account.

Protection of Personal Identifiable Information: We have implemented industry standard encryption and security solutions to protect our servers and databases against intrusion. Sensitive information related to payment is stored on the servers of the billing companies, all the rest (as your personal information) is encrypted and stored on a separate server with no direct write-access. Access to our site and server control panel is transmitted through the internet using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology. SSL technology works with your browser to encrypt your order information before transmitting it to our processing facility.

Conclusion: Any information submitted by the buyer will be used solely for the purpose of completing the transaction, delivering the product and addressing any customer service issues. Any information you give us at our web site is held in strict confidence and will not be used in unauthorized ways.

By creating new account customer agree with our terms & conditions in full range.
