General Privacy

ExchangeRate.com, Inc. respects your privacy and seeks to protect your personal data. Please read on to learn how we obtain, use and protect any personal data.

Information ExchangeRate.com, Inc. collects and why.
ExchangeRate.com, Inc. collects information about our web site visitors. The first bit of data that we collect is a browser cookie. In addition, when registering, using our tell-a-friend tool, when posting comments, and when submitting forms on ExchangeRate.com, ExchangeRate.com may ask for your email address, and whether or not you would like to receive information directly from our partners and advertisers. If you uncheck check the checkbox, we will not allow our partners and advertisers to contact you directly via email. If you provide your email address to ExcangeRate.com, we may occasionally send you email announcements from ExchangeRate.com regarding new features on our web site, special events, news, or information from our partners, sponsors, and advertisers. ExchangeRate.com will not give or sell your email address to any of our partners, sponsors or advertisers unless you have agreed by leaving the checkbox checked thus agreeing to receive information directly from our partners and advertisers.

In addition, a log of all users visiting ExchangeRate.com is kept. This is for security reasons. ExchangeRate.com, Inc. often runs specialized programs to analyze these logs for various reasons including port scanning attempts, hacking attempts, and other reasons related to the security of our web site. In the event that domestic or foreign visitors are suspected of illegal activites by ExchangeRate.com, Inc. we reserve the right and WILL turn over server logs and reports to local, state, and federal authorities.

ExchangeRate.com uses various Networks and other third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies please look up the individual privacy policies of these ad serving companies.

Third Party Cookies
In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser. Information shared with "third party" advertisers is usually non-personally-identifiable.
