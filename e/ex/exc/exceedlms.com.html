Information Gathering and Usage

The following types of user information (“Information”) are gathered:

• Personal Information. We collect personal information (“Personal Information”) for you and those individuals that you have given Web Site access to.  This Personal Information is used for billing purposes, account management purposes and for you to utilize the features of the Web Sites you have purchased access to.  Examples of Personal Information include but are not limited to: first name , such as your name, mailing address, email address, billing information, etc. We also collect Personal Information provided to us via any emails you send to us and via any user information added by you to your Exceed account (including Personal Information for users other than yourself).

• Training Information. We collect information on learning/training activities within the Learning Management System (“LMS”)

We collect this Information for the following general purposes: products and services provisioning, billing, identification and authentication, Web Site improvement, contact, and research.
