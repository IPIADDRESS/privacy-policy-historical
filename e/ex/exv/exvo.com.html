Tolq.com Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Tolq.com's Privacy Policy applies to and covers the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information that may be collected by Tolq.com anytime you interact with Tolq.com and its services, such as when you visit our website, when you purchase or use Tolq.com products, services, or when you call our sales or support associates. The scope of this privacy policy covers all of Tolq.com. It does not apply to entities that Tolq.com does not own or control. By accessing or using Tolq.com and/or any related products/services you agree to our Privacy Policy.

Please take a moment to read the following privacy practices to learn more about our information practices, including what type of information is gathered through our services, how the information is used and for what purposes, to whom we disclose the information, and how we safeguard your personal information. Your privacy is very important to Tolq.com, and we go to great lengths to protect it.

2. Why is personal information collected?

We collect your personal information because it helps us deliver a superior level of customer experience. It enables us to give you convenient access to our products, services and focus on categories of most interest to you. In addition, your personal information helps us in keeping you posted on the latest services announcements, software updates, special offers, and events that you might like to hear about.

When you sign up for your Tolq Account you provide us several items of personal information relevant to this situation, such as your name, email address, phone number, contact preferences, IP address, credit card information, information about the Tolq product(s) you use/subscribe to, and information relating to a support or service issue.

To access some of the Tolq.com products/services, you will be asked to sign up/in with an email address and password, to which we refer as in this privacy policy as your ‘Tolq Account’.

We may retain the details of transactions or banking and payment information about payments you make on Tolq.com (credit card information, account number).

Personal information when you're discussing a service issue on the phone with an associate, downloading information, registering for a seminar, participating in an online survey, or purchasing a product.

We may use personal information to provide products that you have requested as well as for auditing, research, and analysis to improve Tolq.com's products.

We gather information about your usage of, and interaction with the Tolq.com software products/services and websites including computer and connection information, device capability, bandwidth, traffic to and from our website and statistics on page views.

In order to offer you a more consistent and personalized Tolq.com experience, information collected through one of our products/services may be combined with other information obtained through other products/services you are using through Tolq.com.

The next time you purchase a new product or service from Tolq.com, all you need to do is enter your Tolq Account and password; the system looks up the information it needs to assist you. In addition, if you update the information associated with your Tolq Account it will be available for all your transactions with Tolq.com globally.

11. Cookies & other technologies

As is standard practice on many corporate websites, Tolq.com's website uses ‘cookies’ to enable you to sign in to our services and to help personalizing your online experience. A cookie can be described as a small pieces of data we store for an extended period of time on your computer, or any other device. Cookies contain information that can later be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.

One of the primary purposes of cookies is to store your preferences. These technologies help us to better understand user behavior, which parts of our websites are the most popular, where visitors are going and how much time they spent there and so on. We also use cookies and other technologies to make sure that our online advertising is bringing customers to our products and services. We use cookies and other technologies to study traffic patterns on our website, to make it even more rewarding as well as to study the effectiveness of our customer communications. By using cookies we can customize your experience and provide greater convenience each time you interact with us.

As user you have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most of the web browsers automatically accept cookies, but normally you can modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. Please take the note that if you decline cookies, you may not be able to sign in or use other interactive features of Tolq.com. If you accept cookies you have the ability to later delete these cookies that you have accepted.

As is true of most websites, we gather certain information automatically and store it in log files. This information includes Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and click stream data. This information and data is used by us to understand and analyze trends, to administer the site, to learn about the user behavior on the site and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. Tolq.com is entitled to use this information, which does not identify individual users, in our marketing and advertisement services.

In some of our email messages we use a ‘click-through URL’ linked to content on the Tolq.com website. When customers click one of these URLs, they pass through our web server before arriving at the destination web page. We track this click-through data to help us determine interest in particular topics and measure the effectiveness of our customer communications. If you prefer not to be tracked simply avoid clicking text or graphic links in the email.
