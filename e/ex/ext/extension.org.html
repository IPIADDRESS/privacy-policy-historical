eXtension Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to eXtension. We have developed this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our commitment to safeguarding the privacy of those who use the eXtension web site so you'll feel comfortable using this site. This privacy statement will provide you with information regarding what personal information is collected during your visits to the eXtension web site, how it is used, with whom it may be shared and what choices you have regarding collection and distribution of your personal information.

What personal information may be collected?

We collect personally identifiable information, including your name and address, when you register with eXtension or update your account information. We may also ask for information about your areas of interest to provide relevant resources to better serve your needs. You may choose not to share your geographical information or areas of interest, but we encourage you to do so to receive a more personalized experience with eXtension.

You can choose information you want to share during the registration process. See Choice/Opt-Out for more information. This site does not use tracking cookies to collect information about users.

How will my information be used?

Information collected during registration may be used to provide tailored information and resources for each user based on areas of interest and geographical location. Geographical information may be used to provide a customized home page featuring relevant local information and news. Information collected on your areas of interest may be used to provide you with publications and news related to your interests on your personalized home page.

You have the option to allow eXtension to share your personally identifiable information with local Cooperative Extension offices to better serve your needs. You may also choose to receive local Extension newsletters and other communications. To ensure your privacy, you will be provided with a way to unsubscribe from any newsletters you may receive. See Choice/Opt-Out for more information.

Information sharing and disclosure

We do not share or sell your personally identifiable information to third parties. You will have the choice to opt-out of any sharing of personal information with our partner universities or local Extension offices.

While browsing the eXtension site, you may sometimes encounter links to other companies or agencies that are not controlled by eXtension or its partners. Please be aware that eXtension is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage you to be cautious when leaving the eXtension web site and carefully read the privacy statements of other web sites that collect your personally identifiable information.

If you use a bulletin board or chat room on this site, you should be aware that any personally identifiable information you submit there can be read, collected or used by other users of these forums, and could be used to send you unsolicited messages. We are not responsible for the personally identifiable information you choose to submit in these forums.

Access to personally identifiable information

If your personally identifiable information changes, or if you no longer want to be a part of the eXtension web site, you may correct, update, or deactivate it by making the change on your account information page or by emailing eXtension at privacy@eXtension.org

Choice/Opt-Out

When you register to use the eXtension web site, you will be asked to share personally identifiable information. To use this site, the only information you are required to provide is your name and password. You may choose not to share other personally identifiable information. We provide you the opportunity to "opt-out" of having your personally identifiable information used for certain purposes, including sharing your information with local Extension offices, when we ask for this information.

If you choose to receive local newsletters or other communications and later decide you no longer wish to subscribe, you may opt out of receiving them by following the unsubscribe instructions included in each newsletter.

Security

We are committed to protecting the personal information you submit to us, and we follow generally accepted industry standards, including physical, managerial, and electronic procedures, to secure your information, both during transmission and once we receive it. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, however. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. If you have questions about security on our Web site, please contact eXtension at privacy@eXtension.org

Contact information

If you have any questions regarding eXtension's privacy policy, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this site, please contact eXtension at privacy@eXtension.org
