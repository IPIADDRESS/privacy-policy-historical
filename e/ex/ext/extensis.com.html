What personally identifiable information does Extensis Corporation collect from me?

Extensis Corporation stores the Internet Protocol (IP) address of your computer when you visit our site. We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, to administer our Web site, and to gather broad demographic information.

Some personal information is gathered when you register, purchase, or download a product. At that time we may request your name and email address. This information is solely utilized internally at Extensis Corporation, for the purpose of measuring demographics, response, and marketing of special offers.

In addition to registration we may ask you for personal information at other times, including (but not limited to) when you purchase on-line, submit a question to our Technical Support Team, or if you report a problem with our site or one of our services. Wherever Extensis Corporation collects personal information we make every effort to offer you the opportunity to opt-in or opt-out of future communications.

What are “cookies” and how does Extensis Corporation use them?

A cookie is a small data file that certain Web sites write to your hard drive when you visit them. A cookie file can contain information such as a user ID that the site uses to track the pages that have been visited.

But the only personal information a cookie can contain is information you supply yourself. A cookie can’t read data off your hard disk or read cookie files created by other sites. Extensis Corporation uses cookies to track user traffic patterns (as described above), when you purchase on-line or download a product. In addition, cookie technology is often used to offer on-line product discounts to existing Extensis customers.

Generally, we use cookies for:

  1. Downloads: when you download a demo or a product at extensis.com, we use a cookie to store your user ID. We do not store additional personal information about you. We use this ID to identify you in our database, to save you from having to fill out our registration form if you have already registered, and to track page visits.
  2. Purchases: when you purchase a product at extensis.com, we use a cookie to store your user ID. We do not store additional personal information about you. We do not store financial data about you. We use this ID to identify you in our database, to save you from having to fill out our registration form if you have already registered, and to track page visits.

If you’ve set your browser to warn you before accepting cookies, you will receive the warning message with each cookie.

You can refuse cookies by turning them off in your browser. You do not need to have cookies turned on to use our site. However, you do need cookies to receive select promotional and discount offers.

  • As stated above, Extensis Corporation uses information that users voluntarily provide in order to send out electronic offers in which we think our customers might be particularly interested. Extensis Corporation never rents or exchanges our electronic mailing lists with any third parties.

This helps us continue to build a site with the information that interests you.

What are my choices regarding collection, use, and distribution of my information?

If you indicated upon registration that you are interested in receiving offers or information from Extensis, we may occasionally send you e-mail about product updates or special offers and services that we feel might be of interest to you. Only Extensis Corporation (or agents working on behalf of Extensis Corporation and under confidentiality agreements) will send you these e-mailings and only if you indicated that you do not object to these offers.

If you do not want to receive such mailings, simply tell us when you register your information with Extensis. Or, at any time you can choose to unsubscribe from our mailing list by visiting our Unsubscribe URL: www.extensis.com/unsubscribe. Once you are unsubscribed, you will no longer receive such offers or mailings. Please allow two to three weeks for unsubscribe requests to take effect. In addition, you are also able to unsubscribe to our mailing list by clicking on an URL in our newsletter or by sending mail with “remove” in the subject line to either listmaster@extensis.com or postmaster@extensis.com. Extensis Corporation does not sell or rent email information about individual users.

Correcting and updating personal information?

You may change any data we have in your profile at any time by sending an email to postmaster@extensis.com or to listmaster@extensis.com. In addition, any time that you visit extensis.com and go to the download page, your personal data, stored via the cookie, will be automatically filled into the download information screen. At that point, you have the opportunity to change or update any data we have on record.

If you wish to be removed from our mailing list, please click on the unsubscribe URL that appears at the end of each e-mail, or visit www.extensis.com/unsubscribe , where you can automatically unsubscribe yourself from future communications. Please allow two to three weeks for the actual cancellation to take effect. We will never share your email address with marketers or other external parties.

How does Extensis Corporation protect my personal financial information?

Keeping your personal financial information private is vitally important to us. As with all personal information, Extensis Corporation will never rent or sell your personal financial information.

Any time we ask for a credit card number in order to complete a purchase transaction or for verification purposes, we transmit that credit card number in an encrypted format. We use industry-standard, SSL (secure socket layer) encryption.

Your personal information that has been offered to Extensis Corporation has been done through an encrypted format, to prevent loss, misuse or alteration of the data.

Unfortunately, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. As a result, while we strive to protect your personal information, Extensis Corporation cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us or from our online products or services, and you do so at your own risk. Once we receive your transmission, we make our best effort to ensure its security on our systems.

In short, if you post personal information online that is accessible to the public, you may receive unsolicited messages from other parties in return.

Ultimately, you are solely responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your passwords and/or any account information. Please be careful and responsible whenever you’re online.
