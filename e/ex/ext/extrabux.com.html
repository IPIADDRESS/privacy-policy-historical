    Privacy Policy

    This is the website of Extrabux.com (Extrabux or we).

    We can be reached via email at info@extrabux.com.

    This Privacy Policy is effective on June 5, 2006.


    With respect to information we collect: Extrabux does not sell or rent any information that you share with us or one of our affiliated merchants.

    We only collect personally identifiable information volunteered by the user, such as email address, first and last name, and postal address. The information we collect is used by us to send payments to users and to send a newsletter by email. If you do not want to receive the Extrabux newsletter, please let us know by unchecking the box next to "I'd like to receive the Extrabux newsletter", located under the Change Email & Password subtab of My Account.

    For each visitor to our website, our web server automatically recognizes certain non-personally identifiable information, including but not limited to, user IP addresses, access times, and other information, which does not specifically identify an individual. We may use and disclose this non-personally identifiable information for any number of reasons, including but not limited to, working with charities and other partners and improving our website.

    Information from any affiliated merchant, including, but not limited to, information regarding any purchase made through Extrabux such as order numbers and amounts, may be available to Extrabux. Extrabux does NOT receive your credit card number or other personal information that you submit at our affiliated merchants' websites.

    With respect to cookies: Cookies are used by many affiliated merchants to identify you as an Extrabux member. You may stop or restrict the placement of cookies on your computer by adjusting your web browser preferences, in which case you may still use Extrabux, but it may interfere with your ability to receive cash back rewards. Thus, we suggest you enable cookies when using Extrabux.

    With respect to ad servers: We do not partner with or have special relationships with any ad server companies.

    With respect to security: We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site.
