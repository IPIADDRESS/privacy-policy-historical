Privacy Policy

ExpoThemes.com is serious in regard the privacy of Internet users, because our business success depends upon our ability to maintain the trust of our users.

If you have any concern about our products or website's privacy policies, please click here to contact us. We read every message submitted and try to reply to every one promptly. We may also file your comments to improve the program and site, or review and discard the information.

When does ExpoThemes collect personally- identifiable information?

ExpoThemes will collect your information if the information is licensed to us, for example when user registers on ExpoThemes we ask for real name and email address, when user submits content we notes email address to contact him anytime. We won't collect any personally- identifiable information about you, such as address, phone number or email address from this web site without your knowledge, and will take measures to protect your privacy.

How does ExpoThemes use the information?

Once you are registered on our site or subscribed via email, we could send information or notifications to you via email weekly or other post time. And you could easily remove your email address from our mail list.

Once you submit product, we could offer the products information and other available information to the users. Because of spamming risk, we do not disclose your email address on any of the web page or to any other user. However we simply MUST have that email address anyway. It is because we will be forwarding messages, complaints, etc. from users and audience to that address. And we may need to contact you about any issue.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

If you have any question or concern about this matter, please do not hesitate to contact us.
