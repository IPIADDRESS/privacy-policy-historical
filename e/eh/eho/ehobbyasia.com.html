You are always free to decline our cookies if your browser permits, although in that case you may not be able to use certain features on the Sites. Most likely, we employ your “Cookies” to keep track on your shopping cart for our recognition.
Cookies, in conjunction with our Web server's log files, allow us to calculate the aggregate number of people visiting our Web site and which parts of the site are most popular.
