A cookie is a small piece of data that is sent to your browser from a Web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. EUdict uses analytic software to collect data about web browsers and computers but this information contains no personal information about any visitor to our site; that is, no names, addresses, telephone numbers, e-mail addresses, and the like.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. How to control cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish – for details, see cookiesandyou.com. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. If you do this, however, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site and some services and functionalities may not work.
