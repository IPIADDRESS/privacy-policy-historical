Provision of the website and creation of log files

Every time our website is accessed, our system automatically collects data and information from your computer. The following data is collected:

(1) Information about the browser type and version used

(3) Your IP address
(4) Date and time of access

In addition, the data is used to optimize the website and generally ensure the security of our information technology systems.
  • Duration of storage
    We delete this technical data as soon as it is no longer needed, but never later the 3 months after your visit.
  • Option to object and erase your data
    Your rights to objection and erasure to this processing are described further down in this privacy notice.

    Our Website contains various functionalities which will collect, process and store your personal data.
