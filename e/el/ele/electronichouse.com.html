Privacy: Our Commitment to You

EH Publishing is committed to protecting your privacy to ensure a safe and enjoyable online experience.

General
EH Publishing may ask for personal information in order to provide services accessible from our site, including subscriptions, downloads, purchases, and/or contests. EH Publishing uses this information to help facilitate quick delivery of goods and services. EH Publishing tracks domains and anonymous demographic data for the purpose of analyzing trends and statistics.

Submission of personal information is by choice. If you do not wish to provide personal information online you may have access to most of the Web site, with the exception of some online requests. At any point, if you wish to be excluded from receiving any correspondence from EH Publishing or from any of our partners, you may do so by opting out during the process of providing personal information or after the receipt of e-mail from EH Publishing. EH Publishing will not disclose your personally identifiable information to any third party without first providing you with an opportunity to opt out, except to comply with law or to administer the site.

EH Publishing may be required by law enforcement or judicial authorities to provide Personally Identifiable Information to appropriate governmental authorities. If requested by law enforcement or judicial authorities, EH Publishing will provide this information upon receipt of the appropriate documentation. EH Publishing may also provide information to law enforcement to protect its property and the health and safety of individuals as permitted by statute.

Links to External Material
The appearance of hyperlinks that connect to third-party Web sites does not constitute an endorsement by EH Publishing of these Web sites, or information, products, or services contained therein. EH Publishing shall have no responsibilities or liabilities whatsoever for the privacy practices of any third party on its site.

Cookies
Cookie technology can be used to provide tailored information from a Web site. A cookie is an element of data a Web site sends to your browser that can be stored by your browser depending on your preferences. EH Publishing uses cookies to provide you better service when you return to our site.

Infrastructure
EH Publishing operates secure data networks protected by industry-standard password protection and verified systems. Its security and privacy policies are periodically reviewed and enhanced as necessary. Only authorized individuals have access to the information provided by EH Publishing users. Any other sensitive information, including credit card numbers and addresses, is also encrypted prior to transmission.

EH Publishing takes every step to ensure that your information is treated securely and in accordance with its Privacy Policy. Please be advised, however, that no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed secure. As a result, EH Publishing cannot guarantee the security of any information in transit to it.

Information Continuity
EH Publishing believes strongly in providing you with the ability to access and edit the personal information that you have provided. To update the information you've submitted to EH Publishing, or to deactivate your account, please contact us or call 508-663-1500.
