    Privacy Policy

    This privacy statement describes how E-LOAN, Inc. (“E-LOAN”), a wholly owned subsidiary of Banco Popular North America (“BPNA”), collects and uses the personal information you provide on E-LOAN’s Web site: www.eloan.com. It also describes the choices available to you regarding E-LOAN’s use of your personal information and how you can access and update this information. Products and services are intended for U.S. Residents over 18 years of age.

    E-LOAN has been awarded TRUSTe’s Privacy Seal signifying that this Privacy Policy and practices have been reviewed by TRUSTe for compliance with TRUSTe’s program requirements including transparency, accountability and choice regarding the collection and use of your personal information. The TRUSTe program does not cover information that may be collected through downloadable software. The TRUSTe program covers only information that is collected through this Web site, http://www.eloan.com. TRUSTe’s mission, as an independent third party, is to accelerate online trust among consumers and organizations globally through its leading privacy trustmark and innovative trust solutions. If you have questions or complaints regarding E-LOAN’s Privacy Policy or practices, please contact us at 1-866-576-7283. If you are not satisfied with E-LOAN’s response you can contact TRUSTe here.

    Please be aware that other Web sites that you may access through the E-LOAN Web site may collect personally identifiable information about you. E-LOAN’s Privacy Policy does not cover those third party Web sites, and E-LOAN encourages you to read those third party privacy policies prior to providing them with your personal information. To assist you with reading through E-LOAN’s Privacy Policy, E-LOAN has provided answers to questions you may find most important, and have created the following sections:

    HOW DOES E-LOAN COLLECT AND USE MY INFORMATION?

    E-LOAN may collect and use your personal information to respond to your requests for financial products and services offered on E-LOAN’s Web site or to provide you with information about products or services that may be of value to you. Certain personal information E-LOAN collects about you may be publicly available via government records, telephone books, and other widely available media (i.e. the Internet). However, we treat all information you provide to E-LOAN as non-public.

    1. Information you provide

      E-LOAN collects the personal information you provide when you:

      • Contact E-LOAN with a question or suggestion

      Savings Account or Certificate of Deposit

      E-LOAN collects certain information from you when you open a savings account or certificate of deposit vis-à-vis the E-LOAN Web site. This includes personal information such as name, email address, address, social security number, and bank account and routing numbers. This allows you to transfer money between accounts and lets E-LOAN facilitate the transactions.

      Contacting E-LOAN

      When you contact E-LOAN with a question or suggestion we collect your name, email address, and telephone number. E-LOAN only uses personal information that it collects to respond to and/or resolve your question or concern.

    2. Information collected from other sources

      E-LOAN may also collect information about you from the following sources:

      • Consumer reporting agency: E-LOAN uses this information to process your account.

    3. Technical Information

      We use technology to:

      • Gather technical information from your Web browser (IP address, operating systems);
      • Set persistent cookies on your computer; which are not linked to personal information and
      • Collect anonymous, non-personally identifiable information about your navigation throughout our website by using “cookies”, “action tags”, invisible “gifs” placed on the E-LOAN Web site by E-LOAN or its business partners.

      E-LOAN uses this information for routine Web site maintenance; to establish that you are a unique user and/or for purposes of assessing the effectiveness of the E-LOAN Web site and the products and services offered.

    4. Third Party Advertisements

      E-LOAN does NOT use third party advertising companies to provide advertisements on the E-LOAN website.

    DOES E-LOAN SHARE MY PERSONAL INFORMATION WITH THIRD PARTIES?

    E-LOAN may share your personal information:

    • With affiliate companies (companies related by common ownership or control). Examples: E-LOAN may share information about your transactions, experiences or your creditworthiness with its affiliates, BPNA, Popular Equipment Finance, Inc., and Popular Insurance Agency USA, Inc. Access BPNA’s Privacy Policy here and BPNA’s Privacy Practices here.

    • With companies involved in the loan or savings account process.

      Examples: Credit reporting agencies are a necessary part of various banking transactions. Therefore, E-LOAN must provide some of your information to these companies in order to process your account.

    • With companies that perform services on our behalf.

      Examples: E-LOAN sometimes uses service providers to assist it with specific processes or functions such as distributing emails or collecting survey information from E-LOAN customers. E-LOAN requires the service providers it uses to sign business agreements that restrict their use of your information to fulfilling the contracted service.

    • As required by law.

      Examples: E-LOAN may need to share your information with law enforcement or government agencies in response to a subpoena, state/federal audit or other similar circumstances. If E-LOAN is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, you will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on the E-LOAN Web site of any change in ownership or uses of your personal information, as well as any choices you may have regarding your personal information. The E-LOAN Web site may link you to other companies’ Web sites that offer services that may be of interest to you. These companies do not obtain any specific information about you or your transaction on the E-LOAN Web site. You may also access the E-LOAN Web site from another company’s Web site. E-LOAN may collect Web traffic statistics from you and other consumers’ Web browsers, and we may share this aggregated information with outside companies to assist them with assessing the effectiveness of their Web site content. No personal information is shared in this aggregated information.

    WHAT INFORMATION IS SHARED ON SAVINGS AND CD PRODUCTS?

    When you open a savings account or certificate of deposit through E-LOAN, an account is created for you with our parent company, Banco Popular North America (BPNA), a FDIC-member institution and a New York state chartered bank. These deposit accounts are FDIC insured through BPNA. BPNA will use your information solely to provide services on these accounts.

    CAN I OPT-OUT OF COMMUNICATIONS FROM E-LOAN?

    E-LOAN always provides you with the opportunity to opt-out of communications that offer our products and services. You can opt-out of marketing communications at anytime by clicking on the following link: https://savings.eloan.com/optout and completing the applicable section(s). However, please be aware that E-LOAN also sends necessary service and transaction emails which are required.

    CAN I ACCESS MY INFORMATION?

    You can access your account information at http://www.eloan.com anytime, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Simply log in using the Username and Password you entered when you first opened your Savings or CD account. To further ensure your security; please note that three unsuccessful login attempts will lock your account. To unlock it, you will need to call an associate for assistance at 1-866-576-7283 Monday to Friday between the hours of 8:00 am and 5:00 pm Pacific Time. If you wish to cancel your account or request that E-LOAN no longer uses your information to provide you services contact E-LOAN by sending an email request using this form.

    HOW DOES E-LOAN SAFEGUARD MY PERSONAL INFORMATION?

    E-LOAN follows generally accepted industry standards to protect the personally identifiable information submitted to it, both during transmission and once such information is received. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, however. Therefore, while E-LOAN strives to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, E-LOAN cannot guarantee its absolute security. E-LOAN uses Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology supporting up to 128-bit encryption to prevent unauthorized parties from viewing any personal information transmitted to or from E-LOAN during your session. E-LOAN has also obtained a digital certificate from VeriSign for www.eloan.com and its subdomains.

    HOW LONG WILL MY INFORMATION BE STORED?

    Federal and state agencies require that E-LOAN provide aggregated demographic and loan funding information about its loan applicants. E-LOAN maintains electronic and hardcopy loan application information for funded/declined/withdrawn loans for a period of at least 7 years in accordance with federal and state laws.

    WILL THIS PRIVACY POLICY CHANGE?

    To accommodate changes on the E-LOAN Web site, changing technology, and legal developments, E-LOAN may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. E-LOAN will notify you in writing of significant changes to our information privacy practices, and will prominently post the current version of the Privacy Policy at the E-LOAN Web site. E-LOAN may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to E-LOAN’s information practices. If E-LOAN makes any material changes it will notify you by email (sent to the e-mail address specified in your account) or by means of a notice on this Web site prior to the change becoming effective. E-LOAN encourages you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.
