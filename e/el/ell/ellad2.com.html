Privacy policy:

Our website address is: https://ellad2.com.

Ellad2.com respects your privacy! 
In general you can visit Ellad2.com without telling who you are. If you want, you can register on Ellad2.com and create an account, but it is not an obligation.
To create an account we need a name and email address. We don’t store payment information

We do not want to force our customers first to provide all kind information, before they can make a purchase. You do not need this in a “normal store” either. Our intention is to store as little personal information as possible. There are times, however, that we need information from you, such as your e-mail address.

We use your e-mail address to correspond with you.
For example: To confirm your order, to communicate the order status and to be able to send the purchase to you. If you do not wish this information to be used as a basis for further contact, we will respect this.

Information collected by Ellad2.com is only used internally for three reasons:

  1. We collect information to determine how we can improve our website. We use Google Analytics for this. This piece of online tools shows us which parts of the website are the most popular, from which country our visitors are coming and in which language they use the website.
  2. We also use the information we collect to tailor the website to your personal interests. For example: An overview of previous purchases, wish list, order status and downloads.
  3.  Finally, we also keep track of how you found our website. Did you see an advertisement, a message on Facebook or did you use a search engine such as Google or Bing. We analyze this data for statistics and to see what works or does not work. The data is then deleted. 

Cookies

There are various techniques, including so-called “cookies” that can be used to provide you with customized information. In order for our site to work, we send small pieces of information to your web browser. These cookies are not dangerous or scary but just useful. 

Some parts on  our website (for example the Shopping Cart) use cookies to ensure that your shopping cart is your personal choice and not the previous visitor. Cookies are also useful because we can better serve you when you return to our site. You can set your browser to alert you when you receive a cookie so that you can decide to accept it or not.. 

What happens if you registered user?

If you have an account and you log in to this site, we will set a temporary cookie to determine if your browser accepts cookies. This cookie contains no personal data and is discarded when you close your browser.

When you log in, we will also set up several cookies to save your login information and your screen display choices. Login cookies last for two days, and screen options cookies last for a year. If you select “Remember Me”, your login will persist for two weeks. If you log out of your account, the login cookies will be removed.

We are not using third party cookies. Check our website at: http://www.cookie-checker.com/ 

Safety

The website Ellad2.com is hosted on a secure server. All information is stored on secure servers and behind a firewall that is designed to prevent unauthorized access. Communication from your computer to our server takes place over encrypted ssl connections, recognizable, in the address bar, on the green padlock and starting with “https: //”  Our email server is evaluated by Vircom as: “Very strong security” 

We do not sell, rent, send or share data relating to our customers. 

Changes to this document
Changes in this document can be made without warning. Ellad2.com can or cannot notify you of changes to this policy. Any changes are always placed here.
