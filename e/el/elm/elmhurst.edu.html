Privacy Policy

Thanks for visiting our website! Elmhurst College is committed to protecting your privacy. Here’s an overview of the information we collect while you’re visiting, and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We collect information that can be used to identify you as an individual (“Personally Identifiable Information”) only when you provide this information directly to us. You don’t have to provide us with any personally identifiable information in order to use our website; however, if you submit a form on our site, we may store the information we collect through that form.
  • Navigational Information: We collect information about the device you use to access our web pages and about how you use our site, such as your IP address, geographical location, length of visit and pages viewed. We use this information to better understand how our website is being used and to make improvements to the user experience.
  • Cookies: As you browse our website, cookies may be placed on your computer so that we can learn more about how you arrived at our website and whether you’ve visited our website previously. We also might use cookies to pre-fill forms with your contact information, and to target advertising to you when you’re on other websites.

How We Use Your Information

  • Confidentiality: All personal information shared with us remains confidential. We do not share, sell or rent your contact information to any third party.
  • Communications: We may use any contact information you provide to us to communicate with you about our programs, events and other news at Elmhurst College.
  • Advertising: Our advertising partners may show you Elmhurst College ads on other sites based on your previous interaction with us. The techniques our partners employ do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, postal address or telephone number.

Contact Us
For more information about our privacy policy, please contact us.
