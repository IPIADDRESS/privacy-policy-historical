Customer Service

If you have any questions about your order, please email shop@vfiles.com or call 212.804.6400 x 720 Monday-Saturday 11AM-6PM EST.

For all other inquiries, please contact us here.

Returns & Exchanges

Merchandise in saleable condition with all original tags attached may be returned or exchanged within 14 days of receiving your shipment. Merchandise must be in its original packaging with all tags attached and accompanied by both the original sales receipt and a return authorization number.

To receive a return authorization number:

Email shop@vfiles.com and provide your name, order number, name of item(s) you wish to return, and reason for return.

VFILES is not responsible for return shipping costs. We reserve the right to deny a refund if the returned merchandise does not meet our return policy requirements.

All sales final on markdowns, international orders, phone and special orders, pre-orders, socks, swimwear, jewelry, eyewear, fragrances, CDs, books, stationery, magazines, toys, and specific brand and specialty items.

We do not offer price adjustments.

Shipping costs are non-refundable, and international customers are responsible for any duties/taxes that may apply.

In-store return policy

Unworn merchandise can be exchanged for merchandise or store credit within 14 days with a receipt present. Socks, swimwear, jewelry, eyewear, fragrances, CDs, books, stationery, magazines, toys, and discounted merchandise are final sale and can not be returned or exchanged.

Returns Policy Requirements

We reserve the right to refuse a return if the merchandise in question does not meet our standards. All merchandise must be in saleable condition when returned to VFILES.

Where provided, all original packaging to accompany the item—including belts, shoe boxes, eyewear cases, authenticity cards, and dust bags—must be included and returned in its original condition.

Shoes that are returned with marked soles, without a box, or in a damaged box may not be accepted, and may be sent back to the customer.

Customers are responsible for paying for shipping, and purchasing tracking/delivery confirmation. In the event that a customer foregoes secured tracking, VFILES is not responsible for the package if it is lost or otherwise not received.

PLEASE NOTE: We do not offer returns or exchanges for phone orders, special orders, pre-orders, or international orders.

Unauthorized Returns

Any merchandise returned to VFILES without a return authorization number will incur a non-negotiable re-stocking fee at 30% of the cost of the merchandise ordered, excluding tax and shipping.

To request a return authorization number, e-mail shop@vfiles.com and provide your name, order number, name of item(s) you wish to return, and reason for return.

Exchanges

All exchanges are final, and cannot be returned or exchanged further.

Shipping Policy

All orders are processed on business days within 24 hours.

Orders placed after 3PM EST, on weekends, or on holidays are processed the following business day.

Please note this may take longer during sale periods or exclusive releases when it may take up to 48 hours for orders to be shipped.

Shipping rates are determined by weight and destination at point of purchase. The origin of all VFILES orders are shipped from New York via FedEx or UPS.

How do I track my package online?

Upon shipment, you will receive a confirmation e-mail with your tracking number.

You can also track your order by logging into your account and seeing your order history.

Once your order has shipped from our store, please directly contact your courier with any inquiries about the status of your package. FedEx can be reached by calling 1-800-463-3339 or online at fedex.com. UPS can be reached by calling 1-800-742-5877 or online at ups.com.

International Orders

All international orders require a signature for delivery.

Some international orders over $1000 are subject to being held at the nearest FedEx or UPS location for pick-up where signature and valid ID will be required.

Customer is responsible for all customs fees unless they are explicitly charged during checkout.

Service Delays

VFILES is not responsible for unexpected shipping delays caused by circumstances such as severe weather or natural disasters. Similarly, we are not responsible for unexpected shipping delays caused by circumstances related to the courier itself, such as clearance delays. In cases such as these, please contact your courier with any inquiries about the status of your package.

If you have any questions about your order, please email shop@vfiles.com or call 212.804.6400 x 720 Monday-Saturday 11AM-6PM EST.

Failed Deliveries & Returned Packages

In the case that the courier is unable to deliver a package due to an incorrect address entered by the customer, the customer’s refusal to pay duties, multiple failed attempts to reach the customer at point of delivery or otherwise, VFILES is not responsible for payment of return shipping costs levied by the courier. Similarly, VFILES is not responsible for the payment of re-shipping merchandise to the customer. In cases such as these, the customer is responsible for all shipping costs associated with returned packages due to failed delivery.
