  • The Information That We Collect

    VYOMA collects personally identifiable information about Registered Users during the registration process, as required to offer and provide the Service, as listed in the Terms and Conditions, and as otherwise identified below.     VYOMA utilizes a standard technology called "cookies" (see explanation below in the next paragraph) and server logs to collect information about how our VYOMA is used that is not associated with your personal account ("Non-Personal Information"). Non-Personal Information gathered through cookies and Web server logs may include but is not limited to such items as statistics regarding popularity of particular pages within VYOMA, the date and time of visits, the pages viewed, time spent at our Web site, and the Web sites visited just before and just after our Web site, IP address, type and version of operating system, and browser type. We, our advertisers and ad serving companies may also use small pieces of code called "web beacons" or "clear gifs" to determine which advertisements and promotions users have seen and how users responded to them.

    A cookie is a very small text document, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier. When you visit a Web site, that site's computer asks your computer for permission to store this file in a part of your hard drive specifically designated for cookies. Each Web site can send its own cookie to your browser if your browser's preferences allow it, but (to protect your privacy) your browser only permits a Web site to access the cookies it has already sent to you, not the cookies sent to you by other sites. For example, a cookie may contain information about a user's preferences, so that the Web site automatically complies with those preferences whenever the user visits that Web site. When using the Service, we may create "cookies" on your computer for purposes of facilitating and maintaining your current user session, and may collect Personal Information using those cookies in order to enable VYOMA to identify you in connection with the Service and to track use of the Service for purposes consistent with this policy.

We do not collect or maintain Personal Information from people we actually know are under 13, and no part of our Site or Service is designed to attract people under 13. If we later learn that a user is under 13, we will take steps to remove that user's Personal Information from our databases and to prevent the user from utilizing VYOMA and the Service.
