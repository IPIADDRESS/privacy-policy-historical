Privacy and Cookie Use Policy

 

Effective Date: December 2015

 

 1) General

Because your privacy is important to the Company, the Company will operate by the principles listed here, which are incorporated by reference into the Company’s Website’s Terms of Use. This policy was created in order to inform you of the type of information the Company will collect from you and how the Company will use it.

 

 2) What Information is collected? 

The Company may collect information, both personal – identifying information such as your name, e-mail, mailing address, phone number etc. and non-personal – non-identifying information such as your gender, birthdate, or Country in which you live, (collectively the “Information”) from you when you browse on the Company’s Website.

There is also Information that may be automatically collected including (but not exclusive to) your IP address, profile information, aggregate user data, preferences, browser type, computer specifications, and information and specifications of your Vuze Camera.

This Information may be collected in some of the following ways including, but not limited to: by connecting your Vuze products to your computer; through account Registration; your access to customer support; order processing; and any other times in which identifying factors can assist the Company in product development , marketing and in personalizing your Website experience. You may, however, opt to visit the Website anonymously.

 

3) What is your Information used for?

Any of the Information that may be collected from you, may be used to:

  • Personalize your experience (your information helps the Company better respond to your individual needs and present you with options and opportunities relevant specifically to you)
  • Improve the Website (the Company continually strives to improve its Website offerings based on the information and feedback that have been received from you)
  • Improve customer service (your information helps the Company to respond more efficiently to your customer service requests and support needs)
  • Practical considerations such as, but not limited to, sending order and shipping confirmation emails
  • Administer contest, promotion, survey or other site features
  • Send periodic emails

If you choose to register, the email address you provide as part of the registration process may be used to send you information and updates about your account and/or Products.  In addition you may receive occasional company news, updates or service information, etc. to that email.

Note: If at any time you would like to unsubscribe from receiving future emails, the Company includes detailed unsubscribe instructions at the bottom of each email.

 

4) How is your Information protected?

The Company implements a variety of security measures throughout each step of your use of the Website to maintain the safety of your personal Information when you enter, submit, or access your personal Information.  It is important to note though that, despite the Company’s greatest efforts, no security measures are completely fool-proof.

 

5) Does the Company use cookies? 

Yes. (Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computer’s hard drive through your Web browser (if you allow) that enable the site’s or service provider’s systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information.)

The Company uses cookies to understand and save your log in Information for future visits. The Company may contract with a third-party service provider to assist in better understanding the Website’s visitors. These service providers are not permitted to use the Information collected on the Website except to help the Company conduct and improve business and interaction with you.

The Website may be monitored by Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. (“Google”). Google Analytics uses cookies to help the Company analyze how visitors browse a site. No Information is transferred to Google (Google gathers all information directly through its cookies). The Information Google gathers about your use of the  website is retained by Google in accordance with the Google Analytics Terms of Service available here.

 

6) Is any Information disclosed to outside parties?

The Company does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable Information. This does not include trusted third parties who assist the Company in operating the Website, conducting the Company’s business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. The Company may also release your Information when the Company believes the release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce Company site policies, or protect other Company rights, property, or safety. However, non-personally identifiable visitor Information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other uses.

 

7) Third Party Website Links

The Company Website may contain links to or features from other websites (for example Google, Facebook, Instagram).  This Privacy Policy covers on the policies of this Company and not of any other Websites.  The Company is not responsible for the privacy policies of third parties.  If you choose to link to a third party website you should be sure to read the privacy policy of that specific website.

 

8) Children’s Privacy

The Company’s Products and Website Services are targeted for a general audience and not for children under the age of 13.  To the best of the Company’s ability, it will not collect Information from children under the age of 13.  If the Company becomes aware of such Information it will do its best to remove it as soon as possible.

 

9) EU Cookie Directive Compliance

The Company is in compliance with the requirements of the EU Cookie Directive (Directive 2009/136/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council 25 November 2009). The Website uses cookies exclusively for password retention. Furthermore, the Website is aimed at an audience who is technically advanced enough to have a reasonable understanding of the use of cookies and personal Information (including the ability to change browser settings to reject cookies), and is deemed to have consented to such use by directing their web-browsers to our site.
