Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 10/13/2006

At this web site, operated by Vantage Media Corporation, we are committed to protecting your privacy online. This policy describes how we collect and handle your information.

Website Data
When you browse through our site, we collect information on the efficiency and working of our Web site. This information includes your IP address, the number of times a web page is accessed, the browser used, and paths taken through the website, and search terms entered to find our site. We keep this information for an indefinite amount of time to improve the operation of our site and to provide better services to our users.

Contact Information
We also collect personal contact information, (such as name, telephone number, mailing address, and email address), if you enter it when requesting information from a school.  This contact information is stored on our servers and shared with the schools you identify (sometimes through a vendor that is under contract to the school), so that they may contact you with more information. Personally Identifiable Contact Information is not sold to third parties except as explicitly required to fulfill your information request, or if you have requested to receive additional offers from us or our advertising partners.

Cookies
Our website uses cookies (small files on your computer), to provide usage statistics about our web site, and to understand where visitors learn about our web site. We also may use cookies to simplify the re-entering of your data.

Different schools have different methods of following up with student information requests. Some send brochures in the mail, others reply via email or telephone. By using our service to request information from schools, you are giving permission to schools (or their partners and vendors) to contact you using the methods of their choice, to fulfill your information request, notwithstanding your possible listing in the National Do-Not-Call Registry.

Vantage Media Corporation reserves the right to contact you in the future to help improve our operations or to introduce new services, such as surveys, to ensure that schools have followed up with your information requests in a timely manner.
