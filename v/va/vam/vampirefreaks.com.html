Vampirefreaks Privacy Policy

Vampirefreaks.com is a community website where users create their own profiles. The information we collect that is required for each profiles is email address and age. We collect email addresses to prevent users from creating multiple profiles. We collect age information in order to keep in compliance with internet laws and practices. Users under the age of 13 are not allowed to create profiles on this site. IP addresses are also logged in order to prevent multiple account creation. Users can upload personal information to their profiles, which becomes published online. We do not sell any user info or give away any of the information we collect unless we are asked by law enforcement officials to release information. While we do have administrators who monitor the site in order to keep things in order, we do not take responsibility for anything that users publish on the site. This service is provided as-is, and we do not make any claims as to the quality of service.
