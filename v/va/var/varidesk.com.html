USE OF COOKIES

VARIDESK.com uses cookies. Cookies allow you the ability to use features like the shopping cart, and also helps to personalize your experience by remembering preferences or parts of the website you’ve visited. We also use cookies to help us measure the effectiveness of our advertising and website, as well as helping to provide additional insight about user behavior so we can improve our advertising and communication.

Cookies are simple to disable. In Safari, go to “Preferences”, and then go to the Privacy pane and choose “block cookies.” In Internet Explorer, go to “tools”, then “internet options.” On the Privacy tab, under settings, move the slider to the top to block all cookies. In Chrome, touch the Chrome menu and go to “Settings”. In the “Advanced” section, click “Site settings” or “Content Settings”. Deselect “Cookies”. In Firefox, click the menu button and choose “Preferences”. Select the Privacy panel, and under “History”, change the drop down to “Firefox will: Use custom settings for history”. Under “Accept cookies from site”, select Never.

Please note that if you disable cookies in your browser, you may not be able to use VARIDESK.com to it’s fullest, and it may affect your ability to use other popular websites as well.

The cookies used on our websites have been categorized based on the guidelines found in the ICC UK Cookie guide. We use the following categories on our website:

Category 1 – Strictly Necessary Cookies:
These cookies are essential to enable you to browse the website and use these features. Without these cookies, your shopping cart would not work.

Category 2 – Performance Cookies:
These cookies collect information about how you use our websites. This data is used to optimize our websites to enhance the user experience. These cookies do not collect any personally identifiable information like name, address, phone number or financial information. This information is collected in aggregate.

Category 3 – Functionality Cookies:
These cookies remember choices you make while browsing, and give us the ability to serve relevant messages to our site visitors whether they’re on our website or on websites that we advertise on. These cookies do not collect any personally identifiable information like name, address, phone number or financial information. This information is collected in aggregate.

We also use 3rd party vendor re-marketing tracking cookies, including the Google Adwords tracking cookie. This means we will continue to show ads to you across the internet, including on the Google Content Network (GCN). We respect your privacy and are not collecting any identifiable information through the use of Google’s or any other 3rd party remarketing systems.

The third-party vendors, including Google, whose services we use – will place cookies on web browsers in order to serve ads based on past visits to our website. Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user’s prior visits to the VARIDESK.com website. This allows us to market our products and services to those who have shown interest in our service.
