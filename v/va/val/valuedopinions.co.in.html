Cookie Notice

This site uses cookies to provide a better website experience and to improve the services available to you. Continue to use the website as normal if you're happy with this.

Cookies are very small text files that are stored on your computer.

We use cookies to make our site and service works better for you.

With their help we are able to:

  • Allow you to participate in surveys
  • Remember your log-in details
  • Allow you to share pages with social networks like Facebook
  • Improve the quality of our website based on your browsing behaviour
  • Allow you to vote in mini-polls
  • Correctly process your reward
  • Allow you to refer this service to someone you know
  • Send you more and better targeted surveys based on your online behaviour
  • Remember that you accepted this cookie information so you don’t have to see it again
  • Limit multiple accesses to surveys

For more details, please read our cookie policy.

If you decide not to allow Valued Opinions to store information on your computer, you can remove all cookies. But you’ll find that most parts of the website (including taking surveys) may not work or function correctly for you.
