    Cookie Policy


    From time to time, a small file may be placed on your computer to improve the effectiveness of this site and Valmet's services to you. This file is usually referred to as a "cookie" and many sites use this technology. Cookies are pieces of data stored on your computer's (or other internet enabled devices, such as a smartphone or tablet) hard drive or browser, and not on this site.

    The cookies used on the Valmet sites may be stored on your device for a longer time or until you manually delete them (stored or persistent cookies) or be deleted from your device after you leave Valmet‚Äôs web service (session cookies).

    Information gathered by cookies is used to improve our communication and is compiled in order to improve the general level of services from Valmet. We cannot identify you personally from this information.

    Valmet is committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. Valmet applies a high level of protection and processes your personal data in accordance with applicable personal data legislation and privacy principles.
