Privacy Policy

At JJH Enterprises Ltd t/a ValueLicensing.com we are transparent about what personal data we hold and how we use that data. We take your privacy with the utmost importance and will only use your personal information to administer your account to allow us to process orders and to ensure that you are satisfied with the service you have received.

What is ‘personal data’?

Personal data means data which relate to a living individual who can be identified:

  • From those data, or
  • From those data and other information which is likely to come into our possession

 

We collect the following personal data to ensure that our team are in contact with you throughout the order process to give you the best purchasing experience. Personal data may include the following:

  • First name
  • Last name
  • Job Title
  • Business name
  • Business email address
  • Business telephone number
  • IP address

We do not collect special categories of personal data which is explained in the GDPR.

 

Using your personal data – orders

Personal information is collected when you submit for a quote on our website, have submitted your information through our website via social media channels or if you contact us directly by phone or email. We believe in maintaining a high level of customer service and we collect this data to keep in touch throughout the duration of the ordering process.

We keep transaction data for seven years after a transaction has been made. If your visit to the website does not result in a transaction we will retain your personal data for a period of seven years.

 

Collecting data at events

We will also collect personal data at events from those who show an interest in our service. We collect this information via a tablet. Your personal data will then be imported into our CRM system. We will not contact you further unless we are legally permitted or obliged to do so. We require your consent to receive follow up telephone calls or emails to discuss purchasing and selling opportunities.  If you attend an event, you have the right to refuse to give us your consent to receive your personal data and you will not be contacted by us.

Cookies

Our website may place and access certain Cookies on your computer. We use these Cookies to improve your experience of using the Website and to improve our range of products. We have carefully chosen these Cookies and has taken steps to ensure that your privacy is protected and respected at all times.

  • All Cookies used by this Website are used in accordance with all applicable EU and UK law
  • Before the Website places Cookies on your computer, you will be presented with a message bar requesting your consent to set those Cookies. By giving your consent to the placing of Cookies, you are enabling ValueLicensing to provide a better experience and service to you. You may, if you wish, deny consent to the placing of Cookies; however certain features of the Website may not function fully or as intended.
  • Analytical/performance cookies allow us to recognise and count the number of visitors and to see how visitors move around our website when they are using it. This helps us to improve the way our website works, for example, by ensuring that users are finding what they are looking for easily.
  • You can choose to enable or disable Cookies in your internet browser. By default, most internet browsers accept Cookies but this can be changed. For further details, please consult the help menu in your internet browser.
  • You can choose to delete Cookies at any time; however you may lose any information that enables you to access the Website more quickly and efficiently including, but not limited to, personalisation settings.
  • It is recommended that you ensure that your internet browser is up-to-date and that you consult the help and guidance provided by the developer of your internet browser if you are unsure about adjusting your privacy settings.

 

Why we keep your personal data

We keep and use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Provision of products and services
  • Managing relationships with individuals
  • Marketing and advertising
  • Administering our business and our website

We will not share your personal details with anyone else unless we are legally permitted or obliged to do so. We will not use your personal data for anything else. You have the right to be informed about how we are using your personal data.

 

Sharing data with third parties

It is not our practice to share personal data with third parties.

 

Data use outside of the EEA

In the unlikely circumstance that your data is transferred outside of the EEA, we will respect your data and your rights. We don’t transfer information outside of the EU. If we do so in future, this will be within our legitimate interests or where we have a legal obligation to do so. We will then make sure that one of the safeguards required by UK data protection law is in place or is being observed.

 

Marketing emails

From time to time we will also contact you in the form of an email with details of sector expertise, offers and services that we provide. We understand that we require your consent to send marketing materials to you eg emails. If you are not a customer, we can only continue to send marketing materials with your written consent only. If you are a customer, you can opt out of receiving emails by selecting the unsubscribe options at the bottom of each email.

 

Third party websites

This Website may, from time to time, provide links to other websites. We have no control over such websites and are not responsible for the content of these websites. This privacy policy does not extend to your use of such websites. You are advised to read the privacy policy or statement of other websites prior to using them.
