Privacy

Virtual Piano (or its holding company Crystal Magic Studio Ltd) may collect personal information from you through this website. For example, if you register with us in order to receive news and announcements, your information will be stored either manually or electronically.

Any personal information you give to us will always be processed in accordance with the UK Data Protection Act 1998. We will only use the personal information you provide to deliver the services you have requested, or for our lawful, disclosed purposes. We will not make your personal details available outside our organisation without your consent, unless obliged to by law.

This website will automatically track certain information about you in the course of you using this website, such as your IP address and browser identification. This information is used only for preventing malicious attacks, for system administration and for providing statistics to evaluate use of this site.
