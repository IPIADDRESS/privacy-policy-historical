PRIVACY POLICY
VIRTUALSPORTS USER PRIVACY INFORMATION

VirtualSPORTS is committed to responsible management of your information and abides by all rules, both ethical and legal, with respect to the use of consumer information. We strictly adhere to all industry guidelines, and are continuously monitoring and enhancing how we manage the use and security of consumer information to uphold your rights.

INFORMATION COLLECTED
VirtualSPORTS collects personal information such as name, physical address, email addresses, phone number, age and for subscription and session tracking services, I.P. address logging. VirtualSPORTS may also set cookies storing information about the user such as place of access, browser type and version, and tracking movement and activity throughout the site. Other personal information collected by VirtualSPORTS' partners and service providers, as referred to from VirtualSPORTS, is subject to the Privacy Policies of the third party. VirtualSPORTS provides and uses the services of reputable third parties who also provide clear statements regarding responsible use of consumer information.

HANDLING OF INFORMATION
Personal information collected by VirtualSPORTS is used for the purposes as stated on its collection, such as entry into its various games and competitions, subscription to newsletters, entry to contests and giveaways, submission of content to chat and discussion forums, etc. VirtualSPORTS uses industry standard systems, software and encryption whenever possible and appropriate as means of precaution to keep the information disclosed secure. Whereever possible, VirtualSPORTS requests only the minimal required information. However, VirtualSPORTS cannot be held responsible for any breach of security. VirtualSPORTS may also link to a wide variety of other sites and contain advertisements of third parties. We are not responsible for their privacy policies or how they treat information about their users.

USE OF INFORMATION
VirtualSPORTS does NOT UNDER ANY CIRCUMSTANCE provide any information collected from its users to any third parties, unless otherwise advised and opted for by its users during the sign-up period, in circumstances where it is necessary to conform to legal requirements, protect and defend our rights or property, or to protect the interests of other users. The data collected from cookies and IP addresses is used to gather demographic information about the entire VirtualSPORTS audience, which is used to improve the VirtualSPORTS service, as well as track game progress in the various games and competitions. Cookies stored on user machines do not contain any personally identifiable information that may pose any security threat. We also share aggregate information -- not individual data -- with our advertisers to help them better understand our services and to monitor the reach of their advertising on the service. VirtualSPORTS may disclose or exchange with others general statistics or information about its user base. However, this information will not contain any individual or personal information.

ADVERTISING
To enhance your Web experience, this site uses anonymous identifiers ("cookies") to collect anonymous preference and interest visitor information. This information is provided to the Ad-Server and is used by the Ad-Server to create anonymous interest profiles. This information does not include and is never merged with personally identifiable information (such as name, address or email address) or any information that is considered sensitive or controversial or that would compromise your privacy. The Ad-Server(s') profiles may be accessed by the website and marketers to tailor site content, promotions and advertising, and to perform marketing research. Consumers can opt-out from these profiles at any time. For more information on the privacy policies of the Ad-Servers that serve advertisements on this site, including your option not to have this information used for this purpose, refer to: http://us.www.sabela.com/information/privacy.html and http://www.bmcmedia.com/privacy.

Users of VirtualSPORTS' opt-in and registration services may choose, as appropriate, to correct member details or to be removed from selected services. On request, all data will be permanently deleted off our databases. Information on how to change details or be removed from any of our services is provided with each newsletter, or where the service may be used on the web site.
