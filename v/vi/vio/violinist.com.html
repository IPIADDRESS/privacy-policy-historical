Violinist.com requires site users to register in order to access all features of the site. As part of the registration process, users will be asked to provide their name and e-mail address, as well as other identifying information.

Violinist.com does not provide that personal information to a third party, other than web surfers who visit that registrant's free Violinist.com web page. Nevertheless, individuals under age 18 should make their parents aware of this privacy policy and obtain their permission before submitting any information to this site. Individuals under age 13 are prohibited from creating or publishing web pages on Violinist.com.

In addition, Violinist.com will capture the IP address of the machine from which registrants were connecting to the Internet at the time he or she posted to our discussion board or created a classified ad.

(An IP address is a unique number assigned to each computer hooked up to the Internet. It is not your e-mail address. In fact, if you are connecting through a dial-up account, the IP address recorded will be that of a computer at your Internet Service Provider, and not your personal computer. Nevertheless, be aware that your ISP may track user logins, and thereby be able to identify you based on an IP number and time of day, if asked.)

Names and IP addresses are published on the discussion board to identify the individuals posting.

Several pages on the Violinist.com site will attempt to set "cookies" in a user's browser. A "cookie" is a line of text in the cookies.txt file in your browser's folder on your personal computer. When a cookie is set, a web site writes a line of text to that file which includes the web site's domain name and a unique user number. The cookie cannot access any other information on your computer, including your name, e-mail address and passwords.

Cookies are used on this site to determine if an individual requesting a page is a registered user. Only registered users, who have signed in, may create listing pages, post to the discussion board or place ads on the site.

Information collected by Violinist.com on this site becomes the property of Violinist.com, and may be transferred along with the other assets of Violinist.com to any subsequent owners of the site.

Violinist.com participates in the associates programs of Amazon.com and other online retailers. When a user follows a link from Violinist.com to one of these retailers' sites, he or she leaves the Violinist.com web site, and the terms of this privacy policy no longer apply.
