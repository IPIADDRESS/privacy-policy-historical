    • Privacy

    Viavi Solutions values your privacy.
    Viavi Solutions values your privacy.

    E-mail Programs:

    By subscribing to our e-mail programs you have opted in to receive newsletters or product and technology updates via e-mail. We will provide subscription materials for only those programs you have selected to opt in to. If you want to discontinue your participation in any of those programs, you can opt out by clicking on the link found in all e-mail information that we send you or by sending us e-mail at the above address.

    Sharing of Information:

    We will not sell or provide the e-mail and contact information you provided to third party solicitors.

    Our Use of Cookies:

    We use cookies to provide registered users the ability to view webcasts without re-registering, to allow site registrants to make changes to their submitted information and verify username and passwords in our database.

    Information Collection:

    In addition to the information provided to us by subscribers to our e-mail programs, we also collect certain information from visitors to our web pages. The information we collect includes only the domain name of visitors and not complete e-mail addresses. We use this information to evaluate frequency of page visits and use other information such as survey information volunteered by visitors, and/or page visits and other activity tracked in conjunction with subscription login names, to help us further develop and improve the site.
