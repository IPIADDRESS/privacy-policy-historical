These help us tailor our website to our users’ interests and needs in order to provide more relevant product information.
 
If you supply us with your e-mail address by registering on our website or joining our mailing list or by sending us an email, you may receive periodic email updates from us with information on new products, services or upcoming events. Unsubscribing from the list will remove you from our mailing list. Should you wish to receive our mailings again in the future, you will need to join our mailing list once again on www.viatrading.com.

When emailing or calling us to remove you from our contact list, please be ready to provide us with your full name and address to make sure your name is removed from the list.  If you have any questions or require more information about how any of your information is used, please feel free to call or email us anytime.

Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers when registering online will only receive telephone contact from us with information regarding orders they have placed on-line or special events, new products and services or promotions Via Trading is sponsoring. If you do not wish to receive such phone calls, please let us know by emailing us at sales@viatrading.com.
When emailing, please provide us with your full name and address so that we may accurately identify you and remove you from our call list.

 
We do not partner with or have special relationships with any ad server companies. Browsing ViaTrading.com does not distribute your information to any third party ad server companies.

From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change at some time in the future we will post the policy changes to our Web site and notify you of these changes. We will always provide you with the ability to opt out of any new uses. If you are concerned about how your information is used, you can check our Privacy Policy for any changes, or email us at sales@viatrading.com with any questions.
