Secure Ordering
Victorinox AG and its affiliates use a safe and secure online ordering system. Our Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology is advanced encryption software. This secure server software encrypts all of your information including credit card number, address, first and last name, so that it cannot be read in transfer.

What information do we collect? How is this information used?
Victorinox AG and its affiliates respect and safeguard your online privacy.
Victorinox AG and its affiliates collect user information by member registration, sweepstakes and order processing. This information may include name, email address, postal address and phone number, and assist us in fulfilling your order. Although you must enter your credit card number and expiration date during the order process, Victorinox does not retain this information. In addition to order confirmation, we contact individuals who specifically request email updates or have won a sweepstakes. Individuals may opt-out of receiving future online communications at any time without affecting their registration status.

What are cookies? How do we use them?
Cookies are tools used to retrieve information during your online session, such as keeping track of the items placed in your shopping cart. For your added security, Victorinox cookies terminate at the end of your browser session.
Many web browsers are designed to accept cookies, but you may prefer to modify your browser to prevent acceptance. It is possible, however, that some areas of this site will not function properly if you do so.

Tracking Links Clicked
In order to create a better electronic community, we may keep track of patterns of use in the emails we send out. We note which links are clicked in our e-mail communications as specific user profile information to tell us what areas are of most interest to our opt-in email recipients. We may use this information to better personalize the content and promotions that visitors and mail recipients will see in our mailings.

Will Victorinox disclose information it collects to third parties?
Victorinox does not sell, trade, rent or release your personal information (name, email address, postal address, telephone, credit card, etc.) to others without your consent. This site contains links to other sites. Victorinox is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

Choice/Opt-Out
Victorinox provides the opportunity to opt-out of receiving online communications from us at any time without affecting your registration status.

The following options are available for removing information from our database and discontinue future online communications. You can email us at: webmaster@victorinox.com. Please allow 24 hours for removing information from the Victorinox database.

In Summary
We respect and work to protect your privacy. If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with Victorinox you may contact: webmaster@victorinox.com.
