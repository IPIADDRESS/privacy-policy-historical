PRIVACY STATEMENT

 

VICE is committed to protecting the privacy of the personal information you provide to us. Please take a moment to read the details of our privacy statement.

 

 

1.     Non-Personal Identification Information

 

Non-personal information is information concerning the manner in which you access the Website. This information includes the type of browser you use, the operating system you are using and the name of your Internet Service Provider. Non-personal information is automatically collected when you access our Website. You can configure your web browser to either reject the installation of the files that allow us to collect this information (commonly referred to as “cookies”) or warn you before the installation of such files occurs.

 

 

2.     Personal information

 

We will not collect personal information when you visit our Website without your consent. At times, we might ask you, for example, for your name, telephone number, e-mail address, mailing address and your date of birth. We may also ask additional marketing-related questions to help us better understand your needs and those of potential customers.

 

The personal information you provide us with will be used by our authorized personnel in order to better know and understand you, respond to your needs and expectations and to contact you for various promotional purposes. This information is also requested to verify your identity. Rest assured that we will not share, sell, rent, or otherwise give your personal information to any third party without your consent.

 

 

3.     Collection of Information from Children

 

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of thirteen. However, if children under the age of thirteen provide us with personal information, a parent or legal guardian should request that we delete this information from our files by sending us an e-mail. We will use all reasonable efforts to delete children's information from our files.

 

 

4.     DISCLOSURE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

 

4.1    We will not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.

 

4.2    We will only disclose your personal information to third parties:

 

(a)   Where you have specifically given us your consent to disclose your personal information for a designated purpose;

 

(b)   Who are acting on our behalf as our agents, suppliers or service providers, solely to enable us to more efficiently provide you with VICE services. Such entities act under strict contractual controls, requiring them to maintain the confidentiality of all such personal information and to use such information solely for purposes related to the provision of VICE services;

 

(c)   As may be set out in the Terms of Use applicable to VICE service, to facilitate the provision of such services, such as fulfilling legal requirements;

 

(d)   As required by law, including by an order of any court, institution or body with jurisdiction or authority to compel the production of information, or in order to protect or defend a legal interest; or

 

(e)   In connection with a VICE corporate re-organization, merger or amalgamation with another entity, or a sale of all or a substantial portion of the assets of VICE provided that the personal information disclosed continues to be used for the purposes permitted by this Privacy Statement by the entity acquiring the information.

 

 

5.     SECURITY OF INFORMATION

 

VICE uses industry standard security measures to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. While there is no such thing as "perfect security" on the Internet, we try our best to make sure that your transmissions to and from this website are kept secure.  Only authorized VICE employees and its agents (who have agreed to keep information secure) have access to information and only to help VICE administer the site.

 

You may request at any time that the information collected about your child be deleted. Parents may contact us at info@Viceland.com about reviewing or deleting the information collected. You may also use this email address to contact us about any questions you may have about this policy or our practices.
