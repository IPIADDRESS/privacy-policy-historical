PRIVACY POLICY

Vivobarefoot Limited (trading as ‘VIVOBAREFOOT’) are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. The purpose of this privacy policy is to describe the kinds of information that we may gather about you, and how we may use that information. We understand the importance of your privacy and take a number of steps in order to protect it.

Information we collect

Our policy assures you that any personal data We (VIVOBAREFOOT) collect from you or that is provided to us, will be processed by us only. Any information we hold about you is in accordance with the UK Data Protection Act (1998), the data controller of which is Vivobarefoot Limited.

By shopping with us, you are agreeing to our Privacy Policy, in particular, our use of your information. When you provide this information through the website you consent to have this information be used, stored and processed. We collect the following data that is relevant to your account:

  1. Your site navigation and purchasing habits with us.
  2. Personal identifiable information; name, email, shipping address, billing address, phone number when processing your order.

Non-personal information we collect

We automatically collect non-personally identifiable information from you when you visit our site. This information includes your Internet Protocol (‘IP’) address, which is the number assigned to your computer whenever you access the Internet. This information does not identify your name, email address, or further personal details. This IP address will be used to help diagnose problems with our server and site administration

We also collect non-personally identifiable information through the use of cookies. (See Cookie Policy page)

How we use your information

By providing personal information you consent for it to be used and stored by us (VIVOBAREFOOT). Please be assured that whenever we handle personal information, regardless of where this occurs, we (VIVOBAREFOOT) will use and protect that personal information in accordance with our Privacy Policy.  

  1. Email marketing; we may contact you with promotions of interest, launch of new products, potential employment opportunities.

If you currently receive our emails and do not wish to continue receiving them, please review your settings at the bottom of the email to unsubscribe.

  1. Third party marketing; we may make sections of our mailing list available to carefully selected companies whose products and services may be of interest. Your information will only be included if you have specifically given consent when creating an account/placing an order.

Our Policies

  1. We will never collect sensitive information about you without your explicit consent and do not disclose your personal information to other companies, persons or agencies unless you have opted to do so.
  2. The information we hold will be accurate and up to date. You can check information that we hold about you by emailing us.
  3. We will not transfer your information outside the EEA (European Economic Area).
  4. For your protection and ours, we (VIVOBAREFOOT) may release personally identifiable information when we believe it is appropriate to comply with applicable law regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

Vivobarefoot Limited may update its privacy policy in the future. We will notify you about material changes to this Privacy Policy by sending a notice to the email address you provided to us, or by placing a prominent notice on our Website.

If you have any questions or concerns about the privacy or security of your information, you may contact us at customerservices@vivobarefoot.com or hello@vivobarefoot.com
