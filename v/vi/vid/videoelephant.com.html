Privacy Policy

Effective 22nd April 2011

Our Privacy Policy is effective from the date above and can be changed at any time without prior notice to you.

 

Your Personal Information is safe with us.

What information do we collect from you?

  • Your Contact information – names, address, e-mail,
  • Your Payment information – Paypal, e-mails and/or bank details
  • Your Marketing Preferences through your Account panel – files purchases, total earnings, media type preferences

We will only use it to be able to proceed your purchases, make the due payments to you if you are selling through VideoElephant and administer your account.

 

By signing up to www.VideoElephant.com or subscribing to any of our services, you accept our Terms & Conditions. You agree that we can disclose that personal information to our office worldwide, our subsidiaries and affiliates, as well as to any payment processors, if that is a must for your payment transactions to be completed and to legal authorities,if required by the Law. VideoElephant cannot sell your personal information to any third parties.

By subscribing to our Monthly Newsletter, you grant us the permission to e-mail you the latest website products, promotions, news and updates once every month. You should have the option to UNSUBSCRIBE from any of our services and stop receiving that type of communication from our side. No other e-mails/communication shall be sent to you, once you have notified us that you wish to Unsubscribe.

VideoElephant does not use cookies to track your surfing behavior before visiting our website and after leaving it. You can always block cookies from your browser.  If you do that, you will have to enter your username and password at VideoElephant every time you visit our website.

If, at any time, you feel that VideoElephant failed to respect your privacy and the privacy of your personal information, we kindly ask you to contact us.

 

If you wish to terminate your account with VideoElephant, please contact us.
