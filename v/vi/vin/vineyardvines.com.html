Shipping

Where does vineyard vines ship?

  • Street addresses, P.O. Boxes (please check box during checkout) and Military APO/FPO address
  • All 50 states including the District of Columbia (excluding all US Territories)
  • To select countries outside the US.

    The following items are not eligible for international shipping
    - gift cards and e-gift cards
    - promo and coupon codes
    - gifting options (box, receipt and message)
    - personalized items
    - certain products that we're unable to ship outside the US (ties, bow ties, home, accessories, jewelry)

When will my order be shipped?

We strive to process orders as quickly as we possibly can. Orders placed Monday through Friday are usually shipped within 48 hours. Please note that during periods of high volume, it will take longer (sales, major promotions, Friends & Family, during the holidays, etc.) Orders placed over the weekend are usually shipped Monday. If you need your order to go out the same day you place it, please place your order before noon ET using an expedited service (excludes Saturday, Sunday and Holidays) and we’ll be sure to do our best to get you your merchandise in time.

When will my order be delivered?

Once processed, orders are shipped via UPS Ground (unless an expedited UPS service is chosen). UPS Ground is delivered within 5-7 business days to most U.S. locations; however, we've found that orders will arrive even sooner than that.

How can I track my order?

Once an order has shipped, you will receive an email with tracking information. This email might wind up in your "Spam" email folder, so be sure to add vineyard vines to your "safe" list.

What if I’d like to change my order after it’s placed?

We apologize, but we cannot make changes, adjustments, or cancellations to an order after it has been submitted. We may still be able to help, though! Just give us a call at 800-892-4982.

Can I ship to an address that is different from my billing address?

Absolutely! When you set up your vineyard vines account, you’re able to enter separate shipping addresses and save addresses that you may want to ship to in the future.

Why are my purchases being sent to me in multiple shipments?

You may have ordered a number of items that don’t fit into one box, or we are shipping your item from one of our store locations. You will receive an email with a tracking number and item list for each of your packages.

Please note, in order to fulfill your order to the best of our ability we sometimes ship your items directly from one of our stores which can result in multiple shipments for a single order. When this happens you may notice multiple posts to your credit card for a single order. The initial authorization is not a charge and will disappear from your account in a business day or so.

Why is the Yeti Cooler not subject to standard shipping costs?

Since this is a large and heavy item, our shipping provider charges more for us to ship this to you.

Return policy

Full-price items with a vineyard vines receipt:

  • Within 60 days of purchase: Full refund or merchandise credit
  • After 60 days of purchase: Full merchandise credit
  • After 180 days of purchase: Merchandise credit of last retail value

Sale items with a vineyard vines receipt:

  • Within 30 days of purchase: Full refund or merchandise credit
  • After 30 days of purchase: Full merchandise credit
  • After 90 days of purchase: Merchandise credit of last retail value
  • All goods marked as "final sale" are not be eligible for return

vineyard vines products without a vineyard vines receipt:

  • Can be returned for merchandise credit at the last retail value

Merchandise purchased at vineyard vines outlet locations is eligible for exchange within 30 days of purchase. Returns are not accepted and exchanges can only take place at a vineyard vines outlet store.

Merchandise purchased at Whalehouse Sales is final sale—returns or exchanges are not accepted.

Original shipping charges are not eligible for refund on orders that were shipped correctly.

Any personalized, monogrammed, or customized product is not eligible for return or exchange.

Refunds on returns will only be applied to the original form of payment with the shipping and handling fee deducted from the credit total. If you choose to send your goods through another carrier, we recommend you select a service that provides tracking information.

Exchange policy

If you need your exchange in a hurry, feel free to call us at 800-892-4982 and we will do our best to get your new products out to you quickly! You will be charged for the merchandise on its way to you (shipping is on us, of course), and you’ll be credited for your original purchase as soon as we receive your return.

How to exchange merchandise by mail:

  1. Complete the return section on the front side of this receipt and indicate items being returned
  2. Don't forget to let us know the reason for the returned item (please refer to our list of reason codes).
  3. Provide the product information for each new item you would like in the exchange form below.
  4. If the item you are returning is a gift, please fill out the contact information provided below.
  5. If the total value of your exchange is less than the value of the returned item(s), we will refund you the difference according to our return policies.
  6. If the total value of your exchange is greater than the value of the returned item(s), please include your contact information in the form below so we can contact you regarding proper form of payment.
  7. Take note of the tracking number for your records so that you can follow your shipment’s progress.

How long will my exchange take?

During high-volume seasons (holidays and Whale of a Sale, for example) it may take up to 30 days for your exchange to be processed. Should you need to expedite your exchange, we recommend that you purchase your new items from our website and then send the unwanted or incorrect items back to us for a refund. (To be eligible for an expedited exchange, you must be the original purchaser—we are only able to process a refund to the original form of payment). .

Can I exchange a Gift Card?

All sales of Gift Cards are final and are not eligible for exchanges.

If you give us your email address either on the home page, when you register, or order, we will send you emails about events, new arrivals, and promotions that we feel will be of interest to you. To be removed from our email list, simply log in to My Account and opt out of our email promotions.

When you supply your postal address either when requesting a free catalog or placing an order, you will receive the vineyard vines catalog by mail. If you choose to give us your telephone number on the order form, it is for the purpose of contacting you regarding your order. We will not call you regarding promotions or just to chat if we get bored.

If you do not want a vineyard vines catalog, please send us an email to catalog@vineyardvines.com with the following:

  • The phrase "no catalog" in the subject line

Updating email or postal addresses

If you forget your password, you can request that it be emailed to your email address. We will not give passwords out over the phone, nor will we email them to a different email address than the one used to register.

Privacy Policy

UPDATED January 2017

At vineyardvines.com, your privacy is a top priority. Please read our Privacy Policy details. vineyard vines® collects customer information to improve your overall shopping experience with us. We respect your privacy and are committed to maintaining and using this information responsibly. This privacy policy addresses the use and disclosure of information we collect from you on our website as well as information we collect directly from customers on the telephone or in our stores. Personal information relating to you and your order history are only available to you if you provide the correct password that you create on vineyardvines.com. After providing your password, you can edit or delete your information at any time through the My Account page.

If you forget your password, you can request that it be emailed to your email address. We will not give passwords out over the phone, nor will we email them to a different email address than the one used to register. If you request to have your password emailed to you, your credit card information will be deleted from your profile.

Your address book, located in the shipping addresses & contacts page, is your personal storage space. We do not use the names of the people in your address book to send email promotions or catalogs by mail.

Information we share

Any personal information you provide to us on our website when you register or update an account, participate in a contest, sweepstakes, promotion or survey, join our mailing list, request a catalog, shop online, shop by telephone or in our stores, or request information (i.e. name, address, email address, telephone number and credit card information) is maintained in private files on our secure Web server and our internal systems.

If you are visiting vineyardvines.com from a location outside of the United States, you will be connected through and to servers located within the United States. All vineyardvines.com orders will be accepted and fully processed in and shipped from the United States and all information you provide will be securely maintained in our Web server and internal systems located within the United States. We may occasionally share our postal and email lists, as well as your shopping history (but not your credit card information), with third parties whose products or services may be of interest to you and who have their own privacy policies in place. If you do not want us to share your personal information with these third parties, you may opt-out by emailing us at privacy@vineyardvines.com. Please include your name and postal address and write "do not share my information" in the subject line. You may also opt-out by writing to us at the address located at the end of this Privacy Policy and including your name, postal and email address.

Customers who are California residents may request certain information about our disclosure of personal information to third parties for their direct marketing purposes. To make such a request, please email us at privacy@vineyardvines.com and write "California Privacy Rights" in the subject line. Alternatively, you may write to us at the address located and the end of this Privacy Policy.

We also may share your information with unaffiliated third parties who provide services to vineyard vines such as helping us to maintain and manage our customer information, communicate with our customers, service our website, conduct promotions or surveys, order fulfillment, etc. vineyard vines does not authorize any of these service providers to use or disclose customer information other than in connection with providing services to vineyard vines.

In the event of a sale or merger of vineyard vines our customer’s personal information may be shared with the merged or purchasing entity. You should also be aware that we may disclose specific information about you if required to do so by law, governmental request or court order or based on our good faith belief that it is necessary to conform or comply with such law, request or court order or to protect the users of our website or the public.

Links to other sites

To enhance your shopping experience, the vineyard vines site may contain links to other sites. Those Web sites will have their own policies on privacy that you may wish to review. vineyard vines has no responsibility for these linked Web sites.

Cookies

We use cookies to recognize you and your preferences to make shopping easier and provide you with a customized experience on our website. Cookies are bits of information that we automatically store on your computer if your computer accepts cookies. Cookies do not contain any personally identifiable information such as your name, address or any financial information on your computer. vineyard vines places a unique user ID number on your cookie which allows us to access information that will improve your shopping experience - such as holding items in your shopping bag, accessing your address book, or providing you with customized pages based on your past viewing or purchasing preferences. We also use cookies to track traffic to different parts of the website in order to make improvements and optimize your shopping experience. vineyard vines web cookies are gluten free.

If you do not wish to have a cookie on your system, you can set your browser preferences to refuse them. In order to disable cookies, please consult your browser’s help section for instructions.

Privacy for children

vineyard vines is concerned about the privacy of young children and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from a child under 13. vineyard vines does not sell products for purchase by children, and all children’s products we sell are for purchase by adults only.

Security

The security of your information is very important to us. We use the 128-bit Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology to protect the security of your online order information. SSL technology encrypts your order information to protect it from being decoded by anyone other than vineyard vines.

Once you enter the checkout page, your computer will begin communicating with our server in secure mode. You can tell that you are in secure mode because the "http" in our web address will be replaced by "https."

Depending on the browser, you will see either a padlock (Microsoft Internet Explorer, AOL, Netscape Communicator) or a solid key (Netscape Navigator) in the lower section for the browser window.

Only browsers that use the 128-bit Secure Socket Layer technology are able to order through the Vineyard vines web site. If your browser does not support SSL, we recommend that you download an updated version.

While we implement these and other security measures on this site, please note that 100% security is not always possible.

Whenever we make a change, we will post the updated Policy at this site and we encourage you to check our policy periodically. By using our website following the posting of a change in the Privacy Policy, you consent to the updated Policy.
