In-store Pick Up

How does in-store pick up work?

To see if an item is eligible for In-Store Pickup, look for the In-Store Pickup option on the product detail page. Simply select the color, size and quantity and choose a store.

When will my order be ready for pickup?

I know you can’t wait to get your new items and we can’t wait to see you. We will email you as soon as your items are ready (within 4 hours) letting you know it’s time to pick up your order. If your order was placed after 2PM, you'll receive an email the following day.

What do I need to bring with me to pick up my order?

Pick up is easy! All you need is your order confirmation email.

Can a friend pick up my order?

Absolutely! That’s what friends are for. Just make sure they have your confirmation email with the order number.

Not everything I want is available in the store. Can I select certain items to pick up and have others shipped?

Whale yes you sure can! When you check out select Buy Online, Pickup In-Store for certain items, and select your preferred shipping method for your other items.

How long will you hold my order?

We understand sometimes things come up. You will receive and email once the product(s) is ready for pickup, then we will gladly hold your order for 72 hours. You will receive a cancellation email if you don't pick up the order within 72 hours.

When will my credit card be charged?

A hold is placed once you complete the order, but your card isn't charged until the items are picked up.

Your payment will be authorized when you create your order and a hold will appear on your account, but you will not be charged until you pick up your order in the store.

What happens if I don’t pick-up my order?

If you don't pick up your order within 72 hours, your order will be automatically canceled and you will not be charged for the items. Any hold that may have appeared on your card will be released.

Can I cancel my store pickup?

We’re sorry to hear that you want to cancel your order. We would be more than happy to help! Just call the store you designated for pickup or customer care at 1-800-892-4982.

When will my order be shipped?

We strive to process orders as quickly as we possibly can. Orders placed Monday through Friday are usually shipped within 48 hours. Please note that during periods of high volume, it will take longer (sales, major promotions, Friends & Family, during the holidays, etc.) Orders placed over the weekend are usually shipped the next business day. If you need your order to go out the same day you place it, please place your order before noon ET using an expedited service (excludes Saturday, Sunday and Holidays) and we’ll be sure to do our best to get you your merchandise in time.

When will my order be delivered?

Once processed, orders are shipped via Standard Ground (unless an expedited service is chosen). Standard Ground is delivered within 2-7 business days to most U.S. locations; however, we've found that orders will arrive even sooner than that.

How can I track my order?

Once an order has shipped, you will receive an email with tracking information. This email might wind up in your "Spam" email folder, so be sure to add vineyard vines to your "safe" list.

What if I’d like to change my order after it’s placed?

We apologize, but we cannot make changes, adjustments, or cancellations to an order after it has been submitted. We may still be able to help, though! Just give us a call at 800-892-4982.

Can I ship to an address that is different from my billing address?

Absolutely! When you set up your vineyard vines account, you’re able to enter separate shipping addresses and save addresses that you may want to ship to in the future.

Why are my purchases being sent to me in multiple shipments?

You may have ordered a number of items that don’t fit into one box, or we are shipping your item from one of our store locations. You will receive an email with a tracking number and item list for each of your packages.

Please note, in order to fulfill your order to the best of our ability we sometimes ship your items directly from one of our stores which can result in multiple shipments for a single order. When this happens you may notice multiple posts to your credit card for a single order. The initial authorization is not a charge and will disappear from your account in a business day or so.

Borderfree can, however, charge the customer for cost of shipping goods back to Borderfree. Reimbursement by Borderfree will be within 30 days.

Registering & My Account

Do I have to create an account before I place an order?

Nope! You can check out as a guest, and if you change your mind, you can always register at the end of the checkout process.

Why should I become a registered user of vineyard vines?

When you order from vineyard vines in the future, we’ll remember your information such as billing and shipping addresses to ensure a quicker checkout next time around. You can also store other information, access your order history, and manage your email preferences.

How does vineyard vines use my information?

Please find our privacy policy.

How do you remember what products I place in my cart, even though I have closed out of the page?

We remember and track information about your cart using a web technology called "cookies." Please read our cookie policy here. Or, make your own cookies here.

Is vineyardvines.com a secure site?

Yes. The security of your information is very important to us. We use 128-bit Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology to protect the security of your online order information. SSL technology encrypts your order information to protect it from being decoded by anyone other than vineyard vines. Read more about secure mode.

When I register with vineyard vines, will I start receiving email and catalogs?

Yes, you will automatically be added to our mailing lists. You can unsubscribe to vineyard vines emails in your account at any time.

If you would like to be removed from our catalog mailing list, please email catalog@vineyardvines.com with the phrase "no catalog" in the subject line and your name and your complete mailing address in the body of the email.

Changing Email Preferences

If you give us your email address either on the home page, when you register, or order, we will send you emails about events, new arrivals, and promotions that we feel will be of interest to you. To be removed from our email list, simply log in to My Account and opt out of our email promotions.

When you supply your postal address either when requesting a free catalog or placing an order, you will receive the vineyard vines catalog by mail. If you choose to give us your telephone number on the order form, it is for the purpose of contacting you regarding your order. We will not call you regarding promotions or just to chat if we get bored.

If you do not want a vineyard vines catalog, please send us an email to catalog@vineyardvines.com with the following:

  • The phrase "no catalog" in the subject line
  • Your name and your complete mailing address

Updating email or postal addresses

You can edit or delete your email or postal address at anytime through the My Account Page.

What if I forgot my password?

If you forget your password, you can request that it be emailed to your email address. We will not give passwords out over the phone, nor will we email them to a different email address than the one used to register.

Please note: If you request to have your password emailed to you, your credit card information will be deleted from your profile.
