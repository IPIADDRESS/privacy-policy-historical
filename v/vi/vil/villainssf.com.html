Help > Privacy and Security

Thank you for shopping at Villainssf.com. Your privacy and the security while shopping is very important to us. Our privacy and security policy will fill you in about the information we are collecting from you and how and why we use this information.

Personal Information:   Information provided while creating or updating an account* and at checkout (ie: name, address, telephone number, etc) is stored in private files on our secure web server and in our internal systems.

Newsletter:   Newsletter recipients receive advanced notice of sales, new merchandise, events and other Villains news. Email addresses collected at Villainssf.com are only used internally. We respect your privacy and do not distribute your email address to any 3rd parties. If you would like to unsubscribe from our newsletter, click here. We will remove your name from our list a.s.a.p.

Order Security  Orders placed on our site are encrypted using SSL technology**, you can verify this by looking at the URL in your web browser, the "s" in "https://" signals that our site is registered with a SSL certificate. With SSL on both the client and server, information sent to us through the Internet arrives privately and unaltered. Please note that email is not encrypted and is not considered a safe or secure means of transmitting credit card numbers. We will never ask for this information via email.

Policy Changes   Villainssf.com may update this Privacy & Security Policy from time to time as new, unanticipated uses of our site arise. Any changes made will be posted here, by visiting our website you agree to accept any changes made to this policy.

*You can easily update your account information by logging on to your Villainssf.com account and clicking edit my profile.
**Secure Socket Layer (SSL) is a technology used by vendors to ensure the security of online business. It employs mutual authentication, data encryption and data integrity functions for secure transactions.
