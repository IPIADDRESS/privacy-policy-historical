Privacy Protection

VistaPrint recognizes the importance of maintaining your privacy.

When you sign up for an account, place an order, or volunteer information to receive our services, we collect some data about you, including your e-mail and IP addresses. The data helps us continue to serve you effectively: your IP address helps us diagnose problems with our server and administer our web site and your e-mail address allows us to contact you with order confirmations and for internal marketing purposes.

Any financial information you submit is used only in billing for the specific transaction in which the information was submitted. We do not retain credit card information between transactions; consequently, you will be asked to reenter your credit card information each time you place an order.

The Function of Cookies

A cookie is a small file that your browser creates and stores at our request in an approved and standardized place on your computer. VistaPrint uses cookies to assign you a unique customer code and record information about your activity on our site. When you visit or return to our site, we read the cookie to identify you as one of our customers and help you recall orders. The cookie also enables us to recall your past activities, post your account data, and tailor site elements and special offers to you. The cookie does not retain your billing information, nor does it save any of your confidential information in an explicit fashion.

Most web browsers accept cookies by default, but allow users to reject cookies by changing the browser preferences. If you have set up your VistaPrint browser to reject cookies, some aspects of our site, including our Purchase Wizard, will not work properly.

Secure Encryption

We use Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology, which is supported by the vast majority of modern Internet browsers. SSL works best when you are using the latest 128-bit encryption versions of either Microsoft Internet Explorer or Netscape Navigator.

SSL technology represents the highest level of security available on the Internet. It automatically encrypts information traveling over the Internet, verifies the identity of the transacting servers through certificates and digital signatures, and verifies that the integrity of message content is maintained throughout transmission.

VistaPrint's minimum requirement for transactions is the standard 40-bit or 56-bit encryption supported by the following browser: Internet Explorer 5.0. If you are not using this browser, please refer to your browser’s documentation for information on its security features.

For more details on the browsers supported by VistaPrint.com please see our browser requirements page.
