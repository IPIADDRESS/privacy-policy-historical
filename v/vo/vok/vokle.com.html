This Privacy Notice is incorporated into and is subject to the Vokle Terms of Service. Your use of the Vokle and any personal information you provide through the Vokle are subject at all time to this Privacy Notice and the Terms of Service.

By using Vokle, you expressly consent to the information handling practices described in this notice.

The Information Vokle.com Collects

User-Provided Information

You may provide what is generally called “personally identifiable” information (such as your name, email address, zip code, birthday, personal bio, profile photo, profile comments, live video streams, credit card number and billing information) to Vokle if you form an account or attempt to access certain functionality or content at Vokle.

Automatic Information

When you access Vokle, we may send one or more session and/or persistent cookies, which are small text files containing a string of alphanumeric characters, to your computer. A session cookie disappears after you close your browser. A persistent cookie remains after closing your browser and may be reused by your browser on subsequent visits to Vokle.com. Persistent cookies can be removed. Please review your web browser “Help” section to learn the proper way to modify your cookie settings. Additionally, some of Vokle’s business partners and advertisers may use cookies on our website. We have no access to, or control over, these cookies.

Furthermore, Vokle and its authorized agents automatically receive and record certain "traffic data" on their server logs when you visit the site to help diagnose problems with its servers, analyze trends and administer the website. This traffic data includes, but is not limited to, your IP address, a unique user ID, cookies, version of software installed, system type, the content, live chats and pages that you access at Vokle.com as well as the dates and times that you visit the Vokle.com.

The Way Vokle Uses Information

  • Vokle.com uses the information that you provide or that we collect to operate, enhance, and provide all of the features and services offered by Vokle, as well as to track user-generated content and Users to the extent necessary to comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act.
  • We will use your email address, without further consent, for administrative communications such as Vokle.com system updates, customer service, terms of service infringement or defamation issues, or to contact you regarding any content that you have submitted to or downloaded from the Vokle.
  • Vokle may use automatically collected information and/or cookies to: (a) automatically update the website on your system; (b) remember your sign-in information so that you will not have to re-enter it every time you access the Vokle.com; (c) track your entries, submissions, and status in any promotions or other activities; (d) monitor aggregate site usage metrics such as total number of visitors and pages accessed.

When Vokle.com Discloses Information

Rest assured that Vokle not sell your personal information to anyone and that we will share your personal information only as described below.

* Vokle may disclose user information if required to do so by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with state and federal laws or respond to a court order, judicial or other government subpoena, or warrant in the manner required by the requesting entity.

We cannot, however, ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to Vokle, and you do so at your own risk. Once we receive your transmission of information, Vokle.com makes commercially reasonable efforts to ensure the security of our systems. However, please note that this is not a guarantee that such information may not be accessed, disclosed, altered, or destroyed by breach of any of our physical, technical, or managerial safeguards.

If Vokle.com learns of a security systems breach, then we may attempt to notify you electronically so that you can take appropriate protective steps. To receive a free written notice of a security breach you should notify us at privacy@vokle.com.

Our Commitment to Children’s Privacy

If you are under 18 years of age, then please do not use or access Vokle at any time or in any manner.

Protecting the privacy of young children is especially important. For that reason, Vokle does not knowingly collect or maintain personally identifiable information from persons under 18 years-of-age. If we learn that Vokle has collected information from a child under the age of 18, we may delete that information as promptly as possible.

If you are the parent or legal guardian of a child under 18 who has become Vokle member, then please contact Vokle.com at privacy@vokle.com to have that child’s account terminated and information deleted.
