Privacy Notice

Overall privacy statement

We take your privacy seriously.

Because we gather certain types of information about our users we want to help you understand the terms and conditions surrounding the collection and use of that information. This privacy statement discloses the types of information we gather, and how we use it. These privacy practices apply to the Web site that you were viewing when you clicked through to this policy, which is operated by us directly, our partner, or our affiliate.

What information does VoIP Supply collect and why?

We collect several types of data when you create an account and shop with us, Account Data, Transactional Data, and Cookie Data.

Account Data

Account data is simply information that allows us to:

  • Identify you and your transactions
  • Contact you with regards to your transactions
  • To ship your products

Transitional Data

Transitional data is the information required to determine what you purchased from us, what products, payment method and shipping method you have selected, and to bill you for the products or services you ordered from us.

Cookie Data

Cookie data is information from our website that is kept by your web browser. We collect it for the benefit of the user experience with our websites, such as for remembering what is in your shopping cart while you are shopping. We may collect your IP address, for the purposes of identifying your session related data on our site.

VoIPSupply.com will not sell your information to any third party

The data we collect is for our purposes only. It will not be sold to any third party, nor does any third party have the ability to view the data we collect without your prior approval or consent.

All sensitive data is encrypted using a Geotrust SSL certificate.

Should you have any comments or concerns with regards to your privacy when using our websites, please contact us and we will gladly explain in further details anything you may wish to know about the use of your personal information on our sites.
