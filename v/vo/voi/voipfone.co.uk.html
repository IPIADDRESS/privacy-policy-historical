Voipfone Cookie Policy

 

Some cookies are known as essential cookies and are a requirement for the operation of our site and services. For example, these cookies allow users to log in to our user interface and make changes to their service or account, add items to their shopping cart and provide web-based customer services via live chat applications.

Users of our website and services have the option to disable these cookies in their browser but will no longer be able to access their account or configure their services.

These cookies will remain on your device after you have left our website. These cookies help us identify you as a unique visitor these cookies do not contain personal information, and cannot be used to identify you in any way.

We use Google Analytics to collate statistical information about our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to help us to analyse how users use the site. Please click here for more information about Analytics cookies.

 

Removing Or Blocking Cookies

iNet Telecoms Ltd will not use cookies to collect personally identifiable information about you. However, if you wish to restrict or block the cookies on this or any other website, you can do this by making changes to your browser settings.
