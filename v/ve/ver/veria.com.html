Sometimes, Asia TV USA Ltd obtains your personal data from third parties. This collection of your personal data could occur if we buy a business of which you already are a customer or if you disclose your personal data to one of our partner companies. If you do not want us to use personal data which you have not directly provided to us, you can let us know by contacting us at the address listed above.

Asia TV USA Ltd does not authorize any of the Service Providers to make any other use of your information or to contact you outside the context of the services related to the Asia TV USA Ltd Website.

Asia TV USA Ltd reserves the right to disclose information about you as required by law, in response to legal process and law enforcement requests, and as necessary to protect the property, interests and rights of Asia TV USA Ltd, its affiliates, and others.

Also, please allow one (1) week for email requests and three (3) weeks for postal mail requests to be fulfilled.

If you are a parent or legal guardian of a child under the age of 18, and your child has provided Asia TV USA Ltd with personal information using the Asia TV USA Ltd Website, you may contact Asia TV USA Ltd using the Contact Information provided above and we will delete any and all personal information about your child as soon as reasonably possible.

                                                                                    

6. What are cookies? How does Asia TV USA Ltd use cookies on its site?
-Some of our Web Sites will send a "cookie," a small text file, to your computer. This allows us to recognize your computer the next time you visit, without bothering you with another request to register. And, if you use an online "shopping cart," cookies allow us to keep track of the products you purchase. If you do not wish to receive cookies, please refer to the "Help" file of your Internet browser either to learn how to block all cookies or how to receive a warning before a cookie is stored. To learn more about cookies, we encourage you to visit www.cookiecentral.com.

If you visit the Asia TV USA Ltd Website to browse, read, or download information, we collect and store some "visitor information" from you, such as the name of the domain and host from which you access the Internet, the Internet Protocol (IP) address of the computer you are using, the date and time you access the Asia TV USA Ltd Website, and the Internet address of the website from which you linked directly to the Asia TV USA Ltd Website. We use this information to measure the traffic on the Asia TV USA Ltd Website and to help us make the Asia TV USA Ltd Website more useful. We delete this information after some time.
