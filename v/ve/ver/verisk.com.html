Verisk Analytics Website Privacy Policy

The following statement will help you understand how Verisk Analytics collects, uses, and safeguards the personally identifying information you give us when you use this website.

We define "personally identifying information" as information — such as name, address, telephone number, and e-mail address — that can help to locate or identify individuals. "Personally identifying information" also includes credit card information. Protecting the privacy of personally identifying information is important to us.

Visitors to verisk.com are subject to important limitations. Please review our Conditions of Use, which govern your visit to this site.

Information We Collect
When you browse this website, you browse anonymously, unless you submit a form, respond to an e-mail solicitation from Verisk, submit a question or comment through "Contact Verisk," or send us employment information or e-mail. We do not collect or store any personal information — such as your name, address, phone number, or e-mail address — unless you knowingly and willingly provide it.

To help us serve your needs, we may examine your browsing history. When you purchase a product, register for a conference, submit a form, respond to an e-mail solicitation from Verisk, or submit a question or comment through "Contact Verisk," we may use the information you provide, together with your browsing history, to help us understand your needs and provide information that meets those needs.

We may use the personal information you provide to contact you about your request, to provide announcements about products and future conferences, to conduct surveys of our customers, to respond to your inquiries and suggestions, and to contact you for other reasons related to offering and improving our services. We may use and share all of this information, subject to applicable law, with our subsidiaries and affiliates and with other reputable companies that wish to offer products and services in which our customers may be interested.

We may display employment opportunities on this site. If you choose to submit your resume or other information through our website, we use that information only in considering your application for employment. We will share the information only with employees involved in making hiring decisions. We may also submit the information to our subsidiaries and affiliates for their use in considering your application for employment. Verisk keeps employment application information strictly confidential, and does not sell, rent, share, or otherwise disclose any personally identifiable employment application information that you directly provide, except as required by law.

We do not permit other persons, companies or entities to collect personal information through our website.

Cookies
Our Web server may write a "cookie," a small computer file, to your hard drive. We may use cookies to enhance your online experience, to facilitate your use of our site, to recognize you as a repeat visitor, and to collect statistics about your visit to the site. The cookies used by this site do not contain personally identifying information, such as your name, address, telephone number, e-mail address, or credit card information.

Most web browsers allow you to exercise control over cookie files on your computer by erasing them, blocking them, or notifying you when you receive such files. Please refer to your own browser's instructions for information on those functions. If you do elect to disable the cookies from this site, please note that you may not be able to use the "shopping cart." Security
The security of customer information is very important to us, and we take a number of steps to safeguard it. After receiving your personally identifying information, we maintain reasonable physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect it from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, loss, or destruction. We limit access to those employees who assist us in providing the services you order. We also require that third parties to which we disclose personally identifying information adhere to this policy and establish information security procedures.

How We Protect Social Security Numbers
Verisk businesses may collect Social Security numbers (“SSNs”) in the course of business. We strive to protect the confidentiality and security of SSNs in our possession, custody, or control by limiting access to SSNs and maintaining reasonable safeguards to protect against their loss, misuse, or unlawful disclosure.

Although we choose our sites carefully, we are not responsible for the content, maintenance, or privacy policies of those sites. Once you click over to a third-party site, we encourage you to check that site's privacy policy.
