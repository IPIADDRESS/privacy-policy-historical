Visa takes extensive precautions to secure your personal and financial information, and it is only used for the purposes of providing this service. We want you to be assured that your information is properly safeguarded. Personal and payment card information is transmitted using a high level of encryption, and is stored on our secure server behind a firewall to protect against unauthorized access.

Remember, you can always help maintain the security of your information by keeping your password and other personal identifying information confidential.

Visa will not use or disclose any cardholder information entered at the Verified by Visa web site for any purpose other than:

(a) Your account number may be disclosed only to the financial institution that issued your Visa card (and its processors) for the sole purpose of activating your card in the Verified by Visa service.
(b) If you submit your email address and request to be notified when Verified by Visa will be available for your Visa card, Visa will only use your email address to send you notification that Verified by Visa is available for your Visa card.
(c) If you activate on the Verified by Visa web site, your email address will only be used to confirm your activation and alert you to important changes in your Verified by Visa service. Additionally, if you specifically request it, your email address will be used to notify you of shopping offers and promotions for Verified by Visa users, and to notify you as additional online stores join Verified by Visa.

We will never use your email address to send you any unsolicited message or information, nor will we share it with or sell it to anyone for such use.
