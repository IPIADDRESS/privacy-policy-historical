Privacy Policy

The information provided by you is protected and can't be accessed by anyone else. VEXXHOST will only use the customer's personal information only as reasonably necessary to provide the contracted services.

Disclosure of Personal Information
The customer authorizes vexxhost to use it's name, business name, city or country information and comments in marketing documents or as testimonials on our website. At any time, the customer can send a written notice to withdraw this authorization. VEXXHOST will only disclose personal information to a third party if requested by law as evidenced by a valid Court Order of competent jurisdiction if needed.

Disclosure of Non-Personal Aggregates
