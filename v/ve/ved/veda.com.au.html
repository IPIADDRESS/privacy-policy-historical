Privacy

Personal Information

We collect personal information and credit information. Typically, we collect information from publicly available sources or from former, current or prospective credit provider or credit providers.

Uses

Personal information is collected to provide a credit reference service to credit providers and related analytical and statistical functions. This includes the development of scores and other analytical products. Our products assist our clients to understand and manage their data.

Your Choices

If you do not want to provide your personal information to us you need to inform your actual or prospective credit provider of your choice at the time that you first provide your information to them.  

If you are an Australian citizen or resident and we hold personal information about you, you can have access to that information by requesting access via http://www.mycreditfile.com.au/  

If you are a New Zealand citizen or resident and we hold personal information about you, you can have access to that information by requesting access via www.mycreditfile.co.nz

Our Commitment to Privacy

We take privacy very seriously. We undertake initiatives that build privacy enhancing practices and best practice measures into the way we deal with personal information. We regularly consult with consumer groups, advocates and regulators to achieve our objectives. Your suggestions are welcome - please contact us.
