Velti Privacy Statement

 

At Velti we are committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology that provides you with a powerful, safe, online experience. The information collected about you is used to process your requests and ensure a more personalized on-line interaction. Please read on for more details about our privacy policy. At no time will Velti distribute your profile information to any third party without your express permission.

Information Collection and Use

Velti is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement. Velti collects information from our users at several different points on our website.

Cookies

A cookie is a set of parameters stored in the User’s hard disk, containing selected information about the User’s visits to a site. A cookie may retain preferences such as language selection, username or other similar information that would otherwise require frequent submissions by the User. Usage of a cookie cannot in any way lead to any personally identifiable information while on our site. Most cookies are automatically deleted by closing the browser (session cookies).

Cookies may be disabled in the User’s browser, but this will limit some features of our site, and the User may have to repeatedly submit the same information.

Log Files

We use IP addresses to analyse trends of Web usage, administer the site, monitor possible illegal User activity and gather broad demographic information (country origin) for aggregate use. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

Correspondence

For reasons such as improving personalization of our service, and maintaining a high quality of service, we receive and store certain types of information such as emails, faxes and letters whenever you interact with us. In addition, if other Users send us correspondence about your activities on Velti’s site we may store this information in a file for your reference.

Disclosure of Information for Legal Actions

In the event that Velti has reason to believe that a User of its services is causing injury to or interfering with Velti’s rights or property or another Velti User, we or another User may pursue legal action. In the event legal action is taken we may disclose User information to facilitate the proceedings.

Correcting/Updating User Information

You have the option to change or modify information previously provided by revising your user profile online or by contacting Velti via email, phone, or fax.

Security

The Internet, like any other electronic media used for information transmission cannot be claimed to be 100% secure, Security over the Internet is a delicate issue and is primarily based upon trusted organizations and businesses that respect the privacy and security of its Users. Velti uses the latest tools of information encryption and protection for all transactions involving sensitive data (128-bit encryption, secure pages, high security firewall, and secure facility).

Notification of Changes

In the event Velti makes changes to its privacy policy, we will post these changes on our Web site for your review. At all time Velti users should we aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under which circumstances, if any, we disclose it. If at any point we decide to use personally identifiable information in a manner different from that stated at the time it was collected, we will notify users by way of an email. Users will have a choice as to whether or not we use their information in this different manner. We will use information in accordance with the privacy policy under which the information was collected.

Links

This site may contain links to other sites. Please be aware that Velti is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such sites. This Privacy Statement applies solely to information collected by Velti.
