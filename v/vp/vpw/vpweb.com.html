NPS Internet Solutions, LLC is committed to honoring the privacy of our webhosting customers as well as any other clients or visitors to our web site.

We guarantee our clients that their personal and financial information collected via our website, or through any other means of communication is held in strict confidence and is not divulged to any outside source for any purpose whatsoever.

We also are committed to providing a secure pathway for transactions such that our clients information is protected against fraudulent misuse.

On occasion we may supply potential customers the url or web address of a particular clients website simply for advertising purposes and to showcase the speed and reliability or our services. However, the information supplied to the third party is limited solely to the above mentioned web address and no further information, such as names, mailing addresses, or email address is ever divulged.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy please contact us.
