What is this Privacy Policy for?

This privacy policy is for all sites owned and served by Pageant Media and governs the privacy of the users who choose to use it. These sites are listed at https://www.pageantmedia.com/products/

The policy sets out the different areas where user privacy is concerned and outlines the obligations & requirements of the users, the website and website owners. Furthermore, the way this website and Pageant Media processes, stores and protects user data and information will also be detailed within this policy.

Data processing and acquisition

Your personal information is kept private and stored securely for a maximum of two years following your most recent digital interaction with Pageant Media, unless required to successfully execute the terms of a contract you hold with us, as detailed in the GDPR regulation. Every effort has been made to ensure a safe and secure form to email submission process but advise users using such form to email processes that they do so at their own risk. Users have the right to withdraw consent, object to processing, to request rectification/erasure and submit a Subject Access Request through Pageant Media’s data controller for any personal information collected. See end of privacy policy for contact details.

Legitimate Interest

Pageant Media also operates data research projects to provide relevant and appropriate information to select individuals based on legitimate interest in Pageant Media products. Individuals are carefully selected based on detailed criteria that correctly matches products to prospects. Pageant Media, in compliance with GDPR regulation will market to prospects only targeted where a legitimate interest exists and takes a proactive approach to ensure the receiving prospects are carefully selected

“Legitimate Interests” means the interests of our company in conducting and managing our business to enable us to give you the best service/products and the best and most secure experience.

For example, we have an interest in making sure our marketing is relevant for you, so we may process your information to send you marketing that is tailored to your interests. It can also apply to processing that is in your interests as well. For example, we may process your information to protect you against fraud when transacting on our website, and to ensure our websites and systems are secure. When we process your personal information for our legitimate interests, we make sure to consider and balance any potential impact on you (both positive and negative), and your rights under data protection laws. Our legitimate business interests do not automatically override your interests – we will not use your Personal Data for activities where our interests are overridden by the impact on you (unless we have your consent or are otherwise required or permitted to by law).

We process personal information for certain legitimate business purposes, which include some or all of the following:

  • where the processing enables us to enhance, modify, personalise or otherwise improve our services / communications for the benefit of our customers
  • to identify and prevent fraud • to enhance the security of our network and information systems
  • to better understand how people interact with our websites
  • to provide postal communications which we think will be of interest to you
  • to determine the effectiveness of promotional campaigns and advertising

 

Whenever we process data for these purposes we will ensure that we always keep your Personal Data rights in high regard and take account of these rights. You have the right to object to this processing if you wish, and if you wish to do so please get in touch with us (See contact details below). Please bear in mind that if you object this may affect our ability to carry out tasks above for your benefit.
