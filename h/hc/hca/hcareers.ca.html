Information Disclosure

Except as described in this privacy policy, we do not use or disclose, to any outside parties, any personal information that you provide, such as your name, e-mail address, telephone number, and employment information, except when we are permitted or required by law, including the Personal Information Protection Act (British Columbia) and other applicable privacy laws. We do not sell, rent or exchange lists of job seekers or employers with other companies.

We do employ other companies to perform functions on our behalf, e.g. web site development. These other companies will have access to your personal information as necessary to perform their functions on our behalf, but they may not share that information with any third party or otherwise use that information.

When you sign up to use a service of Hcareers, we may use that information to administer and build your Hcareersâ account, to provide the services that you have requested, to understand the needs of our clients and to ensure a high standard of service to our clients, and we may make it available to employers and recruiters in connection with those services. We may also use that information to make you aware of additional news and services that may be of interest to you. You may choose to opt out of receiving these additional news and services by replying to the notice and instructing us to remove you from further notices.

Visitor Information

Hcareers monitors our website visitor information, such as which pages you visit most frequently and which site referred you. This data does not include any personally identifying information. We use this information in order to optimize our website and to assess its usage. We may provide such information to our website contractors for these purposes.

Resumes

To help you find or apply to jobs, Hcareers gives you the option of putting your resume in our database. You can either store your resume in our database, but not allow it to be searchable by employers (which means that you use it only apply to specific jobs online), or you can allow your resume to be searchable by all employers and recruiters who pay for access to our resume database.

Please know that we use our best efforts to grant access to our resume database only to paying employers and recruiters, but we cannot guarantee that other parties will not, without our consent, gain access to this database. You may remove your resume at any time, but know that anyone (authorized or unauthorized) who has had access to our database may have retained a copy of your resume. Please know that we are not responsible for the retention, use, or privacy of resumes in these instances, or for the use or privacy of resumes by any of such parties while resumes are in the database.

To Update Your Account

To update your personal information at any time on Hcareers, please log-in to your account and go to the "User Options" where you may edit your information.

Cookies

Cookies are small files that your Web browser places on your computer's hard drive. We use cookies for remembering your user names and passwords etc. Cookies enable us to understand how our site is used and to make improvements. You have the option of setting your browser to reject cookies, but this will negatively impact your experience on our site.

Feedback

Hcareers allows you to submit feedback to us. This feedback becomes the property of Hcareers and we may use this feedback for marketing, e.g. success stories.

Private Label Sites

Hcareers hosts private label employment sites (Custom Job Banks) on behalf of other companies and permits online applications to job postings. All online applications to job postings on a private label site are placed directly into the employers' Hcareers Inbox to be viewed by anyone privy to or who otherwise gains access to the employers Hcareers User Id & password. Please know that we are not responsible for the retention, use, or privacy of information accessed by others through Custom Job Banks or online applications to job postings.

Children

Hcareers does not collect information about children and further believes that children should get their parents' consent before giving out personal information. If you are concerned about this, we encourage you to use web filtering technology to supervise your child's access to the site.

Changes to our Privacy Commitment

Any changes to our Privacy Commitment will be posted here and will be effective upon posting. If, at any time, you have questions or concerns about Hcareers' privacy commitment, please feel free to e-mail us at privacy@hcareers.com.
