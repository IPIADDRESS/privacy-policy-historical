ATB Holidays is an international travel company found in 1998, adopting office-sale working model on yachting services.

In 2003 the company changed its strategy and started working on Turkey Tours. Achieving growth throughout its history by adding Greece, Egypt, Dubai and the Far East Countries to its ongoing works, at present ATB Holidays has become a primary servicer in 90 countries, offering tours and packages to individual travelers and travel agencies alike.

By a new agreement in October 2011, ATB Holidays has been incorporated into the structure of the major global firm MetGlobal, one of the most experienced and respected hotel and flight provider in online tourism sector. Business partnership intends for domestic and abroad accommodation, flight and package tours provided by the in-field specialist companies MetGlobal and ATB Holidays with the opportunity of growth by combining their customer portfolio.

ATB Holidays is a member of TURSAB, ASTA (American Society of Travel Agents), CLIA (Cruise Lines International Association) and is also an ISO 9001 certified tour operator and travel agency.
