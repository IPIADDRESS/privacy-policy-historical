PRIVACY STATEMENT

This privacy statement covers the website www.hotels.com which is operated by Hotels.com. L.P. Your privacy is important to us. We want to make your experience on the Internet enjoyable and to foster your trust in electronic commerce as a whole. Because we want to demonstrate our commitment to our users' privacy, we disclose our information collection practices.

This privacy statement is an integral part of our website and the services offered to you and cannot, in any way, be separated from the User Agreement or from the membership, usage or transaction process. If you do not agree to our privacy policy in whole, you must not use our website.

By clicking on the "Continue" button when you submit your personal information you consent to the use and processing of your information in accordance with this privacy statement .

Content of the Privacy Statement:
  1. Information collected online about you.
  2. Information use.
  3. Communications from Hotels.com U.K site.
  4. Sharing of Information

If you have questions or concerns regarding this statement, you should contact Hotels.com customer service by email here.

Information Collected Online About You

Information you provide us.

Reservations

When you reserve a hotel room through Hotels.com U.K. site, we ask you to provide personally identifiable information ("PII") by filling out and submitting a "Guest information" form. This information includes your name, home or work telephone number, fax number, email address, and your credit card number, expiration date and billing address. We use this information to process your reservation and to confirm all the details and status by email.

When you return to the Hotels.com U.K. site, you can review your booking under the "View Reservations" link on the top right of the home page. You can access this information using your email address and the itinerary number (given to you at the moment of reservation confirmation).

You may request details of all information held about you by us at any time in writing (which may be subject to an administrative fee).

Information Use

Cookies

Hotels.com U.K. site uses cookies.

Definition of the cookies used by Hotels.com U.K site.: Cookies are small pieces of information sent by Hotels.com UK site into your computer. These cookies neither track nor store any personal identifiable information. The sole purpose of these cookies is to track users' navigation patterns within the Hotels.com UK site.
How long will the cookie be stored in your machine?
The cookie doesn't expire, however, can be manually removed by using the settings of your web-browser by following these steps (based on Internet Explorer): "Tools" ' "Internet Options" and under "Temporary Internet files" select "Delete Cookies".

Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but if you prefer, you can edit your browser options to block them in the future. If you reject the cookie, you may still use the Hotels.com UK site. The only drawback to this is that you may have limited access in some areas of the Hotels.com UK site, particularly in the booking process. This privacy statement covers the use of cookies by Hotels.com only and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.

A cookie file may also be placed on your hard drive by third-party advertising companies or by Hotels.com affiliate companies in the Expedia, Inc. (Nasdaq: EXPE) group of companies. We use third-party advertising companies to place ads when you visit the Hotels.com UK site. These companies may use aggregated statistics about your visits to this and other web sites in order to provide advertisements about travel-related goods and services that you may be interested in. The information they collect does not include your PII.

Log Files

Like most standard Website servers we use log files. This includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement in the aggregate, diagnose problems with our servers and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use.

IP addresses, etc. are not linked to PII. We use a tracking utility called ATG Dynamo that uses log files to analyze user movement.

Communications from the Site

Confirmation email

Right after successfully completing the reservation process, we will send you an email with the review of the hotel details and room type you have selected. This email will also contain your reservation number. You will receive this only once per reservation.

Newsletter

You can subscribe to our newsletter by inserting your email address in the "Sign-up for our e-mail newsletter" field when you make a reservation.

If you choose to receive our newsletter about our special deals in selected hotels in different cities around the world from time to time, we will give you the opportunity to play in sweepstakes and online competitions. The newsletter may also include details of some carefully selected offers of our partners.

Whenever you provide us with your email, Hotels.com U.K. will not distribute, sell nor share any of your details neither with third party companies nor with the IAC's group of companies of which Hotels.com is part of, for commercial purposes.

If you wish to subscribe to our newsletter, we ask for contact information such as full name and email address.

Subscription to this service is FREE and after subscribing, users can un-subscribe at any time. All Newsletters contain a field to "Unsubscribe" from.

Security

Hotels.com takes every precaution to protect our user's information. When users submit personal information via the Hotels.com U.K. site, their information is protected both online and off-line. How?
Hotels.com encrypts (process of converting messages or data into a form that cannot be read without decrypting or deciphering it) the data sent over the internet with SSL (Secure Socket Layer).
During the reservation process you will be asked to enter sensitive information (such as credit card number), that information is encrypted and is protected via SSL. While on a secure page, such as our order form, the lock icon on the bottom right of web browsers such as Netscape Navigator and Microsoft Internet Explorer becomes locked, meaning the transaction is secure.

While we use SSL encryption to protect sensitive information online, we also do everything in our power to protect user-information off-line. All of our user's information, not just the sensitive information mentioned above, is restricted in our offices. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job (for example, our billing clerk or a customer service representative) are granted access to PII. Furthermore, all employees are kept up-to-date on our security and privacy practices. Every time new policies are added, our employees are notified and/or reminded about the importance we place on privacy, and what they can do to ensure our user's information is protected. Finally, the servers that store PII are in a secure environment, in a locked facility.
If users have any questions about the security at our website, users can send an email to Customer Care.
