WE RESPECT YOUR PRIVACY

The Hostway|HOSTING group of companies (altogether “Hostway|HOSTING”) respects the privacy of our Clients and visitors and we are committed to protecting the personal information of our Clients and visitors. We do not collect personal information about you when you visit our website at http://www.hostway.com (“Website”) unless you choose to provide that information to us or as specifically outlined in this Privacy Policy (“Policy”). This Policy is incorporated into and subject to the Master Services Agreement (“MSA”) found online at http://www.hostway.com/legal/msa.html. All capitalized terms that are not defined herein will take on the same definition as in the MSA. In order to fully understand your rights we encourage you to read this Policy.

EU GENERAL DATA PROTECTION REGULATION (GDPR)

Hostway|HOSTING complies with the EU General Data Protection Regulation 2016/679 (“GDPR”) in the Processing of all Personal Data received from European Union (“EU”) member countries. For the purposes of this section of the Privacy Policy, the terms, “Processor”, “Processing” and “Personal Data” will have the same meaning as in the GDPR. The nature and purpose of the Processing will be solely as described in this Privacy Policy. The scope of the Processing will be limited to the Personal Data that you have provided to Hostway|HOSTING, such as when you sign up to receive our newsletter or a sales quote, or when you sign up under the MSA to become a Client. You determine the type and categories of Personal Data that is held by Hostway|HOSTING. Hostway|HOSTING does not collect any Personal Data without your knowledge. Your rights and obligations in relation to the Processing of Personal Data will be as set out in this Privacy Policy, the MSA, and GDPR.

To ensure our compliance with GDPR, Hostway|HOSTING engages a trusted independent third party provider to review our GDPR readiness and administer our data protection impact assessment, which is in addition to our standard SOC1, SOC2, and PCI-DSS audits. Notwithstanding anything in the MSA, if after you conduct a data protection impact assessment or consultation with a supervisory authority you determine that continuing with our Services would be in breach of GDPR, then you must notify Hostway|HOSTING immediately and we will work together with you to reach a mutually agreeable solution.

Data Protection Officer

If at any time you have any questions or concerns about Hostway|HOSTING’s GDPR compliance, please contact our Data Protection Officer directly at: privacy@hostway.com.

Supervisory Authority

Notwithstanding anything herein or in the MSA, if you have a good faith belief that the Processing of your Personal Data infringes GDPR, you have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in the EU member country of your place of residence or work, or in which the alleged infringement occurred. Hostway|HOSTING will provide reasonable cooperation with any supervisory authority consultation.

EU-U.S. PRIVACY SHIELD AND SWISS-U.S. PRIVACY SHIELD

In addition to GDPR, Hostway|HOSTING participates in and has certified our compliance with the EU-U.S. Privacy Shield Framework and the Swiss-U.S. Privacy Shield Framework. We are committed to subjecting all personal data received from EU member countries and Switzerland, respectively, in reliance on each Privacy Shield Framework, to the Framework’s applicable Principles. To learn more about the Privacy Shield Frameworks, and to view our certification, visit the U.S. Department of Commerce’s Privacy Shield List at https://www.privacyshield.gov.

Hostway|HOSTING is responsible for the processing of personal data we receive, under each Privacy Shield Framework, and subsequently transfers to a third party acting as an agent on our behalf. Hostway|HOSTING complies with the Privacy Shield Principles for all onward transfers of personal data from the EU and Switzerland, including the onward transfer liability provisions.

With respect to personal data received or transferred pursuant to the Privacy Shield Frameworks, Hostway|HOSTING is subject to the regulatory enforcement powers of the U.S. Federal Trade Commission. In certain situations, we may be required to disclose personal data in response to lawful requests by public authorities, including to meet national security or law enforcement requirements. In addition Hostway|HOSTING commits to cooperate with EU data protection authorities (DPAs) and the Swiss Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC) and comply with the advice given by such authorities with regard to human resources data transferred from the EU and Switzerland in the context of the employment relationship.

If you have an unresolved privacy or data use concern that we have not addressed satisfactorily, please contact our U.S.-based third party dispute resolution provider (free of charge) at https://www.jamsadr.com/file-an-eu-us-privacy-shield-claim.

Under certain conditions, more fully described on the Privacy Shield website at https://www.privacyshield.gov/article?id=How-to-Submit-a-Complaint, you may be entitled to invoke binding arbitration when other dispute resolution procedures have been exhausted.
