Privacy and Confidentiality

We respect the privacy of our customers and visitors and are committed to protecting your personal information. HostUpon gathers only the domain name (IP address logged as well), but not the e-mail address of visitors to our Web page.

We do NOT trade, barter or sell your personal information to any other organization or third party.

The information we gather is used for internal review and is then discarded, used to improve the content of our services, used to customize the content and/or layout of our page for each individual visitor and to benefit that user.

With respect to cookies: We use cookies to ensure that visitors are not repeatedly sent the same banner ads.

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending us e-mail at support@HostUpon.com

From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change at some time in the future we will contact you before we use your data for these new purposes to notify you of the policy change and to provide you with the ability to opt out of these new uses.

With respect to security: We always use industry-standard encryption technologies when transferring and receiving consumer data exchanged with our site. Our order forms are SSL protected to ensure sensitive customer data including payment information is sent over an encrypted connection.

If you feel that this site is not following any information in our privacy policy, you may contact us at: admin@HostUpon.com
