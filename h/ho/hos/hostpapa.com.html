Privacy Policy

Statement of Privacy Policy

We at HostPapa.com ("HostPapa") are committed to providing both clients and end-users with the utmost in privacy and security. We abide by all industry guidelines and consistently monitor our management and operating methods in order to maintain our valued reputation and integrity. This is our promise to our site visitors.

Type of Information Collected

HostPapa is a site offering web hosting services. Information is collected from site visitors in two ways: when they voluntarily sign up for a web hosting account and when they contact our support staff using the feedback/contact form whereby they submit their name and e-mail address.

Use of Collected Information

Information is collected by HostPapa and is the sole property of HostPapa.

Customer Contact

If a site visitor opts to sign up for a web hosting account or contact us via the feedback/contact form, we have permission to contact the applicant via electronic or postal mail or telephone regarding information that relates directly to the information they have provided.

Cookies

Cookies are only used to keep track of log-in names and passwords. A user's e-mail address is automatically logged in when they revisit the site and they do not have to reenter profile information. This is to keep track of preferences and to better tailor the site to the visitor's needs while providing the most effective service to our clients. Information such as IP address internal session ID, user's domain, type of Internet browser, domain of Web site are collected and used only for traffic auditing and analyzing trends.

Security

In order to ensure security and protection of collected information, all employees are aware of our security policy and practices. Web security is constantly reviewed. Access to the information is limited and only obtained through codenames and passwords. Any loss, misuse, or alteration of information is prohibited followed by appropriate action and termination of employee. This is to uphold our promise to our clients, affiliate members, and property users.

Delete/Deactivate

"Deactivating" refers to the process by which Personally Identifiable Information, upon request, is removed. Because of backups and records of deletions, it may be impossible to delete a consumer's entry without some residual information. An individual who requests to have Personally Identifiable Information deactivated will be functionally deleted. We will not sell, transfer, or use the information relating to a deleted individual in any way. To deactivate your account, please e-mail info@hostpapasupport.com.

Personal information collected through HostPapa may be corrected or updated through various methods. All information must be updated by contacting HostPapa staff either through e-mail or postal mail.
