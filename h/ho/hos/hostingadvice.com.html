Privacy Policy

At HostingAdvice.com, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us here.

Log Files

Like many other Web sites, HostingAdvice.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons

HostingAdvice.com does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.

HostingAdvice.com has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.

Right to Opt Out

HostingAdvice.com gives users the opportunity to opt-out of having their information used for purposes not directly related to the site at the point where we ask for the information, including the sharing of personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes. To opt-out of communications from our third party business partners, please contact them directly.
