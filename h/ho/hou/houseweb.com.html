 HouseWeb Privacy Statement

HouseWeb has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for HouseWeb

HouseWeb is registered under the UK Data Protection Act 1998, number Z4670077.

HouseWeb is the trading name of HouseWeb Ltd

Information Automatically Logged
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site. Your IP address may also be used to help identify you and your details on our database.

Cookies
Like many other sites, our site uses cookies to keep track of your visit to HouseWeb. You can disable this function from your web browser if desired.

Advertisers
We use an external advertising company to display advertisements on our site. These advertisements may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our web site, cookies received with banner advertisements may be collected by our advertising company, and we do not have access to this information.

Registration Forms
Our site's main registration form requires users to give us contact information (such as name, email, postal address and telephone number), demographic information (such as age), and any relevant property-related questions. This form is optional and helps us to profile our visitors.

Contact information from the registration form is used to supply information about our company (if you choose) and to send promotional material from some carefully selected companies to our users (if you choose). Users may opt-out of receiving mailings; see the opt-out section below.

Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site. This information is shared with advertisers on an aggregate basis.

Users of our Service Centre are asked for additional information that is passed on to our Business Partners so that they can quote for services that you require. This information is only sent to a maximum of three companies in each service selected. We may also contact you by e-mail or phone for feedback on the companies you choose (which helps us to maintain our quality standards).

European Economic Area (EEA)
Because of the nature of the Internet, data that is provided by you may pass through any location - including countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA) and by using the web site you consent to the transfer of the data. If you are outside the EEA, you agree to use the data sent to you only for your own purposes and in accordance with the English data protection laws (including the Data Protection Act 1998).

External Links
This site contains links to other sites. HouseWeb is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
