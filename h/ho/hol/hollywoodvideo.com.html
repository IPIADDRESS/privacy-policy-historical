Personally Identifiable Information
What Personally Identifiable Information Is:
Personally identifiable information is your name, address, e-mail address, phone number and/or credit card information.

Where We Collect Personally Identifiable Information
Hollywoodvideo.com collects personally identifiable information when you (1) subscribe to our newsletter, (2) enter sweepstakes, trivia or other contests, (3) buy gift cards, (4) register for online Member Services, (5) use our Store Locator, and (6) submit feedback, questions or "contact us" messages by email.

For more information about where we collect personally identifiable information, please click here.

How We Use Personally Identifiable Information:
Hollywoodvideo.com uses your personally identifiable information to personalize your experience on our Web site and to contact you.

Hollywoodvideo.com also compiles aggregate data from all of our customers to prepare internal reports about which web pages are popular, how many total unique users visit our site, and different traffic patterns on the site. Hollywoodvideo.com does not combine your personally identifiable information with information about web pages visited, purchases made, or any other clickstream data.

For more information about how we use personally identifiable information, please click here.

Who We Disclose Personally Identifiable Information To:
Hollywoodvideo.com will not sell, rent, trade or disclose your personally identifiable information to anyone except as explained in this policy. Hollywoodvideo.com may disclose some personal information to outside companies who help Hollywoodvideo.com with the administration and delivery of newsletters, promotional games, trivia contests and sweepstakes. For example, a company who administers a sweepstakes for us may use your e-mail address to update you on your status in the sweepstakes. Or, our newsletters may be distributed by another company that will use your e-mail or shipping address for delivery. We do not allow these outside companies to use this information for any purpose other than ones we specifically authorize.

The Hollywoodvideo.com Web site contains links to other Web sites. Although we do not provide any personally identifiable information to these companies, we cannot guarantee your privacy once you leave the Hollywoodvideo.com Web site. Hollywoodvideo.com is not responsible for the privacy policies, practices or content of Web sites with links on our site.

Hollywoodvideo.com may also disclose your personal information to law enforcement personnel, government officials, or others when we, in our sole discretion, believe that disclosure is necessary to prevent violations of the law, injury to person or property, or any harm to Hollywood Entertainment Corporation or others.

For more information about the disclosure of personally identifiable information to select companies, please click here.

Cookies
What Is A Cookie?
A cookie is a small piece of data that is sent to your browser from a Web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. Cookies store information about your use of a particular Web site or service and are typically used to make services more convenient and personalized. Most cookies are "session cookies," which are automatically deleted whenever you leave a Web site, although some cookies remain on your computer until you remove them.

How We Use Cookies
We use cookies (1) to measure traffic patterns to areas of our site and to features mentioned in our newsletter and (2) to allow us to provide personalized information in our online Member Services area. It is possible that some parts of the Hollywoodvideo.com site may not function properly if your browser is set to refuse our cookies.

For more information about cookies, how we use them and how to access your browser's cookie settings, please click here.

Children
Our Policy For Guests Under Age 18
Hollywoodvideo.com is committed to protecting the privacy of children. Each visitor confirms that he or she is at least 18 years of age upon registration for our newsletter. Visitors under age 18 should not submit any personally identifiable information to Hollywoodvideo.com.

Hollywood Entertainment Corporation believes that parents should supervise their children's online activites and consider using parental control tools available from online services and software manufacturers that may help provide a child-friendly online environment. These tools may also prevent children from disclosing online their name, address, and other personal information without parental permission.

Most browsers permit you to set the browser to accept, reject, or notify you of cookie use. However, some parts of the Hollywoodvideo.com Web site may not function properly if your browser is set to reject cookies.
