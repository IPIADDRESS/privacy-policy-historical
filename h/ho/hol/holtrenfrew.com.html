    Privacy Policy

    Welcome

    This Privacy Notice explains how Holt, Renfrew & Co., Limited and its related companies and affiliates - Holt Renfrew Holdings Limited and Selfridges Group Limited (“Holt Renfrew”) - collect, use, disclose and otherwise process Personal Information. (defined below)

    This Privacy Notice describes the Personal Information we may collect from you, the reason we collect it, how we use it and with whom we may share it.

    If you would like to know what choices you can make about your Personal Information, jump to Your Choices.


    Updates to This Privacy Notice

    We may make changes to this Privacy Notice, and may apply any changes to information previously collected, as permitted by law. When we make material changes to this Privacy Notice, we will notify you by posting the updated Privacy Notice on our website, and we will update the effective date at the end of this Privacy Notice.


    When does this Privacy Notice apply?

    This Privacy Notice applies to the Personal Information we collect and use when you:

    • visit, shop and interact with our websites www.holtrenfrew.com, www.ogilvycanada.com and any other site where this Privacy Notice is posted
    • visit us in person at one of our retail locations or cafes
    • make a purchase directly from us, or from one of our on-premises luxury brand partners
    • attend one of our events or participate in a promotion and/or contest, and
    • communicate with us by telephone, email, social media or other means

    Many of your favourite luxury brands are sold by independent retailers operating within Holt Renfrew stores. This Privacy Notice does not apply to the privacy practices of these independent retailers as they have their own privacy policies that govern their use of your information. Holt Renfrew may also have access to the information you share with these retailers as they utilize Holt Renfrew’s point of sale system. This Privacy Notice will apply to our use of that information.


    COLLECTION AND USE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

    Personal Information is information about an identifiable individual. It does not include your business contact information, or anonymous or aggregate information which does not identify you.

    For the most part, Holt Renfrew collects information about you when you give it directly to us. For example, when you set up a Holt Renfrew profile, make a purchase, participate in an event, promotion or contest or reach out to us. Like many companies, we automatically collect technical data when you interact with our websites and other technologies. You can learn more how we collect and use Personal Information in the following sections.

    • HOW DO WE COLLECT PERSONAL INFORMATION?
      • Holt Renfrew collects Personal Information in the following ways:
        • When you use our websites: when you make an online purchase, create a profile, browse our website and interact with our online features (such as chat, messaging, commenting or rating)
        • When you provide it to us directly: when you make a purchase participate in our personal shopping services, use our services, interact with Holt Renfrew employees, participate in a promotion or event, enter a contest, submit your resume and job application
        • When you communicate with us: in person, by mail, over the telephone through our websites or in any other direct manner, including social media;
        • When you visit our stores: through video surveillance used in and around our locations, use of our wifi services;
        • From the Holt Renfrew family of brands and companies: we may obtain or receive certain types of customer information from our affiliates and related companies; and
        • From third parties: we may receive information from other sources, for example from our luxury brand and promotional partners.
      • We also use certain technologies to collect Personal Information and technical data from you. See Use of Cookies, Targeted Advertising and other Technologies to learn more.
    • WHAT TYPE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT AND HOW DO WE USE IT?
      • Holt Renfrew collects Personal Information in the following ways:
      • Profile information: If you create a profile with Holt Renfrew, we may collect your name, address and other contact details. We use this information to register you and maintain your profile.
      • Demographic information: If you use our personal shopping services, we may collect additional information such as your measurements, clothing and shoe sizes, and style preferences in order to tailor your shopping experience and provide you with recommendations. We may collect other demographic information such as gender, age ranges and location. We may also collect information about your fashion likes and dislikes. We use this information to help us better understand your needs.
      • Purchase information: When you make an online purchase, we will collect your billing information, such as credit card or other payment number, address and contact information. In store, at check out, you may be asked to provide certain contact information (such as your name and e-mail address). We may collect information about your purchase habits and preferences; we use this information to better understand your preferences and needs, and to provide you exclusive offers and access to programs and to facilitate your interactions with our stylists and sales associates, and to enrich your experience in our stores, and on our website. We also use this information to establish and maintain a record of your purchase history, to create customer accounts and process returns.
      • Location information: We may collect geolocation data. We do this through our collection of information such as IP address or postal codes. We also use various technologies to obtain location information; for instance, when you use our complementary wifi services, your device provides location information to us. We use this information to better understand our customer demographic and analyze trends and traffic flow in and outside our retail locations.
      • Video and audio information: We collect images as well as video recordings of individuals who visit our retail locations or make inquiries. We use this information to deter theft and to enhance the safety and security of our premises. We may also record telephone calls made to or from our call centres, and we may use this information for quality and training purposes.
      • Employment information: We collect information contained in your CV and career applications. We use this information to review and evaluate applications.
      • We collect and use the above information to allow Holt Renfrew to provide, manage and improve our products and services and to understand your needs. We also use this information to inform you about products, services, special offers, programs, contests, promotions or events that Holt Renfrew reasonably believes may be of interest to you.
      • Lastly, we use this information to respond to your inquiries, requests and / or complaints.
    • HOW DOES HOLT RENFREW USE COOKIES, TAILORED ADVERTISING AND OTHER TECHNOLOGIES?
      • Holt Renfrew uses a variety of technologies.
      • IP Addresses
      • We may collect your IP address when you visit our websites to help us diagnose problems, for system administration, to report aggregated data to our partners, and to audit the use of our website. We do not normally link IP addresses to anything personally identifiable.
      • Cookies and other technologies
      • Like many websites, we use “cookies” and other similar tracking technologies (such as pixel tags, scripts, etags and web beacons) on our websites. A cookie is a small text file installed on your computer or other device that enables the website to recognize you. It consists of a unique number and can distinguish you from other visitors. It does not contain any personal details. This means that it is very difficult to identify you personally on the basis of such cookies, or identify you when you visit third party websites.
      • We use two types of cookies on our website - persistent and session cookies. Persistent cookies remain on your device after you leave our website while session cookies are temporary and are deleted from your device after you close your browser. Depending on the type, the cookies may be necessary for the website to function, or improve performance or assist us in recognizing you, such as when you log into your Holt Renfrew account. We collect this data to help us analyze our website flow, performance, content structure, better reflect visitor preferences, prepopulate information so that you do not have to enter it over and over again, track usage and tailor advertisements to you.
      • Our third party service management providers use third party cookies or other digital markers to help us maintain and improve our websites and deliver interest-based advertising.
      • A Flash cookie is a data file placed on a device via the Adobe Flash plug-in that may be built into, or downloaded by you, onto your device. We use Flash cookies to enable Flash features and remembering your preferences.
      • Click streaming / Click tracking
      • These technologies enable us to count visitors to our websites, monitor how users navigate our websites, count how many of our emails were actually opened, or count how many particular links contained within an email were actually viewed.
      • Google Analytics
      • We use Google Analytics, a web analysis service of Google Inc. Google Analytics uses cookies to analyze your use of the websites, to create reports about visitor activities for us and to provide further services associated with the use of the websites and the internet. Although this information is collected through your Google ad settings, it is not provided to us in a personally-identifiable format.
      • See Your Choices to learn what options you have regarding cookies and other technologies.
      • Tailored Advertising
      • Tailored or Interest-based advertising means programs that display advertisements to you based on information from your online activities, such as the actions you take when you visit websites. This information helps understand your interests in order to display ads that you might enjoy and not waste your time with ads for things you are not interested in. We use third party technologies, including third party social media platforms to show you tailored advertising.
      • We use advertising platforms provided by Facebook and others to promote interest-based advertisements to you and to measure how users interact with those advertisements. We may do this using web tracking tools or by converting the email address, phone number or other contact information you have provided to us into a unique value that these platforms may compare to a unique value created from information you provided to those platforms. Also, some of this data will be used to help identify other individuals with similar demographics who may also enjoy seeing our ads. The data remains anonymous. Please visit Facebook for information on Facebook’s advertising privacy policies and how to opt out https://www.facebook.com/about/privacy/. If you would like to opt out of our tailored advertising, please adjust your social advertising settings, use the opt-out features in the advertisement.
      • See Your Choices for how you can opt-out of tailored advertising.
    • HOW DOES HOLT RENFREW USE SOCIAL MEDIA?
      • We may collect information through social media platforms, such as Facebook, Instagram and Twitter when you post information that mentions Holt Renfrew, our products, services, events or promotions. We only collect this information if your privacy settings permit us to see your post. Please note that interactions on social media platforms are governed by the privacy policies of that platform.
    DISCLOSURE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION WITH THIRD PARTIES; CHILDREN

    Your information may be shared or disclosed with affiliated and non-affiliated third parties to assist Holt Renfrew in providing its products, services and for other purposes identified in this Privacy Notice. Your information may also be disclosed to a government or regulatory agency as required by law. Holt Renfrew does not knowingly collect information of children 13 or under.

    You can learn more about how we collect and use Personal Information in the following sections.

    • DOES HOLT RENFREW SHARE OR DISCLOSE MY PERSONAL INFORMATION?
      • In order for us to provide you with our products and services, we require help from third parties. Holt Renfrew may use and share your Personal Information with affiliated and non-affiliated third parties (partners, contractors, service providers, agencies and other organizations) that help us:
        • Process payments and verify credit card details
        • Provide customer service
        • Store and secure data
        • Assist with promotional, advertising and marketing initiatives
      • Holt Renfrew may share some Personal Information we collect with our affiliates and related companies, selected service providers, vendors, lease partners and other organizations outside of Holt Renfrew that help Holt Renfrew provide its products and services.
      • We may share your Personal Information where necessary to protect our interests or in the event of a sale or transfer of all or part of Holt Renfrew. For example, disclosure may be necessary:
        • in order to detect or prevent fraud
        • in connection with a merger with another entity
        • to insure or securitize our assets, or
        • to engage in another form of corporate or financing transaction
      • We may share Personal Information with government, regulatory and law enforcement agencies as requested by these agencies, if the disclosure is required or permitted by law. We may share information to protect Holt Renfrew or its employees or customers, to take action regarding illegal activities or violations of terms of use, or as otherwise required or permitted by law.
    • DOES HOLT RENFREW AGGREGATE AND ANONYMIZE DATA?
      • Holt Renfrew may use Personal Information to prepare aggregated user statistics or information summaries to describe our services and their popularity to our suppliers and prospective advertisers and for other lawful purposes. Holt Renfrew aggregates Personal Information in a way that is highly unlike any individual could be identified.
    • WHAT ABOUT THE PERSONAL INFORMATION OF CHILDREN?
      • Holt Renfrew does not knowingly collect Personal Information from a child under the age of 13 without the consent of the child’s parent or legal guardian. Children under the age of 13 are not eligible to participate in our contests or other promotions. In addition, Holt Renfrew will not knowingly send marketing or other promotional materials to children under the age of 13.
    SECURITY AND STORAGE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

    The security of your Personal Information is important to us. We use reasonably appropriate security measures to protect your Personal Information against unauthorized use, access, modification, destruction or disclosure. We require our third party service providers to use similar safeguards to those we use.

    You can learn more about how we collect and use Personal Information in the following sections.

    • HOW DOES HOLT RENFREW KEEP PERSONAL INFORMATION SAFE?
      • We employ reasonable security measures to protect Personal Information against loss, unauthorized use, access, alteration, modification, disclosure or destruction. These measures include physical, organizational and technological measures.
      • If we use third party service providers, we require such service providers to use similar safeguards to those we use. We also require service providers to limit access to your Personal Information and to keep it strictly confidential. Service providers are only permitted to use your Personal Information for the purpose for which it was collected.
    • WHERE IS INFORMATION STORED?
      • Your Personal Information is stored and/or processed or otherwise used by or on behalf of Holt Renfrew in Canada. Unless there is a legal or regulatory requirement to keep such information in Canada, Personal Information may also be stored, accessed, or used outside of Canada. For example, for our business purposes, we have engaged service providers located in the U.S., and the U.K. and Personal Information may be stored, accessed or used in these jurisdictions or other countries. Personal Information stored in other jurisdictions may be subject to the laws of those jurisdictions, and may not be as comprehensive as the laws in Canada.
    • HOW LONG DOES HOLT RENFREW KEEP MY INFORMATION?
      • We retain your Personal Information only as long as necessary, for relevant for the purposes identified in this Privacy Notice, as required by law, and/or in compliance with our record retention policy.
