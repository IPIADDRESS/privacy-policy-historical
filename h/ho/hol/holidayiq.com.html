This website may also provide us with certain non-personally identifiable information about your access to the website. Such information includes the IP address through which this site is accessed, the time at which this site is accessed and the specific portions of the website accessed by you. This information is not personally identifiable.

HIQ uses this information as a feedback for improvement of the website, and for an analysis of the most frequently accessed portions of the website. This information will not at any stage be passed on to any person.

Usage of cookies

The website uses cookies to improve your interactive sessions and to save time during access. When you return to the website, the information previously provided by you may be retrieved by your computer to enable certain features of the site without having to re-sign in, and allow you easier access to parts of the website customised by you. The cookie would allow your user name to be pre-entered so that only your password would need to be reentered by you at the time of sign in. The cookie would be stored on the hard disk of your computer and would not be available for access by us.

The acceptance of cookies is dependent on the browser settings on the web-browser used by you.
