Hands On is committed to the privacy of our clients and Deaf or Hard of Hearing consumers. Information submitted on our website is done so voluntarily. This information may include: name, address, phone number, and email address. This information is not required to access any part of our site and will only be used for its intended purpose.

Hands On does not sell, rent, or transmit any information submitted on this site to any other organization. Client and consumer information is not used in the creation of mailing lists and it is not stored in any databases accessible from the Internet.

Our web server does collect domain names and IP addresses of visitors to our site. This allows Hands On to track number of visits, average time spent on our site, and pages frequently viewed. If errors occur, they are also reported. This information will be reviewed so that Hands On may improve content and user friendliness of this web site.

As a courtesy, Hands On links to several sites that may be of interest to our Deaf and Hard of Hearing consumers. We can not guarantee that the submission of information on those sites will adhere to our Privacy Statement. Please check each site for their own privacy statement.

At Hands On, we believe the privacy of our clients and consumers is invaluable as evidenced by this Privacy Statement. If you have any questions or comments regarding this statement, please direct them to robray@handsonsvs.com.
