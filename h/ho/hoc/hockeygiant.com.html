Privacy Policy:

HockeyGiant.com will not sell, share or rent any customer information collected on our web site.

The information collected during the order process is for billing and shipping purposes only. We will use the information to process and order, contact a customer with order problems and ship an order to a customer. All financial information is stored securely. When entering sensitive information (ie. credit card, address, etc.) the browser will be "locked" or secure.

Our email list is voluntary. We do not add anybody to our email list that has not chosen to do so online. The information collected in the email list registration process is for demographic reporting only (ie. state). We will not sell or share this list.

Third-Party Web Beacons: We use third-party web beacons from Yahoo! to help analyze where visitors go and what they do while visiting our website. Yahoo! may also use anonymous information about your visits to this and other websites in order to improve its products and services and provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by Yahoo!, click here.
