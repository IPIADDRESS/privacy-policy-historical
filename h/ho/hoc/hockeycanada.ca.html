  • Gift certificates
  • Pre-sale merchandise (unless otherwise stipulated)
  • Items that have been laundered or altered in any way

The Online Store at hockeycanada.ca does its best to ensure that your order arrives on time; however, delivery dates are not guaranteed and your order may arrive after the estimated arrival date for various reasons, such as:

  • Delivery is to a PO Box or APO/FPO address.
  • Delivery is outside the 48 contiguous United States
  • Delivery is outside major city
  • Delays due to orders being placed on a Canadian statutory holiday
  • Delays due to problems with credit card authorization and verification
  • Delays due to weather
  • Unforeseen delays by one of our carriers
  • Delays to the unavailability of product(s)
  • Delays due to customs.

Multiple Shipments
