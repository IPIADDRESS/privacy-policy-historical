Do I have to provide personal information?
You can visit HOB.com or HOB.com Properties as a guest; remaining anonymous by not providing any personal information to us. Without this information, however, you will not be able to fully access all of the features and functionality of the site. We do collect some limited information about your visit but none of this information is personally identifiable to you. This information includes: your Internet location (IP address), your Internet entry point (ISP domain name), the software you use to access this site and its configuration.
 

How is my personal information used?

aggregated, personally non identifiable information
Aggregated, personally non-identifiable, information collected by HOB.com and HOB.com Properties is used to:

· diagnose problems with our computer systems
· identify and analyze trends
· track user movement (anonymously) through our websites
· better administer our websites
· gather broad demographic information

Demographic data are shared with advertisers, always on an aggregate basis. These data are not linked to personally identifiable information.

cookies
HOB.com and HOB.com Properties uses cookies. A cookie is small amount of data, often containing an anonymous unique identifier, sent from our computers through your Internet browser and stored on your computer's hard drive. We set cookies on your computer for a number of purposes, including to:

· keep track of your shopping cart (if applicable)
· make sure you do not see the same ad repeatedly
· deliver content specific to your interests
· save your password so you do not have to re-enter it each time you visit our site third party advertisers

We use an outside ad company to display ads on our site. These ads may also contain cookies. Cookies received with banner ads are collected by our ad company. We do not have access to this information.

contest entrants
HOB.com and HOB Properties regularly run contests and giveaways sponsored, in part, by partner music labels. Participation in these contests is completely voluntary; you have the choice whether or not to disclose the information required for registration. Information requested may include: contact information (such as name and shipping address), and demographic information (such as zip code, age level). Contact information is used to notify winners and award prizes. Additionally, we sometimes share registration information with sponsoring music labels. HOB.com and HOB.com Properties are not responsible for the use of such information by these or other parties.

HOB.com Company Store orders and fulfillment
We use an outside shipping company to ship orders, and a credit card processing company to bill users for goods and services. These companies do not retain, share, store or use personally identifiable information for any secondary purposes. third party linksThis site contains links to other sites. HOB.com and other HOB.com Properties are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites.

personally identifiable information
Our site uses various forms for gathering information. Registration, order, survey, and contest forms all, require some personal information. These forms may require you to provide: contact information (e.g., name, email address), demographic information (e.g., zip code, age, income level), and other applicable information (e.g., credit card information). This information is used to: send prizes to contest winners, send merchandise for online store purchases, to contact you when necessary, and to send information about our company or promotional material from our partners. You may opt-out of receiving future mailings by following the instructions outlined in "Correct/Update & Choice/Opt-out" section below.
 

How is my personal information used?

security of data during e-commerce transactions
HOB.com and HOB Properties use advanced security methods (Secure Socket Layer (SSL) 128-bit encryption) on all pages transmitting private financial information, such as credit card numbers.

physical security of equipment storing personally identifiable information
Access to computer equipment (servers) storing personally identifiable information is restricted to key authorized personnel. These personnel have access to this equipment only for administrative purposes.

employee practices regarding security of personally identifiable information
Authorized personnel access users' information records for the sole purpose of system maintenance.
 

What if I'm a kid?

for kids
·  If you are under 18 years of age, you must let your parent or guardian know about this privacy policy and get their permission before providing us any personal information.

·  You must get your parent or guardian's permission before using any message board or chat feature on this site.

·  DO NOT post your real name on this or any website. Never tell anyone online anything private about yourself.

·  If you win a contest that has a prize your parent or guardian must mail us a signed note to the address listed in the contest rules which 1: grants you permission to enter, and 2: tells us they will accept the prize for you.

·  You will be told to ask a parent before you pay for anything on HOB.com. ·  Some areas of this website may have special rules for children under the age of 18. When you are in those areas, you must follow those rules.

for parents
·  Parents or guardians are encouraged to spend time online with their children to become familiar with the content available on HOB.com, HOB.com Properties, and the Internet in general. Parents should regularly oversee their children's usage of email, other communications, and online transactions. Different software manufacturers can provide tools that help provide a safe online environment for kids.

·  If your child has provided us with personally identifiable information, or if your child has used your credit card without your permission, you should contact us immediately by sending an email to support@HOB.com. We will make all reasonable efforts to delete your child's information from our records.
