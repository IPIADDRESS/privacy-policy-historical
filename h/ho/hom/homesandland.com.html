Homes & Land Privacy Policy

You can browse HomesAndLand.com without revealing personal information about yourself. Like nearly all web sites, we monitor and log the incoming "IP Addresses" of visitors. This anonymous information is collected to assist us in analyzing site use patterns and to help us maintain a secure environment for our site's data. This information is not shared with any other party.

Certain features of our web site request that you enter personal information, including your name, address, email address, phone number, etc. These features include, but are not limited to requests for a free magazine, emails sent to advertisers, and emails sent to Homes & Land Publishers. You are under no obligation at any time to provide us with personal information, although some features may function incompletely or improperly without it. Any feature that requests personal information from you will include a link to this Privacy Policy.

HomesAndLand.com is a venue designed to promote the magazines of Homes & Land Affiliates, LLC and to assist consumers in contacting its advertisers. Therefore, some of the information you send us is shared with our Publishers and their advertisers. Neither Homes & Land Publishers nor their advertisers are governed by this privacy policy.

Some Advertisers on HomesAndLand.com provide links to other web sites not controlled by HomesAndLand.com. These other sites may send their own cookies to users, collect data, or solicit personal information.

HomesAndLand.com may share aggregate information with advertisers, business partners, sponsors, and other third parties. For example, we may disclose what searches visitors to HomesAndLand.com perform most often.

By using the HomesAndLand.com web site and providing your personal information, you consent to the collection and use of your information as we have outlined in this Privacy Policy. Homes & Land Affiliates, LLC may decide to change this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of the information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.

Feel free to contact us any time and we'll answer any additional questions or concerns you may have. Our email address is privacy@homesandland.com.

Homes & Land Real Estate Services
Homes & Land provides real estate services to home buyers, home sellers, as well as real estate agents and brokers. Home buyers can find homes for sale, land for sale and other real estate property listings at HomesAndLand.com. Home buyers can also find new homes for sale, search for new home communities or locate a new home builder. Homes & Land offers home buyers alternative real estate searches by real estate map, real estate magazine, or by MLS listing number. Home buyers searching for acreage and lots can also find land for sale. Our real estate listings include property details such as photos, virtual tours, bedrooms, baths, location and detailed descriptions. You can find all types of home listings, including houses, condos, land, acreage, vacation homes, apartments for rent and new homes. Home sellers can get real estate tips, find a real estate agent or broker, get a free home valuation estimate, get information on local real estate market conditions, and sell their home using HomesAndland.com. With powerful real estate selling tools at their disposal, home sellers should contact a real estate agent or broker in their local market to sell a home. Real estate agents and Brokers can leverage the affluent home buyer and home seller audience of HomesAndLand.com to advertise listings, win more listings and find high-end, qualified home buyers and sellers. Real Estate Professionals can leverage powerful, personal real estate websites, real estate magazines, and real estate virtual tours.
