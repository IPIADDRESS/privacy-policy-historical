Privacy Policy of www.homeschoolmath.net

The main activities of Homeschool Math website (www.homeschoolmath.net) are:

  • To offer free resources for mathematics teaching
  • To sell math ebooks for elementary grades

You can access and browse Homeschool Math website without disclosing any of your personal information (such as name, address, telephone number, email address, or credit card information) nor do we collect information about our visitors from any public records.

If you voluntarily submit any information to us (especially email address), we may use that to contact you, or to answer your questions. Homeschool Math will not trade, sell or share your personal information that you voluntariy submit to us for use by any third party.

Homeschool Math does not enable our visitors to communicate with other visitors or to post information to be accessed by others.

Statistics info:

The hosting company that www.homeschoolmath.net is hosted on automatically collects website statistical information . This information is not personal but aggregate, and includes such things as pages visited, browser type, operating system, visit length, etc. We may also use third party statistics programs to gather statistical information.

Cookies: The worksheet pages that you create with our worksheet generators do use cookies to generate a running number for the worksheets. We do not use cookies to store personal data.

Advertisers: You will see advertisements served by Amazon.com and Google.com on this website. They may use cookie technology to track the activities of anyone who clicks on the links within the advertisements. To see their privacy policies, click here Amazon.com Privacy Notice or Google Privacy Policy.
