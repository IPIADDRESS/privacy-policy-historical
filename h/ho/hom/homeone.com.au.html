Communication Disclaimer

It is Homeone Marketing Australia's policy to respect the privacy of its users.

This privacy statement discloses the privacy practices for Homeone Marketing Australia @ www.homeone.com.au to demonstrate its commitment in respecting your privacy by detailing our data gathering, storage and dissemination practices of your personally identifiable information.

Examples of personally identifiable information include your name, address, phone number, fax number, email address, financial profile, social security number, credit card information and IP address.

Information Collection and Use
Homeone is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement. Homeone collects information from our users at several different points on our website.

Online Enquiry Forms
In order to submit an enquiry form, a user is required to give their contact information. In this case, name and email address. This information is used to contact the sender about the services on our site for which they have expressed interest.

...and no, this does not mean you will receive a string of endless spam mail from Homeone Marketing Australia, our partners or other third parties we deal with. Phew!

Opt-Out or Opt-In
It is our anti-spamming policy that as much as we'd love to send you helpful information to assist with your home improvement needs, we simply do not unless you tell us to, which is why ALL opt-in check boxes remain conveniently unchecked.

"OK, I like your newsletters but I would like to opt-out for a while"

You are able to opt-out of receiving communications from the site by checking the unsubscribe option of the Newsletter page along with your email confirmation.

Newsletter
At some points on our site we provide an opt-in option that you can decidedly check if you'd like to request periodic issues of the insightful Homeone Newsletter. If a user wishes to subscribe to our newsletter, we ask for contact information such as name and email address.

As simple as subscribing, a user has multiple methods of unsubscribing at any time either by the unsubscribe links detailed in the Homeone Newsletter, or by simply typing in your email followed by checking the unsubscribe option of the Newsletter page. Easy!

Communication
"I find spam mail irritating. When and how often shall I expect communication from you?"

The circumstances under which the users can expect email from us are as follows:

Solicited email response. We like to think that we are a responsible business and therefore promptly respond to enquiries, comments, and any other feedback.

Newsletters. By checking the opt-in box on our contact page or typing your email address and subscribing in the newsletter window clearly interprets your consent to receive newsletters. Newsletters are issued once every two months.

Message board response. You will be posted an email indicating when someone has responded to your message board posting. Upon use of our message board, you are granted the choice to opt-in for this reply service by checking the opt-in box.

If you believe that you are a recipient of any other information not specified above which may or may not be interpreted as spam mail, please email security@homeone.com.au immediately.

Log Files
We use IP addresses to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses are NOT linked to personally identifiable information.

Sharing
"Will my neighbours learn of my eating habits if I use this site?"

We WILL share aggregated demographic information with our clients, content providers, partners and advertisers. This information is NOT linked to any personal data that can identify you as an individual person, nor do we distribute or sell personally identifiable information to third parties the likes of direct marketers.

Links
Naturally, this web site contains links to other sites. Homeone Australia is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to read [if available] the privacy statements of each web site that collects personally identifiable information as this privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this, and, co-branded web sites.

Use of Chat Rooms, Forums, and Message Boards
"And if I don't mind the public at large knowing what I eat?!"

When disclosing personally identifiable information online through Chat Rooms, Forums, and Message Boards the information is considered submitted public information. So, when posting public information you are at risk of receiving unsolicited email and therefore we urge you to be extra careful and to use discretion when disclosing information online.

Furthermore, when using public forums, ensure that you keep your address private to avoid the potential of receiving nuisance` spam mail.
