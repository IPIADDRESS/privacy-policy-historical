PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy
At New Century, maintaining our customers' trust and confidence is a high priority. That's why we welcome this opportunity to describe our privacy policy and the steps we take to protect your personal information. In this notice, you will find details about these policies, as well as an opportunity to choose how your personal information will be shared. This Privacy Policy is applicable to New Century Financial Corporation and the New Century family of companies, which includes New Century Mortgage Corporation, NC Capital Corporation, New Century Mortgage Securities, Inc., NC Insurance Services, Inc., Worth Funding Incorporated, NC Commercial Corporation, Home 123 Corporation, and North American Real Estate Solutions.

Security
Security of our customers' personal information is very important to New Century. New Century Mortgage employs extensive security measures to thoroughly protect your transactions with us. We restrict access to your non-public personal information to our employees who have a need to know that information in order to provide products and services to you. We also maintain physical and procedural safeguards to guard your non-public personal information.

Information Sources
In order to provide mortgage loans and other financial products to you, we collect information from a variety of sources, such as:

  • Information you provide to us on applications or other forms, such as name, address, social security number, income, assets, etc.
  • Information we receive from an outside company, such as a credit bureau or employment verification
  • Information about your transactions or experience with us, such as property value appraisals, your payment history with us, etc.

Information We Collect While You Browse Our Website
We do not access information about your identity while you browse our website. You remain anonymous to us. While you view our site, our system tracks the pages you visit, the pages you download, the site you came from, and notes your IP address (but not your identity). We may also place a 'cookie' on your computer that tracks your use of our website. A cookie is a piece of data that is stored on your hard drive. It takes up very little room on your system and helps us estimate the number of persons that use our site and determine the areas of our site that are the most popular. The cookie does not provide us with specific, personally-identifying information about you, such as your name, address, tax identification number, social security number, email address, or phone number. You can remain anonymous while you are browsing our website, but once you complete an application for a loan, we will use the information stated therein to determine if you are eligible for the loan you requested. We may retain all of the information you provide to us in that application process, including personally-identifying information such as your name, income, assets, liabilities, age, social security number, and employment.

Who We Disclose the Information To
We may disclose the information we collect, as stated above, to:

  • Financial service providers, such as banks and investment companies, 3rd party loan servicers, or consumer credit companies.
  • Voluntary organizations and non-financial companies, such as appraisers, escrow agents or title companies.
  • Preferred providers under written joint marketing agreements which are entered into to provide you with other products or services, provided however, in such cases, these joint marketing agreements will require these preferred providers to keep your personal information confidential and will prohibit the use of your personal information for any other purpose other than for the purpose disclosed when the product or service is offered.
  • Government agencies or consumer reporting agencies for specific and limited purposes necessary to:· effect a transaction authorized or requested by you; · effect, administer or enforce a transaction · protect against fraud · make a report to a consumer reporting agency; or · comply with a legal investigation or regulatory requirement

How We Disclose Your Information
Your privacy is important to us. We do not provide personally-identifying information about you to third parties other than our lending partners unless we have your consent or the disclosure is permitted or required by law. The circumstances under which we disclose information include the following:

  • Service Providers and Joint Marketers. We may disclose all of the information we collect to companies that perform marketing services on our behalf or to other financial institutions with whom we have or may have joint marketing agreements unless you have notified us not to share such information by returning the 'New Century Privacy Opt-Out Form' attached or by call us at the phone number listed below under Contact Information.
  • Disclosures to Affiliates. As permitted by law, we may release information we obtain about you from you or third parties to companies with who we are related by common ownership or affiliated by corporate control, unless you notify us before the information is initially communicated that you do not want us to share such information. You can notify us not to share such information by returning the 'New Century Privacy Opt-Out Form' attached or by calling us at the phone number listed below under Contact Information.
  • Other Disclosures to Lending Partners. Our Lending Partners may have special offers, products, or services that may be of particular interest to you. If you would like to learn of such offers, financial products, or services, authorize us to share any non-public personal information we have about you with our Lending Partners for the purposes stated above. If you do not want us to share such information with our Lending Partners for the purposes other than assisting you in obtaining the loan you requested, please check the appropriate box on the 'New Century Privacy Opt-Out Form' attached and return it to us or call the phone number listed below under Contact Information.

When Your Relationship With Us Ends
If our relationship with you ends, such as by your paying off in full any loan we make to you, we will continue to treat and protect your personally-identifiable information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. That means that we may continue to make the disclosures to our Lending Partners and Affiliates described above or permitted by law. However, if you have notified us of your election not to have us share your personally-identifiable information with others, we will continue to honor that request.

How You Can View and Change Your Personal Information
You can review the personally-identifiable information we maintain on you and make any needed corrections to it by contacting us in writing at the address below.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy; Notices
We reserve the right to amend (add to, delete from, or change) the terms of this Privacy Policy from time to time. Our Privacy Policy, as in effect from time to time, is continually posted on our Web site. By electing to become one of our customers or by receiving our products and related services, you agree to receive copies of our Privacy Policy and any amendments to it from our Web site, unless you notify us otherwise in writing at the address below. Notwithstanding the foregoing, we will provide you with a written copy of our Privacy Policy annually.
