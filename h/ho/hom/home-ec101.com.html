Privacy Policy

Updated 7-10-2008

The information we collect

When you comment on Home Ec 101, we ask for a username, email address, and an optional website address. Our software also collects your IP address when you leave a comment. We may associate your IP address with information you provide.

We use web tracking software that may send a cookie to your computer. We use this information to determine where our traffic is coming from and what people are most interested in to better serve our users.

We do not knowingly collect any information that is personally identifiable for children under 13 years of age. While Home Ec 101 is a family website, this website is intended for adult use.

How we use your information

We will never sell, rent, or share, trade, or give away your information with anyone unless legally required to do so. Browser type information including IP address, requested URL, referring URL, and timestamp data may be collected to better serve our readers.

Third party advertising networks may collect your browser information (but not your name, email address, or website address) about your visits to this and other websites. For more information on our current advertising partner, Glam Media’s privacy and security policies, please visit http://www.glammedia.com/.

Security

We take all reasonable security measures to ensure your information is not stolen or misused.

Third party content and links to other sites

We may link to other websites. We are not responsible for their privacy and security policies. We do not share your personal information with these websites.

Terms and modifications to this privacy and security statement

We at Home Ec 101 value your privacy. By using our website, you agree to the terms of our Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to these terms, do not use this website. We may modify this policy at any time. If we do, we will make sure there is a prominent notice of this change posted on the website.
