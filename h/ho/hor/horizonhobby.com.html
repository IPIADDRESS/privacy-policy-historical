Horizon hobby is committed to safeguarding the privacy of its customers.

The following statement discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for horizonhobby.com .

Horizon does not sell, rent, or loan any identifiable information regarding its customers to any third party. Any information you give us is held with the utmost care and security, and will not be used in any manner other than those to which you have consented. Horizon Hobby collects only personal information that is necessary for the efficient service of our customers.

Email / Mailing List

When placing an order, requesting a catalog, or entering a contest at horizonhobby.com, we automatically place you on our list to receive e-mail regarding special offers, new services, and new products. If you do not wish to receive e-mail from Horizonhobby.com simply e-mail subscriptions@horizonhobby.com and type “remove” in the subject line.

If you wish to be removed from the mailing list for the quarterly flyers and/or catalog please email webmaster@horizonhobby.com place “remove from snail mail” in the subject line.

Cookies

Horizonhobby.com does place a feature on your web browser called a "cookie" to assign each member a unique number. By giving each customer a unique number, it allows horizonhobby.com to track the items in your shopping cart and to save past purchase information. This will enhance your experience by saving your shopping cart information for the next time that you visit the horizonhobby.com site. Horizon Hobby does not store credit card information through the cookie application.

Cookies do not allow horizonhobby.com to collect any additional information other than the information that was provided by the member at the time of registration. If you have any additional questions about horizonhobby.com's use of cookies please contact our Webmaster.
