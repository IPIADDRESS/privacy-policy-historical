Aggregated Statistics

HDwallpaperUP may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. HDwallpaperUP may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, HDwallpaperUP does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. HDwallpaperUP uses cookies to help HDwallpaperUP identify and track visitors, their usage of HDwallpaperUP website, and their website access preferences. HDwallpaperUP visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using HDwallpaperUP's websites, with the drawback that certain features of HDwallpaperUP's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by HDwallpaperUP and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.
