Privacy Policy
Treating our clients, employees and partners with respect is one of HDR, Inc.’s core values. This privacy policy reiterates our commitment to that precept.

The only specific personal identification, such as your name or e-mail address, collected via our site(s) is the information you choose to give us. HDR, Inc. gathers this information for our use only. Such information will be used as a means of contacting you only with your permission. None of this information is provided to third party groups.

In order to identify usage trends and evaluate our site(s), HDR, Inc. collects information provided by our servers and user surveys. This information is compiled and analyzed on an aggregate basis and is used only by HDR, Inc. Specifically, this information may include your IP address, host name, pages you visit on our site, certain browser information and the referring URL. In addition, our server logs give us the capacity to determine which host names or host addresses received and responded to our surveys.

Our site(s) uses "cookies" in some limited instances, for example identifying users that prefer to not participate in a specific site survey on future visits. Users may disable cookies for our domain. Because cookies are implemented and behave differently on different client platforms, you may be offered a specific survey again (even though you previously declined) if your system-specific identification changes.

Our site(s) may include links to other sites, however HDR, Inc. is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such sites.

HDR, Inc. operates secure data networks protected by industry standard firewall and password protection systems. Only authorized employees have access to the information you provide.

Any changes to HDR, Inc.’s privacy policy will be posted on our Web site(s). Visitors will always be informed about the use of the information they provide.

Questions regarding this statement should be directed to privacy@hdrinc.com.
