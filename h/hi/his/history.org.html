Children’s Online Privacy

We do not knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. The only exception is our electronic field trip forum for registered schools where e-mail addresses are collected to verify registration.

Offline Information:
We do not collect personally identifiable offline contact information from children.

Third Party Distribution:
We do not distribute to third parties any personally identifiable information from children.

Electronic Postcards:
Although senders must furnish an e-mail address to send electronic postcards, we do not save e-mail addresses on our servers when children send e-postcards from the “Kids Zone.” Also, these e-mail addresses are not distributed to third parties or used for any other purpose.

Read the complete privacy policy statement.
