On This Day

November 21, 1941 Albert Speer, Adolf Hitler’s chief architect and minister for armaments and war production, asks for 30,000 Soviet prisoners of war to use as slave laborers to begin a massive Berlin building program.  When the war was over, Hitler was dead, and Speer was tried as a war criminal at Nuremberg and sentenced to 20 years in Spandau prison in Berlin.
