Presentation Scheduler


The History Channel is one of Sky’s most successful joint-venture channels and now, with big plans ahead, we're taking that success still further and launching a new channel.

A Presentation Scheduler is required to join our busy, energetic, and creative scheduling team, to produce daily transmission schedules for all our channels.

The role includes the creative and accurate scheduling of all broadcast presentation events, timing the schedule and downloading it to transmission.
The daily schedules consist of all the broadcast elements that are to be transmitted, including programme information, commercial breaks, promotions, continuity announcements and all channel branding.

This work requires regular liaison with other departments such as Programming, Promotions, Transmission and Sales.

Your key responsibilities will be:

*To schedule promotions and other presentation events creatively and accurately into the TX schedule.

*Adjusting the timing of the TX schedule to ensure that all our programmes start on time.

*Checking and downloading the final TX schedule to transmission.

*Monitoring the broadcast output.

Candidate Requirements:

As this is an entry level role, we don't need you to be an expert in this field, as we will train you in all the aspects of the role.

However we will expect that you have a degree, that you show passion and enthusiasm for our industry and our channels, that you have excellent communication skills and are a team player who can fit into a well established and pro-active team, and can consistently produce work to a high standard of accuracy.

Therefore if you do have all of the above, and more, and you want to be part of the future of History, then apply by clicking here. Closing date for application is 12th September.
