Privacy Policy

High Fashion Home is commited to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information.  We will not knowingly collect information or correspond via email with persons under the age of 13 without their parent’s consent. We will presume all personal information collected from our website is from persons age 13 and over.

Information Collection and Use

High Fashion Home will not collect any personally identifiable information from you except when specifically provided by you.  Information will be collected when creating an account, placing an order, signing up for emails, or any email correspondence you might have with High Fashion Home.  We do not disclose any personally identifiable information to  unaffiliated third parties.  We do reserve the right to provide such information to our employees, contractors, and agents to enable them to improve website-related services, features, and custom content.  We will use all commercially reasonable efforts to safeguard your information..  Any information that you provide to us is done at your own risk.

Links To Other Sites

Our website may contain links to other websites. High Fashion Home does not control the privacy policies or practices of these websites. You should review those policies before providing any personal information.

Cookies

Like most websites, we use "cookies" to collect information.  Cookies are used to collect non-personally indentifable information to provide you with customized content and enable certain features of the Site.  You can instruct your browser to stop accepting cookies, which may interfere with the functionality of the Site.

Security

High Fashion Home is committed to providing a secure and safe shopping experience.  Our servers use Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) that is tested and verified by VeriSign, Inc., which is a technology company that specializes in data encryption and e-commerce.  When you enter sensitive information on our registration or order checkout forms, you can feel at ease shopping our website with one of the leading companies for certifying that a website is secure and encrypted.
