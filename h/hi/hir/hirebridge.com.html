It is our continuing goal to make your visit to the Web Site more efficient, enjoyable and productive.  By gathering this information, we can customize the Web Site and tailor it to meet your particular needs.  Hirebridge will not share with third parties information about you in which you are identified, except in the following circumstances: (i) where Hirebridge secures your express prior consent; (ii) as necessary to provide you with goods or services you request; and/or (iii) to law enforcement or other government officials or as otherwise required by law as Hirebridge deems in our discretion to be necessary or appropriate. 

In addition, from time to time, Our company  may also use information you provide to inform you of features and services that we  believe would be of particular interest to you.  You can choose not to receive such information by letting us know as provided below.  Our company  may share, sell, lease, rent and otherwise disclose to business partners, advertisers, and other affiliates and third parties information about you, in which you may or may not be identified (such as in the form of statistical, demographic, or aggregated information about visitors generally to the web Site). 

"Cookies"

"Cookies" are small pieces of information that are stored by your web browser software on your computer's hard drive or temporarily in your computer's memory.  The use of cookies is now an industry standard, and users will find them on most major web sites.

We use cookies to personalize the viewing experience of a user on the Hirebridge Web Site.  When the user revisits the Hirebridge Web Site, we  can recognize the user by the cookie and customize the user 's experience at our Web Site accordingly. 

Although most web browsers are initially set up to accept cookies, if you prefer, you, as the user, may decline the placement of a cookie on your hard drive by using the appropriate feature(s) of your web browser software (if available) to delete the cookie.  You, as the user, should understand, however, that certain areas within the Hirebridge Web Site may not function properly if the web browser will not accept cookies.
