Privacy Policy

Last updated September 7, 2017


At Hipmunk, Inc. (“Hipmunk” or “we” or “us”), we want to make your travel experience better. As part of that effort, we want you to feel comfortable when you visit or use hipmunk.com, the Hipmunk mobile applications, our related services, and any other services that link to this Policy (the “Service”). We also understand that you may have some important questions for us about what information we collect when you use our Service, why we collect that information, and how we may use it and disclose it to others. We've created this Policy to help answer those questions. Please read this Policy carefully. We may add to or amend this Policy with other notices. We may also post different privacy statements for some Services and when we do so, this Policy does not apply.

This Policy applies solely to information collected by us through the Services and does not apply to any third-party activities, including third party websites and travel partners linked to from the Services, third party social networking platforms or features we make available through our Services, or third-party ads displayed on our Services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such third-parties. We encourage you to read the privacy statements of those third parties.

What Information Is Collected?

We collect information from you in various ways when you use our Service.

Information Provided by You. We collect information you directly provide to us. For example, we collect your email address, name, billing or mailing address, phone number, preference information, payment details, home airport, booking details, the communications you have with our customer support teams, and other information you provide us on our Service. We may also obtain information from other sources and combine that with information we collect on our Service, including information about bookings you make with third-party travel partners to which we refer you. Certain Automatically Collected Information. When you visit or use the Service, some information about your usage and activity on the Service is collected automatically. For example, when you visit our websites, your computer's operating system, Internet Protocol (IP) address, device identifiers, browser type, device type, domain name, access times, duration of visit, interactions with webpages, ads, and content, platform, time stamp and language, and referring web site addresses may be logged automatically.

Information About Your Use of the Service. When you use the Service, and specifically when you do things like enter a search query or book travel, we may collect certain information about you, including the searches you run on the Service, the date and time when you initiated a search, and the location from which you initiated the search. We may collect information about how you use the Service, including information about your clicks and interactions on the Services. We may retain this information and associate it with your profile information.

Third-Party Logins. We allow users with accounts on third-party services, such as Facebook and Google, to log into the Service using functionality that those third parties provide, such as Facebook Connect and Google's OpenID-based authentication mechanism. When you use this Facebook or Google functionality to log into the Service, we will access, collect, and use specific information related to your Facebook or Google account, such as your name and email address, as described in this Policy and associate it with other information we have about you.

Calendar Integration. With your permission, we collect calendar information from your mobile device and use it to provide suggestions (such as for flights, hotels, and cars) based on your meetings and travel plans. We also allow you to integrate various functionalities from other services, such as Microsoft Outlook and Google Calendar, with Hipmunk. If you choose to take advantage of this integration feature, we will ask you to authenticate your account. If you access the Service using our mobile apps, you may not need to provide your login credentials, as your mobile device may allow this integration automatically if you enable those permissions. When providing those credentials or enabling the permissions, we will access, collect, and use this information, including calendar invite information, as described in this Policy and associate it with other information we have about you.

Concur Login Integration. If your employer has enabled you to connect the Concur account you use through your employer with your Hipmunk account, you may be provided with access to employer discounts for purposes of booking business travel. By allowing your Concur account to access your personal Hipmunk account, Concur and your employer will receive certain information about trips you book, such as date of travel, origin, destination, flight, and booking information. By allowing your Hipmunk to access your Concur account, Hipmunk will obtain certain profile and payment information that you store on Concur and combine it with data Hipmunk has about you and enable Hipmunk to update information on your Concur account including booking and itinerary information. You may disconnect your Concur account from your Hipmunk account by visiting the account settings section of the Service. If your account was created using a Concur account, before you leave your employer you will need to update your Hipmunk account email to a personal email account to retain control of your personal Hipmunk account.

Communications. When you communicate with us, for example through our Virtual Travel Agent, whether via hello@hipmunk.com, Skype, Slack, or other communication services we support, we collect your contact information and the contents of the communication.

Tracking Technologies. To automatically collect information about you as described above we may use certain tracking technologies, such as device identifiers, and other information about you or your device ("Tracking Technologies"). These Tracking Technologies work in different ways, for example some may be downloaded to your device and others may set or modify settings on your device. The following are examples of a few of the methods that may be used to collect information (and subsequent technology and methods later developed):

  • Location-identifying Technologies. The Service may use global positioning systems (GPS), IP address, and other location-based technologies to locate you, deliver you relevant content based on your location, and set your home airport. Upon downloading our mobile applications, we determine your approximate and/or exact location information to provide location-based services.
  • "Cookies" Information: We may automatically collect information using "cookies." Cookies are small data files stored on your hard drive by a web site. Among other things, cookies help us improve our Service and your experience. We use cookies to see which areas and features are popular and to count visits to our Service. If you set your browser not to accept cookies or if you reject a cookie (your help screen or manual should tell you how to do this), it may affect certain features on the Service.
  • Web Beacons. We may collect information using web beacons. Web beacons are electronic images that may be used on our Service or in our emails. We use web beacons to deliver cookies, count visits, understand usage and campaign effectiveness and to tell if an email has been opened and acted upon.
  • ETag, or entity tag. A feature of the cache in browsers. It is an opaque identifier assigned by a web server to a specific version of a resource found at a URL. If the resource content at that URL ever changes, a new and different ETag is assigned. Used in this manner, ETags are a form of device identifier. ETag tracking may generate unique tracking values even if you have blocked HTTP, Flash, and HTML5 cookies

Booking through Hipmunk. If you book travel with our third-party travel partners, we may collect payment details from you including name on card, card number, billing address, expiration date, security code, and phone number, and provide that information to the travel partner to complete the transaction. You may have the option to let us save your payment details, which we will store for the purpose of enabling future bookings. You may add a payment method or edit or delete your saved payment information at any time in your Hipmunk account settings. Such third-party travel partners may use your information for their own marketing purposes and you should review their privacy statements prior to booking.

If you book travel using Hipmunk through certain third-party partners, we may send a copy of the travel booking confirmation to the partner. The confirmation will include personally identifiable information (such as name, email and phone number, and billing address) and truncated payment card details.
