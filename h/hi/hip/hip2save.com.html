    • information on legal transactions between you and Hip2Save is retained for a period of 10 years as of their provision to Hip2Save.

We use cookies to identify one unique visitor from another visitor, to store a visitor’s preferences, and to record session information in order to provide customized webpage content and better service when a visitor returns.
