Privacy & Security

Hippo Blue treats customer privacy with the utmost importance.  All customer information is kept secure and private, we only use personal information to ensure we keep customer relations.  Although in the case of a possible unlawful transactions, details may be given to the authorities in cooperation.  For your protection, any personal information you provide us with will be used in accordance with the Privacy Act. 1988.  It is our policy that we do not give out any customer information via email request. 

Using our SSL security encryption system your credit card, private and personal details are always safely guarded.  We do not store credit card information on our database.  Once a credit card transaction has been processed the information will be deleted once completed.

Hippo Blue uses the services of Paypal to accept Credit cards, Debit Cards, or Direct deposit payments.  Paypal has a common misconception that you NEED an account to use their services,that is definitely not the case.  Paypal has a very high level of security and buyer protection, for more information refer to the official Paypal website at https://www.paypal-australia.com.au/security/buyer-protection.    

It is of utmost importance that our customers feel safe when they are making purchases via our website using their Credit Cards or any of our payment methods. 

For any further information or enquiries please email us at: customerservice@hippoblue.com.au
