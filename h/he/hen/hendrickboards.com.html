Home » Privacy Policy
Your privacy is important to Hendrick & Co. This privacy statement provides information about the personal information that Hendrick & Co. collects, and the ways in which Hendrick & Co. uses that personal information.
 
First and foremost, Hendrick & Co. has never and will never sell any information collected on our site to a third party for any reason whatsoever. All of the information we collect is simply to fulfill orders and communicate with our customers. 
 

Personal information collection
Hendrick & Co. may collect and use the following kinds of personal information: information about your use of this website (including name, email, address, and personal information); information that you provide using for the purpose of registering with the website (including name, email, address, and personal information); information about transactions carried out over this website (including name, email, address, account information, and personal information; information that you provide for the purpose of subscribing to the website services (including Newsletter, coupons and any other information that you send to Hendrick Boards).

Using personal information
Hendrick & Co. may use your personal information to:
    administer this website;
    personalize the website for you;
    enable your access to and use of the website services;
    publish information about you on the website;
    send to you products that you purchase;
    supply to you services that you purchase;
    send you statements and invoices;
    collect payments from you; and
    send you marketing communications.

Where Hendrick & Co. discloses your personal information to its agents or sub-contractors for these purposes, the agent or sub-contractor in question will be obligated to use that personal information in accordance with the terms of this privacy statement.

In addition to the disclosures reasonably necessary for the purposes identified elsewhere above, Hendrick & Co. may disclose your personal information to the extent that it is required to do so by law, in connection with any legal proceedings or prospective legal proceedings, and in order to establish, exercise or defend its legal rights.

Securing your data
Hendrick & Co. will take reasonable technical and organizational precautions to prevent the loss, misuse or alteration of your personal information. Hendrick & Co. will store all the personal information you provide on its secure servers. Information relating to electronic transactions entered into via this website will be protected by encryption technology.

Cross-border data transfers
Information that Hendrick & Co. collects may be stored and processed in and transferred between any of the countries in which Hendrick & Co. operates to enable the use of the information in accordance with this privacy policy. In addition, personal information that you submit for publication on the website will be published on the internet and may be available around the world. You agree to such cross-border transfers of personal information.

Updating this statement
Hendrick & Co. may update this privacy policy by posting a new version on this website. You should check this page occasionally to ensure you are familiar with any changes.

Other websites
This website contains links to other websites. Hendrick & Co. is not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of any third party.

Contact Hendrick Boards
If you have any questions about this privacy policy or Hendrick Boards treatment of your personal information, please email: customerservice@hendrickboards.com
