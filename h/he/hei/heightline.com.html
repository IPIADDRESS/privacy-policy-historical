Cookies

A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user’s computer to get information about the user.  We work with various reputable companies to help us analyse how our website is used, and to optimize our Website to deliver the best possible experience. These companies may also include advertisers however, in such cases, we have no access or control over these cookies once we have given permission for them to set cookies for advertising. If you wish to disable cookies, you can use the settings within your browser to control the cookies that are set on your computer or mobile device. However, please be aware that cookies are important to many aspects of our website – if you set your browser to reject cookies, you may not be able to enjoy all of the features on our platform. To find out how to see what cookies have been set and how to reject and delete cookies, please visit http://www.aboutcookies.org.
