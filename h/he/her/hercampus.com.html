This policy may change from time to time, so please check back periodically to review this information.

Information Collection And Use

Her Internet.com typically receives specific data about its website visitors only when such information is provided voluntarily, such as when our visitors register for features, services, provide resume information for employment opportunities, or send us e-mail. When you provide information to Her Internet.com through our websites, it will be used to fulfill your specific request. In most cases, you will be given the opportunity to select whether you do, or do not, want Her Internet.com to use this information for additional purposes. Her Internet.com reserves the right, in its discretion, to send you bulletins and other important information about your Her Internet.com account. Absent any instructions from you, Her Internet.com may use information you provide to inform you about additional services and products offered by the Her Internet.com family of companies, authorized agents, and other goods and services providers with whom Her Internet.com has relationships and whose offerings might be of interest to you. Her Internet.com will not, however, sell or trade your information unless we are authorized or legally required to do so, or in the case of imminent physical harm to the visitor or others.

For each visitor to out website, our web server automatically recoginizes no information regarding the domain or e-mail address.

This site collects cookies. The information we collect is:

  • Used to improve the content of our Web page.
  • Used to notify visitors about updates to our Web site.
  • Never shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.

During registration, a user must give certain contact information, but we will never sell or give away this information. It is only gathered to contact the user about services on our site for which s/he has expressed interest. Giving additional information helps provide a more personalized experience on our site, but is not required.

Notification Of Changes

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes to this privacy statement page and to all member's e-mail address listed in Her Internet.com 's database.

We are committed to resolve disputes within 24-48 hours. If problems arise, users may contact us by any of the contact information listed on our site.
