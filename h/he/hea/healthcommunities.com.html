Personal Information Remedy Health Media Collects From You

Remedy Health Media collects Personal Information from you through online registration, business reply cards and online surveys and questionnaires that you submit.

Each time you visit www.remedyhealthmedia.com (the "Website"), our web server automatically recognizes your IP address and the web page from which you came. We also may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our servers, to administer the Website and to better serve you in using the Website.

Cookies and Action Tags

The first time a user provides an e-mail address in connection with their activities at the Website, Remedy Health Media assigns an identification number to that e-mail address and deploys a cookie to the applicable user's PC. Whenever that user comes back to the Website using the same PC, the cookie allows the Website to identify the user and to recall the user's e-mail address. If, at any time, a user provides other information in connection with their activities on the Website (such as name, address, birth date, gender, etc.), Remedy Health Media may store that information, along with the user's e-mail address, in its user database. Remedy Health Media may use the Personal Information stored in its database: (i) to effectuate the purpose or transaction for which the information was originally provided by a user; (ii) to pre-populate information fields in the event a user wishes to purchase products and/or services or sign up for and/or subscribe to services, promotions or other offers in the future; (iii) to ensure that a user will not be repeatedly exposed to the same advertisements, offers and/or promotions while visiting the Website; and/or (iv) to, in connection with regular communication with a user, include offers, promotions or advertisements that were historically, or are likely to be, of interest to that user. Remedy Health Media also may use cookies to anonymously track and target the interests of our users to further enhance the experience on the Website. To find out more about cookies, please visit www.cookiecentral.com.

Action Tags, Web Beacons and other Data Collection Methods: An action tag or a web-beacon (also known as a clear gif or a pixel tag) is a method used to track responses or actions by visitors who view certain advertisements or other information on a website. Action tags are 1x1 pixel images embedded in a website page that are used to transparently collect information. Remedy Health Media may use action tags to count the number of times that visitors click on a particular banner ad or visit the pages of our Website and to provide information about what products are viewed or purchased. Remedy Health Media reserves the right to retain this cookie and action tag data indefinitely. Remedy Health Media at times links the data collected through the means described in this paragraph with your Personal Information for purposes of customizing messages to you and customizing Website content for you. At no time will Remedy Health Media share cookie-related, action-tag-related and/or generated information and/or data with third parties.
