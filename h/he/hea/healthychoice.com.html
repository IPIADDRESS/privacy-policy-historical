HEALTHY CHOICE PRIVACY POLICY

The following is a description of how we handle information we learn about you from your visit to our website. The information we receive depends upon what you do when visiting our site.

For example:

  • If you visit our site to read or download information, such as software or articles, we collect and store the following information: The name of the domain from which you access the Internet, the date and time you access the site and the Internet address of the website from which you linked to our site. The information is used to measure the number of visitors to the different sections of our site. This helps us learn which areas are most and least popular and helps us to make our site more useful to our visitors.


  • If you identify yourself by sending email, we use the information you provide to collect consumer comments, answer your questions or address any concerns. The information you provide sometimes leads to modifications of our website.


  • If you fill out a survey to receive free stuff, we collect and store the information you provide. This information can include your name, street address and email address. We also analyze the questions you answer. We do this to understand what products you find most useful, how often you use our products, what competitive products you use and what areas of our site you enjoy or changes you would like to see. The information helps us provide more useful content and products. We do not share, lease or giveaway this information to third parties.


What we do not collect is personally identifying information about you when you visit our site unless you chose to provide such information to us. Again, we never share, lease or giveaway that information to third parties.

We may update this Policy from time to time, so please check in occasionally.
