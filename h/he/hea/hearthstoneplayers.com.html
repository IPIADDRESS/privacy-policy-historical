However, Hearthstone Players does not connect this non-personal data to any personal information collected from you.

Again, these cookies are not connected to any personal information. Third party cookie usage is subject to their own privacy policies, and Hearthstone Players assumes no responsibility or liability for this usage.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Hearthstone Players may disclose your personal information to third parties who work on behalf of Hearthstone Players to provide products and services requested by you. We will share personal information for these purposes only with third parties whose privacy policies are consistent with ours or who agree to abide by our policies with respect to personal information

We have your express consent to share the information for a specified purpose;
We need to respond to subpoenas, court orders or such other legal process;

If you want to opt-out of Google Analytics for Display Advertising, just customize Google Display Network ads using the Ads Settings or using Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on that will instruct the Google Analytics JavaScript not to send any information about the website visit to Google Analytics.
