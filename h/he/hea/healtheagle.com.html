What information does Wasabi Ventures collect and use and what ways (if any) does Wasabi Ventures share this information with third parties?
Wasabi Ventures may collect any or all of the following types of information: (a) traffic or clickstream data, (b) cookies, (c) Internet Protocol (“IP”) addresses, or (d) contact information and other personally identifying information. Wasabi Ventures's collection and use of each type of data is described below, as well as the ways (if any) in which the data may be shared with third parties.

a. Traffic or Clickstream Data. Data about traffic and usage, including keywords, search results, bids, time of search, time of click, browser type and default language setting, is not personally identifying or identifiable, and is not supplemented or combined with personally identifying or identifiable information. Wasabi Ventures may share information it collects with certain selected third parties, but only in anonymous form. Importantly, all information collected from a search query is recorded on a non-personally identifiable basis and is kept anonymous. Wasabi Ventures collects some information regarding use of the Services by observing browser functions and the files (pages) users access. For example, Wasabi Ventures's servers collect information about the most frequently searched terms to help provide the most relevant search results, information about usage to check for fraudulent behavior or other abnormalities and to ensure that our Services are technically superior. In addition to the foregoing applications, Wasabi Ventures uses the information (in aggregate form) to better design and market the Services.

b. Cookies. Like many companies, Wasabi Ventures collects information about online behavior using cookies. If Wasabi Ventures is providing you with search optimization services, Wasabi Ventures tracks usage of your web site through cookies. These cookies contain identification information that enables us to offer you the most relevant advertisements and other content. Cookies let us know about repeat visitors and if we are delivering quality products and services. Wasabi Ventures may also use cookies to identify which sites our repeat visitors are coming from, so that we can place more of our online advertising on these sites, and can, where necessary, pay our affiliates and other business partners. To Wasabi Ventures, using information within cookies is an anonymous way to survey how we are doing and to look for ways to improve the value of the Services to you. You may set your browser software to reject cookies, but you may not be able to use all of the features of the Services. Wasabi Ventures may share information about usage of the Services with third parties, but only in aggregated, anonymous form. In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser.

c. IP Addresses. An Internet Protocol (“IP”) address is a set of numbers that is automatically assigned to your computer each time you connect to the Internet. When you use your web browser to request a web page from another computer on the Internet, your web browser automatically gives that computer your IP address so that the data you request can be sent to your computer. Through your IP address, Wasabi Ventures may track your Internet usage, including identifying Affiliates and geographic location, which may be disclosed to advertisers, search engines and other partners.. Wasabi Ventures may strip a portion of numbers from your IP address and provide such information to our third-party advertising companies. This information collected by Wasabi Ventures though your IP address is not personally identifiable.

d. Contact Information or Personally Identifying Information. Wasabi Ventures may obtain access to your name, e-mail address, physical address, telephone number, fax number, social security number, federal tax ID number, credit card number and other contact or personally identifiable information.

When you become an advertiser with Wasabi Ventures, your contact information is used for billing, marketing and customer care purposes as well as for the purpose of approving and enabling your participation in the Services.
