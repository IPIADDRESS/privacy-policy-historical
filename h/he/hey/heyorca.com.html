Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which HeyOrca Inc. (HeyOrca, We, or Us) collects, uses, maintains and discloses Personal Information about users and customers (each, a User), as well as prospective customers, of the www.HeyOrca.com website (Site) as well as Our online platform and related products and services (the Services).

Personal identification information

This Privacy Policy covers Our collection, use and disclosure of information about identifiable individuals and information which can be used to identify an individual (Personal Information).

We may collect Personal Identification information from Users in a variety of ways, including, but not limited to, when Users visit Our site, register for Services, subscribe to the newsletter and in connection with other activities, Services, features or resources We make available on Our Site. Users may be asked for, as appropriate, name, phone number, address, email address and credit card and billing information.

If a User communicates with Us by telephone, the call will be recorded for internal quality assurance. We may collect the Users name, phone number, or other Personal Information required to fulfill the purpose of the call.

Collection and Use of Personal Information

HeyOrca collects and uses Users Personal Information for the following purposes:

  • Provide, operate, maintain and improve the Services;
  • To improve customer service.
  • Effectively respond to your customer service requests and support needs.
  • To personalize User experience.
  • To improve Our Site and Services.
  • To process transactions.
  • To administer a content, promotion, survey or other Site feature.
  • To send Users information they agreed to receive about topics We think will be of interest to them.
  • To send technical notices, updates, security alerts and support and administrative messages.

 

The email address Users provide for order processing will be used to send information and updates pertaining to their order or other information related to the Services. It may also be used to respond to their inquiries, and/or other requests or questions. If User decides to opt-in to Our mailing list, they will receive emails that may include company news, updates, related product or service information, etc. If at any time the User would like to unsubscribe from receiving future emails, We include detailed unsubscribe instructions at the bottom of each email or User may contact Us via Our Site. However, Users may not unsubscribe from emails that are mandatory for the Services.

We also collect data about how and when you access the Website and Service, including information about the device and browser you use, your network connection, your IP address, and information about how interact with the Service.

 

We use this information to give you access to and improve our Service. We also use this information to personalize the Service for you. Finally, we may use this information to provide you with advertising or marketing.

 

Social Media Insights

HeyOrca uses third-party services to collect publicly available information from social media sites to contact prospective customers and conduct other marketing activities.

Payment Information

We do not collect any credit card or other billing information. Instead purchases will be processed through a third-party payment processor. By using any third-party service, you agree to be bound by such third-partys terms and conditions and privacy policy.

Cookies

As you visit or browse the Website, We collect information about the device and browser you use, your network connection, your IP address, and information about the cookies installed on your device. This information is logged to help diagnose technical problems for analytics and for quality control purposes.

Our Site may also use cookies to enhance User experience. A Users web browser places cookies on their hard drive for record-keeping purposes and sometimes to track information about them. The User may choose to set their web browser to refuse cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent. If they do so, note that some parts of the Site or Service may not function properly.

Third parties, including Facebook, may use cookies, web beacons, and other storage technologies to collect or receive Personal Information from the Websites and elsewhere on the internet and use that information to provide measurement services and targeted ads. Note that we do not provide any interest-based advertising to individuals under 16 years old. Users can limit cookies used by their browsers and third parties by updating settings at: http://www.aboutads.info/choices and http://www.youronlinechoices.eu.

 

Aggregated Non-Personal Information

We may use information in the aggregate (or that has otherwise been anonymized) to understand how Our Users as a group use the services and resources provided on Our Site.

You have the right to access the Personal Information We hold about you in order to verify the Personal Information We have collected in respect to you and to have a general account of Our uses of that information.
