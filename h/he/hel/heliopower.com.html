Privacy Policy
HelioPower cares about your privacy, and we are committed to handling all personal and business information with the utmost discretion. When you visit our web site, we may automatically collect information about your computer configuration through the use of files known as "cookies".

A cookie is a small data file (often including a unique, anonymous identifier) sent to your browser from a web site. If your browser's security settings are configured to allow it, cookies may be stored on your computer's hard drive. Each web site you visit can send its own cookie to your browser, but your browser permits a web site to access only the cookies orginating from it, and not access to any cookie sent to you by another site.

Information which may be automatically collected when you visit our site may include your browser type, operating system, IP address or ISP domain name. Collecting this information helps us to improve web site performance.

Information which you submit through this web site, or separately via post, telephone, fax or other communication, may be used by HelioPower to provide sales, solar energy, energy conservation, water or pool-heating or other advice directly to your home or business. If we feel it is necessary to better answer your request for information, we may also share this information with our vendors, co- or sub-contractors, consultants or businesses so that they can help us serve you. Because of the nature of the information collection and sharing process, we ask that you do not provide HelioPower with any information which you feel must be kept private and/or confidential.
