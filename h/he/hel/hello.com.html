1. What general and personal information do we gather about you?
Hello.com collects a variety of general and personal information about you. We collect general information about you when you visit our web site. This information includes technical information related to your computer and the manner in which you are accessing our site and includes such information as the internet protocol (IP) address of your computer, what operating system your computer is using, what browser software (e.g., Explorer, Netscape or other) your computer is using, and who your internet service provider is, the Uniform Resource Locator ("URL") of the web site from which you just came and the URL to which you go next and certain operating metrics, such as the amount of time you use our service.

In addition, during your registration process with Hello.com, we may ask you to provide us with personal information including, but not limited to, your name, address, city, state, zip code, birth date, phone number, credit card number and email address. Once you are a Member, from time to time, we may ask you to update your personal information so that we may offer more personalized services to you.

5. What are "Cookies" and how are they used?
A "Cookie" is a very small text file placed on your hard drive by a web server.

We use cookies for a variety of reasons. For example, a cookie may be used to identify each Member who uses the Service and allow us to obtain session information for our Members. We may use this information to deliver a more personalized service to you In addition, cookies allow advertisers and merchants to track responsiveness to their marketing efforts and to process transactions at their online stores. Thus, we allow advertisers or partners to set cookies, but only if they agree to comply with our privacy policy.

Please note that, in addition to allowing you to accept all cookies, your internet browser software may also allow you to be warned before accepting a cookie or to reject all cookies. Our Service is dependent on the acceptance of cookies by our Members. Therefore, if your browser is not set to accept cookies, you will be unable to properly use our Service.
