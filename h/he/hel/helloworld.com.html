PRIVACY STATEMENT FOR VMdirect VIDEOMAIL DELIVERY

From time to time, VMdirect stores database files and email addresses for clients. These files are used to deliver email on behalf of clients only. Client lists are NEVER shared with other clients or aggregated in any manner. All client files stored by VMdirect remain the property of the original client. Security of client data is of the highest priority.

VMdirect can track recipient actions within an email if the email client/browser is html capable. The tracking elements reported to clients are whether a recipient has opened the message, estimated recipient connection speed, whether the recipient viewed the video content, time the content was viewed, whether the recipient clicked a link(s), which links were clicked, whether the message was forwarded. Text only email messages cannot be tracked. This tracking is used to help deliver the best content based on connection speed and to determine return on investment for the client.

All tracking is done on behalf of clients and will be reported only to the client that owns any specific campaign. Recipient specific tracking information is never shared with any third parties. Expired campaign data is routinely purged from our systems within thirty days of said expiration.
