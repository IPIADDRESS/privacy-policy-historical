Privacy Policy

At Hair Direct, Inc. (HD), we respect your right to privacy and we understand that, as a visitor to our Web site(s), you prefer to control your own personal information and preferences. For this reason, we may ask you to register or to provide personal information and preferences when you visit certain areas of these sites, request new information, or subscribe to the Hair Direct newsletter.

We will guard the personal information you share with us to ensure the content, services, and advertising that we provide on our Web site(s) are always tailored to your current, designated preferences.

When you provide Hair Direct with your name (or alias), address, or telephone number, Hair Direct and Hair Direct representatives may use your postal address and phone number to alert you to product upgrades, special offers, updated information, classes, and new services. ("Representatives" include value-added resale's and authorized training partners.)

When you provide Hair Direct with your e-mail address (as an alternative to, or in addition to, your mailing address and phone number), Hair Direct may contact you via e mail. Hair Direct never releases e-mail addresses to its representatives. Hair Direct also does not offer, or allow for the selling of, any user-provided information to third parties.

If you choose not to register or provide personal information, you can still visit most of Hair Direct Web site(s); however, you will not have access to areas that require personal identification.

How Hair Direct will protect your personal information. Hair Direct will always protect the personal information that you share with us. Hair Direct stores information internally in a controlled, secure environment.

If Hair Direct requests general demographic information, this information is limited to reporting purposes only; members remain anonymous. Phone numbers, e-mail addresses, and postal addresses are never shared for demographic reports with outside entities.

Hair Direct may occasionally present a special contest or promotion that is sponsored by another company. To qualify for entry, we may ask you to provide personal information. If we plan to share that information with the sponsor(s), we will provide an upfront statement to that effect.

If at any time you wish to stop receiving communication from Hair Direct, feel free to contact us at any time.
