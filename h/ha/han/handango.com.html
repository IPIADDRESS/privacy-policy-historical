Handango Privacy Policy

At Handango, your right to privacy is a primary concern. This Privacy Policy describes the guidelines we use to protect the information we collect about you during a visit to our Internet site. If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at comments@handango.com.

What information do you collect?
Handango requests personal information at various times during the use of our Web site. These requests are made to allow us to provide services you have requested, or to enhance your experience while visiting us. Information we collect may include your name, address, telephone number, email address, and credit card information. In addition, we periodically sponsor contests and other promotions that may require other personal information in order to participate. In all cases, you are under no obligation to provide any personal information.

What do you do with this information?
Only Handango.com has access to users' accounts. We will not give or sell your private information to any outside company for its use in marketing or solicitation. However, we may disclose your information in certain cases where we have your permission or under special circumstances, such as when we believe it is required by law.

Periodically, we will use the information provided to us to notify you of important announcements regarding special offers or important functionality changes to our Web site. If you would rather not receive this information, you can request to be excluded from these announcements by checking the appropriate box in your user profile.

Handango also uses information on an aggregate basis (without individual information) to better understand our users and to improve our site. Aggregate statistics may also be disclosed to potential advertisers and/or business partners.

Do you use "cookies"?
Yes. "Cookies" (small information files stored by your browser) allow us to provide advanced features such as personalization and shopping carts. The cookies we use do not contain any personal information. If you wish, you can change your browser settings to disable cookies.

Your consent.
By using our Web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by Handango. Periodically we may modify our privacy policy. We will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect and how we use it.

Give us your feedback
Handango welcomes your questions and comments about privacy. Email us at comments@handango.com
