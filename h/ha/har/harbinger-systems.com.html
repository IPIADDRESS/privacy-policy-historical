Privacy Policy

Harbinger Systems is very sensitive to privacy issues on the Internet. We believe it is important you know how we treat the information about you we receive from you, on the Internet.

We sometimes collect anonymous information from visits to our sites to help us provide better customer service like keeping track of the domains from which people visit. We also measure visitor activity on our web sites, but we do so in ways that keep the information anonymous. We use your IP address to administer our Web site to give us an idea of which parts of our site users are visiting. This information is aggregated to measure the number of visits, average time spent on the site or pages viewed. We do not link IP addresses to anything personally identifiable.

Harbinger Systems respects your desire for privacy. Personal information collected through forms on this website will not be shared with organizations external to Harbinger Systems without your consent. We will use your personal information only for communications regarding your question or comment
