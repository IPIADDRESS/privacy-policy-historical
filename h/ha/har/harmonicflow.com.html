Harmonic Flow - Privacy Policy

What this Privacy Policy Covers

This policy also covers HARMONIC FLOW's treatment of any personally identifiable information that HARMONIC FLOW's business partners share with HARMONIC FLOW or that HARMONIC FLOW may collect on a partner's site.

· This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that HARMONIC FLOW does not own or control, or to people that HARMONIC FLOW does not employ or manage.

· Anonymous information refers to data that cannot be tied back to a specific individual. For example, HARMONIC FLOW collects some information each time a visitor comes to a HARMONIC FLOW site so we can improve the overall quality of the visitor's online experience. HARMONIC FLOW collects the visitor's IP address, referral data, and browser and platform type. HARMONIC FLOW also collects data each time one of our products is used to access the Internet. HARMONIC FLOW collects this user information for purposes of product monitoring, product improvement, and to aid future development decisions. This information may also be shared with advertisers and our business partners on an aggregate non-personal basis. You do not have to register a product with HARMONIC FLOW before we can collect this anonymous information.

· Personally identifiable information refers to data that tells us specifically who you are, such as your name and postal address, which may be collected in addition to the anonymous data described above. HARMONIC FLOW collects personal information in the following ways from different parts of its Web sites and games:

o Purchasing a HARMONIC FLOW game: HARMONIC FLOW collects personally identifiable information when you purchase HARMONIC FLOW games. The types of information that may be requested include your name, address, telephone number, email address, and credit card information.

o When you register for HARMONIC FLOW news: HARMONIC FLOW collects your email address.

o Other Registration: HARMONIC FLOW may collect personal information when you register for other HARMONIC FLOW services. HARMONIC FLOW may also receive personally identifiable information from our business partners.

HARMONIC FLOW will not this e-mail address information to any mailing lists.
