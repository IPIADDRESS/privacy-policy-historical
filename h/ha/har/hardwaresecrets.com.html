Our Privacy Policy

It is the policy of hardwaresecrets.com to protect your privacy as a reader of this website. This website does not collect personal information about you except for information that you voluntarily provide. All personal information is treated confidentially. However, hardwaresecrets.com is not responsible for the information you volunteer about yourself in the course of discussions in our forums. We consider the following to be personal information: your name, address, phone number, date of birth, and your email address, when correlated to your name or address.

From time to time we set cookies, but no personal information is gathered via these cookies. We use cookies to identify members’ computers so they don't have to re-enter a user id and/or password as long as they are using the same computer. We also use cookies to determine the number of unique readers that visit our site. This information is aggregated then used in our marketing material. We do not have control over cookies or pixel tags (used by advertisers to track traffic) that are placed by advertisers on their pages or advertisements. Some of our advertisers use cookie and pixel tags to measure the effectiveness of Web and email advertisements. We do not give any personally identifiable information to them as part of this relationship.

This site contains links to other sites. Hardware Secrets LLC is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites.
