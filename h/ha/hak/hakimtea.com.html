We agree that we will never share your email with any third party and that we will remove your email at your request or you cant unsubcribe. We don’t currently send advertising via email, but in the future our email may contain advertisements and we may send dedicated email messages from our advertisers without revealing your email addresses to them. If you have any problem removing your email address please contact us via our contact form.

Hakimtea.Com is the sole owner of any information collected on our websites.

Cookies
Currently we assign cookies to our readers in order to save their preferences. This data is not shared with any third party. Accessing our websites is not dependent on accepting cookies and all major browsers allow you to disable cookies if you wish.

Third Party Cookies
Many of our advertisers use cookies in order to determine the number of times you have seen an advertisement. This is done to limit the number times you are shown the same advertisement. Hakimtea.Com does not have access to this data.

Traffic Reports
Our industry-standard traffic reporting records IP addresses, Internet service provider information, referrer strings, browser types and the date and time pages are loaded. We use this information in the aggregate only to provide traffic statistics to advertisers and to figure out which features and editorials are most popular.

We will make every effort to preserve user privacy but Hakimtea.Com may need to disclose information when required by law.

Links

When you leave our websites we encourage you to read the destination site’s privacy policy. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by Hakimtea.Com.

Notification of Changes
When Hakimtea.Com makes changes to this privacy policy we will post those changes here.
