Privacy Policy

Hach Privacy Statement
Hach Company recognizes the importance of the privacy issue in use of the Internet and World Wide Web. Information which you provide to us either directly (during registration, through on-line forms, or via e-mail) or indirectly (data normally collected on traffic to this site) is covered by our Privacy Policy.

Hach Privacy Policy
Hach Company holds in confidence all information (such as your name, address, email, and phone number) given us by customers and visitors to this site. We will not sell or release this information to any organization outside of Hach Company without your knowledge and consent. Information you provide is used to support your customer relationship with Hach, and may be shared on a restricted basis with agents or contractors directly performing services for us. Hach Company may from time to time contact you with news about products or special offers which we feel could be of interest you.

If the policy stated here should change in any way, you will be notified by phone, mail and/or email, and you will have the opportunity to respond before any change affects you. You may at any time ask for more information about our privacy policy, ask to review or update personal information held by us about you, or request that we remove your name from our mailing list by calling 1-800-227-4224 in the U.S.A. or 1-970-669-3050 outside of the U.S.A. We reserve the right to retain certain customer information which relates to billing, account status, and product warranties.

Hach Mailing List Change Requests
If you would like to add your name to our general mailing list for product and company news, or wish to remove or modify your information, please complete and submit our Mail List Form.
Thank you!
