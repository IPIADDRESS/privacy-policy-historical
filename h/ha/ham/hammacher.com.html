Secure Shopping
Protecting your order information is a priority. We utilize SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) to ensure safe, secure transactions. With SSL, information sent via the Internet is encrypted, and can be trusted to arrive privately and unaltered. We believe this element provides the most effective way to ensure that your order information is protected while offering a very secure shopping environment and the most effective way to ensure that your order information is protected.

Please note that email is not encrypted and is not considered a secure means of transmitting credit card numbers. If you still have any concerns about ordering electronically, please call 1-800-321-1484 to place your order over the phone.

Your Privacy Online
At Hammacher Schlemmer, we are committed to ensuring your privacy as a customer. We do utilize "cookies" on our site to retain items you place in your shopping cart through multiple sessions or visits. We also use cookies to give credit to those web sites that direct traffic to hammacher.com for marketing purposes only. Customer information is used strictly to process your order and to improve your shopping experience. We send an email message confirming the receipt of each online order and, if you have specifically requested email updates, we will periodically contact you with news about products and services that may be of interest to you. We do not sell or make available our customer email list to anyone.
