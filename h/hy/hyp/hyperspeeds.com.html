Privacy Policy

Website Visitors

Like most website operators, hyperspeeds.com collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. hyperspeeds.com purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how hyperspeeds's visitors use its website. From time to time, hyperspeeds.com may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

hyperspeeds.com also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses. hyperspeeds.com does not use such information to identify its visitors, however, and does not disclose such information, other than under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information, as described below.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain visitors to hyperspeeds.com websites choose to interact with hyperspeeds.com in ways that require hyperspeeds.com to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that hyperspeeds.com gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who use our forums to provide a username and email address. In each case, hyperspeeds.com collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor’s interaction with hyperspeeds.com. hyperspeeds.com does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information

hyperspeeds.com discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors, and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on hyperspeeds.com behalf or to provide services available at hyperspeeds's websites, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Some of those employees, contractors and affiliated organizations may be located outside of your home country; by using hyperspeeds's websites, you consent to the transfer of such information to them. hyperspeeds.com will not rent or sell potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information to anyone. Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. hyperspeeds.com uses cookies to help hyperspeeds.com identify and track visitors, their usage of hyperspeeds.com website, and their website access preferences. hyperspeeds.com visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using hyperspeeds.com's websites, with the drawback that certain features of hyperspeeds.com's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
