Along other sites, HyperComments also uses “cookies” to collect such information. A cookie is an essential data file that is stored on your computer HD for record-keeping purposes allowing saving your registration ID and login password for prospective logins to HyperComments Site; “session ID cookies” enable certain features of the Site providing authentication, identification of a user session, session contents and some other preferences.

You can tweak your browser by changing its options to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the websites you visit. If you do not accept cookies, however, you may not be able to use some of the Site’s features or all the functionalities of our services.
