We may also use information that we collect to improve the Site and the Application, and for our analyses. We may remove the elements of your information that could identify you. We may use and disclose the resulting “de-identified” information without restrictions. For example, we may create de-identified, statistical, and aggregate data to prepare reports about the Application that do not identify any individual users. We may also use personally identifiable information:
    1. to establish your identity and obtain contact information for you;
    2. to protect against error and fraud;

    4. to develop and manage this Site and the Application;

    6. to understand and respond to your needs and preferences, including to contact and communicate with you and to conduct surveys, research and evaluations;
    7. to prevent potentially prohibited or illegal activity, and to enforce this Site’s Terms of Use;
    8. to develop, enhance, market, sell or otherwise provide this Site’s products and services;
    9. as permitted by, and to comply with, any legal (including contractual) or regulatory requirements or provisions; and
    10. for any other purposes to which you consent.
  1. Disclosure of Personal Information. We may share personally identifiable information about you with the following parties for the purposes described below:
    1. with our contractors and service providers who assist us in managing, administering or delivering the Application. We will require such parties to protect and govern the use of the information about you according to this Privacy Policy and other requirements that we place upon them by contract.

    3. in connection with the sale, assignment, or other transfer of the business, we may transfer the information about you, in which case we will require any such buyer or acquiring party to agree to treat the information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

      2. report suspected illegal or fraudulent activity, prevent physical harm or financial loss, and to investigate violations of this Privacy Policy, or the Site’s terms of use.

    We will not sell or otherwise give information about you to other parties for their marketing purposes.

  2. Cookies / Passive Information Collection. This Site may use “cookies” to collect non-personally identifiable information, such as the type of Internet browser and operating system you use, the domain name of the website from which you came, date and duration of the visit, number of visits, average time spent on this Site, pages viewed and number of cookies accumulated. Cookies are a feature of web browser software that allows web servers to recognize the computer used to access a website. In addition to the identified purposes described in this Privacy Policy, we may use this Site’s information and share it with other organizations with whom we have a commercial relationship to measure the use of this Site, to improve the functionality and content of the Site and to facilitate usage by you. You can reset your browser either to notify you when you have received a cookie or refuse to accept cookies. However, if you refuse to accept cookies, you may not be able to use some of the features available on this Site. Online Communications: In order to provide you with products, services or information, you may voluntarily submit personal information through this Site for purposes such as conducting business online, asking a question, obtaining information, reviewing or downloading a publication, participating in an event, and participating in contests and surveys. If you are known to us as a registered user of an online service, we may combine and store personal information about your use of this Site and the online information you have provided with certain other online and offline information already collected. All collection, use and disclosure of personal information obtained through this Site shall be subject to this Privacy Policy and the applicable privacy laws.
  1. Cross-Border Transfer. Your personally identifiable information is stored in Canada, and may be stored and processed in any other country where we have service providers. By using the Application or by providing consent to us (where required by law), you agree to the transfer of information to countries outside of your country of residence, including to the United States, which may provide for different data protection rules than in your country.

We will retain such personally identifiable information for a period of at least seven (7) years after the completion of the Application’s services to you in order to assist you in reviewing your historical use information, to enable us to provide more comprehensive security, and for other lawful purposes.

You may request details of personally identifiable information, for which we may charge a reasonable fee.
