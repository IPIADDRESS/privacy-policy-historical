Privacy

The only personally identifying piece of information that hyperWALLET stores is your e-mail address. All of your personal financial information is held at your bank, unless you choose to store information in your wallet. Of course, your e-Tailer will require shipping information or may require you to establish an account with them - that information is stored on their servers, not in your hyperWALLET. But we guarantee that they will not have access to any information related to your hyperWALLET (other than to confirm that your purchase has been paid for). If you elect to accept or make credit card payments (only available to eBay Auction users), you will be required to provide information to Beanstream Internet Commerce Inc., our credit card processing partner.

Find out more by viewing our Privacy Policy.

Find out more about Beanstream's Privacy Policy.

Security

hyperWALLET uses the strongest security technologies available to safeguard the confidentiality of your information. Our secure servers sit behind our firewalls, and use 128-bit SSL encryption, the same security standard used by most financial institutions. As long as you keep your Wallet ID number and password private, no one will be able to access your wallet. Find out more by viewing our security measures.

INVOICE STATUS DEFINITIONS
UNPAID: This invoice has been issued and no payment has been made.

INVOICE STATUS DEFINITIONS
PAID-SHIP: This invoice was paid and the funds are secured. The seller can SHIP the merchandise. If this invoice was paid with CASH, the funds are available immediately for CashOut. If this invoice was paid with a credit card, the funds will be available for CashOut in 5 business days from the Payment Date.

INVOICE STATUS DEFINITIONS
PAID- DO NOT SHIP: This invoice was paid with CASH, but hyperWALLET has not verified the funds with the buyer's financial institution. Verification usually occurs in 1 business day. hyperWALLET will automatically notify the seller to SHIP the merchandise after the funds are secured.

INVOICE STATUS DEFINITIONS
CANCELLED - BY SELLER: The seller has decided to cancel this invoice before payment was made. Only the Seller can cancel UNPAID invoices.

INVOICE STATUS DEFINITIONS
CANCELLED - NSF: A payment using CASH could not be verified due to non-sufficient funds (NSF). The hyperWALLET system has recalled the payment and cancelled the invoice.
