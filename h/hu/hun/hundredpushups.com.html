If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at steve.speirs@gmail.com.

Log Files

Like many other Web sites, www.hundredpushups.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track users movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons

www.hundredpushups.com does not use cookies.

DoubleClick DART Cookie

  • Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on www.hundredpushups.com.
  • Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to your users based on their visit to www.hundredpushups.com and other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL - www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

Other technologies ( such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons ) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see.

www.hundredpushups.com has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. www.hundredpushups.com's privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.

About Hundred Pushups

Hundred Pushups is the ultimate program to train the body to go from just one pushup to 100 consecutive reps in less than two months -- sculpting muscles in the chest, abs, back, glutes and arms without a single piece of unwieldy equipment.
