Privacy policy

We are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This commitment guides our handling of any personal data we collect from you, or that you provide us with.

The location function used in our smartphone apps is only used to provide you with easy access to your current location on the map.

In the instances when you have to register for one of our services, this is to let us provide personalized services and communicate separately with you. Your information will never be shared with a third-party.

We store all user information in secure databases protected via a variety of access controls. This data is accessed only for the purposes specified in this privacy policy.

Like most web sites, we use small bits of data called cookies stored on users' computers. Cookies are parcels of text sent by a server to a web browser and then sent back unchanged by the client each time it accesses that server. Cookies are saved on your computer and do not harm or damage your system in any way. Furthermore they make your navigation easier as they save your settings. For example, if you set an aircraft filter and leave the website, the cookie will remember this so that the filter is enabled next time you return. You are able to view and control cookies through your web browser.

Flightradar24 and its partners use cookies to deliver targeted advertisements on an anonymous basis- we do not pass on any personal data to 3rd parties. Information gathered by cookies may be shared with our advertising partners to help measure the effectiveness of an advertising campaign.

By using Flightradar24 you are giving consent to cookies being used.

If you have any questions regarding Flightradar24 and your privacy, please contact us.
