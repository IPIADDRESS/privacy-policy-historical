Advertising and content served through Flip Media technology.
Flip Media does not collect any personally-identifying information before, during or after the delivery of our advertisements, nor from the websites on which those advertisements appear. Flip Media's technology may use non-personally identifying general information related to campaign results such as total number of delivered impressions (ads), click-through, etc.

On some occasions, an advertiser may request that Flip Media provides or assists in the data collection of information supplied voluntarily within the body of Flip Media ads, which may include personal information fields filled by the user. When this occurs, Flip Media only does so on the behalf of that advertiser. This information is understood by Flip Media to be used solely by that advertiser for the purposes identified within the advertisement to provide the user with requested goods, services, or information and as established within that advertiser's privacy policy. Flip Media is not responsible for the advertisers' use of this voluntarily supplied data. Flip Media does not distribute this information to any party other than those indicated within the advertisement. Flip Media never associates any of this information with any data gathered or used in the delivery of that advertisement. This information is provided directly to the advertiser, and is not collected or kept by either Flip Media or the publisher's site on which the advertisement is displayed
