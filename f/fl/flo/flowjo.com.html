We Respect Your Privacy
Our site is cookie free.
We do not pass along your personal information to 3rd parties.

What We Use Your Personal Information For
We use your personal information (names, e-mail addresses, phone numbers, addresses, billing information) strictly for billing purposes during the time of purchase. We collect and store the information securely. Sometimes we may need to contact you about product updates, account or order status updates.

Automatic Information Retrieval and Logging
We log our visitors’ IP addresses as well as browser version number. This aids us in developing the website to better serve our visitors.

The FlowJo Licensing Server (FLS) is our "Site License." Multiple computers at a single site connect to us for authorization. FLS collects minimal usage statistics on the connected computers; time logged on, number of engine requests. No personal or financial information is gathered.
