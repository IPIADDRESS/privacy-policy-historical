      Teens cannot fly unaccompanied on flights to or from Italy.

      Young passengers will be accepted on Stobart Air flights, on the basis that they are travelling as an adult. The parent or guardian will be required to sign a Form of Indemnity at the airport on the day of departure relinquishing Stobart Air and the relevant franchise partner from any responsibility to provide special assistance to these passengers. Stobart Air shall not permit any unaccompanied young person between the age of 12 and 15 inclusive to travel if the parent or guardian fails to complete this form. A Form of Indemnity shall be signed for both sectors of the journey if a return flight is booked
      Trying to book for an unaccompanied child? Visit our Children and Infants Page.
      Please note:
      When not travelling with an adult of at least 16 years of age, some children can be carried under the Unaccompanied Minor Scheme* on flybe operated flights.

      The Flybe Unaccompanied Minor Scheme* is not available to children under 15 years of age on flights operated by Air France and subsidiaries Hop Regional/City Jet. These must be booked directly with the operating airline.* On flights operated by Air France and subsiduaries, children under 15 travelling on Flybe tickets must be accompanied by an adult.
      The Flybe Unaccompanied Minor Scheme* is not available on flights operated to and through Knock - Ireland West (NOC).
