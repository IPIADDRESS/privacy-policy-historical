Your privacy is important to us.

Dana Air has created this Privacy Policy in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy.
  1. Collection and Use of Information
    We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our website. Your IP address is used to help identify you and to gather broad demographic information. We use customer contact information from the tickets booked to send the user information about our company. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor when necessary. Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site.

    We use this data to tailor the visitor's experience at our site, showing them content that we think they might be interested in, and displaying the content according to their preferences. The collection and processing of Customer Data enables us to provide services and products that are most likely to meet your needs and requirements. Dana Air will retain the Customer Data for as long as it is necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by relevant laws.
