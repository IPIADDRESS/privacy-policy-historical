Privacy Policy

 

 

 

 

Your privacy is important to us.

Dana Air has created this Privacy Policy in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy.

 

 

1)             Collection and Use of Information 

 

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our website.  Your IP address is used to help identify you and to gather broad demographic information. We use customer contact information from the tickets booked to send the user information about our company.  The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor when necessary.  Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site.  We use this data to tailor the visitor's experience at our site, showing them content that we think they might be interested in, and displaying the content according to their preferences. The collection and processing of Customer Data enables us to provide services and products that are most likely to meet your needs and requirements.   Dana Air will retain the Customer Data for as long as it is necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by relevant laws.

Debit/Credit card information: We do not collect any card details. The customer is redirected to the payment gateway for authorizing the card information. We accept payments through Verified by Visa enabled VISA debit/credit cards. This is an added security service for VISA card holders.

Dana Air, by any chance, never shares the customer information, whatsoever, with any vendor, outside or third party agents.

 

2)             Links to other websites 

 

This site contains links to other sites.  Dana Air is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites. These links are there to provide any additional information required by the customer.

 

3)             Security Measures

 

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. We have implemented procedural and technical safeguards to ensure that the information that you submit to us will be secure, safe and/or is password protected to prevent misuse.
