Privacy and Security Statement
 

Warning:
  • This is a U.S. Federal government computer system that is "FOR OFFICIAL USE ONLY."
  • This system is subject to monitoring. No expectation of privacy is to be assumed (See below).
  • Individuals found performing unauthorized activities are subject to disciplinary action including criminal prosecution.
 

PRIVAY ACT ROUTINE USES (5 USC § 522a AS AMENDED)

Disclosure (or providing) of information requested in forms contained within this system or provided via email contact is “voluntary.” By providing the information, the individual assumes all responsibility to ensure the information provided is correct and valid and is so maintained.

  • General Public Information: Email addresses will be used to respond to your requests. This information may be forwarded to Government employees who are better able to answer your questions.
  • Vendor Information: Vendor-provided information, including email addresses and company information, will be used to forward items of interest to the email address provided. Vendor-provided information may also be used by Federal contracting officials to develop and publish Interested Vendor Lists for vendor teaming opportunities on solicitations posted on FBO. (See the FBO Vendors Guide.)
  • Federal Users: Federal user information, including name, telephone number and email address, is used by this system to set up FBO buyer accounts for Federal procurement officials authorized to manage, administer and/or perform FBO eCommerce activities. FBO buyer accounts support buyer authentication and access controls for FBO buyer functions and provide point-of-contact information for FBO postings. (See to the FBO Buyers Guide.)
Disclosure: Collected information on individuals may be shared and protected as necessary for authorized law enforcement, homeland security and national security activities. Except for such authorized activities, we do not share any information we receive with any outside parties.

If you have any questions about these policies, please feel free to contact us at fbo.support@gsa.gov or toll-free at 877-472-3779.
