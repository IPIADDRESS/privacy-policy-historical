Privacy Policy

Aron & Sharon (“Aron & Sharon“) operates fbgroupautoposter.com and may operate other websites. It is Aron & Sharon’s policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our websites.

Website Visitors

Like most website operators, Aron & Sharon collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Aron & Sharon’s purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Aron & Sharon’s visitors use its website. From time to time, Aron & Sharon may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

Aron & Sharon also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on fbgroupautoposter.com blogs/sites. Aron & Sharon only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below, except that commenter IP addresses and email addresses are visible and disclosed to the administrators of the blog/site where the comment was left.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain visitors to Aron & Sharon’s websites choose to interact with Aron & Sharon in ways that require Aron & Sharon to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that Aron & Sharon gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up at fbgroupautoposter.com to provide a username and email address. Those who engage in transactions with Aron & Sharon are asked to provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to process those transactions. In each case, Aron & Sharon collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor’s interaction with Aron & Sharon. Aron & Sharon does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

Aggregated Statistics

Aron & Sharon may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. Aron & Sharon may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, Aron & Sharon does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information

Aron & Sharon discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on Aron & Sharon’s behalf or to provide services available at Aron & Sharon’s websites, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Some of those employees, contractors and affiliated organizations may be located outside of your home country; by using Aron & Sharon’s websites, you consent to the transfer of such information to them. Aron & Sharon will not rent or sell potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information to anyone. Other than to its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations, as described above, Aron & Sharon discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only in response to a subpoena, court order or other governmental request, or when Aron & Sharon believes in good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the property or rights of Aron & Sharon, third parties or the public at large. Aron & Sharon takes all measures reasonably necessary to protect against the unauthorized access, use, alteration or destruction of potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information.

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Aron & Sharon uses cookies to help Aron & Sharon identify and track visitors, their usage of Aron & Sharon website, and their website access preferences. Aron & Sharon visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Aron & Sharon’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of Aron & Sharon’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

If Aron & Sharon, or substantially all of its assets, were acquired, or in the unlikely event that Aron & Sharon goes out of business or enters bankruptcy, user information would be one of the assets that is transferred or acquired by a third party. You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer of Aron & Sharon may continue to use your personal information as set forth in this policy.

Ads

Ads appearing on any of our websites may be delivered to users by advertising partners, who may set cookies. These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement to compile information about you or others who use your computer. This information allows ad networks to, among other things, deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by Aron & Sharon and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.
