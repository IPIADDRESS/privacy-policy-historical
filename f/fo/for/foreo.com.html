PRIVACY POLICY

SUMMARY

FOREO is committed to protecting the privacy of all information you may provide. We maintain strict policies and security measures to protect our customers.

When you sign up for FOREO’s mailing list, make a warranty claim or use our online shop, you give us your consent to process and use your personal data in accordance with the terms and conditions of this privacy policy.

INFORMATION COLLECTED ONLINE

FOREO will collect, through online forms, personal information from website visitors who choose to sign up for FOREO’s mailing list, make a warranty claim or use our online shop. If you are under 18 years old, please do not submit any personal details.
FOREO will not collect any personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 18.

To improve our services and enhance your FOREO experience, certain other anonymized information is collected for statistical purposes. This information is not personally identifiable and includes measuring the number of visitors to the various sections of our website and identifying system performance or technical problem areas. The following information is automatically collected about your visit:

The name of the website from which you have linked to foreo.com.

The domain you use to access the Internet.

The country from which your visit to our website originated.

The web browser and operating system you use to view web pages.

The date and time that you visited foreo.com.

THIS WEBSITE USES GOOGLE ADWORDS

This website uses the Google AdWords remarketing service to advertise on third party websites (including Google) to previous visitors to our site. It could mean that we advertise to previous visitors who haven’t completed a task on our site, for example using the contact form to make an enquiry. This could be in the form of an advertisement on the Google search results page, or a site in the Google Display Network. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on someone’s past visits to the foreo.com website. Of course, any data collected will be used in accordance with our own privacy policy and Google’s privacy policy.

COOKIES

Foreo.com utilizes cookies for statistical purposes, to determine which pages are visited and how often, as well as to improve your online experience.

Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you do not have to accept them. You may disable cookies in your web browser’s settings; the user manual that came with your browser software will help you to do this. However, please bear in mind that your visit to foreo.com will be significantly enhanced if cookies are enabled. For more information about cookies, please visit www.allaboutcookies.org.

HOW FOREO USES YOUR INFORMATION

If you choose to sign up for FOREO’s mailing list or make a warranty claim, the personal information you provide will be entered into our customer database. This information will be used to administer, simplify and improve our online services to you, and to help process any orders you place with our online shop.

We will email you FOREO info, offers and news only if you authorize us to do so.

WILL MY INFORMATION BE SHARED WITH OTHERS?

In order to make online purchase deliveries and maintain our website, FOREO contracts with selected third parties. These third parties may process your personal information under strict contractual confidentiality agreements, in order to assist FOREO in providing a quality online service.

Otherwise, FOREO will under no circumstances disclose, sell or transfer any personal information (names, addresses, email addresses, etc.) to a third party, except as may be required by law.

SITE SECURITY

The FOREO website uses 128-bit encryption to protect all personal and / or credit card information you may submit online. However, while we take all reasonable security measures to protect your information, by using our website you accept that no
online data transmission can be 100% secure from access by unauthorized parties, and will therefore not hold us responsible for any breach of security unless this is due to our negligence.

ACCEPTANCE OF PRIVACY POLICY

When you choose to access this website, to sign up for FOREO’s mailing list, make a warranty claim or use our online shop, you consent to our collecting and using your information as described in this privacy policy.

FOREO reserves the right to change and / or update these privacy policies or practices. To keep yourself up to date, you should occasionally review our privacy policy, especially when you use the FOREO online shop.

CONTACT

If you have any questions concerning our privacy policy, please contact us by filling out the Help Desk form.
