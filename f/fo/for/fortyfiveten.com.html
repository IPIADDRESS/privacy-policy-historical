We collect Personal Information you voluntarily provide us, for example by signing up to receive our newsletters. The Personal Information we collect on the Site may include, without limitation, your: name, mailing address, and email address. You may refuse to provide Personal Information, but some parts of the Site (for example, the newsletter signup page) may not be fully available or functional without submitting such information.

If you are younger than age 18, you may use the Site only with the permission and involvement of your parent or guardian. If we discover that we have collected Personal Information from children under 13, we will take commercially reasonable measures to promptly delete such information from our systems.

Anonymous Data we collect includes, without limitation, information regarding your IP address, device type, operating system, browser, and other Site usage details such as page views, clicks, and duration of page visits. The Site may also include tracking technologies such as “cookies” that automatically helps us provide a tailored experience when you visit the Site.
