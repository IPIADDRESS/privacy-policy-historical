PRIVACY POLICY

This site is maintained by Forsyth Technical Community College, 2100 Silas Creek Parkway, Winston-Salem, NC 27103; 336-723-0371.

Your privacy is important to us and we respect your concerns. Any information that you share with Forsyth Tech is kept in the strictest confidence and is used only to respond to you if you request a response, and to supply you with the information that you have requested.

We do not collect personally identifiable information on our web site unless you choose to give it to us via e-mail, phone call or postal mail. The information supplied by you is used only in the normal scope of business here at Forsyth Tech and you will not be contacted unless you specify that you wish to be contacted.

Any information that you supply via our online forms is used only in the purpose of the form itself.

Our server does not collect information on visitors, nor do we have "cookies" enabled on our site. We do not share information that you supply to us with any other organization or entity.

If you have questions about our privacy policy that are not addressed here, please e-mail our webmaster.
