Email addresses are used for the purposes of inviting new friends to join TakePart, to add users to members' friends' networks, and to send notifications related to the Service and TakePart. With the exception of inviting friends, adding friends, and notifications, a member’s email address is not shared or displayed to people within a personal network.     Cookies are small files containing a string of characters sent to your computer that uniquely identifies your browser. TakePart uses cookies to store visitors' preferences and to record session information, for purposes including customizing newsletter, advertising, and Web page content based on browser type and user profile information. We do not link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information you submit while on the Site. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies, but you can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, you must enable cookies from TakePart in order to use most functions on the Site.
