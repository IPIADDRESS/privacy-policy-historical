Privacy Policy Notice

Fox Searchlight Pictures recognizes the privacy concerns of visitors to its site on the Internet. Specifically, we recognize the importance of protecting the privacy of personally identifiable information collected about our visitors. As a general policy, no personally identifiable information, such as your name, address or email address, is automatically collected from visitors to our site. We may also use non-personal information to determine the total number of visitors to our website.

Personally-identifiable information about our visitors is collected only when knowingly and voluntarily submitted. In those instances when we do collect such information, the following applies:

Notice

We will notify you when we are collecting personally identifiable information about you, as well as the uses we intend to make of that information. For example, we may collect such information to provide you with information or product you requested, in connection with contest entry forms or for billing purposes.

Use and Security

Personally identifiable information that is submitted knowingly and voluntarily by visitors to our sites is retained by Fox Searchlight, and is not sold or otherwise transferred outside the company, unless otherwise stated at the time of collection, in which case you'll have the option of instructing us not to share your personal information with third parties. Fox Searchlight maintains appropriate safeguards to ensure the security, integrity and privacy of such information. In addition to using information about you to respond to your specific requests, we may use it in aggregate form to make our site better and more responsive to the needs of our visitors. This statistical compilation of non-identifiable information may also be used by Fox Searchlight or provided to others for marketing, advertising or research purposes.

Children Under 18 Years of Age

Fox searchlight is not targeted to individuals under 18 years of age. Where appropriate, Fox Searchlight will specifically instruct individuals under 18 years of age not to submit personally identifiable information (i.e., full name, address, telephone number or email address) to our website without the prior consent of a parent or guardian. Fox Searchlight will not transfer, share or sell personally identifiable information it knowingly receives from individuals under 18 to any third party for any purposes whatsoever without prior parental consent.

When you view our web site, we may store some information on your computer. This information will be in the form of a 'Cookie' or similar file and will help us in many ways. For example, Cookies allow us to tailor a web site to better match your interests and preferences. Please refer to your Browser instructions or help screen to learn more about these functions.
