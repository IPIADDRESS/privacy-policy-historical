    How This Policy Applies

    This Privacy Policy describes the personal information we collect on our websites when you place an order or sign up for our email list, the anonymous information that we collect as you browse our website, why we collect it, and our legal basis for doing so. It also covers the choices available to you regarding our use of your personal information and how you can access and update this information. Because Fun.com, Inc. is a US-based company, your information will be collected, processed, and stored in the United States. The United States has its own laws governing data protection and government access to information. The rules that protect your personal information under United States law may be different than in your home country. When using any of our services you consent to the collection, transfer, disclosure, storage, and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

    If you have an unresolved privacy or data use concern that we have not addressed, or if you have any questions or feedback, please contact Fun.com, Inc. here.

    Information We Collect

    Information You Provide Directly to Us

    We collect the following personal information from you when you place an order:

    • Contact Information such as name, email address, shipping address, phone number
    • Billing Information such as billing address and credit card information
    • The company name of your business, when shipping to a business address

    We collect the following personal information from you when you sign up for an account:

    • Unique Identifiers such as user name, account number, password
    • Contact Information such as name, shipping address, phone number
    • Billing Information such as billing address

    We collect the following personal information from you when you sign up for our product notifications or marketing emails:

    • Email address

    We collect the following personal information from you when you contact our customer service department via the contact us form or live chat:

    • Name, email address, and order number

    You may decline to provide us with your information in any of these cases. However, this will limit your ability to register for an account, receive emails, or to place an order.

    Website Analytics

    As is true of most websites, we automatically gather information about your computer such as your browser type, referring/exit pages, operating system, and your interaction with different pages of our website. We do not link this automatically collected data to other information we collect about you.

    Cookies, Tracking, and Advertising

    Like many websites, we use cookies and similar technologies in order to provide our shopping experience and also to track and improve our services. Technologies such as: cookies, beacons, tags and scripts are utilized by Fun.com, Inc. and the third-party analytics platforms that we use to optimize your shopping experience. These platforms include:

    • Bronto (our email provider)
    • Certona (product recommendations we show on our website)
    • Google Analytics (tracks how customers use our website)

    Additionally, we partner with third-parties to manage our advertising on other sites. Our third-party partners may use technologies such as cookies to gather information about your activities on our website and other sites in order to provide you advertising based upon your browsing activities and interests. These platforms include:

    • Adroll
    • Bing Remarketing
    • Facebook Remarketing
    • Google Adwords Remarketing
    • Google Dynamic Remarketing

    Finally, our website includes Social Media features, such as the Facebook Like button, and widgets, such as the Share This button or interactive mini-programs that run on our website. These features may collect your IP address, which page you are visiting on our website, and may set a cookie to enable the feature to function properly.

    How We Use This Information

    Providing Products/Services

    We only use the information you provide to us for the specific purpose which you provided it to us – for example, we will not send you marketing emails because you initiated a live chat with customer service. We will use your information to:

    • Fulfill your order
    • Send you an order confirmation email
    • Send you a shipping notification email
    • Administer your account or process returns and refunds
    • Send you product or service information emails, if requested
    • Send you marketing communications, if requested
    • Respond to your questions and concerns
    • Improve our website and marketing efforts
    • Display content based upon your interests

    How We Use Website Analytics Data

    We have implemented Google Analytics, Google Display Advertising, and Google Analytics Demographics and Interest Reporting on our website to help us understand how visitors use our services. These tools use cookies to gather standard Internet log information and user behavior information in an anonymous form. The information created by the cookie about your use of the website is transmitted to Google and is then used to evaluate visitors' use of the website and to compile aggregate statistical reports on website activity for our site specifically. We have followed Google's best practice for IP Anonymization to ensure that no personally-identifiable information will be sent to Google Analytics. See https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/2763052?hl=en for more detail.

    We will never (nor will we allow any third-party to) use the statistical analytics tool to track or to collect any Personal Information of visitors to our website. Google will not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google, nor will Google link, or seek to link, an IP address with the identity of a computer user. We will not associate any data gathered from this site with any Personal Information from any source, unless you explicitly submit that information via a fill-in form on our website.

    If you would like to opt out of being tracked using Google Analytics on any website, then simply head over to Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on and install the add-on in your browsers.

    How We Use Cookies, Tracking Data, and Advertising

    We use cookies to keep track of user shopping carts, to remember our user's preferences (e.g. language and country preference), and for security/login authentication. These cookies are required in order for our website to function correctly and for you to place an order.

    The third-party analytics platforms that we use on our website may set cookies on your computer. We utilize these technologies to analyze shopping and buying trends, to track users' interactions with the website, and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole in order to provide an optimized shopping experience and relevant product recommendations. We may receive reports based on the use of these technologies by these companies on an individual as well as aggregated basis. These cookies are optional; our website will function and you can place an order, though the content you see on the website may not be as relevant.

    Our third-party advertising partners may use technologies such as cookies or beacons to gather information about your activities on our website. This information is used when you leave our website and visit a site that uses the same advertising partner. That website will then display product advertising to you based upon your browsing activities and interests while you were on our website. If you wish to not have this information used for the purpose of serving you interest-based ads, you may opt-out by clicking here. Please note this does not opt you out of being served ads. You will continue to receive generic ads.

    The Social Media features and widgets on our website are hosted by a third-party and may use cookies or beacons. These third-party cookies are set by the individual Social Media service and are used to provide the functionality of the feature as well as collect any additional tracking data. Please note that we do not send your personal information to these Social Media widgets, but they still have access to your web browsing information such as browser, operating system, and IP address. Your interactions with these features are governed by the privacy policy of the company providing it.

    Your browser may give you the ability to control the cookies that are set on your computer. If you block or delete cookies, you may still use our site, but your ability to use some features or areas of our site may be limited. To find out more about how to enable, disable, or delete cookies from your web browser, please visit here.

    How This Information Is Shared

    We will share your personal information with third-parties only in the ways that are described in this Privacy Policy. We will never sell your personal information to third-parties.

    Information Shared Publicly

    Our website offers a publicly accessible blog or community forums. You should be aware that any information you provide in these areas may be read, collected, and used by others who access it. The blog is moderated and you may have comments made by yourself removed by contacting us.

    Any time you write a product review, the details of the review and the manner of which you identified yourself will be publicly accessible to anyone who views our product pages. Any information you provide may be read, collected, used, or responded to by others who access it. Product reviews are moderated and you may have reviews or comments made by yourself removed by contacting us.

    All other information you provide to us will not be shared publicly.

    Fulfilling Your Order

    We may provide your personal information to trusted third-party companies that provide services to fulfill your order, such as fraud detection and investigation, credit card processing, and order shipping. These companies are authorized to use your personal information only as necessary to provide these services to us. These services include:

    • ThreatMetrix (fraud protection)
    • Signifyd (fraud protection)
    • PayPal (credit card processing)
    • Amazon Pay (credit card processing)
    • Matrix Payments (credit card processing)
    • Sezzle (“pay later” payment method)
    • FedEx (address validation and shipping service)
    • UPS (address validation and shipping service)
    • USPS (shipping service)

    Affiliate Tracking

    When you come to our website via a link on one of our affiliates' websites, we are obliged to notify them of the transaction so that they can be paid a commission – this is known as conversion tracking. Your personal information is not transferred to these affiliates. Some of our affiliates include:

    • AvantLink
    • Commission Junction
    • LinkShare
    • Share-A-Sale

    Third-Party Hosting Provider

    Our ordering system and shopping cart database is hosted on server hardware supplied by our IT solutions provider. When we collect your shipping and billing information directly from you for the purpose of processing your order, it is saved on this hardware. Our solution provider does not have access to view your data but does perform administrative functions on the server hardware used to collect it.

    Links to Other Websites

    Our website includes links to other websites whose privacy practices may differ from those of the websites owned by Fun.com, Inc. If you submit personal information to any of those sites, your information is governed by their privacy policy. We encourage you to carefully read the privacy policy of any website you visit.

    Additional Disclosure

    We may also disclose your personal information

    • as required by law such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process, or respond to a government request,
    • when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or in the case of possible criminal acts or security threats,
    • if Fun.com, Inc. is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, you will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on our website of any change in ownership or uses of your personal information, as well as any choices you may have regarding your personal information,
    • to any other third-party with your prior consent to do so

    Correcting And Updating Your Personal Information

    To review, delete or update your personal information to ensure it is accurate or if you no longer desire our service, please sign in to the My Account area of the website and use the self-service tools, or you may contact us.

    You may choose to stop receiving our newsletter or marketing emails by following the unsubscribe instructions included in these emails, you can sign in to the My Account area of the website and use the self-service tools, or you may contact us. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

    Security

    We take security seriously, and the security of your personal data is important to us. When we collect personal information directly from you, we follow industry-standard practices to protect the data, both during transmission and once we receive it – including full-site SSL. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, however. Therefore we cannot guarantee its absolute security. If you have any questions about security on our website, you can contact us.

    We provide two means for you to make a purchase on our site. You can either use our order form or a third-party payment option (PayPal; Amazon Pay; Sezzle). If you use our shopping cart, the transmission of sensitive information (such as credit card information) collected on our order form is encrypted using secure socket layer technology (SSL). If you choose to use a third-party payment option to pay for your order, its privacy policy and security practices will apply to your information. We encourage you to read that privacy policy before providing your information.

    In the event of a data breach, we have a protocol in place for handling the breach and notifying customers and the supervisory authority. We do not store any credit card information when your card is authorized during checkout, so there isn't a chance of your card information being stolen in a data breach after your order is placed.

    Data Retention

    We will retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you wish to cancel your account or request that we no longer use your information to provide you services, please contact us. We will retain and use your information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

    We also collect and maintain aggregated, anonymized information which we may retain indefinitely to protect the safety and security of our website, improve our services, or comply with legal obligations.

    California Residents: Your California Privacy Rights

    Under California law, our customers that are California residents may request certain information regarding our disclosure of personal information to third-parties for their direct marketing purposes. As detailed above, we do not share our customer's personal information with unaffiliated third-parties for direct marketing purposes without their approval.

    If at any time you wish to opt out of having your personal information shared with third-parties and/or receiving information from Fun.com, Inc. (via e-mail or postal mail) about new products, services, or promotions, you can do so. You can contact us through our contact form. Please indicate what publications or services you no longer wish to receive so that we may better address your needs.

    Children’s Online Privacy

    Our website is not marketed to, nor do we knowingly collect or store personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If we learn that we have collected or received personal information from a child under the age of 13 without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information as quickly as possible. If you believe that we might have any information from or about a child under the age of 13, please contact us immediately.

    European Union Residents

    At Fun.com, Inc. we take your privacy seriously and have taken steps to comply with the requirements defined in the European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) that goes into effect on May 25th, 2018. Many of the requirements are outlined in different areas of this Privacy Policy, but this section highlights items that are unique or specific to our European audience.

    Lawful Basis

    Data protection law in Europe requires a lawful basis for collecting and retaining personal information from citizens or residents of the European Economic Area. Our lawful bases include:

    • Contract: in order to fulfil the contract we have with you to provide the product/service that you requested, we need to use your personal data to charge your payment and to ship goods to you.
    • Legal obligation: we may need your data to comply with customs requests or to maintain tax audit records.
    • Legitimate interests: we have a good and fair reason to use your data in ways that are reasonably expected as part of running our business and that do not materially impact your rights, freedom, or interests. For example, we use identity, device, and location information to prevent fraud and to keep our services secure. And we analyze how users interact with our website so that we can better understand which features and design elements are working well and which are not working.

    Your Choices: Data Access, Correction, Deletion, and Portability

    We provide you with reasonable access to the personal data that you may have provided through your use of our services. If you wish to access, modify, delete, or obtain a copy of your information, you may do so at any time by logging in to the My Account section of our website to make changes, or by clicking on the Manage Preferences link in any email we've sent you to modify your preferences. Alternatively, you may contact us via email or live chat, or by phone or mail using the options listed in the Contact Information section of this privacy policy, and we will be happy to help you.

    Please note that while any changes you make will be reflected in the active user database instantly, we may retail some information for backups, archiving, prevention of fraud and abuse, analytics, satisfaction of legal obligations, for audit and compliance records, or where we otherwise reasonably believe that we have a legitimate reason to do so.

    Your Rights: Restricting Processing and Objection

    At any time prior to us shipping out your order, you may withdraw your consent to process your data by contacting us and we will cancel your order. Once an order has shipped we are unable to cancel the processing of your data, but you have the same right to access, correct, delete, or obtain a copy of your data at any time using our self-service tools or by contacting us. Additionally, you have the ability to opt-out from all performance, social media, and advertising cookies on our website by using the Cookie Settings link on the bottom of all pages of our website.

    At any time, you may object to the processing of your personal data, on legitimate grounds, except if otherwise permitted by applicable law. In most cases you are able to control these settings through our self-service tools, but as always you may contact us via email or live chat, or by phone or mail using the options listed in the Contact Information section of this privacy policy, and we will be happy to help you.
