SHOPFREEMART LLC Privacy Policy

When you use SHOPFREEMART LLC products, you trust us with your information. This Privacy Policy is meant to help you understand what data we collect, why we collect it, and what we do with it.

Privacy Policy
Last modified: 07, 24, 2016

As you use our services, we want you to be clear how we use the information that you provide in your member application and the ways in which you can protect your privacy.

Our Privacy Policy explains:
What information we collect and why we collect it.

How we use that information.
The choices we offer, including how to access and update information.
Your privacy matters to SHOPFREEMART LLC so please take time to become familiar with our policies – and if you have any questions contact us.

Information we collect
We collect information to provide better services to all of our users – from figuring out basic stuff like your name, email address, and mailing address and how to contact you in the even there is ever a question regarding your account.

We collect information in the following ways:
Information you give us. For example, your name, email address, telephone number or if you choose to store with your account. We do not collect any credit card information to store on our site. You must reenter your information with each new purchase you make.

Information we get from your use of our services. We may collect information your IP address, time and date stamp whenever you login to your back office with the purpose of preventing unauthorized persons from accessing your account. This information includes:

Device information
We collect device-specific information (such as your hardware model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information including phone number). SHOPFREEMART LLC may associate your device identifiers or phone number with your SHOPFREEMART LLC Account.

Log information
When you use our services or view content provided by SHOPFREEMART LLC we automatically collect and store certain information in server logs. This includes:
details of how you used our service, pages you visited, changes in personal information.

Location information
When you use SHOPFREEMART LLC services, we may collect and process information about your actual location. We use various technologies to determine location, including IP address, GPS, and other sensors that may, for example, provide SHOPFREEMART LLC with information on nearby devices, Wi-Fi access points and cell towers.

How we use information we collect
We use the information we collect from all of our services to provide, maintain, protect and improve them, to develop new ones, and to protect SHOPFREEMART LLC and our users.

As we add other services, we may use the name (username) that you provide for your SHOPFREEMART LLC Profile across all of the services we offer that require a SHOPFREEMART LLC Account.

If you upload a Profile photo, we may display your Profile name and Profile photo on personalize site and ad pages. We will respect the choices you make to limit sharing or visibility settings in your SHOPFREEMART LLC Account.

When you contact Freemart LLC, we keep a record of your communication to help solve any issues you might be facing. We may use your email address to inform you about our services, such as letting you know about upcoming changes, improvements.

SHOPFREEMART LLC executives, principals and employees agree to never appropriate or use names and or contact information of any SHOPFREEMART LLC member to be sold, used or contacted for any private or personal gain. Selling, appropriating or using member’s contact information outside of the limited uses outlined in these Policies is strictly prohibited and is considered a breach of contract on the part of the person committing such an act.

Transparency and choice
People have different privacy concerns. Our goal is to be clear about what information we collect, so that you can make meaningful choices about how it is used. For example, you can:
Review and update your Profile what information you want to have posted to your website.
Control who you share information with through your SHOPFREEMART LLC Account.
Choose whether your Profile name and Profile photo appear in promotional pages.

Accessing and updating your personal information
Whenever you use our services, we aim to provide you with access to your personal information. If that information is wrong, we strive to give you ways to update it quickly or to delete it – unless we have to keep that information for legitimate business or legal purposes. For your protection, when updating your personal information, we may ask you to verify your identity before we can act on your request.

We aim to maintain our services in a manner that protects information from accidental or malicious destruction.

Information we share
We do not share personal information with companies, organizations and individuals outside of SHOPFREEMART LLC unless we have your consent to do so. We require opt-in consent for the sharing of any sensitive personal information.

We provide personal information to our affiliates or other trusted businesses or persons to process it for us, based on our instructions and in compliance with our Privacy Policy and any other appropriate confidentiality and security measures.

We work hard to protect SHOPFREEMART LLC and our users from unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of information we hold. In particular:

We encrypt many of our services using SSL.
We offer you two step verification when you access your SHOPFREEMART LLC Account.
We review our information collection, storage and processing practices, including physical security measures, to guard against unauthorized access to systems.
We restrict access to personal information to SHOPFREEMART LLC employees, contractors and agents who need to know that information in order to process it for us, and who are subject to strict contractual confidentiality obligations and may be disciplined or terminated if they fail to meet these obligations.

When this Privacy Policy applies
This Privacy Policy applies to all members until termination of their membership.
This Privacy Policy applies to all principals and employees of SHOPFREEMART LLC for a period of twelve (24 months after termination of employment of service with SHOPFREEMART LLC.

Changes
Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time. We will not reduce your rights under this Privacy Policy without your explicit consent. We will post any privacy policy changes on this page and, if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice (including, for certain services, email notification of privacy policy changes).

We keep your personal information private and safe — and put you in control.
