The Privacy & Security of your Information is Our Top Priority

FunctionFox is firmly committed to maintaining and protecting the privacy of your personal and company information. Your data is safe and secure with us.

The terms of this policy apply to www.functionfox.com and all other FunctionFox sites. All files are strictly confidential and all information is classified. Our privacy policy tells you how we use information that identifies you personally, so that you can make an informed choice about using this site and its products.

What information do you collect about me?
  • Some information, such as the site that referred you to us, your IP and e-mail address, and navigational and purchase information, may be collected automatically as part of the site's operation. We use this information to generate user profiles and to personalize the web site to your particular interests.
  • On some occasions (such as when you purchase) we may ask for other information through online forms. The information may include: your username, password, full name, telephone number, e-mail address and postal address, your credit card number and expiry date, and other personally identifying information. We may also ask other questions from time to time. Remember that it is always your choice whether you provide this information, you are free to decline to answer any questions or fill out any forms.
  • The information FunctionFox collects is stored indefinitely, and it is used to process your transactions, provide customer service, develop products and services, conduct direct marketing and marketing analysis and make it easy for you to use our web site.
  • Unless you choose not to receive promotional materials from FunctionFox, we may notify you of special promotions, new products or services, or other information that might be of interest to you.
  • We reserve the right to disclose your personal information if we are required to do so by law or if we believe that disclosure is reasonably necessary to comply with legal process, respond to claims, or protect the rights, property or safety of our company, employees, customers, or the public.
  • By using this web site, you understand and agree that we may retain the information we collect from this site indefinitely, and that we may store and accessed it to contact you. You also agree that FunctionFox may use this information for direct marketing (unless you decline to receive direct marketing from us).
What are cookies and why do you use them?

When you visit the functionfox.com web site, we place a "cookie" in the browser directory of your computer's hard drive. A cookie is a tiny piece of information that a web site can store and later retrieve. It cannot be read by anyone else. Cookies enable our web site to recognize the information you have consented to provide and to help us determine what portions of this web site are most appropriate for you. Cookies recognize you and let you view your previous invoices.

We do not use cookies to examine your surfing behavior before or after leaving our web site.

Is my information safe?

FunctionFox maintains a secure online environment so you can safely provide information, purchase services, and access information. We have in place stringent security protocols to prevent the loss, misuse, or alteration of any information. We consider your information sensitive and private and we are committed to handling it with the utmost care. Your data is transmitted via Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) with the same level of encryption used by major credit card and financial institutions around the world. If you would like more information about our security procedures, see our Security page.

If you authorize us to send you promotional materials, we may do so using either the e-mail or the mailing addresses you provided on the billing information screens. From time to time we may notify you of special promotions, new products or services, or other information that may interest you. We do not share any information you provide to us, including your e-mail and mail addresses, with any companies outside FunctionFox except where indicated above.

Does FunctionFox look at or share my proprietary data?
  • No. FunctionFox does not view, analyze, share or otherwise access the proprietary data entered by any of its customers.
  • FunctionFox uses secure servers located off site, to which only our technicians have access.
  • Passwords are changed regularly to prevent unauthorized access by anyone to any information stored by FunctionFox for its customers.
  • We encouraged you — and all our customers to change passwords and usernames regularly as a security precaution.
  • The data storage facility has been designed to provide the highest level of security; protection against fire, flood and earthquake; and has full power backup.
  • Data is backed up off site regularly.
