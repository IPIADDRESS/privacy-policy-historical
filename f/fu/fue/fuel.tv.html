As set forth in our Terms of Use, Fuel TV is a general audience site, is not targeted to children, and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

The Policy describes the collection and use of users' personal information on the Fuel TV Services. It also describes generally Fuel TV's practices for handling non-personally identifiable information (e.g., interests, demographics and site usage) to the extent such information is associated with a particular user or user account. User Submitted Information

Registration Information is the information that users must provide to Fuel TV at registration for or upon subscription to a Fuel TV Service, for example, to create an account, enter contests, promotions or sweepstakes, receive a newsletter, play a game or take a survey, any may include, without limitation, name, email address, first and last name, city and zip code.

Public Post Login Information is the information that you will be required to provide if you elect to post or upload any videos or photos through The Daily Habit or on any of the public forums on the Site and includes your first and last name and email address.

Public Posts consist of your display name, username and any additional information you chose to make public through your posts and comments on the Fuel TV Services. Your Public Posts are always public, which means they are available to everyone and will be displayed in search results both on the Fuel TV Services and on external search engines.

We also may use cookies and other tracking technologies (including browser cookies, pixels, beacons, and Adobe Flash technology including cookies), which are comprised of small bits of data that often include an anonymous unique identifier.
