Privacy

Fujitsu General Limited and its affiliates (the "Fujitsu General Group") are committed to respecting your privacy. Fujitsu General Group actively supports current industry initiatives to preserve individual privacy rights on the Internet.

Fujitsu General Group recognizes the importance of the personal data by which you can be identified, such as your name, address, or e-mail address ("Personal Data"). In general, you can visit www.fujitsu-general.com without revealing any Personal Data. At times we may track the Internet address of people who visit www.fujitsu-general.com and analyze this data for trends and statistics, but the tracked data will not include your Personal Data. Other Fujitsu General Group websites may collect your Personal Data in compliance with applicable laws and regulations. If you do not provide the information we request, you can still visit most of our website but you may be unable to access certain options, offers and services.

Some Fujitsu General Group websites send "cookies" to your computer to better serve you with more tailored information when you return to the website. Cookies are identifiers that include unique characters, but unless you input your Personal Data onto our website you cannot be identified, and will remain anonymous. You can set most browsers to tell you when you are sent a cookie and to refuse to receive it.
