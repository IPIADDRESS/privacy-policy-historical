Privacy and Security Policy
 

(Updated September 26, 2007)
 
This document explains how PUNKU uses and protects any information that we
collect and receive from you (such as your name, address, email address, phone
number, credit card, and any other information that is not otherwise publicly available)
and the security of our online catalogues.
 
TABLE OF CONTENTS
  1. Confidentiality and Security
  2. The Information we Collect
  3. How we use Collected Information
  4. How to access or correct your Account Information
   
1. Confidentiality and Security
Our online store is a secure place to shop or order online. We use industry-
standard SSL encryption to protect data transmissions.
 
Our payment partner, PayPal, has its own SSL encryption so your payment
information is secure. For example, when you enter your credit card number into the
PayPal payment form, it is transmitted in an encrypted (scrambled) form.
 
2. The Information we collect
PUNKU may collect the following information:
Name, address, email address and phone number.
The Internet Protocol (IP) addresses of visitors to our site
On some cases, you can submit information about other people. (For example,
if you order a gift online and want it sent directly to the recipient, you will need
to submit the recipient's address)
   
----Note: We do not store any credit card information.
 
3. How we use Collected Information
We use the information you provide about yourself or about someone else when
placing an order only to complete that order. We do not share this information with
outside parties except the postal system to the extent necessary to complete that
order.
 

We use return email addresses to answer the email we receive. Such addresses are
not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties.

 

Our Online Catalogues give you the opportunity to submit a testimonial about your
experience using our products. Your comments may be made publicly available.

 
Our Online Catalogues give you the opportunity to receive a short email newsletter
about new crafts, resources and promotions. You will not receive any emailed
information unless you specifically request this information.
 
4. How to access or correct your Account Information

If you have an account with us, you can access all your personally identifiable
information by logging into it. We use this procedure to better safeguard your information.

You can correct factual errors in your personally identifiable information by editing
your account information.
