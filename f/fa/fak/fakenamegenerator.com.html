Privacy Policy

What information do you collect?

We store normal statistical information, such as referring URL, IP address, and date/time of visit. With the exception of bulk orders, generated identities are not associated with any specific IP address or visitor.

Bulk identity orders are stored for one month to ensure that the recipient received them. After one month the bulk order is deleted. For tax purposes, we retain sales information for several years.

Do you use cookies?

Our web analytics service and advertisers may set cookies.

Do you sell my information?

No. The only information we will ever sell or release to third-parties is randomly generated.

Advertisers

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie at anytime by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.
