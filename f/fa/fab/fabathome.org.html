Welcome to the Fab@Home Privacy Policy Page

Fab@Home is not a commercial enterprise. The current Fab@Home project members will not distribute or sell any personal information you may have posted on the site, but in that it is an unrestricted wiki site, we cannot guarantee that others will not use the information on this site for their own commercial purposes, so bear in mind that you post your information at your own risk. We may use the contact information that you provide on the Guest Book page to produce a mailing list for ONLY Fab@Home related information - such as software releases, new designs, fabber of the month awards. If you are unwilling to receive such information, please remove your contact information.

If you have complaints or suggestions about this policy, please contact Evan 13:26, 8 December 2006 (EST).
