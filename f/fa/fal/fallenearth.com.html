Information related to how you use the Games

("Analytics")

We may collect technical information about your use of the Games through the use of tracking technologies and analytics.

Personal data we may collect includes the following:

We reserve the right to disclose your identity to any third party who is claiming that any User Content posted by you constitutes a violation of their intellectual property rights, or of their right to privacy.
