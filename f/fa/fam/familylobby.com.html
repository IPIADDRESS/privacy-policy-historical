Our Privacy Policy


IDEAL Computer Services, Inc. has created this Privacy Statement in order to demonstrate our commitment to your privacy. The following details our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website:

Information We Collect

When you order, we need to know your name, e-mail address, mailing address, credit card number, and credit card expiration date. This allows us to process and fill your order, and to notify you of your order status.

How we use the information

We will not disseminate any personally identifying data about clients to parties outside of IDEALCS, Inc. We maintain strict security over all the information you share with us.


1 The information you share is used to enhance your satisfaction
with our products and our relationship with you.


2. We may also use the information we collect to occasionally notify
you about important functionality changes or sales on products and services
you may have selected on previous visits.



How we protect client information

When you place orders, access your account information or send us personally identifying data, that
information is transferred over to a secure server. The secure server software (SSL) encrypts all information you input before it is sent to us; protecting your information against unauthorized access.

What about "cookies"?

"Cookies" are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. Our cookies do not contain any personal identifying information. Most Web browsers
automatically accept cookies, but you can usually change your browser to change that.

Cookies help us give you a more enhanced Web site experience, by letting us monitor what's working and what isn't through site traffic analysis. Please be assured that accepting a cookie does not give us access to your computer or personal information under normal circumstances.
