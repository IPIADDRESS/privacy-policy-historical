Privacy Policy

Our Commitment
FATbit Technologies is committed to providing a safe and secure Internet environment and protecting the privacy of everyone who visits our site. As our valued client, you can be assured that any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential.

Monitoring Site Traffic
No information is gathered about our users while they are visiting our site other than standard statistical information such as IP address, domain name, browser type and pages viewed. This information is used to evaluate site traffic and usage patterns in order to improve our Web site and is not, under any circumstances, shared with third parties.

Cookies
We do not use cookies on this site to track user sessions.

Personal Information Submitted by the User
If a user sends an email to Artistic Pixels for any reason, including submitting a request for a quote, the information submitted will ONLY be used by FATbit Technologies to contact the user. We will not disclose individually identifiable information to any third parties.

"Recommend This Site" Service
A user can electronically forward this Web site to someone else by clicking the "recommend this site" button on every page and submitting the form. The user provides a valid email address and name for themself and the recipient in order to ensure that the service functions properly and so that the recipient knows who recommended the site. This information is not used for any other purpose.

External Links
Although, this site may contain links to other sites, FATbit Technologies is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of any Web site other than http://www.fatbit.com 

Your Questions
If you have any questions or concerns regarding the privacy or security of this site, please contact us via our website. or at this email address - pp @ fatbit.com
