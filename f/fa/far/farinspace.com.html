Privacy

This is the Web site of Dimas Begunoff.

I can be reached via email.

For each visitor to this web site, the web server automatically recognizes only the visitor’s domain name, but not the e-mail address.

I collect e-mail addresses of only those who opt-in and subscribe via e-mail, user-specific information on what pages visitors access or visit, and information volunteered by the visitor, such as survey information and/or site registrations.

The information I collect is used by me to improve the content of the web site and to notify visitors about updates to our web site, this information will never be shared with other organizations for any purposes.

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please contact me and let me know.

With respect to ad servers: we do not partner with or have special relationships with any ad server companies.

From time to time, I may use collected information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in this privacy notice. If the information practices change at some time in the future I will post the policy changes to the web site to notify you of these changes and provide you with the ability to opt-out of these new uses. If you are concerned about how your information is used, you should check back at our web site periodically and/or subscribe to the RSS feed.

Visitors may prevent their information from being used for purposes other than those for which it was originally collected by contacting me.

Upon request we offer visitors the ability to have inaccuracies corrected in their contact information, posts and comments. Visitors can have this information corrected by contacting me.

With respect to security: I have appropriate security measures in place to protect against the loss, misuse, or alteration of information that we have collected from you at this web site.

If you feel that this web site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact me at anytime.
