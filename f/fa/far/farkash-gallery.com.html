Privacy and Security Policy

When you order an item (for example, a catalogue).We collect the information necessary to effect your purchase. That information is delivered directly to the Farkash-Gallery order department. Farkash-Galler may use your order information to send you notices about, or invitations to, upcoming events in the collecting area in which you've made a purchase and other products and services that we believe may be of interest to you. You may request that Farkash-Gallery does not send any such notices or invitations by contacting us via Email.

Security

We treat our customers' personal information as highly confidential and have implemented processes in order to make sure that only employees who need to know specific information have access to it.
