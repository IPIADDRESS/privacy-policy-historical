Farrell Hair Replacement Website Privacy Policy

At Farrell Hair Replacement, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We take your right to privacy very seriously. Respecting and protecting the privacy of clients and prospective clients is our main goal.

When you visit the Farrell Hair Replacement website, or one of our affiliated websites, you may navigate through our website without offering us any information about yourself. Sometimes, however, we do require personal information in order to provide services that you request.

When you fill out our contact forms, register for our infomercial or sign up for our Personal Design Session Tours, you are asked to provide us with your E-mail address. In addition, you may choose to provide Farrell Hair Replacement with additional information, including your age, gender, street address, city, state, phone, and more.

We may also use your personal information to help us create and deliver content that is relevant to you, and to alert you to special offers and promotions, updated information, and other new services from Farrell Hair Replacement.

Farrell Hair Replacement will not sell or reveal to any third party your personal information, except to the extent necessary to comply with applicable laws, police investigations, or in legal proceedings where such information may be relevant. Farrell Hair Replacement does not allow access to any database information by third parties whatsoever.

We strictly protect the security of your personal information and honor your choices for its intended use. We carefully protect your data from loss, misuse, unauthorized access or disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Your personal information is never shared outside the company without your permission, except under conditions explained above.

If you would like to be taken off of our mailing lists or would like to have your personal information removed from out database, please fill out the form below. If you have any questions or comments about our Privacy Policy, please Contact Us or fill out the form below.
