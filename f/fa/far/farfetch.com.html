HOW TO SHOP

BOUTIQUES & PRODUCTS

farfetch.com is an online marketplace that unites a network of independent boutiques. As such, when shopping on farfetch.com, you are able to choose from a very wide selection of authentic designer pieces that are available in the best boutiques in the world, such as L'Eclaireur in Paris, Penelope and Tessabit in Italy, Feathers in London, Henrik Vibskov Boutique in Copenhagen, Anastasia and American Rag in California and Bblessing in New York.

Each boutique has a very individual and distinctive buying style, so when shopping at farfetch.com you have a rich selection of product to choose from, from emerging and niche labels or more luxurious and established designer names. You will find many items on farfetch.com that you will find nowhere else on the web.

We divide our labels into three departments: Luxe, Lab and Contemporary. These three departments each have a distinctive identity and host a range of different labels, allowing you to easily shop similar brands.
  • Luxe is where you will find the high-end of the designer offering, such as Alexander McQueen and Givenchy.

  • Lab is home to experimental and emerging labels. In Lab you will find designers such as b Store, Bless and Henrik Vibskov.

  • Finally Contemporary features labels with a casual, urban attitude, including diffusion lines from the big fashion houses and premium denim brands. Shop Contemporary to discover brands such as J Brand and PRPS.


HOW TO FIND PRODUCTS

At farfetch.com you can shop in multiple ways. The easiest is to browse the site using the dropdown navigation menu at the top of the site, where you can get a quick overview and links to New In products, categories, designers and departments, by gender.

As you move through the site from there, use the more detailed filters on the left hand side of the page to narrow your search. If you know what you are looking for, use the search box at the top right of the site. And you can relocate to a different department using the links within the tool tip box on the right hand side of listing pages.


HOW TO ORDER & PAY

When you find an item you wish to purchase, simply select your size (there is a Size Help link for every item) and the quantity you wish to buy, then click the ADD TO BAG button. You can continue shopping and add more items to your bag, or you can proceed to CHECKOUT to purchase.

Our checkout process is quick and straightforward. No matter how many items you have in your shopping bag or how many boutiques they are sourced from, you only need to checkout once. We ship worldwide via DHL with services from UPS in selected countries. Please select shipping type as appropriate for each boutique you have purchased from. Proceed to Payment and select your payment method, we accept all major credit and debit cards as well as PayPal.

Once you have placed your order you will receive an email acknowledging receipt, before we initiate our validation process. Your order will be shipped directly from the boutiques to you.

ORDERS & SHIPPING

ORDER TRACKING

When you place an order, you will receive an email acknowledging receipt of your order. Once we have verified your order and payment details and have approved your order for shipment, we will email you again to inform you of this.

In the unlikely event that we encounter a problem when processing your order, you will receive an email from us detailing the problem and possibly requesting further information.

All items are shipped from our boutiques, therefore if you have ordered items from more than one boutique, you will receive a delivery from each boutique separately.

You will receive regular updates on your order and delivery status by email, and you can also check the status in your My Account section through all stages from Order Placed, Payment OK, Order Ready to Go, Order Sent and finally Order Delivered. You can also go to www.dhl.com or www.ups.com anytime to view and monitor the status of your shipments, by inserting the tracking number supplied.

Shipping times will vary based on the service selected at checkout, please refer to our Shipping section for more information. Please note farfetch.com cannot take responsibility for any delays to orders due to customs clearance or payment transaction delays.


CANCELLATIONS

farfetch.com is happy to accept cancellations, for any reason, up to the moment your order has been processed by the store. After that, you cannot cancel your order, but you may return it if you wish. If you have not yet received an email confirming the order has been processed, you may still be able to cancel it.

Alternatively, you can contact our Customer Service team from Monday to Friday between 9am-6pm (GMT).

DUTIES AND TAXES


CUSTOMERS IN USA, CANADA, UK, ITALY, NETHERLANDS, FRANCE, SWITZERLAND, SINGAPORE, AUSTRALIA, JAPAN, RUSSIA, SOUTH KOREA, GERMANY, POLAND, BELGIUM, ROMANIA, SPAIN, AUSTRIA, DENMARK, SWEDEN, GREECE, PORTUGAL, BULGARIA, CYPRUS, LATVIA, IRELAND, CROATIA, CZECH REPUBLIC, FINLAND, SLOVAKIA, LUXEMBOURG, LITHUANIA, HUNGARY, SLOVENIA, ESTONIA AND MALTA

We currently ship orders with import delivery duties paid to USA, Canada, UK, Italy, Netherlands, France, Switzerland, Singapore, Australia, Japan, Russia, South Korea, Germany, Poland, Belgium, Romania, Spain, Austria, Denmark, Sweden, Greece, Portugal, Bulgaria, Cyprus, Latvia, Ireland, Croatia, Czech Republic, Finland, Slovakia, Luxembourg, Lithuania, Hungary, Slovenia, Estonia and Malta – this means all import duties are included in your order, so the price you see is the price you pay. You will not receive an additional duty bill from the courier to receive your shipment. If you are ordering from within your own customs zone or country, import duties will not be applicable anyway, however you may have to pay VAT or local Sales Taxes where applicable (for the UK and USA these are included at the checkout).

Customers in the Rest of the World

For customers in the Rest of the World, we ship orders with delivery duty unpaid. This means that import duties are excluded from the prices you see online and you may have to pay import duties upon receipt of your order or at a later date depending on your customs authority. You will not have to pay VAT or Sales Tax but there may be other local taxes that you may need to pay to receive your goods. If you are shipping from an EU store to an EU shipping address – you will not have to pay any duties at all.


We ship worldwide via DHL Express with services from UPS in selected countries. If you have any further questions please click here to submit an enquiry and one of our customer services representatives will get back to you.


SHIPPING TO RUSSIA

Please note: Customs authorities in Russia have recently tightened restrictions on import shipments to Russia. As a consequence, non-document shipments sent to private individuals now undergo extraordinary scrutiny leading to an increased number of customs holds and delays in delivery.
Please be aware that deliveries to Russia can take 5-10 working days to arrive.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION FOR CUSTOMERS IN RUSSIA

  • Currently we can only ship to addresses in Moscow and St Petersburg

  • All items are shipped DDP – all duties and taxes are included in the checkout price.

  • Unfortunately due to Russian regulations, farfetch.com will be unable to refund duties and taxes on shipments into Russia

RETURNS INFO FOR CUSTOMERS IN RUSSIA

Unfortunately returns are not possible for any jewellery (including costume jewellery) or fur goods. Returns of these goods cannot be accepted.

If your return shipment is valued < 1000 Euros;
  • Please book with DHL here.

  • When the English invoice have been issued please contact DHL Russia on ru911@dhl.com or +7(495)9561000 ext 2811 for support in creating Russian translations of the invoice that will be necessary to complete the return
If your return shipment is valued > 1000 Euros;
  • Returns of any goods valued at >1000 Euros will require a fee of US$65 to be paid to Russian customs.

  • Please contact DHL Russia local office Customer Service Call-Center by phone number +7 495 956 1001 so they can prepare a customs bill which you will pay in cash (US$65) when the return pickup takes place (when DHL pick up the goods)

  • Customs bill should be requested one business day before pick-up date

  • Once you have received your customs bill you can then book a pickup book with DHL.

  • When the English invoice has been issued to you please contact DHL Russia on ru911@dhl.com or +7(495)9561000 ext 2811 to support in creating Russian translations of the invoice that will be necessary to complete the return

SHIPPING TO CHINA

Please note: In January 2011 regulations for importing goods into China changed slightly and this may affect you if you place an order for delivery into China. All importers and exporters in China are now required to register with Customs authorities for a Customs Registration number (CR Number). In order to clear you goods, you may be contacted by the shipper (DHL) and asked to provide this number - if you do not have one, the shipper will assist you in identifying a qualified import/export agent to help you import your order.


SHIPPING TO THE USA

For imports to US addresses (from European store purchases), please be aware that US customs may contact you asking you to provide them with your IRS, EIN or SSN number before enabling delivery to your address. You will have a one week window to provide this information. Click here for more details from US customs.
