You have options when applying for federal student aid. You can use a fee-based FAFSA Preparer, similar to using a tax preparer, or you can work directly with the US Department of Education at www.fafsa.ed.gov for no fee. As a fee-based FAFSA Preparer, we work to help make your FAFSA filing experience as easy and successful as possible.


We are a privately owned commercial business, not affiliated with or endorsed by any government entity. Because of that independence, we are constantly innovating new and better tools to help you understand the information needed to file a FAFSA.


Once you've provided the necessary information, whether online or by phone, we will prepare your FAFSA and file it for you with the US Department of Education for as low as $78. Our experienced team is available to help you seven days a week, including most holidays.
