PRIVACY POLICY

Internet user privacy is of paramount importance to Fashion Net. The success of our business depends upon our ability to maintain the trust of our users. Below is information regarding Fashion Net's commitment to protect the privacy of users and to ensure the integrity of the Internet.


User Information


Fashion Net will not sell, rent or give away your e-mail address or any personal information submitted to us.

In the course of delivering an ad to you, we do not collect any personally-identifiable information about you, such as your name, address, phone number or email address. We do, however, collect certain non-personally-identifiable information, such as the server your computer is logged onto or your browser type. The information collected about your visits is used for the purpose of evaluating traffic and measuring ad effectiveness on behalf of our advertisers.


Cookies


Fashion Net, along with millions of other Web sites, uses cookies to enhance your Web viewing experience. Fashion Net's cookies cannot be used to determine any personally-identifiable information about you and do not damage your system or files in any way.


If you have any additional questions, please contact us.

We recommend that you review this Privacy Statement periodically, as Fashion Net may update it from time to time.
