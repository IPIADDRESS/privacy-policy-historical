Privacy Statement and Policies
We created this privacy statement to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy. We use the information we collect about you to process orders and to provide a more personalized experience. We may also use it to share educational and promotional information with you via our email newsletter.  We NEVER sell, trade or rent your personal information to others. This document discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices.
 

What Information We Collect and How We Use It

IP Address
Your IP address is used to help identify you in tracking "User Sessions".  The data is only used in a broad demographic way and never identifies you, as an individual.

Cookies
"Cookies" are small pieces of information that are stored on your computer's hard drive. Your browser must accept cookies to identify you if you choose to create a website with us.  Nothing is done with this cookie except session tracking which allows you to login to the control panel of your website.

Contact Forms
Our site uses a contact form for patrons to request information, products, and services. We collect visitor's contact information (like their name, address, email address).  The customer's contact information is also used to get in touch with the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving  mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.

Links to Third Party Sites
This site contains links to other sites. FineArtStudioOnline.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. Transactions that occur between you and the third party are strictly between you and the third party and are not the responsibility of FASO.  Because FASO  is not responsible for the availability of these outside resources or their contents, you should direct any concerns regarding any external link to its site administrator or Webmaster.

Our Security and Protection of Customer Information
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. When you enter your credit card information, we offer the use of a secure server. The secure server software (SSL) encrypts all information you input before it is sent to us.

We Do Not Disclose the Information We Collect to Outside Parties
FASO does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. Period.
