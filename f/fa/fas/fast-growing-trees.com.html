Your Privacy

We Keep Your Information Private

Fast Growing Trees Nursery recognizes that your privacy is very important to you. We know that providing personal information is an act of trust and we take that seriously. Your email address will not be sold to third parties. If at any time you wish to be removed from our mailing lists, just drop us a line on our website to do so. Our email service providers try to make it as easy as possible to opt out of future emails by including an opt out links.

Cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to your computer's hard disk for record-keeping purposes. Most Web browsers are set to accept cookies. However, if you do not wish to receive any cookies you may set your browser to refuse cookies. In some instances this may mean you will not be able to take full advantage of parts of our service. The use of cookies can enhance your enjoyment of what is offered by Fast Growing Trees Nursery, because they can help personalize your experience of our website. They can make it easier for you to shop online and can ensure that information provided to you is most relevant to your interests. Cookies in and of themselves do not personally identify users, although they do identify a user's browser to the server.
Fast Growing Trees Nursery and its affiliates recognize the trust you place in us when you give us personal information. We need to obtain various information from our users to ensure the effectiveness of our service. The information obtained is used as a whole to gain general statistics so we can better provide our services and so we can identify more effectively other prospective users.
