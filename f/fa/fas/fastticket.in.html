Privacy Policy

FTS (Fast Ticket Services) respects your privacy and recognizes the need to protect the personally identifiable information (any information by which you can be identified, such as name, address, and telephone number) you share with us. We would like to assure you that we follow appropriate standards when it comes to protecting your privacy on our web sites.

In general, you can visit fastticket.in website without telling us who you are or revealing any personal information about yourself. We track the Internet address of the domains from which people visit us and analyze this data for trends and statistics, but the individual user remains anonymous.

Some of our web pages use “cookies” so that we can better serve you with customized information when you return to our site. Cookies are identifiers which a web site can send to your browser to keep on your computer to facilitate your next visit to our site. You can set your browser to notify you when you are sent a cookie, giving you the option to decide whether or not to accept it. The information we collect and analyze is used to improve our service to you.

There are times when we may collect personal information from you such as name, physical address or telephone number. It is our intent to inform you before we do that and to tell you what we intend to do with the information. Generally, you will have the option not to provide the information. If you choose not to provide the information we request, you can still visit the fastticket.in, but you may be unable to access certain options, offers and services.

Your personal information may be used to:

Verify your identity

Assist you to subscribe to FTS´ services

Provide the services you require

Administer and manage those services, including charging, billing and collecting debts

Inform you of ways the services provided to you could be improved

Conduct appropriate checks for credit-worthiness

Gain an understanding of your information and communication needs in order for FTS to provide you with a better service.

FTS will not sell, trade or disclose to third parties any information derived from the registration for, or use of, any online service (including names and addresses) without the consent of the user or customer (except as required by or other legal process or in the case of imminent physical harm to the user or others).

FTS will take appropriate steps to protect the information you share with us. We have implemented technology and security features and strict policy guidelines to safeguard the privacy of your personally identifiable information from unauthorized access and improper use or disclosure. FTS will continue to enhance our security procedures as new technology becomes available.

If our privacy policy changes in the future, it will be posted here and a new effective date will be shown. You should access our privacy policy regularly to ensure you understand our current policies. FTS will attempt to respond to all reasonable concerns or inquiries at the earliest.
