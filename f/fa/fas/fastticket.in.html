Privacy Policy

This is done for the purpose of analyzing the data for trends and statistics and the process does not reveal personal information of the user in any way.

We may, from time to time, need to collect personal information from you that can consist of small details such as your name, address or mobile or landline number. We intend to notify you prior to collecting such information and also give information about how we intend to treat the information.

It is totally at your discretion whether you do or do not want to provide us with the required information. In case you choose not to give us the requested information, you will still be able to browse the FASTTICKET website, but in a limited way as you may be not be able to access some of the options, offers or services.

We provide users of our services with login id and password to logon and avail our services in secure mode. With user’s acceptance of Terms and Conditions of Service agreement, FASTTICKET holds no responsibility for keeping user password confidential and also for any and all activities that arise from abuse or misuse of the password. Users are advised to change their passwords periodically to keep them confidential.

We may use your personal information for various purposes such as to verify user identity as and when required, to provide the services requested by you, to help you subscribe to FASTTICKET, to administer and manage services including, charging, billing and collecting debts.

We may also need user information to suggest users about improvements that could be made to our services, or to gain an understanding about the information and communication needs of our users so that FASTTICKET can provide better services. The user information can also be used for performing appropriate checks for user’s credit-worthiness.

FASTTICKET would like to assure its users that it stores, keeps or accesses user information only for the purpose of providing services to its users and not for any other purpose whatsoever.
