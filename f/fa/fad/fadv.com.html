Use of Cookies and Pixel Tags
Cookies are bits of electronic information that a Website can transfer to a visitor's hard drive to help tailor and keep records of his or her visit to the site. Cookies allow Website operators to better customize visits to the site to your individual preferences. The use of cookies is standard on the Internet and many major Websites use them. First Advantage may use cookies to help provide you with a better experience on our site, however, we do will not store user information or personally identifiable information in cookies. In addition to cookies, First Advantage may use pixel tags, also known as beacons, spotlight tags or web bugs, to improve our understanding of site traffic, visitor behavior, and response to promotional campaigns as a supplement to our server logs and other methods of traffic and response measurement. We also may implement pixel tags provided by other companies, and limit the collection of information for the same purpose. We do not store user information or personally identifying information in pixel tags.

Log Files
We may use IP addresses to analyze trends, administer the site, track user movement, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses are not linked to user information or personally identifiable information.

Security
This Web site takes every precaution to protect our user's information. When users submit sensitive information via the Web site, this information is protected both online and off-line. First Advantage implements a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your business information. User information is contained behind secured networks and is only accessible by a limited number of employees who have specific access rights to such systems. When you place orders or review results or access your account information, we offer the use of a secure server. All sensitive/credit information you supply is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology. While we use SSL encryption to protect sensitive information online, we also take numerous steps that protect our user's information off-line. Access to user information is restricted in our offices. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job (for example, for billing or customer service) are granted access to user identifiable information. All employees are kept up-to-date on our security and privacy practices. Finally, the servers that we store personally identifiable information on are kept in a secure environment, behind locked doors.
