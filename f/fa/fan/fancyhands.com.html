Privacy Policy

We take your privacy very seriously. This business cannot work without trust and this privacy policy outlines just one aspect of our commitment to ensuring you the best service possible.

Personal Information

Fancy Hands tries to make do with only the basic information about our clients. Upon signing up, we save your name, email address and alternate email addresses to ensure we can always communicate with you. If you supply information with a task such as health insurance information, this information is saved in such a way that it's not accessible to anyone other than the assistant working on your task.

We will never sell, rent or give away any of your personal information.

Financial Information

When a user subscribes to the service, all of the payment functionality is handled by secure servers at recurly.com. At no point do we ever have access to your credit card number or any financial information. We will never ask for this information in the process of completing a task for you.

Who owns this site?

Fancy Hands is totally owned and operated by Fancy Hands, Inc.
