    Q: What forms of payment do you accept?

    A: We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express and Discover credit cards/debit cards as well as PayPal, personal checks & money orders. If paying by personal check or money order, please mail your payment to: Fantasy Jewelry Box P.O. Box 336 Jersey , GA 30018.

     

    Q: Will my order be processed securely?

    A: We accept orders online through the Yahoo! Store secure ordering system, which uses industry-leading Secure Socket Layering (SSL) encryption to process all orders. When ordering at our store, your information is transmitted securely over the Internet in encrypted (scrambled) form so that it cannot be accessed by unauthorized third parties.

     

    Q: When should I expect my items to arrive after my order is placed? How do you ship your items? What is your Shipping & Handling Charge?

    A: Our normal order processing time is between 1 to 2 business days. If you choose USPS First Class Mail ($5.95), delivery time for your order will be approximately 3-7 business days after your order is processed. If you choose USPS Priority Mail ($7.95), normal delivery time will take approximately 2-3 business days. All orders shipped via USPS Express Mail ($19.95) will arrive in 1-2 buisness days. International orders shipped via Global Priority Mail ($9.95) will take approximately 5-10 days. Global Express Mail ($21.95) orders will take approximately 3-5 days to arrive. Again, please allow 1-2 days for your order to be processed although approximately 80% of orders are shipped SAME DAY!

    Delays may occur for products on back order, in limited availability or on special order. Orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed on the next business day. Contact our Customer Service Department if you have any questions or comments regarding your order status at customerservice@fantasyjewelrybox.com or by phone at 1-888-613-1997.
