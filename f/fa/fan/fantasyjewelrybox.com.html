Q: How can I contact Fantasy Jewelry Box?

A:You can contact us by either Phone, Email or Live Chat. You can reach us toll-free by calling 1.888.613.1997. Our customer service phone hours are from Monday - Friday 9:30am to 4:00pm EST. Our Live Chat is available during the same hours as our toll free number listed above. You can reach us through chat by clicking the "Chat With A Live Rep" banner at the upper right of your screen, just below the shopping bag icon. If Live Chat is unavailable you will be prompted to send us a message through the chat window which will be answered by email as soon as possible. To email us please use this address: customerservice@fantasyjewelrybox.com.


Q: What forms of payment do you accept?

A: We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express and Discover credit cards/debit cards as well as PayPal, personal checks & money orders. If paying by personal check or money order, please mail your payment to: Fantasy Jewelry Box 1147 Washington St SW Covington GA 30014.

 

Q: Will my order be processed securely?

A: We accept orders online through the Yahoo! Store secure ordering system, which uses industry-leading Secure Socket Layering (SSL) encryption to process all orders. When ordering at our store, your information is transmitted securely over the Internet in encrypted (scrambled) form so that it cannot be accessed by unauthorized third parties.

 

Q: What is Your Privacy Policy?

A:Fantasy Jewelry Box takes your privacy seriously. We do not sell, lease or rent any of your personal information to any outside parties. Your information is stored securely and may be used internally by our company for contacting you regarding an order placed.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.

Third-Party Web Beacons: We use third-party web beacons from Yahoo! to help analyze where visitors go and what they do while visiting our website. Yahoo! may also use anonymous information about your visits to this and other websites in order to improve its products and services and provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by Yahoo!, click here.

 

Q: When should I expect my items to arrive after my order is placed? How do you ship your items? What is your Shipping & Handling Charge?

A: Our normal order processing time is between 1 to 2 business days. If you choose USPS First Class Mail ($5.95), delivery time for your order will be approximately 3-7 business days after your order is processed. If you choose USPS Priority Mail ($7.95), normal delivery time will take approximately 2-3 business days. All orders shipped via Fedex 2nd Day ($15.95) will arrive in 2 buisness days. Order shipped via Fedex Next day Air will be delivered the next business day.International orders shipped via First Class International ($9.95) will take approximately 5-10 days for delivery. Express Mail Intrernational ($24.95) orders will take approximately 3-5 days to arrive. Again, please allow 24 hours for your order to be processed although most orders are shipped SAME DAY!

Delays may occur for products on back order, in limited availability or on special order. Orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed on the next business day. Contact our Customer Service Department if you have any questions or comments regarding your order status at customerservice@fantasyjewelrybox.com or by phone at 1-888-613-1997.

Q: What does your gift box look like?

A: If you choose to have your item gift wrapped, your order will arrive in an attractive pink 3 ½” x 3 ½” jewelry box topped with an elegant white satin bow. Your piece(s) will be placed inside a small black velvet pouch within the box. Additionally, the jewelry box will be nested inside an attractive 5”x5” white shipping box surrounded by elegant pearl white striped tissue paper. Perfect for gift-giving! We are extremely proud of our gift wrapping services and we promise you will be amazed at the response you will get from the lucky recipient!
