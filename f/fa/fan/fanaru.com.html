Fanaru will not share your personal information with the Third-Parties for the purpose of advertising, i.e. sending unsolicited email. Fanaru is not responsible for the information you post on the Fanaru website or for information you provide other Fanaru users, all of which you do at your own risk.

Managing Your Information

You can review the personal information you have provided Fanaru and make any desired changes to that information by logging in to your account on the Fanaru website, clicking on the "My Account" icon in the "Members Area" section of the Fanaru website, by editing the information, and then by submitting the revised information.
