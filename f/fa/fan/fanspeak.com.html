PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy applies to the Fanspeak web site, and it may be updated from time to time, without prior notice.  By using this web site, you acknowledge and accept the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy.  If this Privacy Policy conflicts with the Terms of Use of this web site, the provisions of the Terms of Use shall prevail.  As the terms of this Privacy Policy may evolve over time, we encourage you to review its terms periodically.

At some places on this web site, we collect information that can be used to identify you (“Personally Identifiable Information”), including but not limited to your e-mail or postal address and your telephone number.  We will not share any of your Personally Identifiable Information with any third party without your prior consent, except for that information which you publicly disclose on the site or such information as we may be required by law to disclose.  We do not knowingly collect any Personally Identifiable Information from persons under the age of 13.

Fanspeak may share information that is not Personally Identifiable Information with third parties.  Such information may include aggregated information regarding users and usage of the Fanspeak web site.

Users of the Fanspeak web site may be required to register in order to access certain site content or participate in various Fanspeak activities, services or promotions.  Registration may require users to provide Personally Identifiable Information.  This Personally Identifiable Information will be used to improve the quality of the services offered to you, and it will not be shared with third parties without your prior consent.  Registered users may have the opportunity to elect to receive e-mail and other electronic communications from Fanspeak.  Registered users will also have the opportunity to opt-out of receiving those communications.  Fanspeak does not permit persons under the age of 13 to register on the site.  If Fanspeak becomes aware of registration by a person younger than 13 years of age, Fanspeak will remove that registration information.

This web site makes use of “cookies,” small text files stored on your computer.  Cookies are used to make your use of this web site easier and quicker.  They also provide information regarding your use of this web site which helps us to make the site more useful.  We only use cookies to retrieve information which this web site provides (e.g., user codes); we do not use cookies to retrieve other information.  Please note that web sites that are linked to the Fanspeak site (for example sites of advertisers and sites linked to this site through user postings) may also make use of cookies and other forms of information collection, review, and distribution.  Fanspeak assumes no responsibility for the privacy policies of those other sites.  When following such links, be sure to review the privacy policies of those linked sites carefully.

Certain information, including the following, may be stored in our database and may be archived, even after deletion: activity logs and related records, registration information, listings and review content.  Fanspeak makes good faith efforts to retain all information it stores in a secure setting, however, we can not guarantee that all such stored information will be remain completely secure.

From time to time, Fanspeak content, services or activities may involve the use of passwords or access codes.  Users are responsible for ensuring the security of their passwords.  Users should always exercise caution to make sure that your passwords and other access codes remain secure.

We will disclose information, including Personally Identifiable Information, if required to do so by law.  We will also disclose such information when we have the good faith belief that disclosure is reasonably necessary to: 1.) respond to a legal order or requirement (court order, for example); 2.) enforce our Terms of Use; 3.) respond to a claim that our site or its content violates third party rights, or 4.) protect the safety, rights or property of the public.

Links to third party web sites may be provided on the Fanspeak site.  Those third party web sites may have different privacy practices than the Fanspeak site.  Fanspeak makes no representations regarding the privacy policies of third party sites.  Users should review the privacy policies of all web sites before accessing those sites.

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: info@fanspeak.com.
