Fibers.com Privacy Policy

1.    INTRODUCTION:
At Fibers.com, we respect the privacy of our visitors and customers (collectively referred to as “Users”).  Fibers.com does collect and use information provided by Users to better serve Users and to make Fibers.com as user friendly and accessible as possible.  This Privacy Policy spells out the information Fibers.com collects, how such information is collected, how the information is used and whether such information is disclosed to third parties.  Fibers.com reserves the right to make changes to any part of this Privacy Policy at its sole discretion, at any time and without prior notice.  Continued use of Fibers.com will constitute acceptance of any changes.  If this Privacy Policy is changed in any way, information concerning the changes will be available on this page.  Fibers.com strongly recommends that Users periodically review this Privacy Policy for any changes.

2.    CONSENT TO USE INFORMATION:
By using the Fibers.com Website, each User consents to the collection and use of all information that the User provides to Fibers.com, all information that is provided to Fibers.com on behalf of that User, and all information that Fibers.com collects based on that User’s use of the site, including, without limitation, Personally Identifiable Information (defined below).  If User does not agree to this Privacy Policy, and the terms contained herein, User is not authorized to use the Fibers.com site.  The continued use of the Fibers.com site after any changes or amendments to this Privacy Policy shall signify the User’s acceptance of and consent to the changed or amended Privacy Policy.

3.    FIBERS.COM’S PRIVACY PLEDGE:
Fibers.com is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of Users at all times.  Fibers.com recognizes that this information belongs to the User, and will treat such information accordingly.  Fibers.com will not disclose any Personally Identifiable Information or confidential information of any User to any third parties, except as outlined in this Privacy Policy or as may be required by law.

4.    FIBERS.COM USE OF PERSONAL AND NON-PERSONAL INFORMATION:
Fibers.com collects two kinds of information from on-line visitors, personal and non-personal information.  Visitors to the Fibers.com Website are not required to register, or provide any Personally Identifiable Information to access the Fibers.com site or review the products and services offered.  Fibers.com collects non-personal information to track the total number of guests visiting the Fibers.com site and to help Fibers.com improve the site and the services available on the site.  Such non-personal information includes the browser type and operating system being used by visitors, areas of the sites visited, date and time of access, host or service provider information and identification of the referring site.  This information is used exclusively for Fibers.com’s internal purposes and is not disclosed to any third parties.

 

Personally Identifiable Information, which means any information that can be used to identify or locate a specific person, including, but not limited to e-mail addresses, names, street addresses, phone numbers, fax numbers, credit card numbers and social security numbers, is collected only when voluntarily offered and solely for purposes clearly identified on the Fibers.com site.  Such Personally Identifiable Information is kept strictly confidential and will only be disclosed to third parties under the conditions described in Paragraph 5 of this Privacy Policy.

5.     DISCLOSURE OF INFORMATION TO THIRD PARTIES:

Fibers.com may disclose Personally Identifiable Information as is required by law or under the following circumstances:

Disclosure to Successor.  Fibers.com may disclose User’s Personally Identifiable Information to any successor-in-interest of Fibers.com, such as a company that acquires Fibers.com and its assets.  Users will be notified by e-mail or notice on the Fibers.com Website of any change in ownership or control of Personally Identifiable Information.

Disclosure to Third Party Service Providers and/or Online Partners. Fibers.com may contract with various third parties to help provide, maintain and improve the Fibers.com Website and services.  For example, Fibers.com may use a third party contractor who processes payments, and may subcontract out production, fulfillment, analytics, reporting or other operations.  Fibers.com may also contract with third parties to help manage, monitor and optimize the Fibers.com Website and services and to assist in measuring the effectiveness of advertising, communications and use of the Fibers.com Website.  Fibers.com will use commercially reasonable efforts to prevent such third parties from disclosing Personally Identifiable Information, except for the purpose of providing the services in question.  However, Fibers.com can not guarantee that such third parties will not disclose User information.

Disclosure to Contest Partners.  Fibers.com may conduct contests for the benefit of, on behalf of, or in coordination with one or more third parties.  Fibers.com may share Personally Identifiable Information with said party or parties to the extent necessary to effectively run the Contest and award prizes.  Other than for the conducting of contests, Fibers.com does not share, rent or trade Personally Identifiable Information with third parties for cross-promotional purposes.

 

Disclosure to Online Store Owners. Fibers.com may disclose non-financial, non-sensitive contact information to approved online store owners (or “Sellers”), including, but not limited to names, email addresses, telephone numbers and mailing addresses for internal business record keeping and marketing by the online store owner.  Online store owners must agree not to use any customer information for marketing purposes unless the customer “opts in” to receive such marketing materials from the online store owner.  All online store owners must be approved by Fibers.com and comply with the Fibers.com Terms of Service, the Fibers.com Online Store Owner Agreement and this Privacy Policy.


6.    USE OF “COOKIES”:
Fibers.com may place an electronic “Cookie” in the browser files of a visitor’s computer.  Cookies are pieces of information that a web site transfers to an individual computer drive for record-keeping purposes.  Cookies themselves do not enable Fibers.com to access any personal information about visitors.  Cookies only allow Fibers.com to analyze a visitor’s use of the Fibers.com site.  This analysis helps Fibers.com better understand which areas of the Fibers.com site are more useful and popular so that improvements and updates can be made in the future.  Fibers.com does not use Cookies to retrieve information from a visitor’s computer that was not originally sent in a Cookie.

Fibers.com is committed to preventing any unauthorized access to each User’s account.  Fibers.com has taken reasonable steps to maintain procedures and technology designed to prevent such unauthorized access.  However, the privacy and security of communication or data transmission over the internet cannot be guaranteed, because the internet is not a secure medium.  Fibers.com has taken reasonable steps to protect against unauthorized access to User’s accounts and Personally Identifiable Information used to administer User’s accounts. However, Fibers.com does not assume any responsibility for any harm, loss, or damage the User may experience or incur by the sending of personal and confidential information over the internet by or to Fibers.com.
