Privacy and Security

When it Comes to Keeping Your Financial Information Secure... We're On It.

First Citizens understands the importance of keeping your personal information secure. We use state-of-the-art technology to protect the information you entrust to us. What's more, our associates are committed to guarding your personal information — both online and in person at our branch locations.

You can also play a major role in protecting your personal data by...

  • Keeping materials with your personal or account information in a safe place.
  • Providing personal or account information only to sources you trust.
  • Reducing the amount of paper mail you receive that contains personal or confidential information.

Read our Privacy Policy to learn more about how First Citizens manages customer information and what additional actions you can take.
