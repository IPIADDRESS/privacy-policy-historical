Privacy Policy

FrontStream Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 14, 2017

The privacy of personal information is very important to us.  As such, FrontStream Holdings, LLC (together with all affiliates, “FrontStream,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) presents this Privacy Policy (“Policy”), which describes how FrontStream and its suppliers collect, use, and disclose information collected through the websites, online services, and mobile applications that FrontStream provides (collectively, the “Services”). “Services” includes, for the avoidance of doubt, our websites that allow users to make donations and the services provided by FrontStream through our online Panorama platform.

Please read the entire Policy before using our Services.  In this Policy “Collected Information” means all information collected through the Services, including any information submitted by you. This Policy applies to all users of the Services, including nonprofit organizations, fundraisers, and donors.

By accessing or using the Services or by accepting our Panorama Terms and Conditions, Terms of Use (where applicable) or Services Agreement or any other terms that incorporate this Privacy Policy by reference, you agree on behalf of yourself and any organization that you represent (together, “you”) that you have read and understand this Policy and that you consent to the collection, use, and sharing of information as discussed below. If you do not agree with this Policy, do not access or use the Services. This Policy is incorporated into and made a part of our Panorama Terms and Conditions, Terms of Use (where applicable) or Services Agreement

We reserve the right to modify this Policy at any time. All changes will be effective immediately upon posting to the Services. Your continued use of the ServiCOces after any modification to the Policy will constitute your acceptance of those modifications. Material changes will be conspicuously posted on the Services or otherwise communicated to you.

1.       We Collect Personally Identifiable Information and Other Information that You Submit.

Personally Identifiable Information. You can visit our Services without sharing personally identifiable information, but you will be required to submit personally identifiable information in order use certain other features of the Services.  We will collect any personally identifiable information from or about you that you choose to submit to us, which may include your first and last name, email address, postal address, telephone number, and financial information. Specifically, we collect your personally identifiable information when you sign up for accounts on the Services, in receiving and fulfilling information requests, and when you respond to communications from us (e.g., surveys, polls, and request for feedback). We will use collected personally identifiable information as described below, including to set up your Service accounts, to communicate with you in connection with your accounts, campaigns, and donations, and to provide you with information and communications that you request or that we believe are relevant or may be of interest to you, subject to applicable laws and the terms of this Policy.

Account Information. To create accounts on the Services you will need to submit certain information, which may include your name, telephone number, and email address. You may choose to add additional types of information to your account, such as your communication preferences, your physical location, demographic information (e.g., your age, marital status, ethnicity/race, and gender), and any other information requested.

Payment Information. Donations made using the Services must be made by credit or debit card. All credit and debit card information is provided directly to our PCI-compliant third-party payment processor. FrontStream does not directly access, handle, or store your credit or debit card information. To make a donation, you will need to submit your name and your credit or debit card type, number, expiration date, security code, and billing address. Payment by credit or debit card is subject to the approval of the card issuer. We will not be liable in any way if a card issuer refuses to accept a credit or debit card for any reason. We will maintain a record of the donations that you make using the Services.

Donation Information. If you choose to make a donation using the Services, you will be required to identify the eligible nonprofit organization(s) for which you are recommending a donation and you will be required to submit, in addition to the payment information listed above. FrontStream will collect and maintain all information provided when making a donation through the Services and will use that information to track donation activity and maintain donation records. If you choose to make a donation, your name may be listed publicly at your discretion on our Services and in donor listings that are published from time to time by FrontStream or the recipient of the donation. If you elect to make a donation in honor of another person, that person’s name may be listed publicly on our Services and in donor listings that are published from time to time. Further, if you elect to provide us with contact information for that person, we may send that person a letter or an email acknowledging the donation. Please do not make a donation in honor of another person if you do not have that person’s permission for us to list his or her name publicly.

Mailing List Information. If you sign up or otherwise opt in to receive promotions from us, you will need to submit your name and email address. You may also provide your physical address and telephone number. We will use this information to provide you with promotional and other electronic communications. You may opt out of receiving promotional emails from us at any time as described below. We may use third-party email providers to deliver these communications to you.

2.       Other Information We Collect.

Account Activity. If you use the Services while signed into any account on the Services, we will collect information on how you use your account and the Services.

IP Addresses, Analytics and Related Data. The servers used to operate and provide the Services may collect data pertaining to you and the equipment, software, and communication methods you use to access the Internet and the Services, including Internet protocol (“IP”) addresses assigned to the computers and other devices from where you access the Internet, your Internet service provider (ISP), your device ID number, your approximate geographic location, your browser type, the pages you access on the Services, the web Services you access before and after visiting the Services, the length of time you spend on the Services, date and time stamps, and clickstream data. FrontStream may use this information to administer the Services and their servers, to generate statistical information, to monitor and analyze Service traffic and usage patterns, to monitor and prevent fraud, to investigate complaints and violations of our policies, and to improve the Site’s content and the products, services, materials, and other content that we describe or make available through the Services. We may combine this information with personally identifiable information, other Collected Information, and information obtained from third parties for security and system enhancement purposes. The suppliers that we use to provide the Services may collect information about your visits to the Services and other websites.

Location Information. In addition to the approximate geographic location information collected as discussed above, we may, with your consent, access more specific location information in order to enhance the user experience and increase the usefulness of the Services. This includes both geographical location information that you input using the Services and geographical location information collected by device-based location services (including GPS data). You may choose to allow us to access your location by granting the Services access to your location when prompted or through your device’s location services settings. FrontStream reserves the right to uses Google Maps/Google Earth API(s). Pursuant to the Google Maps/Google Earth APIs Terms of Service, use of this location feature is also subject to Google’s Privacy Policy. For information on how Google categorizes location information, please visit Types of location data used by Google. Both the Google Maps/Google Earth APIs Terms of Service and Google’s Privacy Policy are incorporated into these Terms by this reference.

Cookies. The Services may automatically use cookies, pixel tags, and similar tracking technologies to track Service use and to facilitate and enhance the user experience on the Services.  Cookies are small pieces of information that are stored on your computer’s hard drive by your web browser.  Pixel tags are very small images or small pieces of data embedded in images, also known as “web beacons” or “clear GIFs,” that can recognize cookies, the time and date a webpage is viewed, a description of the webpage where the pixel tag is placed, and similar information from your web browser.  The Services may use both cookies that we implement and cookies implemented by our suppliers and other third parties. We use cookies to track how you access and use the Services, to learn when and how users visit the Services, to learn which search terms are used to find the Services, and to learn which websites direct you to the Services. We also use cookies to help display certain information on the Services and to improve your enjoyment of the Services, for example, by remembering your contact and other information when you access or use the Services. By using the Services, you consent to our use of cookies and similar technologies.  Most web browsers automatically accept cookies. You can decide if and how your web browser will accept a cookie by configuring your preferences or options in your browser.  However, if you choose to reject cookies, you may not be able to use certain online products, services, or features on the Services. Due to the automatic collection of data using cookies, we do not honor “do not track” requests. We may allow our suppliers to place and read their own cookies, web beacons, local shared objects, and similar technologies to collect information through the Services, for example, to collect information that helps us with traffic measurement, research, and analytics.

Social Media. FrontStream may work with third-party social media platforms to allow you to connect and share information with those platforms through our Services, for example, Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn. These features may allow you to share information about your experience on our Services with your friends and followers on those social media platforms and may require us to implement cookies, plug-ins, and application protocol interfaces (APIs) provided by those social media platforms in order to facilitate those communications and features. We may share information that you provide us or that we may collect about your use of the Services with those platforms and that information will be subject to those platforms’ privacy policies, which we encourage you to review. These social networking services may be able to collect information about you, including your activity on our Services.  These third-party social networking services also may notify your friends, both on our Services and on the social networking services themselves, that you are a user of our Services or about your use of our Services, in accordance with applicable law and their own privacy policies. In addition, by choosing to use any third-party social media platform or choosing to share content or communications with any social media platform, you allow us to share information with the designated social media platform. If you choose to access or make use of third-party social media platforms, we may receive information about you that you have made available to those social networking services, including information about your contacts on those social networking services. We cannot control any policies or terms of such third-party platform. As a result, we cannot be responsible for any use of your information or content by a third-party platform, which you use at your own risk.

The Services may also use the advertising services and networks offered by those social media platforms to deliver advertising content. Use of these services requires social media platforms to implement cookies or pixel tags to deliver ads to you while you are logged into the platform.

If you do not wish a social media platform to collect information relating to you via the Services, we suggest that you: (a) hide social media platform plug-ins using an ad blocker; (b) always log off completely from the social media platform before visiting other websites and delete all related cookies; and, (c) where the option made available by the social media platform, disable the advertising services and networks operated by that platform.

Embedded Content. The Services may incorporate content, including feeds scripts embedded in the Services’ code as well as visible content (for example, videos), provided by third parties. In some cases, those third parties collect data about how you interact with their content. For example, YouTube may collect or otherwise have access to usage data on videos embedded on the Services as described in YouTube’s Privacy Policy.
