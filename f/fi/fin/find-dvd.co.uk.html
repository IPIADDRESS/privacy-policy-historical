What Are Cookies?

Cookies are tiny files that hold snippets of information on your computer. Cookies are created by a website when you visit a page and are private to that web site (i.e. other web sites can't read cookies created by another one)

We use cookies to remember who you are as you move around the site, adding and removing items in your price-watch list. We don't store any detailed personal information nor financial information such as credit card details and we remove any cookie we create as soon as it is no longer required.

Please note that cookies can't harm your computer and most websites use cookies in one form or another.

We are giving you this information in response to recent legislation, and to make sure we're honest and clear about your privacy when using our website.
