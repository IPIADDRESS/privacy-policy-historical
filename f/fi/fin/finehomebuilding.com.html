The Taunton Press Privacy Policy

The Taunton Press strives to treat everyone fairly, honestly, and with respect.   All personal information - including customer transactions, Web site registrations, and e-mail correspondence, is always handled with the utmost discretion. The policies described here apply to taunton.com, tauntonplus.com, and other Web sites owned and operated by Taunton New Media, Inc.

Receiving mailings from The Taunton Press
Once you enter your name, address and other information in the The Taunton Press Web site (i.e., submitting an order, registering for  TauntonPLUS, etc.) you may receive regular postal mailings about other related products from The Taunton Press. If you do not wish to receive this information, click here to go to the Privacy Options Form in our Online Subscriber Services area.

Receiving e-mail from The Taunton Press
There are several places on the Web site where we ask for a visitor's e-mail address.  We use e-mail to send customers an order confirmation for purchases made at the Web site.  These are sent automatically as a customer service.  When a customer sends us an e-mail about a particular customer service question, we will respond via e-mail. 

We also offer e-mail updates  about "What's New" at The Taunton Press or TauntonPLUS.  These e-mail bulletins are sent only to those TauntonPlus members who request them.  If you do not want to receive these e-mail bulletins from us in the future, please click here to  change your TauntonPlus member profile.

Sharing Names
From time to time, we may make the list of The Taunton Press customer names and postal addresses available to socially responsible companies whose products or services we feel might be of some interest to you.  If you do not wish to be on this mailing list, please click here to go to the Privacy Options Form.

We do not make the list of The Taunton Press customer/member e-mail addresses available to any other companies.

Other Information Collected by Web Server
Our Web server automatically logs the IP address or domain name (e.g., aol.com or att.net) of visitors to our Web site. The server also recognizes the pages viewed and referring URL.  For the purpose of improving the content of the site, this information is examined in aggregate.  We do not analyze the viewing habits of individual visitors.


Our Use of Cookie Technology
A "cookie" is a small file sent to your computer by our Web server. We use cookies in two areas of the site: in the book/video shopping cart and at TauntonPLUS.   We send a cookie to your computer when you use our Web site shopping cart to purchase a book or video.  The cookie keeps track of what you've put into the cart.  We send a cookie to TauntonPLUS members when they log in so that our computer knows to give TauntonPLUS members a 20% discount on their purchases.  All of our cookies are temporary and delete themselves  when you close your Web browser.
