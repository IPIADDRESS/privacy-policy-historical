Privacy

Privacy Policy for findananny.sg

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at admin@findananny.sg.

At findananny.sg, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by findananny.sg and how it is used.

Postal Codes, Email Addresses and Phone Numbers.
Findananny.sg collects postal codes for the purpose of helping users see approximate locations of other users with whom they may be interested in conducting business.
Phone numbers and email addresses are only revealed to paying members, and then only to members in the "other group". For example nannies will never be able to see the contact addresses of other nannies, nor families see the details of other families.
Contact details cannot be indexed by the search engines.
Contact details will not shared or sold to other parties.

Emails
Emails are sent to alert users the latest ads posted in the group of interest to the group to which each user belongs. Nannies receive excerpts of the latest family ads and families receive excerpts of the nannies ads. No contact information is revealed in these emails.
Users may unsubscribe from the emails at any time. Information about unsubscribing is at the bottom of each email.

Log Files
Like many other Web sites, findananny.sg makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons
findananny.sg does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.

DoubleClick DART Cookie
Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on findananny.sg.
Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to findananny.sg and other sites on the Internet.
Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL - http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. Our advertising partners include...
Google Adsense

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on findananny.sg send directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies ( such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons ) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see.

findananny.sg has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. findananny.sg's privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers' respective websites.
