Fishbowl Inventory Privacy Statement


Eventronix (the makers of Fishbowl Inventory products) is dedicated to maintaining the privacy of our online visitors and customers. Once you have chosen to become an Eventronix customer, we assure you that your personal information will be exclusively used to provide you with the products and services you have selected. When you purchase a product from Eventronix or download an Evaluation Copy from our website, you will be asked to provide contact information and, in the case of a purchase, payment information. The contact information will include your name, address, phone number and email address. The payment information typically includes your credit card number. All information collected by Eventronix will be used for the sole purpose of maintaining our relationship with you as a customer.

How does Eventronix use customer information?
We do not sell or rent customer information. We may use customer information to notify customers of available upgrades to products they have purchased from us.
