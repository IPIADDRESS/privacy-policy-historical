Privacy Policy

   
         
    Fightnews.com respects your right to privacy.

Some general log information is collected from our vistitors, such as browser, platform type and referral data. This is standard for most websites and does not include personal information that can identify you.

If you sign up to receive a Fightnews.com boxing newsletter by e-mail, you will start receiving a newsletter delivered on a regular basis, as well as infrequent other announcements from Fightnews.com. We collect subscribers' e-mail addresses in order to distribute those newsletters and announcements and do not otherwise share them with other companies or partners.

Fightnews may also co-sponsor these events along with other companies, in which case information may be shared with participating sponsors. A registrant's personal contact information will not be released to any third-party organization.

Fightnews.com contains many links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other web sites.

If you have any further questions or concerns about Fightnews.com's privacy policies and use of personal information, please send them to privacy@fightnews.com.
