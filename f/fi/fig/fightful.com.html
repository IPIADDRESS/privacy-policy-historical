Privacy Policy Effective as of August 2016

Shazzu, Inc. (‘Fightful’ or ‘we’ or ‘us’) has adopted a privacy policy (the ‘Privacy Policy’), which describes how we collect, use and share personally identifiable information we obtain online from and about individuals located in the United States.

Fightful’s web site (http://www.fightful.com) and all other related web sites owned or controlled by us (collectively or individually the ‘Site’) are general audience web sites, and we do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age.

Our Privacy Policy applies to ‘Visitors,’ which means that you simply browse the Site, ‘Subscribers,’ which means that you have subscribed to receive one or more Fightful newsletter or partner emails and ‘Members,’ which means that you have registered with fightful.com. The term ‘User’ refers to a Visitor, Subscriber or a Member. By using our Site, you signify your agreement with all the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use our Site.

From time to time Fightful may revise this Privacy Policy to reflect industry initiatives or changes in the law, our personally identifiable information collection and use practices, the features of our Site or technology. If we make revisions that change the way we collect or use your personally identifiable information, Fightful will post those changes in this Privacy Policy and the effective date will be noted at the beginning of such Privacy Policy. Your continued use of the Site after Fightful posts a revised Privacy Policy signifies your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy. Therefore, you should review it periodically so that you are up to date on our most current policies and practices. If Fightful materially changes its practices regarding collection or use of your personally identifiable information, your personally identifiable information will continue to be governed by the Privacy Policy under which it was collected unless you have been provided notice of, and have not objected to, the change. In the event the last e-mail address you provided to us is not valid or is not capable of delivering the notice to you, Fightful’s dispatch of an e-mail containing the notice to that e-mail address shall nonetheless constitute effective notice.

A. COLLECTION: Collection of personally identifiable information by Fightful.

1. General. When Fightful collects personally identifiable information, such as your full name, email address and birth date, from you it is because you are voluntarily submitting personally identifiable information to us in order to register as a Member or a Subscriber, or in order to participate in activities like creating a personal profile (‘Your Profile’), sweepstakes, contests, games, surveys, forums, in connection with content or suggestions you submit to us, or because you want us to furnish you with products, services or information. We may also obtain personally identifiable information from reputable third parties.

You may also choose to voluntarily provide or store non-personally identifiable information in Your Profile (as described in Section A.1(a) below) or provide or store non-personally identifiable information on other public forums provided on our Site, including but not limited to, text, files, images, photos, video, sounds, musical works, works of authorship, private messages, chats or any other materials (collectively ‘Content’) that you choose to post to the Site (Content posted by any User is ‘User Content’). If you choose to make Your Profile or any information from Your Profile public or provide any User Content on other public forums provided on our Site, you do so at your sole discretion and risk.

Members can change their Registration Data (as defined below in Section A(1)a) and Your Profile information at any time and can control how Visitors, other Members and Fightful communicate with them by controlling their account settings, available within the ‘Edit Profile’ portion of Your Profile.

Fightful collects information from you at several different points on the Site in the manner set forth below:

(a). Member Registration and Profile: In order to access certain information and services on the Site, you must first become a Member by completing our member registration form. During the registration process you will be required to provide registration data, which may include personally identifiable information such as your name and e-mail address (such information is the ‘Registration Data’). The Registration Data will automatically become incorporated into ‘Your Profile; however, in the Your Profile section of the Site, you have the option to make such Registration Data (including, but not limited to, your personally identifiable information) public or private. You also have the option of adding certain User Content to Your Profile.

Important Information about Changing and Updating Your Profile

If Your Profile information changes you may correct, update or remove it. You can edit Your Profile information by going to the Your Profile page re-entering data and clicking the ‘Save Changes’ button.

(b). Newsletters and Special Offers: Fightful delivers interesting, stimulating and uplifting newsletters to its Subscribers on a variety of topics. In order to subscribe to our newsletters, you will be required to give your e-mail address and zip code. If you would like to change the e-mail address to which your Fightful newsletters are delivered, please visit our Contact Us page.

Unless a User has opted out of receiving such e-mails during the registration process, Fightful may occasionally send Users who provide us with their e-mail addresses information about special offers, Site updates, contests and other offerings via e-mail. We may also send information about products and offerings of our partners that we think may be of interest to our Members and Subscribers. To opt out of receiving these e-mails from us please visit our Contact Us page.

Important Information about Making Changes to or Unsubscribing to Our Newsletters

If you would like to unsubscribe from our newsletter, you may do so in any of the following ways:

(a) Managing Your Subscriptions: You may make changes to your Fightful newsletter subscriptions at any time by clicking ‘Unsubscribe’ at the bottom of our newsletter.

(b). Surveys, Contests and Quizzes: You may have the opportunity to participate in a number of surveys on our Site. We will use survey responses to guide us as we make improvements and enhancements to our Site. We also offer Users the ability to take personal assessment and other types of quizzes. We encourage you to complete surveys, personal assessments and quizzes so that we can provide you with a more personalized experience on our Site, such as featuring content and advertising that we believe are consistent with your interests. We will not use your responses for any other purpose without your prior consent.

On our Site you may also have the opportunity to participate in contests and other promotions that may require you to provide information about yourself. We will not share your contest information with any third party, except in aggregate form. In addition, if and when we sponsor contests or other promotions, or third parties sponsor such promotions with us; either we or the third party will post relevant privacy information in the official rules and/or registration area for the sweepstakes or promotion. That privacy information, to the extent it conflicts with this Privacy Policy, will govern that particular contest—please remember to read it carefully.

Message Board, Discussion Rooms and Social Networking: in the event Fightful provides message boards, discussion rooms and other public forums on the Site for exchanging information related to spirituality and social networking. Please note that information, User Content or any other materials that you post on our Site, including any information from Your Profile that you choose to make public, will be available for any User of that service to read, and is therefore no longer private and may be disclosed without limitations as to its use by a third party. Fightful reserves the right to reproduce or publish any materials posted on our Site in any other formats.

Fightful will automatically send a one-time e-mail inviting your friend(s) to visit our Site. We will use this information for the sole purpose of sending this one-time e-mail.

2. Cookies. Cookies are small bits of information that the Site place on the hard drive of your computer. Cookies remember information about your activities on our Site and enable Fightful to make your visits to our Site more enjoyable. For example, cookies can store your password for easy log on to a Site you have previously visited, can save your preferences for a personalized home page. Information about your activities on our Site and other non-personally identifiable information about you may also be used to limit the online ads you encounter to those we believe are consistent with your interests.

Third party advertisements displayed on our Site may also contain cookies set by Internet advertising companies or advertisers. Fightful does not control these cookies and visitors to our Site should check the privacy policy of the Internet advertising company or advertiser to see whether and how it uses cookies. Please refer to Section B (Notice) below for more information on third party advertisements.

Fightful may use cookies and similar tools to relate your use of our Site to personally identifiable information obtained from you or a reputable third party. For example, if you have asked us to provide you information about our upcoming products, promotions and events, cookie and/or click stream data about your activities on our Site may allow us to limit the materials we provide you to items we think you will find interesting, based on your prior online activities and preferences. However, if Fightful wishes to combine your personal and cookie or click stream information in this manner, we will obtain your express affirmative consent.

You can program your computer to warn you each time a cookie is being sent or to refuse cookies completely. However, without cookies you may not have access to certain features on our Site, or contests.

Fightful also uses web beacons (also known as action tags or single pixel gifs) to access Fightful cookies and in connection with the services we offer through our Site. A web beacon is a transparent image placed on a web page that sends a signal when a person visits that page. This enables us to assess the popularity and effectiveness of the materials on our Site. We may also use web beacons in connection with newsletter and other e-mails we send to our Users, to let us know whether and when the e-mail was opened. No personally identifiable information is transmitted through web beacons. We also collect IP (Internet Protocol) addresses to analyze trends, administer our Site, track a user’s movement, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use.

We adhere to a strict anti-spam policy.

At Fightful we are 100% committed to protecting your privacy. We won’t reveal or sell your email address to third parties. We won’t send you emails if you don’t want to receive them, either.

When you first sign-up, we send out an email welcoming you and confirming your login name and password. This is our way of notifying you about free readings, special gift offers, new reports or feature updates. You can easily unsubscribe from these at any time and still be able to receive horoscopes by email if you like.

You can rest assured that Fightful will never share email addresses with anyone else. If you decide at any time that you want to stop receiving our member-benefits emails, you can opt-out by clicking on the Unsubscribe link at the end of every email or by visiting the “About You” area of the website.

Fightful will use your personally identifiable information provided under this Privacy Policy in a manner that is consistent with this Privacy Policy. If Fightful obtains personally identifiable information from a third party, such as a business partner, our use of that information is governed by this Privacy Policy.

Fightful may share your personally identifiable information with companies that are affiliated with us. Provided you have given us permission, Fightful may also share your personally identifiable information with advertisers and business partners that are not affiliated with Fightful, but would like to send you information about their products and services. For example, Fightful may share your personally identifiable information with the companies who supply prizes for Fightful’s contests and may have special offers or promotions that might be of interest to Fightful users. Fightful may also enter into agreements with outside companies that possess the technology that allows Fightful to customize the advertising and marketing messages you receive on our Site. Your non-personally identifiable information and click stream data about your activities on our Site may be shared with these companies so this customization may be accomplished.
