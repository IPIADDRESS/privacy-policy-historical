What information do we collect?

Do we use cookies?

Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computers hard drive through your Web browser (if you allow) that enables the sites or service providers systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information. We use cookies to help us remember and process the items in your shopping cart.

Notice for California Users – Five Four is not presently configured to support Do Not Track (“DNT”) signals from web browsers.

By using this Website and agreeing to this Cookies Policy, you consent to Five Four Club's use of cookies in accordance with the terms of this policy.
2.2 There are two types of cookies, session cookies and persistent cookies.
2.3 Session cookies are persistent cookies files, which are deleted from your computer when you close your browser. Persistent cookies files remain stored until you delete them or until their expiry date.
2.4 Session cookies are used to remember log in details when you are browsing the Website and to ensure that you are not repeatedly logged out as you browse the Website. The information will be deleted when you close the browser.
2.5 Persistent cookies store information on your computer so that we will be able to recognise you when you make return visits to the Website, they will be stored on your browser until your erase them or until they expire.
