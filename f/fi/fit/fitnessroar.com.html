Privacy Policy

By fitnessroar - Jan 05, 2018

Thank you for your interest in FitnessRoar services and privacy info. Please note that our privacy policy doesn’t apply to that information which you already have posted publicly or display on this site. In general submission of your personal information is not at all required in order to browse FitnessRoar, you can surf this site without revealing any of your personal information.

Without revealing any personal information, one can visit FitnessRoar any time. Further, in case, you are connecting with FitnessRoar via any social networking site like Facebook, twitter, etc then we are free to collect your profile information from that particular site and not liable for the same.

Note: We respect our visitors/users rights and support “Right to be forgotten”. If you think you’ve ever shared your contact details with us & want us to remove that info, please contact us at  info@fitnessroar.com

Sharing and use of personal information-

Although we use industry’s best standard measures to protect your personal information in our database we cannot have control over the other users with whom you share your user profile and information.
We may use the user information:-
• In order to improve our website
• To improve our content and products information
• To rectify the correct info or content given on the site, if any

However, we do not disclose your personal information to others without your approval or in the occurrence of some

Specific Incidents:-

Thus, it may also possible that reader will be served cookies by us, by our advertisers or by that company which help us delivering advertising on this site. These cookies help us and advertisers to identify users on our site and on other sites as well.
