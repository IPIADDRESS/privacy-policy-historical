User Submitted Information

Registration Information is the information that Members must provide to us at registration in order to create a profile (also called an "account") such as first name, last name, email address, username, password, cable provider, city, state, zip code and gender or enter a contest or sweepstake. Your username will be public, which means it is available to everyone and will be displayed in search results on the Site and on external search engines. Please select your username carefully.

Subscription Information is the information that Subscribers must provide to us in order to sign up for the FX Newsletter or FX SMS Notifications, such as first and last name, mobile phone number, mobile carrier, email address, password.

Public Information and Posts consist of your username and, if you provide one, your profile picture and any additional information you chose to make public through your profile and your posts and comments on the FX Services outside of your profile. If you elect to post comments on any of the public forums provided on the Site you will be required to provide your password and email address prior to posting. Your posts and comments outside of your profile include those made on other Member profiles, such as public messages and on general Site areas, such as community forums, blogs and chats. Profile Content consists of information Members may add to their profile.
