Freeads Privacy Policy

  • Freeads Classifieds collects personal data as provided to us during the registration process.
  • Freeads Classifieds also collects personal data from other parts of the website as required.
  • Freeads Classifieds may hold personal data relating to transactions the user enters into with ourselves and/or our suppliers. Please do not supply your personal data to us if you do not wish us to collect it.
  • Freeads Classifieds may also ask for other data so that we can obtain a better understanding of our users' requirements, and by using the site you are authorising us to share this information with selected partners and third parties.
  • Cookie technology is used to monitor the users' use of Freeads, and this, in turn, is used to help us provide a better service. There are two classes of Cookies based on how long they remain on your system: (i) "Session Cookies" may be used only during your visit to the site and are erased once you close your browser, and (ii) "Persistent Cookies" (if applicable) remain on your browser for up to six months from your last visit to the site and enable us to remember you on subsequent visits.
  • There are many misconceptions about Cookies. Cookies cannot erase your hard drive or otherwise damage your computer, nor do they allow us to collect information stored on your computer.
  • A useful resource on understanding Cookies, including information on how to disable Cookies on your browser, can be found at http://www.allaboutcookies.org.
  • Freeads Classifieds may use personal information about the user to build up a profile of the users' interests and preferences.
  • By entering this site or responding to an advertisement the user will consent to Freeads processing personal data, and is deemed to have accepted all of the terms above and acknowledge that we will be processing their personal data.
