Internet Privacy Policy
for Fred Meyer Stores

Here is how Fred Meyer Stores handles any information we learn about you from your visit to this website.

We use information we collect from you to help make our site, subsidiaries, stores, products, and services more useful to you. We also may create compilations of information that is not personally identifying and provide this data to other companies to help them understand the demographics of our web visitors and customers.

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server may recognize the consumer's domain name and e-mail address (where possible).

We collect the domain name and e-mail address (where possible) of visitors to our Web page, the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail, aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit, user-specific information on what pages consumers access or visit, and information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registrations.

The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web page. Your e-mail address may be used by Fred Meyer Stores or its subsidiaries to contact you if you've given us permission to send you marketing information. If at some point you decide you do not want to continue receiving e-mail from us, please let us know by sending an e-mail to us and telling us to take you off our e-mail list(s).

Fred Meyer Stores DOES NOT sell, lease, or otherwise give personally-identifying information to any other non-affiliated company, person, or agency. "Personally-identifying information" means your name, address, credit card number, telephone number, or any other information by which you can be personally identified. Fred Meyer Stores may disclose personally-identifying information in response to a subpoena, court order, or specific request by a law enforcement agency, or as required by law.

If you supply us with your postal address on-line, you will only receive the information for which you provided us your address. Please provide us with your exact name and address.

If you supply us with your telephone number or fax number on-line, you will receive telephone or fax contact from us only regarding the information for which you provided us your number. Please provide us with your correct phone or fax number.

By using this site, you are agreeing to the conditions of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, do not use the site. We reserve the right at our discretion to change, modify, add, or remove portions of this policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on the site. Please check this page periodically for changes.
