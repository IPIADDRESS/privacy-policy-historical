WiredReality Privacy Policy


Our Principles 

(1) We are fully committed to following laws related to the collection of data and protecting the privacy of kids and adults, and to following our policy on data collection.

(2) We are committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of each visitor. 

(3) WiredReality will not give, trade, sell, or otherwise distribute the personal information you provide to us. 

(4) Wiredreality asks that members under 13 years of age have parental consent when logging on the chat server, registering a nickname, posting any identifyable information, such as email addresses, and personal information.

(5) A parent may contact WiredReality to seek assistance on their child's registration and also request that they be removed.  Before entering a chatroom, a member will be required to enter their age, if they are under 13, they will be required to have a parent sign a parental consent form and have it sent to WiredReality for approval before they are able to chat.


WiredReality Chat 

Registered or unregistered members may chat in WiredReality's chat rooms, which are moderated by volunteers and a moderation robot. However, members under 13 years of age will be required to register at WiredReality before being able to chat.  Members are NOT allowed to give out any information in the chat rooms that will individually identify them. If any such information is given out, the member will be kicked from Chat. Only a parent can determine what is appropriate for your own child. There maybe chats where the topic or the language is not something you wish your child to participate in, its important that you supervise your child's use of the chat area. You should teach your child to not give out any personal information to others online.


Is WiredReality Registration Necessary?

Registering on WiredReality is only necessary for members under 13 years of age for parental consent reasons.  All members over 13 may register if they please, but it is not required.

What Information Does WiredReality Collect? 

WiredReality does not require a member to submit his/her full name, address, etc when registering and filling out a profile on the chat network. We only collect information you voluntarily supply us. A member may choose to leave this blank, profiles may be filled in at the descression of a members parents. WiredReality does not collect email addresses from members. We stress parental involvement and permission before you supply information on our Web site. We do not ask for real names or postal addresses. Instead you are invited to supply any nickname not registered by another member. WiredReality does not encourage members under 13 years of age to post personally identifyable information without parental consent.

Here is a list of the information WiredReality requests on its registration form:

* WiredReality Name (this username is created and used only for access to WiredReality) 
* Password (this password is created and used only for access to WiredReality)
* IP Address  - The IP address is not requested, but is shown on the Wired Profile.   This is used to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site.  We also track the total number of visitors to our site.  Personally identifiable information is not taken in this process.

This information is left to the user to fill in on his/her profile, and is not required to be filled in.

* First Name - A member may choose to write their nickname here.
* Info - A member may choose to write anything in this area.
* Email - A member may choose to post their email address
* URL  -  A members may choose to list thier favorite website.

The WiredReality profile is available to registered members only.  The profile can be seen when a member double clicks another members nickname.  The profile is a way of other members being able to learn a little bit about you before they begin to chat with you.  You may choose to fill in this profile at your descresion, it is perfectly fine to leave your profile blank.
Note:  Membership is only required by members under 13 years of age.


What Does WiredReality Do with the Information? 

WiredReality stores information in its secure database and does NOT share this information with anyone. Personal information is deleted immediately on request. WiredReality never sells this information to any third parties. 


Cookies:

A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's hard drive containing information about the user. WiredReality does not use cookie technology to obtain any information from its online visitors. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information  while on our site.


Public Forums:

This site makes chat rooms, forums, message boards, and/or news groups available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.
