  3. What personal data do we collect?

    1. Information provided by you through FreeStuff.eu
      When you participate in a contest through FreeStuff.eu, you are asked to provide information about yourself. This includes your name, address, date of birth, e-mail address and telephone number. We may also ask further questions regarding your marital status, education and for example your interests, preferences and your receipt of products or information. AllGroove Limited can also use your telephone number to send you a text message. These messages are only to confirm any order you have made. You will not receive SMS marketing from AllGroove Limited.

    2. Information provided by you via surveys
      Besides issuing competitions through FreeStuff.eu, AllGroove Limited or a client of AllGroove Limited can contact you through telephone, email or Internet for a survey. When you participate in such a survey, AllGroove Limited or the client which sends the survey shall process the personal data you provided in the survey. In such surveys you are asked to provide personal data such as your name, email address, address, postal code, telephone numbers, date of birth, gender, salary, education, and marital status, occupation, hobbies, interests, and (online) surfing habits.

    3. Information provided by you to third parties
      AllGroove Limited can obtain information about you through third parties or 'list rental'. AllGroove Limited acquires for example the following personal data through third parties: name, email address, address, postal code, telephone numbers, date of birth, gender, salary, education and marital status, occupation, the industry sector in which you workin addition to information about your hobbies, interests and online habits. AllGroove Limited ensures that you have agreed that your data can be transferred to third parties such AllGroove Limited. If you do not want your data to be further processed by AllGroove Limited, you can send an e-mail to info@freestuff.eu

    4. Information collected through cookies and related technology
      Besides the data which is collected by AllGroove Limited via FreeStuff.eu, via surveys and via third parties, AllGroove Limited uses cookies and other technology such as so-called third-party pixels to collect data about you.

      5. A cookie is a small text file placed on your hard disk by the server of FreeStuff.eu. This allows the server to save your preferences and other data including passwords. Furthermore, AllGroove Limited uses cookies to remember that you have used the website before, to track your visits to the website and to track and aggregate statistical data regarding site visits , for example for reporting purposes for its internal organisation or for third parties.

      You can decide whether to accept or refuse cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies or to notify you each time a cookie is set. You can also learn about cookies by visiting AllAboutCookies.org which includes additional useful information on cookies and how to block cookies using different types of browser. Please note however, that by blocking or deleting cookies used on the website you may not be able to take full advantage of the website.
