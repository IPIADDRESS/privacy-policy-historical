Fresh Privacy Policy

Fresh Promotions is Committed to Protecting Your Privacy

We don't share your information with anyone. This policy describes our practices regarding personal information collected through www.freshpromotions.com.au or by other means.

 What Information is Collected?

All information provided is kept confidential and is used to support our business relationship and the service. This website uses an order inquiry form to allow you to request information, products, and services. We collect your contact information (such as your e-mail address or your company information) to allow us to send samples, orders, information about our products. Your contact information is also used to get in touch with you when necessary. You may opt-out of receiving future communication. Just send an email to contact@freshpromotions.com.au to make this request.

 Personal Information Disclosure:

Fresh Promotions will not give or sell any information you provided to any third parties under any circumstances. The information protected includes anything about you, your order and your purchase history. Your information may be shared with contractors of Fresh Promotions for the purpose of performing services for Fresh Promotions related only to your order or sample request. We may release personal identifiable information when required to by law wherein we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal process.

Choice/Opt-Out:

When you opt-in to receive communications from Fresh Promotions, you will receive information we believe will be of use and interest to you. For example: We keep you informed about the latest deals, new service, sell-out specials, etc. that we think you will find valuable. We may update you on any important changes to our website. If you choose to opt out of our communication, you may do so in the following ways:
