What Information do We Collect from You?
All information you provide will be kept confidential and is used to support our business relationship and the service we provide. This website uses an order inquiry form to allow you to request information, products, and services. We collect your contact information (such as your e-mail address or your company information) to allow us to send samples, orders, information about our products, and promotional materials. Your information may be shared with contractors of Fresh Promotions for the purpose of performing services for Fresh Promotions related only to your order or sample request. Fresh Promotions may release personal identifiable information when required to by law wherein we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal process.
