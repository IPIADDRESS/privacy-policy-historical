Privacy Policy

In plain English, we respect your privacy: your information is not sold, rented or given to anyone outside FreeEmailTutorials.com ("Free Email Tutorials"). But we do encourage you to send us your feedback on our email tutorials and articles.

Information Collected

Aside from information explicitly requested or given, some forms on this site collect your public IP address, (a unique number assigned by your Internet Service Provider when you are online.) The IP address will be used in case of abuse, including but not limited to repetitive form submissions. We also use web analytics software to keep track of trends, such as successful tutorials, what traffic comes from search engines, etc. in order to improve user experience and information relevance.

Third party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits; Google's use of the DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page. Third-party vendors and ad networks may be serving ads on this website, and you may visit those vendors' websites to opt out of cookies (or use previous link).

Information Submitted

Information you may provide through this website is used solely by Free Email Tutorials. However, most information is sent unencrypted (plain text) through email forms. Please do not include any sensitive data in these forms.

Any information transmitted through this website becomes the property of Free Email Tutorials. Please do not send ideas or suggestions unless you are willing to unconditionally relinquish their ownership to Free Email Tutorials.

Although it is unlikely to change, this Privacy Policy is subject to change at any time, without prior notice.
