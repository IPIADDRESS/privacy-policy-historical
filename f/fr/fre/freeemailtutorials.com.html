Privacy Policy

In plain English, we respect your privacy: your information is not sold, rented or given to anyone outside FreeEmailTutorials.com. But we do encourage you to send us your feedback on our email tutorials and articles. The future of this website is in your hands.

Information Collected

Aside from information explicitly requested or given, some forms on this site collect your public IP address, (a unique number assigned by your Internet Service Provider when you are online.) The IP address will be used in case of abuse, including but not limited to repetitive form submissions. We also use web analytics software to keep track of trends, such as successful tutorials, what traffic comes from search engines, etc. in order to improve user experience and information relevance.

Information Submitted

Information you may provide through this website is used solely by FreeEmailTutorials.com. However, most information is sent unencrypted (plain text) through email forms. Please do not include any sensitive data in these forms.

Any information transmitted through this website becomes the property of FreeEmailTutorials.com. Please do not send ideas or suggestions unless you are willing to unconditionally relinquish their ownership to FreeEmailTutorials.com.

Although it is unlikely to change, this Privacy Policy is subject to change at any time, without prior notice.
