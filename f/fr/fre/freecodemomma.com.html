My Privacy Policy...

The following statement outlines my privacy policy.

I do not require visitors to provide any personal information to use the publicly available features of my site.

The Information I Collect From Users and Members
FreeCodeMomma.com may collect personally identifiable information from Members in a variety of ways, including through online forms for registering, and other instances where Members are invited to volunteer such information. FreeCodeMomma may also collect information about how Users use our Web Site, for example, by tracking the number of unique visits received by the pages of the Web Site, or the domains from which Users originate. I may use "cookies" to track how Users use my Web Site (see below). While not all of the information that I collect from Users is personally identifiable, it may be associated with personally identifiable information that Users provide me through my Web Site.

Clickstream Data
I use the aggregate data collected from my site logs to analyze site traffic. This information is not personally identifiable.

Cookies
I use cookies to help me determine how long visitors stay on the site, which parts of the site are most interesting to visitors, which content or sites visitors link to, and which services visitors use.

I currently use third party ad networks to serve ads on my site. The cookies received with banner ads served by these networks are used by these companies to deliver targeted ads.

If you need further information, please let me know at the Privacy Department.
