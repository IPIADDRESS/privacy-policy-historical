FreeIndex.co.uk - Privacy Policy

Home > Privacy Policy

General

  • FreeIndex Ltd collects personal information about you when you create an account. FreeIndex.co.uk will not rent, sell, or share information about you (the user) with anyone - we care about your privacy. Any other data collected on the site is purely for the purpose of running the site and will not be shared, rented or sold.

  • We will not distribute any email addresses to any third party or associated company of FreeIndex.co.uk or use them for anything other than their intended purpose.

  • We reserve the right to send registered users certain communications relating to the FreeIndex.co.uk service, such as service announcements, administrative messages and the FreeIndex.co.uk Newsletter (when subscribed to), that are considered part of your FreeIndex.co.uk account.

  • Emails sent through the site, including contact form enquiries, recommendations and review requests are one-off emails.

Cookies

  • FreeIndex Ltd may set and access basic cookies on your computer as a site user. These cookies are used purely to enhance your user experience and are kept to a minimum. Most cookies are session cookies that expire and are removed when you end your session. You are free to turn cookies off using your browser settings but certain sections of the site will no longer work without cookies enabled.

  • We reserve the right to set Google DART cookies which users can opt out of.

  • More information about cookies is available on this site.

Confidentiality and Security

  • We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs.

  • We have physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with regulations to protect personal information about you.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

  • FreeIndex Ltd may update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your Business Listing or account username, or by placing a prominent notice on the site.
