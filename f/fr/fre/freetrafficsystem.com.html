(i) to allow customers to access limited-entry areas of FreeTrafficSystem.com’s site;

(iii) to develop and provide our web site and our products and services for our customers;

FreeTrafficSystem.com limits its collection of personal information to only that information which is necessary for the purposed mentioned in article 3 of this Privacy Policy. FreeTrafficSystem.com does not sell, rent or disclose your personal information to anyone else, except:

(i) for any of the purposes mentioned in article 3 of this Privacy Policy;

(ii) to FreeTrafficSystem.com’s employees, independent contractors, subsidiaries, affiliates, consultants, business associates, service providers, suppliers and agents, acting on FreeTrafficSystem.com’s behalf for any of the purposes mentioned in article 3 of this Privacy Policy;
