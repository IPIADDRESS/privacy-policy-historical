Privacy Policy

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes your ip address or domain, but not the e-mail address (where possible), the date and time you access our site; the Internet address of the Web site from which you linked directly to our site; and your Operating System and Browser. We use the information to measure visitors. The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web page, used to customize the content and/or layout of our page for each individual visitor, used to notify members about updates to our Web site, and not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.

 We also maintain information volunteered by site visitors, including contest/sweepstakes entries; survey information; board/site registrations; additions to reviews, and other databases for the same purposes.

With respect to cookies: We use cookies to store visitors preferences, record past activity at our site in order to provide better service when visitors return to our site, customize Web page content based on visitors' browser type or other information that the visitor sends.

You may sign up for opt in mailing lists on our site. We do not share any of the information you provide for commercial purposes.

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending us e-mail at smnoel@gmail.com This e-mail address is being protected from spambots. You need JavaScript enabled to view it or simply unsubscribe from the opt in mailing lists. On the forums, if you are subscribed to receive email from admin, either contact us to unsubscribe or access your user control panel and adjust your settings.

With respect to Ad Servers: We try and bring you offers that are of interest to you, we have relationships with other companies that we allow to place ads on our Web pages. Because of your visit to our site, ad server companies may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and click stream information

With respect to security: We always use industry-standard encryption technologies when transferring and receiving consumer data exchanged with our site, When we transfer and receive certain types of sensitive information such as financial or health information, we redirect visitors to a secure server.


Off-Site Links:
Once you access another Web site through a off-site link, whether to an advertiser or to an informational site, you are no longer on FrugalVillage.com, and are then subject to the privacy policy of the specific Web site containing that material.
