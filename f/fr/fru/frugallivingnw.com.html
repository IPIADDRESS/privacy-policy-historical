None of this information can personally identify specific visitors to this site. The information is tracked for routine administration and maintenance purposes, and lets us know what pages and information are useful and helpful to visitors.

Controlling Your Privacy

About

The goal of this blog is to help each Frugal Living NW reader save money, not just for the sake of having more money but to help each of you reach your financial goals. We focus on deals in and around the Pacific Northwest, especially Oregon and SW Washington.

Please become involved in the Frugal Living NW community:
- Comment with your deals and questions.
- Get the deals sent to your email.
- Contact us with ideas and suggestions.
- Post your own sweet deals on our Facebook page.
- Follow Frugal Living NW on Twitter.

Help us continue to make Frugal Living NW a valuable resource for people in the Pacific Northwest!
