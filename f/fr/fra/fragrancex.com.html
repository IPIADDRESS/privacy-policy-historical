Directions for ordering online
To place an order, simply find the product you would like either by using the department tabs or the search feature on top of every page. When you find the product you would like, simply check off the "add" box next to the size or type of the fragrance you would like and click "add to cart". The next screen will allow you to update quantities or remove an item from your cart. To continue either repeat the above steps to find another product, or click "proceed to checkout". If you have an account, enter the login information to checkout, otherwise complete your shipping and billing information, then payment information and click "Place Order" to complete your order. We recommend printing your confirmation page for your records. We appreciate your business and hope you enjoy our service.
