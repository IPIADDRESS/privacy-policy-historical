Information note on personal data protection
The personal data you provide, by means of registration to the FerrariStore website or by  submitting a purchase order, to Ferrari S.p.A. ('Ferrari') and T-Buy S.r.l. Navigation data
T-Buy's web servers automatically record certain information concerning the electronic access systems you use, such as the name of your Internet supplier, the Internet page it comes from, the Internet page(s) visited on our site, the date and the length of the visit.   
IP Address
The identification of the IP addresses is necessary for the management of our Internet site and in particular for the diagnosis and prevention of technical problems, unauthorised intrusion and/or possible excessive use of various services offered by the site. IP addresses are not usually connected to information allowing to  identify you.   
 
Cookies
During your navigation inside our web site, information is gathered that may allow your computer or browser to be identified through files called 'cookies'.  These 'cookies' are a series of encrypted texts that are stored by a website on the user's computer so as to allow quick identification the next time you visit the web site. These files allow the personalisation of a website, provide easier browsing to the user and, through a quantitative verification of the accesses to various web pages, a better presentation of the most requested information. Generally, the cookies we use make use of anonymous information and therefore do not allow the processing of your personal details. Furthermore, the cookies can be used for memorizing your login information and thus make your recognition automatic (consequently, the insertion procedure of User Name & Password is unnecessary).
You can always ask for the cookies to be deactivated, but this deactivation may slow down or prevent access to certain parts of the site.  
 
Except for the navigation information above mentioned, we do not collect any other personal information, except of course for the data that you will be providing when you register with the Ferrari Store site or, if ever, when you register again, take part in a poll or for the execution of an agreement, etc.
