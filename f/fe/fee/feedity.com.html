Privacy Policy

We may change the terms of this Policy from time to time. We recommend that you read it periodically.

The fields for compulsory completion will be explicitly indicated. If you do not enter the requisite data in these fields, you will be unable to register with Feedity.

We may collect further information when you use Feedity. For example, we may record the frequency and scope of your use of Feedity, the duration of your sessions, feeds from public web pages that you use or create, advertisement that you click on, the Internet protocol (IP) address that serves you to access Feedity and the geographic location from where you logged-in. We automatically gather this information, through the use of log files. If you are registered with Feedity, the information we gather may be anonymous or in some instances personally identifiable. If you are not a registered user, yet accessed the site, we will not knowingly collect any information which may personally identify you.

Further information may be collected when we exchange communications with you, or when our designated copyright agent is in contact with you with respect to your content on Feedity.

What shall we do with your personal information?

Cookies

Cookies are packets of information sent by our servers to your web browser and then sent back by the browser each time it accesses our servers. Feedity utilizes cookies and in order to use the service you must enable cookies in your web browser.

Our cookies may contain a variety of information, such as the feeds you have accessed, session durations and IP addresses. We may also use cookies to save you the need to re-enter your user name and password each time you log-in on Feedity.

We may use cookies for various purposes, such as to facilitate the use of Feedity, to collect statistical information about the use of Feedity, to verify information, to adjust Feedity to your personal preferences and for information security purposes.

Some of the Cookies may expire when the session ends and you exit your browser. Other cookies are saved on your computer?s hard drive. If you wish to block our cookies, you may do so by clicking the "options" button on your browser's menu and follow the necessary instructions. You can read your browser's Help file of for further information. However, bear in mind that disabling cookies may complicate or even prevent the use of Feedity, or certain features thereof.
