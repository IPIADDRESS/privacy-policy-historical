Personal Information is collected in order to:

Please note that in the event that your financial Data consists of a low-fee portfolio or investment strategy that we believe can help other members of the community, we may post on our Site your financial Data in a completely anonymous way that does not identify you or reveal your level of assets.

The cookies used by the Service are created per session and registration does not include any information about you, other than your session key (removed as your session ends [usually after 24 hours]) and the ability to login again quickly (removed after two years).
