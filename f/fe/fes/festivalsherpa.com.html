Event Details

Rising up from the relatively quiet backdrop of Enschede, east Holland, Marc Vano and Ivo de Jong are making some serious noise with their fresh approach to electronic music. East & Young are purveyors of a sound that is rooted firmly in the big room/progressive sound, but also draws on influences from far and wide.

East & Young’s ability to put their unique spin on the work of other artists has opened the gates to a batch of remix work for the likes of Kaskade (Ultra), DJ Fresh and Dizzee Rascal (Ministry Of Sound), Kings of Tomorrow (Defected), Foster The People (Sony Music), Nadia Ali, Starkillers & Alex Kenji (Strictly Rhythm), and Sandro Silva (Spinnin’), and more recently Galantis (Big Beat)

The stakes are higher as more young people become enamoured with the music but, in typically laid-back Dutch style, Marc and Ivo relish the new challenges offered by this growth in the dance market: “It’s good for us because the competition is really high, so you have to do your best to keep on making great music”

The youngster, billed as one of the most skilled & sought after DJ’s in the states has built a reputation as one of dance music’s fastest rising producers owing to his incredible turntable skills. Performing nationally and making waves in the EDM world, he has been producing some of the biggest remixes you can find on the internet.

Within 3 years he went from teaching himself how to produce to being called to remix for Superstars : Chris Brown, Far East Movement, Capital Cities & Zedd to name a few. With countless #1 hits on the Infamous Hype Machine, he has been featured on nearly every major music blog and has over 3 million SoundCloud plays.

Panic City has played festivals and major stages alongside EDM heavyweights Zedd, Dillon Francis, Chuckie, Afrojack, Dash Berlin, Carnage, and more at Wintersalt (SF), Soulsurf (Asia) and Soundscape Festival (Lake Tahoe), as well as being a frequent visitor to premiere nightclubs including Lavo & Daylight Beach Club in Las Vegas.
