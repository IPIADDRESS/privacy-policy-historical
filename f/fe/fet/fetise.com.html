Fetise - Privacy Policy

You Can Count On Us

Your privacy is important to us at Fetise.com. That's why we've outlined here the types of personal information that we receive and collect when you use this site; and some of the steps we take to safeguard your personal information. We hope this will help you to make an informed decision about sharing personal information with us.

Fetise.com strives to maintain the highest standards of integrity and discretion in all our operations. Likewise, we are dedicated to protecting the privacy of our customers, consumers and online visitors.

Use of Personal Information

Fetise.com collects personally identifiable information from the visitors to our website only on a voluntary basis. Personal information collected on a voluntary basis may include name, email address, postal address and telephone number. This information is collected if you request information from us, participate in a contest or sweepstakes, sign up to join our email list or request some other service or information from us.

We require these details only to provide you the services we offer. We use your address to deliver the order. We may use your phone number to help you resolve your queries while shopping. We notify you about our updates, promotional schemes through your email. You can inform us if you wish to stop receiving these mailers. We share your personal information with specific employees who are involved in the execution of your transaction and any employee who violates our privacy and/or security policies is subject to disciplinary action.

Once information is reviewed, it is discarded or stored in our files. If we make material changes in the collection of personally identifiable information we will inform you by placing a notice on our site. Under no circumstances, however, will personal information received from any visitor be sold or provided to third parties.

Use of Cookies and Web Beacons

We may use cookies to enable you to personalize your online experience. Cookies are identifiers that are transferred to your computer's hard drive through your Web browser to enable our systems to recognize your browser.

The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalize the site pages, or register with any of our site's services, a cookie enables Fetise.com to recall your specific information on subsequent visits.

We may serve third-party advertisements that use cookies and web beacons in the course of ads being served on our web site to ascertain how many times you've seen an advertisement. However, no personally identifiable information you give us is provided to them for cookie or web beacon use, so they cannot personally identify YOU with that information on our web site.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies or get a notification when a cookie is being sent by modifying your Web browser; however, if you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of our site. Privacy software can be used to override web beacons. Taking either of these actions should not cause a problem with using our site.

Non-Personal Information

In some cases, we may collect information about you that is not personally identifiable. We use this information-which does not identify individual users , it is solely used to analyze trends and administer the site, to track users' movements around the site and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. The information collected is used solely for internal review and not shared

Updates and Effective Date

Fetise.com reserves the right to make changes in this policy. If there is a material change in our privacy practices, we will indicate such changes on this site. We encourage you to periodically review this policy so that you will know what information we collect and how we use it.

Agreeing to Terms

If you do not agree to Fetise.com Privacy Policy as posted here on this website, please do not use this site or any services offered by this site.
Your use of this site indicates acceptance of this privacy policy.
