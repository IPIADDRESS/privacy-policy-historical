  Privacy Statement for Federaljobsearch
  Return to Federal Jobs Homepage
  Federaljobsearch has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: Federaljobsearch.com.

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. We use cookies for other purposes.

Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information (like their name and email address). The customer's contact information is used to send matches to their search profile. Our customer's email addresses and other personal information are NOT shared with our advertisers or any other parties. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.

This site contains links to other sites. Federaljobsearch is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
  Security
  This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Customers profiles are protected by userid and password access only. We do not store any financial information such as customer credit card information or checking account data. All payments and orders are processed by our highly respected, secure transaction processing partner, Itransact Corporation.
  Choice/Opt Out
  Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us at any time.

This site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service.

  1. You can send email to jobs@federaljobsearch.com
  Correct/Update
  This site gives users the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided.
  1. Send an email to jobs@federaljobsearch.com
  2. Visit http://www.federaljobsearch.com, enter your email address and password, and then modify your profile.
  3. Each email job report contains links to allow customers to modify or inactivate their profile.
