Hi Unregistered,

Update:

The F11 golden release is here! So far, the stampede we expected hasn't arrived. As expected, F11 has not been entirely trouble free, but most folks are having little or no trouble with it, and installs are going smoothly.

Remember folks, fedora is a bit of a cutting edge FOSS based distro, so system requirements may well include a sense of adventure and a willingness to fiddle and futz a bit to get things like you want them.

However, one issue seems to be consistent. for those installing from the liveCDs, check the announcement about partitioning requirements!

So far, increased loads haven't been a problem, but if that situation develops, we will still restrict features as we had planned.

Thank you for your patience and your continued support of both fedora and the fedora forum.

Dan
