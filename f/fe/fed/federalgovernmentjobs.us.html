Privacy Statement and Terms of Service

Privacy

federalgovernmentjobs.us values our guests' privacy. We evaluate our business partners and advertisers to ensure similar commitment.

Information Collected

federalgovernmentjobs.us collects industry standard information on the use of the website. Industry standard information includes pages accessed, IP address, date and time of usage, referral page etc. This information may indicate our guests' Internet Service Provider, company or government agency name if accessed from work, location (city), speed of internet access (dial-up, DSL, cable etc), linking webpage and search terms used to locate our website, and browser and operating system brand and version.

We collect information about our guests' usage of external links listed on our website and services. This includes links to agency, online application, and advertiser websites. Advertisers may also provide us with industry standard information about our guests' actions on their websites. These report may include whether a sale, registration, download or other actions was take, items ordered, the amount of a sale, etc.

federalgovernmentjobs.us collects and examines the search terms from our full site search, job search, email subscription and similar features.

federalgovernmentjobs.us utilizes cookies for the website to operate properly. The cookies do not contain and are not used to gather personal information.

In the future, if we collect or evaluate additional information, we will post the details on this webpage.

Information Usage

federalgovernmentjobs.us uses industry standard third party web analytics tools to help evaluate information we collect.

federalgovernmentjobs.us evaluates information collected on an aggregate level to improve our website and services and to ensure our continued operations. Occasiaonlly we may share select portions of this aggregate data (such as overall number of guests, number of email subscribers, etc.) with our business partners and advertisers.

federalgovernmentjobs.us evaluates information collected on an individual level only to resolve technical issues with the website and services. We do not allow any third parties to evaluate individual level information for any reason.

Individual level data not essential to a requested service (such as email subscriptions) will not transfer and will be destroyed should ownership change or on major changes to these policies.
