Privacy Policy

This policy applies to Squiz Pty Limited and Squiz UK Limited ("Squiz") and explains how Squiz uses personal information collected through this website.

Squiz is legally bound to tell you what information is being collected and what it will be used for. There are two types of information collection that occur on this website:

  • Direct collection (i.e. Personal Information you provide): We store the personal information you enter on the Squiz web server and download copies of information from time to time. Most information of this type is collected through forms you fill in such as the "request more information form". In all cases of direct collection Squiz will state what the information will be used for. If any personal information is to be available for public access this will be explicitly stated at the time of information collection (for example the community forums).
  • Automatic collection: We may use a feature of your browser called a cookie to assign your computer a 'User ID'. Cookies do not contain information by which Squiz can identify you. They identify your computer to our servers. You may configure your browser so that you are notified before a cookie is downloaded or so that your browser does not accept cookies. Squiz may use information contained in cookies to make assumptions about the user of the computer and to provide users of that computer with focused advertising which Squiz believes may be of interest, based on that information. To disable your cookies, please refer to the help menu within your Internet browser.

Squiz may use information it collects from you for the primary purpose for which it is collected and for such other secondary purposes that are related to the primary purpose of collection.

Latest Sites

BDO

November 2010

BDO is the fifth largest full-service audit, tax and advisory firm in the world. BDO has more than 46,000 people across 1,138 offices in over 110 countries contributing to a combined worldwide revenue of $5.026 billion. BDO did about 70% of their own implementation on the new BDO Australia website and called on Squiz Support and the Squiz Brisbane office for help when assistance was required. Well done BDO!

http://www.bdo.com.au/

Cairns Regional Council

November 2010

The Cairns Regional Council area is a unique environment and is renowned nationally and internationally as a premier tourism destination. The region attracts more than two million domestic and international visitors each year. It is the only place in the world where two world heritage areas meet - the Great Barrier Reef and the Wet Tropics rainforest. The Cairns Regional Council website is one of the first to implement the new Squiz Easy Suite which enables non technical and occasional users to edit content in Squiz Matrix using an interface styled to look and feel similar to Squiz CMS.

http://www.cairns.qld.gov.au/

Discover Tasmania

September 2010

A stylish new face for the Discover Tasmania website portal went live on 24th September, 2010. Squiz Matrix still handles the heavy lifting and back end integration while Squiz CMS handles the campaign landing pages.

http://www.discovertasmania.com

Department of Planning and Community Development

September 2010

The Department of Planning and Community Development (DPCD) in Victoria is responsible for managing the state’s planning system and building stronger communities. DPCD has recently moved 12 of their core sites from Rapid Web Publishing into one Squiz Matrix site utilising automated migration scripts. The intranet launched on September 9th and the revamped internet site launched on September 16th.

http://www.dpcd.vic.gov.au/

Victorian Volunteering Portal

September 2010

Victoria's Volunteering Portal is an online community and information resource for Victorian volunteers and volunteering organisations. The site utilises Funnelback search to return volunteer opportunities and was developed by Squiz to ensure it looks perfect in IE6. Further functionality including Create Opportunities, SMS / Email notifications will be introduced over the coming weeks.

http://www.volunteer.vic.gov.au

19SMS

September 2010

Mobile Premium Services or 19 SMS Services are information and entertainment services that deliver various forms of premium content to mobile phones. The 19SMS website provides a database for consumers of all 19 SMS "short codes", and tracks how short codes have been allocated over time. This Squiz Matrix implementation features some complex back end administration work that was custom built for Telco Aggregators and Regulators.

http://www.19sms.com.au/

SunWater

September 2010

SunWater is a bulk water infrastructure developer and manager playing a key role in Australia's water industry, owning and managing around $7 billion in water infrastructure assets and supplying approximately 40% of all water used commercially in Queensland. This new Squiz Matrix website features a public access interactive map showing water levels across SunWater's assets in Queensland. The maps show the capacity and current storage level (including a 12 month history) and also the blue-green algae level at the facility (if known).

http://www.sunwater.com.au/

Southern Cross Telco

August 2010

Southern Cross Telco is a nationwide telecommunications company providing "fixed" phone services such as local, STD, IDD and calls to mobiles. Southern Cross Telco is part of the M2 Telecommunications Group Ltd, Australia’s largest network-independent reseller of telecommunications services.

http://www.sctelco.com.au

Charles Sturt University (Redesign)

August 2010

Charles Sturt University has a long and proud history in education which began in the late 1800's when both the Wagga Wagga and Bathurst campuses were Experimental Farms. Following this was a progression in education, from teachers' colleges, an agricultural college, colleges of advanced education and then to the establishment of Charles Sturt University in 1989. Late last year Squiz Canberra provided Charles Sturt University with services for the Faculty of Science website MySource Matrix migration. This year Squiz has provided services to implement a sensational new look for the main website.

 http://www.csu.edu.au/
