SFCU'S PRIVACY PLEDGE

We respect your privacy. We will not share your individual customer information with third parties for any promotional purpose if you give us instructions to that effect.

We will:

  • Never sell our member list.
  • Collect and use information only for legitimate business purposes.
  • Maintain accurate records based on information provided to us.
  • Share information only with companies that assist us in providing products and services to you, our members. These companies will be given only information pertinent to the product or service and must agree to protect confidential information.
  • Provide information when legally required and for credit reporting and fraud prevention.
  • Maintain reasonable and adequate data security measures.
  • Comply with all applicable privacy laws.
