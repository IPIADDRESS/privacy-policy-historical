Privacy Statement

Online Privacy Policy

The Wrigley Company is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to our Web site. We will not collect any personal information (such as your name, address, telephone number, Social Security Number or email address) unless you provide it voluntarily.

Any personal information collected from visitors to our Web site is kept private by the Wrigley Company and is not shared, sold, or otherwise transferred to any third parties outside the Wrigley Company.

Note to Parents

To protect the interests of children, the Wrigley Company has no intention of collecting any personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age, without notice to and the consent of a parent or guardian.

Where appropriate, the Wrigley Company will specifically instruct children not to submit such information on our Web sites.

As stated above, any such information, if collected, will be kept private and will not be shared, sold, or otherwise transferred to any third parties outside the Wrigley Company.

The Wrigley Company reserves the right to change this privacy policy at any time and will immediately post the updated policy on this Web site.
