SkillPages Privacy Policy

SkillPages Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect and use the personal information you consent to share, and help you make informed decisions when using SkillPages, located at www.skillpages.com and its directly associated domains (collectively, “SkillPages” or “SkillPages Website”).

By using or accessing SkillPages, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

SkillPages collects personal information in the form of a User Profile. The User consents to upload and create a User Profile with SkillPages. The User also consents to having the User Profile accessible and available to all.

  1. EU SAFE HARBOUR PARTICIPATION

    We participate in the EU Safe Harbour Privacy Framework.

  2. INFORMATION THAT WE COLLECT AND HOW WE USE IT

    1. We collect information on Users in order to facilitate the operation of the SkillPages Platform.

    2. The User consents and knowingly chooses to disclose all information that is uploaded to the SkillPages Platform in the form of a User Profile. When you add to your User Profile with further information about yourself, you agree that any registration information you give to SkillPages will always be accurate, correct and up to date.

    3. The SkillPages Search Facility is available to all Users. Users are also invited to enter personal information which provides an enhanced set of search results.

      1. When you sign up for a SkillPages User Profile Account or other SkillPages services or promotions that require registration, we ask you for personal information. For certain services, such as our advertising programs, we also request credit card or other payment account information, which we maintain in encrypted form on secure servers. We may combine the information that you submit under your account with information from other SkillPages services, in order to provide you with a better experience and to improve the quality of our services. For certain services, we may give you the opportunity to opt out of combining such information.
      2. SkillPages provides an invitation service when you register with the Platform. If you choose (consent or opt in) to use our invitation service to tell a contact about our site, we will ask you for information needed to send the invitation, such as your contacts email address or we will ask you to consent to having your contact list(s) imported. We will send your contacts an email or instant message in your name inviting him or her to visit the site, and may send reminders to them. SkillPages stores this information to send invitations and reminders, to register a contact connection if your invitation is accepted, to allow you to see invitations you have sent and to track the success of our referral program. All information that you enter or upload about your contacts will be covered by the same terms of this privacy policy as cover your own information. SkillPages also stores this information (imported contacts information) to avoid duplication of contacts and to check when contacts, not invited by the User, join the SkillPages Platform through registration. SkillPages will also store the User’s access details in order to synch the contacts whenever the User logs in.
      3. When you visit SkillPages, we collect your browser type and IP address. This information is gathered for all SkillPages Users. In addition, we store certain information from your browser using ‘cookies’. A cookie is a piece of data stored on the User’s computer tied to information about the User. We send cookies to your computer or other device that uniquely identifies your browser. We use session ID cookies to confirm that Users are logged in. These cookies terminate once the User closes the browser. By default, we use a persistent cookie that stores your login ID (but not your password) to make it easier for you to login when you come back to SkillPages. You can remove or block this cookie using the settings in your browser if you want to disable this convenience feature. We use cookies to collect information about visitors’ use of the SkillPages website. The information collected by cookies enables SkillPages to understand the use of its site. This in turn helps to provide visitors with a better experience. SkillPages will not attempt to personally identify visitors from their IP addresses. We use cookies to improve the quality of our service, including storing of User preferences, improving search results and ad selection and tracking User trends, such as how people search. SkillPages also uses cookies in its advertising services to help advertisers and publishers serve and manage ads across the web. We may set one or more cookies in your browser when you visit a website, including SkillPages sites that use our advertising cookies and that view or click an ad supported by SkillPages’s advertising services.
      4. When you access SkillPages Services, our servers automatically record information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website. These server logs may include information such as your web request, Internet Protocol address, browser type, browser language, the date and time of your request and one or more cookies that may uniquely identify your browser.
      5. When you use SkillPages, you may set up your User Profile, form community networks, send messages, perform searches and queries, set up events, add applications, transmit information through various channels and send emails or other communications to SkillPages. We collect this information so that we can provide you the service and offer personalized features. In most cases, we retain it so that, for instance, you can return to view prior messages you have sent or easily see your community network list. When you update information, we usually keep a back-up copy of the prior version for a reasonable period of time to enable reversion to the prior version of that information.
      6. When you post User Content on the Site you do so at your own risk. We cannot and do not guarantee that User Content you post on the Site will not be viewed by unauthorised persons. We are not responsible for circumvention of any settings or security measures contained on the Site. You understand and acknowledge that, even after removal, copies of User Content may remain viewable in cached and archived pages or if other Users have copied or stored your User Content.
      7. SkillPages will store User’s access details so that when a registered User logs in and accesses the SkillPages Platform SkillPages will offer Users the option to sync their contacts. SkillPages will ask Users to consent to having their contacts synched. If you do not wish to have your contacts synched please unselect the synch button.
      8. SkillPages have the right to reformat and move User Profiles, and User Profile information. SkillPages reserve the right to remove, edit, move or close any account for any reason and to suspend or ban any User account for any reason.
      9. We offer some of our services on or through other websites. Personal information that you provide to those sites may be sent to SkillPages in order to deliver the service. We process such information under this Privacy Policy. The affiliated sites through which our services are offered may have different privacy practices and we encourage you to read their privacy policies.
      10. SkillPages may make available third-party applications through its SkillPages Platform. The information collected by SkillPages when you enable a gadget or other application is processed under this Privacy Policy. Information collected by the application or gadget provider is governed by their privacy policies.
      11. SkillPages may present links in a format that enables us to keep track of whether these links have been followed. We use this information to improve the quality of our search technology, customised content and advertising.
      12. This Privacy Policy applies to the SkillPages Platform and SkillPages Services only. We do not exercise control over the sites displayed as search results, products or services or links from within our various SkillPages Services. These other sites may place their own cookies or other files on your computer, collect data or solicit personal information from you.
    4. Any improper collection or misuse of information provided on SkillPages is a violation of the SkillPages Terms and Conditions of Use.
    5. SkillPages may also collect information about you from other sources, such as newspapers, blogs, instant messaging services and other Users of the SkillPages service through the operation of the service in order to provide you with more useful information and a more personalized experience.
    6. SkillPages only processes personal information for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy and/or the supplementary privacy notices for specific services. In addition to the above, such purposes include:
      1. Providing our services, primarily making User Profiles available to all;
      2. Providing more relevant search results by utilising data provided by the User;
      3. Auditing, research and analysis in order to maintain, protect and improve our services;
      4. Ensuring the technical functioning of SkillPages;
      5. Protecting the rights or property of SkillPages or our Users; and
      6. Developing new services.
    7. By using SkillPages, you are consenting to have your personal data transferred to and processed in such jurisdictions as is necessary.
  3. USE OF INFORMATION OBTAINED BY SKILLPAGES
    1. When you register with SkillPages, you create your own User Profile. We may occasionally use your name and email address to send you notifications regarding new services offered by SkillPages that we think you may find valuable.
    2. User Profile information is used by SkillPages primarily to be presented back to and edited by you when you access the service and to be presented to all.
    3. The User Profile information that you submit to SkillPages will be available to all Users of the SkillPages Platform. All User Profile information will be available in search results generated for Users and may be made available to third-party search engines. People who see your name in searches will be able to access your User Profile information without registering with the SkillPages Platform.
    4. User Profile information is used by SkillPages to improve the functionality of the SkillPages Platform.
    5. SkillPages may send you service-related announcements from time to time through the general operation of the service.
    6. Generally, you may opt out of such emails through your User Account Settings. SkillPages reserves the right to send you notices about your account even if you opt out of all voluntary email notifications.
    7. SkillPages may use information in your User Profile without identifying you as an individual to third parties. We do this for purposes such as aggregating how many people are in a network like a group of electricians, and also personalising advertisements and promotions so that we can provide you with up to date SkillPages promotions and sponsors of community groupings. We believe this benefits you. We may use information about you that we collect from other sources, including but not limited to newspapers and Internet sources such as blogs, instant messaging services, SkillPages Platform developers and other Users of SkillPages, to supplement your User Profile. Where such information is used, we generally allow you to specify in your User Account Settings that you do not want this to be done or to take other actions that limit the connection of this information to your User Profile (e.g. removing photo tag links).

If you are under 14, please do not attempt to register for SkillPages or send any personal information about yourself to us, (including your name, address, telephone number or email address). No one under the age of 14 may provide any personal information to SkillPages on the SkillPages Platform. In the event that we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under the age of 14 without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information.

Doing this allows the advertising network to recognise your computer each time they send you an advertisement in order to measure the effectiveness of their adverts and to personalise advertising content. In this way, they may compile information about where individuals using your computer or browser saw their advertisements and determine which advertisements are clicked. SkillPages does not have access to or control of the cookies that may be placed by the third-party advertisers.

Your User Profile information will be saved in the event that you wish to reinstate your User Profile information. When you delete your account however, it is permanently deleted.
