What personally identifiable information does SketchyMicro collect from you?

Whenever SketchyMicro collects personal information we make an effort to include a link to this Privacy Policy on that page.

When you visit a site on which we advertise and click through our advertisement, we may employ the use cookies pixel tags. The information that we collect through this technology is not personally identifiable.

 

When you call or write to our Customer Service
When you call or write to our Customer Service, we collect contact information, such as name, phone number, mailing address or e-mail address.

 

What are cookies and how does SketchyMicro use them?
SketchyMicro may use cookies to store and sometimes track information about you. A “cookie” is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer’s hard drive. Generally, we use cookies to:

Estimate our audience size and track interest in our services – Each browser accessing the SketchyMicro website may be given a unique cookie which is then used to determine the extent of repeat usage and to help target advertisements based on user interests and behavior.

Save user login information – Each user may have the option to stay logged in to their account, which is an option made possible through the use of cookies.

SketchyMicro also collects IP addresses for system administration and to report aggregate information to our partners or service providers.

SketchyMicor’s primary goal in collecting personal information is in providing you the best service possible through its website. The majority of information that is collected about you is for the purpose of verifying contact information and delivering requested information and services.

SketchyMicro may retain your contact information for as long as permitted by law and as long as is necessary to permit you to use our services without the requirement of resubmitting the same information.

In the event of such an occurrence, every effort will be made by SketchyMicro to inform you of the transfer of your personal information.

Your information may be disclosed as is required by our insurers and by regulatory agencies or to employees, contractors, consultants, and agents of SketchyMicro to perform necessary actions in the normal course of business.
