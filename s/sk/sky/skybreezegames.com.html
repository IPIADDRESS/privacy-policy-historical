Privacy Policy

We at Sky Breeze™ Games agree that information given to us by you or your child will remain private. If you are under the age of 13, your parent or guardian must read this policy with you. The privacy policy is intended to let you know how Sky Breeze™ Games protects your personal information.

Personal Information Purposes:
We use children's information only for our internal purposes to enable them to send electronic e-greeting cards to their friends or to express their opinions of our web site. The information will be used strictly to ensure that our site offers our members the games and activities they prefer. We will use the information to tailor our site to the age of our members, make changes to the Sky Breeze™ cast of characters based on members preferences and offer games that interest our players. Your child will not be asked to give any information that is not related to the Sky Breeze™ site.

If your child chooses to send an electronic e-greeting card from our site, he or she will be asked to enter an email address and first name, but we do not require players to submit personally identifiable information about themselves in order to send an electronic e-greeting card. We do, however, ask players to submit a first name and email address of the recipient of the electronic e-greeting cards.

Sky Breeze™ Games reserves the right to contact your child's email address for notification purposes. If you sign up for the Sky Breeze™ web site, your child will be sent notices of changes, coming events. No personal correspondence from the directors of Sky Breeze™ Games directly to your child will be allowed.

Viewership by a third party:
Viewership of your personal information by a third party is limited to VeriSign, our online payment service. Information given to VeriSign, such as credit card number or billing address, is protected on their web site - www.verisign.com. Please review their policy before deciding to use our web site. Personal information given directly to Sky Breeze™ Games will not be shared with a third party.

Cookies
Cookies are small text files that we place in visitor's computer browsers to store their preferences. Cookies cannot damage user files, nor can they read information from a user's hard drive. Cookies allow web sites to "remember" users activities on a web site as well as users visits to a site. We do not store any personal information into cookies or link cookies to any personal information that players submit online when participating in our activities. You can, of course, disable cookies on your computer by indicating this in the preferences; however, some of the functioning of features on the web site may be affected.

Changes to this Privacy Policy
We reserve the right to change this policy at any time. We ask that you review this Privacy Policy from time to time to insure you are familiar with the most current version of it.

Acceptance of this Privacy Policy
By using this web site, you agree to the terms as well as any posted changes to this Privacy Policy.

If you do not agree to the terms, please do not use our web site.
