Cookies

We may send a "cookie" to your computer. A cookie is a small piece of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. A cookie does only store textual data and cannot be executed or run. Therefore, it cannot read data off your hard disk or read cookie files created by other sites. SRS uses cookies to track which sections of the SRS Network you have visited and how you used them. You can choose whether to accept cookies or if you want to be notified when a cookie is sent by changing the settings of your browser. If you choose not to accept cookies, your experience at our website and other websites may be diminished and some features may not work as intended.
