Privacy Policy

All SCBWI information is the intellectual property of the SCBWI and is to be used only with written permission by the SCBWI Headquarters.

All email addresses and other contact information on the SCBWI website are intended for personal use by SCBWI members wishing to connect with other members. or their Regional Advisors.  Generic email addresses posted are for informational use only and may not be used for any solicitation or advertising. Unsolicited advertising or emails of any kind sent to any SCBWI member including, but not limited to, Regional Advisors, Assistant Regional Advisors, Illustrator Coordinators and any employee of SCBWI,  is prohibited.  Parties engaged in solicitation inviolation of this policy may be subject to legal action as well as immediate termination of the violator's SCBWI membership.

Unless we have your express permission, SCBWI does not share your contact information with anyone, other than reputable contractors doing work for SCBWI (such as the mailing house that handles our Bulletin). Such contractors are never allowed to retain or use your contact information except for the purpose of the work we've assigned them.

Types of personal information we keep in our databases

We keep in our databases your name, contact information, billing information, and publishing history, including your current publisher and literary agent.

How we use your information

We use your personal information to provide and improve our services, for billing, for identification of authentication of members (for member programs and services), to contact members and for internal research. We also periodically send emails to member groups notifying you of various SCBWI events. If you do not wish to receive such notifications, you may choose to opt out either by calling us at 323-782-1010 or clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any SCBWI email.

We don't share your information with third parties without permission

When asked, unless you have advised SCBWI in writing to the contrary, inform people of your agent's name and contact information. SCBWI does not share your other personal information with anyone, other than reputable contractors doing work on its behalf, unless you've expressly permitted SCBWI to do so.  An example of a reputable contractor is the mailing house that handles our Bulletin.  Such contractors are never allows to retain or use your contact information except for the purpose of the work SCBWI has assigned them.

Changes

SCBWI may periodically update this policy. SCBWI will notify you about significant changes in the way it treats your information by sending a notice to the primary e-mail address specified in your membership record or by placing a prominent notice on our site.

Questions

Any questions about this Privacy Policy should be addressed to sararutenberg@scbwi.org or by mail at: 8271 Beverly Blvd., Los Angeles, CA 90048.
