Privacy Policy

Updated April 10, 2005


Eduphoria! This includes information derived from registration, subscription, and use of Eduphoria! software.


Collection / Use of Information

Eduphoria! shall collect and use customer identifiable information for billing, provisioning of services and software, to solve problems associated with software, and to inform customers of new products or services that will better meet their needs. Eduphoria! will use customer identifiable information to market products and services to the customer, but will not disclose or make available any customer identifiable information to any third parties seeking to market products. If a customer chooses not to participate in direct marketing of new products and services from Eduphoria!, that customer’s information will not be used for the purpose of marketing new products.


Security

Eduphoria! has invested and deployed a wide variety of technology and security features to ensure the privacy of customer information on its network. In addition, Eduphoria! has implemented strict operations guidelines to ensure customer privacy is safeguarded at every level of its organization. Eduphoria! will continue to revise policies and implement additional security features as new technologies becomes available.


Communications

Eduphoria! will not read, listen to or disclose to any third parties private e-mail, conversations, or other communications that are transmitted using Eduphoria! services except as required to ensure proper operation of services or as otherwise authorized by law.


Unlawful Activity

Eduphoria! may use customer identifiable information to investigate and help prevent potentially unlawful activity or activities that threaten the integrity of service or network integrity or otherwise violate Eduphoria!’s Service Level Agreement.


Account Information

Eduphoria! will do its best to honor requests from customers for account information, for example, name, address, company, or billing information. The customer is responsible for ensuring that the information on file with Eduphoria! is current and accurate.


Children’s Privacy Policy

Eduphoria! does not sell products or services for purchase by children. Eduphoria! does not knowingly solicit or collect customer identifiable information from children or teenagers under the age of eighteen. Eduphoria! may store information about students. This data will be maintained according to all Federal and State laws, and any local policies that are communicated to us.
