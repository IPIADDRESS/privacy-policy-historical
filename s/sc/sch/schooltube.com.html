Privacy                                                                                                                                                       

What SchoolTube's Guidelines Cover

• How SchoolTube handles personal information* it receives, both on and off the Internet;
• What information SchoolTube collects and how it collects it;
• What you can do to control the information about you that SchoolTube collected, including certain changes you can make to that information; and
• How SchoolTube protects information it collects from misuse.
* Personal information is information that identifies a person, such as their name, address, or email address, that is not otherwise publicly available.

General Guidelines For Information Collection and Use

• SchoolTube collects and compiles statistical information about all visitors to our web site, even if they haven't registered. The information used to compile statistics is passed from a visitor's computer to our server each time a page is requested. This statistical information is logged and provides long-term usage data and trends that help SchoolTube allocate resources and better meet demands during peak usage. This information is not linked to the end user and is not considered personal information. The information collected includes:

1. the type and version of your Operating System and Web Browser;
2. your computer's IP address;
3. the date and time of your visit;
4. what page you visited; and
5. what page you came from.

• When you register with SchoolTube, you are no longer anonymous. Your registration information is used to deliver SchoolTube education and outreach program material and to help SchoolTube develop future program material. Certain statistical information gathered from our subscribers is used to generate reports for governmental agencies. We must report this information because SchoolTube receives Grant money from the U.S. government. SchoolTube does not identify you by name in these reports; it only reports information in the aggregate.
• SchoolTube often collects information from people who attend SchoolTube activities or events or who our staff members meet during the course of their duties. If we believe these people may be interested in the SchoolTube program material, we elect to subscribe them. If so, we notify them via e-mail and ask whether or not they want this material.

Children

• SchoolTube will not ask a child or young person under the age of 13 for more information than is reasonable in order to provide that student with the appropriate Internet Safety information on Youth Empowerment which SchoolTube offers.
Information Sharing and Disclosure
• Other than the following instances, SchoolTube does not rent, sell or share personal information about you with any other person(s) or organization(s):

o SchoolTube may share your information with outsourced service providers in order to provide the materials you requested. For example, to fill an order for training materials, your name, address, and phone number may be provided to a third-party order filling agency so they can ship you the materials.
o When the law requires SchoolTube to respond to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal process, SchoolTube provides the information needed to obey the law. SchoolTube also may provide information in order to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims.
o SchoolTube believes it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of our terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.

• SchoolTube makes every reasonable effort to ensure the security of the data it collects. However, SchoolTube cannot warrant against the disclosure of this information in the event of a security breach or other event beyond our control.

Cookies

• To facilitate the experience our website strives to offer, SchoolTube may use cookies to store various pieces of information on your computer. The information in these cookies is only retrievable by SchoolTube servers. We are confident the information within the cookies is reasonably secure. Although vulnerabilities in this technology exist, by keeping your web browser up to date, it is unlikely the information in these cookies will be misappropriated.
• A cookie is a data file stored on your hard drive by your browser. It does not contain any executable code and cannot gather information from your computer that you have not already provided SchoolTube either in a form, or by requesting a page from our site (see "Information Collection and Use", above).
• Most browsers allow users to choose not to receive cookies. If you disable cookies on your browser, some pages on our site may not work correctly (or at all!), or some features may be disabled.
Your Ability to Edit Your Account Information
• New program information may be added from time-to-time. SchoolTube will attempt to anticipate your interest in this information based on information in your profile, and may sign you up automatically. You may opt-out of these services by editing your account information or re-registering with the same e-mail address (as described above).
• SchoolTube reserves the right to send important announcements or notices to all registered users without extending an opportunity to opt-out of such notices.
• SchoolTube reserves the right to maintain archival copies of contact data of our registrants. The primary purpose of this archive is to prevent accidentally re-adding you to our service if you have requested not to receive further information. Concerns about this archival copy may be addressed to the webmaster.

Confidentiality and Security

• SchoolTube limits access to your personal information to those SchoolTube employees who have a need to come into contact with that information for the purpose of providing program material to you.
• To protect your personal information, SchoolTube employs physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal regulations.
• Your personal information is password protected and, in some cases, encrypted for additional protection.

Changes to this Policy

• SchoolTube may update its privacy policy from time-to-time and will notify you about significant changes to its policy either by:

1. emailing your primary e-mail address registered with us, or
2. prominently displaying a notice on our website.

Other Notices

• On occasion, you may find links on our site that lead to pages provided by organizations other than SchoolTube whose content and associated privacy policy are beyond our control. SchoolTube makes every reasonable effort only to link to content it believes its patrons would approve. However, despite SchoolTube's efforts, you are strongly advised to make yourself aware of any privacy policies applicable to those pages, since SchoolTube cannot be held accountable for the content on, or linked to, such pages.
