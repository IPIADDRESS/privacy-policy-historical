Registering for an Account

In order to create an account, uses must provide information within a registration form.  There are 3 types of accounts that a user may choose to register for: Child Account, Parent Account, Organization (or “Moderator”).  Children under the age of 13 may not provide personally identifiable information, and due to the nature of the site, may not fully use the Services until a legal guardian / parent or approved teacher has given permission by creating a Parent Account or an Organization (“moderator”) account.  Parents and Teachers/Organizations MUST provide valid proof of their identity and adult status  before approving a child’s access to the Site.

 

Information Collected from Children under 13

SchoolTube will not ask a child or young person under the age of 13 for more information than is reasonable in order to provide that student with ability to request approval from a verified adult.   As stipulated by COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act), Schooltube may only ask for non-personally identifiable information, such as a username, password prior to their account creation approval by a verified adult. Schooltube will also request a parent or guardian (such as a Teacher) email address in order to attain approval and valid permission to use the Site.

 

Information Collected in Registration

  • Adult Approved, Child Account: age, username, password, first name, last initial, school name and a parent or guardian (Teacher) email address.
  • Parent Account: First name, last name, address, age, and either form of electronic identity verification: valid Social Security number or valid Credit Card
  • Organization: Organization name, address, point person (Teacher) first name and last name, and either form of electronic identity verification: valid social security number or valid credit card number, or a signed/uploaded legal agreement with a valid signature.

 

Unsolicited Information (User Generated Content)

Schooltube does not allow children under the age of 13 to provide User Generated Content of any type, outside of registration, to the live Site.  A child must be approved by a validated Adult Account (Parent or Teacher/Organization) in order to post content to the Site.

 

Content posted to the Site is then regulated by the Terms of Service both the child and adult agree to at Account creation.

 

Organization “Moderators” and site filters are in place to screen and remove any additional form of Personally Identifiable Information considered unacceptable for Schooltube.com.  Accounts who break the rules concerned Personally Identifiable Information, or other conduct related issues that consider privacy, may be permanently deactivated, or in extreme cases, reported to local authorities for additional review.
