Privacy Policy

SchoolSpring.com, inc. respects the privacy of its users and their right to restrict the use of their personal information. The services provided by SchoolSpring.com require personal information be collected from nearly every user. This information is securely stored, to the best of SchoolSpring.com's abilities, in order to provide the services offered to users seeking jobs posted by SchoolSpring.com clients. User information is accessible to members at any time upon providing a valid username and password on the SchoolSpring.com web site.

Information Collected
SchoolSpring.com collects personal contact information, including address and phone number; social security number; professional information, including work experience, education experience, and academic performance; and job search preferences of its members.

Use of Information
SchoolSpring.com uses the information stated above in order to provide resume searching capabilities to its clients, with the exception of those restricted by the SchoolSpring.com member; to notify members when new jobs are posted that match their job search preferences; and to facilitate and improve the recruiting and hiring process for SchoolSpring.com clients and members.

SchoolSpring.com never sells information about its members to third parties or shares information about its members with third parties without their permission. SchoolSpring.com reserves the right to update this policy at any time without prior notice to members.
