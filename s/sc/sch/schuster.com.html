Privacy Policy

SimonSays.com and SimonSaysShop.com are maintained by Simon & Schuster Online, 1230 Avenue of the Americas, New York, NY, 10020, and are members of the Simon & Schuster family of companies. We can be reached by email at either: ssonline_feedback@SimonSays.com or Shop.Feedback@SimonSays.com.

Simon & Schuster recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of visitors to our web site. We do not collect personally-identifying information about you except when you voluntarily provide it (for example, when you provide shipping and billing information for an online purchase, when you provide an email address or mailing address to receive additional news, offers, or prizes from Simon & Schuster, or when you post a message to a discussion board or to the Reader Comments area).

We consider the following to be personally-identifying information: your name, street address, phone number, credit card information and email address. Personally-identifying information is used by Simon & Schuster to process your online purchases, to inform you about other products and services, to improve the products and services we offer, and otherwise to better meet the needs and preferences of Simon & Schuster customers. Personally-identifying information is treated confidentially within Simon & Schuster. We do not share your personally-identifying information with third parties except for the data provided to those entities involved in processing and fulfilling your online orders. We may also share your personally-identifying information with affiliated companies. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Simon & Schuster is a responsible company and as such we may disclose your personally-identifying information in order to comply with court orders or subpoenas or when required to do so by law. We may also disclose such data in cooperation with a law enforcement or government request.

Users should be reminded that any personally-identifying information that is voluntarily posted by a user to a public area of this web site (for example, a posting to a discussion board or to the Reader Comments area) may always be collected and used by others. We cannot prevent such uses.

COOKIES
This site uses cookies to facilitate the checkout process. A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier that is sent to your browser from a web site's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive. If you choose to change your browser preferences to disable cookies, you will still be able to make a purchase in this store. However, you will have to re-enter your purchasing information each time you return to the store to make a purchase.

SECURITY OF PERSONAL INFORMATION
Please remember that no data transmission over the Internet, or by other means, can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. Thus, while we strive to protect your personal data, Simon & Schuster cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us. Once we receive your data, we use reasonable efforts to ensure the information you provide is secure and not readily available to unauthorized individuals or third parties. We use secure servers to protect all of your personal information.

LINKS
We are not responsible for the data collection practices of any sites linked to SimonSays.com or SimonSaysShop.com, nor does our linking constitute sponsorship or endorsement of the content or practices on these sites. When visiting linked sites, you should refer to each such sites individual privacy policy.

SPECIAL NOTICE TO PARENTS
At present, we do not collect personally-identifying information from children under age 13. If we decide to change that practice, we will post a notice of the change and will comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act. Simon & Schuster encourages you to ask your children to seek your permission before providing information about themselves or their family over the Internet.

SPECIAL NOTICE TO KIDS
Ask your parent or guardian for permission before you send any personal information over the Internet.

CONTACT INFORMATION
If you have questions or comments about our privacy policy or if you wish to remove your name from Simon & Schuster's databases, you may contact us by e-mail at either: ssonline_feedback@SimonSays.com or Shop.Feedback@SimonSays.com. Our privacy policies are under frequent review. We, therefore, encourage you to review this policy periodically.
