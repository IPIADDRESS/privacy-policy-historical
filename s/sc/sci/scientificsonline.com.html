Privacy Statement

This Statement discloses the privacy practices for www.scientificsonline.com.

We have designed scientificsonline.com so that no personal identifying information is displayed online or accessible to the general public. You can browse our Website without letting us know who you are.

To purchase from scientificsonline.com you must provide the following information: name, address, billing address, shipping address, telephone number and e-mail address. We will not sell this information to anyone.

The financial information you provide is encrypted using 256-bit Extended Validation SSL encryption and is verified by COMODO.

While we use encryption to protect sensitive information transmitted by our customers, we also protect your information offline. Our server, which stores customers' personal information, is kept in a secure environment and no credit card information is stored on our server.

Unless you ask us not to, we may contact you via email in the future to tell you about specials, new products and product/industry information.

As always, we appreciate your business!

We reserve the right to change our Privacy Statement at any time.
