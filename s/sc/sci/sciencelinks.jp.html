Privacy and Security Policy

Privacy Policy

JST is committed to the privacy of our visitors. JST collects no personal information about you when you visit our Website unless you choose to provide that information to us.

Here is how JST handles information about your visit to our Website.

Information Collected and Stored Automatically:

JST automatically collects and stores information such as the following concerning your visit. This information does not identify you personally:

  • Your IP address of the domain from which you access the Internet;
  • The type of browser and operating system used to access our site;
  • The date and time you access our site;
  • Pages accessed; and
  • Referring Website (if you linked to us from another Website).

This site-access information is aggregated and used only as source of anonymous statistical data to help us plan for enhancements to make our site more useful to visitors.

Personal Information:

You may choose to provide us with personal Information by filling out a form or sending an e-mail message containing your name, address, e-mail address, specific comments and /or questions. This information is used to respond to your request or otherwise resolve the subject matter of the e-mail. JST does not collect or use information for commercial marketing purposes.

Security Policy

For site security purposes and to ensure that this service remains available to all users, our computer system employs software programs to monitor network traffic to identify unauthorized attempts to upload or change information, or otherwise cause damage.
