Southern California Edison Online Privacy Notice

Privacy Notice

Last updated: November 2012

Southern California Edison (“SCE”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) wants you to know about the information we collect and use when visiting our website, either through your computer or mobile device, or as an SCE customer through our Advanced Metering Infrastructure, which include our SmartConnect and Real Time Energy Meter systems. We realize you expect SCE to appropriately protect your Personal Information from unauthorized access. SCE follows applicable laws, including those established by the California Public Utilities Commission, to protect your Personal Information.

PERSONAL INFORMATION

We define Personal Information as any information used alone or combined with other information that can be used to uniquely identify, contact or locate an individual or groups of individuals. Generally, this information includes names, home addresses, email addresses, account numbers, and energy usage information obtained through SCE’s Advanced Metering Infrastructure, which includes our SmartConnect and Real Time Energy Meter systems.

PRIVACY NOTICES

Please take a moment and review the below privacy notices to understand what information SCE may obtain when using our website or through our Advanced Metering Infrastructure.

  • Website Privacy Notice. This Notice describes SCE’s privacy policy for our website and explains what type of information is collected from you when you visit our website and how information may be used.
  • Notice of Accessing, Collecting, Storing, Using, and Disclosing Energy Usage Information. This Notice explains SCE’s privacy policy regarding certain energy usage information we collect if you receive electric utility services from us.
