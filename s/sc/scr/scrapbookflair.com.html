Privacy Policy
 
1. Your information is secure
We respect your privacy. Scrapbook Flair will never sell, rent, lease or even disclose customer information to third parties, unless required to do so by law.
2. Internet privacy
Scrapbook Flair does not share any personal information about you, or about your Internet usage with anyone, unless required to do so by law.
3. When you order from us
We collect information necessary to complete your order, including your name and email address. We use this information to send you the product registration code. We do not store your credit card information.
4. Unsubscribe from our mailings at any time
Occasionally - in order to offer products and services that may be of interest to you - we will communicate by email. If at any time you no longer want to receive our newsletters or product updates, just let us know and we will remove you from our email list.
   
(Scrapbook Flair reserves the right to change, modify, or update this statement at any time without notice.)
