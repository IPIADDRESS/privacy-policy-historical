Privacy Policy

Introduction

scrible, Inc. (“scrible”) has created this Privacy Policy in order to disclose its use of consumer data collected about you. This Privacy Policy is effective as of February 1, 2008. Any questions regarding this policy should be directed by email to privacy@scrible.com. You may also contact scrible at 204 E. 2nd Avenue, No. 102, San Mateo, CA 94401. The following discloses scrible’s information gathering and dissemination practices for its marketing website.

Information Collected and Received

scrible may collect or receive information about each person who registers with the scrible website including, but not limited to, user name, password, first and last name, email address, street address, gender, occupation, and interests. scrible also collects information on the pages you access and other information you may volunteer, such as survey information.

Uses of Information

We may make the email addresses of our users available to other organizations whose products or services we think you may find interesting. If you supply us with your postal address online or we receive it through one of our relationships, you may, receive mailings from other third party companies. These third party companies may use the information that you provide to scrible or scrible receives from other sources to deliver you mailings with offers about goods and services that may be of interest to you. We use a third party company to distribute our offers.

Except as described in this section, we will not disclose your personal information to any third party without notifying you of our intent to share the information and giving you an opportunity to prevent your information from being shared.

From time to time, we may partner with companies based on the interests of our users. These partner companies will never see your email address or any other information that could identify you or be used to contact you directly.

Any exceptions to this policy of sharing your name, address or email address with a partner company will be done only with your permission.

scrible reserves the right to disclose information when required by law.

We occasionally use other companies to perform services necessary to our operations. In the course of providing these services, those companies may have access to your personal information. By contractual agreement, those companies must treat your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. However, we will not be liable for any damages that may result from the misuse of your personal information by these companies.

scrible may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with scrible’s server and to administer scrible’s web site. Your IP address may also be used to help identify you and your online profile and to gather broad demographic information.

scrible’s site’s registration form requires users to give scrible contact information (such as your name and email address) and demographic information (such as your zip code, age, or income level). scrible uses customer contact information from the registration form to send the user information about scrible and promotional material from some of scrible’s partners. The customer’s contact information is also used to contact the user when necessary. scrible may use demographic and/or profile data to tailor the visitor’s experience on scrible’s site, show the visitor content that scrible thinks you may be interested in, and display content according to your preferences. The demographic and profile data will be shared with advertisers ONLY on an aggregate basis.

We may also disclose, on an anonymous basis, statements made by our customers.

Security

scrible uses industry-standard technologies when transferring and receiving consumer data exchanged between scrible and other companies to help ensure its security. This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under scrible’s control. scrible’s servers are backed up regularly and protected by security systems.

Cookies

“Cookies” are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer’s hard drive. scrible’s site uses cookies to keep track of your session, shopping cart, and advertising delivery. scrible may also use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests and to save your password. scrible may use an outside ad company to display ads on scrible’s site. These ads may also contain cookies. While scrible uses cookies in other parts of scrible’s web site, cookies received with banner ads are collected by scrible’s ad company and scrible does not have access to this information.

Links

scrible may create links to other web sites. scrible will make a reasonable effort to link only to sites that meet similar standards for maintaining each individual’s right to privacy. However, many other sites that are not associated or authorized by scrible may have links leading to scrible’s site. scrible cannot control these links and scrible is not responsible for any content appearing on these sites.

Assignment

If there is a change of control in scrible business (whether by merger, sale, or otherwise) or if there is an asset sale, the user information, including personal information, could be sold as part of that transaction and your personally identifying information potentially could be used by the purchaser. However, if that business materially changes this statement or the information-handling practices as described in this Privacy Policy, you will be notified by email and/or through a notice posted on the Site, and you may opt-out of the use of your existing information in a new manner. This Privacy Policy inures to the benefit of any successors or assigns of scrible or the assets of scrible.

Amendments

scrible may amend this policy at any time. If scrible is going to use personally identifiable information collected through scrible’s corporate site in a manner materially different from that stated at the time of collection scrible will notify users via email and/or by posting a notice on scrible’s Web site for 30 days prior to such use. This Agreement may not be otherwise amended except in a writing that specifically refers to this Privacy Policy and is physically signed by both parties.

Contacts

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this web site, you can contact privacy@scrible.com. Upon request, scrible will provide you with access to information (e.g., name, address, phone number) that scrible collects and maintains about you. This site gives you the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided: (i) email: privacy@scrible.com; or (ii) visit scrible.wpengine.com. There you can also log into your account to update your contact information.

Opt-Out

scrible’s site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from scrible. To opt-out of receiving our communications, you can (i) send email to remove@scrible.com; or (ii) send postal mail to: 204 E. 2nd Avenue, No. 102, San Mateo, CA 94401.
