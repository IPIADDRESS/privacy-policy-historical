Privacy Policy

Safeguarding your privacy is important to us. Before you submit information through our Stein Mart-owned and operated website, we want you to know how it will be used and how it is protected. By using this site, you represent and agree that you have read this Privacy Policy and consent to the use of your personal information ("Information") by Stein Mart and others in accordance with its terms and for the purposes described below.

These published Terms and Conditions of Use govern any dispute that may arise concerning our Privacy Policy or your use of our site, including limitations on damages, arbitration of disputes, venue of disputes in Florida and application of Florida law.
In our Privacy Policy, you will learn:

  • what types of personal information may be collected at this site
  • how your personal information can be used
  • who may have access to the information collected
  • security procedures used to protect the information collected
  • how to contact us to correct or cancel any information sent

Types of information collected and logged at this website:
Information such as your name and address is collected through this Site and via emails you may send us. We maintain standard web logs that record data about all visitors to this site. These logs may contain (1) the internet domain name from which you access the Site (e.g. ail.com, abc.org); (2) your IP address; (3) the type of browser and operating system you use; (4) the date and time you visited this site; (5) the pages you saw on this site; and (6) the address of the website you linked here from. Lastly we connect information, including current session information via "cookies" when you access our site.
We maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with applicable law to protect any non-public personal information we collect.

Cookies:
Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that we transfer to your computer's hard drive through your web browser to enable our systems to recognize your browser and to provide certain features such as storage of items in your Shopping Cart between visits. The Help portion of the toolbar on most browsers will tell you how to prevent your browser from accepting new cookies, how to have the browser notify you when you receive a new cookie, or how to disable cookies altogether. However, cookies allow you to take full advantage of the features on the site, and we recommend that you leave them turned on.


How your Information is Used/Who has Access:
Affiliates/Joint Marketing:
We share information among our subsidiaries and affiliates in accordance with our security standards and confidentiality policies, and applicable laws. We may also disclose your information to third parties that perform marketing services on our behalf or to other companies with whom we have joint marketing agreements.

Business Partners and Agents:
We also may share information with non-affiliated business partners and agents with whom we have agreements to help perform functions on our behalf. Examples include filling orders, delivering packages, sending postal mail and e-mail, removing repetitive information from customer lists, analyzing data, providing marketing assistance, providing search results and links (including paid listings and links), processing credit card payments, and providing customer service. These parties have access to personal information needed to perform their functions, but may not use it for other purposes.

Business Transfers:
As we continue to develop our business, we might sell or buy stores, subsidiaries, or business units. In such transactions, customer information generally is one of the transferred business assets. In the event that Stein Mart or substantially all of its assets are acquired, customer information, including information you have provided to Stein Mart, will be one of the transferred assets.

Legal Protection of Stein Mart and Others:
We release account and other personal information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law; enforce or apply our Terms and Conditions of Use and other agreements; or protect the rights, property, or safety of Stein Mart, our users, or others. This includes exchanging information with other companies and organizations for fraud protection and credit risk reduction. This does not include selling, renting, sharing, or otherwise disclosing personally identifiable information from customers for commercial purposes in violation of the commitment set forth in this Privacy Policy.

With Your Consent:
Other than as set out above, you will receive notice when information about you might go to third parties. You will be given an opportunity to choose not to share the information.

Security of Information:
We work to protect the security of your information during transmissions by using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) software, which encrypts information you input. We reveal only the last five digits of your credit card numbers when confirming an order. Of course, we transmit the entire credit card number to the appropriate credit card company during order processing. It is important for you to protect against unauthorized access to your password and to your computer. Be sure to sign off when you have finished using a shared computer. You can access certain information we maintain about you by contacting us via email at comments@steinmart.com.
