Privacy Statement

Internet Radio Corporation Commitment to Privacy

Internet Radio Corporation, pledges to use information about our subscribers responsibly. This notice applies to all information submitted to the InsiderRadio.com website. Please read on for more details about our privacy policies.

What information do we collect?

When you browse insiderradio.com, you do so anonymously. Internet Radio Corporation does not collect personally identifying information about you.

The insiderradio.com website itself does not currently issue cookies. We do have many links to other websites on the Internet and we have no control over whether or not they issue cookies. (Cookies are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can change this feature in your browser).

If you decide to sign up for our free Insider TipLetter, we ask for your name, e-mail address and other pertinent information. This information is strictly confidential and used solely for our internal purposes. We will not sell this information, however, our sign-up provides a place where you may allow us to send you information from third parties that we think may be of interest to you. If you tell us that you do not want to receive this third party information, we will not send it to you.

If you enter a contest, participate in an online survey or use one of our other promotional features of our website, we may ask for your name, address, e-mail address and other pertinent information to administer the contest and tabulate survey results items. We will not give out this information.

Internet Radio Corporation may provide summary statistics about our subscribers and listeners' interests and preferences to reputable third parties. These statistics do not include personally identifiable information.

Internet Radio Corporation does not use or share personally identifiable information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described in this policy.

How the information is handled.

Personal data, which you have voluntarily entered, is delivered electronically to Internet Radio Corporation servers located in the United States. By using these forms, you consent to the transfer of this data, and to Internet Radio Corporation use of such data as outlined in this policy.
