Privacy Policy


Steel Guru is committed to protect personal information about you which is not publicly available elsewhere. You should read this privacy policy in order to understand what information we may collect and how we use it.

What types of personal information we collect and how we use it?

Personal Information

In the process of providing better service to you, Steel Guru may ask your personal information such as address, phone numbers, email etc. We do not rent, sell, or share personal information about you for marketing purpose without your explicit permission. This information is stored safely on our servers.

Statistical Data

Along with personal information we also keep track of Web sites or pages our subscribers visit via or on our site. We use this information for assessment of popular services and to make existing services better.

We may share certain gathered statistical information with advertisers and other partners. This information WILL NOT include personal details and will be in aggregate form only.

We may disclose your personal information, without notice, only to Government. If required, we may to do so by law to comply legal process served on us or to protect and defend our rights of property.

Use of cookies (Our data on your computer)

Steel Guru may set cookies on your computer to store your preferences. Steel Guru may allow to set cookies to other companies that show advertisements on some pages of our site. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to Steel Guru's cookies.

In case of Change of Policy

In case of significant changes to privacy policy, we will inform you about any change via email. In case you have any questions regarding policies mentioned here, you may contact us by sending us email at info@steelguru.com.
