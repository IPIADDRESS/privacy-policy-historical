Privacy Statement & Policy

Please read through this privacy policy carefully so that you understand what information we, StudyMalaysia, collect through our websites/education fairs/education events/career workshop/education related printed publications/course enquiry form/career interest survey form/education research form and what we do with that information.

It must be borne in mind that StudyMalaysia reserves the right to change, amend and/or vary this Privacy Policy at any time and will notify users of the same by updating the policy here and by including a "NEWLY UPDATED" label with the "PRIVACY POLICY" link on the web sites governed by this policy.

What data we collect

The personal information we collect about you may include but not limited to the following:

  • Name, addresses, telephone numbers, gender, citizenship, marital status, race, qualificationsand age / date of birth
  • Email addresses, fax numbers and mobile numbers or social media accounts
  • Education related information such as academic history, examination result, recent and previous educational institution attended, learning patterns, career interest and study options necessary to facilitate academic services.
  • Passport number, NRIC number, nationality and country of residence
  • Personal information provided via survey, contest, event, career workshop, competition or other marketing research efforts
  • Personal information provided to customer-service representatives to research and resolve issues or questions or lodging complain
  • Corporate-contract, institution, employer and/or other corporate affiliation (i.e. employer name, title, address and contact information)
  • Any such information as we deem necessary or appropriate from time to time in connection with your commercial relation with us

Sources of Information
Your Personal Data has and/or will be obtained from the following sources, where applicable, or such other sources which we may see fit from time to time:

  • information provided or submitted by you through, among others, as applicable, Enquiry Form, Activity Form, Survey Form and Membership Form;
  • from your usage of our websites and any other information you post on our website, facebook, email or otherwise send to us;
  • from printed publications, education fairs, education events, career workshops, interest survey
  • publicly available or publicly accessible information

As the accuracy of your Personal Data depends largely on the information you provide to us, kindly inform us as soon as practicable if there are any errors in your Personal Data or if there Your Personal Data.

What do we do with your personal data
Your personal data may be used for:

  • send information to you via email/sms/mail/whatsapp and social media tools from time to time about useful education related information and/or services or products offered by us, or other entities associated to StudyMalaysia, its business asscociates, , its clients,
  • profile and/or analyse you using and accessing our websites and your pattern and manner of usage,
  • customise the advertising and content you see, to fulfil your requests for certain products and services, and to contact you about specials and new products, whether directly or through our partners, sponsors, advertisers, business associates or affiliates but for the sole purpose of fulfilling the functions above which are related to education and training
  • research, develop, and improve StudyMalaysia and its related business associates services and products,
  • conduct surveys to determine use and satisfaction with StudyMalaysia services,
  • generate statistics in relation to the StudyMalaysia services,
  • promote and market special offers and other services to you, which may be offered by our advertisers, sponsors, partners or associates.

Your personal data may be disclose to:

  • law enforcement agencies
  • government agencies
  • related or associated companies under StudyMalaysia Group, including those established in the fuure
  • companies or individuals that act as our agents, contractors, service providers and/or professional advisers
  • trusted business partners and clients for the provision of their services related to education and training
  • any parties to whom you have given your consent to disclose
  • such other parties an may be permitted under the laws of Malaysia

Sharing
We will share aggregated demographic information with our partners, sponsors, business associates and advertisers. This is not linked to any personal information that can identify any individual person. We use an outside shipping company to ship orders, and a credit card processing company to bill users for goods and services. These companies do not retain, share, store or use personally identifiable information for any secondary purposes. We partner with another party to provide specific services. When the user signs up for these services, we will share names, or other contact information that is necessary for the third party to provide these services. These parties are not allowed to use personally identifiable information except for the purpose of providing these services.

Your Personal Data is important to us. StudyMalaysia will take reasonable care & precautions to prevent the loss, misuse or alteration of your personal information. We will store all the personal information you provide on our secure servers.
