Site Security & Privacy

Stumps is very concerned about site security on the Internet today. That is why we have made sure that we have followed and surpassed all industry standards necessary for a secure site. These include encrypting all data that you give to us, processing your credit card information in a safe and responsible matter, processing your order soon after it is received and not sharing any information that has been given to us by you, our customers. We want you to feel confident when ordering from Stumps, and we are doing everything we can to continue to operate a secure site. If you have any other questions concerning site security, please feel free to e-mail us.

Privacy Statement
Our customer's privacy is our number one concern. That is why we take every precaution to make sure that all customer information is kept completely private. We will at no time sell, rent or give away any of our customer's personal information – this includes their name, e-mail address, mailing address, phone number or any other information that is obtained on our order form. Bottom line is that we handle our customer's personal information as we would want our own handled.

On occasion, we will send informational e-mails to you concerning Stumps information only. These would be specials, contest information or short messages containing information that we think you would want to know. If at any time you wish to stop receiving these e-mails, we give you the option to remove your name from our mailing list with one easy step.
