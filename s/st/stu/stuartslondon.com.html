Fila Vintage

The 1980 was one of the most influential years for Fila . The fashion made famous on the court by Bjorn Borg, was a phenomenon in the UK and around the world. Borg had plenty of success on the Wimbledon courts in the 1980s and he also worked with Fila to come up with fashion designs. One of the most famous designs was the Fila Vintage Matchday Jacket. Although today the re-makes do not bear the BJ logo alongside the Fila logo they are just as popular today as back then. The Fila jackets or any part of the collection were highly desirable items. Today such items can fetch plenty of money as any hardcore collector would know. This Fila Vintage range can be ordered through special suppliers that deal with all types of vintage fashion clothing. The Fila Vintage Company has announced the release of the rarest jackets of all, The Fila Terrinda, available in very distinctive colours and they ooze coolness through every single seam. It`s not just Fila Vintage jackets that can now be purchased though because many other desirable retro items are around. Classic Ski jackets, Vintage Fila Shorts and Polo Tops have been a great success. The white-line collection as have also launched Fila Terrinda track bottoms.  These outfits are prestigious products and are very limited in production. If Vintage fashion is something that gets your passion for fashion bells ringing then Fila is a must for the wardrobe. The re-make of the film the Firm, directed by Nick Love is due to be released this autumn, and the main player is Fila Vintage collection. Fila Vintage collection is available in London and we proud to present the very best in the White line jackets, tracksuit bottoms to Vintage polo’s and shorts.

Newbalance

Retro men’s trainers have become a cool part of the perfect casual outfit.  New Balance trainers started in 1906, when a 33 year old waiter took it upon himself to follow a dream and build an arch support for shoes. The ideology behind New Balance is to provide a stylish yet comfortable trainer. A sport shoe that is durable for performance yet casual enough for a walking out in a pair of denims. New Balance are usually coded into numbers, 410’s, 420’s, 1500, these are common New Balance styles and limited edition numbers from the 70’s and 80s re-issued styles. New Balance even today has a policy to make some styles in Flimby, Cumbria UK. This summer the trainers have had a great makeover and are being sold in a rainbow of colours.  New Balance mens shoes are light weight comfortable and stylish, hence why they have never faded away and always supplied some of the most famous sports personalities around. Styles are being sold throughout the world , with the main cliental in the US, UK and Japan. Global influences help keep the brand fresh with new designs and up to date on retro styles.

Barbour Jackets

Stylish outdoor wear. 1849 a farm in Galloway where John Barbour was born. Barbour left Scotland and started his first shop in South Shields. The main focus of Barbour was the designs that he made. Barbour knew his market well, Tyneside’s ship builders, fishermen, river and rock workers, all these professions needed durable clothing, a distinct sense of style was being achieved by Barbour.

So good were the Barbour jackets that the military asked him for production making waterproof jackets during World War 2.

While the styling evolves to meet new tastes the Sylkoil from 1910 is still the basics of most Barbour jackets. Stuarts London is a proud stockist of Barbour and is an Official Online partner. Barbour Machinist is trained for a year before they are qualified to a make a Barbour jacket. Each jacket takes up to 15,000 stitches. Barbour International is a great jacket which has hit the fashion scene by storm and made waves in the music industry. Signature jackets like the Beaufort have become a trademark design and drive a high part of the Barbour sales.

The full collection of Barbour will be available from winter 2009.
