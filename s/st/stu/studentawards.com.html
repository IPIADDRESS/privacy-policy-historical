Studentawards' privacy policy statement demonstrates our deep commitment to your privacy rights. Since we gather certain types of information about our users, we want the user to fully understand the terms and conditions surrounding the capture and use of that information. This privacy statement describes what information we gather, how we use it, disclose it, and how to correct or change it.

1.  Information Studentawards Gathers and Tracks

Usage Tracking
Studentawards tracks information related to overall user traffic patterns throughout our site in order to make decisions regarding content and services.

Log Files
Log files are used to track and monitor traffic patterns on our site for maintenance purposes. The logs are not used to track or monitor personally identifiable information.

Registration
In order to use Studentawards scholarship matching services, users are required to complete an online registration. To register, the user is asked to supply certain personal information such as e-mail address, name, and home address. Studentawards also collects anonymous demographic information such as Postal code, age, gender, academic information, interests and hobbies. This information is required if the user wants to participate in Studentawards' services.

Notification of New Awards
On an ongoing basis, Studentawards enters new awards into its database and also updates existing awards. When an existing user matches on a new or updated award, Studentawards automatically sends an email message advising the user to return to the Web site to view the new awards information. These email messages may also contain messages provided by our sponsors.

Email Messages
Studentawards periodically provides opportunities for our sponsors to send email messages to our users that are considered by Studentawards to be relevant. No personally identifiable information is, however, released by Studentawards to our sponsors without the user's consent. Email messages may also invite users to take advantage of various promotions, complete surveys, etc. that also allow our awards search service to be provided free of charge. We gather the email addresses of users who voluntarily opt-in during our registration process. Users may remove themselves from this mailing list by clicking here.

User Feedback/Inquiries
Users have the ability to contact Studentawards and provide us with their comments at feedback. Studentawards does not retain personal information included in feedback or inquiries for any purpose other than to respond to the user.

Cookies
Studentawards minimizes the use of cookies to only those necessary to properly route users within our site as well as to track aggregate traffic coming to the site. Cookies add a convenience feature to help the user save time. They are primarily used to help personalize the users' experience on the site. Refusing cookies will not affect the core functions of user registration and awards searching, but may affect the user's ability to use the "Remember My Email" feature on the Home page as well as view offers from Studentawards and our sponsors.

Outside Links
This site contains links to other sites. Users will be taken to a separate site if they click on one of these links. Studentawards encourages you to review the privacy statements of the Web sites you choose to link to from Studentawards.com so that you can understand how those Web sites collect, use and share your information. Studentawards is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on any other Web site a user visits.

Other Circumstances
From time to time, Studentawards may sponsor sweepstakes, contests or other events that require the collection, processing, and storage of individual contact and demographic information to be used for the event. Studentawards may also co-sponsor these events along with other companies but the user's individual contact and demographic information will not be released to non-participating organizations without the user's consent. Studentawards will require that co-sponsors of such events uphold the privacy and appropriate use of the user's information.

2.  Use of Information

Studentawards uses information voluntarily given by our users to enhance their experience on our Web site, whether to provide interactive or personalized elements on the site or to better prepare future content based on the interests of our users. We also use information, which users voluntarily provide, in order to send out electronic newsletters and mailings. Users may opt-in on our registration page if they choose to receive secondary correspondence. Users may remove themselves from this mailing list by clicking here.

Studentawards is able to offer its free services, in part, based of the willingness of our users to be reached by our sponsors. By allowing Studentawards to make this information available, users receive free information on products and services that may be of interest. Studentawards also creates reports on user demographics and traffic patterns for our sponsors. Studentawards does not include any personally identifiable information in our reports. This information allows our sponsors to promote more effectively, and allows our users to receive advertisements that are pertinent to their needs.

3.  Sharing of Information

Studentawards uses the information described above to tailor our content and help our advertisers and third party marketers better understand our audience's demographics. This is essential to keeping our services free. We share personally identifiable information with our advertisers and third party marketers only with the user's consent. Click here to learn more about Studentawards' sharing of identifiable user information with other organizations. Studentawards does not control the practices of our advertisers or third party marketers.

Studentawards also participates in affiliate networks and various co-marketing alliances to promote our site. For this purpose, we receive and share information in order to analyze site traffic and pattern information so we can evaluate the effectiveness of these programs.

Studentawards recognizes the privacy concerns of its members who choose to participate in our online surveys. Our research studies will report only aggregate information and never disclose individual personal data, or allow the ability to ascertain information about individuals.

4.  Correct/Update Policy

Studentawards offers users the ability to correct or change the information collected on our site. Users simply need to login and click on "Edit Profile" under the "Awards Mailbox" navigation link. This feature can also be accessed on the "Awards List" page in the users' personal mailbox. The original information will be visible and users may now edit any information they wish. Once the changes are made, users must hit the "Save and Continue" button to update the database. Users may change this information at any time and as often as necessary. Users who are experiencing problems or who have any questions about how our services work can contact us at info@studentawards.com.

5.  Delete/Deactivate Procedure

Users may unsubscribe to any of services at any time, or delete their user profile in its entirety by clicking here.

6.  Notification of Changes

Users will be notified of any changes to Studentawards' privacy policy statement via our electronic mailings. If we change the use of personally identifiable information, we will send you an email to notify you and get your consent.

7.  Password

In order to login to Studentawards.com and access your personal mailbox, you are required to have a combination of password and email address. You are responsible for keeping your password confidential. Do not share it. These unique identifiers are used to protect your personal information and ensure the security of your mailbox. It is important that you remember your login information.

8.  Security Policy

Studentawards has created security guidelines and this privacy policy to protect the loss, misuse, or alteration of information collected from our Web site. Access to personally identifiable information is only provided to authorized Studentawards employees via a password. Studentawards employees are made aware of our security guidelines during our employee orientation process. Our security guidelines are reviewed quarterly to ensure that the confidentiality of personally identifiable information is protected by the appropriate measures. To find out more about Studentawards.com security, click here.

Studentawards maintains all files containing personally identifiable information at our offices. To contact us click here.

9.  Contact Information

E-mail Studentawards at webmaster@studentawards.com with any questions that you may have regarding this Privacy Policy.
