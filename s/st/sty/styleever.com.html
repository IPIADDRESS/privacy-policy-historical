    Dress smartly to look cool this winter

    As temperature had started dropping, you need not to worry about looking bulky this winter. Generally during winters, it becomes very important to look fashionable because no matter what size you are, you need to look attractive with warm & cozy layers around you. Those bulky layers provide you the warmth but they look ugly but this winter you can tackle this challenge easily with mentioned few tips, so keep your winter wardrobe ready with following:

    Lengthy coats - you can try long length coat with feminine cut. These coats will keep you warm and help you to hide your ugly bulging. You can pair them up with woolen leggings or jeans. Long length coats are in trend now days and will give you a classy look. Thus lengthy coats can be your winter partner.

    Muffler - Mufflers are unique winter gear that gives you a distinctive look. You can try dark colored mufflers with light colored sweaters or pullovers. While keeping you warm it enhances your look.

    Wraps & Shrugs - if you do not feel like wearing a long coat. You can simple wear a pullover with warm and trendy shrugs that would provide a stylish stroke to your look. Shrugs and stoles are available in variety of color and texture with variety of designs. 

    Boots - when you are gearing up for winter, just do not forget to include boots in your wardrobe.  If you are blessed with long well toned legs you can try long boots to flaunt your legs and for shorter sized legs, short boots would do well.

    Jackets – jackets are something which is never out of fashion. Be it leather jacket or woolen jacket, it looks great when you wear them. Jackets are available with both long and short sleeves. Vibrant colored short sleeve jackets are in trend. Pair them up with cool denim jeans.

    Jewelry - if you are planning to embrace a long neck sweater, then try a heavy necklace with a nice bracelet that would spice up your look. Golden and metal jewelry are in fashion now days. Use light jewelry only with your winter wardrobe.

    Winter Hats - last but not the least, a winter hat would complete your winter wardrobe. Winter hats are available in different size and shapes. So, this time keep your head warm with elegant, stylish and sophisticated winter hats.

    As far as accessories are concerned, you can try eye gear and gloves. So, this winter you need to update your wardrobe and get rid of those ill fitted sweaters which make you look bulky. This winter you can try bright colors to bright up the dull winter winds.

    There is nothing like getting a correct winter wardrobe and flaunting them in dead winter. When it is dark and cold outside you can light up the world with your dressing style by following few easy measures. So don’t just sit inside, Dress smartly to look cool this winter.
