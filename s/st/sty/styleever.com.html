    Summer Essentials for your wardrobe

    Between those long hot days, iced tea, and best friends, the hottest season is here. Summers definitely call for a closet refresh. Shelve your wardrobe with cool pieces fit for going out in the sun or comfortable ones for the perfect oomph in your personality. Opt for cool and subtle colors like pink, green, orange and you are set for the season. The essentials that you need to have in your summer wardrobe are key pieces like sunglasses, hat, flip-flops, cotton dresses, etc. Go for fabrics and colors that suit your mood and the occasion. To keep yourself safe this season, be stylish enough and beat the heat with fashion. Take some basic pieces and with them with all sorts of shoes, bracelets and necklaces. You can mix and match basics for cute outfit combinations!

     

    Essentials that should be present in your wardrobe this summer are as follows:

     

    1. Sunglasses

    Your eyes need special care from the harsh rays of the sun and what better way to put on some stylish sunglasses. From aviators to cat-eye, to oversized, to wide-framed sunglasses, everything would be a fit for summers if worn right. Choose the ones matching your personality for the ultimate look.

     

    2. Flip flops

    Flip flops are very comfortable when we think of summers. They are commonly worn by people of all ages and go with several occasions. They are simple yet chic. Flip flops are a win-win for the day time when your feet requires fresh air at all times. Flip-flops are available in a variety these days.

     

    3. Summer dress

    The heat is your enemy in summers. If you will carry off cool dresses perfect for summers, with prints like flowers and stripes, you will feel fresh and rejuvenating throughout. There's nothing better than putting on a cute little sun dress in warm weather! It looks easy and effortless!

     

    4. Shorts

    Shorts are breathable fabric and can be styled in a numerous ways. Show off your pretty legs in normal or high-waisted shorts. They are a staple for summers and the season's hottest style. Don these shorts and rock the diva within you. Great for the summer season and definitely a must-have in your wardrobe.

     

    5. Palazzo Pants

    Flowy, wide-leg pants are amazing when it's hot outside! They're so breezy and help keep you cool at all times. Printed palazzos are totally in trend right now. Play the best of trends in summer season and make the most of it. Palazzo pants make any woman look taller.

     

    Rock your summer closet with these essential pieces to stay cool and trendy at the same time.

    How to wear heels with ease

    Heels are synonymous with the word 'sexy' and add instant oomph to your height and personality when you wear them. The only catch is to be confident and be comfortable while walking, otherwise one slip-off can break down your morale. Almost all girls love high heels but don't wear them very often. Some are fascinated by high heels, while others can't find an occasion to wear high heels.

     

    Some women fear the "high heel" because of its ugly head, many will avoid the occasion when they have to wear high heels. If fear is keeping you away from your high heels that you adore, say goodbye to your phobias ! Walking in high heels is not that difficult, and with a bit of practice, you'll be stepping out in high heels in no time.

     

     

    With so many heels to choose from, it’s always good to know how and where to start. Heels range in size and width, some are easy to walk into than others. Trying a smaller and wider heel at first is a good start for beginners who can then work their way up to a taller and thinner heel. The following types of heels are available in the market:

     

    Kitten Heels

    The kitten heel is a short heel perfect for the day. Its height does not exceed 2 inches. It looks chic with 3/4 pants to reveal the ankle. Perfect for beginners, you can see your walk improving day by day when in the short but cute kitten heel.

     

    Wedges

    Wedges can be fun, flattering, and easier to wear than other types of heels. Wedge shoes are a great alternative to stilettos, and are an instant outfit updater. They look super cute for summer and can be worn forever without pain. They come in exciting prints like floral, stripes, wood looking etc.

     

    Stilettoes

    If you mastered wearing the kitten heel, wedges and peep toe heels, then its time for stilettoes. After all that experimenting, its time to graduate into wearing the sexy stilettoes. These are pencil heels with a height of 3 to 4 inches.

     

    Walking in high heels is never an easy task, but women have been attempting to master this art for years. Women ought to pay a price for sexier legs and shapely butt, that is getting sore toes and blistered feet, and sometimes embarrassing fall in the parking lot. However, the balance between pain and beauty should not be weighted. It's important to know that various kinds of high heels come in many different sizes and one can be chosen accordingly. Stay stylish with high heels and enjoy!

    How to carry off Ethnic Wear in style

    We live in a country of rich culture and heritage where women love to dress up in rich silks, embroideries, long flowy fabrics, traditional handmade garments detailing on the fabrics. We are truly blessed with traditional fashion which is evolving and getting incorporated of those into modern day fashion. In the old era, girls had to wear indian clothes to cover their bodies but its 21st century now, and the concept has changed absolutely. There are numerous choices when we talk about women’s ethnic wear, there are options like classic style salwar-suit and numerous modern twists of it. When selecting any ethnic wear, a woman needs to select the different cuts, styles, lengths, colours, that would fit her personality and she looks trendy at the same time. So, when choosing a kurti, focus on the cuts or style of it. Also, first and foremost, know your bodytype from amon hourglass, rectangle, pear shaped, inverted triangle body shape and apple.

     

     

    When opting for ethnic wear, there are few options available:

     

    Anarkali

    Anarkalis are long over flowing garments that have been in fashion for quite some time now. We have seen and admired many Bollywood beauties wearing the elaborate ethnic outfit at several events. All of them manage to rock the look, however some failed terribly. If you haven’t got enough of the anarkali trend, check out how to rock this beautiful trend according to your bodytype.

    Anarkalis look great on tall women. You need not think twice before going for any anarkali. Pick your favourite colors and latest styles and you will turn heads at any major occasion. If you have a petite built, choose an anarkali style carefully. Avoid too many flares. In case you are short heighted, you have to be extra careful as it can seem like adding a bulf of clothes on your body. Plump women should wear an anarkali with kalis that start from the top instead of the waist. You need to shift the focus away from your heavy side.

     

    Salwar Kameez

    The dress materials for salwaar kameez are carved out of silk, net, brocade, tissue, chiffon, cotton silk, Chanderi silk, etc which is sure to make you look pleasant and attractive. The fabric is great for wearing and skin friendly which hides curves and flabs, making you look extremely charming. If you are planning for a special date over coffee, it is not necessary that you have to wear western outfit. Pure but stylish ethnic wear can also create magic in the mind of your date. The most amazing thing is that you can never go wrong with Indian wear like Salwar Kameez. You can wear this outfit whether you are thin or healthy. The kurtis and salwar kameez sold online on Styleever.com can just make a women look adorable for any occasion that one can think of.

     

    Break your monotonous western wear ideas and get refreshing change by putting on an ethnic pair of clothes that are comfortable and trendy at the same time. Go, experiment with Indian wear and stick to your roots. Be proud of your culture and sport the ethnic in style.
