  • For more information see the full Privacy Policy, "How does Stockgroup use your personal information?"

Accessing Your Personal Information and Opting Out of Marketing

  • In most cases, you can access the personal information we hold about you, and modify the personal information we hold about you; for more information see the full Privacy Policy, "How can you deactivate your account or correct/revise information that we have about you?"
  • You can opt out of receiving marketing material from us at any time; for more information, see the full Privacy Policy

Stockgroup Information Systems uses Double opt-in to ensure everyone who is added to our mailing lists confirms that they want to be on the list.

In a single opt-in, people submit an e-mail address and ask (opt) for specific e-mails to be sent to them. The inherent issue with a single opt-in is that people might submit their name inadvertently, or someone may have submitted their name for them, against their wishes.
Double opt-in is an enhanced permission that tackles these issues.

With double opt-in, the submitted name is not immediately added to a mailing list. Instead, an e-mail is sent to the address, asking for confirmation that the e-mail address should indeed be added. If the recipient of the confirmation e-mail does nothing, the submitted address is taken off of any mailings. The name is only added to a distribution list if the recipient responds to the confirmation e-mail.
