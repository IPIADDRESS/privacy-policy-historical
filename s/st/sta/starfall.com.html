Privacy Policy for Starfall Website

Starfall is committed to protecting your privacy.

We do NOT ask for any private information when you use this website.

Our web servers create normal logs of web page accesses and IP addresses’ of users, but this information is kept private and only used to measure use of our site and improve the content of our site.

Sometimes we ask whether you like a book or game. Your answer is collected anonymously and used only to improve the content of our site

When you fill out a form on our website the personal information you enter is used only by us to respond to your feedback, or to process an order, and you can be assured that all your information, including your email address, is kept private.
