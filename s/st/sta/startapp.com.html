Privacy Policy

Introduction

StartApp is a mobile advertising and data company that provides mobile app developers and mobile advertisers with a platform to deliver personalized, targeted advertisements to mobile consumers that download or interact with mobile applications that use StartApp services.

This privacy policy, written in full below, describes how StartApp collects, uses, shares, and secures the data provided by or collected from visitors to or users of the following:

  • Our website available at startapp.com, and any service or feature associated with our website
  • The advertising platform for our business partners available at: https://portal.startapp.com/#/signin;
  • StartApp’s app developer partners who implement the StartApp Software Developer Kit (“SDK”) within their mobile app for the purpose of displaying advertisements. Our technology enables our partners to show users more relevant advertising that is based on user interests and behaviors. As such, we collect and use certain data about user activity and preferences. More details are provided in the below policy.

StartApp recognizes the importance of your privacy and we are committed to treating any data we collect with care and respect. To this end, we have designed this Policy in a format that is easy to read so that you can clearly understand which data is collected, how we process, use and protect your data, as well as your choice and controls.

The below policy is an integrated part of StartApp’s Publisher Agreement available at: https://www.startapp.com/policy/publisher-terms/ as well as StartApp’s Advertiser Agreement available at: https://www.startapp.com/policy/advertiser-terms/.

If you have any questions about this below policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at support@startapp.com. Our team will be happy to assist you.

StartApp’s Privacy Policy:

[Last Revised: September 25, 2018]

StartApp Inc. (“we,” “us,” “our” or “StartApp”) is a mobile advertising and data company that provides its mobile application developers, advertisers and publishers (“Business Partners”) with an advertising platform for the purpose of delivering targeted and personalized advertisements to individuals (“User(s)”) that download, use or otherwise interact with an App Developer’s app or our Services, as defined below.

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) describes how StartApp collects, uses, shares and secures the data provided or collected from visitors of our Site, Users, and Business Partners (collectively “you” or “yours”) through our various services and features, as follows (collectively shall be defined hereinafter as the “Services”):

  • Our website available at startapp.com(“Site”), and any service or feature provided therein;
  • The monetization platform for our Business Partners available at: https://portal.startapp.com/#/signin;
  • StartApp’s app developer partners’ (“App Developer”) that implement the StartApp Software Developer Kit (“SDK”) within their mobile app (“app(s)”) for the purpose of displaying advertisements. As our technology enables our Business Partners to show Users more relevant advertising that is based on Users’ interests and behaviors, we collect and use certain data about Users’ activity and preferences, all as detailed in this Policy.

StartApp recognizes the importance of your privacy and we are committed to treating any data we collect with care and respect. To this end, we have designed this Policy in a format that is easy to read so that you can clearly understand which data is collected, how we process, use and protect your data, as well as your choice and controls, but before, we would like to explain the basis on which we do so:

  • Where a Business Partner uses our Services, we process the Business Partner’s Personal Data in order to perform our contract with the Business Partner;

  • For Business Partner direct marketing, we have legitimate interest to process such data; or
  • We will also process your Personal Data where you have provided with consent to do so.

This Policy is an integrated part of StartApp’s Publisher Agreement available at:  http://www.startapp.com/policy/publisher-terms/ as well as StartApp’s Advertiser Agreement available at:  http://www.startapp.com/policy/advertiser-terms/.

If you have any questions about this Policy or our privacy practices, please do not hesitate to Contact Us.

Types of Data We Collect

Depending on your interaction with our Services (meaning, if you are just browsing our Site, or if you have registered to our Services, or interact with an app, etc.), we may collect two types of data, as follows:

  • The first type of data is non-identifiable data which may be made available or gathered via your use of the app or the Services (“Non-Personal Data”). We are not aware of the identity of our Site visitors, User or Business Partner from which the Non-Personal Data was collected. The Non-Personal Data which is being collected may include aggregated usage information and technical information transmitted by a device, including but not limited to: (i) certain software and hardware information (e.g., type of device); (ii) the session ID, the time and date you access the app or our Site, connect or disconnect to the Services, etc.; and (iii) browsing data such as: browser type, visited URL, etc. (all as detailed below).
  • The second type is individually identifiable data, namely data that identifies an individual or may with reasonable effort identify an individual (“Personal Data”). Personal Data includes either: (a) information you voluntarily provide us, such as your email address and private password submitted upon registration and contact information such as your name, telephone number, address upon a submitting voluntarily request to contact us; or (b) certain identifiers such Internet Protocol (“IP”) addresses or Android ID, Advertising ID, IDFA or Device ID (“IDs”).  Note that, while IDs are considered as personally identifiable data in many jurisdictions (such as the EEA), there are some jurisdictions in which such data is not considered as Personal Data. We treat all IDs from various jurisdictions as Personal Data.

Please note that we may collect different categories of Personal and Non-Personal Data from you depending on the nature of your interaction with the Services, as detailed below. You are not required by law to voluntarily provide us with information. You can always avoid providing us certain Personal Data; however, you acknowledge that it may prevent us from providing you certain Services. In addition, we will always treat Non-Personal Data as Personal Data if we combine it.

The principal rights under applicable data protection law in relation to Personal Data are as follows: the right to access information; the right to rectification; the right to erasure; the right to restrict processing; the right to object to processing; the right to data portability; the right to complain to a supervisory authority; and the right to withdraw consent (to the extent applicable). We reserve the right to request reasonable evidence to verify your identity before we provide you with any such data in accordance with applicable law. In addition, the process of locating and deleting the data may take up to one (1) month (following the receipt of the validation proof we require) in accordance with applicable law.

Opt Out Right

You may easily opt out of certain data collection, sharing process and targeted advertising related to our Services. Please note that even if you opt-out, you may still receive content and advertising, including from StartApp. Opting-out solely provides you with the choice to receive advertisements that are not targeted to you and to control the data collected on you for the purpose of displaying advertisements.

Please read your device specific information to learn how to opt-out:

  • Android–https://support.google.com/ads/answer/2662922
  • iOS–https://support.apple.com/en-il/HT205223.

You may also Contact Us and we will be happy to assist you. Note that once you opt out, part of the Services provided by us might no longer be available and some features may not work if you revoke the data transfer.

Additional means to opt out, depending on your device are available, such as:

  • You may control how your browser responds to cookies by adjusting the privacy and security settings of your web browser (as detailed in the Cookie section above).
  • You may limit the disclosure of certain data by your mobile device to us and our Business Partners by adjusting the settings on your mobile device. For iOS mobile Devices, go to “Settings” from your Device’s home screen; scroll down to “Privacy”; select “Advertising”; and turn on “Limit Ad Tracking.”  For Android mobile Devices, go to “Google Settings” on your Device; select “Ads”; and check the box labeled “Opt Out of Interest-Based Ads.”
  • You may opt out of certain advertisers’ cookies and browser-enabled, interest-based advertising at the Network Advertising Initiative’s (“NAI”) website-NAI consumer opt-out and the Digital Advertising Alliance’s (“DAA”) website- DAA opt-out page.
  • If you wish to opt out of interest-based advertising, you may also click here, or if located in the European Union click here. Please note you will continue to receive generic ads.
  • Our Business Partners may also provide ways for you to opt out from or limit their collection of data from and about you. Please refer to the privacy policies of the apps to learn more about the privacy practices of our Business Partners. Please note, however, that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of our Business Partners, and other third parties.

Children’s Privacy

StartApp complies with applicable legislation related to children’s privacy protection, including the US Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (“COPPA”). Thus, we do not knowingly collect or solicit Personal Data from children, as they are defined under applicable law, and specifically: under the age of 13 years old, if they are located in the US; (ii) under the age of 16 years old, if they are located in the EU; (collectively: “child” or “children”), except as permitted under applicable law. Notwithstanding the above, in any event, we will not knowingly collect Personal Data from children for the purpose of behavioural advertising. If we later obtain actual knowledge that a User is considered as a child under applicable law, and Personal Data was collected (other than as permitted under applicable law) we will take steps to immediately delete its Personal Data. In addition, we request that children will not voluntarily provide us with Personal Data through the Services. If you become aware or have any reason to believe that a child has shared any Personal Data with us, please contact us and we will take steps to ensure that such Personal Data is immediately deleted from our files, to the extent required under applicable law.

Your privacy is very important to StartApp and we are committed to protecting your Personal Data from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. We have implemented physical, technical and administrative security measures for the Services that comply with applicable laws and industry standards. For more information on our security measures, please click here.

Although we take reasonable steps to safeguard information, we cannot be responsible for the acts of those who gain unauthorized access or abuse our Services, and we make no warranty, express, implied or otherwise, that we will be able to prevent such access.

StartApp also protects User privacy by seeking to minimize the amount of data that we store on our servers in the first place. We retain the Non-Personal Data we collected for as long as needed to provide the Service, carry out our business purpose and to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes and enforce our agreements, in accordance and compliance with applicable laws, but in any event, we will not keep the data longer than 24 months in an identifiable form, or as required by law.  Other data, such as the IDs are kept for maximum time period of 6 months and some for less than 7 days, all as required to provide the Service and in compliance with applicable laws and legislation.

Further, in the event of a data breach, in which we discover your Personal Data is at risk, or might be at risk, we will notify you through the Services or, if technically possible, by sending you an email.
