Startupnight Team

Cem Ergün-Müller

Cem Ergün-Müller is the Founder of Lange Nacht der Startups - Startupnight Berlin. One of the largest and most intense startup events of Europe.

In his other life he is Head of Marketing and Communications at Telekom Innovation Laboratories. T-Labs are the first point of contact for innovation within Deutsche Telekom. Other tasks include the development of communication concepts and strategies for next generation communication services as well as marketing and communication support for T-Labs' spin-offs. He is also Co-Founder of the Digital Lab Berlin, which unites large corporates in their efforts to create innovations with an entrepreneurial mindset.

Born in Milano, Italia, he majored in Marketing and Communication and enhanced his capabilities with a Master in Enterprise Information Management at the Henley Management School of the University of Reading in England.

Winni Petersmann

Winni Petersmann launched on the rock in 1991 as true-One-man show. He lives for the show and the production as a DJ, festival organizer and stage designer. Today, after more than 20 years in the business, Winni Petermann is renowned specialist for people in interaction and continues to be the engine driving on the rock and to grow as an authentic creative power plant in the event culture - with a highly motivated team and a network of international specialists.

His responsibilities include the design and implementation of tailored projects with a high degree of creativity. These include the areas of team development and incentives, just like customer and cultural events and, if needed, flanked by social media. The know-how for specific, targeted marketing has made his concepts to be recognized as reference projects, internationally and at management level.

Sai Kiran

Entrepreneurship has always been close to his heart and it was this passion, that laid the foundation for many companies. While being employed in different companies like ISRO, SAP and Coramandel Industries, he always had a keen interest in Innovation, start-ups as well as IT, Consulting. He holds Bachelor ‘s Degree in Engineering.

Over the years he was also able to gain experience in various industries and fields like Manufacturing, FMCG, Space & Defence to mention a few. These very diverse experiences have given him a wide perspective on various industries and also helped me to gain expertise in more than just one field. He is presently on board of many companies including SBS co working space, Angel Investing company Global venture capital to name a few. He plays major role for Indian start-ups to show case their ideas in European markets.

He loves to play golf and dog lover.

Stefanie Schramm

Steffi is supporting Startupnight this year for the first time. She is primarily a ‘networker’. Some claim she knows everybody in town.

Alongside her engagement with Startupnight, she also supports GTEC Berlin Startup Academy as a manager, is a senior consultant for Berlin Innovations Agency, Journey2Creation, and advises companies on intra- and entrepreneurship programmes.

When she isn’t thinking about sustainably connecting businesses with people, Stefanie likes to spend her time anywhere outdoors with her son and friends.

Sven Hebbe

Sven started working in the T-Labs event team in 2013, and because of this was a part of the first Startupnight team. He has been actively involved in not only the night itself, but also taking care of the preparation beforehand, like the marketing, design work and website.

This year he is looking forward to taking on more responsiblity alongside the website, and is being a point of contact for the startups and looking after organisation before and during the night itself.

Daniela Kozian

Daniela has been a part of the event team at T-Labs since 2012, and since the beginning has been fascinated with organising events of various sizes. She likes the interaction between team members and visitors and is always active at every event, all the way to the end.

She has supported Startupnight since the beginning and this year looks forward, more than ever, to being involved. She takes care of, amongst others, marketing, supervision of startups and the locations.
