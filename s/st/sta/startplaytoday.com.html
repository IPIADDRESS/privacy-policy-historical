Privacy Policy



We want our users to be always aware of any information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it.

We respect each site visitor's right to personal privacy. To that end, we collect and use information throughout our website only as specified in this Privacy Policy.

1. The information we collect is:

E-mail - during registration a user must give certain contact information, but we never sell or give away this information. It is only gathered to contact the user about services on our site for which s/he has expressed interest.

We don’t collect personal information of our users, such as addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and click-stream data.

But Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on your site.

Google's use of the DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

2. Changes to our privacy policy

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes to this privacy statement.

If site users have any questions or suggestions regarding our privacy policy, please contact us via our contact page.
