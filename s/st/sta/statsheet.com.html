Privacy Policy

PRIVACY POLICY Automated Insights, Inc. (“Company”, “we” or “us”) maintains the Websites automatedinsights.com, app.automatedinsights.com, and wordsmith.automatedinsights.com (collectively, the “Website”).

Registered users can use the Website to remotely access and use our proprietary software application (“Wordsmith”). Users provide their own structured data and text (the “User Content”), which Wordsmith transforms into narrative content (the “Articles”). For an additional fee, we also provide training and consulting services to help You better use Wordsmith. All of the foregoing are cumulatively referred as our “Services”.

All information that we collect on the Website, including any information that You provide to create Articles, is subject to this privacy policy (the “Privacy Policy”).

By using the Website, or by clicking to accept or agree to the Terms of Use when this option is made available to You, You accept and agree to be bound and abide by this Privacy Policy.

If You do not want to agree to this Privacy Policy, You must not provide us with any personal information.

This Privacy Policy covers the Company’s treatment of personal information that the Company gathers when You use the Website or the Services, including personal information provided during registration or included in User Content.

This Privacy Policy does not apply to third parties that the Company does not own or control. If You have any questions or comments regarding this Privacy Policy or its enforcement, please contact us at privacy@automatedinsights.com.

1. NOTICE – INFORMATION WE COLLECT AND USE.

A. We collect and store information that registered users (“Customers” or “You”) submit to us, such as their names and e-mail addresses, to allow us to identify Customers, provide the Services and notify Customers of changes or updates to the Services. When Customers provide us with their e-mail addresses, they automatically opt-in to receive e-mails from us (including marketing e-mails). If a Customer would like to opt-out of these marketing e-mails, he, she or it can change its settings through its account page. We also collect and store the Customer’s name and phone number if a Customer elects to provide such information, which we use to assist us in providing the Services. We also collect other information from Customers, such as the Customer’s IP address and browser type. We use the IP addresses of Customers to assist in login and other uses related to Customers’ use of the Services. Customers’ sessions on the Website are tracked for systems administration purposes and to track user trends. We may link IP address and other information to a Customer’s account.

B. You may include your personal information in User Content. With our express prior consent, You may include the personal information of third parties in User Content. We use this information for internal research purposes, to operate, manage and improve our Services offerings, and to create Articles.

C. As You navigate through and interact with our Website, we may automatically collect certain traffic data and usage information to help us manage our content and improve your use of the Website. This may include information about your equipment, your IP address and your browsing actions and patterns when You visit our Website, including the referring and exit pages.

D. Like many other websites, we use a standard technology called “cookies.” Cookies are small pieces of information stored on your hard drive. They can help make the Internet experience quicker and more convenient. Cookies allow Customers to login without entering their user ID and password each time they use the Service. Cookies also allow us to improve the Service. Most browsers have an option for turning off the cookie feature, which will prevent your browser from accepting new cookies, as well as (depending on the sophistication of your browser software) allowing You to decide on acceptance of each new cookie in a variety of ways. If you disable cookies, You will not be able to use the Service.

We generally use the information we collect to create a secure and personalized Service.

A. We gather IP Addresses for systems administration purposes, abuse prevention and to track user trends. We may also use your IP address to identify the general geographic area from which You are accessing our Website. We do not link IP addresses to any personally identifiable information.

B. We collect Customers’ user IDs and password information to enable them to log into and use the Service.

C. Customers are automatically opted-in to receive marketing e-mails from us when they register for the Service. Customers can opt-out of such e-mails by adjusting their account settings, provided that we may still send Customers e-mails relating to their accounts.

D. We collect Customers’ names and phone numbers, which allow us to contact them in account recovery and other scenarios.

E. We use this information for internal research purposes, to operate, manage and improve our Services offerings, and to create Articles.

F. We use traffic data and usage information to help us manage our content and improve your use of the Website.
