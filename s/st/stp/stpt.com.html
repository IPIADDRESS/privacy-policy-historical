Starting Point, Inc. Privacy Notice

This notice outlines the privacy policies of www.stpt.com, www-starting-point.com, and www.mystartingpoint.com. Questions regarding this statement should be directed by e-mail to privacy@stpt.com. Starting Point, Inc. can also be contacted at Starting Point, Inc., located at 8905 Kingston Pike Bldg 12 Ste 307, Knoxville, TN 37923.

Information Collected

Starting Point, Inc. collects information about each user who registers for membership, including, but not limited to, user name, password, first and last name, e-mail address, street address, gender, occupation, website url, website title, website genre and interests. We also collect information on the pages users access or visit, and other information volunteered by users, such as survey information. Additionally, for each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes IP Addresses.

We also use cookies to store visitors' preferences and to record session information, for such purposes including -- to ensure that visitors are not repeatedly sent the same banner ads and to customize newsletter and Web page content based on visitors' browser type.

Uses of Information

The information Starting Point, Inc. collects is used to improve the content of our newsletters and Web page; to notify consumers about updates to our newsletters and Web site; to contact users for marketing purposes; and as otherwise may be necessary for Starting Point, Inc. and our contractors to provide services to subscribers and visitors to the site. This information is also shared with other organizations to contact users and for other marketing purposes.

We may make the e-mail addresses of our users available to other organizations whose products or services we think they may find interesting. If you would like to prevent your information from being shared with third parties, please send a message and write 'privacy' in the subject line to: privacy@stpt.com.

If you supply us with your postal address on-line you may, receive mailings from other third party companies. These third party companies may use the information that you provide at the Starting Point, Inc. or Starting Point, Inc. owned or affiliated sites to deliver you mailings with offers about goods and services of interest to you. You can, however, have your name put on our do-not-share list by writing to us at the above address or emailing us at privacy@stpt.com. Please provide us with your exact full name and address. We will be sure your name is removed from the list we share with other organizations. We use a third-party company to distribute our daily and weekly newsletters.

Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers on-line may receive telephone contact from us as well as from other reputable companies with information regarding new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such telephone calls, please let us know by e-mailing us at privacy@stpt.com.

Third Party Advertising Companies

We have relationships with third-party advertising companies. These third party companies may use the information that you provide at the Starting Point, Inc. site to deliver you email messages and ads about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here. If our information practices change at some time in the future we will post the policy changes to our Web site to notify you of these changes and provide you with the ability to opt out of these new uses. In the event that Starting Point, Inc. or substantially all of its assets are acquired, customer information will be one of the transferred assets.

Upon request we provide you with access to identifier information (e.g., customer number or password), contact information (e.g., name, address, phone number) that we collect and maintain about you. You can access this information by sending us an e-mail at privacy@stpt.com. Upon request we offer users the ability to have inaccuracies corrected in contact information. You can have this information corrected by sending us an e-mail at the above address.

Security

We use industry-standard technologies when transferring and receiving consumer data exchanged with our site to help ensure its security.
