How we collect the information

  • We use various technologies to collect and store information such as cookies, pixel tags, log files and web beacons.

·       Cookies: A "cookie" is a small piece of information that a website assign to the end user's device while viewing a web site. Cookies are very helpful and can be used for various different purposes. These purposes include allowing the end user to navigate between pages efficiently, enable automatic activation of certain features, remembering the end user's preferences and making the interaction between the end user and our services quicker and easier. Cookies are also used to help customize the end user's experience. More information about cookies can be found at www.allaboutcookies.org. The end user's may remove our cookie by following the instructions of the device's preferences; however, if the end user's choose to disable our cookie, some features of our Services may not operate properly and the online experience may be restricted.

·       Pixel Tags: A pixel tag is a type of technology placed on a website in combination with cookie to enable tracking activity on websites.

·       Web Beacon: A web beacon is technique used to track who is reading a web page or email, when, and from which computer.
