Privacy Policy

The Short Version. I hate spam more than you, and I don't want to be part of it. That's why I'll NEVER share your email address or other personal information with anyone. All your information is 100% safe with me.

When you sign up for my newsletter, I'll send you periodical emails. Every email comes with an unsubscribe link at the bottom. If for whatever reason you want to get off my list, simply click that link, and you'll never hear from me again.


The Long Version. This usually includes your name and e-mail address to receive the free newsletter. You provide this information to StrongLifts.com during your registration.

How Do We Use This Information? StrongLifts.com uses this information for internal marketing purposes only. StrongLifts.com does not sell, exchange or release your information (name, e-mail address). StrongLifts.com will never share your information with any other third party or company.

The information inside these logs includes your IP, browser type, ISP, page viewed, date/time stamp, and number of clicks to analyze traffic and administer our site. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. Neither will they ever be shared.

You can find detailed information about cookie management at your browsers' website.

How Do We Store Your Information? The list server that delivers the newsletters of StrongLifts.com stores your information.
