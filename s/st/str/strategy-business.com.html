    Data Collection

    When you use or otherwise interact with our Site, we will collect information about you. We collect this information when you provide it to us and we will automatically collect information about your use of and interaction with the Site. Please note that we may be required to collect certain information about you by law or as a consequence of a contractual relationship we have with you. Failure to provide this information may prevent or delay the fulfillment of these obligations.

    Information You Provide

    You may provide us information about yourself, such as your name, company name, address, and email address as well as job function, level, and industry.

    Note about Sensitive Information: It is not our intention to collect sensitive information about you, for example information about race or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious or other similar beliefs, trade union membership, physical or mental health, sexual life, or criminal record. Please do not provide these types of sensitive information to us.

    Automatically Collected Information

    In addition, we automatically collect information about you and your use of our Site through the use of tracking technologies such as cookies and web beacons. The information we collect using these technologies includes IP address, browser type and language, access time, and referring website.

    “Cookies” are small text files placed on your hard drive that assist us in providing a more customized website experience. For example, a cookie can be used to store registration information in an area of the website so that a user does not need to re-enter it on subsequent visits to that area. For instance, we use cookies to make navigation of our websites easier for visitors and to facilitate efficient registration procedures. PwC also uses site analytics providers, which set cookies in your browser. Site activity information collected by these analytics providers is combined with personal information held by PwC. More information about our use of cookies and third-party tools can be found here.

    When we send you emails and/or newsletters, we use technologies to determine whether the message in the email or newsletter has been opened and links contained within have been clicked on. We combine information collected through these means with other information we maintain about you in order to measure interest in our specific offerings and email campaigns, improve our offerings to specific target audiences, and tailor our interactions with you.

    Data Use

    We use the data we collect for a number of purposes, which include:

    • Fulfilling your order and/or newsletter email requests
    • Managing your registration and email marketing preferences
    • Sending you renewal notifications and/or service notifications
    • Responding to customer service requests
    • Emailing you information about new features and special offers
    • Conducting customer satisfaction and reader surveys
    • Improving our Site, user experience, products, and services

    • Maintaining and improving our security, including fraud prevention
    • Fulfilling our legal requirements, both contractual and statutory, and regulatory compliance
    • Implementing our internal policies
    • Abiding by professional and industry standards, as applicable
    • Understanding how the Site is used and obtaining insights into usage patterns
    • Exploring ways to develop and grow our business

    • As required by applicable law, statute, rule, regulation or professional standard, or through subpoena, search warrant, or other legal process

We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our Site and to read the privacy statements of each and every website before disclosing any personally identifiable information. This Privacy Statement applies solely to data we collect on this Site.

    We retain the data we collect about you for as long as is required for the purposes for which it was collected or as otherwise required by applicable law or professional standards.

    Your Choices

    Should you wish to unsubscribe from our mailing list or any registrations, we will provide instructions within the appropriate area of the Site or in communications to you; or you may contact our webmaster.

In most cases, you may refuse or delete one or more cookies and still access the Site, but site functionality may be impaired. After you finish browsing the Site, you may delete Site cookies from your system if you wish.

We do not knowingly collect or maintain any information about children under the age of 13 on this Site.
