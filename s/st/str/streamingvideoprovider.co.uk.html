Privacy Policy

StreamingVideoProvider Ltd  ("StreamingVideoProvider") values the privacy of our users, members, and others who use www.StreamingVideoProvider.com  , www.StreamingVideoProvider.co.uk and any other web sites or services owned or operated by StreamingVideoProvider (the "StreamingVideoProvider Sites") (collectively or individually, "Users").

This Privacy Notice is incorporated into and is subject to the StreamingVideoProvider Terms of Use. Your use of the StreamingVideoProvider Sites and any personal information you provide on the StreamingVideoProvider Sites remains subject to the terms of this Privacy Notice and our Terms of Use.

Please note that any video, image, blog entry, or other content posted at the direction of Users onto the StreamingVideoProvider Sites becomes publicly-available content, is not considered personally identifiable information subject to this Privacy Notice, and may be collected and used by others.

NOTE TO KIDS: THE STREAMINGVIDEOPROVIDER SITES ARE NOT FOR PERSONS UNDER THE AGE OF 13. If you are under 13 years of age, please visit another site - there are lots of other great web sites for you.

The Information StreamingVideoProvider Collects:

User Provided Information: You may provide to StreamingVideoProvider what is generally called "personally identifiable" information (such as your name and email address) when you choose to participate in the various activities offered on the StreamingVideoProvider Sites such as uploading videos, posting messages and blogging, reviewing and rating videos, entering surveys and contests, and subscribing to newsletters. "Cookies" Information: When you visit the StreamingVideoProvider Sites, we may send one or more cookies - a small text file containing a string of alphanumeric characters - to your computer. StreamingVideoProvider uses both session cookies and persistent cookies. A session cookie disappears after you close your browser. A persistent cookie remains after you close your browser and may be used by your browser on subsequent visits to the StreamingVideoProvider Sites. Persistent cookies can be removed, but each web browser is a little different, so please look through your browser "Help" file to learn the correct way to modify your cookies set-up. "Automatically Collected" Information: When you visit the StreamingVideoProvider Sites or open one of our HTML emails, we may automatically record certain information from your web browser by using different types of technology, including "clear gifs" or "web beacons". This "automatically collected" information may include your Internet Protocol address ("IP Address"), web browser type, the web pages or sites that you visit just before or just after the StreamingVideoProvider Sites, the pages you view on our site, and the dates and times that you visit the StreamingVideoProvider Sites.

The Way StreamingVideoProvider Uses Information:

1. StreamingVideoProvider uses the information that we collect to operate, maintain, enhance, and provide to you all of the features and services found on the StreamingVideoProvider Sites. We will use your email address, without further consent, for administrative purposes such as notifying you of major StreamingVideoProvider Sites updates, for customer service, or to contact you regarding a video that you have posted.

2. StreamingVideoProvider uses all of the information that we collect to understand the usage trends and preferences of our Users, to improve the way the StreamingVideoProvider Sites work and look, and to create new features and functionality.

3. StreamingVideoProvider uses "automatically collected" information and "cookies" information to: (a) remember your information so that you will not have to re-enter it during your visit or the next time you visit the StreamingVideoProvider Sites; (b) provide custom, personalized advertisements, content, and information; (c) monitor the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns; (d) monitor aggregate usage metrics such as total number of visitors and pages viewed; and (e) track your entries, submissions, and status in any promotions, sweepstakes, and contests that we might host.

4. StreamingVideoProvider will not use your email address or other personally identifiable information to send promotional or marketing messages without your consent or except as part of a specific program or feature for which you will have the ability to opt-in.

StreamingVideoProvider does not share your personally identifiable information with other organizations for their marketing or promotional uses without your express consent. To receive a free written notice of a security breach you should notify us at privacy@StreamingVideoProvider.com.

Protecting the privacy of young children is especially important. For that reason, StreamingVideoProvider does not knowingly collect or maintain personally identifiable information on the StreamingVideoProvider Sites from persons under 13 years of age, and no part of our website is directed to persons under 13. IF YOU ARE UNDER 13 YEARS OF AGE, THEN PLEASE DO NOT USE OR ACCESS THE STREAMINGVIDEOPROVIDER SITES AT ANY TIME OR IN ANY MANNER.
