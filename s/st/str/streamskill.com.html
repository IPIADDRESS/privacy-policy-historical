Privacy Policy

By using or accessing JobFindGuide’s website, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Members who use the JobFindGuide Services select or are assigned user names and passwords, all of which are used to identify the Members when they are using JobFindGuide Services.   JobFindGuide does not collect names, postal or email addresses, and phone numbers of non-Member visitors to JobFindGuide's Web site, except to complete pending registration applications, to provide access to product demonstrations, or to respond to inquiries regarding the JobFindGuide Services.   JobFindGuide also collects information necessary to facilitate sending and tracking electronic mail outs for its Members.   JobFindGuide may retain copies of any communications JobFindGuide sends to or receives from Members and other visitors to our site for our records, but may not use any such information except in accordance with the terms hereof.
Members may not post content on the JobFindGuide website(s).
JobFindGuide’s web server collects and saves the default information customarily logged by World-Wide Web server software. Specifically, for each http request received, the date and time, the originating IP address, the object requested, and the completion status of the request is collected and saved.
Use of Collected Information.  JobFindGuide utilizes the information submitted by prospective Members to authenticate and verify the identity of such prospective Members who may be communicating with JobFindGuide. JobFindGuide may use such information to operate, maintain and enhance the JobFindGuide Services and other services offered by JobFindGuide and its partners. JobFindGuide also uses Member information to monitor compliance with the rules and policies of the JobFindGuide Services.   JobFindGuide compiles and analyzes its Members’ information into aggregated research reports. Except as may be otherwise agreed in a separate agreement between JobFindGuide and any Member, JobFindGuide may share this aggregated data with its business partners.

Member access, update or deletion of information collected by JobFindGuide. Upon written request, JobFindGuide will provide to Member a summary of any Member Information retained by JobFindGuide regarding the Member. Members have a responsibility to keep certain information current. Members may correct, change or update the Member Information, Client Email Lists and information about their authorized representatives through the use of the Member login and password, as applicable.

About the Author & Guide

The inspiration for this guide has come from the thousands of jobseekers that I hear from and the students that visited the Leadership Conference at VIU where I presented recently. They are desperately seeking opportunities today and I know that JobFind Guide fills the gap as a great resource to help you network online and find employment opportunities that you can make a success of! M. Firth

Michael J. Firth is a certified teacher, career advisor and social entrepreneur. He has developed resources and innovative teaching practices that are learner driven and make use of current technologies including authoring a brochure for Employing the Homeless in New York City; eLearning strategy for Douglas College Sports Institute; IEPs (Individual Education Plan) for at-risk youth. He has recently served as Ask the Expert blogger, Vice President of leading employment website Joined.com, and has been founder of award-winning technology companies.
