II. INFORMATION WE COLLECT ABOUT YOU

General Information
When you use the Internet, your web browser or client software may transmit certain information to the servers that host the web sites you visit. The information may include the unique number assigned to your server or Internet connection, the capabilities and features of your computer, your geographic location, and your movement within and interaction with the web site, including what pages you look at and what information you download. When you visit ACEP Web Sites, ACEP servers collect such information. ACEP uses this information to generate aggregate statistics about visitors to ACEP Web Sites.

Personally Identifying Information
ACEP only collects personal information that you voluntarily provide to us. "Personal Information" is information through which you can be identified. The Personal Information that ACEP collects through ACEP Web Sites or obtains from its Strategic Partners depends on the nature of your online activity, such as whether you make reservations, participate in a contest, apply for employment, seek information on weddings or meetings, register as a meeting planner, or provide customer feedback or suggestions. For example, Personal Information that ACEP collects from you when you make a reservation may include your name, company, address, home telephone number, work telephone number, e-mail address, fax number, credit card information, purchase and reservation information, and other information that you voluntarily provide. When you complete an employment application, ACEP may also collect your social security number, previous addresses, previous names, convictions for felonies, whether you can provide proof of eligibility to work in the United States, present and past employment, military service, education and training, skills, languages, family members who work for the ACEP, and other information listed in the online application form.
The Personal Information is collected by e-mail, through online forms, and through your activities on ACEP Web Sites. The ACEP Web Sites utilize Secure Sockets Layer (“SSL”), which provides several layers of security for users of the ACEP Web Sites. All data sent to and received from the ACEP Web Sites in SSL mode is encrypted. This ensures that anyone who may be able to intercept and review the data transmitted will not be able to understand the data. SSL also enables you to verify the certificate of the ACEP Web Sites so that you can ensure that the web site is the authentic.

Cookies
ACEP Web Sites use cookie technology. A cookie is a small file that contains information sent by a web site that is saved on your hard disk by your computer's browser. Cookies store information that a web site may need to personalize your experience and to gather web site statistical data, such as which pages you visit, what material you download, your internet provider's domain name and country, and the addresses of web sites you visited immediately before, and after, visiting a particular web site.
ACEP Web Sites use cookies to collect and store your preferences for viewing the ACEP Web Sites, such as whether you disable Flash so that you will not have to do so for subsequent visits. In addition, ACEP Web Sites use cookies to provide a unique identifier to your computer so that we can generate statistics regarding usage of ACEP Web Sites, such as the percentage of repeat visitors and other general statistics, determining the effectiveness of advertisements, and sales generated through advertisements. Cookies are also used to assist your log-in to ACEP Web Sites, such as the site.
To browse the Internet and ACEP Web Sites without cookies, you can configure your browser to reject all cookies or to notify you when each cookie is offered so that you can accept or reject each cookie. You may not be able to conduct some transactions or use some services on ACEP Web Sites if you disable your browser from accepting cookies.

ACEP may use the information collected by us or Strategic Partners: (1) for marketing purposes, including, but not limited to, notifying you of special promotions, offers and events via e-mail, mail, fax and other means; (2) to process and fulfill reservations and make a record of reservations; (3) to enable us to contact you for confirmation or customer service or after you sign up for or participate in certain activities; (4) to respond to inquiries, complaints and other communications; and (5) for profiling or demographic purposes.

IV. DISCLOSURE OF INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Except as provided below, ACEP will not sell, trade, or disclose to third parties any Personal Information obtained through ACEP Web Sites or obtained from Strategic Partners, without your consent. ACEP may disclose Personal Information: (1) to comply with a court order, subpoena, search warrant or other legal process; (2) to comply with legal, regulatory or administrative requirements of any governmental authorities; (3) to protect and defend ACEP, its subsidiaries and affiliates, and all of their officers, directors, employees, attorneys, agents, contractors, and partners, in connection with any legal action, claim or dispute; (4) to enforce the terms of use of ACEP Web Sites; (5) to prevent imminent physical harm; (6) to businesses that ACEP may use for purposes of performing functions on behalf of ACEP, such as companies that process credit card transactions; and (7) in connection with the sale, assignment, or transfer of any ACEP property or ACEP Web Site.

V. CHOICE REGARDING PROVIDING INFORMATION

General information is collected whenever you browse ACEP Web Sites. The ACEP Web Sites do not provide any means for opting out of providing general information when you browse ACEP Web Sites. However, privacy software that prevents the disclosure of general information is commercially available.
You can browse ACEP Web Sites without providing any Personal Information. However, providing Personal Information is required to use some of the services offered through the ACEP web sites, including, but not limited to, making hotel reservations, participating in contests, applying for employment, registering as a planner, obtaining information, and participating in the player rewards program.
If you can opt out of receiving certain communications and offers, opt out options will be indicated when they are available.

General information and Personal Information collected by ACEP Web Sites is stored on secure servers. The secure servers are protected by firewalls and other standard security procedures.
