Privacy Policy

St. Louis Public Radio recognizes that visitors to our site may be concerned about the information they share with us and how we treat that information. St. Louis Public Radio is committed to protecting the privacy and security of users of our site. This statement describes our privacy policy. By visiting stlpublicradio.org, you signify your agreement to the terms and conditions of St. Louis Public Radio’s Privacy Policy.

Personal Information Collected
  • St. Louis Public Radio does not collect Personally Identifiable Information from the stlpublicradio.org web site unless you choose to provide that information to us.
  • We may request personal information when you: make a donation to St. Louis Public Radio; make email transactions; fill out a survey, use our mobile app, or request additional information.
  • St. Louis Public Radio uses “cookies” with Google Analytics and Chartbeat to track website usage. This data helps us to understand the audience reach of our content and to improve the user experience of our website.
Use of Information
  • Information may be used by St. Louis Public Radio to: process and fulfill your donation requests; manage a contest or game; gather your editorial comments and feedback; contact you for additional information or administrative purposes. You may also receive emails regarding future programs, news, and other station information; you may choose to “opt-out” of receiving this email information.
  • Unless explicitly stated otherwise at the time of submission, St. Louis Public Radio will not sell, share, exchange or rent your email address or phone number. Your name and mailing address may be exchanged with other non-profit organizations principally for new member acquisition. At any time you may request that your information not be shared by contacting the membership department:
    membership@stlpublicradio.org
    (314) 516-7474
    St. Louis Public Radio, 3651 Olive St., St. Louis, MO 63121.
  • Please see our Member List Policy for more information.
Credit Card Information Security
  • We take all appropriate steps to protect your privacy. We always use industry-standard SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) encryption technologies when transferring Personally Identifiable Information (such as credit card numbers), and visitors are notified that they are submitting information via a secured server. We have appropriate security measures in place at our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of the information we collect from you on this site.
Links to Third Party Sites
  • We provide links to third party sites. As we do not control these sites, we request that you review the privacy policies posted on these third party sites. St. Louis Public Radio is not responsible for any materials or information on third party web sites or the respective privacy practices of these sites.
Children
  • Visitors between the ages of 13 and 18 must obtain permission from their parents or guardians before participating in any St. Louis Public Radio on-line activities, or providing any personally identifying information. Visitors under age 13 are asked not to provide personally identifying information. Should St. Louis Public Radio learn that it has collected such information, it will be removed from our databases as soon as possible.
Access to Personal Information
  • You can help St. Louis Public Radio maintain the accuracy of your personal information by notifying us of any changes in your address, title, phone number or email address.
  • To review any personal information collected from you retained in our database, please contact our membership department:
    membership@stlpublicradio.org
    (314) 516-7474
    St. Louis Public Radio, 3651 Olive St., St. Louis, MO 63121
Acceptance of St. Louis Public Radio’s Privacy Policy
  • Because technology changes rapidly, we reserve the right to modify, change, add, or remove all or portions of these terms and conditions. All revisions will be posted on this site. We welcome your comments and questions about this privacy policy. If you have comments or questions, please contact the membership department:
    membership@stlpublicradio.org
    (314) 516-7474
    St. Louis Public Radio, 3651 Olive St., St. Louis, MO 63121.
