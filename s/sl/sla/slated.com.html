Your Privacy Rights

Introduction

Slated, Inc. (“Slated”) has created this Privacy Policy in order to disclose its use of consumer data collected about you. This Privacy Policy is effective as of August 18, 2010. Any questions regarding this policy should be directed by email to privacy@slated.com. You may also contact Slated at 36 East 20th Street, Third Floor, New York, NY 10003. The following discloses Slated’s information gathering and dissemination practices for its marketing website.

Information Collected and Received
Slated may collect or receive information about each person who registers with the Slated website including, but not limited to, user name, password, first and last name, email address, street address, gender, occupation, and interests. Slated also collects information on the pages you access and other information you may volunteer, such as survey information.

Uses of Information

We will never sell your email address, or any other personal information, to any third party.

From time to time, we may partner with companies based on the interests of our users. These partner companies may have access to your email address, name, postal address and other information that can be used to identify you or be used to contact you directly. Slated reserves the right to disclose information when required by law.

We occasionally use other companies to perform services necessary to our operations. In the course of providing these services, those companies may have access to your personal information. By contractual agreement, those companies must treat your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. However, we will not be liable for any damages that may result from the misuse of your personal information by these companies.

Slated may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with Slated’s server and to administer Slated’s web site. Your IP address may also be used to help identify you and your online profile and to gather broad demographic information. Slated’s site’s registration form requires users to give Slated contact information (such as your name and email address) and demographic information (such as your zip code, age, or income level). Slated uses customer contact information from the registration form to send the user information about Slated and promotional material from some of Slated’s partners. The customer’s contact information is also used to contact the user when necessary. Slated may use demographic and/or profile data to tailor the visitor’s experience on Slated’s site, show the visitor content that Slated thinks you may be interested in, and display content according to your preferences. The demographic and profile data will be shared with advertisers ONLY on an aggregate basis. We may also disclose, on an anonymous basis, statements made by our customers.

Security
Slated uses industry-standard technologies when transferring and receiving consumer data exchanged between Slated and other companies to help ensure its security. This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under Slated’s control. Slated’s servers are backed up regularly and protected by security systems.

Cookies
“Cookies” are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer’s hard drive. Slated’s site uses cookies to keep track of your session, shopping cart, and advertising delivery. Slated may also use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests and to save your password. Slated may use an outside ad company to display ads on Slated’s site. These ads may also contain cookies. While Slated uses cookies in other parts of Slated’s web site, cookies received with banner ads are collected by Slated’s ad company and Slated does not have access to this information.

Links
Slated may create links to other web sites. Slated will make a reasonable effort to link only to sites that meet similar standards for maintaining each individual’s right to privacy. However, many other sites that are not associated or authorized by Slated may have links leading to Slated’s site. Slated cannot control these links and Slated is not responsible for any content appearing on these sites.

Amendments
Slated may amend this policy at any time. If Slated is going to use personally identifiable information collected through Slated’s corporate site in a manner materially different from that stated at the time of collection, Slated will notify users via email and/or by posting a notice on Slated’s website for 30 days prior to such use. This Agreement may not be otherwise amended except in writing that specifically refers to this Privacy Policy and is physically signed by both parties.

Assignment
If there is a change of control in Slated’s business (whether by merger, sale, or otherwise) or if there is an asset sale, the user information, including personal information, could be sold as part of that transaction and your personally identifying information potentially could be used by the purchaser. However, if that business materially changes this statement or the information-handling practices as described in this Privacy Policy, you will be notified by email and/or through a notice posted on the site, and you may opt-out of the use of your existing information in a new manner. This Privacy Policy inures to the benefit of any successors or assigns of Slated or the assets of Slated.

Contacts
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this website, you can contact privacy@slated.com. Upon request, Slated will provide you with access to information (e.g., name, address, phone number) that Slated collects and maintains about you. You may send an email to privacy@slated.com to change or modify information previously provided.

Communication & Opt-Out
Slated occasionally emails registered users with information on new releases on slated.com or updates on partner sites the user has visited. Slated provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from Slated. To opt-out of receiving our communications, you can submit your email address in the following form: (i) send email to remove@slated.com or (ii) send postal mail to: 36 East 20th Street, Third Floor, New York, NY 10003.
