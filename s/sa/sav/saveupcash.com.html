Personal Data Collection

Non-personal Data Collection
SaveUpCash collects and tracks certain non-personal information such as user's IP address, browser type, access times, usage habits and other information which does not specifically identify any individual. An IP address is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer whenever you are surfing the Web. Our servers log your IP address for the purposes of web traffic analysis and trends.

Use of Personal Data
The information we collect will be analyzed for making relevant and personalized offers to our members based on their browsing or purchasing history. We will not supplement information collected at our Web site with data from other sources. As with any other information that you supply to SaveUpCash, none of the information that we collect will be released in any personally identifiable way.

Use of Cookies
Cookies are small text files that store messages to a web browser by a web server. The message is then sent back to the server each time the browser requests a page from the server. The main purpose of cookies is to identify users and possibly prepare customized web pages for them. So we may use cookies to identify you and allow you to log in once to SaveUpCash and shop without having to log in again. Cookies are also used by many of our merchants in order to identify you as a member of SaveUpCash and these cookies are not subject to SaveUpCash's privacy policies.

Children and Privacy
SaveUpCash always encourages parents and guardians to spend time with their children when surfing in the Internet and thoroughly familiar with the sites visited by their children.

We do not actively market to children, and we never knowingly ask a child under 13 to divulge personal information. No information should be submitted to or posted on SaveUpCash by users under the age of 13 years without the permission of their parents and guardians.
