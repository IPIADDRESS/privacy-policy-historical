Log Files
We do keep standard log files as every other website on internet does to track their user behaviour and traffic. Information on our log files include Your IP (Internet Protocol) address, the country you are visiting us from, The browser you are using (such as Internet Explorer or Firefox), your time of visit and the pages you have visited on our website.

Cookies and Beacons
We use a cookie only to determine your language selection on our site so that we can serve you the same language option on your next visit.

Some third party advertisers on savevid.com (such as Google Adsense Program) may use technology such as cookies and web beacons when they advertise on our site. These cookies are generally collecting information about your IP, your geographic location, the browser you are using and whether your browser is capable of some technologies such as Flash player. This information is usually used by advertising companies for serving you more targeted ads according to your geographical location and interests.

You can choose to disable or limit our cookies or any third-party cookies from your browser settings or internet security program settings if you wish to. However disabling cookies completely may cause some of the functions on this site or other sites not working properly.
