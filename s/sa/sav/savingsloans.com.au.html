Your Privacy

Share

We understand how important it is to keep your personal information private.

People's Choice Credit Union's commitment to properly managing your personal information is detailed in our Privacy Policy.  

The Privacy Policy provides you with important information in relation to the collection, use and disclosure of personal information. People's Choice Credit Union welcomes comments in relation to privacy issues generally, or any privacy issues that concern you. Please contact us with any comments or concerns you may have.

You may also contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner if you want to know more about privacy issues. Their web address is: http://www.oaic.gov.au

We know that while it’s important to receive relevant information from us, sometimes you only want to be contacted in special circumstances or at certain times.

Tell us exactly what sort of information you want and how you want it either by updating your Member Preferences in Internet Banking under the 'Tools' tab, visiting your nearest branch or calling us on 13 11 82.

 You should consider our Disclosure Documents. This information and advice is provided without reference to your personal circumstances so you should consider whether this product fits your objectives, financial situation and needs. Contact us on 13 11 82 with any questions, or visit your nearest branch to talk to a consultant.
