Security Policy

Satya Paul is committed to respecting your privacy and recognizes your need to protect the personal information that you share with us on this web site. As you may know, when you visit sites on the internet, the owner of the site has the ability to collect information about you, from your name and address to your shopping preferences. The following is our privacy policy

Policy Update

Satya Paul may update this privacy policy from time to time. Whenever we make a change, we will post the updated policy on this page. That way users can stay informed of our information collection polices. Accordingly, we recommend that you consult this page frequently so that you are aware of our updated policies.

Type of Information we Collect.

We have the ability to collect information from people who visit our site: Personally Identifiable Information. Personally Identifiable Information is any information by which you can be identified, such as your name, address, telephone number, email address, credit card information, birth date, gender, occupation, personal interests, etc.

What we do with the information

Satya Paul is able to collect Personally Identifiable Information. This can be done only when you voluntarily submit such information on our site. For example, when you register for a satyapaul member account, when you subscribe to an email newsletter, you are supplying us with your Personally Identifiable Information. We use such information to display content and products that we think may be of interest to you, to personalize your online experience and to improve the quality of the content that we provide to you on the site. Whenever we request that you provide us Personally Identifiable Information, we will give you the opportunity to "opt out" of receiving future communications from us. By providing such Personally Identifiable Information to us, you agree that, unless you expressly opt out of receiving such information, we may use your Personally Identifiable Information to fulfill the activity you signed up for, such as contacting you with updates regarding the site. Whether or not you provide such information is completely your own choice. You are not obligated to answer any question that is presented to you. Satya Paul may also disclose your Personally Identifiable information when required by law or court order.

Changing and controlling information about you

If you have provided Personally Identifiable Information but later you object to the use that is being made of that information (for example, you no longer wish to receive promotional or marketing information about products, services, and offerings from Satya Paul or any third parties) you can ask that we stop making use of that information. You can do this by canceling your satyapaul member account. Simply send an email message to us at customercare@satyapaul.com and be sure to indicate 'UNSUBSCRIBE' in the subject line. Your request will be processed as soon as possible. Please bear in mind that cancellation of your member account will result in your not being able to continue to access the relevant services, offers or content.

Security

We understand the utmost importance of protecting the security of your personal information that you share with us. Unfortunately, however, no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. As a result, while we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee or warrant the security of any information you transmit to or from our site, and you do so at your own risk.
