Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 03/16/2011

Data privacy is very important to SaaShr.com SaaShr.com website visitors are invited to review this web document to learn about SaaShr.com's information gathering and disbursement policy for SaaShr.com with regard to this website.

SaaShr.com has been awarded TRUSTe's Privacy Seal signifying that this privacy policy and its practices have been reviewed by TRUSTe for compliance with TRUSTe's program requirements, including transparency, accountability, and choice regarding the collection and use of website visitor personal information. TRUSTe's mission, as an independent third party, is to accelerate online trust among consumers and organizations globally through its leading privacy trustmark and innovative trust solutions.

If you have questions or complaints regarding SaaShr.coms privacy policy or practices, please contact SaaShr.com at 3040 Route 22 West, Suite 200, Branchburg, NJ 08876. If you are not satisfied with SaaShr.coms response, you can contact TRUSTe here. The TRUSTe program covers only information that is collected through the SaaShr.com website and does not cover the use and collection of personal information through our applications.

SaaShr.com complies with the U.S.  E.U. Safe Harbor Framework as set forth by the U.S. Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use, and retention of personal data from the European Union. Information regarding the E.U. Safe Harbor Framework can be found at: http://export.gov/safeharbor.

Information We Collect and How We Use It

When a website visitor contacts SaaShr.com for additional information related to SaaShr.com products and/or services, he or she is requested to provide specific contact information. This contact information includes a contact name, email address, and phone number, which is used by SaaShr.com to respond to the inquiry.

Each user, including SaaShr.com partners and their end-user client companies, where appropriate, who provided his or her contact information may opt-out of receiving specific mailings by sending an email request to be removed from future mailings. Unless otherwise agreed to by electronic signature, contact information gathered is not shared with outside organizations or sold to third parties.

Access to Personally Identifiable Information

SaaShr.com partners and their end-user client companies are able to update their personally identifiable information. If SaaShr.com partners and their end-user client companies request SaaShr.com to delete their personal information, either by postal mail at the contact information listed below, or by using electronic signature, SaaShr.com will do so provided that deletion of data will not prevent SaaShr.com from complying with its legal obligations. SaaShr.com will respond to such requests within 30 days.

SaaShr.com will retain partner and/or end-user company information for as long as such partner account is active. SaaShr.com will retain and use user information as necessary to comply with SaaShr.com's legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce SaaShr.com agreements.

Communications from the Site

SaaShr.com will occasionally send users information on its products, services, special deals, promotions, or industry research information. Out of respect for user privacy, SaaShr.com presents users with the option to not receive these types of communications by requesting so in writing, by electronic signature, or by using the unsubscribe mechanisms included in promotional emails sent by SaaShr.com.

If a partner, eligible employee, or representative wishes to subscribe to SaaShr.com newsletter(s), SaaShr.com will use the name and email address provided by such party to send the requested material. If a partner, eligible employee, or representative wishes to unsubscribe from SaaShr.com newsletters, such party may request to do so in writing, by electronic signature, or by using the unsubscribe mechanisms included in all promotional emails sent by SaaShr.com.

Legal Disclaimer

SaaShr.com reserves the right to disclose user personally identifiable information as required by law and when SaaShr.com believes that disclosure is necessary, in order to protect its rights and/or to comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process served on SaaShr.com website.

Business Transitions

In the event SaaShr.com goes through a business transition, such as a merger, acquisition by another company, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, users' personally identifiable information might be among the assets transferred. Users who are on the current list of recipients of communications referenced earlier will be notified via e-mail or prominent notice on the SaaShr.com website for 30 days of any such change in ownership or control of users personal information.

Security

The security of user personal information is important to SaaShr.com. SaaShr.com follows generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to SaaShr.com, both during transmission and once the company receives it. However, no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. Therefore, SaaShr.com cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Cookies

A cookie is a small text file that is stored on a user's computer for record-keeping purposes. SaaShr.com uses cookies on its website. SaaShr.com does not link the information users submit while on the SaaShr.com site stored in cookies to any personally identifiable information.

SaaShr.com uses session ID cookies to enable session tracking for SaaShr.com customers who login using its website. A session ID cookie expires when a user closes their browser. SaaShr.com does not use cookies to monitor a user navigating the website outside of its business applications.

If a user rejects cookies, the user may still use SaaShr.com's web site, but the user's ability to login and use the SaaShr.com business application may be limited.

Log Files

As is true of most websites, SaaShr.com gathers certain information automatically and stores it in log files. This information includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser types, internet service providers (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating systems, date/time stamp, and click stream data.

SaaShr.com uses this information, which does not identify individual users, to analyze web site visitor trends and to track users' movements on the outside-facing website.

Links to Other Sites

This website contains links to other sites and or other online services that are not owned or controlled by SaaShr.com. Website visitors are advised that SaaShr.com is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites.

SaaShr.com encourages each website visitor to be aware when leaving the SaaShr.com site, and to read the privacy statements of each and every website that collects personally identifiable information.

This privacy statement applies only to information collected by SaaShr.com.

Customer Testimonials

SaaShr.com posts customer testimonials on the SaaShr.com website, which may contain personally identifiable information. SaaShr.com does obtain each customer's consent via an electronically signed or digitally scanned consent form prior to posting the customer's name along with their testimonial. If a customer wishes to have his or her testimonial changed or removed, the customer may do so in writing.

Changes in this Privacy Statement

SaaShr.com may update this privacy statement to reflect changes to its information practices. If SaaShr.com makes any material changes, it will notify its partners and their end-user client companies, where applicable, by email (sent to the e-mail address specified in users' accounts) or by means of a notice on SaaShr.com's website prior to the change becoming effective. SaaShr.com encourages all users to periodically review this page for the latest information on SaaShr.com's privacy practices.

Contact Us
