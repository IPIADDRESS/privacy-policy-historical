Privacy Policy

Sandlot Games takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.

Information Collection and Use:

Mailing List

If you register for our mailing list feature, from time to time we may e-mail you with messages about our services or third party products and services that we believe may be of interest to you. These messages may contain code that enables our database to track your usage of the messages, including whether the email was opened and/or what links (if any) were clicked. However, we do not share your personally-identifiable information with any third party.

Web Site and Game Play

Sandlot Games only collects personally-identifiable information if you decide to register on our mailing list. Sandlot Games does not collect any information from you if you do not register on our mailing list and are simply visiting our site or playing our games. However, third parties that are partners and distributors of Sandlot Games may collect information from you. Sandlot Games does not control what information third parties gather once you leave the Sandlot Games web site. You are encouraged to check the privacy policies of these sites.

The Sandlot Games web site is not directed to children under the age of 13. Sandlot Games respects the privacy of parents and children and is committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). When signing up for the mailing list, Sandlot Games does not collect personal information from individuals who identify themselves as under the age of 13. If a parent believes that his or her child has submitted personal information to Sandlot Games, he or she can contact us at danielb@sandlotgames.com. Sandlot Games will promptly delete the information upon learning that it relates to a child under the age of 13.

Changes to our Privacy Policy:

Sandlot Games reserves the right to change this privacy policy without notice. Therefore, please check back periodically for any changes.
