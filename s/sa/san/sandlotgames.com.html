Privacy Policy

 

Sandlot Games takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices. By visiting Sandlot Games, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

 

Use of Collected Information  

If you register to play one of our games or register for our newsletter, we may e-mail you with messages about our services or third party products and services that we believe may be of interest to you. These messages may contain code that enables our database to track your usage of the messages, including whether the email was opened and/or what links (if any) were clicked. We use your username, e-mail address, and password to uniquely identify you as a user on the Sandlot Games web site. We may use or display your username for the purposes of rankings, competitions, chat identification, and/or game high scores. Your contact information will allow us to notify you about new features, enhancements, resolution of site problems, handle and/or route your customer support questions and issues, and inform you of upgrade opportunities, contests, prizes, and/or special events. However, we do not share your personally-identifiable information with any third party.

 

Web Site and Game Play

Sandlot Games may collect personally-identifiable information if you visit our website; decide to register to receive our newsletter or register to play our games. Information collected may include, but is not limited to the following: date and time the visit occurred to our website, user IP address, and IP browser type. If you participate in our "Share a Level" or “Submit a Level” feature, your e-mail and IP addresses will be shared with Sandlot and the person to whom you send a level to using this feature. We may also use an IP address to exercise our ability to restrict access to the site by users who have violated our Terms of Service.

 

Links to Third Party Sites

Web sites that link to or from a Sandlot Games web site may collect personal information about you. This Sandlot  Games Privacy Policy does not apply to, and cannot control the activities of, other web sites.

 

Chat and Messaging

Sandlot Games may operate message boards and/or chat areas where users can exchange ideas and communicate with each another. When posting to a message board or chat area, the information is being made publicly available in an online environment. Any information posted is the sole responsibility of the individual user.

 

Children

The Sandlot Games web site is not directed to children under the age of 13. Sandlot Games respects the privacy of parents and children and is committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). When registering, Sandlot Games does not collect personal information from individuals who identify themselves as under the age of 13. If a parent believes that his or her child has submitted personal information to Sandlot Games, he or she can contact us at support@sandlotgames.com This e-mail address is being protected from spambots. You need JavaScript enabled to view it . Sandlot Games will promptly delete the information upon learning that it relates to a child under the age of 13.

 

Statistical and Usage Data

If you choose to participate in helping us focus test our games, then we may retrieve information about your hardware system and how your game is used. We use this information to enable you to play your game over the internet. We also use this information to better understand the behavior and preferences of our customers, so we can improve our products and services.

 

Security

Sandlot Games has taken reasonable steps to protect the security of your information by following industry standards developed by industry leaders for the ongoing development, enhancement, storage, dissemination and implementation of security standards for account data protection.

Sandlot Games reveals only the user name, address, and the last four digits of your credit card number when confirming an order. We then transmit the entire credit card number to the appropriate credit card company during order processing. Sandlot does not maintain end user credit card information on its data bases.
