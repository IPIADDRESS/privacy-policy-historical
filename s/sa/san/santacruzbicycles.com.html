Your information may be retained by SCB for a period we determine is reasonably necessary to accomplish our objectives in collecting the information. If we anticipate the use of your information for any purpose other than those described in this Privacy Policy, we will provide you with an effective opt-in mechanism or in any case obtain your prior consent. SCB does not link this automatically-collected data to personally identifiable information.

It remembers information you provided as well as analytics about how you used the website, and can help to simplify your use of the website. You can reject a cookie by setting your browser to notify you when one is received so that you can decide whether to accept it. We use Google Analytics codes to log when users view specific pages or take specific actions on the Site. This allows us to provide you targeted advertising in the future, should we choose to do so. If you prefer not to receive this type of advertising from us, you can opt out at any time by using the DoubleClick opt-out page or the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page. We will assume you have consented to such use unless you so opt-out or specifically advise otherwise.

SCB is not responsible for the content, privacy practices, products or services of such other websites, and SCB disclaims all responsibility and liability associated with such other websites. This Privacy Policy applies only to information collected by the Site.

  9. Security. The security of your personal information is important to us. If you require 100% security, do not correspond or transmit data over the Internet or through email.
