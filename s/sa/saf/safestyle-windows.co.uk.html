What are PVCu Windows and Doors?

PVCu Windows and Doors are often actually uPVC Windows, and sometimes PVC Windows. This refers to the Polymer - Polyvinyl Chloride - used to make double glazed windows and doors. PVCU is strong, yet flexible making it the perfect material for double glazing.  Discover more about the environmental properties and how we make our windows and doors to make our double glazing the cost saving choice for your home.
