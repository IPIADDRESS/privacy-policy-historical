Privacy Policy

What information do we collect?

In general, you can visit and browse safeYouTube.tv anonymously without revealing any personal information about yourself. The only information that we gather during your general browsing can be information such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, the websites that referred you to us, the pages you visit on our website, and the dates/times of those visits. All of these information will not specifically identify you and will be used internally only for web site traffic analysis.

We collect personal information from you just when you register on our site or make a donation. When making a donation or registering on our site, you may be asked to enter your: name, e-mail address or credit card information. This information is collected only with your knowledge and permission.

What do we use your information for?

The information we collect from you may be used in one of the following ways:
1) to personalize your experience, as your information helps us respond to your individual needs in a better way.
2) to improve our website as we continually strive to improve our website services based on the information, feedback and donations we receive from you.
3) to improve customer service as your information helps us to more effectively respond to your customer service requests and support needs.
4) to send periodic emails.

The email address you provide may be used to send you information, respond to inquiries, and/or other requests or questions. And there will always be an unsubscribe link included in each email. In all cases, your information, whether public or private, will not be sold, exchanged, transferred, or given to any other third party for any reason whatsoever. Your privacy on the internet is of utmost importance to us.

How do we protect your information?

We implement a variety of technologies and security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you enter, submit, or access your personal information.

We offer the use of a secure server. All supplied sensitive/credit information is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology and then encrypted into our Payment gateway providers database only to be accessible by those authorized with special access rights to such systems, and are required to keep the information confidential.
