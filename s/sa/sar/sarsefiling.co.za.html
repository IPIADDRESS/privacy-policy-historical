Privacy Policy

  • SARS eFiling may electronically collect, store and use the following personal information of Users
    • name and surname
    • contact numbers
    • non-personal browsing habits and click patterns

    • IP address
    • User selected password
  • SARS eFiling collects, stores and uses the abovementioned information for the following purposes
    • Communicate requested information to the User
    • Provide the User with access to restricted pages on this web site; and
    • to compile non-personal statistical information about browsing habits, click-patterns and access to the SARS eFiling web site
  • Information detailed above is collected either electronically by using cookies or is provided voluntarily by the User. Users may determine cookie use independently through their browser settings
  • SARS eFiling may collect, maintain, save, compile, share, disclose and sell any information collected from users, subject to the following provisions
    • SARS eFiling shall not disclose personal information from Users unless the User consents thereto

    • SARS eFiling may compile, use and share any information that does not relate to any specific individual
  • SARS eFiling owns and retains all rights to non-personal statistical information collected and compiled by SARS eFiling
