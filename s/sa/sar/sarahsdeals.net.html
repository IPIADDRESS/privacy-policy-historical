      What do I mean?

      • YMMV = Your Milage May Vary (May or may not work)
      • MM = MoneyMaker
      • WYB = When You Buy
      • PM = Price Match
      • SQ = Store Coupon
      • MQ = Manufacturers Coupon
      • HTH = Hope This Helps
      • BC = Before Coupon
      • AC = After Coupon
      • B#G# = Buy #(qty) Get #(qty)
      • BOGO = Buy One Get One
      • IP = Internet Printable
      • OOP = Out of Pocket
      • OYNSO = On your next shopping order
      • IR = Instant Rebate
      • RR = Register Rewards
      • UPR = Up+ Rewards (Rite Aid)
      • KG = Kelloggs Coupon Insert
      • PG = Proctor & Gamble Coupon Insert
      • RP = Red Plum Coupon Insert
      • SS = Smart Source Coupon Insert
      • Q or CPN = Coupon
      • ECB = Extra Care Bucks (CVS Pharmacy)
      • MIR = Mail In Rebate
      • SCR = Single Check Rebate (Rite Aid)
      • $.00/# = Amount of coupon off # of items required for coupon
