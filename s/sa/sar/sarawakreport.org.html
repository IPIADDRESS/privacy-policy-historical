Privacy Policy

What if “Your Personal Information”?

“Your Personal Information” means any information about You which is personally identifiable or otherwise relates to You.  This information includes, without limitation, Your name, address, date of birth, telephone number, email address, other contact details, financial details, comments, transactions, call requests and other information from which You can be identified and which You may disclose to Us at any time.

How do we use your personal information?

We use Your Personal Information to:

Data Security and Transfers

We take all reasonable steps to protect Your Personal Information. All the information collected by Us is stored on a secure server. The secure server software encrypts all information You input before it is sent to Us.
We will not knowingly transfer Your Personal Information outside of the European Economic Area for any purpose other than in connection with the potential storage of data and systems on servers that may be located elsewhere.  If Your Personal Information is transferred in such circumstances Your Personal Information will always be subject to required restrictions in accordance with relevant data protection laws.

Cookies

Like many websites, We use “cookies.” Cookies are small pieces of information stored by Your web browser on Your computer’s hard drive that We transfer to Your computer’s hard drive through Your web browser to enable Our systems to recognize Your web browser.  We use cookies on the Website to allow Us to understand who has seen which pages, to determine how frequently particular pages are visited and to determine the most popular areas of the Website. We do not control the use of cookies by third parties.

Our cookies do not collect Your Personal Information but they do collect other information such as the following:
•    the domain name and host from which you access the Internet and the Internet address of the site from which you direct-linked to ours;
•    the date and time you access the Website and pages you visit;
•    your computer’s IP address and information about its operating system, platform and the Web browser type and version you use;
•    demographic and other non-personally identifiable profile information about you; and
•    information to combat fraud or misuse.

Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, although You do not have to.  If You wish to disable cookies then You can do so by following the instructions associated with Your web browser.  Please note that by disabling cookies You may not be able to use all features of the Website.
For more information on cookies and how to disable them, You can consult the information provided by the Interactive Advertising Bureau at www.allaboutcookies.org.

Links to other websites

Please be aware that We may link to other websites through the Website.  We are not responsible for the data policies, content or security of these linked websites.

Miscellaneous

Your personal information is not stored for any longer period than is necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.
This privacy policy is subject to change, so please check it regularly.  We retain the right to amend or vary this policy on notice.  If the purposes for which data is collected change, We will notify You of this change as soon as possible.  If required, We will also obtain Your consent, for instance, where the data constitutes an additional data collection purpose.
