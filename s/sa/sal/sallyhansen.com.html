I. General
Del Laboratories, Inc. respects the privacy of every individual who visits our website. This policy outlines the information Del Laboratories, Inc. will collect and how we will use this information.

II. Collective Data
Del Laboratories, Inc. is currently using "cookies." "Cookies" are text files that are placed in your computer's browser to store your preferences. Cookies, by themselves, do not reveal your e-mail address or other personally identifiable information, unless you choose to provide this information by, for example, registering at our site. However, once you choose to furnish the site with personally identifiable information, this information may be linked to the data stored in the cookie. At this point, we only use cookies through the use of Web Trends. Web Trends provides accurate anonymous traffic analysis. This information allows us to understand site usage and to improve the content and offerings on our sites. The information from Web Trends enables us to provide you with a better experience by allowing us to understand what areas of the website are of interest to our visitors. Should you have concerns, you are certainly free to disable the use of cookies in your browser. If you want more information about the method in which we gather traffic analysis, you should refer to the Web Trends Live Privacy Policy to learn how they collect and use information. You can find Web Trends Live's privacy policy here: http://www.webtrendslive.com/privacy_policy.htm.

III. Personally Identifiable Information
Del Laboratories, Inc. does not collect personally identifiable information about you (such as, your name, address, telephone number, or e-mail address), unless you voluntarily submit it to us. Your name and address information is used only to fulfill our sample and/or coupon offers. Your e-mail address is used only with your permission for sending you information and updates about Del Laboratories, Inc. products, tips, and giveaways.

IV. Children
Del Laboratories, Inc. has no intention of collecting any personally identifiable information from individuals under thirteen. In the event you would like us to delete a child's information from our records, please use the contact information below.

V. Who to Contact
If you have submitted personally identifiable information through a Del Laboratories, Inc. website and would like that information deleted from our records, please contact us at web@dellabs.com. We will make reasonable efforts to delete your personal information from our existing files.

VI. Changes
Any changes to the privacy policy will appear promptly on this page.
