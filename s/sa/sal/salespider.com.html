I. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

A. E-mail Addresses

1.When do we collect your email address?: We do not automatically collect your email address. We collect your email address only if you choose to provide it to us when you complete the optional registration process on Data Capturing Domain, or when you choose to opt in to one of our online offers.

2.How do we use your email address?: If you choose to provide us with your email address you will be opting in to receiving emails from us and we may send emails to you with information relevant to you and from third party advertisers to call your attention to products and offers we believe might interest you. In addition, other registered users with Data Capturing Domain will have the ability to email you to make offers and correspond with you.

3.Can you opt out of receiving emails?: If you prefer not to receive direct communications sent by us, you can opt out by changing your Email Settings [My Account>E-Mail Options] within your personal account in a SaleSpider Media Inc website, or by clicking the "Unsubscribe" button included on the bottom of every email you receive from us. If you prefer not to receive communications from SaleSpider Media Inc, or advertisers or other third parties, you can choose to opt-out by clicking the "unsubscribe link" at the bottom of every email sent. If you choose to opt out, you will not receive email or other communications sent directly by us or our advertisers utilizing information collected from Data Capturing Domain.

4. How to opt out of emails: You can opt out of receiving emails by clicking the "Unsubscribe" button included on the bottom of every email you receive from us. You may also pick and choose which emails you wish to receive on www.salespider.com by changing your "Email Options" under the "My Account" drop-down menu in the top left corner of your logged-in screen.

B. Personally Identifying Information

We do not automatically collect any personally identifying information from visitors to Data Capturing Domain. When you register for the Data Capturing Domain and create a user profile, you will be required to supply certain personally identifying information, including but not limited to your name, address, city, state, zip code. Other personally identifying information may be required, including but not limited to your company name, company size, interests, buying behavior, health, illnesses, education, insurance buying behavior or financial situation, and career plans ("User Profile Information"). You will also be required to supply your email address and a password. Your email and password will not be displayed in your User Profile Information or revealed to third parties unless you specifically provide that information within your User Profile. If you do not want your personally identifiable information collected by Data Capturing Domain, you may browse Data Capturing Domain without registering or choose not to complete the User Profile in its entirety.

You may also pick and choose which emails or newsletters you wish to receive by changing your Email Settings [My Account>E-Mail Options] within your personal account in Data Capturing Domain. You can also choose to opt out of a newsletter or email by clicking the ["Unsubscribe" link] included on the bottom of every newsletter or email you receive from us.

As a registered user of the Data Capturing Domain, you can opt out of receiving emails or newsletters from Data Capturing Domain and SaleSpider Media Inc at any time by changing your Email Settings located at www.SaleSpider.com [My Account>E-Mail Options]. When you submit personally identifying information, if the information might be used to allow third parties to contact you (e.g., to email you offers or newsletters), then you will be notified at the time of submission. Your password will never be provided to any third party. You can also choose to opt out of a newsletter by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link included on the bottom of every newsletter you receive.

No personally identifying information is gathered through web beacons or web bugs.


II. HOW DO WE USE THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT?

A. Purposes for Which Information Is Submitted

Personally identifying information that you voluntarily submit may be used for the purpose(s) for which it was submitted. For example, if you voluntarily submit personally identifying information to receive a newsletter from a third party advertiser, the information that your submit will be used by that advertiser to send you the newsletter and to communicate with you. Personally identifying information may be shared with third-parties to the extent necessary to accomplish the purpose(s) for which the information was submitted. For example, if you use Data Capturing Domain to receive a newsletter or win a prize from one of our advertisers and the newsletter is sent by a third party on behalf of that advertiser, then the third party will have access to your email address for the purposes of sending you the newsletter.

B. Facilitating Use of the Website and Customizing Content, Advertising and Offers

We may use information collected from cookies and other tracking technology to recognize your browser when you visit Data Capturing Domain and to facilitate easy login to Data Capturing Domain, to attempt to personalize Data Capturing Domain for each user, to ensure that your browser does or does not see the same ad over and over again, to sequence ads and offers in a series and to track page usage and paths. We may also use collected information to allow editorial content and/or advertising on the Website to be targeted to groups of users for whom it is most pertinent. This means that users see editorial content and/or advertising most likely to interest them. In connection with these uses, we may disclose information to third parties, but only in aggregate form.

C. Administrative Use and Communications Related to Your Use of the Website

We may use collected information for internal administrative purposes including site administration, troubleshooting, processing of e-commerce transactions or administration of the Data Capturing Domain or SaleSpider Media Inc Network. We may also use collected information to send you communications related to your use of the Salespider.com or Data Capturing Domain or content you post on Salespider.com or Data Capturing Domain. This includes, when applicable, communications regarding account status, renewal/expiration notices and other transactional communications. We may also send you notifications about major changes to Data Capturing Domain and/or to this Privacy Policy. In connection with these uses, third parties that provide support for the operation of Data Capturing Domain or features thereof may also have access to such information. In all cases, however, we will use the information only as permitted by law.

D. Statistical Analysis

We may use collected information to create aggregate statistics about activity on and visitors to Data Capturing Domain, including, the number of return visits to Data Capturing Domain or our advertisers' websites, the number of unique visitors that have viewed or "clicked" a particular ad or visited a particular web page and how much time is spent on each page of Data Capturing Domain. We may also create aggregate demographic statistics. We may use these aggregate statistics to measure interest in various areas of Data Capturing Domain, to better design Data Capturing Domain and to improve our marketing strategy and product offerings. We may share the aggregate statistical information with third parties.

E. Information and Offers That May Be of Interest To You

If you voluntarily supply personally identifiable information that includes contact information, we may, from time to time, contact you or give your contact information to third parties so that they may contact you, to call your attention to products or services that may be of interest to you. When you submit personally identifying information, if the information might be used by a third party, then you will be given the opportunity, at the time of submission, to decline to receive such communications. If you decline at that time, you will not receive such communications from third party utilizing the personally identifiable information collected on Data Capturing Domain. You can choose to opt out of communications from SaleSpider Media Inc by changing your Email Settings [My Account>E-Mail Options] within your personal account in Salespider.com or Data Capturing Domain or by clicking the "Unsubscribe" button included on the bottom of every email you receive from us.

F. Compliance with Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation

In all cases for Canadian residents, the email privacy policy will be modified to ensure compliance with CASL. For more information please see http://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/acts/E-1.6/index.html or http://fightspam.gc.ca/eic/site/030.nsf/eng/h_00211.html.
