Privacy Statement

As a non-profit organization, Salmon-Safe respects your right to privacy. This policy summarizes what personally identifiable information we collect and how we might use this information.

Salmon-Safe will only collect personally identifiable information (such as your name and e-mail address) that you voluntarily provide through our web site or email correspondence. We also may collect general information (such as the type of browser you use, the files you request, and the domain name and country from which you request information) to improve our web site and better meet your needs.

Salmon-Safe will not sell or trade your information to other organizations or third parties. And we'll never share your email address with John Ashcroft.
