Privacy Policy

May 12th, 2010

Samachartoday.com fully supports the privacy policy of users or visitors and committed to protect it in all dimensions. The privacy policy says that how samachartoday.com collects and receive the information about user and how it will use.

Log Files

As usually Samachartoday.com makes use of log files. The log files contained the information of Internet protocol (IP) address, date/time stamp, collects users movement around the site, internet service provider, type of browser, track the exit pages , number of click to analyze trends and other. This information is not linked to any other information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Samachartoday.com does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences. A cookie contains personal information which is provided by the user otherwise there is no chance to know your identity even we assign a cookie to your computer.

Double-click DART Cookie

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on Samachartoday.com

Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to Samachartoday.com and other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL’s

http:www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

Some of the third party ad servers or advertisement partners or ad networks use links and advertisement on our site. They also use the cookie and web cons to track the users information.

Samachartoday.com  has no access to  or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers and they have their own privacy policy which are beyond our control.
