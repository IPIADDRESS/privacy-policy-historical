This Privacy Policy describes how we treat personal information received about you when you visit smeclub.net.

    Your Privacy Is Serious Business To Us!

    The privacy of our visitors and members is very important to us. Since we do gather information about you, we provide this privacy statement to enable you understand the terms and conditions associated with the collection of this data, as well as how we use it and how you can correct or change it. In addition, we make to you the following pledge:

    SMEclub Privacy Pledge

    The information we collect about your business will be used for purposes of site research and development in an effort to better serve your needs.

    We will never contact you without your permission, and we will immediately terminate any communications with you upon your request.

    Links to Other Web Sites

    smeclub.net features content from VENTURE VANGUARD as well as a collection of services that are offered by third parties, including a database of businesses for sale, a business plan builder tool and other services. In order to use these third-party services you will be directed to pages on their web sites, which may or may not be co-branded with our logo. Remember that any information that you provide on any third party pages is being provided directly to the third-party service provider and is not subject to this Privacy Policy. We recommend that you review the privacy policy at each third-party Web site to determine how that site protects your privacy.
