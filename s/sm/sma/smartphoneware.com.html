This Privacy Policy explains how Smartphoneware may collect, use and aggregate personal information about you on www.smartphoneware.com.

Information we collect and how we use it


1. Personal information

When you purchase any Smartphoneware product or service we collect personal information like your name, email, your device IMEI, purchase place and date, product informaion, etc.

This information is necessary for us to fulfil and track status of your order and to provide efficient customer service.

We do not collect or save any credit card or other payment information. This information is only collected by our payment processing partners like eSellerate, Handango, and others. We do not have access to this information.

2. Cookies

Certain features of our website such as the shopping cart, and your mobile device model or platform preferences are using cookies.

A cookie consists of information sent by a web server to a web browser, and stored by the browser for preference keeping purposes. Cookies and related tools should not harm your computer or mobile device, and they do not identify you personally.

We use cookies to provide an accurate navigation for our users and automatically present content that match your device selection.

You may set your browser to notify you when a cookie is sent or to refuse cookies altogether, but certain features of our website will not work without cookies.

3. "Automatically Collected" Information

When you access Smartphoneware website, our server may automatically record certain information from your system by using different types of tracking technology. This "automatically collected" information may include Internet Protocol address ("IP Address"), version of software installed, system type, the content and pages that you access on the smartphoneware website, and the dates and times that you visit the website. "Automatically Collected" Information is not linked to personally identifiable information.

"Automatically Collected" Information is used to understand the usage trends and preferences of our Users, to improve the way the website works and looks, and to create new features and functionality.

4. Google Analytics

Smartphoneware uses Google Analytics to analyse the use of this website. Google Analytics generates statistical and other information about website use by means of cookies, which are stored on users' computers. The information generated relating to the website is used to create reports about the use of the website. Google will store this information. Google's privacy policy is available at: http://www.google.com/privacypolicy.html.
