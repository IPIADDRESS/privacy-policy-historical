Use of your Personal Information

We do not share personal information about you outside smartKPIs.com  unless you give us permission to do so or when the law requires it. However, by providing personal information to smartKPIs.com , you agree that such information may be stored outside your country or held by third parties providing services to eab group but will not be used by such third parties for their own purposes.
