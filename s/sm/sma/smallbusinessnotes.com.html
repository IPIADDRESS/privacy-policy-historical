Small Business Notes is committed to protecting the privacy of Internet users. Below is information outlining our continued support for the responsible management of information.

Small Business Notes does not sell or share email addresses or other personal information that people using this site might provide. Unless otherwise directed by the person submitting a comment, email addresses will only be used as necessary to reply to questions or to follow up on legitimate complaints about misuse of the service.

As a fundamental part of operating the site, usage logs are created, archived, and analyzed for aggregate information about usage patterns. No personal information is contained in these reports. Small Business Notes reserves the right to share this aggregate information when appropriate.

The usage logs also contain IP addresses that could be used by law enforcement personnel to track a specific user. Small Business Notes will cooperate with law enforcement personnel by providing this information in the event that the site becomes part of a legitimate criminal investigation.

Small Business Notes links to many external sites that provide related content. We have no control over the privacy policies of these sites.

Small Business Notes also provides some commercial services in association with third parties. Please see the privacy statements hosted at their sites.
