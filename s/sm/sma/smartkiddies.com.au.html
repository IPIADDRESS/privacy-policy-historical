Privacy Policy

This policy covers the gathering of personal information by Smartkiddies and the way the information is handled by Smartkiddies PTY LTD.

 

At Smartkiddies PTY LTD we recognize the need to respect and protect any information gathered from the Smartkiddies website. We are committed to keeping safe and secure any personal information obtained from users. The information is transmitted and stored in securely.

 

Why we collect information

 

To send confirmation of successful registration to members.

 

To provide access to restricted areas.

 

To give access to users to manage their own information.

 

To inform users of any information regarding Smartkiddies.

 

To provide users with information.

 

To contact users regarding information about their accounts.

 

At Smartkiddies PTY LTD we will not give, rent or lease any personally identifying information about you to any individual, organization or company.

 

Choice

 

Users have a choice whether or not to provide information to Smartkiddies PTY LTD. However to gain access to restricted areas a users information is required.

 

Children

 

As Smartkiddies is an Internet resource used by children we are well aware and committed to providing a safe and secure environment for children. For more information about protecting children online go to http://www.netalert.gov.au/

 

Smartkiddies PTY LTD does not collect personal information(name, age, address) directly from children. Parents/Guardians have complete control over a child's membership, that they have registered, at all times.

 

Smartkiddies PTY LTD does not share information about a student's or school's results or progress.

Ultimately users are responsible for the security of the usernames and passwords issued to them. It is recommended that usernames and passwords be kept safe and secure.
