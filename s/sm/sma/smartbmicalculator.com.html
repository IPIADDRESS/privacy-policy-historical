Privacy Policy for the Smart BMI Calculator

Calculator

All the values that you enter in the questionnaire and all the calculated values shown in the results will remain on your own computer. They are stored only in the results page of your browser and remain there for the duration that this page is open, or available in your browser history. They are not stored on the server that performs the calculations.

Advertising

Advertising enables the Smart BMI Calculator to provide a free service that would otherwise have to be charged.

Third party vendors, including Google, use "cookies" to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website. To find out more about cookies see paragraph Website Metrics below.

Google's use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to you, based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of using the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising by visiting the Google's ads preference manager or aboutads.info.

Website Metrics

Website Metrics gives the webmaster more insight about how the Smart BMI Calculator is used. This knowledge is important for the maintenance and further improvements of this site.

For this purpose, Google Analytics is used. This tool uses "cookies" which are text files placed on your computer for collecting standard Internet log information and visitor behaviour information in an anonymous form. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the website (including IP address) is transmitted to Google. This information is then used to evaluate the visitors' use of the website and to compile statistical reports on website activity for the webmaster.

We will never (and will not allow any third party to) use the statistical analytics tool to track or to collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) of the visitors to our site. Google will not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google. Neither we nor Google will link, or seek to link, an IP address with the identity of a computer user. We will not associate any data gathered from this site with any Personally Identifiable Information from any source.
