Cookies do allow you to store items in your shopping basket between visits, making it more convenient for you to shop with us. Most Web Browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually change your browser settings to prevent that. Even without a cookie, you can still place items in your shopping basket and purchase them; they simply will not remain in your basket when you leave the site.
