Introduction.

The mission of Smarterer is to provide a platform to test and score individuals on their digital, social, and technical skills.

Being part of Smarterer means sharing your profile and test scores with other Users. Here are the two main types of information on Smarterer:

  • Profile Information. This is the information provided by you that is displayed on your profile page. Your profile page is visible to the public.
  • Test and Scoring Information. The tests you take, and your scores, are not private and may be visible to the public as soon as you take them. For example, any potential employer can use Smarterer to see how you scored on a test and how you compare against others who took the same test and use the data to evaluate you as an employee candidate.

At any time Smarterer may display information about you that is not included on your profile page, including (a) which tests you have taken, your scores, your rank, your skill level, and how your skill level has changed over time, and (b) your geographic location, company name, or school name.

In addition to the above, any badges or scores you share on social media sites are public.

Search engines may index your profile page or badge page and display it when someone searches your name.

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time, so please review it frequently. By continuing to use the Smarterer service after notice of changes has been sent to you or published on the Smarterer website, you are consenting to the changes.

Please read this policy and send us questions, concerns and suggestions at support@smarterer.com.

Like most websites, we use cookies and web log files to track site usage and trends, to improve the quality of our service, to customize your experience on Smarterer, as well as to deliver third-party advertising to Users both on and off the Smarterer site. A cookie is a tiny data file that resides on your computer, mobile phone, or other device, and allows us to recognize you as a User when you return to the In website using the same computer and web browser. You can remove or block cookies using the settings in your browser, but in some cases that may impact your ability to use Smarterer. One type of cookie, known as a "persistent" cookie, is set once you've logged in to your Smarterer account. The next time you visit the Smarterer website, the persistent cookie will allow us to recognize you as an existing User so you will not need to log in before using the Services. Another type of cookie, called a "session" cookie, is used to identify a particular visit to the Smarterer website. Session cookies expire after a short time or when you close your web browser. In the course of serving advertisements or optimizing the Services to our Users, we may allow authorized third parties to place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser. Any information provided to third parties through cookies will not be personally identifiable but may provide general segment information (e.g., your industry or geography, career field, or information about your professional or educational background) for greater customization of your user experience. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies, but you can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. Smarterer does not store unencrypted personally identifiable information in the cookies.

To support the Services we provide, as well as to provide a more relevant and useful experience for our Users, we may serve our own ads and also allow third party advertisements on the site. To the extent we do this, these advertisements will be targeted and served to Users, by both Smarterer and other companies, called ad networks. Ad networks include third party ad servers, ad agencies, ad technology vendors and research firms. Advertisements on Smarterer may be targeted to Users who fit a certain general profile category or may be based on anonymized information inferred from a User's profile (e.g., skills, industry, compensation bracket, gender, age, ethnic origin, nationality, religion, or other aspects of your life) in addition to a User's use of the Smarterer site.
