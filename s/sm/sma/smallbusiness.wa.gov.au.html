Privacy

Protecting your privacy and your personal information is important to us. While Western Australian Government agencies are not regulated by the Privacy Act 1988, we will respect and protect the privacy of our users and strive to achieve best practice at all times.

The Small Business Development Corporation's (SBDC) websites do not automatically collect information that would identify individual visitors. On the rare occasion that we may  collect personal information this would only be with your consent. You can access any SBDC website without disclosing personal information.

The following information is routinely collected for statistical purposes when you visit one of our websites:

  • server address;
  • top level domain name (for example .com; .gov; .au; .org );
  • date and time of your visit;
  • pages accessed and documents downloaded; and
  • address of previous website visited and the type of browser used.

No attempt is made to identify individual users or their browsing activities except in the unlikely event of an investigation, where a law enforcement agency may exercise a warrant to inspect the service provider's logs.

 

Email management and collection of personal information

We will record your personal information (including email address) if you send us a message, register for a workshop/event, subscribe to our e-newsletter or join a survey/interest group.

The information you provide may be added to our customer relationship management system (CRM) and used for future communications from us. We will also use any additional contact information you provide to assist us in responding to your questions or enquiries, and to confirm your appointments, or workshop and event bookings.

We will not use or disclose your personal information to any third party.

Subscribers to our e-newsletter can unsubscribe at any time.

 

Cookies

A cookie is a piece of information that a website sends to your browser when you access information at that site. Our websites use cookies to track usage patterns and traffic trends, and to improve the user's experience.

Security

The Small Business Development Corporation uses industry standard Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption to ensure the highest security for credit card and personal details submitted to the Corporation over the internet. Our security certificate is issued by GeoTrust and uses Domain Validated SSL with 2048 Bit encryption. Your personal information will be encrypted by your own computer or device at a bit rate determined by the browser you are using, before it is sent to the Corporation.
