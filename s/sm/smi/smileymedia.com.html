Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") to explain how information is collected on the Site and how this information is used and disclosed.  By using the Site or any of the various services made available to you on the Site (the "Services"), you consent to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

The Site and the Services are not directed to children and are intended solely for use by persons 13 years of age or older. All persons under 13 years of age are prohibited from registering on the Site and should not otherwise provide Smiley Media with any personal information. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from individuals under 13 years of age by and through the Site or the Services. If you believe that a child in your care has registered on the Site or has otherwise provided us with personal information, please contact us. We may ask you to provide additional information to verify your identity and your authority to act on the child's behalf before taking appropriate action, if any, to remove or disable any personal information in our control.

Cookies and Passive Data Collection

Smiley Media automatically, or "passively," may collect certain standard user information and clickstream data, which is a record of click selections within the Smiley Media Advertising Network, for all users. For example, we may log a user's Internet Protocol ("IP") address, operating system, browser type, time and date of visit and page views. We may collect this information through the use of server logs, "cookies" and "pixel tags."

Cookies are small data files sent by a website to a user's browser and hard drive that identify the user's computer to the website. We may use cookies to provide the Services and to allow enhanced features, such as recognizing users.  We may also use cookies to enable customizable content and other new features for users as such content and features become available. If you opt-in to this Privacy Policy, Smiley Media may share, sell, or license cookies collected from you browser and hard drive to third-party advertisers. These authorized third parties may link your name, address and e-mail address to other information which they collect, such as past online/offline purchase information and Web usage from other sites. These authorized companies are contractually bound to secure the data they receive.  To learn more about this practice click here: http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp. You may disable cookies by adjusting the settings on your web browser.

Pixel tags are tiny graphic files that are included in web pages, HTML-encoded email messages and certain other types of content, which may read cookies.

Pages on the Site may also contain similar pixel tags that allow us to count users who have visited those pages, to compile aggregated statistics about Site usage and to deliver co-branded and other new features and services as they become available. Pixel tags on the Site may collect information such as a cookie number, time and date of a page view and a description of the page on which the pixel tag resides.  We may use information gathered from pixel tags to improve the content of the Site and email messages, to help us diagnose problems with our servers and other equipment, to administer the Site, to provide the Services and to provide new or enhanced features or tailored products or services to our users as they become available.

Advertisers and other third parties may place third-party elements, including pixel tags, JavaScript code or similar code on the Site, and may use other tools and techniques, to gather information about users of the Site.  These third party elements may be used on the Site to monitor the effectiveness of advertising or to collect anonymous tracking information.  These third parties may also place cookies on the Site, which may be used to gather anonymous tracking information and the same types of information as we collect by using cookies.  These cookies may be placed on the Site in conjunction with pixel tags and pieces of code placed on the Site by that third party.  Information collected by these third parties in these ways is subject to those third parties' policies governing use and disclosure of personal information.

We may link information gathered by passive information collection methods to your Registration Information and other personal information we obtain about you from other sources.  If we cross-match information in this manner, we will treat such cross-matched information as personal information under this Privacy Policy. Additionally, user IP address, web browser, operating system, screen resolution, language, country and visit date are gathered by passive collection methods for the purpose of ensuring that advertising offers on the Smiley Media Advertising Network are targeted to user demographics.  This information may be disclosed to third parties under the same circumstances as personal information under this Privacy Policy.         

In the course of interacting with the Site, you may be given access to the websites of third parties. The privacy practices of those websites will be described in their own privacy policies, and those third party websites are not governed by the Privacy Policy of Smiley Media. 

When users visit particular pages within the Site, personal information may be placed in the URL or in an HTML form POST. This allows customized features for the user. Users should be aware that other websites visited before entering the Site might place personally identifiable information within a user's URL during visits to those websites, and Smiley Media has no control over the practices of such websites. Accordingly, some of this information may be logged by a website that collects URL information that the user visits.
