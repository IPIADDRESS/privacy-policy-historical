General
SDI is providing this privacy statement for your benefit to let you know our procedures for collecting and processing information you submit on SDI web site(s). SDI respects your privacy and is committed to protect the personal information that you share with us. Generally, you can browse through our web site(s) without providing any personal information. SDI does not collect personal information on its web sites unless you choose to provide it. When you do decide to provide us with your personal information, this statement describes how we collect, use and safeguard your personal information.

Information Collection
Personal information means any information which can be used to identify an individual or entity, including name, address (including e-mail address), contact information, title, birth date, gender, occupation, industry, personal interests and other data necessary in order to provide products or services you have requested. SDI does not collect personal information when you visit our web site(s), unless you elect to provide it. We do collect certain other information, including Internet Protocol addresses and domain names, the web browser and operating system used to access our web sites(s), the files visited and time spent in each file, and time and date of visit. SDI does use a standard technology called a "cookie" to collect information about how you use the web site(s). You may elect not to accept cookies by changing the designated settings on your web browser, however, not utilizing cookies may prevent you from using certain functions and features of web sites. SDI uses the information collected from use of cookies to improve its web site(s).

SDI collects personal information when you register with SDI, when you indicate an interest in or request more information about SDI products and services or when you use our web sites to purchase SDI products and services. SDI only uses the personal information so collected in accordance with this Statement of Privacy and applicable laws.

Use of Information
SDI will only use the personal information you provide to us for the purpose for which it was provided, unless we receive your consent. In addition, SDI uses personal information as well as other information collected from its web site(s) to fulfill your requests for information regarding our products and services, keep you up to date on the latest product and service announcements and special offers, to better understand your needs and provide you with improved service and to personalize your experience on our web site(s). SDI will not transfer or sell your personal information to third parties.

Security
SDI protects your personal information by individualized password access. We protect the security of your personal information through physical, electronic and administrative procedures. While we take all reasonable steps to safeguard your data, we cannot assure the security of the information you transmit to us and encourage you to take every appropriate act to protect your personal data while on the Internet.

Changes and Questions
This Privacy Statement was published on July 22, 2008. SDI reserves the right to amend this statement from time to time. If you have any questions about this statement or the collection and use of your personal information, please contact us.
