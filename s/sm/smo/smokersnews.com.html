The Cigarette Smokers News :
1> provides a link to this Privacy Statement on all pages that ask for personal information

We arecurrently using registration forms as part of the Cigarette Smokers News. We may use demographic and customer profile information to tailor the visitors' experience at our site, showing them content that we think might interest them and displaying the content according to their preferences. We share this information with advertisers only on an aggregate, anonymous basis and not on an individual basis.
