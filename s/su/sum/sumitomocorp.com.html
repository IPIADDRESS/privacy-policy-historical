Privacy Policy

SCOA Web Site Privacy Practices TEST

Sumitomo Corporation of America (SCOA) is sensitive to Internet privacy issues and protecting the privacy of visitors to this Web site. To demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy, SCOA created this statement to describe how we use information that we gather from you.

Personal Information

In general, you can visit SCOA on the Web without telling us who you are or revealing any information about yourself. Our Web servers collect the domain names, not the e-mail addresses, of visitors. We compile this information to determine the number of visits, average time spent on the site, pages viewed, etc.

We use that information to determine the effectiveness of our site and to guide future improvements to the site. There are times when we may request information from you, including your name, address and company information. This personal information is used only to respond to a business inquiry or to respond to a request for company literature.

Information Security

SCOA intends to protect the integrity of your personal identifiable information, and ensure the correct usage of such information. SCOA will not sell your personally identifiable information to others, but we may share your information with Sumitomo companies and/or business partners that may better serve your needs. We have implemented appropriate technical and managerial procedures to maintain and safeguard the information we collect online.

Business Relationships

The SCOA Web site contains links to other Web sites. However, SCOA is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

If you have any further questions, please fill out our contact form.
