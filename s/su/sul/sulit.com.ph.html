  • General Rules
    Sulit.com.ph, as a community-based system, aims for a "self-moderated" community wherein members are allowed to moderate their own posts and also help to moderate posts by other members.  This way, it will be easier to manage a continually growing community.  This is the main reason why Report Buttons are available everywhere. Any member is allowed, and in fact advised, to report anything that they think is in violation of any rules and policies set for Sulit.com.ph.  Although Administrators ("Admins") and Forum Moderators still exist to help moderate the entire community.

    Membership in, and/or usage of, Sulit.com.ph is voluntary. Therefore, any access and/or usage of any services provided by Sulit.com.ph is an admission that you have read, understood and agreed to all the rules and policies set by Sulit.com.ph, including any and all amendments, revisions, additions, etc., which Sulit.com.ph may make from time to time.

    Sulit.com.ph aims to be a family-safe website. In order to uphold this goal, among others, all information to be posted and/or specified within the website must follow the rules set forth below.  Sulit.com.ph reserves the right to suspend the account of members who, in Sulit.com.ph’s own opinion, violates or tends to violate these rules. Furthermore, Sulit.com.ph reserves the right to delete any and all posts, advertisements, reply, comment and/or feedback that in its own opinion violates or tends to violate the rules set forth below.

    • No adult content or any sexual materials. The term “adult content” and/or “sexual materials” shall include, but will not be limited to, the following:
      1. Nudity meant simply to titillate and containing very little aesthetic value;
      2. Portrayal of sexual activity;
      3. Images of the male and/or female genitalia, whether it be lewd/explicit or not;
      4. The average person would find that the dominant theme of the work as a whole appeals to prurient interest and satisfies only the craving for gratuitous sex;
      5. It depicts in a patently lewd, offensive, or demeaning manner, excretory functions and sexual conduct such as sexual intercourse, masturbation and exhibition of the genitals; and
      6.  Any other material, which in the opinion of Sulit.com.ph, deemed and/or considered as adult content or sexual in nature.
    • No offensive materials depicting excessive violence.  The term “excessive violence” shall include, but will not be, limited to, the following:
      1. Anything that glorifies or glamorizes violence;
      2. Anything that satisfies the craving for gratuitous violence;
      3. Anything that contains detailed and/or prolonged images of other kinds of violence;
      4. Anything that glorifies criminals or condones violence; and
      5. Any other material, which in the opinion of Sulit.com.ph, that is offensive in nature and/or depicting excessive violence.
    • No foul, offensive or improper language.
    • No racist, hateful, inflammatory or flaming comments.
    • No reference to any illegal materials and activities.
    • Avoid confusing, deceptive and/or misleading information.
    • No reference, except for those considered as “fair use” under existing laws, rules and regulations, to materials protected by copyright and/or intellectual property laws without the prior permission from the rightful owner(s). However, materials, items and/or articles which are replicas, counterfeits and/or unauthorized copies of an original shall likewise be prohibited.

    Membership in, and/or usage of, Sulit.com.ph is voluntary. Therefore, any access and/or usage of any services provided by Sulit.com.ph is an admission that you have read, understood and agreed to all the rules and policies set by Sulit.com.ph, including any and all amendments, revisions, additions, etc., which Sulit.com.ph may make from time to time.

  • System Usage Policies
    1. As a member of Sulit.com.ph, you are responsible for the security of your member account including its username and password.  No member is allowed to share his/her username or password to another user.  Furthermore, no member or registrant will be allowed and/or permitted to use a username which is or may be considered foul, obscene, racist, hateful and/or inflammatory.
    2. Each user is generally allowed only one 1 active member account.  Usage of multiple active accounts is only allowed under the following circumstances:
      1. Each account is used to group or categorize advertisements (e.g. one for real estate and one for other ads; one for personal use and one for business)
      2. NO duplicate ads and NO interaction between the accounts

Sulit.com.ph bears no responsibility whatsoever for any of these contents.
    6. Respect the privacy of other people.  Don't give any personal information of other persons without their permissions.

    8. Sulit Currencies (SC) are not allowed to be used on any activities outside the standard features of Sulit.com.ph without the permission of Sulit.com.ph.  These activities include (but are not limited to) contests, betting, games, other activities using SC as rewards/prizes and selling SC.

  • Classified Ads Posting Rules
    1. Advertisements must be posted in the correct category.  Site admins and moderators can delete, amend and/or rearrange any advertisement which, in Sulit.com.ph's opinion, is not posted in the correct categories.
    2. Each member is not allowed to have two (2) similar active advertisements under one Main Category (e.g. Buy and Sell, Real Estate, etc.).  If your advertisement falls in more than one subcategory under a Main Category, select the best and post your advertisement in that subcategory.
    3. Each member is only allowed to post an advertisement similar to his/her previous advertisement if the previous one is already deleted or has already expired.  Deleted or expired advertisements can be easily reposted with a click of a link.
    4. Two or more members are allowed to post similar advertisements as long as these advertisements pertain to different items.  Duplicate advertisements and copying of text and/or images of other advertisements are strictly prohibited.
    5. Avoid posting multiple, unrelated items in one (1) advertisement.
    6. Do not post any advertisement in the community forum.  Keep all advertisement posts in the main website or in your signature (only for premium members).
    7. Delete any advertisement that is no longer valid (e.g. sold-out items, jobs filled-in).
    8. Always use the correct tags when creating advertisements.  Advertisements with incorrect, irrelevant and/or inaccurate tags are considered misleading and will be deleted without any prior notice.
    9. Posting one's own advertisements as response message to other advertisements ("advertisement hijacking") of other members is not allowed and strictly prohibited
    10. We denounce and prohibit the sale, offering for sale, advertisement or promotion of counterfeit goods which shall include, but not be limited to, replicas and/or unauthorized copies. Sulit.com.ph holds the member accountable for his/her own ads and posts that tend to, or actually infringes on, any patent, copyright, trade or service mark/name or any other intellectual property right governed under applicable laws.
      1. Intellectual property owners may, and are actually advised by Sulit.com.ph to, report posts, ads, listings and/or signatures, as the case may be, that have potential, tend to and/or actually violating copyright, patents, trade or service marks and/or any other intellectual property right to Sulit.com.ph through its e-mail address: admin@sulit.com.ph
    11. Misleading information in advertisements are strictly prohibited.  Misleading information includes, but are not limited to:
      1. Incorrect/inaccurate pricing
      2. Incorrect/inaccurate condition
      3. Advertisements leading to another website or blog filled with other ads, such as Google Adsense, which asks the user to click on the ads to view their content as described on their posted advertisement from Sulit.com.ph
      4. And images unrelated to the advertisement including thumbnails.
    12. Keyword Stuffing(loading an advertisement with repetitive keywords and/or meta tags) on all posted advertisements is strictly prohibited.
    13. Site admins can delete, amend and/or rearrange any advertisement, which in Sulit.com.ph’s own opinion, violates or tends to violate any of the foregoing rule.

  • Feedback Posting Rules
    1. Feedback is allowed to be given to any member only after an agreement on a transaction has been made.  Any feedback given to any member prior to an agreement and/or without any transaction at all is considered as a violation and may be taken out, removed and/or erased by Sulit.com.ph.
    2. As an exception, a no-rating feedback ("comment-only") is allowed to be given related to the discussion between the two (2) parties but no agreement has been made.
    3. Only feedback related to the transaction is allowed.
    4. Only one (1) feedback is allowed per transaction.  A transaction involving a number of items is still considered as only one (1), or a single, transaction.
    5. As an exception, additional feedback is still allowed within the warranty period of the item and/or service.  This includes feedback regarding after sales service as agreed upon by the transacting parties.
    6. Members receiving a feedback can file a dispute, regarding any feedback pertaining to him/her/it, by using the corresponding report button.
    7. Sulit.com.ph reserves the right to delete any feedback or comment that, in its own opinion, violates or tends to violate any of the foregoing rules.

  • Community Forum Rules
    1. Read the General Rules and System Usage Policies as posted above.  Furthermore, membership in, or usage of services provided by, Sulit.com.ph is an admission that you have read, understood and agreed to all the rules and policies set forth herein including any and all amendments and/or updates thereto.
    2. Do not post advertisements in the community forum.  Keep all advertisements in the main website or in your signature.  Posting unrelated links of your website is one form of an advertisement.  Keep those links in your forum signature.
    3. Post topics in the correct category.
    4. Avoid off-topic discussions.  Create a separate topic instead.  Also avoid posts that will "hijack" the discussion from the original topic.  Posts preventing other members to join the discussion must likewise be avoided (e.g. posts that can be understood by some or only a few members).
    5. Avoid creating a new topic which has already been discussed.  Use the search functionality of the forum to check and verify if an existing and/or related topic already exists.  Any member can only create a similar topic to an existing one only if that existing topic is already archived.
    6. Avoid very short and non-sensical posts.  Furthermore, avoid excessive use of smilies or emoticons. Smilies or emoticons must not exceed twenty-five percent (25%) of your message post (an emoticon is considered as a word).  Therefore, message posts made up of only emoticons are not allowed.
    7. The following writing styles for the forum title and/or message posts are strictly prohibited as they make forum posts difficult to read:
      1. Using ALL CAPS or UPPER CASE LETTERS
      2. Using camel-case text (ThiS Is a sAMplE oF CamEl cASe TeXt)
      3. Using text speak, text lingo, or SMS language (Ds s @n xmpl 0f TxT l1ng0!)
      4. Putting s p a c e s, p.e.r.i.o.d.s, or sp3c!@l ch@r@c+ers in between letters
      5. And using other forms of writing that make forum posts difficult to read
    8. Use descriptive topic title/subject relevant to the post.
    9. Avoid non-sensical topics or topics which are not really meant for discussion.
    10. Posting same messages at different topics multiple times is considered spam and is strictly prohibited.  Furthermore, multiple posts in succession in a topic is strictly NOT allowed unless you are quoting responses.  If you need to add something in your previous and recent post, edit that post instead.
    11. Articles copied from other websites must always contain an attribute link to the address of the copied article.  Copy/Paste articles from other websites NOT worthy for discussion are NOT allowed.
    12. Avoid arguing any moderator-related actions (e.g. closing a topic, moving a topic to a new category, etc.) and penalties given by administrators to reported violations in public.  Any disagreement to moderator-related actions must first be addressed to the moderator or administrator concerned privately (via a private message or email). Unresolved issues related to moderation must then be submitted to the administrator (or other administrators) supported by the positions of both sides.  However, having an argument or debating, disagreeing, or discussing opposing or differing viewpoints against the PERSONAL opinions, suggestions or idea of moderators and administrators are ALLOWED.
    13. Avoid adding irrelevant images to forum posts.
    14. Avoid using languages or dialects other than English or Filipino as a sign of respect to other members who cannot read and understand other languages or dialects.
    15. Be sensible, polite and use common sense when joining any discussion.  Show respect to other members so that they can show respect to you in return.  Discussions should be kept within the spirit of fun, cooperation and information dissemination.
    16. Avoid attacks to any members.  Any public attacks whether explicit or implied will not be tolerated.
    17. Any and all posts on the forum which, in the opinion of Sulit.com.ph, violates or tends to violate these rules and policies may be removed and the author thereof sanctioned with the appropriate penalty as determined exclusively by Sulit.com.ph.

  • Signature Usage
    Signatures are being used so that default information can be appended at the end of your posts (for premium members only).  Advertisements are allowed in signatures (for premium members only).  As a member of Sulit.com.ph, you must follow these signature requirements:
    1. Maximum length/size of signature:
      - 5 lines of text using default font size (10pt) OR 3 lines (12pt) OR 1 line (14pt) with total height when displayed NOT more than 120 pixels
      OR
      - 1 line of banner images and 1 line of text using default font size (10pt)
    2. Maximum height of banner image: 650 x 120 pixels
    3. Maximum size of banner images (total) is 50Kb
    4. Signature must not be distracting to the viewers of our web pages
    5. Links are allowed to be on your signature given that there is not more than 3 of them
    6. NO animated banners (with the exception of Advertisement Ticker 1 being generated by the system, given that it is applied using the existing dimensions)

  • Inactive Members (Deactivated)
    All members who have not logged in for the past twelve (12) months will have their account automatically deactivated.  Once your account has been deactivated, you will be informed accordingly by the system when you try to login again. However, to reactivate your account, you just need to login again using your registered username/email and password.

    Moreover, the system will deactivate member accounts wherein the corresponding e-mail addresses assigned to the accounts reject or bounce our e-mails due to the following:
    • mailbox full;
    • e-mail account deactivated;
    • e-mail account suspended;
    • e-mail domain inaccessible;
    • mailserver with technical problems; and
    • e-mail requiring verification

    Once you have fixed the problem(s) with your e-mail, you can login again to re-activate your account.

     Furthermore, all Members who fail to log in for six (6) consecutive months will have all their active advertisements automatically archived.

  • Privacy Policy

    Introduction
    Sulit.com.ph respects each individual’s right to personal privacy.  We will collect and use information through our Web site only in the ways disclosed in this statement.  This statement applies solely to information collected at Sulit.com.ph’s Web site.


    Part I.  Information Collection
    Sulit.com.ph collects information through our Web site at several points.  We collect the following information about primary visitors: Name and E-mail Address.

     

    Sulit.com.ph does not actively market to children, and we never knowingly ask a child under eighteen (18) to divulge personal information.

     

    We collect the following general data that is not personally identifiable information: Birthday and Gender.  We collect demographic data in order to perform general analysis of our traffic that can help us improve our system better.

     

    We collect this information through registration forms and profile update forms.  The information is collected voluntarily.   To receive our services, Name and E-mail Address is mandatory because our system needs a point of contact with our members.  On the other hand, other information we request is optional.

     

    We do employ cookies. A cookie is a small text file that our Web server places on a user’s computer hard drive to be a unique identifier. Cookies enable Sulit.com.ph to track usage patterns and deliver customized content to users.  Our cookies do have an expiration date. Our cookies do not collect personally identifiable information.

     

    Part II.  Information Usage.
    The information collected by Sulit.com.ph will be used for the purpose of getting in touch with the members.  Users who provide information will receive e-mail notification services regarding items posted in our system.

     

    Registered users will receive additional announcements from us about products, services, special deals, and a newsletter. Out of respect for the privacy of our users we present the option not to receive these types of communications.

     

    The information we collect will not be used to create customer profiles based on browsing or purchasing history.  We will not supplement information collected at our Web site with data from other sources.

     

    We will not share data with any third parties; provided that, in the event that Sulit.com.ph is directed by any competent governmental authority to disclose such data, Sulit.com.ph shall not be held liable for such disclosure.

     

    We offer links to other Web sites.  Please note: When you click on links to other Web sites, we encourage you to read their privacy policies.  Their standards may differ from ours.

     

    Our Web site offers listings, chats, forums and/or message boards.  Please be advised that information posted in these venues becomes public knowledge.

     

    If our policy on information collection or uses changes, we will advise you through our monthly newsletter or by posting an announcement through our system.

     

    Part III.  Access to Information
    Sulit.com.ph maintains the accuracy of our information.  Users may access their own personal information and change any inaccuracies they may find by updating their profile.  Users may delete their information from our database by updating their profile or sending us a support ticket to inactivate their member account.

     

    Part IV. Problem Resolution
    If problems arise, members and/or users may contact Sulit.com.ph by submitting a support ticket at our Help Center.  We are committed to resolving disputes within the most expedient time.

     

    Part V. Data Storage and Security
    Sulit.com.ph protects user information by using secure servers. Nonetheless, Sulit.com.ph may not be held liable for any loss of information and/or data.

     

    Part VI. Third Party Advertising

    We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.
