Privacy Policy

Posted on February 22nd, 2010 by Superstar Magazine

At Superstar Magazine (“Superstar Magazine”) we appreciate your support and do consider your personal privacy to be of high value to us. Your personal information provided is of high importance to us and protecting the information is our top priority.

In adopting this privacy policy (this “Privacy Policy”), our intention is to balance our information collection activities through legitimate means and using your personal information with your expectation of privacy at a reasonable level. This Privacy Policy may be updated or revised without notice and we do encourage you to check it regularly to ensure you are familiar with the terms of our Privacy Policy in the most recent revision.

When does Superstar Magazine collect my personal information?

Your personal information collection may be done in one of the following six methods: (1) by sharing your email address when subscribing to our newsletter, (2) by sharing your personal information when submitting hot tips to our editiorial team, (3) by sharing personal information when making comments on posts or interviews, (4) by pariticipating in any competitions or prize draws that we may run from time to time, (5) by enabling social networks to login you into Superstar Magazine, (6) by submitting your interview for submission to Superstar Magazine.
