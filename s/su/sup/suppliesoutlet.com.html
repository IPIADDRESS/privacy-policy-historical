PRIVACY POLICY

SuppliesOutlet.com is committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect about you to process orders and to provide a more personalized shopping experience. Your personal information will never be, under any circumstances, released, sold or rented to any third party, Guaranteed!

Security Measures

For credit card transactions, SuppliesOutlet.com adds security by using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology. This means that the credit or debit card information you send is encrypted by your computer, and then decrypted again on our side, preventing others from accessing your private information in between. So, when using an SSL capable browser (up to date versions of Firefox, Safari, Netscape and Internet Explorer, for instance), shopping on the SuppliesOutlet.com website is the safest kind of transaction possible with a credit card. Our site is also verified daily to be McAfee secure for your protection.
Order Process Information

To process your order, we require your name, billing address, phone number, email address, shipping address and credit or debit card information. We use this information to process your order and, if any questions should arise, to contact you about your order. In general, we will try to contact you first by email and if that fails, or if time is crucial, we will try to contact you by phone. We share this information, and only that information which is absolutely necessary, with those third parties that are involved in the processing of your order. For example, the financial institution that issued your credit card and the shipping carrier that delivers your order.
Privacy of Information

Aside from the previously mentioned third parties involved in the processing of your order, SuppliesOutlet.com will not share your email address or shopping activity with other parties.
