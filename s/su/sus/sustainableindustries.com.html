Privacy Policy

We have created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this web site.

We use your IP address to gather broad demographic information. Our traffic analysis application gathers this information automatically and we are unable to control that collection. We do not use this information in any way that would identify a unique user.

Cookies
Our site currently does not use cookies.

Personal Information
This web site does not request any personal information or collect any information that personally identifies you or allows you to be personally contacted.

Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. We make every effort to keep any email addresses that we collect (if you join our mailing list) safe.

Contacting the Web Site
