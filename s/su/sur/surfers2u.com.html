PRIVACY POLICY

Surfers2u.com is committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect about you only within the Surfers2u.com Network to maintain your membership profile.

About the Information We Collect:

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted within the Surfers2u.com program.

On some pages, you can register for the Surfers2u.com program. Here is what kind of personal information we collect on these pages:
Email Address
Your Name

Why We Collect the Information We Do.

When you use some of the features of Surfers2u.com, we need to know your name, e-mail address, country and interests. Once we collect this information, it is only used within the Surfers2u.com community and no individually identifiable information is distributed to third parties.

We use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design our website and to share it with advertisers. For example, we may tell an advertiser that X number of individuals visited a certain area on our website, or that Y number of people from country z filled out our registration form, but we would not disclose anything that could be used to identify those individuals.

Security/Confidentiality.

Surfers2u.com does not provide individually identifiable information to third parties. Surfers2u.com uses proprietary technologies to preserve the anonymity of members while matching their interests with targeted information sent via email. Surfers2u.com will under no circumstance reveal any member’s identity to marketers. It is solely at a Surfers2u.com members discretion if he or she chooses to provide personally identifiable information to a marketer by making a purchase, requesting additional information, or communicating directly with the company or organization after visiting its web site.

Limits of Confidentiality.

Due to the existing legal regulatory and security environment, we may be forced to disclose individually identifiable information to third parties. We will use our best efforts to limit disclosure only under these circumstances where necessary by contract, legal process, or the technical operation of the service.

Review/Modify/Terminate Membership.

Surfers2u.com members may choose at any time to review, modify or permanently remove all contact and interest information stored by Surfers2u.com by contacting our support staff. Once removed from the network, a past member will no longer receive any communications from Surfers2u.com. Surfers2u.com will never sell, rent, or otherwise use the contact information of former members.
