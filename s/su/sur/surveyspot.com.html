Privacy Statement
.

Your privacy is important

We use your personal information purely for research purposes and never divulge your personally identifiable information to anyone.

Rest assured that your anonymity is guaranteed. SurveySpot only uses the information on the registration form to assess demographic trends and invite you to the SurveySpot surveys that best match your interest. We do not share any of our individual members' personally identifiable information.

In addition, we will never try to sell you anything; we are only interested in your opinions.

SurveySpot supports the rights of our members by limiting the use of its information for legitimate marketing research purposes only. SurveySpot is active in supporting the following organizations created to uphold ethical survey research:

  • Marketing Research Association (MRA) .
  • American Marketing Association (AMA) .
  • American Association for Public Opinion Research (AAPOR) .
  • Council of American Survey Research Organizations (CASRO) .
  • Advertising Research Foundation (ARF) .
When you fill out a survey, SurveySpot uses your responses along with other participants' responses to inform clients about people's attitudes about their products or services. We use your registration information with hundreds of others' information, and we never disclose the personal names and addresses of our participants to anyone.

We use your e-mail address to send you invitations to participate in new surveys and to contact you with messages about the SurveySpot panel. We use your postal address to mail any survey-related incentives or product samples. We will never share your e-mail address or personally identifiable information with anyone.
