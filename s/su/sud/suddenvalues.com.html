SuddenValues Privacy Policy

Last update October 3, 2010

Protecting the privacy of our customers is a major priority of SuddenValues.com mission and success. This Privacy Statement outlines how your personal information is collected and used by our network of websites and services that are located at the SuddenValues.com, SuddenBlitz.com and the domains and locations of our participating merchant customers. By using this website you acknowledge that you have read, understand and agree to be bound by the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Collection and use of information.

We collect both personally identifiable ("Personal") and non-personally ("Non-Personal") identifiable information from subscribers to our services and those of our merchant customers. Personal information is collected to allow subscribers and merchant customers ("Users") to create accounts, buy vouchers, receive updates and participate in services. Additionally, we use this information to better understand Users needs. Of this Personal information, demographic information is only in aggregate form. Non-Personal information is collected to measure our success and better understand the desires of our Users. SuddenValues is the sole owner of information collected on this site, including those that participate in our services that may have joined at merchant's physical location or website. Our merchant customers are the sole owners of information collected on their sites and at their physical locations. We do not sell, share, rent or otherwise distribute this User information, beyond those Users that elect to become one of our merchant's customers, affiliates that need data to perform the services and/or as required by law.

Visitors to our network of websites and participating merchant locations are offered the opportunity to register for promotional and informational email campaigns that are sent directly from SuddenValues.com and/or its participating merchant customers. In registration, users are required to provide contact information such as name and email address to receive these email campaigns, but may opt-out of future email campaigns at any time. If requested, via an email to opt-out@suddenvalues.com, visitors can receive a notice with the approximate date and source of where their contact information was collected.

Distribution of information.

Your Personal information will never be sold, shared, rented or otherwise distributed to third parties for promotional use, beyond those Users that elect to become one of our merchant's customers, affiliates that need data to perform the services and/or as required by law. Personal information is only disclosed in a limited set of circumstances: (1) to allow our Dealers and merchant customers to perform their duties in providing the services in their community, (2) to enable contracted third parties to assist in their support of services and (3) as required by law or regulating authorities. To the extent legally possible, we require each to maintain this information in confidence.

Personal tracking.

To enable our participating merchants, Dealers and staff to best serve you, we use a variety of technology on our website and within our services to track usage. Some of these include browser based Cookies, email based Web Beacons and server based Log Files. These tracking technologies allow us to measure the success of services in order to best serve you and participating merchants. This information is never sold, shared, rented or otherwise distributed to third parties for promotional use.

Third party links.

We allow Dealers and participating merchants to provide links beyond our Websites and Service. As third parties, they may choose to collect information about you that we cannot control. Please review their individual privacy policies to understand how they collect and use Personal information.

Personal information security.

We continually update the security features of our websites, services and supporting technology to protect your Personal information. Our facilities are protected by state of the art security and watched over by security guards 24/7. All Dealers, participating merchants and staff are educated on best security practices.

Opt-in / Permission based service.

We provide a permission based service, which means you must choose to signup online or at a participating merchant's location. At anytime, you may choose to opt-out or make changes in your interest via a link provided in the footer of all email messages. You can also opt-out by forwarding the email to us at opt-out@suddenvalues.com.

Privacy policy changes.

In the event our privacy policies need to change, the changes will be reincorporated into this document and reposted for your benefit. We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time.
