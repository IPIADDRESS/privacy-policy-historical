Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to information collected on SunRun's website.

Certain features and pages on this website ask for you to provide personal information which may include name, email address, address, or telephone number.

We use this information to determine whether solar and SunRun's service plans are right for you or to otherwise respond to your request. Your privacy is extremely important to us. We will never give or sell your information to third parties without your consent. We do not sell personal information about you to unrelated companies for their independent use.

Personal information about our customers or former customers will only be disclosed as permitted or required by law. Information that has been collected is securely maintained in your file in our records.
