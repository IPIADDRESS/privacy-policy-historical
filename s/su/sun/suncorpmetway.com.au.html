Privacy

At Suncorp Metway we want to serve our customers as effectively and conveniently as possible, which means being able to maximise the use of customer information and technology throughout the Suncorp Metway Group. We also want to ensure that our customers are confident that any information collected by us is treated with the appropriate degree of confidentiality and privacy.

This Privacy Promise seeks to explain how we collect and use customer information. We are committed to our Privacy Promise.
 
Privacy Promise
1. What customer information do we collect?
In order for us to provide you with high quality services, we may collect information contained in the Suncorp Metway computer systems and other related information for use in the business activities of the Suncorp Metway Group as we determine.
 
2. How is the information used?
Customer information collected and used by Suncorp Metway helps us provide clear and up-to-date information about our business and the products and services we offer which in turn enables you to make informed choices and helps us to provide the best service we can.
 
We collect and use customer information for the following purposes:
• administrative and operational purposes;
• to deliver superior service;
• to undertake new product development;
• to market products and services;
• to prevent any unauthorised access to customer details;
• to make recommendations to you about other goods and services we offer that may be of interest to you;
• to comply with any applicable laws and/or regulations; and
• for such further and other lawful uses in the development of our business consistent with our Privacy Promise.
 
If you do not wish to be contacted by Suncorp Metway about the other products and services we offer, simply write to us at the address set out below and we will remove you from any mail, telephone or electronic lists used by us for marketing purposes. 3. Will the information be disclosed to other parties?
We may distribute customer information within the Suncorp Metway Group, or disclose it to other parties in connection with our normal business operations. Otherwise, customer information is not generally available to others and we will not sell customer information to any marketing company or others unless disclosed by us or required by law to do so.
 
4. How do we protect customer information?
We ensure all customer information is protected and kept in accordance with any applicable industry codes or standards and as required by law.
 
We have taken measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, misuse, disclosure or alteration.
 
5. How is customer information updated?
At Suncorp Metway we strive to maintain up-to-date and accurate information about our customers. You may request at any time a copy of the personal information held by us, by contacting us at the address set out above.
 
Should you believe any of the personal information held by us is incomplete or inaccurate, please notify us immediately so that we may update that information.
 
6. Employees
Our employees are aware of their responsibility in maintaining the security and privacy of customer information.
 
We only permit authorised employees to have access to the customer information required to perform their roles within the Suncorp Metway Group.
 
7. Changes to the Privacy Promise
By using the Suncorp Metway website and by accepting the Suncorp Metway Online Access and Suncorp Metway Share Trade Terms and Conditions, you consent to and authorise the collection and use of personal information by us. If we decide to change our Privacy Promise, we will post the changes on this page so that you will always be aware of what information is collected, how we use it and under what circumstances we disclose it.
 
8. Who is part of the Suncorp Metway Group?
Any reference to Suncorp Metway in this Privacy Promise includes a reference to the Suncorp Metway Group which consists of:
 
(a) Suncorp-Metway Ltd ACN 010 831 722; and
(b) Companies that are subsidiaries or related body corporates of Suncorp-Metway Ltd from time to time.

We only permit authorised employees to have access to the customer information required to perform their roles within the Suncorp Metway Group.
 
Feedback
If at any time you believe we have not followed our Privacy Promise or if you have some query or comment about the Privacy Promise, please contact us at the address set out above.
We will make every effort to resolve the problem to the satisfaction of all parties and/or provide you with a response to your query or comment.
