Privacy Policy
for SunriseSunset.com
   

User-Entered Data
This site does not record or collect any information entered by a user of this site.

Your Email Address
If you contact this site via email, your email address will remain private and will not be added to any mailing list. It will only be used to reply to you.

GPS Coordinates
This site does not record, collect, transmit or store any latitude or longitude numbers retrieved from your device. They are only passed along in the URL to calculate times on the mobile or full calendar page.

Using This Site
This site will track a user temporarily to determine site usage statistics. Use of the site may be monitored.

Cookies & Your Favorites or Preferences
This site will use cookies to store some personal preferences, such as your preferred font size on the calendar. Disabling cookies may result in losing some functionality or personalization of the site.

Cookies store their data on your computer. This data is transmitted to the site while browsing. This site does not record, collect or store that data.

Cookies & Advertising
Third party vendors, including Google and others, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site and other web sites on the Internet.

  • Google
    Google's use of the DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads based on visits to this site and/or other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the DART cookie by visiting the advertising opt-out page.
     

You can also set some privacy settings on these sites:

  • IAB opt-out platform
  • Network Advertising Initiative opt-out platform
  • Digital Advertising Alliance platform

These provide options to express choices for all companies that are registered on that platform.
