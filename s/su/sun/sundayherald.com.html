Collection of personal information
Personal information can be collected by us from you when you register to use the website, request services from us or third parties connected with us, e-mail us, use our website, participate in promotions, enter competitions or otherwise provide your personal details.

Other information

Cookies

We reserve the right to use this information to track your movements through our website. Personal information about you is not collected during this process. You can disable the cookies if your browser allows this. For further information on how to do this, please refer to www.about-cookies.org .
