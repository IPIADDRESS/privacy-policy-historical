selinks.com treats your privacy seriously. This section dwells on how selinks.com treats personal information we collect and receive. •    selinks.com believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.
