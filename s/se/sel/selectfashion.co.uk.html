PRIVACY POLICY

At Select it's our duty to maintain the privacy of all our customers and website users.  Our privacy policy is detailed below.  If we make any changes to our policy, we will notify you by updating this statement.

We collect and maintain certain personal information about you when you complete any of our online forms (eg: member's login, contact us), such as your name, telephone number, home address, email address and your age for competitions or newsletters.  We may collect information about our website to enable us to develop it in the future.

We will use this information to send you any requested information and to respond to enquiries made via 'Contact Us'.  We will also send you newsletters, updates and offers from time to time.

If you would prefer not to receive information from us, simply contact us by phone, post or email via contact us, typing "unsubscribe Select" in the subject heading.

We may contact you either by post, or by telephone or by email, initially as a follow-up to your request/enquiry or to answer any questions.  We may also, occasionally, contact you about other products and services that may be of interest to you.   When you contact us to ask for information by post, we shall collect your postal address.

USE OF COOKIES

A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's computer tied to information about the user.  We use both session ID cookies and persistent cookies.  For the session ID cookie, once users close the browser, the cookie simply terminates.  A persistent cookie is a small text file stored on the user's hard drive for an extended period of time.  Persistent cookies can be removed by following Internet browser help file directions.  Cookies are used to manage sessions on our site.  User's must enable cookies to use our site.  Also, users may optionally use a cookie to remember their password in order to automatically log in to our site.  We do not and will not use cookies to collect private information from any user which they did not intentionally submit to us.

THIRD PARTY RELATIONSHIPS

Your information will NOT be shared by us with other companies.  Areas of our site may contain links to other sites that may be of value to you.  Please note that we do not have any control over the content or privacy practises of these sites.  We urge you to read the privacy policy of these sites before submitting any personal information. 

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us.
