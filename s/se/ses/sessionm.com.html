Privacy Policy

SessionM, Inc. takes your privacy seriously. We recognize that privacy is an important issue, so we design and operate our services with the protection of your privacy in mind. Please read the following to learn more about our Privacy Policy.

By visiting or using SessionM’s applications, website and services, and any other linked pages, features, content, or any other services.

Types of Information Collected

The term “personal information” as used in this Privacy Policy refers to personally identifiable information that specifically identifies an individual (such as an individual’s first and last name, address, telephone number, e-mail address, credit card or other account number), and information about that individual or such individual’s activities or preferences when such information is directly linked to personally identifying information. How we collect and use information

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Information you provide. When you sign up for some of our products, we ask you for personal information. We may combine the information you submit under your account with information from other SessionM services or third parties in order to provide you with a better experience and to improve the quality of our services. For certain services, we may give you the opportunity to opt out of combining such information.
  • Cookies. When you visit SessionM, we send one or more cookies to your computer or other device. We use cookies to improve the quality of our service, including for storing user preferences, improving products and ad selection, and tracking user trends, such as how people use our services. SessionM also uses cookies in its advertising services to help advertisers and publishers serve and manage ads.
  • Unique Device ID. SessionM offers publishers and advertisers several methods that enable SessionM to measure user activity, manage point accrual, manage advertising, manage user accounts, assess advertising performance and detect fraudulent access and general usage across the SessionM network of web sites and applications. These methods may include but are not limited to the use of unique device-specific identifiers that are found in most mobile devices, such as the IDFA, UDID, Android ID, MEID and others, in either a raw, hashed or otherwise obfuscated format. These methods also may include the use of a unique server-generated network token, either anonymous or known, that is stored on the device as a cookie or as a user default setting. It is up to the publisher of the underlying application and/or web site to decide which methods are appropriate and consistent with their privacy policy. These methods allow SessionM to record activities such as achievements earned, displayed and claimed, track mobile point balances and measure engagement with SessionM sponsors.
  • Log information. When you access SessionM services via a browser, application or other client our servers automatically record certain information. These server logs may include information such as your web request, your interaction with a service, Internet Protocol address, browser type, browser language, the date and time of your request and one or more cookies that may uniquely identify your browser or your account.
