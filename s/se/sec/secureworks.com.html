Worm Alert - W32.Kibuv.B

W32.Kibuv.B -- Kibuv is a new direct propogation worm that infects through IRC and FTP as well as a variety of Windows vulnerabilities. Kibuv affects corporate networks that make heavy use of Microsoft servers on their perimeter. SecureWorks strongly recommends updating patches at http://windowsupdate.microsoft.com .
