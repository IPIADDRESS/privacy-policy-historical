Cookies, Clear GIFs, Clickstream and Browser Information

We may use "cookies" on or in conjunction with our website to help Securly identify and track visitors, their usage of Securly websites, and their website access preferences. A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. If you do not wish to have cookies placed on your computer you should set your browser to refuse cookies before using Securly's websites, with the drawback that certain features of Securly's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies. We do not link the information we store in cookies to any Personal Information you submit while on our website.
