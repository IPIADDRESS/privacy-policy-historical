This notice applies to all information collected from users or submitted by users on our website. If you are a user of our website, including those users in the European Union Area , your continued use of the website signifies your consent to the information collection, use of cookies, our privacy policy and our terms of use.

Users in the European Union Area who do not consent to the information collection noted below, should discontinue use of our website until we are able to provide an opt-in and opt-out mechanism relating to the collection of personal information.

Personal information collected by us includes:

* Email Address (submitted by users who register for our website)

These companies typically use a cookie or third party web beacon to collect this information. To learn more about this behavioral advertising practice, you can visit networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp.
