SecurityMetrics, Inc. does not disclose, rent, or sell email addresses, security test results, or any other information that we may receive to any third party, unless specifically requested by the customer or requested or required by applicable credit card associations, credit card processors, or in connection with any legal proceedings where disclosure of such data has been requested or required.
