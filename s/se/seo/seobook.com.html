Data Collection

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes only the consumer's domain name and IP address (which is recorded only if they create an account). We do not collect e-mail address unless users create an account requesting to participate on our site.

We collect the e-mail addresses and IP addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail. If you leave your e-mail address in a comment or in your public profile on this site, others will be able to view it. If you enter your domain name in your public profile on this site then others will be able to view your website. If you comment on this site with the intent to lie or deceive people we may publicly disclose your name, addresss, IP address, or other information associated with your comments. So far we have only done this once - to a malicious user that created over a dozen user accounts, tried to shill for their own product, and bashed us.

The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web page. User comments help us understand what our users want, and help make pages more complete. Personal information is not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes. We will never sell or rent your email address. If you fraud or spam us, we may publicly reveal that.

Our Tools and Your Data

We offer a number of free and paid SEO tools. We track aggregate usage of our tools for the purposes of troubleshooting technical issues and preventing abuse of our tools (from bots, scrapers, and so on). We also may monitor aggregate tool usage statistics for tracking conversions from free accounts to paid accounts.

Other examples of how our tools are used are:

SeoBook's Rank Checker is a self-hosted application for use as a Firefox plugin. We do not track the data you input into the Rank Checking plugin.

SeoBook's Keyword Research tools is powered by Wordtracker and our Competitive Research tool is powered by SemRush. You can check the privacy policies of both companies from the links given.

The 3 tools mentioned above also adhere to the disclosures mentioned above regarding the data we collect from the usage of our tools.

SeoBook does not look at the specific use of any of our tools, free or paid.

Cookies

With respect to cookies: We set cookies if you create and log into your user account, to help make your user account easier to use. We use conversion tracking cookies on inbound affiliate links and we use analytics programs like Google Analytics to track conversions and improve our site's utility for customers.

Email Subscriptions

If you are subscribed to our mailing list you may unsubscribe via the unsubscribe link in the footer of any email sent to you from this site. If you are having issues with unsubscribing, and do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending us an e-mail with your username in it to the above address.

Mailing Address

If you supply us with your postal address on-line you will only receive the information for which you provided us your address.

Telephone Information

Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers on-line will only receive telephone contact from us with information regarding orders they have placed on-line, or for requests for communication.

Ad Servers

With respect to Ad Servers: Currently the site has some affiliate links on it. On occasion we may use Google AdSense, which offers paid placement ads relevant to the content of the web pages. We have no way of controlling these ads. Google has started regional testing for these ads and may place ads relevant to your location if possible.

Policy Changes

From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change at some time in the future we will post the policy changes to our Web site to notify you of these changes and provide you with the ability to opt out of these new uses. If you are concerned about how your information is used, you should check back at our Web site periodically. I will never rent or sell email addresses or other personal information.

Customers may prevent their information from being used for purposes other than those for which it was originally collected by e mailing us at seobook@gmail.com.

Upon request we provide site visitors with access to all information [including proprietary information] that we maintain about them.
Consumers can access this information by submitting an e-mail to us at the above address.

Consumers can have this information corrected by sending us e-mail at the above address.

User Security

With respect to security: When we transfer and receive certain types of sensitive information such as financial or health information, we redirect visitors to a secure server. Currently we use Paypal as a third party payment processing agent.

Questions or Comments?

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us via email or at the above addresses or phone number.
