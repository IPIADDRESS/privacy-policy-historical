Privacy Policy

SEO by the Sea does not sell or share personally identifiable data to any third parties whatsoever.

Information collected by SEO by the Sea about visits to the site in SEO by the Sea’s generic web logs will not be shared with others, except for the use of non-identifying and aggregated information to better the design of SEO by the Sea or attract advertisers.

SEO by the Sea uses Google Analytics and Google Webmaster tools to learn about traffic to the site and improve the services offered by the site.

Private information shared with SEO by the Sea through contact form or email or in any other method will remain private unless permission is provided to share the information, or the information is demanded through an order from the Courts or a specific legal authority of State or Federal Government with proper warrant or subpoena.

SEO by the Sea does not target nor purposefully or knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

SEO by the Sea contains links to other websites which have their own privacy practices, and this privacy policy only applies to the pages of SEO by the Sea, and not to any other site which may be linked to by this site.

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this privacy policy, please feel free to contact me.

I recommend that anyone using the Web learn more about privacy issues online, and suggest the following resources as good places to begin to find out more on the topic:

  • Electronic Privacy Information Center
  • Privacy.org
  • Federal Trade Commission – Privacy Initiatives
  • Data Collection – The European Commission

– Bill Slawski
President, SEO by the Sea
