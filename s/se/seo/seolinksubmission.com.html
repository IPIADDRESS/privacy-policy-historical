Personal Information Privacy

We will take all appropriate steps to keep your personal information secure. These steps include limiting access to clients information databases, communicating this policy statement to employees of the company and establishing and enforcing penalties for violating this statement. We will communicate with clients via e-mail or other online delivery devices only if the client agrees to receive those communications.
