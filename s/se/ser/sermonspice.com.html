Privacy Policy for Sermon Spice

The privacy of our visitors to Sermonspice.com is important to us.

In this statement you will find:

  • the information we collect and how it is used
  • how you can opt in or out of Sermonspice.com promotional email
  • how you can review and request changes to your information
  • security procedures
  • how to contact us

Information Collection and Use

The information collected by Sermonspice.com falls into two categories:

(1) information voluntarily supplied by visitors and users of the website

(2) tracking information recorded as visitors and users navigate through the website.  Some of this information is personally identifiable information, but much of it is not.  Personally identifiable information is information that identifies a particular person.

We consider the personally identifiable information contained in our business records to be confidential.  We may sometimes disclose personally identifiable information about you to our affiliates or to others who work for us. For example, when you use certain functions on the website such as Live Chat you may notice that the website is actually collecting or processing information.

Non-personally identifiable information about visitor and users of the website may also be recorded.  This information is used primarily to tailor and enhance the users experience.  We may use this information in an aggregate, non-personally identifiable form to, among other things, measure the use of our website and determine which products are most popular with visitors and users.

We do use cookies to store information, such as your personal preferences when you visit our site, in order to make our website more responsive to the needs of our visitors and users.  You can chose to disable or selectively turn off our cookies in your browser settings, or by managing preferences in programs such as Norton Internet Security.  However, this can affect how you are able to interact with our site as well as other websites.  This could include the inability to log in, and checkout.

Credit card information is never saved unless you choose to save it.  You can delete saved credit card information by logging into the website and clicking on “My Account” or by clicking here: https://www.sermonspice.com/account.

Promotional Email

You may choose to receive, or not to receive, promotional emails about Sermonspice.com’s coupons and products by logging into the website and clicking on “My Account” or by clicking here: https://www.sermonspice.com/account.

You may also unsubscribe from promotional emails by clicking the “unsubscribe” link at the bottom of the email.

Changing Your Information

You may review and change information that you provide to us directly through Sermonspice.com by clicking on “My Account” at the top of the page or by clicking here: https://www.sermonspice.com/account. You may also request changes to some information, such as name, email, address, and phone number by emailing info(at)sermonspice.com or by calling 1-855-703-9243.
