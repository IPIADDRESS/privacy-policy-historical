Privacy Policy

"Personally identifiable information" is information that can be matched with a person's identity, such as a user's address, personal interests, activities, spending habits, and significant relationships. Companies, governments, or other organizations often collect "personally identifiable information" in order to conduct business with individuals or households.

Sermon Spice will only collect information where it is necessary for us to do so, and we will only collect information if it is relevant to our dealings with you.

Sermon Spice will inform users about its proposed use of any information submitted through this website at the point of collection. Purposes for collection of personal information could include provision of customer service, customer surveys, subscriptions, provision of offers of related services, and other exchanges of information at web sites. We will only use your personal information in a way that is fair (with reference to the purposes which are either disclosed to you or are relevant to the reason for your giving us the information). 

Sermon Spice uses a third-party shopping cart service to process all orders placed through our Sermon Spice website.  This service is provided by Ecart Soft.  All billing information will be processed by Ecart Soft and will be shared with Sermon Spice for the purpose of fulfilling your order.  You may view Ecart Soft' privacy policy here.

Sermon Spice will only keep your information for as long as we are either required to by law, or as is relevant for the purposes for which it was collected. 

Sermon Spice will not disclose or share personally identifiable information collected at its web sites with any other companies or organizations without the informed consent of the consumer providing the information (except as required by law or pursuant to a governmental request), unless this is necessary to provide the services or products which you requested when providing us with your information.

Sermon Spice will use information received in email from customers and other individuals only for purposes reasonably related to the subject matter of the communication.

Cookies
A cookie is a file placed on your personal computer's hard drive by a web site that allows it to monitor and/or improve your use of the site. Sermon Spice does not use cookies on our website.  However, cookies may be required when placing an order through the Sermon Salt Sales website.  Any such cookies will be set and maintained by Ecart Soft and are used only to track your order information.  You may view Ecart Soft's privacy policy here.
