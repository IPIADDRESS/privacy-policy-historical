Privacy Policy


Your privacy is critically important to us. This Statement of Privacy governs data collection and usage and applies to https://www.seenit.in. For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, unless otherwise noted, all references to ‘SeenIt’ include https://www.seenit.in and SeenIt. By using the SeenIt website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.


Website Visitors


Like most website operators, SeenIt collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. SeenIt’s purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how SeenIt’s visitors use its website. From time to time, SeenIt may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.


SeenIt also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users. SeenIt only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below.


Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information


Certain visitors to SeenIt’s websites choose to interact with SeenIt in ways that require SeenIt to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that SeenIt gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. In each case, SeenIt collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor’s interaction with SeenIt. SeenIt does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.


Aggregated Statistics


SeenIt may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. SeenIt may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, SeenIt does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.


Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information


SeenIt discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on SeenIt’s behalf or to provide services available at SeenIt’s websites, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Some of those employees, contractors and affiliated organizations may be located outside of your home country; by using SeenIt’s websites, you consent to the transfer of such information to them. SeenIt will not rent or sell potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information to anyone. Other than to its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations, as described above, SeenIt discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only in response to a subpoena, court order or other governmental request, or when SeenIt believes in good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the property or rights of SeenIt, third parties or the public at large. If you are a registered user of SeenIt and have supplied your email address, SeenIt may occasionally send you an email to tell you about new features, solicit your feedback, or just keep you up to date with what’s going on with SeenIt and our products. If you send us a request (for example via a support email or via one of our feedback mechanisms), we reserve the right to publish it in order to help us clarify or respond to your request or to help us support other users. SeenIt takes all measures reasonably necessary to protect against the unauthorized access, use, alteration or destruction of potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information.


Cookies


A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. SeenIt uses cookies to help SeenIt identify and track visitors, their usage of SeenIt website, and their website access preferences. SeenIt visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using SeenIt’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of SeenIt’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.


Business Transfers


If SeenIt, or substantially all of its assets, were acquired, or in the unlikely event that SeenIt goes out of business or enters bankruptcy, user information would be one of the assets that is transferred or acquired by a third party. You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer of SeenIt may continue to use your personal information as set forth in this policy.


Ads


Ads appearing on any of our websites may be delivered to users by advertising partners, who may set cookies. These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement to compile information about you or others who use your computer. This information allows ad networks to, among other things, deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by SeenIt and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.


Privacy Policy Changes


Although most changes are likely to be minor, SeenIt may change its Privacy Policy from time to time, and in SeenIt’s sole discretion. SeenIt encourages visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.
