What information does see-now.com collect and how is it used?
see-now.com may collect any or all of the following three types of information: (a) traffic or click-through data, (b) cookies, or (c) contact information and other personally identifying information. see-now.com's collection and use of each type of data is described below, as well as the ways (if any) in which the data may be shared with third parties.

(a) Traffic or Click-Through Data. Data about traffic and usage is not personally identifying or identifiable, and is not supplemented or combined with personally identifying or identifiable information. see-now.com may share information it collects with certain selected third parties, but only in aggregated, anonymous form. For example, we may tell an advertiser that a certain number of people visited a certain area on our web site. see-now.com collects some information regarding use of the Services by observing browser functions and the files (pages) users access. For example, see-now.com 's servers collect information about the site from which you entered to enable see-now.com to calculate and pay its affiliates, information about the most frequently searched terms to help its advertisers provide the most relevant search results, information about usage to check for fraudulent behavior. (b) Cookies. see-now.com collects information about online behavior using cookies. These cookies contain identification information that enables us to see how our users are interacting with our site and how frequently they are returning. see-now.com cookies do not contain any personally identifiable information. Cookies lets us know about repeat visitors, see-now.com may also use cookies to identify which sites our repeat visitors are coming from, so that we can place more of our online advertising on these sites, and can, where necessary, pay our affiliates and other business partners. You may set your browser software to reject cookies, but you may not be able to use all of the features of see-now.com. see-now.com may share information about usage of the Services with third parties, but only in aggregated, anonymous form. (c) Contact Information or Personally Identifying Information. see-now.com can obtain access to your name, e-mail address and other contact information only (1) when you become an affiliate with see-now.com, provide comments, submit a problem, or request information from see-now.com and provide your name or return contact information, or when you register for the advanced features of see-now.com.

Contact information provided in connection with problems, comments or requests is used only to respond to those problems, comments or requests. This information is not stored or used in connection with traffic or click-through data and is provided to a third party only as necessary to respond to the comment, problem or request. During the registration process, you may be asked to provide certain information, including contact information and other personally identifying information. see-now.com will use registration information to provide updates, and other business purposes related to making available the features and functions of see-now.com. see-now.com may use registration information to make users aware of new products or special offers or promotions. see-now.com will abide by requests from you (in accordance with the procedure below) to discontinue such use. see-now.com stores personally identifiable information obtained through registration securely (as described below) and considers it part of the assets of its business. see-now.com may provide certain registration information to third parties for use in marketing and in designing or providing their own services.
