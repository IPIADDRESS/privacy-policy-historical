Privacy Policy
Searchalot respects and protects the privacy of the individuals that use our Searchalot. Individually identifiable information about you is not disclosed to any third party as explained in this privacy policy ("Privacy Statement").

What Information Do We Collect?
Searchalot does not collect any unique information about you (such as your name, email address, etc.) except when you specifically and knowingly provide such information in the form of an email.

Links to Other Sites
The sites displayed as search results or linked to by Searchalot are developed by people over whom Searchalot exercises no control. These other sites may send their own cookies to users, collect data, or solicit personal information.

With Whom Does Searchalot Share Information?
Searchalot will not share information about you with any advertisers, business partners, sponsors, and other third parties.

Is Searchalot Adware, Spyware or Malware?
Absolutely not! Searchalot does not monitor, collect data or 'spy' on its user base. Any software program that is implying or reporting that Searchalot is 'adware', 'spyware' or 'malware' is incorrect. Searchalot is incapable of tracking your overall web use or deciphering anything on your hard drive. Searchalot does not display pop-ups or any type of advertising.

Some of the search engines listed in the Searchalot may pay a listing fee or may share revenue from advertising within the search results (sponsored links).
