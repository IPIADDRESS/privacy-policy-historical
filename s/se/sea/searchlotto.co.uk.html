Privacy Policy

Updated as of 04/01/2014

What Information Do We Collect

We receive any search query you submit through the Site.

Whenever you visit the website SearchLotto.co.uk, we collect the limited information that your browser makes available. This information includes: (a) your Internet Protocol (IP) address; (b) your browser and platform type (e.g., a Netscape browser on a Macintosh platform); (c) your browser language; and (d) the data in any undeleted cookies that your browser previously accepted from the website.

What Information is passed onto third parties?

SearchLotto.co.uk reserves the right to pass on various information collected from search results. This includes statistical data regarding websites visited - this data is not linked to user profiles.

Do My Personal Details get Passed On?

No. SearchLotto.co.uk will never sell or pass on any personal information which is held, other than statistical data relating to your search activity.

Special restrictions on the use of sensitive data

We will not normally seek data on your racial or ethnic origins, political opinions, religious or other beliefs, trade-union membership, physical or mental health, sex life or criminal convictions. When we do collect such data, it will be limited to data which are required to be kept by law, needed for legal proceedings or to establish, exercise or defend legal rights (including protection against fraud).

Our site contains links to other sites whose information practices may be different than ours. Visitors should consult the other sites' privacy notices as we have no control over information that is submitted to, or collected by, these third parties.

Cookies

When you visit our website we send you a cookie. A cookie is a text-only string of information that a website transfers to the cookie file of the browser on your computer's hard disk so that the website can remember who you are. A cookie will typically contain the name of the domain from which the cookie has come, the "lifetime" of the cookie, and a value, usually a randomly generated unique number.

SearchLotto.co.uk use cookies :

  • 1. To enable the personalisation features on our website (which allow you to login to your account and perform searches through our search engine).
  • 2. To compile anonymous, aggregated statistics that allow us to understand how users use our site and to help us improve the structure of our website. We cannot identify you personally in this way.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies by modifying the settings in your browser. However, you may not be able to use all the interactive features of our site if cookies are disabled.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please visit: http://networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp

Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorised access or disclosure we have put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
