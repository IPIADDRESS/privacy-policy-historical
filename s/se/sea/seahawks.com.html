At such points of collection, the user will have the opportunity to indicate whether he or she would like to "opt out" of receiving promotional and/or marketing information about other products, services and offerings from the Seattle Seahawks and/or any third parties. The Seattle Seahawks reserve the right to perform statistical analyses of user behavior and characteristics in order to measure interest in and use of the various areas ofthe site and to inform advertisers of such information as well as the number of users that have been exposed to or clicked on their advertising banners. Such activities are beyond the control of the Seattle Seahawks.

Users also should be aware that non-personal information and data may be automatically collected through the standard operation of the Seattle Seahawks' internet servers or through the use of "cookies." Cookies are small text files a web site can use to recognize repeat users, facilitate the user's ongoing access to and use of the site and allow a site to track usage behavior and compile aggregate data that will allow content improvements and targeted advertising. Cookies are not programs that come onto a user's system and damage files. Generally, cookies work by assigning a unique number to the user that has no meaning outside the assigning site. Users should be aware that the Seattle Seahawks cannot or does not control the use of cookies or the resulting information by advertisers or third parties hosting data for the Seattle Seahawks. If a user does not want information collected through the use of cookies, there is a simple procedure in most browsers that allows the user to deny or accept the cookie feature; however, users should note that cookies may be necessary to provide the user with certain features (e.g., customized delivery of information) available on the Seattle Seahawks Site.

Upon request, the Seattle Seahawks will allow any user to "opt out" of further promotional contacts at any time. Additionally upon request, the Seattle Seahawks will use reasonable efforts to allow users to update/correct personal information previously submitted which the user states is erroneous to the extent such activities will not compromise privacy or security interests. Also, upon a user's request, the Seattle Seahawks will use commercially reasonable efforts to functionally delete the user and his or her personal information from its database; however, it may be impossible to delete a user's entry without some residual information because of backups and records of deletions.
