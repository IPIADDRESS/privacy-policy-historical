Aksile Media Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. It is Aksile Media’s policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website, https://segmentnext.com, and other sites we own and operate.

Information We collect

Log Data

When you visit our website, we and our third party service providers (including any third-party content, advertising, and analytics providers) may automatically log the standard data provided by your web browser. This data is considered “non-identifying information”, as it does not personally identify you on its own. It may include your computer’s Internet Protocol (IP) address, your browser type and version, the pages you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on each page, your interaction with content or advertisements and other details.

We may also collect data about the device you are using to access our website. This data may include the device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, device settings, and geo-location data. What we collect can depend on the individual settings of your device and software. We recommend checking the policies of your device manufacturer or software provider to learn what information they make available to us.

Personal Data

You can visit the website without telling us who are you or giving us any information that can identify you personally. If, however, you want to register to become a member of the website to comment and use other community features, we may ask for personal Data, such as your:

  • Name
  • Email

This data is considered “identifying information”, as it can personally identify you. We only request personal data relevant to providing you with a service, use it to help provide or improve this service, to contact you with your consent, from time to time, including via email, about us, our products and services, or our partners.

In addition, you are required to submit certain personal data when you apply for a job. This data may include demographic information, information about your preferences and interests. You can choose not to submit any personal data but it will inhibit our ability to process your application.

How we collect information

We collect information by fair and lawful means, with your knowledge and consent. We also let you know why we’re collecting it and how it will be used. You are free to refuse our request for this information, with the understanding that we may be unable to provide you with some of your desired services without it.

Use of information

We may use a combination of identifying and non-identifying information to understand who our visitors are, how they use our services, and how we may improve their experience of our website in future. We do not disclose the specifics of this information publicly, but may share aggregated and anonymized versions of this information, for example, in website and customer usage trend reports.

We may use your personal details to contact you with updates about our website and services, along with promotional content that we believe may be of interest to you. If you wish to opt out of receiving promotional content, you can follow the “unsubscribe” instructions provided alongside any promotional correspondence from us.

We may further use your personal information to administer and process contests, sweepstakes, and promotions. To tailor content, recommendations and advertisements with the help of third parties both on the website and elsewhere online.

Or for purposes as described at the time you provide your information, with your consent, and as further explained in this Privacy Policy.

Data processing and storage

The information (Log Data and Personal Data) we collect is stored and processed where we (Pakistan) or our partners, affiliates and third-party providers (United States) maintain facilities. By using our Site, you consent to data transfer within jurisdictions subject to data protection laws that reflect our commitment to protecting the privacy of our users.

We only retain information for as long as necessary to provide a service, or to improve our services in future. While we retain this data, we will protect it within commercially acceptable means to prevent loss and theft, as well as unauthorised access, disclosure, copying, use or modification. That said, we advise that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and cannot guarantee absolute data security.

If you request your personal information be deleted, or where your personal information becomes no longer relevant to our operations, we will erase it from our system within a reasonable time-frame.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use “cookies” to collect information about you and your activity across our site. A cookie is a small piece of data that our website stores on your computer, and accesses each time you visit, so we can understand how you use our site. This helps us serve you content based on preferences you have specified. Please refer to our Cookie Policy for more information.

We use following types of cookies and tracking technologies for purposes set out below:

Analytics Tracking Cookies These cookies are used to collect information about traffic to our website and users use our website. Information gathered doesn’t identify any individual visitor. It is aggregated information, all the data is anonymized. It includes number of visitors to the website, page(s) they visited, time spent on that page, next page they visited, when they visited the page(s), whether they have visited the website before, and other similar information. Analytics tracking gives us insight into user activity which is essential to the growth of the website.

We use Google Analytics for this purpose. Google Analytics uses its own cookies. You can find more about Google Analytics cookies on: https://developers.google.com/analytics/resources/concepts/gaConceptsCookies

Interested in learning how Google protects your data?  Visit the designated Google page on the subject: www.google.com/analytics/learn/privacy.html

If you want to prevent us from tracking you by any means, you can download this browser plugin by Google – http://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
Essential and Functionality Cookies Essential cookies are crucial to your experience of a website, enabling core features like user logins, account management, shopping carts and payment processing. We use essential cookies to enable certain functions on our website.

Functional cookies allow us to remember your choices when you browse our website, for example, remembering your login details and remembering which intractable element on the website you have already interacted with. Functionality cookies are essential to provide you with more personal experience or to avoid the frustration of having to re-enter your preferences and choices every time you visit the website.
Social Media Cookies Every time you interact with our content on or off social media website like Facebook, Twitter, Google+, Reddit, your action is recorded by these social media websites.
Targeting / Advertising Cookies These cookies track your browsing habits to enable us to show you advertisements that you are more likely to interact with. These cookies use information about your browsing history to group you with users who have similar interests. Based on that information, and with our permission, third-party advertisers can place cookies to enable them to show advertisements relevant to your interests while you are on third-party websites.

These cookies store your location, IP and region ID, which helps us show you locale-specific news and allows our services to operate more efficiently. However, you can disable these cookies by visiting; http://www.youronlinechoices.com/uk/your-ad-choices

You can disable this form of advertisement by removing targeted or advertising cookies. However, you may not see advertisements that are relevant to your interests but you will see advertisements nonetheless.

Provided that you do choose to remove cookies by the companies listed at the above link, note that it doesn’t list all companies who are engaged in behavioral advertising, so you may still receive targeted ads relevant to your interests from companies that are not listed.

Flash Cookie
A Flash cookie is a data file placed on a device via the Adobe Flash plug-in that is built into or downloaded by you onto your device. Flash cookies are used for various purposes, including, without limitation, enabling a Flash feature and remembering your preferences. For more information about Flash and the privacy choices Adobe offers, visit https://www.macromedia.com/support/documentation/en/flashplayer/help/settings_manager.html.

If you choose to adjust your Flash privacy settings on your device some features of the Services may not function properly.

Pixel Tags
We also use pixel tags, small graphics files that allow us and third parties to monitor the use of the website and collect user activity information. A pixel tag installed on a page can collect information such as the IP address of the computer that downloaded the page, URL of the page, the time (and length of time) the page containing the pixel tag was viewed; the type of browser that retrieved the pixel tag; and the identification number of any cookie previously placed by that server on your computer.

We use pixel tags, either provided by us or by our third-party advertisers, service providers and ad networks, to collect information about your visit, including the pages you view, the links you click and other actions taken in connection with our website and Services and use them in combination with our cookies to provide offers and information of interest to you. Pixel tags also enable ad networks to serve targeted advertisements to you when you visit the Services or other websites.

For more information on use of cookies and similar technologies on our Site, you can read our Cookie Policy. To read more on cookies in general, what cookies are set on your computer or mobile device and how to delete them, you can visit http://www.allaboutcookies.org/ and http://www.youronlinechoices.com/uk.
