What are cookies?

Cookies are very small text files that are stored on your computer when you visit certain web pages. Here at snowandrock.com we use cookies to keep track of what you've placed in your shopping bag and to remember you when you return to the site.

Our cookies don't contain any sensitive information, such as your name, address or payment details.

To place orders on snowandrock.com, you will need to have cookies enabled. If you don't wish to have cookies enabled, you'll still be able to browse the site, but you won't be able to place an order.

Our site also uses some third party tools, for areas such as site search, our affiliate marketing programme, recommendations and review services. These tools are provided by our trusted business partners, who may also place cookies on your computer. For more details, please see the section entitled 3rd party cookies, found here.

We are providing this information to ensure that you are fully aware of the cookies that we employ, in order to comply with recent changes in EU legislation. We aim to be open, honest and transparent every step of the way.

Our cookies policy

We use cookies to deliver the best shopping experience we can, continuously aiming to develop and improve our site.

None of the cookies used by either ourselves or our trusted 3rd parties store sensitive information about you.

Please find below the list of cookies that we use here on snowandrock.com.

Cookie Name Further Details
_utma, _utmb, _utmc, _utmz These are google analytics cookies which enable us to view and analyse visitor information and this in turn helps us to improve our website to make your shopping experience the best it can be. Find more information about google's privacy policy here: www.google.com/analytics/learn/privacy.html
Fcp_last_category, LastCategory, LastSuccessfulCategory, These cookies record the categories you've browsed whilst on the website, so that we can provide easy back links to previously viewed categories.
fcp_shopping_basket Retains your shopping basket contents between visits to the website.
Language_iso This cookie is used to remember your language preference.
Country_iso This cookie is used to remember your country preference so that the website displays correct country information and delivery pricing.
Currency_iso This cookie is used to remember your currency preference.
view_format This is a cookie that stores the number of columns of products shown on the product listing pages.
nextPreviousIds Product IDs of the previous and next products
JSESSIONID, K-JSESSIONID-gdncaooa Session cookies that only last for the time you are on our web site. These are used to make the site work and allow you to move from one part of the web site to another and to add items to the shopping basket without having to login repeatedly.
X-Mapping-gbooldlg The Snow+Rock website utilises more than one server. This cookie is used to send your website request to the same server where possible, in order to keep the website running quickly and efficiently.
FCP_MOBILE_LAST_CATEGORY, FCP_MOBILE_LAST_SEARCH,FCP_MOBILE_PRODUCT_IDS These are used within our dedicated mobile website to remember the categories & products visited.

If you wish to block, restrict or remove cookies from snowandrock.com, you can use your browser to do so - see the section entitled managing cookies for further information on how to do this.

Alternatively, you may wish to visit www.aboutcookies.org which contains comprehensive information on how to do this on a wide variety of browsers.

Managing cookies

The Snow+Rock group will not use cookies to collect personally identifiable information about you. However, if you wish to restrict or block the cookies which are set by snowandrock.com, you can do this through your browser settings. The Help function within your browser should tell you how:

For PCs: click on 'Help' at the top of your browser window and select the 'About' option

For Macs: with the browser window open, click on the browser menu and select the 'Preferences' option

Alternatively, you may wish to visit www.aboutcookies.org which contains comprehensive information on how to do this on a wide variety of browsers. You will also find details on how to delete cookies from your computer as well as more general information about cookies. For information on how to do this on the browser of your mobile phone you will need to refer to your handset manual.

Please be aware that restricting cookies may impact on the functionality of the Snow+Rock website.

We may contact you for any of the above purposes whether by telephone, email or in writing.

When you create a shopping account you will be given the option to receive information from us by post or e-mail, about products, promotions or special offers which we feel may be of interest to you.
