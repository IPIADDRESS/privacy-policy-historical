Privacy Policy

Snowy's Outdoors is committed to the privacy of information received through the business.

Snowy's Outdoors complies with the National Privacy Principles Act 2000 which ensures that your privacy and the information that you provide to us is safeguarded at all times.

Snowy's Club Members join at their own request and the information provided therein is used only by Snowy's Outdoors and Snowy's Wholesale. Such information may be used for communication and advertising purposes and may at times be sighted by a third party for purposes such as printing, direct or electronic mailing.

A Snowy's Club Member may discontinue their membership at any time by contacting Snowy's Outdoors at 92 Richmond Road, Keswick SA 5035 or by phoning (08) 83512111, Fax (08) 83512122, or by contacting us online. All customer communication is issued with options to discontinue membership and if so requested, all relevant customer details will be permanently removed and any further communication will cease.

For further information about the way Snowy's Outdoors manages the personal and sensitive information we hold please contact us at 92 Richmond Road, Keswick SA 5035 or by phoning (08) 83512111, Fax (08) 83512122, or by contacting us online.
