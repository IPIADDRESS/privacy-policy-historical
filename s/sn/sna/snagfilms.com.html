Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting SnagFilms. Your privacy is important to us and we know that you care about how we protect your information. This policy covers information SnagFilms collects when you use our service, as well the nature of our relationships with certain third parties that provide services in connection with SnagFilms. We invite you to review this policy explaining how your information is collected, used and shared by SnagFilms.

SnagFilms provides an online video service offering a selection of movies, clips and other content (collectively, “Content”) available on the SnagFilms.com website (the “SnagFilms Site” or “Site”), SnagFilms’s affiliate and distribution partner websites (“Distribution Sites”), and other websites where users may embed the Content (these websites, together with the SnagFilms Site, Distribution Sites, and distribution platforms are referred to collectively as the “Sites”). Our video service, including the Content, video player, and any other features, tools, applications, materials or other services offered from time to time by SnagFilms in connection with its business on any of the Sites are referred to collectively as the “SnagFilms Services.”

Some Distribution Sites or distribution platforms may be co-branded with our name or logo, even though they are not operated or maintained by us. SnagFilms is not responsible for the privacy practices of the websites or platforms operated by third parties.  If you are using the SnagFilms Services on another website or platform, you should check the privacy policy of the other website or platform to determine how it handles any information it collects from you.

By visiting the SnagFilms Site (whether or not you are a registered user) or using any of the SnagFilms Services on any of the Sites, you are accepting this Privacy Policy. If you do not want information about you to be used in the manner set forth in this Privacy Policy, please do not use the SnagFilms Services.

I.  INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Through our Services, users can, among other things, and use all our services, view streaming videos, snag embeddable players, post comments, create and share collections of films (“Collections”), and order products via third party products and services.

We may collect certain non-personally identifiable information when you visit many of our web pages, such as usage behavior, number of pages visited, the number and type of videos streamed, the number of players snagged, the type of browser you are using (e.g., Firefox, Internet Explorer, Chrome), the type of operating system you are using (e.g., Microsoft Windows or Mac OS), and the domain name of your Internet service provider (e.g., AOL, Earthlink).

We may also collect personally identifiable information, including the following:

•    information you provide to us, including your name, email address, location, birth date, gender, likes, favorite movies, interests, and friends on third party sites;

•    information automatically collected when you use our Services, such as your location and information collected via cookies, web beacons, or embedded data collection technologies;

•    information from other commercially available sources, such as data aggregators, public databases, or our business partners, which may include your interests, purchasing behavior and your online activities, such as websites visited and advertisements viewed.

To enhance your experience, many of our web pages use “cookies.” Cookies are text files we place in your computer’s browser to store your preferences. Cookies, by themselves, do not tell us your e-mail address or other personally identifiable information unless you choose to provide this information to us by, for example, registering at the SnagFilms Site or logging in through one of our third-party partner sites. However, once you choose to furnish the site with personally identifiable information, this information may be linked to the data stored in the cookie. We use cookies to understand site usage and to improve the content and offerings on our Sites. For example, we may use cookies to personalize your experience at our web pages (e.g., to recognize you by name when you return to our Site) or save your password in password-protected areas.

“Web beacons” or clear .gifs are small pieces of code placed on a web page to monitor the behavior and collect data about the visitors viewing a web page. For example, web beacons can be used to count the users who visit a web page or to deliver a cookie to the browser of a visitor viewing that page. We may and other third parties may use web beacons on this Site from time to time for this and other purposes.

We may combine all of the information we collect about you and use it individually or collectively in the manner described in this Privacy Policy.

Some of our pages contain links to other sites whose information practices may be different than ours. Visitors should consult the other sites’ privacy notices. Please be aware that SnagFilms does not warn you when you have chosen to link through to another website from the SnagFilms Site.

We may use the information we collect in order to establish and enhance our relationship with you and to improve the user experience we provide.

SnagFilms’s use of this information includes the following general purposes: customizing the Content and advertising you view; responding to your inquiries about our offerings; fulfilling your requests for products, programs or services; offering products, programs or services we believe may be of interest to you; identifying the comments and User Content you post when you use the SnagFilms Services; improving our Services; conducting research; compiling aggregate data for internal and external business purposes; protecting our rights; and other uses in accordance with our Terms of Use; and for any other purpose disclosed to you at the time we collect your information.

We sometimes use the non-personally identifiable information that we collect to improve the design and content of our Site and to enable us to personalize your Internet experience. We also may use this information in the aggregate to analyze site usage, as well as to offer you products, programs, or services.

Anonymous data is information (whether it was originally personally identifiable or non-personally identifiable) that subsequently is used in aggregate form. We may use anonymous data for purposes of research or analysis. We may conduct research on our customer demographics, internet usage, and interests and behavior based on personal data we gather. Although anonymous data may be based in part on personal data, it does not identify you personally. SnagFilms may share this type of anonymous data with a variety of third parties, including its affiliates, advertisers and other current and prospective business partners..

We may also use anonymous data collected for website administration, advertising and promotional purposes, and we may share such information with various affiliated and unaffiliated entities for such purposes. IP addresses are also used to provide anonymous aggregated data to advertisers about the volume of use on the SnagFilms Services, and non-personally identifiable information about your activities may be used to customize advertising messages. Anonymous usage and demographic information may also be shared with our advertisers and business partners.

We sometimes use your information to communicate with you, such as to notify you when we make changes to the SnagFilms Site or to fulfill a request by you to receive our weekly newsletter(s).  We may also use your personal information to provide you with promotional information about SnagFilms’s features, services, and other offerings that may be of interest to you, or to provide information or offers to you that are related to the SnagFilms Services on behalf of our business partners.

Remember that if you make Profile content private, others who already subscribe to or otherwise have the details of such content may still have access to it. When you close your account, we'll deactivate it.  However, any film Collections you created will remain visible on SnagFilms and you will remain listed as a follower of any Collections you followed.
