I. Collected Information

Registration: Registration is required to purchase the SnapApp service or to participate in some SnapApp quizzes, or in any other promotion or contests run by SnapApp. You will be asked for PII as part of the registration process. SnapApp requires that a user be thirteen (13) years of age or older. PII collected at registration may include email address, telephone number, gender, birth date, age, street address, home town, country and postal code.

Additional PII: Additional PII may be collected through use of the site including responses to questions , taking quizzes, surveys or polls, or through various offers provided on site. SnapApp may collect and retain PII relating to email marketing sent to, and opened by, users on our marketing list(s). SnapApp may additionally run promotions that may ask for additional demographic data.

Other Information: In addition to PII, additional information may be collected, such as system information, browser type (e.g., Netscape, Internet Explorer), IP address, the type of operating system you are using (e.g., Windows XP or Mac OS), what version of Adobe Flash you are using (e.g., Flash 9.0), CPU type (e.g. Pentium), your manner of connecting to the Internet (e.g., connection speed through narrowband or broadband access), information about your geographic location, and the domain name of your Internet service provider. We also track some of the pages that you visit on our Website through the use of pixel tags (also called clear gifs). This tracks usage information about users who have received targeted promotional campaigns. In addition, SnapApp uses third-party service provider partners to employ anonymous cookies for accounting and data integrity purposes.

Other sources: We may receive information about you from other sources, including publicly available information or information available from third party sources, such as updated delivery and address information from our carriers or third parties, and add it to our account information, generally for updating purposes. We may also obtain information about you through optional co-registration and/or advertisements on sites operated by third parties.

Cookies: Cookies are pieces of data stored in the browser's memory or written to the user's hard drive that provide the ability to recall information across user sessions. This also enables tracking of various user actions and helps customize the display of site content and personalized advertisements. Most web browsers are automatically set to accept cookies. You do not have to accept cookies to use SnapApp, but SnapApp may not work correctly if cookies are disabled, and some functionality may be completely unavailable. You may reset your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie or to reject cookies generally. The Help menu on the menu bar of most browsers will tell you how to prevent your browser from accepting new cookies, how to have the browser notify you when you receive a new cookie, and how to disable cookies altogether.

You can request that we stop storing cookies on your computer, and that we don't set any more. Please be aware that if you decline the use cookies set by SnapApp, you will be unable to use the site.

Users under 13 years of age are not allowed to participate in quizzes or surveys offered by SnapApp. SnapApp is not responsible for information obtained from underage users who have provided false information regarding their age. However, in these instances parents may visit the SnapApp Unsubscribe page to unsubscribe the child's email, and have his/her information removed. SnapApp is not responsible for any offers presented to underage users who have provided false information regarding their age. Parents must contact offer providers directly. No information should be submitted to, or posted at, any SnapApp Web sites by users under 13 years of age. We encourage parents and guardians to spend time online with their children and to participate and monitor the interactive activities of their children.
