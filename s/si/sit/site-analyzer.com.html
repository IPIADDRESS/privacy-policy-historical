At Site Analyzer, we are keenly aware of the trust you place in us and our responsibility to protect your privacy. We understand that as a customer, the privacy and security of confidential information are important.
The purpose of our Privacy Policy is to outline our practice in this area. By using the Site Analyzer websites and tools, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

 

Security of Information

Site Analyzer servers are located in France. Any information that you provide to Site Analyzer will be transferred to France. By providing personal information to us, you are therefore consenting to its storage and use as outlined. Site Analyzerwill take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, unauthorized alteration, destruction or other malfeasance.

When you place an order with us online, we collect information about you, including your name and address. This information is necessary in order for us to process and complete your order.

Sensitive informations like credit card numbers are transmitted to PCI-DSS Level1 servers. The transission is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol.

Collection of Personal Information

Site Analyzer collects general usage information from our website's visitors. This is done with the help of cookies. Cookies are small files which a website transmits to your hard drive when you visit that site. Cookies cannot read information from your hard drive nor can it read information from other cookies if the setting has been disabled. However, you will still be able to access and browse the Site Analyzer website even if the cookies have been disabled.

 

Use of Personal Information

Site Analyzer does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. Site Analyzer may use personal information that has been collected to:

  • Determine your eligibility to receive support at our discretion.
  • Survey you (and others) in an attempt to improve our website, products, or business.
  • Improve the quality of our services
  • Help us analyze traffic patterns throughout our website.
