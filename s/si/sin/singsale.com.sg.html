Privacy Policy

Your personal information is treated with the greatest of care. Sensitive payment information such as credit card details are NEVER stored on the Internet with our system.

We never sell or rent your personal information. SINGSALE may release personal information under the following circumstances:

Passwords

You are responsible for all actions taken with your user name, email address and password. Therefore we do not recommend that you disclose your SingSale password to any third parties. If you choose to share your user name, email address and password or your information with third parties to provide you additional services, you are responsible for all actions taken with this information and therefore you should review that third party's privacy policy.

Security

Transactions are processed via an industry standard secure https connection using IP Solutions International as a third party payment gateway. This means that you can be assured that your sensitive data such as credit card numbers will remain confidential because all information is 128bit encrypted. The server we connect to is authenticated through PKI certificates issued by a root Certificate Authority. Our payment page is also fully PCI DSS compliant please visit https://www.pcisecuritystandards.org/tech/ for more information.

Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of information that are stored in a browser-related file on your computer's hard drive when you use our Web site. We use cookies to improve the member experience. We also use them to deliver information and fresh content specific to your interests. Cookies are widely used on most major Web sites.

SingSale mostly uses "session" cookies, which are removed from your computer once you leave SingSale. These are used so our service can remember that you have logged in already and prevent you doing so more frequently. The technology we use (Microsoft .NET), requires the use of session cookies. Session cookies expire and are deleted after 30 minutes of no activity on SingSale.

SingSale also uses persistent cookies to personalise the user experience, to help protect our members and to provide us with research information (such as how often members visit SingSale each month). Persistent cookies allow us to track users on SingSale and help us identify users abusing the SingSale service.

Membership Removal

If you wish to be removed from our database for any reason, simply send us an email requesting to be removed via our contact us page.

Updates to our information practices

SingSale reserves the right to add to, delete or change these terms because of frequent changes in Internet technology and applicable law. As such, you should check this privacy policy from time to time for such changes.
