1. Collection and processing of your data

1.1 You can, as a basic principle, visit our website without giving any details as to your person (Personally Identifiable Information (PII)).

1.2 A pre-requisite of the use of our APPLICATIONS or SERVICES which are offered online, in particular the “Signavio Process Editor SaaS”, “Signavio Business Decision Manager SaaS” and/or “Signavio Workflow SaaS”, is registration, which requires the following details to be entered: surname, first name, complete address, e-mail address, designation of the company. Details above and beyond these are non-obligatory details and are marked as such.

1.3 In the case of payments by means of credit cards, the Customer’s credit card data will be requested only over the telephone, and only stored for recurring purchases in encoded form with the credit card processing company selected by Signavio.

1.4 This website uses Google Analytics, a web analysis service of Google Inc. (“Google”). Google Analytics uses so-called “cookies”, text files which are stored on your computer and which enable you to carry out an analysis of the use of the website. The information obtained through the cookie concerning your use of this website will be typically transmitted to a server of Google in the USA and stored there. Signavio activated the option for IP address anonymization provided by Google. In consequence your IP address will be shortened by the last 8 bits within countries, which are member of the European Union or in other contracting countries of the European Economic Area (EEA). Only in exceptional cases, the full IP address will be transmitted to a server of Google in the USA and shortened there. On behalf of the website operator, Google will use this information in order to evaluate your use ofthe website so as to compile reports concerning the website activities and in order to render further services associated with the website use and the Internet use for the website operator. The IP address transmitted by your browser for the use of Google Analytics will not be brought into connection with other data of Google. You may prevent the storage of the cookies through a corresponding setting of your browser software; nevertheless we draw your attention to the fact that in this case you may possibly not be able to use all functions of this website to the full extent. Additionally, you can prevent the capturing of your data on using the website for Google stored in the cookie (incl. your IP address) by downloading and installing the Browser plugin behind the following link: http://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

1.5 For system-related purposes, when the APPLICATIONS or SERVICES are used, Signavio furthermore stores the following data by means of session-cookies: session ID, name of the user, e-mail address. The cookies are set to automatically expire at the latest after 3 months.

1.6 For Signavio’s marketing and optimization purposes solely, products and services provided by WiredMinds AG (www.wiredminds.de) are used. The data collected by WiredMinds is collected, processed and stored and anonymous user profiles are created. User profiles are anonymized entirely whenever possible. Here, cookies could be applied. Cookies are small text files that are saved in the Internet browser of the website visitor. They are used for recognizing the specific Internet browser. The collected data, which could also contain person-related data, will be transferred to WiredMinds or collected directly by WiredMinds. WiredMinds is allowed to use information that is left during a website visit to create anonymous user profiles. The resulting data is not used without a separate agreement of the person concerned and the visits of the website are not identified and consolidated with person-related data without agreement of those people. If IP-addresses are collected, they are anonymized immediately by deleting the last number block. It is possible to disagree to future data collection, processing and storing at any time. If you want to opt out of collection of your visit data, please click the following link or type in your browser address bar: Exclude from Website-Tracking (http://wm.wiredminds.de/track/cookie_mgr.php?mode=dont_track_ask&websitesel=1ff1189f95933423&lang=en)

2. Use and passing on of your data

2.1 We may use the data provided by you to answer your questions, only to the extent required to perform the obligations of the contract and for purposes of the technical administration of our products and services. For this purpose, your data is, in so far as necessary, passed on to other companies, e.g. to service providers partners who levy a charge and to the use of whom you have previously consented. These companies may only use your data for the performance of the contract and not for any further purposes. For the constant improvement of our APPLICATIONS and SERVICES we use software from TracksJS, LLC (http://www.trackjs.com) to analyze errors. In the course of this, actions of a user while operating the APPLICATION are being gathered. However no IP-addresses or other PII are being stored.

2.2 Furthermore, address, contact and order data are collected and processed for our own marketing purposes, also by external IT service providers on our behalf. Signavio creates pseudonymous user profiles for the sole purpose of continuous improvement of our SERVICEoffering “Signavio Process Editor SaaS”, “Signavio Business Decision Manager SaaS” and/or “Signavio Workflow SaaS”.

2.3 If you register for our newsletter we will, only with your expressed consent, use the data provided by you in order to send you our newsletter. When sending the newsletters, we use your e-mail address. The newsletter contains current information about as well as offers of Signavio GmbH. The newsletter is sent out regularly, but not more often than fortnightly. Unsubscription is possible at all times.

2.4 Signavio may integrate “Microsoft Bing Translate” on our Websites or as part of our APPLICATIONS or SERVICES. As part of our APPLICATIONS or SERVICES, this integration is an option requiring a previous explicit activation by the Customer (Automatic Translation functionality). The Customer is in full control if he wants to activate such functionality. On the other hand, if such functionality stays deactivated, “Microsoft Bing Translate” will not become part of the SERVICE offering and no data is being transferred to the Microsoft Corporation. If the Automatic Translation functionality is being used by the Customer, the corresponding data is transferred to and processed by the servers of the Microsoft Corporation, One Microsoft Way, Redmond, WA 98052-6399, USA. The Customer must give their consent for Signavio to do so. The usage of the service is defined in the general usage terms and data privacy terms of the Microsoft Corporation.

3. Right of objection

Of course, you may at any time object or revoke your consent to the use of your data for marketing purposes and the creation of pseudonymous user profiles for the purpose of continuous improvement of Signavio’s SERVICE offering “Signavio Process Editor SaaS”, “Signavio Business Decision Manager SaaS” and/or “Signavio Workflow SaaS”. This does not, however, apply for the data necessary for the fulfilment of the contractual relationship. In this respect, please contact dataprotection@signavio.com.

4. Data security

4.1 Your personal data will be transmitted using SSL encryption (AES, 256 bit certificate) via Internet when you use our APPLICATIONS and SERVICES which are subject to charge. You can recognize the secure transmission from the protocol designation “https://” in the URL line and also from the complete domain “editor.signavio.com”, “workflow.signavio.com“ or under any sub-domains “*.editor.signavio.com” or “*.workflow.signavio.com” (where “*” indicates an arbitrary string).

4.2 We use commercially reasonable efforts to secure our website and other systems through technical and organizational measures against loss, destruction, access, modification or dissemination of your data by unauthorized persons. For additional information on Signavio’s systems and network security, we refer to our information sheet concerning IT and data security, which you can request from us.

4.3 The access to your User account is only possible after entering your personal password. You should at all times treat your access information confidentially and close the browser window when you have ended your communication with us, in particular if you use the computer jointly with other persons.

5. Information, correction, blocking, deletion

Under the Bundesdatenschutzgesetz [Federal Data Protection Act] you have the right to receive information free of charge concerning your stored data and also, where appropriate, a right to correction, blocking or deletion of this data.

In case of any questions concerning the collection, processing or use of your personal data, or concerning information about the data, the correction, blocking or deletion of the same, and also in case of revocation of consent which has previously been given, please contact dataprotection@signavio.com.
