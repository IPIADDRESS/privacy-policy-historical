Cookies

Cookies are small text files containing a user's settings and other data used by websites, and are stored on the user's computer. They are transmitted as parcels of text sent by a server to a Web client (usually a browser) and then sent back unchanged by client each time it accesses that server.

Cookies are required to use the Sight Reading Factory® service. We use cookies to record current session information, but we never use permanent cookies.
