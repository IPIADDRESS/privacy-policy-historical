Privacy Policy

(The Site and private label service, are referred to collectively as the “Business” in this Statement.) Signal takes its obligations regarding privacy very seriously, and it wants to ensure Customers are fully informed about the Information they are providing. “Personally identifiable Information” refers to information that can identify an individual such as a name, or email address.

Signal is a licensee of the TRUSTe Privacy Program. TRUSTe is an independent organization, whose mission is to build users’ trust and confidence in the internet by promoting the use of fair information practices. Signal has selected to disclose its information practices and have its privacy practices reviewed for compliance by TRUSTe, as a sign of our dedication to your privacy. Please address your questions about this Site, or Privacy Statement to support@signalhq.com .If you do not receive acknowledgment of your inquiry within a reasonable time frame, or your inquiry has not been addressed to your satisfaction, you should contact TRUSTe at http://www.truste.org/consumers/watchdog_complaint.php. TRUSTe shall then serve as a liaison with us to resolve your concerns.

Collection of Information

We collect personal information on the Site to facilitate the services offered to you during your use of the business. Customers must register an account on the Site prior to having access to any of the services. To register an account on the Site, we require name, email and physical address information, as well as sensitive financial information including credit card numbers to process the registration. Where applicable, we also collect third party personal information if the registered administrator is different from the initiator of the registration for the service. By registering for the service, the platform exists for Customers to enter third party personal information as described above into their account user list. None of this personal information is used for any promotional purposes, or shared with third parties for their promotional purposes. Signal may also collect non-personally identifiable information from Customers via “cookies” (small text files placed by Signal on user computers), single-pixel GIF image files (also called “Web beacons”), Web server log analysis and other similar technological means. We also receive website navigational information such as IP address and referring URL when our Site is visited. Such information may be used to track site trends and enhance the user experience, and may be shared with third parties in aggregated, non-personally identifiable form. To the extent third parties may place advertising on the Site, such third parties may utilize cookies, or other technological means within the advertising to collect and utilize non-personally identifiable information. Signal is not responsible for information collected by third parties in this manner, nor for the collection or use of Information by other sites to which the Site is linked.

No Collection of Information from Children

This Site is not intended for users under the age of 18, nor does Signal knowingly collect, or retain Information from children under the age of 13.

Use of Information

Signal uses Information collected in connection with the Site in the following ways:

  1. To provide requested information, products and services to users via the Site.
  2. To improve the user experience with the Sites
  3. In connection with the operation of the Sites
  4. For users to obtain information and offers for products and services offered by Signal.

Security

Signal utilizes industry accepted electronic and physical standards of security to reduce the risk of improper access to or manipulation of Information during transmission and storage. We employ the use of SSL (Secure Socket Layer) technology to protect your sensitive information during transmission. While we take every precaution to guarantee safety, no method of security is 100% effective and we encourage you to employ discretion while submitting your information.

Moreover, in the event of a merger, sale, or acquisition of some, or all of Signal’s assets, your personal information could be part of the transferred assets. In such an event, we shall notify you by a notice on the home page. Any changes to the information practices governing your information & your choices would be noted. This third party is not authorized to utilize this information for any purposes beyond facilitating this service on our behalf. We post customer testimonials on our Site that may include a user’s name. We obtain permission of our Customers’ prior to posting their testimonials on our Site. Opting Out from Offers from Signal

You will be added to Signal email newsletter list only if you register to receive newsletters via a form on the Signal website. You may opt-out of receiving our newsletters by clicking on the opt-out link included in every email newsletter we send, or by contacting privacy@signalhq.com. Signal service offerings include acting as a service provider for Customers in providing an “opt-out” platform for their end users. At any time, end users may discontinue receiving SMS offers by sending the SMS message “STOP ALL” to the applicable shortcode. Signal or its Customers shall provide end users with a subsequent SMS message confirming the discontinuance. Additionally, end users may discontinue receiving offers from a particular Customer by sending the SMS message STOP to that Customer’s particular shortcode, or by following other instructions provided by that Customer. Opting out from a particular Customer’s offers will not apply to any communications from other Customers. All information provided to the Customers is owned by said Customer and subject to its privacy statement. We are not responsible for their privacy practices during our role as the Customers’ service provider.

Access to Information

All Customers who provide their Information to Signal may modify and correct such Information by e-mailing us at support@signalhq.com and registered Customers can modify their personal information and accounts by logging into their accounts on our home page and modifying their preferences.

Beginning on January 1, 2005, California Civil Code Section 1798.83 permits customers of Signal who are California residents to request certain information regarding Signal's disclosure of personal information to third parties for such third parties’ direct marketing purposes. To make such a request, please write to: support@signalhq.com.

Signal reserves the right to revise and update this Privacy Policy at any time, and will reasonably notify you of such changes via system notification upon login. Any such revisions will be effective on the date of posting to the Site, and will apply to all information collected by Signal both prior to, and following the effective date. Your use of the Site following any such revisions will be deemed your acceptance of such revisions. Users should periodically visit this page to review the current policies with regard to Information.
