Privacy Statement

Silvaco respects your privacy while you are visiting www.silvaco.com and recognizes your need to protect your personal information. On those occasions when we request certain personal information from you, such as your name, mailing address, job title or e-mail address, you may choose whether to provide it. In some cases, without such personal information we won't be able to provide certain information or services. Silvaco asks that you not provide us with any personal information if you are younger than 18 years of age.

We will use your personal information only within Silvaco. Your personal information may be shared with Silvaco business locations throughout the world, but we will not share it with anyone outside the company. We may also track the Internet addresses of our visitors and analyze this data for trends and statistics. Such information alone will not tell us who you are.

A "cookie" is a marker or an identifier that is entered into the memory of a visitor’s browser and is saved to a file for future reference. Silvaco uses cookies on selected areas of our Website as a tool to "remember" information about our visitors so that they do not have to repeatedly enter their personal information onto numerous forms on the site. Cookies help us tailor our Website to the visitor’s individual interests. You can set your browser to notify you when you are sent a cookie so that you can decide whether or not to accept it. Even without a cookie, you can access most information on our web site.

Silvaco is committed to privacy and will update our policy from time to time to keep it current. If you have any questions or comments about Silvaco privacy practices, please contact Silvaco via email at webmaster@silvaco.com.
