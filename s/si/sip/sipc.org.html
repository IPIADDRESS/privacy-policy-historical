Privacy Policy

The Securities Investor Protection Corporation (SIPC) is sensitive to concerns about privacy. Information collected from your visit will be limited and used solely for the purpose of helping us to assess the areas of our site that are most useful to visitors. (The sole exception to this rule is the SIPC Liquidation Help Center, where information is collected on a strictly confidential basis solely for form generation purposes.) Such information will not be used, released or sold for any other purposes. Questions, comments or suggestions about this policy should be directed to the Webmaster.
