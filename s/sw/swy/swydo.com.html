COOKIES

  • A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive.
  • Cookies are required to use the Swydo service.
  • We use cookies to record current session information, but do not use permanent cookies. You are required to log-in to your Swydo Project Site after a certain period of time has elapsed to protect you against others accidentally accessing your account contents.
