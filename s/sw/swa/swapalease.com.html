Dear Valued Swapalease Customer,

It has been brought to our attention that several individuals posing as legitimate lease buyers have been contacting consumers selling vehicles via online listing services, including Swapalease.com, on a fraudulent basis. As it applies to lease transfers, they are essentially writing their own lease transfer contracts, as opposed to the proper way of transferring a lease under the supervision of the leasing company. In fact, this method of transferring one's lease ('subleasing') is often a breach of contract and may be illegal.

Thankfully, this practice is easy to spot because the only way to properly transfer a lease is through the leasing company with no exceptions. Anyone who tells you they have another way to officially transfer your lease outside of or without the knowledge of the leasing company should not be dealt with. Note that even though the Swapalease.com New Lease and New Loan Programs process differs from a typical lease transfer, the transaction is handled in conjunction with the lease company. Important: Please contact Swapalease.com when you are ready to move forward with your lease transfer. Not only will this ensure we are there to guide you through the proper lease transfer process and to answer any questions you may have, but also to further maintain a safe marketplace for all by preventing subleasing transactions from occurring. Remember that we are here to help! Please feel free to contact us with any questions at 1-866-SWAP-NOW (1-866-792-7669) or via email at support@swapalease.com.
