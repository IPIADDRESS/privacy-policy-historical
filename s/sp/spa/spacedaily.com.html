Data Collection and Opt Out Procedure
Space.TV Corp or its advertising agents may use "cookies" to enhance your experience on the web. Space.TV Corp cannot use these cookies to personally identify you in any way.

What are cookies and why do we use them?
A cookie is a unique number that is provided to you the first time a Space.TV Corp advertising agent utilising this technology serves you an ad. For example, one of our network advertising agencies Engage issues a unique number from its banner engine, which is stored in a cookie file on your computer. This cookie can help Engage target ads to you, reducing multiple ad views. While we believe cookies enhance your Internet experience by limiting the number of times you see the same ad and delivering more relevant content to you, they are not required for Space.TV Corp to deliver Engage advertising banners to you.

If you do not want the benefit of the Engage network cookie, there is a simple procedure to manually delete your cookies. Please consult your web browser's Help documentation for more information.
