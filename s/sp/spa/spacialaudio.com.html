PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: May 30, 2013


1. Consent for Collection of Personal Information

Spacial Audio Solutions will collect and store information which you provide for purposes of your registration with Spacial Audio Solutions, your purchase of services (“Services”) offered through Spacial.com and/or Spacialnet.com (“Web Site”), and for communicating with you regarding your account and providing you with general Spacial Audio Solutions information. By using Spacial Audio Solutions Web Sites and Services, you consent that Spacial Audio Solutions can collect, store, and use the information you provided for these purposes. The information you submit to Spacial Audio Solutions during the registration process will be reviewed to ensure that it is reliable, accurate and complete. With the exception of applicable provisions herein, your information will remain strictly confidential and will not be sold, rented, disclosed or made available to anyone other than Spacial Audio Solutions. Any credit card information which you provide for the purpose of payment for Services purchases from the Web Site will not be stored by Spacial Audio Solutions

2. Use and Disclosure of Personal Information Without Consent

Spacial Audio Solutions may collect, use, or disclose your information without your knowledge or consent if it is reasonably necessary:

  1. in case of an emergency where the life, health or security of an individual is threatened;
  2. to satisfy any applicable law, regulation, legal process or enforceable governmental request;
  3. to enforce applicable terms of this Agreement, including investigation of any possible violations;
  4. to detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security, or technical issues; or
  5. to protect against harm to the rights, property, or safety of Spacial or any member of the public.

3. Security Safeguards

Spacial Audio Solutions will protect personal information through appropriate security measures against such risks as loss or theft, unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use, modification or destruction. Spacial Audio Solutions will protect the information regardless of the format in which it is stored.

4. Modification of the Privacy Policy

Spacial Audio Solutions reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time by posting a revised version on Spacial.com and Spacialnet.com. By continuing to use the Web Site and/or Services after the effective date of any revisions to this Privacy Policy, you shall be deemed to have accepted the revised Privacy Policy and shall be bound by its terms and conditions.
