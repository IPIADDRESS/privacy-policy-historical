Privacy Policy

What do you do with the information you collect?

We collect basic information about our registered users for the sole reason of personalizing their SpyderMate experience and better serving their varying needs. The information we collect from non-registered users is for advertising and statistical purposes only.

Do you share information with third parties?

No. User information is seen only by MentorMate for the purposes of maintaining SpyderMate and providing service to SpyderMate users.

Do you sell my information to anyone?

Absolutely not.

What do you do with my IP address and computer information?

We use your IP address to track your SpyderMate use and your computer information to optimize your SpyderMate experience.

What do you do with my email address and personal information?

We use this information to assist you with SpyderMate use. This information is kept private and is never sold to or shared with third parties.

Can I choose to remove or change my information?

Yes. Simply contact us at spyder@mentormate.com or call us at (612) 823-4000 (Mon-Fri, 8 A.M. to 4 P.M.) to be removed from the SpyderMate registration list or to have your registration information changed.
