Spreedly Privacy Policy

Spreedly collects e-mail addresses of people who send us e-mail. We also collect information on what pages users access and information provided to us by users via site registrations. Such information may contain personal data about you including your address, phone numbers, credit card numbers etc. We are not allowed to disclose such personal information without your written permission. However, certain information collected from you and about you is used within the context of providing Spreedly services. The information we collect is not shared with or sold to others except under the certain circumstances and which your use of Spreedly services is deemed to provide to us a valid consent to disclose the following:

  1. In order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of Spreedly's terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.
  2. Spreedly does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other companies except to provide products or services you’ve requested or when we have your permission.
  3. We will transfer information about you if Spreedly is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, Spreedly will notify you by email or by putting a prominent notice on the Spreedly web site before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

What we do with your information:

Spreedly uses the information we collect for the following general purposes: products and services provisioning, billing, identification and authentication, services improvement, contact, and research.

Security:

The security of your personal information is important to us. When you enter sensitive information, such as credit card number on our registration form, we encrypt that information using secure socket layer technology (SSL). We follow generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, however. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. If you have any questions about security on our Web site, you can send email us at support@spreedly.com.

Disclosure:

Spreedly may disclose personally identifiable information under special circumstances, such as to comply with court orders requiring us to do so or when your actions violate the Terms of Service.

Client Data Storage:

Spreedly owns the data storage, databases and all rights to the Spreedly application however we make no claim to the rights of your data. You retain all rights to your data and we will never contact your clients directly, or use your data for our own business advantage or to compete with you or market to your clients.

Changes to this Privacy Policy:

We reserve the right to modify this privacy statement at any time, so please review it occasionally to ensure you're still in agreement with its provisions. If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you here or by means of a notice on our homepage so that you are aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it.

Questions:

Any questions about this Privacy Policy should be addressed to support@spreedly.com.
