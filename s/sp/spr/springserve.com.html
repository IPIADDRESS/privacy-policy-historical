For example, if you set the opt-out while using Firefox, but then use Chrome, the opt-out will not be active in Chrome. To opt-out in Chrome, you will need to repeat the opt-out process using the Chrome browser.  We recommend that you leave cookies turned on because they allow you to take advantage of some of the Site’s features.  You can decline SpringServe’s use of advertising cookies for interest-based advertising by clicking on the “Opt-Out” button at the top of this page.
