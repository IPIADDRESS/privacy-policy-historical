1. Information Collection and Usage thereof

General

  • Springleap does not require that Users register on the Website in order to view its products or User comments. Registration is required to use certain interactive features such as posting comments, participating in the blog forums, and in order to submit ideas, designs, slogans and media (hereafter referred to as "creations") and participate in blogs and competitions as a User who submits, votes or participates in any interactive springleap activities and member interaction.
  • Springleap collects personal information when you register with Springleap, when you visit the Website, when you make a purchase, or any time you provide information to Springleap about yourself and information that relates to you or your interests. Springleap may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from Users, business partners, third party entities or other companies.
  • When you register, we ask for personal information such as your Christian name, surname, email address, and other personally-identifying information (hereafter referred to as "Personal Information"). When you register with Springleap and sign in to our services and competitions, you are not anonymous to springleap.
  • Springleap and the Website automatically receives and records other non-personally-identifying information ("Non-Personal Information") on our server logs from your browser even if you do not register with Springleap, for example, your IP address, Springleap cookie information, and the pages you visit.
  • Springleap uses Non-Personal Information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfil your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.

Children Under 13

  • Springleap is committed to protecting the privacy of children. Springleap does not knowingly or intentionally collect personal information online from children under the age of 13.
  • If Springleap learns that personally identifiable information of persons under 13 years of age has been collected on Springleap without verified parental or guardian consent, then springleap will take the appropriate steps to delete this information.

Your account name is displayed to other Users when you send and/or post messages through the Website.

  • Springleap uses commercially reasonable technical safeguards to preserve the integrity and security of your Personal Information. We cannot, however, ensure or warrant the security of any Personal Information you transmit to Springleap and you do so at your own risk. Having said this Springleap reserves the right to sue, chose to assist in the prosecution of and/or take any other legal action that is deemed fit by Springleap and it's legal representatives against the offending parties.

The level of access to Personal Information varies relative to the necessity of the employee's responsibilities and requirements in the process of facilitating their work or meeting their agendas and deadlines prescribed by springleap.
