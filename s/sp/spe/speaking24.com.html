Speaking24.com PRIVACY POLICY

GENERAL

Speaking24 collects your personal information when you enter some info in 'Something about you' section on our main site. Speaking24 collects also your contact information (email) when you subscribe to our 'Word of the day' service.

Speaking24 receives and records information from your computer and browser, including your software and hardware attributes, IP address, Speaking24 cookie information and the page you request.

Visitors who have javascript enabled are tracked using Google Analytics. Google Analytics collects some information including network location and IP address, type of user agent used, software manufacture and version number, type of operating system, flash version, screen colors and resolution, javascript support, time and length of visit, pages visited, referring site statistics.

INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE

Speaking24 collects information in section 'Something about you' (on our main website) in order to present it on the 'People online' list (information about you will be displayed only when you're online and for maximum 15 minutes after you leave our main website).

If you'd like to stop displaying information about you immediately (take you out from the list), you have to delete information entered in 'Nick' field. If you do so, information about you will disappear during next refresh of the list.

Speaking24 collects information about your email only for purpose of sending you our "Word of the day" regularly on your email. You can always unsubscribe "Word of the day" service by clicking appropriate link in emails which you receive.

INFORMATION SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

Speaking24 does not sell, share, or rent your personal information to other people or non-affiliated companies, excluding displaying information from 'Something about you' section (as it's written in INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE section).

COOKIES

Speaking24 may set and access Speaking24 cookies on your computer.

Google, as third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on Speaking24 website. You'll find more information about it in THIRD-PARTY ADVERTISING section.

THIRD-PARTY ADVERTISING

We use third-party advertising companies (Google) to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including information from our "Something about you" section and your email) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here: http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

Speaking24 may update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the policy by placing a notice on our site.
