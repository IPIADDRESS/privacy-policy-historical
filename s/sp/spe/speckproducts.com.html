Privacy Policy

We at Speck value our customers’ right to privacy. Speck’s Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, secure and use the information we gather at SpeckProducts.com.  We will periodically update our website with new technology without prior notice in an ongoing effort to enhance features and services for our customers.  If you are concerned about how your information is used, we recommend that you come back and review our Privacy Policy.

Security

We protect your financial information and privacy by hosting our site on secured servers, partnering with secure industry leader Verisign and by using SSL (Secure Socket Layer) data encryption to ensure that no one can read your information as it gets transmitted over the Internet.  We have appropriate security measures in place to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site.

Cookies

Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that we transfer to your computer's hard drive through your web browser. Cookies allow us to provide you with services and features that will enhance your shopping experience.  Most browsers have help features on their toolbars that explain how to prevent cookies from being accepted onto your browser.  Please keep in mind that cookies allow you to enjoy all the rich features on our site, so we recommend that you leave them on.

Your Personal Information

During the order process, we receive and store the following information from you that may include the following:

  • email address
  • name
  • mailing/billing address
  • telephone number
  • credit card information
  • IP address/domain name
  • any personal information that you may have volunteered

We do not sell or share our customers’ personal information with any third parties (except if we are required legally to do so).  Any information that you submit to the site will remain secure.
We collect and store information about the pages viewed by our website visitors. We use this data to identify visitor behavior which will help us to improve the overall site experience.

SpeckProducts.com may contain links to other sites.  We are not responsible for the Privacy Policy content on these sites.  You may want to refer to the policies on those sites to see how they collect and use information.

Emails

By providing us with your email, you may receive periodic emails or mailers from us with new product announcements, events or promotions.  If you would like to stop receiving these emails, please email us, Attention: Unsubscribe from Speck, at customerservice@speckproducts.com to be removed from the list. We do not sell or share our customers’ email information with any third parties (except if we are required legally to do so).  Any information that you submit to the site will remain secure.

Children

Speck is committed to protecting the privacy of children on the Internet.  We do not knowingly solicit or collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13.  There are no computer applications on our website which allow for the determination of the age of a customer or visitor. If a child has provided our website with personally identifiable information, we ask that a parent or guardian call us at +1 (650) 462-2040 or email customerservice@speckproducts.com and we will delete the information from our files.

California Privacy Rights

California Civil Code Section 1798.83 permits Speck customers who are California residents to request certain information regarding its disclosure of personal information to third parties for their direct marketing purposes. To make such a request, please send an e-mail to customerservice@speckproducts.com or write to: Customer Service, 177 Bovet Road, Suite 200, San Mateo, CA 94402.  We will respond to requests made and provide the information within 30 days of receipt of request.  This request may be made no more than once per calendar year.
