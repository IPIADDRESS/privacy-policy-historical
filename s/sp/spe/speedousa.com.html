OUR PRIVACY POLICY

We hold your privacy in the highest regard, and this Privacy Policy will tell you how we safeguard the information you give us.  You agree to the terms of this policy by using the web site, so you should read it before using the site. In order to make your visits to www.speedousa.com as safe and easy as we can, we do obtain details from your computer each time you use our site and this information can be personal in nature. The following should fill you in on how we treat your information.  If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, the practices of our web site, or your interactions with our web site, you can contact us at: Warnaco Swimwear Inc, 6040 Bandini Boulevard, Los Angeles, CA 90040 click here to e-mail us at Service@SpeedoUSA.com.


If you do not agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy and the Terms of Use, please refrain from use of the site.


personal information use and disclosure
The term “personal information” as used in this Privacy Policy refers to your name, home or other address, e-mail addresses, telephone numbers, or other information we can use to identify you as an individual. Our web site may use various forms for users to request information, register with the site, subscribe to our newsletter, create a wish list, purchase products and use our services. We may collect contact information (such as your e-mail address, name, address and phone number) and financial information (such as credit card numbers and your billing address) through these forms. The details from the forms are used to send orders to our customers, to send information about our products and services and to help us maintain our records. You can change your user information at any time, and we encourage you to keep it up to date. 


how to opt out
We may send you e-mail updates or our newsletter featuring information about products, companies and events that we think might interest you. You have the option of deciding not to receive any of our future mailings each time you register for any of our services or any time you order anything online from us. We make information, including personal information, available to a select group of companies that we think may have products, contests, or services that you would enjoy. If you do not want to receive these messages, click here to send us an e-mail to remove your name from the list. Please include your full name and address and specify “no mail”. Service@SpeedoUSA.com.

In addition, www.speedousa.com may share non-personal, or collective or summary information regarding our users with other third parties not mentioned in this policy, including advertising companies which serve or track ads on our web site.  These companies may use information about your visits to this site to measure advertising effectiveness and provide advertisements about goods and services that may be of interest to you. To find out more about advertising company information gathering practices and opt out procedures please click here: http://performics.com/privacy.html policy.


If you do not want to receive messages from third parties, please send us an email at Service@SpeedoUSA.com.  Please include your full name, street address, email address and the mailing label from a prior order or mailing if possible and specify that you do not wish to receive third party mailings. 


Similarly, we may ask you for personal information when you enter sweepstakes, contests or promotions through the site. If you’d rather we didn’t pass your information along to these third parties, you can decide not to participate in the promotion, contest or sweepstakes.

cookies
We do our best to provide you with products, contests, offers, and services that we think you will enjoy. We use cookies (small computer files that stay on your computer and let us know when and how often you visit our site) to keep and track our information about you.  We may use cookies to do the following: access your account information; figure out our audience size for our advertisers; help with processing items in your shopping cart; remember your wish list; track the number of entries in our promotions, sweepstakes and contests; and identify where you visit on our site. We may also match information obtained from cookies with data provided by you or collected by third parties. We analyze this data both in the aggregate and in connection with individuals.  This information lets us know what you may like so that we can give you the services you may want, and we also provide the aggregate analysis of our users to prospective partners, advertisers and other third parties.


if you don't like cookies...
You can have your browser reject the cookies.  If you choose this option, you’ll be required to re-register with speedousa.com every time you visit our site if you would like to participate in the promotions and contests we will hold. Companies that advertise on our site might use their own cookies, and this Privacy Policy does not cover how these companies use their cookies.


Similarly, you’ll see that when you visit us at www.speedousa.com, we provide you the opportunity to link to other sites not operated by us. These sites, including those belonging to our advertisers, may collect personally identifiable information about you. This Privacy Policy does not address the information practices of those other web sites or of our advertisers. Also, we are not responsible for the information practices and content of these sites; they are not under our control.  Be aware of these facts when you decide to click through to another site.

surveys and polls
In order to collect details about you without chatting up every person that visits our site individually, we may conduct periodic surveys and polls of our users. We will either post these on our website or get them out to you by email. The questions in these surveys and polls are sometimes asked on behalf of a particular client or sometimes for several clients at once. Any information we collect from the survey will be edited before we release it to a client, and sometimes we will receive compensation for a portion or all of this information. All surveys are optional and any prizes offered for participating in a speedousa.com survey are awarded based on a random drawing.  When you participate in a survey, you are completely anonymous, and the replies you give us are instantly separated from your personal information. We only ask for your name and email in order for you to receive the prizes connected with the surveys. If you do give us your name or e-mail address in some section of the form where we have not requested it, we will delete it before releasing the data outside speedousa.com.  The survey reports we send out to clients will include aggregated data.  If we ask you for a zip code it is just to help us compare the opinions of users from all different parts of our speedousa.com membership. We will only participate in survey initiatives on behalf of clients whom we believe will protect your privacy. 


chat rooms and other services
You must be at least age 13 to use enter any chat rooms and message boards we may decide to include on the site.  Although we reserve the right to monitor chat rooms and message boards, the volume can be overwhelming. When you give out personal information in chat rooms or on message boards, other people can collect and use that information.  You can wind up receiving unwanted and inappropriate messages.  We’re concerned about your safety, and so we need to emphasize that you should NEVER give out personal information such as your address, e-mail, telephone number, city, or school in any chat room or message board. It’s just a bad idea, and it can be dangerous, so just don’t do it.

security
We maintain this site and all associated data with technical, administrative and physical safeguards to protect against loss, unauthorized access, destruction, misuse, modification and improper disclosure.  No computer system or information, however, can ever be completely or perfectly protected against every possible hazard. Still, at www.speedousa.com we are committed to providing reasonable and appropriate security controls to protect our web sites and their information against foreseeable hazards.  Therefore, we may disclose personal information to government authorities at our discretion, and we also may disclose personal information when we have reason to believe that someone is causing injury to or interference with our rights or property, our CKU users, or other individuals or businesses.
