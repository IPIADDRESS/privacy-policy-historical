Privacy Statement

At Specialty Bottle we are committed to protecting your privacy. We do not sell or rent information about our customers. We will provide information to certain service providers in order to service your account. (For example, if your order is being shipped via UPS, we will share your address and phone number with UPS to facilitate delivery of your product.) Otherwise, all information provided to Specialty Bottle remains confidential.

Specialty Bottle will not collect any personal information about you except when provided by you. The personal information we collect is generally used to process your requests or transactions, provide you with high-quality service, tell you about opportunities we think will be of interest, and understand your needs so that we can serve you better. If you do not want to receive promotional e-mails, you can call us at 206-382-1100, e-mail us at service@specialtybottle.com or click the provided "unsubscribe" link in any e-mail to unsubscribe.

Specialty Bottle may share aggregate or summary information regarding its customers with partners, advertisers or other third parties. This data will never identify you personally.

Like most other Web sites, Specialty Bottle servers automatically track certain information about your activity on SpecialtyBottle.com. This information includes the browser you are using, your IP address and the SpecialtyBottle.com pages that you visit.
