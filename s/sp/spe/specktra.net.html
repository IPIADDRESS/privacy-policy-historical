    Forum Rules

    1) Search before posting to prevent repeating topics. Please click here to learn how to use the forum search function.

    2) Take a look at the Announcement Forum for latest updates. Forum Sticky, Announcements, or Global Announcements all contain important pertinent information that we wish for you to read.

    3) Please post clear and concise topic titles. You won’t get the answers/attention you need if your topic title does not illustrate the information you are seeking, or sharing.

    4) Do not use ALL CAPS TO TYPE YOUR TOPIC TITLES AND MESSAGE IN. It’s not good forum etiquette and comes across as SCREAMING. If your post is in all caps and it disappears… don’t be upset!

    5) If you've posted a question but later found the answer yourself, let us know and we will close the thread. This works the same if you just would like to delete your thread.

    6) We have a “Welcome Forum” for new members to post their introductory thread in. We love meeting our new members!!

    7) Are you interested in being a Moderator/Staff Member? Please contact one of our Administrators for an application.

    8 ) No offensive material including: Porn, Racism, Criticism, Violence, etc.... Staff will not tolerate this.

    9) NO SPAM. Please do not spam the forums, also known as ‘Postwhoring’. This includes postwhoring or spamming to reach the 50 posts needed to access the Clearance Bin forums.

    10) Respect others views and opinions. Harassment or personal attacks will NOT be tolerated.

    11) Help others if you can. It's important and it helps the forum grow.

    12) Read and follow the guidelines. Disobeying of rules will result in a warning, after 3 warnings you will be permanently banned. If you have been permanently banned DO NOT contact the administrators requesting to be unbanned, we will not even entertain the thought.

    13) Respect moderators. They are tasked to keep the boards in good order and to see to it that activity in the forum remains clean and healthy. They deserve your courtesy and cooperation. No complaints or comments in question of moderation in public, we take these very seriously so please send them directly to a staff member. If you think you’ve been treated unfairly, please contact the moderator via private message.

    14) Do not spam personal site URLs inside any post or private message. Registered and active members are permitted to have a link to a PERSONAL site in the signature area, but not in any part of posts or private messages. Links to sites with forum content and theme similar to Specktra.Net are not permitted, nor are they allowed to be promoted via the Private Messaging system. Abuse of this will result in your membership to the forum being terminated. If you own a beauty related site with a forum feature, please email janice@specktra.net for a link exchange.

    15) If you have experienced, or in the middle of a problem swap, please do not vengefully post their personal information on the forums. If you can not contain yourself we will be forced to suspend your account until you cool down. SwapTawk is THE BEST resource for problem swaps.

    16) eBay links in the counterfeit forum are restricted to determining authenticity only. Please do not post affiliate links on the forum. Accounts found to be abusing this guideline will be warned and placed on moderation.

    17) Please use the search function and do your own research before asking questions. We strongly discourage members to be dependent on the forums for minor questions (and worse ones that have been asked and answered hundreds of times before). We simply can't, nor do we want to waste bandwidth on, spoonfeed each and every member who has a question about B2M, depotting, washing makeup brushes, returns etc. These topics have been covered extensively before, can be solved via a visit the official MAC site, and/or are even in the Specktra.net FAQ forum. Not only will help you but it also helps the forums save bandwidth & resources.

    Should you decide you no longer wish to be a member it is important you understand your posts will remain on this board under your username. We do not delete user accounts, even when requested.
