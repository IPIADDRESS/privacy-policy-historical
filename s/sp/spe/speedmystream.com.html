Your participation is voluntary and you have the choice not to disclose any information that we request.

The types of information we gather:

  • Information you provide: We receive and store any information you enter on speedmystream.com or give us in any other way. You provide most such information when you use the Speedmystream site during the speed test and registration process, which can include demographic information such country or browser. Demographic information may be correlated to web usage information collected during your use of the service.
  • Automatic information we collect from the site: We receive and store certain types of information whenever you interact with our site. For example, like many websites, we use "cookies," and we obtain certain types of information when your Web browser accesses the Site, when you use the speed test process, or when you view advertisements and other content served by or on behalf of Licensor on other Web sites. We do not only collect any identifiable information in order to optimize and improve our website.
