SECURITY

The internet brings new risks that must also be recognized and guarded against. We use industry standard security technology and practices, focusing on three key areas - privacy, technology and identification so we can safeguard accounts from any unauthorized access. Our companies use industry standard encryption within our Organization. Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology will be used within the Internet lending session to encrypt the information to provide protection from unwanted disclosure. Depending on your browser settings, a pop-up window may appear to tell you that you are entering a secure page. We use many layers of security - for obvious reasons we cannot disclose all of them, but they include updating our operating systems are with the latest security patches along with our anti-virus software and using firewalls to prevent unauthorized intruders.

PROTECT YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

You play an important role in protecting your information. There are a number of tips for making certain your personal information is protected.

  1. Regular email is not secure, so don't use email to send confidential account information. Instead, use the "Contact Us" link on our web site to send secure messages to our company.
  2. We recommend that you use a recent version of either Microsoft Internet Explorer, Mozilla Fireforx or Netscape Navigator/Communicator to access our web site. These browsers support the latest encryption and will help to ensure that your connection to our site is secure.
  3. Choose passwords that are difficult for others to guess. Don't use your personal information such as your birth date or pet's name as a password. Keep your passwords for each account unique, and don't write your passwords down in a place where others could find them.
  4. Secure your computer by using anti-virus, anti-spyware and firewall software. Also, make sure that you keep your operating system up to date and install the latest security patches.
  5. Remember that our company will never ask for personal information such as passwords and account numbers through email.
  6. Be on the lookout! If you notice anything suspicious, please contact our company immediately.
  7. It's a good idea to check your credit report at least once a year to make sure there is no unauthorized activity. See below for information on obtaining a free credit report.
