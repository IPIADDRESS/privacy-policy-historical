Privacy Policy

  • At Sports Interaction we are committed to protecting the privacy of your personal information. That's why we've invested heavily in world class information management systems to keep your details encrypted and secure.
  • The information that we collect is used to provide you with the best online betting experience while mitigating any possible security risk. We take your privacy seriously and hope the following information gives you complete transparency regarding your rights, the information we gather, how we use it, and how to correct or change it.
  • Any reference to "Sports Interaction" within this Privacy Policy includes Sports Interaction and all or any of its direct or indirect subsidiary undertakings, joint venture partners, and their related companies wherever located in the world as may exist from time to time.

Information We Collect and How We Use It

Sign Up Information

  • When you open an account with us, we ask you for:
    • your name
    • date of birth
    • address
    • email address
    • some financial information.
  • We ask for your name and address so that we can check your identity with third parties as part of our regulations to prevent fraud and money laundering.
  • We ask for your date of birth to ensure you are of legal gambling age; in addition to being used to complete your identity verification.
  • Financial information that is collected is used to process deposits and/or withdrawals on your account, and may be verified by third parties as part of our regulations to prevent fraud and money laundering.
  • As part of your account creation, we supply your information details to authorized third parties to confirm your age, identity and payment details. You agree and understand that these checks may be undertaken by authorized third parties to complete identity verification.

Additional Personal Information

  • Sports Interaction may occasionally contact a player directly to acquire more information in order to validate an online transaction or to distribute winnings. This information includes but is not limited to:
    • age verification
    • address verification
    • financial verification
  • In the event of a registration where we receive notice that a player submitted an inaccurate birth date (upon account creation) and is under the legal Age of Majority, we will immediately close their account and delete their personal information.
  • Sports Interaction may on occasion transfer your data for one or more of the following purposes:
    • the provision of betting services
    • customer service
    • administration of customers' accounts
    • the provision of customer transactions via your banking service provider
    • client identity and money laundering checks
    • risk management and mitigation
    • the provision of a seamless service between the customer's account and other Sports Interaction services which include but are not limited to online casino games and poker.
  • Sports Interaction shares customer information with third parties for security and fraud prevention purposes, the conduct of authorized games and for enforcement and regulatory purposes. Our intention is always to create a safer online environment which enhances the customer experience while protecting their details and privacy.

Cookies

  • We use cookies to track who, how and when players use the site in order to deliver relevant regional and personal information. Cookies are text files which sit on your computer. These cookies are completely safe and secure and will never contain any sensitive information.
  • A cookie placed by Sports Interaction can only be accessed by Sports Interaction and those with whom we work. These contain information such as their name or user identification number for both player protection and risk mitigation purposes.
  • Sensitive information is encrypted for security purposes and no information is ever shared with third party marketers.
  • There are two types of cookies:
    • First Party cookies
      These are cookies set and used by Sports Interaction only to execute basic site and account functions and improve the customer experience.
      There are two types of first party cookies.
      • Session cookies: These are used to allow a user to login to the site. Without them a user session cannot be maintained securely and the site is unusable.
      • Other Cookies: These have an expiration date and are used by Sports Interaction to enable customized site content and user preferences.
    • Third party cookies
      These are cookies used by Sports Interaction for compliance, security and site analysis which are set by a few chosen partners. This information is statistical and anonymous. This information is not used for behavioral advertising purposes.
  • Our security cookies reduce fraud and enable us to run a more efficient and safe site.

IP Addresses

  • Sports Interaction stores IP addresses (the internet equivalent of a car's license plate number) whenever a customer does something that only he or she is allowed to do. Opening an account, logging in to an account or making a transaction are all occasions when Sports Interaction stores a customer's IP address. IP addresses are stored for security reasons. If a customer logs in with two different IP addresses many thousands of miles apart within a short space of time, the difference in IP address can alert us to a potentially fraudulent event, and we can take the necessary action to maintain customer security. As with all data collected by Sports Interaction, IP addresses are subject to the same security protection standards used throughout the site.

Security

  • Sports Interaction recognizes the importance of keeping customer details and payment information safe.
  • Our systems and processes are annually audited and comply with the toughest security industry standards to prevent customer information from being compromised.
  • Some of our measures include:
    • Norton Secure Seal is from Symantec. Symantec is the world's leading Certificate Authority for encryption certificates. We use Transport Layer Security (TLS), the successor to Secure Socket Layer (SSL), encryption. By carrying the Norton Secure Seal we ensure all customer transactions within our site are encrypted to the very highest security levels. Symantec is the Certificate Authority for forty of the world's largest banks and 100% of Fortune 500 companies.
    • Quarterly, independent assessments for compliance with the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS), a worldwide information security standard defined by the Payment Card Industry Security Standards Council. The standard helps prevent credit card fraud through increased controls around how we store and use customer data.
    • Using only Federal Information Processing Standards (FIPS) compliant cryptography systems to encrypt customer data.
  • Sports Interaction is registered under the Data Protection (Jersey) Law at the Office Of The Information Commissioner.
  • Sports Interaction and its security and payment partners may store or process your personal information in Canada, the UK, the USA, Malta and Jersey.
