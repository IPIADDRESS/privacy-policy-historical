The data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within SportXE to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

Gazer Media will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a)conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on SportXE or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Gazer Media; and, (c)act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of SportXE, or the public.
