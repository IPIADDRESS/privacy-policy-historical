Privacy Statement

The Sportsman's Guide uses your personal information to enhance and improve your customer experience. In addition, this policy exists to better inform you of your ability to correct or modify the information you have provided us.

Your privacy is important to us and we are committed to protecting your privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, you may email us at legal@sportsmansguide.com. We welcome your feedback and suggestions to help us improve our policies and practices. This Privacy Policy is only applicable to customers in the United States.

We use personal information to enhance your overall shopping experience by providing you with better customer care.
