Privacy Policy

SportsMemorabilia.com Privacy Policy

Your privacy is of great concern to SportsMemorabilia.com. We use the information you provide to process your order and to ensure that your experience on our site is as enjoyable and efficient as possible. Our primary focus and greatest concern is you, our customer. We respect your privacy and therefore do not sell, rent or loan any of your personal information to any third party. We are committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information that we collect about you to process your orders and to provide a personalized shopping environment. When you purchase products from SportsMemorabilia.com or register for any SportsMemorabilia.com services, we automatically place you on our list to receive e-mail updates about special offers, new products, upcoming autograph sessions and new services. If you do not wish to receive e-mail updates, please send an e-mail to info@SportsMemorabilia.com and place "Unsubscribe" in the subject field or by mail at 4071 Laguna Street, Coral Gables, FL 33146. You may also request to opt out of future emails by calling customer service at (800) 689-2001.

The Information We Gather and How We Use It

System automatically gathers information about the areas you visit on our Internet store. We do not use any of the navigational data that you— as an individual— provide while browsing or shopping on the Internet, nor do we share this data with anyone outside our company. We do use navigational information in the aggregate to understand how our visitors shop in our store so we can make SportsMemorabilia.com better. Our site also uses "cookies" so you can keep track of the items you have placed in your shipping cart. Each time you return to our Internet store our shipping cart will be just as you left it. We may also use and store information you give us (e.g, your shipping or billing address) so you do not have to reenter it each time you visit. In order to fulfill your order and provide you with the highest level of customer service, we must ask you for personal information. SportsMemorabilia.com collects your name, shipping and billing addresses, telephone number, e-mail address and credit card information in order to deliver your order promptly and to the correct address. We use your e-mail address to send you a confirmation of your order. We will also send an e-mail when your package is shipped and include the shipper's tracking number. You may also choose to register with us as an MVP Club Member. Registration provides advantages like faster check out; ability to store shipping addresses; advance notification on upcoming promotions; and exclusive invitations to private promotions and events. By registering, you can also update your personal information through your SportsMemorabilia.com account. SportsMemorabilia.com does not disclose, share, sell, or trade any personal or individual information that it collects to third parties, other than in the course of completing a transaction, providing you with information that you have requested, or in response to an investigation involving legal matters. While we may provide aggregate information regarding site usage to third parties, such as statistics on site traffic and sales, you can rest assured that we will only work with external companies who will respect our customers' privacy.

Cookies

SportsMemorabilia.com reserves the right to use a feature of your Internet Web browser called a "cookie" to assign a unique identification to your computer. Cookies are files that your Web browser places on you computer's hard drive. SportsMemorabilia.com creates and then uses these cookie files to track your clicks as you go through SportsMemorabilia.com

Web site Cookies enable us to provide features that simplify your shopping experience, such as storing items in your shopping cart between visits, saving and retrieving passwords, and recalling information on past purchases and other preferences. This will save you from having to re-enter this information upon every visit to the SportsMemorabilia.com Web site. Most Web browsers are generally configurable to prevent that.

Choice

You have a choice regarding how the information you have provided may be used to make special offers to you. You can direct us to remove your name, address and e-mail address from mailing lists we provide to selected, prescreened companies. If you do not wish to receive information on special SportsMemorabilia.com offers and promotions, please e-mail us at info@SportsMemorabilia.com. Once we receive your request, we will promptly remove you from our marketing list. If your interests or needs change in the future, please let us know.

Consent

By utilizing the Web site of SportsMemorabilia.com, you consent to the collection and use of this information by SportsMemorabilia.com.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

If the Privacy Policy of SportsMemorabilia.com is changed in any way, we will post the changes on this page for your reference.

Safety and Privacy of Minors

SportsMemorabilia.com does not target children, nor does SportsMemorabilia.com expect to attract children to its site. Because of this, our privacy measures have not been specifically developed for their protection on the Internet. We do ask that minors do not submit information to SportsMemorabilia.com without the express consent and participation of a parent or legal guardian.

Third Party Advertising

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by companies, click here.

Links

Occasionally, this site may contain links to other sites. SportsMemorabilia.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of other Web sites. We suggest you check the privacy statement on each site that you visit.

Contacting the Website

SportsMemorabilia.com welcomes your questions and comments about our Privacy Policy. Please send an e-mail to info@SportsMemorabilia.com.
