About Sportswallah
+

Sportswallah is India’s first sports-lifestyle website which aims to strike the right balance between editorials & lifestyle, coupled with live scores and real-time social media trends, sports updates, suiting to an individual’s needs.

Sportswallah is a one-stop shop for everything that is there in sports. We cover Cricket, Football, Kabaddi, Tennis, Hockey, Badminton, Formula 1 (F1), World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) - erstwhile World Wrestling Federation WWF, National Basketball Association (NBA), Cycling and all major Sports in form of News, Images & Videos. We also feature Sports Memes, Humour, Interviews with Athletes and their Lifestyle.

We also aim to provide our viewers with the real-time updates of what’s hot in the sporting world. So, if there is anything hot trending in the world of sports, like Cricket Twenty20 (T20), One Day International (ODIs), Test cricket, Football Clubs, English Premier League (EPL), La Liga, FIFA, Pro Kabaddi League, Wimbledon, US Open, Australia Open, French Open, All England Championship, World Championship,World Cups, League Matches, Olympics, Asian Games, Commonwealth Game, Tour De France and much more you will get that on Sportswallah and that's not all, we also cover Women in Sports!

We are a bunch of sports editors, statisticians, enthusiasts, maniacs who work at Sportswallah. Sportswallah is mentored by one of the most renowned and reputed journalists, Mr. Ayaz Memon. He comes with an elaborated experience of sports journalism across reputed establishments.

Not being a regular news website, we love to relive most famous sporting memories, thus bringing out the nostalgia in you. Also, we are available in Hindi.
