Privacy Policy


Our privacy policy adheres to the US Safe Harbor privacy principles of Notice, Choice, Onward Transfer, Security, Data Integrity, Access and Enforcement. We are strongly committed to protecting the privacy of this site's visitors as well as personal information by following responsible information handling practices in keeping with privacy laws.
 
Anonymous Access
  
You can access and browse our website without disclosing any of your personal data. We do not collect any kind of personal information.
 
Use of Cookies
  
Cookies are small text files containing a string of characters. They are placed on your computer by some websites you visit and are used to uniquely identify your browser. Cookies do not impart personal files or information such as your real name and address. However, they do improve the quality of the service provided to you by storing user preferences and tracking user trends.
 
Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies, but you can reset yours to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent to your computer. Safeguards are in place to ensure that the information contained in a cookie is only disclosed or shared for advertising purposes and/or improving a user's visit of a particular website.

Symantec-Norton.com uses cookies for advertising purposes. No sensitive personal information is stored in cookies and your privacy is never at risk. Just know that:

- Third party vendors, including Google, show ads on the Internet that redirect users to this website.
- Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on visitors' previous visits on this website.
- Visitors can opt out of Google's use of cookies by visiting the Google advertising opt-out page. Vistirs can also choose to opt out of any third party vendor's use of cookies by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt out page.

Also, all Symantec online stores use cookies. Since you will be redirected to the store corresponding to your country in order to make your purchase, we want to make sure you are aware of this fact.
