Use of Cookies
  
Cookies are small text files containing a string of characters. They are placed on your computer by some websites you visit and are used to uniquely identify your browser. Cookies do not impart personal files or information such as your real name and address. However, they do improve the quality of the service provided to you by storing user preferences and tracking user trends.
 
Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies, but you can reset yours to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent to your computer. Safeguards are in place to ensure that the information contained in a cookie is only disclosed or shared for advertising purposes and/or improving a user's visit of a particular website.
