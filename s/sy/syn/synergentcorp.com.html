    1. Information You Provide to Us

    We may collect and store your personal information when you visit this Site, register an account with us, save your information with us online, contact us with a question or concern, or participate in a survey, campaign, contest or promotion.  For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, your personal information may include your name, address, phone number, email address, account user ID and password, gender and other identifying information you provide to us.  Submitting personal information is voluntary, but you may not be able to take advantage of all of our services and features if you do not provide us will all of the requested information. 

    2. General Information Areas

    To track site use, measure site traffic, and improve site navigation and information, we collect basic information such as Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, dates and times of this Site visits.

    We use cookies.  You may control permissions for cookies on this or any other website by adjusting your individual browser settings for customized privacy protection.

    3.  Use of Your Information

    Without disclosing your personal information to third parties, we may use it to enhance the quality of our services and of the content of this Site, as well as to communicate with you via email, by mail, by phone and by other means about our services, membership programs, campaigns and promotions, unless you opt out of our promotional communications.  If you do not wish to receive any promotional communications from us, please let us know by following simple instructions contained in such communications or by contacting Debra Trautman. 

    If you send us an email, we may use your feedback to respond to other inquiries, comments or suggestions without disclosing your personal information. 

    4. Privacy of Your Information  

    We respect your privacy and will not share, sell or disclose your personal information to any third parties without first receiving your permission except as follows.  We may disclose your personal information if required to do so by law; to exercise our legal rights; or if we determine that such disclosure is necessary to prevent, investigate or address suspected illegal activities or fraud. 

    5.  We Protect Your Information

    We have implemented electronic, physical and procedural safeguards to protect your personal information.  In addition to using security software, our business practices limit employee access to confidential information, and limit the use and disclosure of such information to authorized persons.  PGP protocol encrypts registration information when you submit it. Further, our business practices are reviewed periodically for compliance with policies and procedures governing the security and confidentiality of our information.

Unauthorized attempts to upload information or change information on this Site are strictly prohibited and may be punishable under the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act of 1987 and the National Information Infrastructure Protection Act.
