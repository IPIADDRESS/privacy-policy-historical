Privacy Policy

Synotrip.com understands the importance of protecting its users’ privacy.   A) When and why we collect personal information:

Browsing Synotrip.com does not result in any personally identifiable information being collected. However, we may collect statistics including IP address, time of day, date and browser type. This information is used to make improvements to our site.

Cookies on Synotrip.com collect information about your computer. A "cookie" is a small data file that is automatically stored by your browser. Cookies don’t offer any personally identifiable information. Cookies can be turned on or off in your browser’s preferences.   Third-party advertisers that Synotrip.com is associated with may also use cookies to collect information.

Registration on Synotrip.com involves providing your email address. The e-mail address is not made public and will only be used if you wish to receive a new password or wish to receive certain news or notifications by e-mail.   Your profile on Synotrip.com - After registering, you will have the option to create a profile. The information you provide on your profile is publicly viewable.   B) Consent   Synotrip.com assumes that you consent to the collection and use of your information as outlined in section A.   C) Questions     For questions or complaints about the Synotrip.com Privacy Policy please feel free to contact us.
