Privacy Policy

Last edited on: April 29, 2005

Here at shoptoit.ca, we want to build a level of trust with our customers. Your privacy is an important part of that trust, and is the reason why we do our best to protect your personal information. Please take the time to read through this page and understand what this policy is all about.


Cookies

Cookies are small files placed on your hard drive by websites that you visit. They contain snippets of information (such as your username and password), allowing certain settings to be displayed upon return visits to a website. On shoptoit.ca, certain features require us to use cookies.

The ability to block cookies is available in your browser's control panel. Be aware that by doing so, certain functions of shoptoit.ca may not operate. Blocking cookies is not recommended.


Merchant Websites

shoptoit.ca acts as a shopping portal to our merchant websites. Because our merchants' privacy policies differ from our own, it is important to read and understand them upon entering their site. It is also important to understand that when you are making a purchase, you will be making it through these merchant websites and not shoptoit.ca. The information collected during the time of purchase will fall under the merchant's privacy policy.


User Information

Upon signing up for an account on shoptoit.ca, you may be required to provide personal information. This information, which could include your name and email address, may be used to contact you if necessary.

You do not have to sign up for an account at shoptoit.ca to use our search engine, but by not doing so some features of the site may not work for you.


Email Policies

shoptoit.ca may send you periodic emails, which could include changes in service, our newsletter or other important information. You have the option to opt out of these emails in your account settings and may do so at any time.


Advertising

The advertisements on the shoptoit.ca website act as portals to participating merchant websites. These ads, along with their accompanying websites, may or may not use items such as cookies to track your browsing techniques. No personal information will be collected through advertising. When entering a site through an ad, it is important to understand that website's privacy policy.


Policy Changes

This privacy policy will change from time to time. Each time the policy is changed, the "last Edited On" date at the top of the screen will be updated. It is not the responsibility of shoptoit.ca to notify you when the policy changes, however, we will contact you when major changes take place.
