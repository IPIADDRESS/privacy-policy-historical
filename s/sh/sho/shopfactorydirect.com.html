Like many sites, this site electronically receives and records information on our servers from your browser, including your IP address, this site's cookie information, and the pages you request. These help us identify you, monitor your shopping cart and maintain the error-free performance of our servers. Your IP address is used to help identify you and your shopping cart and to gather broad demographic information.
