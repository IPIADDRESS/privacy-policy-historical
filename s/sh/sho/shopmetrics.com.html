  Privacy Statement
 
EFFECTIVE DATE: The most recent version of this Privacy Policy, as revised, is effective as of September 1, 2005.
 
At ShopMetrics, your right to privacy and data security is a primary concern. That's why, when you visit shopmetrics.com, we help you maintain control over your personal data on the Internet. Below are the guidelines we use for protecting the information you provide us during a visit to our Internet site or when you visit one of our customers sites utilizing the ShopMetrics framework.
 
Cookies
ShopMetrics, Inc uses cookies to store user preferences.  Cookies are also created to store session information and user settings.
 
External Links
ShopMetrics, Inc is not responsible for the content in external links on their website(s).  Content and Privacy Policies are the responsibility of their respective owners.
 
Internal Links
ShopMetrics, Inc may provide links in automated email messages that contain codes that are unique to an individual.  These codes are use as an alternative authentication module and can uniquely identify you in the ShopMetrics system.
 
IP Addresses and Logging
The ShopMetrics Platform uses logs extensively to help track processes in our system.  Often times your IP address and account username are recorded in these logs.  These logs may be kept for an indefinite amount of time.
 
Information Security
Security is of utmost importance to everyone here at ShopMetrics, Inc.  All data on our servers and backup media is encrypted where possible and stored in secure rooms and/or vaults where applicable.
 
Information Sharing
ShopMetrics, Inc. maintains a database of Independent Contractors whom have all agreed to make themselves available to ShopMetrics' clients as Mystery Shopping Shoppers/Agents/Auditors.  This list includes personal identifiable information and will be considered available to our clients until the individual updates their ShopMetrics profile and changes their availability status.
 
Data Collection
ShopMetrics collects personal, identifiable information from individuals whom offer their services as an Independent contractor to act as a Mystery Shopping Shopper/Agent/Auditor for Mystery Shopping providers around the country/world.
 
Email
The ShopMetrics Platform has automated emailing functionality.  ShopMetrics, Inc. and their clients may from time to time enable this feature sending messages that they believe may interest the Independent Contractors in the system.  These emails may be personalized with the data submitted or calculated by the data submitted.  Users will have the options to opt-out of certain emails.
