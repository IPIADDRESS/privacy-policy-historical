Privacy Policy

Personal data
Your personal data will not be registered by Shop2market. This with the exception of the Shop2market platform, that requires a personal login. For the use of this information we comply to the guidelines of the IAB, please see the e-mail code of conduct. Your information is not made available or sold to third parties.
