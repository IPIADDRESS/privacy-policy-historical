Privacy Policy

 

Your privacy is important to us here at ShoppingAds.com.  Any information that you provide on our website is used for the sole purpose to contacting you through your specified medium - email, phone, mail, etc.  

Some of this information - such as the customer's name, address, telephone number, and credit card data - is provided to ShoppingAds.com by its customers in order to establish service. Other information - such as the customer's account status, choice of services, and customer logs - is created and maintained by ShoppingAds.com in the normal course of providing service. None of this information will be disclosed to any other party. ShoppingAds.com may use customer information to provide its customers with system information or information about new or upgraded products or services. Customers may opt out of receiving notices of new or upgraded products from ShoppingAds.com by sending a request to info@ShoppingAds.com. In this request to info@ShoppingAds.com customers must identify: contact information, and the date the service was requested.

Please note that this policy applies only to ShoppingAds.com's web site and not to the web sites of other companies or organizations to which we provide links via our web site. However, if you choose to link to third party sites on our site, we are not responsible for the privacy policies of those web sites. Please review the privacy policies of those sites.
