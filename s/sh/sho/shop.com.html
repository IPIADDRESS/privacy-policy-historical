5.        What are "Cookies" and how are they used?

        A "Cookie" is a very small text file placed on your hard drive by a web server.

        We use cookies for a variety of reasons. For example, a cookie may be used to identify each user who accesses our Site and to obtain session information for users of our Site. We may use this information to deliver a more personalized service to our users. In addition, cookies allow advertisers and merchants to track responsiveness to their marketing efforts and to process transactions at their online stores. Thus, we allow advertisers or partners to set cookies, but only if they agree to comply with our privacy policy.

        Please note that, in addition to allowing you to accept all cookies, your internet browser software may also allow you to be warned before accepting a cookie or to reject all cookies. Our Service is dependent on the acceptance of cookies by our Members. Therefore, if your browser is not set to accept cookies, you may experience problems using our Service and will not be able to use all of the services offered on our Site.
