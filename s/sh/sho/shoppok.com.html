Privacy


Shoppok.Com has established this privacy policy to explain to you how your information is protected, collected and used.

We don't run banner ads, pop ups, pop unders, or any other kind of commercial ads.
We don't share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. We don`t sell or rent user`s personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes without users explicit consent.
