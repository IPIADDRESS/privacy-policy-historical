Privacy

Introduction

 

At Shout! Factory we recognize that privacy is important. This notice describes the privacy policies for Shout! Factory, ShoutFactory.com, any other owned or related entities (“Online Privacy Policy”), as well as some of the steps we take to safeguard information.

 

Personal information means any information that may be used to identify an individual, including, but not limited to, a first and last name, a home or other physical address and an e-mail address or other contact information, whether at work or at home. In general, you can visit Shout! Factory's Web pages without telling us who you are or revealing any personal information about yourself. Shout! Factory strives to comply with all applicable laws around the globe that are designed to protect your privacy, and although legal requirements may vary from country to country, Shout! Factory intends to adhere to the principles set forth in this Online Privacy Policy.

 

 

Shout! Factory and Cookies

 

Upon your first visit to Shoutfactory.com, Shout! Factory sends a "cookie" to your computer. A cookie is a piece of data that identifies you as a unique user. Shout! Factory uses cookies to improve the quality of our service and to understand our user base more. Shout! Factory does this by storing user preferences and information in these cookies. Shout! Factory will not disclose its cookies to third parties except as required by a valid legal process such as a search warrant, subpoena, statute, or court order.

 

Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies. You can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. Be aware, however, that some aspects of the Shout! Factory experience may not function properly if you refuse to accept cookies.

 

 

What Information Do We Collect?

 

Shout! Factory collects and stores the information you enter through our website as part of a registration and purchasing process. Certain personal or identifying information may include: name, postal address, e-mail address, telephone number, and, if applicable, credit card number. Shout! Factory may also store information such as time of day, browser type, browser language, and the IP address of the machine you used to submit your personal information. Shout! Factory does not collect any additional unique information about you except if/when you specifically and knowingly provide such information.

 

Credit card information is required before users are allowed to purchase merchandise through any of our sites.

 

 

How This Information is Used

 

Any personal information about you that you submit may be used to improve our marketing and promotional efforts, to analyze site usage, to improve our content, and personalize Shout! Factory's offerings. These uses are intended to tailor the site to better enhance your experience.

 

Except as expressly disclosed herein, Shout! Factory does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information except within Shout! Factory, to Shout! Factory's third-party service providers (which may include business partners, sponsors and advertisers), and to financial institution(s) that processes your credit card transactions, unless required by law via any valid legal process such as a search warrant, subpoena, statute, or court order. If you choose to provide us with your personal information on the Web, we may transfer such information as described above. Without your prior consent, we will not use your personal identifiable information for any purpose other than that for which it is submitted.

 

 

Opt-Out Right

 

You have the right at any time to cease permitting personal information to be collected, used or disclosed by us or by any third parties with whom we have shared the information. To discontinue our use of any personal information, please contact us by e-mail, telephone, or postal mail. After processing the cancellation, we will delete your personal information from our database.

 

 

Third Party Providers

 

As described above, third-party providers are companies that make available services on behalf of Shout! Factory, ShoutFactory.com, and any other owned or related entities. To those third-party providers, Shout! Factory may provide personal information to help us deliver products, information, and services. Shout! Factory will take reasonable steps to ensure that any such third-party providers take similar steps to protect personal information on our behalf. Shout! Factory does not intend to transfer Personal information without consent to third parties who are not bound to act on Shout! Factory's behalf.

 

Our site contains links to other sites whose information practices may be different from ours. Please consult the other sites’ privacy notices as we have no control over information that is submitted to, or collected by, these third parties.

 

 

Security

 

Wherever your personal information may be held within Shout! Factory or on its behalf, we intend to take reasonable and appropriate steps to protect the personal information that you share with us from unauthorized access or disclosure.

 

To prevent unauthorized access, and ensure the appropriate use of information, we use Secure Sockets Layer (SSL), the strongest security available for websites. It encrypts all of your personal information, including credit card number, so that it cannot be read as the information travels from your personal computer to the receiving server. However, transmissions protected by industry standard technology and implemented by human beings can never be absolutely secure. Consequently, Shout! Factory will not be responsible for unauthorized disclosure of personal information due to no fault of Shout! Factory, including errors in transmission or unauthorized acts of third parties.

 

 

Access or Correct Your Personal Information

 

Help keep your personal information accurate. If your personal information has changed, please e-mail the new information to Shout! Factory, and we will update our files. If you would like to review the personal information Shout! Factory has in its files regarding you as a customer, e-mail your request to us along with a description of a recent purchase.

Factory designed website may be copied or retransmitted without the express permission of Shout! Factory Inc.

 

 

Your Consent and Changes to the Privacy Policy

 

By using this ShoutFactory.com website, you consent to the terms of this Online Privacy Policy and the collection and use of your information as we have outlined above, and to our Terms of Service. Shout! Factory may decide to change this Online Privacy Policy from time to time in any way, at any time without notice. In that event, any such changes will be posted to this page.
