The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for the 7,000 websites that comprise the ShopA-Z.com family of websites. We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Websites. Your IP address is used to help identify you, your account, and your shopping cart and to gather broad demographic information.

"Cookies" are small files containing information that are stored on your computer's hard drive by your browser. We use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests, and to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit any of our websites. We also use cookies to track your "click stream" to better understand how our websites are performing which enables us to enhance our websites usability.

Demographic and profile data is also collected on our websites. We use this data to tailor the visitor's experience across all of ShopA-Z.com's 7,000 websites, showing them content that we feel matches their interests, and displaying the content according to their preferences. This information is shared with advertisers on an aggregate basis, and at no time will specific personal information be shared.
