Privacy policy

At Showeet.com (‘We, ‘Us’), the privacy of our visitors (‘Users’) is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by Showeet.com and how it is used.

When Users navigate on Showeet.com, or download templates, they don’t need to give any personal information. There is no membership system enabled at Showeet.com.

But, like many other Web sites, Showeet.com makes use of log files and cookies.

 

Log Files, Cookies and Web Beacons

Log Files

The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track Users’ movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Showeet.com does use cookies to store information about Users’ preferences, record User-specific information on which pages Users access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitor’s browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.

The technologies we use for this automatic data collection may include:

  1. Cookies (or browser cookies): a cookie is a small file placed on the hard drive of User’s computer. Users may refuse to accept browser cookies by activating the appropriate setting on their browser. However, if Users select this setting they may be unable to access certain parts of Showeet.com. Unless Users have adjusted their browser setting so that it will refuse cookies, our system will issue cookies when Users direct their browser to Showeet.com.

  2. Web Beacons: pages of Showeet.com may contain small electronic files known as web beacons (also referred to as clear gifs. pixel tags and single-pixel gifs) that permit us, for example, to count Users who have visited those pages or opened an e-mail and for other related website statistics (for example, recording the popularity of certain website content and verifying system and server integrity).

  3. DoubleClick DART cookies: Showeet.com also may use DART cookies for ad serving through Google’s DoubleClick, which places a cookie on Users’ computer when they are browsing the web and visit a site using DoubleClick advertising (including some Google AdSense advertisements). This cookie is used to serve ads specific to Users and their interests (“interest based targeting”).

    DART uses “non-personally identifiable information”. It does NOT track personal information about Users, such as their name, email address, physical address, etc.

    Users may decide to disable all the Doubleclick Cookies by clicking on: http://www.google.com/settings/ads/onweb/optout?hl=en

Google utilizes the Data collected to track and examine the use of Showeet.com, to prepare reports on its activities and share them with other Google services.

When the Users visit a partner’s website, a cookie may be dropped on their browser.

Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to Users based on their visit to Showeet.com and/or other sites on the Internet.
