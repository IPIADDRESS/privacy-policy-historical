PRIVACY PRACTICE STATEMENT AND POLICY

She Writes, Inc. (“SHEWRITES”) believes that understanding what our customers want regarding collaborations, products, services and opportunities is very important and valuable. SHEWRITES is committed to protecting the privacy of users of our services.  The purpose of this privacy practice statement and policy is to inform you of the kinds of information we may gather about you when you register for and use our services or access our web site, how we may use that information and whether we disclose it to others.

SHEWRITES wants to contribute to providing a safe and secure environment for you if you choose to become a user of our website (“Site”).  SHEWRITES has designed its Site with this belief in mind, and has created this Privacy Policy so that you can have easy access to SHEWRITES’ stated privacy practices.  At any time, however, SHEWRITES may change its privacy practices and policies, and we encourage you to return to this Privacy Practice Statement and Policy (the “Privacy Policy”) frequently to learn about our policies and practices concerning how we collect and treat confidential and personal information that you share with us by visiting and using our Site and services.  Changes in our policies and practices will be posted here and will be effective when posted.

SHEWRITES is the sole owner of the information it gathers on its Site.  SHEWRITES will only share your Personally Identifiable Information ("PII") with outside parties in ways that are described in this Policy from time to time, or if we are required to do so by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary in order to conform to legal requirements, cooperate with law enforcement agencies, or comply with a legal process served on SHEWRITES.

SHEWRITES adheres to the European Union Safe Harbor principles as set forth by the United States Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use, and retention of personal information covered by the Privacy Policy from the European Union.

 

Changes in Privacy Policy

All personal information ("personal information" is defined as any information that identifies or can be used to identify the person to whom such information pertains) that we collect and maintain will be subject to this Privacy Policy, as amended from time to time. SHEWRITES may change this Privacy Policy from time to time at its sole discretion and will provide notice of all changes in the Privacy Policy by posting these changes on the Privacy Policy linked to our Site at https://www.shewrites.com.  If as the result of such changes you want to alter the ways in which SHEWRITES is allowed to use your personal information, you can do so by following the procedure described in the section entitled "Updating your personal information and privacy preferences."  You will be deemed to have been made aware of, and will be subject to, the changes to the Privacy Policy after such notice has been posted with the following exception: if at the time you provide personal information to SHEWRITES, you limit how such information will be used to communicate with you, either by SHEWRITES or by third parties, SHEWRITES will not change your preference in this regard without your consent. However, if SHEWRITES sells assets (or the assets of a division or subsidiary) to another entity, or SHEWRITES (or a division or subsidiary) is acquired by or merged with another entity, you agree that SHEWRITES may provide to such entity Personal Information, including any PII, that is related to that part of our business that was sold to or merged with the other entity without obtaining your further consent. SHEWRITES will provide notice of such asset sales, acquisitions, or mergers on this Site to the extent that Personal Information or PII is transferred.

“Cookies” or Web Beacons — if SHEWRITES sends you cookies, what are they and how does SHEWRITES use them?  What are Web Beacons and how does SHEWRITES use them?

Cookies are small pieces of text that store information specific to your browsing session with SHEWRITES.  A cookie does not contain code or any other malicious data.  We utilize this recurring information to customize your browser experience while you are utilizing SHEWRITES resources, and it ensures the proper credentials are being used to secure your information from unwanted viewers.  The majority of information SHEWRITES retrieves from your cookies is contained in “Session Cookies” and is not permanently stored or remembered.

A Web Beacon is an embedded object that is usually attached to an image or framing object.  In many cases, this embedded code pulls an image or other object from a third party site and is generally used in user tracking.  Web beacons help us measure the effectiveness of our Site and our advertising. For example, web beacons may count the number of individuals who visit our Site from a particular advertisement or who make a purchase from our Site after viewing a particular advertisement or posting, or they may tell us when a web page is viewed and provide a description of the page where the web beacon is placed. Web beacons may also measure the effectiveness of our email campaigns by measuring click-throughs and the frequency that these emails are forwarded.

The information we or our contractors collect through web beacons may include some limited personal information.  They also allow us to recognize users by accessing our cookies. We may also combine the information that we collect through web beacons with other personal information we have collected from you. We use all of this information to better tailor our marketing to you and may use this information for other purposes, such as to enable a shopping cart, customize content on our Site and undertake internal research.

We may allow third parties to compile individual information or aggregated statistics from the use of web beacons on our Site or otherwise placed by us, to enable such third parties to determine the effectiveness of online marketing and to allow such third parties to develop statistics on how often clicking on an advertisement results in an action on the advertiser's site. Aggregate information is anonymous and may include demographic and usage information. No personally identifiable information about you is shared with such third parties for this research.

SHEWRITES may hire other companies to place our banner ads on other websites. Such companies perform tracking and reporting activities on ads served and user reaction ("third-party advertisement servers"). They are not authorized by SHEWRITES to collect any information other than IP addresses and behaviors associated with those IP addresses on our behalf, and we do not give any personal information to them. Third-party advertisement servers are subject to and may have their own privacy policies.

SHEWRITES may allow other companies to display advertisements on certain pages of the Site or within the Products and Services. If you click on these advertisements these companies may place a persistent cookie on your computer which may allow them to display further advertisements to you. It is our policy to require these companies to provide accessible methods for you to disable these cookies. Should you have any complaints about the practices of any of these companies you should contact privacy@shewrites.com.

When you receive an email from SHEWRITES, we may track whether you opened that email and clicked on any links included in the email.

Protecting personal information 

We understand the importance of ensuring that your personal information is secure and protected.  We use a variety of security technologies and procedures to help protect personal information from unauthorized access, use, and disclosure.

The personal information that you provide in connection with registering yourself as a user of the Site or of the programs, products or services available on our Site or from our members is classified as Registration Information. Registration Information is protected in several ways. Access by you to your Registration Information is available to you through a password and unique customer ID selected by you. This password is encrypted. In addition, your Registration Information resides on secure servers where only selected SHEWRITES’ personnel and contractors have access via administrative level passwords. SHEWRITES encrypts your personal information as it is transferred to SHEWRITES and thereby strives to prevent unauthorized parties from viewing such information. Unfortunately, although we have taken these steps, there is a possibility that a third party may, without our authorization, breach our security procedures and access such information.

It is your responsibility to keep your password and unique customer Id confidential. Do not share this information with anyone. If you are sharing a computer with anyone you should always log out before leaving a site or service to protect access to your information from subsequent users.
