We use these services to improve the quality of our services, customize our site content to appeal to your preferences, and to direct our ads to you.

In addition, SheerID uses clear gif images on our website. Gif images are small clear images. These images may be used in page layout, to track the movements of website users throughout the website, or to alert us that a user has opened our email. We use gif images to improve the quality of our services, customize our website content to appeal to you, and to improve the experience of our website users.

We may disclose your information to a third party, if we believe it is necessary to: (a) conform to legal requirements or comply with legal process; or (b) protect and defend our rights or property.

Opt Out
