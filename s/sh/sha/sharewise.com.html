This Privacy Policy applies to all of the services offered on sites owned and operated by us (the “Services”). When we refer to ‘’we’’ or ‘’our’’, we are referring to sharewise GmbH.

1. Fundamental Principles of Privacy Policy

In the operation of our business, we may collect Personal Information about you, and we recognize and respect the privacy rights with regard to, and the importance of protecting, such information. To show our deepest commitment and responsibility to safeguard your Personal Information, we have established this Privacy Policy.

Our management and employees are expected to strictly observe all regulatory, legal and corporate requirements for handling and protecting Personal Information. We have also established internal safety standards, accountability protocols and procedures, and preventive measures to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration or destruction of Personal Information.

As part of our continuous efforts to monitor and ensure the safety of your Personal Information, we regularly review our personal information management system, including compliance by our management and employees with this Privacy Policy.

2. Personal Information

"Personal Information" shall mean information relating to an individual who is or can be identified from such information. Personal Information includes name, date of birth, gender, address, e-mail address, credit card number and expiration date, bank account details, and other information that can be used to identify a specific individual. We may collect Personal Information from you when you sign up for and use our Services, place an order with us, contact us by mail, phone or email, make a written complaint or request, and/or participate in our surveys or questionnaires. Also, when you visit our websites, our web servers automatically collect the IP address assigned to you by your internet service provider, the website from which you visit us, which of our websites you visit, and the date and length of your visit.

3. Proper Acquisition

We acquire Personal Information only by proper and lawful means and not through deception or any other wrongful means. When Personal Information is provided to us by third parties, we take appropriate steps to verify that such information is reliable and obtained in a proper manner.

When you register via your social media account the concerned social media provider will automatically provide to us your personal account data such as your name, social media account ID, email, workplace, gender or further account-related data or profile pictures. Please note that not all of the provided data is stored to our data bases (e.g. no information regarding workplace, gender or profile pictures). However, in such case you are using a third party service which is independent of us and whose data processing procedures we are unable to influence. Please find further information on data protection at the websites of the relevant third party service providers.

4. Use of Personal Information

We use Personal Information only for the following purposes:

  1. To provide our Services and information regarding our products;
  2. For your proper registration as a user and personal verification;
  3. For transaction of everyday business with our customers and business partners;
  4. To tailor our Services for individual customers, such as by providing personalized content, offers or promotions;
  5. To perform analysis and conduct market research for developing and improving our Services in full compliance with applicable data protection laws;
  6. In connection with an investigation of suspected or actual fraudulent or illegal activity such as insider trading; and
  7. To comply with contractual, regulatory and legal requirements.

5. Transfer of Personal Information to Third Parties

Except as permitted by applicable data protection laws, we will not, without your prior consent, provide or disclose your Personal Information to third parties except in the following circumstances:

  1. To comply with any applicable law, regulation, or governmental request;
  2. To protect against immediate harm to personal safety or property, and procuring prior consent from the individual concerned is difficult;
  3. For public health reasons, protection of children’s privacy and other public interest concerns, and procuring prior consent from the individual concerned is difficult;
  4. To provide necessary cooperation to relevant national and local governmental authorities, and procuring prior consent from the individual concerned hampers or jeopardizes such cooperation efforts;
  5. For outsourcing to third party service providers in connection with the performance of certain services on our behalf, such as direct mailing and phone calls, through contractual and written agreements requiring adherence by such third parties to confidentiality and privacy terms;
  6. For outsourcing to processors of payment transactions and provider of related services in connection with your payment transactions, through contractual and written agreements requiring adherence by such third parties to confidentiality and privacy terms.

6. Third Party Service Provider

When we share or disclose your Personal Information with third party service providers for processing or handling Personal Information for us, we conduct thorough investigation to verify the reliability and trustworthiness of such service providers, and take necessary measures to ensure their compliance with our Privacy Policy and any other appropriate confidentiality and security measures.

Cookies are small text files stored and saved on your computer by your Internet browser. You may refuse the acceptance of cookies, by selecting the relevant settings in your Internet browser. In providing our Services, we have further contractual arrangements with multiple online partners. We also have arrangements with marketing firms to use automated means such as web beacons and tracking technologies such as cookies to collect information.

Information collected by such technologies cannot be used to identify individual users and will not result in an invasion of your privacy rights.
