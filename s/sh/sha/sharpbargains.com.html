Also, you may submit your email address for reasons such as to receive newsletters. If you submit your email address, we use it to deliver the information to you. When we disclose information to these service providers, we disclose information to help them to perform their service. Similarly, to help you buy products and provide customer service to you, we must provide your credit card number to financial-services corporations such as credit-card processors and issuers.
