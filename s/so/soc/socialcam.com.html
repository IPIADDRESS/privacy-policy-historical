Information Justin.tv Collects

Justin.tv collects two types of data about users: (1) information that you or others voluntarily submit to us (described below under “User-Supplied Information”) and (2) technical data automatically collected from all visitors to the Socialcam Service (described below under “Automatically Collected Data”).

User-Supplied Information: In using the Socialcam Service, for example when you register with us, or if you send us an email, you will provide Justin.tv with data generally called “personally identifiable” information. If you choose to provide such information, you are giving Justin.tv permission to use and store that information consistent with this policy. Additionally, please know that when you register or sign in to the Socialcam Service, you are not anonymous to us.

  1. Mobile device information: We may store your phone number, as well as information regarding your mobile device, including your mobile phone number, device type and operating system and information in your mobile phone’s address book or in other applications as permitted by the functionality of such mobile device.

Information Regarding “Cookies”: Like many other websites, the Socialcam Service may send one or more cookies – small text files containing a string of alphanumeric characters – to your computer. Justin.tv may use cookies to track user traffic patterns as described above. Justin.tv may use both session cookies and persistent cookies. A session cookie disappears after you close your browser. A persistent cookie remains after you close your browser and may be used by your browser on subsequent visits to the Socialcam Service. Persistent cookies can be removed. You can refuse cookies by turning them off in your browser, however, some features on the Socialcam Service may not function properly if your cookies are disabled. Please review your web browser “Help” file to modify your cookie settings.
