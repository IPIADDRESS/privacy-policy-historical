Traffic Data
In order to improve the usefulness of our web site for our visitors, we automatically collect and maintain statistical information from our web site's data logs that concern network traffic flow and volume ("Traffic Data"). Traffic Data consists of the:

  • name of the domain, or URL, from which the visitor accesses the Internet (e.g., "a company.com"; "a school.edu"; or "an agency.gov");
  • Internet Protocol Number;
  • type of web browser used; and
  • date and time this web site is visited.

Traffic Data does not identify individual visitors. Traffic Data is, however, helpful in permitting us to improve our web site and to maintain its accessability.
