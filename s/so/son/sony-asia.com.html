General

This web site ("Web Site") is managed by Sony Electronics Asia Pacific Pte. Ltd. For each visitor to this Web Site, our Web server automatically recognizes and stores the visitor's IP address/Domain name combination. Automated tracking devices such as a "cookie" may also be used by us. A cookie is a small amount of data that our Web server sends to your Web browser when you visit certain parts of the Web Site and the use of which is intended to assist our understanding of your interest in our Web Site. Your computer provides this information automatically each time you log on, unless you have activated blocking technology available in some browsers. Activating blocking technology, however, may result in a limited experience of our functionality and services and in some cases may mean that we are unable to provide you with the services, or parts of the services, that you have requested. We also may combine your IP Address/Domain name combination with other information which you provide us. You provide this information when, for example, you send us an e-mail, fill out a registration or the feedback form, request for customer support, answer a survey or participate in contests/promotions.

Collection of Personal Data and other user information

Data collected during your visit to the Web Site may be used for (1) providing customer support for Sony products, (2) providing you with information on our latest software downloads & updates to our Web Site, (3) reviewing to improve the Web Site contents or layout, (4) conducting surveys, (5) marketing activities related to Sony products and services.

For the purposes described above, our company may share the information with any associated company in the Sony group either in Singapore or overseas. The information may be shared with third parties we deem appropriate to fulfill the above mentioned purposes. However, in such case, we will endeavor to ensure that the recipients do not use it for any purpose other than the limited purpose determined by us within the scope of your consent and we will endeavor to ensure that they secure the information from unauthorized use. The information may only be disclosed to other third parties in exceptional cases required by laws or regulatory authorities. In particular, we do not rent or sell your personal information to any third party (unless as part of the sale of our entire assets or sale of our business as a going concern).

For example, we request information when you:

  • Register as a member of our online community;
  • Purchase a product or service;
  • Register a product warranty;
  • Complete a survey;
  • Participate in a contest or other promotional offer;
  • Subscribe to a newsletter or mailing list;
  • Apply for employment in Sony

Depending on where you are situated, you may have the right to request access to and correction of personal information about you held by us. If you have that right and you wish to access or have the right to ask for correct or deletion of any of your personal data held by us, or if you have any questions concerning this SONY Privacy Statement, please contact us by sending an e-mail via the feedback form.
