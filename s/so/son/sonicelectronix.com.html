Privacy Policy

Sonic Electronix Inc. collects customer information to ensure a streamlined order processing system. We use this information to ensure accuracy in delivery and to participate in order-related communication. This information is not collected unless you provide it voluntarily. This includes personal information such as your name, shipping address, billing address, telephone numbers, e-mail address, and payment information. To protect your personal information, we allow you to personalize your password so that your information is completely confidential.

Order Processing

When it comes time to check out, you will provide financial information in order to apply a payment to your order. This may include your credit card data, wire transfer information or checking account information. To securely process your credit card data, we must rely on our credit card processor, with whom we share your credit card and contact information. We may also share your personal information with SquareTrade in order to offer services pertinent to your order.

Cookies

We use cookies on our website to personalize your shopping experience. We do not share any personal identifiable information collected by these cookies with any third party. The information collected is used to tailor our website towards your viewing habits and shopping history. You may still navigate SonicElectronix.com without cookies enabled, but you will be unable to use our shopping cart or order from our site.

We do not control any cookies used by third party service providers in conjunction with our website, SonicElectronix.com. Our privacy policy only refers to cookies operated by SonicElectronix.com.

Unique User Log Files

At times we use IP addresses to generate demographic data or as a fraud prevention measure, otherwise they are not shared, analyzed or distributed.

Data Sharing

We may involve third-party companies as a necessary means to fulfill your order. These third parties may be involved with order delivery, e-mail transfer, marketing, and credit processing, among other tasks. Third parties will only be granted restricted access to your personal information for carrying out order related purposes and will not be allowed to use this information for soliciting unrelated services.

Security

Our shopping cart and order forms are SSL encrypted to keep your personal and financial data confidential. We keep all of our servers in secure locations. Our database is heavily monitored and safeguarded to prevent misuse from outside operators. We use a firewall and an intrusion detection system among other security measures.

We restrict access to all of your information. We have security cameras throughout our premises as well as numerous alarm systems in our building. We only permit departmental and employee access to specific parts of your information, and this access is based on their respective role in carrying your order.

Email Newsletter/Specials:

Sonic Electronix email newsletter promotions are sent to users who have requested to receive these offers. We do not share, rent or sell your email address to a third party company.  We send these newsletters periodically based upon the information entered when placing an order or on the newsletter subscription page.

You may opt-out from receiving our promotional communications at any time if you no longer wish to receive them. If you wish to stop receiving our newsletters and promotional communications, you can contact us at newsletter@sonicelectronix.com. We also provide an opportunity to cease this subscription by clicking on the Unsubscribe link located at the bottom of our email newsletter.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update our Privacy Policy, so we recommend that you check back as often as it pertains to your concern. If Sonic Electronix elects to use or disclose information in a manner that amounts to a material change of this policy, we will outline the changes here.
