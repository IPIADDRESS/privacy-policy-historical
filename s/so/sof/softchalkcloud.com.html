Cookies

A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier that is sent to your browser from a Web site’s computers and stored on your computer’s hard drive. Cookies may be required to use the SoftChalk service. We use cookies to record current session information. Additionally, if you choose the "remember me" option, you are issued a permanent cookie. A permanent cookie is one that remains on the hard drive after the browser application has been closed.
