Privacy Statement:

Marrutt Software Training has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate its commitment to privacy.
The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this site;


User Information:

Our Web server doe not recognize a visitors email address, only a domain name.
We only gather email addresses of those who communicate with us via e-email or those who volunteer this information.
Other volunteered information collected are a customers name and address, telephone number, payment information (e.g., credit card number and billing address).

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending us e-mail, or calling us (information at footer).

Marrutt Software Training does not sell any collected information to third parties.


Cookies:

We use cookies to record session information, such as items that consumers add to their shopping cart and other information in relation to the navigation of our site and referring pages, cookies set by our server carry no sensitive data that can identify a individual.


Ad Servers:

We do not partner with or have special relationships with any ad server companies.


Contacting Us:

If you have any additional questions about our dedication to consumer privacy, please contact us.
