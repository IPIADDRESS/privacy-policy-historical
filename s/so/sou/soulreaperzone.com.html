IP addresses, & other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

soulreaperzone.com does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.

Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad & content network privacy policy.

We allow third-party companies to serve ads and/or collect certain anonymous information when you visit our web site. These companies typically use a cookie or third party web beacon to collect this information. soulreaperzone.com has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
