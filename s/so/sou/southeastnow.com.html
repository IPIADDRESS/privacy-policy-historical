What are cookies? 

While using an internet browser to visit or surf any website typically small text files can place themselves in your computer as browser’s history or program data subfolders which are called Cookies. A cookie can be of two types- the first is which expires when you close the browser and the second stays in your computer until you delete it manually. So like another company our readers will get cookies by us or by companies which are running their ads on our website.
