Neither of the types of user data collected at our site will be released to third parties or made publicly available. The information is not provided to these companies for marketing purposes.

We use traffic log cookies to identify which pages are being used. This helps us to analyze web page traffic data and to monitor which pages you find useful and which do not. We only use this information for statistical analysis purposes and then the data is removed from the system.

Our site also contains ads from external companies. Ads displayed by Google AdSense are targeted to be relevant to the content of the website and / or page they are on (i.e. the information is collected from the website contents only, not from visitors themselves).
