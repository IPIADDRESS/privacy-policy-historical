Privacy Policy - eviware

Privacy Policy

We at eviware software ab and our affiliated companies worldwide ("eviware") are committed to respecting your privacy and recognize your need for appropriate protection and management of any personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") you share with us.

Eviware has established this Privacy Policy so that you can understand the care with which we intend to treat your Personal Information.

Personal Information means any information that may be used to identify an individual, including, but not limited to, a first and last name, a home or other physical address and an email address or other contact information, whether at work or at home. In general, you can visit eviware's web pages without telling us who you are or revealing any Personal Information about yourself.

If you choose to provide us with your Personal Information on the Web, we may transfer that Information within eviware or to eviware's third-party service providers, across borders, and from your country or jurisdiction to other countries or jurisdictions around the world.

Eviware strives to comply with all applicable laws around the globe that are designed to protect your privacy. Although legal requirements may vary from country to country, eviware intends to adhere to the principles set forth in this Privacy Policy even if, in connection with the above, we transfer your Personal Information from your country to countries that may not require an "adequate" level of protection for your Personal Information. In other words, our goal is to provide protection for your Personal Information no matter where that Personal Information is collected, transferred, or retained.

Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies

Some of our Web pages utilize "cookies" and other tracking technologies. A "cookie" is a small text file that may be used, for example, to collect information about Web site activity. Some cookies and other technologies may serve to recall Personal Information previously indicated by a Web user. Most browsers allow you to control cookies, including whether or not to accept them and how to remove them.

You may set most browsers to notify you if you receive a cookie, or you may choose to block cookies with your browser, but please note that if you choose to erase or block your cookies, you will need to re-enter your original user ID and password to gain access to certain parts of the Web site.

Tracking technologies may record information such as Internet domain and host names; Internet protocol (IP) addresses; browser software and operating system types; clickstream patterns; and dates and times that our site is accessed. Our use of cookies and other tracking technologies allows us to improve our Web site and your Web experience. We may also analyze information that does not contain Personal Information for trends and statistics.

Where eviware collects Personal Information on the web, we intend to post a purpose statement that explains why Personal Information will be collected and whether we plan to share such Personal Information outside of eviware or those working on eviware's behalf. Eviware does not intend to transfer Personal Information without your consent to third parties who are not bound to act on eviware's behalf unless such transfer is legally required.

If you chose to have a relationship with eviware, such as a contractual or other business relationship or partnership, we will naturally continue to contact you in connection with that business relationship and will need to collect relevant information.

Wherever your Personal Information may be held within eviware or on its behalf, we intend to take reasonable and appropriate steps to protect the Personal Information that you share with us from unauthorized access or disclosure.

To the extent that you do provide us with Personal Information, eviware wishes to maintain accurate Personal Information and if you inform us of changes or errors we will make reasonable efforts to incorporate the changes in your Personal Information that we hold as soon as practicable.

Third Party Services

Third parties provide certain services available on eviware.com on eviware's behalf. Eviware may provide information, including Personal Information, that eviware collects on the Web to third-party service providers to help us deliver programs, products, information, and services. Service providers are also an important means by which eviware maintains its Web site and mailing lists. Eviware will take reasonable steps to ensure that these third-party service providers are obligated to protect Personal Information on eviware's behalf.

Eviware does not intend to transfer Personal Information without your consent to third parties who are not bound to act on eviware's behalf unless such transfer is legally required. Similarly, it is against eviware's policy to sell Personal Information collected online without consent.

Your Consent

By using this Web site or if applicable entering into an agreement with eviware, you consent to the terms of our Privacy Policy and to eviware's processing of Personal Information for the purposes given above as well as those explained where eviware collects Personal Information. Should the Privacy Policy change, we intend to take every reasonable step to ensure that these changes are brought to your attention by posting all changes prominently on our web site for a reasonable period of time.
