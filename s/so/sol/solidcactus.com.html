Solid Cactus Inc., Inc. Web Site Privacy Policy

Solid Cactus Inc. has created this privacy policy to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy while visiting our web site, and/or using our software and utilities. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for www.solidcactus.com, www.ystorebuilder.com, or any Solid Cactus Web Property. (the "Solid Cactus Inc. web sites". Solid Cactus Inc. reserves the right to update this policy as these sites evolve and new sites are added.

Visits to the Solid Cactus Inc. Web Sites

Anyone may visit the Solid Cactus Inc. Web Sites and access information while remaining anonymous, and without revealing detailed personal information. However, there are areas of the Solid Cactus Inc. USA Web Sites that are password protected and that may require the user to enter personal information in order to gain entry. Any such information is used strictly for user verification purposes only.

Solid Cactus Inc.'s Use of Personal Information

Solid Cactus Inc. will endeavor to take appropriate steps to protect the privacy of the information you share with us. Whenever you give Solid Cactus Inc. sensitive personal information, Solid Cactus Inc. will take commercially reasonable steps to establish a secure connection with your web browser. The information we collect may be used to improve the content of our Web Sites, customize the content and/or layout of our page for each individual visitor, or for marketing purposes.

Name, Mailing Address and E-Mail Address

Throughout the Web sites, Solid Cactus Inc. collects this personal user information to fulfill orders and requests made on-line. Additionally on some forms, Solid Cactus Inc. collects “opt-in” data - this personal information is to include users in future Solid Cactus Inc. mailings about products, news and features.

Telephone Numbers

Telephone numbers collected on forms throughout the site are stored in Solid Cactus Inc.'s databases are used solely for the purpose of verifying requests made on-line.

Credit Card Numbers

Credit card numbers are not stored on Solid Cactus Inc.'s Web Sites through use of cookies or database technology and are collected on this web site solely for the purpose of collecting payment information. This credit card information is stored by Solid Cactus Inc.'s databases for billing and verification purposes only.

Personal Information and Third Parties

Solid Cactus Inc. does not provide any personal information obtained through its Web Sites to third parties.

Cookie Technology

Solid Cactus Inc. uses cookie technology to retain information about site visitors. Cookies are used throughout the site on various forms to retain personal information, such as name, address, phone, etc., so that if the user fills out another form on Solid Cactus Inc.'s site, this information is automatically transferred into the appropriate fields in the new form. Solid Cactus Inc.'s cookies are "client-side," and are deleted once the user closes his/her browser. If you do not wish to have a cookie used on Solid Cactus Inc.'s site, you may need to alter your settings in your browser. After altering your browser settings, you will be alerted each time a cookie is being activated, and you may choose to reject cookies on a case-by-case basis.

Links to Third Party Web Sites

Solid Cactus Inc.'s sites contain links to third party Web sites. Solid Cactus Inc. is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these third party Web sites.

Site Security

Several areas of the Solid Cactus Inc. web sites are housed on a secure server. All information entered in this area, including credit card information, is entered onto Solid Cactus Inc.'s secure server area. Credit card numbers are used solely for the purpose of payment processing and are not retained by our cookie technology.
