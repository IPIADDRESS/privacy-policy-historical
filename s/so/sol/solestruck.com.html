THE SOLESTRUCK PRIVACY NOTICE

Because Solestruck understands that the privacy of your personal information is important to you, we have established privacy policies and practices. This notice explains how your data will be used and shared by Solestruck. By visiting Solestruck.com, you accept the policies and practices described in this Privacy Notice.

Information We Collect

Personal information is collected when you sign in to Solestruck.com, upon registration with Solestruck, when placing an order, or when contacting us via phone or email. Additional personal identification information such as user name, password, billing information, and contact information may also be collected in order to offer you Solestruck products and services. This information will be used to fulfill your orders, process your transactions, facilitate your use of our website, market our products and services, respond to your requests, assist you with future shopping, and improve Solestruck.com. Choosing not to provide us with information will limit our ability to respond to your shopping needs. In addition, information such as your IP address and the site that referred you to ours will be gathered automatically. All data acquired via the website is stored indefinitely. Because of the nature of the Internet, data may pass through any country and any jurisdiction, including jurisdictions where privacy laws may be different and less protective than those of your home jurisdiction.

Unless you opt out of receiving promotional materials, we may send you information by email or postal mail to the address you provided. You may also receive notifications of special promotions, new products, or additional services from our third-party associates. If you would like to opt out of receiving correspondence, please send your request to unsubscribe@solestruck.com.

We do not rent or sell your personal information to parties not associated with fulfilling your order, unless sharing this information is necessary to provide you with Solestruck products or is required by law. Solestruck also maintains contracts with third parties for billing, shipping, order processing, and other functions. Solestruck reserves the right to share your information with these third parties, in order to fulfill your order, provide related services, or when it is reasonably necessary to comply with legal process, respond to claims, or protect the rights, property or safety of our company, employees, customers, or the public.

Cookies

A cookie is a small data file that is automatically saved on your computer's hard drive when you visit a website. These cookies may contain information used to record the websites you visit, but do not contain any personally identifiable information such as your name, address, or credit card information. Solestruck uses cookies to give you a personalized shopping experience.

If you have signed up with Solestruck, cookies allow our website to recognize you when you return and give you access to your personal account options. Cookies also let our website remember the contents of your Shoe Box, allowing you to place orders more easily. We may also use cookies to keep track of the ways in which customers find us and navigate through our website. This information will be used internally to help us determine where improvements can be made to our online store.

If you do not wish cookies to be saved to your system, you may change your browser's settings so that your computer does not accept them. If cookies are disabled, however, you will not be able to purchase from our website and we will not be able to recognize you as a registered user.

SIZING GUIDE

HOW TO GET THE RIGHT FIT

Not sure what size to order? All sizes listed on our site are US sizes. We try on each style for you and record the approximate measurements for every size to help you decide which size to buy (because we love you and want you to be happy with shoes that fit)!

PLEASE NOTE: Fit can vary from style to style and from brand to brand, so please check the measurement we show and read the fit notes in each shoe description. Also, check each shoe description for any US to EUR size conversions that brand might have (see image below).

TO FIND YOUR BEST FIT:
1.
Stand on a tape measure or ruler.

2.
Measure the length of your foot (heel to toe) in centimeters (cm).

3.
Select a size to see the corresponding measurement, or read the shoe description to find the measurement.

4.
Choose the size that best matches your foot measurement and decide if you want to go a little bigger for a little extra room.
