What are binary options? Binary options - also known as Fixed Return Options (FROs) - differ from traditional investments in that investors are only required to correctly predict the direction of an asset movement to complete a successful trade (the magnitude of a price movement is completely irrelevant). With binary options there are only two possible outcomes. "Up" or "Down".
