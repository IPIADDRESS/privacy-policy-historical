Privacy & security

We understand the need to safeguard your personal information, and respect your rights under the Privacy Act, 1993. Where you voluntarily provide personal information (e.g. contact details supplied through email correspondence), we will only use that information to deal with you. We will undertake to keep any such information secure and will not disclose it to any third parties.

Where site usage and statistics are concerned, this site does not collect any information that identifies individual users.

Please email privacy@building.govt.nz if you have any queries or concerns about the personal information that we hold about you.
