Participation
We will closely guard any personally identifiable information you disclose to us during registration or at any other point during your BuiltinNewYork experience. Not all offers/programs/opt-ins/opt-outs specify exactly which pieces of information will be shared. By joining BuiltinNewYork, you are able to opt-in to receive mail from BuiltinNewYork. You can opt-out BuiltinNewYork Mail at any time and still maintain an active BuiltinNewYork account.

Information Collection and Use
BuiltinNewYork is the sole owner of the information collected on this site, and from its licensed software. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement. BuiltinNewYork collects information from our users at several different points on our website.

Registration
In order to use this website, a user must first complete the registration form. During registration a user is required to give their contact information (such as name, e-mail address and postal address), additional psychographic information, and information needed for shipping rewards. This information is used to contact the user about new campaign notifications, specific campaign directives, and reward approvals, changes in point offerings. In addition, we ask a user to provide legitimate proof of address. Proof of address is required in order to guard BuiltinNewYork from fraudulent activities. This information is also used for shipping purposes for BuiltinNewYork. If we have trouble processing a Reward, this contact information may be utilized to contact a user.

Our Cookies
A cookie is a piece of data we store for each user session that gives you a unique id number for that session. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on our site. Once the user ends their session, the cookie simply terminates. We use cookies on our site so that you can move from page to page while staying logged into your account. If a user rejects the cookie, they may still use our site, and we will set each link to contain the user's unique id number for your current session.


Log Files
We do track certain information such as type of browser and log on time for our own use. We use this information only to track where our users may be coming from and what type of software they are using. We keep this information so we can continuously improve our users online experience and improve the BuiltinNewYork Web Site. None of this information can ever be traced back to any specific person and is useful only to BuiltinNewYork.

Security
We work to protect your information by using Secure Socket Layers (SSL) software to encrypt your information when it is being transmitted to us. Access to data is limited through the use of internal firewalls and strictly enforced. This website takes every precaution to protect our users' information. When users submit information via the website, your information is protected both online and off-line. While we use both passwords and user names to protect information online, we also do everything in our power to protect users' information off-line. All of our users' information is restricted in our offices. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job are granted access to personally identifiable information. Our employees must use password-protected screen-savers when they leave their desk. When they return, they must re-enter their password to re-gain access to your information. Finally, the servers that we store personally identifiable information on are kept in a secure environment and professionally maintained. You are solely responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your passwords and/or any account information. Please be careful and responsible whenever you're online.

We send all new members a welcoming e-mail to verify password and username. BuiltinNewYork does this in order to establish that the e-mail address given upon registration is not fraudulent. Established members will occasionally receive e-mails containing information regarding new campaigns, rewards and directives from BuiltinNewYork if they have opted-in. "Opted-in" means the user has approved certain e-mails to be sent to them from BuiltinNewYork.
