Home-Based or Shop Photography Business Requirements: 



1. Business License



You first need a business license. You can obtain one here online.  The requirements to obtain one depend on your location.

For example, if you are in an apartment or house, you need to show the lease or ownership papers.  In most cases, you 

just need to pay a flat annual tax which is about $50 in addition to our fee to obtain it.



2.  Assumed Business Name



In short, if you use a trade name to run your photography business, you will need a fictitious business name filing.



3.  Sellers Permit



If you sel paper prints, you will need to charge tax for the photos.  This needs a sellers permit.



4. Business Insurance



  Normally, a small photography business insurance will cover most of what the bond covers and you do not need a bond insurance.   

So, if you are starting a small business and you are worried about getting  a bond, get business insurance but you really do not need even 

insurance.  On the other hand, if you have the extra money, go ahead and get small business insurance.
