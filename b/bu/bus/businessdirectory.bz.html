Naturalight Productions Privacy Statement

Naturalight Productions has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for our entire network of websites.

GENERAL PRIVACY STATEMENT: We are committed to protecting your privacy online and safeguarding any information which you provide during your use of our network. We pledge never to sell, distribute, or disclose the information you to provide to us to any third party. Your information is used for the sole purpose of providing you with information or sending subscriptions you have signed up for. Following are specific details of our privacy statement:

Information Automatically Logged
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site.

Cookies
Our Site rarely uses cookies to deliver content specific to your interests. We sometimes use cookies to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our site or to ensure fresh delivery of banner ads.

Our network forms require users to give us contact information (like their name, email, and postal address), and demographic information (like their zip code, age).

Contact information from these forms is used solely to get in touch with the customer when necessary concerning answering their questions or sending opt-in subscriptions. The customer's contact information is NEVER used to share with other companies who may want to contact our customers.

External Links
This site contains links to other sites. Naturalight Productions is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

Public Forums
This site makes forums, message boards, and/or mailing lists available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.

Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control.
