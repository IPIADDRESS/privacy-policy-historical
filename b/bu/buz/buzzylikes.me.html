Introduction

buzzylikes.me (“Buzzy Likes”) takes the private nature of your personal information very seriously.

Website Visitors

Like most website operators, Buzzy Likes collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Buzzy Likes’s purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Buzzy Likes’s visitors use its website. From time to time, Buzzy Likes may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g. by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

Buzzy Likes also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses. Buzzy Likes does not use such information to identify its visitors, and does not disclose such information, other than under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information, as described below.

Aggregated Statistics

Buzzy Likes may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. For instance, Buzzy Likes may monitor the most popular collections on buzzylikes.me. Buzzy Likes may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, Buzzy Likes does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Buzzy Likes uses cookies to help Buzzy Likes identify and track visitors, their usage of Buzzy Likes websites, and their website access preferences. Buzzy Likes visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Buzzy Likes’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of Buzzy Likes’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

Ads

Ads appearing on any Buzzy Likes websites may be delivered to users by advertising partners, who may set cookies. These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement to compile information about you or others who use your computer. This information allows ad networks to, among other things, deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This privacy policy covers the use of cookies by Buzzy Likes and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.
