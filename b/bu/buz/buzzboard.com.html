Privacy Policy

We value your privacy.

(Last Updated: May 8th, 2018)

BuzzBoard, Inc. (“BuzzBoard” “we” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Statement of Privacy applies to the BuzzBoard website and the BuzzBoard software platform. This statement governs the data collection, processing, and usage practices of BuzzBoard. By using the BuzzBoard website or its services platform, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Information Related to Data Collected and Processed for our Clients on our Services Platform

Service Provider Collection/Use
BuzzBoard is a cloud-based sales enablement platform developed specifically to transform the way enterprise sales teams sell to small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs). BuzzBoard collects information such as business name, phone number, business URL and business location, etc. This data is analyzed to provide intelligent business insights. BuzzBoard collects information under the direction of its Clients; BuzzBoard has no direct relationship with the individuals whose personal data it processes.

Onward Transfer
BuzzBoard collects information for our Clients. If you are a customer of one of our Clients and would no longer like to be contacted by one of our Clients that uses our services, please contact the client that you interact with directly. If you are a Client and would like to update your account, please contact us.

Service Provider, Sub-Processors/Onward Transfer
BuzzBoard may transfer personal information to companies that help us provide our service. Transfers to subsequent third parties are covered by the provisions in this Policy regarding notice and choice and the service agreements with our Clients. We also may be required to disclose an individual’s personal information in response to a lawful request by public authorities, including to meet national security or law enforcement requirements. In cases of onward transfer to third parties of data of EU individuals received pursuant to the EU-US Privacy Shield, BuzzBoard is potentially liable.

Access to Data Controlled by our Clients
BuzzBoard has no direct relationship with the individuals whose personal data it processes. BuzzBoard acknowledges that individuals have the right to access the personal information/data that we maintain about them. An individual who seeks access, or who seeks to correct, amend, or delete inaccurate data, should direct his query to support@buzzboard.com. If requested to remove data, we will respond within 30 business days

Data Integrity
BuzzBoard takes reasonable steps to ensure that data is reliable for its intended use, accurate, complete, and current.

Website Collection and Use Practices
BuzzBoard collects personally identifiable information from users of its website, such as email addresses, names, company names, home, and/or work addresses, and/or telephone numbers.

In the event that you contact us for customer support through the website, we may also request that you provide additional User Information relevant to the support requested by you. Users are under no obligation to provide such information. However, we cannot guarantee that we can provide customer support in the event that relevant information related to the use of our products or services is withheld. We may retain communications relating to customer support, and/or information drawn from such communications, for statistical and/or reference.

Social Media (Features) and Widgets:
Our website includes Social Media Features, such as the Facebook Like button and Widgets, such as the “Share This” button or interactive mini-programs that run on our site. These Features may collect your IP address, which page you are visiting on our site, and may set a cookie to enable the Feature to function properly. Social Media Features and Widgets are either hosted by a third party or hosted directly on our site. Your interactions with these Features are governed by the privacy policy of the company providing it.

Links to Other Websites
Our site includes links to other website whose privacy practices may differ from those of BuzzBoard. If you submit personal information to any of those sites, your information is governed by their privacy statements. We encourage you to carefully read the privacy statement of any website you visit.

Testimonials
With your consent, we may post your testimonial along with your name.

Use of your Personal Information
BuzzBoard collects and uses your personal information to operate the BuzzBoard website and deliver the services you have requested. BuzzBoard may also use your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from BuzzBoard and its affiliates. BuzzBoard may also contact you via voluntary surveys to conduct research on your opinion of current services or of potential new services. You will always have the option to unsubscribe (see To Unsubscribe from BuzzBoard below.) BuzzBoard does not sell, rent, or lease its customer lists to third parties in which we do not have a formal agreement to provide services to BuzzBoard, and our customers or prospects. BuzzBoard does not share personally identifiable information except as described in this policy. BuzzBoard may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about offerings that may be of interest to you. In those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (email, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party. In addition, BuzzBoard may share data with trusted partners to help us perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries or contact you based on your interest to receive such communications. Such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to BuzzBoard, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information. BuzzBoard does not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations, without your explicit consent.

In cases of onward transfer to third parties of data of EU individuals received pursuant to the EU-US Privacy Shield, BuzzBoard is potentially liable. BuzzBoard may transfer your information if BuzzBoard is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, BuzzBoard will notify you before your personal information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy. We will provide an individual opt-out or opt-in choice before we share their data with third parties other than our agents, or before we use it for a purpose other than which it was originally collected or subsequently authorized. You can contact support@buzzboard.com to opt-out at any time.

Tracking Technologies

Log Files
BuzzBoard also collects anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your ZIP code, age, gender, preferences, interests, and favorites using log files.

There is also information about your computer hardware, tablet hardware and software that is automatically collected by BuzzBoard. This information can include: (1) the browser types and versions used, (2) the operating system used by the accessing system, (3) the website from which an accessing system reaches our website (so-called referrers), (4) the sub-websites, (5) the date and time of access to the Internet site, (6) an Internet protocol address (IP address), (7) the Internet service provider of the accessing system, and (8) any other similar data and information that may be used in the event of attacks on our information technology systems.

When using these general data and information, BuzzBoard does not draw any conclusions about you. Rather, this information is needed to (1) deliver the content of our website correctly, (2) optimize the content of our website as well as its advertisement, (3) ensure the long-term viability of our information technology systems and website technology, and (4) provide law enforcement authorities with the information necessary for criminal prosecution in case of a cyber-attack. Therefore, BuzzBoard analyzes anonymously collected data and information statistically, with the aim of increasing the data protection and data security of our enterprise, and to ensure an optimal level of protection for the personal data we process. For these purposes, we do link this automatically-collected data to personally identifiable information such as name, email address, address, and phone number.

The anonymous data of the server log files are stored separately from all personal data.

BuzzBoard services platform tracks your usage experience & geographic coordinates (iPad App) for usage enrichment & deliver better & optimized experiences across its services platform.

Cookies
The BuzzBoard website uses “cookies” to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a web server. Cookies are not used to run programs, or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.

Using cookies, BuzzBoard can provide the users of this website with more user-friendly services that would not be possible without the cookie setting.

By means of a cookie, the information and offers on our website can be optimized with the user in mind. Cookies allow us, as previously mentioned, to recognize our website users. The purpose of this recognition is to make it easier for users to utilize our website. The website user that uses cookies, e.g. does not have to enter access data each time the website is accessed, because this is taken over by the website, and the cookie is thus stored on the user’s computer system.

You may, at any time, prevent the setting of cookies through our website by means of a corresponding setting of the Internet browser used, and may thus permanently deny the setting of cookies. Furthermore, already set cookies may be deleted at any time via an Internet browser or other software programs. This is possible in all popular Internet browsers. If you deactivate the setting of cookies in the Internet browser used, not all functions of our website may be entirely usable.

Advertising
We partner with a third-party ad network to either display advertising on our website, or to manage our advertising on other sites. Our ad network partner uses cookies and web beacons to collect non-personally identifiable information about your activities on this and other websites to provide you targeted advertising based upon your interests. If you do not wish to have this information used for the purpose of serving you targeted ads, you may opt out by clicking here http://preferences.truste.com/Data Retention. Please note this does not opt you out of being served advertising. You will continue to receive generic ads.

Clear Gifs (Web Beacons/Web Bugs)
We employ [or our third-party advertising partner employs] a software technology called clear gifs (a.k.a. web beacons/web bugs), that help us better manage content on our site by informing us what content is effective. Clear gifs are tiny graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies, and are used to track the online movements of web users. In contrast to cookies, which are stored on a user’s computer hard drive, clear gifs are embedded invisibly on web pages, and are about the size of the period at the end of this sentence. We tie [do not tie] the information gathered by clear gifs to our customer’s personally identifiable information.

We use clear gifs in our HTML-based emails to let us know which emails have been opened by recipients. This allows us to gauge the effectiveness of certain communications, and the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

Third Party Tracking Technologies
The use of cookies and web beacons by any tracking utility company is not covered by our privacy statement. We do not have access or control over these third-party tracking technologies.

Legal Disclaimer
BuzzBoard websites will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law, or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law, or comply with legal process served on BuzzBoard or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of BuzzBoard (including enforcing this agreement); and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of BuzzBoard or the public.

Access to Personally Identifiable Information
If your personally identifiable information changes, or if you no longer desire our service, you may correct, update, request deletion on our site by emailing us at support@buzzboard.com to our Privacy team. We will respond to all access requests within 30 days. In some cases, we may not be able to remove your personal information, in which case we will let you know if we are unable to do so and why.

If you request that your user information be deleted from our records, there may be a brief delay in our processing that request, as we will verify that the request is valid and originates from you as opposed to an unauthorized third party. If you request that your information be deleted, we reserve the right to terminate and/or limit your access to the BuzzBoard website.

If you provide BuzzBoard with user information, you have the following rights with respect to that information:

  • To review the user information that, you have supplied to us
  • To request that we correct any errors, outdated information, or omissions in user information that you have supplied to us
  • To request that your user information not be used to contact you
  • To request that your user information be removed from any solicitation list that we use
  • To request that your user information be deleted from our records
  • To opt out of being solicited by this website or third parties

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@buzzboard.com

Security of your Personal Information
No method of safeguarding information is 100% secure. BuzzBoard uses a variety of security technologies and procedures to help protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. BuzzBoard secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When sensitive personal information (such as a credit card number) is collected on our site and/or transmitted to other websites, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol.

Data Retention
The criteria used to determine the period of storage of personal data is the respective statutory retention period. After expiration of that period, the corresponding data is routinely deleted.
We will retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you wish to cancel your account or request that we no longer use your information to provide you services, contact us at support@buzzboard.com. We will retain and use your information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.
