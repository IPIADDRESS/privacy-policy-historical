Privacy Policy
We are committed to protecting your privacy. We collect information given to us when you place an order. This information is used to process your order, contact you regarding the order if necessary and to provide you with the best possible service. Bunches Florapost collect the information directly and do not disclose buyers information to third parties. All information is stored securely, collected lawfully and in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998.

We will not use your details to contact you regarding future promotions and offers unless you opt-in to such marketing during the order process. Any email marketing we may send will include an unsubscribe option. We do not and will not sell our mailing list to third parties. If you wish to add or remove yourself from our mailing lists at a later date or if you have any questions or comments about privacy you can do so by contacting us by telephone on 0800 626 249 or by emailing us at flowers@bunches.co.uk
