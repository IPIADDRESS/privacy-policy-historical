Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and will never share your personal information with anyone.

At BunnyCDN, we take privacy very seriously. We want to be completely transparent about the information that we store and how we keep it secure. We make great effort to ensure the security of your data using the latest encryption methods.

BunnyCDN does not share any personal information with any third party. We do not store any information about your visits other than the information needed to analyze and optimize the content for the best user reading experience through the use of cookies and Google Analytics.

BunnyCDN does not store any of your personal information about you, except your account settings that you use to log in and the information that you entered to represent yourself.

If you wish, this can be disabled at any time by changing your web browser settings for our website.

This privacy policy is subject to change without prior notice. It was last updated on 12/4/2013. If you require more information or have any questions, fell free to contact us at: support@bunnycdn.com
