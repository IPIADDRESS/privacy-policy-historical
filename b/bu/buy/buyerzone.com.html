Privacy Policy


BuyerZone.com is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not rent, sell, or trade any personal information, including e-mail addresses and other contact information, to outside parties. Such information will only be provided with your consent to vendors to process your purchasing requests, or to legal authorities if necessary to compy with the law.

For research purposes, we may use such information to generate statistics on user activity including transaction and traffic patterns, but these statistics will never include any personally identifying information.

Collection of Information
When you register, we ask you to provide us with your name, email address, size of company, and zip code as well as to create a username and password. This information gives you the ability to use special features of the site and receive special discounts. Your email address is also collected when you choose to participate in contests or promotional events sponsored by BuyerZone.com so that we are better able to administer the contest and notify contest winners. We may notify you of important functionality changes to our website, new BuyerZone.com services or special offers; if you do not wish to receive such information, simply e-mail us by filling out our feedback form.

If you choose to buy a product or service online, you will be required to provide us with credit card information that will be forwarded to the vendor in a safe and secure manner. An encrypted record of this information may also be safely and securely maintained in our database for those users who elect to store this information electronically for future use.

To take advantage of our personalized services, you may be required to provide us with additional information such as your name, name of your company, type of company, company size, email addresses of your employees, telephone number, business and shipping addresses. Collection this information allows us to maintain a user profile for you so that we can customize the content of certain pages for you. Additionally, this process allows users to more quickly submit information to BuyerZone.com as well as more quickly receive product and RFP information.

Changing your user profile
You can always change your personal profile or cancel newsletter subscriptions by going to the appropriate area in MyZone. There, you can if you choose cancel your subscription to our Purchasing Alert newsletter, to which you are automatically subsribed upon registration.

We also may notify members about important functionality changes to the web site or new BuyerZone.com services. If you'd prefer not to receive such mailings, or if you'd like to delete your personal profile entirely, just notify us by filling out our feedback form.

Security measures
BuyerZone.com has implemented a number of security measures to protect your personal information. A user's personal information is protected on secured networks and is only accessible by a limited number of employees who have special access rights to such systems. When you place a purchase request or access your personal information, we offer the use of a secure server. All sensitive/credit information supplied by users is transmitted via SSL and PCP technologies and then encrypted into BuyerZone.com's databases to be only accessed as stated above.

Use of cookies
Cookies are bits of electronic information that a web site can transfer to your hard drive to help tailor and keep records of your visit to the site. Cookies allow us to better customize visits to the site to your individual preferences, helping us provide you with the best possible customer experience on our site. The use of cookies is standard on the Internet and many major web sites use them. Although most web browsers automatically accept cookies, you can change your browser to prevent or notify you whenever you are sent a cookie. This setting gives you the chance to decide whether or not to accept it. Even without accepting a cookie, you can still access most of the features on our site.

Third party links
In an attempt to provide increased value to our visitors, BuyerZone.com may choose various third party web sites to link to from its own site. However, even if the third party is affiliated with BuyerZone.com, BuyerZone.com has no control over these linked sites each of which has separate privacy and data collection practices, independent of BuyerZone.com's practices. BuyerZone.com has no responsibility or liability for these independent policies or actions and is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites. These linked sites are only for your convenience and you therefore access them at your own risk. Nonetheless, BuyerZone.com seeks to protect the integrity of its web site and the links placed upon it and therefore requests any feedback not only on its own site, but for sites it links to as well (including if a specific link does not work). Such feedback should be e-mailed to us by filling out our feedback form.

We always welcome your questions and feedback about our privacy policy. Please e-mail us by filling out our feedback form.
