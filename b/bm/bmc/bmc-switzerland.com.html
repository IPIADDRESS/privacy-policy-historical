At BMC we value your privacy.  This privacy policy describes our privacy practices.

 

 

Information we collect.

Personal information we may collect about our customers or prospective customers may include:  your name, mailing address, e-mail address, telephone number, credit card number or other payment information that you may provide, as well as information about your purchases from us, information you may provide in response to surveys or information you include in correspondence with us.  We also may collect information from third parties to identify prospective customers that may have an interest in bikes and bike products.

 

How we use your information.

We may use the personal information which we collect about you to respond to your inquiries and otherwise correspond with you, to process transactions you request, to maintain your account with us (if you have one), for market research, to conduct customer surveys, and as may be necessary to prevent fraud and maintain the security and integrity of our website, products and services.

In addition, we may use your personal information to communicate with you about products and services that may be of interest to you.  If you do not want to receive marketing communications from us, you can opt-out of receiving future communications, by following the opt-out instructions in our marketing e-mails, unsubscribing from our newsletters, or by contacting us at privacy@no.spam.bmc-switzerland.no.spam.com.  Please understand that if you opt-out it may take us a few days to process your request and that you may receive additional communications in the meantime.

 

Information Sharing.

We may disclose information you provide to us to third parties in order to complete a transaction that you requested.  We may employ other companies and individuals to perform certain services and functions on our behalf that involve access to your personal information.  This may include, for example, operating and maintaining our website, taking orders, delivering packages, sending postal mail and email, removing repetitive information from customer lists, analyzing data, providing marketing consultation and assistance, distributing customer surveys, processing credit card and other payments, and providing customer service.  These companies may have access to personal information needed to perform their services or functions, but may not use it for other purposes.

If our company or our assets are acquired by another company, that company will assume responsibility for the personal information collected by us and the purchaser will assume the rights and obligations regarding the information collected, as described in this privacy policy, or you will be given a choice regarding the transfer of your personal information.

In addition, we may disclose personal information when required by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary in order to comply with the law or legal process or is pertinent to judicial or governmental investigations or proceedings.

 

Security.

We maintain appropriate physical, technical, and administrative safeguards to protect the personal information we maintain about our customers.

 

Access and Correction.

We will provide you with access to personal information we maintain about you as long as we maintain that information in a readily accessible format.   We also will work with you to correct any of your personal information if it is inaccurate.

 

Point of Contact.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy or privacy practices, or if you would like to request access to or correction of personal information we maintain about you, please contact:  privacy@no.spam.bmc-switzerland.no.spam.com.

 

Policy changes.

We may revise our privacy policy from time to time. If so we will post the revised policy on our website: www.bmc-switzerland.com.
