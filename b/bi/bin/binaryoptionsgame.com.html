FAQ
How do I play?
Simply predict the direction for the 15 different pairs and assets on the left, by clicking 1 (DOWN), X (WITHIN RANGE) or 2 (UP) according to what you think the value of the pair or asset will be on the results date exact moment shown on the top-right. Then click SUBMIT.


When can I play?
Participation in each game ends on the submission end date and time shown above. The countdown timer represents the exact time left until the game is closed. All times are in GMT.
Each user is allowed only one participation per game.


How & what do I win?
If on the results date and time, all of your 15 predictions are correct, you win USD 100,000. Please note that in order to receive your prize your email, phone and age verification must all be accurate.
