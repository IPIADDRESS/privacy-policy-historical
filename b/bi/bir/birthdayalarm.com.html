Privacy Policy

We, at BirthdayAlarm.com, understand and respect that your privacy should always remain strictly under your control. It is because of this philosophy that you can be confident that the information you divulge to BirthdayAlarm.com will not be accessible to people whom you have not authorized. Neither will it be sold or otherwise distributed to direct marketers or any other third parties. BirthdayAlarm.com is not a source of unsolicited email.

What personal details do you need to know?

The minimum of information we ask for BirthdayAlarm.com is your name, email address and date of birth.

We will not give out any information without your knowledge.

You may update this information at any time by simply clicking on the 'My Profile' button once you have signed in and then updating the details as you wish.

When is my information given out?

Your name, birthday (optionally including year of birth) and email address is made available to other members who you have agreed should be notified of your birthday.

What do you use my personal details for?

We will use your name so that we can personalise your experience with us. We will use your email address to contact you from time to time and we will use your email address for security reasons to confirm that you are who you say you are.

We also collect other data, which may include your IP address and what browser you are using. We use this information to statistically analyze site usage and to customize our site's content, layout and services. The resulting information will allow us to improve our site and better understand and serve our users. It is compiled and analyzed on an aggregated basis only.

Does BirthdayAlarm.com sell my information to third parties?

No. We will not sell or distribute personal information to any third parties.

How secure is your web server?

Our web servers are locked in cages in an internet data center constructed with double aluminum walls and high-impact resistant glass. This center offers the widest range of physical security features including state-of-the-art smoke detection and fire suppression systems, motion sensors, locked-down floor tiles, as well as video camera surveillance and security breach alarms.

BirthdayAlarm.com uses industry standard technology to keep your personal information as safe as possible. Additionally, we have dedicated non-stop power systems, with multiple back-up generators, in case of power failure. As a further safety feature, all the data is backed-up on a daily basis. Our servers are housed on a dedicated ethernet segment with a dedicated router port for added network security.

Will I be added to any mailing lists?

Absolutely not. Your email address will not be disclosed to any third parties.

Do you send unsolicited emails?

BirthdayAlarm.com will not send any unsolicited information, including email. You will, however receive emails which form an essential part of the service.

What do you use cookies for?

Cookies are a standard internet technology, and many major web sites use them to provide useful features for their members. Cookies allow us to store and retrieve login information on a user's system. They provide us with data that we can use to improve our service to you. If you so choose, you can save your login or password so you don't have to re-enter them each time you visit our site.

Cookies themselves do not personally identify users, although they do identify a user's computer. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies. If you'd prefer, you can set yours to refuse cookies. However, you may not be able to take full advantage of our web site if you do so.

How will I be notified of changes?

We will notify you directly if there is a material change in our privacy practices. We will take commercially reasonable measures to obtain written or active email consent from you if BirthdayAlarm.com is going to be using the information collected from you in a manner different from that stated at the time of collection. We will also post the changes in our privacy statement 30 days prior to a change.

How do I cancel my membership?

You always have the option not to become a member or to cancel your membership at any time.

If you are a member and no longer wish to participate in BirthdayAlarm.com you can sign in to the site and click on the 'My Profile' button on the left hand side menu; then select 'cancel membership' and follow the simple instructions.

This action will cancel your membership and delete your details from the system. You will be assured that, BirthdayAlarm.com will not contact you directly at the email address we have on record for you, other than to respond to requests you may make to BirthdayAlarm.com in the future.
