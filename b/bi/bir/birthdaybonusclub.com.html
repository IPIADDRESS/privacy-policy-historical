Privacy

Our Commitment To Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our homepage and in our back-office.

The Information We Collect

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the Birthday Bonus Club websites.

On applying for membership, you will be asked to supply

  • A unique “username”
  • Your name
  • Your Birth Date
  • Your Gender
  • Your Country of residence
  • Your Email Address

On some pages, you can order products, make requests, and register to receive materials. The types of personal information collected at these pages are:

  • Your Name
  • Your Address
  • Your Phone Number
  • Your Email Address

The Way We Use Information

We use your application information for the purposes of

  • Contacting you when a Birthday Request occurs
  • Contacting you with Birthday Request Reminders
  • Contacting you with occasional newsletters
  • Contacting you with notification of new referrals

We do not share the information gathered with any other party.We use the information you provide about yourself when placing an order only to complete that order. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete that order.

We use return email addresses to answer the email we receive. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties.

We use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design our website and to share with advertisers. For example, we may tell an advertiser that X number of individuals visited a certain area on our website, or that Y number of men and Z number of women filled out our registration form, but we would not disclose anything that could be used to identify those individuals.

Finally, we never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described above without also providing you an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise prohibit such unrelated uses.

Our Commitment To Data Security

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

Our Commitment To Children’s Privacy

Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under 13.

How You Can Access Or Correct Your Information

You can access your personally identifiable information that we collect online and maintain in the My Settings page. Your Username and Birthdate may not be altered; if these are entered in error, please contact us and we will change these data for you.

Your Right to Opt Out

You have the right to opt out of your membership at any time and, on doing so, all data colected from you will be deleted.

How To Contact Us

Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please email us at admin@birthdaybonusclub.com
