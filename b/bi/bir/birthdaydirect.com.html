How secure is my personal information?

Your safe and secure shopping experience is our top priority. We have taken every possible precaution to ensure that your transactions with us are 100% safe. Product Disclaimer

We do our very best to make sure our products are as close to the exact product as possible in both our product images and descriptions. However, certain circumstances may cause actual product size and color to vary from that presented on our website.

Due to the many variations in monitors and browsers, product colors may appear different on different monitors. Computer monitors are not all calibrated equally and color reproduction on the Internet is not precise. Since it is not possible to guarantee our online colors will look the same on all computers, we cannot guarantee that what you see on your monitor accurately portrays the color of the actual product.

Due to manufacturing conditions, actual product sizes may vary slightly from product descriptions. Our objective is to provide you with as much information and detail about the product as possible. Please understand that sometimes typographical errors occur. We will never try to intentionally deceive or misrepresent our products.

If exact size and color matching is of extreme importance, it is highly recommended that you order a "test" product before placing your complete order. We hope you understand these circumstances as it is an issue for all online and catalog retailers.
