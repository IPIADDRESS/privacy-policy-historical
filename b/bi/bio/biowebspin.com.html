Privacy Policy

Biowebspin pays a lot of attention to the security and good use of its members data.

1.Information We Collect and How we Use them

The information that we collect, store and use is exclusively intended to help you build your professional network. When registering or using any of our services you expressly consent to the collection, transfer, manipulation, storage, disclosure and other uses of your profile information as described in this Privacy Policy.

More specifically, we collect information:

-when you register an account to become a Biowebspin member.

-when you view and Biowebspin with Biowebspin community, pages, features, and functionality of Biowebspin or of third party applications and you provide any additional information (groups, files, topics, descriptions…). Providing additional information about yourself beyond what is minimally required at registration is entirely up to you. Any information you provide at registration or in the Profile section may be used by Biowebspin as described in the Terms and Conditions and this Privacy Policy, including for the purpose of allowing professional opportunities to find you on Biowebspin, or for serving more relevant advertisements on Biowebspin.  If you submit information for the propose of publishing on Biowebspin (such as a company or an organization's profile, an event, a document…), you agree that any of this information can be made publicly available to any Biowebspin members or visitors as well as partners of Biowebspin.

-through cookies (files placed on your computer) and other technologies that allow us to recognize you, customize your experience, and serve advertisements both on and off Biowebspin. Our site and third parties such as advertisers may add a cookie to help analyze web traffic or to determine targeted advertisements based on your preferences and your visit to our site and other sites on the internet. You can choose to accept or decline cookies. We use Google analytics to get information on Biowebspin website traffic and Google adsence as an intermediate Advertiser  To opt out of Google’s use of cookies, please visit the Google ad and content network privacy policy. To know more about the use of Google Analytics and Google Adsense, we advise you to read their Terms and Conditions.

Emails however which are based on a specific action of the user (such as account passwords, invoices…  etc.) are sent automatically and cannot be turned off. By using any of our services you agree that these automatically generated emails will be sent to you.

-when you interact with third party services available through Biowebspin

-when you interact with Biowebspin’s customer service (contact form) in order to accurately categorize and respond to customer inquiries and investigate breaches of our terms.

 We do not sell, rent, or otherwise provide personally identifiable information to third parties without your consent except where it is necessary to carry out your instructions (to process your payment information, for example).

 

 

2.Your Information Choices and Rights

You can:

-review, enhance or edit your personal information through your personal profile page and your company or organization if you are the owner and have been given the right to manage your company or organization profile

-control the messages you receive from Biowebspin and other members

-close or tell us to close your Biowebspin account or your company or organization if you are the owner and have been given the right to manage your company or organization profile.

To simplify and not confuse the member, all the information which are posted by the member are public on Biowebspin and on the web (except the private message).
