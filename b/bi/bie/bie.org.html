General
Bie.org is committed to protecting your privacy. Since privacy is of the greatest importance to us, we have created this privacy policy to communicate our practices regarding personal information that can be linked to a specific individual, such as a name, address, phone number or e-mail address. We understand that visitors to our Web site need to be in control of their personal information. All data you provide to us is confidential and will not be sold or shared with a third party. Our policies are designed to provide you with a private and secure means of gaining and using information.

Requesting Information from You
Providing your personally identifiable information to Bie.org is completely optional. Except for downloading curriculum materials, registering for workshops online, and giving us feedback, you are free to use our Web site without providing any personal information.

Cookies
Cookies are small text files that are stored on the end user's hard drive but does not itself contain any personal information. They make our Web site easier for you to use because they save your preferences while you're at other Web sites. Cookies also inform us about which areas are your favorites and the types of technologies and Internet services our visitors are using. Knowing this information helps us continually improve the online experience for our visitors and members. Information transferred through cookies will not be used for promotional or marketing purposes, nor will we share that information with a third party.

Links to Other Sites
At Bie.org, you will find links to Web sites whose content we believe may be of possible interest and value to you. Once you leave our Web site, the use of any information you provide is governed by the privacy policy of the operator of the site you're visiting. We do not necessarily endorse the opinions expressed by the Web site you are visiting outside of Bie.org.
