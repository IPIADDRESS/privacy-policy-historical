Privacy is Personal...
and based on trust. At BIG WORDS, we are making every effort to keep your trust, and are committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information you provide to us to process your order, to service your account, and to improve site content and functionality.

Here's How We Use Your Information.
1. When you visit Big Words.com our Web server recognizes your domain name (e.g. aol.com) not your email address
2. When you order, we need your name, email address, mailing address, credit card number, and expiration date to process, fulfill, and service your order.
3. When you enter a contest or other promotional feature on the site, we usually need your name, address and email address so we can manage the promotion and notify winners.
4. We also monitor customer traffic patterns and site usage to help us improve the content, design, and layout of the site.
5. Occasionally, we may use the information you provide to notify you about important content and functionality changes, new services and special offers that we think you'll find interesting. If you would rather not receive these kinds of information, just email us.

Here's How We Protect Your Private Information.
When you place an order or access your Big Words account, you are using our secure e-commerce server and secure server software (SSL). All the information you input is encrypted before it is sent to us. Furthermore, all customer data submitted to us is protected against unauthorized access. Also, be sure to check out our security guarantee.

OK, what About "Cookies"?
Like most e-commerce and membership sites, we use cookies, which are small bits of information stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. Our cookies do not contain any personal information, but they do remember your settings and your bookbag contents when you change screens to order another book.

Here's How We Handle Third Party Disclosure.
Currently, Big Words does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. However, in the future we may make our customer email list and our name/address list available to other reputable organizations whose products or services we think you might find interesting. If you do not want us to share your email address or your name/address with other companies or organizations, please let us know by email.
