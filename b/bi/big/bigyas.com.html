Children’s Information

We believe it is important to provide added protection for children online.  We encourage parents and guardians to spend time online with their children to participate in and monitor their online activity.  Bigyas does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under 18 year of age. If a parent or guardian believes that Bigyas has in its database the personally-identifiable information of a child under 18 years of age, please contact privacy@bigyas.com and we will use our best efforts to remove the information from our records.
