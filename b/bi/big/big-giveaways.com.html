PRIVACY POLICY
Effective Date: February 14, 2011
  1. Purpose of Privacy Policy
    Thank you for visiting the Free Choice Draw ("Free Choice") website. This Privacy Policy is designed to assist you in understanding how we collect and use the personal information you provide to us, and to help you make informed decisions when you use our website.
  2. What information do we collect?
    When you visit our website, we collect two types of information:
    1. personal information you knowingly choose to disclose to us (e.g., your name, mailing address, email address, and telephone number, which you provide to us during the registration process for the Free Choice); and
    2. website use information collected on an aggregate basis as you and others browse our website.
  3. What do we use your personal information for?
    We may use the personal information we collect from you:
    1. To promote and market products or services offered by us, our sponsors, and/or our clients;
    2. To improve on our website;
    3. To enable us to send periodic emails and/or newsletters, and to contact you via any media pertaining to updates and information on products or services offered by us, our sponsors, and/or our clients;
    4. To enable our sponsors to contact you directly via any media (e.g., email, phone text) regarding their products and services;
    5. To transfer, sell, or assign such information to any entity to which we divest all or a portion of our business, or to our clients as we deem appropriate; and
    6. For any other purpose specified at time of collection.
  4. Will your personal information be passed on to any third parties?
    We may disclose any of your data for identification or convey it to third parties If you provide your consent to us sharing your information when you register or enter our online promotion. For example, with your consent, we may disclose certain personal information to our prize sponsors.

    We may disclose your personal information if required to do so by law, including without limitation, by search warrant, subpoena or court order.

    If you provide information directly to our sponsors, such data will be subject to the respective privacy policies of our sponsors. We have no control over the information you supply directly to our sponsors and we encourage you to consult the privacy policies governing their practices.
  5. Can your personal data for identification be updated, corrected or deleted?
    You can choose to have us delete or deactivate your account by sending an email to info@planet49.us. However, please keep in mind that the deletion or deactivation of your account will limit your user options regarding our range of services to those of a non-member.
  6. What security measures are put in place to protect your personal information against loss, misuse or alteration?
    We take reasonable precautions to keep your personal information secured. We restrict access to your personal information to help prevent unauthorized access, modification, or misuse. However, we cannot assume responsibility for the publication of information due to errors in data transmission or unauthorized access by third parties.
  7. Changes in our Privacy Policy
    From time to time, we may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of any material changes to our Privacy Policy by posting any updated policies here. We encourage you to visit this area frequently to stay informed.
  8. Who to contact?
    Please write to us at info@planet49.us if you have any comments or questions about this Privacy Policy, or if you wish to be removed from our mailing list or report any violations of the Privacy Policy.
