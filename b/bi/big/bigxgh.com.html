This policy covers how BiGxGh treats personal information that BiGxGh collects and receives, including information related to your past use of BiGxGh products and services. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable like your name, address, email address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available. In addition, certain BiGxGh associated companies have their own privacy statements.

Information Collection and Use General 

BiGxGh collects personal information when you register with BiGxGh, when you use BiGxGh products or services, when you visit BiGxGh pages or the pages of certain BiGxGh partners. BiGxGh may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies. When you register we ask for information such as your name, email address, birth date, gender, zip code, occupation, industry, and personal interests. Once you register with BiGxGh and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us. BiGxGh collects information about your transactions with us and with some of our business partners, including information about your use of products and services that we offer. BiGxGh automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, BiGxGh cookie information, and the page you request. BiGxGh uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.

Children 

BiGxGh does not allow a child under age 13 to register for any BiGxGh services. BiGxGh does not ask a child under age 13 for any personal information.

Information Sharing and Disclosure 

BiGxGh does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except to provide products or services you’ve requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances: We provide the information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or with BiGxGh under confidentiality agreements. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information. We respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims; We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of BiGxGh’s terms of use, or as otherwise required by law. In this event, BiGxGh will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
