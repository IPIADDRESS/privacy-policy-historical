Welcome to Bigger Bidder's 2nd Chance Mall

Everybody is a winner with Bigger Bidder.s 2nd Chance Mall.

Every time you use bids in a Bigger Bidder auction that you do not win, you will see your 2nd Chance Cash increase by $.68 per bid that you used.

So if you used 11 bids on an auction that you did not win, you will earn $7.48 in 2nd Chance Cash.

Once you hit $25 in 2nd Chance Cash, you can use this cash credit to make a purchase in Bigger Bidder.s 2nd Chance Mall.

Please note that you must acquire $25 in 2nd Chance Cash to purchase an item. Additionally, you can use only $25 in 2nd Chance Cash per item you purchase.

The $25 in 2nd Chance Cash lowers to cost of the item to the published "You Pay: price. To purchase an item, you must pay the "You Pay" price. This is done by making a PayPal transaction to Bigger Bidder. Simply click the "Buy Now" link for the item you wish to purchase and follow the instructions. You are also responsible for paying applicable shipping fees. Items are typically sent via traceable ground shipment.

Thanks again for bidding with Bigger Bidder!
