The BigFix® technology suite has been designed to preserve your privacy and security at all times. By never sending any information about you or your computer away from your computer, BigFix, Inc. ensures that your private information stays private. The following policy spells out our general commitment to privacy, and covers all our products and our web site.

BigFix, Inc. stipulates the following:

  1. At no time during the process of using this website, or using the BigFix software, will information specific to you or your computer ever be sent off your computer without your express prior permission.

  2. BigFix, Inc. will never sell, rent, or use any information that you voluntarily provide, except for our internal product improvement or development purposes, and then, only in an aggregated, anonymous manner.

  3. To download the BigFix software, you will not be asked to provide any information about yourself.

BigFix, Inc. licenses to other companies, such as Software and Hardware makers, certain technology known as the BigFix® Diagnostic Technology, which enables technical support technicians to obtain specific information about your computer, so they can help diagnose problems you have complained to them about. BigFix, Inc. requires that the use of BigFix Diagnostic Technology by third parties is undertaken only in a manner that requires your permission prior to any such information being transmitted from your computer. In addition, BigFix Diagnostic Technology always allows you to review all such information prior to its transmission.

BigFix, Inc. is absolutely committed to protecting your privacy and security. If you have any questions about this Privacy Statement, please contact us at privacy@bigfix.com. If you have any other questions about BigFix, Inc., please contact us at info@bigfix.com.
