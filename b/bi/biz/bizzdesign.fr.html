Privacy Policy

On several pages on this website, you will be asked to enter your personal data. Personal data is information that can be traced back to individuals, such as telephone numbers, e-mail addresses, business addresses or even home addresses. BiZZdesign takes the privacy of our customers and all other people that are connected with BiZZdesign, through the Website, or in any other way, very seriously. BiZZdesign takes every effort to process and store the personal data that you enter during visits to BiZZdesigns website in such a manner that your privacy is protected and ensured. 

If you choose to get informed about BiZZdesign or about the services and solutions provided by BiZZdesign, you will be asked to enter personal data in order for us to inform you. In addition, with (online) job applications, partner contracts, registration for training courses,  information will be requested such as your name, address, telephone number, particulars of your CV and other information that BiZZdesign needs for processing applications. The information that you submit in such cases is gathered, stored and used to answer your request or review your application, or organize your registration. All information sent to BiZZdesign will be treated confidentially and, unless explicitly stated otherwise, BiZZdesign will not make that information available to third parties, unless required by the law or the judicial authorities. 

In addition to personal data, BiZZdesign uses cookies, if required on special parts of the Website. A cookie is a small file that a server stores on your hard disk and can only be recognized by the server that saved the cookie. A cookie shows our server that you have returned to a particular page on our website. Cookies do not register any personal information such as names, addresses, telephone numbers, e-mail addresses or other particulars that can be traced to you individually. We use cookies and similar tools to gather information during your visit to our website, such as your IP address (a number that is assigned to your computer when you are surfing on the Internet), and the date and the time of your visit to our website. If you so wish, you can configure in your browser that cookies are refused or you are warned when cookies are sent. Information collected by cookies can be used to generate statistics about the website, in order to assess the use of the website anonymously and in terms of total figures and to optimize the website and the solutions and services offered to customers. 

Specific laws in the Netherlands govern these conditions, as well as any disputes or claims arising from or in connection with these conditions. BiZZdesign is entitled to change these conditions without further notification. Any changes will be announced on our website immediately.
