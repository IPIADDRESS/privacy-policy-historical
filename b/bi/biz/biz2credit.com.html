OUR APPROACH TO PRIVACY


Biz2Credit, LLC (“Biz2Credit” or “Company”) values the business relationship with its customers and subscribers and is committed to protecting the privacy of the information you share with us. We strive to provide a consistent and comprehensive set of privacy practices for the global Biz2Credit community In order to give you a better idea of our policies and practices regarding use of your information, this privacy policy (“Privacy Policy”) describes:


  • the information we collect about you

  • how we use that information

  • with whom we share that information

  • how we protect that information

  • your choices about what information you provide to us, and our use and ability to transfer this information

  • how you can access that information

  • how you can contact us with any questions you have relating to privacy or our use of your information, and

  • other information regarding our privacy practices and procedures.

This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected on the Biz2Credit.com website and other primary Biz2Credit websites. Some Biz2Credit-branded websites or other websites we operate may be governed by different privacy policies, which will be clearly indicated by links on the footer of the pages on those sites. The policy that applies on any of our domains or sub-domains is always the policy that appears in the footer of each website page.

By using this website and by accepting the Privacy Policy, Terms of Use and the User Agreement, as you are required to do in registration, you expressly consent to the terms and conditions set forth in this Privacy Policy.



INFORMATION WE COLLECT ABOUT YOU


In General. We collect personal information that can identify you such as your name and email address, as well as other information that does not identify you. When you provide personal information through our website, this information will be sent to Biz2Credit servers located in the United States.


         * Information you provide : We may collect and store any personal information you enter on our website during registration, applications or provide to us in some other manner. This includes identifying information such as your name, home or mailing address, email address, and telephone number); information about your business or prospective business; your financial information (such as your income, assets, or debts), or other personal information. We may also periodically obtain both personal and non-personal information about you from business partners, contractors and other third parties.


         * Information we may request : Examples of information that we may request and/or receive about you include credit reports, information about your business and additional demographic information. However, Biz2Credit will not request or access any credit bureau reports or other credit information without your express prior permission in connection with a business loan.


         * DocVault : Subscribers and customers also have the ability to store and access other documents and information on DocVault, Biz2Credit’s information management system. These documents are protected by Biz2Credit’s web security system and only the user has password-protected access. No third party has access to these documents, other than Biz2Credit for site maintenance and technical support purposes.


         * Other business tools : Biz2Credit offers other proprietary Business Tools to our subscribers and customers, including BizAnalyzer, and Financial Calculator. All information input by our subscribers and customers into these Business Tools are protected by Biz2Credit’s web security system and only the user has password-protected access to any Business Tool where they can input personal information. No third party has access to any such information input into our Business Tools, other than Biz2Credit for site maintenance and technical support purposes. Information input into BizConnect is provided to third party lenders in connection with business loans, but only with the express prior permission of our customer.


         * Mobile applications : Biz2Credit also offers its BizAnalylzer service via Android mobile telephones, which mobile service is protected by both the Biz2Credit security features described above and those of the Android service provider. As above, only the Biz2Credit user has password-protected access to information input into this Business Tool.


         * Information we do not collect : In order to increase customer privacy, Biz2Credit does not collect certain kinds of sensitive personal information. Biz2Credit does not request or collect Social Security Number (SSN) and Tax Identification Number (FIN) for any business owner Biz2credit does not request or collect any bank account information, nor does it link to or access this information. In addition, Biz2Credit does not directly accept credit card information. Biz2Credit uses a third-party payment gateway to accept credit card information for all uses on the site, including subscription fees and the purchase of credit reports and other business information provided by Biz2Credit from partners such as D&B and Equifax. All the credit card information processing, including PCI security standards compliance, is done at the end of the third-party payment gateway, and we do not collect or store any such information.


         * Information about others : We may also collect and store personal information that you provide to us about other people (such as co-applicants), including their name, address, phone number, financial information, or other personal information.


         * Information collected automatically : We use various technologies to collect information from your computer and about your activities on our site, including the use of cookies and other technologies. (see "Cookies" below). We automatically collect information from your browser when you visit our website. This information includes your IP address, your browser type and language, access times, the content of any undeleted cookies that your browser previously accepted from us, and the referring website address.


         * Cookies : When you visit our website, we may assign your computer one or more cookies in order to facilitate access to our website, to personalize your online experience, and to record information about your visit to our website. Through the use of a cookie, we also may automatically collect information about your online activity on our website, such as the web pages you visit, the links or advertisements you click on, or the other actions you conduct while on our website. The cookies do not store any personally identifiable information about you. Most browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies. If you choose to decline cookies, please note that you may not be able to sign in or use some of the interactive features offered on our website. (See “YOUR CHOICES ABOUT COLLECTION AND USE OF YOUR INFORMATION”, below).

We also may include web beacons in promotional email messages or newsletters to determine whether messages have been opened and acted upon. The information we obtain in this manner enables us to customize the services we offer our website visitors in order to deliver targeted advertisements and to measure the overall effectiveness of our online advertising, content, programming or other activities.

Biz2Credit and these companies may use tracking technologies, such as cookies, to collect information about users who view or interact with these advertisements and connect to our website. This information allows Biz2Credit and these companies to deliver targeted advertisements and gauge their effectiveness. Biz2Credit does not share any of your personal information with any third parties except as expressly provided below. (See, “WITH WHOM WE SHARE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION”, below). We share information with companies like D&B and Equifax, lenders or credit agencies only with prior written authorization of our customers to forward such business credit reports, credit monitoring updates or other customer credit or personal information to third parties.

User may not login, create a new account, reauthenticate online, order products, view products or use login help when the account is locked out. As above, the user can contact the Call Center/Help Desk to activate account when account is locked out.

For a more detailed description of these security measures, please refer to our Terms of Use, “Security Measures”.
