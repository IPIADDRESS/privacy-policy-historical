Cookies enable us to track user trends, preferences and patterns to better understand our user base and to improve the quality of our service. Most browsers permit users to refuse cookies or to be notified before a cookie is accepted. You can visit our site without using cookies.

However, we only divulge aggregate information, such as how often the average user visits BidTraffic.com. We will not share personally identifiable information with any third party without your express consent.

Consent and Changes to this Privacy Statement
