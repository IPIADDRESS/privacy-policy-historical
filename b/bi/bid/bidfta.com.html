Privacy Statement

Your privacy is important to 2MC (operating as Fast Track IT). This privacy statement provides information about the personal information that we collects, and the ways in which we uses that personal information.

As you browse bidfta.com or bid.bidfta.com, advertising cookies will be placed on your computer so that we can understand what you are interested in. Our display advertising partner, AdRoll, then enables us to present you with retargeting advertising on other sites based on your previous interaction with bidfta.com or bid.bidfta.com. The techniques our partners employ do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, postal address, or telephone number. You can visit this page to opt out of AdRoll’s and their partners’ targeted advertising.

  • By visiting www.bidfta.com, third parties (such as AdRoll and Google) may place cookies (tracking pixels and related technologies) on your browser for targeted advertising purposes.
  • Some of the types of data we collect for targeted advertising could include IP addresses, cookie identifiers, and website activity.
  • This data may be used to track how you use our site by third parties (such as AdRoll and Google) to target ads to you on other sites based on your online activity. You can opt out of receiving targeted advertising using one of these websites: DAA opt-out site www.aboutads.info or the NAI opt-out site at networkadvertising.org/choices
