Privacy Policy

Introduction

Respecting the privacy of our customers and ensuring the confidentiality of customer information is critical to Next Gen Publishing Ltd. ("NGPL "). The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to ensure that we interact with all of our customers in line with current legislation such as the Information Technology Act 2000 and in accordance with their wishes.

The policy


(1) NGPL will use your contact details (postal address, email address, telephone, mobile and fax numbers) to contact you regarding the product and/or services you have requested. This includes re-registration/renewal notifications, surveys on improving our service to you and/or invitations to exhibitions, seminars and shows.

(2) From time to time NGPL may invite you to register in order to receive magazines, email newsletters, enter competitions, and receive services from NGPL websites. This will require you to confirm personal information such as your name, email address, home and work postcodes. This information is mandatory and is required by NGPL for security purposes and enable NGPL to supply a reasonable level of services to users and our advertisers. We will ask for information such as job function, company sector, company/ name and date of birth. During registration, you will also be asked to choose a password for future access to the relevant site(s).

(3) NGPL offers you the chance to opt in to receive additional information from NGPL and its associated companies by email, telephone and/or SMS. This includes other products/services that NGPL and its associated companies offer and that NGPL feels will be of interest to you.

(4) NGPL offers you the chance to opt in to receive marketing information from other carefully selected organisations by email, telephone and/or SMS. NGPL will only pass on these details if you opt in. NGPL takes all measures possible to ensure that the contact you receive will be from reputable organisations. NGPL however cannot accept any responsibility for use of the data once it has been passed on.

(5) NGPL offers you the chance to opt out of receiving additional information from NGPL and its associated companies by post. This includes all other products/services that NGPL and its associated companies offer.

(6) NGPL offers you the chance to opt out of receiving marketing information from other carefully selected organisations by post. NGPL takes all measures possible to ensure that the contact you receive will be from reputable organisations. NGPL cannot however accept any responsibility for use of the data once it has been passed on.

(7) Aggregate statistical information gathered from NGPL registration forms may be passed on to advertisers or others regarding sales or website usage. At no point will this include personal identifying information.

(8) At all times a recipient of a NGPL product and/or service will have the opportunity to amend their contact details and/or to opt in or out of any of the options listed above in points 3, 4, 5 and 6. You can also request, at any time, to have your personal details flagged on the NGPL databases to ensure you do not continue to receive such information. We will not actually delete your record from the NGPL databases as we would no longer be able to identify you, which could lead to you receiving information from us in the future. To action any of these requests please contact the relevant customer care department of the product/service you subscribe to.

(9) NGPL may make anonymous use of information to help diagnose faults, manage the system and gather broad demographic information about our users in general. This information will be stripped of all details that could identify you personally. For example, we may use IP addresses to help fix performance problems with our server.

(10) NGPL uses all reasonable precautions to keep the personal information disclosed to it secure.

Information collected from site visitors
This statement discloses the privacy policy for NGPL for http://www.bikeindia.in This statement does not extend to sites that are linked to NGPL sites but which are not part of the NGPL network. You should refer to those sites for further details about their use of your personal information. If you use a NGPL website certain information is automatically collected so that delivery can be made of the service. Cookies may be stored on your machine by your browser. You can set your browser to disable cookies, but then you might find some website features will not work. When you request a page from the site's web server, the server automatically collects some information about your system, including your IP address. NGPL collects the minimum information necessary to make the service work - this information is essential to send the pages back across the Internet to the right computer. In the course of serving advertisements to NGPL sites, a third party advertiser may place or recognise a unique cookie on your browser, but it will not collect any personally identifiable information about you. NGPL does not have control over the collection of information for advertising purposes. NGPL is not responsible for the content or the privacy policies of third party web sites linked to this site.

NGPL reserves the right to change this privacy policy at any time by posting changes on this page. It is your responsibility to refer to this privacy policy on accessing this site. NGPL will however draw your attention to any material changes to this privacy policy by placing a notice on the site.
