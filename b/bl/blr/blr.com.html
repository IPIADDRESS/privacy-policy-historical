Privacy Statement
Business & Legal Reports, Inc., (BLR) is committed to respecting your privacy concerns with the information you entrust with us. We give users choice and consent over how their personal information is used, and remove names immediately upon request.

Cookies: We use cookies to help our visitors have a friendlier, more convenient visit. Our purpose in using cookies is NOT to snoop on you. For example, we may use them to greet you by name, to store your password so you don't have to log in each time you visit, or re-enter your shipping and billing information when you re-use our shopping cart. We do NOT collect or use the information from cookies to develop a profile about our shoppers.

Security: We store subscriber and password files with personal information securely. Such files are stored in secure areas not accessible to the public.

Removal: We will immediately unsubscribe anyone who requests that their name be removed from our e-mail lists. To be removed, follow the unsubscribe instructions at the bottom of any e-mail from us, or send an e-mail to service@blr.com with the words "Remove from List" in the subject line.

Product alerts: From time to time, we may also send you news bulletins or product alerts on products or services from Business & Legal Reports, Inc., or other carefully screened companies. Any such message will ALWAYS provide you with a link or other easy means of unsubscribing to further e-mails. Alternatively, you can send an e-mail to service@blr.com at any time with the words "Remove product alerts" in the subject field of the message.

Third party use: Occasionally we make our customer list available to other reputable organizations so you may receive (mail) offers you might find interesting. However your e-mail address will NOT be released to any third party and we will NOT share with others any demographic information that you may provide to us. Sporadically we permit carefully selected organizations to advertise in our e-mail publications, let them sponsor product alerts or will sponsor their product offers. To stop receiving such e-mails, follow the unsubscribe instructions in the e-mail or send an e-mail to service@blr.com at any time with the words “Remove product alerts” in the subject field of the message.

Contact us: At BLR, we understand and respect your privacy concerns. We provide the preceding information concerning our privacy policy so that you may visit our website without being concerned for your personal security. We welcome your comments on this policy.
