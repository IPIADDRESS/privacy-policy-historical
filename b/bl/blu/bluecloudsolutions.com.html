PRIVACY POLICY

Please take a moment to review this Privacy Policy, which tells you among other things and in general terms how Gold Coin Kingdom LLC (d/b/a Bluecloud) (“Bluecloud”, “we” or “us” as the context requires) track, collect, maintain and use information concerning you and your use of this website.

What information do we collect and track? 

The information we collect includes the following:

  • Personal credentials. “Personal credentials” is information that we can use to identify you as an individual. For example, when you register a user account with us as may be required to access certain areas of this website, or later use that account to sign in, we collect information such as your name, your email address, your home address and your telephone number. Likewise, when you purchase any product, services or software from us, we collect other information that you provide for that purpose, such as your credit/debit card information or PayPal account information, full name, billing address, mailing address, telephone number and e-mail address.
  • Information concerning your use and navigation of our website. As you visit, navigate and use our website, we may collect any and all information about such use, including for example the type of Internet browser(s) used, your IP address(es), your computer operating system, the website page from which you linked to arrive at our website, the time spent on one or more pages or features, the links you activate on or website, the frequency and length of time of your visits to the website, the information you input into the our website’s search fields, and so forth
  • Information concerning your use of other applications, websites and services to use our website. We may collect information about your use of other applications, websites and services (like Facebook, for example) to connect or post to or otherwise use our website.
  • Your comments and other content posted to our public website pages. We collect your comments and other content that you post to our website’s public comment pages.
  • Your preferences. We may collect information from you concerning your purchases and how you prefer to use our website – for example, how and whether you prefer to receive communications from us concerning your user account, or marketing information from or about us, or marketing information from or about people and companies with whom we do business.
  • Your location. If you have enabled geolocation services on the device that you use to use our website, our website may collect and use that location information
  • Your calls. Your calls to us may be recorded or monitored for our quality assurance, customer service and other purposes.
  • Other. We may collect information about you by means other than specifically described above – for example, you might email us or write to us with questions or comments. We might ask you for specific information that you provide in response.

We use some or all of the information you provide to us and information concerning your use of our website to provide, maintain and improve our products, applications, and services and software, to display and provide advertising to you via our website, and for other purposes related to our current or prospective businesses. As examples, we may use it to do the following:

  • provide the services, products, application or information you request, and process such requests and related transactions;
  • send you confirmations, receipts, updates, alerts, and support and administrative messages and otherwise enable your use of, and our administration and operation of, our website;
  • notify you about important changes to our website;

  • request feedback and otherwise contact you about your use of our website;
  • respond to your emails, submissions, questions, comments, and requests and to provide customer service;
  • monitor and analyze website usage and trends, personalize and improve our website and our users’ experiences with it (such as providing content or features suggested to us by the information that you provide), and increase our website’s functionality and ease of use;
  • help us remember and process the items in your shopping cart or that you’ve purchased or may like to purchase;
  • serve ads, on our website, via other websites or media, based on the information that you provide and your uses of the website; and
  • we may store and process information in the United States and in other countries.

It is our policy not to disclose or share your personal information to other people or companies, except under one or more the following circumstances:

  • You permit or ask us to do so. For example, our website may allow you the opportunity to opt into receipt of advertising and marketing information from people with whom we do business. Opting in to such opportunity permits us to share your personal information for such purposes. If you provide your personal information to a third party via our website, or to us for the benefit of or delivery to a third party (for example, an advertiser on our website), you have permitted and asked us to do so.
  • We provide information to others who assist us in providing our website, applications, products and services to you. We provide your information to others who render services to us and assist us with providing our website, applications, products and services to you. For example, we may engage third party software developers, data storage and disaster recovery vendors, systems architecture designers. In such cases, we require them to acknowledge and abide by our Privacy Policy.

We reserve the right to provide any and all information concerning you and your use of the website as required by law or requested by law enforcement officials – for example, in response to a legal subpoena or a request prompted by a criminal investigation. Without limiting or committing us to a particular course of action in this regard, we may use and disclose your information when we have a good faith belief that access, use, preservation or disclosure of your information is reasonably necessary to (a) satisfy any requirement of law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request; (b) enforce or investigate an actual, possible or threatened violation of our Terms of Use, (c) detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security or technical concerns, or (d) protect the rights, property, and safety of us, our agents, our staff and our users. There are occasions where we may ask you to voluntarily submit additional information about yourself, such as in a form requesting your name, address and telephone number, and email address. We may request this information to enable you to order products, applications and services via the website and for other similar business purposes. We give you the opportunity to elect not to receive future materials from us.

When we deliver ads based on information about your use of the services, it is our policy to keep your identity anonymous and not to identify you personally to third parties, except in accordance with this privacy policy. You may receive email or other messages from us from time to time – for example, automated notifications you requested of updates to discussion forum threads or property address profiles. You may unsubscribe or opt out of receiving electronic correspondence from us either modifying your user account preferences or following any instructions in such electronic correspondence as to how to unsubscribe or opt out from that list. We always reserve the right to contact you from time to time about matters concerning your user account.

Cookies and other use tracking technologies

Cookies are text files that our website intentionally places on your computer. Cookies enable us to relate your use of our website to information that you have provided to us. For example, we may use cookies to track user traffic patterns and to store information that enables our website to remember you and what you purchased when you return to our website. You can refuse cookies by turning them off in your browser. If you’ve set your browser to warn you before accepting cookies, you will receive the warning message with each cookie. You do not need cookies turned on to use our website, but shutting them off could impede or prevent your uses of our website as we intend it to operate. Please refer to your browser instructions for information regarding deleting, blocking, and receiving warnings about cookies.

We also might use web beacons, which usually are small, invisible graphic images that may be used on our website or in emails relating to our website to collect information about usage of the website and whether emails have been received and opened.

Flash cookies might be used in connection with certain videos played via our website. Removing and rejecting browser cookies does not necessarily affect third party flash cookies used in connection with our websites. For more information about flash cookies and how you might remove or disable them, please consult Adobe directly at: http://www.adobe.com/products/flashplayer/security for more information

Third parties that advertise on our website also might use cookies and other tracking technologies. We do not take responsibility for those third party devices, and for this.

How we seek to secure your information

We seek to secure your personal information from unauthorized use, access and disclosure using efforts that are reasonable in our judgment. We do not necessarily use the best efforts ever imagined, nor do we use all possible devices, methods or systems in this regard. Even if we did, none ever should be regarded as perfect or impenetrable. Accidents happen. Sometimes people do not do what they are supposed to do, whether due to carelessness, laziness or worse. Accordingly, despite our reasonable efforts described above, your information could fall into the wrong hands and be used or disclosed in ways you wouldn’t want it to be used or disclosed. Please help us keep your user account and personal information secure by using a strong (!) password and not sharing it with others or keeping it someplace where someone can find it. We will never ask you for your password, so do not give it to someone claiming to be us if you are asked for it.

You are responsible for all uses of our website by any person using your password. If you know or suspect that your password has been stolen or compromised, or that your user account has been accessed without your authorization, please change your password immediately and notify us about it.

Payments and Secure Socket Layer (SSL)

All purchases must be made by credit card, debit card, or payments via PayPal.  All billing related information is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology and then encrypted into a payment gateway provider’s database.  Such information is accessible only by those authorized with special access rights to such systems, and they are required to keep that information confidential. After the completion of a transaction, all payment related information, such as credit card numbers, will not be stored on our servers.

In addition and consistent with the Federal Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), we never will knowingly request personal information from anyone under the age of 13 years. If we discover that a child under the age of 13 years has provided us with personal information, we will endeavor to delete that information.
