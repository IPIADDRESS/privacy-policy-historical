Privacy Policy Page

blue sky scrubs™ Privacy Policy

Updated December 8, 2010

Our Commitment To Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our homepage and at every point where personally identifiable information may be requested.

The Information We Collect

For each visitor to our site, blueskyscrubs.com automatically recognizes the customer's IP address, not the individual's email address. Email addresses are collected from those who purchase products via this web site and sign up for our email campaigns. We also collect all information required for transaction purposes: direct contact, billing, shipping, and payment information. All information transmitted to blueskyscrubs.com is done so securely: look for the closed lock (security) symbol at the bottom of the checkout pages.

How We Use Information

blue sky scrubs, LLP is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We do not sell or rent this information to anyone, and do not share your information with any third party. We will use your information for transactional purposes as well as to communicate with you regarding orders, questions, suggestions, etc.

Our Commitment To Data Security

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

How To Access Or Correct Your Information

You can access all your personally identifiable information that we collect online and maintain by contacting blue sky scrubs directly. We use this procedure to better safeguard your information.

You can correct factual errors in your personally identifiable information by sending us a request that credibly shows error.

To protect your privacy and security, we will also take reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting access or making corrections.

You may log into your account to update your billing and shipping information, telephone number, email address, and account password.

If you no longer wish to receive our newsletters, you may unsubscribe here or by contacting us, as indicated below.

How To Contact Us

Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, or if you would like for us to update information we have about you or your preferences, please contact us as indicated below: Write to: blue sky scrubs 1906 W Koenig Lane Austin, TX 78756 Telephone: 888.302.5837 Email: operations@blueskyscrubs.com
