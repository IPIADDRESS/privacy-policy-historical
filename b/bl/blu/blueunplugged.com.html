Privacy Policy

Blue Unplugged Ltd. is committed to providing users with an Internet experience, which respects their privacy. We adhere strictly to the requirements of the UK Data Protection Act.

  • Web Usage information to develop and improve the Web site.
  • Registration information to keep you informed.
  • Name, address, email, and credit card details to process your order.

How we use the information provided.

  • To process your order and administer your account

Data Security
All the data we collect is stored on a secure server and will not be passed outside Blue Unplugged Ltd. The secure server software encrypts all the information you input before it is sent to us.

What are "cookies" and how do we use them?
"Cookies" are small pieces of information stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. No recognisable personal information is stored in the "cookie". We use "cookies" and your registration details to identify your shopping basket and personalise your experience at our site.
