Privacy Policy

BluePay Inc. (BluePay) is committed to safeguarding the privacy and security of the information we collect. We have implemented multiple security measures to ensure the protection of your information including secure servers and networks that are accessible to a limited number of employees and industry standard encryption technologies when transferring or receiving sensitive information.

Collection of Information

When visiting Web sites, you provide information to the site owners based on where you go and what you do at each site. BluePay collects and retains this information for business purposes only. It is used to improve the quality and functionality of our site as well as to respond to requests for more information on all of the products and services we offer. This information is collected through Web server logs and cookies that allow us to track the aggregated statistics and data from our visitors as they utilize our Web site.

We use FetchBack, Inc. to display retargeted ads to our website visitors across the Internet. Fetchback collects non-personally identifiable information about your visits to our site and your interaction with our products and services. The anonymous information is collected through the use of a pixel tag, which is industry-standard technology used by most major websites. No personally identifiable information is collected during this process.

E-Mail Lists

BluePay Inc, maintains e-mail lists to keep interested parties informed about our company, events, products, support, and more. Emails are typically sent out twice per month, and contain valuable information for our merchants, agents, partners, and other interested parties. Our E-newsletters often contain Web beacons, which collect information such as a cookie identifier and the time and date that the email was viewed or opened. They do not collect any private or identifiable information, but allow us to track how many people opened our E-newsletter. We do not sell the addresses on our list, but we may make information available to specific business partners, sponsors, or service providers.

Users may voluntarily request to join our mailing lists by signing up or opting in through the E-newsletter Sign Up form on our Web site. To be removed from our mailing list, simply email us now or click the unsubscribe link provided in each E-newsletter.

Personal Information

We do not collect personal information from you unless you provide it to us in conjunction with an application for a merchant account or any other service we offer. The information collected is used for the underwriting, approval and set up required for the service you request. We do not license, sell, rent or trade any collected business information about your account or other personal information unless you request it, the information is provided to help complete a transaction initiated by you, or the disclosure is otherwise lawfully permitted or requested.

Third Party Links

In an attempt to provide increased value to our visitors we may have various third party Web sites link to our site. We do not control these sites and our Privacy Statement does not apply to them. These linked sites are for your convenience and your use of them is subject to each individual Web site’s Privacy Statement and Terms of Use. Please review the Privacy Statement from each of those Web sites before browsing them.

Minors

BluePay does not market to or solicit data from minors. The services we provide are for businesses with owners and principals 18 years of age or older.

At BluePay we value keeping information private and secure. Bluepay is a member of the BBB (Better Business Bureau) Online Reliability Program. We reserve the right to update and amend this privacy statement at any time, without notification to our users.
