Blue Global Media Privacy Policy

Blue Global Media (Blue Global, LLC) operates BlueGlobalMedia.com in order to provide advertising and marketing services over the internet to publishers, advertisers and other clients. Blue Global Media (“we,” “us,” “our”) refers to the company and “you” (“yours”) refers to visitors of BlueGlobalMedia.com and our affiliated sites.

Blue Global Media is the sole owner of the content and resources provided by our website, as well as the information collected by the website. Visitor information is collected at various points on BlueGlobalMedia.com. Information is collected through contact forms and applications and may be used to contact visitors if we require extra information to process your requests.

Information Use

Information is collected through the use of cookies, log files and other non-identifying data. We do not sell, distribute or otherwise unethically handle personally identifiable information. Cookies are pieces of data that reveal information about which websites have been visited through your Internet browser. This information does not reveal personally identifiable data and is only used to track visitor activity. Log files are standard online data entries that record Internet protocol (IP) addresses and related information.

Advertiser and Publisher Information

The information provided by our website is available to the general public. However, to enroll in our services, you are required to become an advertiser or publisher to receive appropriate information. Applying as either advertiser or publisher does not guarantee approval and is subject to review. An application must be completed in full in order to be accepted. Applicants who do not meet specific criteria will not be approved or admitted into the affiliate network.

Application Information

When applying as an advertiser or publisher, you will be asked to submit your basic personal and contact information. Additional information is required to become approved for the network. The information revealed in an application is stored safely on the company’s information databases and is never access by unauthorized third parties. If you do not wish to submit your personal information, please do not complete or send an application.

Information Disclosure

We do not disclose personal information unless required by law or other outstanding circumstance. We make all efforts to effectively store, protect and preserve your privacy and personal information. Some customer information may be shared with our advertising partners in order to provide a better user experience. In order to operate as an affiliate network, we must provide information to both publishers and advertisers to offer and maintain services.

Notification of Changes

The privacy policy may be changed at any time and without notice. Changes made to the privacy policy will be effective immediately upon posting. Although we take every effort to ensure the accuracy of our content, we do not guarantee that the information provided by this website is timely or accurate. Changes may be made to any area of the website without prior notification. However, all changes will be made in accordance with our privacy policy and general business practices.
