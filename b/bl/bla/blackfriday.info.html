Privacy Policy


Last updated July 20, 2011

This privacy policy discloses the privacy practices of BlackFriday.info. We are committed to user privacy, and advise you to read the following to understand what information we collect from you and how we use that information. If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please email us.

By accessing BlackFriday.info, you agree that you have read and understand the Privacy Policy below. By using BlackFriday.info, you consent to the use, storage and disclosure of your Information by us as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms and conditions outlined within this document, please do not use the Site.


Information We Collect

We collect Personal Information only when provided to us by our Users. This includes names and email addresses as provided by the User.

Children under the age of 18 may not use BlackFriday.info. We do not collect or maintain information from anyone known to be under the age of 18. No part of the Site is designed to attract anyone under the age of 18.


How We Use Your Information

We only use Personal Information for the purpose it was provided for: to receive our Black Friday email updates and/or to participate in one of our Black Friday contests. Users have the opportunity to Opt Out of email alerts following signup (see options below).

You may submit requests to BlackFriday.info for changing or deleting Personal Information by sending us an email.


User Security

We take your security very seriously and take precautions to protect Personally Identifiable Information that you provide to us. Any Personally Identifiable Information resides on a secure server that only selected personnel and contractors have access to.


Email Opt Out

Users always have the opportunity to opt out of receiving future communications from BlackFriday.info. Every email we send to registered members contains an Unsubscribe link, alternately, you can request that we remove you from our mailing list by emailing us.


Disclosure of Information to Third Parties

When appropriate BlackFriday.info may share demographic and/or aggregate information with our third party advertisers. At no time does such information contain any personal identifiers. BlackFriday.info will only disclose personally identifiable information when required by law, in the event of judicial proceeding, criminal investigation, or court order served on us.


Sale or Transfer of User Information

In the event of the sale, liquidation or dissolution of BlackFriday.info, User Information may be sold or transferred to the party acquiring the Site. By providing your personal information to BlackFriday.info, you consent to the sale and transfer of your information during such an event. At any time you are free to Opt Out of any communications from us or ask that we delete your Information from our database.


Notification of Changes to the Site

We will notify our Users of any changes to this Privacy Policy, changes to how Personal Information is collected, or the sale, liquidation or dissolution of BlackFriday.info. A notice will be posted on BlackFriday.info within ten business days prior to any changes taking effect. An email will also be sent to our registered newsletter readers prior to any changes, allowing them to Opt Out at that time.


Third Party Linking

At times we may include appropriate links to websites of both affiliated and non-affiliated entities within our content, or as a product or service link. When a User clicks on a third party link on BlackFriday.info, he or she leaves our site. BlackFriday.info is not responsible for any information provided on third party websites. Please ensure you understand the terms of service and privacy policy of each third party site before you submit Personal Information.
