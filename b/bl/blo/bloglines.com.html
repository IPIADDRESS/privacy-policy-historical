What Information Is Collected?

We may collect information from you through our website, when you download our products, and when you purchase or register for our products and services. Below are the specific details on what, where, when, and why this information is collected and how it is used.

Registration

The term "personally identifiable information" means any information that may be used to identify you, including, but not limited to, your name, address, email address, Internet Protocol address, phone number, billing information, or other contact information. You may be asked for certain personally identifiable information when registering for or accessing a particular product or service. We do not share this information with any third parties.

When Purchasing Bloglines Products and Services

When you purchase a Bloglines product or service from us, we collect standard payment information, including your credit card number and billing address. We use this information to process your purchase of our product or service. We do not share this payment information with any other companies or third parties. We use Secure Sockets Layer encrypted connections to transmit your payment information, including your credit card number. We store your payment information in encrypted form and we limit the employees that can access this information. In addition, we require that all of our employees and contractors adhere to a confidentiality policy.

Bloglines Database

Bloglines is used to subscribe to RSS feeds. We store this subscription information in our database. We use this information in aggregate to generate statistics and other usage information. We share this information with you, other end users, and other third parties.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored by your browser on your computer when you visit a website. We use cookies to improve our website and make it easier to use. Cookies permit us to recognize you and avoid repetitive requests for the same information. Most browsers will accept cookies until you change your browser settings to refuse them. You may change your browser?s settings to refuse our cookies. However, if you do so, you may not be able access certain Bloglines products and services.

At times, we must disclose relevant personally identifiable information about you to our contractors so they may provide these specific services for us. We require our contractors to keep this information confidential and to only use the information on our behalf.

Periodically, we may send email to you if you have indicated a preference to receive news, updates, special offers, and other information relating to our products and services. You may unsubscribe from these email subscriptions at the profile web page for your account on our website.
