Information we collect and how we use it

Information you provide

Once you allow the site to connect with Facebook, you will be able to use your Facebook login information to log into your bloson account. By using Facebook Connect, you permit bloson to access certain Facebook information related to you, including your profile information, friends and privacy settings. You may also display personal information in order to get more points or promotions or to advertise your interests. Providing additional personal information is voluntarily and not necessary for using the Site. Additional personal information you provide maybe edited or deleted at any time.

Information we collect

Bloson only collects personal information about users that we consider necessary for providing our services in an efficient, customized and user-friendly manner. In addition to the information you provide, our servers automatically record information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website. Such information includes the user's IP address, browser type, browser language, the date and time of your request and the URL that you come from or go to (whether this URL is on the site or not). We use this information to do internal research on our users' demographics, interests, and behavior, in order to serve our users better. We may also collect and store information based upon your behavior on the Site, such as how long you use the services, videos or songs you enjoy and share with friends on bloson, your browsing history on bloson (including any links that you may click within the Site), your preferences, your search history, and any social connections you have created on the site.

Cookies

We use data collection devices such as "cookies" on the bloson Site in order to improve the quality of our service, to store user preferences, to improve product and service recommendations and ad selection, and to track user trends. Cookies are small pieces of data that are placed on your hard drive. Most cookies are "session cookies," meaning that they are automatically deleted from your hard drive at the end of a browser session. You are always free to decline our cookies if your browser permits, although if you disable the cookies you may not be able to use certain features and services offered on the Site. Other websites may use cookies. We have no access to or control over these cookies. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by bloson only and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers or third parties.
