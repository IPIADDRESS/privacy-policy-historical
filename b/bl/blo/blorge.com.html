Privacy statement

This purpose of this privacy statement is to let you know what information is collected about your visit to this site, and how the information is used.

Statistical information about your visit

When you visit BLORGE.com, our server makes a record of your visit and logs the following information for statistical purposes: the date and time of your visit, your server address, the previous site you visited, your top-level domain name (for example, .com, .gov, .au, .nz), the pages you accessed, and the type of browser you used.

No attempt is made to identify individual visitors or their browsing activities, except in the unlikely event of an investigation, where a law enforcement agency may exercise a warrant to inspect the our logs.

Your email address

Your email address is only recorded by BLORGE.com when you send a message, comment, or subscribe to our email newsletter or RSS feed. It will only be used for the purpose for which you provided it. BLORGE.com will not use your email address for any other purpose, and will not disclose it or add it to a mailing list, or disclose it to third parties, without your consent.

Personal information

Some parts of BLORGE.com provide electronic forms that allow you to supply information, for example, to subscribe to our email newsletter.

BLORGE.com does not collect or solicit any personal information that might be unlawful, unnecessary or unrelated to the functions or activities of the site, or unfair or unreasonably intrusive.

BLORGE.com will not supply your email address to any third parties without your prior consent.

Publishing personal information

BLORGE.com will only publish personal information on BLORGE.com if it has been collected for this purpose with your knowledge, or if you have consented to the disclosure.

When giving such consent you should be aware that information published on the BLORGE.com is accessed by hundreds of thousdands of people each month, that it will be indexed by search engines, and that it may be copied and used by any web user.

This means that once information is published on BLORGE.com, we have no control over its subsequent use and disclosure.
