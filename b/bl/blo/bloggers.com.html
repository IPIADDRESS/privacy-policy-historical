Privacy

Our Privacy police is simple

We believe that your data is very important to you. For that reason we won't sell your name, email or any information to anyone. We won't share any capture data with anyone. That's it.

We use the registration data with the purpose of classify and show this information in the format of a Internet directory. Your personal information won't be sell or shared in any way with other entities, your information is yours and Bloggers.com respect that right.

If you want to delete your account, drop us a line -using our Contact Form- and your weblog, and any related information about you, will be erased from our database without hesitation.
