Privacy Policy

At Broadbandbuyer.co.uk we are committed to protecting your privacy online. Our Privacy Policy demonstrates our commitment to protecting the privacy of our customers. We only use the information in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998. Information collected about our customers is used to fulfil purchases and to create a more personalised online experience.

When you shop at Broadbandbuyer.co.uk, we collect personal information from you such as your name, telephone number, e-mail address, delivery/invoice address and a password. Broadbandbuyer.co.uk will not disclose your information to any other third party organisation. Broadbandbuyer.co.uk uses customer information only for the purposes of filling orders, contacting purchasers to inform them of their order status, sending promotional information, and enhancing the user experience.

Broadbandbuyer.co.uk uses cookies to help identify you when you visit the site. The cookies make your use of the site easier, allow you to retrieve an earlier shopping basket, make the site run more smoothly and help us to maintain a secure site At Broadbandbuyer.co.uk you can be assured that your purchasing is secure.

All Transactions and credit card information are processed through Lloyds Cardnet.
