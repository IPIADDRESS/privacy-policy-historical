HOW DOES NETBROADCASTER.COM USE MY PERSONAL INFORMATION?
NetBroadcaster.com's goal in collecting personal information is to provide you, the user, with the most personalized web experience possible and our advertisers with an efficient means to reach the right audience. By knowing a little about you NetBroadcaster.com is able to deliver more relevant content and advertisements, and hence a better service to you. Advertisers give us an ad and tell us the type of audience they want to reach (for example, females over 24 years old). NetBroadcaster.com. takes the ad and displays or emails it to users meeting those criteria.
