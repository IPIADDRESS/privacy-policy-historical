PRIVACY STATEMENT

Overview

We know that privacy on the World Wide Web is an important concern to our customers. We use the information we collect to better understand your preferences, respond to your needs and enhance your overall experience on our website. We do not sell, trade or rent your personal information to others and do not divulge information except as outlined in this privacy policy. (See Sharing)

The information we request, such as your name, mailing address and e-mail address will be used to fulfill your requests for information and assistance regarding Brother products and services. If you indicate an interest in receiving updates we may periodically send you information about other Brother products and services that we feel may be of interest to you. If your specific request requires assistance from one of our selling partners or service providers, we may pass your contact information to them for follow-up however, your information will otherwise be used only by Brother International Corporation, its parent and subsidiary companies. We will not sell or make your information available to other companies who want to tell you about their products without your consent. As our privacy policy continues to evolve we will continue to place the latest version on this website.

This privacy statement policy has been created to explain how Brother International Corporation collects and utilizes information obtained from your site visits. Information Collection and Use

Information Collection

Brother International Corporation collects information from our users at several different points on our Website:

Registration

In order to use some areas of this Website, a user must first complete a registration form. During registration a user is required to give contact information (such as name and email address). We use this information to contact the user about services on our site for which he has expressed interest. In most instances, it is optional for the user to provide information, and unique identifiers (such as, username and password), but encouraged so we can provide a more personalized experience on our site.

Request for Services

This site contains several areas where a visitor may request services (for example to place an order or request operational assistance) offered by Brother or to provide feedback. To fulfill these requests Brother may collect some required information as well as optional information that will help us satisfy your requests.

Profile

We store information we collect through registrations, cookies, log files, clear gifs, and/or third parties to create a profile of our users. A profile is stored information that we keep on individual users that details their viewing preferences. Consequently, collected information is tied to the users personally identifiable information to provide offers and improve the content of the site for the user. This profile is used to tailor a user's visit to our Website, and to direct pertinent marketing promotions to them. Select information from your profile may be shared together with 3rd parties if required to satisfy your request. (See "Sharing" below.)

Cookies

A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's computer tied to information about the user. We use both session ID cookies and persistent cookies. For the session ID cookie, once users close the browser, the cookie simply terminates. A persistent cookie is a small text file stored on the user's hard drive for an extended period of time. Persistent cookies can be removed by following Internet browser help file directions.

Cookies may be used to customize delivery of content or enable special convenience features on our site. For example, by registering for our site, a cookie may be set so that users would not have to log in a password more than once or re-enter their contact information into forms, thereby saving time while on our site. If users reject the cookie, they may still use our site but may have to log in again to access some areas of our site. For example, the user will not be able to take advantage of materials available in our download areas without registering and accepting the resultant cookie. Persistent cookies enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance the experience on our site. See the "Profile" section.

Log Files

Like most standard Website servers, we collect and analyze log files. Information collected includes anonymous data like IP addresses, browser types, referring pages, pages visited and time spent on our site. This information is used in aggregate to analyze and improve our websites. IP addresses, etc. are not linked to personally identifiable information. (Please note that while IP addresses can be tied to personally identifiable information, this is not a practice we utilize at this time.) We use Web Trends software to analyze site activity but not to capture personal information. (More information on Web Trends can be obtained from their website: http://www.webtrends.com)

Security

This Website takes every precaution to protect our users' information. When users submit sensitive information via the Web site, their information is protected both online and off.

When our registration form asks users to enter personal information (such as name address, phone, e-mail, etc.), that information is encrypted and is protected with the best encryption software in the industry - SSL. While on a secure page, such as our order form, the lock icon on the bottom of Web browsers such as Netscape Navigator and Microsoft Internet Explorer becomes locked, as opposed to un-locked, or open, when users are just 'surfing'.

Sharing

These are the instances in which we will share users' personal information:

In specific instances where a visitor's request requires action by one of our staff, Partners or Service Providers, Brother International Corporation may forward or provide access to information from the customer's profile. For example, to fulfill a request for quotation, Brother may forward the pertinent details such as contact information, intent to purchase (time, quantity, models, etc.) as well as any special requests. Your information is forwarded only for this specific one-time use and once transferred is subject to the policies of Brother International Corporation.

For some marketing activities or service related surveys, Brother may enlist the help of a 3rd party provider who may temporarily need to handle your information but will not be given rights to use for non-Brother purposes. For example, during a direct mail campaign, where we may send you announcements per your permission preferences, we may use a 3rd-party mailing house. In this case mailing lists are transferred to the 3rd party for the specific purpose of addressing the marketing materials however this personal information may not be used by the 3rd party for any other use. 

Legal Proceedings

Though we make every effort to preserve user privacy, we may need to disclose personal information when required by law wherein we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a current judicial proceeding, a court order or legal process served on our Website.

Aggregate Information (non-personally identifiable)

We share aggregated demographic information with our partners and advertisers. For example we may, on a periodic basis, present information such as the number of visitors, areas of interest, relationship to Brother, etc. This is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

Links

This Website may contain links to other sites. Please be aware that we, Brother International Corporation, are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such other sites. We distinguish these 3rd party web sites by methods such as either opening them in a separate browser window or within a frame indicating the area within is not part of our site. In these instances the privacy policy of Brother International Corporation website will not apply. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every Website that you visit. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this Website.

Surveys & Contests

Occasionally our site may invite users to participate in surveys or contests. Participation in these surveys or contests is completely voluntary and the user therefore has a choice whether or not to disclose information. The requested information typically includes contact information (such as name and shipping address), and demographic information (such as zip code). Contact information will be used/shared with the contest or survey sponsors to notify the winners and award prizes. Survey information will be used for purposes of monitoring or improving the use and satisfaction of this site. Users' personally identifiable information is not shared with third parties unless we give prior notice and choice. Though we may use an intermediary to conduct these surveys or contests, they may not use users' personally identifiable information for any secondary purposes.
