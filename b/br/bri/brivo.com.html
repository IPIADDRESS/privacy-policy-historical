At Brivo™, we are firmly committed to safeguarding your privacy.

What information do we collect?
We may ask for information about you when you create a username and password or when you sign-up for Brivo services. In order to provide you the product and service you are requesting we will need certain information - such as name, email, phone number, address, and credit card number. We may also use that information to update you on company news and additional Brivo products and services. You can choose not to receive such information at the time you register with Brivo.

Brivo may use cookies or other technologies to provide you a better and more personalized service. Specifically, we use these technologies to estimate audience size, visitor patterns in the aggregate, and to monitor overall site usage.

What will happen to customer information?
We will not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third party, unless approved or requested by you or if we believe that we are legally required to do so. However, we may share aggregated, non-personal information about the use of Brivo services with our partners, and under confidentiality agreements we may match user information with third-party data.

How do we protect customer information?
Data transmitted from your computer to Brivo is encrypted, so sensitive information such as credit card numbers and Keycodes are secure. Like many web sites, data stored at Brivo is securely located behind a firewall.

How can you be removed from our customer list?
If you would like to removed from our customer list, please email us at privacy@brivo.com.

Send us your comments!
If you have any comments, suggestions, or complaints in regards to our privacy statement, please email us at privacy@brivo.com.
