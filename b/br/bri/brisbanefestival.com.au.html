Privacy Policy

Major Brisbane Festivals Pty Ltd,  the organiser of the Brisbane Festival, is bound by the National Privacy Principles under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

Protecting your privacy is important to us. At all times, we will take care to ensure that your personal information is protected and treated confidentially and in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988.

Whenever we collect personal information from you, whether electronically or otherwise, we will make every reasonable attempt to ensure the privacy of that information and that it will only be used for the purposes for which it was collected. In general, the purposes for which we collect your personal information include:

  • Placing you on mailing lists either electronic or postal;
  • To inform you of festival news or upcoming events;
  • Submitting entries to events or competitions; and
  • To assist with enquiries you may have about the festivals.

The Company will not disclose any personal information to any third parties without first obtaining your consent unless such disclosure is required by law. However, to ensure the purposes for which we collect the information are fulfilled, the information maybe disclosed to other companies involved in the organising and conducting of the festivals.

If you believe that we have not dealt with your personal information in a manner that complies with the Privacy Act you should contact us to discuss how we may better deal with it. Similarly, if you wish to access personal information that we hold about you, you may make a request in writing.
