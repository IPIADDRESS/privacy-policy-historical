Bragg Statement of
User Information Privacy

Our commitment to your privacy:
To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our homepage and at every point where personally identifiable information may be requested.


The information we collect:
This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the Bragg web site. On some pages, you can order products, submit questions, requests or correspondence, or register to receive materials.


Information that is collected when you order online:
This product and book, online shopping information is submitted to us through the secure servers of AuthorizeNet - the credit card processing agency we use. AuthorizeNet is accessible at www.authorizenet.com. Please review their site for any questions you might have about processing a credit card order on-line.

The information that is collected on the order pages is used to verify and approve your credit card for the sale and provide proper information for shipping your order. We will not provide any of that information to sources outside Bragg Live Foods.


Information that is collected when you request free health info, and/or write to us either via the USPS or through electronic mail:
We use return e-mail addresses to answer the e-mail we receive, and street addresses for written returns. We make rare mailings of important new info about Bragg, its products and services, and will keep you active on our contact list. You can, of course, request no further contact by either writing to us via mail (at the address below) or by sending an e-mail to info@bragg.com. Please be clear, in the body of your note, as to the action you wish taken.


To whom your information is available and how it's used:
Your information will not be sold or shared with any outside parties. Your information is used primarily to fulfill your request for Bragg Products, Books and Information.

We may use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design our web site and to share with advertisers in a summary form, which presents no individual persons info. For example, we may tell an advertiser that X number of individuals visited a certain area on our web site, or that Y number of men and Z number of women filled out our registration form, but we would not disclose anything that could be used to identify those individuals.

Finally, we will never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described above without also providing you with an opportunity to opt-out, or otherwise prohibit, such unrelated uses.


Our Commitment To Data Security:
To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.


How You Can Access Or Correct Your Information:
You can access all your personally identifiable information that we collect online and maintain by written request. We use this procedure to better safeguard your information.

You can correct factual errors in your personally identifiable information by sending us a request that credibly shows error.

To protect your privacy and security, we will also take reasonable steps to verify your identity before making corrections and will not transmit that information to any sources that cannot be reasonably determined to be authorized by the person to whom the information concerns.


Contact us:
We hope you enjoy visiting www.bragg.com. If you have a request, question or concern, please contact us.
