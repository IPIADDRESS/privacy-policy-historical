Privacy Policy

The privacy policy of BrainPulse Technologies reveals our dedication and commitment to the users of our website. The policy applies solely to the information gathered by the website.

We recognize your right to confidentiality and we are devoted to protecting your privacy. We are the sole owner of the information collected by this website and we do not rent, sell or loan any information collected by the website to others unless legally required. We may store and process your information to understand your requirements and to design our products and services to suit your needs. It may also be used to provide you with information concerning our industry news, special events or features and offers suited for you. Any sensitive information collected from the users of the website is encrypted and transmitted to us in a secured manner. Similarly, we also protect your information offline. The computers and servers where the data are stored are also kept in a secure environment.
