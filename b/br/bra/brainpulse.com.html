Privacy Policy

The privacy policy of BrainPulse Technologies reveals our dedication and commitment to the users of our website. The policy applies solely to the information gathered by the website.



Information Collection and Sharing

We recognize your right to confidentiality and we are devoted to protecting your privacy. We are the sole owner of the information collected by this website and we do not rent, sell or loan any information collected by the website to others unless legally required. We may store and process your information to understand your requirements and to design our products and services to suit your needs. It may also be used to provide you with information concerning our industry news, special events or features and offers suited for you. If you correspond with us via E- mail, we may collect the information and store it with full confidentiality.

Security

Any sensitive information collected from the users of the website is encrypted and transmitted to us in a secured manner. Similarly, we also protect your information offline. Only concerned persons who require the information to perform a particular job are granted access rights to such data. The computers and servers where the data are stored are also kept in a secure environment.

Contact Form

For any queries concerning our policies, please fill out the contact form. The user is required to provide certain information like name and email address and our representative will respond to your queries as soon as possible.
