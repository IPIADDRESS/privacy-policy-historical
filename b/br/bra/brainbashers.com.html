  • The BrainBashers website uses third party vendors, including Google, for its adverts, and they will show users in the EEA only non-personalised ads. Non-personalised ads are targeted using contextual information rather than the past behaviour of a user. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
