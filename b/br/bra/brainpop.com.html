BrainPOP Privacy Policy

1. We Protect Kids' Privacy

We adhere to the goals and guidelines that are put forth by regulatory documents and bodies such as the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998 (COPPA), the Center for Media Education (CME), the Children's Advertising Review Unit (CARU) of the Council of Better Business Bureaus and other organizations who are devoted to providing a safe online community for children.

BrainPOP adheres to the highest standards of privacy and does not sell or otherwise share any individually identifiable information or any information about our users to third parties.

BrainPOP does NOT:

  • Collect more information from children than is reasonably necessary for them to participate in activities.

  • Entice children, by the prospect of a special game, prize or other activity, to provide more information than is necessary to participate in the activity.

  • Share any personal information about children with third parties.

  • Post anywhere on the website any email that is sent to or from our users.

  • Send any mass emails, updates, or advertisements to children or adults registered at BrainPOP.

Why We Collect Information

BrainPOP collects a minimal amount of information from users in order to better serve their interests.

We collect this information for the following reasons:
1) EMAIL: So that children are able to express their questions and ideas and receive a personal answer from our characters in the "Ask Us" sections. *All email correspondence is deleted every 2 weeks.
2) So that users may accumulate points for activities that they complete on the site.
3) So that individuals may post artwork on the site.

The Information That We Collect

When a user joins, he or she fills fill out a form that asks for the following information:

USERNAME
PASSWORD
PARENT'S EMAIL
CHILD'S EMAIL
BIRTH DATE (optional)
GENDER (optional)

Parental Notice

 When children join, we require a "parent email address" in keeping with standard industry safety guidelines for children. This enables us to notify parents that their child has joined BrainPOP. Parents may change their child's information or cancel their child's account at anytime.

Simply contact us to request changes or cancellation

Parent Emails from BrainPOP:
Some email messages to parents, from BrainPOP, are sent automatically; you will get an email when your child registers at the site, if your child enters a contest or sweepstakes, and if your child should win that contest or sweepstakes. If you'd rather not receive email, let us know by contacting us.
