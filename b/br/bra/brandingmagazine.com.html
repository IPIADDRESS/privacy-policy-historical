Featured Publication

#16 The B2B Branding Roundtable

B2B, or business-to-business, branding and marketing once went by the decidedly unsexy moniker of “industrial marketing.” It was a hard-nosed business of products sold on features and price, with marketers often relegated to a sales support role and “brand” considered an indulgent and irrelevant nicety better suited to B2C. No more. Today, B2B often attracts top talent and spends top dollar — $540 billion U.S. dollars in 2015, for advertising alone.
