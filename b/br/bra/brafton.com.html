Privacy Statement

At Brafton we are strongly committed to protecting users’ privacy, and this Privacy Policy governs our data collection, processing and usage policies for our Websites including www.brafton.com.  We periodically update this policy.  If you do not agree with the practices in this Privacy Policy you should not use the Website.  If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we manage the information that you provide us please email us at privacy@brafton.com or write to us at Brafton Inc, 2nd Fl, 2 Oliver Street, Boston MA 02109.

 

  • Information Collected:

 

Brafton collects personal information that you submit to us including contact information or information about you that is available on the internet from sources such as LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook or Google or publicly available information that we acquire from service providers.  

Brafton utilizes various Javascript-based tracking softwares (notably Google Analytics, AdRoll and Pardot) to collect data from every visitor to the website.  If you wish to opt-out of any type of visitor tracking, we recommend disabling Javascript in your browser options.

We use IP addresses to collect statistical information about the areas of the Website that users are visiting, and general traffic data and to identify a particular user in relation to items being viewed. This information allows us to determine what is most beneficial for users, and facilitates us in continually improving the overall site for our users.

The Website uses Cookies.  A “cookie” is a small amount of data that is sent to a user’s browser from a web server and stored on a user’s computer, used to store and track information about the user. When you visit our site, Brafton may use cookies containing information (such as a unique user ID) used to track your usage of our site, and may be used to send you personalized content or offers. Brafton also utilizes basic cookies to enable caching of our content in your browser to help accelerate page loading performance. You can disable cookie storage via your browser’s content settings.

Minors and children should not use the Website.

 

  • Use of Information:

 

We will only use the information you provide to us in order to communicate with you about our products and services or for the purpose for which such information was provided. Users may opt-out of receiving mailings from us by clicking the link in the footer of email communications.  We reserve the right to access and to disclose personal information to comply with applicable l

aws and lawful government requests, to operate our systems properly or to protect either ourselves or our users.

Braton is operated from the United States.  If you are visiting the website from outside of the United States you agree to any processing of any personal information you provide us according to this Privacy Policy.

 

  • Data Confidentiality:

 

Brafton does not share your personal information with third parties.  We may use collective identified customer data and statistics for internal analysis and external marketing purposes.  

 

  • Security:

 

The website has industry standard security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control.
