Cookie:

We use third party advertisments like Google Adsense and infolinks to support our site. Google, as a third-party vendor makes use of cookies to serve ads on this site. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to Bytewiki.com users based on their visit to your sites and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

A “cookie” is a small data text file that is placed in the user’s browser from a web server and is stored on a user’s computer, used to store and track information about the user. Bytewiki.com utilizes basic cookies to enable caching of our content in your browser to help accelerate page loading performance. Cookies themselves do not contain any personal information, and we not use cookies to collect personal information. You can always disable cookie storage via your browser’s content settings.
