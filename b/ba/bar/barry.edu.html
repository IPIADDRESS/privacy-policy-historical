Website Privacy Policy

This site is owned and operated by Barry University. Your privacy on the Internet is of the utmost importance to us. At Barry University, we want to make your experience online satisfying and safe.

Because we gather certain types of information about our users, we feel you should fully understand our policy and the terms and conditions surrounding the capture and use of that information. This privacy statement discloses what information we gather and how we use it.

Information Barry University Gathers and Tracks Online

Barry University gathers two types of information about users:

Information that users provide through optional, voluntary submissions. These include voluntary submissions to create a MyBarry account, ask a question, schedule a visit, apply for admissions, and register for events.

Information Barry University gathers through aggregated tracking information derived by tallying page views throughout our sites and tracking the results of search engine advertisements. This information allows us to better tailor our content to readers' needs and to help our advertisers and sponsors better understand the demographics of our audience. Under no circumstances does Barry University divulge any information about an individual user to a third party.

Email Opt-Out Policy

All emails sent from Barry University to accounts outside of the University (email addresses that are not @mail.barry.edu or @mymail.barry.edu accounts) include the following information:

  • How your email address was obtained.
  • How to opt-out from future emails from Barry University.

You can unsubscribe from future emails from Barry University by visiting http://www.barry.edu/email/unsubscribe.aspx. To unsubscribe from future emails via postal mail, send your request directly to Barry University, Attn: Privacy Administrator, Information Technology, 11300 N.E. 2nd Avenue, Miami Shores, FL 33161.

Security

Barry University operates secure data networks protected by industry standard firewall and password protection systems. Our security and privacy policies are periodically reviewed and enhanced as necessary and only authorized individuals have access to the information provided by our customers.
