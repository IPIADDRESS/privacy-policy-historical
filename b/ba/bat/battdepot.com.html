Advanced Pro Series is powered by the first and only rechargeable lithium-ion cell that overcomes the barrier of temperature. Dr. Battery Green Series

The 3 years warranty Green Series is powered by Boston-Power’s patented Sonata Cells, designed and built with the idea “Green the Earth” as priority. Green Series provides at least 3 times longer battery lifespan than conventional batteries where the benefit is two-fold: less expensive over the long run and less electronic waste.
