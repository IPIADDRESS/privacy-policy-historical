BasicIncome ("us", "we", or "our") operates http://basicincome.network (the "Site"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use and disclosure of Personal Information we receive from users of the Site.

Information Collection And Use

While using our Site, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you. Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to your name, email, address, and identifying IDs ("Personal Information"). Due to the nature of BasicIncome it is very important each person owns only a single account and thus reserve the right to use your personal information to verify your identity.


Log Data

Like many site operators, we collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our Site ("Log Data").

This Log Data may include information such as your computer's Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser type, browser version, the pages of our Site that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages and other statistics.

In addition, we may use third party services such as Google Analytics that collect, monitor and analyze this data.

We may use your Personal Information to contact you with newsletters, marketing or promotional materials. We may also contact you regarding information related to certain activity on your account.


Cookies

Cookies are files with small amount of data, which may include an anonymous unique identifier. Cookies are sent to your browser from a web site and stored on your computer's hard drive. Like many sites, we use "cookies" to collect information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our Site.


Transactions

Transactions you make with others will appear anonymously in the stats page, but will not be shown publicly on the site. We will attempt to keep specific transaction data private, but we cannot guarantee its absolute security.


Messaging

Your message logs when making a purchase are viewable by the seller, buyer, and BasicIncome. BasicIncome may use these messages to help settle disputes.


Security

The security of your Personal Information is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
