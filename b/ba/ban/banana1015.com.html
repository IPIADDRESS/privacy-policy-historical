Use of Cookies

The Banana 101.5 Flint's Rock Radio Web site use "cookies" to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalize Banana 101.5 Flint's Rock Radio pages, or register with Banana 101.5 Flint's Rock Radio site or services, a cookie helps Banana 101.5 Flint's Rock Radio to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. When you return to the same Banana 101.5 Flint's Rock Radio Web site, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the Banana 101.5 Flint's Rock Radio features that you customized.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer.
