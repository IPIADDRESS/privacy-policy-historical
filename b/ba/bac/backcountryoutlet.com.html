About Our Privacy Policy

BackcountryOutlet.com respects your privacy. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. At any time you may contact BackcountryOutlet.com with any questions or concerns that you have about this policy. Please direct any Privacy Policy-related communication to PrivacyPolicy@BackcountryOutlet.com.

Your Personal Information

BackcountryOutlet.com will not sell or rent your personal information to a third party. All data that we keep on you is kept strictly for BackcountryOutlet.com business use to assist you in your current or future purchases or in analyzing sales trends.

Please be aware that signing up for the Bill Me Later payment option automatically subscribes you to a weekly Bill Me Later marketing email as stated in the Bill Me Later Privacy Policies. If you enroll in Bill Me Later but do not wish to receive promotional messages from Bill Me Later, you should opt out by logging into your Bill Me Later account.

Cookies and IP Numbers

We use cookies for other purposes such as site personalization. You can reject cookies and still use the BackcountryOutlet.com site, however, it will disable the ability for the site to recognize your cart, your account and any personalization efforts.
