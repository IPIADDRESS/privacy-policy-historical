Security
SkyDesk's website has security measures in place to protect the access, loss, misuse and alteration of the data and personally identifiable information under its control. The security and integrity of customer data is our business. SkyDesk employs sophisticated security measures and uses encryption technology like banks and the US Government. After the SkyDesk product is installed on the customer's PC, the "data" (from the files selected by the customer) and the customer's personally identifiable information are encrypted right at the customer's PC before being transmitted over the Internet to SkyDesk. Once SkyDesk receives the customer's transmission, it is authenticated, protected from the outside by a firewall and the data is stored in its encrypted form within SkyDesk's system. A PassPhrase (password) is required before decryption of the customer's data can begin. Our software never commits the customer's PassPhrase to permanent storage (anywhere), never transmits it to SkyDesk and it cannot be recreated from anything the customer transmits to SkyDesk. At SkyDesk, access to customer's personally identifiable information is limited to e-mail addresses, contact and billing information. Even credit card information is kept encrypted within SkyDesk's system.
