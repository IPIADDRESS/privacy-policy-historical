Privacy Policy

What Information Do We Collect?

When you visit our Web site you may provide us with two types of information: personal information you knowingly choose to disclose that is collected on an individual basis and Web site use information collected on an aggregate basis as you and others browse our Web site.

When you register to be a member of Ballhype or provide feedback to Ballhype in contact us forms or messages, you provide us information about yourself including your email address. If you choose to correspond with us through email, we may retain the content of your email messages together with your email address and our responses.

Other users of the Ballhype Web site may view your user profile information that is published on Ballhype. They will never have access to your personal information such as IP address, email address, or password.

Ballhype uses small text files called cookies and Web server logs to collect information about how our Web site is used. Information gathered through cookies and Web server logs may include the date and time of visits, the pages viewed, time spent at our Web site, and the Web sites visited just before and just after our Web site.

Cookies help us make the site easier to use by not requiring you to log in each time you visit Ballhype, by remembering which teams or players are your favorites, etc. We may use cookies or Web server logs to help track or diagnose problems on the Web site, improve performance, or to disable accounts that are in violation of our terms of service. You can disable cookies from your browser but we do not recommend this as you will not be able to use many of Ballhype's features.

Occasionally, we may also use the information we collect to notify you about important changes to our Web site, new services and special offers we think you will find valuable. You may notify us at any time if you do not wish to receive these communications by emailing us at customerservice AT ballhype DOT com.

Ballhype does not sell, rent or share identifiable personal information about its members to third parties except as outlined below.

1. Business transfers — should Ballhype enter into a business transaction that involves the buying or selling of assets, user information including email address may be transferred per the transaction agreement. We may also transfer such information in the course of corporate divestitures, mergers, or any dissolution.

2. As required by law or to enforce the safety and security of the Web site, our members or employees.

You may request access to all your personally identifiable information that we collect online and maintain in our database by emailing customerservice AT ballhype DOT com.

Ballhype by its nature includes links to many different Web sites. We are not responsible for the practices employed by Web sites linked to or from our Web site nor the information or content contained therein. Please remember that when you use a link to go from Ballhype to another Web site, our Privacy Policy is no longer in effect. Your browsing and interaction on any other Web site, including Web sites which have a link on our Web site, is subject to that Web site's own rules and policies. Please read over those rules and policies before proceeding.

By using our Web site you consent to our collection and use of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy. We reserve the right to change this policy at any time; those changes will be posted on our Web site to keep you aware of what information we collect, how we use it and under what circumstances we may disclose it.
