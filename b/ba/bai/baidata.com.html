  1. General Rights

The right to access your personal data and correction

You have the right to access, correct or update your personal data at any time. We understand the importance of this and should you want to exercise your rights, please contact us via our contact page https://www.bareinternational.eu   or by sending an email to welcome@bareinternational.com indicating the subject of the contact (e.g. ‘request for access to/correct/update my personal data’).

 

The right to data portability

Your personal data is portable. This means it can be moved, copied or transmitted electronically. However, this right only applies where:

  1. a) The processing is based on your consent;
  2. b) The processing takes place for the performance of a contract;
  3. c) The processing takes place by automated means;

With respect of the above you have the right to receive your personal data provided by you, in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format and have the right to transmit those data to another controller.

If you wish to exercise your right to data portability, please contact us via our contact page https://www.bareinternational.eu   or by sending an email to dataprivacy@bareinternational.com indicating the subject of the contact (e.g.’ request to exercise data portability right’).

The right to deletion of your personal data

You have the right to request the deletion of your data if:

  1. a) your personal data is no longer necessary in relation to the purposes for which we collected it; or
  2. b) you withdraw the consent that you had previously given us to process your personal data, and there is no other legal ground to process that personal data; or
  3. c) any time if you wish to do so; or
  4. d) the personal data is not being processed lawfully; or
  5. e) your personal data needs to be deleted to comply with the law.

If you wish to delete the personal data we hold about you, please let us know via our contact page https://www.bareinternational.eu  or by sending an email to dataprivacy@bareinternational.com indicating the subject of the contact (e.g. ‘request for deletion of my personal data’) and we will respond to your request in 25 days.

If the personal data we collect is no longer needed for any purposes and we are not required by law to retain it, we will do what we can to delete it.

The right to restriction of processing

You have the right to restrict the processing of your personal data if

  1. a) you do not believe the personal data we have about you is accurate; or
  2. b) the personal data is not being processed lawfully, but instead of deletion of the personal data, you would prefer us to restrict processing thereof; or
  3. c) we no longer need your personal data for the purposes we collected it, but you require the data in order to establish, exercise or defend legal claims; or
  4. d) you have objected to the processing of your personal data and are awaiting verification on whether your interests related to that objection outweigh the legitimate grounds for processing your data.

If you wish to restrict our processing of your personal data, please let us know via our contact page https://www.bareinternational.eu  or by sending an email to dataprivacy@bareinternational.com indicating the subject of the contact (e.g. ‘request for restriction of the processing of my personal data’) and we will respond to your request in 25 days.
