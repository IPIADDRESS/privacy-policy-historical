What Are Cookies?

A “cookie” is a small piece of data that a Web site stores in a designated file on your computer. Cookies are commonly used to store user names and/or passwords to facilitate access to the site. Cookies can also be used to store user preferences, login dates and times, and content viewing history. When you visit a website, that site can access that information which it stored in a cookie. Cookies make your online experience easier and more personalized. We use cookies and other technologies to recognize you and personalize your experience, as well as to help us understand how our site is used. Users must have cookies enabled in order for the site to function properly. Cookies themselves do not tell badoinkvr.com any personal information about the user.
