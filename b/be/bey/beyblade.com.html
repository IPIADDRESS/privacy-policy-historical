This is personally identifiable information that identifies a user as an individual. www.beyblade.com does not collect any personal information. The second type is non personal data which doesn’t directly identify an individual. www.beyblade.com may collect non personal information about the use of the website and apps to help us improve our services.

Please note that if an adult posts personal information about themselves on associated social media pages, in addition to being collected, this data is made public for others to see. The adult is responsible for disclosing this data. Please ensure you read the privacy policy of any sites and services you visit.
