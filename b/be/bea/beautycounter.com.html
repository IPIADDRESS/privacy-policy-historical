What is a Consultant:

Think of a Consultant as a personal shopper who can work with you to provide recommendations and purchasing advice throughout your Beautycounter experience. If you want your Consultant to get credit for this order, please select their name from the search results.

What is a Social:

A Social is a gathering where people learn about the Beautycounter mission and can preview, sample, and order products.

Have you recently attended a Social? If so, select a Social from the dropdown menu.

Shop with a different Consultant

Search for a different Consultant. Select a name from the search results, and you will be directed to their personal website to place your order. The next time you visit Beautycounter.com, you will shop with your original Consultant.

If you would like to change your default Consultant, please contact Customer Care or call (888) 988-9108.
