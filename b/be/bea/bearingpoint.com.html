Legal Disclaimer | Privacy Policy



BearingPoint will handle the information we learn about you from your visit to our website in the following manner. The information we receive depends upon what you do when visiting our site.

If you visit our site to read or download information, such as news stories or articles:

We collect and store only the following information about you: the name of the domain from which you access the Internet; the date and time you access our site; and the Internet address of the website from which you linked directly to our site.

We use the information we collect to measure the number of visitors to the different sections of our site, and to help us make our site more useful to visitors.

If you identify yourself by submitting a contact form:

You also may decide to send us personally-identifying information, for example, in an electronic mail message containing a request. We use personally-identifying information (email addresses) from visitors to send periodic email updates regarding BearingPoint services and events. If you do not wish to receive such information, send us an email to consultingweb@bearingpoint.net and we will delete your email address from the list and refrain from sending you any further email notifications.

We want to be very clear: We will not obtain personally-identifying information about you when you visit our site, unless you choose to provide such information to us.

We will never release, sell, or give your information to any other party or organization, without your explicit permission. If you choose to no longer receive information on our services and products, we will honor your choice and refrain from sending you such announcements. You may change your choice at any time.
