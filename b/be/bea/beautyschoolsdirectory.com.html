Privacy Policy
PlattForm Zuma, Inc., the parent company of beautyschoolsdirectory.com, is respectful of your privacy. We track and collect information which helps us analyze who visits our web site and what services on our site provide the most useful information for you. Our request for information collects personal data that is passed along to the school(s) you identify so they can contact you with additional information. We also collect information by placing cookies (small files) on your computer to track how you move through our web site and where you found out about our web site. We do not, however, collect or store any data in the cookies that would allow us to identify you personally.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us by e–mail at info@beautyschoolsdirectory.com.

How do we use the personal information that you provide to us?
Broadly speaking, your personal information is used to help match your interests with schools that provide the training you seek.  Your information is shared with the schools that you select, in order for them to contact you with information about their programs.  If your search yields no results, we may store your information and send you updates if and when programs you specified do become available. We also use personal information for purposes of administering our business activities, providing customer service, and making other products and services available to our customers and prospective customers. Occasionally, we may also use the information we collect to notify you about important changes to the web site, new services and special offers we think you will find valuable.
