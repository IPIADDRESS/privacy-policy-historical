5. What are 'Cookies' and how do we use them?
Cookies are tiny files planted on your computer's hard drive by web servers. Our cookies do not contain any personally identifying information but they do enable us to recognise your web browser (e.g. Netscape of Internet Explorer) and so, each time you log into BEME.com, your computer will be recognised by our web server. You can set your web browser not to accept cookies.
