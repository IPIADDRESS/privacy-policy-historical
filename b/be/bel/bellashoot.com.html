The “Bellashoot Service” (collectively, the "Service") is defined as any website, mobile application, or Internet service under the control of Bellashoot, whether partial or otherwise, in connection with providing a platform for Bellashoot’s services.

Policy Scope. Our Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share and protect information in relation to our web site, mobile services, and any software provided on or in connection with Bellashoot services, and your choices about the us and collection of your information.

By using our Service you understand and agree that we are providing a platform for you to post content, including photos, videos, comments and other materials ("User Content"), to the Service and to share User Content publicly. Our Policy applies to all visitors, users and all others who access the Service ("Users").

Personal Information Defined. Bellashoot considers “Personal Information” to include information that alone or when in combination with other information may be used to readily identify, contact, or locate you, such as: name, address, email address, and/or phone number. Bellashoot does not consider Personal Information to include information that has been anonymized so that it doesn’t allow a third party to identify a specific individual easily.

Personal Information

The Bellashoot Service collects some Personal Information when you register. To register with the Bellashoot Service, you must supply your email address, username and password. We may also collect Personal Information User Content (e.g., first & last name, gender, phone number, photos, videos, comments, and other materials) from you in other ways when you use the Bellashoot Service, such as from your posted content or through your contact with our support team.

Location Information

The Bellashoot Service may access your GPS location through your mobile device so you can easily tag your photos to services & stores near you.

How to find your friends on Bellashoot:

If you choose, you can use our "Find friends" feature to locate other people with Bellashoot accounts either through:

  • your contacts list - If you elect to find your friends through your device's contacts list, then Bellashoot will access your contact list to determine whether your contact is using Bellashoot.
  • third-party social media sites - If you choose to find your friends through a third-party social media site, then you may be prompted to connect to the third-party service understanding that any information that such service may provide to us will be surely governed by this Privacy Policy.
  • a search of names and usernames on Bellashoot - If you choose to find your friends through a name or username search on Bellashoot then simply type a name to search.

Please note: If you choose to invite someone to the Service through our "Invite friends" feature, you can select a person directly from the contacts list on your device and send a text or email from your account. You understand and agree that you are fully responsible for any and all charges that apply to communications sent to or from your device, and because this invitation is coming directly from your account, Bellashoot does not have access to or control this communication.

Cookies, Automatic Data Collection, and Related Technologies.

Additionally, the Bellashoot Service collects and stores information that is generated automatically as you use it, including anonymous usage statistics. To collect this information, the Bellashoot Service may use log files, web beacons, cookies, and other similar technologies.

For example, the Bellashoot Service uses cookies to store users’ preferences, website and/or app activity session information. “Cookies” are bits of information that are stored by your browser or app on your computer or mobile device. This technology allows for the storage of user preferences, user ID, and password. You can turn off the “cookie” function in most current Internet browsers; however, most of the features of the Bellashoot Service may not function properly without them.

The Bellashoot Service automatically collects other system information, such as your web request, IP (Internet Protocol) address, browser language, browser type, domain name(s), referring pages, exit pages and URLs, platform type, number of clicks, pages viewed, landing pages, the order of these page views, the amount of time spent on particular pages, the time and date of your request, and the unique identity of your browser. This information is used to administer the Bellashoot Service, analyze trends, improve the design of the Bellashoot Service, track user movement, and otherwise enhance the Bellashoot Service.

By using the Bellashoot Service, you are authorizing us to gather, parse, and retain data related to the provision of the Bellashoot Service.
