    ABOUT BELIEFNET | PRIVACY

    An Overview
    Beliefnet is strongly committed to both protecting privacy and providing a safe and secure environment for our users.

    The purpose of the Beliefnet privacy policy is to help you understand what kinds of information we may gather about you when you visit Beliefnet, how we may use that information, whether we will disclose it to anyone, and how you can correct the information. Please note that this policy applies only to Beliefnet, and not to other companies' or organizations' websites to which we link. The policy also applies to websites with which we have partnered or "co-branded."


    Information About All Beliefnet Visitors
    In general, our service automatically gathers certain usage information, for example the number and frequency of visitors to Beliefnet and its areas. We use such data only in the aggregate. This collective data helps us to determine the extent to which our customers use parts of the site. With this information, we can make the site as appealing as we can for as many of you as possible.

    Cookies
    In our effort to provide you services customized to your interests, we use "cookies" to keep and sometimes track information about you. A cookie is a small piece of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. We use cookies to: access your account information (stored on our computers) in order to deliver better and more personalized services; estimate our audience size for our advertisers; identify which areas of our site you have visited; and prevent users from voting in polls more then once. You should also be aware that companies that advertise on our site may use their own cookies. This privacy policy does not cover how these companies use their cookies.

    Information About You
    When you sign up to use a service or order a product, we may ask for specific information about you. We will need certain information--such as name, Internet address or screen name, billing address, type of computer, credit card number--in order to provide that service or product to you. We may also use that information to let you know about additional products and services that may interest you. You can choose not to receive such information by letting us know on the registration screen when you sign up for the product or service. We may ask you for information about your interests so that both you and we can take advantage of the interactivity of the online medium, but you may always choose not to respond.

    Disclosure
    We do not disclose information about your individual visits to Beliefnet or information that you may give us, such as your name, address, e-mail address, or telephone number, to any outside companies. But as we mention above, we may share with our website partners aggregated statistical "ratings" information about the use of Beliefnet.

    Special Attention to Kids
    Beliefnet takes special care to protect the safety and privacy of young people using our services. We do not specifically collect information about children and believe that children should get their parents' consent before giving out any personal information. We encourage you to participate in your child's experience in cyberspace and to review our important safety tips before your child explores the Internet. We also encourage you and your child to visit Beliefnet's Kids section, which provides age-appropriate content.

    Third Party Advertising Publisher
    A reputable third party places the advertising you see on Beliefnet. If you would like to know more about their information-gathering practices, or if you would like to learn how you can opt-out of the information-gathering system, please click here.

    Beliefnet Privacy Policy Changes
    If we decide to change our privacy policy for Beliefnet, we will post those changes here so that you will always know what information we gather, how we might use that information, and whether we will disclose it to anyone.
