Privacy policy

These kinds of personal information are used for billing purposes, to fulfill your orders, to communicate with you about your order and Behappy.me, and for internal marketing purposes. By registering at Behappy.me, you agree that all information provided in the registration data is true and accurate and that you will maintain and update this information as required in order to keep it current, complete, and accurate.

If you are a registered user of a Behappy.me website and have supplied your email address, Behappy.me may occasionally send you an email to tell you about new features, new products, solicit your feedback. Behappy.me takes all measures reasonably necessary to protect against the unauthorized access, use, alteration or destruction personal information.

2.  DISCLOSURE OF INFORMATION

Behappy.me discloses personal information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that need to know that information in order to process it on Behappy.me behalf or to provide services available at Behappy.me website, and that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Some of those employees, contractors and affiliated organizations may be located outside of your home country.

Behappy.me will not rent or sell personal information to anyone. Other than to its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations, as described here, Behappy.me discloses personal information only in response to court order or other governmental request, or when Behappy.me believes in good faith tat disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the property or rights of Behappy.me, third parties or the public at large.

3. SECURITY

Your account is protected by a password for your privacy and security. You need to prevent unauthorized access to your account and personal information by selecting and protecting your password appropriately and limiting access to your computer and browser by signing off after you have finished accessing your account.

4. COOKIES

Behappy.me employs cookies. Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that we transfer to your computer’s hard drive through your web browser to enable our systems to recognize your browser. Most internet browsers are initially set to accept cookies. You can set your browser to refuse cookies from web sites or to remove cookies from your hard drive, but if you do, you will not be able to access or use portions of Behappy.me. We have to use cookies to enable you to select products, place them in an online shopping cart, and to purchase those products.
