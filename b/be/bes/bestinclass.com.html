Cookies

A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive. Cookies are required to use the Service. We use cookies to record current session information, to remember login information if requested, to save user preferences, and to gather usage metrics.
