Privacy Statement for Best for Puzzles



Best for Puzzles has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site: Best for Puzzles

General Principles


Best for Puzzles is firmly committed to the principle of personal privacy, and is totally opposed to all practices that infringe this principle (spam, spyware, etc.)

Information Automatically Logged


As is the case with almost all Web sites, our Web server automatically collects standard information such as IP address and referring URL. We use this information to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site. None of this information is personally identifiable.

Email Response From Visitors


We encourage visitors to our site to use our feedback form or send us email if they wish to comment, make suggestions, report problems, etc. Email addresses supplied in this way are used solely to respond on the subject matter in question, and are not collected or used for any other purpose. We do not divulge email addresses to any other party.

External Links


This site contains links to other sites. Best for Puzzles is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. We do however aim to ensure that we link only to reputable sites.

Special Relationships


This site uses a number of third-party agencies for the serving of advertisements. We aim to ensure that only advertisments for responsible and trustworthy companies appear on our site.

Some of our advertising is provided by Google Adsense. See the Google Privacy Statment.

Best for Puzzles is an Affiliate Partner of Amazon.co.uk for the sale of books and other goods. To find out more about how Amazon.co.uk manages the privacy and security of information for customer orders, please see the Amazon.co.uk Privacy Notice.

Contacting the Web Site


If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, please use the Feedback form or see this page for contact details.
