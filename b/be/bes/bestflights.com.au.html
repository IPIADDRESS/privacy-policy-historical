Privacy Statement


Best Flights Pty Ltd is an on-line and Call Centre-based retail travel agent operating in Western Australia (ABN: 44 095 507 010), with its registered office in Perth.

Best Flights undertakes to use its best endeavours to safeguard the privacy of its users.

Best Flights has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy.

The following discloses the information-gathering and dissemination practices for this web site.

If you have any queries concerning your personal information or any questions relating to how we use the information, please email webmaster@bestflights.com.au.

What information do we collect?

When you register as a user or purchase any goods or services on the website we may collect the information necessary to provide the services required.

Such information would at the very least include your name and an e-mail address.

We will also collect information from you if you complete any other forms on our site or if you contact us with comments or specific requests. 

What do we use personal information for?

Should it be necessary to send your details to any carefully selected third parties when collecting or processing your information, we will provide you with details of these parties in advance.

We will also provide you with details of any other parties who wish to provide you with information and details of products and services relevant to your interests.

Any registration forms will give you the opportunity to opt out of receiving such information. 

What safeguards are in place to protect the loss, misuse or alteration of your information?

All information you provide to Best Flights is treated with the utmost confidentiality.

Your information is stored safely on our computer systems and with physical security devices.
