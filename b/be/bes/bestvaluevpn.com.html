What is VPN?

For those of you that don't know; a VPN, which stands for Virtual Private Network, which is essentially an application that shuffles around your data so that it looks like your browsing the internet from somewhere else.

This is great for people that want to download new movies for free, stream Netflix from another country, or protect their online information.

Who uses VPN?

About 16% of adults in the United States use VPN technology to protect themselves online.

VPN use is greater in some areas around the world, with countries like Indonesia (41%), Thailand (39%) and Turkey (36%) leading the way in percentage of population using VPNs, per the Global Web Index.
