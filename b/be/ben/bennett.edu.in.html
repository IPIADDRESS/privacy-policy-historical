Privacy Policy

Our Privacy Policy is a comprehensive document that explains the procedures in which Bennett University collects, uses, maintains and discloses information gathered from users (each, a "User") of the website. This privacy policy is equally applied to the website (www.bennett.edu.in) and all programs and courses offered by the University.

Information Collection and Sharing

We recognize your right to confidentiality and we are devoted to protecting your privacy. We are the sole owner of the information collected by this website and we do not rent, sell or loan any information collected by the website to others unless legally required.

We may store and process your information to understand your requirements and to design our products and services to suit your needs.It may also be used to provide you with information concerning our industry news, special events or features and offers suited for you. If you correspond with us via E- mail, we may collect the information and store it with full confidentiality.

Data Security

Any sensitive information collected from the users of the website is encrypted and transmitted to us in a secured manner. Similarly, we also protect your information offline. Only concerned persons who require the information to perform a particular job are granted access rights to such data. The computers and servers where the data are stored are also kept in a secure environment.

Content And Anti-Spam Policy

Bennett University is consistently working to keep the website free of content spam, advertisement scams and other deceptive practices. Bennett University does not allow or authorize any attempt by a user or an automated system to use its services in a manner that could damage, disable, overburden or impair any aspect of the services offered onto the website. We never endorse a piece of content which attempts to trick others for their own financial gain. Content that deliberately tries to mislead users has no place on our website. We have a proper mechanism for tracking and removal of such spam on the website.

Contact Form

For any queries concerning our policies, please fill out the contact form. The user is required to provide certain information like name and email address and our representative will respond to your queries as soon as possible.
