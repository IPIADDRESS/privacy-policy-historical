Our Solutions
We handle all aspects of developing, managing
& reporting on an employer’s plans.

Acting as a seamless extension of your HR and benefits team, we manage all communication with employees concerning the plans, as well as all service provider relationships. Our full-service approach enables you to focus on critical tasks, while we focus on ensuring your voluntary benefits and employee savings plans provide engagement and participation.

At the very heart of all our services is our global web-based platform. The platform serves as our hub for managing plans, communicating with employees and reporting to employers.
This single-destination digital platform also powers your employee-facing website, where employees can easily enroll, elect benefits, shop for discounts and get updates on their plans. They can also make any customer service requests or inquiries through the site. While the site is custom-designed to look and feel like your main benefits portal, we design, manage and publish all content to the site.
Voluntary insurance benefits:
Adding the value in voluntary benefits

Through our single-destination platform, we maintain all employee interaction with the plan, as well as provide ongoing communication and updates about their benefits.
