User Correspondence:

Therefore, we want to provide you with explicit information regarding how we collect, gather, and identify information during your visit to this site. This information may be expanded or updated as we develop or change our site. Your continued use of our site signifies your acceptance of our privacy policy.

How we gather information about our users:
We receive information about our users through a variety of methods. These methods include forms on Web pages, forms in our software, our software and service registration and licensing procedures, attendance or participation in events that Bentley sponsors or participates in, industry groups and organizations and publications, our partners and through direct communication such as phone conversations.

In our effort to provide personalization and opportunities for prizes and services, we may at times ask you to submit information about yourself. You never have to answer these questions. However, if you should elect to withhold requested information, we may not be able to provide you with some of the services dependent upon the collection of this information

Use of Cookies:
Our Web sites may use "cookies" to process and store information relevant to your use of our Web sites and services. Cookies are identifiers that a Web site can send to your browser to keep on your computer to identify you and make your successive visits to the Website more efficient. Using cookies, we are able to track the Internet address of the domains from which users visit us. We analyze this data for internal purposes. You can set your browser to notify you when you are sent a cookie. This will give you the chance to decide whether or not to accept it.

Bentley will not monitor, edit or disclose information about a user or a user's private content, except that user agrees Bentley may do so in the good faith belief that such action is reasonably necessary: (1) to comply with the law; (2) to comply with legal process; (3) to enforce the Terms; (4) to respond to claims that such content violates the rights of third parties; (5) to protect the interests of Bentley or others. All material submitted to our web sites and services, regardless of form or medium, becomes the property of Bentley.

Bentley does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. Bentley may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. In those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (e-mail, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party.

Bentley may provide your personal information (including credit card information) to Bentley's agents for purposes of processing and/or fulfillment of transactions, including product orders, that are conducted through this site.

As a valued customer, Bentley offers you the choice to opt out of receiving certain marketing communications by changing your personal settings.
