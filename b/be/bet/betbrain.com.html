Disclosure of Personal Information

We will always endeavour to email you prior to any such transfer in order to gain your specific permission to transmit your data to such persons. We may provide aggregate statistics about customers, sales, traffic patters and related site information to reputable third party vendors but these will not include any personally identifying information.

Your Consent
