Returns Policy

Refunds
We want our customers to be fully satisfied with the choice they have made. If for any reason you are not happy with your order, or the delivered product does not match with your specifications, or you find a manufacturing defect, you have the right to cancel your order provided you give us a written notice within 14 working days of receipt. This 14 days period starts from the date the goods have been signed for. The notice of contract cancellation must be given in writing, stating your full name and order ID as subject reference to customerservice@bordelle.co.uk  and request a RAN (Returns Authorisation Number).

In return, you must return the product(s) to us in a new and unused condition, with the original label and tags and in the original bag and packaging. Shipping/postage costs to send back goods are charged to the customer. Please ensure that you use a courier or a registered service to send back goods and we suggest that you insure the goods as they are within your care and you are liable for any damages to the goods while in transit back to us. In case of dispute, we also recommend you retain proof of return, such as a postal receipt or consignment note. Return items not accompanied by a filled out  Returns Form and RAN will not be accepted.

Please note that Bordelle prices do not include custom duties and additional charges may be levied by your local tax office. Should you fail to clear the order through local customs and pay the relevant duties the goods will be returned to Bordelle and a refund less the costs of returning the goods to Bordelle will be automatically made to the card used to purchase the order upon receipt of the goods. We will issue you with a refund for your order excluding shipping costs.

Atelier Bordelle will contact you within 5 days (with the exception of weekends and public holidays) of receipt of the returned goods. Made to Measure & Ultimate Bespoke
The virtual atelier services are provided mainly for international customers and/or for customers who, for whatever reason, are not able to make it to the Bordelle showroom for an appointment. For this reason, Made to Measure orders can be purchased directly on the onlineshop where the customer is charged for the full price of the order upfront. The Made to Measure price is calculated at a fixed mark-up of 40% on the net retail price. On receipt of an online Made to Measure order the client will be emailed a link to a Measurement Form with detailed instructions and advice on the measurements required in order for the atelier to complete the order. The Measurement Form has an online submission feature and on completion, it can be electronically submitted to the atelier. The atelier will accept the customers own measurements under the condition that any inaccuraccies are the customer’s responsibility, and subsequently so are any fit problems that might arise as a result of the customer providing their own measurements. The atelier also offers a professional fitting service in the showroom on all Made to Measure and Ultimate Bespoke orders and we strongly advise our customers to book a fitting appointment if they are locally based and wish to purchase these services.

During busy periods a Made to Measure order and Ultimate Bespoke services may take up to 6 months to complete. Any changes to the order should be highlighted by emailing customerservice@bordelle.co.uk or calling the studio on +44 (0) 208 968 4488 within 24 hours from date of purchase or the atelier will be unable to alter the previously confirmed specifications.
On both Made to Measure and Ultimate Bespoke orders Atelier Bordelle retains a non-refundable 50% of the final price of your order to cover for the ordering of fabric, accessories, pattern drafting and production time. If a Made to Measure and/or an Ultimate Bespoke order has been produced within the agreed time frame and to the specifications of the customer as detailed in the submitted Measurement Form and/or on confirmation of all measurements following a fitting appointment in the showroom, the order(s) are fully non-refundable.

For Ultimate Bespoke the deposit is charged once the client has agreed to go ahead with an order either during an appointment in the showroom or via a telephone or email confirmation.

Please note that for any orders placed within the studio and/or Made to Measure and/or Ultimate Bespoke Services the Distance Selling regulations do not apply.
