Privacy Policy

Bombino Express Pvt Ltd. understands the importance of protecting the privacy of users of the Bombino Express website www.bombinoexp.in.The information collected on the Bombino Express website is used to market and improve the services of Bombino Express and its subsidiaries’, to improve the content and services on the Bombino Express website and make it easier to use and more appropriate to individual users and to contact its users with updates to the website or for other marketing purposes.Bombino Express may use this information, with the users’ permission, for the above purposes.

Bombino Express Pvt Ltd. never shares any information obtained on or from the Bombino Express website with any other organization, and will not do so in the future, unless required by law. Bombino Express will not sell information obtained through the Bombino Express website to any party or parties.

Certain areas of the Bombino Express website, including but not limited to the sections on ‘Tracking’, ‘Service Guide’, ‘Franchisee’, ‘Corporate Solutions’, ‘Careers’ and ‘KYC’ require registration or a password for access. Information obtained from registered users of these areas may also be used for Bombino Express marketing purposes, and cookies may be used in those and other areas, as is described in this privacy policy. Information obtained in these areas may also be used in accordance with agreements governing access to and use of the specific areas, including, but not necessarily limited to the ‘Tracking’ section on the Bombino Express website. In addition, visitors to the Bombino Express website may choose to register for a Bombino Express website username and password to simplify access to some interactive features on the Bombino Express website. Log file data and other information gathered from registered users of the Bombino Express website are used to improve the customer experience of the users of the Bombino Express website.

The Bombino Express website login currently provides users with access to Tracking, Location Finder, Price, Create Shipment and Request Pickup. In the future, Bombino Express may add other features to those accessed through the Bombino Express website Login.

Certain shipment data will be provided to the authorities of the country of transit or destination for customs and tax clearance or for security screening, as required by the laws of such country. The information provided would usually include: shipper name and address, receiver name and address, a description of the goods, number of pieces, weight and value of shipment.

Bombino Express does not request, via unsolicited mail or email, payment or personal information in return for goods in transit or in Bombino Express custody. If the fraudulent email resulted in financial loss you should contact your banking institution immediately through the appropriate channels. Bombino Express is not responsible for any charges or costs incurred as a result of unauthorized or fraudulent activity that abuses the Bombino Express name, service marks and logos.
