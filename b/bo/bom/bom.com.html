bom.com has created this Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") to demonstrate our commitment to customer privacy. Privacy on the bom.com web site (the "Site") is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy describes our information-gathering and dissemination practices. By using the Site or providing any personal information when using the Site, you are agreeing to the collection and use of information in the manner described below.

Information Gathered
Registration and Billing Information: We require users of the Site to provide contact information such as their name, e-mail address and company name and, when applicable, billing information such as billing address and credit card number. When one registers for services on the Site (“Services”) or requests more information about the Services, we may also ask for additional personal information such as title, department name or fax number, or additional company information such as annual revenues, number of employees or industry.

Users can opt out of providing this additional information by not entering it when asked. However, failure to provide certain requested information may impact a user’s ability to access certain customized services.

Log Data: bom.com also collects certain information from visitors to and users of the Site, such as Internet addresses, browser types and referring domains. This information is logged to help diagnose technical problems and administer the Site in order to constantly improve the quality of the Service. We may also track and analyze non-identifying and aggregate usage-and-volume statistical information from visitors and users, and provide such information to third parties.

Cookies: Cookies are files that Web browsers place on a computer's hard drive. bom.com uses cookies to tell us whether users and visitors have previously visited the Site. bom.com also uses a persistent cookie to help save and retrieve usernames of users accessing the Site and to save a user’s preferences from one session to the next. bom.com issues a session cookie only to record encrypted authentication information for the duration of a specific session. The session cookie does not include the username or password of the user. If the cookie is rejected, access to and usage of the Services may be denied or otherwise limited.

Invite a Supplier: If a user elects to invite its suppliers to review and provide input on the data it has entered as part of the Service, then the user will need to provide the name and e-mail address of each such supplier. bom.com will automatically send the suppliers a one-time e-mail inviting them to review and provide input on the requesting user’s data. bom.com stores this information for the purpose of sending this one-time e-mail and to facilitate access by the supplier to the user’s data.

Information Use
bom.com uses the information that we collect to set up Services for users and to improve the Site. We may also use the information to contact users to further discuss their interest in the Site and the Services, or to send users information regarding bom.com or its partners, such as promotions and events. We may also e-mail to users information regarding updates to the Services or the Site. To opt out of receiving such information, users can send an email to privacy@bom.com with the word "unsubscribe" in the subject field.

All financial and billing information collected through the Site is used solely to check the qualifications of prospective users and to bill for Services. This billing information is not used by bom.com for marketing or promotional purposes. Other third parties, such as content providers, may provide content on the Site, but they are not permitted to collect any information; nor does bom.com share any user information with these parties. To facilitate mailings to our customers, bom.com may provide the necessary mailing information to bonded third-party contractors who perform the mailing on our behalf.

Hosted Data
Users of the Service will be using the Site to host data. Data may at times be viewed or accessed by bom.com only for the purpose of resolving a problem, support issue or suspected violation of the Terms of Use for the Site, or as may be required by law. Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality and security of their user registration and password. Except as provided in this Privacy Policy, bom.com will not review, share, distribute, print or reference any data posted to the Site by users.

Sharing of Information
bom.com may share aggregated demographic information with partners and advertisers. This is not linked to any personal information that can identify an individual person.

bom.com may use a credit card processing company to bill users for Services. This company will not retain, share, store or use personally identifiable information for any purpose other than as needed to perform such billing.

bom.com may partner with other parties to provide specific services. If a user registers for these services, we will share names or other contact information that is necessary for the third party to provide these services. These third parties are not allowed to use personally identifiable information except for the purpose of providing these services.

From time to time we may provide information about users to bom.com's subsidiaries and joint ventures in order to provide those users the Service. Each of these entities will comply with the policies described in this Privacy Policy. In addition, if the stock or assets of OpenSpec, Inc. (d/b/a bom.com ), its subsidiaries, its joint ventures or any combination of such are acquired by another entity, some or all of bom.com users’ personal information and hosted data may be provided to such successor. In such a case, the successor will handle the personal information and hosted data in the same manner as provided in this Privacy Policy. Please see our Notice of Changes section below.

Other than as expressly described in this Privacy Policy, bom.com will not share, sell or rent a user’s personally identifiable information or hosted data with anyone outside of bom.com, without such user’s prior permission or unless ordered by a court of law.

The Site contains links to other sites. bom.com is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage bom.com users to be aware when they leave the Site and to read the privacy statements of each and every web site that collects personally identifiable information. This Privacy Policy applies solely to information collected by the Site.

Security
bom.com takes every precaution to protect our users’ information and data against loss, misuse and alteration. As a web-native application, our success will be predicated on the security of that information and data. We use standard industry practices as outlined in our security statement to help prevent unauthorized use of, access to or alteration of user information and hosted data. These practices include the appropriate use of firewalls, SSL encryption, system redundancies, and co-location at a 24/7 secured, controlled environment.

bom.com personnel with access to information gathered from users have been advised of the importance of maintaining the confidentiality of such information and of using it only for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

Choice/Opt-out
Whenever users submit requests for more information or Services from us, they may opt out of entering certain fields. However, such decision may affect the ability to use portions of the Site and Services. If you receive marketing or similar e-mail messages from us and wish to opt out of receiving such messages, then you may simply follow the opt-out procedure specified in such e-mail.

Updating Contact Information
We encourage our users to update and edit their contact information and keep it current. Users can easily correct or update their contact information at any time through the Service or by emailing privacy@bom.com.

Notice of Changes
bom.com may amend this Privacy Policy from time to time. If bom.com is going to use your personally identifiable information in a manner different from that stated at the time of collection we will notify you via email. You will have a choice as to whether or not we use your information in this different manner. In addition, if we make any material changes in our privacy practices that do not affect user information already stored in our database, we will post a prominent notice on our web site notifying users of the change. In some cases where we post the notice we will also email users, who have opted to receive communications from us, notifying them of the changes in our privacy practices.
