We collect two types of data on this site, firstly through the use of cookies & analytics software we collect non-personal & non-identifiable information, this data helps us improve the service by monitoring what aspect of the website users find most useful. You can refuse cookies by blocking them through you internet browser; you may find however use of this website is limited as a result.

This personally identifiable data is passed on to 3rd parties that meet the criteria you have chosen, for example, if you request a quote for a new central heating system we will pass your Personal Information onto to a number of carefully selected suppliers/contractors who can fulfil your request, these companies will then contact you directly to arrange a quote.

5. This website uses Cookies. Cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to your computer's hard disk for record keeping purposes and they can make the World Wide Web more useful by storing information about your preferences on a particular site. The use of Cookies is normal practice in the industry and many major websites use them to provide useful features for their customers. Cookies do not personally identify users but they do identify a user's computer. Most browsers are initially set to accept Cookies. If you prefer, you can set your Internet browser to refuse Cookies.
