Any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Resale or disclosure of information to third parties
 bondage.com does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including email addresses and matchmaker profiles.
Browser information collected on the website
 bondage.com analyzes our website logs to constantly improve the value of the materials available on our website. Our website logs are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.
Use of cookies
 bondage.com uses cookies to track user sessions, and to ease logging in. No personal information is stored in or retrieved from cookies, and session cookies expire after 30 minutes of inactivity.
Privacy of email lists
 bondage.com maintains several email lists to keep interested members informed about changes on the site and other events. Individuals must affirmatively request to join our mailing lists by signing up through a form on our web site. We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on our list to anyone. In addition, we configure our list server software to refuse to divulge the email addresses of our list subscribers to anyone but authorized bondage.com staff, including other list subscribers.
