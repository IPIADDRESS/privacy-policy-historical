8. Applicable laws. Bootable USB will protect your Personal Information in accordance with the United Kingdom laws.

9. IP Addresses. An IP address is a number automatically assigned to your computer whenever you access the Internet. BootableUSB collects IP addresses in order to conduct system administration, and to conduct site analysis.
