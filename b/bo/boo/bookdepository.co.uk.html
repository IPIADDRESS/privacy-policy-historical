Privacy Policy
We use your personal information ONLY in connection to processing your online orders, delivering products and services, processing payments, communicating with you, displaying contents such as your Saved Items and recommending other products and services. All information you give to The Book Depository is stored securely, and is not accessible to other users.
We collect the information you give us via our web forms as well as some automatic information such as your IP address. We use "cookies" and we obtain certain types of information when your Web browser accesses www.bookdepository.co.uk. This information is used as part of our security measures to prevent and detect any fraud attempts on our website for the benefit of our customers. All of our dealings with your data are governed by the Data Protection Act 1998 and your personal informaton will be held securely in accordance with our internal security policy and the law.
The information we collect from you includes  your name and address plus contact details and credit/debit details. We will never collect sensitive information about you without your explicit consent.
If you have any questions about our privacy and security policies, please contact us at help@bookdepository.co.uk
