Privacy Policy


Siren-BookStrand, Inc. respects the privacy of its readers and shall not disclose, distribute or rent its email subscriber information to any third party, nor shall it permit anyone else to do so.

The information collected is stored in a secure database when you make a purchase or subscribe to our newsletter. We use a variety of security technologies and comply with the guidelines set by the credit card industry to aid in diminishing fraud.

By using the information collected, we strive to make your shopping experience the best possible and fulfill your orders in a timely and accurate manner. In order to accomplish this, we use the information to process and track your order, provide quick and helpful customer service, contact you regarding your order, and notify you via our newsletter of new products being offered, if requested.

We do not spam or send unsolicited emails.

Your information is only shared with the organizations required to fulfill your order, such as the payment processor and the delivery company you selected.

We will, under the execution of a valid search warrant, provide any information that law enforcement requests about your purchases or activities on our newsgroups or forums.

We will use your personal information, if needed, to protect the rights of Siren-BookStrand, Inc. and in the enforcement of copyright on behalf of our authors.

We will provide your personal information, if needed, to the authors whose copyrighted works you resell or distribute illegally.

Should you have a concern or question not addressed here, please email us at info@bookstrand.com.
