Privacy policy

BookFinder.com is committed to your privacy. All of us here make heavy use of the Internet outside of our involvement with this site; it's important to us that we deal with your data the way we want our own to be dealt with.

  • We will not share any information on specific users or their searches with third parties.
  • We believe in the principle of minimal data collection. We will not ask users for any more personal information than is needed for basic operation.
  • Any information collected about our site's users will always be analyzed in the aggregate.
  • All information collected about our site's users is stored on our secure servers. Access to that information is strictly controlled and limited to authorized members of the BookFinder.com team.
  • Our site is also usable without cookies, and via anonymizing proxies. Some of the advertising on our site is supplied by third parties; they may use cookies, but do so under a strict privacy policy, audited by TrustE or similar groups. If you're uncomfortable with cookie technology, you may want to disable cookies on your web browser.

Mailing Lists

Membership in any of our mailing lists is anonymous; we will not share member addresses or membership status with any third party. Your participation in our many-to-many lists (e.g. the BookFinder Insider mailing list) will obviously be disclosed to other list members, should you choose to post.
