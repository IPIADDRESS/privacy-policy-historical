Privacy Policy

We believe that the the privacy and security of the visitors and users of this website, “bookmarkmaker.com”, is of utmost importance. As such, we have put in place policies and procedures to protect your privacy and security. This page, known as our “Privacy Policy”, outlines the information we collect about you and how we use it.

Information we collect:

From visitors to the site:

  • Google analytics data (this appears anonymous).
  • Our web logs and other records are stored indefinitely.
  • Submissions on the ‘Contact Us’ page.
  • Any e-mails we receive.

From users who post on the site:

  • All data that you post
  • E-mail address
  • Profile information

How we use (and don’t use) this information:

  • Your E-mail address is used to send information about your listings.
  • Your E-mail address is never shared with any third-parties.
  • Your E-mail address is never displayed on the site unless YOU provide it in the description of a listing or comment that you post (which we strongly advise against).
  • Google Analytics and web logs are used to improve our website.
  • Listing data is stored in our database even after a listing is “deleted” from the site.

Credit Card Data

  • We DO NOT store your credit card information at any time. This site is free. We never ask for any payment, so there is nothing for us to store.

Archiving

Please keep in mind that there are many sites on the Internet, such as google.com, archive.org, and yahoo.com, that may archive or show your posts and that these sites are not affiliated with bookmarkmaker.com in any way.
