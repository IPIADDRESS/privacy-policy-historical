Website Visitors

Like most website operators, Quality Seo Packagez collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Quality Seo Packagez purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Quality Seo Packagez visitors use its website. From time to time, Quality Seo Packagez may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

Quality Seo Packagez also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on QualitySeo Packagez blogs/sites. Quality SeoPackagez only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below, except that commenter IP addresses are visible and disclosed to the administrators of the blog/site where the comment was left.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain visitors to Quality SeoPackagez 's websites choose to interact with Quality SeoPackagez in ways that require Quality SeoPackagez to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that Quality Seo Packagez gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up at QualitySeoPackagez to provide a username and email address. Those who engage in transactions with Quality Seo Packagez are asked to provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to process those transactions. In each case, Quality Seo Packagez collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor's interaction with Quality SeoPackagez . Quality SeoPackagez does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

Aggregated Statistics

Quality Seo Packagez may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. Quality Seo Packagez may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, Quality Seo Packagez does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information

Quality Seo Packagez discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on Quality Seo Packagez's behalf or to provide services available at Quality Seo 'Packagez's websites, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Some of those employees, contractors and affiliated organizations may be located outside of your home country; by using Quality Seo Packagez 's websites, you consent to the transfer of such information to them. Quality Seo Packagez will not rent or sell potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information to anyone. Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Quality Seo Packagez uses cookies to help Quality SeoPackagez identify and track visitors, their usage of Quality SeoPackagez website, and their website access preferences. Quality Seo Packagez visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Quality Seo Packagez websites, with the drawback that certain features of Quality Seo Packagez websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by Quality Seo Packagez and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.
