IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on moretricks.com send directly to your browsers. moretricks.com has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
