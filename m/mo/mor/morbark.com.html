Privacy Policy

Our Privacy Policy

Morbark is committed to conducting its business in a manner that actively supports and ensures your personal privacy. Our Privacy Policy is formulated to comply with applicable laws and regulations.

Personal Information

  • We collect information you choose to submit during your interaction with us.
  • Our web server logs may collect additional information, such as the domain names of visitors to our web sites, in order to measure the use of our web sites and to improve content.
  • For your convenience, we use common Internet technologies such as cookies with our Web sites.
  • We obtain additional information about you, such as equipment sale transaction data from public record data compilation services.
  • We do not intentionally collect information about minors

Uses

  • We may use information you submit to provide you a requested product or service or information about our products and services.
  • We use ownership information to send you necessary information such as warranty information, product improvement programs, safety bulletins, and recall notices.

Sharing

  • We do not sell or trade information about you with anyone.
  • We support your needs by sharing information only with our authorized dealers.
  • From time to time, we may be required to provide your personal information in response to court order, subpoena, or government investigation. We also reserve the right to report to law enforcement agencies any activities that we in good faith believe to be unlawful.
  • We may release your personal information when we believe that such release is reasonably necessary to enforce or apply our Limited Warranty or to protect the rights, property, and safety of others and ourselves.

Third Party Links

  • Links to third-party Web sites may be provided solely for your information and convenience, or to provide additional information for various other goods and services. If you use these links, you will leave our Site.
  • This Privacy Statement does not cover the information practices of those Web sites linked to our Site, nor do we control their content or privacy policies. We suggest that you carefully review the privacy policies of each site you visit.

Updates

  • Any updates or changes to the terms of this Privacy Statement will be posted here on our Site and the date of the newest version posted below. Please check back frequently, especially before you submit any Personally Identifiable Information at our Site, to see if this Privacy Statement has changed.
