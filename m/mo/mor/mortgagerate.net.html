Privacy Policy

MortgageRate.net matches up consumers with our network of mortgage brokers and bankers from all over the United States. As an MortgageRate.net participant in our home financing service, each selected broker agrees with and is committed to MortgageRate.net's  policies f or consumer privacy.

To process customer home financing requests and keep customers informed, customers must provide their names, e-mail and street addresses, telephone numbers, income and other financial data. All of this information is stored in a secure manner and is not accessible through the Internet.

MortgageRate.net is a Internet service that allows its customers to research interest rates and mortgage vehicles for home financing. MortgageRate.net presents the customer with various mortgage options through our network of licensed mortgage brokers.

  • We respect your privacy and value your trust in us. We will do everything in our power to earn your confidence so that you can enthusiastically use our services and recommend us to friends and family.
  • We do not collect the names or addresses of visitors to the Mortgage Rate.net web site.
  • We do not sell, rent or barter mailing lists of MortgageRate.net customers.
  • We do request and store the names, e-mail and street addresses, telephone numbers, numbers, income and other financial information for customers. This information is transmitted to respective mortgage companies for the sole purpose of processing your request.
  • For the sole purpose of processing your request, MortgageRate.net may share the information you provide with up to four lending institutions participating in our program. All mortgage companies operate under federal and state laws regarding customer privacy.
  • Occasionally, we may notify you about a special offer from one of our promotional partners. We do not reveal your name or address to that promotional partner unless you affirmatively respond to the promotional offer.
  • At your request, we will not send you any information or offers unrelated to your finance request.
  • We may publicly reveal general, non-personal data about the MortgageRate.net, such as number of visitors, number of goods and services purchased, etc.
  • MortgageRate.net may share non-personal aggregated data with other third parties for general marketing and demographic purposes.
  • Any changes to our privacy promise will be communicated through the MortgageRate.net Web site at least 10 days in advance of implementation. Information collected before changes are made will be secured according to the old privacy policy.
