Depending on the services you are accessing, you could be asked various times for your contact address in some cases/ provision of certain services (s) or utility(ies). We normally collect information  on how you use our website, including details of your domain name and internet protocol address, operating system, browsers ,cookies, time spent on a page content, and the route you took to navigate through pages and also other data from time to time to help  us provide you with improved products and services.

We have no control or access over third party cookies and we do not store any cookies and just use the facility of net framework for page output cache. This privacy statement does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.

The user should exercise caution and look at the privacy policy applicable to the website in question.

You are solely responsible for keeping your User ID and Password Secure. Your password is the key to your account, You shall be solely responsible for all actions taken under your user name and you may lose substantial control over your personal information and may be subject to legally binding action taken on your behalf. Never disclose your User ID or Password to anyone. If your password is compromised for any reasons, you should immediately change your password.

Users are advised to review the privacy policy periodically.
