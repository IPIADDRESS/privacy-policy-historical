    • Privacy Statement

    We respect your privacy

    Morningside College respects the privacy of all visitors to our web site. Our policy is to protect your privacy, and we will not disclose your information to any third party without your permission.

    Information Gathering

    Personal information that we collect from you is treated as confidential and is used only to fulfill your request or provide a service to you. We collect thsi information in a variety of ways, including online applications, various contact and registration forms, and e-mail.

    "Cookies," small collections of data saved in your temporary internet folders, are used by our host server to keep statistics on user sessions and server hits. Web server activity is logged on a temporary basis and then deleted from our system.

    Our search engine tracks such things as words that are searched, when these words are searched, and how often they are searched. We keep this information confidential and use it to improve the  efficiency of our search engine, to gauge what information is important to our users, and to guide the development and maintenance of the web site.

    Links to other sites

    Our site contains links to web sites operated by parties not affiliated with Morningside College. We link to these sites in order to facilitate access to information that may be useful to you. Morningside College cannot control and is not responsible for the content contained on the web sites of non-affiliated parties. Morningside College is not responsible for the privacy policies of non-affiliated parties. We encourage you to review the security practices of these sites.

    Linking to other web sites on our site is not necessarily an endorsement of these sites by Morningside College. Their views do not represent the views of the employees and students of Morningside College.

    Security

    Our site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration fo the information under our control.

    Questions

    Please direct questions about this privacy statement to: wollman@morningside.edu.
