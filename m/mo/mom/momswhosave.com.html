Tell-a-Friend
If you choose to use our tell-a-friend service to refer a friend to our site, we will ask for the your name and email address and the friend's email address(es). This web site contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy policies of other sites. We encourage you to be aware of the privacy policies of the websites that you visit, especially if you give your personally identifiable information.
 
Children's Privacy
We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. We will delete the information from our records if we learn that we have information in our records from a child under 18.
 
Questions?
