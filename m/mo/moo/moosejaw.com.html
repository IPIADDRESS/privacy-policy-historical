MooseJaw Privacy Policy



Moosejaw Mountaineering and Backcountry Travel, Inc. (“Moosejaw”) is committed to protecting online user privacy because we love you. In order to ensure your privacy, information Moosejaw collects through the Moosejaw.com web site at www.moosejaw.com (“Site’) is used only in the manner and for the purposes described in this Moosejaw.com Privacy Policy (“Privacy Policy.”)



User Consent to Privacy Policy
Any person accessing, browsing, or otherwise using the Site, either manually or via an automated device or program, shall be considered a “User.” All Users are bound by the terms of this Privacy Policy. Users consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of personally identifiable User information (“Information”) pursuant to the terms of this Privacy Policy.



Information Collected from Users
Moosejaw collects the email address of Users that email a question or comment to Moosejaw or submit a customer review.



Moosejaw collects the billing and shipping addresses, including relevant e-mail, phone and credit card numbers and expiration dates of Users who purchase a product from Moosejaw through the Site. This includes Users who register to become Moosejaw members or enter any Moosejaw contests or other promotional features available on the Site. Moosejaw does not store a User’s credit card information, meaning this information must be re-entered each time an item is purchased.



Through the use of “cookies” (For more information regarding Cookies, see below), Moosejaw may collect and store anonymous information relating to Users’ browsing patterns, including, for instance, the User’s browser version, site referral information, IP address, operating system, and other technical Site use information.



COOKIES
The Site uses a feature of Internet Web browsers called a Cookie, which is a file that a User’s Web browser places on a User’s computer's hard drive, to assign an identification code to the computer and to collect anonymous browsing information. On this Site, cookies are used in conjunction with session variables to track a User's shopping cart and expire as soon as an order is completed or if a shopping cart sits idle for a lengthy period without being checked-out.



Moosejaw does not use Cookies to retrieve Information about Users from a User’s computer unless the User knowingly and willingly provided such Information. For instance, Moosejaw’s cookies do not collection any Information and the cookies do not collect, store or contain any credit card information.



You may set your Internet Web browser to notify you when you receive a cookie or to prevent cookies from being sent. If you prevent a cookie from being sent, you may limit the functionality Moosejaw can provide you when you visit the Site, especially when purchasing an item on the Site.

Use of Information
Moosejaw uses the Information collected from its Users to:
  • Respond to Users’ questions or comments;
  • Administer a User’s contest entry or entry in other promotional feature;
  • Fulfill a User’s purchase request and notify Users of their order status; and
  • Provide Users with important functionality changes to the Site, new Moosejaw.com services and special offers we think you will find valuable.


Updating or Deleting User Information
Moosejaw offers Users who have provided Information to Moosejaw an opportunity to review the Information provided to update or correct any errors. Users also can delete any Information provided to Moosejaw. To update or delete Information previously provided or to have your name removed from any mailing lists or other promotional distribution lists, go to http://www.moosejaw.com, sign into your account, and edit your profile.



Disclosure of Information to Third Parties
Unless we have your consent or except as required or permitted by law, Moosejaw will not sell, share, trade or give away any Information that it collects or receives regarding its Users.



Moosejaw may disclose information about our Users to others if we have a good faith belief that we are required to do so by law or legal process, to respond to claims, or to protect the rights, property or safety of Moosejaw or others. Moosejaw may disclose Information to third party in the event that a third party acquires Moosejaw or its assets. (See, Sale or Acquisition section below.)

The Site may provide hyperlinks, which are highlighted words or pictures within a hypertext document that, when clicked, take you to another place within the document, to another document altogether, or may take you to a third party website not controlled by Moosejaw. These hyperlinked third party websites may collect and disclose Information different than this Site. Moosejaw is not responsible for the collection, use, or disclosure of information collected through these web sites, and Moosejaw expressly disclaims any and all liability related to such collection, use, or disclosure.

Children
The Moosejaw.com Site is not directed towards children under 13 years of age and Moosejaw does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age through the Site.

Security
Because we love you, this Site has security measures to protect against the loss, misuse, and alteration of the Information under our control, including credit card information that is submitted to Moosejaw. When Users place orders or access their account information, the Site uses secure server software (SSL), which encrypts all information Users’ input before it is sent to us. Thus, we have taken steps to protect the Information we collect from you from unauthorized access. Unfortunately, no system is perfect; therefore, Moosejaw makes no representations or warranties with regard to the sufficiency of these security measures.
