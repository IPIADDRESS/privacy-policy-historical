Privacy Policy

MPS is committed to honoring the privacy of its users. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy and how we treat information that we collect. This Privacy Statement is governed by our Terms and Conditions. By using this web site, you consent to the data practices prescribed in this statement. We may periodically make changes to this Privacy Statement that we will include on this page. It is your responsibility to review this Privacy Policy frequently and remain informed about any changes to it, so we encourage you to visit this page often.

Information Collection
MPS collects Personally Identifiable Information from you through the use of request forms that may be submitted on this web site and also automatically receives and records information on its server logs from your browser, which may include, but is not limited to, your IP address, cookie information and the page you request. "Personally Identifiable Information" means any information that may be used to identify an individual, including, but not limited to, a first and last name, home or other physical address, an email address, phone number or other contact information, whether at work or at home. You can choose not to provide us with certain information, but then you might not be able to take advantage of many of our features. We do not collect Personally Identifiable Information about you on this website unless you voluntarily provide it to us.
 
Information Use
MPS uses the Personally Identifiable Information it collects from you to provide you with information regarding our products and promotions, support your use of our products and services, process any of your online transactions and for any other purpose related to the operation of this web site and the programs, products and services offered on it.
 
Personally Identifiable Information as well as any other information collected on this web site may be disclosed to our staff and/or agents, so that they can contact you regarding our products, services or promotional offers, respond to your requests, provide certain services to you and operate this website and the programs offered on it.
 
Disclosure
MPS does not rent, sell or share personal information it collects about you to or with third parties. Information collected from you is only used to complete and support your purchases from and use of the web site and to comply with any requirements of law. MPS may disclose personal information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (1) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on MPS or this web site; (2) protect and defend the rights or property of MPS; or (3) act in urgent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of MPS, its web sites or the public. If MPS should ever file for bankruptcy or merge with another company, we may sell the information you provide to us on this site to a third party or share your Personally Identifiable Information with any company with whom we merge.
 
Cookies
Portions of MPS's web site may utilize cookies. These are small files of information that save and retrieve information about a user's visit to the site. This information is used to create anonymous profiles that aid us in monitoring the user's session. We do not collect or store any type of personal or sensitive data such as social security numbers. We do maintain client usage logs that allow us to help monitor how clients are using our web site so that we may better assess the needs of our visitors. We mostly use "session cookies" that are automatically deleted after each visit. Cookies from our site can not be read by other sites. Most browsers will accept cookies until you change your browser settings to refuse them.

All Personally Identifiable Information MPS receives directly from you is stored in a password-protected database residing on a secure network behind an active firewall.
