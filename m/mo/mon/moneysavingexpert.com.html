We are committed to putting the consumer first and being transparent on how we collect, use and protect your personal information. The data controller is MoneySavingExpert.com Limited (referred to in this policy as "we", "us" or "our"). We want you to be confident that the personal information you give us is safe and secure with us, and understand how we use it to offer you a better and more personalised experience on our Site.

    The main purposes we collect and process your personal information are to: show you relevant content and to improve your experience on the Site; provide the services you sign up for, such as our Clubs services; communicate with you directly when you have consented, or when the law allows us to do this. Where we are processing your personal information in relation to our content and/or services, for the purposes of data protection legislation, we are controller of your personal information.

    To operate the MoneySaving tools on our Site we may sometimes need to share your personal information with third parties. They will be responsible for how they process your personal information. They are detailed at section 7.1 of this Privacy Policy and at section 9 of our Terms and Conditions.

    Our Site contains links to third-party websites that are not subject to this Privacy Policy. Those third-party websites are responsible for how they process your personal information. Read our Terms and Conditions for further information on these links. Please make sure you read the terms and conditions and privacy policy carefully before providing any personal information on a third-party website as we cannot accept any responsibility or liability for those third-party websites.

    Our Privacy Policy explains:

    • the personal information we collect;
    • how and why we collect and use your personal information;

    • when and why we will disclose your personal information to MoneySupermarket Group, or to other organisations;

    • how long we will hold your information for; and
    • how to contact us.
