  4. Privacy Policy

    MoneyVidya.com is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy and this statement sets out our privacy policy. If we ever make material changes to this policy, we will announce them. If you have any questions regarding this statement, please contact help@moneyvidya.com

    Why do we store information about our members?

    When members register on MoneyVidya.com we ask for certain information so that we can provide a more interesting and useful service. Members also have the option to provide specific information about their investing habits in order that we can further tailor their experience.

    We store information relating to the Content you contribute to the site (such as picks you have made and discussion you have started) and will occasionally conduct research on the demographics, preferences and site usage behavior of our members. This research is conducted and analysed at an aggregate level and individual members will remain anonymous in this research.

    We collect IP addresses for site security and to help us understand how people use our site so that we can improve your experience. For similar reasons, we may assign your browser a cookie, the cookie does not tell us who you are - only you can tell us that.

    How we use the information

    We collect information about who our members are and how they use our site in order to provide our members with the best experience possible. Sometimes we may use the information to notify you of products, services, specific content, or areas of our site that we think may interest you. You can choose whether or not to receive these notifications by altering your privacy settings at any time by contacting us at help@moneyvidya.com

    We will only ever disclose personal information to 3rd parties, under the following circumstances:

    • When we have your explicit consent
    • As required by applicable law, or in response to legal process to protect the rights or property of MoneyVidya.com
    • If you sign up for a special offer or service from an advertiser or other 3rd party, we will provide the information required for them to fulfill the offer or to provide the service
    • If you recommend MoneyVidya.com or any Content on the site to a friend, our correspondence may disclose you as the recommender.

    We reserve the right to disclose aggregated information about our user base, their demographics, investment preferences, site usage patterns and other areas, in such a way as no individual members can be identified. For example, we might tell an advertiser that we have 20,000 members in Mumbai who work in the IT industry. We would never disclose personal details such as names, email addresses, and phone numbers.

    Privacy whilst using the site

    When you post Content onto our site, that information, along with your screen name, is visible to the public. Please remember that any information you disclose can be collected and used by others. Other members can respond to your posts at any time but will not know your external email address or other contact details (unless you have disclosed it).

    MoneyVidya.com cannot guarantee the security of any information you transmit to us, and you do so at your own risk. You are responsible for maintaining the secrecy of any password and/or account information. We will never ask you for your password in an unsolicited phone call or email.
