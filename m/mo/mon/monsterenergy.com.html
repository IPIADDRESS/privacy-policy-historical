Please note we will not provide you with notice prior to disclosure in such cases.

       

      Opting Out

      We also collect information automatically and through the use of electronic tools that may be transparent to you. We may log the name of your Internet Service Provider, IP address, or use “cookie” technology to assist us with enhancing your experience on our Site, including but not limited to troubleshooting the Site or server (which may require third party technical support), Site administration, using cookies to store your user name and password, sparing you from having to re-enter that information each time you visit, or to control the number of times you encounter a particular advertisement while visiting our Site. In certain cases, you can choose not to provide us with information by setting your browser to refuse to accept cookies, but be aware that doing so may limit your ability to access certain portions of the Site or may require you to re-enter your user name and password.
