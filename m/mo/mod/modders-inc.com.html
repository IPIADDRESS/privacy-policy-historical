If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at webmaster@modders-inc.com.

At Modders-Inc, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by Modders-Inc and how it is used.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information
Certain visitors to Modders-Inc.com’s website that choose to interact with Modders-Inc.com in ways that require Modders-Inc.com to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that Modders-Inc.com gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who use our forums to provide a username and email address. In each case, Modders-Inc.com collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor’s interaction with Modders-Inc.com. Modders-Inc.com does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

Log Files
Like many other Web sites, Modders-Inc makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protcol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies
A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Modders-Inc.com uses cookies to help Modders-Inc.com identify and track visitors, their usage of Modders-Inc.com website, and their website access preferences. Modders-Inc.com visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Modders-Inc.com’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of Modders-Inc.com’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

Privacy Policy Changes
Although most changes are likely to be minor, Modders-Inc.com may change its Privacy Policy from time to time, and in Modders-Inc.com’s sole discretion. Modders-Inc.com encourages visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.

– Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL – http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

Modders-Inc has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
