    Privacy Statement
    ModChipStore.com believes strongly that all personal information of users and customers must be kept private and deserves protection. You can navigate and visit the website without registering or providing any personal information. However, we do require certain personal information and shipping address information for the processing of orders. ModChipStore.com respects your privacy. ModChipStore.com does not sell, rent or loan any personal information that is identifiable to any unrelated third parties. Any personal information given to us will be held with the utmost care and security and will not be used in ways to which you have not consented.

    General Issues

    ModChipStore.com requires certain name information as well as email addresses for submitting online comments and participating in discussion forums. Discussion Forum and Chat Room comments will be accessible by all site visitors. Therefore, if you don't want your comments or concerns to be viewed by any or all visitors, be advised not to submit them. ModChipStore.com reserves the right to edit or remove anything posted to the website that in our sole discretion is deemed to be offensive, illegal or inappropriate in any way whatsoever.

    We may use any personal information obtained and data collected to generate certain statistics and compile reports for internal use as well as for sharing with affiliates, licensees, subsidiaries and advertisers. Although this type of information will be used, none of the statistical data will include any information that will identify you personally. In short, your identity will always be kept anonymous unless used internally.

    How Do I Know My Credit Card and Personal Information is Secure?

    All personal information and credit card information in the order process is completed using a secure server. ModChipStore.com makes use of Secure Socket Layers (SSL) to encrypt all data provided by the customer. We make every possible effort, given today's technology, to ensure your personal and credit card information is kept safe and private.

    Does ModChipStore.com use 'Cookies'?

    When you shop at ModChipStore.com and depending on how you have your browser configured, a small file called a 'cookie' may be stored on your computer. This 'cookie' essentially contains a unique personal identification code that is used in order to save your Shopping Bag and review its contents the next time you bring it up. If you have 'cookies' turned off within your browser settings, your Shopping Cart contents will not be remembered each time you visit the site.

    Will Personal Information Be Disclosed To Any Other Parties?

    Under current ModChipStore.com policy, no personal information is currently sold or made available to any other person or party outside of the ModChipStore.com team. However, this policy may change in the future. If this policy does change, ModChipStore.com will announce any plans to do so and will ask you if you choose to opt in. We will make the assumption that you do not want to opt in, and if you do you would have to respond to our request. If no response is made, none of your personal information will be shared with any other person or party other than the ModChipStore.com team.

As well, we will provide personal information of customers and users for any reports about lawful or unlawful users activities that may be required by any authoritative body.

We reserve the right to disclose any personal information in full or in part in order to satisfy any law, regulation or government request.
