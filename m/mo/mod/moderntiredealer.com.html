Modern Tire Dealer Magazine strives to protect the privacy of visitors to the Modern Tire Dealer Website. Any information collected through Cookies and server logs is used to gauge how the site is being used and how Modern Tire Dealer Magazine can improve its visitors' online experiences. Any and all information collected is kept strictly confidential and no personal information is passed on to any third party

Use of the Modern Tire Dealer Magazine Website does not require any form of registration or subscription to the magazine. Subscribers to the magazine may receive access to additional pages within the site and may receive e-mail detailing new features and news on the Modern Tire Dealer Magazine Website. Visitors who visit the Modern Tire Dealer Magazine Website can also sign up to receive Modern Tire Dealer Magazine's e-mail newsletter without subscribing to Modern Tire Dealer. This e-mail may be sponsored or contain advertising in addition to the information provided by Modern Tire Dealer Magazine in the e-mail message.

When you request a subscription/renewal to our magazine or e-newsletters, we may, from time to time, send you carefully-screened messages on behalf of selected advertisers that might be of direct interest to you. Any e- mail or fax message of this type will be labeled as coming from Modern Tire Dealer Direct. To stop receiving these messages, please follow the instructions at the bottom of each correspondence.
