What information do we collect from users of the Service or this Site, and how do we use it?

We may keep all of this information indefinitely.
The Service contains two primary functions – a "public" function and "private" functions. You have the option to use the public function, the private function, or both.
  • The "public" function enables users to publicly share data with all users of the Service, as well as queries and associated data analyses, and to access, manipulate and collaborate on the data, queries and data analyses shared with the public function by other users.

Usage Data and Cookies

We collect some of this information through the use of cookies – a cookie is a small piece of data that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and then the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. We use cookies to record information about your session which may include your IP address, browser and device type, and which pages you access on the Site.
