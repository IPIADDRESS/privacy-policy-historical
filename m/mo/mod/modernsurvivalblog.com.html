DoubleClick DART Cookie
Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on ModernSurvivalBlog.com. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to ModernSurvivalBlog.com and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL, http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

ModernSurvivalBlog.com has no access to, or control over, these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

ModernSurvivalBlog.com’s privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.
