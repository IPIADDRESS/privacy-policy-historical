When do we collect information?
Quokka Sports, Inc. collects information when visitors request event updates, register, play games, enter sweepstakes or contests, shop online and send a postcard. What information is collected and how we use it is described below.
top

Event Updates
While going through our sites, you may be prompted to enter your email address to receive updates on the event you are viewing. Your email address is used for that purpose only and not shared with any third parties. To remove yourself from email lists please refer to the section below: "How to unsubscribe."
top

Registration
When you register, we ask you to provide your name, email address, zip code and country. You're also given the option to provide additional information which allows us to tailor the communications we send to you to fit your specific interests. We use your email address to notify you of new programs and events we think you will find of interest.

Registration information also may be used internally to better understand who visits the sites. This allows us to improve our content on an ongoing basis.

During registration, you may be asked if you'd like to receive information from one of our sponsors. If you say yes, we may send your email address to the sponsor you specified, but we will not share it with any other third parties. If you say no, we will not share your email address with any third parties.

Quokka Sports, Inc. does not share your registration information with third parties, unless you signed up to receive information from our sponsors.

Games
To enter a game, you may be asked to provide some personal information. This information is only used in connection with playing the game.

When you register for a game, you may be asked if you'd like to receive information regarding similar games. If you answer yes to this question you may receive game information from a third party.

We may also use your email address to notify you of new programs and events that we think you will find of interest (unless you requested not to receive information when you signed up for the game).
top

Sweepstakes and Contests
To enter a sweepstake or contest, you may be asked to provide your email address, mailing address and phone number. This information is needed to notify the winner and is not shared with third parties.

We may also use your email address to notify you of new programs and events that we think you will find of interest (unless you requested not to receive information when you entered the sweepstakes or contest).
top

Online Purchases
When you place an order, we need to know your name, phone number, email address, billing address, and credit card type, number and expiration date. This allows us to process and fulfill your order and to notify you of your order status. Your information is treated with the utmost security. Your information is used by Quokka Sports, Inc. and it's agents for the purpose of aggregate identification of our customers. Your personal information will not be sold.

When you place orders or access your account information, we offer the use of a secure server. The secure server software (SSL) encrypts all information you input before it is sent to us. All of the customer data we collect is protected against unauthorized access.

Quokka Sports Inc. and it's agents may use your email address to notify you of new programs and events that we think you will find of interest (unless you request not to receive information when you place the order).

To remove yourself from email lists please refer to the section below: "How to unsubscribe."
top

Sending Postcards
When you send a postcard, we ask for your email address and the recipient's. This information is not shared with third parties.

We will not communicate with the recipient unless they have contacted us separately.

We may also use your email address to notify you of new programs and events that we think you will find of interest (unless you request not to receive information when you send the postcard).
