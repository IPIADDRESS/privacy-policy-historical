Privacy Policy

Mojostreet Infomedia Services Pvt. Ltd. (“mojostreet,” “we,” or “us”) knows that you care about how your personal information is used and shared. This Privacy Policy explains what information of yours will be collected by mojostreet when you use the mojostreet Service. Additionally, it explains how you can control the collection, correction and/or deletion of such information. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy does not apply to information we collect by other means (including offline) or from other sources. Capitalized terms that are not defined in this policy are defined in our Terms of Use. Our expectation is that mojostreet Users will use this service for harmless social purposes. We would like to extend a cautionary note however, that by its nature, your location information may be subject to misuse and over-monitoring by others including your friends, family, or acquaintances. We therefore request you to manage your own mobile and profile privacy preferences on the service on a periodic basis.

What Information Is Collected?

Provided by User
Mojostreet will store the information that you provide to us during the registration process as well as during the use of the Service. This information may include but is not limited to your email address, birthday, and/ or Facebook/Twitter login ids. While using the service, you may choose to share more information such as locations and tips which may be publicly displayed to other Users and third party sites or services depending on your privacy and opt-in settings. Mojostreet automatically receives information about your location when you use the service. This personal information is used for improving the content of the service, customizing the advertizing on the Service and communicating with you about offers and discounts.

Automatically collected
When you use the service, mojostreet automatically records information including your location, IP address, browser and page requested from your browser or mobile platform. We treat this information as personally non-identifiable and use it only in aggregate form, unless otherwise required to under applicable law. We may provide aggregate information to mojostreet partners about how our customers collectively use the Service.

How Is My Information Protected?
Mojostreet uses commercially reasonable physical, managerial, and technical safeguards to preserve the integrity and security of your personal information. However, mojostreet cannot guarantee the security of user account information. Unauthorized entry or use, hardware or software failure, and other factors may compromise the security of user information at any time.

Your mojostreet account personal information is protected by a password for your privacy and security. You can prevent unauthorized access to your account and personal information by selecting and protecting your password appropriately and limiting access to your computer and browser by signing off after you have finished accessing your account.

How Is My Information Shared?

Sharing with friends
Your “friends” see the location and time of each of your check-ins, tips and photos (that you leave there), presidentships. While your friends will be able to see the time of your check-ins, other users will only be shown the date of the check-in. Individuals reading this information may use it or disclose it to other individuals or entities without our control and without your knowledge. Hence we urge you to exercise caution while adding a user as a “friend”.

Sharing with everyone
Information such as your name and picture, social network links, presidentships, list of friends, tips, photos are shared by default with the mojostreet community. However, you can choose to restrict the visibility of your check-in related information to your friends only.

Sharing with partners and authorities
Mojostreet works closely with its partners in order to provide discounts and other offers to you through mojostreet’s service. Mojostreet may choose to share your personal information with its partners for the potential benefit of customers.
We may release personal information when we believe in good faith that it is necessary in order to comply with the law of the land, to enforce our terms of use or to protect the rights, property, or safety of mojostreet, our employees, our users, or others.

Your Choices About Your Information
You have the right to decline to submit personally identifiable information through the service. If you choose to do so, mojostreet may not be able to provide certain services to you. You may update or correct your account information [and email preferences] at any time by logging in to your account. Currently your check-ins have two privacy settings. By default, check-ins are shared with everyone. You may choose to share a check-in with only your friends at the time of check-in and bypass the default setting.

How Is Information Removed From My Account?
Even after you remove information from your account or profile, copies of that information may remain viewable elsewhere, to the extent it has been shared with others, it was otherwise distributed pursuant to your privacy settings, or it was copied or stored by other users.

Tracking
Mojostreet uses Google Analytics to better understand who is using the Service, and how it is being used. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect and store anonymous information such as the time(s) of visit, pages visited, time spent on each page of the website, IP address, and type of operating system used. We use this information to improve the Service and make it appealing to as many users as possible.

Links
You may come across links to external websites on mojostreet. We are not responsible for the practices employed by these websites. This privacy policy governs only the practices collected on our service.

Will There Be Changes To This Privacy Policy? 
Mojostreet may amend this Privacy Policy periodically. Use of information we collect now is subject to the Privacy Policy in effect at the time such information is used. If we make material changes in the way we use Personal Information, we will notify you by posting an announcement on our Service or by sending you an email. Users are bound by any changes to the Privacy Policy when they use the Service after such changes have been first posted.

What If I Have Questions Or Concerns? If you have any questions or concerns regarding your privacy while using the Service, please send a detailed message to: privacy@mojostreet.com. We will make every effort to resolve your concerns.
