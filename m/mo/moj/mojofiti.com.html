Mojofiti Privacy Policy

Mojofiti’s Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect personal information, and how we use and disclose any such personal information. “Personal information,” as used in this Privacy Policy, is information that allows a person to directly identify another individual.

This Privacy Policy is incorporated into and is subject to Mojofiti’s Terms of Service. By using our site, you expressly consent to the processing of your personal information as set forth in this Privacy Policy. “Processing” means using cookies on a computer or deleting, using, combining or disclosing information, all of which will take place in the United States. If you reside in the European Union or any other country outside the United States, your personal information will be transferred to the United States, and processed and stored under United States privacy standards. By visiting our site, you consent to such transfer and processing in the United States.

How the Mojofiti Platform Operates

Mojofiti provides an online platform that enables a registered User to communicate with other Users through the posting of photographs and personal information (“user content”). Additionally, the Mojofiti Platform incorporates the use of translation technology and actual human translators to enable Mojofiti Users worldwide to make postings that can be read by other Mojofiti Users in spite of linguistic barriers.

While we make a good faith effort to provide accurate translations of all user content, this is not always possible. When you use our service, you should be aware that inaccuracies, defects and errors occasionally exist in the translations provided by the translation technology and Mojolingual Premium Translation Services employed on our Site. As stated in our Terms of Service, all translations are provided on an “AS IS” and “WITH ALL FAULTS” basis. Mojofiti does not intentionally misrepresent any user content, and shall not be held responsible for any inaccurate translations published on the Mojofiti Platform.

The Information We Collect

Our primary goals in collecting your personal information are to provide you with the services made available through our site, to enable us to communicate with you, and to enable you to manage your registered user account.

When you visit Mojofiti, you provide us with two types of information: personal information that you knowingly disclose to us and web site use information that we collect as you interact with our website. First, when you register with Mojofiti, you provide us with personal information, such as your name, your email address, your telephone number, your address, your gender, your birthday and any other personal or preference information that you provide us with. We also collect passive data when you visit our site such as your browser type, your IP address, and aggregate user data.

Additionally, we store certain information from your browser using cookies. A cookie is a piece of data stored on a User’s computer tied to information about the User. We use cookies to identify a User’s internet browser and store the User’s preferences. Data in cookies may also be used to authenticate user sessions or provide services. A session cookie enables certain features of our site and service and is deleted from your computer when you leave the site. A persistent cookie remains after you close your browser and may be used on subsequent visits to the site. Persistent cookies can be removed by following your web browser help file directions. You can also instruct your browser to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from a website.

Third Party Advertising

Advertisements that appear on Mojofiti are sometimes delivered directly to Users by third party advertisers. They automatically receive your IP address when this happens. These third party advertisers may also download cookies on to your computer, or use other technologies such as JavaScript and “web beacons” or “1×1 gifs” to measure the effectiveness of their ads and to personalize advertising content. Doing this allows the advertising network to recognize your computer each time they send you an advertisement in order to measure the effectiveness of their ads and to personalize advertising content. In this way, they may compile information about where individuals using your computer or browser saw their advertisements and determine which advertisements are clicked. Mojofiti does not have access to or control of the cookies that may be placed by third party advertisers. Third party advertisers have no access to your content within Mojofiti unless you choose to share it with them.

The Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by Mojofiti but does not cover the use of cookies or other tracking devices by any of its advertisers.

Mojofiti does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of thirteen or allow such persons to register. If you are under thirteen years of age, please do not attempt to register on our website.
