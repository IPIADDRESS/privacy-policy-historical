Privacy Policy

This policy describes the privacy practices of NMEDA with regard to information collected from visitors to this web site. This policy may be updated periodically, so please check back.

Web Site Use Information

Your privacy is very important to us. Our policy is to collect no personal information about you when you visit this site, unless you affirmatively choose to make such information available to us.

When you visit our website, the web server automatically recognizes only the Internet domain and IP address from which you accessed the site. Our web site utilizes a standard technology called “cookies” and web server logs to collect information about how our web site is used. Information gathered through cookies and web server logs may include the date and time of visits, the pages viewed, and time spent at our web site.

This is done to assist us with improvements to the website. This does not result in the identification of your personal e-mail address or other personal information.

Certain NMEDA web pages require a login and password. Use of these web pages and the information or programs that are downloadable from them are governed by a written agreement between the dealer and The National Mobility Equipment Dealers Association.

How Do We Use the Information That You Provide to Us?

If you choose to share personal information with NMEDA by entering the contest, voting in the contest, or logging in using social media, the information will be used only for the purposes you authorized.

What About Other Web Sites Linked to Our Web Site?

We are not responsible for the practices employed by web sites linked to or from our web site nor the information or content contained therein. Links to other web sites are provided solely as a resource for the users of our site.

Spread the Word

The National Mobility Equipment Dealers Association (NMEDA) is a non-profit organization dedicated to expanding mobility options for people with disabilities. The organization promotes and supports members engaged in the modification of quality transportation and wheelchair accessible vehicles. Over 600 international members work to provide safe and reliable adapted transportation solutions to help build the road to independence. Transportation is the key to freedom and NMEDA is the key to quality transportation.
