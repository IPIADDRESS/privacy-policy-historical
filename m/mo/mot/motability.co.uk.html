Privacy and cookies policy

Motability Operations Limited are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy online.

This policy sets out how we handle your personal information and provides comprehensive information about cookies and the way we use them on www.motability.co.ukOpens in new window .

Privacy

What we collect  

We collect the following information from visitors to our website:

  • IP addresses
  • Information regarding what pages are accessed and when.

Personal information

The personal information we collect about you is made up of the information you give us during your use of the website and our communications with you.

You can access and browse the majority of our website without disclosing your personal data . However, there are two exceptions to this – the Car Search and email forms as outlined below.

Email forms

Our website uses email forms for visitors to request information on services and policy information. We collect visitor contact information (i.e. email and postal address) and unique identifiers (i.e. National Insurance number).

Car Search user accounts privacy

User accounts are used by our Car Search application only to store a user’s shortlist for access from any computer / browser session regardless of cookies being deleted. The user account information and related shortlists are stored in a secure database by us. Only the following personal identifiable information is required to register a new account:

  • first name
  • last name
  • email address.

The Car Search application will only use this information for the following reasons:

  • ‘Name’ is used for a personalised welcome message
  • ‘Email address’ is used as the unique user name and to send the following emails:
  1. New account activation
  2. Forgotten password
  • Where you may explicitly request results/ car details/ shortlist / comparisons to be sent to you

A registered user account is not required to use core features of our online Car Search or to create a car shortlist, see Cookies for more information.

What we do with the information we gather

We use your information as for the following purposes:

  • to provide you with our website
  • to send information to our visitors as specifically requested by them
  • to verify the user’s identity in our record system
  • for internal analysis and research
  • to send publications, event information and marketing communications about our products and services which we think may interest you.

Our website does not enable our visitors to communicate with others or to post information to be accessed by others.

Visitors contact information and unique identifiers will not be shared with third parties other than to our Scheme partners to enable us to deal with your request.

Security

We place great importance on the security of all personally identifiable information associated with our visitors. We have security measures in place to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of personal data under our control. We have organisational and technical security measures in place to safeguard your personal information, and we are registered as data controllers under the Data Protection Act 1998.

Information about cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer by websites that you visit. They are stored on your computer to collect information about the general use of the site in order to make the website work more efficiently and to provide statistical data about our users' browsing actions and patterns.

What types of cookies does the website use?

We use cookies to:

  • remember your browsing preferences when you visit the site that can give you a better experience
  • to collect anonymous statistical information about the way you use the website so that we can improve the way the website works.

Updating your settings

You do not need to allow your browser to accept cookies in order to browse much of our website. If cookies are disabled then although the core features of the website will work some of the functionality of the website may be affected.

To disable cookies select ‘Tools / Internet Options / Privacy /Advanced’ from your internet browser then select the ‘override automatic handling’ option from the advanced settings dialogue box.

To find out more about the way cookies work, how to see what cookies have been set and how to manage and delete them, visit www.allaboutcookies.orgOpens in new window

Related FAQs

I am a Motability customer – how does Personal Independence Payment affect me?

From 8 April 2013, the Government is introducing a new benefit called Personal Independence Payment (PIP) which will replace Disability Living Allowance (DLA) for disabled people aged between 16 and 64.

Motability customers currently use their DLA to pay for their vehicle leases. So, it is important that you are aware of the Government’s planned changes to disability benefits, which could affect your eligibility to use the Scheme.

Find out more about PIP

Can I get discounts on Haven Holidays?

Haven Holidays are working in association with Motability (the Charity that oversees the Motability scheme), to offer customer savings of up to 50% off Haven family holidays and short breaks. For every Haven Holiday booked using this promotion, Haven Holidays will donate 10% of the booking value to Motability's charitable fund.

To find out more information, please visit the information page about Haven Holidays .

Where can I find out about current events?

Information about Motability events is available on our fundraising events area. You can also find details of exhibitions where you will be able to find more information about Motability.

Does Motability do travel insurance?

The charity Motability have teamed up with ACE European Group Ltd. to launch Motability Travel Insurance. You can find out more details about travel insurance or call the freephone telephone number on 0800 519 9957.

Is there a customer magazine?

Lifestyle is a magazine that is sent free of charge to all Motability customers every three months to keep our customers up to date with all our news. If you are not a customer but would like to receive the magazine, you should send a cheque for £10 to cover 1 years subscription (made payable to The Big Agency Ltd) to: 22 Stephenson Way, London, NW1 2HD.

If you are currently receiving Lifestyle and would prefer not to, please contact our Customer Services team to unsubscribe.

Does Motability do pet insurance?

The charity Motability have teamed up with Agria Pet Insurance Ltd. to launch Motability Pet Insurance. You can find out more details about pet insurance or call the freephone telephone number on 0800 369 9094.

Can I get help towards the costs?

It may be possible for Motability to offer financial help towards the cost of the Advance Payment or adaptations. If you are interested in this, please contact our Customer Services team to discuss. Please note that any application will be means tested, and Motability will not fund 100% of the solution.

Who are Motability and Motability Operations?

Motability provide worry-free leasing packages for over 600,000 customers to help disabled people with their personal mobility.

As a fundraising charity, Motability raises over £2.4 million for our Charitable Grants Programme to help disabled people who have or are applying for a Motability vehicle and who may not be able to afford the mobility solution they may need. Motability provides a grant towards car adaptations, Advance Payments for larger more expensive vehicles and driving lessons for young disabled people aged 16-24.

Motability regulates and oversees the activities of Motability Operations, a not for profit organisation which runs and operates the Motability Scheme.
