About Cookies

When you visit the MotionElements Website, we place a text file called a “cookie” in the browser directory of your computer’s hard drive. A cookie is a small piece of information that a website can store on your web browser and later retrieve. Cookies enable the website to recognize the information you have consented to give us and help us determine what portions of the website are most appropriate for your professional needs. As a result, cookies shall allow you to access your clip bins and order archive and edit your Registration Data. Your Internet browser software probably enables you to refuse any cookies placed on your hard drive. If you choose to refuse cookies placed by our website, you will have to log into the website with your user name and password. We do NOT use cookies to examine your surfing behavior before or after leaving a MotionElements website. If at any time you believe that MotionElements has not followed the above policy, or if you would like your information to be removed from our system, please e-mail us at support@motionelements.com.
