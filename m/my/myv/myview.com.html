Return Path Privacy Statement

Effective: November 1, 2006

Protecting your privacy is very important to Return Path. We know that you're concerned with privacy on the Internet, and we've taken many steps to help safeguard your information.

This privacy statement is provided to inform you of our privacy policy and practices and of the choices you can make about the way your personal information is collected online and how that information is used. By personal information we mean information about you collected through this Web site, including your email address, or any other information.

The organization collecting information at this Web site:

Authentic Response is the market research business owned and operated by Return Path. Return Path operates MyView on www.myview.com through its market research service, Authentic Response. Members who opt-in through MyView are signing up to receive survey invitations, not email advertisements. Often, these marketing research companies offer incentives to participants in the Survey. The mailings may come from either MyView or a MyView market research partner.

Panel members can unsubscribe from the service at any time, no questions asked. We know that just because you joined the panel a month ago doesn’t mean that you necessarily want to stay on it forever. Every survey invitation we send is coded with a special footer that allows you to remove your name from our panel and services automatically by clicking a link provided in the message. In addition, you can discontinue your MyView membership by following the instructions at our subscription review site. To update your email address, you will need to unsubscribe the old email address and sign up your new email address.  Please send an email to update-email@myview.com.

We want our panel members to feel comfortable using the Site and participating in MyView Surveys, so we have created this Privacy Policy to let you know what information we may collect from you on the Site, and through the Surveys and how we may use that information. Please take a moment to review the terms of our Privacy Policy. By using the Site and participating in the Service or Surveys, you agree to accept the terms of our Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the Site or participate in the Service or Surveys.

The information we collect

Collection and Use
To simply view the Site without registering for MyView, you will not be required to provide any personal information. However, to register for MyView or to participate in the Surveys, you are required to provide your email address. To serve you better and send you the most surveys, we may also seek other personally and non-personally identifiable information about you from you, our market research partners where you have given them your consent, or where such information is otherwise publicly available. Such information may include postal, demographic or profile data.

If you provide us with your email address or other personal information, or if we obtain such information about you from you or from one of our market research partners, we will retain and use that information solely for the purpose of sending you Surveys. When necessary, we may also use your personal information to ensure compliance with MyView policies and applicable law.

In addition, if you participate in MyView, you will be asked to provide certain demographic data (like your age, gender, zip code, income) and profile data (like occupation, shopping habits and other areas of interest) as certain Surveys may be directed to those participants who meet a particular demographic profile. You will be asked to answer certain “pre-qualification” questions on a pre-qualification page hosted by us. The information we collect on this page will be used to determine whether you are eligible to participate in the Survey and will also be maintained by us to enable us to provide you with more relevant Surveys in the future. Your answers to these pre-qualification questions will not be provided to the marketing research company conducting the Survey or to advertisers. If you qualify for a particular Survey after answering questions on our pre-qualification page, you will be directed to the Survey itself, on pages hosted by the research company. Note that any information you provide on the pages hosted by the research company and during the course of the Survey will be subject to the research company’s privacy policy, not ours.

We also collect information on the "contact us" page and other areas of the site to allow you to contact us directly with any questions or comments you may have. This information is used to respond directly to your questions or comments. We may also file your comments to improve our site or services, or review and discard the information.

Except for the limited situations discussed in this Privacy Policy, we will not share your personal information with third parties. In the event of corporate change, users will be notified in accordance with the “Changes to these terms” section of the policy if their personal information is provided to the new corporate entity as a result of corporate ownership change.

Finally, MyView reserves the right to disclose panel member information in special cases when we have reason to believe that disclosing this information is necessary to identify, contact or bring legal action against someone who may be causing injury to or interference with (either intentionally or unintentionally) MyView’s rights or property, other visitors, panel members, or anyone else that could be harmed by such activities. Authentic Response also reserves the right to disclose visitor or list member information when we believe in good faith that the law requires it.

Aggregate information

From time to time, we may collect general, non-personal, statistical information about the use of the Site and the Service, such as how many visitors visit a specific page on the Site, how long they stay on that page, and which hyperlinks, if any, they “click” on. The company also collects and reports aggregate statistical information on the open rates and response rates generated by the email campaigns we send. We collect this information through the use of technologies such as “cookies” and “IP addresses”, which are discussed in greater detail below. We collect this information in order to determine what types of surveys are of interest to panel members and to therefore enhance the Service. We may also group this information into aggregate visitor data in order to describe the use of the Site and Service to our existing or potential business partners or other third parties, or in response to a government request. However, please be assured that this aggregate data will in no way personally identify you, other list members or any other visitors to the Site.

IP Addresses
An IP address is a number that’s automatically assigned to your computer whenever you’re surfing the web. Web servers, the computers that “serve” web pages, automatically identify your computer by its IP address. MyView collects IP addresses for purposes of system administration, to report aggregate information to third parties and to track the use of the Site. When visitors request pages from the Site or click on a link in a mailing sent through the Service or through MyView, our servers log the visitors’ IP addresses. We reserve the right to use IP addresses to identify a visitor or list member when it will enhance the users experience or if we feel it is necessary to enforce compliance with the Site’s policies or to protect the comapny, the Site, its visitors, the Service, MyView, the panel members or others.

Cookies
Cookies are pieces of information that a websites transfers to an individual’s hard drive for record-keeping purposes. Cookies make web-surfing easier for you by saving your preferences while you’re at the Site. The use of cookies is an industry standard -- you’ll find them at most websites. We use cookies on the subscription review site to verify the subscriber’s login status. They are not used to store personal information, nor to retain historical or transactional subscriber data. By showing how and when visitors use the Site, cookies help us see which areas are popular and which are not. Information such as the total number of visitors and pages viewed is most easily tracked with cookies. We may use the information from cookies to make improvements and updates to the Site and to tailor our services to our visitors’ and survey participants’ needs.

You always have the option of disabling cookies using your browser preferences. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies. You can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, please be aware that some features of the Site will not function properly or may be slower if you refuse cookies.

Firewalls

Protecting the security of your personal information is very important to us. When we store information that you have provided to us when registering for the Service, that information is protected by security measures that include “firewalls” (a combination of computer hardware and software that helps keep unauthorized visitors from accessing information within our computer network), “intrusion detection systems” (a combination of computer hardware and software that helps detect any unauthorized visitors) and other tools such as data encryption and physical security, where appropriate. Privacy policy for children
