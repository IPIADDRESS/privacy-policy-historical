MyLocally Privacy Policy

MyLocally is a division of eLocal Listing, LLC.

eLocal Listing, LLC has a strong commitment to respecting the privacy of all visitors to our directories and local electronic marketplaces. This policy covers how eLocal Listing, LLC treats personal information that it collects and receives, including information related to your past use of eLocal Listing, LLC services. Personal information is information about you and or your business that is personally identifiable such as your name, address, email address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available.


This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that eLocal Listing, LLC does not own or control, or to people that eLocal Listing, LLC does not employ or manage. In particular, eLocal Listing, LLC has no control over the privacy policies of any vendor or publishers website linked through our directories or local electronic marketplaces.

eLocal Listing, LLC collects personal information when you register your company with eLocal Listing, LLC or when you use a credit card. eLocal Listing, LLC may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies.

When advertisers register their company, we ask for information such as: their name, phone number, email address, zip code, occupation, industry and various industry-based criteria. This information may be made available to the public as a listing in our directory pages, within a coupon ad or text ad. Additionally, this information may be made available to a site visitor to whom you/we send a reply to an inquiry.

eLocal Listing, LLC automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, eLocal Listing, LLC cookie information, and the page you request. In accordance with the Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act, eLocal Listing, LLC does not intend to record personal information submitted by children younger than 13 years old. Children under 13 years old should submit personally identifiable information to eLocal Listing, LLC only with the explicit permission of their parent or legal guardian.

eLocal Listing, LLC will not contact children under age 13 about special offers or for marketing purposes. eLocal Listing, LLC does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except to provide services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:

  • We provide the information to trusted (publishers) partners who work on behalf of or with eLocal Listing, LLC under confidentiality agreements. These publishers may use your personal information to help eLocal Listing, LLC communicate with you about offers from eLocal Listing, LLC directory advertisers and our marketing (publishing) partners. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information.

  • We respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims;

  • We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of eLocal Listing, LLC terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.

  • We transfer information about you if eLocal Listing, LLC is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, eLocal Listing, LLC will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

  • eLocal Listing, LLC may set and access eLocal Listing, LLC cookies on your computer.

eLocal Listing, LLC may update this policy. We will place a prominent notice on our portion of the publishers site about significant changes in the way we treat personal information.
