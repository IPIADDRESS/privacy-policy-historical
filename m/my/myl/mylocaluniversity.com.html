  Automotive Technology

Automotive technology is mostly about implementing those technologies that are popularly used in the process of designing and constructing various types of automobiles. The emergence of advanced technology heightens the level of innovation, power and performance that are meant to be incorporated in automobiles.

A formal automotive technology degree program would train you on areas of engine performance, air conditioning, brakes, steering, suspension systems, and other basic automotive fundamentals.

At most automotive technical colleges, students are also trained on power tools, diagnostic aids for trouble shooting, lifting equipment and photo tachometers. Those who complete their courses from automotive technology schools could find jobs as technicians, transmission specialists, automotive engineers, and much more.
