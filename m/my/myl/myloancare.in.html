Our News - Jan 2018
  • 2018-01-15 : Indian bank revises rates on Domestic Term Deposits
    Indian Bank has revised its rates on Term Deposits. The new rates for less than one crore deposits now range from 4.5% to 6%, and those on deposits of one to five crore range from 4.25% to 5%. These rates are effective from 08 January, 2018.
  • 2018-01-15 : Karnataka Bank records 27.5% profits in third quarter
    Owing to the improvement in asset quality and credit growth, Karnataka Bank has recorded 27.52% net profit growth in the third quarter of this fiscal (2017-18). Net profits were registered at Rs. 87.38 crore in Q3 FY18, as opposed to Rs. 68.52 crore in the corresponding period in 2016-17.
  • 2018-01-15 : Yes Bank Q3 profit up 30.62%
    Yes Bank Ltd recorded its net profit increased by 30.62% from a year ago. This is owed to higher net interest income and lower provisions. Net profit stood at Rs. 882.63 crore in the December quarter, as compared to Rs. 675.74 crore a year ago. 
  • 2018-01-15 : HDFC crosses Rs. 3 trillion market cap for first time
    Shares of HDFC crossed the mark of Rs. 3 trillion market capitalisation for the first time as its shares rose over 6.7% due to the Rs. 13,000 crore fund raising through qualified institutional placement (QIP) and preference issue.
  • 2018-01-13 : IDFC Bank, Capital First announce merger
    IDFC Bank and nonbanking financial company (NBFC) Capital First are likely to announce a merger to form an institution with around Rs 1.4 lakh crore of assets and nearly 64 lakh customers. The partnership could also help IDFC Bank to quickly grow its assets.
