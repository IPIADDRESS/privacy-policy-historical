Your Privacy

At MyKnobs.com, we are dedicated to protecting your privacy. We use your personal information just to process orders and to help serve you better.

What information do we gather and how do we use it?

At the time of ordering, we require to know your name, mailing address, e-mail address, credit card number, as well as expiration date. This permits us to correctly process and fulfill your order and to let you know of your order status.

We may also use the information we are provided to notify you about important functionality changes to the Web site, new MyKnobs.com services, and special offers we think you'll find valuable, from time to time. If you would rather not receive the kind of information, visit the Your Account page or send us an email to change your preferences.

How does MyKnobs.com protect customer information?

When you place orders or access your account information, we offer the use of a secure server. The secure server software (SSL) encrypts all information you input before it is sent to us. Furthermore, all of the customer data we collect is protected against unauthorized access by an industry leader Yahoo.

About "cookies"

"Cookies" are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. Our cookies enable us to provide features like storing items in your shopping cart between visits. Most latest Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can change your browser to such activity.

Your consent
