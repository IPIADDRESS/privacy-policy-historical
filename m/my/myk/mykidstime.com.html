Privacy Policy
Users

A user is free to access the MyKidsTime.ie website without providing any information to us.  If you choose to leave information or comment, MyKidsTime.ie will respond to you, addressing and using your information only for the purposes for which you agree. The right to privacy will be respected at all times by us. 

Subscribers

A subscriber will be asked to provide information at the time of subscribing.  Any information you provide to MyKidsTime.ie must be collected fairly, i.e. we must explain how we will use it.  Your personal information will only be used within MyKidsTime.ie.  It will never be supplied to any third parties without first asking your permission, unless we are under obligation by law to disclose it.  We will never sell your email address or other personal information to third parties.  Your information is collected and stored according to Irish Data Protection rules. 

Data Security

We are committed to data security and have put in place appropriate procedures to safeguard any information we collect online.

Child Privacy

We never collect or maintain personal information on MyKidsTime.ie from those we actually know are under 16. 

Contacting Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please send us an email at enquiries@mykidstime.ie.
