Privacy Policy

MyBuys Commitment to Privacy

Every visitor to the Website is important part of our business and we place a high value on any personal information you decide to share with us. This privacy statement describes how MyBuys will preserve and protect any information you provide.

MyBuys Has Specific Content and Events That Require Registration

Your participation on our site and decision to share contact information, is always optional and at you discretion. There are downloads and information request pages that are only accessible by requesting them first via an email request.

You may "Opt Out" at Any Time

Through any HTML invitation or mailing you may choose to discontinue further receipt. There will be a location at the bottom of the electronic content to "opt out", or discontinue further receipt at any time.

Non-disclosure of Information to Outside Organizations

Your contact information received for Website registrations, or business specific information, will not be disclosed to outside organizations for use in solicitations or marketing. There may be occasions where MyBuys shares specific information with business partners to promote cooperative engagements or to satisfy information requests that you have made through our Website.

Customized Web and Personalized HTML Correspondence

MyBuys may make use of technology to deliver a customized HTML notifications and personalized web experience, including the use of cookies. Use of these capabilities does not mean that we automatically know anything about the individual we are communicating with. Cookie information is only used to provide better user interaction. We will not contact you based on information from these cookies, or use cookie information for any other marketing purposes.

Data Collection

Except as described above, the information collected from the Web site is used only to help us customize and organize our Website and user experiences. This information includes site visits, domain information, as well as usage and traffic patterns. This information is not used for identifying individuals.

MyBuys Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site or your dealings with this Web site, please contact us.
