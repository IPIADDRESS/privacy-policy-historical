Privacy Policy

Our privacy policy describes the practices that apply to the MyShape.com site, including, the information we collect about you, when and how we collect that information and what may happen to that information. We believe you should know as much as possible about our practices so that you can make an informed decision about the extent of your use of our site. MyShape.com will occasionally update its privacy statement to reflect company and customer feedback.

Except as described herein, we never sell or trade addresses with other companies, without your consent. Additionally, except as described herein, no personal information gathered via the MyShape registration process will be sold or shared with any third party without your consent. It is possible that the business or certain assets of MyShape (including intellectual property assets) could be sold which includes the sale of customer addresses and other personal information. If the buyer intends to continue the MyShape.com site to conduct business, or intends to honor the existing privacy policy, then such information will be sold without your consent. In all other cases (including sale of personal information to a buyer which will use such information for direct marketing purposes only), MyShape will seek your consent through email, giving you an opt out choice (for example, unless you check a box opting out by the stated deadline, you will be deemed to have consented and your personal information will be sold or shared with other companies for marketing purposes). Notwithstanding any of the foregoing, aggregate, non-personally identifiable information may be sold or shared with the press, advertisers, or suppliers (for example, buying or shopping habits of customers, that 70% of our users are women).

Updating Your Information

Upon your request, MyShape.com will provide you with your customer ID, password or transaction information (e.g., dates of purchases, amounts and merchandise information) and contact information (e.g., name, address, phone number and email address) that we maintain.

Security

MyShape.com is committed to protecting the privacy of your personal information. When we transfer and receive certain types of sensitive information, such as financial information, including your credit card number, name and address, it is encrypted through industry-standard encryption technology known as Secure Sockets Layer (SSL). Depending on your browser, this SSL will have encryption keys of up to 128 bits. Only parties responsible for processing your order have access to your credit card and bank information.

Cookies

MyShape.com uses cookies to record session information, such as items that consumers add to their shopping bag. Cookies are a collection of information stored by your browser on your computer. You must have cookies enabled in order for you to be able to perform many of the general functions on the website. With cookies enabled, the MyShape.com website will have the capacity to deliver a more customized and complete shopping experience, to track visits from our affiliates or partners and to enable the use of our shopping bag. For instance, MyShape.com uses cookies to deliver content specific to your interests and save your password so you don't have to reenter it each time you visit our site.

Links to Other Sites

MyShape.com may occasionally contain links to other sites. Please be aware that MyShape.com is not responsible for the privacy practices of these other sites. We encourage you to be aware when you are leaving our site and to read the privacy statements of every website that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to the MyShape.com website.

Notice of Changes

MyShape.com's privacy policy is subject to change without notice. At any time, you may visit our website to view the current policy.
