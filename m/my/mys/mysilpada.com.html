Cookies and Tracking Technology

Cookies are small files that enable us to better customize your online experience with us. For instance, cookies may assist us in tailoring a web page or advertisement to better match your interests and preferences. Information gathered through cookies may include the date and time of visits, the pages viewed, the time spent at our website, and websites visited just before and just after visiting our websites. Please be assured that cookies are safe. They are not programs that can be used to damage files and your personal information cannot be gathered from them. You are always free to decline our cookies if your browser permits, although, in that case, you may not be able to use certain features on our Site. In addition, Silpada Designs may log the Internet Protocol ("IP") address of the location from where you are visiting our websites. This is a security measure to protect our company information and detect fraud.

Broadly speaking, we use personal information for purposes of administering and expanding our business activities, providing customer service and making available other products and services to our independent Representatives and online visitors.
