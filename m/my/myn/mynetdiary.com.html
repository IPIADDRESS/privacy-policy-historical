Information Collection and Use

Opening a MyNetDiary.com Account

Personal Information

When you open an account with MyNetDiary.com we ask for information such as your e-mail address, name, date of birth, gender, weight, and height. This is information is used to establish your account and calculate body mass index and Recommended Daily Allowances (RDA) of nutrients per USDA recommendations.

Credit Card Information

We use PayPal to process credit card transactions. You may choose to pay for your MyNetDiary.com account on PayPal site using any payment method accepted by PayPal. In this case, we do not obtain and do not store any credit card information.

You may choose to enter credit card information on MyNetDiary.com web site. In this case, we will store credit card information for the duration of the sign up process, until your credit card is charged and your new MyNetDiary.com account is created, and delete credit card number and its expiration date from the database after that. For your convenience, we will store credit card billing address, so you will not need to enter it again when extending MyNetDiary.com membership.

Foods, Activities, and Body Measurements

While providing food and exercise diary services, MyNetDiary.com collects personal foods, activities, and body measurements information. Recognizing highly personal nature of this information, we take special precautions protecting this information. We do not share personally identifiable information with any third parties.

Extending Membership

When you extend your MyNetDiary.com membership and choose to enter credit card information on our site, we will store this information only for the shortest time possible to perform this credit card transaction, and delete it from the database when this credit card transaction is completed.

Referrals

When you refer another person to MyNetDiary.com, you will provide this person's e-mail address. This e-mail address is stored for the sole purpose of identifying when the person referred opens a new account with MyNetDiary.com, so your account could be credited. If you choose so at the moment of making the referral, we can send one e-mail to that address inviting to MyNetDiary.com. We will not use this e-mail address for any other purposes and will not share it with any third parties.

Use of Information and Disclosure

MyNetDiary.com uses collected information for the following general purposes: products and services provision, billing, identification and authentication, services improvement, contact, and research.

We collect the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail, aggregate information on what pages web site users access or visit, and information volunteered by users, such as forum messages or surveys. The information we collect is used to improve the content of MyNetDiary.com and the quality of our service, and is not shared with or sold to third parties, except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission.

Personally Identifiable Information

MyNetDiary.com shares personally identifiable information only when requried to complete your order, in particular, MyNetDiary.com sends credit card billing information to our credit card processor (PayPal).

MyNetDiary.com does not share personally identifiable information with third parties when not required to complete your order.

Aggregate Information

We aggregate statistics of MyNetDiary.com and may publish some of them. None of these statistics will contain any personally identifiable information. For example, we may publish total weight lost by MyNetDiary.com members over time.

MyNetDiary.com Forums

If you use post information to MyNetDiary.com forums, you should be aware that any personally identifiable information you post there can be read, collected, or used by other users of the forum, and could be used to send you unsolicited messages. We are not responsible for the personally identifiable information you choose to post to MyNetDiary.com forums.

Links to Other Sites

MyNetDiary.com web site contains links to other sites that are not owned or controlled by MyNetDiary.com or 4Technologies Corporation. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage you to be aware when you leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every Web site that collects personally identifiable information.

This privacy policy applies only to information collected by MyNetDiary.com web site.

Legal Disclaimer

MyNetDiary.com reserves the right to disclose personally identifiable information as required by applicable law or pursuant to a court or similar order, or as deemed necessary, in MyNetDiary.com's discretion, to protect the legal rights or the property of MyNetDiary.com, a member or third party, or to prevent personal injury.

Technical Details

Cookies

  • A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive.
  • Cookies are required to use MyNetDiary.com services.
  • We use cookies to record current session information.
  • If you choose so during sign in process, we can create a permanent cookie on your computer that will allow access to your MyNetDiary.com account from this computer without entering your user name and password.
  • We use third-party tracking services that use cookies to track non-personally identifiable information about visitors to our site in the aggregate to gather web site usage statistics.

Access Logs

Similar to other web sites, we gather certain information automatically and store it in log files. This information includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, referring/exit pages, operating system, browser type, date/time stamp, and clickstream data.

We use this information, which does not identify individual users, to analyze trends, to administer the site, and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole.

In addition, we record your IP address with your personally identifiable information. We do so to help protect ourselves and our members from abusive users of our site.

Security and Encryption

We use industry-standard encryption technology - 128-bit Secure Socket Layer (SSL) when transmitting sensitive information, such as a credit card information.

As described in the Credit Card Information and Extending Membership sections above, we store credit card information only for a shortest possible periods while your order is being prepared until the credit card is charged successfully, and only if you choose to do so, the other option being payment on PayPal web site. We do not store credit card information on permanent basis.

You are will be required to sign in to your MyNetDiary.com account after a certain period of time has elapsed during current session to protect you against others accidentally accessing your account data.

Data Storage

4Technologies Corporation uses third party vendors and hosting partners to provide the necessary hardware, software, networking, storage, and related technology required to operate MyNetDiary.com.

MyNetDiary.com database is stored on secured servers owned and operated by 4Technologies Corporation.

We may store encrypted database backups off site with a third party storage provider to ensure safety of the data in the case of an emergency or catastrophe.

Business Transitions

In the event 4Technologies Corporation or MyNetDiary.com goes through a business transition, such as a merger, acquisition by another company, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, your personally identifiable information may be among the assets transferred. In this event, we will notify you before personally identifiable information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy by sending a notice to the e-mail address specified in your MyNetDiary.com account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.

Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you about significant changes in this privacy policy by sending a notice to the e-mail address specified in your MyNetDiary.com account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
