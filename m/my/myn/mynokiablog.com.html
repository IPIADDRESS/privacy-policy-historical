IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons

A “cookie” is “used for an origin website to send state information to a user’s browser and for the browser to return the state information to the origin site.”

This is used at MyNokiaBlog.com to recognise when you visit, perhaps to remember settings, information, read articles, polls and commenter details.

Cookies themselves do not contain any personal information, and MyNokiaBlog.com does not use cookies to collect personal information. Cookies may also be used by 3rd party content providers such as newsfeeds.
