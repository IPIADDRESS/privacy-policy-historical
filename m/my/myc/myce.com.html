Privacy Policy

RankOne Media is committed to protecting your privacy. The following privacy policy deals with the privacy issues around our website.

Collection of your personal information

In order to use some services from our website, you will be asked to register and provide us with personal information such as your e-mail address, (user)name and country. We may also collect demographic information like age and gender. Information is asked when you register for an update, post on the community or just register as a member. Registration is a voluntary option.

We may collect information about your visit, including the pages you view, the links you click and other actions taken in connection with our websites. We also collect certain standard information that your browser sends to every website you visit, such as your IP address, browser type and language, access times and referring Web site addresses. Most of the information is automatically collected using the Google Analytics statistics tool. This information is needed to operate this community, not to track you down.

Use of your personal information

We use your personal information in order to operate and improve our websites and the service we deliver. These uses may include making the sites or services easier to use by eliminating the need for you to repeatedly enter the same information; performing research and analysis aimed at improving our products and services; and displaying content and advertising that are customized to your interests and preferences.

Sharing of your personal information

Our community has member profiles which disclose information about your account. However we will not provide or sell your personal information to a third party without your consent.

Use of cookies

Like most other websites, our websites use “cookies” to enable you to sign in to our services and to help personalize your online experience. A cookie is a small text file that is placed on your hard disk by a web page server. Cookies contain information that can later be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. We strongly advise you to enable cookies for a maximum browsing experience. Cookies are, for example, used on the forums so members don´t have to re-enter login details when they visit the site. They are completely harmless and will only make your life easier.

Use of e-mail addresses

We are just like you concerned about controlling unsolicited commercial e-mail, or “spam.” Our websites may use mailing list services for its users but e-mail addresses are always based on “double opt-in”. RankOne Media will not sell, lease or rent our e-mail subscriber lists to third parties without your consent.

Advertisement Serving

When you visit our website, we use third-party advertising companies to serve some or all of the advertisements on our site. Often, these third-party advertising companies may employ cookie and pixel tag technologies to measure the advertisements’ effectiveness. These companies may use non personal information about your visits to our and other web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services that may interest you. As part of our relationship with such companies, we do not provide them any personally identifiable information. Tracking technology utilized by these companies is subject to their own privacy policies. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, visit www.networkadvertising.org.

RankOne Media or its advertisers also have agreements with independent online retailers that enable visitors to purchase items through third party sites. These purchases are subject to the policies and procedures of those sites. Order fulfillment and customer service relating to orders placed at any of those third party sites, including the collection of applicable sales taxes, are the sole responsibility of such sites. Any concerns regarding order fulfillment, customer service, privacy policies or other issues relating to these third party online retail sites should be directed to the Webmaster or site administrator of the site.

Transfer of Information

We reserve the right to transfer any information accumulated as described above in the event of the sale of part or all of our assets and/or stock. By visiting our websites and by registering as a member you consent to the collection and use of information in the manner herein described.

Changes in privacy policy

This privacy policy is subject to change without notice. If you have any questions about this privacy policy please don’t hesitate to contact us. You can contact us by clicking on the contact link located on every page.
