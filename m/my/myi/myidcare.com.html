Privacy Policy – Updated 6/1/2009

ID Experts uses its best efforts to respect the privacy of online visitors. ID Experts does not collect personal information (such as name, address, e-mail address, or phone number) unless you voluntarily provide the information or are a member in ID Experts' Identity Theft program For example, if you have questions about ID Experts' services, you may provide your e-mail address, and a response to your inquiry will be sent to that e-mail address. You do not need to register or provide information to view some sections of this site; however, only members enrolled in the ID Experts Identity Theft program may enter the Member Services section. If you wish to become a member, ID Experts will request your name, address, phone number, e-mail address, and other information. ID Experts will only request and use the information necessary to administer the program.

ID Experts is committed to maintaining privacy and security controls over the information that you provide. Access of your personal information is restricted to the employees who are providing you with the products and services offered by ID Experts. Appropriate security safeguards and encryptions are utilized to protect your information. ID Experts uses passwords, personal identification numbers, and other applicable security measures to prevent an inappropriate entity or individual from gaining access to or knowledge of your data. ID Experts cautions its members and others to be aware that cell phones are not a secure means of transmitting personal information.

ID Experts shares personal information with third parties only if you authorize it, where required by law, and only with the business affiliates that work with ID Experts to provide our products and services to you. ID Experts has agreements with its business affiliates that require them to maintain the confidentiality of any personal information sent to them. In addition, ID Experts requires that they use that information only for the specific and limited purposes related to providing ID Experts products and services, and that they have in place the necessary security standards (approved by ID Experts) to maintain the privacy and security of your personal information. The ID Experts Web site uses a feature of your web browser known as a "cookie," which is a small text file that gathers non-personal information to facilitate access to and use of the site. "Cookies" also allow recognition of repeat visitors, track the pages visited, and collect data to help continue to improve the site. ID Experts does not use cookies for other purposes and does not share the information gathered by the cookies with third parties. Most browsers include a feature that enables the user to accept or deny cookies.

ID Experts may make changes to our Privacy Policy from time to time. If you have any questions about the Privacy Policy please contact ID Experts at assistance@idexpertscorp.com or 800-298-7558.
