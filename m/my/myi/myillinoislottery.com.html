Illinois Lottery Privacy Policy

Effective as of March 25, 2012

INTRODUCTION

Thank you for visiting the Illinois Lottery's website, which includes www.illinoislottery.com and www.myillinoislottery.com (collectively, this "Website"). We recognize that individuals and businesses need to understand how their privacy is protected when they visit this Website, so we have established the following privacy policy (this "Privacy Policy") for our users. You must be 18 years or older to use this Website. Information about your location may also be collected via geo-filtering software if you attempt to purchase Internet Tickets on this Website.

We may change this Privacy Policy at any time without notice to you by posting changes on this Website. We encourage you to check back and review this Privacy Policy from time to time. Your continued use of this Website following our posting of changes will mean that you accept the revised Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy describes the Illinois Lottery's privacy practices regarding information collected from users of this Website. This Privacy Policy describes what information is collected and how that information is used. Because this Privacy Policy only applies to this Website, if you link to another entity's website through this Website, you should examine that entity's privacy policy to determine its website practices (including other state agency websites).

For purposes of this Privacy Policy, "Personal Information" is information about a natural person that is readily identifiable to that specific individual. Personal Information includes such things as an individual's name, address and telephone number. A domain name or Internet protocol address is not considered Personal Information.

II. INFORMATION AUTOMATICALLY COLLECTED WHEN YOU VISIT THIS WEBSITE

If you do nothing during your visit but browse through this Website and review information such as game rules or winning numbers, the Illinois Lottery, through the parties involved in creating, producing, or delivering this Website (including Northstar Lottery Group, LLC and GTECH Corporation) (collectively, the "Contractors"), will automatically gather and store the following information about your visit:

  • The Internet protocol (IP) address and domain name used (for example, aol.com if you are accessing from America Online). The IP address is a numerical identifier assigned either to your Internet Service Provider (ISP) or directly to your computer. Your location may also be identified via geo-filtering of your IP address;
  • The type of browser and operating system you used;
  • The date and time you visited this Website;
  • The webpages or services you accessed at this Website;
  • The website you visited prior to coming to this Website;
  • The website you visit as you leave this Website;
  • Any ads you clicked on to access this Website;
  • If you use the ShareThis feature, what content you share from this Website, with whom you share the content, and where you post the content;
  • If you downloaded a form or other document, the form or document that was downloaded; and
  • The name of the country in which your server is located.

None of the foregoing information is deemed to be Personal Information.

This information that is collected automatically is used to improve this Website's content and to help the Illinois Lottery understand how users are interacting with this Website. This information is also collected for statistical analysis, to determine what information is of most and least interest to our users, to improve the utility of the material available on this Website, and for other purposes described in this Privacy Policy. Methods that your information may be automatically collected include, but are not limited to:

  • Cookies

    Cookies are text files stored on your web browser to provide a means of distinguishing among users of this Website. To better serve you, we use "transactional cookies" to enhance or customize your visit to this Website and to recognize you when you visit again. The software and hardware you use to access this Website may allow you to refuse new cookies or delete existing cookies. Refusing or deleting these cookies may limit your ability to take advantage of some features of this Website.

  • Banner Ads

    Banner ads will be used to advertise Illinois Lottery products. This Website will track the number of clicks on such ads for analytical and marketing purposes.

III. PERSONAL INFORMATION COLLECTED

In addition to the information automatically collected in Section II above, the following information may also be collected:

1. Emails, Surveys & Electronic Newsletter Subscriptions

If you participate in a survey, send us an email during your visit, or subscribe to an electronic newsletter, the following information will be collected:

  • The email address and contents of the email
  • Information volunteered in response to a survey
  • Any information required by or volunteered on the electronic newsletter subscription form.

2. Social Media and RSS Feeds

If you "like", follow us, or otherwise contact us via social media (e.g., Facebook or Twitter), or subscribe to us via RSS feed, the following information will be collected:

  • The social media user name you used (e.g., Facebook or Twitter user name)
  • We will track the number of "likes" and will track how many users are subscribed to our RSS feeds but will not track where they posted the feeds.

3. Players Account

If you choose to sign up for a Players Account (which is required to purchase individual Illinois Lottery game tickets for Lotto, MegaMillions¨ and such other games authorized by Illinois law via the internet ("Internet Tickets"), sign up for and purchase a subscription for specific Illinois Lottery games (a "Players Subscription"), or receive your Illinois Lottery Players' Prepaid Visa¨ Debit Card (the "Players Card")), this Website will collect the following information from you:

a. Mandatory Registration Personal Information

The following Personal Information will be collected at initial registration and is necessary for the creation and administration of a Players Account. This information will be stored by our Contractors and/or certain third parties in a contractual relationship with the Contractors and with each other ("Third Party Service Providers"):

  • name (first, last)
  • whether you are an individual or a business
  • email address
  • username (self-selected)
  • password (self-selected)
  • password hints and security questions
  • (postal) mailing address
  • (postal) billing address
  • whether or not you are a resident of Illinois
  • whether or not you are a United States non-resident alien
  • social security number
  • tax category
  • date of birth
  • phone number

b. Mandatory Personal Information for Funding Players Account

The following Personal Information will be collected and stored when you set up or initiate any transactions such as to make a purchase or transfer funds in or out of your Players Account. It may also be shared for any legitimate business purposes such as to process transactions or service your Player Account. Contractors and their Third Party Service Providers, to the extent required by law or regulation, comply with The Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards (PCI DSS). Please visit http://www.pcicomplianceguide.org/pcifaqs.php#4 for further information about these standards:

  • credit/debit card number or bank account number

c. Additional Information That May Be Collected

If you have a Players Account, the following information may also be collected from you:

  • your transaction history(e.g., record of the games you played, numbers you played, etc.)
  • your financial transaction history (e.g., record of the amount of money you spend on purchases, subscriptions, and corresponding debits or credits to your Player Account, etc.)
  • gender
  • preferred method of contact
  • game play, promotional, and purchase location preferences
  • whether or not you wish to be included in marketing surveys

d. Use of Your Information

By signing up for a Players Account, we will (through our Contractors and their Third Party Service Providers) and you agree to allow them to, perform a "Know Your Customer (KYC)" check to verify your name, social security number, age, date of birth, and/or any other information that you provide to us through this Website. In addition, by signing up for a Players Account, we will verify that you are located in Illinois via geo-filtering of your IP address.

In addition, the Illinois Lottery reserves the right, without limitation, to use Player Account information to enhance responsible gaming initiatives as well as monitor the play of its internet lottery players.

Our Contractors may use your Personal Information for the following purposes:

  • to administer the Illinois Lottery and your Players Account, including processing any Internet Tickets or Players Subscriptions that you purchase or funding your Players Account;
  • to verify your eligibility to play;
  • to contact you in response to your inquiries, comments and suggestions;
  • to otherwise contact you, including to notify you if you win;
  • for the specific purpose for which it was volunteered;
  • to track your use of this Website for internal market research;
  • to improve this Website and the services we promote or provide through this Website;
  • to customize the content and/or layout of this Website for each individual visitor;
  • to ask for your participation in brief surveys;
  • to notify you about promotions, special offers, etc. regarding products and services provided by the Illinois Lottery or its agents;
  • to be provided to the Third Party Service Providers in connection with the legitimate business purposes of the Illinois Lottery, its Contractors or this Website;
  • to generate aggregate statistical studies and to use the results of such aggregate statistical studies for marketing purposes and responsible gaming initiatives;
  • to protect and defend the rights or property of the Illinois Lottery and its Contractors;
  • to enforce this agreement, including the Illinois Lottery Terms and Conditions;
  • as required by law or regulation, or as requested by government or regulatory authorities, including but not limited to gaming regulators and the Internal Revenue Service (IRS), or for the protection of persons or property;
  • in connection with an acquisition, merger, restructuring, sale or other transfer involving all or any portion of the business associated with this Website; and
  • for other business purposes, including those purposes described in Section X below.

By submitting this information, you consent to its use for such purposes. When you sign up for a Players Account, the information you provide will be shared with MetaBank in order to automatically open up an account with and issue a Players Card from MetaBank, which is one of the Third Party Service Providers and the financial institution that is the issuer of the Players Card. Your MetaBank account and Players Card are subject to separate MetaBank terms and conditions and privacy policy, both located at MetaBank Terms And Conditions as well as GTECH's privacy policy located at www.gtech.com/privacypolicy.html More information is available in the Illinois Lottery Terms and Conditions. Any questions regarding your account with MetaBank and the Players Card should be directed to MetaBank. The Illinois Lottery and its Contractors assume no responsibility for the account with MetaBank or the Players Card.

IV. NON-DISCLOSURE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

In the event you decline to provide any Personal Information, you limit the Illinois Lottery's ability to serve you.

As noted above, no Personal Information is collected about you during your visit to this Website unless you provide that information voluntarily by sending an e-mail, participating in a survey, registering for a Players Account, or using other interactive services we may provide. You may choose not to utilize some or all of these interactive services. While your choice not to participate in these activities may limit your ability to receive specific services or products through this Website, it will not prevent you from requesting services or products from the Illinois Lottery by other means and will not normally have an impact on your ability to take advantage of other (non-interactive) features of this Website, including browsing or downloading publicly available information. If you would like to participate in Illinois Lottery promotions and do not wish to enter Personal Information through this Website, contact the Lottery at (800) 252-1775.

V. REVIEW AND CORRECTION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

You can review any Personal Information that is collected about you and recommend changes to your Personal Information that you believe to be inaccurate by using the Contact Us section at the end of this Privacy Policy. Reasonable steps will be taken to verify your identity before granting access or making corrections. Updates to your Personal Information will be made instantly.

You can also review and correct certain Personal Information (such as your name and contact information) by signing in to your Players Account on this Website using your email address and password, and you will be able to edit certain Personal Information in your Players Account profile.

VI. PERSONAL INFORMATION COLLECTION AND RELEASE AUTHORIZATION

To process your Players Account application, you authorize the Illinois Lottery (through its Contractors and their Third Party Service Providers) to collect the Personal Information about you listed in Section III above. Without limiting the foregoing, if you win, you authorize the Illinois Lottery to use and release certain Personal Information, including your name, hometown and any photograph, video or audio recording, in any medium (including but not limited to newspaper, television, radio and internet), for purposes of marketing, advertising or other purposes of the Illinois Lottery. If you do not provide all or any part of the Personal Information requested, your application may not be processed. The Illinois Lottery requires certain Personal Information to comply with the Internal Revenue Code and Tax Law income reporting and tax withholding requirements and under the authority of Internal Revenue Code and Tax Law Section 1604 to announce Illinois Lottery prize awards. The Illinois Lottery is the custodian of subscription and prize records and may be contacted by mail at: Illinois Lottery, P.O. Box 19081, Springfield, IL 62794-9081.

VII. EMAIL COMMUNICATIONS

If you register for a Players Account or otherwise provide Personal Information through this Website, you may receive email messages from the Contractors and MetaBank.

VIII. ADDITIONAL INFORMATION FOR NON-U.S. RESIDENTS

This Website and material provided on this Website are solely for promoting products, programs, and Illinois Lottery operations within the U.S. and specifically within the State of Illinois. Purchasers of Internet Tickets must be Illinois residents and be within the geographical boundaries of the State of Illinois to make purchases. If you provide Personal Information to the Illinois Lottery (including through the Contact Us section below), it may be transferred to and processed on computers in the U.S. and other countries. Do not provide your Personal Information to the Illinois Lottery if you do not want your Personal Information to be transferred to the United States or to other countries, or if the laws in your country restrict these types of transfers. Your provision of Personal Information to the Illinois Lottery will be handled in accordance with this Privacy Policy. Your provision of Personal Information to the Illinois Lottery through this Website means that you agree to this Privacy Policy.

IX. WEBSITE FOR ADULT USE ONLY (AGE 18 AND OLDER)

The Illinois Lottery (through its Contractors and Third Party Service Providers) does not knowingly collect Personal Information from children or create profiles of children through this Website. The registration page requires that registrants be at least 18 years of age to participate in Illinois Lottery transactions. Users are cautioned, however, that the collection of Personal Information submitted in an e-mail will be treated as though it was submitted by an adult, and may, unless exempted from access by State law, be subject to public access. The Illinois Lottery strongly encourages parents and teachers to be involved in children's internet activities and to provide guidance whenever children are asked to provide Personal Information via the internet.

X. DISCLOSURE OF INFORMATION COLLECTED THROUGH THIS WEBSITE

Your voluntary disclosure of Personal Information to the Illinois Lottery, whether solicited or unsolicited, constitutes consent to the collection and disclosure of such information by the Illinois Lottery for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. If you provide Personal Information on this Website, certain state or federal laws may protect it. However, this information is a public record once you provide it, and may be subject to public inspection and copying if not protected by such state or federal laws.

Without limiting the foregoing, Personal Information may be collected or disclosed without your consent if the collection or disclosure is: (1) necessary to perform the statutory duties of the Illinois Lottery, or necessary for the Illinois Lottery to operate a program authorized by law, or authorized by state or federal statute or regulation, including the Freedom of Information Act; (2) made pursuant to a court order, subpoena or by law; (3) for the purpose of validating your identity; (4) with affiliates of the Illinois Lottery and its Contractors to provide the services on this Website; or (5) to federal or state law enforcement authorities to enforce the Lottery's rights against unauthorized access or attempted unauthorized access to the Illinois Lottery's information technology assets or against other inappropriate use of this Website.

In the event of a conflict between this Privacy Policy and any state or federal laws, including the Freedom of Information Act, the state or federal laws will control.

XI. DATA BREACH NOTIFICATION

In the event of a data breach, the Illinois Lottery (through its Contractors and their Third Party Service Providers) will notify all Player Account holders of the breach in the most expedient time possible, without unreasonable delay. The disclosure notification may be via mail, e-mail or other expedient method such as a newspaper publication. Such notification to Illinois residents shall include, but need not be limited to (i) the toll free numbers and addresses for consumer reporting agencies, (ii) the toll-free number, address, and website address for the Federal Trade Commission, and (iii) a statement that the individual can obtain information from these sources about fraud alerts and security freezes. The notification shall however not include information concerning the number of Illinois residents affected by the breach.
