MyxerTones™ Privacy Policy

MyxerTones understand the importance of privacy and are committed to protecting the privacy of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how MyxerTones collects and uses your personal information.

WHY DO WE COLLECT PERSONAL INFORMATION?

MyxerTones collects personal information to improve the manner by which we operate and offer our products and services. We use the personal information to communicate with you about those products and services, and provide effective, timely customer support when needed. We will never rent or sell any of the personal information we collect from you to anyone, and will not share your personal information with anyone without your consent except as described in this Privacy Policy.

WHAT PERSONAL INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

You must open an account or complete an online registration with MyxerTones to download or purchase MyxerTones products and services, including certain free trials. The information we gather is different based upon the services that you use. The information may include your mobile phone number; your name and email address; your billing information; information about what software has been downloaded, what products and content you have purchased, location information (such as address, zip code, and country); computer configuration (such as operating system, connection speed, and peripherals); mobile phone device information and network carrier information; the Internet IP address and URL of the Website that sent you to the MyxerTones registration page; the types of peripherals used by MyxerTones products on your computer; demographic information provided by you (such as language, gender and age, and, if applicable, content preferences and personalization information); your musical usage patterns and types of music you listen to and whether you logged into or out of a MyxerTones service, or changed your password or billing information. We will not sell, rent or otherwise distribute your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as set forth below.

HOW DO WE USE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

MyxerTones uses your mobile phone number to deliver to you mobile phone content such as ringtones, full-track music download, pictures, and videos. MyxerTones uses your other personal information to: Deliver to you and bill you for MyxerTones products and services, and communicate with you concerning your account and customer service issues. This may include verifying authorization of credit card usage before any purchase of premium content or services; determining if you meet the minimum system requirements to use our products, and providing information concerning compatibility and interoperability to you; asking you to participate in surveys about products, services, news and events; offering you product upgrades, content and services for you to purchase; providing you with content, services, instructions, and a more personalized experience based on personal information, such as zip/postal code and any content or other preferences you provide; auto-updating downloaded MyxerTones software; providing you with news, free newsletters, updates, targeted advertising, promotions, and special offers; and better understanding how our products are used. MyxerTones uses your email address to update you on important changes to your account such as the receipt of payments, receipt of promotion credits and, thanks and notification of credits for inviting your friends.

WHEN AND HOW DO WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION WITH THIRD PARTIES?

MyxerTones does not sell, rent, or share your personal information to a anyone unless you consent to provide such information. MyxerTones may disclose relevant information about our customers to our contractors (e.g. a customer service contractor or a credit card clearing service) in order for them to provide specific services for MyxerTones, and to better serve our customers. MyxerTones' contractors are bound by strict contractual requirements to keep all personal information they process strictly confidential and to use such information solely on behalf of MyxerTones. Also, MyxerTones may disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to:

(a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on MyxerTones;
(b) protect and defend the rights or property of MyxerTones and its products; or
(c) act in urgent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of MyxerTones products or the public at large.

MyxerTones Websites and products may contain links to other Websites and products. MyxerTones is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Websites or products. Please visit the privacy policies of these sites in order to understand their privacy policies and data collection practices.

CHANGES TO OUR PRIVACY POLICY

We frequently revise this Privacy Policy to keep it up to date with MyxerTones products, services and Web pages.

NO ERROR FREE PERFORMANCE

We customarily release alpha and beta versions of our products, which are further refined before the release of the final version. Because these products are still in a test phase, we may not always catch an unintended privacy issue, despite our efforts to do so. As such, we welcome feedback on any privacy concerns you may have, and on how to improve our products generally, in all stages of release. MyxerTones does not guarantee error-free performance under this privacy policy. We will use reasonable efforts to comply with this privacy policy and will take prompt corrective action when we learn of any failure to comply with our privacy policy.
