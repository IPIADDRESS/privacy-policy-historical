Privacy policy

This policy was last updated on March 2, 2015 and officially effective.
 

Dear visitor, we respect your privacy and it is important to us. Below is the our privacy and security policy:

 

Security

Protecting customers information is one of our top priorities. 

You personal information submitted via the website is protected both online and offline.

Whenever you are asked to enter sensitive information, that information is encrypted and is protected by Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology with 256 bit encryption. If your browser supports SSL (most current browsers do), it will automatically encrypt the information you provide to us on the secure pages on our site, before sending it to internet or other third party.

Access to your personal details information is restricted to only our employees who require this information to fulfil your order.

All our servers, are housed and maintained in secure locations, with access strictly monitored and protected. Internet access is protected and restricted by firewalls and password protection. 

Cookies

When you visit our site, we will ask your browser to place a permanent "cookie" (a very small text file) on your computer. If your browser is configured to accept cookies, it will accept the cookie and place it on your computer's hard drive. We collect this information solely for marketing purposes : to analyze where our customers are coming from, how often they visit our site and other behavior. We also collect certain technical information from your computer, like your IP address and the address of a referring web site, if any. This information will allow us to enhance our site to our customer's preferences. This information is used only for the benefit of us and you, our customer.

You can still visit our site without cookies if you choose to do so. However, please be aware that if you refuse cookies some site features may malfunction or may be slower. To visit our site without cookies, you can configure your browser to reject all cookies or to notify you when a cookie is set. Check the browsers help menu to learn how to change your cookie preferences.

  • Some of our business partners (retargeting and advertisers) use cookies on our site. We have no access to or control over this process.

 

Registration

In order to place an orders on our website, you must complete the registration form.
