My Wedding Favors collects information in different ways from Visitors and customers who access the various parts of our Services. Data Security

The membership, bookstore and donations information you provide—including credit card information—is secured using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption technology. While we make every effort to ensure the integrity and security of our network and systems, we cannot guarantee that our security measures will prevent third-party "hackers" from illegally obtaining this information. "Cookies" and My Wedding Favors Uses Them

A "cookie" is a small data file that can be placed on your hard drive when you visit certain Web sites. My Wedding Favors may use cookies to collect, store, and sometimes track information for statistical purposes to improve the products and services we provide and to manage our telecommunications networks.
