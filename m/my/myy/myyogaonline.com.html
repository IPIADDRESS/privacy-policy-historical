Privacy Policy

Relaxing the Brain
The process of relaxing the brain is achieved through asana. We generally think of mind as being in our head. In asana (yoga postures) our conciousness spreads throughout the body, eventually diffusing in every cell, creating complete awareness. In this way stressful thought is drained away, and our mind focuses on the body, intelligence, and awareness as a whole. This allows the brain to be more receptive, and concentration becomes natural."

--B. K. S. Iyengar from "Light on Life"
