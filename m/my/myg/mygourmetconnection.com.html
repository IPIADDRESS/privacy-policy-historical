Our Privacy Policy - MyGourmetConnection.com

MyGourmetConnection.com is committed to maintaining the highest standards of privacy for our users.

While visiting the site you could provide us with two different types of information: personal information you choose to disclose to us for contact purposes and anonymous website usage information collected as you browse our site. The anonymous information we collect is statistical in nature and just lets us know which areas of our website are receiving attention and how these various areas compare with one another. None of these statistics are associated with you as an individual.

Should you choose to contact us directly via e-mail with a question or suggestion, we may store your e-mail address for the purpose of contacting you in the future regarding your original request, but this information will never be sold or shared with any other organizations, including those who advertise with us. Naturally, once you follow a link from our site to another company's website, you become subject to the privacy policy posted by that organization.

Should you have any questions about the policy stated above, please feel free to contact us at any time.
