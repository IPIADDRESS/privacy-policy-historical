Does the NetPivotal website use cookies? If so, how are cookies used on the website?
Cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to a user's hard drive for record-keeping purposes while visiting a site. Like most major websites, NetPivotal uses cookies on certain pages of our site. Our cookies do not contain any personally identifying information, we use cookies to inform us of a repeat visit and to help us improve our website and web hosting services.
