Member’s name, geography, and telephone number may also be requested. My-Fat-Pocket.com does not collect any other personal information that readers and / or members do not expressly provide.

My-Fat-Pocket.com may share readers’ and / or members’ personal information for the purpose of delivering MFP communications as required by law.
 
Non-Personal Demographic Information
 
MFP may also request non-personally identifiable demographic information, such as annual income, buying preferences, or age. This information is optional. This data may be used to improve our site and/or tailor the experience, showing you content that we think you might be interested in, and displaying content according to your preferences. We may also share this information with others, such as advertisers interested in advertising on My-Fat-Pocket.com, only in aggregate, anonymous form. In no case will we link aggregate user data with personally identifiable information.
 
Usage Information
 
In addition to collecting personal and non-personal information, My-Fat-Pocket.com may also collect non-personal, aggregated information about members’ use of the newsletter. This information is not personally identifiable. For example, this information will tell us how much time users spend on the MFP site, from which other sites subscribers came, and to what other sites subscribers go. The collection of this information allows us to, among other things, prepare for traffic load demands and to efficiently deliver your newsletter.
 
Choice/Opt-Out
 
My-Fat-Pocket.com complies with the relevant laws under the Republic of Singapore. If readers / members receive communication materials concerning new products or services that was not expressly requested, MFP will provide you with an e-mail address to opt-out.
 
Tracking

My-Fat-Pocket.com also reserves the right to use transparent GIF images to track the e-mails that we send out. In the course of serving advertisements to this site, MFP’s third-party advertisers may place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser.

My-Fat-Pocket.com is not responsible for the content that appears on those sites and does not endorse these sites. Please consult the individual privacy policies of those sites in order to determine how they treat user information.
