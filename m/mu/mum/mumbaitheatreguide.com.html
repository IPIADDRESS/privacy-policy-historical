COOKIES

Like many sites, www.mumbaitheatreguide.com uses cookies. Cookies are data files that are stored on your computer. Cookies do not contain any personally identifiable information.

We use cookies for the benefit of our users. Cookies let us know if we have repeat visitors (a strong indication of happy consumers) and if we are delivering a quality product to our consumers. Essentially, it is an anonymous way to survey how we are doing and to look for other consumers who we think will like our site. Cookies are not programs that come onto a visitor's system and damage files. Generally, cookies work by assigning a unique number to the visitor that has no meaning outside the assigning site. If a visitor does not want information collected through the use of cookies, there is a simple procedure in most browsers that allows the visitor to deny or accept the cookie feature.
