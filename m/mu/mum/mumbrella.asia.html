    • British Etiquette, British Airways. Excuse me?

      When BBH sent me their latest campaign for British Airways earlier this week, I must say I found it hard to keep a stiff upper lip.

      The idea behind the campaign, which will run in social media as a series of videos, is for British Airways to be an ambassador for British etiquette for its Chinese customers.

      It wasn’t the vague undertones of, ‘let’s teach these Chinese how to behave like the British’ that struck me.

      It was the disconnect between what a brand is trying to say, and the reality of the brand experience.

      British etiquette. From British Airways? What poppycock.
