About Your Privacy

Murals Your Way respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This document describes Murals Your Way's Consumer Data Privacy Policy, that is, how we protect your privacy and treat your data when you submit personal information to us. The technical aspects of privacy protection, such as encryption and data transfer security, are described below in the section headed Transaction Security Policies.

The Murals Your Way product order form consists of a number of fields that you are asked to fill in. Some of them are compulsory, others optional. The compulsory fields are:

  • Your name
  • E-mail address
  • Telephone number
  • Billing and Delivery addresses
  • Country
  • Ship Method

These fields are required in order to process your order and deliver the product to you. Murals Your Way stores the information for accounting reasons. Your information will not be shown to third parties not involved in the transaction, nor used to send you any unrequested information. You can choose not to receive information on new Murals Your Way products.

You are also required to give the following information:

  • Credit card type
  • Credit card number
  • Expiration date of credit card

The credit card information is sent directly to our payment provider who processes your order. The transaction has a very high level of security as described in the section below headed Transaction Security Policies.

If you have any questions or require more information regarding Murals Your Way policies, products or other, please contact us.

Transaction Security Policies

Your credit card details are sent directly to Murals Your Way's payment provider (USAepay) over a secure SSL-encrypted connection. They are not processed on our servers at any stage of the transaction. The payments are deposited by Murals Your Way's authorized customer service staff. Other Murals Your Way employees do not have access to USAepay's transaction management system.

Your contact information is sent to Murals Your Way's server over an unencrypted connection and forwarded to our authorized customer service staff. Other Murals Your Way employees do not have access to the information.
