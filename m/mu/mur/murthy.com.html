Privacy Policy

Home > Privacy Policy

The website of the Murthy Law Firm is designed so that you can freely browse the site for information without revealing personal data. Any personal information sent to our Office via eMail, is done so at the free will of the sender. Our eMail lists are made up only of individuals requesting inclusion in our online services and events covering immigration topics. We do not use “cookies” or put anything onto your hard disk.

All information given to the Murthy Law Firm from its prospective clients, clients or agents of its clients is kept strictly confidential and on a need-to-know basis, which is protected under attorney/client privilege. You can be assured that your personal information is used only by our trained legal staff for the purpose of the work you have engaged our Office to do. Your information is never used for marketing or solicitation and is never sold or given to anyone for these purposes.

The Murthy Law Firm never supplies any other person or organization with your information except those government agencies involved in your immigration process (e.g. U.S. Department of Labor, INS, etc.).
