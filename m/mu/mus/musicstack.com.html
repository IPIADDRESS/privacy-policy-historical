Cookies
This site uses cookies, which are tiny files that are downloaded to your computer, to improve your experience. This section describes what information the cookies gather, how we use it and why we sometimes need to store these cookies. We will also share how you can prevent these cookies from being stored however this may downgrade or 'break' certain elements of the sites functionality.

Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that we transfer to your computer's hard drive through your Web browser. They make it possible for us to recognize your browser when you visit. By doing this, we can personalize your return visits. For example cookies save you time during checkout by not requiring you to sign in again, or by remember your currency preference so prices are displayed in a currency you are familiar with. In order to enjoy such benefits, your browser must be set to accept cookies.

For more information on cookies visit https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HTTP_cookie and https://www.allaboutcookies.org/.
