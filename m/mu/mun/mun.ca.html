Website Privacy Statement

Version 1.0
Last Updated 2003-11-21

Memorial University of Newfoundland is committed to protecting and respecting the privacy of users of this website. Memorial supports the protection of privacy and access to information in accordance with all applicable federal and provincial laws.

Memorial collects and stores web usage information and system logs. These records may contain personally identifiable information (e.g. IP addresses) and are used to manage capacity, performance and security, and to help maintain and improve services. Logged information is not shared with, or disclosed to, any third party except as explicitly approved by the website user or as required by law.

This website may also deposit small text files called cookies on your computer. Memorial uses cookies only to enhance or customize your visit. Users may choose not to allow cookies to be stored on their computers. However, some information may not display properly, and some services may not be available as a result.

In the course of accessing this website, you may provide Memorial with personal information. Information volunteered by you is used by Memorial to operate programs and services. Users should refer to the privacy policy associated with the specific program or service for which the information is collected.

Some services (e.g. bulletin boards, web mail) by their nature distribute communications or information that you provide. Information you share via these services may be collected, stored and disseminated by others.

Memorial websites may provide links to external websites. Memorial does not control or endorse these sites and is not responsible for their availability, content, privacy practices, or delivery of services. Memorial encourages you to examine the privacy policy of each website you visit.

Responsibilities of Users

Some websites require authorized access, typically via an account name and password. Individual users are responsible for their own accounts and should never share passwords with other individuals.

To protect your privacy and prevent unauthorized use, log out and completely close all web browser windows when you finish using a web-based service.

About this Statement

The privacy statement for this website is published under the authority of the Director, Computing and Communications.

This statement may be amended from time to time. Refer to the version number and Last Update date at the beginning of this document.
