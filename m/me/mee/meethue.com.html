Privacy Notice for Hue

Philips (“we”) believes strongly in protecting the privacy of the personal data you share with us. We also believe it is important to inform you about how we will use your personal data, and to give you choices about how that data will be used. Therefore, we encourage you to read this Privacy Notice carefully, before you make your choices and give us the right to use your data as stated in this Privacy Notice.

What is the scope of this Privacy Notice?

This privacy notice ("Privacy Notice") applies to all personal data of consumers collected via the use of the Hue lamps and bridge (“Products”), via www.meetHue.com(“Website”) and via the Hue App (“App”). Products and Website are controlled by or under the control of Koninklijke Philips N.V. or any of its subsidiaries (”Philips”), together referred to as the “Services”.

This Privacy Notice applies in addition to any terms and conditions and other policies of Philips for the Services.

Please note that, in certain cases, you will need to create a Hue account in order to use the Services (for example, to control your Products from within and from outside the home, via the App and via the Website). The creation and the management of the account is always done through the “Create Account” section of the Website (for example, https://www.meetHue.com/en-us/register). For details about the personal data collected within the Hue account, please visit the section “How we collect your personal data and which personal data we collect” of the present Notice.

 

Why should you share your personal data with us? (Our purposes)

Sharing your personal data with us is necessary for us in order to provide you with the services that you have purchased from us, including:

  • Using the Services;
  • Using other applications, products and functionalities made available by Philips or third parties that can be connected to the Services;
  • Buying goods on store.meetHue.com;
  • Assist you with after sales services;
  • Save time by storing your preferences;
  • Benefit from software updates;
  • Improve the website/app functionalities analyzing usage statistics;
  • Help us to develop products that are designed around you, optimize customer services and continuously improve our Services.

Further, if you choose to register yourself for an additional service we will also be able to provide you with the following (depending on the service):

  • Communicate in a personal and timely manner, such as responding to your request for contact;
  • Register your purchase so you can receive all the benefits to which your purchase entitles you;
  • Participate in online communities;
  • Receive personalized messages, information and special offers that are relevant to your interests.

At Philips we use the highest quality data security tools to keep your data safe and protect Hue from unauthorized access.

 

Which personal data do we collect?

The type of personal data we collect depends on the way of collection.

  • Using the Website and the App, We will collect your:
    • IP address;
    • userID;
    • types of devices connected to the Hue App;
    • unique ID of the bridge, and the serial number of the bridge;
    • usage data;
    • the crash incident data.

We will store this usage data also for providing you with appropriate after-sales services and troubleshooting. The usage data is stored for as long as needed to provide the services to you.

If you choose to enable geofencing feature within the App, we will also process your location information.

  • Accessing or changing your settings or uploading scenes or images;

 

  • Creating an account on www.meetHue.com (My Hue), we will collect your:
    • Name / Nickname
    • email address;
    • password;

 

  • Using an account created on www.meetHue.com (My Hue), we will collect your:
    • usage data of the Services;
    • IP address;
    • configuration of your Products;
    • scenes and images you choose to upload.

 

  • Buying goods on www.store.meetHue.com. If you choose to use store.meetHue.com to buy products, we will additionally collect your personal information to complete the transaction, to deliver your order and to bill you for the product provided. We may collect your:
    • Name / Surname;
    • Email address;
    • payment information;
    • billing and shipping addresses;
    • telephone number;
    • invoices;
    • order history.

In addition, we may share data with service providers in order to help us collect product reviews from consumers residing in UK who purchase our products from www.store.meetHue.com. If you choose to provide a review for your purchase, we will also collect your product reviews.

  • Connecting the Products with your WIFI network, we will collect your:
    • IP address

 

  • Choosing to make use of additional services from us, such as participating in promotions, subscribing to a newsletter, We may ask for or receive certain personal data, such as your:
    • Name / Surname;
    • Email address;
    • Social media profile;
    • Age / date of birth;
    • Usage of and activities on our Philips Pages;
    • Interests; and
      Other information related to your online behavior.

In addition, we may collect personal data via cookies or similar techniques, including, but not limited to:

  • Your IP address;
  • Your cookie ID;
  • Your web browser;
  • Your location;
  • The web pages you visit on our websites;
  • The advertisements you've viewed or clicked through.

We take reasonable steps to ensure that we securely retain information about you only for so long as is necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, or as required under any contract or by applicable law.

We will disclose personal data only under this policy and/or when required by law.

We recognize our responsibility to protect the information you entrust to us. Philips uses a variety of secure techniques to protect your information, including secure servers, firewalls, and encryption of personal data.

Your choices

We want to know you better so we can serve you better while at the same time, we respect your choices about how we use your personal data.
