Protecting those most vulnerable.

Just how important is your child's online privacy to us? Plenty. In fact, we at MEGA Brands share your concern about protecting an impressionable young audience. This issue is vital to our mission of encouraging safe and healthy learning. It is for this reason that we follow the guidelines established by the Children's AdvertisingReview Unit (CARU) of the Council of Better Business Bureaus. By promoting a trustful and secure environment, we can offer your child a safe and enjoyable online experience. It's just the right thing to do.

MEGA Brands Privacy Policy

We are committed to fulfilling our obligation to protect children from the unauthorized solicitation of personal information. We also acknowledge the prerogative of parents to decide what type of information they want to submit to us in that regard. Our policy is to fully comply with the terms of the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the privacy guidelines of the Children's Advertising Review Unit (CARU) of the Council of Better Business Bureaus. The following text outlines in question and answer format the specific actions we take to effect such compliance both to ensure that there is a safe online environment for the children who visit our site and to give peace of mind to the parents, teachers and caregivers of those children.

Who owns the MEGABrands.COM web site?

The website is owned and operated by MEGA Brands Inc. (“MEGA Brands”), owners of the MEGA Brands® trademark and manufacturers of the MEGA Brands® construction toy system.

The head office of MEGA BrandsInc. is located at 4505 Hickmore, Montreal (QC) Canada, H4T 1K4. For general inquiries or if you have any questions or comments regarding our privacy statement or policies, please contact us by postal mail, at that address, to the attention of "Consumer Service" or by visiting our consumer service center.

Please note that our Privacy Policy applies only to the web sites operated by MEGA Brands Inc. and not to other web sites operated by other companies or organizations to which we may link, as we do not control the activities of those sites.

What types of personal information is collected at MEGABrands.COM?

For the passive display of our products and a description of their features, we do not request personal information of any kind. For interactive online activities, we may request the following information: a user's given name or pseudonym, age, gender and country of residence. If online communication from the company is requested, we will need your e-mail address. We do not request that a child submit information that would allow him or her to be identified offline.


What options do I have in response to a request for personal information?

You can supply the information or you can refuse to supply the information. You can also request the correction or deletion of personal information you have previously supplied to us by contacting us by reqular mail to the attention of "Consumer Service" at 4505 Hickmore, Montreal (QC) Canada, H4T 1K4 or by visiting our consumer service center.


What is the policy for posting personal information on your web site?

At present, we do not post any such information. If we were to post personal information in the future, we would only do so upon obtaining verifiable parental consent, or the consent of a child's caregiver, by requesting that our “Parental Consent Form” be sent to our head office address. (Click here for Parental Consent Form).

Is the personal information that parents consent to give on behalf of their children shared with or distributed to parties that are unrelated to the company?

No, although in an effort to continuously improve our products we may share anonymous compiled statistics about our online traffic with outside product developers and marketing
professionals. We do not sell, rent or exchange personal information, and if we ever considered changing that policy we would first seek parental consent in each instance. Any personal information that is provided to us remains securely in our database where it can be retrieved for the parent to review, correct or request deletion of at any time.

Is the submission of personal information ever needed as a condition for participating in an online activity, or to access part of the website?

Some of our activities, such as story or creation submissions, games, e-cards, newsletters, sweepstakes and contests may require the collection of personally identifiable information. The information collected from participants will also be considered confidential. To enable children under 13 years of age to participate in any such activity, a parent or guardian must print and fill out the Parental Consent Form and return it to us by postal mail or by fax. We do not condition participation in any activity upon disclosure of more information than is reasonably necessary to participate in such an activity. You may of course at any time opt out of such activities.

Do you collect ISP addresses?

Yes. When you access a page on our website, our servers log your Internet Service Provider (ISP) address for the purpose of system administration and aggregate information. Our servers also record your type of browser (e.g. Netscape or Internet Explorer) and your operating system (e.g. Macintosh or Windows) No personally identifiable information, such as your name or your e-mail address is collected from children under 13 without parental consent.

Do you use “cookies”?

Yes, in order to make your browsing easier. Cookies are used to remember your password (for sections that require a password) and to increase your security and that of your child. Cookies do not contain personal information about the individual who is browsing our site. We do not use any information generated by cookies to conduct direct marketing or promotion of any kind.
