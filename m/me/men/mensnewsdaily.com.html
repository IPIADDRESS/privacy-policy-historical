Privacy Policy

Internet user privacy and confidentiality is of the highest importance to Men's News Daily™ . Unless you designate otherwise or we state otherwise in this policy or at the time information is collected, any personal information you provide will not be known to any other party except you and Men's News Daily™.

Men's News Daily™ is a rapidly growing online news source. As such, this policy will change from time to time along with our site.

Traffic Data
Each time a visitor comes to the Men's News Daily™ website, our software collects some basic technical information, including the visitor's domain name, referral data and browser and platform type. However, we will not disclose your individual identity or personally identifiable data to any party.

If and when we do present aggregated information on our traffic data to outside companies, no one will be able to identify you or contact you.


Cookies
A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a server and stored on your computer's hard drive. We use cookies in a very limited manner, primarily to help deliver advertisements so that you won't receive the same advertisement too many times. We use cookies to identify unique browsers that visit us and to track site navigation.

Other sites which place advertisements on our site, or which we may provide a link referencing an article that is available to view on Men's News Daily™, also have the ability to assign their cookies to you in a process that Men's News Daily™ does not control.

Cookies cannot be used to identify individuals, only machines. So these advertisers will not know who a visitor is, nor will they have any personally identifiable information about a visitor.


Unsubscribing from the mailing list
To unsubscribe, please visit our mailing list page and enter the name and email address with which you first joined the list, then select "unsubscribe" and hit the "go" button.


Other sites
Men's News Daily™ contains many links to other sites on the Web. We cannot control and are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such other websites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of these linked sites, because their privacy practices may differ from ours.


Who can I ask if I have any additional questions?
If you have questions or concerns regarding this statement, you should first contact privacy@MensNewsDaily™.
