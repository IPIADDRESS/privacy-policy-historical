Privacy policy

To minimze the efforts and pain of our users, we have been inspired by 500px.

1. Information Collected by Mentimeter

We collect information to make Mentimeter useful for you.

2. Information You Provide to Us

We collect your registration and user profile data if you are a registered user. Our servers also collect log information used to make the website faster and better.

3. E-mail Communications

We will sometimes send you emails if you have provided your adress to us. From time to time, we may send emails to all our users. We do not want to spam you, so we'll do our best to minimize that.

4. Cookies

To remember you, our system will give you a cookie. It's safe.

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

Privacy policies may change. But we promise to always have your best interest in mind.

6. Conditions of Use

Using Mentimeter is subject to Terms and Privacy policy.

7. Payment information

Mentimeter never store payment information but instead uses Braintree, a service that is a certified PSP (Payment Service Provider) with Visa and Mastercard. Your information is safe with them!
