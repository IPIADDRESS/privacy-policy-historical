Privacy Policy

Our Commitment

Mecum Auction is committed to building user trust and confidence by promoting and complying with the use of business practices that help protect the privacy of customers and their data. Because you are a valued Mecum Auction customer, we provide the opportunity for you to learn more about how we collect, use, secure, and share your personal information.

This Notice describes how we protect, collect, use, and share customer information. Customer information means all nonpublic information related to an identified individual, which is sometimes called personally identifiable information. Customer information does not include anonymized data or de-identified data, which do not identify an individual.

Customer Information We Collect

We collect customer information in different ways. For example, we gather information from:
  • Applications and other forms related to our auctions and through conversations with Mecum Auction representatives.
  • Use of mecum.com, our mobile sites and applications, our social media sites and applications, and interactions with our online advertisements.
  • Transactions or experiences with us, such as payments.

Our Privacy Principles

  • We do not sell customer information.
  • We do not allow those who are doing business on our behalf to use our customer information for their own marketing purposes.
  • We contractually require any person or organization providing products or services on our behalf to protect Mecum Auction customer information.
  • We handle information about former and prospective customers the same as existing customers.

We Protect Customer Information

  • We maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect customer information and to comply with federal and state laws. In addition, we regularly review our policies and practices, monitor our computer networks, and test the strength of our security.

At Mecum Auction, we use the following technologies to help keep your transactions secure:

  • Browser electronic document security.
  • Enhanced customer verification process.

How Do We Use Information About You

  • For our everyday business purposes, for public policy purposes, and as permitted or required by law.
  • As needed, to handle your transaction.
  • With companies that perform marketing or other services for us or with whom we have joint marketing agreements.
  • As needed, to protect against fraud and unauthorized transactions and in connection with the investigation, establishment, and defense of legal rights
  • We retain customer information for legal, auditing, regulatory, and business purposes.

Email Privacy Policy

We are committed to keeping your privacy. We practice permission-based email marketing.

Sharing and Usage

We will not sell, license, share, transfer or otherwise disclose individual information to any third party.

Why did you receive this email?

If you received an email from us, it is because you have expressly shared this address to receive emails
regarding this topic (opt-in) or have previously registered at one of our events.

How can you stop receiving emails?

Each email sent through our system contains a link that allows subscribers to unsubscribe from receiving emails from the sender. If you wish to unsubscribe, follow the instructions in the footer of any email. Or forward a copy of the email to info@mecum.com.

If you have any questions about our Anti-Spam Policy, please contact us at info@mecum.com.

Google AdWords Remarketing Privacy Policy

Mecum Auction websites use the Google AdWords remarketing service to advertise on third party websites (including Google) to previous visitors to our sites. It could mean that we advertise to previous visitors based upon demographics, location, online behavior and/or device technology. This could be in the form of an advertisement on the Google search results page, or a site in the Google Display Network. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on someone's past visits to the Mecum websites. Of course, any data collected will be used in accordance with our own privacy policy and Google's privacy policy. Furthermore, you may opt-out of Google's use of cookies by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

We are here to help!
