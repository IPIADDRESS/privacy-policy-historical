mealtrain.com Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 4th, 2019

Meal Train LLC ("Meal Train," "we" or "us") respects the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy (the “Privacy Policy”) is intended to describe for individuals who are users (hereinafter, “you” or “users”) of our website, www.mealtrain.com (the “Website”):

  • the information we collect
  • how that information may be used
  • with whom it may be shared
  • your choices about such uses and disclosures

We encourage you to read this Privacy Policy carefully when using the Services (as defined below). By using the Services, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at contact@mealtrain.com, through the Contact Us page, or by writing to us at:

Meal Train LLC
47 Caroline Street
Burlington, VT 05401

Overview

The Website offers a service that allows for the creation of a community meal coordination service (a "Meal Train") for a recipient, sending invitations to friends, receiving invitations, booking dates for meal delivery, sending donations and gift cards, and researching etiquette tips and tools, (herein referred to as the "Service" or "Meal Train Service," and together with the Website, collectively, the “Services”).

Information we collect about you

We may collect personal information that can identify you, such as your name and email address and other information that you provide to us. We may also collect information that does not identify you. When you provide personal information through our Website, the information may be sent to servers located in the United States and other countries around the world.

  • Information you provide. We may collect and store any personal information you enter on our Website or provide to us in some other manner. This includes identifying information, such as your name, address, email address, image, telephone number, and postal code.
  • Information from other sources. We may also periodically obtain both personal and non-personal information (updated delivery and address information, purchase history, and additional demographic information) about you from business partners, contractors, and other third parties and add it to your account information or other information we have collected.
    • Facebook.com: If you sign in via Facebook, we may obtain your name, basic user profile information, and your email address if you consent to share it.
    • WePay.com: If you donate to a Meal Train via the Meal Train Donation Feature, the payment is processed by WePay, our payment partner. We may obtain your name and email address from WePay, related to the payment transaction.
    • Paypal.com: If you purchase a Meal Train product or service using PayPal, we may obtain your name, email, phone, and mailing address from PayPal as required to fulfill the obligations of that purchase.

Collection and sharing of personal information

How Meal Train LLC uses the information you provide

  • manage your profile and provide you with customer support
  • perform research and analysis about your use of, or interest in, our products, the Services, or content, or products, services or content offered by others
  • develop and display content and advertising tailored to your interests on our Website
  • enforce our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
  • manage our business
  • perform functions as otherwise described to you at the time of collection

Sharing within Mealtrain.com

You can choose not to provide us with certain information, but that may result in you being unable to use certain features of our Website.

Meal Train Calendar Creation:

To create a Meal Train Calendar, you must provide the Meal Train Recipient’s name, email, city, state, and zip code. If the recipient does not have an email address, you can list the organizer’s email as the recipient. Providing the street address is optional. In addition, the organizer must be a registered user and have agreed to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Providing additional personal information including dates available to receive meals, meal preferences, nature of the meal train, photos, and updates is completely optional. The sharing of all provided information is specified as follows:

Invitations and Inclusion on the Participant list:

All participants to a meal train must be invited by the recipient, organizer, or other participants on a meal train. Meal Train LLC will not provide the unique URL upon participant request. Upon being invited, a user can view the Meal Train Calendar, view recipient city, state, and zip code, view any optionally provided information, read Updates, view photos, participate in a Donation Fund (when active), or send a gift card without creating a user account.

If you are invited from within mealtrain.com, book a date, make a donation, or become a registered user, your email will be added to the Participant list for that Meal Train. Nonregistered users will have a truncated version of their email address added to the participant list. Registered users have their First and Last Name displayed.

Inclusion on the participant list enables you to receive updates from the organizer or recipient and receive direct email messages from the organizer, recipient, or other participants. Both registered and non-registered users can manage their communication preferences by clicking on the link at the bottom of most meal train generated emails.

Date Booking:

To book a date, you must be registered user by providing Meal Train your First and Last Name, an email address and agree to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. We will provide you with the ability to select email reminder preferences during the date booking as well as Meal Train specific communication preferences in each email.

If the organizer or recipient books a date on behalf of a registered or non-registered user, the date will display the First and Last name of the other user, the user will be added to the Participant list, and the organizer and recipient can opt for the specific reminder emails.

Donation Fund:

As an Organizer or Recipient of a Meal Train, you may choose to activate the Donation Fund feature. The Donation Fund feature is optional. Activating the Donation Fund feature requires that we send your name, email address, and IP address to WePay.com, our payment partner. WePay will use that information to create a WePay account for you. You are required to verify the WePay account after it is created and accept WePay’s Terms of Use and Privacy Policy. You may choose to decline the account verification. WePay accounts that are not verified are automatically deleted in a timeframe defined by WePay. You can learn more about WePay’s privacy policy by clicking here. (https://go.wepay.com/privacy-policy)

You can participate in a Donation Fund without being a registered user. Upon donating, your name will be displayed on the Meal Train Donation Fund tab (unless marked anonymous) and your email address will be added to the Participant list for that specific Meal Train. A truncated version of your email address will be added to the participant list (unless you are a registered user in which case your First and Last Name will be displayed). Meal Train LLC does not directly collect financial information related to the donation process. All donations are handled by WePay.com and use of the donation feature requires acceptance of their Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. (https://go.wepay.com/terms-of-service)

Gift Cards:

You can participate in sending a gift card to the recipient without being a registered user. Any links to gift cards on Meal Train direct the user to partner sites where gift cards can be purchased. Gift cards are not sold by Meal Train LLC.

Emailing Other Participants:

You must be a registered user to email other participants. General participants are not able to view email addresses of other general participants. Participants on a specific Meal Train may choose to email other participants through mealtrain.com pertaining to the Meal Train. Should a user opt to directly reply to that email from their email client, their email will become visible to the first sender.

We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our service, to provide the service on our behalf, to perform Site-related services (including but not limited to data storage, maintenance services, database management, web analytics, payment processing, email sending and compliance, Donation Fund management, and improvement of the Site’s features) or to assist us in analyzing how the Service are used. These third parties have access to your personal information only for purposes of performing these tasks on our behalf.

    • In connection with a substantial corporate transaction, such as the sale of our business, a divestiture, merger, consolidation, or asset sale, or in the unlikely event of bankruptcy.

Those policies will govern use, handling and disclosure of your personal information once we have shared it with those third parties as described in this Privacy Policy. This information does not contain any personally identifiable information and may be used to develop website content and services that we hope you and other users will find of interest and to target content and advertising. Account changes/deletion and personal information removal

  • Registered User Profile Changes (except for account closure) may be done at any time through the Website. Click on the "My Account" link and changing the desired information.
  • Meal Train Specific Email Communication Preferences: Both registered and non-registered users can manage their communication preferences by clicking on the link at the bottom of most meal train generated emails.
  • Meal Train Specific Removal:
    • Registered Users may delete your name from a Meal Train Participant list by logging into your account and proceeding to the "Dashboard" and selecting the "Remove Me" button. Once you are removed, you will no longer receive communication related to the Meal Train.). You must individually remove your name from each Meal Train you do not wish to participate in.
    • Unregistered Users may send an email to contact@mealtrain.com requesting removal from a specific Meal Train. To ensure accuracy, please provide the email address to be removed.
  • Account Deletion/Personal Information Removal: You may choose to remove all personal information at any time by submitting a request via the "Contact Us" link on the home page of the Website. After you close your account, you will not be able to sign in to our Website, access any of your personal information, and all future events that you have created or scheduled will be cancelled. You can open a new account at any time.
  • If you close your account, we may still retain certain information associated with your account for analytical purposes and record keeping integrity, as well as to prevent fraud, collect any fees owed, enforce our Terms of Service, located as https://www.mealtrain.com/terms/ (the “Terms of Service”), take actions we deem necessary to protect the integrity of our Website or our users, or take other actions otherwise permitted by law. In addition, if certain information has already been provided to third parties as described in this Privacy Policy, retention of that information will be subject to those third parties’ policies.
