If you believe that we might have any information from or about a child under 13, please contact us at support@messageme.com.

II. WHAT INFORMATION DOES COMPANY COLLECT?

We gather various types of Personal Information from our users, as explained more fully below. We only use this Personal Information to provide and personalize and improve our services, to allow our users to set up a user account and profile, to contact users, to fulfill your requests for certain products and services, to analyze how users utilize the Service, and as otherwise set forth in this Privacy Policy. No Personal Information, including any communication privately shared by you via the Service to another user or a group of users selected by you, will be shared by us with any third parties, except as described in this Section and in Section III below. A. Information You Provide to Us:

We receive and store any information you knowingly provide to us. Also, if you click on a link to a third party website, such third party may also transmit cookies to you. This Privacy Policy does not cover the use of cookies by any third parties.

For example, this aggregate data tells us how often users use parts of the Service, so that we can make the Service appealing to as many users as possible. We may also provide this aggregate information to our partners; our partners may use such information to understand how often and in what ways people use our Service, so that they, too, can provide you with an optimal experience.
