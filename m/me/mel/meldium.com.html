Privacy Policy

Meldium takes privacy seriously. Below is how we collect, use, communicate and disclose and make use of personal data when you visit Meldium.com and the service Meldium.

Information You Give Us

When you register we ask for information like name and email address. We store this information in order to provide you with access to the service and to provide personalized services. Meldium does not collect or store any credit card information. Any credit card information is processed securely by Stripe.com.

Authentication Information

You may use Meldium to make connections between various web services. In order for Meldium to make those collections we will need a certain amount of authentication information to enable those connections on your behalf. Once you provide authentication details Meldium will retrieve and modify data on your behalf, but only in a matter as specified by you.

Automatic Information

We receive and store generic information such as IP address, cookie information, software and hardware attributes. This generic information does not reveal the identity of the visitor. We use this data in aggregate to help provide customer service.

When & How We Collect Information

We collect and use personal information solely for the purpose of providing a service specified by us and for other compatible purposes, unless we obtain the consent of the individual concerned or as required by law. We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the fulfillment of those purposes. We protect personal information by reasonable security safeguards against loss or theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use or modification. For more information, see our security policy.

Changes to this Policy

Changes to this policy may occur from time to time. If and when changes are made they will be posted on this page. If we make any material changes we will notify you by email (sent to the e-mail address specified in your account) or by means of a notice on Meldium.com prior to the change becoming effective. All changes will take effect thirty (30) days after they are initially posted on this page.
