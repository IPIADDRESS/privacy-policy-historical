Privacy Policy

Revised Draft v2.0 October 15, 2015

Keeping information private and secure is critically important to us at Cayan, LLC (“Cayan”, “we”, “us” or “our”). We recognize the importance of protection the privacy of information collected about our users, and we operate in accordance with applicable data protection legislation. This privacy policy (this “Privacy Policy”) is intended to help you understand how we gather, use, and disclose information that you (“you” or “User” or “Visitor”) provide to us by visiting our sites, and with whom information is shared.  This Privacy Policy applies to all Cayan-maintained websites, domains, merchant services and merchant portals, stored value and loyalty programs and other channels, and other online resources (the “Sites”), though because of the functionality of some of our Sites, additional terms and notices may be required.

Maintaining a website is an evolving process and we may need to to change this Policy, in whole or in part, at any time, with or without advance notice, by posting a new Policy in its place. Your continued use of the Site following any changes to this Policy will constitute your acceptance of such changes at the time of use.  If you disagree with how we may use your information under this Policy, you should not use the Site.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@cayan.com.

1. What type of information does Cayan gather?

The type of information gathered by Cayan is dependent on the nature of the activity and relationship with you. We provide a variety of products and services through our Sites, each of which is described below or in additional policies connected to those sites.

 

  • Non-Personal Information.  Generally, we collect browser information and other non-user specific information from all Visitors to the Site to support the operation of the system and provide us with broad user patterns and data. Information collected while you are browsing the Sites may be used to analyze trends, administer the Sites, improve Site performance, gather broad demographic information, and for security purposes. This information may be aggregated, which means it may be automatically provided to us but not tied to you as an individual. Examples include information about the websites that you visit just before or after our Sites, time spent at our Sites, and IP addresses, which are numbers automatically assigned to your computer whenever you access the Internet.
  • Cookies.  Cookies are a technology that can be used to provide you with tailored information from a website. A cookie is a small packet of data that a website can send to your browser, which may then store it on your system.  Cayan utilizes cookie technology to help deliver a better user experience, such as remembering your password for a future visit so that you don’t have to re-enter it. Most browser software provides you the opportunity to control cookies. You may modify your browser settings to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it. However, certain conveniences and other functionality on the Sites may be limited if you choose not to accept any cookies.
  • Personal Information.  For some features and services on the Sites, we collect personal information that you volunteer by completing various forms on the Site, including requests to receive information, download materials, or receive agent and/or merchant account applications. When you complete and submit a form or contact us via email, fax or phone (each an “Inquiry”), we may collect, store, and create a corresponding participating member profile.  The information obtained from the Inquiry and its contents, may include personally identifiable information (such as your real name, address, phone number, e-mail address, user IDs, passwords, billing information, or other information) you have provided. Any personally identifiable information you submit via an Inquiry is collected only with your knowledge and active participation, and by doing so you expressly consent to our storing, processing, and using such information for these purposes. When you register to use Cayan’s online processing services (“Gateway Services”), or you submit an application to obtain a Cayan merchant account, you will be required to provide personally identifiable information and may be required enter into a separate written agreement with Cayan. If you order any products or services from businesses, or ‘Customers’ that use our Gateway Services, you may be required to submit personally identifiable information for the purpose of processing those orders. Cayan may also obtain information about you from third party sources, including consumer-reporting agencies.

 

2. Who is collecting my information?

When you asked on the Sites to provide information, you are sharing that information with Cayan, unless specifically stated otherwise. Cayan works with some third party providers to improve the services and customer experience that we can provide to you.

 

  • Phone and Live Chat. When you call us by phone or click to access our live chat feature, Cayan, or our third party service providers, capture certain information about you including your caller identification. We also may record phone calls and live chat transcripts for quality and training purposes, and to improve our services. Contact information provided through these communications is stored in our database and may be compared to verify identification and improve our response.
  • Log Files. When you request information or download a file from the Sites, Cayan may store data about those actions and your IP address in a log file. We may use this information to analyze trends, administer the Sites, track users’ movements, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use or for other business purposes.
  • Tracking and Analytics. Cayan uses third-party services that utilize dynamic number insertion technology to help provide us with information about you when you click through an online advertisement. That technology connects a call that you place to us using a unique toll-free number with keyword and ad source information. We also use analytics tools provided through third parties to help us leverage anonymous identifiers so that we can serve you personalized content now or on a return visit to improve your experience.
  • Third Party Links.  Please be aware that the Site may provide links to third party websites and resources.  Such links are provided as a convenience to you and for informational purposes only.  Cayan is not responsible for and cannot control these third party websites and resources, does not endorse them, and is not responsible for their availability, content, or delivery of services.  Third party websites and resources are not bound by this Policy; they may have their own policies regarding data collection or none at all. We encourage you to read such other third party websites’ privacy policies.

 

3. How is my information used?

Cayan uses information collected from this Site for internal use only, including responding to your Inquiries or to contact you regarding Cayan services, and to provide and improve the services on our Sites. That information will be securely stored in our company databases. Cayan may also share some information with third parties that assist us in providing our products or services to you or in responding to your requests or Inquiries as described below.

 

  • Services. If you apply for a merchant account from Cayan or otherwise become a Cayan customer, we may use and disclose the information you supply to us, to evaluate your eligibility for a merchant account, including disclosure to consumer reporting agencies, relevant financial institutions, and other entities involved in providing the merchant account services, to provide you with our Gateway Services or to work with contracted third parties who will performed certain elements of the Gateway Services, or otherwise as indicated in your application. Cayan may also use your information to contact you about other Cayan offerings and to inform you about general company news and industry trends. Cayan may also use your information for internal business analyses.
  • Email Policy.  Cayan may use your email address to communicate with you about orders you have placed, accounts you maintain, or inquiries you have made about our products and services. In addition, Cayan may send you email communications for customer satisfaction purposes or to inform you of special offers Cayan believes will be of interest to you based on your profile, though we do not provide access to any third parties to do so without your consent. These emails contain an unsubscribe link, which will enable you to opt out of receiving future mailings at any time, should you choose to do so. You may also opt-out of email communications by contacting us at privacy@cayan.com.
  • Transfers.  We may transfer any information in our databases, including, but not limited to, personally identifiable information, to an affiliate or to a third party provider (such as a server hosting provider) as part of a transfer of business assets for the purpose of storing and processing such information on our behalf, and only to provide these services to you. Your provision of Personal Information will be handled in accordance with this Privacy Policy and we required that our affiliates process such information in compliance with our Privacy Policy, and we use all commercially reasonable efforts to only engage or interact with providers that adhere to a similar privacy policy governing their processing of Personal Information. Such information may be transferred to other countries. Your legal rights may be different in these countries, but we will hold your Personal Information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. By using the Sites, you consent to such transfers and processing as described in this Privacy Policy.

 

4. With whom does Cayan share my information?

Except as described in this Privacy Policy, Cayan does not sell, rent, or lease your personally identifiable information to other parties without your consent. If you do not want your personal information collected and used by Cayan, please do not visit or use the Sites.

 

  • Data Transfer. Given the scope of Cayan’s business, personal information may be visible to persons outside you country of residence, including to persons in countries that your own country’s privacy laws and regulations deem deficient in ensuing an adequate level of protection for such information. If you are unsure whether this Privacy Policy is in conflict with applicable local rules, you should not submit your information to us. If you are located within the European Union, you should note that your information will be transferred to the United States, which is deemed by the European Union to have inadequate data protection. Nevertheless, in accordance with local laws implementing European Union Directive 95/46/EC of 24 October 1995 on the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data on the free movement of such data (the “EU Privacy Directive”), individuals located in countries outside of the United States of America who submit personal information do thereby consent to the general use of such information as provided in this Privacy Policy and to its transfer to and/or storage in the United States of America.
  • Legal Process. We may disclose your personally identifiable information in order to comply with requests from law enforcement and government agencies, to enforce the terms of use and other agreements, and to protect Customers and others.  We may do so when we believe in good faith that disclosing this information is otherwise necessary or advisable, including, for instance, to identify, contact or bring legal action against someone who may be causing injury to or interfering with the rights or property of Cayan, another user or anyone else that could be harmed by such activities. We reserve the right to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of the terms of use, or as otherwise required by law. If Cayan or the Sites go through a business transition, such as a merger, acquisition by another company, or sale of all or substantially all of its assets resulting in a change of control of the Sites, your personally identifiable information will likely be among the assets transferred.  You will be notified via prominent notice on our Web site for 30 days of any such change in ownership or control of your personal information.

Cayan has implemented numerous safeguards to reasonably and adequately protect personal information, and uses a variety of means to protect personal information provided by users of our Sites, including the use of firewalls and other security measures on our servers.  Our databases are maintained and protected by physical, technical, and procedural measures that restrict access to user information to authorized persons in accordance with this Privacy Policy. No server is 100% secure, however. You must play a valuable role in maintaining this security. Please take this into account when providing information to us through the Sites. While we strive to protect your personally identifiable information and user privacy, we cannot guarantee the security of any information you disclose online.  We do not make any warranty, express, implied or otherwise, that we will be able to prevent loss, misuse, unauthorized access to, or alteration of personally identifiable information you provide to us.  You make any disclosure of personally identifiable information to us at your own risk.  We urge you to take every precaution to protect your personally identifiable information when you are on the Internet, including when visiting the Site.

We and our servers are located in the United States and are subject to the applicable state and federal laws of the United States. Those who choose to access the Service do so on their own initiative and at their own risk, and are responsible for complying with all local laws, rules, and regulations. We may limit the Sites’s availability, in whole or in part, to any person, geographic area, or jurisdiction we choose, at any time and in our sole discretion. We do not represent or warrant that the Sites, or any part thereof, are appropriate or available for use in any other jurisdiction. If users choose to access or use the Sites, they consent to the use and disclosure of information in accordance with this privacy policy and subject to such laws.
