What Information Do We Collect?


We collect browser information and other non-user specific information to support the operation of the system and provide us with broad demographic data.

There are various forms on the site such as an information request, agent application and merchant account applications. This information is provided to Merchant Warehouse to support the processing of the request.

We also collect the information required to process product orders on the site. This information is kept internally and only name, address and telephone number is provided externally to the delivery services we use.

What do we do with the information we collect?


Merchant Warehouse uses information collected from this website for internal use only, but may from time to time share some information with parties deemed necessary to completing or fulfilling an order or request for information. Information collected on this site is not sold or transferred to any person or party that is not directly involved in normal business operations.

Additional Information


We also use the LivePerson® help system on this site. LivePerson provides us with an online customer service solution so that we may serve you better. The privacy policy for LivePerson is located at http://www.liveperson.com/help/privacy.asp.

Cookies


Cookies are a technology which can be used to provide you with tailored information from a Web site. A cookie is an element of data that a Web site can send to your browser, which may then store it on your system. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it. However, an order cannot be processed on this site if you choose not to accept the cookies here.

Our site makes use of cookies to support the product ordering system. They are used to process the person's entries through the order system.
