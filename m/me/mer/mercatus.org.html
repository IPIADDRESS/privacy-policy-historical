Privacy Policy

Collection and Use of Information

The Mercatus Center collects personal information from visitors on a voluntary basis such as site registrations, program applications, and/or survey information. This may include mailing address, e-mail address, phone number, education information, and interests. In addition, Mercatus Center collects general information about pages visited or accessed.

The information collected is used internally to improve and customize web content and to contact and update consumers who requested information. This information is never sold or shared with organizations for commercial purposes.

Update

Personal information can be updated by logging into your membership profile online.

Unsubscribe

You will only receive information if you have requested it. If you do not wish to receive e-mail from us in the future, please e-mail us at mercatus@gmu.edu with REMOVE in the subject line or visit http://www.mercatus.org/mailing_lists.php to unsubscribe from e-mail updates.

Cookies

Cookies are used to store visitors preferences, personalize web content, alert visitors of new content, and track site usage. They are not used to extract personal information.

Ad Servers

The Mercatus Center does not partner with or have special relationships with any ad server companies.

Privacy

The Mercatus Center has taken steps to make all information received from our online visitors as secure as possible against unauthorized access and use. All information is protected by our security measures, which are periodically reviewed.

Ongoing Privacy Efforts

The Mercatus Center approaches our Internet communications with respect for and commitment to individual privacy. Customer information may intermittently be used for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in the privacy statement. If our practices change in the future the changes will be posted to our web site and the ability to opt out provided. Please check back periodically to see changes and additions. If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the above addresses or phone number.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices or the Mercatus Center web site, you can contact us by e-mail at pjdoland@pjdoland.com or by telephone at (703) 993-4930. We are located at 3301 N. Fairfax Dr., Ste. 450, Arlington, VA 22201.
