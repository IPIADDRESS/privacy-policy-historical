You also have the opportunity to correct or change the information you have provided to us.

Privacy Policy

This privacy policy discloses the privacy practices for M2 Trade, LLC.

A Special Note About Children

Persons under the age of 18 are not eligible for any offers presented by M2 Trade.

Registration

We request information from you on our subscription forms. This information is used for order fulfillment, customer service and to understand your areas of interest. We send this information to the publishers of the magazines you request.

Tracking Technology

If you have been offered our services through one of our marketing partners or affiliates (see "Use of Information" section below), we may share the information you enter with that partner or affiliate. To do so, we may use a technology called clear GIFs, which automatically and securely transfer the information you enter. In the future, we may also use other similar technologies in our offers for the same purpose. If we do so, we will modify our privacy policy accordingly. See the "Notification of Changes" section below.

Log Files

We log IP addresses (a unique number assigned to every computer on the Internet) in order to improve security, analyze trends and administer our offers. We may track your movement within the site, the pages from which you were referred, access times and browser types. We only use this information in aggregate and do not link it to you personally.

Use of Information

  • Contractual Relationships

    We provide your information to, and make it available to, our third party service vendors with whom we have contractual relationships for the purpose of servicing our processing of your magazine subscriptions. These vendors provide various services on our behalf such as customer service and web hosting. These vendors are not allowed to sell, rent or lease your personally identifiable information and are not permitted to use your information for any secondary purpose.

  • Publishers

    When you request a magazine, we forward your contact and address information to the applicable magazine publisher to determine your eligibility for fulfillment. The magazine publishers for the titles you have chosen to receive may use the information you provided to present you with other opportunities in accordance with their own privacy policies. When requesting a magazine, we encourage you to read the privacy statement of the publisher for that magazine; we do not review the privacy policies of the publishers in our program and are not responsible for their actions.

  • Retailers/Special Offers

    When you choose to accept a special retail offer, we forward your contact information to the applicable retailer and/or sponsor for fulfillment. The retailers and/or sponsors for the offers you have chosen to receive may use the information you provided to present you with other opportunities in accordance with their own privacy policies.  

  • Third Party Marketing Affiliates

    If you accept one of our offers by linking through one of our partners or affiliates, your information also may be shared with the partner or affiliate which originated the link to our site. These partners and affiliates may use the information you provided to present you with other opportunities in accordance with their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy policies of our partners and affiliates.

  • Exceptional Circumstances

    Please note that if M2 Trade or any of their lines of business is sold or disposed of as a going concern, whether by merger, sale of assets or otherwise, the relevant customer database of M2 Trade could, in whole or in part, be sold as part of that transaction. We also may have to release personally identifying information when it is necessary to comply with judicial or other government subpoenas, warrants or orders.

Links

Subscription offers may contain links to other sites. Please be aware that M2 Trade is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage you to read the privacy statements of each web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by M2 Trade.

Security

M2 Trade takes measures to protect the personal information you enter by using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption, which is recognized as an industry standard in encryption technology, whenever you enter personal information.

Special Offers and Choice/Opt-Out

After you complete our subscription forms, your answers and contact information are sent to the publisher of the magazines you requested so they can determine your eligibility and fulfill your subscriptions. These publishers may also contact you with special offers. From time to time, we may present you with specially selected offers from M2 Trade or one of our partners. Out of respect for your privacy, you can forfeit any of these opportunities by sending an email to privacy@mercury-magazines.com requesting to opt-out of future offers.

Site and Service Updates

We communicate with you to provide requested services and on issues relating to your subscriptions. We also send site and subscription service updates. You are not able to unsubscribe from these important announcements, which contain important information about your subscriptions and M2 Trade.

Correcting/Updating Personal Information

If you want to change any personal information you previously provided to us (such as your address) you can do so by emailing us at: service@mercury-magazines.com .

Notification of Changes

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on our site by identifying them as changes to our previous privacy policy. We will identify those changes for at least 60 days so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it.

Information Collection and Use

M2 Trade is the owner of the information collected on this site. If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the M2 Trade Privacy Department by emailing us at: privacy@mercury-magazines.com
