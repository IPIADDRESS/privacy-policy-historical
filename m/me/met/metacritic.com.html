Cookies

Cookies are NEVER used to TAKE information from your computer. Instead, they are used to temporarily store a very small piece of information on your computer to facilite your use of our website. In the future, this will enable us to provide personalized content for you on our site. For any password-protected areas of our site, we use cookies to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our site.
