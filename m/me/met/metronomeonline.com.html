Registration

Our registration form requires users to give us contact information (like their name) and unique identifiers (like their email address). We use customer contact information from the registration form to provide services on the Web site and to send the user information about our company and promotional material from some of our partners. Users have a choice to opt-in to receive such newsletters. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the Opt-In/Out section below. Your email address is necessary as a unique identifier when you create an account. All information you provide, including your email address, is uniquely identifying and is not shared with anyone outside MetronomeOnline.com.

Changing Personal Information

If you wish to change any of the personal information that you completed when you registered on MetronomeOnline.com, click the "Profile" link once you sign in. You then get to a page that allows you to update personal information including email address, password, mailing address, and all other options.

This site has security measures in place to ensure that personally identifiable information collected in the future is secure, including limiting the number of people who have physical access to our database servers as well as electronic security systems.

By using our Web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by MetronomeOnline.com. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies, information files that your Web browser places on your computer for the duration of your visit to a Web site. MetronomeOnline.com uses Cookies for keeping your session active and for personalization purposes.

MetronomeOnline.com also uses cookies to track our affiliates as part of our affiliate program and our advertising efforts. These cookies are placed on your computer and serve to track the commissions and generated sales.

Opt-In/Out of Requests for Information

We also give users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communication, such as email, from us by sending such a request to info@metronomeonline.com. If you have any questions, comments or concerns about MetronomeOnline.com privacy policy, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, please contact us at info@metronomeonline.com.
