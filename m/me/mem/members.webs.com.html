Our Commitment to Your Privacy

We fully appreciate the trust that you place in Webs and make it a priority to protect the personal information that you share with us.

This "Privacy Policy" discloses what information we gather and how we use it.

What information does Webs gather?

Webs gathers two types of information about users that are subject to our Policy:

  1. Voluntary submissions

    Information that users provide through optional, voluntary submissions. These are voluntary submissions made when creating an account on Webs, such as your name or email address. When you log into Webs or upload or post Content, we may also record information such as your IP address. Please understand that when you sign into Webs or post Content, your actions are not anonymous to us.

  2. Aggregated tracking

    Information Webs gathers on users through aggregated tracking, primarily through the use of cookies. When you visit Webs and sites hosted by Webs, we may record information such as your software and hardware attributes, the pages you request, and other cookie information. (For more on cookies, please see the section "What are cookies?")


Why does Webs gather information about me?

Webs collects personally identifiable information in order to establish and enhance our relationship with you. This information allows us to better tailor our content to users' needs and to help us better understand the demographics of our audience. Webs may use some of this information for contacting you, customizing the content and advertising you view, improving our services, conducting research, and providing anonymous reporting for clients. We only collect personally identifiable information from you because you are voluntarily submitting the information to us in order to enjoy certain Services. Under no circumstances do we divulge any personally identifiable information about an individual user to a third party unless mandated by law.


How does Webs gather information?

Webs Gathers User Information in the following processes:

  1. Voluntary submissions

    In registering for use of our Services, Webs may ask you to provide your email address, date of birth, location, name, and other information. We may also ask for this information from you in order to allow you to perform specific actions using the Services. Webs may ask you to complete voluntary surveys, polls, and other forms of information gathering. As a user on a site hosted by Webs, a Webs Member may solicit information from you by the use of forms, email, forums, or other Content. Information sent to a Webs Member in this manner is not controlled by Webs and is not subject to our Privacy Policy.

  2. Account tracking

    Webs may internally track statistics about Members associated with your account, such as login frequency, traffic generated, and services used. Webs may use this information to email you or create custom-made offers from Webs or on behalf of our partners. You may opt out of email offers by clicking the removal link at the bottom of such emails.

  3. Visitor and User Tracking

    Webs tracks user traffic patterns throughout all of our sites. However, we do not correlate this visitor information with data about individual users. We do break down overall usage statistics according to a Member's domain name, browser type and MIME type by reading this information from the browser string.

    Webs sometimes tracks and catalogs the search terms that users enter in our Search function, but this tracking is not associated with individual users. We use tracking information to determine which areas of our sites users like and don't like based on traffic to those areas. We do not individually track what users read, but rather how well each page performs overall. This helps us continue to build a better service for our users.


What are cookies?

Webs may place a text file called a "cookie" in the browser files of your computer. These cookies help us to provide you with a better experience. For example, a cookie may be used to store or "remember" your Member login information so that you are not required to manually log into the site at every visit.

The cookie itself may not directly contain personal information although it will enable us to relate your use of this site to information that you have specifically and knowingly provided. The only personal information our cookie contains is information you supply yourself. Our cookies will not read data off your hard disk or read cookie files created by other sites. Webs uses cookies to track user traffic patterns (as described above).

Our advertisers and partners may also set cookies through our Services. For more information, please see the section "What about third parties and advertisers?"

How secure is my information?

For example, we may be forced to disclose information to the government or third parties under certain circumstances, or third parties may unlawfully intercept or access transmissions or private communications. Additionally, in the unlikely event we need to investigate or resolve possible problems or inquiries, we can (and you authorize us to) disclose any information we possess about you to private entities, law enforcement or other government officials as we, in our sole discretion, believe necessary or appropriate.

For information on the security of billing-related information, please see "What about billing information and payments?"


How private is the Content I upload?

Any files, text, or other information ("Content") uploaded or created using our Services are by default hosted for the public and are thus publicly accessible unless explicitly protected by the Member's optional password protection portion of the Services. Files in a password protected directory will only be accessible by means of a username and password which the Member will provide, and which it is the sole responsibility of the Member to protect and maintain.

Even if you do not publish links to Content or submit links to a search engine, individuals or search engines may discover and link to your Content by data-mining, guessing the address, spidering sites, or other methods. If you are concerned about the privacy or confidentiality of Content that you produce, please take all appropriate steps to ensure that sensitive materials are password-protected. Webs makes no guarantee as to the reliability or security of the password protection service, which is subject to our Terms and Conditions as all other Webs Services.

Opting to delete and close your Webs account at any time for any reason.
