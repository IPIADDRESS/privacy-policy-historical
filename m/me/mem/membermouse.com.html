Built On Top of WordPress

A large part of building and managing member sites revolves around site design and content creation. WordPress is an established, world-class content management system that's infinitely extensible through the use of plugins and when it comes to determining how your site looks, is extremely flexible and there are thousands of themes available for immediate download for any need.

Rather than reinvent the wheel, we take full advantage of everything that WordPress brings to the table and deliver MemberMouse as a custom WordPress install.

The first thing you'll notice when using MemberMouse is that it feels like WordPress; it's a familiar and comfortable environment. Everything from publishing and managing content, to applying themes, to using plugins and even to installing your MemberMouse site, will be exactly the same as in the standard WordPress install.

You'll also notice that in intuitive places, WordPress is augmented and extended to provide you with the tools necessary to build and manage member sites. Here are some examples:

  • Access over 15 screens for configuring and managing every aspect of your member site directly from the WordPress admin area.
  • Indicate which pages and posts certain members have access to right from the Edit Page/Post screens.
  • Add pixel codes to a page or post right from the Edit Page/Post screens.
  • Manage member-contributed blog posts right from the Manage Posts screen.

It's one thing to talk about these features, but to really understand the extent of the power, you'll have to check out our online demo to see MemberMouse in action.

Leverage the Power of Amazon S3

  • Amazon S3 is a scalable, reliable, secure and inexpensive data storage solution.
  • MemberMouse provides you with a tool called the File Vault where you sync all of your Amazon S3 content with MemberMouse (videos, audio, PDFs, graphics, etc.)
  • MemberMouse has a built-in feature for piracy-proofing your digital content.
  • Incorporating secure content into your site has never been easier thanks to our secure SmartTags™.
  • All content in the File Vault is protected by our Secure-3™ Content Delivery system.

When a secure piece of content is requested, MemberMouse passes that request on to Amazon S3. After authentication, Amazon S3 returns a self-expiring link that's only valid for a specific period of time. The member can then access the content using this link.

To insert a video or audio, just use our secure media SmartTag™ and MemberMouse will take care of retrieving a secure link and automatically displays the video or audio file in a JW player.

How about protecting eBooks or other PDF content? We have a proprietary PDF protection technology built right into MemberMouse called eBook Lockdown Pro™, that drastically reduces the risk of your profits being siphoned away by piracy.

Just use our secure PDF SmartTag and MemberMouse will limit the number of downloads per user, track their IP address and here's the icing on the cake...

MemberMouse will watermark each page of the PDF with the member's personal information - their name, their email, their customer ID - and if this PDF ends up anywhere besides their desktop, you'll know who the culprit is and they'll know that you know.
