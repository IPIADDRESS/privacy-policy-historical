1. The Information Memocast Collects

Any member profile information, media, video, image, comment, or any other content posted onto the Memocast Portal at your direction becomes publicly-published content, is not considered personally identifiable information subject to this Privacy Notice, and may be collected and used by others.

In addition, Memocast may give you the option to provide us with certain demographic information about yourself (such as age, gender, hobbies, interests, zip code, state, and country).
  • “Cookies” Information: When you visit the Memocast Portal, we may send one or more cookies – a small text file containing a string of alphanumeric characters – to your computer. Memocast uses both session cookies and persistent cookies. A persistent cookie remains after you close your browser and may be used by your browser on subsequent visits to the Memocast Portal. By using these cookies, we can “remember” what you’ve done on the Memocast Portal before and this lets us personalize our site for you and save certain parts of the site or services just the way you like them. These persistent type of cookies can be removed, but each web browser is a little different, so please look through your browser “Help” file to learn the correct way to modify your cookies set-up.
  • “Automatically Collected” Information: When you visit the Memocast Portal or open one of our HTML emails, we automatically record certain information from your web browser by using different types of technology, including “clear gifs” or “web beacons”. This “automatically collected” information may include your Internet Protocol address (“IP Address”), web browser type, the web pages or sites that you visit just before or just after the Memocast Portal, the pages you view on our site, and the dates and times that you visit.
