Privacy & PDP Policy

English | Bahasa Malaysia

Thank you for visiting us online at www.mcdonalds.com.my. If you continue to browse and use this website you are agreeing to comply with and be bound by Golden Arches Restaurants Sdn Bhd (“McDonald’s® Malaysia”) Privacy and Personal Data Protection Policy.

At McDonald’s® Malaysia, we strive to do everything we can to respect the trust customers have in our brand and our commitment to your privacy is no exception. We are committed to protecting and safeguarding consumer privacy.

  1. McDonald’s Privacy and Personal Data Protection Policy This Privacy and Personal Data Protection Policy is issued taking into account the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (“the Act”) that was introduced by the Government of Malaysia to regulate the processing of Personal Data in commercial transactions. The Act requires us to inform you of your rights in respect of your Personal Data that is being processed or that is to be collected and further processed by us and the purposes for the data processing. The Act also requires us to obtain your consent to the processing of your Personal Data. In view of the Act, McDonald’s® Malaysia is committed to ensuring that your Personal Data is protected.
  2. Collection of Personal Data
    Consequently, we wish to inform you that the types of Personal Data collected essentially depend on the purpose of collection. Thus depending on the purpose of collection, the Personal Data collected may be in the form of:
    • Name.
    • Date of birth.
    • Gender.
    • Particulars of identity card / passport.
    • Contact Information, address.
    • Nationality.
    • Race.
    • Photograph, video, CCTV.
    • Education.
    • Income.
    • Feedback, performance review.
    • Employment records.
    • References.
    • Banking particulars, credit card details.
    • Information on tax, EPF and SOCSO.
    • Sensitive information such as criminal record, religion, health etc., if permitted by applicable laws.
    • Such other information as may be required for specific reasons.
  3. Purpose of Collection
    The Personal Data as provided/furnished by you to McDonald’s® Malaysia or collected by McDonald’s® Malaysia from you or through such other sources as maybe necessary for the fulfillment of the purposes (“collected”) at the time it was sought or collected, will be used for such purpose and or for the following reasons (collectively “Purposes”):
    • Assessing application to register for the use of the McDonald’s® Malaysia Website [Personal Data collected maybe in the form of 2(a) - (g), (q) or part thereof, and may also include 2(n) if any payment is required for services or products].
    • Assessing application for franchising [Personal Data collected maybe in the form of 2(a) – (q) or part thereof].
    • Assessing application for employment [Personal Data collected maybe in the form of 2(a) – (q) or part thereof].
    • To communicate with you [Personal Data collected maybe in the form of 2(a) – (e), (q) or part thereof].
    • To provide services, to process billing / payment transactions [Personal Data collected maybe in the form of 2(a) - (g), (n), (q) or part thereof].
    • To respond to your inquiries [Personal Data collected maybe in the form of 2(a), (d), (e), (q) or part thereof, depending on the nature of your inquiries].
    • To administer participation in any contest/program related to the public organized by us [Personal Data collected maybe in the form of 2(a) - (h), (q) or part thereof, and may also include 2(n) if any payment is required].
    • For marketing and promotional activities, market surveys, trend and statistical analysis [Personal Data collected maybe in the form of 2(a) - (k), (q) or part thereof].
    • For finance activities [Personal Data collected maybe in the form of 2(a) - (f), (j), (l), (n), (o), (q) or part thereof].
    • For credit assessments, financial and background investigation as and when deemed necessary [Personal Data collected maybe in the form of 2(a) – (q) or part thereof].
    • For the maintenance and upkeep of customer records [Personal Data collected maybe in the form of 2(a) – (h), (k), (n), (q) or part thereof].
    • Meeting any legal or regulatory requirements relating to our provision of services and products and to make disclosure under the requirements of any applicable law, regulation, direction, court order, by-law, guideline, circular, code applicable to us or our affiliates [Personal Data collected maybe in any form as required by the requirements].
    • To enter into the necessary agreement and/or contract [Personal Data collected maybe in the form of 2(a) – (q) or part thereof].
    • For handling the report for lost item, customer complaints and taking appropriate action relating thereto [Personal Data collected maybe in the form of 2(a) – (g), (h), (k), (n), (q) or part thereof].
    • For security and crime prevention purposes, risks management, safeguarding McDonald’s® Malaysia in the event of any claim, litigation, suits etc [Personal Data collected maybe in the form of 2(a) – (q) or part thereof].
    • For any other grounds or reasons [Personal Data collected will correspond to such grounds or reasons].
    [Note that the reference to the items set out in clause 2 is based on the normal practices, subject to changes if the circumstances require].

    McDonald’s® Malaysia does not sell, transfer or disclose personal information to third parties outside the McDonald’s® Malaysia Family. The Personal Data provided to us will be kept confidential however, we may disclose your Personal Data to the following parties:
    • Within the McDonald’s® Malaysia Family that includes McDonald’s Corporation, our franchisees, our subsidiaries, business partners and our affiliates that provide related services or products in connection with our business;

We may request your assistance to procure the consent of third parties whose Personal Data is provided by you to us and you agree to use your best endeavours to do so.
