Cybersocket is committed to honoring the privacy of Cybersocket subscribers and visitors to Cybersocket.com. Subscribers and visitors can always choose whether or not they wish to disclose personal information to Cybersocket. Certain information is required for registration, but other information is optional  and may be given at the subscriber or visitor’s discretion.

Cybersocket’s advertisers and sponsors are never granted access to any individual’s personal information. Also, by submitting all of the required information, you will receive periodic emails from Cybersocket. If you do not wish to receive these periodic emails, simply follow the instructions in the email to opt out or unsubscribe.
