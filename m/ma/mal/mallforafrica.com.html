Privacy Policy

MallforAfrica is committed to providing a safe and secure shopping environment for its clientele, offering hard to get, top of the line merchandise.

Browsing items offered for sale on this site do not require Visitors to submit personally identifiable information, however, some portions of this site may require personally identifiable information to participate. Portions of the site requiring personally identifiable information and the types of personally identifiable information that may be collected are discussed below.

This site will continue to offer visitors new and ever-changing content, so we may modify our practices from time to time. Please check back with us regarding our policies and practices. In the future, any personal information collected shall be treated in accordance with the privacy policy in effect at the time information is collected.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If anyone If you are UNDER THE AGE OF 18, you may not register an account, make a purchase or provide us with your personal information. Information Collected

Visitors  must be 18 years of age or older to make a purchase on the MallforAfrica app and website.

  • Email address

SHIPPING INFORMATION - (If merchandise is being sent to someone else)  

  • Name (First and Last)

  • Your Email address - So that we may email you shipping confirmation or if there are any problems with your order, special correspondence and special offers.

If you do not complete registration, we will immediately delete the information you submitted during the process from our system.

My Account

With this convenient feature, customers have the option of registering certain information with us while completing an online order. We will store login name (email address), name, mailing address, alternate ship-to addresses, telephone number and credit card information and order history in order to make future purchases more convenient.
