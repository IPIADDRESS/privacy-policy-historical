Privacy Policy
The Mall of America understands that privacy is very important to you. The following policy is intended to protect your privacy and ensure that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. We continue to develop our web site and take advantage of technologies to improve the services we provide. As a result, our policies will continue to evolve. By using the Mall of America web site, you consent to our use of guest information as described in this Online Privacy Policy. As we implement new technology and introduce new services, we will update our Online Privacy Policy. We encourage you to refer to this policy on an ongoing basis for our most current privacy policy practices.

Guest information is used to improve our web site content, to enhance your on-line shopping experience and for marketing purposes. Guest information is gathered when you register to make a purchase, register your email address, or respond to surveys or other solicitations.

The information we gather about you may include your name and email address and demographic information, like your zip code, age, or income level. If you make a purchase, we may also ask you for your social security number, account numbers, or credit card numbers.

We also may automatically track certain information about you based upon your behavior on our web site. We use this information to do internal research on our guest's demographics, interests, and behavior to better understand and service our guests.

We may share guest information with businesses affiliated with Mall of America. They may use this information as we would, and you may occasionally receive product and service information from them. In addition, we may share this information with third parties that we believe are reputable in order to offer products and services that may be of interest to you. Finally, we may share this information with outside vendors and organizations that provide support services to us or that help us with our marketing programs. However, at no time will we share credit card or social security numbers in any fashion other than aggregate demographic information, except as required by law.
