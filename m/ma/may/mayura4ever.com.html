Privacy Policy


Last Updated: 2012 December 01

At Mayura4Ever, we appreciate your readership and loyalty. With respect we value your privacy at first place. So it's our responsibility to let you know what information we gathering and purposes of collecting them to create more transparent relationship with you. This "Privacy Policy" may update at any time and you can check back at any time to ensure that you are familiar with most recent version of our Privacy Policy.


You may provide your personal information


i. By sharing your e-mail when subscribing to our newsletter

ii. By sharing your email when contacting us through our contact form

iii. By sharing personal information you made public, when you post comments on our posts

iv. By sharing personal information when signing up to be a member of our site and following our social network profiles such as Facebook, Twitter, Google+ etc.

In addition to your basic information, our web analytic trackers may collect other information such as country origin, your internet browser and operating system used to surf our site. They will use cookies to improve quality of their service and if you wish to disable cookies, you may go through your browser options. "Mayura4Ever" itself has no access or control over any of those cookies.


How do we make use of your personal information? Do we use them at all?


We use web analytic data to track our site traffic, analyze statistics and identify trends to maximize user experience which beneficial for you. Except you can find below what information we can use to serve you better.

i. The email addresses you provided by subscribing to our newsletters, enable us to send you latest updates of our site. You can find unsubscribe link to opt-out from our newsletters at any time.

ii. The email address you provided us through our contact form will not be used for any other purposes unless for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.

iii. The information associated with your account when commenting on our posts will not be used for any purpose but your contact email can be used if you made any policy violation when you interact with our site.

We are powered by Blogger and third parties or services may also collect your information for their purposes. You can read their privacy policies to familiar with information they have been collecting.

Yes, you can inquire about our Privacy Policy at any time through our Contact form. We appreciate your consideration about our Privacy Policy.
