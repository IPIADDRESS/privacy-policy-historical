Privacy Policy

www.mapsofworld.com (hereinafter referred to as "MOW") respects your privacy and committed to ensure that your privacy is protected by means of protecting your personally Identifiable Information you share with us. Personally Identifiable Information is information by which a person can be identified such as name, address, telephone number, SSN, e - mail ID and date of birth.

Should MOW ask you to provide Personally Identifiable Information when using this website, you can be assured that it will only be used in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

MOW may change this policy from time to time by updating this page. You should check this page periodically to ensure that you are comfortable with any changes. This policy was last updated on December 28th 2007.

  • MOW may use 'cookies' and 'web beacons' to manage your status and verify your identity
  • MOW may collect geophysical IP Address / browser information / other technical information for system and server administration.
  • MOW may collect other information relevant to surveys and offers.
What does MOW do with the information collected
MOW requires this information to understand your needs and provide you with a better service, and in particular for the following reasons:
  • To fulfill the services you have asked MOW to perform
  • For internal record keeping.
  • To improve MOW products and services.
  • MOW may periodically send promotional e - mail about new products, special offers or other information which MOW think you may find interesting using the email address which you have provided. However, you may unsubscribe yourself to such promotional e - mails through the link provided in every such e - mail itself.
  • From time to time, MOW may also use your information to contact you for market research purposes. MOW may contact you by email, phone, fax or mail. Any time you can easily unsubscribe from MOW mailing list and no longer receive such offers and mailings.
  • MOW may use the information to customize the website according to your interests

With whom MOW shares the information
As a thumb rule, MOW will not willfully disclose your Personally Identifiable Information with any third party except under following conditions -
  • When Information is forwarded to Service Providers to fulfill the services you have asked MOW to perform.
  • When MOW is legally compelled to provide the information to the Law Enforcement Authorities or government regulatory or administrative or similar agency as part of a judicial or administrative proceeding to disclose the Information.
Security
MOW is committed to ensure that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure MOW has put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information MOW collects online. While MOW strives to protect your personal information, MOW can not ensure or warrant total security of any information you transmit over the transmission medium.
