Madalin Games only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below, except that commenter IP addresses and email addresses are visible and disclosed to the administrators of the blog/site where the comment was left.

    Certain visitors to Madalin Games's websites choose to interact with Madalin Games in ways that require Madalin Games to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that Madalin Games gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. Those who engage in transactions with Madalin Games are asked to provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to process those transactions. In each case, Madalin Games collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor's interaction with Madalin Games. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

    Aggregated Statistics

    Madalin Games may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. Madalin Games may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, Madalin Games does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

    A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Madalin Games uses cookies to help Madalin Games identify and track visitors, their usage of Madalin Games website, and their website access preferences. Madalin Games visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Madalin Games's websites, with the drawback that certain features of Madalin Games's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
