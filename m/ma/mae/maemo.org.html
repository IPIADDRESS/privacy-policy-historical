The cookies used by Nokia or its third-party designees on the Site are session-specific and are cancelled and expire when you leave the Site and are not stored on your computer. Please note that Nokia may provide aggregate statistics about our customers, sales, traffic patterns, and related Site information to reputable third parties, but these statistics will not include any personally identifiable information.

Nokia is committed to the protection of the privacy of its Site visitors.
