PanGeo Media is committed to protect your privacy. The information we collect while you are visiting our network of sites is used to provide you with a more personalized and richer experience. Please read further for details.

How does PanGeo Media collect information?

Your IP Address
Your IP address is a unique identifier we use to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our web site tracking. We also use your IP address to help identify you and to gather broad and general demographic information.

Cookies
PanGeo Media may use a feature on your Internet Web browser called a "cookie." A cookie is a small piece of information stored on your hard drive. It assigns a unique identification number to your computer and helps us maintain your preferences between visits to provide a personalized experience for you.

PanGeo Media uses cookies to tell us whether or not you have visited our Web site before and to track click-throughs to any of our advertisements. We also collect non-personally identifiable information, such as anonymous session numbers, with cookies.

PanGeo Media allows other companies that are presenting advertisements on some PanGeo Media pages to set and access cookies on your computer. PanGeo Media does not control cookies from third-party advertisers. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not the privacy policy of PanGeo Media . Advertisers or other companies do not have to access to PanGeo Media cookies.

The use of advertising cookies sent by third-party servers is standard in the Internet industry.
