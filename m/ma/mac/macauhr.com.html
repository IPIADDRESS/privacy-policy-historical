IGNITE MEDIA GROUP

PRIVACY POLICY
Ignite Media Group is a media conglomerate with businesses in outdoor, online, print, and special events targeting visitors to Macau, an entertainment destination in Asia. The Group was formed in 2009 by the union of 10 Macau companies (members) such as Macau.com, Destination Macau, DirectelMacau, Ec Ad, Aomen.tv, Qoos, Vproperty.mo, MacauHR, New Macau Box Office, and Macau Ignite Technology.


Information about Online Visitors
Ignite Media Group members gather information about all of our online visitors collectively, such as what areas users visit most frequently and what services users access the most. We only use such data anonymously and in the aggregate. This information helps us determine what is most beneficial for our online users, and how we can continually create a better overall experience for you. We may share this information with our partners, but only in the aggregate, so that they too may understand how Ignite Media Group’s visitors use our websites, and also create a better overall experience for you. We do not disclose information about your individual visits to any outside parties, except when the law requires it.

Personally Identifiable Information
Ignite Media Group members may collect “personally identifiable” information from you via online purchases on our websites, member registrations, online surveys, email newsletter sign ups, contest registrations and other promotions.


Other Information
Apart from this “personally identifiable” information, Ignite Media Group members collect other information that does not personally identify you. Specific or aggregate information may be collected to help maintain accurate accounts, assist in marketing of our products and services and to help make our websites more useful to you. Ignite Media Group members may provide aggregated statistical information about user behavior to reputable third parties. This is not personally identifiable information.


Collection of personal information by business partners
Ignite Media Group members may collect “personally identifiable” information from you via online purchases on our websites, member registrations, online surveys, email newsletter sign ups, contest registrations and other promotions.


Cookies
Ignite Media Group members use a feature of your internet browser called “a cookie” to assign a user ID. Cookies identify your computer to our servers when you visit our websites and extend the functionality of the service offered in certain areas of our websites. We suggest you have Cookies enabled on your internet browser to use Ignite Media Group members’ websites.



How do we protect your personal information?
Ignite Media Group members take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of collected data. These include internal reviews of our data collection, storage and processing practices and security measures, as well as physical and technological security measures to guard against unauthorized access to systems where we store personal data.



Unsolicited commercial email or “SPAM”
“SPAM” means unwanted marketing or commercial email. Ignite Media Group members do not and have never supported or condoned sending SPAM email.




Additional Privacy Protections for Children Using the Internet
Ignite Media Group members join the industry in recognizing that children, including young teens, may not be able to make informed choices about personal information requested online. Accordingly, we do not solicit or collect “personally identifiable” information from children and teenagers under the age of eighteen and request that you certify this requirement on all our websites’ email collection forms.



Amendment of Privacy Policy
We reserve the right to amend the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy from time to time and without notice.



This privacy policy is originally constructed in English and translated into Chinese. Every effort has been made to ensure the translation is accurate and consistent. In case of any disputes or inconsistencies, the English version shall prevail.
