Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy describes our dedication in protecting our visitors and customers information collected on our website (www.makeplayingcards.com). In the policy set herein, "personal and account information" means your name, company name, account number, address, telephone number, and e-mail address plus other information that identifies you. We will not sell any of your personal and account information to third parties.

When you browse our Web site

  1. We collect your IP address to measure our web traffic to help provide a relevant shopping experience.
  2. If you come to our Website via a link, we collect the location of the link that referred you.
  3. We collect information about the browser you are using to help optimize our website experience.
  4. We keep track of pages you visit to help provide you with a more personalized shopping experience.

When you make purchases through our website

When you make a purchase from our Website, we collect your name, address and payment information to process your orders and requests. Your personal and account information might be shared internally to assist with your orders and requests. Makeplayingcards.com will never sell or share your payment information to any third parties. All of our servers operate under 128-bit encryption technology and Secure Socket Layers ("SSL") in all areas where your personal and account information is required. Our Website is certified by VeriSign, which verifies that our Website is authentic with SSL security in place.

When you contact us in writing

When you submit questions, e-mails, survey, comments, suggestions, reviews or other written information to us you grant Makeplayingcards.com and its affiliate(s) permission to use, share, implement, and/or transfer such information for internal use, marketing, advertising and other promotional purposes. Makeplayingcards.com will not sell or share your information collected to any third parties unless otherwise noted. If you do not wish to receive promotional information, you may opt out of future communications by sending us an email to unsubscribe from our e-mail subscriptions.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy please contact us here: enquiry@makeplayingcards.com
