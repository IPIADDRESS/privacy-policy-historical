Privacy Policy

 www.makemenoise.com

 Privacy of our visitors is very important to us. We don’t share & sell any kind of private information to 3rd party while visiting our website makemenoise.com. We keep your and your family privacy in safeguard.

 Log Files

 We collect all the data found in log files and we use these data. Log Files are those files which include your Internet Protocol (IP) address, Internet Service Provider (ISP), Browser you used to visit our site, Time/Date of visit our site and which pages you visited our site.

 Cookies and Web Beacons

 Our Website www.makemnoise.com doesn’t use any cookies.

 We use ads powered by 3rd party on our website www.makemenoise.com to support our website. Our Advertisement Partners may use advanced technology like web beacons, Cookies, JavaScript once their ads displayed on our website, to get information of your IP address and ISP as well and may use some other information for Geo-targeting reason like to get information about specific product on particular site or may be particular location; they adopt these methods to check effectiveness of the advertisement on our website concerning single person.

 We don’t have control over cookies used by 3rd party advertisers.You may refer Privacy policies of these 3rd party ads provider to secure your information. In this Case, www.makemenoise.com’s privacy policy is not applicable and we can’t control such kind of activities by 3rd party ads provider.

 You can disable your browser cookies; by going through respective browser’s cookies option; you can also get information about your browser’s cookies by visiting your browser’s website.
