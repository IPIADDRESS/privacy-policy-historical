Privacy Policy for MakeItMP3.com

Privacy

Your privacy is very important to us at MakeItMP3.com. As such, this Privacy Policy document outlines what data MakeItMP3.com collects and how we manage that information. This policy may be subject to change at any time without notification.

Logs

MakeItMP3.com makes use of log files, which are files that record information including requested video resources, IP addresses, and timestamps. This information may be used for troubleshooting, trend analysis, and site maintenance. Our log files are regularly deleted, and we do not keep a permanent or traceable record of them.

Collected Information

MakeItMP3.com stores information that you enter on the website. This includes requested video links, search queries, and video data like the requested filename, start time, end time, and quality. This information is stored to track and analyze user activity and is associated with anonymized IP addresses. All information you enter is voluntary. None of this information is shared with third parties.

Cookies

MakeItMP3.com does not set any cookies in your browser, in the future should we offer a premium experience, cookies will be utiilized at that time. Cookies may be stored by Google through Google Adsense and Google Analytics usage. For more information on Google's Privacy Policy, visit Google's Privacy Center We have no control over cookies set and managed by third parties, but you may manage and disable cookies at any time through your individual browser's options.

Questions

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy document or would like any further information, please don't hesitate to contact us as info@makeitmp3.com
