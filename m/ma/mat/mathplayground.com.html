Math Playground's Privacy Statement
 
 
In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, MathPlayground.com, MathApprentice.com, ActionMath.com, ThinkingBlocks.com, and SATMathPro.com limit the collection of personal information from children.

Use of Personal Information
Your personal information may be used in the following ways:

Forms - Used by students to send math problems and solutions to MathPlayground.com. The student's first name, school name, state and grade level are required. Last names, email and personal addresses are not requested. If a word problem is selected, we may contact the student's school. We have no way of contacting students directly.

E-mail Correspondence - Used to raise and respond to issues and inquiries of all types. May also be used to distribute information about Math Playground, Thinking Blocks, and other services.
