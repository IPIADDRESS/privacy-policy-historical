Match.com pioneered the online personals industry when it launched on the Web in 1995, and privacy and safety were built into the site from day one.

Ten years later, Match.com continues to diligently keep our site secure for its members. It’s a fact that we collect information from our members. This allows us to deliver products and services to help those members in their search for love. Most importantly, this information helps us do what we’re here for – match people and create successful relationships. We don’t sell any information to third parties, and any data that is shared is purely to enhance the site experience for all involved.

When it comes to privacy on our site, all member profiles are screened by real people for appropriateness before being posted to the site. That includes making sure that the profile does not include any specific identifying information. In addition, all communication between members is done through Match.com's proprietary double-blind email system, with members using an anonymous username and not his or her real email address. This ensures that no personal contact information is revealed until a member is ready to do so.

Please take a moment to read our detailed Privacy Policy below and review our dating safety tips. Protecting our members and keeping their personal information private is as important to us as helping them find love. And that’s really important.
