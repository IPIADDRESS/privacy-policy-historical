Traffic Data

MagazineDiscountCenter.com collects the visitor's domain name/IP Address (e.g., whether the user is logged on from firefox.com or Penn.edu or 198.122.222.0), referral data (e.g., we record the address of the last URL a user visited prior to clicking through to an MagazineDiscountCenter.com site), and browser/platform type (e.g., a Fire Fox browser on a Macintosh platform).
In addition to collecting this type of information, MagazineDiscountCenter.com's advertisement delivery system collects aggregate search terms for internal reporting and targeted advertising. MagazineDiscountCenter.com works with third parties that serve ads for this site.

MagazineDiscountCenter.com also counts, tracks, and aggregates the visitor's activity into our analysis of general traffic flows at MagazineDiscountCenter.com sites (e.g., tracking where traffic comes from and how traffic flows within the MagazineDiscountCenter.com sites). MagazineDiscountCenter.com may merge this information into group data, which may then be shared on an aggregated basis with potential advertisers, research firms, and business partners. When MagazineDiscountCenter.com does present aggregated information to potential advertisers, research firms and business partners, no one will be able to identify you or contact you.
