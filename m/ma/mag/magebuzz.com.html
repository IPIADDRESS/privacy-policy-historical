Privacy Policy

Information Collection

We will collect following information when you visit our website: web browser information, your IP address, your country. Other personal information (such as: name, address, telephone, email address…) are only collected by us when you provide voluntarily.

Information Using

We use your information to improve our website to meet your needs and help you browse our website more effectively. Your personal information will be used to fulfill your orders, respond to your inquiries and supply you with requested information the products and services.
