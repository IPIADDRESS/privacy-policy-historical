Privacy Statement

MageBuzz understand the importance of your privacy. The following policy describes how we collect, use and protect your information.

Information Collection

We will collect following information when you visit our website: web browser information, your IP address, your country. Other personal information (such as: name, address, telephone, email address…) are only collected by us when you provide voluntarily.

Information Using

We use your information to improve our website to meet your needs and help you browse our website more effectively. Your personal information will be used to fulfill your orders, respond to your inquiries and supply you with requested information the products and services.

Using of Cookies

Some of our Web pages may contain "cookies," which are data that may be sent to your Web browser and stored on your computer. Once you log out your account or close your browser, this cookie expires and no longer has any effect. If you disable cookies for our site, you may still use our site, but certain benefits of your shopping experience with us may be impacted.
