Privacy Policy

M&T Bank Corporation understands the importance of treating customer information in a confidential manner. We know privacy is an important element of your banking relationship, and we realize you expect us to collect and maintain personal information in an appropriate and responsible fashion. Accordingly, we want you to be aware of the following policy which outlines how M&T Bank Corporation and its family of banks and their affiliated companies ("M&T") safeguards your personal information:

  • M&T collects, maintains and uses information about customers in order to conduct its business and make available products/services that may be of interest to its customers.
  • M&T will attempt to ensure that customer information is accurate and allow customers the ability to communicate with the Bank if inaccurate information needs to be changed.
  • M&T only allows personally identifiable information to be accessed by M&T employees that have a business reason for knowing such information. Procedures are in place to protect against the unauthorized access to customer information, and M&T will take appropriate disciplinary measures to enforce compliance with such procedures.
  • M&T will only share specific information about its customer accounts or other personally identifiable information with third parties under the following circumstances:
    1. to comply with applicable laws and regulations.
    2. to protect M&T against fraud.
    3. to complete banking transactions or transfers.
    4. to provide products/services that may be of interest to its customers.
    5. to verify the existence and condition of your account for a third party such as a credit bureau or merchant.
    6. at our discretion, within the M&T family of banks and affiliated companies, to the extent permitted by applicable law.

  • If M&T contracts with a third party to perform specific services for M&T which may require us to provide them with access to customer information, M&T requires that such third party maintain the confidentiality of customer information.
