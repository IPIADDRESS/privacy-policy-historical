AND PRIVACY POLICYWe are committed to protecting your privacy. At times, we may ask you to provide information about yourself. For example, some of the information collected may include name, title, address, phone number, email address, and personal information concerning your interests, occupation, gender and other demographic information.Contacting You Concerning Promotions and New Products. We may request you to “opt-in” to receive certain communications at our Point-of-Sale or website. We will not send messages to you concerning promotions or new products or other marketing material unless you have opted-in.   Please see the sections “How Can I Opt Out” below for information on how to stop receiving our e-mail or mobile text communications.Policy on Information from Persons under the Age of 18. We do not seek to collect personally identifiable information from persons under the age of 18. If you are under the age of 18, please do not submit any personally identifiable information to us. If you are the parent or guardian of a person under the age of 18 who has provided personally identifiable information to us, please inform us by contacting Mandee customer service at custserv@mandee.com or by phone at 201-890-0021, ext 2200, and we will remove such information from our database.

How We Use Information You Submit to Us. We use your information to provide services to you, including coupons, customer alerts and special offers. We may combine your personally identifying information and information about your preferences and activities with those of all or a particular group of our shoppers to prepare collective profiles of our users and their activities for our internal use. For example, we may track the number of our users who purchase certain product or use certain features on our website, or compile the total dollar amount of sales transacted in a particular geographic region or to track purchasing trends. Such information, and other information we derive from individual users’ information, allows us to continue to grow and provide you with quality information through more accurate marketing and advertising. We may also share aggregated information concerning our users, without attribution of personal information, to other companies with whom we conduct business.

We will not use or disclose your personally identifiable information, or your e-mail or mobile phone number, to any persons or entities outside of our company and our service providers, contractors, representatives and advisors, nor shall we lease, license, sublicense, rent, transfer, disclose, disseminate or otherwise grant access to such information unless: You have been given an opportunity to “opt-in” and have agreed to so opt-in to such disclosure of your information;

We are required to respond to subpoena, court orders or other requirement of law which requires disclosure of your information. You have otherwise consented to provide your information to such persons or entities. We sell or transfer our MANDEE business to another person or entity.

How Can I “Opt-out” of Receiving Email Communications? We will not send e-mail messages to you if you indicate that you do not wish to receive such messages. If you do not wish to receive email messages from us, please email Mandee at  custcserv@mandee.com your request to opt-out, click the “unsubscribe” link on any e-mail we have sent you, or access the  ”http://www.members.mandee.com/efs/Mandee.do” “mailing preferences” section on www.Mandee.com  and adjust your mailing preferences from the “yes” to the “no” option.  It may take up to 10 days for mailing preference changes to take effect, and you may receive e-mail messages during this time.

Mobile Alert Program. You may subscribe to have promotional text messages sent directly to your mobile device. Promotional text messaging may include event alerts and updates, promotions, and special offers available to mobile alert participants.  This program is voluntary, and messages will be sent to you only if you have opted in to receive them.  Your subscription starts when you provide us your mobile phone number through a text message.  You will receive up to eight (8) messages per month if you subscribe to this service.

You may opt-in to the program at any time by texting MANDEE2000 or any of the store specific keywords found at each Mandee retail location to “MANDEE” (626333).

You may cancel the program at any time by texting “STOP” to “MANDEE” (626333).

You may text “HELP” to “MANDEE” (626333) to receive more information regarding the Mandee mobile alerts program and/or your program subscription.

Message and data rates may apply. Please consult the applicable wireless service agreement for your individual pricing plan. Text messaging may not be available for all service providers or for all handsets. The carriers supported by this program are as follows: AT&T, Sprint, T-Mobile, Verizon Wireless, Alltel, U.S. Cellular, Boost, Nextel and Metro PCS. Cellular phone carriers’ instructions for text messaging may be different. Mandee makes no guarantee that any particular wireless service provider will participate. Mandee, in its sole discretion, may add or delete a cellular carrier at any time, without notice.

How Can I “Opt-out” of Receiving Mobile Text Communications? If you wish to opt out of receiving text messages, you can cancel the program at any time by either: Texting “STOP” to “MANDEE” (626333); or Contacting Client Services by email at custserv@mandee.com or by phone at 973-890-0021, ext. 2200.

You will need to provide your mobile phone number in order to be removed from the program. It may take up to 10 days to process your stop request. You may receive additional text messages from Mandee during this time.
