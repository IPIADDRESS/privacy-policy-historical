      Privacy Policy

      As a customer, affiliate or vendor of Manatron, any information gathered will not be sold, traded, exchanged or given to any individual, company or organization under any circumstance.

      Information Collection
      Manatron.com is the sole owner of the information collected on Manatron.com. Manatron.com collects information from its users at different points on its Web site.

      Cookies
      Manatron.com uses cookies.

      Log Files
      Like most standard Web site servers Manatron.com uses log files. This includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses, etc. are not linked to personally identifiable information.

      Clear Gifs (Web Beacons/Web Bugs)
      Manatron.com only uses clear GIFs for positioning and layout purposes. Manatron.com does not use them in any other manner related to tracking a visitor’s path through our site.

      Legal Disclaimer
      Though Manatron.com makes every effort to preserve user privacy, there may be a need to disclose personal information when required by law wherein Manatron.com has a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a current judicial proceeding, a court order or legal process served on this Web site.

      Business Transitions
      In the event Manatron experiences a business transition, such as a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, a user's personal information will, in most instances, be transferred with any business assets. Manatron.com will notify users by email and/or by posting a 30-day notice on this website before any transfer of personal information. If, as a result of the business transition, a user’s personally identifiable information will be used in a manner different from when the information was collected, the user will be given a choice about the information consistent with our Notification of Changes section.

      Notification of Changes
      If Manatron.com decides to change its privacy policy, Manatron.com will post those changes to this privacy statement, the homepage, and other places deemed appropriate so users are always aware of what information is collected, how it is used, and under what circumstances, if any, it is disclosed.

      If, however, Manatron.com is going to use users’ personally identifiable information in a manner different from that stated at the time of collection Manatron.com will notify users by posting a notice on this Web site for 30 days.

      Links
      This Web site contains links to other sites. Please be aware that Manatron.com is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. Manatron.com encourages its users to be aware when leaving our site to read the privacy statements of each and every Web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this Web site. See Manatron.com’s linking policy for more information.

      Contact Information
      If users have any questions or suggestions regarding our privacy policy, please contact us at webmaster@manatron.com.
