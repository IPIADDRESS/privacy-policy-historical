Privacy Policy

Networks Unlimited and associated divisions and subsidiaries thereof have produced this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our commitment to customer privacy.

We take the privacy of our customers and visitors to our site/s very seriously and will use our best efforts to protect any information provided to us.

We do not sell, trade or disclose information provided to any third-party. We use information solely for the purposes of providing a service to our customers either through direct request for further information to be provided by us or through customisation of our site and facilities to improve our overall service.

Most of the facilities on our site/s are available without having to provide any personal information. We collect and use information in several different ways:

Via email newsletter subscription forms
Information is used solely for the purposes of providing 'opted in' interest related newsletters. Unsubscribe facilities are provided by this service.

Via callback request forms
Information is used to ensure that, where possible, the most relevant person to your request contacts you.

Via registration forms
Information is used to provide the most efficient service we can, As an example, our on-line support form has enabled us to implement a better system for handling enquiries thereby speeding up the resolution process.
Information about our customers is an important part of our business and there will be times where we may find it necessary to communicate with you regarding your use of the services on our site.

In aggregate form (without identifying any user individually)
We may collect information such as IP address, browser type, platform, operating system of your computer. This is used to help us improve the site and the services we provide. It is also used for site administration and for profiling purposes. We do not generally use any of this information to personally identify you.

Use of Cookies on our Site
We do occasionally use cookies (a cookie is a small amount of information passed from the web server to the browser) on our site, for the purpose of tracking your browser session for order processing transactions and to help us provide improved navigational facilities. There is no executable code transferred, nor can it transfer any virus to your computer. You have the option to refuse to accept cookies but the site or certain functions may not operate fully if you so choose.
