Privacy Statement for Manage My Home

Purpose

This privacy policy explains what Manage My Home does with your information and what you can expect from Manage My Home. If you have additional questions please contact us at privacy@managemyhome.com.

Manage My Home reserves the right to update this policy from time to time, so please check it periodically for changes. By registering with managemyhome.com, you are accepting the practices of this policy.

Scope

This policy covers Manage My Home's treatment of personally identifiable information that is collected when you are on Manage My Home's site. This policy also covers Manage My Home's treatment of any personally identifiable information that is shared with Manage My Home by our business partners and Manage My Home's policy with respect to sharing personally identifiable information with our business partners.

This policy does not cover the actions of companies that Manage My Home does not control, or the actions of people not employed by Manage My Home.

Collection and Use of Personally Identifiable Information

Manage My Home collects personally identifiable information when you become a member, fill out your home inventory, and ask an expert. If your membership to Manage My Home is sponsored by one of our business partners, Manage My Home may also receive personally identifiable information from the partner.

Manage My Home uses this information to customize the maintenance reminders that are sent to you periodically and to better answer your questions when you use the "ask an expert" function. The information will also be used to fill your requests for specific information about products and services.

Manage My Home also monitors usage of the site by all subscribers to help us to improve the usability and content of the site.

Manage My Home is committed to protecting your privacy. Any information gathered about you or your home will not be sold, rented, traded or given to a third party without your prior consent. The only exception is a subscriber who is sponsored to this site. In this instance, the individual or business partner who sponsored you, may request your email address.

At any time, you can view your own information at Manage My Home, correct any inaccuracies, add or delete information as you see fit.

Security

Your personally identifiable information is password protected to prevent others from accessing your information. Our site also protects your security through the use of VeriSign's Web Server Identification, ensuring you have reached the correct site and allowing you to transmit sensitive information fully encrypted.

Unsubscribe

At any time, you can request that we stop sending you further information from Manage My Home by simply unsubscribing. When you unsubscribe from Manage My Home your information is deleted from our files.
