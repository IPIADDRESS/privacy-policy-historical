Privacy

At Elite Metal Tools we understand and respect the importance of maintaining your privacy and keeping your information secure. This Privacy Policy and Security Statement shares what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and how we keep your information secure. This Privacy Policy and Security Statement is part of our Terms of Use, which governs your use of elitemetaltools.com. By using elitemetaltools.com you agree to this Privacy Policy & Security Statement and Terms of Use.

What information do we gather?

When you order from elitemetaltools.com, we collect the following information: your name, telephone number, e-mail address, billing information (credit card number, expiration date and billing address), products purchased, any relevant promotion code and information (gift certificate redemption code, tradeshow code, etc…) and, if relevant, the recipient's name, address and telephone number. Information you enter is collected even if you cancel or do not complete an order.

Why do we collect information?

Elite Metal Tools strives to provide our customers with quality products and supplies. In addition to that, we strive to provide knowledgeable and friendly service. We gather information to process orders, to enhance and personalize our customers’ shopping experience and to inform our customers and e-mail subscribers of special offers and discounts.

How do we use the information?

We use the information you submit to process your orders (during this process your ship to information may be provided to appropriate manufacture for the purpose of shipping your purchased product; elitemetaltools.com does not guarantee the security of that information), to provide an enhanced and more personalized shopping experience and to inform you of offers and discounts from Elite Metal Tools.

Elite Metal Tools does not sell, trade or rent your e-mail address. We may, on occasion send offers to our customers on behalf of other trusted businesses, but we do not give those businesses your personal information.

In the event of a merger, consolidation, or sale or transfer of all or substantially all of the assets or business of Elite Metal Tools (a "Successor"), one of the assets which would generally be transferred is the information we collect from our customers and site visitors. However, the use of this information by any Successor would still be governed by the terms of this privacy policy, as amended from time to time, including any amendment after such transaction.

We may disclose any information you provide to Elite Metal Tools when we believe disclosure to be appropriate to comply with any law, regulation, governmental request, or to comply with judicial process; or (ii) if such disclosure is necessary or appropriate to operate this web site; or (iii) to protect the rights or property of Elite Metal Tools, its users, Sponsors, Providers, Licensors, or Merchants.
