This Privacy Policy applies to www.margaritaville.com and any affiliated website on which this policy is posted (collectively, this "Site"). By accessing, visiting or using the Site, you expressly consent to Margaritaville's collection, storage, use and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information You Voluntarily Provide To Margaritaville

If you open an account with us, purchase products or services, sign up for a mailing list, send us an email, or voluntarily share information with us in any other way, we receive and store the information you provide. You can choose not to voluntarily provide your information, but then you may Snot be able to use certain services or features offered through the Site.

Registration, Site Offerings and Other Communications: We may gather certain information voluntarily provided by you, such as your name, user name and password, address, phone number, email address, and other information about your background or personal interests, through various means, such as: (i) the Site's account registration process and mailing list service; (ii) portions of the Site that allow you to post comments and/or communicate with other users, sign up for promotional offers, download screensavers or other software, or use any other services we may offer from time to time (collectively referred to in this Privacy Policy as the "Site Offerings"); and (iii) through any communications you have with us. In addition, we may request information about other users through our "tell-a-friend" or other referral service, which is further explained below in Section 5.

We may also request similar information at our physical locations, including our restaurants, retail stores, and resort properties. However, this Privacy Policy applies only to information collected through the Site. Information about our customers that is collected at our physical locations may be subject to other terms, policies, or applicable local laws.

Online Orders: For the purpose of processing online orders, we may request contact information (including your name, address, email address, and phone number) and financial information (such as your credit card number).

2. Information That We Automatically Collect

Like many websites, we use automated tools or mechanisms to collect certain information pertaining to your use of the Site and online activities. Information that is automatically collected may be used to help us recognize you as a previous visitor to the Site, to serve relevant online advertising to you or to analyze trends and statistics (such as purchasing activities, the number of visits to certain webpages or websites, and similar data) to improve the services provided on our Site.

Use of Cookies: We may collect information about you and your use of this Site through the use of cookies. A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on your computer and that your browser provides to this Site each time you submit a query on this Site. Cookies collect information that may help us identify you (or your computer or network) and tell us how you accessed the Site and what parts were of interest to you. This information may include your Internet protocol address, referring website address, browser type and the times you accessed the Site. Internet protocol address is unique number that is assigned to each computer on the Internet. Referring website address is the domain name or URL of the webpage you were visiting immediately prior to when you accessed, visited or use the Site. Browser is the software application on your computer that lets you "see" websites (such as, Microsoft Internet Explorer, Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, Opera and Apple Safari).

IF YOU DO NOT WISH TO HAVE COOKIES PLACED ON YOUR COMPUTER, YOU CAN SET YOUR BROWSER TO REFUSE COOKIES BEFORE ACCESSING THE SITE. HOWEVER, DISABLING COOKIES MAY PREVENT OR CAUSE CERTAIN FEATURES OF THE SITE NOT TO FUNCTION PROPERLY. YOU MAY ALSO DELETE COOKIE FILES AFTER EACH VISIT DEPENDING ON THE TYPE OF BROWSER YOU USE, USUALLY BY GOING TO THE TOOLS MENU AND SELECTING "DELETE BROWSING HISTORY." SOME BROWSERS ALSO CONTAIN "DO NOT TRACK" FEATURES. FOR MORE INFORMATION, YOU SHOULD REVIEW THE SPECIFICATIONS OF YOUR BROWSER AND CONTACT YOUR SERVICE PROVIDER OR DEVICE MANUFACTURER.

Use of Web Beacons: We may place web beacons on pages of this Website and in emails we send to you. A web beacon, also sometimes called a pixel tag or transparent GIF, is an object that is embedded in a web page. It is usually invisible to you, but allows website operators to check whether you have viewed a particular web page or email communication.

If your information changes, or if you no longer desire to use our services, you may update or delete your profile by visiting the member information page on the Site or by emailing us at webmaster@margaritaville.com.
