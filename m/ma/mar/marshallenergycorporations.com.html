Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to Marshall Energy Corporations the owner of marshallenergycorporations.com (site).

To protect your privacy this statement explains our online information policy and tells you how your information is collected and used.

Please understand you may well have rights under your state’s laws. These laws may strengthen, or at least compliment, any privacy rights you have already have under Federal law. We comply with the privacy policies of all 50 states (as far as we know), but you should contact us to assert any state’s law

Protecting the privacy of children is critical. Marshall Energy Corporations never collects information from anyone under 18, assuming Marshall Energy Corporations has reason to believe she or he is under 18.

Your IP address used to access our website may be logged along with the dates and times of access. This information is used to analyze trends, track users’ movement, and garner demographic information for statistical assessments. However, we do not allow IP addresses to be linked to personally identifiable information.

Other information that may be garnered by Marshall Energy Corporations is the source that referred you to our website, he time you spend on our website, your destination when you leave our website, the type of operating system you are using, as well what web browser you are using.

You should assume that our website uses cookies; you are free to make adjustments in your web browser to disable these. Refusing some cookies may cripple some of our website features, however.

One illustration of limited access is where some content is protected by a username and password. Whether a username and password is generated by us or by you, they will be connected with other information related to you. The username and password will be tied to other account data. Any personal information you provide to others is wholly you decision. As an example, a blog post that you may consider “private” information is in fact “public” and we have no control over that.

Marshall Energy Corporations may collect personal and private information from you to conduct our business. If so, we will not share such information with other parties unless you authorized us to do so. There are instances where your information is shared with email service providers, but they are limited in how they use your information, and they cannot sell or transfer it to others in any way.

However, we may share information about our website visitors as a whole, not individually, with third parties for various purposes, in our sole discretion.

Illegal activity or other serious acts or allegations could create legal liability for our Marshall Energy Corporations. In those cases, we reserve the right to share your and we may be compelled to do so by law. On the other hand, there may be times when Marshall Energy Corporations needs to share your private information to protect our own interests. For instance, in cases of alleged copyright infringement or other intellectual property violations, it may be necessary to share personal information.

Google may use cookies to serve ads on marshallenergycorporations.com. The use of DART cookies by Google enables them to serve adverts to visitors that are based on their visits to this website as well as other websites on the internet. To opt out of the DART cookies, go to http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

Tracking of users through the DART cookie mechanisms are subject to Google’s privacy policies. Other Third Party ad servers or ad networks may also use cookies to track activities on this website to measure advertisement effectiveness and other reasons that will be provided in their own privacy policies.

We have included links on this website for your own use and Marshall Energy Corporations is not responsible for privacy policies on these websites. Their privacy policies may differ from our own.
