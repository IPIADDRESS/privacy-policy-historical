Cookies may also be used to serve relevant, targeted advertisements via third party web sites.

Marvin or third party advertisers may collect and use users' Internet Protocol or “IP” Addresses and other usage data to gather broad demographic information or for use in connection with targeted advertising. Marvin generally does not use IP addresses to personally identify a user.
