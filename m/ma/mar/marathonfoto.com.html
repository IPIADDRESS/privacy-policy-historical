MarathonFoto.com Privacy Notice

MarathonFoto.com knows that you care how information about you is used and shared, and we appreciate your trust that we will do so carefully and sensibly. This Privacy Statement describes how MarathonFoto.com gathers and disseminates information it obtains from you. By visiting MarathonFoto.com, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Notice.

What this Privacy Policy Covers

  • This Privacy Policy covers MarathonFoto's treatment of personally identifiable information that MarathonFoto.com collects when you are on the MarathonFoto.com site, and when you use MarathonFoto.com services. This policy also covers MarathonFoto.com treatment of any personally identifiable information that MarathonFoto.com business partners share with MarathonFoto.com and which MarathonFoto.com shares with its business partners.
  • This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that MarathonFoto.com does not own or control, or to people that MarathonFoto.com does not employ or manage. In addition, this policy does not apply to Chappell.com which has its own privacy policy.

What Personal Information About Customers Does MarathonFoto.com Gather?

MarathonFoto.com requires all visitors who wish to place an order to provide their name, e-mail address, zip code, and credit card information. MarathonFoto.com keeps this information strictly confidential and uses your name and contact information only for the purposes discussed below.

How does MarathonFoto.com use this Information?

  • MarathonFoto.com gives your name and address to the lab that will print your photographs and mail them to you. The lab uses this information solely for order fulfillment.
  • Credit card information is used to charge visitors for products purchased from MarathonFoto.com. MarathonFoto.com order pages use industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect data transmissions.
  • MarathonFoto.com uses your e-mail address to send you an order confirmation e-mail.
  • MarathonFoto.com uses your contact information to send you information about MarathonFoto.com from time to time. You can opt out of this mailing program by either calling customer service at 800-424-3686 or e-mailing them at mfservice@MarathonFoto.com and having your name removed from this list.
  • MarathonFoto.com, on occasion, may share your name, street address or e-mail address with affiliates in related industries such as sports gear and equipment, sports shoes, togs and clothing, training related information, nutritional information and supplements and sports related entertainment, travel and leisure activities. If you do not wish to have your personal information shared with these types of affiliates you may opt out by either calling customer service at 800-424-3686 or e-mailing them at mfservice@MarathonFoto.com and having your name removed from this list.
  • Additionally, MarathonFoto.com will send personally identifiable information about you to other companies or people when:
    • We need to send the information to companies who work on behalf of MarathonFoto.com to provide a product or service to you. Unless we tell you differently, these companies do not have any right to use the personally identifiable information we provide to them beyond what is necessary to assist us.
    • We respond to subpoenas, court orders or other legal processes.
    • We find that your actions on our web sites were intended to disrupt the normal operations of our web sites, alter site content, attempt to compromise security, or otherwise impede our normal business operations.

Your Ability to Correct and Restrict Your Account Information and Preferences

  • MarathonFoto.com provides the ability to correct your MarathonFoto.com Account Information and preferences, including whether you want MarathonFoto.com to contact you about specials, new products or to share your name with affiliates. If you wish to make a change to your personal information or do not want it used for any reason other than order fulfillment, you may opt out by either calling customer service at 800-424-3686 or e-mailing them at mfservice@MarathonFoto.com and having your name removed from this list.

Web Browser Cookie

The MarathonFoto.com website uses browser cookies for its order shopping cart. The browser cookies are not written to the user's computer disk and are deleted when the web browser closes. No personal information is stored in the web browser cookies and they are not used to track users outside of the MarathonFoto.com website.

IP Address

MarathonFoto.com also keeps your IP address confidential. It is not stored in the MarathonFoto.com database and the web server logs of MarathonFoto.com are cleared periodically.

Links to Other Sites

Our site contains links to other sites. If you jump to these other sites, MarathonFoto.com will not forward any information it has received from you to the next site. However, MarathonFoto.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

Security

  • MarathonFoto.com uses a firewall and password protected accounts to prevent the loss, misuse and alteration of information under our control. All machines are in a secured environment.
  • MarathonFoto.com order pages use industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect data transmissions.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

  • MarathonFoto.com may change these policies from time to time. If we make any substantial changes we will post changes here before they go into effect.

Questions or Suggestions

  • If you have questions or suggestions send an e-mail to mfservice@MarathonFoto.com
