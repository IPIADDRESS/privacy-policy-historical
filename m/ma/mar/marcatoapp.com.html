IP Address and Cookies

An Internet Protocol (IP) address can be used to help diagnose problems with a server, report aggregate information, determine the fastest route for your computer to use in connecting to a site, and administer and improve the site.
We use cookies to collect non-personal information that enables us to better understand and improve the usability, performance and effectiveness of our product. Cookies are unique text files sent to your browser and stored on your computer. Disabling the use of cookies may affect your ability to use some features of our product.
Marcato also collects general information about visits to our website. This information includes parameters such as the number of visits and browser details. No personal details are present in this data and it is utilized for the sole purpose of better understanding website usage to improve user experiences.

Contact Information/Financial Information

We will ask you for personal information when you sign up for any specific benefit or purpose that requires registration. Personal information that we collect may vary with each registration and may include one or more of the following categories: name, physical address, an e-mail address, and phone number. Financial information collected such as credit card information including credit card number and expiration date, and billing address is stored and only used to bill the user for products/services. This information will not be used in a manner different than the purpose for which it was originally collected, unless Marcato has first obtained your consent to do so.
You may, at any time, choose to correct or update the information that you have submitted to Marcato by updating your account on our website.

Using/Sharing Your Information

All information collected by Marcato is used for purposes of facilitating our services, providing support to users, improving our services, and administration. We will not share, sell, or rent your personal information to others. The following are exceptions to this general policy:
i.) Affiliates and Service Providers - third parties who need access to Marcato user information for the purpose of hosting our servers or archiving personal information for us are required by Marcato to maintain high level of security.
ii.) Legal Process/Enforcement - Marcato may disclose personal information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such disclosure is necessary to enforce the applicable Terms and Conditions or in conjunction with an investigation or legal action or proceeding.
iii.) Marcato may transfer part or all of your information in the case of a merger, acquisition, or bankruptcy.

Onward Transfer of Personal Information Outside Your Country of Residence

Any personal information that we may collect on this site, will be stored and processed on our servers located only in the United States and by using this site, you consent to the transfer.
