Privacy  

Marden-Kane, Inc. has created this statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website, www.mardenkane.com.

Visitors to our site have the option of giving us contact information, such as their name and email address. The contact information is used by us to provide information, via email and regular mail that we believe may be useful to our visitors when requested. Contact information is not shared, rented or sold to any third parties.

We do not set any cookies nor do we partner with or have special relationships with any ad server companies.

This site has security measures in place at its server hosting facility to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.

If you have provided us with contact information and do not want to receive e-mail from Marden-Kane in the future, please let us know by sending an e-mail to expert@mardenkane.com with "Remove from List" written in the subject line.

From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change at some time in the future we will contact you before we use your data for these new purposes to notify you of the policy change and to provide you with the ability to opt out of these new uses.

Upon request we offer visitors the ability to have inaccuracies corrected in contact information that the visitor has directed to the site by sending us an e-mail to expert@mardenkane.com with "Contact Correction" written in the subject line and the correct information written in the body of the email.
