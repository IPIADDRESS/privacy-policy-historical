Data protection is a matter of trust and your privacy is important to MarkaVIP. MarkaVIP shall therefore only use your name and other information which relates to you in the manner set out in this Privacy Policy. MarkaVIP will only collect information where it is necessary for MarkaVIP to do so and MarkaVIP will only collect information if it is relevant to its dealings with you.

MarkaVIP will only keep your information for as long as MarkaVIP is either required to by law or as is relevant for the purposes for which it was collected. You can visit the MarkaVIP website and use MarkaVIP’s mobile app (collectively called the “Platform”) and browse without having to register for an Account.

1. Data that MarkaVIP collects

Your personally identifiable data (first name, last name, email, billing information such as credit card number and billing address) is processed by MarkaVIP.

Your data is collected and processed to:

  • (a) Administer your Account;
  • (b) Process your Orders (including but not limited to delivery of the Products);
  • (c) Verify and carry out financial transactions in relation to payment you make online (including but not limited to payment verification, fraud protection and credit risk reduction);
  • (d) Provide you with the services and information offered through the Platform;
  • (e) Undertake direct marketing;
  • (f) Audit the downloading of data from the Platform;
  • (g) Improve the layout and/or content of the pages of the Platform and customize the Platform for users;
  • (h) identify visitors on the Platform;
  • (i) Carry out research on MarkaVIP’s users’ demographics; and/or
  • (j) Send you information which MarkaVIP deems you may find useful or which you have requested from MarkaVIP (including information about MarkaVIP’s Products, its newsletters, sales or promotions; information relating to other companies in MarkaVIP’s group or MarkaVIP’s business partners), provided you have indicated that you have not objected to being contacted for these purposes.

MarkaVIP will use your personal data only for the purposes indicated above.

MarkaVIP mainly does business in the Middle East and most data that it collects and processes is in those countries. By giving your data to MarkaVIP, you agree to it being transferred to its commercial partners, who may be located in a country outside the European Union ("EU") where other standards of data protection might apply, including to companies whose intervention as data processors, acting on behalf and under the control of MarkaVIP, is required to accomplish the purposes indicated above.

Subject to the above, MarkaVIP will not disclose, sell, rent or exchange such personal data to any other organization or entity, unless you are first notified and expressly agree, or as otherwise required by law. Payments that you make through the Platform will be processed by CyberSource (a Visa company)

Your actual order details may be stored on MarkaVIP’s server but for security reasons cannot be retrieved directly by MarkaVIP. However, you may access this information by logging into your Account on the Platform. There you can:

  • (a) View the details of your Orders that have been completed, those which are open and those which are shortly to be dispatched; and
  • (b) Administer your address details, bank details and any newsletter to which you may have subscribed.

MarkaVIP draws your attention to the fact that you have the right to access and restrict your data and, where appropriate, request rectification of any incorrect data relating to you.

5. Cookies

MarkaVIP also collects non-personally identifiable information passively using "cookies" and "action tags".

5.1 Cookies

"Cookies" are small text files that are placed on your computer in order to identify your web browser and the activities of your computer on the Platform and other websites. MarkaVIP may use Cookies to allow it to statistically monitor how you are using the Platform to help it improve its online offerings or to determine the popularity of certain content. MarkaVIP furthermore sends a "session cookie" to your computer when you log in to the Platform. These session cookies allow MarkaVIP to recognize if you visit multiple pages during the same browser session, without needing to re-enter your login information multiple times. Although most browsers are initially set to accept cookies, you may reset your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie or to reject cookies generally. Most browsers offer instructions on how to do so in the "Help" section of the toolbar. Please be aware that for technological reasons if you decline the use of cookies you will be unable to login to the Platform.

5.2 Action Tags

"Action tags" also known as web beacons or gif tags, are a web technology used to help track website usage information, such as how many times a specific page has been viewed. Action tags are invisible to you, and any portion of the MarkaVIP Platform, including advertisements, or email sent on MarkaVIP’s behalf, may contain action tags. MarkaVIP may select and use from time to time different third parties to track Platform usage through action tags on the Platform.

By using cookies and action tags together, MarkaVIP is able to gain valuable information to improve the MarkaVIP Platform and online offerings and measure the effectiveness of the MarkaVIP advertising and marketing campaigns.

5.3 What kind of information is stored in cookies?

The cookies used by MarkaVIP may be used to store any personal information. MarkaVIP’s cookies may be traced back to an individual user with extensive research. Once a cookie is activated, it is given an ID number, which is solely used for internal reference and may be used to identify the user and access personal information, such as your name or IP address. The information MarkaVIP receives from the cookies allows MarkaVIP to assess which pages of the Platform are visited the most and see which products were most popular etc.

5.4 Does MarkaVIP use third party cookies?

Cookies from specific business partners are allowed by MarkaVIP for the purpose of tailoring their adverts to your tastes and browsing habits, so cookies from some of MarkaVIP’s partners may be stored on your computer when you visit the Platform. These cookies are temporary/permanent cookies, which will be automatically deleted from your computer at the end of their lifespan. (The data stored is collected at certain times, allowing these companies to see which content has been viewed most frequently. Third party cookies have the sole purpose of allowing MarkaVIP’s partners to advertise products that may interest you.

6. Log information

6.1 General

Every time you visit MarkaVIP, information is sent from your browser to MarkaVIP’s server log files, where it will be saved. The information transferred includes: time and date of visit to the Platform, which pages were viewed, IP address, URL of site that referred you to MarkaVIP, how much information has been stored and which browser you have been using.

The information stored in this log file is then used to improve the shopping experience on the Platform by making MarkaVIP’s navigation more user-friendly, finding errors quickly and directing MarkaVIP’s server’s capacity to where it is needed most. For example, this information allows us to work out MarkaVIP’s busiest times and plan enough server power for those times to keep MarkaVIP’s site running as quickly as possible.

By using the Platform, I am giving MarkaVIP permission to analyze and use protocol data to develop and optimize their online shop.
