Cookies

A cookie is a little set of data, frequently including a unique anonymous identifier, sent to a computer browser of a given website and is stored on the hard drive of your computer.

Cookies are required to use Mailee's service.

We use cookies to record information from the current session, but we do not use permanent cookies. It is necessary to login on Mailee's site after a certain amount of time to protect you against accidental access from others to the content of your account.
