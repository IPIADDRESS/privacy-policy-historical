Maidenform Privacy Practices
At Maidenform.com, your privacy is very important to us. We recognize that as an online customer, you may have concerns about submitting your personal and financial information via the Internet. Therefore, Maidenform, its staff, financial institution, and contractors have taken a proactive role in creating and adhering to the following information gathering and dissemination practices for Maidenform.com.

On this web site we collect information volunteered by the consumer (you) such as:
  • Name, address, phone number, e-mail and other contact information.
  • Information necessary to both complete and verify purchases, including: credit-card numbers and billing addresses.
  • Standard demographic data, such as interest areas, age-range, gender, and shopping habits.
This information is used to fulfill your orders, offer products targeted to your interests, perform customer service and to statistically analyze site usage.

We do not share your information with anyone except our marketing and fulfillment affiliates and contractors who have agreed to be bound by the same principals of confidentiality, or as otherwise required by law.

Occasionally we may use your email or mailing address to contact you about administrative matters or relevant updates to Maidenform.com. If you wish not to receive online promotional information from Maidenform.com, click here.

In order to provide our site visitors with an enhanced experience that is optimized to their preferences, we may also track aggregate navigational information about our site visitors. This includes information such as how many people visit which areas of our web site and for what reasons.

Maidenform.com uses WebTrends Live to analyze website traffic. WebTrends Live does not create individual profiles for visitors. WebTrends Live only collects aggregate data. You should refer to the Webtrends Live Privacy Policy to learn how they collect and use information. You can find WebTrends Live's privacy policy here: http://www.webtrends.com/privacy_policy.htm
