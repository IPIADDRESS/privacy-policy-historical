Every Premium account holder is provided with a Preferences section on the mailVU.com web site. It is the responsibility of each user to ensure the information is correct and current. Users are responsible for the accuracy of their account Preferences. You may update or correct these selections as any time by logging into your account and making the changes on the Preferences web page. You may also leave the optional information fields blank.

The user has the option to opt-out of mailVU.com notifications. This option does not apply to communications primarily for the purpose of administering order completion, support, software warnings and updates, or other administrative and transactional notices as the primary purpose of these communications is not promotional in nature.

Access to and Accuracy of Your Information

The mailVU.com site is accessible 24x7 every day of the year unless down for system maintenance. To view and modify your personal information you may edit the selections in the Preferences page of the web site.

Keeping Your Personal information Secure

A2Stream takes seriously the trust you place in us. We will keep your private data secure consistent with current industry standards. To prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, to maintain data accuracy, and to ensure the appropriate use of the information, A2Stream utilizes appropriate physical, technical, and administrative procedures to safeguard the information we collect.

Personal information, once transmitted to us, will be stored on computers that only selected personnel and contractors have access to via password. These computer systems are located in controlled facilities which have limited access. Your mail messages are limited-access, where access is gained through an obscured URL system. This obscured URL makes it difficult if not impossible for a third party to guess the URL address of your content without an invitation from you including the relevant URL. However, unless the videos and content are password protected, your invitees may forward the URL link to other parties and a possibility exists that an unauthorized third party could discover your videos and/or content.
