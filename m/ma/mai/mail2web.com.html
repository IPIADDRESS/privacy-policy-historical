Privacy Policy
Version 1.3 – November 2005 		mail2web.com Terms and Conditions

 

Purpose.This Privacy Policy (the “Policy”) governs all aspects of how mail2web.com (“We”, “Us” and “Our”) collects, uses, maintains and discloses personal information from all users ("Users", “You” and “Your”) of this website (“Site”) and all Services (“Services”) offered by mail2web.com. The privacy of Users is very important to Us and We are committed to protecting the confidentiality of all information entrusted to Us by You. mail2web.com complies with the requirements of the Canadian Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (“PIPEDA”). Any Services that require any form of payment (one-time or reoccurring) and the MediaBlog are intended for people 18 years of age and over. mail2web.com does not knowingly collect or use any Personal Information from anyone under the age of 18.

mail2web.com is not an e-mail service, but an e-mail application. Registration is not required, but is available to Users interested in customizing the application making it easier for regular Users. We may store e-mail messages, attachments, passwords and session-based cookies temporarily while You are using the mail2web.com application. After You have logged off from the application or the session has timed out, Your email address and your password is removed from our system and the session-based cookie on Your system is deleted automatically. Any remaining email messages and/or attachments that may reside on the server will be removed automatically within one hour. Customers that prefer not to use session-based cookies when using the Anonymous or Customized version of mail2web.com can manually disable session-based cookies in the web-browser they are using, i.e. Microsoft® Internet Explorer, Firefox, etc… Please refer to the instructions contained in your specific browser for more information. All activities that You conduct through the Site are considered private and are not recorded in a personally identifiable way. The data collected for the use of the mail2web.com application is anonymous and is used for statistical purposes only. Click here to learn how mail2web.com works or click here for general help.

Personal Information that mail2web.com Anonymous Services collects. mail2web.com does not collect any personal information unless You choose to use customized services or subscribe to any Premium Services (services that require any one-time or re-occurring payment).

Personal Information that mail2web.com Customized Services collects. The customized version of mail2web.com requires storing Your email address (“Personal Information”) to function, should You wish to provide it. However Your password associated with any email address stored is not collected at anytime. Customized users of mail2web.com are provided with a unique username that is used to access their customized account, and will also select a password specific to this customized account that should be different from any of their email account passwords. This password is maintained by mail2web.com exclusively for the purpose of enabling customized users to manage their mail2web.com account and to add and/or delete services associated with their account. We may also collect demographic data regarding Your income level, occupation, gender and information about the industry in which You work (the “Ancillary Personal Information”) if You choose to use the customized version of mail2web. mail2web may also collect Your name and email address should You attempt to contact us by way of Our web-based feedback and/or support form hosted on the mail2web.com Site, or if You contact us by email for any other reason. Users of the mediablog service have the option to provide additional personal information (“Profile Information”). The Profile Information can include a variety of personal information, including name, email address, messenger account, picture, gender, physical location, interests and other personal information. By default, mail2web.com will not make the Profile Information accessible to the public. However, Users have the option to make some or all of their Profile Information included in an online directory of Users that is accessible and searchable by the public. By indicating that you would like for some or all of the Profile Information to be disclosed, Users are consenting to its inclusion in the online directory. Please be aware that mail2web.com does not generally monitor or review the information in a User’s Profile Information and the information that you choose to provide can be publicly accessible as described above. Please use caution when deciding on what information to include in your Profile Information and what information to make public. mail2web.com will maintain your Profile Information on our servers until such time that you delete, modify or the account is terminated by mail2web.com for being considered inactive, whichever occurs first.

mail2web.com, as part of its mediablog service provided to Users, may collect personally identifiable information (“MediaBlog Personal Information”) from mediablog Users. This includes name, address, and email address, that You provide, and any correspondence that You have with mail2web.com that occurs by way of email, web-based email forms, telephone and/or support tickets that are generated for the purpose of communicating and/or resolving any issues related to the mediablog. The mediablog is defined as a public personal space that permits You to upload images, audio, video and other media files, post/read blog entries, link to friends and allows viwers to subscribe to an email service enabling them to receive updates automatically as the Content changes. All mediablog Users will be provided with a unique URL that is used to access their account, however their existing Customized Service username and password will also be used to administrate this service. This account and password is maintained by mail2web.com exclusively for the purpose of providing Customized Services, including the mediablog service and is used to manage all mail2web.com services through the mail2web.com web-based Control Panel. mail2web.com also collects additional usage information (“Additional mediablog Usage Information”) that is collected electronically regarding how mediablog Users use our website (e.g. by tracking the number of unique page views received by the pages of the mediablog, or the domains from which Users originate) or via cookies that can be used to identify the User should they visit the mediablog again. Additional mediablog Usage Information collected may include other information about Your computer, such as your IP address (a number assigned to Your computer); the type of Internet browser you are using; the type of computer operating system you are using; or the domain name of the website from which You linked to our mediablog. This Additional mediablog Usage Information will be anonymized and will not be linked to Your mediablog Personal Information or any Personal Information collected and will only be collected in the aggregate in connection with all the Users of our mediablog for planning, forecasting and/or evaluation purposes. As such, please note that this Policy does not cover aggregated data from which the identify of an individual cannot be determined. mail2web.com retains the right to use anonymous aggregated data in any way that it determines appropriate.

Access to Personal Information. mail2web.com provides individuals with access to Personal Information held about them and complies with the individual access principal of Schedule 1, Clause 4.9 of PIPEDA.

Pursuant to Schedule 1, Clause 4.9.5 of PIPEDA, if You believe that Your Personal Information on file is not correct, You may request an update of that information by sending a request to the person indicated below. Alternatively, Customized and Premium Service Users can update Personal Information through the web-based Control Panel at anytime. mail2web.com reserves the right not to change any Personal Information but will append any alternative text You believe appropriate.

Pursuant to Schedule 1, Clause 4.9.5 of PIPEDA, You may also request that mail2web.com delete Your Personal Information from mail2web.com’s system and records . However, due to technical constraints and the fact that mail2web.com backs up its systems, Personal Information may continue to reside in mail2web.com’s systems after deletion for some time. Pursuant to the principal in Schedule 1, Clause 4.9.4 of PIPEDA, where information will not or cannot be disclosed, the individual making the request will be provided with the reasons for non-disclosure. Where information will be disclosed, mail2web.com will endeavour to provide the information in question within a reasonable time and no later than thirty (30) working days following the request.

mail2web.com will not respond to repetitious or vexatious requests for access. In determining whether a request is repetitious or vexatious, mail2web.com will consider such factors as the frequency with which information is updated, the purpose for which the information is used, and the nature of the information.

To guard against fraudulent requests for access, mail2web.com will require sufficient information to allow it to confirm the identity of the person making the request before granting access or making corrections.
