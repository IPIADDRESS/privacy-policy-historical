Privacy Policy

Last Updated on May 14, 2010

At Main Street Hub, we value your privacy. We do not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

We collect the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail, aggregate information on what pages users access or visit, and information volunteered by the users (such as survey information and/or site registrations). The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web pages and the quality of our service, and it is not shared with or sold to other organizations or commercial pages, except to provide products or services you’ve requested, when we have your permission, or if it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of Terms of Service, or as otherwise required by law.

Information Gathering and Usage

When you register for Main Street Hub, we ask for information such as your name, company name, email address, billing address, site registrations, and credit card information. We also gather information from third-parties that you allow us access to.

Main Street Hub uses collected information for the following general purposes: products and services provision, billing, identification and authentication, services improvement, contact, and research. Main Street Hub aggregates and makes anonymous certain collected information for the provision of products and services to our users.

Cookies

A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a website’s computers and stored on your computer’s hard drive.

Cookies are required to use the Main Street Hub service.

Changes

We may modify this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, we will notify you by making the revised version available here and will indicate at the top of the Privacy Policy the date that revisions were last made.
