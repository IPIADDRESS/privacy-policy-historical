Privacy Policy

Privacy

Mamamia recognises that your privacy is very important to you. Providing your personal information is an act of trust that we take very seriously. Mamamia will not sell, trade, give or rent any information obtained from you to a third party.

Collection

Mamamia collects the personal data that you volunteer when registering on our website, or through comment forms, or by email. This information will only be collected by lawful and fair means. We also allow you to provide additional non-required information on forums, registration and at other times. Only information marked, as ‘required’ during registration is needed to use services on the Website. This information is used for identification purposes and to contact you if necessary.

Use and disclosure

Mamamia.com.au will not rent, sell or exchange information about you to third parties, except where we are legally obliged to disclose such information.

From time to time we will bring you offers, competitions and deals from people who partner with us here at Mamamia that we feel will benefit our readership.

Mamamia will be honest and open with you about the type of personal information that is collected about you and the actual use of any such information. By giving out your personal information, or filling in an entry or application form, you have consented to the use of this information in future.

Further information

Can be found at the Government Privacy web site: www.privacy.gov.au
