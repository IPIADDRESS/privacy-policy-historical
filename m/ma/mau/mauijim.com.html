In particular, we collect your information in the following ways:

Browsing: If you visit this site but do not buy or transact with the Company in any way, the website will set cookies on your browser and will collect browsing information.

Cookies: A "cookie" is a small amount of information stored on your hard drive which was sent to your Internet browser from a Web server. Cookies collect information on our behalf that will help us better understand how you navigate around the website and how we can target our products and services more effectively to suit your personal interests. In most instances, you are able to set your Internet browser to not accept cookies, or you can opt to be notified when a cookie is sent to your computer and then decide whether or not to accept it.

Creating an Account or Logging In: You may choose to log into the site. If you do not have an account, you may create an account without placing an order. If you do create an account, you will be given the option to “opt in” to our email system: Email Opt In: If you select this option, indicating that you are interested in getting updates from the Company, you will submit your email information and be added to our distribution list. We do not share the distribution list with other companies. You will then receive multiple emails, unless and until you decide to opt-out from any of the messages you have received.

Shopping (whether as a guest or as a registered account-holder): You must enter an email address to shop on our website. Your email will be stored and used to track your orders, along with billing, shipping, and order information. Your credit card information is tokenized and processed as described below.

Submitting Repair Requests: You can submit a repair request online. If you choose to do so, your personal information relevant to the request will be required. If you select this option, you will be routed to the Email Opt In process described above.

Securing Your Information & Credit Card Details

At mauijim.com, we utilize Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology to protect the security of your credit card information. SSL encrypts your order information to avoid the decoding of that information by anyone other than mauijim.com. To make sure you are accessing our secure server before you submit your credit card information, please check your browser to see either an unbroken key or a closed lock (depending upon your browser), then SSL is active. To double-check for security, look at the URL or Location line of your browser during the order process. If you have accessed a secure server, the first characters of the address will change from "http" to "https". Some browser versions and some firewalls don't permit communication through secure servers like the ones we use to process orders at mauijim.com. If for any reason you cannot access the secure server, please feel free to place your order with us by phone at 1.888.666.5905.
