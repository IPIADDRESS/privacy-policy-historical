Maui Jim USA, INC. Privacy Policy

Maui Jim USA, Inc. values its customers and is committed to respecting their privacy. The purpose of our company's Privacy Policy is to inform you of the type of information that we may collect from you when you choose to browse our Web site (www.mauijim.com) and how we might use that information, as well as the choices you have regarding the collection of your information.

 

As a part of our commitment to your privacy, Maui Jim USA, Inc. supports and adheres to the guidelines and practices adopted by the Direct Marketing Association's "Privacy Promise to American Consumers". We have agreed to: (1) provide customers with the notice of their ability to opt out of information rental, sale, or exchange with other marketers; (2) honor customers' requests not to share their information with other marketers; and (3) honor customers' requests not to receive mail, telephone, or other solicitations from Maui Jim USA, Inc.

 

Collecting Information About You

In general, you can visit many of our web pages without telling us who you are or revealing any personal information about yourself. We may track the Internet domain address from which people visit us and analyze this data for trends and statistics, but individual users will remain anonymous, unless you voluntarily tell us who you are.

 

On certain occasions, such as when you purchase a product from the Web site, register with us to receive catalogs, enter a promotional contest or take a similar action, we will ask you to provide personal information about yourself. This information is included, but not limited to your full name, home address and/or shipping address, phone number, E-mail address and credit card information.

 

Using This Information?

Please be assured of the caution we use regarding the personal information you provide during the checkout process. This information is used to process your order and email you a confirmation of the order plus the shipping information. In addition, other internal uses for personal information received through our Web site may arise, which may include analyzing Web viewing trends and informing you of our new products to help enhance your shopping experience with Maui Jim USA, Inc.

 

Cookies

The Maui Jim USA, Inc. website uses a common form of data called "cookies", which are small amounts of information stored on your hard drive which were sent to your Internet browser from a Web server. Cookies collect information on our behalf that will help us better understand how you navigate around the Maui Jim USA, Inc. Web site and how we can target our products and services more effectively to suit your personal interests. In most instances, you are able to set your Internet browser to not accept cookies, or you can opt to be notified when a cookie is sent to your computer and then decide whether or not to accept it.

Your Information Is Kept Secure

At mauijim.com, we utilize Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology to protect the security of your credit card information. SSL encrypts your order information to avoid the decoding of that information by anyone other than mauijim.com. To make sure you are accessing our secure server before you submit your credit card information, please take a look at the lower right-hand corner of your browser. If you see either an unbroken key or a closed lock (depending upon your browser), then SSL is active. To double-check for security, look at the URL or Location line of your browser. If you have accessed a secure server, the first characters of the address will change from "http" to "https". Some browser versions and some firewalls don't permit communication through secure servers like the ones we use to process orders at mauijim.com. If for any reason you cannot access the secure server, please feel free to place your order with us by phone at 1.888.666.5905.


Protecting Children

Maui Jim USA, Inc. takes special care to protect the safety and privacy of children. Our web site is a general audience site. We do not permit anyone under the age of 13 to register with us. We also do not send email correspondence to anyone who tells us that they are under the age of 13 without their parent's consent.


Children under the age of 13 should always ask their parents or guardians for permission before providing any personal information to anyone online. We urge parents and guardians to participate in their children's online activities and use parental control or other web filtering technology to supervise children's access to the web.


Policy Changes

If our information practices change in the future, we will notify you by posting the policy changes on our Web site and/or by communicating directly with you. We will notify you of any such change before we use your information in a manner not previously disclosed in our privacy policy. You will have the opportunity at that time to opt out of any new use of your information.


Third Party Websites

Our company website, www.mauijim.com, may contain links to other sites that are not under our control. These Web sites have their own policies regarding privacy, which we recommend that you read and review before visiting them. We provide these linked Web sites only for the convenience of our visitors and are not responsible for these sites. In accordance with our Privacy Policy, Maui Jim USA, Inc. will not sell, trade or rent any personal information to third party Web sites or companies.
