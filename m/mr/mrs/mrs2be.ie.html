Mrs2be.ie and Mum2be.ie Privacy Policy

29 September 2011

Our Commitment to User Privacy

At Mrs2be.ie and Mum2be.ie (the sites), we take user privacy protection very seriously. Our privacy policy was developed as part of our commitment to deliver a quality service while maintaining integrity in all our dealings with our users. It sets out how we collect, use and protect any personal information you give us when you use our sites, including when you use our mobile apps.

Sharing information with advertisers or other third parties

We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. Unless required by law we will not use or disclose your personal information for any purpose other than that for which it was obtained and in accordance with this policy. This includes names and e-mail addresses.

If you have given us permission to do so, we may contact you from time to time with information on newly added features, news, expert tips and relevant wedding professional special offers. You may opt out of any future e-mails by using the unsubscribe link provided in each email.

Your forum posts

Please remember that anything you post in the site discussion forums is public and is visible to anyone visiting those website pages. You may wish to use a pseudonym rather than your real name when creating your username.

We may aggregate data in an anonymous manner for disclosure to advertisers or business associates for promotion and marketing purposes. In such an event we will ensure that no such data could be used to personally identify you.

Outside providers and embedded objects

From time to time we may partner with an external provider or an advertiser to provide other services, for example we may ask an external company to carry out a survey or to run a competition. If any personal information is required you will be advised beforehand.

Viewing and controlling your personal information

You can see, review and change your personal information by logging on to the sites at any time. Alternatively contact us at office@mrs2be.ie. Please note that to protect the privacy of your account we may require further information to verify your identity before revealing any personal information to you.

Changes to this privacy policy

We may make changes to this privacy policy from time to time. When we do we will notify registered users by email.

Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorised access or disclosure we have put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online. Unfortunately, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. As a result, while we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us or from our online products or services, and you do so at your own risk. Once we receive your transmission, we make our best effort to ensure its security on our systems.

Passwords

Your password is ciphered (a one-way hash) so that it is secure and so that we cannot tell what it is. However, it is recommended that you do not reuse the same password across a number of different websites. Your password is the means of accessing your account, so please guard it carefully and under no circumstances will anyone affiliated with us or any third party legitimately ask you for your password. Should you forget your password for your account, you can use the I forgot my password feature provided by the software. This process will ask you to submit your user name and your email, and then the software will generate a new password to reclaim your account and email it to you.

Cookies

In order to provide you with the full functionality of the site it is necessary to place cookies on your computer to enable you to post messages and to keep track of what pages you have visited. You may choose to modify your browser preferences to reject cookies in this case, however, you may find yourself unable to access certain features of the site which are limited to registered users. Unless you choose otherwise, we will use only temporary session cookies (which are deleted at the end of each session on the site) and not persistent cookies. For more information on cookies and how to delete or control them see http://www.aboutcookies.org.
