Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to User Privacy
At Mrs2be.ie, we take user privacy protection very seriously. Our privacy policy was developed as part of our commitment to deliver a quality service while maintaining integrity in all our dealings with our users.

This privacy statement applies to the site of www.Mrs2be.ie.

Forms
Mrs2be.ie discussion forum registration and website contact forms require users to provide us with contact information, such as names and email address. The user contact information is also used to get in touch with the customer when necessary. We do not share names or e-mail addresses outside of Mrs2be.ie.

Access to Your Information
Mrs2be.ie is the sole entity with access to any information collected during registration.

Your Information In Relation to Others We Link To
You will be able to access other websites through the Mrs2be.ie website. When doing so, you are subject to their policies regarding privacy and data collection and you should read those sites’ privacy policies to make sure you agree to them before using such sites. If you choose to enter a contract at any of these sites, you should read their privacy policies to make sure you agree to them before entering any such contract.

Sharing Information with Advertisers or Other Third Parties
We do not share any personal information with any parties outside Mrs2be.ie, including names and e-mail addresses.

We will contact you with information on newly added features, news, expert tips and relevant wedding professional special offers. All correspondence will be sent through Mrs2be.ie. You may opt out of any future Mrs2be.ie e-mails by using the unsubscribe link provided in each email.

Use Of Information
Information posted by users on the Mrs2be.ie discussion forums is public and visible to anyone visiting those website pages.

Mrs2be.ie may contain images, or other embedded objects from other web sites. These other sites maintain their own policies regarding cookies and the collection and use of personal information. It is the responsibility of the user to review those policies for themselves.

Mrs2be.ie maintains website information about the number of visits a page receives and technical information used to identify trends but this information is not recorded in such a way that it can be cross referenced to any specific site visitor.

Changes to this Policy
Mrs2be.ie reserves the right to change our Privacy Policy at any time. All changes will be posted on the website and will be effective immediately.

Queries or Concerns
Mrs2be.ie is owned and operated by Online Bridal Directory Limited. If you have any queries, or concerns with the privacy of your information collected by Mrs2be.ie, please Contact Us.

This privacy policy also covers use of other related products offered by Online Bridal Directory Ltd.
