The Michigan Virtual University has created this privacy statement to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site: http://www.mivu.org.

Our site's self-selected Contact Us Web form asks users to give us contact information (such as name, mailing address, and/or email address). We use the information from the Contact Us form to send the user requested information or updates about MVU and its projects.

This site contains links to other sites that are not under our control with regard to privacy policies or content. The Michigan Virtual University as represented by this site [http://www.mivu.org] is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such connected Web sites.

Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Personal information is stored in a restricted access, secured database, accessible only to MVU staff. Personal information will not be shared, sold, or distributed in any manner except to support your request for information through the Contact Us form.
