Our Commitment To Your Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our homepage and at every point where personally identifiable information may be requested.

Our Commitment To Data Security

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

Our Commitment To Children’s Privacy

Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 18, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under 18. Under our Terms of Service, children under 18 are no allowed to access our service.

Collection of Personal Information

On visiting this site, the IP address used to access the site will be logged along with the dates and times of access. This information is purely used to analyse trends, administer the site, track user’s movement, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. Importantly, IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

Links to third party websites

We have included links on this site for your reference. We are not responsible for the privacy policies on these websites.

About Me:

Hey, Mikeman here,


Here's a little background on me:


I actually got started in the whole money making business many, many years ago. I started off back around 2002 network marketing with Quixtar, I never really went anywhere with that and soon after I tried my hand at High Yield Investment Programs and Autosurfs back in 2004-2005. That was quite a learning lesson, I made thousands, then lost thousands, basically it's no better than gambling, every program was a Ponzi.


I began reading about IM here and there after that, but never took any action, I read up on the info as something cool to know, but never had the burning desire to put it in action. Since mid June of 2008 however, I was sick of working as a Quality Assurance Analyst / Business Analyst and finally began taking serious action to make a successful business out of it.


I've now setup this blog to chronicle my journey to where I finally reach that full-time income online. Hope you enjoy the updates and get a lot of useful nuggets out of reading along!


-Mike
