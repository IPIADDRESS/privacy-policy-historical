Privacy statement

Data

Mind will only collect, process and store information we have openly collected from you or with your consent, in accordance with data protection laws. Your details will be held only by Mind and will not be made available to any third party, except where otherwise stated. 

You can access and update information supplied by you when registering in the online shop by logging in with your user name and password. Mind's online shop and donation process is secured using a Thawte Digital Certificate. This ensures that the information you send to us via the internet will be encrypted.

Email

If you send information to Mind via email, please be aware that that Mind cannot guarantee its security during transit. Once received, email information will be stored according to data protection law.
