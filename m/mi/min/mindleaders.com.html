Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

MindLeaders and third parties use a browser feature known as a cookie which assigns a unique identification to your computer. Cookies are typically stored on your computer's hard drive and are used by MindLeaders to help track clicks as you move through pages within the MindLeaders website. Cookies may also be used to determine browser plug-ins so that we may optimize your experience on our site. With most Internet browsers, you can erase Cookies from your computer hard drive, block all Cookies or receive a warning before a Cookie is stored. Please refer to your browser instructions or help screen to learn more about these functions. MindLeaders' cookies are linked to personally identifiable information.

We use a third-party tracking service that uses other tracking technologies such as web beacons, scripts, and images to track non-personal information about visitors to our site in the aggregate.

MindLeaders Facebook Application: Facebook Connect

If you are accessing MindLeaders through Facebook request for permission, you are authorizing us to store, collect, and use in accordance with this Privacy Policy any and all information that you agreed Facebook could provide to us through this action. The agreement takes place when you “accept” the application on Facebook.

The purpose of the application is to enable you to purchase and complete a training course, which has been developed in cooperation with Facebook. This training is embedded within an i-Frame on Facebook.

Personal Information such as name, email address, and credit card number are collected within the application is collected directly by MindLeaders. We share this information with third party service providers to provide payment services.

We do share information back with Facebook to perform this service such as:

(a) We will conduct a satisfaction survey of all users and these results will be shared with Facebook. For example, our survey will ask, “how satisfied are you with the training?” “how likely are you to recommend the training?” and “what suggestions do you have to improve the course?” These survey results will be shared with FB aggregated on country basis.

(b) We will provide basic user data for all users (e.g., email, attendance, completion status, price paid for training) to Facebook for internal use by Facebook. This data may be used by Facebook for its own internal purposes.

(c) We will ask you if you would like to post to your wall the fact that you have just completed the Facebook Trainer for Business course. By consenting to this request, you authorize MindLeaders to post and display your training completion status on your Facebook wall.

All of this information is shared to perform the function of taking the training course.
