Privacy

We are happy about your interest in our homepage and our enterprise. However we cannot accept any liability for links to third party content despite our careful control.

It is of primary concern for us to protect your personal data upon your visit to our website. Your data is protected according to the mandatory legal provisions. In the following you will find information, which data will be collected during your visit to our homepage and how it will be used:

1. Inquiry and handling of data

Every visit to a homepage and every query of a file located on the homepage is recorded. Saving this information is relevant for internal system related and statistic reasons. We record the name of the requested file, the date and time of the query, the amount of transmitted data, amount of successfully transmitted data, notification of successful query, web browser and the applying domain.

In addition we record the IP address of the enquiring computer.

  • Cookies

We use so called cookies in different locations of the web pages. They help make our pages more user-friendly, effective and safe. Cookies are small text files which will be saved on your computer and by your browser. Most of our cookies are so called “session cookies”. They will automatically be deleted after your visit. Cookies do not damage your computer and do not contain viruses.

  • Newsletter

If you would like to receive the newsletter offered on our website, we need your email address as well as information that will allow us to validate you are the owner of this email address or to make sure it is in the owner of said address’ consent to receive the newsletter. We do not collect further data. You may at any time revoke your consent to letting us record the information, the email address and to use it in order to send out the newsletter.

Further information about you will only be recorded, should you provide this information voluntarily, e.g. with a request or by registering.

2. Use and disclosure of personal data

Should you have provided us with personal data; it will be exclusively used for answering your inquiries, settling concluded contracts and for technical administration.

Your personal data will only be shared with third parties if this is necessary for the purposes of fulfilling a contract, this especially regards passing on information of your order to a distributor, billing purposes or after we have been given your prior consent. You are entitled to cancel a consent given any time with effect for the future.

The stored personal data will be deleted if you withdraw your consent to storage, if its acquirement to fulfill the intended purpose of the saving is no longer necessary or if its storage for other legal reasons is inadmissible.

3. Google Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google Inc. ("Google"). Google applies Cookies. The information generated by these cookies, about the users’ usage of the website, is transmitted and stored by Google on servers in the United States.

Google will use this information for the purpose of evaluating the users’ behavior on the website, compiling reports on website activity for the website operators and providing other services relating to website activity and internet usage. This Data can be utilized to create user profiles under a pseudonym.

We only use Google Analytics with an active IP anonymization. Therefore Google will truncate/anonymize the last octet of the IP address for Member States of the European Union as well as for other parties to the Agreement on the European Economic Area. Only in exceptional cases, the full IP address is sent to and shortened by Google servers in the USA. Google will not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google.

Visitors can refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate setting in the browser; furthermore users have the option to prevent Google’s collection and use of data by downloading and installing the browser plug-in available at: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=en-GB. Further information on the use of data by Google and other privacy policies can be found on Google websites: https://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/partners/ (“How Google uses data when you use our partner’s sites or apps“), www.google.com/policies/technologies/ads (“Advertising“), www.google.com/settings/ads (“Control the information Google uses to show you ads”).

Users can prohibit Google Analytics of collecting any information by selecting the following link. A Opt-Out-Cookie will then be activated, which prevents the collection of your data in future visits:

Deactivate Google Analytics

Further terms and conditions can be found at http://www.google.com/analytics/terms/de.html or https://www.google.de/intl/de/policies/. We must point out that the code „anonymizelp“ was added to Google Analytics on this website to ensure successful IP-masking. The previous information about Google Analytics is based on a template designed by Dr. Thomas Schwenke, seen on datenschutzbeauftragter-info.de.

4. The use of SALESmanago for web analytics and e-mail services

This website uses technologies of the Benhauer LTD (https://www.salesmanago.de) to collect and store data in order to generate user profiles under a pseudonym. These profiles are used to better understand how visitors use our site, and to help manage, maintain, and report the traffic on our website.  Therefore we may deploy cookies. 

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer by websites that you visit. They are widely used in order to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site, for example keeping track of online patterns and preferences or identifying return visitors. No personal information is stored in the cookie. The value stored in the cookie is an anonymous identifier, which is not linked to any other personal information you may have given us during your visit.

Additionally, only after agreeing to the terms and conditions, personal data can be used in E-Mail correspondence for customer relationship management or advertising purposes.

You can object to the storage of this data at any time, by sending an e-mail to germany(at)salesmanago.com
