MindSumo Privacy Policy
Last Updated: April 17, 2014

Key Terms:

  • Your Information - The personal information of MindSumo users that is collected in the registration process, e.g., name, address, telephone number, email address, academic history, professional history, and resume.
  • User - Registered MindSumo users who submit solutions to Challenges, communicate with Mentors, or engage with other features that are posted on the site located at www.mindsumo.com (the "Site").
  • MindSumo Challenge(s)/Challenge(s) - Specific problems of individual organizations that are posted on the Site for users to try and solve.
  • Challenger(s) - The businesses or other entities that post Challenges on MindSumo to be worked on by Users.
  • Reward(s) - The monetary or other compensation given the winning User of a MindSumo Challenge. The Reward is chosen by the Challenger.
  • Solution(s) - A proposed answer submitted by a User for any individual MindSumo Challenge.
  • Winning Solution - The Solution that is chosen as the recipient of the Reward for a specific MindSumo Challenge.
  • MindSumo Challenge Brief - The document created for each MindSumo Challenge that describes the nature of the Challenge, and the specific deliverables that Users must submit in order to be eligible to win the Reward.
  • Challenge-Specific Provisions - Additional terms and conditions outlined for a particular Challenge that a Challenger stipulates must be agreed to by participating Users.

Information that We Collect MindSumo will collect Your Information when you voluntarily provide such information, including when you register as a User. Such information, which is collected passively using various technologies, cannot presently be used to specifically identify you. We may store such information itself or such information may be included in databases owned and maintained by our affiliates, agents or service providers. This Site may use such information and pool it with other information to track and analyze such information. This research may be compiled and analyzed on an aggregate basis, and we may use and share this aggregate data with our affiliates, agents and business partners. This aggregate information does not identify you personally. We may also disclose aggregated user statistics to current and prospective business partners, and to other third parties for other lawful purposes.
