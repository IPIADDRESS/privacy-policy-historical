Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Minecraft-Mods.org uses cookies to help Minecraft-Mods.org identify and track visitors, their usage of Minecraft-Mods.org website, and their website access preferences.
