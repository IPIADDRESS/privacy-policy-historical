Miller Electric Privacy Policy  

Miller Electric Mfg. Co. ("Miller Electric") respects your personal privacy and is committed to adhering to all applicable U.S. and Canadian laws and industry guidelines relating to privacy and the protection of personal information. This privacy policy ("Privacy Policy") describes how we handle the personal information that you provide to us on this website ("Web Site"). This Privacy Policy applies only to the Miller Electric Web Site, not to any other ITW or third party web sites that may be accessible from here. After reading this Privacy Policy, which is incorporated into and is a partof this Web Site's terms and conditions, you will know:

  1. What information Miller Electric collects from you
  2. How Miller Electric uses your information
  3. What choices you have about how Miller Electric treats your information
  4. How Miller Electric protects your privacy
  5. What Miller Electric does about children visiting this Web Site
  6. How you can contact Miller Electric

Please be sure to read this Privacy Policy before using this Web Site or submitting information to us. Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time and your continued use of this Web Site is acceptance of such changes, so please check this area periodically for updates to this Privacy Policy.

 

1. Information Miller Electric Collects From You

Active Information Collection Like many other web sites, we actively collect information from our visitors both byasking specific questions on this Web Site and by permitting you to communicate directly with us via online forms and e-mail. Some of the information that you submit may be personally identifiable and/or "personal information" under Canadian law. Such personally identifiable or personal information includes information that can be uniquely identified with you, such as your name, business name, address, phone number, e-mail address, etc. However,under Canadian law, "personal information" does not include your business address or other information used to contact you at your place of work. Contact information may be used to send information about our company to our customers and to learn about customer satisfaction with our services. We run contests on our Web Site in which we ask visitors for contact information. The contact information may be used to contact the visitor when necessary. Any credit card information you provide for the purchase of goods is handled in a secure manner and is used only for your intended purchase.

Passive Information Collection As you navigate through a web site, certain information can be passively collected (that is, gathered without you actively providing the information) using various technologies and means, such as navigational data collection. Like most web site operators, we track IP addresses during your visit and use both session and persistent cookies. We use cookies to help identify you when you visit our Web Site and to keep track of your shopping list. You should be aware that we might use these cookies for other purposes now and in the future. Our system also automatically gathers information about the areas you visit on this Web Site and collects operational information about the technology you use, such as your browser, type of computer, operating systems, and Internetservice providers.

We passively collect information to generate statistics and measure site activity in order to understand what areas ofthis Web Site are most popular, which areas may require improvement and what technologies our visitors are using. This information helps us to update and improve this Web Site and enhance the usefulness of customer visits. We also use this information for security purposes, to detect and to block security breaches and to provide you with a safe online environment.

If you are a resident of Quebec, you are hereby informed that (1) the personal information collected by Miller Electric would be considered a "file" being established by Miller Electric within the meaning of applicable legislation of that province, (2) the "object" of that file is to use such personal information as described in Section 2 below, (3) the file will be created and kept by Miller Electric and/or by one or more of its affiliates, and such file may be created and kept on its or their behalf by an outside service provider to Miller Electric or such affiliate(s) at Appleton, WI, (4) only those employees of Miller Electric, such affiliate(s) and/or such service provider(s) whose job functions involve the fulfillment of the object of the file will have access to your file, and (5) if you wish to consult your file or make changes or corrections to it, you may do so by written request to Miller Electric Mfg. Co., PO Box 1079, Appleton, WI 54912.

2. How Miller Electric Uses Your Information

Miller Electric uses your personal information to enhance the operation of this Web Site, allow you to use all of its features, and provide you with the services that you request. Miller Electric may contact you about new features or products in which it believes you may be interested. Miller Electric may share your personal information with reputable vendors or affiliates that assist us in processing information or providing products or services that our customers order. These vendors are restricted from using the information for any purpose. Miller Electric may provide aggregate statistics about our customers, sales, online traffic patterns and related information to reputable third parties, but these statistics will not identify any individual or include any personally identifying information. Miller Electric also will disclose any information that it is required to disclose by law or by an appropriate government authority.

Although Miller Electric does not currently rent, sell, or otherwise share your information with third parties for purposes unrelated to this Web Site, it may do so in the future. If you do not want your information to be shared, you should contact us at info@Millerwelds.com, as described in Section 3.

3. Choices You Have About How Miller Electric Uses Your Information

We strive to provide you with as many choices and as much control as possible regarding the personal information you provide to us. We have created mechanisms to provide you with the following control over your information:

You may opt out of having your e-mail address used for promotional purposes by Miller Electric. This opt out does not apply to information provided to Miller Electric as a result of a product purchase, warranty registration, product service experience or other transactions. If you no longer wish to receive information or promotional materials from us by e-mail you may opt out of receiving these communications by using one of the following methods:

  1. Send an email to info@Millerwelds.com asking to be omitted from future e-mail distributions.
  2. Visit and enter your email address in the Unsubscribe area.
  3. Follow the instructions contained in the promotional email or newsletter.

If you do not wish us to collect cookies, you may set your browser to refuse cookies, or to alert you when cookies arebeing sent. If you do so, please note that some parts of this Web Site may then be inaccessible or not function properly.

You should review this Privacy Policy carefully, because if you do not agree with our practices, your ultimate choice isnot to use this Web Site. Remember, by using any part of this Web Site, you accept and agree to our privacy practices. If we update this Privacy Policy, your continued use of this Web Site (following the posting of the revised Privacy Policy) means that you accept and agree to the terms of the revised Privacy Policy.

4. How Miller Electric Protects Your Information

We work hard to protect your personal information. This Web Site has both technical and procedural security measures in place to protect information under our control against the loss, misuse and alteration, including but not limited to SSL encryption for data transfer, firewalls, restricted access to data, employee training, and other methods. When you place orders, we offer the use of a secure server. The secure server encrypts all sensitive information you input before it is transmitted to us. Only those employees who need access to your information in order to perform their duties are allowed access.

This Web Site contains links to other web sites operated by third parties that may be of interest to you. We cannot control these third party web sites, which may collect personal information from you. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such web sites. When you follow a link and leave this Web Site, you do so at your own risk.
