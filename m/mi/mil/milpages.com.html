Privacy Policy

We are not interested in selling or trading your personal information with a third party. Our website is for members to join and participate in and selling information is the wrong way to go about creating a relationship with our members. We understand personal information is important to each and everyone of us. As a public site we understand there is information that is visible to the world but we will not take advantage of the information in a way to bring financial gain to the site through selling or trading that away.

We want you to feel safe here at MilPages and if you find a reason to believe your information is being used in a way deemed inappropriately please tell us so we can look into it. Upfront though, our intentions is to keep the community safe and fun to be in.
