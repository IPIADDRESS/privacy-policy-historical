2. Information that we collect from you

9. Cookies

milk&more uses cookies for certain features & functions of our website. Cookies are files that store information on your hard drive or browser to ensure that our site works correctly to give you the best possible experience. They make it easier for you to maintain your preferences on the website, and by seeing how you use the website, we can tailor the website around your preferences and measure usability of the website. Below is a list of the cookies we use on milkandmore.co.uk and what they are used for.

Cookie Name What we use it for
XXDCDepotId To check your postcode during registration to find your nearest delivery depot.
XXDCMilkMan To check your postcode, telling you who your milkman will be. This information is displayed when you log into your account online and is automatically updated should your milkman change.
XXDCRoundId To assign your account to the nearest depot & milkman with a 'Round ID number' so we can make deliveries to you and monitor the level of service you receive.
XXDCDeliveryPattern To be able to inform you on which days your milkman will deliver. You can view this information by logging into your account. This will automatically update if your milkman ever changes.
backLinkURL To allow you to navigate around the milk&more website, going back to previous pages without losing the contents of your shopping basket.
holidayPage To enable the holiday feature to suspend your orders while you are away.
ReferaFriend To let you use our Refer A Friend offer by checking your account and generating a unique promotional code for you to share with friends & family to get you both money off your shopping. You can find out more about our Refer a Friend offer here.
RequestCtx.CART_ID To store the items you have put into your shopping basket by assigning it with a unique ID number linked to your milk&more account. If you do not complete your shopping for any reason, the items will stay in your shopping basket until you come back to the milk&more website and either confirm, update or delete the order. You will not receive a delivery of items in your shopping basket until you have confirmed your order.
amendVarAmt To check and update the value of your orders when you make changes.
PayDiff To check your account credit limit against the value of your shopping basket to see whether you need to make a one-off payment for your order.
updStr To store your order details for current shopping basket and regular orders.
virtualShop To see if you are browsing one of our 'Virtual' categories, found at the bottom of the Shop page, and show you the products associated with the part of the shop you are browsing.
XXDCviewFlag To see if you are logged into your account, so that the site can display your account information and let you shop for products.
highlightValue To control the appearanceof the left hand menu to highlight which section you are currently browsing.

Managing Cookies

None of the cookies on milk&more are used to store financial information, or information that could be used to identify you but are essential to ensure that the site works as it should - such as checking to see that you are logged in and can place an order.

You can, should you choose, disable the cookies from your browser and delete all cookies currently stored on your computer. On Microsoft Internet Explorer, this can be done by selecting "Tools/Internet Options" and reviewing your privacy settings or selecting "delete cookies". This may prevent you from taking full advantage of the website. You can find out how to do this for your particular browser by clicking "help" on your browser's menu.
