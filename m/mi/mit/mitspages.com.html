Cookies

A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier that is sent to your browser from a web site’s computers and stored on your computer’s hard drive.

Cookies are required to use the MITSpages.com service.

We use cookies to record current session information, but do not use permanent cookies. You are required to log-in to your MITSpages.com Project Site after a certain period of time has elapsed to protect you against others accidentally accessing your account contents.

MITSpages.com may periodically update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your MITSpages.com primary account holder account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
