The information we collect during use of MightyNest.com is grouped into two categories: personal information, which is information that is identifiable to a specific customer, and aggregate information, which is general information that does not identify an individual customer. Personal information may include your name, email address, mailing address, credit card information and phone number.  MightyNest does not store any credit card information directly on our servers. Other personal information you may share with us if you choose to complete a subscriber profile includes items such as birthday, green living preferences (e.g. vegan, preferred fragrances, etc), and details about your home (e.g. have pets). We may also collect certain demographic information such as your Site preferences, household information, age, gender, geographical location, language, time zone, income and education (some of this information may be optional). MightyNest may use or disclose aggregate information for any purpose.

If you wish to be removed from these notifications, please let us know by emailing customerservice@mightynest.com with your full name and email address.

Cookies

A cookie is a small text file that is stored on a user’s computer for record-keeping purposes. We use cookies to collect anonymous information about how you use this Site by setting and accessing cookies on your computer. These cookies track information such as how often you visit our Site, what pages you view, and where you go after you leave our Site. The cookies are not connected to your personal information. We recommend that you leave cookies turned on because they allow you to take advantage of some of this Site’s features.

Web Beacons

Web beacons, or clear gifs, are tiny graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies, and are used to track the online movements of web users. In contrast to cookies, which are stored on a user’s computer hard drive, web beacons are embedded invisibly on web pages. Web beacons help us better manage content on our Site by informing us what content is effective. We do not tie the information gathered by web beacons to your personal information. We use web beacons to track activity on our website and emails  to understand usage and campaign effectiveness and to tell if an email has been opened and acted upon.
