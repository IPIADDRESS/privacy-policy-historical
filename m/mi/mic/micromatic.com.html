Privacy Policy

We know that your privacy is very important to you. That is why protecting any personal information, such as your name, address, email address or phone number is of the utmost importance to Micro Matic and we value the trust in our relationship.

What information do we share and with whom?
MicroMatic.com does not rent or sell customer information to outside marketers or to anyone else.

At all times, we adhere to our strict standards for providing quality products and services, responding to your needs, and protecting Micro Matics’ customer information.

Our technology supports industry-standard 128-bit SSL encryption. When you enter your credit card number into the order form, it is transmitted across the Internet in an encrypted (scrambled) form and then decoded when it gets to us.

If you supply us with your telephone number online you may receive telephone contact from us regarding your order. We will not contact you by telephone about any of our new products or offers.

Yahoo! Inc. hosts the web site, ordering system, and order data. Yahoo! automatically collects order information but may only use this information in the aggregate. If you accessed this store through Yahoo! Shopping, Yahoo! also automatically collects information about your shopping experience. Please see the Yahoo! Privacy Policy for more information about how Yahoo! uses this information.
