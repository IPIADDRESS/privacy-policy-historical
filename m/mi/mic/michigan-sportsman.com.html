Michigan Sportsman Privacy Policy
Information about you:

This site does not collect personally identifiable information about individuals except when specifically and knowingly provided by such individuals.  An example of this is the information provided when a user signs up for our forums.  Another example is winners of our contests, who may have to provide information to us, which we may in turn need to provide to a third party for shipping prizes.  The Michigan Sportsman does not bear any responsibility for any actions or policies of any third parties.

If you buy merchandise through our bookstore, you will have to provide personal information to Amazon.com with who we are affiliated for shipping.

We collect anonymous aggregated data and provide it to our advertisers and other companies that we do business with for market research and statistical purposes.  For example we might report to an advertiser that 50% of our users fly fish.

You should be aware that any information about yourself that you post on our forums or in the campfire can be collected by others and may result in unsolicited messages from others.

Cookies:

The Michigan Sportsman may place a "cookie" in the browser files of a user's computer.  Cookies are in no way used to track your movements throughout our site or the internet.  You may also get cookies from our advertisers.  The Michigan Sportsman does not control these cookies and these cookies are not subject to our policy.  The use of advertising cookies is common in the Internet industry.  You may disable cookies while using this site, however some portions of the site may not work as well with cookies disabled.
