misterb&b Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 30, 2018

 

Thank you for reviewing misterb&b, Inc.'s (if you reside in any country outside of the European Union) or Pinklab360 SASU/misterb&b Europe (if you reside in the European Union) (collectively referred to as "misterb&b", “we,” “us” or “our”) privacy policy for its mobile application (“App”) and Internet web sites located at the URL’s www.misterbandb.com ("Site"). This Privacy Policy details certain policies implemented throughout misterb&b governing misterb&b's collection and use of personally identifiable information about users of our Site and users of our App.

 

Information Collection and Use.

You can generally visit our Site and use our App without having to submit any personally identifiable information about yourself to us. However, to use certain services and functionality of the Site and the App (such as to post a property for rent or request to book a rental property), you will be required to register for an account with misterb&b. When you register for an account, we will collect your name, email address, mailing address, gender, sexual orientation, username and password. If you are a posting a property for rent, we will also collect the address of the rental property and we may request your photo, identification or passport copy and/or copies of your utility bills. If you signup to receive our newsletters, we will collect your email address to send you our newsletters.

If you contact us with questions or to request information, we will collect the personally identifiable information that you voluntarily transmit with your communications to us. We also may collect personally identifiable information about you that you elect to upload to the Site or our App (such as in rental property descriptions or ratings for properties).

We may use your personally identifiable information to register you to use our Site and App and to provide our rental platform services to you, contact you to deliver certain services or information you have requested, verify your authority to use the App and the Site, to detect fraud, improve the content and general administration of the Site and the App. We may use your photo, identification or passport copy and/or copies of your utility bills to verify your identity. We may also send you targeted marketing and promotions regarding our App and the Site. If you do not wish to receive these marketing or promotions from us, you may opt-out as set forth in the message.

If you register to use our Site, you may engage in direct messages with another user through the Site or App. misterb&b may collect personally identifiable information that you include in such messages and may review such messages from time-to-time for purposes of confirming compliance with misterb&b’s Terms of Use or to detect and/or investigate email scams or similar fraudulent activities.

You may import the contacts from your address book to your misterb&b account. You can also send an e-mail to contacts you manually select, inviting them to join misterb&b. If you wish to activate this feature, we may access your Gmail address book contacts by using the OAuth 2.0 protocol. We will not store or share information about contacts; we will however store the email addresses you sent invitations to.

Additionally, the third party app store providers from which you obtain the App may collect certain data about you, including aggregate data, and your use of the applicable app store is subject to the privacy policy terms of the app store provider. The app store providers may provide us with certain aggregate data about you.

 

Children’s Privacy.

misterb&b recognizes the privacy interests of children and we encourage parents and guardians to take an active role in their children's online activities and interests. Neither the Site nor the App are directed to children under the age of 13. misterb&b does not target its App or this Site to children under 13. misterb&b does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If we learn that a child under the age of 13 provided us with personally identifiable information, we will delete that information. If your child has provided personally identifiable information, please contact us so we can delete it. If you are under the age of 13, please do not provide us with any personally identifiable information.

 

Cookies.

We may use small text files called cookies to improve overall Site experience. A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's hard drive containing information about the user. Cookies generally do not permit us to personally identify you.  We generally use session cookies to save your preferences and such cookies expire when you close your browser. We also may set a persistent cookie to authenticate users (such as for password recognition) and for support for the internal operations of our Site and App. misterb&b may track users over time and over our Sites but we do not respond to browser do not track signals.

Third parties such as providers of external services like web traffic analysis services may also track users over time and over our Site and third party websites and such third parties may also place cookies, via our Site.
