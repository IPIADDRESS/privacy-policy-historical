Privacy Policy

This policy relates to the privacy practices of Torpedo Farm Limited (“we”, “our” or “us”) in connection with software products offered by us via any host e-shop or via our website www.misterhorse.tv (the “Website”).  It sets out the basis on which any personal data that you provide to us, in connection with the use or purchase of these software products will be processed and disclosed by us, in accordance with Irish data protection legislation.  We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of other e-shops or websites you may use.  Any external links to other websites will be clearly identifiable as such.

Collection of Your Information

When you visit an e-shop or the Website to access or purchase our software products we may collect certain information about your usage of the software products, including the IP address from which you access the e-shop or Website, the date and time of your access to the e-shop or Website and the pages you visit.  Where you access or purchase our software products we may also collect personal data about you, including your name, address, email address, credit card or other payment details and other information from your correspondence with us.

Use of Your Information

We may use personal data relating to you to help make our software products more user-friendly, to provide the services and products requested by you, and to manage your use of our products. Your personal data may also be used by us for the purposes of analytics and market research, in order to improve our software products.

We may also use your personal data to send you e-mail alerts about important changes or developments to our software products and to send you customer satisfaction or marketing surveys in relation to your subscription or registered account with us, in order to ascertain how we may improve our service to you.

We may also use your personal data to send you e-mail alerts with details of our products and services, which we feel may be of interest or relevance to you.

Please indicate your preference in respect of marketing, where requested upon registration for our software products.

Disclosure of Your Information

We may disclose personal data relating to you to our agents, service providers and professional advisers and/or to such other third parties as may reasonably be required in connection with the purposes referred to in this Policy or as otherwise required or permitted by applicable law.  This may involve the transfer of your personal data to a country outside of the European Economic Area where the level of data protection is not as high as in Ireland.

Retention of Your Information

We will only retain your personal data for as long as we require it for the purposes referred to in this Privacy Policy, unless we are required by a particular applicable law or regulation to retain it for longer. If you stop using our software products and terminate all applicable agreements with us, we will delete any personal data we hold in relation to you as soon as reasonably possible and will immediately discontinue collecting any further personal data in relation to you.

Your Access and Amendment Rights

You have the right, subject to certain exemptions, to obtain a copy of any personal data which we hold about you (for which we may charge you a small fee) and to have inaccuracies in the information corrected. If you wish to avail of any of these rights, please contact us at support@misterhorse.tv.  In order to protect your privacy, you may also be asked to provide suitable proof of identification when making such a request. 

Privacy Policy Changes

We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time, and in our sole discretion.  We encourage you to frequently check this page for any changes to this Privacy Policy.  Your continued use of our software products after any change to this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.
