Privacy Policy

 

1. This policy sets out how Mio Destino Limited (we or us) will use the personal information that we collect when you deal with us. We will treat that information in accordance with this policy, current legislation and best practice.
2. We will use your personal information to process you orders, deliver any items you have ordered, authorize and process payments, communicate with you about orders, items and promotional offers, update our records and generally maintain your account with us and help us to maintain and improve this website and the services we provide to you.

8. You have the right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you and to have any inaccuracies corrected. If you would like to do so, please write to: The Data Protection Officer, Mio Destino, PO Box 8496, Leicester, LE21 3BG.
