 Privacy Statement
The Multiplayer Online Games Directory (MPOGD) is deeply committed to protecting the privacy of Internet users.

MPOGD does not collect any personally identifiable information about you such as name, address, phone number, or e-mail address. However, MPOGD does obtain some types of non-personally identifiable information such as the Internet Service Provider you use to connect to the Internet, your browser type, or the type of computer operating system you use (Macintosh or Windows, for example). The information collected by MPOGD is used for the purpose of targeting the correct type of games desired, and to make sure our site is easily accessable to the majority of visitors.

MPOGD assures you that any information collected will never be given out to anyone, under any circumstances.

Multiplayer Online Games Directory works with a third party that serves ads to this site. To find out more about how Everyone.net manages the privacy of information in conjunction with serving ads on this site, please go to http://www.everyone.net/main/html/p_policy.html
