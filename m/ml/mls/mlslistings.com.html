MLSLISTINGS INC. PRIVACY POLICY

MLSListings Inc. (“MLSListings”) operates a multiple listing service.  As part of the services offered to our participants and subscribers, we have created and maintain this public website.

The privacy of your personal information is very important to us. We have created this privacy policy to inform you of what information is collected from the user, how it is utilized and with whom it may be shared. This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that MLSListings does not own or control, or to people whom MLSListings does not employ or manage.

If you have questions about the Privacy Policy or how we address any of the following, write to us at support@MLSListings.com or call Customer Support at 866-734-5787.

MLSListings collects personal information only as allowed by law.

We limit the collection of personal information to what is relevant and necessary to accomplish a lawful purpose.  We do not sell, share or rent this information other than as disclosed in this policy. At no time does MLSListings collect information from users other than information users voluntarily give to us.

Information Collection and Use
We do not require a log on to access data available on this website, therefore no personal user information is collected, stored or distributed. 

Links
This website may contain links to other websites. MLSListings does not endorse any third party websites and is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those websites. We encourage you to be aware when you leave our website, and to read the privacy statements of websites that collect personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this website.

Cookies
A cookie is a piece of data stored on a user’s hard drive while on our website. It is not linked to any personally identifiable information, and once the user closes his or her browser, the cookie terminates. If your browser is set to reject cookies, you may still use our website; however, you will be limited in some areas. For example, you will not be able to complete a property search because our website uses cookies to “remember” search criteria while you are on the website.

Servers for our websites automatically receive and record some information into a log. For this website this includes identifying your browser, this website cookie information, IP address and the pages you request. We use IP addresses to analyze trends, administer the website, and gather broad demographic information for use in improving website performance. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

If you contact MLSListings staff directly using the “contact us” feature of this website, your correspondence may be retained for reference and follow up, or further distributed to relevant parties.  The public should have no expectation that correspondence to MLSListings will remain confidential. 

Surveys & Contests

From time-to-time our website requests information from users via surveys or contests. Participation is completely voluntary and you have a choice whether or not to disclose requested information. Contact information will be used to notify winners and award prizes. Survey information will be used for monitoring or improving the use and satisfaction of this website.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

MLSListings may amend this policy from time to time.
