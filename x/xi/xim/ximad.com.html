Privacy Policy

XIMAD Inc. ("XIMAD") respects the privacy rights of our online visitors of our web site www.ximad.com (the "Site") and is committed to protecting the personal information collected about you. To further this commitment, we have adopted this Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") to guide how we collect, store, and use the information you provide us. By visiting our Site, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use.

What information is collected by XIMAD via this website?

  • Information about you – we actively collect personal information about you when you provide it voluntarily. This information may include your name, email address, phone number, credit card details (e.g., type, expiry date), postal address and other information as defined by applicable federal, state or local law.
  • Information about your computer or mobile device – we may passively collect information about your computer and/or mobile device, such as your Internet domain name, IP address, device and/or phone number, the type of browser your computer is using, the date and time of your visit, and the page(s) you visited on our website or in an on-device catalogue (if applicable). This is done automatically using a variety of tools, including cookies, server log files and other forms of navigational data collection, including, without limitation, pixel tags, web beacons or clear GIFs.

What is XIMAD’s reason for collecting this information?

The personal information collected from you is used to respond to your request for XIMAD products and services, for provision of such products and services, to determine your satisfaction with your purchase(s) and to customise our future interactions to meet your needs. The information about your computer and/or your mobile device is used to analyse marketing statistics, identify trends and continually improve the way in which we serve you.

What if I don’t want you to passively collect information about my computer?

Cookies and related tools should not harm your computer or mobile device, and they do not identify you personally. You may set your browser to notify you when a cookie is sent or to refuse cookies altogether, but certain features of our website and on-device clients will not work without cookies or similar tags.

What steps does XIMAD take to protect my personal information, if I choose to provide it?

XIMAD employs a range of security measures, including authentication, auditing, monitoring and industry standard encryption, which is used when you provide your credit card details. We need your help to make our security measures complete. If you feel that the security of your communications with us has been compromised, contact us immediately using the contact information provided at the end of the policy.

Our provision of hyperlinks is for your convenience and does not imply that XIMAD endorses or has an association with the linked sites. Please note that if you transmit your information using an on-device client, your information will be travelling over the wireless network provided by your network provider and XIMAD cannot ensure the privacy of your information when it travels over such third party networks.

Will you be sharing my personal or computer information with others?

Your personal information may be shared with our business partners, such as software developers, network providers, device manufacturers and/or certain retailers, in order to fulfil your request for XIMAD products and services. These business partners should not use this information for any purpose other than to fulfil your request, but we cannot ensure that their confidentiality policies are the same as ours. Your computer information, which does not identify you personally, will be aggregated with the information on other website users for analysis, which may be done by XIMAD or by a subcontractor. Your information may also be released if disclosure is required by law, in the event of a corporate sale, merger or similar event, or our systems administrators need to access the information as part of an investigation into technical problems (e.g., spider, bot, worm), vandalism, or other misuse of the XIMAD website or on-device clients.

What does XIMAD do to limit access to the website by children?

Our website is intended for use by adults and is not structured to attract children. We do not directly seek children as users of our products and services without their parents’ knowledge and consent, nor do we indirectly appeal to them by using animated characters or similar attractions. Persons under the age of 13 should not submit their personal information, or the personal information of their family or friends, via the website or an on-device client. If you believe that your child has provided personal information via our website, please contact us and we will remove the information from our systems.
