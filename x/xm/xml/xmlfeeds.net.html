USGuides.net is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. We want to provide a safe, secure user experience. We gather information about all of our users collectively, such as what areas users visit most frequently and what services users access the most. We only use such information in the aggregate and not on an individual basis. This information helps us determine what is most beneficial for our users, and how we can develop a better overall website for our users. This information may be shared with our partners, but only in the aggregate, so that they too may develop a better overall website for you, as well.

We may also ask you for other information, such as feedback regarding the site, the types of news and information you are interested in, etc., Again, in an effort to develop for you deliver to you the best possible Guide Site.

General Information Disclosure

We do not disclose information about your individual visits to USGuides.net, or personal information that you provide, such as your name, address, e-mail address, telephone number, credit card number, etc., to any outside parties, except when we believe the law requires it. But, we may record and share aggregated information with our partners.

Privacy Commitment Changes

If we decide to change our privacy commitment for USGuides.net, we will post those changes here so that you will always know what information we gather, how we might use that information, and whether we will disclose it to anyone. If, at any time, you have questions or concerns about USGuides.net's privacy commitment, please feel free to email us at privacy@USGuides.net.
