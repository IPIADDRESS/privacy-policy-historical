Privacy Statement


Xtra.ca and our parent company, Pink Triangle Press, are committed to protecting your privacy.

Xtra.ca does not collect personal information on its users. You are not required to register to use any part of Xtra.

In the event that any personal information is collected for a contest, poll or survey (see more below), Xtra.ca never sells, rents nor releases that information to a third person.

Cookies
Xtra.ca does not use cookies.

Advertisers & Linked Websites
Links to advertisers on Xtra.ca, third-party services and other web sites that are accessible via Xtra.ca may have different privacy policies that are not subject to Xtra.ca's privacy policy. It is the responsibility of users to read the privacy statement of such websites.

Xtra.ca's Mailing Lists
If you chose to subscribe to a mailing list operated by Xtra.ca, Capital Xtra, Xtra and/or Xtra West, these lists are currently operated and managed through Yahoo Groups. Membership and member privacy is subject to the terms, conditions and policies as outlined by Yahoo Groups on its website. Xtra.ca does not collect, record nor distribute any personal information from subscribers that is detailed in any Member Directory for mailing lists situated with Yahoo Groups.

Polls & Contests
With regards to on-line polls, surveys or contests operated by Xtra.ca, from time to time we may ask for your e-mail address or some other type of identifier. Any information collected by this means is used for that specific poll, survey or contest. Xtra.ca will not release, sell nor rent any email addresses or other personal information obtained through these features.

Statistics & Demographics
Xtra.ca may collect statistics and demographics about website usage and information about your computer, Internet connection, including your computer's IP address, the type and version of browser and operating system you use, your internet domain and, if you arrived at Xtra.ca via a link from another website, the URL of the linking page.

The use of this information is limited to: assessing usage patterns and demographics of site users; popularity and usage of site features and editorial content; compatibility of our site with popular browsers and operating systems; and, to assist us with our strategic planning and development and advertiser relations. All data and findings collected for these purposes will be presented as anonymous or aggregated information. No personal information about individual users is detailed or released.

Changes to this Statement
Please note that Xtra.ca's privacy statement may be updated and/or revised at any time, without notice to users.
