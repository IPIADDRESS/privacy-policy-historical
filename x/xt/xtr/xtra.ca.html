Privacy Policy

Dailyxtra.com and our parent company, Pink Triangle Press, are committed to protecting your privacy.

While many aspects of dailyxtra.com do not require you to register before using them, other areas require registration or membership. Personal information is collected through these registration and membership processes. Apart from using the information for those purposes, all data and findings collected through these means and used for statistical information is presented as anonymous or aggregated information. No personal information about individual users is released.

Under-Age People

Anybody under 18 years of age should ensure they have permission from their parent or guardian before posting any personal information.

Cookies

Dailyxtra.com does not use cookies when people leave comments. But dailyxtra.com does use cookies to ensure fairness in polls and contests and also to ensure quick entry and use on repeat visits.

Advertisers & Linked Websites

Links on dailyxtra.com to advertisers, third-party services and other web sites that are accessible via dailyxtra.com may have different privacy policies. They are not subject to dailyxtra.com's privacy policy. It is the responsibility of users to review the privacy statement of such advertisers, third-parties and websites.

Dailyxtra.com's Mailing Lists

If you choose to subscribe to a mailing list maintained by dailyxtra.com or Xtra, some of these lists are operated and managed through Yahoo Groups or Facebook Groups. Membership and member privacy on these mailing lists are subject to the terms, conditions and policies as outlined by Yahoo or Facebook on their websites. Dailyxtra.com does not collect, record nor distribute any personal information from subscribers to Yahoo or Facebook Groups for any other purpose.

Other mailing lists are operated and managed by dailyxtra.com. Personal information provided by subscribers will be used for this purpose. In addition, dailyxtra.com and Pink Triangle Press reserves the right to use this information internally to develop marketing and sales information. All data and findings collected for these purposes will be presented as anonymous or aggregated information. No personal information about individual users is released.

Memberships, Polls, Surveys & Contests

With regards to online polls, surveys or contests conducted by dailyxtra.com, from time to time we may ask you to become a member by providing your email address or some other type of identifier. Any information collected by this means is used for that specific membership, poll, survey or contest. Dailyxtra.com may also collect additional information from people registering, becoming a member or participating in a survey, poll or contest. This information includes income, age, location and gender. Dailyxtra.com will not trade, sell nor rent any email addresses or other personal information obtained through these features.

Dailyxtra.com and Pink Triangle Press reserves the right to use personal information collected from these memberships, polls, surveys and contests internally to develop marketing and sales information. All data and findings collected for these purposes will be presented as anonymous or aggregated information. No personal information about individual users is released.

Statistics & Demographics

Dailyxtra.com may collect statistics and demographics about website usage and information about your computer, internet connection, your computer's IP address and geographic location, the type and version of browser and operating system you use, your internet domain and, if you arrived at Dailyxtra.com via a link from another website, the URL of the linking page and the keyword(s) that you used.

This information is used for assessing usage patterns and demographics of site users; popularity and usage of site features and editorial content; compatibility of our site with popular browsers and operating systems; and, to assist us with our strategic planning and development and advertiser relations.

Dailyxtra.com and Pink Triangle Press reserves the right to use this statistical and demographic information internally to develop marketing and sales information. All data and findings collected for these purposes will be presented as anonymous or aggregated information. No personal information about individual users is released.

Changes to this Statement

Please note that dailyxtra.com's privacy statement may be updated and/or revised at any time, without notice to users.
