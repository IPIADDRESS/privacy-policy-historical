XuQa.com Privacy Policy
About XuQa.com
XuQa.com is a free social networking & casual gaming website for young people, maintained by iVentster, Inc. as identified in the Terms of Use. For more information, please visit our blog (http://huqa.blogspot.com)
Information Collection and Use
Registration
When you sign-up on http://www.XuQa.com, you'll be shown two options. In the first option you enter your email, choose a password and create a XuQa login. A confirmation email is sent to your email address, which is used to verify your email address. The other option is to login to XuQa using a third party account (supported third party services are displayed on the XuQa registration page).

You are required to select your college, school or city. You may also enter other personal information and any information you enter is optional and private, and accessible only to you or to those that you authorize.
We are the sole owner of the information collected on http://www.XuQa.com. Personally identifiable information is collected from users at some points on our website. We collect the following personal information as part of the registration process:

- Email address
- Name
- Age
- Location (city/country)
- Date of Birth
- Gender

You may also enter the following optional personally-identifiable information when filling in your profile:

- Secondary email address
- Physical address
- Telephone number
- IM names
- School or College affiliations
How is this Personal Information used?
Your registration information is used for the following purposes:

1. To create your account.
2. Email: to identify you as a unique user, and to create a communications channel for notifying you of important service or security notices (required), or friend notifications (optional). When you sign-up, friend notifications are turned on by default, but they can be disabled by going to the “Settings” Page. Other newsletter or promotional emails are turned off by default.
3. Date of Birth: to verify that you are not an underage user.
4. Location: This is used to automatically place you in a group of people from your country.

Your Optional Personal Information is used for:

1. Used by you to represent yourself to your friends.
2. In search results when users search for people with similar college or school affiliations.
3. Browsing photos: Your photos may be accessible to your friends or other users, depending on your privacy settings on the “Settings” Page.
Tell A Friend
When inviting friends to join the site you may enter their email addresses directly, or use one of our import options to import friend's lists. We will automatically send your friend a one-time email inviting him or her to visit the site. We do not store any of your friend's email addresses if they decline your invitation and do not join the site. We do not store any other information you provide to import friend's lists or invite friends. Such information is provided only and directly to the program or website you have selected to import your contacts from.
As with any site that uses invitations and buddy lists, you should use care when disclosing your personal information to strangers. Be advised that if you send messages containing personal information, that information may be collected and used by others.
Market Research Surveys
If you choose to earn extra Peanuts by completing surveys offered by our research partners, the information you submit in completing this survey will be transmitted to them. However none of this information will be personally identifiable – it shall be used for research purposes only, and in aggregate form. You will know what the survey is about before you take it, and will have the option of canceling it at anytime before or during the survey. We do not work or associate with research companies that are in the business of trading personal information.
Affiliate Offers
You can sign up for a promotional offer by an advertiser to earn Peanuts. This will require you to click on a link, and leave the XUQA site. Affiliate Offers typically require your name, age, location, and email address, though they may require more information. THIS INFORMATION IS TRANSMITTED TO THE ADVERTISER. ANY INFORMATION YOU ENTER ON ANY SITE AFTER LEAVING XUQA.COM BECOMES THE PROPERTY OF THE ADVERTISER, AND ITS USE IS GOVERNED BY THE ADVERTISERS’ PRIVACY POLICY. Logging
As is true of most Web sites, we gather certain information automatically and store it in log files. This information includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system and date/time stamp.
We use this information, which does not identify individual users, to analyze trends, to administer the site, to track users' movements around the site and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. We do not link this automatically-collected server data to personally identifiable information.
Cookies & GIFS
A "cookie" is a small file containing a string of characters that is sent to your computer when you visit a website. iVentster, Inc. uses cookies to keep you logged in to the site, and to enable the proper functioning of other services used by you on the site. We use session ID cookies to confirm that users are logged in. These cookies terminate within a few hours of use. We also use persistent cookies to identify users uniquely to reduce cheating and fraud. These cookies will stay on your computer even after you logout of XUQA. We do not use cookies to collect any non-XUQA-related information from any user. Most browsers are set to automatically accept cookies, however you can set your browser to prompt you or refuse cookies. This may cause problems in running and accessing some services on the website.

We do not use “clear gifs” or other images to record, process, transfer, log or transmit any user information, with one exception: for security purposes, your email address is displayed as an image on your profile so that it cannot be “read” by a spam bot or automated program. Other than this security feature, we do not use images to process any user account or profile information.
Third-Party Advertisers
The ads appearing on this Web site are delivered to users by our Web advertising partners. Our Web advertising partners may set cookies. Doing this allows the ad network to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement. In this way, ad networks may compile information about where you, or others who are using your computer, saw their advertisements and determine which ads are clicked on. This information allows an ad network to deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. We do not have access to or control of the cookies that may be placed by the third-party ad servers or ad networks.
This privacy statement covers the use of cookies by iVentster, Inc. and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.
