XLIVE COLLECTS PERSONAL INFORMATION

User Provided Information: You provide certain personally identifiable information (such as your name and email address) to xLive when choosing to participate in various activities on the Website such as uploading, messaging, commenting, chatting, responding to Website staff, or subscribing to newsletters/alerts.

  • Cookies Information: When you visit the Website, we may send one or more cookies (a small text file used by your web browser) to your computer that uniquely identifies your web browser. xLive uses both session cookies and persistent cookies.
    A persistent cookie remains after you close your browser. Persistent cookies may be used by your browser on subsequent visits to the site. Persistent cookies can be removed by clearing your web browser's cache.
    A session cookie is temporary and disappears after you close your browser.
    You can reset your web browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some features of the Website may not function properly if the ability to accept cookies is disabled.
  • Log File Information: When you use the Website, our servers automatically record certain information that your web browser sends whenever you visit any website. These server logs may include information such as your web request, Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type, browser language, referring / exit pages and URLs, platform type, number of clicks, domain names, landing pages, pages viewed and the order of those pages, the amount of time spent on particular pages, the date and time of your request, and one or more cookies that may uniquely identify your browser.

Web Beacons) which are used to track the online usage patterns of our Users anonymously (i.e., in a non-personally-identifiable manner). In addition, we may also use clear gifs in HTML-based emails sent to our Users to track which emails are opened by recipients.
