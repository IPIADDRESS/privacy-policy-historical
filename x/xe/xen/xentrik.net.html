What are cookies and how does Xentrik use them?
Xentrik may place a "cookie" in the browser files of your computer. Cookies are small pieces of information or files that are stored on a user's computer by the Web server through a user's browser for record keeping purposes. The information can be retrieved to personalize, monitor, or regulate use of the site. Cookies allow Xentrik to automate access and data entry functions of the site, such as login information and to tailor the site to a user's preferences or interests. In addition, cookies allow Xentrik to track usage of the site to determine those areas of the site that are useful or popular (and those that are not), thereby enabling Xentrik to improve and update its service.

No. The only time you will ever receive any kind of mail from Xentrik is when you contact us first, requiring a reply. We do not send out promotional messages of any kind.

Will Xentrik share my personal information with any third parties?
Xentrik will NOT disclose any Personal Information, except as described in this policy.
