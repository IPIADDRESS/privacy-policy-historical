Our Privacy Policy
We strive to make your privacy and internet fraud protection a primary concern for us.

Any information you give to the xenoMED Forums! website will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented. A more detailed explanation about how we safeguard your personal information is described below. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to let us know.

We don't actively submit pages with usernames to search engines, but they are sometimes picked up by a small number of them. If you have a username that is your actual full name, it may be possible to search for your name and find it affiliated with xenoMED.

Resale or disclosure of information to third parties:

The xenoMED Forums! website does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including membership forms or email lists. We ensure your information shall remain safe and protected stored in our database.

Browser information collected on the website:

The xenoMED Forums! website analyzes our website logs to constantly improve the value of the materials available on website. Our website logs are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.

Privacy of our email lists:

The xenoMED Forums! website maintains several email lists to keep xenoMED Forums! members informed about breaking events. Members select to be updated via email by the admin of xenoMED during registration. In addition, we configure our list server software to refuse to divulge the email addresses of our list subscribers to anyone. Any and all information collected at this site will not be sold, reused, rented loaned, or otherwise disclosed to anyone.

Database Content:

The xenoMED website is created around a database containing posts, registration info, survey results, private messages, logged IPs and other activities. The information contained within the database belongs to xenoMED. An administrator of xenoMED, if so inclined, can access information in the database. Posts, IPs, private messages are all accessible within the database. They are private, but not necessarily secret. The information you provide in your posts may be summarized at a non-personal level on occasion.

Your Identity:

The xenoMED Forums! website takes precaution in helping you to maintain your privacy within the membership. Although your email address is verified as you join, the email addresses are hidden and are not viewable by members. (We ask that you are careful that you do not provide your email address nor your exact location in order to maintain safety while you use the xenoMED Forums! website. Be safe! Be smart! Do not give your phone number, address, hospital, and other personal info in public forums.

The xenoMED Forums! website retains the right to provide any information we know about you to legal authorities in the event of legal action arising from your online activity.
