Privacy Policy for xcweather.co.uk

The privacy of visitors to XCWeather is very important to us.

Here is information on what types of personal information we receive when you visit xcweather.co.uk, and how we safeguard your information. We never sell your personal information to third parties.

Log Files

As with most other websites, we collect the data contained in log files. The information in the log files includes IP address, browser type, time of the visit and which pages were viewed.

Cookies and Web Beacons

We use cookies to store information, such as your preferences (mph, kmh, kt etc.) when you visit my site.

In order to pay for XCWeather we use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit. These companies may use information (but not your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to XCWeather and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and would like to know your options in relation to not having this information used by these companies, click here.
