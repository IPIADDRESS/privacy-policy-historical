What cookies are and how they are used
 As part of offering and providing customizable and personalized services, Handshakes may use cookies to store and sometimes track information about you. A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. Several features of this web site require that you accept cookies in order to work properly. Generally, we use cookies for the following purposes: (1) Technical administration of the Web site (2) Customer administration (3) Finding problems with our website (technical or content related.)
