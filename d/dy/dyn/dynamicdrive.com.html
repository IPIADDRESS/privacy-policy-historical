Dynamic Drive- Privacy Statement

Dynamic Drive greatly respects your privacy. Below outlines what information is gathered from our visitors, and how they are used:

  • Dynamic Drive makes use of cookies to help in the rotation of advertisements located on the top of it's pages. Cookies are needed to prevent the displaying of the same ad to an individual multiple times.

  • The email addresses collected as a result of a user subscribing to the Dynamic Drive newsletter will only be used for it's intended purpose of delivering the newsletter to the subscriber. Under no circumstances will Dynamic Drive ever resell, redistribute, or allow third party access to the email list.

  • From time to time, Dynamic Drive will run contests/ sweepstakes. The winner(s) may be asked to supply Dynamic Drive with his/her mailing address. This information will only be used to deliver the prizes to the winner(s), and will NOT be used for any other purpose.

  • Dynamic Drive has access to it's log files, which reveal general information about the make of its' visitors (browser type, OS, referrer agent etc). This information is used to help Dynamic Drive improve it's site design to best match the needs of the visitors.

  • Dynamic Drive holds polls or surveys on it's site from time to time. By voting you are not disclosing any other information other then the answer to the question the poll asks. A vote cannot be traced to a specific individual, and hence your identity remains anonymous. This information may be used to provide advertisers with demographic information.

  • Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on your site.
     
  • Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and other sites on the Internet.
     
  • Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.
     
  • We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.
