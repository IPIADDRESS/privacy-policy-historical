Privacy Policy

Delta® Faucet Company is committed to your privacy. The following policy describes how Delta seeks to assure its Web site visitors how we protect information we receive from them.

What information does Delta collect and how do you use it?

  • We use your IP address (your server's address) to gather broad demographic information. We also may use this information to administer our Web site and to help diagnose problems with our server.
  • When you enter our contests, we ask for your name, address, city, state, ZIP code and E-mail address to administer the contest and notify winners. We do not sell or distribute the information you provide to us to other companies other than to administer the contest.
  • You will be given a choice when registering for our contests whether you would like to be on Delta's electronic mailing list, or whether you would prefer not receive e-mail from Delta.
  • We also may use the information we collect to occasionally notify you about important changes to our Web site or special offers we think you'll find valuable. If you would rather not receive this information, or would like to opt out of any other information request or distribution, simply reply to the e-mail sent to you and type UNSUBSCRIBE as the subject of your reply.
  • Delta does contract with certain third party service providers to administer and facilitate some aspects of our website and operations. These may include customer and website user surveys, contests, sweepstakes, and special offers, wherein the third party may be requesting, collecting, sorting and/or analyzing customer data on Delta’s behalf.

Does Delta disclose the information it collects from its visitors to outside parties?

Except as has already been described, Delta does not sell, trade or otherwise transfer information that visitors voluntarily provide to us. This information is used to better understand visitors' use of our site.
