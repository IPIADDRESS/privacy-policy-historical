Website Security and Privacy Policy

Deloitte & Touche is committed to ensuring that personal information you provide or that we collect about you while you visit our site is protected.

Personal information is usually collected when visitors to our website:

register for a seminar or course hosted by Deloitte & Touche
purchase a Deloitte & Touche product
request that certain material be forwarded to them
make an inquiry requesting further information from Deloitte & Touche
submit résumés
participate in surveys

The type of information we typically collect is: names, addresses, e-mail addresses, and telephone and fax numbers. If you are registering for a seminar, course or purchasing a Deloitte & Touche product over our website we may also obtain your credit card number.

Security

Deloitte & Touche has implemented technology and systems which ensure that your personal information, including your name, address and credit card number is secure and protected from misuse, loss or destruction. When you provide us with personal information, it's done through a secure server, which encrypts all information you provide before sending it to our own server. Only when it reaches the Deloitte.ca servers is your information decrypted.

Also, a limited number of Deloitte & Touche employees are authorized to have access to personal information collected through our website and these employees are required to keep the information confidential.

Privacy

Personal information collected by us is used for the specific purpose stated at the time the information is disclosed by visitors to our website. For example, the personal information is used to send Deloitte & Touche material to a visitor, to answer a visitor's question or to register the visitor for a seminar. The personal information is not used for other purposes or sold to third parties, unless indicated by Deloitte & Touche at the time the information is collected. Deloitte & Touche reserves the right to disclose personal information in the following circumstances:

to third parties who are co-hosting or assisting us with the courses or seminars for which you have registered
to third parties who deliver Deloitte & Touche publications to you
in order to comply with laws and other legal procedures
to protect the rights of Deloitte & Touche

If requested by a visitor, Deloitte & Touche will delete personal information or update inaccurate personal information. Deloitte & Touche does not accept any responsibility for the accuracy of any information provided to us by a visitor.

Deloitte & Touche reserves the right to change this website Security and Privacy Policy at any time.

Information We May Place Automatically On Your Computer's Hard Drive

When you go into the Deloitte & Touche website, we may store some information on your computer. This information will be in the form of a "Cookie" or similar file. Cookies allow us to tailor our website to better match your interests and preferences. With most internet browsers, you can erase Cookies from your computer hard drive, block all Cookies, or receive a warning before a Cookie is stored. Please refer to your browser instructions or help screen to learn more about these functions.
