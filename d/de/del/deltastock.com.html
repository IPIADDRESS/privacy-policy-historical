PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy Statement

Deltastock is committed to ensuring the privacy of its clients’ non-public personal information and to providing them with a safe and secure trading environment.

The following statement explains, how Deltastock collects and uses the non-public personal information provided by clients.

Collection of Information

To help the government fight the funding of terrorism and money laundering activities, all financial institutions are required by law to obtain, verify and record information that identifies each individual or entity that opens an account.

The information provided by clients including, but not limited to full name, residential address, date of birth, e-mail address, ID number, passport or driver's license number etc, is collected for identification purposes only, as required by law, and is kept strictly confidential!

Deltastock collects information from the following direct sources:
  • online application form;
  • account opening documents;
  • transactions made online;
  • e-mail correspondence;
  • visits to Deltastock’s website, etc.
Deltastock collects information from the following indirect sources:
  • Internet Protocol (IP) address;
  • browser type;
  • operating system;
  • Internet Service Provider (ISP);
  • time stamps;
  • transactions placed;
  • banner ads viewed, etc.
Deltastock collects information indirectly through the use of cookies, which are small text files, sent from the web server to the Internet user’s computer. Cookies provide us with operational data, which we can use to improve the user’s interaction with our web site, its navigation and usability. Deltastock uses a SSL certificate to encrypt information transmitted by or to the visitor through our Web site.

Use of Information

Deltastock uses non-public personal information in order to establish and service clients’ accounts and for security purposes. For example, Deltastock may use the personal information to open an account, create an account number, username and password, to contact clients and to verify client’s identity.

Disclosing Information

We do not disclose clients’ non-public personal information to any nonaffiliated third parties or to affiliated entities, except as permitted by law. The following instances are examples when information may be disclosed:
  • To service attorneys, accountants, auditors and other professionals with regard to the administration and servicing of an account;
  • To affiliated companies, with respect to providing clients with personal services and introducing them to future services that might be of value to them;
  • To respond to a subpoena or court order, judicial process or to regulatory authorities;
  • To protect against fraud, unauthorized transactions (such as money laundering), claims or other liabilities.

Protection of Information

Clients’ personal information is kept secure and strictly confidential, and is accessed and used only by the employees, who service the client’s account.

Deltastock uses advanced security software and technology, to ensure a safe and secure trading environment, and to protect clients’ non-public personal, financial and trading information.

Opt-Out Procedure

It is Deltastock’s policy not to disclose non-public client information to non-affiliated third parties. Under the Law, Deltastock is required to provide an opt-out procedure statement. Client is not required to supply any of the personal information that Deltastock may request. However, failure to do so may result in the company’s inability to open or maintain a client’s account or to provide services to the client.

Changes in Privacy Policy

Any changes or revisions to this privacy statement will be posted promptly on Deltastock's website and will serve as notification to clients.

Your Consent

By opening an account with Deltastock and (or) by using Deltastock web site, you give your consent to the collection and use of non-public personal information by Deltastock, as explained in this privacy statement.
