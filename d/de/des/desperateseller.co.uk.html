Right to deletion

In certain limited circumstances, you have the right to request that DesperateSeller.co.uk Ltd erase personal information relating to you that DesperateSeller.co.uk Ltd holds. This right only applies if any one of the following circumstances applies:

  • Where DesperateSeller.co.uk Ltd no longer require the personal information for the purpose(s) for which it was obtained or otherwise used.

  • Where you have exercised your right to object to the processing and there are no overriding legitimate grounds for the processing.

  • For compliance with a legal obligation requiring DesperateSeller.co.uk Ltd to process your personal information or where the processing is necessary for the performance of a task carried out in the public interest
  • For the establishment, exercise or defence of legal claims.

You have the right to request that DesperateSeller.co.uk Ltd restrict the processing of your personal information in the following circumstances:

  • Where you have contested the accuracy of the personal information. In this case the processing of the personal information will be restricted for a period which enables DesperateSeller.co.uk Ltd to verify the accuracy of the personal information in question.
