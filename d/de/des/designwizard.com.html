PRIVACY POLICY

The privacy policy for this website describes how Wavebreak Media / Design Wizard protects and makes use of the information you provide to the company when you use this website and internet services.  The information you provide will only be used in the ways described in this privacy policy. The policy also describes the use of internet cookies. The access to and the use of the Website imply that the User has read and accepts entirely the Privacy Policy and Cookies.

The latest version of the Privacy Policy is published on this page, with the revision date shown at the top. This will be updated from time to time, so we recommend you check the page regularly.  If you have any questions on the policy, please contact us. You will find company contact details on the ‘Contact Us’ page of the website.

 

Data We Gather

When you register on our website, you will be required to complete an application form containing personal information. The following are examples of the types of information we gather;

 

  • Selection of a unique username
  • Name and Job Title
  • Demographic Information, such as postal address
  • Information that may be useful in providing a better user experience or relevant content

The also gather the following types of data;

  • Internet analytic data – see section detailing Internet Cookies
  • Logs of website/services/application usage data for product/feature development

How We Use Data

Design Wizard uses data in the following ways;

  • Website/services customization based on your preferences
  • Products and service improvements
  • Internal records
  • Contacting you (using the contact details you have provided) related to changes, maintenance, queries, or promotional purposes
  • Notifications and Updates

Updates will generally be, but not limited to; new features, product releases, product maintenance and discount or promotional offers. You may opt out of such notifications through the settings in your profile if so wish. We may create usage limits and these will communicated via website notifications and/or email notifications.

We do not disclose your personal information to any 3rd party, unless required to perform a website function/service such as processing a payment or if required to by law.

 Internet Cookies

An internet cookie is a small file placed on your computer, which enables a website to identify your computer as you view different pages on the website and when you return to the website at a later time/date from the same computer.

Design Wizard uses cookies for the following reasons;

  • Identify that you are signed in to our website.
  • Store information about your preferences. This can give a better experience of the website and services based on your personal preferences.
  • Retain your preferences and auto login on return to our website. You may wish to remain logged into to the website (by not logging out) when you next return, automatically applying your personal preferences.
  • Web traffic analysis. Data from website data aggregation services (such as google analytics) helps us improve our services and provides technical data (for example, versions of internet browsers accessing the website) to our development team.

Design Wizard cookies

When logging on www.Designwizard.com, a cookie is created to keep you logged in throughout your visit.

 

When accessing www.Designwizard.com, a cookie is created and stored for one month to automatically log you back in when coming back to the website.

 

Please note that no personal information is stored in these cookies. These cookies can be manually destroyed going in your browser’s settings.  If you do not want your computer to store cookies from our website, you can disable cookies in your internet browser but please be aware that this may limit your use of the website or impact the functionality of the website.
