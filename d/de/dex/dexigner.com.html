Privacy Policy

At Dexigner we recognize that privacy is important. This Policy applies to all of the services and websites offered by Dexigner.

Collected Information

Dexigner offers services that do not require you to register for an account or provide any personal information. In order to provide our full range of services, we may collect the following types of information:

Information You Provide
When you submit inclusion requests or subscribe to the Dexigner Newsletter, we ask you for personal information such as your name and email address.

Cookies
When you visit Dexigner, we send one or more cookies - a small file containing a string of characters - to your computer that uniquely identifies your browser. We use cookies to improve the quality of our service by storing user preferences. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies, but you can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some Dexigner features and services may not function properly if your cookies are disabled.

Logs
When you use Dexigner services, our servers automatically record information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website. These server logs may include information such as your web request, IP address, browser type, browser language, the date and time of your request and one or more cookies that may uniquely identify your browser.

Methods of processing

Dexigner processes personal information on its servers.

Information Sharing

Dexigner does not share your personal information with other companies or individuals outside of Dexigner.
