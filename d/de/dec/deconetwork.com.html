Privacy Statement




Your privacy is protected

Wilcom DecoNetwork is committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology that gives you the most powerful and safe online experience. This Statement of Privacy applies to the Wilcom DecoNetwork Web site and governs data collection and usage. By using the Wilcom DecoNetwork website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Collection of your Personal Information

Wilcom DecoNetwork collects personally identifiable information, such as your e-mail address, name, home or work address or telephone number. Wilcom DecoNetwork also collects anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your ZIP code, age, gender, preferences, interests and favorites.

There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected by Wilcom DecoNetwork. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring Web site addresses. This information is used by Wilcom DecoNetwork for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the Wilcom DecoNetwork Web site.

Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally identifiable information or personally sensitive data through Wilcom DecoNetwork public message boards, this information may be collected and used by others. Note: Wilcom DecoNetwork does not read any of your private online communications.

Wilcom DecoNetwork encourages you to review the privacy statements of Web sites you choose to link to from Wilcom DecoNetwork so that you can understand how those Web sites collect, use and share your information. Wilcom DecoNetwork is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on Web sites outside of the Wilcom DecoNetwork and Wilcom DecoNetwork family of Web sites.

Use of your Personal Information

Wilcom DecoNetwork collects and uses your personal information to operate the Wilcom DecoNetwork Web site and deliver the services you have requested. Wilcom DecoNetwork also uses your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from Wilcom DecoNetwork and its affiliates. Wilcom DecoNetwork may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

Wilcom DecoNetwork does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. Wilcom DecoNetwork may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. In those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (e-mail, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party. In addition, Wilcom DecoNetwork may share data with trusted partners to help us perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to Wilcom DecoNetwork, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

Wilcom DecoNetwork does not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations, without your explicit consent.

Wilcom DecoNetwork keeps track of the Web sites and pages our customers visit within Wilcom DecoNetwork, in order to determine what Wilcom DecoNetwork services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within Wilcom DecoNetwork to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

Wilcom DecoNetwork Web sites will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Wilcom DecoNetwork or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Wilcom DecoNetwork; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Wilcom DecoNetwork, or the public.

Use of Cookies

The Wilcom DecoNetwork Web site use "cookies" to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.

One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalize Wilcom DecoNetwork pages, or register with Wilcom DecoNetwork site or services, a cookie helps Wilcom DecoNetwork to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses, and so on. When you return to the same Wilcom DecoNetwork Web site, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the Wilcom DecoNetwork features that you customized.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the Wilcom DecoNetwork services or Web sites you visit.

Security of your Personal Information

Wilcom DecoNetwork secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. Wilcom DecoNetwork secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to other Web sites, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol.
