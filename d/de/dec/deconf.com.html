Privacy Policy for deconf.com

If you require more information or have any questions about my privacy policy, feel free to contact me by email or using my contact form.

At deconf.com, the privacy of my visitors is of extreme importance to me. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by deconf.com and how it is used.

Log Files and Web Analytics

Like many other Web sites, deconf.com makes use of log files and web analytics services. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages and number of clicks. I use these stats and web analytics services to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movements around the site and gather demographic and interests information. IP addresses and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons

deconf.com does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences and user-specific actions, to customize a webpage based on visitor’s browser type or to store information that the visitor sends via their browser.
