Privacy

The Department of Education and Training does not collect personal information about you when you visit this Site.

The Department's internet service provider may however collect certain non personal information about you, for statistical purposes only. This may include information relating to the traffic and browsing activities of users of the website. This informs The Department about how people use the website and assists to keep improving information quality.

The Department will make no attempt to identify users or their browsing activities, except as required by law.

The Department will record your email address if you provide it, but will only use it for the purpose for which it was provided. It will not be added to a mailing list without your consent.
