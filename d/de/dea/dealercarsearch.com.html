Privacy & Cookie Policy

This Privacy Policy serves to inform you of the data gathered on dealercarsearch.com (“Website”) and your rights as a user. By use of this Website you agree to the [Terms of Use,] the Cookie Policy, and this Privacy Policy. Definitions in this Privacy Policy have the same definition as they do in the [Terms of Use.]

Personal Data
Dealer Car Search must collect certain identifiable personal data in order to offer its business services. This information includes: Customer Name, Physical Address, Phone Number, Email Address, and other information necessary for its services. By providing this information, you consent to Dealer Car Search storing and using the information to provide the services described on this Website.

Dealer Car Search also collects non identifiable personal data for the purpose of analyzing user interaction with the Website. This information may include information about your device, information about your browser, your IP Address, or other relevant information. Dealer Car Search uses Google Analytics to collect and present data for the purpose of site improvement. To see how Google uses data when you use our Website, please visit www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners

User accounts and individual dealer websites are accessible with the username and unique password associated with that username. Do not choose a password associated with any other account and do not disclose your unique password to others.

Dealer Car Search uses the information provided to better serve its customers. Dealer Car Search reserves the right to use the information as permissible by law. Disclosure to these business partners will only occur if necessary to provide the services offered to the customer.

Dealer Car Search may sell some or all of its assets in a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy or other transaction. In such an event, personal data may be part of that transaction.

Dealer Car Search will disclose information as required by law in response to a court order or subpoena.

Security
Dealer Car Search implements technical, administrative, and physical security to protect all user information. This Website utilizes secure socket layer (SSL) encryption to protect transmitted data. No method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is perfectly secure and the absolute security of personal information cannot be guaranteed.
