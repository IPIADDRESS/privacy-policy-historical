What's "OEM"?
OEM stands for Original Equipment Manufacturer. OEM products are built for companies that assemble computers. OEM products may come without a box, instructions, cables, or other items found in retail kits. (OEM CPUs, for example, may exclude the heat sink and cooling fan.)
