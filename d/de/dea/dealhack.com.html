Dealhack does not collect personally identifiable information from users unless such information is specifically and knowingly provided during email communication, "contact us" forms, surveys or contests.

If you would like to email us your feedback or success story but would like it to remain confidential, please express your intention clearly in your email.

Cookies

Non-personal information and data will automatically be collected through the standard operation of our web servers and through the use of "cookies." Cookies are small bits of text data that web browsers store based on requests from web servers. They allow us to recognize repeat users and facilitate your use of the site. Also, our cookies allow us to track usage behavior and compile aggregate data for content improvement, targeted advertising, and marketing and promotional purposes.

Some of our business partners use cookies on our site (for example, advertisers). Please be aware that Dealhack cannot control the use of cookies or the resulting information that is collected by those advertisers or other third parties through the use of cookies. Most browsers allow a user to deny cookies; however, please note that authorizing the use of cookies may be required to provide full access and optimal interaction with certain web site features.

Dealhack provides links to other websites over which we have no control. If you follow those links you become subject to the privacy policy of the site you are visiting.

Third Party Advertising

Dealhack posts ads on the site that are delivered to users directly by Google, LinkShare and Commission Junction. Those companies collect information about users' visits to this site, such as number of times they have viewed an ad (but not user name, address, or other personal information). They use that information to decide which ads to show users of Dealhack. For more information the cookies used by these sites and how to "opt-out", please review Google's, LinkShare's and Commission Junction's privacy policies. Please understand we have no access to or control over how those sites use cookies.

We do not provide information about Dealhack users to other organizations or to people not employed by Dealhack. However, if we are required by law to provide personal information about our users to local, state or federal authorities, we will do so.

You are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy from time to time to ensure that you are aware of any changes.
