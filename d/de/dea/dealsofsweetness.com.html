About Me

My name is Emilee Roberts, and I’m the owner, writer, and creator of both Deals of Sweetness and Pea of Sweetness. I’m happily married to my best friend, Joey, who is a 2x cancer survivor and a retired Veteran. I’m a full-time, homeschooling mom to our 3 special needs kids who amaze, inspire, and humble me every day! We have an Italian Mastiff, Maine Coon cat and 2 gerbils. Here at Deals of Sweetness I want to help you save green while going green by sharing frugal eco-friendly tips as well as deals, product reviews and giveaways!
