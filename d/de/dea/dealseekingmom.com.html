Deal Seeking Mom’s privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.

I will not collect any information from children under 18.

There are occasions when I will ask for personal information, such as when you enter a giveaway.
