EITC PRIVACY POLICY

Emirates Integrated Telecommunication Company recognises the importance of compliance with any and all United Arab Emirates laws concerning the privacy rights of its customers, who are visiting its web site. For this reason EITC has adopted the present privacy policy. EITC’s Privacy Policy addresses the EITC practices related to information collection, use and disclosure of personal information.

Information Collection, Use and Disclosure

EITC does not collect personal information about you, when you visit EITC’s web site, unless otherwise specified, or unless you choose to provide this information to us, thereby consenting to the use of such information. However, we collect and store (i) the IP address, from which you access the internet, (ii) the date and time you access the EITC website, and (iii) the web pages you visit, recorded by the text and graphics files that compose the web page, automatically.

We use this information only for statistical and monitoring purposes and do not disclose it to third parties, unless required by law.

Changes to EITC’s Privacy Policy

EITC reserves the right to change its privacy policy upon its sole discretion at any time and to upload the actual version in force to its web site.
