Privacy Policy When you register at the DCS Web site, we respect your privacy. DCS will never give your user information to anyone unless you specifically tell us you want us to. The information you provide us is used to help personalize our advice and information.

Your name, telephone number and e-mail address is used only for mailing you free brochures, videos, latest tips and events or provide you with free consultation for your skin health program. This information will not be given to any third party or published anywhere without your written consent.

On our registration form you can select whether or not you want to receive e-mail from us. If at any time you wish to be taken off the DCS e-mail list, please click here to unsubscribe with the word "remove" as the only word on the first line of the body of the message. If you need to contact a human to be taken off the mailing list, please send mail at info@dubaicosmeticsurgery.com
