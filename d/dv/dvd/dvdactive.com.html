Privacy Statement


DVDActive have created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for DVDActive.

Information Automatically Logged


We use your IP address to monitor traffic movement through our sites, your IP address isn't normally used to specifically identify you.

Cookies


DVDActive uses cookies to make sure you do not see the same advertisement repeatedly. Cookies may also be used by DVDActive as a means of remembering your DVD region, thereby not requiring you to click on your region on every visit. DVDActive uses cookies to make it easier for you to post messages and to help you keep track of messages that you have already viewed. Cookies may be used on the site as a means of tracking viewers in a non-specific manor to establish how users use our site.

Advertisers


DVDActive mostly use outside advertisement companies to display advertisements. These advertisements may contain cookies and other modern technologies that are secure. Cookies are also used by our own advertisement system to allow advertisers to determine how well a particular campaign has done. While DVDActive uses cookies in other parts of the website, cookies received with banner advertisements are usually only collected by the advertisement company, and DVDActive does not have access to this information.

Surveys


DVDActive's online surveys may ask visitors for demographic information (like postal code, age, or income level). This information will not be used to uniquely identify users. Instead, DVDActive uses this data to tailor our visitor's experience at our sites, showing them content that we think they might be interested in, and displaying the content according to their preferences.

External Links


DVDActive may contain links to other sites. DVDActive is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites. For more information on this topic, please consult the disclaimer.

Special Relationships


DVDActive attempts to form relationships with other web sites, and such relationships can change regularly. If you would like more information, then contact us via email. Such relationships do not include the trading of user details.

Public Forums


DVDActive makes chat rooms, forums, message boards, and/or commenting systems available to our users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. Similarly DVDActive cannot be held responsible for any information that is posted to these services, even though we may decide to remove it.

Security


DVDActive has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. All information stored about users is backed up regularly, and the information stored online is protected.

Choice/Opt-Out


Our site provides users with the opportunity to opt-out of receiving promotional/marketing information from us. You can send an email to support@dvdactive.com or follow the details that are provided in the appropriate emails relating to unsubscribing.

Data Quality/Access


DVDActive gives users the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided: you can send email to support@dvdactive.com

P3P


We provide P3P machine readable files to help your web browser make privacy related decisions automatically.

Dispute Resolution


Disputes will be handled by our customer service representative. Our customer service will promptly correct any errors.

Contacting DVDActive


If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of DVDActive, or your dealings with DVDActive, then you can contact: support@dvdactive.com
