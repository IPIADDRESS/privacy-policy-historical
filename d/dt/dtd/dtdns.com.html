Privacy Statement

The following privacy statement discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this web site.

Effective Date: April 12, 2002

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. IP addresses associated with hostnames and domains are also used in the DNS protocol, and will be publicly available to the Internet community. Since this is an IP redirection service, IP addresses may also be used in statistical tracking.

Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information (like their name and email address) and demographic information (like their zip code, age, or income level). The customer's contact information is used primarily to contact the visitor when necessary. Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site. A member's name and e-mail address may be available in the system's WHOIS records, as it would be as if they had registered a full domain name. A member may opt to conseal their e-mail address from being displayed in WHOIS lookups.

DtDNS does not sell mailing lists or otherwise share membership data with anyone (except as noted above) unless required by law.

This site contains links to other sites. DtDNS is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.


Public Forums

This site makes forums, message boards, and/or mailing lists available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.


Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. All data is backed up in the case of an emergency or misuse.


Choice/Opt-Out

Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us and our partners at the point where we request information about the visitor. We also offer an option in the Account Profile to toggle the option to receive notices. Sometimes important information regarding the operation of DtDNS and its services may be sent to ALL users, regardless of the profile settings.

This site gives users the option for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service by using the Cancel Account option in the profile manager.

This site gives users the option of changing and modifying information previously provided by modifying it in the profile manager.
