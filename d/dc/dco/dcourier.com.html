1. HOW WE COLLECT YOUR INFORMATION

When you use the Site, we collect information, including personal information, when you provide it directly to us and passively through technology such as “cookies.” Below is a more detailed explanation of the categories of information that we may collect.

Information you provide:

Use of the Site: We collect any information that you choose to provide when you browse the Site. For example, if you subscribe to a newsletter, we may collect your first and last name and email address. We also collect the information you provide when you contact us through the Site.  You are not required to provide information in order to browse the Site. However, even if you do not provide any information directly, we still collect information from you as described in the "information collected automatically" section.

Profile information: We collect any information you provide when you choose to create a profile on the Site, such as your first and last name, email address, the image you provide as your avatar, gender, personal website URL, occupation, location, hometown and biographical information you add to your profile. If you would like your comments on the Site to be attributed to your real name, we will need to collect information to verify your identity, including your first and last name, ZIP code, gender, birth year and telephone number.

Subscriptions: To use some of our Services, you will need to create an account and purchase a subscription. We will collect information, such as your first and last name, email address, telephone number, physical address, payment card information and transaction history.

Your submissions: If you choose to comment on any of the content on the Site, we will collect any information that you submit. We also collect any information you provide in conjunction with submitting a news tip; ad for the classified section; birth, engagement, wedding, or anniversary announcement; or event. This may include your first and last name, email address, telephone number, details of the announcement, any images that you upload or the title, description, location and cost of any event. Unless otherwise noted, the information you submit for publication will be publicly accessible or may become publicly accessible, so please exercise caution before submitting any personal information.

Offline services: Some of the Sites may offer offline services, such as sign or billboard advertising. In conjunction with providing those services, we collect information such as your first or last name, email address, payment card information, and information you provide regarding the offline services that you are requesting or seeking additional information about.

Technical support: If you experience any problems with the Site or Services and choose to contact us for technical support, we may need to collect personal information in order to assist you, such as your name, telephone number and email address.

Any other information you provide: We collect any other information that you choose to provide, such as any of your questions, comments or suggestions and our responses. 

Information collected automatically:  

Usage information: We collect certain information automatically through your use of the Site, such as which of the pages on our Site that you access, the frequency of access and what you click on while on our Site.

Device information: We collect information about the device you are using to access the Site, such as hardware model, operating system, application version number, browser and IP addresses.

Mobile device information: In addition to the device information listed above, when you access our Site via a browser on your mobile device, we may collect mobile network information including telephone number, the unique device identifier assigned to your device, mobile carrier, operating system and other device attributes.

Location information: When you use the Site, we may collect information about your actual location. We use various technologies to determine location, including IP address, GPS and other sensors that may, for example, provide us with information on nearby devices, Wi-Fi access points and cell towers.
