PRIVACY POLICY / GDPR

DIARY directory is an online information service / database for fashion, beauty and lifestyle media companies and individuals – including PRs, brands, influencers, journalists, media titles. Our aim is to help these industry professionals communicate with each other in a targeted manner through the provision of information such as preferred contact information, areas of interest, social media stats. Our aim is to be completely transparent and accessible with listing owners regarding the contact information we are publishing for them.
You can contact us regarding the details we have for you on info@diaryd.com / 020 7724 7770. 

DIARY directory Limited (DIARYdirectory.com) takes seriously our responsibilities regarding the security and privacy of the data we list. We work to ensure that everyone in the DIARY directory database knows that they are in the database, and with what details; knows the purpose of the database; and that we are easily contactable with requests for changes or removal.

This privacy policy applies to our use of any and all Data collected by us or provided by you in relation to your use of the Website. Please read this privacy policy carefully. We will keep this page updated.  

The DIARY directory site is used mainly, but not exclusively, by fashion, beauty and lifestyle press, PRs, marketing departments and associated industries (including but not limited to freelance photographers, representation agencies, industry bodies).

  • DIARY directory Limited, or us: DIARY directory Limited, a company incorporated in England and Wales with registered number 07785698 whose registered office is at 102 Crawford Street, London, W1H 2HR;

  • Website: the website that you are currently using, https://www.diarydirectory.com, and any sub-domains of this site unless expressly excluded by their own terms and conditions.

This privacy policy applies only to the actions of DIARY directory Limited and Users with respect to this Website. It does not extend to any websites that can be accessed from this Website including, but not limited to, any links we may provide to social media websites.

Within this policy, "including" is understood to mean "including without limitation"

Information held

Any direct contact details and notes included within DIARY directory are done so with the aim of facilitating targeted communication, allowing industry professionals to contact each other and work together – providing contact details for preferred method(s) of contact, and areas of interest and notes to help other industry professionals be targeted / relevant with the information sent / requests. Your contact information is made available to DIARY directory subscribers only. We will retain your details for as long as you are deemed to be working within, and to be relevant to, the industry.  Processing of your data is necessary for the legitimate interests pursued by the controller or a third party.

We may collect Data incuding the following:

  • Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Gender
  • Job Title
  • Profession
  • Areas of editorial interest for media contacts, creatives and industryINFLUENCERS – that our service might better direct relevant connections
  • Contact Information such as email addresses and telephone numbers
  • Social media handles and data including follower numbers, some engagement stats (industryINFLUENCERS section only) and imagery (industryINFLUENCERS section only)
  • IP address (automatically collected)
  • Web browser type and version (automatically collected)
  • Operating system (automatically collected)
  • A list of URLs starting with a referring site, your activity on this Website, and the site you exit to (automatically collected)
  • in each case, in accordance with this privacy policy.

Additionally:

  • Login data used by members accessing DIARY directory, including IP addresses, times of login
  • Transaction data regarding payments made for DIARY directory services
  • No financial data is collected or stored by DIARY directory – all financial data is collected via GoCardless or Stripe

How we collect your data

  • DIARY directory collects and verifies all listed data, mainly contact details for professionals working within the fashion, beauty and lifestyle media and marketing industries.
  • The data is mainly provided directly by you / your press office / your team, or from publicly available sources including your website, blog or your public social media profiles including LinkedIn.
  • We do not buy data from third party sources.
  • DIARY directory has a policy to send back a 'front-end profile page link' to a contact at all company entries upon their listing’s creation or following amendment of their details.
  • For company entries, we accept edits and confirmation from team members on behalf of the team (usually on receipt of an email from the same domain as the listing).
  • When adding or editing personal entries (such as freelancers), the listee is made aware of the purpose of their entry in DIARY directory, has a link to verify it, and a clear way to request any changes, or the removal of the entry.
  • We do not add personal details (including home addresses, mobile numbers, personal email addresses) to the DIARY directory database without informed consent of the data owner.
  • Dated back end notes regarding changes made to the listings, and on whose authority, are kept within the database, plus a record of correspondence regarding listings.
  • Requests for listing edits or removal, or a request to see details listed for a company / individual, can be made at any time to any DIARY directory team member, or to info@diaryd.com, and we undertake to react promptly and openly.
  • We aim to contact every entry in the DIARY directory database regularly to let them know they are listed and provide an opportunity to see the listing / request full details. 
  • Our regular mailers have clear unsubscribe links. If people unsubscribe from a mailer checking their details, we contact them individually to verify whether they would like to remain in the database. All email bouncebacks from all mailers are checked and amendments / removals made accordingly.

How your information is used

For purposes of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) DIARY directory Limited is the "data controller".

Any or all of the above Data may be required by us from time to time in order to provide you with the best possible service and experience when using our Website. Specifically, Data may be used by us for the following reasons:

  • In order to provide and improve our service to our members, with the aim of facilitating targeted, relevant and efficient communication within the industry.
  • Internal record keeping - including to make notes regarding the DIARY directory team’s interaction with you regarding the data we hold for you, and any amends / confirmations.
  • To manage your DIARY directory membership
  • To create a profile page for you in the DIARY directory industry database – accessible by our members. Your profile is also available to you at any time through a dedicated ‘owner link’ which is only supplied to the direct email address associated with your listing
  • Sending targeted press releases on behalf of selected clients via our DIARY delivers service. All mailings include an unsubscribe link.
  • DIARY directory members may also create their own lists with your data included - all DIARY directory members are reminded to use your data in accordance with GDPR.
  • DIARY directory mailers we may send to you include:
     
    • the DIARY daily news – sent to all members as part of the provided service, and to all those who sign up through our site / our team to request receipt. Recipients may unsubscribe at any time through the link on each mailer
    • New industry vacancy alerts – sent to recipients who have signed up through our site / requested to be added by the team. Recipients may unsubscribe at any time through the link on each mailer
    • Checking mailer – in order to keep your data accurate, we undertake to contact you at least once a year to allow you to review your data that is made available to our members, and to request removal / changes
    • Occasional mailers to our members, for example to let you know about new services now available to you
    • Occasional marketing / market research mailers to promote and improve our service.
