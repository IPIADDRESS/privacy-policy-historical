Privacy Policy

At Diabetes.co.uk, we are committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology that gives you the most powerful, safe, online experience that you can get anywhere.

Privacy Codes of Conduct

Because your privacy is important to us, Diabetes.co.uk sites will operate by the following Code of Conduct.

By using this site, you agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy. Whenever you submit information via this site, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of that information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

  • Diabetes.co.uk sites will ask when they need information that personally identifies you or allows them to contact you ("Personal Information"). Generally this information is requested when registering for a site, when requesting a particular service, or when entering a competition. When possible, Diabetes.co.uk will provide you with the means to make sure that your Personal Information is correct and current.
  • Diabetes.co.uk sites and their necessary service partners use your Personal Information to operate the sites and services and to inform you of new features, services, and products from Diabetes.co.uk and its affiliates. Some Diabetes.co.uk sites may also carefully select other companies to send you information about their products or services which are related to the site's service but are not necessary to its operation (a "Secondary Use").
  • If a Diabetes.co.uk site intends to use Personal Information for a Secondary Use (such as surveys, feedback, customer service), the Diabetes.co.uk site will provide you with instructions on how to decline this service. You may also stop the delivery of informational or promotional mail from Diabetes.co.uk sites by following the instructions on the mail.
  • Diabetes.co.uk is primarily aimed at adults. Diabetes.co.uk sites that are primarily directed at children will not use Personal Information without obtaining the consent of a parent or guardian. When possible, these Diabetes.co.uk sites will provide e-mail notice to parents that children have provided Personal Information to the site.
  • Diabetes.co.uk is owned and operated by Sitefinders Net Ltd. If at any time you believe that a Diabetes.co.uk site has not adhered to this Code of Conduct, please notify Diabetes.co.uk by e-mail at Privacy@Diabetes.co.uk and we will use all commercially reasonable efforts to promptly determine and correct the problem.
