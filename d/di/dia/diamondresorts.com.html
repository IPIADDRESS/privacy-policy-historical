Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which Diamond Resorts International, Inc. and its subsidiaries ("Diamond") collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from you though the use of this website ("Site"). This Privacy Policy applies to the Site and all products and services offered by Diamond.

- What information is held by Diamond.
- How the information will be used.

- What safeguards are in place to protect your information.
- Other information about your privacy.

If you are a Diamond owner or member, own a timeshare interest at a Diamond-managed resort, or reserve accommodations at our resorts as a non-owner or non-member, you will provide your name, address, telephone number and e-mail address as part of our registration process to create a unique login name and password. This information is collected on the registration form for several reasons including: (i) personal identification; (ii) to complete reservations, save points and make payments; (iii) to allow us to contact you for customer service, if necessary; and (iv) to customize the content of our Site to meet your specific needs. In addition, your e-mail address is collected to send you an e-mail message confirming your new registration and each savings request, payment or reservation you transact online.  As an owner, member or guest you may occasionally receive updates from us about discounted availability, special offers, new services and products which may be of interest to you and other noteworthy news items.

This information may be provided by letter, e-mail, telephone or any other reasonable means of communication. If you do not want to receive these updates, in addition to other unsubscribe options, you may also notify us at:

In the United States or Canada by writing to our Customer Service Department at 10600 W. Diamond may sponsor sweepstakes and contests to give owners/members and non-owners/non-members the opportunity to win great travel prizes. Information collected by Diamond for such contests may include contact information and survey questions.

Diamond values opinions and comments from owners, members and guests so we frequently conduct online surveys. These surveys are entirely optional for all owners, members and guests. Diamond collects IP addresses to help diagnose problems with our servers and for system administration.

Like many other websites, our servers automatically record information about your IP address, which ads you click on, the page that directed you to our Site, and the browser type used while accessing our Site. A server log stores the information collected so it can be analyzed.

Diamond takes compliance with its privacy and data protection obligations seriously and intends that its corporate Privacy Policy will materially comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations in the different jurisdictions in which we do business.  In the event of cross-border transfer from the EU/UK or Switzerland to the United States, Diamond will follow mechanisms approved by the United States, the European Union, Switzerland and the United Kingdom for such transfers.  These mechanisms may include (i) the EU–U.S. Privacy Shield (or other similar framework), (ii) the Swiss-U.S. Privacy Shield (or other similar framework), or (iii) standard Model Contract Clauses approved by the European Commission (or other similar framework).  Diamond adheres to the  Privacy Shield Principles of Notice, Choice, Accountability for Onward Transfer, Security, Data Integrity and Purpose Limitation, Access, and Recourse, Enforcement and Liability.

We may collect information from you that is not PII such as the date and time you visited the Site, the web browser you used, and the page you visited that directed you to our Site. This individual information is collected and analyzed to determine how you found out about us, your interests in our products and services, and how to improve our Site. We may also collect information from your activities on the Site and other websites for the purpose of generating advertisements according to your interests and preferences. This may result in our ads being displayed on participating websites based on your browsing activity. This is achieved by allowing third party advertising companies to access things like cookies, IP addresses, and server logs.

Some of the individual information collected may be combined with your PII to further enhance your experience while on the Site.

Owners, Members, Non-Members and Non-Owners can choose to provide Diamond with credit-card information. This information is primarily used to assist in making payments quickly without having to type in the same information repeatedly.
