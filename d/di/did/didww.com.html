    DIDWW Privacy Policy

     

    DIDWW has created this privacy policy in order safeguard the privacy of users of our services. By using our site and services you are consenting to the practices set forth in this Privacy Policy.

     

    DIDWW collects personal and financial information in order to allow users to register for, purchase, and use our services. This information includes your name, address, phone number and email address, and financial details such as credit card information.

     

    In order to protect sensitive data DIDWW uses industry standard technologies such as SSL for transactions with users, to encrypt information so it cannot be read as the information passes over the internet.

     

    DIDWW uses financial information only in order to authorize payments and bill users for products and services. This includes fraud protection and credit risk reduction. When placing an order on the site, the user consent is given to provide financial data to thirds parties necessary to process the transaction between user and DIDWW.

     

    We may disclose your personal and financial information when needed in compliance with laws and regulations, to assist law enforcement, to enforce the terms and conditions of under which users use our services and to protect the rights, property and safety of DIDWW or others.
