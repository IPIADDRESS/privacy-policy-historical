This Privacy Policy applies only to information you provide to us through the Site. If you choose to use our services or otherwise enter into an agreement with us, the collection, use, and disclosure of any information provided to us under the agreement between us will be governed by that agreement.

Information Collected

Our privacy policy is not to collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information, except as described below.

  • You may choose to contact us through the Site to receive more information about our services or job openings. For service information, we ask for your full name, email address, company name and phone number and we collect and store such information if you choose to provide it. For job openings, we ask for your full name, resume, email address, past company name, phone number, social profile links, cover letter or other voluntary extra information, gender, age, and veteran status and we collect and store such information if you choose to provide it. Contacting us is voluntary, and we send emails only to those who first contact us.
  • In order to improve our services and learn about how users interact with our Site, we may automatically collect information such as your browser type, operating system, Internet Protocol (IP) address (a number that is automatically assigned to your computer when you use the Internet, which may vary from session to session), and/or a date/time stamp for your visit. Some of this information may be collected using cookies and stored in log files. “Cookies” are small pieces of information that a website sends to your computer’s hard drive while you are viewing a website. We may use both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them). Persistent cookies can be removed by following your browser’s directions. If you choose to disable cookies, some areas of our Site may not work properly.

You can always opt out of receiving emails from us by emailing support@ditto.com.

You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer of us or our assets may continue to use your information as set forth in this policy.
