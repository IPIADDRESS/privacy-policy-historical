Upon your request, we will close your account and remove your personal information from view as soon as reasonably possible, based on your account activity and in accordance with applicable law. You can delete or deactivate your Dibzees account at any time. When you delete your membership, your personal data is removed from our database unless we are required to save the data. As a rule it takes two to three business days until your information is completely removed from all records.

Dibzees uses so-called “cookies”, which are files that are stored on your computer that can be retrieved to assist in customizing your experience with the online service.
