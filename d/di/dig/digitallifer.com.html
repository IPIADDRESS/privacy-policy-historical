Collection of Information

Accepting and obeying all provisions of this privacy policy is a requirement for all visitors who wish to visit and use our site. No personal information is ever covertly taken from users. All information can only be taken with the user’s expressed consent.

We do not use information from our users to track their Internet browsing, and any user can request that their information be removed from our database by following the instructions below.

Cookies

We use cookies to better understand how users interact with our site, which helps us to improve the site’s user experience. Cookies are small files placed by your Web browser onto the hard drive of your computer. Data from these cookies presents us with aggregate information that can help us to optimize content displays, improve design functionality and deliver nonspecific demographic data to advertisers and business partners. We under no circumstances will use information from cookies to track users or draw additional information from them.

Visitors may set their Web browsers to reject cookies if they wish, but some features of the site may not work as intended unless cookies are enabled. We are not responsible for the sites functionality and use when cookies are disabled in the browser.

Third Party Links

Our site may host advertisements and links from outside entities including advertisers and business partners. These entities necessarily have their own privacy policies governing what they do with information gathered from their Web sites and landing pages, should the user follow links to them.

Information to Third Parties

We do not give out information our users provide to third-party entities, regardless of whether they are advertisers or business partners. The sole exception to this policy is when giving your information is required by law, or at the request of legal authorities. We are necessarily obligated to work with the law to resolve any legal matters and protect our users.

Our Security

We take Web security very seriously and protect our users with anti virus and encryption software. We aim to allow our users to be safe and at ease at all times while using the site.

Changes to our Privacy Policy

Changes to our privacy policy will be prominently displayed on this page. Changes can and may occur at any time.

Opting Out
