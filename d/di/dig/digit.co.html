Cookies may store your preferences and other information but cannot read data off your hard disk or read cookie files created by other sites.

Cookies set by a website owner (in this case, Digit) are called ‚Äúfirst party cookies‚Äù. Cookies set by parties other than the website owner are called ‚Äúthird party cookies‚Äù.
