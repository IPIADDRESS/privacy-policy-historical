At Dignity Memorial Network, Inc., we believe in protecting your right to privacy. Protecting your personal information is important to us, and we created this privacy policy to clearly explain how the information gathered on this site will be used.

The information we collect on our site will never be sold or distributed to any person that is not an employee of Dignity Memorial Network, Inc. or one of its affiliates. Any contact information that you provide to us will never be shared with third parties other than service providers who are part of the Dignity Memorial network of funeral and cemetery providers.  In order to improve the helpfulness of our site, we use the information you provide to respond to inquiries and to send information that has been requested. 

When you submit contact information through our site, you are agreeing to be added to our mailing list to receive periodic correspondences about products and services we can offer. If your wish to stop these correspondences, you will have the option to opt out of this list at any time.
