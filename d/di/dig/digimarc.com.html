Privacy Policy

 

Digimarc Corporation is committed to a Privacy Policy that provides users of Digimarc web properties and related web-based services assurance that their personal information is treated with the appropriate respect.

For further information, please read the Privacy Policy below.

This statement discloses the privacy practices for the Digimarc web properties and related web-based services. If you have questions or concerns regarding this statement, you should first contact the webmaster via our online form. For additional concerns, please contact Digimarc Corporation or call toll-free +1 800 344 4627.

Subscription Registration Program
Photographers, web designers, and other people and organizations involved in image-processing can subscribe to digital watermark embedding and related services through the Digimarc Corporate website and its associated sites by obtaining a Digimarc ID. In order to sign up for a Digimarc ID under the Digimarc Watermark Subscription Registration Program, you must first complete the registration form. During registration you are required to give your contact information (such as name, country and email address) and, if a fee is required, payment information (such as credit card number and expiration date). This information is used to contact you about the services on our site for which you have expressed interest, for billing purposes and to obtain your feedback. It is optional for you to provide demographic information (such as profession, digital camera ownership, embedding tool used) and unique identifiers (such as URL linked to Digimarc). You may update your information in the Member subscription area.

Administrative Email Communications
If you choose to subscribe to the web-based services Digimarc offers, the company may send administrative emails (e.g., providing information on system maintenance) to you, from time to time. As long as you are a registrant of our for-pay services, you will receive these administrative emails. These emails contain important information about the service. We communicate with you to provide requested services and in regards to issues relating to your account via email or phone. As these e-mails are used to provide service-related information, in order to stop receiving administrative emails, you must cancel your subscription to Digimarc for-pay services.

Contests
Digimarc Corporation may run contests and promotions on our web properties in which we ask visitors for contact information (such as their email address). We use this information to contact the winners and to send visitors information about our company. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings from Digimarc Corporate website and its associated properties; see the opt-out section below. Personal information on contest participants, including name, email, or likeness is not disclosed on the Digimarc Corporate website and its associated properties, nor is said information distributed to third parties. Contestants should read the specific rules of each contest for more information.

Newsletter/Opt-Out
You are given the opportunity to opt-out of receiving e-mail regarding new products and features from Digimarc, promotional offers, and information from our partners. If you no longer wish to receive promotional materials or News Alerts, you may opt-out of receiving these communications by referencing the unsubscribe information listed in a particular e-mail, sending a request to unsubscribe (referencing the particular newsletter) to info@digimarc.com or contacting Digimarc Corporation by phone at +1 800 344 4627.

Security
This website takes precautions to protect our users' information. When you submit sensitive information via the website, we endeavor to protect your information both online and off-line. When our registration/order form asks you to enter sensitive information (such as credit card number), that information is encrypted and is protected with encryption software known as Secure Sockets Layer.

While we use Secure Sockets Layer encryption to protect sensitive information online, we also endeavor to secure user-information off-line by limiting internal access to such information on a need to know basis. Furthermore, the servers on which we store personally identifiable information on are kept in a secure environment, which is only accessible by specific employees of Digimarc Corporation.

Links
Digimarc web properties and related web-based services contain links to other sites. Please be aware that Digimarc is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such other sites, including sites owned by companies that have chosen to partner with Digimarc Corporation. We try to make you aware when you are leaving our site. Furthermore, we encourage you to read the privacy statements of each and every website that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by the Digimarc web properties and related web-based services.

Cookies
We use cookies to store information during and between visits to our site. Cookies are a mechanism used to store information about a visitor on that user's particular machine. web browsers store cookies so that the browser itself can remember some information and then, on request, pass all that information up to the server at one time. At the user's discretion, Digimarc web properties and related web-based services may use cookies to remember your user name and password, and maintain "state" during use of certain portions of the site. Portions of our website require the use of cookies. Please note, turning off cookies or rejecting the Digimarc.com cookie will prevent you from utilizing the ImageBridge signup or ImageBridge user pages. At no time do cookies pass information, or become available to, third party web pages outside of Digimarc web properties and related web-based services.

Log Files
When you visit our website, we collect information about your visit. We collect your IP address, browser type, domain name, and we log the length of time of your visit and the number of times you visit our site. This information is used in the aggregate for site monitoring and improvements. This information is not personally identifiable and it does not contain personal information like name, email address.

Sharing of Information
Digimarc web properties and related web-based services contain several forms which gather user information. Unless otherwise specified, or the user chooses to opt out, some contact and demographic information is shared with marketing partners and corporate customers who use our services, such as hit counts by geography (zip code). Contact information (e.g., email address) for those who opt in during registration is shared with business partners, corporate customers and third parties interested in informational or promotional mailing to our registered user base.

Intermediaries
E-Commerce transactions on portions of Digimarc.com pass user information through intermediary service providers. Specifically, we process credit card verification transactions using PayPal credit card verification service. Information collected by intermediaries is used solely for the purpose of verifying payment information and is not distributed.

Feedback on Privacy Concerns
If you would like to express concerns or complaints regarding Digimarc Corporation privacy practices, please send email to info@digimarc.com.

Legal Disclaimer
Personally Identifiable Information and/or Third Party Personally Identifiable Information provided to Digimarc is subject to disclosure pursuant to judicial or other government subpoenas, warrants, or orders.

Digimarc reserves the right to change its privacy policy. If we change our privacy practices, the changes will not relate to previously collected data. If we make any material changes in our privacy practices that do not affect user information already stored in our database, we will post a prominent notice on our website notifying users of the change. In some cases where we post the notice we will also email users, who have opted to receive communications from us, notifying them of the changes in our privacy practices.

About Digimarc Corporation

Digimarc Corporation (NASDAQ: DMRC), based in Beaverton, Oregon, is a leading supplier of secure identity and media management solutions. Digimarc provides products and services that enable the production of more than 60 million personal identification documents, including two-thirds of U.S. driver licenses and IDs for more than 25 countries. Digimarc digital watermarking technology provides a persistent digital identity for various media content and is used to enhance the security of financial documents, identity documents and digital images, and support other media rights management applications.

 
Commercial Solutions

Using our patented digital watermarking technology, Digimarc provides security and brand enhancing solutions for corporations and individuals. Digimarc-inspired solutions can be found in hundreds of millions of images, media files, and printed materials around the world.
