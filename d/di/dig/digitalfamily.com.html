How Artesian Media uses the information it gathers/tracks

Artesian Media uses any information voluntarily provided by our users to enhance their experience in our network of sites, whether to provide interactive or personalized elements on the sites or to better prepare future content based on the interests of our users.

If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please e-mail us at info@artesianmedia.com


We use tracking information to determine which areas of our sites users like and don't like, based on traffic to those areas. Artesian Media does not track what each user reads, but rather how well each page performs overall. This helps us to continue to build a better service for you.

Artesian Media creates aggregate reports on user demographics and traffic patterns for advertisers and partners. Because Artesian Media doesn't track the usage patterns of individual users, an advertiser will never know that a specific user clicked on a specific ad.
