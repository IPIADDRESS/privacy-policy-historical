    • Privacy Policy


    Like many other websites, Disasterstrikes.org does collect some information from visitors. This information is not personally identifying, and is generally information that is made available by browsers and servers. Information collected can include, but is not limited to the browser type, languages, dates, times, length of visits, and referring sites. We collect this information in order to understand our users and better provide for their needs and wants. Occasionally, we may release this information in the form of traffic reports or trends of usage of the website.

    Any personally identifying information, such as internet protocol addresses, will not be released should it be collected.

    Security

    Disasterstrikes.org follows very strict and law abiding rules that govern the safety and security of any information that is obtained via this website. This includes, but is not limited to, physical, electronic, and practical safeguards. Please realize that while we do take every step that we can to protect your information, no method of information transmission online is totally safe.

    Cookies

    Cookies are information that is stored on a person’s computer by a website. This information is updated each time that a visitor returns to a website. We use cookies to track visitors, how they use Disasterstrikes.org, and their preferences regarding the site. If you do not wish to allow these cookies to be placed on your computer, then you should refuse cookies through your browser prior to using the website. Please realize that some functionality may not work correctly without allowing cookies.

    Children

    Disasterstrikes.org does not knowingly accept or collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you are not over this age, do not provide any information to Disasterstrikes.org.

    Changes

    Disasterstrikes.org reserves the right to update and change these privacy policies at any time. While every effort will be made to inform users to these changes, we encourage our users to review this statement regularly and make note of any changes that do occur.
