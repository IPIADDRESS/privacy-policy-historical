Personal information is information that could identify you. Examples of personal information include your name, address, mobile number, education and email address.

We may also publish this information for promotional purposes.

    We take security seriously and take numerous precautions to protect the security of Personally Identifiable Information of those who are registered of have online accounts. Unfortunately, no data transmission over the Internet or any wireless network can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. If you link to any such website, we urge you to review the respective website's privacy policy. We are not responsible for the policies or practices of third parties.
