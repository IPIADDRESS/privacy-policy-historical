Privacy Policy

 Introduction
DiscussCooking.com has a firm commitment to the privacy of our visitors and we respect the consumers rights to privacy. Please take a couple of minutes to read our privacy policies so you can understand how we treat your personal information. If you have any questions then please contact us.

Personal Information
In order to access some areas or services of our site, you may be required to submit personal information (i.e. name, address). We will hold your personal information on our systems for as long as you use the service you have requested, and remove it in the event that the purpose has been met, or until you no longer wish to continue your access to that service.

Children's Privacy
DiscussCooking.com is not directed to children under the age of 13. We prohibit the registration by children under the age of 13 and therefore will not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from kids under 13. If your child is under 13 and has registered their information using a false age, please notify us and we will immediately close the account and nullify all personally identifiable information collected from them.

User Surveys & Promotions
As our site continues to grow and we launch new products we may provide you with details of our new products and services. Please note that your information will only be used within DiscussCooking.com and and will never be supplied to third parties without first obtaining your consent. The only time we may do so is if we are obliged by law to disclose information.
