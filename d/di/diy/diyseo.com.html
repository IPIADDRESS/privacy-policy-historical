User IP addresses may also be recorded for security and monitoring purposes.

DIYSEO may also use a user's email address to send updates, such as a newsletter or news regarding DIYSEO's products and services. Users may choose not to receive email of this type by clicking an "opt out" link located at the bottom of the update.

Unless otherwise agreed by you or required by law, information collected by DIYSEO may be (i) used by DIYSEO for marketing and business purposes, (ii) retained by DIYSEO indefinitely, and (iii) sold or otherwise transmitted to third parties.

DIYSEO may use cookies or other similar tracking means to store user's preferences and to record session information. DIYSEO may link the information it stores in its tracking systems to any personally identifiable information you submit while on the DIYSEO Site.
