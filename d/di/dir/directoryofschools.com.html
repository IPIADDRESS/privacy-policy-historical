WHAT ARE COOKIES AND HOW DO WE USE THEM?

Directoryofschools.com uses a feature of your Internet browser called a "cookie" to assign a unique identification to your computer. Cookies are files that your Web browser places on your computer's hard drive. Cookies can be used to help save and retrieve passwords used on Directoryofschools.com and to save information on past activities and other preferences. That way, you don't have to re-enter information every time you visit Directoryofschools.com.

When you visit Directoryofschools.com, we read the cookie to help target and personalize offers to you. For example, the cookie can help ensure that you don't see an offer that you have already completed. Cookies also let Directoryofschools.com know if We have repeat visitors.

We also use cookies to identify which sites 0ur repeat visitors are coming from. Directoryofschools.com does not and cannot use cookies to retrieve personal information about you from your computer unless such information was knowingly and willingly provided by you. Directoryofschools.com respects your privacy and Directoryofschools.com cookies are not linked to any personally identifiable information.

Directoryofschools.com works with third-parties to serve ads on Our websites. These ads may contain cookies and/or action tags (also known as single pixel gifs) to measure advertising effectiveness and gather completely anonymous information. We have no access to or control over these cookies or action tags.

You are always free to decline these cookies if your browser permits, however, you may not be able to use certain features on Directoryofschools.com and you may be asked to re-enter your password more frequently during your session.
