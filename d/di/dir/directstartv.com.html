Privacy Policy
This site is controlled and operated by DirectStarTV. Our highest priority is to ensure that your privacy is respected, that your transaction is secure, and that any data you provide to us is protected.

Online Security
If you subscribe to any DirectStarTV service, for newsletters or promotional offers, all information is safely stored in a secure database that is protected from unauthorized access.

To check if the page upon which you are entering information is secured, double click the padlock in the lower right side of your browser window (left on a Mac) to view the detail of the security certificate.

Online Privacy
Personal information is collected only when users voluntarily register at a DirectStarTV site to purchase items, receive newsletters, or for other special offers or promotional opportunities. It is DirectStarTV policy to maintain all personal information as confidential and to use it only for the purpose for which it was submitted. If you request services from this site you may receive future offers related to the communications industry. If you are already registered but no longer wish to receive messages from us, please let us know by phone, email or mail.

Sharing Data
DirectStarTV never shares its consumer list with any company or organization, neither does DirectStarTV sell or disclose any personally identifiable information about you unless you specifically register for a special service or promotional offer. You may request that DirectStarTV destroy any information about you and remove you from our database at any time.

Aggregate Data
As with all servers on the Internet, the computers that deliver the Web pages to you identify your computer by its unique IP address or that of your ISP, such as AOL. The servers automatically record the name of the domain from which you accessed the site and the type of operating system and web browser that you use. The data are collected for one-time use only and are analyzed to identify broad demographic trends. This information cannot be used to identify you personally.

Children and Privacy
DirectStarTV encourages parents to spend time with their children online. Under no circumstances does DirectStarTV intentionally collect personal data from any person under the age of 14. If you are under 14, please ask a parent to provide any information or exit this site.
