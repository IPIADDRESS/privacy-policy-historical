Demo Data Collection Process

Signing up for a DirectTrack Demo provides us with your name, email address, company name, web address, business phone and business address. After a prospective customer enters his/her information on the demo request page, that data is transferred to our server in order to evaluate if the prospective customer is suited for a demonstration.
