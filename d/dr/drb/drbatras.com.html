Privacy Policy
  Privacy Policy of Dr. Batras'
   
1. We fully respect your personal privacy and are committed to protecting the privacy of users at all times.
   
2. We will not trade or sell your personal information to any third party without your prior written consent
   
3. In order to help and educate patients, we can with your prior consent photograph the treatment given to you including publishing Before-&-After pictures depicting improvement of the health/condition of the patient in any media for medical, scientific or educational purposes. However, we will not reveal your identity by pictures or by descriptive text accompanying them.
   
4. THE TYPE OF INFORMATION Dr. Batra’s Positive Health Clinic Pvt. Ltd. COLLECTS:
   
i. We generally collect personally identifying information along with your medical history, family history, past history and personal history , age, gender and any allergy which you may have, with your specific knowledge and consent.
   
ii. We use this information for your proper diagnosis and to provide you with the service you have requested. We may also use the information to communicate with you about new features, products or services, or to improve the services that we offer by tailoring them to your needs
   
5. COOKIES
   
i. You may have read about “cookies” nuggets of information that are placed by a Website in a storage place on your own computer. ( Will be activated shortly) We use cookies to control the display of ads, to track usage patterns on the site, to deliver editorial contents, and to record registration and personalization information. For example, if you register on any part of http://www.drbatras.com, you can opt to save your use name and password on your computer. We can provide this convenience by placing a cookie on your computer
   
ii. Our cookies may contain personally identifiable information and such cookies may be shared with our affiliated companies.
   
iii. Some of our advertisers occasionally serve you cookies as well. We do not have control over cookies placed by advertisers.
   
iv. If you don’t want cookies, your Web browser likely includes an option that allows you to not accept them. However, if you set your browser to refuse cookies, some portions of http://www.drbatras.com may not function properly.
   
v. While your individual information is protected as outlined above, we reserve the right to use aggregated, anonymous data about our users as group for any business purpose, such as analyzing usage trends and seeking compatible advertisers and partners
   
6. EXTERNAL LINKS
   
  We provide links to external websites whose content we believe might be of interest to our visitors. While these websites have been initially reviewed by our staff for appropriateness for our audience, we do not control the privacy practices of these external sites and we do not conduct ongoing reviews of their privacy practices. These external sites might collect personally identifiable information from visitors. Before using these websites, we recommend that you review their user and privacy policies. Children should always check with their parents or teachers before clicking onto any new sites.
   
7. We reserve our right to make any changes to any part of this privacy policy at our sole discretion at any time and without any prior notice.
