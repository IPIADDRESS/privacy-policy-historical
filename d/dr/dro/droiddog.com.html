    Collection of Personal Information
    Generally, when you browse droiddog.com you does so anonymously. There are certain visitors to droiddog.com who choose to interact with us in ways that require us to collect personal information. You may provide personal information to droiddog.com in one of the following ways: (1) by sharing contact information when using the “Contact Us” form, (2) by sharing contact information when using the “Submit News” form or (3) by sharing personal information when posting a comment.

Information collected include operating system, browser type, screen resolution, location, Internet Protocol (IP) address, as well as the Web pages you visit.

    Cookie Information
    A cookie is a small data file that certain websites write to your hard drive when you visit them. A cookie file can contain information such as a user ID that the site uses to track the pages you’ve visited, but the only personal information a cookie can contain is information you supply yourself. A cookie can’t read data off your hard disk or read cookie files created by other websites. Some parts of droiddog’s website may use cookies to track user traffic patterns in order to determine the usefulness of our website information and to see how effective our navigational structure is in helping users reach that information.

    If you prefer not to receive cookies while browsing our website, you can set your browser to warn you before accepting cookies and refuse the cookie when your browser alerts you to its presence. You can also refuse all cookies by turning them off in your browser, although you may not be able to take full advantage of droiddog’s website if you do so. You do not need to have cookies turned on to use/navigate through many parts of our website.

    How We Use Your Information
    We use your information to help us improve our services, fulfill any requests you may have and to communicate with you. droiddog.com does not rent or sell personal information about you. Personal information we may collect such as your name and email address that you use when contacting us via the “Contact Us” form or “Submit News” form will not be used for any reason other than for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.
