Privacy Policy

DrinksMixer.com is continuously committed to protecting the privacy of it's visitors. We do not sell, distribute or give out any of our visitors information. The following discloses how information is collected and used on and throughout our web site. Aside from the case where you explicitly give permission to provide your contact information to a third party, any personal information you provide on this site will be only be known to yourself and DrinksMixer.com.



Personal Information and Demographics

DrinksMixer.com does not collect any personally identifiable information about you such as name, address, phone number, or e-mail address, unless you actively provide this by filling out registration forms.

However, DrinksMixer.com does obtain some types of non-personally identifiable information such as your IP address, your browser type and operating system, and the address of the web site that referred you to DrinksMixer.com. This information is collected for use in providing potential advertisers with statistics, to administer the web site, and to track abusive users.



Adding Recipes

Everyone that submit recipes to DrinksMixer.com must be a registered member and hence provide their name and a working e-mail address. This information may be listed on the web site and should be considered public.



Polls & Voting

None of these features require you to provide any personal information. Your IP address is stored along with your answer or vote, but is only used to avoid multiple votes from the same person.



Disclaimer

This site contains links to other sites and DrinksMixer.com can not be held responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such sites.



Further Information

If you have additional questions regarding your privacy on DrinksMixer.com, feel free to contact us using the link at the top of the page.
