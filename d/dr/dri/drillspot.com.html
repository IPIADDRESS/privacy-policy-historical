Privacy Policy

Privacy Statement:
At DrillSpot® we are committed to protecting our customers’ privacy. All personal information provided to DrillSpot® is used only to process and fulfill orders. We keep this information private and protected with advanced encryption technology. Your personal information travels through a SSL that is not accessible to outside users.

When you make a purchase through www.DrillSpot.com, we ask that you provide your name, email address, billing and shipping address, credit card information, and phone number. Credit card transactions are handled by a reputable payment processor. Our processor receives the information needed to verify and authorize your credit card prior to order fulfillment. Our processor is under strict obligation not to sell or share any of your personal information.

We use "cookies" to keep track of your current shopping session. The cookies are used to identify your computer while shopping at www.DrillSpot.com. This enables us to personalize your shopping experience and allows you to save and retrieve your shopping cart at any time. You do not need to accept our cookies in order to browse or shop at DrillSpot®. This anonymous information is also used in our internal metrics and reporting.

We may need to disclose personal information when required by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary in order to conform to the edicts of the law or comply with a legal process served on our website. DrillSpot® fully cooperates with law enforcement agencies in identifying those who use our services for illegal activities. We reserve the right to report to law enforcement agencies any activities and information that we reasonably believe to be unlawful.

Checkout:

Once your order has been placed, your information is saved so you do not need to re-enter it next time you log into your DrillSpot® account. To protect your privacy and security, we will also take reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting access or making corrections.
