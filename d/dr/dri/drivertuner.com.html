Privacy Policy
LionSea is committed to protecting the privacy of all its users and the privacy in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

 

LionSea's Customer Privacy Policy covers the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information that may be collected by LionSea anytime you interact with LionSea, such as when you visit our website, when you purchase LionSea products and services, or when you contact our sales or support associates. Please read the following to learn more about our information practices, including what type of information is gathered, how the information is used and for what purposes, to whom we disclose the information, and how we protect your personal information. Your privacy is LionSea's priority, and we wil try our best to protect it.
Why do we collect your personal information?
We collect your personal information because it helps us deliver a superior-level customer service. It can enable us to give you convenient access to our products and services and focus on categories of greatest interest to you. What's more, your personal information helps us keep you posted on the latest product announcements, software updates, special offers, and events that you might like to hear about.
What information do we collect and how we may use it?
There are a number of situations in which your personal information may help us offer you better products or service. For example:
. We may ask for your personal information when you're discussing a service issue on the phone with an associate, downloading a software update, registering for a seminar, participating in an online survey, registering your products, purchasing a product, etc.
. When you interact with LionSea, we may collect the relevant personal information such as your name, mailing address, phone number, email address, contact preferences, your credit card information and information about the LionSea products you own, such as their serial numbers and date of purchase; and information relating to a support or service issue.
. We also collect information such as your occupation and where you use your computer for the sake of market research to gain a better understanding of our customers and thus provide more valuable service.
. We collect information regarding customer activities on our websites. This helps us to determine whether we have provided useful information to customers and help us understand which parts of our websites, products, and Internet services are of most interest to our customers.
. We may use personal information to provide products that you have requested as well as for auditing, research, and analysis to improve LionSea's products and service.
Publicly displayed information is public!
If you use a bulletin board or chat room on LionSea website, you should be aware that any information you share is visible to other users. Personally identifiable information you submit to one of these forums can be read, collected, or used by other individuals to send you unsolicited messages. LionSea is not responsible for the personally identifiable information you choose to submit in these forums. For example, if you choose to publicize information, which was previously non-public, available by enabling certain user features, LionSea will collect that information from your interaction and the information will become publicly available.
When do we disclose your information?
LionSea will take your privacy very seriously. LionSea will not sell or rent your contact information to other marketers.
Within the LionSea Corporation
To offer you superior service, your personal information may be shared with legal entities within the LionSea Corporation globally who will take steps to safeguard it in accordance with LionSea's privacy policy!
With our service providers, vendors, and strategic partners
There are also occasions when it may be advantageous for LionSea to make your certain personal information available to companies that LionSea has a strategic relationship with or to the peoplle who work for LionSea to provide products and services to you on our behalf. These companies may help us process information, extend credit, fulfill customer orders, deliver products to you, manage and enhance customer data, provide customer service, assess your interest in our products and services, or conduct customer research or satisfaction surveys. These companies are also obligated to protect your personal information in accordance with LionSea's policies, except that we inform you at the time of collection or otherwise.
At times, we may be required by law or litigation to disclose your personal information. We may also disclose your information if it is for national security, law enforcement, or other issues of public importance, disclosure is necessary.
How do we protect your personal information?
LionSea will take precautions, including administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect your personal information against loss, theft, and misuse, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.
LionSea uses secure server hosted by Paypal, SWREG and other 3rd party credit card processors that encrypt all the customer's personal information such as name, address, credit card number, credit card expiration date, etc. before it is sent to us. Encryption works to prevent any malicious use of your personal information.
You can also help us protect your personal data when you are on the Internet by taking precautions such as using a combination of letters and numbers as your passwords, and using a secure web browser like Safari, etc.
Kids
LionSea finds that most users are parents, guardians, or other adults who often purchase our products for family use, including use by minors. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 for marketing purposes, because some information is collected electronically, it can appear to be the personal information of the LionSea purchaser of the product, and will be treated as such by this privacy policy. If a child under 13 submits personal information to LionSea and we learn that that personal information is the information of a child under 13, we will attempt to delete the information as soon as possible.
Cookies and other technologies
As is standard practice on many corporate websites, LionSea's website uses "cookies" and other technologies to help us understand which parts of our websites are the most popular, where our visitors are going, and how much time they spend there. We also use cookies and other technologies to make sure that our online advertising is bringing customers to our products and services. We use cookies and other technologies to study traffic patterns on our website, to make it more rewarding as well as to study the effectiveness of our customer communications. And we use cookies to customize your experience and provide greater convenience every time you interact with us.
As is true of most websites, we gather some information automatically and store it in log files. This information includes Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and clickstream data.
We use this information, which does not identify individual users, to analyze trends, administer the site, track users' movements around the site and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. LionSea will not use the information collected to market directly to that person.
Anti-malware statement
LionSea STRONGLY OPPPOSES all forms of malware. LionSea applications will never intentionally jeopardize our customers' privacy or security. All applications are designed to be easily removable from the computer through an uninstall utility. LionSea does not employ the use of reinstallers and avoids all features that may be found in any type of malware. No LionSea application is designed to collect any personally identifiable information from any user. LionSea applications do not contain, nor are they bundled with, any third party applications (spyware, adware, malware, etc.) that would jeopardize our customers' security or their privacy. All LionSea applications pass both Kaspersky and Rising vigorous anti-virus/malware tests before public release.
Our companywide commitment to your privacy
This policy covers the Intel Corporate website and is applicable worldwide. To make sure the safety of your personal information, we communicate these guidelines to LionSea employees and strictly enforce privacy safeguards within the company.
