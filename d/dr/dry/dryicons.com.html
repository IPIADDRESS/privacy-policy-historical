    Privacy Policy

    We know how much you value and care about your personal information and how it is used. Within this Privacy statement you can find details on how we collect and use, and under which circumstances may share your information.

    Cookies

    A cookie is a small portion of data, often includes an anonymous unique identifier, which is sent to your browser from a web site's servers and stored on your computer's hard drive. Every computer that accesses our website is assigned a different cookie by us. We use cookies for analytics, to improve your experience, track your session and store the contents of your shopping cart.

If we make significant changes to our Privacy Policy which would affect the way we collect and use any of your personal information, and under which circumstances share any information, we will notify you by a notice on our website pages.
