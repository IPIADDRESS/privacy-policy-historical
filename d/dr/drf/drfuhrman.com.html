Privacy Policy
 How we use the information we receive.
DrFuhrman.com respects your privacy and follows these guidelines with your information.

Security:
On the Internet, we use secure lines and technology -- secure server software (SSL) -- to ensure the security and safety of your information and your credit card transaction. Your privacy is protected.

Cookies:
In our Member Support Center, we use cookies to recognize members, grant you privileges in the Member Center and Store, and track usage. Your information is for the use of DrFuhrman.com, and we do not sell your information to third parties.

We do not sell any information of visitors to DrFuhrman.com to third parties. Any usage tracking is for internal use only for further development of DrFuhrman.com.

E-mail:
If you sign up for our mailing list, are a member of our Member Support Center, or subscribe to our newsletters or services, you will receive e-mails from us. E-mails permit us to contact you about your account. If you prefer not to receive e-mail communication, please call us at 800-474-9355 x227 or contact our Technical Support department using our contact form to request your communication preferences.

Links and Affiliates:
Please refer to the individual policies of any links and affiliates found on DrFuhrman.com. Every website has different guidelines, and DrFuhrman.com is not responsible for the content or for the policies of websites to which it may link.

Forums:
The Member Forums in the Member Support Center, while part of a "Member-only" community, are message boards that become accessible and public once you post your messages. DrFuhrman.com cannot be responsible for removing any information you have posted. If you provide e-mail or other information that results in unwanted e-mail or contacts, DrFuhrman.com is not responsible.

Legal Protection and Compliance:
If we are required by law or if we believe, in good faith, that we need to disclose personal information to protect the misuse of DrFuhrman.com or the accounts of our customers, we may disclose personal information to the proper authorities.

Changes:
Please refer to this policy for any future changes, as developments on the internet frequently evolve, and we would like to keep you up-to-date.

If you have any questions, please contact us.
