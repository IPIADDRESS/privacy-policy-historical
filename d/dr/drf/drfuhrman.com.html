Privacy Policy

DrFuhrman.com respects your privacy and follows these guidelines for your information.

Cookies:   In our Member Support Center , we use cookies to recognize members, grant you privileges in the Member Center and Store, and track usage.   Your information is for the use of DrFuhrman.com and we do not sell your information to third parties.

We do not sell any information on visitors to DrFuhrman.com to third parties.   Any usage tracking is for internal use only for further development of DrFuhrman.com.

Legal Protection and Compliance:   If we are required by law or if we believe, in good faith, that we need to disclose personal information to protect the misuse of DrFuhrman.com or the accounts of our customers, we may disclose personal information to the proper authorities.

Changes:   Please refer to this policy for any future changes, as developments on the Internet frequently evolve and we would like to keep you up-to-date.

If you have any questions, please contact info@drfuhrman.com.
