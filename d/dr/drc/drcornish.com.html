Privacy Policy
Private
 
We value your trust, and we respect your privacy. We want your experience with MyHealthyLove.com and the internet to be enjoyable, safe, and secured.
 
Safe
 
Shop and order with confidence.  Your private financial and personal information is secured using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology with 128 bit encryption. All information processed for an order is transmitted via an encrypted secure server.  This feature 'scrambles' your credit card and personal information during the brief seconds that it takes to transmit it from your web browser to our servers.  When it arrives at our server it is 'unscrambled' and processed. This method of transferring customer and financial information is one of the safest method of transmission on the Internet. 
 
Secured
 
For your security and ours, your credit card information is never stored on our server.  Credit card information is deleted immediately after completing a transaction.
 
Personal Touch
 
All books can be autographed by Dr. Grace at your request.  Please write your request in the "special instruction" box when ordering.  CDs and personally-autographed books are non-returnable and non-refundable.  Please contact us within 48 hours of receipt.  Un-autographed books, in purchased condition, are returnable within seven days after you've received them. 
 
Protected
 
We do not rent, reuse, sell, or share your email or contact information with outside parties.  We only use your email address to communicate with you, in order to confirm your order, or to notify you of special offers or programs you might find of interest.  We believe in protecting a child's privacy; therefore, MyHealthyLove.com does not knowingly sell products, or collect personal information from children under the age of 18.
