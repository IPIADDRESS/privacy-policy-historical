Collection of Data/Contact Information/Financial Information

IP Address and Cookies

We use your internet protocol (IP) address to help diagnose problems with our servers and to administer the Sites. We also use your IP address to help identify you and, if you’re a Member of Drupalize.Me, your membership information. We may place “cookies” on your computer’s hard drive through your web browser to keep track of your identity for the purposes of saving your logged-in state so that you do not have to log in each time you visit the Sites. “Cookies” allow the Sites to recognize your computer when you access the Sites. If you would like to prevent the placement of cookies on your computer, or remove cookies from your computer, please access the “Help” portion of your browser or contact your browser provider. Please be advised that if you block or remove cookies from your computer, our Sites will not recognize your computer and you may not have access to all portions of the Sites.

Contact Information/Financial Information

Drupalize.Me’s registration and order forms require users to provide us with contact information to establish user accounts. Lullabot Education may collect each user’s name, address, city, state, postal code, telephone number and e-mail address. Lullabot Education uses this information to administer its business, to enhance and improve its services and products, and to contact the user regarding memberships and administrative matters. Lullabot Education does not rent, sell or exchange user information, and generally restricts access to user information to qualified Lullabot Education employees and agents. Third parties who need access to Lullabot Education user information to provide operational or other support services are required by Lullabot Education to maintain high standards of security. Lullabot Education may use this information to customize your experience and suggest and offer you products according to your preferences. Lullabot Education may also use this information to identify patterns and trends of use, but will only release or publish any such information in a manner that preserves your anonymity.
