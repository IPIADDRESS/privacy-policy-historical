DrPic Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our homepage at all times.

The Information We Collect

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the DrPic.com website. On the homepage, you can upload a Picture File from your computer to our web site. The types of information collected during this process are completely anonymous and include:

Picture File
IP Address
Screen Resolution
Metadata EXIF

Absolutely no types of personal identifiable information are collected at any time on the DrPic.com website including Names, Addresses, or Phone Numbers. There are no opportunities to fill in any form and/or submit personal information to us.

The Way We Use Information

Picture Files that are uploaded to our servers are automatically deleted within 20 minutes. This policy has been implemented since the start of the web site in March 2005 and has remained unchanged.
We use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design our website and to share with advertisers. For example, we may tell an advertiser that X number of individuals visited a certain area on our website, but we would not disclose anything (or have the means to) that could be used to identify those individuals.

Our Commitment To Data Security

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

Our Commitment To Children's Privacy

Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under 13.

How To Contact Us
