Dragon Quest Wiki:Privacy policy

All content on Dragon Quest Wiki is distributed under the Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 3.0 Unported License and open for anyone to edit. This includes any information which is posted by users. Anyone who contributes content on the Dragon Quest Wiki is advised to be aware of the license under which content is released.

Non-registered users

For non-registered visitors, the Dragon Quest Wiki collects information such as screen resolution, operating system, web browser and location. This information is collected in order to make improvements to the site and is not distributed to anyone.

Registered users

For registered users, the Dragon Quest Wiki collects additional information that is entered during registration. Dragon Quest Wiki will not under any circumstances share details about its individual members with any third parties, but reserves the rights to use collected information as a basis for site demographics, which may, in given situations, be proposed to potential advertisers and sponsors.

Please note that if you decide to post personal information on your user page, you are doing so at your own risk. Dragon Quest Wiki is not responsible for anything that occurs as a result of this information being posted, and recommends that you not reveal too much personal information.

Advertising

As of this writing (October 2010), there are no plans to utilize advertising on the Dragon Quest Wiki. However, we reserve the right to utilize site demographic information as mentioned above if advertising is implemented at a later date.
