Our Privacy Policy

We collect basic customer information for the sole purpose of improving our visitors’ experiences. With an optional free subscription to our newsletter, we provide you with information about our new lessons, products and services. We do not sell or rent the information you provide to us online to third parties.

We respect your privacy, and collect only information relevant to our visitors and customers, specific to the following categories:

1) Website Visitors - We store the following information on visitors who download free information from our web pages:

The domain name through which you gained access to our Web site.

The precise time and date that you entered our web site.

The URL of the web page through which you linked to our web site.

2) Newsletter Subscribers - in addition to the above, we also collect the following information on subscribers to our optional periodical Newsletter:

The email address you provided when you subscribed to our newsletter.

A screen name and password, either chosen by you, or assigned by us, to allow you access to our online newsletter. For added security, we encrypt your password before storing it.

Personal identifying information that you provide voluntarily, such as your name.

3) Members and Customers - The following information applies to all customers who purchase any items from our websites:

We currently use 2co.com for all on-line transactions.

Members and/or customers, who purchase downloadable products such as our lessons, are not required to provide an address to us. However, 2co may require this information to process your credit card transaction.

Customers who purchase goods that must be shipped by us are required to provide their valid mailing address in order to receive their purchased order. Again, this information will not be shared, rented, or sold to any third parties.

4) Any User or Member - Any information that we collect in the following ways is used only for purposes of responding to you and addressing your issues:

Users who send us e-mail containing personal information.

Users who sign our guest book.

Users who request email publications.

5) Any other information that we collect as part of our business practices, as described below, is used exclusively by us:

We identify demographic information relative to users who access our site.

Assessing the number and type of users accessing various sections of our site, helps us determine what content and business concepts are working for us and/or are of interest to our visitors, determining technical design features and specifications and helping us assess our system’s performance.

We also use such information in statistical form to assist us in business and marketing strategies and programs. We utilize software programs to create summary statistics which we employ for these purposes.

As a security measure for ourselves and our users, we utilize certain software programs to monitor traffic to our site and within our network. This software also enables us to identify unauthorized upload attempts, unauthorized access to our system, and other attempts to cause damage to our systems.

6) We will not share any of your personally identifiable information with third parties except in the limited circumstances described below, or with your express permission.

We share information with Paypal only to help us process the transactions you request.

We disclose information in response to a subpoena, warrant, court order, levy, attachment, order of a court-appointed receiver or other comparable legal process, including subpoenas from private parties in a civil action.

We disclose information to your agent or legal representative (such as the holder of a power of attorney that you grant, or a guardian appointed for you).
