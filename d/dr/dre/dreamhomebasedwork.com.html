Cookies

We make use of cookies on our Website.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small files with data that we use to identify you as a unique User. We do not associate any of your Personal Information with these unique identifier files. We send cookies to your browser from our website, and these cookies are stored on your computer’s hard drive. You can tell your browser to decline all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. In the event that you decide to decline cookies from our Website, you may not be able to use some parts of our Service. We use cookies to improve your user experience on our Website.

How long do cookies remain on my device?

We use session cookies as well as persistent cookies on our Website. Session cookies are immediately deleted from your hard drive after the end of your browser session. However, persistent cookies remain on your hard drive for a minimum period of time unless you clear them manually from your device.

Don’t want to accept cookies?

You can set your default browser setting to reject cookies from third-party websites. To learn more about cookies, please visit www.allaboutcookies.org.
