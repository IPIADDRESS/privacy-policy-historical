PRIVACY STATEMENT

Dress Barn recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of visitors to our web site. We limit the information collected about you to what is needed to conduct our business, provide superior service and offer services and products that might be of interest to you. We may use your information for marketing or promotional purposes. We limit access to your information to those employees who need it to carry out their business functions.

The Dress Barn web site collects personally identifiable information (such as name, address, telephone number, or email address) from you only when you voluntarily provide such information to Dress Barn. If you do not want your personally identifiable information collected online, we ask that you do not submit such information to Dress Barn.

Dress Barn provides protection against unauthorized disclosure of the information you share with us. Dress Barn will monitor and adopt, as appropriate, new technological developments that are designed to aid in ensuring the accuracy and security of your information.
