Personally identifiable information consists of information that is unique to you, such as credit card numbers and bank account numbers.

2.The use of information
Help make this Site easier for you to use by not having to enter information more than once.
Help you quickly find information, products, and services.
Help us create content on this Site that is most relevant to you.
Alert you to new information, products, and services that we offer.
Registration and Ordering:
During registration, you will be prompted to provide us your name, shipping and billing address, phone number, email address and credit card number. If we encounter a problem when processing your order, we may use the personal information you provide us with to contact you.
Email Addresses:

We will not sell (or trade or rent) personally identifiable information to other companies as part of our regular course of business.
