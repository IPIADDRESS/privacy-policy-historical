Help/FAQ

How can I pay?

At dress-for-less you can pay using PayPal, Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Cash on delivery, Prepaid Purchase, Klarna - Open Invoiceand SOFORT Banking.

Are there any risks when I pay by credit card?

At dress-for-less we take your online security and privacy very seriously. Our security server automatically encrypts your personal details when you enter them. We do also not store your credit card number at all as it is sent straight through to the relevant banks in order to process the payment.

Are there any risks when I pay by debit card?

Maestro is the most-used payment method in the world. This is because of it's excellent security, and the number of vendors who accept it is growing daily! Your transaction is secured by your SecureCode which is an online PIN issued to you by your bank. This number is entered as a confirmation that you are the owner of the Maestro debit card being used for the transaction. You can register for your SecureCode online by clicking here.

What is a MasterCard SecureCode?

Your MasterCard SecureCode is a form of online PIN which makes your purchases with debit card safer for you. This code only needs to be registered for once and can be used for every online purchase you make with your Maestro card. You can register with your bank for your SecureCode by clicking here.

Do you accept international orders?

dress-for-less welcomes orders from over 20 countries. Deliveries to customers outside Germany may take a little longer. Click here for information on delivery.

Where do you deliver to in the UK?

We deliver everywhere in England, Scotland, Wales, Northern Ireland, Jersey, Guernsey and the Channel Islands. Deliveries to all other British territories will be charged at the relevant rates.

In which territories do you deliver in Spain?

We ship your order in the whole territory of Spain. For the Canary Islands, Ceuta, Melilla and Andorra only Standard shipping is available. For these areas import taxes may occur, you have to take them in your own account. On the Canary Island the delivery lead time is 12-15 working days.

In which countries do you deliver?

We send your order in many countries to your home. Select your country of delivery in the head of our website and look at the shipping cost information.

Are international orders safe?

dress-for-less operates in conjunction with DHL, a major international courier. Although your orders may take a few days to reach you, there is no risk in ordering from us. You will get your order as soon as possible!

What about delivery costs?

We deliver to more than 40 countries! Click here for more information about delivery costs to your country.

What about returns?

If you are anything less than delighted with any item for any reason, you can send it back to us within 14 days of receipt. The goods must be returned in saleable condition and in the original packaging. Click here for more information on returns.

Refunds

IV. Cookies and pixel tags

1. What are cookies and pixel tags?

We use cookies on our website. These are small files which are automatically created by your browser and which are stored on your device (laptop, tablet, smartphone or the like) when you visit our website. Cookies do not damage your computer and do not contain viruses, Trojans or any other malware.

Information is stored in the cookie relating to the specific device used. This does not mean, though, that we can immediately establish your identity.

We use pixel tags (also called tracking pixels) in our online offering. Pixels are small graphics which are integrated using the HTML code of our webpage. The pixel tag does not store or change any information on your device, pixels also do not damage your device and do not contain viruses, Trojans or any other malware.

Pixels can send personal data such as your IP address, the referrer URL of the website visited, the time the pixel was viewed, the browser used and previously placed cookie information to a web server. As a result reach measurements and other statistical analyses which serve to optimise our offering can also be conducted.

The use of cookies makes it easier for you to use our website. We use for example so-called session cookies in order to recognise that you have already visited individual pages of our website, to recognise that you have already logged into your user account or to display the shopping basket. These are deleted automatically after you leave our site.

We also use temporary, so-called comfort cookies to optimise the user-friendliness of the website, which are stored on your device for a specific period of time. If you visit our website again in order to use our services, it automatically identifies that you have already visited us and what entries and settings you have made so that you do not have to input these again. These are usually deleted after a maximum of 30 days. Only those comfort cookies with language settings store these for 365 days.

We also use cookies and pixel tags in third-party tools in order to statistically record the use of our website and to analyse the use of our website for the purpose of optimising our offering for you (see under V). These analysis, tracking and targeting cookies allow us to automatically recognise that you have already visited us when you visit our website again. These cookies are deleted automatically after a defined period of time.

The data processed with the use of cookies and pixel tags is necessary for the aforementioned purposes to protect our legitimate interests in the analysis, optimisation and efficient operation of our website as well as the legitimate interests of third parties pursuant to Art. 6 Para. 1 Clause 1 Letter f GDPR.
