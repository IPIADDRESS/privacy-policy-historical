Internet Privacy Statement
At Development Dimensions International, we take the issue of privacy very seriously. We are committed to keeping the personal information of visitors to the DDI web site (www.ddiworld.com) confidential.

DDI does not automatically collect any personal information from anyone who is merely visiting or browsing its web site. Personal information may be requested if the visitor chooses to:

  • Respond to online surveys and registration pages
  • Send an e-mail message to DDI
  • Enter a limited access area of the site.

E-mail addresses, postal addresses, or telephone numbers and other personally identifying data about you is known to DDI only when voluntarily submitted or posted to us. Once it is forwarded to us, it goes to the DDI business area that is equipped to handle your request or feedback. We may use such personally identifying information provided by you to respond to your inquiry or to communicate advance notice of events and new products and services. Our goal is, as always, to provide the best customer service possible.
