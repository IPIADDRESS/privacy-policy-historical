Privacy Policy

By sharing our Privacy Policy, we hope to reassure you that by joining the DwellStudio family, we are equally appreciative of your business and respectful of your requests to protect your personal information. When you purchase or order a product from DwellStudio, or register on our website to receive a catalog, you may choose to give us basic information such as your name, email address, mailing/billing address, zip code, phone and credit card information. When you visit our Web site, we may collect more information such as your Internet service provider's address, the name of the Web page directing you, as well as your activity while on our site. This tracking helps us understand how to better service our customers. The information that you give us, and information about your order may be combined with other personally identifiable information (such as demographic information and past purchase history) available from our records and other sources. This information will be used to make our future marketing efforts more efficient. This information may also be shared with other select companies whose products you may find useful. If you prefer that we do not share your name and address with other companies, please email us at unsubscribe@dwellstudio.com.
