Privacy Policy

Dave Whelan Sports Ltd is committed to complying with the Data Protection Act 1998.

Registration is not required when you order products. However, when you place an order you will be asked to provide certain information about yourself such as your name, address, details of your payment card, telephone number and email address so that we can process and fulfil your order, notify you of your order status and inform you of special offers and promotions.

Your information can be collected in one of two ways; either directly from you when you register with us or from a third party such as credit reference agencies (for example to assess your credit rating).

Any additional information we collect will be only with your permission and any communications we undertake will be only with your permission.

We may use certain information about your use of our website to help analyse and improve the website experience for you.

Cookies may be used to help improve your speed and access to chosen areas of the website. You can change your browser settings to refuse cookies.

Dave Whelan Sports Ltd takes its security of data very seriously and will investigate any known security breaches.
