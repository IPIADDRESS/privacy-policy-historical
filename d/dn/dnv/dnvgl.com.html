DNV GL engages in a broad variety of interactions with the users of My DNV GL and dnvgl.com (“the Website”). The Website is owned and operated by DNV GL AS (“DNV GL”). This privacy statement is meant to allow you as a user to understand how DNV GL obtains information about yourself and your use of our products and services available on or through the Website, including our apps (applications)  (this information is hereinafter referred to as “Data”), and how and for which purposes we store and use it. The Data is considered as personal data in the meaning of applicable law.

We thought it best to highlight the key points of the privacy statement first:

The privacy statement in a nutshell

The Website is based in Norway and our handling of Data is governed by Norwegian law.

Unless otherwise stated in any special terms for individual products, services and apps, the following applies:

  • We monitor your usage of the Website and use Data to develop our website further.
  • When you provide information in a form, we may collect this data to provide you with information, products or services that you request from us or which we have substantiated reason to believe may interest you. 
  • When you register as a user of the Website, you will automatically be onboarded onto our direct marketing programme. We will only communicate with you on topics that we think are relevant to you, and we will only communicate regarding our own activities and services. If you and your employer do not see the benefit of being part of this programme, the emails that we may send you, will include a link for you to change your communication preferences or unsubscribe from future emails.
  • Except in cases where the applications or service providers used dictate otherwise, The Data is stored on DNV GL’s own servers or in data centers made available by reputable service providers in the European Economic Area.
  • Where we identify your individual usage of certain areas of the Website, we may use this information to tailor the way in which we communicate with you, for example notifying you about a change to an app or a service which you have subscribed to.
  • We will not give any third party outside of DNV GL Group access to the Data unless agreed herein or set out in any applicable agreement, or ordered by competent courts.
  • The Data may be used internally within DNV GL for us to operate efficiently.
  • In order to monitor the use of the Website, we use cookies. The use of cookies is detailed in our About Cookies page. It can be disabled by you should you wish to do so.
  • The Privacy Statement may be supplemented by service specific terms in connection with products, services and apps that you may request access to through the Website. In that case, the specific terms will be included in the applicable agreement.

 
If you are concerned about use of the Data or have any questions regarding this Privacy Statement, please contact us at dataprotection@dnvgl.com. DNV GL regrets that only general queries about the privacy statement can be responded to via e-mail.

Data gathered automatically is used for only two purposes: Site management and for law enforcement and possible criminal prosecution, in the case of suspected unauthorized activity.

The Website automatically logs visitor information from which summary statistics are created. We use the summary statistics to help us make our site more useful to visitors, such as assessing what information is of most and least interest to visitors, and for other purposes such as determining the site's technical design specifications and identifying system performance or problem areas.

Use of cookies

The Website uses cookies. A cookie is a small file of letters and numbers that we put on your computer if you agree. These cookies allow us to distinguish you from other users of the website which helps us to provide you with the best possible experience when you browse our website. It also allows us to make our site better by improving the content we offer. For more information about cookies, please see http://www.allaboutcookies.org

The cookies we use allow us to see:

  • The Internet domain and Internet Protocol (IP) address from which you access our website;
  • The type of Internet browser and the operating system of the computer you use to access our site;
  • The date and time you visit our site;
  • The pages that you visit and the content that you download;
  • The pages you shared on social media and to which social media network;
  • If you were referred to our website from a search engine, the address of that website and the search term you used to find us.; and
  • Your patterns of use of the Website and our digital communication with you for the purpose of monitoring and improving our services to you in general and user friendliness in particular.

You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser. However, please note that if you do this, you may not be able to use the full functionality of the Website.

An overview of the various cookies used is available in the About Cookies page.

Google Analytics

This Website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. (“Google”). Google Analytics uses cookies to help the website analyze how visitors use the site.

The information generated by the cookie about your use of the Website (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States. Due to privacy concerns, My DNV GL uses Google Analytic’s IP anonymisation function. Google will truncate/anonymize the last octet of the IP address for states in the European Economic Area. Only in exceptional cases, the full IP address is sent to and shortened by Google servers in the USA.

On behalf of DNV GL, Google will use this information for the purpose of evaluating your use of the Website, compiling reports on website activity and providing other services relating to website activity and internet usage. Google will not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google.

Furthermore, you can prevent Google’s collection and use of data (cookies and IP address) by downloading and installing the browser plug-in available under https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=en. An opt-out cookie will be set to prevent the future collection of your information when you visit this website.
Further information can be found under http://www.google.com/analytics/terms/gb.html.
