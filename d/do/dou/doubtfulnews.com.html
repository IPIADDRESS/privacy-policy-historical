Privacy policy

Doubtful News does not share, sell or rent any emails or IP information with outside sources. We only use non-personal and personal data collected on this site for metrics to judge performance, to moderate comments, or assess abusive visitors. We do not deliberately collect any information for other purposes including sale of said information.

Advertisements on this site use “cookies” and other technologies to track consumers and to identify our affiliate ID for commission. Doubtful News (Lithospherica, LLC) does not collect this information. We have no access to or control over these cookies.

This privacy statement covers the use of cookies by doubtfulnews.com only and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.

This site is hosted by Page.ly and runs WordPress front end. Please view their privacy policies if you are concerned about the data that may be transmitted via those portals.

If you have any particular questions, please contact editor@doubtfulnews.com
