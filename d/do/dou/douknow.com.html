The Service is not available to individuals who are younger than 13 years old.  If you are younger than 13 years old, please do not provide us with any of your information. 
What Personal Information is Collected?
The choice to provide us with your personal information is always yours.  However, your decision to withhold particular information may limit our ability to provide you with some of our services.
DYK receives and stores various types of information about you, as follows:

Registration information

  • DoUKnow? is a social networking tool that operates through the mutual sharing of information.  The Service operates on the assumption that users want to provide and share certain personal information with one another.  We therefore collect some personal information about you when you register with us, namely username and password information, your name, email address, gender, city, province, and country.  We also ask that you complete an “About Me” section, which will appear on your member profile.  All of this information constitutes registration information.  Registration information is used to allow you to set up a member account and profile that can be used to interact with others through the Service.

Information You Choose to Share
We receive and store any information you enter on our Service or provide to us in any other way.  In addition to registration information, you have the option of providing DYK and its members with additional information, including but not limited to your date of birth, mobile phone number, address, website, and education information.  You may also choose to upload to our website an avatar, or photographs or videos of yourself.  And you may choose to create Super DoUKnow lists, events, and groups, and broadcast status updates.  This information is provided on a voluntary basis and allows you to better interact with other members through the Service. 
We may use your personal information in order to adapt our Service to better serve you and our community.  We may also use your personal information to communicate with you about your use of the Service, as well as to inform you about specials and new features.

How is Your Personal Information Shared?

Personal information about our members is an integral part of our business.  DoUKnow? is a social networking tool that facilities the sharing of personal information.  Members voluntarily join the Service to presumably network with others through the sharing of personal information.  Given that the nature of our Service is based on the sharing of information, your username, avatar, gender, date of birth, “About Me” information, mobile phone, address, city, province, country, website, and education information are visible to the public by default.  Note that only your username, gender, city, province, country, and “About Me” information is mandatory in order to use the Service, and that your username must always be public information.

Notwithstanding this default, you have control over the sharing of all of your other information.  The option is yours as to what content and what information you wish to be public, visible only to DoUKnow? Members, visible only to your DoUKnow? Friends, or visible only to you.  Each of these options is explained below.

Visible to the Public: By selecting this option, you acknowledge that your content becomes publicly available information.  This means that anyone, including people who are not registered with DYK, will be able to see it.   

Visible to DoUKnow? Members: By selecting this option, you acknowledge that your content is visible to all members of the Service, regardless of whether you know them.

Visible to DoUKnow? Friends: By selecting this option, you acknowledge that your content is visible only to those individuals who you have chosen to approve as “friends” on the Service.  The content is otherwise not visible to other members of the Service or to the general public.

Visible Only to Me: By selecting this option, you acknowledge that you are providing DYK with content that is personal to you, and you elect to not have such content shared with others.

If you are uncomfortable with the sharing of your information, please ensure your privacy settings are set for the most privacy protective (so that only your username and avatar, if applicable, are disclosed publicly).  If you are not comfortable with even your username and avatar being public, DoUKnow?, given its design and purpose, may not be appropriate for you. 

Note that, depending on your privacy settings, you may be searchable on the Service by the information you provide.  For example, if you choose to have your email address visible to DoUKnow? members, a member may search you by your email address and request to connect as “friends”. 

You should be aware that persons having access to your content through our Service may use it or disclose it to other individuals or entities without our control and without your knowledge.  We therefore urge you to think carefully before you upload any content, or supply any information, that you may deem private.

We neither rent nor sell your personal information to anyone.  We only share your personal information with others in the manner set out in this Privacy Policy, or as otherwise notified to you.
