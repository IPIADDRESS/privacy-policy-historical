The Doctors Lounge Privacy Statement

August 4, 2004

Welcome. The Doctors Lounge would like to thank you for visiting our website. We are pleased to have the opportunity to help make the Internet a secure, private, and reliable place to share information and conduct business. This privacy policy explains how we collect and use information from our website visitors. All of The Doctors Lounge divisions prescribe to essentially the same information collection and use practices, and all of these practices are stated in this privacy policy. If a practice is specific to a particular division in The Doctors Lounge, this policy will describe that practice in detail and refer directly to the appropriate division.

The Doctors Lounge Privacy Policy statement, addresses the following issues:

  • The websites and information covered by this Privacy Policy;
  • Exactly what information may be collected and how it will be used;
  • The consequences, if any, resulting from your refusal to provide personal information;
  • Whether a company uses cookies, tracks IP addresses, or uses other passive means to collect information;
  • Who, if anyone, is collecting your personal information;
  • With whom, if anyone, is your personal information shared;
  • What is a company's policy regarding links to third party sites;
  • What choices you have with regard to the use of your personal information;
  • How you can access, and if necessary, change the personal information a company collects and maintains;
  • How a company ensures data security, quality, and integrity; and,
  • Where and how to ask questions or file complaints.

What does this Privacy Policy apply to?

This privacy policy applies to the website operated by The Doctors Lounge:



www.thedoctorslounge.net



Please note that this privacy policy applies only to information submitted and collected online through the website(s) listed above, and does not apply to information that may be collected by The Doctors Lounge offline. In addition, this privacy policy applies only to sites maintained by The Doctors Lounge and not to websites maintained by other companies or organizations to which we link. The Doctors Lounge is therefore not responsible for the content or activities provided on such sites.

 

Are you a physician or a nurse?

Would you like to join us and help patients online by volunteering even a few minutes of your time?

Are you are serious about helping out?

Then get started here by becoming a member of The Doctors Lounge.

What types of information does The Doctors Lounge collect and how will it be used?

The Doctors Lounge only collects information on a voluntary basis. The personal information The Doctors Lounge collects is used almost exclusively for communications purposes and will usually take the form of: (1) The Doctors Lounge Membership registration form; (2) a consumer complaint or inquiry directed to the Support Team; (3) The Doctors Lounge survey; (4) The Doctors Lounge Message Boards and other medical services; (5) The Doctors Lounge referral services; (6) The Doctors Lounge's newsletter; and/or, (7) other general information contained in an email (e.g., comments). The types of information that may be requested for each activity and the way the information may be used are detailed below.

The Doctors Lounge Membership registration form
Visitors that wish to participate in the activities on the site are required to complete The Doctors Lounge Membership registration form. The requested information may include the individual's name, address, telephone, electronic mail (email) address, job, area of specialty, qualifications as well as demographic data. The Doctors Lounge uses this information to contact the individual regarding participation and, if eligible, to communicate with such a member on an ongoing basis regarding certification and compliance.

Consumer Complaints Directed to the Support Team
From time to time, the Support Team receives complaints or inquiries regarding the The Doctors Lounge services. When a complaint or inquiry is received, the Support Team promptly investigates the complaint or inquiry, and then, when appropriate, responds to the individual who sent the complaint or inquiry via the e-mail address from where the request originated.


The Doctors Lounge Survey
Any one of the The Doctors Lounge divisions may conduct surveys. The surveys are conducted strictly for internal profiling purposes and only keep track of anonymous demographic data.


The Doctors Lounge Medical Services
The Doctors Lounge Medical Services are designed to help both physicians and consumers in extending their knowledge of various medical topics and keep up to date with current medical information. These services include; The Doctors Lounge Message Boards, The Doctors Lounge Mobile Phone Services. Some of our services are conducted through a partnership. The Privacy Statement Composer asks for information such as your company name, company address, company telephone number, and contact information. The collected information is used for the sole purpose of creating a privacy statement.


The Doctors Lounge Newsletter
Visitors who wish to subscribe to the The Doctors Lounge newsletter will be asked to provide their e-mail address. The submitted e-mail address is then used to send you, via e-mail, our quarterly newsletter and to otherwise keep you informed as to what's going on at the The Doctors Lounge.


General Information Contained in Email
The Doctors Lounge's website provides a number of links through which an online visitor can e-mail us. The Doctors Lounge collects personal information contained in the e-mail for the purpose of responding to a consumer's questions and/or comments.


No matter what method we use to collect information and no matter how we use that information, we will only collect the information deemed reasonably necessary to fulfill your online requests and our legitimate business objectives.

What happens if I refuse to provide my personal information to The Doctors Lounge?

If you choose not to submit personal information when requested, you may not be able to access certain areas of our site and may considerably limit the services we can provide you. To simply use our site, however, you do not need to give us any personal information.

Does The Doctors Lounge use cookies, track IP addresses or use other passive means to collect information?

Cookies
The Doctors Lounge uses cookies on its Message Forums to allow registered users to use the forums. A "cookie" is a small file or record-keeping device that websites often store on a user's computer.


IP Addresses
The Doctors Lounge may collect your IP address for safety and security purposes. An IP address is a number that is used by computers on the network to identify your computer every time you log on to the Internet.

Who is collecting my personal information?

The information that you disclose while visiting our website is collected by The Doctors Lounge or a vendor on The Doctors Lounge's behalf, and is used in the manner specified above.

With whom does The Doctors Lounge share my personal information?

From time to time, The Doctors Lounge may employ and share your personal information with vendors who use it solely to provide support for the internal operations of The Doctors Lounge's website(s) (e.g., technical support, order fulfillment, and newsletter delivery). These entities will not use your personal information for any other purpose, and have agreed to maintain the confidentiality, security, and integrity of the personal information they obtain from us.

It is our policy not to sell or rent your personal information. We may, however, disclose your personal information to outside third parties as described in this privacy policy. In addition, we may also disclose your personal information in the good faith belief that we are required to do so by law, or that doing so is reasonably necessary to comply with legal processes; respond to any claims; or, to protect the rights, property, or personal safety of The Doctors Lounge or the public.

Customers may receive information about third party services that are likely to be of interest. Users of our free service will initially be opted in to this program while customers signing up any of our paid service can choose to opt in. Customers can opt out by clicking on the link provided in the email or by visiting our site and opting out at any time.

We may also use personally identifiable End-User information to provide targeted content and commerce opportunities to End-Users based on their demographic and behavioral information. This does not apply to the personal medical information collected in the "Ask a Doctor" section. We do not share the personal medical information of our users.

Finally, we never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online in ways or for reasons unrelated to the ones described above without also providing an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise prohibit such unrelated uses.

What is The Doctors Lounge's policy regarding links to third party sites?

You should be aware that while you are on a website operated by The Doctors Lounge, you may be directed to other sites that are beyond our control and for which we are not responsible. Because we cannot guarantee that the privacy policies of these websites meet our high standards, we recommend that you read the privacy policy of the website to which you link before you submit any personal information.

Can I choose how my personal information is used?

If you wish to opt-out from receiving our newsletter or any other communication, please contact us, at any time, via e-mail at privacyinfo@thedoctorslounge.net. We will be happy to honor your request.
 

How can I access my personal information?

If you wish to review, update, or delete any personal information that we may have collected about you, please contact us via e-mail at privacyinfo@thedoctorslounge.net.

What safeguards does The Doctors Lounge use to protect my personal information?

The Doctors Lounge maintains appropriate safeguards that ensure the security, integrity and privacy of the personal information we collect from all users. Personal information collected by The Doctors Lounge is stored in secure operating environments that are not available to the public and is only accessible by authorized employees. We also have security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.

How do I ask a question or file a complaint?

If you have any questions, complaints or comments regarding our privacy policy or practices, please contact us via email at privacyinfo@thedoctorslounge.net.


By using this site, you signify your assent to the The Doctors Lounge Online Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to this Privacy Policy, please do not use this site. We reserve the right to make changes to this Policy. Please check back from time to time to ensure you are aware of these changes. Your continued use of this site will signify your acceptance of these changes.
