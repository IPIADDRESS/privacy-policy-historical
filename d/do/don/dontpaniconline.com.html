Privacy Policy

﻿When you interact with the Don’t Panic Website may collect personal information about you. For example, when you register, enter a competition, or sign up to a newsletter.

When you use our services, we may use your IP address, cookies and information provided to use to provide certain functionality to you and to better understand how our services are being used.
Your information is securely stored.


What cookies we use

Cookie: Don’t Panic notify cookie acceptance


Name: dpcookie-notification


Purpose: This cookie is used to record if a user has accepted the use of cookies on the Don’t Panic website.


Cookie: Google Analytics


Name: _utma | _utmb | _utmc | _utmz | _qca


Purpose: These cookies are used to collect information about how visitors use our site.


Cookie: Don’t Panic  page views & impressions


Name: dptv | win_impr


Purpose: This cookie is essential for logging the number of viewers to some of our pages and logging the number of impressions to our campaigns.


Cookie: Email remember


Name: dp_member_remember


Purpose: This cookie is set when a user successful logs in to the website. The email address is then displayed the next time the user visits the site.


Cookie: User customisation


Name: fid | dp_visitor


Purpose: This cookie is used to remember a user’s city selection. A default value is set until the user changes the value.


Cookie: YouTube


Name: Cookie Name Unknown


Purpose: We embed videos from our official YouTube channel. When watching videos cookies may be set on your computer to help maintain the integrity of video statistics, prevent fraud and to improve the site experience, among other things. YouTube will not store personally-identifiable cookie information.
