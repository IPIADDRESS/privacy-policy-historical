There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected by Destinations of the World News. Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally identifiable information or personally sensitive data through Destinations of the World News public message boards, this information may be collected and used by others. Note: Destinations of the World News does not read any of your private online communications.

Destinations of the World News encourages you to review the privacy statements of Web sites you choose to link to from Destinations of the World News so that you can understand how those Web sites collect, use and share your information. Destinations of the World News is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on Web sites outside of the Destinations of the World News and Destinations of the World News family of Web sites.

Destinations of the World News collects and uses your personal information to operate the Destinations of the World News Web site and deliver the services you have requested. Use of Cookies

The Destinations of the World News Web site use "cookies" to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.

One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalize Destinations of the World News pages, or register with Destinations of the World News site or services, a cookie helps Destinations of the World News to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses, and so on. When you return to the same Destinations of the World News Web site, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the Destinations of the World News features that you customized.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the Destinations of the World News services or Web sites you visit.

Security of your Personal Information

Destinations of the World News secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. Destinations of the World News secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to other Web sites, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol.
