dosgames.com Privacy Policy

User Information
Your privacy is important to us. That is why no personal information is collected about you by dosgames.com. Our hit tracking service (currently Hitbox, run by WebSideStory) tracks general information about all visitors to the site, such as type webbrowser, operating system, screen resolution, etc. No information about specific visitors is ever tracked or stored. This information is used to ensure that the site is compatible with the majority of visitors. You may view WebSideStory's privacy policy here.


Cookies

To do this, the advertiser(s) may collect anonymous data through the use of cookies. To learn more about Burstmedia, go to http://www.burstmedia.com/release/about.asp. To read ValueAd's privacy policy, go to http://www.valuead.com/privacy.html.


DOS Games Email service
The dosgames.com free email service is hosted by a third party, bigmailbox.com. Information collected during signup for the dosgames.com email service is handled by this third party and is subject to their privacy policy, located here.

dosgames.com openly volunteers its practices of collecting information, and reserves the right to change this privacy policy at any time. Any questions regarding our privacy policy can be directed to webmaster@dosgames.com.
