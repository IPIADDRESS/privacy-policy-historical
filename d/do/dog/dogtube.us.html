How to use the new Dog Tube:

1. Sign up or log in.

2. To add a video to your playlist, click ADD TO PLAYLIST under the video.

3. To add an article to your library, click ADD TO LIBRARY under the article.

4. If you don't have time to read or watch something now, but would like to later, click on the clock that is on the video or article and it will be saved in your WATCH LATER or READ LATER section at the bottom of your folders. Once you have clicked on the clock to READ or WATCH later, the clock will turn yellow to let you know it has been added to your READ LATER or WATCH LATER section.

5. To access your PLAYLIST, LIBRARY, READ LATER, OR WATCH LATER, click on the "MY FOLDERS" section at the top of the page.

6. Each folder in your PLAYLIST and LIBRARY has a category on it. When you save a video or article to your PLAYLIST or LIBRARY, it will automatically go in it's designated category. Each video or article has it's designated category listed at the top of the video or article.
