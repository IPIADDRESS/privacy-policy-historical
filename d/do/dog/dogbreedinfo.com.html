Privacy Policy

The Dog Breed Information Center® does not collect or use your personal information. When mailing in your photos or taking our survey, your name, email address and or home address will not be sold or placed on a mailing list. Dog Breed Information Center® does not collect cookies from its users. This is strictly an information site used for research and your enjoyment.

Dog Breed Info Center(R) uses Google Adsense, who allows third-party vendors to serve ads on our website. As a user of this website, you may opt out of cookies (if the vendor offers this capability). You can find a list of allowed third party ads by going to https://www.google.com/adsense/support/bin/answer.py?answer=94149&topic=14535.

Photos sent in will not be sold but may be used in other Dog Breed Information Center® Projects, books, pamphlets, flyers, magazines and by students working on school projects, as this site is frequently used as a school research site for grade schools.  www.dogbreedinfo.com © 1998-2008 All rights reserved. Schools may not however copy any part of the site and post it online.
