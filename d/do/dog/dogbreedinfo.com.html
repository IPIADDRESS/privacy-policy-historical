Privacy Policy

The Dog Breed Info Center does not collect or use your personal information. When mailing in your photos or purchasing a custom mouse pad, your name, email address and or home address will not be sold or placed on a mailing list. Dog Breed Info does not collect cookies from its users. This is strictly an information site used for research and your enjoyment.

Photos sent in will not be sold but may be used in other Dog Breed Information Center Projects and by students working on school projects, as this site is frequently used as a school research site for grade schools.

Some of Dog Breed Info Centers Advertisers do collect your cookies with the purpose of deciding which ad will be placed for you to see. We have ads placed by Advertising.com. To visit their privacy statement, please visit http://wireless.advertising.com/privacy.html.
