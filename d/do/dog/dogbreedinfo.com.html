Privacy Policy

The Dog Breed Information Center® does not collect or use your personal information. When mailing in your photos, listing in our classifieds or taking our survey, your name, email address and or home address will not be sold or placed on a mailing list. Dog Breed Information Center® does not collect cookies from its users. This is strictly an information site used for research and your enjoyment.

Dog Breed Info Center(R) uses Google Adsense, who allows third-party vendors to serve ads on our website. You can find a list of allowed third party ads by going to https://www.google.com/adsense/support/bin/answer.py?answer=94149&topic=14535.

The third-party advertising companies in which Google uses to serve ads when you visit our website may use information that will help them serve ads that will interest you, however it will not collect your name, address, email address, or telephone number. It looks at visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.

Photos sent in will not be sold but may be used in other Dog Breed Information Center® Projects, books, pamphlets, flyers, magazines and by students working on school projects, as this site is frequently used as a school research site for grade schools.  www.dogbreedinfo.com © 1998-2009 All rights reserved. Schools may not however copy any part of the site and post it online.
