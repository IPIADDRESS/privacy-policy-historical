Your Privacy is Our Concern

DMi Partners recognizes the importance of protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

DMi Partners collects the following types of information:

  • Personally Identifiable Information we collect from you on applications and forms completed on our site as well as from additional sources used to process your request.
  • Information while you browse our site. While you view our site, our system tracks the referring url, the pages you view, the pages you download and your IP address. Additionally, DMi Partners and/or a marketing partner may place a “cookie” on your computer that tracks your use of our site. Please see section below for more information on cookies

Cookies

DMi Partners and/or a marketing partner may place a “cookie” on your computer that tracks your use of our site and other sites in our network. A cookie is a string of data that is stored on your hard drive, which enables DMi Partners to determine the number of unique users accessing different pages.

Sharing of Your Information

We may share all of the information we collect with companies that perform marketing services on our behalf or to financial institutions with whom we may have joint marketing arrangements. Such companies and institutions may choose to contact you to discuss the interest you have expressed via your submission of a form. By submitting the form you are consenting to receive telephone calls from these companies and institutions.

Responsibilities and Representations for Linked Web-Sites

Because DMi Partners can not control the content of sites linking to or from our site, we are not responsible, nor can we make representations to the content of those web-sites or their individual privacy policies.

Updates to Our Privacy Policy
