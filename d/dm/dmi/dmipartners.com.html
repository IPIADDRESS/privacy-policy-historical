DMi and/or a business partner may place a “cookie” on your computer that tracks your use of our Site and other sites in our network. A cookie is a string of data that is stored on your hard drive, which enables DMi and out business partners to analyze the performance of websites and marketing campaigns. Cookies can enrich your browsing experience by allowing sites to learn your preferences or allowing you to avoid signing in every time you visit certain sites.
