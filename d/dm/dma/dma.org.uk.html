  Introduction
This privacy policy sets out details of the ways in which personal information we collect about may be used. This privacy policy only related to personal data collected by the DMA (UK) Ltd via the DMA website. It does not apply to personal data provided to the DMA (UK) Ltd by any other means or via any other website. You should be aware that if you subsequently give any personal information to another company, the uses to which that information may be put will be determined by that company's privacy policy and the DMA (UK) LTD privacy policy will no longer apply.
Top
Use made of personal information
Personal information provided to the DMA (UK) Ltd via the DMA website will be used for the purpose outlined at the time of collection. In addition, it may be used for statistical analysis.
Top
Use of data for direct marketing purposes
At each point of data collection you will be given the opportunity to opt-out of receiving future marketing approaches from the DMA (UK) Ltd. If you do not opt-out, the DMA may provide you with information on DMA products, services and future events. If at any stage you wish the DMA to stop using your data for marketing purposes you can write to DMA (UK) Ltd, DMA House, 70 Margaret Street, London W1W 8SS.
Top
Disclosure of data to third parties
At each point of data collection you will be given the opportunity to opt-out of having your data passed on to third parties. If you do not opt-out, the DMA may, in limited circumstances, share your data with carefully selected third parties so that they can contact you regarding events, products or services that may be of interest to you. If at any stage you wish the DMA to stop using your data in this way you can write to DMA (UK) Ltd, DMA House, 70 Margaret Street, London W1W 8SS.
Top
Use of Cookies
A "cookie" is a piece of information, like a tag, which some websites create on your PC. Our cookies allocate each user of this website with a random number. The cookies used on this site are not used for the collection of any personal information. Our cookies will not be used to analyse your visits to other websites or track any internet searches you may undertake whilst on the website. The information collected via cookies will be used to track movement on a company basis and for statistical analysis
Top
Updating and amending your personal information
If, at any time, you wish to verify, update or amend the personal information you have provided to the DMA (UK) Ltd, you can do so online by sending and email to sean@dma.org.uk. Alternatively you can write to DMA (UK) Ltd, DMA House, 70 Margaret Street, London W1W 8SS. Verification, updating or amendment will take place within 40 days of receipt of request.
Top
Third party sites
Our privacy policy does not apply to third party sites that you may access from this website. We recommend you familiarise yourself with the applicable privacy policy before entering any personal information on a third party site.
Top
Security
We have implemented several security measures to ensure that your personal information is protected. Your password is stored in an encrypted format. Information you provide to us via the DMA site is protected using encryption whenever it is transmitted over the Internet. In addition, we use a firewall to prevent third parties from accessing your personal information and other physical measures to prevent registered users from accessing the personal information of other users.
Top
Consent
By providing your data to the DMA (UK) Ltd you give your consent to the collection, storage, processing and use of your personal information by us as described in this privacy policy.
