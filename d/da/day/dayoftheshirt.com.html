Privacy Policy

Our Commitment To Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used.

The Information We Collect

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the Day of the Shirt website and Day of the Shirt applications for iPhone and other devices and platforms, hereafter referred to as simply Day of the Shirt.

Day of the Shirt occasionally needs to collect information for convenience, practicality, or technical requirements. Like most web sites and applications, Day of the Shirt collects and stores information automatically and through the use of electronic tools that may be transparent to our visitors. For example, we may log the name of your Internet Service Provider, your IP address, and information provided by and about your Internet browser. As we adopt additional technology, we may also gather similar information through other means.

We may also use "cookie" technology on our website. Among other things, we may use cookies or other tracking technology for the purposes of tracking your activities on our website. Also, when you click through to visit a merchant, we may redirect you through services that place cookies for sales or affiliate tracking purposes.

The Way We Use Information

We use email addresses that you may submit for our own outreach and announcements. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties.

We use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design Day of the Shirt, to suggest popular content to users, and to share with advertisers and publishers. For example:

  • We may change the layout of our website or application based on the number of people that perform a particular action.
  • We may tell an advertiser or publisher that X number of people visited a certain area in our website or application.
  • We may tell an advertiser or publisher that X number of people clicked through a link in our website or application.

When information is used in this or a similar manner, we do not disclose anything that could be used to identify the individuals on whom the information is based.

We may disclose your information in response to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or to defend against legal claims. We may disclose your information when we believe it necessary or desirable in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of our policies, and/or to protect our rights and property.

In the future, we may sell to, buy, or partner with other companies or businesses. In such transactions, user information may be among the transferred assets.

How To Contact Us

Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please email hello@dayoftheshirt.com. At this time, we only have the ability to receive and send English-language communications.

Changes To This Policy

Day of the Shirt reserves the right to change this policy at any time. Please check this page periodically for changes. Your continued use of Day of the Shirt following the posting of changes to this policy will mean you accept those changes.
