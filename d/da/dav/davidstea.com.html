Such collection is performed by most webpages, and will not allow you to be personally identified. For example, Davids Tea utilizes “cookies”, which do not contain any personal information that allows you to be identified, but which contains certain statistical information. You can instruct your computer to inform you whenever a cookie is being sent, or you can disallow cookies through you web browser. If you do choose to disallow cookies, some features of this webpage may not function, or your ability to choose some of the options may be limited.

      4. To update you on changes to our services or products, including new promotions.

The Personal Information we collect will vary depending on how you are using the webpage, but may include your name, address, email address, credit card information, credit information, and telephone number.

Should any breach of your personal information occur, we will inform you as soon as possible.
