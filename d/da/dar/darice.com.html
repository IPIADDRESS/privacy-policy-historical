Darice Privacy Policy

www.darice.com (“Darice”) is sensitive to your rights of privacy on the Internet. We believe it is important that you know what information we collect on our website and how the information is used.

In general, you can visit and browse Darice without telling us who you are or reveal any information about yourself. Our hosting servers collect the domain names, not the email addresses, of the visitors. None of the information collected identifies you personally. The information is aggregated to measure the number of visits, average time spent on the site, pages viewed, etc. Darice uses this information to measure the use of the site and to improve its contents.

You may voluntarily provide us with your personal information in order to register an account. We will use this information to help you with your purchases, and improve our products and services for you. We may also use it to see how this website is being used and/or to adjust this website to your personal preferences. Additionally, we may further use this information to send you materials and information such as product announcements, coupons, promotions, or other information that we feel may be of use or value to you.

If you do not want to receive further communications from us via e-mail, please let us know. We are committed to respecting your privacy. Darice will not share any of this information with third parties except marketing companies engaged by Darice to conduct marketing campaigns or promotions under the control and direction of Darice.

Darice reserves the right to change or update this policy at any time. Such modifications will be effective on the date that we post the modified policy on our websites.

Please see our Legal Disclaimer for more information. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or about this website in general, please contact us at your convenience.
