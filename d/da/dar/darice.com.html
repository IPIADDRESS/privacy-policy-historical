Darice Inc. (“Darice”) is sensitive to your rights of privacy on the Internet. We believe it is important that you know what information we collect on our websites and how the information is used.

We will use this information to improve our products and services. We may also use it to see how this website is being used and/or to adjust this website to your personal preferences. Additionally, we may further use this information to send to you materials and information such as new product announcements or other information that we feel may be of use or value to you.

If you do not want to receive further communications from us via e-mail, please let us know. We are committed to respecting your privacy.

Darice may share the information collected at this website with its subsidiaries and affiliates for use as described above. Darice will not share any of this information with third parties except marketing companies engaged by Darice to conduct marketing campaigns or promotions under the control and direction of Darice.

Darice reserves the right to change or update this policy at any time. Such modifications will be effective on the date that we post the modified policy on our websites.
