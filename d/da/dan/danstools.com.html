OUR PRIVACY POLICY -

The Tools Resource Guide

 

We value your right to privacy.

DansTools.com is committed to protecting the privacy of our online visitors. We only collect information from our customers who ask to be on our mailing list and/or receive periodic email announcements. We will not sell, trade, or give your personal information to unauthorized companies or organizations. Anyone can choose to be removed from our mailing list at any time, just let us know.

In addition, online customers who purchase through our Web site must provide their name, email address, mailing address, phone number, credit card number and expiration date. All credit card transactions occur over a secure connection.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we learn that we have personal information on a child under the age of 13, we will delete that information from our systems. DansTools.com encourages parents to go online with their kids. Here are a few tips to help make a child's online experience safer:

Teach kids never to give personal information, unless supervised by a parent or responsible adult. This includes name, address, phone, school, etc. Know the sites your kids are visiting and which sites are appropriate. Look for Web site privacy policies. Know how your child's information is treated. Check out the FTC's site for more tips on protecting children's privacy online.
