PRIVACY & SECURITY

Thank you for choosing to shop at Danier Leather and www.danier.com. Your privacy and security are important to us. For over 28 years, we have been dealing with customers like you in our stores and we pride ourselves on making the shopping experience enjoyable, safe and secure. Our goal is to maintain this experience on line. All personal information and credit card information you share with us are handled with the highest security features and held in the strictest confidence. We will never divulge, sell, barter or share customer information without explicit prior consent from our customers and visitors.

At Danier respecting the privacy and the wishes of our customers is an important part of our everyday commitment to fulfill our Mission: “to Satisfy Our Customers’ Need to Feel Good About Themselves.” Below is our Privacy Protection Policy which spells out our responsibilities and commitments to the secure protection and responsible use of our customers’ personal information entrusted to us.

The Danier Privacy Protection Policy

Why Do We Need Your Personal Information?

When you register for our contests or make a purchase, we collect information such as your name, address, e-mail address and birthdate. We will use this information for warranty claims and to tell you about free gifts, coupons, prizes, exciting offers or new products related specifically to Danier Leather. Your birthdate is used to validate a winner in our win instantly contest(s). We do not share your personal information with third parties, except where they are providing us a direct service for the above; we do not provide personal information to any third parties for any other purposes.

Removing Your Name From Our List

If at any time, you wish to be removed from our e-mailing list, all you need to do is use the hyperlink located at the bottom of each e-mail you receive, or send an e-mail to privacy@danier.com with “unsubscribe” in the Subject heading.

You can also choose not to receive any other marketing communications from Danier, either by communicating this to us during your purchase, or by letting us know at any time thereafter, whereupon we will ensure that you do not receive any marketing communications, including offers, special discounts nor Danier promotional notices. Please communicate this in writing including your name, address, phone number & email address (if applicable) to the following addresses:

privacy@danier.com

OR

Privacy Officer – Unsubscribe
Danier Leather
2650 St. Clair Ave. West
Toronto ON M6N 1M2

Credit Card Security Online

When you decide to make an online purchase all information is encrypted according to the SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) Technology. This technology automatically encrypts your information before it is sent to us. It also encrypts any information we return to you. SSL is widely used and is trusted as providing the highest level of protection of data between you and our server. For more detailed information on SSL, please visit Verisign’s website. SSL only works with SSL-enabled browsers and does not encrypt email.

If you prefer not to use your credit card online then call our 24-hour, 7 days a week customer service at 1-877-9DANIER to complete your order with one of our staff.

A Word About Cookies

Cookies are used as part of the software that runs our site. When you visit, our site sends a cookie to your browser’s cookie file. This cookie file is simply a text file and our cookie is just another line of text. A cookie contains no personal information. It is only in your file during your visit to our site. It does not stay in your file after you have left. Turning off your Internet browser’s ability to access cookies will increase your security but could disable some of the Web site’s functionality.

Links

The Danier Web site may contain links to other sites. Please be aware that Danier is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware of this when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of every Web site that collects personally identifiable information.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to change our privacy policy at any time. Accordingly we recommend that you check this page periodically in order to review our current policies.
