As an online business, ensuring the privacy of your information is a top priority for us. At DankStop, we want to make your online shopping experience both safe and satisfying. Because we gather certain types of information about our users, we feel you should fully understand our policy and the terms and conditions surrounding the capture and use of that information. This privacy statement discloses what information we gather and how we use it.

Information DankStop gathers about you:

1. Electronic newsletters policy

We offer a free electronic newsletter to our customers. DankStop gathers the email addresses of users who make purchases on our site. Users may remove themselves from this mailing list by following the link provided in every newsletter that points users to the subscription management page that offers a simple one click un-subscribe.

  • First and Last Name
