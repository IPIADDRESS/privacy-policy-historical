Privacy

Seven Aspens is committed to protecting the privacy of the information that we collect from users of our Web site. Information is obtained through user-submitted request-for-more information forms. These forms require users to give us contact information (such as name, company, e-mail address, street address, telephone and fax numbers and educational information). This data is used to provide the information to those who inquire about our business and services offered or employment opportunities.

We consider your data to be private and confidential. We will not give, rent, loan, or sell any identifiable personal information to any other third party, unless legally required to do so. We may store and process that information to better understand your needs and how we can improve our products and services. In addition, we may use that information to contact you.

Currently, this Web site makes no use of cookies for identification, tracking, or for any other purpose. If, however, in the future, we decide to use this technology, the information collected from cookies would be solely used for the purpose of better understanding our users and their use of the website.

Our website is protected with a variety of security measures. We employ a variety of mechanisms to ensure that data you provide is not lost, misused, or altered inappropriately.

Our Web site may contain links to Web sites operated and maintained by third parties, over which we have no control. Privacy policies on such linked sites may be different from our privacy policy. You access such linked sites at your own risk. You should always read the privacy policy of a linked site before disclosing any personal information on that site.

If you have any questions about this Privacy policy statement, the practices of this website or your dealings with Seven Aspens, contact us using the information in the contact section on the website. We reserve the right to change this policy at any time by posting a new policy at this location.
