DAS respects our customer's right to privacy online. We hold your detailed customer information in confidence.

Asking for your information
We will sometimes ask customers for personal information such as your name, address, e-mail address, and other details. Providing us your information will simply allow us to serve your on-line needs. When you choose to give personal information to us over the Internet, we strive to keep that information secure.

Personal Information Privacy
The information you provide to DAS over the internet is kept confidential, and is never sold, lent or given to any other entity outside the company. DAS will take all appropriate steps to keep your personal information confidential. Those steps include limiting access to customer information databases, communicating this policy statement to all DAS employees and establishing and enforcing penalties for violating this statement.
