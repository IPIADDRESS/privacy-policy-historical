It may also include statistical data such as the web pages that you visit at The Daily Hodl, the time and date of your visit, and the duration of your visit.

Consent to the Use of Cookies

The Daily Hodl uses “cookies” to ensure the functionality of certain website features. Cookies are files that are sent to your browser from a website you visit. They contain small amounts of data used as an anonymous unique identifier and are stored on your computer’s hard drive. The data is not shared or used for any commercial purpose. It is used to give you a seamless experience at The Daily Hodl as cookies are designed to remember preceding events or user interactions, and other user browsing information. You are free to delete these cookies from within your web browser.

By visiting The Daily Hodl, you agree to the use of cookies.

The Daily Hodl and our website hosting platform strive to secure the personal information you provide to us including your name and your email address, but we cannot guarantee its absolute security. Use of the internet exposes all users to a certain degree of risk. Please take measures to secure access to your computer and to your internet access.
