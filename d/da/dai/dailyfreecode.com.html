We try not to publish your email address to avoid spam, but it is possible that in certain cases your email address may be exposed to public. However we publish all/part of information you submit as part of contribution (including, but not limited to article, etc) to our site. If you prefer not to publish any information, you may not submit any article, post, or any other kind of content to this site. Our site also provides social networking service, wherein all your Friends and Activity on this website would be accessible to everyone using this website. If you do not wish to expose your social details, please don’t share it.

For statistical purposes, we gather and store general visitor information like IP Address, browser version, visting time, duration etc. We may share such information with third parties or publish in our site for marketing purposes.

Cookies
Cookies are small pieces of information stored in your computer. We store small cookies in your computer, to provide a better browsing experience for you. We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website.
  • Third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on DailyFreeCode.Com.
  • Third party vendor make use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to DailyFreeCode.Com users based on their visit to our sites and other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.
  • Remember to always click the Log-out/sign-out link when you’re done using DailyFreeCode.Com, especially if you’re on a shared computer or using a friend’s computer. If you simply quit the browser but don’t log-out/sign-out, anyone can reopen DailyFreeCode.Com on that browser later and access your details.
  • We also store email of last user login. By Registering with DailyFreeCode.Com you agree's that your email would be shared on computer you used to access our website.
