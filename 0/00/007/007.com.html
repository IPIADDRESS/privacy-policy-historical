Cookies and web beacons

We, our third party service providers, and partners, may send "cookies" to your computer or use similar technologies to enhance your online experience at our website and across the Internet.
"Cookies" are small pieces of information stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive.  Cookies identify you as a unique customer and store your personal preferences as well as technical information including click through and click stream data. Click stream data tells us which site pages you have visited and in what order. Cookies manage and measure the performance of promotional messages displayed on or delivered by or through the Site. We use cookies and other technical information to analyse traffic on the Site and to track user trends, to determine how frequently particular pages are visited, to determine the most popular areas of the Site and for technical reasons connected with your use of our Site. This helps us to enhance your online experience and to improve the design and content of the Site to suit your needs. Cookies can either be permanent (i.e., they remain on your computer until you delete them) or temporary (i.e. they last only until you close your browser).
We may also use "web beacons" or similar
technology that monitor your use of our Site and allow us to understand which pages you visit on our Site. These may also be referred to as âClear GIFsâ. Web beacons (or web bugs) are small strings of code that provide a method for delivering a graphic image on a web page for the purpose of transferring data, such as the IP (Internet Protocol) address of the computer that downloaded the page on which the web beacon appears, the URL (Uniform Resource Locator) of the page on which the web beacons appears, the time the page containing the web beacon was viewed, the types of browser that fetched the web beacon and the identification number of any cookie on the computer previously placed by that server.
By accepting this Privacy Policy, you specifically agree to our use of cookies and web beacons as described in this Privacy Policy. Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. However, if you prefer, you may adjust your browser to reject cookies from us or from any other website.  Please consult the "Help" section of your browser for more information. However, please note that certain areas of our Site can only be accessed in conjunction with cookies or similar devices and you should be aware that disabling cookies or similar devices may prevent you from accessing some of our content.
