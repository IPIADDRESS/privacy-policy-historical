PRIVACY POLICY
Version 1.0, 25 January 2012

  • 1. Introduction

    This Privacy and Security Policy explains how we protect and use your personal information. “Personal information” for purposes of this Policy means any information that is recognized as such under South African Law and includes any information which identifies you.

  • 2. Information we collect

    In your use of the Website and Services, we may collect and store the following personal information:

    • 2.1

      name, email address and physical contact information;

    • 2.2

      transactional information based on your activities on the Website;

    • 2.3

      billing information;

    • 2.4

      community discussions, chats, correspondence through our Website, and correspondence with us;

    • 2.5

      website connection information, statistics on page views, traffic to and from the Website's address and standard web log information;

    • 2.6

      additional information we may ask you to submit to authenticate yourself (for example, we may ask you to send us an ID or utility bill to verify your address, or to answer additional questions to help verify your identity);

    • 2.7

      third-party account credentials (“account credentials”) to allow us to access your account and other necessary data at financial and other institutions (“account information”).

  • 3. How we use Personal Information

    • 3.1

      At the outset we would like to give you the comfort that you will always own your personal information and that we do not sell your personal information to anyone, for any reason, at any time.

    • 3.2

      We use and disclose your personal information only as follows:

      • 3.2.1

        to analyse Website and Service usage and to improve the Service;

      • 3.2.2

        to deliver to you any administrative notices, alerts and communications relevant to your use of the Service;

      • 3.2.3

        to enable us to process, validate and verify requests;

      • 3.2.4

        for market research, project planning, troubleshooting problems, detecting and protecting against error, fraud or other criminal activity;

      • 3.2.5

        to third-party service providers appointed by us to enable us to provide the Services to you, and who are bound by these same privacy restrictions;

      • 3.2.6

        to allow us and our authorised third party service providers to access your (“Account Information”) for your use.

      • 3.2.7

        as otherwise set forth in this Privacy and Security Policy.

    • 3.3

      In addition, we may disclose personal information to law enforcement, other government officials, or other third parties as we, in our sole discretion, believe necessary or appropriate in connection with an investigation of fraud, intellectual property infringements, or other activity that is illegal or may expose us to legal liability, or in connection with a merger, consolidation, or sale of our assets.

  • 4. Email address and Password

    As part of customer registration, you must choose an email address and Password ("Password"). Your Password must not be disclosed to any unauthorised third party and you must immediately report any actual or potential unauthorised access to or use of your email address and/or Password. On receipt of such report, we reserve the right to reject any communication from you, suspend the processing of any communication and requests not yet executed by us and immediately deactivate your access to the Service. You may not use the Service until a new Password has been authorised by us. We may from time to time require you to change your Password, and you undertake to comply with such instruction immediately.

  • 5. Cookies

    We automatically collect information and data through the use of "cookies". "Cookies" are small text files a Website can use to recognise repeat users, facilitate the user's ongoing access to and use of the Website and allow a Website to track usage behaviour and compile aggregate data that will allow content improvements and targeted advertising. We may use cookies and log information to: (a) remember information so that you will not have to re-enter it during your visit or the next time you visit the Website; (b) provide custom, personalized content and information; (c) monitor the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns; (d) monitor aggregate metrics such as total number of visitors, pages viewed, etc.

  • 6. Policy Modifications

    We may change this Privacy and Security Policy from time to time and we will post any changes on the Website.

  • 7. Comments and Questions

    If you have any questions, comments or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at thesvens@22seven.com.
