What information does 2020software.com collect from me? 2020software.com collects information from a variety of web pages within our network of accounting and business software related websites. Most importantly we collect your contact information so that we can send you demonstrations and test-drives for accounting and business software. In addition, we will request information about your company or your client's company. This information is used to help us decide which software may be suitable for your purposes.

What are cookies and how does 2020software.com use them? As part of offering and providing customizable and personalized services, 2020software.com uses cookies to store and sometimes track information about you. A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. All sites on 2020software.com where you are prompted to log-in or that are customizable require that you accept cookies.

Generally, we use cookies to:

  1. Remind us of who you are and to access your account information (stored on our computers) in order to deliver to you a better and more personalized service. This cookie is set when you register or "Sign In" and is modified when you "Sign Out" of our services. 2020software.com also uses cookies to store your zip code for other services, such as Yellow Pages.
  2. Estimate our audience size. Each browser accessing 2020software.com is given a unique cookie which is then used to determine the extent of repeat usage, usage by a registered user versus by an unregistered user, and to help target advertisements based on user interests and behavior.
  3. Assist merchants in 2020software.com Stores to track visits to and business at their stores, and to process the items in your shopping basket. This information collected by cookies is sometimes called "click stream" or "click trail" and may also describe which pages you have seen in each merchant's store.
  4. Track your progress and number of entries in some of our promotions, sweepstakes and contests. When a 2020software.com-hosted promotion uses cookies, the information written to the cookie indicates the player's progress through the promotion, and may be used to track entries, submissions, and status in prize drawings.
  5. Measure certain traffic patterns, which areas of 2020software.com's network of web sites you have visited, and your visiting patterns in the aggregate. We use this research to understand how our users' habits are similar or different from one another so that we can make each new experience on 2020software.com a better one. We may use this information to better personalize the content, banners, and promotions that you and other users will see on our sites.

Advertising networks that serve ads onto 2020software.com may also use their own cookies.

2020software.com also collects IP addresses for system administration and to report aggregate information to our advertisers. In addition, 2020software.com Mail includes IP addresses in outgoing mail message headers.

How does 2020software.com use my information? 2020software.com's primary goal in collecting information is to provide you, the user, with accounting and business software information. This information is available free of charge and no compensation is expected. We do offer free demos and brochures for multiple accounting and business software systems. In this case your user information is shared with developers and/or resellers of accounting and business software systems. This information is forwarded to the developer so that you can receive the requested information.  Another goal of 2020software.com is to provide you, the user, with a customized experience on our network of sites. This includes personalization services, interactive communications, online shopping and many other types of services, most of which are completely free to you. In order to provide services free of charge, we display advertisements. By knowing a little about you, 2020software.com is able to deliver more relevant advertisements and content, and hence better service to you.  We also provide newsletters and business forms catalogs and coupons.  We ask if you wish to be included in these mailings but you may receive these mailings regardless.  In all cases you can opt-out of all mailings from 2020software.

With whom does 2020software.com share my information? If you are requesting demos or brochures we will share that information with the developer and/or reseller of that software. For example, some 2020software.com services, like 2020software.com AccountingDemos.com, are offered in conjunction with a partner company. This information is shared with the partner company so that they may provide you with the accounting software demo and brochures.
