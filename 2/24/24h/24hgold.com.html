We have created this email privacy policy to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy and the protection of your information.


Why did you receive an email from us?

We respect your time and attention by controlling the frequency of our mailings.


How we protect your privacy
We use security measures to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of data used by our system.


Personal Information
We will never ask you for personal information--your credit card number, for example--except when you place an order. At that time, we will provide a secure (SSL, encrypted) connection and require you to submit only the information needed to complete and fulfill your order. We will not sell or rent this information to anyone, nor share it in any other way, unless required to by a law enforcement agency that takes the proper steps to require it.


Sharing and Usage
