General

Your privacy is very important to Qatar Airways and we understand how important it is to you. Qatar Airways aims to be as clear and open as it can be about what it does with your information. Once you choose to provide us with any information by which you can be identified, then it will only be used in accordance with this privacy policy. This privacy policy applies to the entire website. Please view this policy every time you use the website as its terms may change and by using this website you are deemed to have read and agreed to the most recent version of this policy.

When you use this website and have not advised Qatar Airways to the contrary, you are agreeing that Qatar Airways may use your personal information for the purposes set out in this policy.

Qatar Airways is a company based in Doha, State of Qatar and it utilises support and other functions which may require the transfer of your personal data to and from the European Economic Area from time to time. By using this website you are acknowledging and agreeing to the processing of your data in countries where the data protection laws are not as extensive as in the European Economic Area. Please refrain from supplying your data and contact us if you do not agree to the transfer of your data in the manner set out in this policy.

What information does Qatar Airways collect?

Qatar Airways may collect the following information:
your name, contact details, and flight booking details;
where you order products Qatar Airways will collect your billing information, transaction and credit information;
Qatar Airways requires that you supply personal information, including [name, address, telephone numbers, fax number and email address] prior to confirming your flight booking; and
Qatar Airways may also ask you to provide information regarding your personal or professional interests, demographics, experiences with its products, and contact preferences in order to provide you with further correspondence about its products and services.
Some pages use cookies, a data analysis technique. Please click here for more information.

How does Qatar Airways use this information?

Qatar Airways uses the information collected to help it understand your needs and provide you with a better service, and in particular for the following reasons:
To assess what special flight offers and services we can offer you.
Internal record keeping.
To improve its products and services.
Qatar Airways’ representatives may follow up, either by email, phone, SMS or mail customers who have used this website as a part of Qatar Airways’ customer care procedures
Qatar Airways may periodically send promotional emails about new products, special flight offers or other information which Qatar Airways thinks you may find interesting, using the email address which you have provided when you registered as a user on this website.
From time to time, Qatar Airways may also use your information to contact you for market research purposes. Qatar Airways may contact you by email, phone, fax, SMS or mail.

How we use cookies

A cookie is a piece of text which if you agree to will be placed on your computer’s hard drive. It helps to analyse web traffic or lets you know when you visit a particular site. Cookies allow web applications to respond to you as an individual. The web application can tailor its operations to your needs, likes and dislikes by gathering and remembering information about your preferences.

Qatar Airways uses cookies to track which pages are being used by whom. This helps Qatar Airways to analyse data about web page traffic and improve this website in order to tailor it to customer needs. Qatar Airways only uses this information for statistical analysis purposes and then the data is removed from the system. Denial of a traffic log cookie should not prevent you from using this website.
