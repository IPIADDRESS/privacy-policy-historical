QikInk.com is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of Users at all times.  QikInk.com recognizes that this information belongs to the User, and will treat such information accordingly.  QikInk.com will not disclose any Personally Identifiable Information or confidential information of any User to any third parties, except as outlined in this Privacy Policy or as may be required by law.

QikInk.com collects two kinds of information from on-line visitors, personal and non-personal information.  Visitors to the QikInk.com Website are not required to register, or provide any Personally Identifiable Information to access the QikInk.com site or review the products and services offered.  QikInk.com collects non-personal information to track the total number of guests visiting the QikInk.com site and to help QikInk.com improve the site and the services available on the site.  Such non-personal information includes the browser type and operating system being used by visitors, areas of the sites visited, date and time of access, host or service provider information and identification of the referring site.  This information is used exclusively for QikInk.com’s internal purposes and is not disclosed to any third parties.

Personally Identifiable Information, which means any information that can be used to identify or locate a specific person, including, but not limited to e-mail addresses, names, street addresses, phone numbers, fax numbers, credit card numbers and social security numbers, is collected only when voluntarily offered and solely for purposes clearly identified on the QikInk.com site.  Such Personally Identifiable Information is kept strictly confidential and will only be disclosed to third parties under the conditions described in Paragraph 5 of this Privacy Policy.
