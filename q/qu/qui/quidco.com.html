Privacy Policy

We can be reached via the Contact Us page.

Quidco does not retain any information about you if you are not a Quidco member.

Quidco does not share or sell your member information or any other data we hold about you to any third party. Your transaction information is stored in order to provide you with cashback.

The Quidco website uses cookies to track your member login as well as third party cookies to track your transactions. Acceptance of these cookies is necessary to use the Quidco site and earn cashback. These cookies do not compromise your Quidco member information.

Quidco does not share or sell your email address provided for the optional Quidco newsletter. The Quidco newsletter may contain information on offers from relevant third party merchants.

If the Quidco privacy policy changes in the future you will be contacted and have the option to opt out of any new information use.

If at any time you wish to view the member information stored by Quidco please submit a request and it will be provided.
