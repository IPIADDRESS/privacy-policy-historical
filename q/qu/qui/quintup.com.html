Privacy Policy

 

At Quintup we consider privacy a major concern. Quintup will never sell your private information nor share it with third parties without written consent from Users. The only circumstances that third parties will ever have knowledge of your existence is through your personal postings of micro jobs or related information that you post on our site.

Content that may be randomly shared with third parties include IP addresses, browser information and cookies, and may be used for purposes of surveying for marketing purposes, but no personal content will be used or shared with third parties. All contact information or personal information shared privately with Quintup will remain with Quintup.

Information that you choose to publish on the site (photos, videos, text, music, reviews, deliveries) – is no longer private, just like any information you publish online.

Technical information that is aggregated by our system may be used for optimization, analytics or content promotion is only meant for a greater user experience. Contact information may be used to contact you only in regard to activities on the website and absolutely no promotion will be done without your written consent.
