Your Privacy Protected
At Quickflix, we think attention to our subscribers preferences for products, services and information is important to building a fun and helpful web experience. This page provides information about how we pay attention while respecting the privacy and security of our subscribers.

How we use e-mail
We send one e-mail message to confirm your registration, and we occasionally use e-mail addresses for promotion of sales or other items of interest to our customers. Please note that we do not sell or share e-mail addresses with other companies.

We encourage you to e-mail us at customerservice@quickflix.com.au with your questions or comments. We read every e-mail we receive.

We do not share information with others
Quickflix does not share, sell or trade e-mail addresses, information collected as part of a survey or specific details about you or your household. Other than verifying credit card information to complete your order, we do not use or release any credit card or financial information for any purpose. For privacy purposes, all information relating to our customers is stored on a highly secure server that is not accessible via the Internet.
