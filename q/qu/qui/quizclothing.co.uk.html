PRIVACY POLICY

(Dated: May 2011)

Quiz is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers and will not disclose any information except where it is vital to provide the services which we offer, including the process of arranging an item being sent to you. Your peace of mind is very important to Quiz and we will do all we can to maintain your privacy.

1. The information we collect

1.1 Registering with Quiz allows you to purchase our products and access the services being offered. The Information that we collect are:-

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Your Address
  • Email address
  • Telephone Number
  • Details of the method by which you wish to pay for the goods

1.2 We collect personal information to allow us to process and fulfil your orders.  Email addresses or telephone numbers collected allow us to contact you urgently if there is a problem with an order. Your information may also be provided to our third party service providers to complete the fulfilment of your order.

2. Communication

2.1 By registering with or becoming a customer of Quiz and by providing us with your email address, phone number and postal address you consent to receive communication in relation to our products, services and special offers that you may be interested in. This will usually be in the format of a newsletter. The information provided may also be used to communicate offers and products of third parties which you may be interested in.

2.2 All customers of Quiz can opt out of receiving marketing material sent by us or/and by third parties. To do this you can visit your account page by logging in and making the necessary changes. Newsletter subscribers can opt out by notifying us through email or by post.

3. Cookies

3.1 Cookies are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. We use cookies to store items in your Shopping Basket between visits. If you are a registered member of our website, we also use cookies to help us identify offers that may be of particular interest to you. We may then communicate those offers to you on the website or in accordance with your contact preferences. We do not track your use of any other websites. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually change your browser to prevent that. Even without a cookie, you can still use most of the features in our store, including placing items in your Shopping Basket and purchasing them.

4. Stored Information & Security

4.1 Quiz takes the security of your personal details extremely seriously. Security policies are designed to protect the personal details under our control. The Information that we have collected from you is maintained in a secure web server, which we believe to be a well protected and secure environment.

5. User Access
