We may then communicate those offers to you on the website or in accordance with your contact preferences. We do not track your use of any other websites. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually change your browser to prevent that. Even without a cookie, you can still use most of the features in our store, including placing items in your Shopping Basket and purchasing them.
