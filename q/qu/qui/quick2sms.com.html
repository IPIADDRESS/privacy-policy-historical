Choices for personal information


  • You need an Quick2SMS Account to use Quick2SMS. Quick2SMS asks for some personal information when you create an Quick2SMS Account, including your email address and a password, which is used to protect your account from unauthorized access. An Quick2SMS Account allows you to access many of our services that require registration.
  • As an Quick2SMS member, you can create a profile or Quick2SMS community that includes personal information, such as your mobile phone number, gender, age, occupation and interests, plus other content. Names of persons who have referred you, names of persons you have referred and names of persons that your referrals have referred would be visible for public viewing.
