Privacy Policy

We value the trust that our customers have placed in us. We will protect that trust by respecting the privacy of all our customers, especially as it related to maintaining the confidentiality of any information, which we obtain.

You can visit our website without giving us any information about yourself.

Once you register with Qualitynet and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.

Qualitynet uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you and conduct researches.
