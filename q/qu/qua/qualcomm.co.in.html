QUALCOMM has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy. The following discloses our information collection, use and dissemination practices for this web site: www.qualcomm.com.


What information is collected
Our site uses mail forms to fulfil customer requests for information and/or enrolment in training courses. We collect contact information (names, addresses and phone numbers) from the mail forms. In the event the user is enrolling in a training course, we will also require additional financial and billing information (like credit card information, billing and shipping addresses etc.). Forms requiring financial and invoicing information are encrypted. When you visit our site, some information such as your Internet or IP address, Internet Service Provider, the site or most recent sites from which you linked and the time and date of your visit may be collected automatically as part of the operation of the site. This information is not linked to your personally identifiable information.


How information is used
IP addresses and other non-personal information of our web site users are used to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our web site. This information may also be used to gather broad demographic information, compile aggregate statistics about the number of visitors to our site and other "site behaviour" information.

We use customer contact information to send the user information requested by our customers about our company and promotional material from some of our partners. Demographic and profile data collected at our site may be used to tailor the visitors' experience, showing them content that we think they might be interested in, and displaying the content according to their preferences.

We do not disseminate/disclose any information collected from the site to third parties.


How the QUALCOMM Site uses cookies
Our web site may use "cookies" to obtain non-personal information. This information will be used to improve the user's online experience by delivering content specific to the user's interests. Users have the choice to accept, reject, or be notified when a cookie is set by configuring their browser preferences.


Third-party Sites
This site contains links to other sites for your convenience. These third-party sites may also request personal information from you. These third-party sites are not covered by this Privacy Statement and QUALCOMM is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites. These sites may have their own privacy policies or statements and we encourage you to learn their practices before providing information. Eudora's privacy statement is located at: www.eudora.com/privacy/


Security
This site has security measures in place intended to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. QUALCOMM has taken the necessary steps to encrypt and securely store personal information that is submitted to QUALCOMM via the www.qualcomm.com web site.


Contacting the Web Site
Any questions about this privacy statement or the practices of this site may be directed to webmaster@qualcomm.com.


Changes
QUALCOMM reserves the right to change, modify, add or remove portions of this Privacy Statement at any time, but will alert you that changes have been made by indicating on the Privacy Statement the date it was last updated. When you visit this web site you are accepting the current version of this Privacy Statement as posted on the site at that time. We recommend that users re-visit this Privacy Statement on occasion to learn of any changes.
