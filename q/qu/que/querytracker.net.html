Privacy Policy
Any information collected by QueryTracker about you and other users, including but not limited to email address, will not be sold, distributed or disclosed to any third party.
You may unsubscribe from the QT Newsletter at any time, by clicking the unsubscribe link located at the bottom of each email newsletters.
Some information about you may be shared among other QueryTracker users, and is limited to the following:

  • Your registered username at QueryTracker

  • The status of your queries, whether public or not. Status information is limited to the date of milestones in your query's life such as when it was created, when it was mailed, and when it was accepeted or rejected. Only the dates and states of these milestones are made public, actual query content is kept private unless you explicitly release it.

  • Any posted comments, reviews, agent or publisher information, or any other information which you explicitly post in any of the common areas of QueryTracker.
