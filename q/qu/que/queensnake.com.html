Preamble and General Principles


The following information is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, disseminate and safeguard the information you provide to us while accessing and using our Site. We request that you read this Privacy Policy carefully.


Revisions to this Policy

Queensnake.com reserves the right to revise, amend, or modify this policy and our other policies and agreements at any time and in any manner. The user should periodically check for any modifications of this Policy by re-visiting this web page and using the "refresh" button on the user's browser.


Personal Information

Queensnake.com collects information from Users who access the various parts of our website. We use this information to provide a customized experience as you use our website and do not share this information with third parties. However, we may disclose personal information collected when we believe that such disclosure is required by law.


Registration

Members are asked to provide certain personal information when they sign up for our website including name, address, telephone number, billing information (such as a credit card number). The personal information collected from Members during the registration process is used to manage each Member's account (such as for billing purposes). This information is collected by our credit card processors/billing companies, and is not shared with third parties, unless specifically stated otherwise or in special circumstances. You may read the privacy policies on their websites at www.ccbill.com, www.verotel.com.


Email Inquiries

When Users send email inquiries to us, the return email address is used to answer the email inquiry we receive. We does not use the return email address for any other purpose and does not share the return email address with any third party.


Internet Data

We collect IP addresses, referral URLs, browser preferences. This information is used internally to track how users come to be referred to our Site for the purpose of compensating our affiliates, advertisers, and other marketing entities. This information is not shared to third-parties. We never discloses any customer-specific information to third parties unless it is reasonable to believe that doing so is required by law, a binding contract, a Court Order, or other state, local, or administrative regulation.


"Cookies"

We may use a cookie to save your settings and to provide customizable and personalized services to you. These cookies do not enable third parties to access any of your customer information.


E-mail list

You will have the option to join our mailing list. If you choose to join the mailing list, your email address will be stored in our database and used periodically to send you news and promotions about our website. You can be removed from the list by following the link contained in the email instructing you on how to unsubscribe. Every email we send you will have a link contained therein which will direct you to a site where you can unsubscribe. These unsubscribe links, when clicked, will automatically remove your email address from our database without any additional action on your part.


Children's Protection

Our sites are designed for adults, but not for use by anyone under the age of 18. We make every effort to ensure that our website contain adequate warnings to make our content obvious and to warn users under the age of 18 that they are not welcome to view our website.


Data Security

We make every effort to ensure the integrity and security of our network and systems, however we cannot guarantee that our security measures will prevent "hackers" from illegally obtaining information.


Questions about Our Privacy Policy

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the practices described herein, you may contact our Support Team at support(AT)queensnake(DOT)com.
