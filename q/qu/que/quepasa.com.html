Quepasa's Services are not intended for, nor directed to, persons under 14 years of age. Quepasa endeavors to help parents ensure that their children have a fun and safe online experience. Even though Quepasa is not directed towards children under the age of 14, we will permit parents to request a review of the information collected by Quepasa of their child's information by sending an email to qpsupport@quepasacorp.com.
